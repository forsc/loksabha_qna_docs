                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3565






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Collection of User Fees by Toll Plazas




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of annual collection of user fees by toll plazas in the State of Jharkhand during the last three years and the current year;(b) the number of toll plazas which are under litigation for remittance of toll collections along with the total amount involved therein in the State; and(c) whether the Government has any proposal to review and reduce the user fee for the National Highways which were constructed more than a decade ago and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The details of user fee collected at fee plazas on National Highways in the State of Jharkhand in the last three years and the current year are as under:Financial Year	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22 (upto February, 2022)User fee collection (Rs. in Crores)	276.95	272.84	310.16	385.01 (b) No fee plaza on National Highway is under litigation for remittance of toll collection in the State of Jharkhand. However, there was contractual dispute over remittance of toll collection, in case of two fee plazas, which was referred to Conciliation Committee of Independent Experts (CCIE) for settlement. The details are as under:S. No.	Name of the Agency	Fee Plaza	Disputed Amount (Rs. in Crores)1	M/s MEP Infrastructure Developers Pvt. Ltd.	Pundag	0.85 2	M/s MEP Infrastructure Developers Pvt. Ltd.	Ghagri	11.73  (c) No, Sir.*****
