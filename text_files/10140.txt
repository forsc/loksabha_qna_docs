                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Closure and Disinvestment of CPSEs




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) functioning under the Department of Heavy Industries (DHI) till date;(b) the details of the number of CPSEs disinvested/proposed to be disinvested, closed under DHI during the last five years, year and CPSE-wise; and(c) the reasons behind the closure and disinvestment of such CPSEs?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                     (SHRI ARJUN RAM MEGHWAL)(a) & (b): There are 31 Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) under the administrative control of Department of Heavy Industry (DHI). The list of 31 CPSEs along with the status of disinvestment and closure during the last five years is annexed.(c): Closure of sick / loss making CPSEs and disinvestment of CPSEs in non-strategic sector will reduce distortions in the market place and will substantially increase efficiency, making Indian production more competitive globally, thereby increasing exports, reducing imports and creating significant new drivers for employment.                                            ***********                                                                                                                               ANNEXURE                               LIST OF CPSEs UNDER DHIS.
