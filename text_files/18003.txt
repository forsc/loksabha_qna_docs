                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1722






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Stopping of Concession in Fare




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the concessions that were given earlier to old age people, physically challenged persons and others in special trains has been stopped;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to provide concessions in passenger fare in the special trains running at present on the lines of the past system;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                          MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY                                         (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): In view of Health Advisory issued due to Covid-19 pandemic, with effect from 20.03.2020, concessions on passenger fare have been withdrawn for all categories of passengers except for the 4 categories of Divyangjan, 11 categories of Patients and Students till further advice.   *****
