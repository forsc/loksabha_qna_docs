                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1552






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






PM KISAN Scheme Beneficiaries  in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of revenue villages where farmers are benefited by the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM KISAN) Yojana in Jharkhand especially in Giridih constituency; (b) 	whether farmers of some revenue villages are deprived of the benefits of this scheme; (c) 	if so, the number of such revenue villages and the details thereof; and (d) 	the time by which the farmers of such revenue villages are likely to receive the amount under this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):  The total number of revenue villages in Jharkhand where farmers have been benefitted by Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojna is 30060, out of which, 2472 revenue villages belong to Giridih Constituency.(b) to (d): Farmers in 1052 villages are yet to get benefit under the Scheme. The details of the villages who have been registered but not received benefits under the Scheme is given at Annexure-I. The Scheme is a continuous and ongoing Scheme, in which the financial benefits are transferred to the bank accounts of the identified beneficiaries as and when their correct and verified data is uploaded by the concerned States / UTs on PM-Kisan web-portal.
