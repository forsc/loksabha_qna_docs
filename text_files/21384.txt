                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2742






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Optical Fibre in Himachal Pradesh




Kishan Kapoor














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is carrying out the work of laying optical fibre in every village of Himachal Pradesh to provide internet facility; (b) 	the number of districts of the country where the work of laying optical fibre has been completed, State/ UT-wise including Himachal Pradesh; and (c) 	the time by which the said work is likely to be completed in the remaining districts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide Broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2.5 lakh GPs) in the country including Himachal Pradesh.  Now the scope of BharatNet is being expanded to provide Broadband connectivity to all the inhabited villages (6 Lakh) through various models including Public Private Partnership (PPP) Model.(b)	State/UT wise details of number of districts in the country including Himachal Pradesh where the work of laying of optical fibre has been completed to provide BharatNet connectivity upto Gram Panchayats is given in Annexure-I.(c)	The remaining districts are targeted to be completed by May 2023.
