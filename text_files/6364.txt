                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

48






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






UGC




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government/UGC has identified the educationally backward districts inGujarat where gross enrolment ratio is lesser than the national average percentage;(b) if so, the names of the districts which have been identified in 2011 census; and(c) the efforts made or being made by the Government to improve the level of highereducation in these districts?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): An Expert Committee constituted by the University Grants Commission (UGC)had identified 374 Educationally Backward Districts (EBDs) where Gross EnrolmentRatio (GER) in higher education was less than the national average of 12.4% based on2001 census data. Amongst the 374 EBDs, 20 districts were identified in the State ofGujarat. The list of 20 EBDs in the State of Gujarat is annexed.The Centrally sponsored scheme of Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan(RUSA) is being implemented by the Ministry of Human Resource Development with theaim of promoting access, equity and quality. Inter alia, the scheme provides centralassistance to States for creation of Model Degree Colleges, infrastructure grants tocollege and universities, creation of universities either through upgradation of existingautonomous colleges or clustering of colleges etc.The Project Approval Board (PAB) of RUSA has approved an amount of Rs.293.15 crores, under various components, for 20 identified EBDs in the State of Gujarat.The list of Model Degree Colleges approved in these 20 EBDs under the erstwhilescheme of Model Degree Colleges is annexed. The GER of Gujarat has increasedfrom 16.5 in 2011-12 to 20.4 in 2018-19.***
