                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2467






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Widening of NHs in Uttarakhand




Tirath Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the single-lane National Highways (NHs) in Uttarakhand;(b)	whether the Government proposes to develop/widen these single-lane NHs to two, four and six lane highways;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the funds released for the purpose during each of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The total length of single lane National Highways in Uttarakhand is 1843 km.(b)	    The development/widening of a section of National Highway is a continuous process and is carried out based on requirement of traffic plying on road, inter-se-priority and availability of funds. (c)&(d)   At present widening of National Highways in Uttarakhand to two& four lanes is being done in 788 km & 338 km respectively. There is no proposal to widen a NH to six lane in the State. The details of funds released for this purpose during last 3 years is at Annexure- A.
