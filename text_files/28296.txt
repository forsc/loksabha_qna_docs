                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

45






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Rolling Auctions




 Chinta Anuradha













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has introduced a mechanism of rolling auctions to expedite  the auction process in the coal mining sector;(b)        if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any new mines have been identified to become a part of this rolling auction process; and(d)	if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COAL, MINES & PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.   STATEMENT IN REPLY TO THE LOK SABHA STARREED QUESTION NO. 45 ANSWERED ON 01/12/2021 ASKED BY SHRIMATI CHINTA ANURADHA, DR. BEESETTI VENKATA SATYAVATHI REGARDING ROLLING AUCTION (a)        Yes, Sir and Madam. (b)        In order to bring greater transparency in the selection of mines and expedite the auction process to carry out more rounds of auctions in a year, a mechanism of rolling auctions of coal mines has been taken up.  Under this mechanism, upon completion of the electronic auction process of a tranche, the next tranche of auction would be launched for the following mines:- a.	Mines where no bid or only single bid was received in the previous tranche of auction (except for those mines where only 1 bid has been received and Ministry of Coal decides to go for second attempt of auction)b.	New mines, if any, identified by Ministry of Coal (c)        Yes, Sir and Madam. (d):      In the currently ongoing auction of 88 coal mines for commercial mining in 13th Tranche under the Coal Mines (Special Provisions) Act, 2015 and 3rd Tranche under the Mines and Minerals (Development & Regulation) Act, 1957,  48 coal mines have been rolled over from the 12th Tranche under CMSP Act, 2015 and 2nd Tranche under MMDR Act, 1957 of auction. ******
