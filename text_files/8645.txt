                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2089






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Import of Crude Oil from Russia




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is seriously considering to import crude oil from Russia and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether any step has been taken by the Government in this direction;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): Bilateral cooperation in hydrocarbon sector is an important pillar of the strategic partnership between India and Russia. Indian oil and gas companies are already importing crude/natural gas from Russia based on the domestic requirements and commercial viability. Companies of both the countries are engaged in expanding import of crude oil from Russia. *****
