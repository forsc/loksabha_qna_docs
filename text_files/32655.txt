                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

63






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Requirement of Steel




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steel requirement of Railways for 2021-2022;(b)	whether the demand for steel by the Railways has been met by SAIL;(c)	if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether the Railways had procured steel from Jindal Steel and Power Limited for the current year and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether the Ministry is of the opinion that additional steel needs to be imported to meet its demand requirements, and if so, whether any effort has been made by the Railways to import steel?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	The steel requirement of Railways (including 12.26 Lakh MT Rail) for 2021-2022 is 14.02 Lakh MT approximately.(b) & (c) 100% Requirement of Rail is met by M/s SAIL. Demand of  steel used for other than Rail purpose are being met  partially by M/s SAIL and balance demand of steel used for other steel products are met by other indigenous producers of Steel.(d)	Yes, Sir. Approximately 2555 MT of steel has been procured from M/s JSPL in the current year. (e)	No, Sir.****
