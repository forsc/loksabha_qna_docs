                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2901






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Increase in Footfall at Delhi Airport




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the increasing footfall at Delhi Airport and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has taken any steps to increase the passenger handling capacity of Delhi airport to reduce long waiting and inconvenience to passengers and if so, the details thereof; and (c) the steps taken by the Government to enhance domestic and international flights frequency to and from Delhi Airport?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a): The passenger traffic has been increasing continuously at Indira Gandhi International Airport (IGIA), Delhi till the financial year (FY) 2020. In the FY 2020, approx. 67.3 million passengers travelled through Delhi airport as compared to approx. 16.2 million passengers in the year 2006.;(b) & (c): In line with the increasing passenger demand over the recent past (prior to COVID), IGI Airport implemented technological improvements at its passenger terminals as well as undertook various interventions on airside to increase the terminal and airside capacities. Delhi International Airport Limited (DIAL) is also undertaking airport expansion project which includes expansion of Terminal 1, laying of new Runway, expansion of Terminal 3 etc. The developments will be undertaken in a phased manner as per the Master Plan so that additional capacities are available in time for catering to the future traffic growth. With the completion of Phase 3A, the passenger handling capacity of IGIA, Delhi would be 100 million passengers per annum. Besides, Delhi airport was connected with 96 destinations operated by 55 airlines in FY2006, which increased to 149 destinations with 65 airlines in FY 2020.
