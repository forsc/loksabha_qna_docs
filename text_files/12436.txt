10.02.2020






Monuments of National Importance




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number and details of the monuments or sites declared of national importance by the Archaeological Survey of India;(b)	the funds spent on such monuments and sites during the last five years; and(c)	the number and details of the cities selected so far under the Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY)?






ANSWER
