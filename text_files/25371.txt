                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1338






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Plastic Park




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    whether there is any plastic park available in the country;b) if so, the details thereof. State-wise;c) the details of the action taken for promoting and implementing the scheme forsetting up of plastic parks in the country; andd) the details about the availability of plastic park in the State of Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS (SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The Department of Chemicals and Petrochemicals has sanctionedthe setting up of ten Plastic Parks across the country and out of these final approvalhas been accorded to eight Plastic Parks. These are located in the States of Assam,Madhya Pradesh (two Plastic Parks), Odisha, Jharkhand, Tamil Nadu,Chhattisgarh and Uttarakhand. The details of these eight Plastic Parks are asunder:1) Madhya Pradesh: Plastic Park at Tamot has been approved with a total projectcost of Rs. 108.00 crore with a grant-in-aid of Rs. 40.00 crore from Governmentof India.2) Madhya Pradesh: Plastic Park at Bilaua has been approved with a total projectcost of Rs. 68.72 crore with a grant-in-aid of Rs. 34.36 crore from Governmentof India.3) Odisha: Plastic Park at Paradeep has been approved with a total project costof Rs. 106.78 crore with a grant-in-aid of Rs. 40.00 crore from Government ofIndia.4) Jharkhand: Plastic Park at Deoghar has been approved with a total projectcost of Rs. 67.33 crore with a grant-in-aid of Rs. 33.66 crore from Governmentof India.5) Tamil Nadu: Plastic Park at Thiruvallur has been approved with a totalproject cost of Rs. 216.92 crore with a grant-in-aid of Rs. 40.00 crore fromGovernment of India.6) Assam: Plastic Park at Tinsukia has been approved with a total project cost ofRs. 93.65 crore with a grant-in-aid of Rs. 40.00 crore from Government of India.7) Uttarakhand: Plastic Park at Sitarganj has been approved in December, 2020with a total project cost of Rs. 67.73 crore with a grant-in-aid of Rs. 33.90 crorefrom Government of India.8) Chhattisgarh: Plastic Park at Sarora, Raipur has been approved recently inApril, 2021 with a total project cost of Rs. 42.09 crore with a grant-in-aid of Rs.21.04 crore from Government of India.Further, two more Plastic Parks at Uttar Pradesh (Gorakhpur) and Karnataka(Ganjimutt, Dakshin Kannada) have been given ‘in-principle’ approval.(c) Under the Scheme, the promotion and implementation of the project is done by theSpecial Purpose Vehicle (SPV), established by the State Government. The SPV isresponsible for attracting investments by offering incentives/discounts and throughparticipation in road-shows, campaigns, industry events etc.(d) No Plastic Park in the State of Gujarat has been approved for implementation sofar. However, the State Government had earlier shown interest and sent a preliminaryproposal on 31.03.17. The State Government has again shown interest vide D.O. letterdated 29.06.2021 received from Vice Chairman and MD, Gujarat Industrial DevelopmentCorporation.*******
