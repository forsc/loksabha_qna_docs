                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Use of Historical Buildings




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether historical buildings in the country are being used as offices for defence research and other works by the Government;(b)	if so, the State and district-wise details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is contemplating to develop some portions of Charkhari Fort in Bundelkhand as tourism centre and open it for the people and tourists inorder to create local employment opportunities;(d)	whether there is any provision to provide relaxation in norms regarding area within 2000 meters under  Defence Works Act, 1903; and(e)	if so, the details of such cases wherein relaxation has been given by utilising the above provisions?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)&(b)	Yes, Sir. The list is at Annexure. (c)	Charkhari Fort in Bundelkhand is not under the protection of Archaeological Survey of India. (d)&(e)	No, Sir. However, the matter of Defence Works Act, 1903 is beyond the purview of Ministry of Culture.
