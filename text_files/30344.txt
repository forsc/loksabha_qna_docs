                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1698






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Restructuring Plan




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Railways is heading for a major restructuring plan that could lead to the closure of major establishments, merger of decades old organisations and private participation in running of its schools and hospitals, if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether it is also a fact that a  recommendation envisages winding up of many organisations working under Railways;(c)	if so, whether the coach factories, locomotive manufacturing units, rail wheel units, etc. would be transferred to the proposed Central  Public Sector Enterprises (CPSE); and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b) Reforms and restructuring as per operational needs is an ongoing exercise in Ministry of Railways to keep the organization efficient, responsive and financially viable. Earlier in 2020, the number of Members of Railway Board were reduced from 08 to 04. Recently, Indian Railway Organization for Alternate Fuel (IROAF) has been closed down on 07.09.2021 while a decision has also been taken for closure of Indian Railway Stations Development Corporation Limited (IRSDC) on 18.10.2021.(c) No such decision in this regard has been taken by the Ministry of  Railways. (d) Does not arise.*****
