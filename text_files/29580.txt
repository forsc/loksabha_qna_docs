                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1156






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Funds for MDMS




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the data on fund allocation for the Mid Day Meal Scheme from 2014 to 2021, year/Statewise,separately categorizing Union Government funds and respective State funds;(b) the details of the components of the Mid Day Meal scheme that Union Government and StateGovernment funds are used to finance respectively;(c) whether the Government is aware that Mid-day meal workers in Uttar Pradesh have notreceived wages for over 8 months and if so, reasons for the delay in wage disbursement andwhether the Government is taking any measures to address the issue and if so, the detailsthereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a): The State-wise and year-wise details of funds allocated/released by Central Government andState Governments under Centrally Sponsored Scheme ‘Pradhan Mantri Poshan ShaktiNirman (PM POSHAN)’ earlier known as ‘National Programme for Mid-Day Meal inSchools’ are given at Annexure.(b):The cost of components of cooking cost, honorarium to Cook-cum-Helpers, Construction ofKitchen-cum-stores, repair of 10 year old kitchen-cum-stores, procurement / replacement ofkitchen devices and Innovation / Flexibility is shared between the Centre and States/UTs asper the approved funding pattern of 90:10 for North Eastern Region (NER) States, 2Himalayan States and UT of Jammu & Kashmir, 100% for Union Territories withoutLegislature and 60:40 for other States as well as Union Territories with Legislature (Delhi andPuducherry). The cost of food grains, transportation assistance and management monitoringand evaluation (MME) are fully funded by Central Government.(c): State Government of Uttar Pradesh has informed that ad-hoc grant for the year 2021-22 wasreceived in May, 2021 with the condition that fund is withdrawn through new procedure underPublic Financial Management System (PFMS). There is some delay in implementation of newprocedure under PFMS. Subsequently, the timeline for implementation of new procedureunder PFMS has been relaxed. Thereafter, State Government issued the financial sanction forwithdrawal of funds and distributed the pending honorarium to cook-cum-helpers.*****
