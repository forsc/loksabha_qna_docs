                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Construction of Houses and Roads for Tribals




C.N. Annadurai














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether construction of houses and roads for tribal inhabitants of Jawadhu Hills in Tamil Nadu is yet to take off due to inordinate delay in clearance from Forest Department; (b)	if so, the reasons therefor; and (c)	the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government for fast tracking clearance from Forest Department to ease the sufferings faced by tribal people in the area? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c) As per Government policy, proposals received for consideration under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 (FCA), are dealt expediously. There is no such proposal regarding construction of houses and roadsin forest area for tribal inhabitants of Jawadhu Hills in Tamil Nadu, received in the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change.Central Government, with a view to facilitate development of critical public utility infrastructure which involve forest areas, has accorded General Approval under FCAfor allowing non-forest use of forest land for 15 categories of projects, including construction/widening of roads involving forest area upto 1 ha.Up-gradation of roads, constructed in forest areas prior to 25thOctober 1980, from ''kutcha to pucca'' is permissible without attracting the provisions of FCA. Black topping and bituminous work of forest roads (management works) which have been brought under Pradhan Mantri Gramin SadakYojana is also permitted without attracting the provisions of the FCA.***
