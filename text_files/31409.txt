                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2573






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Guidelines on Television Rating Agencies




 Chinta Anuradha













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has commissioned a review of Guidelines on Television Rating Agencies in the country under Prasar Bharti; and (b) if so, the major findings and recommendations of the said report?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) & (b): Yes Sir. A Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Executive Officer, Prasar Bharati with members from IIT, C-DOT and IIM, to inter alia, review the guidelines on Television Rating Agencies in the country and to make recommendations on way forward for a robust, transparent and accountable rating system in India. The Committee has made recommendations on strengthening corporate governance and bolstering technical oversight of the existing rating agency, open data ecosystem / return path data, curbing unhealthy business practices, promoting innovation, new business models and competitiveness, etc. The recommendations of the Committee also address various other issues referred to them including the recommendations of the Telecom Regulatory Authority of India on the subject.
