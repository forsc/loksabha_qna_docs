                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Zoological Park/Herbal Parks




Mukesh Rajput














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to establish  any zoological  park/herbal parks on vacant forest land near  Farukhabad:(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, whether  any guidelines have been issued for maintenance  of  such lands so as to save them from  encroachments  and to ensure plantations on the same; and (d)	if so, the  details  thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)and (b) There is no proposal to establish any zoological park/herbal parks on the vacant forest land near  Farukhabad under consideration of the Ministry. (c)and (d) There are provisions under the  Indian Forest Act, 1927 (IFA-1927) to prevent  forest land from encroachments. ***
