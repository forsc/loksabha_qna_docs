                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3169






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Development of Roads in Proportionate to theNumber of Vehicles




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it has come to the notice of the Government that the development of roads is not proportionate to the number of vehicles manufactured and marketed in the country;(b)	if so, the details of plan to develop the roads proportionately in view of the requirements;(c) 	the details of vehicles registered in the country during the last five years, State and vehicle type-wise;(d) 	the increase in investment in motor vehicle sector during the last five years; and(e) 	whether the Government proposes to provide alternate system for transport in the country and if so, the details thereof and the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) and (b)	There are specific guidelines and norms for expansion and upgradation of National Highways on the basis of traffic volume.(c) 	The details of vehicles registered in the country as available in VAHAN database during the last five years, State and vehicle type-wise is annexed.(d)	As per information received from Department of Heavy Industries, the investment in Automobile Industry between F.Y. 2013-14 to 2018-19 is to the tune of Rs. 1,07,07,153 Crores.(e)	The Parliament has recently passed the Motor Vehicle (Amendment) Act, 2019. Section 34 of the Motor Vehicle (Amendment) Act, 2019 provides for insertion of section 88A in the Motor Vehicle Act, 1988 which empowers the Central Government to make schemes for national, multimodal and inter-State transport of passengers and goods.
