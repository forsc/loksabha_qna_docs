                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Setting up of Circuit Bench




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government intends to set up circuit bench of Rajasthan High Court in Udaipur;b)	if so, the time by which it is likely to be set up; andc)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c): High Court Benches are established in accordance with the recommendations made by the Jaswant Singh Commission and judgment pronounced by the Apex Court in W.P.(C) No.379 of 2000 and after due consideration of a complete proposal from the State Government and the Chief Justice of the concerned High Court alongwith the consent of the Governor of the concerned State. 	 At present there is no proposal from the State Government of Rajasthan for establishment of a Circuit bench of Rajasthan High Court at Udaipur.
