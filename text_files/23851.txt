                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4887






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Funds for Drinking Water Availability in Bihar




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has allocated/released/utilised any funds for making drinking water available to Bihar during the last three years; (b)	whether the State Government of Bihar has not apprised the Union Government of works undertaken regarding potable water; (c)	the reaction of the Union Government thereto;(d)	the rules relating to submission of progress report  by the State Government to the Union Government regarding providing potable water; (e)	whether the Government of Bihar is complying with the same; (f)	if so, the details thereof; and (g)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	After subsuming erstwhile National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP), since August, 2019, Government of India, in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal, to make provision of assured potable tap water supply to every rural household by 2024. The details of allocation, release and reported expenditure of the Central fund in Bihar under JJM in 2019-20, and under erstwhile NRDWP in 2017-18 and 2018-19,are as under:(Amount Rs. in Crore)Financial year	Central fund	State share expenditure	Opening balance	Allocation	Release	Expenditure	2017-18	87.29	394.53	355.60	216.10	173.262018-19	226.79	476.63	234.84	148.48	211.302019-20	313.16	787.31	417.35	473.33	150.34(b) to (f) 	For regular monitoring and reporting of the physical and financial progress under JJM, an online ‘JJM - Integrated Information Management Systems (IIMS)’ has been set up. The details of the progress reported by the States/ UTs including Bihar is also in public domain and available on the JJM dashboard athttps://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx(g)	Does not arise.***
