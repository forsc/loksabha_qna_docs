                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






CEZs under Sagarmala Scheme




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any steps have been taken by the Government to develop capacity cargo handling in various ports in Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof;(b) the status of Mannar, Poompuhar and VCIC South Coastal Employment Zones (CEZs) proposed under the Sagarmala Scheme;(c) the funds allocated to the proposed Colachel and Sirkazhi ports along with its status thereof; and(d) the details of the Coastal Employment Units(CEUs) and Port-Linked Industrial Clusters (PLICs) under the three CEZs mentioned above?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes Sir, Ministry of Shipping has undertaken master-planning of Major Ports in  State of Tamil Nadu and identified 31 projects (cost Rs. 10,397 Crore) for implementation to increase port capacity by 127.7 MTPA. Ministry of Shipping has also sanctioned financial assistance of Rs. 217.38 Crore for 8 port projects worth Rs. 492 Crore in State of Tamil Nadu under Sagarmala Programme.(b)	 Ministry of Shipping is examing the possibility of development of a CEZ on land available with the Major Ports as a prototype. (c)	Techno-Economic Feasibility Reports (TEFR)  have been prepared for new ports at Sirkazhi and Kanyakumari. Detailed Project Report (DPR) has  been prepared for  for new port at Kanyakumari Kanyakumari and a SPV formed by three Major Ports in State of Tamil Nadu for development of new port at Kanyakumari .The total expenditure incurred on development of new port at Kanyakumari is  Rs 29.80 crore. (d) Under National Perspective plan of the Sagarmala programme, three Coastal Employment  Zones (CEZs) have been identified in the state of Tamil Nadu. Within each CEZ, there are multiple industrial clusters categorized under energy, material and discrete industries. Detail of the CEZs is tabled below: <pre>SNo	Proposed Coastal Emploment Zone	Potential Districts for Industrial clusters	Ports associated with the CEZ	Potential Industries1	Mannar	Kanyakumari, Tirunelveli, Thoothkudi	Tuticorin	Apparel, Refining.2	Poompuhar	Cuddalore, Peralmbalur, Ariyalur, Tiruchirappallu, Thanjavur, Thiruvarur, Nagapattinam	Cuddalore	Leather Processing, Power Refining.3	VCIC South	Thiruvallur, Chennai, Kancheepuram	Chennai, Ennore, Katupalli	Petrochemicals, Electronics, Steel, Shipbuilding.                                                                    </pre>                                               ****
