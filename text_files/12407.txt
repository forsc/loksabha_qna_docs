                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






NPS for Traders




Kotagiri Sridhar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the National Pension Scheme (NPS) for Traders and Self Employed Persons (Pradhan Mantri Laghu Vyapari Maan-dhan Yojana) is a voluntary and contribution based central sector scheme; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the details of the total number of traders and self employed people who have joined this scheme so far, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Finance) (a) to (c) As informed by the Ministry of Labour and Employment, the National Pension Scheme for Traders, Shopkeepers and Self-Employed Persons (Pradhan Mantri Laghu Vyapari Maan-Dhan Scheme), launched on 12.09.2019, is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme with the following salient features:-•	Enrolments through the Common Service Centres (CSCs) and online self enrolment through www.maandhan.in portal;•	50% monthly contribution by the beneficiary with matching contribution by the Central Government.•	Subscribers, after attaining the age of 60 years, are eligible for a monthly minimum assured pension of Rs.3,000/-.Eligibility: The traders in the age group of 18-40 years with an annual turnover, not exceeding Rs.1.5 crore and who are not a member of EPFO/ESIC/NPS (Government funded)/PM-SYM or an income tax payer, can join the scheme. Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) of India is the pension fund manager responsible for managing the pension fund, to act as central recordkeeping agency and responsible for pension payout.As informed by LIC, 30,025 enrolments have taken place under the Scheme, as on 03.02.2020.
