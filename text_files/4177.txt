                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3868






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Commission for Denotified, SemiNomadic and Nomadic Tribes




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has formulated a plan to set up a permanent Commission for Denotified, Semi-Nomadic and Nomadic tribes; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the concrete steps taken by the Government for development of these most marginalized tribes as per the UN sustainable Development Goals?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b):  No Sir.(c): A Board namely Development and Welfare Board for Denotified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Communities (DWBDNCs) has been constituted vide Gazette Notification dated 21.02.2019 for Development and Welfare of Denotified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Communities.  Further, the following schemes are also being implemented for the welfare of DNTs:(i) Dr. Ambedkar Pre-Matric and Post-Matric Scholarship for DNTs. (ii) Nanaji Deshmukh Scheme of Construction of Hostels for DNTs Boys and Girls.(iii) Assistance for Skill Development of Other Backward Classes (OBCs)/ De-Notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes (DNTs)/ Economically Backward Classes (EBCs).****
