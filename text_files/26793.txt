                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Schemes for Economic and Educational Development




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2458 TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.08.2021SCHEMES FOR ECONOMIC AND EDUCATIONAL DEVELOPMENT †2458. SHRI PARBHUBHAI NAGARBHAI VASAVA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of schemes being run by the Government for economical and educational development of people belonging to Scheduled Castes and Other Backward Classes (OBCs), Senior Citizens, victims of substance abuse, transgender persons, notified, nomadic and semi nomadic tribes at present; and (b) the details of the steps proposed to be taken by the Government for this development in future?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a) & (b):  The focus of the Department of Social Justice’s welfare efforts is on the requirements of the poorest households as represented by the socially, educationally and economically marginalized sections of the society including Scheduled Castes (SCs), Other Backward Classes, Senior Citizens, Victims of Alcoholism and Substance Abuse, Transgender Persons, Beggars, Denotified and Nomadic Tribes (DNTs), Economically Backward Classes (EBCs) and Economically Weaker Section (EWS).  The Department is implementing various programmes/schemes for the welfare of these poor and marginalized sections, the details are given at Annexure.***** AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a) & (b) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 2458  for 03.08.2021 S.No 	Name of the Scheme	  1	Pre-Matric Scholarship to the SC Students Studying in classes IX & X	 2	Pre-Matric Scholarship to the Children of those engaged in occupations involving cleaning and prone to health hazards	 3	Post matric Scholarship for SC	 4	Special Central Assistance to Scheduled Caste Sub Plan	 5	Assistance to VOs working for SCs	 6	Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojana for Girls and Boys	 7	Pradhan     Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana	 8	Central Sector Scholarship of Top Class Education forSC Students	 9	Free Coaching Scheme for SC and OBC Students	 10	National FellowshipScheme for SC Students	 11	National Overseas Scholarship	 12	Pre-Matric Scholarship to OBC for Student	 13	Post-Matric scholarship for OBC students	 14	Dr. Ambedkar Post-Matric Scholarship for the EBC students.	 15	Dr. Ambedkar Pre-Matric and Post-Matric Scholarship for DNTs	 16	Construction   of Hostels  for OBC Boys and Girls	 17	Dr. Ambedkar Scheme of  Interest Subsidy on Educational Loans for Overseas studies for OBCs and EBCs	 18	National  Fellowship for OBC Students.	 19	Assistance for Skill Development of  OBCs/DNTs/EBCs	 20	Protection of Civil Rights Act, 1955	 21	National Action Plan for Drug demand Reduction	 22	Welfare for Transgender Persons	 23	Integrated Programme for Rehabilitation for Beggars	 24	 Scheme for Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers	 25	National Action Plan for Senior Citizens (NAPSrC)	 *****
