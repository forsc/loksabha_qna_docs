                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

713






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Loss of Air India




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the loss of Air India is increasing constantly;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to take any steps to reduce or cut the said loss;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said steps are likely to be taken; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Yes Sir.  (b) to (d): The Government is committed to the disinvestment of Air India. In order to effectively prepare Air India for disinvestment, the Government prepared a plan to bring operational and financial efficiency in Air India. This Plan focuses on the financial and operational efficiencies so that substantial increase in revenue or cost saving could be achieved. Also, operational and financial performance parameters and milestones as fixed by the Government are regularly being reviewed.
