                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3465






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Education relating to Nutrition for Children




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has taken note of the innovations done in the introduction ofnutrition education from some organisations like Juvenate Well Being who are takingforward their research in introduction of the nutrition education with the help of Ministryof Women and Child Development in pre-schools/Anganwadis and if so, the detailsthereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to intropduce such education in schools across thecountry from class I and if so, the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government inthis regard;(c) whether the Government proposes some activity based learning for the tiny tots as a longterm plan to get rid of the problem of malnutrition and create health conscious citizens andif so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government proposes to take expertise from organizations like Juvenate WellBeing for preparing curriculum and implementing the same and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): Content related to Health and Nutrition has been included in the textbooks ofEnvironmental Education at the primary stage. Topics related to nutrition have been incorporatedin Science textbooks developed by NCERT wherever possible where sources of food, nutrientsin food, deficiency of nutrients, balanced diet, nutritional needs of adolescents, etc. have beendiscussed.Under Mid Day Meal scheme all the States/UTs are encouraged to set up SchoolNutrition (Kitchen) Garden to help in addressing malnutrition and micro nutrient deficienciesamong children, enhance the knowledge of children regarding nutritional aspects and givechildren first-hand experience with nature and gardening.******
