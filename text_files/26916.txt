                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2621






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Land Acquisition to lay New Lines




Ramakant Bhargava














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether work related to land acquisition to lay new lines from Manglia (Indore) to Budhani has been launched by the Railway Development Corporation; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the time by which the action regarding acquisition of land is likely to be started by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b): Indore-Jabalpur (342 km) new line project was included in the Budget 2016-17 subject to requisite approvals from the Government. However, this work was finally sanctioned as Indore (Mangaliyagaon)-Budni (205.50 Km) new line project in September, 2018 at a cost of `3261.82 crore. Project has been entrusted to Rail Vikas Nigam Limited (RVNL) for execution. (c):  Project involves acquisition of 1502.84 hectare land.  Land acquisition proceedings have been taken up.   *****
