                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Vacancies in ONGC




Behanan Benny













                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number and the details of vacancies in the Oil and Natural Gas Corporation(ONGC) including the dates since when such vacancies occurred/became active;(b) whether the posts of Chairman and Managing Director and Director (Offshore)have been duly filled up;(c) if so, the details thereof including the dates of such appointments and reasons forthe delay, if any, in such appointments; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha StarredQuestion No. 284 asked by SHRI BENNY BEHANAN and SHRI ANTOANTONY to be answered on 09th August, 2021.(a) In ONGC, Board level posts of Chairman and Managing Director (CMD) fellvacant with effect from 01.04.2021and Director (offshore) with effect from01.05.2021. 289 executive level posts and 329 non executive level posts arevacant at present. Details are given at annexure 1 & 2.(b) to (d) Public Enterprises Selection Board (PESB) conducted selectionmeeting for the post of CMD, ONGC on 04.06.2021 and did notrecommended any candidate keeping in view strategic importance and visionfor the company and its future. For the post of Director (offshore), theincumbent has been recommended by PESB and appointment will be madeafter approval of Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC).*****
