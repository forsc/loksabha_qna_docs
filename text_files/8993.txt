                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Recruitment through Outsourcing




Kaushalendra Kumar













                                                    Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government proposes to retrench employees working in the Railways and if so, the reasons therefor;(b) whether this retrenchment is limited to only Group-D and Group-C employees or it applies to the Group-A and Group-B employees also;(c)  the total number of employees working in the Railways as on date, Group-wise along with the number of employees belonging to OBC, SC, ST and General category;(d) the Group-wise number of posts vacant in the Railways at present and the number of posts likely to be filled by the year 2020, group and category-wise; and(e) whether employees have also been recruited/appointed through outsourcing in Railways and if so, the details thereof along with the total number of employees recruited/appointed through outsourcing as on date including the last date of their contract, Group-wise?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (e):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
