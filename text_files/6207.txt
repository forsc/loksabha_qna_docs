                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Doordarshan Kendra/All India Radio Kendra




Ashok Kumar Rawat













                                                    Annpurna Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the districts, especially the backward and village areas of the country, where there is no Doordarshan Kendra/All India Radio Kendra at present; (b) 	the States from which the Government had received request to set up Doordarshan Kendra/All India Radio Kendra during the last three years till date; (c) 	the action taken/likely to be taken by the Government in this regard; (d) 	the time by which it is proposed to link all the districts in the country with Doordarshan/All India Radio network; and (e) 	whether the Government proposes to set up an Akashvani Kendra in Kodarma district of Jharkhand, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a):	Prasar Bharati has informed that there are 66 Doordarshan Kendras (Studio Centres), and 479 broadcasting stations (includes 228 full-fledged studios and 251 relay centres) across the country. The list of AIR setups and DD setups across the country are available at website of Prasar Bharati at the link http://prasarbharati.gov.in/pbcircular/Administration_660472.pdf  and http://prasarbharati.gov.in/pbcircular/Administration_210200. pdf respectively.(b) to (d): During last three years requests for setting up Doordarshan Kendra (Studio Centre) at 21 locations in 12 states of the country have been received, list enclosed as Annexure-I. At present all channels of Doordarshan Network are available throughout the country via its free-to-air DD Direct-to-Home. Further, 14 requests for establishing of AIR Setups have been received from various state governments, out of which AIR setups have been made functional at 6 places. Details alongwith action taken are given in Annexure-II(e): Presently, there is no approved plan to set up Akashvani Stations in Kodarma district of Jharkhand.*****
