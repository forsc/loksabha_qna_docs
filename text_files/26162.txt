                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1922






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Medical Colleges in Nagaland and Haryana




Tokheho Yepthomi













                                                     Dharambir Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)	the time by which the medical college in Nagaland expected to be completed and become functional;(b)	whether  the  Government  has  any  proposal  to establish  AIIMS  hospital  in  Nagaland  and  if  so,  the details thereof;(c)	whether  the  Government  proposes  to  set  up medical  college  in  Central  University  Pali  Jat, Mahendragarh,  Haryana  and  if  so,  the  time  duration proposed for completion of its work; and(d)	the budget allocated to Haryana for setting up of medical colleges during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) The Ministry of Health & Family Welfare administers a Centrally Sponsored Scheme namely, ‘Establishment of new medical colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals’ under which two medical colleges have been approved in Nagaland.As per the scheme guidelines, the State Government is the implementing agency and the planning, execution and commissioning of the projects is to be done by the State Government. The details of these colleges are as below:-Rs. in crore Phase of Scheme	District	Total approved cost	Central Share (90%)	Total amount released (till date)Phase-I	Naga Hospital (Kohima)	189	170.10	170.10Phase-III	Mon	325	292.50	155The Central Government regularly follows up with the State Government for expediting these works.(b)  There is no proposal under consideration of the Central Government to establish AIIMS in Nagaland. (c)  & (d) Under Phase-I of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme– ‘Establishment of new medical colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals’, one medical college was approved in Haryana at Bhiwani district. The details of funds approved and released for the medical college are as below:-Rs. in crore District	Total approved cost	Central Share (60%)	Amount releasedBhiwani	189	113.40	113.40Further, under Phase-III of this Scheme, all States/UTs were requested to submit proposals along with Detailed Project Report (DPR) for consideration by the Ministry, However,  no proposal was received from the State Government of Haryana for establishment of a medical college at Mahendragarh, Haryana. Under Phase-III of the Scheme, all the approved 75 medical colleges have already been allocated.
