                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Redevelopment of CSMT Railway Station




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat













                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has proposed to redevelop the iconic Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT) Railway Station in South Mumbai;(b) if so, the present status of its redevelopment; (c) the details of the proposed redevelopment plan; and(d) the time schedule fixed for its redevelopment?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)&(b) Yes, Sir. Request for Qualification (RFQ) process for redevelopment of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT) station has been completed.(c) The Redevelopment of CSMT station aims to provide improved amenities, adequate spaces for movements/waiting and to retain/maintain the historic character of this station.(d) After selection of developer, the project is likely to be completed in 4 years.******
