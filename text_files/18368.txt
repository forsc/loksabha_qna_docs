                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2167






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Blue Flag Certification




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of beaches that have been recommended for ‘Blue Flag’ Certification, State/UTs;(b)	the status of recommendations, including the estimated timeline; (c)	the criteria adopted by the Government for making such recommendations; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to provide increased financial assistance to beaches thus recommended, if so, the details thereof; and (e)	whether the government is taking measures to develop more beaches according to ‘Blue Flag’ standards, and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c)  ‘Blue Flag’ certification is accorded by an international agency “Foundation for Environment Education” (FEE) Denmark, based on 33 stringent criteria under four major heads i.e. (i) Environmental Education and Information, (ii) bathing water Quality, (iii) Environment Management and Conservation and (iv) safety and services in the beaches. The National Jury has recommended the following eight beaches for Blue Flag Certification:Sr. No.	Beach name	State/UT1	Shivrajpur	Gujarat2	Ghoghla	Diu, (Daman & Diu)3	Kasarkod	Karnataka4	Padubidri	Karnataka5	Kappad	Kerala6	Rushikonda	Andhra Pradesh7	Golden	Odisha8	Radhanagar	Andaman & NicobarThe appraisal for the Blue Flag Certification by FEE Denmark is scheduled in October, 2020.(d) to (e) The Ministry has allocated funds for sustainable development of these beaches, infrastructure facilities and fulfillment of gaps for the compliance to the 33 criteria of FEE, Denmark, including operation and maintenance of the beach facilities and services till March 2021. It is further envisaged to develop about 100 such beaches in the next 5 years, under the Beach Environment and Aesthetics Management Services (BEAMS) programme of the Ministry.***
