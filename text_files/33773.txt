                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1286






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Cases Recommended to CBI




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and number of cases recommended to CBI for investigation by Rajasthan Government during the last ten years, case-wise; (b) 	the details and number of cases among the above said cases declined for investigation by CBI; and (c) 	the current status of the above-mentioned cases along with the details of cases on which investigation have been started by CBI?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b):  Details of references recommended by the State Government of Rajasthan to CBI for investigation, in terms of Delhi Special Police Establishment (DSPE) Act, 1946, in last 10 years are as following:References received	References in which cases registered 	No. of Registered Cases12	10	37(c):   The status of the aforesaid 37 cases at various stages of investigation/trial is as follows: -                   Investigation	     –  02                   Closure                      –  07                   Trial Stage                 –  27                   Acquittal                   –   01 *****
