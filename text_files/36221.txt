                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3617






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Digital Mechanism to Verify Water Connections




Gautam Gambhir














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is considering any proposal to set up a digital mechanism for verification and funding of water connections; and(b)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                       MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)& (b): Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation 2.0 (AMRUT 2.0) has been launched on 1st October 2021 with an aim to provide universal coverage of household water tap connections in all statutory towns of the country and universal coverage of sewerage and septage management in 500 AMRUT cities.A digital platform by the name of AMRUTAM app has been developed to verify services of water and sewer connections provided outside of AMRUT scheme after 1st November 2021. After States/ Union Territories (UTs) report the connections, Ministry verifies the samples through gig model. A gig consisting of sample connections is created for college students registered on the digital platform who will verify the connections details submitted by the State/UTs.  This is being tested on pilot mode in Odisha and Tamil Nadu.                                  *****
