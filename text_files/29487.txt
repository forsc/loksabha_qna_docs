                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






List of Monuments Included in UNESCO&amp;#8216;S World Heritage List




Vijayakumar (alias) Vijay Vasanth













                                                    Su Thirunavukkarasar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of monuments in the country which have been included in the UNESCO World Heritage List, State-wise;(b)	whether the Harappan City, Gujarat and Ramappa Temple, Warangal have recently been conferred the tag of UNESCO World Heritage List;(c)	if so, the details thereof and the extent to which it will help to augment footfall of visitors;(d)	whether the Government has taken any steps to send nominations for inclusion of more monuments in the UNESCO list; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPOMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	At present, India has 40 World Heritage properties. Out of which 32 are cultural, 07 natural and 01 mixed property. List of World Heritage properties including monuments is placed at Annexure-A.(b) & (c) 	Yes, Sir. In the year 2021, Dholavira: A Harappan city, Gujarat and Kakatiya Rudrashwara (Ramappa) Temple have been inscribed on World Heritage list by the World Heritage Committee. Inscription of any site brings laurels to the country and to the site, which, in turn, helps in increasing footfall of visitors and other economic activities.(d) & (e)	Yes, Sir. Inclusion of monument/sites properties in the UNESCO list is a continuous process. As per Operational Guidelines, for implementation of World Heritage Convention each state party can send only one nomination either cultural / natural or mixed. For the period 2021-22, Santiniketan, India has been submitted to World Heritage Centre for inscription process.***** ANNEXURE-AANNEXURE REFFERED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1243 FOR DATED 06.12.2021WORLD HERITAGE PROPERTIES IN INDIA (Total 40)A.
