                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

129






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Land Monetisation Policy




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry/Army’s Land Monetisation Policy has not been implemented in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether there is any land revenue loss due to non-implementation of the said Land Monetisation Policy and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the land of Sabeya Airbase in Gopalganj, Bihar is lying unused for many years and if so, the reasons for non-utilization of this land; and(e) whether the Government is planning to use this land of Sabeya Airbase for defence purposes, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF DEFENCE                                    (SHRI RAJNATH SINGH) (a) to (e):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
