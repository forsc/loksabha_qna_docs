                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Industrial Training Institutes




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Industrial Training Institutes set up under the Skill Development Programme in various States including Maharashtra;(b)	whether all these centres have started functioning;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and;(d)	if not, the reasons therefor along with the number of youths enrolled in these training centers, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d)	There are 15697 Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) functioning in the country, affiliated with National Council of Vocational Training. The State/UT wise details are attached at Annexure I.Annexure-IAnnexure referred to in reply of part (a) to (d) of LSUQ No.131 on Industrial Training Institutes raised by SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL for answer on 18.11.2019 STATE/UT WISE DETAILSNumber of ITIs State/UT wise as on 31st October 2019Sl.
