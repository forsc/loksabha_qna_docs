Status of Sagarmala Programme in Tamil Nadu




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The status of Sagarmala Programme in the state of Tamil Nadu;(b)	whether the Government has identified projects that are to be implemented as part of Sagarmala Programme; and(c)	If so, the details thereof including public consultations held for each project under Sagarmala Programme in Tamil Nadu’s Coastal Districts?






ANSWER




                                                        (a), (b) & (c)    Under Sagarmala Programme, a total of 86 projects (cost: Rs.106,480 Crores) have been identified in the state of Tamil Nadu. Of these, 36 projects (cost: Rs. 33,545 Crores) have been completed and 50 projects (cost: Rs. 72,935 Crores) are under various stages of  implementation and development.        The project details of the identified projects in the state of Tamil Nadu is annexed.
