                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






PM-FME Scheme




Debasree Chaudhuri














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs), Self Help Groups (SGHs) and Producers Cooperatives benefitted under the Prime Minister Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises Scheme (PM-FME), State-wise;  (b)	the number of existing micro food processing enterprises which have been provided with financial, technical and business support for upgradation under the scheme along with the details regarding the support provided, State-wise;(c)	the capacity building measures undertaken till now to impart skill training, technical knowledge on food safety, standards and hygiene and to improve quality under the scheme; and(d)	the amount of allotted funds utilized under the scheme, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a):	The State-wise number of applications received from Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs), Self Help Groups (SHGs) and Producers Cooperatives under the Prime Minister Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises Scheme (PM-FME) are at Annexure-I. (b):	The State-wise number of existing micro food processing enterprises which have been provided with financial support for the upgradation under the scheme are at Annexure-II. (c):	Under capacity building component of the PMFME Scheme, 760 training modules for various food products have been prepared. Further, 455 Master Trainers (MTs) and 667 District Level Trainers (DLTs) and 741 District Resource Persons (DRPs) have been trained on EDP & various food domains including food safety, standards and hygiene. 1225 Beneficiaries in 13 States/UTs have been trained on EDP and various food products including food safety, standards, and hygiene.  (d):	The State wise details of amount released as Centre Share are at Annexure-III.*****
