                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3377






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Ayush Wellness Centres




P. Ravindhranath














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government provides financial assistance to establish Ayush Wellness Centres in the country including the State of Tamil Nadu under National Ayush Mission; (b) if so, the details thereof and the fund allocated by the Government for this purpose during the last five years to Government of Tamil Nadu under this mission; and (c) the steps taken by the Government to increase the number of Ayush Wellness Centres?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c):      Public Health is a State subject. Setting up of AYUSH Wellness Centres comes under purview of State/UT Governments. However, Central Government is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in the country including Tamil Nadu under which there is a provision for financial assistance to States/UTs for setting up of AYUSH Wellness Centres including Yoga & Naturopathy. During the last five years Rs. 1370.21 lakhs was approved under NAM for setting up of 104 AYUSH Wellness Centres including Yoga & Naturopathy in the State of Tamil Nadu.…………………..
