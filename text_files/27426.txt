                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Medicinal Plants




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that the Government is promoting Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homeopathy in the country; and(b) whether the Government has any estimate of the number of farmers involved in cultivation of medicinal plants and have received the benefits under the AYUSH scheme and if so, the details thereof during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a): Yes. (b): Yes, Sir, the Ministry of Ayush, Government of India had implemented Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National Ayush Mission (NAM) since 2015-16 to 2020-21 through State Implementing Agency. Under the Medicinal Plants Component of the National Ayush Mission scheme, the Ministry of Ayush had supported subsidy@30%, 50% and 75% of the cost of cultivation per hectare for 140 prioritized medicinal plants species on farmer’s land.	The Ministry of Ayush had supported 22,003 number of farmers involved in cultivation of medicinal plants during the financial year 2018-19 to 2020-21 and the detail is given in Annexure – I. The detail of subsidy given by the Ministry of Ayush to the farmers through the State Implementing Agencies for cultivation of medicinal plants under the National Ayush Mission Scheme is given in Annexure – II.
