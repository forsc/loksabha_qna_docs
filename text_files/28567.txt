                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

798






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Guidelins for assisting MPs at Airports




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has issued any guideline to private airlines operating within India for assisting Members of Parliament  (MPs) during air travel;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Airline is responsible to provide assistance to MPs with regard to services such as issue of boarding passes, provision of a handler to assist with baggages and to receive/drop off the elected representatives at entrance/exit of airport;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether a Member of Parliament has the right to choose a seat of his preference and if so, the details thereof; and (f) whether there is any procedure in place for Members of Parliament (MPs) in case of non-compliance/rude behavior from airline staff and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b) Yes Sir. Guidelines have been issued for extending protocol/courtesy/support to Members of Parliament at airports by all airlines including Air India, Airport operators and BCAS.(c) & (d) Airlines have been advised  to facilitate Hon''ble MPs in completion of their check-in formalities at the airport.(e) As per the extant guidelines, airlines have been advised that seats of their choice to be allotted to MPs subject to availability.(f) If complaints from MPs on rude behaviour by airline staffs are received, same are examined, reports are sought from the concerned airlines and appropriate actions are initiated.
