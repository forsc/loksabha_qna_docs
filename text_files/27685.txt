                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Fuel Subsidy




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and total amount of fuel subsidy given by the Union Governmentduring the last ten financial years, year-wise;(b) the expected amount of fuel subsidy during the current financial year;(c) whether the subsidy on LPG has completely been withdrawn and if so, the detailsthereof; and(d) if not, the details of subsidy on LPG at present?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a): The total amount of fuel subsidy paid by Government since F.Y. 2011-12 is Rs. 7,03,525cores.(b): The Budget Estimates for F.Y. 2021-22 for LPG & Natural Gas (NG) subsidy is Rs.12,995 crores.(c) & (d): The prices of petroleum products in the country are linked to the price of respectiveproducts in the international market. The Government continues to modulate the effectiveprice to consumer for Subsidized Domestic LPG. The prices of non-subsidized DomesticLPG are however determined by the OMCs in line with changes in the international markets.The subsidy on the product increases/decreases with the increase/decrease in the productprice in international market and decision of Government on subsidy.x-x-x-x-x
