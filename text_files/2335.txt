                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Grants under Sanitation Schemes




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total amount of grants provided to BPL and APL families under sanitation schemes in rural areas during each of the last three years and the current year; and(b) the details of such grants, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	Total amount of Central share expenditure on construction of Individual household latrines (IHHLs) for BPL and Identified APL households which are eligible for incentive, under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) during the last three years and the current year is as under:-Sl.No.	Financial Year	Central share expenditure on IHHLs (Rs. in crore)1	2016-17	9774.082.	2017-18	11697.803.	2018-19	12654.754.	2019-20	4887.15                           (As on 1.7.2019)(b) 	State/UT-wise details are given at Annexure.
