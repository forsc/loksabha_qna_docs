                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1856






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Demands for Yoga Research Institutes




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is planning to establish more Yoga Research Institutes in different parts of the country, if so, the details thereof; and (b) the number of Government Yoga Research Institutes presently functioning in the country, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):    Central Council for Research in Yoga & Naturopathy (CCRYN), New Delhi, an autonomous body of Ministry of AYUSH, is the apex research organisation for Yoga and Naturopathy. CCRYN has plans of setting up of two Post Graduate institutes of Yoga & Naturopathy Education and Research each at Jhajjar, Haryana and Nagamangla, Karnataka.(b):      CCRYN has established a Central Research Institute (CRI) of Yoga & Naturoapthy at, Rohini, Delhi where Yoga & Naturopathy treatments are being provided both under IPD (20 bedded) and OPD for the benefit of common people.In addition CCRYN also runs 15 Wellness Centres / OPDs in various Govt. hospitals / institutions in different parts of the country, data from where go to CCRYN for research. The details of Wellness Centres/ OPDs run by CCRYN are given in Annexure.
