                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2023






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






NSSIC Scheme




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of ongoing schemes of National Small Scale Industries Corporation for North Eastern States and other States including Assam and Haryana;(b)	the assistance provided to North Eastern States and other states including Assam and Haryana by the National Small Scale Industries Corporation under the Raw Material Assistance Scheme during the last three years; and(c)	whether the Government has also received complaints of irregularities in this regard; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a)&(b):   The National Small Industries Corporation Limited (NSIC), a PSU under the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, provides integrated support services to MSMEs throughout the country including North Eastern States under marketing, technology, finance and other support services. The assistance provided by NSIC under its various programmes in North Eastern States and other States including Assam and Haryana during last three years is given at Annexure.(c)&(d):  Action in respect irregularities, if any, is taken by NSIC as per rules on a continuing basis as and when any such instance is brought to their notice. Further, an online Portal “champions.gov.in” has been launched on 01.06.2020. This covers many aspects of e-governance including grievance redressal and handholding of MSMEs. Through the portal, total 221 grievances relating to NSIC have been replied upto 07.12.2021.
