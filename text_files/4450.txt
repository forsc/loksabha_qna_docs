                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3974






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Countries Tours by Prime Minister




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	The number of countries visited by the Hon’ble Prime Minister of India during the last five years;(b) 	The number of MoUs signed by India and other countries during the said visits by PM during the above period, year-wise; and (c) 	The amount of money spent by the Central Government on the official tours of the PM during the last ten years, year-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (c)	Information on the number of countries visited by Hon’ble Prime Minister during the last ten years, MoUs signed, and amount of money spent on official tours (Chartered flights and hotline) is placed at Annexure-I.
