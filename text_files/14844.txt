                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

309






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






ESI Hospital in Keonjhar




Chandrani Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the total number of beds in the Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) hospital situated in Joda of Keonjhar district, Odisha;(b)the sanctioned strength and also the actual strength of staff in the said hospital; (c)the total amount provided for infrastructure development of the said ESI hospital during the last three financial years and the current year; and (d)the total number of labourers who got treatment in the said hospital during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (d):	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*                                                 ****** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 309 FOR. 16.03.2020 BY MS. CHANDRANI MURMU REGARDING ESI HOSPITAL IN KEONJHAR.(a) to (d):	No, Sir. There is no ESI Hospital in Joda of Keonjhar district of Odisha.*****
