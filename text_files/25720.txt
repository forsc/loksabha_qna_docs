                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1440






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Telecom Towers in the Villages




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of villages in the rural areas of the country where telecommunication towers have been set up during each of the last two years and the current year, State/UT-wise and telecom company-wise; and (b) 	the number of such towers targeted to be set up in the rural areas in the next financial year, State/UTwise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	Sir, the total number of villages in the rural areas of the country where telecommunication towers were installed during each of the last two years and the current year, State/UT-wise and telecom company-wise is attached as Annexure-1.(b)	Telecom companies explore opportunities to expand their network coverage by installing new towers. The number of mobile towers in India has increased from 4,15,724 (as on 31-12-2015) to 6,63,411 (in 22-07-2021). Expansion of mobile towers is dynamic in nature and undertaken by the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) and Infrastructure Providers Category-1 (IP-1) based on their CAPEX capacity, techno-commercial assessment and other parameters. Telecom Companies for most of the States/ UTs have expressed their inability to provide their future plan. However, for some of the States/ UTs they have provided number of towers proposed to be installed in rural areas in the next financial year which is attached as Annexure-2.
