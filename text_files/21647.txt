                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2963






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






CGHS facilities in Darbhanga




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether a CGHS wellness centre has been set up in Darbhanga Parliamentary Constituency and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the CGHS wellness centre at Darbhanga does not have a hospital with modern facilities and a diagnostic centre;(c) if so, whether the Government proposes to include such a hospital and diagnostic centre under CGHS wellness centre Darbhanga; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): Yes. CGHS has taken over a Postal Dispensary in Darbhanga on 01.04.2019 on as-is where-is basis.  (b): CGHS wellness centre at Darbhanga does not have a hospital and a diagnostic centre. CGHS Wellness Centers are set up for providing primary healthcare to beneficiaries. (c) & (d): Presently, there is no such proposal under consideration. However, CGHS beneficiaries can avail secondary and tertiary treatment/ hospitalization at Government Hospitals/ CGHS empanelled private hospitals anywhere in the country.
