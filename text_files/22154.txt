                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Export of Sugar




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that about 25 lakh tonnes of sugar have been contracted for exports so far during the 2020-2021 sugar season and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether it is also true that over 7 lakh tonnes of sugar had been physically exported between 1.10.2020 and 31.1.2021 and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether this includes exports against the 2019-2020 sugar season’s Maximum Admissible Export Quota which was extended till the end of December, 2020; and (d) 	if so the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	As per information received from industry/trade sources, against the export target of 60 Lakh Metric Tonne (LMT) for export during Sugar Season 2020-21, a quantity of about 39 LMT has been contracted between the period from 01.01.2021 to 10.03.2021, out of which a quantity of about 14 LMT has been exported. (b) to (d): 	As per information received from sugar mills  through on-line portal as well as from trade sources, a quantity of about 7.97 LMT  has been exported between the period from 01.10.2020 to 31.01.2021; out of which 2.97 LMT was exported between 01.10.2020 to 31.12.2020 for export against Maximum Admissible Export Quota (MAEQ) of sugar season 2019-20. Further, a quantity of about 5 LMT has been exported for export against Maximum Admissible Export Quota (MAEQ) of sugar season 2020-21 till 31.01.2021.****
