                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Treatment of Non-COVID-19 Patients during Pandemic




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of Non-Corona patients who got treatment in All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) and Central Hospitals after 24th March, 2020, State-wise and hospital-wise;(b) whether it is a fact that there have been more deaths due to other diseases rather than Corona on account of poor health facilities during the Corona pandemic in the country, if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(c) whether the percentage expenditure of Gross Domestic Product (GDP) being made on health is adquate keeping in view the increased population and burden of diseases in the country and if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(d) whether the Government has taken note of the fact that even serious patients have been discharged from hospitals without treatment due to pandemic situation in the country and are forced to wander on roads, if so, the details thereof and corrective steps taken in this regard; and(e) whether it is a fact that the arrangement for the treatment of patients suffering from diseases other than Corona has been stopped during the lockdown period in Central Hospitals due to which a large number of people became victim of untimely death?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	The patients who were not suffering from Corona but still have been treated in AIIMS and Central Government run hospitals in IPD as well as in OPD after 24th March, 2020 in the country is given in Annexure.(b)	No.(c)	As per the National Health Accounts (NHA) Estimates 2016-17 (latest available), public expenditure on health  as a percentage of GDP is 1.2%. (d)	No such patient who was already admitted in AIIMS or Central Government run hospitals whether serious/critical or non-serious was discharged from the hospitals without adequate treatment, due to pandemic situation in the country.(e)	No.
