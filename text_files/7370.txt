                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

953






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Wildlife Sanctuaries




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total numbers of wildlife sanctuaries, national parks, biological parks, bio-diversity parks in the country, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Karnataka;(b)	whether the Government is considering for the expansion/growth of these wildlife sanctuaries, national parks, biological parks, bio-diversity parks, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the amount of funds allocated by the Government for the said sanctuaries/parks during the last five years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	As per the available information, there are 104 National Parks, 551 Wildlife Sanctuaries and 143 recognised Zoological Parks in the country including Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Karnataka.  State/UT-wise list of National Parks, Wildlife Sanctuaries and recognized Zoological Parks is given in the Annexure-I. However the number of Biodiversity Parks is not compiled in the Ministry. (b)	Declaration of National Parks, Wildlife Sanctuaries and Zoological Parks falls under the jurisdiction of State Government. However, any alteration in the boundaries of National Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries requires prior approval of National Board for Wildlife. (c)	Details of funds released to National Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries under the Centrally Sponsored scheme of ‘Development of Wildlife Habitats’ and to recognized Zoological Parks by the Central Zoo Authority during the  last five years is given in the Annexure-II & III. ****
