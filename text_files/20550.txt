                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Promotion of Youth Affairs




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the efforts made by the Union Government to promote the youth affairs and sports in Andhra Pradesh during the last two years, (b) whether the Union Government has received any proposal from the Government of Andhra Pradesh in this regard, (c) if so, the details thereof, (d) the action taken thereon, and (e) the amount sanctioned to Andhra Pradesh against each proposal during each of the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (e):	The Department of Youth Affairs, Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports undertakes various Youth Centric activities all over the country, including State of Andhra Pradesh through its organizations viz. (i) National Service Scheme (NSS) (ii) Nehrua Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) & (iii) Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD). The details are at Annexure-I:The Department of Sports, Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports has also provided financial assistance to Andhra Pradesh under Khelo India Scheme. The details are at Annexure-II & III.*****
