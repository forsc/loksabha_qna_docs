                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

219






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






MoU between NBCFDC and Apollo Medicals




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 219TO BE ANSWERED ON 02.02.2021MoU BETWEEN NBCFDC AND APOLLO MEDICALS †219. SHRI PARBATBHAI SAVABHAI PATEL: SHRI NARANBHAI KACHHADIYA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the National Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) and Apollo Medicals have signed a Memorandum of Understanding(MoU) to provide vaccination training to nurses and pharmacists belonging to Other Backward Classes and Economically Weaker Sections utilizing the funding of NBCFDC; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the number of persons targeted to be trained during the next few months under this MoU?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b):   Yes, Sir.  The National Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) has signed an MOU with M/s. Apollo Medskills Pvt Ltd. for providing COVID Vaccination Administration training programme (as per guidelines of Ministry of Health and Family Welfare) to Nurses, Medical & Nursing students and Pharmacist belonging to the target group of National Backward Classes Finance and Development (NBCFDC) on a co-funding basis. The programme has been sanctioned with the aim of Up-Skilling the competency level of the health care-workers as well contributing to the national programme of Vaccination under progress.(c) The Corporation has sanctioned training programme for 1000 trainees in the state of Bihar, Delhi, Telangana, Tamil Nadu and Uttar Pradesh, which are expected to be commenced in due course.****
