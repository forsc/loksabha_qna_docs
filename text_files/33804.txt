                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Yarn Prices




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government held discussions with representatives of the textiles and clothing sector to discuss the issue of rising raw cotton and yarn prices; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has urged the textile mills to consider the present situation and take the required steps, so that yarn prices could be brought under control and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether it is a fact that the garment exporters in Tiruppur were in strike for two days demanding measures to control the prices of cotton and yarn; and (e) if so, the steps taken in this regard?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (e):	Ministry is constantly engaged with various stakeholders across the entire cotton value chain in respect of the prices of cotton and cotton yarn to harmonize their interest. As per media report, the garment exporters in Tirupur were on strike for two days. Prices are affected by market forces of demand and supply. In addition, cotton being an internationally traded commodity, its price is also affected by the price in the international market. Prices of cotton yarn also moves in tandem with the prices of raw cotton.***
