                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1553






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Developmental Works under MGNREGS




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the Kuccha and pucca developmental works done under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) during the last five years;(b) 	the details of their costs and locations along with details thereof with regard to the State of Maharashtra, district-wise;(c) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to provide relief to the farmers under the above scheme by making amendments and doing works related to construction of contour-dams and digging pits in the fields and if so, the district-wise details of such works and the number of beneficiaries thereof in the State of Maharashtra; and(d) 	whether the Government proposes to amend the scheme to include the use of machinery and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)  There are 262 permissible works under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA). Of this, 182 works are related to Natural Resource Management out of which 85 are water related works and out of total works, 164 works are related to agriculture and allied activities. These works are derived from Schedule I of the Act. In the Schedule I, the works have been classified under major categories as under : As per Schedule I, Section 4(3), Para 4(1),I.	Category: A:  Public works relating to Natural Resource ManagementII.	Category: B:  Community assets or Individual assets for vulnerable sections (only for households in Paragraph 5) III.	Category: C:  Common Infrastructure (Including for NRLM) Compliant Self Help GroupsIV.	Category: D:  Rural Infrastructure In the Management information System (MIS) of Mahatma Gandhi NREGS, provision has been made to capture the details of works in 17 categories as under: S. No.	Name of the Categories1	Rural Infrastructure2	Bharat Nirman Sewa Kendra3	Coastal Areas4	Drought Proofing5	Fisheries6	Flood Control and Protection7	Food Grain8	Land Development9	Micro Irrigation Works10	Other Works11	Play Ground12	Renovation of traditional water bodies13	Rural Connectivity14	Rural Drinking Water15	Rural Sanitation16	Water Conservation and Water Harvesting17	Works on Individuals Land (Category IV) According to the above 17 categories, the category wise details of completed and ongoing works under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during last five financial years is given at     Annexure-I  (b) District wise details of completed and ongoing works along with expenditure incurred under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in the State of Maharashtra during last five financial years is given at Annexure -II. (c)  Out of 262 permissible works under the Scheme, 164 works are related to agriculture and agriculture allied activities. As per Schedule I, Section 4(3), para 2, “ The District Programme Coordinator shall ensure that at least 60% of the works to be taken up in a district in terms of cost shall be for creation of productive assets directly linked to agriculture and allied activities through development of land, water and trees”.  As per Schedule I of the Act, contour trenches and contour bunds are permissible activities. There are 1924 beneficiaries covered under the work of Construction of earthen contour bund and construction of staggered trench work in the State of Maharashtra since inception. The district wise details of Construction of earthen contour bund and construction of staggered trench work in the individual land taken up in the  State of Maharashtra under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS since inception is at Annexure-III. (d) The provision of use of machinery has been made for the activities which cannot be carried out by manual labour, where use of machine may become essential for maintaining the quality and durability of work to be completed under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. No labour displacing machine has been allowed for execution of work under the Scheme.
