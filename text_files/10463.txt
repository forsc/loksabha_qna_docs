                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3905






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






PMGDISHA




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to  state: (a)	the amount of  budget  sanctioned  for the Pradhan Mantri  Gramin  Digital 	 Saksharta Abhiyaan in each of the last two financial years, State / UT-wise 	including Andhara Pradesh; and(b)	the total number of people trained under this scheme during the last three 	years, State/UT-wise






ANSWER




                                                        ) and (b): The budget sanctioned for the Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan (PMGDISHA) Scheme during the FY 2017-18 and FY 2018-19  was Rs. 100  Crore and  Rs. 438 Crore respectively, out of the total budget outlay of Rs. 2351.38 Crore for the Scheme.As the above Scheme is funded as a Central Sector Scheme, hence, 	funds are not allocated to States and UTs. The State/UT-wise details of overall fund requirements based on their earmarked targets are given in the Annexure.
