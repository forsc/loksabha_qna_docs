                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4348






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Fuel for BS-VI Vehicles




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Bharat Stage-VI vehicles need different composition of petrol and diesel, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the refineries in India have been re-configured  to make petrol and diesel for Bharat Stage-VI and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) whether any changes have been made to petrol and diesel to reduce the environmental pollution?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROELUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Bharat Stage VI (BS-VI) vehicles require petrol and diesel with sulphur content not exceeding 10 parts per million (ppm). (b) Refineries have undertaken projects to produce and supply BS-VI compliant petrol and diesel as per mandated date for supply of BS-VI fuels across the country.(c) The sulphur content of both petrol and diesel have been brought down from 50 ppm in BS-IV to 10 ppm max in BS-VI, enabling the introduction of advanced emission control technologies, including Diesel Particulate Filters (DPF) to reduce Particulate Matter (PM) and Selective Catalyst Reduction (SCR) systems for reduction in Nitrogen Oxides (NOx) emissions in BS-VI vehicles.***
