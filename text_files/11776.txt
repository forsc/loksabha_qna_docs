                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

910






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Green Window Programme




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to formulate a Green Window Programme for       catalysing green energy in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of estimated amount therefor;  (d) the details and the names of the States/UTs to be covered under the said programme          across the country;  (e) the details of the targets fixed under the said programme; and  (f)  the time by which it is likely to be started?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)to(c) The Indian Renewable Energy Development Agency Limited (IREDA), a Government Company established in 1987 as a Non-Banking Financial Institution, has been extending financial assistance through innovative financial mechanisms for projects generating electricity and / or energy through renewable energy sources, generally referred to as green energy.  Since its inception, IREDA has disbursed loans of Rs 45,503 crore. In the financial year 2019-20, IREDA has so far disbursed loans of Rs 9,385 crore.(d) IREDA has been financing renewable energy projects across India.(e) IREDA’s performance targets for the financial year 2019-20 include 96.25 per cent loan disbursement of total funds available and revenue of Rs 2300 crore from operations.(f)  Does not arise.*****
