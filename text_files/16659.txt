                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

389






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






New Scheme during Covid 19




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any scheme has been implemented in the country in the current financial year during the COVID 19 pandemic;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the funds allocated to the State of Andhra Pradesh under this scheme;(d) 	whether any third party independent evaluation has been undertaken for the above scheme; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): The Ministry of Rural Development has been implementing schemes such as Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM), Deen Dayal Upadhyay – Gramin Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY),  Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), Shyama Prasad Mukherjee National Rurban Mission (SPMRM) and National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) for development of rural areas through generation of employment, strengthening of livelihood opportunities, promoting of self-employment, skilling of rural youths, provision of social assistance, development of infrastructure etc. The progress of these schemes during 2020-21 is given as follows;(d) & (e): The implementation status of the schemes of this Ministry is being reviewed regularly at various levels such as meeting of Performance Review Committee, Common Review Mission, Third Party Evaluation, National Level Monitors, NITI Aayog etc. On the findings of these reviews, actions as appropriate, including restructuring of the schemes are being taken up. However,  no  third party independent evaluation study has been undertaken so far (including on those particular interventions carried under the schemes, as mentioned above ) during the period of COVID-19 pandemic.  *******
