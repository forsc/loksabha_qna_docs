                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

860






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Construction and Repairing of NHs




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total length of National Highways (NHs) constructed and repaired during the last five years and current year along with the funds spent thereon, State and year wise;(b) the total length of highways being constructed at present in a day in the country; and(c) the number of proposals received for construction and repair of NHs from Bihar during the said period along with the number of proposals pending till date along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Development and maintenance of National Highways is a continuous process and they are kept in traffic worthy condition depending upon the availability of funds and inter se priority.  The State-wise details of the National Highways constructed during the last five years and current year are given at Annexure-I. The State-wise details of the amount spent on development and maintenance of NHs are given at Annexure-II & Annexure-III respectively.(b)	Highways are being constructed at a pace of 25.23 km per day in Financial Year 2019-20 till December 2019.(c)	Details of proposals received and pending from Bihar during last five years and current year are given below. Year	Proposals received	Proposals sanctioned	Proposals pending2014-15	19	19	02015-16	21	21	02016-17	36	36	02017-18	29	29	02018-19	13	13	02019-20	13	13	0
