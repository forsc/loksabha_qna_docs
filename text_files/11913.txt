                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

702






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Construction of Foot Over Bridge




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the steps taken by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) for the construction of foot over bridge near Bharat Mata Mandir on the Surat –Hazira National Highway No. 6; and(b)	the steps taken by NHAI in coordination with the local administration to resolve the problems being faced by the employees of Department of Atomic Energy due to illegal parking of vehicles on the service road near Hazira Ammonia Extension Plant (HAEP) township on the said highway?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The proposal of construction of foot over bridge near Bharat Mata Mandir on Surat-Hazira National Highway no.-6 has already been approved.(b) As regard to illegal parking of vehicle on service road, the same is being removed regularly by the Concessionaire. Further, the matter has been taken up with enforcement agency of State Government to deal the matter as per law & order.
