                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

585






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Profitability of AAI




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether profitability of the Airports Authority of India (AAI) was affected by privatization of Airports of Delhi and Varanasi;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether people of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes would continue to get reservation in the private/to be privatised airports, in various States in the Country; State/UT-wise; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Public Private Partnership (PPP) was implemented at Delhi Airport in 2006 and is a success story. PPP at the airport has helped in creating world class airport and delivery of efficient and quality services to the airport passengers. It has also helped Airports Authority of India (AAI) in enhancing its revenues and focusing on developing airports and Air Navigation infrastructure in the rest of the country.  The AAI has earned revenue from Delhi Airport to the tune of Rs. 16566.30 Crore for the period 2006-07 to 2018-19. AAI has not awarded Varanasi airport under PPP.;(c) & (d): On implementation of PPP, the AAI employees have choice to either remain with AAI or opt to join PPP partner as their employee. Those who continue to be with AAI are governed by the terms and conditions of service of AAI and those who opt for private player are governed by the terms and conditions of service of the respective private company.
