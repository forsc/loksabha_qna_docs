                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Rice and Paddy




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government of India procures rice and paddy from various States every year for supporting the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) or other Food Security schemes; (b) 	if so, the top ten States in 2019-20 and 2020-21 from where rice and paddy were procured as per quantum; (c) 	the agencies that are being engaged by Government to procure rice and paddy from the States on behalf of the Government; and (d) 	What is the quantum of rice or paddy being supplied in 2019-20 and 2020-21 to the State of Assam under these schemes such as PMGKAY by the Government?
