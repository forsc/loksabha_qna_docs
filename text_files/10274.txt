                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

327






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Employment through Railway Recruitment Board




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total number of recruitments made by the Southern Railways through the Railway Recruitment Board during the last five years;(b)	whether there is any region/ State-wise criteria for recruitment in Indian Railways through Railway Recruitment Board; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the total number of people from the state of Tamil Nadu who joined the Indian Railways during the last five years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	to (c):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 327 BY SHRI MANICKAM TAGORE B. TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.12.2019 REGARDING EMPLOYMENT THROUGH RAILWAY RECRUITMENT BOARD(a): During the last five years i.e. 2015-16 to 2019-20 (upto 30.11.2019), a total number of 7,574 candidates have been empanelled for various Group ‘C’ posts (including Level-1) by Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) / Chennai and Thiruvananthapuram for Southern Railway. The year-wise details are as under-Year	        Recruitment made by RRBs for Southern Railway2015-16	        16262016-17	        1952017-18	        10312018-19	        242019-20 (upto 30.11.2019) 4698*Total	          7574**including Level-1 posts(b) and (c): No, Sir. Indian Railways is an organization having pan India character. Candidates are free to apply to any RRB throughout the country in response to the advertisements. A total number of 1433 candidates from Tamil Nadu have been empanelled by all RRBs for appointment on Indian Railways/Production Units during the last five years.*****
