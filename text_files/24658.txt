                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Schemes of Khadi Gramodyog




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the schemes being run by Khadi Gramodyog in Chhattisgarh during the last three years; (b) 	the quantum of allocated funds for the said schemes; and (c) 	the details of number of beneficiaries including especially that of Durg Parliamentary Constituency along with the state of the scheme expansion at present, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b):	Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) is a statutory organization under the Ministry of MSME and engaged in promoting and developing Khadi and Village Industries through the following Schemes viz., i) Khadi Vikas Yojana, ii) Gramodyog Vikas Yojana and iii) Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme.i) Khadi Vikas Yojana (KVY):- KVIC provides assistance to Khadi Institutions for development of Khadi and generation of employment opportunities in the country under various components, viz., Market Development Assistance to the Khadi Institutions and Khadi Artisans, Interest Subsidy Eligibility Certificate (ISEC) Scheme to ensure availability of working capital on reduced rate of interest, Work-sheds to Khadi artisans for providing them sufficient space and congenial work environment, assistance to strengthen weak Khadi Institutions, assistance for modernization of sales outlets of Khadi Institutions, training to the artisans, etc. ii) Gramodyog Vikas Yojana (GVY):- GVY focuses on development of Village based industries under six verticals such as i) Agro Based & Food Processing Industry, ii) Mineral Based Industry, iii) Handmade Paper, Leather & Plastic Industry, iv) Rural Engineering and New Technology Industry, v) Wellness & Cosmetics Industry, and vi) Service & Textile Industry.  The following two verticals comes under GVY:1)	Honey Mission (Beekeeping Programme) (Agro Based & Food Processing Industry) KVIC has launched Honey Mission in 2017-18 to supplement income of farmers, unemployed youth, adivasis, etc.2)	Kumbhar Sashaktikaran Programme (Mineral Based Industry): Under the programme, for up-liftment of the rural potters’ livelihood, skill up-gradation training and new home scale energy efficient equipment like electric pottery wheels, blunger, pug mill, kiln etc. are provided for producing good quality products.iii) Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) is a credit linked subsidy scheme, for setting up of new micro-enterprises and to generate employment opportunities in rural as well as urban areas of the country through KVIC, State Khadi & Village Industries Board (KVIB) and District Industries Centre (DIC).  General category beneficiaries can avail Margin Money subsidy of 25% of the project cost in rural areas and 15% in urban areas. For beneficiaries belonging to special categories such as SC/ST/Women/PH/Minorities/Ex-Servicemen/NER, the Margin Money subsidy is 35% in rural areas and 25% in urban areas. The maximum cost of projects is Rs.25 lakh in the manufacturing sector and Rs.10 lakh in the service sector.Scheme-wise fund disbursed for implementation and development of Khadi and Village Industries activities in Chhattisgarh State, during last three years is as follows:(Rs. in lakh)<pre>Sr. No.	Name of Scheme	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21 (Provisional)I.
