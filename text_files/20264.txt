                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Premium Indent Policy




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has brought out a policy regarding Premium Indent to facilitate freight customers;(b)	 if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time likely to be taken under the new policy for allotment of rakes on priority basis if a customer places a request for Premium Indent?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Yes, Sir.(b) & (c): Policy guidelines for Premium Indent scheme have been issued in December 2020. Salient features of the policy are:–•	The customer can indicate a date of supply of rakes and also whether he/she will load if the rake is supplied after due date on Normal tariff rate.•	The customer is required to pay 5% premium on normal freight in advance. If the rake is supplied later than the indicated date on the indent, the premium paid is adjusted against the normal freight.•	On payment of 5% premium on normal freight, the indent is given priority and assurance in allocation of rake on nominated day(s) indicated by him/her.•	In the goods shed also, the customer is permitted to place premium indent. Customer gets priority for allocation on two days, presently Monday and Friday, as notified under Preferential Traffic Order issued by Railway Board from time to time. On other days, normal order of priority of indents is followed.•	Premium Indent once placed cannot be withdrawn; withdrawal of the indent invites forfeiture of the premium paid.•	This premium indent policy is not applicable to restricted destinations and destinations regulated by quota.•	The scheme is optional.*****
