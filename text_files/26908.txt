                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Privatisation of Railways




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether a National Rail Plan (NRP) for infrastructure development and privatisation of Railways for thirty years has been evolved; and  (b)	if so, the timeline for privatisation of passenger trains, freight trains, stations, production units, railway public sector like CONCOR, Dedicated Freight Corridor corporation, etc. as per NRP?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS  AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): Indian Railways has prepared a National Rail Plan (NRP). The objective of the Plan is to create infrastructure capacity ahead of demand, which in turn cater to future growth in demand right up to 2050 and also increase the modal share of Railways to 45% in freight traffic and to continue to sustain it. However, the NRP does not deal with the privatization of Railways.(b): Does not arise.******
