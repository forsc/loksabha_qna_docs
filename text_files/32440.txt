                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3873






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Creamy and Non-Creamy layer




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.†3873TO BE ANSWERED ON 21.12.2021CREAMY AND NON-CREAMY LAYER †3873. SHRI PRATAPRAO PATIL CHIKHLIKAR: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to do away with the difference of creamy layer and non-creamy layer in respect of backward classes; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) whether the Government is aware that the youth hanging in between the creamy layer and non-creamy layer feel frustration due to failure in getting selected for appointments and admission for education despite having the capability and the students and the youth lose their self-confidence, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) : No Sir.(b): Question does not arise in view of (a) above.(c): The Government of India have issued clear guidelines regarding the category of persons who would be classified as Creamy layer for the purpose of OBC reservation. All OBC beneficiaries who do not fall in the Creamy layer can avail the benefits of reservation. There is no category in between the Creamy and Non-Creamy layer.*****
