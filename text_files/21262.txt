                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






MGNREGS in Bihar and Chhattisgarh




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Mahabali Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether all the labourers registered under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) have been provided 100 days’ work and if so, the State-wise details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) 	the number of labourers who got 100 days’ work under MGNREGS during the last three years, State-wise including Bihar and Chhattisgarh; and(c) 	the number of workers who have been provided works under MGNREGS since 2020 in Karakat Parliamentary Constituency in Bihar and Durg Parliamentary Constituency in Chhattisgarh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b) The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS), is a demand driven wage employment programme. A rural household, adult members of which agree to do unskilled manual work, is entitled for hundred days of wage employment. It provides livelihood security i.e., fall back option for livelihood for the rural households when no better employment opportunity is available. The demand for work is influenced by various factors such as rain-fall, availability of alternative and remunerative employment opportunities outside Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. During the current financial year 2020-21 (as on 04.03.2021), a total number of 49.66 lakh households received 100 days of employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS.In this regard, the States/UTs-wise including Bihar and Chhattisgarh details of total number of households completing 100 days of wage employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the last three years and the current financial year 2020-21 (as on 04.03.2021) is given at Annexure.(c)	The Karakat Parliamentary Constituency is spread in two districts namely Rohtas and Aurangabad of Bihar. The Durg Parliamentary Constituency is spread in two districts of Chhattisgarh namely Durg & Bemetara.
