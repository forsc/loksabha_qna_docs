                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2857






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






PMAY Targets




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has announced any target regarding housing by the year 2022 under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) in the country, particularly for Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the total amount committed for the scheme, year-wise and spent so far; and(c) 	the number of beneficiaries under Economically weaker sections, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        a)	Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), Government of India aims to provide pucca houses to all eligible beneficiaries in the urban areas of the country by the year 2022.Under the scheme, States/Union Territories (UTs) have assessed a demand of 112 lakh houses. The demand reported by Tamil Nadu is for 13.92 lakh houses. b)	 Year-wise details of central assistance committed and released/spent under the scheme as on 25.11.2019 are at Annexure-I.c)	Details of number of houses sanctioned for Economically Weaker Sections as on 25.11.2019, State/UT-wise, are at Annexure-II.                                     *****
