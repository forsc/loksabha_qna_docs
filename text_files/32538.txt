                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Startup Incubation




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry will consider launching a startup incubation focused specifically towards young entrepreneurs in Bihar to improve the economy of the region;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether startups with good foundational values would be promoted at national level to secure support and funding from nationally-recognized capital growth funds post pre-seed round; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b): 	Yes. The Government has taken various initiatives for setting up incubators to support startups through national level schemes. List of some of the schemes is at Annexure-I.  (c) & (d):	 Startup India is a flagship initiative of the Government of India, intended to build a strong eco-system for nurturing innovation and startups in the country to drive sustainable economic growth and generate large scale employment opportunities. Salient features of the Startup India Initiative are at Annexure-II. ******
