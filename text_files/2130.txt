                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Under Construction Projects




Rampreet Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether many developmental projects were under construction during March, 2019;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the names of Ministries of Government of India having relevance to it; and(c)	the number of projects out of these projects which were likely to be completed during the period from March, 2014 to March, 2019 ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) & (b) The Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation monitors on-going Central Sector Infrastructure Projects costing Rs. 150 crore and above on time and cost overruns, on the basis of information provided by the project implementing agencies. As on 01.04.2019, a total of 1405 projects were on the monitor of this Ministry. The details of these Central Sector Infrastructure Projects are available in the flash report at www. cspm.gov.in/publications. (c)    The details of projects which were likely to be completed during the period from March, 2014 to March, 2019 are at Annexure.                                               ****
