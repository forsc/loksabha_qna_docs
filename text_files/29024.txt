                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Ordnance Factories




Ram Kripal Yadav













                                                    V. Kalanidhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of ordnance factories within the country, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu; (b) whether the Government has dissolved the Ordnance Factory Board (OFB) and transferred its assets, employees and management to seven Public Sector Units (PSUs); (c) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons for the corporatization of the OFB; and (d) whether the assurances given to the federations in this context have not been fulfilled, if so, the details thereof and the stand of the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (d): A statement is attached. *****
