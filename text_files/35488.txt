                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2768






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Outbound Migration from States




 Pratap Chandra Sarangi













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Mahendra Singh Solanky
                                                








                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                








                                                    Brijbhushan Sharan Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government maintains the data showing the number of persons who have migrated outward from Odisha to other States of India in the last five years;(b)if so, the number of persons migrating to other States in India due to employment related reasons; and(c)whether the Government proposes to conduct an annual survey to assess outbound migration between States within in the country; if so, the details thereof and the frequency at which the data will be released?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	 3,87,499 persons migrated for the purpose of work or employment from Odisha to other States of India as per Census,  2011.(c):	  	There is no proposal to conduct annual survey. However, Labour Bureau under Ministry of Labour & Employment has launched an All India Survey of Migrant workers with effect from 01.04.2021. *******
