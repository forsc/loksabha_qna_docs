                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Privatisation of Government Hospitals




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be please to state:(a) whether the Government is mulling over to privatise the Government hospitals for better service and condition; and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b): There is no proposal at present under consideration in this Ministry to privatise Central Government Hospitals which are under the administrative control of the Ministry. The hospital services and facilities in the Central Government Hospitals under this Ministry have been strengthened from time to time as per requirement and availability of resources, in order to render better quality services to the patients.
