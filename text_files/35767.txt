                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






RCPLWE in Srikakulam




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of works undertaken in Srikakulam during the last three years under Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Areas (RCPLWE), yearwise;(b) 	the details of fund allocation, utilization and status of progress made on works undertaken under the RCPLWE, year-wise;(c) 	the details of works pending along with reasons for the delay; and(d) 	the details of works to be undertaken in Srikakulam under the continuation of RCPLWE, approved by concerned Committees?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) &(b)	The details of works sanctioned and completed in Srikakulam district of Andhra Pradesh during the last three years and the current year under Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Affected Areas (RCPLWEA) are given below:-                                                          Road length in KmYear	             Sanctioned 	                     Completed* 	     Road length    No. of bridges	  Road length	No. of bridges2018-19	       82.38	       00	            19.06	      002019-20	       12.10	       01	            45.55	      002020-21	        00	       00	            59.25	      002021-22	        00	       00	            26.32	      00* includes length sanctioned in previous years The funds for implementation of the scheme are released by the Ministry to the state as whole. Further release of funds to the Programme Implementation Units (PIUs) at the district level is done by the respective State Governments depending upon the absorption capacity of the PIU. As reported by the State of Andhra Pradesh, the details of funds  utilized for implementation of RCPLWEA in Srikakulam district, during the last three years and current year, year-wise are given below:-  Year	           Expenditure (Rs. in crore)2018-19	                   11.112019-20	                   26.732020-21	                   44.242021-22	                   27.98 (c)&(d)  	8 road works of 37 Km and 1 bridge work are pending for completion. As reported by the State Government, work on 3 roads and 1 bridge is under progress and work on remaining 5 roads is held up due to forest clearance. All the projects under RCPLWEA were targeted for completion by March, 2020. The Ministry had received requests from the State Governments for extension of time to complete the balance projects under RCPLWEA. Accordingly, the Ministry, with the approval of the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs, has extended the deadline for completion of the balance projects upto March, 2023. *****
