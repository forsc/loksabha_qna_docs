                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3073






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Maintenance and Modernization of Roads




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any proposal from the Government of Rajasthan for maintenance and modernization of roads in rural and frontier regions is lying pending with the Union Government; and(b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) No, Sir. This Ministry is primarily responsible for the development and maintenance of National Highways in the country. The work of maintenance and modernization of roads in rural and frontier regions does not pertain to this Ministry.****
