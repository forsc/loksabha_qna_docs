                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2409






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Use of Aluminium for Coaches




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering the use of aluminium instead of steel in the construction of the coaches for improving energy efficiency as well as making the trains faster;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. Indian Railways (IR) has taken the following measures with respect to induction of Aluminium Body coaches:-1.	Modern Coach Factory (MCF) / Rae Bareli has signed a ToT (Transfer of Technology) contract with M/s Dawonsys, South Korea for Design, Development, Manufacturing and Testing of Aluminium Body passenger coaches. 2.	IR plans to procure 400 new generation Vande Bharat Train sets with better energy efficiency and passenger riding experience. The use of Aluminium Body coaches in these new generation energy efficient Vande Bharat train sets will also be considered to the extent possible.3.	Rolling Stock to be procured for Mumbai-Ahmedabad High Speed Rail (MAHSR) will be having Aluminium Body coaches.*****
