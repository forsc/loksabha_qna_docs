                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1051






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Recruitment of Patriot Youth in Para-Military Forces




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes any scheme for recruiting patriotic youth living in border areas in para-military forces;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes any scheme for such recruitment drive every year;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (b):	As per the existing scheme of examination for recruitment to the post of Constable (General Duty-GD) conducted by the Staff Selection Commission (SSC), 20% of the vacancies in Border Guarding Forces namely, Border Security Force (BSF), Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP), Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) and Assam Rifles (AR) are allotted to notified border districts, falling within the area of responsibility of the concerned border guarding force. (c) to (e):	It has been decided to conduct recruitment of Constable (GD) through SSC on yearly basis.******
