                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2645






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Arrangements for Kumbh Mela




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Ministry has made adequate arrangements for the travel of pilgrims who are expected to visit Haridwar during Maha Kumbh Mela, 2021;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	 the number of special trains being run by the Government for the said purpose;(d)	 whether the present availability of trains is not sufficient to meet the requirement of a large number of saints and sears, common pilgrims and tourists from across the globe; and(e)	 if so, the details thereof and the corrective measures taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	 to (e) For the Kumbh Mela (January – April, 2021), at Haridwar, Indian Railways has made adequate arrangements, keeping in view the concerns and suggestions of State Governments, in view of the prevailing COVID-19 situation.  12 pairs of Kumbh Mela Specials, which are in addition to the existing services bound for Haridwar are being operated.  Besides, 15 pairs of trains bound for Haridwar, have also been augmented for generating additional accommodation to the pilgrims.   *****
