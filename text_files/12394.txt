                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Slowdown in Tax Collection




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has experienced a slowdown in tax collection this fiscal year; (b)  if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the plans of the Government for making up for this deficit if such a slowdown is present; (d) whether the Government has experienced a shortfall in both direct and indirect tax collection; and (e)  if so, whether the Government has identified contributing factors for the same and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINSTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (e): The central direct tax and indirect tax collection during the Financial Year 2019-20 (upto December 2019) is as under: (Rs. in crore)Revised Estimate for   Direct Taxes  (2019-20)	Net Direct Tax Collection* 	% Achieved 	Revised Estimate for Indirect Taxes (Non-GST & GST)(2019-20)	Net Indirect Tax collection* (Non GST & GST)	% Achieved11,70,000	6,98,237	59.68%	9,89,243	6,80,630	68.80% *Source: Flash figures of CCAKeeping in view the trends of collection, the revised estimates fixed for direct and indirect taxes in the Financial Year are likely to be achieved. The actual direct and indirect tax collection for Financial Year 2019-20 will be known after 31st March, 2020. *****
