                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1540






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Zero Human Rabies by 2030




Mahendrabhai Kalubhai Munjpara














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any work done by World Health Organization (WHO), Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), World Organization for Animal Health (OIE) and Global Alliance for Rabies Control (GARC) agreed to achieve ‘ Zero Human Rabies’ by 2030(b)	if so, the status of work taken up so far for the same and the entities/agencies with whom these organisations are associating with; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)a):  	      Yes, Sir. As per available information, the World Organization for Animal Health (OIE) collaborate closely with the United Against Rabies (UAR) partners, i.e. World Health Organization (WHO), Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO), and Global Alliance for Rabies Control (GARC) towards achieving the goal of elimination of dog-mediated  Rabies in human by 2030 (Zero by 2030). b) & c):  OIE is a standard setting organization and also develops guidelines and protocols for animal disease control including rabies. National Centre for Disease Control (NCDC), Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, New Delhi is the Nodal Agency having technical collaborations with the Tripartite   (OIE, WHO & FAO) for implementing the National Rabies Control Program (NRCP) approved by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare (MoHFW) in the country. The collaboration provides a coordinated foundation for rabies prevention, integrated with the strengthening of human and veterinary health systems with following progress:•	Conducting Training programmes for Health Care professionals on appropriate Animal bite management and Rabies Post Exposure Prophylaxis•	Strengthen Human Rabies Surveillance System •	Strengthening of Regional Laboratories under National Rabies control Programme (NRCP) for Rabies Diagnosis•	Creating awareness in the community through Advocacy & Communication and Social Mobilization.•	Strengthening the inter-sectoral coordination among health and veterinary sector for prevention and control of Rabies.*****
