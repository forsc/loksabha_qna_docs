                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1057






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Flouting of Aviation Norms




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether some private airlines are not following the instructions given by the Government at the time of issuing license;(b) whether the said airlines are not following the said instructions inspite of the warnings given by the Government;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) the action taken/proposed to be taken against the said airlines;(e) whether the Government has conducted any surprise inspection in this regard; and(f) if so, the details along with the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): No Sir. However, in the recent past the Air Operator''s Certificate (AOC) of some airlines were placed under suspension due to non availability of the aircraft with them which is a prerequisite for grant of license (AOC).;(e) to (f): After the issuance of AOC, the airlines are subjected to regular inspections/surveillance in order to ensure the continued compliance of the regulatory  requirements. The deficiencies observed during such inspections/surveillance are informed to the airline concerned for resolution in a time bound manner as per Surveillance and Enforcement Procedure Manual.
