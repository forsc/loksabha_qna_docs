                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Identification of Religious Sites in Uttar Pradesh




Upendra Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan/strategy to develop the religious tourist places in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the expenditure incurred in development of religious tourist places of the country, State/UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh; (c)	whether the Government has identified religious tourist places for development in Uttar Pradesh; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                                (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism under its PRASHAD Scheme provides Central Financial Assistance to the concerned State Governments/UT Administrations for development of pilgrimage tourism infrastructure at the religious tourist places on receipt of suitable project proposals in line with the Scheme Guidelines.  State-wise details of the projects approved under PRASHAD Scheme on the basis of the project proposals received from the respective State Governments, including the State of Uttar Pradesh, approved costs and the funds released is available at the Annexure.**********
