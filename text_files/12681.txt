                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1522






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Rural Agricultural Centre




Baidyanath Prasad Mahto














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up an agricultural centre at the rural level with housing facilities where all the employees related to rural agricultural sector can work in the office and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether it is proposed to set up sales centres of the cooperative committees/agricultural cooperative committees where the provision of seeds, fertilizers, pesticides etc. can be made as per agricultural norms which can be made available to the farmers on loan basis; and (c)	whether the Government has any such plan and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????		         (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	No Sir, however, the Government has set up 717 Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs) with mandate of technology assessment and demonstration for its application and capacity development.  Each KVK is provided an office building for working and organizing its activities and six staff quarters for housing of its employees.(b) & (c):	No Sir, there is no proposal to set up such centres by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers’ Welfare.*******
