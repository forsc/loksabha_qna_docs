                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3499






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Artificial Intelligence




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 Whether the Government plans to introduce the Artificial Intelligence (AI) system in the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) 21 portal, if so, details thereof including the quantum of funds being allocated for the purpose; and(b)	 The manner in which the Version 3 will differ from Version 1 and Version 2 of the MCA 21 system?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)????? ??? ???????? ????? ???????? ??? ????? ??????	                                    (???????????????????)(a)	MCA21 Version 3envisages various areas such as Helpdesk Solution, e-Consultation Module, Security and Threat Management for utilizing the capabilities of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML). Quantum of funds allotted for implementing AI/ ML is included in the total cost approved by the Competent Authority and as such breakup of cost for AI/ML is not separately available.(b)	MCA21 Version 3 besides features available in Version 1 and Version 2 further includes additional modules such as e-Consultation, e-Adjudication, Compliance Management System and e-Learning. Features such as autopilot surveillance, pre-emptive fraud-detection, compliance management, improved user experience, capability for integration and data exchange with other regulators and government agencies, data analytical capabilities, seamless data dissemination to stakeholders, use of emergingtechnologies like artificial intelligence and machinelearning for effective service delivery are also included in the scope of MCA Version 3.******
