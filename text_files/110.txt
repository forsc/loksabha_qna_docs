                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

72






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Placement of NIFT Students




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has taken cognizance that no placement companies have visited various National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) institutes particularly Kolkata during 2018 and 2019 despite the assurance given by NIFT for placement of students who have obtained their Master’s degree from NIFT institutes;(b)	if so, the details thereof, institute-wise along with the reasons therefor;(c)	the percentage of NIFT graduates provided placement by private companies during the last three years and the current year, institute-wise; and(d)	the details of the guidelines issued by Government to private companies for 100 per cent placement of such NIFT graduates?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	 NIFT campuses including Kolkata campus were visited by the placement companies in the year 2018 and 2019 for placement of students including holders of Masters Degree. Institute-wise number of  placement companies visited to various NIFT Campuses are given below: NIFT Campus	Number of companies visited 	2018	2019New Delhi	139	167Bengaluru	76	70Mumbai	80	72Chennai	39	36Kolkata	42	44Gandhinagar	45	41Hyderabad	23	25Kannur	1	- Total	445	455  (c):	The Percentage of NIFT graduates provided placement during 2015-16 to 2018-19, institute-wise, is given below:S.No.	Campus 	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19		Placement (%)	Placement (%)	Placement (%)	Placement (%)1	Bengaluru	50	98	97	382	Bhopal	34	80	82	393	Bhubaneswar	47	61	79	274	Chennai	42	83	87	385	Gandhinagar	40	83	77	466	Hyderabad	41	64	86	417	Jodhpur	43	48	89	518	Kangra	37	48	59	249	Kannur	42	46	50	2210	Kolkata	66	77	98	5011	Mumbai	48	73	77	3712	New Delhi	42	80	98	3713	Patna	33	53	81	4614	Raebareli	30	45	66	2115	Shillong	33	52	67	30(d):	Companies are provided with NIFT campus placement brochure every year, which carries detailed placement guidelines. In order to ensure 100 per cent placement of such NIFT graduates, NIFT adopts the placement process as given in Annexure. AnnexurePlacement Process at NIFTThe NIFT has a centralized placement process. Companies can employ students from any campus or course of the institute through two routes which are facilitated by the institute:On Campus PlacementsCompanies can register for campus placements, the schedule of which is given for 2018. Upon being given a confirmed time and date, they may come to the campus for the placement procedure.Pre-Placement Offers (PPO)Companies can extend a job offer to students before the on-campus placement. Acceptance of the offer is considered as job placement. In order to provide fair opportunity to all, such students are not allowed to participate in the On Campus placement. These offers may be made on the basis of internships/ training/ projects undertaken by the students in the said company.Eligibility Criteria for Firms for Campus PlacementsThe companies visiting the campus shall match the eligibility criteria listed as under for registering to recruit through NIFT Campus Placement:A company registered under Companies Act of 1956/2013 or a multinational company or global firm having or not having its operations in India conducting business in the fashion or allied sector dealing in the design, manufacturing or distribution. The company may fall under any of the categories mentioned below:1. First Categorisation      -> Product Group2. Second Categorisation -> Type of Firms3. Third Categorisation    -> Type of skill sets/functionsPast Recruiters at NIFTNIFT has always been a favourite destination for impeccable designers, creative managers, tech savvy fashion professionals and not only for the leading core companies in fashion business, but also for leading technology, FMCG, media, journalism, consulting and other companies.******
