                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4534






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Private Medical Colleges




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the details of Government and private medical colleges in the country which provides M.B.B.S. courses, State/ UT-wise; (b) whether the Government is satisfied with the quality of these institutions, if so, the details thereof; (c) the number of colleges out of these which are providing M.D./M.S. courses; (d) whether there is scarcity of medical colleges in the country; and (e) if so, the proposal of the Government to increase the number of medical colleges?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)  & (b):	The details of Government and Private Medical Colleges in the country providing MBBS course, State/UT-wise are given in Annexure-I.                             Permission for starting of a medical college/yearly renewal permission/recognition of degree is granted by the Board of Governors after considering the facilities available in the medical college as per regulations prescribing minimum requirements in terms of infrastructure, faculty, clinical material etc.  The Medical Colleges which fail to meet the required standards are not given permission/renewal permission/recognition.(c):	Out of 536 Colleges, 325 Colleges are providing MD/MS courses.(d) & (e):	To redress regional imbalances in the availability of medical colleges in the country Ministry of Health & Family Welfare implemented a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for “Establishment of new medical colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals”. 58 districts in 20 States/UT have been identified and approved under Phase-I of this Scheme.  The details of 58 Medical Colleges approved under Phase –I are at Annexure-II.Further, Ministry has approved Phase-II of the Scheme to ensure the availability of one medical college in every 3 Parliamentary Constituencies and one Government medical college in each State. 24 new medical colleges in 8 States have been identified under this Scheme to establish new Medical Colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals. The details of 24 Medical Colleges approved under Phase-II are at Annexure-III
