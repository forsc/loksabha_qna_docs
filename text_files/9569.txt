                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Vigyan Jyothi




E. T. Mohammed Basheer













                                                    Andimuthu Raja
                                                








                                                    V. Kalanidhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched/proposes to launch ‘Vigyan Jyothi’ programme to help the girl students in the area of science and technology and mathematics; (b) 	if so, the details of the same and the time by which it is likely to be fully implemented; (c) 	the details of the areas likely to be covered and the funds likely to be sanctioned for the said programme, State/ UT-wise; (d) 	whether the Government has fixed any criteria/target to pick out girl students including from some rural districts for 2020- 2025 under this scheme; (e) 	if so, the details thereof and the number of girls likely to be picked up, State/ UT and district-wise including Nilgiri district and North Chennai area in Tamil Nadu; and (f) 	whether the Government is planning to provide any stipend to these girls, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????; ??????? ?? ???????????? ??????; ?? ?????? ??????? ????????. ???? ?????(a)	Yes Sir.(b)	The Department of Science and Technology (DST) has initiated full implementation of Vigyan Jyoti programme in 50 districts of the country for girl students of Class XI during 2019-20.(c)	All the States and Union Territories are planned to be covered in a phased manner. Rs 20 lakh per Vigyan Jyoti Centre has been sanctioned for implementation of the scheme in the current financial year. The state-wise fund allocation in the year 2019-20 is as follows:Sl.
