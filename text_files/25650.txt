                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

672






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Solar Powered Lighting System




Sunil Kumar Soni













                                                    Vijay Baghel
                                                








                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of villages covered under Solar Powered Lighting System in Chhattisgarh; (b)	the number of tribal majority and economically backward villages out of the said villages;(c)	the criteria laid down by the Government for the implementation of the said system in villages; and(d)	the amount spent for the said system in Chhattisgarh during the last three years along with the progress/achievements made in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (d) The state of Chhattisgarh has reported that 941 villages have been covered under Solar Powered Lighting System in Chhattisgarh and all of these are tribal majority and economically backward villages.Villages which are situated in dense forest areas and where electrification work through conventional means was difficult and expensive, have been electrified through Solar Powered Lighting System. Electrification of these 941 villages was completed before 04.04.2018. An amount of Rs. 382.86 Crore was spent in the last three years on these projects. *****
