                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2123






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Training and Courses of AYUSH




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has stressed for improving capabilities in training and   professional courses of AYUSH within North-Eastern Region of the country; (b)	 if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Assam; (c)	 the details of targets and estimated fund in this regard; (d)	 whether the Government has also planned to develop modern infrastructure under this mission within Assam; (e)	 if so, the details thereof; and (f)	the time by which it is likely to be launched?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTR OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) to (f):Ministry of AYUSH is running theNorth Eastern Institute of Ayurveda and Homeopathy (NEIAH), Shillong (Meghalaya)with 50 seats each in BAMS and BHMS course since the academic year 2016-17. The seats were increased in said courses from 50 to 63 from the academic year 2019-20.Further, this Institute is also running one year Panchakarma Technician Certificate course with 20 seats, each year from the academic year 2018-19.Medical Officers of North-East States were also being provided Panchakarma Therapy Training by this Institute.Targets:-(i)	To provide world –class education in Ayurveda and Homoeopathy at the under-Graduate, Post- Graduate, Doctoral and Post-Doctoral Degree levels.(ii)	To develop, promote and popularize the Ayurvedic and Homeopathic Systems of Educations and Healthcare.Estimated Budget:-FY 	BE	RE	Utilizations2020-21	19.50Crore	46.08Crore	45.09Crore(II)    North Eastern Institute of Ayurveda and Folk Medicine Research (NEIAFMR),Pasighat (Arunachal Pradesh):-        An Ayurveda College with intake capacity of 30 Students in UG (BAMS)course is being set up at NEIAFMR, Pasighat.
