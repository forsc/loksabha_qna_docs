                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1878






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






ITIR Hyderabad




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)	whether the development of the Information Technology Investment Region (ITIR) in Hyderabad was sanctioned six years ago to attract investments in IT/ITES sector and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has received any request from the State Government with regard to release of funds for the said project  and if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government thereon; and(c)	whether the Government has put on hold the aforesaid project and if so, the details thereof including the revised plan, if any, proposed by the Government in this regard along with the reasons therefore?  






ANSWER




                                                        (a):    Yes, Sir. Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India vide notification dated 13.11.2013 had notified Information Technology Investment Region (ITIR) in Hyderabad at a project cost of Rs. 3275 crore. The developmental activities were to be carried out with financial assistance from various concerned Ministries of Government of India.(b) and (c):   No, Sir. The progress and issues in implementation of the ITIR in Hyderabad was reviewed during the meeting held at MeitY on 11.01.2017 with representatives from concerned Ministries viz; Ministry of Railways, Ministry of Road, Transport & Highways and IT (E&C) Department, Government of Telangana wherein Government of Telangana was asked to submit requisite information to the concerned Ministries with intimation to Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. Response from Government of Telangana  has not been received till date.*******
