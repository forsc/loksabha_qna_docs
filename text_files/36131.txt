Procurement of Food Grains at MSP




Sumalatha Ambareesh














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government procured various food grains from across the country at Minimum Support Price (MSP) as per procurement policy; (b) 	if so, the details of various food grains procured during the last three years and current year; State and year-wise; (c) 	whether any limit has been set for the procurement of fair average quality (FAQ) food grains from farmers at Minimum Support Price as per procurement policy; (d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the details of total number of farmers benefitted under the said policy along with the MSP for the last three years, year and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) and (b): 	State-wise foodgrain procured at Minimum Support Price (MSP) for central pool during last three years and current year are as under: I. Wheat at Annexure-I  II. Paddy at Annexure-II  III. Coarsegrain at Annexure-IIIIV- Pulses at Annexure-IV &V.....2/--2-(c) and (d): 	Procurement in a State depends not only upon production but also upon other multiple factors like marketable surplus, MSP, prevailing market rate, demand & supply situation and participation of private traders etc.    	State Government agencies and Food Corporation of India (FCI) purchase wheat & paddy within the stipulated period with prescribed Fair Average Quality (FAQ) specification at Minimum Support Price (MSP) for Central Pool.          	Coarsegrains are procured at MSP by State Governments  as per the procurement plan prepared in consultation with FCI and approved by the Central Government, subject to  its distribution by the State Government  under National Food Security Act (NFSA)/ Other Welfare Schemes (OWS).     	The Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) implements the Price Support Scheme (PSS) for procurement of oil seeds and pulses through Central nodal agencies. This scheme is implemented at the request of the concerned State Government which agrees to exempt the procured commodities from levy of mandi tax and assist central nodal agencies in logistic arrangements, including gunny bags, working capital for state agencies, creation of revolving fund for PSS operations, etc. as required under the scheme guidelines. The procurement of oilseeds and pulses are undertaken by Central agencies at Minimum Support Price (MSP) announced by the Government as and when prices fall below the MSP and compliances of State/UT Govt. to PSS guidelines.(e): 	State-wise farmers benefited through procurement of foodgrains at MSP for central pool during last three years are as under: I.
