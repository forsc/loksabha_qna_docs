                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3948






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Malt Based Foods Under POSHAN 2.0




Pradeep Kumar Singh













                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)     whether it is a fact that the Government has received around 16.31 percent higher allocation in the budget 2021-22, if so, the details thereof; (b)      the type of food and nutritional supplement currently covered under the Mission Poshan 2.0; (c)     whether the Government has any plan to include Malt based foods under Mission Poshan 2.0 for pregnant mothers and children below 10 years, if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and (d)      if not, whether Government is desirous of doing so in near future and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	   Budget Estimate 2021-22 of Ministry of Women and Child Development is 244435.00 crore which is 16.31% higher than Revised Estimate of 2020-21 which is Rs. 21008.31 crore. (b) to (d)   Under the current Supplementary Nutrition Programme (SNP), age-specific meal or food is provided to different categories of  beneficiaries as per nutritional standards specified under the National Food Security Act. Supplementary Nutrition provided to the Anganwadi Services beneficiaries at the AWCs is determined by locally available ingredients and food preferences/habits of the local population as per specified nutritional norms. The inclusion of food and selection of recipes for Supplementary Nutrition rests with the States/UTs.  *****
