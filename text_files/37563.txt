                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4986






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Medical Treatment under AB-PMJAY




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has signed Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with private insurance companies for processing of claims under Ayushman Bharat Yojana;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c)  whether the said MoUs is accessible in the public domain and if so, the details thereof;(d)  if not, the time by which these are likely to be made available in public domain;(e) the number of beneficiaries treated for COVID-19 and non-COVID-19 diseases across the country under AB-PMJAY during FY 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22, State/UT-wise; and(f)  the amount of expenditure incurred for the treatment of COVID-19 patients under AB-PMJAY for FY 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d): Under Ayushman Bharat -Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY), States/UTs have the flexibility to implement the scheme in insurance mode or trust mode or mixed mode i.e. partly insurance mode and partly trust mode. Out of 33 States/UTs implementing the scheme, 10 States/UTs namely Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu, Gujarat, Jammu and Kashmir, Lakshadweep, Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu are implementing it either in insurance mode or in mixed mode. States/UTs implementing the scheme in insurance/mixed mode directly enter into agreements with insurance companies. These agreements are accessible from the respective State Health Agencies. (e) & (f): State/UT-wise details of authorized hospital admissions for COVID-19 and non-COVID-19 for FY 2019-20, FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 and the amount of claims for COVID-19 treatment under the scheme for FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 are at Annexure.
