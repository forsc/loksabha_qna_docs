                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4451






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Allocation for PMS for SCs and OBCs




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4451 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021ALLOCATION FOR PMS FOR SCs AND OBCs4451. SHRI KESINENI SRINIVAS:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of funds allocated as Budget Estimate (BE) and disbursed as actuals, Revised Estimate (RE) from 2014-2020 under post-matric scholarship for Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Other Backward Classes (OBCs) for Andhra Pradesh;(b) the number of homes set up under the National Action Plan for Senior Citizens, State/UT-wise; (c) whether Regional Resource and Training Centres have been set up and if so, the details of the members thereof; and (d) the details of work done by the Regional Resource and Training Centres in terms of monitoring, evaluation and promotional works?






ANSWER




                                                        GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4451 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021ALLOCATION FOR PMS FOR SCs AND OBCs4451. SHRI KESINENI SRINIVAS:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of funds allocated as Budget Estimate (BE) and disbursed as actuals, Revised Estimate (RE) from 2014-2020 under post-matric scholarship for Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Other Backward Classes (OBCs) for Andhra Pradesh;(b) the number of homes set up under the National Action Plan for Senior Citizens, State/UT-wise; (c) whether Regional Resource and Training Centres have been set up and if so, the details of the members thereof; and (d) the details of work done by the Regional Resource and Training Centres in terms of monitoring, evaluation and promotional works?
