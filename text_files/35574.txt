                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2886






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






RMSA in Andhra Pradesh




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of quantum of funds allocated under the Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA) during the last five years in the state of Andhra Pradesh, District-wise; (b) the details of expenditures incurred under RMSA during the last five years; and(c) the current capacity of schools at the primary level to be upgraded to Centres of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION (SHRIMATI ANNPURNA DEVI)**** (a) As informed by State Government of Andhra Pradesh, funds have been allocated to 13 District Project offices for implementation of the Samagra Shiksha activities (Secondary) erstwhile scheme of RMSA for the year 2019-2020 to 2021-2022 is given below.<pre>S.No.	Name of the District	Funds Allocated  ( Rs.In Lakhs)		2019-20	2020-2021	2021-202201	SRIKAKULAM	25.58	512.753	495.1102	VIZIANAGARAM	 	195.375	408.6803	VISHAKAPATNAM	 	252.515	501.9704	EAST GODAVARI	 	948.315	622.6905	WEST GODAVARI	 	1367.844	420.7906	KRISHNA	 	561.835	394.7507	GUNTUR	 	1495.246	460.2508	PRAKASAM	 	1791.590	421.1309	NELLORE	274.17	526.844	376.4110	CHITTOOR	 	1328.490	623.7511	YSR KADAPA	 	434.646	393.9412	ANANTHAPURAM	 	1105.403	700.4813	KURNOOL	37.53	1102.803	625.89 	Total	337.28	11623.659	6445.85</pre>The releases from 2017-18 to 2018-19 were made as centralized payments i.e direct to vendors hence no district wise data is available.(b) As informed by State Government of Andhra Pradesh, the details of expenditure incurred under RMSA during the last five years is as given below:<pre>S.No	Year	Expenditure (Rs.In Lakh)01	2017-2018	33412.0502	2018-2019	29984.4103	2019-2020	30275.6704	2020-2021	9939.6305	2021-2022 (As at 14.03.2022)	26256.70</pre>(c) The Central Government, through integrated centrally sponsored scheme of Samagra Shiksha provides support to the States and UTs for opening/ upgradation of schools as per gaps identified from school mapping. It is mentioned that every financial year, on the basis of state specific school mapping exercise, the state proposes new schools to be upgraded in their respective Annual Work Plan and Budget (AWP&B) for consideration for approval by Project Approval Board (PAB) in Ministry of Education (MOE), accordingly as per availability of fund and rational of the proposal the schools are approved.Under erstwhile Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA), till 2017- 18 and Samagra Shiksha effective from 2018-19, 13093 new secondary schools and 151 new Higher Secondary schools have been sanctioned, out of which States and UTs have reported opening of 12724 new secondary schools and 54 new Higher Secondary schools respectively, till 15.03.2022. ******
