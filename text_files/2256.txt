                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2147






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Increase of CPL and TRP in India




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that number of Commercial Pilot License (CPL) holders and Type Rated Pilots (TRPs) are increasing in the country over the last five years;(b) if so, the details thereof, year-wise;(c) the number of foreign pilots employed by aviation companies over the last five years, airline/year-wise;(d) whether the Government is trying to establish a system to develop homegrown pool of commercial pilots who are type rated so that the dependency on expatriate pilots is minimized; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The number of Commercial Pilot License(CPL) and Type Rated Pilot License holders have been increasing over the past five years. The year wise details are as under:;   CPL issued;Year                            No. of CPL issued;2015                              394;2016                              458;2017                              593;2018                              659;2019(upto 31.5.2019)    313;;Type Rating issued on Pilot License;Year                            No. of Endorsement;2015                              1521;2016                              2602;2017                              2729;2018                              2611;2019(upto 31.5.2019)    2026;;;(c) Details of foreign pilots employed by the aviation companies over the last 05 years are as under:;Year                               Number of pilots;2015                              280;2016                              378;2017                              249;2018                              324;2019(upto June 2019)   404;;(d) & (e) Yes, Sir. To overcome the shortage of pilots, all the Schedule and Non-Schedule airlines have been advised to develop their own strength to reduce the dependency on foreign pilots. Accordingly, airlines train their pilots to upgrade them as Pilot in command/ Commanders to reduce the dependency on foreign pilots.
