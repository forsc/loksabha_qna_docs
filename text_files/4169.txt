                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3852






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






De-Addiction Centres/IRCs




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the de-addiction centres/Integrated and Rehabilitation Centres (IRCs) for addicts active in the country, State/UT-wise; (b) the number of alcoholics-anonymous or assistance for prevention of alcoholism sessions conducted during the last three years and the current year, year and State/UT-wise along with the amount sanctioned for the same; (c) the number of persons enrolled in the de-addiction centres/integrated and Rehabilitation Centres for Addicts each year during the said period, State/UT-wise; and (d) whether the Government proposes to set up more integrated and Rehabilitation Centres for addicts in the State of Karnataka in 2019 and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a): The Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment provides financial assistance under the "Central Sector Scheme of Assistance for Prevention of Alcoholism and Substance (Drugs) Abuse" to Non-Governmental organizations, Panchayati Raj Institutions, Urban Local Bodies etc. for running and maintenance of Integrated Rehabilitation Centres for Addicts (IRCAs) on the basis of recommendation of the State Governments/UTs.  The State/UT-wise details of the de-addiction centres/Integrated and Rehabilitation Centres (IRCs) supported by this Ministry is given in Annexure-I.(b): There is no provision for amount to be sanctioned under the said scheme separately for alcoholics-anonymous or for assistance for prevention of alcoholism sessions conducted.(c): A statement indicating State/UT-wise number of persons enrolled in IRCAs during the said period is given in the Annexure-II.(d): The number of de-addiction centres established in a State/UT depends on the number of proposals received from the State Government/UT Administration and their completeness in all respects as per the norms of the scheme.****
