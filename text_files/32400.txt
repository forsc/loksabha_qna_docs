                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3832






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Beej Gram Yojana




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Beej Gram Yojana implemented in the country particularly in Rajasthan during the last two years and the current year; (b) the details of the funds allocated and utilized in various States of the country, State-wise including Rajasthan during the said period; (c) whether National Seeds Conference had been organized to help farmers during the time of sowing during the last three years in view of the changing scenario of the country; (d) if so, the details of the issues discussed in the conference and the outcome thereof; and (e) the intention of each State Government particularly Rajasthan in this regard and the outcome of such conferences?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Govt. of India is implementing Seed Village Programme (Beej Gram Yojana)since 2014-15 to upgrade the quality of farmer’s saved seeds. Under this programme financial assistance for distribution of foundation/certified seeds at 50% of seed cost for cereal crops and 60% for pulses, oilseeds, fodder and green manure crops is available for up to one acre per farmer. The state-wise details (including Rajasthan) of fund released, utilized and details of physical achievements in last two years and in current year is placed at Annexure-I & II.(c) to (e):	The Government of India organizes Zonal Seed Review Meeting twice a year before onset of each sowing season i.e. Kharif & Rabi. In these meetings, Department of Agriculture of different States/UTs, National & State Level Seed Producing Agencies, Seed Certification Agencies were called to assess the requirement and availability of seed across the country prior to every season, to ensure timely availability of seed to farmers.All the States/UTs (including Rajasthan) actively participate and share information in these meetings. Based on the information received from different stakeholders, seed requirement and availability on all India basis is assessed, which in last three years is given below:- (Quantity in Lakh quintals)Year	Requirement	Availability	Surplus2019-20	387.31	431.01	43.702020-21	443.16	483.66	40.502021-22	465.36	498.83	33.47
