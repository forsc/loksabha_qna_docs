                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3282






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan Pension Scheme




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the salient features of Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan Pension Scheme; (b)the number of beneficiaries of the said scheme in the State of Jharkhand, district-wise; and (c)the details of challenges faced by beneficiaries during digital enrolment, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan (PM-SYM) Yojana provides old age protection in the form of monthly pension. It is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme for providing monthly minimum assured pension of Rs. 3,000/- on attaining the age of 60 years. Enrolment to the Scheme is done through the Common Service Centres, with its network of 3.50 lakh Centres across the country.  In addition eligible persons can also self-enroll through visiting the portal www.maandhan.in. The unorganised workers in the age group of 18-40 years whose monthly income is    Rs. 15,000/- or less and not a member of EPFO/ESIC/NPS and who is not an income tax payer, can join the scheme. Under the scheme, 50% monthly contribution is payable by the beneficiary and equal matching contribution is paid by the Central Government. Life Insurance Corporation of India is functioning as the Pension Fund Manager. The scheme has been made simple and hassle-free.  For the purpose of enrolment, only Aadhar and savings bank/Jandhan account is required. The number of beneficiaries of the PM-SYM scheme in the state of Jharkhand, district wise is given in Annexure-‘A’.*                                                     *******
