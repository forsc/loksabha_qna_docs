                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas in the country, State-wise;(b) the total number of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas in Assam, location-wise;(c) the details of the languages taught as regional language in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas inAssam; and(d) whether 80 per cent students use Assamese language in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya and ifso, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): As on date, 661 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) have been sanctioned in the country.The State/UT-wise details are at Annexure-I.(b) & (c): The location-wise details of the places in the State of Assam along with the details of theregional language being taught in the respective JNVs are at Annexure-II.(d): Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti has informed that 20 out of 27 functional JNVs in the State ofAssam are offering Assamese as the regional language and all the students in these Vidyalayas arelearning Assamese.*****
