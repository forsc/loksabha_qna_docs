                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Crops Damaged by Stray Animals




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the data of crops damaged by stray animals in the country during 2021-22;b)	the details of the crops damaged by stray animals in Bihar during the same period, district -wise; c)	whether the Government has any provision to provide financial assistance for the damaged crops;d)	If so, the details thereof; ande)	If not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????          (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b)	The state governments are primarily responsible for assessment of crops damaged by stray animals.  The Directorate of Animal Husbandry, Government of Bihar has reported that no such data on crop damage is available with the Directorate.(c) to (e) 	Ministry of Environment Forest & Climate Change (MoEF&CC) provide financial assistance to the States/Union Territories under Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Development of Wildlife Habitats, “Project Tiger” and “Project Elephant” for management of wildlife and its habitats including ex-gratia relief for crop damage caused by wild animals as per the cost norms prescribed by the concerned state governments. Under the Revamped Operational Guidelines of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) effective from Kharif 2020, the States may consider providing add-on coverage for crop loss due to attack by notified wild animals wherever the risk is perceived to be substantial and identifiable. The state governments also make provision to compensate farmers for the crop losses as per extant law of the respective states.	 *****
