                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1383






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Villages Without Acccess to Internet Services




Gautam Gambhir














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the total number of villages in the country without access to internet services, State/UT-wise;(b) 	whether the Government is considering any proposal to involve Corporate Social Responsibility(CSR) funds to bridge the digital divide between students in rural and urban areas; and(c)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Internet is currently accessed through mobile wireless 3G/4G technologies and fixed wireline broadband in the country. As per data collected from the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in 2020, it is estimated that 37,439 inhabited villages (as per Census 2011) in the country do not have 3G/ 4G mobile internet coverage. State/UT-wise details are given in Annexure.(b)	& (c) At present no such proposal is under consideration.
