                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Annual Income of Post Offices




Jadon Chandra Sen














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has assessed the reported decline in the annual income earned from the post offices in the country; (b) 	if so, the details and the outcome thereof along with the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the Government propose to open more post offices in the Firozabad district of Uttar Pradesh and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the time by which the said post offices are likely to be opened; and (e) 	whether all the facilities are being provided to customers in the post offices of the said district and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	 Since there is no decline in the annual income earned from the post offices in the country in the last 3 financial years as per details given below, no such assessment was required. However, scheme and product-wise assessment & its performances are regularly assessed by the concerned operating divisions for implementing and changes as required.<pre>(Rs. in Crore)Postal Receipts	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	11511.00	12832.76	13195.68</pre>(b)	 No such assessment was required for overall decline, some traditional schemes like Flat-Rate Parcel, Express Parcel gradually closed. Department of Posts periodically reviews its services and products keeping in view the changing market scenario, customer needs, industry benchmarks etc and takes steps/action to modify service features to improve the postal services and boost the revenue growth of the Department. Technology integration and infrastructure up-gradation is also being done in a systematic manner to increase the efficiency.(c)	No, Sir.(d)	 It does not arise in view of reply to (c) above.(e)	 Yes, Sir. All kinds of Postal Services are available in Firozabad  districts at Firozabad HO viz Mails/ Parcel, Saving Bank/ Saving Certificates, PLI / RPLI, Sukanya Samraddhi Yojna / Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJY)/ Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)/ Atal Pension Yojana (APY), AADHAR  New / Updation, Western Union Money Transfer Service, India Post Payments Bank  Services (IPPB), Aadhar Enabled Payment Service, Cash On Delivery (COD), Book Now Pay Later (BNPL) etc.************
