                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2880






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Revenue from Air India




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total revenue generated from Air India during each year from March, 2018 to March, 2021;(b) the total expenditure incurred towards Air India during March 2020-2021; and(c) the number of routes and countries covered by Air India under Vande Bharat Mission?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) and (b): The total revenue and expenses of Air India Ltd from FY 2017-18 to FY 2019-20 as per audited accounts and for FY 2020-21 as per provisional unaudited accounts is given below:                                                              (Rs in Crores)   FY                   Total Revenue          Total Expenses;2017-18               23,900.50                  29,125.862018-19               26,430.58                  34,905.382019-20               27,546.58                  36,290.172020-21               12,138.77                  18,694.43(Provisional)  (c) : Air India has operated Vande Bharat Mission flights to 76 destinations in 54 countries.
