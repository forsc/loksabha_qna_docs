                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

357






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






PNG Network




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the percentage of families to whom Piped Natural Gas (PNG) is available in comparison to the consumers of LPG in the States of Bihar and Jharkhand;(b) whether the Government has taken/proposed to take any steps to increase the availability of Piped Natural Gas in the States of Jharkhand and Bihar; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) There are 5,400 PNG domestic connections and 164.45 lakh LPG connections in Bihar and the percentage of PNG connections in comparison to LPG consumers is about 0.03%. Jharkhand has no PNG domestic connections as of now and has 51.69 lakh LPG consumers. The percentage of PNG connections to LPG consumers is zero. However, infrastructure has been created for connecting 2,800 consumers with PNG.(b) & (c) PNGRB has authorized 228 Geographical Areas (GAs) for development of CGD Network across country including Bihar and Jharkhand. Out of which, total 15 GAs has been authorized for development of CGD networks in the States of Bihar and Jharkhand. The details are at Annexure.
