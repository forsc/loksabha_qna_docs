                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

982






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Raju Bista
                                                








                                                    Aparupa  Poddar
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS) in the country and if so, the details and the objectives thereof;(b)	the details and the number of sportspersons who have benefitted from the Fund along with the quantum of funds provided to them during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT and sport- wise;(c)	whether the Government has achieved the objectives of the Fund and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether any proposal for approval of Sports Training Centre in the country including Maharashtra, West Bengal, Madhya Pradesh and Karnataka is pending with the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a)	Yes Sir. The Government is running the scheme of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS) with the following objectives:(i)	To provide suitable assistance to outstanding sportspersons now living in indigent circumstances;(ii)	To provide suitable assistance to outstanding sportspersons injured during the period of their training for competitions and also during the competitions, depending on the nature of the injury;(iii)	To provide suitable assistance to sportspersons for medical treatment;(iv)	To provide suitable assistance to promote welfare of sportspersons generally in order to alleviate distress among them and their dependents in indigent circumstances;(v)	To provide assistance to sportspersons living in indigent circumstances for training, procurement for sports equipment, participation in national and international sports events etc.(b)	The details and the number of sportspersons who have benefitted from the Fund along with the quantum of funds provided to them during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT and sport- wise is given at Annexure. (d) Sanction for upgradation/ accreditation of training centres andacademies for various sports disciplines is given under various schemes of the Ministry. It is a continuous process and approvals are given on the basis of proposals received from different states/UTs and other entities from time-to-time as per their technical feasibility. 	****
