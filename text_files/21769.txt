                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2872






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Digital Upskilling of Anganwadi Workers




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has been implementing any programmes to impart digital literacy training to Anganwadi workers;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of training programmes being implemented by the Government?   






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	to(d) Anganwadi Workers are provided job training for 26 working days during which the knowledge, understanding and skills of Anganwadi workers on various Acts, policies, programmes related to women and children, setting up of vibrant Anganwadi Centres, conducting Early Childhood Care and Education activities, health and nutrition care of children and women, Community Mobilisation, Awareness, Advocacy & IEC, Management of AWC and hands on experience through supervised practice to work in Anganwadi Centres are developed. Anganwadi Workers are encouraged for optimal utilization of online training viz. e-Learning which is an interactive, user friendly and self-study platform created to provide access to technical concepts and Electronic Incremental Learning Approach (e-ILA), covering thematic modules on maternal, infant and young child nutrition. ****
