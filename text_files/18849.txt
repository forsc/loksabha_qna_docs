                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

441






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






GI Tag to Mithila Makhana




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       whether there is any proposal to issue GI tag for ‘Makhana’ which is the main produce and  speciality of Mithila region as ‘Mithila Makhana’; and (b)      	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b):   A “Geographical Indication” (GI) tag is granted to a product on receipt of application from eligible applicants (association of persons or producers or any organization or authority) by the Geographical Indications Registry as per the procedure laid out in the Geographical Indications of Goods (Registration and Protection) Rules, 2002.  An application for registration of ‘Bihar Makhana’ was filed by Mithilanchal  Makhana Utpadak Sangh, Purnea, Bihar in the office of GI Registry on 14th August, 2020 and has been taken on record as GI application No. 696. On receipt of application, the GI registry has issued the Formality Check Report on 18th August, 2020 raising certain deficiencies in said application and for its correction with necessary supporting documents. The applicant vide its response dated 28th September, 2020 has made necessary compliance. In addition to making compliance to issues raised in the Formality Check Report, the applicant has amended its application to change the name of proposed GI product from ‘Bihar Makhana’ to ‘Mithila Makhana’. The application has been taken up for processing in accordance with the provisions of the Geographical Indications Rules, 2002, which requires creation of a Consultative Group for recommendation on recognition of the product as ''Geographical Indication product'' and giving an opportunity of opposition.  *****
