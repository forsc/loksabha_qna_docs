                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3575






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Unapproved Genes in BT Cotton Seeds




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether transgenic herbicide / glyphosate tolerant trait genes are used by several BT cotton seed production companies; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the production of BT cotton seeds with unapproved genes is taking place in several States; (d)	if so, the details thereof; (e)	whether the Union Government had set up Field Inspection and Scientific Evaluation Committee;(f)	if so, whether the aforesaid Committee has submitted it preliminary report to the Union Government; (g)	if so, the findings thereof and the action taken thereon; and (h)	if not, the time by which the aforesaid Committee will submit its Report?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICUTLRURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????			                  	   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	In India, only Bt Cotton is approved for commercial cultivation. Few incidences of illegal marketing of Genetically Modified seeds of Herbicide Tolerant (HT) cotton were reported in the States of Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Telangana and Maharashtra. However, no state has reported the production of seed of unapproved Bt gene.(e) to (h):	Department of Biotechnology, Ministry of Science and Technology set up “Field Inspection and Scientific Evaluation Committee (FISEC)” in 2017 for on-the spot inspection of the cotton fields to assess the veracity of spread of unapproved HT cotton. The FISEC submitted its report in May 2018 and made recommendations and action points to be implemented by various central and state government departments and agencies. Short term (1-2 years) and medium term (2-3 years) actions have also been suggested by the committee.DAC&FW has issued advisory to all cotton growing states to control and curb the sale and cultivation of unapproved HT Cotton vis-à-vis its seed production, processing and storage.**********
