                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Small Soil Testing Laboratories




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up small laboratories at the district as well as Tehsil levels in Krishi Vigyan Kendras across the country keeping in view the need for testing the quality of soil of each farm and whether the Government has received proposals from States in this regard; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of the Krishi Vigyan Kendras and small laboratories set up in Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh; and(d) 	the details of funds allocated and utilized for better functioning of Krishi Vigyan Kendras during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	 Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) has set up small laboratories in 658 Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs)  across the country.  Details are given at Annexure I.  Apart from KVKs, state-wise details of small laboratories sanctioned to states is given at Annexure II. (c):	Mini soil testing kits are available in 47 KVKs in Madhya Pradesh and in 23 KVKs in Chhattisgarh  as per the details given below:  Madhya Pradesh :  KVKs at  Sagar, Balaghat, Betul, Chhatarpur, Chhindwara, Damoh, Dindori, Harda, Katni, Mandla, Narsinghpur, Panna, Rewa, Shahdol, Sidhi, Tikamgarh, Umaria, Bhopal, Burhanpur, Ratlam, Satna, Sehore, Seoni, Ashoknagar, Barwani, Dewas, Dhar, Guna, Gwalior, Indore, Jabalpur, Jhabua, Khandwa,Khargone, Lahar(Bhind),  Morena, Neemuch, Raisen, Rajgarh, Shajapur, Sheopur, Shivpuri, Ujjain, Datia, Mandsaur, Anuppur, Hoshangabad.Chhattisgarh: KVKs at  Anjora Durg, Bemetara, Balrampur, Bastar, Bhatapara, Bijapur, Bilaspur, Dantewada, Dhamtari, Durg II, Gariyaband,  Janjgri-Champa, Kanker, Kawardha, Korea, Mahasamund,  Mungeli,  Narayanpur, Raigarh, Rajnandgaon, Korba, Raipur, Jashpur. (d):	The details of funds allocated and utilized for   Krishi Vigyan Kendras during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise is at Annexure-III.
