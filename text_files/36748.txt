                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

355






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Proposal from Jharkhand to Include more Tourism Circuits/Parks




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Tourism Circuits, Tourism Parks and Rural     Tourism Clusters identified by the Government in each State/Union Territory particularly in Jharkhand; (b)  	whether the Government has received requests/ proposals from State Governments of Jharkhand for inclusion of more such Tourism Circuits/ Parks and Clusters and if so, the details thereof and the response thereto; (c)  	whether the Government proposes to include Jharkhand as part of the International Tourism Circuit in the country to promote the culture of the States and if so, the details thereof; and (d)  	the other steps taken by the Government to popularise Tourist Circuits in Jharkhand?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                                   (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.********* STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *355 ANSWERED ON 28.03.2022 REGARDING PROPOSAL FROM JHARKHAND TO INCLUDE MORE TOURISM CIRCUITS/PARKS  (a) to (d):  The Ministry of Tourism under its  ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘Pilgrimage  Rejuvenation and  Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’  schemes  provides  financial assistance  with focus on  development of  tourism  infrastructure in the  country including Jharkhand. The projects are identified and sanctioned in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations, subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc. The  Ministry of Tourism has  revamped its  Swadesh Darshan  scheme  for  sanctioning of  new projects in  the country following  a  destination  and tourist  centric  approach.   The details of the projects sanctioned in ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme under different themes and ‘PRASHAD’ scheme including Jharkhand is at Annexure I.  The  Ministry of  Tourism  promotes  various  tourism  destinations  and products  including  circuits holistically through  its  website, social  media  platforms, events, publicity  campaigns, webinar etc.  in the international and  domestic  markets. Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) has informed that its Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) focuses on holistic development of clusters and to ensure an optimum level of development, twenty one components have been suggested as desirable for the Cluster development. Among the 21 components, Tourism promotion is one of the major component under which States/UTs has proposed investment both under CGF and Convergence for different activities like beautification of tourist sites, infrastructure development at tourist places, Pilgrimage Development for Tourism, construction of Tourism parks, Heritage development, etc. A total of 103 clusters covering 32 States/UTs (including Jharkhand) have proposed an investment of Rs. 492.35 crore both under CGF and Convergence under Tourism Promotion. The cluster wise investment proposed under Tourism promotion component of SPMRM is at Annexure II. So far the state of Jharkhand is concerned, out of the allocated 15 clusters under SPMRM, eight clusters have proposed tourism development components with an investment of Rs.14.38 cr. No requests/ proposals from state governments of Jharkhand has been received for inclusion of more such tourism circuits parks and clusters under SPMRM.*******
