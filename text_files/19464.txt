                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1010






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Employment Exchange in Kheda, Gujarat




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of employment exchanges functioning in Kheda, Gujarat;(b)	the number of unemployed persons male and female separately registered in the employment exchanges during the last three years and the current year in Kheda, Gujarat;(c)	the number of persons including women provided employment through the employment exchanges during the said period in Kheda, Gujarat;(d)	whether all registered companies including domestic and Multinational Companies source their manpower requirement from Employment Exchanges and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the current status of upgradation/ modernisation of employment exchanges across the country along with the funds allocated, released and utilised for the purpose during the said period, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):  There is one employment exchange functioning in Kheda, Gujarat. The number of job seekers, all of whom may not necessarily be unemployed, registered in the employment exchange in Kheda, Gujarat during the last three years and current year is given below:Year	  No. of  Jobseekers registered	Male	Female2017	13089	13582018	11266	10792019	12963	18402020(upto August)	3417	536Source: Government of Gujarat(c) & (d): As per the information received from the State Government, the number of persons including women placed through employment exchange during said period in Kheda, Gujarat is 30191. It is not mandatory for the companies to hire their manpower through Employment Exchanges.(e):  The Ministry is implementing the National Career Service (NCS) Project for transformation of the National Employment Service to provide a variety of employment related services like job matching, career counselling, vocational guidance, information on skill development courses, etc. These services are available online on the National Career Service Portal (www.ncs.gov.in). 	The NCS Project also envisages setting up of Model Career Centres (MCCs) in collaboration with States and other institutions of repute to deliver employment services. As of now, 207 Model Career Centres have been approved (including 07 on Non-funded basis). The Central Government provides financial assistance to the States for establishment of MCCs based on the scheme guidelines. These Model Career Centres can be replicated by the States from their own resources. The State/UT-wise detail of fund approved, released and utilised is at Annexure-I.	In addition, the NCS project has a component of interlinking of employment exchanges with NCS, under which funding is provided to States for upgradation of infrastructures in employment exchanges. Under this component, funds are provided to States/UTs for interlinking of employment exchanges with the NCS portal. The Government provides financial assistance based on the proposals received from States. The State/UT-wise detail of fund approved, released and utilized is given at Annexure-II. *****
