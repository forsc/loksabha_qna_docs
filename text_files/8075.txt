                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Connecting of SH with NH




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any demand or proposal to connect the 110 km long Ramanattukara Palakkad State Highway (SH) via Calicut Airport and Malappuram between National Highway (NH)-66 and NH-544 by a new National Highway and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government has carried out any study regarding the volume of traffic and the congestion on this stretch and if so, the details and the outcome thereof; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor along with the remedial measures being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)	 The stretch of road from Ramanattukara near Kozhikode on NH 66 connecting NH 544 at Palakkad via Kozhikode Airport, and Malappuram in the State of Kerala is a declared National Highway (NH) No. 966.   The work for development of this stretch is included in the Bharatmala Pariyojana and the Detailed Project Report (DPR) work has been awarded to the consultant by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI).  *****
