                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

414






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin




Durga Prasad Rao Balli













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken adequate steps to ensure that duplication of addresses do not take place under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) scheme;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the total number of beneficiaries under this scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh as on date, district-wise; and(d)	the total amount utilized under the scheme during the last financial year, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)a) & b)	The steps taken to ensure the de-duplication in identification of beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) are as follows:i.	The universe of eligible beneficiaries under PMAY (G) include all the houseless and households living in zero, one or two room kucha houses as per SECC data, subject to the exclusion process based on 13 deprivation parameters. ii.	This Universe, thus, prepared is circulated among Gram Sabhas for verification.iii.	Before issue of Sanction Order, the BDO or any block level official authorized by the State Government shall capture through the mobile application “AwaasApp” the geo-referenced photograph of the beneficiary in front of the house where the beneficiary is living, followed by a geo-tagged photograph of the land on which the beneficiary proposes to construct the house and upload it on AwaasSoft.c)	The total number of beneficiaries under this scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh as per Permanent Wait List (PWL) after being verified by Gram Sabha is 2,41,368. Out of these, the Ministry has already allocated targets of 1,70,912 households to the State from financial year 2016-17 till 2019-20. District-wise details as given in Annexure-I.d)	The amount of funds utilized under PMAY-G scheme during the last financial year is Rs. 46,746.99 crore.
