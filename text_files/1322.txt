                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1320






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Textile and Clothing Sector




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the textile and clothing sector is witnessing signs of recovery and is expected to perform better in export and domestic markets this year vis-à-vis pervious years, if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether issue of the rise in imports is a matter concern for the industry; and(c)	if so, the steps proposed to be taken up by Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	As per DGCIS data, exports of textile & apparel including handicraft have increased by 3% from US$ 39,239 mn in 2017-18 to US$ 40,431 mn in 2018-19.(b) & (c):	As per DGCIS data, imports of textile & apparel including handicraft increased by 3.2% from US$ 7,319 mn in 2017-18 to US$ 7,555 mn in 2018-19. To contain increase in imports of textile and apparel, Government increased Basic Customs Duty on 504 lines comprising apparel, carpets, fabric, madeups and others from 10% to 20%. ****
