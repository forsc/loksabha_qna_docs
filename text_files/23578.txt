                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4781






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Mines Operating under HCL




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the location-wise details of the number of the mines operational under Hindustan Copper Limited (HCL);(b)	the yearly total production of the copper from these mines along with the price thereof;(c)	the details of the quantum of profit accrued, mine-wise and year-wise;(d)	 whether any copper plant has been established at Malanjkhand of Balaghat district in Madhya Pradesh;(e)	if so, the time since when it was established and the cost thereof;(f)	the details of the products therein; and(g)	the details of the profit accrued therefrom?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a): The location-wise details of the number of mines operational under Hindustan Copper Limited (HCL) is furnished below:-Location	Name of Mine	StatusDistrict : Singhbhum East State : Jharkhand	Surda	Currently not operational due to non-renewal of mining lease. 	Kendadih	Mine is in development stage, production will start from FY-2021-22.District : JhunjhunuState :Rajasthan	Khetri	Currently operational.	Kolihan	Currently operational.District : BalaghatState :Madhya Pradesh	Malanjkhand	Currently operational. (b): The details of mine wise copper Ore and MIC production and sale price is tabulated below:Mine	FY 2019-20 	Ore (tonnes)	MIC (tonnes) 	Price of MIC (Rs./tonne)*Surda	2,45,369	2065	 4,01,163Kendadih	43,230		Khetri	6,80,005	8732	Kolihan	4,39,518		Malanjkhand	25,44,472	15705	Total	3967594	26502	* The price of MIC varies as per LME and auction prices  (c): The mine-wise yearly details of the quantum of profit accrued is as below: Location	Name of Mines	Mine wise Operating Profit  in FY-2019-20 (Rs. in Crore)District : Singhbhum EastState JharkhandUnder Indian Copper Complex (ICC) Unit	Surda	19.37	Kendadih	Mine is in development stage, production will start from FY-2021-22.District : JhunjhunuState :RajasthanUnder Khetri Copper Complex (KCC) Unit	Khetri	49.81	Kolihan	District : BalaghatState :Madhya Pradesh Under Malanjkhand   Copper Project (MCP) Unit	Malanjkhand	160.88 (d): In Malanjkhand, Copper ore beneficiation plant (Concentrator Plant) has been established since beginning of mining operations in 1982. No copper Smelter & refinery plant is there. In 2017, one copper ore tailing beneficiation plant was installed to recover valuable metals and minerals from the copper ore tails (a waste generated during the copper concentrate production.) (e): Copper ore beneficiation plant (Concentrator Plant) has been established since beginning of mining operations in 1982. Installation of the copper ore tailing beneficiation plant started from April 2017 and the party finally failed to commission the plant and the contract was terminated from 31st August 2020 with efflux of time. The cost of the plant was Rs.201.83 Crore.(f) & (g): Product of the Copper ore beneficiation plant (Concentrator Plant)  is MIC and the details has been provided in part (b) and part (c) of the reply. Commissioning of copper ore tailing beneficiation plant was not successful. No profit accrued as the party (technology provider) was not able to commission the plant.*****
