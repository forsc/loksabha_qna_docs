                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3948






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Revival of Closed Mines




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of mines in the country State/UT-wise including Maharashtra; (b)	the number of sick and closed mines in the country including Maharashtra out of these along with the workers affected as a result thereof, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government proposes to revive some closed/sick mines in the country especially in Maharashtra; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken/being taken to revive the said mines 	and to rehabilitate the affected workers especially in Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) & (b): As per the information provided by Indian Bureau of Mines (a subordinate office of Ministry of Mines), the details of mines for major minerals (working and non-working) as on 01.04.2019 in the country State/UT-wise including Maharashtra is given at Annexure. The information on workers affected on account of non-working of mines is not maintained centrally in the Ministry. (c) & (d): The State governments are advised to take remedial action for revival of non-working mines. The matter is exclusively in the jurisdiction of the State Government.***** ANNEXURE-PQ 3948       Number of Working and Non-Working Mines in the Country as on 01.04.2019Sl. no.
