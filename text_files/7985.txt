                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

151






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Regularization of Unauthorized Colonies




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken any decision to regularize the unauthorized colonies of Delhi and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government is aware that the regularization of unauthorized colonies will give a message that the encroachment of Government land will be regularized in future;(c)	whether the Government has taken any steps to check the encroachments in the refugee colonies of Delhi and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details regarding exact area in Sq. yards/Sq. metres of land sanctioned to the allottees of various colonies in New Delhi and the present possession with allottees?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the Sabha.                               *****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *151 for 28.11.2019 REGARDING REGULARIZATION OF UNAUTHORIZED COLONIES(a):	A Committee headed by the Lieutenant Governor of Delhi, was constituted by Government of India to recommend the process for conferment or recognition of rights of ownership or transfer or mortgage of property and thereby creating opportunity for redevelopment of such areas in a planned manner. Based on the Report of the Committee, Regulations for conferring or recognising ownership or mortgage or transfer rights to the residents of unauthorised colonies have been notified by Delhi Development Authority (DDA) on 29th October, 2019, which are available in detailathttps://dda.org.in/tendernotices_docs/may2018/ATT0000431102019.pdf. (b):	Keeping in view of the fact that over 40 lakh people reside in 1,731 unauthorized colonies identified in Delhi, Government has considered it expedient to recognise and confer rights of ownership or transfer or mortgage to the residents of these colonies as a onetime special measure. As regards encroachment on Government or public land,the respective Government or land managing agencies/ authorities have the responsibility to prevent and evict encroachments.(c) & (d): Government has constituted a Special Task Force vide Notification No.S.O.1243 (E) dated 08.03.2019 under the chairmanship of Vice Chairman, DDA to comprehensively address the issues of encroachments on the public land and violations/enforcement of Building Bye-Laws and Master Plan for Delhi (MPD) 2021.  The Task Force includes officers from Urban Local Bodies of Delhi, Delhi Government, Delhi Police, National Highway Authority of India, Archaeological  Survey of India, etc..This Notification is available at https://dda.org.in/stf/Doc/STF_Gazette_Notification.PDFGovernment of India had allotted about 37,000 plots of land in various colonies as well as constructed 11,086 C-type tenements in twelve colonies to rehabilitate persons displaced from erstwhile West Pakistan and East Pakistan. The C-type tenements were constructed in an area of nearly 28 to 32 sq.yds. and plots were allotted in sizes from 55 sq.yds. to 1,866 sq.yds.
