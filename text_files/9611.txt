                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3034






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






PARIVESH




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the aims and objectives of the PARIVESH portal initiated by the Government;(b)	the total number of clearances given till date since the portal was launched;(c)	the total number of proposals cleared and other pending proposals with the Government from the State of Andhra Pradesh; and (d)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	The aims and objectives of the PARIVESH portal initiated by the Government, inter alia, include online submission, monitoring and management of proposals submitted by Project Proponents to the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC), Government of India as well as to the State Level Environmental Impact Assessment Authorities (SEIAA) to seek various types of clearances (e.g. Environment, Forest, Wildlife and Coastal Regulation Zone Clearances) from Central, State and District-level authorities. It automates the entire process of submitting the application and tracking the status of such proposals at each stage of the processing.(b) The portal was launched at Central level on 10th August, 2018 and at State level on 16th August, 2019. The details of the total number of clearances given, till date, since the portal was launched are as follows:Description	Environmental Clearances issuedCenter Level	333State/UT	381Total	714(c)& (d) As per the available information on PARIVESH, the Government has accorded environment clearance (EC)to 10 project proposals and terms of reference (ToR) to 40 project proposals from the State of Andhra Pradesh. Accordingly, 50 proposals from the State of Andhra Pradesh have been cleared since the launch of the Portal. Further, 16 proposals for EC and 14 proposals for ToR in respect of State of Andhra Pradeshare at various stages of appraisal in  MoEF&CC under Environment Impact Assessment Notification, 2006 and its amendments from time to time.*****
