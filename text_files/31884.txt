                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

312






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Air, Water and Soil Contamination




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the recent study conducted by organisations working for clean environment and reduce pollution which has revealed far high air, water and soil contamination due to toxic substances and their unscientific disposal and handling of fly ash generated by the Thermal Power Generation Plants operating in the country; and(b)	if so, the details of data collected, plant-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI. BHUPENDER YADAV)(a) and (b):- A statement is laid on the Table of the House.***STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*312 ON AIR, WATER AND SOIL CONTAMINATION(a) & (b) Regarding air, water and soil contamination from unscientific disposal and handling of fly ash generated by Thermal Power Plants, there is no report of any recent study conducted by organisations with the Government.Presently, 179 thermal power plants in the country have generated 222.8 million tonnes of fly ash and 205.1 million tonnes have been utilized in various purposes during the year 2020-21. The current fly ash utilization is 92% in FY 2020-21.The plant-wise fly ash generation and utilization in the country is enclosed as Annexure-1. ***
