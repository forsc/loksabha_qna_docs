                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2642






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Mega Food Parks




Umesh G. Jadhav













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    B. Y. Raghavendra
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of mega food parks set up in the country during the last three years in Karnataka; (b)	whether the government is considering to set up mega food parks in each district of the country; (c)	if so, the details thereof, along with the progress made thereon in Karnataka, park-wise; (d)	the number of mega food parks proposed to be set up by the Government in Karnataka; and (e)	the steps that were taken by the Government for better marketing of food items including traditional and locally popular food items through such food parks? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d): Ministry of Food Processing Industries has been implementing Mega Food Park Scheme (MFPS) to create modern infrastructure for the food processing in the country. Ministry does not establish Mega Food Parks on its own. The proposals under the Scheme for setting up of Mega Food Parks are invited through Expression of Interest (EoI) from time to time. Selection of suitable proposals for these projects is done as per the Scheme guidelines.    So far Ministry has accorded final approval to 39 Mega Food Park projects and in-principle approval to 2 Mega Food Parks including 2 projects in the state of Karnataka. Presently, there is no further proposal under consideration with Ministry for setting up of Mega Food Park in the state of Karnataka. Details of Mega Food Park Projects approved by the Ministry including two projects in the state of Karnataka is at Annexure.  (e):	Ministry does not provide financial support for marketing of food items including traditional and local popular food items under Mega Food Park scheme. The scheme provides State of Art infrastructure for setting up modern food processing industries for food items including traditional and locally popular foods based on the cluster approach which offers better marketing avenues for the food products.  *****
