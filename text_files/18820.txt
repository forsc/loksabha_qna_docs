                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Industrially Backward Districts




Subhas Sarkar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has specified Bankura of West Bengal as ‘A’ Category and Purulia as ‘B‘ Category industrially backward districts and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the details of investment subsidy, sales tax exemption, etc. provided by the Government for setting up units in these areas so far; (c) 	the details of initiatives taken by the Government so far for development of industrial estates and related infrastructure in these areas; and(d) 	the details and the number of industries/ MSMEs which have been set up in these districts in the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a)to(c):    Sir, Government of West Bengal has classified developed areas and backward areas into  A, B, C, D and E zones ,under the scheme of ‘Banglashree for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises. Districts of Bankura and Purulia of West Bengal fall under Zone D.  Please refer Gazette Notification No. 1543/MSMET-18011/1/2020 dated 22nd July, 2020 of Government of West Bengal. An eligible micro or small enterprise, located in Zone D, is entitled to State Capital Investment subsidy as mentioned in above mentioned Gazette Notification.(d)  :      	A list of total MSMEs registered in the last three years in Bankura and Purulia is annexed.ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (d) OF THE  LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 243 FOR ANSWER ON 3RD FEBRUARY, 2021.
