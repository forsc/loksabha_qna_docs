                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3647






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Impact of Rain and Floods




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether many States have been badly affected by rain and flood during the last three years;(b) if so, the names of district of each and every State badly affected by flood during the above mentioned period;(c) whether any central team has visited these affected areas;(d) if so, the details of the loss of lives and property, crops, livestock, property etc. in each State as reported by this team; and(e) the financial aid provided by the Government to each State for relief and rehabilitation till date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (e):  Various States in the country have been affected by natural calamities, including rains and floods, during last three years. District-wise information of each State affected by rains and flood is not maintained centrally by this Ministry. State-wise details of the losses of lives, property and loss of  crops  due  to hydro meteorological  calamities including flood -2-L.S.US.Q. NO.3647 FOR 10.08.2021during the last three years, as reported by the State Governments/ Union Territories, are at Annexure-I.  There are institutional mechanisms at the National, State and district level for effective management of natural disasters. The primary responsibility of disaster management, including undertaking disbursal of relief to affected people, rests with the State Governments.  For providing relief to the affected people in case of 12 notified natural disasters including flood, State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) is placed at the disposal of the State Governments.  Additional financial assistance is provided from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF), as per laid down procedure in case of disaster of ‘severe nature’, which includes an on the spot assessment of damages by an Inter-Ministerial Central Team (IMCT). IMCTs had visited the affected States for on the spot assessment of damages in the aftermath of floods.State-wise details of allocation and releases from SDRF/ State Disaster Risk Management Fund (SDRMF) [after recommendations of 15th Finance Commission, which includes SDRF and State Disaster Mitigation Fund (SDMF)] and NDRF during the last three years and current year are given in Annexure-II.*****
