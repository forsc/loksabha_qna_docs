                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Hate Crime against Sikh Community




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the frequent occurrences of hate motivated crimes against members of the Sikh community in the United States and if so, the details thereof;(b)  	whether the Government is aware that as per the data released by the FBI in 2018 Sikhs are the most targeted religious groups in the United States and if so, the details thereof;(c)  	whether the Government has held discussions with officials in the United States regarding these unfortunate incidents; and(d)  	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	The Uniform Crime Reporting (UCR) Program’s annual Hate Crime Statistics report, released by the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), U.S. Department of Justice regarding anti-Sikh hate crime incidents is tabulated below: <pre>YearTotal Hate crimes (motivated by bias based on religion)Anti-Sikh hate crime incidents20181,4196020191,5214920201,24489</pre>(b)	On the basis of the Uniform Crime Reporting Program’s annual Hate Crime Statistics report released by FBI in 2018, Sikhs were not the most targeted religious groups in USA, though the incidents targeting them are a matter of serious concern.  (c) & (d)	The United States of America is home to a vibrant Indian community of 4.2 million, which has been a catalyst in cementing closer ties between the two countries. Instances of hate crime against Indian nationals that came to the notice of the Government were taken up appropriately with the U.S. authorities. ***
