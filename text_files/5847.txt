                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5381






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Details of Irrigation Projects




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State-wise details of the major irrigation projects sanctioned during the current five year plan; (b) whether funds have been sanctioned for implementation of these irrigation projects during this period; and (c) if so, the funds allocated to each project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)   As per information provided by Central Water Commission (CWC), the State-wise details of Major Irrigation projects accepted by Advisory Committee of DoWR, RD & GR on Irrigation, Flood control & Multipurpose Projects, Ministry of Jal Shakti since April, 2017 are given at Annexure.(b) & (c) Water Resources Projects are planned, funded, executed and maintained by the State Governments themselves as per their own resources and priority. In order to supplement their efforts, Government of India provides technical and financial assistance to State Governments to encourage sustainable development and efficient management of water resources through various schemes and Programmes such as Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP) etc. under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY). The projects are included for Central Assistance (CA) under PMKSY-AIBP as per the guidelines of the scheme, availability of funds, scope of scheme etc.Amongst, the projects accepted by the Advisory Committee of DoWR, RD & GR since April, 2017, three projects namely, Polavaram Irrigation Project, Jigaon Irrigation project and Shahpurkandi Dam project are receiving CA. The funds are released on receipt of eligible proposals from the concerned State Government.The Polavaram Project has been declared as a National Project as per Section 90 of AP Reorganization Act, 2014. Funds amounting to Rs 6764.16 crore have been released for this project after 01.04.2014.The Jigaon irrigation Project is one of the projects getting CA under the Special Package to Maharashtra to address agrarian distress in Vidarbha, Marathwada and other chronically drought prone areas of Rest of Maharashtra.  Balance cost of the project as on 1.4.2018 is Rs.4769.85 crore (CA component - Rs.1331.15 crore). During 2018-19, CA of Rs 262.025 crore has been released for the project. Further, in December, 2018, the Union Cabinet has approved the “Implementation of  Shahpurkandi Dam (National Project) on River Ravi in Punjab State” with an estimated cost of Rs.2715.70 crore (Irrigation Component – Rs.564.63 crore).  CA component is Rs.485.38 crore out of which CA of            Rs 61.615 crore has been sanctioned for the project during the current year.*****AnnexureAnnexure as referred to in reply to part (a) of Unstarred Question.
