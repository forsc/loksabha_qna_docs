                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






PRASAD Scheme in Bihar




Giridhari Yadav













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spirituality Augmentation Drive (PRASAD) scheme of Central Government has been implemented in Bihar; (b) 	if so, the location-wise details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government of Bihar has submitted any proposal under the PRASAD scheme; (d) 	if so, the location-wise details along with the actions taken by the Government so far in this regard and the outcome thereof; and (e) 	the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Details of Project approved by the Ministry of Tourism under the scheme “Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive” (PRASHAD) in Bihar is as follows:(i)	“Development of basic facilities at Vishnupad temple, Gaya, Bihar”, sanctioned in 2014-15 at the cost of Rs.4.27 Crore. The amount Rs.2.91 Crore has been released so far.(ii)	“Development at Patna Sahib” sanctioned in 2015-16 at the cost of Rs.41.54 Crore. The amount Rs.33.23 Crore has been released so far.(c):	No new proposal has been received from the State Government of Bihar under the scheme.(d) & (e): Does not arise.*******
