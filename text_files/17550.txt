                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1306






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Central Taxes




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government allocates the share of central taxes for Central Budget schemes to all the states;(b)	if so, the details of share of central taxes allocated to the State Governments during the last three years, State-wise;(c)	the names of heads and the quantum of fund allocated to Rajasthan during this financial year; and (d)	the details of fund allocated for this purpose, plan-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                                THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a)	Government of India allocates and releases share of Central Taxes and Duties to States on the basis of accepted recommendations of the Finance Commission. State Governments use these releases for meeting their requirements in respect of all expenditures including on Centrally Sponsored Schemes.(b)	State-wise details of share in Central Taxes and Duties allocated to the State Governments and released during last three years are enclosed as ‘Annexure-I’.(c)	Head-wise allocation of States’ share in Central Taxes and Duties for Rajasthan for financial year 2020-21 is enclosed as ‘Annexure-II’. (d)	After the abolition of plan-non plan distinction in GoI Budget in 2017-18, the plan-wise details are the same as has been provided in part (c).
