                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2805






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Funding to Culture Promoting Seminars




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any scheme to fund the seminars/ workshops which promote culture at different forums;(b)	if so, the details of such schemes;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of its impact on the cultural tourism of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)&(b)Yes sir. The Ministry of Culture administers a scheme by the name of Cultural Function and Production Grant Scheme (CFPGS) under which financial assistance is provided to ‘not-for-profit’ organizations, NGOs, Societies, Trusts and Universities for organising Seminars, Workshops Conference, Research, Festivals, Exhibitions, Symposia, Production of Dance, Drama-Theatre, Music etc. and small research projects on different aspects of Indian Culture. 	The quantum of financial assistance under Cultural Function and Production Grant Scheme (CFPGS) is restricted to 75% of the expenditure, subject to a maximum of Rs. 5.00 lakh per project.  However, the Ministry may in exceptional circumstances, increase the assistance to any project of outstanding merit and relevance uptoRs. 20.00 lakhs with the approval of Hon’ble Minister of Culture. The Grant is released in two instalments of 75% and 25%. The scheme is open throughout the year.(c)Does not arise.(d)No formal study has been instituted to assess its impact on cultural tourism of the country.****
