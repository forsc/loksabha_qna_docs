                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3488






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Environmental Clearance




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of trees permitted to be cut for the construction of road to provide facility to Mine No. 13 of the National Mineral Development Corporation Limited (NMDC) in Bacheli/Dantewada area of Chhattisgarh during the last three years; and(b)	the policy of the Ministry with regard to reforestation after cutting of trees and whether the NMDC in complying with it and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	25,400 number of trees.(b)	Ministry of Steel does not have its own policy for reforestation. The conditions/ instructions for compensatory afforestation issued in the Stage-I and II Forest Clearance under FC Act 1980 are complied by NMDC. Compensatory Afforestation over the degraded forest land, twice in extent to the area of forest land proposed to be diverted, is to be raised within a period of three years with effect from the date of issue of Stage-II clearance and maintained thereafter in accordance with the approved plan, in consultation with the State Forest Department at the cost of the user agency. NMDC has already deposited Rs 41,70,27,908 (Rs Forty One Crores Seventy Lakhs Twenty Seven Thousand Nine Hundred Eight Only ) in CAMPA Account for the purpose of compensatory afforestation.********
