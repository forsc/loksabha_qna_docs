                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2785






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Import Duty on Apple




Suresh Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has increased the import duty on apple keeping in view the problems being faced by the apple growers in Shimla;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government proposes to mix 5% apple juice in all the beverages; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	& (b) The basic customs duty on fresh apple is 50%. Recently, there has been no increase on Most Favoured Nation (MFN) rate of duty on fresh apple. However, in June, 2019, the Government has imposed additional duty of 20% (above MFN rate) on goods originating or exported from the United States of America. (c)	& (d) No Sir. There is no proposal at this stage to mix 5% apple juice in all the beverages. ****
