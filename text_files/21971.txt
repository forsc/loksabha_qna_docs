                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2999






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Use of Fuel Oil




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has accorded the approval regarding the use of Fuel oil as a fuel in vehicles and if so, the ratio of mixture thereof;(b) if not, the reasons for hundred per cent increase in its import; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROELUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c)	Government has not accorded any approval for use of fuel oil in vehicles. The details of import of fuel oil by PSU/Private companies during the last three years including current year are as given below: Fuel Oil Import                                                      ( in ThousandMetric Tonne) Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21 ( April-January) (P)Private	1097	1195	4461	4807PSU/JV	116	225	122	81Grand Total	1213	1420	4583	4888                Source : PPAC;  (P)- Provisional.From the above it is evident that the reason for increase in import of fuel oil is on account of import by Private Companies.
