                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4006






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Diverse Traditions of Tribes




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to formulate any scheme for mutual cooperation of the tribal people of their status so that they may learn from one another and be familiar with each other;(b) if so, the number of tribal groups, particularly students from this community who have gained such an experience;(c) if not, whether the Government is likely to consider engaging local Non-Governmental Organisations to become aware of their enriched way of living; and(d) whether the Government has published any encyclopedia of tribal life and their enriched cultural traditions and values; if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): Ministry of Tribal Affairs under the scheme ''Support to Tribal Research Institutes’ inter alia provides funds to States/UTs for organizing exchange visits of representatives of   Tribal communities/Group.   Further, TRIFED organizes Adi Mahotsava under ‘Tribal Festival, Research, Information and Mass Education’ scheme across states where tribal artisans across the country participate in national and state-level festivals. The details of above programmes are at Annexure-I . In addition to this, close interaction of tribal population at pan India level to learn and know from each other''s life also takes place through following initiatives.(i).  For the republic day celebrations, 3 tribal guests from each state are invited for one week for the  Republic day celebrations and are taken for tourist places in and around the city. (ii). Under various scholarships scheme implemented by the Ministry, ST students from one state can study across the country and abroad. The schemes help them to interact closely with students from other States & within the States. Ministry has also initiated Tribal Talent Pool program which aims at the development of ST scholars by providing an environment of learning, support, contribution, and recognition and to enable them to participate in various research and evaluation activities undertaken by MoTA at Central and State levels Under this program, Ministry organizes interactive workshops for National Fellowship Scholars.(iii).  Annual Cultural and sports meet are organized for EMRS students, where students from across the country participate. EMRS students are also invited for the Republic Day program.                 However due to covid pandemic, during last 2 years States have been asked to follow covid protocol and prevailing guidelines issued by Central and State Governments while organizing any such programs and exchange visits.  (d): Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides funds to State Governments under the scheme ‘Support to TRIs’ for preparing and publishing encyclopedias on tribal life in India and their rich cultural traditions. Details of encyclopedias published by States are at Annexure-II. The details of other such documents, video and photographs depicting tribal life in India and their rich cultural traditions can also be viewed on repository.tribal.gov.in.
