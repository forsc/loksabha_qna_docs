                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2597






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Applications Received in POPSK




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of applications received at Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK) in the State of Bihar since 2017 and at the Chapra Post Office Passport Seva Kendra in particular;(b)	the number of passports issued out of them against these application requests so received; (c)	whether there is any proposal to open new Passport Seva Kendras/Post Office Passport Seva Kendras in the State of Bihar; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN] (a)	The total number of passport applications received at Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK) in the State of Bihar since 2017 upto 31st October, 2019 is 2,04,486 (including 10,457 applications received at POPSK, Chapra). (b)	The total number of passports issued against these passport applications till 31st October, 2019 is 1,81,087.(c) & (d)	There are 2 PSKs and 33 POPSKs functioning in the State of Bihar. The list is at Annexure – I. The opening of new Passport Seva Kendras/Post Office Passport Seva Kendras in various States in the country is an on-going activity and depends on various factors, including distance from the existing PSK/POPSKs and volume of applications from a particular region.***
