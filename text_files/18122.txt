                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1887






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Collapse of Bridge in Kerala




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether four beams with length of 43 metres of under construction bridge connecting National Highway at Thalassery in Kerala collapsed on 26 August, 2020;(b)     if so, the details thereof;(c)   whether the Government has set up any technical team to enquire into the said accident; and(d)     if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND     HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)](a) to (d)        4 Girders of the Major Bridge (km 174+150) at Mahe bypass on NH-66 in the State of Kerala collapsed on 26.08.2020. National Institute of Technology (NIT), Calicut investigated the reasons responsible for it.  Regarding the collapse of Major Bridge at Mahe Bypass, NHAI issued Show Cause notices to Contractor, Authority Engineer, Safety Consultant, Design Consultant & IIT Chennai as Proof Consultant (engaged by Contractor). Independent Investigation has been carried out by NIT, Calicut. Further action will be decided based on the received replies to the show cause notices and investigation reports.*****
