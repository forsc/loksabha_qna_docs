                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Laboratory for Checking the Quality of Drinking Water




Rodmal Nagar













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government sets up any laboratory for checking the quality of drinking water; (b)	if so, the number of such laboratories in the country, State-wise; and (c)	the number of total households in the country which do not have access to drinking water as on date, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD  SINGH  PATEL)(a) & (b) As reported by States/UTs, so far 2021 drinking water quality testing laboratories at different levels viz. State, district, sub-division and/or block level, has been set up in the country. State-wise details of drinking water quality testing laboratories is at Annex-I.(c) To make provision of potable tap water supply in adequate quantity, of prescribed quality and on regular & long-term basis to every rural household by 2024, since August, 2019, Government of India in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal. Thus, as of date, out of 19.27 Crore rural households in the country, 8.88 Crore (46.09%) households are reported to have provision of tap water supply in their homes. State-wise details is at Annex– II.The State/UT, district & village-wise status of tap water connection in rural households, schools and anganwadi centres as reported by States/ UTs is in public domain and available on JJM dashboard at:https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx*****
