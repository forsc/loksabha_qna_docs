                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

984






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Medical Facilities in Jharkhand and Maharashtra




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) whether the number of hospitals and medical centres in Jharkhand and Maharashtra is short by 22 percent and 35 percent respectively in comparison to the population of these States, if so, the details thereof; (b) the reaction of the Government in this regard; and (c) the efforts made by the Government to solve the said problem?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):    As per Rural Health Statistics (RHS) 2018, the shortfall of Sub Health Centre (SHC), Primary Health Centre (PHC) and Community Health Centre (CHC) in the State of Jharkhand and Maharashtra is as below:State	SHC	PHC	CHCJharkhand	37%	69%	29%Maharashtra	21%	17%	34%(b) & (c):  “Public Health and Hospitals” being a State subject, the primary responsibility of establishment of Public Health Facilities lies with respective State Government. However, under National Health Mission support is provided to States/ UTs for strengthening of their healthcare system including support for establishment/ upgradation of new/ existing public health facilities. This support under NHM is provided on the basis of requirement posed by the States/ UTs in their annual Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs).           As part of Ayushman Bharat, the Government is supporting the States for developing the Sub Health Centres and Primary Health Centres as Health and Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs) for provision of comprehensive primary health care that includes preventive and health promotion at the community level with continuum of care approach.………………..
