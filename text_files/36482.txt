                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3709






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Pandemic Fund




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the fact that only 4 to 5 percent of population is covered under health insurance in the country;(b) if so, the reasons for such low coverage thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to set up a specific pandemic fund to support the uninsured population at the time of need in any pandemic crisis; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d):Department of Financial Services, Ministry of Finance, has informed that the number of lives covered under health insurance was 51.47 crore in the Financial Year 2020-21. This includes Government sponsored schemes like Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) and other State Government sponsored schemes.Services for COVID-19 testing, treatment and vaccination are free for all citizens in Government health facilities. The Government of India provides financial and technical support to States/UTs under Emergency COVID Response Package (ECRP) to strengthen their health infrastructure for immediate response to the pandemic.*****
