                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2451






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Improving Horticultural Produce




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether any study has been done linking consumption of specific fruits and vegetables with negative effects on health and if so, details thereof;(b)     the details of the quantity produced, imported and exported of horticultural produce during the last five years, State-wise and product-wise.(c)     whether the Government has any specific schemes for improving fruit production and productivity in the country, particularly in the State of Bihar;(d)   whether any specific varieties of horticultural produce in the country have geographical indicator tag and if so, the details thereof, product-wise;(e)   whether the Government has any proposal under consideration for providing Geographical Indicator status to more varieties of horticultural produce in the country; and(f )    the details of the steps being taken by the Government to promote “One District, One Product” policy for the promotion of horticultural produce in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????             (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):      ICMR has provided different studies. Details are attached at annexure-l.(b):      The details of the quantity produced of horticultural commodities during the last five years, State-wise and product-wise is attached at annexure- ll (a, b, c, d & e) and the details of quantity imported and exported from the country is attached at annexure III & IV.(c):    Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare implementing a centrally sponsored scheme, Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for holistic growth of the horticulture sector including fruits for production and productivity improvement in the country. All States including Bihar and UTs are covered under MIDH scheme.(d):    The Office of Geographical Indications Registry as on March 03, 2021, have registered One Hundred and Nine (109) Geographical Indications products under Agriculture category as per the Geographical Indications of Goods (Registration and Protection) Act, 1999. The list of State wise Geographical Indications Applications registered under Agriculture category is attached at annexure – V.(e):     Yes, the Government is actively considering conferring more Geographical Indication registration to such goods, where a given quality, reputation or other characteristic of such goods is essentially attributable to its geographical origin. (f):   The Government of India has identified the products for One District One Focus Produce (ODOFP). The products have been identified from agricultural, horticultural, animal, poultry, milk, fisheries and aquaculture, marine sectors for 728 districts across the country. These products are promoted in a cluster approach through convergence of the Government of India schemes, to increase the value of the products and with the ultimate aim of increasing the income of the farmers.
