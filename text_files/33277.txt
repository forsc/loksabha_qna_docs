                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

625






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Non-utilisation of funds under National Health Mission




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether a number of States have not been able to utilize the funds disbursed under National Health Mission;(b)	if so,the details thereof for eachsuch State;(c)	whether the said non-utilization of funds was due to poor planning and lack of proper guidance; and(d)	if so, whether the Government proposes to extend assistance or guidance to such States?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (d): A statement showing funds released and utilized during the Financial Year 2020-21 of the National Health Mission (NHM), State/UT-wise is at Annexure.Further, Public Health & Hospitals is a State subject and the Central Government provides support to State Governments for implementation of various programmes including NHM. Under NHM to improve the financial utilization of funds the following steps have been taken up by the Ministry:•	Performance Audit by the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) of India,•	 Annual Statutory Audits by CAG empanelled major Chartered Accountant Auditfirms,•	Concurrent Audits by Chartered Accountant Audit firms,   •	Public Financial Management System (PFMS) in NHM to track flow of funds on Real time basis, •	Submission of quarterly Financial Monitoring Reports (FMRs) and Statement of Fund Position (SFP) by the States/UTs,•	Implementation of Single Nodal Account in the States/UTs, •	Regular review and monitoring of scheme components.In addition, the Government has continued to allocate more funds to strengthen the National Health Mission (NHM). Allocation under NHM has increased from Rs.27,989 crore in 2020-21 to Rs.31,100 crore in 2021-22 Budget Estimate.
