                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

868






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Impact of New Motor Vehicles Act




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of traffic fines levied after implementation of the Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act, 2019 as on date;(b)	the total amount collected as a result thereof, State/UT-wise;(c)	the total number of road accidents and consequent deaths during the said period;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to amend any of the provisions of the said Act in	the near future; and(e)	 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER
