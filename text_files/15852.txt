                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Setting-up Yoga Centre




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has done any work for setting up AYUSH Yoga centre at each block level in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, States/UT-wise; and (c) the number of these centres approved in the States of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):    As public health is a State subject, setting up AYUSH Yoga centre at each block level in the country comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, Central Government is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in the country including Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan under which financial assistance is provided to States/UTs for setting up of Yoga Wellness Centres. The status of financial assistance provided for Yoga Wellness Centres, State/UT-wise as per proposal received from them, is furnished at Annexure-I.     (c):    The number of Yoga Wellness Centres approved in the States of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan, district-wise is furnished at Annexure-II.………………
