                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1769






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Control Over Dal Lake and Mansabal Lake




Mohammad Akbar Lone














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to take Dal Lake and Mansabal Lake under direct control of Ministry of Tourism in Central Government; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	if not, the future point of view in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism has no proposal to take Dal Lake and Manasbal Lake under its control. However, Ministry under its scheme of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides financial Assistance to the State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Ministry has sanctioned 7 projects for Rs.594.52 Crore in UT of Jammu & Kashmir and UT of Ladakh under above schemes. The development works at Dal Lake and Manasbal lake are covered under three projects as detailed below:<pre>Sl.No	Project name/Year	Destination	Amount sanctioned for the destination	Component1	Integrated Development of Himalayan Circuit: Pehalgam, Kargil, Leh2016-17	Dal Lake	Rs.26.89 Crore	Development of Floating Cafeteria, Shikara Ghats, Sound & Light show, Toilets, Benches, Pathways, etc.2	Integrated Development of Himalayan Circuit: Construction of Assets in lieu of those Destroyed in Floods2016-17			3	Integrated Development of Himalayan Circuit: Jammu-Rajouri-Shopian-Pulwama2016-17	Manasbal lake	Rs 5.00 crore	Development of water sports at Manasbal lake</pre>*******
