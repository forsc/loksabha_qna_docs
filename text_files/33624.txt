                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1046






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Financial Assistance through SAI




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Sports Authority of India (SAI) has given any financial assistance particularly to GV Raja Sports School in Attingal Parliamentary Constituency under Khelo India State Centre of Excellence;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details and the amount of funds released for purchase of sports equipments; and(d) the details of funds released for hiring manpower including coaches?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) and (b) An amount of ? 264.93 lakh has been sanctioned to GV Raja Sr. Sec. Sports School, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala on account of Khelo India State Centre of Excellence. An amount of ? 144.04 lakh has been released till date.(c) ? 36.30 lakh have been released for purchase of sports equipment.(d) ? 66.26 lakh have been released for hiring manpower, including coaches.****
