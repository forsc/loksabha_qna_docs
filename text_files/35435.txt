                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2791






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Funds for Conservation and Development of Religious Heritage in Madhya Pradesh




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the steps taken by the Government for the conservation and development of ancient religious heritage; and(b)	the details of the amount spent for the conservation of religious heritage in Madhya Pradesh especially Gwalior?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	The Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) undertakes conservation and development works of 3693 centrally protected monuments & sites including ancient religious heritage. Conservation and development of monuments is a continuous process and works are taken up depending upon the requirement. (b)	The ASI undertakes conservation and development works  of  291 centrally protected monuments, including ancient religious heritage,  located in Madhya Pradesh.The amount spent on conservation of centrally protected monuments including ancient religious heritage  located in Madhya Pradesh is given as under:       (Amount in Rupees)       Year	Expenditure2018-19	  8,09,48,000.002019-20	12,50,00,000.002020-21	15,00,00,000.002021-22	15,02,00,000.00(Up to 09.03.2022) The amount spent on conservation of centrally protected ancient religious heritage located in Gwalior is given as under:        (Amount in Rupees)       Year	Expenditure2018-19	39,11,708.002019-20	98,44,262.002020-21	61,80,592.002021-22	55,80,849.00(Up to 28.02.2022)
