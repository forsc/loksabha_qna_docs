                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1883






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Demand of Cement




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is reported that there is going to be a huge demand of cement in the next two years;(b) 	if so, the measures taken to increase the utilisation capacity as well as production capacity; and(c) 	the manner in which the Government will bridge the shortage including importing of cement and controlling price increase?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):	No Sir. No such report has been received regarding likely huge demand of cement in next two years. Demand of cement, in general, is linked to growth in infrastructure and housing sectors.  Annual installed capacity of cement in the country is around 510 million tones. Annual cement production during the year 2018-19 has been 337.22 million tones resulting in an average capacity utilization of around 67%. In view of the above, there is enough capacity to meet additional demand of cement in future. ********
