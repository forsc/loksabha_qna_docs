                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3987






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Digitisation of Health Record




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has formulated any scheme for digitization of health record of all the insured persons covered under ESIC and ESIS in the country, if so, the details thereof;(b)whether the Government has also formulated any scheme to integrate its data with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission;(c)if so, the details thereof along with the estimated amount of funds likely to be spent on this; (d)the details of health facilities to be provided thereunder; and(e)the time by which the same is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (e):	Information Technology (IT) system and software are available for digitization of health record of the insured persons, covered under the ESIC and ESI Scheme (ESIS), seeking medical services. The IT systems has a centralized database/ application to be used by respective health centres under the ESI Scheme for documentation of clinical information about the patients.          	ESIC has approached National Health Authority (NHA) for technical feasibility to integrate ESIC health database with   Aayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM)  to facilitate exchange of data.          		For digitisation of clinical records and for other IT Infrastructure, a budgetary provision of Rs.231.23 crore for the year 2022-23 has been made by the ESI Corporation for its all health centres under ESI Scheme.  Digitisation of health records is a continuous process.*******
