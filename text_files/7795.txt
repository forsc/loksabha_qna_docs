                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1534






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






U.N. Sponsored Projects




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the total number of projects being sponsored by UN in India during the last three years; and(b)	the present status of all these projects sponsored by UN?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b) 	According to the Office of the Resident Coordinator of the United Nations in New Delhi, they are involving in approximately 270 projects in India. These projects are in both government and non-governmental sectors. This Ministry does not execute projects of the UN in India.***
