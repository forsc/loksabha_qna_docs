                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3003






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Upgradation of Bagdogra Airport




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a proposal to upgrade the Bagdogra Airport into international airport is under consideration of the Government and if so, the details and the present status thereof; and(b) the extent of progress made so far in the work of modernization of Bagdogra Airport?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) and (b): Bagdogra Airport belongs to Indian Air Force (IAF) and Airports Authority of India (AAI) maintains a Civil Enclave. Development/ Modernization of airports is a continuous process which is undertaken by AAI based on the operational demand, traffic projections, availability of land etc. AAI has undertaken the modification of existing terminal building at Bagdogra Airport by extending Security Hold area by 820 sqm and so far, 50% work has been completed. Further, AAI has projected a land requirement of 104.65 acres to the State Government of West Bengal for development of new Civil Enclave at Bagdogra.
