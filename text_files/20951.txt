                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2201






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana in Madhya Pradesh




Pragya Singh Thakur













                                                    Shiromani Ram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has done registration under Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan (PMSYM) Yojana;(b)if so, the number of persons who have got themselves registered so far;(c)the details of numbers of beneficiaries under the PMSYM Yojana, State/district-wise;(d)the number of people availing the benefits of the said scheme in the Parliamentary Constituency of Shrawasti of Uttar Pradesh;(e)the number of registration done in the State of Madhya Pradesh including the Lok Sabha Constituency Bhopal; and (f)the details of various steps taken by the Government to encourage this scheme furthermore?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (e):	 Yes, Sir. The registration is being done under Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan (PMSYM) Yojana. As on 28.02.2021,   over 44.90 Lakh persons have been registered under the Scheme. The details of numbers of beneficiaries under PMSYM Scheme State-wise are at annexure ‘A’. Over 3000 people are availing the benefits of scheme in the   Shrawasti district of Uttar Pradesh.   As data is maintained as per Local Government Directory with granularity at District level, therefore, Constituency-wise data is not available. In Madhya Pradesh, over 1.23 Lakh   enrolments have been done under Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan (PMSYM) including the Bhopal district.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(f):		The State/UT Governments are required to mobilise the eligible unorganised workers to their nearest CSC Centres.  For this,   State Level Monitoring Committees under Chief Secretaries and District Level Implementation Committee under DC/DM have been constituted in all the Districts & States.  The IEC materials such as Pamphlets, Backdrops, creatives for standee and Video documentaries (in local language) have also been provided to State/UT Governments for sensitizing the Unorganised Workers.*                                                    ******
