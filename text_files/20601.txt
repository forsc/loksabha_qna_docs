                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1766






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Affordable Housing




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether poverty alleviation, affordable housing and sanitation are being addressed through programmes of Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihood Mission (DAY-NULM), Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-Housing for All) and Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM);(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the number of beneficiaries of district Satna since inception of the said schemes; and(c) 	whether the basic infrastructure are being focused upon through the Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) and if so, the details thereof along with number of beneficiaries of district Satna?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)		Yes, Sir(b)		Details are given in the Annexure-I.(c)	Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs is implementing  Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation(AMRUT) in selected 500 cities across the country to provide urban infrastructure for water supply, sewerage, septage management, storm water drainage, non-motorised transport, green spaces and parks. It  is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme with a Central outlay of ?50,000 crore.  Against the plan size of ?77,640 crore, which includes Central Assistance of ?36,036 crore, ?39,011 crore (50%) has been allocated to water supply, ?32,456 crore (42%) to sewerage & septage projects, ?2,969 crore (4%) towards drainage projects, ?1,436 crore (2%) for non-motorised urban transport and ?1,768 crore (2%) has been allocated for green spaces and parks.Details of the Mission and the beneficiaries in Satna are given in Annexure- II.                                         *****
