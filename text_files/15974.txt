                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Eco Sensitive Zones




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether delimitation of Eco Sensitive Zones have been conducted in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (c)	the objectives and the impact thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) & (b) Eco-Sensitive Zones (ESZ) are declared around Protected Areas(PAs) through Notification under the provisions of section 3 (2)(v) of the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 and Rules made thereunder.In 2011, Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEF&CC) prepared Guidelines for development of ESZ around National Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries specifying activities that are to be prohibited,regulated and promoted in the ESZ.Out of total 662 PAs, MoEF&CChas so far,published final ESZ Notifications covering 382PAs and draft Notifications for 156 PAs. State-wise detail of Notified ESZ is Annexed.(c) The objective of notifying ESZ around Protected Areasinter aliainclude minimising anthropogenic impacts, such as man animal conflict, conservation of biodiversityand to ensure sustainable development taking into consideration the needs and aspiration of the local people.****
