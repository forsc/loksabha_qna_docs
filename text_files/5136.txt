                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






ONGC Golf Courses




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether 18 golf courses of Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) have been declared non-core assets by Department of Investment and Public Asset Management;(b) if so, whether the Government has issued any guidelines to public sector company ONGC to sell its 18 golf courses across the country; and(c) if so, the likely benefit of it to the Government?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c): The Consultative Group (CG) on Asset Monetization for laying down procedure and mechanism for asset monetization of Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs)/Public Sector Units/other Government organizations in its meeting held on 27th May, 2019 in NITI Aayog has included two Golf Courses of ONGC- at Ahmedabad and Vadodara – in the list of assets for monetization.****
