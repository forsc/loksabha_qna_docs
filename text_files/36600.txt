                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3613






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Accident Prone Black Spots on NHs




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the accident prone black spots on the newly constructed National Highway Nos. 647, 353I and 347A in Wardha district of Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether any letter/representation has been received from the public representatives in this regard and for removal of these black spots and if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government proposes to initiate any action against the concerned authority for submitting wrong DPR and faulty design and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) There are no notified road accident black spots on NH-647, NH-353I & NH-347A as per the list of black spots identified by the Ministry based on accident data received from States. In the list of accident prone spots in Maharashtra compiled by Deputy Commissioner, Transport Department, Mumbai, 5 accident prone spots have been identified on NH-347A. Permanent measures for rectification of these accident prone spots have already been taken during improvement of Wardha-Hinganghat section of NH-347A to 2lane with paved shoulder standards.  (b) Yes Sir. A reference from Hon’ble MP Shri Ramdas C. Tadas has been received to remove accident spots at 4 locations as per  details  at Annexure-A.(c) Does not arise. The project proposals of DPR Consultant were based on availability of land and other site constraints. The recommendations of DPR Consultant includes necessary provisions for safety in the project proposals.
