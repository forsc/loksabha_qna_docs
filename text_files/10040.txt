                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3589






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Funds for Animal Husbandry and Pisciculture in Solapur District




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of funds provided by the  Ministry  of  Fisheries,  Animal Husbandry and Dairying in Solapur district of Maharashtra during the last two years; and(b) the amount of funds provided by the  Ministry  to  Solapur  district  for pisciculture?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING                                                             (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) & (b) The Government is implementing various schemes / programmes for development of animal husbandry, dairying and pisciculture sectors in all the States/UTs including Maharashtra, namely,i.	Rashtriya Gokul Missionii.	National Programme for Dairy Development iii.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme iv.	National Dairy Plan-Iv.	National Livestock Missionvi.	Livestock Health & Disease Controlvii.	Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries      The details of financial assistance provided to Maharashtra under these schemes during the last two years are as under: Fund Released to the State Government of Maharashtra during the last two years(Rs.In lakh)S.
