                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4602






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Construction along Indo-China Border




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the Chinese construction along the Indo-China border and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has improved infrastructure in our        territory along the border and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of such projects and their Point of Action (POA)?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (c)	Government is aware that China is developing infrastructure in the border regions opposite India in Tibet and Xinjiang Autonomous Regions.	Government gives careful and special attention to improvement of infrastructure for the development of border areas, in order to facilitate the economic development of these areas as also to meet India’s strategic and security requirements.	In the last few years, Government has increased the budgetary allocation for construction of roads and bridges in the border areas. This has helped provide connectivity to the local population and better logistical support for our armed forces.	Government continues to keep constant watch on all developments having a bearing on India’s security and takes all the necessary measures to safeguard its sovereignty and territorial integrity.***
