                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

233






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the amount allocated and spent for skill development under Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (UDAN) and Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana during the last three years, State-wise;(b) 	the details of the persons benefitted by the said Kaushal programmes during the said period and out of them the percentage/number of the beneficiaries of poor and middle class; and(c) 	the details of the persons provided with employment out of the above beneficiaries and percentage increase in the income of the beneficiaries, State and areas-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)     Government is implementing a placement linked skill development programme, Deendayal Upadhyaya-Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) through Ministry of Rural Development, for rural poor youth. DDU-GKY is a demand driven scheme and under the scheme funds are released to States as per demands against approved Action Plans. The State-wise details of the funds released and spent for skill development under DDU-GKY during last three years is given at Annexure-I. There is no sub- scheme or programme named UDAN under DDU-GKY.Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) is being implemented through Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. The State-wise details of funds disbursed under PMKVY 2.0 & 3.0 under the State component of PMKVY in last 3 Financial Years (FYs) is given at Annexure-II.(b) & (c)   The State-wise details of the number of persons benefitted (trained and placed) from DDU-GKY during the last three years is given at Annexure –III. All the youth covered under DDU-GKY are from rural poor category except the UT of Jammu & Kashmir where under HIMAYAT programme all the youth of UT are eligible. There is no separate data on beneficiaries from middle class available under DDU-GKY. The State-wise details of candidates benefitted (trained and placed) under PMKVY as on 30.05.2021, since inception of the scheme, is given at Annexure –IV.Under PMKVY, National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), an implementing agency of PMKVY, has engaged a third party agency, Sambodhi Research and Communications Pvt. Ltd., for the impact evaluation of PMKVY 2016-20. This report highlighted about 15% increase in average monthly salary under Short Term Training (STT) and 19% under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL). Under RPL, 70% participants reported for better chance of finding employment. No such data on income increase with respect to DDU-GKY is available.*****
