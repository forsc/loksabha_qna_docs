                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

440






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Incentives for Production of Paddy Seeds




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is giving any incentive for promoting the production of paddy seeds to farmers; (b) 	if so, the details of such incentives that have been paid to the farmers during the past three years including current year, category-wise/State-wise/yearwise; (c) 	whether the Government has taken any decision to decrease the rate of incentive paid to the farmers; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (d):	The Government of India is providing assistance for production of paddy seed under “Bringing Green Revolution to Eastern India (BGREI)”-a sub scheme of Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) through State Governments. The state-wise; years-wise details of assistance for last three years including targets of current year under BGREI are annexed. There is no decrease in rate of assistance.*****
