                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2798






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Smuggling of Vulture




Queen Oja













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the cases of vulture smuggling have been noticed by the Government;(b)	if so, the number of such cases reported during the last three years; and(c)	the details of the action taken in the said cases?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a), (b) and (c) As per the data available in records of Wildlife Crime Control Bureau provided by the States/ UTs Forests and Police authorities, the details of offences involving vultures during the last three years are as under:S. No	Year	Number of vulture seizure cases1	2019	12	2020	-3	2021	-The cases are registered under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972, by the concerned State Forest Authorities. Arrests have been made and investigation carried out for prosecution.*****
