                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4332






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Employment in Engineering/Medical Sector




Ajay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c): Government is implementing the National Career Service (NCS) Project to provide a variety of employment related services like job search and matching, career counselling, vocational guidance, information on skill development courses, etc. Under the NCS, project assistance is also provided to States/UTs for organising job fairs. The details of the job fairs organised through NCS in collaboration with State Govt. of Assam are as under:- Sl. No.	Year	No. of Job Fairs	No. of candidates given final offer letter1.	2017-18	23	10102.	2018-19	56	8043.	2019-20	35	4424.	2020-21	12	30Total	1262286Besides above, as per information received from Assam, Skill Employment and Entrepreneurship Department (SEED), the State Government has organised 8 job fairs during 2017-2019 and 2038 candidates have been given final offer letter through these job fairs.






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b): The Government is implementing various schemes for providing skill development training to the youth of the country. The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) Scheme and the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) for short-term training, and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) through the Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) for long-term training. In addition, the Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) and Rural Self Employment Training Institutes (RSETIs) schemes, while the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs is implementing the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM). The number of candidates trained and reported placed under the above schemes since 2016-17 is as under:Sl. No.	Schemes	Candidates trained (in lakh)	Candidates placed (in lakh)1	PMKVY	106.96	18.952	JSS*	10.96	-3	NAPS*	9.90	-4	CTS*	52.98	-5	DDU GKY	7.84	5.496	RSETIs	17.67	13.927	DAY-NULM**	4.72	2.74     *JSS, NAPS and CTS schemes do not monitor placement of candidates     ** Figures are from FY 2018-19 to 2020-21The details of number of Engineering Graduates who have graduated from various AICTE affiliated Government & Private Engineering Colleges and Institutes in the country along with percentage of graduates who secured jobs in campus recruitment procedure during the last three years is at Annexure.(c): As per the results of Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) conducted by National Sample Survey Office, Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation during 2017-18 and 2018-19, the estimated unemployment rate for the persons aged 15 years and above on usual status (principal status + subsidiary status) basis in the country is 6.0% and 5.8% respectively.*****
