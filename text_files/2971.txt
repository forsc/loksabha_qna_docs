                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2740






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Digital Reservation Centres




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the digitization of Railways is providing smooth computerized reservation facilities in the remote districts of the country, especially in Bihar;(b)  if so, the details thereof; and(c)  the details regarding total number of reservation centres and counters at the railway stations of Gopalganj and Thawe junctions?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir.  At present, Passenger Reservation System (PRS) is functional at 3443 locations over Indian Railways.   Out of this, in the State of Bihar, PRS is functional at 243 locations.  The facility to book PRS tickets has also been made available at more than 1,42,398 Common Service Centres (CSCs) in the country, out of which 12,837 are in Bihar State.In addition, e-ticketing website www.irctc.co.in has now become very popular and it now handles around 70% of the reserved tickets.  (c) : Gopalganj has one reservation centre with one PRS Counter.  Thawe has one unified Unreserved Ticketing System-cum-Passenger Reservation System (UTS-cum-PRS) counter.In addition, passengers can book tickets online and from CSCs in Gopalganj and Thawe.****
