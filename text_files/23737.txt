                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Allocation of Coal Mines to Private Sector




Chunni Lal Sahu













                                                    Mohan Mandavi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	the total number of allocations of coal mines in the private sector, company wise, year-wise, and State-wise;b.	whether the process of the allocation of mines stated above and the process of allocation of 214 cancelled mines are different;c.	if so, the details of the short comings noticed;d.	if not, whether the Government is considering to cancel the said allocation; ande.	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COAL, MINES & PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) a.	42 coal mines have been allocated to Private Sector  under Coal Mines (Special Provisions) Act, 2015 (CMSP Act, 2015) and Mine and Mineral (Development &Regulation) Act, 1957 (MMDR Act, 1957). The list of mines may be found in the Annexure enclosed. b.	Yes Sir. Under Coal Mine Nationalisation Act, 1973 the coal blocks were allotted through Screening Committee constituted for screening proposals for allocation of coal/lignite blocks for manufacture of iron/steel, captive production of power and production of cement in the public/private sector.Whereas the allocation of 204 coal mines de-allocated by Hon’ble Supreme Court is now made through transparent & competitive bidding mechanism to private sector under the provisions of the Coal Mines (Special Provisions) Act, 2015 and Rules made thereunder.  c.	No short-comings have been noticed so far.d.	No, Sir. Government is not considering the cancellation of said allocation.e.
