                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2529






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






GST on Irrigation Systems




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether considering that the country is facing problems of water and there is need for upgradation of irrigation systems with new technology for farming, the Government proposes to reduce the GST applicable as on date on such products and systems like PVC pipes, sprinkler systems etc.; and(b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare(DAC&FW) is implementing  Per Drop More Crop (PDMC) component of  Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) which focuses on enhancing water use efficiency at farm level through Micro Irrigation technologies viz. Drip and Sprinkler Irrigation systems.                Besides, Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) has developed cost effective, location specific modern agronomic practices like raised bed sowing, alternate furrow irrigation, mulching, Direct Seeded Rice (DSR) through seed drills and drum seeders, System of Rice Intensification (SRI), alternate wetting & drying method, laser land leveling, crop diversification and  varieties which require less water to ensure improved water productivity together with higher crop productivity in the country. Assistance is given to the States through various schemes of DAC&FW for water conservation techniques and tools.	GST rates on goods are notified on the basis of the recommendation of the GST Council, according to which 18% GST on PVC pipes and 12% GST on sprinkler systems have been notified. At present, there is no recommendation of GST Council for reduction of the applicable GST on Irrigation systems like PVC pipes, sprinkler systems etc.*******
