                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1325






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Fund Allocation to Universities




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is a fund cut of UGC funds to the Central Universities – Aligarh MuslimUniversity, Jamia Millia Islamia and Moulana Azad National Urdu University;(b) if so, the details of the fund allocation and distribution to these universities during the last tenyears;(c) the details of the Central Universities/central institutions having with Minority status,State/UT-wise;(d) whether AMU, JMI and MANUU have minority status;(e) if so, the details thereof;(f) whether any special fund is allocated to the universities/institutions with minority status; and(g) if so, the details thereof along with the details of the funds sanctioned to the AMU, JMI andMANUU during the last ten years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): No Sir. However, the Grant-in-Aid to Central Universities are released by UGC onthe basis of funds allocated to Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), anddemand/utilization of funds by the Universities during the previous year. Details of the fundreleased to Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI) and Moulana AzadNational Urdu University (MANNUU) during the last ten years is annexed.(c) to (e): National Commission for Minority Educational Institutions (NCMEI) has grantedMinority Status Certificate (MSC) to Central University Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI).(f) & (g): No Sir.***
