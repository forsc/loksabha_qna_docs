                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Higher Education Loan to SC Students




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4511 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021HIGHER EDUCATION LOAN TO SC STUDENTS 4511. SHRI BHOLANATH (B.P. SAROJ): Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is providing higher education loan for various courses like MBA, MBBS, BTech etc. to Scheduled Castes(SCs) students at zero interest rate; (b) if so, the details and the number of students belonging to SCs who applied for loan with the National Banks to pursue education during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise and sector-wise; (c) the details of the students who applied and got loan to study abroad during the said period, State/UTwise and sector-wise; (d) whether the Government is aware that the SC students as well as students from poor categories are facing lots of difficulties in getting such education loan, particularly in rural areas; and (e) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to redress the said grievances?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA )(a):  No Sir. No loan is being provided to SC students at zero interest rate. The National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC) and National Safai Karamcharis Finance & Development Corporation (NSKFDC) the corporations under the aegis of Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment are providing education loan to Scheduled Castes students for pursuing higher studies at concessional rate of interest of 4% p.a. for male and 3.50% p.a. for female beneficiaries. (b) & (c): Not applicable in view of (a) above.(d) & (e): No such specific information has been received so far.***
