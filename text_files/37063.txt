                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4182






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






MGNREGS in Maharashtra




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether work is being done under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in the country especially in backward areas including Shirdi Parliamentary Constituency in the State of Maharashtra;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the latest status of backward areas as on date especially in Shirdi Parliamentary Constituency in the State of Maharashtra; and(c) 	the details of the central funds allocated and utilised for the purpose during the last three years as on date along with number of beneficiaries, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b) The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven scheme which provides for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.   As per the information shared by the Government of Maharashtra, the works under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS are taken as per the demand for unskilled employment in the rural areas of State including rural areas of Shirdi Parliamentary Constituency.  The Taluka wise details of  person-days generated, number of ongoing works and number of completed works under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in the Shirdi Parliamentary Constituency in current financial year 2021-22 (as on 24.03.2022) is given as below: S.
