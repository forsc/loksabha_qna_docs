                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

618






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Har Ghar Nal Se Jal




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the areas provided with tap water connections as on date under Har Ghar Nal Se Jal yojana;(b) the number of Gram Panchayats of Rajasthan including the Gram Panchayats of Udaipur Lok Sabha Constituency provided with tap water connection and the time by which the remaining panchayats are likely to be connected; and(c) whether any responsibility has been given to the Member of Parliament to monitor the same, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH  PATEL)(a)  	Government of India is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in partnership with States to make provision for potable tap water supply to every rural household by 2024. At the time of launch of Jal Jeevan Mission on 15th August, 2019, out of total 18.94 Crore rural households in the country, 3.23 (17%) crore rural households had tap water connections. Since then, 4.52 Crore (23.84%) rural households have been provided tap water connections thus, as on date, 7.76 Crore (40.89%) rural households have provision of tap water supply.(b)  	Out of total 11,322 Gram Panchayats in Rajasthan, in 63 Gram Panchayats, all households are reported to have provision of tap water supply. Remaining 11,259 Gram Panchayats, including those in Udaipur Lok Sabha constituency, are likely to have provision of tap water supply to households, by 2024.(c)   	Under JJM, provisions have been made regarding participation of Members of Parliament in the planning, implementation and monitoring of Jal Jeevan Mission and the same has been also brought to their notice with a request to make this mission a peoples’ movement.*****
