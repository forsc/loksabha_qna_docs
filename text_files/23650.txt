                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4784






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Toilet Facilities in Trains to Prayagraj




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is no proper toilet facility in the trains which run from Delhi/New Delhi to Prayagraj and beyond;(b)	if so, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government is aware that there is no proper toilet facility in various coaches of 02788 Humsafar Express; (d)	if so, the steps being taken to improve the toilet facility;(e)	whether Humsafar Express train No.02788 which had to arrive on platform No.3 at Prayagraj Chheoki station arrived on platform No.2 without giving any prior information on 20.02.2021; and(f)	if so, the steps being taken to prevent such mistake?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Proper toilet facility is available in all the passenger coaches of Mail/express trains which run from Delhi/New Delhi to Prayagraj and beyond;(b):  Does not arise.(c):  All the coaches of train no. 02788 Humsafar Express are provided with Bio-toilets. (d): All toilets are fully functional and cleaned before the start of the journey. It is also being ensured that all fittings and amenities are intact in the toilets.(e) & (f): Platform No. 02 & 03 are the two faces of an island platform, involving no change of platform. On 20.02.2021, train No. 02788 Danapur - Secunderabad Express had been received on platform No.2 instead of platform No.3 with proper display and announcement, thereby causing no inconvenience to passenger.  *****
