                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4092






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






PhDs in ESDM amd ITES Sectors




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology be pleased to state:-(a)  whether 3,000 PhDs are being supported by the Government under the Scheme to enhance the number of PhDs in the Electronic System Design and Manufacturing (ESDM) and IT/IT Enabled Service (ITES) sectors;(b)   if so, the details thereof;(c)   if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)  the steps being taken by the Government to ensure that its goal of supporting 3,000 PhDs is fulfilled.






ANSWER




                                                        (a), (b), (c) and (d): Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India has launched “Visvesvaraya PhD Scheme for Electronics and IT” in 2014 for a period of nine years with the objective to enhance the number of PhDs in Electronic System Design & Manufacturing (ESDM) and IT/IT Enabled Services (ITES) sectors in the country.  The scheme is designed to support 3000 PhDs (1000 Full Time & 2000 Part Time) in ESDM and in IT/ITES sectors. As on 11-07-2019, total number of beneficiaries under the scheme are 947 Full-Time and 250 Part-Time PhD scholars in 95 academic institutions across the country. *******
