                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

670






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Aircraft Crashes




Prataprao  Jadhav













                                                    Suresh Kodikunnil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of aircrafts of the Air Force which have crashed during the last three years and as on date, year-wise;(b) the quantum of loss incurred due to it;(c) whether the Government has conducted any investigation regarding the said aircraft accidents; and(d) if so, the details and the outcome thereof along with the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)    (a) & (b): The details of aircraft crashes during last three years are as follows:Year	Fighter 	Helicopter	Transport	Trainer2016-17	06	02	01	012017-18	02	-	-	032018-19	07	02	-	022019-20 (till 20.06.2019)	-	-	01	- 	The current quantum of provisional loss for the last three years is approximately Rs.524.64 crore in respect of 11 crash cases which have been closed.(c) & (d):  Every aircraft accident in the IAF is investigated by Court of Inquiry (CoI) to ascertain the cause of accident and the recommendations of the completed Court of Inquiry are implemented.******
