                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






PMAY(G) in Maharashtra




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin) is functional in the State of Maharashtra;(b) 	if so, the details of the sanctioned housing in the State of Maharashtra under the said scheme; and(c) 	the status of the scheme under the Wardha and Amravati districts of the State of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	Yes Sir.(b)	In order to achieve the objective of “Housing for All”, the erstwhile rural housing scheme Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY) has been restructured into Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) w.e.f. 1st April, 2016.  A total of 9,88,041 houses have been sanctioned under PMAY-G in the State of Maharashtra. (c)	The status of PMAY-G Scheme in Wardha and Amravati districts of the State of Maharashtra is as follows:                                                (units in nos.)District	Houses Sanctioned	    Houses CompletedWardha	           13,072	                9,409Amravati	   68,405	               46,035*****
