                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2556






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






NCC units in schools and colleges




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of colleges and schools which has NCC unit connected to army, State-wise; (b) whether any quota has been reserved for girl cadets; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) whether the Government has taken steps to open NCC in schools and colleges opened near border and coastal areas of India and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) List of all Schools and Colleges having NCC Unit is at Annexure.(b) to (d):  Yes, Sir.  In addition to girl units, there is mixed Battalion concept  where around 33 per cent seats are utilized for enrolling girls.  Details are as follows:	(i)  Total enrolled cadets	 -  1347436	(ii)  Boys cadets		-   893074	(iii)  Girls cadets		-   454362	(iv)  Girls’ percentage		-   33.72%(e)  In July, 2020, the Government has sanctioned the scheme of expansion of NCC to Coastal/Border talukas with the strength of one lakh cadets.  The scheme is to expand the NCC Cadet base in 172 border Taluks, 92 coastal taluks and 43 major Air Force Stations.	*****
