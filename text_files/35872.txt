                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






National University for Disabled




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to establish a National University for Disabled;(b) if so, the details thereof and the progress made in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) & (b) The Ministry in September 2021 has engaged ‘EdCIL (India) Limited’, a Central Public Sector Undertaking under Ministry of Education, for preparation of a site specific Detailed Project Report (DPR) for setting up a University of Disability Studies and Rehabilitation Sciences in the Kamrup District, Assam with the objective of developing professionals/personnel in the field of rehabilitation of persons with disabilities besides promoting research in the said area. *****
