                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

873






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Kakinada Anchorage Port




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received a request for approval of works from the State Government of Andhra Pradesh for improving Kakinada Anchorage Port facilities under Sagarmala Programme to increase the existing 2.0 Million Tons handling capacity to about 4.0 Million Tons per annum;(b)	if so, the details and the status thereof;(c)	whether the Union Government will financially support the State of Andhra Pradesh to develop 8 fishing harbours across the 974 km coastline in the State; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): On the request of Government of Andhra Pradesh, Ministry of Shipping has decided to undertake the project of ''improvement of Kakinada Anchorage Port Facilities" under Sagarmala Programme and has appointed Technical Consultant for preparing the Detailed Project Report for the Project. The scope of the project mainly includes construction of Wharf walls, loading point- piled platform, Groyne repairs, dredging and road works.(c)&(d): The project of construction of Fishing Harbour at Juvvaladinne in Nellore District at a total cost of Rs. 288.80 cr. has been approved under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries in a convergence mode of implementation by sharing the central grant with the Ministry of Shipping under Sagarmala Programme. Department of Fisheries, Govt. of India has informed that another 04 Fishing Harbour projects has been recommended by Central Appraisal and Monitoring Committee(CAMC)under Fisheries and Aquaculture infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF) scheme  with  restricted cost at Rs.150 crore per project for interest subvention as per guidelines of scheme.
