                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

684






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Central Road and Infrastructure Fund




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit













                                                    Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of proposals received and approved under Central Road and infrastructure Fund (CRIF) in the current year from Maharashtra including Satara District; (b) the present status of the said proposals along with the time by which the pending proposals are likely to be approved; (c) the number of roads selected for construction under CRIF in the state of Maharashtra, district-wise; and (d) whether any new directions have been issued regarding utilization of funds from CRIF and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) 	No  proposals under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) have been received from the State Government of Maharashtra  for the State of  Maharastra  including Satara District during the current financial year 2020-21 so far.(c)	The present status of ongoing works under CRIF in the State of Maharashtra are annexed. (d)	No Sir.
