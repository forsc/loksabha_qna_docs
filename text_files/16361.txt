                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

73






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Bokaro Steel Plant




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the cases of corruption , irregularity and violation of rules have come into limelight on a large scale due to the nexus of officers and contractors in Bokaro steel plant;(b)	if so, the total number of cases amongst them in which action has been taken and the number of cases which are pending along with the details thereof; and(c)	the strict action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government against the corrupt persons?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	There are 1885 executives and 9522 non-executives (11407 total employees) in Bokaro Steel Plant (as on 31st August, 2020). A total of 77 complaints have been registered in its Vigilance office this year till 31st August on various matters including alleged irregularities, violation of rules and corruption etc. (b)	Receipt and disposal of complaints is an ongoing process. Disciplinary action has been initiated in 5 cases till August, 2020 this year.  (c)	During this year (till 31st August, 2020), disciplinary proceedings {including those carried over from previous year(s)} have been completed against 8 employees with imposition of suitable penalty. ********
