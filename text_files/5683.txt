                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5075






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Corruption in CBI




Giridhari Yadav













                                                    Bharti Pravin Pawar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether incidents of corruption have taken place/are taking place in Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI); (b) 	if so, the details in this regard for the last three years; (c) 	the nature of the said incidents; (d) 	various efforts made by the Government to check corruption in Central Bureau of Investigation; and (e) 	the outcome of the said efforts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (e):   CBI has registered 19 Regular cases against CBI officers involving corruption charges [Prevention of Corruption Act] during the last three years i.e 2016,2017,2018 & 2019 (upto 30.06.2019). CBI has a robust mechanism to deal with internal vigilance matters and complaints of corruption against its own officers. Concerted efforts are made to ensure probity amongst CBI officials.*****
