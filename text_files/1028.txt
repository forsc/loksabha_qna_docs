                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

925






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Unavailability of Sports Equipments




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether various sports in the country are lagging behind due to unavailability of sports equipments to sportspersons; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the sports equipments have been bought from abroad during the last three years; and (d) if so, the names of the countries and agencies from where such equipments were imported and the details of the payments made for such imports?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b): No Sir. Elite athletes preparing for international competitions are provided with State-of-the-Art Sport equipments and infrastructure. Government also supports National Sports Federations for equipments and consumables for national coaching camps.(c) & (d): Yes Sir. Information relating to import of equipments by Sports Authority of India is given at Annexure.***
