                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






PMGSY in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of additional projects under consideration of the Government for approval under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Jharkhand during the current year;(b)	the details of the ongoing projects under PMGSY in Bokaro, Dhanbad and Giridih districts of Jharkhand during the last two years; and(c) 	the details of the total funds allocated and spent in the said districts during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	The Ministry of Home Affairs has recommended additional 129 roads measuring 810 Km and 12 Long Span Bridges under Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Affected Areas (RCPLWEA) for the State of Jharkhand. The State Government has been requested to submit the Detailed Projects Reports of the above works.	Under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)-III, State of Jharkhand has been allocated target length of 4,125 Km. However, the State Government has not submitted any proposal under PMGSY-III for approval. Therefore, no proposal is presently pending under consideration of the Government of India for approval.(b)	The details of the ongoing projects under PMGSY in Bokaro, Dhanbad and Giridih districts of Jharkhand during the last two years are as under:-District	  No. of works	          Length (in Km)	Cost (Rs. in Cr)	    No of Roads	  No of Bridges Bokaro	          36	      8	             120	          86.27Dhanbad	           5	      0	              22	          11.94Giridih	          77	     11	             152	          91.57Total	         118	     19	             294	         189.78	District-wise details of ongoing works are attached as Annexure.(c)	As informed by the State, the details of the total funds allocated and spent in Bokaro, Dhanbad and Giridih districts of Jharkhand during the last two years are as under:-                                               (Rs in Crore)District	2018-19                       	2019-20	Funds allocated	 Expenditure	Funds allocated	  ExpenditureBokaro	       33.00	   26.19	    10.95	   20.73Dhanbad	        3.43	    4.65	    5.00	    2.86Giridih	       37.71	   30.85	   20.00	   31.80****                                                                   AnnexureAnnexure referred to in Part (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 288 for 15.9.2020Details of Ongoing Projects (works) I.
