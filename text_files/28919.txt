                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Modernisation Police Scheme




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil













                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of Central Financial Assistance disbursed under the Assistance to States for Modernisation of Police Scheme and the details thereof, State-wise;(b) the criteria employed for the bifurcation of States into Category A and B and the disparity in funding received by the two;(c) the amount of extra funding in the form of Better Performance Incentives allocated, sanctioned and disbursed under the scheme as on date and to which States;(d) the criteria employed to decide which States are entitled to receive better Performance Incentives; and(e) the list, number and monetary value of items provided as assistance in kind to the police forces State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a): Central assistance is provided to the States under the scheme of ‘Assistance to States for Modernisation of Police’. The amounts of Central financial assistance disbursed under this scheme during the last 3 financial years and the current financial year is as below :-2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.267 FOR 30.11.2021(? in crore) Year	Allocation	Released2018-19	769.00	768.832019-20	791.30	781.122020-21	770.76	103.25*2021-22	620.45	89.17*(*Funds against allocations could not be released to most of the States as they had substantial unspent balances.)The details of the funds allocated and released to the States during the last 3 financial years and the current financial year are at Annexure.(b) The funding pattern is 60:40 between the Centre and States for all States except 8 North Eastern States, including Sikkim and 2 Himalayan States- Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand, for which the funding pattern is 90:10.  (c) & (d)    Where on account of non-receipt of Utilization Certificates (UCs) from some States, it is not possible to release funds to those States, such unreleased amounts are pooled and released to States with no pending/overdue UCs as ‘better performance incentives’ in proportion of the inter-State distribution ratio. (e) 	Under the scheme of ‘Assistance to States for Modernization of Police’, providing assistance ‘in kind’ was discontinued from the year 2019-20.*****
