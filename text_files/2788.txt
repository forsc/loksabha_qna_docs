                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2528






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Proposals under PMGSY




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of proposals accepted for Gujarat especially Dahod district in the third phase of Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b)	the details of the accepted proposals for Dahod district in Gujarat, proposal-wise;(c)	whether it is a fact that Gujarat has not been allocated any funds to start the work on the said proposals; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	A proposal for rolling out of Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadak Yojana-III (PMGSY-III) for strengthening of rural roads primarily those connecting villages to Gramin Agriculture Markets, Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals is under consideration of the Government.*****
