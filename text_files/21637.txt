                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Implementation of PMSSY in Uttar Pradesh




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) is being implemented in the State of Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof, district-wise especially Pilibhit;(c) the details of the funds released for the projects under the said scheme in the State of Uttar Pradesh so far; and(d) the up to date status of implementation of these projects?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d):  Under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY), setting up of 2 All India Institutes of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) and eleven upgradation projects in nine existing Government Medical Colleges/Institutes (GMCIs) in the State of Uttar Pradesh have been approved. List of such approved projects alongwith central fund released and status of implementation of these projects are given at ANNEXURE.
