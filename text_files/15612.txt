                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

391






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Regulations For TV Channels




Ranjeeta Koli













                                                    Faizal P.P. Mohammed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	the details of specific rules and laws to regulate TV channels and the objectives thereof;  (b) 	whether the above objectives are being achieved by the Government and the reaction of the Government in this regard; (c) 	the number of cases regarding violation of the said rules and laws came to notice during the last three years and the action taken thereon, channel-wise; and (d) 		the outcome of the action taken?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR) (a) to (d):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.********** STATEMENT AS REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. 391 FOR ANSWER ON 20.03.2020(a) to (d)	All private satellite TV channels are required to adhere to the Programme and Advertising Codes as prescribed in the Cable Television Networks (Regulation) Act, 1995 and the Rules framed thereunder, as well as the Uplinking and Downlinking Guidelines, 2011 issued by this Ministry.  Appropriate action is taken against TV channels in case violation of the aforesaid Codes and Guidelines is established.  Action against TV channels may extend from issue of warnings or advisories or directing channels to run apology scrolls on their channels and can extend up to taking the channels off air temporarily for varying periods depending on the gravity of the violation.States have set-up State level and District level Monitoring Committees to monitor content telecast on cable TV channels.Details of action taken against violation of Programme and Advertising Codes and Uplinking & Downlinking Guidelines, 2011, by private satellite channels, during the last three years and the current year, are as under:Sl. No.	Action taken	Number of Channels1.	Advisories	312.	Warnings	1293.	Orders for running apology scroll	304.	Off Air Orders	21TOTAL	211 *********
