                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Quality Standards of Products




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps taken by the Government to improve the quality standards of products manufactured under the Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan;(b) 	the steps taken by the Government to uplift the Start-ups and promote the campaign ‘Vocal for Local‘; and(c) 	the steps taken by the Government for helping the Start-ups and other local manufactures to use the e-commerce and online platforms for promoting the same? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a): 	Government of India has taken a number of steps to improve the quality standards of products manufactured under the Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan. Some of these steps taken by the Government are enclosed at Annexure-I. (b): 	The Government has taken a number of steps to uplift the Start-ups and promote the campaign ‘Vocal for Local’. Some of these steps taken are enclosed at Annexure-II. (c): 	Government has engaged with various e-Commerce platforms for               on-boarding of small sellers of indigenous products and shared lists of sellers/ vendors with such platforms. Further, with an aim to increase visibility of such products, e-Commerce platforms have been engaged to provide separate search facility for such products and develop a storefront to showcase these products on their platforms. *******
