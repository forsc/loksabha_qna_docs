                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1874






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Entrepreneurship and Skill Development Programme




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Arun Sao
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Soni
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The number of centers set up by the Government across the country to conduct Entrepreneurship and Skill Development Programme; and(b)	The amount allocated by the Government for the said programme along with the number of people benefitted there from during the last three years, year-wise and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTRY OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	There are 18 Departmental Training Centres, 17 Non-Departmental Training Centers; supported by KVIC, 8 Technical Centers and 4 livelihood Business Incubators setup by NSIC under M/o MSME  to conduct Entrepreneurship and Skill Development Programme. Besides 717 Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras (PMKK) and 247 Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) have been established in the year 2018-19 and 73 more has been added in the year 2019-20 under Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.(b):	The amount allocated and the number of beneficiaries for Entrepreneurship  and Skill Development Programme is tabulated as under:<pre>Financial Year	ESDP scheme, M/o MSME	KVIC, M/o MSME	Ni-MSME, M/o MSME	NSIC, M/o MSME	Amount Allocated (Rs. in lakhs)	No. of Beneficiaries	Amount Allocated (Rs. in lakhs)	No. of Beneficiaries	Amount Allocated (Rs. in lakhs)	No. of Beneficiaries	Amount Allocated (Rs. in lakhs)	No. of Beneficiaries2018-19	193.05	9534	2494.70	88760	101.01	990	358.56	43892019-20	4085.00	239873	3662.05	85956	240.02	2153	395.54	45762020-21	287.05	27656	3183.36	54672	245.40	2535	282.30	2955</pre>The beneficiaries under M/o Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) for the Jan Shikshan Sansthan, the nos. are 1,67,283 in 2018-19, 4,15,332 in 2019-20 and 3,59,796 in 2020-21 and Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK)  total certified achievement is 11,59,535 as on 21.11.2021. 	State-wise details of achievements under  PMKK is attached as Annexure –I, details of funds allocated and No. of Beneficiaries under  ESDP  scheme, KVIC and NSIC of M/o MSME are attached as Annexure-II, Annexure-III & Annexure-IV respectively.
