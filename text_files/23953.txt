                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5028






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Arbitration of NTPC Kahalgaon with HSCL




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry of Power has approached the Ministry of Urban Development for resolution of long pending arbitration of NTPC Kahalgaon with Hindustan Steelworks and Construction Ltd. (HSCL) which has been merged with NBCC;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon;(c) 	whether NTPC has approached newly created Administrative Mechanism for Resolution of Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) Disputes (AMRCD) for resolving the arbitration with NTPC Kahalgaon; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) :		No, Sir.(b) :		Does not arise in view of reply to (a) above.(c) :		No, Sir.(d) :		Does not arise in view of reply to (c) above.************
