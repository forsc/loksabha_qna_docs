                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2479






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Irregularities under MGNREGS in Punjab




Harsimrat Kaur Badal














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has instituted any enquiry into the irregularities and frauds committed by Punjab Government officials in defraying Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) funds;(b)	if so, whether any report has been submitted in this regard;(c) 	whether any chargesheet has been prepared for alleged siphoning of crores of funds under fake names and identities by officials and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	the action initiated by the Government in this regard;(e) 	whether the Government proposes to file criminal charges against all those involved in this scam and if so, the details thereof; and(f) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (f):	Yes, Madam. Complaints received in the Ministry are forwarded to the concerned States/Union Territories (UTs) for taking appropriate action including investigation. In some cases, Central Team, special National Monitors are also sent for investigation into the matter. Steps have been taken to strengthen transparency and accountability which include geo-tagging of assets, Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT), National electronic Fund Management System (Ne-FMS), Aadhaar Based Payment System (ABPS), Software for Estimate Calculation using Rural rates for Employment (SECURE) and establishment of independent social audit units and appointment of Ombudspersons in the States/UTs. Also State/UT specific reviews are undertaken from time to time. On receipt of a complaint related to State Government of Punjab, a Central Team under the leadership of senior officer has been deputed for investigation of the complaint. A detailed enquiry report was sent to the State for Action Taken Report (ATR). On recommendation of enquiry Committee, State Government has taken several steps such as recovery from responsible officers as well as disciplinary action. First Information Report (FIR) has been lodged against Gram Rojgar Sahayak (GRS) by District administration. In this regard, details are given at Annexure. In another complaint related to State of Punjab, NLM team was sent twice, but no significant irregularities were found.******** Annexure referred in reply to parts (a) to (f) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.2479 dated 09.03.2021
