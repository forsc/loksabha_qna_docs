                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

78






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






National Teachers Award




Ganesan Selvam













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the aims and objective of granting National Teachers Award to teachers;(b) the number of teachers awarded National Teacher Award this year and the financial allocation allotted and utilized during each of the last three years and the current year for the said purpose;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to give out-of-turn promotion to the award winning teachers and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the facility or the incentive being granted to award winning teachers?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)  (a) 	The objective of National Awards to Teachers is to celebrate the unique contribution of some of the finest teachers in the country and to honor those teachers who through their commitment and industry have not only improved the quality of school education but also enriched the lives of their students. (b)	This year 44 teachers are awarded National Awards to Teachers. The financial allocation allotted and utilized during each of the last three years and current year for the National Awards to Teachers is as under:<pre>Year	Budget Allocation(Rs. in Lakh)	Utilized Funds(Rs. in Lakh)2018-19	130.00	75.002019-20	134.00	113.912020-21	150.00	68.492021-22	100.00	63.45(As on 31.10.2021)</pre>(c)	No, Sir. Matter relating to promotion is under the purview of respective State/UTs.(d)	An Award Money of Rs.50,000/-, Silver Medal and a certificate signed by the Secretary, Department of School Education & Literacy are given to the awardee teachers.  ******
