                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

766






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Payment of Levy to RCF by Vistara




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of passengers who travelled on flights Vistara UK-817 (04.05 PM-06.50 PM) on July 15, 2021, U.K-837 (05.25 PM-08.15 PM) on April 14, 2021 and U.K-838 (09.05 PM- 11.55 PM) on April 12,2021; and (b) the amount of levy paid to the Regional Connectivity Fund (RCF) by Vistara during each of the above mentioned journey?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) and (b): A levy of Rs. 5000/- on each departure on certain scheduled flights irrespective of number of passengers, has been imposed by the Central Government with aircraft having MTOW (Maximum Take-Off Weight) above 40 Tonnes except for the departure of flights on routes in North East Region, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Union Territories of Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh, Andaman & Nicobar Islands and Lakshadweep Islands.
