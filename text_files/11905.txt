                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

819






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Tap Water Supply




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to supply tap drinking water to each household in the country in near future;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefore; and(c) the details of time-frame for any such proposal in near future for Delhi NCR?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA) (a) to (c) To enable every rural household with Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) across the country including NCT of Delhi, Government of India, in partnership with the States/ UTs has launched Jal Jeevan Mission which aims at providing potable water at service level of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) by 2024. The estimated outlay planned for the Mission is of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore out of which Central share is of Rs. 2.08 lakh Crore and the remaining as State share.***
