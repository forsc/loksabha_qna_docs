                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1533






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Sanction/Allocation of Funds




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has sanctioned/allocated funds under the Credit Guarantee Scheme, Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme and other credit schemes during the last four years ; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefore ?






ANSWER




                                                        FINANCE MINISTER (a) to (c) : Government allocates / sanctions funds, periodically, under Credit Guarantee Scheme and Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS) and other credit schemes to enhance availability of credit at competitive rates. Credit Guarantee Schemes are implemented across the country.  There is no specific State-wise bifurcation of the corpus.The details of funds allocated to various Credit Guarantee Schemes are placed at Annexure I.  State-wise details of subsidy provided under CLCSS during the last four years is placed at Annexure II. ******
