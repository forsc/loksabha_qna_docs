                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1019






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Projects with Russia




Riti Pathak













                                                    Rajan Baburao  Vichare
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of ongoing projects with Russia by Ministry of Petroleum andNatural Gas; and(b) the State-wise details of the number of projects started in Petroleum andNatural Gas sector since the year, 2020 including their cost?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRAPRADHAN)(a) & (b) : Indian PSUs namely ONGC Videsh Ltd (OVL), Indian OilCorporation Ltd (IOC), Oil India Ltd.(OIL), and Bharat PetroResources Ltd(BPRL) have invested in Russia’s oil & gas projects namely Taas Yuryakh,Vankorneft, License-61, Sakhalin-1 and Imperial Energy. All of them areproducing fields with cumulative investment of US$ 13630.76 million till 31March 2020.Nayara Energy (previously known as Essar Oil Limited) has announced during2019-20 to expand Phase I of its Vadinar refinery in Gujarat with an investmentof about US $ 750 million.*******
