                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2406






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Residential Coaching Programme




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                








                                                    Mahabali Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the Government has started residential coaching programme for children above the age of ten years in Jharkhand and Bihar; b) if so, the sports to which the said coaching programmes are related along with the process being adopted for this purpose’ c) the names of the districts where the said coachings are being run; and d) the number of players selected for the last Olympic and Asian Games from Jharkhand and Bihar? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)     YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS               (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)  (a) to (c) Yes Sir, Sports Authority of India (SAI) an autonomous body under this Ministry, is running five SAI Training Centre (STC), two Army Boys Sports Companies (ABSC) and one Extension Centre of STC which caters residential sports training facilities in the age group of 10-18 years in state of Bihar and Jharkhand. District wise Sports Training Facilities is at Annexure.   Decision for setting up of a new training facility in any region is taken based on the factors like conducive environment for a sports discipline in the particular geographical area and availability of resources.   (d) The list of sportspersons, representing India in international events including Olympics and Asian Games is not maintained state- wise.  *****
