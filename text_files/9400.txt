                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2960






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Representation of Minorities




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the representation of the minorities in public bodies is much below to their population; and(b) whether the Government has taken any initiative to ensure the deserving participation of minorities and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	and (b):  One of the recommendations made by the Sachar Committee was that the State Governments be advised to improve representation of minorities in local bodies on the lines of the initiative taken by the Andhra Pradesh Government by way of appropriate enactment of respective State level laws to ensure minority representation in local bodies. As per the decision taken by the Government in this regard, all the State Governments have been advised by the Ministry of Panchayati Raj and the Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs (erstwhile Ministry of Urban Development) to improve representation of minorities in rural and urban local bodies respectively. ******
