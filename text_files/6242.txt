                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

71






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Global Exchange of Cultural Delegates




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to organize global exchange of cultural delegates with different countries in near future;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of plan outlay and selection criteria for any such delegates for the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (I/C)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d)Exchange of visits of artists, writers and experts in cultural field take place from time to time between India and other countries under Cultural Exchange Programmes signed between India and other countries. However, there is no specific proposal to organize global exchange of cultural delegates with different countries.
