                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

39






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Complaints Regarding Corruption




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has received  complaints regarding alleged corruption and irregularities from Madhya Pradesh particularly in Bhopal division of West Central Railway;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of the action taken on the complaints received against the officers due to their departments being in the ‘Agreed List’ during the last two years and the current year;(d)	whether any scheme for removing corruption and irregularities as well   as making the functioning transparent has been formulated by the Government or is under the consideration of the Government; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b) Yes sir. Complaints regarding irregularity in tenders, transfer & posting of staff, catering stalls, demanding illegal gratification etc. have been received.(c)	 The complaints are investigated and action is taken as per provisions of Vigilance Manual.  Such officers borne on the Agreed List during the last two years and the current year have been transferred to non sensitive posts; in case where transfer was not possible due to unavoidable reasons, their financial powers have been withdrawn and kept watch on their working.(d) & (e) Preventive punitive and educative actions including system improvement are taken to check corruption and irregularities as well as to bring transparency in the system.******
