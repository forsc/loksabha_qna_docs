                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3668






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Ambit of Air Traffic Control System




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of such airways in the country which are not under radar or within the ambit of traffic control room;(b) the reasons for the absence of radars or air traffic control room on such airways; and(c) the steps taken to bring these airways under radar or air traffic control system?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a): All  aircraft operations/ airways within the Indian Airspace are under the ambit of Air Traffic Services.;(b) & (c): Does not arise in view of reply to point (a) above.;******
