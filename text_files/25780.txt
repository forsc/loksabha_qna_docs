                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1433






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Vaccine Passports




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Arjunlal Meena
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government plans to introduce vaccine passports to bring ease of travel to and from foreign destinations;(b)	if so, the details of the personal information which would be included in the digital vaccine passport; (c) 	whether the Government is planning to collaborate with other countries to bring out an interoperable passport and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Government has started negotiating with foreign countries and other international organizations for vaccine passports; and(e) 	if so, the details of the Countries/Organizations with which the Government has initiated talks ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (e)	There is no plan to introduce vaccine passport.  While there have been multilateral discussions, including under the framework of International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO), regarding COVID-19 vaccination certificates, no multilateral arrangements in this regard have been reached so far.  India has been part of ongoing discussions to ensure that Indian manufactured vaccines are duly recognised in any multilateral arrangement.  India is also engaging with countries for mutual recognition of vaccination certificates.***
