                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3350






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Foot Fall in Cultural Spaces




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the  Government has any plan to increase the footfall in the cultural spaces of the country; and(b)	the steps taken to make the cultural spaces more popular and attractive for the younger generation? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. Ministry of Culture is making all possible efforts to increase footfall in the cultural spaces through attached/subordinate/autonomous bodies such as: (i)	Protected monuments are conserved and maintained properly.  (ii) 	Essential tourist amenities like toilets, drinking water, wi-fi, cafeteria are provided at monuments.  (iii) 	Concessional tickets are given to students.(iv) 	Ministry has special focus on bringing the youth, specially underprivileged children to Museums & Art Galleries, in collaboration with local NGOs. Transport is arranged for the children, free art/science kit & lunch packets are distributed.(v) 	Organization of regular events in collaboration with educational and cultural institutions.(vi)  	Use of electronic, print and social media to popularize the cultural spaces.(vii) 	Conducting various art festivals, workshops, exhibitions throughout the year. (viii)  	Conducting part time evening courses and full time Diploma/ Post Diploma programmes in dance, music and visual arts.(ix)      Organizing programmes in distant and remote locations of the country.
