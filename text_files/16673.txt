                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Funds under MGNREGS




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) is running out of funds and the allocation has already been used up by mid-year in the current year while monsoon and COVID-19 has also affected work availability;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise along with the reasons therefor; and(c) 	the corrective steps being taken in this regard along with the demands received from each State and the action taken thereon for the future and action plan to tackle this situation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  No, Sir. Mahatma Gandhi NREGA is a demand driven Scheme, and the Government arranges for necessary fund as per need. (b)   Does not arise. (c)   Does not arise.
