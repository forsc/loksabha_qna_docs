                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4503






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






ROB at Nellore City




Adala Prabhakara Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government plans to construct any Railway Over Bridge (ROB) within the Nellore City Limits; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the amount sanctioned for the same;(c)	whether the Government plans to construct overbridges in the suburbs of Nellore district for the benefit of public; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir.  There are 04 level crossings (LCs) Nos. 112, 113, 117 & 118 in Nellor city.  Road Over Bridges (ROBs) has been sanctioned in lieu of 02 LCs No. 112 & 113 on cost sharing basis at an estimated cost of ` 47.11 crore and ` 46.36 crore respectively.   LC No. 117 & 118 are in Nellore station yard and qualify for construction of Railway Over Bridge/ Railway Under Bridge (ROB/RUB) on cost sharing basis as the Train Vehicle Units (TVU) at these LCs are more than 1 lakh.  Accordingly, State Govt. has been requested to submit necessary consents for sharing of cost & closure of LC.  No proposal in this regard has been received from State Government so far.     (c) & (d): Proposals for construction of Road Over Bridge/Road Under Bridge (ROB/RUB) in lieu of Level Crossings (LCs) are either conceptualized by Zonal Railways or received from State Government and other Statutory Authorities.  There are 55 LCs available in Nellore District.  Out of these ROBs/RUBs are sanctioned at 15 LCs. *****
