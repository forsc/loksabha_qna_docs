                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






National Highways in West Bengal




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of new National Highway sanctioned for construction in the State of West Bengal(WB); (b) whether the Government has received any proposal for construction of new NH from Pursura to Deulti Bagnan in Uluberia (WB);(c) if so, the action taken thereon; and (d) the funds allocated and released for development and construction of NHs in WB during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	677 km length of new National Highways (NHs) has been declared in West Bengal since 2014-15.(b) & (c)  Ministry has not received any such proposal from Government of West Bengal for construction of new National Highways from Pursura to Deulti Bagnan in Uluberia (WB).(d)	Funds allocated & expenditure incurred for development and construction of NHs in West Bengal during the last three years are as under.Year	Allocation (Rs. in Crore)	Expenditure (Rs. in Crore)2016-17	5043.82	3025.302017-18	4151.90	4144.242018-19	4354.50	3064.53*****
