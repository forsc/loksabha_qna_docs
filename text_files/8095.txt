                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Air Service under RCS-UDAN




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the cities being connected by air services under the Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS)- UDAN in the country including Maharashtra, State-wise;(b) the time by which the development of infrastructure of Jalgaon airport is likely to be completed under UDAN Scheme; and(c) the time by which air service is likely to be started to connect Jalgaon to Delhi and Pune under UDAN Scheme including night landing facility?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Details of RCS routes awarded state-wise including Maharashtra under RCS- UDAN are attached as Annexure-1.;;(b): Jalgaon airport is in operation. Air services from Jalgaon to Mumbai and Ahmedabad have commenced under RCS - UDAN.; ;(c): Jalgaon is a served airport at present. It is therefore, up to the airlines to provide air services to specific places including Jalgaon depending upon the traffic demand and commercial viability. Jalgaon is a Visual Flight Rules (VFR) airport.
