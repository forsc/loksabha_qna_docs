                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4359






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






National Law University Odisha




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Odisha Government had demanded for the reservation of 25 per cent of seats in National Law University Odisha (NLUO), Cuttack for the students belonging to Odisha?(b) if so, the details thereof and the latest position of this demand;(c) whether any progress has been made in this regard and if so, the details thereof;(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’) (a)  	Yes Sir. In response to Ministry of Law and Justice reference, National Law University Odisha (NLUO) has informed that State Government of Odisha has requested National Law University Odisha for 25% horizontal reservation for the students of Odisha domicile.(b) and (c):	The request of the State Government of Odisha was elaborately discussed and approved in the Special General Council meeting of NLU Odisha held on 04.01.2020 and subsequently notified the same.(d): 	Does not arise in view of (a) above.***
