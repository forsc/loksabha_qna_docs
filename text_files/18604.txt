                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






e-Mandi in Madhya Pradesh




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has started any scheme to introduce e-mandi in Madhya Pradesh; (b)	 if so, the details thereof, location-wise; and (c) 	the number of farmers being benefited by these mandis?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.  3   DUE FOR REPLY ON 2ND FEBRUARY, 2021.(a) to (c):	Government of India has launched National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) Scheme on 14th April, 2016. e-NAM is a virtual platform integrating physical wholesale mandis/ markets of different States/ Union Territories (UTs) to facilitate online trading of agriculture and horticulture commodities through transparent price discovery method to enable farmers to realize better remunerative prices for their produce.So far, 80 mandis of Madhya Pradesh have been integrated with e-NAM platform. The details of mandis from Madhya Pradesh integrated with e-NAM platform are at Annexure-I. Already 10.64 Lakh farmers have transacted and availed benefit of e-NAM in Madhya Pradesh.
