                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Industrial Training Institutes




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the State-wise details of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) established in the country at present;(b) 	whether there is a need to establish more ITIs in the country;(c) 	If so, the details of decision taken by the Government in this regard;(d) 	the number of ITIs proposed to be opened in the country;(e) 	whether a provision has been made to reserve seats for the persons belong to poor families; and(f) 	If, so the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Sir, the State wise details of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) running under Craftsman Training scheme (CTS) are given in Annexure – I.(b)	& (c) Sir, the State Government assess the demand and need of ITIs, no objection certificates are issued as per fulfillment of standards and norms accordingly to ITIs.(d)	Sir, the establishment of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) is State/ UT subject matter, however if no objection isreceived on a proposal for setting up of ITIs, they are examined and decided as per the affiliation standards and norms. The state wise details of new ITIs opened since 2016 are given in Annexure – II.(e)	& (f) Yes sir, Government has made provision of 10% EWS quota for Economically Weaker Sections (EWSs) in admission of ITIs & NSTIs W.e.f. from August 2019 session in addition to existing SC/ST/OBC/Ex service man/PWD as per respective State Government reservation policies.
