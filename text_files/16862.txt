                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

989






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Allocation of Funds for Chhattisgarh




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the details of allocation of funds for various works and schemes under the Ministry by the Government during last two years i.e. 2018-2020 for Chhattisgarh State; and(b)	whether any proposal has been sent by the State Government to the Union Government during last two years i.e. 2018-2020 and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The details of funds allocated /released by the Government for various works and schemes under the Ministry of Women & Child Development  by the Government during last two years i.e. 2018-20 for Chhattisgarh State are given at Annexure-I.(b)	Only one proposal under the National Creche Scheme has been received from State Government of Chhattisgarh during last two years i.e. 2018-20.  The details are as under:National Creche Scheme: Govt. of Chhattisgarh, vide letter dated 20.12.2019, had submitted a proposal for release of grants amounting to 29,75,70,000/- (GoI share) for operation of 2850 Phulwari centres & 400 AWC-cum-creches as crèches under the National Creche Scheme during the FY 2019-20. The Ministry, vide letter dated 10.07.2020, has sought clarifications on the location of the proposed centres, their proximity to existing or proposed AWC-cum-creches and the differences between Phulwari Centres & AWC-cum-creches w.r.t. the mode of operation, services to be delivered etc. ******Annexure-IStatement referred to in reply to part (a) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 989 for 18.09.2020 by Shri Vijay Baghel regarding Allocation of funds for Chhattisgarh.Scheme wise funds allocated/released during last two years i.e. 2018-20 for State Government of Chhattisgarh:(Rs.
