                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4021






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Road Projects under Bharatmala Pariyojana in Uttarakhand




Ajay Tamta














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the present status of five road projects sanctioned under Bharatmala Pariyojana in Uttarakhand;(b)	the details of road projects out of these whose tenders have been issued and the time by which the tenders for the remaining projects are likely to be issued;(c)	the funds allocated and released to Uttarakhand under Bharatmala Pariyojana so far; and (d)	the time by which the construction of these sanctioned road projects is targeted to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Total 8 projects (including residual National Highway Development Project) have been incorporated under Bharatmala Pariyojana in the State of Uttarakhand. (b) to (d) The details are at Annexure.
