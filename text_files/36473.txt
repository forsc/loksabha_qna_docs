                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3688






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Exemption from NEET in Tamil Nadu




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a delegation from Tamil Nadu has approached the Government for getting Presidential assent for the Tamil Nadu Admission to Undergraduate Medical Degree Courses Bill, 2021 which seeks to dispense with the requirement of candidates in the State to qualify in NEET for admission to undergraduate medical courses;(b) if so, the response of the Government thereon; and(c) the stand of the Government in giving exemption to students from Tamil Nadu from NEET Examination for admission to undergraduate medical courses?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) deals with the Bills reserved by the Governor for assent of Hon’ble President of India. As informed by MHA no such bill has been received for assent of the Hon’ble President of India.  *****
