                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Recharge of Ground Water




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds provided to Andhra Pradesh from NABARD to recharge ground water through various methods during the last three years and the current year, year-wise; (b) whether any grant/incentive/subsidy component under the above funds released to Andhra Pradesh by NABARD; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) As per the information received from concerned Department of Govt. of Andhra Pradesh, they have not received NABARD funds to recharge ground water during last three  years and current year.Further, as per information received from NABARD they have released funds for watershed development projects in Andhra Pradesh, which may contribute to improvement in ground water resources. Details are as under:                                                                                                                                         (In Rs Lakhs)Fund	No. of Projects	Sanction of grant	Disbursement of grant			2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Watershed Development Fund	27	1126.93	136.11	60.21	311.69*****
