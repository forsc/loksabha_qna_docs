                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4523






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Centres for Physically Challenged Persons




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the details and the number of centres working in various States, particularly in Maharashtra for the empowerment of physically challenged persons; and(b)	 the details of the grants provided by the Government particularly to the State of Maharashtra during the last three years, centre-wise and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a)	and (b) Relief to the persons with disabilities is a State subject by virtue of entry ‘9’ of the State list of the Constitution of India. However, the Central Government supplements the efforts of the State Governments for the welfare of persons with disabilities.There are nine National Institutes (NIs) under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) specializing in different kinds of disabilities which are working, inter-alia, for rehabilitation and empowerment of persons with disabilities (PwDs). 21 Composite Regional Centres (CRCs) have been approved in various States/UTs as outreach Centres/extended arms of the National Institutes under the Department. Details of these institutions, including those in Maharashtra and funds released to them during the last three years are at Annexure I.In addition, the Department also implements a Central Sector Schemes, namely, Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS) and District Disability Rehabilitation Centres (DDRCs). Under DDRS, grant-in-aid is provided to Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) running projects for the welfare of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) including special school for physically challenged persons aimed at enabling them to maintain their optimal, physical, sensory, intellectual, psychiatric or social functional levels. Financial assistance is also provided to set up and operate DDRCs at District level. The details indicating Centre-wise amount released in the State of Maharashtra during the last three years under DDRS and DDRC are at Annexure-II.*****
