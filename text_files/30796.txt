                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2389






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Inclusion of Real Stories in School curriculum




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has initiated any programme to include the real stories ofpeople who laid down their lives in the freedom struggle of the country in the schooland University curriculum for future generations of the country,(b) if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor; and(c) whether any such programmes/scheme is proposed in the near future and if so, thedetails thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (c) The National Council of Educational Research and Training(NCERT)has given appropriate space to freedom fighters who participated in the freedomstruggle of India in its textbooks of Social Sciences and History from the upperprimary to the senior secondary stages. In these textbooks, there are several referencesto stories of the heroic freedom fighters who were part of the freedom struggle ofIndia.****
