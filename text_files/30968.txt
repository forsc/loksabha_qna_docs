                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2449






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






SANKALP Scheme in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the amount of grant under SANKALP Scheme given to both the aspirational districts of Kalahandi Lok Sabha Constituency including other districts of Odisha; and(b)       the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       A total of amount of Rupees One crore (Rupees ten lakh per aspirational district) was released to the Government of Odisha under SANKALP scheme for the ten aspirational districts, including the two aspirational districts of Kalahandi Lok Sabha Constituency namely Nuapada and Kalahandi.The ten aspirational districts of the Government of Odisha to whom the grants were released are (i) Malkangiri; (ii) Nabarangpur; (iii) Nuapada; (iv) Kandhamala; (v) Balangir; (vi) Kalahandi; (vii) Rayagada; (viii) Koraput; (ix) Gajapati; and (x) Dhenkanal.(b)       The grant was sanctioned for the following activities to be undertaken in the aspirational districts:i.	To assess the current skill profile of the district;ii.	To constitute a District Skill Development Committee headed by District Magistrate/ Collector for monitoring institutions like Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs), Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK), Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) and Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDUGKY) centres and provide a direction to skill related activities;iii.	To develop District Skill Development Plan by conducting skill gap studies or other measures and support the implementation of the identified measures;iv.	To implement action plan based on District Skill Development Plans;v.	Any other skill development related activity as may be required by the District.
