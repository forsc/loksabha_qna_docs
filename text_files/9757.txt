                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Reduction in Consumption of Kerosene




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of households who have been provided LPG connection under the Ujjwala Scheme so far, State-wise including Jharkhand;(b) the total reduction in consumption of kerosene oil due to provision of LPG to households under the said Scheme, State/ UT-wise; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to provide LPG connection to all rural households in the country including in Chatra, Latehar and Panki districts of Jharkhand?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 296 BY SHRI SUNIL KUMAR SINGH TO BE ANSWERED ON 9TH DECEMBER 2019 REGARDING ‘REDUCTION IN CONSUMPTION OF KEROSENE’.(a) The target of providing 8 crore deposit free LPG connections to adult women of poor households under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) has been achieved in September, 2019. The State/UT-wise details including the State of Jharkhand are at Annexure-I.(b) In view of increase in LPG coverage, the allocation of PDS Kerosene has been rationalized across the country and decreasing over the years. The allocation made to States/UTs from year 2017-18 to 2019-20 (upto the 3rd Quarters) are at Annexure-II.(c) Release of LPG connections is a continuous process. LPG distributors are under instructions to immediately register any request for new LPG connection and Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) endeavour to release the same within 7 working days. As on 01.11.2019, there are 27.44 crore LPG customers in the country which cover 96.6 % households including Chatra, Latehar and Panki districts of Jharkhand.***
