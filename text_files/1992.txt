                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Udaipur-Ahmedabad Gauge Conversion




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the broad gauge conversion work of rail line between 	Udaipur and Ahmedabad via Himmatnagar has been 	completed; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated from 	the beginning of the project to the current financial year; (c)	if not, the time by which Railways has set the target to 	complete the said project along with the details thereof; and (d)	the details of incomplete works under the said project and the 	details of additional funds required to complete the project?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. *****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1833 BY SHRI ARJUN LAL MEENA TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 03.07.2019 REGARDING UDAIPUR-AHMEDABAD GAUGE CONVERSION  (a) to (d): Ahmedabad-Himmatnagar-Udaipur gauge conversion including Modasa-Shamlaji Road new line project (317.73 Km) was sanctioned and the latest cost of the project is `1667 crore and expenditure of `1291.14 crore has been incurred upto March, 2019. An outlay of `532.38 crore has been provided for the complete project during 2019-20. Due to limited availability of funds in the earlier years, there has been little progress on this project, however, Since 2014-15, the Budget allotment for this project has improved and the project execution work has been expedited. Gauge conversion work of Ahmedabad-Himmatnagar         (87.25 Km) section has been completed upto March, 2019 and work has been taken up in balance Himmatnagar-Udaipur section and project is progressing well. Also, sufficient funds have been made available during 2019-20 to the project for early completion. On this project, challenging work of long tunnel of around 850 metre and 2 short tunnels of around 150 metre and 100 metre length are to be constructed, for which, agencies have been finalized and construction work taken up. During financial year 2019-20, 24 Km long section from Himmatnagar-Raighadh has already been completed and 24.26 Km long section from Udaipur-Kharwachanda is also planned for completion during the same year, while entire project is planned for completion by financial year 2020-21.*****
