                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2879






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Road Projects in Maharashtra




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the on-going road construction projects in the State of Maharashtra during the year 2019-2020; and(b)	the details of road construction projects being executed by National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) in the State along with the financial allocation made for the year 2019-2020?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) There are a total of 210 on-going road construction (National Highway) projects in a length of 8706 Km amounting to Rs. 96355 Cr., which includes 52 projects being executed by NHAI in a length of 2440 Km amounting to Rs. 40273 Cr. in the State of Maharashtra. There is no State wise allocation for NHAI projects. However, funds are released by NHAI based on project wise requirement and the expenditure incurred during the year 2019-20 over on-going NHAI projects in the state of Maharashtra is Rs. 3770.41 Cr.*****
