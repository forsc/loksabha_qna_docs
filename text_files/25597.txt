                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1472






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Construction of ROBs/RUBs




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received proposals from the Government of Maharashtra for the construction of Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) and Road Under Bridges(RUBs) during the last three  years along with the details thereof, proposal-wise;(b)	the reaction of the Government to the said proposals;(c)	the number of proposals from Maharashtra that are lying pending with the Railways at present along with the details of the said proposals, location-wise; and(d)	the details of the ROBs and RUBs being constructed in Maharashtra at present, location-wise along with the current status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
