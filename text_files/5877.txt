                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5337






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Status of Small Hydro Power Projects




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the estimated potential generation from the existing small hydro-power projects in the country;(b) the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to exploit the potential of such projects;(c) the status of small hydro power projects under construction along with the number of projects functioning in the country, State/UT-wise;(d) the quantum of funds allocated/ and released to the States/UTs for the development of small hydro power projects during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise; and(e) the other steps taken by the Government to achieve the target of exploiting power from small hydro power projects?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER AND MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP (SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) The estimated potential generation from existing 4604.81 MW installed capacity is 12101.44 MU at 30% Plant Load Factor (PLF). (b) The Ministry of New & Renewable Energy brings out Small Hydro Power Schemes from time to time to provide Central Financial Assistance to both Government and Private sector for setting up Small Hydro Power (SHP) projects.(c) The aggregate capacity of Small Hydro Power Projects commissioned by 30th June 2019 in the country was 4604.81 MW. 116 SHP Projects aggregating to 589.99 MW are under construction. The State wise number of under construction small hydro power projects and the projects functioning in the country are given in Annexure A.(d) Year wise, State wise funds released as Central Financial Assistance for SHP Projects set up in various States from FY 2016-17 to FY 2018-19 are given in  Annexure B.(e) Development of Small Hydro Sector is mainly governed by the policies of different State Governments.  Small Hydro Projects are being set up both by Government entities and private developers as per respective State Government policies.  The Central Government has been providing support for the development of Small Hydro Projects both in Government and Private sector inter alia, in following ways:(i) providing support for carrying out Detailed Survey and Investigation (DSI) and preparation of DPRs,(ii) supporting renovation and modernization of old small hydro projects in Government sector,(iii) preparation/ updation of data base of prospective small hydro projects,(iv) support to Alternate Hydro Energy Centre (now Department of Hydro & Renewable Energy) at IIT Roorkee to set up state of art laboratory to provide technical and training support to SHP developers,(v)  providing financial support for setting up new small hydro projects under Govt. & Private Sector for the projects as per prevailing scheme guidelines.*****
