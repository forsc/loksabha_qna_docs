                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Railway Accidents




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether death toll due to Railway accidents in the country is on the rise from the last five years;(b) if so, the details of the railway accidents, zone-wise and year-wise for the last five years in Northern States;(c) the details of number of deaths in railway accidents in the country, zone-wise and year-wise for the last five years; and(d) the details of the total compensation awarded to the families of deceased due to train accidents for the last five years, zone-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) No, Sir.(b) Does not arise.(c) & (d): Year-wise details of deaths and compensation paid in rail accidents is as under:Year	No. of Deaths	Amount of compensation2015-16	      148	` 2,32,49,785/-2016-17	     192	` 2,64,27,604/-2017-18	      97	` 1,29,13,005/-2018-19	      18	` 4,96,52,856/-2019-20 (as on 31st Jan)07	` 2,80,81,205/-*****
