                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4717






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






IIPE Campus at Visakhapatnam




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has sanctioned budget for construction of  Indian Institute of Petroleum & Energy (IIPE) campus at Visakhapatnam; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government or Oil companies have made any contributions towards IIPE endowment fund;(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(e)	whether the Government has fixed any time frame to contribute the entire promised amount to IIPE endowment fund; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)	&  (b): The  Government has approved a capital expenditure of Rs. 655.46 crore for construction of Indian Institute of Petroleum and Energy (IIPE) Campus at Visakhapatnam, out of which an amount of Rs.184.85 crore has already been released. (c)	to    (f):   An amount of Rs.200.00 crore has been released by Government towards an endowment fund of IIPE. A matching amount of Rs. 199.40 crore as endowment fund has been provided by Oil India Limited (OIL), GAIL (India) Limited (GAIL), Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL), Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited (HPCL) and Oil & Natrural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC) together. Thus, the entire amount of endowment fund as committed has already been released to IIPE. ****
