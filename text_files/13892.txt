                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2744






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK) launched in 2014 including the utility and achievements of the scheme for the benefit of the people of the country; and(b) the number of youths benefited through RKSK programme so far, State/UT-wise including Varanasi and Jaunpur in Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	Government is implementing Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK) to address the need and concerns of adolescents of the country.  The key achievements under the various component of the programme are:I.	Facility based approach-	A total of 7947 Adolescent Friendly Health Clinics (AFHCs) established till September 2019. 56.09 lakh adolescents received counselling and clinical services at these AFHCs till December 2019. II.	Menstrual Hygiene Scheme – 3.43 crore adolescent girls have been covered under this scheme in FY 2019-20 both through State and Centre’s budget III.	Weekly Iron Folic acid Supplementation programme - 3.96 crore adolescent boys and girls, both in and out of school provided IFA supplementation every week till January 2020 (data-HMIS) IV.	Adolescent Health Days (AHDs)- Adolescent Health Days (AHDs) are quarterly village level activities conducted to create awareness about adolescent health issues and available services within the community. Till December 2019 60,474  AHDs have been conducted .(b):	State/UT-wise data of adolescents benefitting through the Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram placed at Annexure.
