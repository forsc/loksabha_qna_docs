                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3388






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Wasted Paddy Stock




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has stated in its reply dated 15-12-2021, that ‘no paddy stock was wasted or got damaged due to storage in open spaces‘ despiteample evidence available publicly that suggested otherwise; (b) 	if so, whether approximately 2000 tons of grains were damaged in 2020-21 and according to FCI, 40,546 tons of food grain was damaged since 2014; (c) 	whether in 2015-16, on the recommendation of Shanta Kumar Committee, the Government decided to increase the additional capacity under silos to 100 lakh tons by 2019-20; (d) 	if so, the details of the funds earmarked, actual expenditure made and progress made on the project so far; (e) 	whether out of the awarded 30.75 MLT of storage to be constructed till FY20, only about 10 MLT of storage has been built till date; if so, the reasons for the delay and the progress made in this regard; and (f) 	the details of the costs and contracts awarded for the project?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	Yes Sir. No paddy stock was damaged in Food Corporation of India (FCI) since 2014.…..2/--2(b): 	During the year 2020-21, a quantity of 1850 MT of rice and wheat got damaged mainly due to flood in Odisha. The quantum of foodgrains, which got damaged in godowns of FCI since 2014-15 to 2021-2022 (28.02.2022) is as under:Year	Accrued quantity of damaged foodgrains      (in Lakh Tonnes)	Offtake quantity (excluding DCP states)                  (in Lakh Tonnes)	% Damaged foodgrain against offtake quantity2014-15	0.19	501.75	0.0372015-16	0.03	490.15	0.0062016-17 	0.09	473.31	0.0192017-18 	0.027	452.16	0.0062018-19 	0.05	500.08	0.0102019-20	0.02	455.126	0.0042020-21	0.02	688.57	0.0032021-22 	0.017	691.403	0.002These got damaged mainly due to natural calamities like cyclone, floods, rains etc.(c): 	Yes Sir.(d): 	Silo projects are being executed on Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode wherein overall investment is to be made by the Private Investors and no separate funds are earmarked for construction of Silos.(e): 	As on date, out of Silo projects at 58 locations with 29.25 LMT, projects at 22 locations with 11.125 LMT capacity has been completed/put to use and remaining projects are under various stages of development.
