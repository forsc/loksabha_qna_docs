                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Child Tracking System




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that as per a recent study, the number of schools in the Unified District Information System for Education (UDISE) has declined over the last few years;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise during the last three years, year-wise along with reasons thereto;(c) whether the Government has any plans for early implementation of the child tracking system envisaged in the National Education Policy (NEP);(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e) the details of the benefits likely to be accrued to the students studying in schools by introduction of a child tracking system?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) and (b): The Department of School Education and Literacy (DoSEL), Ministry of Education has developed the Unified District Information System for Education Plus (UDISE+) system to record data on indicators of school education provided by all the States and Union Territories. As per Unified District Information System for Education Plus (UDISE+), total number of schools for the years 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, State- wise are at Annexure.(c) to (e): Under Samagra Shiksha, financial assistance to states and UTs for child tracking has been introduced.******
