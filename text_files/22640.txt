                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3710






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Objectives and Features of Pey Jal Survekshan




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government proposes Pey Jal Survekshan to be 	conducted to ascertain equitable distribution of water, reuse 	of wastewater and mapping of water bodies through a 	challenge process in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details and the criteria fixed for selection of cities for Pey 	Jal Survekshan in the country;(d)	the funds allocated and released under the project so far; and(e)	whether any time frame has been fixed for completion of the 	project and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	 & (b) : Yes, sir. Under the proposed Jal Jeevan Mission- Urban, Pey Jal Survekshan is proposed in selected cities as a challenge process to assess the compliance of service level benchmarks with respect to quality, quantity and coverage of water supply in the city, capacity of Primary/Secondary Sewage Treatment Plant / Faecal Sludge Treatment Plant and volume of wastewater treated and reuse, etc. This will also assess the steps taken for reduction of Non Revenue Water (NRW) through water clusters and rejuvenation of water bodies.(c)	to (e) :  The criteria for selection of cities, fund allocation and time frame for Pey Jal Survekshan will be worked out after the Mission gets approved by the Government.                                   *****
