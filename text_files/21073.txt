                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

204






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Inclusion of Agricultural Produce under MSP




Abdul Majeed Ariff













                                                    S. Jothimani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government intends to include more agricultural produce in the list for providing Minimum Support Price (MSP) and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has received any representation from the State of Kerala for inclusion of Pepper and Raw Coconut in the list of agricultural produce for providing MSP and if so, the status thereof; and (c) 	the   details   of agricultural produce procured under MSP from FY 2018-19 to FY 2020-21 till 31st January 2021, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 204     DUE FOR REPLY ON 9TH MARCH, 2021.(a):	 At present, there are twenty two (22) mandated agricultural crops for which the Government of India (GoI) fixes Minimum Support Price (MSP) for every marketing season. These crops are paddy, jowar, bajra, maize, ragi, arhar, moong, urad, groundnut, soybean, sunflower, sesamum, nigerseed, cotton, wheat, barley, gram, masur (lentil), rapeseed/mustard, safflower, jute and copra.  Fair & Remunerative Price for sugarcane is also fixed.  In addition, MSP for toria and de-husked coconut are also fixed on the basis of MSP of rapeseed/mustard and copra, respectively. These prices are fixed on the basis of the recommendations of the Commission for Agricultural Costs & Prices (CACP), views of State Governments and Central Ministries/ Departments concerned and other relevant factors. 	GoI receives various proposals from time to time to include more and more agricultural produce in the list of crops for providing MSP. Since 2018-19, MSP is fixed at minimum of 50 percent over and above the all- India weighted average projected cost of production of crops.  (b):	 No representation has been received from the State Government of Kerala for inclusion of pepper and raw coconut under MSP. 		MSP for Milling Copra and Ball Copra is announced for every crop season. On the basis of MSP of Copra, MSP of de-husked coconut is fixed. Details are given in Annexure I. (c):	 Statement showing State-wise details of agriculture produce procured under MSP from 201819 to 2020-21 (31.01.2021) are annexed at Annexures from II to VIII.
