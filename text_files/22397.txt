                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

312






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Implementation of PMGDISHA in Rajasthan




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology be pleased to state:-(a)	the number of individuals educated and certified as digitally literate under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan (PMGDISHA) as on date; (b)	whether the implementation of the said scheme has been sluggish in the State of Rajasthan; (c)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (d)	the remedial steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)  to (d):     A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED  QUESTION NO.*312  FOR 17-03-2021 REGARDINGIMPLEMENTATION OF PMGDISHA IN RAJASTHAN…….. (a): Under the PMGDISHA Scheme, as on 12.03.2021, around 4.54 Crore candidates have been enrolled, more than 3.69 Crore candidates have completed the training out of which 2.71 Crore have been certified by duly authorized 3rd party Assessment Agencies.(b) to (d): In the state of Rajasthan, as against the stipulated target of 37,12,000, so far a total of around  22,46,741 candidates have been enrolled and 17,73,993 have been trained, out of which 13,00,509 candidates have been certified under the scheme.Since March 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic resulted in widespread impact on the implementation of PMGDISHA scheme across the country including the state of Rajasthan. Due to COVID-19 and ensuing Lockdowns imposed by Central /State Governments, the training centres had to be kept closed for around six months. After the easing of lockdown restrictions in September 2020, the enrolment, training and certification activities have been opened up in a phased manner while following the social distancing norms. Now the Scheme implementation has picked up across the country including in the state of Rajasthan. After the onset of pandemic, a total of 90,14,374 candidates have been registered, 65,34,990 candidates have been trained, out of which 55,85,613 candidates have been duly certified across the country. Out of this, 4,07,541 candidates have been registered, 2,83,577 candidates have been trained and 2,20,993 candidates have been certified in the state of Rajasthan since the onset of pandemic. *******
