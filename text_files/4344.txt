                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4074






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Import of Soybean




Sunil Baburao Mendhe













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether India’s soyabean imports have hit a record high during the last three years and more buying is expected as domestic supplies tighten following lower production last year; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether traders have signed any deals to sell soyabeans mainly for shipping from the African countries as well as from other countries; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a)	& (b) Import and domestic production data of Soybeans for the last three years is as under:-(Qty. in Lakh MT/Value in US$ Million)Item : Soybean	Domestic Production 	Import Year	Qty.	Qty	Value2016-17	132	0.81	42.372017-18	109	0.89	41.712018-19	137*	1.57	78.57*3rd Advance Estimate(c)	 No such information is available. (d)	Question does not arise. ***
