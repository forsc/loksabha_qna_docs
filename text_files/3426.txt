                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Assistance for MSMEs in North Eastern Region




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the measures and the action taken by the Government for promotion, financial assistance and technology support for Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the North eastern region as per MSME Act;(b)	whether the government proposes any new steps for  generation of more employment by providing  more assistance to the MSMEs in the region; and(c)	  	 if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Government implements Schemes / Programmes  such as Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS),  Lean Manufacturing  Competitiveness Scheme (LMCS), Design Clinic scheme(DCS), Digital MSME (DM), Technology Centres System programme (TCSP), Marketing Assistance & Technology Up-gradation (MATU), Zero Defect Zero Effect (ZED), Micro & Small Enterprises –Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), Scheme on Technology Enterprise & Resource centre(TERC) for the promotion, financial assistance and Technology Support for Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the entire country including North eastern region (NER) .                                                  (b)&(c):     Government    also   implements   schemes    such   as   Prime   Minister’s  Employment Generation Scheme   (PMEGP)   and   Business   Incubation  Scheme to create more employment opportunities in the MSME sector in  the entire country including NER.
