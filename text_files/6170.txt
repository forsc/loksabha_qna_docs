                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5564






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Development of Textile Industry




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has allocated any funds to develop textile industry and corresponding community facility in Ranipur textile area of Jhansi;(b)	if so, the amount sanctioned therefor, year-wise; (c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of the measures taken by the Government to develop textile industry in above-mentioned area of Jhansi and its vicinity?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d):	The Government is supporting the Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) formed by the Textile entrepreneurs and individual textile units for development of common infrastructure, common facilities, Group Worksheds and Installation of Technologically upgraded machineries in the form of subsidies under various schemes viz. Scheme for Integrated Textile Park, Integrated Processing Development Scheme, Group Workshed Scheme, Common Facility Centre and Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme, Schemes for the development of the Powerloom Sector(Power-Tex), Schemes for Technical Textiles,  SAMARTH- The Scheme for Capacity Building in Textile Sector (SCBTS), Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS), Silk Samagra, Rebate of State and Centre Taxes and Levies (ROSCTL) etc. These schemes are aimed at promotion/upgradation of textile industry all over the country including Jhansi. Further, there is no State wise allocation of funds under the above schemes, the Government sanction/release funds to the eligible agencies/organizations on receipt of viable proposal recommended by the concerned State Government.  Two proposals for marketing of handloom products for organizing exhibitions in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh were received and approved, one each in the year 2017-18 and 2018-19 and a sum of Rs. 6.00 lakh for each exhibition was sanctioned/released.  *****
