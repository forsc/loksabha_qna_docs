                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2385






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Funds Allocation to Universities




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated to Universities during the last three years and thecurrent year, State-wise including Chhattisgarh, University-wise and Year-wise;(b) whether the Government has received proposals/requests for allocation of fundsfrom the Universities of Chhattisgarh including Swami Vivekanand Technical Universityduring the above said period;(c) if so, the details and the status thereof along with the funds allocated for thepurpose;(d) whether the Government/University Grants Commission provides grant torecognised Universities established by the State Governments; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(Dr. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (c): Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) is a Centrally SponsoredScheme (CSS) being implemented by the Ministry of Education. RUSA seeks toimprove the overall quality of existing States higher educational institutions by ensuringtheir conformity to prescribed norms and standards and adoption of accreditation asmandatory quality assurance framework; correcting regional imbalances by facilitatingaccess to high quality institutions in rural and semi urban areas. Under RUSA,Universities are supported under the various components such as Creation ofUniversities by way of upgradation of autonomous colleges, Creation of universities byconversion of colleges in a cluster, Enhancing Quality and Excellence in Select StateUniversities, Infrastructure grants to Universities etc. State-wise details of fund releasedto various Universities under RUSA is at Annexure -I. In the year 2019-20, totalcentral share of Rs. 36.0 crore was released to 7 Universities of Chhattisgarh, namelySarguja University, Ambikapur, Bastar University, Jagdalpur, Pt. Ravi Shankar ShuklaUniversity, Raipur, Bilaspur University, Bilaspur, Kushabhau Thakre Patrakarita AvamJansanchar Vishvavidhyalya, Raipur, Indira Kala evam Sangeet Vishvavidhyalya,Khairagarh, and Durg University, Durg. The State-wise details of Universities benefittedunder various components of RUSA Scheme is enclosed at Annexure-II.(d) & (e) : The UGC provides financial assistance to eligible colleges which are includedunder Section 2 (f) and declared fit to receive central assistance (UGC grant) underSection 12 (B) of UGC Act, 1956 as per approved pattern of assistance under variousschemes. However, presently there is no such scheme in operation to provide grants toState Universities.***
