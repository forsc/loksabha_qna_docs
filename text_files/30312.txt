                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1973






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Revenue from Petrol and Diesel




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has collected Rs. 1.60 lakh crores from central taxes on diesel and Rs. 1.12 lakh crores as central taxes on petrol during financial year 2020-21; (b) if so, the details thereof, month-wise and item-wise; and (c) the details of collection as central taxes during 2021-22 till 15th November, 2021 on petrol and diesel, month-wise and petroleum product-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) to (c):	Total contribution of petroleum sector to exchequer, for the year 2020-21 is Rs. 6,72,719 crore, out of which share of Central Exchequer is 68% and for the year 2021-22 (April-September, provisionally) is Rs. 3,49,923 crore, out of which share of Central Exchequer is 62%. x-x-x-x-x
