                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Dream-Liner Flight Services




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Air India has recently stopped all domestic Dream Liner flight services in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for such decision;(c) whether there is any plan to restart the service in any sector from any of the Indian Airports and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government has received any requests from passengers to restart the Dream Liner flight in Dubai- Kochi-Delhi sector and if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) and (b): No, Sir. Only one Flight AI-020/AI-701 Routing Delhi-Kolkata-Delhi has been converted from Dreamliner to Air Bus A-320 aircraft w.e.f. 2nd July 2019 due to HAJ  operations 2019.;;(c):  Air India plans restarting/converting AI-020/701 Routing Delhi-Kolkata-Delhi & back from Air Bus A-320 aircraft to Dreamliner after concluding the HAJ operations 2019 i.e 15th September, 2019.;;(d):  Air India has already started the Dreamliner service on the sector Delhi-Kochi-Dubai and vice versa effective 01st July 2019.
