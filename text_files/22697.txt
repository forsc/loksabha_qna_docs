                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3697






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Relief to Small Entrepreneurs




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering to provide relief to the small entrepreneurs seriously;(b)	if so, whether the Government has taken any steps in this direction so far;(c)  	if so, the details thereof; and(d)  	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (d):	The Ministry runs various  schemes and programmes and  initiatives for the promotion, development and enhancing the competiveness of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprise (MSME) sector. These initiatives are reviewed from time to time for the advantage of MSME sector. Eligible small enterprises also take benefit/relief from these interventions. In this regard, the following Schemes may be mentioned:  •	Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme •	Credit Guarantee Trust for MSE  •	Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme •	Public Procurement Policy •	Online portals such as Samadhan, Sampark, Sambandh, TReDS and GeM Moreover, an economic package was also announced under Atmanirbhar Bharat for MSMEs, which has been a great relief to overcome the adverse effects of lockdown due to COVID 19.
