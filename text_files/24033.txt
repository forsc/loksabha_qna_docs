                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Air Connectivity from Kanpur




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether flights have been started from Kanpur (Chakeri) airport of Uttar Pradesh for different cities of the country as on date and if so, the details thereof along with the names of the flights started operating therefrom;(b) whether the Government has proposed to connect Kanpur by air with other cities also; and(c) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which it is likely to be finalized?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): As per the winter schedule, 2020, scheduled flights at the following routes, are operating to/from Kanpur airport:-;i) Delhi-Kanpur-Delhi;ii) Mumbai-Kanpur-Mumbai Further, Ministry of Civil Aviation launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) on 21-10-2016 to stimulate regional air connectivity and making air travel affordable to the masses. RCS - UDAN is a market driven mechanism. The interested airlines based on their assessment of demand on particular routes submit their proposals at the time of bidding under RCS - UDAN from time to time.;Airports Authority of India(AAI), the implementing agency, has offered following routes as requested by the Government of Uttar Pradesh for connecting Kanpur in special round of bidding UDAN 4.1 under RCS:-1.     Kanpur -Bareilly -Kanpur2.     Kanpur-Saharanpur-Kanpur3.     Kanpur- Ayodhya -Kanpur4.     Kanpur- Gorakhpur -Kanpur5.     Kanpur- Kushinagar-Kanpur6.     Kanpur- Varanasi -Kanpur;If any valid bid is received for connecting Kanpur from the interested airline operators, the same shall be considered as per the provisions of Scheme Document.
