                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2193






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Conservation of Pillars of Ancient Temples




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Government will take steps for the conservation of pillars of ancient temples that have engravings in various languages and most of whichare getting ruined these days especially in Tamilnadu;(b)	whether the Government proposes to conserve them as historical monuments in the museums after decoding the said engravings; and (c)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	The conservation work of protected monuments including pillars of ancient temples with engravings is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) and they are in a good state of conservation. Such procedure is followed for all protected monuments in the country including Tamilnadu.(b)  & (c)	The preference is given for in situ conservation and preservation of the pillars having engravings. However, where issues of safety and security are involved these are shifted and preserved in museums.
