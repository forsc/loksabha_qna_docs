                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2838






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Water Recycling Plants




Rekha Arun Verma













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any provision to recycle water under the drinking water schemes in Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of the water recycling plants set up in the rural areas of Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b) Government of India is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in partnership withStates to make provision of potable tap water supply to every rural household in the country,by2024. Further, under Swachh Bharat Mission–Gramin (SBM–G) Phase-II, which is also under implementation, provisions have been made for the management of grey water generated in villages.The grey water treatment and its reuse is an important component, while planning for rural drinking water schemes under the Jal Jeevan Mission. Under Jal Jeevan Mission, provision has been made for the preparation of Village Action Plan (VAP) by local village community, inter alia which includes treatment and reuse of grey water in convergence with other schemes such as MGNREGS, 15th Finance Commission tied grants to rural local bodies/ PRIs for water & sanitation, SBM (G), District Mineral Development Fund, CSR fund, community contribution, etc. (c) As such, details of plants for recycling of water, are not maintained in this Department.*****
