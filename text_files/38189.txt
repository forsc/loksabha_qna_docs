                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5079






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Complaints of Irregularities in Electoral bonds




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether any complaints have been received about irregularities and pressures in issuing electoral bonds;(b)	If so, the details thereof; and(c)	The future action plan in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY) (a) to (c): The electoral bonds are issued as per the provisions of the Electoral Bonds Scheme. The Government has not received any complaints about irregularities and pressures in issuing electoral bonds.
