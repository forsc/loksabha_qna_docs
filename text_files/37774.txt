                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4975






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Saksham Anganwadi




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of Saksham Anganwadis established across the country, State/UT- wise and district level for Rajasthan;(b)	whether the Anganwadi workers have been adequately trained to use the new  technologies installed in the upgraded Anganwadis and if so, the number of  training sessions conducted and workers who participated therein, State-wise  and district level for Rajasthan; (c)	the total amount of funds disbursed as of February 2022 towards establishing  Saksham Anganwadis, State-wise; (d)	whether the Government has any action plan for the year 2022-23 to build more saksham anganwadis and if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the number of children and mothers serviced under the saksham anganwadis as of February 2022, State-wise and district-wise for Rajasthan?  






ANSWER




(a)        As on date, no Saksham Anganwadi has been established in the country.(b)   to (e)   Does not arise.  ******
