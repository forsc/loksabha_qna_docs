                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

484






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Vande Bharat Mission




Umesh G. Jadhav













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                








                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Faizal P.P. Mohammed
                                                








                                                    Ajay (Teni) Misra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Indians being rescued under the Vande Bharat Mission from various countries during the lockdown arisen by COVID-19; (b) if so, the details thereof, country-wise along with the number of Indians still to be brought back under Vande Bharat Mission in these countries as on date; (c) the number of rescue missions led by India for the evacuation of Indians with the number of people saved through such operations, country-wise and the response of the other countries in this regard;(d) whether the Government has taken/ is taking effective steps in consultation with other countries for the safe evacuation of the rest of the persons trapped in these countries; and (e) if so, the details thereof and the success achieved in this regard, so far?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRYOF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (e)	As on 11 September 2020, more than 13,85,670 Indians have returned to India under the Vande Bharat Mission from various countries. The country-wise list of Indians who have returned to India is attached at Annexure-I. Stranded Indians with the most compelling reasons have all safely returned to their homes. Our Missions are in regular touch with Indians resident in their country of accreditation and are facilitating their return to India on request. Those wishing to return to India can directly book with the concerned airlines under the Vande Bharat Mission or approach the Indian Missions in this regard. ***
