                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Setting up of Cultural Museum in North Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether the Ministry taking into cognizance the rich cultural diversity of the North Bengal districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong, Jalpaiguri, Alipurduar, Cooch Behar and adjoining areas, proposes to set up a cultural museum in the region to promote and preserve the local indigenous culture and traditions; and(b)   the details of assistance provided for Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts under various schemes of the Ministry during each of the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (b):  A Statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. * 288 FOR REPLY ON 09.08.2021(a)	Ministry of Culture operates a ''Museum Grant Scheme'' for preservation and protection of art and culture by providing financial assistance to State Governments/Voluntary Bodies/Societies/Trusts for setting up of museums. At present, Ministry of Culture has not received any proposal for setting up a cultural museum in North Bengal region.  	However, taking into cognizance the rich cultural diversity of this region, Ministry of Culture has already set up the following Museums/Science Centres:- i.	Cooch Behar Palace Museum -  Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) under Ministry of Culture is maintaining and managing this museum in Cooch Behar. The museum displays artifacts of 17th-18th century related to the Cooch dynasty.ii.	North Bengal Science Centre(NBSC), Siliguri in Darjeeling district under Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science (SPoCS). iii.	Sub-Regional Science Centre in Kalimpong district under SPoCS.iv.	Innovation hubs were also set up in NBSC, Siliguri and in Sub-Regional Science Centre, Kalimpong.	Besides, Eastern Zonal Cultural Centre(EZCC), Kolkata under this Ministry has been engaged in promoting and preserving rural, folk and tribal culture of West Bengal. For this purpose, EZCC has been organizing a series of folk and tribal festivals. Most of the art forms of Darjeeling, Kalimpong, Jalpaiguri, Alipurduar, Cooch Behar and adjoining areas have been showcased by EZCC in its different programs.(b)	The details of financial assistance provided for Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts under various schemes of the Ministry during each of the last five years are as under:-Darjeeling                               (in Rs.
