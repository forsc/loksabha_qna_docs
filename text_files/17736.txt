                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1561






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Performance of CPSEs




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the performances of the Public Sector Enterprises (PSEs) under the Government during the      first half of 2020;(b) the results of the navratna companies during this period in critical sectors; and(c) whether there has been any cost cutting efforts in these companies leading to job losses and       if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a), (b) & (c):  The Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) have been discharging their functions during the year 2020 so as to ensure continuous supply of essential goods and services such as Power, LPG & Fuel, Telecom & Internet connectivity, logistics and other similar services, etc. across the country.  The details of financial performance of Navratna CPSEs in the first quarter (April-June) of FY 2020-21 is provided at Annexure-1. Further, as per the information furnished by Navratna CPSEs, though various austerity measures have been undertaken during this period there has been no job loss in these CPSEs.
