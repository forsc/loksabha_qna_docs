                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3211






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Setting Up of Old Age Homes




Kavitha Malothu













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3211TO BE ANSWERED ON 22.03.2022SETTING UP OF OLD AGE HOMES 3211.  SHRIMATI KAVITHA MALOTHU:            DR. G. RANJITH REDDY:            SHRI VENKATESH NETHA BORLAKUNTA:            SHRI PASUNOORI DAYAKAR: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government aims to set up at least one old age home in every district, under Integrated Programme for Senior Citizens; (b) if so, whether the Government is also aware that there are only 19 old age homes in Telangana amongst 33 districts in the State; (c) if so, the steps taken/proposed to take by the Government to set up at least one old age home in every district of Telangana; and (d) the status of performance of existing old age homes in the State?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) to (d):  The Department of Social Justice and Empowerment is implementing a Central Sector Scheme namely Integrated Programme for Senior Citizens (IPSrC) (a component of  Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana) under which grants in aid is provided to implementing agencies like NGOs/Voluntary Organizations etc. for running and maintenance of, inter alia, senior citizens homes (old age homes). The Scheme guidelines have been revised w.e.f. 01.04.2021 and a provision has been made for establishing a senior citizen home in each district.At present there are 16 Senior Citizen Homes run by NGOs assisted by this Department under IPSrC in Telangana.The Department invites proposals on e-Anudaan portal for running and maintenance of senior citizens homes in uncovered districts in every financial year.Quality of services provided to senior citizens in the senior citizen homes assisted under IPSrC is monitored by Project Monitoring Unit (PMU) of this Department as per guidelines of the Scheme.   Grant in aid has been stopped for 02 Sr citizen homes in Telengana State as unsatisfactory services  to the destitute  senior citizens found by PMU in the financial year 2020-21.*****
