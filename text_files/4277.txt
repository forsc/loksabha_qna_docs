                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3941






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






New Rail Line between Chikmagalur-Sakleshpur




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Railways has laid the foundation stone for the new Broad Gauge railway line on the 47 km. Chikmagalur-Sakleshpur section in February, 2014;(b)	if so, the details and present status of the railway line; (c)	whether any funds have been allocated/released for the said railway line during the last three years and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the project is on 50:50 cost sharing basis between the Railways and Government of Karnataka ;(e)	if so, whether the State Government has handed over the balance land totalling 885 acres to the Railways; and(f)	if so, the funds sanctioned for continuation of the ongoing railway projects, Hubli-Ankola, Rayadurg-Tumkur  via  Kalyandurga?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)  to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3941 BY  KUMARI SHOBHA KARANDLAJE TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 17.07.2019  REGARDING  NEW RAIL LINE BETWEEN CHIKMAGALUR-SAKLESHPUR(a): Yes, Sir. (b) and (c):  Work on Chikmagalur-Sakleshpur is a part of Kadur-Chikmagalur-Sakleshpur new line project. On this project, Kadur-Chikmagalur (46 Km) section has been commissioned. On the balance portion of the project i.e. Chikmagalur-Sakleshpur, work on 3 Km stretch from Chikmagalur towards Belur has been taken up on available land, beyond this stretch, work could not be taken up due to non handing over of land by Government of Karnataka.	An expenditure of ` 377.85 crore has been incurred on this project upto March, 2019 and an outlay of ` 1.1 crore has been proposed for the Financial Year 2019-20. However, details of all works in progress and new works, allotment of funds and expenditure on each project are made available in public domain on Indian Railways website i.e. www.indianrailways.gov.in >Ministry of Railways >Railway Board >About Indian Railways >Railway Board Directorates >Finance(Budget).(d) and (e):  Government of Karnataka had consented to provide land free of cost and share 50% cost for Chikmagalur-Belur-Sakleshpur  section  of above project. However, on the request of Government of Karnataka dated 17.01.2019, Belur-Sakleshpur section (26 Km) was frozen by Ministry of Railways in June, 2019 and Belur-Hassan new line was sanctioned in Pink Book of 2019-20 on 50% cost sharing basis.	Due to revised route, the land requirement between Chikmagalur to Belur is 309.15 Acres, out of which, 25.10 Acres has been handed over to Railways.(f): Outlay proposed for Hubli-Ankola and Rayadurg-Tumkur  via  Kalyandurga new line projects for the Financial Year 2019-20 are               ` 2 crore and ` 155 crore respectively.*****
