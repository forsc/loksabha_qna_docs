                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Promotion of Ayurveda




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the steps taken by the Government to promote ayurveda, yoga and naturopathy in the country; (b) the details of concrete policy formulated for creating awareness among the people about yoga and naturopathy and making these systems employment oriented; and (c) the number of persons who have been given employment in these sectors, State-wise including Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):     The Ministry has been implementing Central Sector Scheme for Promotion of Information Education and Communication (IEC) in AYUSH to create awareness regarding AYUSH Systems of Medicine including Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy by organizing National/State Arogya Fairs, Ayurveda Parvs, Yoga Fests, Seminars, Workshops and Conferences etc. The Ministry is also undertaking IEC Campaigns on multiple media including Print media etc. for creating awareness by formulating and executing strategies relating to AYUSH System of Medicine including Ayurveda, Yoga and NaturopathyThe Ministry is also observing Ayurveda Day, International Day of Yoga and Naturopathy Day   to aware the public about the importance of Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy Systems of Medicine respectively.The Ministry has set up a Yoga Certification Board (YCB) under the aegis of Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga with functional autonomy. The aim of YCB is promotion of Yoga as career skill.  YCB undertakes assessment of Yoga professionals for certification of their skill. YCB also undertakes accreditation of Yoga Institutions/Centers at different levels for promoting Yoga in an organized manner.National Institute of Naturopathy (NIN) under the Ministry is promoting Naturopathy and Yoga through short term skill development programs, fellowship programs and through certificate course. The certificate and fellowship programs offered at NIN are primarily devised to increase their scope of getting employed.(c):      As the health is State subject, therefore, such data is not maintained by the Ministry of AYUSH.
