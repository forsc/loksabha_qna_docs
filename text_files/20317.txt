                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1489






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Public Grievances/Complaints




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has created any platform to hear public complaints; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the manner in which information is being sent to the rural areas of the platform where internet service is not available along with the details thereof; (d) 	the details of the State-wise complaints received and their solution during the last three years and the current financial year; (e) 	the details of the nature of complaints; and (f) 	whether the Government has formulated any plan to make the mechanism more pliable and easy and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b):  Yes Sir, the CPGRAMS Portal www.pgportal.gov.in is operational.(c):  Physical complaints are also received in the Ministries/Departments of Government of India.(d):    The details are at Annex-I(e):  The complaints largely pertain to issues relating to Revenue, Health & Family Welfare, Education, Police, Public Health, Engineering Department, Public Works Department, Electricity Board and Social Justice.(f):   Government has undertaken CPGRAMS Reforms for adoption of CPGRAMS version 7.0 in top grievance receiving Ministries. Integration of State Portals with CPGRAMS, Feedback Call Centre for COVID-19 Grievances are other forms of reforms. In Jammu & Kashmir the district portals have been integrated with CPGRAMS.*****
