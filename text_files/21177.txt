                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Establishing Processing-cum-Storage Godown Units




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	Whether the Government has been providing financial support to States for establishing Seed Processing-cum-storage Godown Units at Gram Panchayat Level and if so, the details in this regard;b)	The details of the criteria fixed for such financial support;c)	The details of the funds allocated, released and utilized so far under the said programme, State-wise during each of the last three years; andd)	Whether these Seed Processing units have also been able to alleviate malnutrition by developing bio-fortified varieties of various crops and if so, the details in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)a)	Yes, Government of India has provided financial assistance for establishment of 517 Seed Processing-cum-Storage Godown at Gram Panchayat level of 500 MT each, during financial years 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20.b)	For setting up of Seed Processing and Seed Storage godown each of 500 MT capacity at Gram Panchayat 100% grants-in-aid of Rs. 60 Lakh or actual cost whichever is less is provided, which includes expenditure for procurement of seed processing machinery/equipments, small plant building, drying floor/receiving shed, and construction work of Seed Processing cum Seed Storage godowns.c)	The details of the fund released state-wise in last three years placed at Annexure-I.d)	DAC&FW is providing Breeder seed of Bio-Fortified varieties of Rice, Wheat, Pulses, Oilseeds etc. to State Governments for production zof foundation and certified seed that will help the farmers in commercial production of Bio-Fortified crops.
