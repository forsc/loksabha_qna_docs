                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Social Security for Taxpayers




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya













                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has received any representations to provide social security to taxpayers by way of launching health/medical insurance to all income tax assesses above 60 years of age according to the Assesee’s total income tax contribution till reaching 60 years of age and they are to be pooled under group insurance;(b)  if so, the details thereof along with the action taken in this regard; and (c)  if   no action has   been taken so far by the Government, the timeline by which it is likely to be approved?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF FINANCE(SMT. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)(a)	to (c):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.…….STATEMENT   REFERRED TO   IN   THE REPLY  TO   PARTS (a) TO  (c) OF STARRED    QUESTION    NO *218   BY    SHRI MOHANBHAI KALYANJI KUNDARIYA  AND   SHRI DIPSINH  SHANKARSINH RATHOD TO BE ANSWERED  IN  LOK  SABHA  ON 13.12.2021  REGARDING   “SOCIAL SECURITIY FOR TAXPAYERS”.….. (a)	      :     Sir, no such representation has been received (b)&(c):    Does not arise.……
