                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Promotion of Sports and Sportsperson in North East




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  has  taken  special initiatives to promote sports and encourage North East sportspersons in North  East; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) Yes, Sir. The Union Government, under its “Khelo India – National Programme for Development of Sports” and various Sports Promotional Schemes run by Sports Authority of India (SAI), has taken special initiatives to promote sports and encourage sportspersons in the country, including the North East Region.(b) Under the Khelo India Scheme, several initiatives have been taken to promote sports and encourage sportspersons in the North East Region, including the following:•	62 sports infrastructure projects amounting to ? 423.01 crore have been sanctioned.•	? 5.71 crore in 2018-19 and ? 1.80 crore in 2019-20 were provided for conducting sports events/activities in the disturbed areas.•	217 Khelo India Athletes have been identified.•	08 Khelo India State Centres of Excellence, 152 Khelo India Centres, 20 Accredited Academies and 02 Army Boys Sports Companies have been notified.•	Khelo India Youth Games, 2020 were held in Guwahati, Assam.•	1756 sportspersons from North East Region have participated in 04 editions of Khelo India National School/Youth and University Games conducted so far.•	03 Thang-Ta academies have been established (one each in Assam, Manipur and Tripura).****
