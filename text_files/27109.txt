                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2908






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Financial Impact of Covid-19




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  has  assessed the  financial  impact  of  the  second  wave  of  the  Delta Covid-19 variant on flight operations at airports run by Airport Authority of India (AAI);(b) if so, the details thereof especially for airports in Andhra  Pradesh;  and(c) the footfall at AAI airports between January and July 2021, month and airport-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has reported a reduction in its revenue from Rs. 2976.17 Crore during April-June 2019 to approx. Rs. 889 Crore during April-June 2021. For the airports in Andhra Pradesh, the revenue has reduced from Rs. 36.29 Crore in April-June 2019 to approx. Rs. 10.62 Crore during April-June 2021.;(c): The month-wise and airport-wise passenger footfall at AAI airports, operational for scheduled flights, is at Annexure.
