                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Traditional Weaving Practices in Tribal Areas




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any data of weavers engaged in traditional weaving practices in tribal areas;(b) if so, the State-wise details thereof;(c) the schemes being implemented and proposed by the Government to promote traditional weaving in the country for the welfare of the traditional tribal weavers; and(d) the funds allocated and utilized in this regard during each of the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): As per 3rd Handloom Census (2009-10), there were 6,96,932 scheduled tribe weavers in the country.  State/UT-wise details are given at Annexure. (c): The Office of the Development Commissioner for Handlooms is implementing following schemes for development of handlooms and welfare of weavers, including tribal weavers of the country:-1)	National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP)2)	Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS)3)	Handloom Weavers’ Comprehensive Welfare Scheme (HWCWS)4)	Yarn Supply Scheme (YSS)Under the above schemes, financial assistance is provided for raw materials, purchase of looms and accessories, design innovation, product diversification, infrastructure development, skill upgradation, lighting units, marketing of handloom products and loan at concessional rates.(d):	Details of funds allocated and released/utilised in the last three years and the current year under the Tribal Sub Scheme under the above schemes are as under:-S.No.	Year	Funds allocated(Rs. in crore)	Funds released/ utilised(Rs. in crore)1	2016-17 to 2018-19 (RE)	108.52	96.332	2019-20 (BE)*	48.24	7.18       *As on 16.07.2019Details of overall funds allocated and released/utilised, scheme wise, in the last three years and the current year are given below: (Rs. in crore)S.
