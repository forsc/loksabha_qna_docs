                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1663






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






GeM Portal




Pratima Bhoumik














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the business done through GeM portal during the last two years, year-wise; (b)	whether the Government is going to make it mandatory for all the State Governments and central Ministries to procure only through GeM; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a):	The details of the business done through GeM is as under:-Financial Year	  Value (In Rs. Crore)FY 18-19	         17,462FY 19-20	         22,897(b) & (c):	Rule 149 of the General Financial Rules (GFR) issued by Ministry of Finance, Government of India provides, inter alia, that “the Procurement of Goods and Services by Ministries or Departments will be mandatory for Goods or Services available on GeM”.  As regards procurement by State Governments is concerned, 25 States and 8 Union Territories (UTs) have signed Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with GeM for procurement of Goods and Services through GeM Portal.*****
