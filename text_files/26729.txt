                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






FIR Registered for Cyber Terrorism




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the total number of FIRs filed for the offence of cyber terrorism under section 66F of the Information Technology Act, 2000 during the last five years, State-wise; and(b) the total number of cases arising from these FIRs in which a conviction has been obtained under section 66F of the IT Act, 2000 during the said period, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	The National Crime Record Bureau (NCRB) is the nodal agency which compiles the data on crimes including  cyber terrorism as reported to it by States and Union Territories and publishes the same in its annual publication ‘Crime in India’. The published reports are available till the year 2019. The State-wise details of number of FIRs filed and persons convicted for the offence of Cyber terrorism under Section 66F of the Information Technology Act, 2000 during the last five years i.e. for 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019 is at Annexure********
