                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Targets under PMAY




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Aparupa  Poddar
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has announced any target regarding housing for all by the year 2022 under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) in the country, State-wise particularly in Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra and West Bengal and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the details of total funds allocated and spent under the yojana so far, year-wise; and (c)	the number of beneficiaries belonging to Economically Weaker Sections (EWS), under the Yojana, State-wise including aforesaid States?






ANSWER




                                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE  MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                      (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): Land and Colonisation are State subjects. However, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is supplementing the efforts of States/ Union Territories through Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) since 2015 to provide all weather pucca houses with all basic amenities for all eligible families/ beneficiaries.   There    are    no   yearly   fixed   targets   under    the Scheme. Based on the project proposals submitted by States/ Union Territories (UTs), more than 115 lakh houses have been sanctioned across the country under PMAY-U, out of which more than 95 lakh have been grounded and more than 56 lakh have been completed/ delivered to the beneficiaries so far. The State/ UT-wise details of houses sanctioned including in the States of Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra and West Bengal are at Annexure-I. (b): Under PMAY-U, for construction of 1.15 crore houses, ? 1.89 lakh crore has been sanctioned, of which ?1.12 lakh crore has been released and an amount of ?1.03 lakh crore has been utilised so far.
