                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

809






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Separate Railway Zone for Kerala




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal for setting up of a separate railway         zone for Kerala;(b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the present status of starting a coach factory at Kanjikode, Palaghart district in Kerala;(d) whether the Government of Kerala has submitted any request in this regard; and(e)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):  No, Sir.(b):  Does not arise.(c): The work of setting up of Rail Coach Factory at Palakkad in Kerala was sanctioned in the Railway Budget 2012-13 in Joint Venture/ Public- Private Partnership (JV/PPP) mode. Railways is in process of assessing the requirement of coaches in view of factors like signalling, capacity enhancement, life of coaches, etc. Further decision about coach factory can be taken after this exercise is over.(d) & (e): Yes, Sir. References have been received in 2018 from Government of Kerala, for setting up of Rail Coach Factory at Palakkad.* * * * *
