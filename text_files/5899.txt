                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5467






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Dams on Anas River




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any proposal from Rajasthan Government regarding supply of water for agriculture and drinking purpose to nearby areas by building dams on Anas river, a tributary of Mahi river; (b) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Union Government in this regard; (c) the amount sanctioned and the expenditure incurred by the Government in this scheme; and (d) the details of area that is likely to be irrigated and supplied drinking water?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (d)  The Water Resources Projects are planned, funded, executed and maintained by the State Governments themselves as per their own resources and priority. In order to supplement their efforts, Government of India provides technical and financial assistance to State Governments to encourage sustainable development and efficient management of water resources through various schemes and Programmes.As informed by the Central Water Commission, no proposal regarding supply of water for agriculture and drinking water to nearby areas by building dams on Anas River, a tributary of Mahi river has been submitted by the Government of Rajasthan.*****
