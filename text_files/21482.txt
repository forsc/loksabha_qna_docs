                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2726






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






CSR fund




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria













                                                    Hemant Tukaram Godse
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















 (a) whether the Government has formulated any scheme to utilize Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)fund of coal companies for the development of coalfields;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the details of the development works being carried out thereon;(c) the quantum of funds accrued and utilized by coal PSUs for the purpose during the last three years and the current year;(d) the status of physical progress of such development works/activities;(e) whether any of the coal PSUs has defaulted in spending CSR funds and if so, the details thereof along with the corrective action taken by the Government thereon; and(f) whether any relief is being provided to the families of the miners who suffered accidents and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a):      Coal India Limited (CIL) and its subsidiaries undertake Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) as per Scheduled VII of Companies Act 2013. (b) to (d) : As per CSR policy of CIL, subsidiaries of CIL have to spend 80% of their CSR fund within a radius of 25 kms. of their project sites/mines/area Head Quarter (HQ)/company HQ. Major development works/activities undertaken from CSR funds by coal PSUs are infrastructure development, skill development, social empowerment, water supply, health and sanitation, promotion of sports and providing education facilities etc.  The details of CSR funds allocated and spent during the last three years and current year by CIL and its subsidiaries are furnished in the table below:Fig in Rs. croresFinancial Year	CSR Fund as per Companies Act, 2013	Actual CSR Expenditure2017-18	383.05	483.782018-19	353.98	416.472019-20	396.20	587.842020-21(Subject to audit)	440.13	283.85(Apr. – Dec.) The physical progress of major development works/activities undertaken as part of CSR by CIL and its subsidiaries is at Annexure A. (e):  The coal PSUs are not in default of spending CSR funds as per Companies Act 2013. (f):  In CIL and its subsidiaries, miners are provided the following relief in case of accident: A-       Relief/compensation to the families of mine workers of CIL and its subsidiaries who suffered accidents or die due to mine accident: -1.         Compensation under the Employee’s Compensation Act, 1923.2.         An amount of  ? 15 lakh is paid as Ex-gratia3.         An amount upto ? 1,25,000/- is paid under the Life Cover Scheme4.      In addition to compensation under the Employee’s Compensation Act,1923 an additional amount of 90,000 is paid in case of death or permanent total disablement5.         Upto ? 20 Lakhs as per the Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 20186. Payment of leave encashment and any other dues.8.         Pension as per Coal Mines Pension Scheme, 1998.9.         Compassionate Employment to the dependent of the deceased employee.10.       Monthly Monetary Compensation in lieu of employment to female dependent. The above is revised with every Wage Revision. Presently, the Monthly Monetary Compensation is paid at 26,292.97 per month.
