                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5060






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Schemes to Promote Solar and Wind Energy




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the schemes promoting solar and wind energy along with the quantum of funds that have been allocated for the same;(b) whether the Government has conducted any study or survey to evaluate the total potential of solar and wind energy in the country; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)The details of schemes under implementation for promoting solar and wind energy alongwith the funds allocated in the current financial year are given in Annexure. (b)&(c) Yes, Sir. The potential of solar power in the country has been assessed at around 750 GW by National Institute of Solar Energy (NISE). The potential of wind power in the country has been assessed at 302.25 GW and 695.50 GW at 100 meter and 120 meter above ground level respectively by National Institute of Wind Energy (NIWE). *****
