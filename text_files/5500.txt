                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5099






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






New Rail Line between Majhi Lar Road Stations




Kavita Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has conducted any survey to set up 	new railway line from Majhi  to Lar Road Station of Siwan and Chhapra district of Bihar;  (b)	if so, the details thereof;  (c)	whether the Government proposes to accord it priority considering the density of traffic on this proposed railway line 	and the transport related needs of the inhabitants of this division; and (d)	if so, the details thereof and the time frame fixed for its	completion?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): Yes, Sir. A survey for laying of new railway line from Manjhi-Lar Road via Raghunathpur, Darauli and Guthani (92 Km) was completed in the year 2011-12. As per the survey report, cost of construction of the project was assessed as `742 crore with a negative Rate of Return of 0.99%. 	In view of huge throwforward of ongoing new line projects and unremunerative nature of the project, this project could not be taken forward.*****
