                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Production of Vegetables and Fruits




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama













                                                    Rajiv Pratap Rudy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      whether the Government has set any targets for the production of fruits and vegetables in various States of the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)      the various steps taken by the Government to achieve the said targets during the said period;(c)     the details of the production of fruits and vegetables in various States of the country during the last three years and the current year, State-wise;(d)      the details of assistance provided by the Government to various States for promotion of horticulture crops during the last five years, State-wise;(e)     whether the Government proposes to help vegetable and fruit cultivators in marketing of their produce so they may get remunerative prices, if so, the assistance being provided by the Governments in this regard;(f)   the schemes/programmes being implemented for increasing the production and productivity of vegetables and fruits; and(g)       the steps taken by the Government to encourage research in the field of vegetable and fruit production in the county?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE  ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????             (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) & (b):    No specific targets have been set for the production of fruits and vegetables, as the scheme is being implemented through State Horticulture Missions (SHM) i.e. by State Horticulture Department and action plan for various components are fixed on the basis of demand and requirement of the States. (c):       the details of the production of fruits and vegetables in various States of the country during the last three years and the current year, State-wise is attached as Annexure-l & II.  (d):       the details of assistance provided by the Government to various States for promotion of horticulture crops during the last five years, State-wise is attached as Annexure-Ill.(e):   	Remunerative prices of vegetable and fruit produce depends on several factors like input costs, quality of produce and demand and supply. However, National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) is a virtual platform integrating physical wholesale mandis/ markets of different States/ Union Territories (UTs) to facilitate online trading of agricultural commodities including Fruit & Vegetables through transparent price discovery method to enable farmers to realize better remunerative prices for their produce. Various facilities/ assistance is provided to the farmers in selling agricultural produce in e-NAM mandis in the country.            Besides, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare is implementing Market Intervention Scheme (MIS) to protect the growers of perishable horticulture commodities from making distress sale in the event of a bumper crop during the peak arrival period when the prices tend to fall below economic levels and cost of production.(f):        Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India implements Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for holistic development of horticulture in 28 States and UTs. The sector covers fruits, vegetables, root and tuber crops, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconut, cashew and cocoa. (g):    Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) undertakes research on horticulture (vegetables and fruits) through 23 Research Institutes/Directorates/NRCs under Horticultural Science Division spread across the country which deal with research, development and extension. There are 13 All India Coordinated Research Projects (AICRPs) on Fruits, Vegetable Crops, Palms, Spices, Floriculture, Medicinal and Aromatic plants, Potato, Tuber crops, Arid Zone Fruits, Cashew, Mushroom, Onion and Garlic, Temperate Fruits which undertake location specific research work through State Agricultural Universities in different agro-climatic zones of the country.
