                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






e-Transfer of Fund under MDM Scheme




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the name of States, which are receiving e-transfer of funds from the Government forimplementation of the Mid-Day Meal Scheme;(b) the name of States,which are receiving an advance transfer of funds for the Mid-DayMeal Scheme from the Government;(c) the number of States providing additional food items like milk/egg/fruits/ sweets tochildren at their own costs; and(d) whether any State uses biogas and/ or solar gas for cooking Mid-Day Meals, if so, thedetails thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): All the States and UTs are receiving funds from the Central Government forimplementation of the Mid-Day Meal Scheme through e-transfer. Under the Scheme,Central Government provides financial assistance to all the States and UTs in advance.(c): As per the information received, 25 States are providing additional food items likemilk/egg/fruits/ sweets to children from their own costs.(d): As per the information received, the following states are using bio-gas/solar energy forcooking mid-day-meals:<pre>S.No. Name of States No. of Institutions1. Chhattisgarh 12. Maharashtra 113. Telangana 74. Uttarakhand 7</pre>*******
