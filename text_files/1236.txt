                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1281






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Unani System of Medicine




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to formulate new policy/programme on social awakening regarding Unani System of Medicine; and (b) if so, the details thereof including the time by which the said policy/programme is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b) : The Central Council for Research in Unani Medicine (CCRUM) engages in creation of social awakening among general public through its various programmes: •	IEC activities like Arogyas, Health Melas, Health Camps and Exhibitions. •	Extension health services like Mobile Healthcare programme and School Health programme.•	The Council is also creating social awakening regarding Unani system of medicine through Two specialized programmes namely, Integration of Unani Medicine in NPCDCS and SCSP / TSP Programme.          The National Institute of Unani Medicine (NIUM) has proposed the exhibition at BDA complex Nagabhavi and involving various schools for health check-ups with the aim to socially awaken masses for Unani Medicine Treatment, and the programme will be completed in the ongoing year 2019-20.………………
