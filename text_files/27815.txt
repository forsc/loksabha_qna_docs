                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3359






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Financial Package for Hilly States




Kishan Kapoor














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any special financial package proposed to be provided by the Union Government  to the hilly States to revitalise the most affected tourism industry during the Covid-19 pandemic in hilly areas like Himachal Pradesh; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF THE STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) & (b):  Yes Sir.  The Government has decided to provide working capital/personal loans to Regional Level  Tourist Guides recognised by Ministry of Tourism and Tourist Guides recognised by the State Governments; and Travel and Tourism Stakeholders (TTS) recognized by Ministry of Tourism. This includes such Guides and TTSs of the hilly States/ areas like Himachal Pradesh. *****
