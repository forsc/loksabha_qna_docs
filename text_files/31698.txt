                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3092






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Various Development Schemes for MSME Sector




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of start-ups have received the Government funding in the State of Maharashtra;(b)	whether the Government plans to introduce any scheme or project to boost the start-up mechanism in backward areas of Maharashtra, if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of procurement from women-owned enterprises under Public Procurement for MSEs Amendment Order, 2018 during the last 3 years;(d)	the details of subsidies and credit has been disbursed under the Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) during the last 3 years; and(e)	the details of the amount disbursed under National Awards to outstanding Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country and the selection criteria for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a)&(b): To promote startups across all the State/UTs including Maharashtra,Government of India has launched Startup India initiative in 2016. The Government as part of Startup India initiative has implemented Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) Scheme and Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) to provide financial assistance through Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) and incubators.As on 25th November, 2021, for the State of Maharashtra, Rs. 1,847.97 crore has been invested by such AIFs, into startups, under Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) Scheme. Also under Startup India Seed Fund Scheme, Rs. 2.10 crore has been disbursed to the selected incubators from the State of Maharashtra as on 25th November, 2021.(c): 	The details of procurement from women-owned enterprises under Public Procurement Policy during the last 3 years is given below:<pre>Financial Years	Procurement from MSEs(Rs. in Crores)	No. of MSEs benefitted	Procurement from MSEs owned by Women(Rs. in Crores)	No. of MSEs benefitted2018-19 (166 CPSEs)	40,399.70	1,28,152	232.56	1,4102019-20(151  CPSEs) 	39,665.10	1,57,986	393.51 	3,6662020-21(152  CPSEs) 	40,704.70 	1,75,703	718.69 	4,9602021-22* (103  CPSEs)	22,484.27 	1,06,734	518.31	3,557  </pre>  * as on 08.12.2021 (d): 	The details of margin money subsidy disbursed and units assisted under PMEGP Scheme during the last 3 years: <pre>Year	Margin Money subsidy disbursed(Rs in crore)	Number of units assisted2018-19	2070.00	73,4272019-20	1,950.82	66,6532020-21	2,188.80	74,4152021-22*	1,143.53	36,271  </pre>    *Till 15-11-2021(e):    During 2019-20, National Award Function was held on 26.02.2020, wherein a total of 46 MSMEs were selected as awardees and a sum of Rs. 46 lakh (Rs. 1 lakh to each awardees) were disbursed among the MSME awardees. For selection of awardees, the Ministry of MSME has award specific criteria under different categories of awards.
