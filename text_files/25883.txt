                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1794






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Data on Houses under PMAY-U




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any data with regard to the houses given to poor people under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Urban (PMAY-U) during the last two years and current year;(b)	if so, the details of houses sanctioned in the country, especially Arni Parliamentary Constituency of Tamil Nadu, State-wise;(c)	the number of applications pending with the Government for allotment of houses to the poor under PMAY-U, State-wise; and(d)	the action taken by the Government for early approval?






ANSWER




                                                                THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Urban (PMAY-U),41,75,214 houses have been sanctioned and 20,39,571houses have been completed/delivered to urban poor during the last two years and current year. State/UT wise details of sanctioned and completed houses is at Annexure-I. The details of sanctionedand completed houses in Arni Parliamentary Constituency of Tamil Nadu are at Annexure-II.(c)&(d)Land and colonization are State subjects. This Ministry through various programmatic interventions supplements the efforts of State / Union Territory (UT) Governments for providing houses to eligible urban beneficiaries in the country. Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban [PMAY-U], identification of eligible beneficiaries and implementation of projects are done by the State / UT Governments concerned through their implementing agencies.                                    *****
