                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

659






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Stoppage at Bayana Station




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any proposal for stoppage of Nanda Devi Express at Bayana Railway Station of Bharatpur District of Rajasthan has been received by the Government; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)&(b) Yes, Sir. Representations including from the Hon’ble Member of Parliament have been received for stoppage of 12401/12402 Kota-Dehradun Nanda Devi Express at Bayana.  As part of Zero Based Time tabling exercise a thorough review of all stoppages on Indian Railways is being undertaken based on operational feasibility and patronage.(c) Does not arise. *****
