                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Tribals Indigenous Language




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry has developed, or has plans on developing any mechanism to facilitate better communication during consultative processes between tribal groups and Government officials in the tribe’s indigenous language; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):	Ministry of Tribal Affairs has not developed any such mechanism till date.  No such proposal is under consideration, as Ministry has not been approached by any State or any tribal groups indicating need for developing communication mechanism in tribe’s language. *****
