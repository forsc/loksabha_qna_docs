                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

527






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Cess on Domestic and Imported Coal




K. Shanmuga Sundaram














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Central Government in 2010-11 imposed a cess of Rs. 50 per tonne on domestic and imported coal to create a corpus i.e. National Clean Energy Fund (NCEF) for promoting clean energy initiatives and research;(b) the total amount of the fund raised till date and the details of utilization of the fund for the current year and for the past three years;(c) the details of Ministries which have received funds from NCEF and their utilization;(d) whether unspent balance has been returned to NCEF and if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the amount has been released to Power Ministry for the current year and for the past three years; and(f) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COAL, MINES AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)  (a) to (f)  Information is being collected from other departments and will be provided.******
