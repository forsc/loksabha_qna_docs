                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1910






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Krishi Udan Scheme during Pandemic




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of airlines which have participated in Krishi Udan Scheme during the pandemic;(b) the details of perishable and non-perishable commodities transported thereof, airport, airline and sector-wise; and(c) whether the Krishi Udan Scheme is limited upto pandemic duration or will be extended further in the welfare of farmers, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (b): In the preparatory phase of Krishi Udaan Scheme, between 1 April 2020 and 31 August 2020 as per estimates more than 65,000 metric tonnes of agri-produce was air freighted, of which about 75 per cent was export cargo. Almost every Indian scheduled airline operators including Air India, Air India Express, Air Asia, Blue Dart Aviation, Go Airlines, IndiGo, Spice Jet, and Vistara participated in airlifting agri-produce either in belly holds, in cargo-on-seat passenger charters, or in dedicated freighter operations. The principal airports which participated included Agartala, Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Bhubaneswar, Chennai, Cochin, Coimbatore, Delhi, Guwahati, Imphal, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mangalore, Mumbai, Surat, Trichy, and Vishakhapatnam. Agri-produce uplifted included Mango, Litchi, Cherry, Banana, Rasakdali, Banana Leaves, Pineapple, Custard Apple, Apple, Peas, Babycorn, Coriander, Cucumber, Cluster Beans, Drumstick, Small Onion, Curry Leaves, Betel Leaves, Green Vegetables, Flowers, Walnuts, Shrimps, Meat and Meat Products, Paneer, Tea, Spices, Rice, etc. Airport, airline and sector-wise data is not maintained. ;(c):  The Krishi Udaan Scheme has been approved and circulated to all concerned Ministries/ Departments on 10 September 2020 for implementation. The operation of the Krishi Udaan Scheme is not limited to the pandemic period.
