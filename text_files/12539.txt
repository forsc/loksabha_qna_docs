                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1490






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Compilation of NPR




C.N. Annadurai













                                                    Parvatagouda Chandanagouda Gaddigoudar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the time when the first National Population Register (NPR) was compiled;(b) the year of compilation and expenditure incurred along with the details of Relevant Act and Rules under which it was conducted;(c) the number of times it has already been revised and the years of revision and expenditure incurred therefor;(d) whether it will be updated again in 2020 along with 2021 national census;(e) if so, the estimated cost and amount sanctioned along with time line for conducting each NPR and census; and(f) whether it will be conducted in all States and UTs and if not, the States and UTs which are to be exempted along with the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (f):The National Population Register (NPR) was first prepared in 2010  and updated in  2015 under Sub-rule(4) of Rule 3 of the Citizenship (Registration of Citizens and Issue of National Identity Cards) Rules, 2003, framed under the Citizenship Act, 1955. Under the same Rule, NPR is being updated along with Houselisting and Housing Census Operations of Census 2021 between  1st April, 2020 to 30th  September,  2020  in  all  the States / UTs  except-2/... -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.1490 FOR 11.02.2020Assam.  The scheme for preparation of NPR in 2010 was approved at a cost of Rs. 6649.05 crore including cost of its updation in 2015.  The approved cost for Census 2021 is Rs. 8754.23 crore and for updation of NPR 2020 is Rs.3941.35 crore. *****
