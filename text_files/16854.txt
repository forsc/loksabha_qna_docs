                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

504






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Doubling of Railway Lines in Bharatpur




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the details of works carried out for doubling railway line in Bharatpur district of Rajasthan during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)Railway projects are not sanctioned District-wise/State-wise but zone-wise, as Indian Railways’ network spans across various Districts/State boundaries. However,  Agra Fort-Bandikui (150 Km) doubling project falling partly in the district of Bharatpur has been included in the Budget 2018-19 subject to requisite Government approvals.*****
