                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Beti Bachao Beti Padhao




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 		the salient features and the present status of ‘Beti Bachao Beti Padhao’ programme; (b) 		whether the Government have any plan to include more districts in this programme, if so, the details thereof, State-wise and district-wise; (c) 		whether the Government have received any proposal/request to bring the Scheduled Castes dominated district of Mungeli, Chhattisgarh under the said programme; (d) 		if so, the details thereof along with the decision taken by the Government thereon; and (e) 		the details of the financial assistance provided by the Government for the said programme during the current year, State/UT-wise including Chhattisgarh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	to (e) The Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme (BBBP) launched on 22nd January, 2015 addresses the declining Child Sex Ratio (CSR) and aims to change mindsets to value the girl child. It is a tri-ministerial effort of Ministries of Women and Child Development, Health & Family Welfare and Human Resource Development. The key elements of the scheme include nation-wide awareness and advocacy campaign and multi- sectoral action, initially started in select 100 districts (low on CSR) and got expanded to 61 additional districts in the year 2015-16. Considering the encouraging results of implementation in 161 districts the Government of India has expanded the BBBP Scheme to all 640 districts (as per census 2011) of the Country w.e.f. 8th March, 2018. The Ministry of Women and Child Development has received the request for inclusion of  more districts including Mungeli district of Chhattisgarh under the Scheme.As of now, the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme (BBBP) is implemented across the country in 640 districts (as per census 2011) since 8th March, 2018 including 18 districts of Chhattisgarh.
