                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4434






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Reports of FDI Equity Inflow




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that the Department of Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) used to publish sector-wise reports of FDI equity inflow with details of shares of top investor countries, top RBI regions receiving investment and top companies receiving FDI equity inflows in the particular sector until 2013;(b) 	if so, the reasons for discontinuing the release of such detailed reports; and(c) 	the year-wise details from 2014, of the industries viz., metallurgy, power, electrical equipments, computer software and hardware, electronics, telecommunications, automobile, air and sea transport, railway components, industrial machinery, medical and surgical appliances, chemicals, drugs and pharmaceuticals, textiles, services, education, hotel and tourism, trading, and construction?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a)&(b): The Department for Promotion for Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) regularly publishes sector-wise reports on FDI equity inflow for the top 10 sectors with details of shares of top investor countries, top RBI regions/states receiving investment and top remittance cases (which inter-alia includes name of companies), in the `FDI Newsletter- Annual Issue’. These are uploaded on the department website (https://dpiit.gov.in/) under the heading ‘Publications’ (URL link: https://dpiit.gov.in/publications/si-news-letters/archives?field_year_sia_tid=All&field_month_tid=All&page=14). (c):      The financial-year wise details of FDI Equity inflow reported in the sectors viz. metallurgical industries, power, electrical equipments, computer software and hardware, electronics, telecommunications, automobile industry, air transport (including air freight), sea transport, railway related components, industrial machinery, medical and surgical appliances, chemicals (other than fertilizers), drugs and pharmaceuticals, textiles (including dyed, printed), services sector, education, hotel and tourism, trading, construction (infrastructure) activities and construction development  from April, 2014 till December, 2021 is at  Annexure-I.********
