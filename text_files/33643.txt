                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1065






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Proposals regarding Sports




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and the number of proposals related to sports and youth affairs received from the State of Maharashtra during the last three years and the amount demanded for such proposals; (b) 	the details and the number of proposals approved so far and the matter concerned;(c) 	whether many such proposals are pending with the Government as on date; (d) 	if so, the reasons therefor along with the associated cost escalation; and(e) 	the reaction of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	to (e): The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports has sanctioned 36 proposals for establishment of Khelo India Centres, 1 proposal for establishment of Khelo India State Centre of Excellence and 11 sports infrastructure projects, to the State of Maharashtra during the last three years.The Khelo India Scheme, under which above proposals from Maharashtra were considered, is a demand driven scheme and the proposals are considered based on the technical feasibility of the proposals, their adherence to the prescribed parameters, as well as the availability of funds.*****
