                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2495






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Import of Pulses




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total quantum and types of pulses that have been imported by the Government during the last one year; (b) 	whether production of pulses in the country has fallen, if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the names of countries from which pulses are being/were imported along with the rates at which the same were imported?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a) :  In 2018-19, no import of pulses was undertaken by the Government.(b) : As per the 3rd Advance Estimates released by Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare, production of pulses for the year 2018-19 is estimated to be 23.22 Million MT (MMT) as compared to final estimates of 23.13 MMT and 25.42 MMT for the years 2016-17 and 2017-18, respectively.(c) : Details of major exporting countries of pulses to India is at
