                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1262






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Decline in Per Capita Income




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that per capita income in the country has declined over the years;(b) if so, the details of per capita income during the last five years, year-wise and the likely per capita income in the current year after its decline;(c) whether it is a fact that the per capita income is constantly decreasing since demonetization in the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (d) As per latest information available from National Statistical Office, the per capita income (i.e. per capita net national income at current prices) increased from Rs. 94,797/- in 2015-16 to Rs. 1,34,226/- in 2019-20. Per capita net national income at current prices since last five years is given in the table below. Table: Per capita net national income (NNI) at current prices (Rs.) 	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18#	2018-19@	2019-20*Per Capita NNI 	94,797	1,04,880	1,15,293	1,26,521	1,34,226Note : * : Provisional  Estimates;  # : Second Revised Estimates; @ : First Revised Estimates.    Source: National Statistical Office, MoSPI*****
