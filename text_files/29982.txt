                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1672






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Auction of coal Blocks




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        the coal blocks that are auctioned in the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 at Raigarh district;(b)     the company/companies to which the ownership of these coal blocks have been transferred; and (c)        the details of the amount received in the auction of these coal blocks?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF COAL, MINES AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) to (c): Total 2 coal blocks situated in Raigarh district of Chhattisgarh have been auctioned during the year 2019-20 and 2020-21. The name of coal blocks, allottee company and amount received in the auction is as under :- S. No.	Coal Block	Successful Bidder	Total upfront Amount (in INR.)	1st installment of upfront (in INR) received by MoC1	Gare Palma IV/1	Jindal Power Limited	28,83,86,695	7,20,96,673.752	Gare Palma IV/7	Sarda Energy and Minerals Limited	86,30,45,425	21,57,61,356.25Note : The successful bidder has deposited the first installment of upfront amount i.e. 25% of total upfront amount before issuance of vesting order. The amount of first installment has been transferred to State Government. The remaining upfront amount will be paid directly by the successful bidder to the state government in 3 installments as per the terms and conditions of the agreement.
