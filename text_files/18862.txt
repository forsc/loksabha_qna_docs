                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

261






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Shramik Special Trains in Tamil Nadu




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the total number of Shramik Special train that has been operated during the lockdown period to commute the migrant workers from various parts of the country, especially Tamil Nadu to their home town; (b) the total number of migrant workers who commuted by these ‘Shramik Special Trains’; and(c) the total number of ‘Shramik Special Trains’ that were made operational to Tamilnadu from various destinations, sector-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	To (c) During the period 1st May, 2020 to 31st August, 2020, Indian Railways operated 4621 Shramik Special trains.  In all, 63.19 lakh passengers commuted by the Shramik Special trains.  9 Shramik Special train were bound for stations located in the State of Tamil Nadu during the period 1st May, 2020 to 31st  August, 2020, as detailed below:-1.	Pandharpur – Tiruchchirapalli2.	Borivali – Villupuram3.	 Delhi – Tirunelveli4.	Pune – Tirunelveli5.	Ahmedabad – Tirunelveli6.	Mumbai – Tirunelveli7.	Mumbai – Madurai8.	Mumbai – Tirunelveli9.	Bhagat ki kothi - Chennai.*****
