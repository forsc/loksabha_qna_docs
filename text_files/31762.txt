                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3324






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






VCIC Coastal Economic Zones




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of updated status on the VCIC North and VCIC Central Coastal Economic Zones (CEZs); (b) the data on the number of industries currently operating in the above CEZs; (c)  the value, in crores of combined contribution of both CEZs in Andhra Pradesh to the industrial output of the country; (d) the date on the average turnaround time of the ports of the State during the last three years, especially for Vishakhapatam port; and (e) the details of developmental projects undertaken at the port of Vizag to enhance efficiency and capacity under the Sagarmala initiative?






ANSWER




                                                        (a), (b)& (c) Government of Andhra Pradesh has identified five nodes under VCIC including Visakhapatnam, Machilipatnam, Donakonda, Srikalahasthi-Yerpedu (Chittoor) and Kadapa. VCIC Development Program, with the Financial Assistance of Asian Development Bank (ADB), envisages development of three industrial clusters; Nakkapalli & Rambilli at Visakhapatnam Node and Chittoor South at Srikalahasthi-Yerpedu Node. For multi-tranche financing facility (MFF) Tranche I of the project total expenditure has been USD 114 Mn and physical progress of the project is 62%. The Industrial Clusters proposed under VCIC are new clusters. Industries are yet to be established in these clusters.(d) The average turnaround time of Visakhapatnam port during the last three years are  as follows:2018-19  –  2.51 days2019-20  –  2.48 days2020-21  –  2.80 days (e) 28 projects with total project cost of Rs. 5,293 Cr. have been undertaken by Vishakhapatnam Port Trust under the pillars of port modernization and port connectivity of Sagarmala Programme. Out of the 28 projects, 12 projects with total project cost of Rs. 1,293 Cr. have been completed and 3 projects with total project cost of Rs. 1,114 Cr are under implementation.                                      *****
