                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

363






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Assessment of KVKs




Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any mechanism to assess the quality of services offered by Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVKs); (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government provides financial assistance to the KVKs; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE        	       (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):   The quality of services offered by Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVKs) is assessed regularly by holding meetings of Scientific Advisory Committee of KVKs and also State and Zonal Workshops. Besides, there is also a mechanism for assessing KVKs’ work by the Quinquennial Review Teams and the Third Party evaluation.of KVKs. During the last three years i.e. 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 financial assistance of Rs.909.89 Crore, Rs.979.73 Crore and Rs.1150.21 Crore, respectively has been provided to KVKs. During 2021-22, an amount of Rs.1260.81 crore has been allocated for the KVK Scheme.*******
