                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2279






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






CSR Funds




Santosh Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of parliamentary constituencies where the work has been executed with theamount of CSR fund of the PSU companies of of the Ministry in the country along with thedetails of the work executed with the said fund over there, constituency-wise; and(b) the details of the fund spent thereon, year-wise/ constituency-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)&(b) Public Sector Oil Companies have informed that CSR data is maintainedState/District/project-wise and are made available in public domain. The details ofCSR fund spent by Public Sector Oil PSUs during the last three years are as below:-(Rs. in crore)Oil PSUs 2018-19 2019-20 2020-21HPCL 159.81 182.24 156.35IOCL 490.60 543.38 460.37ONGC 614.64 606.97 552.64GAIL 119.29 125.30 147.67BPCL 177.94 345.57 144.89OIL 133.39 125.40 105.25EIL 10.04 11.20 12.65BL 5.16 5.14 5.14NRL 54.85 87.60 28.00MRPL 31.32 76.09 25.36CPCL 18.89 16.72 1.96Further, State/district/project/activity/agency wise details of funds spent under CSRby above PSUs in the country during the last three are available on their respectivewebsites i.e. www.hindustanpetroleum.com, www.iocl.com, www.ongcindia.com,gailonline.com, www.bharatpetroleum.com oil-india.com, engineersindia.com,www.balmerlawrie.com, www.nrl.co.in, www.mrpl.co.in & www.cpcl.co.in.****
