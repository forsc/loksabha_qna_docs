                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1117






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Development of National Level Textile Cluster




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken note of any representation which sought for release of 1013 crores for development of national level textile clusters in Bhongir district, Telangana; and(b)	if so, the details thereof including the time by which the proposed funds are likely to be released therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ?????? ?????? (??????? ?????? ?????? ?????)MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	Ministry of Textiles has not received any representation seeking release of Rs. 1013 crore for development of textile clusters in Bhongir district of Telangana. (b)	Question does not arise. *****
