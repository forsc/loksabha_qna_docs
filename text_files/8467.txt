                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Ayushman Bharat Yojana in Maharashtra




Kani K. Navas













                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the total number of beneficiaries under the Ayushman Bharat Yojana in the State of Maharashtra including the district of Chandrapur;(b) the number of Government, private and Public-Private Partnership hospitals in the country; and(c) the amount of funds released for Maharashtra under the said scheme so far?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Services at Ayushman Bharat- Health & Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs) are free and universal to all.The total number of beneficiary families under Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) in the State of Maharashtra are 83.6 lakh families. Out of these, 1.8 lakh families are from Chandrapur district.(b):	As on 25.11.2019, the total number of hospitals empanelled under AB-PMJAY are 19,668. Out of these 10,574 are public hospitals and 9,094 are private hospitals.  (c):	Under AB-HWCs (Comprehensive Primary Health Care), Rs. 91.27 Crore were released to Maharashtra during Financial Year 2018-19 and Rs. 121.18 have been allocated for Financial Year 2019-20. Details of funds released to Maharashtra under AB-PMJAY are as under : - Financial Year	Fund released (Rs. In crore)2018-19	266.322019-20	162.03
