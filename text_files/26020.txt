                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Offshore Wind Energy Projects




Adala Prabhakara Reddy













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently introduced initiatives in renewable energy sector like Viability Gap Funding (VGF) options for Offshore Wind Energy;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the projects which have received VGF; and(d) the current status of offshore wind energy sector in the country along with the share of that in the total energy mix?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) Government has not so far  introduced  any Viability Gap Funding (VGF) for Offshore Wind Energy.(b) Question does not arise.(c) Question does not arise.(d) Government notified the ‘National Offshore Wind Energy Policy’ in 2015 providing the basic framework for developing the offshore wind sector. Post notification of the policy, Government, through National Institute of Wind Energy, has issued ‘Guidelines for Offshore Wind Power Assessment Studies and Surveys’ to enable private investors to carryout offshore wind resource assessment. Further, Government is carrying out offshore wind resource assessments and related studies through National Institute of Wind Energy to validate the offshore wind resource potential in identified locations off the coast of Gujarat and Tamil Nadu. At present, there is no offshore wind energy project operational in the country.*****
