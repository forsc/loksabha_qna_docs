                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Projects under HRIDAY




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that only 52 per cent of the amount has been released to the State Governments by the Union Government for different projects under Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana(HRIDAY);(b)	  if so, the details thereof and the projects undertaken by the State Governments during each of the last three years, State-wise;(c)	the status of the projects undertaken by the States till 31st March, 2019; and(d)	the total amount utilized for each project during the said period,  project-wise and State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                    (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): No, Sir. The National Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojna (HRIDAY) is a central sector scheme and the funds are directly released to the cities. Out of the total sanctioned funds of Rs. 418.06 crore, an amount of Rs. 402.44 crore (96.26%) has been released.(b) to (d):  The details are attached as Annexure–A.                                   *******
