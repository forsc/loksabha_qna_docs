                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






District Road Safety Committee




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the dates on which the meetings of the District Road Safety Committee constituted under the chairmanship of the Member of Parliament in Rajasthan were held during the last year and the current year, district-wise; and (b) 	the details of decisions taken by this committee along with the details of the implementation report in Udaipur district of Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)to (b)     A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFFERED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (b) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 149 ANSWERED ON 28-11-2019 ASKED BY SHRI ARJUN LAL MEENA REGARDING “DISTRICT ROAD SAFETY COMMITTEE”.-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------(a)	As per information provided by Government of Rajasthan, the meetings of Road Safety Committee under the Chairmanship of Members of Parliament were held in the Districts of Pali, Udaipur and Dungarpur as under:-District	2018	2019Pali	29.01.201825.02.2018	22.7.2019Udaipur	-	04.09.2019Dungarpur	-	12.10.2019(b)	The proceedings of the meeting of the Committee held in Udaipur district and implementation report thereon as received from the State Government of Rajasthan is given as Annexure-I.
