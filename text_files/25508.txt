                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1314






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Fisheries Production in Madhya Pradesh




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated/proposes to formulate any scheme for the fisheries production and doubling the income of fishermen of the State of Madhya Pradesh; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has taken any step to provide training in the sphere of fisheries and financial assistance to fishermen under Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan; (d)	 if so, the details thereof State and Union Territory wise; (e) 	if not, the reasons therefore; and (f)	 the details of funds released to promote fisheries in the State of Madhya Pradesh during the last three years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA) (a) and (b): 	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) - to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with an estimated investment of Rs. 20,050 crore for a period of 5 years with effect from the from financial year 2020-21 to financial year 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories including Madhya Pradesh as a part of Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Package. Besides, a dedicated fund namely, ‘Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund’ (FIDF) with a total fund size of Rs.7,522.48 crore has been set up for providing concessional finance for creation and strengthening of fisheries infrastructure facilities. These schemes implemented in all the States/UTs including Madhya Pradesh inter-alia aiming towards enhancement of fish production, productivity, income of fish farmers, generation of employment opportunities in fisheries and aquaculture.(c) to (e): 	The Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) implemented as a part of Atma Nirbhar Bharat Package inter-alia accords focus for training, awareness, exposure and capacity building for fishers, fish farmers, fish workers, vendors and other stakeholders. During the financial year 2020-21, due to the COVID-19 pandemic condition, awareness programmes, trainings, publicity initiatives and outreach programmes and other related programme were conducted in the virtual mode covering 7.40 lakh participants through 1016 programmes. State/UT-wise details of central funds release are furnished at Annexure-I. (f): 	Year-wise details of central funds released to promote fisheries in the State of Madhya Pradesh during the last three years as below:Sl. No.	Year	Fund released (Rs. in lakhs)1	2018-19	711.422	2019-20	1859.473	2020-21	2379.16*****
