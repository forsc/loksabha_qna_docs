                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4289






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Promotion and Development of Culture in Rajasthan




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has made any efforts or prepared any scheme for the promotion/development of culture in Rajasthan;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the amount of funds provided for the promotion/development of culture in Rajasthan during the last three years, year-wise; and(d)	the details of the works of promotion/development carried out in Rajasthan through the said amount?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir.  Ministry of Culture deals with the Tangible and Intangible Cultural Heritage of India. This heritage encompasses several strands including monuments and archaeology, museums, folk and tribal art (which includes puppetry also), literature, archives, library, performing arts including music dance and drama, visual arts in the form of paintings, sculptures and graphics. The activities, projects and programmes of this  Ministry are organized under various schemes of Archeology, Museums, Archives, Anthropology, Performing Arts, Public Libraries, Buddhists and Tibetan Institutes, Centenaries & Anniversary, International  Cultural Relations, Gandhi Heritage Mission etc.  The activities/programmes of the Ministry relating to promotion, preservation and conservation of the Cultural Heritage of the Country are administered through a network of 02 Attached Offices, 06 Subordinated Offices and 34 Autonomous Bodies under its control and number of schemes directly operated by the Ministry for promotion and dissemination of Art & Culture.(c) & (d): Sir, The details are given at Annexure.
