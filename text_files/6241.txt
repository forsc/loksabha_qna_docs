                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

56






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Observation of National Library Day




Dhanush M Kumar













                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government observed ‘National Library Day’ to focus on the importance of books and if so, the details thereof along with aims and objectives behind it;(b)	the total number of libraries currently running in the country, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government has created awareness to popularize book reading habits and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government has set up libraries in rural areas where people cannot afford to buy books and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the steps proposed by the Government to promote book reading habit in children?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a)	No Sir. (b)	& (c) There are six libraries under administrative control of Ministry of Culture namely:i.	National Library, Kolkataii.	Central Secretariat Library, New Delhiiii.	Central Reference Library, Kolkataiv.	Delhi Public Library, Delhiv.	Khuda Bakhsh Oriental Public Library, Patnavi.	Rampur Raza Library, RampurThese libraries conduct various activities to popularize book reading habits such as Membership Drive, Book Exhibitions, Lectures, Workshops, Seminar etc.(d)	Library is a State subject.  It functions under the administrative control of the respective State/UT library authorities.  Therefore, setting up libraries in rural area comes under the jurisdiction of State/UT library authorities.(e)	Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata, an autonomous organization of Ministry of Culture provides financial assistance for establishment of Children Corner in public libraries to inculcate the book reading habit in children.        ***
