                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

670






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Regional Ring Road in Telangana




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the proposal for the development of a regional ring road in Telangana, including the areas proposed to be connected along with estimated cost of land acquisition and its development;(b)	the details of land proposed to be acquired for this project;(c)	the details of contribution expected from the State Government for financing this project; and(d)     the time by which this ring road is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)   The Northern portion of the proposed Hyderabad Regional Ring Road Project along Sangareddy-Narsapur-Tupran–Gejwel-Yadagirigutta-Choutuppal section (NH-161 AA) has been included under Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I. The Consultancy for preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) for developing the northern portion of the Regional Ring Road Project has been awarded on 14.07.2021 with completion period as 10 months. The cost of the project, the civil cost and other costs will be finalized based on the outcome of the DPR. (c)	In the meeting held on 29.01.2019 held in Ministry, the State Government was asked to bear 50% of the land acquisition cost besides providing government land free of cost as well as cost of shifting of utilities and other pre-construction activities. Same has been agreed to by Government of Telangana.(d)	On completion of DPR and subsequent appraisal & approval of project, the time of completion, cost, mode of implementation etc. are decided.*****
