                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

413






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Release of Funds for NDDP




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the amount allocated/released under pure milk production and National Dairy Development Programme by the Union Government during the last three years; (b) whether the said amount has not been timely allocated for the last few years and is still pending;(c) if so, the reaction of the Government in this regard; and (d) the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) The details of amount allocated and release under National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD) during last three years for creation of dairy infrastructure including quality milk is as under:-									(Rs. in crore)Year	Revised Estimate (RE)	Release 2016-17	120.00	119.712017-18	170.49	170.4842018-19	270.07	269.98(b)	No Sir, the funds under NPDD scheme have been timely released during last three years.(c) & (d) Does not arise.****
