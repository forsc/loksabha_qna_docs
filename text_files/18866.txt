                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Stoppages of Express Trains




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to approve stoppages of more express trains especially Rayalaseema, Tirupathi, Nizamuddin and Devagiri express trains at Malkajgiri station; (b) if so, the details of the timeline by which the stoppages are likely to be approved; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) With a view to contain the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, Indian Railways discontinued the regular passenger train services w.e.f. 23.03.2020. Prior to discontinuation, 12793/12794 (Tirupati-Nizamabad Rayalseema Express), 12707/12708 (Tirupati-Hazrat Nizamuddin Andhra Pradesh Sampark Kranti Express) and 17057/17058 (Mumbai-Secunderabad Devagiri Express) were having stoppage at Secunderabad /Kacheguda, 03 Km/08 Km away from Malkajgiri. Owing to close proximity of existing stoppage with Malkajgiri, there is no immediate proposal for provision of stoppage of additional train services including aforesaid three long distance trains at Malkajgiri. However, provision of stoppage of trains is an ongoing process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, commercial viability etc.*****
