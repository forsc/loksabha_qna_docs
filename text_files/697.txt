                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

531






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Starvation Deaths in Maharashtra




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Antyodaya Ration Cards cancelled till date in Maharashtra in the financial year 2018-19 along with the details of Government procedure afterwards; (b) 	the details of starvation deaths in the year 2015 in Maharashtra; and (c) 	the measures taken or contemplated by the Government to stop deaths due to starvation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI RAMVILAS PASWAN)(a) to (c): 	As reported by State Government of Maharashtra, about 1,11,569 Antyodaya Cards having 1 and 2 members have been shifted to Priority Households beneficiaries in the year 2018-19.		Further, no starvation deaths are reported in Maharashtra in the year 2015 due to non-allocation/distribution of foodgrains under Targeted Public Distribution System.****
