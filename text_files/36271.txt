                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3609






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






University for Study on Oil and Gas




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up a national level university at Jalore in Rajasthan on the subjects related to oil and gas industries; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to build/ create a pool of experts in the oil and gas sector in the country including Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) & (b): No, Sir.(c): This Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas has set up two institutes which have courses at Diploma/ Under Graduate/ Post Graduate level in Petroleum Engineering and Chemical Engineering to create a pool of experts in the Oil and Gas sector. The Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT), Jais, Amethi, Uttar Pradesh, has two other campuses at Sivasagar, Assam & Bengaluru, Karnataka, whereas Indian Institute of Petroleum & Energy (IIPE) has one campus at Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.In addition, institutes all over the country such as Indian Institute of Technology at Bombay, Madras, Dhanbad, etc. and other Universities have courses on Petroleum Engineering and Technology. Scientific institutes such as Council of Scientific and Industrial Research-Indian Institute of Petroleum have training/ faculty on Oil and Gas Sector and Oil and Gas PSUs have their own training facilities/ institutions in various parts of the country which also help build/ create a pool of experts in the Oil and Gas Sector in the country.*********
