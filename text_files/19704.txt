Welfare Schemes for OBCs




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama













                                                    Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1180TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021WELFARE SCHEMES FOR OBCs 1180.     SHRI RAJESHBHAI CHUDASAMA:               SHRIMATI POONAM MAHAJAN: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the National Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) has been implementing schemes for the welfare of Other Backward Classes (OBCs); (b) if so, the details of funds allocated under each of the schemes during the last three years, State-wise;(c) the details of proposals received, sanctioned and pending under each of the schemes during the said period, State-wise;(d) whether most of the people belonging to backward classes have not been able to avail the schemes of NBCFDC due to lack of awareness and publicity in mass media; and (e) if so, the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a):   Yes Sir, National Backward Classes Finance & Development Corporation (NBCFDC) implement schemes of concessional financing and skill development schemes for welfare of the Other Backward Classes (OBCs). (b):    The details of funds allocated/released under each of the schemes during the last three years, State-wise is annexed at Annexure-A&B.(c):    NBCFDC receives proposals of Annual Action Plan (AAP) from the State Channelising Agencies (SCAs) which are approved subject to fulfillment of prudential norms and availability of funds. The statement showing AAP received and amount approved during last three years is appended at Annexure-C.  No proposal of AAP is pending with NBCFDC as on date.(d) & (e):  No such information has come to the knowledge of the Department. The NBCFDC and its Channel Partners (CPs) give due emphasis on spreading awareness and publicity of schemes through various mediums including Mass Media, besides organizes camps for awareness generation of its schemes among the masses through its Channel Partners in routine.
