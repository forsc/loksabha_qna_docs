                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3808






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Roadmap for the Development of Sports




Pradeep Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any discussion has been held with the representatives of the States and Union Territories in order to prepare a roadmap for the development of sports in the country;(b)	if so, the details of the sports representatives of various States who participated in the above discussion;(c)	the details of the suggestions and proposals received during the said discussion;(d)	whether any proposal for development of sports has been received from the State Government of Bihar; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS {SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR}(a) to (c)	Discussion through virtual conference was held on 20.09.2021 with Sports Ministers of States/Union Territories (UTs). Representatives of Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Manipur, Mizoram, Odisha, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Tripura and Jammu & Kashmir attended the said conference. Various issues like roadmap for upcoming Olympics, promotion of Sports, development of Sports facilities/infrastructure, incentives/training for Sportspersons, reservation for Sportspersons in Government jobs, identifications of talent, National Dash Board for Sportsperson and Sports enthusiast etc. were discussed during the said conference.  (d) & (e)	This Ministry has approved a total of 11 proposals out of 31 proposals for development of Sports under the vertical “Utilization and Creation of Sports Infrastructure” of Khelo India Scheme received from the State Government of Bihar. Sports is a state subject and the responsibility for development of sports rests with the State/Union Territory Governments. Central Government supplements their efforts in this regard. The details of the approved proposals are annexed.***
