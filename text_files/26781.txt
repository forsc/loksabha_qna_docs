                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2443






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Empowerment of Hearing-Impaired, Mute and Divyang Persons




L.S. Tejasvi Surya













                                                    D. M. Kathir Anand
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the estimated number of hearing-impaired, mute and Divyang persons in the country, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) the number of Union Government posts that are provided for such persons to be indicated department-wise;(c) the number of vacant posts along with the duration of the vacancy; and(d) whether the Government has designed special skilling programmes to empower such persons who have completed such skilling programmes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a)   As per Census 2011, there are 2,68,14,994 persons with disabilities (PwDs) in the country which include persons with locomotor disability, hearing impairment, visual impairment, speech impairment, mental illness, mental retardation, multiple disabilities and other categories. As per this Census, there are 50,72,914 persons with hearing impairment. State/UT-wise data regarding population of PwDs and persons with hearing impairment is at Annexure. (b)   The Government notified the list of 3566 categories of Central Government posts identified suitable for persons with benchmark disabilities (disability of 40% or more) on 04.01.2021, out of which 3342 categories of posts have been identified suitable for persons with hearing impairment, which are applicable to all Central Ministries/Departments as per their nature of work.(c)   Department of Personnel & Training has informed that as per data provided by various Central Ministries and Departments, which include vacancies in Central Public Sector Undertakings, Nationalised Banks and financial institutions etc. Out of 15,000 vacancies for PwDs identified in 2015, about 14000 vacancies have been filled up. These vacancies, inter-alia, included backlog vacancies carried forward from earlier years.(d) The Ministry implements the National Action Plan (NAP) for Skill Development of Persons with Disabilities (between the age group of 15 to 59 years) with a view to enhancing their scope of employability in both public and private sectors besides promoting self-employment. Under NAP, financial assistance for skill training of 1.27 lakh PwDs have been provided to empanelled training partners of the Department so far.*****
