                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3614






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Budget Data




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                                     Government of India               Ministry of Finance               D/o Economic Affairs                     Lok Sabha              Unstarred Question No.3614        To be answered on Monday, the 16th March, 2020            Discrepancies in Budget Data                    Question3614. SHRI HIBI EDENWill the Minister of finance be pleased to state:(a)	Whether there was any correction in the data of Union Budget 2020-21 published on the website;(b)	If so, the details thereof including the title, number, head, amount, page  etc. along with the details of persons found responsible for this;(c)	Whether there will be any disciplinary action against them; and(d)	If so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?                         ANSWER              MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE                  (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) To (d):The Appropriation Bill 2020 submitted to Parliament for consideration and passing is based on Demands for Grants for 2020-21, presented to Parliament on 1.2.2020 which is same as the PDF file available on the Website on the Ministry of Finance. There is no difference between these two documents.
