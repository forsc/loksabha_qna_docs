                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

195






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Scholarships to Minority Students




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of MINORITY AFFAIRS be pleased to state:  (a) the number of pre-matric, post-matric, merit-cum-means and other scholarships disbursed by the Union Government to the minority students during each of the last five years;and  (b) the manner in which the number of such scholarships is likely to be increased to 5 crore during the next five years
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS  (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)  (a)to (b): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  ***
