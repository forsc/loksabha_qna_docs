                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Conversion of Road into NHs in Tamil Nadu




D. M. Kathir Anand













                                                     Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any requests from the Government of Tamil Nadu to convert eight roads as National Highways and if so, the details thereof;(b) the measures taken by the Union Government to increase the road infrastructure in the State; (c) the details of the roads in each district of Tamil Nadu that are being covered for financial assistance under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) at present; and(d) the details of the funds released during the last three years for the said roads along with the pending funds due to be released to the State? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)&(b)  Yes Sir.  This Ministry has received request for conversion of 8 State Highways (SHs) having length of 500 Km into National Highways (NHs) in the State of Tamil Nadu.  The Ministry considers declaration of State Roads into new National Highways and development of road infrastructure from time to time based on the requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority and availability of funds.(c)     The details of the roads in each district of Tamil Nadu that are being covered for financial assistance under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) is annexed as annexure-I.(d)     The funds under CRIF are released by this Ministry State/UT wise and not project wise.  The details of the funds released during last three years and current year is as follows.Sl. No.	Year	Funds released(Rs. in Crore)1	2018-19	416.882	2019-20	262.373	2020-21	503.174	2021-22	                  350.15 No further funds is pending for release during current financial year.
