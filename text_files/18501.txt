                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Setting up of Anganwadi Kendras




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    	whether any guidelines for setting up of 4 lakh Anganwadi Kendras across the country have been issued, if so, the details thereof;(b)   	the number of Anganwadi Kendras inaugurated/operational so far under the said guidelines, if so, the details thereof , State-wise; and(c)   	the number of children and women likely to be benefitted through the said Anganwadi Kendras?






ANSWER




 ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT (SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	to(c)    Government of India has approved 14 lakh Anganwadi Centres, including 2,13,859 AWCs and 77,102 Mini-AWCs and an additional provision of 20,000 Anganwadis-on-Demand (AoD). All the 14 lakh approved Anganwadi Centres/Mini-Anganwadi Centres have already been allocated to the State Governments/UT Administrations. The services are currently provided to 855.05 lakh beneficiaries of which 686.30 lakh are children under six and 168.75 lakh are pregnant women & lactating mothers. 245.03 lakh children of 3-6 years are provided pre-school education of which 122.44 lakh are boys and 122.59 lakh are girls.
