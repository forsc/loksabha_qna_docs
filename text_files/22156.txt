                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3381






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Storage of Foodgrains in FCI Godowns




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of foodgrains stored in the godowns of Food Corporation of India (FCI) at present in the country particularly in Maharashtra, locations-wise; (b) 	the reasons of undue delay in loading and unloading of foodgrains in the said godowns; (c) 	the quantum of foodgrains damaged in the godowns of FCI during the last three years, the Statewise and location-wise particularly in the State of Maharashtra; (d) 	whether the godowns of FCI are in dilapidated condition in the country particularly in the backward areas; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	The total stock of Wheat, Rice and Coarsegrains in Central Pool (as on 1st  March 2021) is 813.60 LMT. Location wise details of FCI stock in Maharashtra is at Annexure-I.(b): 	There has been no abnormal delay in loading / unloading operations at any of the said godowns. Sufficient stock levels are maintained at these godowns at all times and even during COVID- 19 pandemic period there was smooth supply for PDS operations......2/--2-(c): 	Foodgrains available with Food Corporation of India (FCI) is stored and preserved scientifically. FCI is storing/ handling large quantities of foodgrains for the purpose of issuance/distribution for Public Distribution System and other welfare schemes of Govt. of India. Out of these Stocks, small quantities of food grains accrue as damaged mainly due to Natural Calamities like Cyclone/Flood, transit damages viz en-route damage in wagon/truck due to contamination, wagon roof leakage etc and in some cases due to negligence of officials/ officers, for which suitable disciplinary action against defaulters are invariably taken against defaulters.Statement showing State-wise and location-wise accrual of damage foodgrains in FCI including  Maharashtra during the last three years and current year are at Annexure II, III, IV AND V.(d) and (e):  FCI inspects the condition of its godown on periodical basis and take corrective action such as repair, renovation to ensure the storage worthiness of godowns.
