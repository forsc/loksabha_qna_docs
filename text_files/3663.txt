                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3314






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Arogya Centres




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the details of the Arogya Centres operational across the country, State/ UT-wise including Gujarat;(b) whether the need of Ayush doctors are increasing at these centres in the country, if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise along with the action taken/being taken by the Government in this regard; (c) whether the Government is in the process of identifying some Arogya Centres to deliver traditional medicinal services at the grass-root level; and (d) if so, the details thereof, State/UTwise and the time by which these are likely to be identified?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (d):      Public Health is a State subject. Setting up of Arogya Centres comes under purview of State/UT Governments. However, Central Government is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in the country including Gujarat under which there is a provision of financial assistance to States/UTs for setting up of AYUSH Wellness Centres including Yoga & Naturopathy. State/UT Government including Gujarat may avail eligible financial assistance by projecting the same through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) as per NAM guideline.………………
