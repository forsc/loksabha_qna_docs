                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2129






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Funds under Central  Schemes




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FINANCE be pleased to state:-(a) 	the details of funds provided by the Government under various Central Schemes during the current financial year, State-wise;(b)	the details of the funds provided to the Government of Jharkhand and utilisation thereof;(c) 	whether the Government of Jharkhand has given any justification for non-utilisation of the funds; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                                                MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE                                         (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a):	The details of funds provided by the Government of India under various Central Sector Schemes and Centrally Sponsored Schemes for the current financial year in a state-wise manner are given at Annexure-I.(b), (c) & (d): The Central Government provides resources to States under Finance Commission Transfers (including devolution from divisible pool of Central taxes and duties) and other grants-in-aid including Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS), Central Sector Schemes (CS), EAP Loans etc. as per allocations provided in Union Budget.
