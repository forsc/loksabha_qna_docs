                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4337






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Licence to Run Kitchen




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) has written to Central Licencing Authority in Chennai to inspect the kitchen at Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) in Andhra Pradesh where Laddu is prepared and directed it to obtain a Central Licence to run kitchen; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the steps being taken to ensure that food safety licence like any other Food Business Operator for the said purpose is duly obtained?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):      Yes.  As required under the  Food Safety and Standards Act 2006, matter was taken up with Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) in Andhra Pradesh for  obtaining  license. TTD has subsequently obtained Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) license.  The details of license obtained by TTD are as follows :1.	M/s AEO Potu Sri TT Tirumala-10017044001209. 2.	M/s Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams- 10017044001212.……………….
