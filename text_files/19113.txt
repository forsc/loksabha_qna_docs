                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Construction of Toilets




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of toilets constructed so far under the “Swachh Bharat Mission (Rural)” in the country;(b) whether any target has been set up by the Government for the years 2020-22; and(c) if so, the details thereof, Block/district/state-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  As per the data reported by the States/UTs on the online Integrated Management Information System of Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM(G)], 10.75 crore Individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed under SBM(G) since the launch of the scheme on 2nd October, 2014.(b) to (c)  SBM(G) is a demand driven scheme, hence no pre-fixed targets can be set.
