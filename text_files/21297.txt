                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2436






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of beneficiaries who have availed assistance under the Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme, State-wise;(b) 	the total amount of assistance provided by the Government (sanctioned and availed) under various heads of the said scheme, State-wise alongwith the overall total assistance provided under the scheme;(c) 	the number of beneficiaries who have availed assistance under the National Family Benefit Scheme, State-wise;  and(d) 	the total amount of assistance provided by the Government (sanctioned and availed) under various heads of the same and the overall assistance provided?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (b)  The details of head wise / State/UT-wise number of beneficiaries, total funds sanctioned and total funds availed during current financial year so far, including 3% percent administrative expenses, under Indra Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (IGNWPS)  are enclosed in Annexure-I.  (c) to (d)  The details of head wise / State/UT-wise number of beneficiaries, total funds sanctioned and total funds availed during current financial year so far, including 3% percent administrative expenses, under National Family Benefit Scheme (NFBS), are enclosed in Annexure-II.  *****
