                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2741






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Outreach of Fixed Lines




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the outreach of fixed line based on optical fibre in the country is quite less in comparison to developed countries and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (b) 	whether the Government has made any effort to extend the outreach of this facility and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	whether the Government has fixed any accountability to expedite this work and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	No authentic information is available to the Government on the outreach of fixed lines based on optical fiber in other countries. (b)	In order to increase the broadband adoptability, Department of Telecommunications (DoT) has published Centralized Right of Way (RoW) Policy 2016, which establishes the process for laying and maintenance of underground cable, grant of permission by appropriate authority, dispute resolution etc. Further, National Digital Communication Policy (NDCP), 2018 has emphasized on establishment of fiber network for expanding mobile and broadband connectivity across the country to create robust digital communications infrastructure. (c)	Yes, Sir. DoT has already started monitoring the adaptation of centralized RoW policy & optical fiber penetration in the telecom networks, under Broadband Readiness Index (BRI) program.************
