                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3868






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Investigation into Pulwama Terror Attack




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government would make the facts public regarding the outcome of the investigation of the Government in the terrorist attack of Pulwama;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the number of soldiers who sacrificed their lives on the border of Jammu and Kashmir after the Pulwama attack; and(d) the action plan of the Government to curb the incidents of infiltration from the borders and to curb the casualties of the soldiers deployed in the security of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	The case is under investigation.(c):    After the Pulwama attack,  82 security force personnel (including 40 CRPF personnel in Pulwama) have laid their lives in fight against terrorism in Jammu and Kashmir. (d):  The Government of India in tandem with the State Governments has adopted a multi-pronged approach to contain cross border infiltration. This -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.3868 FOR 17.03.2020includes strengthening of border management and increased deployment of Security Forces along the International Border / Line of Control.  The   measures  include  construction / maintenance  of border fencing, use of modern weapons and equipments by Security Forces, installation of border floodlights, improved intelligence and synergized intelligence flow and coordinated operations. A technological solution in the form of Comprehensive Integrated Border Management System (CIBMS) has been implemented in some vulnerable border areas. This includes smart fencing, adoption of state-of-the-art surveillance technologies such as thermal imagers, infra-red and laser-based Intruder alarms, aerostats for aerial surveillance and ground sensors etc.*******
