                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

133






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






ITIs




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) functioning in the country at present along with the skill trades being taught in these institutes, State/UT-wise including Andhra Pradesh;(b)	 the total estimated number of students getting training in these institutes and the existing vacancies in various categories along with the plan to fill up these vacancies, State/UT-wise;(c)	 whether the Government proposes to set up new ITIs in the country and if so,the details thereof, State/phase-wise;(d) 	 whether the Ministry has prepared any fresh plan to upgrade ITIs in the country and if so, the details thereof including integration of ITIs with skill development programmes; and(e)	 the details of the amount spent on improvement of ITIs in the country during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (b)	There are 15697 Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) functioning in the country at present and imparting training in 137 skill trades. The detail of State/UT wise ITIs, including the state of Andhra Pradesh, is attached at Annexure I and list of 137 trades is at Annexure II.Around 21 lakh trainees are undergoing training against the total seating capacity of 26.65 lakh in various trades categories. Admission in ITIs is under the administrative control of concerned State Directorates and state wise rounds of admissions are conducted by them to fill vacancies in various categories.(c)	Setting up of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) and its operations are in the domain of respective State/UT Governments. (d)	Yes sir, top grade ITIs, in the country including their integration with skill development program, a World Bank funded scheme Skills Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE) has been approved by Cabinet Committee for Economic Affairs (CCEA). It is a central sector scheme launched in October 2017 for a total cost of Rs. 2200 crore, and 5 years duration; with an objective to improve relevance and efficiency of skills development provided through ITIs and apprenticeship. The scheme implemented with effect from financial year 2018-19, has following four components: S.No. 	Component1	To Improve Performance of ITIs2	To Increase Capacities of State Governments to support and manage ITIs and apprenticeship training 3	To Improve Teaching and Learning 4	To Improve and Broadened Apprenticeship Training(e)	The details of the amount spent on improvement of ITIs in the country during the last three years and the current year, State-wise, under different schemes as given below, is attached in annexure III, IV& V respectively.1.	World Bank assisted Vocational Training Improvement Project (VTIP): The scheme, interalia covers up-gradation of 400 Government ITIs have been covered in 34 States/UTs. The scheme was closed on September 2018.2.	Skills Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE)is a new central sector World Bank funded project at a total cost of Rs. 2200 crore. The project duration is 5 years and the closing date is November 2022. 3.	Upgradation of existing Govt. ITIs into Model ITIs: - Under this scheme, the funds were released for improvement of Govt.
