                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

822






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Tap Water Supply




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government is planning to provide water supply through taps to around 15 crore houses in rural areas in next five years;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/district-wise; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) Yes Sir. Government of India, has launched Jal Jeevan Mission, with an outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore with Central share of Rs. 2.08 lakh Crore, which aims at providing potable water to every rural household through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) at the service level of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) by 2024. (b) As reported by States/ UTs, around 14.60 Crore rural household were to be provided with tap connection as on 01.04.2019. State-wise details are annexed. (c) Does not arise. ***
