                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2605






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Development of Border Villages




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government is allocating special funds for all-around development of villages located near the international borders;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of funds allocated and spent for this purpose during the last five years, State-wise including Rajasthan?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	Government of India is implementing the Border Area Development Programme (BADP) in border areas of 17 States including Rajasthan. BADP is a major intervention for filling critical gaps in provision of essential social and economic infrastructure in the border areas to ensure equitable development in these areas. The works/ projects undertaken under the BADP relate to construction of roads, bridges, safe drinking water supply, health, agriculture and allied activities, social sector activities such as creation of social infrastructure, capacity building and skill development, construction of toilets particularly for women, sports activities, promotion of rural tourism, border tourism etc. These activities help in providing additional job opportunities, improving quality of life and improving standard of living in the border areas. The  details of funds  (Centre Share)  allocated  by Government  of India to the-2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.2605 FOR 09.07.2019 State Governments and spent by the State Governments during the last five financial years 2014-15 to 2018-19 is at Annexure.  Funds are also being released for development of border villages, as Model Villages which enables development of one village of sizeable population surrounded by a cluster of neighboring villages close to the border areas.
