                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Providing seeds for Organic Farming




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is providing seeds to farmers to grow organic farming in the country and if so, the details thereof; and; (b)	the details of financial and other assistance that organic farming has got under the Budget for 2021-22?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Government of India is providing assistance for procurement/preparation of organic inputs including seeds @ Rs. 31,000 and Rs 32,000 per ha for 3 years under organic farming schemes of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) and Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER) respectively.	Both the schemes stress on end to end support to organic farmers i.e. from production to certification and marketing. Post harvest management support including processing, packing, marketing is made integral part of these schemes to encourage organic farmers. The detail of financial assistance is at Annexure. (b):	Under PKVY scheme Rs. 450 crore and under MOVCDNER Rs. 200 crore has been allocated as per Budget Estimate (BE) of 2021-22.
