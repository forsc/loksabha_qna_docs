                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3402






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Fertilizer Supply to Tea Industry




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a) 	the total Production of Urea by Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited, Namrup per annum;(b) 	whether it is a fact that Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited, Namrup is fertilizer supplier to the tea industry and agricultural sector in the entire north-eastern region and if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether it is fact that the Government recently provided Rs. 100 crore package for the revival of Unit II and Unit III of Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited, Namrup; (d)	 if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	whether it is also a fact that the Government proposes to meet with all the stakeholders of Unit IV of Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited, Namrup for its revival?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS                                      (DR.  MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	:  The total production of urea by Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizer Corporation Limited, Namrup per annum is 2.87, 1.56 & 1.32 LMT for the year 2018-19 2019-20 and  2020-21 respectively. (b):    BVFCL  ( in addition to the other suppliers) supplies to the North-Eastern region as per the agreed supply plan issued by the Department of Fertilizers to  meet the monthly requirement as projected by  Department of Agriculture  & Farmers Welfare DA&FW.(c) & (d):   The Govt. of India approved the financial grant-in-aid of Rs.100 Cr to BVFCL to sustain operation of the urea manufacturing units. The funds have been transferred to BVFCL.(e)	:  A high level meeting was held on 11.11.2021 with various stakeholders on setting up of Namrup-IV urea plant at BVFCL (Assam).                                    *********
