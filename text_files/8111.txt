                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1643






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Shelterless BPL Families




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has conducted survey of shelterless poor people belonging to BPL category in the State of Karnataka and if so, the details thereof, district-wise;(b)	whether the Government proposes to provide permanent housing to the said shelterless people; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):	It is the primary responsibility of Governments of the State/ Union Territories to provide shelters for the urban homeless. However, in order to complement and supplement the efforts of States/UTs, Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is administering a scheme of ‘Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH)’ under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), through respective States/UTs.The operational guidelines of SUH provide for conducting a systematic third party survey in urban local bodies to assess accurately the need for shelters at suitable locations. In the survey, so far 7226 homeless persons have been identified in Karnataka. District-wise details is at Annexure-I. As on 25.11.2019, a total of 68 Shelters have been sanctioned by the State Government, out of which, 40 shelters are operational. Further, 5 Non- NULM shelters are also functional.   Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] aims at providing pucca houses to all eligible urban beneficiaries including those of BPL category. As per the demand survey conducted by participating States/UTs, State Government of Karnataka has reported a housing demand of 12.28 lakh houses. It has, so far, got 275 projects approved for construction of 5,57,744 houses involving central assistance of Rs. 8658.05 crore for EWS category of beneficiaries. Of the sanctioned houses, 2,86,994 houses have been grounded, of which 1,18,952 houses have been completed as on 11.11.2019. Apart from the above, under Credit Linked Subsidy component of PMAY (U), interest subsidy of Rs. 251.04 crore has been disbursed to 11,498 beneficiaries of EWS/LIG category.                           *******
