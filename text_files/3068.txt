                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PRIME MINISTER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2897






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






New Committees




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PRIME MINISTER


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government proposes to set up new committees, one on investment and growth and other on employment and skill development;(b) if so, the details of agenda likely to be forwarded by setting such committees;(c) the issues likely to be addressed by the said committees; and(d) the details of the composition of such committees?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION; MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE; MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS; MINISTER OF STATE IN THE DEPARTMENT OF ATOMIC ENERGY; AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE DEPARTMENT OF SPACE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a):	Yes, Sir. The ‘Cabinet Committee on Investment and Growth’ and the ‘Cabinet Committee on Employment and Skill Development’ have been constituted on 06.06.2019.  (b) & (c):  The Standing Committees of the Cabinet, and their functions are set out in the First Schedule to the Government of India (Transaction of Business) Rules, 1961 (ToB Rules). The said Committees will address issues relating to Investment and Growth and Employment and Skill Development respectively.  (d): 	Extant composition of the two Committees is annexed.******
