                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






E-Portal for Real Estate Sector




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government is planning on launching a real e-State portal to serve as an e-commerce platform for real estate sector;(b)	if so, the details thereof including the financial outlay of such a project and the time frame within which such a portal would be operationalised; and(c)	the functions and services that such a portal would render and the objectives it seeks to achieve?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c) No Sir. Government is not planning to launch any e-commerce platform for real estate sector. However, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs has encouraged the real estate developers’ associations- Confederation of Real Estate Developers Association of India (CREDAI) and National Real Estate Development Council (NAREDCO) to develop website/portal for online sale of houses/flats in fair and transparent manner to enable the homebuyers to take informed decisions.                                          ******
