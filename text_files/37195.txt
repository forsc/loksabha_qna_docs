                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4276






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






PLI Scheme for FPIs




Goddeti Madhavi













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has received any applications under the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for FPIs from the State of Andhra Pradesh; (b)	if so, the current status thereof and the amount sanctioned under the PLISFPI to the above State; and (c)	the details of Ready to Cook/Ready to Eat FPIs in Andhra Pradesh? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHALAD SINGH PATEL)  (a) to (c): Yes, Sir. Six applicants, all in the Marine Products segment, have been selected under Production Linked Incentive Scheme for Food Processing Industry from the state of Andhra Pradesh. No applications have been selected in the Ready to Cook/Ready to Eat segment under the Scheme from the state of Andhra Pradesh.   The incentive to the selected applicants under the Scheme is subject to meeting committed investment and achieving requisite sales projection.  	*****
