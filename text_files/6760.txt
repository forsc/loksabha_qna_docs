                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

689






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Shortage of Officers




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether there is shortage of efficient officers in the Ministry;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the work of the Department has been affected due to shortage of officers and lack of quality; and(d)	if so, the reasons therefor alongwith the details of the remedial steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a)	No, Sir.(b), (c) & (d) Does not arise.                                                *****
