                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Identification of Places to be Developed as Theme Based Tourist Circuit




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has identified any places in Maharashtra to develop as theme-based tourist circuits under Swadesh Darshan Scheme; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b):	The Ministry of Tourism under Swadesh Darshan Scheme has sanctioned two projects in Maharashtra, the details of the same as under:(Rs. in crore)<pre>Sr. No.	Circuit/ Sanction Year	Project Name	Amount Sanctioned1.	Coastal Circuit (2015-16)	Development of Vijaydurg, Devgad, Mithbav, Tarkarli, Tondavali, Nivati Fort, Shiroda, Sagareshwar, Mochemad in Sindhudurg 	82.172.	Spiritual Circuit(2017-18)	Development of Waki- Adasa- Dhapewada- Paradsingha- Chota Taj Bagh- Telankhandi- Girad 	54.01Total	136.18</pre>*******
