                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Treatment of New Strain of COVID-19




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is aware of any fusion of multiple systems of medicine to completely prevent the infection caused by new strains of Coronaviruses, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government is aware of any drugs/ medicine to treat the new variant of COVID-19 and if so the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has formulated any plan to develop medicine to completely prevent and treat the new strains of Coronaviruses; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a):	No fusion of multiple systems of medicine to completely prevent the infection caused by new strains of Coronaviruses has been launched.(b): No definitive drugs/medicine based treatment exists against new variant of COVID-19.(c) & (d):	 Researchers across the globe are conducting investigations to develop medicines against coronavirus causing COVID-19. Treatment guidelines do not differ between strains of SARS-COV-2. The treatment guidelines are uploaded on Ministry of Health & Family Welfare website –https://www.mohfw.gov.in/pdf/UpdatedClinicalManagementProtocolforCOVID19dated03072020.pdf and Indian Council of Medical Research website - https://www.icmr.gov.in/crrteam.html.
