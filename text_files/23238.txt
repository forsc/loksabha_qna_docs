                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4151






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Memorials of Freedom Fighters




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is erecting memorials in honour of freedom fighters 	across the country;(b) if so, the names of the freedom fighter, State-wise; and (c) the details of pending proposals with the Government for erecting memorials to 	freedom fighters?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c) Yes, Sir. Government of India erect memorials in honour of freedom fighters across the country. The work of the memorial are executed through an authorised agency after the same is approved/ sanctioned by the concerned Ministry/ Authority. Presently, no proposal for erecting memorials to freedom fighters is pending with Government of India. In Culture Ministry, there is no such specific plan or scheme of erecting memorials in the honour of freedom fighters. However, during commemoration of important personalities and freedom fighters like Lala Lajpat Rai, Jaiprakash Narayan and Rani Gaidinliu, Ministry has sanctioned the fund which was used for various activities. Project at cost of Rs. 7 crores was sanctioned for construction of Rani Gaidinliu Cultural Centre in Nagaland in honour of Rani Gaidinliu, freedom fighter during her centenary commemoration celebrated in 2015-16. Similarly Funds of Rs. 5 Crore was sanctioned to Govt. of Punjab to construct Auditorium in honour of Lala Lajpat Rai during his 150th birth anniversary in 2015-16. Also, total amount of Rs. 4.40 crore was sanctioned for Jaiprakash Narayan Memorial at his birthplace Lala ki Tola, district Saran, Bihar.
