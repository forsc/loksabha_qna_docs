                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

924






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Forest Reserves in Bharatpur




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total reserve forest area in Bharatpur district in Rajasthan;(b)	the amount released for Forest Reserves during the last three years; and(c)	the work done during the last three years in the Bharatpur Forest Reserve?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) The total forest area of Bharatpur district in Rajasthan is 434.93 square kilometre. (b) The amount released during the last three (03) years to the Forest Divisions in Bharatpur District in Rajasthan is as follows:Financial year	Released Amount(Rs. in Lakh)2017-18	626.4042018-19	522.7722019-20	727.989Total	1877.165(c) Major work done during the last three years in the Forest Divisions in Bharatpur District are as follows:i.	Plantation and maintenance works, protection wall, soil and moisture conservation workii.	Fire line maintenance works, construction and repair of pathwaysiii.	Eradication of Prosopis juliflora, Mangur fish and hyacinthiv.	Maintenance of Forest Guard Chowkis, Office, Interpretation Centre, Rescue Centre, Sluice gate repair, etc.v.	Development and maintenance of amenities for tourists etc.*******
