                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2899






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Generation of Green Energy




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to generate Green Energy to meet the energy demand by the Railways;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the MoU has been signed by the Railways with power companies to generate solar and wind energy; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):  Yes Sir.  Indian Railways (IR) envisages to utilize about 10% of its energy requirement from Green Energy sources like Solar and Wind power.IR has planned to source about 1000 Mega Watt (MW) Solar Power as part of its Solar Mission.  Further, Indian Railways also plans to harness  about  200  MW  of  wind  power  based on techno-commercial assessment. Out of this, about 204.82 MW (101.42 MW solar and 103.4 MW wind power) renewable power has already been set up.(c) & (d): No such Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed by Railways with power companies to generate solar and wind power at this stage. Most of the work is being done in Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode (Developer mode) by private partners, in which Railways do not have to incur capital expenditure.*****
