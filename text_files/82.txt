                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Working Women Hostel Scheme




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the criteria set for providing financial assistance for the construction of working women hostels;(b)	whether the Government has formulated any action plan for construction of these hostels to meet the rising demands for them; (c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the status of the said scheme?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) As per the scheme guidelines, Implementing Agency should meet the following criteria for sanction of financial assistance for the construction of hostels for Working Women: (i) It should be either recognized by the State/UT Administration under any existing scheme/law or should be well known with the experience of working in the field for at least 3 years, and its work should be reported to be satisfactory by the State Government/Union Territory Administration concerned ;(ii) It should have a properly constituted Managing Body with its powers, duties and responsibilities clearly defined and laid down;(iii) It should ordinarily have been engaged in the field of women’s welfare/social welfare/ women’s education for a minimum period of three years prior to the request for grant-in-aid under the scheme;(iv) Its financial position should be sound and it should have facilities, resources, personnel and expenditure to undertake the management of such a project;(v) It should run the hostel and the Day Care Centre for pre-school children on a no-profit basis.(vi) The land on which the hostel building is proposed to be constructed should be a public land; (vii) The proposal should be recommended by the State Project Sanctioning Committee before being referred to the Ministry for consideration.
