                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4055






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






New Projects under PMKSY




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of water conservation projects being executed by the Government under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY) at present, State/ UT-wise; (b) whether the Government proposes to sanction new projects under PMKSY-WC and if so, the details thereof; and (c) the plan of the Government for providing water to each and every field in rainfed agriculture areas?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c)  The Government is implementing Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP) since 2009-10 for development of rainfed and degraded areas. In 2015-16, IWMP was amalgamated as the Watershed Development Component of the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (WDC-PMKSY). The activities undertaken inter alia include ridge area treatment, drainage line treatment, soil and moisture conservation, rainwater harvesting, nursery raising, afforestation, horticulture, pasture development, livelihoods for asset less persons, etc. 	Under the erstwhile IWMP, 8214 watershed development projects were sanctioned in erstwhile 28 States (except Goa) [now 27 States and UT of J & K and Ladakh), during the period 2009-10 to 2014-15 covering an area of about 39.07 million hectare. Since inception of the scheme, an amount of Rs.17916.28 crore has been released to the States as Central Share, as on 30.11.2019. The State-wise details of projects sanctioned, area covered, projects completed and funds released since 2009-10 to 2019-20, as on 30.11.2019 are given at Annexure. As per information received from the States, since 2014-15 to 2019-20 (upto second quarter), 6.08 lakh water harvesting structures have been created / rejuvenated. An additional area of 13.47 lakh ha has been brought under protective irrigation. 		The Government is focusing on completion of all on-going projects and therefore no new projects under WDC-PMKSY are being sanctioned.***** ANNEXUREAnnexure as referred to in reply to Parts (a) to (c) of Unstarred Question No. †4055 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 12.12.2019 regarding “New Projects under PMKSY”.
