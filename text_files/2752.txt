                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2569






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Violation of FCRA




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the details of cases registered against various organizations during the last three years regarding violation of Foreign Contributions Regulation Act (FCRA);(b) whether the Government/ investigating agencies are following pick and choose policy;(c) whether it is true that the Government is targeting the organizations who are campaigning against its policies; and(d) if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)   The details of organisations against which cases have been registered during the last three years are as under :-S.No.	Name of the NGO	Year1	New Life Ministries in India, Gospel Home, Behind TB Chickaballapur, Taluk, District Chickballapur, Kolar, Karnataka	20162	M/S Caruna Bal Vikash (CBV), Zachs Enclave First Floor, A-3, Second Avenue Anna Nagar East, Chennai.	20163	Adhane Management Consultants Private Ltd (AMCPL), Zachs Enclave First Floor, A-3, Second Avenue Anna Nagar East, Chennai.	20164	M/S Advantage India, 101-102, Oriental House, Gulmohar Enclave, New Delhi.	20175	Lawyers Collective, Jalaram Jyot, 4th Floor, 63, Janambhoomi Marg, Fort, Mumbai - 400001	2019(b) & (c):   No Sir. (d):   The question does not arise.*****
