                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2710






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Release of Pending Installment of MPLADS




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran













                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    Rajmohan Unnithan
                                                








                                                    Deepak Baij
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received petitions seeking for sanction of the pending installment of MPLADS for Financial Year 2019 and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of the amount released under MPLADS to the Members of Parliament (MPs) of 17th Lok Sabha State-wise/ MP-wise and installment-wise; (c) the details of the pending amount under the scheme before suspending the scheme due to Covid-19 pandemic, MP-wise and Parliamentary Constituency-wise; (d) whether the Ministry is considering to release the pending funds so that the development work can be resumed; (e)  whether the amount needed for the States for development under this scheme is being released to the States to deal with Covid Service or the Government of India is pending the said amount on its choice and if so, the details thereof; (f) whether the Government proposes to abolish MPLADS  and if so, the details and reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a)    Yes Sir. The details of Members of Parliament requested for release of pending instalments after the decision of the Government not to operate Members of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme (MPLADS) during FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 is at Annexure – I. (b)   The details of the amount released under MPLADS to the Members of Parliament (MPs) of 17th Lok Sabha State-wise and MP-wise is at column no. 5 of Annexure-II.  (c)   The number of instalments and amount pending for release before the decision of the Government not to operate Members of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme (MPLADS) in case of 16th and 17th Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha (sitting and ex-Members of Parliament) is as under:-Details of Pending instalment of 2018-19 and 2019-20                       Lok Sabha 	                    Rajya Sabha FY	No. of installments 	Amount (in crore)   No. of installments    Amount (in crore)        pending                                     pending 2014-15	        -	                -	            45	                 112.5	 2015-16	        2	               5.0	            66	                 165.0	 2016-17	       23	              57.5	            68	                 170.0	 2017-18	       98	             245.0	           102	                 255.0	 2018-19	      368	             920.0	           154	                 385	 2019-20	      489	            1222.5	           340	                 850	 Total	      980	            2450.0	           775	                1937.5	 The details of the Members of Parliament of 17th Lok Sabha whose instalments of 2019-20 are pending is at column 6 of Annexure II.  (d) The Government has decided not to operate the Members of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme (MPLADS) for two years, that is, during FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 and decided to place the MPLADS funds for FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 at the disposal of the Ministry of Finance for strengthening its efforts in managing the challenges of COVID-19 which has been declared as a global pandemic and a notified disaster in the country. Therefore, Ministry is not in a position to release any instalment or a part thereof during the FY 2020-21 and 2021-22. (e) The Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation vide Office Memorandum dated 08-04-2020 (Annexure III) has placed the budgetary outlay of 2020-21 of Rs. 3950 crore under MPLADS at the disposal of the Ministry of Finance for strengthening its efforts in managing the challenges of COVID-19, in pursuance of the said decision of the Government.  Further, no allocation under MPLADS (Grants-in-Aids) has been proposed in the Budget Estimate (BE) outlays for 2021-22 of the Annual Budget of the Ministry.  (f) No sir.                                                 *****
