                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

157






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Piped Drinking Water Supply




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the percentage of households having piped drinking water supply, State/ UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh; and (b)	the percentage of households using hand pump water for drinking purpose, State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR JAL SHAKTI(GAJENDRA SINGH SHEKHAWAT)(a)	& (b) A Statement of reply is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in the reply to the Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 157 due for reply on 11.2.2021(a)	As on date, out of 19.18 Crore rural households in the country,6.64 Crore (34.63%) households are reported to have tap water supply. As reported by States/ UTs including Uttar Pradesh, number of households with functional tap water connection, is at Annex–I. These details are in public domain and available on the JJM dashboard athttps://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx.(b) 	This Department does not maintain data related to percentage of households using hand pump water for drinking purpose. However, State/ UT–wise number of hand pumps installed, is at Annex–II.***
