                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

893






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Foreign Aid to Fight Corona Pandemic




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Central Government has received any financial assistance from the foreign countries for the implementation of health projects during Corona pandemic, if so, the details thereof;(b) the names of the programmes started with this aid; and(c) the details of the assistance received by the Central Government in the health sector from World Health Organisation during Corona pandemic?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b): Official Development Assistance (ODA) Grants and ODA loans have been received from Japan to support COVID-19 crisis. In addition, ventilators, test kits etc. have been provided by Germany, United States of America (USA), France and Israel. (c): World Health Organisation (WHO) has provided technical assistance in training of experts and develop technical guidance; support in surveillance, contact tracing and containment activities; lab testing capacity building and quality assurance, strengthening of infection prevention, vaccination campaign and research & innovation.
