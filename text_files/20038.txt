                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1346






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Procurement of Wheat and Rice




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of procurement undertaken in wheat and rice in the year 2020-21 along with the quantum of procurement compared to the previous year; (b) 	whether the Minimum Support Price (MSP) regime has helped in procuring the quantum and ensuring food security in the country; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	The quantum of wheat and rice procured during 2019-20 and 2020-21 is as under:(Fig. in LMT)KMS/RMS	Rice	Wheat2019-20	519.97	341.322020-21	405.32*	389.92* Figures reported is as on 01.02.2021(b) and (c): 	Procurement of wheat and paddy under Minimum Support Price (MSP) operations is on increasing trend which indicates that the MSP regime is helpful and instrumental in ensuring food security in the country.The procurement of wheat and paddy for the last three years and current year is as under:                                                                                      (Fig. in LMT)Year	Wheat	Paddy2017-18	308.24	568.542018-19	357.95	660.112019-20	341.32	773.452020-21	389.92	604.03## Figures reported as on 01.02.2021****
