                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Fencing on National Highways




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the stretches having fencing on National Highways (NHs) in the country including Tamil Nadu; (b) 	the details of the number of stretches on NHs which are still without fencing in the country including Tamil Nadu; (c)	whether the Government has made any plan for their fencing; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be done?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) & (b) Fencing/ boundary wall has been provided in two operational stretches of NHAI i.e. Eastern Peripheral Expressway and Package-4 of Delhi Meerut Expressway (green field portion). Fencing has also been provided in 17 km length in NH-354E in Punjab. Fencing in partially access controlled National Highways (NHs) or NHs without access control is provided only in specific locations as per provision of Contract/Concession Agreement.(c) & (d) Fencing is generally provided all along the expressway to make them access controlled. There is no plan for fencing all along NHs.*****
