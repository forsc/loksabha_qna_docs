                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






5kg Gas Cylinder Connections




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has data on total number of 5kg gas cylinder connections issued under the PM Ujjwala yojana and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether very few 5kg gas cylinder connections have been issued despite the fact that it is a more affordable option among low income families and if so, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government has taken measures to improve the issuance of 5kg cylinder connections, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) whether the Government has data of individuals who have surrendered their LPG subsidy under the give it up initiative and if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c) Under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY), the customers have been given option to select 5 kg or 14.2 kg cylinder connections. As on 01.01.2022, Oil Marketing Companies(OMCs) have released 8.1  lakh  number of 5 kg connections to PMUY beneficiaries. Apart from the above, OMCs have also given swapping option from 14.2 kg cylinder with 5 kg cylinder and vice versa to the PMUY beneficiaries. 14.2 kg beneficiaries  numbering 7.69 lakh have availed this facility to switch to 5 kg cylinders. A seamless and hassle free facility to switch to 5 Kg cylinder exists and the option in this regard is exercised by the beneficiaries. (d)	As on 01.01.2022, 1.08 crore LPG consumers have voluntary given up their LPG subsidy. State/UT-wise details are at Annexure.
