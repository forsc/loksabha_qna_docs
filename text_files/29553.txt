06.12.2021






Tribal Sub Plan in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the funds allocated under various programs under Tribal Sub Plan schemes in West Bengal; and (b) the details of projects implemented under Tribal Sub Plan (TSP) in tribal dominated areas of Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): The details of funds allocated under various programs under Tribal Sub Plan schemes in West Bengal by the Union Government (during the Financial Years 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 as on 18th November, 2021) are stated at Annexure I. Relevant information has been sought from the State Government.(b): Funds are released by Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) to States, including West Bengal, based upon proposals received from State Governments, after the approval of State Level Executive Committee headed by Chief Secretary of the State, and considered in the Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) of MoTA, to bridge the gaps in sectors like education, health, agriculture, skill development, employment-cum-income generation, etc. The minutes of PACs of various financial years are available in public domain at  https://tribal.nic.in/display_PAC_minutes.aspx. The detail of projects approved by PAC, MoTA for West Bengal with, Darjeeling or Kalimpong Districts as locations, during various financial years under various schemes of MoTA is also available thereat. However, the activities approved by PAC under Grants under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution  for West Bengal, with Darjeeling / Kalimpong as locations, for various financial years (F.Y. 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21) are stated at Annexure II.
