                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3192






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Teachers Education Programme




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has granted retrospective recognition for various teacher education program conducted by certain Educational Institute; (b) 	the Courses/Programmes permitted under this policy; (c)	whether this policy will have an impact on the quality of education in the elementary level; (d)	the number of Institutions recognized under this relaxation State-wise; and (e) 	whether the Government has made it mandatory for admission of B-ed, D-ed courses on entrance test basis?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)	to (d) :	Government vide notifications dated 12th May, 2020 has granted retrospective recognition/permission to Central/State Institutions/Universities. In this notification, recognition/permission was granted to eleven teacher education institutions and twelve teacher education programmes. Retrospective recognition/permission to these institutions/universities was granted to safeguard the future of students passed/passing out of these institutions/universities.  The details are enclosed at Annexure-I. (e)	: The National Education Policy (NEP), 2020 recommends entrance test for four year B.Ed. integrated course. However, at present conduct of entrance test for B.Ed./D.Ed. is decided by the affiliating bodies/Universities.   *****
