                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

214






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Work to Labourers under MGNREGS




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether all the labourers registered under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) have been provided 100 days of work in the country, if so, the details thereof, State-wise and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) 	the number of labourers who got 100 days of work under MGNREGS during the last three years, Statewise  including those in the State of Chhattisgarh;(c) 	the number of labourers who have been provided work under MGNREGS since March, 2020 in the State of Chhattisgarh including Bilaspur Lok Sabha Constituency; and(d) 	the role of MGNREGS in creating employment, particularly in rural areas during corona pandemic period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a)to(d): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.  Statement in reply to Parts (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 214 for 03.08.2021(a):	The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven scheme for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. The State-wise details of number of households demanded employment and number of households offered employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in last three financial years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 and current financial year 2021-22 is given at Annexure I.(b): 	State-wise (including Chhattisgarh) number of households who have completed 100 days of work under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the last three financial years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 is given at Annexure II. (c):	The number of households who have been provided employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in the State of Chhattisgarh and in the Districts of Bilaspur and Mungeli since March 2020 is given below:State /District	            March 2020	         FY 2020-21	       FY 2021-22                                                                    (as on 29.07.2021) Chhattisgarh	            9.52 Lakh	        30.61 Lakh	         21.99 LakhBilaspur District	   47,592	        1,38,954	         95,839Mungeli District	   24,600	        1,04,040	         72,772 (d):	Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme. It provides livelihood security i.e., fall back option for livelihood for the rural households when no better employment opportunity is available.  	During the financial year 2020-21, a total of 389 crore persondays have been generated which is 47 % more than the previous period of financial year 2019-20. During the financial year 2020-21, a total of 1.89 crore new job card have been issued. In the financial year 2020-21, a total of 7.55 crore households have been provided employment which 38% more the previous financial year.
