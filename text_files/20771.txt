                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Saksham Anganwadis




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















1933:	SHRI KRIPANATH MALLAH: Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:(a)	the salient features of ‘Saksham Anganwadis‘ Scheme;(b)	the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this scheme in Assam;(c)	the number of districts covered initially under this scheme in Assam;(d)	the details of the target set under the scheme; and(e)	the details of the achievements made so far? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to(e): States/UTs have been supported with infrastructural needs of Anganwadi Centres under Anganwadi Services scheme including in the State of Assam.  ******
