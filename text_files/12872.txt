                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Cultural Troupes




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Cultural Troupes associated with the Ministry of Culture who have performed nationally and internationally during the last five years; and(b)	the steps taken to promote Nepali, Rajbanshi, Bengali and Adivasi performers from the Districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTR OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Ministry of Culture has been empaneling artists/groups for performing in Festival of India (FoI) abroad. The details of artists/groups performed in FoIs abroad during last five years are enclosed.   Moreover, Organizations/ Institutions under this Ministry also organise events within the country through various artists/groups. Records of such artists/groups are not maintained centrally.(b)	To promote Nepali, Rajbanshi, Bengali and Adivasi performers from the Districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur, North East Zone Cultural Centre (NEZCC), Dimapur and Eastern Zonal Cultural Centre (EZCC), Kolkata engage them in the programmes organized by NEZCC/EZCC.AnnexureFestival of India held during the last five yearsSl.
