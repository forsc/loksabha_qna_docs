                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3653






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Agri Entrepreneurial Cell




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik













                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up an Agri Entrepreneurial Cell to encourage agri start-ups and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has identified the key areas for innovative work related to agri start-ups to be taken up and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has also decided to reserve 20 per cent of Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana Fund to set up incubator facilities and to promote entrepreneurs and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government has set up any monitoring mechanism to ensure proper utilization of the said funds and if so, the details thereof; (e) 	whether the Government proposes a policy to nurture agri-startups in the country and if so, the details thereof; and (f) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to connect Farmers Producers Organization through technology?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (e): This Ministry has launched a new programme called “Innovation and agri-entrepreneur development”  under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY-RAFTAAR) in 2018-19 with objective to promote innovation and agripreneurship by providing financial support and nurturing the incubation ecosystem with 8% of annual outlay of the scheme. Contd…2/--2-These innovations may include technological solutions/ processes / products/ services/ business platforms etc. that could enhance productivity or increase efficiency in agriculture and allied activities.In this regard, five Knowledge Partners (KPs) and twenty four Agribusiness Incubators (R-ABIs) to  encourage and strengthen agri start-ups through this programme. List of  designated KPs and R-ABIs is enclosed (Annexure).To monitor this programme, KPs and RABIs will submit consolidated annual progress report along with audited accounts to  the nodal division of this Ministry.  They will upload physical and financial progress status on their website on a regular basis.(f):	Government is implementing National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) scheme wherein online trading of agriculture and horticulture commodities is done for transparent price discovery for farmers  for their produce through competitive online bidding system. So far, 838 Farmers Producers Organization (FPOs) have been onboarded on e-NAM Platform.
