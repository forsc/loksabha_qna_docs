                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1510






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Funds for Modernisation of Courts




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of total funds allocated for modernization, expansion of infrastructure of  Lower Courts, High Courts and Supreme Court in the country during the previous Five Year Plan and current annual plans; (b) whether the funds allocated in this regard are adequate; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, whether the Government proposes to enhance the allocation during the next Five Years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c):	It is the primary responsibility of the State Governments to provide judicial infrastructure for district and subordinate courts.  The Union Government has been administering a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for the Development of Infrastructure Facilities for Judiciary in order to augment the resources of State Governments /UTs by providing financial assistance to the States/UTs in the prescribed fund sharing pattern. It covers the construction of court buildings and residential accommodations of Judicial Officer/Judges of District and Subordinate Judiciary. The High Courts and the Supreme Court are not covered under this Scheme. During the 12th plan period (2012-13 to 2016-17) a sum of Rs. 3637.93 crore has been released under the Scheme. During the last 3 financial years a sum of Rs. 2253.21 crore has been released under the Scheme. The detail of funds released under the Scheme during the 12th Five Year Plan period and last 3 financial years is as follows:Sl.  No.	Year	Funds released (in Rs. crore)1	12th Five Year Plan period (2012-17)	3637.932	2017-18	621.213	2018-19	650.004	2019-20	982.00(d): The allocation for the scheme is decided on an annual basis. As against R.E. of Rs. 593.00 crore under this scheme for 2020-21, the B.E. figure for 2021-22 is Rs. 776.00   crore.***********
