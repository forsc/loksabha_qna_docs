                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3576






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Beautification of Ponds in  Temples




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the list of ponds/tanks adjoining temples managed by ASI in the city of Bhubaneswar;(b)	whether the Central Government proposes to prepare an action plan/detailed project report for revival/renovation/beautification of the water bodies adjoining ancient temples in Bhubaneswar; and (c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	The list of protected ponds/tanks and ponds/tanks attached to protected monuments under the protection of Archaeological Survey of India in the city of Bhubaneswar is at Annexure.  (b)&(c)	As per the existing policy of the Government, the monuments/sites are declared protected under “The Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act 1958 (24 of 1958)” are maintained and conserved by the Archaeological Survey of India as per archaeological norms and principles depending upon the availability of resources and needs of conservation without compromising their authenticity and integrity. The expenditure incurred for conservation and preservation of centrally protected tanks and monuments having tanks in the city of Bhubaneswar is at Annexure-II.
