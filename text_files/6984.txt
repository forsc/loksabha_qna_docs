                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

742






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Protocol of Dignitaries




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any clear definition for the protocol of dignitaries/VIPs travelling by domestic or international flights and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether the airlines and the companies managing airports have been given specific instructions about the protocol of dignitaries/VIPs and if so, the list of such people?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Bureau of Civil Aviation Security (BCAS), an attached office of this Ministry and aviation security regulator in the country has issued Aviation Security (AVSEC) Circular No.18/2009 for extending courtesy to Hon''ble Members of Parliament at airports during their visits to airports. BCAS has also issued AVSEC Order No.06/2015 for exemption from Pre-embarkation Security Checks at civil airports to dignitaries and their accompanying spouse. The facilities provided to dignitaries/Members of Parliament at the airports are given as under:-;- Reserved Lounge facilities at international and domestic terminals of the airports;- Tea/Coffee/Water served free of charge;- Member of Parliament acts as Chairman of Airport Advisory Committee in respect of the airports which falls under his/her constituency;;- Free Access to the Hon''ble MP in the Terminal Building and Visitors gallery on the basis of Identity Card;- Airport Entry Pass to personal staff of MP - one at New Delhi and another for an airport nearest to his/her constituency;- One officer at the airport is designated as Protocol officer at each airport and is responsible for extension of all facilities / courtesies to MPs. ;;The protocol is to be followed by all concerned, including domestic private airports and airlines.
