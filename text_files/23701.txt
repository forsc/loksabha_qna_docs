                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4700






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Fake Certificates during Army Recruitment




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the certificate of some persons have been found fake during Army recruitment;(b) if so, whether the Government is considering to take any action against the said persons; (c) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said action is likely to be taken; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (d):  Yes, Sir.  Whenever a fake certificate is caught, the individual’s candidature is cancelled and he is not allowed to take part in the recruiting process further.*****
