                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4558






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Advance Risk Assessment




Arvind Kumar Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) and SDRFs have mandate of anticipatory or advance risk assessment based profiling of sensitive zones to prevent or mitigate natural disasters, like the one that struck Chamoli, Uttarakhand; and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) and (b):	     As per Section 44 of Disaster Management Act, 2005, National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) is constituted for the purpose of a specialist response to a threatening disaster situation or disaster.  Advance risk assessment based on profiling of sensitive zone to prevent or mitigate natural disaster is beyond the mandate of NDRF.  However, the mandate of State Disaster Response Forces (SDRFs) are governed by statutes of State Government concerned. *******
