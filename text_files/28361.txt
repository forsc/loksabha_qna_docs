                                                    Vijayakumar (alias) Vijay Vasanth
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any data on the  number of stolen/smuggled artefacts and antiquities over a period of time from 2015-2021 in various States of the country, State-wise;(b) whether the Government has successfully brought back many idols and artifacts from across the world including the idol of Goddess Annapurna which were taken 100 years ago and if so, the details thereof and if not, the steps taken by the Government to bring back stolen antiques/artefacts/idols to India;(c) the details of artefacts and the countries from where it was returned during the last twenty years, year-wise and country-wise;(d) whether the Government has handed over the Annapurna idol and other idols and artefacts to the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Varanasi and other temples and places from where it belonged originally;(e) whether the Government of Australia and the USA has handed over 157 artifacts related to Hinduism, Buddhism and Jainism during the recent visit of Prime Minister to USA and if so, the details thereof; and(f) whether the Government is taking any measures to strengthen the vigilance and monitoring system to ensure minimization of thefts of such antiquities in the country and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF TOURISM, CULTURE AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) Yes, Sir. The State-wise details of stolen artefacts and antiquities during the period from 2015 to 2021 are at Annexure I.(b)&(c) Yes, Sir. 107 antiquities including the idol of Goddess Annapurna have been brought back during the last 20 years from different countries. The details of country-wise number of antiquities repatriated are at Annexure II.(d) Government of India has handed over the Annapurna idol to Government of UP. Details of other antiquities returned back to their original places from where they belonged are at Annexure III.(e) The USA has handed over 157 antiquities related to Hinduism, Buddhism and Jainism during the recent visit of Prime Minister to USA. Out of 157 antiquities, first lot of 63 antiquities has reached India. Nine antiquities have been retrieved from Australia.(f) The Archaeological Survey of India is committed towards the protection and safety of antiquities at protected monuments, sites and museums.  For the purpose, watch and ward staff is deployed and their strength have been augmented by deploying private security guards, State Police armed guards and the Central Industrial Security Force.
