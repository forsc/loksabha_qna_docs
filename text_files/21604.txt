                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2847






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Distribution of Medicines to  CGHS Beneficiaries




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) beneficiaries are getting medicines for chronic diseases in CGHS hospitals;(b) the details of action taken to provide medicines for chronic diseases at the time of             non-availability of such medicines in CGHS hospitals;(c) whether the Government has taken a decision for opening up of more CGHS Wellness Centres in rural areas to cater to the needs of ordinary poor people; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): Yes. Medicines for CGHS beneficiaries including that for chronic diseases are provided through CGHS Wellness Centres. In case, any medicine is not readily available at CGHS Wellness Centre, the same is made available to the CGHS beneficiary by the Wellness Centre through Indentation from the concerned Authorized Local Chemist.  (c) & (d): No. CGHS is a contributory health scheme primarily meant for Central Government employees and pensioners and their dependent family members.
