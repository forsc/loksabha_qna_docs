                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology be pleased to state:-(a) 	the recent/amended salient features of Digi-Gaon Project;(b) 	the State-wise details of the number of villages covered under the above said project across the country including the State of Karnataka till date; (c) 	whether the Government has received any request from the States/UTs for establishment of more Digi-Gaon and if so, the details thereof and if not, the details of the Government proposal to expand this project across the country;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to expand Common Service Centres (CSCs) under the aforesaid project and if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof, including the State of Karnataka along with the details of expenditure; and(e) 	whether the Government proposes to include entrepreneurship opportunities for the rural residents specially women under the said project and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        a): 	Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MeitY) initiated the Digital Village Pilot Project on 31st October 2018. CSC eGovernance Services Limited (CSC-SPV) is implementing the Digital Village Pilot Project. The Digital Village concept was based on the DigiGaon of CSC-SPV in 6 Villages. The salient features of the Digital Village Pilot project are:•	700 Gram Panchayat villages with atleast one village from each district of the State/UT are being covered under the pilot implementation of the project. •	BharatNet for broadband connectivity and Digital Seva Platform (DSP) of CSCs in villages across the country are being leveraged for the delivery of services to the rural citizens. •	The deliverables of the project are: i.	Government to Citizen Services (G2C) - online registration for professional entrance examinations, Pan Card Services, new Aadhaar enrolment, its updation/modification, online registration for service selection board, online registration for public service commission, etc.ii.	B2C Services – Mobile / DTH recharge, ticket booking (Rail/Air), etc.iii.	Education Service: Courses offered under this project are as (i) Basic Computer Course (BCC), (ii) Course on Computer Concept (CCC), (iii) Tally – Kaushal Praman Patraiv.	Health Services – Tele-Health & Tele-Veterinary consultation v.	Financial Inclusion- Financial inclusion awareness programme  vi.	Solar panel powered street lights (8 Solar lights per village)vii.	Skill Development - skill development in (i) Automotive Technician, (ii) Handset Repairing, (iii) Field Technician- Home Appliances, and (iv) Electrical Technician.(b): 	State-wise details of the number of villages covered under the project across the country is enclosed at Annexure-I. List of villages covered under the project in Karnataka State is enclosed at Annexure-II. In addition to the 700 villages that are being funded by MeitY for implementation, 4,279 villages are being made digital by CSC e-Governance Service India Limited (CSC-SPV) under its own initiative through funds sourced from Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) which includes 19 villages in Karnataka. State/UT wise detail of villages covered by CSC-SPV through CSR fund support under its own initiative is enclosed at Annexure-III.(c): 	The pilot phase of the Digital Village project is currently under implementation.(d): 	CSC-SPV is the implementing Agency for the “Pilot Project – Implementation of Digital Village” and there is no provision to expand CSCs under the said project.(e): 	Under the project “Pilot Project – Implementation of Digital Village”, there is no specific provision for women. However, Skill Development component as mentioned in reply of para (a) & (b) above would help in expanding entrepreneurship opportunities for rural residents including women.******* Annexure-IList
