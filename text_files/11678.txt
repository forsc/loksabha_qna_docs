                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

627






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Railway Line Between Upshi to Phey




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Government has any plans or has taken any steps to setup the entire Railway line from Upshi to Phey 51 Km this year itself;(b)    if so, the details thereof;  and(c)    if not, the reasons therefor and the time by which the Government plans to do so ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) to (c)   Final Location Survey (FLS) of Bilaspur-Manali-Leh has been undertaken as  a strategic line deposit work of Ministry of Defence.   Upshi-Phey 51 Km stretch is part of this alignment.  Further, consideration of this line is possible once the Final Location Survey report becomes available and results thereon are firmed up.  Presently, there is no other separate proposal under consideration to set up Railway line from Upshi to Phey (Leh).*****
