                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of TRIBES India retail stores and online TRIBES India retail outlets operational in the country and details thereof, state-wise;(b) the number of countries that TRIBES India is selling products made by tribal people;(c) the number of tribal natural products and handicrafts currently being sold/ produced at TRIBES India retails outlets and details thereof, in the country State-wise including Jharkhand, Rajasthan, Bihar, Gujarat and Maharashtra along with the details thereof;(d) the number of such products being sold, nationally and internationally, which possess Geographical Indications (GI) Tags and details, thereof, State-wise; and(e) the steps being taken, if any, to grant GI tags to products of tribal people that are sold nationally and internationally;(f) the total budget allocated by the Government to procure the said products during the last three years and the transactions carried out through these outlets along with the State-wise details thereof;(g) whether there is any proposal to set up a TRIBES India outlet in Mount Abu to provide market to the goods produced by the tribals of Pindwara and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) As informed by Tribal Co-operative Marketing Development Federation of India (TRIFED), the number of TRIBES India outlet as on 31.01.2022 is 119. Details of State-wise TRIBES India retail stores and online TRIBES India retail outlets is given in Annexure.(b) There are no fixed number of countries where TRIBES India products are sold as the items are despatched abroad as and when purchase orders from such places are obtained.
