                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4949






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






National Health Mission




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allotted and utilised under the National Health Mission (NHM) since its inception, State-wise including Tamil Nadu; and(b) the steps taken by Government to assess the implementation and progress of NHM along with the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	& (b): National Health Mission (NHM) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under which the Government of India provides financial and technical support to States/UTs to strengthen their health care systems.The details of funds released and utilized under the National Health Mission (NHM) since its inception, State-wise including Tamil Nadu is at Annexure-I. Public health and hospitals is State subject, hence the primary responsibility for proper implementation of the scheme lies with the State/UT Governments. However, under NHM, regular interactions are held with States/UTs. Various mechanisms exist to assess the implementation and progress of NHM like:•	Review of key deliverables of Record of Proceedings of previous financial year during the NPCC meetings,•	Annual visits to States by Common Review Mission which inter-alia, looks at financial systems and mechanisms.•	Implementation of Public Financial Management System for monitoring and management of funds.•	Regular monitoring visits by senior officials of the Ministry.Further, Government of India also reviews /assesses the implementation of NHM through external surveys such as, National Family Health Survey, Annual Health Survey and Sample Registration System.The present status of progress under NHM is placed at Annexure-II.
