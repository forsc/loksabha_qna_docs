                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Construction of Kendriya Vidyalayas




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently inaugurated 13 newly constructed buildingsand laid down the foundation stones of 6 Kendriya Vidyalayas located in 12States;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has received any proposal from the State AndhraPradesh to allot Kendriya Vidyalayas in the next academic year; and(d) if so, the details thereof and their status thereon?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) 13 newly constructed buildings of Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) were inauguratedand foundation stones were laid for 6 KV buildings recently, the details of which are asunder:-<pre>STATE Buildings Inaugurated Foundation stone laidArunachal Pradesh - LongdingAssam Golaghat, Haflong & Tamulpur -Bihar Arraria -Chhattisgarh - KawardhaGujarat Jetpur & Viramgaon -Jharkhand - GiridihKarnataka Kodagu ChennapatnaMadhya Pradesh Satna No.2 & Betul -Odisha Sambalpur JagatsinghpurTamil Nadu Parambalur -Telangana Miryalguda NizamabadUttar Pradesh Shivgarh -</pre>(c) & (d) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that it has received 12proposals from the State Government of Andhra Pradesh for opening of new KendriyaVidyalayas at Anakapali (Distt. Visakhapatnam), Naidupet, Kavali, Kalichedu & WestGudur (Distt. Nellore), Vankayalapadu Village, Krishnayapalem & Navuluru (Distt.Guntur), Srikalahasti, Gudipala & Jangalapalle (Distt.Chittoor) and Ongole (Distt.Prakasam).Except the proposal for opening of a new KV at Anakapalli which has been foundto be fulfilling the mandatory requirements as per the norms of KVS, the remainingproposals have some discrepancies / inadequacies and these have been brought to thenotice of State Government of Andhra Pradesh for necessary rectification.*****
