                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1870






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Paris Climate Accord




K. Subbarayan














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the attention of theGovernment has been drawn to the decisionof the Government of United States ofAmerica to withdraw from the Paris ClimateAccord; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof and thereaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)and (b) Government of India is aware that the Government of the United States of America (USA) notified the Secretary-General of United Nations on 4 November 2019of its decision to withdraw from the Agreement which shall take effect on 4 November 2020 in accordance with article 28 (1) and (2) of the Agreement.USA is the second largest Greenhouse Gas (GHG)emitter presently. However, if cumulative historical emission is considered, USA would belargest GHG emitter. So, its withdrawal will affect control of cumulative global GHG emissions.It will also affect the availability of international funds for climate change, as USA being a developed countryis acontributor to climate finance.*****
