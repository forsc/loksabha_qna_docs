                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3505






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Trains to Tourist Places




Shiromani Ram














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any scheme to connect all the major historical cities/tourist places of the country with the Railways;(b)	if so, whether there is any scheme to directly connect Shrawasti Parliamentary Constituency which is a famous Buddhist site with the major cities of the country; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	Indian Railways, through Indian Railway Catering & Tourism Corporation (IRCTC), is operating various tourist trains such as Bharat Darshan/ Aastha Circuit Tourist, Pilgrim Tourist  Trains, State Special Tourist Trains, Deluxe Tourist Trains,  Buddhist Circuit Train and Luxury Tourist Trains  which are connecting major historical cities/tourist places of the country viz. Ayodhya, Varanasi, Ujjain, Agra, Prayagraj, Baidyanath, Bodhgaya, Rajgir, Nalanda, Nagpur, Indore, Aurangabad, Chitrakoot, Mathura, Vrindavan, Puri, Gangasagar, Tirupati, Madurai, Rameshwaram, Srirangapatnam, Mysore, Dwarka, Ankaleshwar, Nasik, Jaisalmer, Jodhpur, Bikaner, Jaipur, Udaipur, Ajmer, Khajuraho, Orchha, Sarnath, Kushinagar, Sravasti, Fatehpur Sikri, Nathdwara, Nandigram, Sitamadhi, Hampi, Mumbai, Gokarana, Belur, Jabalpur, Coorg, Thanjavur.(b) and (c):Yes, Sir.  New Rail line project Bahraich-Khalilabad (240 km) via Shrawasti has been sanctioned at a cost of Rs.4940 crore.*****
