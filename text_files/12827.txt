                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1655






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Buildings for Kendriya Vidyalayas




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that many Kendriya Vidyalayas do not have their ownbuildings or there is shortage of rooms according to the number of students;(b) if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof;(c) the names of the Vidyalayas for which funds for the construction of thebuildings/rooms has been released along with details thereof;(d) the details of the schools of the Madhya Pradesh which do not have sufficientrooms in proportion to the number of the students and also schools without theirown buildings; and(e) the time by which the said schools are likely to be provided funds?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) As on date, 283 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are running in the temporaryinfrastructure provided by various sponsoring authorities. The State / UT-wise details areat Annexure-I.(c) KV wise details of fund released during the year 2019-20 for the construction ofthe building/rooms are at Annexure-II(d) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that there are no such KV inMadhya Pradesh which do not have sufficient rooms in proportion to the number ofstudents. As on date, there are 25 KVs (including 2 KVs in project for which permanentschool building is provided by the concerned project authority) namely Chaurai, No.2Chhindwada, Harda, Malanjkhand, Pandhurna, Chanderi, GanjBasoda, No. IINeemuch, Dabra, Hattanagar, Lakhnadaun, Umaria, Amba, Jamai, BSF Tekanpur,Multai, CRPF Bangrasia, Kasarwad, Anuppur, Shyamgarh, CRPF Nayagaon, AgarMalwa, Garha, Amarkantak (Project) & Sagar No. 4 (Project) functioning in temporarybuildings provided by concerned Sponsoring Authorities in Madhya Pradesh.(e) Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, whichdepends upon identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favour ofKVS by the sponsoring authorities, submission of drawings / estimates by constructionagency, availability of funds and requisite approvals etc. As such, no definite time framecan be given in this regard.*****
