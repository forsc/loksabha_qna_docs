                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4404






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Central Legislation for Regulation of Yoga and Naturopathy Systems




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether there is any central legislation for regulation of education and practice in Yoga and Naturopathy systems; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor; and (c) the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):      No, currently there is no central legislation for regulation of education and practice in Yoga and Naturopathy.(b) & (c):      The Government has established a Board namely, “National Board for Promotion of Yoga & Naturopathy” (NBPYN) in February, 2016 to take policy decisions regarding promotion of Yoga & Naturopathy in the country. Steps for empowering the existing mechanism of NBPYN are being taken up to strengthen the aspects of regulating education and practice of Yoga and Naturopathy.......................
