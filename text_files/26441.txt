                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Credits to NBFCs




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the criteria for banks to provide credits to Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs); and(b) the data related to bank wise amount of credit/loans to NBFCs from 2014-2019?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a): The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has informed that banks formulate loan policy with the approval of their Boards of Directors within the prudential guidelines and exposure norms prescribed by RBI to extend various kinds of credit facilities to NBFCs, subject to conditions laid out in RBI’s Master Circular on Bank Finance to NBFCs, dated 1.7.2015. RBI has further apprised that as per the said Master Circular, RBI places restrictions on banks on financing of certain activities undertaken by NBFCs like granting of bridge loans to NBFCs, providing of loans against the security of shares or debentures, execution of guarantees that directly or indirectly enable placement of deposits with NBFCs, and investing in securities or instruments issued by NBFCs.(b): RBI has informed that the details regarding the amount of credits or loans given to NBFCs by scheduled commercial banks (SCBs) between 2014 to 2019 are not maintained by it. As per RBI data, the bank-wise details of the loan outstanding (fund based) of NBFCs given by scheduled commercial banks, as at 31.3.2015 and as at 31.3.2019 is at Annex. RBI has further informed that data prior to 31.3.2015 is not maintained with it.*****
