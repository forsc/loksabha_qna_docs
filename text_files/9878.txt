                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






UGC & AICTE




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether teachers are working on daily wages and on contractual basis on the payment less than theapproved pay for the regular staff in higher educational institutes under UGC and AICTE;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) the category-wise total number of sanctioned posts of regular employees in the higher educationalinstitutions and the number of contractual teachers;(d) whether hiring such teachers creates adverse impact on the quality of education and the dignity ofthe institutions;(e) if so, the remedial measures being taken in this regard;(f) whether the Government is aware of the adverse impact caused on the lives of the said teachersand their families on being removed/rendered jobless after 5/10/15 years of service; and(g) if so, the details of the remedial measures taken in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (g): The University Grants Commission (UGC) has framed UGC Regulations on MinimumQualifications for Appointment of Teachers and Other Academic Staff in Universities and Collegesand Measures for the maintenance of standards in Higher Education, 2018 wherein the clause 13provides that the fixed emoluments paid to contract teachers should not be less than the monthly grosssalary of a regularly appointed Assistant Professor.As per All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) report 2018-19, the total number ofteachers in Higher Educational Institutions is 1416299 out of which 90418 are Temporary andVisiting teachers. The category-wise details are at Annexure.Since total absence of faculty create larger problem on quality, teacher on temporary basis fillin the gap in imparting lectures till a regular teacher is engaged.Vide its letter dated 4th June, 2019, UGC has asked all Universities to fill its facultyvacancies.*****
