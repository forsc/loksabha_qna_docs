                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

810






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Compensatory Afforestation




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of locations where compensatory afforestation has been carried out for various projects in the country for the past two years;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra; and(c)	the details of the CAMPA funds sanctioned to Maharashtra during the last two years and the current year, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b) The Central Government has launched a portal namely PARIVESH (“ Pro Active and Responsive Facilitation by Interactive and Virtuous Environmental Single Window Hub”) for the purpose of making proposals for non-forest use of forest land under Forest (Conservation) Act,1980 (FCA,1980). Compensatory Afforestation (CA) is one of the most important conditions stipulated by the Central Government while approving these proposals. There is a provision to upload the locations of the CA area in the form of KML files, Survey of India Toposheet and DGPS Maps, etc. in this portal for all States/UTs. The State/UT wise details including Maharshtra for the past two years are annexed at Annexure-I	(c) The details of the CAMPA funds allocated to Maharashtra year-wise and the details of funds spent out circle-wise, as provided by the State, is annexed at Annexure-II and Annexure-IIIrespectively.*****
