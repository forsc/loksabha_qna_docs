                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4812






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Scholarship for Differently Abled Students




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the assistance provided by the Government to the States for disabled students so as to enable them to go for higher studies during the last three years;(b)	whether the number of disabled students getting  scholarships/assistance for higher studies is low across the country; and(c)	if so, the action taken by the Government to increase the number of beneficiaries?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a)	The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities is implementing the Central Sector umbrella Scheme of Scholarships for  Students with Disabilities (SwDs) for higher studies of  disabled students    with the following components: 1)	Post-matric Scholarship for SwDs (For Class XI to Post-graduate Degree/Diploma)2)	Top Class Education Scholarship for SwDs (For Graduate and Post-graduate Degree/Diploma in notified Institutes of excellence in Education).3)	National Overseas Scholarship (For Master’s Degree/Ph.D. in Foreign Universities).4)	National Fellowship for PwDs (For M.Phil. and Ph.D. in Indian Universities).5)	Free Coaching for SwDs (For recruitment examinations for Group A & B posts and entrance examinations for admission to technical and professional courses).Under the above scheme there is no provision to release the funds to the States. The scholarship amount is released directly to students in their bank account through DBT mode.	The details of funds released to students with disabilities for higher studies during last three years are at Annexure.(b)	No, Sir. The number of students with disabilities getting scholarship has shown an increasing trend from a low of 306 students during 2014-15 to that of 23852 students in 2018-19.(c)	Not applicable in view of answer to (b) above.  *****
