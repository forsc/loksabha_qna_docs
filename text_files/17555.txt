                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1348






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Upkeep of Historical Monuments by Corporates andLocal Citizens




L.S. Tejasvi Surya













                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan of approaching corporate organisations in order to seek their contribution in the upkeep of popular historical monuments, if so, what has been the response to these efforts; and(b)	the measures taken by the Government to meet the increasing expenditure for the same and whether the Government is considering any policy of involving local citizens by inculcating a sense of ownership amongthem towards such monuments?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	National Culture Fund (NCF) has been set up in 1996 by the Ministry of Culture, Government of India for providing a platform for corporate organisations to contribute in the upkeep of historical monuments, providing facilities, etc.   Further, the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India is implementing ‘Adopt-a-Heritage’ scheme where corporate sector is involved as ‘Monument Mitra’ for providing facilities and maintaining thereof at historical monuments. (b)	The expenditure on conservation, preservation and annual maintenance of centrally protected monuments is being met out from regular budget allocated to the Archaeological Survey of India. The NCF and Adopt-a-Heritage scheme is an attempt to involve others in conservation and preservation of our national heritage. *****
