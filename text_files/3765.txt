                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3457






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Kasturba Gandhi Vidyalayas in the country as on date, State-wise;(b) whether there is uniformity in allotting/sanctioning of said Vidyalayas and if so, the detailsthereof; and(c) whether the Government proposes to sanction at least one Kasturba Gandhi Vidyalaya forevery zilla panchayat of Chikkodi district of Karnataka State and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a): State/UT-wise details of Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (KGBVs) in the country is atAnnexure.(b): At the time of inception, KGBVs were set up in Educationally Backward Blocks (EBBs) of aState/UT where the female rural literacy rate was below the national average (i.e. below 46.13% asper Census 2001) and gender gap in literacy was more than the national average of 21.67%. Thescope of the scheme was enlarged to cover the EBBs that had rural female literacy below30% and towns/cities having minority concentration with female literacy rate below the nationalaverage of 53.67% as per Census 2001. Now, the scope has been extended to cover all the EBBswith rural female literacy below the national average 46.13% as per Census 2001.(c) : A total of 145 KGBVs have been sanctioned in Karnataka State out of which 141 KGBVsare operational. KGBVs have been sanctioned in all EBBs in Karnataka. Out of 141 operationalKGBVs, 4 KGBVs are located in Chikkodi district of Karnataka State, details of which are asunder:-<pre>Block/ Municipal Area, whereKGBV Sanctioned & Operational Place of KGBV Type ofKGBVGokok Waderhatti I *Raibag Byakud IGokok Waderahatti IV **Raibag Mekhali IV* From classes VI to VIII.** Stand-alone Girls’ Hostels for classes IX to XII.</pre>****
