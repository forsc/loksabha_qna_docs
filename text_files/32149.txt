                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Habeas Corpus Pleas




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Habeas Corpus pleas continued to be pending in the Supreme Court and High Courts, till date;(b)	whether the number ofpending Habeas Corpus pleas has increased over the years;(c)	if so, the reasons therefor;(d)	the number of cases filed under Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act (UAPA) in last three years along with the number of total Habeas Corpus pleas made by the UAPA accused, the number of such pleas rejected, and those pending, till date, State-wise; and(e)	the number of convicted person under the UAPA in last three years, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER

However, State/UT-wise cases registered, cases charge-sheeted, cases convicted, persons arrested, persons charge-sheeted and persons convicted under the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act during 2018-2020 is as per Annexure-II. Latest published data pertains to the year 2020.	 ANNEXURE-ISl.
