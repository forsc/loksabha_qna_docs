                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

411






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Navodaya Vidyalayas




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Navodaya Vidyalayas operational in the country till date;(b) whether the Government is considering to open new Navodaya Vidyalayas and toimprove the standard of the present Navodaya Vidyalayas and if so, the detailsthereof;(c) the amount of budget allocated by the Government for Navodaya Vidyalayas duringthe last three years, year-wise;(d) the provision in regard to repairing the old buildings of Navodaya Vidyalayas and forconstruction of new buildings; and(e) the standard fixed by the Government to look after the students of NavodayaVidyalayas and to provide them with basic facilities?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) As on date, 636 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) are operational in the country.(b) Navodaya Vidyalaya Scheme envisages opening of one Navodaya Vidyalaya in eachdistrict of the country. Opening of new JNVs is a continuous process which depends on thewillingness of the concerned State Government to make available requisite suitable land freeof cost for construction of the permanent building and making available required temporarybuilding, free of rent, to run the Vidyalaya till the permanent building is constructed. However,actual sanction and opening of new JNV depends on availability of funds and approval by theCompetent Authority.Navodaya Vidyalayas are maintaining high standards in the field of academics, sports& National Cadet Corps, extra-curricular activities, social service etc. For furtherimprovement, teaching and non-teaching staff are being imparted training from time to time.Students are provided necessary support to prepare for various competitive examinations.(c) The amount of budget allocated for Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) during the lastthree years is as under:<pre>Year Budget allocated (Rs. in crores)2016-17 2619.572017-18 3185.002018-19 3213.00</pre>(d) The provision in regard to repairing the old buildings of Vidyalayas and forconstruction of new buildings is as under:<pre>(Rs. in crores)Year Funds allocated by NVSfor maintenance &repairs of old JNVbuildingsFunds allocated byNVS for constructionof new buildings2016-17 57.03 482.802017-18 84.89 483.132018-19 141.25 232.39(e) Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) has fixed norms for providing basic facilities to thestudents of JNVs. Facilities like residential infrastructure, hygienic food, bedding, uniforms,text books, daily use toilet items, medical care, stationery, travel expenses, etc. are providedto the students free of cost.*****
