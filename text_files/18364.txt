                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2150






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Uranium Exploration in Amrabad Tiger Reserve




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has given in-principle approval for exploration of uranium in Telangana’s Amrabad tiger reserve;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government is aware that this decision would adversely affect the flora and fauna of the area including the number of tigers alongwith contamination of the Krishna river, which is the lifeline for Telangana;(d) 	if so, the reaction of the Government thereon; and(e) 	whether the Government is considering to rescind its decision relating to exploration of uranium in Amrabad Tiger Reserve, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) The Government has not given in-principle approval for exploration of uranium in Telangana’s Amrabad Tiger Reserve.(b) to (e) Questions do not arise.*****
