                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

839






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Ahmedabad-Himmatnagar NH




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government/NHAI has received any suggestion from the local Gram Panchayats and other private bodies for the Ahmedabad –Himmatnagar National Highway(NH)-48;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto; (c)	whether the Government has finalised the time duration to complete the work on the said highway; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) No such proposal have been received. However, there is demand for underpasses at different locations by Gram Panchayat and local bodies time to time, which have been considered based on their feasibility. (c) to (d)  Yes Sir, presently there are two ongoing works of  six laning of Ahmedabad-Himmatnagar National Highways covering an aggregate length of 108 km. Both these works where targeted to be completed by January, 2021.*****
