                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4623






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Projects under AMRUT




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of ongoing projects under Atal Mission for Rejevenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) in Chhattisgarh;(b) 	the details of the funds allocated and utilised in Chhattisgarh under the said Mission, district-wise;(c)	whether complaints regarding delay in the projects have been received and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether cost of the said projects have also increased and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether the State Government has made any request for addtional allocation of funds under the said Mission and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                          THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                        (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): 	State of Chhattisgarh has taken up 293 projects worth ?2,270 crore under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), out of which 241 projects worth ?804 crore have been completed and 52 projects worth ?1,470 crore are under implementation. (b):	The approved plan size of State of Chhattisgarh under AMRUT for the entire Mission period is ?2,192.76 crore, which includes committed Central Assistance of ?1,009.74 crore. So far, ?969.12 crore has been released as Central Assistance for project implementation, of which ?841.49 has been utilized.  Additionally, Central Assistance of ?192.15 crore has been released to the State of Chhattisgarh under other components of AMRUT, of which ?189.39 crore has been utilized.  Under AMRUT, Central Assistance is not released district-wise or city-wise or project-wise.(c) & (d):		No complaint regarding delay in the implementation of AMRUT projects in the State of Chhattisgarh has been received so far.  The 52ongoing AMRUT projects of the State of Chhattisgarh are 79% physically complete and are likely to be commissioned by the date of end of the Mission i.e. 31 March 2023. (e):	Government of Chhattisgarh has not made any request for allocation for additional funds under AMRUT scheme.                                           ******
