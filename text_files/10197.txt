                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Mission Organic Development Initiative of Ladakh




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering Mission Organic Development Initiative of Ladakh (MODI) policy passed by Ladakh Autonomous Hill Development Council, Leh and already submitted to the Minister for Agriculture and Farmers Welfare for its approval and funding;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government has any plans to create awareness for already issued soil health cards to the farmers of Ladakh;(e)	if so, the details thereof ;(f)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(g)	whether the Government plans for setting the crop type to Rabi Crops only for the Ladakh region under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	The Special General Council of Ladakh Autonomous Hill Development Council, Leh in its meeting held on 9th March, 2019 approved the policy , strategy and action Plan of “Mission Organic Development Initiative of Ladakh (MODI)”. Government of India has not received any request from Ladakh Autonomous Hill Development Council for financial assistance of MODI.		Two organic clusters (40 ha) were taken up in Ladakh region out of 28 clusters allocated to Jammu & Kashmir in 2015-16 under PKVY.  Government of India approved 10 PKVY-Model Cluster Demonstrations (200 ha) in Leh Ladakh in November, 2019 for promotion of Sea-buckthorn crop in the region to be implemented through Sikkim State Organic Certification Agency (SOCA).Contd…2/--2-(d) to (f):	under Soil Health Card Scheme, 8367 demonstrations and 310 farmer melas have been sanctioned to Jammu and Kashmir including Ladakh during the current financial year 2019-20. Detail of   farmer melas / fair and demonstration conducted for the farmers of Ladakh is as:		(i) Farmers fair/mela    - 25 Nos (for 25 villages)		(ii) Demonstrations      - 409(g):		Ladakh administration is free to opt for crops in either   Kharif / Rabi or for   crops in both seasons as per Operational Guidelines of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana*******
