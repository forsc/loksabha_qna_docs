                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Modernizing Amenities/Facilities at Stations




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government intends to redevelop and modernize amenities and facilities for passengers at the railway stations in Kerala namely, Ezhukone, Mavelikkara, Chengannur, Changanassery and Kuri under Southern Railways; (b) if so, the details thereof along with funds allocated and works in progress/proposed/awaiting sanctions;(c) whether the Government further intends to construct under pass at Chenthara, Mylom, and Kariyara to facilitate ease of traffic and further construct a rail over bridge in lieu of Avaneeswaram level cross to decongest traffic movement and ensure ease of traffic that is likely to aggravate in the event of post covid resumption of train services from Kollam-Sengottai path;(d) if so, the details thereof along with details of any representation received in this regard from Kerala Government and appropriate authorities of Southern Railways; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government thereto? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
