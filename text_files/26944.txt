                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Implementation of BharatNet Project




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has given any approval for the revised implementation strategy of BharatNet Project through Public Private Partnership (PPP) model and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the said mode of implementation of the project will be done in sixteen States of the country and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the estimated maximum viability gap funding approved for the above PPP model and the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) to (c) 	Yes Sir. On 30.06.2021, Union Cabinet accorded approval for a revised strategy for implementation of BharatNet through Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model in 16 States of the country covering about 3.61 lakh villages including GPs.  These States are Kerala, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, West Bengal, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur, Mizoram, Tripura, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Assam and Madhya Pradesh. The total approved maximum cost is Rs. 19,041 crores on Viability Gap Funding (VGF) for implementation of the PPP Model of BharatNet in these 16 States.  ********
