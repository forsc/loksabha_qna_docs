                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3508






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






PMAY in Punjab




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of people (beneficiaries) who have been enlisted under PMAY and PMAY plus in the State of Punjab;(b)	the number of beneficiaries who have received grant under PMAY, PMAY plus in Punjab till date, district-wise; and(c)	the number of beneficiaries who will receive grants under PMAY and PMAYplus in the current year, 2019-20 in Punjab, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): The number of households listed in Permanent Wait List (PWL) of Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) in the State of Punjab is 65,076 as on 09.12.2019. The State Govt. of Punjab was given target of 14,000 houses in Phase I of PMAY-G out of which 13,989 houses have been sanctioned and 13,552 houses have been completed, as on 09.12.2019.After due verification of the remaining households in the PWL, the State of Punjab found only 10,000 eligible households in the PWL. Accordingly, the State has been allocated target of 10,000 houses for the year 2019-20 to saturate the PWL. Out of the target of 10,000 houses, the State Govt. has sanctioned 463 houses as on 09.12.2019. The district-wise number of houses sanctioned from PWL of PMAY-G in the State of Punjab is at Annexure.Details of households, who were eligible but could not find place in the PWL of PMAY-G, were captured though the mobile application Awaas+. The number of households captured in Awaas+ in the State of Punjab is 1,30,698. Financial Assistance to households registered on Awaas+ for construction of houses is dependent on the outcome of the data validation.******
