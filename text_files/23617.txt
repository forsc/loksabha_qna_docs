                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4680






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






New Trains in Karnataka




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the new trains proposed to be introduced/have been introduced by the Government in the State of Karnataka during the last two years; and(b) the details of the number of persons benefited therefrom?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) Indian Railways do not run train services on State- wise basis as Railway network straddles across various State boundaries.  However, 12 pairs of trains have been introduced in South Western Railway, which primarily serves various stations located in the State of Karnataka, during the financial year 2018-19 to 2019-20 on originating/terminating basis.(b) State-wise statistics of number of passengers is not maintained. However, the year-wise details of number of passengers booked on South Western Railway, which primarily serves the State of Karnataka, is as under:Financial Year	          Number of passengers booked (in millions)2018-19	                              193.512019-20	                              185.46*****
