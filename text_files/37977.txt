                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5239






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






School of Excellence




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has introduced any project as “School of Excellence” in India; (b) if so, the details of the project and details of the application procedure; (c) the details of the application received by each State; and (d) the details of the expenses given by the Government to each student?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (d):  The Finance Minister in her Budget Speech on 01.02.2021 has announced to qualitatively strengthen more than 15,000 schools from across the country to include all components of the National Education Policy, 2020. The objective is to develop them as exemplar schools in their regions, handholding and mentoring other schools to achieve the ideals of the policy.****
