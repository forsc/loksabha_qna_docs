                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

183






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Telecommunication facilities




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether tele-communication facilities are not available in the backward and remote villages of Gujarat particularly in Bharuch district; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the number of such villages in the State and reasons therefor; and (c) 	the action being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b) 	As per data provided by Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) and field units of Department of Telecommunications as on December 2020, it is estimated that out of 17843 inhabited villages in the state of Gujarat, as per Census-2011, 512 villages don’t have mobile telecommunication connectivity which includes 11 inhabited census villages of Bharuch district. The remaining villages of the country are covered by the Government and Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in a phased manner. The District wise details of villages which don’t have mobile connectivity for the State of Gujarat is given in Annexure-I. (c)	Government has taken various initiatives under Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) to improve mobile telecommunication connectivity in backward and remote areas of the country. Under USOF Scheme for Provisioning of mobile connectivity in 354 uncovered villages, 70 villages in the State of Gujarat have been included.
