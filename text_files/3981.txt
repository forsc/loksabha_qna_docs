                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3680






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Shortage of Anti-Landmine Vehicle




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) and other paramilitary forces are struggling with the shortage of protected anti-landmine vehicles;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of anti-landmine vehicles sanctioned for procurement by the Government through open tender under modernization of police scheme during the last three years to deal with the risk posed by IEDs in the areas affected by left-wing extremism;(c) the number of such vehicles procured during the last three years ascompared to the number of vehicles proposed to be procured; and(d) the steps taken by the Government to meet the shortage of anti landmine vehicles in the CRPF and other para-military forces?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d): The procurement of any equipment, including Mine Protected vehicles (MPVs) is guided by operational requirement and priorities, competing requirements and availability of funds. The requirement of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs)/Assam Rifles (AR)/National Security Guard (NSG) for any item, including Mine Protected Vehicles, changes with time and their deployment pattern. Acquisition of such items is an ongoing process. ******
