                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2438






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Recruitment by RRBs




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) functioning in the country along with the number of vacant posts in Railways at present, zone wise; b)	the number of examinations conducted by RRBs during each of the last five years and the number of candidates selected and recruited;c)	whether RRBs has conducted examination of Non-Technical category recently;d)	 if so, whether the candidates who appeared in the examination have raised some concern after the results were announced;e)	 if so, whether the Government has appointed any committee to address the issues raised by the candidates and if so, the details thereof; andf)	the time by which the issues raised by the candidates are likely to be resolved, tests are likely to be conducted and appointments made in a time bound manner?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (f):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2438 BY DR. SUBHASH RAMRAO BHAMRE, DR. AMOL RAMSING KOLHE, SHRI SUNIL DATTATRAY TATKARE, SHRI HEMANT SRIRAM PATIL, SHRI KULDEEP RAI SHARMA AND DR. DNV SENTHILKUMAR. S. TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 16.03.2022 REGARDING RECRUITMENT BY RRBs(a) & (b): 	The details are appended.(c) to (e):	Yes, Sir. A Committee of officials from Railway Board including Chairpersons of Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) & other Railway Officials has been constituted on 26.01.2022 to redress the concerns raised by candidates. The Committee has given its report which has been accepted.(f):	A notice containing decisions taken by Committee and schedule for declaring revised result for all Pay Levels & of 2nd Stage Computer Based Test (CBT) has been uploaded on official websites of RRBs on 10.03.2022.*****
