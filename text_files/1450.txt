                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1310






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Medical Board for Abortion




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has urged all the States/UTs in the country to set up a permanent medical board to deal with cases where pregnant women and girls seek nod for abortion;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the response received from the States/UTs in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):		As per advice of the Hon’ble Supreme Court, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare issued directive, vide D.O. No. M.12015/58/2017-MCH, to State/UT Governments to constitute Permanent Medical Boards. The board will be dealing with  cases referred to by the Hon’ble District Courts/High Courts/Supreme Court which may require urgent Medical Termination of Pregnancy beyond 20 weeks so that immediate opinion may be given and urgent action may be taken.(c):	As per the reports received from the States/UTs, 29 States/UTs have constituted Permanent Medical Boards. 	It is under process in 4 States/UTs. State/UT wise Status of formation of Permanent Medical Boards is annexed.
