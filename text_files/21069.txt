                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






FDI Inflow to Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has information about the Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) flow to Odisha and if so, the details thereof during the years 2018 and 2019;(b) whether the FDI flow to Odisha is comparable to other neighbouring States like West Bengal and Andhra Pradesh and if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of sectors where FDI is flowing into Odisha and whether there is any study done by theGovernment on the issues relating to FDI climate in Odisha and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a), (b)& (c)	The state wise FDI Equity inflow is maintained w.e.f. October, 2019 onwards. Prior to this, the FDI data was maintained region wise. The statement indicating details of FDI flow to Odisha and its neighbouring states from October, 2019 to November, 2020 including details of sector wise FDI equity inflows in respect of Odisha from October, 2019 to November, 2020 is at Annexure-1.
