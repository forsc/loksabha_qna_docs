                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

834






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






LED Lighting on NHs in Tamil Nadu




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of the accidents and thefts that occur on most of the National Highways (NHs) due to lack of central median lighting and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has received any proposal to provide central median LED lamps on NHs in Tamil Nadu; (c)    if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto; and (d)    the steps taken by the Government to provide central median LED lamps in all NHs in Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)         As per the report on Road Accident 2018 published by the Ministry, the Ministry does not compile data under the category of “accidents and thefts that occur on most of the National Highways due to lack of central median lighting”.(b)  to  (d)    As per information received from National Highway Authority of India (NHAI), the details on the proposals received in Regional Office of NHAI for providing LED lighting along highways are as under:-  (i).  Tambaram – Tindivanam section of NH-45: VIP reference received from Shri S.Rajendran, Hon’ble MP, Viluppuram Constituency, proposal of Rs.3.78 Crore towards lighting has been prepared by field unit of NHAI.(ii).    Ennore-Manali Road Improvement Project (EMRIP) : Greater Chennai Corporation requested for illumination of Ennore Expressway Phase-II from Km 1/600 to Km 7/537, proposal of Rs.4.40 Crore has been prepared by field unit of NHAI.(iii)    Chennai Bypass – Based on request of Police Department, the proposal for illumination of entire stretch of Chennai Bypass for an amount of Rs. 20.90 Crore has been prepared by field unit of NHAI.(iv)      Maduravoyal – Sriperumbudur section of NH-4: A request for lighting on this stretch has been received from Dy. Commissioner of Police.*****
