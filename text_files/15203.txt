                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Rural Road Works in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of road works completed in Jharkhand especially in Giridih, Bokaro and Dhanbad districts under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b) 	the pending cases of road works in Jharkhand under this scheme especially in Giridih, Bokaro and Dhanbad districts; and(c) 	the estimated time frame for completion of these road works?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c): Since inception till 12th March 2020, a total of 7,604 road works measuring a length of 28,694.73 km has been sanctioned to the State of Jharkhand under various interventions/verticals of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), out of which 6,404 road works measuring a length of 23,835.75 km have been completed and 1,200 road works of length 4,256.25 km are at various stages of completion.
