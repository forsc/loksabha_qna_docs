                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5189






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Mobile Network in Ladakh Region




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any plan to improve the existing mobile networks in Ladakh region and for installation of new Mobile Towers in the far-flung villages and hamlets of the said region; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the Ministry has sanctioned/released any funds for augmentation of mobile/telephone network in the aforesaid region out of Universal Service Obligation Fund in the last five years; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	Yes Sir. (b)	Total 125 number of Mobile Towers in Ladakh region (Kargil-72 & Leh-53) are provisioned under the USOF Scheme for providing connectivity to the 361 villages of uncovered areas of Ladakh & Kargil (J&K region), Border area and other priority areas.(c)	& (d): The details of funds sanctioned/released for augmentation of mobile/ telephone network in Ladakh region in the last five years from Universal Service Obligation Fund is given below:<pre>Funds sanctioned/released in Ladakh during last five years from USO FundSl. No.	Financial Year	Amount in Rs.(VPTs/ DSPTs)1.		2014-15	Nil2.		2015-16	Nil3.		2016-17	9,14,5004.		2017-18	1,34,1255.		2018-19	1,54,654</pre>
