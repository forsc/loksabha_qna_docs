                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2451






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Pension Schemes for Widow, Elderly, Unemployed BPL People




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has implemented any pension scheme for the widows, elderly and unemployed persons living Below Poverty Line (BPL), if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of the funds allocated, sanctioned and utilised under the scheme during the last three years, State/UT-wise/area-wise;(c) 	whether the Government has proposed to double the amount allocated under the scheme and if so, the time by which it is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)    Under the National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP), monthly pension is being paid at the rate of Rs.300/- per month  to widows in the age group of 40 to 79 years and Rs.200/- per month to old age persons in the age group of 60 to 79 years, all belonging to below poverty line households. The amount of pension is increased to Rs. 500/- per month in respect of these beneficiaries on attaining the age of 80 years and above.  There is no provision of pension to unemployed persons living below poverty line.    (b)    The State/UT-wise details of funds allocated, sanctioned and utilized under the scheme during the last three years is given at Annexure.(c)      No, Sir. There is no such proposal for consideration to double the amount under NSAP.  *****
