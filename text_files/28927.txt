                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

307






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






MGNREGS in Maharashtra




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of funds allocated by the Union Government under the MNREGS during last three years year-wise and State-wise;(b) 	the number of the persons benefitted of the scheme in the country specially in the Ahmadnagar district and Shirdi parliamentary constituency of the State of Maharashtra;(c) 	whether the funds allocated under this scheme have been utilized timely; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a), (c) & (d):      Mahatma Gandhi NREGA is a demand driven wage employment programme and funds are released to the States/UTs on the basis of agreed to Labour Budget and performance of the States during the financial year and there is no provision of State/UT-wise allocation of funds.  State/UT wise release of funds for wage, material and administrative component in the last three financial years under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is given at Annexure.
