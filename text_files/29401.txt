                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1094






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






AIIMS in West Bengal




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether new AIIMS  has  been  established  in West Bengal during the last three years;(b)	whether the Government is planning to establish new AIIMS in West Bengal, especially in Malda Uttar Parliamentary Constituency; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c) Setting up of a new AIIMS at Kalyani in West Bengal was approved by the union Cabinet in October 2015 at a cost of Rs.1754 crore. Construction work of this AIIMS has been substantially completed. MBBS classes of AIIMS Kalyani have commenced from the academic year 2019-20 and OPD services have been operationalized since 27.1.2021.The Government has plans to set up AIIMS in each State in a phased manner under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY).  As stated above, one AIIMS at Kalyani in West Bengal has already been sanctioned.
