                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3526






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Bypass in Jharkhand




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a bypass is proposed to be constructed in Chatra district of Jharkhand; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the funds sanctioned and released for the purpose so far;(d) the time frame set for completing the construction of the said  bypass; and(e) whether any executing agency for the said bypass has been engaged and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) Yes sir, Ministry has sanctioned estimate for Chatra Bypass amounting to Rs. 235.42 Crore.(d)	Work is scheduled to be completed within 2 year after award of the work.(e)	No, sir.*****
