                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Food Processing Units in Andhra Pradesh




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that State Industrial Development Corporation, Government of Andhra Pradesh (AP), with a view to push foodprocessing in the State, has constituted AP Urban Infrastructure Asset Management Ltd, as project consultancy;(b)	whether the Government is also aware that the State Government of Andhra Pradesh is planning to set up, at least, one food processing unit, incubation centre, food testing laboratory, skill development centre in every Parliamentary Constituency; and(c)	if so, whether the Government is going to help the State to push in food processing in      AP and export the same to earn foreign exchange for the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) & (b): Yes Sir, As conveyed by Andhra Pradesh Food Processing Society (APFPS), Industries & Commerce Department, Government of Andhra Pradesh, it has appointed AP Urban Infrastructure Asset Management Ltd as Project management Consultancy for the project of establishing secondary food processing units across all the Parliamentary Constituencies. APFPS has plans to set up one incubation centers and one skill development center in each district through centrally sponsored  PM Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme administered by Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) and one Food Testing Laboratory in each district through MoFPI and other Government schemes. (c):	MoFPI supports all the state Governments, including Andhra Pradesh, in their endeavor to develop Food Processing sector through Central Sector Umbrella Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAPADA Yojana (PMKSY) and Centrally Sponsored PMFME scheme.  A total of 3 Mega Food Parks, 28 Cold Chain Projects, 1 Agro Processing Cluster, 4 Food Processing Units, 1 Operation Greens project and 4 Food Testing Laboratories have already been approved for assistance under component schemes of PMKSY in Andhra Pradesh.*****
