                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Work Demand




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the difference between work demanded and work provided in each State;(b)	whether all these families have been provided with unemployment allowances;(c)	the details of the current status of delayed payments, compensation and unemployment allowance provided in each State; (d)	the details of the drought-proofing and water conservation works that have been undertaken in each State; and(e)	the number of drought-proofing and water conservation works that have been completed out of the total number of such works undertaken?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As per the Management Information System (MIS), State/UT-wise details of employment demanded by households and employment offered to households during the FY 2018-19 under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) is given at Annexure-I.(b) &(c):	As per Management Information System (MIS), State/UT-wise details of delay compensation paid and unemployment allowance in the FY 2018-19 under MGNREGS is given at Annexure-II.(d) & (e):	As per Management Information System (MIS), State/UT-wise details drought proofing and water conservation & water harvesting works undertaken under MGNREGS in the FY  2018-19 is given at Annexure-III.*****
