                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3023






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Toll Gates in Tamil Nadu




Ganesan Selvam














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the total number of toll gates on the National Highways in Tamil Nadu (TN);(b)  whether  the  revenue  generated through  each  such  toll  gate  in  TN  has increased during each 	of the last three years and the current year; (c)   if  so,  the  details  thereof,  toll gate-wise;(d) whether the toll gates established by the State Governments are notified by the Union 	Government and if so, the details thereof;(e)  whether  the Union Government has fixed any time limit for the entry and exit at toll gates in 	Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof; and(f)  the measures being taken by the Union Government to ensure that all toll roads are fit for smooth 	travel?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The total number of user fee plazas operational on National Highways in the state of Tamil Nadu are 52. (b) &(c) Yes, Sir.  The revenue generated through fee plazas under Public Funded Projects in Tamil Nadu has increased during each of the last three years. The details are at Annexure-A.(d) Yes, Sir. In cases where a particular stretch of National Highway is entrusted to a State Government/ Union Territory (UT), the executing agency of the concerned State Government/ UT complies with the notification issued by the Central Government.(e) At present, as per Rule 9(2) of the National Highways Fee (Determination of Rates & Collection) Rules, 2008, user fee at the rate of one & half times of the fee applicable for one way journey is payable for two one way journey through a fee plaza within a period of 24 hours from the time of payment.(f) All National Highways are being maintained in traffic worthy condition through Operation & Maintenance (O&M) contractor in Public Funded projects and by Build Operate & Transfer (BOT) / Operation Maintenance & Transfer (OMT) Concessionaire in Public Private Partnership (PPP) projects.
