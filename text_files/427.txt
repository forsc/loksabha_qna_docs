                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

345






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Bonded Labour




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the estimated number of bonded labourers in the country at present, State/ UT-wise;  (b)the initiatives taken for eradication of bonded labourers in the country and success achieved as a result thereof, State/ UT-wise; (c)whether the Government has set up rehabilitation centres for the freed bonded labourers and if so, the details thereof along with the number of rehabilitation centres set up in each State; and (d)the total amount of funds released and utilised for this purpose during each of the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	The requisite information is annexed as Annexure(c):		It is under jurisdiction of the State Government.(d):		Funds released to State Government for rehabilitation of Bonded Labour:-(Rs. in lakh)2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19221.60	260.70	664.50	253.50*                                                      *******
