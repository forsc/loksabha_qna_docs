                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Devendrappa Y
                                                








                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    Rajan Baburao  Vichare
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                








                                                    A. Chellakumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the aims and objective of launching Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Man-dhan (PM-SYM) scheme; (b)whether the Government has achieved the objective for which PM-SYM was launched and if so, the details thereof and the total number of unorganized workers enrolled so far under PM-SYM scheme in the State of Karnataka and Tamil Nadu;(c)the amount allocated for this scheme during each of the last three years and amount utilized and reasons for very low utlization of allocated funds;(d)the other steps taken by the Government to enroll more and more unorganized workers under the scheme;(e)the expediture and implementation details of the PM-SYM so far; and (f)the category-wise number of people who are eligible to avail benefits under the PM-SYM Yojana in Maharashtra and the number of the workers who have got themselves registered to avail benefits under this scheme in Maharashtra ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	The primary objective of Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan (PM-SYM) pension scheme is   to provide old-age   protection   to       eligible Contd..2/-:: 2 ::unorganized workers, in terms of monthly pension. The workers of unorganized sector in the age group of 18-40 years having monthly income not more than Rs. 15000/- are eligible for enrolment under this scheme. Under the Scheme,  minimum assured monthly pension of Rs. 3000/- will be provided to the unorganized workers after attaining the age of 60 years. As on 01.12.2021, total 45,78,524 unorganized workers have been registered under the PM-SYM pension scheme. In Karnataka and Tamil Nadu, 1,08,039 and 57,764 workers respectively  have enrolled themselves under the  pension scheme. (c) to (e):	The amount allocated for this scheme during each of the last three years and amount utilized  are as under;Year	Fund allocated (Rs. In crore)	Expenditure (Rs. In crore)2018-19	50	49.492019-20	408	359.952020-21	330	319.71All the State/UT Governments have been requested to popularize the scheme and mobilize the target groups/eligible workers for  enrolment under the PM-SYM scheme. Most of  the States/UT Governments have extended the support in implementation of PM-SYM Scheme and taken effective steps to mobilize the unorganised workers. The Central Government has also taken several initiatives  to  increase the enrolment under PM-SYM Scheme  such as periodic review of the progress of the scheme with States/UTs,  publicity of the scheme through IEC activities, constitution of State Level Monitoring Committees (SLMC) and District Level Implementation Committees (DLIC) to ensure  implementation of the PM-SYM Scheme and mobilization of beneficiaries through State Governments. (f):		As on 01.12.2021, total 5,90,666  workers have registered  themselves  under this scheme in Maharashtra.  The category wise details of enrolment under PM-SYM Scheme in the State of Maharashtra are as under;State Name	Category	Total	General 	OBC	SC	ST	Maharashtra	201755	244632	78488	65791	5,90,666*******
