                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether as per the data on the web portal of e-SHRAM, 22.83 Crore workers from organized sectors are registered under the e-SHRAM portal;(b)the details of access to social security schemes by the registered workers;(c)the occupation, State and caste-wise disaggregated information on provision of pension schemes for registered workers;(d)the occupation, State, caste and gender-wise disaggregated information on enrolment under Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY); and(e)the occupation, State, caste and gender-wise disaggregated information on enrolment of workers registered under Ayushman Bharat programme of Government of India?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (e):	As on 22.03.2022, more than 26.87 crore unorganised workers have been registered on e-SHRAM portal. All registered workers are entitled to get benefit of an accidental insurance cover of Rs. 2.0 Lakh for a year free of cost through Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY). The registered workers on e-SHRAM are also facilitated to get enrolled in Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Mandhan    (PM-SYM) pension scheme. The data on the number of unorganised workers who have enrolled themselves under Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)/Ayushman Bharat is not maintained on e-SHRAM portal.******
