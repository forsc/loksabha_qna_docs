                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

622






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Powers and Jurisdiction of ISRMB




Midhun Reddy













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any progress in defining the powers and jurisdictions of Inter-State River Management Boards (ISRMBs) like the Krishna and Godavari; and (b) if so, the details thereof and the prospective timeline for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI AND TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) & (b)  In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section(1) of section 87 of the Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act, 2014, and in pursuance of the decisions taken in the 2nd meeting of Apex Council held on 06.10.2020, the Central Government has notified the jurisdiction of Krishna River Management Board and Godavari River Management Board on 15th July, 2021.*****
