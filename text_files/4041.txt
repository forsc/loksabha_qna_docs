                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3866






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Increase in Agricultural Production




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the efforts being made by the Government for increasing agricultural production in Jharkhand in view of rising population and declining area of agricultural land in the State; (b) 	the details of schemes implemented/being implemented by the Government for increasing agricultural production in Jharkhand; (c) 	the extent to which the production has increased as a result of the said efforts and through such schemes in Jharkhand; and (d) 	the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (d):  The Government of India has been implementing various schemes/programmes for increasing agricultural production to meet the demands of increasing population in the State of Jharkhand. These schemes are National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), Bringing Green Revolution to Eastern India (BGREI)-a Sub-scheme of RKVY, National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA), Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), Pradhan  Mantri  Krishi Sinchai Yojna (PMKSY), Rainfed Area Development (RAD) etc. Besides, Government has also increased the Minimum Support Price (MSP) for all notified Kharif & Rabi crops including pulses and other commercial crops for the season 2018- 19 atleast 50 percent over cost of production. The agricultural production is dependent of various factors and it varies from year to year while agricultural production increased from year 2016-17 to year 2017-18 and production saw a decrease in the year 2018-19 from the year 2017-18. ******
