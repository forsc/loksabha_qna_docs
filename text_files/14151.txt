                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2954






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Setting Up Industrial Corridors




Sanjay Kumar Bandi













                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Government for setting up Hyderabad-Warangal and Hyderabad-Nagpur Industrial Corridors;(b) 	whether the Government has conducted any study regarding their setup;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	the details of the other measures taken/being taken by the Government in the current year for setting up Industrial Corridors in the country? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): 	The Government of Telangana had submitted a proposal for setting up of Hyderabad – Warangal Industrial Corridor and Hyderabad – Nagpur Industrial Corridor. The same was considered by National Industrial Corridor Development & Implementation Trust (NICDIT) and it was decided that Government of Telangana would carry out a feasibility study and identify available land for this project. Subsequently, the Government of Telangana submitted final feasibility reports for the above stated industrial corridors in October, 2019. To consider these feasibility reports, recently, a meeting has been held in January, 2020 between the officials of National  Industrial Corridor Development Corporation Ltd. (NICDC Ltd.) and Telangana State Industrial Infrastructure Corporation Ltd. (TSIIC) wherein it has been decided that TSIIC would submit a revised proposal in accordance with the institutional & financial structure as approved by the Government of India for development of industrial corridor projects.(d): 	Besides progress of ongoing works related to creation of trunk infrastructures at various nodes of Delhi Mumbai industrial Corridor (DMIC); there is sunstantial progress in work related to two nodes at Tumakuru (Karnataka) and Krishnapatanam (Andhra Pradesh) in Chennai Bengaluru Industrial corridor (CBIC). Also at Ponneri node       (Tamil Nadu) under CBIC, there is substantial  progress in completion of various formalities as per approved Institutional and Financial Structure for Industrial Corridor Projects.  In addition to the above projects, there is some progress in documentation work relating to development as Industrial Corridors in Telangana and Odisha. *********
