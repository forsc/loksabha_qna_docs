                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

849






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Appraisal of Projects under PMAY(U)




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik













                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Central Sanctioning Monitoring Committee (CSMC) in its recently concluded 48th meeting under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) has approved/sanctioned 371 proposals from participating States/UTs for the construction of 231532 houses for the urban poor recently and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the total funds sanctioned/approved including the ratio of shares of States and the Union Government thereto;(c)	the total number of States/UTs participated in the said meeting and the number of houses sanctioned/ approved for these States/UTs, State/UT-wise; (d)	whether the Union Government proposes to construct the houses under the said scheme by using the new and innovative technologies to make them eco-friendly and earthquake resistant by using materials like bamboo, thatch and grass etc. and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of milestone achieved by the PMAY(U) Mission as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) { PMAY(U) } in its 48th meeting held on 30.10.2019  approved 371 project proposals from six States and one Union Territory for construction of 2,31,532 houses for the urban poor. These houses involve an investment of Rs. 7321.84 crore which includes central assistance of Rs. 3472.99 crore, State share of Rs. 1618.07 crore and beneficiary contribution of Rs. 2230.78 crore. State/UT – wise details of these projects are at Annexure.(d). Yes, the Ministry is facilitating and encouraging the adoption of new, innovative and green technologies and building materials for faster and quality construction of houses under PMAY (U) Mission as part of its Technology Sub-Mission. Presently, about 14 lakh houses are in various stages of construction by various States/UTs using new & innovative technologies. The use of new and innovative technologies includes technology for structural andnon-structural uses of bamboo as suited in specific geo-climatic conditions. All the houses designed and constructed using new and innovative technologies are compliant with National Building Code/ Indian Standards. (e). Under PMAY (U) Mission , a total of  19,471 projects have been sanctioned so far for construction of 93,00,949 houses involving central assistance of Rs. 1,45,949  crore of which an amount of Rs. 57,896 crore has been released. Of the sanctioned houses, 55, 40,801 have been grounded for construction of which 28, 06,465 have been completed.                                     ******
