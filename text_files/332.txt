                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

378






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Question Papers Leak




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut













                                                    Kanakmal Katara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the incidents of leaking of question papers, copying and cancellation ofthe examinations raises serious question mark on the education system in thecountry and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has conducted any inquiry in this regard and if so, theoutcome thereof;(c) the details of such people/racket busted in the country during the last three yearswhich have dodged the students by deceiving or enticing them to get passed inexams, State/UT and year-wise; and(d) the action taken/being taken by the Government against such people includingthe efforts being made by the Government to check the recurrence of suchincidents?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The Central Board of Secondary Education has informed that the Board hascontinuously been taking several measures to conduct examination in a smooth & fairmanner. However, as per media reports, there are unscrupulous elements/ groups whoare involved in unfair practices including leaking of question papers, cheating, hackingof online system in both pen and paper as well as in online examinations. It has alsobeen observed that Social Media platform such as Youtube and Whatsapp is usedextensively for using unfair practices.(b) & (c): In the year, 2018 examination in the subject of Economics had to becancelled for Class XII due to leakage and re-examination was conducted by the CBSE.Similarly, Class X Maths paper had also leaked in the same year, but it was decided notto hold a re-test, as the alleged leak seemed very restricted in spread. Further, thestatistical analysis of trends of evaluation in Class X Mathematics on randomly basisshowed no unusual gains in respect of examinees in relation to other subjects.CBSE through Regional Director, Delhi Region lodged six complaints with DelhiPolice to investigate the matter and find out the culprits who compromised the sanctityof examinations and the credibility of the Board. The Delhi Police formed a SpecialInvestigation Team (SIT) to investigate the matter. The Delhi Police has informed theBoard that a Centre Superintendent of an exam centre in Una, Himachal Pradesh, histwo accomplices as well as the Bank Officer of the Custodian Bank were arrested forleaking the class X Maths and class XII Economics paper. Similarly, the Principal andtwo teachers of Mother Khazani Convent School in Delhi along with the owner of Easycoaching Institute were arrested in Delhi for leaking the class X Maths paper. The CBSEhas disaffiliated the schools concerned for compromising with the sanctity of theexamination.(d): The following measures have been taken by CBSE:? Board has updated its system based on the recommendations of High PowerCommittee to make it more robust.? CBSE has taken the help of ICT.? Centre Superintendent are appointed from other schools at the self-centre.? Dy. Centre Superintendent are appointed at each centre irrespective of numberof candidates.? Observers are appointed at each centre.? Board’s officials are also deputed in sensitive cities and centres.? No communicative devices were allowed inside examination centre.? Last entry to the Examination Centre was made by 10.00 A.M only.? Candidates were instructed to appear in school uniform to appear inexaminations.? Action against Social Media.***
