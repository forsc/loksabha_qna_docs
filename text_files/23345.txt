                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4428






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Protection of Crop




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is taking or proposes to take any concrete step for protection of crop by giving them subsidy in collaboration with the State Governments for erecting barbed wire/fence/boundary wall around the farms to protect ready crops from wild animals, Nilgai and other animals in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise/district-wise;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????             (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c)	Ministry of Environment Forest & Climate Change provides the financial assistance to the States under Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Integrated Development of Wildlife Habitats, “Project Tiger” and “Project Elephant” for management of Wildlife habitats in the country by erecting physical barriers, such as, barbed wire fence, solar powered electric fence, bio-fencing using cactus boundary walls, etc. to prevent the entry of wild animals into crop fields. Under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana – Remunerative Approaches for Agriculture and Allied Sectors Rejuvenation (RKVY-RAFTAAR), States can take up any agricultural activity under the scheme, including fencing of agriculture land, with the approval of State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) of the state. The steps taken for protection of Crops in various states are at Annexure-I.
