                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

756






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Filling the Post of Vice Chairman in RGNIYD




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the Government has any proposal to expedite the process of filling up the post of Vice Chairman in Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD);b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;c) whether the Government is facing any administrative and bottleneck issues with regard to its activities as deemed University;d) if so, the details and present status thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard; ande) the steps taken by the Government to provide more financial support for the development of RGNIYD?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a)&(b): As per Section 12(2)(b) of the RGNIYD Act, Vice-Chairperson is nominated by the Visitor(i.e. President of India). A number of applications including direct applications were received by Ministry over time but none of the candidates were found suitable for appointment to the post of Vice-Chairperson. Filling up of the post is under consideration.(c)&(d): No, Sir. RGNIYD is an Institute of National Importance and is not facing any major administrative issues.(e): RGNIYD is getting budgetary support from Government of India in the form of Grant-in-Aid taking into account actual requirement of RGNIYD.******
