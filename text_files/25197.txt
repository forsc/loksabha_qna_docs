                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1030






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Multidisciplinary Education and Research Improvement in Technical Education




Shiromani Ram














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the time by when Multidisciplinary Education and Research Improvement in TechnicalEducation (MERITE) Project is likely to be launched along with the time for which thisproject is likely to be in force;(b) whether the Government has the proposal regarding the gap which has come in the newproject after the Third Phase of Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme(TEQIP-IIT);(c) whether the Government has any remedial plan for the employment of persons who areassociated with TEQIP-III in this adverse situation; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme (TEQIP) phase-lll, initiatedby the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India with assistance from the World Bank to improve thequality of engineering education since 1s April 2017, is going to conclude on 30% September 2021.A token amount of Rs. 10 crore has been provided for “Multidisciplinary Education ResearchInnovation in Technical Education (MERITE)” in the recent budget 2021-22 to start preparatoryworks.The Government of India has been making continuous efforts to ensure that the focus StateGovernments prepare a sustainability plan for the Technical Education Quality ImprovementProgramme (TEQIP-III), so as to avoid any academic standstill beyond the project period i.e 30thSeptember 2021. Ministry of Education has written to the Chief Secretaries of Focus Statesrequesting to plan for continuation of activities undertaken during TEQIP- III, for maintaining thequality of technical education.*****
