                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2941






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Reclamation Plan and NLC Limited




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details for closing TPS 1 in NLC India Ltd.; (b) 	the details of the reclamation plan likely to be implemented therein; and(c) 	the number of trees that are likely to be planted at the reclaimed site?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES  (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a): Details for closing TPS 1 in NLC India Ltd is as follows:1. 	Unit-7 (1x100 MW) has already been decommissioned on 22.09.2018.2. 	Balance units of TPS-I will be decommissioned between April 2020 and Sep.’2020.(b) & (c): Reclamation Plan and tree plantation plan of NLC India Ltd. is given below:NLCIL Reclamation Plan -2019-20 & 2020-21Year	Reclamation (in Ha)	Trees plantation(in Nos.)	Target	Actual till(Feb. 2020)	Target	Actual  till(Feb. 2020)2019-20 (As per approved Mine closure plan)	156.50	207.49	47,000	48,6672020-21(As per approved Mine closure plan)	186.50	-	45,000	-***
