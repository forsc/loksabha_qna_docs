                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3876






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






State Contribution under MGNREGS




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the wages paid to the workers under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in different States;(b) 	whether States, in addition to the Central funds are also contributing from its funds towards the wages paid to the workers under MGNREGS; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof and the quantum of funds contributed towards it?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)   State/UT-wise details of fund released for wage component under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) in current financial year 2021-22 (as on 16.12.2021) is given at Annexure.   (b) & (c)  Central Government provides 100% wage payment as per the notified wage rate applicable to the beneficiaries under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. However, State may also pay wages over and above the notified wage rate.  Currently, State of Jharkhand and Odisha are making payment over and above the notified wage rate in some areas of the State.  *****
