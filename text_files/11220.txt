                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Vacant Posts of Central Universities




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that thousands of posts are lying vacant at Central Universities across thecountry;(b) if so, the details of number of vacant teachers and other staff post, university-wise;(c) the time by which the Government proposes to fill these vacant posts;(d) whether the Government has any proposal to absorb already working ad hoc/temporary/contract/guest faculty as permanent teacher; and(e) if so, the details thereon and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) In recent days, 934 vacant posts have been filled up and advertisements for recruitmentto 6706 posts have been issued in the Central Universities under the purview of Ministry ofHuman Resource Development. The details of vacancy of teachers and other staff, universitywisein these Central Universities are enclosed at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.(c) University Grants Commission (UGC) vide its D.O. letter No.F.1-14/2019 (CPP-II) dated4th June, 2019 has prepared the Guidelines for Recruitment of Faculty in Universities, Collegesand Institutions Deemed to be Universities outlining the selection procedure and the time framefor recruitment which has been circulated to all Universities to adhere to the guidelines. TheUniversities have also been requested to take steps to ensure that the vacancies in the Universitiesare filled up at the earliest.Further, UGC vide its letter No.F.1-14/2019 (CPP-II) dated 31st July, 2019, 7th August,2019, 5th September, 2019 and 22nd October, 2019 again requested to ensure that vacantpositions in Universities are filled at the earliest.(d) & (e) The appointment of teachers in Central Universities is done under the rules and byelawsof the concerned Universities.*****
