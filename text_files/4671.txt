                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4116






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Promotion of Sports




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete













                                                    Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has introduced any policy/programme for promotion of sports among rural youth in the country especially in Odisha and if so, the details and the achievements made in this regard;(b) whether infrastructural facilities are not available at national level for promotion of sports activities in the country particularly backward and rural areas due to which athletes of rural areas are unable to nurture their sports;(c) if so, the reasons therefor along with the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard;(d) the financial assistance provided by the Government for promotion of rural sports during the last three years, Statewise; and(e) whether the Government proposes to start special drive for ST students in sports and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c): ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the responsibility of promotion of sports, including creation of sports infrastructure, rests with State/ Union Territory Governments. Central Government supplements the efforts of State/ Union Territory Governments by providing assistance to training facilities, coaching and financial support to sportspersons under various schemes such as Khelo India, schemes ran by Sports Authority of India (SAI) centres and Assistance to NationalSports Federations (NSFs). These schemescover the rural areas in the country including those in the state of Odisha. SAI has implemented the various Sports Promotional Schemes through its 273 sports centres located in various States. Presently, 14236 talented sports persons including 8698 (residential trainees) are being trained in 27 sports disciplines. Further, 1178 sporting talents in Khelo India School Games, 2018 and 2437 sporting talents in Khelo India Youth Games, 2019 have been selected under Khelo India scheme for further training to excel in various National/ International competitions.(d) Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports (MYAS) provides funds to SAI for implementation of schemes for promotion of sports through training centres established by SAI. State and Union Territory (UT) wise funds are not provided to SAI. Information related to allocation of funds under various SAI sports promotional schemes is at Annexure-I. The locations of the SAI centres through which the SAI schemes are implemented is at Annexure-II. Further the funds are released to States/ UTs to bridge the gaps for development of sports infrastructure under Khelo India Scheme. The position of funds provided to various States under Khelo India Scheme during the last three years is at Annexure-III and the details of the projects sanctioned during last three years are at Annexure-IV.(e) Sports Promotional Schemes implemented by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports cater to the entire population of the country including the tribal people.****
