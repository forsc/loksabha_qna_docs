                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1522






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Households covered under Food Security Scheme




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of percentage of households covered under Food Security Scheme in the State of Telangana, district-wise; (b) 	the details of funds allocated and released for the said scheme during the last five years to the State of Telangana, year-wise; (c) 	the details of funds not utilised by the State Government, year-wise; (d) 	whether it is true that the percentage of households covered under the Food Security Act in the State of Telangana is very low as compared to the target fixed; (e) 	if so, the reasons therefor; and (f) 	whether the Government proposes to add more beneficiaries under the said scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):  	The National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) provides for coverage of 60.96% of the rural and 41.14% of the urban population of Telangana, which at Census, 2011 population work out to 191.70 lakh persons.  The Act is being implemented in the State of Telangana w.e.f. October, 2015. Presently, 191.62 lakh beneficiaries are identified for receiving highly subsidized foodgrains under the Act.  A statement showing the details of district-wise households and Food Security Card in Telangana State as  forwarded by the State Government are at Annexure-I...........2/--2-(b):  		Telangana is a Decentralized Procurement (DCP) State. There is no State specific allocated fund under Food Subsidy to DCP States under NFSA. Food Subsidy is released to DCP States on basis of claims submitted by States to this Department.  Under this scheme, the fund released to State Government of Telangana during the last five years is at Annexure-II.          	Central Government also provides Central assistance to States/UTs under NFSA for meeting expenditure on intra-State movement and handling of foodgrains and fair price shop dealers’ margin as per approved norms. A statement indicating the funds released to State Government of Telangana under this scheme is at  Annexure-II.(c): 		As per the State Government of Telangana, the entire funds released by Central Government have been utilized.(d):  		The coverage under NFSA for the State of Telangana is 191.70 lakh persons. Presently, 191.62 lakh beneficiaries under NFSA have been identified by the State Government, which is approx 99.96% of the intended coverage.  Hence, present coverage under NFSA is not low as compared to intended coverage.(e):    	Question does not arise.(f):		There is no such proposal under consideration.**** ANNEXURE - IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1522 FOR ANSWER ON 20.09.2020 IN THE LOK SABHA.Statement showing the details of District wise households and Food Security Card in Telangana State.(As on September, 2020)Sl  NO	Name of the District	Number of households, as per census 2011	Cards issued under NFSA	Percentage1	Adilabad	156683	125446	80.062	Bhadradrikothagudem	279190	147859	52.963	Hyderabad	849051	348359	41.034	Jagityal	253619	190813	75.245	Jangaon	139238	85294	61.266	JayashankarBhupalapalli	111082	61808	55.647	JogulambaGadwal	132261	90798	68.658	Kamareddy	222513	140942	63.349	Karimnagar	258485	148528	57.4610	Khammam	382929	232939	60.8311	KumuramBheemAsifabad	120420	93756	77.8612	Mahabubabad	195889	144660	73.8513	Mahabubnagar	185931	118366	63.6614	Mancherial	206983	101385	48.9815	Medak	168677	116616	69.1416	Medchal	598112	289346	48.3817	Mulugu	78540	49642	63.2118	Nagarkarnool	196261	116924	59.5819	Nalgonda	401728	312431	77.7720	Narayanpet	114577	77804	67.9121	Nirmal	165763	97501	58.8222	Nizamabad	369031	326617	88.5123	Peddapalli	209677	116692	55.6524	RajannaSircilla	138992	106951	76.9525	Rangareddy	559150	380659	68.0826	Sangareddy	329208	319230	96.9727	Siddipet	238049	257899	108.3428	Suryapet	285691	141999	49.7029	Vikarabad	194956	151304	77.6130	Wanaparthy	123544	83501	67.5931	Warangal Rural	187525	133368	71.1232	Warangal Urban	269180	117818	43.7733	YadadriBhuvanagiri	180677	119911	66.37Grand Total	8303612	5347166	64.40****ANNEXURE -
