                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1856






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Irrigation Projects




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State-wise details of the funds allocated for various irrigation projects particularly in backward and rural areas of the Maharashtra during the last three years and the current year; (b) the details of the funds received from the Union Government and utilized by the States along with the funds which remained unutilized, year-wise; and (c) the reasons for non-utilisation of funds?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI  & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c)  The Water Resources Projects are planned, funded, executed and maintained by the State Governments themselves as per their own resources and priority. In order to supplement their efforts, Government of India provides technical and financial assistance to State Governments to encourage sustainable development and efficient management of water resources through various schemes and programmes as per the guidelines of such schemes.During 2016-17, ninety-nine (99) on-going Major/Medium irrigation projects (and 7 phases) in the country (including 26 Projects of Maharashtra) having balance estimated cost of Rs.77,595 cr. (Central Assistance-Rs.31342.50 cr.) under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) - Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP) have been prioritised in consultation with States for completion in phases. Funding mechanism has been approved by the Government for providing Central and State Share under LTIF through NABARD.Details of funds released and expenditure incurred by various States in respect of above projects under PMKSY-AIBP during last three years and current year are at Annexure. Funds are released from time to time after the utilisation certificates are submitted by the State Governments in respect of CA already released.Further, a Special Package for completion of Irrigation Projects to address agrarian distress in Vidarbha, Marathwada and other chronically drought prone areas of Rest of Maharashtra has been approved during 2018-19. Under the Special Package, 8 MMI projects and 83 SMI projects with a balance cost of Rs. 13,651.607 crore as on 01.04.2018 are planned to be completed in phases up to 2022-23. The targeted additional potential through these projects is 3.77 LHa. CA of Rs.800 crore has been released for these projects upto March, 20 and Rs.313.955 crore has been released during 2020-21 (so far). Expenditure of Rs.3692.231 crore has been reported to be incurred on these projects.*****  ANNEXURE Annexure referred to in the reply to part (a) to (c) of Unstarred Question No 1856 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 22.09.2020 regarding “Irrigation Projects”.
