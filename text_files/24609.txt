                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

42






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Drinking Water Connections in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The number of households in Odisha which have been provided with drinking water connection under Jal Jeevan Mission alongwith the number of people benefited there from, district-wise; and(b)	The details of funds allocated to Odisha under the said mission, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF JAL SHAKTI (SHRI GAJENDRA SINGH  SHEKHAWAT)(a) & (b) A Statement of reply is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in the reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 42 due for reply on 22.07.2021(a) 	As on 15th August, 2019, at the time of launch of Jal Jeevan Mission, in Odisha, out of 85.67 lakh rural households, 3.11 lakh households (3.74%) had piped water connections. In the past 22 months, 23.38 lakh households have been provided tap water connections thus increasing the tap water supply to26.49 lakh households (30.93%). The district-wise details of households which have been provided with tap water connection is at Annex–I.(b) 	The details of Central fund allocated to Odisha under Jal Jeevan Mission in 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22, are as under:Financial Year	Fund allocated	Fund drawn by the State2019-20	364.74	364.742020-21	812.15	609.112021-22	3,323.42	* 830.85					(Amount Rs. in Crore)* as on 19.07.2021Under Jal Jeevan Mission, the ministry does not make district-wise allocation of funds. *****
