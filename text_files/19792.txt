                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Major Civilisational, Cultural and Spiritual Tourist Desinations




Arjunlal Meena













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the Country’s major civilizational, cultural and spiritual tourists’ destinations; (b) 	whether the Government facilitates growth or support tourism on these destinations and if so, the details including the expenditure incurred and estimated benefits thereof; (c) 	whether the Ministry has formulated plans for developing the under construction Shree Ram Janmboomi Temple in Ayodhya as a major cultural and civilizational heritage destination to attract foreign tourists; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Development and Promotion of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government.  The Ministry of Tourism, under its Swadesh Darshan scheme is developing thematic circuits in the country in planned and prioritized manner in order to develop tourism infrastructure at multiple destinations.  As of now fifteen thematic circuits have been identified under this scheme for development namely; North-East, Buddhist, Himalayan, Coastal, Krishna, Desert, Tribal, Eco, Wildlife, Rural, Spiritual, Ramayana, Heritage, Sufi and Tirthankar.  The Ministry of Tourism under its Ramayana Circuit theme has sanctioned a project ‘Development of Ayodhya’ for an amount of Rs.127.21crore in the year 2017-18. The list of projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan scheme is annexed. (c) & (d):	The  Ministry of  Tourism as  part of  its  regular  promotional  exercise  promotes  various  tourism  destinations  and products of the  country  in  domestic  and  international  potential  markets in  a  holistic  manner. These promotions  are  undertaken through  various  communication  platforms  such  as  media  campaigns,  events,  webinars,  road shows,  participation in  fairs  &  festivals, social media platforms  etc. *******
