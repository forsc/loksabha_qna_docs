                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1237






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






MoUs




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has signed MoUs with multilateral bodies or other Governments for Skill Development Programmes in India, and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of measures taken by the Government to improve funding and innovation in Skill Development Programmes; and(c)      whether the Government is planning to set up a National Institute of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) Yes, Sir. In order to improve innovation in skill development programmes, the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship has signed various Memoranda of Understanding/Agreements/Joint Declaration of Intent with Nine (9) countries in the area of skill development and Vocational Education & Training, enhance and sharing of lessons learned in skill development, professional education and training, mutual recognition of qualifications to facilitate mobility of skilled work force, capacity building and development of competence based curricula etc. A Statement indicating the name of countries with which this Ministry has signed various Memoranda of Understanding/Agreements/Joint Declaration of Intent along with areas of collaboration is at Annexure.	Under the ‘Indo-German Programme for Vocational Education and Training (IGVET) signed with Germany, there was a commitment of EUR 3 million which has now been enhanced to EUR 4 million.(c) 	The Joint Declaration of Intent signed between Germany and the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship on the advancement of Dual Vocational Education and Training (VET) and Collaboration on Training of VET Cluster Managers and Indian Skill Development Officers includes provision for establishment of a National Institute of Skill Development (NISD) for conducting world class research, policy evaluation and guidance for improving the Indian Skill eco-system.ANNEXUREAnnexure as referred to in reply to part (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1237 for answer on 25.11.2019 asked by Shri Jayant Sinha regarding “MoUs”.S.No.	Name of the Country	Type of Agreement 	Sectors covered1.	Germany	Joint Memorandum of Understanding	Cooperation in the field of Skill Development and VET, Dual apprenticeships and workplace-based skill development, Capacity Building including Competency based curricula, Training of VET cluster managers etc.		Implementation Agreement	Public and private sectors work together to create conditions conducive to improving cooperative vocational training in India.		Joint Declaration of Intent 	Training of VET Cluster Managers and Indian Skill Development Officers that are directly engaged in the design and implementation of the processes of Dual VET in Clusters		Joint Declaration of Intent 	Cooperation in the field of Skill Development and VET, Dual apprenticeships and workplace-based skill development, Capacity Building including Competency based curricula, Training of VET cluster managers, renewable energy, e-mobility, energy-efficiency etc.		Joint Declaration of Intent.	Skill for sustainable growth 2  	Japan 	Memorandum of Cooperation.	To train future shop floor leaders of Japanese standard level in India with Japanese method of working and to establish Japanese Endowed Course (JEC) with existing engineering colleges in India to train future candidates for middle management engineers in manufacturing sector.		Memorandum of Cooperation 	Technical Intern Training Programme (TITP)3	Switzerland 	Memorandum of Understanding.	Skills development, professional education and training.
