                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1666






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






New Trains in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the new trains proposed to be introduced/have been introduced by the Government in the state of Bihar during the last two years; and (b) the details of the number of persons benefited therefrom?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)  Indian Railways (IR) does not operate train services on State-wise basis as its network straddles across various State boundaries.  At present, all regular passenger train services are cancelled in view of the COVID-19 pandemic.  Based on status of COVID-19 in various parts of the country, some special trains have been introduced.  IR is continuously coordinating with the State Governments and monitoring status of COVID-19 in various parts of the country and additional special trains will be introduced based on request. However, during the last two years (2018-19 & 2019-20), 21 pairs of train services serving the stations located in the State of Bihar on originating/terminating basis have been introduced.  Besides, introduction of trains is an on-going process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources, etc.(b)  State-wise statistics of number of passengers is not maintained. However, the year-wise details of number of passengers booked on East Central Railway which primarily serves the State of Bihar is as under:-Financial Year	Number  of passengers booked (in millions)2018-19	249.532019-20	238.80*****
