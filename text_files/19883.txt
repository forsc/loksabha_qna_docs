                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1128






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






PMKVY in Madhya Pradesh




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      the details of the registered institutes in Madhya Pradesh under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, District-wise; (b)      whether these institutes are recognized by any examining body;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether the certificate issued by these institutes are recognized by the Government/State Government?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	The district-wise number of empanelled training centres (TCs) in the State of Madhya Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2.0 (2016-20) is given at Annexure. (b) and (c)      TCs under PMKVY are being recognized (accredited and affiliated) by National Skill Development Corporation/concerned Sector Skill Council under the aegis of the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. (d)       Yes Sir. Presently, certificates under PMKVY are being issued by Sector Skill Councils (SSCs) which are recognized industry bodies under the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India.
