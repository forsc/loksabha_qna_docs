                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1847






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Hike in Kerosene Price




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government has the data of price hike of kerosene since 2010;(b) if so, the details thereof, year-wise; (c) the reasons of current price hike; (d) whether the Government would increase the quota of kerosene to States especially to Kerala; (e) if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor; (f) Whether the Government would decrease the quota of kerosene to the States; and (g) if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) to (c):	The prices of petroleum products in the country are linked to the price of respective products in the international market. The retail selling price of PDS Kerosene at Delhi/Mumbai since 1st April 2010 is given as Annexure.(d) to (g): 	Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas makes allocation of PDS Kerosene to States/UTs including Kerala on quarterly basis for the purpose of cooking and lighting only. PDS Kerosene allocation of States/UTs is rationalized taking into account the factors such as increase in domestic LPG/PNG connections, electricity coverage, non-lifting of PDS Kerosene quota by the concerned States/UTs etc.
