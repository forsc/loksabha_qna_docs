                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Clean Fuels for Vehicles




Gautam Gambhir














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is promoting clean fuels for vehicles in order to cut down the level of pollution and carbon emission in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and the clean fuel norms approved by the Government;(c) whether the Government has issued any guidelines/norms regarding the clean fuel resources available in the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROELUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Ministry of Road Transport & Highways has informed that, vide Notification No. GSR 889(E) dated 16.09.2016, it has mandated mass emission standard for BS-VI throughout the country with effect from April 1, 2020. Accordingly, fuel quality of Motor Spirit (MS) and High Speed Diesel (HSD) has been revised with respect to sulphur from 50 ppm (BS-IV) to 10 ppm (BS-VI).(c) & (d) The clean fuel resources used in vehicles include domestic and imported Petrol, Diesel & Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) meeting regulatory norms
