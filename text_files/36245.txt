                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3364






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






SCTIMST




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total number of backlog reservation vacancies in Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology (SCTIMST) Group-A category for individual posts/grade;(b)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to fill the backlog vacancies in Group-A category;(c)	whether SCTIMST obtained any exemption from implementing reservation policies of the Government of India and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Ministry of Higher Education had issued an order to fill up backlog vacancies in the ministry and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether the steps have been taken by the Ministry of Science and Technology/Department of Science and Technology (DST) to fill the backlog vacancies in the Autonomous Institutions under DST and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY & EARTH SCIENCES(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)??????? ?? ???????????? ??? ?????? ??????? ???????? ??  ????? ?????? (???????? ??????)  (??. ???????? ????)(a)	Sir, Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology (SCTIMST) is a Statutory Body under an Act of Parliament. The reservation in Group A category of SCTIMST has been implemented prospectively with effect from 16.08.2021 i.e. the date of publishing of Regulation Amendment with respect to Reservation in Group ‘A ’ category in the Gazette of India. After notification of the above regulation, no direct recruitment for Group ‘A’ posts has been done. Therefore, there is no backlog vacancy exists in such posts.(b)	Does not arise.(c)	No such exemption has been obtained.(d)	No such order is in receipt from Department of Higher Education.(e)	Instructions issued by Department of Personnel & Training in this regard are being followed wherever applicable. *****
