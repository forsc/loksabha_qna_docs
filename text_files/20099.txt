                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Industrial Development Centres




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the existing Industrial Development Centres in the country; (b)	the details of the funds allocated and utilised during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise;      (c)      	whether the Government proposes to set up more Industrial   Development Centres across the country; and (d)	if so, the details thereof location, State and Union Territory-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b):     	The Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade has supported Industrial  Development Centres  through ‘Industrial Infrastructure Upgradation Scheme (IIUS)/Modified IIUS (MIIUS)’. 58 IIUS/MIIUS projects have been sanctioned in the country. Funds allocated and utilized by these Centres during last three years State & UT- wise are annexed (Annexure). (c) & (d):	Setting up of Industrial Development Centres across the country comes under  the purview of the concerned State Government itself. However, Central Government provides support to the States through its schemes/programmes, once the proposal to develop the Industrial Development Centres/Parks/Clusters/Industries in a particular region is received from the concerned State Government.******** ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1586 FOR ANSWER ON 10th  FEBRUARY, 2021.
