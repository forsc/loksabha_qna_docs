                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1656






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Air Safety and Air Pollution Violations




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of air safety and air pollution violations by airlines across the country;(b) whether DGCA through its oversight mechanism ensures that all airlines/airport operators comply with its specific legislations and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether rising number of cases of air safety violations by airlines have led to health problems for citizens during the last three years and if so, the details thereof;(d) the steps taken by the Government to resolve the safety issues; and(e) whether all the airlines have complied to the Government guidelines and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) The Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) has formulated regulations towards engine emissions certifications based on International Civil Aviation Organization''s (ICAO) International Standards and Recommended Practices (SARPs) as contained in ICAO Annex 16 and has issued a Civil Aviation Requirement (CAR) Section 6 - Design Standards and Type Certification  Series C Part I. With regard to air pollution, no violation has been reported to the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). The details of Air Safety violations for last three years are attached as Annexure.(b) To ensure the compliance of its regulations, DGCA periodically carries out  regulatory audits, surveillance and spot checks of airlines/airport operators and maintenance organizations as per the Annual Surveillance Plan. The deficiencies observed are immediately brought to the notice of the concerned operators for taking necessary remedial actions. Action as per regulations is taken against the concerned organization/ individual in case of gross violation of the laid down norms.(c) No, Sir.(d) & (e) Yes Sir, all the airlines are in compliance with DGCA regulations. The various measures taken by DGCA to ensure compliance include:;i) Implementation of recommendations emanating from investigation of aircraft accidents and hazardous incidents.ii) Issue of Civil Aviation Requirements / Air Safety Circular.iii) Surveillance by flight inspectors.iv) Regulatory audit of operators.v) Periodic spot checks.vi) Special operating precautions in poor weather conditions.vii) Prevention of bird strike incidents.viii) Action against defaulters.
