                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2647






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Plug and Play Model




Rekha Arun Verma













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of projects set up in Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra under the ‘plug and play model‘;(b) 	the total foreign investment for the year 2020 till now; and(c) 	the details of investment in the ‘plug and play model‘ in Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra during the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b): 	Government has approved development of 11 Industrial Corridors with 32 Projects to be developed in 04 phases in the country. Under Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC) Shendra – Bidkin Industrial Area(SBIA) in Maharashtra with an investment of Rs. 3000 Crore and Integrated Industrial Township (IIT) project at Greater Noida in Uttar Pradesh with an investment of Rs. 617.20 crore by GoI/NICDIT as equity are being development under “plug and play model”. Further, private sector investment for these projects during the last three years is given as under : -<pre>States	Name of the Project	Investment in last three years  (Rs. In crore)Uttar Pradesh	Integrated Industrial Township - Greater Noida	3710 CroreMaharashtra	Shendra Industrial Area	2801 Crore</pre>(c): 	Total foreign investment in Maharashtra for the year 2020 (till now) is Rs. 1118.75 Crore.*******
