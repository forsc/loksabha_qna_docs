                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3753






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Compensation to Land Owners




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of National Highways (NHs) constructed in Odisha during the last three years; (b) whether the land of the private individuals including of tribals was acquired for any of these NHs and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether any compensation in lieu thereof has been given to the land owners and if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the steps taken by the Government to pay compensation to the eligible land owners?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  Up-gradation and improvement of National Highways (NHs) is a continuous process. During the last three years, National Highways of aggregate length of 1250 km have been constructed in the state of Odisha.(b) & (c) Land has been acquired from private individuals including that from tribals for development of National Highways infrastructure. During the last 3 years, Rs. 1603.00 Crore has been paid as land compensation to the affected persons in respect of NH projects in Odisha. (d)  Does not arise.*****
