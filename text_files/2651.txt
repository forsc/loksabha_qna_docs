                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Jobs through Employment Exchange




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of job seekers who registered themselves in employment exchanges and were provided employment or those who received call letters for jobs through Employment Exchanges in the country during the last five years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Jharkhand; and(b)	whether the number of registrations with Employment Exchanges has increased, if so, the details thereof during the said period, State/UT-wise including Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b): As per the information received from the States/UTs, the number of job-seekers, all of whom may not necessarily be unemployed, who got registered themselves in employment exchanges and job-seekers placed through employment exchanges in the country including Jharkhand is given below. The State/UT-wise details are given to the extent available at annexure. Year	Registration(in Lakh)	Placement(in Lakh)2012	97.22	4.282013	59.69	3.492014	59.57	3.392015*	69.39	3.952016*	59.60	4.05*Provisional
