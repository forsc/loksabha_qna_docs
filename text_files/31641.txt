                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3074






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






National Drinking Water Security Pilot Projects




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is implementing National Drinking Water Security Pilot Projects in the country; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise during each of the last three years along with the funds allocated/released/spent so far and the parameters adopted  therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) Since August, 2019, Government of  India in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal after subsuming erstwhile National Rural Drinking Water Supply (NRDWP) to make provision of tap water supply to every rural household of the country, by 2024. The details of budgetary allocation, fund drawn & reported utilization by States/ UTs under erstwhile NRDWP for 2018-19 and under JJM for 2019-20, 2020-21 is annexed.*****
