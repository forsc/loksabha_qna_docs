                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4359






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Impact of Climate Change on Crop Production




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of any study that reveals the impact of climate change on crop production;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the details of crops identified which are less sensitive to climate variations; and (c)	whether the researchers have mapped the relationship between annual variation in temperature and rainfall in the country and if so, the details of thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) & (b)Indian Council of Agriculture Research (ICAR) has conducted studies under National Innovations on Climate Resilient Agriculture (NICRA) project, which indicates variable impacts of climate change on production of certain crops like rice, wheat, maize, sorghum, groundnut, chickpea, soybean and potato production in different regions of India. (c)As per information furnished by the Ministry of Earth Sciences, increase in temperature would result in increase in moisture content in the atmosphere due to more evaporation and can lead to increased rainfall.*****
