                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






TSP/ Post Matric Scholarship




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to restart Tribal Sub Plan (TSP) and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (b)thedetails of State-wise number of tribal students who  were  provided  with  Post  Matric  Scholarships between 2013 and 2020?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) Government is implementing Tribal Sub-Plan (TSP) since 1974-75 as a strategy for tribal development. TSP has now been renamed as Scheduled Tribe Component (STC). Besides, Ministry of Tribal Affairs 40 Central Ministries / Departments have been mandated by NITI Aayog to earmark STC funds in the range of 4.3 to 17.5 percent of their total Scheme allocation every year for tribal development.
