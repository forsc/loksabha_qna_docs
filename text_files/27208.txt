                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2964






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Possibility of New and Renewable Energy Sources




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any survey has been conducted in the country particularly in the State of Bihar regarding the possibility of new and renewable energy sources; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)to(c)The details of estimated renewable energy potential identified for various renewable energy sources in the country including the State of Bihar are given in Annexure-I.*****
