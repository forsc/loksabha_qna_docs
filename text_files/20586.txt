                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1679






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






National Bus Rejuvenation Programme




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government is considering a National Bus Rejuvenation Programme to boost bus based public transport for cities across the country;b)	if so, the details thereof and the time by which the programme will be launched; and c)	Whether the States have been or shall be consulted in the framing of the Programme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                    (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir.  Government is considering to launch a new Scheme, in consultation with all stakeholders including States, to augment bus based public transport in 111 cities through procurement of over 20,000 city buses for 5 lakh plus population cities, including Hilly/ UT/ North East State capital cities with an estimated outlay of Rs.18,000 crores.  Due process for approval has commenced.                                               *****
