                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2577






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Increasing Income of Farmers through Animal Husbandry




Chandan Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has decided to increase the income of farmers in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether there is a need to promote animal husbandry, particularly rearing milk animals for increasing farming income;(d) if so, whether the Government has formulated a plan to construct fodder houses to provide fodder for milk animals, particularly for cows and buffaloes at national level; and(e) if so, the details of the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIER, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN) (a) to (c) In order to complement and supplement the efforts of the States/UTs  for promotion of dairy and animal husbandry sectors thereby increasing the income of farmers, the Government is implementing various schemes to enhance production and productivity of bovines & small ruminants (sheep & goat), Piggery & Poultry namely:I.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission, II.	National Programme for Dairy Development, III.	National Dairy Plan-I,IV.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development SchemeV.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund, VI.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activitiesVII.	National Livestock Mission, VIII.	Livestock Health and Disease ControlContd…(d) & (e) The Government is implementing a Sub Mission on Feed and Fodder Development under a Centrally Sponsored Scheme “National Livestock Mission” since 2014-15, under which financial assistance is provided to the  Animal Husbandry Departments of the States/UTs for feed and fodder development under the following components : a)	Fodder Production from Non-forest wasteland/rangeland/grassland/non-arable landb)	Fodder production from Forest landc)	Fodder Seed Procurement/ Production  & Distributiond)	Introduction of Hand Driven Chaff-Cuttere)	Introduction of Power Driven Chaff-Cutterf)	Distribution of low capacity, tractor mountable Fodder Block Making units, hay baling machines/reapers/forage harvestersg)	Establishment of silage making Unitsh)	Establishment of by-pass protein production unitsi)	Establishment of Area Specific Mineral Mixture / Feed Pelleting/ Feed Manufacturing Unit.j)	Establishment/modernization of  Feed Testing Laboratories***
