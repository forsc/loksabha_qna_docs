                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4496






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Scholarship for SC Students




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4496 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021SCHOLARSHIP FOR SC STUDENTS 4496. MS. DIYA KUMARI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details and the number of Scholarships granted to students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SCs) during the last financial year, State-wise; (b) the total number of scholarships provided to students belonging to Scheduled Castes in Rajasthan during the last five years, category-wise; (c) whether the Government proposes to introduce new scholarships for girl students belonging to the Scheduled Castes; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a):     This Department deals with the following Scholarship Schemes for SC students:-(i) Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC students (PMS-SC); (ii) Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC students studying in Class IX & X;(iii) Top Class Education for SC Students;(iv) National Overseas Scholarship Scheme for SC etc. candidates (NOS).The Post Matric and Pre-Matric Scholarship Schemes are Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented through State Governments and UT Administrations.  The details of beneficiaries, State-wise, during the last financial year under these schemes are attached at Annexure. The Top Class Education for SC students and NOS schemes are Central Sector Schemes and State-wise allocation is not made under them.Total beneficiaries under the Top Class Education for SC students and National Overseas Scholarship schemes during the last financial year were 1375 students and 100 students respectively. (b):  The total number of scholarships provided to students belonging to Scheduled Castes in Rajasthan during the last five years under the Post-Matric Scholarship scheme for SC students and Pre-Matric  Scholarship scheme for SC students are as under:-Year	PMS-SC	Pre-Matric (SC)	Beneficiaries *2015-16	143602	850312016-17	310639	235422017-18	191184	739452018-19	339157	2336512019-20	457915	168095There is no State-wise allocation/release of funds in Top Class Education for SC students and NOS schemes and thus State-wise figures for number of beneficiaries are not maintained.(c) & (d):   No such proposal is under consideration.***** Annexure Statement referred to part (a) in reply to Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 4496 for answer on 23.03.2021 regarding “Scholarship for SC Students”State –wise beneficiaries under Post and Pre Matric Scholarship Scheme during the last financial year (2019-20)S.NO.
