                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4926






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Use of Coir Geo Textiles in Roads




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to use Coir Geo textiles in the construction of roads; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government has received any proposal from the Government of Kerala in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and (d) whether the Government proposes to promote the use of Coir Geo textiles in the construction of roads and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d) Yes sir, Govt. has taken initiative to use Coir Geo textiles in Road construction specially in stabilization of slopes of road embankment. Ministry of Road Transport & Highways issued guidelines to promote use of Coir in road construction and directed implementing agencies to use Coir in road projects in Kerala, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Puducherry & Andhra Pradesh. Pilot projects in Tamil Nadu, Sikkim & Nagaland have already been taken up. No proposal has been received from the Govt. of Kerala in this regard. *****
