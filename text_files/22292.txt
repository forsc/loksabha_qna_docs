                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3352






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Attack on Police




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that there has been a spurt in the incidents of attack on police personnel by miscreants in various parts of the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to check this serious trend in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)  to (c):	‘Police’ & ‘Public Order’ are State subjects under the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India.  The responsibilities to maintain law and order and prevention, detection, investigation and prosecution of crime are with the respective State Governments.  States are competent to take action in this regard under extant laws.  Data on incidents of attack on police personnel by miscreants is not maintained centrally.******
