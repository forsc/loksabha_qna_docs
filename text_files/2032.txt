                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2025






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Use of Labour Intensive Technology




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any new National Manufacturing Policy (NMP) has been declared by the Government;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether any provision has been made by the Government to promote the use of labour intensive technology;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	the number of additional workers targeted for employment as a result of promoting the use of labour intensive technology and the time by which this is likely to be done?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):	The National Manufacturing Policy was notified by the    Government in 2011.(c) to (e):	The policy identifies employment intensive industries like textiles and garments, leather and footwear, gems and jewellery and food processing, etc. as special focus attention sectors. The policy provides special attention for Small Scale Industries as they offer employment opportunities both for self-employment and jobs across diverse geographies.  *******
