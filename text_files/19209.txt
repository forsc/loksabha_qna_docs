                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

51






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






National Highway-516-E




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether National Highway (NH) - 516-E has been declared as Green National Highway Corridor and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the present status of Rehabilitation and Resettlement (R&R) of the project along with the steps taken by the Government to release Rs.210 crores for R&R package;(c)	whether the said corridor is going to be executed in three packages;(d)	if so, the details of each package along with the expenditure involved for each package, length of road covered in each package and proposed acquisition of land, etc.; and (e)	the details of land acquired so far ?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e)  A STATEMENT is laid on the Table of the House.
