                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2022






Establishment of Oxygen Plants




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of oxygen plants set up by the oil and gas companies which have been inaugurated so far; (b) the number of the said plants which have started functioning or are likely to start functioning in Gujarat; and (c) whether the said plants are likely to be set up in the rural and the tribal areas of Gujarat and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                               MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) to (c)  Public Sector Oil Companies have set up 121 Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) Oxygen Plants in the Hospitals in different States of the country including 08 in the State of Gujarat. As of now, there is no proposal under consideration for setting up of PSA Oxygen Plants in the rural and the tribal areas of Gujarat by Oil & Gas Companies. ****
