                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

364






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Rail Land Development Authority




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the revenue earned through auctioning of land by Rail Land Development Authority during the last three years;(b)	the locations where Rail Land Development Authority has identified land for auctioning in the next three years and the estimated value; and(c)	whether any location from Assam features in the list particularly in the districts of Bongaigaon, Barpeta, Nalbari and Chirang and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	Rail Land Development Authority (RLDA) leases the land through open competitive bidding process. The revenue earned by RLDA during last three years is as follows:-Year	       Revenue earned                  ( ` in Cr)2017-18	            42.932018-19	            82.812019-20	           933.27(b)	RLDA has been entrusted with 84 sites, out of which 26 sites have been bid out till now. The remaining sites which have earning potential of approximately `6500 Cr. are identified for bidding during next two to three years.(c)	No, Sir.*****
