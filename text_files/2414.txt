                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2202






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Ongoing Highway Projects




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the ongoing highway projects in the State of Karnataka, the funds allocated, sanctioned and status of implementation of each project;(b)	 whether the Government proposes to take up any more projects related to development of highways in the State of Karnataka and if so, the details thereof; (c)	 whether the Government proposes to convert the National Highway-48 stretch between Bengaluru and Hassan into Total Control Access Expressway and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	 whether the Government proposes to build an expressway between Bengaluru and Chennai and if so, the details thereof and the proposed deadline for the implementation of the project?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) 	The details of the ongoing National Highway projects in the State of Karnataka, the funds allocated, sanctioned and status of implementation of each project are at Annexure – I (b)	The details of National Highway projects in the State of Karnataka planning to commence during current financial year 2019-20 are at Annexure – II. (c)	There is no proposal to convert Bengaluru-Hassan section as access control expressway at present.(d)	Yes sir. The proposed Expressway is greenfield expressway of having length of 285 km. The Project is under DPR stage.  The project will be taken up based on the outcome of the DPR and availability of funds.
