                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4817






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Modernization of Thoothukudi Fishing Harbour




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Tamil Nadu to modernize and upgrade the infrastructure in Thoothukudi fishing harbour, if so, the details thereof; and (b)	 the status and funds allocated along with the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (b) : It has been reported that the National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB) during 2012-13 approved the project of Government of Tamil Nadu for moderization and renovation of Fishing Harbour at Thoothukudi at a total cost of Rs.1205.56 lakh with 100% financial assistance and released the entire approved project cost of Rs. 1205.56 lakhs to the  Government of Tamil Nadu. The Government of Tamil Nadu has reported that the infrastructure works to modernize and upgrade the fishing harbour at Thoothukudi has been completed in all aspects and put to use. In addition, the Government of Tamil Nadu has in the month of May, 2019 submitted a proposal for creation of additional berthing facilities at the Thoothukudi Fishing Harbour at an estimated cost of Rs 1800 lakh for seeking funds under the Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF), which has been sent to the Nodal Implementing Agency i.e. National Fisheries Development Board, Hyderabad for evaluation.*****
