                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

297






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Expansion of Scope of PM-KISAN Scheme




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry has plans to include landless agricultural labourers, sharecroppers and vulnerable agricultural households under the PM-KISAN Schemes, (b)	if so, the details of the steps taken and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c)	whether the Government is considering to increase the quantum of assistance under the said Scheme, if so, the details about the increment including the expected date of its implementation and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????         (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Landholding, irrespective of the size of the land, is the basic eligibility criterion for receiving benefits under the Scheme. Under the PM-KISAN Scheme, financial benefits of Rs. 6000/- annually is being given in three equal installments of Rs. 2000/- each, every four months to all the landholding farmer families, subject to certain exclusion criteria, through direct benefit transfer mode once their correct and verified data is received from respective State/UT Governments on PM-KISAN portal and its subsequent validation through Aadhar/PFMS/Income Tax database. At present, there is no proposal to include landless agricultural labourers and sharecroppers under the ambit of Scheme (c):	As of now, there is no proposal to increase the quantum of assistance given under PM-KISAN Scheme.	 ******
