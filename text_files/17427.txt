                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

904






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Development of MSME Sector




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has provided adequate financial support and facilities to the Karnataka Government for the development of MSME sector in the State;(b) 	if so, the details thereof during the last three years, year-wise; and (c) 	the effective measures  the Government has taken to impart requisite training and support to the entrepreneurs in MSME sector of the state and in  particular to the women entrepreneurs?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):	Ministry of MSME implements various schemes and programmes for the overall growth and development of MSME Sector in the country including in the state of Karnataka. The financial support under these schemes /programmes goes directly to the beneficiary or the designated implementing agencies and not to the state Government. However, the details of the major schemes in the state of Karnataka of the Ministry during the last three years, year-wise are as under:<pre>Name of the Scheme	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Number of projects assisted under PMEGP Scheme	2115	3657	2697Credit Guarantee Approved  	No of Guarantee Approved  	Amount (Rs. in Crore) of Guarantee Approved  	No of Guarantee Approved  	Amount (Rs. in Crore) of Guarantee Approved  	No of Guarantee Approved  	Amount (Rs. in Crore) of Guarantee Approved  	20845	1967.77	27796	2496.55	68572	4068.61Mirco and small enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP) Funds sanctioned / released (Rs. in crore) 	17.05	26.07	5.22</pre>(c):	Ministry of MSME implements the Entrepreneurship and Skill Development Programme across the country including the in state of Karnataka to motivate youth representing different sections of the society including women to promote self employment or entrepreneurship as one of the career options. The ultimate objective is to promote new enterprises, capacity building of existing MSMEs and inculcating entrepreneurial culture in the country.
