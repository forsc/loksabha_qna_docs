                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1448






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Shifting Production from China to India




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of multinational companies of United States and other foreign countries  which shifted their base from China to India during the last one year; (b)    	the details of the foreign investment received so far in the country, State-wise; (c)  	the details and the number of new employment generated /likely to be generated from such multinational companies in the country; and (d)  	the details of the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government for bringing  in more manufacturing industries in the country to boost economy?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):     4 companies in the Electronic System Design Manufacturing (ESDM) sector have diversified their manufacturing base from China to India. However, there are several companies who have indicated their plan/interest to relocate.  (b):      Details of state-wise FDI Equity inflow reported during October, 2019 till November, 2020 are at Annexure A. (c):     Employment for 31,509 people is likely to be generated from the relocation projects at present. (d):    The Government has set up a high level Empowered Group of Secretaries chaired by the Cabinet Secretary, and a Project Development Cell (PDC) in ministries/ departments with a view to provide support and facilitation to investors for investing in India.
