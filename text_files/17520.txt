                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Assessment of Online Classes




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the transition to online mode of teaching of school students has raised concerns over the technical and economic support from the parents at home;(b) if so, the give details;(c) whether the Government has made any assessment to study the impact of online teaching on students and parents; and(d) if so, the details of findings of the assessments?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d) In order to enable online education benefit to the children of every category and teachers, Learning Enhancement Guidelines have been issued.https://www.mhrd.gov.in/sites/upload_filehs/mhrd/files/Learning_Enhancement_0.pdf  	The Learning Enhancement Guidelines have been released on 19 August 2020 during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic. These have been prepared on the basis of the recommendations and reports of States/UTs which are as following:<pre>•	Learning enhancement during COVID-19 for students without digital devices.•	Learning enhancement during COVID-19 for students with limited access to digital devices.•	Learning enhancement during COVID-19 for students with digital devices.</pre>**********
