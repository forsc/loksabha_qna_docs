                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

58






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Post Banks




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the business of Mahila Pradhan Kshetriya Bachat Yojana (MPKBY) and Standardized Agency System (SAS) agents has been diverted/ adversely affected due to formation of Post Banks, thereby decreasing their earnings by way of commission and if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	the remedial steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to ameliorate the distressed conditions of more than five lakh registered agents, mostly women under MPKBY and SAS?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) & (b)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) & (b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *58 FOR 20TH NOVEMBER, 2019  ON “POST BANKS ”.(a) 	No, sir.(b) 	Services under India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) and Authorized Agents (MPKBY/SAS) under Small Savings Schemes are unrelated and independent of each other. No steps are therefore proposed/required to be taken by the Government in respect of the Authorized Agents of Small Savings, post formation of India Post Payments Bank.
