                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2709






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Mobile Network in Arunachal Pradesh




C. Lalrosanga














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to enhance the mobile networks in the eastern most region of Line of Actual Control (LAC) in Arunachal Pradesh and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the private Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) are reluctant to operate in these locations due to lack of economic viability and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to provide mobile network to villagers residing in the aforesaid region and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (d)	The Government through Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) scheme, under Comprehensive Telecom Development Plan for the North-Eastern Region (NER) has planned to provide mobile services in 2215 uncovered villages of Arunachal Pradesh including the villages in eastern most region of Line of Actual Control (LAC). Further, the work of installation of 149 mobile towers along National Highways has also been taken up.As on 31st October, 2019, there are 2623 Base Transceiver Stations (BTS) of private Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) operating in state of Arunachal Pradesh. Private TSPs continue to explore the possibility of expanding coverage to the uncovered areas depending on various techno-commercial considerations.	************
