                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1629






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Opening of Government Degree College




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Francisco Cosme Sardinha
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of districts at present in the country which do not have anyGovernment Degree College;(b) whether the Government proposes to open Government Degree Colleges in allthe districts of the country including Goa and Sivohi district of Rajasthan;(c) if so, the time by when the said plan will likely to be implemented and fundallocated for infrastructure;(d) whether the Government has received any complaint against NGO’s that aftergetting grants/ assistance from the Government they either close or cut short theiracademic programmes; and(e) if so, the State/UT-wise details of such NGOs during the last three years till date?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): As per All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) 2018-19, 14 districts donot have a Government degree college.(b) & (c): Education being in the Concurrent List, creation of new institutions isthe responsibility of both Central and State Government. However, recognising theneed for central assistance, the Central Government has implemented variousschemes supporting State Governments.Under the erstwhile scheme of setting up of Model Degree College (MDC) in 374Educationally Backward Districts (EBDs), University Grants Commission hadapproved assistance to 64 Model Degree Colleges. The Centrally SponsoredScheme of Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA), which subsumed theabove mentioned erstwhile scheme has further approved central support to 130Model Degree Colleges.(d) & (e): Under RUSA, no central grants are released from the Ministry to NonGovernmental Organizations (NGOs). The central grants are released directly to theState Government/UTs.*****
