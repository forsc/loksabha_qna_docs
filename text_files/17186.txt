                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1098






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






AYUSH Colleges




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the number and details of of AYUSH colleges across the country, State-wise, stream-wise and curriculum-wise;(b) whether the Government proposes to establish medical colleges of Ayurvedic curriculum across the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c) whether any criteria have been fixed for establishing new colleges in private sector and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government proposes to promote Ayurveda and streamline/facilitate research of AYUSH medicines and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	The requisite information may please be seen in the statement attached at       Annexure ‘A’(b):	 At present there is no such proposal under consideration of the Central Government.(c):	Under the regulation 13A of Indian Medicine Central Council Act, 1970, person/Trust from private sector can apply to the Central Government for establishing new Ayurved colleges. The details of the Minimum standard requirement of Ayurveda Colleges and attached Hospital may please be seen at the link as follow: https://www.ccimindia.org/rulesandregulations.php (d):		To promote Ayurved as well as other AYUSH systems amongst people within and outside the country, various IEC activities are being taken up with adequate dissemination of information through electronic, press and social media. On 21st of June, every year, International Yoga Day is organized at national and international level. On 17th of October, 2017 ‘Ayurveda Day'' was celebrated. International and national conferences, workshops, stake-holders meets, industry conclave have been organized on regular basis.		Central Government has launched a campaign on AYUSH for immunity to create awareness among the public about preventive and promotive healthcare, based on the principles and practices of AYUSH systems such as healthy food, lifestyle, Yoga, medicinal plants, home remedies for strengthening the natural immune system. 		National AYUSH Mission is the flagship scheme of Ministry under which grants-in-aid is provided to States / UTs to take up various activities to develop and promote Ayurved as well as other AYUSH healthcare facilities.		To streamline/facilitate research of AYUSH medicines, total 05 Research Councils dedicated to different AYUSH systems or healthcare are functioning under Ministry of AYUSH.
