                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2334






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






FCI Granaries Overflowing




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the FCI granaries are overflowing and stock may get damaged, if not, relocated soon; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the procurement of wheat and rice in the central pool is increasing over the years; (d) 	if so, the details thereof and Government’s reaction thereto; and (e) 	the details of foodgrains donated during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a)&(b):   	Total storage capacity of foodgrain, Stock position and Foodgrain stocking Norms in central pool as on 1st day of every quarter  during the year are given below:                                                                                       (Fig. in Lakh MT)As on	Total Storage capacity @	Stock Position	Foodgrain Stocking Norms1st January,  2019	851.54	637.81	214.101st April, 2019	855.68	568.08	210.401st July ,2019	878.55	812.94	411.201st October, 2019	857.58	669.49	307.70@including intermediate storage capacity being used by State government..............2/--2-(c) & (d):  	Procurement of wheat and Rice for central pool during last five year are as below:                                                                               (Fig. in Lakh MT)commodities	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Wheat	280.23	280.88	229.62	308.24	357.95	341.33Rice	320.40	342.18	381.06	381.85	443.31	158.82**As on 18.11.2019(e):   Details of wheat and Rice donated form central pool to Ministry of External Affairs during last five year and current year are as below;                                                                                (Fig. in Thousand MT)commodities	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Wheat	0	0	0	110	0	0Rice	0.5	0.10	0	0.10	2.10	0**Till 19.11.2019****
