                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4237






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






PMGSY in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names and the number of schemes sanctioned under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) (I-III) Phases in Odisha;(b) 	the roads sanctioned under all three phases of the said yojana and their work progress report in each District of Odisha;(c) 	the details of the amount allocated to Odisha under the said scheme; and(d) 	whether various such schemes are pending for years due to paucity of funds and if so, the time by which the said schemes are targeted to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)& (b) In the State of Odisha, 71,766 km road length has been sanctioned under various interventions/ verticals of PMGSY and out of which, 64,688 km road length has been completed as on 23rd March 2022.The details of work under various interventions/ verticals of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) are as under: -                                                                    (Road length in Km)Verticals	    Sanctioned	             Completed	                  Balance*	         Road     Bridges          Road      Bridges        Road          Bridges                 length	    (Nos.)         length     (Nos.)        length	  (Nos.) PMGSY-I	       61,130	    528	          58,482	467	     274	    61PMGSY-II	3,672	     30	           3,632	23	      23	     7RCPLWEA@	  486	      2	             343	0	     142	     2PMGSY-III	6,478	     71	           2,231	0	   4,247	    71Total:	       71,766	    631	          64,688	490	   4,687	   141 @ Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Areas (RCPLWEA) *Balance road length is less than the difference of sanctioned and completed length due to the reasons that some projects were completed with less than the sanctioned length due to reduction in road length, change in alignment, construction of part length by other agencies, etc. The road length sanctioned under all three phases of the said yojana and their work progress report in each District of Odisha is given at Annexure. Details of projects can be seen at  www.omms.nic-> progress monitoring->State MPR Abstract Report.   (c)     A sum of Rs.21,630.75 crore has been released as central share of programme fund as on 23rd March 2022 and an expenditure of Rs.28,545.28 crore (including state share) has been incurred towards implementation of the PMGSY in the State of Odisha. (d)     No Sir. There is no paucity of funds under PMGSY.  As on 01.04.2021, the state had an unspent balance of Rs.1,368.88 crore (excluding Security Deposits). Rs.400 crore central funds were released to the state during the financial year 2021-22. The unspent balances as on 23 March 2022 (including state share) with the state is Rs. 653.15 crore. The Government has recently extended the timeline for completion of PMGSY-I, II and Road Connectivity Project Left Wing Extremism Affected (RCPLWEA). PMGSY-I&II are now targeted for completion by September 2022. RCPLWEA is targeted for completion by March 2023. The timeline for completion of PMGSY-III is March 2025. ******
