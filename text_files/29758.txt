                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1413






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Promoting Youth Sports in Bihar




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Bihar is actively participating in Youth Affairs and Sports activities; (b) 	if so, whether any concrete steps have been taken by the Government to promote Youth Affairs and Sports in the State of Bihar during the last three years and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the amount provided to the State Government of Bihar for development of this area during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) 	Yes, Sir.(b)	Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports promotes youth and sports through its various Schemes/Programmes in the country as a whole including the State of Bihar as per the following details:i) Department of Youth Affairs:Department of Youth Affairs of the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports has three major schemes for development of Youth in the country, namely 1. Rashtriya Yuva Sashaktikaran Karyakram (RYSK) consisting of seven sub-schemes namely (i) Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS),  (ii) National Youth Corps (NYC),          (iii) National Programme for Youth & Adolescent Development (NPYAD), (iv)International Cooperation (IC), (v) Youth Hostels (YH), (vi) Assistance to Scouting & Guiding Organisations (S&G), (vii) National Young Leaders Programme (NYLP).  2. National Service Scheme (NSS) and 3. Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD). NYKS undertakes various Programmes and Activities across the country including the State of Bihar. The Programmes are organized by NYKS through its large network of Youth Clubs/ Mahila Mandal and Volunteers at Rural and District level in the State of Bihar are as under:•	Training of Youth on Clean Village- Green Village•	Training of Youth on Jal Jagran Abhiyan•	Combating COVID-19: Mass Awakening and Action Campaign •	Establishing Disaster Risk Reduction & Preparedness Teams •	Youth Led Fit India Movement, Youth Wellness and Positive Life Style•	District Youth Convention•	Declamation Contest on Patriotism & Nation BuildingBesides above, NYKS also implements the National Youth Corps (NYC) and National Young Leaders Programme (NYLP) Schemes of the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, in the State of Bihar for promoting national integration, personality & life skills development, volunteerism, leadership development & awareness on local contemporary issues confronting youth.	Under National Service Scheme (NSS), the youth volunteers of NSS of Bihar participates in Pre-Republic Day Parade Camp (Pre R D Camp) organized in Patna/ Bihar and selected volunteers from the Pre R D Camp also participate in Republic Day Parade Camp held in New Delhi. NSS organization also organizes various other programmes such as (i) Organizing Awareness Camps on health, family welfare and nutrition, adult literacy, pre-school education (ii) Cleanliness Drive, (iii) Tree Plantation, (iv) Blood Donation Camps in different States including Bihar.ii) Department of Sports:Department of Sports, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports provides financial assistance for promotion of sports activities through National Sports Federations, training in Sports Authority of India (SAI) Centres, support under Khelo India Scheme.Sports Authority of India (SAI), implements various Sports Promotional Schemes namely:  (i) National Centres of Excellence (NCOE), (ii) SAI Training Centre (STC), (iii) Extension Centre of STC,  (iv) National Sports Talent Contest (NSTC) across the country to identify talented sportspersons in various age groups and nurture them to excel at national and international levels. The details of the activities undertaken for promotion of Sports in the State of Bihar during the last three years are given at Annexure.The initiatives undertaken in the State of Bihar under the Khelo India Scheme:Annual Sports Competitions:  In Khelo India Games athletes participate from all the States including Bihar.
