                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3124






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Telecast on Ill-effects of Population Explosion




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to formulate a scheme to strictly telecast/broadcast advertisements, documentaries, stories and motivational episodes related to population explosion and its likely ill-effects on private channels and radio including Doordarshan; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the time by which the said scheme is likely to be formalised?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR) (a) to (c): Doordarshan does stories and discussion programmes relating to population growth. All India Radio (AIR) has a dedicated Family Welfare Unit with branches at all Capital and Regional entities looking after programmes related to various aspects of family welfare including ill-effects of population explosion. AIR regularly broadcasts programmes which promote small family norms highlighting inter alia, significance of controlling population. Documentaries, success stories, family serials, talks, discussions and Phone-in programmes related with increase in population form a part of special audience programmes for women, rural youth and industrial workers, etc. at all AIR stations in the country.*********
