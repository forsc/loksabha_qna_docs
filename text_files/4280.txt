                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3951






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Golden Quadrilateral Project




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government proposes to run trains on the routes like  Golden Quadrilateral and Diagonals connecting major and important cities; (b)	 if so, whether the Government proposes premium trains like     Rajdhani to be operated in these proposed routes; and (c)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c)  The  Golden Quadrilateral and Diagonals are among the busiest and most saturated sectors of Indian Railways, on which premium trains such as Rajdhani Expresses and Shatabdi Expresses are already operational. At present, there are no specific proposals to introduce additional Rajdhani Express type of trains on these sectors. However, introduction of trains is an ongoing and continuous process on Indian Railways subject to commercial justification, operational feasibility and availability of resources.*****
