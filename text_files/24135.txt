                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

176






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Lack of Electricity in Schools




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) According to UDISC Report (2019-20) only 80% of schools in the country have electricity ;(b) if so, please furnish the details of schools where there is no electricity and the action plan of the Government by when all the schools are likely to have electricity ?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b): As per Unified District Information System for Education Plus (UDISE+) 2019-20 there are 1507708 schools in the country out of which 1257897 schools have electricity. Improving the infrastructure in the schools including providing electricity to the schools is a continuous process. The Department of School Education and Literacy has launched an Integrated Scheme for School Education – Samagra Shiksha which subsumes three erstwhile Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA), Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA) and Teacher Education (TE) from 1st April, 2018. Samagra Shiksha focuses inter alia on improvement in school infrastructure including electricity in all States and Union Territories. Further, education being a concurrent subject, States and Union Territories are also expected to undertake this activity.*******
