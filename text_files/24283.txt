                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

136






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Training Under Skill India Mission




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of people enrolled for skilled training under Skill India Mission in the last three years;(b)	the number of people enrolled for all India trade test in the last three years;(c)	the number of people who cleared the All India Trade Test (AITT) and have been awarded National Trade Certificates in the last three years;(d)	the number of such awardees who have been given employment in Government or semi Government departments and organizations?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) Number of persons enrolled / trained under the various schemes of the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship under the auspices of Skill India Mission in last three years is as follows :-Sl.No	Name of the Scheme	Persons enrolled /trained during last three years i.e. 2018-19 to 2020-211	Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 1.0 /2.0	83,48,1482	Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS)	9,42,4113	National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS)	7,38,0744	Craftsman Training Scheme – training through Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs)*	39,75,924 	Total	1,40,04,557•	Admission in ITIs pertains to the academic year 2017-18 to 2019-20 as admission for 2020-21 has not been completed yet.  (b)  & (c ) :     S. No	Year	Number of people enrolled for all India trade test in the last three years(in Lakhs)	Number of people who cleared the All India Trade Test (AITT) and have been awarded National Trade Certificates in the last three years    (in Lakhs)1.	2018-19	14.5	8.072.	2019-20	14.2	8.033.	2020-21	11.89	5.69Total	40.59	21.79 (d)       Ministry does not maintain database of employment of such awardees which are controlled by the respective State Governments and Private Bodies.  However, as per  ‘Tracer Study of ITI Graduates –Final Report’ published in January, 2018, 81.9% of end term graduates were available in the job market.  56.8% of them are wage employed or salaried, 6.7% are self- employed or in family business.
