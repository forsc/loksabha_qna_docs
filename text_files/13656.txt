                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2337






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Setting up of Port in Maharashtra




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has given approval for setting up     of a port at Vadhavan in Maharashtra and if so, the     details thereof;(b) whether a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) will be formed     with Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust and if so, the details     thereof;(c) the details of the source of income/investment for the     project; and(d) the time by which the port will be ready for commercial     operation?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)  Yes, Sir. Government of India has accorded in-principle approval to develop port at Vadhavan in Maharashtra on landlord model by a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) incorporated under Companies Act, 2013 with Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT) as a lead Partner. (c)	The project is proposed to be financed through a blend of equity and debt. The SPV will provide equity and raise debt from multilateral financial institutions. (d)	The Project has been planned to be completed in two phases. First phase is planned to be ready for operation in five years subject to necessary statutory clearances and investment approval.                                                     *****
