                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2143






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






PMJAY in Health and Wellness Centres




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) provide free healthcare facilities under PMJAY;(b) if not, whether the Government is planning to bring all HWCs under its ambit;(c) whether HWCs provide screening and management of non-communicable diseases, mental ailments, ophthalmic and dental healthcare; if so, the details thereof;(d) whether HWCs are employed in task of undertaking COVID tests; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	& (b) UnderAyushman Bharat – Health & Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs), 1.5 lakh Sub Health Centres and Primary Health Centres are to be transformed into Health and Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs) across the country for provision of Comprehensive Primary Health Care (CPHC) that includes preventive healthcare and health promotion at the community level with continuum of care approach by December, 2022.The AB-HWCs are not under the PMJAY. There is no such proposal.Services at these AB-HWCs are free and universal to all individuals residing in the service area.(c)	Under Ayushman Bharat – Health & Wellness Centres (AB-HWC), Comprehensive Primary Healthcare is being provided to include promotive, preventive, curative, palliative and rehabilitative services. Services like Population based enumeration of persons above 30 years of age and screening of individuals prioritizing high risk cases for Non Communicable Diseases (NCDs) – Hypertension, Diabetes and three common cancers (oral, cervix and breast) along with communicable diseases like TB and leprosy has been initiated at these AB-HWCs.Operational Guidelines for screening of the five NCD conditions have been issued and shared with the States.(d)	& (e) AB-HWCs are not employed in the task of undertaking COVID tests.
