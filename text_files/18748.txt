                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

60






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Employment under MGNREGA and GKRA




Chunni Lal Sahu













                                                    Mohan Mandavi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any scheme to provide employment at Panchayat level and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the number of people provided with employment so far under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) during COVID-19 pandemic, State-wise; and(c) 	the number of people provided with employment so far under Garib Kalyan Rojgar Abhiyan (GKRA) during COVID-19 pandemic, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):      The Government is implementing Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme under the eponymous Act of   2005, through States/UTs which provide at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult member volunteers to do unskilled manual work. Gram Panchayat is the implementing agency under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme at Panchayat level and atleast 50 percent of the works are to be implemented by Gram Panchayats.(b):       State/UT-wise details of number of persons provided employment under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) during the current Financial Year 2020-21 (as on 25.01.2021) is given at Annexure. (c):       State-wise employment generated (In Persondays) under Garib Kalyan Rojgar Abhiyan during COVID-19 pandemic is as below:S.
