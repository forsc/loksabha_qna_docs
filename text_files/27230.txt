                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2969






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Construction of Harbour at VOC Port




Thalikkottai Rajuthevar Baalu














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposed to construct the outer harbour at VOC Port at Tuticorin in view of the draught restrictions in the existing harbour; and(b)	whether the project works have started and if so, the details including physical and financial progress thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes, Sir. The Government of India had taken a decision to develop the Outer Harbour Project at VOC Port, Tuticorin. (b)	The Project has not been taken up. The Port had incurred an expenditure of Rs. 5.87 Cr for the Detailed Study for the Project. However, no expenditure has been incurred for construction of the Project.                                              *****
