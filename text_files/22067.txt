                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3114






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan in Uttar Pradesh




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of youth trained under Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan so far in Uttar Pradesh; (b) 	the number of centres established in Uttar Pradesh at present and the names of sector where these youths have been trained in those institutes; and (c) 	the details of service centres where employment has been provided after training?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):	The Government had announced Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan (ANB) on 12.05.2020 with the aim of making the country self-reliant and to focus on local manufacturing, local market and local supply chains. Under ANB, the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship has undertaken various initiatives to meet the requirement of trained skilled manpower. The Ministry through its flagship scheme, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), has been providing short-term skill development training in 37 sectors through affiliated Training Providers/ Training Centres (TCs) across the country including the State of Uttar Pradesh (UP). Under the Scheme PMKVY 2.0 (2016-2020), as on 19.01.2021, 106.96 lakh candidates have been trained across the country, which include 15.87 lakh candidates from UP.  Under the Scheme, 404 TCs have been operational in UP. Further, the Ministry has launched the Atmanirbhar Skilled Employee Employer Mapping (ASEEM) portal to help skilled people find sustainable livelihood opportunities. As on 05.03.2021, 1.29 crore skilled personnel have been on-boarded on the portal and around 8.4 lakh job offers have been made.
