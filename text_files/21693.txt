                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2781






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Media Infrastructure Development Programme




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing Media Infrastructure Development Programme;(b)	if so, the details and salient features thereof;(c)	whether the targets fixed thereunder for the years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 have been achieved;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES[SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR](a) to (e)	''Media Infrastructure Development Programme (MIDP)'' scheme was formulated during the 12th plan period with the objective to improve and augment the infrastructure support to the Media Units of the Ministry in the form of machinery, equipment and infrastructure enabling them to perform at competitive levels in the media sector of the country. The scheme was also approved for continuation beyond 12th plan period till 2019-20.In accordance with the decision taken for rationalisation of schemes, the activities envisaged under the MIDP scheme was subsumed in the Establishment Expenditure of the Ministry with effect from Financial Year 2020-21.*****
