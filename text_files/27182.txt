                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2855






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Implementation of JJM




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has implemented Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM); (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the number and the details of the districts where tap connections have been provided in each and every house under Har Ghar Jal Mission including the districts of Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI  PRAHLAD  SINGH  PATEL)(a) & (b) 	Since August, 2019, Government of India in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal to make provision of potable tap water supply to every rural household in the country by 2024. The estimated outlay of the mission is Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore.(c) 	As reported as on date, 76 districts across the country have become ‘Har Ghar Jal’ districts  i.e. provision for tap water supply to every household has been made. State/ UT-wise details of these districts is as under:State/ UT	No. of districts with 100% households having provision of tap water supply A & N Islands	3Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu	3Goa	2Gujarat	5Haryana	19Himachal Pradesh	3Jammu & Kashmir	2Maharashtra	1Puducherry	2Punjab	4Telangana	32                Source: JJM-IMISIn Tamil Nadu, none of the districts has all households with the provision of tap water supply. State/ UT, district and village-wise progress as well as status of provision of tap water supply, is available on JJM dashboard, which is public domain at:https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx*****
