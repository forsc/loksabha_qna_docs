                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2362






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Details of States Earned Maximum Revenue From Tourism




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the measures taken by the Government to improve tourism in North India and the revenue generated from international tourism;(b)	the details of the States which have earned the maximum revenue from tourism during the last three years and the details of those States which has got huge tourism potential but are unable to tap it; and (c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM             		       (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a): 	The Ministry of Tourism   is undertaking a series of   activities for encouraging citizens to   travel within the country including Northern India. The Ministry is regularly organising Tourism Promotional Events, Road Shows, Fam Trips, Students Study Tours, Workshops, Quiz Programmes, Social Media Promotions etc. under the banner of Dekho Apna Desh, Ek Bharat Shrestha   Bharat (EBSB) and Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav.   The measures taken by the government in last one year to improve tourism in North India are at Annexure.Further, Ministry of Tourism, Government of India does not maintain data on region-wise revenue generated from tourism. (b) & (c): Ministry of Tourism, Government of India does not maintain State-wise data on revenue generated from tourism.******
