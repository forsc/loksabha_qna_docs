                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

537






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Funds for Welfare of Minority Communities




Badruddin Ajmal













                                                    Pratima Mondal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of expenditure incurred on schemes meant for welfare of minority communities along with the funds allocated and released for these schemes during the last three years, scheme-wise; (b)	the details of ratio of benefit obtained by the various communities;(c)	whether percentage of increase in funds for minority welfare is very low as compared with other Ministries;(d)	 if so, whether this Ministry ever approach with request of increase in funds; and(e)	if so, the details and the response thereof, if not, the reason therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SH. MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	Scheme-wise details of the fund allocated and released under various schemes to meet the scheme objective during the last three years i.e.  2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 is at Annexure.(b)	As per the 2011 census, the percentages of six notified minority communities out of the total Minority population is Buddhists-3.61%, Christians-11.9%, Jains-1.9%, Muslims-73.66%, Parsis-0.02 and Sikhs-8.91. The Ministry maintains data of community wise beneficiaries only for the scholarship schemes. The ratio of beneficiaries in the scholarship schemes among the minority communities during the last three years is Muslims 75.58%, Christians 12.15%, Sikhs 8.19%, Buddhists 2.80%, Jains 1.27% and Parsis 0.01%.(c)	to (e) The allocation of Ministry of Minority Affairs increased over the years by 7% from Rs. 4700.00 crore in 2018-19 to Rs.5029.00 crore in 2020-21. ******
