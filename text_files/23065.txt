                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Simplification of Adoption Procedure




Pratima Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of children adopted by Indians and foreigners during each of the last three years and the current year;(b)	whether the number of adoptions have declined during the said period;(c)	if so, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government has received complaints regarding complicated formalities involved in the adoption procedure, if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the action taken by the Government to simplify child adoption procedure in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER  MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (e)       The numbers of children adopted under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children Act), 2015 (JJ Act) by Indians and Foreigners during each of the last three years and current year are as under: Year	Adoption by Indian Citizen	Adoption by Foreign Citizen2017-18	3276	6512018-19	3374	6532019-20	3351	3942020-21(upto16/03/21)	2866	394 Due measures are taken to further ameliorate the concerns of the stakeholders and streamline the process, including refining CARINGS portal for better service delivery.*****
