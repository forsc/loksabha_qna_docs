                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1133






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Welfare of Silk Sector




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government is planning to give any special scheme/packages for the welfare of silk sector at Kolar District in the State of Karnataka, if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government has identified any areas for the implementation of this scheme, if so, the details thereof indicating the districts identified and the criteria for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):  Yes, Sir.  An exclusive Cluster Development Project covering around 3500 farmers is under implementation for the development of Bivoltine sericulture in Kolar district.  Besides, Ministry of Textiles through Central Silk Board (CSB) has implemented an ‘Integrated Scheme for Development of Silk Industry (ISDSI)’ a Central Sector Scheme (CSS) viz. ‘ Silk Samagra’  for development of silk industry in the country including Karnataka State.Under the scheme, assistance is extended to sericulture farmers for raising of Kissan Nursery, plantation with improved Mulberry varieties, irrigation, Chawki Rearing Centres with incubation facility, construction of rearing houses, rearing equipment, door to door service agents for disinfection and input supply, support for improved reeling units like Automatic Reeling units, multiend Reeling machines, Improved Twisting machines and support for post yarn facilities for quality silk and fabric production.***
