                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2980






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Assistance to Small Entrepreneurs




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether things made by small Indian entrepreneurs is costly as the same category of articles produced by China and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether day to day used domestic products have not that finish as produced by China and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to help small entrepreneurs to compete with Chinese ones; and(d)	if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI) (a)	to (d):	The prices and quality of different products differ in different countries and also change over time.The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises implements various schemes /programmes to support micro and small industries in order to enhance their competitiveness. These schemes/programmes include Credit Linked Capital Subsidy-Technology Upgradation Scheme (CLCS-TUS); Credit Guarantee Scheme; Credit Linked Capital subsidy Scheme; Cluster Development Programme; Public Procurement Policy for MSMEs and Market Development Assistance Scheme etc.
