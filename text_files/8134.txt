                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1777






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Madurai Smart City Projects




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)  the amount spent so far on the smart city projects in Madurai district of Tamil Nadu along with the heads under which the money has been spent; (b)  the number of projects completed in Madurai; and (c) the status report of all smart city works undertaken including the deadline for completion of the works?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): Government of India has released Rs 196 crore to Madurai Smart City. Madurai  has reported utilization of Rs. 156.81 crore. The Project-wise details of expenditure is given in Annexure-I. (b): Madurai Smart City has reported completion of one project for providing Infrastructure and Basic Amenities at Fruit Market. ( c): Madurai was selected in Round 2 in September 2016. Project- wise status and completion of projects as reported by Madurai Smart City is given in Annexure II.                               *****
