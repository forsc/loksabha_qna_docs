                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1117






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Tourism Projects Approved in Telangana




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the Tourism Projects approved, sanctioned and executed in each of the identified Tourism Circuits in the State of Telangana during the last five years including the current year, year-wise; (b) 	the details of the Tourism Projects completed so far and the time by which the remaining projects are likely to be completed; (c) 	the reasons for the delay in completion of the pending projects; (d) 	the details of funds allocated, released and utilised for the development of tourism sector in the state during the said period, year-wise; and (e) 	whether the State Government of Telangana has submitted any project proposal to the Government and if so, the details thereof along with the present status of ongoing projects in the state of Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	Development and  promotion  of  Tourism  is  primarily  the  responsibility of the  State  Governments / UT Administrations.  However, the Ministry of Tourism under ‘Swadesh  Darshan’  scheme  is developing thematic circuits in the country in planned and prioritized manner in order to develop tourism infrastructure at multiple destinations.  As of now fifteen thematic circuits have been identified under this scheme for development namely; North-East, Buddhist, Himalayan, Coastal, Krishna, Desert, Tribal, Eco, Wildlife, Rural, Spiritual, Ramayana, Heritage, Sufi and Tirthankar.  The  Ministry  of  Tourism has  sanctioned  3  projects  in the  State of  Telangana  which  are  under  different  stages of  implementation.  The  list of the  projects  sanctioned  under  Swadesh  Darshan  in the  State of  Telangana  is annexed. Sanctioning of new projects under Swadesh  Darshan  scheme  is  under review.  ********
