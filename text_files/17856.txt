                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






PMAY in Bihar




 Sunil Kumar Pintu













                                                    Alok Kumar Suman
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the houses proposed to be built and completed in district Gopalganj of Bihar are not following the specified criteria under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G);(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the total amount of funds allocated, disbursed and utilised in Bihar under PMAY-G, district-wise including Gopalganj;(d) 	the total number of beneficiaries/families provided houses under the said Yojana in Bihar, districtwise including district Gopalganj of Bihar till date; and(e) 	the time by which cent per cent families are likely to be covered under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)&(b) The State Government of Bihar has reported that implementation  of  PMAY-G  in  Gopalganj district is being done  as  per Framework of  PMAY-G and directions issued by Ministry of Rural Development from time to time.(c)   Details of funds allocated, disbursed and utilised in Bihar under PMAY-G are given at Annexure-I. District wise utilisation of fund including for district Gopalganj are given at Annexure-II.(d)   The total number of beneficiaries / families provided houses under the said Yojana in Bihar, district-wise including Gopalganj  of Bihar till date is given at annexure ‘III’.(e)   To achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by the year 2021-22, PMAY-G aims to provide pucca house to 2.95 crore beneficiaries in rural areas across the country including eligible beneficiaries of PMAY-G of Bihar.     ******Annexure -I  Annexure referred to in reply to part (c) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1591 to be answered on 20.09.2020.
