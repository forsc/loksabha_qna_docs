                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2342






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Funds for Welfare of SCs




Mimi Chakraborty














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of funds allocated for welfare schemes of Scheduled Castes since 2015 along with the schemes being implemented by the various Ministries; (b) the total amount of funds spent so far towards the schemes and the amount lying unspent since 2015; and(c) the reasons for the funds lying unspent and the steps taken by the Government to ensure that the funds allocated are fully spent and in an efficient manner?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): Various Central   Sponsored and Central Sector schemes are being implemented by the Ministry for welfare of Scheduled Castes. Scheme wise allocation of funds and expenditure from 2015-16 to 2018-19 is enclosed at Annexure.At the end of Financial Year, the unspent balance remained utilized under different schemes lapses.(c):  Does not arise *****
