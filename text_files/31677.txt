                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3168






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Har Khet ko Paani




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the work carried out by the Government under the Har Khet Ko Paani component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) in various States of the country including Rajasthan by 2022; (b) whether the Government has set any target for this yojana; (c) if so, the details thereof along with the progress made so far in this regard; (d) whether the Government has any mechanism for monitoring of this yojana; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) to (c) During 2015-16, Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) was launched with an aim to enhance physical access of water on farm and expand cultivable area under assured irrigation, improve on farm water use efficiency, and to introduce sustainable water conservation practices.  Har Khet Ko Pani (HKKP) is one of the components of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY). HKKP, in turn, consists of four sub-components, (i) Command Area Development & Water Management (CADWM), (ii) Surface Minor Irrigation (SMI) & Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies, and (iii) Ground Water (GW).With the new scheme of 99 prioritized AIBP projects approved in July 2016, central assistance under CADWM has been restricted to these prioritized projects only. Presently, 88 projects (including 1 project for non-structural intervention) have been included under PMKSY-CADWM, targeting additional culturable command area (CCA) of 45 lakh hectares, for the total estimated cost of Rs 18,736 crore, out of which targeted central assistance (CA) component is Rs.8,236 crore.State-wise details of central assistance (including Rajasthan), and CCA covered, under CADWM during 2016-21 is placed at Annexure – I. Further, State-wise details in respect of SMI & RRR of Water Bodies component of PMKSY-HKKP, including Rajasthan, are placed at Annexure-II, while that for Ground Water component is placed at Annexure-III. However, under Ground Water component, no proposal from Government of Rajasthan has been submitted for inclusion. Contd…P/2//2//(d) & (e) Projects under PMKSY- HKKP are continuously being monitored and evaluated through Central Water Commission (CWC) regional offices under Ministry of Jal Shakti, and also by the project monitoring unit of PMKSY. Progress of CADWM projects is also being monitored through online CADWM Information System (cadwm.gov.in). Further, progress of CADWM projects is also reviewed periodically through a Project Implementation Review Committee (PIRC). Eight meetings of the Project Implementation Review Committee (PIRC) have been held so far.Further, SMI & RRR of Water Bodies component of PMKSY-HKKP is also being monitored on sample basis by the field offices of CWC. In addition, all the components of PMKSY-HKKP are being monitored from time to time by the respective Wing Heads at Department of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation level.*****
