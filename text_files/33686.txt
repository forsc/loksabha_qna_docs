                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1108






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Schemes for North Eastern States




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the schemes being implemented by the Government in North-Eastern States including the States of Assam and Haryana and other States at present; (b) whether any proposal from the said States in this regard is currently pending with the Central Government; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) The Government is implementing various schemes to enhance production and productivity of bovines & small ruminants (sheep & goat), piggery & poultry across the country including North-Eastern States including the States of Assam and Haryana and other States at present namely:i.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission(RGM)ii.	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)iii.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund(DIDF)iv.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities (SDC&FPO)v.	National Livestock Mission(NLM)vi.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund(AHIDF)vii.	Livestock Health & Disease Control(LH&DC)viii.	National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP)ix.	Livestock Census and Integrated Sample Survey (LC&ISS)(b) and (c) No proposals from the said States are currently pending with the Central Government under above schemes.***
