                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5127






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Incorporating MSME in Defence Corridors




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to incorporate small and medium Sector Companies in proposed Defence corridors including in the vicinity of Jhansi and nearby areas in near future;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) the details of the status of such proposal especially in Jhansi and nearby areas along with the time limit for its completion, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (c):  Hon’ble Finance Minister in his budget speech (2018-19) had announced setting up of two Defence Industrial Corridors in the country.  In pursuance to the said announcement, it was decided to set up one of the corridors in Uttar Pradesh and the other in Tamil Nadu which would also cover MSME needs.  The six nodes of Uttar Pradesh Defence Industrial Corridor includes Lucknow, Kanpur, Agra, Aligarh, Chitrakoot and Jhansi.*****
