                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

235






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Population Enumeration




T. R. Paarivendhar













                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of population of Tamil Nadu as per Census, 2011;(b) the details of population break-up thereof as per Census, 2011, community-wise;(c) whether there is any decrease/increase of any community during the period; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The total population of Tamil Nadu as per Census 2011 is 7,21,47,030. (b) to (d): 	The community-wise population of the State as per Census 2001 and 2011 and their decadal growth are given below:-2-L.S.US.Q.NO.235.
