                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2907






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Impact of Global Slowdown on IT Firms




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a) whether global economic slowdown may impact IT firms in the country including in Hyderabad; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefore; and (c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        ):  No, Sir.  As per National Association of Software and Services Companies (NASSCOM), the Indian IT industry revenue continues to grow annually at 8.6% reaching  $164 Billion in FY 2018-19. (b) and (c):  Does not arise.   However, Government initiatives such as Digital India, Start-up India, Smart Cities, Promotion of Digital Economy and e-Governance programmes has provided growth opportunities to IT industry. Further, Government has approved “National Policy on Software Products” with a vision to make India a global player in software development, production, supply of innovative and efficient Software Products.   *******
