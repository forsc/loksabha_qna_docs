                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2268






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Online Fraud in making Passports




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether many persons have become victims of online fraud in the name of making passports; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number and details of such victims and the total amount involved in the said fraud, State-wise? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS   [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	It has been brought to the attention of the Ministry about the existence of certain websites which are fraudulently claiming to make passports. Ministry has taken steps to spread awareness about such fraudulent websites and issued advisories through the social media. Citizens have been advised to use the official passport portal (www.passportindia.gov.in) of the Ministry for passport related services.(c)	Data on the number and details of applicants who have used these fraudulent websites to apply for a passport and the total amount involved cannot be quantified definitively as these cases are due to fake websites run by fraudsters.***
