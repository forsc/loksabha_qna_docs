                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






NABARD Loan to Telangana




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     the details of the loan sanctions and disbursals by NABARD to the Government of Telangana and its corporations and Public Sector Undertakings in the years 2014-15, 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21;(b)   the details of loan sanctions and disbursals of NABARD to irrigation projects including Mallanna Sagar reservoir under Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project in Telangana during the said period;(c)   whether NABARD has conducted any independent appraisal of the Mallanna Sagar reservoir and Kaleshwaram irrigation projects including independently checking the cost estimates of the irrigation projects and if so, the details thereof;(d)   whether NABARD ensured a transparent competitive bidding process for the Mallanna Sagar reservoir and Kaleshwaram irrigation projects and if so, the details thereof;(e)   the details of loan sanctions and disbursals by NABARD for check dams in Telangana; and(f)    whether NABARD has enquired into complaints of high levels of corruption in the construction of check dams on Musi River in Telangana and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) & (b): National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) extends financial support to State Governments under various initiatives namely Rural Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF), Warehouse Infrastructure Fund (WIF), Food Processing Fund (FPF) for creation of various rural infrastructure.  Further NABARD also extends financial assistance under NABARD Infrastructure Development Assistance (NIDA) for State Government Corporations for infrastructure creation and under Credit Facility to Federation (CFF) for supporting procurement of food grains.As apprised by NABARD, the details of loans sanctioned/disbursed during the period 2014-15 to 2020-21 under RIDF, WIF, FPF, NIDA and CFF to State Government of Telangana are at Annexure. The details of loan sanctioned to irrigation projects under NABARD Infrastructure Development Assistance (NIDA) are as under:(? crore)Year	Agency	Sanctioned Amount	Total Disbursement 2019-20	Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project Corporation Ltd.	1500.00	795.542020-21	Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project Corporation Ltd.	4674.83	2107.82(c):  NABARD has informed that the Detailed Project Report (DPR) of Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project including cost aspects has been approved by Central Water Commission, New Delhi and the copies of all such necessary clearances were submitted by Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project Corporation Limited (KIPCL) to NABARD. The same has been used as a base document for sanction. Further, a team from NABARD conducted a pre-sanction monitoring visit of the project covering aspects like status of project implementation, progress of works, expenditure incurred, quality control mechanism, etc. (d):  As apprised by NABARD, they have no role in bidding processes adapted by State Government Corporations, including Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project Corporation, Limited (KIPCL). Also, KIPCL approached NABARD only after the tendering processes were completed and works had commenced under respective projects.
