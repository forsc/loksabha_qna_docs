                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3356






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Teacher Eligibility Test




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to recognize Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) for life instead of seven years;(b) if so, whether the Government has taken any steps so far in this direction;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): Yes Sir. The Government has approved the validity period of Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) qualifying certificate for appointment, unless otherwise notified by the appropriate Government, would remain valid for life. *****
