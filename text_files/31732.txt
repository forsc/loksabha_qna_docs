                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Requirement of Fertilizers




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state :(a)  the details of requirement and actual supply of fertilizers to all States during the month of October and November 2021, State and month-wise;(b)  whether the Government of Telangana has brought to the notice of the Union Government the shortage of Fertilizers in the State;(c)  if so, the details thereof; and (d)  the response of the union Government thereto?






ANSWER

(b) & (c):   No Sir. There has been no shortage of any fertilizer during the ongoing Rabi 2021-22 Season in the State of Telangana. The same has been acknowledged by the State Government.
