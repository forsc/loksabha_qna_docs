                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2938






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Pending Pension Cases of ICMR




Thalikkottai Rajuthevar Baalu














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that employees retiring from ICMR’s various centers including Chennai have not received their pension and retirement benefits even after lapse of eight months post their superannuation;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and(c) the details of necessary steps taken by ICMR to avoid such delays and clear all pending cases since 2019?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): No.(b) & (c): In view of (a) above, does not arise.
