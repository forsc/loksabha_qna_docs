                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2654






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Privatisation of Mining Sector




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Government is going to privatise the mining sector of the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (c)	the number of Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) and their performance report for the last five years and the status of private players in the country; and(d)	the details of proposal of the Government to modernise the mining technology, safety, growth and reformation plans by amending the National Mineral Policy, 2019? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) & (b): Section 5 of the MMDR Act, 1957 provides for grant of mineral concessions to an Indian national or a company as defined in the Companies Act, 2013. The mining leases are granted through e-auction. Therefore, private players are actively involved in mining sector.
