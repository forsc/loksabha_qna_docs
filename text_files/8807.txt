                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2302






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Crisis in Automobile Sector




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the automobile sector has been going through a crisis relating to shortage of capital goods currently;(b) if so, the efforts made by the Government in this regard;(c) whether the Government proposes to provide any special package to the said sector; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                  (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)                              ********(a) & (b): No concerns over shortage of capital goods have been reported by automobile sector. (c) & (d): The Government, as a policy maker, always attempts to keep and improve momentum of the economy through a package of measure for comprehensive and continued development of the industry as and when required.                                       ***********
