                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

237






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Shortage of Funds under MGNREGS




Ganesan Selvam













                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether many State Governments are running out of funds for Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee scheme and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(b) 	the number of States which have negative net balance;(c) 	whether shortage of funds has led to delay in the payment to MGNREGS workers and if so, the total outstanding amount for payment to MGNREGS workers; and(d) 	the other steps taken by the Government to augment the funds for MGNREGS for its successful implementation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (d):     Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme is a demand driven scheme. There are enough funds with the scheme to fulfill all the liabilities of the scheme for the States which have met the entire requirement for release of second tranche of funds. The Ministry has already sought additional resources for the scheme.Fund release to States/UTs is a continuation process and Central Government is committed in making funds available keeping in view the demand for work. The Ministry seeks additional funds as and when required for meeting the demand for work on the ground.As per the provision of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, the beneficiaries are entitled to get wage payment within 15 days of closure of Muster Roll of the work. Government of India has also issued a detailed Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) to the States to ensure timely wage payment.******
