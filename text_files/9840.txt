                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






BS-IV to BS-VI




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has issued an order to implement BS-VI standards from 1 April, 2020, if so, the justification for leapfrog from BS-IV to BS-VI skipping BS-V standards; and(b) whether the auto sector is ready for this leapfrog, if not, the reasons for the same and the incentives the Ministry is giving to auto sector for this transformation?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROELUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Government has decided to leapfrog directly from BS-IV to BS-VI, skipping BS-V to curb pollution across the country. Ministry of Road Transport & Highways has issued a Notification vide No. GSR 889(E) dated 16.09.2016, mandating mass emission standard for BS-VI throughout the country with effect from April 1, 2020.(b) The Hon’ble Supreme Court on 24th October 2018, has ordered that no new motor vehicle conforming to the emission standard BS-IV shall be sold or registered in the entire country with effect from 1.4.2020.
