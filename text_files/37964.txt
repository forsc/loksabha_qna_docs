                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5178






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Recognition of Degrees and Diplomas of Foreign Universities




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has developed any mechanism to categorize and recognize degrees and diplomas from different foreign universities and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether the Government has the data of foreign students studying in India and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a): As per Gazette Notification No.(45)F.15-17/94-TS.IV(.) dated 13th March, 1995 those foreign qualifications which are recognized/ equated by Association of Indian Universities, New Delhi are treated as recognized for the purpose of employment to posts and services under the Central Government.(b): As per the All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) Report 2019-20, there are 49,348 Foreign National Students enrolled in Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) in the country. The details are available in AISHE Report 2019-20 at https://www.education.gov.in/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/statistics-new/aishe_eng.pdf.*****
