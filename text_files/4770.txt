                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4463






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Tree Plantation in Drought Prone Areas




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated any action plan for large scale tree plantation in drought prone areas of Chhattisgarh;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the funds earmarked for the purpose during the last two years along with the funds actually utilised during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)to (c) The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change has not formulated any specific action plan for large scale tree plantation in drought prone areas of Chhattisgarh.However, the Ministry (MoEF&CC) is supporting State/UT Governments to take up afforestation programmes under various Centrally Sponsored Schemes such as National Afforestation Programme (NAP) and Green India Mission (GIM). The NAP which is implemented for tree plantation in degraded forests through people’s participation also supports Pasture Development Model as one of its seven plantation models for addressing drought prone areas in the country including Chhattisgarh. The funds released under NAP during last two years to the states including Chhattisgarh are given in Annexure.Afforestation activities are also taken up under various programmes/funding sources such as Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) and Compensatory Afforestation Fund. Further the plantation activities outside forest areas like agro forestry/farm forestry /social forestry including massive plantation on all such lands are also being carried out cross- sectorally by various departments, Ministries, Non-Government Organizations (NGOs), Civil Society, Corporate etc., under various Centrally Sponsored Schemes and also under different State Plan/Non Plan Schemes including Externally Aided Projects.*****
