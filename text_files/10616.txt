                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4080






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






ODF Deadline




Bhola Singh













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Khan Saumitra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has set any deadline for cities to achieve ODF+ and ODF++ status and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the name of cities achieved the status of ODF+ and ODF++; (c) 	whether the Government has any proposal/provision for providing sanitary napkins in public toilets and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the details of steps being taken by the Government to ensure sanitation in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)  :  The deadline set for attaining Open Defecation Free (ODF) status is 31.03.2020. However all States/ Union Territories have already declared themselves ODF. As for Open Defecation Free+ (ODF+) or ODF++ status, no deadline has been set. However, Government is targeting all cities to achieve higher certification/ status once they have attained ODF Status.(b) : Name of cities certified as ODF+ (one time) and ODF++ (one time) is annexed.(c) : Provision of Sanitary Napkins is advised in the Community Toilet–Public Toilet (CT-PT) Cleanliness Protocol for ODF+ status. (d) :  Steps taken/ being taken by Government to ensure safe and sustainable sanitation in urban areas include, (i). Construction of 6,095,635 Individual House Hold Latrine (IHLL) and 561,298 Community/Public Toilet (CT/PT) have been constructed. (ii).Government has released the “Faecal Sludge and Septage Management (FSSM) Policy, 2017” which addresses safe management (collection, transportation and processing) of faecal sludge from toilets to ensure that untreated sludge is discharged into open drains, water bodies or in the open.(iii). Multi-media campaigns have been undertaken for public education and mass publicity and changing people’s behaviour.(iv). Under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) Mission, one of the five thrust areas is sewerage and septage management for which the duly approved projects of State Annual Action Plan are funded. Projects funded include decentralized, net worked undergone sewerage system including augmentation and rehabilitation of existing/ old sewerage system and Sewage Treatment Plants (STPs).                                  *****
