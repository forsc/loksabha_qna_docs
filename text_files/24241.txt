India’s Debt-to-GDP Ratio




Kalyan Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FINANCE be pleased to state: (a)	Whether it is a fact that India’s debt soared to 58.8% of the gross domestic product in the fiscal year ended in March from 51.6 per cent a year ago and the external debt to GDP ratio rose to 21.1 per cent as of March-end 2021 from 20.6 per cent a year ago; (b)	If so, the details thereof; and(c)	The details of the proposal to overcome the crisis and lowering India’s debt to GDP ratio which has increased from 74 per cent to about 89.56 per cent of the gross domestic product in recent times?
