                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1463






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Beneficiaries under MGNREGA




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of backward/extremely backward families benefited under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) so far, Statewise; and(b) 	the details of such families particularly in the State of Uttar Pradesh, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), 2005, provides for at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household including backward/extremely backward families whose adult member volunteers to do unskilled manual work. This is a demand driven scheme. The data relating exclusively to beneficiaries of backward/extremely backward families under the Scheme are not maintained at the level of this Ministry.  However, as per information uploaded by the States/UTs in the Management Information System (MIS), the State/UT-wise number of Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe households provided employment under the Scheme, including Uttar Pradesh, is at Annexure-I.  District-wise number of Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe households provided employment under the Scheme in Uttar Pradesh is at Annexure-II. Annexure-I referred in reply to parts (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1463 dated 11.02.2020 Number of Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe households provided employment in the Financial Year 2019-20 (as on 03.02.2020)Annexure-II referred in reply to parts (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1463 dated 11.02.2020 FY 2019-20 (as on 06.02.2020)
