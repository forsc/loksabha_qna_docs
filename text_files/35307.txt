                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2690






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






MSMEs in Bihar




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) functioning in the State of Bihar at present; (b) 	the number of such industries established under various schemes during the last three years in the State of Bihar, district-wise; and (c) 	the number of industries established in Vaishali and Muzaffarpur districts under Vaishali Parliamentary Constituency in Bihar after the launch of Mudra Yojana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA)(a):	As per Udyam Registration Portal, the total number of classified micro, small and medium enterprises registered in Bihar during 1st July 2020 to 14th March, 2022 were 3,00,372.(b):	The number of micro, small and medium enterprises which are set up in Bihar during last three years and registered on Udyam registration portal were as follows:<pre>Year	Number of units setup2019-20	32,6292020-21	64,7432021-22*	86,240  </pre>                   *as on 11.03.2022District wise units set up in Bihar during last three years and registered on Udyam registration portal are attached as annexure-I.(c): 	Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY) has been launched on 08.04.2015. The number of MSMEs established during 08.04.2015 to 14.03.2022 in Muzaffarpur and Vaishali  districts of Bihar and registered on Udyam Portal are as under:<pre>Districts	Number of MSMEs establishedMuzaffarpur	16,144Vaishali	10,601</pre>
