                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Shortage of Staff in OFD




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Indian Ordnance Factories are facing shortage of senior level officers and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the large number of Indian Ordnance Factories Service (IOFS) officers are on deputation in other Government Departments/Organizations;(c) if so, the details of such officers with time period of their deputation, Departments/ Organizations-wise;(d) whether the Government is planning to restrict the deputation period of IOFS officers and if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Ministry has taken any action for reversion of IOFS officers in their parent cadre and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) Yes, Sir. At present there are only 02 Members (HAG+ Level) in Ordnance Factory Board against sanctioned strength of 09 Members. Further, in HAG level, actual strength is 11 nos., with respect to sanctioned strength of 18 nos. (b) No, Sir.  Presently, only 102 IOFS officers out of current strength of 1515 IOFS Officers are on deputation. The existing proportion of officers on deputation is 6.75%, which is well within the specified limit of 7.5% as per guidelines of this Department.(c) Does not arise in view of (b) above.(d) No, Sir. IOFS officers are sent on deputation to other Departments/Ministries as per the scheme of Government of India for various organised cadres.(e) No, Sir.*****
