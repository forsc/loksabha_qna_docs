                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Koradi Thermal Power Plant




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether his Ministry has issued notice to Maharashtra State Power Generation Company (MAHAGENCO) for non-installation of Flue Gas Desulfurisation (FGD) at Koradi Thermal Power Plant;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken against the power plants showing non-compliance to the timeline provided under the Ministry notification dated 7th December, 2015 to install FGD till now, power plant wise; and(c)	the action taken by MAHAGENCO for installation of FGD at various Thermal Power Plants in Maharashtra, plant-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) and (b): Ministry has not issued any notice to Maharashtra Generation Corporation (MAHAGENCO) for non-installation of Flue Gas Desulphurisation (FGD) at Koradi Thermal Power Plant.Ministry vide its notification dated 31.03.2021 has provided revised timeline for compliance of emission standards of 2015 by coal based Thermal Power Plants (Annexure–I).  (c):The details of FGD installation status in various thermal power plant units of MAHAGENCO, Maharashtra are at Annexure–II.***
