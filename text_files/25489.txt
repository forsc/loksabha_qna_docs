                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1237






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF)




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of dairy farmers in the State ofMadhya Pradesh who are beneficiaries of AnimalHusbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF)or are registered thereunder;(b) the details of steps taken by the Government tocover the maximum number of potential beneficiaries;and(c) the outcome of the efforts made by theGovernment in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	Total 20 number applications were registered in the portal from Madhya Pradesh under Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF). Out of 20 applications, four applications were found eligible and two applications were sanctioned for loan by the banks. (b)	The Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying has been taking various steps to cover maximum beneficiaries. Some of the steps taken by the Departments to popularize the scheme are as under:(i)	Credit Guarantee fund trust for Animal Husbandry & Dairying (CGFTAHD) has been established and Funds of Rs 109.00 crore were released during two years.(ii) Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for Banks issued and uploaded on portal.(iii) Model DPR’s for Cattle feed unit/ Silage manufacturing unit and dairy product manufacturing unit uploaded on portal for facilitation of entrepreneurs.(iv) Inclusion of Breed improvement technology and Breed Multiplication farm as an additional category- Establishment of IVF Centers, Sex sorted semen facility and Breed Multiplication farm (v) To provide the hand holding at the grass root level, Common Service Centre has been integrated with AHIDF portal.(vi) Guidelines have been translated into 13 languages.(vii) The documentary has been made in 13 languages.(viii) Meeting with the Banks has been held along with DFS on the pending applications. (ix) Credit Extension & Publicity Division of Department of Animal Husbandry Department, as part of “AzadiKaAmritmahotsav” has organized a webinar on "Easy credit availability to livestock and dairy farmers under AtmaNirbhar Bharat Abhiyan through “Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund”. (x) An Awareness Webinar has been taking place with the potential Beneficiaries like Individuals/ Farmers Producer Organizations, MSME etc.(c) With the efforts made by the Department of Animal Husbandry, the achievement made so far is as under:1	Total application received on the portal	9522	Applications eligible as per AHIDF guidelines 	Dairy processing 54 (Rs. 656.80cr)Meat processing 8 (Rs.179.25 cr)Animal Feed Plant 56 (Rs. 409.76cr)Total   118 applications (Loan amount of Rs. 1245.81 cr.)  3	Total project cost for applications marked eligible by the Department 	Rs.  1692.55 Crores4	Total Loan for applications marked eligible by the Department 	Rs. 1245.81 crores5.	Applications sanctioned by Banks	346.	Total loan amount sanctioned by banks against 34 eligible applications 	Rs 757.96 Crores7.	Total project cost for 34 applications sanctioned by banks	Rs 1001.42 Crores8	Total project cost for which interest subvention has been released during 2020-2021against 10 projects	Rs 661.85 crores9	Total loan sanctioned for which interest subvention has been released during 2020-2021against 10 projects	Rs 493.97 Crores10 	Total interest subvention released during 2020-2021	Rs 12.74 Crores
