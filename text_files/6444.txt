                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Subsidies for Kerosene and Cooking Gas




Bache Gowda B.N. 














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of amount spent on subsidies for kerosene and cooking gas during the last three years; and(b) the number of States where the Direct Benefit Transfer for Kerosene (DBTK) or cooking gas subsidy is provided?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)The details of subsidies reimbursed by the Government on PDS Kerosene and Domestic LPG for the last three years are given below:(` Crore)Particulars of subsidy reimbursement2016-172017-182018-19PDS Kerosene8,7818,6964,296Domestic LPG13,00013,12216,570(b)The Direct Benefit Transfer for Kerosene (DBTK) Scheme is under implementation in entire State of Jharkhand with effect from 1st July, 2017. For cooking gas, the PAHAL (DBTL) scheme, 2014 has been implemented in the entire country with effect from 1st January 2015 wherein the subsidy on Domestic LPG is being transferred to eligible consumers directly to their bank account.x-x-x-x-x
