                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

216






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Setting up of FPIs




Kanakmal Katara













                                                    Keshari Devi Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of persons who have applied for setting up of Food Processing Industries (FPIs) in Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh separately during the years 2018-19 and 2019-20 along with the details in this regard; (b) 	the number of applications sanctioned in Uttar Pradesh and district-wise in Rajasthan including Banswara - Dungarpur along with the details of the sanctioned applications;(c) 	the amount of budget allocated/utilized for setting up of Food Processing Industries in Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh separately during the years 2018-19 and 2019-20; and(d) 	the percentage of subsidy provided by the Government for setting up of Food Processing Industries? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (d): A statement is laid on the table of the House.*****  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *216 FOR ANSWER ON 09TH MARCH, 2021 REGARDING “SETTING UP OF FPIs”(a):	The number of applications received during FY 2018-19 and 2019-20 for setting up food processing industries/ units/ projects in the state of Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh under different component schemes of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) is placed at Annexure -I.(b):	The details of projects approved under PMKSY during FY 2018-19 and 2019-20 in the state of Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan is placed at Annexure II and Annexure III respectively.(c):	The component schemes of PMKSY are demand driven and therefore, state wise budget is not allocated under these schemes. However, the amount of grants-in-aid released for the projects in Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh during the 2018-19 and 2019-20 is placed at Annexure IV.(d):	Financial assistance provided in the form of Grant-in-Aid for setting up of food processing industries varies under the different components of the scheme. The details are placed at Annexure V.*****
