                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3190






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Ayurvedic Council of India




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:a)	whether Government proposes to constitute Ayurvedic Council of India on the lines of Medical Council of India;b)	if so, the details thereof and, if not, the reasons therefor; andc)	the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) to (c):  There is no proposal to constitute Ayurvedic Council of India. However, the National Commission for Indian System of Medicine (NCISM) has been constituted on 11th June, 2021 with the main function to regulate the education and practice of Indian System of Medicine (Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha and Sowa-Rigpa Systems of Medicine) under the provisions of the NCISM Act, 2020. In pursuance of the provisions of NCISM Act,2020, the Indian Medicine Central Council Act, 1970 has been superseded and the Central Council of Indian Medicine established thereunder was dissolved with effect from 11th June, 2021.
