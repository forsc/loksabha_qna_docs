                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1802






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






New Road Projects




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the Government proposes to award 68 road projects worth about Rs. 5 lakh crore in the next three months and if so, the details thereof; and(b) the total number of road projects awarded since April, 2019 till date along with the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Award of road projects covering a length of 4,471 km with an approximate cost of Rs. 50,000 crore has been targeted during next three months. (b)	168 road projects covering length of 3215 km have been awarded since April, 2019 till date.*****
