                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

529






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Distribution of Medicinal Plants




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to distribute medicinal plants across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Assam and the reasons therefor; (c) the details of the estimated fund required for this purpose;(d) whether the Government has fixed any target under this mission and if so, the details thereof; and (e) whether the Government has plan to run any campaign for the purpose across the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a & b):Yes Sir, presently, the National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB), Ministry of Ayush, Government of India is implementing a Central Sector Scheme on “Central Sector Scheme for Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants”.under which project based support is provided for creation of Nursery/Quality Planting Material etc. for distribution of medicinal plants saplings. Also, National Medicinal Plants Board, Ministry of Ayush is distributing medicinal plants among farmers, students, general public etc. under ‘Ayush Aapke Dwar’ campaign as a part of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav throughout the country. The state-wise details of medicinal plants saplings distributed including Assam is at Annexure-I.(c): The fund for creating and distributing medicinal plant saplings is provided as per guideline’s provision of “Central Sector Scheme for Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants”.(d & e): Under ‘Ayush Aapke Dwar’ campaign, a target of distribution of 75 Lakhs saplings of medicinal plants fixed for one year. Medicinal plants saplings of Tulsi, Giloe, Ashwagandha, Amla, Bael, Sahjan etc. have been distributed among farmers, students, and households through various supported projects under “Central Sector Scheme for Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants”. At state level the campaign is also facilitated by Regional-Cum Facilitation Centres and State Medicinal Plants Boards.
