                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

376






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






City Gas Infrastructure




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government has received as much as 430 bids for city gas infrastructure in different geographical areas and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether it is also a fact that Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board has authorized 228 geographical areas in 27 States and Union territories covering approximately 53 per cent of the geographical area and 70 per cent of its population and if so, the details thereof; and (c) whether it is also true that so far 11 rounds have been held for the award of city gas infrastructure and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)	to (c)Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) is the authority to grant authorization to the entities for developing of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in Geographical Areas (GAs) as per PNGRB Act, 2006.Upto the 10th CGD bidding round, PNGRB has authorized 228 GAs, comprising 407 districts in 27 States/UTs covering 53 per cent of the geographical area and 70 per cent of its population, for the development of CGD networks. PNGRB has launched 11th CGD Bidding Round on 17.09.2021 and has received 439 bids from 26 entities against 61 GAs. Letter of Intents (LoI) for 52 GAs have been issued by PNGRB to 13 entities. Details of GAs authorized after the 10 bidding round (round wise) are at Annexure****
