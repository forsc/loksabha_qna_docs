                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Bullet-Proof Jackets




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that there is a continuous decline in the quality of life saving bulletproof jackets meant for the Defence forces of the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(c) the details of the manufacturer of these jackets along with the quality norms for it and the number of jackets provided to the Defence forces so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b):  No, Sir.(c)  A contract for procurement of 1,86,138 Bulletproof Jackets (BPJs), through Capital route, has been concluded with M/s SMPP Pvt. Ltd., in April, 2018 and against it, 1,00,000 BPJs have been received so far.  The quality assurance of BPJs is carried out by Directorate General of Quality Assurance (DGQA) based on the parameters of General Staff Qualitative Requirements (GSQR) and Acceptance Test Procedures approved by DGQA.*****
