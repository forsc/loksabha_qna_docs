                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

37






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Seed-Hub Centres




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has sanctioned 25 seed-hub Centres across the country for increasing availability of quality seeds of nutria-cereals (Millets); (b)	if so, the details thereof and the location of such seed-hub Centres, State-wise; (c)	whether the Government has released any payment to States as a part of this initiative; and (d)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 37   DUE FOR REPLY ON 4TH FEBRUARY, 2020.(a) to (d):	 The Government of India has sanctioned 25 seed hub Centres across the country for increasing the availability of quality seeds of Nutri-Cereals (Millets) under the Project “Creation of Seed-Hubs for increasing Indigenous Production of Millets in India”, which is being implemented by ICAR- Indian Institute of Millets Research (IIMR), Hyderabad. Out of the  total outlay of Rs.964.10 lakhs under the National Food Security Mission (NFSM)-Nutri-Cereals, an amount of Rs.723.08 lakhs has been released during 2019-20 to IIMR, Hyderabad for seed hubs.  For 19 out of these 25 centres, funds have been allocated for infrastructure development for seed processing, seed production and storage of seeds. Details of locations of these centres in different states along with funds allocated to each centre is annexed. 	 The remaining 6 centres located at UAS, Bengaluru, Karnataka; AICRP Sorghum-MPKV Rahuri, Maharashtra; AICRP Sorghum, PDKV, Akola, Maharashtra; VNMKV, Parbhani, Maharashtra; PTJSAU, Palem, Telangana; and OUAT, Berhampur, Odisha, which are already implementing seed hub programme for pulses / oilseeds, have been identified for quality seed production of Nutri-Cereals (Millets) also by utilizing existing infrastructure facilities under pulses / oilseeds.
