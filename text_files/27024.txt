                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2588






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Army Recruitment Camps




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of recruitments for Armed Forces made by Army Recruiting Offices in different states during the last three years and the current year and the State-wise details thereof;(b) the number of recruitment camps organized in Uttar Pradesh, especially in Bundelkhand during the last five years, district-wise;(c) whether the Government proposes to organize new army recruitment camps in Bundelkhand;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) if not, the reasons therefor; and(f) the criteria to be adopted for organizing the camps and the number of selected youths in Uttar Pradesh during the said period, and the district-wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) The number of recruitments for Armed Forces made by Army Recruiting Offices in different states during the last three years and the current year and State-wise are at Annexure A.(b)	 Recruitment Rallies conducted in Uttar Pradesh in last five Years:-Year	Uttar Pradesh 	Bundelkhand Area2016-17	10	22017-18	9	22018-19	9	22019-20	8	2(c ) to (e ): Yes, Sir.  Subject to COVID-19.(f) The location of Recruitment Rally is decided from amongst the districts under jurisdiction of the Army Recruiting Office (ARO) in consultation with the civil administration. Recruitment of personnel below officers rank from different states is being done in proportion to the Recruitable Male Population (RMP) of those states.
