                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Old Age Homes




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of old persons in the country and the number of destitutes out of them; (b) whether the Government provides financial assistance to the States to provide shelter to destitute old persons in Old Age Homes; (c) if so, the details thereof;  (d) if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) the steps being taken by the Government to set up old age homes in large numbers for destitute old people in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a): As per 2011 Census Report, the number of Senior Citizens in the country is 10.38 crore ie.,8.6% of the total population.(b) to (e): This Ministry is implementing a Central Sector Scheme namely Integrated Programme for Senior Citizens (IPSrC) under which grants in aid is given for setting up, running and maintenance of Senior Citizens Homes (Old Age Homes)/Continuous Care Homes, Mobile Medicare Units etc. to the Implementing Agencies (IAs) such as NGOs/VOs, PRIs, Local Bodies, etc. through Registered Societies.The Government has enacted the Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens (MWPSC) Act, 2007 which inter-alia directs State Governments to establish and maintain old age homes, at least one in each district, to accommodate a minimum of 150 senior citizens, who are indigent.  Besides the provisions of the MWPSC Act, 2007, this Department has also accorded priority in sanctioning of new project proposals of Senior Citizen Homes in un-covered Districts on the basis of recommendations of the State Governments, to expand the coverage of such Homes in all Districts in the country. Number of Senior Citizens Homes (Old Age Homes) being supported under the Scheme of IPSrC, State-UT wise, is at Annexure.*****
