                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

216






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Private Schools Providing Education to Tribal Students




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government provides financial aids/grants to private schools providing    education to Tribal students; (b)	 if so, the details thereof and top 10 private institutes being provided financial aids/grants; (c) 	whether the Government audits the grants and verifies if indeed needy tribals are being provided education and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	whether the Government checks such Private Institutes to see that the culture, heritage, mother tongue of the tribal community is maintained and preserved and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): The Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides financial assistance to Voluntary Organisations / Non-Governmental Organisations under the scheme of ‘Aid to Voluntary Organisations working for the welfare of Scheduled Tribes’ for running and maintenance of residential schools, non-residential schools and educational complexes. Details of top ten organizations which have been given highest amount of funds under the scheme during last three years for running the said schools are at Annex.(c) & (d): To ensure proper utilization of funds by the NGOs/VOs, audited statements of accounts as well as utilization certificate, duly certified by Chartered Accountants, pertaining to previous releases are invariably obtained. The Ministry considers projects under the scheme based upon the annual inspection carried by the District Authorities and scrutiny & recommendation of multidisciplinary State Level Committee every year.
