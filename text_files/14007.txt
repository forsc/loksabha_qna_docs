                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2778






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Transfer Policy in Railways




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a number of employees in the Northern Railways have 	been working over there for more than last ten to fifteen years;(b) the details of rules with regard to postings and transfers in the Railways;(c) the  number  of  such  employees  in  the  Northern  Railways  at present who have not been transferred during the last five years as per rules along with the details of their names and posts;(d) the number of employees associated with various unions in the Northern Railways who have not been transferred for the last five to six years and if so, the details thereof along with their names;(e) whether  the   Government   proposes   to   transfer  the  said employees as per the rules;(f)  if so, the details thereof; and(g) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) 	and (b): The transfer of railway employees is governed by Railway  Board''s  Master Circular  No.24/1991  dated 	08.04.1991 and policy circulars issued from time to time by Railway Board, which  inter alia  prescribe  tenure  rules  for  sensitive  posts,various types of transfer and policy in respect of office bearers of recognized unions.(c) and 	(d)   As  per  the  information  obtained  from   Northern Railway, 388 employees have not been transferred for last five to six years. The details are Appended as Appendix-I. 291 employees are associated with various unions. The details are Appended as Appendix-II.(e) to 	(g): Transfer of the employees of Zonal Railways comes under the purview of General Managers who are required to  take   decision	  as per   the  	instructions  	in	force	  and operational/ administrative requirement.*****
