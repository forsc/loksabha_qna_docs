                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4019






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Yoga centres at Panchayat level




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)













                                                    Pragya Singh Thakur
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA,YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to set up yoga centres at panchayat level in the country including Machhli Shahar district of Uttar Pradesh and Bhopal district of Madhya Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise, districtwise;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the number of these yoga centres currently operational across the country, State/UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c):	As Public Health is a State subject, setting up of yoga centres at panchayat level in the country including Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, Central Government has approved 12,500 AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres which also includes Yoga component, to be operationalized by Ministry of AYUSH through State/UT Governments including Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in a phased manner by the year 2023-24.It is envisaged to upgrade AYUSH Dispensaries and Sub-Health Centres identified by State/UT Governments as AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres for providing AYUSH based preventive, promotive, curative and rehabilitative care. The details of State/UT-wise approved AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) are furnished as Annexure I.(d):	A total of 522 AYUSH HWCs are functional under progressive category across the country. The details of State/UT-wise functional under progressive category AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) are furnished as Annexure II.
