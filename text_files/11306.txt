                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Guidelines for NPR




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has brought out the guidelines for National Population Register (NPR);(b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether it is mandatory to disclose information about Aadhaar, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License, if holding, non-disclosure would attract fine;(d) whether there has been statements from various quarters that furnishing these details are optional or voluntary; and(e) if so, the response of the Government thereto and the reasons for making such information compulsory and also attracting fine, if not-disclosed?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	An instruction manual for updation of National Population Register (NPR) 2020 for enumerators and supervisors has been prepared.  (c) to (e):	The NPR updation will be undertaken through house to house enumeration for collection of specified particulars relating to each family and individual.  The respondent has to provide the information true to the best of his knowledge and belief.  Aadhar Number is collected voluntarily.   *********
