                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

22






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






International Airport at Jewar, U.P.




K. Shanmuga Sundaram













                                                    P. Velusamy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has finalised the deal for an international airport at Jewar in Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details of the bidders and the expected date of completion of the project as per their bidding;(c) whether the main bidder has given sub-contract to any other authority to implement the project;(d) if so, the name of the sub-contractor or any Special Purpose Vehicle who would be engaged to carry out the project;(e) the estimated cost and the number of passengers expected to utilise the new airport on completion of the project; and(f) the status of the existing IGI Airport, Delhi and its utilisation after completion of the above said airport?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Jyotiraditya M. Scindia)(a) to (f): A statement is laid on the table of the House.Statement in reply to parts (a) to (f) of the Starred (*) Question No. 22 for answer on 03.02.2022 regarding ''International Airport at Jewar, U.P.'';  (a) to (e): Government of India granted ''in principle'' approval to Government of Uttar Pradesh (GoUP) on 08.05.2018 for setting up a new greenfield international airport at Jewar, Noida. GoUP appointed Yamuna Expressway Industrial Development Authority (YEIDA) as the implementing agency for the project under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode. YEIDA completed the bidding process wherein M/s Zurich Airport International AG emerged as the successful bidder. GoUP subsequently awarded the project to M/s Zurich Airport International AG for development of the airport. M/s Zurich Airport International AG has formed a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) -Yamuna International Airport Private Limited (YIAPL). The Concession Agreement has been signed on 7th October, 2020 between YIAPL and Noida International Airport Limited (NIAL), a State Government Company. ; ; GoUP has informed that the task of acquisition of 1334 hectare of land for Phase-I of the project has been completed and the possession of the land has been obtained. The Concessionaire has initiated selection of Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) Contractor through tendering. ; As per the Master Plan submitted by YIAPL, the forecast for initial year traffic is 4 Million Passengers Per Annum (MPPA) for the airport against the initial capacity of 12 MPPA for Phase I. The estimated project cost for Phase I is Rs. 8914 crore including the land acquisition cost. As per the Concession Agreement, the scheduled completion date of airport is 29th September, 2024.  ; (f): The IGI Airport, Delhi is an operational airport. Government of India has granted ''In-Principle'' approval for setting up of a new greenfield international airport at Jewar, Noida as a second airport to serve the need of Delhi National Capital Region. IGI Airport, Delhi will continue to remain operational.
