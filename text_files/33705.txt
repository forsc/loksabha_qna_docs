                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1127






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






National Investigation Agency




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering setting up a National Investigation Agency (NIA) Permanent House in Bengaluru;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for the establishment of the NIA Permanent House;(c) the details of the resources and funds that will be made available for the setting up of the NIA Permanent House in Bengaluru; and(d) the total number of NIA Permanent Houses in the country and the cities they are located in?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d):  In pursuance of the policy of “Zero Tolerance” towards terrorism, Central Government has strengthened the institutional framework  and enhanced capacity of concerned agencies which  include provision of adequate funds. The Central Government, on 5th January, 2022, sanctioned 6 new branch offices, including at Bengaluru for the National Investigation Agency (NIA). 52 posts have been sanctioned  for its newly created branch office at Bengaluru.With the sanction of 6 more branches, the NIA, with headquarters at New Delhi, has 18 branch offices at Hyderabad, Guwahati, Imphal, Kochi, Chennai, Lucknow, Kolkata, Jammu, Raipur, Chandigarh, Ranchi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Bhopal, Patna, Ahmedabad, Jaipur and Bhubaneswar.*****
