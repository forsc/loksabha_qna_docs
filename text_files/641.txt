                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

475






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Terrorist Hideouts




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the terrorist hideouts detected and their locations in Kashmir during the last three years;(b) the number of terrorist nabbed under the ‘cordon and search operations’; and(c) the details of the action taken against them and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a)	 to (c) The Government has adopted a policy of zero tolerance towards terrorism. Quick Cordon and Search Operations are conducted by the Security Forces after gathering information on terrorists in a proactive manner. During 2016-2018, 97 terrorists have been nabbed in these Cordon and Search operations, and action initiated under relevant provisions of law. Security Forces have busted nearly 29 terrorist hideouts in Kashmir valley during 2016 - 2018. *****
