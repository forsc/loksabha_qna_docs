                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3648






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Construction of Roads in Maharashtra




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of roads built in Maharashtra including those under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during each of the last three years; and(b)	the status of the roads under construction?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The details of roads built in Maharashtra under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during each of the last three years are as follows:Year 	       No. of road & bridge works	Length (in km)2016-17	              213	                  2000.702017-18	              191	                   569.762018-19	               99	                   266.83(b):	Since inception till 12.07.2019, out of 6,876 road & bridge works covering a length of 27,713.909 km sanctioned under PMGSY, 6,572 road & bridge works covering 26,329.546 km has been constructed. State has reported that 261 road and bridge works covering 633 km are in progress.*****
