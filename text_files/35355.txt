                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2482






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Recruitment Drives




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of recruitment drives undertaken by Railways during the last three years, Zone-wise; (b)	the number of regular employees appointed by Railways through these recruitment drives;(c)	the number of recruitment drives for which the results are yet to be declared;(d)	the number of candidates waiting for appointment even after the declaration of results;(e)	the details of original time-limit prescribed by the Railways for the declaration of results and the reasons for the delay in this regard; and (f)	the details of recruitment drives for which eligibility conditions were amended by Railways after the submission of applications by candidates?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (f):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2482 BY SHRI NAMA NAGESWARA RAO TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 16.03.2022 REGARDING RECRUITMENT DRIVES(a) to (c): 	The details are appended.(d):	Offer of appointments are given by Zonal Railways / Production Units to all the empanelled candidates subject to candidates satisfying all eligibility criteria, including character and antecedents.(e):	No time limit is prescribed for declaration of Results in the Centralized Employment Notifications (CENs).(f):	No eligibility conditions were amended after the submission of applications by candidates.*****
