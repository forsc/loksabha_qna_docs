                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Sports Quota




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to introduce a National Sports Board toensure education for sports persons and if so, the details thereof and if not, thereasons therefor;(b) the number of seats available for sports quota in Central University(s), along withthe details for the last three years; and(c) the number of students enrolled in Delhi University from sports quota, during thelast five years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Ministry of Human Resource Development does not have any proposal tointroduce a National Sports Board.(b): As per information received from Central Universities under administrativecontrol of Ministry of Human Resource Development, the details regarding number ofseats available for sports quota in central universities for the last three years isannexed.(c): The number of students enrolled in Delhi University from sports quota is givenhereunder.<pre>Year Admission under Sports Quota2016 12332017 14512018 13512019 1298</pre>****
