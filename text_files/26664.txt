                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2378






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Timely Payment to Workers under MGNREGS




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number or people provided employment under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) by the Government, if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether payment to MGNREGA workers of Mainpur under Gariaband district of Chhattisgarh have been paid on time like other places in 2021, if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	whether there is any case of negligence in payment has come to the sight of the Government, if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme which provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. In the current financial year 2021-22 (as on 31.07.2021), 6.78 crore persons were provided employment under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA). In this regard, States/UTs-wise detail of the employment provided to persons under programme is given at Annexure.(b)	Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is a demand driven wage employment guarantee programme. During the current Financial Year 2021-22 (as on 31.07.2021), around 99.96 % Fund Transfer Orders (FTOs) have been generated within 15 days from the date of closure of the Muster Roll in the State of Chhattisgarh. The details of payment Fund Transfer order (FTO) generation for workers of Mahatma Gandhi NREGA of Mainpur under Gariaband district of Chhattisgarh   have been provided by the State Government of Chhatisgarh as under: Months	             T +8 days	          T+15 days	More then15 daysJanuary, 2021	       98.73%	           0.92%	    0.35February, 2021	       97.66%	           0.81%	    1.53March, 2021	       95.59%	           2.39%	    2.03%April, 2021	       99.84%	           0.16%	      0May, 2021	       99.64%	           0.04%	      0June, 2021	       99.73%	           0.27%	      0July, 2021	       99.75%	           0.25%	      0Here “T” is the date of closure of muster roll.  (c)	State Government of Chhatisgarh has informed that no case of negligence in payment has come to the sight of the Government.*****
