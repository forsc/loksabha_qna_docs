                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

820






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Closure of VOC Port at Thoothukudi




 Kunhalikutty P.K.














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that VOC port, Thoothukudi, has been closed down since last few years;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the logical importance of the port; and(c)	the details of workers affected due to closure of VOC port and the efforts being taken to re-open the port?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	No, Sir.(b)	Does not arise. VOC Port is strategically located close to the east west international sea route. The Port has a maximum draft of 14.20 meters and it can handle bulk vessels of capacity upto 95,000 DWT. Port is well connected with 6 lane road connecting NH 45 B and NH 138. It mainly serves a primary hinterland of South Tamil Nadu, secondary hinterland to Coimbatore, Tirupur region and distant hinterland to North Tamil Nadu.(c)	Does not arise. 						        *****
