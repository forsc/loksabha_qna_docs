                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2041






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Strike by Staff of Spicejet




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the strike called by the Employees of Spicejet Airlines;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government collects advance information about such issues in private airlines to prevent closure of airlines and the loss to the Banks and the public thereby; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) and (b): The Airline has informed that there had not been any strike called by employees of Spicejet. However, there have been a few instances of protest by staff at some places / stations.  (c) & (d): Each airline prepares its business plan on the basis of their own market assessment and liabilities. Based on their business plan, the efficient operations and financial resources are the responsibility of the Airlines.
