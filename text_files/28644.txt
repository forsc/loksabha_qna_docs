                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

551






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






National Design Institutes




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of National Design Institutes in the country, State/Union Territory-wise;(b) 	whether the Government is considering to establish a National Design Institute in Rajasthan;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a): 	Sir, Five National Institutes of Design have been established in the country as under: <pre>Name of State/Union Territory	Name of National Institute of DesignGujarat	National Institute of Design, AhmedabadAndhra Pradesh	National Institute of Design, Andhra PradeshHaryana	National Institute of Design, HaryanaMadhya Pradesh	National Institute of Design, Madhya PradeshAssam	National Institute of Design, Assam </pre> (b) to (d): 	The National Design Policy, 2007 envisaged that four new National Institutes of Design shall be set up in addition to the National Institute of Design, Ahmedabad. Accordingly, Government of India has recently established four new National Institutes of Design, one each in Andhra Pradesh, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh and Assam to cater to the requirements of different regions of country. At present, there is no plan to further set up new National Institute of Design in the country.********
