                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3070






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






PMAY in Karnataka




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana in Karnataka,  particularly Bengaluru;(b)      the number of houses constructed in Karnataka, particularly Bengaluru under this Yojana;(c)     whether the Government has any plans to extend the timeline given for this Yojana;(d)      if so, the details thereof; and(e)     the targets fixed for the State under the Yojana, particularly for Bengaluru city and other districts along with achievements made there under so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                             (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b): Status of progress along with number of houses constructed 00under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban){PMAY(U)} in the State of Karnataka including that of Bengaluru are at Annexure. (c)& (d): No, Sir. PMAY (U) aims to provide central assistance to States/Union Territories (UTs) in providing housing and basic civic amenities to the urban poor including slum dwellers of the urban areas in the country by the year 2022.(e): Under PMAY (U), Government of India does not fix any target for construction of houses in a State/UT.   Houses under PMAY (U) are sanctioned for eligible beneficiaries based on the project proposals submitted to the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs with due approval of the State/UT Governments. States/UTs are required to undertake a demand assessment and validate the same to determine the actual requirement of houses to be built under PMAY (U) during the period of “Housing for All by 2022” Mission. District-wise progress made under PMAY (U) in Karnataka including that of Bengaluru is given at Annexure.                                           ****
