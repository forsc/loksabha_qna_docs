                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

264






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Assistance for Combating Illicit Trafficking inDrugs




Kuruva Gorantla Madhav













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has issued Revised Guidelines of the Scheme for “Assistance to States and UTs” for strengthening their enforcement capabilities in combating illicit trafficking in Narcotics Drugs and Psychotropic substances;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the amount allocated and released to the State of Andhra Pradesh under the above scheme during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	& (b) Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Home Affairs had issued revised guidelines in respect of the scheme for “Assistance to States and UTs” vide communication No. I-12020/52/2017-NCB-I dated 05th January, 2018. Under the scheme, financial assistance is provided to States for strengthening their enforcement capabilities in combating illicit trafficking in Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances and for capacity building activities including training of personnel. Assistance is provided for procurement of (i) Surveillance equipment; (ii) Laboratory equipment; (iii) Vehicles for patrolling/surveillance; (iv)Computers and accessories; (v) Fax machine and photocopiers and (vi) Training & other equipments useful for enforcement.-2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.264 FOR 15.09.2020(c) During the period from 2017-18 to 2019-20, a total amount of   Rs. 1,10,94,111/- has been released to the State of Andhra Pradesh  as financial assistance under the scheme.*****
