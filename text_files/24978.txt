                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Environment Conservation




Balak Nath














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the schemes being implemented by the Government for the environment conservation;(b)	whether any subsidy or grant for opening nurseries and encouraging plantation of trees in Rajasthan is being implemented by the Government;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the other steps being taken by the Government for environment conservation? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) The Government of India through the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change is implementing various schemes for environment conservation like National Mission for a Green India; Integrated Development of Wildlife Habitats; Conservation of Natural Resources and Ecosystem; National Plan for Conservation of Aquatic Ecosystems; Biodiversity Conservation; Conservation of Corals and Mangroves; Development and Promotion of Clean Technology and Waste Minimisation Strategies; and National Green Corps Programme. Funds collected towards compensatory levies are also used for various activities aimed at forest and environment conservation.(b) and (c) A sum of Rs. 1.40 crore and Rs. 1.95 crore has been released as a central share for undertaking implementation of the National Afforestation Programme in Rajasthan in the year 2017-18 and 2018-19 respectively. Besides, Rs.1748.26 crores have been transferred to State Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) of Rajasthan as share of State Government from National Authority, CAMPA in the year 2019-20 for carrying out various forest and wildlife conservation and development activities including afforestation and raising of nurseries.(d) Government of India implements several policies/programmes and initiatives for environment conservation like Nagar Van Yojana, National Clean Air Programme (NCAP), Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojna (PMUY), National Solar Mission, Smart Cities Mission, Green Highway Policy, National Green Aviation Policy, National Water Mission, Jal Jeevan Mission, Jal Shakti Abhiyan, Swachh Bharat Mission and National Electric Mobility Mission Plan (NEMMP).****
