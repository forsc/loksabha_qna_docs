                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1573






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Digi-Gaon Project




V. Kalanidhi













                                                    Andimuthu Raja
                                                








                                                    S. Ramalingam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology be pleased to state: (a)	the salient features of Digi-Gaon Project;(b)	the number of villages covered initially under the project across the country, State/UT-wise including the State of Tamil Nadu;(c)	the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this project since its inception across the country, State/UT-wise including the State of Tamil Nadu;(d)	whether the Government proposes to expand this project across the country and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including the State of Tamil Nadu;(e)	whether the Government proposes to expand Common Service Centres (CSCs) under the aforesaid project and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including the State of Tamil Nadu along with the funds likely to be sanctioned for the same; and(f) whether the Government proposes to include entrepreneurship opportunities for the rural residents specially women under the said project and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	and (b):  Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) initiated the Digital Village Pilot Project titled “Pilot Project- Implementation of Digital Village” on 31st October 2018. The project is being implemented by CSC eGovernance Services India Limited. 700 Gram Panchayats(GP)/Village with atleast one GP/village from each district of the State/UT are being covered under the pilot implementation of the project. The following are the salient feature of the project-i.	Government to Citizens Services (G2C) - Pan Card Services, New Aadhaar enrolment, Updation and modification, Online registration for service selection board, Online registration for public service commission, online registration for Board of professional entrance examination.ii.	B2C Services – Mobile / DTH recharge Ticket Booking (Rail/Air), etc.iii.	Education Service: Courses offered under this project are as (i) Basic Computer Course (BCC), (ii) Course on Computer Concept (CCC), (iii) Tally – Kaushal Praman Patraiv.	Health Services – Tele-Health &  Tele-Veterinary consultation v.	Financial Inclusion- Financial inclusion Awareness programme  vi.	Solar panel powered street lights (8 Solar lights per village)vii.	Skill Development - skill development in (i) Automotive Technician, (ii) Handset repairing, (iii) Field Technician- Home Appliances, and (iv) Electrical Technician.700 Village across India from the State/UT are covered under the pilot implementation of the project. 32 villages one each from each of the 32 district of Tamil Nadu are being covered under Digital Village Pilot Project. State/UT wise list of the villages is enclosed at Annexure-I.(c): The total budget outlay of the project is Rs.98.324Crores for the Project duration 36 months. The Project does not provision funds for State Governments or their agencies. So far, Rs.20.0 Crore has been released to the project implementing agency and Rs.11.178Crore has been utilized under the project. The tentative funds earmarked from MeitY for the State of Tamil Nadu to cover 32 villages under “Pilot Project- Implementation of Digital Village” is Rs.319.8lakhs. The State/UT wised funds utilization, as received from CSC eGovernance Service India Limited (Project Implementing Agency), is enclosed at Annexure-II.(d):  The Digital Village Pilot project titled “Pilot Project- Implementation of Digital Village” is already being implemented across India as per details mentioned in Annexure-I (e):  CSC eGovernance Services India Limited (CSC-SPV) is the implementing Agency for the “Pilot Project – Implementation of Digital Village” and there is no provision to expand CSCs under the said project. (f):   Under the project “Pilot Project – Implementation of Digital Village”, there is no specific provision for women.
