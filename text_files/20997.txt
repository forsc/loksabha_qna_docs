                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






IGST Refund Cases




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that there has been huge pile up of IGST refund cases and that the claimants have lost hope on getting such refund and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether it is also true that there have been complaints of malpractices at GST headquarters and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has received any complaints in this regard; and (d) if so, the steps taken by the Government on such complaints?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a): No Sir. 98% of IGST refund claims amounting to Rs. 1.78 Lakh Crores have been disposed off.(b) and (c): No Sir.(d): No Sir, in view of answer to (b) and (c) above.******
