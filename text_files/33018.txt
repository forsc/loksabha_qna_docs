                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Fund Allocation under the Scheme for Public Health Initiatives




Sunil Kumar Soni













                                                    Vijay Baghel
                                                








                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds allocated by the Government across the country under the Public Health Initiatives Scheme for Ayush; (b)	the details of funds allocated and the name of institutions, Government and Non-Government to whom it was allocated across the country, State-wise including Chhattisgarh; (c)	the number of AYUSH practitioners who are currently providing their services under this scheme, State-wise; and (d)	the diseases being treated and the centers where it is being treated successfully through the said scheme, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a), (b), (c)  The Public Health Initiatives Scheme is a component of Central Sector Scheme namely AYURSWASTHA Yojanaw.e.f. 14.08.2021.  Under this components funds are directly provided to the Organizations (Government/Non-Government).  Fund of Rs.4.90 Crores (Grant-In-aid-General) was allocated each year under erstwhile PHI scheme during FY-2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20 & 2020-21.  	The details of funds released by the Government across the country along with name of institutions & type of Institutions (Government & Non-Government) under the Public Health Initiatives during the last three years are at Annexure.  	Large number of AYUSH service providers including the relevant teaching faculties, AYUSH practitioners engaged in the project, the post graduate AYUSH students, nurses as well as supporting staff are providing their services to the public. (d)  The details of diseases being treated and centers where it is being treated through the scheme are shown at Column 6 of Annexure. *****
