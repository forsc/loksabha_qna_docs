                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4214






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Demolition of Hindu Temples




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the reason for demolition of Hindu Temples in Odisha, when Indonesia, a Muslim country can protect the temples, which are symbols of the Indian culture;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any measures have been taken to protect the heritage of the country or any steps have been taken for conducting heritage survey; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)& (b)	There are 80 Centrally Protected Monuments/Sites in the State of Odisha. There is no instance of demolition of any Centrally Protected Monument. (c)& (d)	All   Centrally Protected Monuments/ Site are governed by the Ancient Monuments and   Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958 (AMASR Act) and Rules 1959 made there under.   Regular watch and ward staff is deployed at monuments as well as private   security guards at selected monuments. Field offices of Archaeological Survey   of India conducts regular survey/explorations to identify and discover the   historical places, monuments/sites, etc. which is a regular phenomenon.  Declaring of monuments as Monuments of National importance is a continuous process and is done under the provisions of AMASR Act.  *****
