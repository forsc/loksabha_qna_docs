                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3967






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Construction of ROBs




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State-wise number of functional and under-construction Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) in the country including Chhattisgarh;(b)	the State-wise number of accidents that occurred for want of ROBs in the country along with the number of deaths/ injured in the said accidents during the last three years and the current year, State-wise;(c)	the corrective steps taken/being taken by the Government to construct more Railway Over Bridges in the country;(d)	the details of the targets fixed for completion of the said projects;(e)	the details of the proposals sent by various State Governments including Chhattisgarh during the last three years; and(f)	the details of the funds allocated for this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)   (a) to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
