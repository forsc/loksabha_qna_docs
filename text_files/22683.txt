                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3847






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Strengthening of NCC and NSS




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether National Cadet Corps (NCC) and the National Service Scheme(NSS) have been playing a key role in empowering the youth; (b) if so, the details thereof and the initiatives taken by the Government to encourage NCC and NSS students across India specially in Uttar Pradesh; (c) whether there is any lack of synergy between NCC and NSS in empowering the youth and if so, the steps taken/being taken by the Government to revamp and strengthen NCC and the NSS and the outcome of the meeting convened for this purpose; (d) the details of the role and responsibilities of the Government in this regard; and (e) whether the Government proposes to suggest way to reform and strengthen NCC and NSS and if so, the details along with the composition and mandate thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) & (b): Yes Sir. National Cadet Corps (NCC) and National Service Scheme (NSS) have been playing key role in empowering youth. The details of activities in respect of NCC and NSS are placed at Annexure-I.(c) & (d):	There is no lack of synergy between NCC and NSS. A National Coordination Committee of all Youth Organisations (NCCYO) has been constituted. The details of the NCCYO is placed at Annexure-II.  (e) 	The details of initiatives to strengthen NCC and NSS are placed at Annexure-III. 	*********
