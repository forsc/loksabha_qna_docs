                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Skill Development Institute on the Line of ITI




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether  the Government proposes to set up Skill Development Institutes on the lines of Industrial Training Institutes(ITI);(b)	if so, the number of districts of Telangana in which Skill Development Institutes are proposed to be set up and whether the said institutes are also proposed to be set up in Zahirabad Parliamentary Constituency;(c)	the names of the places where such institutes are likely to come up includingtheirnumber;(d)	the time by which these institutes are likely to be set up; and(e)	if not, the reasons there for?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) 	Under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, aspirational model training centres for short term training called Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKKs) are being set up in every district, ensuring coverage of all the Parliamentary Constituencies (PCs). Currently, 721 PMKK have been set up. Directorate General of Training, an attached office under the Ministry of Skill Development 	& Entrepreneurship is implementing as scheme titled “Skill Development in 47 Districts affected by Left Wing Extremism (LWE)”, under which Government of India is funding for establishment of 47 new Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in 47 districts,1 ITI per district, of 10 States and 68 Skill Development Centres (SDCs) in 34 districts, 2 SDCs per district, of 9 States in the LWE affected districts in the country. (b)	As on 10th July 2021, 721 PMKKs have already been established. Of these 29 PMKKs are established in Telangana including the Medak District of Zahirabad Parliamentary Constituency. State-wise detail of PMKK including of Zahirabad Parliamentary Constituency is given in Annexure-I.	There are 16,769 Training Centres (Central and State Component) under PMKVY 2.0 and PMKVY 3.0 across the country.  549 Training Centres (Central and State Component) under PMKVY 2.0 and PMKVY 3.0 are in Telangana.  Out of which 19 are in Zahirabad Parliamentary Constituency.  Central and State component details is given at Annexure-II.Under the scheme titled “Skill Development in 47 Districts affected by Left Wing Extremism (LWE)” one district, Khammam of Telangana is covered. ITI at Kothagudem and 02 SDCs at Bhadrachalam and  Wazedu in Khammam District of Telangana are under various stages of construction. There is no proposal to setup such institutes in Zahirabad Parliamentary Constituency.(c) 	The details of districts covered under the scheme titled “Skill Development in 47 Districts affected by Left Wing Extremism (LWE)” are given at Annexure-III.(d)	The period of implementation of the scheme titled “Skill Development in 47 Districts affected by Left Wing Extremism (LWE)” was upto 31st March, 2021. However, Standing Finance Committee (SFC) in its meeting dated 16.02.2021 recommended the continuation of the scheme beyond March, 2021 upto March, 2024. (e) 	In view of the above, question doesn’t arise.
