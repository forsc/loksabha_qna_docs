                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2226






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






IIM




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is working along with the Indian Institute of Management(IIM) towards Women empowerment through any programme;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of women who will be benefitted under this programme; and(c) the States where this programme is being conducted?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c):	Women empowerment is the stated objective of the Government. The female student enrolment in IIMs for the year 2018 is 1404, for the year 2019 the female student enrolment is 1748 and for the year 2020 the female student enrolment is 2421. The female students after passing out from IIMs hold leadship position in various organisations.   *****
