                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3595






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Food Distribution through Mobile App




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether food items at ration shops are proposed to be distributed to the eligible beneficiaries through Mobile App; (b) 	whether poor people who cannot afford mobile expenditure are likely to face difficulties as a result of this decision; (c) 	whether the consumers who are having mobiles are not considered poor; and (d) 	whether the Government proposes to take any measures to solve the problems of the beneficiaries?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (d): 	The Department of Food and Public Distribution in collaboration with all States/UTs is implementing a scheme on ‘End-to-End Computerization of TPDS Operations’ to improve the efficiency and transparency in Public Distribution System (PDS) operations. Under the Scheme, the automation of Fair Price Shops (FPSs) is also being done by the respective States/UTs, which involves the use of Electronic Point of Sale (ePoS) devices at the FPSs for sale of foodgrains and electronic record keeping of all sale transactions and authentication of beneficiaries.  The subsidized foodgrains are being distributed by State/UT Governments to the beneficiaries under NFSA using these ePoS devices which are installed at about 4.44 lakh FPSs out of a total of about 5.34 lakh FPSs in the country.****
