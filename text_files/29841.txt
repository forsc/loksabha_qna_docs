                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Fisheries Start-ups




Sukanta Majumdar














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has started the country‘s first-of-its-kind, a dedicated business incubator to nurture fisheries start-ups in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC), is the implementing agency for fisheries sector incubators;(d)	if so, whether NCDC has identified the first batch of ten incubates from four States;(e)	if so, the details thereof; and(f)  	 the details of other steps being taken in fisheries sector under the central flagship Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) in the country including in the State of West Bengal? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	to (e) :  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) with a highest ever estimate investment of Rs. 20050 crore for a period of 5 years with effect from 2020-21 to 2024-25 in all the States/Union Territories including in the State West Bengal. PMMSY inter-alia provides supports for establishment of Fisheries Incubation Centers. During the year 2020-21, the Department of Fisheries, Government of India has accorded approval to the proposal of National Cooperative Development Corporation(NCDC) at a total cost of Rs.37.15 crore, including setting up of a Fisheries Business Incubation Center and Rs. 3.00 crore has been earmarked for this purpose under PMMSY. NCDC has reported that they have set up the Fisheries Business Incubation Centre at Laxmanrao Inamdar National Academy, Gurugram under PMMSY and this is the first incubation centre under PMMSY. NCDC has reported that they have identified the ten incubatees from four States namely Himachal Pradesh, Maharashtra, Gujarat and Bihar as per the details furnished at Annexure-I. (f) : 	PMMSY is designed to address critical gaps in fish production and productivity, quality, technology, post-harvest infrastructure and management, modernization and strengthening of value chain, traceability and in establishing a robust fisheries management framework for fishers’ welfare. PMMSY inter-alia supports development of inland fisheries and aquaculture including brackishwater aquaculture, development of marine fisheries including mariculture and seaweed cultivation, development of coldwater fisheries in North-eastern and Himalayan States/UTs, development of ornamental and recreational fisheries, development of post harvest and cold chain infrastructure through technology infusion, fish markets and marketing infrastructure, development of deep sea fishing, development of aquatic health management facilities, strengthening of the framework to safeguard and security of fishermen, provision of insurance of fishing vessels and fishermen and provision of livelihood support for fishers during the fish ban period.*****
