                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2076






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Shram Suvidha Portal




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has introduced Shram Suvidha Portal and if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of objectives of the said Portal;(c)whether the Government has implemented all the provisions of the Shram Suvidha Portal;(d)if so, the details thereof; and(e)if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (e):	The Ministry of Labour & Employment has launched a unified Web Portal ‘Shram Suvidha Portal’ on 16.10.2014, which caters to four major Organizations under the Ministry of labour, namely•	Office of the Chief Labour Commissioner (Central)•	Directorate General of Mines Safety•	Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation; and•	Employees’ State Insurance CorporationContd..2/-:: 2 ::Main Features of the Portal are as follows:•	Unique Labour Identification Number (LIN): LIN is allotted to units after the registration to facilitate online inspection & compliance.•	Online Return: Units are now required to file single self-certified and simplified annual return instead of filling separate returns as required earlier.•	Transparent Labour Inspection Scheme through computerized system on risk based criteria.The Unified Shram Suvidha Portal brings transparency and accountability in enforcement of labour laws and ease complexity of compliance. It is reviewed on periodical basis so as to upgrade its existing services as well as to develop new services as per legislative mandate and emerging economic and industrial scenario. ******
