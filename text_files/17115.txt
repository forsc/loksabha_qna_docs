                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Pharmacopoeia Commission for Indian Medicine and Homoeopathy




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has approved the establishment of Pharmacopoeia Commission for Indian Medicine and Homoeopathy under the AYUSH;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it is fact that this Commission will be the agency for the uniform development of standards for AYUSH drugs and formulations to prevent duplication of drug standardization work; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) & (b): Consequent upon approval of the Union Cabinet, Government of India vide Gazette Notification dated 6th July, 2020 has re-established Pharmacopoeia Commission for Indian Medicine & Homoeopathy (PCIM&H) as a subordinate office under Ministry of AYUSH by merging Pharmacopoeia Laboratory for Indian Medicine (PLIM), Ghaziabad and Homoeopathic Pharmacopoeia Laboratory (HPL), Ghaziabad.  Erstwhile PCIM&H was an autonomous organisation established since May, 2010 with addition of Homoeopathy component in March 2014.  Details of the PCIM&H as provided in the Gazette Notification is available at http://egazette.nic.in/(S(lrewcna53tntanlqxfh4fay0))/Digital.aspx. (c) & (d):  Pharmacopoeia Commission for Indian Medicine & Homoeopathy will perform as the central agency to develop, amend, harmonize and lay down the standards of Ayurvedic,Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy drugs; to publish respective Pharmacopoeias and Formularies; and to prevent duplication of drug standardization work as detailed in the Gazette Notification available at http://egazette.nic.in/(S(lrewcna53tntanlqxfh4fay0))/Digital.aspx
