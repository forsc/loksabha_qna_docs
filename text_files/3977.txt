                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3665






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Foreign Funding of NGOs




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the total amount of foreign funds received by various organisations including Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) during each of the last three years;(b) whether any mechanism is in place to monitor and review the activities and functioning of such organisations;(c) if so, the details and findings of the said review and the outcome thereof; and(d) the details of the follow up action taken thereon?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a): Foreign Contribution (FC) received by various associations/NGOs during the last three years is as under:-Financial Year	FC received (Rs. In Crores) 2015-16	17803.21 2016-17	15343.15 2017-18	16894.37 (b): Yes Sir. Monitoring Unit of FCRA Wing monitors the receipt and utilization of foreign contributions.(c) & (d): Registration of more than 14,800 associations has been cancelled during the last five years because they committed violations of the provisions of The FCRA, 2010. Details of such associations are available on the FCRA portal www.fcraonline.nic.in.******
