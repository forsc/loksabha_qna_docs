                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

36






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Paddy Crop




Gangasandra Siddappa Basavaraj














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that paddy farmers from Punjab have sold out record bumper crop of paddy at MSP of Rs. 1,988/- per quintal for the 2020-21 season; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	The Minimum Support Price (MSP) of paddy fixed by Government of India for Kharif Marketing Season 2020-21 is Rs. 1868/qtl for Paddy Common and Rs. 1888/qtl. for Paddy Grade ‘A’ variety. During Kharif Marketing Season 2020-21, a record quantity of 202.77 LMT paddy has been procured in Punjab at MSP fixed by Government of India benefitting 14.89 lakh farmers and Rs. 38,284 Crore paid towards MSP to the farmers of Punjab.*******
