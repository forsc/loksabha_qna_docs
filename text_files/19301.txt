                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

637






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Works Undertaken for Minorities




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the types and names of works that are accorded sanction by the Government under the Ministry; and (b) the details of types and names of works undertaken and carried out and the heads under which assistance has been provided to the people belonging to the minority communities in Madhya Pradesh during the last three years, district-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTRY OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):    The Ministry is implementing Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) in 1300 identified Minority Concentration Areas (MCAs) of the country with the objective of developing socio-economic assets and basic amenities for improving quality of life of the people in these areas.   Priority Sectors under the scheme are education, health, skill development and women centric projects.  Since, 2014-15 till 31.12.2020, the Ministry has accorded approval to projects of Rs.14896.29 crore with central share of Rs.10356.70 crore.    The projects approved include Residential Schools (165), College Buildings (32), School Buildings (1527), Navodaya Vidalayas (6), Additional Class Rooms/Blocks (23233), Smart Classes (9993), Hostels (646), ITI Buildings (95), Polytechnics (13), Skill Centres (15), Health Projects (1932), Unani Medical College (01), Working Women Hostel (22), Drinking Water Supply Projects (18692), Anganwadi Centres (6014), Sadbhav Mandaps (403), Sadbhavana Kendra (01), Common Service Centres (143), Market Sheds (574), Hunar Hubs (8), Sanitation Facilities (5317), Sports Facilities (14). (b):	The Ministry, during the last three years has sanctioned projects of Rs 369.82 crore  with central share of Rs. 221.89 crore to the State of Madhya Pradesh under PMJVK scheme.  The projects approved include 1 project for up-gradation of Medical Education & Multi-specialty facilities at Gandhi Medical College and linked hospitals comprising construction of Centre of Excellence for Orthopedics, construction of Regional Institute of Respiratory Diseases, construction of Nursing Hostel cum College, and up-gradation of Hamidiya Hospital Bhopal; 4 Sadbhav Mandaps, 1 Rain Basera, 1 Residential School, 1 Girls Hostel, 1 Smart Class Room.
