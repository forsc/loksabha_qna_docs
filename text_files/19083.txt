                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

507






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Online Dashboard for PM SVANidhi




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has launched online dashboard of PM Street Vendor’s Atmanirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi) recently and if so, the details thereof;b)	the objectives and salient features of PM SVANidhi and the number of street vendors likely to be benefitted;c)	the number of applications received and sanctioned so far, State-wise;d)	whether Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) is the implementing partner for the scheme and if so, the details thereof; ande)	whether the Government is in the process of preparing a plan to capture socio economic profile of all PM SVANidhi beneficiaries and if so, the details thereof including the tenure of the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS				(SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	The dashboard of PM SVANidhi scheme launched on August 28, 2020 provides a snapshot of various aspects of the Scheme. These include ranking of the Lending institutions, States and cities, categorization of applications by gender and vending activity,number of applications received, sanctioned & disbursed, amount disbursed, digital payments undertaken by the beneficiaries, cash back paid, interest subsidy paid, Letter of Recommendation (LoR) issued, average number of days taken to sanction a loan, average age of the applicants, etc.(b)	The Prime Minister Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi Scheme (PM SVANidhi) aims at facilitating collateral free working capital loans upto ?10,000 of 1 year tenure to about 50 lakh street vendors across the country. It provides for incentives in the form of interest subsidy @ 7% per annum on regular repayment of loan and cash-back upto?1,200 per annum on undertaking prescribed digital transactions. In order to cover the Street vendors left out of the Survey or who are from rural / peri-urban areas but vend in the urban areas, ‘Letter of Recommendation’ (LoR) has been introduced to make them eligible to avail benefits of the Scheme.The scheme provides for Socio-economic profiling of beneficiaries and their families to determine their eligibility for various Central government welfare schemes and link them to these schemes. It also aims to facilitate beneficiary Street Food Vendors to e-commerce platforms to serve customers in the post COVID times. (c)	A statement giving State / Union Territory wise details of the loan applications received and sanctionedis at Annexure-I.(d)	Yes, Sir. A Memorandum of Understanding was entered into with Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) on June 19, 2020(e)	Ministry has launched the program of socio-economic profiling of PM SVANidhi beneficiaries and their families in 125 cities, selected for saturation in the first phase, on January 4, 2021. The program aims to assess the potential eligibility for various Central welfare schemes and to further facilitate the access to these schemes.                                            *****
