                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Urban Sports Infrastructure Scheme




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the projects sanctioned during the last three years under Urban Sports Infrastructure Scheme;(b) the number of projects out of them completed safer; and(c) the names of the projects not being completed within their scheduled time out of the above mentioned sanctioned projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) The Urban Sports Infrastructure Scheme (USIS) was operational from 2010-11 to 2015-16, wherein a total of sixty-two sports infrastructure projects got sanctioned.(b) Out of the sixty-two projects sanctioned under USIS, forty-one have been completed and ten foreclosed/cancelled.(c) The list containing names of eleven projects which have not been completed within the stipulated time and are still under progress is at Annexure.****
