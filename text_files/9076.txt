                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2740






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Environmental Clearance for Railway Projects




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the ambitious projects of the Railways for capacity expansion by way of gauge conversion and doubling of railway lines has come to a standstill for non-receipt of environmental clearance;(b)	if so, the location/project-wise details thereof;(c)	whether the revenue loss and the occurrence of delay has been assessed;(d)	if so, the details thereof;(e)	whether the Ministry of Environment has been urged by the Railways to speed up the process of granting approval in a time-bound manner on a fast-track mode;(f)	if so, the reaction thereto; and(g)	the other measures taken to obtain environmental clearance to the said projects? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (g): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
