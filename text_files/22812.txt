                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3810






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Construction of New Over Bridges/Culverts on NH-2




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of demands/requests for construction of a new culvert/over bridges on NH-2 received from the local representatives, villagers and students from Bagodar in Giridih district and Barwadda of Dhanbad district;(b) 	the action taken by the Government in this regard; and(c) 	whether the Government proposes to construct new over bridge/culvert at the said location and if so, the time by which it is likely to be constructed; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d)	About 11 and 3 demands/requests for construction of different types of structures such as Foot Over Bridge (FOB), Pedestrian Underpass (PUP) and Light Vehicular Underpass (LVUP) have been received in the 6-laning of Gorhar-Khairatunda & Khairatunda-Barwadda project stretches respectively. Construction of PUP at km 384.428 has been completed. Proposals for construction of FOB at km 337.350 and PUP at km 346.800 have also been approved. Proposals for remaining demands/requests are in different stages of examination/appraisal/approval. Work of these structures is started after necessary appraisal/approval and completion of bidding process, if required, within the approved completion period.*****
