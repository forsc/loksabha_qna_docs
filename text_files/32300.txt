                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3732






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Sports Competitions under Khelo India Scheme




Khalilur Rahaman














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of sports competitions organized at the national level by the Government under Khelo India Scheme; and(b) the facilities made available for the above competitions at the national level especially for the identification of sports talent at the rural level and for participation in sports competitions of international level especially in the State of West Bengal during the last three years; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) So far, three editions of Khelo India School/Youth Games, one edition of Khelo India University Games, two editions of Khelo India Winter Games and one Khelo India Under-17 Girls Football League have been organised at the national level by the Government under the Khelo India Scheme.(b) and (c) Khelo India Games act as a unique experience for the young and budding athletes, who get an opportunity to showcase their talents at the national level and motivate them with facilities of international standards, like, sports apparel, technical conduct, accommodation, catering, transportation, and live telecast. These games have been able to create a comprehensive eco-system for players and they provide opportunity for the talent to get identified through show-casing of her/his performance at the national level competitions. Under the Khelo India Scheme, this Ministry has sanctioned one sports infrastructure project at a cost of Rs. 26.77 crore and eight accreditated sports academies in the State of West Bengal.****
