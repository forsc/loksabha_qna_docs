                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2972






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Withdrawl of Stoppages in Karnataka




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways has withdrawn the stoppages of many trains at various stations in Karnataka in view of COVID-19,; (b) if so, the details thereof, station-wise and District-wise;(c) whether the Government has taken any decision to restore the stoppages at such stations and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of the number of trains which were restored in Karnataka after the receiving requests from  Members of Parliament?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b) To prevent the spread of Covid-19 pandemic, Indian Railways discontinued all regular passenger carrying trains w.e.f. 23rd March, 2020 and only special trains with limited stoppages keeping in view the suggestions and concerns of State Governments and health advisories issued, were being operated.  (c) Indian Railways have decided to operate regular train services with rationalized time table. (d) Indian Railways do not operate trains on State-wise basis as Railways network straddle across State boundaries. However, 87 Mail/Express train services and 86 passenger train services over South Western Railway, which primarily serve the State of Karnataka, have been operationalised.*****
