                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2385






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Grants to Educational Institutions




Mohammad Azam Khan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State-wise details of total number of Universities and Educational Institutionsin the country which are not receiving grant from the Union/State Governments;(b) whether the Government has any scheme to encourage/upgrade suchUniversities and Educational Institutions;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): As informed by University Grants Commission (UGC), the state-wise list of theUniversities in the country which are not receiving grant from the Union/StateGovernment are available at https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/0659135_LSPQ-No-2385-Annexures.pdf.(b) to (d):The Universities which are included under Section 12(B) of UGC Act-1956are eligible to receive central assistance for the students and teacher centricschemes. As informed by UGC, as on 31.03.2019, 39 Deemed to be Universitiesand 7 Private Universities included under Section 12(B) of UGC Act, 1956 arereceiving financial assistance.*****
