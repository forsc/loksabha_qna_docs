                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2767






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Classification under New CPSE Policy




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has classified under new Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) policy for Atmanirbhar Bharat and banking, insurance and financial services as one of the four strategic sectors;(b)	if so, the details of the benefit derived to the Indian economy and benefit to the industry; and (c)	if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(DR. BHAGWAT KISHANRAO KARAD)(a)	Yes, Sir.(b)	The New Public Sector Enterprise (PSE) policy for Atmanirbhar Bharat, inter alia, intends to accelerate growth of public sector enterprises through infusion of private capital, technologies and management practices thereby contributing to economic growth and new jobs. It will also help raise resources from disinvestment receipts which can be used for social sector and infrastructure development programmes of the Government.(c)	Does not arise in view of (b) above.
