                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

83






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Eklavya Model Residential Schools in Andhra Pradesh




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that 14 blocks in Andhra Pradesh have been identified where there are more than 50 per cent of Scheduled Tribe (ST) population and 20,000 or more tribals for setting up of Eklavya Model Residential Schools(EMRSs); (b) if so, details of blocks and districts identified; and (c) steps being taken to set up EMRSs in the identified blocks and by when they are going to become functional?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b) Yes, Sir. This Ministry has sanctioned Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) in 14 blocks of Andhra Pradesh fulfilling the prescribed criteria stipulated under revamped scheme of EMRS. The list of these EMRSs including blocks and districts is at Annexure.(c) Formal Sanction Order has already been issued to Construction Agencies to initiate the construction activities at all these 14 locations. Further, construction activities at 09 locations are in progress and at 05 location pre-construction activities are being carried out.  State Government of Andhra Pradesh has identified other government buildings for running of EMRSs till construction work is going on and has made 12 out of 14 sanctioned EMRSs functional and the remaining 02 are scheduled to be made functional on availability of suitable building.
