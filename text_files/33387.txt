                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

777






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






National Social Security Fund




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has set up the National Social Security Fund for informal sector workers and to improve the ease of doing business;(b)if so, the details thereof;(c)the number of workers of informal sector likely to be covered under the said fund in each State particularly in Odisha; and(d)the steps taken by the Government to ensure the last mile delivery of benefits of various social security schemes to the informal sector workers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	The Unorganised Workers’ Social Security Act, 2008 mandates the Government to provide social security to all the workers of informal/unorganised sector. The said Act has been subsumed in the Code on Social Security, 2020. Adequate provisions have been provided under Section 141 of the Code on Social Security, 2020 to create a Social Security Fund for the welfare of all unorganised workers, Gig workers and platform workers.  Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(c):		 The workers of unorganised sector of  the State of Odisha are also benefited under various social security schemes detailed as under:Schemes	Number of beneficiariesPradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan (PM-SYM) pension Scheme	1,70,936 (as on 28.01.2022)Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)	31,45,850 (as on 27.10.2021)Pradhan Mantri Surksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)	89,84,859 (as on 27.10.2021)(d):		 On, 26.08.2021, the Ministry of Labour & Employment launched e-SHRAM portal with an objective to create National Database of Unorganised Workers (NDUW) to facilitate delivery of Social Security Schemes/ Welfare Schemes of the Central /State Governments to the workers of informal/unorganised sector. As on 02.02.2022, more than 24 crore workers have been registered under the e-SHRAM portal.******
