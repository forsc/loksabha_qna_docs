                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2048






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






AYUSH Treatment Facilities in Rural Areas




Ramesh Bidhuri














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether Government has taken any steps/ measures for providing adequate AYUSH treatment facilities, especially in the rural areas of the country;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and(c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government for setting up of new AYUSH hospitals in the country, State/UT-wise and Stream-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYURVEDA, YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) & (b) Public Health being a State subject, the primary responsibility to provide adequate AYUSH treatment facilities in the States/UTs including rural areas of the country lies with the respective State/UT Governments. However, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is provision of financial assistance for setting up of up to 50 bedded integrated AYUSH hospitals, upgradation of standalone Government AYUSH hospitals & dispensaries, co-location of AYUSH facilities at Primary Health Centres (PHCs), Community Health Centres (CHCs) and District Hospitals (DHs) for providing AYUSH treatment facilities in the States/UTs including rural areas of the country. The State/UT-wise status of AYUSH healthcare facilities supported by the Ministry of AYUSH as per the proposals received from the State/UT Governments in State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) and as per the guideline of NAM is furnished at Annexure-I. (c)  Under NAM, there is provision for financial assistance for setting up of new up to 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital. Grant-in-aid is being provided to State/UT Governments as per the proposals submitted through SAAPs and as per the guideline of NAM. The State/UT wise list of up to 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals approved is furnished at Annexure-II.
