                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2407






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Solar City




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the proposal to convert Shirdi City of Maharashtra into a solar city has been approved by the Union Government;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the status of the said proposal?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy had given ‘in-principle’ approval for developing Shirdi as solar city under the scheme “Development of Solar Cities” in 2011 and  released grant for  preparation of Master Plan with few Detailed Project Reports. However, these projects were not taken up by Shirdi Administration as Shirdi Nagarpanchayat was unable to arrange the beneficiary share of Rs. 2.50 crore as per the guidelines of the scheme.*****
