                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Reservation in Promotion




Sangeeta Azad














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether 1054 teachers were demoted on 27th August, 2018 in Department of Educationparticularly primary education in Azamgarh district despite the order of the SupremeCourt on dated 26th September 2018 and 10th May, 2019 that had declared reservation inpromotion valid;b) the reasons for not following the orders of Hon. Supreme Court;c) the time by which such directions are likely to be issued by the Government so thatdemotion could not take place in view of orders of Hon. Supreme Court; andd) if not, the reasons thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d) : Education comes under the Concurrent List of the Constitution and the Recruitment,Promotion & Service conditions of the teachers appointed by the States come under the purviewof the concerned State Governments.
