                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3331






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Dredging in Brahmputra River for Waterways




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal for dredging/mining of Brahmaputra river for development of waterways on the river;(b)	if so, the time by which this work on Brahmaputra river is likely to be started;(c)	the details of other schemes/projects proposed on Brahmaputra river for the purpose;(d)	the details of fund allocated for the purpose during the last three years; and(e)	the details of works undertaken therefrom? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) takes up maintenance dredging to remove shoals to maintain 1.5 to 2.5 m deep and 45 m wide navigation channel for movement of vessels on NW-2 as per requirement.  (c) & (e) Details of schemes/ projects and works undertaken for development of shipping & navigation on river Brahmaputra are at Annex-1.(d)    The details of the fund allocation for the development of infrastructure in NE States under IWAI in the last three years and current year is as under:                                                                                                           (Year-wise B.E. figures in Rs. in Crore)<pre>Sl.No.	IWT Projects 	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-221.	National Waterway (NW) No. 2 (River Brahmaputra) & NW-16 (river Barak) and studies for 18 new NWs w.e.f. 12.04.2016	120.00 	80.00	40.00	89.10                                                </pre>  Annex.-1Details of projects and works on Brahmaputra River (NW-2):1.	Development of NW-2 at a cost of Rs. 461 crore during 2020-21 to 2024-25 has been undertaken by IWAI.  The waterway is being maintained with fairway of 1.5 m to 2.5 m Least Available Depth (LAD) and 45 m width, with day and night navigation aids and terminals.	The development of NW-2 enables waterway connectivity of North East region with Kolkata/ Haldia ports via Indo-Bangladesh Protocol Route.  The approved activities on NW-2 are as under:?	Construction of Jogighopa terminal. ?	Alternative Road to Pandu Port. ?	Ship repair facility at Pandu.  ?	Fairway development and maintenance works.?	Operation and Maintenance of navigational aids. ?	Operation and Maintenance of fixed & floating terminals.?	Procurement of 4 Ro-PAX vessels. ?	Operation and Maintenance of vessels.?	Consultancy training. ?	Maintenance of e-portal etc. ?	4 Nos. Tourist Jetties at Jogighopa, Pandu, Biswanathghat & NeamatiIn addition to above, 3 projects have been sanctioned under Central Sector Scheme (CSS) for construction of 56 nos. floating terminals at various Ghats on River Brahmaputra.                                                *****
