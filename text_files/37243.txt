                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4448






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






CSC for Cyber Services




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Jyotirmay Singh Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the  Minister of Electronics and Information Technology be pleased to state:-(a) whether the Government has set up Common Services Centres (CSC) to make cyber services available in Gram Panchayats (GPs); (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the number of CSCs set up in the States of Jammu and Kashmir and West Bengal so far, district-wise; and (d) the time by which all GPs are likely to get a dedicated CSC in the country including the States of Jammu and Kashmir and West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): Yes, Sir. Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has implemented the network of Common Services Centres (CSC) under the Digital India Programme. CSC is an important way of extending governance in public services including cyber services (vizAadhaar services, financial inclusion services, educational services, G2C/B2C services, healthcare services) to every citizen. The CSC project envisages setting up of at-least one CSC in each of 2.50 lakh Gram Panchayats (GPs) across the country, for delivery of various Government-to-Citizen (G2C) and other citizen-centric e-Services to citizens. It is a self-sustainable entrepreneurship model which is run by Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs). The number and kind of services being provided currently through CSCs are at Annexure-I.As on February 2022, total 4,63,705 CSCs are operational across all states & Union Territories of the country, out of which, total 3,63,887 CSCs are operational at Gram Panchayat (GP) level. The list of number of CSCs in the country (State/UT-wise) is at Annexure-II.(c): The district-wise details of the number of CSCs set up in the UT of Jammu and Kashmir and State of West Bengal are at Annexure-III.(d):In the State of West Bengal and UT of Jammu & Kashmir, all the Gram Panchayats are covered with at least one registered CSC.CSC e-Governance Service India Limited (CSC SPV) covers  villages,  depending  on feasibility, availability of internet connection and stable power supply.******* Annexure-IList of Services offered through CSCsSL NO.
