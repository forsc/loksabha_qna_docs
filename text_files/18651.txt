                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

197






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Targets set under PMMSY  for Fishermen’s Welfare




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of steps taken under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojna (PMMSY) for robust fisheries management framework and for fishermen’s welfare and for promotion of fish farmers producer organizations;(b) 	the steps taken for infrastructure development and post-harvest management in fisheries and aquaculture sector under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojna;(c) 	the steps taken for enhancement of production and productivity in fisheries and aquaculture sector under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojna;(d) 	whether under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojna target for production growth rate and export growth rate have been set by the Government and if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	whether the set target has been achieved by the Government and if not, the details of steps taken/being taken by the Government to achieve the target?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (e):  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Fisheries Husbandry and Dairying has launched a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)”- a scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India as a part of the Atma Nirbhar Bharat relief package with a highest ever estimate investment of ? 20050 crores comprising of Central share of ?9407 crores, State share of ? 4880 crores and beneficiaries contribution of ?5763 crores in fisheries sector over a period of 5 years from FY 2020-21 to FY 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories to address critical gaps in fish production and productivity, quality, technology, post-harvest infrastructure and management, modernisation and strengthening of value chain, traceability, establishing a robust fisheries management framework and Fishermen’s welfare.   PMMSY inter-alia also envisages formation of fish farmers producer organizations (FFPOs).  PMMSY focuses on area expansion, diversification and intensification of aquaculture including productive utilisation of land and water; supply of quality and affordable seed and feed; species diversification, genetic improvement of targeted fish species; aquatic health management; promotion of scientific methods and innovations in fisheries; infusion of modern technologies such as Re-circulatory Aquaculture Systems, Biofloc, Aquaponics, cage culture; promotion of deep sea fishing;  promotion of mariculture including seaweed cultivation, development of Ornamental Fisheries etc.Moreover, PMMSY targets to enhance fish production to 220 lakh metric tons by 2024-25 at an average annual growth rate of about 9%, increase aquaculture productivity to 5 tons per hectare, enhance the export earnings to Rs.1,00,000 crore; reduction of post-harvest losses up to 10%, doubling of incomes of fishers and fish farmers by 2024-25, Enhancement of the domestic fish consumption from about 5-6 kg to about 9 kg per capita.*****
