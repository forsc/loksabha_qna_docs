                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

121






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Information Technology Hubs in Bihar




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to develop Information Technology (IT) Hubs in small cities in Bihar and if so, the details thereof; (b)  the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (c)  whether the Government proposes to give an impetus to Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) : A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.							(US$ in Billion,     INR in Rs. Crores) STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA  STARRED QUESTION NO. *121 FOR 10.02.2021 REGARDING INFORMATION  TECHNOLOGY HUBS IN BIHAR*******(a)	and (b): Government of India has taken various initiatives for promoting IT/ ITeS industry in various parts of country including Bihar. Till date, 60 Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) centres have been established of which 52 centres are in Tier II/ III cities.This includes one STPI Centre in Bihar at Patna (Patliputra). Government has also approved two new STPI Centres for Bihar at Darbhanga and Bhagalpur.The STPI Centre at Patna (Patliputra) is being augmented through provision of additional 1 Lakh Sq. Ft. of space. List of existing and approved STPI Centres are at Annexure-I and II.Further, for creation of employment opportunities in IT/ ITeS industry particularly in Tier II/ Tier III cities, Government is implementing India BPO Promotion Scheme (IBPS). Under this Scheme, incentives are provided for setting up of BPO IT/ ITeS operation. 12BPO units have started BPO/ ITeS operations in Bihar while providing direct employment to more than 1500 people. National Policy on Software Products (NPSP) - 2019 has been notified by the Governmentto promote software product ecosystem in the country. In accordance with NPSP, Government has approved the Next Generation Incubation Scheme (NGIS) with a budgetary outlay of Rs. 95.03 crore for establishing software product incubation facilities in12 Tier II/ III STPI centres including an incubation facility in Bihar at Patna.Till date, 8,817 Gram Panchayats (GPs) have been connected with High Speed Optical Fibre Network in Bihar and are ready to provide Digital Services. National Informatics Centre (NIC) has established a Digital Government Research Centre in Patna for promoting state of art e-Governance services. A cloud enabled Data Centre has also been established in Patna which is operational since June 2019.Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) has established a facility to promote research and development in the field of Cyber-security and Forensics, Internet of Everything, Artificial Intelligence, and Advanced Analytics in Patna, Bihar.For providing IT services to rural and semi- urban population, Government is supporting establishment of Common Service Centres (CSCs). A total of 34,495 CSCs are functional in Bihar of which 29,684 CSCs are in Gram Panchayats.(c):  As per extant Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) policy, 100% FDI is allowed in IT sector under automatic routesubject to applicable laws/ regulations. A total of Rs. 1,39,217 crore FDI has been committed in Computer Software and Hardware during FY 2017-2020 and Rs. 1,31,169 crore in current financial year till September, 2020.****** Annexure-ISate-wise list of 60 existing STPI CentresS.
