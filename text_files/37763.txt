                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5038






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Mahila Shakti Kendra in Uttar Pradesh




Pradeep Kumar Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the details of the work done under the Mahila Shakti Kendra scheme in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years; (b)       the amount allocated and spent thereunder during the said period; and (c)       the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on this scheme along with the steps taken by the Government to deal with it?






ANSWER




(a) :      Various activities and initiatives have been undertaken in Uttar Pradesh under  Mahila Shakti Kendra (MSK) Scheme during the last three years. These include  (i) convergence of various departments and stakeholders at District and Block levels, (ii) orientation and training of various stakeholders on women related schemes and programmes including adolescent girls on social, health and economic aspects, and (iii)  awareness generation activities etc. (b) :    The amount released/ spent under the Scheme to the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last three year is as under:Financial Year	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Amount released (In Lakh)	362.13	17.16	11.67 (c) :     The implementation of scheme has been affected due to pandemic  because gatherings were not allowed and one to one contact was also restricted. However, the Government has taken appropriate steps as per COVID-19 guidelines and protocol to ensure implementation of various activities under the scheme. The Government has decided to subsume the existing scheme of MSK under the component of National, State and District Hub for Empowerment of Women under Mission Shakti for implementation during the 15th Finance Commission Cycle.*****
