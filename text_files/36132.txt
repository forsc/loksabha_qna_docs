                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3450






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Construction of Godowns




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total debt owed to the Food Corporation of India (FCI) and the amount of funds allocated by the Government during the last three years, year-wise; (b) 	whether godowns have been constructed by the Government and if so, the number of such godownsconstructed so far including the number of godowns required further; and (c) 	the storage capacity of the private godowns in the country along with the grants provided by the Government for the construction of the godowns, Statewise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):	The debt position of Food Corporation of India FCI for last three years are as follows:Year	Types of Loan (Rs. in crore)	CCL(Cash Credit Limit)	STL(Short Term Loan)	Bonds (GoI Guaranteed Bonds)	WMA (Ways & Means Advance)	NSSF(National Small Savings Fund)	Total2018-19	5,198.05	41,226	15,738	NIL	1,91,000	2,53,162.052019-20	3,565.28	40,700	29,000	NIL	2,54,600	3,27,865.282020-21	2,708.79	20,500	37,000	NIL	NIL	60,2092021-22(as on 28.02.2022)	389.46	1,950	45,000	NIL	NIL	47,339.....2/--2-Fund allocated to FCI on account of Food Subsidy is as under :- Year	Subsidy allocated during the year(Rs. In Crores)2018-19	70,0982019-20	75,0002020-21	4,62,789** It includes additional subsidy of Rs. 84,317.30 due to PMGKAY and arrear of subsidy amounting to Rs. 2,43,779 crore.FCI has been constructing many foodgrain storage depots (FSDs) under Central Sector Scheme. Food Storage godowns are being constructed at those locations where there is storage gap of food grains to maintain the adequate stock level for meeting the demand of local population, thus to provide food security to all people in India. These godowns are used to keep buffer stocks, procurement and distribution of food gains to deficit areas.Following godowns/silos have been constructed in last 3 years and current year:1.	8 Godowns having capacity of 0.4870 LMT under Central Sector Scheme.2.	9 silos having capacity of 4.875 LMT under PPP mode.3.	41 Godowns having capacity of 3.56 LMT under Private Entrepreneur Guarantee Scheme.In addition, FCI continuously assesses and monitors the storage capacity and based on the storage gap assessment, storage capacities are created/hired through the following schemes:-1.	Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) Scheme2.	Central Sector Scheme (CSS)3.	Construction of Silo''s under PPP mode4.	Hiring of godown from CWCs/SWCs/State Agencies5.	Hiring of godown through Private Warehousing Scheme (PWS)(c):	As on 01.02.2022, storage capacity of the private godowns/silos being used by FCI in the country are given below: 1.	104.91 LMT capacity developed by Private Parties under Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) Scheme2.	11.50 LMT capacity of Private Parties is hired under Private Warehousing Scheme (PWS)3.	9.38 LMT capacity Silos created under PPP scheme are hired. No grants are provided by the Government for construction of Private godowns.*****
