                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Construction of Civil Aviation Research Centre




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to construct Civil Aviation Research Centre at Begumpet airport in Telangana;(b) if so, the approved cost thereof and the facilities to be provided thereunder along with the time by which it will be constructed/completed;(c) whether the Government proposes to set up Aviation University at Begumpet airport for imparting education relating to aviation sector, keeping in view huge space available at the airport which is located in the middle of the city; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Jyotiraditya M. Scindia)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT IN RESPECT OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 253 REGARDING "CONSTRUCTION OF CIVIL AVIATION RESEARCH CENTRE" TO BE ANSWERED ON 05.08.2021; ; (a) and (b) Yes Sir. The Civil Aviation Research Organization (CARO) is being constructed at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad by Airports Authority of India (AAI). The approved cost is Rs.402.13 crore. ; The facilities at CARO include as under:; i.  R&D facilities for airports and Air Navigation Services (ANS);  ; ii.  Air traffic management communications domain simulators, network emulator, visualisation and analysis labs, surveillance labs; navigation systems emulation and simulation labs etc.; iii.  Cyber security and threat analysis labs, data management centre, project support centre, software solutions and tools centre and network infrastructure centre.  The construction of CARO is expected to conclude by December 2023.;;  (c) & (d) No such proposal is currently under consideration at the Ministry of Civil Aviation.
