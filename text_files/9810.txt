                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3289






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Privatisation of Ashoka Hotel




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Government has any plan to privatise Five Star Ashoka Hotel at New Delhi; (b) 	if so, the reasons for the same; and (c) 	whether Ashoka Hotel is incurring loss and if so, the details thereof during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b):	As per the Disinvestment Policy of the Government of India, Department of Investment and Public Asset Management (DIPAM), Ministry of Finance, Government of India constituted an Inter Ministerial Group (IMG) for Hotel Ashok – Hotel Samrat Complex in New Delhi. DIPAM floated a Request for Proposal (RFP) for engagement of Consultant to study lease terms & conditions of land, prevailing laws, provisions and restrictions (if any) pertaining to construction & development of property in the area, existing arrangements being used, Operations & Maintenance Contract, Sub Leasing and utilization of land in the Hotel Ashok – Hotel Samrat Complex. (c):	Ashok Hotel has not incurred any losses in the last five years.********
