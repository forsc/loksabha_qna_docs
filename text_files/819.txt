                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

716






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






LHB Coaches




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to replace conventional coaches with the modern Linke Hofmann Busch (LHB) design coaches in all distance trains originating from Kerala to other destinations; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps taken by the Railways for improving catering service using the most hygienic and   latest equipment for food preparation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	& (b) Indian Railways do not run trains on State-wise basis as railway network cuts across State boundaries. As such, induction of rakes including Linke Hofmann Busch (LHB) is not undertaken on State-wise basis. However, at present, 22 pairs of train services Originating from /Terminating at /Passing through the stations located in the State of Kerala are being operated with LHB coaches. Besides, replacement of additional trains with LHB coaches is an on-going process on Indian Railways and is being undertaken in a phased manner.-2-(c)46 Base Kitchens/Kitchen Units have been upgraded by IRCTC during the last 2 years. The latest kitchen equipments such as Tilting Bratt pans, Tilting boiling kettle, Vegetable cutting machine, Chapati making machine including peda cutting machine et al. have been installed in the upgraded Kitchen Units.          	In addition, to ensure real time monitoring of Kitchen activities, CCTV cameras have also been  installed in 39 Base Kitchen, Food Safety Supervisors have been deployed at Kitchens to monitor compliance of food safety norms and hygienic practices.                                                *****
