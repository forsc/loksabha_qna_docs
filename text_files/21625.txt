                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2915






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Free Vaccine to ASHA Workers




Sangeeta Azad














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether rural healthcare workers such as ASHA workers and such others have been placed in the category of frontline workers;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to provide free COVID-19 vaccine of Corona to frontline workers; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): Rural health care workers such as ASHA workers have been placed in the category of Health Care Workers for the purpose of Covid-19 vaccination. This category includes Healthcare providers and workers in healthcare settings both government & private.(c) & (d): The National Expert Group on Vaccine Administration for COVID-19 (NEGVAC) has prioritized Health Care Workers, Front Line Workers, persons aged 60 years and above, and those aged between 45 to 59 years with identified comorbidities for COVID-19 vaccination. COVID-19 vaccination is available free of cost at Government COVID-19 Vaccination Centres (CVCs) to persons of above mentioned priority groups.
