                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

116






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Allahabad University




Asaduddin Owaisi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that mass irregularities including complaints of sexual harassment against faculty members by students have been reported in Allahabad University recently; (b) if so, whether the Vice-Chancellor of the University has resigned amid charges of irregularities; (c) if so, whether the National Commission for Women (NCW) inquired into the sexual harassment of students in that University and submitted a report to his Ministry; and (d) if so, the action taken or being taken by the Government against those involved in the irregularities and sexual harassment cases in Allahabad University? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT( SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’ )  (a)  to (d):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. 116 FOR 10.02.2020 ASKED BY SHRI ASADUDDIN OWAISI, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING ALLAHABAD UNIVERSITY  (a) to (d): The Government has received some complaints relating to irregularities in management of affairs of the University of Allahabad.  Show Cause Notice was issued to the University to explain its position on the various allegations.  An Interim Report of inquiry on the complaints relating to quality of food served to inmates of girls’ hostels, unsafe environment in girls’ hostels, complaints of corruption, etc. against the Vice Chancellor, was also received from the National Commission for Women.   The former Vice-Chancellor of the University of Allahabad tendered his resignation on 31.12.2019 from the post on personal grounds which have been accepted on 03.01.2020. In addition to this, it has also been decided to conduct an enquiry into the allegations of financial, academic and administrative irregularities,  including the recommendations contained in the said Interim Report of the National Commission for Women regarding misconduct against the former Vice-Chancellor of the University.  A three Member Committee has been constituted to enquire into these allegations.  ****
