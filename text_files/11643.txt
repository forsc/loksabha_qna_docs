                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

558






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Penalties Imposed on TSPs




Abdul Khaleque













                                                    Chandan Singh
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    S. Jagathrakshakan
                                                








                                                    M. Selvaraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether private and public sector Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) owe over Rs. 92000 cr. as licence fee penalty to the Government and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the recent verdict on the Annual Gross Revenues (AGR) by the apex court has affected the TSPs and telecom industry and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the total amount of money to be paid by TSPs including public sector TSP post the Supreme Court verdict on AGR, TSP-wise; and (d) 	whether the Government has formulated any plan to recover the said due amount and if so, the details thereof including the steps taken/being taken by the Government to recover the due amount and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Yes, the details of the outstanding amount thereof TSP-wise are enclosed as Annexure-I and Annexure-II.(b)	The COAI and few Telecom Service Providers have represented to the Department of Telecom to express their apprehension about the possible adverse effect on Telecom companies in view of the verdict on Adjusted Gross Revenue (AGR) matter by the apex court.(c)	The TSPs-wise outstanding amount after Hon’ble Supreme Court Judgement are enclosed as Annexure-I and Annexure-II.(d)	The Government has directed the Licensees to make the payments in accordance with the order of the Hon’ble Supreme Court dated 24.10.2019.
