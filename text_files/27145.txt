                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Additional Drinking Water Schemes




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is considering to formulate additional schemes for densely populated districts of rural areas in order to resolve the water problems;(b) 	if so, whether it is being considered for Uttar Pradesh also; and(c) 	if so, the whether it is being considered to formulate any central scheme to provide water connectivity for the rural population of Firozabad district? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE  FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI  PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c) To enable every rural household, including those in rural areas of Uttar Pradesh inter alia which include Firozabad and densely populated districts across the country, to have potable tap water supply in adequate quantity, of prescribed quality and on regular and long-term basis, Government of India in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal. The estimated outlay of the Mission is Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore, out of which Central share is Rs. 2.08 lakh Crore.*****
