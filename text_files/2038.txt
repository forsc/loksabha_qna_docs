                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1992






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Allotment of Linkages




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether as per Telangana Act 12th schedule new linkages will be allotted as per new coal distribution policy;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT- wise along with the present status thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a)to(c): New coal linkages have been allotted to the thermal power plants of Telangana State Power Generation Corporation (TSGENCO).  The details of coal linkages allotted to TSGENCO from Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL) are given below:Thermal Power Plant	Capacity (in MW)KTPS-VII	800Bhadradri	1080Yadadri	4000 The details of Bridge Linkage granted to the Thermal Power Plants of Telangana States are given below:Thermal Power Plants	Capacity (in MW)Singareni Thermal power plant of SCCL	1200Kakatiya TPP, Stage-II of Telangana State Power Generation Corporation Limited (TSPGCL) 	600 As per the statutory requirements of A(3) of the Twelfth Schedule of the Andhra Pradesh Reorganization Act, 2014 and on the basis of recommendation of the Ministry of Power, ''in principle'' approval for grant of tapering linkage to 2x800 MW Phase-I of Telangana Super Thermal Power Project of NTPC has been accorded.The details of coal linkages allotted to Andhra Pradesh Power Generation Corporation APGENCO from Coal India Limited (CIL) are given below: Thermal Power Plants	Capacity(in MW)Rayalseema U-6	100NTTPS St-V	800Sri Damodaran Sanjeevaiah St-II	800*****
