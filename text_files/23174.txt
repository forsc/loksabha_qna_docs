                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Base Unit to Calculate Number of Teaching Posts




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has asked the University Grants Commission (UGC) to convey to all Universities to start its process of teachers‘ appointment by calculating vacancies based on individual departments;(b) if so, details thereof;(c) the number of Universities and Colleges that are going ahead with appointments and are calculating vacancies on the basis of departments which may affect the representation of SC/ST candidates and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Commission had announced that an individual department should be considered as base unit to calculate number of teaching posts to be reserved for SCs/STs?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): The Government promulgated The Central Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers’ Cadre) Ordinance, 2019 on 07.03.2019 to provide for the reservation of posts in appointments by direct recruitment of persons to teachers’ cadre by considering Central Educational Institutions as one unit. Subsequently, this Ordinance has been repealed and The Central Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers’ Cadre) Act, 2019 notified on 09.07.2019.	The University Grants Commission (UGC) has informed that all Central Universities under the purview of UGC are following the reservation of posts in teaching cadre by considering the university as a unit.*****
