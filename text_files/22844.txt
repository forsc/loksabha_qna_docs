                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3823






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Prime Minister‘s 15 Point Programme




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds spent by the Government on minority development between financial years 2015 and 2021; and(b) the percentage of the budgetary allocation made by the Government and spent on the Sikh, Jain, Buddhist and Parsi communities respectively during the above period? 
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):     Ministry of Minority Affairs implements various programmes/ schemes for socio-economic and educational empowerment of the six (6) centrally notified minority communities namely Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Parsis, Jains and Muslims. An amount of Rs. 21028.92 crore has been Spent from 2015-16 to 2020-21 as on 28.02.2021.(b):	Community-wise allocation of funds under the schemes of the Ministry is not made in the budget .However, during 2015-16 to 2019-20, 36,99,296 scholarships have been sanctioned to the students belonging to Sikh, Jain, Buddhist and Parsi communities. Community-wise number of beneficiaries given in the table:	Name of Minority Community	Sikhs	Parsis	Jains	BuddhistsTotal No. of Scholarships	25,41,879	4,562	4,08,415	7,44,440********
