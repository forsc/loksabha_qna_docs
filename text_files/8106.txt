                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1625






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Effect of Vibrations from Airport Express Metro Line




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government is aware about the facts that vibrations due to operation of high speed of Airport Expressway Metro rail the grit wash of various CPWD quarters/building particularly situated at Baba Kharak Singh Marg, New Delhi are in a collapsing state which is fatal and if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(b)	whether the Government has received any complaints regarding falling of grit wash in these colonies and if so, the details thereof and action taken thereon;(c)	whether CPWD organizes regular inspection of such buildings to check dangerous accident and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government proposes to replace grit wash with other alternatives to check such dangerous accidents; and (e)	if so, the details thereof and the further corrective measures being taken by the Government to tackle such situation?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e)	The grit wash of various Central Public Works Department (CPWD) quarters / buildings including quarters situated at Baba Kharak Singh Marg, New Delhi are not affected due to operation of Airport Express line metro rail.  Complaints relating to falling of grit wash are due to normal wear and tear and same are attended promptly.  Regular inspection is carried out by the field staff of CPWD and wherever required, grit plaster is repaired.                           *****
