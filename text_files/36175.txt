                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3466






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Development of Cities




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided to develop a large number of new cities across the country to meet demand for urbanization;(b)	if so, the details thereof, States-wise especially for Haryana State; and(c)	the steps being taken by the Government to workout a framework for developing new cities in a planned manner? 






ANSWER




                                                                               THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                    MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	to (c): The XVth Finance Commission has recommended a performance-based challenge fund of ?8,000 crore for incubation of new cities, wherein the amount available for each proposed new city is ?1,000 crore and a State can have only one new city under the proposed scheme. Thus a maximum of eight States can avail this grant for eight new cities over the award period of the Commission. As per recommendations, an Expert Committee has been constituted by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs in December, 2021 to finalize the minimum eligibility conditions and bidding parameters for selection of the cities from States including Haryana.                                      *****
