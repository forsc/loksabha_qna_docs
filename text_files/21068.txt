                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Recommendations made by DGTR




Y. S. Avinash Reddy













                                                    Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of recommendations made by the Directorate General of Trade Remedies (DGTR) on anti-dumping, anti-subsidy and safeguard related matters during the last five years; (b) the number of recommendations received and accepted; and (c) the number of recommendations rejected along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a), (b) and (c):  In last 5 years (since 1st April, 2016), DGTR has made final findings recommendations in 181 ADD, 10 Anti-subsidy (CVD) and 6 Safeguard (SG). Out of these, number of recommendations accepted are 140 ADD, 6 CVD & 5 (safeguard) and no. of recommendations rejected are 21 ADD, 1 CVD & 1 safeguard.  Rest  are under process of examination by the Central Government. For acceptance or otherwise, the recommendations of DGTR are comprehensively examined taking into account overall public interest and interest of stakeholders such as applicant industry, other Indian producers and downstream user industry.*****
