                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

256






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Trains to District Headquarters




Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State-wise details of the district headquarters where train services are not available; (b) whether it is proposed to devise any work plan to connect all the district headquarters in the country with superfast train services;(c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) whether the Government proposes to introduce any action plan to cover Kanpur city and Kanpur dehat district headquarters with superfast train services?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS,  COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d) Indian Railways do not operate train services on State-wise basis as railway network straddle across State boundaries. However, most of the district headquarters, connected by Rail head, have connectivity by train services. In other cases, connections are generally available with changeover. Furthermore, improving connectivity by way of introduction of new trains and extension by existing services, including those for Kanpur area, is an ongoing process on Indian Railways, which is dependent inter-alia on factors like operational feasibility, traffic justification and availability of resources etc. Kanpur is very well connected to major destinations like Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, Chennai and other major cities.*****
