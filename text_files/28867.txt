                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Sanctioned /Allotted Posts of IPS




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Indian Police Services (IPS) posts/cadres sanctioned/allotted by the Government for posting in various posts for the State of Maharashtra;(b) the total number of IPS cadre in various posts in the State of Maharashtra; and(c) the total number of IPS officers presently working in the Maharashtra State as on date, post-wise/cadre wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a). As per the Cadre Review Notification dated 16.10.2018, total 317 IPS posts have been allotted/sanctioned to the State of Maharashtra. (b) and (c). The status of IPS cadre posts and post-wise Post	Senior Duty Posts Allocation	Presently workingDirector General	04	08Additional Director General	16	34Inspector General	29	23Deputy Inspector General	32	40Superintendent of Police	91	135Total	172*	240-2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.317 FOR 30.11.2021*In addition to the above, 145 posts consisting of Central Deputation Reserve, State Deputation Reserve, Training Reserve and Junior Post Reserve & Leave Reserve have been allocated to the State of Maharashtra.*****
