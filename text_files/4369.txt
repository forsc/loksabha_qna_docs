                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3914






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Completion of Infrastructure Projects based on PPP mode




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government proposes to create a policy framework for early completion of infrastructure projects based on Public-Private Partnership (PPP) mode;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the names of projects which got delayed during the last two years and the time-limit prescribed to complete the said projects along with the State/District –wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) & (b):  A Task Force was constituted with the objective of effective delivery of projects including those based on Public Private Partnership mode within time and budget for Central / State Government and Public- Sector Enterprises.		The Task Force has recommended various reforms in project and program management to complete and deliver complex projects with quality, and within time and budget in its report.		The Report is available on the NITI Aayog website, accessible at: https://niti.gov.in/writereaddata/files/document_publication/Task-Force- Report_Final-r1.pdf.(c): 	As on 01.05.2019, a total of 65 ongoing projects under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode were on the monitor of this Ministry, of which 37 were delayed. The details of these Projects are available in the flash report at www.cspm.gov.in/publications.                                                ****
