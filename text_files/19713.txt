                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

92






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Job Fairs Organised in Madhya Pradesh




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has prepared any plan with the State Governments to organize job fairs at district level across the country ; and (b)	if so, the details thereof including the number of job fairs organized in Madhya Pradesh during the last three years, district-wise and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (b): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.  ** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *92 DUE FOR REPLY ON 08-02-2021 BY DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR REGARDING “JOB FAIRS IN MADHYA PRADESH”.(a) to (b): Government is implementing the National Career Service (NCS) Project to provide a variety of employment related services like job matching, career counselling, vocational guidance, information on skill development courses, etc. The NCS project comprises three components: 1)	 National Career Service (NCS) Portal (www.ncs.gov.in).2)	Model Career Centres (MCC) having state of art infrastructure to provide all employment related services including outreach activities and job fairs in collaboration with States/UTs and other institutions. The Government provides financial assistance to these centres for an amount up to Rs. 50 Lakh per centre based on the proposal of State/UT/Institutions as per scheme guidelines. As of now the Government has approved 207 Model Career Centres (MCCS) (including 7 on Non-funded basis).Out of the total 207 MCCs, 10 MCCs have been approved in Madhya Pradesh.3)	Interlinking of Employment Exchanges- Under this component, funds have been provided to States/UTs to interlink Employment Exchanges with NCS Portal and to organize job fairs. Till date, funds have been released to 28 States/UTs as per scheme guidelines. The details of the job fairs organized through NCS in collaboration with States/UTs including Madhya Pradesh is attached at Annexure. Besides this, State Governments also organize job fairs from their resources as per their requirements.
