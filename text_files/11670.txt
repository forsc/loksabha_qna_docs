                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

667






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Telecom Connectivity in Tripura




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of telecom connectivity in the State of Tripura; (b) 	whether the Government has extended 4G services in each district of Tripura; (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor along with the time by which 4G services will be provided in each district of Tripura; (d) 	the role of Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) in this regard and the details of the funds utilized therefrom during each of the last three years for the aforesaid purpose; and (e) 	the details of fund utilization by BSNL in connection with this matter?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) to (c)	As per the data collected from the Telecom Service Providers(TSPs) and Department of Telecommunications (DoT) field units in 2019, it is estimated that there are 8 inhabited villages (as per census 2011 data) in Tripura, which are not covered by mobile services. Government and TSPs have extended 4G services in each district of Tripura. District-wise 4G coverage is as below.<pre>District	Total Number of Villages 	Number of Villages with 4G coverageDhalai	148	97North Tripura	166	144South Tripura 	268	242West Tripura 	281	274Grand Total	863	757</pre>(d) to (e) 	Mobile Services in 2 uncovered villages and 7 sites along National Highway in Tripura are being provided with USOF funding through M/s Bharti Hexacom Limited as per the agreement signed on 08.12.2017 for providing 2G services. As on 27.01.2020, a total of 3 towers have been installed and providing services.In Tripura, no scheme for provisioning of mobile services is being implemented through BSNL and therefore, no funds have been disbursed to BSNL for providing mobile services.************
