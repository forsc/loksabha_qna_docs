                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2918






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Vacant Posts of Professors/Assistant Professors




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Universities in Maharashtra till today along with the posts of Professors and Assistant Professors lying vacant therein;(b) the time by when these posts are likely to be filled; and(c) the steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to fill these posts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (c): The University Grants Commission (UGC) has informed that as on 31.03.2021 there are 72 degree awarding Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) in the State of Maharashtra including 01 Central University, 24 State Public Universities, 18 State Private Universities, 21 Institutions Deemed to be University and 08 Institutions of National Importance. Out of these HEIs, only one Central University, namely, Mahatma Gandhi Antarrashtriya Hindi Vishwavidyalaya is under the purview of UGC wherein 8 posts of Professor and 2 posts of Assistant Professor are vacant as on 01.01.2022. Mahatma Gandhi Antarrashtriya Hindi Vishwavidyalaya has advertised the vacant posts and scrutiny of applications is underway. The UGC has further informed that State Universities come under the administrative purview of State Government concerned and onus of filling up the vacancies lies with the respective University / State Government. However, UGC has repeatedly requested all the Universities to fill up the vacancies, at the earliest. Recently, UGC vide its letter No.F.1-11/2021 (SCT) dated 2nd September, 2021 has requested all Central Universities to fill up vacancies in a Mission Mode within a period of one year starting from 5th September, 2021. *****
