                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

743






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






NH Projects in Maharashtra




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the highways declared as National Highways (NHs) along with details of NHs which are under construction at present in the State of Maharashtra;(b) the details of development work of NHs taken up in the State during the last three years and current year;(c) whether the targets fixed for the said work has been achieved and if so, the details thereof; and (d) the funds allocated and released for the purpose during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  The details of the highways declared as National Highways (NHs) in the State of Maharashtra are at Annexure – I. The details of NHs which are at construction stage at present in the State of Maharashtra  are at Annexure – II. (b)	The details of National Highway (NH) projects taken up in the State of Maharashtra during the last three years and current year are at Annexure – III.(c) 	Targets for construction of NH length were fully achieved despite some delays on account of Land Acquisition  and forest clearances. The NH length constructed in the State of Maharashtra during the last three years and current year are as under: Year	Length of NHs  constructed in Maharashtra(in Km.)2018-19	2292.652019-20	2422.362020-21	2870.122021-22 (As on 31-10-2021)	791.76 (d)  	The details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred on the development/construction of NHs in the state of Maharashtra during the last three years and current year are given as below:(Rs. in Crore}S. No.	Year	Allocation* 	Expenditure			State PWD	NHAI1	2018-19	7051	7445	69022	2019-2020	10341	10998	60223	2020-2021	9849	9226	86934	2021-2022 (As on 31-10-2021)	5924	3656	3673	*No State wise allocation has been made to National Highways Authority of India (NHAI)
