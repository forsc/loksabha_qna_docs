                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

532






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Scientific Research




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is lack of resources, operational autonomy and investment for high quality scientific research in the Indian Research Institutes;(b) if so, the steps being taken by the Government in this regard;(c) whether any circular has been issued by the Government with regard to the appointment of investors in science institutes; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND EARTH SCIENCES(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)??????? ?? ???????????? ??? ?????? ??????? ???????? ??  ????? ?????? (???????? ??????)  (??. ???????? ????) (a) & (b) No Sir, the autonomous R&D Institutes under the administrative control of Ministry of Science and Technology are supported adequately by the Government for pursuing R&D activities, building and maintaining the desired R&D infrastructure and national R&D human resource development. Rather, there has been a regular enhancement in allocation of grants to the autonomous R&D Institutes under the Ministry during the last five years.(c) & (d) No Sir, no such circulars have been issued.
