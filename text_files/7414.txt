                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Special Package to Chhattisgarh




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether there is any provision to provide special package to State Governments to tackle dangerous diseases like Malaria and Dengue;(b) if so, the details of the fund allocated to States/UTs and utilized by them particularly in Chhattisgarh during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(c) if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government proposes to set up more hospitals to tackle the said diseases in Chhattisgarh; and(e) if so, the details thereof, location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Directorate of National Vector Borne Disease Control Programme provides technical guidance and financial support for specified activities/items for only vector borne diseases as per NHM norms. The prevention and control of the vector borne diseases is primarily the responsibility of the State Governments.(b) & (c):	The Details of the allocation & expenditure to state of Chhattisgarh are as below:-Rs. In lakhsYears	Allocation	Expenditure2016-17	908.00	5525.982017-18	3364.50	12695.102018-19	1086.00	4295.902019-20*	651.53	1588.93•	Till October, 2019(d) & (e):	Government of India (GoI) has identified following Seven hospitals as requested by the State Government to function as Sentinel Surveillance Hospitals for Dengue to provide free diagnosis in the State of Chhattisgarh.1. 	Pt. J.N.M Medical College, Raipur2.   Sardar Vallabh Bhai Patel District Hospital, Bilaspur3.	Jagdalpur Medical College (Maharani Hospital), 	Jagdalpur, Bastar4. 	District Hospital, Ambikapur, Surguja5. 	Medical College, Rajnandgaon6. 	Medical College, Raigarh7. 	District Hospital, KorbaGoI provides dengue test kits to these hospitals free of cost. Till date, total 37 IgM ELISA kits for Dengue (1 Kit- 96 tests) are supplied to these identifies hospitals as per their technical requirement.Further, Under the aegis of Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) AIIMS Raipur a teaching cum tertiary care Institute been established at Raipur, Chhattisgarh with an sanctioned cost of Rs. 820 crore. The AIIMS is functional and providing medical services to tackle all types of diseases including malaria and dengue. Currently 700 beds are functional and average OPD attendance is around 1750.Apart from AIIMS, two State Government owned Medical Colleges at Bilaspur and Jagdalpur in Chhattisgarh are being upgraded through PMSSY with sanctioned cost of Rs. 200 crore each. Total number of beds to be added is around (289X2=) 578.
