                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2122






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Indians Settled Abroad




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total Number of Indians settled in other countries and country wise the list for last 10 years;(b) 	the total number of Indians who gave up Indian citizenship and took the citizenship of other countries during the last ten years; and (c) 	the year-wise details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) 	 As per the data available with this Ministry, a total of 1,33,83,718 Indians have settled in other countries. The country-wise data is placed at Annexure - I.(b) & (c) 	 As per the data available with this Ministry for the period 2015 till October 2019, 5,84,364 Indians gave up Indian citizenship. The details are placed at Annexure – II.***
