                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2932






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Special Package to Maharashtra for Promotion of Tourism




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of priority schemes for the development of tourism in Maharashtra and funds allocated to Maharashtra for these schemes during the last three years; (b)	the funds spent out of the allocated funds during the said period and the details of the residual funds, scheme-wise; (c)	whether any special package is given by the Government to States in order to promote tourism and if so, the occasion when Maharashtra had been given any special package; and (d)	whether the Government has set up any monitoring mechanism regarding the progress status of said schemes and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM			  	      (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	The Ministry of Tourism has provided financial assistance for the development of tourism related infrastructure under the ‘Swadesh Darshan’, ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ and ‘Assistance to Central Agencies for Tourism Infrastructure Development’ Schemes to the State of Maharashtra. The details of the projects sanctioned in the State of Maharashtra under the above schemes is annexed. (c):	The Ministry of Tourism does not sanction/provide special package to the States/UTs in order to promote tourism.(d):	The Ministry of Tourism monitors the physical progress of the sanctioned projects through field inspections and review meetings held periodically with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations/Central Agencies. In addition, the Ministry of Tourism has developed a dashboard for monitoring the implementation of the sanctioned projects under the Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD Schemes.******
