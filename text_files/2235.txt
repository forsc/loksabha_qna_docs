                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2069






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Market share of Air India




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Air India has slipped below first three ranks in terms of market share;(b) if so, the reasons therefor along with the continuous decrease in the number of passengers; and(c) the various steps taken by the Government to improve the quality index of Air India?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) Air India remained in the top three in terms of domestic market share in the current year, 2019 (from January, 2019 to May, 2019). Air India has the largest market share in the International Market.;(b) Does not arise in view of (a) above.;(c) In the meeting taken by Finance Minister on 07.09.2018, operational and financial performance parameters and milestones were fixed by the Government and are regularly reviewed through review meetings held under the chairmanship of Secretary, Ministry of Civil Aviation. The Government is committed to the disinvestment of Air India.
