                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4910






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






PMAY Targets




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)		whether the Government has set any targets under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) and if so, the details thereof;(b)		the actual achievements made against the targets thereunder during each of the last three years, State-wise; and (c)		the details regarding total fund allocation made and spent under the yojana during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): 	In pursuance to Government’s vision of ‘Housing for All’ by 2022, the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) has been implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) Mission since June 25, 2015 to assist States/Union Territories (UTs) both technically and financially in providing all weather pucca houses with basic civic infrastructure to all eligible urban households by year 2022. Against the validated demand of 1.12 crore houses across the country, so far, 1.11 crore houses have been sanctioned under the four verticals of PMAY-U Mission. Out of the sanctioned houses, more than 75 lakh houses are in various stages of construction, and over 43 lakh houses have been completed. (b) & (c): The State/UT-wise details of houses sanctioned, completed and year-wise completion during each of the last three years along with Central Assistance released and utilised under PMAY-U Mission are at Annexure.                                          *****
