                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres in Rajasthan




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the district-wise and place-wise details of Ayush Health and Wellness Centres functioning in Rajasthan; (b) whether the Government is considering to set up new Ayush Health and wellness Centres in the state; (c) if so, the district and location-wise details thereof along with the current status of setting up of such centres; and (d) the district-wise details of the proposals received for setting up of such centres in Rajsamand, Nagaur, Pali and Ajmer districts during the last three years along with the plan of the Government for setting up of the said centres?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYURVEDA, YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH)(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 323* FOR 25TH MARCH, 2022(a) to (c)	Yes, Sir. As per the proposal received from the State Government of Rajasthan in their State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), the Ministry of Ayush has approved for upgradation of 1000 Ayush Dispensaries into Ayush HWCs in the year 2020-21 and 2021-22 and Central Share of Rs. 5315.40 Lakh has been released to the State Government of Rajasthan. The district-wise and place-wise detail of approved Ayush HWCs and their current status of setting up / functionality is furnished at Annexure- I. (d) 	As per the proposal received from the State Government of Rajasthan in their SAAPs of NAM, the Ministry of Ayush has approved for upgradation of 138 Ayush Dispensaries into Ayush HWCs in Rajsamand, Nagaur, Pali and Ajmer districts in the year 2020-21 and 2021-22. The district-wise details of the proposals received regarding setting up of the said centres in Rajsamand, Nagaur, Pali and Ajmer districts during the last three years is furnished at Annexure- II.*****
