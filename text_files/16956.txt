                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

894






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Four-Laning Project on Barhi-Kodarma on NH-31




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the construction work of four laning of Barhi-Kodarma section on National Highway No-31 is being done on a very slow pace;(b)       if so, the reasons therefor; and(c)       the time frame set for completion of the said work?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c) The work of 4-laning of Barhi-Kodarma section of NH-31 was awarded in February, 2019. The same has been started on 18.11.2019 with scheduled completion date as 17.11.2021. In August, 2020, the physical progress was 10.50%. Due to Covid pandemic, slow progress was observed in last few months. This is being speeded up. *****
