                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

754






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Work on NH-6 in Maharashtra




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of construction of National Highway-6 from Pimprala at Khamgaon to Chikhali Malkapur under Buldhana region in Maharashtra;(b)	whether the work on the said NH has been held up and if so, the date since when the said construction work has remained closed alongwith the reasons therefor; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government for timely completion of the said project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) National Highway-6 from Pimprala at Khamgaon to Chikhali Malkapur under Buldhana region in Maharashtra is part of Amravati- Chikhli section. Earlier, 4-laning work of Amravati-Chikhli Stretch was implemented on BOT (Toll).  Appointed Date of the project was declared on 9.11.2016. The Concessionaire achieved physical progress of 22% upto July 2018. Subsequently the work was stopped due to the financial crisis with the Concessionaire. Therefore, Concession Agreement was terminated on account of Concessionaire’s default on 02.08.2019. For completing the balance work of the project, the bids have been received and evaluation of bids has been initiated.*****
