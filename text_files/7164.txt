                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

704






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Construction of Crossroad/Flyover




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	whether a crossroad with a cost of 17.5 crore rupees was proposed to be constructed at 	the location on Gwalior Road Army Airstrip connecting east-west four-lane corridor 	under Golden Quadrilateral project in Jhansi district of Uttar Pradesh and if so, the 	details and the present status thereof;(b) 	the reasons for delay in construction of the said crossroad/flyover; and (c) 	the time by which construction of the said crossroad/flyover is likely to be started and 	completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) No sir. At present, there is no such proposal for construction of cross road on Gwalior road army airstrip.  However, there is a provision for construction of a flyover at that location.  Construction of this flyover had been stopped by 664 Army Aviation Squadron (R&O), Jhansi due to safety concern.  *****
