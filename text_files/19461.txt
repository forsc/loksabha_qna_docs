                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

722






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Sanskrit Cinematic Films




L.S. Tejasvi Surya













                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the percentage breakup of films by language registered with CBFC, year-wise since 2008;(b)	the number of Sanskrit cinematic films (feature film, documentary and others) which have received CBFC certification in India, year-wise since 2008;(c)	whether the Government intends to provide incentives for the production, release and distribution of Sanskrit language films and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the number of Sanskrit cinematic films registered with CBFC and the number of Sanskrit cinematic films as a percentage of the total number of films with CBFC?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR):(a) The statement indicating percentage breakup of language-wise films certified by CBFC, year-wise since 2008, is at Annexure A.(b) The number of Sanskrit cinematic films (feature film, documentary and others) which have received certification in India, year-wise since 2008 are as follows:Year	Number of Sanskrit Films certified by CBFC From January 2008 to March 2015	02015-16	12016-17	12017-18	32018-19	02019-20	42020-21 (up to December, 2020)	4(c) There is no such proposal under consideration of the Government.(d) The number of Sanskrit cinematic films registered with CBFC and the number of Sanskrit cinematic films as a percentage of the total number of films with CBFC is at Annexure B.
