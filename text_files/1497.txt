                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1451






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Setting up of Indian Institutes of Art and Culture




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up an Indian Institute of Art and Culture (IIAC )on the lines of Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and Indian Institutes  of Management (IIMs); (b)	if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof; and(c)	the time by which these institutes are likely to be set up and made operational ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL )(a)	No, Sir. (b)	& (c) :  Does  not arise  in view of (a) above
