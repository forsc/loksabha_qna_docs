                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

578






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Import of Copper




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India’s copper imports have reported a sharp increase in the last two years; (b) if so, the reasons therefor; (c) whether it is true that India became a net importer beginning 2018-19; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)	to (d): The import of refined copper has increased from 44245 tonnes in 2017-18 to 92290 tonnes in 2018-19., whereas as exports declined from 378555 tonnes in 2017-18 to 47917 tonnes in 2018-19, which resulted in net import of 44373 tonnes in 2018-19 from net exports of 334310 tonnes in 2017-18. During the above period domestic production declined largely due to closure of a smelter since May, 2018, which has annual capacity of 4 lakh tonnes per annum. *****
