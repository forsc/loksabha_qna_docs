                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3569






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Land and Border Crossing Agreement




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of countries that have signed the land and border crossing agreement with India;(b) 	the other subjects on which agreement have been signed in order to promote financial and social connect; and (c)	the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) 	Land Border Crossing agreement has been signed between India and Myanmar in 2018.  (b) & (c)	The details of other subjects on which agreement have been signed with Myanmar in order to promote financial and social connect are at Annexure-I.  ***
