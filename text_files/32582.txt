                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

41






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






National Broadband Mission




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that the National Broadband Mission aims to provide 10 Mbps broadband speed at the end of this fiscal i.e., 2021-22; (b) 	if so, whether the Government is in line to achieve this target; (c) 	whether it is true that broadband speed of 4 Mbps target in 2020-21 has not been achieved so far; (d) 	If so, the manner in which the Government is planning to achieve 10 Mbps at the end of 2021-22; (e) 	whether it is also true that a target of 25 Mbps by 2022-23, 30 Mbps by 2023-24 and 50 Mbps by 2024- 25 is planned to be achieved; and (f) 	if so, the details of the plan of action that the Government has to achieve in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	Yes, it is true.(b) to (d) 	As per Ookla speed test global index March 2021 report (https://www.speedtest.net/global-index/india), India was experiencing Median download speeds of 8.19 Mbps in case of mobile broadband and around 38.85 Mbps in case of fixed broadband. As per December 2021 report (https://www.speedtest.net/global-index/india), India is experiencing Median download speeds of 14.17 Mbps in case of mobile broadband and around 47.48 Mbps in case of fixed broadband. (e)	Yes, it is true.(f)	Plan of action under the Mission include:i.	All villages to have access to broadband;ii.	Accelerate Fiberization; iii.	Increasing tower density; iv.	Increase fiberization of telecom towers; v.	 Easing of Right of Way (RoW) permissions in coordination with State/UTs & other central ministries.
