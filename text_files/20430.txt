                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Relief Package for MSME Sector




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any data of business benefit for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) Sector out of COVID-19 relief package titled under Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan; and (b)	if so, the details thereof including break-up of the same, State-wise ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. *149 FOR ANSWER ON 11.02.2021(a) & (b): 	 Government has taken a number of initiatives within the COVID-19 relief package under Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan for providing impetus to the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) sector in the country. These are: (i)	Rs. 3 Lakh crore Collateral Free Automatic Loans for business, including MSMEs.(ii)	Rs.20, 000 crore Subordinate Debt for Stressed MSMEs.(iii)	Rs. 50,000 crore equity infusion through MSME Fund of Funds.(iv)	New Revised criteria for classification of MSMEs. (v)	No global tenders for procurement up to Rs. 200 crores. State/UT wise number of beneficiaries under ‘Rs. 3 Lakh Crore Collateral Free Automatic Loans for business, including MSMEs’ is given at Annexure-I.State/UT wise number of beneficiaries under ‘Subordinate Debt for Stressed MSMEs’ is given at Annexure-II.State/UT wise number of MSMEs registered on the ‘Udyam Registration’ Portal is given at Annexure-III.
