                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Central Medical College & Hospital at Assam University




Rajdeep Roy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the reasons for not establishing Central Medical College and Hospital at Assam University, Silchar even after submission of DPR by the University; and (b) the steps being taken by the Government for the establishment of a Central Medical College and Hospital at Assam University after having being intimated of it’s selection vide letter no. F.24-11/2008(CU) dated 15.01.2010?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’ )(a) & (b):  The proposal for establishment of Central Medical College and Hospital in Central Universities including the Assam University, Silchar during the 11th Plan was not approved by the Government. ******
