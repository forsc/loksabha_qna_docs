                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Financial Assistance to Odisha




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to grant financial assistance to Odisha under the Special Assistance Scheme during the current financial year;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated for the same;(c) 	the time by which the said allocation is likely to be released to the State Government; and(d) 	the details of infrastructure projects identified for development by the State Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF FINANCE(SMT NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*293 REGARDING ‘FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE TO ODISHA’ RAISED BY SHRI BHARTRUHARI MAHTAB, MP, LOK SABHA FOR ANSWER ON 09.08.2021 (a) & (b): Yes Sir. The Union Government has allocated a sum of Rs.374 crore to the State of Odisha under the scheme ‘Special Assistance to States for Capital Expenditure for 2021-22’.(c) & (d): The State Government of Odisha is yet to submit a proposal under the scheme for 2021-22.                                                ******
