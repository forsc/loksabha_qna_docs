                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






PNG Supply in Bihar




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to supply Piped Natural Gas (PNG) to the households in Bihar including Katihar Parliamentary Constituency under city Gas distribution network; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether any agency has been engaged for implementation of the above proposal; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d):Establishment of CNG Stations and providing PNG connections is part of the development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network and the same is carried out by the entities authorized by Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB). Upto the 10th CGD bidding round, 11 Geographical Areas (GAs) including Katihar District, have been authorized in the state of Bihar by PNGRB. Further, in order to increase coverage of CGD networks, PNGRB has issued Letter of Intent (LoI) to BPCL for development of CGD network in one GA (Darbhanga, Madhubani, Supaul, Sitamarhi and Sheogar districts GA),  in Bihar recently.Details of GAs authorized in Bihar are given at Annexure. Further, PNGRB has launched 11A CGD bidding round for development of CGD network, which includes Banka district of Bihar.***
