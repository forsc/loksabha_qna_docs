                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

639






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Registered IT Companies




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)     the number of registered Information Technology (IT) companies functioning in the country as on date, State/ UT-wise; (b)   whether more than 50 percent of the companies functioning in IT sector are not registered; and(c)      if so, the details thereof including the number of such companies, State/ UT-wise and the action taken/ being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): As per Ministry of Corporate Affairs, there are total 31922 registered Information Technology (IT) Companies functioning in the country as on date. The State/ UT-wise list is Annexed.(b):  Government has no information with regard to companies working without registration in Information Technology (IT) Sector.(c): 	Does not arise.******* AnnexureS.
