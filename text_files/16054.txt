                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4583






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Satellite Centres




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is planning to start a PGI satellite centre at Ferozpur in Punjab;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the State Government has requested his Ministry to expedite the matter; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Yes, the Government has approved the proposal for setting up of PGI Satellite Centre at Ferozpur in Punjab at an estimated cost of Rs.490.54 crore. A land measuring 27.5 acre has been taken over by the Institute from the State Government of Punjab for the project.(c) & (d):	No references have been received from the State Government for expediting the matter after the approval.
