                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Scholarship Schemes in UP




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of applications received and funds approved for pre-matriculation, post-matriculation and Merit-cum-Means scholarship schemes implemented for minorities in Uttar Pradesh during the last five years;	(b) whether the State Government has fully utilised the funds and if not, the reasons therefor; and	(c) whether any amount is outstanding with the State Government under this scheme and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a): The Ministry of Minority Affairs implements three Scholarship Schemes viz. Pre-Matric, Post Matric and Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Schemes for the educational empowerment of students belonging to economically weaker sections of the six notified minority communities i.e. Buddhist, Christian, Jain, Muslim, Parsi and Sikh in all States/UTs across the country. The details of applications received, verified and funds approved under the 3 Scholarship Schemes for the applicants of Uttar Pradesh during the last five years i.e. from 2016-17 to 2020-21 are as under:-Scheme	Final verified  applications from U.P.	Scholarships disbursed (in Rs. Crore)Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme	37,21,766	1066.24Post Matric Scholarship Scheme	5,31,248	328.20Merit-cum-Means Scholarship Scheme	57,379	142.57 (b) & (c): The scholarships are disbursed directly in the beneficiary account, under Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode.*****
