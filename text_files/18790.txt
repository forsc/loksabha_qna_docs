                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

205






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Anganwadi Centres




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry has approved the convergence of Anganwadi Centers (AWCs) in the most backward districts of Andhra Pradesh under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS) with Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether this was to be completed by 2019 and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the details of districts identified in Andhra Pradesh as backward districts, number of Anganwadi Centres proposed to be constructed and the final status of the same; and(d) 	the number of AWCs having permanent buildings and the number of such centres functioning from rented accommodation in Andhra Pradesh, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)&(b):  The Construction of Anganwadi Center (AWC) is a permissible activity under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS for all States/UTs. Expenditure up to Rs.5.00 lakh under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is allowed and the balance cost as per the State specific estimate may be mobilised from the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) schemes of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India and other schemes as per their provision.  AWC can be taken up as per demand in the rural areas after following due process under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS and ongoing activity. There was no timeline for completion of AWCs by 2019.(c):  	Ministry has not identified any backward district of any State /UT including Andhra Pradesh for construction of AWC. The work can be taken as per demand in rural areas of any districts. (d):    This Ministry does not maintain number of AWCs having permanent buildings and the number of such centres functioning from rented accommodation centrally. However, the number of AWC building constructed under the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in Andhra Pradesh district-wise is annexed.******Annexure referred in reply to part (d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.205 dated 02.02.2021
