                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1724






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Induction of Women in Combat




Amar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Army has resisted the induction of women into combat arms on several grounds;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to induct women in combat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (c):  Women officers are commissioned  in all Arms and Services except Infantry, Armoured Corps, Mechanized Infantry, and Regiment of Artillery.  The Hon’ble Supreme Court has also upheld the policy of the Government for not opening areas of operation for women officers.*****
