                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

582






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Cities Covered under PMAY




Jayanta Kumar Roy













                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the details of cities covered under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) in Odisha and West Bengal under various phases of this scheme; (b)  the details of the houses sanctioned and constructed and also the amount of grant given in this regard in each district of the said two States, phase-wise; (c)  the details of beneficiaries in the States who have been provided houses in various phases under the yojana so far; (d)  whether the targets fixed under the yojana for above two States have been achieved so far; and (e)  if so, the other steps being taken by the Government to provide housing for all in these States?






ANSWER




                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)to (c):The details of cities covered, houses sanctioned, constructed and provided to the beneficiaries as well as central assistance released to each district under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban [PMAY-U] in the State of Odisha and West Bengal are at Annexure I and Annexure II respectively.(d):Under PMAY-U, Government of India does not fix any target for construction of houses in a State/Union Territory (UT). All State/UT Governments are required to undertake a demand survey to assess their housing requirement for the eligible beneficiaries and prepare project proposals accordingly. As per the demand survey, the States of Odisha and West Bengal have reported housing requirement of 1.80 lakh and 5.50 lakh respectively. Against these demands, 1.77 lakh and 5.28 lakh houses have been sanctioned for Odisha and West Bengal respectively. (e):The States have been advised to ground for construction the balance sanctioned houses, complete all houses already grounded and saturate the demand city-wise to achieve the Mission of Housing for All within the stipulated period.                                    *****
