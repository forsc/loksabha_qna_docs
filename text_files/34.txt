                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

94






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Setting up of Virology Institute in Kerala




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of virology institutes and research centres functioning in thecountry, State/UT-wise; (b) whether the Government has received any request from the Government of Kerala or from any other quarter for setting up of a new virology institute/ laboratory in the State;(c) if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government in this regard;(d) whether the Government has given any financial assistance to the State of Kerala for setting up of a new virology institute/laboratory in Kerala; and(e) if so, give details and the present status of implementation of the project?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	A National Institute of virology is functioning at Pune, Maharashtra, with its four field stations at Kerala, Mumbai, Gorakhpur and Bangalore. Apart from this, details of 95 viral Research and Diagnosis Laboratories, State/UT-wise sanctioned by the Deptt., are given in annexure.(b):	No such request has been   received in the current year.(c):	Does not arise.(d) & (e):	Financial assistance to the tune   of Rs. 12.49 cr. has been given to following four Institutes in Kerala, for setting up VRDLs from 2014 to 2018: 1.	Govt. Medical College, Trivandrum (Operational)2.	Govt. Medical College, Kozhikode (Operational)3.	Govt. Medical College, Thrissur (Operational)4.	National Institute of Virology Field Unit at Alappuzha (under process)
