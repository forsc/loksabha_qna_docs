                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1538






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Industrial Nodes in Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the places identified for development as industrial nodes or cities in Maharashtra under different Industrial Corridors;(b) 	the status of proposed industrial nodes above, their investment and employment potential;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (d):  	The status of industrial nodes identified in the state of Maharashtra are  as under: 1.	Shendra Bidkin Industrial Area (SBIA) under Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC)  (a)Shendra Industrial AreaSpecial Purpose Vehicle (SPV) i.e. Aurangabad Industrial Township Limited has been incorporated. State Government has transferred the entire 8.39 sq kms of land to the SPV and matching equity of Rs. 602.80 Crore has been released by Government of India (GoI) through National Industrial Corridor Development and Implementation Trust (NICDIT). Major trunk infrastructure works have been completed.  93 plots have been allotted including allotment of 100 acres to anchor investor i.e., HYOSUNG a South Korean company. An investment of over Rs. 6,500 crore has been attracted in this area. The project was dedicated to the nation by Hon''ble PM on 7th September 2019. (b) Bidkin Industrial Area – Out of total 32 sq. kms, State Govt. has transferred land admeasuring 28.8 sq. kms and matching equity of Rs. 2397.20 Crore was also released by GoI through NICDIT. Trunk infrastructure works are nearing completion.  As per Development Plan, employment generation potential for SBIA is approximately 7.5 lakhs (Direct & Indirect). 2.	Dighi Industrial Area under DMIC:State Government has confirmed availability of 2,402 Ha (5,935 acres) of land, out of which 1,466 Ha (3,622 acres) is in possession of State Government. Detailed master planning and preliminary engineering for the land parcels have been initiated.  As per the Development Plan, employment generation potential for Dighi Industrial Area is approximately 3.2 lakhs (Direct & Indirect). 3.	Satara node under Bengaluru Mumbai Industrial Corridor (BMIC) –In March 2021, State Government of Maharashtra has confirmed the availability of 5,000 Ha land at Satara for development of industrial node.  Out of which 314.06 Ha land is in possession of State Government. Tender document for selection of consultant for Detailed Master Planning and Preliminary Engineering has been issued. ********
