                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3754






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






New Janaushadhi Stores




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any progress has been made in implementation of Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Jan-Aushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) Scheme since 2020-21, if so, the details thereof; (b) the number of new stores opened under the scheme in the country; and (c) the steps taken to facilitate citizens in locating PMBJP stores?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): Yes Sir, lot of progress has been made in implementation of Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) since 2020-21. Additional 1,132 new Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) have been opened during the period from 1st April, 2021 to 28th February, 2022. As on 28.02.2022, about 8,689 PMBJKs have been opened across the country, covering all districts of the country. State-UT-wise list of PMBJKs is attached as Annexure.Further, the product basket of medicines and surgical devices has increased from 1,451 and 204 to 1,616 and 250 respectively during the period. The total sale has also increased from Rs. 665.83 crore during 2020-21 to Rs. 814.21 crore in the current financial year till 28th February, 2022. (c): A mobile application “Janaushadhi Sugam” has been launched, which is an important facility for the general public by providing a digital platform at the tip of their fingers, by the virtue of which they can avail a host of user-friendly options like - locate nearby PMBJK (direction guided through Google Maps), search Janaushadhi medicines, analyze product comparison of Generic vs. Branded medicine in the form of MRP saving etc. AnnexureStatement referred to in parts (a) & (b) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Q.
