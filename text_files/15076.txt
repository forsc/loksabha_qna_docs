                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

329






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






PMAY-G in Tamil Nadu




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of houses proposed to be built and completed in the Tirunelveli district under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin) (PMAY-G);(b) 	the total amount of funds allocated, disbursed and utilised in Tamil Nadu under PMAY-G, district-wise including Tirunelveli; and(c) 	the total number of beneficiaries under the said Yojana in Tamil Nadu, district-wise including Tirunelveli?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*329 TO BE ANSWERED ON 17.03.2020  (a) to (c): To achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by 2022, Ministry of Rural Development has been implementing Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) (restructured from the erstwhile Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY) with effect from 01.04.2016. The Central Government allocates targets and funds to the State as a unit and the further  sub-allocation of targets and funds to Districts/Gram Panchayat/Villages is made by the State Government itself. In the Tirunelveli district under PMAY-G, 7,102 houses have been constructed against the target of 13,175 as on 12.03.2020.  The Central Government releases to  Tamilnadu State during the last four financial years are as under:- S.No.	Financial Year	       Amount of Central Release                                            (in lakhs)1.	2016-17	                          69059.7702.	2017-18	                          84848.5783.	2018-19	                          50279.8104.	2019-20	                          48752.12A Statement indicating district-wise details, including Tirunelveli District, of number of beneficiaries, funds allocated, disbursed and utilized in the State of Tamil Nadu under PMAY-G is given at Annexure-I.*******
