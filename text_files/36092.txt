                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Evacuation from Ukraine




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the time when the Ministry of External Affairs first approached to the Ministry of Civil Aviation for evacuation of Indian citizens from Ukraine;(b) the number of flights which were sought, datewise; and(c) the estimated number of people needed evacuation?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) Ministry of External Affairs was in touch with the Ministry of Civil Aviation from the middle of February, 2022, regarding air bubble operations, addition of commercial flights and subsequently for operation of flights by private airlines under Operation Ganga.(b) Flights were sought based on the numbers of Indian nationals crossing the border to the Western neighboring countries of Ukraine that includes Poland, Slovakia, Romania and Hungary. 90 evacuation flights were operated under Operation Ganga, including 14 Indian Air Force flights. The details of the flights operated under Operation Ganga are given at Annexure.(c) Around 22,500 Indian nationals have returned from Ukraine to India from 1 February - 11 March, 2022.******
