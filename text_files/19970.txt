                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






4G Network of BSNL




Shankar Lalwani













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps taken by the Government to launch BSNL’s 4G network and the time by when it is likely to be done;(b) 	the details of the districts proposed to be covered under the said 4G network in the first phase, State/UT-wise including Bhavnagar and Botad districts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b) The Government on 23.10.2019 has approved administrative allotment of spectrum for 4G services with Government support to Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) as part of its revival package. BSNL has floated the Expression of Interest (EOI) on 01.01.2021 for prior registration/Proof of Concept (PoC) from Indian Companies interested in participating in BSNL’s upcoming 4G tender.BSNL has also informed that it has already introduced 4G services on a limited scale at few locations using its existing spectrum for 3G services.  As on 31.12.2020, a total of 8,629 4G e-Nodes are operational in BSNL mobile network.  BSNL plans to roll out 4G services on large scale after finalisation of tender for procurement of the 4G equipment.
