                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

852






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Seekho Aur Kamao Scheme




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of applications received for opening training centres under “Seekho aur Kamao” Scheme in the State of Chhattisgarh during the year 2019-20; and(b)	the number of institutions selected by the Government under the said scheme in the State of Chhattisgarh for this purpose during the last year?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI. MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) & (b) The Ministry of Minority Affairs does not directly invite applications for opening training centres under the “Seekho aur Kamao” Scheme nor selects institutions state wise for implementation under the said scheme.Under the Seekho aur Kamao Scheme, this Ministry however, invites applications from Project Implementing Agencies (PIAs) which also include Government agencies/autonoums bodies from all over India for imparting skill training to the trainees. Ministry issues allotment letter for number of trainee to be trained by a particular PIA. During the allotment in the FY 2018-19, one PIA from the state of Chhattisgarh was empaneled for a period of two years. *****
