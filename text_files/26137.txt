                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






National Geospatial Policy




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is implementing National Geospatial Policy in the country and if so, the details and objectives thereof;(b) whether there is any monitoring mechanism to track the implementation of the National Geospatial Policy and if so, details thereof;(c)  whether there is any mechanism to support Indian mapping companies under the National Geospatial Policy so that the monopoly of Google Maps can be checked; and(d)  if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND EARTH SCIENCES(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)??????? ?? ???????????? ??? ?????? ??????? ??  ????? ?????? (???????? ??????)  (??. ???????? ????) (a):   No, Sir. (b), (c) & (d):  Does not arise.
