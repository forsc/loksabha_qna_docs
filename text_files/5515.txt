                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

456






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Upgradation of Railway Stations




R.K. Singh Patel













                                                    Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any decision to upgrade 400 railway stations including Chitrakutdham Karwi railway station under North Eastern Railway in view of tourism and commercial importance;(b) if so, the details thereof and the present status in this regard;(c) whether Sawai Madhopur station has been included therein, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of developers who have shown keenness to redevelop these railway stations?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF STARRED QUESTION NO.456 BY SHRI R.K. SINGH PATEL AND SHRI SUKHBIR SINGH JAUNAPURIA TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 24.07.2019 REGARDING UPGRADATION OF RAILWAY STATIONS(a) to (d): Ministry of Railways through various agencies is undertaking techno-economic feasibility studies of Railway stations including Chitrakutdham Karwi and Sawai Madhopur stations in a phased manner.  Based on the outcome of these feasibility studies, stations are planned to be taken up for redevelopment in phases, especially the stations located in major cities and important tourist destinations.  The cost of station redevelopment project is to be met by leveraging commercial development of land and air space in and around the stations.	The developers are to be selected through transparent bidding process.  After the techno-economic feasibility studies reports are ready, the bids are to be invited for stations in a phased manner.  *****
