                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1191






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Quality Control of Steel Products




M V V Satyanarayana













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has constituted a Technical Committee for examination and analysis of the applications received for issuance of clarifications, whether the products are covered under the purview of the Steel and Steel Products (Quality Control) Order 2020;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the total number of such applications received and scrutinized from the State of Andhra Pradesh and a State-wise break-up thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)&(b): Yes, Sir. Ministry of Steel has constituted a Technical Committee, comprising officials of BIS and domain experts from the industry to examine  applications for import of steel products whether the steel products being imported fall within the purview of the Steel Quality Control Order or otherwise. Based on the recommendation of Technical Committee, Ministry of Steel issues requisite clarification to the applicants.  (c): While information was earlier not being maintained State-wise, Ministry of Steel has set up a dedicated QCO portal for processing the applications since 27th July, 2020. As per the state-wise break-up of 472 applications (Annex) received in the Portal so far, no application has been received from the State of Andhra Pradesh. ********
