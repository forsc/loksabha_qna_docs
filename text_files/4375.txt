                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3934






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Re-employment of Higher Officials




Giridhari Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any information regarding re-employment of higher officials of Ministry of Communications, BSNL and MTNL in private telephone companies after retirement; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has laid down any rules regarding re-employment of higher officers in other services after retirement and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (d) 	Commercial employment of a pensioner before expiry of one year from the date of retirement, who immediately before his retirement was a member of Central Civil Service Group ‘A’, requires sanction of Government in terms of Rule-10 of Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972 as amended from time to time.As per available records, details of higher officials who joined commercial employment  in  private  telephone  companies  in  violation  of  Central  Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972 and action taken by Government are as under:<pre>S.No.	Details of Officials	Action taken 1	Shri P.K. Khindri, Former General Manager, Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) T&D Circle, Jabalpur	Penalised with stoppage of full pension for a period of two years for joining M/s Reliance Industries.2	Shri Shabbir Ahmed,Director, BSNL Corporate Office, New Delhi	Penalised with deduction of 25% of pension for one year for joining M/s Reliance Infocomm. Limited.3	Shri Satya Pal Kalsi,Former Chief General Manager,BSNL, Uttar Pradesh (East) Circle.	Penalised with deduction of 25% of pension for one year for joining M/s Reliance Infocomm. Limited.4	Shri P. Harihar Kumar,Former General Manager, Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited (MTNL)	Penalised with forfeiture of pension for the period from 01.07.1999 to 24.05.2000 for joining M/s Bharti Cellular Limited.</pre>
