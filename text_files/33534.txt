                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

956






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






E-Sports




Mahua Moitra














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken cognizance of reports from Forbes, an American Business Magazine that India is the 16th largest E-sports Market in the world and E-Sports also forms part of the Asian Games 2022;(b) if so, the details thereof and the position of the Government with regard to E-Sports in the country;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to come out with a national E-Sports regulation or Act, if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government proposes to support E-Sports aspirants in the Asian Games 2022, if so, the details thereof and(e) whether the Government is likely to take a lead in promoting E-Sports in the country and and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS {SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR}(a) to (e):	Promotion and development of any sports is mainly the responsibility of the concerned National Sports Federation (NSF). This Ministry supplements the efforts of the NSFs by providing financial assistance to the recognized NSFs for preparation and participation of Indian sportspersons/teams in international sporting events. E-sports which is in the nature of competitive sports has also been included in the Asian Games, 2022 as a medal sports discipline. E-sports falls under the purview of the National Sports Development Code of India, 2011 for the purposes of regulating the sportspersons and other related stakeholders in the field of e-sports. ***
