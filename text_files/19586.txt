                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

762






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Mahila e-Haats




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu













                                                    Sanjay Jaiswal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















762. 	SHRI LAVU SRI KRISHNA DEVARAYALU:        	DR. SANJAY JAISWAL: Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state: (a) 	The aims and objectives of Mahila e-Haats; (b)  	The number of entrepreneurs registered on the ‘Mahila e-Haat’ portal across the country during the past three years, State/UT-wise; (c) 	Whether the e-portal has played a key role in financially empowering the women across the country and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	Whether the Government has taken note of the fact that just 5 entrepreneurs from Andhra Pradesh and Telangana have registered under Mahila e-Haat; (e) 	Whether the Government has taken note of the lack of adequate Mahila e-Haat marketing platforms; and (f) 	If so, the details there of including the efforts being made by the Ministry to make Mahila e-Haats accessible to every women entrepreneur in the country? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) “Mahila E-haat”, is an online marketing platform for women entrepreneurs.(b) to (d)  The State wise list of women entrepreneurs registered at "Mahila E-haat" is Annexed. (e) and (f)  Government has promoted platforms such as Government e-Market Place (GeM) and e-market place managed by National  Small Industries  Corporation  (NSIC)  to support  the  entrepreneurs. In view thereof, the list of vendors registered at Mahila E-haat has been allocated to Ministry of Food Processing Industries, Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises and Ministry of Commerce and Industry to facilitate their market linkage.**** Annexure Statement  referred  to  in  reply  to  part  (b)  of  the  Lok  Sabha  Unstarred Question No. 762 to be answered on 05.02.2021 asked by Hon’ble Members of Parliament, Shri Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu & Dr. Sanjay Jaiswal regarding " Mahila e-Haats”Name of State/UT	No. of women entrepreneurs registered under Mahila E-haatAndhra Pradesh	05Assam	02Bihar	03Chhattisgarh	15Delhi	36Goa	01Gujarat	12Haryana	09Jharkhand	07Karnataka	12Kerala	35Madhya Pradesh	37Maharashtra	54Meghalaya	07Odisha	03Punjab	07Rajasthan	09Tamil Nadu	08Telangana	09Uttar Pradesh	33West Bengal	17TOTAL	321
