                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3730






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Beekeeping in Meghalaya




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Integrated Bee Development Centres (IBDCs) have been established  in the North-Eastern Region (NER) under the National Beekeeping & Honey Mission (NBHM),  if so, the details of the number of such centres, state-wise;(b)	the number of honey testing laboratories established in Meghalaya under NBHM; and(c)	the steps taken to promote the beekeeping industry in Meghalaya.






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a, b & c):	 Two Integrated Beekeeping Development Centres (IBDCs) have been set up in North-Eastern Region (NER), viz.; (i) at College of Horticulture and Forestry (CHF), Pashighat, Arunachal Pradesh, and (ii) at Central Agricultural University (CAU), Imphal, Manipur by National Bee Board (NBB) under the scheme “Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), which was being implemented for promotion of beekeeping before launching of National Beekeeping and Honey Mission (NBHM).   At present, there is no honey testing lab established in Meghalaya under National Beekeeping and Honey Mission. However, trainings/awareness programmes are being conducted to promote scientific beekeeping in North East Region including Meghalaya. *****
