                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Achievements of KVIC




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) has been successful in achieving their objectives during the last five years and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the details of the development schemes/programmes being implemented by KVIC and the funds allocated therefor during each of the last five years, State/ UT-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has initiated any reform and development programme for KVIC; (d)	 if so, the details thereof along with the extent to which it is likely to help KVIC in achieving its objectives and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the other steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (e):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY PARTS (a) TO (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *468 FOR ANSWER ON 25.07.2019(a):	Yes, Sir. The State-wise release of margin money subsidy, which is treated as target, and the achievements in terms of margin money subsidy utilized for setting up micro enterprises, for the last five years under PMEGP, is given at Annexure-I.(c) to (e):	Yes, Sir. The Khadi Reform Development Program (KRDP) has been initiated with financial assistance from Asian Development Bank (ADB). KRDP has been designed to revitalize the Khadi sector with enhanced sustainability of Khadi, increased incomes and employment for spinners and weavers, increased artisans’ welfare and achieve synergy with village industries. ‘Khadi Reform Package’ envisages reform support in the following areas: (i) Artisan Earnings and Empowerment, (ii) Direct Reform Assistance to 400 Khadi Institutions & (iii) Implementation of a well-knit MIS.300 Khadi Institutions have been identified for reform assistance. The Programme contains a provision for updation of Central Sliver Plants of KVIC, market development activities like market survey, design inputs, brand building, ‘Khadi Mark’ promotion, development of clusters in five traditional village industries, opening of new sales outlets in metropolitan cities and State capitals, and renovation and modernization of institutional sales outlets. KRDP is closely monitored and reviewed at the level of implementing agency i.e KVIC and the Ministry. Efforts for systemic improvement in the light of implementation problems are a continuous process. ‘Khadi Mark’ has also been introduced under KRDP to guarantee the genuineness and purity of Khadi sold in the market. Improvement in sale of KVI products is expected by introduction of ‘Khadi Mark’. New initiatives taken by the Government for promotion of Khadi products are mentioned in Annexure-II.
