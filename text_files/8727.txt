                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2287






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Employees State Insurance Hospital in Hyderabad




Adala Prabhakara Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether Employees State Insurance Hospital in Hyderabad is riddled with scams; (b)if so, the details thereof;(c)whether any arrest has been made in this regard; and(d)if so, the measures taken/being taken to put in place a mechanism to avoid recurrence of such scams?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	A news article on discrepancy in purchase of medicines for State Government run ESIS hospitals in Telangana and consequential arrest of an official published in newspaper, has been reported to Employees’ State Insurance Corporation, (ESIC), New Delhi.(d):	 	From time to time, ESIC issues guidelines to various State Governments for procurement of medicines through ESIC Rate Contract/State Rate Contract including compliance of General Financial Rules.*****
