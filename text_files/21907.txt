                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3005






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Software Application for Labour Survey




Subrata Pathak













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has launched labour survey software application in the recent past;(b)if so, the details along with aims and objectives thereof; (c)the salient features of the said labour survey software application;(d)the details of labour survey being undertaken by labour bureau, institutions in the country; and (e)the manner in which the results of this survey will be beneficial for the Government for future Planning, estimates, strategy and policy for the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	Yes, Sir. The Government has launched labour survey software application on 18th February, 2021.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(b):	The aim of the labour survey software application is to implement online capturing of data in the surveys being undertaken by Labour Bureau to ensure timely processing of data and release of results.(c):	The labour survey software application will facilitate conduct of surveys being undertaken by Labour Bureau in digitalization mode through Tablets/Mobiles by utilizing latest Information Technology tools.(d):	Labour Bureau, an attached office of the Ministry of Labour & Employment has been entrusted with the task of conducting the following All- India Surveys: -(i)    All India Survey on Migrant Workers;(ii)   All India Survey on Domestic Workers;(iii)  All India Survey on Employment Generated by Professionals (iv)  All India Survey on Employment Generated in the Transport Sector and(v)  All India Quarterly Establishment based Employment Survey (AQEES). (e):	The results from these surveys would help the Government to take suitable evidence based policy intervention and decision making. ******
