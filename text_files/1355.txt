                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1170






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Sam Pitroda Committee in 2014




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed













                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the recommendations of Sam Pitroda led Expert Panel on Prasar Bharati which submitted its report in 2014; (b)	the detailed recommendations made by the Committee; (c)	whether these recommendations have been implemented fully or partially; (d)	whether the Government has started manpower and other audit of Prasar Bharati, All India Radio and Doordarshan on the lines of the recommendations made by the Expert Committee, if so, the details thereof and the time by which this exercise is likely to be completed; and(e)	the other steps being taken by the Government to make Prasar Bharati, All India Radio and Doordarshan more viable? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING[SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR](a) to (c): The Sam Pitroda Committee gave various recommendations in its report of 24.01.2014 on matters such as governance, human resource, content, technology, etc. which are available on the website of the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting. These recommendations were considered by the Government and an Action Taken Report (ATR) was finalised. Steps towards implementation have been taken on the recommendations accepted by the Government. (d):	Contract for carrying out Manpower Audit of Prasar Bharati was awarded in November 2018, with a time-line of 15 months. (e): Prasar Bharati has taken several steps for financial strengthening including Monetization of Digital Terrestrial Transmitters (DTT), e-auction of channels on DD Free Dish, Digital Monetization of News, in-house content and Archival Content. ********
