                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Schemes to Popularise Tourist Sites in Rajasthan




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a)	the schemes of the Government to popularize tourist attraction sites in Rajasthan; (b)	whether any campaign has been started abroad to attract foreign tourist to the religious, old havelies and tourist attraction places of Rajasthan;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the total foreign exchange earned from tourists visiting Rajasthan during the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Ministry of Tourism under the schemes of “SwadeshDarshan” and “National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive” - PRASHAD  provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administration/Central Agencies  for development of tourism infrastructure in the country, including Rajasthan,  in consultation with them. (b)&(c):	Ministry of Tourism, promotes India in a holistic manner, including Rajasthan and as part of  its on-going activities, it releases global print, electronic and online media campaigns in  important and potential markets overseas, under the ‘Incredible India’ brand-line, to promote various tourism destinations  and  products  of the country.  Promotions are also undertaken through the website (www.incredibleindia.org) and Social Media accounts of the Ministry. The promotions are undertaken through the “Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality Scheme (DPPH) and “Overseas Promotion and Publicity including Market Development assistance” (OPMD) Schemes of Ministry of Tourism. (d):	The Foreign Exchange Earnings (FEE) for the last two years, pan India, are as follows :-2019 -    Rs.211661 crore (provisional)2020 -    Rs.50136 crore   (provisional)However, the state wise details of Foreign Exchange earned through tourism is not maintained by Ministry of Tourism.*****
