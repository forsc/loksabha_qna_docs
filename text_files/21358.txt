                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2558






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






OFC Connectivity in Madhya Pradesh




Riti Pathak













                                                    Rajan Baburao  Vichare
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Will the Minister of COMMUNICATIONS be pleased to state the number of villages in Sidhi district of Madhya Pradesh which have been connected by Optical Fiber Cable (OFC)?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to create network to connect all the Gram Panchayats (approximately 2.5 lakh) with broadband in the country, including Sidhi District of Madhya Pradesh. As of now, 152 Gram Panchayats (including Block Headquarters) have been connected over Optical Fiber Cable (OFC) and also made Service Ready in Sidhi District.************
