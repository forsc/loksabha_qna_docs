                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4973






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Indians Repatriated under Vande Bharat Mission




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether many students go abroad to study different subjects and if so, the details thereof during each of the last five years and the current year, country- wise and State/UT- wise including Bihar;(b)	the number of students studying medicine and engineering abroad, country- wise, stream- wise; and(c)	the number of Indians repatriated from foreign countries under Vande Bharat Mission during the last five years and the current year, State/ UT- wise including Bihar?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	Sizeable number of Indian students go abroad for studies. As per the information received from Bureau of Immigration (BoI), the total number of students, including from Bihar who proceeded abroad for studies during the last five years are as follows: <pre>Sl No.Calendar YearNo. of students1.20174,54,0092.20185,17,9983.20195,86,3374.20202,59,6555.20214,44,5536.2022 (up to 20.03.2022)1,33,135</pre>		The country-wise details of number of Indians who disclosed their purpose of visit as “study” while going abroad from 2017 to 2022 (till 20.03.2022) is placed at Annexure-I.(c)		Vande Bharat Mission (VBM) was launched on 7 May 2020 to repatriate stranded and distressed Indian nationals in various parts of the world due to the COVID-19 pandemic. As on 21 March 2022, 3.09 crore passengers including from Bihar (in–bound and out– bound) have been facilitated in the flights operated under the VBM and Air Bubble Arrangements.***
