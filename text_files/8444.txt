                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2058






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Setting up AIIMS-like Institutions




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Bharti Pravin Pawar
                                                








                                                    Narendra Kumar
                                                








                                                    Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru
                                                








                                                    Ramya  Haridas
                                                








                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                








                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                








                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                








                                                    K. Muraleedharan
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                








                                                    S.R. Parthiban
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has approved setting up of new AIIMS-like institutes in various parts of the country, if so, the details thereof along with their present status, State/UT-wise;(b) the guidelines in place for setting up of AIIMS-like institutes in the country;(c) whether various State Governments have requested for setting up AIIMS-like hospitals in their respective States, if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and the decision taken bythe Government on each such request/ proposal;(d) whether the Government has set any plan outlay and time limit for completion of such AIIMS institutes in the country, if so, the details thereof; and(e) the funds allocated and utilized for the proposed AIIMS institutions, institution-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	22 AIIMS have been announced/sanctioned to be set under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY). Six (06) AIIMS i.e. Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Raipur and Rishikesh are functional. Details are as per Annexure.(b):	Guiding principle for establishing additional AIIMS like institutions/hospitals in the country is the Presidential address to the Parliament dated 9th June, 2014 wherein it was inter-alia announced that “to address the shortfall of health care professionals, health education and training will be transformed and AIIMS like institutes will be established in every State in a phased manner”.Under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY), All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) are being set up in various states of the country as per the budget announcements.(c):	Yes. Apart from 22 new AIIMS which have been announced/sanctioned, proposals for setting up AIIMS-like hospitals have been received from Arunachal Pradesh, Goa, Karnataka, Kerala, Tripura and Mizoram. AIIMS are being set up in various parts of the country in a phased manner as per budget announcements keeping in view gaps in tertiary healthcare infrastructures in different regions of the country and availability of funds.(d) & (e):	Details of approved outlay, time limit for completion of AIIMS and fund position is attached as Annexure.
