                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

625






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Capital Procurement for Defence




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently given approval of Capital Procurement for the Defence forces amounting to over Rs. 3300 crores of indigenously designed and developed equipment to promote the Make in India Initiative; and(b) if so, a broad break up of the sectors for which the amount is earmarked?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (b):  The Defence Acquisition Council (DAC) has given approval of Capital Procurement for the Defence Forces amounting to over Rs.3300 crores of indigenously designed, developed and manufactured equipment to maintain the impetus on “Make in India” Initiative.  The three projects are the Third Generation ATGM, Auxiliary Power Units for T-72 and T-90 Tanks and Electronic Warfare (EW) systems for mountain and High Altitude terrain.*****
