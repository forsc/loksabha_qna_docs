                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

82






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Tribal University




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a proposal to set up Tribal University in Banswara/Dungarpur district;(b)	if so, the time by which the said University is likely to be set up;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether land has been allocated for the said University;(e)	if so, the details thereof; and (f)	whether reservation in appointments is proposed to be followed/prescribed in the said 	University?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’)(a) to (f) A statement is laid on the table of the House. ***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (F) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 82 TO BE ANSWERED ON 08.02.2021 ASKED BY SHRI KANAKMAL KATARA, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING TRIBAL UNIVERSITY(a) to (f)	Setting up of Central University is an ongoing process. However, at present there is no proposal under consideration in the Ministry for setting up of a Central Tribal University in Banswara/Dungarpur district, Rajasthan.*****
