                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2965






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Delhi-Lucknow NH Project




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of work on the Delhi-Lucknow National Highway (NH); (b)	the time frame set for completion of the said NH; and (c)	the number of toll booths that are functioning on this NH along with the revenue generated therefrom?






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)   The section wise present status of Delhi-Lucknow National Highway works, the time frame set for completion and the number of toll booths along with the revenue generated are as under:Name of Section	Present Status or Physical progress	Time frame set for completion	No of Toll Plazas	Revenue Delhi to Hapur Bypass	Completed except  ROB work and its approaches	December, 2021	1	Rs. 50.99 lacs per dayHapur Bypass -Moradabad Section 	72.72 %	February, 2022	2	Toll Plaza 1– Rs.  23 lacs per dayToll Plaza 2– Rs. 12 lacs per dayMoradabad-Bareilly Section 	Completed 	Completed	2	Toll Plaza 1– Rs. 40 lacs per dayToll Plaza 2– Rs. 33 lacs per dayBareilly-Sitapur section 	37.5%	March, 2022	Nil	Not applicableSitapur to Lucknow Section	Completed	Completed	2	Total revenue collected by BOT Concessionaire is Rs. 465 crores.*****
