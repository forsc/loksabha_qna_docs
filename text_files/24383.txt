                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

376






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Setting up of Pharma Park




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Uttar Pradesh for setting up Pharma Park; (b) if so, the details thereof, district-wise; (c) the name of the places where Pharma Parks have been established recently along with the cost incurred in this regard; (d) whether world class Bulk Drugs and Medical Devices Parks (Pharma Park) have been established in States including Uttar Pradesh; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS                                   (SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) to (e):  Department of Pharmaceuticals has received a proposal from the Government of Uttar Pradesh for establishment of a Bulk Drug Park under the scheme for “Promotion of Bulk Drug Parks”. The proposed site for bulk drug park is located in Saidpur Village (Mehrauni Tehsil) of Lalitpur District. The appraisal of the proposals received under the schemes viz. Promotion of Bulk Drug Parks and Promotion of Medical Device Parks has been taken up. The schemes provide for the selection of 3 states for providing grant-in-aid to Bulk drug Parks and 4 States for providing grant-in-aid to Medical Devices Parks as per the evaluation criteria laid down in the published guidelines of the scheme.xxxxx
