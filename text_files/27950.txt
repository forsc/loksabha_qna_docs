                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3648






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL) comes under the category of Maharatna companies owned by the Government;(b)	if so, the total number of branches of BHEL in the country and abroad, location-wise; and(c)	the annual turnover of BHEL along with the total number of employees working therein? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a):	Yes, Sir.(b):	The details of branches of BHEL are given at Annexure.(c):	The financial turnover of BHEL during last financial year 2020-21 was Rs. 16,296 Crore.  The total number of employees working in BHEL as on 31.03.2021 was 32,131. ------------  ANNEXUREMajor locations and number from where business activity is undertaken by BHELNational locations (in India)	i) Registered & Corporate Office : New Delhi.ii) Manufacturing Units/Divisions (16 nos.) : Bengaluru (3 Units), Bhopal, Goindwal, Haridwar (2 Units), Hyderabad, Jagdishpur, Jhansi, Ranipet, Rudrapur, Thirumayam, Tiruchirappalli (2 Units) and Visakhapatnam.iii) Corporate R&D Division : Hyderabad.iv) Repair Units/Centres (2 nos.) : Mumbai and Varanasi.v) Regional Offices - Power Sector (4 nos.) : Noida, Kolkata, Nagpur and Chennai.vi) Service Centres (8 nos.) : Chandigarh, Kolkata, Nagpur, Noida, Patna, Secunderabad, Vadodara and Varanasi.vii) Regional Marketing Offices (15 nos.) : Bengaluru, Bhubaneswar, Chandigarh, Chennai, Guwahati, Jabalpur, Jaipur, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, New Delhi, Raipur, Ranchi, Secunderabad and Vadodara.viii) Other business offices : Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, New Delhi, Noida.ix) Customer project sites : spread across the country. International locations (abroad)	Main Offices : Dhaka (Bangladesh), Damascus (Syria), Kathmandu (Nepal).Major Sites/Local Offices :Rampal (Bangladesh), Wangdue Phodrang (Bhutan), Trongsa (Bhutan).------------
