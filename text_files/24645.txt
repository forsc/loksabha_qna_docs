                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

583






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Houses for Urban Poor




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has asked the States to submit details as to the number of houses for poor in urban areas required by 2022 and if so, the details thereof; (b)  whether 12 lakh houses for urban poor are required in Andhra Pradesh; (c) if so, the details thereof, district-wise, with a particular reference to East Godavari district; and (d) the manner in which the Union Government is going to ensure that 12 lakh houses are allotted to Andhra Pradesh and the goal of Housing for All by 2022 would be achieved in Andhra Pradesh? 






ANSWER




                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):Yes sir; under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana–Urban [PMAY-U] States/UTs have undertaken demand survey and reported validated demand of around 112.24 lakh houses in urban areas of the country. (b) & (c):The State of Andhra Pradesh has reported a demand of 20.50 lakh houses under PMAY-U. Based on the proposals submitted by the State, 20,37,411 houses have been sanctioned. District-wise details, including that of East Godavari district, of these houses are Annexed. (d): Against the total sanctioned houses for Andhra Pradesh, 14,90,522 houses have been grounded for construction; of which 4,62,353 houses have been completed. The State has been requested to ground construction of balance sanctioned houses, complete the already started and saturate the housing demand city-wise within the Mission period to achieve the goal of Housing for All.                                  *****
