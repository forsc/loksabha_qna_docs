                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1733






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Irrigation Projects in Odisha




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of minor and major irrigation projects on which work is in progress under the Centrally Sponsored Schemes in Odisha along with the estimated cost involved therein including the Central/State share prescribed for the purpose, location-wise; (b) Whether the Government has decided to grant special package for completion of irrigation projects to address agrarian distress in Kalahandi, Bolangir and other chronically drought prone areas of Odisha and if so, the details thereof; (c) Whether the Government also proposes to extend the said special package to other agrarian distress/ drought prone areas of the country; and (d) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the decision is likely to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  During 2016-17, ninety-nine (99) on-going Major/Medium irrigation projects under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)- Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP), have been prioritized in consultation with States, for completion in phases by December, 2019 along with their Command Area Development & Water Management (CADWM) works under Har Khet Ko Pani (HKKP) component. Funding arrangement for both the Central & State share is to be through NABARD under Long Term Irrigation Fund (LTIF). Further, Surface Minor Irrigation (SMI) projects are also covered under HKKP component for financial support through budgetary means. Though the said scheme was till Dec, 2019, however Ministry of Finance vide their letter dated 10.01.2020 conveyed the continuation of ongoing Centrally Sponsored Schemes for a period up to 31st March 2021.Of 99 prioritized projects under PMKSY-AIBP & HKKP, eight (8) Projects are from the Odisha, including those benefitting Kalahandi, Bolangir, Koraput (KBK) districts and Drought prone areas of the State. Details of these projects along with their estimated cost, central share and state share, are annexed herewith. Further, there is no Surface Minor Irrigation (SMI) project of the State presently included for funding under the PMKSY-HKKP. (b) to (d)  Presently there is no such proposal under consideration in Ministry of Jal Shakti.***** ANNEXURE Annexure as referred to in reply to Unstarred Question  No. 1733 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 11.02.2021 regarding “Irrigation Projects in Odisha”.
