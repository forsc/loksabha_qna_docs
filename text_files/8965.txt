                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2433






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






MGNREGS in Uttar Pradesh




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds allocated to Uttar Pradesh by the Government under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS) during the last four years and the current year;(b)	whether the Government proposes to increase the guaranteed wage employment from the present 100 days to 150 days;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government proposes to link MGNREGS with the agricultural activities such as paddy and wheat production to ensure sustenance of farmers; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) is a demand driven wage employment programme. Details of Central fund released to Uttar Pradesh under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the last four years and current year 2019-20 (as on 27.11.2019) are given below:                                                          (Rs. in lakh)Financial Year	   2019-20	2018-19	         2017-18        2016-17	        2015-16	Uttar Pradesh	 447164.04	547575.02	369177.65	391584.94	269569.44	(b)&(c): No, Sir. However, an additional 50 days of wage employment are provided over and above 100 days in the notified drought affected areas or natural calamity areas in the country on recommendation of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare. On the recommendation of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, an additional employment of 50 days of wage employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA has been provided over and above 100 days per households in the notified drought affected areas of 50 districts in FY 2015-16 and 8 districts in FY 2016-17 of Uttar Pradesh. (d):	No, Sir.(e):	 Does not arise.
