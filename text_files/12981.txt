                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1791






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Pravasi Bhartiya Bima Yojana




C. R. Patil














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of insurance policies sold under the Pravasi Bhartiya Bima Yojana so far; (b)	the details regarding the number of the claims made by the policy buyers under the above scheme so far; (c)	the number of such claims accepted and those rejected and number of death claims out of the rejected claims; and (d)	whether the Government has established any mechanism for appeal or review of dismissed death claims and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (a) to (c): Ministry of External Affairs(MEA) has informed as under:  (Period from 1.4.2014 to 31.12.2019)S.No. 	Details of policy and claims	Number1.		Number of insurance policies sold under the Pravasi Bhartiya Bima Yojana	33,32,4972.		Number of the claims made by the policy buyers	11903.		Number of claims accepted	5404.		Number of claims rejected	6045.		Number of death claims out of the rejected claims	433 (d): MEA has informed that in case of non-acceptance of any claim by any insurance agency due to any reason such as non-submission of claim in time, or lack of documents, the complainant can approach Protector of Emigrants  in his/her area, or  Protector General of Emigrants (PGE) or Indian Missions/ Posts abroad. On being approached by the complainant, MEA takes up the matter with the concerned Mission/ Posts abroad for obtaining the missing documents and also takes up the matter with the concerned insurance companies for early settlement of claims.*****
