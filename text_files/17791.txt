                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1417






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Police Personnel Infected with COVID-19




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of police personnel infected with COVID-19 while on duty and the number of such personnel who lost their lives, State-wise;(b) whether the Central/State Governments have provided compensation to the victim’s family; and(c) if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)   The data in respect of Police personnel who died due to COVID-19 across the country is not maintained  Centrally. However, in respect of Central Armed Police Forces, total number of personnel infected with COVID-19 and number of personnel who lost their lives as on 14.09.2020 is given below: Name of the Force	Total infected with Covid-19 	Deaths BSF 	8934	24CRPF	9158	36CISF	5544	24ITBP	3380	7SSB	3251	7NSG	225	0ARs	1746	7Total 	32238105-2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.1417 FOR 20.09.2020       In addition to normal benefits available to a CAPF personnel on death, it has been decided to provide    Rs. 15 Lakh to Next of Kins through “Bharat Ke Veer” funds, in cases CAPF personnel dies due to COVID-19 infection contracted while deployed on Covid-19 related duties.*****
