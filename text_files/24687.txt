                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

465






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Non Conventional Solar Energy




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether all the States are not naturally endowed with natural resources to generate non-conventional solar energy and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has made any study to identify natural resource potential for generation of nonconventional energy of various States and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has asked all the States to abide by a naturally determined uniform and sub categorized Renewal Purchase Obligation (RPO); (d) if so, the details thereof and the response of the States thereto; (e) whether States with low potential of nonconventional energy has been demanding to exclude them for compliance of RPO targets; and (f) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) The Renewable Energy potential varies from State to State. The State-wise details of estimated renewable energy potential (excluding large hydro) identified for various renewable energy sources in the country are given in Annexure. (c) & (d) Ministry of Power has notified uniform Renewable Purchase Obligation (RPO) trajectory up to the year 2021-22 under two broad categories of solar and non-solar seeking balanced growth of Renewable Energy (excluding large hydro) in all States. The uniform RPO trajectory has been finalized keeping in view the national targets for solar and non-solar (wind, biomass, small hydro etc.) renewable energy capacity. In terms of Section 86(1)(e) of the Electricity Act 2003, respective State Electricity Regulatory Commissions can also notify state-specific RPO targets from time to time.(e) & (f) The Ministry of New & Renewable Energy has not received request from any State to exclude them from compliance of RPO targets.*****
