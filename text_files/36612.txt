                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3673






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Construction Quality of NHs




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of complaints received about poor quality, slow pace of construction and negligence in following the parameters fixed for construction of National Highway (NH)-12 in the State of Madhya Pradesh;(b) 	the details of action taken by the Government thereon; (c)	whether the Government has received complaints about poor quality, arbitrary conduct of  contractors and various other irregularities in the ongoing construction of Jabalpur-Jaipur NHpassing through Narsinghpur and Raisen districts; (d)	if so, the details thereof along with the action taken by the Government thereon; (e)	if not, whether the Government proposes to take remedial steps to check and ensure the quality work on that NH; and (f)	if so, the time by which these steps are likely to be taken?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)&(b)       No compliant  has been received regarding slow pace of construction and negligence in construction. However, few complaints (a total of 7) have been received about poor quality of construction work of National Highway-12 in the State of Madhya Pradesh. The details of complaints and action taken thereof by the Ministry is  at Annexure-I (c)&(d)      Yes Sir. In Jabalpur–Jaipur section of NH–12, one complaint in four – lanning work of Sindoor-Bareli section of NH-12 passing through Raisen District has been received. The details of complaint and action taken thereof by the Ministry is  at Sl. No. 4 of Annexure-I. (e) &(f)       The project works are supervised during construction by the Authority Engineer engaged on the project by the implementing agency. If any quality issue is noticed during the construction itself, the contractor is immediately instructed to rectify the defect noticed and the same is ensured.
