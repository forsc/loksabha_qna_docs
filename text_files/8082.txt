                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1733






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Kannur Airport




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of airlines utilising Kannur airport in Kerala;(b) the number of passengers who utilised the airport on daily average; and(c) the time by which the Kannur airport will break even in its operations?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Four airlines are utilising Kannur Airport in Kerala i.e. Air India, Air India Express, Indigo and Go Air.;(b): The daily average number of passengers in Kannur Airport has been close to 4000 from 01 January 2019 till 15 November 2019.  The highest daily average of 5131 was recorded in August 2019.;(c): The viability  of airport depends upon revenue and costs of the airport. The major sources of revenue are aeronautical revenue and non-aeronautical revenue. The airport revenue depends upon landing charges and the number of aircrafts landing. The aeronautical charges are determined by AERA.
