                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






National Social Assistance Programme




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of people who have availed the benefit of the National Social Assistance Programme and the details thereof, State-wise;(b) 	the amount of funds allocated, sanctioned and disbursed to achieve the objectives set out by the National Social Assistance Programme and the details thereof, State-wise;(c) 	the steps taken by the Government to ensure that social protection by the scheme is uniformly available, in line with the objectives of the scheme; and(d) 	the plan, if any, to increase the quantum of assistance being extended under the 5 sub-components of the scheme to make it better suited to the contemporary times?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	& (b): The State-wise details are enclosed at Annexure A and Annexure B.(c): 	The Government provides financial support to persons belonging to below poverty line families under the schemes of National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP), which is being uniformly implemented in rural and urban areas of all the States/ UTs of the country. (d): 	There is no proposal to increase the quantum of assistance under the schemes of NSAP. ******
