                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3643






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Sanskrit University at Prayagraj




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the facilities being provided by the Government in Ganganath Jha Research Institute of Central Sanskrit University at Prayagraj;b) the number of the teachers employed and the students enrolled in the said institute;c) whether the Government has any plan for purchasing land and constructing buildings for complex of the institute;d) if so, the details thereof; ande) the steps taken or being taken by the Government to extend the complex of the above-said institute keeping in view the requirement of today?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) The Ganganath Jha campus at Prayagaraj, Allahabad is one of the 12 campuses of Central Sanskrit University. It is located in a land leased by the State Government of Uttar Pradesh to the erstwhile Ganganath Jha Research Institute. This Institute was taken over by the Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan on 1.4.1971 which is presently called Central Sanskrit University. The Central Sanskrit University is fully funded by the Government of India.(b to e) There are 6 teachers and 77 regular Ph.D. students in the Ganganath Jha campus and there is no plan for purchase of land for any activities in the near future for this campus.*****
