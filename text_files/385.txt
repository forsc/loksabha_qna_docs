                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Tourism Projects in Kerala




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any new projects or ongoing project is pending under the Ministry in the State of Kerala; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government is considering Cochin, Kerala as one of the major tourist location in Kerala for implementing new projects; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	whether any special schemes are available for the tourism development; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (f):	 The Ministry of Tourism, under the schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies, provides central financial assistance to State Governments/ Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. Submission of project proposals by the State Governments/ UT Administrations for funding under the above schemes is a continuous process. The projects are sanctioned subject to their adherence to relevant scheme guidelines, submission of suitable detailed project reports, availability of funds and utilization of funds released earlier.The list of projects sanctioned by the Ministry to the State of Kerala is given at the Annexure.******
