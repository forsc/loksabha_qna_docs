                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Funds Released to BTC




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the amount of funds released to Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC) by Ministry of Culture during the last five years;(b)	the names of the schemes under which these funds have been released;(c)	the complete details of the works done with the funds released;  and(d)	whether the funds were released directly to BTC or through StateGovernment?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) Sir, no funds are released to Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC) by this Ministry during the last five years.(b), (c) & (d) Question doesn’t arise.*****
