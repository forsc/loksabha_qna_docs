                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

169






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Promotion of MSMEs




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to encourage, promote and protect the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country especially in Gujarat under the Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a)	& (b): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. *169 FOR ANSWER ON 09.12.2021(a) & (b):  	Under Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan, Government has taken a number of initiatives within the COVID-19 relief package to encourage, promote and protect the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country including the State of Gujarat, which inter alia include;(i)	Rs.3 lakh crore Collateral free Automatic Loans for business, including MSMEs (Existing overall guarantee limit under ECLGS has been enhanced from Rs. 3 lakh crore to Rs. 4.5 lakh crore). (ii)	Rs. 20,000 crore Subordinate Debt for MSMEs.(iii)	Rs. 50,000 crore equity infusion through MSME Self-Reliant India Fund.(iv)	New revised criteria of classification of MSMEs.(v)	New Registration of MSMEs through ‘Udyam Registration’ for Ease of Doing Business.(vi)	No global tenders for procurement up to Rs. 200 crores.As reported by Department of Financial Services, as on 19.11.2021, under the Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS), amount of guarantees and numbers of guarantees to MSME beneficiaries in the State of Gujarat are given below.<pre>ECLGS	Number of guarantees issued	Micro	Small	Medium	Total MSMEsGujarat	2,79,308	36,802	17,783	3,33,893All India	1,02,99,278	4,99,360	2,60,907	1,10,59,545% in Gujarat	2.7	7.4	6.8	3.0</pre><pre>ECLGS	Guarantee Amount (Rs. in crore)	Micro	Small	Medium	Total MSMEsGujarat	4,734.77	6,693.5	5,148.16	16,576.43All India	66,219.09	67,138.89	42,721.46	1,76,079.44% in Gujarat	7.2	10.0	12.1	9.4</pre>As reported by Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) as on 01.12.2021, under the Credit Guarantee Scheme for Subordinate Debt (CGSSD), amount of guarantees and number of guarantees to MSME beneficiaries in the State of Gujarat are given below.<pre>CGSSD	Number of  Guarantees approved	Guarantee Amount ( Rs. in lakh)Gujarat	24	241.54All India	754	8,147.35% in Gujarat	3.2	3.0</pre>Details of registration of MSMEs as per Udyam Registration Portal (w.e.f. 01.07.2020) as on 03.12.2021 in the State of Gujarat are given below.<pre>Udyam Registration	Number of Enterprises registered on ‘Udyam Registration’ Portal	Micro	Small	Medium	Total MSMEsGujarat	4,53,282	36,559	4,090	4,93,931All India	55,48,922	2,96,991	33,162	58,79,075% in Gujarat	8.2	12.3	12.3	8.4</pre>
