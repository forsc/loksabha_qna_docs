                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

392






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Funds to MGNREGS




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of works taken up in Salem district in Tamil Nadu during the last three years under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS);(b)	the quantum of funds sanctioned and utilised during the said period along with the criteria adopted to take up the works, year-wise; and(c)	the details of workforce engaged under MGNREGS along with the wages paid to them during the said period, gender-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)&b): 	As per the information shared by State Government of Tamil Nadu, the details of works taken up and the quantum of funds sanctioned and utilized in Salem District in Tamil Nadu during the last three years is given below:  Financial Year	                   2016-17	   2017-18	        2018-19Number of works taken up 	   14704	    13675	         27577Funds sanctioned(Rs. in lakh)	                   27900	    25383	         28654Funds utilised (Rs. in lakh)	   34264	    24311	         24037Mahatma Gandhi NREGA is a demand driven wage employment programme having bottom up approach and priorities of work are set at Gram Sabha level.(c):	As per the information shared by State Government of Tamil Nadu, the details of gender-wise workforce engaged under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS alongwith the wages paid to them during the last three years are given below: Year	          Workforce engaged	  Wages paid                                           (Rs. in lakh)	        Men	       Women 2016-17	      10893	       268418	   16068.182017-18	      12300	       250583	   17188.932018-19	      11617	       217634	   17317.15Total	      34812            736638      50578.26
