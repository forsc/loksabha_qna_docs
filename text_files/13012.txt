                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2042






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers




Jyotsna Charandas Mahant














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †2042TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.03.2020REHABILITATION OF MANUAL SCAVENGERS †2042. SHRIMATI JYOTSNA CHARANDAS MAHANT: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has implemented/ proposes to implement the self employment scheme for the rehabilitation of manual scavengers through National Safai Karamcharis Finance and Development Corporation in Chhattisgarh; and (b) if so, the details thereof along with the status across the country, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER STATE OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT( SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE )(a): Self Employment Scheme for Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers (SRMS) is a Central Sector Scheme implemented through National Safai Karamcharis finance and Development Corporation to provide rehabilitation benefits to the manual scavengers identified in all States and Union Territories including Chhattisgarh.(b):The identified manual scavengers are eligible for the following rehabilitation benefits under SRMS:(i)	One time cash assistance of Rs.40,000/-  for identified manual scavengers.(ii)	Skill Development Training with Stipend of Rs.3,000/- per month for identified manual scavengers and their dependants. (iii)	Capital subsidy upto Rs. 3,25,000/-to those who have availed bank loans upto Rs.15 lakh for self employment projects.
