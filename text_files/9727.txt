                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Bar-Code System to Domestic Medicines




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that the Government is contemplating on bringing Bar-Code system to Domestic Medicines to curtail the fake drugs;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which it is likely to come into force?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c): 	Department of Pharmaceuticals under the Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers vide Public Procurement (Preference to Make in India), Order, 2017 (revised) dated 14.01.2019 about pharmaceutical formulations had made it mandatory for all medicines procured under Public Procurement to have barcode/ QR code at primary level packaging from 01.04.2019, in order to enable tracking and tracing of the pharmaceutical formulations, thereby establishing the authenticity of the products.       	Subsequently, Department of Pharmaceuticals has decided to give pharmaceutical industry a transition time of one year for universal implementation of such technologies from April 2020.
