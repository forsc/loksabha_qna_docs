                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Information through SMS for Ration Delivery




Subhash Chandra Baheria














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the consumers are informed through messages sent on their mobile about delivery of items to the ration dealer for distribution to beneficiaries; (b) 	if not, whether the Government is making any efforts in this regard; and (c) 	if so, the time by which the said system is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (c): 		As part of the Public Distribution System (PDS) reforms undertaken by this Department in collaboration with all States/UTs, 31 States/UTs have so far computerised the Supply Chain Management (SCM) operations for the supply of foodgrains up to the Fair Price Shops. This involves online monitoring of the receipts and issuance of foodgrains in godowns/depots, online generation of delivery orders, truck challans, gate passes, etc. The facility for sending SMS alerts to FPS dealers and some beneficiaries on their registered mobile numbers, for informing the dispatch of foodgrains from godowns, is also enabled in many States/UTs.*****
