                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2361






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Rope-way to Swarngiri Fort




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Swarngiri Fort in Jalore district boosts of a thousand years of historical splendor and the beauty of architecture is reflected from this fort, a beautiful example/sample of architecture and this fort of Jalore is an example of bravery and patriotism; (b)	whether Rs.8.82 crore was approved/sanctioned for the ropeway to this fort on 28 june, 2017 out of which Rs 7 crore has to be spent on rope-way and the rest amount has to be spent on tourism facility centre, surface parking, raling for stairs, clean drinking water and benches for sitting etc; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether this work was to be accomplished within 18 months from the date of the issue of the fund but even after this period was over, the work has not commenced; if so, the reasons therefor; and(e) 	the reasons for delay in work and the details of the steps taken by the Government to complete this work?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM    			        (SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Yes, Sir. Swarngiri Fort is famous for its history and architecture. (b) and (c):		The  Ministry of Tourism  in the year  2017-18 sanctioned  the project for  development of  Heritage Circuit  in Rajasthan which included  development of  various  components  at  Jalore Fort. A  total  amount of  Rs. 8.82 Cr. was  sanctioned for  Jalore  Fort which included Rs. 7.00 Crore for Development of  ropeway and Rs. 1.82 Cr. for other  components such as parking, railings on pathway(steps) leading to Fort, tourist facility centre, surface parking, drinking water facility, benches etc.(d) and (e):		Rajasthan Tourism Development Corporation Ltd. (RTDC)  is  the implementing agency  of this  project and  as per the sanction order no. 5(04)/2021-SD  dated 28.06.2017 of the project, it was  to  be  completed and commissioned within 18 months. The progress of the project was reviewed during the Regional Review Meeting held on 16th January 2020; where in the State Government of Rajasthan informed  that tendering has been done twice for ropeway and no response was received. It was highlighted that work has not yet commenced for remaining components as well.  Thereafter a decision was taken that all the sanctioned components, at Jalore fort, stand dropped as work order has not yet been awarded for ropeway and work has not commenced for the remaining components.   ********
