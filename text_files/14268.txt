                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3145






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Participation of Girls in Sports




Pratima Bhoumik














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has undertaken any schemes or programmes to encourage the participation of girls in sporting activities;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of girls benefited from such programmes, State-wise, during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) to (c) The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports is running schemes for promotion of sports as well as attaining excellence in sports. These schemes include:-(i) Khelo India Scheme; (ii) Assistance to National Sports Federations; (iii)Special Awards to Winners in International sports events and their Coaches; (iv) National Sports Awards, Pension to Meritorious Sports Persons; (v) National Sports Development Fund; and (vi) Sports Training Centres run through Sports Authority of India. The Ministry is promoting sports among women under the ‘Khelo India Scheme’ having a dedicated vertical for this purpose.Details of the above schemes are available in the public domain on the websites of this Ministry and the Sports Authority of India.Further, Sports Authority of India (SAI) is implementing various promotional schemes for sports across the country to identify talented sportspersons, including girls sportspersons in the age group of 8-25 years, and nurture them to excel at national and international levels:-i) National Centre of Excellence (NCOE); ii) SAI Training Centre (STC); iii) Extension Centre of STC; and iv) National Sports Talent Contest Scheme (NSTC).State-wise details are given at Annexure – I.For the promotion of sports among the talented girl sportspersons, SAI has established one National Centre of Excellence (NCOE) and three SAI Training Centres exclusively for girls, the details of which are given at Annexure – II.Further, under the Khelo India Scheme, 2877 Khelo India Athletes have been selected in 21 sports disciplines since August 2018, out of which 1428 are girls. State-wise details of the same are at Annexure – III.*****
