                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4139






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Disinvestment of SCI




Mimi Chakraborty













                                                    K. Shanmuga Sundaram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided for disinvestment of Shipping Corporation of India (SCI);  (b)	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether it is fact that the Government has changed the disinvestment plan in  2017, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	the present stake of the Government and sale proposal therein;(e)	whether any steps have been taken by the Government to utilize the ships in war or national emergency after the sale of SCI;(f)	whether the Government is having any other agencies to deploy their own ships in case of emergency and if so, the details thereof; and (g)	the details of the rescue operations performed by SCI during the last three years and the current year till date?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d)  Yes sir. The Government has decided for disinvestment of Shipping Corporation of India (SCI). The present stake of the Government in SCI is 63.75% and the proposal of its entire stake sale is in process.(e)  Yes sir. Government can requisition any Indian assets, including Indian ships, during a war or national emergency.(f)   Director General of Shipping has power to issue directions to ships registered under Merchant Shipping Act or licensed under Merchant Shipping Act in public interest or in the interest of Indian Shipping to proceed for any particular purpose.(g)  The details of the rescue operations performed by SCI during the last 3 years and for current year are attached at Annexure – 1.
