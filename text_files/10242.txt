                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Drug Addiction




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that several independent reports/surveys indicated the increasing threat of drug addiction among the youth of the country particularly within the student community; (b) if so, whether the Government has any data on the number of people involved in drug addiction; (c) the number of drug de-addiction centres active in the country particularly in the State of Assam; (d) the action being taken to stop the spread of drug addiction among the youth of the country; and (e) the amount sanctioned/ released to the drug de-addiction centres during last three years, year and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): A National Survey to collect state wise data on Extent and Pattern of Substance Use was conducted in the country during the year 2018. The report presents major findings of the survey in terms of proportion of Indian population using various substances and those affected by substance use disorders. This survey indicates that there are wide variations in the extent and prevalence of substance use across different states and between various substances. Study regarding pattern and prevalence of drug use in the country in the age –group of 10-75 years was done in the report.          As per the report, prevalence (in %) and estimated number of individuals who are currently users of various psychoactive substances (as per the estimated population in 2018) is at Annexure.(c): The list of drug de-addiction centre under the scheme of Assistance for Prevention of Alcoholism and Substance (Drugs) Abuse including Assam is at Annexure-I(d): The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has formulated and is implementing a National Action Plan for Drug Demand Reduction (NAPDDR) for 2018-2025. The Plan aims at reduction of adverse consequences of drug abuse through a multi-pronged strategy. The activities under the NAPDDR, inter-alia, include awareness generation programmes in  schools/colleges/Universities, workshops/seminars/ with parents, community based peerled interactions intervention programmes for vulnerable adolescent and youth in the community, provisioning of treatment facilities and capacity building of service providers.(e): The amount sanctioned /released to States for de-addiction centres during last three years under the scheme is at Annexure-II.****AnnexureSubstance	Chidren & Adolescents (10-17 years)	Adults (18-75 years)	Prevalence( in %)	Estimated no. of users	Prevalence( in %)	Estimated no. of usersAlcohol	1.30	30,00,000	17.10	15,10,00,000Cannabis	0.90	20,00,000	3.30	2,90,00,000Opioids	1.80	40,00,000	2.10	1,90,00,000Sedatives	0.58	20,00,000	1.21	1,10,00,000Inhalants	1.17	30,00,000	0.58	60,00,000Cocaine	0.06	2,00,000	0.11	10,00,000ATS	0.18	4,00,000	0.18	20,00,000Hallucinogens	0.07	2,00,000	0.13	20,00,000*****
