                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3679






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






NGOs in Education Sector




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the irregularities have been reported and complaints received regarding the functioning of a large number of NGOs operating in the field of education with the help of grants from abroad and big companies;(b) if so, the details thereof during the last five years, State-wise and date-wise including Rajasthan;(c) whether any assessment has been made of the funds spent through NGOs in the field of education for Government schools in the country; and(d) if so, the details of the funds spent in the field of education especially in Rajasthan during the last five years NGOs-wise, head-wise and year-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (d): There is no scheme or programme of Ministry of Education under which grants from abroad and big companies are released to NGO. Education is in the Concurrent List of the Constitution and majority of schools are under the jurisdiction of the State/ UT Government. The decision of the State/ UT Government to involve Non-Governmental Organizations (NGO) is based on State’s requirement and the expertise or specialization of the NGO.***
