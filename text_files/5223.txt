                                    ANSWERED ON:

Allocation under Public Distribution System




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the total demand received so far from each State Government for the supply of wheat, rice, sugar, foodgrains and kerosene under Public Distribution System (PDS) during the current year along with the allocation made for each State, month-wise;(b) 	whether the said demands have been fully met; and(c) 	the quantum actually collected/lifted by each State Government each month during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.****  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF THE STARRED QUESTION NO.*428 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 23.07.2019 IN THE LOK SABHA(a) to (c):  	Allocation of foodgrains to the States/UTs for Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) under National Food Security Act, 2013  is made on the basis of percentage coverage as determined for that State/UT by Govt. of India, number of beneficiaries identified by that State/UT and scale prescribed as per the provisions of NFSA.  The total allocation and offtake of rice and wheat under TPDS during the current financial year State-wise/month-wise is at Annexure I and II respectively.
