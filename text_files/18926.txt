                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

336






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Broadband Connectivity in Jharkhand




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number and details of the gram panchayats connected under Bharatnet and ready to provide service, State/UT-wise; (b) 	the number of Gram Panchayats targeted to be connected with broadband connectivity under the said project in Jharkhand, district-wise; and (c) 	the number of Gram Panchayats where the said service has become operational as on date along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)  	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity at all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2, 50,000) in the country. As on 27.01.2021, under BharatNet project (Phase-I & II), a total number of 1,51,431 Gram Panchayats (including BHQs) have been made Service Ready.  The State/UT-wise list of Service Ready GPs is attached as Annexure-I. (b) & (c) 	A total number of about 4653 Gram Panchayats (GPs) are targeted to be connected with broadband connectivity under the said project in Jharkhand, out of which 3433 GPs (including BHQs) have been made Service Ready. The District-wise details of the total number of GPs and Service Ready GPs in Jharkhand are given in Annexure-II.
