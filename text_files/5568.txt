                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5152






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Installation of Escalators at Stations




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry of Railways has decided to install escalators at all ‘A’ category stations in the country;(b)    	if so, the number of ‘A’ category stations where escalators have been installed along with the details thereof; and(c)  	the reasons for not installing escalators at Dehri-on-Sone ‘A’ category station in Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
