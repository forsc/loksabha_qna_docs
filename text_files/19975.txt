                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1430






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Bid for 5G Spectrum Auction




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to auction 5G spectrum and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government is planning to allow non telecom operators to bid for 5G spectrum; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)  The Government has decided to conduct auction of spectrum in 700 MHz, 800 MHz, 900 MHz, 1800 MHz, 2100 MHz, 2300 MHz and 2500 MHz frequency bands. Successful bidders in the auction can deploy any technology using the auction-acquired spectrum. Notice Inviting Applications (NIA) for the spectrum auction has been issued on 06.01.2021. As per the NIA timetable, auction is scheduled to start on 01.03.2021.(b)&(c) Any licensee that holds a Unified License Access License (UASL)/Cellular Mobile Telephony Service (CMTS) License/Unified License (UL) with authorization for Access Services for that Licensed Service Area (LSA); or any licensee that fulfils the eligibility for obtaining a Unified License with authorization for Access Services and gives an undertaking to obtain a Unified License for access service authorisation; or any entity that gives an undertaking to obtain a Unified License for access service authorisation through a New Entrant Nominee as per the DoT guidelines/licence conditions, can bid for the Spectrum subject to other provisions of the Notice Inviting Applications (NIA) for auction.
