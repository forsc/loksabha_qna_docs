                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2839






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Affordable Housing




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government had sanctioned the plan to build affordable houses in 6 States including Bihar under Urban Housing Scheme in financial year 2017;(b)	if so, the amount sanctioned by the Government along with the number of houses proposed to be built in each state, State-wise;(c)	the present status of this scheme; and (d)	the amount released under the scheme, State-wise?






ANSWER




(a)	to (d):  The Union Government has sanctioned 24,87,819 houses to the States/Union Territories (UTs) including State of Biharwith Central Assistance of Rs. 37,914.72 crore during the financial year 2017-18 under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban(PMAY-U) Mission. The State/UT-wise details of houses and Central Assistance sanctioned during financial year 2017-18 along with present status with respect to houses and Central Assistance sanctioned and released under PMAY-U Mission are at Annexure.                                    *****
