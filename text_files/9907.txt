                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Objective Oriented Programs




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any scheme has been formulated to review the objective orientedprograms conducted in the Universities which receives grants from the Government;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the steps being taken by the Government to review the fulfilment of theobjectives of the establishment of institutions like JNU; and(d) the action taken on the anti-national, aimless, improper and undesirable programsafter the review of JNU along with the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b): The Universities are autonomous bodies established or incorporated by orunder a Central Act, a Provincial Act or a State Act which lay down the purpose oftheir establishment along with institutional objectives. Therefore, it is the responsibilityof the establishing State / Centre that Universities fulfill their purpose of establishment.(c) & (d): All Central Universities, including Jawahar Lal Nehru University (JNU), arerequired to enter into a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the AdministrativeMinistry which clearly spell out performance parameters, output targets in terms ofdetails of programme of work and qualitative improvement in output along withcommensurate input requirements. Accordingly, Vice Chancellors of CentralUniversities including JNU under the Ministry have submitted MoUs comprising, interalia,performance parameters of current academic year and corresponding targets forthe next academic year.******
