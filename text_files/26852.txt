                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2580






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Data of Foreign Investment




Rama Devi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether details of foreign investment made in the country, State-wise, are not maintained;(b) 	if so, the reasons therefor; and(c) 	in the absence of the above information the strategy followed for the development of states on account of foreign investment in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a): 	State-wise data on foreign investment reported through Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) equity inflow in the country is being maintained w.e.f. October, 2019. (b) &(c):  	Do not arise. ********
