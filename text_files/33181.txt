                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

66






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated under the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) during the last five years, State-wise including Maharashtra and also district-wise; (b) the total number of Universities approved/ established under RUSA during the above mentioned period; and (c) the number of Universities approved/established under RUSA in rural districts, State-wise including Maharashtra and also district-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c): A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (C) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 66 TO BE ANSWERED ON 07.02.2022 ASKED BY SHRIMATI POONAM MAHAJAN, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING RASHTRIYA UCHCHATAR SHIKSHA ABHIYAN(a): The details of funds allocated  State-wise and District-wise during the year  2016-17 to 2020-21under the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) are at Annexure -I.(b) & (c): Total number of 78 Universities were approved for grants under the various components of RUSA during the last five years. Out of which 7 Universities fall in State of Maharashtra. State-wise and District-wise distribution of these Universities is given at Annexure-II. *****
