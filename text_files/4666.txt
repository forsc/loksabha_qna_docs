                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4281






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Merchant Navy Institutes




Vijay Kumar Dubey













                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there are some Merchant Navy Institutes in the country which are not approved by any State Government or DG Shipping and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has any data submitted by these Institutes for the employment of their trained seafarers and if so, the details thereof;(c )	the details of Bunker sales to Foreign Merchant Ships at Indian Ports during the year 2015, 2016, 2017 and 2018; and(d)	whether the Government plans to exempt bunker supply to merchant ships from GST which could generate employment for seafarers with more bunker barges and bringing  huge amount of Foreign Investment to the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	The Government has approved 163 Maritime Training Institutes in India.  The information regarding Maritime Training Institutes which may be operational without obtaining due approval is not maintained in a compiled form in the Ministry of Shipping. (b)	No, Sir.(c)	International Bunkers: Product wise Sales in Thousand Metric Tons (TMT) is as below:<pre>Product	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19 (P)FO (Fuel Oil)	398.9	436.8	523.5	368.2	469.5HSD (High Speed Diesel)	32.4	35	39.9	30.1	37.4Total	431.3	471.8	563.4	398.2	506.9                                                      </pre>(d)	Government has reduced GST from 18% to 5% on bunker fuel used in Indian flag vessels.  This reduction has had a positive impact on the bunker sale in India.                                        *****
