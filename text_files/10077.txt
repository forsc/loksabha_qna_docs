                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Renaming of States/Cities




Deepak (Dev) Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to rename Agra and if so, the details thereof;(b) the number of proposals for renaming States/cities currently pending with the Government; and(c) the reasons for stalling the renaming of West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)    No such proposal is under consideration of Government.(b) & (c) No proposal for renaming of Cities is currently pending with the Government. A proposal for change in the name of the State of West Bengal has been received. The decision on change in the name of a State is made after taking into consideration all relevant factors.*****
