                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Diversion of Forest Land




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether between 1982 and 2014, 2,689.51 hectares (ha.) of forest land including 330.50 ha. from the Palamu Tiger Reserve has been diverted, without obtaining the prior permission of the Ministry or the Supreme Court;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and (c)	the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a), (b) & (c)	No Sir.  In so far as the Palamau Tiger Reserve is concerned, forest area of 1007.29 ha. was diverted after due approval of the Standing Committee of National Board for Wildlife in its 43rd meeting held on June 27, 2017, for construction of North Koel Reservoir project.***
