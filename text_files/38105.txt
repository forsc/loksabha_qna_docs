                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4818






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Highway Connectivity in Assam




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government propose to formulate any action plan to further strengthen and develop the highways connecting the State of Assam with neigbouring countries like China, Bhutan, Bangladesh and Myanmar in view of its strategic importance;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has fixed any time-limit in this regard; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d) A proposal of 2 laning of Udaipur- Srimatapur (Comilla) road through Tripura to provide connectivity to Bangladesh is under consideration. Highway routes already developed/under progress  to connect Assam with neighbouring countries like China, Bhutan, Bangladesh and Myanmar are given at Annexure-I
