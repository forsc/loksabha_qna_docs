                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

24






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Skill Development Projects in Ranchi




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	number of projects related to skill development being run in Ranchi Lok Sabha Constituency;(b)	the amount spent during in the last two years and number of institutes that are running under these projects; and(c)	numbers of youth trained under skill development projects alongwith the details of the companies where they got placement ?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing various schemes/projects related to skill development across the country including Ranchi Lok Sabha Constituency. The major projects implemented by the MSDE are Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS), Craftsman Training Scheme (CTS) and Jan Sikhshan Sansthan (JSS).(b) & (c)	The details of fund released, number of institutions, candidates trained and placed under the above mentioned schemes in the Ranchi Lok Sabha Constituency during the last two years (2019-20 to 2020-21) are as follows:S.No.	Schemes	Fund released (Rs. In lakh )	Number of Institutes 	Candidates Trained/enrolled	Candidates Placed1	PMKVY*	191.36	44	15213	12122	JSS	90.82	1	2815	- (not monitored)3	NAPS**	174.40	184	332	-(not monitored)4	CTS	-	51	8060	-(not monitored)*PMKVY- Fund from April, 2020 to 31st   October, 2021.**Under NAPS, fund are released to State of Jharkhand
