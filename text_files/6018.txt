                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

491






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is aware that under the Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA), many private medical practitioners are serving poor pregnant women once in every month by conducting free tests and offering free treatment in their own clinics/nursing homes; (b) if so, the details thereof, district, State/UT-wise; (c) the number of women benefited so far from the said scheme in Rajasthan, particularly in Udaipur district; (d) the measures being taken by the Government to honour such private medical practitioners to encourage more practitioners to extend such services; and (e) whether the Government proposes to take any new steps to include more farmer families under the said scheme, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (e):  A statement is laid on the Table of the HouseSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 491* FOR 26TH JULY, 2019(a)  The Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan has been launched by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare (MoHFW), Government of India to provide fixed-day assured, comprehensive and quality antenatal care universally to all pregnant women on the 9th of every month.OBGY specialists / Radiologists/ Doctors working in the private sector are encouraged to volunteer for the campaign and provide voluntary services at nearby government health facilities on the 9th of every month.  Beside private sector doctors, the doctors in public sector are also rendering services as part of PMSMA in conducting Ante natal checkups for pregnant women. (b) Private doctors can register for the campaign through any of the following mechanisms:o	Toll Free Number - Doctors can call 18001801104 to registero	SMS- Doctors can SMS ‘PMSMA <Name> to 5616115o	PMSMA Portal- Register at www.pmsma.nhp.gov.ino	Register using the ‘Volunteer Registration’ Section of the Mobile Application5799 volunteers (doctors) have registered for the campaign across the country. State / UT wise details of the same are annexed. (c) More than 19.8 lakh antenatal checkups have been conducted under the programme in Rajasthan and more than 67600 antenatal checkups have been conducted under the programme in Udaipur.(d) National Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan ''IPledgefor9'' Achievers Awards Ceremony was organized on 29th June 2018. A total of 36 private sector practitioners were given individual awards and two organizations namely FOGSI and IMA were felicitated for their contributions to the initiative. A virtual ‘Hall of Fame’ has also been launched to recognize their contributions: https://pmsma.nhp.gov.in/hall-of-fame-2/. Several States such as Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Haryana, Rajasthan etc. have also awarded private sector practitioners for their contribution to PMSMA. (e)  All pregnant women including pregnant women from farmer families are entitled to receive services under PMSMA. National and State level IEC campaigns are regularly organized to encourage more and more pregnant women to avail PMSMA services. A mobile and web based application has been designed to help pregnant women find their nearest PMSMA facility. In order to access this service, pregnant women can visit https://pmsma.nhp.gov.in/or download the ''PMSMA'' mobile application.
