                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4009






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






New Highways in Andhra Pradesh




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has expanded and developed new highways in Andhra Pradesh during 2017-2018 and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government/NHAI proposes to develop a new highways between Khammam and Kovvur via LND-Data and Dondapudi Panchayats and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the villages along this proposed route have requested By-Passes around LND-Data and Dondapudi and if so, the reaction of the Government thereto; and(d) 	the steps taken by the Government to ensure that lives and livelihoods of people of affected villages are not disrupted?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	This Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways in the Country. Development of a total length of 799 kms of National Highways have been taken up in Andhra Pradesh during 2017-2018 at a total cost of Rs. 11243 crores. (b) & (c)	The National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has taken up preparation of detailed project report (DPR) for “Construction of 4 lane Khammam- Devarapalle section (Greenfield alignment) under Bharatmala Pariyojana in the States of Telangana & Andhra Pradesh”. As the said Greenfield alignment does not pass through the villages of LND Data and Dondapudi Panchayats, construction of bypasses around these villages does not arise.(d)	Land of the affected villages is acquired as per the procedure laid down in NH Act, 1956 and compensation is determined by the Competent Authority for Land Acquisition as per RFCTLARR Act, 2013.*****
