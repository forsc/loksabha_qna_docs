                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

997






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Electrification Work in Bihar




Rama Devi














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether electrification work has not been done yet in many rural areas of Sheohar district in Bihar and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the number of villages in said district which still have no access to electricity; and(c) 	the works being done to provide electricity to all villages in the said district?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP( SHRI  R.K. SINGH) (a) to (c) :	As reported by the State, all the inhabited census villages in Bihar, including those in the district Sheohar, stand electrified on 28th April, 2018.**************
