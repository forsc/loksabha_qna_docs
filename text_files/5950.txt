                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5295






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






DAY-NULM




Chandra Sekhar Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government is considering to extend outreach of its flagship project Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) among the poorest of poor in the country through a series of events;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Union Government has taken note that numbers of eligible people have not been able to get benefit under the Mission, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (d) 	whether the Union Government is also considering to conduct ‘safety net survey’ to identify eligible people who are left out under the said scheme and if so, the details thereof;(e) 	whether the survey is also being conducted to identify eligible Self-Help Group (SHG) members not covered under the said Mission and if so, the details thereof indicating the number of SGH members working at present in the country, State-wise; and(f) 	the steps taken by the Government to make significant progress under the Mission?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b): The Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is implementing Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana - National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) to reduce poverty and vulnerability of urban poor households by enabling them to access gainful self-employment and skilled wage employment opportunities, for improvement in their livelihoods on a sustainable basis. The mission also aims to build strong grass-root level institutions of the urban poor.The Ministry has organized ‘Shehri Samridhi Utsav’, a fortnight of events under DAY- NULM, from 1st -15th February 2019, to extend its'' outreach to the poorest of the poor and the most vulnerable, showcase the initiatives and strengthening the safety net for the beneficiaries by facilitating their access to other welfare. (c) to (e): One of the activities during the Shehri Samridhi Utsav was to identify eligible Self Help Groups (SHG) members through Safety Net Survey for linking them with various government schemes namely, Swachh Bharat Mission (U), Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (U), Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana, Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana, Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana, Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Yojana and National Nutrition Mission. Statement showing number of SHGs formed under DAY-NULM since 2014-15, State/UT-wise is at Annexure.(f): The Ministry is regularly monitoring implementation of the Mission in all the States/UTs through meetings, video conferences and correspondences.        *****
