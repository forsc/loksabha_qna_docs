                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

314






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Challenges Faced by Armed Forces




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the country faces the challenges of modernising its ageing military hardware, indigenising its military hardware to ensure self-sufficiency and harmonising its combat power to ensure that it can defend its borders against security threats; (b) if so, the details thereof and the measures being taken by the Government to meet these challenges; and (c) the steps taken by the Government in regard to the country’s problems of prioritising spending and getting its armed forces to plan to fight together rather than coordinate their operations in integrated theatres?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF DEFENCE                                    (SHRI RAJNATH SINGH) (a) to (c):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
