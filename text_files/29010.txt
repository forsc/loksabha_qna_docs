                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

405






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






EWS in Bihar




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.405TO BE ANSWERED ON 30.11.2021EWS IN BIHAR †405. SHRI SUNIL KUMAR PINTU:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is aware that the senior citizens belonging to the Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) in the country, especially in Bihar are not getting the benefits of social security schemes due to irregularities in the system; and(b) if so, the action being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) & (b): Under the umbrella Scheme of Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana (AVYAY) for the welfare of Senior Citizens, the details of funds released to Bihar during the financial years  2018-19 to 2021-22 is given at Annexure.Further, under the Scheme of Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana (RVY), the distribution camps to provide physical aids and assisted living devices have been conducted in ten (10) districts of Bihar i.e. Bhojpur, Buxar, Gaya, Begusarai, Jamui, Purnea, Araria, West Champaran, Sitamarhi and Aurangabad. ***** Annexure referred to in Reply to the Part (a) & (b) to the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 405 to be answered on 30.11.2021Details of funds released to Bihar under the Scheme of Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana (AVYAY) during 2018-19 to 2020-21 (Financial in lakhs)Sl.
