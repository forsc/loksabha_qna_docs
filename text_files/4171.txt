                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3858






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






PMS Scheme for OBC Students




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Post Matric Scholarship Scheme (PMS) is being implemented for the students of Other Backward Classes (OBCs) in the various States in the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) the details of the funds allocated, released and utilised by various States including Chhattisgarh during the last three years and the current year, State-wise; (c) whether the funds allocated by the Government under the aforesaid scheme have not been utilised fully; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b):Yes Sir, Scheme of Post-Matric Scholarship for students of Other Backward Classes (OBCs) is being implemented in States/UTs having OBC population.Details of the funds allocated, released and utilised under the scheme of Post Matric Scholarship for OBC students for various states including Chhattisgarh during the last three years and current year State-wise is given in Annexure.(c) & (d): Yes Sir, the following States/Union Territories have not utilized the fund fully released by the Government:Andhra Pradesh, Haryana, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Punjab, Rajasthan, Telangana, Uttarakhand, Delhi, Assam and Manipur.Physical and financial progress report along with. Utilisation Certificate is sought from the concerned States/Union Territories. Regular Review meetings is also held with the concerned State  Government/Union Territory Government officials****
