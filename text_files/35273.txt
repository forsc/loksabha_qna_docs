                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2615






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Implementation of Projects under AMRUT




Dileshwar Kamait













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                








                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the district-wise details of the projects being run in various States of the country including Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, Haryana, Jammu & Kashmir, Bihar and North-Eastern States including Assam under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT);(b) 	whether the Government has any mechanism for monitoring of the projects launched by various municipal corporations and other bodies;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                                         THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                                 MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):	Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) scheme was launched on 25 June, 2015 in 500 selected cities.  City-wise details of the projects being run in various States and Union Territories (UTs) of the country including Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, Haryana, Jammu & Kashmir, Bihar and North-Eastern States including Assam under AMRUT are given at ANNEXURE-I.(b) & (c):	In order to fast track the implementation of the Mission, its progress has been periodically reviewed with States /UTsand monitored through regular video conferences, webinars, workshops etc. by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs .There is a dedicated AMRUT portal for tracking the progress of projects. States/ UTs are required to update data on the portal on regular basis.  Implementation of projects at field level are assessed and monitored by Independent Review and Monitoring Agencies (IRMAs), appointed by each State and UT.(d):		Does not arise.                                      *****
