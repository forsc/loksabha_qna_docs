                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

403






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Creation of New Jobs in MSME Sector




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to create new jobs as compensation for the loss of livelihood caused due to phasing out or reducing reliance on industries like plastic and coal (Unsustainable Industries) and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government proposes to ease the transition of the MSME industries involved in these sectors, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. *403 FOR ANSWER ON 31.03.2022(a) & (b): 	Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) has prepared a Comprehensive Action Plan (CAP) for providing assistance to the MSMEs for elimination of notified Single Use Plastic items. These initiatives would help in transition of the MSME industries involved in this sector.	Ministry of Coal has intimated that “No transition away from coal is happening in foreseeable future”.
