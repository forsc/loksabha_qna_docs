                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3522






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Vacant Posts Under Reserved Categories




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether posts, reserved for Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes in different categories under various Departments/PSUs are lying vacant; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the year-wise and category-wise details of appointments made during the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (c):   Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT) monitors the progress in filling up of backlog vacancies reserved for Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Scheduled Tribes (STs) with ten Ministries/Departments, having more than 90% of the employees of Central Government. DoPT also collects online data on appointments of SCs and STs in the posts and services of the Central Government from all the Ministries/Departments of the Central Government. Year-wise and category-wise details of backlog reserved vacancies and appointments of SCs and STs as provided by the Ministries/Departments during the last three years(as on 01.01.2018, 01.01.2019 and 01.01.2020), are at Annexue-I and Annexure-II respectively. The consolidated data for the current year is being collected.*****
