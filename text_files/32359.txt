                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3791






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






MGNREGS in Andhra Pradesh




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) works undertaken in Andhra Pradesh in 2019-20, 2020-21 and2021-22, district-wise;(b) 	the details of mandays provided to labour during the above period, month-wise, year-wise and districtwise;(c) 	whether it is true that second tranche of MGNREGS dues to the tune of Rs. 5,000 crores are pending for payment to the State Government of Andhra Pradesh; and(d) 	if so, the steps taken by the Government to release the pending dues?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):       District-wise detail of works taken up in the State of Andhra Pradesh in financial years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 (as on 15.12.2021) under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is given at Annexure-I. (b):    District-wise and month wise detail of persondays generated in the State of Andhra Pradesh for the financial years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is given at Annexure-II. (c) & (d):         There is no pending liability for wage component as on 15.12.2021 Mahatma Gandhi NREGS for the State of Andhra Pradesh. As informed by the State of Andhra Pradesh through Scheme MIS (NREGAsoft), Rs. 1,341 Crore is getting reflected as payment due for material and administrative components as on 15.12.2021. A proposal from the State of Andhra Pradesh has been received by this Ministry for release of funds for material and administrative components. The proposal was examined in detail and the State was requested to submit a detailed proposal as per the provisions of the Act and guidelines. ******
