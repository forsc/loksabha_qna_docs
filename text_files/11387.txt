                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

287






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Processing of Agro Produces




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated any long term plan for processing of agriculture produce in rural areas of Rajasthan;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c): The Ministry of Food Processing Industries is already implementing a Central Sector Umbrella Scheme – PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA - (PMKSY) with an allocation of Rs. 6000 crore for the period 2016-20 with the scheme components viz: (i) Mega Food Parks, (ii) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, (iii) Infrastructure for Agro-processing Clusters, (iv) Creation of Backward and Forward Linkages (v) Creation / Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities, (vi) Food Safety and Quality Assurance Infrastructure and (vii) Human Resources and Institutions for overall development food processing sector by, inter alia, creation of modern infrastructure and efficient supply chain for facilitating preservation and processing of various agro-produce  such cereals, vegetables, fruits, milk, fish, poultry & meat etc. in rural and urban areas of the country including in the State of Rajasthan. PMKSY is a comprehensive package resulting in creation of modern infrastructure with efficient supply chain management from farm gate to retail outlet. Under the schemes of PMKSY financial assistance in the form grants-in-aid is provided to farmers, entrepreneurs, organizations such as Central and State PSUs/Joint Ventures/Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)/NGOs/Cooperatives/SHGs/ Public and Private Companies etc. to set up food processing industries/projects/units. These Schemes are demand driven. Financial assistance is provided to the eligible applicant from all parts of the country including the State of Rajasthan as per the Scheme guidelines and against the Expression of Interest issued by the Ministry from time to time.*****
