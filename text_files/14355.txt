                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Assistance from HUDCO




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether a large number of States have demanded assistance from Housing and Urban Development Corporation Limited(HUDCO) for construction of houses; andb)	if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)& (b)Yes Sir. Housing and Urban Development Corporation Limited (HUDCO) has been receiving project proposals for loan assistance for construction of houses from various States.During the financial year 2019-20, HUDCO received14   Project proposals from five States for a total loan assistance of Rs. 1,767 crore out of which 8 schemes envisaging  construction of 2,36,859 dwelling units have been approved by HUDCO till date. The total loan amount approved for these 8 schemes is Rs. 862.97 crore.                                   ******
