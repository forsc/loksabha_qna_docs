                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2808






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Revitalizing Canals of Ernakulam




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any project proposed to revitalize canals of Ernakulam, Kerala; (b)	 if so, the total project cost and details of the proposed project including the details of the names, length, location etc. of the canals; (c) 	whether the Government is considering ‘the Amsterdam model’ of “canal rejuvenation” as a model to be implemented in Ernakulam; (d) 	whether the Government is aware of the fact that around 1,000 to 1500 families are scattered on the canal banks and if the Government can give them proper accommodation with toilets and sewage facilities, relocation may not be a problem; and (e)	if so, the details thereof and proposed plans to utilise the valuable urban land recovered?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b).	Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) was launched by Government of India  on  25 June  2015 in 500 Mission cities for a period of five years i.e. from 2015-16 to 2019-20 with focus on development of basic urban infrastructure in the Mission cities. Water supply, sewerage & septage management, storm water drainage, green spaces & park, and non-motorized urban transport are the thrust areas under the Mission.  State Government of Kerala has taken up 6 projects under AMRUT relating to revitalization of canals in Ernakulam. The details of the projects is annexed. (c). Kerala Government has informed that rejuvenation of 5 major canals mainly Thevara canal, Perandoor canal, Market canal, Chilavanoor canal, Edapally canal for a total length of 35 kms, with a total cost of Rs.1356 crore, has been proposed under Kerala Infrastructure Investment Fund Board(KIIFB) project, detailed project report of which was prepared by Kochi Metro Rail Limited (KMRL).(d)& (e). Around 600 scattered families are identified in the canal banks and rehabilitation activities are included in the KMRL proposal.                                           *****
