                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3124






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






ASPIRE Scheme




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the Technology Business Incubators (TBIs) and Livelihood Business Incubators (LBIs) set up and functional during the last three years under A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry and Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE) scheme to entrepreneurship and to promote innovation in rural industry, State and year-wise;(b) 	the number of youth trained and skilled through various TBIs and LBIs set  up under ''ASPIRE'';(c) 	the details of investment made to set up TBIs and LBIs under ''ASPIRE'' during the above period, State and year-wise; and(d) 	the details of the fund or funds created along with the value thereof under ''ASPIRE'' scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (c): 	A statement showing the details of the 91 Technology Business Incubators (TBIs) and Livelihood Business Incubators (LBIs) that have been approved and functional since 2015-16 under ‘A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry and Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE)’ to enhance  entrepreneurship and to promote innovation in rural industry is Annexed. State and year-wise details of investment made to set up TBIs and LBIs under ''ASPIRE'' during the above period, are also given in the statement.(b):	The number of youth admitted in different LBIs during the period December 2016 to December 2019 is 33885 and the number of youth among these trained and passed out of these LBIs is 29925, remaining are under training.  (d): 	Fund of Funds with a corpus of Rs.310 crore was established under ASPIRE programme with SIDBI as Fund Manager. Under the Fund of Funds programme, commitments are being made to individual Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) registered with SEBI. These AIFs will invest in MSMEs that are Agro/Rural focused. As per information furnished by SIDBI, as on February 29, 2020, 5 Alternate Investment Funds (AIFs) were supported with an aggregate commitments of Rs.47.50 crore of which an amount of Rs.27.01 crore has been disbursed. Further, these AIFs invested in 49 MSMEs an aggregate amount of Rs.456.70 crore (out of which 27 MSMEs are agro and rural focused with an aggregate investment of Rs.164.4 crore).
