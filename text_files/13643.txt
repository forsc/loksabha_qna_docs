                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2464






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Funds for Toilet Construction




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any details about the funds used for the construction of toilets across the country; and(b) if so, the details of such funds and the number of benefited households, State-wise?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) As per the data reported by the States/UTs on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) of Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen)[SBM(G)], Rs. 53032.10 crore has been utilized as Central share for construction of individual household latrines (IHHLS) under SBM(G) from 2.10.2014 to 3.3.2020. State/UT-wise no. of IHHLs constructed and Central share expenditure incurred for construction IHHLs under SBM(G) from 2.10.2014 to 3.3.2020 is given at Annexure-1.
