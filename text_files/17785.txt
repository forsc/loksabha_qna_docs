                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1520






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Industry Status to Agriculture




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether it is not true that agriculture plays a vital role in the economic development of the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	 whether it is also not true that there have been genuine demands to grant ‘industry‘ status to agriculture to push it further in all respects;(c)	 the details of each of the benefits being given to industry during the last ten years;(d)	 the details of each of the benefits being given to agriculture during the last ten years; and(e) 	whether there are any constraints that the Government is facing in granting ‘industry‘ status to agriculture? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	Agriculture plays a vital role in the economic development of the country as it ensures food and nutritional security to the people and provides livelihood and employment opportunities for the majority of population. Besides, it supplies raw material to non-agricultural sectors. 	Given the importance of agriculture sector, the Government has substantially enhanced the budget allocation for the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare to Rs. 142762.35 crore (BE) during BE 2020-21.(b) to (e):	Government has been receiving suggestions from various quarters to accord industry status to the agriculture sector.  However, Government seeks to bestow on the agriculture sector as many benefits as available to those in the manufacturing sector, such as, easy availability of credit and other inputs and creation of infrastructure facilities for development of agriculture sector, including marketing and post- harvest management. 	Details of various interventions/initiatives taken by the Government for the benefit of farmers during the last few years are given in the Annexure.
