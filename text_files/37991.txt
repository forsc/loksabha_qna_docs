                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5075






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Standardization of Skill Development




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the percentage of women applicants out of the total number of applicants belonging to Karnal Parliamentary Constituency of Haryana who were imparted training last three years;(b) 	whether the Government has initiated action for providing recognition and standardization of Skill Development under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana(PMKVY); (c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry is delivering skills to the youth across the country through its flagship scheme Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY). Under PMKVY, during last three years i.e. 2018-21, 25,459 candidates have been trained / oriented in Karnal Parliamentary Constituency (Karnal and Panipat districts); out of which, 10,822 are female candidates which is around 43% of total trained / oriented candidates. (b) and (c)      Under PMKVY, candidates are trained in short term training (STT) and oriented in Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL). Training is being imparted under NSQF (National Skills Qualification Framework) aligned job roles and scheme follows the standardized Common Cost Norms.
