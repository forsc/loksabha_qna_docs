                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Development of Highways in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey













                                                    Vishnu Dayal Ram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of National and State Highways in Jharkhand; (b) the details of State roads that have been declared as new NHs in the State of Jharkhand during the last three years; (c) whether the Government has provided funds for development of the above new NHs; and (d) if so, the details thereof along with the amount of contracts for the said roads and the funds released and utilised thereon, project-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS       (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) This Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways (NHs) in the country. At present, there are30(thirty) nos. of NHs in the State of Jharkhand. These are NH-18, 19, 20, 22, 33, 39, 43, 49, 114A, 118, 133, 133A, 133B, 139, 143, 143A, 143B, 143D, 143H,143AG,218, 220, 320, 320D, 320G, 333, 333A, 343, 419 & 522.(b) The details of State roads that have been declared as new NHs in the State of Jharkhand during the last three years is at Annexure.(c) & (d) Central Government takes up development and maintenance of these newly declared NHs after entrustment of these NHs to therespective implementing agencies, viz. State Government/NHAI.
