                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3835






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Veterinary RT-PCR Lab in Rajasthan




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether, the Government proposes to set up veterinary RT-PCR lab etc. in the State of Rajasthan for testing of viscera, sample etc. of animals considering the geographical location and people’s dependency on animal husbandry in the States;(b)	if so, the time by when it is likely to set up; (c)	if not, the reasons along with the details thereof; (d)	whether the State Government has sent any proposal to the Union Government regarding setting up of any lab managed by the Centre in the State of Rajasthan; and (e)	if so, the details of the progress and the decision made on the said proposal? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING(PARSHOTTAM  RUPALA )(a)	& (b) Animal husbandry, including setting up of disease diagnostic laboratories, is a State subject. However, Livestock Health &Disease Control (LH&DC) scheme of Central Government providesfinancial assistancewith central share (90:10 for North Eastern States & Himalayan States, 100% for Union Territories and 60:40 for all other States)towards supplementing the efforts of State/Union Territory Governments for strengthening such laboratories. 	Accordingly, Central Government has released Rs.1041.24 lakh during 2020-21, which includes Rs.20.00 lakh,for strengthening of State Disease Diagnostic Laboratory (SDDC), Jaipur(Rajasthan).(c)	Question doesn’t arise. (d)	& (e)The question does not arise in view of reply at (a) and (b) above. *****
