                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1062






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Pulse Research Institutes




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	  the number of Ayurvedic Colleges, Pulse Science and Pulse Research Institutes at present along with the number of undergraduate and post graduate seats in the said colleges and institutes, State-wise including Madhya Pradesh;(b)	 the number of research works carried out in the Postgraduate Ayurvedic Colleges during the last five years along with the details of the medicines developed with the said research works;(c)	 whether effective medicines developed with these research works are being manufactured on a regular basis;(d)	 if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the details of the medicines being used in the treatment of the patients?






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTRY OF AYUSH( SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	At present there are four hundred nineteen(419) Ayurvedic Colleges functioning in the country. (State-wise details including Madhya Pradesh are placed at Annexure-I). Further, at present there are twentynine thousand four hundred seventy (29470) Under Graduate seats and five thousand thirty nine (5039) Post Graduate seats available in Ayurvedic Colleges. State-wise detailsincluding Madhya Pradeshare placed at Annexure-I. Sir, at present there is no Pulse Science and Pulse Research Institute.However, Pulse Science is taught as a part of teaching curriculum to Under-Graduate students under the subject of Rog-Nidan (in Theory and Practical). Presently no course for Pulse Science is running separately in the Teaching Institutes of Ayurveda.(b)	, (c), (d) & (e) The research work done in Post- Graduate Ayurvedic Colleges is not utilized to develop medicines. The research work data generated in teaching Hospital associated with Ayurvedic Colleges is used for training of students and also in public Health related matters.However, Government of India has established Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences, an autonomous body under the Ministry of AYUSH as an apex organization for undertaking, coordinating, formulating, developing and promoting research in Ayurvedic sciences on scientific lines. The activities are carried out through 30 Institutes/Centers/Units located all over the India and also through collaborative studies with various Universities, Hospitals and institutes.  CCRAS is also continuously engaged in development of new Ayurvedic drugs. So far, CCRAS has developed 11technologies which have been transferred through National Research DevelopmentCorporation (NRDC). The details are appended at Annexure –II.***
