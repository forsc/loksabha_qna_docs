                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

240






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Crop Loss due to Heavy Rains and Floods




Amol Ramsing Kolhe













                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Kunwar Danish Ali
                                                








                                                    Ratansinh Magansinh Rathod
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether many States in the country have been affected by heavy rains and consequent floods in the recent months resulting in destruction of standing crops, fruits and vegetables to a great extent;(b)	 if so, the details of the total loss of crops/paddy/ vegetables and the districts that have been severely affected;(c) 	whether the State Governments have demanded any assistance to compensate for the loss suffered by the farmers;(d) 	if so, the details thereof and the funds allocated to each State;(e) 	whether the Government has any plan to waive loan of farmers in the affected districts, the details thereof; and(f) 	the corrective steps taken by the Government to avoid such damage of crops of fruits and vegetable in the future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):    The State Government is primarily responsible for providing necessary relief measures in the wake of natural calamities. For undertaking relief measures, funds are available with the State Government in the form of State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF). Additional financial assistance, over and above SDRF, is considered from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) for natural calamities of severe nature and is approved on the basis of Memorandum received from the State Government, in accordance with established procedures.  As per the information provided by the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), State-wise details of crop area affected due to flood/landslide etc. during 2021, as reported by the State Governments, are given at Annexure-I. Allocation and release of funds under SDRF during 2021-22 (as on 25.11.2021) are at Annexure-II.(e):     No, Sir.(f):     Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) provides training to the farmers on effective flood management in flood plains and flood-prone ecologies of India.
