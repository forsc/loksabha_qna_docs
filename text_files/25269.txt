                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1041






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Healing System of Tribals




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any initiative to protect the healing system of tribals in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to establish any institute to promote traditional knowledge of tribals with respect to healing in the state of Kerala; and(d) if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA) (a & b) Yes, Sir.  Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides funds to State Tribal Research institute / renowned research institutes for under taking research work on Tribal medicinal practices and medicinal plants under the schemes “Support to Tribal Research Institutes” and ‘Tribal Festival, Research Information and Mass Education’. Research organisations conduct many programmes and activities to identify, document and collect details about ethnomedicine,healers and their therapeutic practices of treatment of diseases.  Moreover, Ministry has made Tribal Research Institute, Uttarakhand as the nodal & coordinating TRI for all projects related to traditional medicine and healing practices. The details of such  projects undertaken by State Tribal Research Institutes / research organizations are annexed. (c & d) There is no proposal in the Ministry  to establish any institute in the State of Kerala to promote traditional knowledge of tribes for healing.    AnnexureAnnex referred to in reply to part (a) & (b) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1041 for answer on 26.07.2021A.
