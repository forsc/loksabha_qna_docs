                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

582






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Establishment of New Educational Institutions of AYUSH




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether in view of shortage of AYUSH facilities/ hospitals in rural areas, the Government proposes to establish new educational institution for AYSUH system of medicines in the country and if so, details thereof;(b)	whether the Government or private sector is being encouraged/promoted to get world-class recognition for the products of Ayurveda and other systems of alternative medicines for the expansion in rural areas, having enough land for opening of such facilities;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the reasons for neglecting rural parts of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	:	There is no such information about shortage of AYUSH facilities/Hospitals in rural areas. However, the Government has proposed to establish new educational institution for AYUSH system of medicines in the country and the details are provided in Annexure I.(b)	to (d): Government of India, Ministry of Ayush has implemented Centrally Sponsored Scheme under National Ayush Mission to strengthen State Government Quality Control Mechanism, office of State Licensing Authorities (Ayush)., Ayush Pharmacies and Drug Testing Laboratories since 12th Plan. Further Central sector Scheme for augmenting quality of Ayush drugs Ayush Oushadhi Gunavatta Evam Uttpadan Samvardhan Yojana (AOGUSY) has been implemented during the current five year plan i.e., up to 31.3.2026. So far Government has published Ayurvedic Formulary of India (Part I-Ill) containing 986 Formulations, Siddha Formulary of India (Part I-Il) containing 400 Formulations and National Formulary of Unani Medicine (Vol. 1-VI) containing 1230 Formulations. Monographs of quality standards of 645 Single drugs and 203 Formulations of Ayurveda; 139 Single drugs and 1 Formulation of Siddha; 298 Single drugs and 201 Formulations of Unani; and 1117 drugs of Homeopathy (Vol. I-X) have been published. The Government has strengthened 35 State Government Drug Testing Laboratories for testing of Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy (ASU&H) medicines in the country. Under Rule 160A to 160J of Drug & Cosmetics Rules 1945, 65 private Drug Testing Laboratories are approved for testing of Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy Drugs. There is one Central Drug Laboratory i.e. Pharmacopoeia Commission for Indian Medicine & Homoeopathy Laboratory for the purpose of testing or Analysis of ASU&H Drugs. In order to promote the export of Ayush products Government has taken following initiatives: 1. WHO-GMP (COPP) has been given to 31 Ayurvedic Drug manufacturers to facilitate the export of Ayurveda, Siddha, and Unani & Homoeopathy Drugs. 2. Ayush Standard/Premium mark certificate is being given by Q.C.I. 3. Exemption is given for labelling of ASU drugs under Drugs & Cosmetics Rules 1945.*****
