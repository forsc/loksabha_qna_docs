                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3270






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Scholarship Scheme for Students of Rural Areas




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3270 TO BE ANSWERED ON 16.03.2021SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME FOR STUDENTS OF RURAL AREAS †3270. SHRI VIJAY KUMAR: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the Union Government has received proposals from the State Government of Bihar for any scholarship scheme to increase awareness towards education among primary to high school students of rural areas in the current year;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any fund has been allocated by the Government to the States to provide scholarships in Government schools of rural areas to create awareness towards education; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (d): No Sir, no such proposal has been received from the State Government of Bihar. However, as per scheme guidelines all the State Governments and Union Territory Administrations are advised to publish the scheme and invite applications by issuing advertisement in local language, in the leading newspapers of the State and through their respective websites and other media outfits.*****
