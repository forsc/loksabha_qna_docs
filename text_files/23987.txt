                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5045






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Bio-gas Plants




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the details of Bio-gas Plants set up in the country during the last three years and the current year, district and year-wise; (b) whether 8,500 Bio-gas Plants are supposed to set up in the State of Telangana;  (c)  if so, the details thereof; and   (d) the details of said plants that are going to be set up, with a particular reference to Ranga Reddy district?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) The district wise details of biogas plants installed in the country during the last three years   ( 2017-18  to 2019-20) and current year 2020-21 are given at Annexure-I. (b) to(d) For the current financial year 2020-21, under the New National Biogas and Organic Manure Programme ( NNBOMP) of this Ministry, a target of 1000 number of biogas plants has been allocated to the State of Telangana, including for the district of Ranga Reddy.*****
