                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4774






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Development of Tourism Infrastructure in Bihar




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil













                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds sanctioned by the Government and the amount utilized for development of tourism infrastructure in the country with specific reference to Bihar and Telangana during the last five years, scheme and year-wise; (b) 	the total number of tourist sites for which development works have been done during the above period in the country including Bihar and Telangana; (c) 	the details of the number of foreign tourist arrivals in the country during the said period, State/UT-wise and their growth figure; and (d) 	whether the Government has prepared any comprehensive roadmap to develop tourism infrastructure across the country especially in States like Bihar and Telangana having abundant tourism potential and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	The Ministry of Tourism, under the schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies, provides Central Financial Assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of thematic tourist circuits in the country, for holistic development of identified pilgrimage and heritage destinations and for the development of tourist infrastructure at destinations owned by the Central Agencies.The projects under the above schemes are identified in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies and are sanctioned subject to their adherence to relevant scheme guidelines, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, availability of funds and utilization of funds released earlier. Based on the above, the details of projects sanctioned by the Ministry under the above schemes for development of tourism- related infrastructure in the country including the States of Bihar and Telangana during the last five years is given at Annexure I.The Foreign Tourist Arrivals (FTAs) in the country during the years 2014 to 2018 alongwith the corresponding year on year growth rates are as below:<pre>Year	FTAs (in million)	Growth (in %)*2014	7.68	10.22015	8.03	4.52016	8.80	9.72017	10.04	14.02018	10.56 (P)	5.2</pre>* The growth has been calculated in terms of absolute totalP: ProvisionalThe State/UT- wise breakup of Foreign Tourist Visits (FTVs) during 2014 to 2018 are given at Annexure II. ********
