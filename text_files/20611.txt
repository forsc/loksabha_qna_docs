                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1680






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Promotion of Green Energy




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the target for electricity generation from new and renewable energy sources during the last three years and the current year; (b) whether there is any proposal for concessions to State Governments in terms of taxes and incentives for promotion of green energy; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether there is any proposal for coastal States for optimum generation of electricity from new and renewable energy sources as they have better avenues for this; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) The Ministry has been working to achieve the target of 175 GW of installed Renewable Energy capacity (excluding large hydro) by 2022.Ministry of Power has notified long term growth trajectory of Renewable Purchase Obligation (RPO) as share of renewable energy in the total electricity consumption (excluding consumption met from large hydro sources of power). RPO targets, notified by the Ministry of Power, for the last three years and current year were as under:Year 	RPO ( in %)2017-18	14.252018-19	17.002019-20	17.502020-21 	19.00(b&c) The Government has taken several measures for the promotion of renewable energy in the country. These  inter-alia include:•	permitting Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) up to 100 percent under the automatic route, •	waiver of Inter State Transmission System (ISTS) charges and losses for inter-state sale of solar and wind power for projects to be commissioned by 30th June 2023, •	fiscal incentives such as accelerated depreciation, Goods and Services Tax (GST) at lower rates, concessional custom duty etc,•	declaration of trajectory for Renewable Purchase Obligation (RPO) up to the year 2022, •	setting up of Ultra Mega Renewable Energy Parks to provide land  and transmission on plug and play basis, •	Schemes such as Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM), Solar Rooftop Phase II, 12000 MW CPSU Scheme Phase II, etc,•	laying of new transmission lines and creating new sub-station capacity under the Green Energy Corridor Scheme for evacuation of renewable power,•	notification of standards for deployment of solar photovoltaic system/devises, •	setting up of Project Development Cell for attracting and facilitating investments,•	Standard Bidding Guidelines for tariff based competitive bidding process for procurement of Power from Grid Connected Solar PV and Wind Projects, •	Government has issued orders that power shall be dispatched against Letter of Credit(LC) or advance payment to ensure timely payment by Distribution Licensees to RE generators.(d & e) No sir.*****
