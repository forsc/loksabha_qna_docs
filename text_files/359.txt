                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Spiritual Tourism Circuit Project




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has approved a Sree Narayana Guru Spiritual Tourism Circuit Project linking Sivagiri and other places in Kerala; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated and released?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) and (b):		The Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned the project ‘Development of Sivagiri Sree Narayana Guru Ashram- Arruvipuram- Kunnumpara Sree Subrahmania-   Chembazhanthi Sree Narayana Gurukulam’ in Kerala under Spiritual Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme in the year 2018-19 for Rs.69.47 crore. Till date, no funds have been released by the Ministry under this project.  ********
