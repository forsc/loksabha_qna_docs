                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1032






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






LPG Beneficiaries under PMUY




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                     Bhagirath Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of gas connections issued and the total number of gas connectionsremaining to be issued under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) in the country tillJune, 2021, State-wise and district-wise;(b) the criteria laid down for selecting eligible families under the said scheme and thebase year for their selection;(c) whether the Government proposes to give gas connection also to the remaining neweligible families in addition to the selected eligible families at present in several Statesby covering them under the said scheme; and(d) if so, the time by which it is likely to be done and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) As of 30.06.2021, 8 crore LPG connections are released under Pradhan MantriUjjwala Yojana(PMUY). The target under the scheme, to release 8 crore LPGconnections by March 2020, was achieved during September 2019. State-wise detailsof LPG connections released under PMUY are at Annexure.(b) Under PMUY, LPG connection was released in the name of adult woman of thepoor family, subject to the condition that no LPG connection exists in the name of anyfamily member of the household and fulfilling other terms and conditions. Initially, thebeneficiaries under PMUY were identified either from SECC list or from seven otheridentified categories. Subsequently, Government extended the benefit to all left out poorfamilies under PMUY subject to fulfilment of terms & conditions.(c) & (d) Hon''ble Finance Minister, during her budget speech on 1st February, 2021,has announced to provide 1 crore more LPG connections under PMUY in addition to 8crore LPG connections already released. The modalities for releasing 1 crore LPGconnections under PMUY have already been finalised and have been communicated tothe Oil Marketing Companies.----------
