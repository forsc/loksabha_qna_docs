                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3822






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Betel Leaf Cultivation




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government is aware that betel leaf cultivation is done in 13 States of the country including Sandalpur, Aantri and Cheenor villages of Bhitarwar, Gwalior and Malwa region of  Madhya Pradesh and the crop gets destroyed due to excess heat, cold and rain; (b)	 if so, whether betel leaf crop was destroyed totally in Gwalior as recently temperature came down to 2.2  celsius there; (c)	 whether farmers do not have adequate resources for the next crop as compensation given to them is quite meagre and if so, the details thereof; (d)	 whether cultivation of betel leaf is not covered under crop insurance scheme; and(e)	 if so, whether the Government proposes to include betel leaf cultivation under Crop Insurance Scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Betel leaf is widely cultivated in the different States of the country including in Sandalpur, Aantri and Cheenor villages of Bhitarwar, Gwalior and Malwa region of Madhya Pradesh and that adverse weather conditions affect the betel leaf production.(b) & (c):	No such information has been received in the Ministry.(d) & (e):	Financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage arising out of natural calamities & adverse weather incidence are provided under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) & Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS). Crops and area under PMBSY and WBCIS are notified by the concerned State/UT Government. Government of Madhya Pradesh has informed that Betel vine is not covered under crop insurance.********
