                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Promotion of KVIC Products




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to promote and export products manufactured in villages under Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC); (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has accorded the status of Export Promotion Commission to KVIC to facilitate it; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	whether new employment opportunities have been created for women and rural population as a result of it; and(f) 	if so, the details of the employment opportunities created during each of the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b): 	The Government of India, through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC), is promoting Khadi and Village Industries (KVI) activities in the country. The entrepreneurs/ units assisted through implementation of various KVI Schemes by KVIC are engaged in production and sales of their products. The steps/initiatives taken by the Government to promote and export products manufactured in villages under KVIC is placed at Annexure-I.  (c) & (d):	Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India has extended Deemed EPC Status to KVIC during 2006 for promotion of KVI products in the global market. The membership of KVI-EPC will facilitate provision of participation in International Trade Fairs/ Awareness programme etc. Around 1088 KVI Institutions and PMEGP/REGP Units have been taken its membership to enter the field of Export.(e):	Ministry of MSME, through KVIC, implements a flagship scheme viz. Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) in the country. Under this Scheme, new employment opportunities created for women during last three years are as follows:<pre>Year	Estimated Women Employment under PMEGP2016-17	834332017-18	1253522018-19	203472</pre>(f):	State/UT-wise estimated employment created during the last three years in entire KVI Sector is placed at Annexure-II.
