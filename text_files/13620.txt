                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2455






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Swachh Bharat Mission




Arvind Dharmapuri













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the achievements made under Swachh Bharat Mission in the State of Telangana during the last three years;(b) the funds sanctioned for Swachh Bharat Mission to the State of Telangana during the said period;(c) whether the funds released have been utilized fully for the purpose of providing sanitation facilities in the rural areas;(d) whether the Government has observed any deviation of guidelines in the implementation of the programme and diversion of funds to other purposes/schemes and if so, the details thereof; and(e) if so, the steps taken by the Government in violation of guidelines?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) The number of individual household latrines (IHHLs) constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM(G)] in Telangana during the last three years, as per the data reported by the State on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS), are as under:Financial Year	No. of IHHLs constructed2016-17	5,20,0292017-18	15,52,9752018-19	4,70,819All the 14,200 villages of the Telangana State have declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF).  (b) Central share released to Telangana under SBM(G) during the last three years are as under: -Financial Year	Funds released  (Rs. in crore)2016-17	135.722017-18	481.942018-19	515.05(c) to (e)  The funds released under SBM(G) are usually utilized by the States as per the SBM(G) guidelines. Whenever any deviation from guidelines in utilization of funds is observed, the same is taken up with the States for appropriate corrective measures.
