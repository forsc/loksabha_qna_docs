                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

436






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Horticulture Production Hub




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that the North Bengal region is a horticulture production hub with huge potential for pineapples, oranges, mangoes, bananas, litchi, jackfruit, lime, etc., which are in high demand across the world;(b) 	whether some fruit processing facilities are already in existence in the North Bengal districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong; and(c) 	whether the Government is considering to establish a food processing plant in the North Bengal region given the high quality of fruit production and suitable agro-climatic conditions, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a).	 Yes Sir, North Bengal region is a potential Horticulture Production Hub for various fruit crops namely, pineapple, orange, mango, banana, litchi, jackfruit, lime etc. The major growing areas of these fruit crops are:Fruit Crop	Major Growing AreasPineapple	Jalpaiguri, Cooch behar,  Darjeeling, Uttar DinajpurOrange	Darjeeling and KalimpongMango	MaldaBanana	Jalpaiguri, Cooch behar, DinajpurLitchi	Malda and parts of DinajpurJackfruit	Jalpaiguri, Cooch behar, Plain area of DarjeelingLime / lemon	All the district of North Bengal (b) & (c).  Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) does not set up food processing infrastructure on its own. However, it provides credit linked financial assistance (capital subsidy) in the form of Grants-in-Aid to entrepreneurs for setting up of food processing industries, including for processing of fruits, under component schemes of Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY). PMKSY is demand driven and financial assistance is provided to the eligible applicants selected on the basis of Expression of Interest (EoI) issued from time to time. MoFPI has approved two projects in Darjeeling District of West Bengal for assistance under Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities Scheme of PMKSY. Related details are placed at Annexure. *****
