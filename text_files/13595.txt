                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2443






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Collection of Toll Tax




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy













                                                    M.K. Vishnu Prasad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the  amount  of  toll  tax  collected  by  the Government on National Highways (NHs) during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise and particularly in Andhra Pradesh  and Tamil Nadu;(b)  whether  the  Government  has  spent  any  funds from  the  said  revenues  on  the  development  of  NHs during  the  said  period  and  if  so,  the  details  thereof;  (c) whether some States including Tamil Nadu have raised objections  for  collection  of  toll  tax  from  NHs during  the  last  three  years;  and(d)  if  so,  the  details  thereof  and  the  consultations undertaken  with  various  stakeholders  in  this  regard along  with  the  outcome  thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The State-wise details of the user fee collected by the Government on National Highways during the last three years and the current year are at Annexure. (b) Yes, Sir. The details of funds released to National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) for the last three years and current year under toll remittances are as under -Amount in Rs. Crore2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 (upto 29.02.2020)7,500.00	8,462.14	9,570.13	10,600.00Expenditure incurred for the development of National Highways under Permanent Bridge Fee Fund by the States/UTs is as under-Amount in Rs. Crore2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 (upto 29.02.2020)72.29	21.42	4.09	2.61(c) &(d) Government of Tamil Nadu has requested Ministry to exempt all State Government vehicles on duty and State Transport undertaking town buses from payment of user fee. The buses of Kerala State Road Transport Corporation are not paying user fee on fee plazas of NHAI. NHAI has taken up the issue several times with the State Government and has also raised claim on State Government of Kerala to reimburse the loss in toll revenue. Further, four fee plazas under public funded projects and one fee plaza under Public Private Partnership (PPP) project are non-operational in different states due to public agitation.
