                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3817






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Performance of NHAI




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the extent to which the Government is satisfied with the performance and functioning of National Highways Authority of India (NHAI); (b) whether the Ministry of Road and Transport Highways has made a remark that it is not satisfied with the performance of the NHAI officials; (c) if so, the reasons therefor; (d) whether the Government has taken any action against the officials of NHAI who were held responsible for non-performance and other reasons; and (e)  if so, the details thereof for each of the last three years ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS   (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (e) National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has constructed 3979 kms against the target of 4500 kms during the financial year 2019-20. NHAI has constructed 3382 kms till 16.03.2021 against the target of 4000 kms during the current financial year (2020-21). Thus, NHAI has largely met the targets of highway construction.*****
