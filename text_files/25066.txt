                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

979






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Norms for Ease of Doing Business




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has fixed any norms for Ease of Doing Business in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the names of such States who have completed various such norms so far;(c) whether the Union Government has given permission for borrowing to the States who havecompleted such norms and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of employment opportunities in various such states increased after getting additional borrowing permission so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) Yes sir;(b), (c) & (d)   In order to strengthen resources in the hands of State Governments in the wake of unprecedented COVID-19 pandemic, Government addressed to all the States, laid out an additional borrowing of 0.25% for each reform of the Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) to the States for FY 2020-21 subject to implementation of following specific state level reforms: (i) Implementation of One Nation One Ration Card System; (ii) Ease of Doing Business reform; (iii) Urban Local body/ utility reforms and (iv) Power Sector reforms. Twenty States were allowed additional borrowing of ? 39,521 crore during 2020-21 for implementation of Ease of Doing Business (EODB) reform. EODB is a continuous process and its outcome would be visible in due course. State lists those who have completely implemented the reform on EODB are annexed as Annexure-A. Annexure-AS.
