                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1076






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Imports of Petroleum/Natural Gas




Rajendra Agrawal













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                








                                                    Harish Dwivedi
                                                








                                                    Rodmal Nagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the share of crude oil imports and domestic production in the total annual consumption inthe country during the last three years and the current year including the details of the importof petroleum products during the last three years, year-wise;(b) whether the Government is increasing production of domestic oil and gas and alsopromoting biofuel or any alternative fuel, if so, the details of concrete steps taken to reducedependency in imports along with the action of the Government for collaboration with anyother country in this regard; and(c) whether the Government has any tentative target year to bring the imports of petroleumand natural gas to zero and make the country Atma Nirbhar in the energy sector, if so, thedetails thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) The share of crude oil imports and domestic crude oil production in the total crude oilprocessing/annual consumption of crude in the country during the last three years and currentyear are given below:-(Quantity in Million Metric Tons (MMT)Particulars 2017-18 2018-19 2019-20 2020-21(Apr-Dec)(P)Quantity of crude oil imports (MMT)-A 220.4 226.5 227.0 143.2Quantity of domestic crude oilproduction (MMT)-B 35.7 34.2 32.2 23.0Total crude oil processing quantity/Consumption of crude (MMT) –C# 251.9 257.2 254.4 160.4Share of crude oil import as a percentageof total crude oil processing (%)-A/C% 87.5 88.1 89.2 89.3# Total crude processed during a financial year is different from the sum of crude imported and processedduring that year.Source: Petroleum Planning & Analysis Cell, (P): ProvisionalThe quantity of Petroleum, Oil & Lubricants (POL) products import during the last threeyears and the current year is given below:2017-18 2018-19 2019-20 2020-21(Apr-Dec)(P)Total POL products import (in MMT) 35.5 33.3 43.8 32.0Source: Oil companies & DGCI&S, (P): Provisional(b) & (c) : Government has taken multiple steps to enhance exploration & production of oiland gas to reduce imports and make the country AtmaNirbhar through various policies underProduction Sharing Contract (PSC) regime, Discovered Small Field Policy, HydrocarbonExploration and Licensing Policy, Setting up of National Data Repository, etc.. Governmenthas also provided functional freedom to National Oil Companies and wider private sectorparticipation by streamlining approval processes including electronic single windowmechanism.Government has launched National Biofuel Policy, 2018, to boost availability of biofuels incountry and use of alternative fuels like ethanol, bio-diesel and bio CNG through EthanolBlending, Bio-diesel blending and Sustainable Alternative Towards AffordableTransportation (SATAT) initiative respectively.*************
