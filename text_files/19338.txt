                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Introduction of NETC through FASTag




Dhanush M Kumar













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the  objectives  for  introduction  of  National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) through FASTag;(b) whether the Government is considering to extend the deadline for 100 percent implementation of FASTag system  of  toll  collection  and  if  so,  the  details  thereof;(c)  the  measures  taken  by  the  Government  to discourage  cash  transactions  at  toll  plazas;(d)  the  number  of  FASTags  issued  during  the  last two years in the State of Tamil Nadu and the challenges faced  by  the Government  to  implement FASTag;(e)  whether  the  Government  has  made  any comparative analysis of the collection of toll before and after implementation of FASTags;(f)  if  so,  the  details  thereof;  and(h)  the  steps  taken  by  the  Government  to  resolve the difficulties in the implementation of FASTag?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) through FASTag has been implemented on pan India basis to reduce waiting time, remove bottlenecks and ensure seamless movement of traffic at fee plazas by allowing electronic payment of user fees on National Highways.(b) There is no such proposal under consideration.  (c) To discourage cash transactions, Government, vide Gazette Notification no. GSR 523(E) dated 24.08.2020, notified that fee payable towards discounts as under sub-rules (2), (3) and (3A) of rule 9 of the National Highways Fee (Determination of Rates and Collection) Rules, 2008, shall be paid through pre-paid instruments, smart card or through FASTag or on board unit (transponder) or any other such device.(d) As on 12.01.2021, a total number of 18,64,115  FASTags have been issued in the state of Tamil Nadu as per Vehicle Registration Number.(e) &(f) After the implementation of FASTag in phased manner in December, 2019, waiting time in FASTag lanes has significantly reduced, which will further improve as the system gets more operationalised. With a monthly fee collection of Rs 2,088.26 Crore through FASTag in December 2020, FASTag penetration has reached 73.36%, as compared to 44.31% in December 2019. (h) Citizens are being informed about benefits of adopting FASTag through various platforms such as social media, print media, hoardings and advertisements etc. Multiple channels and Point of Sale (PoS) have been operating, including e-commerce platforms for easy availability of FASTag to customers.*****
