                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2152






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Implementation of (PM-SYM) Yojana




Dulal Chandra Goswami













                                                    Locket Chatterjee
                                                








                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the status of implementation of Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-SYM);(b)the details of the State-wise number of persons enrolled under the scheme at present; and(c)the details regarding management of funds including the details of institutions which have been entrusted this task;(d)whether the Government has done registration of all applications under PMSYM Yojana;(e)the details of the numbers of beneficiaries under the PM-SYM Yojana, district-wise for the State of West Bengal; and(f)the details of various steps taken by the Government to encourage this scheme furthermore in West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	As on 27.07.2021, over 45 lakhs workers (including bulk enrolment) have been enrolled under the Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana (PM-SYM). The State wise details of number of enrolment under the PM-SYM are given at Annexure A.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(c):		Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) is the fund manager of the scheme. The details of funds are given at Annexure B.(d) & (e):	The district wise details of enrolment for the State of West Bengal is at Annexure C. (f):		The Government is committed to extend the benefits of PM-SYM Scheme to the unorganised workers of all States/UTs including West Bengal. In order to ensure successful implementation of this scheme in West Bengal, a collaborative implementing mechanism has been adopted where the States including West Bengal are required to mobilise the eligible unorganised workers to their nearest Common Service Centres to enroll under the scheme. The State/UT Governments have also been advised to constitute State Level Monitoring Committee under Chief Secretary and District Level Implementation Committee under District Collector (DC)/ District Magistrate (DM)  to supervise and monitor the implementation of the scheme. The Information, Education and Communication (IEC) materials such as Pamphlets, Backdrops, creatives for standee and Video documentaries (in local language) have also been provided to State/UT Governments for sensitizing the Unorganised Workers.***                                               ******
