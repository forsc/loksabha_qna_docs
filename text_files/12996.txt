                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1860






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Senior Citizens Living Below Poverty Line




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †1860TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.03.2020SENIOR CITIZENS LIVING BELOW POVERTY LINE †1860. SHRIMATI SHARDABEN ANILBHAI PATEL: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has the data of senior citizens living below the poverty line and suffering from age related disabilities and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) whether the Government is focusing on any specific region with regard to Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana and if so, the details thereof; and (c) the number of camps organised in Gujarat during the year 2017?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a): No such data is maintained in this  Department.(b): There is no regional focus under the Scheme of Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana (RVY). The camps under the scheme are organized at any location in the country.(c): During the year 2017, one camp at Vadodara was organized in Gujarat under RVY Scheme.						*****
