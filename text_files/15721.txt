                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Targets under NULM




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the States are unable to achieve the actual and financial targets set under the National Urban Livelihood Mission (NULM) and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	the details of the targets fixed and the achievements made during the last two years under NULM, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE ) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                    (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) & (b)Under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana - National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) Annual Action Plans are prepared by the States/UTs. Further, the funds are released to the States/UTs to achieve the targets set under their respective plans. The progress of implementation is reviewed by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs and governments of States / Union Territories regularly.   A statement showing year-wise and State/UT-wise details of central funds released and utilisation thereof during financial year 2017-18 & 2018-19 is at Annexure-I. Further, the targets and achievements under various components under the Mission, State/Union Territory-wise for last two years are at Annexure-II.                                                 *****
