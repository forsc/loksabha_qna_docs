                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4822






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Vocational Training in Tribal Areas




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the types of schemes implemented for vocational training in tribal areas especially in Jharkhand during the last five years;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the number of tribes benefitted by such schemes in Jharkhand during the last five years; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d) :	Various developmental programmes are implemented by the Government for upliftment of people  including those from tribal communities including in the  State of Jharkhand. Schemes of Ministry of Tribal Affairs under which support is given for vocational trainings to tribals are Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS), Institutional Support for Development & Marketing of Tribal Products/Produce, Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution, Development for Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs) and grants provided under the scheme of Aid to Voluntary organizations working for the welfare of STs.Funds are given for all Scheduled Tribes and are generally not for any specific tribes.  Fund released under above mentioned scheme during current year (2019-20) is given below: (Rs. in Lakh)SCA to TSS	PVTG	Article 275(1)	Aid to Voluntary organizations1330.16	249.99	2661.80	7833.04    *****
