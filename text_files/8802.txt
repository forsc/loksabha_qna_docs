                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2516






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Shortage of Cold Storage




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a huge shortage of cold storages and warehouses in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has prepared any action plan to overcome this situation; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): As per the study undertaken on All India Cold Chain Infrastructure Capacity (Assessment of Status and Gap) in August, 2015 by National Centre  for Cold Chain Development (NCCD), an autonomous body under the Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare, cold storage facilities with capacity of 35 million tonnes are required to cater the need of farmers in the country.(c) & (d): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY), which comprises of component schemes namely (i) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, (ii) Mega Food Park, (iii) Creation of Backward & Forward Linkages, (iv) Creation / Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities (v) Agro processing Cluster and (vi) Operation Greens. Cold storages are established as a part of these schemes. The prospective entrepreneurs may apply against the Expression of Interest as and when floated by this Ministry.Cold storages are also established through the schemes of Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) namely (i) Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for development of Horticulture in the country and (ii) Capital Investment Subsidy for Construction/ Expansion / Modernization of Cold Storages and Storages for Horticulture Products of National Horticulture Board. A total number of 5427 cold storages with capacity of 226.69 Lakh MT have been established in the country as a part of the components of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) of Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) and through the schemes of Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) The state-wise distribution of financially assisted cold storages under the aforesaid schemes along with their respective capacities as on 31.10.2019 is placed at ANNEXURE.Department of Food and Public Distribution has also created a storage capacity of 758.80 Lakh Metric Tonnes for storage of food grains in the country.*****
