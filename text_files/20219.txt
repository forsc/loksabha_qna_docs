                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Applications under RTI




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of RTI applications filed, answered and rejected, year-wise since 2014; (b) 	the number of appeals/complaints made, answered and rejected by Central Information Commision (CIC) and State Information Commissions (SICs); (c) 	the year-wise number of unanswered and pending appeals made to CIC and SICs and the reasons therefor; (d) 	the number of sanctioned posts and vacancies separately for CIC and SICs; and (e) 	the reasons for not filling up the vacancies?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (e):    A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 135 BY SHRI UTTAM KUMAR REDDY NALAMADA REGARDING ‘APPLICATIONS UNDER RTI’(a):  The number of RTI applications filed, answered and rejected year-wise since 2014 pertaining to Central Public Authorities:(Data provided by CIC)Years	No. of RTI applications	Filed	Answered	Rejected2014-15	755247	NA	633512015-16	976679	NA	646662016-17	915749	NA	604282017-18	1233207	    950715*	632062018-19	1370129	1060617*	643442019-20	1374315	1086657	58634        *Provisional data.(b):  The number of appeals/ complaints made, answered and rejected by Central Information Commission (CIC) is as below:Year	Registered(Appeals / Complaints Made)	Disposed(Answered)	No. of Returned cases (Rejected) 2014-15	35396	20181	N/A2015-16	25960	28188	N/A2016-17	23811	32344	N/A2017-18	25815	29005	178702018-19	22736	17188	189882019-20	22243	16720	182542020-21Upto  (January,2021)	17143	13142	13208Contd.P.-2/--2-(c): The Year-wise details of unanswered and pending appeals made to Central Information Commission is as under:Year	No. of Unanswered and Pending casesUpto 2014	582015	32016	72017	162018	7002019	175412020	185132021upto Jan,2021	1732Total	38570It is the endeavour of the CIC to dispose off pending Second Appeals/Complaints at the earliest possible time. In 2020, the Covid-19 situation and lock-down have been reasons for lesser number of hearings, since the appellants and CPIOs were unable to attend the hearings.As far as State Information Commissions are concerned, the requisite data is maintained by the States.(d) & (e):  As per the provisions of Section 12(2) of the RTI act, 2005, the Central Information Commission shall consist of-1.	The Chief Information Commissioner and2.	Such number of Central Information Commissioners not exceeding ten, as may be deemed necessary.	Presently, the Chief Information Commissioner and seven Central Information Commissioners are in position in the Central Information Commission.The number of sanctioned posts in the Secretariat of Central Information Commission - 160.The number of vacant posts in the Secretariat of Central Information Commission - 08The filling up of posts is a continuous process.	As per the provisions of Section 15(2)of the RTI act, 2005, the State Information Commission shall consist of—		(I)	the State Chief Information Commissioner, and	(II)	such number of State Information Commissioners, not exceeding ten, as may be       deemed necessary.	Data in respect of the vacancies of Chief Information Commissioner and Information Commissioners in the State Information Commissions is maintained by the States.*****
