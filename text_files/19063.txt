                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Consumption Expenditure Survey




Amar Singh













                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry or the National Statistical Commission has received a request from the Chief Economic Advisor to make the results of the Consumption Expenditure Survey 2017-18 public; (b) if so, whether the Ministry has acceded to the request and made the results public; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING [RAO INDERJIT SINGH] (a):  Yes, Sir.(b) & (c): This Ministry has  carried  out  an  all-India  survey  on  Household  Consumption Expenditure  during  NSS  75th round  (July 2017- June 2018).  The Ministry has followed a rigorous procedure for vetting of data and reports which are produced through surveys.  The results  of  this survey  were  examined  and  it  was  observed  that  there  was  a significant  variation  in  the  levels  in  the  consumption  pattern  as well as in the direction of the change while comparing with other administrative data sources. The matter is being looked in to and finalization of the results of the Consumer Expenditure Survey 2017-18 is under consideration.                                                  *****
