                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

446






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Pending Wage under MGNREGS




Arun Sao













                                                    Aparajita Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the amount for wage payment is pending to the States under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS);(b) 	if so, the details thereof of Chhattisgarh, State-wise including in Bilaspur and Mungeli district;(c) 	whether an amount for payment of wage and material is pending in Bilaspur district of Chhatisgarh;(d) 	if so, the details thereof including the reasons for such pendency of wages; and(e) 	the time by which the pending amount is likely to be released?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)&(b)     Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is demand driven wage employment programme. Wage payment is made on regular basis.  Under DBT wage payment is made directly to the bank/post office account of the beneficiary by the central Government through the National Electronic Fund Management System after receiving the Fund transfer order (FTO) from State Government through PFMS.  The wage payment for state of Chhattisgarh is being made through DBT, except 4 IAP districts wherein cash payment provision is made as an exception till October 2020.  There is no pendency of wage payment for the state of Chhattisgarh as on 11.09.2020. In case of some of the states, as on 11.9.2020, FTOs are pending and the detail is at Annexure.  The department aims at clearing the pendency on daily basis.(c)&(d)  Fund  release is a continuous process. Government of India releases material and admin funds to State Government and State Government releases the fund to its Districts accordingly. Government of India does not release material funds directly to the District.
