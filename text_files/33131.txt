                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

520






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Hostels for Working Women




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of working women hostels established in the country, State-wise including district-wise in Rajasthan;(b) 	the total amount of central funds disbursed to the hostels since 2014, State-wise, year-wise, and district wise for Rajasthan;(c) 	the total capacity of such hostels along with the number of residents and vacancies in the hostels as of January 2022, State-wise and district wise for Rajasthan;(d) 	whether the Government plans to establish more such hostels, provide incentives for creation of such hostels and if so, the details thereof state-wise including Rajasthan; and(e) 	the number of such hostels which were operational during the pandemic induced lockdown and the number of those provided with IT capacity to support work from home or hybrid employment models since the COVID-19 pandemic struck in March 2020? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPEMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) 	There are 500 Working Women Hostels functional in the country. State-wise list of functional hostels is placed at Annexure I.  District-wise details of hostels functional in Rajasthan are placed at Annexure II. (b) 	State-wise, year-wise total amount of Central funds disbursed to the hostels since 2014 is placed at Annexure III. No new proposal for construction/rent of Working Women Hostel has been received from the State of Rajasthan since 2014-15 till date. (c) 	State-wise total capacity of such hostels along with number of residents and vacancies in the functional hostels is placed at Annexure I. District-wise such details for the State of Rajasthan is at Annexure II.   (d) 	Working Women Hostels is a demand driven scheme and sanction for new hostels is given on receipt of proposals from the State Government/UT Administration as per Scheme Guidelines. Provision has been made, inter-alia, for single women and students undergoing training or pursuing higher education to avail Working Women Hostel facility in the revised scheme under the new Mission Shakti. (e) 	Government has not received any information on the closure of Working Women Hostels during the Covid-19 pandemic induced lockdown.
