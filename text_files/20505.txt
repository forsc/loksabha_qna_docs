                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1898






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Construction of Medical College in Mohanlalganj (UP)




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the process for selection of agency for construction of medical college approved under centrally sponsored scheme in Mohanlalganj Parliamentary Constituency of Uttar Pradesh is under consideration of the Government;(b) if so, the time by which action is likely to be taken by the Government in this regard;(c) whether the Government proposes to issue any directions for setting up of the said medical college; and(d) if so, the time by which it is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):  The Ministry of Health & Family Welfare administers a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for “Establishment of new medical colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals” for establishment of medical college by upgrading the district hospitals in districts which do not have either a government or a private medical college.Under three Phases of the Scheme, establishment of 27 new medical colleges have been approved in Uttar Pradesh, as proposed by the State. However, no proposal is received from the State Government of Uttar Pradesh for establishment of new medical college in Mohanlalganj Parliamentary Constituency under the said Scheme.
