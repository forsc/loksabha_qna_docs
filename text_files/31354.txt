                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Encouraging Cooperatives




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been an effort to identify and further encourage cooperatives who have made difference in the lives of the common man:(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, whether there will be an effort in this direction, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ?????? 	(SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) to (c): The Country has a rich cooperative heritage and a robust cooperative sector which has been greatly contributing to the national economy as well as boosting the productivity in various sectors, especially agricultural sector. The percentage share of cooperatives in national economy in certain key sectors has been as follows:Fertilizer production: 28.80%Sugar production: 30.60%Liquid Milk Marketing: 84.17%Wheat Procurement: 13.3%Paddy Procurement: 20.4%(Source: Indian Cooperative Movement – A Statistical Profile, 2018 – NCUI) Government has further created a new Ministry of Cooperation with a view to provide a separate administrative, legal and policy framework for strengthening the cooperative movement in country. Its aim is to deepen co-operatives as a true people-based movement reaching up to the grassroots and develop a cooperative based economy model where each member works with a spirit of responsibility. ******
