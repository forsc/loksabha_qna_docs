                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2621






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Launch of Smart City Projects




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched Smart City Projects and if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of the works undertaken in the State of Gujarat under Smart City Projects; and(c)	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and expenditure incurred for the above projects till date?






ANSWER




                                                                              THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                    MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (c): Government of India launched Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015.  100 Smart Cities have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018. As per SCM Guidelines, Central Government will provide financial support to the extent of ? 48,000 crore over five years i.e., on an average ? 100 crore per city per year.  An equal amount on a matching basis will be contributed by the State Government/Urban Local Body (ULB).  As on 4 March 2022, 100 Smart Cities have tendered out 6,928 projects worth ? 1,91,238 crore; work orders have been issued in 6,282 projects worth ? 1,65,503 crore; 3,576 projects worth ? 59,958 crore have been completed.  In the State of Gujarat, 6 cities i.e. Ahmedabad, Dahod, Gandhinagar, Rajkot, Surat & Vadodara have been selected for development as Smart Cities. The status of the projects of Smart Cities in Gujarat is given below:                                                                      (Amount in ? crore)DPR Stage	Tender Stage	Work Order Stage	Work Completed	TotalNo. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount3	108.00	16	1,970.99	113	8,849.41	212	5,688.17	344	16,616.57(Source: SCM Geospatial Management Information System (GMIS) as on 4 March 2022)As on 4 March 2022, Government of India has released ? 29,213.60 crore for 100 Smart Cities, out of which ? 25,177.65 crore (86%) has been utilized. For Smart Cities in Gujarat, Government of India has released ? 2,115.00 crore, out of which ? 1,887.86 crore (89%) has been utilized.                                              *****
