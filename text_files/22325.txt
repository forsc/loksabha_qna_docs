                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3652






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Construction of Nuclear Power plant in Odisha




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plans for construction of a nuclear power plant in the State of Odisha?(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any funds have been earmarked by the Government in this regard; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (Dr. JITENDRA SINGH):(a)	There is no proposal under consideration of the Government to set up a nuclear   power plant in the state of Odisha.(b)to(d)  Do not arise in view of (a) above.******
