                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3201






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Multi Speciality Hospital in Bhagalpur




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the status of initiation of the work of multi specialty hospital proposed under centrally sponsored scheme in Bhagalpur, Bihar; and(b) the time by which the work is likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(Dr. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b) Government of India has sanctioned up-gradation of existing Government     Medical College (GMC) at Bhagalpur, Bihar under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY).  This includes setting up of a 202 bedded Super Specialty Block (SSB) with 7 Super Speciality departments, viz. Nephrology, Neurology, Neurosurgery, Urology,   Cardiology, CTVS and Trauma & Emergency medicine, with 9 Major OTs and 42 ICU beds. Work on the project has already started and progress of construction is 75% as on 31-7-2021.*********
