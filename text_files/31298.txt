                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






MSMEs in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) functioning in West Bengal as on date; (b)	the data on the number of MSMEs in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts;(c)	the details of loans sanctioned under the MUDRA Yojana in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts since the inception of the scheme; and (d)	the details of loans disbursed to the MSME sector under Prime Minister Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Scheme and the number of MSMEs benefitted therefrom in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) to (d): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. 280  FOR ANSWER ON 16.12.2021(a)& (b):	As per Udyam Registration Portal, the total number of classified micro, small and medium enterprises registered in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts and in the state of West Bengal as on 12.12.2021 is given below:<pre>District/State*	Micro	Small	Medium	TotalDARJEELING	5,258	625	54	5,937KALIMPONG	354	12	0	366WEST BENGAL	1,53,190	13,430	1,604	168,224  </pre>  *From 01.07.2020 to 12.12.2021(c):	The details of loan sanctioned under Mudra Yojana in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts are given below:<pre>District	Number of Accounts Sanctioned	Sanctioned Amount(Rs in Crore)DARJEELING*	8,57,840	4,220.90KALIMPONG*	8,377	55.58*Mudra Portal (District wise data is available since 01.04.2016 till 26.11.2021)</pre>(d):	The details of loan disbursed in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts under Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) under Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Scheme is given below:<pre>District	Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme	Number of Disbursement	Disbursement Amount(Rs in Crore)DARJEELING	67,564	378.04KALIMPONG	1,788	5.38</pre>Under Credit Guarantee Scheme for Subordinate Debt (CGSSD), the number of guarantees approved and amount of guarantees approved in West Bengal were 25 and Rs.2.06 crore respectively as on 03.12.2021.
