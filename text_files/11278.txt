                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

294






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Death due to Cold Wave




Parvatagouda Chandanagouda Gaddigoudar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of persons who died due to cold wave during 2019, State-wise;(b) the details of compensation paid to the kith and kin of those who died due to cold wave, State-wise; and(c) the details of assistance provided by the Government to the States for construction of cold shelters?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	The primary responsibility to take necessary measures to prevent deaths from cold wave in the  country  including construction of cold shelters rests  with the concerned State Government.  The data on deaths due to cold wave is not being maintained centrally by the Ministry of Home Affairs. However, only four States namely Himachal Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand and Punjab have reported deaths (Himachal Pradesh-03, Uttar Pradesh-03, Jharkhand- 01 and Punjab- 21) due to cold wave during the year 2019.The damages caused due to cold waves & frost stands included as one of the eligible natural calamity for providing assistance by State Governments from State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF)/National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) as per the norms. A copy of items and norms is available at https://www.ndmindia.nic.in/images/pdf/SDRF-NDRFNorms-dated8april2015.pdf******
