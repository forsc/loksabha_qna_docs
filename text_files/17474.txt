                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Inclusion of Bidi under Demerit Goods




Hemant Patil













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal to include bidi under the list of demerit goods; (b) if not, the reasons for not including bidi under the list of demerit goods, when it is equally harmful as other tobacco products; (c) the total amount of tax collected from all types of tobacco products in the last financial year; (d) the steps taken by the Government to impose additional cess on tobacco products to decrease their consumption; and (e) the details of the GST due to various states of the country particularly Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) and (b): Bidi attracts GST at highest rate of 28% just as other tobacco products. At present it does not attract compensation cess. (c):  the total amount of tax/duty collected from suppliers/manufacturers of tobacco products in the last financial year is as under:(in Rs Crore)Levy	2019-20GST	53540Excise duty including NCCD	1610Source: GSTN, DG Systems (d): NCCD on tobacco products was increased in Budget 2020-21.(e): GST including Compensation Cess paid by suppliers engaged in supply of tobacco products in Maharashtra is estimated to be Rs 5035 Crores in 2019-20.
