                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4416






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Child Pornography Cases




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of registered cases of online child pornography and child sexual exploitation content under the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights during the last three years, year-wise;(b)  	the  recommendations  made  by  National Commission  for  Protection  of  Child  Rights  so  far  to tackle child pornography; and (c) 	whether any report has been prepared by National Commission for Protection of Child Rights regarding this and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c) The National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR) has received 56 complaints/cases of online child sexual exploitation content during the last three years and the current year (2019-20). The State / UT-wise details is at Annexure-I. Besides, the Commission also inquired to check availability of child pornography material and found 31 such websites containing child pornography material.
