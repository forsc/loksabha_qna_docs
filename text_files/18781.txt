                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

160






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Road Construction in Naxal Affected Areas




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the blocks in naxal affected areas of Giridih and Hazaribagh districts of Jharkhand State where roads have been constructed;(b) if not, whether any proposal for road construction is under consideration of the Government;(c) if so, the details therof; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a to d): Names of the blocks of Giridih and Hazaribagh districts of Jharkhand, where roads have been constructed under the schemes Road Requirement Plan-I (RRP-I), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) and Road Connectivity Project in LWE affected Areas (RCPLWEA) are as under:Name of Block	Name of Districts Bagodar, Bengabad, Birni, Deori, Dumri, Gandy, Gawan, Giridih, GiridihSadar, Jamua, Pirtand, Raj Dhanbar and Tisri.	GiridihBarhi, Barkagaon, Barkattha, Bisnugarh, Chauparan, Churchu, HazaribaghSadar, Ichak, Karedari, Katkamdag, Katkamsandi and Padma.	HazaribaghDetails of the roads constructed in Giridih and Hazaribagh districts under RRP-I, PMGSY and RCPLWEA are placed at Annexure-I.
