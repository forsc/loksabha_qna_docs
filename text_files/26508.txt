                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Skill Development Schemes in Backward District




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has set any target to provide employment and self-employment especially in States having underdeveloped and backward districts under various skill development schemes;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise, especially Santhal Pargana of Jharkhand;(c)	the number of unemployed youth given employment during each of the last three years and the current year under various skill development schemes, scheme-wise, State/UT-wise; and(d)	the number of skill development schemes in operation andthe sector-wise number of Skill Development Centres, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) &(b) As per the National Policy for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, 2015 India has a target to train 402.87 million by 2022. This overall targets includes 104.62 million new entrants to existing workforce in the country who need to be skilled to meet industry requirement. In addition 298.25 million of existing workforce need to be reskilled/upskilled. This target is an estimation based on NSSO and CENSUS data, as per the methodology mentioned in the ‘National Policy for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship 2015’. NSS 66th and NSS 68th round data were used to calculate the existing workforce in Indian economy and workforce with formal vocation training within it. Calculations were made for all the age groups in the workforce. Skilling is demand driven, no State-wise break-up of the target is available. Further, Government has identified 112 Aspirational Districts where targeted development initiatives are being implemented to provide opportunities for people in these districts that have trailed in development journey of the country.
