                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Waste Management Accelerator




Kotagiri Sridhar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched a Waste Management Accelerator forAspiring Women Entrepreneurs (WAWE Summit 2019) under the AICTErecently.(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the benefits for the Women entrepreneurs under this programme initiated by theAICTE?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (b): Yes Sir. The Waste Management Accelerator for Aspiring WomenEntrepreneur (WAWE) Summit 2019-20 has been launched as a joint programmebetween All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and International Instituteof Waste Management (IIWM) by the Hon’ble Minister for Human ResourceDevelopment on 21st September, 2019 at New Delhi.(c): The WAWE accelerator offers learning/internship opportunities along withtechnical guidance to women entrepreneurs in Green Technologies and CommunityWaste Management at reputed waste management companies. This programme alsoprovides information to women entrepreneurs on various funding avenues.*****
