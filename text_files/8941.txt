                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2475






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






MIS Portal under AIC




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched a Management Information System  (MIS) portal under Accessible India Campaign recently for Divyangjans and if so the details thereof;   (b) the details of the aims and objectives of launching this portal along with its benefits;(c) whether it will be used as a single platform for monitoring the progress being made against each target  of Accessible India Campaign (AIC);(d) ) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government has created awareness to popularise this portal and if so, the details thereof; (f)  whether the Divyangjans from rural and remote areas are getting benefitted from this portal and if so, the details thereof; and (g) the measures taken by the government to eliminate obstacles and barriers in indoor/outdoors facilities? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) to (b): Yes Sir. A Management Information System portal has been launched on 18.09.2019.   Stage wise information along with photographs is uploaded on this Management Information System portal by States/UTs Government and Ministries/Departments of Central Government. The benefit of this portal is online monitoring of work implemented against the targets of accessibility in built up environment, transport system and Information & Communication Technology under the Accessible India Campaign. (c) to (d): No Sir. Apart from Management Information System portal, the progress of targets under Accessible India Campaign is monitored in PRAGATI meetings, Central Advisory Board (CAB) of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment    and Committee of Secretaries (CoS) apart from video-conferences, emails and letters to States/UTs and Ministries/Departments of Central Government. (e): Yes, Sir. All States/UTs and Ministries/ Departments of Central Government have been informed about this portal.   Training programmes of nominated nodal officers of States/UTs and Ministries/Departments of Central Government have been conducted to create awareness, publicity and uploading of stage wise accessibility details on this Management Information System portal.(f):   The accessibility features provided under Accessible India Campaign   are for benefits of all, including divyangjan from rural and remote areas. (g):  With the vision of creating barrier free environment in built-up environment, transport system and Information & Communication Technology, Government has launched the Accessible India Campaign to actualise the accessibility provisions notified under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (RPwD) Act, 2016 for independent and safe living of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs).  Apart from identified targets under Accessible India Campaign, funds are also released to create barrier free indoor/outdoor facilities under the Scheme for Implementation of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act (SIPDA).  *****
