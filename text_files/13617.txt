                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Housing for All in Odisha




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has a scheme called “Housing for All” for the State of Odisha;(b)	if so, the number of houses constructed in the State of Odisha during the last two years and the current year;(c)	the details of beneficiaries in Koraput/Rayagada districts in Odisha during the last year and the current year;(d)	whether the State Government of Odisha has notmade any budgetary provision for Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana since 2017-18; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the total funds received by the State Government of Odisha from the Union Government for PMAY since 2015?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)a)	Yes; Union Government through its schemes of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] and Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin) [PMAY(G)] under ‘Housing for All’ Mission aims to provide all weather pucca houses with basic amenities to the eligible beneficiaries in urban and rural areas of the country including the State of Odisha. (b) Out of 1,54,021 houses sanctioned under PMAY (U) for the State of Odisha, 62,453 houses have been constructed during last two years and current year. Under PMAY(G), 7,44,636 houses have been constructed in the State during the said period. (c) Number of houses sanctioned in Koraput and Rayagada Districts of Odisha during the last year and the current year are given as under:District name	PMAY(U)	PMAY(G)Koraput	4117	16683Rayagada	1046	9503(d) & (e) As reported by the State Government of Odisha, a budgetary provision of Rs. 1,300.00 crore has been made by the State since 2017-18 for implementation of PMAY(U). The State Government has released Rs. 3,706.14 crore of State share under PMAY (G) during the year 2017-18 and 2018-19. Union Government has released central assistance of Rs. 1,073.38 crore and Rs. 11,089.06 crore under PMAY (U) and PMAY (G) respectively to the State Government of Odisha since 2015.                                           *****
