                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2093






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Vacancies in Kendriya Vidyalayas




Sunil Dattatray Tatkare













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the shortage of teachers in Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) has adverselyaffected students of class X to XII in both the shifts and if so, the total number ofposts of teachers lying vacant in KVs in the country till date, State/UT-wise alongwith the measures taken/being taken by the Government to fill up the same;(b) whether the e-classrooms established by the KVs with a view to provide qualityeducation is not working as teachers are not using it or most of them are notfunctional; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the strict action takento ensure the use of e-classrooms by teachers?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that as on 15.11.2019, thereare 5949 teaching posts lying vacant against 48236 sanctioned posts in differentKendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in the country. State/UT-wise details are annexed. Filling upof vacancies is a continuous process and action is taken from time to time as per theprovisions of the relevant recruitment rules for filling up of the posts. Teachers are alsoengaged on contractual basis for temporary duration by Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathanto ensure that the teaching-learning process is not hampered.(b) & (c) No, Sir. The e-classrooms established in Kendriya Vidyalayas with a view toprovide quality education are being used by teachers. The e-content developed by theteachers and available from open resources is being used as per need of subject andtopic to enrich the quality of teaching learning process.*****
