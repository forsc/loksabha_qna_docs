                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

41






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Defence Establishments




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether his Ministry has issued any circular to all the three Forces in November, 2016 regarding redevelopment of old houses and buildings located in the vicinity of Defence establishments;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the said circular of the Ministry is being complied with by all the three Forces, particularly by the Navy in Mumbai and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has received complaints/representations from public representatives regarding noncompliance of the said circular and if so, the details thereof during the last three years; and(e) the action taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?
