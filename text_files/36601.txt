                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3621






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






National Highways in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total length of National Highways (NHs) constructed in the State of Odisha during each of the last three years and the current year; (b) the length of State Highways (SHs) converted to NHs during the said period in the State; (c) the length of NHs which was planned for construction in the districts of Dhenkanal and Angul and the work on which is yet to be completed; (d) the details of NH projects in the State on which the work is yet to be completed; and (e) the time by which the same is likely to be completed? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS (Shri NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)               The length of National Highways constructed in the State of Odisha during the each of the last three years and the current year is as under:- Year	Length of NH constructed in km2018-19	519.702019-20	426.502020-21	672.502021-22	457.90 (b)           The total length of state roads converted to NHs during the last 3 years and the current year is 330.80 Km.  (c)          The details of NHs passing through the districts of Dhenkanal and Angul and the works in progress therein are as under:- NH No.	Name of the Project	Length in km.NH  55	Cuttack-Angul section of NH-42 (new NH-55)	85.00NH 55	Angul- Sambalpur section of NH-42 (new NH-55) 	71.30NH 53	Talcher-Kamakhyanagar section of NH-200 (new NH-53)	41.20 (d) & (e)          The details of ongoing NH projects in the State of Odisha along with the scheduled/ likely date of completion is at Annexure.
