                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3347






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Beaches under Blue Flag Standards




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has initiated a programe to develop Indian beaches and marinas under the Blue Flag Standards;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the total number of beaches in the country currently having the Blue Flag 	Certification, beach wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c):The Ministry has embarked upon a programme for ‘Blue Flag’ Certification for select beaches in the country.   This Certification is accorded by an international agency, i.e., Foundation for Environment Education, Denmark, based on 33 stringent criteria in four major areas, i.e., (i) Environmental Education and Information, (ii) Bathing water quality, (iii) Environment Management and Conservation and (iv) Safety and services in the beaches. The ‘Blue Flag’ is an Eco-tourism model endeavouring to provide the tourists/beach goers a clean beach, hygienic bathing water, tourist friendly facilities/amenities and a healthy environment, besides endeavoring a sustainable development of the area.Through the Beach Environment & Aesthetics Management Services (BEAMS) initiative of the Ministry, infrastructure development works, facilities and services are progressing in the identified beaches, as per the criteria laid down for ‘Blue Flag’ certification.   So far, no beach has been accorded the ‘Blue Flag’ certification. ****
