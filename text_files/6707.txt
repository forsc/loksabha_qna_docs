                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

359






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Attacks of Maoists




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted any study regarding the attacks of Maoist in various parts of the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether attacks of Maoist were reported in Kerala during the last five years;(d) if so, the details of attacks reported along with the death or injury reported in Kerala due to the attacks of Maoist during the last three years;(e) whether any case has registered against Maoist during the last three years in Kerala;(f) if so, the details thereof;(g) whether encounters between Maoist and police were reported from Kerala during the last three years; and(h) if so, the number of Maoist and police personnel died and injured in the Maoist attacks and encounters during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	No Sir. The MHA has not conducted any study related to ‘Attacks of Maoists’ in various parts of country. -2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO. 359 FOR 19.11.2019(c) to (h):	The details of LWE related incidents reported in last five years in Kerala are as under:-Year	2014	2015	2016	2017	2018	2019 (upto 31.10.2019)Incidents	8	10	5	-	-	3Exchange of Fire	1	2	3	-	-	3LWEs Killed	-	-	2	-	-	5SF Killed/ Injured	-	-	-	-	-	-        ''Police’ and ‘Public order’ being State subjects, data regarding registration of cases arising out of LWE violence is maintained by the State Government. *****
