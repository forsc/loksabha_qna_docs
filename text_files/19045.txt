                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

414






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Employment of IT Graduates




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a) the number of recently employed graduates of Information Technology in Information Technology industry in the country; (b) whether this number has increased/decreased during the last three years;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) the number of women employees therein; (e) whether the employment opportunities are likely to increase for graduates of Information Technology in Information Technology industry in the coming years; and (f) if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (f):  As per National Association of Software and Services Companies (NASSCOM) estimates for FY2019-20, Information Technology (IT) industry employs over 43 lakh people in the country which includes IT graduates also. In the last 3 years, the net addition of employees has been consistently increasing from 39.7 lakhs in FY2017-18 to 43 lakhs in FY2019-2020 which is indicative of the growing employment opportunities in the sector. Further, out of the total employee base of Indian Information Technology industry, over 35% are women.   ******
