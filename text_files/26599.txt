                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2452






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Installation of PoS Machines




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government intends to install Point of Sale (PoS) machines at fair price shops; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the total number of fair price shops in the country, State-wise including Madhya Pradesh; (d) 	whether the Government plans to set-up more fair price shops and construct FCI godowns in the country during forth coming years; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b):  	Under the Public Distribution System (PDS) reforms, so far, the electronic Point of Sale (ePoS) devices are operational in about 4.98 Lakh (92.7%) Fair Price Shops (FPSs) out of nearly 5.38 Lakh FPSs across the country. (c): 	A statement showing State/UT wise number of FPSs, including Madhya Pradesh and total FPSs in the country is at Annexure. (d) & (e): 	The Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) under National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) is operated in joint responsibilities of the Central and State/UT Governments, wherein inter-alia the operational responsibilities of, licensing of FPSs, deciding the number of FPSs, continuous monitoring and oversight of all FPS operations, etc. rests with the concerned State/UT government. Yes, for construction of FCI Godowns.  Government has taken the steps to augment the storage capacity under the extended Central Sector Scheme (2017-2022) totaling to 1,05,890 MT and 59,560 MT capacity for North East States and other than North East States respectively.***** AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (c) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2452 FOR ANSER ON 03.08.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.Statement showing State/UT wise number of FPSs, including Madhya Pradesh, and total FPSs in the country:Sl.
