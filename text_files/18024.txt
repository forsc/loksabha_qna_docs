                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1762






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Train from Poriyahat




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any proposal for running train from Poriyahat to Godda district of Jharkhand; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the time by which the said train is likely to start its operation between these two stations?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) Poreyhat-Godda (16.50 Km) new line section is a part of Hansdiha-Godda (32 Km) new Broad Gauge rail line project sanctioned in the year 2011-2012. So far, Hansdiha-Poreyhat (15.5 Km) new line section has been completed and commissioned. Work has been taken up on the remaining Poreyhat-Godda new line section. As such, introduction of the train from Poreyhat to Godda is not feasible, at present.  *****
