                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Value of MTNL & BSNL




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the value of MTNL and BSNL as on date; (b) 	the manner in which it has been arrived at, the agency which has decided it and the basis of valuation; (c) 	the details of land and building, towers, equipment and tech facilities owned by BSNL and MTNL along with their present market value; (d) 	the present liability of BSNL and MTNL; (e) 	the necessity of disinvestment of MTNL and BSNL; and (f) 	whether the Government has shortlisted any buyer for BSNL and MTNL and if so, the criteria to sell, the details of the buyer and the cost thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b)	As per Audited Financial Statement, the assets of Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) and Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited (MTNL) as on 30.09.2021 are Rs. 1,33,952 Crore and Rs. 3,556 Crore respectively. The assets of BSNL and MTNL have been arrived on Historical cost basis. (c) 	As per financial statement, net block of property, plant and equipment in respect of BSNL and MTNL as on 31.03.2021 are Rs. 89,878 Crore and Rs. 3252 Crore respectively. Details in respect of BSNL and MTNL are at Annexure-I and Annexure-II. (d) 	Total liability of BSNL and MTNL as on 30.09.2021 is Rs. 85,721 crores and Rs. 30,159 crores respectively. (e) & (f)	There is no proposal under consideration for disinvestment of BSNL and MTNL.
