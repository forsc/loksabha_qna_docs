                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2381






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Integrated Waste Land Development Programme




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                       Integrated Waste Land Development Programme†2381.  SHRI BALUBHAU ALIAS SURESH NARAYAN DHANORKAR:Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:(a)	whether 5.53 crore hectares of land is lying unused despite implementation of Integrated Waste Land Development Programme, Drought Prone Area Programme and Desert Development Programme by the Government in the country, if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government has reduced the target set in this regard as the funds allocated for making the land arable during the Eleventh Five Year Plan has not been spent and if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                                                     ANSWER                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT                                  (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE)(a) & (b)	As per ‘Wastelands Atlas of India’ published by National Remote Sensing Centre, Hyderabad,  in year 2005, 2010 and 2019 the total wastelands in the country were estimated as below:Year of publication	Satellite data period	   Area of total Wastelands                                                       in crore hectares2005	                       2003	                    5.5272010	                       2008-09	                    5.660#2019	                       2015-16	                    5.576*           # Wastelands statistics are revised version*Total Wastelands in the above table is after including unsurveyed area of J & K State (erstwhile), which were not included in the earlier mapping. After excluding unsurveyed area of J & K, total wastelands are 4.645 crore ha for the year 2008-09 and 4.556 crore  ha for the year 2015-16.The Department of Land Resources has fully utilized the budget allocated during the Eleventh Plan. The details of budget allocated and utilized are as  below:			                        (Rs. in Crore)Year	     Revised Estimate	         Released/ Expenditure2007-08	        1114.54	                     1164.542008-09	        1545.00	                     1544.002009-10	        1819.80            	     1762.652010-11	        2458.00	                     2456.732011-12	        2314.20	                     2312.09During the Eleventh Five Year Plan, the target for sanctioning of projects under Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP) was 2.265 Crore hectare, against which, projects covering an area of 2.42 crore hectares were sanctioned. *****
