                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






NCERT Centre in Nellore




Adala Prabhakara Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the NCERT Centre at Nellore is likely to help teachers in States like AndhraPradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Kerala and Andaman and Nicobar Islands in acquiringtraining and honing their skills and if so, the details thereof; and(b) the hurdles being faced by the Government in starting this centre and if so, the stepstaken to overcome it?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) The proposed Regional Institute of Education (RIE) of the National Council ofEducational Research and Training (NCERT) at Nellore, Andhra Pradesh, will cater tothe needs of the States of Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu and the UnionTerritory of Puducherry.(b) Setting up of a new Regional Institute of Education (RIE) requires appraisal andapproval by the prescribed Authorities. In this connection, NCERT has recently awardedthe work of preparation of a Detailed Project Report (DPR) for the proposed RIE atNellore, Andhra Pradesh, to a professional agency.****
