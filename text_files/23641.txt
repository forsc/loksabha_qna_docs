                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4760






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Modernisation of Railway Stations in Maharashtra




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the zone-wise details of the funds allocated for the modernisation of railway stations located particularly in the backward and rural areas of the country during the last three years and the current year along with the status of Maharashtra in this regard; (b) the amount likely to be spent on each railway station along with the target fixed for the next financial year;(c) the target fixed for the modernisation of those railway stations where the work is in progress; and(d) the number of stations in Maharashtra which are targeted to be modernised?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
