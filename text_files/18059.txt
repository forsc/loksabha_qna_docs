                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1793






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Exploitation of Labourers Abroad




Shantanu Thakur













                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to bring a new law for        providing adequate security against the exploitation of Indian        Labourers abroad; and(b)	if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	Yes . (b)   The proposed New Emigration Bill seeks to establish a robust, transparent and comprehensive emigration management framework that facilitates easy, safe and orderly migration, harnesses our demographic dividend, empowers our migrants proceeding for overseas employment and strengthens their welfare and protection during all stages of migration. An inclusive, streamlined regulatory framework is also envisaged under the proposed Bill with participation of State governments, Union Territories and other stakeholders. ***
