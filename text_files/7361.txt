                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1028






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Homoeopathy Unit at CGHS Dispensary




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has received a large number of representations/ requests from various quarters including Members of Parliament, CGHS beneficiaries etc. for opening of a homoeopathy unit in Dispensary No. 73 situated at Sector 5, Gurgaon since its opening, till-date, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government is aware that no action has been taken in the matter so far and the said dispensary is still devoid of this facility despite many such representations/requests;(c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(d) the steps taken/to be taken to initiate the setting up of homoeopathy unit at the said dispensary to overcome the difficulties/hardships being faced by the CGHS beneficiaries/patients?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	Yes. Requests for opening of a homoeopathy unit at CGHS Wellness Centre No. 73 located at Sector-5, Gurgaon have been received from Members of Parliament (MP)/ ex-MPs forwarding therewith representation(s) from some association and CGHS beneficiaries.  One Homoeopathy Unit at the said Wellness Centre has been approved by the Government. Action has been initiated to make it functional.
