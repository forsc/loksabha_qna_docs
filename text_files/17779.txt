                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1508






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Funding for ATMA Scheme




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has allocated any funds to the State of Kerala under the Agriculture Technology Management Agency (ATMA) Scheme during the financial year 2019-20 and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the amount of funds released from the allocated amount so far to the State of Kerala; and(c)	the details of the NGOs involved in the State of Kerala under the ATMA scheme? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	Yes, the Government allocated Rs.11.39 crore (Central share) to the State of Kerala under Agriculture Technology Management Agency (ATMA) Scheme during the financial year 2019-20;(b):	The entire Central share of allocated funds i.e. Rs.11.39 crore was released to Government of Kerala during the financial year 2019-20;(c):	The State Government of Kerala has reported that no NGOs are involved in the State under the ATMA scheme. *******
