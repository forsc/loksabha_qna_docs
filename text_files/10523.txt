                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4095






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Announcement of Names of Airports




Dayanidhi Maran














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has issued any direction to all airlines regarding announcement of names of airports especially Chennai airport and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Indian Airlines like Indigo, Spice Jet, Go Air, Air Asia & Air Vistara are not announcing the names of Chennai K. Kamaraj Domestic Airport and Chennai Anna International Airport and instead just calling it Chennai Airport, if so, the details thereof;(c) the measures taken by the Ministry of Civil Aviation to ensure that all the Airlines both Indian & International to follow their guidelines while making their announcement in regards to Chennai K.Kamaraj Domestic Airport and Chennai Anna International Airport and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether any penalties has been levied by the Ministry on the erring airlines in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d) No such guidelines have been issued by the Ministry of Civil Aviation or the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) to the airlines regarding announcement of names of airports. Airlines have their own policy for announcement regarding names of airports and the content of the announcement varies from airline to airline. The name of the Chennai airport is ''Chennai International Airport'', however, as reported by various airlines, inflight announcements done by them at the time of landing at Chennai is as follows:S.No.   Airline Name       Announcement1	            Indigo	                  Chennai 2           Spice jet       Chennai International Airport3             Go Air        K. Kamaraj Domestic Airport4             Air Asia     Chennai International Airport5           Air Vistara    Chennai International Airport;6            Air India        K. Kamaraj domestic airport for;                                   domestic flights and Chennai Anna;                                    International Airport for  ;                                       international flights
