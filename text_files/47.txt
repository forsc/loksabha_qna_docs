                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Government Hospitals in Delhi




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of all hospitals run or funded by Central Government in Delhi;(b) the total functional beds in said Central Government Hospitals in Delhi as of June 2019, hospital-wise;(c) the details of doctor to patient ratio in all these hospitals in Delhi; and(d) the steps taken/being taken to improve the doctor-patient ratio?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	The details of hospitals run by Central Government in Delhi are as under: Vardhaman Mahavir Medical College (VMMC) and Safdarjung Hospital, Post Graduate Institute of Medical Research Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital and Lady Hardinge Medical College and Associated Hospitals. The details of hospitals/institutes funded by Central Government in Delhi are as under:      All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), National Institute of Tuberculosis and Respiratory Diseases (NITRD) and Vallabhbhai Patel Chest Institute (b):	Total functional beds in Central Government hospitals as of June 2019 are as under:Name of the Hospital/Institute	Functional BedsVMMC and Safdarjung Hospital	2298PGIMER Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital	1447Lady Hardinge Medical College and Associated Hospitals(i) Smt. Sucheta Kriplani Hospital(ii) Kalawati Saran Childrens Hospital	877375(c):	The doctor-patient ratio in these Central Government Hospitals are as under:Name of the Hospital	Doctor-Patient RatioVMMC and Safdarjung Hospital	 IPD  - 1:18OPD – 1:50PGIMER Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital	 IPD-   1:15OPD-   1:41Lady Hardinge Medical College and Associated Hospitals(i)	Smt. Sucheta Kriplani Hospital(ii)	Kalawati Saran Childrens Hospital	 IPD  – 1:8 OPD – 1:14 IPD    -  1:14 OPD  -  1:15  (d):	The Government has taken various steps to improve the doctor-patient ratio by creating more posts of doctors and filling up vacant posts in various departments of these hospitals in Delhi.
