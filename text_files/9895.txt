                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3351






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Higher Educational Institutions in Karnataka




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds sanctioned and released during 2017-18 for Higher Educational Institutions set up inKarnataka;(b) the allocations made during the last three years and the details of expenditure incurred so far,institution-wise;(c) the details of total funds required for each of the institutions and money sanctioned this yearand earlier years, year-wise and institution-wise; and(d) by when all the institutions would be moved to their permanent campuses and the status ofsetting up of permanent campuses of Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Dharwad,Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Raichur and IIT, Dharwad from the existingtemporary/transit campuses?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The fund sanctioned and fund released/ expenditure during the year 2017-18 for CentralHigher Educational Institutions set up in Karnataka are Rs. 827.94 Crores and Rs. 832.83 Croresrespectively.(b) and (c): The institute-wise details are Annexed.(d): The work for construction of permanent campus for IIT Dharwad will start in first weekof February, 2020. For IIIT Dharwad, construction work is going on. For IIIT Raichur,construction of permanent campus has not yet started.***
