                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4423






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Setting up of Ayurvedic Hospital




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has undertaken any steps to improve the existing infrastructure and human resources in Ayurvedic Hospitals and promote the establishment of Ayurvedic Hospitals in the country including Erragadda hospital in Medak District, Hyderabad, Telangana; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise, districtwise; (c) the steps taken by the Government to set up designated departments for Ayurveda in various All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), defence hospitals and about 100 ESIC hospitals and various border security force and other paramilitary hospitals as well as 12.5 lakh health and wellness centres to expand access to AYUSH services and promote AYUSH at the grassroot level; and (d) the details of the steps taken to open state-ofthe-art homoeopathy labs and promote AYUSH globally along with reducing cost of treatment for patients compared to other medical treatment particularly in tackling chronic diseases in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):      As public health is a State subject, improvement of existing infrastructure and human resources in Ayurvedic Hospitals and establishment of Ayurvedic Hospitals in the country including Erragadda hospital in Medak District, Hyderabad, Telangana comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision of financial assistance for upgradation of standalone Government AYUSH hospitals for undertaking construction, renovation of existing premises, furniture, fixtures, equipments, contingency, medicine and engagement of manpower and setting up of upto 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital. Under NAM, grant-in-aid is being provided to State/UT Governments as per proposals received through State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs).                        The number of AYUSH hospital for which financial assistance provided for upgradation including Erragadda hospital in Medak District, Hyderabad, Telangana and upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH hospitals approved under NAM by Central Government, State/UT-wise is furnished at Annexure-I.(c):     Central Government has signed Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Ministry of AYUSH and Ministry of Defence, seven Ayurveda units have been established at Palliative Care Centre, Base Hospital Delhi Cantt., Air Force Hospital, Hindon, Ghaziabad and five identified Ex-Serviceman Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS) Polyclinics in Delhi-NCR. ESIC has established Ayurvedic units in various Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) Hospitals across the country, which is furnished at Annexure-II. ESIC has also set up Panchakarma, Ksharsutra and Yoga departments under Ayurveda in ESIC hospitals across the country which are as follows:Designated departments	No. of ESIC hospital where designated departments arefunctionalPanchakarma	8Ksharsutra	4Yoga	17All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Raipur has set up Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy Department.            Further, Ministry of AYUSH has envisaged to establish a total 12500 AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) by upgradation of existing State Government AYUSH dispensaries and Sub Health Centres by the year 2023-24.(d):     Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH), an autonomous organization under Ministry of AYUSH has following Homoeopathy labs:1.	Pharmacological laboratory and Animal House at Dr DP Rastogi Central Research Institute for Homoeopathy, Noida, Uttar Pradesh.2.	Bio safety level (BSL)-2 Standard Virology laboratory at Dr Anjali Chatterjee Regional Research Institute for Homoeopathy, Kolkata, West Bengal.Further, under Central Sector Scheme for promotion of International Cooperation,                           (IC Scheme), the Ministry of AYUSH undertakes various measures to promote & propagate AYUSH systems of medicine globally and to create awareness about AYUSH systems of medicine.………………….
