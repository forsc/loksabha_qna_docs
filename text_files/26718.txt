                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Missing Children




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is an increase in missing of children aged between five years to twelve years in cities like Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Chennai and Kolkata in recent years;(b)	if so, the details in these cities during the last three years;(c)	the number of missing children who have been traced and returned to their families; and (d)	the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI AJAY KUMAR MISHRA)(a) to (d):	National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes statistical data on crimes in its publication “Crime in India”. The published reports are available till the year 2019. Specific information on missing and traced children for the category of age group between five years to twelve years is not maintained centrally. However, the details of missing and traced children for the category of five years and above and below fourteen years in Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai and Kolkata during the years 2017-2019 is provided at Annexure.*****
