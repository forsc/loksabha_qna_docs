                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4930






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Funds under Intensive Irrigation Development Programme




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the details of the proposals received by the Government from the State Government of Maharashtra for allocation of funds under Intensive Irrigation Development Programme for agriculture development scheme in the State during the last five years; (b) 	the number of proposals approved out of the proposals received and the number of proposals pending so far; (c) 	the time by which the pending proposals are likely to be approved and sanctioned by the Government; (d) 	the amount released to Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme being implemented as a sub-scheme of Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) from 2012-13 during the last five years; and (e) 	the number of beneficiaries under this scheme, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):	 The Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme (VIIDP) was implemented in the State of Maharashtra as a sub-scheme of Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) from 2012-13 to 2015-16.  Implementation of VIIDP as a sub-scheme  of RKVY was discontinued from 2016-17.   The year wise amount released and expenditure under  the programme from 2012-13 to 2015-16 is as under:                                                                  Rs. in croreS.N.	year	Release	Expenditure1	2012-13	300.0	300.02	2013-14	150.0	150.03	2014-15	150.0	150.04	2015-16	75.0	75.0	Total	675.0	675.0         The district-wise number of beneficiaries benefitted under the programme is Annexed.              Since 2015-16, Maharashtra is also receiving assistance under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)- Per Drop More Crop. An area of 4,34,202 ha has been covered under Micro Irrigation and Rs.1135.46 Crore released as Central Assistance.
