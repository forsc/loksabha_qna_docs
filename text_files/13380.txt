                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2129






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Clearing of Dues by Telecom Companies




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the telecom companies have cleared the dues to Department of Telecommunication as per Hon’ble Supreme Court order and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	if not, the balance amount due from each company; and (c) 	the details of the measures taken by the Government to clear the said dues?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	& (b) 	Some of the telecom companies have made the payments to Department of Telecommunication as per Hon’ble Supreme court order. The TSPs-wise payments & provisional balance dues are enclosed as Annexure-I.(c)	The Government has directed the Licensees to make the payments in accordance with the order of the Hon’ble Supreme Court dated 24.10.2019.
