                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






CSR Policy




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any study has been carried out to check the effectiveness and implementation of CSR policy;(b)	if so, the details and the findings thereof; and(c)	the details of steps taken by the Government during the last 5 years for making the scheme more effective and result-oriented?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS 	                         (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)???????????????????????????????????????????                             (?????????? ???? ?????)(a)&(b): No such study has been carried out by this Ministry.(c): The Government has constituted High Level Committee (HLC-2015)in2015 to suggest measures for improved monitoring of the implementation of CSR policies by the companies under section 135 of the Companies Act, 2013. Subsequently in the year 2018 another High Level Committee (HLC-2018) was constituted to review the CSR framework and make recommendations to develop a more robust and coherent CSR regulatory and policy framework, and underlying ecosystem. The HLC-2018 has submitted its report on 07th August, 2019 which is available on the Ministry’s website at www.mca.gov.in.******
