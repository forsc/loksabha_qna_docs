                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






DISHA Meetings in Tamil Nadu




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of DISHA Meetings conducted in Namakkal and Karur district of Tamil Nadu from 2019 to till date;(b) 	whether the recommendations given by DISHA committee to Namakkal District Administration are complied in a timely manner and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the number of recommendations given by the DISHA committee to Namakkal District Administration which are not complied with from 2019 along with the reasons for not complying the same;(d) 	whether DISHA committee can recommend disciplinary actions against the erring officials and if so, the details of devised mechanism for it; and(e) 	whether DISHA committee chairperson have the power of inspection as per the existing guidelines and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) 	The Namakkal and Karur districts of Tamil Nadu have conducted six and four meetings of District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (DISHA) respectively since 2019. (b) & (c) As per the information provided by the state government, most of the recommendations given by DISHA Committee to Namakkal District Administration have been complied in timely manner. The detailed status of compliance is at Annexure-I(d) 	As per DISHA Guidelines, the District level DISHA Committee may refer any matter, with respect to the complaints or alleged irregularities received regarding the implementation of the programmes, to the District Collector or suggest suitable action to be taken in accordance with rules which should be acted upon by the District Collector within 30 days.(e)  	According to DISHA Guidelines, the DISHA Committee has the powers as well as authority to summon and inspect any record with respect to the complaints or alleged irregularities received regarding the implementation of the programmes. However, the Chairperson of DISHA Committee is not exclusively vested with the power of inspection.******
