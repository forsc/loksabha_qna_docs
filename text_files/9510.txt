                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Integrated Child Development Scheme




Giridhari Yadav













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the details including the names of such districts in Bihar where Integrated Child Development Scheme (ICDS) has been implemented at present;(b) 	the number of women and children beneficiaries covered under this scheme, district-wise; and(c) 	the details of the funds allocated to Bihar under the said scheme last three years along with the funds utilized thereunder, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILDDEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	Anganwadi Services is a universalised Scheme under Umbrella ICDS and is being implemented across the country including all districts of Bihar.(b)	As on 30th September, 2019 out of total 1,04,090 operational Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) in the State of Bihar, 96,727 AWCs are reporting on ICDS-RRS portal. As per the data reported on ICDS-RRS portal, district-wise and category-wise number of beneficiaries covered under the scheme is given at Annexure-I.
