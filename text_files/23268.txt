                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4282






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






PMKVY 2.0 in Rajasthan




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Centers that have been established for the procurement of Skill Certifications, and Recognition of Prior Learning certificates (SC&RPLC) under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) and district wise number of Centers established State-wise including Rajasthan;(b)	whether the targets set for PMKVY 2.0 have been achieved and the status of implementation of PMKVY 3.0 targets;(c)	the number of people, and separately migrant workers, that have received SC&RPLC, for each State and each district in the State of Rajasthan;(d)	the State-wise and district wise details for Rajasthan, of the number of Special Projects instituted for providing skill development services to migrant workers under the PMKVY;(e)	the number of migrant workers who have been upskilled at such special projects; and(f)	the number of skilled youth that have received employment skilling under the PMKVY, State-wise and district-wise for the State of Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (f)       Under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2.0, there is no provision of procurement of certificate from the Training Centres (TCs). Only the training is given at the TCs, and based on the assessment of trained candidates by separate assessment agencies, certificates are issued by the concerned Sector Skill Councils (SSCs) to the passed candidates. The State-wise number of TCs empanelled in the country under Short Term Training (STT) is given at column (a) of Annexure I. The District-wise number of TCs under STT in State of Rajasthan is given at column (a) of Annexure II. There is no provision of having TCs under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) component of PMKVY 2.0.             The State/UT-wise number of candidates placed under STT component of PMKVY 2.0 is given at column (a) of Annexure I. Similarly, district-wise number of candidates placed in State of Rajasthan under STT component of PMKVY 2.0 is given at column (a) of Annexure II. (b)       Under PMKVY 2.0, as on 19.01.2021, around 1.07 crore candidates have been trained / oriented across the country against a target of 1 crore candidates. Further, under PMKVY 3.0, as on 26.02.2021, 75,950 candidates have been enrolled across the country. (c) to (e)          The State/UT-wise number of candidates certified under PMKVY 2.0 is given at Annexure I. Similarly, district-wise number of candidates certified in State of Rajasthan under PMKVY 2.0 is given at Annexure II.             In order to support Garib Kalyan Rozgar Abhiyan (GKRA), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is imparting skill development training to migrants returned due to pandemic under the central component of PMKVY 2.0 in 116 districts of 6 States, namely, Assam, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, Odisha, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh. The skill training of migrant workers under GKRA is underway. The State-wise details of candidates enrolled for training and upskilling under central component of PMKVY 2.0 for the GKRA programme is given at Table 1 of Annexure III and district-wise details for State of Rajasthan is give at Table 2 of Annexure III.
