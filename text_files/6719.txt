                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

411






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Law and Order Situation




Bhola Singh













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether law and order situation in the country including in West Bengal and Karnataka is deteriorating;(b) if so, the details thereof and the total number of incidents of crime that came to light during the previous year and the current year along with the details of the action taken by the Government against the guilty persons, State-wise including West Bengal;(c) whether the Government has sought any report from the State Government of West Bengal on the cases of brutal murders that took place recently in the State;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the comprehensive measures taken by the Government to improve the law and order situation in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	The law and order situation in the country is generally under control.  ‘Public Order’ and ‘Police’ are State subjects under the seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India.  The  responsibility  of  maintaining  law -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.411 FOR 19.11.2019and order and protection of life and property of the citizens rests primarily with the respective State Governments. The State Governments are competent to deal with such matters under the extant provisions of laws.The data on crime in India is maintained by National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB).  The latest data on cases of murder, State-wise, including West Bengal, are enclosed at Annexure.Government of India continuously monitors the internal security situation in the country, issues appropriate alerts and advisories, shares intelligence inputs, extends manpower and financial support including by deployment of CAPFS, and provides guidance and expertise to the State Governments for maintenance of security, peace and public tranquility. ******
