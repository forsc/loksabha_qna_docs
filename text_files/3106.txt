                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2951






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Unit-Run Canteen




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Unit-Run Defence Canteens in the country, location-wise;(b) the norms/criteria laid down for establishment of a new canteen in a specific area; (c) whether the Government plans to set up a new canteen in Rajsamand district of Rajasthan; and (d)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  There are over 3700 Unit Run Canteens (URCs) spread all across the country.  The location of URCs cannot be divulged  since it will give away operational locations of units and sensitive establishments.(b)  Posted strength of minimum 100 personnel or minimum dependency of 5000 Ex-servicemen and financial and administrative viability.(c) & (d):  No, Sir.*******
