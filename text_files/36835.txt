                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4015






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Funds from Religious Institutions




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether funds are received by the Government from various religious institutions;(b) if so, the names of such religious institutions/spots wherefrom these funds have been received during the last three years;(c) the amount of these funds received during the said period, State/UT-wise; and(d) the details of the utilisation of funds received from these religious institutions?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (d): Religious Institutions pay tax and non tax revenues to the Government as per the existing legal framework. However, the information about the total amount paid by them to Government under various heads is not maintained separately.*****
