                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Education Projects




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of education projects being run in the country, State-wise including Bihar;(b)	the details of funds allocated/released for these projects during the last three years and the current year, State and year-wise; (c)	the total amount of funds spent on higher education projects during the aforesaid period, Statewise; and(d)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government for effective implementation of such education projects in the country?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a):  Details of education projects/schemes of MHRD are indicated at Annexure-I.(b):	State and year-wise, the details of funds allocated/released for the projects/schemes during the last three year and current year of Ministry of HRD are indicated at Annexure-II.(c):  Total amount spent on Higher Education Projects during the last three years and the current year:<pre>Rs. in CroreYear	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20(as on date)	TotalReleases	28933.38	33559.43	31812.79	34433.97	128739.57</pre>Details of State-wise funds releases on major projects/schemes of Higher Education are given at Annexure-II.(d):  The steps taken/being taken by the government for effective implementation of projects/schemes are indicated at Annexure-III.*****
