                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Maintenance of National Highways




A. Chellakumar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the maintenance of certain stretches of National Highways (NHs) has been entrusted to the concerned State Governments and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether certain State Governments are not ready to bear the amount for the maintenance of these NHs and if so, the details thereof; and (c) the amount spent and paid by the Government of Tamil Nadu for the repair of NH-66 during the last two years and the current year, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) About 57,954 km length of National Highways (NHs) are entrusted with various State Governments/ Union Territories (UTs). (b) The Ministry is responsible for Development and Maintenance & Repair (M&R) of NHs. The Ministry allocates funds for various executing agencies, including State Governments/ UTs, for development and maintenance of NHs.The inter-se priority for carrying out M&R of NHs generally depends upon nature and extent of damages, overall condition of NH stretches, traffic density, minimum works required to be undertaken to ensure traffic worthiness of NHs, the NH stretches under Defects Liability Period (DLP) obligation or stretches under Build, Operate and Transfer (BOT)/ Hybrid Annuity Mode (HAM)/ Operate, Maintenance and Transfer (OMT) Concessions, ongoing works on NHs, status of action taken up for new developmental works, availability of funds, etc. (c) 	The Ministry does not allocate NHs-wise funds for M&R of NHs. Year-wise funds allocated for M&R of NHs entrusted with the State Government of Tamil Nadu and expenditure incurred during last two years and current year are as follows: - Amount Rs. in croreSr. No.	Financial Year	Allocation	Expenditure1	2017-18	74.39	72.992	2018-19	   52.77	42.64 (Provisional)3	2019-20	21.92	2.20 (upto 30.06.2019)Apart from these, the details of funds spent by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) for M&R of NH-66 in the State of Tamil Nadu during last two years and current year are as follows: -                  Amount Rs. in croreSr. No.	Financial Year	Expenditure1	2017-18	5.632	2018-19	6.98 (Provisional)3	2019-20	1.13(upto 30.06.2019)*****
