                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3044






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Amrit Dhara Scheme




Anurag Sharma













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Santosh Kumar
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the aims and objectives of Amrit Dhara scheme along with the support provided by the Government to achieve these objectives; (b) 	whether the Government has fixed any time limit to complete the said scheme and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) 	the specific locations in various States where this scheme will be made operational especially in Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar and North-Eastern region?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	The Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation, Ministry of Jal Shakti, does not have any scheme called Amrit Dhara.(b) & (c) Do not arise.
