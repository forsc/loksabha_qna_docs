                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

492






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Medical facilities under NRHM




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether adequate medical facilitates are being made available in rural areas in the State of Chhattisgarh, if so, the details thereof;b)	the extent to which National rural Health Mission has achieved its objectives in the country particularly in Chhattisgarh; andc)	whether the Government proposes to establish super specialty healthcare centres in the rural areas and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)a) to (c) Under National Health Mission (NHM), technical and financial support is provided to States/UTs for strengthening their healthcare systems, upto District Hospital level, based on the proposals submitted by them in their Program Implementation Plans (PIPs), subject to availability of resources. However, as per the information received from the State Government of Chhattisgarh, there are two Multi Super Specialty Hospitals which are approved in Bilaspur and Jagdalpur under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) scheme.  Adequate medical facilitates are available in rural areas in the State and the details thereof are as below as per information received from State Government of Chhattisgarh:Sr. No.	Types of Health Facility	Year 2005	Till date 20221	Medical College	2	132	District Hospital	16	243	Community Health Center	116	1704	Primary Health Center	517	7925	Sub Health Center	4692	52046	Special Newborn Care Unit (SNCU)	0	237	Newborn Stabilization Unit  (NBSU)	0	1788	Newborn Care Corner (NBCC)	0	17049	Nutrition Rehabilitation Center (NRC)	0	8010	Dialysis Unit	0	811	ICU Unit	0	42	NHM is successfully achieving the following objective in the country including Chhattisgarh to provide universal access to equitable, affordable & quality health care services to the people including rural areas and same is summarized as below: Sr. No.	Health Indicator	Chhattisgarh		Year 2005	Till date 2022	Source1	Maternal Mortality Ratio (MMR)	335	159	SRS 2016-182	Infant mortality rate (IMR)	63	40	SRS 20193	Institution Delivery 	18.0	85.7	NFHS-54	Birth Rate	27.2	22.2	SRS 20195	Death Rate	8.1	7.3	SRS 20196	Total fertility rate (TFR)	3.4	1.8	NFHS-57	Prevelance Rate (NLEP)	3.6	1.55	State Data8	TB Notification Rate	49%	58.7%	Nikshay Portal9	TB Success Rate	83%	85%	Nikshay Portal10	Malaria - Annual Parasite Incidence (API)	8.1	0.83	State Data11	Fully Immunization	48.7	79.7	NFHS-5******
