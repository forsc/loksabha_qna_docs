                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Grant to Health Sector




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat













                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has released Health Sector Grant to Local Bodies of various States;b)	if so, the details in this regard, State/UT-wise particularly to Maharashtra  ;c)	the details of the criteria fixed for selection of local bodies and amount of grants;d)	the extent to which health systems in various such local bodies have been strengthen as a result thereof; ande)	the extent to which it will plug the critical gaps in the health care system at the primary health care level, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	and (b)The Government has released 15th Finance Commission recommended Health Sector Grants to all the States including the State of Maharashtra as per State-wise details given in Annexure-I.     (c) to (e) On the basis of the health infrastructure available in the States, 15th Finance Commission identified interventions that will directly lead to strengthening the primary health infrastructure and facilities in both rural and urban areas. These grants for health through Local Governments will be spread over the five-year period from FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26 and facilitate strengthening of health system at the grass-root level.The components identified for upgradation of primary health care infrastructure in Rural and Urban areas along with the summary of the amount earmarked year-wise are given in Annexure-II.
