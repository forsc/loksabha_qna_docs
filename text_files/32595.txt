                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Number of villages with mobile services




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of villages which have not yet been provided with mobile services so far, State-wise along with the reasons therefor; and (b) 	the number of Wi-Fi hotspots that have been installed at Gram Panchayats (GPs) and services are provided therefrom, State-wise including Santhal Pargana in Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	As per data provided by Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) and field units of Department of Telecommunications as of December 2020, it is estimated that out of 5,97,618 inhabited villages in the country, as per Census-2011, 25,067 villages don’t have mobile connectivity. Out of 25,067 uncovered villages around 11,000 villages have been included in Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) schemes. The remaining villages of the country are covered by the Government and Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in a phased manner. The State/UT wise details of the uncovered villages are given in Annexure-I.(b)	Under BharatNet Project of Government of India, as on 03.01.2022, 1,04,259 number of Gram Panchayats(GPs) of the country including GPs of Santhal Pargana in Jharkhand have Wi-Fi Hotspots installed. There are 53,913 number of GPs where Wi-Fi Hotspots are providing services. Further, in Santhal Pargana, 517 number of Wi-Fi Hotspots are providing services. The State/UT wise details of the GPs are given in Annexure-II.
