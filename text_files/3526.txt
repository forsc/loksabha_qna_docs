                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3249






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Participation of Women in Economic Activities




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the participation of women in economic activities has increased in the recent times and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has any data to estimate the number of women participating in various sectors of the economy including rural economy, if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is aware that the increased participation of women in economic activities has led to exploitation of women and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the number of cases or incidents of exploitation of women reported during the last three years including the current year, State/year-wise;(e)	whether the Government has taken any measure to prevent such exploitation against women and to encourage them to participate in economic activities and if so, the details thereof; and(f)	the response of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATISMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	& (b): As per the last three rounds of Annual Employment – Unemployment (EUS) Surveys conducted by Labour Bureau, Ministry of Labour & Employment in the year 2012-13, 2013-14 and 2015-16, the Worker Population Ratio for females aged 15 years & above according to usual status approach are 25.0%, 29.6% and 25.8% respectively. (b)	& (d): As per the data of the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), a total number of 34,651 cases in 2015 and 38,947 cases in 2016 were reported under rape (Section 376 of the IPC). A total number of 8685 and 7305 cases have been registered under insult to the modesty of women (section 509 of IPC) during 2015 and 2016 respectively. Further total numbers of 82422 and 81346 cases have been registered under assault on Women with intent to outrage her Modesty (Section 354 of IPC) during 2015 and 2016 respectively. Year-wise number of cases reported State/UT wise is at Annexure.(e) 	& (f): The Ministry of Women and Child Development is administering the Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013covers all women, irrespective of their age or employment status and protects them against sexual harassment at all workplaces whether organized or unorganized. Students, apprentices, labourers, domestic workers and even women visiting an officer are included in the Act.In order to ensure the effective implementation of the Act, the Ministry has developed an online complaint management system titled Sexual Harassment electronic–Box (SHe-Box) for registering complaints related to sexual harassment at workplace. SHe-Box portal offers the facility of making online complaints of sexual harassment at workplace to all women employees in the country, including all government and private
