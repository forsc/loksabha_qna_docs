                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3937






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Development of Sports Infrastructure




Dayakar Pasunoori













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government of Telangana has requested the Union Government to sanction Rs. 200 crore for various developmental works related to sports and its infrastructure, if so, the details thereof and action taken on each issue;(b) whether it has also been requested to set up a Cricket academy in each State, if so, the details thereof and action taken thereon; and(c) whether the State Government has also requested the Union Government to link the works of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme to sports and also to develop the grounds and stadiums in rural areas to help the sportspersons, if so, the details thereof and action taken thereon?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) This Ministry has not received proposals worth Rs. 200 crore for various developmental works related to sports and its infrastructure from the Government of Telangana. However, under Khelo India Scheme, proposals worth Rs. 168.01 crore have been received from various eligible entities from the State of Telangana. Proposals worth Rs. 19.50 crore have been sanctioned as per Annexure-I.(b) As per information received from Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), State/Union Territory wise list of cricket academies is at Annexure- II. BCCI being resource organization provides for promotion of sport of Cricket. This Ministry does not set up cricket academies.(c) While no request from State/Union Territory Governments has been received in this Ministry for linking the works of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme to sports, this Ministry is working with Ministry of Panchayati Raj and Rural Development to develop sports fields in rural areas from the funds made available to Gram Panchayats including under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme to sports.
