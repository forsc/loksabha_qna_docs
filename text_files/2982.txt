                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2767






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Supply of Drinking Water through Trains




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received any request from the Government of Tamil Nadu to bring drinking water in trains from other districts in the State to the capital of Tamil Nadu;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the action taken by the Government; and(d)  the terms and conditions laid down for this arrangement?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b) A request has been received from Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Board (Government of Tamil Nadu) for arranging transportation of water in Railway Wagons from Jolarpettai to Villivakkam, Chennai. (c) and (d) Based on the request of Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Board (Government of Tamil Nadu), deployment of 2 tank wagon rakes consisting of 50 wagons each has been planned for transporting water from Jolarpettai to Villivakkam, Chennai. The first rake has already been supplied and the second rake will be supplied as per request. The movement of water will be as per extant rules.*****
