                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Khoya Paya Portal




Pradeep Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the number and details of children benefited through the KhoyaPaya web portal developed by the Ministry; (b) 	whether the facilities like child help desk have been set up at the railway stations; and (c) 	if so, the number and details of places where the said facility has commenced, State/UT-wise?
