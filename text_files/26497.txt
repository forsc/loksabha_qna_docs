                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Skill Development for Divyangjan under NSDC




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar













                                                    Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the details of the Skill Development Programme and initiatives taken for divyangjan under the Skill Development Corporation;(b)      the details of the said implementation State-wise along with the list of programmes launched in Parbhani and Osmanabad district of Maharashtra;(c)       whether the Government is likely to consider to organise Rozgar Mela for divyangjan or persons with special abilities; and(d)       if so, the details of the Prabhani and Osmanabad Parliamentary Constituencies?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) is implementing central component of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) across the country including Parbhani and Osmanabad district of Maharashtra, which is for all sections of the society, including Divyangjan (Persons with Disability). Under PMKVY, there are incentives to encourage participation of Divyangjan in the skill training, namely, monthly conveyance support (Rs. 1500 for Conveyance outside the Home District/ Rs.1000 for conveyance within the Home District) being directly paid to the candidates while financial support of Rs.5000 for assistive devices, aid and appliances; and boarding and lodging as per Common Cost Norms being provided to the Training Provider (TP). Also, an exclusive Sector Skill Council for Divyangjan known as Skill Council for PwD (SCPwD) has been created for addressing the skilling needs of the Divyangjan. SCPwD empanels Divyangjan-friendly training centres, which are structurally designed to cover Braille, computer screen reader for visually impaired; sign language interpreter for speech and hearing impaired, etc.(b)     48,476 Divyangjan have been trained / oriented as on 10.07.2021 under central component of PMKVY across the country, out of which 122 and 1 Divyangjan are from Parbhani and Osmanabad district of State of Maharashtra, respectively. The State-wise number of  Divyangjan trained / oriented under central component of PMKVY, as on 10.07.2021, is given in Annexure.(c)      Yes, Sir. Rozgar Melas are being conducted by NSDC for all persons including Divyangjan. (d)       In the State of Maharashtra, 64 Rozgar Melas were conducted and around 20 thousand candidates were registered. Out of total 64 Rozgar Melas, 2 Rozgar Melas were conducted in Osmanabad district of Maharashtra. However, no Rozgar Mela was conducted in Prabhani district of Maharashtra.
