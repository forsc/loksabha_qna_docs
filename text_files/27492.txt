                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3029






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the quantum of funds sanctioned and released under Beti Bachao Beti Padhao scheme to the State of Telangana for the last five years, district-wise;(b) 	whether any diversion of the funds from the sanctioned scheme to any other schemes has been found, if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the measures being considered to arrest such diversion in future?   






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a)         Under BBBP Scheme 1 district namely Hyderabad was selected for Multi Sectoral Intervention since inception in 2014-15 and 7 more districts selected for Multi Sectoral Intervention from F.Y. 2018-19. District wise status of funds sanction to various district of Telangana in last five years is as under:     	 	Year (Amount sanctioned in Lakhs) *S No.	District	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19 	2019-20 	2020-21  1	Adilabad	NA	NA	23.50	49.95	25.00 2	Hyderabad	26.91	31.50	25.00	25.00	25.00 3	Karimnagar	NA	NA	43.70	50.00	22.55 4	Mahbubnagar	NA	NA	25.00	38.50	25.00 5	Nalgonda	NA	NA	25.00	25.00	25.00 6	Nizamabad	NA	NA	25.00	25.00	22.00 7	Rangareddy	NA	NA	25.00	49.99	49.99 8	Warangal	NA	NA	25.00	45.96	25.00 (*) Amount sanctioned includes funds released and revalidated for respective financial year.  (b) & (c)  No Sir, Does not arise. ****
