                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

872






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Wildlife Sanctuaries




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has conducted any study to ascertain the total economic value derived from the various wildlife sanctuaries/national parks in the country;(b)	if so, details thereof, Wildlife Sanctuary/National Park-wise;(c)	whether the Government has conducted any study of the total number of people employed in various roles across the National Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries;(d)	if so, the total number of such persons employed in the National Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries, reserve-wise and role-wise; and(e)	the estimated total contribution of National Parks/Wildlife sanctuaries to the economy of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a), (b) and (e)Economic value of 16 Tiger Reserves in the country which include respective National Parks and Sanctuaries have been estimated by Indian Institute of Forest Management, Bhopal with support of National Tiger Conservation Authority in two phases respectively in 2013-15 and 2016-19. The details of same are at Annexure.(c) and (d) No such study was conducted by the Ministry.***
