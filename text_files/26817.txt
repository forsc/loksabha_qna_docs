                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2516






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Micro-irrigation in Maharashtra




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil













                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the total area covered district-wise in Maharashtra by Micro Irrigation in the country till now under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana - Per Drop More Crop (PMKSY-PDMC);(b) the details of the central assistance sanctioned and released to Maharashtra in the last three years;(c) the details of the funds disbursed to various States under the Micro Irrigation Fund by NABARD;(d) the major findings of the evaluation study of PDMC carried out by NITI Aayog; and(e) the steps being taken by the Government to promote awareness among farmers about use of Micro- Irrigation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	An area of 57.30 lakh ha has been covered under Micro Irrigation in the Country throughPer Drop More Crop component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana(PMKSY-PDMC) from 2015-16 to till date.Out of which 6.55 lakh ha area has been covered in the State of Maharashtra. The district wise coverage of Micro Irrigation in State of Maharashtra under PMKSy-PDMC is Annexed.(b)The details of Central Assistance sanctioned and released under PMKSY-PDMC to State of Maharashtra in the last three years is as under:  Sl.No.	Year	Amount (Rs. crore)1.		2018-19	360.02.		2019-20	325.03.		2020-21	300.0Total	985.0(c)	Total loan amount of Rs. 1857.29 crore was disbursed to various statesunder Micro-Irrigation Fund(MIF) corpus created with NABARD and the details of amount disbursed as on  28th July, 2021 is as under:  Sl.No.	State	Disbursement(Rs. crore)1.		Andhra Pradesh	616.132.		Gujarat	252.303.		Tamil Nadu	937.474.		Haryana	46.335.		Punjab	5.06Total	1857.29(d)	NITI Aayog conducted evaluation study on PMKSY-PDMC scheme in 2020.  The major findings of the study are as under: •	The scheme is relevant in achieving national priorities such as substantially improving on-farm water use efficiency, enhancing crop productivity, generating employment opportunities etc.•	Micro irrigation adoption has helped to save water. Saving of water across various states are around 30 to 50% in Andhra Pradesh, 50% in Karnataka, 30% in Tamil Nadu, 70% in Jharkhand, 40 to 50% in Uttar Pradesh, 50% in Punjab, 40% in Bihar.•	Water use efficiency has improved around 30% to 70%.•	Created direct and indirect employment opportunities.•	Income of farmers has increased in the range of 10% to 69%.(e)	Farmers are encouraged to adopt micro-irrigation under PMKSY-PDMC through wide publicity in press & print media, publication of leaflets/booklets, organization of workshops, exhibitions, farmer fairs, information on State/Government of India web portals etc. Further, Indian Council of Agricultural Research through Indian Institute of Water Management, All India Coordinated Research Project on Irrigation Water Management Research and Consortia Platform on Water is addressing issues related to judicious use of water including drip Irrigation and sprinkler irrigation.
