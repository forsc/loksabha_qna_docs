                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1228






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Tax Disputes




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of total amount of funds that are locked up in cases at various quasi-judicial, appellate and judicial forums under Service Tax and Central Excise put together as on date;(b) the total number of settlements done and the amounts involved in the Sabka Vishwas (Legacy Dispute Resolution) Scheme, 2019 which was created with the objective of settling pending disputes of the Service Tax and Central Excise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) The details of total amount of funds that are locked up in cases at various quasi-judicial, appellate and judicial forums under Service Tax and Central Excise put together as on dated 31.12.2019 are as follows:S.N. 	Forum	Number	Amount( Rs. in Cr.)1	Supreme Court	2327	31088.962	High Court	9952	45893.473	CESTAT	51841	184860.654	Commissioner (Appeals)	16068	8015.865	Adjudication (Upto Commissioner)	64368	145313.856	Total	144556	415172.83(b)  The total number of settlements done under the  Sabka Vishwas (Legacy Dispute        Resolution Scheme, 2019) as on dated 05.02.2020 are 49534 and amount involved in these cases is 24970 cr.
