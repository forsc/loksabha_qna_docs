                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2482






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Metro Rail Projects in Chhattisgarh




Jyotsna Charandas Mahant














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering to start Metro Rail Projects in large cities like Raipur and Bilaspur of Chhattisgarh; and(b)	if so, the details thereof and the manner in which the Government or any other agency is likely to invest funds for the said Metro Rail Projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a)	Urban Transport, which is an integral part of urban development, is a state subject. Accordingly, respective State Governments formulate proposals in regard to Metro Rail Projects with appropriate funding pattern, along with Comprehensive Mobility Plan, Alternative Analysis Report, Detailed Project Report etc. No such proposal from the  State  Government  of  Chhattisgarh has been received in this Ministry in respect of Raipur and Bilaspur thus far.(b)	 Does not arise.                                        ******
