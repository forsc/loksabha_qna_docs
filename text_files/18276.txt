                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2057






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Selling of SCI




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided to sell Shipping Corporation of India (SCI);(b)	if so, the details thereof and the percentage of stake to be sold;(c)	whether the Government had appointed assets valuers for valuation of all assets of SCI and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether assets valuers have submitted their report to the Government and if so, the details thereof;(e)	whether the Government is likely to consult all stakeholders along with the employees union before making disinvestment in SCI and if so, the response of the stakeholders in this regard; and (f)	the time by which the disinvestment is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (f): CCEA in its meeting held on 20.11.2019 accorded “in – principle” approval for strategic disinvestment of Government of India’s shareholding of 63.75% in SCI along with transfer of management control to a strategic buyer. M/s Protocol Insurance Surveyors & Loss Assessors Pvt. Ltd. have been appointed as the Asset Valuer for the Strategic Disinvestment of Shipping Corporation of India. The process of transaction is underway.                                          *****
