                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2668






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Environmental Clearance to Construction Companies




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has imposed a ban on the issuance of No-Objection Certificate for environmental clearances to the various construction companies and industries in the country, including Karnataka; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Ministry had formed any high-level committee for conducting inspection and probe the concerned realtors and companies; (d) 	if so, whether the committee has submitted its report to the Ministry and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	whether the National Green Tribunal has taken note of the said cases and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) & (b) The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change has not imposed any ban on issuance of No-Objection Certificate (NOC) for environmental clearances to construction companies and industries in the country, including Karnataka. The Ministry accords prior environmental clearance for projects or activities listed in the Schedule to the Environment Impact Assessment (EIA) Notification, 2006 (and its amendments) after having followed the prescribed process in the said notification.(c)	No Sir.(d)	Does not arise.(e)	No Sir. *******
