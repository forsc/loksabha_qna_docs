                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4677






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Companies Registered with ESIC




Arjun Singh














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has any data of companies registered with ESIC in West Bengal;(b)if so, the details thereof; (c)if not, the reasons therefor;(d)the details of companies registered in Barrackpur Parliamentary Constituency of North 24 Pargana district; and(e)the steps being taken by the Government to cover deprived workers under ESIC and to bring them into mainstream?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (b):	Yes, Sir. The total number of employers registered under the Employees'' State Insurance (ESI) Act, 1948 as on 31.03.2018 in West Bengal is 62,736.(c):		Does not arise in view of (a) & (b).(d):		In Employees’ State Insurance Corporation, the data regarding number of employers registered under the ESI Act, is not maintained Parliamentary Constituency-wise. However the total number of employers registered under the ESI Act, 1948 under the jurisdiction of Sub-regional Office, Barrackpore, as on 31.03.2018 is 8,581.(e):		Under ESIC 2.O programme, ESI Scheme is to be extended to all districts of the country.  Periodical surveys are conducted for bringing more units and their employees under the coverage of ESI Scheme.  As on date number of fully and partially implemented districts under ESI Scheme is 354 and 187 respectively.******
