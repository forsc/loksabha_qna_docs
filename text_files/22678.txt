                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3825






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Promotion of Scout and Guides Movement




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government plans to implement new schemes to promote the scout and guides movement in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the total budgetary allocations for the National Youth Development and contribution from non budgetary sources;(c)	whether measures have been taken to promote National Service Scheme and Scout and Guide movement in every school and university in the country; and(d)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS{KIREN RIJIJU}(a)	This Ministry runs a Scheme namely “Assistance to Scouting & Guiding” under which funds in the form of grant in aid are released to Scouting & Guiding Organisations for conducting Scouting & Guiding activities across the country. No new scheme is envisaged.(b)	The total Budgetary allocation for National Youth Development is as under:<pre>Scheme/Year	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21NPYAD	? 25 Lakhs	? 21 Lakhs	? 20 LakhsNYDF	Nil	Nil	Nil </pre>Contribution from non-budgetary sources for NPYAD and NYDF is NIL.  (c)	& (d) Measures have been taken to promote National Service Schemes (NSS) and Scout and Guide movement in every school and university in the country. NSS has its presence in 479 Universities covering 17,676 Colleges/Technical Institutions and 51 +2 Councils/Directorates and 12,087 Senior Secondary Schools across the country. This Ministry releases financial assistance to NGOs in the form of grant-in-aid to promote scout & guide activities across the country.****
