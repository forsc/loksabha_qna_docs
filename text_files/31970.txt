                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3341






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA)




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of empanelled centres under the Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA), State/UT-wise and district/block-wise for Rajasthan;(b)	the total number of patients covered under the PMSMA, category-wise , State/UT wise including district/block-wise for Rajasthan;(c)	the number of doctors registered and volunteer their services under the PMSMA, State/UT-wise including district/block-wise for Rajasthan;(d)	whether the government is taking any steps to encourage allied health care professionals to volunteer with the PMSMA and if so, the details thereof;(e)	the total number of IT-enabled registrations with the PMSMA, State/UT-wise including district/block for Rajasthan; and(f)	the steps taken by the Government to increase awareness about and encourage such IT-based registration?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND                            FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	A total of 18862 facilities are empanelled under the Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA) across State/UTs. The State/UT wise details including that of Rajasthan are placed at Annexure-1(b)	 As on 13.12.2021 (since inception), 3.04 crore pregnant women received antenatal care under Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA) across State/UTs. The State/UT wise details including that of Rajasthan are placed at Annexure-2(c)	& (e) A total of 6998 volunteers are registered as on 13.12.2021 across State/UTs. The State/UT wise status of volunteers registered (IT-enabled) under Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA) including that of Rajasthan are placed at Annexure-3(d)	As per PMSMA Guideline, there is no provision for allied health care professionals to be registered as volunteer.(f) Following steps have been taken to increase awareness and encourage doctors for IT-based registrations under Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA)•	Appeal to private medical practitioners to volunteer and join hands for PMSMA by Hon''ble Prime Minister on the occasion of "MANN KI BAAT" episode on 31st July''2019.•	PMSMA ‘IPledgeFor9’ Achievers Awards have been instituted to celebrate individual and team achievements and acknowledge voluntary contributions by private doctors for PMSMA.•	Centralised PMSMA Portal has been created for registering private practitioners.•	MoHFW in collaboration with professional organisations viz. FOGSI (Federation of Obstetric and Gynecological Societies of India), IMA (Indian Medical Association) and others, promote PMSMA through platforms like video conferences with member doctors, Loksabha TV, etc.
