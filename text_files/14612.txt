                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3402






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Kisan Channel




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched new kisan channel to be aired/broadcasted on radio/television; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the names of the States of the country where kisan channel is being broadcasted; (d)	whether the Government has appointed agriculture graduates and cameramen separately for the said channel; and (e)	if so, the number of experts and employees engaged in the said channel, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c): The “DD Kisan” Channel, dedicated to agriculture and related sectors, was launched on 26th May, 2015.  The channel is available on Prasar Bharati’s free to air DTH Platform “DD FreeDish” to the viewers and also on all private DTH services and Cable Television Networks across the country.(d) & (e): Forty seven TV professionals with relevant experience have been especially engaged for DD Kisan channel.***********************
