                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3517






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Investigation of Cases by Delhi Police




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the investigation into the said cases has been completed by the Delhi Police;	(b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which compensation is likely to be disbursed to the family of victims;(c) if not, the time by which the said investigation is likely to be completed and the compensation disbursed;(d) whether there is any mechanism to monitor and ensure timely completion of the process; and(e) if so, the details thereof along with the guidelines, if any, regarding time limit for disbursement of the compensation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):  The present status of both cases of accidents that occurred during the year 2018 near road construction site in Delhi and action taken by the Delhi Police along with persons killed is as under:	Year	Case Registered	Persons Killed	Persons Arrested	Present Position	Location2018  	FIR No.47/18 dt.15.02.2018 u/s 288/304A IPC PS Mansarover Park	01	02	Investigation of the case has been completed	Near Jindal Oil Mill, Shahdara fly-over	FIR No.527/18 dt.10.12.2018 u/s 279/304A IPC PS Vikas Puri	01	01	Investigation of the case has been completed.	Outer Ring Road, Vikas Kunj, near MCD Dustbin, Vikas Puri	-2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.3517 FOR 10.12.2019(c) to (e):  The Public Works Department of GNCTD has informed that the compensation is paid to the victim as per directions of the Motor Accident Claim Tribunal (MACT).  No direction from (MACT) has been received till date in the said cases.*****
