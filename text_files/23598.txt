                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4689






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Central Monitoring of MPLADS




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has developed any central monitoring mechanism for the MPLAD Scheme;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government also proposes to bring its monthly report; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a)  Yes Sir.  (b) The Guidelines on Members of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme(MPLADS) provide for a detailed monitoring frame work. Chapter 6 of these guidelines  contain  detailed monitoring mechanism for implementation of the scheme; where the roles of the i) Central Government, ii) State / UT Government, iii) District Authority and  Implementing Agencies are given in  Para 6.2, Para 6.3, Para 6.4 and Para 6.5 respectively. (c)  & (d)  Annexure -VI of the guidelines on MPLADS contains the proforma for Monthly Progress Report (MPR), to be prepared and submitted separately for each sitting / former Rajya Sabha / Lok Sabha Member by the Nodal District Authority to this Ministry with a copy to concerned Member of Parliament, on monthly basis.                                                      *****
