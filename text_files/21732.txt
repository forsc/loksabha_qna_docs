                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2871






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Plantation of Trees




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that Ficus (Peepal) and other similar trees have been planted beneath the Delhi Metro Elevations and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the growth of such trees in later stage would hamper the structures of Delhi Metro Elevations; (c) 		if so, the details thereof; (d) 		whether the Government proposes to relocate such trees to other places; and (e) 		if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) Yes Sir. The details are given in Annexure as per the information provided by Forest Department, NCT of Delhi. (b)& (c)  No Sir. Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has informed DMRC is    regularly trimming such trees for the safety of the structures.   (d)& (e) No Sir. Question does not arise. *****
