                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2842






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






PLI Scheme




Goddeti Madhavi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether companies have been selected for the production-linked incentive (PLI) scheme for manufacturing telecom equipment in India and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details regarding the reasons to why software is not covered in the specified list of telecom and networking ‘products’ to be manufactured; and(c) 	the details regarding the steps taken by the Government to ensure that expansion in domestic telecom manufacturing is done along with nationally owned product development, so as to limit large foreign exchange outflows? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b)	Yes Sir. 31 companies have been selected for telecom and networking products manufacturing under the Scheme, as given in ANNEXURE. The development of software is not covered in the specified list under Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for telecom and networking products. (c)  	24 out of 31 selected companies are domestic companies, with 16 from Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). The Scheme is targeted to boost domestic manufacturing and reduce import dependence of telecom and networking products.
