                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

855






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Corona Vaccines for Children




Rakesh Singh













                                                    Brijendra Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of children upto 18 years of age who have contracted COVID-19 as on July 2021;(b) whether COVID vaccines for children are in the pipeline/trial stage, if so, the details thereof;(c) whether antidotes were found in children before test trial, if so, the details thereof; and(d) the expected time by which the said vaccine is likely to be developed and made available to children?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a): A total of around 11% of the total COVID-19 cases have been reported in persons aged less than 20 years in the country. (b) to (d): The National regulator i.e.  CDSCO has granted permission to conduct clinical trials of COVID-19 vaccines in children to M/s Bharat Biotech, Hyderabad (in Age group 2 to 18 years) for Phase-II/III of Whole-Virion Inactivated SARS-CoV-2 Vaccine and to M/s Cadila Healthcare Ltd., Ahmedabad (in Age group =12 yrs and above) for Phase-III clinical trial of DNA based Vaccine. The outcome of these trials will depend on data emerging from the trial for the purpose of its approval and availability in the country.
