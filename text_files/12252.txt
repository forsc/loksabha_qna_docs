                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Review of  Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to review the ‘Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan’; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to include computer education also under ‘Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan’; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’) (a) to (d):   A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (A) TO (D) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 101 FOR REPLY ON 10.02.2020 ASKED BY HON’BLE MPs SHRI DILIP SAIKIA AND SHRI RAMESH CHANDER KAUSHIK REGARDING REVIEW OF SARVA SHIKSHA ABHIYAN(a) to (d): The Department of School Education & Literacy  was implementing  the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA), Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA) and Teacher Education (CSSTE) till 2017-18. These schemes were reviewed and  accordingly, an Integrated Scheme for School Education-Samagra Shiksha was launched from 2018-19 by subsuming SSA, RMSA and Teacher Education (TE).  Samagra Shiksha has provision for computer education for Classes VI to XII.*****
