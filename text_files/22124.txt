                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3427






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






PM-KISAN Payments in Maharashtra




Aparupa  Poddar













                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has data on the recovery of farmers in Maharashtra and their agricultural lands post second tranche of funds allocated to farmers in 2020 under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana;(b)	Whether all farmers have been able to receive the funds as per the three instalments promised; and(c)	If not, the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Yes Sir.  As on 11th March, 2021, around Rs.78.37 crore has been recovered from the farmers found ineligible under the Scheme, in  the State of Maharashtra.(b) and (c):	Yes, Sir. PM-KISAN is a continuous and ongoing Scheme, and as and when the corrected and error free data of the eligible beneficiaries are received from the States/UTs, the requisite amount is transferred to the account of beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer. The beneficiaries, whose names are uploaded on PM-KISAN Portal by the State/UT Government in a particular 4-month period / trimester, shall be entitled to receive benefit for that trimester and for further installments pertaining to the subsequent trimesters.*****
