Pradhan Mantri Janjatiya Vikas Mission




Anurag Sharma













                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Janjatiya Vikas Mission in the country including North Eastern Region and if so, the details thereof and the aims and objective behind the move; (b) whether the scheme is an amalgamation of a number of existing schemes and if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of most backward tribal dominated districts that will be targeted under this mission;(d) the estimated number of tribals in remote areas who will be provided employment opportunities; and(e) the other steps taken by the Government for the welfare of tribals living in most backward areas?






ANSWER
