                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

81






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Survey on Migrant and Domestic Labourers




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has made any survey on migrant and domestic labourers in the country;(b)if so, the details thereof and time by which its Report is likely to be submitted;(c)if not, the steps taken by the Government in this regard;(d)whether it is also proposed to set up a Commission to look into the well being of migrant and domestic labourers in the country; and(e)if so, the details thereof and the time by which the said Commission is likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI BHUPENDER YADAV)(a) to (c):	Labour Bureau, an attached office of the Ministry of Labour & Employment has been entrusted with the task of conducting the  “All India Survey on Migrant Workers” and “All India Survey on Domestic Workers”. An Expert Group has been constituted under the Chairmanship of renowned Statistician Prof. S.P. Mukherjee to finalise technical aspects of surveys. The field work of All India Survey on Migrant Workers has been started w.e.f. 1st April 2021. (d):		No such proposal as on date is under consideration of the Government. (e):		Doesn’t arise in view of (d) above.*****
