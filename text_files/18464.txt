                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether any research institutes are being run by the Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and(c) whether the Government has reviewed the functioning of these institutions and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the achievements made by them so far?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	Yes.(b):	There are 31 peripheral Research Institutes run by Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS)  in different parts of the country as per details given at Annex-I.(c):	The Government of India has constituted Review Committees from time to time to review the functioning of CCRAS and its peripheral Institutes. The details of Review Committee   constituted  by the Government, are as under : I.	Katoch Committee in 2016, under the Chairmanship of Dr. V.M. Katoch, former Secretary, DHR & Former DG, ICMR;  II.	Jalaja Committee in 2016, under the chairperson Smt. S. Jalaja, former Secretary, Department of AYUSH, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare;  and III.	Katoch Committee in 2017, under the Chairmanship of Dr. D.C. Katoch, Advisor (AY), Ministry of AYUSH. (d):	The achievements made by the Council during 2019-2020 are given at Annex-II.
