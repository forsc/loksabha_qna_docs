                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3899






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Vacancies in Post Offices




Devendrappa Y














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether services offered by post offices have deteriorated due to shortfall in staff strength and if so, the details thereof along with the number of staff in post offices, State/UT-wise and post-wise; (b) 	whether Government proposes to conduct any special drive to fill-up the existing vacancies in post offices across the country; (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the action proposed by the Government to arrest deteriorating services in post offices across the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (d) 	Services offered by post offices have not deteriorated inspite of shortfall in staff Strength as the vacant posts are being manned, by appropriately redeploying the available staff, to ensure that postal services are not affected. Postal Circle-wise and post-wise working strength and vacancies are given in enclosed statement.(b) & (c) 	Filling of vacant posts is a continuous process and is being done regularly by holding departmental promotion committee, departmental examination and direct recruitment through Staff Selection Commission and also by individual Postal Circle as per the provisions of the relevant recruitment rules. At present there is no proposal to conduct special recruitment drive.
