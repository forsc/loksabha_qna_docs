                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Integrated Child Development Scheme




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has taken note of the irregularities committed by certain officials working under Integrated Child development Scheme (ICDS) programme in Karimnagar district, Telangana, if so, the action proposed to be taken against such officials; and(b)	the quantum of funds allocated and utilized by the State of Telangana under ICDS Scheme during the last five years, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTRY OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	Anganwadi Services is a Centrally sponsored scheme implemented by the States/UTs across the country. Complaints, if any, received in the Ministry are forwarded to State Governments/ UT Administrations for taking necessary action. However, as per the information received from Government of Telangana, a comment was made in Chaper-III vide Para 3.4 of CAG Report for the year ending March 2016 with regard to "Payment to food suppliers without receipt of Food Stocks", to the tune of 1.2 crores estimated during the financial year 2012-13 under Supplementary Nutrition Programme (SNP) under Anganwadi Services. Preliminary enquiry was conducted and Departmental action has been initiated against the officials involved. FIR has also been booked against certain officers and concerned firms.(b)	The status of funds allocated and utilized by the State of Telangana under Anganwadi Services during the last five years is tabulated as below:  (Rupees in Lakhs)  	Sl.
