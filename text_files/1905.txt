                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1709






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Threat of IS




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken note of reports that Islamic State (IS) was targeting to attack tourist places in Kerala;(b) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard;(c) whether the National Investigation Agency (NIA) or any other agency hasbusted an IS module in the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (b) :  Intelligence inputs on terror threats are regularly shared with State Governments / Agencies concerned with advice to take appropriate measures and sensitize their field formations to prevent any untoward incident.(c) to (d):  In connection with 3 IS related cases, NIA had conducted raids in Kerala (April, 2019) and in Tamil Nadu (June 2019) and arrested 4 persons.*****
