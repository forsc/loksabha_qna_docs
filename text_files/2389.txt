                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Benefit to MSME Sector




Raj Hans Hans














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has asked the Federation of  Indian Export Organisations to submit a report on issues like shifting from subsidies to cheaper availability of loan in foreign currency etc. ;(b)	if so, the details thereof ; and (c)	whether Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises would benefit out of it and if so,  the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (c): 	 The Government of India conducts studies on issues such as export subsidies and export credit from time to time.  Most of these studies indicate that the Indian Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises would benefit from both export subsidies and cheaper availability of loans in foreign currency.
