                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

897






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Four-Laning Projects in Karnataka




Sumalatha Ambareesh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken up the works of four-laning projects in the State of Karnataka and if so, the details and the present status thereof;(b)	whether it is a fact that these four-laning projects are running behind schedule and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c) 	whether the Government has undertaken any measures to review the work schedule of the said projects and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether the Government has paid compensation to the affected land owners with due interest and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) A total of fifteen 4-laning projects in the State of Karnataka, comprising length of 989.84 km with total cost of Rs. 17,678 crores are in different stages of implementation. The list of projects along with progress is provided at Annexure. Out of 15 projects, 07 projects are running behind schedule due to delay in land acquisition, forest clearance, quarry permission etc. and stoppage of work by local residents for want of additional structures, higher compensation etc. Several meetings have been conducted at higher level for expeditious completion of the highway projects.	 (d)	The compensation for the acquisition of land acquired along with interest is determined and paid to the affected land holders as per provisions of Right to Fair Compensation in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (RFCTLARR Act, 2013) applicable to National Highways Act (NH), 1956.
