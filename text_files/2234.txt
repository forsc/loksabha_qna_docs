                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

186






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Role of Small and Medium Enterprises




Chandan Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether small and medium enterprises play a key role in the manufacturing sector of the country;(b)	if so, the percentage-wise contribution of small and medium enterprises in the total production of the manufacturing sector during the years 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18;(c)	whether some units of small and medium enterprises have also been closed down during the said period; and(d)	if so, the number of units closed during each year of the said period along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI) (a)to(d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *186 for answer on 04.07.2019(a)&(b):   Yes Sir. As per the information received from Central Statistics Office, Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation, the contribution of MSME Manufacturing to All India Manufacturing Output during 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18 was 34.2%, 33.8% and 33.4% respectively.(c)&(d):    As per the information received from Reserve Bank of India (RBI), stressed accounts referred to the Corrective Action Plan Committee during the half year ended March 2017, September, 2017, March 2018 and September, 2018 are 1,00,803; 87,062; 1,30,208 and 1,50,165 respectively and the number of Sick MSMEs was 4,86,291in 2016.
