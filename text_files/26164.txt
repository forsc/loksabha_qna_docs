                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1928






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Discrepancies in the Death of Doctors




Karti P. Chidambaram














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)	the total number of deaths of doctors due to COVID-19, till date;(b)	whether the Government is aware of the discrepancies in the deaths of doctors as recorded by the Centre and as assessed by Indian Medical Association, if so, the reasons for such discrepancy;(c)	the total amount of insurance claims settled under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package for frontline workers other than doctors, month-wise till date;(d)	the total amount of insurance claims pending settlement under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package for frontline workers other than doctors, month-wise till date; and(e)	whether the Government is planning to further extend the scheme, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILIY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (e) Ministry of Health & Family Welfare monitors cases of COVID-19 and its management as per the details provided by the States. Life insurance benefits are being provided under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package (PMGKP): Insurance Scheme for Health Workers Fighting COVID-19. The scheme provides a comprehensive personal accident cover of Rs. 50 lakhs to healthcare providers, including community health workers and private health workers drafted by the Government for COVID-19 related responsibilities, who may have to be in direct contact and care of COVID-19 patients and who may be at risk of being impacted by it. Claims related to a particular State/UT are certified and forwarded by the Health Department of respective states/UTs for the purpose of claiming the insurance.At present the scheme is for a period up to 180 days w.e.f. 24.04.2021 with a provision for extension up to 180 days more.  The number of infections are being reviewed regularly for taking a decision with respect to extending the scheme from time to time.As on 15th July 2021, a total no. of 921 doctors and healthcare workers have been paid insurance claim @50 lakh each under the said scheme.State/UT wise details of claims received, paid, pending under the old and new implementation mechanism of the Scheme are at Annexure I and Annexure II respectively.
