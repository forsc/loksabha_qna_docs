                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

523






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






ROB at Avaneeswaram




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering to commence the construction of ROB at Avaneeswaram (LC No. 519) under Madurai division in Kerala; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard;(c)	whether the Railways has received a priority list of ROBs/RUBs from the State Government of Kerala for construction on cost sharing basis; and(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the present status and priority during this year in Madurai division?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): No, Sir.  This level crossing (LC) does not qualify for its replacement by Road Over Bridge (ROB) on cost sharing basis as traffic density at this LC is less than one lakh Train Vehicle Unit (TVU).(c): No, Sir.(d): Does not arise.*****
