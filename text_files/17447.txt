                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Unemployed Youth under PMKVY in Odisha




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the number of unemployed youth in Odisha, specifically Bhubaneswar, who were provided skill development training every year under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) since its inception; (b)       the percentage of candidates across districts in Odisha who were offered placement after training; (c)        the percentage of candidates offered placements who were from aspirational districts of Odisha; (d)       the percentage of female candidates offered placements; and (e)       whether the Government has set any targets to provide employment to unemployed youth under various schemes and if so, details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (2016-20),  1.27 lakh candidates have been trained in the State of Odisha under Short Term Training (STT) courses. In addition 2.52 lakh candidates have been oriented under recognition of prior learning (RPL) component in the State. The year wise data is as follows: Component	FY 2016-17	FY 2017-18	FY 2018-19	FY 2019-20	TotalTrained in STT	1,281	47,711	28,421	49,176	1,26,589Oriented in RPL	3,173	18,549	64,837	1,66,390	2,52,949 (b) to (d)  Under placement linked  STT component of PMKVY (2016-20), out of  1.27 lakhs  candidates trained, 0.99 lakh  candidates were certified, of which 0.52 lakh candidates have been provided with the placement, which is around 52.52% of the certified candidates. Of the  total placed candidates  21 percent i.e 11,306 candidates are from aspirational districts and  48 percent i.e 24,829  are female candidates. (e)    Various programs implemented by Government of India for skilling aim to enhance the employability of the youth to access employment opportunities. The PMKVY (2016-20) scheme envisaged to impart skill development training to 1 crore youth of the country and assist certified trained youth in placement.
