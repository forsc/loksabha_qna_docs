                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Empowering Panchayats and Gram Sabhas




Ravindra Kushawaha













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is contemplating to provide more power to the Panchayats and Gram Sabhas; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the names of the various schemes proposed to be implemented directly at the level of Panchayati Raj Institutions?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c) Panchayat, being "Local Government", is a State subject and part of State list of Seventh Schedule of Constitution of India. Article 243G of Part IX of the Constitution allows discretion to State/Union Territory (UT) to endow, to the Panchayats with powers and authority including in areas listed in the Eleventh Schedule of the Constitution, to enable them to function as institutions of local self-governance to prepare plans and implement schemes for economic development and social justice. The extent and pace of devolution, including the monitoring of functionaries, to Panchayats vary across States. A statement showing subject-wise status of devolution in States (Tata Institute of Social Sciences Devolution Study 2015-16) is given at Annexure. The Government is implementing the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) involving Panchayati Raj Institutions for job card to wage seekers, execution of works, monitoring, supervision, social audit and consolidation of plans etc. The Institutional mechanism under Jal Jeevan Mission has Paani Samiti/ Village Water & Sanitation Committee (VWSC)/ User group at Gram Panchayat level. *****
