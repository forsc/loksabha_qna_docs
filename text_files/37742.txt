                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4996






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Defence College in Madhya Pradesh




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any plan to open a defence college in Madhya Pradesh is under consideration of theGovernment;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the places in Madhya Pradesh where the defence academies are functional at present;(c) whether the plan to open a separate defence academy in tribal areas and aspirational districts of Madhya Pradesh is under consideration and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the action plan formulated by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) to (d):  At present, there is no such proposal under consideration of the Government.  Non Commissioned Officers (NCO) Academy has been re-located from Binaguri to Dhana on        10th August, 2020.*****
