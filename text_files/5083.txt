                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4781






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Training of Beedi Rollers




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether it is a fact that the Government had launched a programme for Skill Development Training of beedi rollers to engage them in alternate jobs for their livelihood across all beedi rolling States and districts; (b)if so, the details thereof;(c)the number of beedi workers engaged in beedi rolling industry in the country, State-wise;(d)the number of workers given training under the skill development training programme for beedi rollers and number out of them shifted to alternate livelihoods; and(e)whether any analysis has been undertaken to show that their total earnings have increased, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	This Ministry has initiated a Skill Development Programme in collaboration with the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship and National Skill Development Corporation to provide alternative livelihood to beedi workers and their dependents.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::Various Courses in which Skill Development Training has been imparted1.
