                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3012






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Forts in Maharashtra under ASI




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Forts in the State of Maharashtra under the Jurisdiction of Archaeological Survey of India;(b)	the steps taken and/or proposed to be taken for promotion of cultural and heritage value of these Forts by the Government of India; and(c)	if so, the details thereof during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	There are 47 forts which are protected by ASI in the State of Maharashra. (b)&(c)	The conservation, preservation work of   protected monuments including forts in Maharashtra is a continuous process   and attended to regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India, as per need and priority considering the   availability of funds and resources.*****
