                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4578






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Rented Anganwadi Centresin Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of Anganwadi centres out of the total sanctioned Anganwadi centres in Maharashtra functioning under the rented premises;(b)	the district-wise details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has proposed to include the construction of Anganwadi centres in Maharashtra under the ambit of MGNREGA Scheme;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILDDEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	& (b) As on 16th March 2020, against 1,10,486 sanctioned Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) in State of Maharashtra, 21,604 are functioning from rented premises. District-wise details of AWCs functioning from rented premise against total operational AWCs in State of Maharashtra is Annexed.(c)	 to (e) Construction of AWC buildings in convergence with MGNREGS has been allowed for all States/UTs from the year 2015-16 @ Rs.7.00 per AWC building. However, state Government of Maharashtra had started the construction of AWCs in convergence with MGNREGS from the year 2017.********* AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4578 FOR 20.03.2020 REGARDING RENTED ANGANWADI CENTRES IN MAHARASHTRA ASKED BY SHRI VINAYAK RAUTDistrict-wise details of Operational AWCs and AWCs running from rented premises in the state of MaharashtraS.
