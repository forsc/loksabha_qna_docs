                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

706






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Political Influence in Indian Cricket




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of huge political influence in Indian cricket;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) the details of persons currently associated with BCCI; and(d) the steps taken by the Government to reduce political influence in Indian Cricket?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (d) Sir, the responsibility for promotion of the sport of cricket rests with the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). Elections to the BCCI have been held under the supervision of the Supreme Court Appointed Committee of Administrators and the former Chief Election Commissioner Shri N. Gopalaswamy was the Electoral Officer of the BCCI elections. The list of board members of the BCCI is at Annexure-I. The Government does not interfere in the constitution or elections of members to the BCCI or its constituent associations.****
