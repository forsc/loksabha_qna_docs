                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Rural Connectivity Projects in Uttar Pradesh under PMGSY




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the rural connectivity projects sanctioned in Uttar-Pradesh during the last five years including but not limited to works sanctioned under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana;(b) 	the rural connectivity projects completed by the Government in the State of Uttar-Pradesh during the last five years;(c) 	whether rural connectivity projects sanctioned during the last five years in Uttar-Pradesh have not yet been completed; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE)(a)      Rural connectivity projects sanctioned to the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last five years under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) and Road Connectivity Projects for Left Wing Extremism Areas (RCPLWEA) are as under: -PRADHAN MANTRI GRAM SADAK YOJANA (PMGSY) Year	  No. of works sanctioned	Length of roads sanctioned (km)	    Roads	Bridges	2016-17	    666	           1	              5297.712017-18	     21	           0	               172.792018-19	    122	           0	               926.082019-20	      0	           0	                 02020-21	    898	           5	              6287.38Total	   1707	           6	             12683.96 ROAD CONNECTIVITY PROJECTS FOR LEFT WING EXTREMISM AREAS (RCPLWEA) Year	        No. of works sanctioned	      Length of roads sanctioned (km)	        Roads	Bridges	2016-17	         0	   0	                     02017-18	        13	   0	                 254.082018-19	         0	   0	                     02019-20	        12	  11	                 287.232020-21	         0	   0	                     0Total	        25	  11	                 541.31 (b)        Rural connectivity projects completed by the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last five years under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) and Road Connectivity Projects for Left Wing Extremism Areas (RCPLWEA) are as under:- PRADHAN MANTRI GRAM SADAK YOJANA (PMGSY) Year	     No. of works Completed *	Length Completed (km)	        Roads	Bridges	2016-17	         462	   1	        3095.252017-18	         340	   1	        4106.462018-19	         431	   0	        1614.372019-20	          54	   0	         311.832020-21	          93	   0	         607.81Total	        1380	   2	        9735.72*A number of these projects were sanctioned in earlier years also.
