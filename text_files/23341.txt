                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Objectives of National Food Security Mission




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the main objectives of the National Food Security Mission; and(b)	the details of increase in productivity in Gujarat under the said scheme crop-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	In order to enhance production and productivity of food crops, the Government is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Food Security Mission (NFSM) in the country including Gujarat. The mission aims at increasing foodgrain/food crop production through area expansion in niche regions and productivity improvement. (b):	The details of increase of productivity of major food grains in the state of Gujarat is as under:(Kg per hectare)Sl. No	Crops 	2007-08	2020-21*	Change(+/-)1	Rice 	1942	2454	5122	Wheat 	3013	3178	1653	Total Pulses	843	1099	2564	Total Foodgrains	1831	2094	263            *2nd Advance Estimates for 2020-21.*****
