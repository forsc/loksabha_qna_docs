                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Hospitals under National Rural Health Mission




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to make any provisions to modernise and mechanise the rural hospitals of Maharashtra under National Rural Health Mission (NRHM);(b) whether the Government has provided or proposes to provide funds district-wise in Maharashtra;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of the guidelines of the Government for contractual recruitment of skilled/professional employees under NRHM?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) has now subsumed under National Health Mission (NHM). Under NHM, support is provided to States/ UTs including State of Maharashtra for strengthening of their healthcare system including upgradation of rural public health facilities. This support is based on the proposals received from States in the form of Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs). The proposals are appraised in National Programme Coordination Committee (NPCC) meetings and approvals are accorded to States/ UTs in the form of Record of Proceedings (RoPs). Public Health and Hospitals, being a State subject, the States are fully involved in implementation of NHM and have the flexibility to plan and implement state specific action plans. The State PIPs are envisaged to be an aggregate of the district/city health action plans, and include activities to be carried out at the state level.The District specific RoPs are released and implemented by respective States/ UTs. Central share under NHM is released to the respective States, which in turn releases to the Districts.(d):	“Public Health Hospitals” being a State subject and the implementation of NHM including contractual recruitment of skilled/professional employees is done by respective States/ UTs as per their guidelines and policies.
