                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Implementation of SVEP




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the progress made so far in the implementation of Start-up Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP) under the National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) across the country, State-wise including Maharashtra;(b)	the details of expenditure incurred on the implementation of the SVEP since the launch of the scheme till now, State-wise; and(c)	whether any review of SVEP has been undertaken, if so, the details and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Start-up Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP), the sub-scheme under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihood Mission (DAY-NRLM) has the objective of helping Self Help Group (SHG) and their family members to set-up small enterprises in the non-farm sector. SVEP projects are implemented in a block and are of four-year duration. The scheme was approved on 6th May, 2015 and the Action Plans for implementation of SVEP were invited from the State Governments during 2015-16. The Detailed Project Report (DPRs) preparation, approvals and fund release started in 2016-17. The enterprise formation started during 2017-18. As on 31st May, 2019, SVEP has been approved in 153 blocks across 23 States including Maharashtra, out of which in 108 Blocks, DPR has been approved and the programme implementation has been started. As on 31st May, 2019, a total of 53,084 enterprises have been supported under SVEP. The State wise details are given in Annexure – I.  (b):	Till 31st May, 2019, the total release to States under SVEP as Central share is Rs. 23,277.43 lakhs. The State-wise details of releases are given in the Annexure –II.(c):	The programme has been launched only in 2015-16 and has been effectively grounded only in 2016-17. Annual review of the SVEP scheme was conducted from 23rd to 26th July, 2018. The progress of implementation is also reviewed and monitored periodically in the Performance Review Meetings held at the State Rural Livelihood Missions and in the Ministry from time to time. A mid-term evaluation of SVEP scheme has been initiated in the month of April’ 2019 to complete it by September’ 2019.*****
