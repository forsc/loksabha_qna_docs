                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3197






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Construction of Houses in Tamil Nadu




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	the physical target fixed by the Government for construction of houses under Housing for All scheme during the last three years in Tamil Nadu; (b) 	the details of the houses actually been constructed in the State of Tamil Nadu during the said period;  and(c)  	the details of funds allocated under this scheme to the State of Tamil Nadu during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                                         THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) since 25.06.2015 for giving Central Assistance to States/ Union Territories (UTs), including the State of Tamil Nadu, for providing all-weather pucca houses to all eligible urban households. Government of India has not fixed any target for construction of houses in a State/ UT under PMAY-U.  States/ UTs, including State of Tamil Nadu, have undertaken demand survey under the scheme for assessing actual demand of housing.  Against validated demand in the State of Tamil Nadu, 7,19,853 houses have been sanctioned under PMAY-U.  Out of total sanctioned houses, 3,91,961 houses have been sanctioned during the last three years.(b)& (c)	Out of the total sanctioned houses, 3,43,265 houses have been completed and delivered to beneficiaries and Central Assistance of Rs. 4963.16 crore was released to the State Government of Tamil Nadu during the last three years i.e FY 2018-19 to FY 2020-21.                                               *****
