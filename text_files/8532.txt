                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Grant to Artisans




Queen Oja














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether the Government has any plan to provide any grant to the artisans in Assam who have been keeping the heritage of Assamese Culture by resorting their distinguished handicraft activities, sculptures in terms of bamboo, wood, earthen, brass, iron, other metals etc. and handloom & Textiles to boost up their moral to uphold such activities in the society traditionally; (b)  	if so, the details of the grants processed and if not, the time by which the said grant is likely to be released; and (c)  	the amount of grant to be provided to each of the artisans of Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c)	In the Handicrafts Sector of Ministry of Textiles, Government of India, under Human Resource Development, the funds have been sanctioned for training through Guru Shishya Parampara component to the artisans in Assam who have been keeping the heritage of Assamese Culture by resorting their distinguished handicraft activities, sculptures in terms of bamboo, wood, earthen, brass, iron, other metals etc. and handloom & textiles to boost up their moral to uphold such activities in the society traditionally. The details of the grants sanctioned in the last three years to each of the artisans of Assam is given as per the Annexure.   For Weavers of Handloom Sector, Block Level Cluster (BLC) was introduced in 2015-16 as one of the components of National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP). Financial assistance upto Rs. 2.00 crore per BLC for various interventions such as skill upgradation, Hathkargha Samvardhan Sahayata, product development, construction of workshed, project management cost, design development, setting up of Common Facility Centre (CFC) etc. is provided. Besides, financial assistance upto         Rs. 50.00 lakh is also available for setting up of one dye house at district level. The proposals are recommended by the State Government. During 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, 42 numbers of BLCs have been sanctioned and Rs. 29.67 crores has been released so far for the Assam State. Under Handloom Marketing Assistance which is one of the components of National Handloom Development Programme, in order to provide marketing platform to the handloom agencies/weavers to sell their products directly to the consumers, financial assistance is provided to the States/eligible handloom agencies for organising marketing events in domestic as well as overseas markets. During 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, 53 numbers of expos have been sanctioned and Rs. 7.65 crores has been released so far for the Assam State.
