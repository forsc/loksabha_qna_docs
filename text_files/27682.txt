                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Agreement with International Expert for Biofuel




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has fixed any target for the generation of biofuel inthe country including in Bihar;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has signed any agreement with internationalexperts for biofuel; and(d) if so, the details thereof including the fund sanctioned for the purpose inBihar particularly for sugarmills for the generation of biofuel?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM &NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): National Policy on Biofuels-2018 proposes an indicative target of 20%blending of ethanol in petrol and 5% blending of biodiesel in diesel by 2030 acrossthe country including in Bihar.(c) & (d): Government of India has signed a Memorandum of Understanding(MoU) with Government of Brazil on 24th January 2020 on Bio-EnergyCooperation. The MoU provides a framework to cooperate, inter-alia, in biofuels,bio-electricity and biogas supply chain. India is a member of International EnergyAgency’s Technology Collaboration Programme (TCP) on Bio-Energy. Thiscollaboration programme has been set up with the aim of improving collaborationand information exchange between countries that have national programme in bioenergyresearch, development and deployment. India has been attending thesemeetings and in the process getting benefit of the global development in theprogramme.******
