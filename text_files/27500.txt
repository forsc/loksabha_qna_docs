                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3046






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Swadhar Greh Scheme




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)













                                                    Hemant Tukaram Godse
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)   	whether the Government has launched the Swadhar Greh scheme to support women victims of difficult circumstances in need of institutional support for rehabilitation; (b)   	if so, the details thereof along with the benefits intended under the scheme and status of its implementation since inception; (c)   	whether the construction of houses under Swadhar scheme has been stopped in some States particularly in Uttar Pradesh due to non release of funds by the Union Government; and (d)   	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) and (b) Yes Sir, the Government has launched the Swadhar Greh scheme to support women victims of difficult circumstances in need of institutional support for rehabilitation. The Scheme envisages providing shelter, food, clothing and health as well as economic and social security for the women victims of difficult circumstances. As on 31.03.2021, Swadhar Greh Scheme has been implemented in 30 States/UTs. The details of number of Swadhar Greh and beneficiaries under the Swadhar Greh Scheme since inception is at Annexure I. (c) The construction of houses under Swadhar scheme has not been stopped in States particularly in Uttar Pradesh due to non release of funds by the Union Government. (d) Does not arise.********Annexure IAnnexure referred to in reply of part (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.3046 for reply on 06.08.2021 regarding Swadhar Greh Scheme. The details of number of Swadhar Grehs and beneficiaries under the Swadhar Greh Scheme since inception is as under: Year	Number of Swadhar Homes	Number of Beneficiaries2016-17	551	165302017-18	544	172912018-19	398	126382019-20	417	129082020-21	361	79672021-22	361	7956
