                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1870






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Spilling of Excreta by Aircraft




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken note of the fact that human waste or excreta are spilled on the houses/ residential colonies near airports across the country by the aircrafts while landing at the airports;(b) if so, the reasons for not taking any preventive measures; and(c) whether the Government has issued guidelines to all the companies including aviation companies providing services on airports to ensure non-spilling of waste from tanks collecting human waste or excreta from aircrafts while landing at airports/terminals?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c) Aircraft design does not allow for disposal of human waste or excreta during flight, take off or landing. Human waste or excreta collected in flight is disposed off on landing at the airport in accordance with the procedure laid down by the airlines.;
