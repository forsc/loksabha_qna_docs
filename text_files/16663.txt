                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

396






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Funds for House Construction under PMAY




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Diya Kumari
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the amount of assistance given for construction of houses in rural areas is less than the total amount required, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(b) 	the amount provided under Prime Minister Awas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) during the last two years and the current year including the details of the citizens benefitted thereunder, State/UT-wise;(c) 	whetehr the Government is aware that many States are not releasing the funds and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the amount for individual house construction;(e) 	if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be increased; and(f) 	if not, the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):     In order to achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by 2022, the erstwhile rural housing scheme Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY) has been restructured into Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) w.e.f 1st April, 2016.  Under PMAY-G the beneficiary is provided  unit assistance of Rs.1.20 lakh in plain areas and Rs.1.30 lakh in hilly States, difficult areas and IAP districts for the construction of a house. The Central Government provides assistance under PMAY-G for construction of houses to the beneficiaries.  Some States Tamilnadu, Kerala, Himachal Pradesh, etc. also provide top amount to the beneficiaries. In addition to the Unit Assistance to the PMAY-G beneficiaries, the following benefits are also provided:i.	Up to 90/95 persondays of unskilled labour during house construction is being provided under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme.  ii.	Eligible PMAY-G beneficiaries are also provided Rs.12,000/- for the construction of a toilet under Swachh Bharat Mission (G), MGNRES or any other dedicated financing source.iii.	The PMAY-G beneficiaries have a bouquet of options of house designs according to local geo-climatic conditions, using locally available construction materials and technologies for construction of  houses. iv.	Convergence with Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) for LPG connection, Electricity connection under Saubhagya and safe drinking water under  Ja Jeevan Mission, etc.,  (b):    The details of release of Central share to the States/UTs during the last two years and current year and houses allocated  to States/UTs are given at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.  (c):      As per the Framework for Implementation of PMAY-G , State has to transfer the Central funds received in the State Consolidated Funds within 15 days to the State Nodal Account of the scheme at State level.  Next instalment of the Central Share is not released until and unless corresponding matching State Share is released by the respective State/UT. (d) to (f)    At present, there is no proposal under the consideration of the Ministry for  increasing the Unit Assistance for PMAY-G beneficiaries. *******
