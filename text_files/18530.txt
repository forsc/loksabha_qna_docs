                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2294






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Ayush Hospitals in Andhra Pradesh




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the number and details of Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homeopathy (AYUSH) hospitals functioning in the country including Andhra Pradesh;(b) the number and details of staff strength, including doctors, and the present position in each of the traditional systems of medicine, State/UT-wise, hospital-wise, stream-wise;(c) the corrective measures taken by the Government to supplement the efforts of various State Governments to promote AYUSH in the country, State/UT-wise including Andhra Pradesh;(d) whether the Government proposes to set up a 50-bedded AYUSH hospital in Andhra Pradesh; and(e) if so, the details thereof, stream-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	The number of Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homeopathy hospitals functioning in the country including Andhra Pradesh is furnished at Annexure. (b):	Since, public health is a State subject, the details of staff strength, including doctors and the present position in each of the traditional systems of medicine, State/UT-wise, hospital-wise, stream-wise is not maintained by the Central Government.(c):	The Ministry is supplementing the efforts of the State Governments including Andhra Pradesh to promote AYUSH system through Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), which inter-alia envisages better access to AYUSH services, strengthening of AYUSH educational institutions, facilitate the enforcement of quality control of Ayurveda, Siddha and Unani & Homoeopathy (ASU & H) drugs and sustainable availability of ASU & H raw-materials by promoting medicinal plants in the States/UTs.(d) & (e):	As public health is a State subject, setting up of 50 bedded AYUSH Hospitals in Andhra Pradesh comes under the purview of respective State Government. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) there is a provision for financial assistance for setting up of upto 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital. Under NAM, two integrated AYUSH Hospitals upto 50 bedded have already been approved in the State of Andhra Pradesh at Kakinada & Visakhapatnam as per the proposal received from them through State Annual Action Plan. Further, Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 792.31 lakhs and Rs. 331.37 lakhs for these two hospitals.
