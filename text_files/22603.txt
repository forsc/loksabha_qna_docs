                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3773






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Drone Use Permission




Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has granted conditional exemption for Remotely Piloted Aircraft Sytem (RPAS) drones, usage to the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (MoAFW);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has allowed drone deployment by the Agriculture Ministry for remote sensing data collection in Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) only; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The Government has granted conditional exemption to the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmer''s Welfare (MoAFW) from the Rule 15 A of the Aircraft Rules, 1937 and the provisions contained under Section 3, Series X, Part- I  of Civil Aviation Requirements (CAR) to use Remotely Piloted Aircraft Systems (RPAS) for locust control vide order No. AV-22011/2/2020-SDnIT-MoCA dated 21 May 2020.;(c) & (d) Yes, Sir. The Government has granted conditional exemption to the MoAFW from the Rule 15 A of the Aircraft Rules, 1937 and the provisions contained under Section 3, Series X, Part- I  of Civil Aviation Requirements (CAR) to use RPAS for collection of remote sensing data in agricultural areas of 100 districts for Gram Panchayat level yield estimation under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojna (PMFBY) vide order No. AV-29017/46/2020-SDIT-MOCA dated 18 Feb 2021.;
