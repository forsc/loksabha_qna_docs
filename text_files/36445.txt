                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3834






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






New Ayurveda College




Gautam Gambhir













                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering any plan or proposal to set up new Ayurveda colleges in the National Capital Territory of Delhi;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	 the number of Ayurvedic Colleges functioning in the country State/UT-wise including Maharashtra; and(d)	the total number of students pursuing post graduate courses in these colleges, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)No Sir, at present No proposal to set up new Ayurveda Colleges in the National Capital Territory of Delhihas been received for the academic session 2022-23.(b)    In view of (a), information is Nil.(c) At present there are four hundred forty nine (449) AyurvedicCollegesfunctioning in the country including Maharashtra. The State-wise/UT-wisedetails are placed at Annexure-I. (d) The Number of Post-Graduate (PG) Intake Capacity in these Ayurveda Colleges for the academic session 2021-22 is attached as Annexure-II.****
