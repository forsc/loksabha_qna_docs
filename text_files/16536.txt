                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

329






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Cultivation of Crops with Input Subsidies




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	whether the Ministry is aware of the guiding methodology adopted by the Government of Telangana by asking its farmers to grow only those crops to get input subsidy and MSP; (b):	if so, whether it has also come to the notice of the Ministry that farmers, as advised by the State Government through its local agriculture extension officer, could earn 25-30% more on crops asked by State Government to grow; (c):	Whether the Ministry is also aware that there is 98% increase in paddy production; 95% in maize and 74% in cotton in a year; and (d):	 if so, will the Ministry consider adopting this methodology all over the country to get more production and help farmers to earn more?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	 As per inputs received from the Government of Telangana, the Government of Telangana has introduced regulated farming from Kharif 2020 season where farmers were encouraged to grow various crops based on market demand of various agricultural commodities. (c):	In Telangana, for Kharif 2020 crops, so far only the Green Gram has been harvested. (d):	 The Ministry of Agriculture is providing assistance to all State Governments including Telangana for various interventions under its schemes for enhancing crop production and productivity.*****
