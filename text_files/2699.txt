                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2454






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Employment Opportunities in Steel Plants




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total number of persons provided employment by the Steel sector in the country during the last five years, State-wise;(b)	whether the Government proposes to set up any steel plant in the State of Haryana to increase employment opportunity in the State; and(c)	if so, the time by when it is likely to be set up and the location of the proposed plants?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	Steel being a deregulated sector, specific decision on recruitment of employees in private steel sector is taken by individual steel companies/investors on the basis of their requirement. However, state-wise details of employment provided in public sector during last five years and current year is given below:-Steel Authority of India Ltd (SAIL)State (Plant/Unit)	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 (upto May,2019)Chhattisgarh  (Bhilai  Steel  Plant)	553	446	519	159	85	0West Bengal (Durgapur Steel Plant, Alloy Steel Plant,IISCO Steel Plant,Raw Material Division,Central Marketing Organisation,Collieries,Environment Management Division, Growth Division)	1108	938	471	100	154	5Jharkhand (Bokaro Steel Plant,SAIL Refractory Unit,Research & Development Centre for  Iron & Steel,Central of Engineering & Technology,Management Training Institute,SAIL Safety Organisation,Central Coal Supply Organisation)	620	145	328	171	82	17Contd……../2- -: 2 :-Orissa (Rourkela  Steel  Plant)	348	532	107	33	120	22Tamil Nadu (Salem  Steel Plant)	6	53	0	2	0	0Karnataka(Visvesverya  Iron  & Steel Plant)	0	10	10	0	0	0Maharashtra (Chandrapur Ferro Alloy Plant)6	73	0	0	0	0Total 	2641	2197	1435	465	441	44Source: SAIL     Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Ltd. (RINL)State (Plant/Unit)	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20(upto June,2019)Andhra Pradesh (Vishakhapatnam Steel Plant)	180	289	586	72	759	277Source: RINL (b)	No, Sir.(c)	Does not arise in view of (b) above.********
