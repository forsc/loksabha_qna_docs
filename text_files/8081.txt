                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1780






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Linking of Motor Insurance Premium with Traffic Violations




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to link motor insurance premium with traffic violations and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether any committee/working group has been constituted for the purpose; and(c) 	if so, the salient terms of reference and the time given to the committee to submit its report?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) No such proposal is under consideration with the Ministry.*****
