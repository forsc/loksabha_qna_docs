                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Environmental Clearance for Coal Mines




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether environmental clearance to start several coal mines located in Jharkhand have not been issued;(b)  if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of coal mines issued environmental clearance along with the number of coal mines for which such clearance is pending in Jharkhand during the year 2020-21?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (c) : 14 Projects in Jharkhand are yet to be issued environmental clearance till now. During 2020-21, environmental clearance has been issued for 11 projects of Jharkhand. ***
