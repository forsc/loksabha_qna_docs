                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2206






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Khadi Products in Railways




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Railways has any proposal to use Khadi Towels, Bed Sheet, Pillow Covers in all trains running in the country in order to popularise Khadi products among people; (b)	if so, the money to be spent by the Railways on Khadi products during the year 2020-21; and(c)	the amount of money spent by the Ministry of Railways on Khadi products during last four years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) No, Sir.  Bed sheets, pillow cover and towel are provided for use of the passengers only in AC sleeper class coaches (I AC, II AC, III AC).  As per existing policy, these items are procured from Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) to the extent KVIC can supply.  (b) Does not arise in view of (a) above.  However, railway will continue to procure Khadi products from KVIC as per existing policy, to the extent KVIC can supply.(c) The amount spent by the Ministry of Railways on all Khadi products during last four years is as below:-Year	Amount2015-16	`55.02 Cr.2016-17	`65.50 Cr.2017-18	`54.71 Cr.2018-19	`83.38 Cr.Total	`258.61 Cr.*****
