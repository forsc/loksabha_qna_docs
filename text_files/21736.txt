                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2865






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Innovation and R&D under National Research Foundation




Manne Srinivas Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     the funds allocated and steps being taken for Innovation and R&D under the National Research Foundation and the criteria fixed and the modalities worked out to ensure that the overall research ecosystem of the country is strengthened with focus on identified national-priority thrust areas;(b)     the State-wise details of the implementation of the NRF scheme along with funds sanctioned/utilised so far since its inception;(c)     whether the fund allocatons to states/UTs have been increased to meet the demands/targets of each State under NRF; and(d)     if so, the details thereof State/UT-wise as on date and if not the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)?????????  ??  ??????  ??????  ??????, ??????? ?? ????????????  ?????? ?? ?????? ???????  ??????(??. ???? ?????) (a)     Yes Sir, Steps for establishing a National Research Foundation (NRF) have been initiated. NRF aims to catalyse, facilitate, coordinate, seed, grow, and mentor research in academic institutions around the country, particularly at universities and colleges where research capacity is currently in a nascent stage. This will be first of its kind foundation to promote research and development across the country. The Committee on Establishment Expenditure (CEE) approved the proposal for creation of National Research Foundation at a total cost of Indian Rupees 50,000.00 crore over a period of 5 years commencing from 2021-22. (b) to (d)     question does not arise as NRF is yet to be established.
