                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1508






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Catering Licenses




Arvind Ganpat Sawant













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether representations have been received since 1st April 2017 from legal heirs of deceased catering vending Licensees of GMU and SMU static units for transfer of licenses in their names; (b) if so, the number of licenses transferred to legal heirs till date, division-wise along with the names of licenses, stations, unit, date of transfer of licences; and (c) whether, in some cases, transfer of licenses to legal heirs has not been made till date, if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): Yes, Sir. (b): 75 licenses have been transferred to legal heirs since 1st April 2017 till date and the details like division-wise names of licensees, stations, unit, date of transfer of licenses, etc. are appended.(c): Transfer of license to legal heir is done as per the extant policies. In cases where eligibility criteria are not met or required documentation is not submitted, transfer of license is not considered.	*****
