                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2858






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Railway Hospitals




Pritam Gopinath Munde













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways proposes to bring the entire Railway Health System on a single architecture to provide faster, seamless and hassle-free healthcare to its beneficiaries;(b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the names of the agencies likely to be assigned this project and the criteria fixed for identification and selection of agencies for the said purpose; (d) the number of railway hospitals and beneficiaries who are likely to be digitized; and (e) the details of the expenditure likely to be incurred and the time by which the entire work on this project will be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e): Yes, Sir.  Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) has been implemented on Railway  Hospitals  with  RailTel Corporation of India Limited as executing agency.  The  e-Sushruta  HMIS Software of C-DAC (Centre for Development of Advance Computing) has been deployed for the same.	The facility is for all Railway Hospitals and Health Units over Indian Railways and eligible Railway Medical beneficiaries. 	The sanctioned estimate of Pan-India Railway implementation work of HMIS is ?63.28 Crore.  	*****
