                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Flights to Tirupati under UDAN Scheme




Adala Prabhakara Reddy













                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched any flights to Tirupati under the UDAN scheme;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of flights being operated in the State of Andhra Pradesh under the said scheme, till date;(d) whether the Government is planning on allocating new routes thereunder to the State of Andhra Pradesh; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (e): A statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT IN RESPECT OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 159 REGARDING "FLIGHTS TO TIRUPATI UNDER UDAN SCHEME" TO BE ANSWERED ON 11.02.2021.;; ; (a) and (b): Routes from Tirupati connecting to Kolhapur, Hubli, Belgaum & Kalaburgi which were awarded under UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik), have already been operationalized.  (c): Details of  UDAN flights connecting airports in Andhra Pradesh operationalized under UDAN so far, are attached as Annexure-A.; ; (d) and (e): Airports Authority of India (AAI), the Implementing Agency has recently conducted 4th round of bidding under UDAN. Routes connecting unserved Kurnool airport to Vishakapatnam, Bengaluru and Chennai have been awarded recently by the Implementing Agency in Phase II of round 4.0.
