                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3001






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






RGSA Scheme




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) is being implemented to support for economic development and income enhancement and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the details of provisions made for socio-economic development under the said scheme; and (c)	whether the Government has set any target to achieve under the RGSA scheme, and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL)(a)	Yes, Sir. The  Centrally  Sponsored  scheme  of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) being implemented w.e.f. 2018-19,  inter-alia provides financial support to States/ UTs  for micro projects to be undertaken by Gram Panchayats (GPs)/clusters of GPs  for Economic Development & Income Enhancement in the form of viability gap funding for micro projects catering to subject areas enlisted in the Eleventh Schedule of the constitution for devolution to Panchayats based on the merit of the proposal and its viability and sustainability.  (b)	The primary objective of the scheme of RGSA is to develop governance capability of Panchayats to deliver on Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) relating to poverty, public health, nutrition, education, gender, sanitation, drinking water, livelihood generation etc. which are in the realm of Panchayats, thereby helping in socio economic development of the people living in the Panchayat area.(c)	Since scheme is demand driven in nature, hence no such target has been fixed.****
