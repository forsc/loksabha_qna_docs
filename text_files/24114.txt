                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

111






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Halt on Pucca Road due to Pipeline work




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether it is a fact that 55 feet unmelted road from Delhi Dwarka Mor toBhagwati Garden is not being converted into a pucca road due to ongoingpipeline work of Indian Oil Corporation, if so, the reasons therefor;(b) Whether the land has been acquired by Indian Oil Corporation along with thedetails of conditions laid down at the time of land acquisition;(c) Whether the Indian Oil Corporation proposes to lay tiles etc. on this unmetalledroad for the convenience of the people at this place;(d) If so, the time by which it is likely to be done and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e) The steps taken by the concerned department to address the inconveniencebeing faced by the people using this unmetalled road?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRIRAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (e) The RoU (Right of User) from Delhi Dwarka Mor to Bhagwati Garden wasacquired by IOCL in 1980 as per provision of PMP (Petroleum and MineralsPipelines) Act 1962. Two strategic Oil pipelines i.e. Mathura Jalandhar Pipeline andPanipat Bijwasan ATF (Aviation Turbine Fuel) Pipeline are passing through this 55 ftwide stretch. For accessibility for maintenance activities, safety and security of theseimportant pipelines, the area under this RoU is maintained as encumbrance free inun-metalled/ un-tiled condition which is as per the provisions mentioned in Section 9of PMP Act of 1962 prohibiting permanent construction in the RoU
