                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

577






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Adoption of Child




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of in-country adoptions during the last five years till date, age-wise and gender-wise; (b)	the number of infants below the age of one year adopted during the said period; and(c)	the period taken for declaring a child legally free for adoption? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a)       State-wise number of in-country adoptions age-wise and gender-wise during last five years period as on 31.01.2022 is at Annexure-I. (b)       The number of infants below the age of one year adopted during said period is at Annexure-II. (c)       As per Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015, which provides that Child Welfare Committee declares the surrendered child as legally free for adoption after the expiry of sixty days from the date of surrender and in case of orphan or abandoned child, within a period of two months from the date of production of the child, for children who are up to two years of age and within four months for children above two years of age.
