                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4092






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Development of Small Cities




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to provide any central assistance to the States for developing small cities of the country on the lines of big cities particularly in the States of Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal; and(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/ city-wise and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	 & (b) No, Sir. The process of selection of 100 Cities under Smart Cities Mission (SCM) has been concluded through 4 rounds of selection from January, 2016 to June, 2018 and there is no proposal under consideration of the Government to add any more cities under SCM, at this juncture.                          *****
