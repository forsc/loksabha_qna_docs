                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3084






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Increasing Seats in Kendriya Vidyalayas




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to open a Kendriya Vidyalaya school in each districts of Tamil Nadu; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has any proposal to increase the seat strength of each classes of bifurcated Kendriya Vidyalaya schools in Tamil Nadu; (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (e) 	whether the Government is aware that there is a huge demand from the people of Nagapattinum and Mayiladuthurai districts to open a Kendriya Vidyalaya school in both these districts; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Opening of new Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) is a continuous process. Proposals for opening of new KVs are considered only if sponsored by Ministries or Departments of the Government of India / State Governments / Union Territories (UTs) Administrations, committing resources, including land, temporary accommodation etc. as per prescribed norms of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) for setting up a new KV. The proposals received from various sponsoring authorities for opening of new KVs complete in all respects have to compete with other proposals under the “Challenge Method”.(c) & (d) There is no such proposal under consideration. (e) & (f) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has informed that no proposals in the prescribed proforma have been received for opening of new KVs at Nagapattinam and Mayiladuthurai districts of Tamilnadu. *****
