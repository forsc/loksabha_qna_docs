                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Commissioning of Thawe-Chapra Rail Line




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether third line commissioning of Thawe-Chapra, Maharajganj and Captanganj is pending since long despite repeated requests to complete it;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons for delay; (c)	whether there is alleged nexus between private bus operators and Railway officials to not complete the work so that more private buses can run, if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether the Railways has fixed responsibility on the construction department for not completing the third line commissioning and if so, the details thereof; and (e)	whether any time frame has been fixed for completion of this work and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): There is no sanctioned work of third line from Thawe-Chapra and Maharajganj- Kaptanganj. Hence, commissioning of these sections as third line does not arise.  (b) to (e): Do not arise.*****
