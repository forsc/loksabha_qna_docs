                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3597






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Number of Universities




Debasree Chaudhuri














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Universities at present run by the Government in the country and the number of students enrolled therein ;(b) the total number of private Universities/Colleges at present in the country and the students enrolled therein;(c) the total number of students belonging to reserved categories enrolled in private Universities/Colleges; and(d) the details of safeguards in the National Education Policy, 2020 which ensures higher education to students from marginalised, disadvantaged and under-represented groups?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(Smt. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a): The details of total number of Universities run by the Government in the country and the number of students enrolled therein are available at Annexure-I.(b) & (c): The details of total number of Private Universities/Colleges in the country and the category wise details of the number of students enrolled therein are available at Annexure-II.(d): The National Education Policy, 2020 aims to ensure that no child loses any opportunity to learn and excel because of the circumstances of birth or background. It proposes special emphasis to be given on Socially and Economically Disadvantaged Groups (SEDGs). In higher education, it aims at addressing specifically lack of knowledge of higher education opportunities, economic opportunity cost of pursuing higher education, financial constraints, admission processes, geographical and language barriers and lack of appropriate student support mechanisms, enhancing access by establishing more high-quality HEIs in aspirational districts, Scholarship/Fellowships for SEDGs/Girls/Divyangs, etc******
