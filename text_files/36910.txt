                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4071






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Relief Package for Revival of Tourism in Himachal Pradesh




Pratibha Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether foreign and domestic tourism in India and specially in Himachal Pradesh has been on decline due to Covid-19 pandemic during the last two years;(b)	if so, the steps taken to revive foreign and domestic tourism in the country and in the State of Himachal Pradesh; (c)	whether the Government has drawn any plans to bring back foreign and domestic tourists after the slowdown in Corona Cases in the country and in Himachal Pradesh; and(d)	the details of relief package announced and disbursed to the stressed hospitality sector in the country and specially in Himachal Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM					              (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Yes, Sir. Tourism in India including in Himachal Pradesh was severely affected due to Covid-19 pandemic during the last two years. However, further to the improvement in the Covid-19 situation in the country Domestic Tourism has already shown signs of recovery in many parts of the country.(b) to (d): The detail of various fiscal and non-fiscal relief measures announced by the Government of India, which are expected to assist the tourism industry in India, including in Himachal Pradesh are given at Annexure.*******
