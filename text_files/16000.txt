                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4434






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Seats in Medical Colleges




Ramulu Pothuganti













                                                    Mala Roy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of the UG/PG Medical seats available in all the Government/private medical colleges in the country, Government/private medical college-wise, State/ UT-wise;(b) whether it is a fact that the cost of getting medical seat in the Deemed University/private medical college/University is charged very high, if so, the details thereof;(c) the reasons for charging higher price for medical seat by the Deemed University/Private University;(d) whether the Government is considering to regulate and reduce the price for medical seat in the Deemed Universities/Private Universities/Colleges, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e) whether any committee has been constituted by the Government to look into this aspect, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	As per information received from the Board of Governors in supersession of Medical Council of India (BoG-MCI), a total of 541 (278 Government + 263 Private) Medical Colleges with 82926 MBBS seats and 44136 PG seats are available in the country. State-wise break up is at Annexure. (b) to (e):  In the case of Government medical colleges, the respective State Governments are responsible for fixation of fee and in the case of private unaided medical colleges, the fee structure is decided by the Committee set up by the respective State Government under the Chairmanship of a retired High Court Judge in pursuance of the directions of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India. Clause (i) of Sub-section (1) of Section 10 of National Medical Commission Act, 2019 provides for framing of guidelines for determination of fees and all other charges in respect of fifty percent of seats in private medical institutions and deemed to be universities which are governed under the provisions of the Act.
