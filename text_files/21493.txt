                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Fund Requirement through Borrowings




Manne Srinivas Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                








                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps being taken to meet fund requirement through borrowings for financing the National Project of Railways along with the repayment liability in the last five years and the current year as on date; and (b)	the future action plan chalked out in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b) : Funds for National Projects are provided through Gross Budgetary Support from General Exchequer. For accelerated progress of identified National Projects in 2018-19 and 2019-20, Ministry of Finance approved financing from borrowings under Extra Budgetary Resources (EBR). Accordingly, expenditure of ? 5078.57 crore in 2018-19 and               ? 2498.41 crore in 2019-20 was incurred on National Projects from EBR.There was no repayment liability on this account during the last five years and the current year (2020-21).  Repayment of EBR for National Projects will start from 2021-22. Provision for repayment and lease charges for the same in 2021-22 has been made by Ministry of Finance under Gross Budgetary Support. For 2021-22, ? 12985 crore have been provided for National Projects from Gross Budgetary Support.***
