                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2484






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Implementation of MSP




Sangeeta Azad














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                          (a) whether Minimum Support Price (MSP) is implemented in various States in                             proportion to population;  (b)  if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c)  if not, the reasons therefor; and  (d)  the time by which it is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):     Minimum Support Prices (MSP) is determined for the country as a whole and not region or state-specific. The objective is to promote regionally differentiated production strategy and encourage an efficient state of agricultural production in the country. However, region specific parameters are kept in view by the Commission for Agricultural Cost and Prices (CACP) while formulating price policy for agricultural commodities. Food Corporation of India (FCI) procures cereals. The Government implements an Umbrella Scheme “Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay SanraksHan Abhiyan” (PM-AASHA), comprising Price Support Scheme (PSS), Price Deficiency Payment Scheme (PDPS), and pilot Scheme of Private Procurement and Stockist Scheme (PPSS) for the procurement of pulses, oilseeds and copra.The Cotton Corporation of India (CCI) is the central nodal agency for undertaking price support operations and National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Ltd. (NAFED) supplements the efforts of CCI for cotton procurement. Jute Corporation of India (JCI) is the Price Support Agency (PSA) for jute.                                                            **********
