                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1949






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Yojana




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        the total number of persons in the country who have received benefits under “Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Yojana”; (b)        the total number of persons in Telangana who have got benefits under the said Yojana;  and (c)        the number and details of the persons in Kamareddy district receiving benefits under the said Yojana?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI) (a) :         The Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented by the Ministry in identified Minority Concentration Areas (MCAs) with the objective of  developing socio economic infrastructure and basic amenities for the welfare of the people living in the area.    The MCAs have been identified on the basis of concentration of notified minority population i.e. 25% or more and backwardness in terms of socio-economic and/or basic amenities as compared to national average.  Under the PMJVK 1300 MCAs have been identified in 308 Districts (as per Census 2011) of the country including 12 MCAs of Telangana.   The infrastructure projects created in the identified MCAs under the scheme are used by the entire population of the area.   The projects sanctioned under the scheme till 31.3.2020, including individual beneficiary projects are Residential Schools - 168;  New School Buildings - 3041,  Degree Colleges - 39, Hostels - 1319,  Additional Class Rooms - 42505, Additional Class Room Blocks - 367,  Smart Classrooms & Teaching Aids - 10053,  Industrial Training Institutes - 210, Polytechnics - 56,  Health Centres - 5252, Anganwadi Centres - 41105, Working Women Hostels - 23, Common Service Centres -136;  Sadbhav Mandaps - 403,  Skill Training (Individuals) – 87278,  Skill Development Centres - 21, Hunar Hubs - 08, Market Sheds – 630, Pucca Houses - 359805, Bicycles – 14960, Toilet Blocks - 7059,  Drinking Water Supply Projects - 51522,  Sports Facilities - 11 etc.  The details are available at www.minorityaffairs.gov.in.  (b) :  With regard to the State of Telangana, Ministry has sanctioned projects of Rs.1151.36 crore till 31.3.2020.  The type of projects sanctioned are  Residential Schools - 54,  School Buildings - 4, Additional Class Rooms – 133,  Teaching Aids – 367,  Hostels - 20, ITIs - 2,  Skill Training (Individuals) – 3975, Health Centres – 9, Anganwadi Centres – 50, Common Service Centres - 11, Bicycles - 2896 etc.  As stated in Para (a) above, the infrastructure projects created under the PMJVK scheme are used by the entire population of the area.     (c) :  Kamareddy District of  Telangana is not an identified MCA under the PMJVK.***
