                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

67






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Food Processing Industries




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of food processing enterprises in the country, category-wise (Large, Medium, and Micro) and State-wise; (b)	the total number of SHGs (Self Help Groups) and Producer Cooperatives involved in food processing, State-wise; and (c)	the details of projects scheduled in Odisha, especially in Kalahandi and Nuapada? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a)  & (b): As per the latest Annual Survey of Industries (2017-18), the total number of Food Processing units/ factories registered and operational in the country are 40,160 and 34,562 respectively. The state wise details of these food processing units/ factories are at Annexure-I. The Ministry of Rural Development has informed that activity wise data of Self Help Groups (SHGs), including that for food processing, are not maintained under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM). As per NRLM database, there are  total of  67,12,050 SHGs in the country, including 4,45,012 SHGs in Odisha.(c):	Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a Central Sector  scheme PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector across the country including in Odisha. PMKSY is demand driven and financial assistance is provided to the eligible applicants selected on the basis of Expression of Interest (EoI) issued from time to time. A total of 11 projects have been/ are being assisted under various component schemes of PMKSY in Odisha, out of which 7 are operational. None of these projects are located in Kalahandi and Nuapada. Scheme wise project status is   at Annexure-II. The Ministry is also implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme- PM Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises Scheme (PM FME) with One District One Product (ODOP) approach. ODOP for Kalahandi and Nuapada is Fish Based Products and Millet Based Products respectively. A total of 4,709 micro enterprises are targeted to be assisted in Odisha under the scheme during five years i.e.  2020-21 to 2024-25. *****
