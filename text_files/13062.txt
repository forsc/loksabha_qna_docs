                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Rural Roads under PMGSY




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the average length in kilometres of rural roadsconstructed in the country under Pradhan Mantri GramSadakYojana (PMGSY) during the last three years andthe current year so far;(b) 	the percentage of villages and habitationsconnected by rural roads in March 2016, State-wise;and(c)	the percentage of villages and habitationsconnected by rural roads as of now, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in reply to Parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No *174 due for answer on 03.03.2020 (a): The length of rural roads constructed and average length of road constructed per day under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years and the current year till 27th February, 2020 are as under:- Year (s)	Length of Roads constructed (km)	Average Length  per day (km)2016-17	                47,457	                                  130.012017-18	                48,715	                                  133.462018-19	                49,041	                                  134.352019-20 (27.02.2020)	19,678.13	                           59.45 (b)to (c):  The unit for PMGSY is a Habitation and not a Revenue village or a Panchayat.
