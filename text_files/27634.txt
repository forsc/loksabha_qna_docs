                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3420






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Schemes for Latent Talent of Poor Students




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of schemes being implemented by the Government for the development of latent talent of poor students at the school and college level in the country particularly in rural areas;(b) whether the Government has implemented any schemes/projects for setting up of Model Degree Colleges for improving education in educationally backward districts of the country;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the quantum of funds earmarked for the purpose; and(d) the steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to improve the infrastructure/quality of education in these educationally backward districts?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN) (a) The Pradhan Mantri Innovative Learning Programme has been started to identify and encourage talented children to enrich their skills and knowledge. In centres of excellence across the country, gifted children are mentored and nurtured by renowned experts in different areas, so that they can reach their full potential. The program aims to cover two areas namely Science and Arts. Around 60 students are selected broadly from Class 9 to Class 12 all over the country.(b) to (d): Model Degree Colleges (MDCs) have been sanctioned in 194 Educationally Backward Districts (EBDs) of which UGC had sanctioned 64 and 130 MDCs were sanctioned under the centrally sponsored scheme of Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA). The State wise details of MDCs approved under RUSA is Annexed.MDCs aim at increasing access to higher education in EBDs, aspirational districts, unserved and underserved districts where access to higher education is low due to lack of higher educational institutions. ****
