                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1226






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the achievements of the Swadesh Darshan Scheme as of date; (b) 	whether there are plans to expand the Scheme to include more theme based tourist circuits; (c) 	whether there are plans to extend the ongoing/ planned theme based circuits to Jharkhand; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	For integrated development of Theme-Based Tourist Circuits in the country, Ministry of Tourism has launched Swadesh Darshan Scheme in 2014-15. Since its launch in January 2015; the Ministry has sanctioned 76 projects for Rs.5813.55 Crores, out of which 7 projects are complete and 26 projects are nearing completion.  Sanctioning of new projects in the scheme is under review. Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned a project ‘Development of Dalma- Chandil- Getalsud- Betla National Park- Mirchaiya–Netarhat’, Jharkhand under Eco Circuit Theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme for Rs.52.72 Crore in 2018-19.********
