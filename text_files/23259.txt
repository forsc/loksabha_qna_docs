                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Funds for Protection of Unprotected Heritage Structures




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of unprotected heritage monuments/sites in various parts of the country, State/UT-wise;\(b)	whether the Government provides funds for maintenance of unprotected heritage structures/ monuments in the country;(c)	if so, the details thereof during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh and if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government has received any proposal from Uttar Pradesh regarding allocation of funds for protection of heritage structures; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a), (b) and (c)	Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) looks after 3693 centrally protected monuments/sites notified under Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958 all over the country.  Budget allocated to ASI provides for conservation and preservation of centrally protected monuments/sites. However there is also some provision of funds for unprotected heritage structures/ monuments under sub HoA 22.01.27 - Minor Works (Restoration of Unprotected Monuments) allocated by Ministry of Culture. Details of allocation and expenditure during last two years and current year is as under (amount is in lakhs) :Year	Allocation Rs.	Expenditure Rs.2018-19	150.00	99.002019-20	30.00	15.432020-21	70.00	30.00(d)&(e)	Yes sir. A proposal for allocation of funds for conservation and development of selected Centrally Protected Monuments/Sites and museums has been received from Govt. of Uttar Pradesh and is being considered.*****
