                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

754






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Provision of Potable Water




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has proposed any scheme for the State Governments to provide potable water to the public; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the funds allocated by the Union Government to the State Governments in this regard during the last five years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) Yes Sir.(b) Government of India has approved Jal Jeevan Mission(JJM) with an outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore, which aims at providing potable drinking water at the service level of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) to every rural household of the country through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024. (c) The fund allocated to the States/ UTsunder erstwhile National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) (now subsumed under JJM),during the last five years is annexed.****
