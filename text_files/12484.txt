                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1338






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Incidents in JMU & JNU




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken the cognizance of recent violent incident against thestudents in Jamia Milllia University by police and on students sitting on strike by goons insidethe JNU premises; and(b) if so, the reaction of the Government thereon and corrective measures taken by theGovernment to provide safe environment to the students inside the University area?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) and (b): There have been reports of some violence in Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI) andJawaharlal Nehru University (JNU). The Central Universities are statutory autonomousorganization established under an Act of Parliament and are competent to deal with suchmatters appropriately.*****
