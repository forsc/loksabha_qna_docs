                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

813






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Payments of PMAY Beneficiaries




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the amount to be paid to beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) of the Central Government has been paid to the State Governments but the State Governments have not paid this amount to the beneficiaries; (b) 	if so, the number of such States identified by the Central Government; and (c)  	the directions given to such State Governments by the Central Government so that the beneficiaries may likely get the amount under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana and the details thereof, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)to (c) The Ministry has been implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY (U)] since June, 2015. Admissible Central Assistance under the scheme is released in instalments to State/ Union Territories (UT) Governments for transferring it to identified beneficiaries through theirimplementing agencies. State/UT-wise details of Central Assistance released and transferred to the beneficiaries under PMAY (U) as per Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) report till 31.07.2020 is Annexed.           In order to ensure that the benefit of the scheme of PMAY (U) reaches the beneficiaries at the earliest, States/UTs are regularly advised through various means like Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC) meetings, letters, Video Conferences etc. for expeditious utilization of central assistance for the purpose it is released and submission of Utilization Certificates (UCs) thereof. *****
