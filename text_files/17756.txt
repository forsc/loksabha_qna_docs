                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1483






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Features of PM KISAN Scheme




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	 the salient features of the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme; (b):	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this Scheme within Tamil Nadu so far, district-wise; (c):	the details of the number of beneficiaries covered under this scheme within the district of North Chennai so far; (d):	whether the Government has transferred any amount into the farmers’ accounts during the period of lockdown within Tamil Nadu; (e):	if so, the details thereof, district-wise and if not, the reasons therefor; (f):	 whether the Government is planning to transfer some more installments in their accounts in advance due to COVID-19 crisis; and (g):	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme is a Central Sector DBT Scheme, under which, financial assistance of Rs.6000/- per annum is provided to all landholding farmer families across the country, subject to certain exclusion criteria relating to higher income status, to enable them to take care of expenses related to agriculture and allied activities as well as domestic needs.  The amount is transferred in three 4-monthly installments of Rs.2000/- directly into the bank accounts of the beneficiary farmers identified by the State/UT Governments.  Payment is done on the basis of Aadhar seeded data of beneficiaries, except for the State/UTs of Assam, Meghalaya, Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh, which have been given exemption in this regard upto 31st March, 2021.Contd…..2/-- 2 -(b):	Data of only financial benefit released under the Scheme are maintained. The district-wise details of the benefit released to farmers in Tamil Nadu (as on 16th September, 2020) is given in Annexure-I.(c):	Under the PM-KISAN Scheme, 100 beneficiaries have been benefitted so far within the district of Chennai which includes North Chennai.(d) & (e):	The district-wise details of benefit released under PM-KISAN Scheme to the State of Tamil Nadu during the period of lockdown (till 16th September, 2020) is given in Annexure-II(f) & (g):	No Sir. There is no proposal to transfer instalments under the Scheme in advance.  However, financial benefit was released in the beginning of the 4-monthly periods of April-July, 2020 as well as August-November, 2020 so as to enable early cash availability to the beneficiary farmers during the Covid-19 pandemic period.
