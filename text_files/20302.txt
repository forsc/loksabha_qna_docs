                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1441






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






RCC Bridge on Jaljali River




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that the Project Concept Paper for Construction of an RCC Bridge over river Jaljali connecting Aloopati in Barpeta  District and Nagarbera in Kamrup District has been submitted to the Government of Assam vide letter CE/STBR/NESIDS/41/2018-19/28 and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the government has assured that follow up action on the Project Concept Report will be taken and the bridge will be constructed; and(c)	if so, the time likely to be taken for the bridge to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a) to (c)  Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region has not received Project Concept Paper for Construction of an RCC Bridge over river Jaljali connecting Aloopati in Barpeta District and Nagarbera in Kamrup District from the Government of Assam.*****
