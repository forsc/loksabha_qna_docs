                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Notified Skilled Workers




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether any agreement has been signed with other countries regarding the ‘notified skilled workers’;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the names of the notified sectors where skilled labourers are likely to be sent; and(d) the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	The Government signed a Memorandum of Cooperation (MoC) in January 2021, with Japan for Cooperation on “Specialised Skilled Workers”. The MoC will help to promote the movement of skilled workers from India to Japan. (c) & (d)	The MoC covers 14 sectors under which skilled workers from India who meet the work skills standards and Japanese language requirements would be eligible for employment in Japan. These include nursing care, material processing, industrial machinery manufacturing, electric and electronic information, construction, shipbuilding and ship-related industry, automobile maintenance, aviation, lodging, agriculture, fisheries, food & beverages manufacturing, food services industry and janitorial services. ***
