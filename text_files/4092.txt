                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3846






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Drug Price Control




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that the measures being taken by the Government since the year 2011, for drug price control have resulted in a drastic slowdown in launching of new drugs in the country by drug manufacturing companies; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; (c) whether the Government proposes to impose custom duty on some life saving drugs; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)                               (a) & (b): The Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO) grants permission to manufacture/ import new drugs under the provisions of the Drugs and Cosmetics Rules, 1945. As per the information available from the CDSCO, there has not been any drastic slowdown in launching of new drugs by drug manufacturing companies since coming into effect of the new Drugs (Prices Control) Order, 2013 (DPCO). The details of number of permissions granted by the CDSCO to manufacture/ import new drugs since the year 2011 are as under:Year	Number of Permissions2011	5262012	3112013	2992014	2542015	1722016	1942017	2122018	239Total	2207(c):	No, Sir. (d):	In view of reply to (c) above, does not arise.xxxxx
