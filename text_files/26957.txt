                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Garib Kalyan Rojgar Abhiyan




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Indian Railways has identified the infrastructure projects which are developed under Garib Kalyan Rozgar Abhiyan; (b) 	if so, the number of such projects identified in Maharashtra;(c)  whether the Indian Railways has generated more than 5.5 lakhs mandays of work under Garib Kalyan Rozgar Abhiyan in selected States;(d) 	if so, the details thereof,  State-wise particularly in Maharashtra;(e) 	the number of migrated workers are engaged till date under the Garib Kalyan Rozgar Abhiyan in Indian Railway; and(f) 	whether the Railways has identified a lot of works for execution by the State Government/local authorities under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme which provide direct and indirect benefits to the rural communities and if so, the number of works done under MGNREGA in Maharashtra including Vashim-Yavatmal, Usmanabad, Ramtek and Parbhani districts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (f):   A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.   *****
