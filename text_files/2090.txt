                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1996






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






New DEMU Trains




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to introduce more Diesel Electric Multiple Unit (DEMU) trains all over India; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any route for DEMU is under consideration in Kerala;(d) if so, the details thereof; (e) whether any new facilities are required for starting DEMU; and(f) if so, the details thereof and the action plan prepared in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b) Introduction of trains including DEMU trains is an on-going process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources etc.(c) & (d) At present, there is no proposal for introduction of DEMU train services serving the stations located in the State of Kerala.(e) No new additional maintenance facility is required for starting DEMU service over Indian Railways.(f) Does not arise.*****
