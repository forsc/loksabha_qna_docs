                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3827






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






NH Projects in Maharashtra




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the details of new National Highway (NH) projects sanctioned for construction in the State of Maharashtra especially in Vidarbha region during the last three years; (b)  whether the Government has received any proposal for construction of new NHs from the State Government during the said period; (c)      if so, the action taken by the Government thereon; and (d) the funds allocated, released and utilised for development/construction of NHs in Maharashtra during each of the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  to ( c)	Details of  proposals received from the State Government  of Maharashtra and sanctioned for construction of  new NHs including those in Vidarbha region during the last three years are as under:S. No.	Year	Number	Amount (Rs. in Crore) 1	2017-18	99	171492	2018-19	9	12693	2019-20	109	2343(d)  	The details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred on the development/construction of NHs in the state of Maharashtra during the last three years are given as below:(Rs. in Crore}S. No.	Year	Allocation* 	Expenditure			State PWD	NHAI1	2017-18	2966	2964	40692	2018-19	7051	7445	69023	2019-2020	10341	10998	6022 *No State wise allocation has been made to National Highways Authority of India (NHAI)*****
