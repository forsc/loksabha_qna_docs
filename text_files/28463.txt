                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Evaluation of the Level of the Higher Education




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any survey or study has been conducted by the Government to evaluate the levelof higher education in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the action plan of the Government to improve the standard of higher education in thecurrent global scenario?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) carry outStudent Satisfaction Survey as part of their accreditation process by which the quality ofhigher education is evaluated in the country. Till now 665 universities and 13370 collegeshave been accredited by NAAC. The Ministry of Education also ranks Higher EducationInstitutions (HEIs) in India every year on quality parameters identified under the NationalInstitutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).(c) The Government of India, together with University Grants Commission (UGC), All IndiaCouncil for Technical Education (AICTE) and higher educational institutions have beenconstantly working through various schemes and initiatives such as Rashtriya UcchatarShiksha Abhiyan (RUSA), Scheme for Trans-disciplinary Research for India’sDeveloping Economy (STRIDE), Consortium for Academic and Research Ethics(CARE), National Mission in Education through ICT (NME-ICT) for improvement ofquality of higher education in the country. UGC has framed the ‘Quality Mandate forHigher Education Institutions in India’ that aims at prompting better employment, sociallyproductive activities, training students in essential soft skills, orientation of teachers andaccreditation of institutions.***
