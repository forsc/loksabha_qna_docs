                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2814






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Monuments and Temples for Inclusion in World Heritage List of UNESCO




P.P. Chaudhary













                                                    Arjunlal Meena
                                                








                                                    Jagdambika Pal 
                                                








                                                    Kaushalendra Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of temples which are in the tentative list and have not been finalized for consideration in the World Heritage List of UNESCO;(b)	the details of monuments which have been considered by UNESCO for the World Heritage List during the last four years, State-wise including Bihar;(c)	whether the Government has proposed any plan for the development, conservation and maintenance of State protected monuments and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government proposes to conduct a comparative study of heritages of historical importance situated in Bihar so that their names could be proposed to UNESCO; and(e)	if so, the extensive details thereof and the name of such heritages?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPOMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Seven (07) temples are in the tentative list. Details are at Annexure - A.(b)	Details are at Annexure - B.(c)	There is no such plan for the development, conservation and maintenance of State protected monuments. However, ASI undertakes conservation of state protected monuments as civil deposit work as and when requested by the State Governments.(d) & (e)	The submission of proposals by the State Governments/ Owner/ Custodian of heritage properties for inclusion in Tentative List of UNESCO based on their Outstanding Universal Value (OUV), authenticity, integrity, as per Operational Guidelines of World Heritage Convention, is an ongoing process. All the proposals received by the Government are assessed on the basis of justification of their OUV, criteria and statements of authenticity, integrity and comparison with other similar properties. Based on the assessments, proposals are forwarded to the World Heritage Centre for their inclusion in the Tentative List of UNESCO.
