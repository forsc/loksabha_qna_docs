                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3063






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






100 Days of Guraranteed Wage Employment




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that in the 100 days of guaranteed wage employment per head programme of the country, an average of only 53 days job has been provided during the last ten months till January 2022;(b) 	if so, the reason therefor and the detailed report including applications received and employment given therein, State-wise; and(c) 	the steps taken by the Government to fulfill the target and generation of employment in rural areas?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c) Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme which provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. It provides livelihood security, i.e. fall back options for livelihood for the rural households, when no better employment opportunity is available.
