                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5199






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Dyeing Units with Zero Liquid Discharge Plant




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE be pleased to state the list of dyeing units in the district of Karur, Namakkal, Erode, Salem, Trippur with and without the Reverse Osmosis (RO) and Zero Waste Discharge Plants? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)As per the information received from the Government of Tamil Nadu, there are no dyeing units under operation without Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) System in the districts of Karur, Namakkal, Erode, Salem  and Tiruppur.  The list of dyeing units with Reverse Osmosis (RO) and  ZLD plants  in Karur, Namakkal, Erode, Salem  and Tiruppur districts is at Annexure I. ***
