                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2118






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






LPG Connections under PMUY




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of households that have been provided LPG connections underUjjwala Scheme so far; and(b) the steps taken by the Government to increase the speed of the scheme in Jaunpurdistrict of Uttar Pradesh; and(c) the time by which the Government intends to provide all rural households in Jaunpurdistrict with LPG connections?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c) Oil Marketing Companies(OMCs) have released about 8 crore LPGconnections under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana(PMUY) including 3.05 Lakh inJaunpur District of Uttar Pradesh. Further, Hon''ble Finance Minister, during her budgetspeech on 1st February, 2021, has announced to provide 1 crore more LPGconnections under PMUY in addition to 8 crore LPG connections already released.Jaunpur district is also covered under these additional connections as per norms. Thosehouseholds which are not eligible under Ujjwala can get LPG connection under thenormal provisions.--------
