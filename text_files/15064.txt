                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3816






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Agricultural Colleges




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the number of Agriculture Colleges is lesser than the number of Engineering/Medical Colleges in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; (c)	the details of the Agriculture Colleges opened till date and proposed to be opened during the next year in the country, State/UT-wise; (d)	whether the Government has received a number of proposals from various States including Uttar Pradesh for opening Agriculture Universities/Colleges; (e)	if so, the number of such proposals received during the last three years and the current year, State/ UT-wise; and (f)	the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????          (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	Agriculture including Agricultural Education is state subject under the Constitution of India.  Therefore, data on Agriculture colleges is not maintained at Government of India level.	(d) to (f):	No, Sir.	*******
