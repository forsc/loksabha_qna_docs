                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4123






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Formulation of Startup Policies




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the State Governments that have formulated Startup policies at present; and(b) 	the details of the top performing States in terms of registration and execution of startups? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ??????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	The details of the State/UT Governments that have formulated Startup policies as on 15th March 2020 are at Annexure-I.(b):	Since the launch of Startup India initiative on 16th January 2016, a total of 28,979 Startups have been recognized by DPIIT as on 1st March 2020. The State/UT wise breakup of these startups is at Annexure-II. DPIIT launched the first edition of State/UT Startup Ranking Framework on 6th February, 2018 to encourage States and UTs to take proactive steps towards strengthening the Startup ecosystem within their jurisdiction. There were 38 action points categorized into 7 broad areas such as Startup Policy and Implementation, Incubation support, Seed Funding, Angel and Venture Funding, Simplification of Regulations, Easing Public Procurement and Awareness & Outreach. The ranking methodology created a healthy competition among States to further learn, share and adopt best practices. 27 States and 3 Union Territories participated in the exhaustive ranking exercise. The final rankings of SRF 2018 were announced on 20th December, 2018 and the States were felicitated by DPIIT. The details of the States/UTs in terms of their performance as per States Ranking Framework 2018 are at Annexure-III. ******
