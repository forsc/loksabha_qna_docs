                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

311






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Training Centres for Bamboo/Cane




Pratima Bhoumik














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to setup more Vocational Training and Skill Development Centres for Cane and Bamboo for their full exploitation, branding, packaging and marketing in India and abroad and if so, the details thereof along with the state-wise details of the new centres; and(b) whether the Government has put a ban on import of finished products and raised the import duty by 25 percent on raw bamboo items, if so, the details thereof and its impact on domestic bamboo?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of NorthEastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a)	National Bamboo Mission has identified three existing institutes viz (i) North East Cane and Bamboo Development Council (NECBDC), Guwahati, (ii) Indian Council of Forest Research & Education (ICFRE), Dehradun and (iii) Kerala Forest Research Institute (KFRI), Peechi, Kerala and NECBDC has established five institutes which impart skill development in cane and bamboo sectors as under:(i)	Training-cum-Production Centre, Ukhrul, Manipur(ii)	Bamboo Technology Park, Jote, Arunachal Pradesh(iii)	Integrated Cane and Bamboo Development Project at Tamenglong, Manipur(iv)	Incubation Centre at Sairang, Aizwal, Mizoram.(v)	Incubation Centre at Hi-Bermiok, Berthang, SikkimAt present, there is no plan to set up more centres.(b)	Government has increased customs duty on bamboo for use in manufacture of agarbati from 10 per cent to 25 per cent, to encourage use of domestic bamboo. Government has not put a ban on import of finished bamboo products.***
