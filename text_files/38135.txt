                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Status of Proposals Received from Odisha under PRASHAD Scheme




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of States included in the “National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive’ (PRASHAD) along with the details thereof; (b)	the status of the proposal received from the Government of Odisha under the said scheme; (c)	whether other religious places are likely to be included in the said scheme in future; and (d)	the details of the funds allocated and spent by the Government under the PRASHAD Scheme across the country including Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                                (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a),(b)&(d):	State-wise details of the projects approved under PRASHAD Scheme on the basis of the project proposals received from the respective State Governments, including the State of Odisha, approved costs and the funds released is available at the Annexure.(c):	Approval of new projects under PRASHAD Scheme is a continuous process which is done on receipt of the projects proposals from the respective State Governments/Union Territory Administrations in adherence to the Scheme Guidelines. *******
