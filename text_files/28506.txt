                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

181






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Development of Ancient Places of Pilgrimage in Nanded




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has received any proposals seeking development of ancient places of pilgrimage in Nanded;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of action being taken by the Government to develop places of pilgrimage in Nanded district;(d)	whether various other places including Nanded are likely to be included in the budget 2022-2023 to promote development of tourist places; and (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 				(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)    (a):	Sir, no proposal from the State Government of Maharashtra for development of ancient places in Nanded is pending in the Ministry of Tourism. (b): 	Does not arise. (c) to (e): 	Sir, development and maintenance of the pilgrimage  and other tourism places, primarily, is the responsibility of the respective State Government/UT Administration. However, Ministry of Tourism also under its “PRASHAD” and “Swadesh Darshan” Schemes provides Central Finance Assistance for development of such places.  The Ministry of Tourism identifies destinations/places and approves projects for development of such places under these schemes, as a continuous process, on the basis of the proposal received from the respective State Government / Union Territory in adherence to the Scheme guidelines. ***********
