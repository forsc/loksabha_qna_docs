                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1960






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Workforce employed in Textile Industry in Tamil Nadu




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of workforce employed in the apparel and textile sectors across the country particularly in Tamil Nadu, as on date; (b) whether the Government has received any complaints with regard to working conditions of employees and steps taken to augment/improve the working conditions of the workforce; and(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the further steps taken by the Government to protect and safeguard the interests of the workforce?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	The textile industry is one of the largest source of employment generation in the country with over 45 million people employed directly which also include a large number of population in Tamil Nadu. The State-wise data of employment related to Textile sector is not maintained.(b) to (d):	No such instance has come to the notice of the Government.  However, Government has been implementing various welfare schemes, to augment/improve the working conditions and protect & safeguard  the interests of the workforce in textile sector. ***
