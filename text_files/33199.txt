                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

68






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Tribal Tourism in Meghalaya




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry has undertaken any action plan to encourage Tribal Tourism in Meghalaya post-Pandemic;(b)	if so, the details of the steps taken and the tribes considered for such an endeavour;(c)	the details of the steps taken to promote tribal culture of North Eastern Region (NER) and Meghalaya in particular;(d)	the details of tourists in NER during the last five years, State and year-wise; and(e)	the investment made in Tourism Sector in NER during the last five years, State and year-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM					(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to ( e) :	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*68 ANSWERED ON 07.02.2022 REGARDING TRIBAL TOURISM IN MEGHALAYA. (a)   to (c):	 Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic tourist destination including tribal tourism in Meghalaya under the “Incredible India” brand and promotional activities are undertaken in Domestic and International markets through its various schemes such as Domestic Promotion & Publicity including Hospitality (DPPH) and Overseas Promotion & Publicity including Market Development Assistance (OPMD) without distinction of any tribe. Post Pandemic, digital promotions are regularly undertaken through the Social Media accounts of the Ministry on different themes such as heritage tourism, tribal tourism, culture tourism, amongst others. Webinars are organised under the overall “Dekho Apna Desh” theme showcasing the diverse culture, heritage, destinations and tourism products of the country, including North East India. The details of webinars being organized focusing on NER   under this series include: Cruising the Brahmaputra River, the World’s Greatest Natural History & Heritage River Cruise, Adventure tourism in NE India, Bailey Trail in Arunachal Pradesh, Northeast India for the immersive traveler, Northeast India: Experience the exclusive villages, Assamese Cuisine- The Gourmet Unexplored, Mystique Nagaland – Land of Culture, Adventure and Festivals. New initiative has been made named Prime Minister’s Development Initiative for North East, (PM-DevINE) to enable livelihood activities for youth and women and filling the gaps in various sectors that will also help tourism growth in North Eastern Region (NER). Details of steps taken in this regard are placed at Annexure I. (d): 	The details of tourists in NER during the last five years, State and year-wise is at Annexure II. (e):	The investment made in Tourism Sector by the Ministry in NER during the last five years, State and year-wise is at Annexure III. *****
