                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3528






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Excise Duty Collection




Abhishek Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Government’s collection of excise duty on petroleum products, progressively from prepandemic period, 2019-20 to the current time; and (b) the details of Government’s expected collection from petroleum products given the price reduction announced on 3rd November?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCESH. PANKAJ CHOUDHARY(a): The total Central Excise Duty, including cesses, collected from petroleum products, from pre-pandemic period, 2019-20 to current period is as below:                                      (In Rs.cr)2019-20	2020-212,23,057	3,72,970(b): Budget Estimates (2021-22) for Union Excise Duty is ?3,35,000 crores. Revised Estimates for current financial year are not finalised.*****
