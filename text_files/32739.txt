                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Action for Breach of Passenger Service System




Dayakar Pasunoori













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the action taken by SITA (an aviation IT company) after breach of its Passenger Service System (PSS) under which data of passengers has been breached through a cyber attack;(b) whether the Government has taken any action on the said IT company and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has allowed SITA to store PSS outside India and if so, the reasons therefor; and(d) whether the Government proposes to replace SITA by other service provider, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a): SITA has confirmed that several specific actions have been taken by them to strengthen various IT systems and processes to avoid recurrence of such incidents.;;(b): This was an incident of Cyber-attack, which was dealt by Air India Limited (AIL) itself.;;(c): After the merger of Air India and Indian Airlines, it was decided to host the PSS of Air India on a third party to be selected on a turnkey basis through a global tender. M/s SITA was selected through a global tender process in 2011. The Air India PSS data is kept at data centre of SITA. ;(d): With the disinvestment of Air India Limited (AIL), there is no longer any equity stake of Government of India in AIL. No action thus lies on the part of the Government.
