                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2854






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Telecom Linkages in Diara Region of Bihar




Ram Kripal Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to link the villages of Bihar including Diara region with internet and mobile network; and (b) 	the number of villages of Diara region of Bihar linked with internet and mobile network during each of the last three years and the amount of fund spent by the Government during the said period in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity at all Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country including Bihar. In Bihar, including Diara region, BharatNet Phase-I has been implemented through Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) and BharatNet Phase-II is being implemented through Private Sector Model by Bharat Broadband Network Limited (BBNL). As on 27.06.2019, by laying 19,793 km optical fibre cable (OFC), a total of 6208 Gram Panchayats (GPs) have been made Service Ready for broadband connectivity in Bihar and the remaining GPs are under implementation. Provision of mobile connectivity at 2355 tower locations, identified by Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) in the Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas (LWE Phase-I), has been implemented through Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited under the Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) Scheme. Out of these, 250 towers have been installed in Bihar.Under LWE Phase-II project under USOF, mobile connectivity by using 2G + 4G technology is to be provided in inhabited and uncovered villages at 3465 tower locations (identified by MHA, in the Left Wing Extremism affected areas in 10 States).  Out of these, 106 towers locations have been identified in Bihar for providing mobile connectivity under this project. Further, Government has allocated 965 Megahertz spectrum through auction in October 2016 to various telecom service providers for access services in the country including Bihar Licensed Service Area. During this auction, the spectrum as detailed below has been allocated in Bihar Licensed Service Area:<pre>Spectrum Band	Quantum of Spectrum Allocated1800 MHz	11.8 MHz (Paired)2100 MHz	10 MHz (Paired)2300 MHz	20 MHz2500 MHz	10 MHz</pre>In order to enhance the mobile and internet penetration, 3G/4G networks are being rolled out progressively by the telecom service providers in rural as well as urban areas.
