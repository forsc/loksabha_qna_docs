                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

615






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Soil Testing Laboratories




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Soil Testing Laboratories (STL) in the country State-wise;(b) 	the capacity of the said laboratories, State-wise;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to set up more such laboratories in the country;  and(d) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	As per information received from State Governments, at    present, 1304 static Soil Testing Laboratories (STLs), 165 Mobile STLs, 6326 Mini Labs & 154 Village Level STLs are working in the country with an annual analyzing capacity of 150.62 lakh soil samples. State-wise details are given at Annexure-I. Apart from these,  1076 minilabs have been  provided to Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs) and  research centres  of Indian Council of Agricultural Research under the scheme. (c) & (d):	New Soil testing laboratories are sanctioned as per proposals of  State Governments under Soil Health Management component of National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA).
