                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

111






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Safety Audit of Petrol Pumps




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of the fact that modern/adequate resources for fire safety are not available at several petrol pumps due to which if any incident of fire happens at the petrol pumps then it cannot be controlled immediately and there is wide possibility of massive accident; (b) 	if so, the details of the steps taken by the Government in collaboration with the State Governments for the fire safety audit of the petrol pumps; State-wise; and (c) 	the number of fire incidents at the petrol pumps in the last three years and the losses incurred/valuable lives lost due to the said incidents; State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	& (b)	Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) namely Indian Oil Corporation Ltd (IOCL), Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (HPCL) and Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (BPCL) have informed that adequate resources for fire safety are provided at Petrol pumps as per Petroleum Explosive & Safety Organization (PESO), guidelines. A Petrol Pump is commissioned only after strictly following stipulations as laid out in Petroleum Rules 2002, after compliance of which, license to operate the petrol pump is provided to OMCs by PESO. Further, the OMCs strive to maintain these standards by means of regular and surprise inspections, and strict action is taken as per Marketing Discipline Guidelines in case deficiencies are noticed.(c)	State-wise  details  of  fire  incident at petrol pumps, losses incurred and valuable life lost during the last three years (2017- 18 to 2019-20 and April – June 2020) is at  annexure.*****
