                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

568






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Doubling of Railway Track over the Brahmaputra River




Queen Oja













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                








                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any proposal for doubling of the railway track constructed over the Brahmaputra river passing through Guwahati;(b)	if so, the reaction of the Government thereto; and (c)	whether any action has been initiated for the doubling of the railway track constructed over the Brahmaputra river  passing through Guwahati and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):	Based on public demand and Railways’ requirement, Saraighat Bridge doubling project (including double line from Agthori to Kamakhya) over river Brahmaputra has been included in Budget 2017-18 subject to requisite Government approvals.  Detailed investigations for important bridge have been taken up.  As existing single line bridge is Rail-cum-Road bridge, Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH) has been requested to give their consent for cost sharing of proposed Rail-cum-Road bridge for doubling. *****
