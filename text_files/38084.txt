                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Multi Modal Logistics Park at Jogighopa




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the time by which Multi Modal Logistics Park at Jogighopa is likely to start functioning;(b)   the revenues likely to be generated as a result thereof in the three years from start of its operation;(c)    the expected employment to be generated as a result thereof in the next five years;(d)  whether the Government proposes to establish an airport at Jogighopa as part of Multi Modal Logistics Park;(e)    if so, the time by which the airport project is likely to be taken up and completed; and(f)     if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)    The Multi Modal Logistics Park at Jogighopa is likely to start functioning after completion of the construction work at the site. The completion date of construction work at MMLP Jogighopa is 31.12.2022. (b)  As per the assessment carried out at project preparation stage, revenue of an amount of Rs. 1000 Cr (approx) is estimated in the first three years from start of operation. (c)    As per the Detailed Project Report (DPR), the estimated total employment to be generated after commencement of MMLP is about 11521 people. (d)    No Sir. (e)     Does not arise. (f)   The concept of Multi Modal Logistics Park does not envisage the establishment of transport mode itself rather this deals with providing connectivity at least from two transport modes.  MMLP Jogighopa is being provided connectivity from Highway, Railways & Inland Waterways.   *****
