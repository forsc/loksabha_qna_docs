                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3717






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






PMAY in Rajasthan




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of residential units constructed/which were to be constructed in Rajasthan under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana during each of the last three years including the current financial year along with the details thereof;(b)	the details of criteria and rules set to become the beneficiary of the said yojana; and(c) the details of the residential units constructed in Rajasthan    during the last three years along with the funds allocated for the said purpose, location/district-wise?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                               (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):  A total of 2,16,724 houses have been sanctioned in State of Rajasthan under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U). The details of houses sanctioned during each of the last three years including the current financial year are at Annexure-I.(b):	The eligibility criteria for selection of beneficiaries under PMAY-U are as under:(i)	A beneficiary family will comprise husband, wife, unmarried sons and / or unmarried daughters. An adult earning member (irrespective of marital status) can be treated as a separate household.(ii)	Income eligibility of the household under the scheme for Economically Weaker Section (EWS) is up to ?3,00,000, for Low Income Group(LIG) - from ?3,00,001 to ?6,00,000, for Middle Income Group (MIG)–I from ?6,00,001 to ?12,00,000 and MIG-II – from ?12,00,001 to ?18,00,000.(iii)	The beneficiary family should not own a pucca house (an all weather dwelling unit) either in his/ her name or in the name of any member of his/ her family in any part of India.(iv)	Further, such person with pucca house having built-up area less than 21 square meter (sqm.) may be included for enhancement of existing dwelling units upto 30 sqm.  (c): District-wise details of houses constructed under PMAY-Uq	 in the State of Rajasthan along with Central Assistance released during the last three years are at Annexure-II.                                     ******
