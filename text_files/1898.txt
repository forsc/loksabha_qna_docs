                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Gram Samridhi Yojana




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government had approved and is planning to launch the Gram Samridhi Yojana, which will be implemented with the help of World Bank and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government is planning to generate jobs in rural areas by strengthening small farmers and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has taken initiatives for creation of a non-banking financial institution for the food processing sector and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether the Government is planning to roll out model food processing policy in the country and if so, the details thereof along with the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): Yes Sir. The Gram Samridhi Yojana aims to enhance the competitiveness of micro enterprises in the unorganized segment of the food processing industry and promote formalisation of the sector.The Gram Samridhi Yojana is planned to increase better job opportunities and availability of off-farms jobs.(c):	In pursuance to the Budget announcement for the financial year 2018-19, a new scheme to promote establishment of “Specialized Agro Processing Financial Institutions” as a Non-Banking Finance Company (NBFC) is under consideration.(d):	Draft National Food Processing Policy is under formulation.*****
