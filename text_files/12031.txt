                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1000






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Allocation of Funds




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY ??????? ?? ???????????? ??????be pleased to state: (a) 	the detail of funds allocated to various projects sponsored by the Ministry during the last three years, State-wise; and (b) 	the funds utilised by each State during the said period, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): The Ministry of Science & Technology does not implement any centrally sponsored scheme and does not have any provision for making States/ UTs wise target, allocation and release under Science & Technology Schemes/ Projects. The Ministry, however, supports R&D projects to investigators and institutions related to development of Science & Technology including fundamental research through competitive grant mechanism in all the States/UTs. This includes support to research and development labs and institutions, support to Scientists and Researchers for investigator centric research proposal, strengthening Science & Technology (S&T) infrastructure in Universities and Institutions of Higher Learning through Fund of Improvement of S&T Infrastructure in Universities and other Higher Educational Institutions (FIST), Promoting S&T Entrepreneurship and Innovation, Technology Business Incubators, Technology Development and Demonstration as well as empowering women and weaker sections of the society trough science. In addition, Ministry in coordination with State Governments, implements schemes to attract young students to pursue careers in Science. A programme on Innovation in Science Pursuit for Inspired Research (INSPIRE) was initiated under which more than one million children have benefited in the country. The Ministry is also implementing schemes such as Technology Development and Demonstration Programme (TDDP), Access to Knowledge and Studies (A2K + Studies), Common Research and Technology Development Hubs, Promoting Innovations in Individuals Start-ups and MSMEs (PRISM), Biotechnology Research & Development, Industrial and Entrepreneurship Development.*********
