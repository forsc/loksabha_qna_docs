                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Tax on Petrol and Diesel




Ramesh Bidhuri













                                                    Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is imposing any new tax on the per litre production and cost of diesel and petrol due to which the rates of petrol and diesel are likely to be increased; (b) the details of tax being imposed by the Government on petrol and diesel at present; (c) whether the Government proposes to reduce the said tax so that the prices of petrol and diesel may decrease; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF FINANCE(Ms. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)(a), (b), (c) and (d): A statement is placed on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.184 RAISED BY SHRI RAMESH BIDHURI AND SHRI SUKHBIR SINGH JAUNAPURIA FOR 2ND DECEMBER, 2019 REGARDING TAX ON PETROL AND DIESEL (a)	No Sir. At present no new tax on petrol and diesel is being considered.(b) The details of taxes imposed by the central government on petrol and diesel at present is as below:Particulars	CENTRAL EXCISE	CUSTOMS	Basic Excise Duty (Rs. per litre)	Special Additional Excise Duty (Rs. per litre)	Additional Excise Duty (Road and infrastructure Cess) (Rs. per litre)	Basic Customs Duty (as a % of Assessable Value)	Additional Customs Duty (CVD) (Rs. per litre)	Additional Customs Duty (Rs. per litre)	Social Welfare Surcharge (as a % of Basic Customs Duty mentioned at (5))(1)	(2)	(3)	(4)	(5)	(6)	(7)	(8)Petrol	2.98	8.00	9.00	2.50%	2.98 + 8.00	9.00	3%Petrol (branded)	4.16	8.00	9.00	2.50%	4.16 + 8.00 	9.00	3%High Speed Diesel 	4.83	2.00	9.00	2.50%	4.83 + 2.00 	9.00	3%High Speed Diesel (branded)	7.19	2.00	9.00	2.50%	7.19 + 2.00	9.00	3%	(c) and (d)	At present there is no proposal to reduce taxes on petrol and diesel.*****
