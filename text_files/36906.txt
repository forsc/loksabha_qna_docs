                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3988






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Infrastructural Development Projects in States under SDS




Rajbahadur Singh













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Brijbhushan Sharan Singh
                                                








                                                     Pratap Chandra Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	the key objectives and features of the Swadesh Darshan scheme (SDS) along with the details of sanctioned projects under Eco Theme under SDS; (b) 	the Detailed Project Report (DPR) for major ongoing infrastructural development projects in the State of Madhya Pradesh, Odisha, Uttar Pradesh and NCT of Delhi; (c) 	the details of funds allocated for the said States under SDS towards strengthening Tourism infrastructure through various spiritual circuits; and (d)	the details of Foreign Exchange Earnings (FEEs), in US$ from Tourism in the said States during the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			               (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	The major objective of the ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme includes development of circuits with tourist potential in a planned and prioritized manner, promote cultural value of the country to generate livelihoods in the identified regions, enhancing the tourist attractiveness in a sustainable manner by developing world class infrastructure etc.  The Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies etc. for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. The projects under these schemes are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports (DPR), adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc. The  details of the projects  sanctioned  in the country  under  PRASHAD  scheme and  various themes  including  eco and spiritual themes in  Swadesh Darshan  scheme is  at  annexure  I.The Ministry of  Tourism has  not  sanctioned  any  project  in  Delhi  under  Swadesh Darshan and  PRASHAD  schemes  however the details of the projects  sanctioned  in  Madhya Pradesh, Odisha  and  Uttar Pradesh is at  annexure  II. The Ministry of Tourism do not maintain State –wise Foreign Exchange Earnings (FEEs). However, the details of Foreign Exchange Earnings (FEEs), in US$ from Tourism in the country from 2017 to 2020 is at Annexure- III. ********
