                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2265






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Entrusting Passport making to Private Firm




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has entrusted the task of making passport and its renewal to any private firm; (b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the task of making passport has great importance due to security reasons; and(d) if so, the reaction of the Government to the decision for entrusting the said task to a private party in this situation?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Government has not entrusted the task of making passport and its renewal to any private firm.(b)     Does not arise. (c)     Yes.(d)    	Government has not entrusted the task of making passport and its renewal to any private firm.***
