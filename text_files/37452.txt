                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4564






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Extension of PMGKAY




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any plans to extend PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) till March 2024; (b) 	if not, the reasons for the same; and (c) 	the details of quantity of monthly grains being allotted to States under PMGKAY, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)  & (b): 	The Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) has been extended for a further period of six months from April, 2022 to September, 2022 by the Government.(c):  	State-wise details of quantity of monthly foodgrains being allotted to States/UTs under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) is at Annexure. Monthly quantity keeps on increasing/decreasing as per addition/deletion of Ration Cards due to various reasons***** AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (c) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION No.4564  FOR ANSWER ON 30.03.2022 IN THE LOK SABHADetails of quantity of monthly foodgrains being allotted to States/UTs under PMGKAYSl No	States/UTs 	Quantity of monthly foodgrains (in MTs)1	Andaman & Nicobar Islands	304.312	Andhra Pradesh	134110.523	Arunachal Pradesh	4201.724	Assam	125583.485	Bihar	435581.716	Chandigarh	1331.767	Chhattisgarh	100385.008	Daman & Diu D&NH	1491.069	Delhi	36389.9710	Goa	2660.9111	Gujarat	170856.9812	Haryana	63245.0013	Himachal Pradesh	14322.2814	Jammu And Kashmir	36205.3415	Jharkhand	131850.1216	Karnataka	200965.0017	Kerala	77400.0618	Ladakh	719.4719	Lakshadweep	109.0420	Madhya Pradesh	241291.9921	Maharashtra	350083.4222	Manipur	9984.6423	Meghalaya	10727.5924	Mizoram	3341.0825	Nagaland	7023.4426	Odisha	162073.1827	Puducherry 	3121.2828	Punjab	70756.5129	Rajasthan	220006.2330	Sikkim	1893.9431	Tamil Nadu	182346.8332	Telangana	95810.9533	Tripura	12414.0734	Uttar Pradesh	735960.3835	Uttarakhand	30970.0036	West Bengal	300918.85Total	3976438.01*****
