                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3546






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Setting up of Science Centres in All States




Khan Saumitra













                                                    Narendra Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up science centres and Zonal mechanism for research assistance in all States;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has received requests from the State Governments for promoting research and development work in their respective States; (d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM & DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)to(e): Under the Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science (SPoCS), National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), an autonomous organization under the Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India, has developed a chain of Science Cities/ Science Centres/ Innovation Hubs throughout the Country.As per the scheme guidelines, the projects are taken up on receipt of the proposal from State-Govt/UTs with commitments of encumbrance free land, cost of the project (capital & corpus), operation, manpower and management of the project etc.List of new projects that are in progress in various States/UTs under SPoCS Scheme is attached at Annexure-A.
