                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1543






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Decline in Auto Sales




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the sales of automobiles in both commercial and passenger vehicle segments declined rapidly for the straight ninth month, resulting in significant job losses;(b) if so, the details thereof, segment-wise;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to declare a revival package for the auto industry; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) & (b): As reported by Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM), domestic sales for passenger vehicles and commercial vehicles segments from January-August  2019 and 2020 are as below:Domestic Sales (in Numbers) Category	Year	Jan	Feb	March	April	May	June	July	AugPassengerVehicles	2019	280,091	272,243	291,861	247,541	226,975	209,522	190,115	189,129	2020 	263,164	251,516	140,910	0	33,546	105,617	182,779	215,916Commercialvehicles	2019	87,591	87,436	109,022	68,680	68,847	70,771	56,866	51,897	2020	75,289	58,670	13,027	                31,636*	NA	NA*Data for commercial vehicles is available on quarterly basis since April 2020.No confirmed data on job losses is available with the government. (c) & (d): The Government, as a policymaker, always attempts to sustain and improve momentum of the economy through a package of measures for comprehensive and continued development of the sector.						******
