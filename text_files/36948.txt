                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Creation of Cultural Infrastructure




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the allocations made under the Scheme of Financial Assistance for Creation of Cultural Infrastructure during the last five years, State-wise(b)	the details of the outcome achieved through the scheme during the said period; and (c)	the details of the infrastructure projects planned for the upcoming financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)  The scheme by the name of Financial Assistance for Creation of Cultural Infrastructure is a Central Sector Scheme under which no State / UT-wise allocation of fund is made. However, allocation of budget under the scheme during the last five years is as under:                     (Rupees in crore)Sl. No.	Financial Year	BE/RE1.	2016-17	31.502.	2017-18	26.533.	2018-19	20.904.	2019-20	31.905.	2020-21	9.816.	2021-22 (till date)	17.28 The scheme has following three components:(i)    Financial Assistance for Building Grants including Studio Theatres  (ii)   Financial Assistance for Tagore Cultural Complexes (TCC)(iii)  Financial Assistance for Allied Cultural Activities(b)  The details of the outcome achieved through the scheme during the last five years are at Annexure-I.(c)    The scheme of Financial Assistance for Creation of Cultural Infrastructure is open throughout the year for the applicant to submit their application for financial assistance. Under the components of the scheme, financial assistance is provided to NGOs/Not-for-profit organization/ Govt. Department / Govt. sponsored agencies/ Universities etc. for creation of cultural infrastructure/complexes etc. Thereafter, the completed eligible applications are placed before National Appraisal Committee (NAC) / Expert Committee for examining the proposal to be sanctioned / approved.
