                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

930






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Drugs Seized by NCB




Asaduddin Owaisi













                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total contraband drugs seized by the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) from ports and airports during the last three years;(b) the number of cases registered, persons arrested, convicted and jailed during the said period, State-wise;(c) whether there is any limit to keep such drugs in possession that are legal or illegal and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether NCB has arrested many persons on small amount of drugs in possession or as conspirator and failed to establish the source of large quantities of drugs seized in different ports and airports and other establishments;(e) if so, the steps taken or being taken by the Government to make NCB more professional; and(f) whether the Government has directed to form dedicated Anti-Narcotics Task Forces in every State, if so, the details thereof and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The details of maritime drugs seizure taken over by Narcotics Control Bureau  (NCB)  from  Navy  and  Coast  Guards  during the year 2019, 2020 & 2021 is at Annexure-‘A’.  The seizure made at airports by NCB during the year 2019, 2020 & 2021 is at Annexure-‘B(b):	 The details of state-wise number of cases registered by NCB, persons arrested, persons convicted and persons jailed during the year 2019, 2020 & 2021 is at Annexure-‘C.(c) to (e):	As per Section 8(c) of NDPS Act, 1985 (as amended) possession of any quantity of narcotic drug and psychotropic substance is illegal.  NCB has made a total 83 seizures of small quantity of various drugs and 56 persons arrested during last three year from 2019 to 2021.  (f):	Instructions were issued to Chief Secretary of all the States for creation of dedicated Anti Narcotics Cell in all affected States, vide MHA order No. I-12023/2/2017-NCB-II, dated 29 July, 2019. The said directions were again reiterated vide NCB’s letter No. III/4/NCORD-APEX/P&C/2021/376, dated 21.01.2022.******
