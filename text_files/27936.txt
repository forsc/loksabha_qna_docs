                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3670






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Failed PM-KISAN Payments




Preneet Kaur














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the failed payment transfer under the PM-KISAN Yojana during the 2020 lockdown;(b)	if so, the details thereof, if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	the detailed data on the number and value of failed payment transfers over the past two years, State/UT-wise and month-wise; and (d)	the details of failed transfers that have not been resolved till date, including the number and value of such pending failed transactions and the reasons therefore?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	 Various reasons have been identified by the Union Government for the transactions failure under PM-KISAN Scheme such as Account closed/Transferred, Invalid IFSC, Account Inactive, Account Dormant, Amount exceeds limit set on Account by Bank for Credit/Debit per transaction, Account Holder Expired, Account Blocked or Frozen, Inactive Aadhar, Network failure etc.  (c) & (d):	Under PM-KISAN Scheme the benefits are not transferrable every month. The benefits are transferred in three equal installments of Rs.2000/- each in every four-months.  The total number of transactions done under PM-KISAN Scheme has been 68,76,55,195 of which the number of failed transaction is less than 1%. The cumulative number of PM-KISAN Scheme transactions which have failed since 1st February, 2019 till 30thJune, 2021 is 61,04,877 out of which 20,88,010transactions were successfully reprocessed. States/UTs have been requested to make necessary changes, so that those can be processed immediately. To tackle the issues of transaction failures and process the payment to such registered farmers’ families, a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) has been developed and issued to States/UTs for necessary action at their end.  In cases where the corrective measures are to be taken by States/UTs, such transaction failure records are opened to respective State/UT for correction under the “Correction Module” tab of PM-KISAN portal. Thereafter, all the transaction failure records are reprocessed for the payment of the respective installment under PM-KISAN Scheme.The Department through weekly video conferencing take up the matter with State/UT Governments to expedite the resolution of the issue and reprocess the failed transactions for the payments of the respective installments under PM-KISAN Scheme. The State-wise details of total failed transactions, failed transactions which are successfully reprocessed and the pending failed transactions along with the value of such failed transaction under PM-KISAN Scheme is given at Annexure-I.
