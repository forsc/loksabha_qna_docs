                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3221






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






MOU for Food Product Standards




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has signed an Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with any country to mutually recognize each other’s food product standards and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	if not, whether talks have been initiated by the Ministry to enter into such agreements; and (c)	if so, the details in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	    : 	No, Sir.(b) & (c) : 	Preliminary discussion have been held on the issue of mutual recognition of food standards and food control systems between the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) and the Bhutan Agriculture and Food Regulatory Authority (BAFRA).
