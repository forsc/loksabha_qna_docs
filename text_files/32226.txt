                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3609






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Skill Development Training in Bihar




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the number of institutions functioning presently in district Karakat Parliamentary Constituency in Bihar to impart skill development training; and (b)       the number of youths benefitted by the said training in the said Constituency?






ANSWER




(a)       Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing various programmes/ schemes for the skill development of youth across the country, including Karakat Parliamentary Constituency in the State of Bihar. Presently, total 125 training centres are functional in the Karakat constituency of the State of Bihar under the programmes/schemes of MSDE namely; Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras (PMKKs), Jan Shikshan Sansthans (JSSs) and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS). The details of functional training centres and number of candidates trained in Karakat Parliamentary Constituency covering districts of Rohtas and Aurangabad are given below: Districts under Karakat Parliamentary Constituency	 Number of functional training centres	 Number of candidates trainedRohtas	73	49,019Aurangabad	52	29,093Total	125	78,112 (b)       Above reply (a) may be referred to.
