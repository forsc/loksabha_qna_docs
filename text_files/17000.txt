                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

733






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Basic Sports Infrastructure




Shantanu Thakur













                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any measure for setting up basic sports infrastructure in the States of West Bengal and Gujarat;  (b) if so, the details thereof; and  (c) the quantum of funds allocated, released and utilized by the Union Government for this purpose including the details of the basic infrastructure built up during the last three years and the current year? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS  { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }  (a) to (c) Yes, Sir. ‘Sports’ being a State subject the responsibility for providing sports facilities, including construction of stadiums rests with the State / Union Territory Governments. Central Government supplements efforts of the State/Union Territories by bridging the critical gaps in sports infrastructure. Accordingly, under the Khelo India Scheme, this Ministry has sanctioned 2 projects in West Bengal and 4 projects in Gujarat for development of sports infrastructure during the last three years and the current year. The details of funds released by the Union Government for these projects for the aforesaid time period are given at Annexure - I. Further, financial assistance has been provided through National Sports Development Fund and Sports Authority of India for development of sports infrastructure in West Bengal and Gujarat during the last three years and the current year. The year-wise details of the same for the aforesaid time period are given at Annexure – II. ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (c) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †733 TO BE ANSWERED ON 17.09.2020 REGARDING “BASIC SPORTS INFRASTRUCTURE” ASKED BY SHRI SHANTANU THAKUR, SHRI NARANBHAI KACHHADIYA, SHRI JASWANT SINGH BHABHOR AND SHRI PARBATBHAI SAVABHAI PATEL, HON’BLE MEMBERS OF LOK SABHA ****
