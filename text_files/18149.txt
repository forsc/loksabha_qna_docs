                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1972






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Driver Training Centres




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of driver training centres/institutes operational in Uttar Pradesh; (b)	whether the Government proposes to open more such centres/institutes in future; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                          THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)](a)  to  (c)   The Ministry of Road Transport & Highways implements a scheme of setting up of model Institutes of Driving Training and Research (IDTR) and Regional Driver Training Centres (RDTCs) in the country. Under the Scheme one Driving Training Institute (DTI) was set up in Kanpur. Further, one model Institute of Driving Training & Research (IDTR) has been sanctioned to the State Government of Uttar Pradesh for Raebareilly in 2014. The scheme has been continued during the current FY 2020-21 for new proposals from the States/UTs.******
