                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2495






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Soil  Fertility




Rampreet Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of the States/UTs in which the agriculture land has been found to be facing the maximum challenges in terms of soil fertility; (b) 	the number of farmers in various States/UTs in the country including those in Bihar who have been given soil fertility cards; and (c) 	the number of soil testing laboratories set up in various States of the country including Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	The names of States/ UTs in which the agriculture land has been found to be facing maximum challenges in terms of soil nutrient deficiency is given  at Annexure – I. (b):   The total number of farmers given soil health card is 1073.89 lakh and 1311.80 lakh during cycle – I (2015-17) and cycle- II (2017-19) respectively. This includes 64.69 lakh and 60.10 lakh cards given in Bihar during cycle- I and cycle – II respectively.  The State/UT – wise number of farmers issued soil health cards in given at Annexure – II.        (c):	There are 7949 soil testing labs in the country including 1293 static labs, 165 mobile labs, 6337 mini labs and 154 village level labs. There are 47 labs in Bihar which includes 38 static labs and 9 mobile labs.  The state- wise number of soil testing labs in given at Annexure – III.
