                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4122






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Annual Programme Implementation Plan




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether payment of Rs.195.29 crore to Maharashtra Government is pending due to lack of grants for 2019-20 for the supply of nutritious diet under ICDS scheme and if so, the time by which it is likely to be released;  (b)	the total amount lying pending with the Union Government from 2003 to 2021 in this regard, year-wise and the reasons therefor; (c)	whether there has been an increase in the number of beneficiaries due to COVID-19 pandemic and if so, the details thereof;  (d)	whether the Union Government has released the required funds to Government of Maharashtra in accordance with its demand and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons, therefor, and(e)	whether the Maharashtra Government has sent any proposal to the Union Government for approval of revised Annual Programme Implementation Plan (APIP) and if so, the details thereof, and  (f)	whether the Government has accorded approval to it and if so, the details thereof?   






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) : No amount is pending with Government of India under Supplementary Nutrition Programme (SNP) due to lack of grants.   (b) :No amount is pending with Govt of India. Unspent balances / excess expenditure incurred in a particular year is adjusted in the subsequent year.    (c)  :  As per data available upto September 2020, there appears to be an increase in the number of beneficiaries. During the Covid period, THR was delivered by AWWs at the doorstep of beneficiaries under Anganwadi Services.(d) to (f)  :        Funds are allocated and released as per Annual Programme Implementation Plan (APIP)  prepared by the States. Based on the revised demand from Government of Maharashtra, funds  under SNP were released for the year 2019-20.  ****
