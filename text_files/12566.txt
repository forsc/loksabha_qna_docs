                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1566






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Release of Balance Funds




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the steps taken by the Government for payment of the balance amount of 130.23 crore rupees as per the criteria of the scheme as on September, 2019 under District Rural Development Agencies (DRDA) in Rajasthan;(b)	the time by which the funds are likely to be released;(c)	whether the State Government has not fulfilled all the formalities in this regard; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Under the Scheme of DRDA Administration, grants are given to the States for meeting Administrative costs of DRDAs (salary and contingent expenses subject to a maximum of 30% of the expenditure on salary) subject to specific norms.  Being a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, the funding is shared between Centre and States in the ratio of 60:40 from the financial year 2015-16. The funding ratio in respect of North Eastern States (including Sikkim), Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand is 90:10 (Centre : State). In respect of UTs 100% expenditure on salary and contingency (subject to a maximum 30% of salary) is funded by the centre. During the current financial year Rs.545.47 Lakh has already been released to State Government of Rajasthan for DRDAs. As per the guideline of the Scheme any expenditure over and above the funds released by Centre is to be met by the State Government (since 2018-19).(b)	to (d): In view of above does not arise. ****
