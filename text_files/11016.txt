03.02.2020






Grants to Artists




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the grants provided to the artists individually and Non-Governmental Organisations to promote art and culture in the country is still continuing/has stopped now;(b)	the names of the States in which grants have been provided to individual artists and Non-Governmental Organisations during the last three years along with the details of amount provided; and (c)	the details and the criteria for total grant provisioned for promoting art and culture during the year 2020-21?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)     Yes, Sir. The schemes under which grants are provided to the Artists and NGOs are still continuing.(b)	Scheme of Financial Assistance for Promotion of Art and Culture (05 Components) and Scheme of Scholarship and Fellowship for Promotion of Art and Culture (03 Components) are Central Sector Schemes and grants are not released to the State Governments.
