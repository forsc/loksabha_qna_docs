                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2703






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Third Phase Projects of DMRC




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has completed all of its third phase projects and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) 	the details of phase of work which is going to be executed by the DMRC in 	the next phase;(c)	whether there is any proposal for further extension of the Dwarka-Najafgarh Delhi Metro Rail Line and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details the total amount of debt repaid by the DMRC till date from its revenues earned?






ANSWER




                                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                         MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                               (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):	  Yes, Sir. Delhi Metro Rail Corporation Limited (DMRC) has completed its Phase-III projects and its extensions.(b):	   Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has now planned to execute Phase IV projects comprising six corridors. Out of this, construction of the 03 Priority Corridors sanctioned by Government in July 2019 at the total completion cost of ?24,948.65 crores has been started and has achieved physical progress of 21.5%. The details of the corridors are as under:S.
