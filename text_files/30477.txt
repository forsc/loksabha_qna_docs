                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2060






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Setting up of Petrol Pumps along Highways




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to allow Dhaba owners to build petrol pumps and toilets along Highways in the country; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)& (b) No Sir. However, Ministry vide letter dated 26.06.2020 and 02.09.2021 (amendment) has issued guidelines/ Norms for grant of permissions for construction of access to Fuel Stations, Wayside amenities, connecting roads, other properties, Rest Area Complexes & such other facilities.  *****
