                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2823






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Committee for PVTGs




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government set up a Committee for the framing of guidelines on Habitat Rights for Particularly vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTG) guaranteed under the Forest Rights Act;(b) if so, the details including date on which it was set up and composition of the Committee; and(c) whether the Committee includes representation from people belonging to PVTGs, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): Ministry of Tribal Affairs has constituted an Expert Committee on 21.02.2020 under the Chairpersonship of Shri Hrusikesh Panda, Former Secretary, Tribal Affairs on improvement of the recognition and vesting process of community rights under the Forest Rights Act, 2006.   The Composition of the Committee is as at Annexure1. The committee has 2 subgroups who will focus on (i) Habitat Rights of PVTGs and (ii) Seasonal Resource access to Nomadic and Pastoralist communities. The details of members of these sub groups is given at Annexure-I and Annexure-II.
