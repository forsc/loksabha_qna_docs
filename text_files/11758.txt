                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

64






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Housing for Urban Poor




Asaduddin Owaisi













                                                     Imtiaz Jaleel Syed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the scheme of housing for urban poorunder Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) was launchedin June 2015 with the aim to build 1.12 crore houses;(b) if so, the total number of houses handed over to the beneficiaries so far and the steps taken to achieve the said target;(c) whether the Government has recently sanctioned 6.5 lakh houses under the scheme and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(d) the total funds released by the Government under the scheme to the States; and(e) the cost sharing ratio of Centre and States under the Scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              [SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI](a) to (e): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the Sabha.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *64 DUE FOR 06.02.2020 REGARDING “HOUSING FOR URBAN POOR”(a)	& (b): 	Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] was launched on 25.06.2015 for addressing the housing requirement of the people belonging to Economically Weaker Section (EWS), Lower Income Group (LIG) and Middle Income Group (MIG) categories in urban areas.States/UTs have undertaken demand survey under the scheme for assessing actual demand of housing.  The validated demand reported by States/UTs so far is around 1.12 crore.  Based on the project proposals received so far from the States/UTs, a total 1,03,22,560 houses have been sanctioned under the Scheme; out of this, 60,50,991 are at various stages of construction and 32,07,573 are completed/delivered.  Further, States/UTs have been requested to get the project proposals for all their remaining demand of houses sanctioned by March, 2020 so that construction of all houses may progressively be completed by 2022. (c):	Yes, Sir.  A total 6,70,239 houses were sanctioned in the month of December, 2019. State/UT-wise details are at annexure - I.(d): 	Central Assistance of Rs. 63,676.50 crore has been released so farto the States/UTs under the scheme.(e)	Central Government provides different levels of financial assistance under following four verticals of the Scheme:S.
