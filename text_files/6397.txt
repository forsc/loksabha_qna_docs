                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

18






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Sector Skill Councils




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of fund disbursed to each of the Sector Skill Councils (SSCs), during the last three years, year-wise; (b)	 the details of utilisation of funds and types of projects funded by each of the SSCs during the last three years; (c) 	the corrective steps taken in the light of the Sharda Prasad Committee report on ‘Conflict of interest’ in the composition and governance of Sector Skill Councils; and (d) 	the action taken by the Government on the Committee’s recommendation that all Sector Skill Councils may be scrapped due to such conflict of interest?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) 	The year wise details of fund disbursed to and utilised by each of the Sector Skill Councils (SSCs), during the last three years is given at Annexure. SSCs also generate income from activities like assessment fee, affiliation fee etc. This revenue along with the funds received from NSDC are utilized by the SSCs for performing their functions and includes salary to employees, rent for office premises, utilities, travel and conveyance, assessment fee to assessment agency etc.(c)&(d) 	The Government constituted a Committee under Shri Sharda Prasad, former Director General, Directorate General of Employment & Training, Ministry of Labour & Employment, Government of India, for rationalisation and optimization of the functioning of Sector Skill Councils, on 18.05.2016. The Committee submitted its Report in December 2016. The Committee has made several recommendations pertaining to, inter alia, classification, role, governance structure and financing of Sector Skill Councils. The Report was placed in public domain on 25th April, 2017 and consultations were held with all stakeholders involving- SSCs, Industry Associations, State Governments, user industries & Central Ministries/ Departments. Since, then the National Skill Development Corporation has issued guidelines for avoidance of conflict of interest in promoting organization of SSCs in August, 2018.
