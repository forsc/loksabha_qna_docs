                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

386






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Foreign Funding received by NGOs




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) who have received foreign funds during last three years in Bihar;(b) the quantum of foreign funds received during the said period, year-wise, State-wise;(c) whether such NGOs are submitting audited records as required under the existing laws; and(d) if not, the nature of action taken to ensure submission of audited records?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI) (a):   The number of NGOs which received foreign contribution during the last three years in Bihar is as under: -Year	Total number of NGOs2017-18	2432018-19	2042019-20	185-2-L.S.US.Q.NO.386 FOR 20.07.2021(b) to (d):	Year-wise and state-wise details of foreign contribution received by NGOs is available in the public domain on the web portal www.fcraonline.nic.in. The Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act, 2010 and rules made there under require that all FCRA NGOs/Associations have to file audited Annual Returns every year. Failure to do so attracts penal actions that include financial penalty and suspension/cancellation of FCRA certificate. Most of the NGOs file their Annual Returns. Action is taken against the defaulting NGOs.   ********
