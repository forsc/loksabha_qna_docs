                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Scholarships to Students




Chandra Sekhar Sahu













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.315 TO BE ANSWERED ON 30.11.2021SCHOLARSHIPS TO STUDENTS 315.      SHRI CHANDRA SEKHAR SAHU:              SHRI GIRISH BHALCHANDRA BAPAT:               DR. PRITAM GOPINATHRAO MUNDE:               SHRI RAHUL RAMESH SHEWALE: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has been granting scholarships to students of socially backward section of the society; (b)if so, whether children belonging to different castes studying in the same class get different amounts as scholarships, if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government proposes to rationalise all scholarships given to meritorious students belonging to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and minorities to award equal amount to students of the same class; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the time by when the final decision is likely to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a): This Ministry is providing scholarships to students of socially backward section of the society. The details of the scholarship schemes are given at Annexure. (b) to (d): The merit-based scholarships for SC and ST students (e.g. Top Class Scholarship) are already largely similar; and there is no such scheme for OBCs and Minorities.***** AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 315  for 30.11.2021 A.  Department of Social Justice & Empowerment SN	  Name of the Scheme1	Post-Matric Scholarship to the SC Students Studying in ( Class XIth and above)2	Pre-Matric Scholarship to the SC Students Studying in classes IX & X3	Pre-Matric Scholarship to the Children of those engaged in occupations involving cleaning and prone to health hazards (Class I-X)4	Pre- Matric Scholarship for OBC Students5	Post- Matric Scholarship for OBC Students6	 Dr. Ambedkar Post-Matric Scholarship for EBC Students7	Dr. Ambedkar Pre-Matric and Post-Matric Scholarship for EBC Students for DNT students8	National Overseas Scholarship for SC students (Masters & Ph. D)9	 Top Class Education B.  Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities SN	  Name of the Scheme1	Pre-Matric Scholarship to the SC Students Studying in classes IX & X2	Post-Matric Scholarship ( for Class XI to Post Graduate Degrees/Diploma)3	Top Class Education Scholarship (For Graduate and Post Graduate Degrees/Diploma in notified Institutes of the Excellence in Education)4	National Fellowship for PwDs (for M. Phil and PhD. In Indian Universities)5	National Overseas Scholarship for (Masters Degrees & Ph.D in Foreign Universities)   C. Ministry of Tribal Affairs SN	  Name of the Scheme1	Pre-Matric Scholarship for ST students (Class IX and X)2	Post Matric Scholarship for ST students (Class XI and above)3	National Fellowship and Scholarship for Higher Education for ST    students4	National Overseas Scholarship for ST students.  D. Ministry of Minority Affairs SN	  Name of the Scheme1	Pre-Matric Scholarship2	Post Matric Scholarship3	Merit –cum-Mean Based Scholarship  *****
