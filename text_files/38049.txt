                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4813






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Water Stress in Haryana




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of water stressed districts/blocks identified in the State of Haryana;(b) the aims and objectives of Jal Shakti Abhiyan (JSA);(c) the details of blocks identified under JSA in the State of Haryana; and(d) whether it is a fact that block level water conservation plans have been prepared in the State of Haryana and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI  (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU) (a) The Dynamic Ground Water Resources of the country are assessed jointly by Central Ground Water Board (CGWB) and State Governments. As per the 2020 assessment, out of the total 141 assessment units (Blocks) in Haryana, 85 Blocks have been categorized as ‘Over-exploited’, 12 blocks as ‘Critical’ and  14 Blocks as ‘Semi- critical’, the details of which is at Annexure-I. (b) Jal Shakti Abhiyan-I (JSA-I) implemented  in 2019 in 1592 blocks out of 2836 blocks in 256 water stressed districts of the country to promote water conservation and water resource management by focusing on accelerated implementation of five target interventions, viz. water conservation & rain water harvesting, renovation of traditional and other water bodies/tanks, reuse and recharge of bore wells, watershed development and intensive  afforestation.  “Jal Shakti Abhiyan: Catch the Rain”  JSA:CTR implemented in 2021 in all districts (Urban and Rural) of the country with five focused interventions- (1) rainwater harvesting & water conservation (2) enumerating, geo-tagging & making inventory of all water bodies; preparation of scientific plans for water conservation (3) Setting up Jal Shakti Kendras in all districts (4) intensive afforestation and (5) awareness generation.(c)  81 blocks in 19 districts were included in JSA 2019 in Haryana while 143 blocks of all 22 districts were covered in JSA:CTR 2021. The details of blocks included in JSA 2019 are given in Annexure-II.
