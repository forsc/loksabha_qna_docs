                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1667






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






ROB/FOB in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Road Over Bridges (ROBs), Foot Over Bridges (FOBs) and Under Bridges proposed to be constructed by Railways in Jharkhand, location wise;  (b)	whether any target has been fixed for completing the construction of all proposed ROBs, FOBs and Under Bridges and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the number of ROBs, FOBs and Under Bridges completely built and commissioned during the last three years; and(d)	the number of ROBs, FOBs and Under Bridges projects which are pending or have not been commissioned?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d):  38 Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) and 153 Railway Under Bridges (RUBs) in lieu of Level Crossings (LCs) and 72 Foot Over Bridges (FOBs) at various railway stations are proposed to be constructed in the State of Jharkhand.     During last three years and the current year, 23 ROBs, 91 RUBs and 36 FOBs have been  completed in the State of Jharkhand.     Above ROBs/RUBs and FOBs are in various stages of planning, design and development and are targeted to be commissioned in stages.*****
