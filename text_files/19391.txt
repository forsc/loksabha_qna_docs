                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

578






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Khelo India Centres of Excellence




Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has inaugurated 8 Khelo India Centres of Excellence in Various States recently;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of criteria for the eight States selected for the location of these Centre for Excellence;(d) whether the Government Proposes to provide a centre of excellence in the State of Andhra Pradesh; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. The Government has inaugurated eight Khelo India Centres of Excellence in various states recently. The details of the same are at Annexure.(c) These centres have been identified based on the recommendation of the State Governments. Factors such as sports science setup, availability of infrastructure and manpower, past achievements, etc. were taken into consideration while identifying these centres.(d) and (e) Yes, Sir. Dr. YSR Sports School, YSR District, Kadapa, Andhra Pradesh, has been approved as Khelo India State Centre of Excellence.*****
