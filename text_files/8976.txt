                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2491






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Rural Development Schemes




Ganesan Selvam













                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of welfare/rural development schemes being run by the ministry for tribals residing in the country;(b)	whether the Government has been able to achieve the target set for implementation of rural development schemes in tribal areas;(c)	the problems faced by the Government in implementing rural development schemes in tribal areas;(d)	whether the State Governments have utilized the funds allocated for rural development schemes;(e)	if so, the details of funds utilized/unutilized during the last three years and the current year, scheme-wise and State-wise; and(f)	whether the Government has taken any effective steps to resolve the problem of the said unutilized funds in various States and for the optimum utilization of funds allocated under various rural development schemes and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (c):	For development of rural areas through employment generation, strengthening of livelihood opportunities, promoting self-employment, skilling of rural youths, provision of social assistance, infrastructure development etc., the Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM), Deen Dayal Upadhyay – Gramin Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY),  Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), Shyama Prasad Mukherjee RuRBAN Mission, National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) and Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) in the country including tribal areas. The Ministry of Rural Development released 99.52 percent of its total outlay for 2018-19 under different schemes to the States/ UTs, including tribal areas.Whenever, any problem with respect to implementation of any scheme is brought to the notice of the Ministry, the matter is immediately taken up with the concerned State Government/UT for necessary action and corrective measures.(d)	to (f):	The funds under various programmes are released to States/UTs in accordance with the programme guidelines. The Department constantly pursues the issue of fund utilization with the state/UT governments through Performance Review Committee Meetings and monitoring the submission of Utilization Certificates (UCs) to ensure optimum utilization of funds and to ensure timely disbursement and sound financial management.In order to ensure the programme benefits reach the rural poor, the Ministry has evolved a comprehensive multi-level and multi-tool system of monitoring and evaluation of the implementation of rural development programmes, including Performance Review Committee Meetings, District Development Co-ordination and Monitoring Committee named as “DISHA” Committee meetings, National Level Monitors, Area Officers Schemes, Common Review Mission, Concurrent Evaluation and Impact Assessment Studies. Third-Party evaluation of Ministry of Rural Development’s Schemes is also carried out regularly and actions, as appropriate, are taken on the findings. The State/ UT-wise funds utilized/ Expenditure incurred under various rural development schemes during last three years and current year are given at Annexure-I(a) to I(g).
