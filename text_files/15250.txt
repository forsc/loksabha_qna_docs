                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3999






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Cadre Service Allocation




Devendrappa Y














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Government proposes for examination based cadre service allocation to UPSC qualified probationers; (b) 	if so, the details of the present practice in this regard and of divergent plans proposed; and (c) 	whether the new system would pay weightage and thus respect position to the top most posts of civil servants and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b): Service allocation to the candidates recommended by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) on the basis of Civil Services Examination (CSE) is done on the basis of rank in the CSE, preferences of various participating services expressed by them, medical status, eligibility of the candidate and vacancy position in the relevant category at his/her turn. Further, Cadre Allocation to the All India Services (AIS) Officers is done on the basis of rank, preferences for various zones and cadres and vacancy position in the relevant category at his/her turn as per the Cadre Allocation Policy applicable for the CSE Year. (c):   Does not arise*****
