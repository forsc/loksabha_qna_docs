                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

670






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Implementation of PM-JAY in Telangana




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri - Jan ArogyaYojana (PM-JAY) health insurance scheme is being implemented in the State of Telangana and if so, the details thereof;(b) the total number of beneficiaries covered under the scheme in the State;(c) the total number of beneficiaries covered under the scheme for COVID-19 treatment in Telangana; and(d) the total expenditure incurred under the scheme for treatment of patients in Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d): Ayushman Bharat -Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is implemented in the State of Telangana since 18th May 2021.A total of 87.6 lakh beneficiary families are eligible for healthcare benefits under the scheme in Telangana.As of 24th January 2022, a total of 2.39 lakh hospital admissions worth Rs. 517.7 crore are authorized for treatment, including for COVID-19, of patients in Telangana.*****
