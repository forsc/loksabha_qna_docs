                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

234






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Train Ambulance Services




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Indian Railways provides train ambulance facility in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Railways is considering to have dedicated train ambulance services to transfer the poor sick passengers from one destination to another and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Railways has any facility to transport dead bodies from one place to another by train and if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, whether the Railways is considering to develop such facility so that people can transport the dead bodies of their relatives?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d) : A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF STARRED QUESTION NO.234 BY DR. (PROF.) KIRIT PREMJIBHAI SOLANKI TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 04.12.2019 REGARDING TRAIN AMBULANCE SERVICES(a) No, Sir.(b) No, Sir. (c) Yes, Sir. Facility for transportation of dead bodies (corpses) through Railways has been available since long. A dead body can be transported through Railways duly following the laid down provisions. (d): Does not arise. *****
