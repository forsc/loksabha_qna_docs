                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3600






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Teaching/Non-Teaching Vacancies




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a large number of posts of teachers and non-teaching staff are lying vacant in CentralUniversity, Prayagraj due to which teaching work is badly affected in the University;(b) whether the Government proposes to fill the said vacant posts in a time bound manner as perreservation provisions;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said posts are likely to be filled; and(d) whether the Government proposes to implement the teacher-student ratio fixed by theUniversity Grants Commission (UGC), if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): The number of vacant teaching and non-teaching posts in the University of Allahabad,a Central University in Prayagraj, is 550 and 519 respectively. The University has alreadyadvertised all the vacant teaching posts and 32 non-teaching posts. The University GrantsCommission (UGC) has prepared the Guidelines for Recruitment of Faculty in Universities,Colleges and Institutions Deemed to be Universities outlining the selection procedure and thetime frame for recruitment which have been circulated vide its letter dated 4th June, 2019 to allUniversities to adhere to the guidelines.(d): In order to implement the teacher-student ratio, the additional teaching posts are sanctionedby UGC on the basis of workload of teaching through an expert committee.*****
