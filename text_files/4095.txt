                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3647






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Connectivity of Villages in Rajasthan




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether all the villages and Dhanis of Bharatpur and Alwar Constituency of Rajasthan have been connected under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether any time frame has been fixed for completion of the said work and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	The unit of programme under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) is a habitation and not a revenue village. Since inception till 12th July, 2019, out of 550 eligible and feasible habitations of more than 250 populations as per 2001 Census in Alwar district, 548 habitations have already been provided connectivity. In Bharatpur district, out of 357 eligible and feasible habitations of more than 250 populations as per 2001 Census, 355 habitations have already been provided connectivity during the same period. State has reported that work for connecting 1 habitation is in progress under other scheme whereas remaining habitations are in the process of de-sanctioning.*****
