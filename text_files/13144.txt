                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2053






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






FPIs in Telangana




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government for setting up of Food Processing Industries in the State of Telangana and in Mahabubnagar Parliamentary Constituency, Telangana;(b)	whether the Government had conducted any study in the State of Telangana for setting up of food processing industries;(c)	if not, the steps taken for setting up of food processing industry in the State including necessary measures taken for setting up Food Industries in the sector of NH-44 Kothur to Bangalore and NH-167 Kothur to Raichur; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d): The Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing the Central Sector Umbrella Scheme- PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA (PMKSY) with the scheme components viz: (i) Mega Food Parks, (ii) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, (iii) Infrastructure for Agro-processing Clusters, (iv) Creation of Backward and Forward Linkages (v) Creation / Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities, (vi) Food Safety and Quality Assurance Infrastructure and (vii) Human Resources and Institutions (viii) Operation Greens for, inter alia, creation of modern infrastructure and efficient supply chain for facilitating preservation and processing of various agricultural and allied produce like vegetables, fruits, milk, meat & poultry. Under these schemes credit-linked capital subsidy in the form of grants-in-aid is provided to  the individuals, farmers, Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), Entrepreneurs, Cooperatives, Societies, Self Help Groups (SHGs), Private Companies and Central/ State PSUs etc. for setting up of food processing industries/ units/ projects across the country including in the State of Telangana. These schemes are demand-driven. Financial assistance is provided to the eligible applicants and against expressions of interests issued by MoFPI from time to time.The details of projects assisted/being assisted under PMKSY in various part of Telangana state including in Mahabubnagar Parliamentary Constituency is at Annexure.MoFPI has not conducted any study for setting up of food processing industries, which is specific to state of Telangana.*****
