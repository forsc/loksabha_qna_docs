                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2608






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






FPI in Singur




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to set up a food industry in the barren land of the Singur Tata Nano plant; and(b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) &(b):  No, Sir. The Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) does not set up any food processing industries/ units/projects/plants on its own. It provides financial assistance as capital subsidy in the form of grant-in-aid to the individuals, farmers, Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), Entrepreneurs, Cooperatives, Societies, Self Help Groups (SHGs), Private Companies and Central/ State PSUs etc. under its umbrella scheme of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for setting up of food processing industries/ units/ projects.  *****
