                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3137






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Proposals regarding Old Age Homes




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.†3137TO BE ANSWERED ON 22.03.2022PROPOSALS REGARDING OLD AGE HOMES †3137. SHRI GANESH SINGH: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has received any proposals from the States during the year 2021-22 under the schemes regarding old age homes and prohibition and if so, the details thereof; (b) the number of proposals out of the said proposals approved along with the number of proposals which are pending, State-wise including Madhya Pradesh; and (c) the time by which the said proposals are likely to be approved?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) to (c): Under the Scheme, State Action Plan for Senior Citizens (SAPSrC) a component of Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana (AVYAY), there is no provision of releasing  funds  to State/UT Governments for Senior Citizen Homes. Under the, National Action Plan of Drug Demand Reduction (NAPDDR), there is no provision of funds to be released to State/UT Govts. under State Action Plan from current financial year 2021-22 onwards.*****
