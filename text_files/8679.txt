                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2240






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






UGC




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether some top management and other important posts are lying vacant in the institutions like University Grants Commission that are dealing with higher education; (b) if so, the details of such posts; (c) since when these posts are lying vacant, post-wise; (d) whether the charge of these posts have been given and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) to (e): Filling up of vacant posts in the institutions is a continuous and ongoing process. The University Grants Commission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) are two important Regulatory Bodies dealing with higher education, under the aegis of the Ministry. The posts of Chairman and Vice-Chairman are presently filled up in both the UGC and the AICTE. The posts of Secretary, UGC and Member-Secretary, AICTE are also not vacant. Presently, 3 posts of Members are vacant in UGC.******
