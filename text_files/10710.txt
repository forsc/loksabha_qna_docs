                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Four Laning Project on NH-17




Francisco Cosme Sardinha














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of four laning project on National Highway (NH)-17 (renamed as NH-66) from km 535.500 to km 611.000; (b)	whether the cross drainage requirements of the project have been taken into considerations in view of the problems faced by the people along the above stretch during the monsoon;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the time frame set for the completion of the project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (d) The present status along with target date for completion of four/six laning projects from Km 535.50 to Km 611.00 on NH-17 (New NH-66) is annexed.(b) & (c) Yes, the requirement of cross drainage works involving construction of 23 box/pipe culverts in Canacona bypass project and 84 box/pipe culverts in Margao bypass project have been approved as per site requirement.
