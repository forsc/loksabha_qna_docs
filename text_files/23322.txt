                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4393






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Production of Maize




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether maize production for 2020-21 crop year is expected to be 30 million tonnes;(b) 	if so, the details of increase in the production this year compared to last year;(c) 	whether the Ministry feels that it is sufficient to meet the domestic demand or is there a need for import of maize;(d) 	if so, the details of import proposed by the Ministry;(e) 	whether any consultations have been held with poultry representatives about their requirement of maize and how the Ministry is going to meet the same; and(f) 	if so, the outcome of such consultations?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????             (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	The production of maize is 30.16 million tonnes during 2020-21 (2nd Advance Estimates). The production of maize has increased from 28.77 million tonnes in 2019-20 to 30.16 million tonnes during 2020-21. (c) & (d): 	The import and export of maize is dependent on various factors viz,. domestic demand of diverse sectors, production, prices in domestic & international markets etc. The maize is utilized for poultry feed (47%), animal feed (13%), industrial (starch) products (14%), human food/processed food (20%), exports & other purposes (6%). Import/Exports are market driven. There is no any proposal of the Ministry for import of maize.(e) & (f): 	Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry & Dairy, on the basis of representations received from Poultry Industry conducts meeting to resolve their issues with regard to maize prices for poultry feed. Besides, Government of Haryana has also launched a scheme Mera Pani Meri Virasat to diversify paddy towards maize in 2020-21. *****
