                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2362






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






SPV under SCM




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether a number of cities under the Smart City Mission (SCM) have formed the Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) and if so, the details thereof; and (b)      the structure, composition and the number of members of each SPV formed  under the cities?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                             (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	 & (b) As per the Smart City Mission Statement & Guidelines, implementation of the Mission at the Smart City level will be done by a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) created for the purpose. The SPV will be a Limited Company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, in which the State/Union Territory and the Urban Local Body (ULB) will be the promoters having 50:50 equity shareholding.  The Board of Directors of the SPV will have representatives of Central Government, State Government, ULB and Independent Directors,  in addition to the Chief Executive Officer and Functional Directors. Additional Directors may be taken on the Board by SPV, as considered necessary. The SPVs plan, appraise, approve, implement, manage, operate, monitor and evaluate their Smart City projects.  All 100 Smart Cities have formed their SPVs.                                             ….
