                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5057






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






NESRIP Project




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the work of Barpeta Kalitakuchi (58.5 km) road is going on under North Eastern States Road Investment Programme (NESRIP) project;(b) 	if so, the progress of the said work;(c) 	the time by which the work is likely to be completed; and(d) 	whether it will be completed within the stipulated time-frame?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development ofNorth Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a)	Yes Sir, the work of “Barpeta to Kalitakuchi” (58.5 Km) road is going on in the State of Assam under ADB assisted North Eastern States Road Investment Programme (NESRIP) project.(b)	Physical progress of the said work is 90.73% as on 31st May, 2019.(c)	The work is likely to be completed by December, 2019.(d)	The work is expected to be completed within the stipulated timeframe.
