                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3846






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






National Law University Odisha




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the progress made by the Government on the demand made by the Government of Odisha to reserve twenty five percent seats in the National Law University Odisha(NLUO) for the students belonging to the State of Odisha;(b)   whether the Government has taken note that the delay in taking a decision in this regard has affected the academic career of local aspirants; and(c)   the time by which the Government is likely to take a decision in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c)  The National Law University Odisha vide Notification dated 04.01.2020 has approved providing of 25% horizontal reservation to the students who have domicile of State of Odisha across categories for the admission to undergraduate and post-graduate programmes at National Law University Odisha.
