                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4106






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Wheat Based Nutrition Programme




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    )      whether the Government is aware that 2241 MTs wheat and 1200 MTs rice were allotted to the State of Kerala under wheat based nutrition programme for the 3rd and 4th quarters of 2020-2021; (b)       if so, the details thereof; (c)      whether the Government is aware that the number of beneficiaries has increased under the programme during the COVID-19 pandemic; (d)       if so, the details thereof; and (e)      whether the Government is considering to provide an additional allotment of rice and wheat for the 3rd  and 4th  quarter of 2020-2021 due to COVID pandemic and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a)  to  (e):        Yes Sir.  Based on the data furnished by the State,  revised allocation of food grains has already been made to state of Kerala,  as per their entitlement for the 4th quarter  in 2020-21. *****
