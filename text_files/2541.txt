                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2389






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Maharashtra




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Kendriya Vidyalayas across the country, State and district-wise;(b) whether the Government has taken any steps to open Kendriya Vidyalayas inthose districts where there are no Kendriya Vidyalayas;(c) if so, whether the Government proposes to open such Vidyalayas inOsmanabad (Maharashtra) Parliamentary Constituency as there is noVidyalaya ; and(d) if so, the time by which it is likely to be started in view of the demands of thepublic?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) As on date, 1206 Kendriya Vidyalayas are functioning across the country.State and district-wise details are annexed.(b)& (c) Proposals for opening of new KVs are considered only if sponsored byMinistries or Departments of the Government of India/State Governments / UnionTerritories Administrations thereby committing resources for setting up a new KV aswell as the availability of necessary sanction of the Government. The proposalsreceived from various sponsoring authorities for opening of new KVs also have tocompete among other proposals under the “Challenge Method”.No proposal has been received in the prescribed proformae from the StateGovernment of Maharashtra for opening of a new KV in Osmanabad ParliamentaryConstituency in Maharashtra.(d) Does not arise.
