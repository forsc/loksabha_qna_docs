                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1898






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Availablity of Oxygen Beds and Ventilators




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Kalyan Banerjee
                                                








                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)	The total number of ventilators support as well as Oxygen support beds available in various categories of hospital in the country, State/UT and category-wise; and;(b)	The number of Oxygen beds and ventilator beds added between March 2020 till date






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARTI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (b): As reported by the States on COVID India portal, State/UT total number of ventilators support & oxygen support beds available for COVID-19 management are at Annexure. The number of oxygen beds added between April 2020 till date for managing COVID-19 cases in the country, as reported by states till 28th July 2021 is 3,71,279. The number of ventilator beds added between April 2020 till date for managing COVID-19 cases in the country, as reported by states till 28th July 2021 is 44,199.
