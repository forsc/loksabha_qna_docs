                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1479






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Renovation of Railway Bridges




A. Chellakumar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Railway bridges in various zones of Indian Railways, Zone-wise;(b) whether any study/survey has been conducted to assess the present condition, repair and renovation of bridges, if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has introduced any scheme for reconstruction/strengthening of rail bridges, State-wise especially in the backward and tribal areas of Maharashtra, if so, the details thereof as on date; and(d) the funds allocated and utilised for the said schemes during the last three years and the current financial year along with status of such bridges?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  *****
