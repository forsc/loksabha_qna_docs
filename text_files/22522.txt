                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3615






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Adarsh Station Scheme




Kripanath Mallah













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                








                                                    Jaskaur Meena
                                                








                                                    Pankaj Chowdhary
                                                








                                                    Janardan Singh Sigriwal
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Kirit Premjibhai Solanki
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of Adarsh Station Scheme; (b) the number and the details of railway stations identified to be upgraded/renovated under this scheme in the country including Assam, Maharashtra, Bihar and Rajasthan; (c) the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this scheme during the last three years, State and year-wise; (d) the details of the target set and achievements made so far along with the results thereof; (e) the details of the number of stations pending to be upgraded/renovated; and (f) the time by which these are likely to be upgraded?   






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
