                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1055






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Allocations under NRDWP




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated to Rajasthan to provide clean drinking water under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) during each of the last three years; (b) the districts where this programme is being implemented; (c) the district-wise number of villages in the rural areas of the State which do not have access to drinking water; (d) whether any measures have been taken to protect the water resources as reservoirs to tackle drought situation under NRDWP; (e) if so, the details thereof; and (f) the other steps taken to ensure sufficient supply of clean drinking water all through the year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) The funds allocated to Rajasthan to provide clean drinking water under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) during each of the last three years are as under:-	(Rs. in Crore)Financial Year	Funds allocated (Central)2016-17	1072.922017-18	714.242018-19	655.41(b) & (c) As reported by State Government of Rajasthan, the NRDWP is being implemented in all 33 districts of Rajasthan.  The district wise details of habitations in the rural areas of the State which do not have access to drinking water are at Annexure.(d) & (e) To protect the water resources as reservoirs to tackle drought situation, artificial recharge and rain water harvesting are being implemented under various other schemes of Government of India i.e. Pradhan MantriKrishiSinchayeeYojana (PMKSY), Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP) and Command Area Development & Water Management (CAD & WM) Programme etc.(f) Rural drinking water supply is a state subject. However, Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation assists the States for improving the coverage of rural drinking water supply, by providing them with technical and financial assistance through the centrally sponsored National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP). Further, under NRDWP, States can also take up water supply schemes to provide safe water to quality affected habitations.
