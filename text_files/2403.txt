                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2158






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






New NHs in Odisha




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any new National Highways (NHs) in the State of Odisha has been sanctioned by the Government recently;(b) 	if so, the name of these National Highways;(c) 	whether the Government has allocated more funds for widening and converting highways  of the State into international standards during last four years; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Twelve National Highways with an aggregate length of 1114 kms have been        Notified from April, 2014 to till date. Details are at Annexure-I.(c) & (d)   Development & maintenance of National Highways is a continuous process and the same are carried out based on traffic requirement, availability of funds and inter se priority of works. Funds allocated for development of National Highways in Odisha during the last four years is Rs. 6276 Cr.
