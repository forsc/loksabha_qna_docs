                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2720






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Welfare of De-Notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ashok Mahadeorao Nete
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has not made key appointments to the Board for development and welfare of De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-nomadic communities announced in February; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for the delay;(c) whether the Government had set up any committee under NITI Aayog to identify de-notified tribes, nomadic tribes, semi-nomadic tribes and notified tribes that are not yet formally classified; (d) if so, the details thereof along with the mandate of the said committee; (e) whether the Government proposes to implement the recommendations of Renke Commission and Idate Commission set up for these communities; and (f) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b): As per the cabinet approval the following action has already been taken regarding key appointments to the Board for development and welfare of De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-nomadic communities: I.	Appointment of Chairperson and Members of the Board has been notified vide gazette notification dated 11.03.2019. II.	Joint Secretary, D/o Social Justice and Empowerment has been appointed as ex-officio Member of the DWBDNCs vide gazette notification dated 26.06.2019. (c) & (d): Yes, a committee under the Chairmanship of Vice Chairman, NITI Aayog has been setup to complete the process of identification of the De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Communities that have not yet been formally classified by the National Commission for De-Notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes (NCDNT) also known as Idate Commission. (e)&(f): Pursuant to announcement made by Hon''ble Finance Minister during interim budget speech and after consideration of the recommendations made by the National Commission for Denotified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes (NCDNT), also known as Idate Commission, a proposal was sent vide Cabinet Note dated 15th February, 2019 soliciting approval of the Cabinet for Setting up a Development and Welfare Board for Denotified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Communities (DWBDNCs) and Setting up of a Committee under the chairpersonship of Vice-Chairman, NITI Aayog alongwith three members to be nominated by the Government of India, to complete the process of identification of the Denotified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Communities (DNCs) that have not yet been formally classified.****
