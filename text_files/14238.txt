                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2988






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






New Railway Lines beside Paddy Fields




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether due to laying of new railway lines beside paddy fields, the nearby landscape level has been raised thereby obstructing movement of farm equipment/vehicle of poor farmers in Tamil Nadu; (b)	if so, whether the Government has issued any preventive measures in this regard and an advisory for providing an alternate route for the farmers by the concerned authorities has also been issued; (c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d):  For construction of new lines, Railway  acquire  only a small strip of  land. Height of Railway embankment is finalised taking into account the topography of areas and Highest Flood Level (HFL) in the area etc.  Whenever New Line is laid by Railway, sufficient provisions in the form of Level Crossing, Subways, Road Under Bridges (RUB), Road Over Bridges (ROB), etc. is made depending on local demand, site conditions etc., to ensure farmers do not face difficulty in crossing the track including farming equipment/vehicle.*****
