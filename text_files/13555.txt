                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2392






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Road Projects in Karnataka




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and the present status of road projects which  have  been  started  in  Karnataka  during the last four years and the current year;(b) 	whether the Government is aware that the work of four laning of Kundapur to Surathkal and Nantoor toTalapady sections of NH-17 in  Karnataka has yet not been completed and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has set any time-frame for completion of Kundapur to Surathkal and Nantoor to Talapady section of NH-17 and if so, the details thereof; and(d)  	the  present  status  of  four  laning  of Goa-Karnataka Border to Kundapur section of NH-66, four laning of Addahole to Bantwal section of NH-48 and four laning of Tumkur to Shimoga section of NH-206 (Package I, II and III)?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) 	2/4/6-laning, strengthening, improvement etc. works in the length of about 3,200 km have been started during the last 4 years and the current year in the State of Karnataka, out of which, length of about 2,000 km have been completed.  (b) & (c)  The balance work of four laning of Kundapur to Surathkal and Nantoor to Talapady section of NH-17 has been delayed due to delay in land acquisition, deletion of Mulky and Padubidri Bypasses, delay in approval of GAD for ROB, delay in utility shifting, financial crisis with the Concessionaire etc. Progress of these projects is monitored at various levels for ensuring early completion.(d)	150 km road stretch has been completed in the 4-laning project of Goa-Karnataka Border to Kundapur section of NH-66 (187.24 km). The work i.e. 4-laning of Addahole to Bantwal section of NH-48 was started in March, 2017 but Contractor has requested for pre-closure of the project due to various reasons.  Therefore, fresh DPR has been initiated. The civil construction works in the stretch from Tumkur to Shimoga section of NH-206 have been started in Packages I and II.  Moreover, work has also been awarded in Package III.*****
