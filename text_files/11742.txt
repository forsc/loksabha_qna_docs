GDP Growth




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the quarterly rate of growth of the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) from the 2004-2005 fiscal year till September 2019 quarter as per the methodology used to calculate it prior to the changes introduced in 2015; and(b)	the quarterly rate of GDP growth from 2004-05 till September, 2019 based on the new methodology introduced by the Government in 2015?
