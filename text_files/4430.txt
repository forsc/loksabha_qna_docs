                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Creation of Separate IAS/IPS Cadre




Tapir Gao














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from the Government of Arunachal Pradesh for creation of a separate IAS/IPS cadre for Arunachal Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the steps taken so far by the Government for creation of separate IAS/ IPS cadre of Arunachal Pradesh; and (c) 	whether any time frame has been kept by the Government for creation of separate IAS/IPS cadre for Arunachal Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (c):  The State Government of Arunachal Pradesh, among other State Governments, has submitted proposal for creation of a separate cadre for IAS/IPS/IFS officers for the State.  Such proposals are examined on the basis of consensus amongst the constituents of the Joint Cadre Authority by the Ministry of Home Affairs.*****
