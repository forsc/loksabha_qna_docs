                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3635






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Conservation of Jagannath Temple




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the fact that Shree Jagannath Temple, Puri  has developed some cracks;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the ASI has been entrusted the work of conservation and to complete it in a time-bound manner; and(d)	if so, the timeline provided to the ASI to complete the task?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) 	The conservation work of Shree Jagannath Temple is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) and continuous watch is kept on its condition.  The minor cracks are monitored and attended regularly which have no structural distress so far. (c) & (d)	Yes Sir, the conservation work of protected monuments including Shree Jagannath Temple is a continuous process.  The major works identified at this temple have been completed in a time bound manner, which have also been appreciated by the Hon’ble High Court of Odisha.
