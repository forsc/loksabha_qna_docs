                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

142






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Pradhan Mantri Garib Anna Kalyan Yojana




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to continue the distribution of food grains under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) Phase V in the light of the continuing impact of Covid pandemic on vulnerable communities; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the State-wise and month-wise details of central allocation and food grain distribution under phase I to V; (d) 	the total number of people benefited by PMGKAY in the Bolangir of Odisha and Jalpaiguri of West Bengal; and (e) 	the steps being taken by the Government for effective implementation of PMGKAY?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RTHE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)__________________________________________________________________________________(a) & (b): 	The distribution of foodgrains under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojna (PMGKAY) scheme (Phase-V) commenced in December, 2021. The total foodgrains allocation under PMGKAY-V was about 159 LMT out of which about 47.5 LMT of foodgrains have been distributed till 28.01.2022. .......2/--2-(c): 	The details are placed at Annexure-I.(d): 	Additional foodgrains @ 5 kg per person per month for around 324 lakh beneficiaries including those in Bolangir, to State of Odisha and for around 602 lakh beneficiaries including those in Jalpaiguri, to State of West Bengal, under Phase-I-Phase V of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) have been allocated for free of cost distribution.(e):  	Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) is operated under the joint responsibility of the Central and the State/UT Governments. The responsibility of identification of beneficiaries under TPDS (and PMGKAY also, as targeted beneficiaries are same) lies with respective State/UT.  Complaints regarding NFSA and /or PMGKAY or any State/UTs which are received in this Department, regarding any irregularities in the functioning of the TPDS are forwarded to the concerned State/UT Government for inquiry and appropriate action at their end. Regular review of foodgrains distribution under PMGKAY is done, which includes discussions, reports etc.Information, Education and Communication (IEC) Campaigns through social media, banners and creatives at prominent public places all over the country has been carried out to create better awareness of the PMGKAY among the targeted beneficiaries.***** Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY
