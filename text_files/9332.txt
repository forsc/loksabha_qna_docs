                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2949






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Surveys under Indian Bridge Management System




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any survey under Indian Bridge Management System for North Eastern States including Assam is pending with the Government;(b)	if so, the details and present status thereof;(c)	whether the Government has formed any task force to promote better management and strong structure of bridges in Assam; and(d)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OFROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b)    Ministry has conducted inventory and condition survey of bridges on National Highways in India under Indian Bridge Management System. The inventory of all bridges has been carried out one time in a year and condition survey has been carried out twice in a year (pre monsoon and post monsoon). Inventory and condition survey data of bridges on National Highways in North Eastern States including Assam has been collected through the consultant from November, 2015 to till March, 2019. (c) Government has not formed any specific task force to promote better management and strong structure of bridges in Assam. However, all bridges are kept in traffic worthy condition through repair, rehabilitation and reconstruction as assessed and required by field officials for better management of existing bridges based on regular inspection and monitoring so that safe and uninterrupted flow of vehicles can be ensured. (d) Does not arise. *****
