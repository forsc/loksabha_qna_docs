                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Funds to Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS)




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has allocated any funds for setting up Eklavya Model Residential Schools in Madhya Pradesh; and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b) Yes, Sir. Government has allocated funds for setting up and running of Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) in Madhya Pradesh which is as follows:-(Rs. in lakh) Year	2020-21	2021-22 (till 30.11.2021)	Fund released for Recurring expenses	Fund released for Creation of Capital Assets	Fund released for Recurring expenses*	Fund released for Creation of Capital AssetsFunds released to Madhya Pradesh for setting up and running of EMRS	10583.36	3876.00	-	40.00 *Proposal for recurring funds has not been received from the state for the year 2021-22 till date since sufficient fund is available with the State Government.  *****
