                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






PMGSY in Jammu & Kashmir and Madhya Pradesh




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Vishnu Dutt Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the targets fixed and achieved under the various stages of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Jammu and Kashmir and Madhya Pradesh along with the details thereof; and(b) 	the funds released for the maintenance of roads constructed under the said scheme in Jammu and Khajuraho including Bundelkhand during the last three years alongwith the road-wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	The details relating to Road works and Long Span Bridges (LSBs) sanctioned and completed including the status of habitations connectivity in respect of Jammu & Kashmir and Madhya Pradesh under various verticals of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) are given at Annexure.(b) 	As per the programme guidelines, maintenance of roads constructed under PMGSY is the responsibility of the State Governments and Central Government doesn’t provide any funds in this regard. However, some of the better performing States are provided financial incentives based on the pre-defined parameters which they use for maintenance of roads constructed under PMGSY.During the last three years and the current year, the details of financial incentive provided to Jammu & Kashmir and Madhya Pradesh are as under-State/UT	                      Financial Incentive (Rs. in Crore)	                             2017-18	2018-19	   2019-20	2020-21           Total                                                                    (as on 28/02/2021) Jammu & Kashmir	                       0.00	  9.31	     0.00	  0.00	          9.31Madhya Pradesh	                     219.55	144.19	   180.27	198.98	        742.99Road-wise details of funds spent from financial incentive on maintenance of roads constructed under PMGSY can be seen at www.omms.nic.in> Expenditure on Renewal of Roads (Centre column). ***** Note- Targets for road length have not been allocated for PMGSY-I and RCPLWEA.*****
