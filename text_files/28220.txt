                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3548






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Cooperation among Ministries




Arjunlal Meena













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Aparajita Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry will undertake activities regarding co-operation amongst multiple Ministries, if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of various projects and missions which the Ministry will undertake; and(c) whether the Ministry will oversee the functions of National Institute of One Health as the implementation of a comprehensive One Health Policy would require co-operation from multiple Ministries, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ?????? (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a)	As per the Government of India (Allocation of Business) Rule, 1961 Ministry of Cooperation will oversee General Policy in the field of Co-operation and Co-ordination of Co-operation activities in all sectors. However the Ministries concerned are responsible for Co-operatives in the fields.(b)	The plans and programme for cooperatives will emerge as per the need of the Sector. (c)	No, Sir. ****
