                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3900






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Livestock Census




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has conducted livestock (cow, buffalo, goat, etc) census;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (c) whether the Government proposes to provide nutritious animal food to cattlemen/cowsheds at an affordable or subsidized rates;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, whether the Government is likely to make provision to provide nutritious animal food in the same manner as food grains are provided to the people at affordable rates?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI  PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a): Yes, the Government has conducted the latest 20th Livestock Census in 2019 with the participation of State/Union Territory Governments for total 16 livestock species including cow, buffalo, goat etc. Livestock Census is being conducted in every five-year interval and so far 20 Livestock Censuses had been conducted in the country. Moreover, the 20th Livestock Census was the first Livestock Census conducted with the use of mobile technology through tablet computers and further transmitting the data online.The key results of the 20th Livestock Census has also been released in the publication titled “20th Livestock Census-2019 All India Report”.(b): The State/UT-wise details of 20th Livestock Census is given in Annexure-I.(c) to (e): Central Government in conjunction with the State Governments and Union Territories has already taken necessary steps to provide nutritional fodder to the animals through various ongoing Centrally Sponsored Schemes viz., National Livestock Mission (NLM) with a Sub Mission on Fodder and Feed Development and Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY). Under these schemes, financial assistance is provided to all the States and Union Territories for various fodder development activities planned by them. Under National Livestock Mission, assistance is mainly provided for quality fodder seed production thereby increasing nutritional green fodders as well as incentivizing entrepreneurs for silage making/ fodder blocks/hay bailing units.	In addition, Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF) is also in operation since year 2020-21 for incentivizing investments for establishment of animal feed manufacturing units including establishment of animal feed testing laboratories thereby ensuring nutritional fodder and feed to the livestock.
