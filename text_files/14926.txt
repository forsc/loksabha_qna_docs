                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3492






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Skill India in Maharashtra




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil













                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the works done so far in the districts of Maharashtra under “Skill India” scheme during the last three years;(b)	the number of people trained and given employment through the above said scheme in Maharashtra, district-wise and year-wise; and(c)	the details of the works proposed in these districts under “Skill India” scheme and by when these will be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry is implementing  Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20 to impart Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) on pan-India basis including State of Maharashtra. Under this scheme, training is being imparted in various job roles. Apart from these, empanelled training centres mandatorily impart training to all enrolled candidates on Soft Skills, Entrepreneurship, Financial and Digital Literacy. As on 17.01.2020, the number of candidates trained under Short Term Training (STT) courses was 1,66,870 and 7,12,383 candidates were oriented under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) component of PMKVY 2016-20 in the State of Maharashtra. Out of the 1,18,486 certified candidates under Short Term Training, 49,142 have been placed in the State of Maharashtra.
