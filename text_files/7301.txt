                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

951






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Handloom Cluster in Maharashtra




Bharti Pravin Pawar













                                                    Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any proposal to set up handloom cluster in Maharashtra;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the details of the funds allocated in this regard and the locations thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d)	the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d):	6 Block Level Clusters (BLCs) have been sanctioned under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) on the basis of viable proposals recommended by State Government of Maharashtra to extend financial assistance for implementation of various interventions like Product Development, setting up of Common Facility Centre (CFC), design inputs, construction of workshed, purchase of technological up-gradation items (looms/accessories), lighting units, skill up-gradation etc. Details of location of BLCs sanctioned with total cost, Government of India share and funds released during last three years and current year (till date) are as under:- (Rs.in lakh)S. No.	Name/location of BLC and district	Total Cost	GoI Share	Amount released1.	Panch Gaon, Nagpur (Skill up-gradation)	8.560	8.560	8.5602.	Paithan, Aurangabad	172.735	168.4585	89.0303.	Mohadi, Bhandara	170.180	166.104	27.3204.	Solapur, Solapur	167.510	158.849	81.0205.	Yeola, Yeola	177.010	169.014	82.2806.	Panch Gaon, Nagpur	166.170	161.033	7.00Total	862.165	832.0185	295.21*****
