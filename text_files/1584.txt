                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1560






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Exploration and Production of Oil and Gas Reserves




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of districts of Rajasthan having Petroleum and Natural gas reserves from where oil and gas are being explored at present along with the details thereof;(b) the names of the companies engaged in exploration of oil and gas in different districts of Rajasthan; and(c) the annual revenue earned by the Government from the said companies along with the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM& NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): At present, oil and gas are explored from Jaisalmer, Barmer and Jalor districts of Rajasthan.(b): Oil & Natural Gas Corporation, Oil India Limited, Vedanta Limited, Focus Energy Limited and Bharat Petro Resource Limited are operating in the State of Rajasthan.(c): Exploration and Production companies operating in Rajasthan have paid ? 4257.55 crore as Royalty to State Government of Rajasthan and ? 4801.35 crore as Cess to Central Government during the financial year 2018-19.*****************
