                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2738






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Construction of Houses under PMAY




Upendra Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of affordable houses constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) for the benefit of urban poor during each of the last three years particularly in Uttar Pradesh, State/UT-wise;(b)	whether the Government has any time frame for completion of the yojana and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the new targets fixed under the yojana for the next three years, year/State/UT-wise; and(d)	the proposed action plan to achieve the desired targets?






ANSWER




                                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                        MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): The State/ Union Territory (UT)-wise total number of affordable houses constructed under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) during each of the last three years including Uttar Pradesh is at Annexure.(b)to (d): So far, out of 1.15 crore houses sanctioned under PMAY-U, a total of nearly 55.45 lakh has been completed and delivered to the beneficiaries. Remaining houses are at different stages of construction. The Ministry has advised States/UTs to expedite the construction of sanctioned houses so that these are completed within the stipulated timeline. There are no fixed yearly targets and houses are sanctioned based on project proposal submitted by States/UTs after approval of State Level Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (SLSMC).                                       *****
