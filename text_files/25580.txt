                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1389






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






FDI in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any information about FDI flow into Assam and if so, the details thereof during the last three years and current years, year-wise;(b) 	the value of actual investment compared to the value of signed MoU, in respect of Assam;(c) 	whether the FDI flow into Assam is comparable to other neighbouring States like West Bengal, Odisha, etc. and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	the details of the sectors where FDI is flowing into Assam and whether any study has been done by the Union Government on the issues relating to FDI climate in Assam; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a): 	The State-wise details of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) reported through equity inflow in the country are maintained w.e.f. October, 2019 onward. Accordingly, the financial-year wise details of FDI Equity inflow reported into Assam during October, 2019 to April, 2021 are as under:<pre>S. No.	Financial Year	FDI Equity inflow (Amount in US$)1.	2019-20 (Oct, 2019 to Mar, 2020)	29,34,398.912.	2020-21	1,11,88,071.473.	2021-22 (April, 2021)	- 	Total	1,41,22,470.38 </pre>(b): 	Data on actual investment compared to the value of signed MoU is not centrally maintained. (c): 	Yes Sir. The State-wise details of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) reported through equity inflow in the country during October, 2019 to April, 2021 are at Annexure-I. (d) & (e): 	The sector-wise details of FDI Equity inflow reported into Assam during October, 2019 to April, 2021 are at Annexure-II. No such study has been done by this department.********
