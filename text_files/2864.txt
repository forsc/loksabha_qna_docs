                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2716






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Online Registration of Farmers




Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of farmers in the country, State-wise;(b) 	the number of farmers for whom online registration has been completed in various States of the country including Uttar Pradesh; and(c) 	the time by which registration of the rest of the farmers are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	  The Agriculture Census 2015-16 contain figures of farmers in the country in the form of operational holding, which is defined as, “all land which is used wholly or partly for agricultural production and is operated as one technical unit by one person alone or with others without regard to the title, legal form, size or location”. Accordingly, a statement giving State/UT-wise number of operational holdings in the country is laid on the table of the House.(b):	There is no provision or scheme for any online registration of farmers in the Central Government.(c):	Does not arise.
