                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Promotion of CNG




Vinod Chavda













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of cities in the country having Compressed Natural Gas(CNG) service, State/UTwisedetails thereof;(b) whether the Government is considering to connect remaining cities of the country with theirservice and if so, the details thereof and whether it is also being proposed in Rajasthan andbordering States of the country; and(c) whether the Union Government is taking steps for promotion of green gas such as CNG in thecountry;(d) whether the Government is considering alternate other green fuels in the wake of inflatingdiesel prices and if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Government has any plan to increase the CNG pump in Kutch Lok Sabhaconstituency and if so, the details of the plan and policy?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c) Yes. Union Government is taking steps for promotion of green gas such as CNG in thecountry. The number of CNG stations in various States/UTs as on 31.03.2021 is at Annexure.Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) is the authority to grant authorization tothe entities for developing of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in Geographical Areas (GAs)as per PNGRB Act, 2006. PNGRB identifies GAs for authorizing the development of CGDnetwork in synchronization with the development of natural gas pipeline connectivity and naturalgas availability. As per Minimum Work Program assigned by PNGRB through competitivebidding the authorized entities will set up 8181 CNG stations including Rajasthan over a periodof 8-10 years across the country.(d) The Government has notified the National Policy on Biofuels-2018 on 04.06.2018. Anindicative target of 20% blending of ethanol in petrol and 5% blending of biodiesel in diesel isproposed by 2030 in the Policy. Further, in order to promote Compressed Bio Gas “SustainableAlternative Towards Affordable Transportation (SATAT)” initiative has been launched on1.10.2018 wherein Oil and Gas Marketing Companies (OGMCs) are inviting Expression ofInterest (EoI) from potential entrepreneur to procure CBG.(e) PNGRB has authorized Gujarat Gas Limited (GGL) for development of CGD network inKutch (West) GA. As on 31.05.2021, GGL has set up 12 CNG stations based on the demand andtechno-commercial viability in the GA.****
