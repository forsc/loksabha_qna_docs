                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Proposals for Tribal Sub-Plan




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details regarding the proposals received by the Government under the Tribal Sub-Plan Area during the last three years, State-wise; and (b) 	the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                         (a) & (b):	As per extant provisions, Scheduled Areas are co-terminus with Tribal Sub-Plan(TSP) Areas (i.e. Integrated Tribal Development Project (ITDP) / Integrated Tribal Development Agency (ITDA) only). Funds are provided to the States by Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA)under the Scheme ‘Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS)’ and ‘Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution’ for welfare and development of scheduled tribes in the ITDP / ITDA areaincluding Education, Health, Drinking Water, irrigation, connectivity, employment-cum-income generating programmes, skill development etc.Receipt of proposals from the State Governments under ‘SCA to TSS’ and ‘Grant under Article 275(1) of the Constitution’ is a continuous process. MoTA has adopted mechanism of Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) since 2014 for appraisal and approval of project proposals received from the State Governments under ‘SCA to TSS’ and ‘Grant under Article 275(1) of the Constitution’. The Committee is headed by Secretary (Tribal Affairs) with representatives of State Governments, Financial Advisor, Planning Commission, etc.  Minutes of the meetings delineating activities / projects approved for the State Governments under the said scheme and grant are invariably uploaded on the MoTA’s official website http://tribal.nic.in. Funds are released to the State Governments against the approved activities / projects keeping in view Utilization Certificate (UC) and Progress Report in respect of funds released in previous years. State wise details of fund released to State Governments under the scheme ‘SCA to TSS’ and ‘Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution’ for the last three years are at Annexure 1 and Annexure 2 respectively. Apart from above, State Governments utilize funds earmarked under State TSP for the development of tribals in the ITDP / ITDA area.
