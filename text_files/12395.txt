                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1193






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Kendriya Vidyalaya in Tamil Nadu




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to set up new Kendriya Vidyalayas inTamil Nadu particularly in Perambalur Parliamentary Constituency;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the total number of new KVs that have been established in the State of TamilNadu during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are opened primarily to cater to the educationalneeds of the wards of transferable Central Government Employees includingDefence personnel by providing a common programme of education. Proposals foropening of new KVs are considered only if sponsored by Ministries or Departmentsof the Government of India/State Governments / Union Territories Administrationsthereby committing resources for setting up a new KV as well as the availability ofnecessary sanction of the Government. The proposals received from varioussponsoring authorities for opening of new KVs also have to compete among otherproposals under the “Challenge Method”.Three Kendriya Vidyalayas were sanctioned in the State of Tamil Nadu inMarch, 2019 at BSF, Kittampalayam, ITBP Idayapatti and ITBP Shivgangai.Administrative orders for opening of these Vidyalayas can be issued by KendriyaVidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) only after the sponsoring authorities transfer therequisite extent of land in favour of KVS and also hand over the possession oftemporary building(s) for commencement of the Vidyalayas. KVS has informed thatno proposal has been received in the prescribed format from the State Governmentof Tamil Nadu for setting up of a new KV in Perambalur Parliamentary Constituency.(d) During the last three years 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and the current year,two new KVs have been opened in the State of Tamil Nadu at Golden RockTiruchirapalli and Udumalpet respectively.*****
