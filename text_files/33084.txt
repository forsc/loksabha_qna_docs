                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

465






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Dornier Aircraft




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Aparupa  Poddar
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                








                                                    C. R. Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has plans for the development and production of Dornier aircraft and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has initiated steps for research and development in this regard and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to sign any Memorandum of Understanding with a foreign player/ company in this regard and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has identified any facility for the production of Dornier Aircraft and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the quantum of funds allocated for research and development of Dornier Aircraft? 






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) Yes, Sir.  Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) a Defence Public Sector Undertaking (DPSU) under the Ministry of Defence has been manufacturing 19 Seater Dornier Do-228 aircraft  since  1983  under  Transfer  of  Technology  from  Germany. More  than  150  military Do-228 aircrafts have been produced and supplied to various customers.  Two civil Do-228 aircrafts have also been produced by HAL.(b)  The Research & Development efforts for an indigenous civil transport aircraft have been initiated by HAL to develop a prototype Hindustan-228 aircraft, based on Dornier Do-228 platform.(c)  HAL has signed two MoUs with foreign companies for exploration of possible co-operation with no financial commitment, regarding development of an amphibian variant of Hindustan-228 aircraft.(d)  HAL’s Transport Aircraft Division (TAD) at Kanpur was identified as production agency for the Do-228 in 1983.(e)  For development of Hindustan-228 prototype and indigenization of the various systems, HAL has allocated Rs. 94.41 Cr from its internal resources.*****
