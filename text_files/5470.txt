                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Use of Steroids in Cows




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	Whether it is a fact that cows are being fed steroids to fatten them up  quickly and increase their production of milk, if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether it is also true that consumption of the milk of such cows poses a threat to human health and fertility if so, the details thereof and steps being taken by the Government to combat the same ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)    There is no such report on feeding of steroids to cows to fatten them up quickly and increase their production of milk.b)	 The Department has also not received any information regarding threat to human health and fertility on consumption of milk of such cows.
