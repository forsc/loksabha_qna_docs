                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1070






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Funds for Development of States




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government provides funds to the State Governments under various heads for the development of their respective States;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any mechanism has been put in place to monitor the expenses incurred from the said fund;(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the mode of the functioning of the said mechanism; and (e)	if not, the reasons therefore?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b): Yes, Sir.  The Union Government provides funds to the State Governments under the Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS), Central Finance Commission grants (FC grants) and Other Grants /Loans/Transfers (OGLT) as per the guidelines of the respective Scheme within the availability of Gross Budgetary Support with the Central Government. Besides, funds are also released directly to implementing agencies under Central Sector Schemes (CS) for developmental activities in the states. The details of central transfers during 2019-20(Actual) and 2020-21 (Revised Estimates) under CSS, FC Grants, CS and OGLT are as under:-                                            (Rs. in crore)S/No.	Fund  Type	               2019-20     2020-21                                        Actual	  Revised Estimates 1.	Centrally Sponsored Schemes	309553	   3879002.	Finance Commission Transfers	123710	   1823523.	Central Sector Schemes	        757091    12636904.	Other Grants /Loans/Transfers	198707	   191155(c) to (e): The concerned State Governments and Central Ministries/Departments review and monitor implementation of the schemes as per the scheme specific guidelines to ensure effective utilization of funds including expenditure incurred.*********
