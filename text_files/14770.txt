                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3496






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Smart Class




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Chirag Paswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to set up smart class system in allKendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) and Navodaya Vidyalayas (NVs) in near future,specially in Tripura and Delhi/NCR and if so, the details thereof;(b) if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) the details of implementation procedure and time limit of completion for anysuch proposal in near future?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Smart class systems have been set up in 907 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) and 545Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) in the country. Out of these, 6 KVs and 4 JNVs inTripura and 77 KVs &22 JNVs in Delhi / NCR are having smart class systems.(b) Does not arise.(c) Setting up of smart class system is a continuous process which depends uponvarious factors such as opening of new schools, addition of higher classes, availability offunds, availability of Internet connectivity etc. and as such no specific time frame can begiven in this regard.*****
