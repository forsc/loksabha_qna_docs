                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1806






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Incentives for Electronics Manufacturing




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY  be pleased to state::- (a)	the number of companies that have applied for availing benefit underthe Production Linked Incentive Scheme (PLI) for Large Scale electronicsmanufacturing State-wise, from  the start of the scheme  till now;(b)	whether COVID-19 pandemic has affected the registration of companies under this scheme and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is planning to extend the date of applicationunder this scheme and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasonstherefor?






ANSWER




                                                        a): Production Linked Incentive Scheme (PLI) for Large Scale Electronics Manufacturing, notified on 01.04.2020, was open to receive applications till 31.07.2020. Under the Scheme, a total of 22 applications have been received till 31.07.2020. Details of the applicants are annexed.(b): COVID -19 pandemic has not affected the registration of companies under the PLI Scheme. (c): Production Linked Incentive Scheme (PLI) for Large Scale Electronics Manufacturing was open to receive applications till 31.07.2020. At present, there is no plan to extend the date of applications under the PLI Scheme.*******AnnexureApplications received under PLI SchemeSl.
