                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3077






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Shortage of Quality Seeds




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is shortage of quality seeds in the States including backward areas due to which agricultural production has remained stagnant; (b) if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(c) the demand and availability of various crop seeds during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise; (d) the details of the schemes being implemented to enhance the production/availability of quality seeds in the country; and (e) the funds allocated/released and utilized under the above schemes during each of the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	There is sufficient quantity of certified/quality seed available in the States. The availability of certified/quality seeds have significantly contributed in achieving record agriculture production in the country. The details of agricultural production of major crops is available at Annexure-I.(c):	As reported by the States, the details of demand and availability of certified/quality seeds of various crop State-wise for the last three years and current year is at Annexure-II.(d):	Government of India provides financial assistance to different States and Government Seed Producing Agencies for production and distribution of seeds and for other interventions related to seed sector viz. Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Materials (SMSP), National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), etc. The pattern of assistance for seed production, distribution and other seed related activities under the schemes of DA&FW is at Annexure-III.(e):	The details of funds released and utilized under Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Materials (SMSP) scheme during last three years and the current year is as under:Financial Year	Amount Released(in crores)	Amount Utilized(in crores)2018-19	338.42	286.632019-20	298.71	228.292020-21	262.44	171.982021-22	125.12	Utilization report is still awaitedThe details of funds released and utilized under National Food Security Mission scheme during last three years and the current year is as under:Financial Year	Amount Released(in crores)	Amount Utilized(in crores)2018-19	1309.59	1214.172019-20	1269.49	1280.572020-21	1025.27	1076.302021-22*	515.42	553.77*As on 15.03.2022The details of funds released and utilized under National Food Security Mission- Oilseeds & Oil palm (NFSM-OS&OP) scheme during last three years and the current year is as under:Financial Year	Amount Released(in crores)	Amount Utilized(in crores)2018-19	242.54	230.352019-20	213.70	217.522020-21	371.01	213.522021-22	129.65	-
