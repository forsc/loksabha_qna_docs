                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Liquid Medical Oxygen Supply by Steel sector




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi

                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Arjunlal Meena
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of total capacity of public sector and private sector steel companies in production of oxygen especially Liquid Medical Oxygen (LMO) in country during the last three years;(b)	whether the public sector plants provided LMO during the second wave of pandemic and if so, the details of quantities supplied by the public as well as the private sector plants along with their capacity;(c)	whether the steel production is bound to get impacted since the oxygen used in making of steel is diverted for medical usage and if so, the details thereof since Covid-19 pre and post situation plant-wise;(d)	the details of supplying oxygen daily for Covid use, State-wise and loss incurred by steel industry/companies so far;(e)	the measures taken to increase production of LMO in the country in general as well as specifically by public sector and also technical and other policy interventions brought to ease growing demand of LMO; and(f)	the details of impact created through transportation of LMO from the production sites?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL	    (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)	The details of total capacity of production of Liquid Medical Oxygen (LMO) of public and private sector steel companies during the last three years are as follows:-(in tonnes per day)Year	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22Total Capacity	2492	2494	2748	4102Contd……..2/--: 2 :-(b)	During the second wave of pandemic, the public and private sector steel plants together supplied 230262 tonnes of LMO between 1st April – 25th July, 2021. The plant-wise details in this regard are given in Annexure-I.(c)to(e): During peak of the second wave, steel sector had enhanced the LMO production considerably. As against 538 MT of LMO supplied on 1st April, 2021, the supply of LMO peaked to 4749 MT on 13th May, 2021. State-wise details of LMO supplied during April-July, 2021 are given in Annexure-II. Measures taken by the steel plants to increase production of LMO in the country included the following:-(i)	Reduced production of liquid Nitrogen and Liquid Argon.(ii)	Reduced usage of gaseous oxygen thereby reducing steel produced.	The enhanced production of LMO by reducing usage of gaseous oxygen led to reduced production of steel by about 500,000 tonnes during the period April-June, 2021.(f)     The LMO produced in steel plants were supplied to 22 states/UTs as indicated in Annexure-II utilizing transportation modes of road, Oxygen express (Railways) and transport aircrafts of the Air Force.********
