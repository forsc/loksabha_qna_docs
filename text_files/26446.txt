                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2291






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Pending Grants




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there are any pending grants of the 13th Finance Commission which are  yet to be released to any of the States/UTs;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise and amount wise including the reasons for not releasing balance grant of 13th Finance Commission to the respective States; and(c)	the timeline by which pending grants of 13th Finance Commission shall be released to the respective States?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (c) No, Sir. 13th  Finance Commission during its award period from 01-4-2010 to 31-3-2015  had recommended grants-in-aid to States for various sectors with grant specific conditions for availing the same. As the Government had accepted the recommendations of the 13th  Finance Commission alongwith conditionalities, the recommended grants were released by the Central Government to the concerned States who fulfilled the sector specific stipulated conditions before the expiry of the award period on 31-03-2015.
