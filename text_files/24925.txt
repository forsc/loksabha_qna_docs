                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

722






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Registered Newspapers and Magazines




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of registered newspapers and magazines published in the country along with the number of such newspapers and magazines published on daily, weekly, fortnightly and monthly basis, name-wise;(b)	the languages in which newspapers and magazines are published in various States of the country, State-wise;(c)	the number of newspapers and magazines which have not filed annual returns during the last three years across the country, State-wise;(d)	the total number of newspapers and magazines authorized to receive advertisements from DAVP in the country along with the details thereof; and(e)	the number of newspapers and magazines which have deposited levy fees during the last three years, name-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS {SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR}:(a): As on 14.07.2021, total number of newspapers and periodicals registered with Registrar of Newspapers for India (RNI), under the Press and Registration of Books Act, 1867 is 1,44,740.  Periodicity-wise and name-wise details of the same are available at url: https://egov.rni.nic.in/Webforms/ReportRegdPeriodityWise.aspx. (b): State-wise details of registered newspapers and periodicals in different languages are available at url: https://egov.rni.nic.in/Webforms/ReportRegdLanguageandState.aspx.(c): State-wise details of registered newspapers and periodicals who have yet to file the annual statement are available at url: https://egov.rni.nic.in/Webforms/reportNoAnnualReturn.aspx. (d): As on 19.07.2021, total 7,394 publications are empanelled with Bureau of Outreach and Communication (erstwhile DAVP) for dissemination of information through advertisements. The details of the empanelled publications are available at url: http://davp.nic.in/Upload/(S(ypbs4h55cp2sngjt1qw2bpnl))/davp_empanel_status.aspx.(e):  The number of newspapers and magazines who have deposited the levy fees to Press Council of India (PCI) during the last three years range from 2418 to 3317. The name-wise list of such newspapers and magazines is available on the website of PCI at the url: https://presscouncil.nic.in/Content/1981_3_Notifications.aspx. *******
