                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3395






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Promotion of Employment and Trade related to Textiles




Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of various types of employments and trades related to textiles which have been promoted by the Government under Make in India programme; (b) whether the said programme is being implemented in Uttar Pradesh; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (d):	Government is promoting employment and trade under the broad objective of Make- in-India Programme catering to various sub-sectors of the Textile Sector which includes Spinning, Weaving, Garmenting, Processing, Jute, Silk, Wool, Handicraft and Handloom. Various schemes such as Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS), National Technical Textile Mission (NTTM), Silk Samagra, Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP), National Handicraft Development Programme (NHDP), National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) and Integrated Processing Development Scheme (IPDS), etc are being implemented for overall promotion and development of Textiles Sector at Pan India level including Uttar Pradesh. Details of funds released in Uttar Pradesh under major schemes of ATUFS, National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) & Raw Material Supply Scheme and schemes implemented by O/o DC (Handicrafts) and Silk Samagra during 2018-19 to 2021-22 are at Annexure.
