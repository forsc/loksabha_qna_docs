                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5250






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Unmanned Level Crossings in North Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry has any data on the number of accidents caused due to unmanned railway crossings of both human and animal lives across North Bengal districts; and(b)	if so, the measures taken by the Railways to minimize the risk for the local population?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	During the last two years i.e. 2017-18 and 2018-19, no consequential train accident took place at unmanned level crossings on Eastern Railway and Northeast Frontier Railways covering all North Bengal Districts.(b):	All unmanned level crossings on Broad Gauge have been eliminated on 31st  Jan 2019.*****
