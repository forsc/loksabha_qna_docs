                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

694






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Metro Rail between Delhi and Meerut etc.




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any plan to run the Metro Rail from Delhi to Meerut, Muzaffarnagar and Hastinapur, a historical place of Mahabharat era; and  (b) 	if so, the time by which it is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b):	 Urban Transport is an integral part of Urban development, which is a State subject. Planning and development of Urban Transport system including Metro Rail is done by respective State Governments/ Union Territories. Government of India considers financial assistance to these projects as per extant policy subject to feasibility of the project and availability of the resources, as and when posed by respective State Governments/Union Territories. Government of India in March, 2019 has approved 82.15 kilometer rail based Regional Rapid Transit System (RRTS) corridor between  Delhi and Meerut via Ghaziabad which is scheduled to be completed in six years.                                        *****
