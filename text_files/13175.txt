                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1995






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Suicides due to Enhanced Cultivation of GM Crops




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Union Government has any statistics to link the increasing farmer suicides with enhanced cultivation of Genetically Modified Crops; and (b)	 if so, whether the Government has taken any measures to check the high cost of Genetically Modified seeds?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) under the Ministry of Home Affairs compiles and disseminates information on suicides in its publication titled ‘Accidental Deaths and Suicides in India’ (ADSI). These Reports on suicides upto 2018 are available on its website. However, no data is available linking farmers suicide to cultivation of Genetically Modified Crops.(b):	 The production and distribution of certified/ quality seeds to the farmers is primarily the responsibility of respective State Governments through the State Department of Agriculture, State Seeds Corporation, State Agriculture Universities, State Seed Multiplication Farms, Seed Cooperatives, Agro Industries Corporation/ State Marketing Federation etc. 	Cotton Seed Price (Control) Order-2015 regulates the Bt Cotton seed price every year to provide Bt Cotton seed at affordable prices for the benefit of farmers.********
