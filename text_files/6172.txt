                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Weavers Service Centres




Vijay Kumar













                                                    Virendra  Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government is setting up Weaver Service Centres in the country;(b)	whether priority is being accorded to Madhya Pradesh along with other States under the said scheme, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether any initiative is being taken by the Government to link Weavers Service Centres with e-commerce, if so, the details thereof?.






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c):	28 Weavers Service Centres are functioning across the Country, including one at Indore in Madhya Pradesh State. In order to promote e-marketing of handloom products, a policy framework was designed under which any willing e-commerce platform with good track record can participate in online marketing of handloom products. 23 e-commerce entities have been engaged for online marketing of handloom products. E-commerce entities are associated with the handloom clusters/pockets through Weavers Service Centres to tap full potential of e-marketing. Workshops/seminars are organised to motivate/educate handloom weavers/ producers for online marketing of handloom products.*******
