                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1519






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Pending Wages under MGNREGS




Sougata Ray













                                                    Rajmohan Unnithan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total amount pending for payment to various States towards the wages under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) along with the reason for undue delay in this regard;(b) 	the total amount pending for payment towards the wage under said scheme in Kasaragod district of Kerala;(c) 	whether the Government has allocated any amount for clearing the wage arrears under the MGNREGS and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Government has fixed any time limit for clearing the wage arrears under MGNREGS and if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	whether the Government has any proposal to abolish the project and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d): The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), 2005 is a demand driven wage employment programme. Hence, no State/District-wise allocation is made including Kasaragod district of Kerala. Fund release to the States/UTs is a continuous and dynamic process and Central Government is committed to making funds available keeping in view the demand for work. The Ministry releases funds periodically to States/UTs in two tranches with each tranche consisting of one or more instalments based on agreed to Labour Budget (Persondays), opening balance, pace of utilization of funds, pending liabilities of the previous year, if any,  overall performance and  submission of requisite documents. During the current financial year 2019-20 (as on 05.02.2020), the Ministry has released Rs. 62057.99 crore to all States/UTs under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) including pending wage liabilities.
