                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Cold Storage facility for COVID-19 Vaccine




P. Velusamy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the existing cold storage facility is sufficient to store COVID-19 vaccine;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the steps taken by the Government to store additional vaccine?






ANSWER




                                                          THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): The existing cold chain storage facility is sufficient to store COVID-19 vaccines, there are a total of 305 Walk-in Coolers (WICs), 100 Walk-in Freezers (WIFs), 55,442 Ice Lined Refrigerators (ILRs), 50,881 Deep Freezers (DFs) and 528 Solar Direct Drives (SDDs) across more than 29,000 cold chain points in the country.*******
