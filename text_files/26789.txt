                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2416






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Irrigated Agricultural Land




Devendrappa Y














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	        the total amount of agricultural land in the country, State-wise;(b)	       the percentage of irrigated land and the sources of irrigation; and(c)	the per acre productivity of irrigated and non-irrigated agricultural land of major    crops like paddy, wheat, maize, etc.?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)		The latest State-wise details of the total amount of agricultural land in the country for the year 2017-18, available with Directorate of Economics and Statistics, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare is at Annexure -I.  (b)	As per the latest data available on Land Use Statistics, the percentage of Net Irrigated Area to Net Area Sown in the country for the year 2017-18 is 49.9 %. The details of area irrigated by various sources is given below:                            (thousand hectares)Source of Irrigation	Area irrigatedGovernment Canals	15548Private Canals	171Tanks 	1707Tube-wells	33778Other wells	11014Other Sources	7260Net Irrigated Area	69478                                 Source: Directorate of Economics and Statistics, MoA&FW(c)		As per the Third Advance Estimates for the year 2020-21, details of per acre productivity / yield of major crops is at Annexure-II.
