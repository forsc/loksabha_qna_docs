                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1245






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Tax Benefits for PMVVY Subscribers




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government intends to extend tax benefits like other schemes to the subscribers of Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana Yojana (PMVVY) and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of funds which the Government has allocated towards this scheme; (c) whether the Government intends to allocate more funds towards the scheme and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government has found any irregularity and difficulty in implementation of such a scheme; and (e) if so, the details thereof along with the number of subscribers to this scheme from Bengaluru specifically and Karnataka in General?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a):  Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana Yojana (PMVVY) is exempted from Goods and Services Tax (GST). (b) & (c):  The scheme is being implemented through Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC). The scheme provides for an assured return of 8% per annum payable monthly for 10 years. The difference between return generated by LIC and the assured return of 8% per annum is borne by Government of India as subsidy on annual basis and is reimbursed to LIC.The amount reimbursed by Government of India to LIC, since inception of the scheme, is tabulated below:                                                                                    (Rs. In Crores)YEAR	PMVVY2017-2018	58.022018-2019	152.172019-2020 ( till September 2019)	88.34 (d) & (e): The scheme has been implemented seamlessly. As per LIC, the number of subscribers to this scheme from Bengaluru and Karnataka are 14,405 and 24,067 respectively  as on 31.10.2019.
