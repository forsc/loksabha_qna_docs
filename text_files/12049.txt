                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Handloom Cluster




Giridhari Yadav













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to establish a handloom cluster in Bihar;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the district/ region identified and funds allocated for the same; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor along with the reaction of the Government thereto? 






ANSWER




                                                        ?????? ?????? (??????? ?????? ?????? ?????)MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)&(b)	The Government of India, Ministry of Textiles, has sanctioned 3 Block Level Clusters (BLCs) under National Handloom Development Programme(NHDP) and 10 Block Level Clusters under Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) in Bihar State. A statement showing cluster-wise funds sanctioned along with district is annexed.(c)	Does not arise in view of the reply to parts (a) & (b) above.*****
