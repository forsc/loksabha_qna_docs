                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Solid Waste Management under SBM




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the features of the Solid Waste Management under Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM); (b) 	the details of funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this mission within Tamil Nadu during the last three years and the current year; (c) whether the Government covers dump yard sites under Solid Waste Management and if so, the details of the funds shared by the Government; (d) whether the Government has proposed to cover Kodunagaiyur dump site which has grown to a formidable size due to garbage being dumped by Chennai Corporation and became a health menace causing respiratory troubles, skin problems and allergies to the common people; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                          THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): The features of Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) 2.0, launched on 01st October, 2021, are :  i). Sustainable Solid Waste Management (SWM),  (ii). Sustainable sanitation and treatment of used water,(iii). Awareness creation with large scale citizen outreach to create ‘Jan Andolan’ and institutionalise ‘Swachh’ behaviour,(iv). Create institutional capacity to effectively implement pragmatic interventions to achieve Mission objectives. (b): Details of funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized by Tamil Nadu during the last three years and the current year under SBM-U is annexed. (c): Remediation of dumpsites, including all legacy dumpsites, is covered as an admissible element for funding under SBM-U 2.0. SBM-U 2.0 has been launched on 1st October, 2021. Under SBM-U, from 02.10.2014 to 31.03.2021, an amount of ?6,373.54 Crore has been released for SWM, including for dumpsite remediation, to all States/ Union Territories (UTs). (d) and (e): A proposal for Central Share (CS) funding for  Kodunagaiyur dump site project at a total cost of ?642 Crore has been received by the Government. This project would be considered for funding under SBM-U 2.0 provided the proposal is submitted by Government of Tamil Nadu, fulfilling all the conditions stipulated in the SBM-U 2.0 Operational Guidelines for such funding.                                   *****
