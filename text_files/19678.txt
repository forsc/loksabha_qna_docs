                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the number of aspirational districts that have been taken up for development by Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) under his Ministry under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) during the last three years, State-wise;(b) the details and the number of new jobs, if any, created by the activities taken up under the above mentioned CSR activity, State-wise;(c) the total amount spent by the Ministry for the above mentioned CSR activity during the last five years, State-wise; and(d) the other steps being taken by the Government for spending of CSR fund on public welfare?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (d): The Department of Public Enterprises (DPE) through an Office Memorandum No. CSR-08/0002/2018-Dir(CSR) dated 10th December 2018 issued “Guidelines for CSR expenditure of CPSEs”  this inter-alia conveyed that Aspirational Districts may be given preference under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR). Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) under the Department of Heavy Industry (DHI) namely Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL), Andrew Yule & Co. Ltd. (AYCL), Bridge & Roof Co. (India) Ltd.
