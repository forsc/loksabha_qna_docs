                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4099






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






National Highways in Bihar




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of National Highways (NHs) passing through the State of Bihar;(b) whether any four-lane NH has been constructed in the State and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether there is any proposal for construction of new NHs in the State; and (d) if so, the details and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	There are 55 number of National Highways (NHs) passing through the State of Bihar.(b)	Yes, Sir. The details are at Annexure-I.(c)	& (d) Ministry hasdeclared 12 roads passing through Bihar in 2018 and 2019as new National Highwayson which preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) are in progress.
