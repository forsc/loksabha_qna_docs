                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Blue Flag Certificates for Beaches




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has started a pilot project to develop the Indian beaches according to the Blue Flag Standards;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether beaches from the coastal States had been chosen for the Blue Flag certification under the project; and(d)	if so, the details of beaches that have been selected?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE  IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)  (a) and (b): Under Integrated Coastal Zone Management Project Phase-I, Ministry of Environment Forest & Climate Change (MoEFCC) has initiated Beach Environment & Aesthetic Management System (BEAMS) Programme to develop beaches according to the Blue Flag Standards in coastal States/UTs. Under this programme, various activities on pollution abatement, beach beautification, awareness programmes on environment education and safety & surveillance are undertaken  in identified beaches to achieve Blue Flag Standards for the beach.(c) and (d): Under BEAMS program, Ghoghala Beach in Diu, Shirvrajpur beach in  Gujarat, Padubidri and Kasarkod Beach in Karnataka, Kappad Beach in Kerala, Mahabalipuram beach in Tamil Nadu, Eden Beach in Puducherry, Rishikonda Beach in Andhra Pradesh, Golden Beach in Odisha and Radhanagar Beach in Andaman & Nicobar Islands have been chosen for Blue Flag certification.****
