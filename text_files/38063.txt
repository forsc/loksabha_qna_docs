                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4657






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Development of National Highways




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta













                                                    Mahendra Singh Solanky
                                                








                                                    Rajbahadur Singh
                                                








                                                    Brijbhushan Sharan Singh
                                                








                                                     Pratap Chandra Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the length of National Highways (NHs) under construction and constructed during the last three years and the current year in the States of Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh;(b) the length of NHs constructed under various Public Private Partnership (PPP) modes in the country, State-wise; and(c)  the details of the road assets proposed to be monetized under the National Monetization Pipeline along with the revenues expected therefrom, State-wise including Odisha, Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) The length of NHs under construction in Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh as on February 2022 is 3864 km & 3553 Km respectively.	Length constructed in the State of Uttar Pradesh & Madhya Pradesh during last three financial years and current FY is as under:State	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22(As on Feb-22)	Constructed length ( KM)Uttar Pradesh	882	694	917	530Madhya Pradesh	829	714	877	535 (b) Length of NHs constructed on PPP mode state wise is placed at Annexure. (c) Road assets proposed to be monetized in States of Odisha, Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh by NHAI is as per following:Sl No	State	No of stretches	Total length of stretches (Kms)1	Odisha	5	3492	UP	6	2613	MP	5	391The Traffic and Technical studies in above stretches is underway by NHAI, therefore it is too early to indicate state wise expected revenue.
