                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1911






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Smart City Projects in Ahmedabad




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount spent so far on the smart city projects in Ahmedabad along with the heads under which the money has been spent; (b) the number of projects completed in Ahmedabad; and (c) the status report of all smart city works undertaken including the deadline for completion of the works?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under Smart Cities Mission (SCM), Central Government has released Rs. 343 crore for Ahmedabad Smart City. As part of their matching grant, the State Government of Gujarat has released Rs. 317.5 crore to the City. Of the total available funds, Ahmedabad Smart City has utilised Rs. 431.73 crore as on 11.09.2020. The utilization under Project Fund is Rs. 414.23 crore  and under Administrative & Office Expenses (A&OE) is Rs. 17.50 crore.(b)	As on 11.09.2020, Ahmedabad Smart City has completed 42 projects worth Rs. 1411.23 crore.  Under the Smart Cities Mission (SCM), apart from the Government of India funding of Rs. 500 crore per city and an equal matching amount by the State/Urban Local Bodies, Smart Cities are utilizing funds from convergence with other Missions/Programs, Public Private Partnerships, loans, own resources etc. (c)	As on 11.09.2020, under the Smart Cities Mission, 4,903 projects worth Rs. 1,68,734 crore have been tendered, out of which 4,068 projects worth Rs. 1,31,792 crore are under implementation/completed. Smart Cities are expected to complete their projects within 5 years from the date of selection as Smart City.                                  *****
