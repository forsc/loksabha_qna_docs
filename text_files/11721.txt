                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

531






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Reservation in Sainik School




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the details of Sainik Schools in the country;(b) whether reservation in admissions is permitted only for SCs/STs in Sainik Schools;(c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for not including OBCs for reservation in admission;(d) whether the Government will consider extending reservation for OBCs in Sainik  Schools also; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) At present, there are 31 Sainik Schools functioning in the country. State wise details of Sainik Schools in the country is placed at Annexure.(b)  to (e):  Admission to Sainik School is strictly merit based through a competitive exam and medical fitness. As per Sainik School Society Rules and Regulations 1997, seats for admission in Sainik Schools are reserved for candidates belonging to SC, ST and Defence categories. It has been decided on 10th January, 2020 to have 27% reservation for OBCs in admissions to align with the formulations of the M/o Human Resources Development on the matter of admissions.*****
