                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4788






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Road Projects in Madhya Pradesh




Gajendra Singh Patel













                                                    Durga Das Uikey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any new projects on National Highway-AB road in 	Madhya Pradesh is under consideration and if so, the details 	thereof;(b)	whether any new road routes have been approved in 	Khargone-	Barwani and Betul-Harda-Harsud Parliamentary 	Constituencies 	of Madhya Pradesh;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	the scheme of the Government for road projects in aspirational 	districts;(e)	whether any new proposal has been approved for the said 	districts and if so, the details thereof; and(f)	the details of the approved road projects in aspirational 	districts of Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	Following new projects have been envisaged on Agra-Bombay Road:            Name of Project	Length (km)	StatusFlyover at MR-10 Junction, VUP at Ralamandal & Arjun Baroda (Indore-Dewas Section	3.61	Bidding stage.Flyover at Rau Circle (Indore-Dewas Section)	1.22	Project awarded.Realignment of Bakaner Ghat (Indore-Khalghat Section)	8.87	DPR stage.(b) & (c)	Following new major projects have been planned in Khargone-Barwani parliamentary constituency:            Name of Project	Length (km)	Stage4-laning of Indore-Edlabad (Package-II) section of NH-347BG	33.40	Bidding stage.4-laning of Indore-Edlabad (Package-III) section of NH-347BG & 753L	40.40	Awarded.Realignment of Bakaner Ghat (on Indore-Khalghat Section of NH-52)	8.87	DPR stage. Following new major projects have been approved in Betul-Harda-Harsud parliamentary constituency:                        Name of Project	Total Length	Stage4-laning of Nanasa to Pidgaon (Indore-Harda Pkg-III) from km 95.000 to 142.445 km	47.445 km	Construction stage.4-laning of Harda-Temagaon (P-I) from km 0.00 to 30.00 km	30 km	Construction stage.4-laning of Temagaon – Chicholi (P-II) from km 30.00 to 81.00 km	51 km	Bidding stage.4-laning of Chicholi-Betul (P-III) from km 81.00 to 121.248 km.	40.24 km	Construction stage. 	In addition, for Vadodara-Khandwa-Betul economic corridor which passes through Chota Udepuur-Alirajpur-Badwani-Julawania-Khargone-Deshgaon-Khandwa-Ashapur (Harsud) – Betul, preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) has been initiated.(d) to (f)         Five projects comprising length of 273 km with cost of Rs. 2,177 crore are   in construction stage and nine projects comprising length of 493 km with     cost of Rs. 3,791 crore are sanctioned/in bidding stage.*****
