                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2092






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Employment Generation




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken various steps for generating employment in the country like encouraging various projects involving substantial investment and increasing public expenditure on schemes like Prime Minister‘s Employment Generation Programme, MGNREGS, Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana and Deendayal Antodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission ,etc for the benefit of youth and if so, the details thereof;(b) the progress made by the Government in creating new jobs for the unemployed youths in Bihar particularly in district Gopalganj;(c) the number of jobs created through the various programmes and schemes in Bihar during the last two years, district-wise; and(d) whether the National Career Service Project which comprises a digital portal for job-matching in a dynamic, efficient and responsive manner and has a repository of career content for job seekers is benefiting the youth of district Gopalganj of Bihar and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (d): Employment generation coupled with improving employability is the priority of the Government. Government has taken various steps for generating employment in the country like encouraging various projects involving substantial investment and increasing public expenditure on schemes like Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) and Deendayal Antodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) run by Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises, Ministry of Rural Development and Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs, respectively. Details of the  progress of the scheme/ programme made in Bihar during the last one year is given as follows:Schemes/Year	2019-20	2020-21Employment Generated underPMEGP	17768(No. of persons)	6232(No. of persons)(As on 31.12.2020)Candidate placed under DDU-GKY	5861(No. of candidates)	2147(No. of Candidates)(Till January, 2021)Persondays Generated under (MGNREGA)	1418(in Lakh)	1755 (in Lakh)(As on 28.01.2021)     Source: Concerned MinistriesGovernment is implementing the National Career Service (NCS) Project which comprises a digital portal that provides a nation-wide online platform for the job seekers and employers for job-matching in a dynamic, efficient and responsive manner. Around 12.41 lakh active jobseekers and more than 2000 active employers are registered on NCS portal from Bihar including Gopalganj district.*****
