                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

123






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Fish Production and Consumption




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     the details of production and requirement based consumption of fish in the country, State-wise;(b)     whether the Union Government has made proper arrangements for pisciculture and storage of fish in Bihar; (c)      if so, the details thereof; and (d)     the steps taken/being taken by the Government to provide training to the farmers for pisciculture along with employment opportunities related to pisciculture?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a)  to  (d)  :      A Statement is placed on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to the Lok Sabha Starred Question No.*123 put in by Shri Dulal Chand Goswami, Member of Parliament, Lok Sabha for answer on 11th February, 2020 regarding Fish Production and Consumption(a) :  The total fish production in India stands at 134.22 lakh tonne (provisional) for the year 2018-19. State-wise details of the fish production in the country during 2018-19 is furnished at Annexure-I. The consumption of fish in India ranges between 5 to 10 Kg per capita [Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO)  report “The State of World Fisheries and Aquaculture 2018”]. (b) and (c) :  Yes, Sir. The Union Government in the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has approved the proposals of Government of Bihar to the tune of Rs.320.35 crore for development of fisheries including pisciculture and post-harvest facilities in the State under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries during past four years (2015-16 to 2018-19). Details of fisheries activities and infrastructure facilities approved for Bihar during past four years under the CSS are furnished at Annexure-II. (d) :  The Government of India in Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, under the CSS Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries has a specific focus on providing training to the farmers for pisciculture along with employment opportunity to pisciculture farmers. During the past four years and current year (2015-16 to 2019-20), a total of 85,274 fishers, fish farmers and other stakeholders have been trained in the fisheries including pisciculture and post harvest technology with a view to generate employment opportunities to the trained manpower in the sector. *****
