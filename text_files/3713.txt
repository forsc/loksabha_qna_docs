                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3356






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Development of Textile Sector in Tamil Nadu




M. Selvaraj













                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the details of the various schemes undertaken by the Ministry in the State of Tamil Nadu during the last three years and the current year; (b) whether the Government is implementing various schemes/projects pertaining to development of textile sector in Tamil Nadu;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated and released during each of the last three years and the current year;(d) the share of State Government under these schemes/projects; (e) the current status of all these schemes/projects; and (f) the total number of people engaged in the textile industry benefited by the implementation of  these schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	to (f) The Government is implementing various textiles schemes across the country which are aimed at betterment of textile sector including the State of Tamil Nadu. The details of some of the major schemes are at Annexure-I.          The funds are allocated schemes-wise on PAN India basis.
