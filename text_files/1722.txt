                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Marketability of Onions and Potatoes




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that the market of onion and potato in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra is in a state of crisis; (b)	 if so, the steps being taken for its rehabilitation; (c) 	whether optional arrangements have been made for traders and mathadi workers; (d) 	the details of the steps taken for repairing and maintenance of the market for onion and potato in agriculture market located at Navi Mumbai; and (e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	As informed by Government of Maharashtra, seven buildings comprising 234 godowns in this onion potato market yard were declared by Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation in the year 2003 as dilapidated and unsafe for use.  Mumbai APMC had proposed re-development of the said market yard in the year 2004. But due to various reasons and non- cooperation from the Gala owners /traders & its Association, the Mumbai Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) has not taken up the work of redevelopment of said market. Mumbai APMC had given various options and proposed various arrangements for traders and mathadi workers.(d) & (e):   The said market is beyond repairing & maintenance conditions. Presently, various petitions related to the said subject are pending in Hon’ble High Court for decision. So, as per available reports, presently redevelopment of work cannot be started until there is approval from Hon’ble High Court & Government of Maharashtra.********
