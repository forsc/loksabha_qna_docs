                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1185






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Media and Entertainment Skill Council




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has received applications for setting up training partner centres of Media and Entertainment Skill Council (MESC) centers in various states especially in the North Mumbai Parliamentary constituency of Maharashtra State during last three years; b)	the dates on which the applications were received for setting up the said centres especially in North Mumbai Parliamentary constituency during the above period till date;  c)	the details of the number of applications approved and lying pending out of the applications received as on date especially of North Mumbai parliamentary constituency; andd)	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which these applications are likely to be approved?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	 Yes, Sir.  To set-up the training partners centers, the process is driven through centralised portal that is “Skill India Portal (SIP)” managed by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC).  As per the application details for Media and Entertainment Sector as available in the SIP, there are 825 such applications out of which 133 are from Maharashtra during last three years.  (b)	During the period, in North Mumbai Parliamentary constituency 10 applications have been received on 28/Sep/2019, 22/Oct/2019, 10/Feb/2020, 19/Feb/2020, 23/Mar/2021, 8/Apr/2021, 22/Jun/2021, 28/Jun/2021, 13/Jul/2021 and 12/Oct/2021.(c)	The status of applications received for Maharashtra (Mumbai and Mumbai Suburban) from 2019 to 2021 is given below:Training Partner Name	District	Application Submission Date	Application StatusBRSP Rojgaraghadi Foundation	Mumbai	12/Oct/2021	Payment awaitingTruly Yours Welfare Society	Mumbai Suburban	28/Jun/2021	ApprovedTruly Yours Welfare Society	Mumbai Suburban	22/Jun/2021	Conditionally accreditedInstitute for Design of Electrical Measuring Instruments	Mumbai Suburban	8/Apr/2021	RejectedBeautiful Tomorrow Foundation	Mumbai	13/Jul/2021	ApprovedTapasya School of Classical Dance & Music	Mumbai Suburban	10/Feb/2020	RejectedTSM Education Private Limited	Mumbai	19/Feb/2020	Conditionally accreditedGreycells Education Limited	Mumbai Suburban	22/Oct/2019	Conditionally accreditedE Plus Technologies (India) Pvt Ltd.	Mumbai Suburban	28/Sep/2019	BlockedYuva Vikas Society	Mumbai	23/Mar/2021	Conditionally accredited(d)  	Approval / conditional accreditation is an ongoing process subject to fulfilment of all relevant conditions.
