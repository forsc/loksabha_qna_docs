                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3670






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Rajdhani Express from Bhagalpur




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is contemplating to start Rajdhani Express Train from Bhagalpur station; and (b) if so, the details thereof?	 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b) No, Sir. Prior to discontinuation of all regular passengers carrying trains w.e.f. 23rd March, 2020, to prevent the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, Bhagalpur – Delhi sector was served by 09 pairs of trains. In view of the prevailing situation, only special trains, with limited stoppages, keeping in view the concerns and suggestions of State Governments are being operated and accordingly, 04 pairs of train services are presently operating between Bhagalpur and New Delhi as Special train services.  At present, there is no proposal to start new Rajdhani Express train on the Bhagalpur-Delhi sector. *****
