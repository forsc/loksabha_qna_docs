                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

760






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Partnership With Companies to Strengthen the Tourism Industry




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has entered into partnership with any companies to strengthen the hospitality and tourism industry in the country especially during pandemic;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the measures taken by the Government so that the tourism industry of Gujarat gets maximum benefits?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM  			       	     (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	The Ministry of Tourism has signed MoUs with three Online Travel Companies namely EaseMyTrip.com, Cleartrip Pvt. Ltd. and Yatra Online Pvt. Ltd. to get a clear picture on the geographical spread of the Hospitality Sector, its size, structure and existing capacity so as to offer relevant services to the Hospitality Industry [like assistance in COVID-19 preparedness through SAATHI (System for Assessment, Awareness & Training for Hospitality Industry), Star Classification etc.], under National Integrated Database of the Hospitality Industry (NIDHI).Further, the ‘Adopt A Heritage: Apni Dharohar, Apni Pehchaan’, initiative is a collaborative effort by Ministry of Tourism, Ministry of Culture and Archaeological Survey of India (ASI), State/UTs Governments for promotion of Heritage Tourism which aims to encourage public sector companies, private sector companies and corporate citizens/NGOs/ individuals to take up the responsibility of developing and upgrading tourist amenities at tourist sites.(c): 	The measures taken by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India for the benefit of the tourism industry in the country, including that of Gujarat, are given at Annexure. *****
