                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

568






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Free Coaching and Allied Scheme




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of the existing coaching centres identified in the State of Telangana under Free Coaching and Allied Scheme for the candidates of Minority Communities;(b)	the details of the funds allocated and released to these coaching centres under the said scheme during the last three years;(c)	the details of the students of minority community benefited under Scholarship schemes, Free Coaching and Allied Scheme during the last three years and the current year, State/UT and year-wise;(d)	the details of schemes aimed at providing free coaching to minority students during the above period; and(e)	whether there exists any implementing agency to have a check on such coaching institutions/organizations and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OFMINORITY AFFAIRSSHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	& (b):	The Ministry of Minority Affairs implements the Free Coaching and Allied Scheme (Naya Savera) under which free coaching is provided to students/candidates who belong to the six notified minority communities and whose total annual family income from all sources does not exceed Rs. 6.00 lakhs per annum. Eleven Project Implementing Agencies (PIAs) were empanelled under the scheme w.e.f. 2017-18 from the State of Telangana and an amount of Rs. 7.71 crore has been released to the PIAs in the State of Telangana during the financial years 2017-18 and 2018-19. (c): In the last three years, 1,90,52,213 students have benefitted under the scholarship scheme.  In the last three years and the current year 36839 students / candidates have benefitted/ been allocated under the free coaching and allied scheme. The State-wise and Year –wise details of students of minority community benefitted under Scholarship schemes and Free Coaching and Allied Scheme during the last three years and current year are at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively. (d): The Ministry of Minority Affairs implements Naya Savera– Free Coaching and Allied Scheme under which free coaching is provided to students of six centrally notified minority communities namely Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains, and Zoroastrians (Parsis). The scheme provides coaching to students so as to enable them to qualify in entrance examinations for admission in technical/ professional courses, and various Competitive examinations for recruitment to Group ‘A’, ‘B’ and ‘C’ services. The scheme is implemented through Project Implementing Agencies (PIAs)/Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs). Details are available on www.minorityaffairs.gov.in.(e): Coaching institutions/organizations are monitored by State Government as per provisions of the scheme including checking their infrastructure and mid-term inspection report. In addition, National Productivity Council (NPC) which is an independent agency under administrative control of Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India had been engaged to conduct concurrent monitoring of the scheme w.e.f. 2016-17 to 2018-19.  Apart from this, in 2020 the Indian Institute of Public Administration (IIPA), New Delhi has conducted the third party evaluation study also of the scheme.***
