                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Voluntary Retirement




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Virendra  Kumar
                                                








                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Government has any data with regard to the Voluntary Retirement applied for by the Civil Servants IAS/IPS/IRS during the last five years and if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof; (b) 	the number of applications of Voluntary Retirement Scheme(VRS) accepted by the Government during the last five years, State/UT-wise and year-wise; and (c) 	whether Government has any data with regard to the IAS/IPS/IRS officers who first applied for VRS and later withdrew their applications and if so, the State/ UT-wise details of the last five years thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (c): In respect of All India Services, including IAS and IPS officers, the requests for voluntary retirement are being received and processed both by the concerned State Government as well as the Central Government as per the provisions of the Rule 16(2) and Rule 16 (2A) of All India Services (Death-Cum-Retirement-Benefits) Rules, 1958, as the case may be. Hence, the consolidated data relating to Voluntary Retirement applications filed/accepted/withdrew by IAS/IPS officers is not maintained by the Central Government.  Further, in respect of IRS, which is a Central Civil Service, as per the information received from Department of Revenue, Ministry of Finance, the number of voluntary retirement applied/ accepted/withdrew by  IRS officers during the last five years is given in table below. Further, the state-wise data is not maintained by Department of Revenue.	CBDT	CBIC	TotalNo. of IRS Officers applied for VRS	161	253	414No. of IRS Officers whose VRS application Accepted by Central Government	129	212	341No. of IRS officers who first applied for VRS and later withdrew their applications	6	23	29*****
