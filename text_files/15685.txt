                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4551






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Vacant Posts in CBFC




Shiromani Ram













                                                    Malook Nagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of vacant posts in the advisory committee of the Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC);(b)	the time by which these posts are likely to be filled up; and(c)	the number of former Members of Parliament and mayors who have written letters for the advisory committee of the CBFC and the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR):(a) and (b): The Central Government constitutes an advisory panel at each of the nine regional offices of the Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) for the purpose of enabling the Board to efficiently discharge its functions under the Cinematograph Act, 1952.(c): During the Seventeenth Lok Sabha, references from four former Members of Parliament have been received for appointment of advisory panel members in the CBFC. All such references are examined as per extant rules.******
