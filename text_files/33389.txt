                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

807






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






ESI Dispensaries




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of the number of ESI dispensaries in Narasaraopet, Andhra Pradesh and their operational status;(b)whether it is true that the Ministry has decided to construct a new ESI hospital in Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh with 100-bed capacity;(c)if so, the details thereof; and(d)whether the Ministry is going to allot work to CPWD, if so, the time by which the hospital is expected to become operational?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		Primary, secondary and super specialty medical facilities to Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) beneficiaries of Narasaraopet are provided through ESI Scheme (ESIS) dispensary, Ganapavaram, ESIS hospital, Vijayawada and 7 tie-up hospitals.  (b) to (d):	It had been decided to set up a 100-bedded ESIC hospital in Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh and the project is at present in land identification/allotment stage.  Construction agency is decided after possession of the land and approval of estimates.******
