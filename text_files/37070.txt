                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4189






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Scheme for Economic Empowerment of DNTs




Rajbahadur Singh













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Srinivas Kesineni
                                                








                                                    Mahendra Singh Solanky
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Brijbhushan Sharan Singh
                                                








                                                    Jagdambika Pal 
                                                








                                                     Pratap Chandra Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4189TO BE ANSWERED ON 29.03.2022SCHEME FOR ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT OF DNTs 4189. SHRI RAJBAHADUR SINGH: SHRI KESINENI SRINIVAS: SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI: SHRI P.P. CHAUDHARY: SHRI MANOJ TIWARI: SHRI BRIJBHUSHAN SHARAN SINGH: SHRI SANGAM LAL GUPTA: SHRI JAGDAMBIKA PAL: SHRI MAHENDRA SINGH SOLANKY: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the aims, objectives, goals and expected outcome of the Scheme for Economic Empowerment of De-Notified Tribes (DNTs) (SEED); (b) the steps taken by the Government to provide ease of access to the beneficiaries in the rural areas for enrolment to the scheme; (c) whether the Government has ensured userfriendly interface and simplification of process in the online portal which will be used for the implementation of the SEED; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a):  A Scheme for Economic Empowerment of DNTs(SEED) has been formulated for livelihood support for the Denotified, Nomadic and Semi Nomadic communities. The interventions under this are:i.	To facilitate youth of these communities to secure jobs through competitive examinations by providing coaching of good quality;ii.	To provide health insurance to DNT communities under PM-JAY;iii.	To promote secure livelihoods though economic activities and by organising them into self help groups;iv.	To provide financial assistance for construction of houses to members of the DNT communities.The Scheme will benefit 6250 students by providing free coaching, 4,44,500 families by providing health insurance, 2,000 clusters for livelihood initiative and 4,200 families by providing houses in next five years.(b to d):  A portal has been developed for inviting application through online system for easy access to the beneficiaries under the Scheme. Utmost care has been taken to make this portal user friendly.*****
