                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






DD/AIR Kendra




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the names of the districts of the country particularly in the backward and rural areas of Maharashtra without any Doordarshan/Akashvani Kendra as on date; (b) the names of States from which request to set up Doordarshan/Akashvani Kendra have been received by the Union Government during the last three years till date; (c) the action taken or proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard; and(d) the time by which all the districts of the country are likely to be connected with Doordarshan/Akashvani  network?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) Prasar Bharati is having a network of 655 Doordarshan & 485 Akashwani installations across the country. State-wise locations of Doordarshan(DD) setups are available at DD’s website: https://doordarshan.gov.in/doordarshan-network. The details regarding All India Radio(AIR) stations in Maharashtra are listed in the Annexure . (b) & (c)	 Requests from State Governments / Public representatives (MPs/MLAs) / Government representatives etc. are received from time to time for new installations.  Decision on the same is taken on the basis of scheme guidelines, inter-se priority and availability of funds.(d)	 New Technologies such as Satellite Broadcasting and Internet Streaming have made it possible to cover districts across the country with DD/AIR network without the necessity of having physical and local installations.All areas of the country (including those located in backward and rural areas) have been provided with Multi-channel TV and radio coverage through “DD FreeDish” (a Direct to Home(DTH) satellite platform of Prasar Bharati) except Andaman and Nicobar islands where these services are provided through C band. Prasar Bharati is also providing its AIR and DD channels digitally through internet and also on NewsOnAir app on “Android” and “iOS” platform for mobile users.******
