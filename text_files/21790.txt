                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2884






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Textile Park at Salem




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Tamil Nadu for setting up of a Textile Park at Salem; and (b) if so, the details thereof including the time by which the proposal is likely to be sanctioned?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b):	A proposal has been received from the State Government of Tamil Nadu for setting up of M/s Vedhaa Apparel Park at Vedaranyam Taluk, Nagapattinum District of Tamil Nadu under Scheme of Integrated Textile Park (SITP) of Government of India. Details of Textile Parks under SITP in state of Tamil Nadu are given in ANNEXURE.In addition, Tamil Nadu State has sent a request for sanction of two Mega Textile Parks in Tamil Nadu under Government of India  scheme.
