                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

533






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Funds to Odisha under Various Schemes




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka













                                                    Anubhav Mohanty
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the names of various schemes being implemented by the Ministry in Odisha  at present;(b)	the number of beneficiaries of such schemes, district-wise;(c)     whether the funds under these schemes have been spent within the prescribed    time limit, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d)     whether the Government of Odisha has forwarded proposals requesting for  release of funds under various schemes to the Ministry;(e)     if so, the details of all those schemes where the release of funds to the Government of Odisha is pending along with the reasons therefor; and(f)      the further steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Union Government for early release of funds in order to enable the Government of Odisha to carry out the said schemes successfully?






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)    The names of various schemes being implemented by the Ministry in the State of Odisha at present are as under:1.	POSHAN Abhiyaan2.	Anganwadi Services  Schemes3.	Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) 4.	One Stop Centre and Universalization of Women Helplines5.	Child Protection Services Scheme6.	Scheme for Adolescent Girls(SAG)7.	Swadhar Greh Scheme8.	Ujjawala Scheme9.	Working Women Hostel              (b) & (c)    The funds are released and utilized as per schematic norms.     The number of beneficiaries is annexed at Annexure-I. (d) to (f) The State Government of Odisha has forwarded proposals for release of funds under Swadhar Greh & Ujjawala Schemes. State Governments/UTs are regularly advised to submit the Utilization Certificates under the scheme on time to enable Central Government to further release of fund as per Budget allocations and schematic norms.**** Annexure-I No. of beneficiaries under various Schemes in the State of Odisha  S.No.	Name of Scheme	No. of beneficiaries1.
