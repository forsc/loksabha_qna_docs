                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

818






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Small Scale Industry Status to Dye Making Industry




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government is likely to award small scale industry status to the ancient jewellery dye making business of Palghar district of Maharashtra which is quite popular among the tourists and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether any coordination has been set up with the Government of Maharashtra or any scheme is being formulated for awarding recognition to the jewellery dye making business as that of an industry and exploring a market for it under tourism and if so, the details thereof ? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Under the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development Act, 2006 (MSMED Act, 2006), the Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises have been classified as follows:-<pre>	MANUFACTURING	SERVICESMICRO	Up to Rs.25 lakh	Up to Rs.10 lakhSMALL	Above Rs.25 lakh & up to Rs. 5 crore	Above Rs. 10  lakh  & Up to Rs. 2 croreMEDIUM	Above   Rs. 5  crore   &  up toRs. 10   crore	Above Rs. 2 crore & up to Rs. 5 crore </pre>(b):	The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) implements various schemes and programmes for promotion and development of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) throughout the country, including Maharashtra. These include the schemes/programmes such as Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries(SFURTI), A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry and Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE), National Manufacturing Competitiveness Programme(NMCP), Micro  and Small Enterprises-Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), National Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe Hub(NSSH), etc.
