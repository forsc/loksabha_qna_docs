                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

270






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






DAY-NRLM Scheme in Maharashtra




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the allocation under the Deendayal Antyoday Yojana - National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAYNRLM) in the State of Maharashtra for the year 2019-2020, district-wise;(b) 	the number of Self Help Groups who have availed Aajeevika loans;(c) 	the type of benefits given to beneficiaries under the said scheme;(d) 	the amount released to the State of Maharashtra under the scheme during the last three years and the current year; and(e) 	the details of the amount released or likely to be released to Bhandara and Gondiya districts of Maharashtra under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 Under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM) and National Rural Economic Transformation Project (NRETP) funds are allocated and released to States based on their approval of Annual Action Plan by the Empowered Committee of this Ministry. No funds are being released directly to Districts from this Ministry under these schemes. However, The allocation under DAY-NRLM in the state of Maharashtra for the year 2019-20 is 762.84 Crore (Both Central and State share).
