                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4355






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Road Projects in Rajasthan




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has started the road projects regarding the construction of Debari Bypass and the conversion of four-lane roads into six-lane roads in Rajasthan and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government proposes to provide facilities to the people affected by the said road projects and undertake the work of Vehicle Underpasses (VUP), Pedestrian Underpasses (PUP), Passenger waiting room and plantation etc. works; (c)	if so, the details thereof and the time frame set for the purpose; and(d)	the details of the completed works along with the funds spent thereon so far by NHAI in Udaipur region?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 Yes Sir. The work for Six lane Greenfield  new Udaipur bypass from Debari to Kaya commenced on 30.11.2017. Further, upgradation from four lane to six lane for two projects namely Debari to Chittorgarh of National Highway 76 and Kaya to Shamlaji section of National Highway have also been taken up.(b) & (c) Yes Sir. The details of Vehicular Underpasses (VUP), Pedestrian Underpasses (PUP) and  Passenger Waiting Room on the work in Udaipur region are given below:Sl. No.	Name of the Projects	VUPs	PUPs	Bus Shelter	Anticipated date of completion1	Six laning of Chittorgarh-Udaipur section of NH-76	11	18	6	31.05.20202	Six lane Greenfield New Udaipur Bypass from Debari to Kaya section	2	12	0	30.09.20203	Six laning of Udaipur-    Ratanpur-  Shamlaji section of NH-8	7	27	25	30.11.2020(d)	Two works namely (i) Four laning of Gomati Chouraha-Udaipur section of NH -8 and (ii)Four laning of Swaroopganj –Pindwara-Udaipur section have already been completed by National Highways Authority of India in Udaipur Region, with expenditure of Rs. 856.93 crore and Rs. 896 crore respectively (as on February, 2020). *****
