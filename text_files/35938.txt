                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Development of National Highways




Srinivas Kesineni













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Jagdambika Pal 
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the length of National Highways (NHs) under construction and constructed during the last three years and the current year in the States of Andhra Pradesh, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh; (b) the details and the length of NHs constructed under various Public Private Partnership (PPP) modes in these States, State-wise; and (c) the details of the road assets proposed to be monetized under the National Monetization Pipeline along with the revenue generation expected therefrom in these States? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) & (b) Length of NHs under construction in current year and the length constructed during the last 3 years on PPP mode and non-PPP mode in the States of Andhra Pradesh, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh is as under:  Name of State	Length of NHs constructed during last 3 years in km	Length of NHs under construction in km	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22	Non PPP	PPP	Non PPP	PPP	Non PPP	PPP	Non PPP	PPP1	2	3	4	5	6	7	8	9Andhra Pradesh	403.71	29.00	373.00	220.00	409.00	107.00	238.00	44.00Rajasthan	433.83	284.17	426.22	589.78	675.00	151.00	579.73	37.27Uttar Pradesh	559.00	166.00	454.00	194.00	732.00	187.00	613.00	135.00 (c)     The traffic and technical studies of road assets proposed to be monetized in the States of Andhra Pradesh, Rajasthan & Uttar Pradesh under the National Monetization Pipeline of NITI Aayog are presently in progress. Hence, the expected revenue generation under the National Monetization Pipeline in these States can be worked out only after the receipt of Technical bids.  ******
