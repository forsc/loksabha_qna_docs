                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2254






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Inclusion of Mithila Region into Ramayana Circuit




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to include Mithila region of Darbhanga in the Ramayana Circuit; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Tourism has identified Ramayana Circuit as one of thematic circuit for development under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme. The Ministry has initially identified 15 destinations for development under this circuit in the country which includes Sitamarhi, Buxar & Darbhanga in Bihar. ********
