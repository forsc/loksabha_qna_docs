                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3523






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Action Plan for Heavy Industries




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any new action plan for heavy industries for Khargone Badwani Parliamentary Constituency in the near future;(b)	the details and locations where heavy industries are operating in the country, State-wise, particularly in Madhya Pradesh;(c)	whether any new scheme for heavy industries is under consideration for tribal areas; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a):	No Sir.(b):	The list of operational Central Public Sector Enterprises under administrative control of this Ministry alongwith location of its headquarters is attached at Annexure.(c):	No Sir.(d):	Does not arise.----------- Annexure S.No.	Name of Central Public Sector Enterprise	Location of Registered Office.1	Andrew Yule & Co.
