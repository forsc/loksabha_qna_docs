                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4905






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Rajya and Zila Sainik Boards




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the Rajya and Zila Sainik Boards functioning in the country, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Boards implement various schemes/ programmes for welfare of ex-servicemen;(c) if so, the details thereof including progress made and funds spent under these schemes during the last three years, State-wise;(d) whether the Government has reviewed the performance of these Boards and if so, the details and the outcome thereof; and(e) the other steps being taken by the Government to improve the functioning of these Boards?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a):  There are 34 Rajya Sainik Boards and 403 Zila Sainik Boards functioning across the country and the details are at Annexure-I.(b) & (c):  Kendriya Sainik Board (KSB) which is an attached office of Ministry of Defence runs various welfare schemes for ex-servicemen with the help of Rajya Sainik Boards.  To avail the benefits of welfare schemes of KSB, the beneficiaries apply online on the website of KSB.  The applications are scrutinized and verified by respective Rajya Sainik Boards/Zila Sainik Board.  After approval, KSB credits the amount directly into the bank accounts of the beneficiaries.  The details of State wise funds spent during last three years is at Annexure-II.(d):  The Rajya Sainik Boards and Zila Sainik Boards function under the administrative control of State Government.  Performance of the Rajya Sainik Boards and Zila Sainik Boards is reviewed by concerned State Governments. (e):  Not applicable in view of the reply to point (d) above.*****
