                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4593






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Status of Various Schemes in Assam




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the schemes being run by the Government in Assam;(b)	the status of the schemes being run by the Government in Assam and North Eastern States of the country;(c)   the details of  the funds provided to North Eastern States during the last three years along with the district-wise details of utilisation of this fund thereof;(d)   the number of recipients of awards given by the Ministry during the last three year; and(e)   the details of the Committees constituted by the Government during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a), (b) & (c) Ministry of Culture does not operate any State specific scheme. It operates a number of central sector schemes as given in Annexure. These schemes are applicable for all States/ UTs. The Ministry does not allocate funds State and district wise. However, funds allocated to North Eastern States and actual expenditure thereof during the last three years are as follows:-                          (Rs. in crore)Financial Year	        2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Fund allocation  	175.50	175.50	175.50Actual Expenditure 	127.12	103.96	122.61(d) As per available information, a total of 72 persons from North Eastern region including Assam have received awards during the period.(e) The Ministry has not constituted any State/ Region specific Committee during the period.Annexure Annexure referred to in reply to part (a), (b) and (c) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 4593 for 22.7.2019 regarding status of various schemes in Assam.
