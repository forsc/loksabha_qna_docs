                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Namami Gange Programme




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds sanctioned by the Government for the expenditure under Namami Gange Programme (NGP) from 2014-15 to 2019-20; (b) the funds spent till date out of the allotted funds, year and State-wise; and (c) the details of the projects launched, completed and cost incurred till date under the said programme, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  Government of India has launched the Namami Gange Programme in June 2014, with the total budgetary outlay of Rs.20,000 crore for the period from 2014-15 till 31 December, 2020.
