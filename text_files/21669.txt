                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






National Commissions for AYUSH Systems of Medicine




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has any proposal to set up National Commissions for Homeopathy and Indian System of Medicines;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the proposed functions of the Commissions;(c) the time by which the said Commissions are likely to be set up; and(d) the status of implementation of Siddha system of medical treatment in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):  Yes, the National Commission for Indian System of Medicine (NCISM) Act, 2020 and the National Commission for Homoeopathy (NCH) Act,2020, were published in the official Gazette of India on 21/09/20. The main functions of the said Commissions is to regulate the education and practice of Indian System of Medicine and Homoeopathy respectively.(d):	    The Siddha system is a recognized system under Ministry of AYUSH. The education and practice of Siddha system of medicine is regulated under the provisions of Indian Medicine Central Council Act, 1970. Further, under the NCISM Act, 2020, Siddha system is also included as one of the Indian System of Medicine. There are 13 colleges with attached hospitals under Siddha System of Medicine as per the details at Annexure I. The Central Government has established an apex research council namely Central Council for Research in Siddha (CCRS), Chennai and National Institute of Siddha (NIS), Chennai which are implementing Siddha system of education, research including medical treatment in the country. Details are as per Annexure II and III.
