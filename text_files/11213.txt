                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

124






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






UGC




T. R. Paarivendhar













                                                    A. Ganeshamurthi
                                                








                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that a committee headed by Shri M.M. Salukhe had suggested to anew national board (UGC) to conduct the examination on-demand system;(b) whether the new national board will address the needs of millions of students countrywide;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) the purpose and motive to bring the new system of on-demand exam; and(e) the salient features of the proposed new on-demand exam?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a): Yes, Sir. The University Grants Commission has informed that the Committee hassuggested for the establishment of a National Board to conduct Examination On-Demand.(b) & (c): This system is envisaged to provide flexibility to the students, specially thoseenrolled under open and distance education mode, to undertake assessment based upontheir readiness.(d) & (e): The Committee has recommended the following salient features for this system:<pre>(i) Related syllabus, study material and question banks to be made available to thecandidates;(ii) Open to all, no age limit, minimum requirements of eligibility etc;(iii) Use of technology/automation;(iv) Ensure highest dependability, openness, reliability, transparency and recognition.</pre>******
