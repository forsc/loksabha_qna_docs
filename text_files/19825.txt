UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






CSR Spent in Bihar




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any amount has been spent under the CSR by the corporate companies in Bihar;(b) if so, the details thereof during each of the last three years, district-wise and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) the details of the companies which have spent the amount during the said period in Bihar, company-wise and sector-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                           (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)   ????? ??? ???????? ?????  ???????? ??? ????? ??????                              (???? ?????? ???? ?????) (a) to (c):  Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) is a Board driven process and the Board of the company is empowered to plan, decide, execute and monitor the CSR activities of the company based on the recommendation of its CSR Committee. The entire CSR architecture is disclosure based and CSR mandated companies are required to file details of CSR activities annually in MCA21 registry.
