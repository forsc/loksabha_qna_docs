                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

176






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Re-structured Seeds Village Scheme




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether re-structured Seeds Village Scheme to upgrade the quality of farmers saved seeds is being implemented; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government is aware that financial assistance under the scheme has not reached the farmers directly, if so, the details thereof and the corrective measures taken in this regard;(d) 	whether the Government is implementing any scheme to provide seeds at lower prices to SC/ST/OBC farmers living below the poverty line; (e)	if so, the details thereof along with the number of such farmers benefited in Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra during each of the last three years and the current year; and (f) 	whether the Government has utilized the services of Non-Governmental Organizations to provide seeds to the farmers, and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):       Yes. Since 2014-15, the restructured Seed Village Scheme is being implemented as a component of Sub-Mission for Seed and Planting Material (SMSP).(b):   In the restructured scheme financial assistance of 50% for distribution of certified/foundation seeds of cereal crops & 60% for distribution of certified/foundation seeds of oilseeds, pulses, fodder and green manure crops is available.Contd…2/--2-(c):    Under the scheme, Government of India provides financial assistance to the  implementing agencies of different States for onward distribution to farmers and DBT has also been implemented so that the assistance could reach the farmers directly.(d):     The scheme is being implemented to benefit small and marginal farmers of all categories and there are differential norms of assistance in some components for SC &ST category of farmers.(e):      The details of beneficiaries in Tamilnadu and Maharashtra State under the Seed Village Scheme are placed at Annexure-I.(f):      Non Governmental Organizations(NGOs) are not eligible for financial assistance under this scheme.
