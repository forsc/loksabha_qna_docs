                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2190






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Janaushadhi Kendra in Tezpur




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Pradhan Mantri Janaushadhi Kendras in Tezpur Parliamentary Constituency of Assam; and (b) the details of the procedure for obtaining sanction for setting up such Kendras under Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(Dr. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)                               (a)  There are two functional Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) in Tezpur Parliamentary Constituency of Assam, as on 05.12.2021. (b)  As per guidelines of Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP), any individual having D. Pharma/B. Pharma degree or any individual/organization who has employed D. Pharma/B. Pharma degree holders for getting drug licenses from the concerned State Government, is eligible to open Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendra (PMBJK) provided it fulfill the following requirements: (i)	 Minimum 120 sq. ft. space, duly supported by proper documents; (ii)	 Registration details of the pharmacist with State Pharmacy Council; and (iii)      Certificate/proof from respective authorities for the applicants under the category of women entrepreneurs, divyangs, SCs and STs along with an Undertaking.The applicants can check the guidelines and apply online for opening PMBJKs through the official website (janaushadhi.gov.in) of the Pharmaceuticals & Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI), the implementing agency of the scheme. PMBI grants in principle approval subject to fulfilment of procedural requirements. On submission of a drug license in the name of PMBJK from the drug authority concerned by the applicant, final approval is granted by the PMBI. *****
