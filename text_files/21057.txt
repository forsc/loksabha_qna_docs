                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






New Projects for Madhya Pradesh




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recently proposed to launch new projects to promote tourism on a large scale in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether any such project has been approved for Madhya Pradesh where huge tourism potential is available; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	The Ministry of  Tourism as  part of  its  regular  promotional  exercise  promotes  various  tourism  destinations  and products of the  country  including those  in  Madhya Pradesh  in  domestic  and  international  potential  markets in  a  holistic  manner. These  promotions  are  undertaken through  various  communication  platforms  such  as  media  campaigns,  events,  webinars,  road shows,  participation in  fairs  &  festivals, social media  platforms  etc.  The Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies including Madhya Pradesh  for development of tourism infrastructure in the country.  The details of the projects  sanctioned  under  ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘PRASHAD’ schemes is Annexed.******
