                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






MCI Recognition of Medical Colleges




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFAREbe pleased to state:(a) the number and names of Medical Colleges recognised by the Medical Councilof India (MCI) in the country during the last three years;(b) the amount of funds allocated by the Government for providing facilities in these colleges after being recognised by the MCI;(c) whether the Government has assessed the progress/improvements made in the building facility, Medical equipments and teaching faculty after the utilisation of above funds for the upgradation of the colleges; and(d) if so, the details and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)  to (d)  Medical Council of India (MCI) after verifying the required teaching and other facilities i.e. faculty, residents, clinical materials, building as per Minimum Standard Requirements (MSRs) recommends to the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India to recognize the eligible Medical College under Section 11(2) of Indian Medical Council (IMC) Act, 1956.  Ministry of Health & Family Welfare on the recommendations of the Medical Council of India issues necessary Notification recognizing the Medical College. During the last three years, 59 Medical Colleges were recognized. Details of these Medical Colleges are given at Annexure.The Central Government runs a Centrally Sponsored Scheme to provide assistance to Government Medical Colleges for increase of Under Graduate and Post Graduate seats. None of the Government Medical Colleges recognized in the last three years has been assisted under the scheme as on date.
