                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






NH Projects in Punjab




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and present status of National Highways (NHs) which are under construction in the State of Punjab;(b) whether all these NHs are likely to be completed within their stipulated time period and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the funds allocated and released for these NH projects, projects-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)          Total 33 projects on National Highways in a length of 580.49 Kms amounting to Rs. 5573.56 Crore are under construction in the State of Punjab. The projects are delayed due to Pandemic COVID-19, delay in land acquisition, utility shifting and forest clearances.  The details of these projects including their sanctioned cost and expenditure are at Annex-I.
