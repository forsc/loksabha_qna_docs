                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

402






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Assistance to Establish Cold Storage in Telangana




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has provided any assistance during the last three years to establish cold storage in order to protect fruits and vegetables from decaying in Telangana; and (b)	If so, the details thereof, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):   Yes, under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), Government has provided assistance during the last three years to establish cold storage in order to protect fruits and vegetables from decaying in Telangana.   A total of 19 cold storage units having total capacity of 125441 MT were sanctioned with financial assistance of Rs. 2498.00 lakh during the last three years. Out of these, 3 units were sanctioned during 2018-19, 9 units during 2019-20 and 7 units during 2020-21. 	Further, National Horticulture Board (NHB) has also sanctioned 5 cold storage units havingtotal capacity of 47320 MT with financial assistance of Rs. 896.83 lakh in the State of Telangana during last three years. All these cold storage units were sanctioned during 2019-20.********
