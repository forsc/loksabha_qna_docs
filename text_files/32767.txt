                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

14






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






GST on Textile Products




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal to increase GST on textile products; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has taken any steps recently to improve the competitiveness of the textile sector in India; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	GST rate and exemptions are prescribed on the recommendations of the GST Council. As regards increase in GST rates on textiles, the matter was placed before the GST Council in its       46th Meeting, dated 31st December 2021, where the council recommended to defer the decision to change in the rates in textiles recommended in the 45th GST Council meeting. Consequently, the existing rates in textile sector are continuing beyond 1st January 2022.(c) & (d):	Government has recently introduced Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme and Pradhan Mantri Mega Integrated Textile Region and Apparel (PM-MITRA) Scheme, which inter-alia intend to improve the competitiveness of textile sector.***
