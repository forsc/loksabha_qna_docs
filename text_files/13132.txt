                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1961






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Fruit Processing Units




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has allocated any funds or subsidy for mango pulp and tamarind processing units in Tamil Nadu;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government has received any request to provide support for the export of processed mango pulp and other food products from Tamil Nadu;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the stand of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a). & (b): Yes Sir. Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a Scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for creation/expansion of food processing industries including mango pulp and tamarind processing in the country. State-wise funds are not allocated under the scheme. As on date, 219 food processing units have been approved under the scheme. Out of which, 19 food processing units are in the State of Tamil Nadu. One food processing unit namely M/s Jaycee Organics LLP at District Tirunelveli in Tamil Nadu proposes to use mango as raw material to convert to mango pulp. The details of units approved, grants-in-aid sanctioned and released under the scheme is at Annexure.(c) & (d): No Sir,(e):	Government promotes export of processed food products through Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) which comes under the administrative control of Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India. *****
