                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3867






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Review of SAGY




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to review the Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana(SAGY) in order to remove the problem being faced by the Peoples’ representatives therein;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to allocate funds especially for this programme; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	There is no proposal to  review the Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) as of now. However, SAGY is reviewed periodically by the Ministry of Rural Development. The Ministry has evolved a comprehensive multilevel and multi-tool system of monitoring and evaluation of the implementation of its programmes which include review by the Union Ministers, Performance Review Committee meetings, National Level Monitors, Area Officers Scheme, Concurrent Evaluation and Impact Assessment studies. Concurrent monitoring mechanisms include the progress updated by States/Districts through SAGY website regarding identification of the Gram Panchayats, progress tracking of Village Development Plans, and the progress reported for Panchayat Darpan indicators. (c) & (d):	Under the Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana framework, the development of Gram Panchayats has been envisaged through the convergence and implementation of existing Government Schemes and Programmes under the administrative control of respective Ministries. There is no proposal under consideration to allocate funds specially for this programme. *****
