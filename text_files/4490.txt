                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4133






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Work on NH-183 and NH-183A in Kerala




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of construction of  National Highway (NH)-183 and NH-183A in Kerala;(b)	    the funds allocated, released and utilised for construction work of these projects;(c) 	whether acquisition of land is required for the purpose; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	Detailed Project Report (DPR) for widening to 2 lane with paved shoulders configuration of  NH 183 from Kollam to Kumily and NH 183A from Bharanikavu to Vandiperayar in Kerala has been taken up by Ministry through State PWD. The development works are sanctioned based on outcome of DPR, inter-se priority and availability of fund. Further, 7 works of strengthening of existing NH have been sanctioned by Ministry on NH 183 and NH 183A during last 3 years, details of which are at Annexure I.(c) & (d)	Land Acquisition is to be done as per requirement based on outcome of Detailed Project Report (DPR).
