                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Protection Wall of Bank Erosion on Farmers’ Land




Kuldeep Rai Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether it is a fact that an amount of  Rs.  23  crore  has  been  earmarked  for protection wall of bank erosion on farmers’ land;(b)	 if so, the progress made during the last two years; and(c)	  if  not,  whether  the  Government proposes to allocate the required fund in the budget 2019-20 and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):     Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare does not have any programme/ scheme for protection wall of bank erosion on farmers’ land.   Further,   subject of flood management including erosion control falls under the purview of the States, as such the flood management and anti erosion schemes are planned, investigated and implemented by the State Governments with their own resources as per priority within the State.  As per information,   central assistance is not provided for protection wall of bank erosion on farmers’ land.                   ********
