                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3965






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Committee on 5G Technology




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh













                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that a committee under Principal Scientific Advisor (PSA) has been constituted to give recommendations relating to 5G and technology trials in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has set any timeline for the said committee to give its recommendations; (c) 	if so, the time by which the committee is likely to submit its recommendations to the Government; (d) 	whether the Government has taken note of the security concerns including surveillance over 5G technology trials; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (e)	A Sub-committee on 5G and technology trials having representatives from relevant Departments/Ministries has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Scientific Advisor to give recommendations on various issues, including security, relating to 5G and technology trials in India. The Government has not set any timeline for the committee to submit its recommendations.
