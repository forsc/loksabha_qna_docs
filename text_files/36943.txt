                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4093






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Amendment in Prohibited Area Limit Around Monuments




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of monument-wise number of the unauthorized constructions within the prohibited and regulated areas of the protected monuments.(b)	whether the Government is aware that the existing 100 mtr. prohibited area limit of protected monuments is causing hindrance in execution of public works like metro, flyovers, elevated roads and hardship to the land owners with reference to small and non-ticketed monuments;(c)	whether any proposal is pending to revisit such 100 mtr. prohibited area and 200 mtr. regulated area limits; and(d)	if so, the details of such proposal?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	The details of encroachments/unauthorized constructions are being compiled.(b) to (d) 	Yes, Sir. The Government has taken a decision to examine the legal issues affecting construction activities around centrally protected monuments.     *****
