                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4154






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Double Decker Metro Line




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any proposal for construction of double decker corridor on the route of Metro line-9 to be introduced from Dahisar to Bhayander; and(b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                            (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b):  	Mumbai Metro Line-9 between Dahisar to Bhayander is being implemented by Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) under State Sector project of Government of Maharashtra. MMRDA has informed that three double decker flyovers are planned in this metro alignment with metro rail system on the upper level, as detailed below:-(i) Flyover of length 1010 meters covering Silver Park and Hatkesh Junctions.(ii) Flyover of length 754 meters covering SK Stone and Nayanagar Junctions.(iii)	Flyover of length 1050 meters covering Deepak Hospital and Golden Nest Junctions.                                     *****
