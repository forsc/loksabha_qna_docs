                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Rashtriya Kamadhenu Aayog




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that theConstitution of India casts upon every citizen a duty todevelop scientific temper, humanism and the spirit ofinquiry and reform under Article 51A;(b) if so, whether the Government will take stringentaction against spreading unscientific and irrational factsin the name of Rashtriya Kamadhenu Aayog (RKA)against the Constitutional mandate;(c) whether the Government will consider reviewingthe unscientific and irrational contents in the website ofRashtriya Kamadhenu Aayog; and(d) whether the Government will consider preventingthe RKA from conducting ‘Kamadhenu Gau VigyanPrachar-Prasar Examination’ as it would result in thespread of unscientific and irrational information in thecountry?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a).     Yes, sir.(b) to (d)	The website of the Aayog has already been reviewed and unscientific content has been removed.  No KamadhenuGau Vigyan Prachar-Prasar examination has been conducted by Rashtriya Kamdhenu Aayog.
