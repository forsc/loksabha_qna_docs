                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1364






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Bio Fuel Processing Plants




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of bio fuel;(b) whether the Government has fixed any target in the country in this regard, if so, the State-wise details thereof including Rajasthan along with its present status;(c) whether the Union Government has accorded sanction to set up bio fuel processing plant in Rajasthan, if so, the details thereof along with present status thereof;(d) the details of the amount sanctioned and utilised in this regard;(e) whether the Government has signed or proposes to sign any Agreement/Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Brazil for bio fuel, if so, the details thereof; and(f) the amount sanctioned and the target fixed under the said Agreement/MoU?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): ‘Biofuels’ are fuels produced from renewable resources like biodegradable products, wastes and residues from agriculture, forestry, tree based oil other non-edible oils and used in place of or in blend with diesel, petrol or other fossil fuels for transport, stationary, portable and other applications.(b): The National Policy on Biofuels announced in 2018 has laid out indicative targets of achieving 20% blending in petrol and 5% blending in diesel by 2030 in the country.(c) & (d): No, Sir.(e) & (f): Government of India has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Government of Brazil on 25th January, 2020 on Bioenergy Cooperation. The MoU provides a framework to cooperate, inter alia, in biofuels, bioelectricity and biogas supply-chain.******
