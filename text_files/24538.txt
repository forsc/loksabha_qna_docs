                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

346






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Funds for Development of Agriculture Sector




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	the details of the funds allocated to Uttar Pradesh for development of the agriculture sector during the last five years, scheme-wise; b.	the current growth of the agriculture sector;c.	the percentage of growth rate of agriculture sector targeted to be achieved in order to double the income of the farmers; and d.	the steps taken by the Government to achieve the said growth rate for the agriculture sector?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  The details of funds allocated to Uttar Pradesh for the development of the agriculture sector during the last five years, scheme-wise are at Annexure. (b) The growth rate in Gross Value Added in Agriculture and Allied Sector during 2020-21 was 3.6 percent at 2011-12 prices.  (c) & (d)  The Government has adopted several developmental programmes, schemes, reforms and policies that focus on higher incomes for farmers. All these policies & programmes are being supported by higher budgetary allocations and non-budgetary financial resources by way of creating Corpus Funds like Micro Irrigation Fund. Other major steps include promotion of 10,000 FPOs scheme, supplementary income transfers under PM-KISAN, Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojna (PMFBY), Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojna (PMKSY), Increase in Minimum Support Price (MSPs) for all Kharif & Rabi Crops, Har Med Par Ped, etc. The latest major intervention includes ‘Atma Nirbhar Bharat – Agriculture’ which includes creation of  an Agricultural Infrastructure Fund (AIF) worth Rs. 1 lakh crore, National Bee and Honey Mission and special drive to distribute Kisan Credit Cards.
