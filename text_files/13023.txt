                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1866






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Woman Employment in MGNREGS




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the pace of providing employment to women under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) has slowed down in rural areas during the last few years;(b)	 if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) 	the number of women workers provided employment under this scheme during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise/UT-wise;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to provide regular/permanent employment to women keeping in view the declining tendency of women participation under MGNREGS;(e)	 if so, the details thereof; and(f) 	the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)&(b):          The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme.  The rate of participation of women in Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the last three years and current year is above the statutory requirement of 1/3rd women participation.  The details in this regard are as under:Financial Year	                     2016-2017	   2017-2018	2018-2019	2019-2020(As on 27.02.2020)Percentage of participation of Women	56%	     54%	   55%	           55% (c):      Details of State/UT-wise employment provided to women under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the last three years and current FY 2019-20 (as 27.02.2020) is given at Annexure. (d):      No, Sir. (e)& (f):          Do not arise. ********
