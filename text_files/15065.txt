                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3908






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Closure of Biscuit Factories




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has been notified of any closure of biscuit factories due to declining demand;(b)	if so, the details of such factories closed including their number, State-wise along with the number of workers laid off and current status of the farmers;(c)	whether the Government is taking any steps to redress the concerns of biscuit factories?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	No Sir.(b):	Does not arise.(c):	Ministry of Food Processing Industries (Mo FPI) i s implementing a Scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for creation of processing and preservation capacities for agri-horti products (including biscuits) in the country. Under the scheme, grants-in-aid @ 35% of the eligible project cost is provided in General Areas and 50% of the eligible project cost in North Eastern States including Sikkim and Dif icult areas including Himalayan States (Himachal Pradesh, J&K & Uttarakhand), notified ITDP areas and Islands, subject to a maximum of Rs.5.00 crore.*****
