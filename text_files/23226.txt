CSR Funds




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds available and utilised by each of the Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSUs) for the fiscal year 2019- 20;(b) the priority areas for which the CSR funds assistance have been considered and utilised;(c) the details of utilisation of the CSR funds of CPSUs for the fiscal year 2018-19, State-wise; and(d) the details of the CSR funds assistance, which have been considered and utilised through Non-Governmental Organisations for the fiscal year 2019-20 along with the names of such NGOs and the amount of funds sanctioned therefor?






ANSWER

(???? ?????? ???? ?????) (a) : As per the information provided by Department of Public Enterprises (DPE), the details of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds allocation and CSR expenditure by Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) for the financial year 2019-20 is given in Annexure.(b): Department of Public Enterprises, on the recommendations of Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) Conclave held in April, 2018, issued guidelines on 10.12.2018 to all administrative Ministries & CPSEs for adopting a theme based focused approach every year on CSR expenditure by CPSEs. The guidelines inter-alia provide that 60% of annual CSR expenditure by CPSEs will be towards a thematic programme and preference may be given to aspirational districts identified by NITI Aayog. For the financial year 2018-19, the theme identified was School Educational and Health Care.
