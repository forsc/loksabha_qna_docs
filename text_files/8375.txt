                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1873






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Hospital Management Information System




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government regularly conducts any quality check/audit of the district hospital data collected by Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) for better data collection and efficient service delivery; and (b) if so, the details thereof including the agency or organization which has been entrusted with the respective responsibility?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	& (b):      Public health being a State subject, primary responsibility to provide healthcare services to citizens lies with State/UT governments. However, under National Health Mission (NHM) scheme, financial support is being provided to States/UTs for roll out of Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) based on requirements posed by States in Project Implementation Plan (PIP) proposals.For efficient service delivery the implementation of HMIS is being periodically reviewed by the Ministry in consultation with States during various meetings including PIP, Quarterly Review Meetings (QRM) & Common Review Meetings (CRM). 	 However, no agency/ organisation has been entrusted with the responsibility to quality check/ audit of district hospital data.……………….
