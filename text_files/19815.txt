                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1079






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Revenue Generated from Medical Tourism




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of foreign tourists who have visited the country for medical tourism during the period from 2009 to 2019; (b) 	the country-wise percentage of the foreign medical tourists visited for the period from 2009 to 2020; (c)	the number of foreign tourists who have visited the States of Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Maharashtra and Delhi for medical tourism during the period from 2009 to 2020; (d) 	the revenue generated from Medical Tourism during the period from 2009 to 2020; and (e) 	the steps taken by the Government for the development and promotion of medical tourism to augment foreign medical tourists in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Details of Foreign Tourists Arrivals (FTA) for medical purpose during 2009 to 2019 are given below: <pre>Year	FTA on medical visa2009	1123892010	1559442011	1388032012	1710212013	2368982014	1394472015	2339182016	4270102017	4950562018	6407982019	697453</pre> Please note that till 2013 Ministry of Tourism used to compile purpose wise Foreign Tourist Arrivals on the basis of the information available from disembarkation card.  From 2014 onwards, data purpose of Arrivals has been compiled by clubbing various visa type categories in which foreign tourist travel to India. (b):	The details of percentage distribution of foreign tourists arrivals from leading countries for medical purpose during 2009 to 2019 are given in Annexure I. Purpose wise data for year 2020 is not available.(c):	Data on purpose wise foreign tourists visits to different States/UTs is not centrally maintained by Ministry of Tourism.(d):	Ministry of tourism, Government of India does not maintained data on revenue generated from tourism.  (e):	The steps taken by the Government for development & promotion of medical tourism in the country are given in Annexure- II.  *******
