                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2940






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Funds Allocated under CAMPA




Chunni Lal Sahu













                                                    Mohan Mandavi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds allocated under Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) during the years 2018-19 and 2019-20, scheme, year and State-wise;(b) 	whether an audit has been conducted regarding the allocation of fund and if so, the percentage of fund on which audit has been conducted;(c) 	whether 100 percent audit is likely to be conducted on the amount provided during the last year and the current year to the States including Chhattisgarh, if so, the details thereof; and (d)	 the total number of complaints received by the Government regarding expenditure of the allocated fund along with number of people found guilty including action taken against them during the said period, year-wise and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)&(b)	The State/UT wise funds disbursed by the Ad-hoc CAMPA during the year 2018-19 and 2019-20 is attached at Annexure-I. After coming into force of the Compensatory Afforestation Fund (CAF) Act, 2016 with effect from 30.09.2018 an amount of ?48,477.77 crore has been disbursed as on 31.01.2021 to 31 States/UTs including Chhattisgarh who have established their State Funds. The transfer/disbursement have been  made after these States/UTs have completed the reconciliation of funds, head wise and component wise. The details are attached at Annexure-II. (c)	The Compensatory Afforestation Fund (CAF) Act, 2016 provides for audit of the accounts of each State Authority by Comptroller and Auditor-General of India.(d)	All complaints received in the National Authority are forwarded to the concerned State Government/UT Administration for enquiry. In addition, the State Governments/UT Administrations also take action on their own on the complaints received by them, and after enquiry, take necessary action including initiation of disciplinary proceedings  against the guilty personnel.***
