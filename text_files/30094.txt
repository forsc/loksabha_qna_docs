                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

122






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






National Young Leaders Programme




C.N. Annadurai













                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is implementing National Young Leaders Programme (NYLP) in the country and if so, the details thereof alongwith aims, objectives and various components of the said programme;(b) the details of funds allocated/released and expenditure incurred under the said programme since its inception, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra;(c) the number of programmes organized in Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra under NYLP since its inception; and(d) the details of various activities undertaken and the number of youth actively participated/involved in governance process under NYLP, particularly in the above two States?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*122 FOR 07.12.2021 REGARDING NATIONAL YOUNG LEADERS PROGRAMME ASKED BY    SHRI C. N. ANNADURAI AND SHRI ANURAG SHARMA, HON’BLE MPs:(a) 	Yes, the Government is implementing National Young Leaders Programme (NYLP) since December, 2014 with the objective to develop leadership qualities among the youth to enable them to realise their full potential and in the process, to contribute to the nation-building process.  The Programme aims at motivating the youth to strive for excellence in their respective fields and to bring them to the forefront of the development process. It seeks to harness the immense youth energy for national-building. At present, various components of NYLP include Neighbourhood Youth Parliament (NYP), Youth for Development Programme (YFDP), National Young Leaders Awards (NYLA) and National Youth Advisory Council (NYAC). (b) The details of funds allocated/released and expenditure incurred under the said programme since its inception, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra is placed at ANNEXURE I.(c)  The number of Programmes organized under NYLP in Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra since inception is placed at ANNEXURE II.(d)  (i) Under the Youth for Development Programme (YFDP), youth are involved in Shramdaan (voluntary labour) which helps them to develop their personality and leadership qualities and promote the spirit of ‘dignity of labour’ and channelizes the immense youth energy towards nation building. For instance, Cleanliness activities are a special area of focus as far as activities under this Programme are concerned. Department of Youth Affairs as part of ‘Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav’ had taken up Clean India campaign on the model of ‘Jan Bhagidari to Jan Andolan’ with the target of collecting 75 Lakh kgs of waste, mainly Single Use Plastic in the span of one month from 01 st October, 2021 – 31 st October, 2021. Brief report on the campaign is as under:<pre>1.	Total No. of Volunteers involved                   	98,98,1022.	Total Plastic waste collected/disposed in (Kgs)          	1,14,10,750 Kg3.	No. of Educational institution, Primary Health Centre, Public/Community places cleaned/ covered             	1,71,1054.	No. of Villages/Slums cleaned/covered          	3,75,1225.	No. of water bodies cleaned/covered             	48,4596.	No. of Historical/Iconic location/ covered	43,709</pre> (d)   (ii) Under NYLP an institutional platform have been created in the form of Neighbourhood Youth Parliaments (NYP), where the youth in the age group of 15-29 years, articulate their views on various issues affecting their lives and draw attention of the local administration. Neighbourhood Youth Parliaments provide a platform to educate youth about contemporary socio-economic development issues confronting local communities in general and the youth in particular like literacy, consumer awareness, community health, cleanliness, child marriage, environment conservation, women empowerment, skill development etc.  The details regarding number of youth actively participated /involved in governance process under NYLP, particularly in Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra is placed at ANNEXURE III.***
