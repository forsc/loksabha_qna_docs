                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3434






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






City Compost Scheme




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the main purpose of the city compost scheme; (b)	whether villages would be adopted by  the fertilizer companies to promote the use of compost; and (c)	if so, the name of the villages adopted for this purpose in Gujarat? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI  D.V. SADANANDA  GOWDA)(a)	:	The main objective of the City Compost Scheme is to reduce the volume of waste to land fill/dumpsite by converting the waste into useful organic fertilizers and  complementing the objectives of “SWACHH BHARAT ABHIYAN” campaign of Government.  (b) & (c):	  To promote the use of city compost, the Fertilizers marketing Companies have adopted 498 villages across the Country.  A list of 56 villages adopted by the fertilizer marketing companies in Gujarat is at Annexure.  ********
