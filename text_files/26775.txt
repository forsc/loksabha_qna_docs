                                    ANSWERED ON:

National Awards against Drug Abuse & Illicit Trafficking




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2393 TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.08.2021NATIONAL AWARDS AGAINST DRUG ABUSE & ILLICIT TRAFFICKING 2393. SHRI A. GANESHAMURTHI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is giving national awards every year on the day of International day against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking for outstanding services rendered by Institutions and Individuals in the field of prevention of alcoholism and substanace (Drug) abuse; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of application has been received from the various Institutions and Individuals for consideration of this award, name-wise and State-wise; (d) the criteria adopted to give this award to Institutions and Individuals; (e) whether steps will be taken to prohibit sale of alcoholism in the country; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a) & (b): Government gives National Awards every alternate year on the day of International Day against Drug Abuse and  Illicit Trafficking  for outstanding services rendered by Institutions and Individuals in the field of prevention of    alcoholism and substance (Drug) abuse.  Last award ceremony was held on 26.06.2018 in Vigyan Bhavan, New Delhi. Subsequent award  ceremony could not be held  in the years 2020 or 2021 due to COVID-19.2.    National awards are conferred for outstanding services in the field of prevention of alcoholism and substance (Drug) abuse for the following categories:I.	Institutional Categorya.	Best Integrated Rehabilitation Centre for Addicts (IRCA) for providing rehabilitation services to alcoholics and drug users.b.	Best Panchayati Raj or Municipal Body working for prevention of alcoholism and substance (drug) abuse.c.	Best Educational Institution doing outstanding work in awareness generation and prevention of alcoholism and substance (drug) abuse.d.	Best Non-Profit Institution, other than Serial Number (a), (b), (c), with outstanding contribution in Prevention of Alcoholism and Substance (Drug) Abuse.e.	Best Research or Innovation.f.	Best Awareness Campaign.II.	Individual Categorya.	Outstanding achievement by an individual(c):  The applications were called in for the year 2020,   but the awards were not given due to outbreak of  COVID-19. The category-wise details of applications received in 2020 are as under:S.No.	Category	No. of proposals received1. The national award are open to all Indian Institutions, Organizations and individuals without any distinction or discrimination on the grounds of religion, race, sex, caste or creed.ii.	To be eligible for the National Awards, it would be necessary that the nominee is recommended by someone authorized to recommend the nominations.iii.	The self nomination for the award shall not be considered by the screening committee and the selection committee.iv.	No individual or organization has been awarded should be considered for the National Award under the scheme for the next three awards.(e)  and (f): The prohibition of sale of alcohol  comes under the purview of State Governments.***** Annexure-IList of Applications received, State-wiseS.No.	Name	State	Category1.
