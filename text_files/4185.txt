                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3713






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Panchayat Elections




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry is aware that Panchayat elections have not been held since 2005 in the GTA region (earlier DGHC), Darjeeling, West Bengal;(b) the details of the States where Panchayat elections have not been held since 2005;(c) the manner in which the Government plans to implement grassroot level schemes in the absence of elected Panchayat bodies; and(d) the steps taken by the Government to restore the panchayat system in the States where Panchayat elections have not been held?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  Yes Sir. (b)   The details of States where Panchayat election is currently due is as under:Sl. No.	   States	             Month and Year when  election is due1	 Andhra Pradesh	             Gram Panchayat: August2018                                     Block Panchayat:   April 2019                                     District Panchayat: April 20192	 Arunachal Pradesh	     May 20183	 Tamil Nadu	             October 2016(c) Panchayat is a State subject and the elections to Panchayati Raj Institutions are held by the States under their respective Panchayati Raj Acts. For conduct of elections to the Local Bodies, States have also setup State Election Commissions. The Gram Panchayat level schemes are being implemented for those GPs as well where the elections are not held. (d) Ministry of Panchayati Raj regularly advises the States for holding regular and timely elections to Panchayats.**********
