                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4804






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Welfare Schemes for North Eastern States




Queen Oja














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of welfare schemes being run by the Ministry for North Eastern States including Assam;(b)	the funds allocated for said schemes during the last three years;(c)	whether these funds have been spent within the stipulated time limit; and(d)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	to (d):  Ministry of Culture does not operate any welfare scheme. It operates a number of central sector schemes that are applicable to all States/ UTs including North Eastern States. Funds allocated/utilized under various centre-sector schemes during the last three years are given below:-(Rs. in crores)Year	Fund allocated	Fund utilised 2017-18	61.00	43.042018-19	65.76	48.332019-20	42.69	35.30
