                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2860






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Textile Park in Maharasthra




Raksha Nikhil Khadse













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                








                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Kapil Moreshwar Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any representation/request or has any plan of setting up Integrated Textile Parks (ITPs) in the State of Maharashtra particularly in Bhiwandi; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the action taken/being taken by the Government in this regard; (c) the details of the funds sanctioned for each textile park; and (d) whether any location has already been identified in the State for this purpose, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (d): There is no proposal received for setting up of Integrated Textile  Park in Bhiwandi, Maharashtra under Scheme of Integrated Textile Park (SITP).  However, 13 Textile Parks under SITP were sanctioned in the State of Maharashtra, details of which are given in Annexure.
