                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2341






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Imports of Defective/Sub-Standard Steel




Pritam Gopinath Munde













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the fact that sub-standard or defective steel is being imported which is hurting the domestic manufacturers and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Ministry of Commerce and Industry has convened a high level meeting to discuss the matter and if so, the outcome thereof;(c)	whether there is a need to contain imports of defective and sub-standard steel to save/safeguard the interest of domestic manufacturers;(d)	if so, the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard; and (e)	the guidelines issued by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry for quality control for import of various steel products?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	During FY 2018-19, a quantity of 367530 Metric Tonne of defective steel was imported in India. (b)	Yes, Sir.  A High Level meeting chaired by Commerce & Industry Minister was convened on 11.06.2019 to discuss the issue of supply of steel at competitive rates to EEPC members.(c)to(e): Ministry of Steel has implemented quality control orders on 53 steel and steel products which is applicable for both domestic productions as well as imports. The Steel Quality Control Order are implemented in the public interest for protection of human, animal and plant, safety of environment, prevention of unfair trade practices and national security. ********
