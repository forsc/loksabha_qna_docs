                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3853






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Appointment on Compassionate Ground




Lallu Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of appointments made under the Ministry on compassionate grounds and on direct recruitment for the year 2014, name and age-wise;(b)	whether minors are entitled to apply for appointment on compassionate ground;(c)	if so, the details of manual (rules and regulations) in this regard;(d)	the date on which committee had been constituted for appointment on compassionate ground regarding cases under consideration and the decision taken by the committee in this regard on the cases under consideration and the number of meetings held by the committees in this regard and the details of names and posts of the members of the committee; and  (e)	the reasons for not appointing the dependents of deceased persons on compassionate ground, in the year 2014 in case such appointments are pending since 2000?   






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	The information on Compassionate Appointments made in the year 2014 may be treated as ‘NIL’ in respect of Ministry Proper and Office of Development Commissioner (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises). As regards the appointment on Direct Recruitment, information is as per Annexure.(b):	No.(c):	Does not arise.(d):	Does not arise.(e):	Does not arise.
