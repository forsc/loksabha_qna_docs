                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Schemes for Assam




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any schemes of skill training for youths under Ministry of Skill Development are being implemented in Dhubri and Goalpara districts of Assam which are among the most backward districts in the country;(b)	if so, the details scheme-wise and district-wise; and(c)	the details of the fund released and disbursed for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing various schemes of skill training for youths all over the country, including, interalia, in the Dhubri and Goalpara district of Assam. The major schemes being implemented are Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) and National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) for short term training and Craftsman Training Scheme (CTS) for long term training through Industrial Training Institutes (ITI). Besides, under the scheme Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS), one JSS in Dhubri district of Assam has been sanctioned in June 2021. (b)	The scheme-wise details of candidates trained under the above mentioned districts are given below:S.No.	Schemes	District name		Dhubri	Goalpara1	PMKVY*	15,456	7,5082	NAPS**	46	553	CTS***	43	68   *PMKVY data from 2016-21, November, 2021.   ** NAPS data from 2016 till 31.10.2021    ** CTS data from 2018-20.(c) 	Under PMKVY scheme (from 2016 to October, 21), Rs. 6.17 crores and Rs. 4.16 crores of fund has been released toDhubriandGoalpara district respectively. Under JSS scheme, Rs. 20 lakh has been released for setting up the JSS in Dhubri district.
