                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1075






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Intake of Students in Medical Colleges




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that Government had granted permission to start/open new medical colleges to start MBBS course/batch across the country for the academic year 2016-17, but refused to grant permission for intake of students in successive years;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of such medical colleges/ universities including private colleges which were not granted permission for intake of MBBS students and status and validity of the students studying in those colleges;(d) whether the Government has any intention to accord permission for renewal/intake of MBBS students to those colleges having all infrastructural facilities instead of opening/start new medical colleges;(e) if so, the details thereof; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (f): As per information provided by the Board of Governors in supersession of Medical Council of India (BOG-MCI), a total of 34 Medical Colleges were granted Letter of Permission (LoP) in the academic year 2016-17 for establishment of new medical colleges under Section 10A of Indian Medical Council Act, 1956 by the Central Government, as per the              directive /approval of Supreme Court Mandated Oversight Committee (OC). The status for successive years i..e 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 is at Annexure.  Permission to medical colleges is governed by Section 10 (A) of Indian Medical Council Act, 1956. Permission for establishment/ renewal permission to a medical college is granted by Central Government on the basis of recommendations by Medical Council of India (MCI) [currently by Board of Governors in supersession of Medical Council of India ] after assessment as per Minimum Standard Requirements under the MCI Regulations, 1999. The permission is  initially granted for one year and is renewed on yearly basis subject to verification of achievements of annual targets.  Medical Colleges which fail to meet the minimum standards are not given permission/renewal permission. In the event of closure of a college, based on the proposal of the concerned State Government, the students of such college are shifted to other medical college (s) in the State.
