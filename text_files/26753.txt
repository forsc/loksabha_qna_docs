                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Recovery of Illegal Arms and Ammunitions near LOC




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Gaurav Gogoi
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantum of illegal arms and ammunitions recovered by the security forces during the last three years in the areas near LoC, year-wise;(b)	the steps taken to check the flow of such illegal arms and ammunitions; (c)	whether the Government has taken any measures to counter cross border terrorism with the use of technology, if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the measures taken to develop cyber-warfare tactics of the security forces of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NISITH PRAMANIK)(a):	The year-wise details of illegal arms and ammunitions recovered by the security forces during the last three years in the areas near Line of Control (LOC) is at ANNEXURE-A.(b) & (c):	The Government has adopted a multi-pronged approach to counter cross border crimes i.e. infiltration, terrorism, smuggling etc which has an appropriate mix of technology and human resource. Innovative troop deployment,  proactive use  of  surveillance   and  monitoring   devices     has -2-LS.USQ. NO.2470 FOR 03.08.2021enhanced the ability to detect and intercept terrorists attempting infiltration/ exfiltration. The security forces regularly carry out threat assessment and analysis of past infiltration attempts and based on these reviews, drills and procedures are modified to counter emerging threats.(d):	The Government has formulated Defence Cyber Framework with responsibilities assigned to various agencies. Forces are continuously upgrading their systems and procedures to check and prevent cyber threats. To enhance the cyber warfare tactics, continuous training and upgradation of technical skills for personnel are being undertaken.******
