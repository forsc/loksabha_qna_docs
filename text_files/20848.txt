                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2093






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Kendriya Vidyalaya in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of the dilapidated condition of the present school building of Kendriya Vidyalaya, Barpeta, Assam and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the reasons for the delay in the completion of the school building of Kendriya Vidyalaya, Barpeta; (c) 	whether the Government has stopped sanctioning funds mid-way meant for the construction of the school building; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)	Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that the Kendriya Vidyalaya (KV), Barpeta, Assam is functioning in the temporary accommodation provided by the Sponsoring Authority i.e. State Government of Assam. As per the norms of KVS the sponsoring authority has to undertake repair maintenance of the temporary building till the KV is shifted in the permanent school building constructed by KVS. However, day to day maintenance is being done by the school authorities. (b) to (d) Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, which depends upon identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favour of KVS by the sponsoring authorities, submission of drawings / estimates by construction agency, availability of funds and requisite approvals etc. *****
