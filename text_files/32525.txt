                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

61






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Freight Smart Cities




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the aims and objectives of the proposed ‘Freight Smart Cities‘;(b) 	the details and the budget outlay and phases of implementation for Freight Smart Cities;(c) 	whether the 10 cities for the project have been identified and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether cities in the State of Rajasthan are under consideration for the project and if so, the details thereof district-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (d): 	A workshop on ‘Freight Smart Cities’ was held with the objective of exploring the measure to make the cities smart on the perspective of transportation of freight moving within and through the city.  Based on the deliberations, 3 cities have been identified for formulation of city logistics plan on pilot basis.  Since ‘Freight Smart City’ is not an approved scheme, therefore, no budgetary outlay and stages of implementation are available.  ***
