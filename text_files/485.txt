                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Loans to SCs




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has been implementing any scheme to give special impetus for providing loans to Scheduled Castes in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir.  Department of Social Justice and Empowerment provides concessional financial assistance in the form of loans through National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC) and Industrial Financial Corporation of India (IFCI) under Credit Based Schemes and Venture Capital Fund for Scheduled Castes (VCF-SC) respectively. The funds disbursed and beneficiary covered under the schemes of NSFDC and the scheme of VCF-SC of IFCI during the last five years (2014-15, 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19) respectively are given as under :NSFDC:Financial Year	Amount Disbursed (Rs. in crore)	Beneficiaries Covered (Numbers)2014-15	270.27	70,8852015-16	378.94	71,9152016-17	478.98	82,1052017-18	600.88	1,08,3402018-19	671.21	81,431TOTAL	2400.283,14,677VCF-SC:Financial Year	Amount Disbursed(Rs. in crore)	Beneficiaries Covered (Numbers)2014-15	1.00	12015-16	69.15	152016-17	45.12	182017-18	53.46	182018-19	33.86	11TOTAL	202.59	63(c): Not applicable in view of the reply given above.****
