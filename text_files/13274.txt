                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

185






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Baramulla-Kupwara Rail Link




Mohammad Akbar Lone














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal/received any request for the extension of rail link from Baramulla to Kupwara and Kupwara to Lalab;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the present status of the extension of these rail links?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY		(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	to (c)  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 185 BY SHRI MOHAMMAD AKBAR LONE TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 04.03.2020 REGARDING BARAMULLA-KUPWARA RAIL LINK.      (a)	to (c) Yes, Sir.  Survey of new Broad Gauge Railway line between Baramulla and Kupwara (39 km) was completed in 2010-11.  The estimated cost of the project at price level of 2010-11 was  628 crore with Rate of Return (ROR) of (-) 7.48%.   The project could not be taken forward after survey due to financially unviability. In November, 2018, survey of the projected line had been sanctioned to assess the cost at the current level and same was undertaken. Further, consideration of this proposal shall be possible once survey report becomes available and results thereon are firmed up.No survey for extension of rail link from Kupwara to Lalab has been completed /under consideration, at present.*****
