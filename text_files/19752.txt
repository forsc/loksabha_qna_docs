                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Damage to Kharif Crops




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is heavy damage of kharif crops due to heavy rain and flood in different parts of the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government has any proposal to wave off the loans taken by farmers of the affected districts in the country; and(d)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	As per the information provided by the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), State-wise details of crop area affected due to flood/landslide during 2020, as reported by the State Governments, are given at Annexure.(c): No, Sir.(d): Does not arise.
