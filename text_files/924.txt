                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

874






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






SMS Service for Passengers




Lallu Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to formulate a plan for providing information to the passengers through SMS regarding delay in operation of trains like Rajdhani and Shatabdi; (b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which the said facility is likely to be made operational in the trains plying to Bihar ; (c) whether the passengers have to pay any charges for availing this facility; and(d) if so, the details and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b) : At present, in case a train gets delayed by more than an hour, then Short Message Service (SMS) alerts are sent to the passengers boarding the train from the next/upcoming station. This facility which was initially introduced in Rajdhani and Shatabdi type of trains has since been extended to cover other Mail/Express trains also. The SMS is sent to the passengers who furnish their mobile number at the time of booking Railway tickets.                      (c) and (d) :  At present, no charges are payable by passengers for receiving these SMS alerts.*****
