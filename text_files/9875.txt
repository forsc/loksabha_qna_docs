                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Swayam Portal




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of courses and various disciplines enlisted in the Swayam Portal;(b) whether there is a huge difference in the number of students registering for the coursesand number of students completing the exam through Swayam Portal;(c) whether the 32 free Educational Channels been launched under Swayam Prabha Scheme;(d) whether the students are not aware of this scheme; and(e) if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken by the Government to promote thescheme?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b) The total number of Courses run on the SWAYAM portal till date is 2867. The disciplinescovered include (i) Annual Refresher Programme in Teaching (ARPIT), (ii) Architecture andPlanning, (iii) Education, (iv) Engineering and Technology, (v) Humanities & Arts, (vi) Law, (vii)Management & Commerce, (viii) Maths & Sciences and (ix) School Education.There would always be a difference in the number of students registering for the course andnumber of students taking the examination. Some learners do not complete the course due tovarious personal reasons. Also the registration is free in SWAYAM.(c) to (e) Yes, MHRD, Government of India is running 32 free SWAYAM Prabha educationalchannels which are accessible through DD Free Dish. This was formally launched by Hon’blePresident of India on 09.07.2017 during the National Convention on Digital Initiative. Workshopsare regularly organized in the Educational Institutions across the country by MHRD, UGC andAICTE to create awareness.***
