                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1351






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Funds Allocation for Girls Hostel




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the funds allocated for Prime Minister’s girls’ hostel has been reduced from Rs. 30crore to Rs. 10 crore last year;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b): No Sir. There is no scheme called Prime Ministers’ Girls Hostel under the Department ofSchool Education and Literacy. However, under this Department, till the year 2017-18, GirlsHostel Component of erstwhile Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA) provided hostelfacility for girl students in the age group of 14-18 years, studying in classes IX to XII andbelonging to SC, ST, OBC, Minority communities and Below Poverty Line families and KasturbaGandhi Balika Vidyalayas (KGBVs) were residential schools at upper primary level for girlsbelonging to disadvantaged groups such as SC, ST, OBC, Minority and BPL under Sarva ShikshaAbhiyan (SSA).Under the Samagra Shiksha, effective from 2018-19, the existing KGBVs at upperprimary level and Girls Hostels at secondary level have been extended/converged to provideresidential and schooling facilities at upper primary level up to Senior Secondary level. Thescheme envisages to provide access and quality education to girls from disadvantaged groups bysetting up residential schools from upper primary to senior secondary level and to ensure smoothtransition of girls from elementary to secondary and up to class XII wherever possible.Funds approved for the KGBVs and Girls Hostels are as under:<pre>(Rs. in crore)2017-18 2018-19KGBVsunder SSAGirls Hostelsunder RMSAKGBVs underSamagra Shiksha1855.48341 467.1126 3269.86748</pre>(c) : Does not arise.*****
