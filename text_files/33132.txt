                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Non-utilisation of Welfare Funds




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the amount allocated and released under the welfare of working children in need of care and protection during the last three years has not been utilized by some of the state Governments;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and (c)	the action taken by the Union Government against such States? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a)  to (c) :  Funds under the Child Protection Services Scheme are released according to the requirements and demands made by the respective States/UTs.  The State-wise details of the funds released and utilized under the Child Protection Services Scheme during the last three year period is at Annexure-I.
