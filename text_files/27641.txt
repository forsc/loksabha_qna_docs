                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3444






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Providing Jobs on Compasssionate Ground




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has any data with regard to the deaths of their staff during their serviceperiod/on duty and if so, details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) the number of jobs given to the kins/dependents on compassionate ground in each of the lastfive years, State/UT-wise; and(c) the number of cases pending with the Ministry with regard to giving jobs on compassionateground to the dependents of the deceased staff who died during their service period and the actiontaken by the Government to resolve these pending cases?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Yes, Sir. The details of deceased staff of the main Secretariat of the Ministry of Educationwho died during their service period / on duty for the last 5 years is given at Annexure.(b) Four dependents of deceased government servants have been given jobs on compassionategrounds during the last five years.(c) The appointment on compassionate grounds is governed by Department of Personnel andTraining’s O.M. No.14014/02/2012-Estt (D) dated 16.01.2013 whereby such appointments canbe made up to a maximum of 5% of vacancies falling under direct recruitment quota in anyGroup ‘C’ posts in the main Secretariat of the Ministry. The cases are considered by a ScreeningCommittee duly constituted for the purpose and the eligibility of the applicants is evaluated onthe basis of a point-based evaluation system to determine the status of financial destitution of theapplicants’ family. The applicant with the highest score in the point scale under the zone ofconsideration is recommended by the Screening Committee and approved for appointment by thecompetent authority. The last meeting of the Screening Committee was held on 18.03.2021.*****
