                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1613






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Stadiums functioning under SAI




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of sports stadia/stadiumsfunctioning under Sports Authority of India (SAI) acrossthe country as on date, State-wise(b) whether the Union Government has any proposalto privatise these or some sports stadia/stadiums;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the reasonstherefor;(d) whether the Union Government is in the processof setting up of new sports stadium in the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a)	The following Stadia, which are all located in Delhi, are being maintained and utilized by Sports Authority of India (SAI) :-1.  Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium Complex2.  Indira Gandhi Sports Complex3. Dr.Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Swimming Pool Complex 4.  Major Dhyan Chand National Stadium 5. Dr.Karni Singh Shooting Ranges (b)	There is no proposal under consideration in this Ministry to privatise any of these Stadia. (c)	Does not arise.(d)	A proposalfor creation of Sports Complex in Naranpura, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, at a total estimated cost of Rs.458.65 crore, is under consideration in this Ministry.(e) 	Does not arise.*****
