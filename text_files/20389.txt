                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1753






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Piped Drinking Water to Villages




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of villages in Uttar Pradesh having piped drinking water facility;(b)	the number of villages covered/ to be covered under such scheme during the current financial year to get piped drinking water; and(c)	the time by which all the villages are likely to be covered there under?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	As on 08.02.2021, out of 97,494 villages in the State of Uttar Pradesh, 17,781villages are reported to have piped drinking water supply.(b)	As reported, every household in 2,011 villages in Uttar Pradesh are getting tap water supply and 12,920 villages are planned to be provided with piped water supply during 2020-21.(c)	As reported, all villages in Uttar Pradesh are likely to be provided with tap water supply by 2024.****
