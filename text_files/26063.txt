                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1736






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






National Programme for Development of Sports




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has started a National Programme for Development of Sports branded as ‘Khelo India‘ so that girls and boys in large number may participate in sports;(b) the funds allocated/released for Rajasthan under the said programme;(c) whether the Government proposes to set up regional centres in Jalore and Sirohi districts in Rajasthan for implementation of the said sport promotional schemes;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) Yes, Sir. The ‘Khelo India – National Programme for Development of Sports’ was launched in 2016 with the aim to strengthen the entire sports ecosystem to promote the twin objectives of mass participation and promotion of excellence in sports across the country.(b) Funds are allocated by the Union Government Scheme-wise, not State/UT-wise. However, funds released for development of sports in Rajasthan under Khelo India Scheme during the last five years are as under:- (Amount in ? crore)<pre>S. No.	Financial Year	Funds Released1	2016-17	38.902	2017-18	62.053	2018-19	14.874	2019-20	69.265	2020-21	36.05</pre>(c) to (e) The Union Government, through Sports Authority of India (SAI), has established 03 SAI Training Centres (STCs), 07 Extension Centres of STCs, 09 Khelo India Centres and has adopted 08 Akharas in the State of Rajasthan. There is currently no proposal to set up regional centres in Jalore and Sirohi districts. ****
