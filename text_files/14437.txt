                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3089






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Toilets Constructed under SBM




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of toilets constructed during the last two years and the current year under Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM)-Gramin, State/UT-wise;(b) the percentage of the toilets which are fully functional and attached to sewage lines; and(c) whether the usage of toilets has been increased in rural areas during the said period and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	State/UT-wise number of individual household latrines(IHHLs) constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM(G)] during the last two years and the current year are given at Annexure.(b) 	As per National Annual Rural Sanitation Survey (NARSS) conducted during 2018-19 through an independent verification agency under World Bank support to SBM(G), 98.6% toilets were functional.  Under SBM(G), Government promotes construction of twin-leach pit toilets. Under this technology, human faeces automatically becomes manure in 1-2 years and no sewage system is required.(c) 	Yes, sir.  As per the report of NARSS 2018-19, toilet usage has increased to 96%.
