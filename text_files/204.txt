                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

20






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Health Melas under NRHM




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has released funds for organizing Health Melas in every Parliamentary Constituency in the country under the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM); (b) if so, the details of the amount released in this regard, State-wise; (c) the details of funds utilized for organizing health Melas so far in State/ District/Constituency-wise particularly in Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh; (d) whether any directives have been issued by the Government to the States which have not utilized the said funds; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (e):  A statement is laid on the Table of the HouseSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 20* FOR 21ST JUNE, 2019(a) and (b): The Government approves activity of organising Health Melas in Parliamentary Constituencies based on proposals received from the State Government and normally releases lump sum funds for activities approved under the NHM including for Health Melas, wherever approved.           In addition, during the F.Y. 2018-19 in February 2019 specific funds have been given to States for organising Health Melas in Parliamentary Constituencies under the National Health Mission (NHM). Details  at Annexure-I.  (c): The details of funds utilised under NHM during 2018-19, as reported by the States under the activity of Targeting Naturally Occurring Gathering of People/ Health Mela is at Annexure-II. (d) and (e): The Government regularly reviews the expenditure on approved activities including Health Melas through regular correspondence, video conferences and meetings with the State Governments officials so as to improve the utilisation of funds.
