                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






TV Relay Centres




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of TV relay centres established and functioning at present in the country, particularly in backward districts, location-wise; (b) 	the expenses incurred on functioning of these relay centres during each of the last three years; (c) 	the number of relay centres not working properly/ effectively and the number of such relay centres located in tribal areas; and (d) 	the details of the steps taken by the Government to improve their functioning?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) : Prasar Bharati has informed that presently, there are 589 TV transmitters of varying capacity located in various parts of the country, including in the backward districts. List of transmitters located in 115 Aspirational districts is enclosed as Annexure.(b) : The expenditure incurred on T.V. Relay centres throughout the country for last three years and current year is as given below:-Year	Expenditure(Rs. in Crore)2017-18	403.582018-19	501.572019-20	417.102020-21 (upto Jan. 21)	341.71(c) & (d) : Maintenance and upkeep of Prasar Bharati’s infrastructure is an ongoing process and technical issues related to transmitters are addressed from time to time by Prasar Bharati. At the same time, Prasar Bharati is also providing a bouquet of 161 TV channels including all DD channels with no monthly subscription fees through DD Free Dish, Direct to Home (DTH) service. In addition to this, Prasar Bharati is providing its DD channels digitally through internet and also on NewsOnAir app on “Android” and “iOS”  platform for mobile users. *****
