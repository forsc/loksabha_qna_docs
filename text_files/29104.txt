                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

541






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Loss of Food Grains




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry maintains any record of the loss of food grains due to problems with the storage and transportation infrastructure; (b) 	if so, the details of the amount of food grains lost due to these reasons in the last five years; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	whether any measures are being taken to rectify these problems and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b): 	Yes Sir. The details on the quantity of foodgrains, which is accrued as non-issuable in Food Corporation of India (FCI) during transportation in last five years is as under:                                                                     (Fig. in Lakh tonnes)Year	Quantity of foodgrains transported	Quantity of transit damage accrued2016-17	410	0.00182017-18	420	0.00262018-19	383	0.00272019-20	389	0.00102020-21	594	0.0008Moreover, no damage of central pool foodgrains occurred in FCI during the last five years due to lack of storage infrastructure.  (c): 	Does not arise in view of (a) above.(d): 	Steps taken by the Government of India to prevent losses of foodgrains during transportation:?	Covered rail wagons free from roof leakage are used for movement of foodgrains so as to avoid damage during transit. ?	Low density polyethylene (LDPE) sheet is spread over the floor of wagons to avoid contamination of foodgrains with extraneous matter.?	LDPE sheet/tarpaulins are placed on door flaps of wagons to prevent the foodgrains to get affected from rain water during transportation. ?	Tarpaulins sheet are placed at platforms at the time of loading & unloading of wagons/trucks to collect the spillage to avoid wastage of foodgrains.*****
