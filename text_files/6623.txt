                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Culture Fishing and Fishing Assistance




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps taken to certify fishermen under Recognition of Prior Learning Scheme;(b)	the number of trainees enrolled by Agriculture Skill Council of India for the segments of ‘Culture Fishing’ and ‘Fishing Assistance’ in the country including Andhra Pradesh;(c)	the placement statistics of such trainees;(d)	whether such training in the fishery segment also includes the training of disaster management; and (e)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a): 	Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) for people engaged in Fishery Sector is being undertaken under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) and State funded skill programmes.(b): 	The total No. of trainees enrolled for RPL by various affiliated Training Providers is as under;Segment	EnrolledCulture Fishing ( Aquaculture Worker, Shrimp Farmer and Hatchery Production Worker)	4,445Capture Fishing (Inland capture fisherman cum primary processor, Marine capture fisherman cum primary processor)	15,816Fishing Assistance	RPL  yet to be taken upTotal	20,261(c): 	The trainee enrolled in Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) Programme are already engaged in their respective occupations and no fresh placement is undertaken for them.(d) and (e):	No Sir.  The Qualification Packs developed cover the skills required for an individual to perform a specific task.  Disaster Management capsule is not included in the Qualification Packs currently in use.****
