                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

987






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Improvement of Dairy Sector




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    S. Muniswamy
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated any scheme to improve the condition of the Dairy Sector including co-operative milk federations;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the amount of funds earmarked, released, and utilized under such schemes during the last three years, year-wise and State-wise, including the State ofKarnataka;and(d)	the achievements made under these schemes during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRIPARSHOTTAMRUPALA)(a)	The Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing following schemes for Dairy Development across the country:1)	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)2)	Dairy processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF)3)	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities (SDCFPO)(b)	The details of the schemes are at Annexure-I.(c)	Since the schemes are demand driven,the state -wise allocation is not made. The State-wise, Year-wise details of the schemes of funds released, utilized under during the last three years, including the State of Karnataka for NPDD, DIDF and SDCFPO are at Annexure-II, Annexure-III and Annexure IV.(d)	The achievement made under NPDD, DIDFand SDCFPO is at Annexure-V.
