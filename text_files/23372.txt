                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4538






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Agro Machinery Manufacturing




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government is having a long term plan to develop Agro Machinery Manufacturing in a big way in the country; and(b)	  if so, the details of the program of action in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	ICAR’s Central Institute of Agricultural Engineering (CIAE), Bhopal and the centres of All India Coordinated Research Projects (AICRPs)/ Consortia Research Platforms (CRPs) located in different parts of the country are engaged in research and development of farm machinery in India. The CIAE organizes interaction meets with agricultural machinery manufacturers and efforts are made by the institute to promote agricultural machinery manufacturing through R&D, multiplication of research prototypes, capacity building, testing of commercial prototypes, confidential testing of their new product, etc. This ensures increased capabilities, improved quality of product and improvement in production line.  *****
