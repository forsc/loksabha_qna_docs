                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1375






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Drop-out Rates in IITs & IIMs




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the drop-out rates are being reported heavily in IITs, IIMs and other higher education level courses;(b) if so, the details thereof, Course-wise/State-wise during the last five years and the current year; and(c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to correct the situation in future?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) No, Sir(b) In view of the (a) above, the question does not arise. However, details of dropout from some of the Higher Education Institutions is as under<pre>Percentage of dropout in some institutesYear	IIT	IIM	Other Institutions2015-16	2.25	1.04	7.492016-17	1.60	1.06	8.562017-18	1.71	1.03	6.762018-19	1.46	0.50	5.362019-20	0.68	0.78	2.82</pre>(c) As can be seen from above, there has been decrease trend in dropout rate over the last 5 years. This has been achieved by taking a number of corrective measures to minimise the dropout which include appointment of advisor to monitor the academic progress of students and peer assisted learning.***
