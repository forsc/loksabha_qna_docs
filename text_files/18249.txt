                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1973






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Slum Population




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the details of total number of people residing in slum clusters in metro cities is available;(b)	 if so, the details thereof during the last five years, city and year-wise; (c)	the total number of people living in slum clusters in metro cities resettled in permanent houses during last five years under different schemes of the Government; and(d)	 the details of the number of such beneficiaries during last five years, city and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b): Yes, Sir.The Census is conducted on decadal basis in India. The details of number of people residing in slum clusters in metro cities as per Census 2011 is given below: S.
