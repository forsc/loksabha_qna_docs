                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Indian Institute of Information Technology




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) isoperating from Transit Campus at Hubli in Karnataka;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of funds allocated and released towards the institute during the last three years;(d) whether the funds released is sufficient for establishment and operationalisation of permanentcampus; and(e) if not, the Government’s reaction thereto and the time by which the said IIIT is likely to shift to newcampus?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTRY OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’)(a): Yes Sir.(b): IIIT Dharwad was established in April, 2015 under the Scheme of “Setting up of 20 new IndianInstitute of Information Technology (IIITs) in Public Private Partnership model” approved by theUnion Cabinet on 26.11.2010. It is currently operating from its transit campus at Dharwad, Hubli,Karnataka.(c): The details of funds released during the last 3 years is as under:<pre>Year Funds allocated Funds ReleasedCapital Revenue Total Capital Revenue Total2016-17 4.00 2.00 6.00 4.00 2.00 6.002017-18 0.50 2.50 3.00 0.50 2.50 3.002018-19 2.00 0.00 2.00 2.00 0.00 2.00Total 6.50 4.50 11.00 6.50 4.50 11.00</pre>(d): Yes Sir. IIIT Dharwad has prepared the Detailed Project Report (DPR) for construction ofpermanent campus at a cost of Rs. 114 crore. A sum of Rs. 28.66 crore has been released so far,including Rs.17.66 crore in 2019-20, by the Govt. of India to the IIIT Dharwad for constructionpurpose. The Govt of Karnataka has also released Rs. 20 crore to the Institute. Thus, the funds arereleased to the Institute in accordance with the progress of construction work.(e): As per the timeline of the DPR, the date of completion of construction is July, 2020.****
