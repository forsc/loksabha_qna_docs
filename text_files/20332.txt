                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1813






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Central Aid to Minority Organisations




Kalyan Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether it is true that different minority organisations are not getting their Central aid in time; (b)	 if so, the detail of allotment and disbursement of funds since 2017 therefor, head/ State-wise; (c)	whether the Union Government carry forward the funds from previous budgets and if so, the reasons therefor; and (d)	the reasons  for lower allocation of budget for minority schemes as compared to previous five years average?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SH. MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)  No, Sir (b) Question does not arise. (c)  There is no provision of carrying forward of the funds from last consecutive budgets in the Ministry.(d) The allocation of budget to the Ministry of Minority Affairs since 2016-17 is given below.  There has been no constraint in the implementation of the schemes/programmes in the last five years of the Ministry owing to budget. The Budget Estimates have been increasing over the years as reflected in the table below:-        (Rs. in crore)Year	Budget Estimates2016-17	3827.252017-18	4195.482018-19	4700.002019-20	4700.002020-21	5029.00******
