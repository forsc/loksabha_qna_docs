                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

789






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Conservation of Cultural Heritage and Vedic Tradition




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken till date or  proposes to take any steps to conserve the culture heritage and Vedic tradition of the country  which has  taught the lesson of Guru Bhakti, brotherhood and service of parents to the whole world;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a & b)   Yes Sir.   Indira Gandhi National Centre for the Arts (IGNCA) , an autonomous body  under the Ministry of Culture through its Vedic Heritage Portal  has taken steps to document and conserve the Vedic traditions of the country. The portal is in the public domain  and  can be accessed at www.vedicheritage.gov.in ( it registers monthly hit of approximately 6 lakhs).  This portal  is a vast resource of  textual details of Vedic Samhitas, Brahmanas, Aranayakas, Upanishad,  Vedangas, vedic rituals on Shrauta Sutras in audiovisual format, lists of vedic institutions and eminent scholars(c ) 	Does not arise.****
