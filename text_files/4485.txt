                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Schemes for Minorities




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total annual allocation made for the Ministry during the last three years;(b) 	the details of schemes taken up for minorities in Assam during the above period; (c) 	whether there is any State level advisory body including Members of Parliament for this purpose; (d) 	if not, whether the Government is considering/would consider about this and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the funds allocated to Assam during the said period from the Maulana Azad Foundation and Central  Wakf  Council? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) to (e): A statement is laid on the table of the house.*****
