                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3111






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Low cost Regional Flights




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to start low cost regional flights in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and the total number of commercially operational low cost airports in the country;(c) the details of the new airports and proposal to start flights therefrom;(d) whether the Government has also proposed to start ?Udan Package? on the lines of rail tourism in the country, if so, the details thereof; (e) whether the Government has any proposal to start air services from Manpur airport, Abu road under the scheme; and(f) if so, the time by which air services are likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Ministry of Civil Aviation has launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) on 21-10-2016 to stimulate regional air connectivity and making air travel affordable to the masses. RCS - UDAN is market driven scheme. The interested airlines based on their assessment of demand on particular routes submit their proposals at the time of bidding under RCS - UDAN from time to time. Selected Airline Operators (SAOs) have operationalized 266 RCS routes involving 48 RCS Airports, including 3 Helipads so far. Details of the operationalized 266 RCS routes are attached as Annexure-1.;;(d): AAI, which is the Implementing Agency has awarded 46 Tourism Routes under third round of bidding under RCS - UDAN. Details of the Tourism routes awarded are attached as Annexure-2.;;(e) and (f): Manpur (Abu Road) Airport figures in the tentative list of unserved Airports of RCS - UDAN document. However, no Airline bidder has submitted bids till completion of the third round of bidding.
