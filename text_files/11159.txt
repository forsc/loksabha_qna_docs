                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

72






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Setting up of New Petrol Pumps




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of petrol pumps in the country, State-wise with special reference to Telangana;(b) whether any assessment has been made to know the number of petrol pumps in the country, if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c) whether it is true that OMCs have decided to set up 65,000 petrol pumps in the country, if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether focus would be made to set up petrol pumps in rural areas and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) The details of State-wise number of Retail Outlets (ROs) of Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) including in Telangana State as on 01.01.2020 are at Annexure.(b) to (d) Expansion of ROs network by Public Sector OMCs in the country including in the Rural and Remote areas is a continuous process. ROs are set up by Public Sector OMCs at identified locations based on field survey and feasibility studies. Locations found to be having sufficient potential as well as economically viable are rostered in the Marketing Plans for advertisement for setting up ROs.Public Sector OMCs have issued advertisements for award of new Retail Outlet dealerships on 25.11.2018 and 14.12.2018 on pan India basis including in rural areas. Status of these advertisements as on 01.01.2020 are as below :-Location advertisedLOI issuedRO Commissioned79312183101815****
