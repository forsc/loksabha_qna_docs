                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

302






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Funds Allocated under PMMSY to Andhra Pradesh




Talari Rangaiah













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds allocated under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Yojana (PMMSY) to the State of Andhra Pradesh during the last two years; (b)	whether the Government proposes to set up Integrated Modern Coastal Fishing Villages in the State of Andhra Pradesh in the current financial year under this scheme; and (c) 		if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(DR. L. MURUGAN)(a) to (c):  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) - A scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India with an investment of Rs.20050 crore for a period of 5 years with effect from the financial year 2020-21 for development of fisheries in all States/Union Territories including Andhra Pradesh. During the financial year 2020-21, the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has accorded approval to the proposal of Government of Andhra Pradesh at a total cost of Rs.275.60 crore with central share of Rs.79.566 crore under the PMMSY. The PMMSY inter-alia envisages set up Integrated Modern Coastal Fishing Villages in the marine States/UTs including in the State of Andhra Pradesh. The Government of Andhra Pradesh under its Annual Action Plan of 2021-22 has proposed for setting up three Integrated Modern Coastal Fishing Villages in the State.  *****
