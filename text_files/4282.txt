                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3959






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Electrification in Jharkhand




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the percentage and length of railway lines that have been electrified in Jharkhand; and(b)	the details of ongoing and upcoming electrification projects of railway lines in the districts of Hazaribagh and Ramgarh in Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) AND (b) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3959 BY SHRI JAYANT SINHA        TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 17.07.2019 REGARDING ELECTRIFICATION IN JHARKHAND(a)	As on 01.07.2019, in the State of Jharkhand out of 2,585 Route Kilometer (RKM) of Broad Gauge network, 1,925 RKM i.e. 74.46% have been electrified. (b)	Electrification work is in progress on Koderma-Hazaribagh-Barkakana-Ranchi (203 Route Kilometer) rail line which is passing through Hazaribagh and Ramgarh districts of the State of Jharkhand.  Koderma-Barkakana (136 Route Kilometer) has been commissioned on electric traction. Balance portion i.e. Barakakana-Ranchi (67 Route Kilometers) New line work is in progress. Electrification will also be completed  along with new line.*****
