                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4236






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Implementation of SIPDA




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether research on disability related technology, products and issues under the Scheme for Implementation of Rights of People with Disability Activities (SIPDA)  is still continued;(b)	if so, the details of steps taken to promote research in the application of science and technology for the development of appropriate indigenous aids and appliances during each of the last three years and the current year; and(c)	the funds released to various Project Implementing Agencies (PIA)/Non-Government Organisations (NGO)/Government Organizations as well as activities undertaken by said recipients along with the number of projects funded and completed during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a)	A sub-scheme namely, “Financial support to Study and Research on priority areas of disability sector and for Research & Development of suitable products, aids & appliances for empowerment of  Persons with Disabilities” is approved for implementation as part of the Scheme for Implementation of Rights of Persons with Disability Act (SIPDA) over the next five years i.e. from 2021-22 to 2025-26.(b) & (c) During the last three years and the current year, 10 Research projects have been financially supported under Research Scheme being implemented by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities as per list enclosed at Annexure, out of which, 08 Research projects have been completed.
