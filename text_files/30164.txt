                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1825






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Subsidy to Sugar




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any plans to withdraw subsidy given to the sugar exporters; and (b) 	if so, the details and the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b): 	Due to favourable price of sugar in the international market and manageable stocks mainly attributed to diversion of sugar to ethanol and export of around 70 LMT of sugar during the previous sugar season 2020-21 (October- September), no subsidy on export of sugar is being presently  extended by Government of India for current sugar season 2021-22.****
