                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3048






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






National Eligibility-cum Entrance Test




Lallu Singh














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to conduct National Eligibility cum Entrance Test twice a year;(b)	whether the Government has received any memorandum in this regard;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the decision likely to be taken by the Government in this regard along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): National Testing Agency (NTA) under the Ministry of Education conducts Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) for undergraduate programme in medical sciences, in consultation with Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. In 2021, the NEET (UG) will be conducted by NTA only one time. (b) to (d): NTA has informed that they have not received any memorandum in this regard.*****
