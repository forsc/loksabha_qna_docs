                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






NCERT Books




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the NCERT books have been printed through empanelled private publishers;(b) if so, the criteria adopted for appointment of publisher of NCERT Books;(c) whether the Government is providing papers through selected paper mills to thesepublishers for printing of NCERT Books;(d) if so, the details of papers allotted to each publisher for printing of NCERT Books duringeach of the last three years along with the expenditure incurred thereon;(e) whether the Government has received complaints for using poor quality papers for printingof NCERT Books by the empanelled publishers/printers;(f) if so, whether the Government has conducted any audit of empanelled publishers forutilization of allotted papers; and(g) if so, the outcome thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Yes, Sir.(b) A statement indicating the qualification criteria for empanelment on the panel of NationalCouncil of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) offset printers is annexed asAnnexure-I.(c) Yes, Sir.(d) A statement indicating the details of the paper allotted to publishers for printing ofNCERT Books during the last three years along with the expenditure incurred thereon is annexedas Annexure-II.(e) to (g) The paper for printing of NCERT textbooks is centrally procured by NCERTfrom the mills and supplied to the empanelled printers as per print order. Every year, the paper isgot tested from the government inspecting agency. In the year 2017-18, a few complaints werereceived from individuals regarding poor quality of printing and paper. Therefore, the quality ofpaper was got tested from the government labs. On the basis of lab reports, action was takenagainst two mills.*****
