                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2124






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Open Parking on Roads




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the width of roads has reduced by 15 to 60 percent due to open parking 	owing to which issue of accidents related to pedestrians is becoming a matter of grave 	concern;(b)	if so, the plan formulated by the Government to tackle this situation;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to bring a comprehensive national policy regarding parking; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) No such information is available with the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways.(b) to (d) As per section 138 (e) of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, State Governments have been empowered for the maintenance and management of parking places and stands and the fees, if any, which may be charged for their use. Further, as per section 201 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 penalty may be imposed for causing obstruction to free flow of traffic. *****
