                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Promotion of AYUSH




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA,YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the details of the schemes being implemented/ lying pending for promotion of Unani, Homoeopathy, Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy (AYUSH) and the amount of funds allocated/utilized for the said purpose during the last three years;(b) whether the Government proposes to allot any special funds for promotion of the said systems of medicine at several health centres in the country, State/ UT-wise and if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of Naturopathy and Yoga Hospitals empanelled under Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) for Yoga & Naturopathy treatments/procedures in Maharashtra along with Package rates fixed therefor;(d) the details of the Government hospitals in the country where Ayurvedic treatment facilities are available, State/UT-wise during the last three years; and(e) whether adequate publicity has been given through vernacular newspapers about the availability of such facilities in the country, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a): Ministry of AYUSH is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) through States/UTs for the Development and promotion of AYUSH (Ayurveda, Unani, Homoeopathy, Siddha, Yoga and Naturopathy) system. The National AYUSH Mission (NAM) inter-alia makes provision for the following activities:-(i)	Co-location of AYUSH facilities at Primary Health Centers (PHCs), Community Health Centers (CHCs) and District Hospitals (DHs).(ii)	Up gradation of exclusive State Government AYUSH Hospitals and Dispensaries.(iii)	Setting up of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals.(iv)	Upgradation of State Government AYUSH Educational Institutions.(v)	Setting up of new State Government AYUSH Educational Institutions in the State where it is not available in Government Sector.(vi)	Strengthening of State Government/ Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy (ASU&H) Pharmacies and Drug Testing Laboratories (DTL).(vii)	Cultivation and Promotion of Medicinal Plants. (viii)	Upgradation of Health facilities (AYUSH Dispensaries and Health sub-centers) into AYUSH Health and Wellness Centre under the Ayushman Bharat.Under NAM, during last three years from 2017-18 to 2019-20, Ministry of AYUSH has released Rs. 1440.476 crore to different State /UT Governments for the development and promotion of AYUSH systems of medicines.The Ministry of AYUSH also runs a scheme for propagation of AYUSH system.  The scheme, namely, Information, Education and Communication (IEC) provides financial assistance to various organisations involved in AYUSH related activities for conducting propogational programmes.  These programmes include National Arogya Fair, State Arogya Fair, Exhibitions, Seminars, health melas. The virtual medium including social media platforms are also extensively used for propogational activities. The details of the funds allocated under IEC scheme during last three years are as under:                                           (Rs.in Crore)Year	BE	RE	AE2017-18	34	37.1	31.072018-19	34	64	63.492019-20	52.6	44.5	43.34(b): 	There is no such proposal to allot any special funds for promotion of the said systems of medicine at health centres in the country.(c):	 Presently, there is no Naturopathy and Yoga Hospitals empanelled under CGHS for Yoga & Naturopathy treatments/ procedures in Maharashtra. However, the package rates applicable for Yoga & Naturopathy are available on CGHS website at https://cghs.gov.in/showfile. php?lid=4845. (d): The following national institutes under the Ministry of AYUSH have attached hospitals which provide quality healthcare services in Ayurveda System of Medicine:-i) National Institute of Ayurveda, Jaipur, Rajasthanii) All India Institute of Ayurveda, New Delhiiii) Institute of Teaching and Research in Ayurveda, Jamnagar, Gujarativ) North Eastern Institute of Ayurveda and Homoeopathy, Shillong, Meghalaya(e): 	During FY 2020-21, publicity has been given through vernacular newspapers with regard to granting Institute of National Importance (INI) status to Institute for Training and Research in Ayurveda (ITRA), Jamnagar and the deemed to be University status (de-novo category) to National institute of Ayurveda (NIA), Jaipur having facilities for Ayurvedic treatment.
