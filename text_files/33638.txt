                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1060






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Financial Grant to North East States




Tokheho Yepthomi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of financial grant that has been provided to North East States under Disaster Management during 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021- 22; and(b) the details of the amount for each State during the said period,year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	The primary responsibility for disaster management rests with the State Governments concerned. The State Governments undertake relief measures in the wake of natural disasters, from the State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF), already placed at their disposal, in accordance with Government of India’s approved items and norms. Additional financial assistance is provided from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF), as per laid down procedure in case of disaster of ‘severe nature’, which includes an assessment based on the visit of an Inter-Ministerial Central Team (IMCT). The detail of state-wise allocation and release under SDRF and NDRF to North East States for the year 2018-19 to 2021-22 is at annexure.*******
