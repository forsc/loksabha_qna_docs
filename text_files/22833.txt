                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3842






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Bids for Toll-Operate-Transfer Auction




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has invited financial bids for tranche five of Toll-Operate Transfer (TOT) auction; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the total number of bids received by the Government/ NHAI for TOT auction, company-wise; (d) the action taken by the Government thereon; and (e) the bid amount fixed by the Government and total revenue expected through this auction?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS   (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b) Bids for tranche five of Toll-Operate Transfer(TOT) i.e., TOT Bundle 5(A-1) and TOT Bundle 5(A-2) in the state of Gujarat [Palanpur-Radhanpur Samakhiyali Section of NH-27 (Km  589.6 to Km 430)] were invited by NHAI on 24.09.2020.(c)  6 bids for each bundle were received by NHAI from the following bidders:1.	M/s Adani Road Transport Limited – Skylark Infra Engineering Pvt. Ltd.2.	M/s Cube Highways and Infrastructure III Pte Ltd.3.	M/s D.P. Jain & Co. Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd.4.	M/s Indian Highway Concessions Trust5.	M/s IRB Infrastructure Developers Ltd.6.	M/s Prakash Asphaltings and Toll Highways India Ltd(d) to (e) Initial Estimated Concession Value (IECV) or reserve price determined for TOT Bundle 5(A-1) and TOT Bundle 5 (A-2) is Rs.800 crore and Rs.821 crore respectively.*****
