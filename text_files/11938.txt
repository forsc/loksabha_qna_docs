                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

792






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Extension of NHs in Uttar Pradesh




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to extend National Highways (NHs) in Uttar Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether certain roads in Uttar Pradesh have been declared as NHs and construction thereof has been started during the current financial year;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the time by which construction of these highways is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	Central Government notifies certain State Roads as new National Highways (NHs) from time to time based on requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority and availability of resources.  	 (c) to (e)	3971 km of State Roads have been declared as new NHs in the State of Uttar Pradesh since May, 2014. Development and maintenance of National Highways (NHs) is a continuous process. Works on NHs are accordingly taken up as per inter-se-priority, traffic density and available resources. 55 numbers of widening/improvement works on various NHs in Uttar Pradesh costing Rs. 46865 crore are in various stages of implementation with completion period of 2-3 years.   *****
