                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2002






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Greenfield Airport in Kerela




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received a proposal from the State Government of Kerala for setting up of a new greenfield airport in Kottayam district in Kerala;(b) if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c) the details of the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (d) whether the Government has conducted any technical, economical and environment impact assessment study in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (d): Yes, Sir. Government of India has received a proposal from M/s. Kerala State Industrial Development Corporation (KSIDC) for grant of ''site clearance'' for setting up of Greenfield Airport at Kottayam. The proposal was examined in Ministry of Civil Aviation (MoCA) in consultation with Ministry of Defence, Airports Authority of India (AAI) and Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). Subsequently, KSIDC had submitted a Techno Economic Feasibility Study (TEFS) to MoCA. Based on the observations of AAI and DGCA, KSIDC has been requested to furnish updated/final TEFS report.
