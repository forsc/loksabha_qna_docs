                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1814






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Housing for All in Jharkhand




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the amount of funds released to Jharkhand for the vision of Housing for All; and(b)	the number of persons benefitted under the said scheme in Jharkhand as on date and the details thereof, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)&(b): So far,Rs. 1955.05 crore have been released to the State Government of Jharkhand for the vision of Housing for All.  A total of 2,12,822 houses have been sanctioned against the assessed demand of 2.49 lakh, out of this 1,51,624 houses have been grounded for construction and 86,247 houses have been completed and delivered to the beneficiaries of Jharkhand under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) Mission, so far. The district-wise details of persons benefitted under the scheme is at Annexure.                                     *****
