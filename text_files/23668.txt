                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4654






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Insurance Scheme for NRIs




Arvind Dharmapuri














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether there is a comprehensive insurance scheme for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) living in foreign countries, especially in the Gulf countries;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, whether the Government intends to formulate such insurance scheme for the benefit of NRIs and if so, the details thereof? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (c)	Pravasi Bharatiya Bima Yojana (PBBY) was launched in 2003 to provide insurance cover to Indian migrant workers. This has been amended from time to time with the overarching objective of expanding the benefits for emigrant workers, including ensuring expeditious settlement of claims.Presently, the PBBY Scheme is mandatory for all Emigration Check Required (ECR) category workers going to all ECR countries, including the Gulf countries. The scheme provides an insurance cover of ?10 Lakhs in case of accidental death or permanent disability and other benefits at a nominal insurance premium of  ?275 and ?375 for a period of two and three years respectively.***
