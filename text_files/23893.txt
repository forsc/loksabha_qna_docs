                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

429






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Construction of Houses under PMAY




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of urban houses which were projected to be 	constructed in the country, particularly in Shirdi Parliamentary 	Constituency in Maharashtra under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojna 	(PMAY) during each of the last three years, location-wise;(b)	the number of houses which have been contructed as on date and 	the number of those which are under construction/getting delayed 	along with their locations, particularly in the Shirdi Parliamentary 	Constituency; and(c)	the reasons behind the delay in the construction of such houses 	and the time by which these houses are expected to be 	completed?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                      (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	to (c) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 429to be answered on March 25, 2021regarding CONSTRUCTION OF HOUSES UNDER PMAY(a)&(b) Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) Mission, during the last three years, viz.,2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, about 78 lakh houses had been sanctioned in the country. The sanctioned houses include about 9.85 lakh for the State of Maharashtra including 5,039 houses in the Shirdi Parliamentary Constituency. The location wise details of houses sanctioned during the last three years i.e. 2017-20 and the details of houses sanctioned, completed, under construction in Shirdi Parliamentary Constituency under PMAY-U are  annexed as Annexure.(c)	The houses sanctioned under PMAY-U are targeted to be completed by the year 2022.                        ******
