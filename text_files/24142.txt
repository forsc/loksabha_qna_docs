                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






NCERT Syllabus




S. Venkatesan













                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the NCERT had conducted a meeting on syllabus with various SCERTs;(b) if so, whether the NCERT directed the SCERTs to submit the syllabus and NCERT proposes to give directions with regard to addition and deletions which the SCERTs are supposed to implement ; and(c) if so, the purpose and motive for conducting such meeting and give such arbitrary direction to the SCERTs functioning under various State Governments?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) has informed that they have not conducted any meeting on syllabus with various SCERTs.(b) & (c) No such direction has been given.****
