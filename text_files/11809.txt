                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

841






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Universities offering Courses in Civil Aviation




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of number of public and private institutes/universities offering courses in civil aviation, State-wise; (b) whether there is any plan to set up new civil aviation institute/university in Telangana and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has taken any step to make civil aviation courses/training economically affordable for all and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) whether there is any regulation to control the fee charged by private institutes/universities offering civil aviation courses and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION     (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) : Various Universities are offering courses in Civil Aviation. These include Rajiv Gandhi National Aviation University (RGNAU) in Uttar Pradesh and University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Uttarakhand.A list of DGCA Approved Flying Training Organizations as on 31.03.2019 is at Annexure I.A list of List of DGCA Approved Aircraft Maintenance Engineer (AME) Training Institutes under CAR Section 2 Series E Part VIII is at Annexure II(b) : At present, no plan has been finalized by the Government to set up new Civil Aviation institute/university in Telangana;(c) : In the past, trainer aircrafts were provided to some flying clubs through Aero Club of India. Land was made available to them on concessional rates by the State Governments.(d) : No, Sir. At present the fees charged by the private institutes/universities are not regulated by the Ministry of Civil Aviation.
