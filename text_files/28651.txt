                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

813






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Allocation/Utilization of Funds under PMAY-U




Arvind Dharmapuri














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds allocated and utilized under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY-U) during the last five years, State and district-wise including Telangana;(b)	the targets set for construction of houses under the scheme and the achievements made thereunder during the said period pertaining to the State;(c)	whether the Union Government is facing any difficulty in sanctioning and/or completing the construction of PMAY-U houses in the State; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken/being taken by the Government to accelerate the pace of construction of houses under the scheme in the State?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS              (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):	Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY-U), Central assistance of ?87596.84 crore has been allocated/ released for States/ Union Territories/ Central Nodal Agencies during the last five years against which ? 76,308.97 crore has been utilized. State/ UT wise and district-wise details including Telangana are given at Annexure.(b):	Under PMAY-U, Telangana has undertaken demand survey for assessing actual demand of housing and reported a demand of 2.25 lakh houses against which 2,26,166 houses have been sanctioned in the State as on 22.11.2021. During last five years ( FY 2016-17 to FY 2020-21 ), 1,84,913  houses have been sanctioned under PMAY-U in Telangana. (c) & (d):  The State of Telangana has grounded all the sanctioned houses under PMAY-U and completed 1,98,654 houses. The progress of scheme is reviewed regularly with States/ UTs through Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC) meetings, Video Conferences, field visits etc. All States/ Union Territories, including Telangana, have been advised to complete all the sanctioned projects/ houses within the stipulated timeline.                                      *****
