                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1064






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Development of Greenfield Ports




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Maddila  Gurumoorthy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of financial assistance provided for the development of the proposed four non-major greenfield ports of Bhavanapadu Port in Srikakulam district, Kakinada SEZ Port in East Godavari, Machilipatnam Port in Krishna, and Ramayapatnam Port in Prakasham district till date; and(b) the details of funds allocated for developing the Inland Waterways network in the State of Andhra Pradesh as on date? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) 	Under Sagarmala Programme, no financial assistance has been provided to Government of Andhra Pradesh for development of non-major Greenfield ports of Bhavanapadu Port in Srikakulam district, Kakinada SEZ Port in East Godavari, Machilipatnam Port in Krishna and Ramayapatnam Port in Prakasam District till date.
