                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3210






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Doordarshan/FM Transmission




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the population and areas in percentage of the country including Gujarat that have not been covered under Doordarshan network so far, if so, the reasons therefor along with the names of such areas, State-wise; (b) 	the measures that are being taken by the Government for coverage of Doordarshan network in that area; (c) 	whether the Government had made any effort to launch FM radio services in Dahod district of Gujarat and if so, the details of the efforts undertaken; (d) 	whether the Government had invited tender to launch FM radio services in Dahod district of Gujarat, if so, the details thereof; (e) 	whether the Government got any request for replacement of FM transmitter which was installed in Amreli district of Gujarat, if so, the details thereof; and (f) 	the time by which the FM transmitter that was installed is likely to be replaced with higher capacity transmitter?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) & (b):  Doordarshan provides multi-channel TV coverage to the entire country including Gujarat, through Doordarshan’s free-to-air DTH service ‘DD Free Dish’. Good quality DTH signals can be received anywhere in the country including Gujarat. (c) & (d):  A project for setting up of 100 W FM transmitter at 100 locations including Dahod has been approved. (e) & (f):  At Amreli, a 100 W FM transmitter is operational. At present, there is no proposal for its replacement with a higher power transmitter.	******
