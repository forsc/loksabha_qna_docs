                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

632






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Green Highways in Andhra Pradesh




 Chinta Anuradha













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to build Green Highways to increase connectivity in the State of Andhra Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the time frame set for completion of these projects?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)   The stretches of NH-516E from Koyyuru – Araku (length - 183.95 km) and from Bowdara – Vizianagaram (length - 26.94 km ) have been taken up for development under Green National Highways Corridor Project (GNHCP) scheme of the Ministry with the loan assistance of World Bank. The development will be done in six packages. The schedule date of completion is December, 2025. The package wise details of the stretches are as under:Package	Stretch/Chainage	Length (km)	Sanctioned Cost (Rs. in Cr) I	Koyyuru to Chaprathipalem(Km 120.000 to Km 165.500)	45.50	349.29II	Chaprathipalem to Lambasingi (Km 165.500 to Km 205.000)	39.50	311.22III	Lambasingi to Paderu(Km 205.000 to Km 253.430)	48.43	361.24IV	Paderu to Gondiguda(Km 2.400 to Km 27.480)	25.08	222.61V	Gondiguda to Araku (up to Bhallaguda)(Km 27.480 to Km 51.772)	24.29	296.66VI	Bowdara to Vizianagaram(Km 0.000 to Km 26.937)	26.94	127.91	Total	210.89	1668.93*****
