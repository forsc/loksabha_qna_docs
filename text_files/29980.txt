                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1663






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Auction of Coal Mines




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number and details of coal mines auctioned in the country so far; and (b)	the coal field-wise details of all the mines auctioned so far?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) & (b) : Total 62 coal mines have been auctioned so far in the country.  Details of 53 such coal mines stands allocated is at Annexure-I and details of 9 coal mines for which allocation has been terminated is at Annexure-II.
