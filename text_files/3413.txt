                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2975






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total funds allocated and utilized under the Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI); (b) 	the current number of projects/ programs that are being carried out through the SFURTI scheme and the number of artisans who have been benefited from the scheme, State/UT-wise; (c) 	the details of the projects and activities in the State of Kerala under the SFURTI, particularly in relation to replacement of equipment, setting up of common facilities, support for development of new products, designs, packaging, market promotion, capacity building, etc.; and (d)	 the total funds allocated to the Thiruvananthapuram Khadi Cluster and the number of artisans who have been benefited from it?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) was revamped in the year 2014.  Year-wise total funds allocated and utilized under SFURTI is as follows:                                                  (Rs. in crore)<pre>Year	Allocation (RE)	Utilization2014-15	2.00	02015-16	50.00	30.292016-17	75.00	66.802017-18	10.00	9.752018-19	86.03	86.032019-20*	125.00 (BE)	0.047</pre>                       *released up to 5.7.2019(b):	Currently 142 proposals have been approved under SFURTI.  State/UT-wise Nodal Agency-wise indicating District as well as amount approved under ‘SFURTI’ and number of artisans benefited is placed at Annexure-I.(c):	Five proposals have been approved in the State of Kerala under SFURTI. The amount of grant/support is for construction of Common Facility Centre (CFC)/Raw Material Bank, purchase of Machinery/equipments, market promotion, capacity building etc. as per the need of clusters. The details of the clusters are placed at Annexure-II.(d):	A total of Rs.81.72 lakh was allocated to Thiruvananthapuram Khadi Cluster in 11th Plan and 520 artisans benefitted from it.
