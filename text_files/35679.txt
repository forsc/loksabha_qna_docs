                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2992






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Age Demographics under MGNREGS




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry has kept a record of age demographics of the number of people employed under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS);(b) 	if so, the number of people employed under MGNREGS during the last three years;(c) 	the details of percentage increase in the number of the people in the age group of 18-30 in MGNREGS registrations in 2020-21 compared to 2019-20 and the reasons for the substantial increase in the registration;(d) 	whether the Ministry has recorded the Statewise and national average number of days of employment given to registered households and people during the last five years; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b)   Yes, Sir. The details of age-wise number of person availed employment under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) in the last three financial years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 are given below: Financial Year	Age-wise (in year) number of persons availed employment (in lakh)	        18-30	   31-40	41-50	   51-60	61-80	   Greater than 802018-19	        56.75	  227.51	233.53	  161.05	93.85	       4.112019-20	        56.24	  221.32	238.39	  167.23	100.08	       4.772020-21	       101.88	  309.19	329.96	  230.77	138.54	       7.44 (c)    Mahatma Gandhi NREG Scheme is a demand driven scheme and the registered households may apply for work as per the provision of the Scheme. A total of 2.75 crore persons were registered up to financial year 2019-20 in the age group of 18 years -30 years which increased to 2.94 crore persons (7%) up to FY 2020-21 under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. (d) & (e)    Details of State/UT-wise and National level average days of employment provided per households under the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the last five financial years 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 is given at Annexure.*****
