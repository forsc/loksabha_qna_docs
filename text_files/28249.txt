                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

38






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






PMJDY Accounts




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of accounts opened under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) post the lockdown due to Covid-19 along with the details of funds mobilized through these accounts during that period;(b) 	whether the Government is planning to come up with any scheme to provide monetary benefits in terms of interest on their money for the beneficiaries PMJDY; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) As informed by banks, as on 25.03.2020 (pre-covid lockdown), the total number of Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan accounts were 38.33 crore with a deposit balance of Rs.1,18,434 crore, whereas as on 10.11.2021 (post-covid lockdown), the total number of Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan accounts are 43.85 crore with a deposit balance of Rs.1,48,069 crore.(b) & (c) No Sir. However, all saving bank accounts including PMJDY accounts earn interest on deposits as per the Board approved policy of Banks.****
