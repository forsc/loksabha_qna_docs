                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

23






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Rejuvenation and Beautification of Bijli Pasi Quila




Upendra Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the amount of funds released by the Union Government at present for the conservation and maintenance of the historical monuments particularly Bijli Pasi Quila including those in Barabanki and Lucknow;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the proposals approved by the Government for the rejuvenation and beautification of the Bijli Pasi Quila and the famous Mahadeva Temple, Barabanki of the Pandav era; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) &  (b)  	Bijli Pasi Quila, Lucknow (UP) is not a  Protected Monument under Archaeological Survey of India (ASI).  There is no protected monument in Barabanki District.  However, 20 monuments are protected by ASI in Lucknow  District. The funds released for their conservation and maintenance is given in annexure- I.(c) &   (d) 	The question does not arise.  ANNEXURE - IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION No.23 FOR 29.11.2021.Budget allocation and expenditure in the last three years and current financial year for Lucknow Circle of ASI.
