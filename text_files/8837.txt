                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Shortage of Officers and Staff in FCI




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is a huge shortage of officers and staff in Food Corporation of India (FCI); (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the measures taken by the Government to meet this shortage; (c) 	whether the FCI proposes to containerize its transport of foodgrains; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the benefits likely to accrue from it?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): 	As on 30.09.2019, there are 21181 personnel posted against sanctioned strength of 42038 in FCI. The category-wise details is as follows:Category	As on 30.09.2019	Sanctioned Strength	In position	VacancyCategory I	1111	855	256Category II	6221	4232	1989Category III	27345	13454	13891Category IV	7361	2640	4721Total	42038	21181	20857..........2/--2-During last 05 years, 5494 vacancies have been filled in FCI for Cat-I, II and III Officers/officials.For the post of Watchman (cat-IV), 984 candidates have joined against the 3491 vacancies advertised as below:Year	Candidates Joined2017-18	2712018-19	4752019-20 (up to 31.10.2019)	238Total	984Further, in year 2019, advertisement for Cat-II & III was published on 28.09.2019 & 23.02.2019 where 330 vacancies and 4103 vacancies were advertised respectively.(c): 	FCI is already moving food grains by container through CONCOR where ever found economical as compared to the rail movement.(d): 	Details of container movement during last three years are as follows:Year	Container rakes moved	Financial Savings (in Rs.)2016-17	13	44 lakhs2017-18	134	662 lakhs2018-19	167	796 lakhs2019-20 (up to Oct’19)	182	424 lakhs***
