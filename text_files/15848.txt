                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4561






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






AYUSH for Healthcare




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to provide requisite number of hospitals for the practitioners of AYUSH and if so, the action taken thereon; (b) whether the Government has initiated any action for creating awareness among the people regarding the importance of AYUSH for healthcare, if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government proposes to provide modern radio diagnosing equipments and laboratory facilities with trained manpower for the proper functioning of AYUSH hospitals, if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government proposes to fix and analyse the quality standards of medicine by scientific validation and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):      Public health is State subject and to provide requisite number of hospitals for the practitioners of AYUSH comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision for setting up of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH hospital. Under NAM, grant-in-aid is provided to State/UT Governments as per the proposals received through their State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs).           The number of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals approved under NAM since inception of the scheme till date is furnished at Annexure. (b):       Government of India is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in the country through State and UT Governments for creating awareness among the people regarding the importance of AYUSH for healthcare.    The Mission inter-alia makes provision for the following:-(i) Co-location of AYUSH facilities at Primary Health Centers (PHCs), Community Health Centers (CHCs) and Districts Hospitals (DHs).(ii) Upgradation of exclusive State Government AYUSH Hospitals and  Dispensaries.(iii) Supply of essential drugs to AYUSH hospitals and dispensaries(iv) Setting up of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital.(v) Upgradation of State Government Under-Graduate and post-Graduate Educational Institutions.(vi) Setting up of new AYUSH Educational Institutions in the States where it is not available in Government Sector.(vii) Strengthening of State Government/State Government Co-operatives/Public Sector Undertakings Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy (ASU&H) Pharmacies(viii) Strengthening of State Drug Testing Laboratories for ASU &H Drugs (ix) Support for cultivation of medicinal plants including processing and post-harvest management.           Further, the Ministry is also observing important days dedicated to AYUSH systems of Medicine viz. International Day of Yoga, Ayurveda Day, Unani Day, Siddha Day, Naturopathy Day and Homoeopathy Day etc. to create awareness about the benefits of AYUSH systems of medicine.(c):     Under NAM, there is a provision for financial assistance for setting up of upto 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital and upgradation of exclusive State Government AYUSH Hospitals wherein provision has also been made for equipping the hospital with essential diagnostic facilities, equipment and furniture. Further, there is also provision for engagement of contractual manpower in above hospitals.(d) and (e):     Central Government has established Pharmacopoeia Commission for Indian Medicine & Homoeopathy, under which respective Pharmacopoeia Committees are working to develop the quality standards of Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy drugs. Further, Central Government has also established Pharmacopoeial Laboratory for Indian Medicine and Homoeopathic Pharmacopoeial Laboratory for setting up of the Pharmacopoeial standards and act as central level drug testing Laboratory in respective systems of medicine.……………….
