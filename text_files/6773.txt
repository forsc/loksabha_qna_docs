                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

650






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Disinvestment in NLCI




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        whether the Government is going to disinvest Neyveli Lignite Corporation of India (NLCIL) and if so, the details thereof; and (b)        whether the Government has any plan to sell the shares to the State Government of Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a)        The Alternative Mechanism, in its meeting held on 13.04.2017, approved disinvestment of 15% paid up equity capital in NLC India Limited (NLCIL) through Offer for Sale (OFS) by promoters through the Stock Exchange Mechanism to maintain the Minimum Public Shareholding (MPS) norms of Securities & Exchange Board of India (SEBI). Out of the approved 15% disinvestment, OFS of 5% paid up equity is completed during the financial year, 2017-18. Disinvestment through OFS route is undertaken through Stock Exchange Mechanism by open market bidding, subject to favourable market condition and the appetite of the investors. (b)        There is no such proposal under consideration of the Government.  * * * *
