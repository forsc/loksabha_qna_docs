                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4817






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






PM Gati Shakti




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of schemes approved by the Government to strengthen connectivity under different modes of transportation under PM Gati Shakti-National Master Plan in the country particularly in Chhattisgarh;(b)  the total funds allocated and utilised under the schemes, scheme-wise;(c)  the present status of connectivity in various States and Union Territories in the country;(d)  whether the Government proposes to approve more schemes for Chhattisgarh on a priority basis by providing special status; and(e)  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (e) In October, 2021, Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) has approved the proposal for development of ''PM GatiShakti National Master Plan (NMP)'' for providing multimodal connectivity infrastructure to various Economic Zones of the Country. The PM GatiShakti NMP will depict the economic zones and the infrastructure linkages required to support them with an objective to holistically integrate all the multimodal connectivity projects and remove missing gaps for seamless movement of people, goods & services.       	Under PM Gati Shakti-National Master Plan, following highway projects in Chhattisgarh state are included:-(i)   Raipur-Vishakhapatnam Economic Corridor of total length 464 km and out of this 124 km passes in Chhattisgarh. (ii)  Durg-Raipur Arang Bypass of length 92 Km in Chhattisgarh.(iii)  Bilaspur-Urga from km. 0.00 to 70.20 of NH 130A.        	Total fund approved for Raipur-Vishakhapatnam in Chhattisgarh state is Rs. 4174 Cr. Rs. 1038 cr. is approved for Land Acquisition for Durg-Raipur Arang Bypass.  Total fund approved for Bilspur-Urga  is Rs. 1855.59 cr.All 28 Districts in Chhattisgarh are connected through National Highways. *****
