                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1802






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Central Road and Infrastructure Fund




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the roads proposed to be constructed in the State of Uttar Pradesh (UP) under the Central Road and Infrastructure fund(CRIF); (b) whether any such proposal has been received for Prayagraj (UP) and if so, the details thereof; and (c) the number of proposals received by the Union Government for construction of roads in UP during the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 under CRIF along with the details of roads for which funds have been released during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) At present projects amounting to Rs15439.14 crore (2000-01 to 2020-21) are sanctioned for construction in the state of UP under CRIF including the works of Prayagraj/Allahabd. No new  proposal has been received from the state of UP during the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 under CRIF including that of Prayagraj. *****
