                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4822






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Expertise in Outsourcing




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	Whether the Government lacks indigenous expertise in outsourcing;b)	If so, the reaction of the Government in this regard;c)	The private agencies with which contract has been signed for outsourcing during the last three years and the works performed by them;d)	The expenditure incurred on them; e)	The reasons for the above said outsourcing?






ANSWER




                                                                             THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                 MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a to e) The General Financial Rules 2017 (GFR 2017) allow the Central Government establishments to outsource certain services based on their requirements.  As Ministry and its offices are competent to procure services of outsourcing agencies at their level to meet their requirements, the centralized data is not maintained in this regard.                                   ******
