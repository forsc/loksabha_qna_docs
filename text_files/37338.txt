                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4346






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






PMGSY in Uttar Pradesh




Girish Chandra














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of new roads selected under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in the State of Uttar Pradesh;(b) 	the funds allocated, released and utilized for implementation of the yojana in Uttar Pradesh during the current financial year;(c) 	the length of roads constructed in rural districts of Uttar Pradesh under PMGSY during the last three years; and(d) 	the number of habitations provided access to roads under the yojana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)      The State of Uttar Pradesh has been allocated road length of 18,937.50 Km under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana phase III (PMGSY-III), against which, 2,534 number of  road works measuring  18,770.17 Km have already been sanctioned to the State till 24th March, 2022 as per the following details :-Year	                                   Sanctioned	      No. of Roads	   Road Length (km)	Value of Projects (Rs. in crore)2020-2021	  898	                6,287.38	        4,177.802021-2022	1,636	               12,482.79	       10,068.07Total	        2,534	               18,770.17	       14,245.86 (b)        A sum of Rs. 1,418.55 crore were released as central share and expenditure (including state share) of Rs. 1,840.98 crore was incurred for implementation of PMGSY in Uttar Pradesh during the current year till 23rd March, 2022. (c)        The details of road length constructed in the districts of Uttar Pradesh under PMGSY during the last three years and current year (as on 23.03.2022) are as under: Year	         Road Length constructed (km)2018-19	                 1,6882019-20	                   3762020-21	                   7182021-22 (as on 23.3.2022)	         2,223 (d)       Since inception till 23rd March, 2022, a total of 1,55,770 habitations in the population category 250+ and 5,858 habitations in the population category 100-249 have been provided all weather road connectivity under PMGSY in various States/ UTs. In the State of Uttar Pradesh, 11,748 habitations have been provided all-weather road connectivity during the said period.******
