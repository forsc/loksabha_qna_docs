                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Impact of Smuggling on Indian Economy




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) has released its study titled ‘Invisible Enemy: Impact of Smuggling on Indian Economy and Employment’;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the smuggling of goods in various industries including textiles, readymade garments, cigarettes, machinery and parts and consumer electronics prevented India from adding over 16 lakh jobs in 2017-18 as per the above study;(d) 	if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(e) 	whether 11.35 lakh job opportunities were lost because of backward linkages and multiplier effects as finished products were smuggled into India;(f) 	if so, the reaction of the Government thereto; and(g) 	the details of the corrective steps being taken by the Government for the identification of such issues and providing remedy in this regard? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (g): 	 Yes Sir, the Study is under examination.*****
