                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4545






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Ethanol Production Crops




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government gives subsidy directly to the farmers cultivating Ethanol Production crops and if so, the details thereof, if not, the reasons therefor; (b) 	whether the Government has any plan to give subsidy directly to the farmers to encourage cultivating Ethanol production crops; (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor; and (d) 	whether the Government is aware of production cost of each litre of Ethanol in the country, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (c): 		No, sir. In order to ensure the food security of the country, the Government through Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) is implementing National Food Security Mission (NFSM) in 28 States and 2 UTs of the country for increasing the production and productivity of rice, wheat, pulses, coarse/nutri cereals, commercial crops (cotton, jute and sugarcane), oilseeds and oilpalm. NFSM is a crop production oriented scheme being implemented to ensure food security of the country and not with the objective of production of ethanol...........2/--2-(d): 	The ethanol in the country is being produced from different feedstocks including sugarcane and foodgrains. The production cost of ethanol depends upon number of factors viz feed-stock, efficiency of the plant, cost of other inputs etc. The procurement price of ethanol for current Ethanol Supply Year (ESY) 2020-21 has been fixed as under:                                    (in Rs. per ltr.)Feed stock	2020-21         (Dec-Nov)Sugarcane juice	62.65B- Heavy molasses	57.61C-Heavy molasses	45.69Damaged food grains 	51.55Rice available with FCI 	56.87Maize 	51.55*****
