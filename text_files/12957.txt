                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1674






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Request from Defence Department




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FINANCE be pleased to state: (a) 	whether the Ministry has declined the repeated request from the Defence Department regarding the fund allocation to procure food and clothes for jawans deployed  at high altitude areas; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of reasons as to why the budget requirement demanded by the defence department goes denied by the Ministry; (d) 	whether the Ministry has any data of the budget demanded by the Defence Ministry and allocated amount during the last four years; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) 	  No, Sir. The budget allocations for ‘Defence Services’ catering to the three (3) Services viz. Army, Navy and Air Force are not provided item-wise and made, mainly, for revenue expenditure and capital expenditure, separately, for ‘Defence Services’. For the revenue expenditure, the only bifurcation indicated in this Ministry is on ‘salaries’ and ‘non-salaries’.(b) & (c) Do not arise.(d) & (e)The details of projected requirement of funds for ‘Defence Services’ and the amount provided through budget for the last four years are as follows:  								          (Rs.incrore )Year	Demand projected	Amount provided through Budget2015-16	2,87,386	2,46,7272016-17	2,91,984	2,49,0992017-18	3,23,217	2,62,3902018-19	3,95,851	2,82,733The budget allocations are made based on the resource position of the Government. *****
