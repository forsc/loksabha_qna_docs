                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4717






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Leakage of CGL Question Papers




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Question Papers of each and every shift of CGL 2017 Tier-II Exam scheduled from 17.02.2018 to 21.02.2018 were leaked before the start of the exam; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of CBI Investigation regarding leakage of question papers and the action taken against responsible officials and others involved therein; and (d) 	the details of the steps taken by the Government to ensure that SSC conducts the examination in a fair and efficient manner, free from all malpractices?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b):   No, Sir. However, there have been allegations of cheating/leakage of some questions of Combined Graduate Level (Tier-II) Examination, 2017 (Paper-I) scheduled for the first shift on 21.02.2018.(c):  CBI had in FIR no. RC AC1 2018A 0009 dated 22.05.2018 filed charge-sheet on 02.08.2019 in the court against three accused (private persons) for the offences punishable u/s120 B, r/w 420 of IPC, Section 13(2) r/w 13(1)(d) of the PC Act 1988 and Section 66-D of the IT Act.(d):  The Staff Selection Commission maintains a high standard of integrity, discipline and efficiency in the conduct of its examinations to ensure merit-based selection. To facilitate this process, the Commission has taken comprehensive measures which, inter-alia, include audit of examination venues, conduct of mock-tests, deployment of Commission’s representatives as Inspecting Officers at the examination venues, physical frisking of candidates with the help of Hand-Held Metal Detectors, comprehensive CCTV coverage of the examination venues, biometric registration of candidates, deployment of low powered mobile jammers, etc. No allegation of Question Paper leakage has come to the notice of the Commission with respect to the examinations conducted subsequent to the Combined Graduate Level (Tier-II) Examination, 2017.*****
