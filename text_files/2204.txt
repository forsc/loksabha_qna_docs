                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1963






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Outsourcing of Visa Handling Process




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government has outsourced the visa handling process in many of its Embassies and Consulates;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether such outsourcing of visa handling process entails a security risk; and (d) if so, the steps taken/being taken by the  Government to address the issues of security risks? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	Yes.  (b)	Visa outsourcing has been implemented in 61 Indian Missions/Posts located in 33 countries. (c) & (d)	While front-end services including scrutiny and acceptance of visa applications, collection of visa fee and return of applicant’s passport and other documents are rendered by the outsourcing service provider, the final authority for issuance of visas remains with Indian Mission/Post concerned and visas are issued as per laid down procedures and guidelines and only after thorough scrutiny, in order to avoid any security risks.***
