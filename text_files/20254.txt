                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1815






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Accidents on Mumbai-Agra NH




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government has received reports of disregard of road design and road signage on expressways particularly in the recently constructed Mumbai–Agra National Highway (NH) in Ozar area of Nashik district causing increased probability of road accidents;(b) If so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto;(c) The number of road accidents that have taken place in that area so far; and(d) Whether the Government has prepared any effective mechanism to rectify such mistakes and prevent road accidents and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) & (b) No Sir. The Ozar area falls on 6-laned Pimpalgaon-Nashik-Gonde section of NH-3. This stretch has been completed as per Codal provision and all requisite road signages have been installed.(c)  18 of accidents have been reported in Ozar region of NH-3 in the past one year. The majority of accidents have happened due to over speeding.(d)  In order to segregate local traffic from Highway traffic, National Highway Authority of India had taken up construction of two Bridges on both sides of service lane on Banganga river alongwith two flyovers at Gadakh corner and Saikheda Junction.*****
