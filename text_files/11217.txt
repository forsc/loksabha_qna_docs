                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

140






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Vacant Posts in Universities




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the studies are getting adversely affected due to academic and other staffposts lying vacant in the Universities of the country;(b) if so, the details of the posts lying vacant, University-wise;(c) the details of the budgetary allocations made to the Rajasthan, University-wise; and(d) the time by which the professors and academic staff are likely to be appointed?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): Occurrence and filling up of vacancies is a continuous process. The onus offilling up the posts lies on Central Universities which are autonomous bodies created underActs of Parliament. However, the University Grants Commission (UGC) continuouslymonitors it with Universities. UGC has prepared the Guidelines for Recruitment of Facultyin Universities, Colleges and Institutions Deemed to be Universities outlining the selectionprocedure and the time frame for recruitment which has been circulated to all Universitiesto adhere to the guidelines. The universities have also been requested to take steps toensure that the vacancies in the university as well as in the colleges affiliated to universityare filled up at the earliest and also the status of the recruitment process is uploaded onthe University Activity Monitoring Portal of UGC.In order to ensure that studies of students are not affected, the CentralUniversities undertakes various temporary measures such as hiring ad-hoc faculty, Guestfaculty, Contract Faculty and Re-employment of faculty. At present, there are total1,77,235 sanctioned teaching posts and 3,69,587 sanctioned non teachings posts invarious universities across the country out of which 32,581 teachings posts and 93,696non teaching posts are lying vacant. A detailed statement of the teaching and nonteachings posts in the Universities is available athttps://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/0053281_LSPQ-No.-140,-Annexure-I.pdfand https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/0567047_LSPQ-No.-140,-Annexure-II.pdf respectively.Further, a detailed statement of budgetary allocation, as provided by UGC, madeto Universities of Rajasthan is as under:<pre>*****S.No. Name of the University Allocation (in lakhs)Central University (2019-20)1.
