                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4551






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Hysterectomy among Women Workers




Sakshi Ji Swami Maharaj













                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is aware that 4605 women have undergone hysterectomy in Beed district of Maharashtra to work as sugarcane cutter continuously; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has constituted a panel to probe this case; (d) if so, the details thereof; (e) whether the panel has submitted its report to the Government in this regard; and (f) if so, the findings of the report of the panel?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Survey of 99 private hospitals in Beed district has revealed that, in the past 3 years i.e. in 2016-17, 2017-18 and 18-19, 4605 hysterectomy operations have been performed. (c) & (d):	The state government has constituted a seven member committee on 26 June 2019 under the chairmanship of Dy.President of the legislative council of the state. The other members of the committee including 2 female MLAs from the state legislative council, Principal secretary, Public health department, Director Health Services, Prof and HOD OBGY BJ Medical college Pune and Representative from UNICEF.(e) & (f):	The committee has conducted two meetings with all stakeholders and civil society organisations. The committee has not submitted its report till now.
