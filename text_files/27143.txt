                                    JAL SHAKTI

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2769






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Control of Erosion




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the fact that acute erosion occurs every year at various locations of Medertari, Fakirganj, Salmara and Hazirhat area on south bank of river Brahmaputra in Dhubri, South Salmara and mankachar districts of Assam, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has any data on area eroded away during the last three years, if so, the details of the number of families which have been affected by the erosion; and (c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to control erosion in the area?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	  The Government is aware of the fact that acute erosion occurs every year at various locations of Medertari, Fakirganj, Salmara and Hazirhat area on south bank of river Brahmaputra in Dhubri, South Salmara and Mankachar districts of Assam.
