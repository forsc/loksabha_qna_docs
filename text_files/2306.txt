                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2076






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Allotment of Land to Hospitals




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of private hospitals in Delhi which had been allotted land at concessional rates on the condition of providing free of cost treatment to the 25 per cent people of poor category;(b) whether these private hospitals are complying with the terms and conditions of land lease deed;(c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the corrective steps taken by the Union Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)	(a)	The names of the allottees, who have been given land on concessional rates for construction of private hospitals in Delhi by Land & Development Office and Delhi Development Authority are enclosed at Annexure-A and Annexure-B respectively. (b)to (d) While land is allotted to Private Hospitals in Delhi by Land &Development Office and DDA, the monitoring regarding free treatment to the patients belonging to EWS Category is within the jurisdiction of Govt. of NCT of Delhi. The Government of NCT, Delhi has constituted Monitoring Committee as per the directions of Hon’ble High Court of Delhi & the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India. In case of any violation reported by the Monitoring Committee, Land and Development Office and DDA initiate the action as per terms of allotment/Lease Deed.                                 *****
