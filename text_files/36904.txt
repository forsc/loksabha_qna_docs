                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3958






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Development of Taal Chhapar Sanctuary as Tourist Spot




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that more than 25 per cent of foreign tourists visit Rajasthan alone which is highest in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof during the last five years with particular reference to Rajasthan, Country/tourist-wise;(c)	whether the Government proposes to construct tourist spot/memorial of world class by strengthening and renovation of Tall Chhapar sanctuary and well (Bavadi) situated in district headquarters in Churu district and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government proposes to release funds for these tourist places and if so, the time by which it is likely to be released and if not, the reasons therefor;(e)	whether the Government proposes to accord administrative and financial approval to upgrade hotels of Rajasthan as a management institute under Food and Craft Institute; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                               (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	No, Sir.(b):	Does not arise.(c) & (d):	Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations for development of tourism infrastructure in the country including Rajasthan. Submission of project proposals by the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations under the above schemes is a continuous process. The projects are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc.Details of projects under PRASHAD and Swadesh Darshan in Rajasthan including Churu District sanctioned by Ministry of Tourism, Government of India are at Annexure. Projects under these schemes are under various stages of implementation. (e) & (f):	No proposal has been received by the Ministry of Tourism from       State Government of Rajasthan to upgrade hotels of Rajasthan as a management institute under Food and Craft Institute.******
