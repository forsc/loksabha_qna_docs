                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

576






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






CGHS Wellness Centres




Balak Nath













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the  criteria  fixed  for  opening  of  dispensary/ hospital in the country  under the Central Government Health  Scheme(CGHS)  along  with  the  details thereof;(b)	the details of places where dispensaries/hospitals are being run at present in the country under the CGHS along with the details thereof, State-wise;(c)	the   number  of  beneficiaries  who  received treatment facility in Rajasthan under the CGHS during the last five years along with details of such beneficiaries, district-wise;(d)	whether the Government proposes to set up new dispensaries/hospitals in the districts having more than 1000 beneficiaries under the CGHS scheme; and(e)	if so, by when along with the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (e): The norms for opening a new Wellness Centre under Central Government Health  Scheme(CGHS) are as under :-i. In an existing CGHS City:-For opening a new CGHS Wellness Centre in an existing CGHS City, there has to be a minimum of 2000 Card holders (serving employees of Central Government and Central Civil Pensioners).ii. Extension of CGHS to a new City:-For extension of CGHS to a new City, there has to be a minimum of 6000 Card holders.Opening of new CGHS Wellness Centre(s) is considered as per extant norms & subject to availability of resources.At present, there is no proposal to open new CGHS Wellness Centres in the districts where there are more than 1000 beneficiaries under the CGHS.A statement showing State-wise CGHS Wellness Centres (Allopathic) in the country is at Annexure. A statement showing details of beneficiaries who availed treatment facilities under CGHS in Rajasthan during last five years is as under :-Sl. No. 	CGHS city	No. of CGHS beneficiaries (till 28.01.2022)1.	Jaipur	43,32,0292.	Ajmer*	66,4583.	Jodhpur*	64,0064.	Kota*	22,458Total	44,84,951* : CGHS took over Postal Dispensary w.e.f. 01.04.2019.*******
