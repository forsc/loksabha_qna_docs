                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1905






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Export of Corona Vaccine




S. Ramalingam













                                                    Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    Faizal P.P. Mohammed
                                                








                                                    Kirti Vardhan Singh
                                                








                                                    Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah
                                                








                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                








                                                    Jagdambika Pal 
                                                








                                                    Badruddin Ajmal
                                                








                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                








                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has entered into agreement with other countries including neighbouring SAARC countries for the supply of COVID-19 vaccine/ Covaxin in response to the commercial orders from foreign countries for procuring COVID-19 vaccine;(b) if so, the details of the agreements, number of vaccines ordered, delivery period and the vaccines exported along with the revenue earned or likely to be earned by the Government thereon;(c) whether the export of COVID vaccine from India is part of a bilateral programme or part of the broader WHO plan, if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government is extending special help to SAARC/neighbouring countries by supplying these vaccines as grants/free of cost, if so, the details thereof and if not the special purchase agreement entered into with such countries for supplying the vaccines;(e) whether the Government had recently reviewed the issue of supply of COVID-19 vaccines doses to foreign countries both as grants and through commercial basis, if so, the details thereof and the outcome thereon; and(f) whether the supply of vaccine to other countries will have any adverse effect on controlling disease in the country and if so, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b): External supplies of vaccines are being undertaken with due regard to domestic production and adequate availability for the national vaccination programme. Supplies have been made of 167.7 lakh doses to 20 countries until 9 February 20201. This includes gift of 62.7 lakh doses to 13 countries and commercial procurement of 105 lakh doses by 8 countries. Details are at Annexure.(c) to (e): India is a member of COVAX facility (created by Gavi – Global Alliance for Vaccines & Immunization, CEPI – Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations and    WHO – World Health Organisation) and is both a recipient of vaccines from the facility as well as a supplier of vaccines. In addition to COVAX facility, external supplies have been made bilaterally through gifts by the Government of India and commercial sales by the manufacturers.(f): External supplies are being undertaken with due regard to domestic production and adequate availability for the national vaccination programme.
