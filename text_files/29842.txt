                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1498






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Compensation to Workers due to Delay in Payment of MGNREGS Wages in Andhra Pradesh




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether a wage seeker under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) is entitled to receive payment of compensation at the rate of 0.05% of the unpaid wages per day of delay beyond the sixteenth day of closure of muster roll in case of not receiving payment of wages within fifteen days from the date of closure of muster roll, if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the total number of MNREGS transactions which took more than 15 days to transfer wages to workers in Andhra Pradesh (AP) since 2019, year-wise and district-wise;(c) 	the total amount of compensation paid to workers due to delay in payment of MGNREGS wages beyond 15 days in AP since 2019, year-wise and district-wise; and(d) 	whether there was a delay in payment of wage and no compensation was given to the workers, if so, the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):       Yes, Sir.  As per Para 29 of Schedule II of the Mahatma Gandhi National Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), in case the payment of wages is not made within fifteen days from the date of closure of the muster roll, the wage seeker shall be entitled to receive payment of compensation for the delay, at the rate of 0.05% of the unpaid wages per day of delay beyond the sixteenth day of closure of muster roll.(b) & (c):  As per the information shared by the State of Andhra Pradesh, the total number of Mahatma Gandhi NREGS transactions which took more than 15 days to transfer wages to the accounts of unskilled workers and total amount of compensation paid to unskilled workers due to delay in payment of Mahatma Gandhi NREGS wages beyond 15 days in State of Andhra Pradesh in financial years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 (as on 01.12.2021) is given at Annexure(d):      Delay compensation is calculated as per the provisions of the Act. The State Government has to pay the delay compensation as per the notified rules for delay compensation based on the provisions of the Act. The amount due for compensation is duly verified and approved by the State Government and, henceforth, the payment for delay compensation is made to the eligible worker.            As per information shared by State of Andhra Pradesh, the delay at various levels is being computed and delay compensation has also been paid accordingly.  The details of approved delay compensation, delay compensation paid and delay compensation due in the State of Andhra Pradesh in the financial years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 (as on 03.12.2021) is given below:                                                                              (Rs. in lakh)Financial Year 	                2019-20	             2020-21	           2021-22 ( as on                                                                        03.12.2021)Approved delay compensation	  64.74	              16.23	                 2.69Delay compensation paid	          63.01	              15.60	                 2.24delay compensation due	           1.73	               0.63	                 0.45 (As per MIS) ******
