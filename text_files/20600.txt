                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1763






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Housing in Telangana




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that 1.25 lakh houses were sanctioned to the 	State of Telangana between 2016 and 2019 and if so, the 	details thereof; (b) 	whether out of the above, only 65,000 houses have been 	completed in the State; (c) 	if so, the details thereof including the status of the remaining 	houses and the time by which these are likely to be completed 	and handed over to the beneficiaries; and (d) 	the details of houses sanctioned between 2019 and 2021 	alongwith the status of the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c) Yes sir; in the State of Telangana 1,19,465 houses were sanctioned under the scheme of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban [PMAY – U] between 2016 and 2019. All these sanctioned houses have been grounded for construction; of which 74,868 houses have been completed. Houses sanctioned under the schemeare to be completed and delivered to the beneficiaries within the scheme period i.e. by March, 2022.(d) Between 2019 and 2021, 52,200 houses have been sanctioned under the scheme for the State of Telangana. Of these, 52,055 houses have been grounded for construction and 41,060 houses have been completed.                                          *****
