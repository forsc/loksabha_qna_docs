                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






PMFBY for Kharif and Rabi Seasons




Santosh Pandey













                                                    Debasree Chaudhuri
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of farmers covered under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) for the kharif and rabi seasons during the last three years, State-wise including Chhattisgarh;(b) 	the quantum of premium collected and claims paid out by insurance companies during the same period, State-wise and company-wise;(c) 	whether the insured farmers were deprived of insurance despite the authentic report of no rain, heavy rain and scanty rain with respect to which the farmers of Khairagarh and Dongargaon tehsils of Rajnandgaon district have submitted complaints;(d) 	whether the premium amounts deducted by Union Bank Sakuldaihan, Punjab National Bank Bhandarpur etc. of Rajnandgaon district were retained after harvesting of the crop;(e)	 if so, the name of the guilty in this regard; and(f) 	the action being taken against the insurance company?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER of AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): 	State-wise and Kharif & Rabi season-wise details including Chhatisgarh of farmer applications insured  during last three years i.e. 2018-19 to 2020-21 under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) are at Annexure-I.(b):  	State-wise and insurance company-wise details of premium collected  and claims paid by insurance companies during last three years are given in Annexure-II.(c):  	As reported by the State Government of Chhattisgarh, settlement  of admissible claims of eligible farmers of Khairagarh and Dongargaon Tehsil of Rajanandgaon district have already been paid by the concerned insurance company.   Status of complaints in respect of loss due to no rain, scanty rains in these Tehsils is given below:Season	Tehsil Khairagarh	Tehsil Dongargaon	Farmer ApplicationsReceived 	Application Status	Farmer Appli-cationsReceived	Application Status		Paid	Ineligible		Paid	IneligibleKharif 2018	32	15	17	17	14	3Rabi 2018-19	0	0	0	0	0	0Kharif 2019	30	18	12	14	11	3Rabi 2019-20	45	14	31	2	2	0Kharif 2020	42	33	9	13	11	2Rabi 2020-21	2	2	017	1	1	0(d)  to  (f) : 	Punjab National Bank  branch Bhandarpur & Tilai  and State Bank of Indi Branch Khairagarh have not entered the farmer’s information/data  for Kharif 2019 season under PMFBY in National Crop Insurance Portal (NCIP) within the prescribed time limits due to which the concerned insurance company had refunded the premium amount of 1423 farmers to the concerned banks. 	Further, in season Rabi 2019-20, State Bank of India, Khairagarh did not enter the requisite data/information of 308 farmers in NCIP in prescribed time limit and also requested to the concerned insurance company to hold excess premium with the company.   	Inspite of giving additional time for data entry in portal for the season Kharif and Rabi 2019-20, the concerned bank, did not enter the farmer’s detail on NCIP.   Accordingly, as per Operational Guidelines of the scheme, banks were asked to pay the claims to the concerned farmers. 	The same matter was also presented by the concerned bank in the State Level Grievance Redressal Committee. On the basis of information provided by concerned bank, insurance company and as per the provision of the scheme, banks have been found guilty and State Government has asked the concerned banks to pay claims of aggrieved farmers as per provisions of the Operational Guidelines.
