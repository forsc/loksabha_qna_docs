                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3005






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Recruitment of Coaches/Trainers




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut













                                                    Rekha Arun Verma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to recruit 1500 coaches or trainers from among all the players of the country to fill up the existing vacant posts and ensure better co-ordination;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of coaches/trainers likely to be recruited in Maharashtra; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c) No Sir. The total sanctioned strength of coaches’ in Sports Authority of India (SAI), an autonomous body under the aegis of this Ministry is 1524, out of which as on date, 478 posts are vacant in various grades viz. Assistant Coach, Coach, Senior Coach, Chief Coach.Assistant Coach grade post is a feeder grade post, filled through Direct Recruitment, out of which 5% and 1% posts are reserved for Olympians and Para-Olympians respectively. Apart from this 10 posts in the grade of coach are also reserved for Olympics/ Para-Olympics medal winners.Individuals having Diploma in Sports coaching or an excellent track record as sportspersons are considered against the vacancies of Assistant coaches in SAI. The selection of coaches is not done State wise.*****
