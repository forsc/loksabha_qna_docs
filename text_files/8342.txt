                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2043






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






NRISR in Ladakh




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has any proposal to establish a National Research Institute For Sowa-Regpa (NRISR) in Leh, Ladakh; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) if not, the reasons therefor and the time by which the Government plans to do so; (d) whether the Government has any plans to promote Hydrotherapy (hot spring water) in the country particularly in Ladakh; (e) if so, the details thereof along with the proposals of the Government plans in this regard; and (f) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c):    The National Research Institute for Sowa- Rigpa(NRISR) at Leh, Ladakh was established by the Ministry of AYUSH in 2009. In addition, the Government has recently approved  establishment of a National Institute of Sowa-Rigpa(NISR), an autonomous organization under the Ministry of AYUSH, at Leh, Ladakh. (d) to (f):      At present there is no plan to promote  Hydrotherapy (hot spring water) under this Institute in Ladakh. However, the Institute may consider a plan for Hydrotherapy  in Ladakh in future.. ………………..
