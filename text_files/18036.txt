                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1774






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Details of schemes




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the work-wise and scheme-wise details of the amount of funds allocated by the Government for Chhattisgarh State during the last two years i.e. 2018-2020; (b) whether the Union Government has received any proposal from the State Government during the last two years i.e. 2018-2020; and  (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a)  Under the Central Sector Schemes (CSS) of Ministry of Coal, no fund is allocated to State Government. However, funds spent in Chhattisgarh State under various CSS during the last two years are as under: Sub -Scheme	Year	Fund disbursed in the blocks/projects in Chhattisgarh (Rs. Crore)1 Detailed Exploration in Non-CIL blocks	2018-19	138.50	2019-20	300.962. Promotional (Regional) Exploration in Coal & Lignite	2018-19	34.88	2019-20	21.863. Research & Development (R&D )Scheme	2018-19	0.00	2019-20	0.004. Conservation and Safety in Coal Mines	2018 -19	0.89	2019 - 20	2.155. Development of Transport Infrastructure in Coalfields area (DTIC) 	2018 -19	6.135	2019 - 20	4.34Total	509.715 (b)       No, Sir. (c)        Does not arise in view of (b) above.   ……..
