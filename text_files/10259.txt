                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

329






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Adjusted Gross Revenues




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the salient points of the Supreme Court Judgement dated 24-10-2019 in the matter of Adjusted Gross Revenues (AGR) of Telecom Service Providers (TSPs); (b) 	the details of the AGR arrears that have to be recovered by the Government from private and public TSPs, year-wise and TSP-wise; and (c) 	the roadmap/timelines formulated by the Government to recover the aforesaid AGR arrears?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) to (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *329 FOR 11TH DECEMBER, 2019  ON “ADJUSTED GROSS REVENUES”.(a)	The Hon’ble Supreme Court in CIVIL APPEAL NOS. 6328 - 6399 OF 2015 [ UNION OF INDIA Vs. ASSOCIATION OF UNIFIED TELECOM SERVICE PROVIDERS OF INDIA ETC.] pronounced its judgment dated 24-10-2019 wherein the appeals filed by the Department of Telecom were upheld and the appeals filed by the Telecom Service Providers were dismissed.The salient pointsof the judgement dated 24-10-2019 are attached as Annexure-I.(b)	The provisional Adjusted Gross Revenue dues of the Telecom Service Providers on account of License Fee and Spectrum Usage Charges are based on the assessments carried out/ demands raised and are subject to revision in light of the Hon’ble Supreme Court order dated 24-10-2019. Details are at Annexure-II (a) and Annexure-II (b).(c)	The Hon’ble Supreme Court while pronouncing the judgement on 24-10-2019 has granted 3 months’ time for compliance of the order. The Department of Telecom has issued a letter to all the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) directing them to make the payments in accordance with the order of the Hon’ble Supreme Court dated 24-10-2019 and submit the relevant documentsto ensure the compliance within the stipulated timeframe.
