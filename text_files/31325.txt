                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2898






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Updated Time Table as per New Stoppages




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to instruct the authorities to update the timetable as per developing new stoppages; and (b) if so, the details of new stoppages that have been started or shifted in between Pune to Miraj section?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) Updation of time table is a regular exercise on Indian Railways which inter-alia includes incorporating change of timings and stoppages.(b) In the recent past, no additional stoppages have been provided at stations located on the Pune - Miraj section. Besides, rationalization of stoppages is an on-going process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility and commercial justification.*****
