                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the salient features of the Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana and the current status of its implementation in the country; (b) 	the list of States/UTs that are currently eligible under the scheme; and (c) 	whether the State of Gujarat is currently eligible under the scheme and if so, the details of the sanctioned/ ongoing projects that are being undertaken by the State of Gujarat as well as the funds transferred under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR(a):	Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) has been launched in  2007-08 and the scheme has been  revised  with effect from 01.11.2017  as Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana - Remunerative Approaches for Agriculture and Allied sector Rejuvenation (RKVY-RAFTAAR) with broad objectives of making farming a remunerative economic activity through strengthening the farmer’s effort, risk mitigation with  major focus on development and creation of pre & post-harvest infrastructure, besides promoting agri-entrepreneurship and innovations. Under this scheme,  funds are released to the State Governments on the basis of projects related to agriculture and allied sectors, approved by the State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) headed by the Chief Secretary of the concerned State which is the empowered body to approve projects for implementation under this scheme.(b) & (c):	All states and UTs are eligible under the scheme.  Projects approved for implementation under the scheme by Gujarat during the current year 2020-21 is given at Annexure-I and II. An amount of Rs.67.08 crore has been released to the State Government of Gujarat under the scheme during 2020-21 as first installment.
