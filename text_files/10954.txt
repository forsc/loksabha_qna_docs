                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4226






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Handloom Clusters




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any proposals for the development of mega handloom clusters in Kerala under the  Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme are pending approval, particularly in Waynad region;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the existing Handloom Clusters in Kerala and the financial assistance provided to the respective implementing agencies?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): The Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) is targeted at development of Mega Handloom Clusters in geographical locations covering atleast 15000 handlooms with the Government of India contribution upto Rs.40 crore per cluster over a period of 5 years.  There is no proposal pending for approval relating to development of any mega handloom cluster in the state of Kerala. (c): Based on the viable proposals received from the Government of Kerala, 06 Block Level Clusters (BLCs) have been sanctioned under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP).
