                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2826






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Assistance for Tree Plantation




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has provided financial and technical assistance to State Government of Gujarat for tree plantation schemes; (b)	 if so, the details thereof, scheme-wise; (c)	 the amount of financial assistance provided to Gujarat during the last three years, year-wise; and (d)	 the number of trees planted during the last three years with the said financial assistance, year-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	 and (b) The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC) implements    two major afforestation schemes for increasing forest resources in the country, including Gujarat, namely National Afforestation Programme (NAP) for tree plantation in degraded forests through people’s participation and National Mission for a Green India (GIM), for plantation on landscape basis. Besides, the funds are released under Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) to States/UTs for carrying out afforestation activities. An amount of Rs. 233.19 crore has been released to Gujarat under NAP since inception of the scheme in 2000 and an area of 1,08,214 ha has been sanctioned for new afforestation in the corresponding period. (c) and (d) During last three years no funds have been released to Gujarat under NAP and GIM. However, an amount of Rs. 1484.60 crore, as share of the State as per Compensatory Fund Act, 2016, has been transferred to State CAMPA of Gujarat from National Authority of CAMPA during 2019 for taking up various activities including afforestation as per approved Annual Plan of Operation (APO).
