                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3592






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Schemes for Quality Agricultural Inputs




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the schemes being run by the Government for the upgradation of farmers by providing them good quality seeds and better agricultural equipments;(b)	 if so, the details thereof; and(c)	 if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)a)	Government of India through its various schemes viz.Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Materials (SMSP), National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Bringing Green Revolution to Eastern India(BGREI), Rashtriya Krishi VikasYojana(RKVY) and SubMission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM), provides financial assistance to farmers on seed distribution, seed production, farmers training, seed treating drums, seed storage bins and farm mechanization by promoting Custom Hiring Centersfor distribution of various agricultural equipments to farmers.b)	The pattern of assistance under above-mentioned schemes is available at Annexure-I.c)	     Question does not arise.
