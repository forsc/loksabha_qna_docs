                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5155






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Establishment of Digital Banking Units




Arun Sao













                                                    Kripanath Mallah
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up 75 digital banking units in 75 districts of the country; (b)	the details thereof along with the funds allocated for the purpose and the targets set in this regard;(c)	whether the Government has identified the locations in this regard in the country, if so, the details thereof including the districts selected for the purpose in the country, State-wise including Assam and Chhattisgarh; and(d)	the time by which the digital banking units are likely to be set up in the country?	






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of Finance(a) to (d) As announced in the Union Budget 2022-23, Seventy-five (75) Digital Banking Units (DBUs) in 75 Districts of the country will be set up so as to ensure that the benefits of digital banking are provided in the country in a consumer-friendly manner.As apprised by Reserve Bank of India (RBI), a Committee has been set up to prepare a roadmap for successful implementation of the said Programme. RBI has further apprised that criteria, such as, relatively higher percentage of women population, youth, MSMEs, exports, geographically and strategically important locations, North-East area, SHG penetration, moderate level of digital adoption etc., will be taken into consideration while finalizing the list of 75 districts for setting up of DBUs. No funds have been allocated by RBI for setting up of DBUs. The DBUs are expected to be operationalized by the end of the current calendar year. *****
