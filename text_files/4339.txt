                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4034






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Over Bridge at Katihar City




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken a decision to build over bridges or under passes at all the railway crossings in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the railway crossing in the middle of Katihar city causes traffic jam for hours which leads to chaotic situation in the city;(d)	if so, whether the Government proposes to build an over bridge at this railway crossing; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. As per Pink Book, 1581 Nos. of Road over Bridges (ROBs) have been sanctioned. Out of these, 249 ROBs has been fully completed and Railway portion of 121 ROBs has been completed. 5751 Nos. of Road under Bridges (RUBs) have been sanctioned. Out of these, 2617 RUBs has been completed.(c) to (e): There are traffic detentions due to Level Crossing (LC) No. KB-1, Goshala gate being situated in the middle of Katihar town. Road construction department, Government of Bihar has undertaken the work of construction of ROB at this location on deposit terms (fully funded by Road construction department, Government of Bihar). General Arrangement Drawing (GAD) of this ROB has been approved by Railways on 08.09.2016. Further action has to be taken by Road construction department, Government of Bihar.*****
