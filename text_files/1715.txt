                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1574






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Objectives of PMKSY




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether all the States are covered under the PMKSY and if so, the details of medium/major irrigation projects identified in consultation with States along with the irrigation potential of these projects; and (b)	the quantum of Central Assistance (CA) released under various components of PMKSY especially AIBP, CAD and DRIP-II for the State of Karnataka during the last three years and the current year ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)&(b):	The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) is being implemented from the year 2015-16 in various States of the  country.  The details of the components of PMKSY are as under: i.	Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Programme (AIBP): To focuses on faster completion of ongoing Major and Medium Irrigation projects including National Projects.  Implemented by Ministry of Jal Shakti.  ii.	PMKSY-Har Khet Ko Pani (HKKP): Source augmentation, distribution, ground water development, lift irrigation, diversion of water from water plenty to water scarce areas, repair, restoration, renovation of traditional water bodies.  Implemented by Ministry of Jal Shakti.During 2016-17, ninety-nine (99) on-going Major/Medium irrigation projects under PMKSY- (AIBP), having ultimate irrigation potential of 76.03 lakh ha have been prioritized in consultation with States, for completion in phases up to December, 2019 along with their Command Area Development & Water Management (CADWM) works.  State wise details of these 99 projects is given at Annexure-I.    State wise Central Assistance released under the components of PMKSY including Karnataka is given at Annexure II to IV.The Ministry of Jal Shakti initiated a new Dam Rehabilitation and Improvement Project (DRIP) phase-II which is a State Sector scheme and central assistance from Union Government to the States is not envisaged under it.
