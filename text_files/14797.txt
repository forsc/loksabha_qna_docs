                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Daily Allowance and Dearness Relief




 Kunhalikutty P.K.














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Government of India Ministry of FinanceDepartment of ExpenditureLok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3619To be answered on Monday, 16th  March, 2020Phalguna 26, 1941(Saka) Increase/decrease in DA/DR3619: Shri P.K. Kunhalikutty: Will the Minister of Finance be pleased to state:(a)	Whether the Daily Allowance (DA) and Dearness Relief (DR) for Central Govt. employees and pensioners have become due with effect from 1st January, 2020;(b)	If so, the details thereof; and (c)	The quantum of DA/DR that has become due to Central Government employees and the time by when this increased amount will be disbursed to them? 






ANSWER




                                                        AnswerMinister of State in the Ministry of Finance(Shri Anurag Thakur)(a), (b) & (c): Yes Sir. Dearness Allowance and Dearness Relief are granted to serving employees and pensioners of the Central Government respectively each year with effect from 1st January and 1st July and normally paid in the month of March and September respectively. The rate of DA/DR will be announced after the approval of the Cabinet.                                           ****
