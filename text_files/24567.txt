                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

447






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Aid to Farmers during COVID Crisis




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the numbers of farmers who benefitted from the Central Government schemes during the COVID-19 crisis, State-wise;  (b)	the number of farmers left without any help from the Government as on date, State-wise; and (c)	the system of calculation based for allotment of benefits to farmers by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) Number of farmers benefitted under major beneficiary oriented schemes of the Department of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare are given in the Annexure.(b)   Agriculture is a State subject. However, Government of India is implementing a number of Schemes, both Central Sector and Centrally Sponsored for the benefit of farmers and the agriculture sector as a whole. Beneficiaries are selected by State Governments and therefore, such details are maintained by them.  (c)   There is no uniform criterion adopted for selection of beneficiaries and this may differ from scheme to scheme. However, landholding is taken as the eligibility criterion for most schemes of the Department
