                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

262






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






FCI Procurement of Wheat




P. Velusamy














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total quantum of procurement of wheat by the Food Corporation of India (FCI) for public distribution in the last three years and the current year; (b) 	the details of actual dues to FCI from the Government on account of subsidy to be released and the reasons for the delay; (c) 	whether the Government has received any request from FCI for raising the capital of Government, if so, the action taken so far and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the total borrowings of FCI as on date and the amount of loan capital?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	The total procurement of wheat in central pool by Food Corporation of India (FCI), State Government and its agencies during last three years and current year is as under:Rabi Marketing Season (RMS)	Quantity (in LMT)2016-17	229.612017-18	308.242018-19	357.952019-20	341.32......2/--2-(b): 	The outstanding subsidy due to be released to FCI as on 31.03.2019 was Rs. 1,86,171 crores. For Financial Year  2019-20, Budget Estimate for Food Subsidy to  FCI was Rs.1,51,000 crore and  Rs. 75,000 crore in Revised Estimate . Funds for food subsidy are released to FCI in time as per the monthly expenditure plan/quarterly expenditure plan as per available budget allocation subject to approval of Ministry of Finance as and when required.(c):  In Budget Speech 2018-19, vide Para 120, it was announced to restructure the capital of Food Corporation of India through equity enhancement. Accordingly, Rs. 500 crore was released on 22.03.2019 (FY 2018-19) and Rs. 1000 crore has been released on 24.01.2020 (FY 2019-20).(d):	The total borrowings of FCI as on 27.01.2020 and amount of loan capital are as under:-Particulars	Borrowing Amount (Rupees in crore )Cash Credit Limit (CCL)	6084.12Short term loan(STL)	15,800National Small Saving Fund (NSSF) loan	1,91,000Bonds	29,000Total	2,41,884.12****
