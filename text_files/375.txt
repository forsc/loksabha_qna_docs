                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Promotion of Eco-Tourism




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the schemes launched by the Government to promote eco-tourism in the country; (b) 	whether any funds have been allocated for such tourism projects; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	The Ministry of Tourism launched the Swadesh Darshan scheme in the year 2014-15 for Integrated Development of Theme-based Tourist Circuits in the country. Eco- circuit is one of the fifteen thematic circuits identified for development under the scheme. The Ministry has also identified Eco-tourism as one of the Niche Tourism areas for development in the country.For promotion of various tourism destinations and products in the country including eco-tourism, the Ministry produces publicity material and collaterals in English and foreign languages and releases print, electronic and online media campaigns in important domestic and international markets under the ‘Incredible India’ brand-line. Promotions are also undertaken through the website and Social Media accounts of the Ministry.(b)  and (c):	The details of projects sanctioned by the Ministry under the Eco-circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan scheme are given at the Annexure.*******
