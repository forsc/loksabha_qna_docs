                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3202






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Upgradation of SHs to NHs inUttar Pradesh




Jadon Chandra Sen














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of proposals received from the Government of Uttar Pradesh for upgradation/conversion of State Highways (SHs) into National Highways (NHs) during the last three years; (b) the number of proposals sanctioned till date along with the funds allocated/released for such projects; (c) whether any time limit has been fixed to complete all these projects; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) During the last three years, 37 proposals for upgradation/conversion of State Highways/State roads to National Highways, received from Government of Uttar Pradesh have been declared as new National Highways. 11 numbers of widening/Improvement works on these new NHs costing Rs. 395 crore have been sanctioned with completion period up to 1 year. *****
