                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






GST on Fertilizers and Pesticides




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated any plan to reduce GST on fertilizers and pesticides and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the use of chemicals in agriculture is the reason behind indebtedness of farmers and suicides committed by them and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government proposes to provide pesticides and fertilizers to small farmers free of cost under any scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR CHEMICALS & FERILIZERS(SHRI  BHAGWANTH KHUBA)(a):   No such proposals for reduction of GST on fertilizers and pesticides is under consideration of the Government. (b):	No such data is maintained. However, since pesticides are chemicals and inherently toxic in nature and are designed to kill pests, diseases and weeds, these are needed to be used in prescribed dosages and methodology mentioned on label and leaflets. Judicious use of pesticides does not pose any risk or hazard to human beings, animal & matter related therewith. (c):	There is no such scheme to provide fertilizers and pesticides to small farmers free of cost.  However, Government provides subsidized fertilizers to farmers across the country.                                   ***********
