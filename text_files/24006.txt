                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4951






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Repairing of Dilapidated NH-26




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the repair work on dilapidated stretch from Borigumma to Sunki via Jeypore, Koraput on NH-26 has started;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the details and the present status of work on Jeypore, Koraput bypass on NH-26; and(d) the time by which the said work is likely to be completed? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Capacity upgradation and/or rehabilitation of Borigumma to Sunki stretch of NH-26 has been taken up in three packages as under:Widening to two lane with paved shoulder from km 319.280 to 345.480 km (Boriguma to Jeypore) on NH-26 is in progress with present physical progress of 38% and scheduled date of completion of 07.05.2021. Periodic Renewal work from km 345.480 to km 351.480 (6.00km) and km 367.480 to 376.480 km (9.00km) has been completed in 2019-20 and the stretch is in good condition. Strengthening from km 351.480 to km 367.480 & km 376.480 to km 434.767 (Koraput to Sunki Ghat) is in progress with physical progress of 32% and scheduled date of completion of 18.07.2021.	 (c) & (d)	Alignments of Jeypore bypass and Koraput bypass have been approved and the process of Land Acquisition is in various stages. The work can be taken up only after completion of land acquisition depending on availability of resources, traffic levels and inter-se priority. *****
