                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

14






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






PRABANDH PORTAL




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched PRABANDH portal to compile data of out-of-school children in the age group of 6-14 years and those belonging to socially and economically disadvantaged group;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has planned to provide financial assistance for the first time in the current session 2021-22 for out of school children in 16-18 years of age group to continue their education through open/ distance learning mode;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Government has any plan to ensure continuation of education of orphaned children during COVID pandemic;(f) if so, the steps being taken in this regard; and(g) whether the Government has cancelled 12th Board Exams for 2021 amidst Covid-19 pandemic and if so, the details thereof including proposed evaluation criteria?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): Yes. This Department has developed an online module for compiling the data of Out of School Children (OoSC) identified by each State/UT and their mapping with Special Training Centres (STC) on the PRABANDH Portal (http://samagrashiksha.in). The concerned State/UT validates the child wise information of the identified OoSC and STC uploaded by the concerned Block Resource Centre of the State for monitoring the progress of mainstreaming of OoSC.(c) & (d): Under Samagra Shiksha, for the first time in 2021-22, financial assistance upto Rs. 2000 per annum has been envisioned for supporting Out of School Children of age group of 16-19 for completing their education through NIOS/SIOS, for accessing course materials and certification.(e) & (f): This Department and Ministry of Women & Child Development have issued a joint D.O. letter No. 13-10/2021-IS-11 dated 16.06.2021 to facilitate education for children orphaned due to COVID-19 by bringing them under the fold of the various schemes run by Department of School Education & Literacy, and outlining the role of teachers, District Education Officers and Child Welfare Committees.(g): Yes. The Evaluation criteria is available at the website of CBSE. The link for the same is https://www.cbse.gov.in/cbsenew/documents/Tabulation%20Policy%20Class%20XII%202021.pdf.*****
