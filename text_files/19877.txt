                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1290






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






KCC Issued in Odisha




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of Kisan Credit Cards (KCC) issued under the scheme till date in the state of Odisha; (b)	the target fixed for issue of number of Kisan Credit Cards in the previous two years ending December, 2020; (c) 	the total number of complaints received by the Government regarding non-issue of Kisan Credit Cards to the eligible farmers during FY 2018-19 in the state of Odisha; and (d) 	the action taken by the Government in this regard and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The total number of KCC issued under the scheme in the State of Odisha till October, 2020 is 41.62 lakh.(b):	The aim of Government is universal coverage of all farm households under KCC.  In the first instance, the target under KCC saturation drive is to cover all PM Kisan beneficiaries.(c) & (d):	Kisan Credit Card is issued by Scheduled Commercial Banks, Cooperative Banks and Regional Rural Banks for which the nodal agencies are RBI and NABARD respectively.  Complaints for non-issuance of KCC are received by the RBI and NABARD and are forwarded to concerned banks for redressal.  However, such data is not centrally maintained by them. The Government has fixed a time line of 15 days for issue of KCC on the receipt of the completed applications for KCC.*****
