                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3682






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Drugs Seized by NCB




Pradyut Bordoloi













                                                    Srinivas Kesineni
                                                








                                                    Maneka Sanjay Gandhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is cognizant of the Supreme Court’s observation in 2016 of the low percentage of destruction/disposal of seized narcotics, if so, the details of measures taken to address the same;(b) the details of the quantity of drugs seized by the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) between 2014 and 2021, State-wise;(c) the details of disposal of quantity of drugs seized by the NCB during the said period, State-wise;(d) the total quantity of drugs seized and total quantity of drugs destroyed during 2019, 2020 and 2021, year-wise; and(e) the details and reasons for which the NCB has not updated its monthly Drug Situation Report (DSR) since March 2019, and the time by which the reports of pending months are likely to be made available to the public?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (c):	Disposal of seized narcotic drugs is done as per Section 52A of the Narcotic Drugs & Psychotropic Substances (NDPS) Act, 1985, and in consonance with  Department  of Revenue  notification vide GSR 38(E), dated  16.01.2015, and observations issued by the Hon’ble Supreme Court vide order, dated 28.01.2016, in the case of Union of India Vs Mohanlal & Anr. State-wise details of drugs disposed of by NCB between 2014 and 2021 (till Nov) are at Annexure –A. (b):	State-wise details of drugs seized by the NCB between 2014 and 2021 (till Nov) are at Annexure –B. (d):	The details of drugs seized and drugs destroyed by the NCB for 2019, 2020 & 2021 are at Annexure –C.	(e):	The monthly Drug Situation Report was uploaded on NCB website till March 2019 and the same was discontinued as the data is being captured in the Annual Report.  The Annual Reports for the year 2019 and 2020 will be published shortly.******
