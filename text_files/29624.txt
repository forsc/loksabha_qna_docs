                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1265






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Budget Allocation for SSA and MDMS




Pramila Bisoyi














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the budget allocated and utilisation reported by States under SamagraShiksha Abhiyan (SSA) since 2018-19, State/year-wise;(b) the details of funds released against the demand from States since 2018-19,particularly; and(c) the details of the budgetary allocation and utilisation under MDM since 2019-20 andapproved funds under Mid Day Meal Scheme for 2021-22, State/year-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) & (b): State/UT-wise details of the Central share released and expenditure incurred(including State share) under the scheme of Samagra Shiksha since 2018-19 is given atAnnexure-I.(c): State/UT-wise details of the Central assistance allocated and utilisation under Mid-Day Meal Scheme since 2019-20 and Central assistance approved for 2021-22 is given atAnnexure-II.*****
