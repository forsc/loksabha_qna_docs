                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4479






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Poshan Abhiyaan




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    whether the Ministry has identified only four districts for implementation of Poshan Abhiyaan in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has noted that instead of selecting only four districts in two States, selecting, at least, one district in each State to implement Poshan Abhiyaan on pilot basis for better understanding the ground realities in each States is more appropriate since Ministry has already conducted survey in 27 districts of the country; (c)	 if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; and (d) 	whether it is true that earlier Ministry proposed to start this on pilot basis in 10 districts and if so, the reasons for reducing it to only four districts?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	No Sir. All the districts of States/UTs have been covered in the POSHAN Abhiyaan earlier known as National Nutrition Mission. West Bengal has not yet started implementation of the POSHAN Abhiyaan. (b)	to (d) Does not arise.*****
