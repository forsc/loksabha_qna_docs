                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

332






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Celebration of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsava




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of programmes organized by the Government in pursuit of celebration of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsava; and(b)	the details of the programmes proposed to be organized during the course of celebration of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav in the days ahead?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a) & (b)	Celebrations of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav commenced on 12.03.2021 starting a 75 week countdown to 75th anniversary of our country’s Independence and will end post a year on 15th August, 2023. It is an intensive, countrywide campaign focusing on citizen participation as Jan Andolan with a whole-of-Government approach with large number of events being organized by the Central Government, State Governments, etc., in various parts of the country. The Ministry of Culture, which is the nodal ministry for celebrations of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, has informed that up to 24th November, 2021, 6714 programmes have been organized by the various Ministries of the Government of India under the Mahotsav.*****
