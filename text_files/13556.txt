                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2395






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






E-Highway on Delhi-Mumbai Section




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to construct an electric-highway between Delhi and Mumbai;(b) 	if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c) 	the estimated cost thereof and the time by which the work on this highway is likely to be completed;(d) 	whether the Government is collaborating with other countries for technical support for construction of this highway and if so, the details thereof; and(e)  	the number  of  electric  charging  stations established by the Government till now, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e) No such project for construction of an Electric-Highway between Delhi and Mumbai has been taken up.*****
