                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

67






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Storage of Edible Oil




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government has asked the States to provide the details of storage of oil seeds and edible oils with the mill owners and stockists; (b) 	if so, the steps being taken to check irregularities in the hoarding of edible oils and bringing transparency in the availability thereof; (c) 	the measures being taken by the Ministry to ensure that the oil seeds and oils of Gujarat are mainly used in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b): 	In order to check hoarding and bring transparency in the availability of edible oils and oilseeds, the Union Government has imposed stock limits on Edible Oils and Oilseeds for a period upto 31st March, 2022. The Removal of Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions on Specified Foodstuffs (Amendment) Order, 2021 has been issued w.e.f.  8th October, 2021.  (c): 	Under the said Order, the States/Union Territories have been entrusted to impose stock limit on edible oils and oilseeds on the basis of available stock and consumption pattern of the State/Union Territory with  certain exemptions. This Order has been issued to ensure the smooth availability of edible oils and oilseeds in the country.*****
