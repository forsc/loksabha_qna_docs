                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1779






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Priority in Budget for Tourism Development




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has accorded priority to tourism development in the budget 2020-21; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the details of benefited area?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a): 	Yes, Sir.(b): 	The Government has earmarked an allocation of Rs.2499.83 crores in the tourism sector. The allocation for different activities is as under:<pre>Rs. In croresName of the Scheme	BE 2020-21Asstt. to Central Agencies for Tourism Infrastructure	80.00Viability Gap Scheme for Revenue Generating Tourism Projects	30.00PIDDC	5.00Swadesh Darshan	1200.00PRASHAD	207.55Capacity Building for Service  Provider	60.00Asstt. To IHMs/FCI/IITTM/ NIWS/NCHMCT	70.00Domestic Promotion & Publicity including Hospitality	140.00Overseas Promotion and Publicity including MDA	450.00Market Research	9.66Champion Services Sector Scheme	123.00Total (Scheme)	2375.21Establishment Expenditure	124.62Grand Total	2499.83</pre>******
