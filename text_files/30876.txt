                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2342






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Zero Buffer Zones




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of draft and final notifications released with respect to Ecologically Sensitive Zones across the nation for the last three years;(b) 	the State-wise details of amendments proposed to these notifications in this regard;(c) 	whether the Government has received any such proposal/amendment  submitted  by  the  Government  of Kerala in this regard;(d) whether the  Government  has  received  any proposal from any State Government including Kerala for zero buffer zones; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) The Ministry of Environment, Forest & Climate Change have issued 161 Final Eco-sensitive Zone (ESZ) notifications covering 176 Protected Areas and 176 Draft/Re-draft notifications covering 202 Protected Areas around the Protected Areas across the nation in the last three years and the current year upto30thNovember 2021 The detail of the notifications is given in Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.(b) Amendments were made in respect of five Final Eco-sensitive Zone notifications mentioned in part (a) during the last three years. The ESZ notifications state-wise wherein amendments have been made are given in Annexure-III.(c) The Ministry of Environment, Forest & Climate Change have received 23 proposals for publication of draft Eco-sensitive Zone notifications from the State Government of Kerala. The draft notifications published could not be finalised due to non submission of requisite information by State Government, except in respect of ESZ around Mathikettan Wildlife Sanctuary, which was issued vide S.O. 4729(E), dated 28.12.2020. The State Government of Kerala has sought revision in respect of 19 ESZ proposals. (d) & (e) The Ministry have received ESZ proposals with zero ESZ extent from five State Governments. These proposals have been examined and revised proposals from the respective State Governments have been sought, except in respect of one proposal from the Union Territory of Andaman and Nicobar.******
