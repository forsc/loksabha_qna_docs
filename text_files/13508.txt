                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Revival of Mining Operation




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has received any proposals from the States especially Maharashtra for the revival of mining operation in their areas to restore livelihood of the common people; and (b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)	& (b) No, Sir. No such proposal from Government of Maharashtra has been received in the Ministry of Mines.*****
