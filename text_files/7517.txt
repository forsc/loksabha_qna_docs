                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1314






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Development  of Tourism  in Bundelkhand




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the proposed Central schemes for tourism in the Bundelkhand region which has been the symbol of courage and valour of Chandela rulers of Mahoba and centre of action of Maharani Laxmibai of Jhansi, etc.; (b) 	the schemes of the Ministry to sensitize the tourists visiting Bundelkhand to the architecture of temples located therein and the folk art, culture and literature, diffused in the entire region; and (c) 	the details of the action taken/being taken on the pending proposals sent by the Government of Uttar Pradesh for development of tourism in Bundelkhand region?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. The projects under these schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. The Ministry also provides financial assistance to State Governments/UT Administrations for organizing Fairs, Festivals and Tourism related Events which have the potential for promoting Tourism.
