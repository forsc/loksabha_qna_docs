                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3986






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Grace Marks to Athletes in Government Service




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of grace marks awarded to the athletes in Government service participating in State and National Level meets;(b) whether the Government has noticed that a number of athletes are still unemployed even after their very good performances in the meets; and(c) if so, the steps taken to accommodate such athletes in Government service?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (c): All Ministries/Departments including Department of Sports follow the instructions of Department of Personnel & Training (DoP&T) issued from time to time for providing incentives viz. recruitment, promotion, increments, etc. to sportspersons. The consolidated instructions of DoP&T is available on public domain at http://documents.doptcirculars.nic.in/D2/DO2est/14034 01 2013-Estt.D-03102013-A.pdf. However, these instructions are not binding for States/UTs’ governments.As per these instructions, there is no provision for awarding grace marks to sportspersons on the basis of their performances in sports competitions. However, upto 5 percent reservation is provided in direct recruitment in Group ‘C’ posts for meritorious sportspersons.Besides, Sports Authority of India recruits Olympian/Para Olympians as Coach and Assistant Coach. Institutions under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports namely National Sports University, Imphal, Netaji Subhash National Institute of Sports, Lakshmibai National Institute of Physical Education and Lakshmibai National College of Physical Education offer academic programmes in sports coaching, physical education, sports and allied fields to enable sportspersons to pursue career in sports.*****
