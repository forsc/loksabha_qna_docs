                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4459






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Upgradation of Ayush Hospitals




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is contemplating any scheme in the coming years for establishment/upgradation of Government Ayush college under Ayush Mission; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (c) whether the Government proposes/considering any proposal to construct new building for Ayush colleges/ dispensaries; and (d) if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (d) Public Health being State subject, establishment/upgradation of Government AYUSH colleges/ dispensaries comes under purview of State/UT Governments. However, Central Government is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) through States/UTs under which there is provision of financial assistance to States/UTs for setting up of new AYUSH educational Institutions in the States where it is not available in Government sector, upgradation of AYUSH Under Graduate/Post Graduate educational Institutions and upgradation of AYUSH dispensaries. State/UT Government may avail eligible financial assistance by projecting the same through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) as per NAM guideline.           The State/UT-wise number of unit approved, as per proposal received from States/UTs, for setting up of new AYUSH educational Institutions, upgradation of AYUSH Under Graduate/Post Graduate educational Institutions and upgradation of AYUSH dispensaries are furnished at Annexure.……………..
