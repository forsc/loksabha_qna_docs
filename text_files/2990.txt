                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2803






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Electrified Railway Network in North East Region




Pratima Bhoumik














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government had set any target and length in kms. of the new broad gauge rail network as well as electrification of existing network in the North East States of Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Manipur, Meghalaya, Assam, Tripura and Mizoram; (b)	if so, the details thereof during the last three years, year and State-wise;(c)	whether the Government has plans to run electric trains in the existing electrified tracks connecting these 7 States;(d)	if so, the details in this regard;(e)	the details of difficulties being faced by the Government in achieving the target set; and (f)	the steps taken/proposed to be taken to overcome these difficulties?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
