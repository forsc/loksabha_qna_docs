                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2199






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Tax Collection from Maharashtra




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount collected through central taxes from Maharashtra by the Union Government during the last three years; (b) the funds allocated to Maharashtra during the last three years; (c) whether there has been an increase in funds allocation to Maharashtra after implementation of Goods and Services Tax; and (d) if so, the details thereof along with the response of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINSTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a): The amount collected through Central Taxes from Maharashtra by the Union Government during the last three years is as under:Direct Taxes                          (Rs. in crore)Financial Year	Direct Tax2016-17	3,14,056.272017-18	3,84,277.532018-19	4,25,390.84 Goods and Services Tax                                                                                           (Rs. in crore)Financial Year	Total  2017-18 (July-March)	68,7902018-19	1,13,3502019-20 (April to October)	69,875        The State/UT-wise data on collection of Non-GST Indirect Taxes is not maintained.(b) to (d):  The following funds have been allocated to Maharashtra during the last three years.(Rs. In crore)Particulars 	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Devolution towards State’s share 	19,141.92	21,143.64	23,906.13Grant-in-Aid from Central Government	21,652.58	21,822.93	38,467.71*Total	40,794.50	42,966.57	62,373.84        *RE            *****
