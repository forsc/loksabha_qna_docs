                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

383






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Welfare Schemes under MGNREGS




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of the welfare scheme of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) since 2014 until 2021?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):	The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) is an Act to provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.Under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS), the year-wise details of persondays generated, percentage of women participation, persons provided employment, completed works and percentage  of expenditure on agriculture & allied works since financial year 2014-15 to 2021-22 (as on 15.07.2021) are given at  Annexure.						*****
