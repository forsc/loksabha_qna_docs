                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4038






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Expansion of Salem Airport




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has begun the land acquisition for expansion of the Salem Airport, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has any plan to utilise the unused 1500 acres land of Salem Steel Plant; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Generally, land for airport projects is provided by concerned State Government/ Union Territory (UT) Administration to Airports Authority of India (AAI) free of cost and free from all encumbrances. For development/expansion of Salem Airport, AAI has projected a land requirement of 446 acre to the State Government of Tamil Nadu.;(b): No, Sir. no such proposal is under consideration in Ministry of Civil Aviation/AAI at present.;(c): Does not arise.
