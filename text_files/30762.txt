                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2311






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Countervailing Duties on Import of Steel Products




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has suspended the countervailing duties on import of stainless-steel flat products from Indonesia and/or China in Budget 2021- 22 till September, 2021 and has now extended the suspension for another five months and if so, the reasons therefor;(b)  whether it is a fact that this has been done despite reports of unfair trade practices of these countries including non WTO-compliant subsidies and if so, the reasons therefor; (c) whether it is also a fact that due to this, there was a 115 per cent surge in import of steel during April - September, 2021 on YoY basis and if so, the details thereof;(d the monthly details of steel imports including country-of-origin during the last five years including 2021; and (e) the details of domestic demand, production capacity and unutilised production capacity and import of steel during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) and (b):  In view of sharp rise in prices of Steel products, countervailing duties on Stainless Steel Flat Products were temporarily revoked till 30th September 2021 in the Union Budget 2021-22. This revocation was further extended upto 31st January 2022  (c) and (d) : The monthly details of the steel imports including country-of-origin for the last five years, including the current year 2021 are attached in the annexure.(e):  Data on overall consumption of total finished steel in India during last five years is given below: -Total Finished Steel (alloy/stainless + non-alloy) ConsumptionYear	Qty (mt)  2016-17	84.04  2017-18	90.71  2018-19	98.71  2019-20	100.17  2020-21	94.89                                                                 Source: JPCData on capacity, production and capacity utilization of crude steel in India during last five years is given below: -Year	Crude steel	Capacity (mt)	Production (mt)	Capacity utilization (%)2016-17	128.28	97.94	762017-18	137.98	103.13	752018-19	142.24	110.92	782019-20	142.3	109.14	772020-21	143.91	103.54	72Source: JPC                                                                                                Data on overall import of total finished steel by India during last five years is given below: Total Finished Steel (alloy/stainless + non-alloy) ImportYear	Qty (mt)2016-17	7.222017-18	7.482018-19	7.842019-20	6.772020-21	4.75Source: JPC                                                                                            ******
