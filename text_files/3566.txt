                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Environment Clearance for Projects




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has imposed certain standards/conditions for the projects seeking environment clearance in the past three years;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	 the steps taken by the Government to bring uniformity for the projects seeking environment clearance?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c) In order to bring uniformity and expede the appraisal process of Environmental Clearance without compromising environmental norms and the rigor of environment impact assessment, the Ministry has issued Standard Environmental Clearance conditions for various sectors vide OMs no. F. No. 22-34/2018-IA.III dated 9th August 2018, 4th January 2019 and 8th January 2019. The standard Environment Clearance conditions are considered by the concerned Expert Appraisal Committee with due diligence while recommending the Environmental Clearance. The expert appraisal committee can modify or prescribe additional conditions based on the project specific requirements.***
