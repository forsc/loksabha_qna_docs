                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

192






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Distribution of Fertilizers in Bundelkhand




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state :(a)	whether the Government has taken cognizance of the shortage of fertilizers in the Bundelkhand region of Uttar Pradesh;(b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Union Government together with the Government of Uttar Pradesh has taken steps to address the issues related to the distribution of fertilizers in Bundelkhand; and(d)	If so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS                         (DR.  MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	to (d):    A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 192* FOR 10.12.2021 REGARDING “DISTRIBUTION OF FERTILIZERS IN BUNDELKHAND” TABLED BY SHRI KUNWAR PUSHPENDRA SINGH CHANDEL.                                          ----------(a) & (b):   Sir, there is no shortage of fertilizers in Bundelkhand region of Uttar    Pradesh.  The same has been confirmed by the State Government also. (c)	& (d):    Yes Sir, Union Government together with the Government of Uttar Pradesh has taken all necessary steps to ensure comfortable availability of fertilizers in the State including Bundelkhand region. In Bundelkhand region i.e. Jhansi, Lalitpur, Jalaun, Hamirpur, Mahoba, Banda and Chitrakoot of UP, fertilizer availability during the ongoing Rabi Season 2021-22 (from 01st October 2021 to 6th December 2021) is comfortable as follows:                                                             (Figures: In MT)S.No.	Product	  Requirement	Availability	Sales	Closing Stock1	UREA	   90279	128438	        87169	   412692	DAP	    99279	156870	       149200	    76703	MOP	      479	   532	          319	     2134	NPKS	      1573	   4129	          3819	     310Further, following steps have been taken by the Department of fertilizers to ensure comfortable availability of Fertilizers in all the States including Bundelkhand region of Uttar Pradesh during ongoing Rabi 2021-22 season:-i.	An attempt has been made to assess the field requirement in consultation with the states and accordingly, the district wise micro planning has been done which is being executed on daily basis.ii.	24x7 interactions with the state governments, companies and railways have been established through web platforms/control room.iii.	The stake holders viz., manufacturers, ports, railways have been sensitized to operate on maximum capacity to produce load, and move the fertilizers to the targeted destinations.iv.	State Governments are impressed upon to use i-FMS dashboard stock figures for district wise stock availability and suitable interventions. Same source is used by DoF so as to be on board with states and closely oversee that the recommended movement for each district/ rake point.v.	The loading and voyage time of imported fertilizer shipments have been monitored on daily basis and required suitable interventions have been made from time to time. vi.	Every day there is a close monitoring 24x7 and regular interaction with states, fertilizer companies and railway board. As 90% of the fertilizers are moved through railway rakes, so the loading and movement of these rakes is being executed in a very targeted manner (in consultation with respective state governments), so as to address the field demand suitably.                                           ********
