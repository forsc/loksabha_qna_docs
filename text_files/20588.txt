                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1700






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






AMRUT in Darjeeling and Kalimpong




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of projects under AMRUT Yojana currently ongoing 	in the districts of Darjeeling and Kalimpong;(b) 	the details of funds allocated for the projects; and(c) 	the status of the projects and the estimate timeline for 	completion?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Darjeeling and Siliguri towns of Darjeeling district are covered under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) Scheme.  No town of Kalimpong district has been covered under AMRUT. Details of projects taken up under AMRUT in the  towns of Darjeeling and Siliguri by the Government of West Bengal are given as Annexure – I.(b)	Under AMRUT, outlays have been allocated State-wise and not city-wise.  Cost of projects taken up under AMRUT in the  towns of Darjeeling and Siliguri are given in Annexure – I.  (c).	Status of projects taken up under AMRUT in the towns of Darjeeling and Siliguri are given in Annexure – I. As per information provided by the State of West Bengal on AMRUT portal, out of 18 projects taken up in Darjeeling and Siliguri, 14 projects have already been completed. Remaining grounded projects are to be completed during the year 2021.                                                     *****
