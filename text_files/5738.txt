                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5313






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






NH Projects in Jharkhand




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the length of National Highway (NH) projects started and completed by the Union Government in Jharkhand during the last five years; and (b) the quantum of funds utilised thereon especially for Chatra, Latehar and Palamu districts of Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The length of National Highways (NH) projects startedand completed by the Union Government in Jharkhand during the last five years are 701.25 km and 472.78km respectively.(b) The quantum of funds utilized in the state of Jharkhand thereon is Rs 3881.59 crore. The quantum of funds utilized in the districts of Chatra, Latehar and Palamu districts is Rs. 227.42 crore.*****
