                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2205






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






LPG Connections in UP




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of LPG gas consumers in terms of percentage of total population of thecountry, State/ UT-wise;(b) whether there is acute shortage of LPG cylinders in Uttar Pradesh;(c) the steps being taken by the Government to enhance the number of LPG distributors to meetthe demand of LPG cylinders; and(d) whether the Government proposes to provide LPG connection to 70 per cent families in UttarPradesh by the year 2021?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) As on 01.07.2021, there are 29.11 crore domestic LPG consumers across the countrycovering 99.99% of estimated households. State-wise details are at Annexure.(b) As on 01.07.2021, the LPG refill booking pendency is less than 1 day in the State of UttarPradesh. Further, Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have reported that they endeavour to meetthe genuine demand of customers. However, sometimes delay in supplies occurs for thereasons beyond control viz. natural calamities, strikes, absenteeism, transport breakdowns,shortage of product availability, occupancy of jetty at ports etc. In such scenario, all out effortsare made by OMCs for meeting the demand of LPG by augmenting bulk LPG supplies,arranging additional imports, positioning of bulk at the plants through alternate routes andoperation of LPG bottling plants on Sundays/Holidays and in extended hours.(c) Appointment of LPG distributorships is a continuous process and locations for setting up ofLPG distributorships are identified based on sale potential that makes them commercially viable.As on 01.07.2021, there are 25116 LPG distributorships across the country including 4128 inthe State of Uttar Pradesh.(d) Release of LPG connections is a continuous process. LPG distributors are underinstructions to immediately register any request for new LPG connection. As on 01.07.2021,there are 4.25 crore domestic LPG customer in Uttar Pradesh. To ease out the process ofissuance of new connection, the prospective LPG customers have been given option to registerfor new connections on the Mobile App/Portal of the concerned OMC. Over the counter NonsubsidisedDomestic connections have been made available. Provision for secondaryconnections have also been made available for those primary connection holder, who havemoved from their native place of residence to another place. The facility of Online Transfer ofLPG connection to another distributor serving in the same area has also been provided to LPGcustomers through respective OMC’s web-portals as well as their Mobile Apps.
