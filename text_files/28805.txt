                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

433






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Naxal Encounter in Maharashtra




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Naxals were killed in an encounter in Gadchiroli district, Maharashtra recently, if so, the details thereof during the last five years and the current year, till date and the reasons therefor, State wise;(b) the number of naxals/police killed, injured in such encounters State-wise;(c) the number of fake encounters reported/along with the action taken thereon; and(d) the corrective steps being taken by the Government to stop such fake encounters?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)& (b): 27 Left Wing Extremists (LWEs), including Milind Teltumbde, Member, Central Committee of CPI (Maoist), were killed in an exchange of fire (EoF)with Police under the jurisdiction of Police Station Korchi, district Gadchiroli, Maharashtra on 13.11.2021. Following the EoF, the Police recovered 29 weapons, including AK-47 Rifle–06, Underbarrel Grenade Launcher–01, SLR Rifle–09, INSAS Rifle–01, and .303 Rifle–03.--2--LS.US.Q.NO. 433 FOR 30.11.2021The number of LWEs killed during the exchange of fire with security forces and security forces killed, injured during the last 05 years and the current year, State-wise, is given at Annexure I.(c)& (d):No case of fake encounter in the LWE affected States has been reported.A strong constitutional and legal mechanism exists, which include National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) and State Human Rights Commission (SHRC), to prevent and deal with the case of fake encounter. NHRC has issued guidelines for conducting magisterial enquiries in case of death of a person in police action. The Hon’ble Supreme Court of India has also laid down a detailed procedure to be followed in investigation of a case of death of a person during police encounter for thorough, effective and independent investigation. These mechanisms and rulings are in addition to the provisions given in the criminal laws for registration and fair investigation of an offence.
