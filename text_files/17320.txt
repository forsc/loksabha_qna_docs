                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

785






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Long Term Irrigation Fund




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that the funds borrowed from NABARD under the Long Term Irrigation Fund (LTIF) have been underutilized and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of the borrowings and utilisation of funds from NABARD during the last three years; (c) whether the Government is planning to reevaluate the allocation of funds through budget in order to ensure judicious use of monetary resources; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI  & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b)  The Central and State Share for the 99 prioritised irrigation projects (and 7 phases) is released under LTIF through NABARD from time to time as per requirements for both the Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP) works and Command Area Development and Water Management (CADWM) works under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana. In addition to these priority projects, Central Assistance is also released for Polavaram National Project, Shahpurkandi Dam Project and North Koel Project through NABARD.
