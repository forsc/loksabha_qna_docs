                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Modern Healthcare System




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)   whether the Government proposes to empower the citizens through the modern healthcare system;b)   if so, the details thereof;c)   the funds allocated and utilized for developing modern healthcare system in the country during the last three years;d)   whether the Government has any plan to provide health ID to every citizen; and e)   if so, the details thereof and the time by which said project will be implemented along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c) “Public Health & Hospitals” is a State subject. Government is providing technical and financial support to state government through various schemes including NHM (National Health Mission) to improve health Infrastructure and to provide accessible, affordable & quality health care to citizens.Government has initiated various schemes/programmes to supplement the efforts of the State/UT governments and to provide quality healthcare across the country. Details of the few schemes are given below:Ayushman Bharat Health & Wellness Centres:  Under this scheme, the States are supported for establishment of 1.5 lakh Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) across the country for providing comprehensive primary care related to preventive, promotive and curative. As on date 80466 Health & Wellness Centres have been operationalized. Ayushman Bharat, Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) provides health coverage up to Rs 5 Lakh per family per year to around 10.74 crore poor and vulnerable families as per Socio Economic Caste Census (SECC).Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM): With vision to create National Digital Health Ecosystem across the country and to enable seamless online platform, Hon’ble Prime Minister announced the launch of National Digital Health Mission (now known as Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission) on 15th August, 2020 in 6 Union Territories to facilitate the services like issue of Health ID, creation of registries for Doctors & health facilities and creation of personal health record etc. Further, on 27th September, 2021, Hon''ble Prime Minister announced the nationwide rollout of the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM).Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission (ABHIM) is aimed at strengthening crucial public health infrastructure in the country such as setting up of Integrated Public Health labs for disease diagnosis, upgradation and expansion of health infrastructure with focus on critical care and support to research institutions. Further, through eSanjeevani National Telemedicine Service, teleconsultations are enabled between doctors and including tele OPD services to ensure continuity of care and availability of health services to all citizens in the confine of their homes free of cost.Specifically, under NHM, financial support is provided to States/ UTs for setting up of new facilities and upgradation/ modernization of existing facilities based on the requirement posed by the states. State/UT wise details of funds released and utilised under NHM during the last three years i.e. F.Y. 2018-19 to 2020-21 is given at Annexure A.(d) & (e) Government has made provision for every citizen to create his/her health ID. Till 30 Nov 2021, a total of 14,07,06,627 Health IDs have been created. Creation of Health ID is voluntary. However, NHA is creating awareness among the citizens about the usages and advantages of Health ID so that maximum participation can be ensured.
