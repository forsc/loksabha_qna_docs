                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3454






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






R&D in CPSEs




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	  whether the Government had fixed any R&D targets to be achieved by CPSEs and if so, the details of CPSEs which have met those targets during the last five years; and (b)	 the details of the other steps taken by the Government to promote R&D activities in CPSEs?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(DR. BHAGWAT KISHANRAO KARAD)(a)  R&D (Research & Development) activities have been prescribed to Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) as per Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) guidelines of Department of Public Enterprises (DPE). The year-wise details of number of CPSEs in respect of which R&D parameters were stipulated along with corresponding achievements in last five years from 2015-16 to 2019-20 are provided below:Year	CPSEs given MoU targets on R&D	CPSEs which achieved MoU targets on R&D	Total no. of R&D parameters given 	No. of R&D parameters achieved 2015-16	48	44	174	1432016-17	86	75	111	992017-18	57	47	72	602018-19	65	56	104	902019-20	62	52	103	85(b) From the financial year 2021-22, the revised MoU framework for CPSEs prescribes expenditure on R&D / Innovations and Initiatives linked to the profit of the CPSE. ****
