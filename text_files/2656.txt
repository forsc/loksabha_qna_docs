                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2391






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Development of Tourist Spots in North Eastern Region




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the North-Eastern Region has remarkable tourist spots and has the potential for development to attract foreign tourists; (b) 	if so, the details of action taken by the Government for promotion of tourism and development of tourist spots in the North Eastern Region during the last three years; (c) 	whether the Government has received such proposals from State Government of Assam for promotion and development of areas as tourist spots during the last two years; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b):	Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned various projects in the North Eastern region to enhance tourist experience by developing/ upgrading tourism infrastructure, diversify tourism products, provide skilled manpower to tourism and hospitality industry and to promote prominent tourist spots in North Eastern Region through its schemes and programmes. The Government of India also promotes tourist destinations in the country including North Eastern Region through seminars, advertisements (print & electronic) fairs and exhibitions etc. North Eastern States are given complementary space at important travel marts/ exhibitions such as WTM London, ITB Berlin etc. Ministry of tourism under its hospitality scheme during 2018-19 invited/ hosted tour operators, travel agents, media etc. to cover important events in North Eastern Region.Year wise details of the important projects sanctioned to North-Eastern Region in last three years is given at Annexure.(c)  to (d):	The government of Assam had submitted two proposals for developing Spiritual  and North East Circuit under Swadesh Darshan scheme and forwarded   a proposal   of Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC) for tourist campus at Dheer Bil at Kokrajhar under the scheme Assistance to Central Agencies in last two years.  The proposals were however, either part of the already sanctioned projects or needed to be modified in accordance with the spirit of schemes. The State Government was informed accordingly.********
