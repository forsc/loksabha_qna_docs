                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






NARI Portal




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the ‘NARI Portal’ and ‘e-SAMVAD’ are playing important role in empowerment of needy women;(b)	if so, the details thereof the manner in which the said portals have helped women; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government to ensure country wide access of these portals?






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) to (c) : National Repository of Information For Women (NARI) Portal was launched on 2nd January 2018 to provide the information about the government schemes and legislations for women and to provide links to the Ministries, Departments and autonomous bodies offering these on one platform. The e-Samvad portal was launched to provide a platform for NGOs and the civil society for feedback, suggestions etc. Information about and links to various government schemes and benefits for women are also available on various other Government websites and online facilities, including the website of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, the UMANG app etc.  ***
