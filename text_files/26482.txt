                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2256






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Utilisation of funds under PMUY




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the features of the Ujjawala Scheme;(b) the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this scheme within TamilNadu during the last three years and the current year(c) the details of the number of cooking LPG connections provided under this scheme withinTamil Nadu so far; and(d) whether the Government is aware that this programme has failed within the country dueto the hiked price of cooking LPG, if so, the steps being taken by the Government in thisregard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) was started on 01.05.2016 and the target ofthe Scheme has been achieved in September, 2019. The Salient features of the PMUYScheme are as under :-? LPG connection is released in the name of adult woman of the poor family, subject tothe condition that no LPG connection exists in the name of any family member of thehousehold and fulfilling other terms and conditions.? Initially, the beneficiaries under PMUY were identified either from SECC list or fromseven other identified categories. Subsequently, Government extended the benefit toall left out poor families under PMUY subject to fulfilment of terms & conditions.? Under the Scheme, cash assistance of upto Rs 1600/- for providing new LPGconnection is provided by the Central Government.? The beneficiary is also provided an optional interest free loan to take Hot Plate orpurchase first refill or the both from Oil Marketing Companies(OMCs).(b) No State-wise target was fixed under PMUY. However, the Scheme was started with abudgetary support of ? 12,800/- crore and the Government has made payment to the tune of? 12,749.53 crore to OMCs against the connections released by OMCs.(c) OMCs have released total 32.38 lakh PMUY connections in the State of Tamil Naduunder the Scheme.(d) OMCs have reported that the average consumption of PMUY beneficiaries in the currentFinancial Year i.e. 2020-21 has increased by 44 % compared to Financial Year 2019-20.-------
