UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Financial Assistance to Unemployed Women Scientist




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        the assistance being provided by the Government to unemployed women scientists to make a career in the field of science and technology under the “Women Scientist Scheme”;(b)          the number of beneficiaries benefitted by this scheme so far, State/UT-wise; and(c)          the budgetary provision made in the present budget for this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND EARTH SCIENCES(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)??????? ?? ???????????? ??? ?????? ??????? ??  ????? ?????? (???????? ??????)  (??. ???????? ????)(a)  The ‘Women Scientist Scheme’ of the Department of Science and Technology provides various opportunities to unemployed women scientists and technologists, especially those who had break in career, to make a career in the field of Science and Technology. There are three major components of Women Scientist Scheme namely, i) Women Scientists Scheme-A (WOS-A) for conducting research in Basic & Applied Sciences, ii) Women Scientists Scheme-B (WOS-B) for S&T interventions for Societal Benefit and iii) Women Scientists Scheme-C (WOS-C) that enables them to become Intellectual Property Rights (IPRs) professional.  In WOS-A and WOS-B, the age limit is 27-57 years and fellowship amount for M.Sc, M.Phil/M.Tech and Ph.D categories is Rs. 31,000/-, Rs. 40,000/- and Rs. 55,000/- per month respectively and House Rent Allowance (HRA) as per Government of India norms. The total project cost, for 3 years’ duration under these three categories, is Rs. 20 lakh, Rs. 25 lakh and Rs. 30 lakh respectively excluding overheads and HRA.  However, in WOS-C, women scientists of age group 27-45 years are eligible to apply. The fellowship in WOS-C for M.Sc, M.Phil/M.Tech and Ph.D categories is Rs. 25,000/-, Rs. 30,000/- and Rs. 35,000/- per month respectively.(b)  Around 1000 women scientists have benefitted in last 3 years under the 3 components of Women Scientists Scheme.
