                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2643






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Allocation of Power from KKNPP




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Union Government has received request from State Government of Tamil Nadu to allocate the entire power generated from the Kudankulam Nuclear Power Plant (KKNPP) I & II;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the Government''s response thereto;(c)	whether the Union Government  has  expedited the process of establishment of the KKNPP unit 3 & 4 and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the proactive measures taken by the Union Government in this regard along with  the total funds allocated for  the said  projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)&(b)No, Sir.  Presently, there is no such request from State Government of Tamil Nadu, in respect of KKNPP I & II.  However, Ministry of Power has received a request to allocate the entire power generated from the Kudankulam Nuclear Power Plant (KKNPP) Unit – 3 & 4 from Shri P. Thangamani, Minister of Electricity, Prohibition and Excise, Government of Tamil Nadu vide D.O. Letter No.120/Planning/TANGEDCO/2018 dated 21.05.2018. Further, it is to intimate that the KKNPP Unit-3&4 are under construction and allocation order in respect of these Units is yet to be issued.  Allocation of power from individual project is made at appropriate time keeping in view the status of implementation of project.(c)&(d)Presently, construction activities are in full swing for KKNPP-3&4. The project has achieved an overall physical progress of 34.62% as of October 2019. The steps taken to expedite setting up of KKNPP-3&4 includes obtaining statutory clearances well in advance, signing/follow-up of contracts with Atomstroyexport (ASE) for supply of Long Manufacturing Cycle (LMC) equipment, First Priority Equipment(FPE), development of working documentation and tendering process for Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) packages for various equipment and systems. Constant monitoring of progress of activities is taken up at multiple levels in Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (NPCIL) and the Government to expedite the project. The sanctioned cost of KKNPP-3&4 is Rs. 39849 crore.  The plants are constructed with 70:30 debt-equity.*****
