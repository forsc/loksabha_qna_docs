                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

762






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Density of Hospitals




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has made any study with regard to the density of hospitals across the country, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government plans to establish more hospitals in the next three years and if so, the number of hospitals that have been planned along with the category of such hospitals; and(c) the number of such new hospitals being planned in Assam during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a):     As per information received, no study with regard to the density of hospitals has been carried out. However, there are standard guidelines under Indian Public Health (IPH) for setting up the hospitals in the State/UTs.(b) & (c):    Health being  a  State  subject,  the  primary  responsibility  of providing  health  care  and  hospital  services  to  its  citizens  is  that  of  the  State  Governments/ Union Territories. However, under National Health Mission (NHM), financial and technical support is provided to the State Governments (including Assam)/UTs to strengthen their health care system based on   the requirements posed by the States/UTs in their Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs). One All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) has been approved for Assam in Guwahati by Government of India.
