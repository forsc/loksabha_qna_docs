                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1045






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK)




S. Jothimani













                                                    Pradyut Bordoloi
                                                








                                                    Vincent H Pala
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the criteria adopted to select/choose pilot districts for implementing Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK);(b) whether the Government is planning to expand the number of districts and the number of blocks under each district to eventually cover all adolescents under Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK) similar to programmes under RMNCH, if so, the details thereof of all such districts including Karur district in Tamil Nadu;(c) whether there are any plans to intensify awareness generation of RKSK and Social and Behaviour Change Communication (SBCC) in order to improve health promotion among adolescents, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) the details of budget heads under Adolescent Health which are falling short in utilisation, along with details of national and State-wise figures thereof;(e) the details of the measures being taken to improve utilisation; and(f) the total sanctioned strength vis-a-vis existing strength for RKSK counsellors and the plans to address human resource challenges in areas with shortfall of personnel?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Government is implementing Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK) to address the need and concerns of more than 25 crore adolescents of the country.  RKSK is a paradigm shift from the existing clinic-based services to promotion and prevention and reaching adolescents in their own environment, such as in schools, families and communities. It covers interventions based on community, facility and school based interventions.To ensure good quality of implementation, the Peer Educator Programme was started as a pilot and districts were selected as per ranking of districts based on Composite Health Index and identified as High Priority Districts (HPDs). During the first phase, about 200 districts including Karur district of Tamil Nadu were selected by the States for implementation. Within, these districts, 50% of the blocks were covered for implementation of Peer Educator programme in entirety.		Government plans to saturate all the blocks in the selected Peer Educator districts first and then expand in remaining districts gradually based on proposals received from States in their Programme Implementation Plans.(c):	Government has undertaken awareness generation activities by developing communication material for mass media and mid media in the form of TV and radio spots, posters, leaflets and other interpersonal communication materials.Peer Educators have been identified and trained at village level to provide information to adolescents on key adolescent health issues and give impetus to the demand for Adolescent Health Services.(d) to (f):	The State/UT-wise details of funds approved and utilised during last 3 Years is at annexure. There are 1671 existing Adolescent Health counsellors against sanctioned post of 2036. In order to improve utilization and bridge the gap of shortfall of personnel, regular National and Regional level reviews, along with supportive supervision visits and video-conference with the States are undertaken
