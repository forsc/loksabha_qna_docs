                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3927






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Repairing of Silchar-Guwahati NH




Rajdeep Roy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the dilapidated condition of the National Highway (NH) connecting Silchar to Guwahati via Haflong;(b)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to repair and maintain the said NH; and(c)	the funds allocated and released  for the purpose?		






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) 	Yes Sir.  The National Highway section from Nrimbanglo to  Harangajai via Jatinga got damaged due to landslide, sinking & hill movement. Two no. of packages in this stretch viz. AS-22 (Nrimbanglo to Jatinga) & AS-21 (Jatinga to Harangajao) on NH-54 were foreclosed due to landslide, sinking & hill movement. Consultant has been engaged for preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) of 4 lane highway through new design/alignment in consultation with JICA (Japan Infrastructure Co-operation Agency). The existing stretches are being maintained by deploying maintenance Contractor.(b) & (c)  NHAI is regularly engaging maintenance Contractors to keep this section of NH in traffic worthy condition. The maintenance Contractors for the sections from Jagiroad to Maibong and from Nrimbanglo to Jatinga are in place and for section Jatinga to Harangajao, the procurement is in process. For maintenance of this National Highway, Rs. 71.13 Crore was allocated and  a total of Rs. 48.20 Crore has been released during last three years. *****
