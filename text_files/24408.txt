                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

264






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Lockdown impact of PMAY-G in Tamil Nadu




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the lockdown has impacted the progress of PMAY-G in the State of Tamil Nadu, if so, the details thereof; and(b) 	the number of houses constructed during the period March 2020- June 2021 in the State of Tamil Nadu, the details thereof, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	During the period of COVID-19 pandemic induced nation-wide lock down, all construction activities including construction of houses under PMAY-G had also been slowed down, retarding the pace of construction of PMAY-G houses and construction activities have resumed after relaxation of COVID-19 guidelines. However, due to timely provisioning of funds, regular review of progress at the level of Ministry   and commitment of the State Government, a total of 52,184 houses had been completed in Tamil Nadu State under the Rural Housing Scheme during Financial Year 2020-21.(b)	A total of 74,234 houses have been constructed during the period March 2020-June 2021 in the State of Tamil Nadu. The district-wise details are given in Annexure.*****
