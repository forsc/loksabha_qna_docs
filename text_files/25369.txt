                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Upgradation of PMKSY




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of components relating to the development of fruit and vegetable growers that have been included under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY);(b)	the provisions made under the said scheme in regard to financial assistance for setting up new units for creation/expansion of food processing and preservation capacities;(c)	whether any financial assistance has been provided for the development of new food processing and preservation units in Rajasthan under the said scheme; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PASHUPATI KUMAR PARAS)  (a) to (d): A statement is laid on the table of the House.**** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *112 ANSWERED ON 27.07.2021 REGARDING “UPGRADATION OF PMKSY”.(a) : The component schemes under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) are (i) Mega Food Parks, (ii) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, (iii) Infrastructure for Agro-processing Clusters, (iv) Creation of Backward and Forward Linkages (v) Creation / Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities, (vi) Food Safety and Quality Assurance Infrastructure, (vii) Human Resources and Institutions and (viii) Operation Greens. The schemes provide financial assistance for creation of modern infrastructure, efficient supply chain and setting up manufacturing units for preservation and processing of fruits, vegetables and other agro produce in the country. (b): The scheme of Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) provides grants-in-aid for setting up units @ 35% of eligible project cost in general areas and @50% of eligible project cost in the North East States including Sikkim and difficult areas namely Himalayan States/UTs (i.e. Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh and Uttarakhand), State notified ITDP areas & Islands subject to maximum of Rs. 5.00 crore per project. (c) & (d): Yes Sir. Ministry has so far approved 12 food processing and preservation units in Rajasthan under CEFPPC Scheme. The details are at Annexure. ****
