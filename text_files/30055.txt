                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1615






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Gati Shakti Yojana




Kuldeep Rai Sharma













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Rattan Lal Kataria
                                                








                                                    Kripanath Mallah
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched the Gati Shakti Yojana for Multi Modal Connectivity and improvement of infrastructure in various economic zones to increase the working capacity of industries and local manufacturers;(b) 	if so, the details, aims and objectives thereof along with the details of challenges faced and the steps taken by the Government to achieve the objective of the scheme and achieve India’s five million economy goal;(c) 	whether the Government has fixed any target to increase the cargo capacity at ports under the scheme and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether the Government has planned to launch the Gati Shakti Yojana across the country and if so, the number and details of States likely to be covered under the scheme, particularly Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH) (a) to (d):    	Yes, Sir.  PM GatiShakti- National Master Plan (NMP) would be an integrated plan depicting the economic zones and the multimodal connectivity infrastructure with an objective to holistically integrate the interventions of various Ministries / Departments and address missing gaps to ensure seamless movement of people, goods & services.  To attain this objective and overcome the challenge of breaking silos, intensive and regular coordination between various Ministries / Departments of Government of India along with the State Governments and private sector industries / experts / stakeholders have been carried out during the planning and implementation stages. PM GatiShakti-NMP is a pan-India initiative covering Ministries / Departments at National level and States are also encouraged to adopt this framework.******
