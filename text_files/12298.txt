                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Shale Gas Production




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Krishna-Godavari is one of the six sedimentary basins identified as prospective shale gas production;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the measures taken by the Ministry for exploration and exploitation of shale gas from KG Basin under Shale Gas Policy; and(d) the details of the anticipated quantity of shale gas in KG Basin?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): Krishna - Godavari Basin is one of the six basins identified having Shale Gas potential. Ten nomination blocks in Krishna-Godavari Basin have been allotted to Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) Limited for exploration of shale gas and oil. As per assessment of ONGC, Krishna - Godavari Basin have potential of 50.74 Trillion Cubic Feet of Shale Gas.*********
