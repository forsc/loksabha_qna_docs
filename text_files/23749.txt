                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4617






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Cement Units




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the big medium and small cement units located in backward and rural areas of the country particularly in Maharashtra at present;(b) 	the details of the cement production during the last three years and till date, state-wise/cement plantwise;(c) 	the total number of persons employed in the said cement plants;(d) 	whether any proposal to set up a cement plant is under consideration of the  Government; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise the including up-to-date status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):	The details of cement plants in the country are placed at ANNEXURE-A. As per the information provided by State Government of Maharashtra, the details of the big, medium and small cement units located in backward and rural areas of Maharashtra are placed at ANNEXURE-B.(b):		Total cement production during the last three years is as under:<pre>Financial Year	Cement Production (in Million tons)2017-18	299.122018-19	337.322019-20	334.482020-21 (upto January 2021)	232.57State-wise/ plant-wise cement production data is not maintained centrally. </pre>(c):	The details of total number of persons employed in cement plants are not maintained centrally.(d) & (e): 	Cement Industry has been de-controlled in 1989 and de-licensed in 1991 under the policy of economic liberalization. Consequent to the policy of economic liberalization, investment decisions have been left to the commercial judgment of the entrepreneurs regarding set up of a cement plant. ********
