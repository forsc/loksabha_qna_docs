                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4068






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Youths Own Entrepreneurship




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any data to show the number of youths trained from Odisha who have started their own entrepreneurship venture;     (b) if so, the details thereof; 	     (c) whether the Government is providing any further assistance to promote their venture; and if so, the details thereof; 	 (d) if not, the reasons therefor; and   (e) the number of youths employed during the last three years and current year?              






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)    Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) is the flagship scheme of the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE) implemented by National Skill Development Corporation. The objective of this Skill Certification Scheme is to enable a large number of Indian youth to take up industry-relevant skill training to help them in securing a better livelihood. Individuals with prior learning experience or skills are also assessed and certified under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL). The number of candidates trained, certified, reported placed and self-employed under PMKVY in the state of Odisha is available at Annexure.Further, Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Rural Self Employment Training Institutes (RSETIs) in the field of skill development for rural poor youth under the National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) for gainful employment with an overall objective of poverty alleviation in the country. Rural Self Employment Training Institutes (RSETI) is a Bank lead- Ministry of Rural Development funded training institution established by the Sponsor Banks in their Districts, to provide training for Skill and Entrepreneurship Development. Ministry of Rural Development extends financial support for the construction of RSETI building and also bears the cost of training the Rural Poor candidates. Any youth in the age group of 18-45 years having an aptitude to take up self-employment or wage employment and having some basic knowledge in the related field can undergo training at RSETI. Some of the trained candidates may also seek regular salaried jobs / wage employment.Out of 588 RSETIs functioning across the country sponsored by 23 Banks under the aegis of Ministry of Rural Development, 30 RSETIs are functioning in the state of Odisha. These 30 RSETIs functioning across the state of Odisha are extending Skill and Entrepreneurship development training to rural unemployed youth free of cost and MoRD is reimbursing the training cost of rural poor youth to RSETIs. These trained youth are becoming self employed by commencing their self-employment ventures or taking up wage employment.Under RSETI scheme, the details of number of candidates trained and settled under self-employment by commencing their own entrepreneurship ventures during last 3 years (2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21) and current financial year up to 28.02.2022  in Odisha is as under:- FY 2018-19	FY 2019-20	FY 2020-21	FY 2021-22 TILL 28.02.2022Trained 	Settled under Self Employment	Trained 	Settled under Self Employment	Trained 	Settled under Self Employment	Trained 	Settled under Self Employment21803	15438	20489	12905	15321	10644	13578	11524 (c) 	RSETIs are coordinating the RSETI trained candidates with Banks to arrange for credit facilities required to commence their own self-employment venture. Upon completion of training, Training Providers are required to ensure placement for the trained youth with sustainable salary as per the minimum wages of the State. A total of 9,009 youths have been employed under the programme during the last three years and current year.
