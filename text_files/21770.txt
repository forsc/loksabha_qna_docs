                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Severe Childhood Malnutrition




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has taken note of the severe malnutrition prevailing among children in the country;(b) 	if so the details of different forms of child malnutrition prevalent in the country, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government has taken any steps to ensure uninterrupted supply of nutritious food to various beneficiaries under the ICDS programme during the COVID pandemic; and (d)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (b) The details of State/UT-wise prevalence of different forms of severe malnutrition among children as per NFHS-4 (2015-16) are annexed. States have been advised to  launch a drive for identification of SAM children for referral to hospitals (if required) and AYUSH centres in accordance with detailed action plan to be finalized in consultation with the Chief Medical Officer.(c) & (d) During COVID-19, all Anganwadi Centres across the country were closed to limit the impact of the pandemic. However, to ensure continuous nutritional support to Anganwadi beneficiaries, Anganwadi Workers and Helpers distributed Supplementary Nutrition at the doorsteps of beneficiaries once in 15 days. In addition, Anganwadi Workers and Anganwadi Helpers have assisted the local administration in community surveillance, creating awareness as well as other work assigned to them from time to time. ****** ANNEXURES.
