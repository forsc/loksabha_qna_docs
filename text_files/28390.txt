                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

171






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Quality of Training in Industrial Training Institutes




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	The number of Industrial Training Institutes running across the country at present, State-wise;(b) 	Whether the Union Government is satisfied with quality of training being imparted by these Industrial Training Institutes, if not, whether any steps are being taken to improve the quality of such Institutes;(c) 	Whether the Union Government propose to establish other Industrial Training Institutes in the country including Rajasthan;(d) 	If so, the details thereof; and;(e) 	If not, the reasons therefore?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Sir, the State wise details oftotal number of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) running under Craftsman Training Scheme (CTS), are given in Annexure – I.(b)	Sir, improving quality training in Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) is a continuous process. Towards this interalia standards and norms of affiliation are reviewed; curriculum & courses are revised as per industry practices, trainer / teacher recruitment is advocated and assessment practices are strengthened.The Union Government is also running a project of Skill Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE) where 426 ITIs are supported for upgradation including 14 ITIs of Rajasthan.(c),   (d) & (e) Sir, Union Government accredit/ affiliate Industrial Training Institutes, the day-to-day administration of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) and their establishment is State/ UT subject matter.Since, 2016 total 3600 Industrial Training Institutes have been established in the country, details are attached at Annexure-II. In the State of Rajasthan, the Union Government has established 2 National Skill Training Institutes (NSTIs) in Jaipur and Jodhpur.
