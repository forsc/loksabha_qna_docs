                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Training to Tribals under PMKVY/ JSS




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government maintains records indicating the number of individuals belonging to Tribal communities of Rajasthan who have been trained as a part of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana(PMKVY), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) and Skill India Mission;(b)	if so, the district-wise details thereof specific to Rajasthan;(c)	  whether the Government had fixed any targets for the number of individuals to be trained under the aforementioned schemes;(d)	if so, the details thereof, specific to Rajasthan including Udaipur district as well;(e)	whether the targets have been achieved by the Government;(f)	if so, the details thereof, and if not, the reasons therefore including a timeline for achieving the targets of each of the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes Sir. Skill India Mission aims to empower the Indian youth through skill development. Under Skill India Mission, the government through 21 Central Ministries/ departments is implementing various schemes and programmes for short duration trainings. Major schemes/programmes of the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship under short duration skill ecosystem for individuals including Tribal Communities are, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS), National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS), Customized Crash Course Programme for COVID Warriors. Hence, Ministry is also maintaining record of candidates including tribal communities trained under PMKVY and JSS across India, including Rajasthan. (b)	District-wise number of tribal candidates trained under PMKVY and JSS in the State of Rajasthan is given at Table A and Table B of Annexure, respectively.(c)	Yes Sir, training targets under the PMKVY and JSS schemes have been fixed by the Ministry. (d)	Under PMKVY, total target was to train 4.26 lakh candidates (3.98 lakh in PMKVY 2.0 and 0.28 lakh in PMKVY 3.0) in the State of Rajasthan including district of Udaipur. Under JSS, minimum 1800 beneficiaries have to be trained by each JSS in every financial year and there are five JSS in the State of Rajasthan. There is no JSS in the Udaipur district.(e)	Yes sir, training targets have been achieved under PMKVY and JSS. (f)	Under PMKVY, 9.21 lakh candidates (9.1 lakh in PMKVY 2.0 and 0.11 lakh in PMKVY 3.0) have been trained / oriented in the State of Rajasthan against the target of 4.26 lakh.Under JSS, in last FY 2020-21, 8,660 candidates have been trained in five JSS in the State of Rajasthan against the target of 9000.
