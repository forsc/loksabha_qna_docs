                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2186






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Foreigners Residing in India




Bhagwant Mann














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of students coming to India for studies during the last five years, country-wise;(b)	the number of foreigners residing in India for employment, country-wise; and(c)	the number of foreigners migrated from India during Covid-19 pandemic?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	Foreigners who arrived in India on Student Visa during the last five years is given as under:<pre>Year201620172018201920202021 (Up to 30.11.2021)Arrival72,17270,46372,26874,68920,56123,439</pre>Country/Region wise details are at Annexure-I.(b)	20,607 foreigners are staying in India on Employment Visa as on 	December 02, 2021. Country/Region wise details are at Annexure-II.(c)	41,51,758 foreigners departed from India during the period January 01, 2020 to November 30, 2021.
