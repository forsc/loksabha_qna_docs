                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1776






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Housing and Basic Civic Amenities to Urban Poor




Supriya Sadanand Sule













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)	the number of programmes being implemented by the Government to assist States/UTs in providing housing and basic civic amenities to the urban poor including slum dwellers of the urban areas in the country;(b)	whether the Government has received proposals from several States for the development of slums; (c)	if so, the details thereof and the current status of each of the projects, State/UT-wise; (d) 	whether the Government has allocated funds to provide civic amenities in the slums and for development of slums in the country during the last two years and if so, the details thereof; and   (e) 	whether the Government proposes to conduct a survey regarding slums in the urban areas and to detect them by adopting Remote Sensing and Geographical Information System and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                                   THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                            (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) Scheme from past six years to assist States/Union Territories (UTs)by providing all weather pucca houses with basic civic amenities to the urban poor including slum dwellers of the urban areas in the country. (b) & (c): Yes, Sir. The details of State/UT-wise proposals received for the development of slums and current status of the projects under PMAY-U Schemeare at Annexure. (d): No, Sir. (e): No, Sir.                                            *****
