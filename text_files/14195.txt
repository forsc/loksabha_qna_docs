                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2772






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Visakhapatnam Navy Base




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the reaction of the Indian Navy on the proposed Secretariat at Millennium Towers and its likely impact on defence establishment in Visakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh;(b) whether it is a fact that Visakhapatnam is in the hit list of Pakistan due to Indian Navy’s establishment and making it the capital is likely to bring in a lot of population and make it more sensitive and vulnerable; and(c) if so, the reaction of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  to (c):  Indian Navy has not received any proposal regarding setting up of a Secretariat at Millennium Towers in Visakhapatnam, by Government of Andhra Pradesh.*****
