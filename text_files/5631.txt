                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Development of Air Bases




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government has decided to develop five air bases in the State of Andhra Pradesh and has requested Government of Andhra Pradesh to provide suitable land for development of these Air bases;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c)  whether any places have already been identified in the State for this purpose and if so, the details thereof; and(d)  the status of the project as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (d): Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh had written to Raksha Mantri regarding setting up of an Air Force Command and training base in Andhra Pradesh at Donakoda/Sagar Airfield, Vijayawada, Vishakapatnam and Suryalanka.*****
