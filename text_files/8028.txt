                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1622






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Road Projects in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)      the details of roads constructed in Odisha under Centrally sponsored schemes   and    Central Road Fund along with other external aided projects during the last three years; (b)	the length of these road projects along with allocation of funds, project-wise; and(c)	the details of funds utilised thereon, project-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): There is no separate project-wise allocation made by this Ministry. The details   in respect of Central Road Fund (CRF) and externally aided projects in Odisha for the last three years from 2016-17 to 2018-19 are given below:-Name of Scheme	Length completed(in km)	Accrual/ Allocation (Rs in Cr.)	Amount reimbursed to State Government/Expenditure (Rs in Cr.)Improvement of State roads under Central Road Fund (CRF)	314	977	614Development of National Highways under external assistance 	175	500	481*****
