                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2674






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Wagon Workshop in Warangal




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any proposal from Telangana requesting for establishment of Wagon Workshop in Warangal and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has taken any decision in this matter and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the obstructions being faced by the Government in this regard and the remedial measures being taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 and (b) A Workshop for Wagon Periodical Overhauling at Kazipet, near Warangal, was sanctioned in 2016-17 at a cost of ` 383 crores. Ministry of Railways has assigned the work to Rail Vikas Nigam Ltd (RVNL), the executing agency, and is awaiting transfer of suitable land from the State Government in order to start the work.(c) 160 acres of land is to be acquired and be provided by State Government for the workshop, which is yet to be made available to Railways.*****
