                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Upgradation of FASTag System and Technology Infrastructure




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  has  noticed  various difficulties  in  the  implementation  of  FASTag  and digital  payments  for  toll  through  Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) technology in different parts of the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and the action proposed for  addressing  the  issues  of  upgradation  of  technology infrastructure  along  with  the  funds  allocated  to  the States  for  the  purpose;(c)  whether  the  electronic  toll  collection  through FASTag system has suffered a setback due to congestion at toll booths across the country resulting in an estimated annual  loss  due  to  wastage  of  fuel  of  over  Rs.  12,000 crore; and(d) if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of  the  Government  thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) &(b) National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has observed various difficulties during the implementation of FASTag. NHAI has taken all the required steps to mitigate the issues at field such as creating public awareness through various channels, ease of availability of FASTag, etc.  Technology upgradation is a continuous process and NHAI is resorting to the same as per requirements.(c) &(d) According to the Government mandate, all lanes of fee plazas on National Highways except one hybrid lane have been declared as FASTag Lane of the fee plaza. As a result, waiting time in such FASTag lanes have significantly reduced. However, the waiting time in the hybrid lane which also accepts cash has increased because of non-FASTag vehicles. *****
