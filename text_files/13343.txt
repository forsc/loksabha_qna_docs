                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2197






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Contract Labourers




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	whether a large number of labourers are working on contract basis in Railways;(b):	if so, the number of labourers engaged on a contract basis during the last two years and the current year;(c):	whether such labourers are being paid salary and allowances according to the prescribed rules; and(d):	if not, the system in place for payment of salaries and allowances to these employees?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir. Contract labourers are engaged by the Railway field units on need basis from time to time. Data of contract labourers’ details is not maintained centrally.(c):	Yes, Sir.(d):	Does not arise.*****
