                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1781






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Increasing Number of Warehouses




Rattan Lal Kataria














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has increased the number of warehouses for the storage of foodgrains by using scientific method during the last three years; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether accountability has been fixed for the spoilage of foodgrains; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	whether there is adequate storage facility in Haryana; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	Yes, Sir.(b): 	Food Corporation of India (FCI) is augmenting/modernizing its storage capacity through following schemes: -1.	Nine silos have been developed with capacity of 4.875 LMT in last three years and current year, under Public-Private Partnership (PPP) mode...........2/--2-2.	41 Godowns have been created with capacity of 3.56150 LMT in last three years and current year under Private Entrepreneur Guarantee Scheme.3.	8 Godowns have been created with capacity of 0.4870 LMT in last three years and current year under Central Sector Scheme.In addition, Godowns are also hired from Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC)/State Warehousing Corporation (SWC) and Private Warehousing Scheme (PWS) on need basis. Number of Depots owned/hired by FCI increased from 2045 on Oct’2018 to 2223 on Oct’ 2021.Further, Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC) has created following additional capacity during last three years:2018-19 - 0.39 LMT2019-20 - 0.47 LMT2020-21 - 1.82 LMT(c): 	Cases of spoilage, if any, of food grains are investigated and departmental actions are initiated against the defaulting officers/officials found responsible.(d): 	Departmental action taken during last 3 financial years and current years is as below:Departmental Action takenYear	Major Penalty	Minor Penalty2018-19	6	112019-20	0	52020-21	0	22021-22 (till Oct)	0	0Total	6	18(e): 	Yes, Sir.(f): 	FCI and State agencies have covered capacity of 107.39 LMT as on 31.10.2021 in Haryana, against stock position of 92.02 LMT.Further, CWC has 7.01 LMT capacity warehouses in Haryana.****
