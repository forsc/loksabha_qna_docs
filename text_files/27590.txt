                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

297






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Subsidy on LPG Cylinder




Bhagwant Mann














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the subsidy on domestic cooking gas has been abolished in thecountry; and(b) if so, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRIHARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha StarredQuestion No. 297 asked by Shri Bhagwant Mann to be answered on 9thAugust, 2021 regarding “Subsidy on LPG cylinder”.(a) & (b): The prices of petroleum products in the country are linked to the priceof respective products in the international market. The Government continues tomodulate the effective price to consumer for Subsidized Domestic LPG. The pricesof non-subsidized Domestic LPG are however determined by the OMCs in linewith changes in the international markets. The subsidy on the productincreases/decreases with the increase/decrease in the product price in internationalmarket and decision of Government on subsidy.
