                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2603






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Houses for Cyclone affected Persons




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether five lakh houses are likely to be sanctioned under PMAY-G by the Government specially for those persons whose houses got completely/substantially damaged by the severe cyclone Fani; and(b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G), five percent of annual central allocation is retained at the Central Government level as a reserve fund.  This fund is used for financing the proposals under Special Projects received from the States/UTs inter-alia for rehabilitation / relocation of families whose houses have been completely / substantially damaged on account of  various factors including natural hazard viz., cyclone. All the eligible rural households, who are included in the Permanent Wait List of PMAY-G and whose houses have been completely / substantially damaged on account of cyclone Fani, are eligible for sanctioning of houses under Special Projects of PMAY-G. *****
