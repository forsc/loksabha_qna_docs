                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Funds to ASI for Excavation Projects




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Archaeological Survey of India is facing a financial crunch forcing it to close its excavation projects  across the country particularly in Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has also proposed to provide additional funds to ASI for its excavation projects; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the total number of excavation projects closed recently by ASI due to availability of inadequate funds?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	No, Sir.(b)	No, Sir. The following funds allotted by the Government for exploration/ excavation during the last five financial year:Financial Year	   Allotment (Rs. in lakhs)2015-16	     570.002016-17	     505.002017-18	     530.002018-19	     625 .002019-20	     191.66 (till June)(c)	No excavation project was closed recently by ASI due to inadequate fund.  ********************
