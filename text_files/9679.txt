                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






CGHS Dispensaries/Wellness Centres




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the places where dispensaries/ wellness centres are functioning under CGHS in the country at present;(b) the number of beneficiaries who availed treatment under CGHS in Rajasthan during the last five years, district-wise;(c) whether the Government proposes to open new dispensaries/wellness centres under Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) in the districts having more than 1000 beneficiaries; and(d) if so, the time by which these are likely to be opened along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	A statement showing Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) Wellness Centres functioning in the country is at Annexure-I. (b):	Data regarding district-wise number of beneficiaries who availed treatment under CGHS is not maintained. However, a statement giving details of the number of times the beneficiaries availed facilities under CGHS in Rajasthan during last five years & the current financial year, is at Annexure-II.  (c) & (d):No.
