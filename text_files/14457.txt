                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3053






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Setting up of Central ToolRoom-cum-Training Centres




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to set up some central tool room-cum-training centres in the country;(b)	if so, the places identified for the purpose, State-wise;(c)	whether one such central tool room-cum-training centre is proposed to be set up in Rourkela; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (d):	23 Technology Extension Centers have been sanctioned for being set up at locations indicated in the Annexure.
