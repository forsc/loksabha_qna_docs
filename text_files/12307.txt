                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1351






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






ESI Hospitals Operating in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number and details of ESI hospitals operating in Jharkhand at present, district-wise;(b)whether the Government proposes to set up new ESI hospitals in Giridih and Bokaro districts of Jharkhand and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)the healthcare facilities provided by the Government to the workers in absence of ESI hospitals nearby; and(d)the details of the places selected in this regard and quantum of funds earmarked and proposed to be released in this financial year for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):		 At present, three Employees'' State Insurance (ESI) hospitals are operational in the state of Jharkhand.  The district-wise details are as under:  Sl. No.	District	Name of the Hospital1.	Dhanbad	ESIS Hospital Maithan2.	East Singhbhum	ESIC Hospital Adityapur 3.	Ranchi	ESIC Hospital Namkum (b):		 No proposal has been received from the State Government of Jharkhand to set up new ESI hospital in the district of Giridih or Bokaro districts of the Jharkhand.(c) & (d):	 In the absence of nearby ESI hospital, beneficiaries may avail health care facilities through hospitals empaneled by the state government under the ESI scheme or claim reimbursement from the state ESI scheme for the treatment availed by them.******
