                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1842






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Schemes for Development of NER




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is paying special attention to the North Eastern States;(b) 	if so, the details of various schemes started for the North Eastern States during the last three years, year and State-wise under the North Eastern Venture Fund;(c) 	the details of schemes started for the State of Assam including the names of the districts in which the works related to it have been started;(d) 	the names of schemes in Assam under which allocation has been made during the last three years, scheme-wise, the amount of funds allocated;(e) 	whether any agreement has taken place with any entity for North Eastern Venture Fund; and(f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a)	Yes Sir.(b)	In addition to various ongoing schemes, the Ministry of Development of the North Eastern Region (MDoNER) jointly with North Eastern Development Finance Corporation Ltd (NEDFi), has set up the North East Venture Fund (NEVF), a dedicated Venture Fund for the North East Region. The Fund was formally launched on 9th September 2017. The fund is in line with the Central Government’s vision towards encouraging entrepreneurs and Start-ups for early stage & growth stage companies with new products & technologies or innovative business models. The investment under this scheme ranges from Rs.25 lakh to Rs.10 crore per venture, which is long term in nature with investment horizon of 4-5 years.(c) to (f)	The scheme is applicable for whole of the  North Eastern Region including Assam.
