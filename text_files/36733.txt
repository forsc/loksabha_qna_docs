                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

358






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Central Government Schools in Andhra Pradesh




Goddeti Madhavi














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the list of Central Government Schools in Andhra Pradesh, district-wise; and (b) the list of number of students enrolled, drop-out rate and teacher-to-pupil ratio at primary, secondary and senior secondary levels for all the schools in the above list, gender-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. ******STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 358 RAISED BY SHRIMATI GODDETI MADHAVI, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT TO BE ANSWERED ON 28/03/2022 REGARDING CENTRAL GOVERNMENT SCHOOLS IN ANDHRA PRADESH. (a) Central Government Schools namely Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) are under the Department of School Education & Literacy, Ministry of Education. Presently, 35 KVs and 15 JNVs are functioning in the State of Andhra Pradesh. The District-wise details are at Annexure-I.(b) The details of the gender-wise students enrolled, teacher-to-pupil ratio at primary, secondary and senior secondary in each KV in the State of Andhra Pradesh are at Annexure – II. The details of the gender-wise students enrolled, teacher-to-pupil ratio at secondary and senior secondary in each JNV in the State of Andhra Pradesh are at Annexure – III.  No case of dropout has been reported in KVs and JNVs functioning in the State of Andhra Pradesh. *****
