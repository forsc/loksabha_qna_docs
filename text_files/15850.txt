                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4580






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Homoeopathy Pharmacovigilance Centres




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to set up Homoeopathy Pharmacovigilance Centres in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and (c) the time by which Homoeopathy Pharmacovigilance Centres are likely to be set up in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to ( c):	  Ministry of AYUSH has implemented Central Sector Scheme of Pharmacovigilance initiative for Ayurveda, Siddha Unani and Homeopathy drugs since December, 2017.  Under this scheme, in order to develop the network for reporting of homoeopathy drugs-related adverse events and monitoring of misleading advertisements, intermediary pharmacovigilance centre for Homoeopathy drugs has been established in  National Institute of Homoeopathy (NIH), Kolkata (West Bengal).  Following are the State/UT-wise details of eleven Peripheral Homoeopathic Pharmacovigilance Centres (PPvCs) set up under the scheme- 1.	Andhra Pradesh:  Regional Research Institute of Homoeopathy,         Dr. GGH Medical College Campus, Gudivada.2.	Assam:  Regional Research Institute for Homoeopathy, Guwahati,3.	Karnataka: Father Muller Homoeopathy Medical College & Hospital, Mangaluru4.	Kerala: National Homoeopathy Research Institute in Mental Health - NHRIMH,  Kottayam, 5.	Madhya Pradesh:  Govt. Homoeopathic Medical College and Hospital,  Bhopal. 6.	Maharashtra:  Regional Research Institute for homoeopathy, Navi Mumbai. 7.	Odisha: Dr. Abhin Chandra Homoeopathic Medical College & Hospital, Bhubaneswar. 8.	Tamilnadu: Sarada Krishna Homoeopathic Medical College, Kanyakumari9.	Uttar Pradesh: Dr. D.P. Rastogi Central Research Institute for Homeopathy, Noida10.	West Bengal:  Mahesh Bhattacharya Homoeopathic Medical College & Hospital, Howrah. 11.	West Bengal: Dr. Anjali Chatterjee Regional Research Institute For Homoeopathy, Kolkata.…………………..
