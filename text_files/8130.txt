                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1746






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






AMRUT Scheme in Rajasthan




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of the cities of Rajasthan selected under AMRUT Scheme;(b)	the funds sanctioned and released under this scheme to the State of Rajasthan;(c)	the details of Authority/entity entrusted with the responsibility to monitor and judicious utilization of these funds; and(d)	The details of the completion status of these scheme, city-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a).	The 29 cities in Rajasthan have been selected as AMRUT cities namely, Kota, Hanumangarh, Kishangarh, Churu, Bhilwara, Jhunjhunun, Sujangarh, Baran, Bundi, Jodhpur, SawaiMadhopur, Bharatpur, Jhalawar, Gangapur City, Hindaun, Bhiwadi, Beawar, Tonk, Nagaur, Sikar, Udaipur, Bikaner, Alwar, Chittaurgarh, Dhaulpur, Ganganagar, Pali, Jaipur, and Ajmer.(b). The total State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) for the State of Rajasthan under AMRUT is Rs.3223.94 crore including committed central assistance of Rs.1541.95 crore; of which Rs.871.42 crore of central assistance has been released so far for implementation of projects under AMRUT.(c). A State level High Powered Steering Committee (SHPSC) chaired by the State Chief Secretary, steers the Mission Programme in its entirety and is entrusted with the monitoring and resource mobilization under AMRUT.(d). The progress of AMRUT projects in Rajasthan is at  ANNEXURE.                                *****
