                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Modernization of Sports Infrastructure




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether talented players have been selected and are being trained in Maharashtra under ‘‘Khelo India’’ programme,(b) if so, the details thereof during the last three years?(c) the details of sports infrastructure created and modernized as well as expanded in the country under ‘‘Khelo India’’ programme State-wise including the State of Maharashtra; and(d) whether the Government has any proposal in this regard, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. Under the “Talent Search and Development” vertical of the Khelo India Scheme, a total of 2964 Khelo India Athletes have been identified for training, out of which 418 are from Maharashtra.(c) and (d) Under the “Utilisation and Creation/Upgradation of Sports Infrastructure” vertical of the Khelo India Scheme, this Ministry has sanctioned 267 sports infrastructure projects of various categories across the country on the basis of proposals received from the respective State/UT Governments and other eligible entities. Out of these, eleven projects are located in Maharashtra. State/UT-wise details of projects sanctioned under the Khelo India Scheme are at Annexure.
