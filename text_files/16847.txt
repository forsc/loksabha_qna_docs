                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






India Post Payment Bank




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has prepared any plan to provide India Post Payments Bank service in all the rural areas of the country; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) services are provided in all parts of the country including all the rural areas subject to technical feasibility considerations.(b)	IPPB has 650 branches/controlling offices, almost one in each district across the country. IPPB has also enabled 1.36 lakh Post Offices (including 1.10 lakh in rural areas) to provide Banking services at the counters. It has also equipped nearly 1.8 lakh Postmen and Grameen Dak Sevaks with Smartphones and biometric devices to enable them to provide Doorstep Banking Services. ************
