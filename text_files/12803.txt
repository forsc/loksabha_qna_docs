                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1726






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Import of LNG from Qatar




Asaduddin Owaisi













                                                     Imtiaz Jaleel Syed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India has offered Qatar stake in gas fields power plants in return for long term off-take assurance for its LNG shipments and reduce prices in tune with global trend;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total import of LNG at present from Qatar and prices thereof; and(d) the extent to which India is likely to be benefited in the light of international scenario of hike in natural gas prices, sanctions and low production and export curbs?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): The Government of India and Qatar discussed about LNG supplies to Indian market as well as the prices keeping in view the global LNG market condition.(c) & (d): Indian companies imported 10 MMT LNG during the last FY 2018-19 from Qatar, as per the contracts entered by them with Qatari companies. The fluctuations in global crude and LNG prices affect the price of natural gas in the country.  *****
