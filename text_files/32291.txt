                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3723






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Information to Citizens at Village Level




Gajendra Singh Patel













                                                    Durga Das Uikey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any action plan for providing information to citizens at village level and to ensure for publicising broadcasting; (b)	the arrangements made by Government so that information on welfare schemes of Government reaches to people at panchayat level;(c)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(d)	if not, the time by which it is likely to be made?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS{SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR}(a) to (d): Ministry of Information and Broadcasting disseminates information in English and various Indian languages on Government policies, programmes, initiatives and achievements through various media units like Press Information Bureau (PIB), Bureau of Outreach and Communication (BOC), Doordarshan and All India Radio, utilizing various media vehicles like print, electronic media, social media, internet websites, outdoor publicity, live arts and interpersonal media. Communication through printing and distribution of New India Samachar, a fortnightly publication, up to the level of Gram Panchayat is also a medium of informing people to the last mile about the ongoing and new programmes and policies of Government of India. The e-version of New India Samachar is being sent to approx. 6.50 crore readers in flip book as well as PDF format in 13 languages via emails using E-Sampark platform of MyGov India.
