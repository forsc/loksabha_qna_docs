                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4043






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Linking of Aadhaar and Driving Licence




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to make it compulsory to link Aadhaar Card with Driving Licence;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ) to (c): As per information received from Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, in compliance to the order of the Supreme Court dated 26th September, 2018 in Writ Petition (Civil) No. 494 of 2012- Justice K. S. Puttaswamy (Retd.) & Anr. Vs. Union of India & Ors., Ministry of Road Transport and Highways stopped the process of verification using Aadhaar for the Driving Licenses. ******
