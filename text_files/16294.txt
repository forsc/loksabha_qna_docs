                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

116






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






NPAs of MSMEs




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a the fact that small and medium traders of Gujarat who wanted to pay back bank loan but Public Sector Banks (PSBs) have declared their accounts as Non-Performing Asset (NPA) and sent to the NCLT;(b)	if so, the details thereof including the number of such cases reported by PSBs in the State of Gujarat, district-wise;(c)	whether the actions are being taken in accordance with the norms of Reserve Bank of India, if a loan is paid back by the borrower and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the action taken by the Government against guilty banks in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a): As per data reported by PSBs, no account of small and medium traders of Gujarat who wanted to pay back bank loan has been declared as NPA and sent to the National Company Law Tribunal during the preceding or the current financial year (till 31.8.2020).(b) to (d): Questions do not arise.*****
