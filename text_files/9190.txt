                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Transfer of MES Employee




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari













                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the guidelines and rules with policy decisions under which civilian employees of Military Engineering Service (MES) whose spouses are in civilian jobs are being governed;(b) the number of employees transferred to other stations whose spouse is in Government job during the last three years, rank-wise;(c) the number of employees who approached CAT in connection with their posting to the same place where his or her spouse is working; and(d) whether despite CAT orders, some employees are not being transferred and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with corrective steps being taken in this regard to follow the existing guidelines?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  Civilian employees of Military Engineering Services (MES) whose spouses are in Civilian jobs are governed by the guidelines and rules as are applicable to the Government employees under the extant guidelines and rules of Government of India.(b) to (d):  Information is being collected and will be placed on the Table of the House.*****
