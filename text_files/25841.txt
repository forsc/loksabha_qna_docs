                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

154






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Khadi Prakritik Emulsion Paint




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken initiative for providing assistance to start business of Khadi Prakritik Emulsion Paint from cow dung under Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) and Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs); and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO THE PARTS (a)&(b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No.*154 FOR ANSWER ON 29.07.2021(a) & (b):	Yes Sir.  The Government has taken the following initiatives through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) for providing assistance to start business of Khadi Prakritik Emulsion Paint from cow dung: (i)  KVIC through its Kumarappa National Handmade Paper Institute (KNHPI), Sanganer, Jaipur has developed the technology for manufacturing of Khadi Prakritik Paint and providing technical training to interested Entrepreneurs to set-up their enterprises of manufacturing of Khadi Prakritik Paint. (ii)	A new production cum training unit of Khadi Prakritik Paint has been set up in the premises of Kumarrapa National Handmade Paper Institute with the production capacity of 1000 litres per day.(iii)	KNHPI has started training programmes under manufacturing of Khadi Prakritik Paint since January, 2021 and upto 15.07.2021, 418 candidates have been trained.  State/UT-wise number of candidate trained during the period from 18.01.2021 to 15.07.2021 is Annexed.(iv) 	Transfer of Technology: KNHPI also transfers all the expertise and information of manufacturing of Khadi Prakritik Paint, such as sketches, drawing, materials, formulae, etc. to prospective entrepreneurs and also assists in selecting & installing machine and equipment.
