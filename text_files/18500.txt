                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Ayush Health and Wellness Centres under NAM




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the details of the total number of Ayush Health and Wellness Centres (HWC) built under the National Ayush Mission (NAM) Scheme since September 2014 in the country including Andhra Pradesh, State/UT-wise;(b) the details of such HWCs which have been allocated under the PM-JAY Scheme;(c) the details of Primary Health Centres, Community Health Centres and District Hospitals constructed and upgraded since September 2014 in Andhra Pradesh;(d) the funds allocated by the Government to Andhra Pradesh under the NAM Scheme; and(e) whether any survey or study has been conducted after the implementation of the NAM Scheme to gauge whether people prefer health services in AYUSH or allopathy, if so, the details thereof and the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	A total 3063 AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) have been approved and Rs. 188.06 Crores released to the States/UTs till date under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) including Rs. 3.88 Crores to the State Government of Andhra Pradesh for upgradation of 42 AYUSH dispensaries into AYUSH HWCs. State-wise status of approved Health and Wellness Centres is furnished at Annexure. However, establishment of AYUSH HWCs comes under the purview of respective States/UTs.(b):	There is no such provision of allocation of AYUSH HWCs in the PM-JAY Scheme.(c):	As Public Health being a State subject, construction and upgradation of Primary Health Centres, Community Health Centres and District Hospitals comes under the purview of the respective State/UT Government. However, under NAM there is a provision of financial assistance for colocation of AYUSH facilities at Primary Health Centres (PHCs), Community Health Centres (CHCs) and District Hospitals (DHs). Under NAM, Ministry of AYUSH supported for colocation of AYUSH facilities at 2 PHCs, 1 CHC and 1 DH in the State of Andhra Pradesh since September 2014 till date as per the proposal submitted by the State Government of Andhra Pradesh through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP).(d):	Under NAM, Rs. 71.93 Crores have been released to the State Government of Andhra Pradesh from the year 2014-15 to 2019-20.(e):	No such survey or study has been conducted.
