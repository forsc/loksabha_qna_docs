                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Funds to Corporations




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has released/sanctioned funds/grants for corporations/municipalities in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh States; and (b) if so, the details thereof indicating the funds released and utilisation reported during the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b): Central Assistance under various programmes/schemes of this Ministry are transferred to Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) through respective State Governments based on the guidelines of the respective programmes/schemes. The grants under 14th Central Finance Commission are transferred by Ministry of Finance to ULBs through respective State Governments based on the guidelines issued by Ministry of Finance in this regard.Detail of funds releasedto and utilized by the States of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana under various Missions and 14th Finance Commission during the last three financial years and current financial year is at Annexure.                      *****
