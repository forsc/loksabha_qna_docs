                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1005






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Ayurvedic Research Centre




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Balak Nath
                                                








                                                    Abdul Khaleque
                                                








                                                    Mahesh Sahoo
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of funds allocated for research and development in last three years separately for each segment of Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy; b)	whether there is any plan to set up Ayurvedic Research Centre in Assam particularly in lower Assam region and in Odisha particularly in Dhenkanal HPC;c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; d)	whether the Government proposes to formulate any Scheme for setting up of Research Centre apart from promoting Yoga and Naturopathy and creating awareness among the people about it; and e)	if so, the name of the Scheme and the time by which it is likely to be launched?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	The details of funds allocated for research and development in last three years separately for each segment of Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy is at Annexure.(b)	& (c) No Sir. At present Ministry does not have any proposal to set up Ayurvedic Research Centre in Assam particularly in lower Assam region and in Odisha particularly in DhenkanalHPC. However there is one research institute at Assam i.e. Central Ayurveda Research Institute  Beltola, Guwahati-781028 and one research institute at Orissa state i.e. Central Ayurveda Research Institute, Bharatpur, Bhubaneswar, Odisha-751003.(d)	No Sir.(e)	Does not arise.
