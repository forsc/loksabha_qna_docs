                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5185






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






TRIFED in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the projects undertaken under Tribal Cooperative Marketing Development Federation of India Limited (TRIFED) and the number of retail outlets located in West Bengal;(b) whether any TRIFED outlet has been opened in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of agencies involved and number of beneficiaries under TRIFED in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):    The Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides funds to Tribal Co-operative Marketing Development Federation of India (TRIFED) for marketing of tribal products by empaneling tribal suppliers comprising of individual tribal artisans, tribal Self-Help Groups (SHGs), Organisations / Agencies / NGOs working with tribals etc under the scheme ‘Institutional Support for Marketing and Development of Tribal Products / Produce’ in tribal dominated States/UTs including West Bengal. TRIFED is also engaged in direct sale of tribal products, including art & craft items through its network of Tribes India Showrooms, exhibitions, Aadi Mahotsav. Further, marketing is done through online e- commerce portal developed by TRIFED and also online retailers like Amazon, Flipkart etc. The details of funds granted to TRIFED for the marketing activities during the last three years are as under: (Rs. In lakh)Institution	2018-19 	2019-20	2020-21TRIFED	6250.00	11850.00	9500.00 (b):    Currently there is no retail outlet in the district of Kalimpong. The details of retail outlets located in various Districts of West Bengal including Darjeeling under the brand name of “Tribes India” are as follows:1.	Tribes Shop - Darjeeling at Hayden Hall Institute, 42 Laden La Road, Darjeeling, West Bengal -734101.2.	Tribes Shop - Airport at NSCBI Airport Arrival Gate No 1(A)&1 (B), Kolkata-700052.3.	Tribes Shop - LIC at S N Banerjee Road esplanade Kolkata-700013.4.	Tribes Shop - EZCC at EZCC, Broadway Road, IB-Block, Sec-III, Salt Lake City, Kolkata-700106.  (c):  The agencies empanelled with TRIFED, under Regional Office West Bengal from the district of Darjeeling and Kalimpong are listed below:From Darjeeling District: 1.
