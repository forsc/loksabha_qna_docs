                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3003






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Metro Train Projects




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the cities in Madhya Pradesh where new projects to run Metro trains are proposed;(b)	the parameters set to ensure smooth operation of rail projects in metro cities; and(c)	the places in Madhya Pradesh where metro train projects are operating smoothly?






ANSWER




                                                                          THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                   MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE) (a)	Urban transport, which is an integral part of urban development, is a state subject. Hence, respective State Government/Union Territory (UT) is responsible for initiating and developing urban transport infrastructure including metro rail projects. The Central  Government  considers  financial  assistance  for   such projects in cities or urban agglomerates, based on feasibility of the proposal and availability of resources, as and when posed by the concerned State Government/Union Territory (UT). The proposals in respect of metro rail projects in the cities of Bhopal and Indore proposed by Government of Madhya Pradesh have been approved by Government of India on 30-11-2018 and the construction work in these metro projects is currently underway. No new metro projects other than Bhopal & Indore have been proposed by Government of Madhya Pradesh.(b)	The metro rail projects are approved in accordance with Metro Rail Policy, 2017 which ensures systematic planning and smooth operation of such projects.(c)	At present, Metro Rail is not operational in any city of Madhya Pradesh.                                       *****
