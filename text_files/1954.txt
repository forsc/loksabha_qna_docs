                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1697






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Inclusion in the Central List of OBCs




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Siddha Community has been included in the list of Other Backward Classes (OBCs) in Rajasthan but despite fulfilling all the criteria, the same has not been included in the central list; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the State Government of Rajasthan has forwarded a proposal to include Siddha community in the central list of OBCs; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) to (d): Yes Sir. ‘Sidhh’ caste/community is included in the State List of Backward Classes in the Rajasthan.  As per provisions Section 9 of the National Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1993, the NCBC had examined the request for inclusion of “Siddh” community in the Central List of OBCs for the State of Rajasthan and did not accept the same vide Advice No. Rajasthan 61/2001 dated 11.06.2001.****
