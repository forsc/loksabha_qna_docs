                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2477






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Achievements in Rural Development




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the achievements of the Ministry during the last two years;(b)	whether any presentation has been made before Hon’ble Prime Minister regarding the achievements of the Ministry during the last two years;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	the areas in which it could not achieve targets and the areas where it excelled;(e)	the target fixed for the next three years along with the roadmap to achieve them;(f)	the details of the difficulties about which Hon’ble Prime Minister has been apprised of along with the details of the suggestions put forward to deal with them; and(g)	the details of the important areas on which the Ministry is likely to focus during the next three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (g):	The Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM), Deen Dayal Upadhyay – Gramin Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) and National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) to bring about overall improvement in the quality of life of the people in rural areas through employment generation, strengthening of livelihood opportunities, promoting self-employment, skilling of rural youths, infrastructure development, provision of social assistance and other basic amenities. 	Details of achievements under different Rural Development programmes are at Annexure-I. Targets under different programmes are subject to the approval of competent authority, respective programme/scheme guidelines and availability of funds. 	The implementation of the programmes/schemes of the Ministry are reviewed and discussed regularly at various levels. Programmes/ schemes of the Ministry are considered and approved by the competent authority as per Government of India (Allocation of Business) Rules, 1961 and the Government of India (Transaction of Business) Rules, 1961. Rural Development programmes and schemes are aimed at addressing multidimensional poverty through evidence based selection of areas and beneficiaries. Different Rural Development programmes are implemented based on their respective guidelines/framework for implementation. The Ministry of Rural Development implements schemes in partnership with  the State Governments/ UT Administrations with a clear focus on strengthening of livelihood opportunities, creation of sustainable community assets, provision of basic infrastructure and improvement in the quality of life of the rural people. *********                                                                                  Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED IN STATEMENT IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2477 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 03.12.2019 REGARDING ‘ACHIEVEMENTS IN RURAL DEVELOPMENT’I.
