                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1740






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






NCERT Regional Institute of Excellence




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to establish NCERT Regional Institute ofExcellence at Nellore and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether the foundation stone for the same has been laid recently by theMinister of Human Resource Development and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) It is proposed to set up a new Regional Institute of Education (RIE), as aconstituent unit of National Council of Educational Research and Training, at Nellore,Andhra Pradesh.(b) The foundation stone for said RIE has been laid at the identified site on the27th December, 2016 jointly by the then Hon’ble Minister of Urban Development,Housing and Urban Poverty Alleviation and Information & Broadcasting and the thenHon’ble Minister of Human Resource Development.****
