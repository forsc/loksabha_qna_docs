                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Australian Dairy Majors




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the Government had entered into an agreement with Australia by which the Government intends to allow Australian dairy majors to enter into country’s dairy sector;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether it is also true that farmers and their unions have strongly opposed such move; and(d)	whether it is a fact that many farmers in the country who do not have land were depending on the dairy sector and therefore, allowing such majors into dairy sector will make their life miserable?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	 No agreement with Australia to allow Australian dairy majors to enter into country’s dairy sector has been signed.(b)	Does not arise in view of (a) above.(c)	Yes Sir.(d)	Does not arise in view of (a) above. *********
