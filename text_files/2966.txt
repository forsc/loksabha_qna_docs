                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2694






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Lactose Free Dairy Products




Kaushal Kishore













                                                    Upendra Singh Rawat
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government proposes to take steps for the easy availability and cost effectiveness of dairy products for lactose resistant people in the country; b)	if so, the details thereof; c)	whether the lactose free milk products being sold in the country are being suitably regulated by the Government for their pricing as well as quality aspect to encourage their ready availability as well as discourage any possible health hazards from the use of these products;d)	if so, the details thereof; and e)	if not, the reasons therefor ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING (DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)a)  to  e) : FSSAI has informed that, currently, there are no standards for lactose free milk. However, FSSAI has notified draft standards for low lactose/ lactose free milk on 1st May, 2019 for lactose resistant people for seeking comments of stakeholders. After finalization of standards, the same will be enforced through the State /UT Governments who are primarily responsible for enforcement of provisions of Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006 and Rule and Regulations framed thereunder.              As regards the pricing, it is governed by market demand & supply situation and its cost of production. The Department does not control pricing of milk and milk products. *****
