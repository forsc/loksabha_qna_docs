                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






One Stop Centres




M.K. Vishnu Prasad













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    Brijendra Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)    whether the Government has approved the proposal for setting up of 180 additional One Stop Centres (OSCs) for women empowerment across the country during 2020; (b)    if so, the details thereof along with number of the said centres established till date, State wise including Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh;  (c)    the details of fund allocated under OSCs during the last three years, State/UT wise including Haryana; (d)    whether any specific services are being provided to women in the said centres and if so, the details thereof; and  (e)   the number of women benefited under the said scheme so far, State-wise including Haryana?






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (e)  Government has approved 733 One Stop Centre (OSC) across the Country, out of  which 700 are operational. The OSCs provide a range of services such as police facilitation, legal aid, psycho-social counseling, medical aid and temporary shelter in an integrated manner, under one roof, for violence affected women and women in distress.           The State wise list of the approved and operational OSCs alongwith the number of women that have approached the OSCs including those from the State of Andhra Pradesh, Haryana and Tamil Nadu is at Annexure -I.
