                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3115






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Monitoring of Animals for COVID-19 linked Infections




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether  the Government has constituted/appointed any panels or any Central/State-level task forces to provide support and guidance to field level officials to monitor animals for pre and post COVID-19 infections; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise as on date; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a), (b) and (c) The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change has issued advisories to the States/UTs on managing National Parks and Sanctuaries while following due COVID protocols and containment and management of COVID-19 in exotic species of animals. Advisories have also been issued by the Central Zoo Authority on precautionary measures to be followed by the zoos. In addition, the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying has circulated advisory/guidelines to all State Animal Husbandry Department, Health and Wildlife authorities regarding SARS-COV-2 in animals which include monitoring and guidance including risk assessment in respect of various animal categories, general prevention control, risk pathway and risk reduction, disinfection, feed and feeding practice, waste management and key messages. However, no panels or task forces to provide support to field level officials to monitor animals for COVID-19 infections have been constituted/appointed by the Ministry.  ***
