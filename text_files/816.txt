                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Display Boards at FOBs for Safety




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to place display boards with brief details thereon regarding safety of each of the Foot Over Bridges (FOBs) after conducting their regular audit to avert the accidents/mishaps to passengers using the Foot Over Bridges constructed on the old railway  stations of the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, Zone/Division-wise;  (c) whether the Government has provided such information at Tilak Bridge Railway Station and other such stations; and(d)   if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)                   (a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House*****
