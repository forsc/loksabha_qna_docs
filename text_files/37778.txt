                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4952






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Expansion of NCW




Manickam Tagore B













                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the details of funds allocated to National Commission for Women (NCW) and State Commission for Women (SCW) during each of the last three years, State-wise including Maharashtra;(b) 	whether the said allocation is adequate and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	if not, whether any further demand for allocation of fund has been made by the Commission and if so, the details thereof;  (d) 	whether the Government is considering to expand and broaden the ambit of NCW and if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	whether the State Commissions for Women were also asked to expand their ambit, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




(a)   	The details of funds allocated to National Commission for Women (NCW) during the last three years and expenditure incurred thereon by the Commission are as under:                                       (Figures  in Crore)Year	Budget Estimate (BE)	Revised Estimate (RE)	Expenditure incurred2018-19	24.00	24.00	22.782019-20	25.00	25.00	23.952020-21	26.00	21.88	20.98 State Commissions for Women (SCW) are set up by the respective State Governments / Union Territories (UT) and are not subordinate to NCW. They do not function under administrative control of either Ministry of Women and Child Development (MWCD) or NCW. Funds are allocated to the SCWs by the respective States/ UTs. (b) 	The expenditure incurred by NCW vis-a-vis budget allocation shows that funds allocation to the Commission is adequate to meet their requirements.(c) 	No, Sir.(d)	 No such proposal is presently under the consideration of the Government.(e) 	Does not arise.******
