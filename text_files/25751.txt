                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1576






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Improvement in Financial Status




Sudarshan Bhagat














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there has been qualitative improvement in the financial condition of the people living in North Eastern States of the country during the last five years;(b)	if so, the State-wise details thereof; and (c)	if not, the details of development works carried out by the Government in these States? 






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of Development of North Eastern Region[Shri  G. Kishan Reddy](a)	to (c)  As per latest data available with National Statistical Office, the per capita Net State Domestic Product (NSDP) at current prices (base year 2011-12)in the North Eastern States has increased in last five years. The details of State-wise per capita net State Domestic Product at current prices from 2015-16 to 2019-20 is given in the table below: Table: Per capita Net State Domestic Product (NSDP) at current prices(base year 2011- 12) (in Rs.)<pre>State	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Arunachal Pradesh	116985	123744	139029	149634	164615Assam	60817	66330	75151	82837	90758Manipur	55447	59345	71507	75229	84746Meghalaya	68836	73753	77504	84725	92175Mizoram	114055	127107	155222	176620	204018Nagaland	82466	91347	102003	117691	130230Sikkim	245987	280729	349163	380926	425656Tripura	84267	91596	100444	112849	125191</pre>Source: National Statistical Office, Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, GoI	Further, Government has implemented various schemes and projects in the North Eastern Region to strengthen infrastructure in rail, roads, air connectivity, telecommunication, power and inland waterways. Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region has also implemented a number of projects in the physical infrastructure relating to water supply, power, connectivity, specially the projects promoting tourism and social sector for creation of infrastructure in the areas of primary and secondary sectors of education and health.
