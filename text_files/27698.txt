                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3407






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Facilities of ESI Hospitals




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the State-wise total number of hospitals and dispensaries run by ESIC along with the number of beneficiaries including Rajasthan; and(b)whether the facilities of some ESIC hospitals have been made available to the general public by charging nominal user charge and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	The State-wise total number of hospitals and dispensaries including Rajasthan is placed at Annexure-I.	The State-wise total number of beneficiaries including Rajasthan is placed at Annexure-II. (b):	Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) Corporation has approved to allow non-insured persons/general public to avail medical services through its underutilized hospitals at Alwar, (Rajasthan), Bihta, (Bihar), Gulbarga (Karnataka), Bareilly, Varanasi, Lucknow and Kanpur (Uttar Pradesh).       	The ESIC Corporation has been taking nominal user charges from such beneficiaries, availing medical services from these hospitals.   Further, ESIC hospital at Gulbarga (Karnatka) is providing IPD services @ 5% of Central Government Health Services (CGHS) package rates, while ESIC hospital at Bihta (Bihar) and Alwar (Rajasthan) are providing medical services to non-IPs without charging any user fee.**                                                       *****
