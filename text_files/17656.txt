                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1606






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Problems of Fishermen in Orissa




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has reviewed the problems of fishermen of Orissa; (b) if so, the details of the steps taken by the Government;(c) whether the Government has any plan for fisheries in other States including Orissa; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a)	& (b):  The developmental concerns of fishermen of Odisha including progress on implementation of various schemes are reviewed and monitored by Government at regular intervals. Further, various welfare schemes are being implemented by the Government to address the problems of the fishermen such as saving-cum-relief, motorization of traditional crafts, safety of fishermen at sea, marketing infrastructure, assistance to fishermen for procurement of FRP boats, assistance for deep sea fishing vessels, establishment of fishing harbours and fish landing centres, dredging of fishing harbours, development of ice plants, cold storages, development of retail and whole sale fish markets, group accident insurance schemes, model fishermen villagesbesides the schemes for development of inland fisheries sector.Further, the Government of Odisha under State Plan Scheme is also assisting marine fishermen affected during fishing ban in turtle conservation area.(c)	& (d) The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing various schemes/ programmes for development and promotion of fisheries and aquaculture in the country including Odisha. Recently, the Department of Fisheries, Government of India has launched a new flagship Scheme namely ‘Pradhan Mantri MatsyaSampada Yojana (PMMSY): A scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India’. The PMMSYinter-alia, provides financial assistance forenhancement of fish production and productivity related activities, infrastructure and post-harvest management in fisheries and aquaculture sector, fisheries management and regulatory framework in the country including Odisha. *****
