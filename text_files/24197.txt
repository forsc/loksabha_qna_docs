                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

26






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Review of Stimulus Package




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has reviewed the stimulus package announced last year for revival of the economy after lockdown;b)	if so, the details thereof along with the benefit to each sector of the economy; andc)	the details of sector wise financial assistance announced and actual assistance provided so far? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):   Yes, Sir. The review of the progress made in implementation of the stimulus package announced under AatmaNirbhar Bharat Packages is done regularly.   The packages comprise of schemes for various sectors of the economy and also schemes having impact across sectors. AatmaNirbhar Bharat Packages comprise of various long term schemes/programs to boost the economy whose outcomes would be visible over a period of time.  A Statement indicating measures announced under the package is at Annexure-I.
