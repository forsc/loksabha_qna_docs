                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1861






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Distribution of Vaccination




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat













                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a)	Whether the Government has prepared any action plan for vaccine distribution in the country. (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Union Government has received any complaints regarding implementation of the aforesaid action plan; and (d)	if so, the remedial measures taken or proposed by the Government to address the hardship faced by the State/UTs regarding implementation of action plan? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d) Government of India has been supplying Covid-19 vaccines free of cost to the States/UTs for administration to prioritized beneficiaries as recommended by NEGVAC under the National Covid Vaccination Programme. Advance visibility of these vaccine allocations i.e. the total doses of vaccine that would be available to a State/UT, is provided to States/UTs 15 days in advance so as to enable States/UTs to plan for acceleration of vaccination coverage while being cognizant of the available vaccines.The allocation of vaccines to States & UTs is being done on the basis of pro-rata target population, the progress of vaccination in the State/UT and vaccine wastage.Some instances of fake Covid Vaccination Camps in Maharashtra and West Bengal have come to the notice of Government of India. Concern State Governments have been directed to investigate these incidences and take stringent legal and administrative actions.
