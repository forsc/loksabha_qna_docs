                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Revamping of Tuli Paper Mill




Tokheho Yepthomi














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has started the process of revamping of the Tuli Paper Mill in Nagaland;(b) if so, the details thereof and the progress made so far in this regard; and(c) if not, the time by which the revamping work is likely to be commissioned?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARVIND GANPAT SAWANT)(a) to (c): The Nagaland Pulp & Paper Company (NPPC), District Tuli, Nagaland is a subsidiary of the Hindustan Paper Corporation Ltd. (HPC). This unit has been non-operational since 1992. The National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) through an order dated 13.06.2018 directed the initiation of the Corporate Insolvency Resolution Process (CIRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), 2016 against HPC. The NCLT, during the hearing of the case on 02.05.2019, has ordered for the liquidation of HPC.*****
