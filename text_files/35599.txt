                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Educational Institutions in Bihar




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the detail list of Centrally Sponsored Educational Institutes established in Bihar since, 2014; (b) the number of students enrolled each year, the member of teaching faculty and the number of non-teaching staff in the said institutes and the details thereof, since 2014;(c)  the number of graduate students passed out from the said institutes, since 2014; (d) the funds sanctioned and released for the overall development of said institutes during the last three years and the current year; (e)  whether the total sanctioned funds have not been released by the Government; (f)  if so, the details thereof; and (g) the steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to release the remaining funds for the overall development of said institutes located in Bihar? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a):The list of Centrally Funded Educational Institutes established in Bihar since 2014 is as below:<pre>S.No	Name of the Institute	Year of Establishment 1	Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Bodhgaya	20152	Mahatama Gandhi Central University (MGCU), Motihari	20163	Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Bhagalpur – (PPP Mode)	2017</pre>(b) :The number of students enrolled since 2014 (year-wise),  number of teaching faculty and number of non-teaching staff in these institutes is below:<pre>S.No	Name of the Institute	Students Enrolled		2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-20	2021-221	IIM, Bodhgaya	NA*	30	54	38	70	117	200	2952	MGCU, Motihari	NA*	NA*	273	290	55	474	812	6613	IIIT, Bhagalpur	NA*	NA*	NA*	67	100	68	120	251*   Not Applicable</pre><pre>No. of Teaching and Non- Teaching StaffS.No	Name of the Institute	No, of Teaching Faculty	No. of Non Teaching Staff 1	IIM, Bodhgaya	35	302	MGCU, Motihari	128	403	IIIT, Bhagalpur	18	22</pre>(c): The number of graduate students passed out from these institutes, since 2014 is as below:<pre>S.No	Name of the Institute	No. of Graduate students passed out since 2014 1	IIM, Bodhgaya	2912	MGCU, Motihari	4743	IIIT, Bhagalpur	67</pre>(d) to (f):	The funds sanctioned and released during the last three years and the current year are as below:<pre>(Rs. in Crore)S.No.	Name of the Institute	Fund Sanctioned	Fund Released		2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-221.		IIM, Bodhgaya	27.05	62.72	56.42	61.52	27.05	62.72	56.42	61.522.		MGCU, Motihari	13.62	29.91	35.98	34.55	13.62	29.91	35.98	31.403.		IIIT, Bhagalpur	74 (Total)	0.00	1.33	8.59	21.00</pre> (g): Funds are released to the institutes periodically as per the requirement of the institute within the budgetary provision.***
