                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Lack of Ambulance Service in Jammu and Kashmir




Mohammad Akbar Lone














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether there is lack of ambulance services at Primary Health Centres in Baramullah, Bandipore and Kupwara districts of UT of Jammu and Kashmir; and(b) if so, the details thereof and corrective steps taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b) : As reported by UT Government of Jammu & Kashmir there are adequate number of Ambulances  in place at each of the Primary Health Centres of district Baramulla, Bandipora and  Kupwara.  Districts of Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir. The details are as under : District	No. of PHCs 	No. of Ambulances at PHCsBaramulla	31	23Bandipora	06	04Kupwara	31	20
