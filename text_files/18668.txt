                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

17






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Computer Education at Village Level




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to provide at least one computer in each Panchayat in the country to achieve the target of imparting computer education at village level;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Uttar Pradesh;(c)	the details of the amount allocated to State Governments including Uttar Pradesh for the said purpose during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(d)	whether the amount made available is sufficient for the said purpose; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor along with the steps taken by the Government to increase the allocated amount for the said purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (e) A statement is laid on the table of the House STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN PARTS (a) to (e) IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 17 FOR ANSWER ON 02.02.2021 REGARDING COMPUTER EDUCATION AT VILLAGE LEVEL___________________________________________________________________________(a), (d) & (e) Panchayat being local government which is part of the State list of 7th Schedule of the Constitution of India, is a State subject and is responsibility of State. However, Ministry of Panchayati Raj (MoPR) supplements the efforts of the State including provision of Computers through its schemes and programmes. Under the restructured scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) being implemented w.e.f. 2018-19, this Ministry has been supplementing the efforts of state governments in this regard by providing financial support for computers and peripherals to States/ UTs for GPs on a limited scale. Further, the untied/ basic grants under 15th Finance Commission can be used by Rural Local Bodies (RLBs) for location-specific felt needs, except for salary or other establishment expenditure.(b) The details of number of GPs for which computer was approved under the Annual Action Plan of States/ UTs including Uttar Pradesh during 2018-19, 2019-20 & 2020-21 (till date) is given in Annexure -I. No computers were approved during 2017-18 under the erstwhile scheme of Capacity Building-Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan (CB-PSA). (c) The details of funds approved under the Annual Action Plan for Computers, State-wise during 2018-19, 2019-20 & 2020-21 (till date) is given in Annexure – II.*****
