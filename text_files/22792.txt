                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4036






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






AYUSH Treatment for COVID-19




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA,YOGA AND NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Indian Medical Association has expressed concerns about issuing AYUSH treatment protocol for COVID-19 treatment including alternative modes of treatment;(b) if so, the details thereof including the basis of anecdotal evidence and individual subjective experiences, if any; and(c) the other grounds which were taken into consideration before implementing the AYUSH treatment protocol?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a):	National Medical Commission (NMC) has informed that they have not received any such concern from India Medical Association about issuing AYUSH treatment protocol for COVID-19 treatment including alternative modes of treatment.(b) & (c): In view of (a) above, the questions do not arise.
