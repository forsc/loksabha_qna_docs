                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5202






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Performance of Schemes Launched




Sunil Dattatray Tatkare













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the schemes launched under the Ministry during the last three years along with the details of the targets set and the achievements made under each of the said schemes;(b)	the details of the proposals received from various States along with the proposals approved by the Government under the schemes sponsored by the Ministry, State-wise;(c)	the details of the funds allocated and utilised for implementation of various schemes of Ministry during each of the last four years and current years; scheme and State-wise;(d)	whether the Government has set any time-limit for completing the pending schemes and if so, the details thereof; and (e)	whether the Government has any mechanism to monitor the funds allocated to the States/organisations for the purpose and if so, the details thereof and the action taken against the Non-Governmental Organisation (NGOs), if any, found guilty in this regard?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a): Ministry of Coal has not launched any new scheme during the last three years. (b):  Central Sector Schemes (CSSs) of Ministry of Coal are not directly linked with any State and as such no proposal has been received from various States.  (c):- Details of funds allocated and utilized for implementation of CSSs of the Ministry during last four years and current years are given below:-(Rs in Crore)Name of Scheme	Name of Sub - Schemes	2019-20 (upto June, 2019)	2018-19	2017-18	2016-17	2015-16		BE / RE	Expenditure	BE / RE	Expenditure	BE /RE	Expenditure	BE /RE	Expenditure	BE /RE	ExpenditureExploration of Coal and Lignite 	Promotional Exploration	100	10	150	121.005	60	55.78	50	45	115	105.05	Detail Drilling in Non CIL Blocks	500	190	350	309.55	115	103.5	89.5	80.55	168	151.2Research & Development	 	25	9.34	10/25	24.27	10	8.8	10	8.5	20.25	18Conservation, Safety and Infrastructural Development in Coal Mines (including Tribal Sub Plan) 	Environment Measures and Subsidence Control	0.5	0	0.5	0	0.5	0.0531	0.5	0	0.5	0	Conservation and Safety	4	0	59.5	59.5	200	200	80/170	170	170	170	Development & Transport Infrastructure	130.50	43.50	140	140	299.5	299.5	70/180	180	75	75 (d): The CSSs of Ministry of Coal are ongoing schemes, which are reviewed for continuation or otherwise from time to time as per applicable guidelines. (e): All S&T projects under Research & Development (S&T) scheme are monitored by Standing Scientific Research Committee (SSRC) of Ministry of Coal (MoC). Further, the scheme of Conservation, Safety and Infrastructure development in coal mines are monitored by Coal Conservation & Development Advisory Committee (CCDAC) through Coal Controller Organisation. No project/scheme is awarded to any Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO) by the ministry.  *****
