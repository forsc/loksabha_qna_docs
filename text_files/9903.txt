                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3372






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Scheme for Top Class Education Scholarship Scheme




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of the “scheme for top class education” scholarship scheme;(b) the details of steps taken by the Government to increase the awareness of this scheme among students;and(c) the college/university-wise number of students who have received the benefits of this scheme?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')The Schemes for Top Class Education Scholarship are being implemented by the Department ofEmpowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD), Department of Social Justice andEmpowerment(DoSJE) and Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA). As per information received fromthem, the status is as follows: -<pre>(a):S.No. Name of Department/MinistryName of Scheme Salient Features1 Department ofEmpowerment ofPersons withDisabilities(DEPwD)Central Sector umbrellaScheme of Scholarshipsfor Students withDisabilites- Top ClassEducation Scholarship forGraduate Degree/ PostGraduate Degree/Diploma level courses innotified institutes ofexcellence in Educationidentified by DEPwDThis scheme is being implemented forGraduate Degree/Post-graduateDegree/Diploma level courses in notifiedinstitutes of excellence in educationidentified by DEPwD. Till 2017-18, onlyPost- graduate Degree/ Diploma coursesin notified institutes of excellence ineducation were allowed. However, fromthe academic year 2018-19, graduate levelcourses in 240 notified institutes have alsobeen included in this Scheme.The salient features of this scheme are :a) No. of slots: 300 + Renewal students.b) Parental Income Not exceeding Rs.6lakh p.a.c) Quantum of financial assistance:(i) Maintenance allowance: @ Rs.3000/-p.m. for hostellers and Rs.1500/-p.m. forday scholars.(ii) Special Allowances (related to typesof disabilities like reader allowance,escort allowance, helper allowance etc.):Rs.2000/- p.m.(iii) Books & Stationery: Rs.5000/- p.a.(iv) Reimbursement of tuition fees andnon-refundable charges paid/payable tothe Institute: Up to Rs.2.00 lakh p.a.(v) Reimbursement of expenses forpurchase of a computer with accessories:Rs.30000/- per awardee as a onetimegrant for the entire course.(vi) Reimbursement of expenses forpurchase of Aids and Assistive Devicesincluding necessary software related tothe particular disability of the selectedcandidate: Rs. 30,000/- as a one-timegrant for entire course.2 Department of SocialJustice andEmpowerment (DSJ&E)Scheme of Top classEducation of SC studentsThe scheme provides for scholarship toSC students who secure admission in theinstitutions notified under the Scheme,having annual family income of Rs. 6lakh or less for promoting qualityeducation amongst SC students. Atpresent, total of 220 institutes are notifiedunder the Scheme.The students of notified institutes aregiven(i) full tution fee and non-refundablecharges(there is a ceiling of 2.00 lakh p.a.for private sector institutions).(ii) living expenses to the beneficiaryRs.2220 p.m.(iii)books and stationery Rs. 3000/ p.a.and(iv) a latest computer/laptop of reputedbrand with accessories like UPS andprinter limited to Rs. 45000/- per studentas one time assistance during the course.3 Ministry of TribalAffairsTop class Education forST studentsThe scheme is for ST Students to getHigher Education in prestigiouseducational institutions in the country.The salient features of the scheme are:(i)Scholarship is given to ST students forpursuing studies in prescribed courses inany of the 246 institutions of excellenceacross the country like IITs, AIIMS,IIMs, NIITs, etc. identified by theMinistry.(ii)Total number of scholarships is 1000per year.(iii)Family income from all sourcesshould not exceed Rs.6.00 lakhs perannum.(iv)Scholarship amount includes tuitionfees, living expenses and allowances forbooks and computer.</pre>(b) To bring awareness about these schemes, steps taken by the concerned ministries are as follows:-Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities:- The scheme has been on boarded NationalScholarship Portal (NSP). In order to popularize the scheme, a numbers of initiatives have beenundertaken such as publishing of advertisements across the country through national newspapers andregional newspapers, sensitizing the states through video conferencing. To create awareness about NSPState Governments release advertisement on web-site of their Social Welfare Departments.Department of Social Justice and Empowerment:- A radio programme namely ‘Sawarti Jaayain JivanKi Rahain’ is broadcast time to time to increase the awareness of this scheme among students. Thenotified institutions are also advised time to time to advertise the scheme guidelines on their Notice Boardto increase the awareness among the students.Ministry of Tribal Affairs:- The scheme is implemented through National Scholarship Portal (NSP). Alllisted college/institutes are advised to bring awareness about the scheme amongst the students andnecessary instructions are issued to register on the portal.(c):- The details of the beneficiaries in respect of Department of Empowerment of Persons withDisabilities, Department of Social Justice & Empowerment and Ministry of Tribal Affairs are atAnnexure-I, Annexure-II and Annexure-III respectively.*****
