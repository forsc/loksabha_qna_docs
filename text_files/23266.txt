                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4225






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Agriculture Training of Haryana Students




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of students of Haryana State being imparted skill development training by various ministries and the Skill Development Ministry;(b)	 the number of trainees who got employment;.(c)	the percentage of female students out of the said number;(d) 	whether the Government is imparting training to the students of Haryana in the field of agriculture as per the need of State; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




(a) to (c): 	Under the aegis of the National Skill Development Mission launched in July 2015, the Government is implementing more than 40 skill development schemes/ programmes across 20 Central Ministries/ Departments, for providing skill development training on a pan India basis, including the State of Haryana. The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing the following Schemes for skill development of youth including women in Haryana viz. Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) Scheme, National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS), and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) through the Industrial Training Institutes. The details of persons trained under these schemes since 2016-17 in Haryana are given below:Sl. JSS	29,872	79.68%	*	* 3	NAPS	1,06, 092	87.05%	*	*4	CTS (ITIs)[2019-20]	48,593	18.77%	*	*5	Fee-Based Programme by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) 	2,83,234	62.75%	54,443	26.63%* These Schemes do not have placement as a criterionAs regards skill training by other Central Ministries, under Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) of the Ministry of Rural Development, 30,957 candidates in the State of Haryana have been trained from 2014-15 till December 2020, out of which 27,116 candidates have been placed. Under the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAU-NULM) of the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, 19,980 candidates have been trained in the State of Haryana since its launch in September 2013. During this period, 2,906 beneficiaries have been assisted for setting up individual/ group micro enterprises under this scheme.  (d) & (e): 	13,351 candidates in the State of Haryana have been given training in the field of agriculture under PMKVY 2.0 (2016-2020) as on 19.01.2021. Besides, 7,809 candidates have been trained in the field of agriculture under the fee-based programmes of NSDC. Further, Agriculture Skill Council of India (ASCI), under the aegis of MSDE, facilitates skill training programmes in Haryana under various Central and State funded schemes like PMKVY, Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture, and schemes being implemented through the Haryana Skill Development Mission. Skill training in Haryana has been imparted in 33 Job Roles/ Qualification Packs developed by ASCI across different programmes.
