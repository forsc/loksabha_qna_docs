                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3880






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Quality of NH Construction




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of complaints received by the Government regarding poor quality of construction of National Highways (NHs) during the last three years and the action taken thereon in the country, State-wise with particular reference of Thane district, Maharashtra;(b)	whether the Government has developed any system to monitor/inspect the quality of construction work  of NHs;(c)	if so, the details thereof and the inspections conducted by the Government during the said period along with the outcome thereof;(d)	the steps taken by the Government to ensure quality of construction of NH; and (e)	the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government against the contractors/companies found guilty in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 Addressal of Complaints regarding quality of Construction Work on National Highways and corrective measures taken thereof is a continuous process. As regards Complaints in Thane District, two complaints had been received by Ministry regarding the ongoing projects of 2 laning with paved shoulders of Shahpur – Patgaon – Khopoli section of NH-548A. Based on inspection carried out by MoRTH Officials, Non-Conformity Reports (NCRs) were issued in respect of cracks in Concrete panels and the defective panels were repaired as per guidelines. Further, the projects are being reviewed from time to time and the Contractors have been directed to expedite progress of the work by mobilizing additional resources.  (b) to (e)	 Works of National Highways are executed duly following the Specifications and Standards laid down by Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRT&H) and Indian Roads Congress (IRC). Consultants are appointed by executing agencies of the Ministry such as NHAI/ NHIDCL/ BRO/State PWDs for day-to-day supervision of the works at site. In case, any deficiencies are observed regarding quality, Non-Conformity Reports (NCRs) are issued and it is ensured that Contractors/ Concessioners duly rectify the quality issues in a time bound manner. Officials of NHAI/NHIDCL/BRO/ State PWDs and Ministry also undertake inspection on random basis and take corrective measures to remove such deficiencies. Ministry has also constituted a Quality Control Zone (QCZ) to have random inspection of NH works across the country and necessary instructions are being issued to executing agencies / Consultants as well as Contractors/ Concessioners to ensure quality works at site.    ******
