                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2548






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Payment of Wages under MGNREGS




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the mechanism adopted by the Government for the payment of wages to beneficiaries under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS) as on date;(b)	whether the Government is aware that some difficulties have been faced by the beneficiaries owing to either illiteracy, lack of bank/post office branches nearby in rural areas;(c)	if so, the remedial action taken/likely to be taken by the Government for revamping banks/post offices in rural areas; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system has been adopted under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) for wage payment. The Government has started National Electronic Fund Management System (NeFMS) in 24 States and 1 UT for direct payment of wages into the account of MGNREGA workers. All the DBT payments of wages under MGNREGS are routed through Public Financial Management System (PFMS). (b) to (d):	Banks have engaged last mile functionaries for ensuring payment of wages of MGNREGA workers and basic banking facility through hand-held devices. The Department of Posts is also providing Subscriber Identification Module (SIM) based hand-held devices to Branch post offices to offer online postal & financial services in the rural areas. The Ministry has provided relaxation in DBT payment of MGNREGA wages in some rural areas where bank/post office network is not adequate & internet connectivity is poor.
