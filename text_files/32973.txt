                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

428






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Bharatmala Pariyojana




Geeta Kora













                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)		the present status of ongoing road projects under Bharatmala 	Pariyojana in Jharkhand, Jammu & Kashmir and Madhya Pradesh;(b)		the details of the projects completed in various phases under the 	said pariyojana till date in the aforesaid States/UT; and(c)		the time by which the remaining projects are likely to be 	completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)  The status of projects identified, approved & awarded and length completed under Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I in the States of Jharkhand, Jammu & Kashmir and Madhya Pradesh are as under: Name of State/UT	Total Identified	Awarded	Approved (under bidding/award)	Length completed (Km)	Length (Km)	Cost(Rs. in crore)	Length (Km)	Cost(Rs. in crore)	Length (Km)	Cost(Rs. in crore)	Jharkhand	738	19,768	432	10,445	-	-	280Jammu & Kashmir UT	398	22,935	175	14,857	-	-	35Madhya Pradesh	2,927	49,831	1,320	26,436	166	3,016	425 	All projects envisaged under Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I are targeted for completion by 2024-25.*****
