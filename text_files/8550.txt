                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Setting up of Science Cities in Madhya Pradesh




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                








                                                    Annpurna Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Science Cities have been set up in the State of Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand;(b)	if so, the place-wise details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is proposing to set up science cities in each of district of Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) No, Sir. However, Regional Science Centers have been set up at Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh and Ranchi, Jharkhand.(c), (d) & (e) No, Sir. No such proposal has been received from the State Governments for setting up of Science Cities in Madhya Pradesh & Jharkhand. However, Science Centres are proposed to be set up in Madhya Pradesh at Jabalpur and Ujjain.
