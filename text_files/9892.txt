                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






CIIL




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Central Institute of Indian Languages (CIIL) imparts training tonon-native school teachers, prospective teachers and general public through tenmonth diploma course to teachers across the country in all southern languages;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of people including teachers enrolled for the said diplomacourse of CIIL during the last five years, year/language-wise including Kannada,Tamil, Telugu and Malayalam; and(d) the details of the total fund allocated for promotion of Indian languages bythe CIIL?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): Yes Sir. The Central Institute of Indian Languages (CIIL), Mysoreimparts training to non native school teachers, prospective teachers through tenmonth diploma course to teachers in its Regional Language Centres located atMysore, Lucknow, Patiala, Solan, Pune, Bhubaneswar, Guwahati. The details oflanguage courses imparted at different centres are as follows:<pre>No Name of Centres Languages taught1 Southern Regional Language Centre,MysoreTamil, Kannada,Telugu, Malayalam2 Eastern Regional Language Centre,BhubaneswarBengali, Maithili,Oriya, Santali3 Western Regional Language Centre,PuneGujarati, Konkani,Marathi, Sindhi4 Northern Regional Language Centre,PatialaDogri, Kashmiri,Punjabi, Urdu5 Urdu Teaching and Research Centre,SolanUrdu6 Urdu Teaching and Research Centre,LucknowUrdu7 North Eastern Regional LanguageCentre, GuwahatiAssamese, Bodo,Manipuri, Nepali</pre>The number of people including teachers enrolled in different diplomacourse in CIIL during the last five years is given in annexure.(d): Grants allocated to CIIL for promotion of Indian Languages during thelast five years is as under:(Rs. in crores)2015-16 2016-17 2017-18 2018-19 2019-2030.00 40.50 40.50 40.07 45.07***
