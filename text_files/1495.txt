                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Scheme for Pension and Medical Aid to Artistes




Srinivas Kesineni













                                                    Durga Prasad Rao Balli
                                                








                                                    H. Vasanthakumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of the Scheme for Pension and Medical Aid to Artistes (SPMAA).(b)  the quantum of amount given to each beneficiary under the SPMAA .(c)  the number of film/TV artistes who have benefited under this Scheme during the last four years in the country including  Tamil Nadu, Telangana and Andhara Pradesh; and(d)  whether the Government is contemplating to expand such scheme to Drama artistes also and  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) The Government is implementing a Scheme namely “Scheme for Pension and Medical Aid to Artistes”.  The objective of the Scheme is to improve financial and socio-economic status of the old aged artistes and scholars who have contributed significantly in their specialized fields of arts, letters etc. but leading a miserable life or are in penury condition. (b)  An amount of maximum Rs.4,000/- per month is being given to each beneficiary.(c)  Art form-wise data is not maintained by the Ministry.  (d)  The drama artists are already covered under the Scheme.
