                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Self-Reliance in the Field of Electrical Equipments




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the efforts being made by the Government to achieve self-reliance in the field of electrical equipments (lights installed during festivals and special occasions); and(b) 	the efforts being made by the Government to reduce the import of the said equipment from China?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) :	Ministry of Power (MoP) vide orders dated 28.07.2020 and 17.09.2020, has granted purchase preference to local suppliers with respect to Power sector, pursuant to Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade’s (DPIIT’s) revised order dated 04.06.2020 under Public Procurement (Preference to Make in India) Order, 2017. In order to encourage  self-reliance  in Light emitting diode (LED)  Lighting,  a  Production  Linked  Incentive (PLI) scheme  has  been approved by Government of India, which  will attract investors and boost local manufacturing of components.Further, Department  of  Commerce  has  announced  a  new  policy  of  Compulsory Registration Order (CRO),  under  which all LED Products will have to be registered after testing.**********
