                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1285






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Operation of Resorts/Hotels in Elephant Corridors




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Resorts, Hotels, Home stays operating illegally in Elephant corridors of Nilgiris district; (b)	the number of Resort, Hotel, Home stay owners who are prosecuted for violation of environmental guidelines; (c)	whether due to these encroachments on forest land, human-animal conflict is increasing; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)&(b)	As per information received from the State of Tamil Nadu, there are 38 Resorts complex existing in the said elephant corridor. Further, as reported by the State illegal resorts falling in the notified elephant corridor and disturbing movement of elephants have been sealed by the District Administration. (c)&(d)	No encroachment has been reported on forest land due to the said resorts.  There is no evidence on increasing human-wildlife conflict in the said corridor.  The details of mortality of wild animals in the said corridor are as below:Sl. No.	Details of Incident	Date1.		One male elephant electrocuted	12.09.20142.		One Makhna elephant drowned in a septic tank	22.03.20183.		One female tiger poisoned	20.11.20204.		One male elephant burned	19.01.20215.		One male sloth bear electrocuted	10.08. 2021***
