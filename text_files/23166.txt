                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Adequate Hostels for Students of the Universities




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether all the Central Universities in the country have adequate hostels for the students and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) the details of number of students in each Central University and total number of hostel rooms; (c) whether it is true that there have been demands from students of Central Universities for more hostel accommodation and if so, the details thereof; (d) the steps taken by the Government to provide adequate hostel rooms and hostel facilities for students of Central Universities and if so, the details thereof; (e) whether the Government has taken up the matter of inadequate hostels with Central Universities and if so, the details thereof; and (f) the steps taken by the Government to help and support the construction of new hostels by the Central Universities and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): As per information made available by University Grants Commission (UGC), total 84,299 students were residing in 555 hostel campuses in 42 Central Universities, as on 01.04.2020. In three Sanskrit Central Universities, total 4660 students can be accommodated in 32 hostel campuses, as on 18.03.2021. The Central University wise details of number of hostel campuses, enrolled students in the University and total number of students residing in hostels are given at annexure.(c) to (f): From the year 2017-2018, the Central Universities can avail funding from Higher Education Financing Agency (HEFA) for development of University infrastructure including construction of hostels as per their needs and demands. ******
