                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

454






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Privatisation of Fair Price Shops




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the number of fair price shops operationalised under PDS across the country including Tamil Nadu; (b) 	whether the Government is planning to privatise the shops running under Public Distribution System across the country, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (c) 	whether the Government is aware that it has been opposed by all the shop owners running fair price shops within the country, if so, the details thereof and the Government''s stand thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	There are 5.39 lakh operational Fair Price Shops (FPSs) across the country including Tamil Nadu. A State-wise statement showing the number of Fair Price Shops is Annexed.(b):  	No, Sir.(c): 	Question does not arise.****
