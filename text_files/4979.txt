                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4579






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Pradhan Mantri Innovative Learning Programme




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is working on introducing “Pradhan Mantri InnovativeLearning Programme” to enhance and inculcate the talent of children in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the facilities proposed to be provided for quality education and enhancing the talent inthe children and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government proposes to implement the said programme in schools locatedin Anand district of Gujarat; and(e) if so, the number of schools where the said programme is likely to be implemented alongwith the names of schools and the time thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (e) The Government intend to implement the Pradhan Mantri Innovative LearningProgramme to enhance and inculcate the talent of children in the country. The modalities forthe same are being worked out.****
