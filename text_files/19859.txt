                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1017






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Entrepreneurship Skill Development Programme




Kani K. Navas













                                                    Malook Nagar
                                                








                                                    Ajay (Teni) Misra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of youths who are being given training under Entrepreneurship Skill Development Programme(ESDP), State/UT and District-wise including Tamil Nadu;(b)	whether the Government has the details regarding the number of people who got employment through this programme;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	the State/UT-wise including Haryana and district wise total number of training institutions functioning under this scheme; and(e) the State/UT-wise and district-wise total amount spent by the Government in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (e): Entrepreneurship Skill Development Programme (ESDP) is being implemented by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises with the objective to nurture the talent of youth by making them aware of the  various aspects required for setting up Medium and Small Enterprises. The programme focuses on motivating the youth representing different sections of the society including SC/ST/Women, differently abled, Ex-servicemen and BPL persons to consider self-employment or entrepreneurship as one of the career options. The activities conducted under the ESDP scheme include Industrial Motivational Campaign (IMC), Entrepreneurship Awareness Programme (EAP), Entrepreneurship-cum- Skill Development Programme (E-SDP) and Management Development Programme (MDP). 		Details about the beneficiaries covered and expenditure under the programme during 2018-19 to 2019-20 are as under:- S.No	2018-19	2019-201	Total no. of Trainees	TotalExpenditure 	Total no. of Trainees	TotalExpenditure 2	9459	1,53,35,807	2,32,652	274816159.1State-wise break-up of beneficiaries and expenditure under the scheme is available at Annexure-I and district-wise break-up of beneficiaries and expenditure for the States of Haryana and Tamil Nadu is available at Annexure-II.Further, in order to inculcate entrepreneurship skills in the trainees to make them able to establish business, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), the flagship scheme of Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, has mandatory provision for 155 hours training module with the coverage of Employability and Entrepreneurship apart from the core curriculum on skill development. Out of total 1,06,89,788 beneficiaries covered under PMKVY 2.0, 2,77,243 could opt for self-employment.               In addition to the above, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) has introduced Pilot Project in November, 2019 with the objective to encourage the youth through entrepreneurship education, training, advocacy and easy access to entrepreneurship network, the project implemented through the Institutes under skill ecosystem  i.e. Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs), Polytechnics, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras (PMKKs) and Jan ShikshanSansthan (JSS), has the coverage of 10 States and 2 Union Territories (viz.
