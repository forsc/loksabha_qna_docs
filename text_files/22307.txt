                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3382






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Naxal Affected Districts in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether four districts in Jharkhand including Bokaro, Dhanbad, Giridih and Ramgarh have been identified as naxal-affected districts;(b)	if so, whether the Government has provided financial assistance to the above districts to identify and fill critical gaps in infrastructure and services between 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21; (c)	if so, the details of financial and other assistance provided to the districts in Jharkhand during the above period;(d)	the details of physical targets set and achieved in the said districts during the above period; and(e)	whether the Government has any plans to extend this scheme beyond 2020-21 and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a): Yes Sir, Bokaro, Dhanbad, Giridih and Ramgarh districts of Jharkhand are covered under Security Related Expenditure (SRE) Scheme and are considered as Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected  districts.(b): For filling the critical gaps of Public Infrastructure and Services in the worst LWE affected districts, funds are released to States under ‘Special Central Assistance (SCA) for the most LWE Affected Districts’. Bokaro and Giridih districts are included under SCA Scheme. -2-L.S.US.Q NO. 3382 FOR 16.03.2021(c): Funds released to Jharkhand under SCA Scheme are as under:                                                           (Rs. in crore)State	       2018-19	 2019-20    2020-21Jharkhand	433.33	 433.29	     199(d): During 2018-19 and 2019-20, 194 works have been undertaken for  Bokaro, of which 117 have been completed. For Giridih 490 works have been undertaken, of which 278 have been completed. For 2020-21, Rs.199 crores have been transferred to the State in the month of November-2020, which are yet to be transferred to the districts by the State.   (e):  The Scheme was approved for a period of 03 years from 2017-18 to 2019-20. The scheme has already been extended for one year 2020-21. ******
