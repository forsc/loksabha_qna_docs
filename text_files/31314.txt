                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3078






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






High Price of Covid-19 Tests at Airports




Rajmohan Unnithan













                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                








                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that some airports in the country including Rajiv Gandhi International Airport at Shamshahbad in Hyderabad and Kerala are conducting Covid-19 tests for national/ international passengers at higher price than normal market ratio and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has received any complaints/representations in this regard and if so, the details thereof and actions taken thereon; and(c) the measures taken/being taken by the Government to check above issue?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c): Concerned Airport Operators have informed that they are charging Rs. 500/- at Kannur, Cochin, Bengaluru, Delhi and Rs. 750/- at Hyderabad International airports for Standard RT-PCR test. There are two type of tests i.e. Standard RT-PCR test and Rapid RT-PCR (Molecular Test Systems) at the airport. The rates for standard RT-PCR are generally decided and notified by the respective State/UT Governments, which are followed by the laboratories operating at the airports.;;Ministry of Health and Family Welfare(MoHFW) has issued the revised guidelines on 30th November, 2021 in the view of reporting of a new variant named Omicron, classified as a variant of concern. Ministry of Civil Aviation in coordination with MoHFW and Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) is implementing these guidelines at International Airports. Ministry of Civil Aviation continuously monitors the implementation of the revised guideline of MoHFW and takes appropriate actions based on the evolving situation.
