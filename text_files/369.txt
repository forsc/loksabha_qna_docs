                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Funds Allocated under Various Tourism Schemes




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds allocated/granted under various tourism schemes of the Ministry to the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last three years; (b) 	the details of funds released and yet to be released under such schemes to the State during the last three years, district-wise; and (c) 	the time by which these funds are likely to be released?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Tourism provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) for projects to various States/UT Govts. Including the State of Uttar Pradesh that are complete as per existing scheme guidelines and are sanctioned subject to availability of scheme/funds, and utilisation of funds released earlier. The details of funds sanctioned and released under various schemes of the Ministry of Tourism to the State of Uttar Pradesh during last three years is at Annexure. Funds are released to the Implementing Agencies project wise and not the district wise in accordance with the milestones specified in the guidelines of the respective schemes. ********
