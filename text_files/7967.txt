                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1572






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Integration among Armed Services




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the progress made with regard to promoting integration among the three armed services; and(b) the details including the rank, power, authority and mandate of Chief of Defence Staff position as announced by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  Steps have been taken to promote integration among the three  armed forces from time to time, which include establishment of Integrated Defence Staff, two Tri-Service Commands, three Tri-Service Agencies in specialized fields, etc.	The Government has recently approved in principle the creation of a post of CDS.  An Implementation Committee was also approved and constituted to further determine and finalize the exact responsibilities, an enabling framework for this new post and all other issues involved to ensure smooth operationalization.*****
