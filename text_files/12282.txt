                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1187






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Battery Operating Stations




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is allowing/proposes to allow the existing retail outlets of the Petroleum products (Petrol/Diesel Pump) to establish/install the Battery Operating Stations as a large number of batteries operated two, three and four wheeler vehicles have been launched in the market; and(b) whether the Government has formulated any guidelines in this respect and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies have initiated battery swapping stations and charging stations at retail outlets on pilot basis. As on 01.01.2020, they have installed electric vehicle chargers at 48 retail outlets.(b) The revised guidelines on “Charging Infrastructure for Electric Vehicles” issued by Ministry of Power on 1.10.2019 are available in the website of Ministry of Power.****
