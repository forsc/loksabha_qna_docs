                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3096






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






NCSSR




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features and objectives of the National Centre of Sports Sciences and Research (NCSSR);(b) the details of the amount sanctioned and utilized for this purpose, State-wise including Chhattisgarh;(c) the details of the amount/grant provided University/College-wise during the last three years alongwith Universities/Medical Colleges identified for grant by the Government; and(d) whether the identification procedure of some more Universities/Medical Colleges for this purpose is under consideration of the Government and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) The scheme of National Centre of Sports Sciences and Research (NCSSR) aims to support high level research, education and innovation with respect to high performance of elite athletes. The scheme has two components: One is setting up of NCSSR center and other is focused on providing support for setting up of Sports Sciences Departments in selected Universities/Institutes and Sports Medicine Departments in selected Medical Colleges. The aims and objectives of NCSSR is enclosed at Annexure ‘A’.(b) & (c) The present approved cost of the scheme is Rs.107.00 crore for NCSSR and Rs.237.00 crore for support to Sports Sciences and Sports Medicine Departments in selected Universities and Medical Colleges.A statement of funds released for setting up of NCSSR Centre and selected Universities/Institutes for setting up of Sports Sciences Departments and selected Medical Colleges for setting up of Sports Medicine Departments is enclosed at Annexure ‘B’.(d) No, Sir.
