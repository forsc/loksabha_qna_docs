                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2674






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Coal Blocks




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any arrangement for transfer of fund of about Rs.560 Crores paid by M/S. Hindalco on account of Talabira-I coal block; and  (b) if not, the details thereof and whether the Government has made necessary advisory for release of claims of the State Governments?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) & (b) The amount of Rs.560 crore payable by M/s. Hindalco Industries Limited on account of  coal block Talabira -I is the additional levy amount for coal produced from Talabira -I coal block.  The issues of additional levy accruing either to Central Government or to  State Government concerned was examined in consultation with Ministry of Finance and Ministry of Law & Justice. Department of Legal Affairs, Ministry of Law & Justice was requested to obtain the opinion of Ld. Additional Solicitor General (ASG) in the matter.  Ld. ASG of India has opined that the amount would not accrue to the State Government.   ***
