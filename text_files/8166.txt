                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1773






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Violence during Muharram Processions




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details regarding the people being affected by the violence during  Muharram processions, State-wise;(b) the details regarding the people affected by the violence in West Bengal because of Muharram during the last five years; and(c) whether the Government has taken any steps to educate the people to decrease the violence by them during Muharram and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	 to (c)  ‘Police’ and ‘Public Order’ are State subjects under the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India and State Governments are responsible for prevention, detection, registration and investigation of crime and for prosecuting the criminals through their law enforcement agencies.  The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) does not maintain such data.*****
