                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

650






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Revival of Stressed MSMEs




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the number of stressed small and  micro companies during the period of Covid -19 within Tamil Nadu;(b)	the details of the number of small and medium companies declared bankrupt during the period  of Covid-19 within Tamil Nadu; and(c)	the steps being taken by  the Government for revival of these companies as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):	The state wise details of stressed small and micro companies during the period of covid-19, are not maintained in the Government of India. However, Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code was under suspension during this period.(c):	In order to revive the MSME with stressed accounts, the Government has announced Credit Guarantee Scheme for Subordinate Debt. Under this scheme, promoters of stressed MSMEs viz. SMA-2 and NPA accounts which are eligible for restructuring as per RBI guidelines on the books of the Lending institutions, will be provided with credit facilities to be covered under guarantee by the CGTMSE. The promoters will infuse the funds for revival of such Units. Moreover, Government has also announced Rs. 3 lakh crores Collateral free Automatic Loans under Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS)  for business, including MSMEs, for those accounts which are classified as SMA-0 and SMA-1.
