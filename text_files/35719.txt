                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Work under PMAY-G in Uttar Pradesh




Ashok Kumar Rawat













                                                    Arun Kumar Sagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of work being carried out under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Awas Yojana (PMAY-G) in the country particularly in Misrikh and Shahajahanpur Parliamentary Constituencies of Uttar Pradesh;(b) 	the present status of these works as on date; and(c) 	the details of the Central funds allocated for the said purpose including its utilization during each of the last three years as on date, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b) In order to achieve the target of “Housing for All” in rural areas, Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) is being implemented with effect from 1st April, 2016 to provide assistance to eligible rural households with overall target to construct 2.95 crore pucca houses with basic amenities by March, 2024. The targets allocated, houses sanctioned and houses completed under PMAY-G in the country and in districts related to Misrikh and Shahjahanpur Parliamentary Constituencies of Uttar Pradesh as on 15.03.2022 are given at Annexure I. (c)        The State-wise details of central funds released and utilized (including State Share) during each of the last three years i.e. financial year 2018-19 to 2020-21 are given in Annexure II.  *****
