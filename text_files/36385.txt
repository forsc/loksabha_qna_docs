                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3441






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Shortage of Guards




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether goods trains are being run during night hours without guards and if so, the reasons therefor;(b)	whether there is a shortage of guards as per the claims made by Moradabad Division and if so, the facts thereof;(c)	whether the Government is also contemplating on running trains without guards;(d)	if so, whether the Government has taken any steps in this matter till now and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether such a situation be a reasons for any major railway incident and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a):	Some goods trains are being run without guards during night hours in case of operational exigencies with extensive safety precautions as laid down under General and Subsidiary rules (G&SR).(b):	Presently there is some shortage of Guards. Against the total sanctioned strength of 843 Guards, currently there is vacancy of 169 Guards in Moradabad Division.(c) & (d):	End of Train Telemetry (EoTT) system has been provided as a Pilot Project on some of the freight trains.(e):	No untoward incident has been reported in the last three years on this account.    *****
