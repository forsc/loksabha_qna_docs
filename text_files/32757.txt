                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

350






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Flying Training Organisations in NER




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Flying Training Organisations approved by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) in North Eastern Region (NER); and(b) whether the Government has taken any action for setting up of Flight Training Schools, Aircraft Maintenance Engineering Schools or any such Civil Aviation Training Institutions in Meghalaya, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) and (b) The Ministry of Civil Aviation and its associated organisations have taken a number of steps to promote training of pilots in the country. In September 2020, the Airports Authority of India (AAI) came up with a liberalised FTO policy wherein the concept of airport royalty was abolished and land rentals were significantly rationalised for new FTOs being established at AAI airports. ;;Award letters for nine new FTOs at five AAI airports have been issued in May 2021 and October 2021. One of the award letters has been issued for an FTO to be established at Lilabari Airport in Assam;;Till date, no proposal has been received for setting up of Flying Training Schools, Aircraft Maintenance Engineering Schools or any such Civil Aviation Training Institution in Meghalaya.
