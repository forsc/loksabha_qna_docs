                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1687






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






NID Telangana




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any proposal for setting up of NationalInstitute of Design (NID) in Telangana;(b) whether the Government is aware that the CM of Telangana has submitted aMemorandum to PM in this regard when he met him recently; and(c) if so, what action the Government has so far taken on setting up of NID inTelangana?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) : As per information received from Department for Promotion of Industryand Internal Trade, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, a proposal has been receivedfor setting up of National Institute of Design (NID) in Telangana and a memorandumwas submitted by Hon’ble Chief Minister of Telangana to Hon’ble Prime Minister inthis regard.The National Design Policy 2007, with a view to spreading quality education indesign to all the regions of India stated setting up of four NIDs on the lines of NID,Ahmedabad. Accordingly, 4 new NIDs envisaged in the states of Andhra Pradesh,Assam, Madhya Pradesh and Haryana, located in Amravati, Jorhat, Bhopal andKurukshetra respectively, were approved in the meeting of the cabinet held on25.02.2014. All the 4 new NIDs have commenced academic activities and theircampuses are under construction. As all four NIDs envisaged under the NationalDesign Policy 2007 have been set up, there is currently, no plan to establish any newNID.**********
