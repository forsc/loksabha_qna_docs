                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2045






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Development of NHs in Gujarat




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether the Government has formulated any action plan for the 	development of National Highways (NH) and upgradation of State 	Highways passing through the State of Gujarat;(b)     if so, the details thereof;(c)    whether the Government has any programme for the maintenance 	of the said highways; and(d)     if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)     The Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways (NHs). The development and maintenance of NHs is a continuous process. Ministry regularly undertakes development of deficient and substandard State roads and notified them as National Highways. Further, the Ministry keeps on receiving proposals from the State Government of Gujarat for declaration of State roads as new NHs. The Ministry considers declaration of some State roads as new NHs from time to time based on requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority, traffic density, strategic importance and availability of funds. (b)     Details of State Roads notified as NHs in the State of Gujarat during the last Financial year and current year are as under: - Financial Year	2020-21	2021-22Total Length (Kms)	844 Kms 	141Kms National Highway Nos.	351K,351G,151K,848K,151AD,927C,927K	168G (c)     The Maintenance and Repair (M&R) of stretches of National Highway is a continuous process and is planned to keep all sections of National Highways in traffic worthy condition. At present, maintenance of recently declared National Highways are covered under Periodical Renewal (PR), Improvement of Riding Quality Programme (IRQP), Long Term Maintenance Scheme and Short Term Maintenance Scheme.      (d)     Works amounting to Rs. 64.64 crores are already sanctioned so far for the maintenance of aforesaid National Highways.   *****
