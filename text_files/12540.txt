                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1498






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Identity Cards for those included in NRC




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has issued National Identity Cards to the people included in the final list of NRC in Assam;(b) if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the time by which the Government proposes to issue the National Identity Card to the people included in the final list of NRC in Assam;(d) the number of minor applicants excluded from the NRC List in Assam;(e) whether 1.9 million people have been excluded from the NRC List in Assam and if so, time by which they will receive the order of rejection; and(f) the total amount of fund allocated to the State Coordinator of NRC, Assam, for execution of the entire NRC exercise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (e):On the direction of the Hon’ble Supreme Court, the hard copies of the supplementary list of inclusions and online family-wise list of exclusions have been published on 31stAugust, 2019. A total of 3,11,21,004 number of persons are found eligible for inclusion leaving out 19,06,657 number of persons. Any person not satisfied with the decisions for inclusion of name may prefer appeal before the designated Tribunal constituted under the Foreigners (Tribunals) Order, 1964.-2/… -2-L.S.US.Q.NO. 1498 FOR 11.02.2020Separate list of exclusion of children has not been published. No Identity Card to the people included in the NRC list in Assam has been issued.(f):	The approved cost of the scheme of Updation of National Register of Citizens (NRC), 1951 in Assam is Rs.1602.66 crore out of which Rs.1348.13 crore has been released to the Government of Assam till date.*****
