                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1024






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






ESI Hospital in Kurki Village, Karnataka




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has any plan to establish an ESI Hospital in Sy. No.141 of Kurki village, Narasapur Hobli, Kolar Taluk in Karnataka;(b)if so, the details thereof;(c)whether any representations have been received in this regard; and(d)if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a)to (d):	Based upon a proposal received from the Government of Karnataka, the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) has given “In principle” approval for setting up of a 100 bedded ESI hospital at Narsapura, Kolar in the state of Karnataka.	*******
