                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2557






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details regarding the present status of implementation of the Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY), State/UT-wise;(b)	the number of villages adopted under SAGY, State/UT-wise; and(c)	the amount of funds allocated and utilised so far, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	The Hon’ble Members of Parliament have identified 1,484 Gram Panchayats under Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) till 3rd July 2019. The Gram Panchayats adopted under SAGY prepared Village Development Plans (VDP) through a participatory process under the guidance of Hon’ble Members of Parliament. The VDP included prioritised time-bound activities to achieve holistic progress of the village. So far 1,297 Gram Panchayats have uploaded their VDP containing 68,407 projects, as per the data available on SAGY website (http://saanjhi.gov.in). Out of these, 38,031 (56%) projects have been completed as on 3rd July 2019. Under the SAGY framework, the development of Gram Panchayats is envisaged through convergence and implementation of existing Government Schemes and Programmes under the administrative control of respective Ministries without allocating additional funds.
