                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

51






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






AIIMS Projects




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the number of All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) under construction in the country, State/UT-wise; (b) the date of sanctioning/launching of each project along with the current status of these projects; (c) the time period within which these projects are proposed to be completed; (d) the number of Extra Corporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) machines and ICU beds available in the country, State/UT-wise; and (e) the number of ventilator ambulances available in the country, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	to (e) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 51* FOR 4TH FEBRUARY, 2022(a) to (e) Setting up of 22 new AIIMS has been approved under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY), 6 of which at Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Raipur and Rishikesh are fully functional. Status of remaining 16 new AIIMS under construction, with their date of sanction, current status and the timeline for their completion, is at Annexure I.The State/ UT-wise number of ICU beds and ventilators, as reported by States / UTs in the COVID India Portal is at Annexure II. State/UT-wise number of ambulances, as per NHM-MIS report, is at Annexure III. Details of State/UT-wise availability of ECMO machines is not maintained centrally.   ********
