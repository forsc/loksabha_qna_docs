                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

896






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Funds Allocation for Welfare of Minorities




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the funds allocated to Uttar Pradesh for development and welfare of minorities 	during the last five years and the current year, year-wise and programme             wise; (b) 	the details of funds utilized by the State during the said period; and (c) 	the details of funds utilized by the State under Multi-sectoral Development 	Programme for development of minorities during the said period?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) to (c): Ministry of Minority Affairs is implementing various schemes/programmes in the country including Uttar Pradesh for welfare and development of six centrally notified minorities namely Buddhists, Christians, Jains, Muslims, Parsis and Sikhs. Out of the schemes being  implemented by this Ministry, only one scheme namely Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (earlier known as Multi-sectoral Development Scheme) is Centrally Sponsored Scheme, under which funds are released to the State Governments including Uttar Pradesh. Other Schemes are Central  Sector Schemes being implemented directly by the Ministry or through implementing agencies empanelled under the various schemes/programmes, details of which are available on the website of the  Ministry i.e. www.minorityaffairs.gov.in.  	Under PMJVK, from 2014-15 onwards  287 Additional Classrooms (ACRs), 2 ACR Blocks, 707 Anganwadi Centres, 25 Common Service Centres (CSC), 31  Sadbhavna Mandaps (1 known as Sanskritik Sabhavna Mandap),1,73,143 Cybergram , 3,865 Drinking and Water Sanitation Projects (handpumps, Tubewells, etc) , 27 Health Projects (1 Unani Hospital, 4 Homeopathic Hospital, 3 Ayurvedic Hospital) , 20 Degree College, 15 Hostels (11 Girls Hostel), 39 ITIs, 2 additional work in ITI, 1 market Shed, 4 Polytechnics , 339 School Buildings, 2 Working Women Hostels, 666 Toilets (in school and community) , 2764 Teaching Aids etc.	The details of funds released to State of Uttar Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram and funds utilised by the state  during the last five years and the current year are as under:S. No.Financial Year	Fund Released(Rs. In Lakh)	Fund utilised  by the State (Rs. In Lakh)1	2015-16	32462.00	20248.162	2016-17	14364.00	5746.763	2017-18	15182.02	6072.814	2018-19	37653.07	11811.605	2019-20	16207.94	1620.79During 2020-21, an amount of Rs. 4552.91 Lakh has been released to state of Uttar Pradesh  as on 31.08.2020.   *****
