                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3096






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Development of Amravati Project under HRIDAY




Adala Prabhakara Reddy













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the progress made on the ‘Development of Amravati’ project under Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY); (b)  the details of the works done during the mission period of HRIDAY in Andhra Pradesh; and (c)   the funds allocated/released/utilised under the above yojana during the mission period, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS(SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE) (a) to (c) The National Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY) was launched on 21st January, 2015 and has ended on 31st March, 2019. The details of works undertaken and completed in Amaravati (Andhra Pradesh) and the funds released for the works during the Mission period of HRIDAY scheme are as under:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------- S.No.	      Name of the work	                         Funds released                                                                        (Rs. in crore)----------------------------------------------------------------------------------1	Up-gradation of approach roads to heritage sites	10.012	Development of heritage walk	                         2.323	Development of Amaravati pond (Nunegundam Cheruvu)	 4.27----------------------------------------------------------------------------------                                     Total	                 16.60----------------------------------------------------------------------------------Under HRIDAY Scheme, funds were allocated to cities for the entire Mission period and not year-wise.
