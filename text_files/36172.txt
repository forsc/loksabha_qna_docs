                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Rapid Rail Corridor




Balak Nath














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of proposal regarding Delhi-Alwar Rapid Rail Corridor Project;(b) 	the details of funds sanctioned, allocated and disbursed for the said project, area-wise;(c) 	whether there is a possibility of delay in Delhi-Alwar Rapid Rail corridor;(d) 	if so, the reasons therefor;(e) 	the details of the land acquired for the said project; and(f) 	the details of compensation provided and initiative taken for rehabilitation under the said project.






ANSWER




                                                                                 THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                     MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE) (a):    	Detailed Project Report (DPR) for Delhi-Alwar Regional Rapid Transit System corridor has not been received. National Capital Region Transport Corporation (NCRTC) has submitted DPRs for (i) Delhi - Gurugram - SNB (Shahajahanpur-Neemrana-Behror) Urban Complex covering a length of 106.50 Km and (ii) SNB Urban Complex - Sotanala RIICO Industrial Area covering a length of 33.30 Km, which are part of Delhi-Alwar RRTS project.  (b): 	Sanction, allocation and disbursement of funds is undertaken once the project is approved. However, NCRTC has informed that Government of Haryana has released ?224.50 cr and Government of Rajasthan has released ?5 cr. (c) & (d):	The RRTS projects are cost intensive projects which require extensive inter-ministerial consultations and their approval depends on feasibility of projects and availability of resources. (e):  NCRTC has informed that Government of Haryana has handed over 36.62 Ha of land for the project. (f):  Does not arise.                               ******
