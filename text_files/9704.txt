                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Youth Friendly Health Service Centres




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of “Youth Friendly Health Service Centres” functioning in the country, State/UT-wise particularly in Bihar; and(b) the funds being allocated for functioning of these centres, State/UT-wise during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	The details of adolescent friendly health clinics, run under Rastriya Kishore Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK), State/UT-wise, including Bihar, for which financial support is provided to the States/UTs under National Health Mission based on Programme Implementation Plans received from them, are placed as per Annexure-I.(b):	The details of the funds being allocated State/UT-wise for functioning of these clinics, during the last three years are as per Annexure-II.
