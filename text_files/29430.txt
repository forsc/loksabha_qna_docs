                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

723






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Installed Power Capacity




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total installed capacity of power during the last five years, State-wise;(b)	the contribution of private and public sector in the total installed capacity; and(c)	the share of renewable energy in the total installed capacity during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a): State-wise details of total Installed Capacity of power during the last five years and the current year (as on 30.10.2021) are given at Annexure-I.(b):	Sector-wise details of Installed Capacity and % share during the last 5 years and the current year are given at Annexure-II. As on 31.10.2021, the share of Public Sector is 51.86 % whereas the share of Private Sector is 48.14% in All India Installed Capacity.(c):	Mode-wise details of Installed Capacity along with Renewable Energy Sources (RES) % (including Hydro) share during the last 5 years and the current year are given at Annexure-III.  As on 31.10.2021, the share of Renewable Energy Sources is 38.27% in total Installed Capacity.
