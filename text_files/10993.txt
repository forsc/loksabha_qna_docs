                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4327






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Shortage of Skilled Manpower




Kavita Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government had contemplated to start nursing courses in almost all Sadar hospitals to overcome the acute shortage of skilled manpower in health sector;(b) if so, the names of Sadar hospitals and other hospitals where nursing training centres have been set up in Bihar during the last year and the current year;(c) whether the Government has linked the nursing training centres with hospitals to impart training to the nurses during their studies to create better skilled manpower; and(d) whether the Government proposes to set up a nursing college equipped with complete infrastructure in Siwan district of Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	  No.(b):	 List of Nursing Institutes set up in Sadar Hospitals and other Government Hospitals during 2018-19 and current year for running Auxiliary Nurse & Midwife (ANM) programme and General Nursing Midwives (GNM) programme in the State of Bihar is annexed.  (c):	  As per guidelines of Indian Nursing Council, it is essential for nursing training centres to have affiliation with parent hospital/ district hospitals. (d):	  No such proposal has been received in this Ministry.
