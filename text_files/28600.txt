                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

484






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Made-in-India Programme




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the types of textile related Indian jobs and businesses which have been promoted by the Ministry under “Make-in-India” programme? (b) whether the scheme is being implemented in the State of Maharashtra; and (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (c):	Government is promoting jobs and businesses under the broad objective of Make- in-India Programme catering to various sub-sectors of the Textile Sector which includes Spinning, Weaving, Garmenting, Processing, Jute, Silk, Wool, Handicraft and Handloom. Various schemes such as Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS), National Technical Textile Mission (NTTM), Silk Samagra, Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP), National Handicraft Development Programme (NHDP), National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) and Integrated Processing Development Scheme (IPDS) are being implemented for overall promotion and development of Textiles Sector at Pan India level including Maharashtra. Details of funds released in Maharashtra under the schemes of ATUFS, National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) & Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS), schemes implemented by O/o DC (Handicrafts) and Silk Samagra during 2018-19 to     2020-21 are at Annexure-I  ANNEXURE –IRelease of funds to State of Maharashtra in respect of schemes of Ministry of Textiles during the period 2018-19 to 2020-21 	Name of the Scheme	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21ATUFS (Rs. in crore)	1.09	21.84	61.01National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) & Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS)  (Rs. in crore)	0.090 	0.532	1.070All schemes of O/o Development Commissioner (Handicrafts) (Rs. in lakhs)	248.61	347.76	281.94Silk  Samagra (Rs. in lakhs)	16.17	475.55	0.00*              *(Non submission of utilization certificate by Maharashtra Sericulture Department and therefore, funds could not be     released)***
