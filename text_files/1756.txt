                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1676






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






MSP for Grains and Pulses




Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the names of the districts in Uttar Pradesh where wheat, gram, masoor, peas and mustard have been purchased under Minimum Support Price Scheme in the year 2018-19;(b)	 the number  of agencies through which  foodgrains  have  been  purchased including  the  quantum  of  foodgrains purchased at Minimum Support Price; and(c) 	the status in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 The names of the districts in Uttar Pradesh where wheat, gram, masoor and mustard have been purchased under Minimum Support Price Scheme in the year 2018-19 are at annexure-I and annexure-II. However,   Minimum Support Price (MSP) is not announced for peas.(b) & (c):	 Food Corporation of India (FCI) and National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Ltd. (NAFED) are two Central Nodal agencies through which foodgrains are being purchased at MSP. The State-wise quantum of foodgrains purchased at MSP during the year 2018-19 are at annexure-III, annexure-IV and annexure-V.
