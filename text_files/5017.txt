                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Vacant Posts in CICT




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether many key posts/positions have not been filled in theCentral Institute of Classical Tamil (CICT);(b) if so, the number of positions that have been filled by theGovernment since the inception of this institute;(c) whether the number of research studies undertaken in this instituteis also less due to non allocation of required funds; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government inthis regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): Yes Sir. For filling up the post of Director, Central Instituteof Classical Tamil (CICT), Chennai, a circular has been issued by thisMinistry on 09.05.2019.For recruitment of other posts also, notification was issued by theInstitute on 12.10.2012. There was a court case against thisadvertisement in the Hon’ble High Court of Madras which had stayed therecruitment process. CICT has released advertisement on 17.07.2019for filling up of those posts for which stay order has been vacated bythe Hon’ble High Court.(c) & (d): Grants are allocated to CICT on year to year basis as perthe requirement of the Institute. These grants are utilised by theInstitute for the purpose of research activities without any restrictions.CICT has released 39 books and 8 CDs so far. Academic programmes likeseminars and training programmes are supported by the Institute.***
