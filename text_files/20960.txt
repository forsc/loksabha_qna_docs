                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2254






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Data on Unemployment Level




Amar Singh













                                                    K. Muraleedharan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has data on unemployment levels in India in 2016-17;(b)if so, the details thereof including whether data for 2016-17 was collected through the 6th Annual Employment-Unemployment Survey and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)whether the Government intends to place in the public domain, unemployment data for the period 2016- 17;(d)if so, the details including the date by which it is expected; and(e)if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (e):	Labour Bureau, an attached office of the Ministry of Labour and Employment has conducted  household based Annual Employment Unemployment Survey (EUS)  2016-17 and released report thereon. According to the survey, Unemployment Rate defined as the number of persons unemployed per 1000 persons in the labour force, for persons aged 15 years & above based on the Usual Principal & Subsidiary Status UPSS(PS+SS) approach was 3.9%.******
