                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1567






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Higher Education Projects




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of projects sanctioned by the Government under the importantprojects related to various higher educational institutions, State/UT-wise includingMaharashtra;(b) the main features of the research projects started by such institutions; and(c) the details of their overall utility in the society?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) : Government of India is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme ofRashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) in the States/UTs to improve/reformthe State Higher Education system. Under RUSA, the central assistance is providedto institutions in various components such as establishment of New Model DegreeColleges (NMDCs), Creation of Universities by way of upgradation of autonomouscolleges or clustering of colleges, Enhancing quality in select State Universities,New Professional Colleges (NPCs), Research, Innovation and Quality Improvement,etc. A total amount of Rs. 13571.62 Crores has been approved under RUSA so far.The number of projects sanctioned under RUSA in various States/UTs includingMaharashtra is at Annexure.A total of Rs. 37.2734 Crores has been approved under the Component ofResearch, Innovation and Quality Improvement under RUSA 1.0. Further, underRUSA 2.0, an amount of Rs. 850 Crores for 17 Universities at Rs. 50 Crores eachhas also been approved. Out of this Rs. 50 Crores, Rs. 15 Crores is to be used forthe establishment of Entrepreneurship, Innovation and Career Hubs and the balanceof Rs. 35 Crores for various research projects. The proposals on research projectsare on thematic areas like sustainable energy technologies, efficient renewableenergy power generation, etc. The research projects are aimed at fosteringinnovation and evidence based policy in areas of national priorities and also to bringabout societal changes either through public policy design or intervention.
