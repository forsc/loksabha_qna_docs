                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Terrorist Infiltration




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of incidents of terrorist infiltration into the country during each of the last three years; (b) the number of terrorists killed and those caught alive in the course of their infiltration during the above period; and(c) the number of security personnel who got martyred and those who sustained injury in the encounters during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):  	   A statement is laid on the Table of the House.--2--STATEMENT IN REPLY TO THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *340 FOR 16.07.2019(a) to (c):	Attempts of Infiltration from across the border are mainly in Jammu and Kashmir which has been affected by terrorist violence, sponsored and supported from across-the border.Government of India has adopted a multi-pronged approach to contain cross border infiltration.  This includes multi-tiered deployment along the International Border / Line of Control, border fencing, improved intelligence and operational coordination, equipping Security Forces with advanced weapons and taking pro-active action against infiltrators.Net infiltration in the Jammu & Kashmir has reduced by 43% in first half of the year over the corresponding period of 2018.   As per available information, details of infiltration, infiltrators killed and arrested, security personnel martyred and injured during proactive engagement with infiltrators during the last three years in the state of Jammu and Kashmir are as under: -Year	Net estimated infiltration	Number of infiltrators killed 	Number of infiltrators arrested 	Number of Security Forces Personnel Martyred during proactive engagement with infiltrators	Number of Security Forces personnel injured during proactive engagement with infiltrators2016	119	35	03	15	372017	136	59	00	07	112018	143	32	01	05	01*****
