                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Status of Work on Flyover and Bypass




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of Eranhipalam flyover project in Kozhikode district under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF);(b)	the steps taken by the Government to complete National Highway (NH)-66 Bypass in Kozhikode;(c)	whether the company has submitted financial closure report for the project after stipulated time and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether the Ramanattukara-Calicut Airport segment of NH-966 is clogged with heavy traffic and if so, the steps taken by the Government to redress the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	The proposal of Eranhipalam flyover under Kozhikode district under CRIF is yet to be received from State Govt. of Kerala. (b) & (c) Concession Agreement for 6 laning of Kozhikode Bypass was executed on 18.04.2018 with M/s Calicut Expressways private Limited (Concessionaire). To revive the concession Agreement, NHAI and Concessionaire has executed settlement agreement through Conciliation Committee of Independent Experts on 11.01.2021. Concessionaire has submitted financial Closure documents on 15.09.2020.  (d)	Traffic on  Ramantukkara-Calicut  Airport segment of NH-966 is around 40700 PCU. This section is proposed to be developed as a part of Palakkad to Kozhikode section of NH-966 under Bharatmala Pariyojana and DPR is under progress. NHAI has requested State Government of Kerala to share 25% cost of land acquisition as was done for development of NH-66.
