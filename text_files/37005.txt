                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

364






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Strengthening of Panchayati Raj System




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is working on a new action plan related to rural development through Panchayati Raj in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether any proposal regarding further strengthening of Panchayati Raj system sent by some State Governments is pending with the Union Government; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Union Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a)	to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.364 FOR ANSWER ON 29.03.2022 REGARDING STRENGTHENING OF PANCHAYATI RAJ SYSTEM(a) The Ministry of Panchayati Raj is working to actively involve the Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs) for attainment of United Nation’s set targets of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to ensure poverty free and enhanced livelihood, healthy, child friendly, water sufficient, clean and green, self-sufficient infrastructure, socially secured, good governed and gender parity villages by the year 2030.   (b) No, Sir.(c) Does not arise.***
