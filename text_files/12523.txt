                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1416






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






De-Radicalisation Camps




S. Venkatesan













                                                     Imtiaz Jaleel Syed
                                                








                                                    Rajmohan Unnithan
                                                








                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Chief of Defence Staff (CDS) has recently given statement and DGP, J&K has also supported the existence of de-radicalisation camps in the country especially in Jammu and Kashmir;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c) the number of such camps in the country and the number of persons in the said camps;(d) the number of persons in such camps de-radicalised so far;(e) whether the Government has taken a step further in harassment of youth in the country especially in J&K through these camps; and(f) if so, the response of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) There is no report of existence of any de-radicalisation camps in the country especially in the Jammu and Kashmir. (b) to (f)    In view of (a) above, the question does not arise.*****
