                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

671






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Houses under PMAY-U in Andhra Pradesh




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of houses sanctioned under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) for the State of Andhra Pradesh, year-	wise; (b) 	the details of the number of houses built along with the start 	and the end dates of completion of ongoing construction 	projects under this yojana, year wise; (c) 	the area of land that needs to be acquired in order to complete 	building of houses under this yojana along with the details of 	the land that has already been acquired by the Government; (d) 	whether re-tendering has been done by the State Government 	for projects under PMAY since 2019-2020; and (e)	 if so, the value of the projects delayed by this re-tendering 	along with the details of the number of houses built under the 	new tender?






ANSWER




                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS				(SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)	(a)& (b): Year-wise details of houses sanctioned, completed/ built along with houses presently under construction under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban [PMAY-U] in the State of Andhra Pradesh are Annexed.(c):	As reported by the State Government of Andhra Pradesh, land measuring 826.20 acres has been purchased by the State to provide houses to identified beneficiaries of the State under PMAY-U. (d) & (e): Yes sir; as reported by the State Government of Andhra Pradesh, 54,056 houses were taken up after re-tendering with project cost of Rs. 2,996.50 crore, of which nearly 23,000 houses have been completed.                                 *****
