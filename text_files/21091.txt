                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Construction of Roads in Remote and Border Areas under PMGSY




Kishan Kapoor














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total length of roads in kilometers constructed in remote and border areas of northern States of the country under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY); and(b) 	the details in respect of Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Punjab till December, 2020? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	& (b)  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply of Lok Sabha Starred Question 215 for answere on 9.3.2021(a)	& (b)  A total of 37,451 km road length has been sanctioned under PMGSY, out of which, 31,660 km road length has been completed in the remote and border areas of North Indian States/Union Territories under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY). Details of the same including Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Punjab is given at Annexure-I.*****Annexure-IDetails of road length sanctioned and completed in remote and border areas of north Indian States (Since inception till December, 2020 under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY))
