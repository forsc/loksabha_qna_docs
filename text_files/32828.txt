                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Implementation of PMAY-U




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of implementation of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U), State/UT and year-wise during the last five years; (b)	the number of houses sanctioned under each component of PMAY-U, State/UT and year-wise; (c)	the number of houses grounded for construction under each component of PMAY-U, year and State/UT-wise; (d)	the details of budgetary allocation, sanction, and release of funds, State/UT and year-wise during the above period; (e)	the States which have not done well under the yojana and the reasons therefor; and (f)	whether the Government proposes to change the structure of the said yojana and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                     THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): State/ Union Territory (UT) and year-wise details of houses sanctioned and completed under Pradhan MantriAwasYojana-Urban (PMAY-U) during each of the last five years are given at Annexure-I.(b)  & (c): State/UT-wise and year-wise details of houses sanctioned and grounded for construction in Beneficiary-led individual house construction/ enhancement (BLC), Affordable Housing in Partnership(AHP), Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS) and“In-situ” Slum Redevelopment (ISSR) components of PMAY-U during aforesaid period are given at Annexure-II and Annexure-III respectively.  (d):  State/ UT-wise and year-wise details of Central assistance sanctioned/allocated and released under PMAY-U during each of the last five years are given at Annexure-IV.(e):	States/UTs formulate project proposals as per their assessed demand of houses under PMAY-U in a phased manner based on their resources and get them approved by State Level Sanctioning& Monitoring Committee (SLSMC). After approval from the concerned SLSMC, States/UTs submit the proposals to the Ministry for approval of admissible Central Assistance by the Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee. Against assessed demand of 112.24 lakh houses, 114.02 lakh houses have been sanctioned so far under PMAY-U.Completion of projects generally takes 12-36 months under different verticals of the scheme. All the projects sanctioned within the scheme period i.e. till March, 2022 are to be completed as per the time schedules mentioned in respective Detailed Project Reports (DPRs). (f)   No Sir; there is no such proposal under consideration of Government.                                      *****
