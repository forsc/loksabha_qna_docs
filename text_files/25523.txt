                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Status of Homes Constructed under PMAY-G during COVID




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                








                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that less than six percent of houses under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) have been completed this year with the COVID-19 pandemic stopping the progress, if so, the details thereof, State-wise and the corrective steps taken/being taken in this regard;(b) 	whether only fifty-five percent of the total targets reached under PMAY-G and far away for ensuring Housing for All targets including budget sanctioned/spent so far till date, if so, the details thereof, State-wise and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) 	the details of funds sanctioned/incurred under the scheme, State/UT-wise, as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	The Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) to provide assistance to eligible rural households for construction of 2.95 crore Pucca houses with basic amenities to achieve the objective of “Housing for All”. Under PMAY-G, a total of over 12.20 lakh houses have been completed   during the year 2021-22 against an estimated target of construction of 44.09 lakh houses.(b)& (c) Against the target of 2.02 cr allocated under the scheme till the financial year 2020-21, 1.50 cr houses have been completed so far, which is 75%. Regular VC meetings with the States/UTs are being held at the Ministry level for reviewing the progress of the scheme. In addition, daily monitoring of the progress is being done on various parameters such as gaps in sanction of houses, provision of land to landless beneficiaries, cleaning of Permanent Wait List (PWL) of PMAY-G, gaps in release of Central Share/ State Matching Share from treasury into SNA, etc. The review meetings are being held with States for better understanding of the constraints being faced and redressing the same for increasing progress of PMAY-G. 	The Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) is a centrally sponsored scheme and the funds are being shared by the Central and State Government in 60:40 ratio (90:10 in North Eastern State and hill states including Jammu & Kashmir & 100% Central Share in case of UTs including Ladakh).
