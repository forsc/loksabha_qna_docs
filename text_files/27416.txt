                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Physiotherapy Institutes and Dental Colleges




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Union Government is planning to set up more physiotherapy institutes and dental colleges across the country, particularly in Maharashtra;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which such institutes and colleges are likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): Central Government has no proposal/Scheme to set up dental colleges. However, the applications received by the Dental Council of India from State Governments/private entities are processed as per provisions of the Dentists Act, 1948 and Regulations made thereunder. So far as physiotherapy institutes are concerned, there is no Scheme for opening of such institutes.
