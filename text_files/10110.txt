                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3638






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Encounters with Maoist




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of encounters between armed forces/police with Maoists in the country since the last three years, State-wise; (b) the number of paramilitary personnel/policemen killed/wounded in such encounters; (c) the number of Maoists killed in such encounters; (d) whether the left wing extremist groups are getting any type of assistance from any other groups; (e) if so, the details thereof; (f) whether the Government is thoroughly monitoring the actions of Thunder Bolt’s, set up to combat Maoists; and (g) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	The numbers of Left Wing Extremism (LWE) related incidents, Security Force personnel killed/ injured and the extremists killed are tabulated at Annexure I.(d) & (e):	There are no inputs regarding LWE groups receiving any type of assistance from other groups.-2-L.S.US.Q.NO.3638 FOR 10.12.2019(f) & (g):	‘Police’ and ‘Public Order’ are State subjects, however, the Central Government supplements efforts of the State Government through various measures.             For the State Special Forces, the Central Government organizes training with Greyhounds of Andhra Pradesh and Army. Government of India also assists in capacity building of the Special Forces and Intelligence Wings through funds from Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS) having an outlay of Rs 381 Crores for period of 03 years from 2017-18 to 2019-20.*********
