                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2840






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Repairing of Damaged Roads in Karnataka




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up a separate fund to carry out reconstruction and repair of roads damaged by recent floods in Karnataka;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has conducted any survey to find out the details/length of roads affected in various districts of Karnataka;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) 	No, Sir.(b)	Does not arise.(c) to (e) An aggregate length of 842 Km. of National Highways are reported as damaged in various districts of Karnataka. Rs 5.00 Crore has been earmarked during the current financial year 2019-20 for temporary restoration of stretches to make the road traffic worthy. The permanent works are undertaken under various Annual plans and programmes of MoRT&H/NHAI.*****
