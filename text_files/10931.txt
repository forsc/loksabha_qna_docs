                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






National Green Tribunal




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of cases disposed off by the National Green Tribunal between 2011 and 2019;(b)	whether the National Green Tribunal has reported about the prevalence of several vacancies of its member posts; (c) 	if so, whether the Government has any plans in place to expedite filling the said vacancies and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the regional benches of the National Green Tribunal are non-functional;(e) 	if so, the details thereof; and(f)	the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a): The number of cases disposed off by the National Green Tribunal (NGT) from the date of its inception, 04.07.2011 to 30.11.2019 is 28,761.(b)and (c): The selection of eligible and suitable candidates for filling up the vacancies of Judicial and Expert Members of the NGT is an ongoing process. The Government has recently appointed two Expert Members in October, 2019, out of which one Expert Member has assumed office at NGT on 01.11.2019. For the six vacant posts of Expert Members, fresh applications have been invited.The last date of receipt of applications was 29.11.2019.(d) to (f): The casesfiled/ pending at the Zonal Benches at Bhopal, Kolkata and Pune are being taken up for hearing atthe Principal Bench, New Delhi, through Video Conferencing. The Southern Zonal Bench at Chennai has started its function with effect from 02.12.2019.******
