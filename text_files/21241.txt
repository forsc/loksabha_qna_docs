                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2430






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Rehabilitation/Assistance to Fishermen Communities




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal for rehabilitation/assistance of fishermen communities affected by cyclones/sea erosion in various States; (b)  	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c)  	whether the State Government of Tamil Nadu has submitted any proposal for release of funds from the Union Government for rehabilitation of fishermen; and (d)  	the details of the steps being taken by the Union Government for construction of sea wall to protect fishermen from cyclones? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a)	and (b) : The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries Animal Husbandry and Dairying supplements the efforts of the State/UT Government for overall development of fisheries and aquaculture including providing assistance for safety of fishermen at sea. With regards to providing rehabilitation/assistance of fishermen communities affected by cyclones/sea erosion, the primary responsibility rest with the State/UT Government. The Ministry of Home Affairs renders assistance to State Government during natural calamities/disasters.  (c)	Presently no proposal relating to rehabilitation of fishermen is pending with Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Government of India.(d)	Under the Scheme and Programme implemented by the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries Animal Husbandry and Dairying, there is no provision for construction of sea wall. *****
