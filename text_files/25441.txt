                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Integrating Land Records with PMFBY




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number and names of the States that have integrated their land records with the web portal of the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY); and (b) the number of people that got payment of claims under the PMFBY from Uttar Pradesh including the complete details of beneficiaries from Aonla constituency?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) : 	Three State Governments namely, Maharashtra, Odisha and Rajasthan have integrated their land records with National Crop Insurance Portal (NCIP) of the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY).(b) : 	Since the inception of the scheme in Kharif 2016 till Kharif 2020, claims of Rs 2809.3 crore have been paid to against 37.38 lakh farmer applications in Uttar Pradesh.  Season-wise details of claims paid and number of farmer applications who got claims in Uttar Pradesh  and  Bareilly &  Budaun districts covering Aonla Parliamentary constituency are given in Annexure.
