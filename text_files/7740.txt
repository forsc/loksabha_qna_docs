                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1393






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Crime in Railways




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the reasons for not taking the assistance of modern technology equipped devices to check the incidents of crime at the prominent railway stations in Jabalpur under West Central Railway Zone; (b) whether there is any proposal for installing high definition cameras at major railway stations in Jabalpur; (c) if so, the reasons for the delay in installing these equipments;(d) whether the Government proposes to install the modern equipments very soon keeping in view the safety of the passengers and their belongings; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the time-frame fixed for this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): The assistance of modern security gadgets like surveillance system, baggage scanners are being taken to curb criminal activities at Jabalpur, Katni, Satna, Damoh, Sagar and Maihar railway stations of Jabalpur division. (b) to (e): 12 CCTV cameras are already installed at Jabalpur Railway station. In addition to above, work for installation of 50 more cameras under Nirbhaya Fund is under progress.****
