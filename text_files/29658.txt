                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Accounts Opened under SSY




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the new accounts opened under the Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY) since 2018, which was launched to provide stable collections for future expenses of the female children; (b)	the details of States with the highest and lowest number of accounts opened under the scheme along with the reasons therefor; and (c)	the details of the distribution proportion of private and public scheduled banks opening accounts under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	The number of new accounts opened under Sukanya Samriddhi Account (SSA) from 01.04.2018 to 31.10.2021 is 1,42,73,910.(b)	The details of States with the highest and lowest number of account under SSA as on 31.10.2021 is given below:Statement Showing details of States with highest number of accountsS.No.	Name of  the State/UT	No of Accounts as on 31.10.2021 1	Uttar Pradesh	29,12,6322	Tamil Nadu	26,03,8723	Maharashtra	23,13,5844	Madhya Pradesh	22,17,3755	Karnataka	21,08,078Statement Showing details of States with lowest number of accountsS.No.	Name of the State/UT	No of Accounts as on 31.10.2021 1	Lakshadweep	3312	Andaman and Nicobar Islands	5,3053	Ladakh	6,2094	Mizoram	10,2045	Sikkim	11,455The reasons for varying number of accounts in different states could be the size of the population, ability and willingness of parents to save etc.(c)	The details of the distribution proportion of public and private banks in number of accounts since launch of the scheme till 31.10.2021 is as below:S.No.	Name of the Bank	Distribution proportion in per cent1	Public Banks	96.242	Private Banks	3.76Note: While calculating distribution proportion, accounts opened under SSA in various Post Offices have not been considered.
