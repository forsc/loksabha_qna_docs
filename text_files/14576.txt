                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3388






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Yoga Centres/Ayurvedic Hospitals




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has set up yoga centres and Ayurvedic hospitals at various tourist places in view of rising popularity of yoga and Ayurveda and for the benefit of visiting foreign tourists in the country, if so, the details thereof; and (b) whether the Government is considering to set up a yoga centre and Integrated Ayurvedic Hospital in Madhya Pradesh, particularly in Khajuraho, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):    As Public Health is a State subject, opening of yoga centres and Ayurvedic hospitals at various tourist places in the country including Madhya Pradesh comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision of financial assistance for setting up of Yoga Wellness Centre and setting up of upto 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals in the States/UTs including Madhya Pradesh. State/UT Governments may avail eligible financial assistance by projecting the same through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) as per NAM guidelines.                The details of Yoga Wellness Centre and setting up of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH hospitals approved by Central Government, State/UT-wise including Madhya Pradesh since inception of NAM to till date is furnished at Annexure.…………….
