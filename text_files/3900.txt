UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3562






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Pilgrim and Tourism Places in UNESCO List




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of pilgrim or tourism places in Tamil Nadu awarded by UNESCO;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the income and expenditure of such places during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)& (b) There are two UNESCO declared World Heritage Monuments in Tamil Nadu. Details of which are mentioned below:-1.	The Group of Monuments at Mamallapuram,District-Kanchipuram2.	The Great living Chola Temples at Thanjavur, Gangaikondacholapuram and Darasuram, Districts- Thanjavur and Ariyalur(c)	The details of the income earned and expenditure incurred on above sites are placed at Annexure-I.
