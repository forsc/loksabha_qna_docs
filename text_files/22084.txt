                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3274






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Suicide Victims in Farming Sector




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that 7.4% of total/suicide victims belong to the farming sector;(b) the steps taken/to be taken by the Government keeping in mind the proportion of people dependent on agriculture as a means of livelihood is high and most farmers do not have job security and they are dependent on a number of factors to earn a livelihood; and(c) whether any concrete measures are being taken by the Government to reduce these statistics despite the fact that the agriculture sector is the backbone of our nation, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):  	    The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) under the Ministry of Home Affairs compiles and disseminates information on suicides in its publication titled ‘Accidental Deaths and Suicides in India’(ADSI). These Reports on suicides are available upto 2019 on its website. A total number of 1,39,123 suicides were reported in the country during 2019, which includes 10,281 (5957 farmers and 4324 agricultural laborers) suicides in farming sector.(b)&(c):   Agriculture being a State subject, the State Governments take appropriate measures for development of agriculture in the State. However, Government of India supplements the efforts of States through appropriate policy measures and budgetary support and various schemes/ programmes. The various schemes/ programmes of the Government of India are meant for the welfare of farmers by increasing production, remunerative returns and income support to farmers. A list of various initiatives taken by the Government is Annexed.
