                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2160






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Oil Refinery in Assam




Tokheho Yepthomi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of new Oil Refinery sanctioned in Assam, location-wise including thecapacity of those refineries in 24 hours;(b) the number of oil fields in operation along Assam-Nagaland boundary, districtwiseincluding the number of oil fields under construction, location-wise;(c) the number of oil refineries which have been upgraded in production capacityincluding the increased capacity?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROELUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) No new Oil Refinery has been sanctioned in Assam.(b) There are total 39 numbers of oil & gas field operated by ONGC in the state ofAssam. Out of these, eight fields are falling close to Assam-Nagaland Boundary viz.Geleki, Khoraghat, Nambar, Kasomarigaon, Suphyam, Dayalpur, Borholla andMekrang. In addition, Changpang field of Nagaland falls in close proximity to Assam-Nagaland border. However, operations are suspended at Changpang field since May1994. Twenty seven fields are presently in production. The Asset-wise / District-wiselist of Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC)’s oil and gas fields in the state ofAssam is at Annexure-I.Details of blocks awarded in Assam and Nagaland underProduction Sharing Contract (PSC) Regime is at Annexure-II.Under DiscoveredSmall Field Bid Round-I, 8 Contract Areas have been awarded is at Annexure-III.Under Discovered Small Field Bid Round-II, 5 Contract Areas have been awardedis at Annexure- IV.Oil India Limited (OIL) is presently operating in two explorationblocks located along Assam-Nagaland boundary viz. 1) NELP Block AA-ONN-2010/2 located in Karbi-Anglong district and 2) OALP Block AA-ONHP-2018/3located in Dima Hasao district.(c) The capacity of Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL), Bongaigaon Refineryhas been upgraded from 2.35 to 2.70 Million Metric Tonne per Annum(MMTPA) inthe year 2020.*************
