                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Protection of Child Rights




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  	whether the Government has reviewed the implementation of the law related to protection of child rights;(b) 	if so, the details and the outcome thereof;(c) 	the details of the shortcomings found during the said review; and(d) 	the appropriate action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (d).  The Government reviewed the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012  in the light of the rise of incidences of child sexual abuse in the country, inter-alia. Accordingly, the POCSO Act, 2012 was amended in 2019 in order to make it more effective in dealing with cases of child sex abuse. It addresses the need for stringent measures to deter child sex abuse in the Country on one hand and the menace of relatively new kind of crimes on the other hand. In furtherance of the POCSO (Amendment) Act, 2019, the Government notified the  POCSO Rules, 2020  for effective implementation of the Act. *****
