                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3311






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Repayment of Loans by Andhra Pradesh




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has any details of the loans to be repaid by the Government of Andhra Pradesh to Centre, World Bank, Asian Development Bank, NABARD and other financial institutions and banks and re-payment made during the last three years and the current year, particularly after the implementation of the Andhra Pradesh State Reorganisation Act;b)	if so, the details thereof, institution-wise and the purpose thereof, work-wise;c)	whether Andhra Pradesh has requested for relaxation in loan repayment/re-scheduling of loans and if so, the details thereof and the action taken by various agencies so far, post bifurcation of the State;d)	the details of the loans being utilised for each project in Andhra Pradesh;e)	whether the liabilities and other loans crossed more than Rs. 2.56 lakh crore by the end of March 31, 2019 and if so, the details thereof; andf)	the details of the comparative assets and liabilities of each State during the last three years and the current year compared to Andhra Pradesh, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) (a)	& (b)	As per the provisions of Article 293 (1) of the Constitution of India, the executive power of a State extends to borrowing within the territory of India upon the security of the Consolidated Fund of the State within such limits, if any, as may from time to time be fixed by the Legislature of such State by law. The States raises funds/ loans through externally aided projects and Open Market Borrowing (which are untied in nature) for implementing schemes/ programmes which are best suited to their local needs and requirements. As gleaned from State Finance Accounts and State Budget documents of Andhra Pradesh, the details of outstanding loan to be paid and repayment by the Government of Andhra Pradesh to Central Government, financial institutions, etc. at the end of March of 2016-17 (FA) to 2019-20(BE) are at Annex-I and Annex-II respectively.(c) 	Special Assistance Measure by way of repayment of loan and interest for the Externally Aided Projects (EAPs) signed and disbursed during 2015-16 to 2019-20 by the State was approved for the State Government of Andhra Pradesh, for additional Central share which the State might have received during 2015-16 to 2019-20, if the funding of Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) would have been shared at the ratio of 90:10 between the Centre and the State. In compliance to the same and based on the request received from the State Government of Andhra Pradesh in financial year 2018-19, an amount of Rs.15.81 crore has been released towards repayments of interest liabilities of EAPs signed and disbursed by the State during 2015-16 to 2017-18 in financial year 2018-19.(d)	The details of loans utilised for Externally Aided Projects (EAP) in Andhra Pradesh is at Annex-III.(e) & (f)	As gleaned from Reserve Bank of India report titled “State finances: A study of budgets of 2019-20”, the state-wise details of outstanding liabilities at the end of March of 2016-17 (FA) to 2019-20 (BE) are at Annex-IV.
