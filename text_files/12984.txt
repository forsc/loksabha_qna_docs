                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1797






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Income Tax Raids




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    1797. SHRI PRADYUT BORDOLOI:Will the Minister of Finance be pleased to state:(a)	the number of raids conducted by the Enforcement Directorate and the Income Tax Department each year since 2010, including in the State of Assam, agency-wise;(b) the number of investigations initiated, investigations concluded, convictions secured and cases appealed by each agency each year during this period; and(c) the number of investigations and cases pending at the beginning and end of each financial year with each agency during this period?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a):- The number of search & seizure/ raids conducted by the Income Tax Department and Enforcement Directorate for each year since 2010 including state of Assam is as follow:Financial Year (F.Y)	Number of search & seizure actions by Income Tax Department	Number of raids conducted by Enforcement Directorate 		Prevention of Money Laundering Act(PMLA), 2002	Foreign Exchange Management Act(FEMA), 19992010-11	555	7	922011-12	621	14	482012-13	422	21	602013-14	569	42	762014-15	545	70	1102015-16	447	163	772016-17	1152	226	2222017-18	582	368	1262018-19	983	519	1512019-20 (upto January, 2020)	893	311	121(b) & (c) :-  Investigation regarding tax evasion is an ongoing process and whenever any instance comes to the notice of the department, appropriate action including searches, surveys, assessment of income, levy & recovery of tax, imposition of penalty and launching of prosecution, is taken as per law, depending upon the facts and circumstances of each case. 	The data with regard to the prosecutions launched by the Income Tax Department from FY 2010-11 is as under:(F.Y.)	Number of Prosecution cases 	Number of convictions secured	launched during the F.Y.	disposed of by the Courts	2010-11	244	273	512011-12	209	196	142012-13	283	68	102013-14	641	103	412014-15	669	76	342015-16	552	66	282016-17	1252	46	162017-18 	4527	233	682018-19 	3512	570	1052019-20(as on 31.1.20)	1149	353	41Various actions conducted by the Income Tax Department are subject to appeals before various appellate authorities including Commissioner of Income Tax (Appeals) Income Tax Appellate Tribunal, High Courts and the Hon’ble Supreme Court.Further, in respect of Enforcement Directorate, the details of number of investigations initiated, investigations concluded, convictions secured, cases appealed and number of investigations pending at the beginning and end of each year separately for PMLA and FEMA are enclosed as per Annexure– I & II.*****
