                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Achievements of DAY-NRLM




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the targets and achievements in both physical and financial terms in the implementation of Deen Dayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Rural Livelihood Mission (DAY-NRLM) in the country during the last three years, State/UT-wise;(b)	the details regarding budgetary support for DAY-NRLM for 2018-19 and adequateness of the proposed BE for the scheme in 2019-20; and(c)	the status of implementation of DAY-NRLM in Tamil Nadu since the launch of the scheme in 2019-20?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a)	The targets and achievements in both physical and financial terms in the implementation of Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana - National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM) in the country during the last three years, State/UT-wise is provided in ANNEXURE- I and ANNEXURE-II respectively.(b)	The Budget Estimate (BE) and Revised Estimate (RE) provision for DAY-NRLM during 2018-19 was 5750.00 crore and 5783.50 crore respectively. The actual release during the year was Rs 5783.48 crore.In Budget Estimate (BE) 2019-20, the provision for DAY-NRLM is Rs 9024.00 crore which is approximately 57 % higher than last year’s BE. The enhanced provision in BE 2019-20 is considered adequate to meet the programme requirements for the year.(c)	 DAY-NRLM is being implemented in Tamil Nadu through Tamil Nadu Corporation for Development of Women.  The scheme is being implemented in 31 Districts and 385 blocks of Tamil Nadu. Cumulatively, up to 31st October, 2019 about 37.05 lakh women are mobilized into 3.10 lakh SHGs. In addition, 16872 village organizations and 12150 secondary level federations have been promoted. These community-based organizations have been provided capitalization support to the tune of Rs. 323.15 crore by the Mission.
