                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Daily Vaccination Rate for COVID-19




Faizal P.P. Mohammed













                                                    Manish Tewari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the current daily vaccination rate for COVID-19 is sufficient to meet the proposed target of vaccinating 300 million people by August, 2020, if so, the details thereof;(b) if not, whether the Government is planning to increase the daily vaccination rate in order to meet its target;(c) the number of people inoculated with the second dose of vaccine so far; and(d) whether the Government has noted that only one in ten people have got inoculated with the second dose, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): The current rate of COVID-19 vaccination is satisfactory and is being further increased through increase in number of COVID-19 Vaccination Centres (CVCs) in both government and private health facilities.In view of dynamic nature of COVID-19 pandemic and inclusion of new priority groups, no specific time frame is fixed for completion of vaccination.(c) & (d): As on 8th March 2021, a total of 37.61 lakh beneficiaries have received 2nd dose of COVID-19 vaccine. These include 66.67% & 43.50% of the eligible beneficiaries for 2nd dose amongst Health Care Workers and Front Line Workers respectively.
