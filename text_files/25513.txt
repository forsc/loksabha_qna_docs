                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1343






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Fishermen in Uttar Pradesh




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated any scheme to increase the fisheries production and the income of the fishermen in the State of Uttar Pradesh, if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to impart fisheries related training to fishermen and provide financial aid to them, if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of the training camps organised in the State of Uttar Pradesh along with the number of persons who were imparted training there and the number of persons who got engaged with fisheries/fisheries production after such training, district-wise; (d) 	the details of funds released for fisheries during the last five years in the State of Uttar Pradesh, districtwise; and (e)	the details of the fisheries production capacity in the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last five years, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a):	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) - to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with an estimated investment of Rs. 20,050 crore for a period of 5 years from financial year 2020-21 to financial year 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories including Uttar Pradesh as a part of Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Package. Besides, a dedicated fund namely, ‘Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund’ (FIDF) with a total fund size of Rs.7,522.48 crore has been set up for providing concessional finance for creation and strengthening of fisheries infrastructure facilities. These schemes are implemented in all the States/UTs including Uttar Pradesh inter-alia aiming towards enhancement of fish production, productivity, income of fish farmers, generate employment opportunities in fisheries and aquaculture. (b) and (c):	Under PMMSY, focus is also accorded for training, awareness, skill development, exposure, skill up-gradation and capacity building for fishers, fish farmers, fish workers, vendors and officials. Under the PMMSY, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying during the financial year 2020-21 has accorded approval to the proposals of Government of Uttar Pradesh at a total cost of Rs.398.99 crore and released first installment of central share of Rs. 60.08 crore to the State Government for assisting the fishermen and fish famers for development of fisheries in the State.  The Government of Uttar Pradesh has reported that a total 216 number of training camps were organized in which 5504 persons were imparted training. It is further reported that most of the trainees are engaged in fish production activities. District-wise number of persons imparted training in the State of Uttar Pradesh is furnished at Annexure-I. (d) and (e): 	 District-wise details of funds released for fisheries and district-wise details of fish production during last five years in the State of Uttar Pradesh as reported by the Government of Uttar Pradesh are furnished at Annexure-II and Annexure-III respectively. *****
