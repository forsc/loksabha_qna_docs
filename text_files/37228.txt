                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Mega Silk Park




Prajwal Revanna














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set mega silk park in Mysore district of karnataka and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of the employment that is likely to be generated in the region by setting up of such park; and (c) the funds sanctioned by the Government for setting up of the said park?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a):	The Government has approved setting up of Mysore Mega Silk Cluster under Comprehensive Powerloom/ Silk Mega Cluster Scheme with a project cost of Rs. 49.19 cr in Mysore.(b):	The Silk Mega Cluster is expected to benefit about 6,000 looms in improving their production in the area. (c):	First instalment of Rs. 2.49 cr has already been released to the project on 31.12.2021. ***
