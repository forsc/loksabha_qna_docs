                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






People Trained under DAY-NRLM




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and the number of people trained in agricultural and in the non-agricultural sector under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana - National Rural Livelihood Mission (DAY-NRLM) to enable them to become self dependent during the last three years in Maharashtra;  and(b) 	the number of women who have been provided funds through the Self-Help Groups during the Financial Year 2020-21 thereunder?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) The Ministry is implementing the Mahila Kisan Sahshaktikaran Pariyojana (MKSP), Start-up Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP), Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana and Rural Self Employment Training Institutes (RSETIs) as  sub-schemes under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana -National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM). The primary objective of the MKSP is to empower women in agriculture by making systematic investments to enhance their participation and productivity, as also create and sustain their agriculture-based livelihoods.The objective of SVEP is to help the rural poor (from Self Help Group ecosystem) to set-up enterprises at the village level in non-agricultural sector. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) is a placement linked skill development program to build the capacity of rural poor youth to address the needs of domestic and global skill requirements. Rural Self Employment Institutes (RSETIs) programme runs with an approach of short-term training & long-term hand-holding of entrepreneurs to impart training and skill upgradation of rural poor youth geared towards entrepreneurship development.As informed by the Maharashtra State Rural Livelihoods Mission and State Mission Management Unit, DDU-GKY Maharashtra, the number of people trained in agricultural and non-agricultural sector for the last three years in the State of Maharashtra is given in the table below: Year	      Agricultural Sector	Non Agricultural Sector2018-19	        3,61,350	            70,1652019-20	        5,26,943	            60,0992020-21   	5,01,813	            42,500 (b)  DAY-NRLM provides Revolving Fund (RF) from Rs. 10,000 to 15,000 per SHG and Community Investment Fund (CIF) up to Rs.2.50 lakh per SHG. Apart from this, DAY-NRLM also facilitates credit mobilisation for SHGs in the form of Bank loans.
