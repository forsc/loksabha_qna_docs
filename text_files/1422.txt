                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Treatment in CGHS Empanelled Hospitals




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has accorded approval to retired Government employees to get treatment from CGHS approved hospitals without referral from dispensaries;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to give this facility to other CGHS card holders such as serving Government employees, security personnel/military personnel etc. in CGHS approvedhospitals; (c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a): 	Yes. Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) beneficiaries aged 75 years and above are permitted to seek direct OPD consultation from specialists of private hospitals empanelled under CGHS without   referral from CGHS Wellness Centres.(b) to (d): 	Presently, there is no such proposal. However, vide OM dated 15.01.2018, instructions have been issued to simplify referral system under CGHS in respect of all CGHS beneficiaries in all CGHS Cities. As per these instructions, all CGHS beneficiaries are permitted to seek OPD consultation from specialists at private hospitals empanelled under CGHS after being referred by any Medical Officer/ CMO of the CGHS Wellness Centre.
