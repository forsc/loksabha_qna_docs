                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

760






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Haj Quota for MPs




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any scheme to allot Haj quota to all the Members of Parliament; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the date from which this scheme is being implemented; and (c)	if not, whether the Government proposes to fix a quota of at least 10 Haj pilgrims to a Member of Parliament and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	 No, Sir. (b)	& (c)	Does not arise in view of (a) above.*****
