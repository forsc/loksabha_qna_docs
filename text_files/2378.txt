                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






NH Projects on PPP Model




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	 whether the National Highways (NHs) projects constructed/being constructed through Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode are unable to take off as the private partners are unable to arrange finances for the projects and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the private partners are unable to arrange for the technical and non-technical work force to implement National Highways projects and if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto; and(c) 	the details of arrangements being made for acquisition of land for construction of  NHs?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) No Sir. However, some of the National Highways projects, being undertaken through PPP mode, are facing some stress depending upon the financial strength of the bidders.(b)  No such report has been received.(c) Land acquisition is being made by appointing State Government Officers as Competent Authority. Land Acquisition and administration charges are paid to them for completion of land acquisition at the earliest.Hindrances, if any, are cleared with the assistance of State Administration for early completion of Civil Works.*****
