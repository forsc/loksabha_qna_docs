                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

286






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Removal of Communities for OBC List




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.286TO BE ANSWERED ON 30.11.2021REMOVAL OF COMMUNITIES FOR OBC LIST 286. SHRI S. MUNISWAMY: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment has received any requisition from Ministry of Tribal Affairs to delete the Parivara, Talawara and Siddi communities of Karnataka State from the OBC List; and(b) if so, the reason for the delay in removal of names of these three communities from the Central OBC List in spite of both the houses of parliament having passed the bill in 2020?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a):    Yes, the Ministry has received the requisition from Ministry of Tribal Affairs to delete the Parivara, Talwara only.     (b):    In terms of Article 343A(2) of the Constitution, only Parliament can include in or exclude from the Central List of SEBCs, after the Presidential notification of the Central List of SEBCs.*****
