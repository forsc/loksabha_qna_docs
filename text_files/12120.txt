                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1059






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Manpower Augmentation in Anganwadis




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 		whether the Government has proposed the engagement of more manpower under the 	Anganwadi scheme and if so, the details thereof; (b)		whether the Government has taken any action to improve the infrastructure and working 	ambience of Anganwadis to provide effective service delivery, if so, the details of action 	taken thereon; (c)		the details of the action taken by the Government to revise the incentives provided to 	Anganwadi teachers and workers; (d) 		whether the Government has taken note of the fact that lack of basic amenities and 	infrastructure at Anganwadi Centres has adversely affected the children enrolled 	thereunder and if so, the details thereof; (e)		the initiative taken by the Government to provide adequate infrastructure and manpower 	for the smooth functioning of Anganwadis; and (f)		whether the Government has proposed a new scheme for providing buildings and other 	infrastructure facilities for smooth functioning of Anganwadis, if so, the details therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The Government at present has not proposed the engagement of more manpower under the Anganwadi Services Scheme.(b)	Joint guidelines for construction of 4 lakh Anganwadi Centre (AWC) buildings across the country in convergence with Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) were issued on 17.02.2016. Under the revised guidelines, provision for construction of 4 lakh new AWC buildings @ Rs.7.00 lakh per AWC building has been made. Out of this, Rs.5.00 lakh are provided by Ministry of Rural Development. Central share for construction of AWC building is Rs.1 lakh per AWC building for all States/UTs, which is reimbursable on completion of construction work and remaining Rs.1 lakh is borne by the respective State/UT.Further, provision has been made for construction of toilets in 70,000 AWCs with drinking water facilities in 20,000 AWCs from 2017-18 to 2019-20 under Swachhata Action Plan. Apart from this, there is a provision of up-gradation of AWC buildings @ Rs.2.00 lakh per AWC building under the scheme. Funds for construction of AWC building, construction of Toilets in AWCs, providing drinking water facilities and for up-gradation of AWC buildings are released to States/UTs. (c)	The Government with effect from 1st October, 2018 hasenhanced the honorarium of Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) from ?3,000/- to ?4,500/- per month; AWWs at mini-AWCs from ?2,250/- to ?3,500/- per month; Anganwadi Helpers (AWHs) from ?1,500/- to ?2,250/- per month. The Government has also introduced performance linked incentive of ?250/- per month to AWHs.  They are paid performance linked incentive of ?500/- per month for using ICDS-CAS (Common Application Software) under POSHAN Abhiyaan. (d) to (f)For providing basic amenities and infrastructure at AWCs across the country, the Government has released funds of an amount of Rs.6735.65 lakhs in F.Y. 2017-18, Rs.7266.54 lakhs in F.Y. 2018-19 and Rs.138.54 lakh during current year for construction of toilets and providing drinking water facilities in AWCs. Funds to the tune of Rs. 25774.90 lakh in financial year 2017-18 and Rs.14904.70 lakh in financial year 2018-19 have been released to States/UTs for up-gradation of AWC buildings. Ministry has also issued directions to States/UTs to delegate powers to District Magistrates/Collectors for the recruitment of Anganwadi Workers, Anganwadi Helpers and Supervisors on 14th March, 2018 for smooth functioning of Anganwadis. ****
