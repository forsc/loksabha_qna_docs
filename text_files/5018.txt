                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4566






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






MUDRA Loans in NE States




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of people benefited by MUDRA loans in the North East (NE) States; and(b)	the details of the beneficiaries and the performance of various categories of loans disbursed under MUDRA yojana since its implementation in the NE States, category and NE State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (b)  As on 12.07.2019, over 76.87 lakh loans  amounting to Rs. 32,910.30 crore have been sanctioned to borrowers in North Eastern States under the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) since the inception of the scheme.Category-wise, State wise details of loans sanctioned in North Eastern states is at Annexure - 1.
