                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3003






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Housing Projects of CGEWHO




Vinod Kumar Sonkar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Central Government Employees’ Welfare Housing Organization (CGEWHO) undertakes housing projects for the Central Government Employees in various parts of the country;(b)	if so, the details of housing projects undertaken by CGEWHO since its inception;(c)	whether the CGEWHO has recently launched residential projects in Tier 2 cities and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(d)	the details of housing projects completed by the CGEWHO upto December, 2019; and (e)	whether CGEWHO is planning to launch more residential projects in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities and if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the CGEWHO in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a),(b)&(d) Yes Sir.Central Government Employees Welfare HousingOrganisation (CGEWHO) has completed 31 housing projects since inception and 02 housing projects are under progress. Details are given at Annexure.(c)  No Sir.(e) CGEWHO conducts demand survey to access the demand potential in various cities and based on such survey decision 	to launch new residential projects in a particular cityis taken.                                     ******
