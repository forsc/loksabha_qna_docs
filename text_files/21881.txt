                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3077






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Offline study




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any arrangements for the students for regular offline study;(b) if so, details thereof; and(c) whether the Government has taken any measures in University campuses to stop further transmission of coronavirus disease; and(d) if so, details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b): The University Grants Commission (UGC) has issued guidelines dated 05.11.2020 for Re-opening the Universities and Colleges Post Lockdown due to COVID-19 Pandemic. These guidelines are available at https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/4613471_Guidelines.pdf . (c) & (d): These guidelines have been issued in consultation with the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare and incorporate the measures required to stop further transmission of Coronavirus disease. ******
