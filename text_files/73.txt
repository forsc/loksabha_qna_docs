                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Medical Devices




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to cover the commonly used medical devices under the Drugs & Cosmetics Act to enable the Government to ensure their quality & performance and if so, the details thereof;(b) the list of the new medical devices added along with the devices notified earlier;(c) whether the Government also proposes to bring the high-end medical devices like MRI equipment, CT Scan equipment, X-ray machines etc. under the purview of drug law; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	The Government has covered a number of medical devices under the Drugs & Cosmetics Act to ensure their quality and performance. The list of notified medical devices which are being regulated along with those which have been notified to be regulated from a specified date is annexed.The list includes high end medical devices like MRI Equipment, CT scan Equipment,  X-Ray Machines, etc.
