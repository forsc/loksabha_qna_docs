                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2687






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Modernisation of Police




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken up a project to provide radios and mobile radios for Hyderabad and Cyberabad under Modernisation of Police Force scheme;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the status of the project and the time by which it is going to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c): The State Government of Telangana proposed the project to provide advanced walkie-talkie Radios and Mobile Radios for Hyderabad, Cyberabad and Rachakonda Commissionerate in their State Action Plan (SAP) for the financial year 2019-20 under scheme of ‘Assistance to States for Modernisation of Police(ASMP)’. The total estimated cost of the project is ? 716.25 lakh, out of which ? 429.75 lakh is the central share, which was released to the State Government during the financial year 2019-20. As per information obtained from the Government of Telangana, open tenders were notified twice in this regard. Due to  lack of  sufficient competition, the Techno Finance Committee constituted by the State Government decided to cancel the tender and float open tender afresh. *****
