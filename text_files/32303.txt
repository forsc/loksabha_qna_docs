                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3735






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Funds for Schemes in Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount provided under various schemes of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying to various States including the States of Haryana, Assam and North-Eastern States during the period from the year 2014 to 2021; (b) whether the said amount has been spent within the time limit; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, whether the Government has fixed any responsibility in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER       THE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) to (d): To supplement the efforts of the States/UTs for promotion of fisheries, animal husbandry and dairy sectors, the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Government of India is implementing various schemes across the country including Haryana, Assam and North-Eastern states such as (i) Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY), (ii) Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF), (iii) Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM), (iv) National Livestock Mission (NLM), (v) National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD), (vi) Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF), (vii) Supporting Dairy Cooperative) and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPO) engaged in dairy activities, (viii) Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS), (ix) Entrepreneurship Development and Employment Generation (EDEG) and (x) Livestock Health and Disease Control (LHDC), etc.. Due to COVID-19 situation, the normal pace of activities has impacted the implementation of the schemes by State/UTs. After easing of the lockdown situation and restoring of logistics, the States/UTs have  now geared up for implementation of the schemes and utilisation of the central funds. State-wise and year-wise details of funds sanctioned/released for development of fisheries, dairying and animal husbandry during the period from the year 2014 to 2021 are at Annexure-I.*****
