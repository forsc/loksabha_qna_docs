                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Category wise Recruitment




Girish Chandra














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total number of recruitments made by the Railway Recruitment Board, category-wise (Unreserved/OBC/EBC/SC/ST)  during the last five years in Northern railway; (b)	whether there is zone-wise/UT-wise criteria for the recruitments in the Indian Railways through Railway Recruitment Board; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the total number of persons recruited in Indian Railways from Uttar Pradesh during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
