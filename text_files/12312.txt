                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1210






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Indian Institute of Skills (IISs)




Nihal Chand Chauhan













                                                    Jaskaur Meena
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up Indian Institute of Skills (IISs) in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Rajasthan;(c) 	the details of the funds allocated/sanctioned for the institutes;(d) 	whether the Government has identified the locations for this in Rajasthan; (e) 	if so, the details thereof, district-wise;(f) 	whether the Government is planning to set up these institutes under PPP model; and (g)	if so, the details thereof and the time by which these are likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (g) 	Yes Sir,the Government of India has approved the proposal of setting up ofIndian Institute of Skills (IISs) at three locations in the country at Kanpur (Uttar Pradesh), Mumbai (Maharashtra) and Ahmedabad (Gujarat) on Not-for-profitPublic Private Partnership (N-PPP) basis.As of now, there is no proposal for setting up of IIS in Rajasthan.
