                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1002






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Proposals regarding Environment and Forest




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether a number of proposals regarding environment and forest concerning Solapur district of Maharashtra is lying pending with his Ministry for the last three years;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the number of proposals sanctioned by his Ministry; and(c)	the number of proposals received from Solapur University during the last three years along with the time by which such proposals are likely to be sanctioned?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	to (c) As per the information available as on 18th November 2019, five (5) proposals concerning Solapur District of Maharashtra are pending for Environmental Clearance in this Ministry. The list of the proposal is at Annexure. A total of Thirteen (13) proposals have been granted Environmental Clearance in the district during the last three years in Solapur District. There is no available information regarding any pending proposal fromSolapur University during the last three years.****
