                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

600






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has established AYUSH Health & Wellness Centers across the country;b)	if so, the details of AYUSH Health & Wellness Centers established in North-eastern parts of the country including Tripura;c)	whether the Government has any data with regard to patients benefited in these Centres in the country;d)	if so, the details thereof during the last one year, State/UT-wise; ande)	the details of amount spent so far under National AYUSH Mission in the North-east including Tripura during the last two years; year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) & (b)	Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Ayush Mission (NAM), Ministry of Ayush has approved for operationalization of 1632, 2816 and 3065 AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) during the year 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 respectively (total 7513 Ayush HWCs) through State / UT Governments across the country as per the proposals received from them in their State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). 	Further, as per the proposals received from the North-Eastern parts of the country including the state of Tripura in their State Annual Action Plans of NAM, the Ministry has approved for operationalization of 310 Ayush HWCs. The State-wise detail of approved Ayush HWCs in North-Eastern parts of the country including the state of Tripura is furnished at Annexure- I.(c) & (d)	Yes, Sir. As per the reports submitted by the State/UT Governments, 97,92,861 patients have been benefited in Ayush HWCs in the country. The details of patients benefited in these Centres from January 2021 to December 2021, State/UT-wise; is furnished at Annexure- II.(e)	The details of amount spent by the Ministry of Ayush under National AYUSH Mission in the North-east including Tripura during the last two years; year-wise is furnished at Annexure- III. *********
