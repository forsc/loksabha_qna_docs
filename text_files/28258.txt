                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

99






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Special Financial Package




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	Whether the Government has released the special financial package of Rs.700 crore requested by Andhra Pradesh;b)	if so, the share of this requested amount has been released to the State; c)	whether entire amount has not been released and if so, the reasons therefor; d)	whether the Government follows up the release of funds by an inquiry as to the utilization of the same vis-à-vis the requested purpose; ande)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY) (a) to (c): The Union Government has released an amount of Rs. 1750.00 crore to the State Government of Andhra Pradesh for development of seven backward districts of the State in terms of the Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act, 2014.(d) & (e): Yes Sir. Utilisation Certificates amounting to Rs. 1049.34 crore against the funds released amounting to Rs.1750 crore have been received from the State Government of Andhra Pradesh. The Union Government through NITI Aayog monitors utilisation of funds released under the Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act, 2014 to the State.                                                                         ********
