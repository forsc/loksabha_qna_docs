                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1966






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






National Highway Projects




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of ongoing National Highway (NH) projects in the country particularly in Madhya Pradesh;(b) the number of NH projects in the country lying pending due to want of No Objection Certificate from Department of Environment and Forests; and(c) the number of such NH projects in Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) As on 30th of November 2021, there are 2283 NH projects that are ongoing in the country, of which 90 are in the State of Madhya Pradesh. (b) & (c) No Objection Certificate from Department of Environment and Forest is required in 169 ongoing NH projects in the country, of which 9 are in the State of Madhya Pradesh. *****
