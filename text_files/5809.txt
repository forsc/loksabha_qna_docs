                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5305






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Ocean Orium Park in Andhra Pradesh




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up Ocean Orium Park in Rishikonda, Visakhapatnam district in Andhra Pradesh under Public Private Partnership (PPP) model;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the progress made in this regard;(c)	whether fishery technology expertise delegations from foreign countries like Germany and China have visited to study this project; and(d)	if so, the details along with the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	As per the information received from Government of Andhra Pradesh, there is no such proposal under consideration for Ocean Orium Park in Rishikonda, Visakhapatnam.(b) to (d) Do not arise.                                          *****
