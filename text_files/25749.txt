                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1507






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Projects under NEC




Tokheho Yepthomi














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION be pleased to state the details of approved projects under NEC and NLCPR from March 2018-2021 from the State of Nagaland?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of Development of North Eastern Region [Shri G.Kishan Reddy]Under the “Schemes of NEC” and NERSDS implemented by NEC, total of 32 projects costing Rs.619.44 Crore were sanctioned from March 2018-2021 for the State of Nagaland. Sector-wise break-up is given below:<pre>Sl. No.	Sector	No. of Projects	Approved Amount (Rs.in Cr.)1	Agriculture & Allied	8	24.702	Human Resource Development & Employment (Education & Social)	4	14.493	Human Resource Development & Employment (Sports)	1	2.924	Industries	10	24.445	Information & Public Relations	1	0.676	Medical & Health	1	3.007	Power	1	5.838	Transport & Communication	5	541.529	Tourism	1	1.88 	Grand Total	32	619.44</pre>NLCPR Scheme was discontinued with effect from 15.12.2017 and replaced with North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS).  NLCPR Scheme is continuing for the limited  purpose of meeting committed liabilities of projects sanctioned till 2017.  Under NESIDS, 13 projects for Rs. 233.68 crore were sanctioned for the State of Nagaland.  Sector wise break-up of the projects is given below:<pre>Sl. No.	Sector	No. of Projects 	Approved cost       (Rs. In crore) 1	Education	2	16.972	Health	2	38.963	Power	1	20.964	Road & Bridges	7	153.715	Water Supply	1	3.08GRAND TOTAL	13	233.68</pre>
