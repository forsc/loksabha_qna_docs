                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana  in Chhattisgarh




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        the number of districts covered under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana in Chhattisgarh; (b)        the number of such migrant labourers to whom skill development training has been provided by the Government under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana in Chhattisgarh, district-wise; (c)        the number of migrant labourers who returned to their homes during Covid-19 lockdown in Chhattisgarh; (d)        the sector-wise details of jobs provided by the Government to migrant labourers after imparting them Skill Development Training under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana; and (e)        the number of employed-migrant-labourers in each sector of the country, State-wise and district-wise including Chhattisgarh?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)        Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), 28 districts of the State of Chhattisgarh have been covered.(b)        Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) is for skill development of youth included migrant labourers. In State of Chhattisgarh, 1,72,665 candidates have been benefitted from the implementation of the scheme. The district-wise number of candidates benefitted in the State of Chhattisgarh under PMKVY is given at Annexure I.(c)        As per the data received from the State, 5,26,900 Migrant workers have returned to their home State of Chhattisgarh.(d)       Under PMKVY, placement opportunities are being provided to STT certified candidates, while RPL does not mandate placements as it recognizes the existing skills of candidate. The sector-wise number of migrants trained, certified and placed under STT and candidates oriented and certified under RPL in PMKVY-special programme are given at Annexure II.(e)        Also, in order to support Gareeb Kalyan Rojgar Abhiyan (GKRA), Ministry has implemented special programme under PMKVY for the training of reverse migrants in 116 districts of State of Assam, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, Odisha, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh.
