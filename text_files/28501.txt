                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

167






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Funds for Development of Sites in Kottayam




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the Government has listed any proposals from the State Government of Kerala of sites in Kottayam to be developed under the Swadesh Darshan and PRASAD Schemes;  (b) 	if so, the details thereof;  (c) 	whether the Government has allocated any funds for the development of the aforesaid sites; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof, including the amount of funds utilized?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM				(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a)	to (d) The Ministry of Tourism has  sanctioned 5 projects  under  SwadeshDarshan scheme  and  1 project  under Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive  (PRASHAD)  scheme  in the State  of  Kerala, which does not  include  Kottayam.  The  details of  the projects  sanctioned  in the  state of  Kerala  under  SwadeshDarshan  and  PRASHAD  schemes  are  annexed. ******** Annexure  STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (d) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.167 ANSWERED ON 29.11.2021 REGARDING FUNDS FOR DEVELOPMENT OF SITES IN KOTTAYAM THE DETAILS OF PROJECTS SANCTIONED UNDER SWADESH DARSHAN AND PRASHAD SCHEME.
