                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1493






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Cancellation of Trains due to Fog




Pratima Bhoumik













                                                    Virendra  Kumar
                                                








                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that like previous years this year also trains are running late/cancelled due to fog and if so, the details thereof, Zone-wise; (b) whether the uavailability of path is (Platform/ lines) also one of the reasons for delay in trains, if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard so far; (c) whether there is any shortage of washing pit lines and stabling lines and if so, the details thereof Zone-wise; and (d) the number of new washing pit lines that have been established in the last five years, zone-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	 to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
