                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Indian Textile Exports




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the targets fixed and achieved regarding export of textiles, item-wise;(b)	the share of major textile exporting countries of the world, country-wise and the steps taken by the Government to augment the share of Indian textile exports; and(c)	whether the textile workers are facing problems due to global and domestic textile industry due to falling exports, rising imports and slow down, if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken by the Government to protect them? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	A target of UDS 20,100 million was fixed for cotton, man-made, woollen and silk textiles during 2018-19 against which exports were valued at USD 18,254 million:(b):	Details of share of major textile exporting countries of the world:Country	2018 (Bn USD)	% ShareChina	121	34%USA	22	6%India	21	6%Germany	16	4%Italy	13	4%Turkey	13	4%Rep. of Korea	12	3%Taiwan 	10	3%Pakistan	8	2%Viet Nam	8	2%             Source: Trademap(c):	As per the data of Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence and Statistics, textile exports have increased by 7% from USD 19 billion in 2017-18 to USD 20.4 billion in 2018-19 while textile imports have remained stagnant at USD 5.6 billion in 2018-19.  To curtail imports of textiles, Government has doubled the Basic Custom Duty from 10% to 20% on 383 apparel HS lines (8 digit) w.e.f. 16th July 2018.  Further, Government has raised BCD (from 10% to 20%) on other textile products i.e. 298 MMF fabric, 5 Silk fabric, 22 other fabric, 75 Carpet, 9 Made-ups and 15 other textiles HS lines (8 digit).*******
