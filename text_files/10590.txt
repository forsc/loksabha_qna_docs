                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Cities with Metro Rail




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of the cities in which metro rail facility is available, State-wise;(b) 	the names of the cities for which proposals are pending with the Government and the work has not been started so far;(c) 	the names of the cities where the Government is proposing to provide metro facility by 2035, and(d)	 the budget allocated for these projects?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	The state - wise names of the cities where metro rail facility is available are in the Annexure.(b): 	Proposal for central financial assistance for implementation of metro rail in the cities of Chennai (Phase-II), Kochi(Phase-II), Bangalore (Phase 2A & 2B), Nagpur (Phase-II), Mumbai, Nasik and Thane have been forwarded by the respective State Governments. Metro rail project is cost intensive, which requires extensive inter-ministerial consultation and approval depends on feasibility of the project and availability of resources. (c):	Urban transport is an integral part of urban development, which is a state subject. Respective State Governments are, therefore, responsible planning and developing urban transport including metro rail in cities or urban agglomerates. The Central Government considers financial assistance for such proposals on the basis of the feasibility of the project and availability of the resources, as and when posed by the concerned State Government. (d):	Rs. 19,102.88 crore has been allocated in Budget Estimate of 2019-20 for metro rail projects including Regional Rapid Transit System (RRTS).                                     *****
