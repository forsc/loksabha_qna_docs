                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4989






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Bridge over River Ganga




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether the Government proposes to construct a flyover/bridge over the river Ganga to facilitate the travel of villagers of Kadhagola in Katihar district across the river;(b)     if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government proposes to formulate any comprehensive plan with regard to this river which runs between Bihar and Jharkhand; and(d)     if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b) This Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways. Kadhagola in Katihar district is not on any National Highway and therefore this Ministry has no plan to construct bridge over river Ganga at Kadhagola.(c) to (d) This Ministry has planned to construct bridges across Ganga river near Buxar,  Dighwara-Sherpur-Patna, parallel to existing MG setu in Patna, Mokama, Munger and  Sahebganj in Bihar & Jharkhand States. *****
