                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3278






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana




Manoj Rajoria













                                                    Shardaben Anilbhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the total number of labourers registered so far under Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana (PM-SYM) across India along with the list thereof, State-wise; (b)whether the Government proposes to increase the contribution to be made by the Shram Yogis and to increase the amount of pension to be paid to them after 60 years of age in future and if so, the details thereof;(c)whether the Government proposes to provide the amount of contribution made by the Government to the labourers on 10 years or more contribution; and(d)the implementation status of PM-SYM and the details of the institutes entrusted with the management of funds?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		As on 27.07.2021, the State-wise details of number of enrolment under Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana (PM-SYM) scheme are given at Annexure.(b) & (c):	At present, there is no proposal to alter the structure of the scheme.(d):		Eligible unorganised workers can enroll themselves either at nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or through the portal maandhan.in. The pension fund is managed by Life Insurance Corporation (LIC). *                                                       ******
