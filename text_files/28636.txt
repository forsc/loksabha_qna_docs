                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

226






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Encroachment of Hindu Temples in South India




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Hindu temples are registered in South India and out of these the number of temples that are under the Government administration and private management;(b)	the details of those Hindu temples that are under encroachment in above both category;(c)	the details of the instructions given by the Government of India to the State Governments to remove the encroachments from the temples in South India; and(d)	whether these instructions have been followed and if not, the  reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	There are 1097 monuments and sites which  have been declared as Centrally Protected  in the State of Andhra Pradesh (135), Karnataka (506), Kerala (29), Pudduchery- UT (07), Tamil Nadu (412) and Telangana (08). Out of these 448 are Temples.(b)	The details of temples where encroachments reported are at Annexure.(c) & (d)	Wherever encroachment is reported, notice is given to the defaulter to remove the same; failing which, Demolition Order is issued as per provisions of the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act,1958 with request to Commissioner or Collector to cause demolition of the encroachment.  Sometimes litigations in various courts also cause delay in removal of encroachments.
