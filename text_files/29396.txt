                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

877






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Ujjwala Yojana




Mimi Chakraborty














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of beneficiaries under the PM Ujjwala Yojana as on August 31, 2020; (b) the total number of month-wise free refills that each beneficiary has availed under the Garib Kalyan Yojana since January 2020; and (c) the total number of free refills provided to Ujjwala beneficiaries since January, 2020 under the Garib Kalyan Yojana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) 	The total number of beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Ujjawala Yojana(PMUY) as on August 31, 2020 was 8.01 crore. (b) & (c) During Covid-19 pandemic, a Scheme for providing upto 3 (three) free of cost LPG refills to PMUY beneficiaries was started w.e.f. 01.04.2020 under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package (PMGKP). The scheme completed its term on 31.12.2020. Month-wise details of free refills provided to PMUY beneficiaries under Scheme are as under :- Month	No. of refills delivered under PMGKP (in lakh)April, 2020	429.3May, 2020	378.19June, 2020	305.24July, 2020	104.93August, 2020	89.36September, 2020	88.8October, 2020	12.81November, 2020	4.86December, 2020	3.63Total	1417.12----------
