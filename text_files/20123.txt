PMGSY in Jharkhand




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the target for the total length of roads to be constructed in Jharkhand under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years and the current year, year-wise as on date;(b) 	the length of roads completed and under construction in Jharkhand especially Hazaribagh and Ramgarh districts under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana; and(c) 	the details of the total cost incurred, including maintenance cost during the said period, year-wise as on date?
