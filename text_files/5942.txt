                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5382






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Direct Flights for International Destinations




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the airport-wise number of direct flights for international destinations from various airports of the country;(b) the number of direct flights launched and withdrawn by Air India from Lucknow for international destinations during the last three years and the current year;(c) the reasons for withdrawing the said flight services; and(d) the details of the frequency and number of direct flights operated by all aviation companies from Lucknow to international destinations?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) The detail of airport-wise number of direct flights for international destinations (as per summer schedule 2019) from various airports of the country is at Annexure-I. ;(b) Air India Limited has neither launched nor withdrawn any direct flights  from Lucknow for international destinations during the last three years and the current year. (c) Does not arise.;(d) The details of the frequency and number of direct flights operated by all aviation companies from Lucknow to international destinations is at Annexure-II.
