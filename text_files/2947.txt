                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Funds to Traditional and Small Fishermen for Fisheries Infrastructure




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is considering allocating any amount to the traditional and small fishermen to ensure such storage and marketing facilities as ice-box, cold storage, ice-plant and infrastructure facilities;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c)	 if not, the outline of the Government in this regard;(d) 	the total land converted into ponds by fishermen/peasants for fishing purposes during the last five years;(e) 	the details thereof, State and year-wise; and(f) 	the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (f) : The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries inter-alia provides financial assistance for strengthening of Post Harvest Infrastructure facilities fisheries such as (i) Ice plants,  Cold storages, Ice plants-cum-cold storages, (ii)  Renovation/modernization of existing Ice plants, Cold storages and Ice plants-cum-cold storages, (iii) Development of retail fish markets and allied infrastructure and (iv) setting up of mobile/retail fish outlets. The CSS also provides financial assistance for fish transport infrastructure such as Refrigerated Trucks/Containers, Insulated Trucks, Auto Rickshaw with ice box, Motorcycle with ice box, Bicycle with ice box and procurement of insulated fish & ice holding boxes. Besides, in order to address the infrastructural requirements for fisheries sector, a dedicated fund namely Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Fund (FIDF) has been launched during 2018-19 with a fund size of Rs 7522.48 crore. Under the FIDF concessional finance is also provided for development of cold storage, ice-plants, fish transport and cold chain infrastructures.	State-wise details of area taken up for fish culture during last five years from 2014-15 to 2018-19 are furnished at Annexure-I
