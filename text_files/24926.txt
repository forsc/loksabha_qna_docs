                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

725






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Foreign Direct Investment




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	The details of present Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) limit in different channels of communication like television, news channels and FM radio etc. in the country;b)	Whether the Government is contemplating any changes regarding FDI policy so as to improve quality and standards of different channels of communications to international level; andc)	If so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS             {SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) to (c) :        Under the extant FDI policy of the Government of India, the FDI limit for newspapers is 26%, for satellite news channels and FM radio it is 49%, while for non-news channels, FDI upto 100% is permissible. At present, there is no proposal to change these limits.*****
