                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1405






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Jewellery Parks




Pragya Singh Thakur













                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                








                                                    Nayab Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering to set up jewellery parks in various cities including Mumbai to promote the export of gems and jewellery; (b)	if so, whether any time limit has been fixed to complete the said parks; (c)	whether there is any proposal to set up such parks in Madhya Pradesh including Bhopal, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh and cities like Delhi; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a)	to (d): At present, there is no proposal for setting up of Jewellery Park in any city by    the Government.                                        ******
