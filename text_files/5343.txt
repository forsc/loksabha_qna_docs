                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4928






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the roads constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana(PMGSY) during the last three years i.e.2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 in terms of kilometers along with the funds spent thereon, State-wise;(b)	whether the Government has fixed any time frame to complete the construction work of the remaining/selected roads under the PMGSY; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The State-wise details of roads constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years i.e. 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 in terms of kilometres along with the funds spent thereon are given at Annexure.(b) & (c):	All the eligible and feasible habitations in 500+ population categories in plain areas and 250+ in North-Eastern and Himalayan States, Deserts, Tribal and Backward Districts,  were targeted for providing all-weather road connectivity by March, 2019. Out of 1, 78, 184 habitations as per Census,2001 identified for coverage under the PMGSY, 1,66,234 habitations have been connected through all-weather roads, including 16,086 habitations connected by States out of their own resources.  4,053 habitations have been dropped or were not non-feasible. Of the remaining 7,897 sanctioned habitations, States have reported that 2,495 habitations are not feasible as of now due to reasons such as forest clearance, land issues and Court cases etc. The remaining feasible habitations are targeted for completion at the earliest.  The task of providing all-weather road connectivity to all eligible and feasible habitations in 100-249 categories in critical Left Wing Extremism, as also the phase-II of PMGSY are targeted for completion by March, 2020. The Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Areas, which was launched as separate vertical under PMGSY is targeted for completion by March, 2020.*****
