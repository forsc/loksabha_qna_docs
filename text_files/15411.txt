                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3951






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Medical Supply to China




S. Ramalingam













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has assured the Chinese Government to supply medicines and other medical equipments to tackle the coronavirus (Covid-19) outbreak which claimed more than 2500 lives in China;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the value of medical consignment sent to China;(d) whether the Chinese Government has agreed and accepted the consignment sent from India; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (e)	Prime Minister had written to Chinese President Xi Jinping on 8 February 2020, in view of the coronavirus outbreak in China, conveying the solidarity of the people and Government of India with the people of China and the readiness of Government of India to provide assistance.   	The Government of India provided 15 tonnes of medical assistance comprising masks, gloves and other emergency medical equipment to China on 26 February 2020. These supplies were delivered by an Indian Air Force C-17 special flight which landed in Wuhan, China. The details of the medical assistance provided are as under:<pre>Medical Supplies Sent To ChinaS.No.Description of CommodityQuantity1.Surgical Masks1 Lakh pieces2.Surgical Gloves5 Lakh pairs3.Infusion Pumps75 pieces4.Enteral Feeding Pumps30 pieces5.Defibrillator21 pieces6.N-95 Mask4000 pieces</pre>These medical supplies costed approximately Rs. 2.11 crores. They were handed over to the Hubei Charity Federation in Wuhan, China.***
