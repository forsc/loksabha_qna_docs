                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

975






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Financial Assistance to States for Pandemic




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Union Government has extended financial assistance to the State Governments for taking safety measures and control the COVID-19 pandemic;(b) if so, the the financial assistance provided by the Union Government to States/UTs including the State of Chhattisgarh;(c) the names of the schemes through which the funds are being provided to the State Governments by his Ministry;(d) whether State Governments have also been provided support through National Health Mission (NHM); and(e) if so, the district-wise details thereof including Bilaspur, Mungeli and Gaurela-Pendra-Marwahi districts of the State of Chhattisgarh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	Yes. Ministry of Health & Family Welfare has extended financial assistance to the State Governments for managing COVID. The disbursement from India COVID-19 Emergency Response and Health system preparedness Package through NHM the States including that for Chhattisgarh is at Annexure.All States and UTs are provided with necessary financial support through National Health Mission under “India COVID-19 Emergency Response and Health system preparedness Package”. During FY 2020-21, funds of Rs. 4256.79 crore have been released to States/UTs as on 10.09.2020.(e)    District-wise allocations are decided by States, based on need. The information on district-wise allocation is not maintained by Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.
