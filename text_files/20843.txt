                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2073






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Promotion of Hindi




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken concrete steps for the promotion of Hindi Language especially in terms of its electronic usage;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the suggestions of Kendriya Hindi Samiti and departmental Hindi Advisory committees; and(d) the status of upgradation of Government websites in Hindi?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)& (b) Yes Sir. The Department of official language has launched the ‘LILA-Rajbhasha’ web/mobile app, ‘LILA-Pravah’ web/mobile app, ‘E-Mahashabdkosh’ web/mobile app and ‘E-Patrika Library’ for promotion of the official language of the Union of India–Hindi. The Department has also launched a compilation of Hindi-English sentences under the ‘E-Saral Hindi Vakya Kosh’ and a memory based translation tool ‘Kanthasth’ also. Three institutes under the Ministry of Education have been working for the promotion and propogation of Hindi language viz., Kendriya Hindi Sansthan (KHS), Agra, Commission for Scientific and Technical Terminology (CSTT), New Delhi and Central Hindi Directorate (CHD), New Delhi. KHS conducts various courses for promotion of Hindi language in Non-Hindi speaking states through online mode. CSTT works towards Evolution and Standardisation of Scientific and technical terminology in Hindi language and has published technical Glossaries/Departmental Glossaries/Definitional Dictionaries and Journals which are available on CSTT Publication website. CHD implements various schemes such as preparation of dictionaries in Hindi and various Indian and foreign  languages, correspondence Courses, supplementary Educational Material and standardization of Devnagri Script and hindi spellings . The dictionaries and Glossaries are available on the institute’s website. (c) The suggestions of the Kendriya Hindi Samiti in its 31st meeting held on 06.09.2018 and the Departmental Hindi Advisory Committee of this Ministry in its meeting held on 14.05.2018 are placed at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.(d) The Department of official language has informed that website of almost all the Ministries/Departments are partially bilingual.****
