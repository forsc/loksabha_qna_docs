                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1815






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Highway Projects in Assam




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of ongoing projects to build, develop and expand the 	highway network in the State of Assam indicating the new 	projects and the pending projects along with their date of 	sanction;(b) 	the details of contracting agencies assigned by National 	Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited 	(NHIDCL) to execute these projects;(c) 	the details of projects completed within the stipulated time 	schedule;(d) 	the details of projects pending along with the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the action taken against the defaulting contractors/agencies for 	their inability to complete the projects within the stipulated time 	period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)  (a) The details of ongoing projects on National Highways (NH) in the State of Assam are given at Annexure-I. (b) The details of contracting agencies to whom work assigned by National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL) are given at Annexure-II.(c) The details of projects completed within the stipulated time schedule in the last three years in the State of Assam are given at Annexure-III.(d) The details of delayed projects in the state of Assam are given at Annexure-IV.(e) The actions like imposing liquidity damage, termination of contract, declaring non- performer, black listing etc. are taken on case to case basis against the defaulting contractors for their inability to complete the projects within the stipulated time period.
