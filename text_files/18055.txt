                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1751






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Vande Bharat Mission




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle













                                                    Hanuman Beniwal
                                                








                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                








                                                    Arun Sao
                                                








                                                    Basanta Kumar Panda
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Indians who were registered and brought back to India under the ‘Vande Bharat Mission’ from various countries during the global COVID-19 pandemic till date, State and country-wise;(b) whether there are still such Indians stuck in foreign countries who want to return to India;(c) if so, the details thereof and the action being taken by the Government in this regard; and (d) the details of the future schemes of the Government under Vande Bharat Mission?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN] (a) to (d) 	As on 16 September 2020, the number of Indian nationals who have returned to India under the Vande Bharat Mission is 14,47,481. The State-wise and country-wise details are at Annex I and Annex II. Most Indian nationals stuck in foreign countries have returned to India. The Vande Bharat Mission is ongoing with Phase 6 commenced on 1 September 2020. With increase in charter flights and bilateral Air Travel Bubble arrangements with select countries, frequency of flights has increased, enabling Indians to directly book tickets for travelling to India. ***
