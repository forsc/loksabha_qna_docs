                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Welfare of Physically and Mentally Challenged Persons




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the assistance provided and works done in the direction of education, training and rehabilitation of physically and mentally challenged people in the country, State-wise particularly in Maharashtra as on date;(b) the details of the beneficiaries during the said period, State-wise particularly in Maharashtra; and(c) the other schemes launched/to be launched by the Government for the welfare of physically and mentally challenged people along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT (SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) to (c) The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities is providing financial assistance to several schemes / projects through its National Institutes, National Trust, National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC) and Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation of India (ALIMCO) as well as through Autonomous Bodies/ Organizations / Institutes and Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) for education, training and rehabilitation of physically and mentally challenged people in the country, including the State of Maharashtra. Details of assistance provided, works done and the details of the beneficiaries under the major schemes implemented by the Government for Divyangjans are at Annexure.****
