                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2212






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Loss of Jobs in State of Gujarat




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any official and authenticated information regarding loss of jobs in various fields in the State of Gujarat during COVID-19; and (b)	whether the Government has figures of the badly affected areas due to heavy loss of jobs and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): The data on employment/ unemployment is being collected through Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) conducted by National Statistical Office (NSO), Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation (MOSPI) since 2017-18.	As per the results of annual Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) conducted during 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, the estimated Worker Population Ratio (WPR) and the estimated unemployment rate for persons of 15 years and above on usual status basis in the country and in the state of Gujarat is as under:      (in percentage )Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	WPR	UR	WPR	UR	WPR	URAll India	46.8	6.0	47.3	5.8	50.9	4.8Gujarat	47.4	4.8	49.7	3.2	54.7	2.0    WPR-Worker Population Ratio         	  	   UR- Unemployment Rate*****
