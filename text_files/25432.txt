                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1290






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Horticulture Crops Production in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the production of horticulture crops including vegetables, fruits, and flowers, in Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur districts of West Bengal;(b)	whether the Ministry has taken cognizance of the fact that the production of oranges and cardamom in Darjeeling and Kalimpong regions has gone down in past decades;(c)	if so, the steps taken by the Ministry to assist the growers;  (d)	whether the Ministry will consider establishing a central university  for horticulture or agriculture in North Bengal, particularly in Darjeeling or Kalimpong District; and(e)	if so, the details of the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)   The details of the production of horticulture crops including Vegetables, Fruits, and Flowers, in Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur districts of West Bengal for the year 2019-20, as reported by Government of West Bengal is at Annexure-I(b) & (c) As reported by Indian Council of Agricultural Research-Indian Agricultural Research Institute (ICAR-IARI) and Uttar Banga Krishi Viswavidyalaya (UBKV), the area and production of Orange (Mandarin) has gone down in Darjeeling and Kalimpong in past decade because of shifting of area to Vegetables Cultivation & Spices (large Cardamom). Area under large Cardamom in Darjeeling and Kalimpong are increasing. ICAR-IARI has reported the following remedial steps for addressing the issue:- 1.	Started production of healthy planting materials (grafted and nucellar seedlings).2.	To address the issue of rootstock availability, the Institute has produced more than One Lakh rootstocks.3.	Four village clusters in the region of Sitong, Mungpoo, Balason and Icche gaon was adopted and farmers were mobilized and trained for scientific Darjeeling mandarin cultivation.4.	Under IARI, extension and technology transfer programme with the assistance of Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), Government of West Bengal and National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD), so far 20,000 new healthy planting materials were distributed among the farming communities.5.	Approximately, 100 Ha area has been covered under new plantation by IARI (Research Station), Kalimpong.6.	Four nurseries were established for production of healthy planting materials of Darjeeling Mandarin by the farmers at village level in Mungpoo, Sitong, Balason and Icche gaon of Kalimpong and Darjeeling hills.UBKV has informed that they are giving major emphasis to Clonal selection of Darjeeling Mandarin Orange and production of quality planting materials to improve the production of Oranges in the region.As per Directorate of Arecanut and Spices Development (DASD), the production of Cardamom has not gone down in Kalimpong and Darjeeling region during the last decade. In fact, the production of Cardamom in Darjeeling region has increased from 476 Tonne in 2017-18 to 483 Tonne in 2020-21 and production in Kalimpong region has increased from 347 Tonne in 2017-18 to 352 Tonne in 2020-21. (d) & (e) Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) has reported that there is no such proposal under consideration in the ICAR for establishing a Central University for Horticulture or Agriculture in North Bengal.
