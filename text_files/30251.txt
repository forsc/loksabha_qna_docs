                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1613






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Jaisalmer-Kandla Rail Project




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the current status of Jaisalmer-Kandla rail project; (b) whether the Government of Rajasthan has given its consent in principle to provide land for the said project; and (c) if so, the details thereof and the follow up action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) : Jaisalmer is already connected by Broad Gauge rail line to Kandla Port via Jodhpur. However, for direct connectivity, surveys for Jaisalmer to Barmer and Barmer to Bhabhar were completed in 2012-13. As these projects were financially unviable and since adequate connectivity already exists, these projects have not been taken forward.  (b) & (c) :  Do not arise.*****
