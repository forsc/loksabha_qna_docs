                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2479






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






NH Projects in Kerala




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to give priority to  land  acquisition  process  for  the  development of National Highways (NHs) and if so, the details of action taken to 	expedite the process;(b) 	the details of action taken for the speedy disposal of pending cases before the court with  	regard to the land acquisition for the development of NH in Kerala;(c)  	the present status and proposed date of completion of process of land acquisition for NH projects in Kollam, Kerala;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to start the work on NH projects for which land is already   available and if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	whether the Government proposes to start the NH development work at Kollam bypass 	in which land is available and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) The Kerala Government has consented to share 25% land acquisition cost for development projects on NH-66, for which a Financial Participation Agreement (FPA) has been signed amongst Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, PWD Kerala and Kerala Infrastructure Investment Finance Board (KIIFB) on 03.10.2019 in order to categorically establish procedure/ modalities for remittance of amount corresponding to land acquisition cost to be borne by the concerned stakeholders. Progress of these projects/ land acquisition is monitored at various levels with all stakeholders to ensure early completion.(c) to (e) 3 (A) notification under NH Act 1956 for the project stretch Kottankulangara to Kadambattukonam section of NH-66 (old NH-47) has been published and process for notification under section 3(D) has been initiated. 2-laning of Kollam bypass has been completed. Kollam Bypass is part of NH-66 (old NH-47) and the Detailed Project Report (DPR)/ Feasibility Study for 4/6 laning of this stretch has been initiated. Development of project is taken up post finalisation of alignment (greenfield/ or brownfield), cost estimates, land acquisition requirement etc. based on outcome of DPR/ Feasibility Study with due consideration to traffic density, up-gradation requirements, lane configuration (i.e. 2-lane/4-lane/6-lane etc.), Right of Way (ROW) determination, project viability, inter-se-priority and availability of funds.*****
