                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1153






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Central Assistance Scheme for ST




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     the details of the grants-in-aid being provided under Special Central Assistance Scheme to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA-TSS) to Scheduled Tribes by the Government in the country including Rajasthan;(b)     the details of the amount provided to Rajasthan under this Scheme during the last three years;(c)     whether the Government proposes to increase this grants-in-aid, if so, the details thereof; and(d)    whether the Union Government has received proposals from the Rajasthan Government in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): The details of the funds released to the State Governments including the State of Rajasthan during last three years under the scheme of SCA to TSS is furnished vide Annexure. (c): Grants-in-aid under the scheme of SCA to TSS is provided to the State Governments according to their share of allocation out of total allotment under the scheme. The share of allocation of the State is arrived at on the basis of following criteria:(i)  2/3rd weightage to proportion of ST population in the State as per latest census to total ST population.(ii) 1/3rd weightage to proportion of tribal dominated areas (i.e. Districts where tribal population is 25% or more and LWE affected Districts).(d):      Based on the proposals received from the Government of Rajasthan under the scheme of SCA to TSS, an amount of Rs.5911.00 lakh has been approved for the year 2020-21 so far.Annexure Annexure referred to in reply to part (a) & (b) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1153 for answer on 19.09.2020 Details of the Funds released to the State Governments under the scheme of SCA to TSS during the last three years(Rs. in lakh)S.No.	 States	Funds Released		2017-18	2018-19	2019-201	Andhra Pradesh	3624.77	5617.39	12470.502	Arunachal Pradesh	0.00	2211.83	9224.293	Assam	0.00	0.00	2710.084	Bihar	0.00	0.00	0.005	Chhattisgarh	14327.57	10342.65	9415.536	Goa	559.09	352.31	0.007	Gujarat	10270.41	11765.38	8975.558	Himachal Pradesh	2291.20	3628.00	2394.189	J & K	3626.50	3749.80	4509.0010	Jharkhand	11372.49	8564.52	6201.4911	Karnataka	5955.37	5347.76	7434.0012	Kerala	808.43	335.00	0.0013	Madhya Pradesh	22828.70	16968.97	13415.2514	Maharashtra	13760.38	13802.57	11929.0915	Manipur	3790.38	5442.48	1434.0216	Meghalaya	0.00	2739.20	3788.0017	Mizoram	0.00	1220.00	3140.0018	Nagaland	0.00	3225.00	2951.1219	Odisha	11975.00	17553.22	8691.1820	Rajasthan	10051.83	10327.93	11461.4121	Sikkim	5986.00	0.00	0.0022	Tamilnadu	894.10	315.00	450.5623	Telangana	4493.55	2850.32	5361.2924	Tripura	1649.77	1294.38	1362.9725	Uttarakhand	679.00	1012.88	600.0026	Uttar Pradesh	458.35	0.00	779.9127	West Bengal	5397.11	5833.41	5862.58 	Total	134800.00	134500.00	134562.00 *****
