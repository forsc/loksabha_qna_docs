                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Financial Package for Revival of Tourism




Kishan Kapoor














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to announce any special financial package for States to revive tourism sector which is the most affected by the Coronavirus pandemic; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):  Representations for financial relief received from industry stakeholders and State Governments have been taken up with the Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance.   As of now, the Ministry of Finance has not made any announcement regarding special financial package for states for the revival of the tourism sector.*******
