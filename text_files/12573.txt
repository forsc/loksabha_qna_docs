                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1478






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






PMGSY in Odisha




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of roads built under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years, State-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has the data regarding the number of roads built under the said scheme in the aspirational districts such as Rayagada/Koraput in the State of Odisha;(c) 	the details of financial incentives given for best performing States, State-wise; and(d) 	the details of the parameters for determining the best performing States and the ranking of Odisha in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): The number of roads constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)  during the last three years, State-wise are given at  Annexure-I.(b): As per the available information, a total of 33,402 roads measuring length 1,34,273.547 Km road length has been constructed under PMGSY in Aspirational Districts in the Country. The details regarding roads constructed under PMGSY in the Rayagada and Koraput Districts of Odisha are as under:- Aspirational Districts	                               Completed	                             No. of Roads	         Road Length                                                                   (km)Koraput	                                740	               3,594.00Rayagada	                        560	               2,917.46(c): A sum of Rs. 804.19 crore was released as financial incentives under PMGSY to the best performing States during the year 2018-19 as per the following details:-Rank	              Name of State	               Amount of Financial                                                       incentive (Rs. in crore)1	             Uttar Pradesh	                     116.632	             Madhya Pradesh	                     144.1193	             Haryana 	                              13.204	             Kerala	                               5.115	             Andhra Pradesh	                      32.986	             Rajasthan	                             150.057	             Bihar	                              79.438	             Tamil Nadu	                              30.149	             Assam	                              36.1810	             Himachal Pradesh	                      26.1211	             Jammu & Kashmir	                       9.3112	             Odisha	                              73.6813	             Karnataka	                              46.6314	             West Bengal	                      40.54(d): The parameters for determining the best performing States for grant of financial incentives under PMGSY in 2018-19 were as under:-i.	Achievement of Target Length during the year;ii.	Achievement of Target Habitations during the year;iii.	Length constructed using New Technology during the year;iv.	Expenditure on maintenance of roads within Defect Liability Period (DLP)[Percentage of expenditure incurred as against the requirement for maintenance of roads within DLP];v.	Road Length renewed during post DLP - % renewed[Length of roads renewed post 5 year DLP (cumulative length renewed as against total road length post DLP];vi.	Inspection of maintenance work- National Quality Monitor Inspections.The rank of Odisha during 2018-19 was 12.***
