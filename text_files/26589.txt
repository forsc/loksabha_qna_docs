                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2116






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Implementation of National Virtual Library




Gautham Sigamani Pon













                                                    Jayant Sinha
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has failed in its implementation of schemes under the National Mission on Libraries (NML) and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(b)	the details of funds provided for archives, Government libraries and museums in the State of Tamil Nadu during the last three years and the current year, year-wise and scheme-wise;(c)	the current status and implementation of National Virtual Library of India (NVLI) under NML and its achievement;(d)	whether the Government proposes to enact a National Public Library Act as only few States have enacted and implemented library legislations and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the steps being taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to develop libraries and promote reading habits in the people across the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	No, Sir.(b)	Details in Annexure-A.(c)	Indian Culture Portal (https://indianculture.gov.in/) is available on public domain as the output of the NVLI component of NML. •	It has a total of about 2.5 lakh digital artefacts, with metadata. It also has more than 9 lakh bibliographic entries.•	The content is presented in 19 categories, such as Rare Books, E-Books, Archives, Paintings, and Union Catalogues. •	It also has 9 categories of content, such as Stories, Snippets, Historical Cities, and Forts.•	The portal is currently available in English and Hindi.•	The portal can be accessed through an App called Indian Culture, available on both Android phones and iPhone.(d) As per Seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India, Library is a State Subject and the developmental activity of libraries comes under the purview of the respective State/Union Territory authorities.(e) Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation (RRRLF), Kolkata, an autonomous organization under Ministry of Culture provides financial assistance under its matching and non-matching schemes for development of public libraries on the basis of applications recommended by the respective State Library Authorities. Financial assistance is provided under its component ‘Setting up of NML Model Libraries’ to develop certain State Central Libraries, District Libraries and Libraries under the Ministry of Culture.Annexure-A for reply to the part (b) of LSUSQ No. 2116Details of funds provided for archives, Government libraries and museums in the State of Tamil Nadu during the last three years and the current year, year-wise and scheme-wise.Financial Year2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22   (upto June, 2021)No. of Beneficiaries	Amount released	No. of Beneficiaries	Amount released	No. of Beneficiaries	Amount released	No.  of Beneficiaries	Amount releasedARCHIVESScheme of Financial Assistance to State/UT archival repositories, government libraries and museums	1 Rs. 30,32,500/- --	 -- --	-- -- -- MUSEUMSMuseums Grant Scheme	1	Rs. 19,00,000/-	1	Rs. 3,30,88,000/-	1	Rs. 5,41,38,918/-	--	--Continuing Scheme-Existing Science Centre of National Council of Science Museum 	1 Rs. 1,80,09,000/-	1 Rs. 2,10,33,000/- 1	 Rs. 1,67,08,000/- 1	 Rs. 24,85,000/-Innovation Hubs Schemes under Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science	3 Rs. 28,12,000/- 2 Rs. 39,60,000/- 3	 Rs. 22,52,000/- --	 --PUBLIC LIBRARIES INCLUDING GOVERNMENT LIBRARIESNML Scheme	3	Rs. 45,64,896/-	3	Rs. 19,85,805/-	1	Rs. 5,17,745/-	--	--Matching and Non-Matching Scheme of Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation (RRRLF) 	1065	Rs. 9,65,52,047/-	44	Rs. 1,83,93,928/- 2590	Rs. 1,48,27,398/-	-- --
