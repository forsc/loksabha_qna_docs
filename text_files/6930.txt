                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

486






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Aadhaar Card to NRIs




(Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to provide the facility of Aadhaar Card to NRIs; (b) if so, the time by which the said facility is likely to be implemented; (c) the documents which are likely to be considered valid for issuing Aadhaar card to NRIs; and (d) the number of such persons so far issued Aadhaar card under the said system?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):   Yes, Sir. Non Resident Indian (NRI) shall be entitled to obtain Aadhaar number after arrival in India.(b):   Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) has already implemented facility of such Aadhaar enrolment for NRIs w.e.f.  20th September, 2019.(c):   Valid Indian Passport is accepted as Proof of Identity (PoI), Proof of Address (PoA) and Date of Birth (DoB). While, the Indian Passport is mandatory as PoI document for NRI, other valid PoA and DoB documents available to a Resident in Schedule-II of Aadhaar (Enrolment & Update) Regulations, 2016 and amendments thereunder, are accepted from NRI also. (d):   As on 14th November 2019, over 2800 Aadhaar have been generated to NRIs.********
