                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

701






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






NHs under Bharatmala Pariyojana




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has started work on National Highway (NH) Nos. 744, 183, 183A 	in Kerala under Bharatmala Pariyojana;(b) 	if so, the details and the present status thereof along with the funds allocated for the purpose 	during the last year and the current year; and(c) 	the present status of the new NHs on Alappuzha-Kodaikanal and Kayamkulam-Munnar 	sections in Kerala? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) No civil work for upgradation has been commenced on NH-744, 183 and 183A. However, the work for Detailed Project Report/Feasibility Study for widening of NH-183 and NH-183A has been initiated and study of alignment options for development of NH-744 has also been initiated.  (c) The road stretches connecting Alappuzha (on NH 66) to Vazhoor (on NH 183) via Changanassery and Kayamkulam (on NH 66) to Thiruvalla (on NH 183) (which is part of the road connecting Kayamkulam to Munnar) are not National Highways. However, these stretches have been identified as the potential stretches for declaration as new National Highways subject to outcome of DPR study as well as tying up of funding requirements. Study of alignment in the stretch connecting Alappuzha (on NH 66) to Vazhoor (on NH 183) has been initiated.*****
