                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






PRASAD Scheme in Bihar




Giridhari Yadav













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the PRASAD scheme of the Government has been implemented in Bihar; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, location-wise; (c) 	whether any proposal has been sent by the Government of Bihar under PRASAD scheme; (d) 	if so, the details thereof, location-wise and the action taken by the Government so far along with the outcome thereof; and (e) 	the reaction of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	The Ministry has identified Gaya and Patna Sahib as destinations for development under the PRASHAD Scheme in Bihar. The details of projects sanctioned under the scheme are as follows:i.	“Development of basic facilities at Vishnupad temple, Gaya” sanctioned in 2014-15 at the cost of Rs.4.27 Crore. The amount Rs.2.91 Crore has been released so far.ii.	“Development of Patna Sahib” sanctioned in 2015-16 at the cost of Rs.41.54 Crore. The amount Rs.33.23 Crore has been released so far.*****
