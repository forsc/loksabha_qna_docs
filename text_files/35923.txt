                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Road Projects in Kerala




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of major road construction projects being undertaken in the State of Kerala;(b)	the funds allocated for the purpose;(c)	the present status of widening project of National Highway-66 in the State; and(d)	the details of compensation/rehabilitation packages given to the affected persons with regard to this project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) & (b)	Upgradation to 4/6-lane of National Highway-544, 966A, 66 for length of about 204 km with cost of Rs. 4,035 crore has been completed. At present, 13 works in length of about 335 km with cost of Rs. 27,520 crore are in execution stage. In addition, 7 works for length 218.65 km have been awarded. Works for length of 52.15 km have been targeted for award in March, 2022. (c)	The status of widening projects on National Highway (NH)-66 in the State of Kerala is as under:No. of projectsLength (in km)	Status2	43.75	Completed13	334.81	Ongoing7	218.65	Awarded1	37.5	Balance for award1	13	DPR stage(d)	Compensation/rehabilitation to the land owners/affected persons are paid as per relevant provisions of “The Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013” & “The National Highway Act 1956”. *****
