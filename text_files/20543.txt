                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1629






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Target Olympic Podium Scheme




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any proposal for increase in the budgetary allocation to the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports and if so, the details thereof; (b) the funds allocated, received and spent under Target Olympic Podium (TOP) Scheme, till date; (c) the list of sportspersons who have been provided assistance under the Scheme, till date; and (d) the criteria in place for choosing sportspersons who will be provided assistance thereunder for Olympics?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a):	For the current financial year 2020-21, the Budget Estimate (BE) for Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports is Rs.2826.92 crore, as against Rs.2216.92 crore in 2019-20, which reflects a 27.5% increase. BE figures for FY 2018-19 and 2017-18 were Rs. 2196.35 crore and Rs.1943.21 crore respectively, which shows that efforts have been made to increase budget allocation of the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports over the years.(b):	No separate funds are allocated for Target Olympic Podium (TOP) Scheme. However, funds are released from National Sports Development Fund (NSDF) Scheme to Sports Authority of India (SAI) for expenditure on TOP Scheme. The details of funds released under NSDF to SAI for TOP Scheme are as under: <pre>Year	Funds Released to SAI(Rupees in crore)	Funds utilized by SAI(Rupees in crore)2016-17	20.00	19.612017-18	10.00	4.872018-19	10.00	8.842019-20	8.50	8.222020-21	5.00	-</pre>In addition, NSDF provides Out of Pocket Allowance (OPA) to TOPS athletes selected in Core Group at the rate of Rs.50,000 per month and to Development Group at the rate of Rs.25,000 per month, for athlete Details of funds released to athletes are as under:<pre>Year	Funds (OPA) Released to Athletes in Core Group(Rs. in crore)	Funds (OPA) Released to Athletes in Development Group(Rs. in crore)2016-17	-	-2017-18	6.51	-2018-19	5.94	-2019-20	5.63	-2020-21	4.60	3.00</pre>(c):	104 athletes and 2 teams are included in the TOPS Core Group and 269 athletes are included in the TOPS Development Group, whose details are in Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.(d): Selection criteria in place for selecting sports persons who are provided assistance under TOP Scheme are as follows:i)	Current performanceii)	Past performanceiii)	Performance in comparison to Global performance trendsiv)	Current age in comparison to High performance agev)	Global competitors’ analysisvi)	Performance progression ratevii)	World Rankings and National Ranking****
