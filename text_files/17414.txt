                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

799






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Assistance to Small Scale Units




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up new financial credit institutions to assist small scale units;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the manner in which it is likely to be done;(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) 	whether the Ministry has launched a portal named "Champions" and if so, the details thereof including assistance to be received by the people through this portal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a): 	No, Sir.(b): 	Doesn’t arise in view of above.(c): 	The Ministry seeks to efficiently channelize its credit schemes through the vast network of the existing RBI registered financial institutions viz. Scheduled Commercial Banks, Non Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) and Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) in the country for the benefit of eligible MSMEs.(d): 	An online portal ‘Champions’ has been launched on 01.06.2020 by Hon’ble Prime Minister. This covers many aspects of e-governance including grievance redressal and handholding of MSMEs. Through the portal, total 19,230 grievances have been redressed upto 14.09.2020. All public sector banks have also been onboarded on the portal for direct redressal of grievances.
