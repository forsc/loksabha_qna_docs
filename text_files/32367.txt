                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3799






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






National Animal Disease Control Programme




Manjulata Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying has taken into consideration timely supply of Foot and Mouth Disease (FMD) vaccine to the State of Odisha under National Animal Disease Control Programme and the release of Central Share in time as per the Budget Provision for effectively and timely implementation of different Centrally sponsored schemes like Livestock Health and Disease Control Programme, Assistance to States for Control of Animal Diseases, Peste des Petits Runinants - Eradication, National Livestock Mission and National Animal Disease Control Programme; and(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER THE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) and (b)Yes, Sir. The Department has taken into consideration timely supply of Foot and Mouth Disease (FMD) vaccine to Odisha. Under National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP), 93.28 lakh doses of FMD vaccine were supplied to Odisha in 2020-21 and in 2021-22,around 11.00 lakhFMD vaccine doses, as demanded by the State, have been allotted.  Proposals under National Livestock Mission and Livestock Health & Disease Control (LHDC)Programme are examined as per operational guidelines, availability of funds, status of unspent balances with States and availability of vaccines etc.. Under LHDC scheme, Rs. 189.84 lakh was released in 2020-21 and Rs. 2896 lakh has been released in 2021-22, so far.Further, in 2020-21, Rs. 1407.49 lakh and Rs.1032.76  lakh were released, respectively, under NADCP and National Livestock Mission.****
