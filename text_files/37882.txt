                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Heritage Circuit for Global Travellers




 Hema Malini














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched several schemes recently aimed at preservation, sustenance and better interpretative of components in the heritage circuit to meet the needs of the global travellers; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the steps taken by the Government to attract more global travellers to various tourists destinations of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        	  (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c): 	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*********STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*460 ANSWERED ON 04.04.2022 REGARDING HERITAGE CIRCUIT FOR GLOBAL TRAVELLERS(a) & (b):	 The Ministry of Tourism has launched 2 flagship schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. Swadesh Darshan scheme aims at development of thematic circuits and has sanctioned 10 projects under heritage theme in this scheme. The  Ministry has now   revamped  Swadesh Darshan  scheme  in  order  to  develop  sustainable  and  responsible  destinations  with  tourist  &  destination  centric  approach. 	The PRASHAD scheme of the Ministry of Tourism aims at integrated development of identified pilgrimage and heritage destinations. The  details of the projects  sanctioned  under  Heritage  theme of  ‘Swadesh Darshan’  scheme and the projects sanctioned under  ‘PRASHAD’  scheme  is annexed. (c):	 The Ministry of Tourism undertakes several promotional activities in potential international markets to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country in a holistic manner.  This includes release of media campaigns under ‘Incredible India’ brand-line. Promotion is also done through social media activities, webinars, participation in promotional events, website etc. In addition, the Indiatourism overseas offices of the Ministry of Tourism and Indian Missions overseas also undertake various promotional activities to attract more global travellers to various tourist destinations of the country.*******
