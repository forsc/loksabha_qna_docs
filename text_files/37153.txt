                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Pension to Widows




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether applications have been received from women beneficiaries under the pension scheme for widows and destitute women in Maharashtra during the last three years; and(b) 	if so, the number of applications received thereunder along with the number of women benefitted thereby, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)      Yes Sir.(b) 	Details of  district-wise applications received from women under the pension scheme for widows and destitute women and number of women benefitted in Maharashtra during the last three years is given at Annexure. *******
