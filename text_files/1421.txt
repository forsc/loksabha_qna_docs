                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






MoU on Cancer Research Initiative




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has signed the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between India and U.K. on Cancer Research Initiative and if so, the details thereof along with its outcome as on date;(b) the details of identification of a core set of research challenges that address issues of affordability, prevention and care of cancer;(c) the details of the total research funding for the initiative along with details of outcome as on date;(d) whether the Government hospitals are ill equipped to meet the massive burden of escalating cancer care costs, if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Government is likely to focus on low cost approaches to cancer treatment and if so, the details thereof






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	No.(b) & (c):	 Does not arise.(d) & (e):	Govt. has augmented facilities to meet the burden of escalating cancer care costs, with the following steps:I.	Cancer prevention, screening and early detection upto district level under National Health Mission (NHM).II.	Setting up State Cancer Institutes and Tertiary Care Cancer Centers across the country.III.	Oncology  focus in new All India Institutes of Medical Sciences and upgraded institutions under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojna (PMSSY)IV.	A new National Cancer Institute at Jhajjar, Haryana.V.	Affordable Medicines and Reliable Implants for Treatment (AMRIT) Deendayal outlets have been opened at 159 Institutions/Hospitals to make available medicines and transplants at discounted price.
