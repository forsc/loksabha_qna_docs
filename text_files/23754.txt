                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4663






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Forign Investors




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of foreign investors who have invested in India during the last five years; and(b) 	the details of investment made sector-wise along with the of year-wise investment amount during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH) (a):     	The remittance wise details of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) equity inflow as maintained by Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade (DPIIT) which inter alia includes the name of foreign collaborator are very voluminous and are available on DPIIT’s website under the link: https://dipp.gov.in/publications/si-news-letters (b):     	Sector wise & year wise details of the investment reported through FDI equity inflow during last five financial year & current financial year (upto December, 2020) are at Annexure-I. ********.
