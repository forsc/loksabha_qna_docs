                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2978






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Trade Deal between the US and India




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether a trade deal is in the offing between India and the United States of America (USA) and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the trade deal is disadvantageous to the farm and manufacturing sectors and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of the benefits, if any, that the trade deal will accrue to India; and (d)	whether the trade deal will increase exports to the USA, a country with whom India already has a trade surplus?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	to (d):   Trade related issues are a part of any ongoing economic relationship, and will continue to be discussed and addressed as a part of the regular bilateral trade engagement between India and the US.India has an open mind in dealing with any trade related matter while keeping its domestic and trade interests in mind. Decisions are taken in the larger public interest after having consultation with all stakeholders.****
