                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3489






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Ranking of Central Universities




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number and rankings of the Central Universities, State Universities and Private Universitiesfunctioning in the country, State/UT-wise;(b) the number of Open Universities in the country;(c) whether the certificates issued by the Open Universities are valid across the country;(d) the number of universities which have been black listed in the country along with the names thereof;and(e) the year-wise details of the amount of assistance given by the Government to these Universities alongwith the details thereof during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a): Sir, there are total 908 Universities (49 Central Universities, 399 State Universities, 126 Deemed to beUniversities, and 334 Private Universities) in the country. The list of top 200 Central/ State/ Private/Deemed to be Universities, as per NIRF ranking is available atwebsite https://www.nirfindia.org/2019/UniversityRanking.html(b): There are 14 State Open Universities and one Central Open University in the country.(c): Yes, Sir. The degree certificates (at Undergraduate/Postgraduate level) issued by the OpenUniversities recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) to offer programmes through Openand Distance Learning (ODL) mode, are valid if they are offered as per the provisions stipulated in theUGC (ODL) Regulations, 2017 and its subsequent amendments.(d) & (e): As per UGC Public Notice dated 24th April, 2017, Sam Higginbottom Institute of Agriculture,Technology & Sciences (SHIATS), Allahabad has been debarred from offering distance educationprogrammes from 2016-17 to 2019-20. In this regard, the UGC vide its letters dated 21.3.2016 and5.7.2016 had intimated SHIATS, Allahabad that the admission process for distance educationprogrammes from the academic year 2016-17 should not be initiated before obtaining prior approval ofUGC. The UGC has informed that this University was given the financial assistance of Rs. 10.51 lakhduring the year 2016-17 and no grant has been given to it during 2017-18 and 2018-19.*****
