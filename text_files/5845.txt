                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5351






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Cleaning of Ponds




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of amount spent on cleaning of ponds in the country including Uttar Pradesh during the last three years, State and year-wise; (b) whether the cleaning work of ponds has been completed; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government has made/proposes to make any plan for proper cleaning of ponds; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (e)  Works related to water resources development & management are planned, funded, executed and maintained by the State Governments themselves as per their own resources and priorities.  In order to supplement the efforts of the State Governments, Government of India provides technical and financial assistance to State Governments to encourage sustainable development and efficient management of water resources through various schemes and programmes.Central Assistance (CA) for restoration of water bodies is provided under the scheme of Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies, which is a component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY).  The Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies Scheme aims at restoration of irrigation potential by improvement and renovation of water bodies along with other multiple objectives such as enhancing the tank storage capacity, ground water recharge, increased availability of drinking water, improvement of catchment of tank commands etc.Under the RRR of Water Bodies scheme, a total of 2064 water bodies with an estimated cost of Rs.1765 crore have been taken up for restoration in various States since XII Plan onwards, out of which works of 1160 water bodies have been reported to be completed. Total storage revival target for these schemes is 908 million cubic metre (MCM) and out of which, 500MCM has been revived up to March, 2019 as reported by the States so far. Total Central Assistance amounting to Rs.369.11 crore has been released to the various States up to March, 2019 for completion of works of these water bodies. No proposal under the RRR of Water Bodies scheme has been submitted by the state of Uttar Pradesh during last three years.
