                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Implementation of NHM in Rajasthan




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of programmes and health schemes being implemented in Rajasthan under the National Health Mission (NHM) along with the names of the programmes and schemes for which the Government has allocated funds during the last three years and the details thereof;(b) the percentage share of the funds contributed by the Union and the State Governments under the National Health Mission; and(c) whether the right to change eligibility of personnels to be recruited/appointed under the NHM lies with the Nodal Officer of the concerned State Government or Mission Director of the State, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	As per the information received from the State Government of Rajasthan, the details of programmes and health schemes implemented in Rajasthan under the National Health Mission (NHM) during 2016-17 to 2018-19 are at Annexure-I to Annexure-III. The funds allocated to State of Rajasthan in this regard is as below:Financial Year	Funds Allocation/Released/Contribution from GoI & State Govt. (in Lakhs)2016-17	185585.962017-18	229704.682018-19 (Unaudited)	243650.98(b)	 The Centre-State funding pattern is 60:40 for all the States w.e.f. FY 2015-16, except the North-Eastern States and other hilly States viz. Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand, for which the Centre-State funding pattern is 90:10. In the case of UTs the entire NUHM programme is fully funded by Central Government.(c)	“Public Health & Hospital” being a State subject, implementation of NHM including recruitment/ appointment of personnels is the responsibility of respective State Governments.
