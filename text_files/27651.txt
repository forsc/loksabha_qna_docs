                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Welfare Schemes for Musicians




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the schemes and welfare provisions by which the Ministry has supported the musical talent of musicians in the Carnatic, Hindustani and Western traditions in India during the past seven years;(b)	whether musicians in the Western tradition from Andhra Pradesh and the North Eastern States have represented India abroad in international cultural events and competitions; (c)	if so, the details of these musical performances including the date and location of the parent events; and(d)	the State-wise and genre-wise number of musicians who performed abroad representing India during each of the last seven years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH-EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d)     Ministry of Culture administers various schemes under the umbrella schemes of  (i) Scheme of Scholarship and Fellowship for Promotion of Art and Culture (ii) and Scheme of Financial Assistance for Promotion of Art and Culture etc. under which financial support in the form of grant such as Repertory Grant, Cultural Function & Production Grant, Grant to Organisations with National Presence and Scholarship / Fellowship is being provided to support all categories of artists including musicians in different cultural fields as well as categories of music including Carnatic, Hindustani etc. during the past seven years and beyond. Further, the Ministry has supported the musical talent of musicians under the Scheme ‘Festival of India Abroad’. Festival of India is organized in other countries and the musicians are supported for their participation by way of providing for travel/stay/transportation etc. & also performance fee in accordance with the Guidelines of the scheme.
