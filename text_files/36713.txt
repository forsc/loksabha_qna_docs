                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3575






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






NSSH/Public Procurement Policy




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of percentage of procurement from SC/ST enterprises under Public Procurement Policy by the Ministries, Departments and CPSEs during the last five Financial Years including the current Financial Year under National Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe Hub (NSSH); and(b) 	the details of total plan outlay and the funds allocated and spent in this regard during the said period, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA)(a):	The details of percentage of procurement from SC/ST enterprises under Public Procurement Policy by the Ministries, Departments and CPSEs during the last five financial years including the current financial year under National Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe Hub (NSSH), as per the Sambandh Portal, are given in the table below. <pre>S. No.	Financial Year	Percentage of Procurement from MSEs owned by SC/ST (%)1.	2016-17	0.24%2.	2017-18	0.48%3.	2018-19	0.54%4.	2019-20	0.53%5.	2020-21	0.52%6.	2021-22(As on 17.03.2022)	0.72%</pre> (b):	 The year-wise details of funds allocated and utilized under National SC-ST Hub scheme are mentioned below:<pre>F.Y.	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22BE(Rs.
