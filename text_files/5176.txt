                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Agricultural Tourism Projects




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any agricultural tourism projects are going on in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to initiate a special project and package for agriculture tourism in Thrissur, Kerala where the agricultural university, Kerala Forest Research Institute and many unique kole wetlands are present; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to send a team to study about the possibility of such projects; and (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Identification and development of tourist sites/destinations/centres is primarily the responsibility of the concerned State Government/Union Territory Administration. The Department of Tourism, Government of Kerala is implementing ‘Green Farm Kerala’ Scheme for promotion farm tourism. This scheme aims for preparing the farms/plantations in the State to receive tourists. Ministry of Tourism on its part has instituted a National Tourism Award in the category of ‘Best Rural/Agri/Plantation Tourism Projects’ to motivate the stakeholders in the tourism sector for implementing such projects.(d):	No, Sir.(e):	Does not arise.********
