                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3671






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Industries Parks




Narayana Swamy Abbaiah














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any industrial parks for mineral based industries are under consideration of the Government; (b) 	if so, the details and the status thereof;(c) 	whether any such industrial parks are also under consideration for Chitradurga and Tumkuru districts in Karnataka; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):	No, Sir. No such proposal for industrial parks for mineral based industries are under consideration in the Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade (DPIIT). (b) to (d):	Do not arise.*****
