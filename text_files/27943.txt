                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Empowerment of Panchayats




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds provided to the panchayats for the development of villages in the State of Bihar including Jehanabad, Arwal and Gaya districts during the last three years;(b)	whether the Government proposes to set up a separate fund for the empowerment of Panchayats;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the progress achieved in this regard, so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL)(a) An amount of Rs.14,892.41 crore was released to the State of Bihar for its Rural Local Bodies, including the districts of Jehanabad, Arwal and Gaya under the Fourteenth and Fifteenth Finance Commissions during 2018-19 to 2020-21. Under the Incentivisation of Panchayat Scheme of the Ministry of Panchayati Raj (MoPR), an amount of Rs.3.44 crore was released to the State of Bihar as incentives in the form of award money for the Panchayats including Panchayats in districts of Jehanabad, Arwal and Gaya doing good developmental works selected under various categories of awards under the National Panchayat Award during the last three years. (b) No, sir. (c) & (d) Questions do not arise in view of (b) above.***
