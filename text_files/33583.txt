                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1005






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






GSB and Roller under MGNREGS




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the works for which provisions are made to be undertaken by the Government under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS) and the rules for granting approval to such works;(b) 	whether there is a provision of constructing gravel road (remote village road) under MGNREGS, if so, the provisions regarding its approval and whether Granular Sub-Base (GSB) and roller are allowed therein, if so, the details thereof along with those responsible for its quality;(c) 	the number of approved remote roads in Seoni district of Balaghat Parliamentary Constituency of Madhya Pradesh in the last three years along with the name of the work and funds approved for them;(d) 	whether GSB and rollers have been used in it and if not whether there is any provision to take action against such construction companies; and(e) 	if so, the action likely to be taken thereon along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE)(a) & (b): Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment scheme with bottom up planning. The works are identified, approved and prioritized out of shelf of work by the Gram Panchayat, recommended by Gram Sabha in each State/UT as per the provision of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA). Accordingly, the works are approved and taken up by Panchayat as per the demand. The planning of works is done as per the estimated person days to be generated for the work in a financial year at the time of assessment of labour budget.    There are 262 permissible works under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA, which includes construction of gravel road. Granular Sub-Base (GSB) work and compaction by roller are allowed for construction of gravel roads under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. The Programme Implementing agency (PIA) is responsible for the quality of the work. (c) & (d):     As per the information shared by the Government of Madhya Pradesh, the details of gravel road works taken up in Seoni district of Madhya Pradesh in last three financial years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 along with sanctioned amount is given at Annexure. No contractors are permitted to be engaged in construction of works under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS.Granular Sub-Base (GSB) work and road compaction by roller are done in the construction of gravel roads as per the provision of the Scheme. There is no issue reported for any specific gravel road for not doing GSB work and compaction by roller. (e)   Does not arise.*****
