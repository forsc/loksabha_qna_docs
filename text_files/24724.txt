                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

642






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Construction of NHs in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of National and State Highways in Jharkhand;(b) the details of State roads that have been declared as National Highways (NHs) in the State during the last three years; (c) whether the Government has provided funds for development of these new NHs; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  There are 31 National Highways & 18 State Highways in Jharkhand;(b)  The details of State roads that have been declared as National Highways in the State during the last three years are given as under:Year	Declared National Highways (NH)2018-19	NH-333A, NH-320G, NH-143AG, NH-320D, NH-218, NH-143B, NH-143H and NH-143D2019-20	Nil2020-21	NH-320D (c) & (d) The government has provided funds for following works:Sl No.	NH No.	Work	Amount (in Cr)1.	143B	Widening and Strengthening of road to two lane with earthen shoulders in km 36.100 to km 60.600 (Dumri –Kurud more)	81.592.	218	Strengthening & reconstruction of road from km 10.000 to km 54.000	85.283.	333A	Strengthening/Reconstruction of road from km 30.400 (Sunderpahari) to km 57.450 (Dharampur More)	76.50 *****
