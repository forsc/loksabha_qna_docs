                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4662






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Land Pooling




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has made mendments/proposed new changes to make land pooling mandatory and the  changes are aimed at overcoming hurdles faced in execution of Delhi Development Authority (DDA) scheme ; and (b)	if so, the details  and the  present  status thereof and the  details  of views of various stakeholders taken into  consideration in this  regard ?






ANSWER




                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) & (b) DDA after consultation with various stakeholders, has submitted a proposal to Government, which includes mandatory land pooling to facilitate smooth implementation of land pooling policy in Delhi.                                    ******
