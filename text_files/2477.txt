                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

211






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Kendriya Vidyalayas




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is fact that Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in Mandapam and Ramanathapuram are functioning from rented buildings;(b) 	if so, whether it is proposed to construct new buildings for the KVs in those places; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.****** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 211 RAISED BY SHRI K. NAVASKANI, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT TO BE ANSWERED ON 8TH JULY, 2019 REGARDING KENDRIYA VIDYALAYAS. (a) Kendriya Vidyalaya, Mandapam in Ramanathapuram District is functioning from its permanent building since 1996.  KV, Rameswaram in Ramanathapuram District is functioning from temporary building provided free of cost by the State Government.(b) 	Yes, Sir.  It is proposed to construct new building for the KV at Rameswaram. (c) & (d) Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, which depends upon identification of suitable land, completion of land transfer formalities, submission of drawings/estimates by construction agency, availability of funds and requisite approvals etc. *****
