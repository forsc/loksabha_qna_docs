                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2898






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Development of MSMEs Cluster




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes a new project on Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs);(b)	 whether the Government proposes to establish a new hub or industrial parks for MSMEs;(c)	 whether the Government is considering to develop MSMEs cluster in Maharashtra State; and(d)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b):	Ministry of MSME implements Micro and Small Enterprises – Cluster             Development Programme (MSE-CDP). Under the scheme, financial support is extended to projects of the State Government to create / upgrade Infrastructural facilities in new / existing industrial estates / industrial areas. Under “Scheme of Funds for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI)”, traditional industry artisans are organised in clusters by providing technology support, marketing support & skill development.  (c):	 Ministry of MSME through its schemes namely “Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP)” and “Scheme of Funds for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI)” establishes Common Facility Centers (CFCs) in the clusters for development of Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) across the country including Maharashtra.(d):	Under MSE-CDP and SFURTI, 47 nos. Common Facility Center projects have been approved for development of clusters in Maharashtra, The details of CFCs are as under:<pre>S. No.	Name of the Scheme 	No. of approved projects	No. of Completed/Functional projects	No. of ongoing projects1.	MSE-CDP	22	14	82.	SFURTI	25	10	15	Total	472423</pre>
