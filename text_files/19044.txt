                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

413






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Software Park in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state: (a)  whether the Government is aware that the Software Technology Park at Deoghar in Jharkhand is ready to be operational; (b)  if so, the time by when the Government proposes to make it functional; (c)  whether the Government has made any assessment regarding employment generation from the said park at Deoghar; and (d)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): Yes, Sir, The Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) centre at Deoghar in Jharkhand is operational since February, 2019.  (c) and (d):  Two BPO units have been supported through the STPI Deoghar centre under the India BPO Promotion Scheme (IBPS) of the Government and they are in process of establishing their facilities.******
