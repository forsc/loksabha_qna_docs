                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1091






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Benefits to Pregnant Women




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the steps taken to provide early registration, medical and other benefits to pregnant women;(b)	whether the Government has reviewed the programmes being run for the pregnant women and if so, the outcome thereof; (c)	the State-wise details of the fund allocated for this in the last financial year 2018-19; and(d)	whether the fund allocated under this scheme has been spent as per the guidelines and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The Ministry of Health & Family Welfare is taking following steps to provide early registration, medical and other benefits to pregnant women:(i)	Tracking of eligible couple under RCH portal for early registration of pregnancy.(ii)	Name Based Web enabled Tracking of Pregnant Women to ensure antenatal, intranatal and postnatal care.(iii)	Village Health, Sanitation and Nutrition Days in rural areas as an outreach activity, for provision of maternal and child health services.(iv)	Promotion of institutional deliveries and early registration of pregnancy through Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY).Apart from this, the Ministry of Women and Child Development is implementing the Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) with effect from 01.01.2017. Under PMMVY, cash incentive of ?5,000/- is provided in three installments to eligible Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers (PW&LM) during the period of pregnancy and lactation for the first living child to improve health seeking behaviour amongst them. The first installment of ?1,000/- is provided to the eligible beneficiary for early registration of the pregnancy. At least one Ante-Natal Check-up (ANC) is necessary to avail second installment of ?2,000/-. The third installment of ?2,000/- is provided to the eligible beneficiaries on the registration of the birth of the child and completion of first cycle of BCG, OPV and Hepatitis-B or its equivalent/substitute. Further, under Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY), the pregnant woman is provided facility of institutional delivery as well as monetary benefit.(b)	The PMMVY is reviewed periodically through Video Conferences with States/UTs and thorugh National and Regional level workshops. As outcome of the reviews, the uptake of the scheme has increased and more beneficiaries are brought under the ambit of the scheme.(c)	The State/UT-wise details of funds released under PMMVY during the year 2018-19 are Annexed.
