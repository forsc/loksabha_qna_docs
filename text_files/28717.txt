                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

351






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Promotion of Rural and Indigenous/Tribal Games




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the games identified under the “promotion  of rural and indigenous/tribal games” component of Khelo India;(b) the details and the number of annual national competitions/activities/demonstrations held for rural and indigenous/tribal games since 2018 and the games for which competitions have been held;(c)  the  number  of  NGOs  and  sports  federations/associations  to  whom  financial  support  has  been provided for promotion of these games since 2018 and the amount given, State-wise; and(d) the number of top sports persons identified for these games, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) Mallakhamb, Kalaripayattu, Gatka, Thang-Ta, Yogasana and Silambam have been identified for promotion by this Ministry under the “Promotion of rural and indigenous/tribal games” component of the Khelo India Scheme.(b) Demonstration events for Mallakhamb, Thang-Ta, Kalaripayattu and Gatka were held during the 3rd edition of the Khelo India Youth Games, 2020 at Guwahati, Assam. Further, thirty-six sports competitions/events were organised in 2019-20 by this Ministry as a part of the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat programme to promote indigenous and tribal games in various disciplines, namely, Gatka, Roll Ball, Tug of War, Kalaripayattu, Thang-Ta, Kho-Kho, Mallakhamb, Shooting Ball, Sqay, Kabaddi, etc. in different parts of the country.(c) Four National Sports Federations (NSFs) have been provided financial assistance under the “Promotion of rural and indigenous/tribal games” component of the Khelo India Scheme as per the details tabulated below. State-wise data is not maintained in this regard in this Ministry.<pre>S. No.	Name of the Federation (NSF)	Funds Released (in ? crore)		2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22(up to 30.09.2021)1	Mallakhamb Federation of India	-	2.34	1.47	0.062	Indian Kalaripayyattu Association	-	0.61	0.21	0.503	National Gatka Association	-	0.27	0.23	0.184	Thang-Ta Federation of India	-	0.48	0.30	0.15	Total	-	3.70	2.20	0.89 </pre>(d) A total of 283 athletes have been identified in the disciplines of Mallakhamb, Thang-Ta, Kalaripayattu and Gatka who are being provided Out-of-Pocket Allowance (OPA) under the “Promotion of rural and indigenous/tribal games” component of the Khelo India Scheme. State-wise details of the same are at Annexure.
