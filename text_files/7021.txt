                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






National Awards to Outstanding MSMEs




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Sandhya Ray
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has started giving annual  National Awards to outstanding Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country for outstanding efforts in entrepreneurship;(b) 	 if so, the details thereof and the criteria fixed in this regard, category-wise;(c)  	the number of applications received by the Government from MSMEs 	for this purpose during each of the last three years, category-wise;(d)	 the names of the MSMEs which have been awarded such award during the said period, State/UT-wise and the quantum of funds allocated and spent during the last three years for these Awards; and(e) 	whether there is lack of awareness in MSMEs for such awards and if so, the corrective steps taken/being taken by the Government to spread awareness in MSMEs for such awards?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN  GADKARI)(a): 	Yes, Sir. (b):	The criteria for National MSME Award includes investment, production, turnover, employment, net profit, innovation, quality, export, etc. (c): 	The number of applications received from the MSMEs for National Awards during the last three years is at Annexure -I. (d): 	The list of MSMEs who received national awards during the year 2016-17 is at Annexure-II. The allocation of funds and the amount spent during the financial year 2016-17 to 2018-19 under the budget head of National Awards has been Rs.11.37 Cr. and Rs. 8.11 Cr. respectively. (e): 	The Ministry of MSME creates awareness in respect of the National Awards by way of various awareness programmes organised by its field offices and also through other channels of communication.
