                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

802






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Power Generation in Chhattisgarh




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether separate companies have been set up for generation and distribution of power/electricity in Chhattisgarh;(b) 	if so, the names of companies which have been set up; and(c) 	the names of the persons appointed Chairman of these companies along with the details of the criteria followed in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP( SHRI  R.K. SINGH) (a) to (c) :	Yes, Sir. As per information received from Government of Chhattisgarh, separate companies have been set up for generation and distribution of power in Chhattisgarh. The names of the companies are (i) Chhattisgarh State Power Generation Company Limited; and (ii)	Chhattisgarh State Power Distribution Company Limited.Shri Shailendra Kumar Shukla has been appointed as Chairman of the Chhattisgarh State Power Generation Company Limited and the Chhattisgarh State Power Distribution Company Limited vide State Government Order No. 139/F-1-5/PCApptt./2018/13/1 dated 28.01.2019 and Order No. 143/F-1-5/PCApptt./2018/13/1 dated 28.01.2019 vesting power conferred in Article 77(i) and Article 92 of Article of Association of Chhattisgarh State Power Generation Company Limited and Chhattisgarh State Power Distribution Company Limited respectively.                                                **********
