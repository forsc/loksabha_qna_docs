                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3684






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Budgetary Allocation to Anganwadi Centres




Mahesh Sahoo













                                                    V. Kalanidhi
                                                








                                                    M. Selvaraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of Anganwadi Centres across the country, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu;(b) 	the details of facilities being provided through such centres;(c) 	the details of annual budgetary allocation to Anganwadi Centres during the last five years, state/UT-wise;(d) 	whether the Government conducts audit of all the Centres annually and if so, the details thereof particularly with regard to Odisha;(e) 	whether the Government has any proposal to set up more Anganwadi Centres and for upgradation of existing centres in the country; and(f) 	if so, the details thereof indicating the targets and time line fixed and funds required, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu especially in Nagapattinam district?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) 	State/UT-wise number of Anganwadi Centres across the country including Tamil Nadu is at Annexure-I.(b) 	Under Anganwadi Services Scheme, the package of following six services is provided to the beneficiaries through a network of 13.89 lakh operational Anganwadi Centres across the country:i.	Supplementary Nutrition (SNP)ii.	Pre-school Non-formal Education, iii.	Nutrition & Health Education, iv.	Immunization,v.	Health Check-up, andvi.	Referral ServicesThree of the six services, viz., Immunization, Health check-up and Referral Services are related to health and are provided through NRHM & Public Health Infrastructure.(c) 	State/UT-wise budgetary allocation under Anganwadi Services Scheme during the last 5 years is at Annexure-II.(d) 	The Scheme is implemented by the State Governments/UT Administrations and conducting the audit of Anganwadi Centres rests with the States/UTs, however, implementation and utilization of funds by the States/UTs is monitored by way of obtaining Physical Progress Reports/Utilization Certificates/Statement of Expenditure/Quarterly Progress Progress and also by capturing the data on Poshan Tracker.(e) & (f) 	Anganwadi Services Scheme is a universal scheme and all the 14 lakh Anganwadi Centres have been sanctioned to all States/UTs. Recently Anganwadi Services Scheme has been revised and subsumed under Saksham Anganwadi and Poshan 2.0.
