                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1756






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Financial Assistance to Uttarakhand




Tirath Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the financial assistance provided to Uttarakhand under various Centrally sponsored programmes for labourers during the last three years, year-wise and scheme-wise;(b)	the number of labourers benefited by the said schemes during the last three years, year-wise, district-wise and scheme-wise;(c)	whether the State Government has requested for assistance in their efforts through financial assistance in schemes/programmes being implemented for the labourers; and(d) 	if so, the details of the assistance provided by the Union Government for the said     purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a)	to (d): Ministry of Labour and Employment is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme namely National Career Service (NCS). The NCS project includes establishment of Model Career Centres (MCCs) in employment exchanges and institutions of repute to provide variety of employment related services leveraging the use of technology. The scheme also provides for assistance to States for IT up-gradation and minor refurbishing of Employment Exchanges and for organizing job fairs. The funds are released to States under various components of NCS against the proposals for establishment of Model Career Centres and interlinking of Employment Exchanges. A detail of the financial assistance provided to Uttarakhand is given below.Name of the Project/Scheme	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Upto 31 Jan, 2020National Career Service (NCS) Interlinking, Model Career Centres (MCC)	85.86	10.00	22.94	--The NCS does not deal with labourers. However; NCS provides employment related assistance to jobseekers and employers.*****
