                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1056






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Malpractice in NEET Examination




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of persons arrested from 2018-2021 till date in relation to malpractice in the NEET exam, state-wise, particularly in Tamil Nadu; and (b) the status of investigation in all the cases registered in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b) As informed by National Testing Agency (NTA), the details of Unfair Means (UFM) cases of NEET (UG) 2019, 2020 & 2021 are as follows:Year	No. of UFM Cases 2019	42020	52021	15Result of all such candidates has been cancelled.******
