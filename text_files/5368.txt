                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5005






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Production of Green Vegetables in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the production of green vegetables in Assam; (b) 	whether the farmers sell their produce (vegetables) directly in the market, if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the number of Government cold storages to store the vegetables and the details thereof; and (d) 	whether the Union Government proposes to set up a cold storage in Barpeta Parliamentary Constituency in Assam, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	As per 2nd Advance Estimates (2018-19) of production of Horticulture Crops in the country, the details of production of green Vegetables in Assam are given at Annexure. (b):	The Government of Assam has reported that mostly farmers sell their produce directly in the local Markets and Haats. In some localities, middlemen collect the produce from farm fields in Vegetable growing areas and transport for sale in Wholesale and Town Markets.(c)  & (d):	As reported by the State Government,  presently following three Government Cold Storages are being run (by the Assam State Agricultural Marketing Board) :-Sl. No.	Location	District	Capacity (in Tonnes)1	Singimari	Kamrup	20002	Gossaigaon	Kokrajhar	20003	Chenijan	Jorhat	2000Contd…2/--2-		Besides, 33 Cold Storages, with capacity 1.75 Lakh tonnes, have been sanctioned for Assam upto 31.03.2019,  by the National Horticulture Board (NHB),  under their  sub-scheme of the Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)  implemented by this Department. The component of Cold Storage is  credit linked and back-ended subsidy  (50%  in case of North-East; made available to entrepreneurs, private companies, cooperatives, farmer groups), is inter-alia released only after the project has been  completed and is in operation. The component of Cold Storages is demand driven and powers have been delegated to States for sanction of projects up to Rs. 5.00 crore. This Department does not propose to set up Cold Storages directly but provides financial support to States in the form of subsidy under State Horticulture Mission (SHM) of MIDH, and the NHB.   Entrepreneurs/farmers desiring to setup cold storages in Barpeta can avail the assistance by approaching SHM/NHB.
