                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2860






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Conversion of SH into NH




Manjulata Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to convert the 35 kms stretch of State Highway (SH) from Aradi to Bhadrak as National Highway (NH) and if so, the details thereof along with the time frame set for its implementation; and(b) whether there is any proposal to convert Jamujhadi - Basudevpur- Dhamra road as NH and if so, the details thereof along with the time frame set for its implementation ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)&(b) Basudevpur to Dhamra is part of NH-316A and is entrusted to NHAI. Ministry of Road Transport & Highways keeps on receiving proposals from various State Governments/UTs etc., including from the State Government of Odisha for declaration of State roads as new NHs.  Ministry considers declaration of some of the State roads as new NHs from time to time based on requirement of connectivity, inter-se-priority and availability of funds.*****
