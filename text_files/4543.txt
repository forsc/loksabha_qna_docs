                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4295






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Upgradation of Thiruvalla-Erumely Road




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government has received any representations from certain quarters for the upgradation of Thiruvalla-Erumely Road in Kerala under Central Road Fund (CRF); (b)	if so, the details thereof and the response of the Union Government thereto;(c)	whether the Government of Kerala has recommended this road for upgradation under CRF; and(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Union Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Yes, Sir. (b)	Hon’ble Member of Parliament, Pathanamthitta vide his letter dated 08.08.2018 requested to take up the Upgradation of Thiruvalla-Erumeli Road in Kerala under Central Road Fund (CRF) Scheme. The request was forwarded to the Government of Kerala to consider the proposal under Central Road Fund (CRF) Scheme for Kerala.(c)	No, Sir.(d)	Does not arise.*****
