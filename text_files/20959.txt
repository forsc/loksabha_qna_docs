                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2246






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Cases of Child Labour




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether it is true that the cases of child labour are rampant in certain States and if so, the details of the data of such States; and(b)the details of State/district-wise data on child labour since March 2020?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	Child Labour is an outcome of various socio-economic problems such as poverty, economic backwardness, illiteracy etc. The Child & Adolescent Labour (Prohibition & Regulation) Act, 1986 inter-alia provides for complete prohibition on employment or work  of children below 14 years of age in any occupation or  process and  also prohibits employment of  adolescents (14-18 years)  in hazardous occupation and processes.  As per 2011 Census, the number of main workers in the age group of 5-14 years in the country was 43.53 lakh. The state-wise data is annexed.*                                                  ******
