                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3166






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






C-ATFM




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to open an Air Traffic Flow Management-Central Command Centre (C-ATFM) in New Delhi;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the objectives thereof;(c) the time by which it is likely to be opened and start functioning;(d) whether the Government proposes to open such centre in other parts of the country and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the location identified for the same and the steps taken/being taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has constructed a state-of-the-art Air Traffic Flow Management Central Command Center at Vasant Kunj in New Delhi. The Center functions as the nodal point for monitoring nationwide air traffic flow and managing air traffic demand congestion at major airports and air-spaces across the country. The Central Command Center has been functional since 22.06.2019.;;(d): No, Sir.;;(e): Does not arise.
