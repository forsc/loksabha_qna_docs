                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1734






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Coal Mines




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total numbers of coal mines in Maharashtra especially in Chanadrapur district;(b)	the number of coal mines which were closed due to COVID-19 in Chandrapur district; and(c)	the measures taken by the Government to restart these coal mines?  






ANSWER




MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a):There are 44 working coal mines in Maharashtra. Out of these 20 are located in Chandrapur district.(b):No coal mine in Chandrapur district has been closed due to covid-19.(c): Question does not arise in view of answer to part (b) above.*****
