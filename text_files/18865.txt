                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

277






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Malook Nagar













                                                    Shiromani Ram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the name of the historical cities/places which are proposed to be connected by trains under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme of the Government;(b)	whether the Government proposes to connect the historical cities of Hastinapur and Shravasti with other major cities of the country through rail-links under the said scheme; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):      Railways does not run trains under the Swadesh Darshan scheme of the Government.  However, Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is operating on multiple theme based circuits across the country which connect historical cities/places viz. Ayodhya, Varanasi, Ujjain, Agra, Prayagraj, Baidyanath, Bodhgaya, Rajgir, Nalanda, Nagpur, Indore, Aurangabad, Chitrakoot, Mathura, Vrindavan, Puri,  Gangasagar, Tirupati, Madurai, Rameshwaram, Srirangapatnam, Mysore, Dwarka, Ankaleshwar, Nasik, Jaisalmer, Jodhpur, Bikaner, Jaipur, Udaipur, Ajmer, Khajuraho, Orchha, Sarnath, Kushinagar, Sravasti, Fatehpur Sikri, Nathdwara, Nandigram, Sitamadhi, Hampi, Mumbai, Gokarana, Belur, Jabalpur, Coorg, Thanjavur.(b) & (c):         Do not arise.*****
