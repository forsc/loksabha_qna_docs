                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4274






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Problems of Aqua Farmers




Adala Prabhakara Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING ??????????, ??????? ?? ????? ?????? be pleased to state:(a) 	whether it has come to the notice of the Government that Nellore district in Andhra Pradesh which is known for its aqua and fisheries industry in the country is facing many problems and is finding it difficult to get affordable prices; and(b) 	if so, the details of the steps taken to solve the problems of aqua farmers and the details of incentives which are being offered to them?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	to (b): Andhra Pradesh is one of the leading producer of fish and is known for aquaculture and fisheries industry in the country. Nellore District is one of the major centre of fisheries and aquaculture in the State of Andhra Pradesh like several other Districts. The Government of Andhra Pradesh has reported that there is no such problems noticed in Nellore District. For the development of fisheries and aquaculture in the country including in Andhra Pradesh, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme of Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) with highest ever investment of Rs. 20050 crore with effect from the financial year 2020-21. The Department of Fisheries, Government of India during the financial years 2020-21 and 2021-22 has accorded approval to the fisheries development proposals of Government of Andhra Pradesh at a total cost of Rs. 657.11 crore and released central funds of Rs. 149.63 crore to the Government of Andhra Pradesh for development of fisheries and aquaculture in the State including in Nellore District.    *****
