                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4322






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Financial Condition of Airlines




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details regarding the financial condition of Air India and all the other private operators in the country; (b) the details regarding the passenger traffic and revenues reported during the last five years of each of these airlines, year/airline-wise;(c) the details regarding the Revenue Passenger Kilometres (RPK) and Available Seat Kilometres (ASK) during the last four years; and(d) the details of the steps taken by the Government for improvement of financial condition of airlines in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): As per information made available by the Scheduled Indian Carriers, the statement regarding the financial performance and revenues for last five years is at Annexure - I and the Scheduled Domestic Passengers carried by Indian Airlines for last five years is at Annexure -II.;;(c) As per information made available by the Scheduled Indian Domestic Carriers, Available Seat Kilometers (ASKM) & Revenue Passenger Kilometers (RPKM), for last four years is at Annexure - III.;;(d): The Government constantly responds to the needs and challenges faced by the airline industry from time to time and has taken several measures, including reduction of Central Excise Duty applicable on Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) from 14% to 11% w.e.f. 11th October 2018 and rationalization of GST provisions, with a view to revive the airline industry and ensure the sustainability and health of the industry.
