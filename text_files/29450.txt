                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Disbursement Under PMMY




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the present status of disbursement of loans under the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) in West Bengal;(b) the number and details of beneficiaries under the scheme till April, 2021;(c) whether any specific targets in this regard have been fixed in respect of West Bengal: and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




 FINANCE MINISTER (a) to (d):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.****** STATEMENT REFERRED IN REPLY TO THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *103 TO BE ANSWERED ON 06th DECEMBER, 2021 BY SHRI SAUMITRA KHANREGARDING DISBURSEMENT UNDER PMMY(a) As on 29.10.2021, over 3.17 crore loans amounting to Rs. 1.33 lakh crore have been disbursed in the State of West Bengal under Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY), since inception of the Scheme in April, 2015.(b)    Till  April, 2021 over 29.56 croreloans amounting to Rs. 15.52 lakh crore have been sanctioned under the Scheme.(c) and (d) The Government only allocates annual targets for the amount to be sanctioned by the Member Lending Institutions (MLIs) i.e. Scheduled Commercial Banks (SCBs), Regional Rural Banks (RRBs), Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) and Micro Finance Institutions (MFIs) under PMMY. For the current financial year (FY), target of Rs. 3.00 lakh crore has been fixed for MLIs, under the Scheme.
