                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






PMGSY in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the rural roads, in kilometers, that have beenconstructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram SadakYojana(PMGSY) in Bihar specially in district Gopalganj during the last three years;(b) 	the number of roads proposed to be constructed in 2019-2020 in district Gopalganj of Bihar; and(c) 	the total funds released for rural connectivity project?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The details of rural roads constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Bihar and in the Gopalganj District during the last three years are as below:-Year (s)	   Length of Roads constructed (km) 	           Bihar	           Gopalganj District2016-17       	  6597.905	                  117.7642017-18	          5221.59	                   52.1852018-19	          4225.63	                   48.843 (b):	 The State of Bihar has been given a target length of 4,300 Km  under PMGSY during the financial year 2019-20. As intimated by the Government of Bihar, 3 roads having a balance length of 1.624 Km are targeted for completion  in Gopalganj district during the financial year 2019-20.   (c):	 Since inception till 27th February, 2020, a sum of Rs. 22,337 crore has been released to the State of Bihar as Central Share for implementation of PMGSY .*****
