                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2238






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Activities under Rashtriya Poshan Maah




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)      the details of activities undertaken under the Rashtriya Poshan Maah, 2021 calender in Sangli district of Maharashtra; and (b)  the number of children observed under Rashtriya Poshan Maah Scheme in said district? 






ANSWER




(a) & (b)      The month of September is celebrated as Rashtriya Poshan Maah across the country. The Rashtriya Poshan Maah 2021 witnessed wide participation and enthusiasm from convergent Ministries, States/UTs and field functionaries. It also witnessed wide participation from , community groups, Panchayati Raj Institutions , staff at District and Block level.The month long celebrations were focused on the following four themes:i.	Plantation activity on “Poshan Vatika”ii.	Yoga and AYUSH for Nutritioniii.	Distribution of ‘Regional Nutrition Kit’ to Anganwadi beneficiariesiv.	Identification of SAM children and distribution of nutritious food  The major activities undertaken during Poshan Maah in Maharashtra included –Growth Monitoring and identification of SAM/MAM children, Hygiene, Water & Sanitation related activities, Anemia camps, Poshan ke Panch Sutras (First 1000 days), Plantation drives and development of Poshan Vatika, Distribution of Regional Nutrition Kit.About 1,89,090 children under 6 years of age participated in various activities during the Rashtriya Poshan Maah 2021 in Sangli District of Maharashtra.*****
