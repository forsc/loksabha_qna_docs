                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4965






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Welfare Schemes for SCs




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the schemes of the Ministry being implemented in Rajasthan; (b) the funds allocated for the said schemes during the last three years and the current year; (c) the status of implementation of schemes in Dausa district of Rajasthan; and (d) the number of scholarships allotted for students belonging to Scheduled Castes in the Dausa district along with the number of children who have benefited from it?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b):  The schemes being implemented by the Ministry in Rajasthan for welfare of Scheduled Castes (SCs) and expenditure made thereon during last three years and the current year, is given in Annexure.(c): Under the scheme for Grant in Aid to Voluntary and other organisations working for Scheduled Castes, two projects are currently running in the Dausa district. National Safai Karamcharis Finance and Development Corporation has disbursed Rs.2.20 lakhs during the last three years for covering 3 beneficiaries in Dausa district of Rajasthan.  National Schedule Castes Finance and Development Corporation has released Rs.94.25 lakhs for 107 beneficiaries during last three years and current year (as on 30.06.2019).As regards other schemes, it is stated that district wise-data is not maintained in the Ministry.(d):  The Central Assistance is released to State Govt. Further release of funds to districts authorities for releasing due scholarships to the eligible beneficiaries are made by the concerned State Govt. and district wise beneficiary details are not maintained by the Central Government.****
