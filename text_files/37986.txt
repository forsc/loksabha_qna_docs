                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Fake Sites of Education Department




Shankar Lalwani













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the cases of fraud in the name of providing employment through some fake sites of the Education Department have come to the notice of the Government; (b) if so, the action taken by the Government thereon; (c) whether the Government has identified anyone in this regard; (d) if so, the action taken against those; and (e) the steps taken/likely to be taken by the Government to prevent repetition of the said cases in future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (e): As and when any complaint is received in the Department, the same is forwarded to the concerned authorities for appropriate action. Also, press notes are circulated for creating awareness among general public to be aware of such fraudulent website. ******
