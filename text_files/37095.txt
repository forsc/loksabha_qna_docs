                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4214






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






100 Days Work under MGNREGS in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether 100 days work has been provided to all the registered workers under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), if so, the State-wise details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) 	the number of workers who got 100 days’’ work under MGNREGS during the last three years, State-wise including Assam; and(c) 	the number of workers who have been provided work under MGNREGS in the districts of  Barpeta and Bongaigaon since 2019 to till date, year, State and district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b)   Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme which provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. It provides livelihood security, i.e. fall back option for livelihood for the rural households, when no better employment opportunity is available.  State/UT-wise (including Assam) number of households completed 100 days of employment under the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in the financial years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 is given at Annexure-I. (c)   District-wise (including Barpeta and Bongaigaon districts) number of workers availed employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in the financial year 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 and current financial year 2021-22 (as on 24.03.2022) is given at Annexure-II. *****
