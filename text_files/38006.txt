                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Coaching, Vocational Guidance and Training




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has the details of people availing Coaching, Vocational Guidance & Training under National Career Service Centres for SC/ST job Seekers and if so, the details State-wise details thereof;(b) whether the Government has any plan to start any new National Career Service Centre in the State of Kerala in districts with a considerable SC/ST Population;(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) whether the Government will start a new National Career Service Centre in Idukki, which hosts a considerable SC/ST population and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a): State wise number of beneficiaries provided Coaching/ Vocational Guidance/ Training under the National Career Service Centres for SC/ST is at Annexure.(b) to (d): National Career Service Centres (NCSC) for SC/ST  has already been established at Trivandrum in Kerala State. At present, 25 States/UTs have been covered under the network of NCSC for SC/ST with one centre in each of these States/UTs.	******
