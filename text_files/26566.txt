                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2464






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Exclusive Subsidy Policy




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved any exclusive subsidy policy for urea fertilizer produced through coal-gasification under joint ventures between GAIL India Ltd., Coal India Ltd, Rashtriya Chemicals and Fertilizers Ltd (RCFL) and Fertilizer Corporation of India (FCIL) among others, if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether some fertilizer companies are reviving their plans by setting up a new greenfield urea plants in some States by increasing the installed capacities of million tonnes per annum and also estimated project costs of the urea projects and set targets, if so the details thereof and progress made, State wise including the State of Telangana also;(c)	the amount of funds sanctioned/ spent for each project in the last 5 years and current year as on date, state wise; and(d)	the steps being taken for specific subsidy to promote this innovative technology of coal-gasification for the first time in the country as on date and results achieved, along with funds sanctioned/ spent, State wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(MANSUKH LAL MANDAVIYA)(a):	Yes, Sir. On 28th April, 2021, the Government of India has notified an ‘Exclusive Subsidy Policy for Urea produced through coal gasification route by Talcher Fertilizer Limited (TFL)’, which envisages that the concession rate/subsidy for the urea produced through coal gasification route by Talcher Fertilizers Ltd (TFL) for a period of 8 years from the date of start of production will be determined by providing 12% Post Tax IRR on equity. (b):	 Yes, Sir. The Government of India has decided to revive Ramagundam, Gorakhpur, Sindri and Talcher Units of Fertilizer Corporation of India Ltd. (FCIL) and Barauni Unit of Hindustan Fertilizer Corporation Ltd.
