                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4312






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Promotion of AYUSH




 Kunhalikutty P.K.














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the funds allocated for promotion and development of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, Homoeopathy, and Naturopathy, separately during the last three years and the current year; (b) the details of funds spent separately, year-wise and State/UT-wise especially in Kerala; (c) the details of the amount that remained unutilized, system-wise; and (d) the details of complaints regarding inadequacy of funds and the manner in which the same is addressed?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (d):      Government of India is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) for promotion and development of Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy (AYUSH) in the country. The details of funds allocated/released, spent and unutilized, year-wise and State/UT-wise including Kerala is furnished at Annexure. However, no system-wise funds are released by Central Government.             The funds allocated for promotion of AYUSH systems of medicine under the Central Sector Scheme for Promotion of Information Education and Communication (IEC) in AYUSH during the last three years and the current financial year is as follows:Year	Actual Expenditure/Allocation (Rs. in Crore)2016-17	24.662017-18	31.072018-19	63.692019-20	52.60Under IEC Scheme, the funds are not allocated to States/UTs separately.             Further, request has been received from State/UT Governments time to time seeking more financial assistance through Supplementary State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). As per the expenditure/utilization reported by State/UT Governments and as per the actual requirements posed by them, Central Government has sanctioned the available funds to State/UT Governments.
