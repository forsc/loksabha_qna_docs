                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1190






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Skill Training to Weavers




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government formulated made any scheme to impart training to the weavers under modern skill training; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the skill training imparted to the weavers during the last few years; State-wise; (d) the number of institutes with which the contract has been signed for skill training; and (e) the details of the number of weavers who were imparted skill training in Haryana State?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (e):	Ministry of Textiles is implementing an umbrella skilling scheme viz. Samarth- Scheme for Capacity Building in Textile Sector (Samarth) for addressing the skilled manpower requirement in various textile segments including upskilling of weavers. Need-based skill upgradation programmes for handloom workers in technical areas viz. weaving, dyeing, designing, etc. conducted earlier under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) and Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) are now conducted under Samarth. State-wise including Haryana details of skill training imparted to the handloom workers during last three years (2018-19 to 2020-21) is given at Annexure-I. Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) to impart skill development training to prospective youth of the country including weavers. Presently, the third phase of PMKVY i.e. PMKVY 3.0 is being implemented. State wise including Haryana details of candidates trained under PMKVY 2.0 and PMKVY 3.0 schemes of Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) in weaver related job roles is given in Annexure II.Since 2019, Ministry of Textiles has partnered with 13 State Government Agencies, 92 Textile Industry, 10 Industry Associations/ Export Promotion Councils and 4 Sectoral Organizations of Ministry of Textiles for skill development. Under PMKVY 3.0, District Skill Committees have been entrusted with the task of identifying local demand, based on which training is arranged in the district.
