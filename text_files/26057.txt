                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Establishment of Sports Academies




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of sports academies established by the Government, State-wise;(b) whether the Government purposes to establish new sports academy in Uttar Pradesh;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) Under the “Support to National/Regional/State Sports Academies” vertical of the Khelo India Scheme, sports academies are accreditated for the training of the Khelo India Athletes. A total of 236 academies have been accredited in the country so far. State/UT-wise list of number of academies supported under the Khelo India Scheme is at Annexure.(b) to (d) Accreditation of academies is an ongoing process and sports academies are accreditated upon receiving expressions of interest from the State/Union Territory governments after following the due process under the Khelo India Scheme. 09 sports academies have so far been accreditated in the State of Uttar Pradesh. *****
