                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2121






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Violence Against Women




Dushyant Singh













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether there is in an increase in number of cases of domestic violence against women reported between March 22nd 2020 and July 31st 2020 as compared to previous months of the same year; (b)	if so, the details of the cases registered on the basis of said reports along with the number of cases registered during the previous six years, State-wise; (c)	the number and the date of cases registered for domestic violence and the date of disposal of such cases prior to March 22nd 2020, State-wise; (d)	the quantum of funds allocated/ utilized under Mahila police Volunteer scheme, States-wise; (e)	the number of One Stop Centres established, State-wise; and (f)	the total number of beneficiaries under the scheme and their distribution based on the type of assistance given to them?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (f) : National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes information on crimes in its publication “Crime in India”. The published reports are available till the year 2018. State-wise and year-wise total number of cases of domestic violence during the  previous six years is available on the website of National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), https://ncrb.gov.in.             From the beginning of lockdown due to Covid 19 pandemic,  the National Commission for Women (NCW) launched an Ad campaign through electronic media and social media inviting women who have suffered any kind of violence to come forward and report it. Further, in addition to handling complaints received through regular modes, NCW has also launched a WhatsApp number 7217735372 on 10.04.2020 for reporting domestic violence cases. The additional modes provided by NCW facilitated reporting of cases, including from women who had been experiencing violence at home for past several years. The complaints received by NCW are acted upon by coordinating with victims, police and other authorities to provide necessary assistance. Further under the One Stop Centre and Women Helpline Schemes of this Ministry more than 54 lakh violence affected women and women in distress have been assisted since launch of these schemes in 2015.
