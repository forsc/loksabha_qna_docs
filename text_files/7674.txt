                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Setting up of e-Assessment Centres




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the criterion adopted by the Government in selecting the location for the setting up of 8 regional e-assessment centres;(b)	whether it is true that two such centres headed by Principal Commissioner of Income Tax are to be established in Hyderabad and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any monetary or human resource were transferred from Andhra Pradesh to set up the said centres in Hyderabad, Telangana; and (d)	if so, the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	The eight Regional e-assessment centres (ReAC) were set up on pilot basis in eight metro cities.  These metro cities were identified on the basis of availability of infrastructure and resources. (b)	Yes, 2 Principal Commissioners of Income-tax (ReAC) (PrCIT ReAC) have been established in Hyderabad vide notification no.77/2019 F No.187/7/2019-ITA-I dated 03.10.2019 and their details are as under: -(i)	Principal Commissioner of Income-tax (ReAC) Hyderabad-I &	(ii)	Principal Commissioner of Income-tax (ReAC) Hyderabad-II(c)	& (d) To cater to the requirements of newly established PrCIT(ReAC) at Hyderabad, suitable resources were transferred from Principal Chief Commissioner of Income-tax, Andhra and Telangana.  This was done with the purpose of ensuring the smooth functioning of the ReAC and equitable distribution of available resources. *****
