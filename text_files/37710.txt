                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4677






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Polyol Manufacturing Unit




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the decision to discontinue the polyol manufacturing unit is still under review/consideration and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) the details of expenditure in connection with the polyols plant at Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (BPCL)Kochi along with expenditure on the Detailed Project Report (DPR) preparation, investigations, studies, and inaugural function;(c) whether the decision of BPCL director board to scrap the polyols plant is a wrong move considering the demand of its consumption and imports to our country and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has any data on the import of polyols and if so, the details thereof for the last six years, year, importer and importing country-wise;(e) whether the Government proposes to make India a self-reliant country in this sector and if so, the details thereof; and(f) whether the Government proposes to narrow the big gap of production and consumption of polyols and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)to (c)Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (BPCL) has discontinued the Polyols project at their Kochi Refinery due to substantial increase in the project cost, making it commercially unviable. The total expenditure till date is approximately Rs.425.9 Crore including site development and DPR/ Project Management Consultancy (PMC) / Licensor.  (d)  Yes, the details of imports of Polyols from 2015-16 to 2020-21 is as under:Year	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Quantity(in Metric Tonnes)	186239	175766	218541	248491	239755	169499The details of import for the last six years and importing country-wise are attached at Annexure.(e) and (f) In order to promote domestic manufacturing capacity of Polyols, Government has reduced the import duty on Propylene Oxide from 7.5% to 5.5% in the year 2018-19. Propylene Oxide is the principal raw material for the production of Polyols.**********
