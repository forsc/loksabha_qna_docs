                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

579






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






New Passport Seva Kendras




Rajan Baburao  Vichare













                                                    Manoj Rajoria
                                                








                                                    M.K. Vishnu Prasad
                                                








                                                    Sarmistha Sethi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number and details of Passport Seva Kendras (PSKs) functioning in the country, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra;(b)  the number of persons who availed services of these centers during the current year along with the passports issued/renewed during the last three years, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the demand for passport is rising in the country and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether the Government has any proposal to open more number of PSK in the country and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra and Odisha?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (d)	As on date, there are 521 Passport Kendras (93 Passport Seva Kendras and 428 Post Office Passport Seva Kendras) are operational in the country. In Maharashtra, 46 Passport Kendras (8 Passport Seva Kendras and 38 Post Office Passport Seva Kendras) are operational. State wise list of Passport Seva Kendras, Post Office Passport Seva Kendras and list of Passport Kendras in Maharashtra are attached at Annexure I, II & III respectively.The number of persons who availed services of these centers during the current year along with the Passports issued /renewed during last three years State/UT-wise is attached at Annexure-IV.The demand for Passports has been rising. However, due to COVID-19 Pandemic in the year 2020-21, passports applications witnessed a decrease.Opening of PSK is an ongoing activity and depends on various factors including distance from the Passport Kendras and volume of applications from a particular region.***
