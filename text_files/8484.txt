                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

198






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






AMS Laboratories




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Accelerator Mass Spectrometry (AMS) laboratory is essential to find the date of an object found in an archaeological excavation and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the number of AMS Laboratories functioning in the country;(c)	Whether the Archaeological Survey has any plan to set up an AMS laboratory in Tamil Nadu in collaboration with UGC as it is an archaeologically rich State; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(Prahlad Singh Patel)(a) to (d):A statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY PART (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *198 FOR 02.12.2019(a) AMS laboratory is one amongst the instrumentation techniques to date an organic object.  (b) In India, the AMS labs are functioning at the following places:  i.      Physical Research Laboratory, Ahmadabad ii.	Inter-University Accelerator Center, New Delhi(c)  & (d)	No, Sir.
