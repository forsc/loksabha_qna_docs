                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4681






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Price of Petroleum Products




K. Muraleedharan













                                                    Chandeshwar Prasad
                                                








                                                    P. Velusamy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the use of petrol/diesel is increasing world wide including in India, if so, the details of cost/increase in the cost of petroleum products/crude oil in domestic/ international market along with the import cost in India during the last three years and the current year, year-wise and petrol/ diesel-wise;(b) whether the Government has taken/ proposes to take any steps to control the increased price of the petroleum products and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the petroleum marketing companies are incurring losses on sale of petroleum products in the country and if so, the details thereof, company-wise; and(d) whether the Government has any scheme to mitigate the effect of costlier petroleum products on the common man and inflation and if so, the details thereof?
