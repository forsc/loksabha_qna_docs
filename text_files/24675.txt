                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Preservation of Step Wells




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has formulated any scheme for the preservation of step-wells in the country; and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI AND TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) & (b)  This Department do not have any specific scheme for preservation of step-wells in the country. However preservation of Step wells can be taken up as per extant guidelines under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) which permits activities like renovation of traditional water bodies including desilting of irrigation tanks and other water bodies, and conservation of old step wells/baolis etc.  *****
