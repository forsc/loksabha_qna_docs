                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4213






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Agricultural Schemes Executed Through NGOs




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The names of Schemes related to agriculture being run by the Government through Non-government Organisations (NGOs) at present;(b)	The number of beneficiaries of the said schemes in the country; and(c)	The details of training programmes being run by the Government through NGOs in the country specifically in Chhattisgarh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	Normally, assistance to Non – Governmental Organizations (NGOs) associated with agriculture sector is not provided directly by the Department of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare.  The States are free to provide such assistance under some schemes and programmes. 	The Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) Scheme of ICAR is being implemented by different organizations including NGOs. At present, 103 KVKs are under NGOs. These KVKs served 1.08 crore farmers through their different activities during the last one year. These KVKs conducted 10031 training programmes on agriculture and allied sectors benefitting 2.97 lakh farmers. None of the KVKs of Chhattisgarh are under NGOs.	Model Cluster Demonstration – A Sub Scheme under the Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) scheme is being run by the Government through Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) at present.  State-wise details of number of beneficiaries under Model Cluster Demonstration-a sub scheme under Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) scheme are at Annexure.  No training programme is being run by the Ministry of Agriculture through NGOs in the state of Chhattisgarh under Model Cluster Demonstration Further, under National Horticulture Mission (a sub-scheme of MIDH), an NGO has been declared as National Level Agency
