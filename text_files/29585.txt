                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1324






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Environment Clearance for Contruction of an Underpass




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has issued necessary environment clearance for constructing an underpass by the State Government with the support of KIIFB at Koduvally town in Kozhikode-Kollegal NH 766;(b)	whether the Government has observed increasing man-animal conflicts in the State of Kerala, if so, the details regarding the number of such incidents reported, district wise;(c)	whether any compensation is available for mananimal conflict cases, if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government has observed that a large number of crops were being destroyed due to animal attacks in Kerala; and(e)	if so, the details regarding the same, district-wise and the amount of compensation granted for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) As per EIA notification, 2006, as amended from time to time, all new projects or activities listed in the Schedule of this Notification, to be undertaken in any part of the country either by the private or Central/State Government agencies require prior environmental clearance. ‘Construction of underpass’ as a standalone project/activityis not listed in the schedule of theaforesaid Notification and therefore no environmental clearance is required as per the aforesaid Notification from the Central Government for such an activity. Further, no application seeking Environmental Clearance for construction of underpass in question or for any stretch of Kozhikode-Kollegal NH 766has been received in the Ministry nor has any Environmental Clearancebeen issued in this regard.(b) and (c) The management of forests and wildlife is primarily the responsibility of concerned State/UT Governments and the details of man-animal conflicts in the country are not collated in the Ministry. However, as per the information provided by the State Government of Kerala, district wise details of number of incidences (Corp damage, death injury, house damage, property damage) and compensation paid in the state of Kerala due to wildlife attack from 01.01.2018 to 30.11.2021 is enclosed as Annexure - IIn Kerala, compensation is being paid to victims of wildlife attack as per the “The Kerala Rules for Payment of Compensation to victims of attack by Wild Life, 1980”. The present rate of compensation being given for the loss of crop/loss of human life due to wildlife attack are as follows:Sl. No.	Details of loss of life and property/injury	Amount of compensationa.	Death 	Rs. 10,00,000/-b.	Death due to snake bite outside forest	Rs. 2,00,000/-c.	Injury 	Maximum Rs. 1,00,000/- (people belonging to SC/ST community will be provided full expense for the treatment based on medical certificate of a Medical Officer.)d.	Loss of property/crops	100% of the loss (limited to 1,00,000/-)(d) and (e) The State Government of Kerala has reported that damage to agriculture crops in the State is caused by wildlife attack. The district wise details for compensation paid against crop loss due to wildlife attack, for the period from 01.01.2018 to 30.11.2021 is enclosed as Annexure – II.*****
