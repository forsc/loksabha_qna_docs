                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3681






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Release of Payment




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                     Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that NKC Projects Private Limited company obtained big contract tender of national highways from the Government and entrusted work to many small companies including Jam-Jam Company Private Limited after receiving full payment from the Government but payment is not being made to these small companies which is causing hardships to the officials/workers of these small companies;(b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto;(c)  	the action  being  taken/ by the Government against such companies like NKC Projects Private Limited; and(d) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to ensure that the funds released to such  companies reach to the intended eligible users? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d)	This is a dispute related to non-settlement of dues between M/s NKC Projects Private Limited & its contractors/small companies. NHAI has no role regarding release of payment to such small companies engaged by M/s NKC Projects Private Limited. M/s Jam-Jam Construction Company can approach the appropriate forum for the redressal of grievance/settlement of pending claims in accordance with the provisions of law.*****
