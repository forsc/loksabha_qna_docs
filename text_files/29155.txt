                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

751






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Drinking water connection in Assam and Odisha




Abdul Khaleque













                                                    Mahesh Sahoo
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the number of households provided with drinking water connection in Assam and Odisha under Jal Jeevan Mission, district-wise;(b)	the details of the amount of fund sanctioned, released and utilized under the programme so far, State-wise; and(c)	the details of number of households in rural Assam that has been provided with water connection under the scheme particularly in the districts of Barpeta and Bongaigaon in Assam and Angul and Dhankanal in Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) and (c)	Government of India, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in partnership with States to make provision of potable tap water supply to every rural household by 2024. The district-wise number of rural households provided with potable tap water supply as on 28.11.2021 including in Barpeta & Bongaigaon in Assam, and Angul & Dhenkanal in Odisha is in public domain on JJM dashboard, which is available on:https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/jjmindia.aspx(b)The State-wise details of the allocation, funds drawn by these States and reported expenditure under Jal Jeevan Mission are as under:							            (Amount in Rs.
