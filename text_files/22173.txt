                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3312






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






PMGSY in Bihar




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the roads constructed in the districts of various States including Bihar, under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), phase-wise;(b) 	the amount sanctioned, spent/unspent under PMGSY during the last three years, particularly in the districts of Bihar;(c) 	the number of roads and length thereof, proposed for construction in Bihar under PMGSY, district-wise; and(d) 	the status of work done under PMGSY in the district of Saran including the amount sanctioned, released and timeline and progress of completion?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Rural Roads is a State subject and Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) is a one-time special intervention to provide rural connectivity, by way of a single all-weather road, to the eligible unconnected habitations in the core network. Since inception to till date (11.3.2021), the details of the roads constructed in the districts of various States/UTs including Bihar under various verticals are as under:Verticals	Roads constructed (Nos.)
