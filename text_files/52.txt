                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

12






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Medicinal Plants and Herbs




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the total quantity of medicinal plants and herbs grown in the country, State/UT-wise during the last three years; (b) the details of the financial assistance provided for encouraging farming of medicinal plants and herbs during the last three years; and (c) the total quantum of herbs exported from the various States during the said period, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):	Presently, the Ministry of AYUSH is implementing a “Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM)” under which there is a component on ‘Medicinal Plants’ which primarily aimed at supporting cultivation of medicinal plants on farmer’s land with backward linkages through establishment of nurseries for supply of quality planting material and forward linkages for post-harvest management, processing, marketing infrastructure etc. The cultivation of medicinal plants is supported by providing subsidy @ 30%, 50% and 75% of cost of cultivation to farmers through the identified implementing agency of concerned states throughout the country. The State/UT-wise detail of the medicinal plants species and area supported for cultivation under NAM scheme during last three years is at Annexure-I. (b):     The State/UT-wise detail of financial assistance provided for encouraging farming of medicinal plants during the last three years is at Annexure-II.(c ):    The data on quantum of herbs exported from various States during the said period is not available.………..
