                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3572






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay Sanchar Kaushal Vikas Pratisthan Scheme




Shantanu Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has set-up any infrastructure under Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay Sanchar Kaushal Vikash Pratisthan Schemes during the last three years and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of the beneficiaries of the said scheme during the same period; and(c) 	whether the Government is planning to implement the said scheme in West-Bengal and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	A pilot scheme to open ‘Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Sanchar Kaushal Vikas Pratisthans’ was launched in 2017 by Department of Telecommunications for skill development training in telecom sector ,  which envisaged to train 10,000 candidates in one year through these 10 centres. The scheme was implemented through Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) using its infrastructure. Ten such centres were opened for the pilot scheme at: 1. Jabalpur (Madhya Pradesh) 2. Mysore (Karnataka) 3. Bhubaneswar (Odisha) 4. Ranchi (Jharkhand) 5. Nagpur (Maharashtra) 6. Rajpura (Haryana) 7. Pune (Maharashtra) 8. Ghazipur (Uttar Pradesh) 9. Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh) 10. Kalyani, Kolkata (West Bengal). The scheme has been completed in 2019-20.(b)	The centre-wise details of beneficiaries (candidates trained) are as below:<pre>Period: 03.07.2018 to 03.11.2019No	Centre	Candidates trained1	Jabalpur (Madhya Pradesh)	13882	Mysore (Karnataka)	10893	Bhubaneswar( Odisha)	16004	Ranchi (Jharkhand)	8035	Nagpur ( Maharashtra)	16316	Rajpura (Haryana)	3807	Pune (Maharashtra)	17608	Ghazipur (Uttar Pradesh)	1259	Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh)	23010	Kalyani, Kolkata (West Bengal)	1021	Total	10027</pre>(c)	One of the ten PDDUSKVP centres  was opened at  Kalyani (West Bengal) which trained/certified 1021 candidates.*****
