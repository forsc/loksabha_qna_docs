                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1355






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Categorisation of Castes




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1355 TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021CATEGORISATION OF CASTES1355.    SHRI KOTHA PRABHAKAR REDDY:              SHRIMATI VANGA GEETHA VISWANATH: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the steps being taken by the Government for categorization of some castes including Scheduled Castes (SCs) in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh; and(b) the progress made along with report submitted in this regard and the action taken thereon, caste-wise during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b):   11 Proposals for OBCs and 1 proposal for SCs from the state of Andhra Pradesh and 88 proposals for   OBCs and 1 proposal for SCs from the state of  Telangana were received.11 castes/sub-castes/ synonyms   of Andhra Pradesh and 87 castes of Telangana have been included in the   Central Lists of OBCs vide Gazette Notification dated 11.08.2016.  Proposal of   1 caste of Telangana has been referred to National Commission for Backward   Classes for advice.Proposal   of Beda (Budaga) Jangam community of Andhra Pradesh for inclusion in the list   of SCs has been referred back to the State Government with a request to   submit a complete proposal along with ethnographic details.The proposal of Are Katika , Katika community of Telangana was processed as   per laid down procedure. As the Office of the Registrar General of India   (ORGI) did not support the same even on a second reference, accordingly the  same has been rejected with the approval of competent authority.*****
