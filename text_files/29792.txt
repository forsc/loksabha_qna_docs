                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1448






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Daily Sahakar Scheme




Vijay Kumar Dubey













                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched the Dairy Sahakar Scheme, if so, the details thereof and the objective behind the move;(b)	the details of amount allocated under the said scheme;(c)	the nodal agency appointed to implement the scheme;(d)	the role of National Cooperative Development Corporation in implementing the scheme; and(e)	the other steps taken by the Government to strengthen the dairy sector in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	to (d)  The National Cooperative  Development Corporation  (NCDC) has framed  Dairy Sahakar  scheme in convergence with schemes of Government of India through NCDC credit linkage.  Under the scheme credit linkage to the tune of Rs.5000 Crore to be provided by them  for  all aspects of dairy business  (e)     	 In order to strengthen the dairy sector in the country, the  Department is      implementing   the following dairy development schemes/ activities:i.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM)ii.	National Programming for Dairy Development (NPDD)iii.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF)iv.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organisations engaged in Dairy sector (SDCFPO)v.	Kisan Credit Card (KCC) for Animal Husbandry      vi   Animal Husbandry and Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF)****
