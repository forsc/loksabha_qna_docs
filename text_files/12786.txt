                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1746






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Apprenticeship Scheme with Stipend




P. Ravindhranath














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has initiated National Apprenticeship Scheme with stipend support and sharing of the cost of basic training to 50 lakh youth by 2020; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the number of beneficiaries under this scheme, State-wise including the state of Tamil Nadu till now;(c)	whether the Government is also providing supplementary fiscal assistance to the States to make it fruitful; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the follow up actions taken by the Government to make it effective?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	and (b) 	The Government of India launched “National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme” (NAPS) in August,2016 with stipend support and sharing of the cost of basic training  with a cumulative target of training 50 lakh youth during  Financial Year 2016-17 to 2019-20. Following are the details of financial support components under NAPS: i.	Stipend support : reimbursement of 25% of prescribed stipend subject to a maximum of Rs 1500/- per month per apprentice with employers. The stipend support would not be given during the basic training period for fresher apprenticesii.	Sharing of  cost of Basic Training: @ Rs7500/- for a maximum of 3 months or 500 hours in respect of  apprentices who come directly to apprenticeship training without any formal trade training. State/UT- wise, the total number of beneficiaries under NAPS including the state of Tamil Nadu till 25th February,2020 is given in Annexure-I.(c) and (d) 	The government is not providing any supplementary fiscal assistance to the States to make NAPS more fruitful.
