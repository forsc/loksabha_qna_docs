                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

398






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Guidelines to Transfer IAS/IPS Cadre




S. Ramalingam













                                                    A. Ganeshamurthi
                                                








                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government is issuing any established guidelines to change/transfer IAS/IPS cadre service from State Pool to Central Pool; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the total number of IAS/IPS cadre service changed/transferred from State Pool to Central Pool for the last three years, State-wise along with the reasons thereof; (d) 	whether concerned State Government has given its approval for such pool transfer; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a):   No, Sir; there is no provision to change/transfer of IAS/IPS Cadre from State pool to Central Pool. Officers of IAS/IPS cadre are appointed/posted at the Centre only on deputation basis (including Central Staffing Scheme).(b) to (e):    Does not arise.*****
