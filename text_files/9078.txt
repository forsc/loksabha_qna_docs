                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2750






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






ROBs/RUBs in Tamil Nadu




Andimuthu Raja













                                                    V. Kalanidhi
                                                








                                                    S. Ramalingam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the number of ROBs and RUBs sanctioned for construction within Tamil Nadu, district-wise during the last three years and the current year; (b) the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilised for their construction during the above mentioned period;(c)  the details of the number of bridges that are sanctioned and are under construction in Tamil Nadu, district-wise?(d)   whether the Government has acquired and handed over the land for all sanctioned bridges within Tamil Nadu;(e)  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor along with the action taken by the Government in this regard;(f)  the time by which these are likely to be acquired and handed over; and(g) whether the Government has fixed any time limit for their construction, if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (g): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. *****
