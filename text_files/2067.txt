                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1870






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Implementation of Bharat Net Project




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Madhya Pradesh State Electronic Development Corporation Limited has the internal capacity for implementation of Bharat Net Project; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	the time by which the project report is likely to be approved; and (d) 	the time by which funds are likely to be provided in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (d)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country, including Madhya Pradesh. The Phase-I of the project in Madhya Pradesh has been assigned to Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) for implementation.For expediting the implementation of the BharatNet project, the Union Cabinet on 19.07.2017 approved a modified strategy for implementing BharatNet. According to the approved modified strategy, the remaining 1,50,000 (approx.) Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country are to be implemented in Phase-II through three models of implementation; viz. State-led model, Private Sector, Central Public Sector Undertaking (CPSU) model.As approved by the Telecom Commission (now known as Digital Communications Commission), the Phase-II of the project is being implemented through BSNL in Madhya Pradesh.
