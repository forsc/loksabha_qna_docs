                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2600






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Development of District Hospitals in Tripura




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether a number of proposals are pending with the Union Government from the State Government of Tripura for the development of district hospitals, sub-divisional hospitals and primary health centres in Tripura; and (b) if so, the present status of such proposals and the details of the steps taken by the Union Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):     No such proposals are pending under the National Health Mission with the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.        Further under National Health Mission (NHM), financial and technical support is provided to the State Governments/UTs to strengthen their health care system including district hospitals, sub-divisional hospitals and primary health centres based on the requirement posed by the States/UTs in their Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs).  In FY 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 51.10 Cr. has been approved for infrastructure strengthening which includes District Hospitals, Sub Divisional Hospitals, and Primary Health Centres for Tripura State, out of total approval of Rs. 302.37 Cr for all programme/scheme of NHM.(b):         Does not arise.…………………..
