                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4845






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Setting up of Artisan Welfare Fund Trust




Sunil Dattatray Tatkare













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the aims and objectives of setting up of Artisan Welfare Fund Trust (AWFT); (b) 	whether the Government has achieved the objectives of setting up AWFT and if so, the details thereof and if not, the corrective steps taken in this regard; (c) 	the contribution made by artisans and khadi institutions towards the trusts; (d) 	the number of AWFT set up by the Khadi and Village Industries Commission in the country, State/UT-wise especially in Andaman and Nicobar Islands; (e) 	the quantum of funds available with the AWFT during each of the last three years and the number of artisans benefitted out of it; and (f) 	whether the Government has made any assessment on the working of AWFT and if so, the outcome thereof and the other steps taken by the Government for the welfare of khadi artisans?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	The aims and objectives of setting up of Artisan Welfare Fund Trust (AWFT) are to create a welfare fund for the benefit of Khadi Artisans and provide security to artisans.(b):	Yes, Sir.  This fund is utilized for assistance to artisans in fields like medical, educational, maternity, construction of house, etc.  As on 31.03.2020, out of 2737 number of registered Khadi Institutions, 2594 Khadi Institutions have taken membership of these trusts. The total deposit with the Trusts was to the tune of Rs. 153.29 crore as on 31.03.2020 and an amount of Rs. 33.30 crore was withdrawn by Artisans for their needs.(c):	As per the AWFT guidelines, artisans contribute 12% of their earning to AWFT with matching contribution by the Khadi Institution to the Trust.(d):	A total of 23 Artisan Welfare Fund Trusts have been set up by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) in the country. State-wise details is annexed.  No Trust has been set up in Andaman and Nicobar Islands as there is no Khadi Institution.(e):	The quantum of funds available with AWFT during the last three years and the number of artisans benefitted are follows:<pre>Year	Funds available (Rs. in crore)	No. of Artisans benefitted (in lakhs)2017-18	116.01	4.632018-19	114.57	4.902019-20	153.29	4.91</pre>(f):	The State Director of KVIC is the Vice-Chairman of the Artisan Welfare Fund Trust in the concerned State wherein the performance of the AWFT is reviewed periodically.  The following are being implemented by the KVIC for the welfare of Khadi artisans, other than the AWFT:i)  Assistance up to 30% of ‘Prime Cost’ is provided to artisans under ‘Modified Market Development Assistance’ (MMDA) scheme.ii) ‘Workshed Scheme for Khadi Artisans’ introduced in 2008-09, provides financial assistance upto to Rs.60000/- to individual Khadi artisan for construction of worksheds for providing healthy working space.
