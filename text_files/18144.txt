                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1953






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Plan to Minimise Traffic Congestion




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has prepared any plan to minimise traffic congestion for optimum capacity utilisation of roads and if so, the details thereof; and (b)	the details of bridges included so far under Indian Bridge Management System (IBMS) State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS [GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)](a) No, Sir,(b) Details of bridges included under Indian Bridge Management System (IBMS) is as annexed.
