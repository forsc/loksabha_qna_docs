                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1827






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Funds under PMAY-U




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds sanctioned, released and utilised under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) during the last five years, year-wise and State/UT-wise; (b) the details of construction of house under the ‘Housing for All’ mission during the last five years, year-wise and State/UT-wise; (c) whether the “Housing for All” mission provided house to all beneficiaries throughout the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                   THE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING & URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): Year-wise and State/ Union Territory (UT)-wisedetails of funds sanctioned, released and utilized under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U)during the last five years are at Annexure-I, Annexure-II and Annexure-III respectively. (b) to (d): Under “Housing for All” Mission, this Ministry is implementing PMAY-U in urban areas whereas the scheme of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) is being implemented by Ministry of Rural Development in rural areas for providing houses to all eligible beneficiaries throughout the country. State/ UT-wise details of total number of houses sanctioned and constructed/completed during the last five years under PMAY-U and PMAY-G are at Annexure-IV and Annexure-V respectively.                                         *****
