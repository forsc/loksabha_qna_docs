                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Schemes for Farmers Welfare




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of schemes being implemented by this Ministry for the welfare of the farmers at present;(b) 	the fund allocated/released for welfare schemes meant for farmers during last three years and the current year; (c) 	whether some States are not showing interest in implementing farmer welfare schemes; and (d) 	if so, the details of such States and the action taken by this Ministry in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Agriculture is a State subject. The Government of India supplements the efforts of the State Governments through various Schemes/ Programmes. Names of major schemes along with fund allocation/ release being implemented by the Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare in the country during the last three years and the current year is at Annexure–I. All these Schemes/ Programmes of the Government of India are for the welfare of farmers of the country.   (c) & (d):	The Government of West Bengal has not shown any interest in implementation of farmer welfare schemes like PM-KISAN. The Government of India is constantly pursuing the matter with the State Government for effective implementation of the scheme.
