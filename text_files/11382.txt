                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Drug Abuse among Youths




Kirron  Kher













                                                    Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 442TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.02.2020DRUG ABUSE AMONG YOUTHS442. SHRIMATI KIRRON KHER: SHRI B.B.PATIL:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:           (a)  whether surveys/studies have been conducted to estimate the number of young people who are addicted to drugs and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;      (b)  the number of rehabilitation centres in the country, State-wise including Punjab, Haryana and  Chandigarh;    (c)  the details of the number of deaths reported amongst the youth, nation-wide on account of drug overdose;   (d) the grants transferred to Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) and other eligible   organizations working in various States/UTs for counselling and rehabilitation of people addicted to drugs during the last three years; and     (e) the other steps taken by the Government to control drug abuse, especially among the youth in the country particularly in Punjab, Haryana and Chandigarh






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)A National Survey to collect state wise data on Extent and Pattern of Substance Use was conducted in the country during the year 2018. The report presents major findings of the survey in terms of proportion of Indian population using various substances and those affected by substance use disorders. This survey indicates that there are wide variations in the extent and prevalence of substance use across different states and between various substances. As per the report, prevalence  and estimated number of users of various substances among adults (18-75 years) and children/adolescents (10-17 years) is at Annexure. The detailed survey report is made available on the Ministry’s website link i.e. http://www.socialjustice.nic.in/writereaddata/UploadFile/Survey%20Report636935330086452652.pdf(b) The list of rehabilitation centre under the scheme of Assistance for Prevention of Alcoholism and Substance (Drugs) Abuse  in the country, State-wise including Punjab, Haryana and Chandigarh  is at Annexure-I  (c) National Crime Record Bureau under the Ministry of Home Affairs have informed that a total number of 874, 750 and 778 people have died during 2014, 2015 and 2016 respectively due to drug overdose in the country. Out of which, 543, 493 and 471 people between the age group of 14  and 45 have died during 2014, 2015 and 2016 respectively.   (d) The amount sanctioned /released to States for de-addiction centres during last three years under the scheme is at Annexure-II.  (e) The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has formulated and is implementing a National Action Plan for Drug Demand Reduction (NAPDDR) for 2018-2025. The Plan aims at reduction of adverse consequences of drug abuse through a multi-pronged strategy. The activities under the NAPDDR, inter-alia, include awareness generation programmes in  schools/colleges/Universities,workshops/seminars/ with parents, community based peerled interactions intervention programmes for vulnerable adolescent and youth in the community, provisioning of treatment facilities and capacity building of service providers.
