                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Irregularities in Distribution of ABY Cards




Sakshi Ji Swami Maharaj













                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether cases of gross negligencehave been reported in card distribution under the Ayushman Bharat Yojana(ABY), if so, the details thereof;(b) whether ABY cards have been reported to be found in dustbins indifferent parts of the country including at local sub-divisional hospital in Champaran, Bihar and if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Union Government has directed the State Governments to take appropriate action against the guilty in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)  to (c) It was reported in Agra and Pilibhit District, Uttar Pradesh that in some cases cards were found to be issued to non-beneficiaries by Common Service Centres (CSCs) without following due diligence. The District Magistrates of Agra and Pilibhit Districts were directed to get a field investigation conducted.       Based on the report all Cards that were confirmed to be issued incorrectly were disabled. Further, 900 CSCs of Agra District and 3 CSCs of Pilibhit district were deactivated and FIR was lodged against the CSC Incharge. The District Manager of Pilibhit District has also been terminated.      The State Head of CSC has been directed to regularly conduct audit and monitor the CSC centres and ensure that CSCs follow the laid down guidelines to ensure that only genuine beneficiaries are enrolled under the scheme.     No such case has been reported from Bihar.-
