                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2031






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Projects under Sagarmala Initiative




Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the number of projects initiated by the Government as of September 2020 under the Sagarmala initiative, State-wise; (b)    the percentage of increased utilisation of coastline and navigable waterways since the inception of the project and the estimated increase in amount of exports, since the launch of the Sagarmala initiative, if any; and (c)    the number of jobs created through the projects started under the above initiative during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) The number of projects (state/UT-wise) which have been initiated as of September  2020 by the Ministry of Shipping under Sagarmala Programme is annexed. (b) There has been an increase of approx 52% (from 80 MTPA in 2014-15 to 122 MTPA in 2019-20) in the cargo carried through coastal shipping since inception of Sagarmala. Cargo transportation on National Waterways has also increased by 33.81 % (from 55.03 MTPA in 2017-18 to 73.64 MTPA in 2019-20) in the last three years. However, there is no  direct correlation between increase in utilisation of coastline & navigable waterways and increase in export.(c) Approximately, Eighteen thousand jobs have been created through Sagarmala projects undertaken by Major Ports and  funded by MoS under Sagarmala since inception of Sagarmala Programme. This does not include man days of jobs created at construction stages of projects and indirect jobs.
