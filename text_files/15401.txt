                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4004






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Common Service Centres




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:-(a)	whether the Government proposes to expand common service centres to Gram Panchayats under Digital India Programme and if so, the details therefore;(b)	the total number of Gram Panchayats connected with common service centres under Digital India Programme, State/UT-wise including Telangana;(c)	whetheranytime-limit has been prescribed by the Government for connecting all the Gram Panchayats with the said service in the country including Telangana and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	thenumber of services available at these centres along with the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a):  Yes, Sir. This Ministry initiated Common Services Centre (CSC-2.0: A Way Forward) Project in August, 2015 under pillar-3 of Digital India for the delivery of Government-to-Citizens (G2C) and other e-Services to citizens within their locality. It aims to establish at-least one Common Services Centre in each Gram Panchayat to cover all the 2.50 lakh Gram Panchayats in the country.(b):  Total 2.41 lakh Gram Panchayatshave one or more functional CSCs. The States/UTs-wise details of these Common Services Centres (CSCs) at Gram Panchayat level is placed at Annexure-I.(c):   Time limit was set by Government to cover all Gram Panchayats by March 2020.
