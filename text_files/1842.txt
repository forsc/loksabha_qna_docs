                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1762






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Rake Points for Fertilizers




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government is considering to open new rake points for fertilizers in Madhya Pradesh;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has any proposal to bring urea under the category of decontrolled fertilizers and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether infrastructure of existing fertilizer rake points need to be strengthened; and(e)	 if so, the time by which it is likely to be carried out?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a):  No, Sir. Existing rake point facilities are enough to handle fertilizer rakes in Madhya Pradesh.(b):    In view of (a) above, question does not arise.(c):    No, Sir.  There is no such proposal.(d):    Existing rake point facilities are enough to handle various commodities including fertilizers.  Also, strengthening of existing infrastructure is a need based ongoing process subject to commercial justification, operational requirement, technical feasibility and resource availability.(e):     In view of (d) above, question does not arise.********
