                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1702






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Sanitisation of Trains




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering to properly sanitize all the trains running in the country;(b)	if so, whether the Government has taken any steps in this regard;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	to (d) All the coaches of trains are sanitized regularly during maintenance at maintenance depots before putting on service. Further, cleaning/disinfection of coach toilets is ensured at nominated en-route Clean Train Station (CTS) and within the trains.On Board Housekeeping Service (OBHS) staff wherever available in Trains have also been advised to periodically clean the doors, door latches, taps and door handles and other areas which are frequently touched by the passengers.****
