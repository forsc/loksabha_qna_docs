                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

674






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Establishment of Educational Institute for Minorities




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has decided to establish a first world-class educational institute for minority communities in Alwar, Rajasthan; (b) if so, the detail thereof along with funds allocated for the purpose;(c) whether the construction of the building for this international university has started;(d) if so, the present status thereof and the time by which it would be functional; and(e) if not, the reason for delay in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) to (e):  Maulana Azad Education Foundation (MAEF) an autonomous body under the aegis of this Ministry,  has decided  to establish a  National Institute and Hunar Hub in Alwar District of Rajasthan, for which 15 acres land situated at  Village Kohrapipli, Tehsil - Kishangarhbas, has been acquired from State Government of Rajasthan.*****
