                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2018






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Administration of Covishield and Covaxin




D. Ravikumar













                                                    Mala Roy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of persons administered so far with Covishield/Covaxin, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government has given any clearance to the Pfizer-Biotech vaccine in India even after several deaths were reported internationally, if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the company has approached the Government seeking indemnity against possible adverse events and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a): As on 9th February 2021, a total of 59.56 lakh doses of Covishield and 3.02 lakh doses of Covaxin have been administered. The State/UT-wise details are at Annexure.(b): Pfizer-Biotech vaccine has not received any clearance for use in India.(c): In view of (b) above, question does not arise.
