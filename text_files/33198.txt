                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

555






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Admission in Various Courses under NEET




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the demands received from the States/ stakeholders to fill up various quota under OBC, EWS and others in NEET for admission to undergraduate medical courses, NEET-PG and other such courses;(b) the details of admissions made for reserved categories and related action taken thereon till date, State/UT-wise; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor and the relevant demands pending with the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c) The Government of India through Medical Counseling Committee (MCC) of Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS) is the allotment authority entrusted by the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India to conduct counseling for All India Quota (AIQ)/ Deemed/ Central Universities seats of Under Graduate courses (MBBS/ BDS) and Post Graduate (MD/ MS/ Diploma) courses as per the scheme devised by the Hon’ble Court. The Hon’ble Supreme Court has allowed reservation for OBC and EWS in the AIQ Scheme from the academic year 2020-21, which has been duly implemented in the counselling process. Remaining admissions are conducted by the States as per their extant provisions. ****
