                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

379






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Funds released under PMAY




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds released to State Governments of Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) during the last three years; and(b) 	the names of districts covered in each State covered under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	To achieve the objective of “Housing for All”, the Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) w.e.f. 1st April, 2016 in rural areas which is being facilitated by Ministry of Rural Development and Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana Urban (PMAY-U) since 25th June, 2015 in urban areas which is being facilitated by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.
