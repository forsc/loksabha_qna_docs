                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1111






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Development of AYUSH/Ayurveda Cluster




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government provides assistance for development of AYUSH/ Ayurveda clusters in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of allocation of funds to AYUSH/ Ayurveda clusters set up in the country, State/UT-wise during the last three years; (c) whether any new proposal for setting up of these clusters in the country is pending with the Government for approval; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):    Ministry of AYUSH had implemented Central Sector Scheme for Development of AYUSH Clusters during 11thfive-year plan. However, this Scheme has since been closed w. e. f. from 01.04.2017 after a due examination of the status of the Scheme. Thus, at present the Government does not provide assistance for development of AYUSH/Ayurveda clusters in the country.(b):    Funds were allocated to complete on-going projects under Central Sector Scheme for Development of AYUSH Clusters during last three years with details as follows:Year 	Funds allotted to clusters 	States in which cluster is situated2016-17	Nil	Nil2017-18	Rs.100.00 lakhs	Uttarakhand2018-19	Rs.100.00 lakhs	Uttarakhand(c) & (d):   Does not arise as the Scheme has been closed.…………………
