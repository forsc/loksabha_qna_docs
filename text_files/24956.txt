                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

907






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Funds for Textile Workers in North Eastern States




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of funds allocated by the Government to North Eastern States including Assam under the Textile Workers Rehabilitation Fund Scheme during the last three years; (b) whether the said amount has been spent within the stipulated timeframe; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government has fixed any accountability in this regard; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (e):	There was no State-wise allocation of funds under the erstwhile Textile Workers Rehabilitation Funds Scheme (TWRFS). Government of India had introduced Textile Workers Rehabilitation Fund Scheme (TWRFS) with effect from 15.09.1986 to provide relief to the workers rendered jobless due to permanent closure of Non-SSI Textile Mills in private sector. With effect from 01.04.2017, TWRFS has been merged with Rajiv Gandhi Shramik Kalyan Yojna (RGSKY) under Ministry of Labour and Employment as notified vide Notification No. S.O.1081 (E) dated 06.04.2017. ***
