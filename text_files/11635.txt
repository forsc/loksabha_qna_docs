                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

564






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Bijnor-Daranagar-Hastinapur-Meerut Railway Line




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether in 2004 the then Minister of Railways, approved a rail line from Bijnor--Daranagar Ganj Hastinapur--Dauralla–Meerut in the parliamentary constituency of Bijnor;(b)	if so, the reasons for not implementing the same;(c)	whether there is any proposal to start Hastinapur-Bijnor-Dehradoon train in future; and(d)   if so, the time by which it is likely to commence?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a )   No, Sir.	(b)	Does not arise.(c) & (d) The updating survey for construction of new BG line between Bijnor--Hastinapur--Meerut(63.50 km) was completed in May, 2017.  As per survey report, project was found financially unviable, as such it could not be taken forward.However, Dehradoon is already connected to Bijnor and train services are available between these two stations.*****
