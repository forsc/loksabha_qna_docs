                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

542






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Cases Sent by CBI to ACB




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether various cases presented to the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) are forwarded to the agencies of the concerned States/Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB); (b) 	if so, the number of cases related to Rajasthan that have been forwarded to various agencies of Rajasthan Government/ ACB by the CBI during the last three years, case-wise; and (c) 	the provisions for seeking monitoring/investigation reports from the concerned States with regard to the cases forwarded by CBI along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b):   Yes Sir,A total of 830 complaints related to Rajasthan have been forwarded to various agencies of Government of Rajasthan by CBI during the last three years, i.e. 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019 (upto 31.10.2019) for taking necessary action at their end. The year-wise breakup for the last 3 years is as under:Year	Number of complaints2016	1712017	2622018	2752019 (upto 31.10.2019)	122Total	830(c):    There is no provision to monitor/follow up the complaints forwarded to State agencies for necessary action.*****
