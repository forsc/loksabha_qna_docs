                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

33






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Status of SPMRM in Haryana




Brijendra Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government plans to extend the Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) to more clusters in Haryana;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) 	the status of projects in the clusters approved under said scheme in Haryana along with the details of amenities planned/completed in these clusters as of June, 2021?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a) to (c) A statement is laid on the Table of the house. Statement in reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No.33 to be answered on 30/11/2021.(a)& (b)	Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) was launched in the year 2016 in mission mode to develop 300 clusters in the country holistically by provisioning of economic, social and physical infrastructure facilities. The state of Haryana was allotted 10 clusters by the year 2018 out of 300 clusters allotted to the country. Since the allocation of all 300 clusters, as approved by the Union Cabinet, has been done, currently there is no proposal to extend the Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) to more cluster in Haryana.(c) 	Under Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission, there are ten clusters allocated to the State of Haryana.  As on 30 June 2021, there are 1564 projects (works) approved for the ten clusters of Haryana, out of which 527 projects have been completed, 303 projects are ongoing and 734 projects are yet to start. Out of the 21 components under Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission, works like piped water supply, citizen services centres, village street lights, organic farming, upgradation of schools, agriculture equipment banks, irrigation facilities, sports infrastructure, bus stands, solid liquid waste management, tourism promotion, etc. have been planned for the clusters of Haryana. Details of project/works and status of completion are at Annexure*******
