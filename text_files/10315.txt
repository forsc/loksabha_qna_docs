                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3890






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Tea Production in Assam




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State-wise percentage of contribution in tea production including the State of Assam at present;(b)	whether the head office of the Tea Board of India is located in Kolkata, West Bengal and the tea producers, particularly of Assam have to face a lot of problems due to this reason;(c)	if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether the Government proposes to move the office of Tea Board of India to Guwahati or whether the Government has received any such request from various producers; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a):	The State wise percentage of contribution in tea production, including the State of Assam, during 2018-19 is given in the following table:State	2018-19	Production (M.Kgs)	%  to ProductionAssam	701.35	51.95West Bengal	394.22	29.20Other NE States	28.46	2.11Tamil Nadu	160.15	11.86Kerala	60.76	4.50Karnataka	5.10	0.38All India	1350.04	100.00	Source:  Tea Board(b) & (c):	The Head Office of Tea Board is located in Kolkata, West Bengal. The tea producers of Assam are serviced by Zonal Office for North-East, set up at Guwahati, headed by Executive Director.(d) & (e):	No, Sir.  **********
