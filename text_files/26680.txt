                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






DDU-GKY in Telangana




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the youths are getting financial benefits and sufficient employment opportunity after completion of their training under Deen Dayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), if so, the details thereof in specific to the State of Telangana?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) 	Yes Sir, Deen Dayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) mandates that a minimum of 70% of the youth getting training needs to be placed by the Project Implementation Agencies (PIAs). Further, on completion of training by candidates, DDU-GKY provides fund for post placement support and mobile tracking. In Telangana, since start of the DDU-GKY programme in the year 2016, a total of 62,162 candidates have been trained and 44,855 candidates have been offered jobs. Further, in Telangana, an expenditure of Rs. 290.06 lakhs for post placement support and Rs. 134.56 lakhs for mobile tracking for the candidates has been incurred so far.******
