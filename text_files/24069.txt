                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Categorisation of Hindu Minorities




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah













                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Hindus being fewer vis-a-vis total population of the respective States, State-wise;(b)	whether the Government proposes to categories them as minorities and extend the benefits to them as available to minorities; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a): Office of the Registrar General of India has informed that the State-wise Census 2011 Total and Religion-wise Population is available at the following link:          https://censusindia.gov.in/2011census/Religion_pca/RL-0000.xlsx (b) & (c): The Central Government notifies a community as minority in the  context of India as a whole, and not for individual State.  The issue of declaring any community as a minority with in a State comes under the purview of the State concerned.*****
