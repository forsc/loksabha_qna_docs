                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2155






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the number of accounts opened under the Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana started for girls in the country during the last two years, State/UT-wise;(b)	the concrete steps taken for successful implementation of this scheme and to provide maximum benefit to the girls across the country;(c)	whether the Union Government has fixed any role of the States under the Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	A table showing State/UT-wise number of accounts opened under the Sukanya Samriddhi Account for the last two years, is attached as Annexure-I.(b):	The Scheme is being given due publicity and campaigns to open accounts are launched by Post Offices and Banks from time to time for information of the public.(c)&(d):	No. The Union Government has not fixed any role of the States under the Sukanya Samriddhi Account Scheme.
