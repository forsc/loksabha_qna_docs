                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

189






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Financial Assistance for Flood-hit States




S. Muniswamy













                                                    Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh
                                                








                                                    Rahul Gandhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has announced a special relief package/financial assistance for Kerala in the aftermath of two consecutive floods and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has also sanctioned funds to deal with the situation arising out of floods caused by heavy rain in about 25 districts of Bihar including capital Patna and Karnataka and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c)	the details of financial assistance sought by various States including Kerala, Bihar and Karnataka for relief and rehabilitation, State-wise;(d)	the details of financial assistance/special relief packages announced including sanction and allocation of funds for flood-affected areas during the last five years and the current year, State-wise;(e)	whether the sanctioned funds have been released to the States/State Disaster Management and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(f)	 The details of criteria fixed by the Government to allocate funds to deal with disasters like floods?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE & CORPORATE AFFAIRS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	For the year 2018-19, based on the assessment by Inter-Ministerial Central Team (IMCT) and recommendation from SC-NEC, High Level Committee(HLC), Govt. of India approved additional financial assistance amounting to Rs. 3048.39 crore from National Disaster Relief Fund(NDRF). Further, Rs. 192.60 crore as Central share of State Disaster Response Fund (including advance) were released to the State during 2018.  For the year 2019-20, IMCT visited Kerala to assess damages in the wake of floods during 2019.  Report of the IMCT is awaited for further action.  However, Rs. 52.28 crore have been released to the State as Central share of State Disaster Response Fund in July, 2019.  (b)	In the wake of damages caused due to floods in 2019, Rs. 1200.00 crore were released to the State of Karnataka and Rs. 613.75 crore to Bihar (including advance from SDRF) ‘on account basis’ for immediate relief operations  pending final assessment by IMCT and approval of HLC.(c)	State-wise details of financial assistance sought by various States including Kerala, Bihar and Karnataka for relief and rehabilitation are given in Annexure-‘A’ attached. (d)	 & (e) Yes, State-wise details of allocation and release of financial assistance/Special relief packages during the last five years and the current year is given in Annexure-‘B’ attached.      (f)  Central Finance Commission recommends State-wise and Year-wise fundsfor SDRF(including Central : State share) to be released within its award period of five years for meeting the expenditure to be incurred on providing immediate relief on account of notified & specified natural disasters including floods based on the approved items and norms of assistance under NDRF/SDRF.
