                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Drug Sale at Jan Aushadhi Stores




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) the number of operational generic medical stores functioning and the number of stores which have been closed in the country, State-wise; (b) the policies of the Government to resolve the problems regarding supply of medicines in such stores; (c) whether any plan is being formulated by the Government to check the sale of branded medicines at Jan Aushadi Stores; and (d) if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D.V. SADANANADA GOWDA)(a) As on 10th March, 2021, 7507 Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) are functional in all the districts of the country. As on 10th March, 2021, total 433 PMBJKs have been closed. State/UT- wise list of functional and closed PMBJKs is enclosed as Annexure.(b) Medicines are being supplied to PMBJKs by Bureau of Pharma PSUs of India (BPPI), the implementing agency of the scheme through its central and regional warehouses and country-wide network of distributors. In order to ensure adequate availability of medicines at the PMBJKs, the logistics system is being strengthened. At present, three warehouses are functional at Gurugram, Chennai and Guwahati and the fourth one is under construction at Surat. Further, 37 distributors have been appointed across the country to ensure supply of medicines to remote and rural areas. (c) & (d) Under the Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP), only generic medicines are supplied by BPPI for sale through PMBJKs. In order to improve functioning of the scheme, Information Technology (IT) enabled End-to-End supply chain system with Point-of-Sale (POS) application for value added services has been implemented in all PMBJKs. This system allows selling only generic medicines. Further, PMBJKs are allowed to sell allied medical products commonly sold in chemist shops and generic medicines which are not in the basket of PMBJP.PMBJKs are periodically investigated by the marketing officers of BPPI to eliminate possibilities of any misconduct. In case of complaints, including complaint of sale of any branded medicines through PMBJK, marketing officers of BPPI investigates the matter and strict action is taken against the defaulting store owners.
