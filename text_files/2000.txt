                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1852






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Construction of ROBs/RUBs in Karnataka




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of proposals for construction of Railway Over Bridge (ROB) and Railway Under Bridge (RUB) received from Karnataka;(b)	the details of the proposals received from the State Government, Members of Parliament and others;(c)	the number of ROBs and RUBs that were constructed in Karnataka during the last three years, district/zone-wise;(d)	the amount spent on construction of the ROBs and RUBs in Karnataka;(e)	the number of proposals for ROBs and RUBs of Karnataka that are pending till date; and(f)	the details along with steps taken/proposed to be taken to complete all the ROBs and RUBs in Karnataka within the stipulated time?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)   (a) to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
