                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Criteria for Setting up a Hospital under AYUSH




Guman Singh Damor














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the criteria laid down for setting up a hospital under AYUSH;(b)	whether the Government proposes to set up any collection and processing units for medicinal plants in tribal areas having these plants in abundance;(c)	the details of degrees required to become a doctor under AYUSH; and(d)	the names of the examinations required to be cleared by a person having foreign degrees in Ayurveda/Homeopathy to become a AYUSH doctor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) Public health being a State subject, setting up of hospital under AYUSH comes under the purview of the respective Sate/UT Government. However, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision of setting up of 50/30/10 bedded Ayush hospitals. State/UT Governments may avail the eligible financial assistance by submitting the suitable proposal through State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) as per the NAM guidelines.  Further, as per the criteria mentioned in the NAM guidelines, State/UT Governments have to furnish the Detailed Project Report (DPR), Land ownership rights and commitment of creation of regular posts for the proposed hospital. (b) Ministry of Ayush had supported 25 processing units, 42 rural collection centres/district collection centres/retails outlets throughout the country from the financial year 2015-16 to 2020-21 under the Medicinal Plants component of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National AYUSH Mission. The details are given at Annexure I. (c) The details of under graduate degrees recognized by National Commission of Indian System of Medicine (NCISM) & National Commission of Homoeopathy (NCH) are given at Annexure II.(d)   As per The National Commission for Indian System of Medicine Act, 2020, provision 15 (4) “Any person with foreign medical qualification shall have to qualify National Exit Test for the purpose of obtaining licence to practice as medical practitioner of Indian System of Medicine and for enrollment in the State Register or the National Register, as the case may be, in such manner as may be specified by regulations”.         As per The National Commission for Homoeopathy Act, 2020, provision 15 (4) “Any person with foreign medical qualification shall have to qualify National Exit Test for the purpose of obtaining licence to practice Homoeopathy as medical practitioner of Homoeopathy and for enrollment in the State Register or the National Register, as the case may be, in such manner as may be specified by regulations”.***********
