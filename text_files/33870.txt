                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1558






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2022






Setting up of CNG Pumps




Hanuman Beniwal













                                                    Ajay Nishad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of the criteria fixed for setting up of CNG pumps in the country;(b) whether CNG pumps are likely to be set up in each state of the country; (c) if so, the details of the action plan and the target fixed for this purpose in the States of the country, Statewise including Rajasthan and Muzaffarpur in Bihar;(d) the details of the number of pumps which are currently in operation, State-wise including Rajasthan and Muzaffarpur in Bihar; and (e) the details of CNG consumption in the country during each of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)	Government has not fixed any criteria for the establishment of CNG stations. CNG stations are set up by City Gas Distribution (CGD) entities authorized by Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) for development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in a Geographical Area (GA). These CNG stations are set up by the entities as per the Minimum Work Plan determined by PNGRB and their techno-commercial feasibility.(b) to (d)	Upto the 10th CGD bidding round, PNGRB has authorized 228 Geographical Areas (GAs) in 27 States/Union Territories including the states of Rajasthan and Bihar for the development of CGD networks as per the target set, action plan proposed and timeline determined in the MWP. Further, a part of future action, in the 11th round, PNGRB has received bids against 61 GAs covering 215 (212 full & 3 part) districts and Letter of Intent (LoIs) have been issued for 52 GAs. Further, PNGRB has invited bid on 07.01.2022 for 5 GAs covering 27 districts under the 11A CGD Bidding Round. This will potentially cover 98% of population & 88% of geographical area of the country. As on 31.12.2021, 3628 CNG stations have been commissioned across the county. The state wise details of CNG stations currently in operation and the target fixed are at Annexure.(e)	As per the information submitted by Petroleum Planning & Analysis Cell (PPAC), CNG consumption in the country during each of the last three years is as under:(in MMSCM)FY 2019-20	FY 2020-21	FY 2021-22 (April-September)4632	3678	2462*****
