                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Port-Based Projects




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that port-based industries are likely to come into being once the Sagarmala and other port-based projects are completed;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any empanelled partner organisation of port-based project has been mandated to conduct deep-sea diving and other port-based courses in coastal areas like Nagapattinum District of Tamil Nadu;and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)& (b)	Under the Sagarmala Programme, it was proposed to create an institutional framework for enabling Port Led Industrialization and incentivize employment generation. The proposal was taken up with the Department of Expenditure (DOE) and as per Minutes of Meeting of EFC held on 09.07.2019, it was recommended that Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways should explore possibility of industrialization with land available with the major port.More than 8000 Acres of land have been used for Industrialization by Major Ports which has generated more than 2.5 Lakh direct and indirect employment.(c)&(d)	No partner of port based project is empanelled to conduct deep sea diving course in  Nagapattinum District of Tamil Nadu under Sagarmala- DeenDayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU- GKY) convergence programme. However, phase wise courses sanctioned to project implementing agencies for the state of Tamil Nadu is provided in Annexure I & II.Annexure IPhase I courses under DDU GKY in Tamil Nadu<pre>S. No	District	Course Name1	Thiruvallur	Hospitality Assistant, Arc & Gas Welder2	Kancheepuram	Electrical Technician, Repair & maintenance of domestic electrical appliances3	Tuticorin	Tally & ERP Accountant                                                  </pre>Annexure IIPhase II courses under DDU GKY in Tamil Nadu<pre>S.
