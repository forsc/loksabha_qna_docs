                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3183






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Provision of High Quality Seeds




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government provides cross-breed seeds and hybrid seeds to the farmers for free so that the productivity can be increased;(b)	if so, the number of distribution centres set up in the country including in Uttar Pradesh and Bihar;(c)	the funds allocated to be spent annually under this scheme;(d)	whether the Government is aware of the shortage of fertilizers; and(e)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE????????????????????????	(SHRI NAREDNRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (b): The Government has approved a pilot project for Hybrid seed mini kits distribution of Mustard through National Seeds Corporation (NSC) during Rabi-2021-22 under National Food Security Mission-Oilseeds (NFSM-OS). Hybrid seeds of Rapeseed & Mustard has been distributed in 7 districts of 5 states to the farmers through State Department of Agriculture on free of cost. Details of Hybrid seed distribution is tabulated below: S. No.State	Districts	Minikit (in No.)	Qty (in qtls)1	Gujarat	Banaskantha	20000	3002	Haryana	Hisar	15000	2253	Madhya Pradesh	Murena, Sheopurkala	30000	3504	Rajasthan	Bharatpur	20000	2605	Uttar Pradesh	Etah, Varanasi	35000	480	Total		120000	1615(c): Fund allocated under distribution of Hybrid seeds of Rapeseed & Mustard was to the sum of Rs. 7.80 crore during 2021-22.(d)& (e):Department of Fertilizers has informed the availability of fertilizers is comfortable across the country and no shortage is being reported by the States/UTs.
