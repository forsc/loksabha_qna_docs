                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Supply of BS-VI Auto Fuel




Kani K. Navas













                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of the BS-VI quality auto fuel quantities supplied during the last three years, district/ city-wise; (b) whether the Government intends to expand the list of districts/cities so supplied; and (c)   if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a)      The details of quantity of BS-VI auto fuel supplied by Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) during the last 3 years, State/UT wise are annexed.  (b) & (c)  BS-VI Auto Fuel was implemented effective 01.04.2020, across all India. Presently all OMCs Retail Outlets including newly commissioned Retail Outlets, on Pan India basis, are being supplied with BS-VI Motor Spirit (MS) & High Speed Diesel (HSD).*****
