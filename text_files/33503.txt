                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

925






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin




Dibyendu Adhikari













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    S.R. Parthiban
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds allocated/sanctioned/released/utilized under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) during the last three years, year-wise and State-wise particularly in the States of Gujarat, Rajasthan, Bihar, Maharashtra, Jharkhand and Tamil Nadu;(b) 	the number of houses constructed/completed/sanctioned/allotted to the beneficiaries under the PMAY-G during the last three years, year, State and district-wise;(c)	whether it is true that some areas are facing shortage of funds from Union Government installment particularly in the Banaskantha/Amreli District of Gujarat, Jalore and Sirohi districts of Rajasthan, Araria district of Bihar, Latur district and Madha of Maharashtra and Chatra, Latehar and Palamu Districts of Jharkhand during the said period;(d) 	the number of beneficiaries presently in the waiting list for providing house under the scheme at present particularly in the aforesaid areas during the said period;(e) 	the details of steps taken by the Government for allotment of houses to all the wait listed beneficiaries in the aforesaid areas?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE)(a): The details of funds allocated, Central Share released & funds utilized (including state share) under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) during the last three years 2018-19 to 2020-21, year-wise and state-wise including the States of Gujarat, Rajasthan, Bihar, Maharashtra, Jharkhand and Tamil Nadu are given  in Annexure-I. (b): The year wise and State wise number of houses targeted, sanctioned and completed under the PMAY-G during the last three years 2018-19 to 2020-21 including the States of Gujarat, Rajasthan, Bihar, Maharashtra, Jharkhand and Tamil Nadu is given in Annexure-II. The district wise progress is available on website of PMAY-G on www.pmayg.nic.in---->AwaasSoft------>Reports------->Annual target and allocation. (c): The details of unspent balance available in State Nodal Account (SNA) of PMAY-G of States of Gujarat, Rajasthan, Bihar, Maharashtra and  Jharkhand, as on 04.02.2022 are given as under:- Name of the State	Unspent balance (Rs. In crores)Gujarat	                      254.86Rajasthan	              464.32Bihar	                    1,248.25Maharashtra	            1,206.12Jharkhand	              525.16The Central Assistance under PMAY-G is released directly to the State/UT considering the State/UT as a unit. Further release of these funds to beneficiaries in various districts is done by the respective State/UT Governments. The above mentioned states have considerable unspent balance with them to disburse to the beneficiaries. Therefore, there is no shortage of funds in the above States. In general, the Ministry is ensuring timely release of Central Share of funds to States/UTs as per provisions of the Framework For Implementation of PMAY-G and guidelines of the Ministry of Finance. (d) & (e): The targets allocated, houses sanctioned and completed under PMAY-G for the States of Gujarat, Rajasthan, Bihar, Maharashtra, Jharkhand and Tamil Nadu is given as under:-S.
