                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Airport at Warangal




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to construct airport in Warangal and Mamnoor in Telangana State; and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Government of India (GoI), Ministry of Civil Aviation has issued a Greenfield Airport Policy for development of greenfield airports across the country. The Policy permits a State Government, airport company or individual developer to establish a greenfield airport after obtaining ''site clearance'' and ''in principle'' approval from Ministry of Civil Aviation. For the purpose, the applicant proposing to set up an airport is required to make an application to the Steering Committee for the first stage clearance i.e. ''site clearance'' in the prescribed proforma. Ministry of Civil Aviation has not received any proposal from the State Government or any other airport developer for construction of airport in Warangal and Mamnoor in Telangana State as per Greenfield Airport Policy.;;However, on the request of State Government of Telangana, Airports Authority of India (AAI) has undertaken  preliminary study of the sites identified by Govt. of Telangana including Warangal site (Mamnoor village) for possibility of further development of aviation infrastructure in the State.
