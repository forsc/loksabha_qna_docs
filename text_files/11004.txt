                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Establishment of NIV




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India (IBBI) proposes to setting up a National Institute for Valuers (NIV) on the lines of Institute of the Chartered Accountants of India;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the list/name of the present Registered Valuers with Registered Valuers Organization working across the country, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FORFINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS 			      (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)????? ??? ???????? ?????  ???????? ??? ????? ??????                       (???? ?????? ???? ?????)(a)	& (b): Ministry of Corporate Affairs has constituted a Committee of Experts on 30.08.2019 under the Chairpersonship of Dr. M.S. Sahoo, Chairman, IBBI to examine the need for an Institutional framework for regulation and development of valuation professionals.(c): A list containing names of the present Registered Valuers with Registered Valuers Organization working across the country is available in the public domain at https://ibbi.gov.in/service-provider/rvs.*******
