                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Funds Spent on Conservation of Monuments in Andhra Pradesh




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of works undertaken till date under Adarsh Smarak initiative in the list of monuments in Andhra Pradesh (AP) identified as Adarsh Smarak;(b)	the list of centrally protected monuments in Srikakulam district in Andhra Pradesh and the details of works undertaken, funds spent, for conservation, protection, preservation, repair of centrally protectedmonuments in Srikakulam during the last ten years;(c)	the list of works, activities to be undertaken for centrally protected monuments in Srikakulam as per the annual conservation programme drawn on the basis of need and priority, monument wise details;(d)	the names and designations of officials directly responsible for the conservation, preservation, development, repair of Centrally protected monuments in Srikakulam district; and(e)	the details of funds allocation and utilisation for each district of Andhra Pradesh during the last two years, district-wise along with the list of features of Central Schemes for Library Development, improvementand promotion?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)  	There are three Centrally protected monuments initiated in Andhra Pradesh under Adarsh Smarak programme.  These are Sree Veerabhadra Temple, Lepakshi, Anantapuram District, Buddhist Remains at Salihundam,  Srikakulam District and  reconstructed monuments at Nagarjunakonda, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh.(b)	Details are given in Annexure-I(c) 	As per Annual conservation programme for the current financial year 2021-22, the works identified for the centrally protected monuments in Srikakulam district are given in Annexure-II.(d)	The Centrally protected monuments in Srikakulam district are maintained by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI), by the respective Superintending Archaeologist for the conservation, preservation, development, repair of Centrally protected monuments in Srikakulam district, Andhra Pradesh.(e)	Details are given in Annexure-III Annexure-ISTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (b) OF THE LOK  SABHA  UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3256 FOR 09.08.2021List of Centrally protected monuments in Srikakulam district in Andhra Pradesh and the details of works undertaken, funds spent, for conservation, protection, preservation, repair of centrally protected monuments in Srikakulam during the last ten years;
