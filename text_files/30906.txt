                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2410






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Preservation of Bekal Fort




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Archeological Survey of India (ASI) is celebrating World Heritage Week;(b)	whether the Government has taken note that the Bekal Fort in Kasaragod is falling apart due to lack of conservation and official apathy;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government has any plan to support Bekal fort to restore the sea wall and preservation of cultural monuments in this Historical Asset; (e)	if so, the details thereof; and (f)	whether the Government has allocated any funds for this fort and other popular destinations in North Kerala and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Yes, Sir. World Heritage Week is celebrated from 19th – 25th November every year.(b) & (c)	The Bekal fort in Kasaragod under the protection of Archaeological Survey of India (ASI), is well conserved. Conservation works are executed as per the norms of Archaeological Survey of India (ASI). There are six ongoing conservation works on this fort. Details are given in  Annexure-I.(d) & (e)	Yes Sir.  The protected monuments are inspected periodically and conservation works are taken up as per requirement. The conservation of Sea wall (Bastion) at Bekal Fort has already been taken up. (f)	Yes, Sir. The Funds allocated and expenditure incurred on Centrally Protected Monuments in North Kerala in the last three years is given in Annexure-II.
