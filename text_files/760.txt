                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

661






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Underpass between Konch-Ait Railway Line




Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the underpass in between the Konch-Ait Railway Line is under construction;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether complaints have been received regarding irregularities in the construction of the said underpass and of water-logging during rainy season; and(d)	if so, the action taken on the said complaints?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. 8 Road Under Bridges (RUBs) are sanctioned on Ait-Konch section, out of which 3 RUBs have been completed and commissioned during the year 2018-19. Construction of one RUB is in progress. Balance 4 RUBs are technically not feasible.(c) & (d): Earlier complaint was received regarding probable water logging after construction of Subway. Cover shed has been provided to avoid ingress of water in the underpass.    *****
