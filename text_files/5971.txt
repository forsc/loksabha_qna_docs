                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5489






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Dengue and Chikungunya in BBMP Areas




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state:(a) whether the increase in number of Dengue and Chikungunya cases in Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) areas of Bengaluru has come to the notice of the Government; (b) if so, the number of such cases reported including the number of persons treated and deaths therefrom, BBMP zonewise; (c) the number of staff working to implement the prevention and control measures for dengue and chikungunya in BBMP areas, cadre-wise; and (d) whether there is a proposal before the Government for the zone-wise recruitment of assistant entomologist and entomologist in order to monitor and supervise the control Vector borne diseases measures in BBMP areas and if so, the details of the cadre and recruitment rules in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):  Yes. There is an increase noticed in the case of Dengue and Chikunguniya in Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) areas of Bangaluru. However, the number of cases of both Dengue and Chikungunya reported till 23rd July 2019 are lesser than that in the corresponding period of  2018. The Zone-wise comparative number of Dengue and Chikungunya cases and deaths reported from BBMP (till 23/7/2019)  during  2019 and corresponding period in 2018 are  at Annexure.Contd……-2-(c):   The number of staff working to implement the prevention and control measures for dengue and chikungunya in BBMP areas as are follows:- Gangmen for fogging and spraying              486Asha Workers                                                556Link Workers                                                  396Health Inspectors                                          198Medical Officers                                             114 Programme Officers                                       03 Deputy Health Officers                                   14  Health Officers                                               08(d):    As of now there is no such proposal. However, one post of Biologist is sanctioned in Mosquito Control Unit of BBMP under Urban Vector Borne Diseases (VBD) Scheme, which is vacant.
