                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2332






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






FPOs in Uttar Pradesh




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) given credit linkage 	from banks in Uttar Pradesh so far;(b) 	the number of Farmer Producer Organisations registered in the said State so 	far including the number of FPOs out of the same linked with NABARD to 	receive necessary assistance; and(c) 	the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 As per the details received from various implementing agencies, a total of 36 FPOs in Uttar Pradesh have been provided credit linkage by NABARD. Further 03 FPOs in Uttar Pradesh are provided credit linkage by Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium (SFAC).(b) & (c):	 As informed by the Government of Uttar Pradesh, a total of 571 Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) have been registered in the State of Uttar Pradesh out of which a total of 282 FPOs are linked with National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development(NABARD) for receiving necessary assistance. The details thereof is at Annexure-I.
