                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1772






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Maintaining Peace and Strengthening Security




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has identified the main obstacles in maintaining peace and strengthening security in the country during the last three years;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of the efforts being made for dealing with the said obstacles?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	The internal security situation in the country is reviewed on a regular basis by the Government and appropriate measures to strengthen the security and address security threats are taken. In this regard, close and effective coordination is maintained amongst security and intelligence agencies at the Centre and the State level. The Multi Agency Centre (MAC) has been strengthened to function on 24x7 basis for  real  time  collation  and sharing of intelligence for seamless flow of -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.1772 FOR 02.07.2019information between the Central agencies and the State security agencies. The Central Government follows a multi pronged strategy involving security related measures, developmental interventions, etc., to deal with the internal security challenges, which include: -?	Strengthening of intelligence setup;?	Augmenting the strength of Central Armed Police Forces;?	More effective immigration control;?	Strengthening of the coastal security set up;?	Effective border management, establishment of observation posts, border fencing, flood lighting, deployment of modern and hi-tech surveillance equipment,Though law and order is a State subject, the Central Government keeps a constant watch on the overall security scenario in the country. Alerts and advisories are issued to the law enforcement agencies whenever any threat to internal security is perceived.********
