                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1907






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Sorbhog Airport in Barpeta




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there was an Airport at Sorbhog in the district of Barpeta during the Second World War;(b)  if so, the details thereof and the present condition of the said airport;(c)  whether Government proposes to reconstruct the airport or build a war memorial at the site; and(d)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b) :  Yes Sir. The airport covers 383.71 acres of land out of which, 249.32 acres has been eroded by river Beki.  The balance is under old encroachments.(c) & (d):  There is no such proposal under consideration in this Ministry.*******
