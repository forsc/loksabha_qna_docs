                                    ANSWERED ON:

Storage Facilities in FCI




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the agreements entered into with private players for construction of silos and other food storage facilities for the Food Corporation of India (FCI) since 2014 including details of the private investors, State-wise; (b) 	the details of payments made by FCI to private players for storage of foodgrains since 2014, year-wise; (c) 	the details of outstanding dues to FCI and the loans availed by FCI since 2014, year-wise; (d) 	whether FCI has requested for equity infusion and if so, the details thereof including the equity infused by the Government; (e) 	whether the increase in outstanding dues to FCI is hampering its operations; and (f) 	if so, the steps being taken to strengthen FCI and modernise existing storage facilities and augment capacity in the public sector?
