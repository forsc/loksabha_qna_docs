                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

858






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Loss to Airlines due to COVID-19




Supriya Sadanand Sule













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the estimated loss suffered by Air India and private airlines due to restriction in passenger air traffic due to COVID-19 pandemic;(b) whether the Airport Authority of India (AAI) and Private airport operators are forced to seek funds to meet their working capital requirement and if so, the details thereof;(c) the reasons for AAI opting for bank loans though the company still has large cash reserves; and(d) whether the Government is planning to help AAI and Private airport operators in overcoming the problem and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a):The revenue of Indian carriers has reduced from INR 25,517 crore during April-June 2019 to INR 3,651 crore during April-June 2020. In respect of Air India, the total revenue has reduced from INR 7,066 crore during April-June 2019 to INR 1,531 crore during April-June, 2020.(b) to (d):  COVID-19 has adversely impacted the financials of Airports Authority of India (AAI) and it is likely for AAI to go in loss for the Financial Year 2020-21. Therefore, in order to meet the Working Capital Requirements, AAI has taken Fund Based Working Capital facility of Rs.1500 Crore from State Bank of India for the Financial Year 2020-21. For Delhi International Airport Ltd. and Mumbai International Airport Ltd., AAI has approved deferral for a period of three (3) months i.e. upto 15.07.2020 to make Monthly Annual Fee Payment against its Annual Fee obligation. Bangalore and Hyderabad airports have also been granted deferment of four months w.e.f. 01.07.2020 for payment of Concession Fee to the Government of India.
