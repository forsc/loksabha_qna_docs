                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Organic Food Production




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the percentage of organic food production across the country, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu;(b) 	whether organic food production is still very low within the country, if so, the reasons therefor and the steps taken/ being taken by the Government in this regard;(c) 	whether it has come to the notice of the Government that several farm produce are naturally organic but these are not certified; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof, State and UT-wise and the reasons therefor along with the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):  	The state wise percentage of organic food production certified across the country including Tamil Nadu under Participatory Guarantee  System-(PGS)-India and  under National Programme on Organic Production(NPOP) is given at Annexure I and II.(b):	Organic food production is gradually increasing. Government of India has been promoting organic farming under two dedicated schemes namely Mission Organic Value Chain Development North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER) and Parampragat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) since 2015 through State Governments. Under these schemes, support has been provided for formation of farmers’ clusters/ Farmer Producer Organization; incentives to farmers for input procurement, value addition including post harvest infrastructure creation, packaging, branding, publicity, transportation, organic fairs etc. Organic Farming has also been supported under other schemes viz Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) and Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), Network Project on Organic Farming under ICAR.Export of the Third Party certified organic products facilitated by Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA), Ministry of Commerce and Industry. Participatory Guarantee System for India (PGS-India) facilitated by Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Govt. of India   caters to the domestic organic market.Contd…2/--2-Govt. of India has launched e-Commerce platform-JaivikKheti portal (https://www.jaivikkheti.in/) to facilitate marketing of organic produce and easy access of the same for the consumers entailing low transaction cost. PGS-India and third party certified organic produce could be marketed through this portal.(c) & (d):	 Yes,  several farm produce are naturally organic but these are not certified. However, Government of India has initiated action through PKVY programme to get the produce of the farmers certified as PGS green/ PGS organic under farmers cluster approach. This certification is cost effective and affordable to the farmers. All the produces of a farmer registered under the cluster can be certified under this programme.
