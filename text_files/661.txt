                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

728






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Raising Funds of MPLADS




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to raise funds of Member of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme( MPLADS);(b) if so, the details thereof ; and(c) the time by which said policy/ programme is likely to be implemented ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) There is no proposal under consideration of the Government to raise fund of Member of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme (MPLADS); and(b) and (c) Question does not arise.                                             *****
