                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Government Functionaries on Social Media




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    Faizal P.P. Mohammed
                                                








                                                    A. Chellakumar
                                                








                                                    M. Selvaraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics and Information Technology be pleased to state:(a)	the number and the details of Government functionaries including Ministries, Departments and Government officers who have signed up for the social media platform Koo in their official capacity;(b)	whether the Government had issued any directive requiring  the aforementioned Government  functionaries  to sign up on the said app and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the level of involvement and the nature of support given by the Government to such apps and other indigenously developed  apps; and(d)	whether any incentives over and above the cash awards are provided by the Government to these  apps and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        a):  Key organisations from the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) have set up accounts on Koo, India’s own micro-blogging platform. The handle details are as given below:Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology: @MeityMyGov: @MyGovindiaDigital India: @DigitalindiaUmang App: @UmangThe information with regard to other Ministries / Departments and officers who have signed up Koo is not maintained by the MeitY.(b):  No, Sir.  (c) and (d):  MeitY in partnership with Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)-Niti Aayog launched Digital India AatmaNirbhar Bharat Innovation Challenge. The innovation challenge was a joint initiative of AIM, MeitY Startup Hub (MSH) and MyGov. All stakeholders were involved from Conceptualization to evaluation stage. A total of 6,940 applications under various categories as given below were received:S. No.	Category	Individual	Organization	Total Applications1	Business	610	532	1,1422	Health & Wellness	517	384	9013	E-Learning	579	483	1,0624	Social Networking	780	375	1,1555	Games	244	82	3266	Office & Work From Home	355	307	6627	News	166	71	2378	Entertainment	201	119	3209	Others	487	648	1,135Total Applications	3,939	3,001 	6,940Salient features: The shortlisted Apps will be put up on leader board for the information of citizens. Apps that are found to be suitable for use of Government will be adopted and guided for maturity. The relevant Apps may also be listed on GeM. To give a push to India App ecosystem by identifying the best Indian Apps that has the potential to become leaders in their respective categories. It was decided that the winners in each category would be monetarily awarded. The award amount for First was INR 20 Lakh, Second was INR 15 Lakh and the third was INR 10 Lakh. A total of INR 3.80 Crore has been awarded to 24 winners.*******
