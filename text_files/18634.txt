                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

49






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Agriculture Sector Skill Council




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has introduced any scheme to impart and promote skill training in agriculture through the Agriculture Sector Skill Council; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c)	 whether the Government has set any target to achieve through the Agriculture Sector Skill Council, if so, the details thereof along with the funds earmarked and spent for the said purpose during the last three years; and (d) 	the total number of people benefited under the said scheme till date, year-wise and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): Agriculture Skill Council of India (ASCI) is an autonomous organization working under the aegis of Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE). ASCI works towards capacity building and upgrading skills of farmers, rural youth, wage workers and self-employed persons engaged in organized / unorganized segments of agriculture & allied sectors.However, the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (MoA&FW) is conducting skill training courses through wide network of National Training Institutes, State Level Training Institutes, Krishi Vigyan Kendras and State Agricultural Universities, across the country in coordination with Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) and ASCI. The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) has introduced Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) to promote skills in farming through ASCI.  Contd…..2/-- 2 -The skill trainings are conducted on the Qualification Packs (QPs) in agriculture and allied areas developed by ASCI in compliance with National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF). (c):	The MoA&FW has not set any target to achieve through ASCI. However, the skilling targets are allocated to the identified training institutes by the Ministries/Departments for conducting skill training courses in agriculture and allied areas. The funds are released to these training institutes for conducting skill trainings as per Common Cost Norms notified by MSDE. However, no funds are earmarked for Agriculture Sector Skill Council.(d):	The schemes operated by MoA&FW and MSDE and people benefited are given below:Scheme	 Beneficiaries (No.)Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (MoA&FW)	37200Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (MSDE)	965546Total	1002746The details of year-wise and State-wise candidates trained under skill trainings are given in Annexure – I & II.
