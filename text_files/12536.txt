                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1482






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Caste Based Census




Durga Prasad Rao Balli














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Members of Parliament have sought Caste based census of the Other Backward Classes (OBCs) in previous Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the progress thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b): In 16th Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha, Members of Parliament have sought answers of questions related to conduct of caste based census and to conduct census of Other Backward Classes. The intent of the Government for conducting Census 2021 has been notified in Gazette of India on 28th March, 2019.  The Census Schedule is designed in consultation with all stakeholders including Central Ministries. In Census, the castes and tribes which are specifically notified as Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes as per Presidential Order under the Constitution (Scheduled Castes) Order 1950 and the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order, 1950 amended from time to time are enumerated.*****
