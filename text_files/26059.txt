                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1671






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Honorarium to jobless Coaches




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is contemplating to provide honorarium to jobless coaches and essential facilities for the practice of the sportspersons to compensate the losses due to discontinuing of the sports competition at the district, State and the country level due to COVID-19 and lockdown; and(b)	the details of the facilities being provided in Uttar Pradesh especially in Bijnor Parliamentary Constituency?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	Sports is a ‘State’ Subject and development of Sports, including providing financial support to sports coaches and sports facilities to sportspersons across the country, rests with the respective State Government and National Sports Federations (NSFs). The Government of India supplements and complements the efforts of the State Governments and NSFs in this regard.The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports is not implementing any Scheme that provides for grant of honorarium to jobless coaches. However, the coaches and athletes who were covered under various schemes of the Sports Authority of India (SAI), Khelo India and Target Olympic Podium Scheme (TOPS) continue to get financial assistance as per their entitlement and norms of the scheme, and no contractual coach was terminated by SAI during lockdown period. Further, though conventional training of sportspersons was suspended during the lockdown period, Standard Operating Procedures for opening of sports venues and resumption of sports training have been issued by the Ministry and the SAI and training of sportspersons have resumed as per these SOPs.(b)	The details of centres under SAI sports promotional schemes, Khelo India scheme and accredited sports academies in the State of Uttar Pradesh, including Bijnor Parliamentary Constituency, and sports disciplines catered to therein, are placed at Annexure – I, II & III respectively.*****
