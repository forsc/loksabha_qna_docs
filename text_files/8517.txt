                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2083






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






NCPUL




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has set up National Council for Promotion of Urdu Language (NCPUL) to promote, develop and propagate Urdu language;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c)  whether the Council has started its operation in Telangana; and(d)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d)	 Yes, Sir. National Council for Promotion of Urdu Language (NCPUL) was set up as an autonomous body by the Government in the year 1996 to promote, develop and propagate Urdu language.  For this, the Council runs following schemes / Certificate and Diploma courses for entire country which includes Telangana: <pre>1.  One year Diploma in Computer Applications, Business Accounting and Multilingual Desk Top Publishing (CABA-MDTP). 2.	Two Year Diploma in Calligraphy and Graphic Design3.	One year Diploma in Urdu language4.	Vocational course (Providing Skill Development and Training Course).5.	Press Promotion (financial assistance to small and medium urdu newspapers).6.	Financial Assistance for seminar/lecture series, Mushaira, Publication ofManuscripts / Bulk purchase of books etc.7.    Publication of books and Periodicals in Urdu language.8.	Academic Projects on preparation of dictionaries, terminologies, encyclopedia etc.9.	Organizing  International Conference and National Conference10.	Workshop on capacity building of Urdu Journalism.11.	Book Promotion through sales of Urdu books by organizing,/participating in book fairs and exhibitions across the country.</pre>*****
