                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1058






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Group-wise Data of Beneficiaries under PMAY-G




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of houses sanctioned and completed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) since inception of the scheme till date, State/UT-wise;(b) 	whether the data of the beneficiaries of said scheme is maintained according to their social and religious groups;(c) 	if so, the total number of houses sanctioned and completed and the total percentage of beneficiaries of SC/ST category among them;(d) 	the blockwise number of SC/ST category beneficiaries in Bhagalpur district each year from the inception of PMAY-G till date; and(e) 	the details of the total number of houses completed for beneficiaries of PMAY-G and the total number and percentage of SC/ST beneficiaries in Bihar including Bhagalpur every year from beginning of PMAY-G scheme till date, district/region-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE)(a)     Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) aims at providing pucca houses for all persons hvaing kutcha houses upto 2 rooms or less and whose names are included in properly verified Permanent Waiting List (PWL), irrespective of their social/religious affiliation. In terms The State/UT-wise details of targets allocated, total number of houses sanctioned and completed under the PMAY-G since its inception on 01.04.2016 till 03.02.2022 is given in Annexure-I. (b) & (c) Yes Sir. The data of the beneficiaries is maintained for SC, ST, and minority categories separately. Under PMAY-G, a total of 2,17,58,440 houses have been sanctioned till 03.02.2022, out of which 48,59,777 houses (22.34%) have been sanctioned for SC category beneficiaries and 47,64,373 houses (21.90%) have been sanctioned for ST category beneficiaries. Further, a total of 1,71,48,007 houses have been completed till 03.02.2022 which includes 39,58,363 houses (23.08%) of SC and 36,57,896 houses (21.33%) of ST beneficiaries.   (d)  The block wise number of SC & ST beneficiaries in Bhagalpur district, Bihar under PMAY-G since 01.04.2016 till 03.02.2022 is given in Annexure-II. (e)  The district wise number of houses completed under PMAY-G since 01.04.2016 till 03.02.2022, number & percentage of houses completed for SC/ST beneficiaries in Bihar including Bhagalpur district is given in Annexure-III.*****
