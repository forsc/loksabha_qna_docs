                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Rail Connectivity to Sonepat




Ramesh Chander Kaushik














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether there is no rail connectivity to the Parliamentary constituency Sonepat in Haryana as on date;(b)   if so, the reasons therefor;(c)  whether any survey has been conducted in order to provide rail connectivity there; (d)  whether any time limit has been fixed for the completion of the same; and(e)   if so, the details in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	 Sonipat is well connected with rail route and also is a junction of Delhi-Ambala main line. In addition, there are following more railway stations in the jurisdiction of Sonipat Parliamentary Constituency: 1.Bhodwal Majri    2.Ganaur    3.Sonipat    4.Rathdhana    5.Pilu Khera6. Safidon     7. Barsola      8. Jind      9. Ishanpur Kheri     10. Gohana -2-  11. Harsana Kalan halt      12. Sandal Kalan          13. Rajlu  Garhi.   14.  Lalit khera     15 Butana          16. Rabra         17. Bhadwansi.   18. Bhainswan    19. Bishanpura     20.  Kinan        21. Jaijaiwanti   22. Julana        23. Jind City    24. Pandupindara    25. Bhambhewa     26. Mohana   27. Gaon Baroda       28. Mudlana       29. Barahkalan     30. Budha Khera    31. Silla Kheri     32. Karsindhu  33. Khandrai     34.  Lath     35.  Dhurana     36. Rukhi(b)	to (e)    Do not arise.  	*****
