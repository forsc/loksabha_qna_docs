                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2561






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






RO-RO Train Service




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government is aware  that Saurashtra  including Rajkot and Morbi are major hubs of manufacturing and transporting of essential commodities along with ceramic tiles to various parts of the country;(b)  whether the Government is contemplating to operationalise RO-RO train service from Gujarat to various parts of the country;(c)  if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	Yes, Sir.(b) to (d):  As per extant practice, Zonal Railways receive the proposals for RO-RO services from parties and forward the same to Railway Board for evaluation.  Two proposals were received by Railways for RO-RO traffic from Rajkot to Surathkal, which were subsequently withdrawn by the parties.     An in-principle approval for operation of RO-RO service between New Palanpur-New Rewari stations over Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Limited (DFCCIL) has also been given.*****
