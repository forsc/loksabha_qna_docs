                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3780






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Allocation of Funds under PM-KUSUM




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam        Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM), State-wise; (b) the number, capacity and details of decentralised grid connected renewable energy power plants created under PM-KUSUM along with the targets set for 2020-21; and (c)the number of solar powered pumps installed under PM-KUSUM, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM) is a demand driven scheme. Capacities under the Scheme are allocated on the basis of the demand received from States/UTs and, therefore, State-wise funds are not allocated under the Scheme. The fund released to various States so far under PM-KUSUM are listed at Annexure-I.(b) Against the cumulative sanction target of 5000 MW in the year 2020-21, total 4859 MW capacity has been sanctioned to various states for installation of decentralised grid connected solar power plants under Component-A of the PM-KUSUM Scheme. These projects are under different stages of installation.(c) Till 28.2.2021 total 24,688 standalone solar pumps have been installed and 64 grid-connected agriculture pumps have been solarised under Component-B and Component-C of the scheme respectively. State-wise details are placed at Annexure-II. *****
