                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

258






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Train Services




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways is considering the proposals to start a Thiruvananthapuram-Velankanni- Madurai Train and a Main-line Electric Multiple Units (MEMU) service connecting Thiruvananthapuram to Kollam, Nagercoil and Kanyakumari; (b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which the Ministry proposes to execute these proposals; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH  GOYAL)(a) to (c):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 258 BY DR. SHASHI THAROOR TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 10.07.2019 REGARDING TRAIN SERVICES(a) No, Sir. (b) Does not arise.(c) Presently, Thiruvananthapuram is connected with Madurai by four pairs of express and one pair of passenger trains While there is no direct train service between Thiruvananthapuram and Velankanni, a special train is being operated upto 28.07.2019 between Ernakulam and Velankanni. As far as connections between Thiruvananthapuram and Kollam are concerned, 62 pairs of train services are running between Kollam and Thiruvananthapuram/Kochuveli. Further, one pair of MEMU train viz. 66304/66305 Kollam-Kanniyakumari MEMU via Thiruvananthapuram is being operated on the Kollam-Thiruvananthapuram-Kanniyakumari sector in addition to existing Mail/Express as well as passenger service operating on this sector. However, introduction of trains including MEMU service is an on-going process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources etc.*****
