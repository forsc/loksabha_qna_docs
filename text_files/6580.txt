                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

312






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Claims of Gujarat under NFS scheme




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Gujarat has made any claim for its share under the National Food Security Scheme, if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	the steps taken by the Government for issuance of the said claimed fund?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):	Yes sir, Gujarat has made following complete claims proposals for its share under the National Food Security Scheme;2017-18(Rs in Cr.)	2018-19 (Rs. in Cr.)	2019-20 (Rs. in Cr.)Claimed	Rs.178.02	Rs.151.30	No proposal received from State Government(b):	The claims were examined as per the Provisional Cost Sheet (PCS) issued by this Department and following admissible releases were made:2017-18(Rs in Cr.)	2018-19 (Rs. in Cr.)	2019-20 (Rs. in Cr.)Admissible amount Released	Rs. 168.15	Rs.111.89	No proposal received from State Government****
