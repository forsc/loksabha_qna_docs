                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

245






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Expansion of Airports




Vinod Chavda














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans to expand airports in metro cities;(b) if so, the details of expansion/refurbishment of airports in Gujarat;(c) whether automation in cargo handling facilities is ready to be implemented at airports in metro cities; and(d) if so, the details of facilities being introduced/implemented in Gujarat including those on the lines of the Delhi International Airport Limited?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement in reply to parts (a) to (d) of the Starred (*) Question No. 245 for answer on 05.12.2019 regarding ''Expansion of Airports''; ; (a) & (b): Yes, Sir. Based on the demand, the concerned airport operators have undertaken expansion works at various airports in metro cities including cities in Gujarat as under:; ;   Airport                                   Work; Chennai            Reconstruction of integrated terminal building and allied works; Kolkata             Construction of new Technical Block cum Control Tower and associated works; Delhi                 Expansion of Terminals 1 and 3; Bengaluru         Construction of 2nd Runway and Terminal 2; Ahmedabad      Construction of Domestic Apron including Link Taxi Track and allied works Surat                 Extension of Terminal Building and other works; Vadodara          Construction of ATC tower cum technical block;  (c) & (d): International Air Cargo handling facilities exist at 26 Airports including Joint Venture/Private Airports in the country and  Domestic Air Cargo handling at 30 Airports. AAI Cargo Logistics and Allied Services Company Limited (AAICLAS) being subsidiary of AAI, manages 19 nos. of International Air Cargo facilities by self-handling as well as through O&M agencies. Among AAICLAS facilities for International Air Cargo handling, Chennai, Kolkata & Ahmedabad Airports are the major cities. AAICLAS has established automation in the form of Elevated Transfer Vehicle  System (ETV) at export cargo area,  Automated Storage and Retrieval System (AS&RS) at import area, Integrated Cargo Management System (ICMS)-Cargo IT System, Conveyor Roller Beds, Scissor lifts for Pallets delivery, Embedded adjustable platforms for loading/ unloading of Cargo, Vertical Standard Racking System, Ball Mate floored Aircraft loading pallets/containers handling cold storage system and export pallet/container make up system etc. at Chennai & Kolkata Air Cargo Terminals. Further, AAICLAS has taken action to construct new International Air Cargo Terminal with modern facilities at Ahmedabad Airport.
