                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3865






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Fish Farmers Development Agencies




B. Y. Raghavendra














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Fish Farmers Development Agencies working in the State of Karnataka;(b)	 the work undertaken by such agencies in the State for the development of fisheries during the last three years; and(c) 	the Central assistance sanctioned during the aforesaid period for assisting the Fish Farmers Development Agencies of Karnataka?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (c) : The Government of Karnataka has reported that the Fish Farmers Development Agencies in the state of Karnataka are not working from the year 2011-12.  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries has been providing central financial assistance to the State Governments/Union Territories (UTs) for development of fisheries. Under the CSS, a total central assistance of Rs. 120.42 crore has been released to the Government of Karnataka during last three years i.e. 2016-17 to 2018-19 for development of fisheries and aquaculture in the State.****
