                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

714






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Ujjawala and Swadhar Greh Programme




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of shelter homes established for women survivors trafficking under the Ujjawala and Swadhar Greh programmes across the country, State/UT-wise;(b)	whether the Government has any proposal for setting up more shelter homes for women survivors trafficking of under the Ujjawala and Swadhar Greh programmes; and (c)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Rajasthan? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)  State/UT-wise Number of Ujjawala and Swadhar Greh (As on 05.04.2021)Sl.No	State	Ujjawala Homes	Swadhar Homes1.	Andaman & Nicobar Island	0	12.	Andhra Pradesh	5	223.	Arunachal Pradesh	0	14.	Assam	20	165.	Chandigarh	0	16.	Chhattisgarh	3	37.	Delhi	0	28.	Goa	1	09.	Gujarat	2	810.	Himachal Pradesh	0	111.	Jammu & Kashmir	0	312.	Jharkhand	2	513.	Karnataka	13	5214.	Kerala	3	715.	Madhya Pradesh	0	1616.	Maharashtra	5	917.	Manipur	22	2318.	Meghalaya	0	219.	Mizoram	2	1120.	Nagaland	1	221.	Odisha	12	5422.	Puducherry	0	123.	Punjab	0	224.	Rajasthan	1	925.	Sikkim	0	126.	Tamil Nadu	4	3627.	Telangana	5	2328.	Tripura	0	329.	Uttar Pradesh	2	1330.	Uttrakhand	2	131.	West Bengal	2	33 	Total	107	361 (b) & (c) New homes are opened under the Ujjawala and Swadhar Greh schemes in the States/UTs, including Rajasthan, on the basis of the proposals received from the States/UTs.The Ministry has decided to implement the Umbrella Scheme for Safety, Security and Empowerment of Women under ‘Mission Shakti’ for improving the condition of women in the country. The existing schemes of the Ministry including the scheme of Ujjwala and Swadhar Greh will be subsumed in ''Mission Shakti’ with a new component of integrated protection and rehabilitation home namely ‘Shakti Sadan’ with new and improved features.*****
