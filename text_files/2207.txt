                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1935






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Working of Tribunals




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether 272nd report on Assessment of Statutory framework of Tribunals in India has recommended for the formation of a single nodal agency under the aegis of the Ministry of Law and Justice to monitor the working of Tribunals;	(b) if so, the Government has taken specific steps in pursuance of this recommendation;	(c)  if so, the details thereof; and	(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)			Yes, Sir.(b) to (d)		Comments from various stakeholders concerned with the matter i.e. Ministries/Departments administering the various Tribunals have been requested to  on the recommendations of the Law Commission made in its 272nd report. The comments from some of the ministries departments are  awaited.
