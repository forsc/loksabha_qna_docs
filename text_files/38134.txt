                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5099






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Promotion of Tribal Tourism in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any programme to encourage tribal tourism in the State of Odisha;(b) 	if so, the details of initiatives taken by the Government to preserve and promote tribal culture and art works; and (c) 	the details of funds sanctioned and utilised for tourism development in the State during the last three financial years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM				      (SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c): The Ministry of Tourism promotes various tourist destinations and products of the country including Odisha holistically in Domestic and International markets and also encourages tribal tourism products through its ongoing promotional activities such as advertising, social media promotions, promotional events etc. The Ministry of Tourism has also identified Tribal theme as one of the themes under its Swadesh Darshan Scheme and has sanctioned 4 projects for an amount of Rs.371.47 Crore under this theme in the country for development of tourism infrastructure.  In addition, the Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned projects for tourism infrastructure development in the country including Odisha under its Swadseh Darshan and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ schemes. The details of the projects sanctioned under these schemes in the State of Odisha is  annexed. The Ministry of Culture has informed that to protect, promote and preserve various forms of folk/tribal art and culture throughout the country, the Government of India has set up seven Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs). These ZCCs conduct number of activities viz. cultural programmes, seminars, workshops, exhibitions and craft fairs in tribal areas for the development of Tribals/STs. Performing Arts Bureau of the Ministry of Culture also implements various financial grant schemes to preserve, promote and protect various forms of folk/tribal art and culture.********
