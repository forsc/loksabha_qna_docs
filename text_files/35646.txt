Loans Taken by States




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details and the amount of loans taken by various States/ UTs of the country including Chhattisgarh during the financial years 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21, and 2021-22 along with thenames of the institutions from which these loans were taken, year-wise;b)	whether the reasons and utility of such loans are reviewed by the Government and if so, the details thereof; andc)	if not, whether the Government is likely to review the said loans and if so, the time by which itis likely to be done?
