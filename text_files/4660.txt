Inland Navigation




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Bharatsinhji Shankarji Dabhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of rivers and canals identified for inland navigation in the country including the States of Gujarat and Kerala; and(b)	the steps being taken by the Government to optimize development of national waterways in the country including the said States?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	111 inland waterways (including existing 5 National Waterways) were declared as National Waterways (NWs), spread over 24 States (including Gujarat and Kerala) under the National Waterways Act, 2016 to optimize the full potential of waterways mode of transport in the country.  A list of these NWs including the States covered by them is at Annex -1.(b)	Based on the outcome of techno-economic feasibility and Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) on the new NWs, technical interventions are determined for safe navigation and shipping on technically viable NWs.  As per the DPRs completed so far, 36 NWs have been found to be technically viable, including those in Gujarat and Kerala (Annex.-2).
