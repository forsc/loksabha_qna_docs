                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2501






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Distribution of Seeds and Fertilizers in Chattisgarh




Mohan Mandavi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the State of Chattisgarh has distributed hybrid seeds and fertilizers to the farmers within the stipulated time; (b)	if so, the details thereof, district-wise/area-wise; (c) 	whether the apathetic attitude of the bureaucrats is often a threat to time bound distribution of hybrid seeds and fertilizers to the farmers leading to decrease in production; (d)	the remedial measures taken to solve this problem; and (e) 	the details of action taken by the Government against such persons responsible for this during the years 2020-21 and 2021-22? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)a)	Yes. Government of Chattishagrh has informed that hybrid seeds and fertilizers are distributed to farmers of Chhattisgarh State within stipulated time.b)	Various seed producing agencies like State Agriculture Department, State Seed Corporation, National Seed Producing Agencies, Seed Cooperatives and other Private Seed Companies etc. are involved in production and distribution of hybrid seeds to the farmers in the State. The District-wise details of distribution of Hybrid Seeds is placed at Annexure–I and Fertilizers at Annexure–II.c)	As informed Government of Chattishagrh no such incidence have been noticed in the State.d)	&  e)   Question does not arise.
