                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

357






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Joint Maritime Exercise




Parvatagouda Chandanagouda Gaddigoudar














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India has recently conducted Joint Maritime Exercise with many countries;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the names of countries with which such naval exercises were undertaken during the last three years, year-wise along with the aims and objectives thereto and the expenditure involved therein;(c) the number of naval persons and naval ships participated in each of the joint naval exercises; and(d) the details of such exercises that are likely to be undertaken with other countries in the coming years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (d): A statement is attached.*****
