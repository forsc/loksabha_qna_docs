                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2665






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






CSR Funds for Ujjain




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether any activities under corporate social responsibility (CSR) have been carried out by the oil companies under the Ministry in Ujjain; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether any work under CSR is in progress in Ujjain;(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, whether any such work is proposed to be carried out in future; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (e) The Oil and Gas Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs), as a part of their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) undertake CSR activities under the heads identified in the Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013. The details of the various CSR activities carried out in Ujjain by Oil and Gas PSUs are given in Annexure. ****
