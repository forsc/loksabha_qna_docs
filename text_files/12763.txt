                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1656






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Schemes for Promotion of Tourism in Haryana




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the schemes being implemented for promoting tourism across the country including Haryana; (b) 	whether the Government has identified any tourist spots for promoting tourism in Mahendragarh, Bhiwani, Dadri and Nuh (Mewat) districts of Haryana and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	if not, whether the Government proposes to develop Dhosi Pahari, Jal Mahal, Sahukar Gumbad (Chor Gumbad), Chhatta Rai Bal Mukand Dass (Birbal Ka Chhatta) etc. as tourist spots in Mahendragarh district and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to identify several ancient forts and sites in the said districts and improve those sites; and (e) 	the time by which the said sites are likely to be developed along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	Ministry of Tourism undertakes promotional activities in domestic and international markets through its schemes of ‘Domestic Promotion & Publicity including Hospitality (DPPH)’ and Overseas Promotion & Publicity Including Market Development Assistance (OPMD). Under these schemes, Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination under the Incredible India brand-line. As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country. In addition, the Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) to State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country including Haryana. Submission of project proposal by the State Government’s/Union Territory Administrations and its sanctioning is a continuous process. The projects under the scheme are identified by the respective State Governments/Union Territory Administrations & then taken up for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.
