                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3592






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






One Person Company




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of registered company as One Person Company (OPC) in India during the current year, State-wise; (b)  the number of the said companies out of them which have been registered by the NRIs;(c) whether some of OPC have changed their identity as Public or Private company during the current year as per the flexibility given by the present Government; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION; MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING; AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                                                       [RAO INDERJIT SINGH](a): During the current year  (from 01.01.2021 to 10.12.2021) total 9781 number of One Person Companies (OPCs) were registered under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 (the Act) and the state wise  details of these companies are as under: - 2 -State and Union Territory 	Number of OPCsMaharashtra	1603Uttar Pradesh	1051Delhi	925Karnataka	886Tamil Nadu	737Telangana	618West Bengal	508Haryana	471Bihar	384Gujarat	360Kerala	333Andhra Pradesh	312Rajasthan	277Orissa	227Madhya Pradesh	206Jharkhand	141Assam	133Jammu & Kashmir	106Punjab	100Uttarakhand	96Chattisgarh	84Himachal Pradesh	53Goa	45Chandigarh	42Manipur	20Tripura	17Andaman & Nicobar	12Pondicherry	11Arunachal Pradesh	6Meghalaya	6Nagaland	5Ladakh	3Dadra & Nagar Haveli	2Mizoram	1Grand Total	9781(b): No information for OPCs registered by NRIs is available separately.(c) and (d): Total 239 number of OPCs have converted into public or private companies during the current year.*******
