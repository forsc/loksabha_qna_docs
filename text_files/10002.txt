                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Jan Aushadhi  Shops




Gautham Sigamani Pon














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Union Government has directed all the State Governments to ensure opening of Jan Aushadhi Shops in every hospital in the State to provide affordable medicines to the people; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the implementation of said order in each of the States; (c) whether the State Governments have given any feedback adhering to the directions of the Union Government; (d) if so, the details thereof along with the States which are lagging behind in opening of these Jan Aushadhi Shops in the hospital areas; and(e) the action being taken by the Government against the States where these shops have not been opened till now along with the steps being taken to ensure compliance of its orders by the State Governments?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)  to (e):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED Q.NO. 318 (18TH POSITION) FOR ANSWER ON 10.12.2019 REGARDING JAN AUSHADHI SHOPS(a) Bureau of Pharma PSUs of India (BPPI) is the implementing agency of Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) which works under the administrative control of the Department of Pharmaceuticals, Government of India. BPPI and Department of Pharmaceuticals have written letters to Principal Secretaries (Health & Family Welfare) of all States requesting to provide rent free space in Government Hospitals for opening Janaushadhi Kendras.  (b) As on 05.12.2019, 5845 Janaushadhi Kendras are functional across the country. Out of this, 895 are functioning in Government premises. From 01.04.2018, 347 outlets have been opened in Government premises. State wise list of Janaushadhi Kendras functional at Government premises is enclosed as Annexure-I. (c) In response to the request made by BPPI and Department of Pharmaceuticals, various State Governments have responded and implemented Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana in their States. They have given feedback regarding implementation, which has been incorporated in Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) entered into with respective States by BPPI.  Detail of MoUs entered  into is attached as Annexure-II.(d)  State wise list of Janaushadhi Kendras functional at Government premises and list of states which are lagging behind in opening of Janaushadhi Kendras in the hospital areas is enclosed as Annexure-III. (e) Department of Pharmaceuticals has written request letters to Hon’ble Chief Ministers, Chief Secretaries and District Magistrates of these states to open Janaushadhi Kendras in maximum number of government hospitals to make available quality generic medicines at affordable prices to the citizens. BPPI  has requested these states several times for providing free space in Government Hospitals for opening of Janaushadhi Kendras and conducted meetings with State Governments officers. Positive progress is being made in this direction.
