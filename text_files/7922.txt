                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Sensitive Posts




Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the number of posts kept under sensitive category in Railways along with the time-limit of transfer pertaining to sensitive posts according to the transfer policy during the last three years and the current year;(b)  the number of such railway officials who have not been transferred even after the completion of their tenure; and(c)	   the reasons therefor and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERECE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	Approximately, 1,35,000 of posts have been identified as sensitive in Railways. Instructions exist for rotational transfer of the officers/staff working on sensitive posts every four years.(b)&(c) Most of the Railway officials working on sensitive posts have been transferred after completion of their tenure and others are in process. The matter is being pursued regularly and all concerned have been directed to take necessary action in the matter in expeditious manner to clear the backlog.*****
