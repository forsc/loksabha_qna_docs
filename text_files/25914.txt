                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1614






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Allocation of Funds under [SBM (G)]




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of funds allocated and released under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen)-SBM (G)] to the State of Telangana from the date of inception of the mission, year-wise;(b) the details of the number of beneficiaries benefitted from the mission, district-wise; and(c) whether the State Government is lagging behind in building toilets as per the schedule give by the Union Government, if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Swachh Bharat Mission-Grameen [SBM(G)] is demand driven programme, hence no fixed State-wise and year-wise allocation of funds could be made under it.  For implementation of the programme, each year State Governments submit their Annual Implementation Plans (AIPs) to the Government of India. The AIPs are approved by the National Scheme Sanctioning Committee (NSSC) constituted for the purpose. Accordingly, annual allocation of funds is made for the States/UTs considering their AIPs.  However, the funds are released subject to fulfilment of requisite conditions as per programme guidelines and availability of funds. Details of annual allocation of funds and the amount released to Telangana under SBM(G) from 2014-15 to 2021-22 (Upto 26.7.2021) are as under:-(Rs. in crore)Year	Funds allocated	Funds released 2014-15	211.26	105.622015-16	128.39	128.392016-17	135.72	135.722017-18	462.02	481.942018-19	1030.10	515.052019-20	137.34	119.922020-21	215.30	46.862021-22 (Upto 26.7.2021)	180.66	0The release amount during 2017-18, 2019-20 and 2020-21 includes Performance Incentive Grants funds also which were released based on the results of National Annual Rural Sanitation Survey (NARSS) Rounds, for which no prior allocation was made.(b) 	All the villages in the country including Telangana have declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF).  The benefits of ODF are accrued to all the people in a village.  However, district-wise number of Individual Household Latrines (IHHLs) constructed under SBM(G) in Telangana, as per the data reported by the State on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) of SBM(G), are given at Annexure.(c) 	No, Sir. *****
