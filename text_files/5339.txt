                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4902






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Allocation under MGNREGS




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total amount allocated by the Government under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) to the various States in the country, especially Tamil Nadu;(b)	the number of beneficiaries under MGNREGS in the State of Tamil Nadu, district-wise; and(c)	the details of the funds likely to be advanced to Tamil Nadu during the financial year 2019-20?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme. Hence, no State/UT-wise allocation of fund is made. Fund release to States/UTs is a continuous process and Central Government is committed to making funds available keeping in view the demand for work. States/UT-wise details of central fund released, including Tamil Nadu, under the MGNREGS during the current financial year 2019-20 (as on 17.07.2019) are given at Annexure-I.(b):	Details of district-wise households provided employment and persondays generated under MGNREGS in the State of Tamil Nadu during the current financial year 2019-20 (as on 17.07.2019) is given at Annexure-II.(c):	MGNREGS is a demand driven wage employment programme. The release of funds is a continuous process. The agreed to labour budget of Tamil Nadu for FY 2019-20 is     22 crore persondays. The Ministry releases funds periodically in two tranches with each tranche consisting of one or more instalments based on agreed to Labour Budget (LB), opening balance, pace of utilization of funds, pending liabilities of the previous year, if any and overall performance. *****
