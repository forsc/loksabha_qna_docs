                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5063






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Waste Land Development Board




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether letter no.28.2.24/89-90 MM-III dt.08.02.1991 by Dr. D.D. Das, Director, TE, West Land Development Board, Government of India was sent to The Assistant Inspector General, Forest, West Land Development Board” under Pushkar severage and forest project;(b) 	if so, the issue regarding for which letter was sent; and (c) 	the details of the entire noting-drafting regarding the said letter?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c) As per Record Retention Schedule of the Government of India, the project files are classified under Category ‘C’ and have the retention schedule of maximum 10 years.  Since the matter pertaining to Pushkar severage and forest project is more than 30 years old and weeding of files is done from time to time as per Record Retention Schedule, no such old records are available at present.*****
