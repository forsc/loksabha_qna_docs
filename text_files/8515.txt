                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2082






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Fake Universities




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether University Grants Commission (UGC) has issued a list of fake universitieswhich are self-styled, unrecognized institutions functioning in contravention of the UGCAct, warned students not to be taken in by their claims and most of these institutionshave been on the UGCs, watch-list for well over a decade, despite the regulator’s Anti-Malpractice Cell has been taking appropriate legal action against them and if so, thedetails thereof;(b) the steps being taken by the Government to close all the unrecognized collegeswhich are on the list so far State-wise;(c) the steps being taken by the Government to strictly monitor all the colleges to followthe guidelines of UGC in future to protect the interests of the students also;(d) whether there are a large number of fake universities that are operating in foreigncountries also; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. On the basis of complaints received from the students, parents,general public and also through electronic print media, University Grants Commission(UGC) has released the list of 24 fake Universities which are offering Degreeprogrammes without recognition under Section 2(f) and Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956.In addition, two more Institutions namely Indian Institute of Planning and Management(IIPM), New Delhi and Bhartiya Shiksha Parishad, Lucknow, UP are also not recognizedby the UGC. The details of these fake Universities are available on its websitewww.ugc.ac.in.The Government/UGC has taken the following steps for closure of theseUniversities so that the students do not fall into the trap of these fake Universities:i. The Government / UGC has issued letters to the State/UT PrincipalSecretaries/Education Secretaries for taking appropriate action against the fakeUniversities located in their jurisdiction.ii. UGC has issued Show Cause / Warning Notices to fake Universities cautionedthat running of Undergraduate and Postgraduate degree courses and givingmisleading advertisements shall attract severe action under the provisions ofappropriate laws including the UGC Act and the Indian Penal Code, etc.iii. UGC has lodged FIRs in the respective Police stations against Indian Institute ofPlanning and Management (IIPM), New Delhi and Bio-Chemic Education GrantCommission, Nadia, West Bengal.Apart from the above, UGC has taken the following steps to spread awarenessamong the general public, students & parents from the fake Universities:i. UGC has published the list of fake Universities on its website i.e. www.ugc.ac.in.ii. At the beginning of every academic session, UGC issues Press Release and PublicNotice regarding State-wise list of fake Universities in the country in nationaldailies and newspapers in Hindi and English to warn the aspiring students,guardians and the public at large not to take admission in the courses run by theself-styled, unauthorized fake Universities/Institutions of higher educationfunctioning in different parts of the country.(d) & (e): No such complaints have been received by the Government.*****
