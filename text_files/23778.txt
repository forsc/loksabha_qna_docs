                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4666






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Wi-Fi in Gram Panchayats




Devendrappa Y














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Gram Panchayats (GPs) with installed Wifi as on date, State/UT-wise; (b) 	the number of GPs with operational Wi-Fi as on date, State/UT-wise; and(c) 	the number of Wi-Fi users as on date State/UT-wise and the average data consumed per Wi-Fi per user per month, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) to (c)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country. Further, as part of this project, the last mile connectivity, through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband technology including Fibre to the Home (FTTH), is to be provided at all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country.State/UT-wise details of Wi-Fi hotspots in GPs and data consumption are attached as Annexure-I.
