                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2556






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Funds for Coal Industries




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has provided any funds to the State of Rajasthan to develop infrastructure for coal industries;  (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the manner in which the Government proposes to develop the infrastructure for coal industries in the State of Rajasthan? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)  (a):  No Sir. (b):  Does not arise in view of reply part (a) above. (c): Financial Assistance is given through CCDAC (Coal Conservation and Development Advisory Committee) to develop the rail/road infrastructure to all coal/lignite mines throughout India. The Coal/Lignite mine has to submit their proposals to Coal Controller''s Organization (CCO) which is placed then before the CCDAC headed by Additional Secretary, Ministry of Coal. If the proposal/project is approved by the CCDAC, the claim of the Coal/Lignite company is reimbursed by the Ministry of Coal.***
