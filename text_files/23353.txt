                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4462






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Promotion of Micro-irrigation




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of micro-irrigation systems that the Government has promoted to improve efficient usage of water for agriculture;(b)	the details of projects carried out in establishing micro/drip irrigation systems in the State of Jharkhand as of September 2020; and(c)	the details of the number of beneficiaries and the acreage of land covered under advanced irrigation systems in Jharkhand as of September 2020?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): 	The Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers’ Welfare is implementing Per Drop More Crop component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY-PDMC) from 2015-16 in the country which focuses on enhancing water use efficiency at farm level through Micro Irrigation systems viz. Drip and Sprinkler Irrigation systems.Under the scheme, subsidy is provided to farmers @55% to Small &Marginal and @ 45% to Others for installation of Micro Irrigation systems.(b) & (c):	An area of 22027 ha covered under Micro Irrigation Systems (Drip Irrigation 18921 ha & Sprinkler Irrigation 3106 ha) and 31080 number of farmers benefitted under PMKSY- PDMC in Jharkhand from 2015-16 to till date. *******
