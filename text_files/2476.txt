                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

210






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan




Durga Prasad Rao Balli














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of funds released to the Government of Andhra Pradesh under the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA) during the last four years including the current financial year; (b)	the reasons for delay in sanctioning the funds to the State under SSA during the current financial year; and (c)	the number of Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas which have been established in Andhra Pradesh and across the country during the last four years, State/UT-wise and the funds released in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)  to (c) : A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *210 TO BE ANSWERED ON 08.07.2019 ASKED BY SHRI BALLI DURGA PRASAD RAO REGARDING “SARVA SHIKSHA ABHIYAN”(a):	Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA) was operational till 2017-18 and now this stands subsumed in Integrated Scheme for School Education – Samagra Shiksha effective from 2018-19. The details of Central share released under SSA/Samagra Shiksha (Elementary Education) during the years 2015-16, 2016-17, and 2017-18 and 2018-19 in respect of Andhra Pradesh are as under:Central share released under SSA<pre>Year	Central share  (? in Crore)2015-16	668.112016-17	633.022017-18	704.312018-19	863.08</pre>(b):	No delay has occurred. (c) :  The number of Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (KGBV) established in Andhra Pradesh and across the country for the last four years, State/UT-wise is given at Annexure-I. Fund released to States are as per overall PAB approval and not component wise.*****
