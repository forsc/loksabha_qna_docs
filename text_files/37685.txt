                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4609






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Solar Powered Street Lamps




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any scheme is being implemented or any kind of assistance is being provided for solar powered street lamps and solar water pumps in rural areas;(b) if so, the details thereof, scheme/assistance-wise; and(c) the quantum of funds spent on the said work during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c)The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) is implementing the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM), with provisions for financial support for installation of 20 lakh standalone solar water pumps under Component-B and solarization of 15 lakh existing grid-connected agriculture pumps, including through feeder level solarisation, under Component-C. For these components, Government of India provides Central Financial Assistance of 30% of the benchmark cost or cost discovered through tender, whichever is lower. In case of North-Eastern States including Sikkim; hilly States of Himachal and Uttarakhand; UTs of Jammu and Kashmir, Ladakh, Andaman & Nicobar Island and Lakshadweep, higher Central Financial Assistance of 50% of benchmark cost or cost discovered through tender, whichever is lower, is being provided.Installation of solar street lights was carried out under Atal Jyoti Yojana and Off-grid and Decentralised Solar PV Application Programme, which have been closed w.e.f. 1.4.2020 and 1.4.2021 respectively.The state/UT-wise amount of funds released by MNRE as central financial support during last three years and the current year for the above referred schemes are given at Annexure.*****
