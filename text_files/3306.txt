                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3095






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Ongoing/Pending Projects of NHs




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of ongoing/pending projects of National Highways (NHs) in the country particularly in Bihar;(b)	the amount sanctioned and released by the Government to implement each of these projects in the country during the last three years and the current year; (c)	the steps being taken by the Government to complete these projects in the country particularly in Bihar within a stipulated time period;(d)	whether the Government has received any representations from public representatives regarding various anomalies in National Highway projects in the country particularly in Bihar during the said period; and (e)	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government thereon? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 There are 1682 number of ongoing / pending projects of National Highways in the country. Out of these, 86 number of ongoing / pending projects are particularly in Bihar.(b)	The amounts allocated and released to complete these projects in the country during last 3 years and current year are as follows :-Rs. in CroreYear	Allocation	Expenditure2016-17	1,00,710	73,7722017-18	1,09,498	1,00,1982018-19	1,30,111	1,28,0212019-20 (till June, 2019)	1,46,560	33,384(c)	The monthly, quarterly, annually periodical review meetings are being taken up by the Government at various levels to complete these projects in the country particularly in Bihar within a stipulated time period.(d)	& (e) There is no such representations received on anomalies. However, the representations of suggestive nature have been received and feasible suggestions have been taken care of during implementation.*****
