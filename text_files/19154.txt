                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

499






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Renovation of Indore Airport




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to renovate Indore Airport;(b) if so, the details thereof including the amount spent for its renovation during the last five years; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Upgradation of an airport is a continuous process and is undertaken by the Airports Authority of India (AAI) from time to time depending on the availability of land, commercial viability, traffic demand/willingness of airlines to operate to/from such airport etc. AAI has undertaken renovation of infrastructure at Indore Airport every year in the phased manner and has incurred an expenditure of Rs. 1863.85 lakh during last five years on the development of Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar Airport, Indore. ;;
