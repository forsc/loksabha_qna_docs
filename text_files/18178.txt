                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2020






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Arsenic contamination in Drinking Water




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the high level of Arsenic contamination in drinking water in the State of Jharkhand; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the budgetary provisions made, funds released and utilized for tackling contamination in drinking water during the current year; (d) whether the Government is satisfied with the achievements made so far; and (e) if not, the proposed plan of action and timeline for providing safe drinking water in the State of Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) 	The State Government has reported 2 Arsenic-affected habitations in Sahibganj district of Jharkhand State.  (c) to (e)	To enable every rural household in the country to have assured potable water supply through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024, Government of India in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal.Under Jal Jeevan Mission, priority is to be accorded to provide safe water in quality-affected habitations. For Jharkhand State, status of central allocation, amount released and reported utilisation of fund under JJM in 2020-21 is as under:				(Amount Rs. in crore)Opening Balance	Allocation	Amount Released	Total available fund	Fund Utilised268.39	572.24	62.34	330.73	111.32Under Jal Jeevan Mission, State Government has prepared Annual Action Planfor 2020-21inter alia which includes provision for potable water supply in quality-affected habitations. Regular follow-up is done by the Ministry with State Government to expedite the implementation.****
