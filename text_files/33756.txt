                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Hospitals run by CIL




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                      (a)       the details of number of hospitals run by Coal India Ltd across the country;   (b)      the details of total sanctioned strength of doctors in all the hospitals run by it and the number of existing vacancies particularly in Odisha, hospital-wise;   (c)      whether the Government proposes to raise the retirement age of doctors in Coal India Hospitals from 60 years to 65 years and bring it at par with the retirement age of doctors across the country;   (d)     if so, the time by which decision in this matter will be taken; and  (e)      if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a):  A total of 70 Hospitals are run by Coal India Ltd (CIL) across the country. In addition to the Hospitals, 359 Dispensaries are also being run by CIL.(b):  As per the approved manpower, the total sanctioned strength of  doctors in CIL & its Subsidiaries are 1568. The vacancy position of one of the Subsidiaries of CIL located at Odisha (MCL) is 82 (sanctioned is 173 and working is 91). Hospital wise vacancy in Odisha is annexed. (c):  No Sir.(d):  Does not arise in view of answer to part (c) above.(e):  There is no DPE guideline issued on the enhancement of superannuation age from the current 60 years in CPSES without which the superannuation age cannot be enhanced in CIL. ****
