                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3603






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Vacant Posts in Delhi University




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of vacant posts in Delhi University including those of Professors, Assistant Professorsand Associate Professors;(b) whether any measures are being taken to clear the pending appointment in Delhi University and ifso, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which the said appointments are cleared by the Government?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) to (c): University of Delhi has informed that 165 posts of Professors, 428 posts ofAssociate Professors and 263 posts of Assistants Professors are vacant and the University ismaking efforts for filling up the vacant faculty positions in a time bound manner. The Universityhas since advertised 263 posts of Assistant Professors on 02.07.2019.*****
