                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2635






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Schemes for Promotion of Agriculture




Supriya Sadanand Sule













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	  the  details  of  the  centrally sponsored schemes being implemented for promotion  of  agriculture  in  the  country along  with  the  details  of  the  central assistance provided to the States for running these schemes during the last three years and the current year, State and year-wise;(b)	  whether  the  Government  has received some proposals from the States/ UTs  for  allocation  of  more  funds  under these schemes;(c)	 if so, the details of such proposals and the action taken by the Government on each proposal, State/UT-wise;(d) 	whether the Government has made any  assessment  regarding  proper implementation  of  various  centrally sponsored  agricultural  schemes/ programmes in the country;(e)	 if so, the details thereof along with the  achievements  made  under  these schemes/ programmes, State/UT-wise; and(f) 	whether the agricultural schemes are lagging behind in achieving the desired results  and  objectives,  if  so,  the  details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the  corrective  steps  taken  by  the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	A statement indicating the schemes and funds released to State Governments during the last three years and current year (as one 30.06.2019) is placed at annexure-I.(b) & (c):	 Central assistance for the current year  are released to the State Governments based on their Annual Action Plans for 2019-20,  submission of utilization certificates, progress reports for previous releases and availability of funds.Contd…2/--2-(d) to (f):	The Government of India continuously evaluates the performance of all Centrally Sponsored and Central Sector Schemes, all of which contribute to the objective of doubling of farmers income by 2022. Impact and concurrent evaluations of schemes are carried out to highlight best practices and rectify shortcomings, if any, for improving outcomes. Details  of achievement  made  under some  major Schemes namely  Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY), Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) & Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER), Sub-Mission on Agriculture Mechanization  for the last  four years  and current year State wise are placed at annexure to II, III, IV  & V respectively.
