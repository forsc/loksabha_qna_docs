                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

805






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Manufacturing of Toys by MSMEs




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises as per category involved in manufacturing toys, State-wise; (b)	the number of MSMEs as per category involved in manufacturing of toys in Jharkhand; (c)	the details of India’s share in global toy manufacturing industry; and (d)	the steps envisaged under the National Programme for Development of Toy Industry to promote domestic manufacturing of toys?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):	As per MSME data available under Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum, there are 7560 registered MSMEs in India under the National Industrial Classification Code 2008 (NIC 2008) for toy manufacturing (codes number 32401, 32402, 32409) which includes 128 registered MSMEs in Jharkhand. State/UT wise details are attached at Annexure. (c):	No data with regard to India’s share in global toy manufacturing industry is available with the Ministry.(d): 	To promote the domestic manufacturing of toys, Ministry of MSME is continuously making efforts to support toy manufacturing through its various schemes aimed at providing credit support for new enterprise creation and expansion, technological assistance and upgradation, infrastructure development, skill development and training, enhancing competitiveness and market assistance of MSMEs. Besides, awareness programmes on CE certification, improved plastic processing for toys, packaging, IPR, etc. are organized for toy industry on regular basis. Further, in Union Budget 2020-21, to promote make in India and create a level playing field for MSMEs, basic Customs Duty on toys has been increased from 20% to 60%.
