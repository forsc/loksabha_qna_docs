                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

391






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Payment Backlog on account of Fertilizer Subsidy




Narayana Swamy Abbaiah














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is taking steps for clearing the payment backlog on account of fertilizer subsidy to fertilizer companies;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the amount of fertilizer subsidy passed on to farmers in Karnataka during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICAL & FERTILIZERS(SHRI  D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a)	to (c):   A statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO PART (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 391* FOR 23.03.2021 REGARDING “PAYMENT BACKLOG ON ACCOUNT OF FERTILIZER SUBSIDY” ASKED BY SHRI A. NARAYANA SWAMY(a) & (b):	 The Government has provided funds amounting to Rs. 64,598.30 Crore over and above the amount budgeted for the year 2020-21 to clear the payment backlog on account of fertilizer subsidy. All the carry over liabilities as on 1st April 2020 in respect of Indigenous P&K, Indigenous Urea and City Compost  have been cleared and the carry over liabilities in respect of Imported P&K amounting to Rs. 106.19 Crore and Imported Urea amounting to Rs. 84.71 Crore are likely to be cleared by the end of March, 2021 subject to compliance of procedures as per policy.(c):	The amount  of  fertilizer subsidy paid to the fertilizer companies (without recovery) on sale of fertilizers at subsidized rates to the farmers in the state of Karnataka during 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 (so far) is Rs. 2638.41 Crore, Rs. 3106.75 Crore and Rs. 6179.43 Crore respectively.********
