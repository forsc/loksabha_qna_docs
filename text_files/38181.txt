                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






National Zoo Policy




Vinod Kumar Sonkar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has recently drafted the National Zoo Policy in the Country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the details of the funds allocated/released by the Government to the zoos across the country including Uttar Pradesh zoos;(c)	whether the Government has taken decision to cancel the licences to run zoo in various cities of the country including zoo in Uttar Pradesh;(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e)	the other corrective steps being taken by the Government for the betterment of zoos in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE   (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	The Central Government notified the National Zoo Policy, 1998 on 28th October 1998. (b)  	Establishment and operation of zoos is the responsibility of respective zoo operators. The Central Zoo Authority provides financial assistance to zoos as per the Guidelines for prioritizing grant of financial assistance to zoos (2012). The details of funds released by the Central Zoo Authority to zoos in the country, including zoos in Uttar Pradesh, are as under:Year	Funds released (Rupees in lakhs)	Zoos of Uttar Pradesh 	Zoos of  the country2019-2020	Rs. 34.34 lakhs	Rs.499.83 lakhs2020-2021	Rs.24.40 lakhs	Rs. 598.13 lakhs2021-2022	Rs.15.18 lakhs	Rs.762.00 lakhs(c) and (d)	The Central Zoo Authority stipulates conditions for recognition/renewal of zoos and improvement of the operating conditions in the respective zoos. The recognition granted to a zoo may be cancelled by the Central Zoo Authority as per the provisions of section 38 H of the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 if the zoo fails to comply with the norms specified in the Recognition of Zoo Rules, 2009.Details of zoos whose recognition was cancelled by the Central Zoo Authority during the period 2019-20 to 2021-22 are given below:S.No.	Year	State	Name of the Zoo1	2021-2022	Karnataka	Tungabhadra Mini Zoo, Hosapete2		Maharashtra	Mahatma Gandhi Zoo, Solapur3		Maharashtra	Snake Park, Dholgarwadi4		Tamil Nadu	VOC Park Zoo, Coimbatore5		Telangana	Deer Park-Kesoram Cement, BasanthnagarS.No.	Year	State	Name of the Zoo1	2020-21	Gujarat	Great Golden Circus2		Karnataka	Children’s Park cum Mini Zoo, Bellari3		Odisha	Municipal Corporation Deer Park, Cuttack4		Andhra Pradesh	GVK Deer Park, JugurupaduS.No.	Year	State	Name of the Zoo1	2019-20	Nil	Nil(e) The other corrective measures taken up by the Central Zoo Authority from time to time for betterment of zoos include:i.	Providing technical assistance for preparation of Master Plans for long-term development of zoosii.	Capacity building programmes for zoo personnel iii.	Issuance of guidelines on various aspects of zoo management.*****
