                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2922






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Status of Developing Tourism Circuits in Andhra Pradesh




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of tourism circuits which have been planned in Andhra Pradesh;(b) 	the details of funding distributed for the development of various tourism circuits for the State of Andhra Pradesh;(c) 	the breakup of the expenditure incurred in each of the component; (d) 	the status of the developing tourism circuits in Andhra Pradesh; and(e)	the deadlines and the exact issues in case there is any backlog in implementing these plans?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			    	      (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	 The Ministry of Tourism under its ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme has sanctioned 03 projects covering Coastal and Buddhist Thematic Circuits in  Andhra Pradesh, which are physically complete. The details of the projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan scheme in the  State of Andhra  Pradesh is  annexed.********
