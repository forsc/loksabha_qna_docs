                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4432






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Warehouse Infrastructure Fund




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether 45 warehouse projects were sanctioned under the Warehouse Infrastructure Fund for Andhra Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the status of these projects, district-wise; (c) 	whether 26 of these warehouse projects have been withdrawn; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	the status of the block level warehouse projects sanctioned under PPP mode through viability gap funding for Andhra Pradesh and Guntur district; and (f) 	the status of the warehouse projects being built by Food Corporation of India (FCI) and Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC) in Andhra Pradesh and Guntur district?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (f): Yes, National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) had sanctioned 45 warehouse projects under Warehouse Infrastructure Fund (WIF -2014-15) to Andhra Pradesh State Warehouse Corporation (APSWC) on 16 March 2017. Out of 45 projects, 26 projects remained Non Starter Projects (NSPs) due to unavailability of land and therefore have been withdrawn.The latest status of the sanctioned projects and the list of deleted projects is at Annexure-I.At present, no warehouse projects are being directly built by Food Corporation of India (FCI) in Andhra Pradesh. However, Construction of warehouse is being undertaken through Private Parties/Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC)/ State Warehousing Corporations (SWCs) and other State Agencies under Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) Scheme. Capacity of 87,800 MT has already been completed and 25,000 MT capacity is under construction as on 28.02.2021, under this scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh.
