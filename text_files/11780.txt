                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

61






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Day Boarding Centres




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS be pleased to state:  (a) the details of the process for setting up of day boarding centres by the Sports Authority of India; (b) the facilities being/proposed to be provided to the players and institutions in such centres; (c) the number of day boarding centres set up in Rajasthan during the last five years, sport-wise and district-wise; and (d) the details of the kit and the quantum of funds provided to the day boarding centres and players from time to time in Rajasthan during the last five years? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)  (a) to (d) A statement is laid on the table of the House.   STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. 61 FOR 06/02/2020 ASKED BY SH. RAHUL KASWAN REGARDING DAY BOARDING CENTRES.    The Sports Authority of India (SAI) does not set up Day Boarding Centres.  SAI runs Regional Training Centres, SAI Training Centres and National Institute of Sports (NIS), Patiala.  These SAI training facilities are available to residential as well as non-residential athletes selected under various sports promotion schemes of the Department of Sports and SAI.  In the State of Rajasthan, SAI is running its training centres at Jodhpur, Alwar and Jaipur.  In these 3 SAI Training Centres, a total of 231 athletes are benefitting which includes 104 day boarders.   *****
