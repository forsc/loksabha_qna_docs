                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1891






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Ayurvedic Hospital




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) the details of the Government Ayurvedic hospitals which are being run in rented buildings in the country including Maharashtra;(b) whether the Government has decided to shift above said hospitals from all such rented buildings and if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether inconvenience is likely to be caused to the people of that area by the above said decision and if so, the details thereof and the corrective steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYURVEDA, YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a): Public health is a State subject and the running of Government Ayurvedic hospitals in the States/UTs including Maharashtra are under the domain of respective State/UT Governments. However, as per the information received from the State/UT Governments, the status of Government Ayurvedic hospitals being run in rented buildings including Maharashtra is at Annexure. (b) & (c): As public health is a State subject, the decision to shift above said hospitals from all such rented buildings come under the purview of respective State/UT Governments.
