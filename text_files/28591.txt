                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

579






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Steel Plants




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State-wise total number of steel plants functioning in public and private sector in the country at present;(b)	whether the Government is working on any scheme for modernisation and expansion of the said plants and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of extent to which the production of steel has been increased in public and private sector during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)	State-wise details of steel plants functioning in public and private sector producing crude steel, and which were operational in the country during 2020-21 are at Annexure. (b)	Steel being a deregulated sector, specific decisions regarding setting up of the steel plants, modernisation and expansion of the steel plants are taken by individual steel companies/investors based on commercial considerations and market dynamics.(c)	Details of production of crude steel by public and private sector in India during the last five years are given below:-Year	Crude steel Production	Public Sector	Private Sector	Total	Qty.(in mt)	Qty.(in mt)	Qty.(in mt)2016-17	18.46	79.48	97.942017-18	19.75	83.38	103.132018-19	21.50	89.43	110.922019-20	20.90	88.23	109.142020-21	19.51	84.03	103.54Source:  Joint Plant Committee (JPC); mt=million tonnes********
