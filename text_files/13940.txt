                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2542






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Export of Handloom




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of countries to which handloom have been exported during each of the last three years as on date; (b) whether the Government has formulated any special plan in view of the rising demand of Indian handlooms globally; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a): 	The details of 20 major countries where handloom items were exported during the last 3 years is placed at Annexure-(b) & (c): Ministry of Textiles participates in various International fairs, Buyer Seller Meet (BSM), Live demo of Handloom weaving etc. with Handloom Exporters / Weavers through Handloom Export Promotion Council (HEPC) to promote export of Handloom Products in the International market. Financial assistance is provided to the  exporters/weavers for participating in the above events. This Ministry has plans to organize two big tickets events in domestic market and scale up 01 international fair in USA/UK besides participating in 12 international events under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) scheme and 04 international fairs under North Eastern Region Textiles Promotion Scheme (NERTPS). The details are as follows: International Fairs under NHDP:S.N.
