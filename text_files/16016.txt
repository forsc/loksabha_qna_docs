                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4476






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Union Government has introduced a new health insurance scheme Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi (RAN) for the benefit of Below Poverty Line (BPL) families;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the special features of the RAN scheme;(d) the extent to which the RAN has been useful to the BPL families; and(e) the total number of BPL families benefited out of this scheme, State/UT-wise during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e):  Under the Umbrella Scheme of Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi, financial assistance is provided to patients belonging to families living below State/UT-wise threshold poverty lines and who are suffering from major life threatening diseases/cancer/rare diseases, for medical treatment at Government hospitals. Financial assistance upto Rs.15 lakh is provided for eligible patients, which is released in the form of ‘one-time grant’ to the Medical Superintendent of the Government hospital where the treatment is being received. Umbrella Scheme of RAN is not an insurance scheme.  The scheme has three components, namely (i) Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi (RAN) – to provide financial assistance to patients suffering from life threatening diseases other than Cancer, (ii) Health Minister’s Cancer Patients Fund (HMCPF) - to provide financial assistance to patients suffering from Cancer; and (iii) Scheme for financial assistance for patients suffering from specified rare diseases. Financial assistance under the scheme is not released to States/UTs, but to the hospitals where eligible patients receive treatment. Number of patients benefitted under HMCPF and RAN during last three years is as under :  Year	Component	No. of patients benefitted2016-17	HMCPF	3109	RAN	11202017-18	HMCPF	3934	RAN	15102018-19	HMCPF	1773	RAN	1090Hospital-wise details of beneficiaries are at Annexure-I and II.
