                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Inter-State Border Dispute




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether several States in the country are involved in inter-state border disputes at present;(b) if so, the reasons therefor including the details of such States;(c) whether the Union Government has received any information of protests or fighting in this regard;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the role of the Union Government in redressing the inter-State border dispute in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI) (a) & (b):  As per available information, there are boundary disputes arising out of demarcation of boundaries and claims and counter claims over territories between Haryana-Himachal Pradesh, UT of Ladakh-Himachal Pradesh, Maharashtra-Karnataka, Assam-Arunachal Pradesh, Assam-Nagaland, Assam-Meghalaya and Assam-Mizoram.-2-L.S.US.Q. NO.1296 FOR 27.07.2021(c) & (d):  Occasional protests and incidents of violence are reported from some of the disputed border areas.  (e):  The approach of the Central Government has consistently been that inter-state disputes can be resolved only with the cooperation of the State Governments concerned and that the Central Government acts only as a facilitator for amicable settlement of the dispute in the spirit of mutual understanding.****
