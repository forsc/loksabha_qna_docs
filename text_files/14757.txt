                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3537






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Andhra Pradesh Rural Inclusive Growth Project




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the World Bank has sanctioned the Andhra Pradesh Rural Inclusive Growth Project with a loan component of US$ 75 million in 2015;(b)	if so, the aims and objectives of the project;(c)	the reasons as to why even after more than four years the project is yet to be completed;(d)	the amount of money which has so far been released and utilized along with and the time and cost overrun of the project; and(e)	the time by which the project is going to be completed at this pace?






ANSWER




                                                                                          MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE                                   (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (e): The World Bank had approved the Andhra Pradesh Rural Inclusive Growth Project with loan amount of US$ 75 million on December 19, 2014. The project is proposed to close on June 30, 2020. The project became effective on February 2, 2016 and is under implementation. The aims and objectives of Project are to enable selected poor households to enhance agricultural incomes and secure increased access to human development services and social entitlements. So far, US$ 47.16 million out of total loan amount has been released and utilized under the Project.
