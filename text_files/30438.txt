                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Projects under Bharatmala Pariyojana




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-
