                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Schemes under Disha Committee




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of schemes and programmes of the Government covered by District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (DISHA);(b) 	whether coordination between the State Government and Central Government has been envisaged as designed in DISHA so that resources are used optimally especially with respect to the central sponsored schemes, if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the details of the outcomes achieved so far especially in the State of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	District DevelopmentCoordination and Monitoring Committee (DISHA) covers all non-statutory schemes of Government of India. However, at present, data of 43 Schemes/Programmesis made available on DISHA Dashboard  and the list of these 43 Schemes are at Annexure. (b)	Yes, Sir. DISHA Committees have been formed to ensure better coordination through review and  monitoring of the non-statutory central schemes to ensure theirefficient and time bound implementation. For this purpose,these Committees have been formed under the Chairpersonship of Hon’ble Members of Parliament and also include elected representatives at all levels and office bearers concerned.The constitution  of DISHA  Committees at the State level,display of data of different schemes at different levels on  DISHA Dashboard etc. are  additional steps for this end. (c)	In Maharashtra, District Level DISHA Committees have been formed in 36 Districts.So far,176 meetings of District Level DISHA Committees have been reported by State of Maharashtra.  The year -wise details are as under: Year	      Number of Meetings2016-17	           552017-18	           542018-19	           292019-20	           182020-21	           20*******
