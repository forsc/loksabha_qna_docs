                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2913






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






FDI Hosting Countries




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether India has moved to top ten FDI hosting countries in the last three years; (b)	 if so, the details of projects, MoUs signed during the last three years and the actual progress made so far in this regard; (c) 	whether it is a fact that during the last three years the average FDI inflows in the country is less than the targeted investments and if so, the details and the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the details of FDI projects with investments during the last three years and the current year, State and department-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): 	As per the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD)’s World Investment Report (WIR) 2017, 2018 & 2019, India has been in the top ten FDI hosting countries during each of the last three years.(b): 	No such data on FDI projects or MoUs is centrally maintained.(c):	 Government does not fix targets for FDI inflows as FDI is largely a matter of private business decisions. FDI inflows depend on a host of factors such as availability of natural resources, market size, infrastructure, general investment climate as well as macro-economic stability and investment decisions of foreign investors.(d): 	The State and department-wise information on FDI projects is not centrally maintained. However, sector-wise details are at Annexure-I.  *******
