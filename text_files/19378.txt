                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

645






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Road Infrastructure Projects in Assam




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched various projects to  boost the road infrastructure in the State of Assam;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the present status of ongoing road projects along with the expected time of their completion?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)  (a) to (c)    Yes, Sir. Total 48 works amounting to Rs. 10036.12 crore in a  length 928.36 km are ongoing  in the state of Assam.  Work wise details are attached at Annexure-I.
