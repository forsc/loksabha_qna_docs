                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2356






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Manual Cleaning of Sewers




Vinod Kumar Sonkar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has proposed to provide financial support for wider acceptance of technologies to ensure no manual cleaning of sewer systems andif so, the details thereof;(b)	 whether the Government has proposed to provide several incentives for development of affordable housing and real estate sector and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has proposed to develop five new smart cities and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the other steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	No, Sir.(b)	The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs(MoHUA) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban with the objective of providing affordable houses to all eligible beneficiaries in urban areas of the country by the year 2022 through four components detailed in Annexure.(c)&(d)	DEPARTMENT for PROMOTION of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) has intimated that the Finance Minister had announced the proposal for developing five new	Smart cities in collaboration with the States.  The sites for these cities would be selected on the basis of availability of contiguous land parcels, allocation of water and power for the project by the State Government and concurrence on the Institutional and Financial structure approved by the Government of India for Industrial Corridor Projects. Project development activities have been initiated in consultation with the respective State Governments.                                       *****
