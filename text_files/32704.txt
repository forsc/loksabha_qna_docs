                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






ROBs in Jaunpur




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that Jaunpur district is surrounded by railway lines from all the sides which leads to large traffic jams in the city;(b)	if so, whether the Government intends to build Rail Over Bridge (ROB) on all the railway crossings in Jaunpur district to tackle the problem of traffic jams;(c)	if so, the details of the ongoing ROB projects in Jaunpur district at present;(d)	the status of each ROB project as on date;(e)	whether any project is lagging behind schedule; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (f): Two Railway lines Zafrabad-Jaunpur City-Sultanpur and Zafrabad-Faizabad pass through the Jaunpur city.  At present there are 7 level crossings (LCs) and 1 ROB for public to cross the tracks.          Two ROBs at LC No. 42A and 43A have been sanctioned by Railways during the year 2016-17 on cost sharing basis with State Govt. of Uttar Pradesh.  However, the work of approaches has not been sanctioned by State Govt.        On the basis of consent of cost sharing, one ROB at LC No. 9B has been shortlisted for sanction during 2022-23.   Consent for cost sharing from State Govt. is awaited in respect of other four ROBs at LC Nos. 4 Spl., 5B, 6A & 38A.  *****
