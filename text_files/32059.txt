                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3659






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Nomination of Members from Tribals




Himadri Singh














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the members nominated from the tribal class in the Executive Council, Finance Committee, Building Committee in Indira Gandhi National Tribal Unversity, Amarkantak, Madhya Pradesh; (b)the details of the teacher appointed against the posts of 07 Professors, 13 Associate Professors and 40 Assistant Professors vide Advertisement No. IGNTU / Rec. Cell/ T-01/2020 dated 07/09/2020 and the number of applications received for these posts; (c)the time by when the handover of all the buildings were given to the University built up to October, 2021; and (d)the credibility of the online entrance test, 2021 conducted for the admission at the undergraduate and postgraduate level in the said University and the details of reasons for the delay of five months in admission?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) The details of members nominated from the Tribal Class in the Executive Council (EC), Finance Committee (FC) and Building Committee (BC) in Indira Gandhi National Tribal University (IGNTU) is as under:-<pre>S. No.	Name of the Council/Committee	Nominated member 1	Executive Council	One2	Finance Committee	None3	Building Committee	One</pre>(b) The details of teachers appointed and total applications received against advertisement no. IGNTU/Rec. Cell/T-01/2020 dated 07.09.2020 are as under:<pre>Post	Advertised	Applications received	AppointedProfessor	22	177	04Associate Professor	41	687	07Assistant Professor	40	2875	19</pre>(c) The University has taken over 13 buildings and 8 buildings are ready for handing over.(d) Remote Proctoring was deployed in the online entrance test, 2021 which enhanced the credibility of the examination.  The admission process was completed following the guidelines of the University Grants Commission in light of COVID-19 pandemic. ******
