                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2919






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Identifying Coal Mining Areas




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has identified those coal mine areas in the country where the Coal India Ltd. has not yet started mining work;(b) if so, the details of such mining areas along with the States where coal areas are situated;(c) the quantum of coal reserves estimated to be found in these coal areas; and(d) the schemes formulated by the Government to start mining in these coal mining areas?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES  (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) to (c) : Coal India Limited has identified 55 future projects to enhance coal production. Out of these,  there are 13 Mines, where mining work is yet to start. The details of such mines with the states and quantum of coal reserves are given below: SL NO	Sub.	STATE	AREA 	RESERVES (in MT)	Name of project / Mine	Type1	ECL	WEST BENGAL	SODEPUR	102.94	Dhangajore	UG2	ECL	JHARKHAND	RAJMAHAL	603.99	Chuperbhita	OC3	ECL	JHARKHAND	RAJMAHAL	188.35	Simlong 	OC4	ECL	WEST BENGAL	SALANPUR	317.69	Itapara	OC5	ECL	JHARKHAND	RAJMAHAL	579.59	Kayada-Chaudhar Gariapani	OC6	ECL	JHARKHAND	RAJMAHAL	1928.17	Brahmani/ Chicropastimal* 	OC7	ECL	JHARKHAND	RAJMAHAL	411.21	Amrakonda Murgadangal* 	OC8	BCCL	JHARKHAND	VIKRAMSHILA	322.16	Pirpainti 	OC9	BCCL	JHARKHAND	VIKRAMSHILA	230.69	Mandar Parvat 	OC10	WCL	MADHYA PRADESH	PATHAKHERA	74.1	Shaktigarh	UG11	WCL	ODISHA	GHOGHARPALLI AND RAMPIA 	806.02	Ghogharpalli and Rampia 	OC12	SECL	MADHYA PRADESH	SOHAGPUR	20.19	DAMINI OC	OC13	SECL	MADHYA PRADESH	SOHAGPUR	11.29	BATURA WEST EXTN	OC (d): Coal India Limited has formulated schemes for all these projects and will be implemented by 2023-24.***
