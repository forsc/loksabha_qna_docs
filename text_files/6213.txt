                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5533






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Press Information Bureau




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Mala Roy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the objectives of the constitution of Press Information Bureau (PIB) by the Government;(b)	whether the PIB has not fulfilled its objectives, if so, the response of the Government thereto;(c)	whether the Government has reviewed the activities of PIB and if so, the date on which such a review was conducted and the outcome thereof; and(d)	whether there is any proposal to ensure that all Governments including press releases issued by PIB shall be made available in all Indian languages, if so, the time by when the same shall be done and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE & MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING                                {SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR}(a)&(b) Press Information Bureau (PIB) is the nodal agency of the Government of India to disseminate information to the people through Media (print, electronic and digital) on Government policies, programmes, initiatives and achievements. PIB is working for the desired objective for which it was constituted.(c)	Publicity Programmes and other activities undertaken by PIB is regularly reviewed by Government at various levels.  (d)	Presently all important Press Releases issued by Press Information Bureau (PIB) are translated into 13 languages.******
