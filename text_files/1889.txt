                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1794






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Status of Roads under PMGSY




Srinivas Kesineni













                                                    Durga Prasad Rao Balli
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of roads constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) including the lengths of roads;(b)	the funds sanctioned and released for each component of roads and the status of their completion during the last four years; (c)	the details of roads being planned/approved/ under consideration for the upcoming projects in the State; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As per the available information, a total of 1,49, 682 roads measuring length of 6,00,884 Km has been constructed across the Country under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) since inception till 26th June, 2019.(b):	The funds sanctioned & released to the States and road length completed during the last four years are as under:-Year(s)	               Sanctioned           Funds released             Road length completed                      (Rs. in crore)	    (Rs. in crore)	        ( in Km)2015-16	               15,186.71	      15,186.71	                  36,4492016-17	               17,584.49	      16,507.85	                  47,4462017-18	                15650.29	       15650.29	                  48,7152018-19	               1,5365.19	      1,5365.19	                  49,038(c) & (d):	All road works measuring length 6,47,646 Km under PMGSY-I has been sanctioned. Under PMGSY-II, against the target of 50,000 Km, 41,127 Km road length has already been sanctioned to 18 States. Further, under Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Areas (RCPLWEA), which is a separate vertical under PMGSY to provide road connectivity in 44 districts of 9 States, which are critical from security angle, 5,066 Km road length has been sanctioned.
