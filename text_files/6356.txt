                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

162






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Funds for Public and Private Projects




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the release of funds for public/private projects has been pending for the last five years and if so, the details thereof and the reasons for the delay in release of funds, State-wise, particularly Bihar;(b) 	whether there is any State-specific reason for the projects being held up and if so, the details thereof;(c)	 whether the pending projects are concentrated across a few sectors and if so, details thereof; and(d)	 the details of measures taken by the Government to revive the stalled private and public projects especially in the key infrastructure sectors?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c): The Union Government allocates funds to the States under Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSSs)/ Central Sector Schemes (CSs) as per scheme guidelines. Most public projects are initiated under approved envelope of CSSs/CSs. Budget is released to State Governments/agencies as per scheme guidelines/GFR. Budgetary provisions are known to Ministries/Departments as soon as Union Budget is approved by the Parliament.  Ministry of Finance make releases towards Externally Aided Projects to State Governments. Details for last 5 years are given at Annexure.(d): The Union Government has set up an institutional mechanism viz. Project Monitoring Group (PMG) for resolving a variety of issues with a view to fast tracking the approvals for setting up and expeditious commissioning of large Public, Private and Public-Private Partnership (PPP) projects. The primary focus of PMG is to expedite the clearances from Central and State Authorities for setting up of projects.                                              *****
