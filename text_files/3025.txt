                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2857






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Railway Connectivity to Char Dham




Ajay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the budget sanctioned for the rail line being laid to connect the Char Dham from Rishikesh, Uttarakhand as on date;(b)	whether the work of railway line has since been started and the details of the length of rail line laid so far; and(c)	whether the said project is likely to be completed on time and if so,    the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	to (c)   Final Location Survey (FLS) has been sanctioned to connect Char Dham, which is under progress.  Further appropriate action shall be taken after FLS is completed.*****
