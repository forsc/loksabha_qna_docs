                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4584






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Unmanned Railway Crossings in Jaunpur




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of unmanned railway crossings in Jaunpur district; (b)	the steps being taken to convert unmanned crossings into manned ones in Jaunpur district;(c)	the details of the number of people who lost their lives in these unmanned railway crossings;(d)	whether any assistance is provided by the Government in case loss of life due to negligence of Railways; and  (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b): There are no unmanned level crossings in Jaunpur district.  All unmanned level crossings (UMLCs) from Indian Railway on Broad Gauge (BG) were eliminated on 31.01.2019 for safety consideration.(c): Nil.(d) & (e): Do not arise.  *****
