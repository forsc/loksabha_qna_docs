                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2698






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Revamping of MGNREGS




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is proposed to revamp Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS) to include more functions under the scheme so that agricultural operations are not affected; and(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	No, Sir. Schedule -I of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (MGNREGA) provides to ensure that atleast 60% of the works to the taken up in a districts in terms of cost shall be for creation of productive assets directly linked to Agriculture and Allied Activities. There are 260 works which are permissible under MGNREGA. Out of these, 164 works are related to agriculture and allied activities.*****
