                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4342






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Job Opportunities for Pharm D Degree Holders




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the steps/measures taken by the Government to encourage the job opportunities of Pharm D degree holders;(b) whether the Government has instructed the States/UTs for amendment of recruitment rules for including Pharm D for post of Pharmacist, approving this qualification for various teaching posts, considering short-term studentship/ scholarship programme for award of fellowship, writing to all accredited hospitals, utilizing the services of Pharm D for high quality pharmaceutical care in clinical departments etc., if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has reviewed the progress made by States/UTs in this regard, if so, the details of the results that have emerged along with the number of Pharm D degree holders employed after acquiring the degree; and (d) whether the Government has also instructed CDSCO, DGHS and States/UTs for inclusion of Pharm D degree for the post of Drug Inspector to widen the scope of employment for Pharm D degree holders, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):    Some of the steps taken by the Government for the Job Opportunities for  Pharm. D Degree Holders are as under:i.	 Ministry of Health & Family Welfare has issued Gazette notification clarifying that a person holding the Pharm. D qualification, being higher qualification, shall automatically become eligible for appointment to various posts where a person holding Diploma in Pharmacy or Bachelor of Pharmacy or Master of Pharmacy qualification is eligible to be appointed.   ii.	 Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) Regulation called “Minimum Qualification for Teachers in Pharmacy Institution Regulations, 2014” have been notified wherein Pharm. D qualification has been included as an approved qualification for various teaching posts in pharmacy institutions in India. iii.	 PCI has also requested various Councils/State Governments to consider Pharm. D as an essential qualification for various posts.iv.	State Governments/UTs, Central Ministries/Departments, etc. have been requested to amend their Recruitment Rules to include Pharm. D as qualification for various pharmacy posts. v.	 National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH) and Quality Council of India have been asked to instruct all accredited hospitals to utilize the services of Pharm. D degree holders for quality services.  vi.	Tata Memorial Centre Advanced Centre for treatment, Research and Education in Cancer has included Pharm. D in One Year Fellowship in Oncotherapeutics.vii.	 PCI is already taking action to amend Pharmacy Practice Regulations, 2015 for inclusion of the post of Clinical Pharmacist with qualification, duties and responsibilities.viii.	University Grants Commission (UGC) has already specified Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm. D) degree as Master’s level under Section 22(3) of the University Grants Commission Act, 1956.ix.	Pharm. D has been included as an essential qualification for Patient Safety – Pharmacovigilance Associates by Indian Pharmacopoeia Commission.x.	Drugs Technical Advisory Board (DTAB) has already agreed •	to amend Rule 49 (Qualifications of Inspectors) of Drugs & Cosmetics Rules, 1945 to include the qualification of Doctor in Pharmacy (Pharm.D)•	to amend the Drugs and Cosmetics Rules, 1945 to include Pharm. D degree as one of the qualifications for competent technical staff for manufacturing and testing of drugs.Data of employment of Pharmacists is not maintained centrally.(d) :  No.………………………
