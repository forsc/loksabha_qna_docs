                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3306






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Welfare Schemes for ASHA Workers




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Accredited Social Health Activists (ASHAs) enrolled, State/UT-wise including Gujarat;(b)	whether the Government intends to provide some career progression path such as reserving some seats in ANM courses, fee subsidy etc. to ASHAs who might be interested in pursuing further studies?(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details of other schemes rolled out for welfare of ASHAs in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR) (a)         ASHAs are envisaged to be community health volunteer and are only entitled to task/activity based incentives. As per NHM-MIS report (as on 30.06.2021), State-wise details of total number of ASHAs under National Health Mission (NHM), including Gujarat, is placed at Annexure-I.(b) & (c) Public Health and Hospitals is a State Subject, therefore the facilities provided to ASHAs including career progression path varies from State to State including the best performing ASHA with aptitude for leadership and mentoring are selected as ASHA Facilitators. As per operational guidelines of Community Processes, ASHA who have formal education of 12th class who are interested and willing they can take admission to ANM/GNM schools as a career progression path. However, specific policies are implemented by the States/UTs for career progression of ASHAs.d)	In the year 2018, the ASHA benefit package was introduced acknowledging significant contribution and commitment of ASHAs. The package providing coverage for:•	Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Beema Yojana (PMJJBY) with a benefit Rs. 2.00 Lakh in case of death of the insured (annual premium of Rs. 330 contributed by GOI).•	Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Beema Yojana (PMSBY) with a benefit of Rs.2.00 lakh for accidental death or permanent disability; Rs. 1.00 lakh for partial disability (annual premium of Rs. 12 contributed by GOI).•	Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan Dhan (PM-SYM) with pension benefit of Rs. 3000 pm after age of 60 years (50% contribution of premium by GOI and 50% by beneficiaries).Under the recently launched Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package, Insurance Scheme has been introduced for all health workers, including ASHAs. This insurance scheme provides an insurance cover of Rs. 50.00 Lakhs in-case of loss of life on account of COVID-19 related duty.
