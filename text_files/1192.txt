                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1054






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Portal for MSMEs




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched a portal psbloansin59minutes.com for providing easier credit to MSMEs;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the loans sanctioned under the scheme so far; (c)	whether the Government has launched a solar charkha scheme for MSME sector; and(d)   	if so, the details thereof along with the progress achieved under the scheme so far ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a):    Yes, Sir.(b):   As on 31.03.2019, more than 50, 706 proposals have got in- principle approval and 27, 893 proposals have been sanctioned.(c)&(d):    The Solar Charkha Scheme for MSME Sector was launched in 2018 with an outlay of Rs.550 crore to set up 50 Solar Charkhas across the country. 19 proposals have been received under the Scheme so far.
