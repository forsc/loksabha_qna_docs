                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1472






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Operational Cold Storages




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of cold storages including those for mangoes operational in the country, as on date, State/ UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh; (b) 	the present capacity of these cold storages, particularly in Uttar Pradesh, district-wise;(c) 	the number of new cold storages of mangoes sanctioned and set up during the last five years, State/ UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh; and (d) 	the reasons for not setting up the sanctioned cold storages so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	District-wise data for Number and Capacity of Cold Storages is not maintained centrally. However, State-wise number and capacity of cold storages including those for mangoes created in the country including Uttar Pradesh as on 31st December, 2019 is at Annexure – I.(c) & (d):	 Assistance is provided for setting up of cold storages in the country under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) scheme of Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare and Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) scheme of Ministry of Food Processing Industries.State/UT-wise number of new cold storages sanctioned during the last five years including those for mangoes in the country including Uttar Pradesh as on 31st December, 2019 is at Annexure – II. All cold storages sanctioned till 2018 – 19 have been setup and cold storages sanctioned during current year are in different stages of construction.
