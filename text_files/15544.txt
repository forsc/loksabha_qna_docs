                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4178






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Renaming of Airports




Rahul Kaswan













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the proposal for changing the names of the airports are pending with the Union Government and if so, the details thereof;(b) the criteria for changing the names of airports and the authority for its monitoring;(c) whether the Union Government proposes to name Jaipur International Airport after Late Shri Bhairon Singh Shekhawat;(d) if so, the time by which the said airport is likely to be renamed and if not, the reasons therefor;(e) whether the Union Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Rajasthan in this regard; and(f) whether the Union Government proposes to construct new airport in Rajasthan and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): A request for naming/renaming of airport is considered by the Union Cabinet based on the recommendations of the concerned State Government supported by a resolution passed in the respective State Legislative Assembly. Proposals for renaming of Vijayawada, Tirupati, Agra, Gorakhpur, Bareilly, Kanpur (Chakeri), Adampur, Shirdi, Kolhapur, Jabalpur, Chandigarh, Dehradun and Imphal airports/ civil enclaves at defence airports, along with the resolution passed in the respective State Legislative Assemblies have been received by the Union Government. (c) to (e): The Union Government has not received any proposal from the State Government of Rajasthan for renaming of Jaipur International Airport after Late Shri Bhairon Singh Shekhawat. (f): The proposal for development of airports at Bhiwadi, Kota and civil enclave at Uttarlai in Rajasthan are at different stages viz. seeking ''in principle'' approval, handing over and mutation of land by State Government  etc. for processing the proposals.
