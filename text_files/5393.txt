                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4923






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






IFFCO in Prayagraj




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the budget allocated for IFFCO, Phulpur in Prayagraj district during the last five years; (b)	whether any irregularities has been found in this regard and if so, the details of the action taken thereon; (c)	whether there is any proposal to increase the production of IFFCO, Phulpur in Prayagraj district;  (d)	the details of the total number of employees in the said unit and the number of permanent and temporary employees out of them;    (e)	the number of permanent and temporary employees appointed during the said period; and (f)	the details of the amount spent on social works by IFFCO, Phulpur, Prayagraj district during the above mentioned period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS.(SHRI  D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)  (a) & (b): No, Sir.  There was no budget allocation to Indian Farmers Fertiliser Cooperative Limited (IFFCO), Phulpur during the last five years.(c):	At present, there is no proposal to increase the production of IFFCO, Phulpur. (d):	The details of the Permanent and Temporary employees in IFFCO, Phulpur as on 15.07.2019 are as below:-Particulars	No. of employeesPermanent 	665Temporary 	22Total 	        687 (e):	The details of the permanent and temporary employeesappointed during the period from 2014-15 to 2018-19 are as below:Year	        Permanent	Temporary2014-15	        7	         02015-16	        4	         22016-17         0	         02017-18	        4	         92018-19	        15	         6(f):	The details of the amount spent on social work by IFFCO, Phulpur during the period from 2014-15 to 2018-19 are as below:-Year	      Amount Spent (In lakh)2014-15	      3862015-16	      3762016-17	      4382017-18	      4912018-19	      464*********
