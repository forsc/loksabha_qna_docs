                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1653






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Welfare of Civilians in Cantonment




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to provide financial assistance for the welfare of civilians residing in cantonment area and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to set up a high level committee to take note and solve the problems of the civic population residing in these areas and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps taken by the Government or envisaged to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) The Central Government provides financial assistance in the form of ordinary Grant-in-Aid to deficit Cantonment Boards.  During 2019-20, 56 Cantonment Boards out of 62 received such Grant-in-Aid amounting to Rs.361.60 crores.  In addition to the above, grants for creation of capital assets to the tune of Rs. 10 crores have been given to Cantonment Boards for undertaking various infrastructural projects.(b) & (c):  To address the issues of residents of Cantonments, Ministry of Defence constituted an Expert Committee on 31.08.2018 on Working of Cantonment Boards.  Based on Expert Committees’ report, the Ministry of Defence has taken decisions on a number of issues relating to defining repairs of houses in Cantonment areas, allowing construction of individual w.c/toilets in authorized houses for the benefit of residents and constitution of specific three Committees of Cantonment Boards namely Finance Committee, Education Committee and Health & Environment Committee for greater democratization in the Cantonment.*****
