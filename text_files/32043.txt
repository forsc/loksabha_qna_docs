                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3436






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Tests conducted for TB




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the total number of tests conducted by the Government for TB in the past five years, State/ UT – wise, including Bihar;b)	whether the Government has any data on the total number of TB tests conducted and cases reported by private hospitals and testing centres;c)	if so, the details of such tests conducted in the past five years; State / UT – wise, including Bihar;d)	whether the Government has specified the mechanism and details for collaborations between State Governments and NGOs/ private organisation for TB testing and reporting; and e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)a)	The total number of tests conducted by the Government for TB in the past five years, State/ UT – wise, including Bihar is enclosed as Annex-I.b)	& (c) The total number of TB cases reported by private hospitals and testing centres is enclosed as Annex-II.d) & (e) The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has developed a “Guidance Document on	Partnerships - 2019” which specifies the mechanism and details for collaboration of NGOs 	/ private organization under the programme. The document is available in the following 	link - https://tbcindia.gov.in/showfile.php?lid=3456.
