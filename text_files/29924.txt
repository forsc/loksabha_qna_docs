                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1580TO BE ANSWERED ON 07.12.2021IMPLEMENTATION OF DAPSC †1580. SHRIMATI SANDHYA RAY: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the fund allocated and sanctioned during the current year under Development Action Plan for Scheduled Castes (DAPSC) scheme for the development of Scheduled Caste community; (b) whether the Government is monitoring the proper utilization of said DAPSC funds from time to time, if so, the details thereof; and (c) the schemes initiated by the Government for betterment of SC communities and differently abled persons?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR) (a): The funds allocated and sanctioned during the current year under Development Action Plan for Scheduled Castes (DAPSC) Scheme for the development of Scheduled Caste Community is given at Annexure-I (Statement 10A of Budget 2021-22).(b): Yes, the government is monitoring the proper utilization of the said DAPSC funds from time to time.  Since, it being a monitoring framework, meetings under the chairmanship of HMSJE are organized from time to time to review the allocated budget and its proper utilization by the Ministries/Departments, to which it has been allocated. The last of these meetings were held on 8th and 9th November, 2021. Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment is the Nodal Ministry for Monitoring the Schemes for the benefits of Scheduled Castes formulated by various Ministries/Departments which constitute the DAPSC Scheme.  In order to monitor the allocated budget and the utilization of funds by the Ministries/Departments an e-portal developed in the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment with the name E-Utthaan is capturing the various parameters of the Schemes being implemented by Ministries/Department under DAPSC.(c)  This Ministry has been given the mandate of monitoring of DAPSC Programme which includes schemes framed by various Ministries/Departments for the benefits of Scheduled Castes communities. The Schemes under the ambit of DAPSC of the  Ministries/Department for the year 2021-22 as culled out from E-Utthaan is given at Annexure-II.
