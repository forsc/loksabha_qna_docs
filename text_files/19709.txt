                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1197






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






PMS for SC Students




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1197 TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021PMS FOR SC STUDENTS †1197. SHRIMATI SHARDABEN ANILBHAI PATEL: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of the annual target fixed to provide scholarships to the students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SCs) under the Post Matric Scholarship Scheme in the country; (b) the basic criteria to provide Post Matric Scholarship to the students belonging to Scheduled Castes; (c) whether Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for the Scheduled Castes students in the country is being adversely affected due to non-availability of funds; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) the reaction of the Government thereto? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a):   The scheme is demand driven/open ended as regards to eligible beneficiaries, institutions, courses and reimbursement of fees. Hence, the nature of the scheme is such that physical targets cannot be fixed.(b):  The basic criteria for the students to avail the Post Matric Scholarship to the students belonging to Scheduled Castes are:-(i) The scholarship holder should be an Indian National;(ii) The income from all sources of the parents/guardians of the applicant should not exceed        Rs. 2.5 lakh; and(iii) The scholarships will be given for the study of all recognized post-matriculation or post secondary courses pursued in recognized Institutions/Universities and Colleges as per the scheme guidelines.(c):   No sir.(d) & (e):   Does not arise in view of (c) above.*******
