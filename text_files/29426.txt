                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1120






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






First and Second Dose of COVID-19 Vaccination




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar













                                                    Ranjeeta Koli
                                                








                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    P.R. Natarajan
                                                








                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of COVID-19 vaccination in the country including the number of vaccine doses given so far, State/UT/district-wise including tribal areas of Dahod, Gujarat;(b)	the number of first dose and second dose of COVID-19 vaccines administered, State/UT-wise and the targeted time by which both the doses of COVID-19 is likely to be administered across the country;(c)	the number of people administered free vaccination doses in Government centres and those administered vaccine on payment basis in private centres;(d)	whether there is a need to administer booster dose after giving two doses of vaccines, if so, the reaction of the Government thereof;(e)	the status of COVID-19 vaccination to the children below the age of 18 years; and(f)	whether the Government is having sufficient stock of COVID-19vaccination and have also additionally imported COVID-19 vaccines, if so, the details of the such imported vaccines, item/country/price-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (f)As on 29th November 2021, a total of 122.42 crore doses has been administered. TheState/UT-wise coverage of eligible beneficiaries with 1st and 2nd dose of COVID-19 vaccine is placed at Annexure-1.It is the endeavor to vaccinate all eligible 1st dose and due 2nd dose beneficiaries aged 18 years and above by December 2021.As on 29th November 2021, a total of 26,07,940 vaccine doses administered in the Dahod District of Gujarat.From 1st May’21till date a total of 102.38 crore doses (96% of the total doses) of COVID-19 Vaccine have been administered free of cost at Government CVCs while only 4.08 crore doses (4% of the total doses) were administered at Private CVCs.The need to administer booster dose is deliberated by the National Technical Advisory Group on Immunization (NTAGI) and National Expert Group on vaccine administration for Covid (NEGVAC) based on scientific evidence.Government of India is supplying Covishield and Covaxin manufactured in India under Covid-19 vaccination programme and sufficient stock is available with States/UTs. Government of India has not imported any COVID-19 vaccine for free supply to States/UTs under National COVID-19 Vaccination program. However, permission has been granted for import of Sputnik vaccine and is being administered in private CVCs.****
