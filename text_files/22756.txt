                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3704






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Six-lanning Projects




Manjulata Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the estimated cost of the six-lane expansion works of NH-16 from Chandikhole to Bhadrak and from Bhadrak to Balasore;(b) 	the time by which the said expansion work is likely to be completed; and(c) 	the details of agencies assigned the job for execution? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c)	The details of sanctioned costs, completion dates and assigned agencies for the six-lane expansion works of NH-16 from Chandikhole to Bhadrak and from Bhadrak to Balasore are as under:S.No.	Name of work	Sanctioned cost(Rs. crore)	Name of agency	Date of Completion1.	6-laning of  NH-16 from Chandikhole to Bhadrak	1230.55	M/s DBL Chandikhole Bhadrak Highway Pvt Ltd.	December, 20212.	6-laning of  NH-16 from Bhadrak to Balasore	844.67	M/s BG Bhadrak Baleshwar Highway Pvt Ltd. 	March, 2022*****
