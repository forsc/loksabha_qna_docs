                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






National Highway Projects




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of proposed work of National Highways (NHs) in the States of Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Karnataka, Gujarat and Chhattisgarh during the period from 2022 to 2024; (b) the details of tenders awarded in November-December, 2020 and January, 2021 for the said works in the above States; (c) whether the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has a policy of not awarding work which is above the estimated cost; (d)  if so, the details thereof; and (e) if not, the details of tenders awarded which were above the estimated cost during 2019 and 2020?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
