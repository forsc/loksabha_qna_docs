                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1989






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Financial Assistance to BPL Families




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government provides financial assistance under the provisions of Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi and other components like Health Ministers, Discretionary Grant, Cancer Fund etc. to patients belonging to families living Below Poverty Line (BPL)  for medical treatment; (b) if so, details of schemes programmes along with  beneficiaries thereunder during the last three years,  State/UT-wise; (c) whether the procedures to get assistance under these Funds are cumbersome and time consuming; (d) if so, whether there is urgent need to make these  schemes more transparent and accessible to patients  through online portal; and (e) if so, the necessary steps being taken by the  Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (b) Yes, The Government is implementing Umbrella Scheme of Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi to provide financial assistance to patients belonging to families living below State/UT-wise threshold poverty line.  Financial assistance upto Rs.15 lakh is provided for eligible patients and  released in the form of ‘one-time grant’ to the Medical Superintendent of the Government hospital where the treatment is being received. The scheme has following three components:(i)   Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi– to provide financial assistance to poor patients living below threshold poverty line and suffering from life threatening diseases relating to heart, kidney, liver, etc  for their treatment at Government hospitals/institutes having super specialty facilities.(ii)  Health Minister’s Cancer Patient Fund – to provide financial assistance to poor patients living below threshold poverty line and suffering from cancer, for their treatment at Regional Cancer Centres (RCCs)/Tertiary Care Cancer Centres (TCCC) and State Cancer Institutes (SCI).(iii)	Financial assistance Scheme for poor patients suffering from rare diseases – to provide financial assistance to poor patients living below threshold poverty line and suffering from specified rare diseases for their treatment at Government hospitals/institutes having super specialty facilities.          General criteria governing the Umbrella Scheme of Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi is at Annexure-I. Financial assistance for eligible patients is released to treating hospitals.  There is no State/UT-wise allocation of funds under RAN scheme.  Hospital-wise amount of assistance provided and number of patients benefitted under the Scheme during the last three years is at Annexure-II.           Under the Health Minister’s Discretionary Grant (HMDG), a maximum amount of Rs.1,25,000/- is provided to patients whose annual income does not exceed Rs.1,25,000/-, to defray a part of the expenditure on hospitalization/treatment in Government Hospitals.     .  Hospital-wise amount of assistance provided and number of patients benefitted under the Scheme during the last three years is at Annexure-III.(c) to (e): The procedures to get financial assistance under RAN and HMDG schemes have been simplified and benefits under these schemes are accessible and provided timely to patients.
