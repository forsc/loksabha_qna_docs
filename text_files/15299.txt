                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3970






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Survey of Projects under MPLAD Scheme




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposed to conduct any study/ survey across the country regarding implementation of projects under Member of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme (MPLADS); (b) if so, the details and the outcome thereof; (c) the funds allocated and released under the said scheme during each of the last three years and the current year; (d)  the details of funds utilised/ spent with regard to the above projects, State/ UT-wise during the said period; and (e) the total number of projects completed under the said scheme during the last three years, State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) and (b) The Ministry undertakes study/ survey of works completed under Members of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme ( MPLADS)  from time to time. Last such study/ survey was conducted in selected district through independent agencies in the year 2012-13. During the current year also it is proposed to undertake the same for evaluation of the scheme. (c)  The funds allocated and released  for last three years and current year upto 29.02.2020 under the MPLADS is as follows:-                                                                  (Rs. in crore )    Financial  year 	             Budgetary allocation 	Amount released        2016-17	                            3950.00	           3499.50        2017-18	                            3950.00	           3504.00        2018-19	                            3950.00	           3949.50        2019-20 ( as on 29.02.2020)	    3950.00	           3242.50(d) and (e)	Details of funds utilized/ spent and works completed,  State/ UT wise  ( as per data provided by District Authority through MPLADS portal) during the last three years including current year is annexed.                                           *****
