                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4108






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






BSNL Network in Gujarat and Haryana




Arvind Kumar Sharma













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of villages in Haryana and Bhavnagar and Botad districts of Gujarat where Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) network is still not available; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to formulate any scheme to bring network coverage to the said areas; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) to (c) 	Number of villages uncovered by mobile network of Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) in Haryana and Bhavnagar and Botad districts of Gujarat is as under:<pre>S.No.	State/District	Number of uncovered villages1.	Haryana State	23702.	Bhavnagar District, Gujarat	193	Botad District, Gujarat	4</pre>However, as per the data collected from the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) and Department of Telecommunications (DoT) field units in 2019, only one (1) village in the State of Haryana and 13 villages in the Bhavnagar District, Gujarat are uncovered by mobile network. All villages in Botad District, Gujarat are covered by mobile network.Mobile coverage in the uncovered villages in the country is being provided by the Government and the TSPs in a phased manner. 70 uncovered villages in Gujarat State are targeted to be covered with mobile network under 354 uncovered villages scheme funded by the Universal Service Obligation Fund ( USOF).**********
