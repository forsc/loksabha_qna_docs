                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2426






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Construction of Fishing Harbour in Andhra Pradesh




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the time by which the State Government of Andhra Pradesh submitted proposal for construction of a fishing harbour in Nizampatnam in Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh; (b) 	whether it is true that the project cost is estimated to be Rs. 340 crore; and(c) 	if so, the status of the project and the time by which it is likely to be completed and operational?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (c) : The Government of Andhra Pradesh has submitted a proposal for construction of fishing harbour at Nizampatnam in Guntur District at an estimated cost of Rs. 340.78 crore and sought the concessional finance under the Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF). The proposal of Government of Andhra Pradesh for construction of the fishing harbour at Nizampatnam at an estimated cost of Rs 340.78 crore has been recommended restricting the interest subvention upto 3% per annum by the Government of India for the project cost of Rs 150 crore under the provisions of Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF).*****
