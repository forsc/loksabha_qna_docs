                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3625






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Bankrupt Companies




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether several major companies operating in various sectors in the country are on the verge of insolvency as can be seen from the fact that Jet Airways has already gone bankrupt and Air India, BSNL, MTNL, Vodafone, Airtel, banks and the automobile sector is going through a financial crisis and if so, the details thereof, company-wise; (b) whether the onus for the said situation lies upon the wrong economic policy of the Government or elsewhere and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has any study report in this regard; and (d) if so, the details thereof along with the remedial steps taken by the Government to rectify the situation?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS	                                (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)????? ??? ????????? ????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? 		  	            (???? ?????? ???? ?????)(a)to (d):  No Sir, the term “Major companies” are not defined under the Companies Act. However, there are some companies against which insolvency proceedings have been initiated under the provisions of the Companies Act. As per the records of the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) Jet Airways (India) Limited is “Under Corporate Insolvency Resolution Process” before the Hon’ble Tribunal, Airtel Broadband Services Private Limited is amalgamated. Air India Limited, Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited (MTNL), Vodafone Idea Limited are active companies and filed Statutory Returns including Financial Statements up to the Financial Years 2019 except Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL), which has filed the same documents till 2018. MCA administers only Companies Act/Limited Liability Partnership Act and Banks are not under the Jurisdiction of the MCA. However, Ministry of Finance (MOF), Department of Economic Affairs (DEA) has provided NIL information in this regard.*******
