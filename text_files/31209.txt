                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3000






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Rooftop Solar System




Tirath Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has achieved target with regard to the installation of rooftop solar system in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c) if not, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c) Government of India approved Rooftop Solar Programme Phase-II with an objective to achieve 40 GW of RTS capacity in the country by 2022. The programme envisages installation of 4,000 MW of RTS capacity in the residential sector by providing Central Financial Assistance and incentivizing distribution companies for achievement in total RTS capacity addition above yearwise baseline capacity.Against the target of 4,000 MW RTS capacity for residential sector, over 3,300 MW capacity has been allocated to various Discoms/ State Implementing Agencies based on the demand received from them, of which over 1132 MW capacity has been reported installed. The state-wise details in this regard are given at Annexure. The implementation of programme has been significantly affected due to Covid-19 pandemic. Ministry has already given extension of five months on account of first wave of Covid-19 pandemic and another four months on account of second wave of Covid-19 pandemic. Some of the States, have requested for further extension in the execution of time-line. *****
