                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1779






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Funds for NH-207A in Nagaland




Tokheho Yepthomi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds provided for National Highway-207A in Nagaland during the years 2018-19 and 2019-20; (b)	whether the Government proposes to provide funds for two-laning of the said highway; and(c)	if so, the details thereof along with the time frame set for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	There is no National Highway (NH)-207A in Nagaland but there is NH-702A in Nagaland.  Ministry allocates funds State wise not NH-wise.  Allocation/Expenditure made for Maintenance & Development of NHs in Nagaland during 2018-19 & 2019-20 are Rs. 726.52 crore & Rs. 462.54 crore respectively.(b) & (c) Two laning of NH-702A in 82 km length is in progress and 2 laning of the balance stretch will be taken up based on inter-se priority, traffic density & availability of funds.*****
