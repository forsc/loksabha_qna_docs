                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Construction of Jetty in Navi Mumbai




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether approval to start the construction work of a jetty in Bhaucha Dhakka and Mandwa including Nerul area of Navi Mumbai in Maharashtra has been granted for transportation through waterways; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the manner and the agency by which the construction work of the jetty has been started in these three places; (c) the total funds provided in this regard; (d) whether the construction work was monitored by a local representative to complete these works at a rapid pace; and (e) if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        (a&b) Yes Sir.  Construction of Ro Ro Jetty & allied facilities at Mandwa and Ro Ro terminal at Bhaucha Dhakka  were undertaken by Maharashtra Maritime Board (MMB) and Mumbai Port Trust(MbPT) respectively. The construction work of jetty at Nerul area of Navi Mumbai  has been undertaken by City and Industrial Development Corporation of Maharashtra (CIDCO). Mumbai Port Trust awarded the work of  Ro Ro terminal at Bhaucha Dhakka through a transparent tendering process. The works of construction of Ro Ro Jetty and allied facilities at Mandwa and construction of jetty at Nerul, Navi Mumbai were also awarded by inviting e-tenders by MMB and CIDCO respectively.(c)	Total fund provided for the projects is as under:<pre>S.No	Project	funds provided ( in Crore)	By1	Ro Ro Jetty at Mandwa and allied facilities	148.73	MMB & Ministry of Shipping2	Ro Ro terminal at Ferry Wharf (Bhaucha Dhakka)	27.00	MbPT3.	Jetty at Nerul, Navi Mumbai	111.00	CIDCO                                                       </pre>(d &e)	  The works of Ro Ro Jetty & allied facilities at Mandwa and Ro Ro terminal at Bhaucha Dhakka have been completed. The work of Ro Ro terminal at Bhaucha Dhakka was monitored by Civil Engineering Department of MbPT along with third party external agency. The works of Ro Ro Jetty & allied facilities at Mandwa and jetty at Nerul, Navi Mumbai were monitored by local representatives. Shri Rajanji Vichare, Hon’ble MP from Thane Lok Sabha visited the project site of jetty at Nerul, Navi Mumbai on 13.07.2019 and 24.01.2020 respectively.                                                *****
