                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

51






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Eklavya Model Residential Schools




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Dinesh Chandra Yadav
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently reviewed the functioning of Eklavya Model Residential Schools scheme in the country and if so, the details of the said review and the outcomes thereof along with the details of the number of Eklavya schools functioning in all the States;(b) the location-wise number of Eklavya Vidyalayas opened in Rajasthan and Gujarat during the last three years and the details of Eklavya Vidyalayas likely to be opened during the remaining period of the present scheme;(c) the details of establishment/construction of buildings/hostels for Eklavya Model Residential Schools in the country including Rajasthan and Gujarat; and(d) whether the Government is contemplating to open Eklavya Model Residential Schools in all the tribal tehsils of Rajasthan as well as in educationally backward districts and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes, Sir. This Ministry alongwith National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) i.e. an autonomous body under this Ministry, has been regularly monitoring the status of functioning of EMRSs like quality of education, available infrastructure in schools, training program for teachers etc.   NESTS has also conducted multiple teachers’ trainings to enhance the quality of education in collaboration with National Apex bodies like NIEPA, NCERT etc., NESTS is also facilitating CBSE affiliation of EMRSs for maintaining the uniform quality standards. So far out of 367 functional EMRSs across the country, 231 EMRSs are affiliated with CBSE and 47 have applied for CBSE affiliation and effort is going on for CBSE affiliation for other schools.(b)	The location wise number of EMRSs sanctioned during the last 3 years and EMRSs to be sanctioned under revamped scheme for the State of Rajasthan and Gujarat is at Annexure I and Annexure II respectively.(c)	Each Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) is envisaged to be set up with capacity of 480 students per school to provide quality education to ST students from class VI to XII. Accordingly, provision has been made for Hostels for 480 Students (240 Girls Students and 240 Boys Students).  A statement indicating State-wise details of construction status of EMRSs in the country including Rajasthan and Gujarat is at Annexure-III.(d)	It has been decided by the Government every block with more than 50% ST population and at least 20,000 tribal persons as per census 2011, is scheduled to have an Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) subject to availability of suitable land by State Government. A total number of 31 EMRSs have already been sanctioned for the state of Rajasthan out of which 21 EMRSs are reported to be functional.Annexure IAnnexure-I as referred in part (b) in Answer to Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.†51 for 29.11.2021 raised by Shri Sumedhananad Saraswati, Shri Dinesh Chandra Yadav, Shrimati Rathva Gitaben Vajesingbhai regarding "Eklavya Model Residential Schools".
