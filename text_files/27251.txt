                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2772






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






All Weather Road Connectivity




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of progress achieved in connecting Union Territory of Ladakh through all weather road axis in winters; (b) the status of infrastructure projects of all weather connectivity in the State of Arunachal Pradesh; (c) the extent to which the development of road infrastructure in the State of Uttarakhand is likely to facilitate tourism such as Char Dham Yatra and steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (d) the benefits likely to accrue from such development to civilians residing in border areas?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	the status of progress achieved in connecting Union Territory of Ladakh through all weather road axis in winters is annexed as Annexure ‘A’.(b)	the status of infrastructure projects of all weather connectivity in the State of Arunachal Pradesh is annexed as Annexure ‘B-1’, ‘B-2’ and ‘B-3’.(c)	The development of road infrastructure in  the state of Uttarakhand will facilitate tourism such as  Char Dham Yatra is as under :-(i) Connectivity between major tourist places as a direct connectivity or as a feeder route.(ii) Overall economic development of undeveloped area, increase in employment and business opportunities.(iii) Lower transport cost.(iv) Improved road transport corridors.(v) Mitigation measures/stabilization of chronic landslide & sinking spots/zones to avoid landslides.(d)	 The benefits likely to accrue from  development of road infrastructure in border areas will:-(i) Provide connectivity to strategically important towns/villages for faster movement of Security Forces.(ii) Provide timely access to medical facilities.(iii) Usher in over-all social and economic development of the region.
