                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

488






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Funds for NESIDS




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the amount of funds released for North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS) and amount unused, State-wise;(b) 	whether there is emphasis on the construction of buildings of importance to human development such as schools and hospitals and if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the number of projects currently approved under the scheme and the percentage completion of the projects, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh] (a)	The details of funds released under the North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS) , State-wise, are given below:-<pre>(Rs. in crore)S. No.	State	Funds released1.	Arunachal Pradesh	12.022.	Assam	73.113.	Manipur	30.594.	Meghalaya	24.225.	Mizoram	71.376.	Nagaland	0.907.	Sikkim	41.328.	Tripura	0.30 	Total	253.83</pre>Utilization Certificates (UCs) of the funds released under NESIDS have not yet become due.(b)	The scope of this scheme, inter alia, also covers creation of infrastructure in the areas of primary and secondary sectors of education and health. Three (03) projects costing Rs.36.33 crore under education sector and four (04) projects costing Rs.75.96 crore under health sector have been sanctioned.(c)	55 projects have been sanctioned under this scheme.  The state-wise details of the projects are as under:- <pre>S. No.	State	    No. of projects1.	Arunachal Pradesh	162.	Assam	83.	Manipur	84.	Meghalaya	65.	Mizoram	36.	Nagaland	97.	Sikkim	28.	Tripura	3 	Total	55</pre>None of the projects under the scheme have become due for the completion as yet.
