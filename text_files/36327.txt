                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3437






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Promotion of Handloom Sector




Girish Chandra














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps to promote Uttar Pradesh’s rich handloom sector on the 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi; (b) if so, the details thereof and (c) the names of programmes developed and the funds allocated therefor by Government in this regard?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (c):	The Government of India and Uttar Pradesh Government undertook various activities to promote handloom sector across the country on the 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi. Weavers’ Service Centres (WSCs) at Varanasi and Meerut organised the following activities:•	Live demonstration for spreading awareness about using environment-friendly, safe for skin vegetable dyes.•	Showcasing use of khadi yarns, display of khadi dress materials and stoles prepared by WSC, Varanasi.•	Live demonstration of khadi weaving.•	Dissemination of information on Handloom schemes implemented by Ministry of Textiles.•	An exhibition was organized by Khadi and Village Industries Commission, Varanasi on 2nd October 2019. WSC Varanasi participated in the exhibition.Further, to promote the handlooms and welfare of weavers across the country, Ministry of Textiles is implementing the following schemes:1)	National Handloom Development Programme;2)	Raw Material Supply Scheme;Under the above schemes, financial assistance is provided to the eligible handloom agencies/weavers for raw materials, common infrastructure development, marketing of handloom products in domestic/overseas markets, Weaver MUDRA Loans etc. ***
