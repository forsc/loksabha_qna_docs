                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4537






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Soya Beans Cultivation




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	     whether the Government is implementing any Centrally Sponsored Schemes for the development of Soya beans cultivation in the country.(b)	if so, the details thereof and the financial assistance provided during each of the last three years;(c)	whether the country has large potential for its growth of Soya beans cultivation; and(d)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):  Yes sir. The Government is implementing centrally sponsored scheme of National Food     Security Mission (Oilseeds & Oil Palm) from 2018-19 in the country with the objective to increase the production and productivity of edible oils including Soybean crop.(b):	          A statement  indicating state-wise financial allocation, release and expenditure from   2017-18 to 2019-20 under NFSM (OS&OP) is enclosed at Annexure-I(c) &(d):	  Yes sir. Soybean is cultivated in various states of the country. A statement    indicating state-wise area, production and yield of soybean for three years (2018-19 to 2020-21) is enclosed at Annexure-II.
