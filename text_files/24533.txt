                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Impact of Pandemic on Direct Agricultural Marketing




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether direct agricultural marketing was adversely affected during the 	lockdown period in the year 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the details of the number of farmers linked to the Kisan Rath Mobile app so far 	and the number of farmers benefited, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	No Sir.  In view of the above, the question does not arise. (c):	 A total of 4,85,103 farmers are registered on the Kisan Rath Mobile Application. A farmer linked to Kisan Rath may benefit through various options available on the app such as posting load, receiving response against these loads, viewing partial loads nearby, viewing service providers nearby.  However, the state-wise status of registration of farmers on Kisan rath at Annexure-I.
