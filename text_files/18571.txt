                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

216






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Progress of Gram Swaraj Abhiyan




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government will inform the house regarding the progress of Gram Swaraj Abhiyan in Odisha. (b)	the number of village people in the State who have benefited under different segments, the details thereof, district-wise and provide figures thereto; and (c)	the number of villages which are yet to benefited therefrom?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c) : Gram Swaraj Abhiyan” was organized from 14th April to 5th May, 2018 under the aegis of Department of Rural Development and the same was extended to 117 Aspirational Districts from 1st June, 2018. As a special endeavor during the Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, efforts were made to saturate the eligible households /persons under flagship pro-poor programmes namely, Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana, PM Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana (Saubhagya), Ujala scheme, PM Jan Dhan Yojana, PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana, PM Suraksha Bima Yojana and Mission Indradhanush. As per the information provided by the Department of Rural Development, the district-wise number of beneficiaries for different segment under the extended Gram Swaraj Abhiyan in the State of Odisha is at Annexure.   In these districts the numbers of beneficiary have already saturated except in the case of PM Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana (Saubhagya).  *****
