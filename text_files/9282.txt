                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2884






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Construction of Multi-purpose Indoor Hall




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposed for construction of multipurpose indoor hall in Nasik district of Maharashtra;(b) if so, the details and the current status thereof; and(c) the funds released/utilised for the same?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) to (c) Information relating to infrastructure projects sanctioned in Nasik district, Maharashtra is at Annexure I. At present, no proposal for construction of multipurpose indoor hall in Nasik district of Maharashtra is under consideration of this Ministry.*********
