                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Operating Efficiency of Railways




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Railways is not able to meet its operating cost of passengers and other coaching services;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor, zone/State-wise;(c)	the details of the losses of Railways during each of the last three years and the current year on account of passengers fare;(d)	whether the profit earned from freight services is used to compensate the loss incurred on passenger and coaching services and if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the other corrective measures taken/being taken by the Railways to make it a profitable entity?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
