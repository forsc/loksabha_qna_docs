                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1152






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Nodal Agencies for CSR Funds




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the companies are fully and appropriately utilising the CSR budgets;(b) if so, the details of CSR budget mandated and the nodal agencies commissioned to complete these projects during the last three years, State-wise and project wise;(c) whether the Government has obtained the names of the incomplete projects and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether any action has been taken against the nodal agencies for not completing these projects on time and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE    AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                          (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)    ????? ??? ???????? ????? ???????? ??? ????? ??????                                (???? ?????? ???? ?????)(a) to (d):  Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) is a Board driven process and the Board of the company is empowered to plan, decide, execute and monitor the CSR activities of the company based on the recommendation of its CSR committee. A company can also undertake its CSR activities through a registered trust or a registered society or a Section 8 company as per Rule 4(2) of the Companies (Corporate Social Responsibility Policy) Rules, 2014. The entire CSR architecture is disclosure based and CSR mandated companies are required to file details of CSR initiatives annually in MCA21 registry. State/UT-wise and Sector-wise details of activities as filed by companies in the MCA 21 registry are at Annexure - I and Annexure - II respectively. Completion status of CSR projects or activities are not maintained by the government. Whenever any violation of CSR provisions is reported, action against such non-compliant Companies are initiated as per provisions of Companies Act, 2013 after due examination of records and following due process of law. ******Annexure - IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1152STATE/UT-WISE CSR EXPENDITURE (IN RS.
