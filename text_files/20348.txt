                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Survey of Karnaprayag-Joshimath Railway Line Project




Tirath Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether there is a proposal to connect Karnaprayag to Joshimath and Rudraprayag to Sonprayag;(b)   if so, whether the survey for the said project has been completed;(c)	whether there is any proposal to connect Ramnagar to Chaukhutiya and Chaukhutiya to Gairsain;(d)	if so, the details thereof and whether the survey of the said project has been completed; and(e)	if not, the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	&  (b):  Rudraprayag is a proposed station in ongoing Rishikesh-Karnaprayag New line project.  Beyond Karnaprayag, survey for connectivity to Joshimath and Sonprayag has been taken up.(b)	to  (e) Survey for construction of new line between Ramnagar and Chaukhutia with extension up to Gairsen (211.96 km) has been completed.  The project could not be taken forward; being financially unviable.*****
