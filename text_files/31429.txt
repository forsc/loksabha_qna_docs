                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2593






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Shortage of Funds under MGNREGS




Dulal Chandra Goswami













                                                    Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru
                                                








                                                    P. Velusamy
                                                








                                                    Pramila Bisoyi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has allocated Rs. 73,000 crores at Budget Estimate stage for the current financial year 2021-22 for Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) which is an increase of Rs. 11,500 crores as compared to Budget Estimate for the financial year 2020-21;(b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the actual amount /funds spent, released, sanctioned and utilized under the scheme as on date and the unspent balance not utilized during the last three years, year-wise, Statewise particularly the States of Bihar and Andhra Pradesh;(c) 	whether the Government has any information about the shortage of funds under the scheme and if so, the details thereof, State-wise including the State of Andhra Pradesh;(d) 	whether the Government will provide additional funds under this scheme in case of any shortfall in completing the scheme for the current financial year and if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	the details of the demand projection of funds under MGNREGS for the ongoing financial year, Statewise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):       Yes, Sir. (b):   	State/UT-wise (including for Bihar and Andhra Pradesh), the details of funds released for wage, material & administrative components under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) in last three financial years 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 is given at Annexure-I.  State/UT-wise (including Bihar and Andhra Pradesh), the details of unspent balance under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in last three finacial years 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 is given at Annexure-II.  (c) to (e) : Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is a demand driven wage employment programme and funds are released to the States/UTs on the basis of "agreed to" Labour Budget (LB) and performance of the States/UTs during the financial year. Fund release to States/UTs is a continuous process and Central Government is committed to making funds available keeping in view, the demand for work.  Further, the funds release under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is based on the provisions of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) and guidelines. As per guidelines, the first installment of the first tranche is released in the first half of April after adjusting unspent balance available with the States and considering the pending liabilities, if any. The second tranche is released on submission of proposal in the prescribed format by the State and subject to fulfillment of all the prescribed conditions. The proposal can be submitted after a State has utilized 75 percent of the total available funds. The funds for second tranche on and after 1st October can only be released after receipt of Audited Utilization Certificate and Audit Report of previous financial year.  There is a budget allocation of Rs.73000 crore under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during FY 2021-22 at Budget Estimate (BE) stage. Recently, Ministry of Finance allocated additional funds of Rs.10,000 crore  for Mahatma Gandhi NREGS as an interim measure. Further, allocation may be made upon assessment of demand during the Revised Estimate (RE) stage. ******
