                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Sick MSMEs




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of registered Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country from 2014-2018;(b)	the total number of sick MSMEs in the country as on date, State/UT-wise;(c)	the number of MSMEs referred to the Corrective Action Plan Committees as stressed accounts from 2014-2018, State/UT-wise;(d)	whether the Ministry has conducted any study to judge the impact of demonetization on MSMEs across the country and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	The total number of Enterprise Memorandum-II filled by the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) during 2014-15 was 4.25 lakh. Since the inception of Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum in September 2015, the total number of MSMEs registered on Udyog Aadhaar Portal as on 15.07.2019 are 71.12 lakh. (b)& (c):	As per the information received from the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), the number of sick MSME accounts as on March 31, 2016 was 486291. The State/UT-wise number of sick MSME accounts as on March 31, 2016 is given as Annexure. The number of stressed accounts referred to the Corrective Action Plan Committees during the half year ended March 31, 2017; September 30, 2017; March 31, 2018 and September 30, 2018 are 100803, 87062, 130208 and 150165 respectively. (d)&(e):	The Ministry has not conducted any study to judge the impact of demonetization on MSMEs across the country.
