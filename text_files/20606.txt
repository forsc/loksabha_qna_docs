                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1633






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Off Grid Solar Photovoltaic Application Programme




Pratima Bhoumik













                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has extended Off-grid solar photovoltaic application programme for North Eastern States and Jharkhand;(b) if so the details thereof; and(c) the status of the sanctioned/installed solar street lights, solar study lamps and solar power parks, Statewise including North Eastern States and Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)&(b)The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy launched Off-grid and Decentralized Solar PV Applications Programme Phase-III in August 2018 for installation of solar street lights and solar power packs and distribution of solar study lamps in the country, including the North Eastern States and Jharkhand, during 2018-19 and 2019-20. The Programme was further extended till 31.3.2021 for implementation only in North Eastern States.(c) State-wise details of sanction issued and achievements reported for solar street lights, solar study lamps and solar power packs under the Programme till 31.12.2020 is placed at Annexure. Sanctions were issued to States/UTs based on demand received from them. No demand was received from the State of Jharkhand and therefore, no capacity was sanctioned to the State under the Programme.*****
