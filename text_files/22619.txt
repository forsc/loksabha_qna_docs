                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3844






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Installation of EMAS




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) has considered the option of installing the Engineered Materials Arrestor System (EMAS) at vulnerable airports to prevent aircraft from overshooting runways;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether Supreme Court has given notice to the DGCA in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) and (b): Installation of EMAS (Engineered Material Arrestor System) at Mangalore Airport was considered by Airports Authority of India (AAI) in consultation with the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) in year 2015. However, EMAS was not considered operationally viable due to operational constraints and therefore, was not recommended by DGCA for implementation.;(c): A PIL W.P. (C) No. 998/2020 Rajen Mehta vs. DGCA has been filed. The petitioner has prayed for issuance of direction to DGCA in respect of EMAS. Hon''ble Supreme Court of India vide Order dated 16.02.2021 has issued notice to DGCA to file a counter affidavit in the matter.
