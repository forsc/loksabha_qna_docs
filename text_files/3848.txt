                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Restructured Workload Norms




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has restructured workload norms of Assistant and Associateprofessors;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has started Assessment Performance Index (API) for collegeteachers;(d) if so the details thereof;(e) whether the Government is considering Ph.D. as an eligibility for appointment in colleges;and(f) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)to (f):The University Grants Commission (Minimum Qualifications for Appointment ofTeachers and other Academic Staff in Universities and Colleges and other Measures for theMaintenance of Standards in Higher Education) Regulations, 2018 lays down norms forworkload of teachers in universities and colleges. As per the said Regulations, the directteaching-learning work load for Assistant Professor and Associate Professor/ Professor is 16hours per week and 14 hours per week respectively.The said Regulations provide new simplified assessment criteria and methodologyfor performance evaluation of teachers, which includes research score for Universities toimprove research output and is more teaching-oriented for Colleges.As per the said Regulations, Ph.D. Degree shall be a mandatory qualification fordirect recruitment to the post of Assistant Professor in Universities with effect from01.07.2021.The aforesaid Regulationsis available on University Grants Commission’swebsiteathttps://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/4033931_UGC-Regulation_min_Qualification_Jul2018.pdf.*****
