                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2433






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Educated Unemployed People




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any data regarding educated unemployed people from tribal community; (b)	if so, the number of educated unemployed people from tribal community of Rajasthan from the year 2014 to 2018; and (c)	the arrangements made by the Government to provide employment to the said people and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	As per the information received from Rajasthan State, the number of registered educated unemployed youths from tribal communities of Rajasthan  from the year 2014 to 2018 is as follows:Year	No. of candidates2014	154112015	186192016	80232017	154002018	16768(c):	The department is organizing Skill, Employment & Entrepreneurship (SEE) fairs/ Campus Placement shivirs in tribal areas for providing Employment/ Training /Self-employment opportunities to the tribal unemployed youths. Details of shivirs and beneficiaries are as follows:-Year	No. of Shivirs	No. of beneficiaries2014-15	13	39332015-16	23	211692016-17	57	189852017-18	59	7647*****
