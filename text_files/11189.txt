                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

206






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Suspension or Termination of Employees




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether Government is aware of random lay-offs of employees of the MNCs in the country;(b)if so, whether Government has monthly report and data on the suspension and or termination of staff since January 2017; and (c)if so, the action taken by Government with the agencies to continue their staffs and thereby expansion of projects thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	Labour is in the Concurrent List. State Governments are mandated to take appropriate action on the disputes falling in their sphere. Such data is not maintained centrally. However, Labour Bureau, an attached office of Ministry of Labour & Employment, brings out statistics on “Industrial Disputes, Closures, Retrenchments and Lay-offs in India” based on the voluntary returns received every month from the Labour Departments of the States and the Regional Labour Commissioners (Central). The information on layoffs and that on units affecting Retrenchment during 2017-2019 (received till 27th January, 2020) are at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.**                                                      *****
