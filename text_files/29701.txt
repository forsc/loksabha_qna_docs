                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






National Investment Fund




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the National Investment Fund set up to channelize the proceeds of disinvestment of CPSEs is still operational and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the amount channelized in to the fund from 2012-2021; and (c)	the detailed breakdown of purposes for which the proceeds from disinvestment have been utilized from 2012-21? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a)	Yes Sir. The ‘National Investment Fund’ (NIF), is a reserve fund maintained in the Public Account of India. The disinvestment proceeds are channelized into the fund after due appropriation authorized by the Parliament.(b) & (c) The details with regard to the amount of the proceeds from disinvestment channelized into the fund and amount utilized for the purposed during 2012-21 is given in the enclosed Statement.
