                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

98






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Yoga Centres and Events




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has launched a mobile application to enable people to locate yoga centres and events in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the certified yoga instructors in the country can enrol themselves in this app; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):     Yes.(b):     Yogalocator is a mobile application developed by Ministry of AYUSH. It has a facility for registration of Yoga trainers, Centres and Events. It will help public to locate trainer/centre/event of own choice alongwith navigation.(c):     Yes.(d):   A facility has been created in the Yogalocator application wherein Yoga instructors can register themselves and after registration details of them will be displayed under the registered city and public can view this under registered trainers. Application also has a facility to navigate to the location of instructor/trainer from the location of the citizen.……………………
