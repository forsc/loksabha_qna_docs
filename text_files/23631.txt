                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4727






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Frequency of Samta Express




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government proposes to run Samta Express once in a week from Jagdalpur to Delhi via Raipur in order to provide direct benefit of rail facilities to North Indians; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the trains being run from tribal dominated Bastar to Delhi; (d) whether there is no direct train from Delhi to the said place; and (e) if so, whether the Government is going to consider running Samta Express once in a week for this place?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e) Owing to operational and resource constraints including non-availability of infrastructural facilities at Jagdalpur, there is no immediate proposal to operate 12807/12808 Visakhapatnam-H. Nizamuddin Samta Express from Jagdalpur via Raipur.  However, passengers of Jagdalpur area desirous of travelling to Delhi may do so by suitable changeover at Visakhapatnam/Rayagada. Besides, introduction/extension of train services over Indian Railways is an ongoing process subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources, etc.*****
