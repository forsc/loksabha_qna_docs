                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

594






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Crash of Air Force Helicopter




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether an Indian Air Force helicopter was shot down early this year killing six people;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether any enquiry has been conducted in this regard; and(d) if so, the details and the outcome thereof along with the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (d):  One Indian Air Force helicopter was shot down on 27 February, 2019.  All six personnel onboard were killed.  It was a case of mistaken fratricide, during the war-like situation, developed due to the hostile action of Pakistan Air Force (PAF) at the LoC near Srinagar.  The inquiry has been concluded and concerned individuals have been held blameworthy.*****
