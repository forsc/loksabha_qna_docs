                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3274






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Home Delivery Charges




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the consumers of Petroleum Products including LPG in the hill districts of Darjeeling and Kalimpong are charged loading-unloading transportation charges over and above the Maximum Retail Price set by the Government and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and(b) the steps taken to address the issue?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have reported that all distributors, except Durgam Kshetriya Vitraks, are under instructions to make home delivery of LPG cylinders in their authorised area of operation including rural areas without any additional charges. However, the State/UT Government can fix additional delivery charge in consultation with the OMCs only in cases where it is necessary in view of the geographical terrain and /or the distance in the area of the distribution
