                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3575






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Modernisation of ITI




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry is taking any concrete measures to revive the Industrial Training Institute (ITI) in Darbhanga which provides training for employment and which is almost on the verge of closure and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to equip the said institute with modern facilities in coordination with the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship; and (c) 	if so, the time by which the proposal is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (c) 	Yes Sir. The Government ITI at Darbhanga was selected as Centre of Excellence and funded a sum of Rs. 2.625 Crore, as 75% central share out of the total sum of Rs.3.5 Crore under the scheme “Externally Aided Project for Reform and Improvement in Vocational Training Services rendered by the Central and the State Governments”. With this fund, the Government ITI, Darbhanga has done the following:(i)	Added 2 years course under Craftsmen Training Scheme in Information Technology Sector(ii)	Constructed new Centre of Excellence building: and (iii)	Modified and upgraded the IT Labs.
