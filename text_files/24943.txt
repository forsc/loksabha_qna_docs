                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

868






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Modernization of Broadcasting Infrastructure




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has allocated funds amounting to Rs. 473 crores to Broadcasting Infrastructure Network Development (BIND) Scheme for modernization of broadcasting network infrastructure during the financial year 2019-20; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof and present status of progress made in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) & (b) : Yes Sir. Rs.473.00 crore was allocated to Prasar Bharati in 2019-20 at BE (Budget Estimate) stage under the “Broadcasting Infrastructure and Network Development (BIND)” scheme. The various components of the scheme are as under:i.	Modernization (including Digitization), Augmentation & Replacement of Transmittersii.	Modernization, Augmentation & Replacement of Satellite Broadcast equipmentiii.	Modernization & Digitalization of Studiosiv.	FM Expansion/Replacement (AIR)v.	Expansion of DTHvi.	Strengthening of Coverage in Sensitive areas by AIR vii.	High Definition TV (HDTV)viii.	Expansion of TV Channels ix.	Broadcasting on Alternate Platformx.	Augmentation of Civil Infrastructure including Staff Quarters and other misc. worksxi.	E-governance project by AIRxii.	Content Development and SAP (Swachhata Action Plan)The allocation of the funds is made in a phased manner. The allocation of funds in the year 2019-20 and expenditure as under:(Rs. In Crore)Financial Year	Allocation	Revised Allocation	Expenditure2019-20	473.00	235.40	289.02** 	This amount includes unspent balance of FY 2018-19.  *****
