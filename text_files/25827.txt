                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1618






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Expenditure on Central Vista Project




Anto  Antony













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    Pradyut Bordoloi
                                                








                                                    Gaurav Gogoi
                                                








                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                








                                                    K. Muraleedharan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total estimated cost of the Central Vista Redevelopment 	Project and the number of years this is estimated to be spread 	over;(b)	the details and costs incurred on the said project, as on date;(c)	the estimated costs to be incurred on the project for the financial year 	2021-22; and(d)	the estimated costs of demolishing existing structures as part of 	the Central Vista Redevelopment Project, both in total and per	structure proposed to be demolished ?






ANSWER




                                                                             THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                 MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) : Under the Central Vista Redevelopment/ Development Master 	Plan, only 2 projects viz. construction of New Parliament Building 	and Redevelopment of Central Vista Avenue are being implemented, as on date. The details are as under: (i)	Construction of New Parliament Building at an estimated cost of	? 971 crore is being undertaken and is targeted to be completed 	by October, 2022. (ii)	Redevelopment of Central Vista Avenue at an estimated cost of ? 608 crore is being undertaken and is targeted to be completed by 	November, 2021. (b) : Till date, the expenditure incurred on these 2 projects are ? 238 crore for New Parliament Building and ? 63 crore for 	Redevelopment of Central Vista Avenue. (c) : The estimated costs to be incurred on these 2 projects for the 	financial year 2021-22 is ? 1,289 crore.(d) :  The estimated cost of demolition of existing structures has not 	been worked out separately.                                  *****
