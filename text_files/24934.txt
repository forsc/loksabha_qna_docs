                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Skill Training to Weavers




Nayab Singh













                                                    Gajendra Singh Patel
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Bhatia
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has prepared any scheme for providing training to weavers under modern skill training programme; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the number of weavers imparted skill training during the last three years, State-wise including Haryana and Madhya Pradesh; and (d) the names of the institutes with which contract has been signed for imparting skill training?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (d):	Skill upgradation is a continuous process.  Need-based skill upgradation programmes for handloom workers in technical areas viz. weaving, dyeing, designing, etc. were earlier conducted under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP), Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) and are now under SAMARTH- Capacity Building in Textile Sector.  Training programmes for handloom workers are conducted through 28 Weavers’ Service Centres (WSCs) functioning in the country under administrative control of Ministry of Textiles. List of WSCs and State-wise details of handloom workers imparted skill upgradation training including in Haryana and Madhya Pradesh, from FY 2018-19 to FY 2020-21 are annexed as Annexure-A and Annexure-B respectively.
