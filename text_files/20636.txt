                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1999






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Notification of TB Cases




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed













                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has expressed concern over the 60 percent decline in notification of TB cases during the lockdown period resulting in higher household transmission leading to spurt in cases;(b) whether the Government has taken measures for normalization of TB disease eradication programme;(c) if so, the details thereof and instructions issued to States to take immediate steps to see the rise in such cases and take suitable measures to restrict such cases; and(d) the other steps taken by the Government in consultation with States in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a): For the period Jan to Dec 2020, 18.03 lakh TB cases were notified which is 25% less than 24.04 TB cases reported in 2019.Currently, there are no evidences to suggest that there are spurt in TB cases resulting from higher household transmission.(b) to (d): Government has taken multiple measures for normalization of TB disease eradication programme:i.	Provision of free TB drugs of extended duration and door-step deliveryii.	Guidance on ensuring uninterrupted functioning of TB diagnostic laboratories.iii.	Issuing multiple advisories to States and districts to ensure uninterrupted services during COVID-19 iv.	Guidance for Bi-directional TB-COVID screening and screening of TB among ILI/SARI cases issued to State/UTs.v.	Multiple reviews with State Government to monitor progress in implementation of National TB Elimination Programme (NTEP).
