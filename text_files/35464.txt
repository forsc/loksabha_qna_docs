                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2920






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Tourism Circuits Developed under SDS in Gujarat




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of circuits that have been developed under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme (SDS) in Gujarat so far;(b) 	the funds allocated to Gujarat during the last three years under the scheme; and (c) 	the achievements of the SDS at present?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 				      (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	The Ministry of Tourism under its ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme has sanctioned three projects in Gujarat as per the details  given  below:<pre>Sr.No	Name of Circuit	Year of Sanction	Name of the Project 	Sanctioned Amt. in ? crore	Released amount ? in Cr. (till date) 1	Heritage Circuit	2016-17	Development of Ahmedabad- Rajkot- Porbandar –Bardoli- Dandi	58.42	56.212	Heritage Circuit	2016-17	Development of Vadnagar-Modhera 	91.11	87.253	Buddhist Circuit	2017-18	Development of Junagadh- Gir Somnath- Bharuch-Kutch- Bhavnagar- Rajkot- Mehsana	26.68	22.28</pre>(c):	All the three projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan scheme in the  State of  Gujarat  are  physically complete.********
