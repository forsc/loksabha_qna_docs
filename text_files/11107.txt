                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Tourism Circuits under Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Chandra Prakash Choudhary













                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Seth
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of the tourism circuits in Jharkhand and Andhra Pradesh which have been selected under the Swadesh Darshan scheme; (b) 	the names of the places which have been selected under these circuits; (c) 	the details of amount sanctioned for carrying out works under the scheme; and (d) 	the time by which these works are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	The details of the project sanctioned in Andhra Pradesh and state of Jharkhand under the Swadesh Darshan scheme is placed below:-<pre>State /Year of sanction	Detail	Sanctioned (Crores)	Project status	Timeline of the projectAndhra Pradesh 2014-15	Development of  Kakinada - Hope Island - Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary - Passarlapudi - Aduru - S Yanam - Kotipally under Coastal Circuit	67.84	100%	CompletedAndhra Pradesh2015-16	Development of Nellore - Pulikat Lake - Ubblamadugu Water Falls – Nelapattu- Kothakoduru- Mypadu - Ramateertham - Iskapalli under Coastal Circuit	59.70	90%	January 2020Andhra Pradesh 2017-18	Development of: Shalihundam-Thotlakonda- Bavikonda- Bojjanakonda- Amravati- Anupu under Buddhist Circuit	52.34	45%	March 2020Jharkhand2018-19	Development of Dalma- Chandil- Getalsud- Betla National park- Mirchaiya- Netarhat under Eco Circuit	52.72	0%	18 Months</pre>*******
