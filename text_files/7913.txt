                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1561






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Intercity Train




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a proposal has been accorded approval to run intercity train from Junagarh to Visakhapatnam;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it is a fact that there is no stoppage of any express train at Nayapara Road and Rupra Road whereas Nayapara Road is the railway station of district headquarter;(d) if so, whether any approval has been accorded to give stoppage of express trains at either of  these stations; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) No, Sir. (b) Does not arise. (c) to (e) No, Sir. At present, Nawapara Road and Rupra Road stations are already being served by 4 pairs & 7 pairs of Mail/Express trains respectively. These are in addition to 3 pairs & 4 pairs of Passenger train services, serving the stations respectively.  Besides, provision of stoppages on Indian Railways is an ongoing process.*****
