                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Shaala Darpan




Vijay Kumar Dubey













                                                    Sandhya Ray
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government is implementing Shaala Darpan initiative in Kendriya Vidyalayas(KVs);(b) if so, the details thereof and the aims and objectives of this initiative;(c) the initiative undertaken and the problems faced by the Government under the Shaala Darpanscheme during the last three years;(d) the present status of first phase of Shaala Darpan;(e) whether the Government has directed the States for introduction of similar system in the StateGovernment schools and if so, the response of the State Government of Madhya Pradeshthereto; and(f) whether the Government has made any arrangement for parents to monitor their child’sprogress through mobile phones and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Yes Sir. Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has implemented Shaala Darpan initiative in 1099Kendriya Vidyalayas.(b) The aims and objectives of Shaala Darpan are as under:-i. To serve as a single integrated platform to address academic and administrativerequirements of various stakeholders namely students, teachers, management &parents.ii. To enhance academic delivery systems thereby improving overall quality ofeducationiii. To promote participation of stakeholders e.g. parents etc.iv. Bring in uniformity/standardization of implementation of policies, processes andprogramsv. To improve overall e-governance and promote Transparency, Efficiency andAccountability(c) Since the implementation of Shaala Darpan in 1099 Kendriya Vidyalayas, KendriyaVidyalaya Sangathan has faced problems as mentioned below:i. Delay of 2-16 months in delivery of every phase.ii. Connectivity to remote server is major issue as the application is not web based.iii. Too many links in the application which caused confusion in usage.iv. Application was lacking support Unicode and is not bilingual.v. HR Dashboard has no provision of inclusion of data about employees of 31 Units(KVS (HQ)+25 Regions+05 ZIETs).vi. Concurrency was not supported and the users need to work in odd hours to completethe work.vii. Huge consumption of NIC Hardware resources.viii. Bio Metric machines were distributed in 562 Kendriya Vidyalayas only out of total of1099 KVs.ix. Only 9,969 OMV cards were delivered to students whereas there are more than 12lakh students.(d) Due to the problems faced in implementation of Shaala Darpan, the project has been limitedto 1099 Kendriya Vidyalayas.(e) Yes Sir. The Government of Madhya Pradesh had conveyed their willingness to join the pilotproject on Shaala Darpan.(f) No Sir.****
