                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4883






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Identification of Beneficiaries of PM Kisan Yojana




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the farmers deserving ‘Samman Nidhi’ have been identified in Madhya Pradesh; and (b) 	if so, the details of the number of deserving/non-deserving farmers for the said scheme in Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	As per the Agri-census data of 2015-16, subject to certain exclusions, the tentative number of beneficiaries in Madhya Pradesh is 1,03,65,783. As of now, data of 26,49,181 eligible beneficiaries have been uploaded in PM-Kisan portal and 24,36,696 have been accepted after 1st level validation. PM-Kisan is a continuous and ongoing Scheme, in which the financial benefits is transferred to the bank accounts of the identified beneficiaries as and when their correct and verified data is uploaded by the concerned States / UTs on PM-Kisan web-portal. *******
