                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Audit of Schemes




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government undertakes any audit to ascertain the progress of the various schemes under the ministry;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of achievement of the scheme in the State of Assam and particularly in the districts of Barpeta and Bongaigaon; and(d)	the details of the financial allocation and utilisation over the last three years and the quantum of funds spent for various programs?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Ministry have an internal mechanism for audit by Chief Controller of Accounts. Director General of Audit (Central Expenditure) also conducts the audit of the major schemes of the Ministry on a yearly basis.(c)	Under PMKVY (2016-20), as on 17.01.2020, 2,01,479 candidates have been trained / oriented in State of Assam including 6,507 and 3,162 candidates in district of Barpeta and Bongaigaon, respectively.(d)	Under PMKVY (2016-20), funds amounting to Rs. 5,903.4 Cr. have been released to implementing agencies i.e. NSDC (Rs. 5,034.44 Cr.) and States/UTs/SSDMs (Rs. 868.96) for the implementation of the scheme. Out of total released funds, utilization certificates for funds amounting to Rs. 5,114 Cr. (Rs. 4900 Cr. from NSDC and Rs. 214 Cr. from States/UTs/SSDMs) have been submitted to the Ministry.
