20.07.2021






PMGSY in Odisha




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of progress made in the construction of roads in the State of Odisha under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) as on 31st March 2021;(b) 	whether the large number of roads are yet to be completed under the PMGSY in the State;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	the target set for the years 2020-21 and 2021-2022 to provide rural connectivity under PMGSY?
