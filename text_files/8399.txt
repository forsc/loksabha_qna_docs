                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1900






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Parental Leave to Single Father




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is considering to provide all facilities and amenities linked to ‘parental leave’ to single father on the lines of maternity leave to mothers, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government is likely to provide all monetary benefits to single father which is prevalent in the countries like Australia, Iceland, New Zealand, Norway and Sweden, if so, the details thereof; (c) whether this would lead to gender equality for working fathers in India, if so, the reaction of the Government thereto; (d) whether there is any welfare scheme which covers the working single father; and (e) if so, the details thereof and allocation of funds thereunder during 2019- 2020?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):   There is no such proposal for providing facilities and amenities linked to ''parental leave’ to single father on the lines of maternity leave to mothers.(b) & (c):  There is no such proposal for providing monetary benefits to single father which is prevalent in the countries like Australia, Iceland, New Zealand, Norway and Sweden. (d) & (e):    There is no such welfare scheme.………………..
