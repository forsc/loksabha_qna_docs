                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1644






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Hospitals run by CIL




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)       the number of hospitals being run by Coal India Ltd (CIC) across the country;  (b)       the details of total sanctioned strength of doctors in all the hospitals run by it and the number of existing vacancies particularly in Odisha;   (c)      whether the Government is considering raising the retirement age of doctors in Coal India Hospitals from 60 years to 65 years and bring it at par with the retirement age of doctors across the country;  (d)     if so, the details thereof; and  (e)      if not, the reasons thereof and the time by which a decision in the manner will   be taken?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)   (a):  All total of 70 Hospitals are run by Coal India Ltd (CIL) across the country. In  addition to the Hospitals,  358 Dispensaries are also being run by CIL. (b):    As per the recently approved manpower, the total sanctioned & working   strength of     Medical Discipline in CIL & its Subsidiaries are 1528 and 981  respectively. The vacancy     position of one of the Subsidiaries of CIL located at Odisha (MCL) is 79 (sanctioned is 172   and working is 93).     ( c):    No Sir.    (d) & (e):  Does not arise in view of answer to part (c) above.****
