                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

256






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Grants to VOs




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.256 TO BE ANSWERED ON 20.07.2021GRANTS TO VOs256. MS. DIYA KUMARI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of grants allocated to Voluntary Organisations (VOs) and training institutions under the Grants-in-Aid scheme for the upliftment of backward classes during the last three years;(b) the detail list of voluntary organisations and training institutions working for the upliftment of the backward classes, particularly in Rajasthan, District-wise;(c) whether the Government monitors the expenditures incurred by these organisations for upliftment activities; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a): The Government through National Backward Classes Finance  and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) facilitates Skill Development Training to the Other Backward Classes (OBCs) having annual family income below Rs. 3.00 lakhs. These skill programmes are undertaken by standard institutions and not by Voluntary organizations. The Skill Training programmes and grant allocated to Training Institutes during last 3 years is as under:Financial Year	No. of Beneficiaries	Amount Allocated (Rs. in Crores)2018-19	25040	30.872019-20	30491	60.172020-21	15750	21.15Total	71281	112.19(b): This Ministry has no association with voluntary organisations  for the skilling programmes. These training programmes are implemented primarily through Government and other reputed training institutes. The list of training institutes through which the skill development training programmes were conducted for NBCFDC’s target group in the financial year 2020-21 in Rajasthan is enclosed.(c) & (d): Monitoring of the training programme is done by the Corporation through participation is Selection Committee meetings, assessment process, surprise inspections, video call etc.Further, the Corporation takes Utilization Certificate duly certified by the Chartered Accountant and statement of expenditure along with supporting documents from the Training Institutes.***** Annexure-INational Backward Classes Finance and Development CorporationDetails of Training Institutes working for the upliftment of Backward Classes in  RajasthanS No	Location 	Implementing Agency 1.
