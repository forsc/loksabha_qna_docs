                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5347






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Supply of Safe Drinking Water




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the schemes under which the Government has supplied/ provided safe drinking water for consumption in the rural habitations during the last five years; (b) whether the Government proposes to increase pace of such schemes in view of the present drought situation and if so, the details thereof; (c) the number of habitations in such States/districts which are having contaminated water and where drinking water is supplied through pipelines including the number of such habitations in the country where pipelines are yet to be laid; and (d) the names of the States in the country where nitrate, phosphate, carbon toxicity, polycyclics aromatic hydrocarbon, fluoride, boron substance and arsenic are found in the ground water?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)& (b) Water is a state subject. State Government is empowered to plan, design, approve and execute drinking water supply projects / schemes in rural areas. As reported by States on Integrated Management Information System (IMIS), details of completed schemes during the last five years are at Annexure-I.	Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation (DDWS), Ministry of Jal Shakti has directed the States to complete all the ongoing schemes which are at advanced stage of completion which would ultimately help in improving the coverage of drinking water availability  and mitigating drought like situation in the country.(c)and (d)	As reported by the States on Integrated Management Information System (IMIS), State-wise number of (i) Quality affected habitations having access to safe drinking waterthrough piped water supply or without piped water supply is at Annexure-II and State-wise number of rural habitations affected with Arsenic, Fluoride & Nitrate is placed at Annexure-III. Other details are not maintained by this Ministry.
