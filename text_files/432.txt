                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

221






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Setting up of Retail Outlets




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of new Retail Outlets (ROs) allotted and functioning and not functioning during the last five years, Statewise;(b) the increase in sales of petroleum products during the said period, State-wise;(c) the amount spent on Foreign Exchange for import of petroleum products during the said period, year-wise; and(d) the numbers of staff employed in newly allotted ROs during the said period, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Details of State/OMC-wise number of Letter of Intents (LOIs) issued, Retail Outlets commissioned and LOIs pending for the last five years (2014 to 2019) are given in Annexure-1.(b) Details of State-wise increase in sales of Motor Spirit (MS) and High Speed Diesel (HSD) in terms of percentage growth in respect of Oil Marketing Companies i.e. BPCL, IOCL & HPCL during the last five years are given in Annexure-2, 3 & 4 respectively.(c) OMC-wise details of Foreign Exchange incurred on import of petroleum products (MS&HSD) during 2014-15 to 2018-19 are as below :-…2..-2-(US$ in Millions)Financial YearBPCLIOCLHPCL2014-151372339.271.952015-16847603.585.602016-171148545.9105.382017-181440262.5398.792018-191902270.0652.66(d) The staff/worker working at the retail outlets are employed by the dealers. The number of staff deployed at retail outlets depends on various factors like type of market (Urban/Rural/Metro), nature of clientele (2/3W/Car/Commercial vehicles), number of dispensing units installed, the retail outlet working hours, number of shifts etc.****
