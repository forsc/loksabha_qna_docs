                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Starvation  Deaths




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that country is still facing starvation deaths; (b) 	if so, the details of the report thereon during the last five years; (c) 	whether it is also a fact that recently there were incidents of starvation deaths in Tripura relief camps; and (d) 	if so, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): 	No State Government/Union Territory Administration has reported any incident of death due to starvation. However, a few reports have of-late appeared in the media alleging starvation deaths in some states, but on enquiry, the allegations of death due to starvation have not been substantiated.(c): 	No Sir.(d): In view of (c) above, does not arise.****
