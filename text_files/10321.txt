                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3765






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Developmental Works under MPLAD




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of initiatives that have been taken by the Government to assess  the developmental works done by MPs under MPLADS; and (b) the parameters to assess such works?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE ( INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a)  and (b) The Ministry  had undertaken third party physical monitoring of works completed using Member of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme(MPLADS) fund in selected districts through independent agencies viz. NABARD Consultancy Services Private Limited (NABCONS) and Agricultural Finance Corporation (AFC) Limited. Programme Evaluation Organisation (PEO) of erstwhile Planning Commission had also conducted an evaluation of the Scheme.	The agency inspects/visits the work sites to verify the assets with respect to the approved technical specifications, financial approvals, the basic quality, its utility for intended purposes, etc.                                           *****
