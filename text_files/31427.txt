                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Promotion of Dairy Industry




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the schemes being run by the Government to promote dairy industry in the country, State-wise; (b) the measures taken along with the schemes being run by the Government to make dairy industry a profitable business; (c) the details of places where the largest number of dairy industry are functional in the State of Madhya Pradesh; and (d) the details of grants being provided to States related to dairy industries?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	& (b) Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing following Dairy  Development schemes  to promote the dairy across the country :I.	National programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)II.	Dairy Processing & Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF)III.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives & Farmer Producer Organisations engaged in dairy activities (SDPFPO)IV.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF)	To make dairy industry a profitable business, assistance is provided in the form of   Grant-in-aid and low interest loan besides contribution by the Cooperatives, Farmer Producers Organization (FPO), Self Help group (SHG) etc under NPDD for quality milk infrastructure in all States, strengthening of milk procurement system, processing  and marketing infrastructure in aspirational and backward districts in the State  of  Uttar Pradesh, Bihar,  Punjab, West Bengal, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Uttarakhand. In case of DIDF  loan assistance is provided  to cooperatives  milk producers  companies, registered SHG/FPO  for  creation and modernisation of milk processing and value addition capacities  in the form of  loan assistance  with 2.5% interest subvention. Under Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations (SDCFPO), working capital loan is provided to the cooperatives and  milk producers companies  with 2% interest subvention  and additional  2% interest subvention on timely payment. The scheme AHIDF provides loan assistance to FPO, Private Companies, Individual entrepreneur and section 8 companies with interest subvention of 3%. The schemes DIDF, SDCFPO and AHIDF are for implementation in all the States.(c) The major  districts where  the largest numbers of dairy industry are functional includes Sehore, Shajapur, Rajgarh, Dewas, Ujjain, Ratlam, Mandsaur, Gwalior, Bhind, Morena, Jabalpur, Chindwara, Seoni, Balaghat & Sagar(d)  Scheme wise details of grants alongwith loan assistance and interest subvention provided to State Implementing agencies including Madhya Pradesh (M.P) related to Dairy industries are as below: (Rs. in Crore)S.
