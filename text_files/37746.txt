                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4982






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Alleged Harassment of Civilians in Cantonment Areas




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether instances of alleged harassment and exploitation of civilians by defence officials incantonment areas in the country have come to the notice of the Government;(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise during the last three years; and(c) the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government against those found g






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) to (c):  No case of exploitation of civilians by defence officials in Cantonment areas have come to notice of Government. However, certain references/representations relating to inconvenience due to closure of roads by Local Military Authorities have been received from residents in a few Cantonments like Secunderabad (Telangana), Danapur (Bihar), Kamptee (Maharashtra), Belgaum (Karnataka), Saugor (Madhya Pradesh) & Jalandhar (Punjab).  In this regard, MoD has issued detailed instructions to all concerned authorities for compliance vide letter dated 04.09.2018.*****
