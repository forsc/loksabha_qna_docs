                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

505






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Ayushman Bharat Yojana Cards




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of cards issued under Ayushman Bharat Yojana in Maharashtra;(b) the number of beneficiaries who have been benefitted therefrom during last three years;(c) the number of private and government hospitals of Maharashtra included under the said Scheme; and(d) the details of amount provided to patients as assistance under the said Scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d): Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) was launched on 23rd September 2018 to provide health coverage up to Rs. 5 lakh per annum per beneficiary family to around 10.74 crore poor and vulnerable families identified from Socio Economic Caste Census (SECC) data of 2011 on the basis of select deprivation and occupational criteria across rural and urban areas respectively. As of 31st January 2022, the number of beneficiaries verified and provided with Ayushman cards in Maharashtra is 74 lakh. As of 31st January 2022, approximately 4.39 lakh hospital admissions worth Rs. 1,142 crore are authorized under the converged scheme in Maharashtra during the financial years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22. A total of 970 hospitals are empanelled under the scheme in Maharashtra. This includes 671 private and 299 Government hospitals.*****
