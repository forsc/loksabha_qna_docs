                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Utilisation of CAMPA Fund




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of implementation of Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) Fund, State-wise, with a particular reference to Karnataka;(b)	the amount that has been given to States, particularly to Karnataka last year;(c)	the amount collected from January, 2019 till March, 2020, and the steps  taken to distribute this amount to States; and(e)	the manner in which the Ministry will ensure that CAMPA Funds are  utilized for the purpose for which they are released? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b) The Compensatory Afforestation Fund (CAF) Act, 2016 has come in to force with effect from 30.09.2018. Since then, State Compensatory Afforestation Fund (State Fund) under public accounts of 33 States/UTs have been established and the State Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (State CAMPA) have been constituted in these States for managing and utilizing the State Fund for the purpose of the Act. In addition, CAF amounting to Rs.48,606.39 crore have been transferred to 32 States. Utilization of State Fund is done as per approved Annual Plan of Operation (APO) of the respective State CAMPA. The status of establishment of State Fund, constitution of State CAMPA and state-wise transfer of CAF is attached at Annexure – I. Approval of APOs for the year 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 of the respective State CAMPA is attached at Annexure - II.(c) The CAF or compensatory levies collected during financial year 2018-19 and 2019-20 is attached at Annexure - III. Distributing CAF to States is a continuous process.(e) The States have their own established set of monitoring mechanism. Further, independent concurrent monitoring and evaluation and third party monitoring of works undertaken from State Fund is included in the Annual Plan of Operations of the respective State CAMPA and States are required to carry out internal and third party monitoring of the Compensatory Afforestation (CA) and other activities.In addition, ‘measurable output’ of all physical activities and targets of each permissible activity along with period required for achieving the measurable output is monitored by the State Executive Committee and State Steering Committee of the respective State CAMPA.****
