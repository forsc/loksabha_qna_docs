                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3072






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Cotton Production




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it has come to the notice of the Ministry that Guntur in Andhra Pradesh (AP) is the major district where cotton is grown; (b) 	whether it is true that production has gone up from 7 lakh bales in 2012-13 to 10.75 lakh bales in 2014-15 and the productivity from 3.89 bales/hectare in 2013-14 to 5.21 bales/hectare in 2014-15; (c) 	if so, the details of the production and productivity in 2015-16 to 2018-19, year-wise; (d) 	whether the Ministry has tried to ascertain the reasons for sudden increase in yield and if so, the outcome thereof; (e) 	the efforts being made to maintain the momentum to permit other companies to produce Bt. Cotton seeds; and (f) 	the status of organic cotton production in the country, particularly in Guntur in AP?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	Yes, Guntur is one of the major cotton growing district in the State of Andhra Pradesh.(b):	Location-wise production and productivity data is not available. However, the state-wise production during cotton year 2012-13 to 2014-15 and productivity during cotton year 2013-14 & 2014-15 of seed cotton (kapas) finalized by the Cotton Advisory Board (CAB) in the State of Andhra Pradesh is as under:-  State	Production(Quantity in lakh bales)	Productivity (Yield) (Kg/Hectare)	2012-13	2013-14	2014-15	2013-14	2014-15Andhra Pradesh	84.00	78.00	77.00*	555.04	501.17* Including Telangana.  (c) & (d):	Statement showing year-wise production and productivity of cotton in the state of Andhra Pradesh during cotton year 2015-16 to 2018-19 is given below:-Crop Year	Cotton Production (Qty in lakh bales)	Productivity (Kg/Hectare)2015-16	23.75	606.232016-17	19.00	684.322017-18	21.26	559.472018-19 (P)	14.50	396.94P-Provisional(e):	Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare provides the financial assistance to the States and seed producing agencies for production and distribution of seeds through various ongoing crop development programmes/schemes viz. National Food Security Mission, Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture, Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Materials under National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology etc.(f):	Organic textiles are the low volume of production and the demand for organic cotton-based products is negligible within the country, mostly because of its high price. ***
