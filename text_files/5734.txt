                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Interlinking of NH-26 and NH-76




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to link NH-26 with NH-76 passing through Jhansi and nearby areas to ensure better connectivity and a hassle free traffic system; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the steps taken/proposed to be taken in this regard; and (d) 	the time frame set for the start and completion of Bundelkhand Expressway? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) National Highway (NH)-26 is linked to NH-76 through NH-25. In addition, four laning of 2 projects (Jhansi-Khajuraho section) connecting Jhansi, Orchha, Niwari, Mauranipur, Nowgong, Chhatarpur and Bamitha linking NH-26 (New NH-44) to NH-76 (New NH-339) is in implementation stage. (d) Bundelkhand Expressway is a project of State Government of Uttar Pradesh (Uttar Pradesh Expressways Industrial Development Authority). As informed by the State Government, various projects of Bundelkhand Expressway, are in bidding stage and expected commencement of these project is in October, 2019 with construction period of 36 months. *****
