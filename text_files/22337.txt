                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3478






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






National Industrial Corridor Programme




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the list of industrial corridor projects being developed under National Industrial Corridor Programme in the country along with the status of these industrial corridors;(b) 	whether any industrial corridor has been planned for the state of Haryana;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	the list of Multi Model Logistic Hub proposed/ under construction on these industrial corridors?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):   	Government of India has accorded approval for development of 11 corridors (32 projects) in four Phases as part of National Master Plan for providing multimodal connectivity to economic zones which are under various stages of conceptualization / development / implementation. The list of projects in the respective states along with status of implementation is  at Annexure. (b) & (c): 	Following two Industrial Corridors are covering the State of Haryana: i.	Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC)ii.	Amritsar Kolkata Industrial Corridor (AKIC) Under DMIC, development work for Integrated Multimodal Logistics Hub (IMLH) Project at Nangal Chaudhary (886 acres) has been initiated. Master plan has been prepared and environmental clearance has been obtained for the project. Implementation works related to External Connectivity are in tendering stage. Under AKIC,  State Government has confirmed 1,500 acres for development of Integrated Manufacturing Cluster(IMC) at Hisar.  Development work  has been initiated and consultants for master planning and preliminary engineering have been appointed.(d): 	Under National Industrial Corridor Programme, in addition to the above, development work for a Multi Modal Logistic Hub (MMLH) at Greater Noida in Uttar Pradesh (334 Ha) under DMIC  has been initiated. *******
