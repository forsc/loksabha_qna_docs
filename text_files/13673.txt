                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2330






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Assistance to Artisans for Construction of Worksheds




Queen Oja













                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry provides financial assistance to artisans to construct workshed and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the number of Khadi and other artisans benefited under the workshed scheme in Andhra Pradesh during each of the last four years and the current year; (c) 	the total number of Khadi artisans who were provided assistance for construction of worksheds in the States of Assam and Gujarat under the workshed scheme for Khadi artisans during the last five years; (d) 	the details of the steps being taken by the Government to promote Khadi products and their sale; and (e) 	the details of production and sale of Khadi products in the Country, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Yes Sir. Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) extends financial assistance to Khadi Institutions for construction of Worksheds to Khadi artisans under “Workshed Scheme for Khadi Artisans”. The Scheme introduced in 2008-09 envisages providing sufficient space and congenial environment to artisans for smooth and fatigue free working leading to enhanced production and increased earning. The scheme is demand driven. The pattern of assistance under the scheme is as follows:<pre>Component	Area / unit	Amount of assistanceConstruction of Individual Workshed 	20 Sq. mtrs.	Rs. 60,000/- or 75% of the cost of workshed (90% for NER), whichever is less. Construction of Group Workshed (Min. 5 & Max. 15 artisans) 	10 Sq. mtrs. per artisan	Rs. 40,000/- per artisan or 75% of the cost of workshed (90% for NER), whichever is less. </pre>(b):	Number of Khadi Artisans benefitted under Workshed Scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh during last four years and current year is as under:<pre>Year	No. of Khadi Artisans benefitted in Andhra Pradesh 2015-16	502016-17	2002017-18	1132018-19	202019-20 (as on 31.1.2020)	118 /Pre>(c):	Number of Khadi Artisans provided financial assistance under Workshed Scheme in Assam and Gujarat during last five years and current year is as under:<pre>Year	No. of artisans benefitted	Assam	Gujarat2014-15	380	2002015-16	193	502016-17	183	2002017-18	170	0*2018-19	0*	0*2019-20(as on 31.1.2020)	118	0*     *No demand has been received by KVIC</pre>(d):	The steps taken by the Government to promote Khadi products and their sale is placed at Annexure-I.  (e):	State-wise details of production and sale of Khadi products in the country is placed at Annexure-II.
