                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2603






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Construction of Flyover in Kerala




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any proposal for construction of a flyover near Thiruvarattukavu temple located at 529+800 LHS in the proposed Attingal bypass on National Highway-66 in Kerala;(b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to convene a meeting of all the concerned stakeholders for execution of this long pending project at the earliest; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d) A reference has been received from Adv. Adoor Prakash, Member of Parliament (Lok Sabha) for construction of a flyover near Thiruvarattukavu temple in order to save a portion of temple building. The temple is located at km 529.800 (LHS) of National Highway (NH) 66. 6-laning work of the stretch from Kadambattukonam to Kazhkuttom of NH-66 including Attingal bypass has already been awarded on 03.03.2022. The alignment of this stretch at temple location is designed in such a way that there is no damage to the Main temple building and only small portion at South West corner of the temple campus is getting affected. All necessary precautions  are being taken to ensure that the Temple is not damaged. At present, there is no proposal to construct flyover at temple location. *****
