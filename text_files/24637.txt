                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

539






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Proposals for Construction of Houses  under PMAY-U




T.R.V.S. Ramesh













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recently approved 708 proposals for construction of 3.61 lakh houses under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U); (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of Houses approved in the country including for the State of Tamil Nadu, State/UT-wise and district-wise; (d) 	the total budget allocated for PMAY-U during the last three years; and (e) 	the total number of houses constructed under PMAY-U during the last three years in the country including Andhra Pradesh, State/UT-wise and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                 THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)& (b)Yes Sir; during the 54th meeting of Central Sanctioning & Monitoring Committee (CSMC) for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban [PMAY-U] held on 8 June 2021, the Government of India has approved 708 project proposals for construction of 3.61 lakh houses in the State of Andhra Pradesh, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Manipur, Odisha, Rajasthan and Tripura. State wise details of proposals / houses are at Annexure I. (c) Details of number of houses sanctioned under PMAY-U in the country, State/UT-wise and district-wise including the State of Tamil Nadu are at Annexure II. (d)The total budget allocated for PMAY-U during the last three years i.e. in F.Y. 2018-19, F.Y. 2019-20 and F.Y. 2020-21 are?6,505 crore, ?6,853.26 crore and ?21,000 crore respectively. Apart from budgetary allocation, ?20,000 crore, ?15,000 crore and ?10,000 crore respectively were provided as Extra Budgetary Resource (EBR) during these Financial Years.  (e) State/UT wise and year wise number of houses constructed under PMAY-U in the country, including State of Andhra Pradesh are at Annexure III.                                    *****
