                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5007






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






SBM in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status and progress of Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) in 	West Bengal, especially in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts; (b) 	the funds allocated under SBM for its its implementation; (c) 	whether the Government has achieved ODF+ and ODF++ status 	as per the set deadline of 31.03.2020;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the steps being taken by the Government to ensure sanitation 	in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                         (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	& (b): Progress of Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) in the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) of West Bengal, including  Darjeeling and Kalimpong is at Annexure. The Mission-period allocation for implementation of the Mission in West Bengal is ? 911.34 crores, out of which ? 410.42 Crores have been released.  (c)	& (d) :  No Sir. Only 2 Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) (Barackpore Cantt. and Lebong Cantt.) in West Bengal have been certified as Open Defecation Free Plus (ODF+) and none of the ULBs are certified ODF++.  (e) :  Besides releasing Central Share (CS) of funds to  States and Union Territories (UTs), Government has introduced ODF+,  ODF++ and Water+ protocols to impart  further impetus to sustain the ODF outcomes. Under these protocols the entire value chain of sanitation is addressed, including access to toilets, cleanliness of CT/PTs, safe management of faecal sludge and waste water to prevent discharge of untreated liquid waste in open land or water bodies.                          *****
