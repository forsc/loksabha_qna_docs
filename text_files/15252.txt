                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

350






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Coal Reserves




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of total coal production in the country since 2014, year-wise and state-wise;(b)	the details of the Proved, Indicated and Inferred coal reserves in the country, state-wise; and(c)	India’s current global standing in the total coal reserves?  






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)to(c): A statement is laid on the table of the House.
