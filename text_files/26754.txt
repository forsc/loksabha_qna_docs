                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2474






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Bifurcation of State




T. R. Paarivendhar













                                                    S. Ramalingam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to bifurcate any State in the country including the State of Tamil Nadu;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons, purpose and motive for such bifurcation;(c) whether the Government has received any demand from any individual or organization to bifurcate any State in the country including the State ofTamil Nadu; and(c) if so, the time by which the proposed bifurcation be made?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI) (a) to (d): Demands and representations are received from time to time from various individuals and organisations for creation of new States.  Creation of a new State has wide ramifications and direct bearing on the federal polity of our country. Government moves on the matter of formation of new States after taking into consideration all relevant factors.  Presently no such proposal is under consideration.*****
