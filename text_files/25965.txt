                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1698






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Flights from Rupsi Airport




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) the number and details of new airports constructed in the country during the last three years;(b) the time by which Rupsi airport of Kokrajhar is likely to become fully operational for the public;(c) the details of places the country to which flights access from Rupsi airport will be made operational?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c): Ministry of Civil Aviation launched Regional Connectivity Scheme - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) on 21-10-2016 to stimulate regional air connectivity and making air travel affordable to the masses. Airports/heliports/water aerodromes of Airports Authority of India(AAI)/Defence/ State Government/ Central Public Sector Undertaking(CPSU) which is included in the awarded routes of UDAN and requires upgradation/development for commencement of UDAN operations are developed under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme. The number of unserved/underserved airports developed under UDAN since last three years, is as under:-2018-2019 - 132019-2020 - 062020-2021 - 18Rupsi airport identified under UDAN, has been upgraded. The Selected Airline Operator(SAO) has already commenced fights from Rupsi to Guwahati and Kolkata.
