                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Welfare of Senior Citizens




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether 142 million Senior Citizens are living in India as per World Health Organisation report; (b) whether the Government has any scheme to formulate a policy for protection and welfare of Senior Citizens, especially their livelihood, security and healthcare and if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a):  As per Census Report, 2011 of Government of India, the population of senior citizens in the country is 10.38 crore (i.e. 103.8 million) only.(b): Yes, Sir. The National Policy on Older Persons (NPOP), 1999, which reaffirms the commitment to ensure the well-being of the older persons, is already in existence. The Policy envisages State support to ensure inter-alia financial security, healthcare and nutrition, shelter, education, welfare, protection of life and property to senior citizens and availability of services to improve the quality of their lives. Besides, the Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Act, 2007 also provides for livelihood, healthcare, protection of life and property etc. to the senior citizens.     (c): Does not arise. *****
