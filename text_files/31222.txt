                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3063






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Off Grid Renewable Power Generation




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of renewable energy projects sanctioned by the Government in the country during the last three years, State-wise including Tripura and Uttar Pradesh;(b) the status of completion of various schemes sanctioned during the said period, scheme and Statewise;(c) whether the Government has taken any steps to boost/ promote off-grid renewable power generation in the country during the said period;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of the funds allocation made in this regard during the said period, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy has sanctioned projects in the country including for the state of Tripura and Uttar Pradesh, under its various schemes for renewable energy capacity addition.State/UT-wise details of renewable energy capacity addition achieved in the country from 2018-19 to 2021-22 (as on 31.10.2021), including the States of Tripura and Uttar Pradesh are given in Annexure-I.(c) & (d) During last three years, for promotion of off-grid renewable power generation, the Ministry of New and Renewable has been providing Central Financial Assistance under the following schemes:(i) Installation 20 lakh standalone solar pumps under Component-B of the PM-KUSUM Scheme. (i) Installation of solar street lights and off-grid solar power plants, and distribution of solar study lamps under Off-grid & Decentralized Solar PV Applications Programme Ph-III. (ii) Setting up small biogas plants (1-25 m3) for production of clean cooking gas, based on cattle dung and other mixed organic in remote, rural and semi-urban areas of the country under New National Biogas and Organic Manure Programme (NNBOMP) Scheme of the Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE), financial support has been provided for.(iii) Setting up biogas plants in the medium size range of 30 m3 to 2500 m3 per day under Biogas based Power Generation (Off-grid) and Thermal energy application Programme (BPGTP). (e) The details of Central Financial Support (CFA) released by the Ministry to various implementing agencies in the country for implementation of renewable energy projects (including off-grid renewable energy projects) from 2018-19 to 2021-22 (as on 29.11.2021) are given in Annexure-II.*****
