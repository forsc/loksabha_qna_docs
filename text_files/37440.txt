                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4472






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Shut Down of Rice Mills in West Bengal




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of rice mills in the State of West Bengal, district-wise; (b) 	whether the Government has shut down any rice mills in West Bengal; (c) 	if so, the reasons therefor; (d) 	whether the Government intends to set up new rice mills in Bishnupur constituency of Bankura district; and (e) 	if so, the time by which the Government intends to do the same?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):  		Government of West Bengal has informed that Food & Supplies Department of the state only empanels eligible and functional rice mill for custom milling of paddy for its Public Distribution System (PDS) operations. Total number of rice mills empanelled by Food & Supplies Department, Government of West Bengal as on 22.03.2022 is 588 only. District-wise number of empanelled rice mills in West Bengal is at Annexure. (b) to (e): 	Government of West Bengal has further informed that Rice Mills are of private individuals or industrial/commercial ventures. Food & Supplies Department, Government of West Bengal does not have any role in opening or closure of any rice mill.**** AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4472 FOR ANSWER ON 30.03.2022 IN THE LOK SABHA.DISTRICT-WISE NO.
