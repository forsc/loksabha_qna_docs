                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






PM-KISAN Scheme in Nagaland




Tokheho Yepthomi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of beneficiaries of the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana in the State of Nagaland; and (b)     the total amount given to Nagaland under the Yojana from 2019-2021?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	A total of 2,06,523 beneficiaries have been given the financial benefits of more than Rs. 299.26 crore through various installments under PM-KISAN Scheme in the State of Nagaland. 	The financial year-wise details of amount transferred to the beneficiaries of Nagaland is given below:S.No.	Financial Year	Amount transferred (in Crore Rs.)1.	2018-19	5.552.	2019-20	91.823.	2020-21	122.194.	2021-22 (till 30.11.2021)	79.70Total	299.26******
