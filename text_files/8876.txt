                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Fish Mortality Rate




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether fish mortality rate has increased a lot due to marine heat waves; (b)	 if so, whether the Government is considering to take any steps to save fishes; (c)	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said steps are likely to be taken; and (d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING:(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a)	No, Sir. 	Fish mortality due to heat waves have not been reported or observed from Indian seas.(b)	to (d) 	Does not arise                                                                *****
