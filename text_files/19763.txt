                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1140






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Environmental Clearance for Oil/Gas Exploration Projects




Pritam Gopinath Munde














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the production of crude oil and natural gas in India has been going downand if so, the reasons therefor;(b) whether the Government has decided to waive off environmental clearance foronshore and offshore gas as well as oil exploration projects and if so, the facts in thisregard;(c) whether the Government has assessed its consequences on Islands, fishingareas, breeding grounds and migratory routes, etc. and if so, the details thereof and ifnot, the reasons therefor; and(d) the steps taken by the Government to minimize the consequences of onshoreand offshore gas as well as oil exploration projects?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): Production of crude oil and Natural Gas in India for the last six years is as under:Year 2014-15 2015-16 2016-17 2017-18 2018-19 2019-20Crude Oil (MMT) 37.46 36.94 36.01 35.68 34.20 32.17Natural Gas (BCM) 33.65 32.25 31.90 32.65 32.87 31.18Natural Decline in all aging fields and increase in water-cut in major oil/gas producingfields are the main reasons for decline in oil/gas production.(b) to (d): MoEF&CC vide gazette Notification dated 16th January, 2020 has changedthe category for onshore and offshore oil and gas exploration projects from A to B2under EIA Notification, 2006 wherein the permissions related to Environment shall beaccorded by the State Environment Impact Assessment Authority. Ministry ofEnvironment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEF&CC) has not waived off clearancefor onshore and offshore gas and oil projects. Environmental concerns/safeguards areaddressed while issuing the environment clearance.***
