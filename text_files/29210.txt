                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

536






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Puri-Konark new Railway Line Project




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of the Puri-Konark New Railway Line Project;(b) the steps taken by the Railways to expedite the implementation of the project; and(c) whether the Railways has considered to sign the MoU with State Government for this project, if so, the expected date of its initiation and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c): Survey for construction of a new railway line between Puri and Konark (33 km) has been completed and the cost of the project has been assessed as ?725.78 crore. The survey report has been taken up for firming up the requirement and financial viability of the project, including aspect of cost sharing with State Government of Odisha.*****
