                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Accidents involving Two-Wheeler Riders




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether  there  has been a spike in the number of accidents and deaths of young two wheeler riders in various States including Kerala during the last three years and the current year;(b)	if so, the details thereof  and the total number of deaths of two wheeler riders in road accidents, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government has decided to establish highway interceptor squads on National Highways to prevent and detain such two wheeler riders; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken or proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?  






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) As per the data received from police department of all States/UTs within the category of motorized vehicle, two wheelers classified according to vehicle type accounted for the highest share in total road accidents as well as in fatalities during the last three calendar years i.e 2014 to 2017.  Details regarding the total number of road accidents, & persons killed in terms of road user category two wheelers during the calendar years 2014 to 2017 including Kerala is shown in the table below:Years	Total Road Accidents in India	Accidents involving two wheelers	Share in total road accidents	Total persons killed in India	Persons killed involving two wheelers	Share in total persons killed 2015	501,423	144391	28.8	146,133	36803	25.22016	480,652	162280	33.8	150,785	44366	29.42017	464,910	157723	33.9	147,913	44092	29.8For the state of KeralaYears	Total Road Accidents in Kerala	Accidents involving two wheelers in Kerala	Share in total road accidents in Kerala 	Total persons killed in road accidents in Kerala	Persons killed involving two wheelers in Kerala	Share in total persons killed in Kerala 2015	39014	13093	33.6	4196	998	23.82016	39420	13218	33.5	4287	1293	30.22017	38470	14821	38.5	4131	1371	33.2State-wise number of road accidents in India classified according to type of vehicles involving two wheelers during the calendar year 2015 to 2017 is given at Annexure. (c ) & (d)  No Sir, Government  has  not deployed any  interceptor squads on National Highways to prevent  and detain such two wheeler riders.
