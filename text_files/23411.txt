                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Government Godowns in Maharashtra




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details where Government Godowns are available for storing foodgrains and vegetables in Maharashtra district-wise; (b) 	the policy of the Government regarding setting up of godowns for storing foodgrains and vegetables; (c) 	the details of the beneficiaries who receive highly subsidized foodgrain under the National Food SecurityAct (NFSA), State/UT-wise particularly in Maharashtra; and (d) 	whether the Government proposes to review the percentage of the beneficiaries covered under National Food Security Scheme in the country again; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	Food Corporation of India (FCI) deals in procurement, storage and issuance of Wheat, Paddy / Rice and Pulses, meant for Public Distribution System (PDS) under NFSA. Details of godowns owned / hired by FCI for storing foodgrains in Maharashtra are at Annexure-I. ...........2/--2-	As far as National Horticulture Board (NHB), Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare is concerned, the Board is implementing only a scheme namely “Capital Investment Subsidy for Construction/Expansion/Modernization of Cold Storages and Storages for Horticulture Products”. The details of district-wise cold storage sanctioned in the State of Maharashtra from 2010-11 to 2020-21 (as on 18.03.2021) is given at Annexure-II.(b): Requirement of storage capacity depends on the level of procurement, & offtake under National Food Security Act (NFSA) & Other Welfare Scheme (OWS).  FCI continuously assesses and monitors the storage capacity and based on the storage gap assessment, storage capacities are created/hired. The Government has been implementing following schemes to meet the requirement of storage space for foodgrain under following schemes:-1-	Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) Scheme2-	Cover and Plinth (CAP) Hiring Scheme 20193-	Construction of SILO4-	Central Sector Scheme “Storage and Godown”	Under the Capital Investment Subsidy for Construction/Expansion/Modernization of Cold Storages and Storages for Horticulture Products scheme, credit linked back-ended subsidy @ 35% of the capital cost of the project in general areas and 50% in case of North East, Hilly States and Scheduled areas for Cold storage capacity above 5000 MT and up to 10000 MT is sanctioned for construction/ expansion /modernization of cold storage and Controlled Atmosphere (CA) store. In case of NE Region the capacity above 1000 MT are also eligible.(c): 	State/Union Territory-wise statement of number of beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Act, 2013 is at Annexure-III.(d) & (e): 	At present, no formal proposal for increasing the number of beneficiaries under the National Food Security Act, 2013 is under consideration of the Government.*****ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 4408 FOR ANSWER ON 23.03.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.Godowns owned / hired by FCI for storing foodgrains in Maharashtra(Figures in MT)S.
