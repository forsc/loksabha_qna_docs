                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

278






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Construction of Houses under PMAY-U in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	 the details of the houses constructed in Odisha under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U), year and urban local body-wise; and(b)	 the details of the total allocation made and expenditure incurred by the Government under this head in the State, year and urban local body-wise?






ANSWER




                    THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                               (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b): Details of the houses constructed/completed alongwith central assistance sanctioned/ approved, released and utilized in the State of Odisha under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U), year wise and Urban Local Body (ULB)  wise are at Annexure-I, Annexure-II, Annexure-III and Annexure-IV respectively.                                        *****
