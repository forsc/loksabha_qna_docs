                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5508






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Film and Television Institute of India




M V V Satyanarayana














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the aims and objectives of the ‘Film and Television Institute of India (FTII)’;(b)	the details of courses and scholarships available under this institute; and (c)	whether the Government is contemplating to open a regional office of the Film and Television Institute of India in Vijaywada/Visakhapatnam/ Rajahmundry, Andhra Pradesh in view of the persistent demand from the aspirants, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR):(a)	The aims and objectives of Film and Television Institute of India are enclosed at Annexure-A.(b)	The detail of courses and scholarships available under FTII is enclosed at Annexure-B.(c)	No, Sir.******************************************
