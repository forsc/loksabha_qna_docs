                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4357






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Bidi Workers




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the State-wise number of the bidi workers working in the bidi industries and the number of women and children therein;(b)whether the Government has taken any step to ensure minimum wages, education for children and benefits of health schemes to improve the condition of workers working in this hazardous industry; and (c)if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):		Details of the State-wise number of the bidi workers working in the bidi industries and the number of women therein is at annexure - I. No Children have been engaged in Bidi making(b) to (c):	The Bidi making does not come under Scheduled Employments in Central Sphere and fall under the jurisdiction of State Sphere. Healthcare facilities are provided to the bidi workers and their dependents through 10 Hospitals and 286 Dispensaries. Financial Assistance for Education is provided to the wards of the bidi workers ranging from Rs.250/- to Rs.15,000/- depending upon their class/courses.*                                            ******
