                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1892






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






USOF




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Universal Service Obligation Fund(USOF) was set up under Telecom Policy, 1999 and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the percentage of gross revenue required to be deposited by the telecom operator companies in the said fund as per the said Telecom Policy; (c) 	whether all the said companies have deposited the requisite amount in the USOF in accordance with the rules laid down in this regard and if so, the details of the deposits made as on date; (d) 	the details of the amount of funds utilized by the Government from the USOF for setting-up mobile networks in the country, location and State-wise, particularly in the backward and Scheduled Caste-dominated areas during the last three years along with the achievements made as a result thereof; and (e) 	the amount of money available in the said fund as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	As envisaged in the New Telecom Policy 1999, Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) was established under Indian Telegraph (Amendment) Act, 2003 which came into force from 1st April, 2002.(b)	As per the New Telecom Policy 1999, the percentage of revenue share towards universal access levy was to be decided by the Government in consultation with Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI)  At present, as per license agreement the License fee shall be 8% of the Adjusted Gross Revenue (AGR), inclusive of USO Levy which is 5% of the AGR.(c)	The definition of AGR is subjudice in Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, so companies have deposited the amounts on self – assessment basis, based on their understanding of AGR. The Universal Service Obligation(USO) Levy collected since inception is Rs 99,674.59 crore.(d)	 The details are at Annexure. (e)	An amount of Rs. 50,554 Crore was available in the USO Fund at the end of May 2019.
