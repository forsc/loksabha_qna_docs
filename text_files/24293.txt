                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Women Trained under the Revised Scheme




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      the percentage of the budget allocated for the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana in January 2021 and utilization thereof; (b)      the number of people proposed to be trained in 2021 and already been trained so far, State-wise; (c)	the percentage of women trained under the revised scheme in the first half of 2021?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) i.e. PMKVY 3.0 was launched on 15th January, 2021 with an aim to train 8 lakh people with an outlay of Rs. 948.9 crore. Till June’21, Rs. 193.1 crore (approx.) has been released to implementing agencies which is 20.35% of the total outlay of the scheme. State/UT-wise number of candidates trained under PMKVY 3.0, as on 10.07.2021, is given at Annexure.(c)	Under PMKVY 3.0, as on 10.07.2021, 1.12 lakh candidates have been trained; out of which, 0.57 lakh candidates are women which is around 51 % of total trained candidates.
