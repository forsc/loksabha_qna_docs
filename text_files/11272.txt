                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Soil Health Card Scheme




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ramya  Haridas
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether positive results are being achieved in agricultural production after the introduction of the soil health card scheme; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c)	 whether the said soil health card scheme has been implemented in all the parts of the country; (d)	 if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Delhi, Uttar Pradesh and Bihar; (e)	 if not, the time by which it is planned to be completed by the Government; and(f)        the details of the action plan in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):      National Productivity Council (NPC) in its study on ‘Soil Testing Infrastructure for Faster Delivery of Soil Health Card in India’ submitted in February, 2017, reported that there has been overall increase in yield of crops to the tune of 5-6% due to application of fertilizer and micro nutrients as per recommendations available in the Soil Health Cards.(c) to (f):	 Soil Health Card Scheme has been implemented in all States/Union Territories (UTs) of the country except UTs of Chandigarh where as reported, main occupation of farmers is dairying and Lakshadweep where farmers use organic manures only.          Statement indicating State- wise number of Soil Health Cards issued to farmers during cycle I (2015-17), cycle II (2017-19) and current year (2019-20) are given at   Annexure.
