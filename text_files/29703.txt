Reservation for SCs/STs in PSBs




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of reservation for SC/STprovided with employment in Public Sector Banks andother financial institutions including Life Insurance Corporation of India for the last three years, State-wise;(b) the total credit facilities and other benefits beingprovided by such Public Sector Banks and Life InsuranceCorporation of India to SC/ST people for the last threeyears, State-wise; and(c) the total number of SC/ST people benefitted inthe State of Tamil Nadu during the said period?






ANSWER

Further, it is stated that credit facilities are not being provided by Life Insurance Corporation of India.
