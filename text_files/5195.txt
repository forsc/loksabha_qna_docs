                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4768






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Vocational Training to Eligible Candidates




Bhartruhari Mahtab













                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has met the target of providing vocational training to eligible candidates under the National Skill Development Corporation Programme; (b)	if so, the number of candidates benefited under the said programme during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise; (c)	the year-wise and State/UT-wise number of candidates who have passed out and have been employed based on NSDC’s vocational training during the said period; (d)	whether the Government has tied up with various industrial associations like FICCI, CII to run such vocational training programmes under their mandatory Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) contributions; and (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) 	National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) meets the training targets through its training partners running training programmes across the country. Training targets and achievement along with the placement (year-wise) under fee based model are as under:Financial Year	No. of candidates trained in fee based model	No. of candidates placed2016-17	15,91,519	6,64,9522017-18	     18,82,053 	        7,72,085 2018-19	     20,18,069 	        6,62,315 Total	54,91,641	     20,99,352 	NSDC is also implementing agency of Government schemes namely Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) which is the flagship skill development scheme. The details of candidates enrolled, trained and placed during the last 3 year under PMKVY are as under:Year	Enrolled	Trained	Assessed	Certified	Reported Placed2016 - 17	597214	223885	115657	85104	2812017 - 18	2408401	2154883	1860809	1624695	4543372018 - 19	1729500	1894429	1650851	1458326	6747172019 – 20 (till 12.06.2019)	626114	637335	593512	622834	77714Grand Total	5361229	4910532	4220829	3790959	1207049(d) to (e)	The Government has not tied up with any industrial associations like FICCI, CII to run such vocational training programmes under their mandatory Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR). However, National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) under Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) has signed Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with several PSUs and Corporates to promote skill development initiatives under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).
