                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

601






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






AYUSH Hospitals in Scheduled Tribes Areas




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of AYUSH Hospitals, dispensaries and Health Centres existing in the scheduled tribes’ areas, State/UT-wise particularly in Assam, Odisha, Jharkhand, Meghalaya, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh; (b) Whether the Ministry of AYUSH has any Scheme to promote and establish AYUSH Hospitals/AYUSH Medical Colleges in scheduled districts and Healthcare systems in the scheduled areas among the tribals in the country and if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of plans, measures and strategies formulated by the Government to encourage and motivate the Scheduled Tribes Students to become AYUSH Practitioners; and(d) the steps undertaken by the Government to engage and promote the traditional medicine practitioners as AYUSH practitioners?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) The number of AYUSH hospitals, dispensaries and AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres State/UT-wise including the scheduled tribes’ areas, particularly in Assam, Odisha, Jharkhand, Meghalaya, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh is at Annexure -I.(b) Public Health being a state subject, the primary responsibility to establish AYUSH Hospitals/AYUSH Medical Colleges in scheduled districts and Healthcare systems in the scheduled areas among the tribals in the country, lies with the respective State/UT Governments. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is provision of financial assistance for setting up of 50 bedded/30 bedded/10 bedded integrated AYUSH hospitals, establishment of new AYUSH Colleges in the States where availability of AYUSH teaching institutions is inadequate in Government Sector & setting up of AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres in the country including tribals area. The detailed status of units approved under NAM is furnished at Annexure-II. (c) 7.5% seats have been reserved in overall reservation of Under Graduate/Post Graduate all India quota seats for Scheduled Tribes.(d)  Ministry of Ayush engage and promote traditional practitioners through its Research Councils i.e. Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS), Central Council for Research in Unani Medicine (CCRUM) Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH) and Central Council for Research in Siddha (CCRS) through documentation & validation of medicinal plants used by various tribal groups for the treatment of various diseases.**********
