                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3035






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Community Health Centres and Primary Health Centres




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will be Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)  whether it is a fact that maximising the efficiency of Primary Healthcare Centres (PHCs) will result in a significant saving of funds;(b)  if so, the steps proposed to be taken to strengthen the primary healthcare in the country;(c)  whether any concrete plan is being chalked out for improving the healthcare at village/Panchayat level to realize the New India vision by 2022, as healthcare at village level is languishing the most;(d)  the present status of Community Health Centres (CHCs) and Primary Health Centres (PHCs) in the country State/UT-wise including Maharashtra and Jharkhand;(e)  whether there are any studies to gauge the efficiency of the PHC in the country; and(f)  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (c):  The National Health Policy, 2017 lays down the objective of providing affordable, equitable, and accessible health care in the country. To meet these objectives, technical and financial support is provided to States under National Health Mission(NHM) for strengthening health care systems based on proposals received from the States/UTs. Public Health and Hospitals,being a State Subject, the primary responsibility of providing health care lies with the respective State/UT Governments,including in the states of Maharashtra and Jharkhand. For providing comprehensive primary health care at the community level, the Government Of India has launched Ayushman Bharat-Health and Wellness Centres (AB-HWC) initiative, in the year 2018, whereby 1.5 lakh Sub-Health Centres(SHCs) / Primary Health Centres are being upgraded into HWCs.A package of 12 health services are being provided at these HWCs, which  reduce the need for secondary and tertiary treatment as well as lessen the burden of “out of pocket expenditure”, for those seeking primary health care,  closer to their homes. Details of 12 packages provided at HWCs is at Annexure-IAlso, the Fifteenth Finance Commission has recommended grants aggregating to Rs.70,051 Crores, over the period of five years (2021-2026) through local governments, for strengthening healthcare system at the primary health care level.(d): The present status of Community Health Centres (CHCs) and Primary Health Centres (PHCs) in the country, State/UT-wise, including in Maharashtra and Jharkhandare at Annexure II(e) & (f): An evaluation study on the functioning of the AB-HWCs has been conducted. The Ministry of Health conducts regular reviews with the States/UTs regarding the functioning of the Health system including of the PHCs & the AB-HWCs. The Ministry also monitors the performance of the PHCs through the Health Management Information System(HMIS) & AB-HWC portal.
