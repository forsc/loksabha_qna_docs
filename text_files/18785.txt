                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Allocation for Rural Development Schemes




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has failed in the efficacy of the rural schemes due to their poor implementation process and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government has not yet sanctioned the allocated funds for Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) and Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Rural) for 2019 -2020 and 2020-2021and has changed the revised budget over (-)2 percent for 2020-21;(c) 	if so, the budget allocation and expenditure sanctioned from 2017-18 till December 2020; and(d) 	the present status of women Self-Help Groups(SHG) under MUDRA scheme during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	No, Sir.  The Ministry of Rural Developmentaccords emphasis on proper implementation of all its rural development schemes. In order to ensure the programme benefits reach the rural poor, the Ministry has evolved a comprehensive multi-level and multi-format system of monitoring and evaluation of the implementation of rural development programmes, including Performance Review Committee Meetings, District Development Co-ordination and Monitoring Committee (“DISHA”) meetings, National Level Monitors, Area Officers Schemes, Common Review Mission, Concurrent Evaluation and Impact Assessment Studies. As per the reports/returns received from the state governments/UT Administrations, the implementation of these programmes have been found to be satisfactory. Third-Party evaluation of the Schemes is also carried out regularly and actions, as appropriate, are taken on the findings.(b)&(c): The persondays generated, allocations of fund and release under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) during the last three years and current year are detailed below:*There was a budget allocation of Rs. 61,500 crore at BE for Mahatma Gandhi NREGA and the allocation has been increased to Rs. 1,11,500 crore at RE during 2020-21.  Under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin(PMAY-G), the overall target is to construct 2.95 Crore houses during the period 2016-17 to 2021-22, wherein 1 Crore houses were set to be achieved in Phase-I (2016-17 to 2018-19) and 1.95 Crore houses in Phase-II (2019-20 to   2021-22). A total of 1.26 crore houses have been constructed so far as on 28th January, 2021.
