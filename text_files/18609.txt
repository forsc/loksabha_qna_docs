                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

18






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Public Investment in Agriculture




Arjunlal Meena













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                








                                                    Anil Firojiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated the National Agriculture Development Scheme with the main objective of boosting public investment in agriculture and allied sectors; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c)	 the names of the States where the said scheme is operational; and (d) 	whether any scheme has been formulated in districts and States on the basis of climatic conditions for agriculture, technologies and availability of natural resources; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):	No such Scheme has been formulated by this Ministry. However, Government of India has launched the Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan (ABA) to strengthen Infrastructure, Logistics, Capacity Building, Governance and Administrative Reforms for Agriculture.********
