                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Subsidy for Residue Management Machinery




Preneet Kaur














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to increase the subsidy provided to farmers for procurement of insitu-crop residue management machinery;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to allocate extra funds of Rs 100/- per quintal, to the scheme brought by the Government of Punjab, to compensate small and marginal farmers; and(d)	if so, the time period within which such funds will be disbursed and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	There is no such proposal as of now to increase the level of subsidy. However, as per existing norms, subsidy @ 50% of the cost of machinery is already being provided to the farmers for purchase of machines on individual ownership basis and subsidy @80% of the project cost is provided to the Co-operative Societies of farmers, Farmers Producer Organizations (FPOs), Registered Farmers Societies and Panchayats for establishment of Custom Hiring Centres, under the Central Sector Scheme on ‘Promotion of Agricultural Mechanization for In-Situ Management of Crop Residue in the States of Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh and NCT of Delhi’.   (c):	As of now, no such proposal is there.(d):	Question does not arise. *******
