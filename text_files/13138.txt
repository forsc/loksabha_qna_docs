                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2015






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Implementation of PMKSY




Chandra Prakash Choudhary













                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of various schemes/projects being implemented under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY), along with the details of investments committed and status of these schemes/projects, State-wise, including Jharkhand;(b)	the funds utilised, allocated and released under the scheme during the last three years, State-wise including Jharkhand;(c)	the details of financial assistance extended for setting up of fruit and vegetable processing units, State-wise and the details of such units in Jharkhand covered/ benefited under these schemes during the last three years;(d)	whether the funds released are sufficient to achieve the desired objectives of the   scheme in Jharkhand; and(e)	whether implementation of PMKSY has helped in ensuring remunerative prices for agriculture produces to the farmers and doubling their income, if so, the details of the benefits that have reached the farmers through this scheme since inception of the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a): The State-wise projects assisted under Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY) along with investment leveraged is placed at Annexure-I.(b): State-wise allocation of funds is not made under PMKSY. The Scheme is demand-driven and grants-in-aid is released to approved projects which are selected against the expression of interest (EoI) issued by the Ministry from time to time. Funds allocated and released/utilised under PMKSY during the last three years is as follows:Year	Funds Allocated (Rs. in crore)	Funds Released/Utilized (Rs. in crore)2016-17	688.56	677.162017-18	633.84	605.582018-19	870.33	591.38(c) & (d): Details of fruit and vegetable processing units assisted under the scheme of Creation / Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) is placed at Annexure-II. No such project is approved in the State of Jharkhand for want of eligible proposal.(e): Based on the information received from the promoters of the projects under schemes of PMKSY an estimated fifteen lakh farmers benefitted out of the completed/operational projects by disposing of their surplus produce which otherwise would have gone waste without such food processing projects/units.*****
