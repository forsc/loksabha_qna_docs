                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

632






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Suryamitra Mobile App




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government  has launched or proposes to launch “Suryamitra” mobile app and if so, the facilities being provided to the people through this app; (b)  the number of people who have used this app so far;(c) the number of employment opportunities generated for youths through this app; and(d) the details of fees being charged for the services provided on the app and the amount of fees recovered so far for the service?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) Ministry of New and Renewable Energy is implementing the Suryamitra Skill Development Programme through National Institute of Solar Energy (NISE), an autonomous organization under the Ministry. In June 2016, NISE launched a GPS based Suryamitra Mobile Application on a pilot basis to connect trained Suryamitras to those who need  services like installation, operation and  maintenance of solar equipment such as solar rooftop systems, solar water heating systems etc. The Mobile Application was discontinued in October 2017 because it was not found useful.(b) 2084 individuals used the App. (c) 2837 service requests were registered by 2084 individuals but only 99 requests were        responded to by the trained Suryamitras.(d) The App was free of cost for the end users as well as for the trained Suryamitras. *****
