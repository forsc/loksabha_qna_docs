                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

444






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Funds under Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan




Mahesh Sahoo













                                                    Abdul Khaleque
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has transferred any funds under the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan during the year 2020-21 ; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (c) 	whether the Government has issued guidelines for the release and utilisation of grants to the Gram Panchayats and local bodies under the 14th Finance Commission Award for Gram Panchayat Development Plan (GPDP)?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL)(a)	Yes Sir.(b)	Under the scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA), State-wise details of fund released during 2020-21 is attached as Annexure.(c)	Yes Sir.***
