                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1262






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Migrant Labourers in Rajasthan




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of migrant labourers in Rajasthan who had returned during COVID-19 lockdowns; (b)the number of districts of Rajasthan covered under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY);(c)the number of migrant labourers in Rajasthan who were imparted skill development training under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, district-wise;(d)the sector-wise details of job opportunities that are provided to migrant labourers after training under PMKVY; and(e)the details of the number of migrant labourers placed in each sector, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		13.08 Lakh migrant labourers returned during COVID-19 pandemic to Rajasthan as reported by the Government of Rajasthan. (b):		All the 33 Districts of Rajasthan are covered under the the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) scheme. However, as a part of Garib Kalyan Rojgar Abhiyaan (GKRA), special training programme under PMKVY was implemented in 22 districts having high concentration of returnee migrants during COVID – 19. (c) to (e):	The details are enclosed at Annexure I & Annexure II.**                                                  *********
