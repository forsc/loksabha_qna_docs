                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3278






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Departmental Exam for Kendriya Vidyalaya Teachers




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the departmental examination was conducted for the teachers ofKendriya Vidyalaya last year;(b) if so, whether the results of the said examination have not been announced tilldate;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) whether the Government proposes to announce the results of the saiddepartmental examination shortly in view of the difficulties being faced by theteachers and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The results of departmental examination have been announcedby the Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan.(c) & (d) Do not arise.*****
