                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3084






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Using Moringa as Water Purifier




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the moringa seeds have proven to be a perfect water purifier besides beingvery cost effective and healthy;(b) if so, whether the Government is considering investing more funds into the researchand development of this local vegetable plant to achieve better and larger benefits forproviding cheaper and clean drinking water to the masses and if so, the details thereof;and (c) whether the Ministry has coordinated with the Ministry of Agriculture for increasingthe production of Moringa vegetables for larger, better and beneficial use and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	& (b)  The research and development project on “Use of MoringaOleifera Seed Flour as a potential source for water purification” was assigned to Central Food Technology Research Institute, Mysoreby the Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation (DDWS) withan objective to  carry out the research on the use of MoringaOleifera Seed Flour as a potential source for water purification.In the research report, it has been concludedthat the MoringaOleifera Seeds Flour is effective to remove only  turbidity of the water and doesn’t have any effect on parameters like Chemical Oxygen Demand, acidity, alkalinity, hardness, pH, microbial activity, TDS and conductivity. It needs to be coupled with other water treating compounds or processes to produce a safe and economic water purifier. No further request for funding the said R&D project is received in theDepartment  of Drinking Water and Sanitation.(c)  In view of the above, does not arise.
