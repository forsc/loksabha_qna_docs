16.03.2020






Funds Allocated for Maintenance of Monuments




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the district-wise list of protected monuments and protected archaeological locations of cultural importance in Haryana and Jharkhand;(b)	the details of the budget allocated for the maintenance of the said monuments;(c)	whether the Government has received proposal seeking inclusion of more monuments in the said list/category;(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto;(e)	the list of monuments of cultural importance destroyed in the country including Rajasthan, Haryana, Maharashtra and Jharkhand; and(f)	the efforts being made by the Government for the maintenance of the said monuments and protected archaeological locations?
