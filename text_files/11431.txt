                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Society for Welfare Schemes




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formed any society to run all the Centrally sponsored farmer welfare schemes; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has found different methods to improve monitoring and targeting on farmers schemes; and (d) 	if so, details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a)  &   (b):	A National Farmer’s Welfare Programme Implementation Society has been registered on 23.12.2019 to operate certain Central Sector Schemes related to farmers’ welfare.(c) & (d):	 The Department regularly monitors the progress of schemes through meetings with concerned officers of the State Governments/UTs, visits of nodal officers of the Department to the States/UTs, video conferencing, Rabi and Kharif Conferences, etc.  Besides the beneficiary details of different schemes of the Department are uploaded in the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) portal for monitoring and tracking of beneficiaries, etc.  Further, financial assistance extended to the beneficiary is directly credited into their bank accounts through the DBT mode.******
