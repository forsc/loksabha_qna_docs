                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3759






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Delay in getting Passport




M. P. Abdussamad Samadani














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has noticed any delay in getting the passport for the applicants at Malappuram Passport Seva Kendra as compared to the other centres;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the remedial steps taken by the Government in this regard; and(c)	whether the number of tatkal passport applicants is more at Malappuram as compared to the other centres and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) 	There has been no delay in issuing passports for the applicants at Mallapuram PSK.(b)	Not applicable. (c)   Malappuram PSK being the biggest PSK under Regional Passport Office (RPO) Kozhikode and one of the biggest in the entire state of Kerala, the number of Tatkal applicants are proportionately high at Malappuram PSK. Daily 100 Tatkal appointments are released at Malappuram PSK, which is higher than the daily release of Tatkal appointments at other PSKs under RPO Kozhikode (Tatkal appointments released at other PSKs vary from 50 to 70 per day). The details of Tatkal applications processed at all five PSKs, including Malappuram, during the last two months are as follows:<pre>Name of PSKsTatkal applications processed during last 2 monthsJanuary 2022February 2022Malappuram13331387Kozhikode779828Vadakara406453Kannur545671Payyannur600670Total36634009</pre>***
