                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3489






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Targets under PMGSY




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has made any assessment to find out the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) roads constructed during the last three years and the current year particularly during lockdown periods in various States and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(b) 	whether traffic survey for all the proposed roads under PMGSY needs to be done at the time of preparation of DPRs; and(c) 	if so, the details of the criteria being adopted for conducting traffic survey for such roads?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	The details of road works constructed during the last three financial years and current year (as on 5th August 2021) under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) are given at Annexure.(b & (c) As per PMGSY guidelines, Programme Implementation Units (PIU) shall ensure that independent third-party traffic survey is done on all candidate roads and corresponding Major District Roads (MDRs). 3 days traffic count shall be taken at the time of Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) preparation in case the projected traffic is 2 Million Standard Axle (MSA) or less; and 7 days traffic count shall be taken at the time of DPRs preparation if the projected traffic is more than 2 MSA. As per the PMGSY-III guidelines and amendment carried out by the Ministry, independent 3rd Party traffic survey including axle-load survey shall be carried out for all the proposed roads where the projected traffic is more than 1 MSA irrespective of carriageway width as stipulated in IRC:SP:72:2015 through National Institute of Technology (NIT)/ State Technical Agency (STA)/ Government Engineering Institutes or independent third party agencies/ consultants. Independent 3rd party traffic survey on roads of more than 1 MSA is to be carried out by installing Automatic Traffic Counter and Classifier (ATCC).***** Annexure referred to in reply of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3489 for 10.08.2021AnnexureState-wise details of road works constructed under PMGSY during the last three years and the current year (as on 05.08.2021)
