                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

322






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Schemes for Orphans and Physically Challenged Persons




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the schemes being run by the Government for the orphans and physically challenged persons; and(b) the details of the amount spent under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) for the orphans and physically challenged persons during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a)	1. Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities has no scheme for providing assistance for orphans exclusively. However, the following schemes are being run for the welfare of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs):(i)	Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS) (ii)	Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids/Appliances (ADIP)(iii)	Scheme for Implementation of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (SIPDA)(iv)	Scholarship for Students with Disabilities (v)	Indian Spinal Injury Centre(vi)	Setting up of State Spinal Injury Centre(vii)	Central Sector Scheme of “Support for establishment / modernization / capacity augmentation of Braille Presses” Details of the schemes are at Annexure-I. 2. The Ministry of Women and Child Development (M/o WCD) is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme i.e. “Child Protection Services (CPS)” by providing financial assistance with an objective to create a safe and secure environment for overall development of children in need of care and protection by undertaking a situational analysis of children in difficult circumstances. M/o WCD provides financial support under CPS for children with special needs @ Rs.  10.48 lakhs (per annum).As per Section 2 (14) (iv) of Juvenile Justice  Act, 2015, a child who is mentally ill or physically challenged or suffering from terminal or incurable disease, having no one to support or look after or having parents or guardians unfit to take care is included as a “child in need of care and protection”. However, the primary responsibility of execution of the Act lies with the State/UTs.(b)	The details of amount spent under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) for PwDs is enclosed as Annexure-II.  *****
