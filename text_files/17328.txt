                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

826






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Functional House Hold Tap Connection (FHTC) inOdisha




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the percentage of households with Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) in Odisha;(b) the data for urban and rural districts of Odisha indicating the percentage increase in Households with FHTCs from 2019 to 2020;(c) whether any timeline has been fixed for making FHTCs available to all households; and(d) if so, details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)& (b) As reported by State, as on 01.04.2019, 3.74% rural household in the State of Odisha were having tap water connections, which has increased to 12.33% as on 14.09.2020.(c) & (d) To enable every rural household in the country to have potable water through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024,Government of India is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM)-Har Ghar Jal in partnership with States, at an estimated outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore.****
