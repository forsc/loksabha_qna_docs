                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1378






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Child Labour Technical Advisory Committee




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Child Labour Technical Advisory Committee has held any meetings in the last three years;(b)if so, the minutes of the meetings thereof; and(c)if not, the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	Technical Advisory Committee held three meetings for its deliberations since 07.10.2016. The Committee submits its recommendations to the Ministry of Labour and Employment for amendment in the schedule of Child and Adolescent Labour (Prohibition and Regulation) Act, 1986. Based on  its recommendations, amendment in the Schedule of  Child and Adolescent Labour (Prohibition and Regulation) Act, 1986  was lastly  carried out in the Year 2017, vide Gazette Notification dated 30.08.2017. (c):	Does not arise in view of above.  ******
