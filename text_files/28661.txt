                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

869






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Allotment of Houses under Atal Awas Yojana




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of houses allotted under Atal Awas Yojana in other States including Haryana, Assam and North Eastern States;(b)	whether the targets fixed have been achieved by the Government within stipulated time;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether any accountability has been fixed by the Government in this regard; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE  MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS               (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (e): Government of India is not implementing any housing scheme with the name of Atal Awas Yojana. However, Government of India is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana to provide all weather pucca house to all eligible families/beneficiaries by 2022.                       *****
