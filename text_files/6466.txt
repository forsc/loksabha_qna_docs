                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

229






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Shagun Web Portal




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the salient features of "Shagun" web portal of the Government;(b) the details of funds to be allocated under the said programme across the country including theState of Gujarat;(c) whether the Government has linked the website of all the schools under it;(d) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(e) the details of the target fixed under the same; and(f) the number of States likely to be linked with this programme in the beginning along with thedetails thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The salient features of School Education Shagun Online Junction of all portals andwebsites relating to the Department of School Education & Literacy, States and UnionTerritories (UTs) is as under:<pre >? Junction has more than 80 portals and websites which include those on schemes,autonomous bodies, monitoring apps & portals and the school education related websiteof the States and UTs.? Enable access to web resources relating to school education in a holistic manner.? Single platform to access all e-learning material thereby making it easier for students andteachers to make an informed choice.? Bring focus on the Student, the Teacher, the Principle, and the School –entities whichhave lost their relevance in the mesh of multiple and siloed structure of the informationsystem. Also, enable availability of information on thematic issues.</pre>(b): No separate fund has been allocated for the above programme.(c) & (d): At present, websites of all the schools under Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan(KVS), Navadoya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) have been linked through their respective KVS andNVS websites. Except this few websites of all the Schools affiliated with Central Board ofSecondary Education (CBSE) have been linked with CBSE Affiliation website, however thedetails of all the CBSE schools is present on this website. Presently, websites of schools underStates and UTs are not linked.KVS Websites URL :<pre>? https://kvs.gov.in/? https://kvs.gov.in/about-kvs/directories/kvs-directory</pre>NVS Websites URL :<pre>? https://navodaya.gov.in/? https://navodaya.gov.in/nvs/en/Contact-Us/RO/</pre>CBSE Affiliated Schools :<pre>? http://cbse.gov.in? http://cbseaff.nic.in/</pre>States & UTs :<pre>? http://seshagun.gov.in/states_uts</pre>(e): No target has been fixed.(f): Does not arise in respect of reply of part (e).****
