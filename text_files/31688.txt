                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3027






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Closure of Khadi Sales Outlets




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether some of the Khandi sales outlets of the Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) have been closed down during the last severn years; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) 	the reasons of their closure; and (d) 	the details of people who were employee but lost their jobs due to closure of these Khadi sales outlets?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) to (c): 	Yes Sir.  During the last seven years, a total of 122 sales outlets of Khadi Institutions have been closed due to the un-violability of the location.  However, 624 new sales outlets of Khadi Institutions have been opened during the same period at the viable places.  State-wise list of closed and newly opened Khadi Institutions is annexed.(d): 	No manpower have become unemployed due to the closure of the sales outlets as these people have been redeployed in other activities of the Khadi Institutions.
