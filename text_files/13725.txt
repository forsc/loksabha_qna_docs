                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2519






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Implementation of DAY-NULM




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the extent to which the Government has achieved the objectives of implementing the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) in the country;(b)	the number of beneficiaries who improved their livelihood under the said mission across the country during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(c)	the details of the funds provided by the Government to States along with its utilizations under the said mission during the said period, State/UT-wise;(d)	whether the cases of irregularities/ corruption/ diversion of funds in implementation of the said mission have come to the notice of the Government during the said period and if so, the details thereof along with action taken thereon; and(e)	the other steps taken by the Government for effective implementation of the said mission across the country?






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) & (b)	"Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM)", implemented since 2014, aims at reducing poverty and vulnerability of urban poor households. So far, 4,39,780 Self Help Groups have been formed, out of which 3,34,971 SHGs have been given revolving funds; 10,58,599 candidates have been skill trained, out of which 5,92,528 candidates are placed for self or wage employment. Further,4,93,734 beneficiaries have been assisted for setting up of individual/ group micro enterprises, and 6,00,712 number of loans have been given to SHGs under SHG Bank-Linkage programme. So far 1367 Shelters for Urban Homeless are made functional. Also, 2,449 cities have completed street vendors survey, wherein 15,35,240 street vendors have been identified and 9,25,765 street vendors have been issued identity cards. A statement showing state-wise physical progress of States/UTs under various components of DAY-NULM, during Financial Year 2016-17 to 2019-20 (as on 28.02.2020), is at Annexure-I.(c)	A statement showing state-wise and year-wise details of funds released to States/UTs and those utilized by the State/UT Governments under DAY-NULM, during FY 2016-17 to 2019-20 (as on 28.02.2020),  is at Annexure-II.(d) 	Under DAY-NULM funds are allocated to States/UTs in a consolidated manner, out of which appropriate amount is apportioned by States/UTs for various components, as per their requirements. The General Financial Rules 2017 provide for submission of audited statements by the States/UTs on annual basis certifying the funds have been used for the purpose they are meant for.
