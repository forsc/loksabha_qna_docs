                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Centrally Sponsored Schemes




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 125TO BE ANSWERED ON 02.02.2021CENTRALLY SPONSORED SCHEMES †125. KUNWAR DANISH ALI:Will the Minister of  SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details and the number of centrally sponsored schemes being run in the country; (b) whether the Government proposes to reduce the number of such schemes or to discontinue them; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government is considering to conduct social audit of welfare schemes of social sector; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) :  The details of centrally sponsored schemes presently  being run by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment are enclosed at Annexure. (d) & (e):  The Schemes are reviewed and evaluated by a third party to find out the impact of Schemes at grass root level and changes in the life of beneficiaries after the implementation of the Scheme. ****Annexure Annexure as referred to in reply to part (a) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 125 due for answer on 2.2.2021 S.  No.	Programme/Schemes1	Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC Students studying in class IX and X2	Post Matric Scholarship for SC Students3	Pre-Matric Scholarships for the children of those engaged in unclean occupation involving cleaning and prone to health hazards4	Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojana for Boys5	Pradhan  Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana 6	Central Assistance to States/UTs for effective implementation of the Protection of Civil Right Act 1955 and Prevention of Atrocities Act, 1989 7	Assistance to Scheduled Castes Development Corporations 8	Pre-Matric Scholarship for OBCs students9	Post-Matric Scholarship for OBCs students10	Construction of Hostel for OBC Boys and Girls11	Dr. Ambedkar Scheme of Pre-Matric Scholarship for students belonging to Economically Backward Classes (EBCs)12	Dr. Ambedkar Scheme of Pre-Matric Scholarship  and Post Matric Scholarship for students belonging to Denotified  and Nomadic Tribes  (DNTs)13	Nanaji Deshmukh Scheme of Construction of Hostels for DNT Boys and Girls14.	 	National Action Plan for Senior Citizens15.	 	National Action Plan for Drug Demand Reduction****
