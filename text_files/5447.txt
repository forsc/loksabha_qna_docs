                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4925






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Demand and Supply of Milk in North East India




Queen Oja














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has made any assessment regarding demand and supply of milk in North Eastern States of the country including Assam;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	 the names of the schemes being implemented by the Union Government to promote dairy farming in North Eastern States including Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER	THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying has not made any assessment regarding demand   and supply of milk in North Eastern States of the country including Assam. However, a study on current and future demand of milk and milk products in the country including North Eastern States has been commissioned by National Dairy Development Board under National Dairy Plan- I. The major findings of this study are expected to be available by November 2019.(b)	Does not arise in view of reply at (a).  (c)	The Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing “Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme” to promote dairy farming in North Eastern States including Assam.*****
