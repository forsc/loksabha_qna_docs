                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

157






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Mahila Coir Yojana




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Sandhya Ray
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing the Mahila Coir Yojana (MCY) in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives including its implementation status in the country, State/UT-wise including Tripura;(c)	the number of women beneficiaries of the Yojana in the country along with the financial allocation made for this Yojana during each of the last three years and the current year; (d)	whether the government has achieved the objectives for which the Yojana was started, if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the problems faced by the Government in implementing this Yojana and the other steps taken by the Government for more participation of women in coir production and its distribution?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (e): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *157 FOR ANSWER ON 28.11.2019(a) & (b):	Coir Board, a statutory body under the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), is implementing the Mahila Coir Yojana (MCY) which is a component under the umbrella Scheme “Coir Vikas Yojana (CVY)” for development of coir industry in the major coconut producing states of the country. Mahila Coir Yojana is a women-oriented self employment scheme implemented by Coir Board for imparting training to women artisans on conversion of coir fibre into yarn. The amount of stipend given to the trainees is Rs.3000/- per month. During the year 2017-18, the scheme was approved for continuation from 2017-18 to 2019-20 with the following objectives:•	To provide training on sophisticated machinery/advanced technology in order to scale up the skill of the Mahila Coir Workers.•	To integrate MCY training along with LBI (Livelihood Business Incubation) in order to make the MCY trainees more entrepreneurial.•	To encourage the trained women to avail of assistance under Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) to set up coir units.State/UT-wise details of training, including Tripura, provided under MCY is at Annexure-I. (c):   	The details of financial allocation and beneficiaries trained under Mahila Coir Yojana during the last three years  and current year are as follows:<pre>Year	Financial allocation (Rs. In Lakhs)	Number of Trainees2016-17	121.50	22742017-18	44.70	12092018-19	166.00	21952019-20	182.60	796* </pre>    *Up to 31.10.2019.  In addition, 280 Nos. of Trainees are undergoing training. (d) & (e):    The objectives of the scheme have largely been achieved with training provided to 6474 women workers. Government is covering more and more women workers by developing new machinery and improving manufacturing processes for making these activities less strenuous.
