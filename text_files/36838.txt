                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4030






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Investment by LIC




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of investments made by LIC of India in State Government securities, State-wise; and (b)	the details of the Railway projects executed from the funds given by the LIC of India during financial year 2015-2020?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (a): State-wise details of investment by Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) in State Government securities outstanding as at 31.12.2021, are at Annex.(b): Ministry of Railways has informed that over the financial year 2015-16 to 2019-20, ?1,01,234 crore were provided by the Indian Railway Finance Corporation for financing railway projects, which includes ? 20,500 crore of funds invested by LIC. These funds were utilised in enhancement projects of railways, mostly doubling and electrification projects.***** AnnexLok Sabha unstarred question no. 4030, regarding "Investment by LIC"Investments made by LIC in State Government securities as on 31.12.2021In crore RupeesState/ Union territory	Investment amountAndhra Pradesh	92,862.19Arunachal Pradesh	1,913.66Assam	7,514.73Bihar	31,897.43Chhattisgarh	8,862.51Goa	5,226.16Gujarat	43,310.68Haryana	43,830.28Himachal Pradesh	11,110.71Jammu and Kashmir	11,295.85Jharkhand	17,295.19Karnataka	36,635.32Kerala	55,517.81Madhya Pradesh	42,345.91Maharashtra	76,398.68Manipur	2,800.29Meghalaya	2,664.71Mizoram	686.38Nagaland	3,541.26Odisha	7,002.11Puducherry	1,505.88Punjab	43,413.74Rajasthan	49,024.21Sikkim	2,366.24Tamil Nadu	1,04,009.50Telangana	90,099.85Tripura	3,946.58Uttar Pradesh	58,830.29Uttarakhand	12,502.15West Bengal	1,04,448.86Source: Life Insurance Corporation of India
