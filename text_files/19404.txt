                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

699






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






AYUSH hospitals and dispensaries under NAM




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA,YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the number of AYUSH hospitals and dispensaries upgraded during the last three years under the National Ayush Mission (NAM) in the Country, State/UT-wise, Stream-wise;(b) the details of Primary Health Centres (PHCs), Community Health Centres (CHCs) and District Hospitals (DHs) that have been upgraded with AYUSH facilities, State/UT-wise;(c) the total number of seats offered to students at various AYUSH educational institutions under NAM, since its inception;(d) the increase, if any, in the number of seats in the past three years; and(e) the estimated amount spent by the Government on NAM during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a):	The number of AYUSH hospitals and dispensaries upgraded during the last three years under the National AYUSH Mission (NAM) is furnished at Annexure-I.(b):	The number of Primary Health Centres (PHCs), Community Health Centres (CHCs) and District Hospitals (DHs) upgraded with AYUSH facilities, State-wise under the National AYUSH Mission (NAM) is furnished at Annexure-II.(c) & (d):	Under NAM, there is no provision to offer seats to students at various AYUSH Educational Institutions.(e):	Under NAM, Grant-in-aid of Rs. 1440.47 Crore has been released to States/UTs during the last three years for different activities as per proposal received through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP).
