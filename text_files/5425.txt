                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5002






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Procurement Centres in Gujarat




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the paddy and wheat procured at Minimum Support Price (MSP) from the State Government of Gujarat during the last four years and the current year, year-wise;(b) 	the details of the procurement centres opened in Gujarat during the said period;(c) 	whether the farmers are compelled to sell their produce at a price lesser than the production cost due to inadequate number of procurement centres; and(d) 	if so, the response of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): The details of paddy and wheat procured by FCI/State Govt. Agencies at Minimum Support Price (MSP) for Central Pool during last four years and the current year are as under:                                                                                (Fig in LMT)Kharif Marketing Season (KMS)	Paddy	Rabi Marketing Season(RMS)	Wheat2014-15	0.00	2015-16	0.732015-16	0.01	2016-17	0.002016-17	0.01	2017-18	0.072017-18	0.01	2018-19	0.372018-19	0.14	2019-20	0.05.........2/--2-(b): 	The number of paddy and wheat procurement centres opened in Gujarat during the last four and current marketing season is as under:Paddy 	Wheat KMS 	No. of Procurement centres	RMS	No. of Procurement centres2014-15	65	2015-16	1972015-16	64	2016-17	1892016-17	59	2017-18	2032017-18	59	2018-19	2032018-19 	82	2019-20	202(c) and (d): 		Gujarat is a DCP State wherein procurement of foodgrains is carried out by State Govt. through its agencies. Government of India assists the State Government in procurement operations in DCP States only on the specific request of the State Government. ***
