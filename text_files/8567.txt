                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2102






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Physical Education Teachers and Coaches




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the eligibility criteria of Physical Education teachers and coaches in schools;(b) whether the Bachelor of Physical Education degree has been made mandatory for PE teachersand if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the existing procedure for recruitment of PE teachers and sports coaches in Governmentschools; and(d) whether the PE teachers and sports coaches in schools are meant to be the same person and ifso, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) : The National Council for Teacher Education has laid down minimum qualificationsfor persons to be recruited as Physical Education Teachers at primary, upper primary, secondaryand senior secondary level vide Gazette Notification dated 12.11.2014.(c) & (d) : Education is a subject in the Concurrent list of the Constitution, therefore therecruitment, service conditions and deployment of teachers is under the domain of the concernedState/Union Territory (UT) Government.*****
