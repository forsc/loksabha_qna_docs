                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4088






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Toll on Delhi-Manali Route




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of National Highways (NHs) from Delhi to Manali which pass through Sri Anandpur Sahib region/zone;(b) 	the details of toll plazas/gates on these highways;(c) 	the total amount of toll that travellers have to pay on these roads in Anandpur Sahib that fall on the Delhi-Manali route; and(d) 	whether the toll amount is very high and if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d) The Kurali-Kiratpur Sahib section of NH-205A from km 28.600 to km 73.200 in the State of Punjab passes through Sri Anandpur Sahib region/zone.  The Toll Plaza on this stretch is located at km 35.00 (Behrampur village) in Sri Anandpur Sahib region/zone. Toll rates has been fixed as per provisions of NH Fee Rules, 1997 and its amendments. Notified rate of toll fee (in Rupees) on this stretch is as follows:-Type of vehicle	Single Journey	Return Journey	Monthly PassCar/Jeep/Van	50.00	70.00	1440.00LCV	85.00	125.00	2525.00Bus/Truck	170.00	250.00	5050.00Upto 3 Axle Vehicle	270.00	405.00	8115.004 to 6 Axle	270.00	405.00	8115.00HCM/EME	270.00	405.00	8115.007 or more Axle	270.00	405.00	8115.00*****
