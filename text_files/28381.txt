Jan Shikshan Sansthans




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of Jan Shikshan Sansthans (JSS) established in the country alongwith total amount disbursed to them, State wise including Rajasthan;(b)	the total number of students enrolled with the JSS, category wise and State- wise, down to district level for Rajasthan, the steps being taken to establish new and increase the areas covered by JSS;(c)	whether the Government conducts regular audits to check the functioning of the JSS, if so, the total number of such audits conducted State-wise and down to district level for Rajasthan;(d)	the total placement percentage of the JSS, State-wise data down to district level for Rajasthan, total number of private sector collaborations, and steps taken to invite private sector participation in establishing and recruiting students from JSS; and(e)	the details of the JSS who have been given IT capacity post the covid-19 pandemic ?






ANSWER




(a)	The total number of Jan Shikshan Sansthans (JSSs) functional in the country as on date is 307 which include 8 JSSs in Rajasthan. The State/UT-wise number of JSSs is at Annexure I. The State/UT-wise details of recurring Grants-in-Aid released to JSSs during the last four years are at Annexure-II.(b)	The details of total number of beneficiaries (State/UT-wise, District-wise and Category-wise) enrolled under the JSS scheme including Rajasthan during FY 2021-22 is at Annexure III. The Government expands the scheme from time to time to cover new areas by setting up new JSSs on the basis of requirement and availability of funds. (c)	Yes Sir. The Comptroller & Auditor General (C&AG) conducts the audit of JSSs          on regular basis. The Financial & Performance Audit of JSSs is also conducted by the Ministry from time to time through C&AG empanelled agencies. The Financial and Performance Audit was recently conducted during FY 2019-20.(d)	This data is not centrally maintained by the Ministry.(e)	No Sir.
