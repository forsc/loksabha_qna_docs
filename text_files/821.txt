                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

726






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Difficulty in Funding of Installation of CCTV Cameras




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that Government is finding it difficult to fund its project of installing CCTV cameras at railway stations and in certain trains;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether any proposal is pending with the Government to raise the amount from various funds, including the Nirbhaya Fund, for funding the aforesaid project; and(d)  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): No, Sir.(b): Does not arise.(c)&(d): It has been decided to provide Close Circuit Television (CCTV) based surveillance system at railway stations and coaches of passenger carrying trains with funding mainly from budgetary sources and partly through Nirbhaya fund for approximately 983 stations. ****
