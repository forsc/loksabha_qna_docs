                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Sharing Pattern under SDRMF




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the 15th Finance Commission has recommended sharing pattern ratio of 75: 25 between the Centre and States in States Disaster Risk Management Fund(SDRMF);(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether it is a fact that with a score of 90:10, the Government will allow a special dispensation of sharing pattern 90:10 at par with the North Eastern States; and(d)	if not, the reason therefor ? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a)	& (b): Yes Sir. The 15th Finance Commission has recommended the cost-sharing ratio between the Union and State Governments of 75:25 for general states and 90:10 for North-East and Himalayan States in the SDRMF.(c) to (d):	The Union Government vide Para 13 of the Explanatory Memorandum as to the action taken on the recommendations made by the 15th Finance Commission in its report for the award period 2021-22 to 2025-26 has inter-alia accepted the recommendation on continuation of the existing cost-sharing ratio between the Union and State Governments of 75:25 for general states and 90:10 for North-East and Himalayan States.
