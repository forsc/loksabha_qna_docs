                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1352






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Protection and Maintenance of Samadhis




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government looks after or assists the State Governments in looking after the Samadhis/Mausoleums of freedom fighters in various parts of the country; (b) if so, whether it has come to the notice of the Central Government that the Mausoleum of Maulana Abul Kalam Azad in Delhi has been in squalid condition since many years;c)whether the Government is undertaking any steps to protect or manage such sites, including from problems of encroachment, ineffective maintenance etc., and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE &TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d): The Ministry of Culture does not look after Samadhis/Mausoleums. However, as per available information CPWD looks after the following Samadhis/Mausoleums: Raj Ghat, Kisan Ghat,  Sangharsh Sthal, Vir Bhumi, Shakti Sthal, Shanti Van, Samta Sthal, Vijay Ghat, Ekta Sthal, Sadaiv Atal and Mazar.Mausoleum of Maulana Abul Kalam Azad situated near Jama Masjid, Delhi is in good condition. There is no damage to any structure inside the mausoleum. General upkeep and maintenance of Mausoleum is done regularly and there is no encroachment on the plot.
