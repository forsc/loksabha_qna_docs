                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4136






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Environmental Clearance for Projects




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of power, irrigation, mining and road transport projects that are lying pending with the Union Government for the last three years for environmental clearance, State/UT-wise including Telangana; (b) 	the project-wise reasons for pendency of environmental clearance for such projects along with the time since when these projects are lying pending; and (c) 	the steps taken by the Government to accord approval to these projects at the earliest? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)& (b) There are 36 projects pertaining to power, irrigation, mining and road transport categories which are at various stages of appraisal under Environment Impact Assessment Notification, 2006 in the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change. The details are placed at Annexure I.(c)	Ministry has launched a single window hub PARIVESH on 10th August, 2018   for online submission, monitoring and management of environmental clearances, which is a Single-Window Integrated Environmental Management System, developed in pursuance of the spirit of ''Digital India'' and capturing the essence of "minimum government and maximum governance".   A work-flow based application has been rolled out for online submission, monitoring and management of proposals submitted by Project Proponents to the (MoEF&CC), as well as to the State Level Environmental Impact Assessment Authorities (SEIAA) to seek various types of clearances (e.g. Environment, Forest, Wildlife and Coastal Regulation Zone Clearances) from Central, State and district-level authorities. It automates the entire process of submitting the application and tracking the status of such proposals at each stage of the processing, thus reducing the time lines and increasing transparency and accountability.
