                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2947






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Fund Allocation for Bharat Gaurav Train




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru
                                                








                                                    Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                








                                                    Harish Dwivedi
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                








                                                    P. Ravindhranath
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the details of the Bharat Gaurav trains being launched under Indian Railways to tap the huge potential of tourism;(b) the details of the funds allocated, sanctioned and planned to be disbursed for the next five years, State/ Union Territory-wise;(c) the tentative timeline and schedule on which the trains will be made operational, State/Union Territory-wise especially of States of Maharashtra and Andhra Pradesh;(d) whether the Government has also proposed to spare 180 trains exclusively for the same;(e) the details of special facilities being offered by the Government in these trains;(f) whether sector level contractors are being taken into consideration to begin the operation of the said train; and(g) the details of total employment and revenue likely to be generated by undertaking this project?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	Ministry of Railways have issued policy instructions on 23.11.2021 vide Commercial Circular No. 14 for operation of theme based tourist circuit trains ‘Bharat Gaurav’ over Indian Railways and the same is available on Indian Railway’s website https://bharatgauravtrains.indianrailways.gov.in. The objective is to tap the vast tourist potential of India by showcasing India’s rich cultural heritage and magnificent historical places to the people of India and the world.(b)	No fund is envisaged to be allocated for this scheme as this policy is a revenue generating model. (c)	Timeline and schedule for making these trains operational will depend on many factors, marketing of tour packages and placement of rake demands.   The trains may cover any State or Union territory including the states of Maharashtra and Andhra Pradesh depending upon the itinerary planning. (d)	The number of trains will depend upon the coach composition of a train requested by the service provider, which may comprise a minimum of 14 coaches and a maximum of 20 coaches per train.  Indian Railways have identified 3033 coaches which are offered under this scheme. (e)	Any special facilities and comprehensive tour services comprising meals, accommodation, transport, sightseeing, tour guide shall be decided and provided by the service provider. Indian Railways would facilitate service providers in smooth operation of these trains. (f)	Under the policy, Service providers may include State Tourism Development Corporations, Public Sector Undertakings or others who may be at sector level.(g)	Promotion of tourism under this scheme will generate a multiplier effect on increase in employment opportunities in tourism and related sectors. The revenue earned by railways will be in accordance with the applicable charges notified vide the said policy instructions and will depend upon the number of trains operated. ****
