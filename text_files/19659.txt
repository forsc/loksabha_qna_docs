                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1066






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Ecologically Sensitive Zone in Kerala




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of that because of the Ecologically Sensitive Zones and Ecologically Sensitive Areas notifications, many tribal hamlets will have to be got evacuated in Attapadi in Palakkad district in Kerala;(b)  whether the Government has received any representations in this regard to extend the implementation of these notifications; and(c)   if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) State Government of Kerala has informed that as per the draft Eco Sensitive Zone notification, no tribal hamlets will be evacuated in Attapadi in Palakkad District in Kerala.(b) State Government of Kerala has received some representations in this regard.(c) Based on the representations, State Government of Kerala has informed, the Hon’ble Forest Minister of Kerala convened a meeting on 28.09.2020 and decided to exclude all human habitations from the proposals of Eco Sensitive Zone. Accordingly, revised draft proposals for declaring Eco Sensitive Zone around Sanctuaries in the State had been submitted to MoEF & CC for further action. *****
