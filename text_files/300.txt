                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

264






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Higher Educational Institutions




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there are three lakh vacancies in Government run higher educationinstitutions;(b) if so, the time since when these vacancies have been accumulated;(c) whether the education standards in higher education institutions are deteriorated dueto this huge number of vacancies and if so, the details thereof along with the reaction ofthe Government thereon;(d) whether the Government has issued any guidelines to fill up vacant posts in all theuniversities, colleges and institutions and if so, the details thereof, and(e) the steps taken by the Government to monitor the recruitment process to fill upvacant posts without any further delay?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): As per All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) portal 2017-18, there issanctioned strength of 1407373 teaching posts and 1062659 teachers are in position(including all types of teaching posts) in Universities and Colleges across the nationwhich includes Government, Government-aided and private Higher EducationalInstitutions. However, in Government run Higher Educational Institutions (Universitiesand Colleges) sanctioned strength of teaching posts is 330903 out of which 256783teachers are in position (including all types of teaching posts) and 74,120 posts arevacant.(b) to (e): Occurring of vacancies and filling up is a continuous process. The UniversityGrants Commission (UGC) continuously monitors it with all Universities. In this regard,UGC vide its D.O. letter No.F.1-14/2019 (CPP-II) dated 4th June, 2019 has prepared theGuidelines for Recruitment of Faculty in Universities, Colleges and Institutions Deemedto be Universities outlining the selection procedure and the time frame for recruitment.These guidelines have been circulated to all Universities to adhere to them. TheUniversities has also been requested to take steps to ensure that the vacancies in theUniversity as well as in the Colleges affiliated to University are filled up at the earliest.Acopy of the Guidelines circulated to all Universities is available athttps://www.ugc.ac.in/ugc_notices.aspx?id=2418.Further, this Ministry has requested all Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) to fill upfaculty related data on National Higher Education Resource Centre (NHERC) portal athttps://nherc.in in order to fill up all vacancies in various higher educational institutionson an urgent basis.*****
