                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3503






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






City Gas Infrastructure




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that Rs.80,000 crore investment is envisaged in setting up city gas infrastructure in the 61 geographical areas and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether it is also true that 65 geographical areas are spread over 215 districts in 19 States and one Union territory and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether it is also a fact that in the latest gas bidding round, one oil marketing firm in public sector and one private firm were found to be bidders; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d)	Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) had launched 11th City Gas Distribution (CGD) Bidding Round on 17th September, 2021 for development of CGD Network in 65 Geographical Areas (GAs) spread over 215 districts (212 complete and 3 part) in 19 States and one Union Territory. For 61 GAs, a total of 439 bids were received from 26 entities which include public sector and private sector bidders. List of bidders participated in the 11th CGD bidding round is at Annexure.PNGRB has authorized 40 GAs and issued Letters of Intent (LoIs) for 21 GAs. As per assessment by PNGRB, investment of more than Rs. 80,000 crores is likely to be made by the authorized entities in the next 8-10 years.*****
