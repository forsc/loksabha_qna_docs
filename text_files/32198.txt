                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3618






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






PM Jeevan Jyoti and PM Suraksha Bima Yojanas




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the date since which ‘Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana’ and ‘Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana’ were launched in the country;  (b) the number and details of bank account holders registered under the said schemes, State-wise and scheme-wise;(c) the number and details of registered bank account holders who have been benefited since the start of the said schemes till October 2021 including the amount deposited thereunder, State-wise; and(d) the number and details of women who have been insured under the said schemes till October 2021 in the country including the number of women who have been provided assistance along with the types of assistance provided to them?	






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of Finance(a) & (b) Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY) and Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) were launched on 9th May, 2015 with a view to enhance the level of insurance penetration in the country and to provide insurance cover to common people, especially poor and the under-privileged sections of the society.  While PMJJBY offers life insurance cover of Rs 2 lakh, PMSBY offers accidental death or total permanent disability cover of Rs.2 lakhs and permanent partial disability cover of Rs. 1 lakh. State-wise details of beneficiaries enrolled under the Schemes is at Annexure A.(c) As per information furnished by Insurance Companies, 5,12,915 numbers of claims amounting to Rs 10,258 crore and 92,266 numbers of claims amounting to Rs 1,797 crore have been disbursed under PMJJBY and PMSBY respectively as on 27.10.2021.
