                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

531






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Awareness about Road Safety




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     	the total number of road accidents that took place in the country during each of the last three years; and (b)     	the measures being taken by the Government to increase awareness about road safety?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)     As per the data received from Police Departments of various States/UTs the total number of road accidents that took place in the country for the calendar year, starting from 2017 to 2019 are shown in the table given below: Year	Total Road Accidents2017	4,64,9102018	4,67,0442019	4,49,002(b)      To increase awareness about road safety, the following steps have been taken by the Government:     (i)      The Ministry of Road Transport & Highways has notified “Member of Parliaments’ Road Safety Committee” in each district of the country to promote awareness amongst road users under the Chairmanship of Hon’ble Member of Parliament (Lok Sabha) from the district. (ii)    The Ministry implements a scheme for undertaking publicity measures and awareness campaigns on road safety to create awareness among road users through the electronic media, print media, NGOs and other organizations, etc. (iii)     Observance of National Road Safety Month/Week every year across the country for spreading awareness and strengthening road safety. (iv)  A Certification Course for Road Safety Auditors has been commenced in Indian Academy of Highway Engineers (IAHE). (v)   Issue of Guidelines for protection of Good Samaritans and draft rules as per MV (Amendment) Act, 2019 has been published. *****
