                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2982






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Drinking Water Schemes in Haryana




Arvind Kumar Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has anyscheme or project for drinking water in Rohtak/Jhajjar and Kosali regions of Haryana;(b) if so, the time by which the saidregions are likely to get drinking water; and(c) if not, the steps proposed to betaken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (b)  As reported by State Government of Haryana, drinking water facilities have already been extended to the Rohtak / Jhajjer and Kosli regions.  Further to improve the water supply, 94 projects costing Rs. 92.96crore in Rohtak region, 122 projects costing Rs.173.01 crore in Jhajjar region and 78 projects costing Rs. 104.98 crore in Kosli region are in progress in these regions.   The said improvement projects are likely to be completed by 30.06.2022, subject to availability of adequate funds.(c)   Does not arise.
