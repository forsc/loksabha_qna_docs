                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

376






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Yuva Sahakar Cooperative Enterprise Support and Innovation Scheme




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil
                                                








                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has launched a Yuva Sahakar Cooperative Enterprise Support and Innovation Scheme 2019 under the National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) recently giving wings to young entrepreneurs in cooperatives; (b)	 if so, the details thereof and the number of young entrepreneurs benefitted across the country including in Telangana, district-wise; (c) 	whether any support is provided under the scheme to various agriculture cooperative societies in the States and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the National Cooperative Development Corporation has provided loan/incentive/subsidy to cooperative institutes for development of cooperative infrastructure in the country; and (e) 	if so, the details of such assistance/ funds given by the Government to the said institutes during each of the last three years and the current year, institute and State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC), a statutory corporation under the administrative control of Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare has launched “Yuva Sahakar – Cooperative Enterprise Support and Innovation Scheme 2019” in October, 2019. Cont.2/-2-(b):	The details of the Scheme are given at Annexure-1. No proposal from cooperatives across the country, including the Telangana State has been received under the scheme. (c):	Yes Sir. All types of cooperatives, including agricultural cooperatives, are eligible for availing financial assistance for new, innovative and value chain enhancement intended projects under the Scheme. (d):	Yes Sir. NCDC has been providing loan to cooperatives for development of cooperative infrastructure in the country since 1963. Subsidy or incentive of Government of India as per the Central Government guidelines is routed through NCDC for cooperatives. (e):	Details of State/UT-wise financial assistance provided by NCDC during the last three   financial years and for financial year 2019-20 (up to 11/11/2019), are given at Annexure-2.
