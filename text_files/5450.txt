                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4938






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Fishing Harbours and Cold Storage Facilities in Andhra Pradesh




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to set up fishing harbours and provide cold storage facilities in coastal districts of Andhra Pradesh to tap the potential of fisheries sector in the State for the provision of livelihood and income generation of small-scale fishermen; (b) 	if so, the details of the steps taken by the Government in this regard along with the present status thereof; (c) 	the time by which such a project is likely to be taken up; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (d) :  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries inter-alia provides financial assistance for strengthening of Post Harvest Infrastructure facilities fisheries such as  (i) Construction of new fishing harbours and fish landing centres and (ii) Up-gradation/expansion/repair/renovation of the existing fishing harbours and fish landing centres, (iii) Ice plants,  Cold storages, Ice plants-cum-cold storages, (iv)  Renovation/modernization of existing Ice plants, Cold storages and Ice plants-cum-cold storages etc. Besides, in order to address the infrastructural requirements for fisheries sector, a dedicated fund namely Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Fund (FIDF) has been launched during 2018-19 with a fund size of Rs 7522.48 crore. Under the FIDF concessional finance is also provided to the State/State Entities and other Eligible Entities for development of fishing harbour, fish landing centres, cold storage, ice-plants, fish transport and cold chain infrastructures.	The Government of Andhra Pradesh has submitted proposals for construction of three new fishing harbours at (i) Juvvaladinne in Nellore District, (ii) Vodarevu in Prkasham District, (iii) Uppada in East Godavari District and (iv) Phase II development of two existing fishing harbours at Machilipattinam in Krishna District and Nizampattinam in Guntur. The details of the proposals and present status are furnished at Annexure-I.*****
