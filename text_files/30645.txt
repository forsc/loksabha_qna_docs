                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2106






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






COVID-19 Vaccination in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the salient features of the vaccine being used to combat the COVID-19 in Odisha;(b)	 the number of districts covered so far within Odisha under the vaccination process; and(c)	The steps being taken by the Government to cover remote/inaccessible areas under the mission in Odisha? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)  (a) to (c) National COVID-19 Vaccination Programme is ongoing across all States/UTs covering all the districts including Odisha since 16th January 2021. Presently, three vaccines are administered under National COVID-19 Vaccination Programme after approval from the National Regulator i.e. Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO). These are (i) Covishield manufactured by M/s Serum Institute of India Pvt., Ltd., Pune, used at both Government & Private CVCs (ii) Covaxin: manufactured by M/s Bharat Biotech International Limited, Hyderabad, used at both Government & Private CVCs and (iii) Sputnik V: imported by M/s Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories Ltd, Hyderabad, used at Private CVCs.COVID-19 Vaccines Operational Guidelines, provides guidance for organizing session at remote areas for lesser number of beneficiaries ensuring no vaccine wastage, provision of special mobile vaccination teams and Near to Home Vaccination Centers. Further, from 3rd November 2021, “Har Ghar Dastak Abhiyan” has been launched wherein missed beneficiaries for 1st dose and due beneficiaries for 2nd dose are identified and vaccinated through house to house activity across the country including Odisha.*****
