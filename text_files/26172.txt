                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2025






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Tree plantation




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of plantations done by the Government and private organizations during the last two years, State/UT-wise including Chhattisgarh;(b) 	whether the Government has fixed any targets to encourage tree plantation in the country; and(c)	 if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) The progress of afforestation / tree plantation in the last two years by all agencies including Government, NGOs and private agencies, State/UT-wise including Chhattisgarh is given in Annexure-I.(b)&(c)	Annual afforestation / tree plantation targets are fixed by the Ministry to all States/UTs. The target of afforestation / tree planting conveyed to States/UTs for the year 2021-22 is given in Annexure-II.*****
