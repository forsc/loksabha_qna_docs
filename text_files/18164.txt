                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1986






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Dwarka Expressway Project




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of the Dwarka Expressway Project and the time by which it is expected to be operational;(b)	the details of funds spent thereon till now since the date of start of the project; and(c)	whether the said Project’s completion is affected by COVID 19 pandemic situation in the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)] (a)& (b)	Construction of Dwarka Expressway Project has been taken up in four packages and all are under different stages of implementation. The work is expected to be completed by December, 2022. The total amount of Rs. 1,110.00 crores has been spent on the said work till date.(c)	Yes Sir. Completion of the said project has been affected by COVID 19 pandemic and approximately three month’s interim time extension has been envisaged.*****
