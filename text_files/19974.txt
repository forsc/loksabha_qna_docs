                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1425






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Salaries of BSNL Contract Workers




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken cognizance of the fact that BSNL contract workers in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts have not received their salaries regularly since January 2019 and if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; and(b)	 the time by which the salary due to these workers will get fully cleared?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b) Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has informed that wages of the contract workers deployed by the contractors in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts have been paid upto the month of March 2020.  Due to poor financial situation of BSNL, payment to contractors have been delayed. The situation in the current financial year aggravated due to the COVID-19 pandemic.BSNL has further informed that funds are released and payments are being made in phases depending upon the availability of funds.Department of Telecommunications (DoT) has directed BSNL to look into the issue of timely payment to contract workers on priority. Further, to strengthen the BSNL, the Government approved a revival plan of BSNL.
