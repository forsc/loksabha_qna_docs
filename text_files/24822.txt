                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

677






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






TOP Scheme




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of financial assistance provided to athletes under Khelo India and TOP Scheme during the last three years, year and State-wise;(b) the details of the number of infrastructure development projects taken up under Khelo India Scheme; and(c) the details and the names of the infrastructure projects that have been sanctioned, completed and are in progress across the country, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) The amount of financial assistance provided to athletes under Khelo India Scheme under the concerned verticals, namely, “Talent Search and Development” and “Support to National/Regional/State Sports Academy” and TOP Scheme during the last three years is given below:(Amount in ? crore)<pre>Financial Year	Khelo India Scheme	TOP Scheme2018-19	18.19	14.322019-20	48.76	12.412020-21	43.05	15.66 </pre>TOP Scheme and the concerned verticals of Khelo India Scheme are athlete-centric wherein assistance is given to specific athletes or to the academies they train at instead of to States/UTs. Hence, State/UT-wise data is not maintained.(b) and (c) 272 sports infrastructure projects have been sanctioned under the Khelo India Scheme since its inception in 2016-17. The State/UT-wise details of these projects are at Annexure.****
