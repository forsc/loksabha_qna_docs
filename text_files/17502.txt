                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1179






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Permanent Building for Kendriya Vidyalayas




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken any measures to construct permanent building for Kendriya Vidyalayas in Telangana State and particularly in Jharasangam area;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the time likely to be taken for the construction of the said building?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): Construction of permanent buildings for Kendriya Vidyalayas is a continuous process which depends upon various factors such as identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favour of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan by the sponsoring authorities, submission of drawings/estimates by the construction agency, as well as availability of funds etc.  As such no time frame can be given in this regard. Construction work of permanent building at Jharasangam is at planning stage.****
