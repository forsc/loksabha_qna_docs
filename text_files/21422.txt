                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

235






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Sainik Express




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Sainik Express 14021/14022 is proposed to be run on daily basis;(b) if so, the time by which it is likely to become operational; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)	(a) to (c) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 235 BY SHRI NARENDRA KUMAR TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 10.03.2021 REGARDING SAINIK EXPRESS(a) to (c) Prior to COVID-19 pandemic, 14021/22 Delhi Sarai Rohilla-Jaipur Sainik Express was being operated as a tri-weekly service and it has been decided to increase its frequency from 3 to 4 days a week. However, the train is presently being operated as a tri-weekly special service, in view of prevailing situation.  Owing to operational constraints increase in its frequency, to daily is not feasible, at present.*****
