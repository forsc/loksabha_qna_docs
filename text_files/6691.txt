                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

416






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Common Service Centres




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has set up/provided the facility of Common Service Centres (CSCs) and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (b)	the details of various services being provided by CSCs at the level of Gram Panchayats; and(c)	whether the Government has provided any amount for assisting the CSC operators and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)a)	The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India initiated a project namely “Common Services Centre (CSC-2.0: A Way Forward Project)” in August, 2015 under Digital India Programme, which aims to set up at least one CSC at Gram Panchayat (GP) level to cover all around 2.5 lakhs GPs across the country. The project is being implemented by M/s CSC e-Governance Services India Limited (CSC Special Purpose Vehicle; i.e. CSC –SPV). The State-wise status of CSC - 2.0 roll-out as on September, 2019, is at Annexure-I. b)	The details of various services being provided by the CSCs in Gram Panchayats is at Annexure-II.	c)	There is no financial assistance provided to the operator. One-time amount of Rs. 3500 is provided for branding of CSC.       *****
