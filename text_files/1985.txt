                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

179






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Pine and Lemongrass in Railway Offices/Trains




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to provide pine and lemongrass in railway offices and trains;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the time by which the said arrangement is likely to be made; and(c)	the name of the agency selected to make the said arrangement?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 179 BY SHRI RAVINDRA SHYAM NARAYAN SHUKLA ALIAS RAVI KISHAN TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 03.07.2019 REGARDING PINE AND LEMONGRASS IN RAILWAY OFFICES/TRAINS(a) to (c):  No, Sir.  However, Railway Board has issued instructions to all Zonal Railways and other units on 05.10.2017 for use of ‘De-Odourizing-cum-Disinfectant Fluid’ to IS: 10758/1983, which has some content of pure strained absolute essential oil like pine, lemon grass or any other essential oil, having pleasing odour.         *****
