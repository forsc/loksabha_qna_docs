                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Railway Projects




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of new railway projects sanctioned in the State of Rajasthan during the last five years, zone-wise and year-wise; (b) the number of projects sanctioned during the last five years on which work is going on at present along with project-wise details of expenditure incurred so far;(c) whether the Government proposes to sanction any new project in the State; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. ***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 323 BY SHRI BHAGIRATH CHOUDHARY TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.12.2019 REGARDING RAILWAY PROJECTS(a) and (b): Railway projects are not sanctioned State-wise, but Zone-wise, whose jurisdiction cuts across the State boundaries. However, during the last five years and current year (2014-15 to 2018-19 and 2019-20), 04 new lines, 01 gauge conversion and 08 doubling projects covering a total length of 1,772 km and costing `23,206 crore falling fully/partly in the State of Rajasthan were included in Railway Budget. The details of these projects are appended.During 2009-14, 798 Km length (61 Km New Line, 675 Km Gauge Conversion and 62 Km Doubling) has been commissioned in the State of Rajasthan at an average rate of 160 Km per year.During 2014-19, 1516 Km (80 Km New Line, 483 Km Gauge Conversion and 953 Km Doubling) has been commissioned in the State of Rajasthan at an average rate of 303 Km per year, which is 190% of commissioning during   2009-14.Average Annual Budget allocation for infrastructure projects and safety works, falling fully/partly in the State of Rajasthan, during 2014-19 has been enhanced to `2951 crore per year,  from `682 crore per year (during 2009-14). Thus, the average annual budget allocation during 2014-19 is 433% of average annual budget allocation of 2009-14 (`682 crore per year).(c) and (d): Sanction of projects for development of Railways is a continuous process. Railway projects are sanctioned, based on demands raised by State Governments, Central Ministries, Members of Parliament, other public representatives and Railways’ own requirement, participation by State Government or private entities etc. They are taken up on the basis of remunerativeness, last mile connectivity, missing links and alternate routes etc.*****
