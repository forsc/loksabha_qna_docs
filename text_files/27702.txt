                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3418






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Petrol Refilling Stations




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State-wise/Union Territory-wise details of petrol refilling stations/retail outlets in the country along with the number thereof; (b) whether the Government proposes to open new petrol refilling stations/retail outlets in different stations during the current financial year; (c) if so, the State/Union Territory-wise details thereof including district-wise details of Madhya Pradesh; and (d) the details of the prescribed eligibility conditions in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a)&(b) Public Sector OMCs have informed that expansion of Retail Outlet (RO) network is a continuous process to ensure adequate availability of motoring fuels like petrol and diesel throughout the country. State/UT-wise number of Retail Outlets (ROs) of OMCs as on 30.6.2021 and number of new RO planned for commissioning during 2021-22 by OMCs are given in Annexure-I. (c) District-wise details of ROs planned for commissioning in Madhya Pradesh during 2021-22 is given in Annexure-2. (d) Procedure/Norms/Criteria/Eligibility for allotment of Retail Outlet Dealerships in the country are available on the respective websites of the OMCs i.e. www.iocl.com, www.bharatpetroleum.com & www.hindustanpetroleum.com.  ****
