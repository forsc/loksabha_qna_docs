                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

327






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Fit India Cyclothon




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil













                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the programmes being undertaken by the Government for identifying the talents amongst youth in rural areas of various States and UTs particularly in Maharashtra;(b) the details of the number of beneficiaries under these programmes during the last five years, State/UT-wise;(c) the details and the objectives of the ‘Fit India Cyclothon’;(d) whether the Government has constituted any Committee/team to assess the condition of all the stadiums in the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *327 ANSWERED ON 21.12.2021 REGARDING “FIT INDIA CYCLOTHON” ASKED BY SHRI UNMESH BHAIYYASAHEB PATIL AND DR. HEENA GAVIT, HON’BLE MEMBERS OF LOK SABHA(a) and (b) The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports implements the following schemes for the development of sports in the country, including for the youth in rural areas of Maharashtra:(i) Khelo India Scheme; (ii) Assistance to National Sports Federations; (iii) Special Awards to Winners in International Sports Events and their Coaches; (iv) National Sports Awards; (v) Pension to Meritorious Sportspersons; (vi) Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Sports Welfare Fund; (vii) National Sports Development Fund; and (viii) Running Sports Training Centres through Sports Authority of India (SAI).Details of the above schemes are available in the public domain on the websites of this Ministry and SAI.State/UT-wise details of number of athletes supported under Khelo India Scheme of this Ministry and Sports Promotional Schemes of SAI and are at Annexure I and II, respectively. (c) The Fit India Movement was launched on 29th August, 2019 by the Hon’ble Prime Minister with a view to make fitness an integral part of our daily lives. Since its inception, this Ministry has been actively spreading awareness about fitness through various campaigns which, inter-alia, include a campaign, namely, the Fit India Cyclothon, to create awareness on fitness through cycling. Fit India Cyclothon can be organised by any Government or private organisation, school, college, university, individual, interested groups, residential associations, communities in villages, towns, workplaces, etc.(d) and (e) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the responsibility for promotion and development of sports rests primarily with the respective State/Union Territory Governments. Central Government only supplements the efforts of the State/UT Governments under its various schemes. The stadiums under the control of this Ministry have dedicated administrators and the available facilities as well as the maintenance thereof are reviewed/assessed regularly with the service providers and other stakeholders. ****
