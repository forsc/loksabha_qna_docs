                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3747






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Setting up KISCE in Gujarat




Rameshbhai Lavjibhai Dhaduk














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the provisions made by Government under Khelo India Scheme to build new Infrastructure complexes and Construction of Stadia Complex for Sportspersons in the Lok Sabha constituency of Porbandar in Gujarat; and(b) whether the Government is setting up Khelo India State Centres of Excellence (KISCE) under Khelo India in the District of Porbandar in the State of Gujarat, if so, the details thereof along with the time by when the same is being set up?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) Under the “Utilisation and Creation/Upgradation of Sports Infrastructure” vertical of the Khelo India Scheme, this Ministry has sanctioned five projects in Gujarat. No proposal has been received to construct a sports stadium in the Lok Sabha constituency of Porbandar in Gujarat.(b) The Khelo India Scheme is a demand-driven scheme under which one of the existing training centres of the State/UT is designated as Khelo India State Centre of Excellence (KISCE) on the basis of proposals received from the respective State/UT Governments. So far, no proposal has been received from the State of Gujarat for setting up of a KISCE in Porbandar district.****
