                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4633






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Agreement between India and Russia




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India and Russia have begun negotiations on an agreement for long-term assured supplies of crude oil and Liquified Natural Gas (LNG) to diversify its energy sources and do away with over dependence on West Asia and also working on easier and economical sea transport route from Russia to India, if so, the details thereof;(b) the time by which the oil and LNG are likely to reach India; and(c) the total value of the agreement and term / duration of the agreement?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c): GAIL signed a Liquified Natural Gas (LNG) Sale Purchase Agreement (SPA) with Gazprom Marketing & Trading Singapore Pte Ltd. (GMTS) for supply of around 2.85 Million Metric Tonne Per Annum (MMTPA) LNG for 20 years. The LNG supplies under the above contract have commenced from June 2018 onwards.On 05th February 2020, Indian Oil Corporation Limited and Rosneft signed the first ever Term Contract for importing of 2.0 Million Metric Tonnes (MMT) of Urals grade crude oil during the year 2020. *****
