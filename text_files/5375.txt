                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4795






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Setting up of KRIBHCO Units




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government proposes to set up/has taken steps to set up new KRIBHCO units in Telangana and Nellore district of Andhra Pradesh to help the farmers;  (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the roadmap worked out in this regard; and (c)	the details of the progress made so far and the details of funds sanctioned/spent by KRIBHCO units in Telangana during the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS.(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) to (c):	At present, Department of Fertilizers has no proposal to set up new Krishak Bharati Cooperative Limited (KRIBHCO) unit in Telangana and Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh.********
