                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

530






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Implementation of AMRUT Scheme




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the steps taken for implementation of Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) scheme in Maharashtra till date;(b) 	the details of the cities of Maharashtra selected for AMRUT scheme;(c) 	the details of Detailed Project Report (DPR) and fund allocation for implementation of AMRUT scheme in Maharashtra; and(d) 	the progress of AMRUT scheme and the time frame for completion of projects under AMRUT scheme in Maharashtra?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a).	Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), was launched on 25 June 2015 in 500 selected cities of the country including 44 cities of the State of Maharashtra.  The Mission period has been extended up to 31 March 2021. Government of India has already approved State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs)of State of Maharashtra amounting to ?7,759.32 crore for the entire Mission, which include committed Central Assistance of ?3,534.08 crore. As on date, Central Assistance amounting to ?2,776.25 crore has been released to the Government of Maharashtra for implementation of projects under the Mission.(b).	List of 44 cities of Maharashtra covered under AMRUT has been attached as ANNEXURE.(c) & (d).	As on date, State of Maharashtra has taken up 196 projects under AMRUT costing ?7,825.66 crore, of which, 133 projects costing ?787.83 crore have been completed, 63 projects costing ?7,037.83 crore are under implementation. State of Maharashtra has taken up projects in excess of their approved SAAP size and the entire excess cost shall be borne by the State Government.                               ****
