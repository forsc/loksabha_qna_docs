Demand and Supply of Fertilizers




Khan Saumitra













                                                    Sanjay Jaiswal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  whether the production of fertilizers at national level has increased during the last five years and if so, the details thereof; (b)	 whether the Government has received any reports of shortage of fertilizers in the country and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including West Bengal;(c) 	the status of demand and supply of fertilizers in the country during the last three years and the current year so far, State/UT-wise and also give the break-up in regard to each type of fertilizer in respect of West Bengal;(d) 	whether the supply to the States has not been adequate to meet their requirements during the last three years and the current year so far and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including West Bengal; and (e) 	the steps taken/being taken to ensure timely supply of adequate quantity of fertilizers to the farmers at reasonable prices?

MANSUKH MANDAVIYA) (a) to (e):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. (a):    The production  of all major fertilizers during  last five years are given below:-( Figure in LMT)Year	        2016-17	 2017-18  2018-19	  2019-20	   2020-21Fertilizer Production	414.41	 413.61	  413.85	  425.92	   433.66The above data shows marginal decrease in production of all major fertilizers during 2017-18, which is insignificant. (b):   No, Sir.(c):  The details of requirement, availability and sales State/UT wise (including the state of West Bengal), year-wise for last three financial years (i.e. 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21) including ongoing financial year 2021-22 for all fertilizers  are at Annexure.(d):	The overall availability of fertilizers during the last three financial years including the ongoing financial year 2021-22 has been comfortable across the country. However, some states/districts had witnessed shortfall for a brief period of time during last and ongoing season. Accordingly, based on the requests of State Governments, additional rakes of fertilizers were moved to meet the requirement. (e):  The following steps have been taken by the Department of fertilizers to ensure comfortable availability of Fertilizers in all the States:-i.	Attempt has been made to assess the field requirement in consultation with the states and accordingly, the district wise micro planning has been done which is being executed on daily basis.ii.	24x7 interactions with the state governments, companies and railways have been established through web platforms/control room.iii.	The stake holders viz., manufacturers, ports, railways have been sensitized to operate on maximum capacity to produce load, and move the fertilizers to the targeted destinations.iv.	State Governments have been impressed upon to use i-FMS dashboard stock figures for district wise stock availability and suitable interventions. Same source is used by DoF so as to be on board with states and closely oversee that the recommended movement for each district/ rake point.v.	The loading and voyage time of imported fertilizer shipments have been monitored on daily basis and required suitable interventions have been made from time to time. vi.	Every day there is a close monitoring 24x7 and regular interaction with states, fertilizer companies and railway board. As 90% of the fertilizers are moved through railway rakes, so the loading and movement of these rakes is being executed in a very targeted manner (in consultation with respective state governments), so as to address the field demand suitably.
