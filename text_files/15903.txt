                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4658






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Health Fair Organised by SAIL




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the funds are provided by the Steel Authority of India (SAIL) and other undertakings for organizing health fairs in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of health fairs organized by them during the last three years and the current year, date and location-wise; and(d)	the details of the funds spent by them separately during the said period, date and location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)to(d): None of the Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) under Ministry of Steel including Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) has organized any health fair during the last three years and the current year. However, communities living in the periphery of Plants/Units/Mines of CPSEs under Ministry of Steel avail the basic healthcare facilities through Hospital, Dispensaries, Mobile Medical Units, etc. Further, preventive healthcare is made available through regular health camps organized for the benefit of people living in various villages in the periphery of plants/mines.********
