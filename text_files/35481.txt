                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2914






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Training under PMKVY/SANKALP




 Chinta Anuradha














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the details of Skill Development Centres set up in Andhra Pradesh since inception of the scheme in 2016, District-wise; (b)       the number of youth who got trained, be it under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikash Yojana (PMKVY), Skill Acqisition and Knowledge Awareness for Livelihood Promotion (SANKALP) Programme, Standard Training Assessment and Reward (STAR), Udaan scheme, etc., from each of the above centres; (c)        the number of youth who got job opportunities or have been able to start their own small time unit for livelihood; (d)       the aims and objectives of PMKVY 3.0; (e)       the details of physical targets set under PMKVY 1.0 and 2.0 and whether targets have been achieved; and (f)        if not, the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       Under Skill India Mission, Ministry is implementing various programmes/ scheme for the skill development of youth across the country including in the State of Andhra Pradesh such as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) through Industrial Training Institute (ITIs).  The district-wise number of skill development centres that have imparted training in the State of Andhra Pradesh under the programmes of MSDE, are given at Table I of Annexure. (b)       The district-wise candidates trained/enrolled under various programmes/ scheme of MSDE such as PMKVY, JSS and CTS through ITIs in the State of Andhra Pradesh is given at Table II of Annexure. ‘Udaan’ was a Special Industry Initiative by Ministry of Home Affairs for the Youth of Jammu & Kashmir. Also, National Skill Certification and Monetary Reward Scheme (also known as STAR Scheme) was implemented by Ministry of Finance and around 14 lakh candidates were trained across the country including State of Andhra Pradesh. Further, there are no provisions of setting up of skill development training centres under SANKALP. Under SANKALP, Ministry has launched Adarsh Gram Skill Camp for RPL certification. Under this, 116 candidates have been oriented in Chitoor district of State of Andhra Pradesh. (c)        Under placement-linked component of PMKVY, 2.95 lakh candidates were self- employed in various sectors across the country including 10,415 candidates from State of Andhra Pradesh. (d)       Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) has launched the demand-driven Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana 3.0 (PMKVY 3.0) on 15.01.2021. The objectives of the scheme are to: i)	Create an ecosystem for the youth to make informed choices on the available skilling avenues.ii)	Provide support to youth for skill training and certification.iii)	Promote sustainable Skill Centres for greater participation of private sector.iv)	Benefit 8 lakh youth over the scheme period. (e) and (f)	The targets and candidates benefited under PMKVY 1.0 and 2.0 are given below:                                                                                                         (No. in lakh)Phase	Target	Candidates Trained / OrientedPMKVY 1.0 (Phase I)  2015-16	24.00	19.86PMKVY 2.0 (Phase II) 2016-20	100.00	109.98 Under PMKVY1.0, target was not achieved due to newly introduction of Recognition of Prior learning (RPL) component and its commencement in the last phase of scheme. Further, the targets of PMKVY 2.0 have been achieved. Presently, Ministry is implementing third phase of PMKVY, i.e., PMKVY 3.0 which was modified on the basis of the evaluation studies and learning from the implementation of PMKVY 1.0 and 2.0.
