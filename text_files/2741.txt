                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2539






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Inclusion of Names in NRC




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of persons whose names have been included in the National Register of Citizens (NRC) which is being updated vigorously in Assam till date;(b) the arrangement to be made by the Government for the persons whose names have been excluded from NRC;(c) whether the Government has the figures regarding the said persons;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of the persons whose names have not been included in the NRC in Assam, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The complete draft NRC, Assam list covering 2,89,83,677 persons has been published on 30.07.2018. (b) to (e):	After the disposal of claims and objections, the final NRC is to be published by 31st July, 2019 as per the direction of the Hon’ble Supreme Court. The persons excluded from the final NRC may prefer appeal before the designated tribunal constituted under the Foreigners (Tribunals) Order, 1964.*****
