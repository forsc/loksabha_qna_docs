                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4588






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






New Industrial Policy




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any steps have been taken to ensure long term competitiveness of manufacturing and industrial sector;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government intends to announce New Industrial Policy; and(d) 	if so, the probable time for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (d): 	Development and promotion of industrial sector is a continuous process. Government has already taken a number of measures towards long term competitiveness of manufacturing and industrial sector. These include implementation of Production Linked Incentive Scheme of Rs. 1.97 lakh crore in identified sectors, reduction in corporate tax rates, integrated development of infrastructure under PM GatiShakti to improve logistics, amalgamation and rationalization of Central labour laws, reduction in compliance burden, setting up of Empowered Group of Secretaries (EGoS) and Project Development Cells (PDCs) to monitor investment projects, setting-up of National Single Widow System (NSWS). Government is committed to adopt and take steps including announcement of any new Policy, if so required to address the present and future challenges of the industry sector in an expeditious manner.   ******
