                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3345






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Technical Training under PMKVY in Bihar




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any initiative has been taken for providing employment to youths by imparting various technical training to them under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana;(b) 	if so, the average percentage of youths trained and provided employment in private/Government sectors in Katihar district of Bihar in the last two years; and(c)	details of the locations selected and the technical courses being offered in Katihar district of Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing its flagship scheme, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY). PMKVY has two training components, namely, Short-Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL). Under PMKVY, STT is linked to placement, while RPL does not mandate placements as it recognizes the existing skills of candidate. Under the PMKVY-STT, placement is linked with payout to training providers. The last tranche, i.e., 30 percent of total payout is disbursed to training providers only on ensuring placement of the candidates. Further, Rozgar Melas are organised with the support of Sector Skill Councils, training providers and implementing agencies to directly link the candidates with the potential employers.(b)       Under PMKVY-STT, in the last two years (2019-21), as on 10.07.2021, 3,447 and 2,388 candidates have been trained and certified respectively in the Katihar district of the State of Bihar. Out of certified candidates, 941 candidates have been placed in various sectors, which is around 40%.(c)         Ministry promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) in every district for imparting skill training in Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode throughout the country.Under PMKVY-STT, as on 10.07.2021, 16 TCs including 1 PMKK (located at Opposite Katihar Medical College, Karim Bagh, Katihar, PIN- 854105) have been empanelled in the district of Katihar in Bihar.Under PMKVY, in the district of Katihar in the State of Bihar, trainings have been imparted in various NSQF (National Skills Qualification Frameworks) aligned job roles including technical job roles. Name of some of the job roles including technical job roles wherein training has been imparted under PMKVY in district of Katihar, State of Bihar, is given below:i.	Self Employed Tailorii.	Assistant Bar Bender & Steel Fixeriii.	Assistant Construction Painter & Decoratoriv.	Assistant Masonv.	Assistant Shuttering Carpentervi.	CCTV Installation Technicianvii.	Field Technician - Computing and Peripheralsviii.	Field Technician - UPS and Inverterix.	Carpenter - Wooden Furniturex.	General Duty Assistantxi.	Home Health Aidexii.	Retail Outlet Attendant (Oil & Gas)xiii.	Consignment Booking Assistantxiv.	Consignment Tracking Executivexv.	Consumer Energy Meter Technicianxvi.	Lineman Distribution (Multi Skilled)xvii.
