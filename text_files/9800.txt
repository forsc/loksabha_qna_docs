                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3420






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Forest Right Act Revoked in Mizoram




Pratima Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that Forest Right Act (FRA) was revoked in Mizoram dueto stoppage of fund by the Union Government; and(b) the funds allocated by the Government during the past three years and current year for FRA, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): - Mizoram Government has informed on 05.12.2019 that the Forest Right Act (FRA) was revoked in Mizoram due to stoppage of fund by the Union government and the notification for the revocation of FRA in Mizoram has not been issued yet. The Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) held on 29.04.2015 had not approved proposal from the State amounting to Rs. 10 lakh for implementation of FRA in Mizoram as FRA issues in the State were already settled.
