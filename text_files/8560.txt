                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Setting up of Coastal Base Maritime Tourism Circuit




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up coastal base maritime tourism circuit along with the seashore of the country; (b) 	if so, whether the coastal circuits will be exclusively connected with water transportation and be maintained with same hospitality and facilities therefor; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	if not, the new plan to activate new openings for tourism development in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	The Ministry of Tourism under its scheme of Swadesh Darshan is developing thematic circuits in the country in a planned and prioritized manner. Coastal circuit is one of the fifteen thematic circuits identified for development under this scheme with the objective of developing tourism infrastructure in the coastal areas.The project under Swadesh Darshan scheme covers both new as well as existing tourism destinations. Under the scheme the Ministry provides financial assistance to the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure with the objective of providing better experience and facilities to the visitors at these sites and providing employment opportunities to the local community. *****
