                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Completion of NH Projects




Jayant Sinha













                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has achieved the targets of completion of National Highway (NH) projects during the last five years;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor along with the steps taken/being taken by the Government to complete these projects in a stipulated time frame;(c)	the number of incomplete NH projects, State/UT-wise;(d)	the percentage of projects suffering from cost and time overruns;(e)	the length of NHs constructed in Tamil Nadu along with the funds allocated and utilised for the purpose; and(f) 	the length of NHs under construction in the country presently particularly in Tamil Nadu and the time limit set for completion thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	to (b) Target and achievement during the last five years are as follows:Year	Target (Length in km)Len	Achievement (Length in km)2014-15	6300	44102015-16	10950	60612016-17	15000	82312017-18	15000	98292018-19	10000	10855Target in some years could not be achieved fully mainly due to the delay in land acquisition, utility shifting, non-availability of soil/aggregate, poor performance of contractors, environment/forest/wildlife clearances, ROB & RUB issues with Railways, public agitation for additional facilities, arbitration/contractual disputes with contractors etc. The steps taken by the government to complete the projects in stipulated time frame are given at Annexure-I.(c) to (d) There are about 1495 projects ongoing in the country out of which about 30% projects are suffering from time-overrun. The State / UT wise details of ongoing projects are given at Annexure-II.(e) 	Ministry has constructed 1426 km of National Highways with an expenditure of about Rs 9221 Crore in the state of Tamil Nadu during last five years. (f)       52,948 km of National Highways projects are under implementation out of which 2,501 km of projects are in Tamil Nadu.  These projects are targeted for completion in next 4 years.
