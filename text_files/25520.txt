                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1191






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Allocation of Funds under MGNREGA in Telangana




Sanjay Kumar Bandi













                                                    Raju Bista
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds allocated under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) to the States of Telangana and West Bengal including the Darjeeling and Kalimpong district during the last seven years, year-wise;(b) 	the details of MGNREGS projects undertaken in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts;(c) 	the details of spending by the Government of Telangana along with the number of beneficiaries district wise and year-wise; and(d) 	whether any deviation of funds for specific purpose has been reported, if so, the steps taken/being taken by the Union Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):	Mahatma Gandhi NREGA is a demand driven wage employment programme under which Central funds are released to the States/UTs on the basis of “agreed to” Labour Budget. No State/UT-wise allocation of funds is made under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA Scheme. Wage payment is released into the accounts of beneficiaries directly. Funds under material and administrative component are released to the States/UTs. No fund is released to Districts by Central Government.
