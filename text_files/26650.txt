                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2221






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






New Registered Companies in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details number of new companies registered in Assam since Financial Year 2011-12 in all categories;(b) out of the above companies how many companies are still in the business, category-wise; and(c) whether the Government conducts audit to determine the closure of companies? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION; MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING; AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS                           [RAO INDERJIT SINGH](a) and (b): A total of 5432 number of companies were registered in the State of Assam from financial year 2011-12 to financial year 2020-2021. Out of these, 4775 number of companies are having active status. Rest of the companies are either dissolved, amalgamated, converted to Limited Liability Partnerships, struck off u/s 248 of the Companies Act, 2013, in the process of strike off or closed due to such other reasons. Category wise details of these active Companies are as under:-Categories of Companies	Number of CompaniesPublic Limited Companies	176Private Limited Companies	4278One Person Companies	321Total	4775 (c): No audit to determine the closure of companies is required to be conducted by the Government as per the Companies Act.*****
