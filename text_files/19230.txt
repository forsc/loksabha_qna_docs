                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

603






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Self Help Groups in MSME Sector




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the list of Self-Help Groups engaged in MSME sector in Haryana, district-wise; (b) 	the details of the effects of the small finances in respect of these Self-Help Groups in Bhiwani-Mahendragarh-Charkhi Dadri area; (c) 	the measures taken by the Government to promote Self-Help Groups in MSME sector Haryana especially in Bhiwani-Mahendragarh-Charkhi Dadri area; and (d) 	the status of the progress of Self-Help Groups in Haryana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Ministry of MSME, through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC), has identified Self- Help Groups (SHGs) in MSME sector in Haryana State who are being assisted under its schemes and the District-Wise details are as follows:<pre>	Sr. No.	Name of Scheme/Programme	Name of District	No. of SHGs 	Status1	Honey Mission	Kaithal-01Sirsa-01	02	Identified for assistance2	Kumbhar Sashktikaran Programme	Kaithal-05Rohtak-04Bhiwani-02Jind-02Charkhi Dadri-04Hisar-02	19	Identified for assistance3	PMEGP  	Panchkula-01	01	Application to 1 SHG under PMEGP has been forwarded to the bank	Total		22	</pre> In addition, under Startup Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP), as a sub scheme under Deendayal AntyodayaYojna-National Rural Livelihood Mission, assistance is provided to rural poor including SHGs to set up enterprises at the village level in non agricultural sector. 	For the state of Haryana, implementation of SVEP in 11 blocks has been approved and enterprise formation has started in 6 blocks, the details of which are given in the table below:<pre>S. No.	District	Block	DPR Approval	Total enterprise to be formed (4 years)	Enterprise formed (Dec 2020)1	Bhiwani	BawaniKhera	Sep-16	1,643	1,7322	Fatehabad	Fatehabad	Sep-18	1,292	7653	Jhajjar	Matanhail	Sep-18	1,175	6354	Karnal	Gharaunda	Sep-18	1,285	6435	Mewat	Tauru	Sep-16	1,343	1,4046	Panchkula	Pinjore	Sep-18	1,133	506</pre>(b):	Financial assistance under PMEGP to the identified SHG in Panchkula, Haryana is under process in the bank. Besides under SVEP projects, an amount of approximately Rs. 35,000 is provided as Community Enterprise Fund (CEF) to any SHG member desiring to set up an enterprise. Under Honey Mission and Kumbhar Sashktikaran Programme, no direct financial assistance is provided to beneficiaries (SHGs). (c):	KVIC, a statutory unit of Ministry of MSME has conducted 4 awareness camps for the promotion of schemes viz. PMEGP, SFURTI, Honey Mission, Kumbhar Sashktikaran Programme etc.(d):	As on 31st December, 2020, 42355 SHGs have been formed under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojna -National Rural Livelihood Mission in Haryana.
