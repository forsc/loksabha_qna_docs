                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1436






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Computerisation of Primary Agricultural Credit Socieites




Goddeti Madhavi













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has prepared an action plan to computerize all Primary Agricultural Credit Societies (PACS) across the country and link them with District Banks, State Co-operative Banks and NABARD;(b)	If so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the States that have already undertaken the implementation of the above program?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF COOPERATION ???????? ??????        (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) to (c):      Yes, Sir. A scheme of computerization of Primary Agricultural Credit Societies (PACS) is under formulation in consultation with various stakeholders including NABARD and State Governments. States like Uttarakhand and Telangana have computerized some functionalities of PACS. ****
