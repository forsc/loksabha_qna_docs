                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

453






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Operation of Soil Health Scheme




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	the number of Soil Health Cards printed and dispatched in both cycles so far; (b) 	the funds allocated, under the programme and the details of the funds utilized, State/UT-wise; (c) 	the total number of Soil Testing Labs, State/UT-wise;(d) 	whether all the Soil Testing Labs are infrastructurally equipped, if not, the reasons therefor; (e) 	the percentage of samples collected and tested under the programme; and (f) 	whether the Government has experienced any obstacles in the implementation of the programme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):  	During the 1st Cycle (2015-2017) of the scheme, 10.74 crore soil health cards and during the 2nd Cycle (2017-19), 10.53 crore soil health cards (as on 21.06.2019) have been printed and distributed by State Governments to farmers across the country.  (b): 	The details of funds rereleased to States and utilized by them is given at Annexure-I.(c) & (d):	As per information received from State Governments, at present, 1304 static Soil Testing Laboratories (STLs), 165 Mobile STLs, 6326 Mini Labs & 154 Village Level STLs are working. The state-wise details are given at Annexure-II.Contd…..2/-- 2 -(e) & (f):	The samples collected and tested under the Soil Health Card Programme are given below:-(Figures in lakh)Cycle/year	Target for sample collection 	Soil Samples Collected	Samples tested	Percent of sample collection	Percent of sample testedCycle-1(2015-2017)	253.54 	253.49	253.49	100%	100%Cycle -2(2017-2019)	273.99 	271.66	263.48	99.15	96.16The programme has been implemented smoothly so far.
