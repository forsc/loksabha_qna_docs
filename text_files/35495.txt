                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2839






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Budgetary Allocation and Utilisation for Migrant Workers




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of the monitoring centres set up to track the movement of migrant workers and other job-related grievances related to Covid-19, including the location of such centres and their status including start date of functioning centres;(b)whether the Government intends to set up additional monitoring centres in other locations across the country;(c)if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d)the details of budgetary allocation, approvals and utilization of the existing monitoring centres for the financial year 2021-22 at present therein;(e)whether the data collected by monitoring centres is publicly accessible; and (f)if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		 The details of monitoring centers set up during the pandemic are enclosed as Annexure.(b) & (c):	There is no proposal to set up any additional monitoring centre as the existing monitoring centres are found adequate.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(d):		No separate Budget is required for monitoring centres.(e):		The complaint data pertains to individuals and hence not publicly accessible.(f):		The complaints/grievances related to pending wages, termination of jobs or other service matters during the period of pandemic were received at the monitoring centres for re-dressal. The complaints pertaining to the State sphere were forwarded to the respective State Governments.*                                                               ********
