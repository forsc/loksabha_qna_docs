                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Institutional deliveries under Janani Suraksha Yojana




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) Whether the Government maintains any data regarding the number of institutional deliveries effected under the Janani Suraksha Yojana in the state of Uttar Pradesh, if so, the details thereof district-wise and if not, the reasons therefor;(b)	The number of deliveries that took place in a Government or accredited private healthcare facility or at home of the pregnant mother under the supervision of Asha worker during the last two years in Jaunpur district; and(c)	The rank/status of Uttar Pradesh with regard to performance of the above scheme among all the States?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a):  Yes sir. The data regarding the number of institutional deliveries effected under Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) in the State of Uttar Pradesh (District-wise) as shared by the State for  FY 2020-21 is attached at    ANNEXURE-I.(b):  The number of deliveries that took place in Government or accredited Private Healthcare facilities or at home of the pregnant mother under supervision of ASHA worker during the last two years under JSY in Jaunpur District as shared by the State are as follows:1.	Government or accredited Private Healthcare facilityFinancial Year	Number of beneficiaries2019-20	399592020-21	399852.	Home Delivery Year	Total Beneficiaries2019-2020	12020-2021	8(c):  The status of Uttar Pradesh with regard to performance of JSY scheme among all the States is attached at ANNEXURE-II.***
