                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Toxic Leaded Paints




A. Chellakumar













                                                    M. Selvaraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has banned production and use of toxic lead paints in the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the export of toxic lead paints to neighbouring countries by various paint companies during the last year and the current year; and(c) 	the action taken by the Government to restrict the export of banned products from the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (c):	Any manufacture, trade, import and export of Household and Decorative Paints containing lead or lead compounds (calculated as lead metal) in excess of 90 parts per million (0.009 per cent) of the weight of the total non-volatile content of the weight of the dried paint film is prohibited vide Notification No. G.S.R. 1030(E) dated 1st November, 2016 issued by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (Annexure-1). Some items under HS Code 2824 (lead oxides, red lead and orange lead) have lead or lead compounds less than 90 parts per million (0.009 per cent). Details of export of items falling under HS code 2824  to neighbouring countries are attached as Annexure-2.******
