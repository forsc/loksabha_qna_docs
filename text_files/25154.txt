UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1074






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Record of Illegaly Exported Artefacts




Brijendra Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has maintained a record of artefacts of historical significance taken abroad illegally prior to independence;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is taking steps to assert illegally exported artefacts as India''s cultural property to encourage their voluntary return; and(d) if so, the details of artefacts successfully returned through said means?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) No Sir.(b) Question does not arise.(c)&(d) Yes Sir. The Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) is constantly making best efforts through Indian missions abroad for retrieval of illegally exported antiquities.
