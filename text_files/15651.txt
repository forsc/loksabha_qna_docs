                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4462






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Digital Mapping




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of the project for digitally mapping of entire country by the Survey of India; (b) 	the details of States in which the project started and the areas covered so far; (c) 	the details of funds allocated for the purpose; (d) 	the time by which the remaining States are likely to be covered; and (e) 	the time by which the project is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY, MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES ANDMINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????, ??????? ?? ???????????? ?????? ?? ?????? ??????? ????????. ???? ?????(a)	The digital mapping of the entire country has been completed in 2009 on 1:50K scale.  This digital data can be freely accessed by Indian citizens on Survey of India’s (SoI’s) Public Portal i.e. https://indiamaps.gov.in and by Government agencies on https://g2g.indiamaps.gov.in using authorized access.  The digital data/maps can also be purchased from Survey of India. (b) & (c):  Details of States in which the project has been started based on request received from the concerned State Government  are as under:-State	Area / Habitations covered	Scale	Funds allocated (in Rs.)	RemarksHaryana	Entire Haryana State (44,212 Sq.km)	1:500 for urban areas and 1:800 for rural areas	150.00 Crore	Fund allocation varies due to different deliverables of the projects.  Maharashtra	Complete Abadi areas (40,000 Habitations)	1:500 scale	76.00 Crore	Karnataka	Part of Karnataka State (51,000 Sq.km)	1:500 for both urban & rural areas	76.00 Crore	Madhya Pradesh	Complete Rural / Urban habitations (number of habitations = 55,000)	1:500 for both urban & rural areas	40.10 Crore	(d)	Requisition from remaining States has not been received.(e)	The projects for Haryana, Maharashtra, Karnataka & Madhya Pradesh are targeted for completion by 2024.                                                      ******
