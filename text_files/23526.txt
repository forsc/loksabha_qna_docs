                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4528






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Residential Schools for SCs




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4528 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021RESIDENTIAL SCHOOLS FOR SCs †4528. SHRI ARUN SAO: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has formulated any scheme to establish residential schools in Scheduled Castes (SCs) majority blocks in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, State, district and block-wise including Chhattisgarh State; (c) whether there is any proposal to set up a residential school each in SCs dominated block Masturi in district Bilaspur and block Mungeli of district Mungeli in Chhattisgarh State; and (d) if so, the details and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI  RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a): This Department has not formulated any scheme to establish residential schools in Scheduled Castes (SCs) majority Blocks in the country.  Department of School Education and Literacy, Ministry of Education establishes residential schools, viz. Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya (KGBVs) and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalyas (JNVs) in Scheduled Castes (SCs) majority/concentrated Districts. Further, KGBVs are set up in Educationally Backward Blocks, but not in SC majority Blocks. Therefore, decision to establish residential schools is not made on the basis of SC majority Blocks. As such, this ministry has not formulated any scheme to establish residential schools in Scheduled Castes (SCs) majority Blocks in the country.  (b) to (d):  Do not arise.***
