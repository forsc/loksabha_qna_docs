                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1224






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Operation Green Scheme for Value Addition




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing Operation Greens Scheme to enhance value chain for tomato, onion and potato crops and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the details of the farmers who are likely to benefit from this scheme, State-wise; (c)	the details of the budget allocated for subsidy under the scheme and the expenditure incurred till date, State-wise; and (d)	the details of long term development projects under the Operation Greens Scheme and the inputs along with outcomes observed thereto?    






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES  (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	Yes Sir, in pursuance to Budget announcement for the year 2018-19, Ministry has launched a central sector scheme “Operation Greens – a scheme for integrated development of Tomato, Onion and Potato (TOP) value chain” with a budgetary allocation of Rs. 500 crore in November 2018.  Under short term price stabilization measures of this scheme, during glut situation subsidy is provided at the rate of 50% for transportation and storage, for evacuation of TOP crops from production surplus areas to consumption centres. Further, in pursuance of Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan, this subsidy has been extended from Tomato, Onion and Potato (TOP) crops to a total of 41 notified horticulture crops (TOTAL) if prices fall below the average prices for last three years or 15% below of previous year’s price. 	Under long term integrated value chain development of this scheme, Grant-in-aid is provided at the rate of 50% (70% in case of FPOs and SC/ST) of the eligible project cost, subject to maximum of ? 50cr per project, for setting up of integrated value chain development projects for Tomato, Onion and Potato in identified clusters in major producing States. (b) to (d):  Under long-term measures, Ministry had approved 6 integrated value chain projects with total project cost of ? 363.29cr including approved grant-in-aid of ? 136.81 cr, which are expected to create 46,950 MT of storage capacity; 3.36 Lakh MT/annum of processing facility; benefit approx. 35,380 number of farmers and generate approx. 18,063 number of direct and indirect employment. The State-wise details of these approved projects are attached at Annexure-I. The details of the budget allocated and expenditure since 2018-2019 are as under:-(Rs. in cr)Year	BE	RE	Actual Expenditure 2018-19	  0.00 	 200.00 	5.50 2019-20	  200.00 	32.48	2.842020-21	127.50	38.20	22.29 There is no State-wise allocation of funds under the scheme.*****
