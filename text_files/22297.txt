                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3400






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






EWS Reservation




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3400 TO BE ANSWERED ON 16.03.2021EWS RESERVATION 3400. SHRI THOMAS CHAZHIKADAN: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is aware that in the State of Kerala, most of the applicants are not able to avail the benefits of Economically Weaker Section (EWS) Reservation as the criteria fixed for house plot and agricultural lands are not suitable to Kerala;(b) whether there is no segregation of house plots and agricultural lands in Kerala and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether there is a necessity to amend the criteria of assets as prescribed by the Government since the residential houses are constituted in the agricultural land itself in Kerala and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government has received any memorandum/petition to amend the criteria and if so the details thereof; (e) whether the Government would consider to amend the criteria to suit Kerala as the applicant is eligible even if he possesses agricultural land less than five acres; and (f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR) (a) to (d): Yes Sir. A request has been received from the State Government of Kerala for amending the prescribed criteria for EWS reservation in view of the prevailing conditions in that State.(e) & (f): There is no proposal to amend the EWS  eligibility conditions and specify a separate criteria for the State of Kerala .*****
