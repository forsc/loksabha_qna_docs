                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4476






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Population of Elephants




Agatha K. Sangma














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the population of elephants in the country, State/UT-wise; (b)	the total number of captive elephants owned by private individuals or religious institutions; (c)	whether these captive elephants are covered by public liability insurance scheme; (d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e)	if not, the reasons therefor and the response of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) The State-wise details of elephant population in the country as per the last synchronized elephant estimation conducted in 2017 are given in Annexure.  (b) The total number of captive elephants owned by private individuals and religious institutions are 1774.  (c) No sir.(d) Does not arise.(e) There is no provision in the Wildlife (Protection) act, 1972 regarding public liability insurance scheme for captive elephants. ***
