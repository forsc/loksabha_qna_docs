                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Schemes for Tribal Students




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of current schemes of the Government for tribal students; (b) the State-wise details of the funds allocated for such schemes; (c) whether any time line has been fixed for completion of the said schemes; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):   This Ministry implements the following ongoing Centrally Sponsored/ Central Sector schemes for Scheduled Tribe students in the country:  (i)    Pre-Matric Scholarships for ST students.(ii)   Post-Matric Scholarships for ST students(iii)  National Fellowship & Scholarship for Higher Education for ST students(iv)  National Overseas Scholarships for ST students for studying abroad(v)   Grants in aid to Voluntary Organisations Working for the Welfare of   STs(vi)Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS)    (b):   In respect of Pre and Post Matric schemes the   funds are released to States/UTs. The funds released during the last three years is at Annexure.From Sl.No iii to v these are the Central Sector schemes and funds are not allocated State-wise. In EMRS, the funds are released to National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) an autonomous organisation.   (c)& (d):    The cabinet has approved these schemes for continuation of next Finance Cycle from 2021-26.
