                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1181






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Allocation of Foodgrains During Pandemic




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                








                                                    N.K. Premachandran
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to provide sufficient quantity of foodgrains to the States considering the Covid-19 pandemic; (b) 	if so, the details thereof alongwith the quantity of foodgrains distributed to the State and UT’s in addition to the sanctioned quota, State/UT-wise; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to distribute other essential commodities to the ration card holders through public distribution system considering the Covid19 pandemic; (d) 	if so, the details thereof along with the number of PDS beneficiaries covered under the said scheme; (e) 	whether the Union Government proposes to supply nutritious food to women and children through public distribution system considering the Covid-19, pandemic; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):    In addition to foodgrains provided under NFSA @ highly subsidized rates of Rs 1/2/3 per kg for nutri-cereals/wheat/rice respectively to Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) households and priority households(PHH),under PMGKAY scheme, additional allocation of foodgrains @ 5 kg per person per month free of cost was made by Govt of India for all the beneficiaries covered under Targetted Public Distribution Systyem (Antyodaya Anna Yojna & Priority Households) including those covered under Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) for a period of 8 months i.e April-Nov, 2020. Further, under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat Package (ANBP), this Department had also allocated additional food-grain covering all the States/UTs for migrants/stranded migrants, who are neither covered under National Food Security Act (NFSA) nor State Scheme PDS Cards, @ 5 kg per person per month free of cost for a period of two months (May & June, 2020). ...........2/--2-(b):   Quantity of foodgrains allocated during 2020-21 to various States/UTs under PMGKAY and ANB are placed at Annexure-I.(c):     No Sir.(d):     Does not arise.(e) and (f):  No Sir, However, since 2019-20, a pilot project on supply of fortified rice under PDS in 15 States (one district per state) is operational.****Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (b) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION No. 1181 FOR ANSWER ON
