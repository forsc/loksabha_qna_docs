                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1683






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Selling of Land and Assets




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to sell the land and other assets of Sick Fertilizer Units in the near future;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and   (d)	the details of the steps taken/likely to be taken in this regard, specially in Uttar Pradesh and North East region? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS.(D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) to (c):	At present, there are two sick fertilizer companies viz., Madras Fertilizers Limited (MFL) and Fertilizers and Chemicals Travancore Limited (FACT) situated in the states of Tamil Nadu and Kerala respectively.  MFL has identified about 70 acres surplus vacant land for sale to Chennai Petroleum corporation Limited (CPCL), a group company of IOCL.  Similarly, FACT has identified total 651.479 acres of land for sale to Government of Kerala (481.79 acres) and Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (169.689 acres).  Transaction for sale of land from FACT to Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited has already been executed. (d):	There are no sick units in UP and North East.**********
