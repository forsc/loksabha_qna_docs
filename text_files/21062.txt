                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Excise Duty on Petrol and Diesel




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and amount of excise duty on petrol and diesel being charged per litre from the consumers in the country; (b) the total amount of excise duty collected from the consumers during the period from January last year to January current year; and (c) whether the Government proposes to do away with the said duty and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a):  The details of total central excise duty on petrol and diesel is given as under:Commodity	Total central excise duty(Rs. per litre)Petrol (unbranded)	32.90Petrol (branded)	34.10Diesel (unbranded)	31.80Diesel (branded)	34.20(b):  The total amount of central excise duty collected on petrol and diesel during the period January, 2020 to January, 2021is about Rs. 3,40,750 crores. (c) : At present, there is no such proposal.*****
