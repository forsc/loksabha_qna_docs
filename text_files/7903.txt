                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1545






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Backlog of Vacancies




Sangeeta Azad














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Indian Railways provide maximum employment opportunities to the unemployed and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes are not being recruited in proportion to their reserved seats;(c) 	if so, whether the Ministry is likely to fill up the backlog vacancies of SCs, STs and OBCs;(d) 	if so, the time by which the backlog vacancies will be filled; and(e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Yes, Sir. Indian Railways provide direct and indirect employment opportunities. Vacancies in the organization are filled through open Contd…..P/2-..2..recruitment. Indirect employment opportunities occur through schemes like Station Development Programmes that envisage private investments for construction, operation and maintenance of infrastructure, the commercial utilization of sparable Railway land and redevelopment of stations, all of which create many business opportunities and boost employment generation.(b): Figures of direct recruitment during last 3 years are as under: Year	           Candidates empanelled       Total	          UR	 SC	 ST	OBC	Total	         19224	 6352	 3247	11591	40414Prescribed %age	 50.5%	 15%	7.5%	27%	100%%age	         47.6%	 15.7%	 8.0%	28.7%	100%(c): Does not arise.(d): Does not arise.(e): Does not arise.*****
