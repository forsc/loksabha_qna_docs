                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3813






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Speed Limit for Commercial Vehicles




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government porposes to increase the speed limit for commercial vehicles specially for buses and car operators on various categories of highways in the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the details of speed limits fixed for various categoris of vehicles at present?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) and (b) No such proposal is under consideration in the Ministry.(c) The Government vide notification S.O. 1522 (E) dated, 6th April, 2018 has fixed the maximum speed limit in respect of different classes of motor vehicles on different roads in India as under:-Maximum speed per hour in kilometers on roads in IndiaS. No.	Class of Motor Vehicles	Expressway with Access Control	4 lane and above divided carriageway (roads with Median strips/Dividers)	Road within Municipal Limits	Other Roads(1)	(2)	(3)	(4)	(5)	(6)1.	Motor vehicles used for carriage of passengers comprising not more than eight seats in addition to the driver’s seat ( M1 category vehicles)	120	100	70	702.	Motor vehicles used for carriage of passengers comprising nine or more seats in addition to the driver’s seat ( M2 and M3 category Vehicles)	100	90	60	603.	More vehicles used for carriage of goods ( All N category Vehicles)	80	80	60	604.	Motor Cycles	80*	80	60	605.	Quadricycle	-	60	50	506.	Three wheeled vehicles	-	50	50	50*  If permitted to ply on Expressway.*****
