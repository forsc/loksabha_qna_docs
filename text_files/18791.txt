                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Road Construction Under PMGSY




Y. S. Avinash Reddy













                                                    Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the funds allocated, released and utilised under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in each of the years since 2014, State-wise;(b) 	the planned/allocated and completed length of surfaced roads since 2014, State and year-wise including Andhra Pradesh; and(c) 	the allocated and completed length of surfaced roads in the State of Telangana during the said period, Gram Panchayat-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  Allocations  to the states are  made  based on their absorption capacity and depending upon the quantum of Budget Estimates/ Revised Estimates which keep on varying from year to year.   The detail of funds released (central share) and expenditure (including the state share) under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) since 2014, state-wise are given at  Annexure-I .(b)  The information regarding planned/allocated and completed length of surfaced roads since 2014, State and year-wise including Andhra Pradesh is given at Annexure-II. (c)  Under PMGSY, gram panchayat-wise details are not maintained. District-wise and year-wise status of length constructed under PMGSY in the State of Telangana is given at Annexure-III.***** Annexure-IAnnexure referred to in reply Unstarred Question No. 215 for reply in Lok Sabha on 02.02.2021Statement indicating State-wise/Year wise  Release and Expenditure under PMGSY since 2014-15 till 2020-21 (as on 31.12.2020)(Rs in crore)
