                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2278






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






On-shore and Off-shore Drifting




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of production of oil through on-shore and off-shore drifting; and(b) whether there is any road-map for the drifting production of oil for the next five years along with the corresponding budget earmarked for the said period?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) The details of domestic onshore and offshore oil production for the year 2018-19 are as below:(MMT- Million Metric Tonnes)OnshoreOffshoreTotalCrude oil Production (in MMT)17.3316.8634.20% share50.68 %49.32 %100 %(b) Plan of action has been prepared to augment production of crude oil and natural gas. All the exploration and production related projects and activities are implemented by oil companies from out of their internal or external resources. Government does not provide any budgetary support for production related activities.********
