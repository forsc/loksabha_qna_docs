                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4743






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






PMEGP in Maharashtra




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the number of people having applied under Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) during the last three years, State and year-wise;(b) 	the number of persons benefitted from this programme during the said period;(c) 	the number of persons benefitted from this programme in Maharashtra during the last three years, district and year-wise;(d) 	whether the self-help group, production cooperative societies and charitable trusts have been benefitted in Maharashtra during the said period; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA)(a) & (b):	Ministry of MSME, through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC), is implementing Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) for generating self employment opportunities in the country by setting up micro-enterprises in non-farm sector. The number of persons who have applied and the number of persons who have  benefitted (project/units set up) under PMEGP during the last three years, State and year-wise is given at Annexure-I.(c):	The number of persons benefitted under PMEGP in Maharashtra during the last three years; district and year wise is given at Annexure-II.(d) & (e):	The number of Self-Help Groups, Cooperative Societies, Registered Institutions and Charitable Trusts who have been benefitted in Maharashtra during the last three years is given below:(Project/Units in numbers)<pre>Year	Self-Help Group(SHG)	Cooperative Societies	Regd. Institutions	Charitable Trusts2018-19	7	0	1	02019-20	3	1	1	02020-21	2	0	3	2</pre>
