                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Renaming Railway Station




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the local public representatives have made suggestions to the Railway Administration and the State Government  to prefix Shiravane before Juinagar Railway Station at Harbour rail route of Navi Mumbai ;(b)  if so, whether the Railways has received the said proposal from the Chief Secretary of the State Government of  Maharashtra;(c)  if so, whether the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has taken any initiative to rename this railway station;(d)   if so, the details thereof; and (e)   if not, the reasons therefor ?         






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) :   A representation was received from Shri Rajan Vichare, Hon’ble Member of Parliament, for renaming of Jui Nagar railway station.(b) :    No, Sir.(c) to (e) : Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, is the competent authority to approve change in the name of a railway station on Indian Railways.  It does so on the recommendation of the State Government concerned.          Zonal Railway implements the change of name of railway stations after Gazette notification issued by State Government after getting approval from Ministry of Home Affairs in this regard.******
