                                    CULTURE

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4642






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Setting up of Indian Institute of Heritage and Conservation




Supriya Sadanand Sule













                                                    Sajda Ahmed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up an Indian Institute of Heritage and Conservation with the status of a deemed university and if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives behind the move;(b)	whether the Government has identified the place where this institute will be set up and the time by which this institute will start functioning; (c)	whether the Government has decided to set up more museums and if so, the funds earmarked for setting up of new museums and development of the existing ones; (d)	whether the Government also proposes to set up tribal museum and if so, the details thereof and the time by which this will be set up; and(e)	whether initiatives have been taken for cultural push to promote new ASI protected monuments across the country including West Bengal along with the details of the ASI heritage sites being developed by the private and corporate entities across the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) MINISTRY OF CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) Ministry of Culture is planning to establish an Indian Institute of Heritage and Conservation (IIHC) to impart higher education/research/training in the field of cultural heritage of country.  The establishment of IIHC is at conceptual stage.(c)	Ministry of Culture operates Museum Grant Scheme under which financial assistance is provided to State Governments, voluntary institutions, Societies and trusts registered as a society under the Indian Societies Act of 1860 (XXI) or a similar legislation of the state governments for setting up of new museums and development of existing museums. The details of fund earmarked during last 3 year and current year for setting of new museums and development of the existing museum under “Museum Grant Scheme” is give below.Financial Year 	Funds allocated (Rs. in lakhs) 2016-17	2500.002017-18	3500.002018-19	4808.002019-20	5558.00(d)	Ministry of Tribal has established Tribal Research Institutes (TRIs) in 24 States and 2 UT, out of which, 14 TRIs have cultural Museum. Further, Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) has sanctioned projects for setting up tribal freedom fighter’s museums in various States to honor tribal freedom fighters, who struggled against the Britishers and refused to bow down so that the coming generations may know how our tribals made sacrifices for the pride of mother India. The Details of museums sanctioned along with estimated cost and funds released by MoTA are at Annexure-I. (e)	Archaeological Survey of India is maintaining 3691 centrally protected monuments throughout the country out of which, at 13 monuments, works related to environmental development, providing visitors facilities, booking counter, interpretation centre, etc. is being taken up in collaboration with Private and Corporate entities under NCF Projects.
