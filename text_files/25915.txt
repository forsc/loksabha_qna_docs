                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1627






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Piped Water Connections in Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the target set for piped water connection to be provided in Maharashtra;(b) the time by which the target is going to be achieved; and(c) the achievement made so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI  PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c) 	As on 15thAugust, 2019,  at the time of launch of Jal Jeevan Mission, in Maharashtra, out of 142.36 lakh rural households, 48.44 lakh households (34%) had piped water connections. Since then, 43.63 lakh households (30.65%) have been provided tap water connections, thus increasing the tap water supply to 92.07 lakh households (64.67%). Remaining 50.329 lakhs rural households are planned to be provided with tap water connection by 2024.*****
