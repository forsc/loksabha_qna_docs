                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

570






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Commissioning of Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Limited




Pashupati Nath Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the plan formulated by the Government for timely commissioning of Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Limited (HURL), Sindri, Jharkhand;(b)	whether the project has been delayed due to COVID-19 pandemic; (c)	if so, the details and present status of the commissioning of the said project thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS( DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a).:   Government of India mandated revival of Sindri project through a Joint Venture company of nominated PSUs named Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Limited (HURL). Commissioning of the plant was scheduled within 36 months from the zero date i.e by May, 2021. The progress of the Sindri project is being monitored on regular basis by Department of Fertilizers and NITI Aayog as well. (b) & (c).: Yes Sir, the progress of the project was affected adversely due to Covid-19 pandemic. The Sindri project has faced several challenges like shortage of manpower & construction material, non-availability of Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM), travel restrictions, shortage of oxygen for industrial use, delay in supplies of Long Lead Instruments (LLIs) and onshore & offshore items due to Covid-19 pandemic. The Sindri project is now expected to be commissioned by June, 2022                                             XXXXXXX
