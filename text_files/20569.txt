                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Establishment of MSMEs




Mahendrabhai Kalubhai Munjpara














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to establish any industry/factory in Surendranagar district in Gujarat under MSME sector;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) does not set up any industry / factory. It envisions a vibrant MSME sector by promoting growth and development of the MSME Sector in cooperation with various Ministries/Departments, State Governments and Stakeholders through providing support to the MSME enterprises.
