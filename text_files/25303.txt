                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

936






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Skill Development Centres in Gujarat




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      the number of Skill Development Centres functioning in Gujarat under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana at present and district-wise details thereof;(b)      the number of students who have enrolled in Skill Development Centres for training in Gujarat and the number of people who have received training till date;(c)	whether the Government has made any efforts to provide employment to students who have received the said training;(d)	if not, whether the Government is making any effort at the central level to provide employment to the trained students; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), short term training is undergoing in the 186 training centres including 28 PMKK in the State of Gujarat. The district-wise number of training centres is given at Annexure. (b)       Under PMKVY, 2.09 lakh and 1.83 lakh candidates have been enrolled and trained, respectively, in the empanelled training centres under short term training (STT) component in the State of Gujarat. Also, 1.99 lakh and 1.94 lakh candidates have been enrolled and oriented, respectively, in Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) component of PMKVY in the State of Gujarat. (c)	Under the scheme, placement has been linked with payout to training providers. The last tranche, i.e., 30 percent of total payout is only disbursed to training providers on ensuring placement of the candidates. Further, Rozgar Melas are organised with the support of Sector Skill Councils, training providers and implementing agencies to directly link the candidates with the potential employers.(d)	In view of (c) above, question does not arise.(e)	In view of (c) above, question does not arise.AnnexureThe district-wise number of operational training centres in the state of Gujarat under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)S.
