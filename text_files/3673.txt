                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3409






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Unani College/Hospital in Ganderbal




Farooq Abdullah














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the infrastructure facilities provided to Unani College/Hospital at Zazina Badampura, Ganderbal is completed since long but it has not been made functional; and (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (b) Public Health being a State subject, opening of Unani College/Hospital comes under purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, Central Government has released Rs. 18.00 Crore during 2010-11 for setting up of Unani College at Zazina Badampura, Ganderbal. Further, Rs. 25.33 lakhs has also been released from 2015-16 to 2018-19 for upgradation of above college.          The building has not been taken over from the Executing Agency. However, deployment of Teaching faculty/consultants have been made by the State Government who are working presently in the associated Unani Hospital Shalteng, Srinagar.………………….
