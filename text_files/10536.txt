                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Sales Centres of Khadi and Gramodyog Commission




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of the retail sales centres of Khadi and Gramodyog Commission working in the country, State-wise;(b) 	whether many retail sales centres of Khadi and Gramodyog Commission are in dilapidated condition; (c)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	 Khadi and Village Industries Commission has 23 Departmental Sales Outlets/Centres (DSOs) under its 07 Khadi Gramodyog Bhavans. State-wise DSOs and its branches are as follows:<pre>State	DSOs 	BranchesDelhi	1 – DSO at Regal Building, Connaught Circus	6 – Branches [BKS Marg, Ashoka Hotel, RK Puram, Hauz Khas, Rohini & NOIDA]West Bengal	1 – DSO at Chandi Chowk, Kolkata	4 – Branches [Gariahat Road, Burdwan, Darjeeling & Kharagpur] Maharashtra	1 – DSO at Vile Parle, Mumbai	2 – Branches [Andheri-W & Nasik]Kerala	1 – DSO at M.G. Road, Ernakulum, Cochin	2 – Branches [Thiruvananthapuram & Guruvayoor]Bihar	1 – DSO at Patna	-Madhya Pradesh	1 – DSO at Bhopal	1 – Branch [Jawahar Chowk, Bhopal]Goa	1 – DSO at Margaon, Goa	1 – Branch [Panaji]</pre>(b)&(c):	No,   Sir.  The Outlets are renovated and modernized from time to time on need basis.(d):		Does not arise.
