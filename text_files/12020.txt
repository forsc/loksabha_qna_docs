                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1092






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Installation of 1KW FM Transmitters




Mahendrabhai Kalubhai Munjpara














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of steps taken/being taken regarding installation of 1 KW FM Transmitters at existing LPT/HPT DD sites sanctioned under 12th Plan at 100 places across the country;b)	whether the Government proposes to setup 1 KW FM transmitters in Surendranagar, Gujarat;c)	if so, the details thereof; andd)	if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) There is no approved project for installation of 1 kW FM transmitters at existing LPT/HPT DD sites. (b) to (d) A decision has been taken to setup a 100 W FM transmitter at Surendranagar, Gujarat. ******
