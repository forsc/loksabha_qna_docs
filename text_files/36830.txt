                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3985






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Stand-up India Scheme




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Nandigam Suresh
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of beneficiaries under the Stand-Up India Scheme in Andhra Pradesh during the last three years;(b)	whether the Ministry is to provide entrepreneurial training to the beneficiaries to enhance their businesses; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of Finance(a) to (c) A total number of 3,248 loans amounting to Rs. 787.81 crore have been extended under Stand Up India Scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh, during the last three years and current financial year, upto 28.02.2022. Year-wise details of loans sanctioned under this Scheme are as follows:(Amount in Rs. Crore)Financial year (FY)	Number of accounts	Sanctioned Amount2018-19	1,362	347.922019-20	913	230.582020-21	469	106.412021-22 upto 28.02.2022	504	102.9Total	3,248	787.81Apart from linking prospective borrowers to banks for loans, the online portal www.standupmitra.in developed by Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) for Stand Up India Scheme is also providing guidance to prospective entrepreneurs in their endeavour to set up business enterprises, starting from training to filling up loan applications, as per bank requirements. Through a network of more than 8000 Hand Holding Agencies, this portal facilitates step by step guidance for connecting prospective borrowers to various agencies with specific expertise viz. Skilling Centres, Mentorship support, Entrepreneurship Development Program Centres, District Industries Centre, together with addresses and contact number.*****
