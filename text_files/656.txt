                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

710






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Enhancement of MPLADS Fund




Manoj Rajoria













                                                    K. Muraleedharan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes  to enhance  the MPLADS funds as the last increase in the allocation was done in the year 2011-12;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the current status and the date of last follow up in this regard;(c)	whether there is any mechanism in place for physical monitoring of MPLAD Scheme;(d)	the year in which such physical monitoring was last conducted by the Government; and(e)	the periodicity of such physical monitoring ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)   (a) There is no proposal under consideration of the Government to enhance of Member of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme (MPLADS) funds. (b) Question does not arise.(c), (d) and (e) The MPLAD guidelines provide for monthly/ quarterly/ yearly timelines for inspection/ review of assets created through MPLADS funds. The guidelines also provides for third party inspection- physical audit and quality check by the State nodal Departments. The Union Government had conducted a third party physical monitoring exercise in the year 2014 for evaluation of the scheme.                                         *****
