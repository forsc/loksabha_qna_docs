                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2952






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Extent of Sanitation Facility




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government made progress in providing basic sanitation facilities to almost two-third or the 650 million people who stopped practicing open defecation between 2000 & 2017; and(b) if so, the details thereof and progress made in the State of Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) The Government had launched Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) [SBM(G)] with effect from 2nd October, 2014 with the aim to attain Open Defecation Free (ODF) India by 2nd October, 2019 by providing access to toilet facilities to all rural households in the country.  Under SBM(G), 9.63 crore toilets have been constructed till 05.07.2019.  As a result, sanitation coverage in the rural areas of the country which was 38.7% on 2.10.2014 has increased to more than 99% and 30 States, 622 Districts, 6,212 Blocks, 2,50,658 Gram Panchayats and 5,68,367 villages have been declared ODF.  In Telangana, 29,73,419 Individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed under SBM(G) and out of total 10,993 villages in the State, 8,331 (75.78%) villages have been declared ODF up to 5.7.2019.
