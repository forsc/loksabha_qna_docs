                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3523






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Working Days under MGNREGS




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to increase the number of working days under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS) in Gujarat and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the State Government of Gujarat has sent any proposal requesting the Union Government in this regard, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the time by which decision in this regard is likely to be taken?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	No, Sir. The mandate of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), 2005 is to provide at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult member volunteers to do unskilled manual work. However, an additional 50 days of wage employment is provided over and above 100 days in the notified drought affected areas or natural calamity areas in the country on recommendation of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare. (b) & (c):	Yes, Sir. State Government of Gujarat had requested in January, 2019 to provide 50 additional persondays over and above 150 days in all blocks of Kachchh district.  During the FY 2018-19, on the request of the State Government of Gujarat and on the recommendation the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare, the Ministry provided additional 50 days of employment in 11 districts with 51 block for drought/natural calamity affected areas of Gujarat (including Kuchchh District) under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS.
