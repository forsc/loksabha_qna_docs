                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

459






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Shortage of Onion Seeds




Balak Nath













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether there is a shortage of onion seeds in the country; (b) 	if so, whether the Government has decided not to export onion seeds;(c) 	if so, the reasons therefor and if not, the details of the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (d) 	whether the Government provides subsidy for constructing storage facility for onions, and if so the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (c):    Yes, due to heavy and erratic rainfall, the onion seeds production crops got affected in the major onion growing State i.e. Maharashtra resulting to shortage of onion seeds in the country. Accordingly, Govt. of Maharashtra requested to withhold the export of onion seeds and the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) vide Notification no.43/2015-20 dated 29th October, 2020 prohibited the export of onion seeds.(d):     Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare through Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) provides assistance for creation of low cost onion storage structure for which assistance is given @50% of maximum cost of Rs.1.75 Lakh/Unit having a capacity of 25 MT.   					*****
