                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1723






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) in the State of Kerala; (b)	whether the Government proposes to increase the allocation for the State of Kerala  under PMJVK and if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)  (a) : The Ministry of Minority Affairs implements the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) in 1300 identified  Minority Concentration Areas (MCAs) in 308 Districts of the Country which include 870 Blocks, 321 Towns and 109 District Headquarters, with the objective to develop socio economic assets and basic amenities. In the last 7 years, under PMJVK, more than 43000 basic infrastructure projects have been created such as schools, colleges, hostels, community centres, common service centres, ITIs, Polytechnics, Girls Hostels, Sadbhavana Mandap, Hunar Hub etc in backward Minority concentrated areas across the country.The coverage of the scheme in the State of Kerala has been increased from only 5 MCAs (4 Blocks & 1 Town) in 2 Districts to 73 MCAs in 2018-19, which include 7 District HQs, 23 Blocks and 43 Towns covering 13 Districts in the State. Since inception of the Scheme, the Ministry has approved projects worth Rs. 207.20 crore in the State of Kerala. The projects approved includes 209 Additional Class Rooms (ACR), 1807 Bicycles, 9 Community Service Centres (CSCs), 19 Drinking Water Projects, 33 Health projects, 2 Hunar Hubs, 1 Industrial training centre (ITI), 2 Market Sheds, 17 Toilets, 2 School Buildings, 3 Skill Development Centers and 3 Working Women Hostels.	(b) & (c): State-wise allocation is not made under PMJVK. The projects under PMJVK are proposed by the State Government based on the felt need to address the development deficits identified in the Minority Concentration Areas (MCAs) of the State concerned. ***
