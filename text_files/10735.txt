                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4036






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Widening of National Highway-343




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the accident prone black spots on the NH-343 near Nawadih in the Garhwa district of Jharkhand and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether any project has been launched by the Government to widen the road at this stretch and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the protection wall has been repaired to make it stronger and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) whether any traffic signals and fluorescent lights have been installed in that area to prevent further accidents and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a & b) Yes, sir. State Government of Jharkhand has submitted an estimate costing Rs. 19.88 Crore for “Realignment /Improvement of curves and safety, traffic calming measures at km 146 of NH 343 in Garhwa District” which is being considered for sanction during financial year 2019-20.(c) Old and damaged protection wall has been repaired and work on construction of new RCC Retaining wall behind the old protection wall on the valley side is in progress.(d) Retro reflective road signages, rumble strips, crash barriers etc. have been installed for traffic safety as per IRC guidelines.*****
