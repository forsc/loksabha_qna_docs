                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3301






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Implementation of Ayushman Bharat Yojana in NE Regions




Anil Firojiya













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the present status of Ayushman Bharat Yojana;(b) the State-wise amount of the funds allocated in this regard;(c) the name of the States which are spending the allocated funds properly;(d) whether the said scheme is being implemented in North-East States including Assam on a large scale; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) Ayushman Bharat-Health and Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs) is being implemented in all States/ UTs except UT of Delhi. On the basis of the proposals received from States/ UTs, approvals for 52,744 AB-HWCs have been accorded so far. Out of these, 19,567 AB-HWCs are operational as on 5th July, 2019 as reported by the States/UTs. The details are at  Annexure- I.The present status of implementation of Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is at Annexure-II.(b) State-wise allocation of funds for AB-H&WCs under NHM for the F.Y. 2019-20 is at annexure III.  In Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY), the funds are allocated on PAN India basis and accordingly, Rs.6400 crore have been have been allocated at the Budget Estimate stage. The funds are released to the State/UT based on their receipt of proposal after due compliance and furnishing of requisite information. No adverse information has been received in this regard.(d)  Yes.(e) The present status of implementation of AB-PMJAY including North-East States and Assam is at Annexure-II.
