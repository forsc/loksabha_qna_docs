                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Textile Park




T. R. Paarivendhar













                                                    Bache Gowda B.N. 
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the fact that the then Tamil Nadu Chief Minister Late Smt. Jayalalitha has announced that a textile park is to be set up in Perambalur, but the promise has not been fulfilled till now;(b)	whether the Government will take necessary efforts to set up or extend financial assistance to the Government of Tamil Nadu to set up the said textile park in Perambalur;(c)	whether the Government plans to set up mega textile parks in the country; and(d)	if so, the details of the places/States where mega textile parks are proposed to be set up in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	The Government is implementing the Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP) which provides support for creation of world-class infrastructure facilities for setting up of textile units, with a Government of India grant up to 40% of the project cost with ceiling limit of Rs. 40.00 crores for each  park. The Scheme is a demand driven scheme. No proposal is pending for setting up of Textile Park in Perambalur, Tamil Nadu in the Ministry of Textiles, Government of India. (c) & (d):	The concept of Mega Textile Parks is at a stage of discussion.****
