Roads Constructed under PMGSY




Santokh Singh Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of roads constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana(PMGSY) in Jalandhar constituency, Punjab during the last three years, year-wise;(b)	the details of ongoing works under PMGSY in Jalandhar;(c)	whether the Government has received any new proposals for construction of roads in the said region; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government in this regard?
