                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Meat Processing Industries




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of meat processing industries in the country in public as well as in private sector, State-wise; (b)	whether the Government has any scheme to promote meat processing industries and meat exports; and (c)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	As per the latest Annual Survey of Industries 2016-17, there are 183 registered units for Processing and Preserving of Meat. State wise number of registered units is at Annexure. (b) & (c): The Ministry of Food Processing Industries is implementing a Central Sector Scheme of Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada  Yojana (PMKSY) for providing financial assistance to set up Units for processing of agricultural produce including meat.   Under CEFPPC, credit linked capital subsidy in the form of grants-in-aid of 35% of the eligible project cost in general areas and 50% for the North East States including Sikkim and difficult areas namely Himalayan States (i.e. Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir and Uttarakhand), State notified ITDP areas & Islands subject to max. of Rs. 5.00 crore per project is provided. The Department of Commerce, with the mandate to promote export, at present, does not have any specific scheme to promote meat export. *****
