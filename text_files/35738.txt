                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Sports and Youth Welfare Activities




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of the proposals received regarding sports and youth welfare activities from the State of Chhattisgarh during the last three years; (b) whether the proposal for starting a national centre of excellence in district of Rajanandgaon is pending with the Ministry; (c) if so, the details thereof and the action taken in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) :	The Union Government keeps receiving proposals from various State/Union Territory governments from time to time. Proposals are sanctioned according to the parameters of the respective Schemes subject to the completeness of the proposals, technical feasibility and availability of funds under the Schemes.This Ministry has received 21 Proposals for opening of Khelo India Centres (KICs) and 02 Proposals for opening of Khelo India State Centre for Excellence (KISCEs) from the State of Chhattisgarh under the vertical of Khelo IndiaScheme namely, the State level Khelo India Centres. Out of these proposals, 07 proposals of KICs and 1 KISCEs were accepted, given at Annexure.(b) and (c): No such proposal has been received from the State Government of Chhattisgarh for starting a National Centre of Excellence in the district of Rajanandgaon.However, a reference from Hon’ble Member of Parliament (LokSabha) has been received for      up-gradation of existing SAI Training Centre as National Centre of Excellence (NCOE) in the disciplines of Basketball & Hockey. The proposal is at planning stage for feasibility study.*****
