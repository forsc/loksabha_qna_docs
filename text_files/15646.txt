                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4368






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Road Projects and Schemes in Kerala




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the road projects and schemes related to Kerala pending with the Union Government during the last three years;(b) 	whether there is any delay in the execution of the said projects and schemes, if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the initiatives taken to expedite clearance/execution of such projects and schemes during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)    No road project/scheme on National Highways entrusted to State PWD is pending with the Union Government.  As regards National Highways entrusted to NHAI, the list of projects in Detailed Project Report (DPR) stage in enclosed at Annexure-I. The projects will be taken up after acquisition of land depending on inter-se priority of works and availability of funds.
