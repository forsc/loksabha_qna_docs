                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3790TO BE ANSWERED ON 21.12.2021SKILL DEVELOPMENT OF SCs 3790. SHRIMATI SARMISTHA SETHI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the schemes including skill development schemes being run by the Government for the welfare of Scheduled Castes (SCs) in the country, State-wise; (b) the funds sanctioned, utilised and the achievements made during the last three years, Statewise; (c) whether any target has been fixed in this regard; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the achievements made, scheme-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a) to (d):    The following schemes including skill development schemes being run by the Department of Social Justice & Empowerment for the welfare of Scheduled Castes (SCs):i.	Centrally Sponsored Scheme for implementation of the Protection of Civil Rights Act,  1955   and the Scheduled Castes  and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989ii.	Special Central Assistance to Scheduled Castes Sub Plan (SCA to SCSP)iii.	Capacity of hostels sanctioned under Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojana (BJRCY)iv.	National Overseas Scholarship (NOS)v.	National Fellowship Scholarshipvi.	Pre-Matric Scholarship to the children of those engaged in occupations involving cleaning and prone to health hazards.  vii.	Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC Students (PMS-SC)  viii.	Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC students studying in Classes IX and X      ix.	Self Employment Scheme for Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers (SRMS)   x.	Top Class Scheme for SC Students xi.	 Free Coaching Scheme for SC Studentsxii.	Scheme for Residential Education for Students in High Schools in Targeted Areas (SHRESHTA) (Earlier known as Grant-in-Aid to Voluntary and other organizations working for Scheduled Castes).    xiii.
