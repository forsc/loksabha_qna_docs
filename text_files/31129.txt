                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2977






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






BharatNet Project in West Bengal




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the number of Gram Panchayats of West Bengal including that of Purulia Parliamentary Constituency benefitting from high speed optical fiber cable network and optical mix of media (i.e. optical fiber cables/radios/satellites) facilities at present?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)BharatNet project is implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country through an optimal mix of media (Optical Fibre Cable/Radio/Satellite). As on 30.11.2021, 2,298 GPs are Service Ready in West Bengal, including 154 GPs of Purulia Parliamentary Constituency. The infrastructure created under BharatNet project is a national asset, accessible on a non-discriminatory basis to the Service Providers, and the same can be utilized to provide internet services to the citizens.  *********
