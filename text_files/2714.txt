                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2477






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Allotment of Gas Agencies




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and number of gas agencies allotted and the block headquarters that have been allocated during the last three years and the current year, year-wise; and(b) the action taken by the Government for efficient gas supply specially to the rural customers?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) As on 01.04.2016, there were 17,916 LPG distributorships in the country which has increased to 23,776, as on 01.06.2019, out which 15,871 distributors are primarily serving the rural customers. For ensuring efficient gas supply especially to the rural customers, Oil Marketing Companies advertised 6,347 new LPG distributorships under Unified Guidelines for Selection of LPG distributorships. Out of this, 3,687 distributorships have been commissioned already, which are mostly in rural areas.----------
