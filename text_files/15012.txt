                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3758






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Organic Urea




Sanganna Amarappa Karadi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning for the use of organic urea in the fields by the farmers to make their crops more productive and healthy; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government is also planning to open some new organic urea stores in the villages for easy access for the farmers; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Urea is classified as Straight Nitrogenous Chemical Fertilisers in Fertilizer of Control Order (FCO), 1985 that is not allowed for organic farming as per standards prescribed under National Programme for organic production (NPOP) and Participatory Guarantee System – India (PGS-India) programme of organic certification.	City Compost, vermicompost, phosphate rich Organic Manure, organic manure, bio – enriched organic manure etc. are specified as organic manures under FCO. The production and use of organic manures along with biofertilisers has been promoted under various schemes such as Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), Mission Organic Value Chain Development in North East Region (MOVCDNER), National Food Security Mission (NFSM), and Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY).(c) & (d):	Does not arise.	*******
