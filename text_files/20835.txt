                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

192






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Refinery Project in Cauvery Basin




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Chennai Petroleum CorporationLimited or any other company hasanyproject on handor plans to develop a new refinery project in CauveryBasinRefinery,Nagapattinam;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the details ofNational Iranian OilCompanyparticipation in suchprojects;(c) whether it would be in Public Sector, PrivateSector or Public-PrivatePartnershipand also the detailsof stakeholders and pattern of funding in this regard;and(d) whether the Government has approved or hasany plans to approve the mergerofthe ChennaiPetroleum Corporation Limited with the Indian OilCorporationLimitedand if so, the details of proposedshare swap ratio of the National Iranian OilCompany, ifany?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRIDHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.--------------STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (d) OF LOKSABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.192 BY SHRI THIRUMAAVALAVANTHOLTO BE ANSWERED ON 08thMARCH, 2021 REGARDING REFINERYPROJECT IN CAUVERY BASIN(a) to (c) :Chennai Petroleum Corporation Limited (CPCL) is setting up a 9 MillionMetric Tonne per Annum (MMTPA) capacity refinery at Nagapattinam, TamilNadu.The proposed 9 MMTPA refinery project at Nagapattinam is planned on a JointVenture basis between Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) and CPCL togetherholding 50% stake(each holding 25%) and the balance 50% to be held by financial /strategic / public investors. However, no participation of National Iranian OilCompany in this project is envisaged.(d) At present, there is no proposal of merger of CPCLwith IOCL.***********
