                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

271






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Targets for Construction of Houses under PMAY-U




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of houses targeted to be constructed 	under Pradhan Mantri AwasYojana - Urban (PMAY-U) 	under ‘Housing for all’ by the year 2022; (b)	the number of houses constructed so far and the number 	of houses that are yet to be constructed as per the target 	in Karnataka State; (c)	the details of financial assistance provided under PMAY-	U at present; and (d)	whether the Government proposes to increase the same and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                                     THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):	Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) since 25.06.2015 for giving Central assistance to States/Union Territories (UTs) for providing all weather pucca houses to all eligible urban beneficiaries. Government of India has not fixed any target for construction of houses in a State/ UT under PMAY-U. States/Uts have undertaken a demand survey under the scheme for assessing actual demand of houses.	Against validated demand of 112.24 lakh houses, 1,14,02,341 houses have been sanctioned so far under PMAY-U. Of the sanctioned houses, 92,37,907 houses have been grounded for construction of which 54,39,898 houses have been completed/delivered. All States/UTs have been advised to complete all the sanctioned projects/ houses within the stipulated timeline. (b):	Under PMAY-U, 6,93,531 houses have been sanctioned in the State of Karnatakaso far. Of the sanctioned houses, 5,47,375 houses have been grounded for construction; of which 2,64,926 houseshave been completed/delivered and remaining 1,46,156 houses are yet to be grounded.  (c) & (d):Central assistance provided under different verticals of PMAY-U is as under: S. No.	Vertical	Central Assistance to each beneficiary1.	Beneficiary-led individual house construction/ enhancement (BLC)	?1.50 lakh2.	Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP)	?1.50 lakh3.	“In-situ” Slum Redevelopment (ISSR)	? 1.00 lakh4.	Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS)	Upto? 2.67 lakhNo proposal to increase admissible financial assistance is under consideration of the Government.                                   *****
