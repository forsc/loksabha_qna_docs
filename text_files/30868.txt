                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Preservation of Culture, Language and Script of Konds Tribes




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the Konds (also Khond, KondhorKondha) are the largest tribe in Odisha from the Kandhmal district;(b) whether the Government is aware that the people of this tribe speak Kui language which is a rare Dravidian language written in Oriya script; and(c) if so, the steps taken by the Government to preserve this primitive tribe, its culture, language and its script?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): Yes Sir. As informed by Government of Odisha, Konds is the largest tribe of Odisha distributed in various pockets of  southern Odisha including Kandhamal  district. This community speaks ‘Kui’ language in Kandhamal district and ‘Kuvi’ language in Rayagada district. Kuvi language belongs to Dravidian linguistic group. (c):  Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides grants to State Tribal Reasercah Institutes for preservation and promotion of tribal languages.   Further New Education Policy also stipulates that young children learn and grasp quickly in their home language and mother tongue. Accordingly, Central and State Government are making efforts to encourage multi-lingual policy for imparting education.The details of efforts made in preservation of language and culture are as under: A. Language Preservation (i) Prepared Bilingual Dictionary in “Kui” and  “Kuvi” languages.(ii) Prepared Trilingual Proficiency Module in “Kui”  and “Kuvi” languages. (iii) Prepared Supplementary Readers in “Kui” and  “Kuvi” language under Multi Lingual Education  (MLE) Intervention.(iv) Prepared Primers for the students of Class-I, II, & III in both “Kui” and “Kuvi” languages. (v) Capacity building training of SSD School Teachers has been undertaken in both “Kui” and “Kuvi” languages  B. Culture Preservation (vi) Rare art, craft and artefacts are collected and  showcased in Odisha State Tribal Museum  (OSTM) to preserve and propagate tribal culture of  this community. (vii) Various aspects of culture have also been documented (Audio Video Documentation) and showcased in Odisha State Tribal Museum (viii) Published Photo Handbook and also Monograph (The Kondhs of Odisha) on Kandha (Khond, Kond) community.*****
