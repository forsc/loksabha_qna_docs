                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Ammonium Nitrate Reserves




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether in the wake of recent massive explosion of Ammonium Nitrate in Beirut, the Government has taken stocks of Ammonium Nitrate reserves in the country;(b)	if so, the details of the measures taken to safeguard the reserves;(c)	whether there were news reports that large quantity of Ammonium Nitrate has been transported from Tamil Nadu port to Hyderabad city recently;(d)	if so, whether it is safe to keep large quantity of reserves in densely populated cities; and (e)	the details of the protocol for the storage of Ammonium Nitrate reserves in major cities?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS (SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) Yes.  The stock of Ammonium Nitrate is monitored on daily basis through Ammonium Nitrate Return system. The total stock of Ammonium Nitrate as on 15/09/2020 lying in the storehouses and in the police custody is tabulated below:Sl.No.	Ammonium Nitrate Premises	Stock in MT1.		Ammonium Manufacturer & Converter	1597.262.		Stevedore, Bag and Store Ammonium Nitrate	4532.803.		Ammonium Nitrate possession for sale and use	46112.554.	Additional Quantity seized by Police throughout the country for destruction 149.34 Total		52391.95 Source: Petroleum Explosive Safety Organization (PESO)(b) The following measures have been taken:-i.	Advisory for Storage, Handling and Management of Ammonium Nitrate has been issued by PESO to all stake holders.ii.	All the port authorities /Customs Commissionerate are informed to take preventive action on stored or seized Ammonium Nitrate.iii.	The data for exhibits/seizures of Ammonium Nitrate made by police at all police stations throughout the country has been called for and safe destruction of the same has been planned.(c) Yes. Ammonium Nitrate (686.24 MT) was seized by Custom Authority at Chennai port. The above quantity of Ammonium Nitrate was auctioned and shifted safely to store house at Mechal-Malkajgiri District of Telangana for safe storage and use.(d) Ammonium Nitrate is safely stored in the specially licensed store houses maintaining required safety distances and conditions of licence granted under Ammonium Nitrate rules 2012. (e) Ammonium Nitrate storage houses for storage of Ammonium Nitrate are specially constructed as per the provisions of Ammonium Nitrate Rule 2012 and maintained safe distances for safety of Ammonium Nitrate.  All the conditions of the licenses are to be followed by the licensees. The details of the protocols to be followed for the storage of Ammonium Nitrate reserves are attached as Annexure 1.******
