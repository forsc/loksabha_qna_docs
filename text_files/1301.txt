                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1172






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Eco Task Force




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of deployment of eco-task force battalions which are presently engaged in protection of environment, State-wise; (b) 	whether the Government is working on any plan for better utilization of new technology so as to enhance the capacity as well as efficiency of the said battalions; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to raise more battalions in coordination with the State agencies in future; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE  IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO) (a)	At present, there are ten  Eco Task Force (ETF) Battalions, engaged in the work of environmental protection at different locations of the country out of which establishment cost of six ETF Battalions are being funded by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEF&CC).  The State-wise deployment of these Battalions is given in Annexure.(b)	& (c) ETF Battalions have adopted several effective measures to enhance the capacity and work quality of the ETF  by employing new technology likedripand sprinkler irrigation, root trainer technology, pro-tray technology, bio andvermin composting, mist chambers, green and poly houses etc. in their work.(d)	& (e) The Ministry of Defence considers the raising of ETF  Battalion if the concerned authority i.e. State Government or Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change etc. make firm commitment to bear the entire expenditure in respect of the proposed ETF  Battalion. The State Governments have been encouraged and advised to raise ETF Battalions from their own funding with technical support of the Forest Department as has been done by the States like Delhi, Himachal Pradeshand Maharashtra. ****
