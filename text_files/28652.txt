                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

832






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Smart City Projects in Madhya Pradesh




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the names of the ongoing housing projects in various States under Smart City Mission (SCM);(b) the names of the districts in Madhya Pradesh which are progressing fast under SCM;(c) whether any new projects are under consideration for tribal dominated urban areas;(d)   if so, the details thereof; and(e)   the details of the ongoing projects under SCM in Indore?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                 (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (e): The details of ongoing housing projects under Smart Cities Mission (SCM) in various States/Union Territories are given in Annexure-I. The details of progress of Smart City projects in the State of Madhya Pradesh including Indore are as follows: -                                                                   (Amount in ? crore)Smart City	Tender Stage	Work Order Stage	Work Completed	Total Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount		Bhopal	7	3,478.00	34	898.44	57	2,433.24	98	6,809.68Gwalior	18	800.15	30	1,052.10	27	492.27	75	2,344.52Indore	22	446.07	31	3,648.88	224	3,438.37	277	7,533.32Jabalpur	9	206.96	63	2,613.92	55	517.41	127	3,338.29Sagar	34	536.65	29	1,347.35	24	105.39	87	1,989.39Satna	10	248.13	29	520.96	24	346.51	63	1,115.60Ujjain	9	240.21	37	1,124.21	23	347.77	69	1,712.19Total	109	5,956.17	253	11,205.86	434	7,680.96	796	24,842.99(Source: SCM Geo-Spatial Management Information System)There is no proposal under consideration of the Government to include new projects under SCM for tribal dominated urban areas.                             *****
