                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2746






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Vacancies in Central and State Control Boards




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of vacancies existing in the Central and State Pollution Control Boards against the sanctioned manpower at both Central and State level; and(b) 	the steps being taken to address the skilled manpower shortage in Central Pollution Control Boards of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) A total of 79 positions are vacant in Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) in different cadres against the total sanctioned strength of 521.As per information received from State Pollution Control Boards(SPCBs)/Pollution Control Committees (PCCs) of a total sanctioned strength of 9440 in different cadres of all SPCBs/PCCs are 4163 posts are vacant. (b):Filling up of vacant positions in Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) is  an ongoing process. As and when the vacancy arises, due process is initiated  to fill up the vacancy. *****
