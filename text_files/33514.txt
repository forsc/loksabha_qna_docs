                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

936






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Computerisation of Primary Agricultural Credit Societies




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that Telangana has computerized Primary Agricultural Credit Societies;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether there are any plans by the Government to replicate the same in other States to strengthen credit societies, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ??????		(SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) and (b): Yes sir. National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has informed that Telangana has undertaken computerization of Primary Agricultural Credit Societies (PACS) by the Telangana State Cooperative Apex Bank Ltd. (TSCAB) in collaboration with NABARD.(c): Central Government is formulating a project for Computerisation of 63,000 functional PACS in all States/UTs in the country.*****
