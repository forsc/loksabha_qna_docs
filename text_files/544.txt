                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

501






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Cold Storage for Bananas




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	 whether  the  Government  has received  any  request  from  the   State Government  of  Tamil  Nadu  or  farmers union  to  set  up  a  Banana  cold  storage warehouse and Banana Research Centre in Thotiyam, Musiri, Kulithalai and Lalkudi in Tamil Nadu where banana is cultivated in large quantity;(b)	 if so, the details thereof and the time  by  which  the  Banana  cold  storage warehouse and Banana Research Centre in Thotiyam, Musiri, Kulithalai and Lalkudi would be set up;(c)	  if  not,  whether  the  Government would  consider  such  proposal  for implementation in future;(d)	 if so, the details thereof; and(e)	 the total number of Banana cold storage warehouses and Banana Research Centres functioning  in the country  as on date, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):	  No such request has been received by Union Government.National Research Centre for Banana is already functioning under ICAR at Tiruchirapalli, Tamil Nadu which is very near to Banana clusters like Thotiyam, Musiri, Kulithalai and Lalkudi and the centre provides support to the banana growers. Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare is implementing Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for development of Horticulture in the country, which includes assistance for development of Post Harvest Management (PHM) including establishment of cold storages, pack house, pre-cooling units, controlled atmosphere (CA) storage, reefer vans and setting up of ripening chambers etc. The component is demand/ entrepreneur-driven from among entrepreneurs, private companies, cooperatives, farmers groups etc through commercial ventures for which government assistance is available in the form of credit linked back ended subsidy. No cold storage specifically for storing Banana has been sanctioned under MIDH. However, State – wise details of cold storage sanctioned under MIDH in the country is at Annexure – I. State – wise details of Banana Research Centres functioning in the country is at Annexure – II.
