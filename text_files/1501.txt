                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1520






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Policy for Cultural Integration




Janardan Mishra














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether there is an urgent need of an effective policy on cultural integration of different cultures being followed by the country for healthy inter-mingling of their beliefs and rituals;b) 	if so, the reaction of the Government and the efforts made so far in this regard; andc) 	the future plans of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c) 	The objectives of the Ministry of Culture are to preserve, promote and disseminate all forms of art and culture, including integration of different cultures, through various schemes. ************************
