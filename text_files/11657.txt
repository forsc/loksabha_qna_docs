                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

618






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Amount due on Public Sector Companies




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether a huge amount of the Department of Telecommunications is due on Gujarat Narmada Valley Fertilizers, GAIL, Rail Tel, Power Grid and other public sector companies; (b) 	if so, the details of the said outstanding amount department and company-wise; and (c) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to recover the said outstanding amount?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Yes, Sir(b)	The details of the company-wise outstanding amount is enclosed as Annexure.(c)	Demand notices have been issued to the Licensees.
