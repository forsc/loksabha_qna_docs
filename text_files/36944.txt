                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4097






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Maintenance and Upkeep of Sun Temple




Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Uttar Pradesh for maintenance and upkeep of the Sun Temple located in Bhittar Gaon of Kanpur district and Kushmanda Devi Temple located at Ghatampur which are about 100 years old under the Temple Heritage Project;(b)	if so, the action taken for the development of these heritage sites;(c)	if not, whether the Government has mooted any action plan for beautification, upkeep and development of Temples on the sidelines of Amrit Mahotsava; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d)	Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) ensures the conservation and maintenance of 3693 monuments & sites declared to be of national imporatnce under “Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958”.  The Sun Temple located in Bhittar Gaon of Kanpur and Kushmanda Devi Temple located at Ghatampur are not under the protection of  ASI, nor any proposal has been received from the State Government of Uttar Pradesh.There are 743 centrally protected monuments and sites, including temples under the ASI in Uttar Pradesh.  Necessary conservation works are carried out by the ASI as per requirement and availability of funds. The details of allocation and expenditure for the last three years and the current financial year 2021-22 are given as under:                                  (Amount in Lakhs.)S. No.	Year	Allocation	Expenditure1.	2018-19   1597.00	1595.172.	2019-20   2235.00	2235.003.	2020-21   2226.00	2222.004.	2021-22	4175.00	  3907.00  (up to 09.03.2022)
