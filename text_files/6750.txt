                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

494






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Transport Facility to Nuclear plants




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government  formulates  any  scheme for means of transport to ensure easy access to nuclear plants;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to connect Rawatbhata atomic power station with four lane road or rail network;(d)	if so, the details  thereof  and  if not,  the  time by which  it  is likely to be considered; and(e)	whether the Government  proposes  to set  up  any  atomic  power station in Uttar Pradesh and if so, the details thereof along with the locations identified for this project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR.JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)&(b)The various aspects related to access to the site including roads for transport of over dimensional and heavy equipment and emergency planning are studied at the site selection stage itself. Requirements for their augmentation if any, are taken up with the national / state authorities.(c)	There is no proposal at present to connect Rawatbhata Atomic Power Station with four lane road or Railway Network.(d)	Does not arise in view of (c) above.(e)	There is presently no proposal under consideration of the Government for setting up a nuclear power station in Uttar Pradesh.	*****
