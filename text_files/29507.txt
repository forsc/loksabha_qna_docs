                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Skill Development in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the details of the number of Skill Development Centres operational in the country specially in Bihar; (b)       the details of the number of such Skill Development Centres operational in district Gopalganj of Bihar; (c)        the number of people who have been provided training by these Centres in district Gopalganj of Bihar and total amount spent for this purpose during last three years; (d)       the number of beneficiaries who have been benefited by Government for their startups in district Gopalganj of Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       Under Skill India, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing various programmes/ scheme for the skill development. The number of operational skill development centres across the country including in the State of Bihar under the programmes of MSDE is given below: Programme/Scheme	No. of operational centres across country	No. of operational centres in State of BiharEmpanelled Training Centres (TCs) under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (including operational Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK))	1,484	134Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) (including newly sanctioned)	307	21Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs)	14,716	1,331 (b)       The number of operational skill development centres in the Gopalganj district of Bihar under the programmes of MSDE is given below: Programme/Scheme	No. of operational centresEmpanelled TCs under PMKVY (including operational PMKK)	7Jan Shikshan Sansthan	1*Industrial Training Institutes 	21*newly sanctioned. (c)        Under PMKVY, 9,430 candidates have been trained/oriented in Gopalganj district of State of Bihar by 31 empanelled training centres (including PMKKs) during last three years (2018-21). Under PMKVY 3.0, there is no provision for district -wise allocation of funds. Considering per candidate average cost (Rs.15,193), the estimated expenditure for training of 9,430 candidates was approx. Rs. 14.33 crore.   Further, in the district of Gopalganj in Bihar, there are 21 ITIs and as on 01-12-2021, 1,825 candidates have been enrolled under Craftsman Training scheme (CTS) in the year 2020. Under JSS, no training took place in the Gopalganj district of Bihar as there is no JSS in the district.  (d)       In order to inculcate self employment skills in the candidates for entrepreneurship / micro entrepreneurship, apart from domain / core curriculum, training in English, Employability & Entrepreneurship (EEE) module (155 hours) is being imparted under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY). Also, Training Providers provide support to the candidates for entrepreneurship development through facilitating bank linkages along with providing guidance and mentoring support. Further, under demand driven PMKVY 3.0, the aspirations of the candidates have been given primacy which aims to support the self-employment as well. The scheme has benefitted youth across the country including State of Bihar. Under PMKVY, as on 21.11.2021, 62 self-employed candidates are from Gopalganj district of the State of Bihar.
