                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1557






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Upliftment of Tribal People




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to prepare any special action plan for the upliftment of tribal people in order to bring them and the tribal areas of the country into mainstream keeping in mind their education and welfare; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):	Major part of infrastructure development in tribal dominated areas and provision of basic amenities to tribal people in the country is carried out through various schemes / programmes of concerned Central Ministries and the State Governments. Central Ministries have been mandated to earmark 4.3% to 17.5% of their total Scheme allocation every year for projects and schemes for the welfare and development of tribal population of the country. Details of Tribal Sub Scheme (TSS) / Scheduled Tribe Component (STC)/ Tribal Sub Plan (TSP) funds allocated by Central Ministries / Departments, State Governments and Ministry of Tribal Affairs under various schemes catering to Education, Health, Agriculture, Livelihood, Housing, Drinking water & Sanitation, Employment Generation, Skill Development, Women and Child Development etc. during 2014-15 to 2018-19 are as below:(Rs. in Crore)TSP/ TSS / STC component	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Central Ministries / Departments 	16,111	15,628	19,873	25,999	31,803State Component	83,167 	96,369	1,11,435	1,39,290	1,52,494Funds under MoTA	3,850	4,550	4,799	5,293	6,000Total:	103,128	116,547	136,107	170,582	1,90,297******
