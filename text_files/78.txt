                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

96






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Funds under National AYUSH Mission




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the detail of the funds provided/ released for different activities under National AYUSH Mission during the last three years in the country, State-wise particularly Rajasthan; (b) the number of Integrated AYUSH hospitals proposed to be set up in Rajasthan along with the funds allocated by the Government in this regard; and (c) the district-wise number and details of villages selected as ‘AYUSH Gram’ in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):      The detail of the funds provided/released for different activities under National AYUSH Mission during the last three years in the country, State-wise including  Rajasthan is furnished at Annexure-I.(b):      Public Health is a State Subject and opening of Integrated AYUSH hospitals comes under the purview of the respective State/UT Government. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is provision of financial assistance for setting up of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals. The details of approved upto 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals along with the funds allocated to the State of Rajasthan is furnished at Annexure-II.(c):      Under NAM, there is provision of financial assistance for AYUSH Gram. For implementation of this activity, financial assistance of Rs.30.00 lakhs has been sanctioned to State Govt. of Rajasthan in the year 2016-17. The details of the villages in Rajasthan which have been selected under AYUSH Gram, as reported by the State Government is furnished at Annexure-III.
