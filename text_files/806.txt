                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Vision of 5 Trillion Dollar Economy




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether India is expected to become a 5 trillion dollar economy by 2025; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the steps being taken by the Governmentin this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	A Working Group under the chairmanship of Hon’ble  Minister of Commerce and Industry  has projected the Indian Economy to reach USD 5 trillion  by 2024-25.     The International Monetary Fund in the World Economic Outlook Report of October 2018 has also projected that India’s Gross Domestic Product will reach USD 4.6 trillion  in 2023.(b): 	The Working Group has set out a roadmap for the agriculture, manufacturing and services sectors  to attain the projected target. ******
