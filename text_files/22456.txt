                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3654






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Delay in Payment from MTNL to DDA




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there has been a delay in payments from the Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited (MTNL) to the Delhi Development Authority (DDA) in regards to sale of MTNL’s land assets; and(b)	 if so, the details thereof including the quantum of funds owed by MTNL to DDA and the reasons for the delay in payment to MTNL therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)         MTNL has not monetized any land assets through sale till date.(b)         Does not arise. *****
