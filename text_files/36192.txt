                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3579






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Possibilities of Renewable Energy Projects




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any survey has been conducted by the Government for exploration of possibilities of new and renewable energy projects in the country, State-wise including Madhya Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the renewable energy projects being implemented in Madhya Pradesh during the last three years and the current year; and(d) the details of budgetary provisions made in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) The state-wise details of renewable energy potential estimated for various renewable energy sources (including large hydro) in the country including the State of Madhya Pradesh are given in Annexure-I.(c) The State-wise and source-wise details of  renewable energy capacity addition  (including large hydro) in the country including the State of Madhya Pradesh  during the last three years & current year i.e. from 2018-19 to 2021-22  (upto February’2022) are given in  Annexure-II.(d) Most of the grid connected renewable energy projects in the country are being implemented by private sector developers selected through transparent competitive bidding without Central Financial Assistance.   The year-wise details of funds released by the Ministry for the implementation of renewable energy programmes/schemes in the country during last three years  and the current year i.e 2018-19 to 2021-22 (upto 14-3-2022) are given below:                                                                                        (Rs in crore)Year	Amount2018-19	4470.802019-20	3562.102020-21	3083.722021-22 (upto 14-3-2022)	3793.79
