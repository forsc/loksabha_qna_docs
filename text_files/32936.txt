                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Projects on Gomti River




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the proposed projects like building dams, recycling plants, development of ghats, etc. on Gomti river in Jaunpur district; (b) the time by which the Government intends to complete all the proposed projects on the Gomti river in Jaunpur district; and (c) the funds spent by the Government for the said projects?       






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) to (c): A project for pollution abatement of river Gomti has been taken under Namami Gange Programme. The project is for creation of Interception and Diversion (I&D) structures for the drains falling in the Gomti river and a Sewage Treatment Plant(STP) of 30 MLD in Jaunpur town.  The sanctioned cost of the project is Rs 206.05 crores including Operation and Maintenance (O&M) cost for 15 years, out of which, an expenditure of Rs. 92.04 crores has been made.Bajrang Ghat, Hanuman Ghat and River Front Development (RFD) works at Jaunpur are being developed on the Gomti river at a cost of Rs. 8.05 crores, out of which, an expenditure of Rs. 1.18 crores has been made.  The above projects are likely to be completed by June, 2022.  ****
