                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1672






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Homeless Population




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has data to show the number of people who are homeless in each State; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Odisha and data specific to Bhubaneswar; (c)	whether the Government has taken any measures to provide shelter to the homeless population; (d)	if so, the details thereof including the number of shelters present and the number of people who have been benefitted from such measures over the last three years; and(e)	the details/data for the above specific to Bhubaneswar and the State of Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) & (b):	Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is administering ‘Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH)'' Scheme under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), through respective States/Union Territories (UTs). SUH provides for conducting systematic third-party survey by urban local bodies to indentify the number of urban homeless. So far, 25 States/UTs have completed their survey and the details are at Annexure. Odisha has reported that 3,967 number of homeless persons have been identified in Bhubaneswar.(c) & (d):	It is the primary responsibility of Governments of the States/UTs to provide shelter for the urban homeless. However, in order to complement their efforts, MoHUA is administering ‘Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH)’, which focuses on providing permanent shelters equipped with basic facilities for the urban homeless. State/UT level Project Sanctioning Committee (PSC) is competent to approve proposals for establishment of shelters. So far, 1,580 shelters are operational in States/UTs with a total capacity of 86,192 persons under the Mission.(e):	In Odisha, 36 shelters are functional under Mission, with total capacity of 1,865 persons. In Bhubaneswar, 07 shelters are functional under Mission, with a capacity of 268 persons.	                                                 *****
