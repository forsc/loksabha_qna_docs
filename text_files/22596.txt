                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Suggestions to Make Air Travel Comfortable




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel













                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether suggestions are received through the website of the Ministry of Civil Aviation also with reference to the announcement made during the flight inviting suggestion to make the air travel more comfortable; and(b) the details of the number of suggestions received so far including the number of suggestions out of these which have been implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. The Air Sewa Portal operated by the Ministry of Civil Aviation (MoCA) is used for redressal of complaints of air passengers.  Complaints received through Air Sewa Portal are monitored by the Air Sewa Cell in the Ministry for their redressal in a time bound manner. Some of these complaints also contains suggestions. All such complaints and suggestions are shared with the concerned airline, airport or stakeholder. ;;During the period Jan-Dec 2020, 20,079 complaints/ suggestions were received of which 19,456 complaints/ suggestions have been addressed.
