                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1578






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Financial Assistance to Drought Affected States




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has noticed that several parts of the country are affected by severe drought; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard; (c) 	whether the Government is providing financial assistance to drought affected States; (d) 	if so, the details of such assistance given to various States during the last five years, State-wise and year-wise; (e) 	whether the Government has any plan to enhance the compensation/financial assistance to drought affected States; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	As per second India Meteorological Department (IMD) Long Range Forecast, released on 31.05.2019, rainfall over the country as a whole for the 2019 South-West Monsoon Season (June to September) is likely to be NORMAL (96% to 104% of the Long Period Average). No State has submitted any Memorandum seeking financial assistance from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) during Kharif 2019 season. In the eventuality of drought and other natural calamities, the State Governments are empowered to initiate necessary relief measures from State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) which is readily available with them. Additional financial assistance, over and above SDRF, is considered from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) on receipt of Memorandum from State Governments and in accordance with extant norms and procedures. Details of assistance sought by the States ad assistance approved by the High Level Committee in respect of drought during the last five years i.e, 2014-15 to 2018-19, are at Annexure –I.  (e) & (f):	Financial assistance for drought relief is provided on the basis of enhanced SDRF/NDRF norms issued by Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) on 08.04.2015. Currently, no such proposal is under consideration for changing the norms.
