                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2501






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Pilgrim Special Train




Rameshbhai Lavjibhai Dhaduk














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether daily trains have been provided for the pilgrim of Saurashtra area of Gujarat to travel  to Haridwar;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government is taking steps to provide new trains from various parts of the country to Porbandar; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b)   At present, 19565/66 Okha-Dehradun Express(Weekly)  is available between Okha & Haridwar.(c) and (d) Presently, Porbandar is  served by 06 pairs of trains which connect it inter-alia to stations like Mumbai, Delhi, Lucknow, Kolkata, Secunderabad, Madgaon, Thiruvananthapuram etc.  At present, there is no proposal  for introduction of new train service(s) from Porbandar. However, introduction of train services is an on-going process on Indian Railways, subject to operational feasibility, commercial justification, competing  demands  etc.*****
