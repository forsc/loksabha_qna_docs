                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3722






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Appointment of Notary Officers




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)	whether State-wise interviews were conducted for appointment of notary officers during the last year;					  (b)	if so, whether certificate of appointment has been issued to all the applicants for appointment as notary officers in Marathwada region of Maharashtra;		(c)	if so, the details thereof; and 		(d)	if not, the reasons therefor ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD) (a)	Yes, State-wise interviews for appointment of notaries were conducted during the last year.(b)	This department does not maintain any Region-wise data relating to appointment of notaries. The data maintained is State –wise.  (c)	758 number of certificates of practice for the State of Maharashtra have been issued so far.(d)	The remaining certificates of practice of notary for the State of Maharashtra will be issued in due course of time after completing the procedural requirements.
