                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

812






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Proposals from Maharashtra under PMAY




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of proposals under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) submitted by Maharashtra to the Union Government during the year 2019-20;(b) whether request has been made by the Government of Maharashtra to the Union Government regarding receiving of the funds under PMAY;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the amount  of funds demanded by the State Government; and(d) the amount provided/likely to be provided by the Union Government under the said scheme to Maharashtra State during the year 2019-20, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)to(d) Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)], during the year 2019-20, 217 projects involving 1,70,126 houses have been sanctioned for the Maharashtra. For projects approved during the year 2019-20, an amount of about Rs.2,551crore has been approved as Central Assistance, out of which about Rs.1,020 crore has become due for release as 1st installment subject to fulfillment of certain requirements(Annexure – I).Further, during the year 2019-20, under CLSS component of PMAY(U), 60,622houses involving Rs. 1,437.92 crore have been sanctioned/ delivered.                                                  *****
