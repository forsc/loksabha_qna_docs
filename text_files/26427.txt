UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2240






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Funds for Covid-19 Relief Operations




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Ministry has allocated funds to various State Governments for Covid-19 relief operations in 2020-21 and 2021-22; and(b)	If so, the details thereof during the said period, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a)	& (b): The Central Government provides funds for meeting the expenditure on relief provided to the affected people under the State Disaster Response Funds (SDRF) to the States for the notified disasters, in accordance with the approved items and norms. Keeping in view the spread of COVID-19 in the country, the Central Government vide letter dated 14th March 2020, by way of a special one-time dispensation, decided to treat COVID-19 as a notified disaster for the purpose of providing assistance under SDRF towards containment measures in the States. Consequently, Central Government by way of special onetime dispensation allowed the States to utilize up to 50% of their annual allocation of SDRF for containment measures of COVID-19 in conformity with approved items and norms for the FY 2020-21 and further extended to the financial year 2021-22. During the financial year 2020-21, an amount of Rs 22,262.43 crore was released to the States under the central share of SDRF. In addition, for the financial year 2021-22, first installment of Central share of SDRF amounting to Rs. 8873.60 crore has been released in advance to all States on 29th April, 2021. State wise details of funds released under SDRF is at Annexure-I.Moreover, during the financial year 2020-21, funds to the tune of Rs.8257.88 crore have been released by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare to the States/UTs towards the India COVID-19  Emergency  Response and  Health System  Preparedness  Package,  through  its                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Contd….2/--	2 -Agency National Health Mission (NHM). This amount of Rs.8257.88 crore includes Rs. 110.60 crore towards insurance of health workers. Details of which are given at Annexure-II.  This amount is in addition to Rs.1113.21 crore provided to the States in the year 2019-20.In addition, ‘India COVID-19 Emergency Response & Health System Preparedness Package: Phase-II’ has also been approved by the Cabinet for 2021-22 with Rs 23,123 crores (with Rs. 15,000 crore as Central Component & Rs 8,123 crore as State component).***** ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2240 FOR ANSWER ON 02.08.2021 (Annex I & II).Annexure-IDetails of Central Share of State Disaster Response Fund released to the States.S.No	State	Central share of SDRF (Rs in crore)		2020-21	2021-22 (First installment)1	Andhra Pradesh	1119.00	447.602	Arunachal Pradesh	250.00	100.003	Assam	772.00	308.804	Bihar	1416.00	566.405	Chhattisgarh	432.00	172.806	Goa	12.00	4.807	Gujarat	1324.00	529.608	Haryana	491.00	196.409	Himachal Pradesh	409.00	163.6010	Jharkhand	568.00	227.2011	Karnataka	791.00	316.4012	Kerala	314.00	125.6013	Madhya Pradesh	1820.00	728.0014	Maharashtra	3222.00	1288.8015	Manipur	42.00	16.8016	Meghalaya	66.00	26.4017	Mizoram	47.00	18.8018	Nagaland	41.00	16.4019	Odisha	1604.00	641.6020	Punjab	573.43	198.0021	Rajasthan	1481.00	592.4022	Sikkim	50.00	20.0023	Tamil Nadu	1020.00	408.0024	Telangana	449.00	179.6025	Tripura	68.00	27.2026	Uttar Pradesh	1933.00	773.2027	Uttarakhand	937.00	374.8028	West Bengal	1011.00	404.40 	Total	22262.43	8873.60 Annexure-IIState/UT wise details of Central Release of Grants in aid through Agency NHM for management and control COVID-19 during  financial year 2020-21 and  2021-22 			(Rs in Crore)S.
