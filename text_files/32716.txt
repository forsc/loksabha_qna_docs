                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Supply of Khadi Products by KVIC




C.N. Annadurai













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is an increase in the annual turnover of Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) during each of the last three years and current year and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether KVIC has received any orders from any State Governments for supply of Khadi products and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of the expenditure incurred by the Government for the development of KVIC during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise; (d) 	the number of Khadi Research and Development Centres functioning in the country; and (e) 	whether the Government proposes to issue fresh instructions to all PSUs/ Public Sector Banks, the State Governments and other the Government entities for use of Khadi items and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a): 	Yes Sir. Khadi and Village Industry Commission (KVIC), promotes Khadi and Village Industry (KVI) programmes through Khadi Institutions and entrepreneurs in the country. There is an increasing trend in production and sale of KVI products during the last three years and the current year (upto 31.12.2021) as may be seen from figures given below:                                                                                                                 (Rs. in crore)<pre>Particulars	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22 (as on 31.12.2021)Production value of KVI products	58130.34	67667.3172235.15	62504.29Sale value of KVI products	74292.09	88875.5495741.36	84405.16</pre>(b):	Yes Sir.  KVIC has supplied KVI products to various organizations of the State Governments through its Departmental Khadi Gramodyog Bhavans (KGBs). KGBs-wise value of supply of Khadi products to the State Government Organizations during current year is as follows:                                                                                  (Rs. in lakh)<pre>Sl.No.	Name of KGBs	2021-22 (as on 22.01.2022) 1	KGB, Kerala	0.152	KGB, Kolkata	11.883	KGB, Delhi	0.804	KGB, Mumbai	0.695	KGB, Goa	2.66</pre>(c):	State-wise disbursement of funds (Govt. of India budgetary support) under Khadi and Village Industries sector during last three years and current year are annexed.(d):	KVIC has no Departmental Khadi Research & Development (R&D) Centre anywhere in the country. However, KVIC implements its R&D activities through premier Research Institutions and Khadi Institutions across the country by extending financial assistance to carry out the R&D /Dissemination projects in the Khadi Sector. The Research Institutions that have undertaken projects under R&D Khadi during last five years are Indian Institute of Technology (IIT)-Delhi, Mahatma Gandhi Institute for Rural Industrialization (MGIRI)-Wardha, Vivekananda Institute of Bio Technology-West Bengal, four Khadi Institutions (1. Khadi Gramodyog Prayog Samiti-Ahmedabad, 2. Udyog Bharati Gondal-Ahmedabad, 3. Kshetriya Punjab Khadi Mandal-Mohali, 4. Khestriya Shree Gandhi Ashram-Akbarpur, Varanasi) and others.(e):	KVIC had been catering to the needs of Government Departments and bulk buyers such as Railways, Defence, Health & Family Welfare Departments, Paramilitary Forces and other Central Ministries & State Government Departments to increase the sale of Khadi products categorized under Rate Contract (RC) and Non-Rate Contract (Non-RC) by Directorate General of Supplies & Disposals (DGS&D) till 30.6.2017. With the introduction of Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal from 1.7.2018, KVIC has uploaded 26 varieties of Khadi items on the GeM portal.During 2020-21, the total supplies to various Government Departments /Agencies under RC and Non-RC items are as follows:                                                                                                                             (Rs. in lakhs)<pre>S.
