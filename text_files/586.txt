                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

440






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Funding for Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    ) the funds sanctioned for animal husbandry, dairying and fisheries during the last five years, the details thereof, State-wise and year-wise; (b) the details of expenditure of the allotted fund; and (c) the reasons for non-utilization of the allotted fund and whether any study has been conducted in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) to (c) The funds sanctioned, the details of expenditure of the allotted fund and non-utilised fund for animal husbandry, dairying and fisheries during  the last five years, State-wise and year-wise are given at Annexure-I to VIII. ***
