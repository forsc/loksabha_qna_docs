                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Adarsh Anganwadi Kendras




 Dharambir Singh













                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has taken note that many Anganwadi Kendras across the country lack basic facilities such as toilets, power and drinking water and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has proposed to develop Adarsh Anganwadi Kendra/modern Anganwadis across the country, if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Jalore Sirohi constituency;(c)	whether the Government has proposed to equip all Anganwadi Centres with solar panels across the country to provide 24x7 power supply and if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard; and(d)	whether the Government has taken note of Anganwadi food scams and if so, the details thereof along with the action taken in this regard, State/UT-wise including Haryana?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	State/UT-wise details of number of Anganwadi Centres with drinking water and toilet facilities is Annexed.(b)	& (c) The scheme provides for up-gradation and improvement of infrastructural facilities in AWC buildings @ Rs. 2.00 lakh per AWC building, maintenance @ Rs.3,000/- per AWC and furniture & equipment @ Rs.10,000/- per AWC and @ Rs.7,000/- per Mini-AWC once in  5 years. Apart from this, funds for construction of Toilets and for providing drinking water facilities in AWCs are also provided to States/UTs. In addition, funds @ Rs. 5,000/- per AWC per annum is provided to States/UTs for imparting non-formal pre-school education to the children in all AWCs. (d)	No instance of food scam has come to the notice of the Ministry.
