                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1867






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari













                                                    Gautam Gambhir
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the disbursement of collateral-free loan amount to MSMEs under the Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) between 15 May and 1 September, 2020, State-wise;(b) 	the share of public sector banks and private sector banks in the disbursement of loans to the number of MSMEs under the said scheme, State-wise during the same period;(c) 	the latest occupational sector-wise break-up of number of MSMEs by total disbursed amount under the said scheme, State-wise;(d)	the amount of lending by banks to MSMEs under the ECLGS between March and August 2020; and(e) 	whether the Government is considering enhancing credit guarantee schemes for MSMEs in lieu of property rates falling due to COVID-19 and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OFMICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a), (b) & (d):	    State-wise disbursement of collateral-free loan to MSMEs with the share of public and private sector banks under the Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) is at Annexure I.(c):	The latest occupational sector-wise break-up of loans disbursed to number of MSMEs as on 17.09.2020 is at Annexure II.(e):	There is no proposal for enhancing credit guarantee schemes in the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.
