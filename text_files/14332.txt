                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3093






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Road Projects in Chamarajanagar




V. Srinivas Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of poor condition of roads/highways in tribal areas of Chamarajanagar in Karnataka; and (b) 	if so, the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard and improve the road infrastructure in that area to help in improving tourism?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)   National Highway (NH)-209 (New No. 948) in Chamarajanagar District starts from Karnataka/Tamil Nadu Border at Km 265/800 and ends at Bangalore city at Km.458/420.  The Strengthening of the stretch from Km. 265/800 to Km. 287/500 has been approved during 2019-2020. The two lane with paved shoulders/ four laning of the stretch from Km.287/500 to Km.458/420 is in advance stage of completion and the road is in motorable condition. NH-150A passing through Chamarajanagar District is in traffic worthy condition. *****
