                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Action Plan for Sick PSUs




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps taken by the Government to address the sickness of Public Sector Enterprises/Units; and (b)	the details of the outcome thereof along with the action plan envisaged to improve the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR) (a) & (b): A statement is laid on the Table of the House. * * * Statement referred to in Lok Sabha Starred Question No.*284 for reply on 16.03.2021 on “Action Plan for Sick PSUs”----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------(a) & (b): Department of Public Enterprises (DPE), being a general policy making Department for CPSEs, has issued on 29th October, 2015 guidelines for “Streamlining the mechanism for revival and restructuring of sick/ incipient sick and weak Central Public Sector Enterprises: General principles and mechanism of restructuring". Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) function under the administrative control of concerned Ministries/Departments and all matters relating to revival/ restructuring of CPSEs are dealt by the concerned administrative Ministry/ Department on a case-to-case basis. In terms of these guidelines, the administrative ministries/ departments monitor the health of CPSEs and identify the sick/weak/incipient sick CPSEs as per the criteria laid down therein based on their performance and take redressal measures. The criteria laid down in the guidelines facilitate pre-emptive corrective actions to check onset of sickness and take timely appropriate steps to put the CPSEs back to good financial health. The concerned administrative Ministries/ Departments are also responsible to formulate revival/restructuring/closure plans for such CPSEs based on the principles outlined therein on a case-to-case basis in consultation with stakeholders and after obtaining the approval of the competent authority and implement the plan. ***
