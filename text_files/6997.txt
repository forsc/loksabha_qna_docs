                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

811






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Growth of International Passenger and Cargo Traffic




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of inbound and outbound international passengers during each of the last five years from metros and tier-two cities, airport-wise;(b) whether there is any increase in international passenger traffic in tier-two cities and if so, the details thereof along with the manner in which this increase can be compared with airports in metro cities;(c) the details of bilateral seat sharing agreements entered into with different countries and the dates on which these agreements were revised last;(d) whether there is any increase in the allocation of seats to metro and tier two cities and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the percentage of increase in cargo transportation, particularly perishable items from tier-two cities and metros and the manner in which transportation of cargo from tier-two cities can be compared with that of metros in the last five years, airport and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b) The details of inbound and outbound international passenger during each of the last five years from metros and tier-two cities, airport-wise alongwith the percentage increase in international passenger traffic from both categories of cities is attached as Annexure-I.;;(c) & (d) The details of bilateral seat sharing memoranda of understanding entered into with different countries alongwith increase in allocation of seats in the last five years is attached as Annexure-II.;;(e) The percentage of increase in cargo transportation from metros & tier-two cities airport-wise and year-wise is attached as Annexure-III.
