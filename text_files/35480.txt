                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2895






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Small Industrial Training Institute




Sukanta Majumdar














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing the scheme of Small Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) to promote technical education in the rural areas of the country;(b)	if so, State/UT-wise details thereof including West Bengal;(c)	the details about the ITIs established along with the fund spent in this regard during the last three years in West Bengal; and;(d)	the details of the total number ofstudents enrolled and trained under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) in the last four years, State wise including West Bengal;(e)	whether in comparison to other countries, India has very small percentage of skilled workforce and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and(f)	the proposed corrective steps to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) There is no scheme of small Industrial Training Institutes under Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) However, government has comprehensive network of more than 14000 Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) across India. Setting up of ITIs and its operation are in the domain of respective State Governments, whereas preparation of norms and designing curricula along with conducting examination and certification are the responsibility of Central Government. Industrial Training Institutes are governed under Craftsmen Training Scheme. The detail of ITIs functioning across the country in rural areas including West Bengal is given in Annexure – I(d) The total number of students enrolled and trained under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) in the last four years, state-wise including West Bengal are given at Annexure – II(e) & (f) Our country has a smaller percentage of skilled workforce as compared to other countries like United Kingdom, Germany, United State of America, Japan and South Korea. The main reason is the small workforce available with formal skill training, and availability of skilled or un skilled work force largely through non-formal channels.The following corrective steps have been taken:-•	Aligning courses as per international standards of National Skill Qualification Frame Work (NSQF).•	Mapping of existing skills, understanding skills gap, imparting necessary remedial measures by re-skilling and up-skilling.•	Imparting training in industry relevant employable skills under various schemes.•	Coverage of new age courses like Internet of Things, Drone Pilot, Geo-Informatic Assistant, to keep pace with changing technology and global demand.Annexure – IAnnexure referred to in reply to para (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question Number 2895 to be Answered on 21-03-2022.
