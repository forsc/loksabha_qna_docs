                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Compensation to Textile Workers




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government maintains a centralised data of Textile Workers who work with raw cotton, who have been diagnosed with Byssinosis; (b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government proposes to introduce compensation for textile workers working with raw cotton, who get diagnosed with Byssinosis, including insurance and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (d) whether the Government proposes to make it mandatory to sensitise employees of potential health hazards similar to byssinosis before engaging them in employment and if so, the details thereof; (e) whether the Government proposes to formulate guidelines for textile factories to ensure effective prevention of health hazards like byssinosis and other lung conditions; and (f) if so, the details thereof including the time by when the same would be completed?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	No such data has been maintained.(c) to (f):	There is no such proposal. ***
