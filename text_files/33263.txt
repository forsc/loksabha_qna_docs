                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

890






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Promotion of Education and Research




Ramesh Bidhuri














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Government has taken any steps for promotion of higher education and research study in rural areas and small cities of the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of new Universities/institutes/research centres opened in small cities during the last three years, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (c): The Ministry of Education (MoE) and the University Grants Commission (UGC) have taken various steps to promote inclusive reach of Higher Education and Research Studies in Rural areas which includes (i) Establishment of Educational Institutions in rural areas (ii) Enabling digital reach of higher education in rural areas and small cities. During the last three years, 141 universities have been established as per following details:<pre>Year	State University	Private University	Deemed to be University	Central University	Total University2018-19	17	40	3	1	612019-20	14	18	1	0	332020-21	18	24	1	4	47Total	49	82	5	5	141</pre>State/ UT-wise list of Universities along with their year of establishment is available at https://www.ugc.ac.in/oldpdf/Consolidated%20list%20of%20All% 20Universities.pdf.Further, 02 Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs) have been established, one each at Agartala, Tripura in year 2018-19 and Raichur, Karnataka in year 2019-20.*****
