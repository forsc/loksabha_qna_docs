                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3541






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Assistance to States for Agriculture Sector




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has provided suitable assistance to the State Governments to improve basic facilities in the agriculture sector; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has made an assessment of the impact of the schemes with regard to the income of farmers since 2014; and (d) ) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFAR E???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (b) Major schemes being implemented by the Government to improve basic facilities/infrastructure in agriculture include Per Drop More Drop component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PDMC-PMKSY) for the development and promotion of micro irrigation facilities, Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) for strengthening pre and post harvest infrastructure, Integrated Scheme on Agricultural Marketing (ISAM) for creation of marketing infrastructure, Sub Mission on Seed and Planting Material (SMSP) for Strengthening and modernizing infrastructure for seed production, storage, certification and quality control, Sub Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) for the promotion of farm mechanization etc. Government also set up an Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF) of Rs 1 lakh crore in 2020 for the creation of infrastructure at the farm gate. Other schemes such as National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) etc support production related aspects for the benefit of farmers.(c) to (d)  The last Situation Assessment Survey was conducted by NSSO in 2012-13 However in 2016, Government constituted an Inter-ministerial Committee to examine issues relating to “Doubling of Farmers Income” and recommend strategies to achieve the same. *****
