                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2615






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Settlement of Agricultural Insurance Claims




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the rate of settlement of agriculture insurance claims is very low in some States; (b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Bihar; and (c) the details of the insurance claim amount paid to the farmers for the crop damage for the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c) :  Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is an yield index based scheme and  mainly implemented on area approach basis.  Claims are worked out on the basis of shortfall in actual yield viz-a-vis the threshold yield in the notified area as per given formula in the scheme.   The quantum of claims under the scheme depends upon the happening of natural calamity/yield of the crop and may differ from crop to crop and area to area.	The scheme is voluntary for the States and the Bihar State Government is not implementing the PMFBY since 2018-19.	State-wise details of the insurance claim amount paid to the farmers during the last three years i.e. 2018-19 to 2020-21 under PMFBY are Annexed.
