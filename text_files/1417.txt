                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1185






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Funds to Telangana in Health Sector




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state the details of funds allocated and released in health sector, State/ UT-wise including Telangana?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY) Funds are released by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare under the various Centrally Sponsored Schemes for the Health Sector.  The details of the releases made to                 States /UTs including Telangana are as under: 1.	National Health Mission (NHM) at Annexure-1. 2.	Ayushman Bharat –Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) at Annexure-2. 3.	Health and Medical Education at Annexure- 3 to7.
