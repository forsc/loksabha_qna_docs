                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1801






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Construction of Houses under Urban Housing Schemes




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the number of houses proposed to be constructed under Urban Housing Scheme, State-wise;(b)  the details of the houses constructed under Urban Housing Schemeso far, State-wise;(c)   the number of houses sanctioned to be constructed under Urban Housing Scheme in the country specially in the State of Uttar Pradesh;(d)   whether the Government proposes to formulate any action plan to facilitate availability of interest free debt to meet the increasing requirement of housing in the urban areas in Uttar Pradesh; and(e)  if so, the timeline by which it shall be done and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




(a) to (c):	Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] since 25.06.2015 for providing assistance to States/Union Territories (UTs), including the state of Uttar Pradesh, for addressing the housing requirement in urban areas. States/Union Territories (UTs) have undertaken demand survey under the Scheme for assessing actual demand of housing.  The validated demand reported by States/UTs so far is around 112 lakhs.Based on the project proposals received so far from States/UTs, total 93,00,949 houses have been sanctioned under the scheme; out of this 55,40,801 are at various stages of construction and 28,06,465 are completed/delivered. State/UT-wise details are at Annexure.A total of 14,51,384 houses have been sanctioned for the State of Uttar Pradesh; out of this 8,70,251 are at various stages of construction and 3,50,810 have been completed/delivered. (d):  No, Sir.(e):  Does not arise.                                 *****
