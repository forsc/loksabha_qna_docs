                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3457






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Recruitment in IITs




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of IITs which have issued notifications for faculty recruitment in 2020 and 2021;(b) the details of posts for which recruitment notifications have been issued, IIT- wise;(c) whether the notifications contain details of number of vacancies reserved;(d) if so, the details thereof, IIT-wise; and(e) the details of posts which have backlog and recruitment did not take place, IIT-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (e) : IITs are autonomous institutes governed by the Institutes of Technology Act, 1961 as amended from time to time and the Statutes framed there under. Filling up of faculty vacancies is a continuous process and IITs issue rolling Advertisement, which is open throughout the year for all candidates, who fulfill the required qualifications and experience for faculty positions in IITs. As IITs follow flexi cadre structure of 10:1 student to faculty ratio, the vacancies vary from time to time. IITs have also launched special recruitment drive for reserved categories in addition to existing rolling advertisement, as per the recent directives of this Ministry.*****
