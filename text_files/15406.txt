                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4098






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Centre of Excellence




Sanganna Amarappa Karadi














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)  whether the Government is planning to set up a Centre of Excellence in the rural areas and aspirational districts to educate the people, especially in Koppal district of Karnataka;(b)  if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be set up; and(c)  whether the Government proposes to provide any financial help to individuals willing to open such centre of excellence and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):  Currently, there is no proposal under consideration of Government of India to set up Centre of Excellence in the rural areas and aspirational districts to educate the people. (b) and (c):   Do not arise.*******
