                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4078






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Upgradation of Highways




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present ranking of Uttar Pradesh in the country with regard to the National Highways (NHs);(b)	the length of NHs upgraded in the State during the last three years and the current year;(c)	whether the Government proposes to upgrade additional roads/ highways which are connected to NHs in the State; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The length of National Highways (NHs) in the State of Uttar Pradesh is 11,737 km, which is second largest in the country.  (b)	The year-wise details of NHs length upgraded in Uttar Pradesh are as under:S. No.	Year	Length (km)1.	2016-17	5842.	2017-18	6943.	2018-19	8824.	2019-20 (till October, 2019)	415 (c) & (d) This Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways in the country. The Ministry also sanctioned works for development/improvement of State roads under Central Road Fund (CRF) amounting to Rs. 15,439 crore for the State of Uttar Pradesh. The up gradation/development of other roads/highways in the State is the responsibility of concerned State. *****
