                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1410






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Food Park in Tamil Nadu




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has any proposal to establish a food park in Perambalur in Tamil Nadu for the improvement of job opportunities for unemployed youths in Perambalur and its surrounding areas;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, whether such proposal will be considered for implementation in future; and(d)	the total number of new food parks that have been established in Tamil Nadu in the last three years, location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): Ministry of Food Processing Industries has been implementing Mega Food Park Scheme (MFPS) to create modern infrastructure for the food processing and presently there is no proposal under consideration with the Ministry for establishment of Mega Food Park in Perambalur in Tamil Nadu.(c): 	The proposals under scheme for Mega Food Park for setting up of Mega Food Parks in the country are invited through Expression of Interest (EoI) from time to time. The willing entrepreneurs in the country including Tamil Nadu may apply for setting up of Mega Food Parks as per provisions of the guidelines of scheme for Mega Food Park as and when EoI is invited by the Ministry. Selection of suitable proposals for these projects is done as per the Scheme guidelines, based on the merit, through a stringent appraisal process as per the prefixed criteria.(d):	Ministry has not accorded final approval to any project for setting up of Mega Food Park in the state of Tamil Nadu during last three years.*****
