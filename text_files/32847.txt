                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Unspent Funds under Various Schemes




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether it is a fact that some crores of funds were lying unspent with the State Governments under various schemes of the Government upto December, 2022;(b) if so, the response and reaction of the Union Government thereto;(c) the details of the funds allocated for various urban centric infrastructure and other development schemes during the last three years; and(d) the details of the steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to strengthen the monitoring mechanism of the ongoing schemes both at the Central and the State levels by emphasizing proper utilization of allocated funds appropriately in a time bound manner?






ANSWER




                  THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)	(a) to (d)	Urban Development is a State subject. Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs assists the States / Union Territories (UTs) in urban development through its Missions- Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) 2.0, Smart Cities Mission (SCM), Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) 2.0, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) and also addresses livelihood concerns under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Livelihoods 	Mission (DAY-NULM) and PM Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi).  The funds are allocated and released to the States/UTs in accordance with the guidelines of these Missions/Schemes. The status of Central Assistance (CA) allocated and utilized is given in the Annexure.   Monitoring of utilization of funds allocated to the States / UTs under the Missions / Schemes by the respective Divisions is a continuous process and is carried out through institutional frameworks that consist of progress reports, utilisation certificates, field visits of senior officers, review meetings in States / UTs and review through video conferences. It is also done through geo-tagged Information Technology based Management Information System (MIS).                                *****
