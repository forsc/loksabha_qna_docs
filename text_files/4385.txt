                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3993






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Speed Post Centres




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of speed post centres operating in the country, States/UT-wise including Maharashtra; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to set up more such centres across the country including Maharashtra; (c) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise along with the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) 	The Department of Posts, under a new operational arrangement has changed the nomenclature of Speed Post Centres in to National Sorting Hubs (NSHs) and Intra Circle Hubs (ICHs).  As on date there are 91 NSHs and 149 ICHs, which are mapped to NSHs.  The State-wise details are at Annexure-I(b) to (d) 	The NSHs/ICHs are upgraded/downgraded/closed and established based on mail volume, geographical locations, operational feasibility etc.  At present, the Department has received a proposal from Maharashtra Circle for creation of an ICH at Latur, examination of which has been initiated.
