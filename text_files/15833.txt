                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4483






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Ayurveda System of Medicine




Ravindra Kushawaha














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government provides assistance to various institutions for research and processing in Ayurveda system of medicine; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the funds allocated to this sector during the last three years, State/UT-wise; and (d) the details of the progress made by AIIMS set up in Rishikesh to carry out research in Ayurveda?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a)	to (c): Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) is an autonomous body under the Ministry of AYUSH having 31 research centres across the country for undertaking research activities in Ayurveda. The Council is 100% financed by the Ministry. Ministry of AYUSH also runs a Central Sector Scheme of Extra Mural Research (EMR) since 11th Five Year Plan to support Research & Development projects undertaken by scientific and medical institutions. Funds for research in Ayurveda are  provided directly to CCRAS and to individual project institutions. Allocation of funds during last three years to CCRAS and EMR Scheme for research in Ayurveda is as under:	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20CCRAS	Rs. 188.09 crore	Rs. 270. 66 crore	Rs. 257. 57 croreEMR Scheme	Rs. 2.49 crore	Rs. 1.44 crore	Rs. 1.26 crore(d):     There has been no funding for research to AIIMS, Rishikesh from Ministry of AYUSH.
