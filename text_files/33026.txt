                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

589






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Details of AYUSH Hospitals and Dispensaries in Gujarat




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of AYUSH Hospitals and Dispensaries operating across the country, State/UT-wise including district-wise in Gujarat;(b) whether the Government has taken any steps for standardization of Ayurvedic, ethnic and traditional medicinal herbs;(c)  if so, the details thereof; and(d)  the time likely to be taken to implement the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a): The details of Ayush Hospitals and Dispensaries operating across the country, State/UT-wise including district-wise in Gujarat as per the Annual Statistical Publication “AYUSH IN INDIA-2020” of the Ministry of Ayush is furnished at Annexure-I.(b), (c) & (d): Yes, Sir. Pharmacopoeia Commission for Indian Medicine & Homoeopathy (PCIM&H), on behalf of Ministry of Ayush lays down Pharmacopoeial Standards and Formulary Specifications for Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani & Homoeopathy (ASU&H) drugs which serve as official compendia for ascertaining the identity, purity and strength of the drugs included therein. List of publications published till date is furnished at Annexure – II. 	Further, being an appellate laboratory for testing of ASU&H drugs; samples received from Government agencies especially from the Hon’ble court etc. are being tested for ensuring compliance to the prescribed standards. *****
