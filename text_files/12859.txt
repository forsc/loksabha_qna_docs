                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1784






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Pioneer Scheme




Indra Hang Subba














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Maker Village is planning to associate with All India Council forTechnical Education (AICTE) by launching pioneering schemes to promoteentrepreneurship and innovation;(b) whether the AICTE maintains a list of unapproved institutes based on theinformation received from various sources;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the list includes (264) technical and (116) architecture institutes; and(e) if so, the steps being taken by the Government to control such construction offake institutions?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Yes, Sir. The “Maker Village” of Kerala had approached the All India Council forTechnical Education (AICTE) for collaboration.(b) to (d): The AICTE maintains a state-wise list of (264) unapproved technical instituteson its website. The same may be seen at AICTE website https://facilities.aicteindia.org/dashboard/pages/angulardashboard.php#!/unapproved . The list does notcontain architectural institutes.(e): To curb the menace of unapproved institutions, AICTE issues Public Notices andindividual letters to such institutes to seek AICTE approval for conducting technicaleducation programmes or to close down. The concerned State Governments are alsoadvised to take appropriate action against such institutes. In case, any unapprovedinstitute applies to AICTE for approval, it is considered as a new technical institute andthe application is processed as per relevant provisions of AICTE Approval ProcessHandbook.*****
