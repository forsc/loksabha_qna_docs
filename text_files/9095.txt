                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2542






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Aluminium Park in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the proposal to set up an Aluminium Park in Odisha has been pending since 2014-15 financial year; and(b)	if so, the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to implement the said proposal ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)	& (b): A Joint Venture Company (JVC), Angul Aluminium Park Private Limited (AAPPL) has been formed on 30/07/2010 as special purpose vehicle by NALCO and Odisha Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation (IDCO), a Government of Odisha Undertaking, for development of Angul Aluminium Park at Angul. In the JV Company, IDCO is having 51% equity share and balance 49% is held by NALCO.*****
