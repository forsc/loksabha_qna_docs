                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

327






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Advanced Forensic DNA Lab




Dhanush M Kumar













                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has established a new Advanced Forensic DNA Analysis Lab under Nirbhaya Fund Scheme;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the fund allocation and its capacity of examining cases per year;(c) the nature of cases to be handled by this new Advanced Forensic DNA Analysis Lab;(d) whether the Government has made provisions for all States/UTs to utilize this Lab and if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government has taken steps to set up similar facilities in respective States for quick disposal of forensic cases and if so, the details thereof;(f) whether the Directorate of Forensic Science has set up Central Forensic Science Laboratories (CFSL) across the country; and(g) if so, the details thereof along with research and development activities undertaken by the CFSL during each of the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):  A State-of-the-art DNA Analysis Centre has been set up in Central Forensic Science Laboratory, Chandigarh under the Nirbhaya Fund scheme with an allocation of Rs.99.76 crores. This facility has an annual capacity to examine-2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.327 FOR 04.02.20202000 forensic cases relating to sexual assault, homicide, paternity, human identification and Mitochondrial DNA.  (d) 	Yes, sir.  Central Forensic Science Laboratories, including the new DNA Analysis Centre, have been mandated to receive cases/exhibits referred by Central Government Agencies, Higher/Lower Courts, Autonomous bodies, Public Sector Undertakings, Banks and from States/Union Territories. (e)  	Ministry of Home Affairs has approved setting-up and upgrading of DNA analysis and cyber forensic units in State Forensic Science Laboratories in 13 States/ Union Territories under the Nirbhaya Fund scheme.  Apart from this, 25 States have been given an allocation of Rs.108.40 crore for strengthening Forensic Science facilities, including DNA analysis facilities, as part of annual State Action plan under the scheme for Modernization of Police Forces in the year 2019-20.(f) and (g): The Directorate of Forensic Science Services has six Central Forensic Science Laboratories (CFSLs) situated at Bhopal (Madhya Pradesh), Chandigarh, Guwahati (Assam), Hyderabad (Telangana), Pune (Maharashtra) and Kolkata (West Bengal).
