                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1305






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Welfare Schemes for Weaker Sections




Riti Pathak













                                                    Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1305TO BE ANSWERED ON 27.07.2021WELFARE SCHEMES FOR WEAKER SECTIONS †1305. SHRIMATI RITI PATHAK: SHRIMATI POONAM MAHAJAN: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether any welfare schemes/projects are being implemented by the Government for upliftment of weaker sections of general category in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) the number of persons benefited from these schemes/projects during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) : There is no exclusive scheme for upliftment of weaker sections of general category.  However, the Government is implementing the following Central Sector schemes for upliftment  of Economically Backward Classes (EBCs):i.	Dr. Ambedkar Post-Matric Scholarship for EBC Students.ii.	Dr Ambedkar Central Sector Scheme of Interest Subsidy on Educational Loans for Overseas Studies for Other Backward Classes (OBCs) and Economically Backward Classes (EBCs) under SHREYAS Scheme.iii.	Assistance for Skill Development for OBC/DNT/EBC.(b) & (c):   Fund released and the number of persons benefited from the above schemes during the last three years is given in Annexure-I.*****                                             Annexure-I(Amount in Lakh)SL.
