                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Nirbhaya Funds




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of Nirbhaya Funds allocated and utilized since 2014, State-wise;(b) the quantum of Nirbhaya Funds allocated and utilised to implement the Safe City Component of the Nirbhaya Scheme since 2014, city-wise;(c) whether the Government is aware of irregularities in the tendering process to implement the Safe City component in Bengaluru; and(d) if so, the details thereof including the steps being taken by the Government to rectify the irregularities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a): 	 The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) is implementing several projects for safety of women under the Nirbhaya Fund scheme through the States/Union Territories. Total fund allocation approved and released to States/Union Territories, year-wise, by MHA under Nirbhaya Funds scheme, including Safe City projects, is at Annexure I.--2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 2328 FOR 09.03.2021(b): The details of fund allocation approved and released to States/UTs for Safe City projects in the last five years city-wise is separately given at Annexure II.(c):  The State Government of Karnataka has informed that there have been no irregularities in tendering process. (d) : Does not arise in view of reply to part (c) above. ******
