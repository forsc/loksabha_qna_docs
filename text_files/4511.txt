                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4208






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Extension of NH upto Tirupattur




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to extend the existing Tiruchi-Perambalur National Highway (NH) upto Tirupattur Brahma Temple in Tamil Nadu for the convenience of pilgrims;(b)	     if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c) 	if not, whether the Government is likely to consider the demands of the people of Tirupattur and pilgrims to extend the NH upto the said temple in near future; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)	Tirupattur Brahma temple is located at a short distance of 4.5 Km from Siruganur which is already on the 4 lane National Highway No. 38 between Perambalur and Trichy. There is no proposal at present to extend NH-38 up to Tirupattur Brahma Temple.   *****
