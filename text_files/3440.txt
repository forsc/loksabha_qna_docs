                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3037






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Integrated Rural Water Supply and Sanitation Project Proposals




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the proposalspertaining to Integrated Rural Water Supplyand Sanitation Project in the State ofMaharashtra as on today; and(b) the action taken or proposed to betaken by the Union Government in thisregard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	The State Government of Maharashtra has informed that the Water Supply and Sanitation Department of the State is implementing World Bank aided “Maharashtra Rural Water Supply and Sanitation Programme” (Loan No. 5357-N) commonly known as Jalswarajya–2 Programme. Under this Programme, no integrated rural water supply and sanitation projects are proposed.(b)  Does not arise.
