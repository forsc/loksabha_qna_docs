                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1745






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Mobile Towers in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to/has proposed to set up mobile towers in Odisha to minimize the problem of telecommunication connectivity; and (b) 	if so, the details and the number of mobile towers set up in the State, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Yes, Sir. The Government has proposed setting up of mobile towers in Odisha to minimize the problem of telecommunication connectivity.(b)	Under Left Wing Extremism (LWE) Phase-I Project of Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF), 256 mobile towers are providing services in 21 districts of Odisha State. District- wise details are given at Annexure-I.  	Under LWE Phase-II Project of USOF, 158 mobile towers are proposed for installation in 5 districts of Odisha State. District-wise details are given at Annexure-II. Moreover, 3933 villages under 7287 Aspirational District Scheme are proposed for installation of mobile towers in 10 districts of Odisha State. District-wise details are given at Annexure-III.
