                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

374






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Promotion of Fisheries




Uday Pratap Singh













                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                








                                                    Nayab Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING ??????????, ??????? ?? ????? ?????? be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has formulated/proposed to formulate any scheme to double the production of fish and income of fishermen, including the States of Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the details of the steps taken under Atamnirbhar Bharat Mission to provide training and financial assistance for fisheries to the fishermen during current and last three years, State/UT-wise; and (d) 	the details of the funds issued for promotion of fisheries in the States of Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh during each of the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) to (d):  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has launched a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)” as a part of the Atma Nirbhar Bharat relief package with a highest ever investment of Rs. 20050 crore in fisheries sector. The PMMSY is implemented over a period of 5 years with effect from the financial year 2020-21 to 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories including Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh for sustainable development of fisheries and aquaculture sector. The scheme intends to address critical gaps in fish production and productivity, quality, technology, post-harvest infrastructure and management, modernization and strengthening of value chain, traceability, establishing a robust fisheries management framework and fishers’ welfare including enhancement of fish production and income of fishermen.  PMMSY inter-alia lays special focus on training, skill development, skill up-gradation and capacity building through trainings, awareness building programmes and exposure visits to stakeholders especially fishers, fish farmers, fish workers, fish vendors, entrepreneurs, officials, fisheries Cooperatives and members of Fish Farmer Producer Organization. An amount of Rs. 100 crore is earmarked in PMMSY for Training, Awareness, Exposure and Capacity Building and is being utilised to impart training through National Fisheries Development Board, ICAR institutes, Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs), other organisations and State Departments. A total of 3774 outreach events covering about 20.78 lakh beneficiaries have been completed so far.   State/UT-wise details of the central funds released to the States/UTs including Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh during the last three years and current financial year for development of fisheries and aquaculture are furnished at Annexure-I. *****
