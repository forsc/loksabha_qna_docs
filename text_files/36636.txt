                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3866






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Defence Material Production Units




Suresh Pujari














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to establish Defence Material Production Units and allied Industries across the country, particularly in industry less districts having such feasibility;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to establish such industry in Bargarh and Jharsuguda of Odisha, particularly in areas like Padampur sub-division of Bargarh which has not seen an industry setup since independence;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Government proposes to explore such feasibility in the near future to do justice to the areas so that business and employment opportunities are created to enhance economic growth of those neglected areas and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) & (b): Three Defence Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) namely Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL), Bharat Dynamics Limited (BDL) and Mishra Dhatu Nigam Limited (MIDHANI) have taken action for establishment of following new Defence Material Production Units/Facilities:Name of DPSUs	New Unit/Facility	LocationBEL	Defence System Integration Complex	Palasamudram, Andhra Pradesh	Advance Night Vision Devices	Nimmaluru, Andhra Pradesh 	Space Electronics	Bengaluru, Karnataka	Land based EW Systems 	Ibrahimpatnam, Telangana	Explosive based products	Nagpur, Maharashtra	Plant for handling and storage of explosives/warheads	Vellore, Tamil NaduBDL	Propellant Manufacturing Plant	Jhansi, Uttar PradeshMIDHANI and NACLO	Utkarsha Aluminium Dhatu Nigam Limited (UADNL), Aluminium Alloy Flat Rolled Product (FRP)	Nellore, Andhra Pradesh (c) to (e): There is no new proposal for establishing a Defence Manufacturing Production Unit in Odisha as on date.  However, with increasing reliance being laid on domestic design & manufacturing, large number of facilities both in private and public sector are being set up across the country. *****
