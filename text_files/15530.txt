                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






National Sponsorship Programme for Differently abled Sportspersons




Gautam Gambhir














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering any proposal for a National Sponsorship Programme for differently abled sportspersons in view of their exceptional performance and facing of acute shortage of resources; and (b) if so, the details thereof? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI KIREN RIJIJU]   (a) & (b) The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports is already implementing various Schemes which cater to the differently abled sportspersons. This Ministry treats differently-abled sportspersons at par with the abled sportspersons and provides all requisite facilities besides their special needs.   Para sports have been placed in the “Priority” category for financial assistance under the Scheme of Assistance to NSFs for preparation and participation of the para-athletes in the various international competitions.  Under the Scheme of Special Awards to Medal Winners in International Sports Events and their Coaches, amount of award money for medalists of Paralympic Games, Para Asian Games and Commonwealth Games for Para-Athletes have been fixed at par with medalist of Olympics, Asian Games and Commonwealth games.  In addition to the above, the Khelo India Scheme being implemented by this Ministry, has „Promotion of Sports among persons with disabilities? as one of the verticals, for providing more avenues for promotion of sports among persons with disabilities.  ****
