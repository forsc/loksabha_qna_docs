                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5552






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Blood Bag Production




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the HLL (Hindustan Latex Limited) Lifecare Ltd. has proposed to set up a blood bag production unit at Konni in Kerala; (b) if so, the details and the present status thereof; (c) whether Government of Kerala had allotted land to HLL for this purpose; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):  Yes.  Government of Kerala had allotted  land on lease to HLL Lifecae Limited in Iravon Village, Konni Taluk, Pathanamthitta District, Kerala for the purpose of setting up Blood Bag production unit on 1st July, 2015.	Thereafter, on 4th February, 2019 the Government of Kerala entered into an agreement with HLL granting State land for temporary occupation for non-agricultural land purposes.…………………..
