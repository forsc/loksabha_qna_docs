                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

254






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Upgradation of State Highways to National Highways




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the criteria laid down for conversion/upgradation of various State Highways (SHs) to National Highways (NHs) in the country; (b) the details of various SHs developed as NHs during the last three years, State/UT-wise;(c) the funds allocated and utilized for the said purpose during the said period, State/UT-wise; (d) the details of the proposals from Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh and Chhattisgarh which have been approved in this regard along with those proposals which are lying pending for approval; and (e) the details of SHs identified for upgradation as NHs in Chhattisgarh under the National Highways Development Project along with the number of SHs upgraded as NHs thereunder so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e)	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
