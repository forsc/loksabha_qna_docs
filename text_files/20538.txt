                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Increase in Number of MSMEs




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any data on the increase in the number of MSMEs annually since 2018 and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government has any data on female participation in MSMEs and if so, the details thereof; and (c)	whether the Government has details of employment generated by MSMEs and if so, the details thereof during the last three years?                 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):   As per Udyog Aadhaar Portal, number of total MSMEs registered  in All India and number of female MSMEs registered in All India are as follows: <pre>Description	2018-19 (Since inception to 31 March 2019)	2019-20(01.04.2019 to 31.03. 2020)	2020-21(01.04.2020 to 30.06.2020) 	All India   	Female MSMEs	All India   	Female MSMEs	All India   	Female MSMEsTotal MSME	6495954	840910	9058212	1391861	10232468	1573754Increase in Registration of MSMEs	-	-	2562258	550951	1174256	181893</pre>(c): 	 Under the Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), the estimated employment generated (number of persons) in micro enterprises during the years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21(as on 31.12.2020) are 5.87 lakh, 5.33 lakh and 2.55 lakh respectively.
