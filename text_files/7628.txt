                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1301






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Engineering College




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether approval has been given to private and public sector companies to openengineering colleges in the country;(b) if so, the State-wise number of new engineering colleges opened and the number ofengineering colleges closed in the country during the last three years; and(c) the efforts being made by the Government to maintain the quality of education inthe engineering colleges?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Yes Sir, approval has been given to private and public sector companies to openengineering colleges in the country.(b) State-wise number of new engineering colleges opened and closed in the countryduring the last three years and current year is enclosed at Annexure.(c) All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) has been established under theAICTE Act, 1987 with a view to the proper planning and co-ordinated development of thetechnical education system throughout the country, the promotion of qualitativeimprovement of such education in relation to planned quantitative growth and theregulation and proper maintenance of norms and standards in the technical educationsystem and for the matters connected therewith. In pursuance of the same, a number ofinitiatives have been taken by AICTE from time to time including a package of measuresknown as the ‘Quality initiative program of AICTE’ launched in March, 2017. Theseinclude:<pre>? Model Curriculum for Under Graduate and Post Graduate courses.? Perspective Plan? Examination Reforms? Mandatory Internship and Induction Programme for students? Accreditation of Technical Courses? Teachers’ Training Policy? Soft Skill Development Programmes and Industry Readiness? Startups Initiative and Smart India Hackathon</pre>****
