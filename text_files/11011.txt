                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

66






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Exclusive Project for Cultural Heritage of Thrissur




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to initiate an exclusive project for the cultural heritage of Thrissur which is known as the Cultural Capital of Kerala and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government has any projects related with the cultural heritages in particular cities, towns and villages and if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the details of fund allocated for the projects related to the cultural heritage and cultural tourism in the country since 2014?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)   No, Sir. (b) & (c)   Ministry of Tourism, Government of India had launched the Swadesh Darshan Scheme in January, 2015 for integrated development of theme based tourist circuits in the country. Under the scheme, fifteen (15) thematic circuits have been identified for development namely; North-East Circuit, Buddhist Circuit, Himalayan Circuit, Coastal Circuit, Krishna Circuit, Desert Circuit, Tribal Circuit, Eco Circuit, Wildlife Circuit, Rural Circuit, Spiritual Circuit, Ramayana Circuit, Heritage Circuit, Sufi Circuit, and Tirthankara Circuit.	Under the Spiritual Circuit in Kerala, Ministry of Tourism has approved development of Kasaragod, Wayanad, Kannur, Kozhikode, Palakkad, Mallapuram, Thrichur, Ernakulam, Idukki, Kottayam, Allaphuza, Pathananamithitta, Kollam and Trivandrum for Rs. 85.23 crores.	Further, Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned a number of projects relating to cultural heritage under the Heritage Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme. Details of the projects are annexed (Annexure).
