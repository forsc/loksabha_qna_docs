                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1565






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Use of Vacant Land in Rajasthan




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the unused land belonging to Railways in the country including tribal areas of Banswada and Dungarpur of Rajasthan;(b)	whether there is any scheme for the commercial use of unutilized land and if so, the names of institutions allotted land for commercial utilization by the Railway’s so far;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether the Railway Land Development Authority (RLDA) has been entrusted to do the same? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a):    Out of total land of 4.81 lakh hectare, Indian Railways has about 0.51 lakh hectares of vacant land (not surplus) which is mostly in the form of narrow strips along tracks.  However, there is no unused land available in tribal areas of Banswara and Dungarpur of Rajasthan.(b) to (d):    Yes, Sir. Vacant Railway land not required by Railways for its immediate future  operational  needs  is  also  leased  through  open, transparent and competitive bidding for commercial/property development in interim period wherever feasible through Rail Land Development Authority (RLDA), in order to mobilize additional financial resources. As on date, 110 sites measuring 336.29 Hectare (approximately) have been entrusted to RLDA. *****
