                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Duty on Export of Raw Bauxite




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any representation to eliminate duty on exports of non-plant grade raw bauxite which is unconsumable for any purpose in the country;(b)  if so, the response of Government thereto; and (c) if not, the time by which action is likely to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a):  Certain representations have been received to abolish export duty on non-plant grade bauxite.(b) and (c): The issue was examined as part of budget exercise. Taking into account the overall facts, it was decided to maintain status quo in the matter.
