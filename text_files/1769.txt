                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1723






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Procurement of Mung, Groundnut, Gram and Mustard




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the parameters and rules fixed by the Government to procure mung, groundnut, gram, mustard, etc. from farmers at minimum support price; (b) 	whether it is a fact that the Government has fixed procurement limit at minimum support price; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the details of powers assigned by the Government of the States concerned?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	Mung, groundnut, gram, mustard etc. notified pulses and oilseeds of Fair Average Quality (FAQ) are procured from farmers at Minimum Support Price (MSP) in consultation with the concerned State Governments/Union Territories under Price Support System (PSS) as per its prescribed guidelines.(b) & (c):	No procurement limit has been fixed under PSS. Central Government procures 25% of the actual production of the commodity for that particular season and beyond this, State Government may procure the same at its own cost.******
