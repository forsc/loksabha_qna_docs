                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

565






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Revenue Loss




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the industry has undertaken revenue analysis of estimated/projected loss of revenue to the Government, presently; and(b) 	the impact of proposed reduction of GST on revenue vis-à-vis recovery on account of increase in sales, industry-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): 	As per information received from Department of Revenue (State Taxes Division), no such report is available. *****
