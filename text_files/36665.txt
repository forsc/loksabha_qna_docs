                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3847






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Agreement with World Bank for NNM




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)   	whether the Government has signed an agreement with the World Bank for 200 million Dollar loan deal for the National Nutrition Mission (NNM);(b)   	if so, the programmes proposed to be run by the Government with the said loan and the names of the States identified the quantum of funds proposed to be spent; (c)   	the name of the districts of the country included in the programme under this scheme, State-wise;(d)  	whether the Government proposes to run this scheme by integrating it under ICDC and NNM; and(e)   	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (b) Government of India signed a loan agreement worth $ 200 million with the World Bank for POSHAN Abhiyaan for all States/UTs in order to address the challenges of malnutrition.(c) All States / UTs are covered under POSHAN Abhiyaan. The States/UTs wise detailed list  of the districts is at Annexure I.(d) to (e) Mission POSHAN 2.0 announced in the Budget 2021-22 for all States/UTs is an Integrated Nutrition Support Programme which seeks to address the challenges of malnutrition in children, adolescent girls, pregnant women and lactating mothers through a strategic shift in nutrition content and delivery and by creation of a convergent eco-system to develop and promote practices that nurture health, wellness and immunity. Poshan 2.0 will seek to optimize the quality and delivery of food under the Supplementary Nutrition Program.Mission Poshan 2.0 will contribute to human capital development of the country; address malnutrition challenges; promote nutrition awareness and good eating habits for sustainable health & well-being and address nutrition related deficiencies through key strategies. Under the programme, nutritional norms and standards and quality and testing of THR will be improved and greater stakeholder and beneficiary participation will be promoted besides traditional community food habits. Poshan 2.0 will bring 3 important programmes/schemes under its ambit, viz., Anganwadi Services, Scheme for Adolescent Girls and POSHAN Abhiyaan.
