                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Funds Allocated for Preservation of Heritage Places of Madhya Pradesh




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether appropriate steps have been taken for the preservation and conservation of heritage places and monuments of Madhya Pradesh and Jammu and Kashmir State;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the result thereon; (c) 	whether the Government has specifically chalked out any plan to conserve the forts, ancient temples and other heritage sites at the Guna, Shivpuri and Ashok Nagar districts of Madhya Pradesh and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the total amount allocated and utilized for this purpose during the last two years and the current year; and (e)	the time limit set for the development of such places?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)  & (b) 	Yes Sir, the conservation work of protected monuments in the country including Madhya Pradesh and Jammu and Kashmir is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) depending upon the requirements of different sites and availability of resources and they are in a good state of preservation.(c)  to  (e)	Conservation works of protected monuments in the country including Shivpuri and Ashok Nagar districts of Madhya Pradesh is a continuous process.  There is no protected monument of ASI in Guna district.  The routine maintenance work including watch and ward etc. is attended regularly at all the protected monuments whereas special repairs work is attended on the selected monuments as per the need at sites and availability of the resources. The details of amount allocated and utilised during the last two years and allocation for the current financial year for conservation of protected monuments in Madhya Pradesh including in the districts of  Shivpuri and Ashok Nagar are given below:                                        (Amount Rs. in Lakhs)Sl. No.	Year	Allocation 	Expenditure1	2017-18	  780.00	779.982	2018-19	  840.0	        809.483	2019-20	  1200.00	1189.00 (up to 25.02.2020)
