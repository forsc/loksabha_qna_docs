                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

883






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Ayush System of Medicine




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether AYUSH system of medicine and related documents are being made available through digital mode and if so, the details thereof;(b) the time by which it is likely to be made available through digital mode and the benefits likely to accrue therefrom;(c) whether AYUSH Department has launched five portals for research and medicine; and(d) if so, the details and the salient features thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(DR. MUNJPARA MAHENDRABHAI)(a):	Yes. The Ministry of Ayush and organizations under the Ministry have their websites /portals for publishing various information on Ayush and related documents. As far as Ministry is concerned, information/ documents such as Annual reports, Guidelines/ advisories, documents related to treatment /prevention of COVID-19, Newsletters, related documents of Schemes of the Ministries etc are made available on the website.(b):	The documents are routinely uploaded on the website of the Ministry for information of all.(c):	Yes. Five portals are launched for research and medicine by the Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) an autonomous body under the Ministry.  (d):	The details are at Annexure-I.
