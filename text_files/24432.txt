                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

329






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Allocation of Budget under MGNREGS




Hanuman Beniwal













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the budget allocated to Rajasthan by the Union Government and utilization thereof under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) during 2019-20, 2020-21 and till June, 2021 of the current financial year, the details thereof, item wise; and(b) 	the amount which remained unspent by the Rajasthan Government out of the total budget allocated under MGNREGS during the aforesaid period and the reasons therefor for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):   	The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme which provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. Being a demand driven programme, no State/UT-wise (including State of Rajasthan) financial allocation is made.  The details of funds released by Central Government to the State of Rajasthan for the implementation of Mahatma Gandhi NREG Scheme during the last two financial years 2019-20, 2020-21 and the current FY 2021-22 (as on 30.06.2021) is given below:                                                       (Rs. In crore)Indicators	            2019-20	   2020-21	       2021-22Central funds released	    6891.74	   9129.02	      3271.79(b):        As per information provided by State Government of Rajasthan, the unspent balance with State was Rs.1186.46 crore as on 31.03.2020 at the end of financial year 2019-20, and an amount of Rs. 214.87 crore was remaining unspent as on 31.03.2021 at the end of FY 2020-21.******
