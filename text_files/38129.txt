                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5260






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Tribal Ecosystem




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of the initiatives taken by the Government along with the funds allocated for transformation of the tribal ecosystem in the country including State of Odisha;b)	whether the Government proposes to take steps for the conservation and development of religious places of the tribals across the country including Odisha; andc)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):  Government has adopted integrated approach for welfare and development of tribal people and transformation of the tribal ecosystem across the country. Tribal Sub-Plan (TSP), now called as Scheduled Tribe Component (STC) is a dedicated source of fund for provision of support for education, health, agriculture, livelihood, housing, drinking water & sanitation, employment generation, skill development, women and child development, preservation of cultural heritage etc. 40 Central Ministries / Departments apart from Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) has been mandated to earmark Tribal Sub-Plan funds in the range of 4.3% to 17.5% of their total Scheme allocation every year for overall tribal development. Details of funds allocated by different Central Ministries / Departments under their schemes are reflected in the Union Budget as Statement 10B which is laid on the table of the House. State-wise details of total funds released for the welfare of tribals during the last three years under the STC of Central Ministries / Departments is at Annexure - 1. Details of funds allocated and spent by States / UTs under their Schemes for welfare and development of STs during 2018-19 to 2020-21 is at Annexure 2. (b) & (c): There is no such proposal under consideration of Ministry of Tribal Affairs.
