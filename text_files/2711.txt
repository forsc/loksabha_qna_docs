                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2476






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Entrepreneurship Development




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether entrepreneurship development is being encouraged as per area specific requirements and if so, the details thereof; and(b) 	the details of the specific scheme prepared for inaccessible, difficult, hilly and remote areas?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) 	With an aim to promote a culture of entrepreneurship among India’s youth and inspire them to set-up their own enterprise and create employment opportunities for others, Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, instituted National Entrepreneurship Awards Scheme (NEAS) in 2016. Under NEAS, awards are conferred to the aspiring first generation entrepreneurs to recognize and honour their efforts and achievements and those individuals/organizations who are working as eco-system builder in the field of entrepreneurship development. The awards also seek to highlight model of excellence for others to emulate and improve upon.Through the Scheme, it has been attempted to make the Awards process inclusive and highlight the achievements of outstanding young entrepreneurs from different socio-economic backgrounds across sectors/ industries in varied geographies. The 3rd edition of National Entrepreneurship Awards was held in New Delhi on 4th January, 2019 which had the provision for conferring three awards (out of total 43 awards) to entrepreneurs from difficult areas.
