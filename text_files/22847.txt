                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Sagarmala Programme in Odisha




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of implementation of various projects under the Sagarmala programme in Odisha, along with their names;(b) the projects proposed to be developed in Odisha under the Sagartat Samriddhi programme, along with their names; and(c) the details of the impact, the Sagartat Samriddhi programme will make in the implementation of the Sagarmala programme?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) There are a total of 46 projects worth Rs.58,402 crores under the four pillars of Sagarmala programme in the state of Odisha. Pillar wise classification and details of the projects is annexed.  (b) & (c) The Sagartat Samriddhi programme for holistic development of coastal districts is under conceptualization stage and primary data collection has been carried out for it.
