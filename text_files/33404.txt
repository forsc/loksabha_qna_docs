                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

724






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Zero Liquid Discharge Plants




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the list of Textile industries (fabric dyeing and chemical treatment industries) in Districts of Namakkal, Erode, Tiruppur, Karur which are running without Zero Liquid Discharge plants, district-wise;(b)	the details of action taken against the owners of the Industries who fails to comply with the norms, Industry-wise; and (c)	the reasons for no criminal action taken against the Industries who pump the waste water into ground using borewells in the above mentioned districts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a ) to (c) As per the information received from the Government of Tamil  Nadu, there is no textile industries (fabric dying and chemical treatment industries) under operation without Zero Liquid Discharge System in the districts of Namakkal, Erode, Tiruppur and Karur.All the textile industries are achieving Zero Liquid Discharge System, thereby RO Permeate and Multi Effect- Evaporator (MEE) Condensate are recycled in their process and the remaining reject are evaporated and finally put into Agitated Thin Film Drier (ATFD) for recovery of mixed salt. There is no criminal action taken against the industries by the Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board, as all units are complying the stipulated norms. ****
