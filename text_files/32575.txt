                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

137






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






PM Khanij Kshetra Kalyan Yojana




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil













                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has identified more sectors for inclusion in the high-priority sectors list under the PM Khanij Kshetra Kalyan Yojana(PMKKKY);(b) if so, the details and reasons for the sectors so identified ;(c) if not, whether the Government is planning to include more sectors under the high-priority sectors list; and(d) if so, the sector-wise (high priority and other) utilisation of funds under the PM Khanij  Kshetra Yojana in Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (c): As per the PMKKY guidelines, at least 60% of the funds collected under DMF are to be utilized for High Priority Areas namely Drinking Water, Environment Preservation and Pollution Control measures, Health Care, Education, Welfare of Women and Children, Welfare of aged and disabled, Skill Development, Sanitation.
