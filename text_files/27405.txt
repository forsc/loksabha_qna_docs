                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3086






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Female Infancticides




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)	the details of female infanticides reported during 2020-21, State/UT-wise;(b)	whether the government is formulating any policy/proposes to take specific measures to check the incidence of female infanticide; and(c)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a): As per the communication received from National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), the latest published data of female infanticide pertains to the year 2017-19. Details are at Annexure.(b) & (c): Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) Scheme aims to address declining Child Sex Ratio (CSR) and related issues of empowerment of girls and women over a life cycle continuum. The objectives of the scheme are, to prevent gender biased sex selective elimination, to ensure survival and protection of the girl child and to ensure education and participation of the girl child. The key elements of the scheme include nation-wide media and advocacy campaign and also multi-sectoral intervention in selected districts
