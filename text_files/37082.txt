                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4201






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






New Central Media Accreditation Guidelines for Freelance Journalists




Raksha Nikhil Khadse













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has proposed new central media accreditation guidelines for the freelance journalists with restrictive reporting of Government affairs and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	the details of new rules for accreditation to journalists with stringent provisions of revoking?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (b) 	The Government has issued “The Central Media Accreditation Guidelines, 2022” for grant of accreditation to working journalists and other categories of persons including freelance working journalists at the headquarters of the Government of India, in accordance with the eligibility criterion and other conditions, etc. laid down under these guidelines. The Central Media Accreditation Committee, chaired by the Principal DG, PIB and comprising of up to 25 Members representing different media organisations, discharges the functions laid down under these guidelines and takes decision including suspension/withdrawal of accreditation on the basis of various criterion laid down therein.
