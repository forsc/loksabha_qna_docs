                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3703






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Adoption of E-20 Fuel




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to introduce E-20 fuel as automotive fuel;(b) 	if so, the salient features thereof; and (c)	the steps taken by the Government to facilitate development of E-20 compliant vehicles as well as adoption of mass emission standards for this fuel?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) Yes, Sir. The Government has notified G.S.R. 156(E) dated 8th March, 2021 regarding mass emission standards for E-20 fuel. Further, the Government has also notified G.S.R. 172(E) dated 11th March, 2021 regarding safety requirements for motor vehicles running on E-20 Fuel and higher blend of ethanol to seek comments from the concerned stakeholders.*****
