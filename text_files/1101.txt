                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

896






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Construction of NHs




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total kilometres of highways constructed during each of the last five years, State-wise;(b)	whether it has steadily increased during the said period and the targets fixed have been achieved;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	the new targets fixed by the Government for the next three years, State-wise including Tamil Nadu including per day construction target; and(e)	the steps initiated by the Government to attain the fixed targets?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  The State-wise details of National Highways constructed during each of the last five years are given at Annexure-I.  (b) to (c) Target and achievement during the last five years are as follows which shows that achievement has steadily increased during the last five years: (length in km)Year	Target 	 Achievement2014-15	6300	44102015-16	10950	60612016-17	15000	82312017-18	15000	98292018-19	10000	10855(d) to (e) Ministry set target on financial year basis, there is no such target fixed for next three years. However, ministry has set the target of 11000 km for the construction of National Highways including 330 km in Tamil Nadu for the current financial year 2019-20. The steps initiated by the Government to attain the fixed targets are given at Annexure-II.
