                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2844






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Financial Assistance to Polytechnics/ ITIs




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel













                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any scheme under which financial assistance is provided to Polytechnics/Industrial Training Institute (ITIs) of various States, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b)	the details of Polytechnics/ITIs in Gujarat to which financial assistance has been provided under the said scheme during the last three years;(c)	whether the Government provides any funds to amend courses in Polytechnics in consultation with the local industries; and(d)	the details thereof especially Polytechnics in Gujarat which have been provided funds for this purposes? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes, Sir. Directorate General of Training (DGT) under Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) provides financial assistance to Polytechnics and ITIs for the following Schemes: 1.	‘Scheme of Polytechnics’ comprising following four components:i.	Setting up of new Polytechnics in Un-served and Underserved Districts: Under this scheme, 296 new polytechnics are being set up by the State Governments /Union Territories in un-served and underserved districts with financial assistance from Government of India limited   to   Rs. 12.30 crore   per   polytechnic (Rs. 8.00 crore for civil works and Rs. 4.30 crore for machinery, equipment) to meet the capital costs of establishing a polytechnic.ii.	Central assistance for Up-gradation of selected Polytechnics: Under this scheme, financial assistance upto Rs. 2.00 crore per Polytechnic is provided by Government of India to upgrade infrastructure facilities of 500 selected polytechnics for modern equipment, replacement of obsolete equipment, providing modern facilities for application of IT in teaching, learning and testing processes and creating infrastructure facilities as well as introduction of new diploma courses.  iii.	Central assistance for Construction of Women''s Hostels in selected Polytechnics: The scheme envisages financial assistance subject to a maximum of Rs. 1.00 crore for each polytechnic, to be provided to 500 existing AICTE approved Government / Government aided Polytechnics, for the construction of women’s hostel. iv.	Community Development Through Polytechnics (CDTP): CDTP scheme envisages providing non-formal, short-term, employment-oriented skill development programmes, through AICTE approved Polytechnics, to various sections of the community, particularly the rural, unorganized & disadvantaged sections of the society, to enable them to obtain gainful self / wage employment. Recurring grant upto Rs. 17.00 lakh per polytechnic per annum is provided by the Central Government. 2.	Upgradation of existing Government ITIs into Model ITIs: Existing Govt. ITIs in states have been taken up for Upgradation into Model ITI which is supposed to evolve as an institution showcasing best practices, efficient and high-quality training delivery and sustainable and effective industry relationship. Under this scheme, 30 Model ITIs in 28 State / Union Territories are being provided financial assistance from Government of India upto Rs. 10 crore per Model ITI towards the Upgradation. 3.	Skill Development in 47 Districts Affected by Left Wing Extremism: The scheme envisages establishment of 47 new ITIs (1 ITI per district in 47 districts of 10 States) limited to Rs. 7.34 crore per ITI. Out of Rs. 319.55 crore as Central allocation, Rs. 226.47 crore has been released under the Scheme.  4. Enhancing Skill Development Infrastructure in NE States: The scheme envisages establishment of 34 new ITIs along with upgradation of 22 existing Government ITIs by introducing three new trades per ITI and supplementing infrastructure deficiencies in 28 ITIs by constructing new hostel, boundary wall and replacing old and obsolete tools and equipment. Under this scheme, financial assistance is limited to Rs. 9.5 crore per ITI for establishment of new ITI. Out of Rs. 385.28 crore as Central allocation, Rs. 253.49 crore has been released under the Scheme. 5. Skills Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE): It is World Bank assisted project for a total cost of Rs. 2,200 crore. The objective of the project to improve relevance and efficiency of skills development provided through ITIs and apprenticeship. Under STRIVE, 426 ITIs including 29 Private ITIs have been selected in 33 States of India.(b)	    The details of polytechnics/ITIs in Gujarat to which financial assistance has been provided under these Schemes during the last three years is given at Annexure I.  (c)&(d)  This Ministry does not provide any funds to amend courses in Polytechnics in consultation with local industries.
