                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Medical Colleges and Seats




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) total number of medical colleges and seats available thereunder in India; and(b) the number of medical students who have joined public medical institutions in the country after taking medical graduation degree during the last five years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b): As informed by the National Medical Commission, there are 596 Medical Colleges including 313 Government Medical Colleges with 89875 MBBS seats including 46560 Government MBBS seats are available in the country. The details of Government colleges with MBBS seats in last 05 years are as under:-AY	Government Colleges	MBBS Seats2017-18	227	312782018-19	245	334722019-20	279	422222020-21	289	434352021-22	313	46560.
