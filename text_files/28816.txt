                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Affected Area




Arun Sao













                                                    Vijay Baghel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds allocated for the selected States under Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Affected Areas (RCPLWEA) since the launch of the above scheme along with the State-wise and yearwise details thereof;(b) 	the details of the roads and the names of the places which are to be connected under this scheme in the state of Chhattisgarh;(c) 	the details of the work that has been completed under the said scheme particularly in the state of Chhattisgarh along with the details of pending work; and(d) 	the reasons of pendency along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)     The details of Central share of funds released and total expenditure (including State Share) for the selected States under Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Affected Areas (RCPLWEA) since the launch of the scheme is given at Annexure-I.(b) to (c) Details of roads sanctioned, constructed and balance in various states and that in Chhattisgarh (district wise) is given at Annexure II. Further, the details of the roads and the names of the places which are to be connected under this scheme is in public domain at omms.nic.in. It can be viewed through weblink: omms.nic.in->proposals->State wise list of roads.(d)     Reasons for pendency of works under RCPLWEA are delay due to Left Wing Extremism activities, lack of response to tenders by contractors, difficult working conditions, forest clearance issues, etc.*****
