                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

342






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Special Assistance to Universities under Skill India Mission




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to provide special facility/ assistanceto the universities of the country under Skill India Mission;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of universities identified along with the amount of funds proposedto allocate to this purpose; and(d) if not, whether any framework has been prepared for future and if so, thedetails thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): There is no special facility or assistance to Universities under Skill IndiaMission. Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) hasformulated a model framework/ guidelines to lay down minimum standards andnorms for the functioning of Skill Universities. University Grants Commission isrunning skill courses through Community Colleges, Bachelor of Vocation courses(BVoC) & Deen Dayal Upadhyaya (DDU) Kausal Kendras which are operationalin different colleges. Currently there are 531 colleges / universities under theseschemes (275 BVoC colleges, 188 Community Colleges and 68 DDU KaushalKendra Colleges).*****
