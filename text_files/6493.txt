                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

281






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Establishment of Central Agricultural Universities




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plans to establish new Central Agriculture Universities and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government plans to establish such universities in Uttar Pradesh, and if so, the details thereof; (c)	the steps undertaken by the Government to upgrade existing facilities in Agriculture Universities such as research labs, modern scientific equipment among others; and (d)	whether the Government plans to entrust Indian Council of Agricultural Research with regulatory powers over private agriculture colleges/universities and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	There is no such proposal at present.(b)	Does not arise in view of (a) above.(c)	The Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) provides need based funding support to Agricultural Universities under Plan Scheme “Strengthening and Development of Higher Agricultural Education in India” to upgrade research laboratories, and scientific equipment’s, and other infrastructure.(d)	No, Sir.*******
