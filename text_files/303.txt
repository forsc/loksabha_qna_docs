                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

277






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Kendriya Vidyalaya in Gujarat




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government had taken a decision to set up aKendriya Vidyalaya (KV) in each Constituency/ district across the country;(b) the number of districts in Gujarat where any KV has not been set up so faralong with the total number of KVs functioning in Gujarat, District-wise;(c) whether the Government is considering to open more KVs in denselypopulated districts of Gujarat;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) There is no decision to set up Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in each constituency/ districts of the country.(b) KVs have not been set up in 7 districts of Gujarat. The districts-wiseinformation about KVs functioning in Gujarat is as under:-Jamnagar – 7,Ahmedabad -6, Kutch -5, Gandhinagar – 5, Vadodara – 5, Surat– 3,Rajkot – 2 and 1 each in Bhavnagar, Banaskantha, Sabarjabtga, Junagarh,Porbander, Panchmahal, Dahod, Surendranagar, Anand, Bharuch, Mehsana andPatan districts.(c) to (e) No such proposal is under consideration at present.******
