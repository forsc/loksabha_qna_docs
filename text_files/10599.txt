                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4017






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Policy on Cleanliness




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether there is any policy on cleanliness of shaft area, stair-case and adjacent corridors including common areas of CPWD quarters/buildings located in Delhi particularly in NDMC area; (b)	Whether the Government is aware of the facts that there is no arrangement by CPWD for cleanliness of the said area of CPWD quarters/buildings located in NDMC area particularly at BKS Marg Phase-I and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c)	Whether the Government proposes to make a policy for cleanliness drive on daily basis after consultation with other stake-holders including NDMC keeping in view Prime Minister’s popular scheme Swachh Bharat Mission; and(d)	If so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said policy is likely to be implemented and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes sir.  (b) & (c) Cleaning of shaft area, stair case and adjacent corridors including common areas of General Pool Residential Accommodation (GPRA) quarters/buildings maintained by Central Public Works Department (CPWD) located in Delhi including Baba Kharak Singh (BKS) Marg Phase – I is done by CPWD.(d)	Does not arise in view of reply to Part (a) to (c) above.                               ******
