                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

46






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Unani System of Medicine




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Unani system of medicine is not given much importance as Ayurveda despite being placed along with Ayurveda and Homeopathy under the Ministry of AYUSH; (b) if so, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government proposes to form a separate board for Unani system of medicine on the lines of Ayurveda and Homeopathy; (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (e) whether the syllabus of Ayurveda and Unani are almost the same and surgery is taught and training is given/provided to the students of both the systems at Under-Graduate and Post-Graduate levels; and (f) whether the Government has given permission to Post-Graduate Ayurveda Doctors to perform surgery and has denied such permission to Post-Graduate Unani Doctors and if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) to (f) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO.46 * FOR 4TH FEBRUARY, 2022(a) & (b) All regulated traditional system of medicine under Ministry of AYUSH like Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Unani, Siddha & Sowa-Rigpa are given equal importance.  To provide the impetus to Unani system of medicine, Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India has setup the Central Council of Research in Unani Medicine (CCRUM), New Delhi and National Institute of Unani Medicine (NIUM), Bengaluru, for education and research in Unani system of medicine which includes development of new drugs as well as conducting clinical trials on scientific lines.  A satellite institute of National Institute of Unani Medicine with 200-bedded hospital has been established at Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh.  A total number of 22 Institutes/Centres/Units are also working under the CCRUM across the country. State-wise details are placed at Annexure I. Both CCRUM and NIUM under Ministry of AYUSH are engaged in Unani education at PG and Ph. D level and have taken up a number of clinical research studies for various diseases which include Arthritis, Bronchial Asthma, Anemia, Anxiety, Depressive Disorders, Neuro-degenerative diseases, life-style disorders like Hypertension, Obesity dyslipidemia, diabetes and various skin diseases like vitiligo etc. The details of various clinical researches undertaken by the CCRUM is at Annexure-II.As on 01.04.2020, 1748 Unani dispensaries and 265 Unani Hospitals are functioning in the Country.  As per available data in the Ministry, Unani education is being imparted through more than 52 colleges upto UG level and more than 15 colleges at PG level. Also 566 licensed Unani pharmacies are functioning in the country.In order to promote and establish Unani System of Medicine at global level, CCRUM is actively engaged with WHO and many activities in this regard have been taken up with WHO such as Developing Benchmarks for training and practice in Unani Medicine, Developing Benchmarks for International Unani Terminology and Inclusion of Unani Morbidity Codes in ICD-11 ASU TM 2 Chapter.Furthermore, each year to commemorate the birth anniversary of Unani Scholar and Social Reformer Hakim Ajmal Khan, Unani Day is celebrated and National/International Conference is being organized in different parts of the country in a very grand manner to promote Unani System of Medicine.  Delegates and experts from leading Academic and Research organizations participates in these events.(c) & (d)   There is a provision of Board of Unani, Siddha and Sowa-Rigpa under National Commission for Indian System of Medicine Act, 2020, in which there is a provision of members of Unani discipline as Members as per Section 19 (1) (b) of NCISM Act, 2020.  (e)	The syllabus of Ayurveda and Unani as prescribed by erstwhile Central Council of Indian Medicine (now National Commission of Indian System for Medicine) are parallel and almost on similar lines. Surgery is included/taught and students of both the systems are trained in it at Under-Graduate and Post-Graduate levels in the respective subjects.(f)	In the Minimum Standards of Education Regulation 2016 (PG-Ayurveda), an amendment was made in 2020 to allow the PG scholars of Shalya and Shalakya to perform 58 surgical procedures independently. Thereafter, the Regulation was challenged in the Court of Law i.e. Supreme Court through WP(C) No. 0069 of 2021 filed by Indian Medical Association.  The matter is subjudice.-------
