                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2515






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Har Medh Par Ped




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Balak Nath
                                                








                                                    Jaskaur Meena
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of Har Medh Par Ped Scheme;(b) the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized so far in Rajasthan under the said scheme;(c) whether the Government is implementing Har Medh Par Ped Scheme to promote agro forestry, if so, the details thereof;(d) the total number of trees planted in the country including Sikar, Jhunjhunu, Jalore, Sirohi of Rajasthan under the said scheme along with the achievements of this scheme, year-wise; and(e) the target fixed under the said scheme and the achievement made so far in this regard along with the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is presently implementing a scheme viz. Sub-Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF)-Har Medh Par Ped, since 2016-17. The salient features of the scheme areto encourage and expand tree plantation in a complementary and integrated manner with crops and livestock to improve productivity, employment opportunities, income generation and livelihoods of rural households, especially the small farmers. Multipurpose tree species like Sheesam, Teak, Eucalyptus, Malabar Neem, Ardu, Sandalwood, Poplar, Imli, Mango, etc. are promoted for plantation on farm land along with agricultural crops.The scheme is implemented in the States having liberalized felling and transit-regulations for transport of timber. Presently, the scheme is being implemented in 23 States/Union Territories (UTs) viz. Andhra Pradesh, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Punjab, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Uttra Pradesh, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Manipur, Nagaland, Jammu &Kashmir and Ladakh. The scheme is being implemented on sharing of expenditure between Central and State Government in the ratio of 60:40 for general category of States, and 90:10 for North Eastern and Himalayan States and 100% in case of UTs & National Agencies.(b):	 The details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized so far in Rajasthan under the SMAF scheme are as under:(Rs in Lakh; Central Share)Financial Year	Allocation	Sanction/Release	Expenditure	Unspent Balance2017-18	1200.00	600.00	45.15	554.852018-19	400.00	0.00	160.08	394.772019-20	100.00	0.00	75.44	319.332020-21	15.00	0.00	64.37	254.962021-22	50.00	--	--	---Total	1765.00	600.00	345.04	254.96 (c): 	Yes, Sir. Details are as given at point (a) above. (d): 	Since inception of the scheme in 2016-17, around 401.0 lakh trees have been planted and an area of93,809 hectares has been covered under the scheme.
