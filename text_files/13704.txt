                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2475






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






National Capital Region




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the areas covered under National Capital Region (NCR) area/ district-wise;(b)	whether the supervision and control of all the regions of NCR rest with Delhi administration or with the administration of States to which these regions belong and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Urban Housing Policy in NCR is likely to be on uniform pattern and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI  HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)to(c): The sub-region wise details of National Capital Region (NCR) are as under: Sub-Region 	Name of the Districts	                                         Area                                                                                (sq.kms.)Haryana	         Faridabad, Gurgaon, Mewat, Rohtak, Sonipat, Rewari,            25,327                 Jhajjar, Panipat, Palwal, Bhiwani, Charkhi Dadri,                 Mahendragarh, Jind and Karnal (14 Districts)	              Uttar Pradesh	 Meerut, Ghaziabad, Gautam Buddha Nagar, Bulandshahr, Baghpat,   14,826                 Hapur, Muzaffarnagar and Shamli (8 Districts)	               Rajasthan	 Alwar and Bharatpur (2 Districts)	                          13,447Delhi	         Entire NCT of Delhi 	                                           1,483NCR	         Total  (24 districts and NCT Delhi)	                          55,083The supervision and control of all the sub-regions of National Capital Region rests with the administration of States to which these sub-regions belong. There is no uniform Housing Policy in NCR.                                    *****
