                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3090






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Anganwadi Kendras in Chhattisgarh




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Anganwadi Kendras out of total Anganwadi Kendras in Chhattisgarh where arrangement of power supply of electricity has been made; (b) 	the steps being taken by the Union Government for upgradation of dilapidated Anganwadi Kendras; (c) 	whether the Union Government are contemplating on any proposal to execute this work; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILDDEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	As per the information received from the Government of Chhattisgarh, out of 51,384 Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) operational in Chhattisgarh, 38,894 AWCs have their own buildings, and out of which 17,846 AWC buildings have been electrified.(b) to (d) For improving the condition of AWC buildings, Government of India provides funds for upgradation of AWC buildings @ Rs. 2.00 lakh per AWC building.
