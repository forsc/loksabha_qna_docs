                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1032






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Export of Mango




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether India is to export mango to U.S. after hiatus;(b)	if so, the comparative details thereof since 2015 onwards to till date and revenue generated, country-wise and quality-wise of mango which has great demand and the manner in which it will be useful to the farmers; and(c)	the trade policies signed with USA and other countries in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ?????? 		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Yes, Sir. USA has agreed to resumption of export of mangoes from India from the season 2022.(b):	The requisite details of exports of mangoes since 2015 onwards to   all countries including USA are annexed. Resumption of export of mangoes may increase the income of farmers.(c):	A Phytosanitary Framework Agreement has been signed between Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (MA&FW) and United State Department of Agriculture (USDA) - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) resuming export of mangoes from India. Further, a framework agreement has been signed between Department of Plant Protection, MAF&W and USDA for Agriculture market access. Market access for export of mangoes is also available with several other countries where mangoes are being exported i.e. UAE, UK, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait etc.
