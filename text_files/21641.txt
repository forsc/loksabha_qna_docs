                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2956






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Children Hospitals




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the present status of functioning of Government hospitals meant for children at Banda and Chitrakoot districts of Bundelkhand region in Uttar Pradesh;(b) whether OPD for children has been resumed in these hospitals; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): Public Health and Hospitals being the State subject, the primary responsibility of ensuring functioning of Government Hospitals and ensuring the provision of provision of essential services lies with the respective State/UT Governments.As per the information received from the Government of Uttar Pradesh, there is no exclusive Government or private hospitals for children functioning at Banda and Chitrakoot districts of Bundelkhand region in Uttar Pradesh. OPD for children are being provided by pediatricians in District Hospitals, Banda and Chitrakoot and at CHC Antra. The facilities of SNCU/NRC are available in both District Hospitals. NBSU facility is available in CHCs at Nairaini, Manikpur, Pahardi and Shivrampur.
