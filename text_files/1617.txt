                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1394






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






New Tourism Projects to Promote Tourism




Balak Nath














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recently proposed to launch new tourism projects to promote tourism in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has prepared any special scheme for investment in tourism infrastructure development in various States including Rajasthan and if so, the details thereof along with the tourist places identified; and (d) 	the funds allocated for this purpose, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism, under the schemes viz., Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) and “Integrated Development of Theme Based Tourist Circuit in the Country, Swadesh Darshan (SD)" provides Central Financial Assistance for infrastructure development and beautification of tourist spots on receipt of suitable DPRs submitted by State Governments/Union Territories, subject to availability of funds, liquidation of pending utilisation certificate against the fund released earlier and adherence to the relevant scheme guidelines. Ministry of Tourism also promotes India as a holistic destination in the tourism generating markets to promote various Indian tourism products and to increase India’s share of the global tourism markets. For Promotion of Tourism, the Ministry undertakes several promotional activities in the domestic and international markets for promotion of the varied tourism products and destinations of the country. As part of its promotional activities, the Ministry produces publicity material and collaterals in English and foreign languages, annually releases print, electronic and online media campaigns in important and potential markets overseas and within the country under the ‘Incredible India’ brand-line. Promotions are also undertaken through the website and Social Media accounts of the Ministry.  	The list of projects approved under the schemes for infrastructure development in various States including Rajasthan is annexed.*******
