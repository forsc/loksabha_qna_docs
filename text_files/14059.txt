UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2769






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Complaints regarding Illegal Mining




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of complaints received about illegal mining in the country during the last three years, State/ UT-wise;(b)	the details of the action taken on these complaints, year-wise;(c)	whether it is a fact that the number of cases of illegal mining have increased in the country during the said period but action has been taken registering a few cases only; and(d)	if so, the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS  (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (d): Based on the quarterly returns on illegal mining submitted by various State Governments to the Indian Bureau of Mines (a subordinate office of this Ministry), the state-wise case details of illegal mining reported from the year 2016-17 to 2018-19 alongwith the action taken is given at Annexure. It can be concluded from the table given at Annexure that the cases of illegal mining showed an upward trend from 2016-17 to 2017-18.
