                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4145






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Immigration Fees




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of money collected as immigration fees from the people who went abroad during the last three years; and(b) the details regarding the use of this amount?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	No immigration fee is collected by the Immigration Authorities from the people going abroad. However the Protector General of Emigrants under the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) received Emigration Fee from those people who are going abroad on Employment visa. Amount collected/received during last three years is given as under:Sl. No.	Year	Receipts (in Rs.)1.	2018-19	7,97,26,496.002.	2019-20	8,33,07,047.003.	2020-21	2,14,02,208.004.	2021-22 (up-to February 2022)	5,23,31,546.00(b):	The Emigration Fee received by the Protector General of Emigrants is deposited in the Revenue Receipts of the Central Government under ‘Other Administrative Services’ head as part of Consolidated Fund of India.******
