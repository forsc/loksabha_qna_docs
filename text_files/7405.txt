                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1056






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Solar Air Purifying Unit




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to establish Mega Solar Air Purifying Units in the near future; (b) 	if so, the details thereof specially in Delhi/NCR; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the details of plan outlay and implementation procedure for any such proposal in the near future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (d)   A proposal titled “Project for Demonstrating the Feasibility of Reducing Air Pollution Exposure in Urban Area by Using Large Scale Cleaning System” was submitted by Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IITB) for control of air pollution under EPC (Environment Protection Charge) funds. The project aims to develop and deploy large scale air purifying system named Adaptive Clean Air Network (ACAN) with downdraft flow type for reduction of Particulate Matter (PM). The proposal was forwarded to Department of Science and Technology (DST) on for evaluation and inputs. The proposal was discussed by a DST appointed Expert Panel. The project proponent is  to submit response to queries and based on the response Expert Panel will come out with final decision on award of project.*****
