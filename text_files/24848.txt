                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

761






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Health Melas




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to organise Health Melas in each district of the country including district Gopalganj of Bihar under Rural Health Mission, if so, the details thereof and the funds released for the same, district-wise;(b) the details of each district where the released funds have not been utilized to organise Health Melas under Rural Health Mission; and(c) whether the Government has issued guidelines for ensuring optimum utilization of the funds released for Health Melas and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b):	Public Health and Hospital is a State subject and the responsibility of providing health care services lies with the respective State Governments. However, under National Health Mission(NHM) Financial and Technical Support is provided to the States for Health System Strengthening including for infrastructure, human resources, organizing Health Camps and other community outreach programme. Such support is based on the proposals received from the States under Programme Implementation Plan (PIPs) as per the norms and available resources. However, during the Financial Year 2018-19 in February,2019 specific funds have been given to States for organizing Health Melas under National Health Mission (NHM). Details is at Annexure-I(c):	The Government regularly reviews the expenditure on approved activities  through regular correspondence,video conferences and meeting with the State Government Officials, so as to improves the utilization of funds. The details of funds utilized under National Health Mission (NHM) during FY 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 as reported by the State under the activity of Targeting Naturally Occurring Gathering of People/ Health Mela are at Annexure-II.
