                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Monitoring Mechanism for the Activities under CAMPA




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds allocated to the States under Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) during the last three years including the current year, State-wise and year-wise;(b)	whether the Government has in place a monitoring mechanism to check if the CAMPA funds are being used for the intended purpose, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c)	whether the Government has received complaints of any irregularity in the work being done under CAMPA head, if so, the details thereof along with the corrective action taken by Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) The details of funds transferred to the States/UTs under Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) during 2019-20 to 2021-22 by National CAMPA is enclosed in Annexure -I. (b) & (c)  The CAMPA fund are being utilized by States/UTs for compensating the loss of forest & tree cover, ecological services and hydrological regime due to diversion of forest land by undertaking afforestation activities, and various activities for protection of forests, biodiversity and wildlife as per Annual Plan of Operations duly approved by the Executive Committee of National CAMPA. Plantations taken up under CAMPA fund are regularly monitored by State/UT Forest Departments through their Forest officers, Monitoring & Evaluation Wing and independent third party agencies. Forest Survey of India carries out analysis of geo-spatial data (polygons) of plantations uploaded by the State/UT Forest Departments over e-Green Watch portal for accuracy of location, area and year of plantation. Further, the Ministry takes appropriate action on the complaints received regarding utilization of CAMPA fund in the States/UTs through the concerned State/UT Government. *****
