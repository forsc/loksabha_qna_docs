                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

285






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






PMMSY in Bihar




Dileshwar Kamait














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the State of Bihar has been benefited under the Pradhan MantriMatsyaSampadaYojana (PMMSY); and(b)	  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasonstherefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES,ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a)	and (b):	A Statement is placed on the Table of the House.***** Statement referred to in reply to the LokSabha Starred Question No †*285 put in by Shri DileshwarKamait, Honourable Member of Parliament for answer on 16thMarch, 2021 regarding PMMSY in Bihar.(a) and (b): Yes Sir. The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan MantriMatsyaSampadaYojana (PMMSY) - to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with highest ever investment of Rs.20050 crore in fisheries sector in all the States/Union Territories including Bihar for a period of 5 years with effect from the financial year 2020-21. Under the PMMSY, an indicative investment of Rs.1390 crore has been envisaged for development of fisheries and aquaculture in Bihar during the period of five years.During the current financial year (2020-21), the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has accorded approval to the proposal of Government of Bihar at a total cost of Rs.183.32crore under the PMMSY for development of fisheries in the State. The approved activities primarily include construction of hatcheries, rearing ponds, grow-out ponds with inputs, biofloc ponds with inputs, stocking of fingerlings in reservoirs and wetlands, ornamental fish culture, Re-circulatory Aquaculture System, installation of cages in reservoirs, ice plants, fish transport vehicles, fish markets, fish feed plants, mobile fish kiosks, MatsyaSeva Kendra, brood bank, aquatic referral labs etc. *****
