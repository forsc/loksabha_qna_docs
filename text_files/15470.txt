                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4049






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Services of IPPB




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the services offered by the India Post Payment Banks (IPPB); and (b) 	the post offices in which IPPB is able to provide its services, State/UT-wise?         






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE) (a)     India Post Payment Bank (IPPB) is a wholly owned government scheduled payments bank offering a range of products such as savings and current accounts, remittances and money transfer, direct benefit transfers, bill and utility payments, enterprise & merchant payments including Government to Citizen (G to C) payment and Aadhar Enabled Payment System (AePS). These products, and related services, are being offered across multiple channels (Counter Services, Micro-ATM, Mobile Banking App, SMS and IVR, and at the doorstep).(b)	A list indicating number of post offices in which IPPB services are being provided State/UT-wise is at Annexure-I.
