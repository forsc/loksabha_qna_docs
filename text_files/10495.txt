                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3939






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Routes under UDAN




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of number of routes and airports operationalized under UDAN as on October 31, 2019;(b) the steps taken by the Government to improve regional connectivity including the State of Karnataka under the UDAN scheme;(c) the list of un-served/underserved airports in Karnataka included under RCS -UDAN 2 and UDAN 3;(d) whether the Government has identified 19 Airports in Karnataka, including Chikmagalur under RCS and if so, the details thereof;(e) whether any route touching the State of Karnataka is proposed to be included under UDAN scheme and if so, the details thereof;(f) whether the Government is planning to include Chikmagalur in UDAN scheme and if so, the details thereof; and(g) whether the Government proposes to upgrade/modernise any airports situated in Karnataka and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (e): Under Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik), 222 RCS routes have been commenced by the Selected Airline Operators (SAOs) and details of such RCS routes are attached as Annexure-A. The RCS - UDAN has been launched to stimulate regional connectivity across the country including Karnataka by making it affordable. Flight operations from Belagavi, Hubballi, Mysuru, Vidyanagar and Kalaburgi (Gulbarga) have been commenced under RCS - UDAN. ;;Details of the routes awarded connecting the state of Karnataka till third round of bidding are attached as Annexure-B. Except Chikmagalur: Bellary, Karwar, Kolar and Kushalnagar figure in the tentative list of unserved airports of RCS - UDAN version 4.0. ;(f) and (g): RCS - UDAN is market driven scheme. The interested airlines based on their assessment of demand on particular routes submit their proposals at the time of bidding under RCS - UDAN. Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs has approved the proposal for revival of existing unserved / underserved airports / airstrips  of the State Governments, Airports Authority of India, Public Sector Undertakings and Civil Enclaves at an estimated cost of Rs. 4500 Crore. An airport which is included in the awarded routes of RCS - UDAN and requires upgradation/development for commencement of RCS operations, is developed under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme.;
