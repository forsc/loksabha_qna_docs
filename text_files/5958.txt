                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5481






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Airport in Purnea




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of works being carried out at Purnea/Chunapur airport in Bihar;(b) the names and details of the firms/ companies engaged in construction work of the said airport; and(c) the amount paid by the Government till date along with the details of work for which the said payment was made as well names of firms/ companies to whom payment were made, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Purnea Airport belongs to Indian Air Force and has runway length of 9000 feet suitable for operations of AB-321 type of aircraft. Airports Authority of India (AAI) has projected a land requirement of 50 acres to the State Government of Bihar for development of a Civil Enclave at Purnea Airport Station. So far, no construction work has been carried out by AAI at the airport.;;(b) & (c): Do not arise.
