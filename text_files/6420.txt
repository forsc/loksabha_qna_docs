                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






ESI Hospitals in Gujarat and Assam




Queen Oja













                                                    Abdul Majeed Ariff
                                                








                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number and details of ESI hospitals operating in Gujarat and Assam at present, district-wise;(b)whether the Government proposes to set up new ESI hospitals in Assam and Bhavnagar and Botad districts of Gujarat and State of Kerala including in Alappuzha and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c)the health care facilities provided by the Government to the workers in absence of ESI hospitals nearby; and(d)the details thereof along with the places selected in this regard and quantum of funds earmarked and proposed to be released in this financial year for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):		The number and details of Employees'' State Insurance Hospitals operating in Gujarat and Assam, at present, is given in the Annexure.(b):    The Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) has not received any such proposal from respective State Governments to set up new ESI hospitals in Assam, Bhavnagar and Botad (Gujarat), Kerala including Alappuzha. As per ESIC norms, an ESI hospital is proposed for setting up once the number of Insured Persons (IPs) in the area fulfills the prescribed norms.Contd..2/-::  2  ::(c):		 In absence of nearby ESI Hospitals, the health care facilities to ESI beneficiaries is provided through empanelled Government/private hospitals. Further, there also exist a provision through which beneficiaries may claim reimbursement of medical bills for services taken directly by them from Government/private Hospital. (d):		In view of reply to (b) no quantum of funds have been earmarked.		However, ESIC has earmarked an amount of Rs. 4,25,036.02 lakh towards ESI Scheme for the financial year 2019-20 for providing medical care by State Governments. Out of this, budgetary provisions for the financial year 2019-20 to the State of Assam, Gujarat and Kerala are as under:Sl. No.	State	Amount (Rs. in lakh)1.	Assam	3,660.792.	Gujarat	28,036.673.	Kerala	16,842.62*                                                  *******
