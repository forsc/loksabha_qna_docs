                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






MGNREGS Fund




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken proactive steps for bringing in more transparency in the remittances of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) Fund and for plugging the leakages of the Fund; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government for linking the job cards with the Aadhaar numbers of the beneficiaries?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system in wage payment has been adopted under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Scheme (MGNREGS) with a view to bring transparency and to minimize leakages in the system. The Government has started National Electronic Fund Management System (NeFMS) in 24 States and 1 UT for direct payment of wages into the account of MGNREGA workers. All payments under MGNREGA are routed through Public Financial Management System (PFMS) except for accounts in the Postal Department. The State Governments/UTs have been advised to get the registered households verified through Panchayat functionaries to ensure correctness in the database of the programme. (c):	The use of Aadhaar for enrolment in MGNREGS has been made mandatory to bring in more transparency in the scheme. States/ UTs have been advised to take the following steps:(i)	Complete the Aadhaar based authentication of the beneficiaries covered under MGNREGA.(ii)	The beneficiaries who have provided the Aadhaar number but the same has not been validated, such cases may be expeditiously checked and got verified; and(iii)	To address the failures of Aadhaar based authentication, alternative methods for identifying such persons shall be adopted after finding the causes of failure in such cases.*****
