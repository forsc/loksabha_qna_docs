                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Special Textile Manufacturing Zones




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Santosh Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has any proposal to establish Special Textile Manufacturing Zones in different parts of the country in near future, especially in Bihar, North East Uttar Pradesh and nearby areas;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of textile units approved by Government so far ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d):	At present, there is no proposal to establish Special Textile Manufacturing Zone in the country.  However, the Government is implementing the Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP) which provides support for creation of world-class infrastructure facilities for setting up of textile units, with a Government of India grant upto 40% of the project cost with ceiling limit of Rs. 40.00 crores for each park. The Scheme is a demand driven scheme. The approved textile units in Textiles Parks set up across the country are at Annexure.
