                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

455






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Production and Consumption of Fish




Basanta Kumar Panda













                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the production and need based consumption of fish in the country, State-wise including Kalahandi and Naupada districts of the State of Odisha;(b) whether the Government has made appropriate provisions for storage of fisheries in the State of Odisha;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps taken/being taken to generate employment opportunities in the field of fisheries and to provide training to the farmers for the said purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING (Dr. L MURUGAN)(a): The fish production in the country stood all time high at 141.64 lakh tonnes during 2019-20 and the state/UT wise details of fish production and per capita fish consumption reported during the year 2019–20 is furnished at Annexure- I.The details of fish production and consumption of fish in Kalahandi and Nuapada districts of Odisha are given below:District	Production	ConsumptionKalahandi	0.28 Lakh MT	0.28 Lakh MTNuapada	0.06 Lakh MT	0.06 Lakh MT(b) to (d): The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)” with highest ever investment of Rs. 20050 crores in the fisheries sector over a period of five years up to 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories. The scheme intends to addresscritical gaps in fish production and productivity, quality, technology, post-harvest infrastructure and management, modernization and strengthening of value chain, traceability, enhancement of fisheries export, establishing a robust fisheriesmanagement framework, training of capacity building and fisher’swelfare. PMMSY aims to generate 55 lakh direct and indirect employment opportunities. During the financial year 2020-21, the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has accorded approval to the proposal of Government of Odisha at a total cost of Rs. 216.25 crore with central share of Rs. 68.84 crore under the PMMSY. The approved project of 2020-21 amongst the others also setting up 3 cold storages / ice plants and modernization of 4 cold storages/ ice plants, supply of 10 refrigerated vehicles and 35 insulated vehicles and investment of Rs. 1600 lakhs approved for these post-harvest related activities.*****
