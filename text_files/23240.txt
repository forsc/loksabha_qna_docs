                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Criterial to Declare Structures/Sites as ASI Protected Monuments




Arvind Dharmapuri














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government  has a determined criteria to declare structures/ sites as “ASI Protected Monuments”;(b)	the number of monuments which are declared to be of national importance in the State of Telangana;(c)	whether the Government has drawn any plans for the conservation and development of the protected monuments in the State of Telangana for posterity and to make them as popular tourist destinations and if so, the details thereof; and (d)	The details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred on conservation, and development on each protected monument in the State of Telangana during the last 10 years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENTCHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Yes sir. Criterion to declare any monument as of   National importance (centrally protected monument) is specified in the   Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958.(b)	8 Monuments/Sites are declared as of National importance in the State of Telangana. (c)	Conservation, preservation and environmental development work of the  Centrally Protected Monuments/Sites is a continuous process. Every year the Annual   Conservation Programme for Centrally Protected Monuments / Sites in the country including State   of Telangana  is  drawn as per requirement, on case to case basis, depending upon the availability of funds and   resources. Due to regular care by the ASI the protected monuments in   the country including Telangana are in a good state of preservation.   (d)	The expenditure   incurred on conservation, preservation and environmental development of   centrally protected monuments in Telangana is given at Annexure.
