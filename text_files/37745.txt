                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4987






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Fund for Development of Defence Technology




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any provision of corpus for the Technology Development Fund set up to encourage participation of public/private industries in the field of Defence Technology development;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of grants that have been made available to the private sector, especially the Micro Small & Medium Enterprises sector for developing Research & Development in the defense technology?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a): The Government had announced an initial sum of Rs. 100 Cr to set up Technology Development Fund (TDF) vide para 143 of Union Budget 2014-15 to provide necessary assistance to industries specially MSMEs and Startups in the field of defence technology development.  (b): Under the Technology Development Fund (TDF) Scheme, public/private industries especially MSMEs and Startups are funded upto 90% of total project value upto Rs. 10 Crore as grant-in-aid for development of cutting edge technologies, that are currently not available in the country.  (c):  At present, a total of 30 projects have been awarded to various MSMEs under TDF scheme for development of the technologies.*****
