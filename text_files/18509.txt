                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Digitisation of Anganwadi Centres (AWCs)




Devendrappa Y














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering a proposal for effective monitoring of Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) across the country through digitisation;(b)	if so, the extent to which digitisation has been achieved, State-wise;(c)	the proposed timeline and the strategies implemented under this project, State-wise; and(d)	the steps taken by Union Government and the State Governments to improve nutrition, literacy and numeracy in Anganwadi Centres? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) to (c)  Currently, the Ministry runs the mobile based job-aid  Application (ICDS-CAS) in an attempt   to use technology for improved  service delivery. This system covers only 351 districts of the country.  State-wise status of implementation of ICDS-CAS is at Annexure-I.  ICDS-CAS is not integrated with Mother & Child  Tracking System/Reproductive Child Health (RCH) portal of MoHFW.  The Ministry of WCD has conceptualized a digital platform," Poshan Tracker",   which will be an overarching system, providing facilities, services and interlinkages, and thereby also promote real time data with analytics.  (d)    The child beneficiaries under the Anganwadi Services Scheme are provided Supplementary Nutrition as per the guidelines of the Scheme. Monthly Village Health and Sanitation Day and Community Based Events are organised by States/UTs to improve status of nutrition of children. In addition,children (3-6 years) are provided non-formal Pre-School Education service in all AWCs. Funds @ Rs. 5,000/- per AWC per annum is provided to States/UTs for imparting non-formal pre-school education to the children.  *****  Annexure-IStatement referred in reply to part (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.2251 for 23.09.2020State-wise status of ICDS-CAS UsersSl.
