                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

88






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Child Health and Nutrition Mission




Harish Dwivedi













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    whether the Government has launched Child Health and Nutrition Mission (CNM) or Nutrition campaign across the country and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra;(b)	the details of work being undertaken by the Government under this mission/campaign;(c)	whether the Government has reviewed the success of mission across the country, if so, the details and the outcome thereof;(d)	whether the Government has received any information regarding the challenges faced by the authorities in implementing the said mission; and (e)	if so, the details of the corrective measures taken to remove those shortcomings/challenges?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	Government of India has set up POSHAN Abhiyaan on 18.12.2017 for a three year time frame commencing from 2017-18 with an aim to reduce stunting in children (0-6 years), under-nutrition (underweight prevalence) in children (0-6 years) and Low Birth Weight@ 2% p.a. and reduce anaemia among young children(6-59 months), women and adolescent girls @ 3% p.a. All the districts of the country have been covered under this Mission.The details of work undertaken under this Mission are ensuring convergence with various programmes; incentivizing States/UTs for achieving goals; IT enabled Real Time Monitoring (ICT-RTM); Evaluation; weighing efficiency and making nutrition visible; community mobilization awareness advocacy;  IEC, Jan Andolan- to educate the people on nutritional aspects, on-line course on Nutrition for Children, etc. (c):	Since POSHAN Abhiyaan has been set up only on 18.12.2017, no review of the success of the Mission has been carried out so far.(d) & (e):	Challenges/difficulties faced by the States/UTs in implementing the scheme are addressed through meetings, video conferences, etc.*****
