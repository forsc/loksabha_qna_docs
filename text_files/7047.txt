                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Smart Name Plates




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Government is proposing to install smart name plates       in every house in the smart cities by which the property tax,        electricity and water bills and even garbage collection can be        tracked; (b)  if so, the details along with the selected smart cities in the priority       list where such arrangements will be made; and(c)  the details of the facilities likely to be linked to the smart name      plates?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (c) The objective of Smart Cities Mission (SCM) is to promote cities that provide core infrastructure such as adequate water supply, assured electricity supply, sanitation including solid waste management, efficient urban mobility and public transport, etc. to give a clean and sustainable environment and a decent quality of life to its citizens through application of `Smart’ solutions. The implementation of the SCM at the City level is done by a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) created for the purpose. The SPV plan, appraise, approve, release funds, implement, manage, operate, monitor and evaluate the Smart City development projects. A total of 5,151 different projects, at an estimated cost of Rs. 2,05,018 crores, have been included by the 100 selected Cities in their Smart City proposals. Some Smart Cities i.e. Ujjain, Jalandhar, Amritsar & Bhopal have reported projects related to installation of Smart Name Plates which may be linked to collection of electricity bills, water bills, property tax, license fees etc in these Smart Cities.                                            ……
