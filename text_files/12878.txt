                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1835






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Schools and Hostels in Raigad




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Primary, Secondary, High School, Higher Secondary School andashrams/hostels being run in Raigad Lok Sabha constituency;(b) the arrangements made for the security in girls hostels and whether regular inspectionsare carried out by the concerned authorities; and(c) the sanctioned strength of principals and teachers and the number of posts lying vacant inthese institutions by the time by which these vacancies will be filled?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): As per U-DISE data of Ministry of Human Resource Development for the year 2017-18,there are 2100 primary schools, 173 Upper primay schools (grades 1 to 8), 73 Higher SecondarySchools (grades 1 to 12), 918 Upper primary only with grades 6 to 8, 7 Higher Secondary withgrades 6 to 12 and 36 Secondary/ Senior Secondary with grades 1 to 10. There are 27 Aashramprimary schools, 7 Aashram Upper primary schools, 2 Aashram Secondary and 2 AashramHigher Secondary schools. Also there are 3 Aashram Secondary/ Senior Secondary (grades 6 to10), 111 Aashram Secondary/ Senior Secondary (grades 9 & 10) and 159 Aashram HigherSecondary schools (grades 9 to 12).(b): Most of the States have developed guidelines /instructions to provide safeenvironment for girls so that they may pursue and complete their education in a fearlessenvironment. MHRD has also issued a set of Guidelines on Safety and Security ofchildren vide letter No. 10-11/2014-EE.4 dated 9th October 2014 and these guidelinesare available on the official website of Ministry of Human Resource DevelopmentThe letter is accessible through the following link:-https://mhrd.gov.in/hi/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/upload_document/Guidelines_oct.pdf(c): As informed by State Government of Maharashtra, the sanctioned strength ofPrincipals, teachers and vacancies in Raigad Lok Sabha constituency are as under:-<pre>Sanctioned Working VacantHeadMasterTeachers HeadTeachersTeachers HeadmasterTeacherZilaParishadschools140 4547 104 4197 36 350PrivateAidedschools18 224 18 196 0 18</pre>As informed by State Government of Maharashtra, vacancies of teachers are filledby ‘Pavitra Portal’ under central process and vacancies of Head Master are filled bypromotion of teachers. All administrative matters including recruitment of teachers is thesubject matter of State Government and Central Government has no role in it.*****
