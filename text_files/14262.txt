                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Sports Quota




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has reviewed the sports quota vacancies to be filled up in public sector in the country;(b) if so, the total number of vacancies to be filled up in various sectors as on date; and(c) the details of measures taken by the Government for filling up these vacancies?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (c): The general policy of sports quota for recruitment of meritorious sportspersons in Central Government Offices is dealt/reviewed by the Department of Personnel and Training (DoP&T) in consultation with concerned stakeholders, including the Department of Sports. Instructions/guidelines on incentives viz. recruitment, out of turn promotion, special leave, etc. to meritorious sportsperson under sports quota issued by DoP&T, are followed by all Ministries/Departments in the Government of India, including the Department of Sports. Moreover, the data relating to vacancies filled up in various Government Departments/Sectors under sports quota is not maintained by the Department of Sports.*****
