                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






AMRUT in Rajasthan




Balak Nath














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any scheme named by ‘AMRUT Yojana’ is being 	implemented by the Government;(b) 	if so, the name of the selected cities of Rajasthan there under;(c) 	the funds sanctioned and released under this scheme; and(d) 	the details of its completion status, city-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a).	Yes, Sir. Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) was launched on 25 June 2015 in 500 selected cities across the country.  The Mission focuses on the development of basic urban infrastructure in the sectors of water supply, sewerage and septage management, storm water drainage, non-motorised urban transport and development of green spaces and parks. Mission also mandates a set of 11 Reforms for all the Mission cities and Capacity Building activities for the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs).The Mission was launched for the period of 5 years from the financial year 2015-16 to the year 2019-20, which has been extended up to 31 March 2021. (b).	List of 29 cities of Rajasthan covered under AMRUT scheme has been given in Annexure-I.  (c).	For the State of Rajasthan, State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) amounting to ?3,223.94 crore, which includes committed Central Assistance of ?1,541.95 crore, have been approved for the entire Mission period. So far, Central Assistance of ?1,457.97 crore have been released to the Government of Rajasthan for the implementation of AMRUT projects. (d).	The city-wise details of projects taken up under AMRUT in the State of Rajasthan are given at Annexure – I.                                             *****
