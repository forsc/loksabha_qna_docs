                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Norms and Standards of Skill Development




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Institutions registered under Skill Development Initiative of the Ministry hitherto;(b)	the details of the Institutions as referred to above that are functional and imparting requisite skills training as per extant norms/standards; and(c)	whether any proposal is under consideration of the Ministry to impart entrepreneurship training so as to broaden the gamut of employment generation and if so, the steps taken in this direction?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing its demand driven scheme, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20, for imparting skill development training in Short Term Training (STT) courses and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) across the country. STT training is being imparted through empanelled training centres (TCs) / training providers (TPs). For ensuring quality of training, empanelment of TCs/TPs are done through an online portal, SMART (Skill Management and Accreditation of Training Centre) with inspection done by independent third party assessment agencies. As on 17.01.2020, 13,209 TCs/TPs were empanelled under the scheme for STT courses.(b)	Training under the scheme has been done in all empanelled TCs/TPs i.e. 13,209. As on date, training is being undergoing in 1,264 TCs / TPs.(c)	Under PMKVY (2016-20), in order to inculcate entrepreneurship in the candidates, training in English, Employability & Entrepreneurship (EEE) module of 155 hours is being imparted in addition to core curriculum of course. Further, TPs provide support for entrepreneurship development through facilitating bank linkages along with providing guidance and mentoring support.In addition, PMKKs are designated as Hand Holding Agencies for MUDRA loans on Udyamimitra Portal (SIDBI). Also, a MoU has been signed between National Skill Development Corporation and National Centre for Excellence of RSETIs (NACER) to enhance self employment training and handholding.
