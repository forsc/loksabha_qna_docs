                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4549






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Average Download and Upload Speed




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recorded the average download and upload speed in Mega Bytes Per Second (MBps) in urban cities and rural areas; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) 	the minimum download speed in MBps for 4G internet services to be provided by cellular broadband services in the country; (d) 	whether the Government has announced an increase in the minimum download speed to be provided by broadband services for 4G; (e) 	if so, the details thereof; (f) 	whether the Government shall pay consumers for the subsequent increase in the minimum download speed provided and if so, the details thereof; and (g) 	whether this will result in an increase in the out of-pocket expenditure for consumers in the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b)	Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has been obtaining Crowd-sourced data about download and upload speed for different service providers through TRAI My speed App. Average download & upload speed License Service Area (LSA) wise across India for Quarter (Oct 21- Dec 21) is placed as Annexure. No separate information is available for urban/rural areas.(c)	No minimum download speed for 4G internet services has been specified. However, the speed of mobile internet is dependent on number of factors such as distance of user from the cell site, the number of users being served by the cell, traffic handled by the cell, the user equipment/mobile handset etc. (d)	No Sir.(e) to (g)	Does not arise.
