                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1393






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Drinking Water Projects under MGNREGS




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any project is being implemented under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) to provide safe drinking water;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including West Bengal; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven scheme with bottom up approach. The works are approved & prioritized by the Gram Sabha at the Gram Panchayat level. Hence every year, the action plan (shelf of Work) of the permissible works are prepared & approved by the Gram Sabha under the scheme and can be taken up as per the demand only.  As per the Schedule I of Mahatma Gandhi NREGA, Para 4 (1),I. Category: A : Public works relating to Natural Resource Management (i) Water Conservation and water harvesting structures to augment and improve ground water like underground dykes, earthen dams, stop dams, check dams with focus on recharging ground water including drinking water sources are permissible activities that can be taken up for implementation.  (b): As per information uploaded by the States/UTs including West Bengal in the Management Information System (MIS), the ground water recharge & water conservation  works for the drinking water source  taken up under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the year 2019-20 (as on 3rd February, 2020) are at Annexure. (c): Does not arise.
