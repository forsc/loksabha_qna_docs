                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1114






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Digitalization of Education




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes digitalization of education in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether there is any programme of action planned in this regard; and (c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a)	to (c) National Education Policy - 2020 has detailed provisions for online and digital education which, inter-alia, includes creation of National Educational Technology Forum (NETF) as a platform for free exchange of ideas on the use of technology to enhance learning, assessment, planning, administration and so on both for School and Higher Education. The details can be accessed on – https://www.mhrd.gov.in/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/NEP_Final_English_0.pdf***
