                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Foreign Exchange Earned Through Medical Tourism




Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of tourists that have come to India as medical tourists during the last five years and details thereof, State-wise; (b)	the amount of foreign exchange earnings generated by Medical Tourism during the last five years and the details thereof, State-wise;(c)	the steps being taken, if any, to create awareness and attract more medical tourists to India; and (d)	whether the Government proposes, to create a dedicated medical tourism policy and if so, the details thereof?   






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM             		       (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	 The details of Foreign Tourists Arrivals (FTAs) in India on Medical purpose during 2016 to 2020 are given below:Foreign Tourists Arrivals (FTAs) in India on Medical purpose<pre>Year	FTAs on medical purpose (in Lakh)2016	4.27 2017	4.952018	6.412019	6.97 2020	1.83 </pre>Source: Bureau of Immigration, Government of IndiaFurther, Ministry of Tourism, Government of India does not maintain data on State/UT wise visits of foreign tourists according to purpose and foreign exchange earnings generated from these visits. However, the overall Foreign Exchange Earnings through tourism during 2016 to 2020 are given below:Foreign Exchange Earnings from tourism in India <pre>Year	FEE from tourism (in Rs. Crore)2016 #2	1541462017#2	1778742018#2	1948812019#2	2116612020#2	50136</pre>#2 Revised estimates(c) & (d):	 In order to create a robust framework and synergy amongst the Central Government Ministries, State Governments and Private Sector for promoting India as a Medical and Wellness tourism destination through a dedicated agency to create awareness and attract more medical tourists to India, a draft National Strategy and Roadmap for Medical and Wellness Tourism has been formulated by Ministry of Tourism. In order to make the document more comprehensive, Ministry of Tourism has invited feedback/ comments/ suggestions on the draft National Strategy and Roadmap from identified Central Ministries, all the State Governments/UT Administrations and industry stakeholders.Further, Ministry of Tourism has taken several steps to create awareness and attract more medical tourists to India, which are at Annexure.******
