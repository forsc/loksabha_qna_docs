                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4015






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Industrial Clusters




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is developing industrial clusters in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana including incubation center with joint ventures in Kakinada, East Godawari district in Andhra Pradesh and if so, the details thereof and progress made; and (b) 	the role of NASSCOM in setting up of warehouses in Vishakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh and progress made under Joint Venture with Andhra Pradesh Government?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ???????????? ( ???? ??? ??????) THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE &INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a)	Yes, Sir. Government has developed the following Industrial Clusters under Industrial Infrastructure Up-gradation Scheme (IIUS)/ Modified IIUS (MIIUS) in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana:1.	Auto Components Clusters, Vijaywada2.	Up-gradation of Hindupur Growth Centre & IP Gollapuram, Anantpur3.	Up-gradation of Industrial Growth Centre, Bobbili, Vizianagaram4.	Pharma Cluster, Hyderabad5.	Pashamylaran Industrial Area, MedakThere is no project under IIUS/MIIUS scheme in Kakinada and East Godavari District in Andhra Pradesh.(b)	As per the information provided by NASSCOM, they have set up the Startup warehouse under its 10,000 Startups initiative in Vishakhapatnam in an agreement with the Andhra Pradesh Government in 2016 and since then have incubated 33 startups both physically and virtually combined helping them with initial mentoring, handholding technical sessions, investor connect and mentor connects. Startups has also got chance to exhibit at various NASSCOM events for market access.******
