                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2835






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






CGHS facilities in Karnataka




Prajwal Revanna














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of criteria fixed for selection of cities under CGHS;(b) the details of cities where CGHS wellness centers have been established during last three years;(c) whether the Government proposes to establish CGHS wellness center in Mysore city and if so, the number of beneficiaries including serving and pensioners likely to be benefited there from; and(d) whether the Government proposes to establish wellness centers in other tier 2 cities of Karnataka namely Mangalore, Hubli-Dharwad, Gulbarga and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): As per the criteria for opening a new CGHS Wellness Centre in a new city, there has to be a minimum of 6000 card holders.(b): New CGHS Wellness Centres have been established at Varanasi, Baghpat, Gwalior, Sonipat, Kochi, Kannur, Kozhikode, Faridabad(new) and  Srinagar during last three years.In addition, CGHS has taken over 33 Postal dispensaries located at the following places w.e.f. 01/04/2019:- Agra, Ajmer, Aligarh, Ambala, Amritsar,  Bareilly, Behrampur, Chappra, Cuttack, Darbhanga, Dhanbad, Dibrugarh, Gaya, Gorakhpur, Guntur, Jallandhar , Jalpaiguri, Jodhpur, Kota, Moradabad, Muzzafarupur, Nellore, Raipur, Rajamundri, Saharanpur, Silchar, Siliguri, Tirunelveli, Tiruchirapalli, Vadodra, Varanasi, Vijaywada and  Vishakahapatnam.(c) : Yes. The number of beneficiaries likely to be benefitted from the proposed Wellness Centre at Mysore is around 20,000. (d): No.
