                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3205






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Funds Allocatation for Sports




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds allocated and spent by the Government on various sports disciplines and funds allocated and spent by the Government on various sports scheme during the last three years;(b)	whether it is a fact that Hockey has been sanctioned funds disproportionate to other sports disciplines despite the fact that it is not the national game in the country;(c)	whether it is a fact that sports disciplines which are most popular in the country and world over, are neglected to favour Hockey; and(d)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a)	Assistance to training and competition of sportspersons and teams is done through many schemes of the Ministry and the expenditurefor activities namely,the participation in international tournaments, the conduct of national championships, the conduct of international tournaments in India, procurement of sports equipments, etc, is primarily incurred from the funds under the Scheme of Assistance to National Sports Federations(NSFs). The funds under the Scheme of Assistance to NSFs are sanctioned and released to NSFs and other concerned stakeholders towards meeting expenses on eligible components as per the norms of the Scheme. The details of funds released and expenditure incurred on the Scheme of Assistance to NSFs, as well as, the funds released to NSFs are given at Annexure-I. Further,customized training for core and developmental group athletesis supported under TargetOlympic Podium Scheme (TOPS), for which funds are released as per the requirements of the athletes.The details of expenditure incurred under TOPS is given at Annexure-II. Under Khelo India Scheme, Talent Search and Development vertical has the mandate foridentification of talent and nurturing them to attain high levels ofachievement. The funds allocated and the expenditure incurred under Khelo India Scheme is given at Annexure-III. Details of funds allocated and spent by the Government on various other sports schemesare given at Annexure-IV.(b)	to (d) Funds are allocated to eligible National Sports Federations for promotion and development of the sport for which it has been recognized. Government adopted dynamic criteria for categorization of sports disciplines which was based on the performance of disciplines in recognized international events like Olympic/Asian and Commonwealth Games, etc. so as to motivate recognized NSFs for better preparedness. Hockey has been placed in High Priority category. Hockey has been provided financial assistance for the conduct of coaching camps, international exposure, engagement of Indian and Foreign coaches, purchase of equipment, etc. Funding is provided based on the evaluation done in the Annual Calendar for Training and Competition (ACTC) Meeting where the NSFs present their requirements.
