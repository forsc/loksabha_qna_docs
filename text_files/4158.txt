                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3784






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Upliftment of Women of Backward Communities




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any report on literacy and employment rate of women belonging to socially and educationally backward communities; and (b) if so, the details thereof along with the various ongoing projects at present for the empowerment of women belonging to socially and educationally most backward communities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a): No such evaluation has been done in the Ministry.(b): National Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC), an organization under the aegis of Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has the following two women specific schemes for targeted women whose annual family income is less than Rs. 3.00 lakh per annum:(i)    Mahila Samriddhi YojanaTo provide Micro Finance  to women entrepreneurs of Backward Classes.(ii)    New Swarnima Scheme for WomenTerm Loan to inculcate the spirit of self-dependence among the women of Backward Classes****
