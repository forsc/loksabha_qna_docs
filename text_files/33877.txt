                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1598






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2022






Subsidy on LPG




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of number of LPG gas connection given under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY), State-wise; (b) the details of LPG gas connection given under PMUY in Kerala, district-wise; (c) whether the Government has initiated action for providing subsidy to the consumers who have taken gas connection under PMUY; (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(e) whether the Government proposes to credit the subsidy amount to the consumers under PMUY with arrears and if so, the action taken thereon; and (f) whether the Government proposes to consider the grievances of the poor consumers converted into LPG under the scheme due to the hike in price of cooking gas and stopping the crediting of subsidy amount and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)  State-wise details of LPG connections released under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) as on 01.02.2022 are at Annexure-I. (b)  District-wise details of LPG connections released under PMUY in the State of Kerala as on 01.02.2022 are at Annexure-II.(c) to (e) All the domestic consumers including consumers enrolled under PMUY get refills at non-subsidised price and the applicable subsidy is directly transferred into their registered bank accounts. (f)  To reduce upfront cash outgo for poor consumers, Oil Marketing Companies(OMCs) have given an option of 5 kg cylinder LPG connection. Further, Prices of LPG in the country are based on Saudi Contract (CP), the benchmark for international prices of LPG. Saudi CP has rises approximately 258 % from April, 2020 to November 2021 (236 USD/MT to 846 USD/MT). However, for domestic LPG, the Government continues to modulate the effective price to consumer to insulate the common man from rise in international prices.
