                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Neem Coated Urea




Gautam Gambhir














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the percentage of neem coated urea and pesticides being produced in Government owned facilities; and (b) whether it is a fact that the Government is facing shortage of neem oil used as input and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS( D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a):    Department of Fertilizers on 25th May 2015, made it mandatory for all the indigenous producers of urea to produce 100% of their total production of subsidized urea as neem coated urea. Entire quantity of indigenously produced urea and imported urea is being neem coated w.e.f. 1st September, 2015 and w.e.f. 1st December, 2015 respectively. The percentage of neem coated urea being produced in Government owned facilities ( CPSE) with respect to the total indigenous production of Neem Coated Urea in the country during the years 2017-18 onwards are as under:Year	    Urea ( Fig. In ‘LMT’)	                                       % age of Public Sector	                     Total Indigenous Production    By  Public Sector	2017-18	                     240.23	                           69.88	     29.09 %2018-19	                     240.00	                           70.16	     29.23 %2019-20 ( up to Nov. 19)      159.57	                           42.09	     26.38 %HIL ( India) Ltd. Is the only CPSE under administrative control of the Department of Chemicals & Petrochemicals that produces pesticides. The production of 43 major pesticides and insecticides (Techinical) in the country in 2018-19 was. 2,17,607 MT, out of which HIL’s production was 3350.05 MT i.e. 1.54% of the total production.  (b):	No shortage of Neem oil used as input for the production of Neem Coated Urea, has been reported upon.*********
