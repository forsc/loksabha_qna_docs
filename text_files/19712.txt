                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Free Coaching Scheme for SC and OBC Students




T. R. Paarivendhar













                                                    A. Ganeshamurthi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1215 TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021FREE COACHING SCHEME FOR SC AND OBC STUDENTS 1215.     DR. T.R. PAARIVENDHAR:               SHRI A. GANESHAMURTHI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Union Government is implementing free coaching scheme for Scheduled Caste (SC) and Other Backward Classes (OBC) students across the country to compete in the competitive examinations for various recruitments; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details regarding the type of coaching and eligibility for taking free coaching by SC and OBC students; (d) the total number of students benefitted under the coaching scheme during the last three years, State-wise; (e) the total number of study centres in Tamil Nadu giving such free coaching to these students, district-wise; and (f) the total number of SC and OBC students from Tamil Nadu who have enrolled and availed free coaching under this scheme, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c):  Yes, Sir. This department is implementing the Free Coaching Scheme for Scheduled Caste (SC) and Other Backward Classes (OBC) students to enable them to appear in competitive exams for obtaining admission in technical and professional courses and for securing appropriate jobs in public/private sector.Students belonging to SCs and OBCs having total family income from all sources of          Rs.8.00 lakh per annum or less are eligible for assistance under the scheme. In addition to the course fees as per entitlement for the course under scheme guidelines, eligible students are also paid a monthly stipend of Rs. 3000 per student for local and Rs. 6000 per student for outstation students.(d): Under this scheme, available slots are allotted amongst the institutes empanelled under the Scheme. The details of state-wise empanelled institutes and total number of students benefitted during the last 3 years is attached. (e) and (f):  In Tamil Nadu, one institute in Chennai district was empanelled during 2016-17 for under the Free Coaching Scheme and during the last 3 years 300 students were enrolled and availed free coaching from this institute under this scheme.***** AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply to parts (d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1215 dated 09.02.2021 regarding ‘Free Coaching Scheme for SC and OBC Students’Number of Institutes Empanelled Under the Free Coaching Scheme for SC and OBC students and students enrolled thereinS.No	Name of the state 	Number of Institutes	Total number of SC and OBCstudents enrolled during the last 3 years1	Assam	1	2002	Bihar 	1	3003	Chandigarh	2	5004	Delhi 	8	18505	Gujarat 	3	6006	Haryana 	1	3007	Himachal Pradesh	1	2008	Jammu and Kashmir 	1	3009	Karnataka 	2	40010	Madhya Pradesh 	5	110011	Maharashtra 	5	300*12	Manipur 	1	30013	Meghalaya	1	10014	Odisha 	2	40015	Punjab	3	60016	Rajasthan 	3	90017	Tamil Nadu 	2	50018	Tripura 	1	10019	Uttar Pradesh 	2	500*4 Institutes of Maharashtra have not reported the number of enrolled students. *****
