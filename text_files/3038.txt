                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2728






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Cases Sent by CBI to Anti-Corruption Bureau




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the various complaints/ cases received by the CBI are likely to be sent/forwarded to the Anti-Corruption Bureau of the concerned States; and (b) 	if so, the number of complaints sent to the concerned States by the CBI from 1st January, 2013 to 1st May, 2019 along with the details therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b):   Yes Sir, CBI has sent 7139 number of unregistered complaints to the Anti-Corruption Bureau/Anti Corruption Division/Anti Corruption Organization of concerned States from 1st January, 2013 to 1st May, 2019. The year-wise break up of 7139 complaints sent by CBI to Anti-Corruption Bureau/Anti Corruption Division/Anti Corruption Organization of States is as given below.YEAR WISE NUMBER OF UNREGISTERED COMPLAINTS SENT TO ANTI CORRUPTION BUREAU /DIVISION /ORGANISATION OF STATESYEAR	2013	2014	2015	2016	2017	2018	2019	TOTALNumber of complaints sent to Anti - Corruption Bureau/ Anti Corruption Division/ Anti Corruption Organization  of States by CBI.	1116	895	924	958	1458	1376	412	7139*****
