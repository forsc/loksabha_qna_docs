                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

999






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Details regarding PMJVK




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of the offices which are looking after the facilities provided under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) along with the schemes which have been announced under PMJVK, State/district-wise; (b)  the details of the officers under PMJVK, criteria for appointment, their emoluments, State-wise; and (c) the details of the schools/colleges, health centres, hostels, drinking water facilities, polytechnics, ITIs, market sheds for artisans-farmers, sadbhav mandaps, job-oriented skill development centres, hunar hubs, constructed under PMJVK along with construction cost for each, date of construction along with the date of registration, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	& (b):  The Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented by this Ministry in 1300 identified Minority Concentration Areas of the country.  The proposals are sanctioned by the Ministry and are implemented by State Governments/UT Administrations through the existing state machinery which also includes manpower.  (c)	The Ministry has sanctioned during the period 2014-19, a number of projects under the scheme which includes 25 Degree Colleges, 1152 School Buildings, 20228 Additional Class Rooms, 506 Hostels, 71 ITIs, 5 Polytechnics, 411 Sadbhav Mandaps, 92 Residential Schools, 530 Market Sheds, 5090 Anganwadi Centres, 821 Health Projects, 2285 Water Supply Projects, 11676 Pucca Houses etc. State-wise details of the projects are available on the Ministry’s website at http://www.minorityaffairs.gov.in.***
