                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

260






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Hunar Hubs




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik













                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the country to train and encourage indigenous talent of master artisans, craftsmen as per the needs of the market;(b)	if so, the details and the salient features thereof along with the time by which whether the Government has decided to establish ‘Hunar Hubs’ in every State of these are likely to be established;(c)	the number of such hubs sanctioned/established by the Government in different parts of the country so far and business done in these hubs;(d)	the number of master artisans, craftsmen and culinary experts including women who have been provided employment and employment opportunities during each of the last three years; and(e)	whether the Government will provide training to master artisans, craftsmen and traditional culinary experts as per the modern needs in these ‘Hunar Hubs’ and if so, the details thereof along with the names of places where these hubs will be established in the near future, State/ location-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI) (a) to (e):   A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (e) of LokSabha Starred Question No. *260 by Shri Sanjay Sadashiv Rao Mandlikand Shri KhagenMurmu Members of Parliament regarding Hunar Hubs.(a) to (e):  The Ministry of Minority Affairs has included construction of Hunar Hubs as one of the activities under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) in identified Minority Concentration District Headquarters.  The Hunar Hubs are sanctioned by this Ministry for enabling artisans/entrepreneurs to carry out exhibitions/melas/food-courts/skill training etc.   The assets are to be maintained and managed on self-sustaining basis by the Minority Welfare Department/District Administration of the State/UT concerned.  Proposals for construction of Hunar Hubs are forwarded by the State Government/UT administration as per their requirement for consideration of the Empowered Committee on PMJVK.   Till date, 04 Hunar Hubs have been sanctioned on the basis of proposals received from the States of Kerala, Maharashtra and Meghalaya and their details are as given below:-(Rs. in lakh)State	District	Minority Concentration Area	Total Cost of the Project	1stInstallment of Central Share ReleasedMeghalaya	West Garo Hills 	Tura Town	531.00	143.37Kerala	Kottayam	Erattupetta Town	210.00	37.80Kerala	Kottayam	Erattupetta Town	82.00	14.76Maharashtra	Ahmednagar	Shrirampur Town	57.00	10.26Further, Hunar Haats are organised by this Ministry under “Upgrading the Skill and Training in Traditional Arts/Crafts for Development” (USTTAD) scheme to give an effective platform to minority artisans and culinary experts from across the country to showcase and market their finest handicraft and exquisitely crafted indigenous handmade products.  So far, 14 Hunar Haats have been organized since November, 2016 at the following venues:-(a)	India International Trade Fair (IITF), New Delhi in 2016, 2017, 2018 & Nov. 2019,(b)	Baba Kharak Singh Marg, New Delhi in Feb. 2017, Feb. 2018 & Jan. 2019, (c)	Puducherry  in Sep. 2017 and Oct. 2018, (d)	Mumbai in Jan. 2018 and Dec. 2018, (e)	Prayagraj in Sep. 2018 and November 2019, (f)	Jaipur in Aug-Sep. 2019.The Hunar Haats going to be organised in the coming days are:-S.
