                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Pending MSP Claims for Settlement




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the claims from Government of Maharashtra are pending with the Union Government for settlement of claims of procuring agencies for purchases on Minimum Support Price (MSP); (b) 	if so, the details of such claims pending for settlement since last five years; and (c) 	the measures taken by the Government for settlement of such claims?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (c): 	Admissible amount of Provisional Food Subsidy is released to Decentralized Procurement (DCP) States such as Maharashtra, based on their quarter-wise claims.            The claims received from State Government are processed  keeping in view the opening and closing balance of stock, procurement, allocation and distribution of foodgrains, Food Corporation of India (FCI)  reconciliation, utilization certificate, Economic Cost of foodgrains etc. among others.              In the last five years an amount of Rs 2862.66 crore has already been released (as on 28.02.2021) against their claims, the details of which are annexed.****ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2370 FOR ANSWER ON 09.03.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.Details of the provisional food subsidy released to Govt. of Maharashtra during last five yearsFinancial Year	Amount (Rupees in Crore)2016-17	-2017-18	583.662018-19	0.002019-20	1920.172020-21 (as on 28.02.2021)	358.83Total	2862.66Details of the provisional food subsidy claims under scrutiny in DepartmentS. No.	Details of claims submitted by Government of Maharashtra	Status1	Provisional claims  for Financial Year 2020-21  of Rs3148.92 crore	Admissible amount of Rs.1481.92 crore  against the claim of Quarter 1 of  2020-21 would be released shortly and claims for Quarter 2 and 3 are under process in consultation with State Government.*****
