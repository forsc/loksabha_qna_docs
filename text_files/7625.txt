                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1295






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Tamanna Aptitude Test




Nand Kumar Singh Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the “Tamanna” aptitude test developed by CBSE and NCERT; and(b) the details of the number of seats allotted by the Government for EWSbeneficiaries in Kendriya Vidyalayas?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and the National Council ofEducational Research & Training (NCERT) have developed TAMANNA (Try andMeasure Aptitude and Natural Abilities) - An aptitude test for Senior School Students toenable stakeholders to know the aptitude of students of Class IX & Class X. The detailsabout use of aptitude test, dimensions measured in the test, construction andstandardization of the test, administration and scoring and understanding the meaningof aptitude test scores are available in the test manual. The text booklet and manualhave been uploaded on the websites of Ministry of Human Resource Development(MHRD), CBSE and NCERT.(b): Economically Weaker Section (EWS) students are included in the 25%reservation in Class-I in Kendriya Vidyalayas as per The Right of Children to free andCompulsory Education (RTE) Act, 2009 and the Kendriya Vidyalaya SangathanAdmission Guidelines 2019-20.****
