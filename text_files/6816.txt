                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Discontinuation of Housekeeping Services




M.K. Vishnu Prasad













                                                    S. Jagathrakshakan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Railways have decided to discontinue the onboard housekeeping services and toilet maintenance, in the running trains in Southern Railways;(b)	if so, the reasons therefor; (c)	whether any alternative arrangement has been worked out to ensure cleanliness in the trains, in view of complaints of unhygienic conditions like presence of cockroaches and rats running in the trains; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) No, Sir.(b) to (d) Do not arise. *****
