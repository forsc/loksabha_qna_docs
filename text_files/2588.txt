                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2490






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Enrolment in Private Schools




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the enrolments in the private schools in the country have increased in rural areasduring the last 5 years, State-wise including Karnataka;(b) if so, the details along with the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government has conducted any survey to check whether the standard ofeducation in private schools is satisfactory;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) : Yes, Sir. The State-wise details for the last five years is as at Annexure.(c) to (e) : The National Achievement Survey (NAS) conducted for Class X, wherein samples ofprivate schools which are affiliated to the CBSE and ICSE boards were also included, have shownthe overall performance of these private schools in Class X as satisfactory.
