                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1680






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Akshay Urja Shops




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Queen Oja
                                                








                                                    Janardan Singh Sigriwal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether the Government proposes to set up ‘Akshay Urja Shops’ across the country; (b)   if so, the details thereof indicating the number of ‘Akshay Urja Shops’ across the country so far, State/ UT-wise including Haryana, Bihar and Assam; and(c)   the steps taken by the Government to expand network of ‘Akshay Urja Shops’ by   encouraging private entrepreneurs and Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) to set up and operate such shops in all the districts of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c)Currently, Government is not running any scheme to set up Akshay Urja Shops in the country. However, the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) was earlier promoting establishment of Akshay Urja Shops in major cities of the country with a view to cover sales and services of all renewable energy devices and systems in all districts of the country. The implementation of the scheme was reviewed by the Ministry and it was decided to discontinue the scheme beyond 12th Plan period. Before, discontinuation of the Scheme, total 412 Akshay Urja Shops were sanctioned by MNRE. State/UT wise numbers of Akshay Urja Shops sanctioned by the Ministry, including the States of Haryana, Bihar and Assam are given in the Annexure.*****
