                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1372






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Easy access to Monuments for Differently abled Persons




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is trying to make easy access of all the monuments/cultural heritages sites in India for the Persons with Disability;(b)    if so, whether the Government has identify some monuments/cultural  heritages where such facilities are yet to be created;(c)    if so, the details thereof State-wise;(d)    whether the Government has fixed some timeline to create such facilities in all the cultural heritages sites across the country especially in Uttar Pradesh for the easy access for the person with disability; and(e)   if so, the details thereof?   






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Yes, Sir. (b) to (e)  	Amenities like pathways, ramps, wheelchairs, brail signage, besides other facilities such as drinking water, toilets, parking cloak rooms, etc, are provided at centrally protected ticketed monuments and monuments where footfall is high.Creation of such facilities at protected monuments is based on need and feasibility and their upgradation is a continuous phenomenon.    *****
