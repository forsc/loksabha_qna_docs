                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3062






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Houses under PMAY-U




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the houses sanctioned and completed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) till date, category-wise and State-wise;(b)	the details of the funds sanctioned and utilized under the scheme till date; and(c)	whether the Government has received proposals from the State of Kerala, especially for projects in Wayanad and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                             (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	& (b) Details of State-wise and category-wise houses sanctioned and completed alongwith central assistance sanctioned and utilized/released under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana( Urban ) ( PMAY(U)) are given at Annexure.(c) Based on the Project proposals received from the State Government of Kerala, 454 projects under PMAY (U) have been sanctioned for the State for construction of 1,30,454 houses involving central assistance of Rs. 2,066.01 crore of which an amount of Rs. 1,244.75 crore (including Rs. 274.69 crore under Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS) component  of PMAY (U)) has been released. In Wayanad, 14 projects have been sanctioned under the scheme for construction of 4287 houses involving central assistance of Rs. 65.25 crore of which Rs. 41.22 crore has been released. 3372 houses in Way and have been grounded of which construction of 1980 houses have been completed.                                         *****
