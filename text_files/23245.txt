                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4245






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Beautification of Devasthan of Tribal Areas of Maharashtra




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken or proposes to take steps for beautification of Devasthan located in tribal areas across the country, particularly in Maharashtra;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government proposes to provide essential basic amenities to the devotees and to beautify the main and ancient cave and temple of Lingo Jango, deity of tribes, located in Kachargad in Salekasa Taluka of Gondiya district under the Parliamentary constituency of Gadchiroli-Chimur in Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENTCHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Conservation and preservation of centrally protected monuments/sites is a continuous process taken up as per need and requirement, case to case basis considering availability of funds and resources.(b)	Does not arise. (c)	The ancient cave and temple of Lingo Jango,   deity of tribes, located in Kachargad in Salekasa Taluka of Gondiya district   under the Parliamentary constituency of Gadchiroli-Chimur in Maharashtra are not under the care and protection of the Archaeological Survey of India.
