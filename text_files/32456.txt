                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3889






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Artificial Limbs Distribution




Sudarshan Bhagat














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is distributing artificial limbs and assistive equipments for Divyang persons on a large-scale; (b) if so, the details thereof for the last five years, year-wise and State-wise; and (c) if not, the reasons for reduction being noticed in such distribution during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK) (a). Yes, Sir. The Government is implementing a Scheme entitled ‘Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids and Appliances (ADIP)’ to provide various aids and assistive devices including artificial limbs, to eligible persons with disabilities (Divyangjan) across the country,  which can promote their physical, social and psychological empowerment by reducing the effects of disabilities and enhancing their economic potential.  (b)       The year-wise and State-wise details of beneficiaries provided aids & assistive devices including artificial limbs during the last five years under the ADIP Scheme is at Annexure.  (c)  Does not arise, in view of reply to part (b) above.  *****
