                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

347






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Station Redevelopment Programme




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has issued revised strategy for its ‘Station Redevelopment Programme’ after interacting with developers, investors and other stakeholders;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.347 BY SHRI RAJESHBHAI CHUDASAMA TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 03.02.2021 REGARDING STATION REDEVELOPMENT PROGRAMME(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. During various interactions with developers, investors and other stakeholders, various issues including longer lease period for commercially developed assets, multiple sub leasing and simplified bid procedures were raised. In response to the suggestions received from the developers, investors and other stakeholders, revised scheme for redevelopment of stations has been formulated.  Ministry of Railways, through various agencies, is undertaking techno-economic feasibility studies of Railway stations.  Based on the outcome of these feasibility studies, stations are being taken up for redevelopment in phases, especially the stations located in major cities, pilgrimage centres and important tourist destinations.  *****
