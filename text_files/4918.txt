                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4544






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Digitization of Records in KSCH




Bhartruhari Mahtab













                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Central Information Commission (CIC), in its Order dated 7 May, 2019 has directed the Kalawati Saran Children’s Hospital (KSCH), New Delhi to develop a robust mechanism for the digitization of all administrative records of the Public Authority in accordance with Section 4 of RTI Act, 2005; (b) if so, the details and the compliance thereof; and (c) the steps taken/being taken by the KSCH for digitization of administrative records so far along with the time by which such records is likely to be digitized?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Yes.(b):	A proposal to digitize administrative records has been prepared. Digitization of all hospital activities is covered in e-hospital mechanism and to implement the same, a dedicated unit has been setup.(c):	The unit which is setup with a Nodal Officer for fast track implementation of e-hospital mechanism also covers digitization of administrative records. So far following number of files/records have been digitized in Kalawati saran Children’s Hospital:a.	Service Book    -  548b.	Guard files        -  151c.	Dispatch Book  -  109
