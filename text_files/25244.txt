                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1037






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Utilisation of CSR Funds




Sougata Ray













                                                    Hemant Tukaram Godse
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of profits of nationalized oil companies of the country during the last threeyears;(b) the details of projects executed/activities undertaken by the nationalized oilcompanies/Private sector Petroleum and Natural Gas companies from their CSR fundsduring the last three years;(c) the total expenditure incurred on these projects/ activities, comapny-wise; and(d) the details of the criteria followed to select the projects?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) Year-wise details of profit earned by Public Sector Oil Companiesduring the last three years is as below :-(Value in Crore)Oil PSUs 2018-19 2019-20 2020-2168032.00 26487.00 72827.00(b)&(c) The projects/activities wise details of funds spent under CSR by PublicSector Oil Companies during the last three years are available on their respectivewebsites.(d) CSR activities are undertaken by Public Sector Oil Companies underthe heads identified under Schedule VII of the Companies Act 2013 with specialfocus on Health (Nutrition, Sanitation, and Drinking Water), Education, SkillDevelopment, Rural Development, Women Empowerment, Environment CentricInitiatives and Care for the Elderly & Differently-abled persons.****
