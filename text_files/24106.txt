                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

41






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Vaccination Programme for Schools and Colleges




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas













                                                    Diya Kumari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to check the spread of Covid-19 pandemic and to launch special vaccination programme for the students of schools and colleges; and(b) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Ministry of Home Affairs, vide its order dated 29th April, 202l has circulated the advisory issued by Ministry of Health and Family Welfare for the containment of COVID-19 in the country. As per the advisory "100 % vaccination for the eligible age-groups shall be undertaken duly creating additional vaccination centers and optimal capacity utilization of existing Centers".A National Expert Group on Vaccine Administration for COVID-19 (NEFVAC) has been established by Government of India which provides guidance on all aspects of COVID-19 vaccination including prioritization of population groups. Effective from 1st May 2021, all citizens of the country above the age 18 years are eligible for free vaccination in the Government vaccination centre and those who can afford may avail the services at the private vaccination centre. Therefore, all the students above the age of 18 years are eligible for vaccination against COVID -19.*****
