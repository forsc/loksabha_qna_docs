Beti Bachao-Beti Padhao (BBBP)




Asaduddin Owaisi













                                                     Imtiaz Jaleel Syed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The number of districts identified under Beti Bachao-Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme in Telangana and Maharashtra;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	The targets set and achieved in all the States/UTs under the scheme;(d)	The total amount allocated under the scheme to different States/UTs and utilized so far;(e)	whether the Government has reviewed the success of the scheme since its inception; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and further steps taken or being taken by the Government to ensure the success of the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                         ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b):   The number of districts covered under Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) in Telangana and Maharashtra are as under: State	Total number of districts covered under BBBP	Number of districts covered under Multi sectoral Intervention along with Advocacy and  Media  Campaign	Number of districts covered under Advocacy and  Media  CampaignTelangana	10	8	2Maharashtra	35	31	4 (c):  The overall objective of the BBBP Scheme is to address the issue of decline in Child Sex Ratio and create an enabling environment for education of the girl child.Child Sex Ratio (Census by Registrar General of India) is a decadal process. Hence, an intermediary targets i.e. Sex Ratio at Birth have been set as a monitoring parameter for the progress of Scheme. SRB (as per Health Management Information System (HMIS)  of Ministry of Health and Family Welfare) has shown an improving trend of 16 points at National level from 918 (2014-15) to 934 (2019-20). The State/UT wise SRB of 2014-15 and 2019-20 is at Annexure-I. (d):  The total amount allocated under the scheme to different States/UTs and utilized so far is at Annexure II. (e) & (f) The Government reviews the success of the Scheme at various levels viz., at national level by a National Task Force headed by Secretary, Women & Child Development, at State level by State Task Force headed by the Chief Secretary and at district level, by the District Task Force headed by District Collector/Deputy Commissioner.*************Annexure-IStatement refered to in reply to Part (c) of Lok Sabha Admitted Question No. 2269 for 23.09.2020 regarding Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) raised by SHRI ASADUDDIN OWAISI and SHRI SYED IMTIAZ JALEEL.
