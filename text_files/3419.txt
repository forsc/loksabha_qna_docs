                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3094






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Expert Committee on MSME Sector




Srinivas Kesineni













                                                    Raksha Nikhil Khadse
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has proposed and set up an expert committee on Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) sector to review the framework and suggest long term solutions in this sector for the economic and financial sustainability in the recent economic reforms;(b)	if so, the details thereof including the recommendations made by the Committee;(c)	whether the Government has considered/accepted the recommendations of the said Committee; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the time-frame fixed by which the suggestions/recommendations of the Committee would be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a): 	No, Sir. (b) to (d):	 Does not arise in view of the above.
