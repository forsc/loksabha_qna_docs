                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2239






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Ayurvedic Covid-19 Vaccine




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA,YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has given approval to any Ayurvedic Covid-19 Vaccine in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof, vaccine-wise;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of the Government agencies involved in the development of the vaccine?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) & (b):	Ministry of AYUSH in the Central Government has not given approval to any Ayurvedic Covid-19 Vaccine. (c) & (d):	It is not legally permitted nor feasible under the provisions of Drugs & Cosmetics Act, 1940 and Rules thereunder to manufacture Ayurvedic medicines for parenteral route administration. Accordingly, no Government agency under the purview of Ministry of AYUSH is involved in the development of Ayurvedic vaccine.
