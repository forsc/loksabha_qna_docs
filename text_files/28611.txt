                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

632






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Promotion  of Handicraft




Maddila  Gurumoorthy














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposals to promote handicrafts in and around Srikalahasti and also weavers who are weaving sarees from Venkatagiri constituency in Andhra Pradesh; and (b) if so, the details thereof alongwith the Corporations, Agencies and organizations engaged/ involved in achieving meaningful growth of the Handicrafts Sector in Srikalahasti and weavers from Venkatagiri Constituency in Andhra Padesh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	Yes Sir, the Office of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts) implements various schemes for promotion and development of handicraft sector in the State of Andhra Pradesh including Srikalahastiand Venkatagiri constituency. The details of interventions/activities like organising marketing events, skill upgradation training, design development workshops, distribution of modern tool kits and organising seminar etc by the Office of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts)in Andhra Pradesh during last five years are as under:Financial Year	No. of intervention executed	Amount Released(Rs. in lakhs)	No. of beneficiary covered directly2016-17	36	1619.71	22382017-18	19	360.10	19012018-19	14	269.20	97672019-20	39	184.94	13542020-21	74	313.89	3775Total	182	2747.84	19035Office of Development Commissioner (Handlooms) also implements various interventions for development of handloom sector and to promote the marketing channels in domestic as well as international market.  The details of marketing event / expo organized by the Office of Development Commissioner (Handlooms) in Andhra Pradesh during last five years are as under:Financial Year	No. of event events executed	Amount Released(Rs. In lakh)	No. of beneficiary covered (directly and indirectly)2016-17	5	52.00	11,0002017-18	10	50.49	23,0002018-19	2	41.50	5,0002019-20	7	47.00	15,0002020-21	6	58.40	10,000Total	30	249.39	64,000Further, the following major agencies/organisation have been engaged for the promotion of handicrafts and weavers in the State of Andhra Pradesh including Srikalahasti and Venkatagiri constituency:•	M/s State Handicrafts Development co-corporation, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh.•	M/s Shilparamam Arts, Crafts and Cultural Society, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh.•	M/s Society for Weavers’ Welfare in Andhra Pradesh (SWWAP), Ratnagiri, Andhra Pradesh•	District Rural Development Agency, Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh.•	M/s Chittoor Handicrafts Producer Company Limited, Chittoor.•	National Handloom Development Corporation, Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh. ***
