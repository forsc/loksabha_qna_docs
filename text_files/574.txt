                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Compensation for Farmer Suicide




Giridhari Yadav













                                                    Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether one lakh rupees is given to the families of those farmers who die or commit suicide due to drought whereas atleast five lakh rupees is given as compensation to those who die from alcohol consumption;(b) 	if so, the reason for the discrimination which reflects in giving lesser compensation to the farmers;(c) 	whether the Government is proposing to give atleast five lakh rupees to the families of those farmers who have died or committed suicide due to drought; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b): 	Ex-Gratia payment is provided by the respective state Governments to families of farmers who commit suicide as well as those who die due to alcohol consumption. (c) & (d):	There is no such proposal under consideration of the Government of India.*******
