                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Tribal Languages in Telangana




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that tribal languages such as Gondi,Koya, Kolmi, Kondh and Banjara in Telangana are endangered; (b) If so, the details of efforts is made by the Government to protect the above five tribal languages from Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) Out of the 5 stated tribal languages, Gondi is the endangered language according to UNESCO list. (b) Government of Telangana has taken following efforts to protect and preserve the Tribal Languages. 1.	Published Primers in Koya, Lambadis, Gondi and Kolami Languages.2.	Published Varnamala\, Local Rhymes and Local Stories in tribal languages as well as in Telugu.    3. Undertaken development of Mother Tongue Based Mutlilingual Education (MTBMLE) programme to impart education to tribal children of classes I, II in their mother tongue as prescribed in Right to Education Act, 2009 and New Education Policy, 2020.  *****
