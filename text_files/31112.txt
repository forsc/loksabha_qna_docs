                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2810






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Mobile Towers Installation




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether mobile companies are committed to install mobile towers in order to improve mobile network;(b) 	if so, whether installation of new towers is under consideration in Nanded, Yavatmal and Hingoli of Maharashtra; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) to (c)	The technology wise details of existing Base Stations installed in Nanded, Yavatmal and Hingoli districts of Maharashtra as on 30.11.2021 are as follows: -<pre>Sl. No	District name	2G 	3G 	4G 1.	Nanded	1127	328	34682.	Yavatmal	725	268	29313.	Hingoli	302	93	1226</pre>	In order to address the growing traffic demands of telecom services and for enhancement of Quality of Services (QoS), Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) augment their network resources & optimize the capacities across the country including in Nanded, Yavatmal and Hingoli districts of Maharashtra on regular basis as per techno-commercial feasibility and viability.*********
