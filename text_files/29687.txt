                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1307






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Allocation of Funds from NABARD




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of funds allocated to Haryana and North Eastern States including Assam by NABARD since the year 2014; (b) whether the Government has received any complaint in this regard; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government has fixed any accountability of anyone in this regard; and (e) it so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a): The details of funds allocated to Haryana and North Eastern States, including Assam by NABARD since 2014 under different funds/activities are given at Annexure.(b) to (e): NABARD, which is an All India Financial Institution for Agriculture and Rural Development sector works in close coordination with the State Governments in sourcing of project proposals under various schemes such as RIDF, LTIF, MIF, WIF, NIDA, TDF, etc. implemented by them. Post sanction and disbursement of the funds, NABARD monitors implementation and progress of the various schemes in close coordination with the implementing Departments of the State Governments. They also work closely with the Government of India (GoI) and share their reports from time to time. The GoI has also launched a Centralised Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System (CPGRAMS), an online web-enabled system to enable submission of grievances with the facility of tracking on real time basis. Concerned Ministries/ Departments/ Organisations under GoI scrutinize and take actions for speedy and favourable redressal of the grievances received through this portal.Also, complaints in regard to various issues relating to agriculture credit received by Government/ RBI are taken up for appropriate redressal. ****** Annexure Funds Allocated to Haryana and North Eastern States under various Funds (2014-15 onwards)				(Rs. in crore)States	Refinance	RIDF	Other Business Funds*	Development Programmes $Assam	5,119	7,136	1,312	39Arunachal Pradesh	33	1,862	11	18Manipur	108	513	423	30Meghalaya	277	1,062	13	37Mizoram	478	1,216	121	26Nagaland	197	131	8	28Sikkim	104	412	217	32Tripura	1,713	1,904	30	2Haryana	52,326	7,387	45,627	58 * include NABARD Infrastructure Development Fund (NIDA), Credit Facilities to Federations (CFF), Direct Refinance Assistance (DRA) to Co-operative Banks, Financial Inclusion Fund (FIF), Warehouse Infrastructure Fund (WIF), Food Processing Fund (FIF), Long Term Irrigation Fund (LTIF)&Micro-Irrigation Fund (MIF)$ includes Farmer Producer Organizations (FPO) promotion, climate change fund, National Adaptation Fund for Climate Change (NAFCC), Tribal Development Fund (TDF), Watershed Development Fund (WDF)&Farm Sector Promotion Fund (FSPF)
