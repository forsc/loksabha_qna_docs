                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

78






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Pradham Mantri Kalyan Vikas Yojana in Maharashtra




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number of persons trained under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) in Maharashtra particularly Nasik, since the inception of the scheme to till date, district/year-wise;(b) 	the total amount of funds allotted for this scheme in Maharashtra particularly to Nasik since the launch of scheme; (c) 	the number of people provided employment after getting training from Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Training Centres during the said period; and(d)	the target fixed for providing employment to those who have not been provided employment so far despite receiving training?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       The district-wise and year-wise candidates trained/oriented under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana in Maharashtra including Nashik district, as on 10.07.2021, is given at Annexure. (b)       Under State component of PMKVY, Rs. 234.49 Cr. (Rs. 214.44 Cr. under PMKVY 2.0 and Rs. 20.05 Cr. under PMKVY 3.0) have been allocated for the State of Maharashtra.(c)        Under placement linked component (short term training) of PMKVY, as on 10.07.2021, 77,785 candidates of State of Maharashtra have been provided with the placement. (d)       Under PMKVY, no such targets have been fixed for providing employment to those who have not been provided employment so far despite being trained. However, placement under the scheme has been linked with payout to training providers. The last tranche i.e. 30% of total payout is being disbursed to training providers on ensuring placement of candidates.
