                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Funds for NULM




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy













                                                    Chirag Paswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the quantum of funds released under National Urban Livelihood Mission (NULM) in the last two years, State wise; b)	the quantum of funds utilised from the NULM budget specifically for NULM Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH) including details of funds spent on constructing shelters, shelter repairs and maintenance, State-wise; and c)	the details and the number of shelters constructed in Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)	Under Deendayal Antodaya Yojana - National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), funds are released to States/ UTs in a consolidated manner, out of which appropriate amount is apportioned by States/ UTs for various components of the Mission, including Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH), as per their requirement. A statement showing state-wise details of funds released by Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs and utilized by States/ UTs, including Telangana, during 2018-19 to 2019-20 under the Mission is at Annexure-I.(c)	So far, 34 shelters have been made functional in Telangana, with a capacity of 2,813 persons, under the Mission.                               *****
