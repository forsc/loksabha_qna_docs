                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1491






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Subsidy on Agricultural Inputs




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is providing various kinds of subsidies to the agricultural sector; (b) 	if so, the detail thereof and the amount allocated for this purpose during the last five years, year-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has made any provision to provide special subsidy to the farmers of flood affected States including Assam; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Agriculture is a State Subject.  Government of India facilitates the State Governments through many Centrally Sponsored Schemes.  A statement showing the amount released (including subsidy) for these Schemes during the last five years is annexed.(c) & (d):	Under the existing items and norms of assistance from State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF)/National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF), immediate assistance in the form of input subsidy is provided to the farmers affected due to notified natural calamities including floods under these funds.  In Assam for providing relief to the farmers affected due to floods of 2019, an amount of Rs.603.61 crore is available in SDRF as on 01.04.2019.  Further, both the installments of central share of SDRF amounting to Rs.559.00 crores is also available with the State in its SDRF for providing relief to the affected people. Further, the Government of India has sanctioned an additional financial assistance of Rs.314.83 crore from NDRF to the State of Assam for floods in 2019, which interalia includes an assistance of Rs.111.85 crore as agriculture input subsidy.  The aforesaid amount of Rs.314.83 crore has been adjusted against the excess amount sanctioned to the State earlier.
