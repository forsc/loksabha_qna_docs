Export of Oil meal and Oil meal Products




Gautham Sigamani Pon













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar

                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantum of oil meal and oil meal products exported to major countries, quantity and country-wise during the last three years indicating the share of India’s oil meal exports in global exports; (b)	whether the export of oil meal products from the country has declined during the last three years and the current year and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; (c)	whether the Government has assessed the impact of decline in export of oil meal and if so, the details and the outcome thereof; (d)	whether the Government is considering to implement any special incentive scheme to encourage oil meal production in the country and if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the steps being taken by the Government to boost the export of agricultural products including oil meals?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY(SMT. ANUPRIYA PATEL)(a)	The quantum of oilmeal and oilmeal products exported to major countries quantity and country wise during the last three years indicating the share of India’s oilmeal exports in global exports, is at Annexure – I.(b) & (c)	The export of oilmeal declined from 4493293 Tons in the year 2018-19 to 2655789 Tons in the year 2019-20 due to better price realization for the product in domestic market. Subsequently, exports had a steep boost in the year 2020-21 with the total export of 4366554 Tons (91.45% growth) (Annexure-II). Furthermore, upto September, 2021, there is 1.69% growth in exports of oilmeal over the same period in the year 2020-21.  (d)  	Ministry of Food Processing industries (MoFPI) is implementing various schemes like Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY), Pradhan Mantri Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme (outlay of Rs. 10,000 crore) and Product Linked Incentive (PLI) scheme (outlay of Rs. 10,900 crore) for supporting creation of modern infrastructure, setting/upgrading the food manufacturing units, value chain development in perishables, backward and forward linkages, financial, technical and business support for upgradation/setting up of micro food processing units etc. The schemes are available for oilmeal industry also.(e) 	To promote agricultural exports including oilmeals, the Government has introduced a comprehensive Agriculture Export Policy (AEP) to harness export potential of Indian agriculture and raise farmers’ income. The Department of Commerce provides assistance through several other schemes, to promote exports, including agriculture export viz. Trade Infrastructure for Export Scheme (TIES), Market Access Initiatives (MAI) Scheme, etc. In addition, assistance to the exporters of agriculture products is also available under the export promotion schemes of Marine Products Export Development Authority (MPEDA), Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA), Tea Board, Coffee Board and Spices Board.
