                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

471






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Funds for Welfare Schemes




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of schemes being implemented by the Government across the country including Assam and Haryana at present; (b) the fund allocated for these schemes across the country during the last five years, State-wise; (c) whether the Government proposes to formulate any special schemes for Haryana and North Eastern States including Assam; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): The details of the schemes being implemented presently throughout the country including Assam and Haryana and the funds allocated State-wise during the last five years is at Annexure.(c) & (d): There is no proposal to formulate any special scheme for Haryana and North Eastern State including Assam.****
