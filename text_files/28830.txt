                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

393






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Lakhpati Self Help Group Women




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recently launched an initiative to create Lakhpati Self Help Group (SHG) women to enable rural SHG women to earn at least Rs 1 lakh per annum;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the number of blocks that have been covered under the National Rural Livelihood Mission till date along with funds allocated and utilized under the said Mission, State/UT wise details thereof; and(d) 	the number of Self Help Group (SHG) formed along with number of women mobilized into such SHGs, State/UT wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	& (b) Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihood Mission (DAY-NRLM) a centrally sponsored scheme was launched in June 2011. It aims to reduce poverty through promotion of diversified and gainful self-employment and skilled wage employment resulting in appreciable income increase of the rural poor on sustainable basis. There are various sub-schemes like Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana (MKSP), Start-up Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP), Aajeevika Gramin Express Yojana (AGEY), National Rural Economic Transformation Project (NRETP), Deendayal Upadhyay Gramin Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), Rural Self Employment Training Institutes (RSETI) being implemented within the mission for enhancing the income on sustainable basis of the rural poor; The mission seeks to achieve its objective through investing in four core components viz., (i) social mobilization and promotion of sustainable community institutions of the rural poor (Self Help Groups-SHGs, Village Organizations-VOs, Cluster Level Federations); (ii) financial inclusion, (iii) sustainable livelihoods; and (iv) convergence and entitlements. So within the ambit of the mission, SHG members are being facilitated for promotion of sustainable livelihood so that they may reach an aspirational goal of having minimum of a lakh rupee as annual income.  (c) As of 31st October, 2021, 6768 blocks have been covered under DAY-NRLM. The details of funds allocated and utilized State/UT wise from FY 2011-12 to FY 2021-22 is attached at Annexure –I.(d) The number of Self Help Group (SHG) promoted along with the number of women mobilized into SHGs, State/UT wise is attached at Annexure - II.
