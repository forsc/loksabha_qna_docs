                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1915






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Guidelines for Transport during Lockdown




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah













                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to formulate any consolidated transport policy for Delhi-NCR on the lines of consolidated health policy in view of COVID-19 pandemic;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has taken note of the lack of mutual co-ordination amongst Delhi-NCR and various States in following guidelines for transport during the lockdown period; and (d)	if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)   No such proposal is under consideration.(c) and (d) During the process of Lockdown various orders and guidelines were issued by the Ministry of Home Affairs.   The guidelines issued by the Ministry of Home Affairs allowed the inter State movement of all goods and cargo.  The Ministry of Home Affairs and the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways were continuously monitoring such movement of goods / cargo and provided all assistance for the smooth movement across States / UTs.   *****
