                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

411






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Diversification of Crops




Brijendra Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps being taken for diversification of crops especially in areas dominated by paddy-wheat crop cycle; (b) 	the budget allocation and expenditure incurred on this over the last 5 years, State-wise; and (c) 	the status of crop diversification, especially weaning the farmers away from water guzzling crops like paddy and sugarcane?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	The Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) is implementing Crop Diversification Programme (CDP) in Original Green Revolution States viz: Punjab, Haryana and western Uttar Pradesh, as a sub scheme of Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) since 2013-14 to divert the area of water guzzling paddy crop to alternative crops pulses, oilseeds, coarse cereals, nutri cereals, cotton and agro forestry. Under CDP, assistance is provided for four major interventions viz., alternate crop demonstrations, farm mechanization & value addition, site-specific activities & contingency for awareness, training, monitoring, etc.(b):	The State-wise allocation (central share) and expenditure / release of central share under CDP during 5 years is given below:Contd…2/--2-                            					(Rs. in crore)S. Haryana	2015-16	49.75	49.75		2016-17	33.94	16.97		2017-18	7.55	7.55		2018-19	3.02	1.51		2019-20	3.02	-3. Government of India also provide flexibility to the states for state specific needs/priorities under RKVY. The state can promote crop diversification including diversification of paddy and sugarcane crops under RKVY with the approval of State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) headed by Chief Secretary of the State..           The State Government of Haryana has also launched its own pilot scheme for substituting paddy by maize and other crops in 7 districts of the State during 2019-20.*******
