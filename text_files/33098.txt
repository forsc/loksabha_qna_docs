                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

611






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Decommissioning of INS Khukri




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decommissioned INS Khukri, the first indigenously built missile corvette of the Country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor and its contribution during the life time;(c) whether any steps are being taken to replace the retiring fleet;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) to (e): (i) Yes, Sir. INS Khukri was decommissioned on 21 December, 2021.  INS Khukri was commissioned on 23 August, 1989 and has been in service for 32 years.(ii) The ship had outlived its useful Services life and efforts to extend its Operational life were not economical.  During its Operational life, INS Khukri has participated in operations across the entire spectrum of Naval Operations.(iii) Augmentation of Force levels of the Indian Navy is a continuous process and is undertaken in accordance with Long Term Integrated Perspective Plan (LTIPP)*****
