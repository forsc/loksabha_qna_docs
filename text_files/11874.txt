                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

727






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






CADWM




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of interventions carried out under Reclamation of waterlogged areas component of Command Area Development and Water Management (CADWM) in Jharkhand; (b) the details of funds received and utilized under the above mentioned scheme for construction of agricultural field drains in Jharkhand; and (c) the percentage of portion of agricultural field in which requisite infrastructure for micro-irrigation has been developed in Jharkhand, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  As per information made available by the State Government, no work has been carried out under the ‘reclamation of waterlogged areas’ component of the ‘Command Area Development and Water Management (CADWM)’ programme in Jharkhand.(b)  CADWM programme is presently being implemented under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) for 99 AIBP (Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Program) prioritized projects in the country. Out of these prioritized projects, one project (namely, Subernarekha Multipurpose Project) is in the State of Jharkhand which has been included under CADWM programme targeting balance Culturable Command Area (CCA) of 66.645 thousand hectare at an estimated cost of Rs.747.53 crore with Central Assistance (CA) of Rs.133.32 crore. Government of Jharkhand is yet to avail any Central Assistance under CADWM programme for the construction of agricultural field drains.(c)  As per the ‘land utilization’ compilation of the Ministry of Statistics and Program Implementation, the State of Jharkhand has net sown area of about 13.85 lakh hectares.
