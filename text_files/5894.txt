                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Standard for being Minority




Mukesh Rajput














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government will clarify the standard in terms of population percentage to be recognised as minority if so, the details thereof; and (b)	the population of Sikhs, Christians, Jains, Buddhists and Muslims minority in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	The Central Government notifies minority communities in consultation with various stakeholders under Section 2(c) of National Commission for Minorities (NCM) Act, 1992. (b)	The population of Sikhs, Christians, Jains, Buddhists and Muslims in the country, as per Census 2011, is as given below:-Sr. No.	Community 	Population1	Sikhs	2,08,33,1162	Christians	2,78,19,5883	Jains	44,51,7534	Buddhists	84,42,9725	Muslims	17,22,45,158**********
