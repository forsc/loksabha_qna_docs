                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2217






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Agreement with Foreign Countries




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has signed any agreement with the foreign countries relating to Science& Technology during the last five years and the current year;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the details of benefits likely to be accrued as a result of such agreements? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????, ??????? ?? ???????????? ?????? ?? ?????? ??????? ??????(??. ???? ?????) (a)	Yes Sir. (b)	During the last five year, the Government has signed Inter-Government Science and Technology Agreements with Denmark, Indonesia, Uzbekistan and USA.(c) 	India is sharing its Science & Technology knowledge through various bilateral programmes and contributing to India and partner country’s Science & Technology landscape. These Bilateral Collaborations help in generation of new knowledge, sharing of expertise, cost and optimal utilization of resources, providing access to advanced facilities and sophisticated instruments which are not available domestically and allowed participation in international mega science projects. The collaboration in turn also enhanced the quality and output of scientific research in the country in terms of quality of papers published through International Collaboration and Human Resource Development.*****
