                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Upliftment of Tharu Tribals




Baidyanath Prasad Mahto














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to train Tharu tribes to bring them into mainstream; (b) 	if so, the State-wise and district-wise details thereof; (c) 	the details of the work done for the upliftment of Tharu tribes under Valmiki Nagar Parliamentary Constituency; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b): The Ministry of Tribal Affairs, inter alia, is providing Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub Scheme (SCA to TSS) and Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution of India to State governments for the socio-economic development of Tribal population, including Tharu tribe, which is a notified ST community in Bihar, Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand. The details of the grants approved for release to the Government of Bihar, Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand during the year 2019-20 under the above two initiatives is as under:(Rs. In lakh)Name of Scheme / Programme	Bihar	Uttar Pradesh	UttarakhandSpecial Central Assistance to Tribal Sub Scheme (SCA to TSS)	1180.02	936.55	600.00Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution of India	600.00	1247.09	1204.54(c)	& (d): The projects undertaken are for Scheduled Tribes / Scheduled areas in the State.*****
