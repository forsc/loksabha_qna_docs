                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3132






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Dairy Cooperative Societies




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of registered dairy cooperative societies, State and district-wise;(b)	whether the Government is keen to fragment unilateral dominance of few milk federations by motivating/providing incentives to dairy cooperative societies to form regional federations to reach the untap markets and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the time likely to be taken to extend them to establish their brand and marketing strategies?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) State-wise and Milk Union wise number of dairy cooperative societies as on March 2021 is given at Annexure.(b) & (c) Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying has not received any proposal to form regional dairy federations from State Governments. However, supplementing efforts of States, the Department has been providing financial assistance under following dairy development schemes to State dairy cooperatives for strengthening their dairy value chain including strengthening of marketing infrastructure to reach untapped areas:-(i) 	National Programme for Dairy Development(ii) 	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund(iii) 	Strengthening Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities****
