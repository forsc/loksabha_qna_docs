                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4046






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Maintenance of Toilets under Swachh Bharat Mission




Vinod Chavda














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the toilets built in Gujarat under the Swachh Bharat Mission are notproperly maintained, as per the various news reports;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) whether any action has been taken against the officials responsible for it; and(d) if so, the details thereof along with the measures taken to make the toiletsusable?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (d)   Under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin), the beneficiaries themselves are responsible for the maintenance of their household toilets.   However, Government of Gujarat has taken a number of initiatives for sustainability of Open Defecation Free (ODF) status like monitoring usage through ODF stickers, awareness campaigns in schools and villages, gram sabhas, etc.
