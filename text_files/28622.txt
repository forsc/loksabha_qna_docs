                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

732






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Beneficiaries under PMAY




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of beneficiaries of the grants given under Pradhan Mantri Grameen aur Seheri Awas Yojana, State/district-wise; and(b)	the number of cases pending under the yojana in the Parliamentary Constituency of Bijnor?






ANSWER




                                                              THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS               (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	:The State/district-wisedetails of number of beneficiaries of the grants given under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G) and Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) are at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively. (b)	: Under PMAY-G, a total of 32,414 houses have been sanctioned and under PMAY-U, a total of 19,330 houses have been sanctioned in the Parliamentary Constituency of Bijnor. No fresh demand under PMAY-G and PMAY-U is pending.                          *****
