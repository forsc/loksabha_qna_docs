                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2291






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Production of Petroleum




Amar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the target currently fixed for 2040 in terms of production of Petroleum along with the current timeline for production boost;(b) the details of the inflow of capital which has to be invested in the petroleum companies to achieve this target, State/ PSU-wise;(c) the current State-wise segregation of production segregated according to PSUs; and(d) whether there are any steps mandating the treatment of waste products, if so, the details thereof, if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c) Tentative production targets have been fixed based on projection of crude oil and natural gas production upto FY 2023-24. The projection targets from 2019-20 to 2023-24 are as under:YearCrude Oil(in MMT)Natural Gas(in BCM)2019-2035.0434.552020-2135.7939.322021-2238.2346.922022-2338.6154.532023-2438.9155.68*MMT - Million Metric TonnesBCM- Billion Cubic MetrePage 2 of 2The tentative domestic investment in next five years by Oil & Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) and Oil India Limited (OIL) in exploration and production related activities is about ?1,60,140.60 crore and ?17,575.83 crore respectively. However, this projected investment forecast for next 5 years is tentative and is based upon existing acreage and own assessment of ONGC and OIL.State-wise/company-wise details of domestic oil (in Thousand Metric Tonnes) and gas (in Million Metric Standard Cubic Meters) produced during the year 2018-19 is as under:StateCompanyCrude oil Production (in TMT)Natural Gas Production (in MMSCM)Andhra PradeshONGC296.21081.58Arunachal PradeshOIL8.612.24AssamONGC990482OIL3281.52481.93GujaratONGC4501.41334GSPC33.9311.56JharkhandONGC-3.58RajasthanONGC-4OIL3.0227.74Tamil NaduONGC3951207.35TripuraONGC31554(d) As such no waste products are generated during the production of crude oil and natural gas. However, effluent/sludge/wastewater generated during the process is treated in Effluent Treatment Plant.********
