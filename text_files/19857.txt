                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

965






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Skill Development in Haryana




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has assessed that the trainers taking training from Skill Development Centres are getting employment according to their skill expertise;(b) 	 if so, the details thereof and the number of trainers getting employment during the last three years in Haryana district-wise;(c)	 the number of Skill Development Centres set up in the Country including Haryana;(d)	 whether there is any proposal to set up new Skill development centres; and(e)	 if so, the details thereof?  






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)      Under short term skilling eco-system, trainers are being trained through Training of Trainers (ToT) programme which is being conducted by Sector Skill Councils (SSCs) and facilitated through National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). Under ToT, 50,689 trainers have been certified across 37 sectors. ToT programme is for the development of professionals, who are already associated / employed with training providers. The state-wise number of trainers certified under Training of Trainers (ToT) Programme is given at Annexure. (c) to (e)             Till date, 6 Trainer and Assessor academies have been set up as trainer development centre  up for conducting ToT programmes; one each in Andhra Pradesh, Haryana, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Telangana and Uttar Pradesh. The National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) in consultation with Sector Skill Council (SSC) is facilitating the setting up of more such academics by private players.
