                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1430






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






FPIs in Bihar




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the Government is planning to develop Bihar as Food Processing Hub and if so, the details thereof;  (b)	the details of Food Processing Industries (FPIs) functioning in Bihar and the number of  employees working therein, district-wise; (c)	the details of the total FPIs which have received technical assistance from the Government during the last four years and the current financial year, district-wise; (d)	 the quantum of funds allocated and the amount sanctioned to FPIs as on date; and (e)	 whether the Government proposes to increase the number of FPIs in Bihar, if so, the    details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)  & (e).  Government has launched a new centrally sponsored scheme- PM Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) with an allocation of Rs. 10,000 crores to directly benefit 2 lakh micro enterprises during 2020-2025 across the country.  A total of 10261 units will be supported in Bihar under the scheme.  Rs. 5,04,52,000 has been released to Bihar for hiring of PMU, undertaking studies, promotional activities and other activities permissible under the scheme guidelines. (b).	As per latest Annual Survey of Industries (ASI) 2017-18 estimates, a total of 747 factories are operational in Bihar in Food & Beverages sector (National Industrial Classification code 10 & 11) and a total of 24,258 persons are engaged therein.  (c) & (d). The details of financial assistance provided under PMKSY to projects in Bihar is placed at Annexure. MoFPI does not operate any scheme to provide technical assistance to food processing industries in the country including in the State of Bihar. *****
