                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3241






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Review of FCI




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has constituted any committee to review the functioning of the Food Corporation of India (FCI); (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the details of fund allocated to various departments in FCI during the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): 	Yes, Sir. Government has initiated a massive effort for transformation of FCI for enhancing transparency in its operations by emulating best practices at international level for procurement of foodgrains, inventory management, warehousing, distribution etc. with the objective to integrate the same in FCI ecosystem.(c): 		Year wise allocation of expenses of various schemes of FCI is given in
