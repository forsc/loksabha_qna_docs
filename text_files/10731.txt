                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4023






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Construction of Ring Road around Amritsar under Bharatmala




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to construct ring road around the city of Amritsar under the Bharatmala Pariyojana;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	if not, whether the Government has any plans to include the city of Amritsar in next stages of Bharatmala Pariyojana; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)  Government of India had approved Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I in October, 2017 for development of highway stretches with an aggregate length of about 34,800 km (including 10,000 km residual NHDP stretches) at an estimated outlay of Rs. 5,35,000.00 crores.  In Bharatmala Pariyojana, total 28 nos. of Ring Roads as well as 191 nos. of choke points/congestion points have been identified for development in the country including provision of intervention (01 no. bypass) at Amritsar in the State of Punjab.*****
