                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

372






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Promotion of MSME Units




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Arun Kumar Sagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the package proposed by the Government to promote the MSME sector; (b) 	whether the Government has any proposal to set up MSME units in extremely backward States and scheduled caste dominated areas of the country including Rajasthan and Shahjahanpur Parliamentary Constituency; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	The Ministry of MSME implements various schemes and programmes for promotion of MSME Sector in the country. These schemes and programmes inter-alia include Prime Minister’s Employment Generation programme (PMEGP), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry & Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE), Credit Guarantee Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises, Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP) etc.  In addition to this, Government of India from time to time also announces special packages, programmes for the MSME Sector such as announcements made on 2nd November 2018 under ‘Support and Outreach Initiative for MSME Sector’ and announcements made under ‘Aatmanirbhar Bharat’ in May 2020. (b) & (c):	 Promotion and development of enterprises is a State subject. The Central Government supplements the efforts of the State/UT Governments through various schemes, programmes and policy initiatives for promotion, development and enhancing the competitiveness of MSMEs and to increase employment opportunities in the country. The benefits of the schemes implemented by the Ministry of MSME are available across the country including in the state of Rajasthan and Shahjahanpur Parliamentary Constituency.
