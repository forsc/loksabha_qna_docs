                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Development of Villages under SAGY




 Kunhalikutty P.K.













                                                    Jyotsna Charandas Mahant
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that development work of villages under Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) has not so far achieved its proposed targets in the State of Chhattisgarh;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(c)	the total number of villages identified under SAGY in Chhattisgarh and the funds provided to each village as on 31.03.2019?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	The Hon’ble Members of Parliament have identified 44 Gram Panchayats in Chhattisgarh under Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana(SAGY). For the Gram Panchayats adopted under SAGY, Village Development Plans (VDP) are prepared  through a participatory process under the guidance of the respective Hon’ble Members of Parliament. The VDP includes prioritised time-bound activities to achieve holistic progress of the village. So far 39 Gram Panchayats have uploaded their VDP on Saanjhi portal containing 3,145 projects as communicated by the Govt of Chhattisgarh. Out of these, 1,952  projects have been completed and 286 projects are in progress as on 12 July 2019. Under the Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana framework, the development of Gram Panchayats has been envisaged through convergence and implementation of existing Government Schemes and Programmes under the administrative control of respective Ministries and State Governments. As such, data on funds provided to SAGY Gram Panchayats under different Schemes is not maintained centrally in this Ministry. *****
