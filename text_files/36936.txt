                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4023






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Preservation of Kalaripayattu




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)   whether the Government has any scheme for preserving Kalaripayattu which is a martial art designed for the ancient battlefield (the word "Kalari" meaning "battlefield"), with weapons and combative techniques that are unique to India; and b)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b) In order to preserve and promote the rich and diverse Intangible Cultural Heritage of India, a scheme titled “Safeguarding the Intangible Cultural Heritage and Diverse Cultural Traditions of India” is being run by Sangeet Natak Akademi, an autonomous body under the Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India. However, there is no specific scheme for preserving ‘Kalaripayattu’ martial art. If a proposal is brought before the Ministry of Culture, we will consider it in terms of rules laid down for the same.
