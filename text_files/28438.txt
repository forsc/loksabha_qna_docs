                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

113






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






PM Poshan Yojana




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Central Cabinet has approved to continue/ amend/ rectify the Centresponsored National PM Nutrition Scheme in schools for the next five years;(b) the number of schools and students expected to benefit from the above scheme inthe country;(c) the number of schools from Gujarat included in this scheme;(d) the number of students likely to get benefit from this scheme; and(e) the details, thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (e): Central Government has approved the Centrally Sponsored Scheme“Pradhan Mantri Poshan Shakti Nirman (PM POSHAN)” earlier known as‘National Programme for Mid-Day Meal in Schools’ for providing one hot cookedmeal in Government and Government – aided Schools from 2021-22 to 2025-26.The scheme covers about 11.80 crore children studying in 11.20 lakh schools acrossthe country. The States and UTs wise (including Gujarat) details of number ofstudents and schools likely to get benefit under the scheme are given at Annexure.
