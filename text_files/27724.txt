                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3225






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Guidelines for Tourists on Hill Stations




Ravneet Singh













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that there has been a huge increase in the number of people visiting various tourist spots, especially the hill stations in the country since the easing of Covid-19 restrictions and ICMR has raised concerns over it; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (c) 	whether the Government is aware that non-adherence to Covid-appropriate behaviour by these tourists can lead to a serious situation and lead to a surge in coronavirus cases and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to put any curbs on visits to tourists spots in its guidelines or take any other measures to ensure Covid-appropriate behaviour by the people visiting various tourist spots in the country; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor along with the action taken by the Government against the tourists which are flouting the Covid rules and over crowding the hill stations of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	Ministry of Tourism, Government of India does not maintain tourist spot wise data of tourist footfall.  However, State/UT wise details of Domestic Tourist Visits (DTVs) and Foreign Tourist Visits (FTVs) during last three years are at Annexure. (c) to (e):	Development and Promotion of tourist places is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/Union Territory Administration. However, with a view to prepare for a post COVID-19 revival, Ministry of Tourism has formulated Operational Recommendations for different segments of Tourism Service Providers in the Travel sector for facilitating smooth and safe resumption of business. Such recommendations have been issued for Travel Agents, Tour Operators, Tourist Transport Operators, Tourist Guides and Facilitators. They have been formulated in consultation with State Governments and tourism / hospitality stakeholders and in keeping with the overall guidelines issued from time to time by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare.*******
