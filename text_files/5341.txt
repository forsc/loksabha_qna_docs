                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4926






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Implementation of PMGSY




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total length of roads constructed in the country underPhase-2 of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), State-wise;(b)	the details of the construction of roads completed in terms of percentage in the said phase, State-wise;(c)	whether Phase-3 of PMGSY has been implemented in the country; and(d)	the names of the States selected under Phase-3 of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana along with the standards and criteria set by the Government under this phase?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	The State-wise details of total length of roads constructed in the Country under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana-II (PMGSY-II), both in terms of Kilometre and percentage (%) are given at Annexure.(c) & (d):	The Government has approved phase-III of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) for consolidation of Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia, to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals at  an estimated cost of Rs. 80,250 crore. All the States are eligible under phase-III of PMGSY. The Government is taking steps for implementation of phase-III of PMGSY. *****
