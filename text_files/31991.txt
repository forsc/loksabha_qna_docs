                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3598






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Compliance Burden in HEI




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government and University Grants Commission (UGC) has initiated a series of online talk with stakeholders to streamline the forms and procedures to reduce the compliance burden in the Higher Educational Institutions (HEI);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it is a follow-up action of Government for the ease of doing business to ease the lives of these stakeholders; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (d): Yes Sir, as a follow-up to the Government’s focus on ease of doing business, the Ministry of Education (MoE) and University Grants commission (UGC) have started a series of online interactions with stakeholders to streamline the forms and processes for reducing compliance burden in Higher Education Sector. In this regard, virtual workshops have been organized by UGC on 31st March, 2021 and 19th April, 2021. Suggestions/inputs regarding burdensome compliances in UGC Regulations have been sought from Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) and stakeholders by UGC vide their public notice dated 8th November, 2021.For further reduction of compliance burden, UGC has taken the following measures:i. merged the UGC Regulations for Open and Distance Learning Programmes and Online Programmes into the UGC (Open and Distance Learning Programmes and Online Programmes) Regulations;ii. facilitated institutions to allow upto forty per cent of the total courses being offered in a particular programme in a semester through the UGC (Credit Framework for Online Learning Courses through Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds) Regulations, 2021 notified on 25th March, 2021; andiii. enabled students to submit an undertaking with respect to Anti Ragging in University and Colleges through online mode instead of hard copy at the time of admission.******
