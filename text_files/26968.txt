                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2611






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Data of NSO




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the National Statistics Office (NSO) has released the data for the period January 2019 to June 2021 on the basis of administrative records available with the selected Government agency to assess the progress of employment in Chhattisgarh; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the outcome of the report?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION, MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a) to (c): No Sir. National Statistical office (NSO), Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation does not release state-wise employment data based on administrative records. However, this Ministry has been bringing out the formal sector employment outlook related statistics at All India level through monthly press release since April 2018, covering period from September 2017 onwards using information on the number of subscribers who have subscribed in three major schemes, namely the Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF) Scheme, the Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) Scheme and the National Pension Scheme (NPS).  The number of new subscribers who joined the Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF), Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) and National Pension Scheme (NPS) during the financial year 2019-20 were 110.41 lakh, 151.45 lakh and 7.57 lakh respectively. These figures for 2020-21 and 2021-22 (up to May 2021) were 85.49 lakh, 115.05 lakh, 6.29 lakh and 13.14 lakh, 19.34 lakh, 1.06 lakh respectively.                                               *******
