                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

453






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Accommodation to Military Personnel




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it has come to the notice of the Government that the military personnel/armymen are facing difficulty in getting official accommodation for their families in the metros like Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad etc. and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to provide official family accommodation for serving personnel in such cities, if so, the details thereof and the action taken in this regard;(c) whether changing schools mid-term due to transfer of armymen is a major challenge to them;(d)  if so, the details thereof and the action taken for changing the transfer procedure; and(e) whether the army personnel posted in risk areas are facing difficulty in getting insurance cover or insurance claims, if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard?
