                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Encounter Killing




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of encounter killings in the country, State wise, including Uttar Pradesh, from January 1, 2017 till date;(b) the number of FIRs registered of encounter killings, State wise, including Uttar Pradesh above said period;(c) the number of ongoing investigations against police officers on charges of encounter killings; and(d) the number of police officers convicted for chargers of encounter killings in the same period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	As per information received from the National Human Rights Commission (NHRC), a State-wise statement showing cases registered under category death in police encounter for the period from January 1, 2017 to January 31st , 2022 is given at Annexure.	(b) to (d):	No such record is maintained centrally.  Besides, as per the seventh schedule to the Constitution of India, “Police” and “Public Order” are  State  subjects.  The National  Human  Rights  Commission  (NHRC) had issued guidelines dated 12.05.2010 laying down procedure to be followed for investigation of cases of deaths during the course of police action. Pursuant to the guidelines framed by NHRC, every death during the course of police action is to be reported within 48 hours of its occurrence.  Prompt prosecution/ disciplinary action is to be initiated against all delinquent officers found guilty in magisterial enquiry/police investigation. It is for the concerned authorities to take action against the erring public servant as per extant rules, procedures, etc.*******
