                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2166






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Assistance from UNIDO




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of technical and economic cooperation being received from United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO) to MSME sector during the last three years;(b)	whether there is any institutional mechanism to solve sectoral issues in Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country and if so, details of major issues that have been solved by the UNIDO during the last three years; and(c)	the list of all preference policies being implemented by the Government for procurement of goods from MSMEs during the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI) (a):	Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) has received technical and economic corporation from United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO) through the projects (i) Promoting Market Transformation for Energy Efficiency in Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) and (ii) Global Cleantech Innovation Programme (GCIP) India,  in the last three years. (b):	National Board for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (NBMSME) examines the factors affecting the promotion and development of MSMEs and recommends policies and programmes relating thereto.(c):	Ministry of MSME implements Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) which mandates 25% of annual procurement from MSEs by Central Ministries/Departments/Public Sector Enterprises, including 3% from MSEs owned by Women entrepreneurs.
