                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2832






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Mangrove Forests




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total area of mangrove forests in the country, State/UT-wise;(b)	the details of financial assistance provided to each State and Union Territories during the last three years for the protection of mangrove forests;(c)	whether the Union Government proposes to provide financial assistance to State Government of Kerala to take over mangrove forests situated in private lands in the State;(d)	if so, the derails thereof; and(e)	the measures taken by the Government for protection of mangrove forests?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	As per the latest Forest Survey of India (FSI) Report titled ‘India State of Forest Report (2019)’, the mangrove cover in the country is 4,975 sq. km.
