                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1118






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Cultivation of Rice in Tamil Nadu




P. Velusamy













                                                    K. Shanmuga Sundaram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total cultivation of rice in the country and out of which what is the total cultivation of rice in Tamilnadu;(b)	the quantum of rice production in Tamilnadu and the MSP (Minimum Support Price) fixed for paddy cultivation;(c)	whether the Government is having any proposal to increase the MSP since the Government is targeting to double the income of farmers;(d)	if so, the details thereof;(e)	whether the Government is having any proposal to increase the insurance amount under PM Fasal Yojana and reducing the premium paid by the paddy cultivators; and (f)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????              (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	 The total area under paddy in the country is 450.67 lakh ha including20.38 lakh ha in Tamil Nadu(4th Advance estimates 2020-21).(b) 	The production of rice in Tamil Nadu during the year 2020-21 is 72.83 lakh tonnes(4th Advance estimates 2020-21) and the Minimum Support Price (MSP) ofpaddyfor the year 2021-22 is as below;(In Rs. Per Qtl.)Paddy Variety	Minimum Support Price (MSP)Common	1940Grade ''A''	1960(c)& (d) :	In order to provide remunerative returns to the farmers, the Government has increased the MSP for all mandated crops with a return of at least 50 per cent over all India weighted average cost of production from the year 2018-19. (e)& (f) :	There is no proposal regarding change in methodology to calculate claims under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) scheme.*********
