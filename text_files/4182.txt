                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3793






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Phase II and III of PMGSY




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether construction under Phase II and III of the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) has started in Bihar;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor;(c)	the places where construction under Phase II has started along with the status of the same;(d)	whether all the incomplete construction of Phase I will be taken up under Phase II & III and if so, the details thereof; (e)	the details of the States where the PMGSY has not moved to Phase I; (f)	the details regarding the roads constructed under PMGSY in Bihar during the last four years, district-wise; and(g)	the quantum of funds allocated and utilized for the same in Bihar during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	The State of Bihar has been allocated a target length of 2465 km for upgrading the existing rural roads under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana-II (PMGSY-II). As reported by the State Government, DPRs for 2,465 Km under PMGSY-II is under preparation. The Government has recently approved phase-III of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) for consolidation of Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia, to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals at  an estimated cost of Rs. 80,250 crore. The Government is taking steps for implementation of phase-III of PMGSY. (d):	Works sanctioned under PMGSY-I shall be completed by the State under PMGSY-I itself.(e):	All the State Governments have got sanctions under PMGSY-I. 19 States have already transited to PMGSY-II.  The States of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland and Uttarakhand and Union Territories have not yet moved to PMGSY-II. (f):	Details of roads constructed in Bihar during the last four years district-wise are at Annexure.(g):	The details of funds released by Government of India and utilized under PMGSY in the State of Bihar  during the last four years are as follows:-                                                           (Rs in Crore)Years	              Funds released	        Funds Utilized*2015-16	                2781.00	                    2211.272016-17	                2958.33	                    3315.732017-18	                1349.31	                    1539.642018-19	                 140.00	                    1874.77* Expenditure in excess of actual release comprises of unspent balance of previous year and State share.*****
