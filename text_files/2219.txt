                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2042






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Upgradation of Airports in Bihar




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of airports in Bihar where upgradation work is under way along with the target date fixed to complete the said work;(b) the total amount being incurred on upgradation of these airports by the Government along with the total amount incurred so far;(c) whether there is any resentment among general public due to unnecessary delay in said projects; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has undertaken the upgradation work at Patna and Gaya Airports in Bihar with the Projected Date of Completion (PDC) in October, 2022 and March, 2020 respectively.;;(b): AAI has earmarked a sum of Rs. 1216.90 Crore and Rs. 24.81 Crore for the development of Patna and Gaya airports respectively and so far an expenditure of Rs. 35.50 Crore and Rs. 5.09 Crore has been incurred on the two airports respectively.;;(c): There is no such delay in the projects.;;(d): Does not arise.;
