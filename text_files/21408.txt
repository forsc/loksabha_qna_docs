                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2673






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Recruitment of Women Officers under Territorial Army




D. M. Kathir Anand













                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recruited women officers under Territorial Army 2019 notification;(b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government is taking any measures to upgrade the weapons and quality of bullet proof jackets; and (d) if so,  the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b):  Yes, Sir.  The details of women officers recruited in Territorial Army is as under:Year	No. of women officers	RemarksThrough Army Selection Board (ASB)2019	3	Commissioned2020	1	Selected for CommissionThrough Service Selection Board (SSB)2019	13	Cleared SSB.  Commissioning is subject to availability of vacancies.(c) & (d):  The weapons & equipment and bullet proof jackets for the Indian Army are upgraded periodically after a deliberate process of planning and in consultation with all stakeholders.  The present quality of Bullet Proof Jacket is good.	Apart from various schemes for procurement of weapons and equipment, 12 contracts under normal procurement and 30 contracts under Fast Track Procedure (FTP) have been concluded during the current financial year.  As per the contract signed in 2018 for providing 1,86,138 Bullet Proof Jackets, 1,20,000 have been delivered.*****
