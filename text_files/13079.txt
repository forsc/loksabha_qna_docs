                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1950






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Damage to Foodgrains in FCI Godowns




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether damage of foodgrains in godowns of Food Corporation of India has become a common feature; (b) 	if so, the details of those godowns where foodgrains have been damaged during the last five years; (c) 	the reasons for the damage of foodgrains stored in every godown during the said period, year-wise; (d) 	whether responsibility has been fixed against the persons responsible for the said damage; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	No Sir. (b) & (c): 	Does not arise in view of (a) above.(d) & (e): 	Out of the total stock held by FCI a very negligible quantity of foodgrains have become damaged/non-issuable. Whenever accrual of any quantity of damaged foodgrains is noticed, each case is investigated immediately and action by the concerned authority is invariably taken against officers/officials found responsible.	The number of officer/officials of FCI found responsible for damage of foodgrains during last five years are as under:Year	Number of official2014-15	772015-16	312016-17	132017-18	132018-19	0***
