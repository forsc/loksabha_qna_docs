                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Import of Palm Oil




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has allowed the import of refined palm oil without license for one more year till December, 2022; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the total quantity of palm oil imported from June, 2021 till date; (d) 	whether it is also true that free import was necessitated to contain the domestic edible oil prices; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) and (b): 	The Government vide notification No. 46/2015-2020 dated 20th December 2021 has extended the free import of Refined Palm Oils under ITC HS 15119010, 15119020 and 15119090 for a period up to 31.12.2022.(c): 	62.84 LMT of palm oil was imported from June 2021 to 14th March 2022.(d) and (e): 	In order to improve the domestic availability and to keep prices of edible oils under control, the Government has allowed import of edible oils under Open General Licence (OGL). ****
