                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2348






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Uniform Structure of Road Tax




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     whether a Group of Ministers on Transport had recommended a uniform structure of road tax for vehicles across States on the basis of invoice price of vehicle and if so, the details thereof;(b)     whether the Government has asked the States to levy a uniform road tax on personal vehicles   to resolve the anomaly in the tax system in the country;(c)     if so, the details thereof along with the response of the States thereto; (d) 	the response of the automobile industry regarding uniform taxation across the country; and(e) 	whether anomaly in tax is also responsible for decline in sale of vehicles during the last year and if so, the details thereof along with the remedial steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e)	Group of Ministers on Transport under the chairmanship of Shri Yunus Khan, Transport Minister, Government of Rajasthan had recommended all States/UTs to rationalize motor vehicle taxes and develop a system for seamless movement of vehicles across the country. The matter of Road Tax Policy for Motor Vehicles is a State Subject and Central Government deliberates on this issue with the States in meetings of Transport Development Council (TDC). ******
