                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Food Testing Labs




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the details of food testing laboratories which can detect/test the content of Trans-fat in the food effectively in the country, State/UT-wise; (b) the number of laboratories set up/proposed to be set up to ensure food quality during the last three years, State/UT-wise especially in Punjab, Tamil Nadu and Rajasthan; and (c) the time by which the said laboratories are likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                         ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):   As informed by Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI), at present, there are 111 Food Laboratories which can detect /test the content of Trans-fat in the food in the country. The details of these food testing laboratories,State/UT-wise, is at Annexure-I.(b) to (c):   State Food Testing Laboratories (SFTLs) are set up by respective State/UT Governments. However, Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) is implementing a Central Sector Scheme for “Strengthening of Food Testing System in the Country including Provision of Mobile Food Testing Labs” since 2016-17. Under the Scheme, 39 SFTLs of 29 States/UTs have been taken up for upgradation. This includes upgradation of one SFTL in Punjab (Kharar), 2 SFTLs in Tamil Nadu (Chennai & Madurai) and 3 SFTLS in Rajasthan (Jaipur, Jodhpur & Udaipur). Also, FSSAI recognizes and notifies Food Testing Laboratories under Section 43(1) of Food Safety and Standards (FSS) Act, 2006, Rules and Regulations made therein to analyse the food samples ensuring quality of food testing in the country. During the last three years viz. 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-2020 (till date), 68 Food testing laboratories have been recognized and notified under Section 43(1). Details of these laboratories, State/UT-wise, are at Annexure-II.……………….
