                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

507






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Profit/Loss Making CPSUs




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSUs) running in the country, State-wise;(b)	the details of profit/loss making CPSUs during the last three years and the amount of investment made therein as on date along with the net asset value thereof;(c)	whether the NITI Aayog has requested the Government to evaluate the assets and merge the public sector undertakings running in losses with the undertakings earning profit; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARVIND GANPAT SAWANT)(a to b):   As per Public Enterprises Survey 2017-18 that was laid in Parliament on 27.12.2018, there were 339 Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) in the country as on 31.03.2018 of which 257 were in operation and 82 were under construction. Out of 257 CPSEs, 184 CPSEs are profit making, 71 CPSEs are loss making and 2 CPSEs are neither profit nor loss making. The details of State/UT-wise operating CPSEs (257) along with profit/loss for the years 2017-18, 2016-17 and 2015-16, investment and net fixed assets as on 31.03.2018 are given at Annex.(c): NITI Aayog has not made any such request to the Government.(d): Does not arise.
