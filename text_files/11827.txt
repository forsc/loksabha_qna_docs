                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

733






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Financial Assistance under PMAY




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of households which have been provided first and last instalments of financial assistance so far under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) since its inception along with the details thereof, State-wise;(b) the total number of toilets constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) along with the funds provided to construct such toilets and the details thereof, State-wise; and(c) whether the Union Government proposes to constitute new autonomous bodies under AMRUT scheme and if so, the number of new autonomous bodies proposed to be constituted along with the details thereof, State-wise ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): State/Union Territory (UT)-wise	details of beneficiaries who have been provided first and last instalments of Central Assistance under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) since its inception are at Annexure – I.(b) Total number of toilets constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) till 31-12-2019 is 67,11,058 (Individual House Hold Latrines (IHHL) - 61,31,239 and Community Toilets (CT)/Public Toilets (PT) – 5,79,819). State/UT-wise details of number of toilets constructed along with the funds released are at Annexure – II.(c):	There is no proposal under consideration for constituting new autonomous bodies under AMRUT Yojana.                                                *****
