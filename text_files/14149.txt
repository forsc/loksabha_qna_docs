                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2912






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Formation of Start-Up Companies




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of Start-Up companies formed in the country in the last five years, State-wise; (b) 	the details of the overall cost involved in these companies with the share of the Government, promoters and loans;(c) 	the details of the fields/areas in which these companies were formed and the total number of employment generated by these companies in the last five years, State-wise and year-wise; and(d) 	the total number of existing companies and the details of those wound up as on December 2019?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	The data of all startup companies formed in the country is not centrally compiled. However, since the launch of Startup India initiative on 16th January 2016, a total of 28,979 Startups have been recognized by DPIIT as on 1st, March 2020. The State wise breakup of these startups is at Annexure-I.(b):	The details of the overall cost involved in these companies with the share of the Government, promoters and loans are not centrally maintained.(c):	The details of the fields/areas in which the DPIIT recognized  Startups were formed are at Annexure-2. As on 1st March 2020, a total number of 3,37,335 employment has been reported by 27,137 DPIIT Recognized startups. The details of total number of employment generated by these companies since the launch of Startup India initiative, State-wise and year-wise are at Annexure-3.(d):	As on 1st, March 2020, a total of 28,979 Startups have been recognised by DPIIT. The details of those wound up is not centrally maintained.******
