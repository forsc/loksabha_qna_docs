                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3606






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Policy for Welfare of Tourism Employees




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether tourism in Odisha was affected during and post-pandemic;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the losses suffered in the industry; (c) 	whether the Government has taken measures to revive the tourism industry in Odisha and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government has initiated schemes or policy for the welfare of tourism employees across the country and specifically for Odisha;(e) 	if so, the details thereof and the total beneficiaries thereunder; and (f) 	the comparative performance of the tourism sector during the years 2020-21 and 2019-20?
