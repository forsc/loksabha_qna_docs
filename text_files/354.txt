                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

26






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Repatriation of Artefacts




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has identified artefacts/ jewels that are still held in museums of foreign countries;     (b)     If so, the details thereof; country-wise; and(c)     the steps taken to get back these treasures?    






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(Prahlad Singh Patel)(a) to (c) A Statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT REFFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 26 FOR 24.06.2019(a)  No Sir. Data on artefacts / jewels that are still held in museums of foreign countries is not available with the ASI.(b) Question does not arise.(c) 	The Archaeological Survey of India is constantly making its best efforts through Indian missions abroad for retrieval of illegally exported antiquities.
