                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3917






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






ST Population of Uttar Pradesh




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the percentage of population of Scheduled Tribes (ST) in Uttar Pradesh at present;(b) the percentage of reservation for ST at present;(c) whether the Union Government has received any proposal to increase the reservation according to the percentage of population from the State Governments of Uttar Pradesh; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a): The population of Scheduled Tribes (ST) in Uttar Pradesh is about 0.6 % of the total population of the State as per Census, 2011.(b) to (d): As informed by Tribal Development Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh that there is a provision of  2  percent reservation for Scheduled Tribes in the State and  no proposal has been sent to Union Government by the State Government to increase the reservation. *****
