                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3593






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Skill Training Domestic Workers




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the initiatives undertaken by the Government to provide skill training to domestic workers; and (b)       the details of such initiatives/schemes undertaken by the Government to provide skill training to domestic and workers working in unorganized sector?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) under which training/orientation is being imparted to candidates in various National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) aligned job roles across 37 sectors, including Domestic Workers Sector. In the Domestic Workers Sector, training/orientation is given to candidates in four job roles namely, child caretaker (non-clinical), elderly caretaker (non-clinical), general housekeeper and housekeeper-cum-cook aligned to the Domestic Workers Sector Skill Council.(b)	Under PMKVY, as on 21.11.2021, around 2 lakh candidates have been trained / oriented in the Domestic Worker Sector. Further, 2.61 lakh candidates have been trained under Domestic Worker Sector Skill Council across various schemes/initiatives other than PMKVY which include CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) / Fee Based projects, and Govt. of India and State funded projects.
