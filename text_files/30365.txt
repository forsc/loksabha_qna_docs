                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1759






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Facilities in Trains




Suresh Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to resume catering and refreshment facility in trains;(b) if so, the details thereof; (c) if not, the reasons therefor; (d) whether it is also proposed to provide bedsheets, pillows, hand towels and blankets in the trains;(e) if so, the time by when it is likely to begin;(f) whether this service is likely to be made available in all the trains; and(g) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c): Ministry of Railways have issued instructions on 19.11.2021 to resume the services of cooked food in trains. The service of Ready To Eat meals will also continue. (d) to (g): With the outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic, as part of precautionary measures linen and blankets have been withdrawn. In view of the continuing COVID-19 pandemic, this precautionary measure will remain in force.*****
