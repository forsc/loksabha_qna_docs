                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

789






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Death Toll Due to Floods




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the death toll have increased due to recent floods in different parts of the country as compared to the previous year; (b) if so, the details thereof, Statewise; (c) whether the Government has released funds to the affected States to carry out the reconstruction of roads and bridges; and (d) if so, the quantum of funds released for the purpose, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	 & (b)  As informed by Disaster Management Division, Ministry of Home Affairs, the States/UTs-wise death toll  due to rain oriented natural calamities during the year 2017-18, 2018-19 and  2019-20 is at Annexure I. (c) & (d) Yes. As informed by Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, the State/ UT wise funds allocated so far during 2019-20 for immediate restoration and for ensuring traffic worthiness of the National Highways damaged due to rainfall/ floods is at Annexure-II.
