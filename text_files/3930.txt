                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Employment Opportunity




Chandan Singh













                                                    Pratima Mondal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has constituted two panels under the leadership of the Prime Minister to increase annual growth rate and the number of employment opportunities in the country;(b)	if so, the facts in this regard;(c)	whether the Government has fixed priority regarding the said matters;(d)	if so, the priorities of the Government in both these areas;(e)	the details of the targets set regarding the objectives to be met by the said panels; and(f)	the reason leading to unaltered unemployment rate in India in the past two years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (f): Yes, Sir. The ‘Cabinet Committee on Investment and Growth’ and the ‘Cabinet Committee on Employment and Skill Development’ have been constituted on 06.06.2019. The Standing Committees of the Cabinet, and their functions are set out in the First Schedule to the Government of India (Transaction of Business) Rules, 1961 (ToB Rules). The said Committees will address issues relating to Investment and Growth and Employment and Skill Development respectively.  Extant composition of the two Committees is annexed.
