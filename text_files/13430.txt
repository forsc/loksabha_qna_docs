                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2092






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






NCC Training to Girls




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    D. M. Kathir Anand
                                                








                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether it is a fact that many schools, colleges including universities do not provide National Cadet Corps (NCC) training to girls;(b) if so, the details thereof during the last five years and the current year and the reasons therefor; and(c) the steps taken/being taken to improve the institutional training imparted to the NCC cadets in schools and colleges?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  No, Sir.  Opportunities to girl cadets are being provided in schools, colleges including universities wherever NCC training is being provided.(c)  The steps taken to improve the institutional training imparted to NCC Cadets are as follows:(i)  	Review of institutional training syllabus was carried out in May-June, 2019 to make it more relevant and contemporary for NCC students.(ii)	385 additional Associate NCC Officers (ANOs)/Care Taker Officer (CTOs) have been commissioned into NCC in 2018.(iii)	Plan to sanction additional 637 Girls Cadet Instructors (GCIs) over a period of 5 years is under implementation.(iv)	Additional vacancies have been catered for in both the Officer Training Academies (OTAs), i.e., OTA Kamptee and OTA Gwalior, to the tune of 575 vacancies and 130 vacancies respectively, to ensure better  training output for ANOs (Male and Female).(v)	Orientation Training Course (PIOC) of all the military posted Permanent Instructor (PI) staff is ensured within first three months of the tenure by respective NCC State Directorates/Group Headquarters.(vi)	At institute levels, following issues are being regularly ensured:(a)	Parades as per programme each week.(b)	Monitoring of ANOs performance.(c)	Warning to poor performing institutes.(d)	Utilisation of existing school classrooms for better media exchange.(e)	Strict conduct of Certificate exams by all State Directorates.*****
