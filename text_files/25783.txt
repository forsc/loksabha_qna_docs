                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Allocation of Coal Blocks




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the private players are allocated coal blocks in the country;(b) if so, the details of the total number of coal blocks that are allocated to private players during the last three years, year-wise and coal block-wise;(c) whether the Government has achieved the target of revenue generation from allocation of coal blocks; and(d) if so, the response of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)  (a) & (b): Yes, and Details of coal blocks allocated to private players during last three years (FY 2018-19 to 2020-21) is at Annexure. (c) & (d): Ministry of Coal does not set any target of revenue generation from allocation of coal blocks.
