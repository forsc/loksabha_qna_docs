                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1799






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Rural Connectivity under PMGSY




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the rural connectivity projects sanctioned in Uttar Pradesh (including the district of Ambedkar Nagar) during the last five years (including but not limited to works sanctioned under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana);(b)	the rural connectivity projects completed by the Ministry in Uttar Pradesh (including the district of Ambedkar Nagar) during the said period;(c)	whether the rural connectivity projects sanctioned during the last five years in Uttar Pradesh (including the district of Ambedkar Nagar) have not yet been completed; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As reported by the State Government of Uttar Pradesh, a total of 825 works measuring length of 6,663.857 Km were sanctioned during the last five years under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in the State of Uttar Pradesh, including 20 road works measuring length of 127.425 Km in the district of Ambedkar Nagar.(b):	A total of 1,225 works measuring length of 8,725.57 Km has been completed in the State of Uttar Pradesh under PMGSY during the last five years, including 32 works of 165.280 Km in Ambedkar Nagar district. The completed road works includes those sanctioned before five years.(c) & (d):	147 works measuring length 1,269.955 Km, including 3 works of Ambedkar Nagar district (18.600 Km)  which were sanctioned during the last five years have not yet been completed. As reported by the State Government, 45 works are under progress while the remaining works are affected due to forest clearance and other issues. *****
