Setting up of National Committee to Commemorate Birth Anniversary of Netaji




Nisith Pramanik

                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has set up a National Committee to 	commemorate the 125th birth anniversary of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose ;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	Whether the National Implementation Committee has approved proposals 	for 	programmes/projects;(d)	if so, the details thereof including funds sanctioned for 	programmes/projects;  and (e)	the other steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?ANSWER






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State (I/C) for Culture & Tourism(Shri Prahlad Singh Patel)(a)	Yes Sir, Government has set up a High Level Committee to commemorate the 125th birth anniversary of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose.(b)	Government has set up a 85 member High Level Committee under the Chairmanship of Hon’ble Prime Minister for commemorating the 125th birth anniversary of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose in befitting manner at national and international level. A copy of Gazette Notification issued on 8 January 2021 is enclosed. (c)	; (d) and (e):	  In the very first meeting of National Implementation Committee held on 25 November, 2020, a presentation was made to the NIC regarding the themes and activities proposed on 75 years of independence by the Committee of Secretaries constituted for this purpose. Commemoration of 125th anniversary of Netaji was part of the proposed commemorations. NIC asked Ministries to dedicate/launch projects during commemoration and to communicate their action plans. Meanwhile, a preparatory meeting under the Chairmanship of Hon’ble Culture Minister was held on 15th December 2020 to discuss modalities for celebration of 125th birth anniversary of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose beginning from 23rd January, 2021. The decisions/ suggestions given during preparatory meeting are to be placed before the NIC for consideration as and when next meeting of NIC takes place. In the meantime, the following projects and programs were organized by Ministry of Culture on 23rd January, 2021, to launch the commemoration (Annexure).” ANNEXUREActivities undertaken at the inaugural event of commemoration of 125th birth anniversary of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose on 23rd January, 2021 at Kolkata:1.	(A) Victoria Memorial Hall & Museum:•	Cultural program (based on INA songs/songs associated with Netaji)•	Exhibition on Netaji•	Projection mapping (Sound & Light show on Netaji)•	Book release•	Release of commemorative stamps(B) Belvedere (National Library)•	International conference on Netaji•	4 day artists’camp2. 	Cultural program at Cuttack, (Netaji’s birth place) on 23rd January, 2021 by South Zone Cultural Centre 3. 	Exhibition on Nand Lal Bose’s iconic “Haripura Panels”at Haripura near Surat on 23rd January, 2021 (where Netaji was first nominated as Congress president).4. 	Declaration of 23rd January as Parakram Diwas. Gazette Notification for this published on 19th January 2021.
