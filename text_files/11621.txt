                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

541






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Extension of Lokmanya Tilak Express




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Railways is considering the request to extend Lokmanya Tilak Express - 18519/18520(Mumbai - Vishakhapatnam) to Rayagada, which is commercially viable without additional expenses apart from serving the needs of tribal dominated undivided Koraput District; (b) if so, the details thereof and the timeline by when this would be extended; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor and the alternate arrangement to address the demands of people?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) No, Sir.  Extension of 18519/18520 Visakhapatnam-Lokmanya Tilak (T) Express upto Rayagada is not feasible, at present, due to operational and resource constraints including non-availability of maintenance facilities at Rayagada. However, passengers of Rayagada/Koraput may avail change-over at Vizianagaram/Visakhapatnam.*****
