                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2172






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Funds to Municipal Corporations




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total funds provided to the three municipal corporations namely Thane, Navi Mumbai and Mira Bhayander by the Union Government under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) scheme so far;(b) the status of development works initiated in these municipal corporations under the said scheme;(c) the details of development works completed along with the total fund incurred thereunder; and(d) the details of development works for which funds are likely to be made available in ensuing years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):	Under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs approves the State Annual Action Plans submitted by the States/Union Territories (UTs) and releases Central Assistance to the respective State/UT as per the Mission guidelines. Central Assistance is not allocated/released city-wiseunder the Mission. (b) & (c):	Details of works initiated under AMRUT by the State of Maharashtra in Mira Bhayander, Navi Mumbai and Thane municipal corporations are placed as Annexure. So far, 6 projects of development of Green Spaces/Parks costing ?9.03 crore have been completed in Navi Mumbai and Thane municipal corporations.(d):	Government of Maharashtra has informed that except the projects under progress in these 3 municipal corporations as per annexure, no further projects are proposed for these cities under AMRUT.                                     *****
