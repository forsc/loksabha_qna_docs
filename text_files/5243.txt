                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Vidyashree Residential Schools




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of places where “Vidyashree” residential schools for Scheduled Caste girls are being operated by the Government; (b) whether any fund has been allocated by the Government for effective implementation of the said scheme and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has introduced any monitoring mechanism in this regard; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI THAAWARCHAND GEHLOT)(a) to (d):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  Statement referred to in reply to Part (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 439 raised by Shri Parbathhai Savabhai Patel and Shri Naranbhai Kachhadiya for 23.07.2019 regarding “Vidyashree Residential School”(a)	: At present, no scheme named “Vidyashree” is being implemented by the Ministry.(b)	to (d) : Does not arise.
