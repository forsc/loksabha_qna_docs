                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

77






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Implementation of PMAY




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) has been successful and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government proposes to increase the interest  subsidy for the first time home buyers in urban areas and if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the current status of the Rajiv Awas Yojana?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.                                          *****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY OF PARTS (a) to (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *77 FOR ANSWER ON 21.11.2019 REGARDING IMPLEMENTATION OF PMAY.(a)	Yes Sir. Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)], against an assessed demand of around 1.12 Crore houses in urban areas of the States/UTs, 93.00 lakh houses have so far been sanctioned, of which 55.40 lakh houses have been grounded and 28.06 lakh houses have been completed/occupied.(b)	No Sir. (c)	Rajiv Awas Yojana (RAY) has been discontinued and liabilities against the 183 ongoing  RAY projects have been subsumed in PMAY(U).                                            *****
