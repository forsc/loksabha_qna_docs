                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3120






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






User Development Fee at Airport




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  proposes  to  increase the  user  development  fees,  parking  fees,  landing  fees and  housing fees  at  airports;(b) if so, the details thereof including the percentage of increase in price since 2017;(c) whether the Government is considering not to increase such charges during the Covid-19 pandemic, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of the amount earned by the airports in Kerala through the user development fees since 2014?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c): User Development Fee, parking fees, landing fees and housing fees are determined separately for Major and Non-major Airports. To recover the entitled Revenue Requirement, aeronautical charges consisting of Landing, Parking and UDF etc. are approved by Airports Economic Regulatory Authority of India (AERA) / Ministry of Civil Aviation (MoCA). As per the AERA Act, 2008, tariff for major airports is determined by Independent Economic Regulator i.e. AERA for a period 5 years. For non-major airports, tariffs are determined by MoCA based on return on the capital invested, operating expenses, taxation and depreciation etc. For non-major airports, tariffs for domestic flights have been revised w.e.f. 01.10.2020 after the last  revision in the year 2009.;(d).
