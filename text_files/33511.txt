                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Allocation of Funds for Agro Processing Clusters




Midhun Reddy













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil
                                                








                                                    Maddila  Gurumoorthy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has allocated funds to the States of Andhra Pradesh and Maharashtra under the Scheme for Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters for the year 2021-22 and if so, the details thereof (b)	the details of the total number of agro-processing clusters operationalized during the year 2021-22 under the Scheme for Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters; and (c)      the details of the number of food processing units operationalized in Agro Processing clusters during the year 2021-22, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	The Scheme for Creation of Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters (APC) scheme is not state specific, therefore, state wise funds are not allocated under the scheme. However, during the year 2021-22, this Ministry has approved grants-in-aid of Rs. 23.584 Cr. for 3 projects in States of Andhra Pradesh and Maharashtra under APC scheme as per below details:(Rs. in Crore)State	No. of projects approved	Grants-in-aid approvedAndhra Pradesh	1	5.879Maharashtra	2	17.7053Total	3	23.5843 (b):	The details of 5 agro processing clusters wherein core processing infrastructure & basic enabling infrastructure have been completed during the year 2021-22 under the Scheme for Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters are at Annexure-I.(c):	the details of the 7 food processing units operationalized in Agro Processing clusters during the year 2021-22 are at Annexure-II. *****
