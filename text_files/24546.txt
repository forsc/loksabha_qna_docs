                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

377






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






PMFBY Beneficiaries




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the beneficiaries under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) in the last two years, State-wise; (b) the funds utilized for the said scheme in the State of Bihar; and (c) the number of insurance companies which are empanelled for the implementation of the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) : States-wise details of number of farmer applications enrolled and premium subsidy provided to insured farmers during last two years i.e. 2019-20 and 2020-21 under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana  are Annexed.(b) : Bihar State Government  implemented the PMFBY in 2016-17 and 2017-18 and discontinued the scheme from 2018-19.  No State-wise allocation or release is made under the scheme.  Being a Central Sector Scheme, funds towards committed liabilities of Government on account of premium subsidy are released to the Central nodal agency, which in turn transfers the funds to the concerned insurance companies.   Total Premium subsidy of Rs. 2060.94 crore has been provided for the years 2016-17 and 2017-18 to the farmers of Bihar State by the Central and State Government in the ratio of 50 : 50.(c) : All the 5 Public Sector general insurance companies and 14 Private Sector general insurance companies have been empanelled by the Government of India for implementation of PMFBY in the country.  However, specific implementing insurance company is selected by the concerned State Government through a transparent bidding process.
