                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1879






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Hepetitis-B




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                             Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government have taken any measures for prevention of the deadly disease of Hepatitis;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any treatment for the people infected by deadly diseases like Hepatitis-B is available in the country; and(d)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTHAND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b): The Government has launched National Viral Hepatitis Control Program (NVHCP) in 2018, under the aegis of  National Health Mission (NHM), offering diagnostic and treatment services for Hepatitis C, A and E. The scope of the program was expanded to include diagnosis and management of Hepatitis B in 2019. Hepatitis B is a vaccine preventable disease & the program provides for administration of Hepatitis B birth dose vaccine & Hepatitis B Immunoglobulin to the babies born to Hepatitis B positive mothers. The NVHCP envisages prevention of Viral Hepatitis through awareness generation and behaviour change communication. All diagnostic and treatment services are provided free of cost under NVHCP at the designated Government health facilities.(c) & (d): Lifelong treatment of Hepatitis-B is available.  States/UTs are provided support under NHM based on the proposals received from them for treatment of Hepatitis B for those who require the same. The list of treatment sites, State/UT wise, is given at Annexure.
