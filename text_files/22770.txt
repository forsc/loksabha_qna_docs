                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3727






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Industry and Investors Facilitation Centre




Raksha Nikhil Khadse













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government proposes to establish an Industry and Investors Facilitation Centre to provide correct and timely information on policies and programme of the Government and to resolve the investors issues;(b)  whether the Facilitation Centre will work as a fast track mechanism in resolving the issues of renewable energy projects and taking the Ease of Doing Business (EODB) to a notch higher; and(c)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)to(c)MNRE has constituted a Project Development Cell (PDC) to handhold and facilitate domestic and foreign investors interested in setting up RE projects/manufacturing units or financing such activities in India. The PDC promotes Ease of Doing Business; works toward resolving investor issues; and reaches out to potential investors, providing them correct and timely information on policies and programmes of Central and State Governments.*****
