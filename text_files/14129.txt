                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2886






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Strength of Railway Protection Force




Amol Ramsing Kolhe













                                                    Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Ramcharan Bohra
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)		       the sanctioned and actual strength of the Railway Protection Force (RPF) including women constables along with their deployment, zone-wise; (b)	       whether many posts of Railway Protection Force is still vacant and not filled, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c)            whether Railway Protection Force has been able to achieve the objective of protecting and security of railway property, if so, the details thereof and if not, the corrective steps being taken in this regard;(d)      whether the Government has approved setting up of one Commando Training Center under Works Programme of Railways;(e)       if so, whether the Government has identified suitable and adequate land for setting up of the Commando Training Center, if so, the details thereof; and(f)	         the steps being taken/proposed to be taken to strengthen RPF during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
