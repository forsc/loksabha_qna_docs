Protected Monuments in Bihar




Chirag Paswan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the monument and location-wise details of the protected monuments in Bihar as on date;(b)	the amount of funds spent by the Government on the maintenance/ protection and     construction thereof monument-wise during the last three years; (c)	whether the State Government has sent a request to the Centre for including more monuments in the list of protected monuments; and(d)	if so, the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER
