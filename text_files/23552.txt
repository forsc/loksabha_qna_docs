                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4578






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






E-Visa for All Countries




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is contemplating e-visa facility for all the countries;(b) if so, the time by when and the details of regional offices identified thereof; and(c) the details for the procedure of the proposed e-visa?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	E- Visa facility is granted to nationals of various countries after taking into consideration, inter-alia, the issues of security, inbound tourism and investments, bi-lateral relations etc.  Extension of this facility is an ongoing process.   Presently, citizens of 171 countries can avail themselves of e- Visa facility extended by India. *******
