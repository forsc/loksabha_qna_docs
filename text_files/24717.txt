                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

627






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Road Projects in Bihar




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of National Highway (NH) and other road transport projects being undertaken in Bihar at present;(b) the details of projects running behind schedule; and(c) the details of projects which have been sanctioned but the work thereon is yet to commenced? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c) 95 National Highway projects are being undertaken in Bihar at present. The 27 projects running on schedule (Annexure-I) and 42 projects are running behind schedule (Annexure-II). The balance 26 projects sanctioned recently (Annexure-III) and the work thereon is yet to be commenced.
