                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2697






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Corruption Cases registered by CBI




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) has registered corruption cases during the last three years; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the total number of corruption cases registered by CBI current year; and (d) 	the extent to which these are less or more in comparison to the cases of last year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b):   Corruption cases registered by the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) during the last three years are as under:  -Year	No. of cases registered2018	4602019	3962020	425(c) & (d):  Total number of corruption cases registered by CBI in current year i.e. in 2021 till 31.01.2021 is 39. Comparison to the cases of last year is given below:Year	No. of cases registered	Increase/Decrease from Last year- No. of cases (in %)2018	460 (632 in 2017)	-172 (27.21 %)2019	396	-64 (13.91 %)2020	425	+29 (7.32%)*****
