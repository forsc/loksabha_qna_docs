                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3358






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Leak of Sensitive Information




Faizal P.P. Mohammed













                                                    Jasbir Singh Gill
                                                








                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                








                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                








                                                    K. Subbarayan
                                                








                                                    Vincent H Pala
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken cognizance of leak of whatsapp chat on confidential and sensitive information including scrapping of Article 370 which surfaced during investigation of TRP scam by Mumbai Police;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has conducted any inquiry in this regard;(d)	if so, the details and the outcome thereof; and(e)	the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) :		 No Such information has come to the notice of the Government.(b) to (e) :   Question does not arise in view of (a) above ******
