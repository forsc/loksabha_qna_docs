                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Port at Dugarajapatnam




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government proposes to set up a port     at Dugarajapatnam as promised under the Andhra Pradesh     Reorganisation Act, 2014; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons     therefor; (c) whether the Union Government has received any alternate     location from the State Government for the same; and (d) whether there are any other plan to develop ports in     Andhra Pradesh as promised under the Andhra Pradesh     Reorganisation Act, 2014 and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)&(b)  The development of new Major Port at Duggirajupatnam in Andhra Pradesh is included in Schedule XIII of the Andhra Pradesh Re-organisation Act, 2014. NITI Aayog had examined the proposal for development of new Major Port at Duggirajupatnam in consultation with Ministry of Shipping, Ministry of Finance and Government of Andhra Pradesh and it has been concluded that the project will not be viable due to strong competition from nearby Ports of Krishnapatnam, Ennore and Chennai which are 40, 80 & 80 KMs away respectively from the proposed Port. (c)	Yes, Sir. State Government of Andhra Pradesh had requested the Government of India for providing financial assistance for development of Ramayapatnam as a Major Port in lieu of Duggirajupatnam. (d)	No, Sir. State Government of Andhra Pradesh has already notified the port limits of Ramayapatnam Port as a Non Major Port in Andhra Pradesh.                                                       *****
