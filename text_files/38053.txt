                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4601






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Roads in Coastal Areas




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government proposes to construct sturdy roads 	in the coastal areas of the country; and (b) if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken by the Government in this regard so far? 






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	& (b) Different components of National Highways like pavement, bridges, structures etc. are designed and constructed as per Indian Roads Congress (IRC) standards & guidelines and specifications and policy guidelines of Ministry of Road Transport & Highways. These standards, guidelines and specifications have provision for design and construction of different components of National Highways in different climate and exposure conditions including coastal areas. Design and construction of National Highways in coastal areas is done meeting the appropriate requirements of standards, guidelines and specifications.*****
