                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Construction of ROBs in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of suggestions/proposals received for construction of Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) in Jharkhand especially in Dhanbad, Bokaro and Giridih districts during the last three years; (b)	the number of railway crossings where sanction has been granted for the construction of the said ROBs;and(c) 	the present status of each sanctioned ROBs and proposed ROBs in Jharkhand during the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): Total 13 proposals have been received for construction of Road Over Bridges (ROBs) in lieu of level crossings in the State of Jharkhand, including Dhanbad, Bokaro and Giridih districts during the last three years.  Out of these, 6 ROBs have been sanctioned and 7 proposals are under process of sanction during 2020-21.  (c): As on 01.04.2020, total 40 ROB works are appearing in Pink Book falling in the State of Jharkhand. Present status of these ROBs is as under-•	3 ROBs: fully completed.•	5 ROBs: Railway portion completed•	11 ROBs: Works are in progress.•	16 ROBs: Alignment plan is under finalisation.•	5 ROBs: Consent of State Govt for cost sharing is awaited.    *****
