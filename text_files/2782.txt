                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2496






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Watershed Development Scheme for Rainfed Areas




B. Y. Raghavendra














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the achievements made under the Watershed Development Scheme for rainfed areas during the last three years, State-wise particularly in Karnataka; (b) the average of agriculture land brought under this scheme, State-wise; and(c) the details of the future programme in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a)	The State-wise achievements including that of Karnataka under the Watershed Development Component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi SinchayeeYojana (WDC-PMKSY) for rainfed areas are at Annexure.(b)	WDC-PMKSY does not envisage agriculture land to be brought under the scheme, rather, it envisages culturable wasteland and degraded land to be brought under protective irrigation. The State-wise details such as land brought under protective irrigation duringthe last three years is given at column (d) of Annexure. (c)	The WDC-PMKSY is an ongoing scheme which will continue till March, 2020.****
