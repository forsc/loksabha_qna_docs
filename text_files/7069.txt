                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Development of Ganga River as Inland Waterway




Durga Prasad Rao Balli














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government is developing Ganga River as inland waterway;(b)	if so, the steps being taken in this direction and expenditure incurred thereon; and(c)	whether the Government proposes to operate Ro-Ro Ferry and Ro-Pax Ferry on the said National Waterway and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR SHIPPING(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b)  Yes, Sir.  Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) is implementing the Jal Marg Vikas Project (JMVP) at an estimated cost of Rs. 5369.18 crore for capacity augmentation of navigation on the Haldia–Varanasi stretch National Waterway-1 (NW-1) with the technical and financial assistance of the World Bank.   Projects worth Rs. 1800 crore (approx.) have commenced on the ground in a time period of three years after statutory clearances and an expenditure of Rs. 1447.43 crore has been incurred up to 31.10.2019.   Multi Modal Terminals at Varanasi (UP) and Sahibganj (Jharkhand) have been completed.(c)       	No, Sir.   However, the interventions in JMVP include five pairs of Ro-Ro terminals at Rajmahal (Jharkhand) - Manikchak (West Bengal), Samdaghat (Jharkhand)-Manihari (Bihar), Kahalgaon-Tintangha (Bihar), Hasnapur-Bakhtiyarpur (Bihar), Buxer (Bihar)-Saraikota (Uttar Pradesh). *******
