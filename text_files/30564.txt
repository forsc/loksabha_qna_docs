                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Vacancies in Tribunals




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) number of vacancies in various Tribunals as of November, 2021, tribunal-wise;(b) the steps being taken by the Government to appoint/fill up vacancies in these tribunals; and(c) the timeline by which the appointments are expected to be completed for all the vacant seats, tribunal-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a)	to (c). As per information presently available about three tribunals, it is informed that the following is the vacancy position. The Vacancy status of Tribunals under Department of Revenue is as underName of the Tribunal 	Name of the Post	Sanctioned strength 	Vacancies CESTAT	President 	1	0	Member (Judicial)	16	6	Member (Technical)	16	11AT(SAFEMA)	Chairman 	1	1	Member	4	4		There are 29 vacant posts of Accountant Members and 28 vacant posts of Judicial Members in the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT). In respect of these vacancies 22 recommendation .of names have been approved for appointment and offers have been issued to all recommended 22 candidates. 		The selection process to fill up the vacant posts of Members, Chairperson in Tribunals is a continuous process and the same has been initiated for the vacant post of Members in CESTAT and vacant posts of Chairperson and Members in Appellate Tribunal (SAFEMA).
