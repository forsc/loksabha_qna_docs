                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Development of Animal Husbandry, Dairy and Fisheries




Sunil Dattatray Tatkare













                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the percentage of Gross Domestic Product (GDP) spent on animal husbandry, dairy and fisheries during each of the last four years, State-wise; (b) whether the Government has achieved the growth rate targets in the animal husbandry sector, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) the details of the various schemes being implemented by the Government for the development of animal husbandry, dairy and poultry in various States during the last three years and the current year; (d) whether the Government has conducted any assessment regarding the working and performance of various schemes under animal husbandry, dairy and fisheries during the last three years and if so, the details thereof; and (e) whether the Government has developed a central monitoring system to implement its schemes properly, if so, the details thereof along with the further steps taken/being taken by the Government for better development of animal husbandry, dairy and fisheries in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING  (SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)	(a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the HouseSTATEMENT REFFERED TO IN LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 227                     PART (a) TO (e) ON DEVELOPMENT OF ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRY AND FISHEIRES TO BE ANSWERED ON 09-07-2019.(a)	The percentage Gross Domestic Product (GDP) spent on animal husbandry and fishery sectors are under compilation as expenditure figures are to be collected from various States and Union Territories.(b)	As per the outcomes of Integrated Sample Survey, the growth rate for major livestock products such as milk, egg and meat during the year 2017-18 are 6.6% 8.0%   and 3.7% respectively.(c)	In order to complement and supplement efforts made by the States and Union Territories, Government of India has been implementing following schemes for the development of animal husbandry, dairy and poultry.1.	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)2.	National Dairy Plan-I (NDP-I)3.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS)4.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund Scheme (DIDF)5.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities (SDCFPO).6.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission7.	Breed Improvement Institutes8.	National Livestock Mission9.	Livestock Health and Disease Control Programme(d) and (e) The assessment of performance of various schemes are undertaken by way of regular monitoring of the schemes through review meetings, video conferencing sessions, etc. Further, third party evaluation of all schemes of the sector are done periodically. An evaluation study of National Dairy Plan Phase – I was conducted during 2017 and the guidelines of Dairy Development Schemes provide for monitoring of schemes both at Central and State Government level. Government has also constituted Central Monitoring Units for monitoring and evaluation of semen stations and Artificial Insemination (AI) training institutes. Further, under Fisheries a two tier system such as Central and State level approval and monitoring committees have been institutionalized for the implementation of Blue Revolution*******
