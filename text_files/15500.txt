                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

375






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Smart Cities in Goa




Francisco Cosme Sardinha














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of the cities in Goa selected among the major 100 cities in the country by the Government under the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) along with the phases in which these cities have been selected; (b) 	the names of cities where work has been started after allocation of budget under the said Mission, State-wise; (c) 	whether requisite budget was allocated for Panaji city also after its selection for development as smart city under the said Mission and if so, the funds allocated and spent so far thereon along with the period during which such funds were allocated; and (d) 	whether the work in Panaji city has almost stalled due to the administrative laxity?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THEMINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.                                  STATEMENTSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY OF PARTS (a) to (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *375 FOR ANSWER ON 19.03.2020 REGARDING “SMART CITIES IN GOA”.(a) to (d) Panaji Smart City in Goa was selected in Fast Track Round of the Smart Cities Challenge in May 2016.All 100 Smart Cities in the country have already started implementing their Smart City Proposals. The State-wise list of 100 Smart Cities is given in Annexure-I.So far, Government of India has released Rs. 196 crore for the Panaji Smart City, of which Rs. 106 crore has been utilized by the Smart City.Out of total 42 projects worth Rs. 1,556.4 crore in Panaji Smart City, 4 projects worth Rs. 43.6 crore have been tendered, 9 projects worth Rs. 333.5 crore have been grounded and 9 projects worth Rs. 14.7 crore have been completed.                                              *****
