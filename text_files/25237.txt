                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1007






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Auction of Oil and Gas Fields




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has not made use of ONGC and Oil India Limited inincreasing hydrocarbon production till now;(b) whether the Government proposes to auction large oil and gas fields; and(c) if so, whether the Government has taken any steps in this regard so far and if so,the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c)The bulk of production of oil & gas in the country comes from ONGC andOil India Ltd (OIL). The total production of oil and gas in the country during 2020-21 was 30.49 Million Metric tons of oil and 28.67 Billion cubic metre of gas.ONGC and OIL produced 76% of oil and 85% of gas out of total production.The Government introduced the policy reforms on 28th Feb, 2019, in explorationand licensing policy for enhancing domestic exploration and production. Thereforms provides that in respect of the large and marginal nomination fields,ONGC and OIL can choose field specific production enhancement modelsincluding farm out and Joint Ventures/ Technical Service Model.*****
