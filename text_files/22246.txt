                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Aatmanirbhar at Panchayat Level




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any scheme has been formulated by the Government for village/Panchayat/district level to move ahead for making the country “Aatmanirbhar”;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a)  to (c):  The Ministry of Panchayti Raj is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Sawraj Abhiyan (RGSY) to strengthen the Panhcyati Raj Intuitions (PRIs) with the main thrust of developing their governance capabilities, inter alia, on optimum utilization of available resources and convergence with other schemes to address issues of national importance, raise their own sources of revenue towards their endeavour for financial self-reliance and deliver on the sustainable development goals to contribute thereby towards Aatmanirbhar Bharat.     ****
