                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

857






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Houses for Homeless People




Deepak (Dev) Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of people who have got houses under Pradhan  Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) so far;(b) the total number of homeless people in the country; and(c) the timeline by which all the people will get the houses under PMAY?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)to(c): Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) (PMAY(U)) since 25.06.2015 for providing assistance to States/ Union Territories (UTs) for addressing the housing requirement in urban areas. States/UTs have undertaken demand survey under the scheme for assessing actual demand of housing.  The validated demand reported by States/UTs so far is around 1.12 crore .Based on the project proposals received so far from the States/UTsunder the Scheme, a total 1,03,22,560 houses have been sanctioned; out of this, 60,50,991 are at various stages of construction and 32,07,573 are completed/delivered. As per Census–2011, total houseless population in the country is 17,73,040 (Urban - 9,38,348 and Rural - 8,34,692).States/UTs have been requested to get the project proposals for all their remaining demand of houses under the PMAY(U) sanctioned by March, 2020 so that construction of all houses may progressively be completed by 2022.Further, Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) since 01.04.2016 to achieve the objective of “Housing for All” in rural areas by 2022. As on 01.02.2020, total 139.02 lakh houses have been sanctioned; out of which, 93.90 lakh houses have been constructed so far under the Scheme. The timeline for achieving the target of “Housing for All” in rural areas under the PMAY-G is March, 2022.                                           ******
