                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

606






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Launching of Mobile App ‘Yoga Locator’




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched a mobile App ‘Yoga Locator’ in   compliance of ‘Digital India’ campaign;(b) if so, the objectives thereof; (c) the number of yoga centres and yoga trainers registered on the said app all over the country, State-wise including Gujarat; and (d) whether the Government is taking any steps to create awareness regarding use of ‘Yoga Locator’ App and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINISTRY OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	Yes, sir. The Ministry of Ayush has launched “Namaste Yoga Mobile Application” an updated version of Yoga Locator Mobile Application in the year 2021. (b)	The objective of the Namaste Yoga Mobile Application is to provide information to public to look for yoga centres, yoga trainers, yoga events, videos on yoga asanas, etc. The application has navigation facility. The fitness tracker feature of Namaste Yoga mobile application helps to monitor daily steps and calories burnt by the user. The application is available on both Android and iOS platforms. (c)	The detail of the total number of yoga centres registered on the app all over the country, state-wise including Gujarat as on 02/02/2022 is at Annexure ‘A’. The number of yoga trainers registered on the app all over the country, state-wise including Gujarat as on 02/02/2022 is at Annexure ‘B’.(d)	The ‘Namaste Yoga App’, an updated version of the Yoga locator App was launched by the Ayush Minister in the year 2021. Ministry of Ayush has utilized its Social Media Platforms for creating awareness regarding use of the Application. Further, it also channelised Press Media and Electronic media as a result of which the launch of the Application was covered widely by prominent newspapers & electronic media.**********
