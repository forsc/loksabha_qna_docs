                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Agricultural Production




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantum (in kg) of India’s top ten crops (in terms of quantity) that arrived in agricultural markets since 2014,year-wise;(b)	the quantity produced (in kg) of India‘s top ten crops since 2014, year- wise;(c)	whether those people involved with selling these crops outside designated markets have been prosecuted, since 2014 till August, 2020; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	The quantum (in kg) of India’s top ten crops, arrived in agricultural markets since 2014 (as per 4th Advance Estimates of 2019-20) is given at Annexure- I and their production is given at Annexure-II. (c) & (d):	The selling of crops in designated markets during the period 2014 to August, 2020 is governed by Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) Act, which is implemented by State Governments who take actions in case of violation of Act.
