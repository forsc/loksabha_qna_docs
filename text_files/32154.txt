                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3617






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Poaching Cases




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of any poaching cases of endangered and rare wildlife (listed in Scheduled I, II, III and IV of Indian Wildlife Act 1972) in twin Districts of Ladakh, if so, the details of cases registered since 2014;(b)	the details of culprits convicted as per law and the details of the outcome of these cases and if not, the reaction of the Government thereto; and(c)	whether the Government has any report of Wildlife Crimes and Wildlife Trades from UT Ladakh in the recent years and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)and (b) The protection and management of wildlife and its habitat is undertaken by the concerned States/Union Territories. Information pertaining to poaching cases is not collated at the level of the Ministry.(c) As per the records available in the Wild Life Crime Control Bureau, two wildlife cases relating to killing of Urial sheep have been reported in Ladakh. The cases were detected in Nimmo and Gaya Meru. *****
