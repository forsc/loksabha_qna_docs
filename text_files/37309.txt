                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Employment under MGNREGS




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds released and spent under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) since April, 2021, State and year-wise;(b) 	the number of registered migrant labourers/families provided employment under MGNREGS during the said period, State and year-wise; and(c) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the number of days from 100 days under MGNREGS in view of the loss of livelihood due to COVID-19 global pandemic and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)  State/UT-wise details of fund released under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) during the current financial year 2021-22 (as on 23.03.2022) is given at Annexure I.  (b)   State/UT-wise number of households who have been offered employment under the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in the current financial year 2021-22 (as on 23.03.2022) is given at Annexure-II. No separate date of migrant workers is maintained under the programme. (c)  Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act  (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) is to provide at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment in a financial year to each household in rural areas of the country whose adult members volunteers to do unskilled manual work. In addition to this, there is a provision for upto additional 50 days of unskilled wage employment in a financial year in drought/natural calamity notified rural areas. As per Section 3 (4) of the Act, the State Governments may make provision for providing additional days of employment beyond the period guaranteed under the Act from their own funds. *****
