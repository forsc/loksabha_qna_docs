                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1796






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Astroturf Hockey Grounds




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details regarding number of Astroturf hockey grounds in India, State-wise;(b) whether the Government intends to construct/provide/built more Astroturf grounds in the country and if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government intends to construct/provide more Astroturf grounds in Punjab and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) Most of the Astroturf hockey grounds in various parts of the country are under the jurisdiction of respective bodies/departments of State/UT Governments. As such, the information about them is not maintained in this Ministry. However, the details regarding the Astroturf hockey grounds under Sports Authority of India are given at Annexure - I.(b) and (c) Yes, Sir. The Union Government under its Khelo India Scheme, which is a demand driven scheme, has already sanctioned a total of twenty-one AstroTurf hockey grounds/ synthetic hockey turfs/ hockey fields in the country, including one in the State of Punjab. The details regarding the same are at Annexure – II.
