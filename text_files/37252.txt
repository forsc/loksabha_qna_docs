                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Scope of NCDC




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to expand the scope of the National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC);(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the steps taken by the Government to ensure cooperation and coordination amongst multiple Ministries/departments?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF COOPERATION ???????? ?????? 	 (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) & (b) National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) is governed by NCDC Act, 1962. Section 9 of NCDC Act describes the functional scope of NCDC.(c) The Ministry of Cooperation has a mandate of “general Policy in the field of Co-operation and Co-ordination of cooperation activities in all sectors where the Ministries concerned are responsible for Co-operatives in the respective fields.” The Ministry of Cooperation is facilitating cooperation and coordination amongst multiple Ministries/departments through regular communication and consultation with Stakeholders. Central Government Ministries have been requested for integration of cooperatives both as participant and beneficiary with the existing schemes and programmes of government. ******
