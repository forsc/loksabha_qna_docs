Training to Staff Appointed in AWS




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF EARTH SCIENCESLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1956TO BE ANSWERED ON FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 12, 2021TRAINING TO STAFF APPOINTED IN AWS1956. 	SHRI GOPAL CHINNAYA SHETTY:Will the Minister of EARTH SCIENCES be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the staff appointed in Automated Weather Stations (AWS) are suitably trained to dispose of their duty of monitoring data and providing accurate information, if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether regular inspections are being conducted to ensure quality control, calibration and utilization of data obtained from AWS to develop a weather forecast model; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND MINISTRY OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a)Automatic Weather Stations are automatic stations, for which no dedicated staff is  required on day to day basis for its operation. However, Surface Instruments Maintenance & Monitoring Unit at IMD, Pune is monitoring the functioning of AWS network and imparts periodic training to the Met Officials at field stations across India in servicing, calibration & maintenance of AWS. (b) & (c)Met Officials posted at 206 Synoptic Meteorological stations across the country are maintaining AWS within 150Km distance from their observatory and carry out servicing, comparison with standard & Calibration in order to ensure quality of AWS data.List of IMD owned Synoptic Observatories taking part in Servicing/Calibration/Comparison with standards of AWS within 150KM from these observatories are given in Annexure.AnnexureS.No	Name of observatory	Distirict	State1	Port blair	South Andaman	A.N.
