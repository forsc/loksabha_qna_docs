                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2403






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Coastal Employment Zones




Bhagwanth Khuba













                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to bring a new law to     govern ports and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is a fact that in a bid to promote port-land     development, the Government has sought land from various     States for setting up a Coastal Employment Zone and if     so, the details thereof; and (c)  whether the Government has included Karnataka and      Tripura State for setting up a Coastal Employment Zone,      if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons      therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Sir, the Cabinet has approved Ministry’s proposal for replacing The Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 with a new legislation. The Major Port Authorities Bill, 2020 is expected to be introduced in the current Budget Session of the Parliament. The new legislation is aimed at providing more operational autonomy to the Major Ports and facilitate decision making.(b) & (c) Ministry of Shipping is examining the possibility of development of CEZ on land available with Major Ports initially as a prototype. 	                                    *****
