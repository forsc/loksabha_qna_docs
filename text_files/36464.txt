                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3902






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






License for Manufacturing of AYUSH-64




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of companies that came forward to obtain license for manufacturing AYUSH-64, state-wise;(b) whether there is any possibility of the transfer of technology to such companies through the Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) and National Research and Development Center (NRDC);(c) if so, whether the Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) is likely to provide technical assistance in the manufacturing of AYUSH-64  to medicine manufacturers; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	The technology of AYUSH-64 was transferred to the interested manufacturers through an agreement between National Research Development Corporation (NRDC) and Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS). The list of 46 manufacturers to whom the technology of AYUSH-64 is transferred by the concerned agencies is placed at Annexure-I. The manufacturers after technology transfer obtain license of the product from the concerned State Drug Licensing Authority (SLA). The details of approvals given to such companies by respective SLAs for manufacturing of AYUSH-64 are placed at Annexure-II.(b)	Yes, the details and the procedure are annexed at Annexure III. (c)	Yes, Sir.(d)	After technology transfer to a manufacturer by NRDC and CCRAS, the complete know-how document of AYUSH-64 containing all relevant information required for production is provided to the manufacturer. If required by the manufacturer, provision for training is also available. Also, to facilitate the stake holders, CCRAS has issued a Notification dated 30.04.2021 for commercialization of AYUSH-64 developed by CCRAS.
