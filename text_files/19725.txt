                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1350






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Upliftment of Safai Karamcharis




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.  †1350TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021UPLIFTMENT OF SAFAI KARAMCHARIS†1350.     SHRI DILIP SAIKIA:                SHRI RAMESH CHANDER KAUSHIK: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the schemes being run by the Government for the upliftment of sanitation workers/safai karamcharis in the country, State-wise including Assam and Haryana;(b) whether the Government has received any type of complaint regarding the schemes run by the State Governments for the upliftment of sanitation workers; (c) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon;(d) the quantum of funds allocated by the Government after the year 2014, State-wise;(e) whether the Government has fixed any responsibility for the irregularities in these schemes; and (f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a):   Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, through its Apex Corporation, National Safai Karamcharis Finance and Development Corporation (NSKFDC) has been implementing various loan and non-loan based schemes and programmes for upliftment of Safai Karamcharis all over the country including Assam and Haryana.   Details are given at Annexure-I. (b) and (c):  No Sir.(d):The details of State-wise funds allocated to the States from Financial Year 2014-15 to 2021-22 (upto 31.01.2021) are at Annexure-II.(e) and (f): No such irregularities in the schemes, implemented by NSKFDC, have been reported in this Department.**** Annexure-IDetails of loan and non-loan based schemes and programmes for upliftment of Safai Karamcharis by NSKFDCS. No.	Name of the scheme 1	General Term Loan (GTL)2	Mahila Adhikarita Yojana (MAY)3	Mahila Samridhi Yojana (MSY)4	Micro Credit Finance (MCF)5	Education loan (EL)-For study in India-For study abroad6	Green Business (GB)7	Scheme for “Pay and Use” community toilets 8	Swachhta Udyami Yojana (SUY)(Scheme for procurement of sanitation related equipments/vehicles)9	Individual / SHG / JRG /Cooperative10	Municipal Corporation/Jal Boards, Public Health & Engineering Departments, Cantonment Boards, Railways etc.11	Skill Development Training Programmes (SDTP)*** Annexure-IIStatement showing State-wise funds disbursed by NSKFDC from 2014-15 to 2020-21 as on 31.01.2021(Rs.in Lac)Sl.No.	Name of the State/UT	2014-15 to 2020-21 (as on 31.01.2021)1	Andhra Pradesh	16942.862	Assam	995.623	Bihar	1361.254	Chandigarh	99.235	Chhattisgarh	3865.866	Delhi	1400.007	Gujarat	13724.568	Haryana	134.429	Himachal Pradesh	1662.1010	Jammu & Kashmir	2049.0111	Jharkhand	1732.5512	Karnataka	5036.0113	Kerala	12754.8014	Madhya Pradesh	3218.1515	Maharashtra	5028.2416	Manipur	0.0017	Meghalaya	0.0018	Mizoram	0.0019	Nagaland	613.4920	Odhisha	1545.7521	Puducherry	136.0022	Punjab	2286.0023	Rajasthan	7945.5624	Tamil Nadu	6910.5925	Tripura	1540.4426	Telangana	2211.0527	Uttar Pradesh	14390.6028	Uttarakhand	1349.6729	West Bengal	2121.05	Total	111054.84****
