                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

912






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Solar Microgrids




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of villages which have access to solar microgrids, State/UT-wise;(b) whether such microgrids have been successful in boosting economic activities in such villages;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps being taken to expand the capacity of solar microgrids?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) So far solar micro grids of 2299.2 kWp aggregated capacity have been installed in 73 villages of the country with financial support from the Ministry. State-wise details are given below:State	No. of Solar microgrids installed 	Aggregated Capacity (kWp)Jharkhand	9	210.4Uttar Pradesh	64	2088.8Total	73	2299.2(b) and (c) Solar micro-grids provide better livelihood opportunities to the population living in remote villages through increased productive man-hours, thereby having positive impact on economic activities in these villages, such as operating rice hullers, grain grinders, oil expellers, sewing machines and other small enterprises.(d) Under the Solar Off-grid and Decentralized Applications Programme, the Ministry provided financial support up to 30% of the cost of microgrid systems for installation in the rural areas of the country.*****
