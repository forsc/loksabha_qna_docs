                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Paddy Procurement




Nama Nageswara Rao













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of target set for paddy procurement by Government of India for 2021-22 (Rabi and Kharif seasons); (b) 	the details of paddy procured by GOI from various States during the last five years, year-wise, season-wise and State-wise; (c) 	the reasons that FCI is not procuring balance 5 lakh tonnes of rice produced in Rabi season last year from Telangana; (d) 	whether CM of Telangana has written letter to PM to enhance rice procurement target beyond 40 lakh tonnes during ongoing Kharif season to 90 lakh tonnes from Telangana as has been done in Punjab; and (e) 	if so, the details of action taken on the above request?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): The target set for paddy (in terms of rice) procurement by Government of India for central pool during 2021-22 (Kharif Crop) is 521.89 LMT. Target for Rabi crop are decided before the onset of Rabi season.   (b): 	The details of paddy (in terms of rice) procured by GOI from various States for central pool during the last five years, year-wise, season-wise and State-wise is at Annexure I.(c):  	During Rabi season of KMS 2020-21, a record 61.87 LMT of rice has been procured by State Government/ its agencies against the target of 55.00 LMT of rice fixed in consultation with State Government. The surplus rice offered by the State government is accepted by FCI under central pool stock.(d) & (e):	Yes, Sir. In the States/UTs Food Secretaries meeting held on 17.08.2021, procurement estimate of 40 LMT of rice for the State was  fixed by Government of India in consultation with the State Government and Food Corporation of India (FCI) considering estimated production, marketable surplus and agricultural crop pattern. Increase or decrease of procurement estimate is decided on the basis of estimated production, marketable surplus and agricultural crop pattern.                                          *****
