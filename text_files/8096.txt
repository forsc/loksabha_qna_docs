                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1792






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Privatisation of Air India




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided on the final privatisation of Air India and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has any expected time-period within which such privatisation will be completed;(c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government is offering a 100 per cent stake in the airline to private sectors; and(e) if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (e): The Government is committed to the disinvestment of Air India. Air India Specific Alternative Mechanism (AISAM) has, inter-alia, approved the 100 percent sale of Government of India stake in Air India along with Air India Express Ltd and Air India stake in AISATS. The preparation of Preliminary Information Memorandum (PIM) for inviting Expression of Interest (EOI) for the strategic disinvestment of Air India is in process, the contours of which will be decided by AISAM.
