                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Income from Textile Parks




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has set up textile parks in various regions across the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the annual turnover of the said textile parks as on date;(c)	whether there has been an expected rise in the income of textile sector as a result of setting up the said textile parks; and(d)	if so, the details thereof during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. The Government is implementing the Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP) under which Textile Parks have been set up across India, details of which are at Annexure. The detail of annual turnover of Textile Parks are not centrally maintained and monitored by the Ministry.(c) to (d):  Do not arise.
