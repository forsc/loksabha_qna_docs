                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

425






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Funds for Farm Mechanization




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had recently released funds for various activities of Farm Mechanization like establishment of Custom Hiring Centres, Farm Machinery Bank, High-tech Hubs and distribution of various agricultural machinery etc. to different States; (b)   if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (c)   the funds transferred to the State of Andhra Pradesh in the last two years for the above mission and a breakup of the same of the different heads under which the funds were sanctioned?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	The Department of Agriculture Farmers Welfare is implementing Sub- Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) under which funds have been released to the States for establishment of Custom Hiring Centres, Farm Machinery Banks, Hi-tech Hubs and distribution of various agricultural machinery etc. The statement of State-wise release of funds during 2021-22 (as on 15.07.2021) is enclosed as Annexure-I(c): 	During 2019-20 and 2020-21 an amount of Rs. 271.51 Crore as a Central Share has been released to the Government of Andhra Pradesh under SMAM.
