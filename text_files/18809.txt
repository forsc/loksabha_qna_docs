                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

98






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Operation Green Scheme




Umesh G. Jadhav













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing ‘Operation Greens Scheme’ for the benefit of farmers and consumers in the country, and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government has launched any new scheme under the said scheme recently, if so the details thereof; (c)	the details of fruits and vegetables which are eligible for the subsidy under the new scheme; (d)	the amount of funds allocated for implementation of Operation Green during the last year including the current year; and (e)	the timeline by which the above schemes/plans will be implemented in the country, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES  (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a):	Yes Sir, in pursuance to budget announcement for the year 2018-19, Ministry has launched a new central sector scheme “Operation Greens – a scheme for integrated development of Tomato, Onion and Potato (TOP) value chain” with a budgetary allocation of Rs. 500 crores in November 2018. Under this scheme, during glut situtation subsidy is provided at the rate of 50% for transportation and storage for evacuation of TOP crops from production surplus areas to consumption centres under price stabilisation measures.Further, Grant-in-aid is also provided at the rate of 50% (70% in case of FPOs and SC/ST) of the eligible project cost, subject to maximum of Rs. 50 crores per project, for setting up of integrated value chain development projects in identified clusters in major producing states. (b):	Yes Sir, in pursuance of Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan, subsidy at the rate of 50% for transportation and storage under price stabilization measures in eligible production is extended from TOP crops to a total of 41 notified horticulture crops (TOTAL) for a period of six months, if prices fall below the average prices for last three years or 15% below of previous year’s price. The timelines of the scheme are further extended upto 31.03.2021. Further, transportation subsidy under the scheme is extended to all fruits and vegetables through Kisan Rail Scheme (w.e.f 12.10.2020) provided by Indian Railways , by way of charging 50% of their freight charges. Scheme also provides the transportation subsidy to the notified Fruits and Vegetables being transported by air from the NER and Himalayan Region States, by way of charging 50% freight by Airlines. (c):	The list of notified fruits and vegetables which are eligible for subsidy under new scheme - Operation Greens TOP to TOTAL is as under: -  Fruits- Mango, Banana, Guava, Kiwi, Litchi, Papaya, Mousambi, Orange, Kinnow, Lime, Lemon, Pineapple, Pomegranate, Jackfruit, Apple, Almond, Aonla, Passion fruit, Pear, Sweet Potato, Chikoo.Vegetables: - French beans, Bitter Gourd, Brinjal, Capsicum, Carrot, Cauliflower, Chillies (Green), Okra, Cucumber, Peas, Garlic, Onion, Potato, Tomato, Large Cardamon, Pumpkin, Ginger, Cabbage, Squash and Turmeric (dry). (d) & (e):  The details of the funds allocated for implementation of scheme for the last year and current year are as under:- (Rs. in Crore)Year	BE	RE2019-20	200.00	32.482020-21	127.50	38.20 Operation Greens scheme was approved and implemented to provide Grant-in-aid for setting up of integrated value chain development projects in identified clusters of major producing states and to provide transportation/storage subsidy under price stabilisation measures in all over India in November’2018. Extended Operation Greens scheme-TOP to TOTAL under Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan for transportation/storage subsidy in identified clusters through out India approved and implemented in June, 2020. *****
