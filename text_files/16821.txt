                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

747






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Atal Jyoti Yojana




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the districts included under Atal Jyoti Yojana, State-wise;(b) the quantum of funds spent in each district during the last three years under the said yojana, districtwise;(c) whether solar lights can be installed in Bhiwani, Mahendragarh and Dadri districts of Haryana under the said scheme;(d) if so, the details thereof along with the budget allocated for the said purpose; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) Phase-I of Atal Jyoti Yojana (AJAY) Scheme, launched in September 2016, covered all districts of the states of Assam, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha and Uttar Pradesh. Under Phase-II of AJAY Scheme, launched in December 2018, coverage was expanded to include: i.	All districts of five states covered under Phase-Iii.	All districts of hilly states/UTs i.e. Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh and Uttarakhandiii.	All districts of North Eastern states including Sikkimiv.	All districts of Andaman & Nicobar Islands and Lakshadweepv.	Balance 48 aspirational districts other than those covered in (i) to (iv) above.(b) District-wise details of funds spent in AJAY Phase-I  since its launch in September 2016 are placed at Annexure-I and district-wise details of funds spent in AJAY Phase-II since its launch in December 2018 are placed at Annexure-II.(c) to (e) Bhiwani, Mahendragarh and Dadri districts of Haryana are not covered under the AJAY scheme as only aspirational districts of Haryana state are covered under the scheme.*****
