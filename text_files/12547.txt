                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1525






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






OIC Cards




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Overseas Indian Citizenship (OIC) cards that have been issued by the Government of India so far;(b) the number of Overseas Indian Citizenship cards which have been revoked until January 2020;(c) whether the Government has framed any criteria for revoking the Overseas Indian Citizenship; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	Till January 31, 2020, 35,40,267 Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) cards have been issued.(b):	The data related to cancellation of registration as OCI cardholder is not centrally maintained.  (c) and (d):	   OCI card may be cancelled as provided in the Section 7 D of the Citizenship Act, 1955.*******
