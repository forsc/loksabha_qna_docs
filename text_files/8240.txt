                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

170






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Ayushman Bharat Yojana




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the number of beneficiaries under the Ayushman Bharat Yojana till date, Statewise; (b) the number of hospitals empanelled under the said Yojana, State-wise; (c) the number of beneficiaries provided assistance under the Yojana in Rajasthan and Delhi during the last year along with the funds released and spent in this regard, State-wise; (d) whether the number of applicants under ‘Pradhan Mantri Sahayata Yojana’ has decreased after implementation of the Ayushman Bharat Yojana; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (e):  A statement is laid on the Table of the HouseSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 170* FOR 29TH NOVEMBER, 2019(a) Ayushman Bharat Yojana comprises two components namely (i) Provision of Comprehensive Primary Healthcare  through Ayushman Bharat - Health and Wellness Centers (AB- HWC), and (ii) Ayushman Bharat- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB- PMJAY). Services at Ayushman Bharat- Health & Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs) are free and universal to all.State-wise total number of eligible beneficiaries and number of beneficiaries who have availed the treatment under the AB-PMJAY are at Annexure I. (b) State-wise number of hospitals empanelled under AB- PMJAY are at Annexure II. (c) Based on the proposals received from the States/ UTs, AB-HWC is being implemented in all States/ UTs except UT of Delhi. Details of funds released under AB-HWCs are at annexure III. The Union Territory of Delhi is not implementing AB-PMJAY.  As per data shared by the State Health Agency number of hospital admissions under AB-PMJAY in Rajasthan till 25.11.2019 is 4,36,303. The scheme has been launched in Rajasthan on 1st September, 2019 and the State has not sought any release of funds so far. State-wise details of funds released under AB-PMJAY are at Annexure-IV.(d) & (e) Questions or information pertaining to Prime Minister’s National Relief Fund are not admissible under Rule 41 (2) (viii) and 41 (2) (xvii) of the Rules and Procedure and Conduct of Business in the Lok Sabha since the corpus of the fund is entirely constituted out of voluntary public contributions and not from any allocation from the Consolidated Fund of India.
