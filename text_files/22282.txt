                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3308






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Special Central Assistance to Odisha




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds released under the Special Central Assistance (SCA) for the Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected districts for the State of Odisha during the last three years;(b)	whether there has been any change in the number of LWE affected districts in the States of Odisha, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh and Andhra Pradesh;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the districts of Odisha which have witnessed the largest decline of LWE influence?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Funds released to Odisha under Special Central Assistance (SCA) Scheme during the last 03 years and current financial year are as under:                                                                     (Rs. in crore)State	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Odisha	10	66.67	66.66	14.25-2-L.S.US.Q NO. 3308 FOR 16.03.2021(b) & (c):	The LWE Affected districts, covered under Security Related Expenditure (SRE) Scheme, were reviewed in April-2018. Presently 54 districts of Odisha, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh and Andhra Pradesh are covered under SRE Scheme w.e.f. 01.04.2018. Prior to April-2018, 64 districts of these States were  covered under SRE Scheme. List these districts in the States of Odisha, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh and  Andhra Pradesh is at Annexure-I.    (d):	15 districts of Odisha are covered under Security Related Expenditure (SRE) for Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas. Of these, 7 districts namely Boudh, Sambhalpur,  Nayagarh, Angul, Deogarh, Navarangpur, and Sundergarh have reported no LWE violence incidents in the year 2020.******
