                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3438






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Centrally Sponsored Schemes for Higher Education




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any centrally sponsored scheme(s) for the improvement of higher education in India;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details and number of institutions benefited and targets set and achieved under the schemes for improvement of higher education in India during the last five years, State/Year-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) being implemented by the Ministry of Education. It is an overarching scheme, being operated in mission mode for funding the state universities and colleges in order to achieve the aims of equity, access and excellence. The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) is aimed at inter-alia correcting regional imbalances in access to higher education, especially in un-served and underserved areas by setting up quality higher educational institutions and upgrading the existing ones. All the funding under RUSA is norm based and future grants are performance based and outcome dependent.(c) State/Year wise targets are not fixed under the Scheme. However, State-wise details of number of units approved and release made under the various components of RUSA Scheme are at Annexure -1. State/Year-wise details of the total amount released during the last five years are at Annexure-II.*****
