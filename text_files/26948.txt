                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2734






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Construction of New Railway Lines in Bihar




Chhedi Paswan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the construction of a new railway line with a cost of Rs.106.20 crore from Pahleja to Banjari of Rohtas district of Bihar under East Central Railway, Hazipur was included in the railway budget for 2008-2009;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Rail Budget for the year 2014-15 had a provision of an allocation of Rupees Ten lakh only for construction of a railway line of 36.4 kilometers from Dehri-on-sone to Banjari;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the time by which the above mentioned both proposed railway lines are likely to be constructed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e):  Dehri-On-Sone - Banjari (36Km) New Line project, originating from Pahleza, was included in Railway Budget 2008-09 at  a cost of ?106.20 crore.  A token amount of ?10 Lakh was allocated to this project in the year 2014-15.  Final Location Survey (FLS) has been completed in 2015 at an assessed cost of ?836 crore with (-) 4.15% Rate of Return.  Meanwhile, Railway has reviewed all the projects based on last mile connectivity, missing links, traffic potential on the project, capacity enhancement, availability of land, forest/wild-life clearance etc. and based on the review, Railway projects have been prioritized. Presently, Railway’s focus is on completion of capacity enhancement projects and last mile connectivity projects and Dehri-On-Sone - Banjari New Line project does not come under this priority.*****
