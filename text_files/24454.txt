                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

28






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm




Bhartruhari Mahtab













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the target date fixed vis-a-vis the achievements made under the National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm during the last three years; (b) 	whether the Government has formulated any plan for creation of oilseeds hubs with a focus on regional approach for larger availability of high-yielding quality seeds as well as for training of farmers and extension officials in this field and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the steps taken by the Government to increase the production and productivity of oilseeds and oil palm in the country, especially in Latur, Satara and Solapur districts of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.   28  DUE FOR REPLY ON 20th JULY, 2021. (a):	The production target fixed and achievement made under National Food Security Mission (NFSM)-Oilseeds during 2018-19 to 2020-21 is given below. Production of Oilseeds (lakh tons)2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Target	Achievement	Target	Achievement	Target	Achievement360.00	315.22	361.00	322.19	370.00	365.65 (3rd adv. Estd)The year-wise area expansion target and achievement made under NFSM-Oil Palm during 2018-19 to 2020-21 is given below. Area Expansion under Oil palm (ha)2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Target	Achievement	Target	Achievement	Target	Achievement26157	11807	17780	13274	22815	14090(b):	Under the NFSM (Oilseeds), Government of India has set up 36 oilseeds seed hubs during 2018-19 and 2019-20 with an objective to increase the availability of high yielding quality seed. The crop and state under different Seed hubs are given below: Crops 	No of seed hubs 	StatesRape Seed & Mustard	8	Assam, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, West BengalSoybean	6	Chhattisgarh Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Telangana, KarnatakaGroundnut	6	Tamilnadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Gujarat, Odisha, KarnatakaSesame	2	Sunflower	3	Telangana, West Bengal, KarnatakaSafflower	3	Maharashtra, TelanganaNiger 	3	Madhya Pradesh, Karnataka, OdishaCastor 	3	Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, TelanganaLinseed 	2	Madhya Pradesh, Bihar, OdishaTotal 	36          Contd…2/-   -2-           Under NFSM –Oilseeds & Oil Palm assistance is being provided for training of farmers and extension officials through States, ICAR/SAUs/KVKs @ Rs.24000/- per training of 2 days for 30 farmers and @ Rs.36000/- per training of 2 days for 20  extension officials.(c):	NFSM-Oilseeds: The Government of India is promoting the production and productivity of oilseeds through the National Food Security Mission (Oilseeds) in all districts of India, including Latur, Satara and Solapur in Maharashtra. Under the special programme for intercropping and high potential districts, 6910 quintals of soybean seeds have been allotted for Latur and Satara  districts. 40,000 mini kits for soybean have been allotted for Solapur district, and 275 mini kits of groundnut have been allotted for Satara district.  Under this scheme, interventions such as purchase of breeder seeds, production of foundation and certified seed, distribution of certified seeds and seed minikits of latest high yielding varieties are undertaken. For Kharif 2021, a total of 9.25 lakh number of Oilseed minikits of high yielding varieties have been allocated for distribution in all the major Oilseed growing states.  Further transfer of technology through Front Line Demonstrations (FLDs) and Cluster Front Line Demonstrations (CFLDs) are also being provided to the farmers through the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR).  The scheme also provides for input distribution such as Integrated Pest Management (IPM), soil ameliorants, plant protection chemicals, micro irrigation & supply of farm machinery and implements.NFSM-Oil Palm:  The scheme is being implemented in twelve states in India. In order to promote Oil Palm cultivation, assistance is provided for planting material, maintenance and intercropping costs for 4 years, micro irrigation, borewell, pumpset, farm pond, establishment of seed garden,  support to Oil Palm processing unit in North East/Hilly states and Training of farmers & extension workers, demonstrations and distribution etc. In the 12 states namely, Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Karnataka, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Odisha, Tamil Nadu and Telangana,  the farmers are getting benefits of NFSM-Oil Palm. *****
