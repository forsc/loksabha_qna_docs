                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4127






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Houses Constructed under PMAY-U




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the houses constructed under each component of PMAY-U, State-wise;(b)	the details of houses allotted to beneficiaries under each components of PMAY-U, State-wise;(c)	the details of houses constructed under the scheme that are vacant, State-wise;(d)	the details of social-audit conducted as per the guidelines of PMAY-U, State-wise; and(e)	whether social audits should have been conducted as of now and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)		State/ Union Territory (UT)-wise details of houses constructed under each component of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) Mission are at Annexure-I. (b)	State/ UT-wise details of houses allotted/occupied under each component of PMAY-U Mission are at Annexure-II.(c)	The State/UT-wise details of constructed houses where occupancy is under process under PMAY-U Mission are at Annexure-III. (d) & (e)  Social Audit provides the beneficiaries and other stakeholders an opportunity to scrutinize the procedures and benefits of the Mission. It ensures better implementation, accountability, transparency and participation.   The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) assists State/ Union Territory (UT) Governments in undertaking social audit of the projects being implemented under the Mission by providing 100% financial assistance for social audit with the approval of CSMC. Such social audits are to be carried out by State/UT Governments and Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) through credible institutions including technical institutions viz. Indian Institutes of Technology, National Institutes of Technology etc. and architectural and design institutes and through students of such institutions.  	 The key processes of the guideline mandate that the State Level Nodal Agency hires an Independent Facilitating Agency (IFA). It designs tools for Social Audit, forms and trains Social Audit Committee and conducts social audit. So far, five States namely Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Jharkhand, Kerala and Mizoram have conducted social audit and four other States namely Assam, Manipur,  Tripura and Uttarakhand have engaged IFAs to conduct social audits. All other States and UTs are in the process of engaging IFAs.                                                *****
