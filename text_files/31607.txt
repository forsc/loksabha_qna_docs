                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3024






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Development of Smart City in Bihar




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the date of announcement of smart city in district Bhagalpur in Bihar and the total work done so far and the total work that is left and the details of companies involved in the work; (b)	whether the work being done in the smart city is being investigated, if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the companies found guilty have been blacklisted or fined; and (d)	if so, the details thereof including the details of companies and agencies involved in the matter?






ANSWER




                                                                       THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	to (d):  Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015. 100  Smart  Cities  have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018.  Bhagalpur was selected for development as a Smart City in Fast-Track Round in May 2016.   Status of projects of Bhagalpur Smart City is given below:-(Amount in Rs. crore)Smart City	DPR Stage	Tender Stage	Work Order Stage	Work Completed	Total no. of Projects	Total Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount		Bhagalpur	9	5.00	4	291.59	19	1,657.93	2	17.08	34	1,971.60(Source: Geospatial Management Information System, as on 12 November 2021)--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------Implementation of SCM at city level is done by a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) created for the purpose. These SPVs, including Bhagalpur Smart City, plan, implement, operate, and monitor projects encapsulated in their Smart City Proposals (SCPs).  Bhagalpur Smart City has informed that they use the services of Project Development and Management Consultant M/s MaRS Planning and Engineering Services for regular monitoring of progress of projects. Third party tests are also being done through reputed institutes, as and when required.  Details of projects being executed by Bhagalpur Smart City through SCM funds and the names of executing agencies therein is given in  Annexure-I.The executing agency for Development of Open Space (Sandy’s Compound) project M/s Singhal Enterprises has been fined for delay in execution. In addition, M/s Om Shankar Construction Private Limited, the earlier contractor of Construction of Townhall project was recommended for blacklisting.                                               ****
