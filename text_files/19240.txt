                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

704






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Pricing of COVID Vaccines




Mohammad Jawed













                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                








                                                    K. Subbarayan
                                                








                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                








                                                    Nayab Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to make the Corona Vaccine easily available to the entire population, if so, the steps taken in this regard and the price at which it is likely to be provided to the common man;(b) whether the Government proposes to purchase Corona Vaccine at a nominal cost and provide the same to States/UTs, if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the rate per vaccine would be uniform for all States/UTs including Bihar,(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e) the number of vaccinations carried out as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	The roll-out of COVID-19 vaccine in the country is being done in a phased manner to the population groups prioritized by National Expert Group on Vaccine Administration for COVID-19 (NEGVAC). The first phase of vaccination of priority groups of Health Care Workers and Front Line Workers is being carried out with COVID-19 vaccine supplied free of cost to States/UTs, including Bihar, by Government of India. Persons aged 50 years and above comprise the subsequent group for vaccination, as per the prioritization recommendation of NEGVAC. (e):	As on 02nd February 2021, a total of 41.38 lakh vaccinations have been carried out across India.
