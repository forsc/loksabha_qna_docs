                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3505






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Capacity Building of Panchayats




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of budgetary allocations towards capacity building of Panchayats on issues of child development protection and rehabilitation, State-wise and particularly of Bhubaneswar; and(b)	the details of funds utilised so far in the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b) Ministry of Panchayati Raj has been implementing restructured Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) with effect from 2018-19. The primary objective of this scheme is to strengthen Panchayati Raj Institutions through capacity building and training (CB&T) of Elected Representatives and its stakeholders with priority to Gram Panchayats covered under Aspirational Districts and Mission Antyodaya to deliver on Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), which include women and child development. There is no separate budgetary allocation under the scheme of RGSA towards CB&T of Panchayats on issues of child development protection and rehabilitation including Bhubaneswar. Details of State/ Union Territory (UT) wise Annual Action Plan (AAP) having CB&T component approved and fund released during 2019-20 is  at Annexure-I. The funds under the scheme are released after the utilisation certificate of previous instalments provided to State/UTs is submitted by the respective State/UTs.
