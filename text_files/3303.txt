                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Rectification of Black Spots on NHs




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of black spots identified on National Highways (NHs) across the country, State/UT-wise including Jharkhand; and (b) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to reduce and rectify these black spots particularly under  Hazaribagh and Ramgarh districts in Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)     Based on fatalities in 2011, 2012, 2013 & 2014 calendar years, 789 black spots have been notified with unique ID numbers assigned to each of the spots. State-wise breakup of the same has been given at Annexure. Ministry has taken adequate steps towards improvement of road safety through various measures including engineering measures on National Highways. Guidelines for investigation and rectification of the road accident black spots on National Highways have been notified. Ministry has issued Protocol for identification and rectification of road accident black spots on National Highways vide letter number RW/NH/1507/109/2015/P&M (RSCE) dated 28-10-2015.
