                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

762






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






AIBP in Uttar Pradesh




Ashok Kumar Rawat













                                                    Arun Kumar Sagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether work is being carried out under Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Programme (AIBP) particularly in Shahjahanpur and Misrikh Parliamentary Constituencies of Uttar Pradesh in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of the Central fund allocated including the utilization of the said fund and the number of beneficiaries during each of the last three years as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) to (c) After launch of Under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana - Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (PMKSY-AIBP) in 2016, ninety-nine (99) on-going major/ medium irrigation projects were prioritized for assured financial assistance, along with their command area development works.       However, none of the above projects has its command in Shahjahanpur or Misrikh Parliamentary Constituencies of Uttar Pradesh*****
