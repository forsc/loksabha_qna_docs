                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2639






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Exam Centre




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is no examination centre for the districts of Thoothukkudi, Thenkasi, Tirunelveli and Kanyakumari for the exams conducted by the Union Public Service Commission(UPSC); (b) 	whether the Government will establish an exam centre in Thoothukkudi for the students from these districts and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor including the criteria followed by UPSC in establishing centres for the students taking the exam?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a):   The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) at present operates no examination centre at Thoothukkudi, Thenkasi, Tirunelveli and Kanyakumari.(b) & (c):   General policy of the Commission in the matter of opening of new Centres is driven by the security/ integrity/ logistical considerations and also the number of Commission’s Centres already under operation in a particular State/ neighbouring regions and likely number of candidates for an examination etc. Presently, the Commission operates 5 (five) centres in the State of Tamil Nadu viz. Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Tiruchirapalli and Vellore in respect of the Civil Services (Preliminary) Examination.*****
