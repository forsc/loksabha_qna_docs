                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Financial Expenditure Planning for Promotion of Arts and Culture




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has made any financial expenditure planning for the art and culture promotion scheme, promotion of museums, international cooperation, centenary and annual programmes of the Ministry during the year 2021-22;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has made any action plan across the country for Central autonomous bodies, museums, academies, libraries and other institutions of culture; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b): Yes Sir, Budget Estimates of Ministry of Culture for the year 2021-22 is at Annexure.(c) & (d): Yes Sir, as per the mandate of Ministry of Culture, this Ministry deals with the tangible and Intangible Cultural Heritage of India. This heritage encompass several strands including monuments and archaeology, folk and tribal art, literature, archives, library, performing arts  including music dance and drama, visual arts in the form of paintings, sculptures and graphics. All activities of the Ministry relating to promotion, preservation and conservation of the Cultural Heritage of the Country are implemented through a network of two attached offices, 6 subordinated offices and 34 autonomous organizations under its control and number of schemes directly operated by the Ministry for promotion and dissemination of Art & Culture. Its activities, projects and programmes are organized under various schemes of archeology, museums, archives, anthropology, performing arts, public libraries, Buddhists and Tibetan institutes, centenaries and anniversary, internal cultural relations, Gandhi heritage mission.Annexure referred to in reply to part (a) to (b) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Qustion  No. 2085 for 08.03.2021 regarding Financial Expenditure Planning for Promotion of Arts and Culture(Rs. in lakh) S.
