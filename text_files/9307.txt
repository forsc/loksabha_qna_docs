                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2795






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Plantation of Trees along National Highways




Upendra Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to plant trees on both sides of highways along with other facilities during the development of highways in Uttar Pradesh including district Barabanki and nearby districts; and(b) if so, the details thereof?                                      






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b) Yes, Sir. The plantation on both sides of highways during the development of highways in the country is carried out as per relevant provisions of Manual published by Indian Roads Congress (IRC), Green Highways Policy, 2015 and other guidelines of Government of India. National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) planted 3.41 lakh plants along national highways in the state of Uttar Pradesh, out of which 1.68 lakh have been planted in Barabanki and nearby Districts.NHAI has initiated action to develop other facilities such as wayside amenities, bus-bay, truck-lay-by etc., as per site specific requirement and availability of funds.*****
