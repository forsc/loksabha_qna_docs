                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4079






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Functioning of Various Schools




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and number of Central Government schools like Kendriya Vidyalaya, Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya and others in the country which are project schools, funded by other organizations, State-wise; (b) the total amount of funds released and utilised for running the said schools between 2010-2021, Year-wise; (c) whether the Government is considering taking over some of the above schools for better functioning and continuity and if so, the details of the schools taken over so far year-wise and State-wise; and (d) whether some of the schools have also been handed over to private entities for better functioning and avoiding closure of the schools and if so, the details of the schools handed over so far year-wise and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) & (b) Central Government Schools namely Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) are being run by the autonomous bodies viz. Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) and Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) respectively under the Department of School Education & Literacy, Ministry of Education. Both KVS and NVS are fully funded by the Government of India. However, some KVs are opened on the request of Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) / Institute of Higher Learning (IHL) as Project KVs for educational needs of the wards of the employees and fully funded by respective organization. No funds have been released for functioning of project KVs by the Government of India. As on date, there are 134 Project KVs. State/UT-wise details are annexed.  (c)There is no such proposal under consideration of the Government at present. (d)No, Sir. *****
