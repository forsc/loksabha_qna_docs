                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1115






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Schemes for Welfare of Farmers




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number and details of various Schemes being implemented by the Government for the welfare of farmers across the country during the last three years, year-wise;(b) the total amount/budget earmarked, released and utilized on the above Schemes for the last three years particularly in Tamil Nadu;(c) whether the Government has reviewed the above Schemes in the recent past, particularly in Tamil Nadu; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????U? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  Details are given at Annexure– I.	(b)	Details are given at Annexure– II.(c) & (d) Evaluation of major Centrally Sponsored schemes implemented by the Department across the country including the state of Tamil Nadu was done by the NITI Aayog in 2020.  The report finds most of the schemes relevant to address the needs of the sector.
