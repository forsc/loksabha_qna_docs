                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Foreign Companies




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a): the details of foreign companies functioning in the country, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh;(b): the details of revenue/profit earned and tax given by the said companies during the last three years and the current year;(c): whether the said companies have defaulted in the payment of taxes during the said period;(d): if so, the details of such companies, company-wise and year-wise along with the loss incurred by the exchequer during the said period; and(e): the penal action taken by the Government against these companies for the recovery of outstanding taxes along with the steps taken to stop such practice?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a), (b), (c), (d) & (e): The information is being collected and shall be laid on the table of the House.
