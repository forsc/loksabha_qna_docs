                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






MoU between ICAR and DIC




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    B. Y. Raghavendra
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether an MoU was signed between Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) and Digital India Corporation (DIC), Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) recently;(b)	 if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives behind the move; (c)	the benefit that is likely to be accrued to farmers from the said MoU; and(d) 	the other steps taken by the Government for speedy implementation in providing Tele Agriculture Advisories to the farmers across the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICUTLRURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????			                     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Yes, Sir.  An MoU was signed between Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) and Digital India Corporation (DIC), Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (Meity) on June 09, 2021.(b)	The main aim of this MoU is to jointly implement “System of Agri-information Resources Auto-transmission and Technology Hub Interface (Kisan Sarathi)” which is digital platform, initially conceptualized by Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) and now jointly implemented with Digital India Corporation, Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology to empower farmers with latest agricultural technological knowledge. It takes leverage of existing Interactive Information Dissemination System (IIDS) developed by DIC in accordance with above MoU.(c)	This is a pull and push based interactive digital advisory system to deliver location specific agricultural technological information and advisories to farmers through multimedia in their local language as per their requirements.(d)	This platform has been developed and launched.*********
