                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EARTH SCIENCES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4494






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Automatic Weather Stations




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up more new Automatic Weather Stations (AWS) for Krishi Vigyan in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and location-wise; and (c)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY	AND EARTH SCIENCES (Dr. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. Under the Gramin Krishi Mausam Sewa (GKMS) scheme, India Meteorological Department (IMD) is in the process of installing 200 additional Automatic Weather Stations (AWS) in 200 Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs), selected for establishment of District Agro-Met Units (DAMUs). The details ofthese200 KVKs are given in Annexure. (c)Does not arise.
