                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1495






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






PM Kisan Samman Nidhi




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	the steps taken by the Government to assist landless farmers, agricultural labourers and sharecroppers; (b):	the details and the number of families dependent upon agriculture who do not own land, State-wise; (c):	 whether all such families are covered under the PM Kisan Samman Nidhi scheme; and (d):	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The Government has extended assistance to landless farmers, agricultural labourers and sharecroppers through the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) and Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY). (b):	The data on number and area of operational holdings (defined as all land which is wholly or partly used for agriculture production and is operated as one technical unit by one person alone or with others without regard to the title, legal form, size or location) is collected as part of Agriculture Census conducted at five yearly intervals.
