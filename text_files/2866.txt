                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2583






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Funds Released under PMAY to Kerala




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantum of funds released to the State Government of Kerala under the Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana (PMAY) during the last five years;(b)	the names of districts covered under the said Yojana in the State; and(c)	the number of houses constructed/likely to be constructed in Ernakulam district of Kerala under the said Yojana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	To achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by 2022, in rural areas of the country, the Government has re-structured the erstwhile rural housing scheme Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY) into  Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G). PMAY-G is being implemented by Ministry of Rural Development w.e.f. 1st April 2016. In urban areas of the country, Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) is being implemented by Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs  w.e.f. 25.06.2015.  The funds released to the State of Kerala under rural housing schemes (IAY & PMAY-G) and Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) during 2014-15, 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 is as below:Year	            Central Assistance released            Central Assistance sanctioned                    under PMAY-G/IAY (Rs. in Crore)	   for release under PMAY-U                                                            (Rs.in  Crore)2014-15	                     206.03	                         25.862015-16	                     357.18	                         00.782016-17	                     100.49	                        154.432017-18	                      21.41	                        372.652018-19	                      NIL	                        753.35  (b):	All districts of Kerala are covered under PMAY – G. Under PMAY-U, actual demand of housing is assessed after a demand survey is undertaken by the States / UTs.(c):	As on 1st July 2019, a total of 728 houses have been constructed in the Ernakulam district of Kerala under PMAY – G and 6,673 under (PMAY-U)*****
