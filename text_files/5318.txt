                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4844






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Monitoring of Rural Development Schemes




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry has any mechanism to monitor the various Rural Development Schemes to ensure proper implementation and use of funds allotted to those schemes in the rural areas of the country;(b)	if so, the details of the monitoring mechanism in place during the last two years;(c)	the number of cases reported concerning loopholes in the system during the last three years, State-wise;(d)	the quantum of funds sanctioned for various Rural Development Schemes during the last three financial years, State-wise; and(e)	the amount of funds utilised under the said schemes during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	Review of implementation of various Rural Development programmes is an ongoing process. Programmes are reviewed through Performance Review Committee Meetings, Common Review Mission, Area Officers Schemes, District Development Co-ordination and Monitoring Committee named as “DISHA” Committee, Reports of National Level Monitors (NLMs). The NLMs are deputed to visit districts periodically, generally twice a year, to monitor and report on various aspects of implementation of all rural development programmes. The NLM Teams, after the visit to the districts, prepare and submit their reports to the respective district authorities as well as Ministry of Rural Development. NLMs have reported some irregularities in implementation of Rural Development Programmes in the Districts visited by them. The issues reported w.r.t MGNREGS relate to maintenance and updating of job cards, delay in payment of wages, lack of people’s participation in MGNREGS planning process, absence of Social Audit process in some villages. Many cases were reported where the PMAY-G target beneficiaries have not been allotted/ provided land. Some issues related to shortage of rural masons in some of the villages have also been reported. In some instances delay in the release of pension under NSAP has been reported. Slow progress in mobilization of rural poor households into Self-Help Groups has been reported in some States. These reports are regularly shared with the  states for necessary follow up action at their end.(d) & (e):	The details of funds allocated/released during last three years under various Schemes, implemented by Department of Rural Development, State-wise and Scheme wise, are placed at Annexure I(a) to I(h).*****
