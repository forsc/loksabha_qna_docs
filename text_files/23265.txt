                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4217






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Skill Development Training to Tribal communities




Dean Kuriakose













                                                    Gajendra Singh Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched any scheme to provide Skill Development Training to the tribal communities especially in the Kerala and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the amount of funds allocated by the Government for this purpose;(c) 	the number of youths being/been imparted skill development training by various Ministries and Skill Development Ministry in Kerala during the last three years and the current year;(d)	whether the Government has received any proposal from Kerala, if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the other steps taken by the Government to improve skill development in Kerala?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)  The Government is implementing various schemes/ programmes across 20 Central Ministries/ Departments for skill development of the youth covering all sections of the society, including tribal communities. The number of candidates, including tribal candidates, who have benefitted under skill training programmes of various Ministries viz. Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Ministry of Rural Development and Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs in Kerala from 2017-18 to 2020-21 and fund released to Kerala under these schemes are as follows:Sl. No.	Schemes	Candidates benefitted (in lakh)	Fund released (in Rs. crore)1.	Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana 	2.14	22.002.	Jan Shikshan Sansthan Scheme	0.31	10.82*3.	National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme	0.22	1.294.	Craftsmen Training Scheme	1.04	-5.	Deen Dayal Upadhyaya - Grameen Kaushalya Yojana	0.38	295.77**6.	Rural Self Employment Training Institutes	0.36	5.31**7.	Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission	0.15	94.01* From 2018-19 to 2020-21(up to February 2021) ** From 2017-18 to 2020-21 as on date(d) & (e)  Presently, MSDE has not received any proposal from the State of Kerala regarding launching of skill development scheme for tribal communities. The Ministry has, inter-alia, taken the following steps to improve skill development in the States including Kerala:?	MSDE has allocated adequate funds to State Skill Development Missions (SSDMs) under PMKVY 3.0 for promotion of the scheme and has given full authority to utilize the funds for adopting the communication outreach, which works best at local level. ?	MSDE engages periodically with the media and does advocacy with regard to skill development and its relevance to the current market demand, through Rozgar Melas, Kaushal Mahotsav, and counselling sessions for youth, interactive sessions and activities, panel discussions, Kushal Yuva Samvaad and also through events to celebrate their success, achievements and milestones.?	MSDE, through the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), organizes skill competitions such as IndiaSkills which are conducted across State, regional and national levels to make skills aspirational for youth.  ?	Various organizations under the aegis of MSDE viz. NSDC, Directorate General of Training, Indian Institute of Entrepreneurship, and National Institute of Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development (NIESBUD) create awareness at their respective levels.
