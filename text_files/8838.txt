                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Uniform Rates of Onion




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is a proposal to provide onions to the customers at uniform rate in all the States across the country;(b)	 if so, whether the production of onion during the current year has declined in comparison to that of the last year; and (c) 	if so, whether the Government has delayed the import of onion and if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a) : No sir. There is no such proposal.(b) : As per the estimates released by Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DACFW), production of onion during 2018-19 (3rd Advance Estimates) is 234.85 lakh MT as against 232.62 lakh MT in 2017-18 i.e. higher by 2.23 lakh MT.(c) : No Sir.*****
