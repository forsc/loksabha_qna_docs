                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Construction and Maintenance of Public Libraries




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Public Libraries functioning at present in the country, State/UT- wise including Bihar;(b)	whether the Government is planning to carry out construction or maintenance work of public libraries located in various parts of the country including Gujarat during the last three years;(c)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Bihar; (d)	the details of funds spent for this purpose, State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) Library is a State subject as per the Seventh Schedule under Article 246 of Constitution of India. Data regarding State wise number of functional Public Libraries in the Country is not maintained by Ministry of Culture as Public Libraries function under the administrative control of respective State/UT Library Authorities being State Subject. However, there are six Public Libraries functioning under Ministry of Culture namely National Library, Kolkata, Central Reference Library, Kolkata, Central Secretariat Library, New Delhi, Delhi Public Library, Delhi, Khuda Bakhsh Oriental Public Library, Patna and Rampur Raza Library, Rampur.(b), (c) & (d) Library is a State subject and public Libraries in States/Union Territories function under the administrative control of the respective State/Union Territory authority. However, Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata, an autonomous organization under Ministry of Culture provides financial assistance to public libraries under its ‘Matching Schemes of Assistance to Public Libraries’ towards increasing Accommodation on the basis of applications recommended by the respective State Library Authorities.This Ministry operates National Mission on Libraries Scheme. Under the subcomponent of “Up-gradation of Infrastructure”, financial assistance is provided for infrastructural development in one (1) State Central Library and one (1) District Library in participating States/UTs. The details of fund sanctioned for the purpose by Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata is at Annexure-I and by National Mission on Libraries is at Annexure-II including the State of Gujarat and Bihar.
