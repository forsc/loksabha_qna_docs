                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Airports in Rajasthan




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of operational airports in Rajasthan at present;(b) the total number of airports, out of these, which are meant for common citizens and those of strategic importance separately;(c) the extent of increase registered in the movement of passengers from these airports during the last three years; and(d) whether for expansion of these airports, the Government proposes to inter-connect the main cities through Air India?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement in reply to parts (a) to (d) of the Starred (*) Question No. 373 for answer on 19.03.2020 regarding ''Airports in Rajasthan''.;  (a) & (b): Presently, there are seven operational airports in Rajasthan. Out of these, four airports viz. Jaipur, Kishangarh, Kota and Udaipur are owned and operated by Airports Authority of India (AAI). The remaining three airports viz.  Bikaner, Jaisalmer and Jodhpur are strategic airports owned by defense authorities where AAI maintains civil terminals.;  (c): The total footfall at these seven airports has increased from 52,24,640 in 2016-17 to 75,81,334 in the year 2018-19.;  (d):  Airlines plan their domestic flight schedule on a specific route/cities based on market demand, commercial viability and their company policy. Further, during stakeholders discussions airlines are requested to mount flights from un-served airports.
