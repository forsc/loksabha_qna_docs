                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3679






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Mobile App for Custom Hiring Service Centres




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recently launched any mobile application to connect farmers with Custom Hiring Service Centres in their nearby vicinity and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the details of aims and objectives of launching this application along with its benefits; (c) 	the details of the language options which the app is likely to offer; (d) 	whether the Government has created awareness to popularize this mobile app; (e) 	the total number of custom hiring service centres registered on this mobile app for the purpose of renting agricultural machineries and equipment; and (f) 	the total number of farmers especially small and marginal farmers who are getting affordable access to cutting-edge technology at their doorsteps with the help of this mobile application?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a)  to  (f) : 	Yes Sir.      The Government has developed and launched Multi lingual Mobile App “CHC- Farm Machinery” which helps the farmers for getting rented farm machinery and implements through Custom Hiring Service Centers (CHC) in their area. Contd…2/--2-      	The above App was launched with the aims and objectives to provide an easy access to the High Value & Hi tech agricultural machinery available in CHCs in nearby local areas through a mobile application at compatible hiring rates to small and marginal farmers and farmers in difficult areas .     The benefits of this App are as below:1)	Facilitate Farmers to avail Custom hiring services.2)	Facilitate CHCs to provide Custom hiring services to farmers.3)	It is a multilingual app customized as per the need of the farmers.4)	Facilitate farmers to avail services of CHCs without any computer support system.5)	Small and marginal farmers can have an instant access to CHCs on a simple tap on mobile.6)	Mobile apps will help to reach outreach the mechanization among the farmers in quick time.7)	Mobile-apps has an interfaces that allow Farmers and CHC Providers to share their experience.8)	Would assist in Strengthening Farm Mechanization in low Mechanized areas. 9)	Provides Business Model to the Rural Youth.		At present the App is in twelve languages i.e. Hindi, English, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam,  Marathi, Nepali, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu, Urdu.	The steps taken to create awareness and  to popularize this App is  enclosed as Annexure-I.     	As on date, 41,992 CHCs with 1,33,723 Agricultural machinery for renting out are registered on this Mobile app. Total 1,12,505 farmers as users are registered on this Mobile App for getting affordable access to cutting-edge technology at their doorsteps with the help of this mobile application.
