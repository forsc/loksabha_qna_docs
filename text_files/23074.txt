                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4299






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Steel Import and Export




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantity of steel imported and exported to/from various countries;(b)	the details of steel production in the country, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government is considering to bring in policy changes related to National Steel Policy, 2017; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	Details of country-wise import and export of finished steel during last three years and current year i.e., April-January, 2020-21 (prov.) are placed at Annexure-I & Annexure-II respectively.(b)	Details of state wise details of crude steel production during last three years and the current year i.e., April-January, 2020-21 (prov.) are placed at Annexure-III.(c)&(d): No, Sir.********
