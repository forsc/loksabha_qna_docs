                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1664






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Landline Telephones




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of landlines surrendered during the period since the contractors have been appointed in MTNL;(b) 	whether any penalty has been imposed for non maintenance of services satisfactorily; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) 	Till 30.11.2021, total 1,63,182 landlines have been surrendered since August, 2020.(b) & (c) 	Penalty of Rs. 5,71,07,677 has been imposed on the contractors for non maintenance of services satisfactorily.  *******
