                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Inclusion of Arunachaleshwar Temple under Cultural Religious Thematic Circuit




C.N. Annadurai














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the places/institutions included for development as cultural-religious thematic circuit under Swadesh Darshan Scheme; (b) 	whether the Government is planning to develop Tourism Circuit for Shiva Temples in the country under Swadesh Darshan Scheme and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	the total outlay for each circuit and layout and time line for development of those circuits; (d) 	the time by which the Arunachaleshwar temple in Tiruvannmalai region which attracts thousands of pilgrims for darshan and Girvallam (circumambulation of the Arunachaleshwar Hill) would be places in the map of cultural-religious thematic circuit for its development; and (e) 	the steps taken to provide basic amenities to lakhs of pilgrims visiting Arunachaleshwar Temple at Tiruvannmalai for promotion of religious tourism?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Tourism under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme is   developing   thematic tourist circuits in the country with the objective of providing   enhanced tourism experience to the visitors.  Ministry has identified   fifteen thematic circuits for   development namely Buddhist   Circuit, Krishna Circuit, Spiritual Circuit, Ramayana Circuit, Sufi Circuit,   and Tirthankara Circuit.  The sites taken up for development under the   scheme includes cultural and religious sites including Shiva   Temples in   the country. The details of projects sanctioned under the Swadesh Darshan   scheme are at annexure. In   addition to above the Ministry under its PRASHAD scheme is also developing   tourist facilities at the pilgrimage and heritage sites in the country. The   Shavite temples covered under the scheme includes Somnath (Gujarat),   Srisailam (Andhra Pradesh), Baidyanathji Dham (Jharkhand)) , Omkareshwar (Madhya Pradesh), Trimbakeshwar (Maharashtra), Ekambeshwar Temple (Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu), Markandey Mahadev Temple (Varanasi ,Uttar Pradesh) , Kedarnath (Uttarakhand) and Amarlingeshwar Swamy Temple (Amravati, Andhra Pradesh). There   is no circuit wise outlay under Swadesh Darshan Scheme.  As per the scheme guidelines the timelines   of the project varies from 18 to 36 months. (d) to (e):	The projects under the scheme are identified for development in   consultation with the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations and   are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable   Detailed Project Reports, adherence   to scheme guidelines and utilization of   funds released earlier. The State Government of   Tamilnadu   has not submitted any proposal for development of   Arunachaleshwar Temple in   Tiruvannmalai. *******
