                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






National Nutrition Mission




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)        the details of policy action being undertaken under the ambit of National Poshan Mission since its inception to till date; (b)       the funds allocated and utilized under National Poshan Mission during the last two years and the current year, State-wise; (c)       whether funds have been released to Bihar under this Mission during these years; and (d)        if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a)        POSHAN Abhiyaan (ertswhile National Nutrition Mission) was launched on 8th March, 2018 with the vision of a malnutrition-free India. The POSHAN Abhiyaan aims to reduce malnutrition from the country in a phased manner and achieve improvement in nutritional status of Children from 0-6 years, Adolescent Girls, Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers in a time bound manner. Mission Poshan 2.0, an integrated nutrition support programme has been announced in budget 2021-2022 to strengthen nutritional content, delivery, outreach and outcomes with focus on developing practices that nurture health, wellness and immunity to disease and malnutrition. Steps have been taken to improve nutritional quality and testing in accredited labs, strengthen delivery and leverage technology to improve governance. States/UTs have been advised to promote use of AYUSH systems for prevention of malnutrition and related diseases. A programme to support development of Poshan Vatikas at Anganwadi Centres to meet dietary diversity gap leveraging traditional knowledge in nutritional practices has also been taken up. (b) to (d)  The details of the funds released and utilised State/UT wise including the state of Bihar under POSHAN Abhiyaan during the last two years and the current year is at Annexure-I. Further, the details of the funds released to the state of Bihar in Supplementary Nutrition component of the Anganwadi Services Scheme is at Annexure-II.
