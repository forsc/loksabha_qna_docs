                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

52






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Financial Assistance for Sugarcane Farmers




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount of financial assistance sanctioned by the Government for sugarcane farmers in terms of crores of rupees during the month of December; (b) 	the framework prepared to transfer the said assistance direct to the accounts of the farmers; and (c) 	the number of sugarcane farmers likely to be benefited from the said decision?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):	With a view to facilitate export of sugar during the sugar season 2020-21 and thereby improving the liquidity position of sugar mills enabling them to clear cane price dues of farmers for sugar season 2020-21, Central Government has approved Rs. 3500 crores for providing assistance to sugar mills for expenses on marketing costs including handling, upgrading and other processing costs and costs of international and internal transport and freight charges on export of sugar.(b): 	In order to ensure that the assistance is directly credited into the accounts of farmers, the sugar mill shall open a separate no-lien account in a nationalized bank/cooperative bank and furnish to that bank the list of farmers along with bank accounts details and extent of cane price dues payable to the farmers for the sugar season 2020-21 and cane price arrears of previous sugar seasons, duly certified by the Cane Commissioner / Director of Sugar of the state concerned. The bank shall credit the amount of assistance to the farmers’ accounts on behalf of the mills against cane dues payable and subsequent balance, if any, shall be credited into mill’s account.(c): 	The decision will improve the liquidity position of sugar mills and enable them to clear cane price dues of farmers.  There are about 5 crore sugarcane farmers including their families, which will be benefited by this decision.***
