                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1007






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Establishment of Open Gym/Health Club




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to establish open gym/health club for public in main cities;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether there is any such proposal pending for Ernakulum city in Kerala and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government is having any scheme or planning to provide funds to establish open library including e-learning facilities in the country for the development of youth; and(e) if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a)to (c) No Sir. Sports being a State subject the responsibility for promotion and development of sports, including establishment of gym/health club for public in main cities, primarily rests with State Governments.(d) and (e) Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports is not having any scheme for providing funds to establish open library, including e-learning facilities, in the country.****
