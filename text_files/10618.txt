                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4076






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Mission Bhagiratha




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of loans given to Telangana Drinking Water Supply Corporation Limited (TDWSCL) by HUDCO for Mission Bhagiratha;(b)	the number of households that have been provided drinking water taps under Mission Bhagiratha and the number of households that had been identified as beneficiaries;(c)	the names of the villages in which purified and clean drinking water has been provided by taps to 100 per cent of the houses under Mission Bhagiratha;(d)	whether HUDCO has any mechanism in place to monitor the expenditure of the loans given by them and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the details of the other Central Public Sector Undertakings and lending institutions such as HUDCO, NABARD who lent money to TDWSCL along with the amount sanctioned, released including the dates thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA) (a)  As reported by Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs, HUDCO had sanctioned a loan of Rs. 2,500.00 Crore to Telangana Drinking Water Supply Corporation Limited (TDWSCL) on 18.03.2015 against project cost of Rs.3,188.52 Crore. The project envisages development of 15 Segments of the scheme covering 6 districts of the State namely, Warangal, Khammam, Karim Nagar, Medak, Adilabad and Nalgonda (erstwhile undivided districts of Telangana). Further in another scheme HUDCO had sanctioned a loan amount of Rs. 2,250.00 Crore to Telangana Drinking Water Supply Corporation on 01.09.2016 against project cost of Rs. 2,500.00 Crore. The project envisages development of the scheme in 3 Segments namely, Jurala Segment in Mahaboob Nagar Dist., MiryalaGuda- Huzoor Nagar Segment in Nalgonda District,  HMWSSB Segment in  Warangal District. (b), (c) and (e) Mission Bhagiratha is a State Government scheme and information is yet to be provided by the State Government. (d) As per information provided by HUDCO, loan releases are being regulated based on the Utilisation Certificates and Progress Reports furnished by TDWSCL and also the physical and financial progress is monitored through periodic site inspections.****
