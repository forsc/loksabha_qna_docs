                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

83






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Multi-State Cooperative Societies




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Multi-State Co-operative Societies registered in the country;(b)	the number of the co-operative societies, out of the above, which are functional and the details thereof, State-wise;(c)	the Co-operative Societies which have been provided grants for their development during the last five years and the current year alongwith the amount of funds provided in this regard, year-wise, society-wise and State-wise including the State of Chhattisgarh; and(d)	the sectors in which the Government proposes to promote the co-operatives in future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ??????  ( SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 83 DUE FOR REPLY ON 8TH FEBRUARY, 2022(a) & (b) 1481 Multi-State Co-operative Societies have been registered in the      country out of which 77 Multi State Co-operative societies are non functional as winding up proceedings are underway. State-wise details of Multi State Co-operative Societies is at Annexure-A. (c)    Societies / Projects assisted through State Government by National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) including the State of Chhattisgarh during the last five years and the current year is at Annexure-B.(d)          The country has a rich cooperative heritage and a robust cooperative sector which has already been greatly contributing to the national economy as well as boosting the productivity in various sectors. However, to give new dimensions and further strengthen the co-operative movement in various sectors in the country through policy and other interventions, Government has created the new Ministry of Cooperation to provide a separate administrative, legal and policy framework for strengthening the cooperative movement.
