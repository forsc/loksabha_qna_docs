                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

201






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






20th Livestock Census




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that the Government has released the key results of the 20th livestock census; (b) whether it is true that cattle population has increased in the country since the previous census; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a): Yes, the Government has released the key results of the 20th Livestock Census in the publication titled “20th Livestock Census-2019 All India Report”.(b): Yes, the total cattle population in the country has increased of 1.3% since the previous census i.e., 19th Livestock Census. (c): The details of cattle population of 20th and 19th Livestock Census is given in the following tableCategory	Population (in Million)19th LC	Population (in Million) 20th LC	% change of cattle population of 20th LC over 19th LC	Rural	Urban	Total	Rural	Urban	Total	Rural	Urban	TotalCattle	183.74	7.17	190.90	185.27	8.19	193.46	0.83	14.33	1.34
