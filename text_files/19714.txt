                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1238






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Enrolment of SC and OBC Students




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1238TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021ENROLMENT OF SC AND OBC STUDENTS 1238.   SHRIMATI NUSRAT JAHAN RUHI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details and the total number of Scheduled Caste (SC) and Other Backward Classes (OBC) students enrolled under coaching classes for different competitive examinations, State-wise; (b) the details and the total number of SC and OBC students who have passed in the examinations for which they enrolled in coaching class during the last three years, State-Wise; and (c) the total number of SC students provided with pre-matric scholarship since 2016 till date?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) and (b):   Under the Free Coaching Scheme for SC and OBC students, available slots are allotted among the institutes empanelled under it. The details of state-wise number of empanelled institutes and total number of students enrolled and passed for different competitive examinations during the last 3 years is attached.(c): Pre-matric Scholarship Scheme for SC students is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented through State Government/UT administrations. Total number of SC beneficiary students covered under this scheme since 2016,  as reported by the States/UT is 127.06 lakhs.**** AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply to parts (a) and (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1238 dated 09.02.2021 regarding ‘Enrollment of SC and OBC students’Number of empanelled institutes under the Free Coaching Scheme fopr SCs and OBCs and total number of students enrolled and passedS.No	Name of the state	Number of Institutes	Total number of SC and OBC students enrolled during the last 3 years	Total number of SC and OBC  students passed during the last 3 years  as reported by institutes1	Assam	1	200	312	Bihar	1	300	21#3	Chandigarh	2	500	994	Delhi	8	1850	2455	Gujarat	3	600	1046	Haryana	1	300	057	Himachal Pradesh	1	200	Not reported8	Jammu and Kashmir	1	300	879	Karnataka	2	400	12510	Madhya Pradesh	5	1100	6711	Maharashtra	5	300*	Not reported12	Manipur	1	300	11513	Meghalaya	1	100	914	Odisha	2	400	22 (one institute has not            reported the performance)15	Punjab	3	600	119(one institute has not         reported the performance)16	Rajasthan	3	900	15717	Tamil Nadu	2	500	5718	Tripura	1	100	Not reported19	Uttar Pradesh	2	500	214*4 Institutes of Maharashtra have not reported the number of enrolled students.# One institute has reported the detail of passed students for their both the centers viz.
