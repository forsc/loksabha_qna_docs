                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3471






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Chrome Ore Mines




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of Chrome Ore bearing States in the country at present;(b)	the number of Chrome Ore Mines leased out to public and private sectors in those States so far separately;(c)	whether some of the Chromite Mines have been closed down in those States; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons for their closure, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	As per available information, the reserves/resources of chromite are mainly located in Odisha. Other states contributing to resources of chromite in the country are Manipur, Nagaland, Karnataka, Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. State wise reserves/resources of chromite as on 01.04.2015 is given as Annexure-I.(b)	Details of state wise no. of leases of chromite with an area in the country for public and private sector is given as Annexure-II.(c)&(d): Yes, few of the chrome ore mines have been closed down in those states. Details of such closed mines alongwith reasons thereof is furnished as Annexure-III.********
