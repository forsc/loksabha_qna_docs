                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1473






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Village Knowledge Centres




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Village Knowledge Centres programme being implemented by the Government is achieving its targeted goals;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the details of the number of Village Knowledge Centres established so far and their working along with the details of nodal agencies/officers; and(d) 	the details of the banks that are providing details of the schemes to the farmers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Council for Advancement of People’s Action and Rural Technology (CAPART) has been merged with National Institute of Rural Development and Panchayati Raj (NIRD&PR) with effect from 01.05.2020. Village Knowledge Centre had been one of the several priority areas for intervention by selected Nodal NGOs of erstwhile CAPART. It has been decided by the Executive Council of erstwhile CAPART in its 50th meeting held on 30.9.2009 to close the Nodal NGO scheme. As such, Village Knowledge Centre support is no longer in operation.(b) to (d) Question does not arise in view of (a) above.*****
