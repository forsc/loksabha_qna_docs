                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2130






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Variation in LPG Prices




Karti P. Chidambaram














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the reasons for continually high LPG prices, even as international prices fell;(b) the details of the variation in gas cylinder prices, subsidised and non-subsidised over the lastfive years, State-wise;(c) the number of initial beneficiaries of the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) who havesought cylinder refills, State/year-wise;(d) the number of initial beneficiaries of the PMUY who have sought at least three cylinderrefills, State-wise;(e) the number of people using LPG as the primary or exclusive fuel, year-wise along with themeasures being taken to ensure LPG usage beyond first-use; and(f) whether the Government has an understanding of the impact of increasing LPG prices onoverall household expenditure?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a): The prices of petroleum products in the country are linked to the price of respectiveproducts in the international market. Prices of LPG in the country are based on Saudi ContractPrice (CP), the benchmark for international prices of LPG and during October, 2020 to April,2021 Saudi CP has risen from 359 USD to 607 USD. India imports more than 55 % of its LPGrequirement.(b): The details of average prices of domestic LPG cylinders (14.2 kg) at Delhi market duringthe last five years and the current year is given as under:Year Domestic LPG RSPRs./14.2 Kg cylinder2016-17 549.692017-18 653.462018-19 768.122019-20 694.732020-21 695.152021-22 (till July) 821.75(c): The State-wise number of PMUY Beneficiaries taken LPG refills during the last twofinancial years and the current year (Apr-Jun’21) is provided at Annexure-1.(d): The State-wise number of initial PMUY beneficiaries who have taken at least three refillsas on 31.05.2021 since inception of the scheme is provided at Annexure-2.(e): As on 01.07.2021, there are 29.11 crore active Domestic LPG consumers (includingPMUY Customers) on all India basis. Year-wise numbers of Active Domestic customers (incl.PMUY Customers) during last three years are given below:Period Active Domestic customers (incl. PMUY) (in crore)2018-19 26.542019-20 27.872020-21 28.95The various steps being taken to improve LPG consumption among PMUY beneficiaries is givenbelow:? Deferment of loan recovery from subsidy amount to reduce the net cash outgo of aPMUY consumer to encourage refill consumption for six refills or period of one yearwhichever is earlier during FY 18-19 and also during Aug ’19 – July ’20.? Swap option from 14.2 kg connections to 5 kg connections which will reduce the upfrontcash outgo for a PMUY consumer and thus encourage refill consumption.? PMUY connections were given option of 5kg DBC package for the financial ease of thecustomer. To avail the 5kg refills, the upfront cash outgo for a customer will be low.? During lockdown on account of Covid-19, existing PMUY beneficiaries were supportedwith free refills scheme under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana. Under this scheme,an advance was given to the linked bank account of the beneficiaries using which refillscould be availed.? Conducting Pradhan Mantri LPG Panchayat (PMLP), a community-based program tocreate awareness about the scheme and also to address any issue pertaining to LPG usage.? After achieving 8 Crore LPG connections, PMLP targeted nil or low refill consumers, toconvince them about sustained usage of LPG. Total 1.46 lakh LPG Panchayats wereconducted during 2018-20.(f): In order to insulate the common man from the impact of rise in international oil prices,the Government continues to modulate the effective price to consumer of Domestic LPG. Theprices of non-subsidized Domestic LPG are however determined by the OMCs in line withchanges in the international markets. Government provided upto 3 free of cost LPG cylinderrefills to PMUY consumers in the year 2020 to provide support during COVID pandemic.The impact of increase in prices of LPG can be seen on inflationary trend measured byWholesale Price Index (WPI). The weightage of LPG in the WPI index is 0.64%.
