                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

891






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Accidents and Construction on NH-86




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of casualties in accidents during last three years on various National Highways (NHs) including Kanpur-Sagar NH-86 e-specially between Kanpur Kabarai  stretch; (b)  whether the Government has conducted any survey to identify the accident prone points; (c)     if so, the details thereof; (d)   whether the Government proposes to widen the Kanpur-Sagar NH-86 between Kanpur to Kabarai; and (e)     if so, the time by when it is likely to be completed? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	As per information received from police department of all States/UTs, the total number of persons killed and injured in road accidents on National Highways (including expressways) in the country for the calendar years i.e 2015 to 2017 is given in the table below:- Years	Total Number of accidents on National Highways in the country	Total Number of persons killed on National Highways in the country	Total Number of persons injured on National Highways in the country2015	142268	51204	1453412016	142359	52075	1462862017	141466	53181	142622Further, for National Highways (NH) network under National Highways Authority of India, (NHAI), NHAI collects and compile the data at RO level. The data for last three years in respect of NH-86 are given in the Table below:-.ACCIDENTS ON NH-86 Sl.No.	Year	No. of accidents	Fatal	Grievous1	2016	302	65	1572	2017	333	48	2013	2018	356	46	218(b)	& (c )  Ministry has taken adequate steps towards improvement of road safety through various measures including engineering measures on National Highways. Based on fatalities in 2011, 2012, 2013 & 2014 calendar years, 789 black spots have been identified and notified with unique ID numbers assigned to each of the spots. Guidelines have been notified for investigation and rectification of the road accident black spots on National Highways. (d) & (e)  Kanpur-Kabrai section of NH-86 has been identified for four laning under Bharatmala Pariyojana-2/LOT-4 in lieu of existing two lane with paved shoulder. Final Inception Report and QAP has been submitted by the DPR Consultant.*****
