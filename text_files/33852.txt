                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1443






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2022






Allotment of CNG/ Petrol Pumps and LPG Agencies




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of petrol pumps/LPG agencies/CNG pumps allocated to various categories of people including SCs/STs by the Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) during the last three years and current year, OMC/State/UT/ Category-wise separately; and (b) the number of petrol pumps/ LPG agencies/ CNG pumps proposed to be allotted and established during the year 2022-23 State-wise OMC/State/UT/ Category-wise separately?






ANSWER




                                                           MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) & (b)   Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have informed that expansion of Retail Outlet (RO) network is a continuous process to ensure adequate availability of motoring fuels such as petrol and diesel throughout the country. Appointment of LPG distributorships is also a continuous process and the locations for setting up of LPG distributorships are identified based on sale potential that makes them commercially viable. State/UT/OMC/Category-wise number of Petrol Pumps and LPG Agencies allotted (Letter of Intents issued) to various categories of people including SCs/STs by Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) during the last three years and current year (2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 and current year (Apr-Dec, 21) are given in Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively. Establishment of CNG stations is part of development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) Network and the same is carried out by the entities authorized by Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB), which do not reserve allocation by category. State/UT-wise number of CNG stations established during the last three years is given in Annexure-III.  ****
