                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3532






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Rural Tourism Parks




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up rural tourism centres and tourism parks for promoting tourism in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of such centres and parks proposed in Rajasthan; (d) 	whether many religious places in Sikar district of Rajasthan including Khatu Shyamji, Jeenmata, Balaji and Lohagarh have great tourism potential; (e) 	whether the Government is making efforts to develop these places from tourism view point; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (f):	Ministry of Tourism under its Swadesh Darshan Scheme is developing tourism infrastructure in identified circuits including Tourist Parks and Tourism Clusters across the country having tourism potential in a planned and prioritized manner. Rural circuit has been identified as one of the thematic circuit for development under the scheme.   The projects under the scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Identification and selection of the circuits and approval of projects under the Swdaesh Darshan scheme is a continuous process.
