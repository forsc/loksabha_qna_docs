                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Food Parks Chains under PMKSY




Arun Sao













                                                    Vijay Baghel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the mega and mini food parks, cold storage chains infrastructure and food testing laboratories set up under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY), State-wise including Chhattisgarh;(b)	the details of the financial allocation made to improve the food processing infrastructure under the said scheme, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government has made any assessment regarding the number of farmers benefitted/being benefitted from the said scheme;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the details of the employment opportunities generated through this scheme especially in rural areas? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a):	State-wise details of Mega Food Parks, Agro-Processing Clusters (Mini Food Parks), Integrated Cold Chain projects and Food testing laboratories assisted under corresponding component schemes of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY), since the inception of these schemes, are placed at Annexure-I. (b):	 Being Central Sector  and demand driven scheme, no state wise allocation is made under PMKSY. The total financial allocation made under PMKSY since 201617 is placed at Annexure-II. (c) to (e): Ministry has conducted evaluation study of the relevant component schemes of PMKSY. The evaluation study of Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure Scheme under PMKSY, conducted by M/s NABARD Consultancy Limited (NABCONS) in Year 2020, has estimated that captive projects under the scheme have resulted in increase in farm-gate prices by 12.38 % and each project is estimated to benefit more than 9500 farmers. The estimation of farmers benefitted and employment opportunity generated through projects completed under the component schemes is placed at Annexure-III. *****
