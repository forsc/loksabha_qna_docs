                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Digital Panchayats




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of panchayas digitalised so far in the country under digital India; and(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) and (b) Under the Digital India Programme, BharatNet project is being implemented in phased manner to provide broadband connectivity in all the Gram Panchayats(GPs) in the country through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband technology including Fiber to the Home (FTTH). As on 27.01.2021, a total number of 1,51,431 GPs including Block Head Quarters (BHQs) have been made Service Ready. State/UT-wise Service Ready GPs is at Annexure-I.*****
