                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1349






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Declaration of Sabarimala as National Pilgrimage Centre




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has declared Sabarimala as a National Pilgrimage Centre; (b) 	if so the details thereof; and (c) 	the response of the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism does not declare any place as National Pilgrimage Centre however Ministry provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) to State Governments/Union Territory Administrations for various tourism projects, under the Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD Schemes subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. For development of Sabarimala in Kerala Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned two projects under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme as detailed below:-•	Development of Spiritual Circuit of Sabarimala - Erumeli-Pampa-Sannidhanam for Rs.99.99 Cr in 2016-17.•	Development of Spiritual Circuit of Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Aranmula- Sabrimala for Rs.92.22 Cr in 2016-17.********
