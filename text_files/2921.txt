                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2620






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Free Coaching to SC and OBC Students




Bidyut Baran Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether free coaching facility is being provided by the Government for students belonging to Scheduled Castes and Other Backward Classes; (b) if so, the details thereof and the numbers of such coaching institutes being run across the country at present, State/ UT-wise and course-wise; (c) whether any fund has been allocated by the Government to the States for implementation of this scheme; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. Financial support for coaching is being provided by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment to eligible students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Other Backward Classes (OBCs) under the Scheme of Free Coaching for SC and OBC Students to enable them to appear in competitive examinations and succeed in obtaining an appropriate job in public/ private sector.	Thirty one institutes with forty seven coaching centres have been empanelled across the country for providing coaching under the Scheme. State/UT-wise details of these institutes along with the courses approved are at Annexure.(c) & (d): The Scheme does not have provision for State-wise fund allocation. The coaching fee and stipend amount are released directly to the concerned institutes and students, respectively.****
