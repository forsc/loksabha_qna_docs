                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

924






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Sale of PSUs




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to sell some Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) in near future;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	 if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	 whether some plants are being sold in five years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(DR. BHAGWAT KISHANRAO KARAD)(a) to (d) The government has given ‘in-principle’ approval for strategic disinvestment of 35 CPSEs and/or Subsidiaries/ Units/ Joint Ventures of CPSEs with sale of entire or substantial stake along with transfer of management control.
