                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

378






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Free Wi-Fi Facility




 Kunhalikutty P.K.














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Airport Authority of India is implementing/in the process of implementing a full fledged free Wi-Fi facility to passengers at the Calicut International Airport as the current one is not available/working properly; and(b) if so, the details thereof including the name of service provider?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement in reply to parts (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Starred  (*)  Question No. 378 for 18.07.2019 regarding ''Free wi-fi facility''. ;; (a) & (b): Airports Authority of India (AAI) is providing free Wi Fi service to the passengers at Calicut International Airport at the departure side of the International terminal and the entire domestic terminal (arrival and departure both). The Wi Fi service is being provided through the service provider M/s Firefly Networks. The Wi Fi service provided at Calicut airport is functioning properly and allowing passengers to use free Wi Fi upto a data limit of 200 MB or 45 minutes, whichever is earlier. AAI has also approved a plan to provide free Wi Fi upto 45 minutes at the arrival side of the international terminal with probable date of commissioning as 31.08.2019.
