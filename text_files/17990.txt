                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1700






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Trains from Gandhidham to Delhi




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the people of Jalor district centre have no direct rail service from Jalor to Delhi and Jaipur even after ten years of broad-guage conversion due to which they have to cover a journey of 200 kilometers to Jodhpur to board train to reach Jaipur and the people belonging to Sancher, Raniwada, Bhinmal, Jalor have to cover a distance of about eight hundred miles in private buses to reach Delhi which is expensive and causes difficulty particularly to children and women;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to introduce a train from Gandhidham to Delhi via Jalor (Samdadi-Bhildi); and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) Prior to discontinuation of passenger train services to prevent spread of COVID-19 pandemic, Samdhari (59 km from Jalor) was connected to both Delhi and Jaipur by 01 pair and 02 pairs of Mail/Express services respectively. Passengers of Jalor desirous of travelling to Delhi and Jaipur may avail services of these trains by a single changeover at Samdhari.At present, all regular passenger train services are under cancellation in view of the COVID-19 pandemic. As such, there is no immediate proposal for introduction of trains between Gandhidham and Delhi via Jalor.*****
