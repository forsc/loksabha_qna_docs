                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2096






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Funds to Eklavya Model Residential School




C.N. Annadurai













                                                    Sanjay Seth
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                








                                                    Janardan Mishra
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the funds allocated and utilized for the Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) during the last three years and the current year, State-wise;(b) whether it is a fact that the quantum of funds required for the development and promotion of education in tribal dominated areas could not be allocated under this scheme;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government would provide the required funds for implementation of this scheme and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)  (a)    The State/UT-wise details of funds released and utilized for the Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) scheme during the last 3 years and current year is at Annexure.(b) and (c):   No Sir. There has been no shortage in allocation and utilization of funds.(d): EMRS scheme was being funded under Grants under Article 275 (1) of the constitution of India upto the year 2019-20.  However, from the year 2020-21, it is being funded with a separate budget line created for this scheme.  As per norms of the scheme, funds are released to state societies and construction agencies through National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) which is an autonomous organization under this Ministry. The budget allocation of the scheme for the year 2020-21 (RE) and 2021-22 (BE) is Rs. 1200 crore and Rs. 1418.04 cr respectively which is being utilized exclusively for implementation of this scheme.   Thus, Government is committed to provide funds and has allocated required funds for implementation of this scheme.   Annexure Annexure as mentioned in the reply to part (a) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Qs.
