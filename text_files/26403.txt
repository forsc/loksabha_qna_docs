                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Relief Package




Prathap Simha













                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the main features of the Government’s relief package announced during the second wave of the COVID-19 pandemic and the number of sectors covered under the relief package;(b) the planned recovery path for COVID-hit sectors like tourism, hospitality, sports and non-essential manufacturing; and(c) the number of people estimated to be benefited from the relief package, sector-wise?






ANSWER




                                                             THE MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)    (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (c): The Government of India on 28th June, 2021 announced a relief package of Rs.6,28,993 crores to support Indian economy in fight against COVID-19 pandemic.  A Statement indicating details of the package announced on 28th June, 2021 for COVID affected sectors is at Annexure-I.  The Government monitors the crucial parameters of the economy and takes steps to boost the sectors of the economy, where necessitated. In the wake of second wave of COVID-19, the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana, to ensure food security of poor/vulnerable, has been relaunched in May, 2021 and free of cost foodgrain @ 5 Kg per person is being provided to about 80 crore beneficiaries under  National Food Security Act (NFSA) till November 2021. The package envisages relief/stimulus to various sections/sectors by way of loans, subsidies, concessions, increased allocation etc., benefits of which will accrue to an individual/sector based on demand/performance.   Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) 3.0 was announced on 31.3.2021 to cover enterprises in hospitality, travel & tourism, leisure & sporting sectors and the overall ECLGS Scheme including the previous two versions have been extended by three months from March 31, 2021, till June 30, 2021, or until guarantees for the entire Rs 3 lakh crore amount are issued.
