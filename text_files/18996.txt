                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Exploration of New Mines




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether new mines are being discovered in the country by the Government for the development of mining activities;(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise, including Chhattisgarh;(c) the details of the development of mining activities in the country during the last five years, State-wise; and(d) the steps being taken including the survey of new mines by the Government for the development of mining sector?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) & (b): The development of any mining activity in the country is directly related to the number of potential blocks explored and resource augmented by the exploration agency which further leads to the auction of the blocks.  In last five years, from 2015-16 onwards, Geological Survey of India (GSI, an attached office under Ministry of Mines) and Mineral Exploration Corporation Limited (MECL, a public sector undertaking under Ministry of Mines) have handed over 143 (GSI-108 and MECL-35) exploration reports (G2/G3) to the State Government. The State-wise summary of the exploration reports handed over by GSI is at Annexure I. Further, 24 exploration reports of Field Season 2019-20 of GSI have been finalized and shall be handed over to State Governments. In the current Field Season of 2020-21, GSI has taken a total of 70 G2/G3 exploration programmes all over India covering commodities like Gold, Base Metal, Iron Ore, Manganese, REE, PGE etc. MECL is carrying out exploration for various mineral commodities in 28 blocks for Filed Season 2020-21. (c): As per the information received from respective State Governments, 105 mineral blocks have been auctioned in the country and a total of 61 mineral blocks are in pipeline for auction. The state-wise details are at Annexure II.
