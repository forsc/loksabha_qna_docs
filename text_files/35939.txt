                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2680






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Plantation along National Highways




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has formulated any plan for plantation on a large scale along the National Highways(NHs) particularly in Bihar; (b) if so, the quantun of funds spent thereon during the last financial year; (c) the details of the Highways in the State of Bihar identified for such plantation plan; (d) whether the Government has any plan to expand the forest cover in the country by such plantation along NHs; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (b) The Ministry of Road, Transport & Highways has promulgated Green Highways (Plantation, Transplantation, Beautification and Maintenance) Policy-2015 with the aim to develop Green Corridor on all National Highways including Bihar. The plantation activities are integral part of highway construction projects and the funds for plantation are not earmarked separately for projects in which plantation is part of Concession Agreement. For other plantation projects, funds have been allocated and released to Government Departments based on their estimate and to other Agencies based on the approved tender. (c)  Details of Highways identified for such plantations in financial year 2020-21 are as follows: 1. Muzaffarpur-Sonbarsa (NH-77)2. Muzaffarpur-Barauni (NH-28)3. Patna-Bakhtiyarpur section of NH-304. Chhapra-Gopalganj (NH-85)5. Muzaffarpur-Purnea (NH-27)6. Muzaffarpur-Siwan-Gopalganj (NH-28)7. Purnea-Gayakota section of NH-318. Piparkothi-Motihari-Raxaul section of NH-28A9. Forbesganj-Simrahi section of NH- 5710. Chhapra-Hajipur section of NH-1911.Devapur to Kotwa section of NH-28 (d) to (e) The plantation along National Highways is accounted for Tree Outside Forest (TOF) category of forest cover in the country. National Forest Policy of India, 1988 envisages to expand forest cover to 33% of geographical area of the country and plantation along National Highways (NHs) is carried out as per provisions of Green Highways (Plantation, Transplantation, Beautification and Maintenance) Policy- 2015. *****
