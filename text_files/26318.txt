                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2046






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






District Child Protection Units




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the number of District Child Protection Units formed and currently operational, State-wise;(b) 	the status of funds allocated and sanctioned since these units are responsible for the rehabilitation and alternative care for children along with other services; and(c) 	whether any study has been conducted by the Ministry to assess the effectiveness of these centers, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c):  The State-wise number of District Child Protection Units (DCPU) formed and currently operational,  supported under Child Protection Service(CPS) Scheme is at Annexure-I.  The details of funds released and utilised by the States/UTs during last year is at Annexure-II. The Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Amendment Bill, 2021,  to amend the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015, has been  passed by the Parliament. The  amended  Act  shall empower District Magistrates (DM) with the responsibility of care and protection of vulnerable children and the DM shall supervise the functioning of  District Child Protection Unit. ***  Annexure-I Annexure to part (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.2046 for 30.07.2021 raised by Smt.
