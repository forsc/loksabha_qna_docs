                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1964






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Naya Savera Yojana




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) 	whether the Government has launched Naya SaveraYojana for free coaching;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the number of people benefitted in States including Odisha along with the financial assistance provided there under?
                                                





ANSWER




Minister of Minority Affairs(Shri Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi)(a)& (b) The Ministry of Minority Affairs is implementing Naya Savera Scheme under which free coaching is provided to students belonging to economically weaker section of the six centrally notified minority communities namely Muslims, Sikhs, Christians, Buddhists, Jains and Zoroastrians (Parsis). The scheme provides coaching to students so as to enable them to qualify in entrance examinations for admission in technical / professional courses and various Competitive examinations for recruitment to Group ‘A’, ‘B’ and ‘C’ services. The scheme is implemented through Project Implementing Agencies / Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs).      (c) 	The number of people who have benefited under the scheme in the Country including Odisha is 1,01,725 and the financial assistance provided for the scheme is Rs. 3,13,07,91,786.*******
