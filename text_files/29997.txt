                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1827






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Pending Cases at UPSC Level




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it has come to the notice of the Government that several number of cases are pending for clearance at the UPSC level even after completion of selection process and if so, the details thereof; and (b)	whether the Government has studied the reason thereof and the appropriate steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b): No, Sir. The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) conducts examinations /recruitment tests for selection of candidates to various Group ‘A’ and Group ‘B’ services /posts against the vacancies reported on the basis of the Rules of Examination /Recruitment Rules. The details / dossiers of the recommended candidates, selected through examinations / recruitment tests are immediately forwarded by UPSC to the concerned Nodal Ministry /Department /Union Territory /Statutory Body for issue of offer of appointment etc. The recommendations of the Departmental Promotion Committees / Selection Committee Meetings for appointment by promotion /deputation are approved by Chairman, UPSC on the same /next day and are sent immediately to the Ministry concerned.*****
