                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1291






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Indebtness of Farmers




Sukhbir Singh Badal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of indebtedness of farmers in the country including in the State of Punjab; (b)	whether the Government has conducted any study in regard to indebtedness and financial distress of farmers during the last two years; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to waive off debts taken from banks and money lenders of farmers in the country including State of Punjab; and (e)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): 	As per the NSSO 70th Round, Report No. 577, Household Indebtedness in India 2013, the state-wise debt per agricultural households including Punjab is given in Annexure. (d) & (e): 	No Sir.
