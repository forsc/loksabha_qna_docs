                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

66






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Pradhan Mantri Kalyan Vikas Yojana in Rajasthan




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the number of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Kendras in the country, district-wise; (b) 	the details of the total number of students trained over the last three years, including Rajasthan; and(c) 	the data on total number of students that got employed after the training in the last three years, including Rajasthan? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Kendra (PMKK) in every district of the country for imparting skills training. The State-wise number of PMKKs and districts covered under PMKK is given at Annexure.(b)	Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, during last three years i.e. 2018-21, 85.65 lakh candidates have been trained / oriented across the country including 6.83 lakh candidates in State of Rajasthan.(c)	 Under short term training component of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, during last three years i.e. 2018-21, 15.29 lakh candidates have been provided with placement across the country including 1.07 lakh candidates of State of Rajasthan.
