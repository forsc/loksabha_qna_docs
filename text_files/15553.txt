                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Employment Situation




Anil Firojiya













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been a drastic change in the employment situation of some pilots and other staff due to alleged excess tension and financial burden;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether Indian Commercial Pilots Association (ICPA)  has  formulated  a  representation  to  the management of Air India raising objection on the changes in the present manners of payment; and(d)  if  so,  the  reaction  of  the  Government  in  this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): No, Sir.;;(b):  Does not arise in view of (a) above.;;(c) and (d):   Yes, Sir.  There have been slight delays in payment of salaries to employees of Air India Ltd, mainly because of constraints of liquidity and availability of funds.  However,  the salaries and allowances are being paid to all employees including pilots of Air India on a regular monthly basis.
