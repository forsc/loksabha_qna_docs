                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3023






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Projects of NHAI




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) is considering to raise Rs.10,000 crore for its projects and if so, the details thereof;(b)	Whether NHAI is also preparing DPR of Kooteripattu flyover in Arni region of Villupuram district in Tamil Nadu ;(c)	If so, the details thereof along with the estimated cost of the project; and(d)	The various proposed/upcoming projects of NHAI for the next three years and the length of new national highways that have been built during the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	During financial year 2019-20, NHAI has raised Rs. 54075.68 crore against a target of Rs. 75000 cr. For FY 2020-21 the IEBR target is Rs. 65000 crore.(b) to (c) Kooteripattu junction at Km 133+650 in Tindivanam – Ulundurpet section of NH-45 has been identified as a Black Spot based on the accidents during 2011-14 (ID No.TN-091-03).  A double cell Vehicular Underpass (VUP) is proposed to be constructed at Kooteripattu junction for which an estimate for Rs.33.54 Crore has been sanctioned by NHAI under Change of Scope (CoS) to be executed by the BOT Concessionaire, M/s Ulundurpet Expressways Ltd.  For execution of the work, additional land of 0.300 Ha is required for which 3D Notification is under publication.  After completion of LA process, the work would be taken up with construction period of 12 months.(d)	Details of proposed/upcoming projects of NHAI for the years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 are as under :Year	Project Length (in km)2019-20	37522020-21	91482021-22	9358 Details of length of NHs constructed by NHAI during the last three year and the current financial year is as under : Year	Length Constructed(in km)2016-17	26282017-18	30712018-19	33802019-20 (upto 28.02.20)	3277*****
