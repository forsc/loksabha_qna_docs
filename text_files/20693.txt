                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1978






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Mega Textile Parks




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to develop seven mega textile parks across the country; (b) if so, the details thereof along with time frame for completion of the said parks; (c) whether the above proposal would help in doubling the textile industry to 300 billion dollars by 2025; (d) the details of employment opportunities likely to be created through the setting up of these mega parks; and (e) whether the mega textile parks can be compared with similar parks in China, Vietnam, Ethiopa,       if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (e):	In the Union Budget 2021-22, it has been announced that a scheme of Mega Integrated Textile Region and Apparel (MITRA) Parks will be launched. Under the Scheme, 7 Mega Textile Parks will be established over 3 years.	The scheme will enable the textile industry to become globally competitive, attract large investments and boost employment generation. Under the scheme world class infrastructure with plug and play facilities will be created to enable create global champions in exports.  ***
