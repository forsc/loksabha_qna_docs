                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1449






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Production of PPE Kits and Ventilators




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Indian companies have produced Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) kit and ventilators especially for corona patients in the country recently; (b) 	if so, the number of PPE kits and ventilators that have been produced by these companies till date; (c) 	whether India is presently exporting PPE kits and ventilators to friendly countries as a good gesture; and (d) 	if so, the names of these countries?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b):   	Yes, sir. The data for production is not collected. However, the procurement by HLL Lifecare Ltd. from Indian companies of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) kit and ventilators is given in the  Annexure I. (c) & (d):  	 An export ban was imposed by India on the export of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) kit and ventilators to ensure adequate availability of these medical equipments. However, with the creation of sufficient production capacity, the export of PPE kit with a quota of 50 lakh per month, was permittedfrom July 27th, 2020 and was made exportable without any restrictions from August 25th, 2020. The restrictions on the export of ventilators were lifted and made freely exportable w.e.f. August 4th, 2020. These products have been exported commercially thereafter.
