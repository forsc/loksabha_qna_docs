                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3410






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Access to COVID-19 Data Collated by ICMR




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken cognizance of the request made by scientists, appointed for tracking/ developing new prediction model for COVID-19 pandemic, for wider access to the granular COVID-19 testing data collated by the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR);(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for such data not being made available to the above scientists;(c) the steps taken by the Government to make the ICMR database accessible to the scientists;(d) whether the data is available in real-time; and(e) if not, the average time taken between requesting access to the data and access being granted?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFAREDR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR(a) to (e): 	All testing data collated by the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) is accessible to National Informatics Centre (NIC), Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Govt of India, National Centre for Disease Control (NCDC), National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA), National Health Authority (NHA), and States/UTs Government. ICMR has a well-defined protocol for “Access to COVID-19 Health and Scientific Data”. As per the protocol an Independent Data Access Committee objectively/systematically reviews data access request pertaining to COVID-19 data repository. Data is available on real time basis.*****
