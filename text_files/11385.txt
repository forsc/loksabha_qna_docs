                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

256






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Achievements of CEFPPC Scheme




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacity (CEFPPC) scheme has been successful in achieving the objectives regarding creation of processing and preservation capacity as well as the modernization/expansion of existing food processing units with a view to increase the level of processing and value addition;(b)	 whether there has been any reduction of wastage through this;(c)	whether the quality of the end products has been improved; and(d)	the details of the fund allocated by the Government for this purpose, State- wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		Yes Sir.(b):	Due to increase in the level of processing, value addition and preservation capacities, there has been a reduction of wastage of agri-horti produce.(c):	Due to adoption of modern and innovative technologies by the food processing units approved under the Scheme, the quality of the end products has also been improved.(d):	The State-wise funds are not allocated under the scheme. However, a statement indicating details of number of projects approved, grants-in-aid sanctioned and released state-wise is at Annexure.*****
