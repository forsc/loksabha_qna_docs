                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1655






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Schemes for Improving Profitability of Agriculture




Rampreet Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	 whether  the  Government  has implemented  several  schemes  to  make agriculture  a  profitable  venture  in  the country;(b)	 if  so, the  names of  the scheme implemented for this purpose upto March, 2019;(c)        the time since when these schemes have been implemented in the country; and(d)	 the per hectare average production rate  of  wheat,  gram,  paddy,  oil  seeds, cotton,  sugarcane etc.  during  the  period from March, 2014 to March, 2019?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):     A statement indicating schemes and year since these have been implemented by Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare to make agriculture a profitable venture in the country is at Annexure-I.(d):	 A statement indicating per hectare yield of wheat, gram, paddy, oil seeds, cotton, sugarcane etc. during the period from March, 2014 to March, 2019 is at Annexure-II.
