                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5061






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Overnight Train from Mettupalayam




P. Velusamy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is having any proposal to extend the Coimbatore-Mangalore Intercity Express (22609/ 22610) to Nagercoil and to introduce an overnight train service from Kanyakumari to Mettupalayam via Palani for the benefit of people of southern States and pilgrims;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the steps taken/to be taken on this proposal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH  GOYAL)(a)	 No, Sir. There is no such proposal at present.(b)	 & (c) Do not arise.*****
