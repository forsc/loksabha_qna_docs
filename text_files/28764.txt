                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

87






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






COVID-19 Vaccination for Prisoners




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has maintained data of the number of prisoners who have received their COVID19 vaccinations; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Bihar; (c) whether the Government proposes to formulate a strategy to complete inoculation of prisoners in an expedited manner considering prison overcrowding; and (d) if so, the details thereof including the timeline by when the inoculation process is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 87* FOR 3RD DECEMBER, 2021(a) to (d) As on 29th November 2021, a total of 4,89,959 vaccine doses have been administered to Prisoners across States/UTs. Out of these 3,32,588 are 1st dose and 1,57,371 are 2nd dose, State/UTs wise details is at Annexure - 1.Adequate doses to vaccinate all eligible 1st dose and due 2nd dose beneficiaries aged 18 years and above (including prisoners) by December 2021 have been made available to State/UTs.
