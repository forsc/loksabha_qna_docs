                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4113






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Four Laning Project on Chhindwara-Nagpur Highway




Nakul K. Nath














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government/NHAI proposes four-laning of the Chhindwara-Nagpur Highway;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the estimated cost of the project; and(d) 	the time frame set for completion of the project and the time by which work on the said project is likely to start?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d)	The stretch from Chhindwara to Saoner of NH-69 has been upgraded to 2-lane with paved shoulders in March, 2015 and the stretch from Saoner to Nagpur of NH-69 has also been upgraded to 4-lane. Work of 4-lane from Chhindwara to Saoner of NH-69 has not been initiated.*****
