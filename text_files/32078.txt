                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3655






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Development of Tourism in Nabadwip and Nadia




Jagannath Sarkar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any suggestions/ representations from the Public Representative to improve, develop and promote tourism in Nabadwip and Nadia;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard;(c)   if not, the reasons therefor; (d)	whether the Government proposes to provide financial assistance to the State of West Bengal for development and promotion of tourism in Nabadwip as well as Nadia; and      (e)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                	        (SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):		Yes, Sir. Ministry of Tourism has received representation from the Public Representative requesting to undertake works for development and promotion of Nabadwip in00 Nadia. Since identification and  development of  tourism infrastructure is  undertaken primarily  by  the State  Government, the request  has  been  forwarded to the  State Government of  West Bengal. The Ministry of Tourism under its ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive’ (PRASHAD) schemes provide financial assistance to the State Governments for development of tourism infrastructure.  The project under the schemes  are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines,  utilization of funds released earlier etc.. Proposal for development of Nabadwip, Nadia is not under consideration in Ministry of Tourism in above mentioned schemes.The Ministry of Tourism  undertakes  holistic  promotion  of  various tourism destinations and products  of the country  including  those  in  West Bengal under its  schemes  of  ‘Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality (DPPH)’ and ‘Overseas Promotion and Publicity including Marketing Development Assistance (OPMD)’ in  Domestic  and  International  markets. *******
