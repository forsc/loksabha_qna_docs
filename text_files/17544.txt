                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1338






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Dispute between India and Nepa




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether there is any dispute between India and Nepal regarding the birth and life of Gauthama Budha (b)    if so, the details thereof;(c)    whether the Government is aware of the claim made by the Nepal Government regarding the birth place of Lord Ram;(d)    if so, the details thereof; and(e)    the steps being taken by the Government to prevent such cultural aggression by neighbouring countries?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a): No Sir. (b): Does not arise(c) to (e):  Government has not received any official communication in this regard from                  Government of Nepal. Government has also noted the clarification issued by the Government of  Nepal on 14.07.2020 on the subject.******
