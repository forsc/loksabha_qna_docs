                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1897






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Reserved Forest Land




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of such forest land declared as reserved in the country, State/ UT-wise along with Rajasthan;(b)	 the total quantum of land out of the reserved forest lands that have been encroached upon during the last five years, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether any steps have been taken by the Government to evict the encroached forest land from illegal occupation; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) The details of State/UTs wise Recorded Forest Area as reported by State/UTs Forest Department and published in the India State of Forest Report-2017 is given in    Annexure-I.(b) As per the information provided by the State/UTs, details of forest area under encroachment alongwith percentage are given in Annexure-II. (c) & (d)   Protection of forests from various threats, including encroachment, is primarily the responsibility of the respective State/ UT Governments as per the existing Acts/Rules. The State Forest Departments also issue directions to field officers/staff for regular patrolling and take action against encroachers of forest land remove encroachments, if any, as per extant law. Government of India issues advisories to the States/UTs to remove encroachment on forest land. Funds are provided to States/UTs for forest protection measures such as survey and demarcation of forest boundaries, fixing of boundary pillars etc under the Compensatory Afforestation Fund Act, 2016 and Compensatory Afforestation Fund Rules, 2018, funds. **********
