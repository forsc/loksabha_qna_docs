                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3384






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Advance Training Institute (ATI)




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of the Advance Training Institution (ATI) State-wise;(b)	Whether the Government has any policy to develop the syllabus of these institutions consistent with modern technology; and(c)	Whether the Government has any policy to develop the teaching process in (ATI) Dasnagar, Howrah and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        a)	In the year, 2018 erstwhile 8 Advanced Training Institutions and 25 other institutions have been renamed as National Skill Training Institute.  The list of NSTI state- wise with their earlier name is attached in Annexure –I.b)	The Directorate General of Training through its Sectoral Trade Course Committee (STCC) composed of academics and industry experts collaborates to formulate, and modify syllabus with the assistance of Central Staff Training & Research Institute in tune with industry practices and aligned to National Skill Qualification Framework. Updation of syllabus is a continuous process.  The NSTIs have also introduced industry 4.0 relevant courses such as IoT Technician - Smart Health Care, Smart City& Smart Agriculture and IT Cloud Computing & Networking.c)	The Government has in place the policy to develop the teaching process in the way of training of trainers development programme under Craft |Instructor Training Scheme (CITS) and conducts the Programme in 18 different trades at NSTI (erstwhile ATI), Howrah. In addition, it also undertakes CTS courses. Copy attached as Annexure- II.  Annexure – IDetails of National Skill Training Institutes (NSTI’s)(erstwhile ATIs, FTI, MITI & RVTIs )Sl.
