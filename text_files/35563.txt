                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2851






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Revitalising Infrastructure and Systems in Education




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under Revitalizing Infrastructure and Systems in Education (RISE), State-wise since 2015; and(b) the steps taken/being taken by the Government under RISE?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) & (b): “Revitalising Infrastructure and Systems in Education (RISE) by 2022” was approved by Government of India on 4th July, 2018 by suitably extending/modifying the existing financing norms of Higher Education Financing Agency (HEFA) to accommodate the infrastructure needs and financial capability of several categories of institutions under higher education, school education and institutions under the Ministry of Health.The Scheme is applicable for eligible Centrally Funded Higher Education Institutions (CFHEIs). The demands for funds/loans directly come from the Institutions and loan is sanctioned as per the Credit Policy of HEFA. Funds are released after execution of agreement between Institution and HEFA.Progress of the projects is monitored through a dashboard/project monitoring system. HEFA Board quarterly reviews the progress of the project and suitably guides the institutions.The details of funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under Revitalizing Infrastructure and Systems in Education (RISE), State-wise since 2015 are given at Annexure I. *****
