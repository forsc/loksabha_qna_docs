                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1594






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Fake Railway Recruitment Cases




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of fake railway recruitment cases reported during the last five years;(b)	the action being taken against the persons involved in this racket; and(c)	the manner in which the innocent people who became victims by the racket are being compensated?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): Sixty (60) complaints have been received alleging fraud in the name of providing jobs on Railways.(b): The complaints are primarily against private persons. The complaints have either been forwarded to police authorities or the complainants have been advised to themselves approach police authorities. There are also cases where complainants have simultaneously filed the complaint with police authorities alongwith sending the complaint to Railway Administration.(c): In view of (b) above, the issue of compensation is not applicable. However, members of the public are regularly being informed and educated regarding recruitment scams through media and other means so that they do not fall prey.*****
