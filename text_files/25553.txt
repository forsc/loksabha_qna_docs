                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1207






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Disability Certificate




Pratima Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether adults are eligible for specific learning disability certificate; (b) if so, the details of such centres across the country that conducts NIMHANS SLD Battery for adults; and(c) the details of process for applying for SLD certification?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) & (b)  On 04.01.2018, the Central Government notified disability assessment guidelines in respect of various disabilities recognised under the Rights of Persons with disabilities (RPwD) Act, 2016. These guidelines lay down the manner of evaluation of disabilities. In respect of specific learning disability (SLD), the certification is done for children aged 8 years & above only. The child has to undergo repeat certification at the age of 14 and 18 years. The certificate issued at the age of 18 years is valid for life.      The said guidelines also specify composition of medical authorities by States/UTs for assessment in respect of various disabilities including SLD. (c)  On 15.06.2017, the Central Government notified RPwD Rules, 2017. Rule 17-20 of these Rules deal with the manner of application & grant of certificate of disabilities. Further, the Central Government has made it mandatory for States/UTs to issue certificate of disability through UDID portal www.swavlambancard.gov.in  w.e.f. 01.06.2021.  This portal also provides for online submission of application for obtaining certificate of disability including SLD. *****
