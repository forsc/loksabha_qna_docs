                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5026






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






New Passport Kendra




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to open a new Passport Kendra in each district of the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government is aware that no passport Kendra is functioning in Nagapattinam District of Tamil Nadu;(c)	if so, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government proposes to open a Passport centre through Postal Sewa Kendra; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)  to (e)	Ministry in association with Department of Posts (DOP) had announced in January, 2017 the opening of Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSKs) at the Head Post Offices (HPO)/Post Offices (PO) in each Parliamentary constituency where there is no Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK). As on date, there are 521 Passport Kendras (93 Passport Seva Kendras and 428 Post Office Passport Seva Kendras) operational in the country. The list of Passport Kendras (93 PSKs and 428 POPSKs) is at Annexure-I & II. A Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK) is already functioning at Sirkali Head Post Office in Nagapattinam District under Mayiladuthurai Parliamentary (Lok Sabha) Constituency. In addition to this, one more POPSK is also functioning at Tiruthuraipoondi Head Post Office under Nagapattinam Parliamentary Constituency. ***
