                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3948






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Setting up of Archery Academy




Guman Singh Damor














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up an archery academy in Ratlam, Madhya Pradesh as youth here are particularly known for the archery skills;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the time by which a decision is likely to be taken in this regard in future?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c): ‘Sports’ being a State subject promotion of sports, including setting up of sports infrastructure facilities, is primarily the responsibility of State/ Union Territory (UT) Governments. Central Government supplements the efforts of State/ UT Governments through financial assistance to National Sports Federations (NSFs) and running expert training facilities in Sports Authority of India (SAI) centres and for bridging sports infrastructure gaps in States under Khelo India Scheme. As such, no proposal of setting up an archery academy in Ratlam, Madhya Pradesh is under consideration in this Ministry.Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports through Sports Authority of India (SAI) has been running various facilities across the country to train budding sportspersons as well as elite sportspersons in several sports disciplines, including Archery. The facilities can be availed by the talented youth hailing from the various States/ Union Territories. The details of the available training facilities run by SAI in the State of Madhya Pradesh is at Annexure.*****
