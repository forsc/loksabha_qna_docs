                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3678






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Military Airport in Purnea, Bihar




Santosh Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a military airport is operational in Purnea, Bihar since 1960 and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has decided to open it for civil aviation services temporarily;(c) if so, the details of the progress made in this regard; and(d) the time by which civil aviation services are likely to be started from the said airport?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a): Purnea airport is an Air Force Station (AFS) owned and managed by the Indian Air Force (IAF).;(b) to (d): A joint feasibility study for the development of a Civil Enclave at AFS, Purnea at a cost of Rs.150 crore was conducted by the Airports Authority of India (AAI) and the IAF with representatives of the State Administration of Bihar and accordingly, a land requirement of 50 acre was projected to the State Government of Bihar. The completion of the project is dependent on various factors including the handing over of land free of cost and free from all encumbrances to AAI by the State Government of Bihar, various project clearances, financial issues etc.******
