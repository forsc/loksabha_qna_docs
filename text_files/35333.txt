                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2384






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Resumpation of Operation of Suspended Trains




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the percentage of mail express trains that are being operated by Indian Railways in comparison to its earlier operations despite challenges posed by Covid so far;(b) the time by which all the trains from all important places of the country particularly from Gujarat are likely to be made fully operational; and(c) the time by which the stoppages of trains that were suspended earlier are likely to be restored?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW) (a) to (c) To prevent the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, Indian Railways had discontinued all regular passenger carrying trains w.e.f. 23rd March, 2020. The train services are being resumed and are increased in a phased manner.  As on 10th March, 2022, Indian Railways have resumed operation of 99 percent of Mail/Express train services, operated during pre-Covid Period, which are being operated as per rationalised time table.*****
