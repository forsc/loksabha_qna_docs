                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4168






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






New Technology for Construction of Houses




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	the present status of implementation of the Government’s vision of ‘Housing for All’ in the country, State/UT-wise;b.	whether it is true that NBCC is in the process of importing new technology called Non-Tectonic System from Hungary, US and Russia which helps in completing construction of house in 7-10 days; andc.	if so, details of such technology and status of procuring the same by NBCC?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): 	State/UT-wise details for present status of implementation of ‘Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) - Housing for All’ Mission in the country are at Annexure.(b) & (c):	National Buildings Construction Corporation Ltd. (NBCC) has signed a Memorandum of understanding (MoU) with M/s Gremound Engineering Ltd., Hungary on July 05, 2016 to promote Gremound’s Non-Tectonic open building system in India, to build live able houses in mass quantities at extra speed. The Gremound Non-Tectonic system is based on two fundamental technologies namely Gypsum Technology and Concrete Technology. 	NBCC has advised M/s Gremound to take up the matter with Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), Building Materials & Technology Promotion Council (BMTPC) and Central Public Works Department (CPWD) to get this technology certified and included in the Schedule of Rates.                   *****
