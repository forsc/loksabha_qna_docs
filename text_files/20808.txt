                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1986






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Implementation of NPCDCS




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether several workers have been appointed under National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke (NPCDCS) in the country during the last five years, State/UT-wise especially Rajasthan;(b) if so, the details thereof including the report submitted to the Government regarding the successful implementation of the said programme;(c) whether the Government proposes to incorporate the said workers in National Ayurveda Mission or some other programme so that they can be employed by their experience in health services; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a):	Yes.(b):	The National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke (NPCDCS) was initiated by Ministry of Health & Family Welfare in 2008. The Ministry of AYUSH initiated a pilot project to integrate AYUSH Systems with NPCDCS in 2015 – 16. The project has been implemented in eight Districts of eight States viz. Gaya (Bihar), Bhilwara (Rajasthan), Surendranagar (Gujarat), Krishna (Andhra Pradesh), Darjeeling (West Bengal), Nashik (Maharashtra), Sambalpur (Odisha) and Lakhimpur-Kheri (Uttar Pradesh). The total 682 manpower, including 207 in Rajasthan, is engaged to implement the programme under three research councils i.e. Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS), Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH) and Central Council for Research in Unani Medicine (CCRUM). The evaluation of the programme undertaken by National Institute of Health & Family Welfare reveals that the integration of AYUSH systems with conventional healthcare systems was a successful low cost working model. (c) & (d): No decision has been made to incorporate NPCDCS programme in National AYUSH Mission.
