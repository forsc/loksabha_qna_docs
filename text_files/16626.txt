                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

236






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Employment Guarantee Schem




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Government is aware of the demand for continuing the Employment Guarantee Scheme;(b)   if so, the details thereof;(c)   whether the people in rural areas are getting one hundred days of assured employment;(d)   if so, the details thereof; and(e)   if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	& (b): The mandate of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) is to provide for not less than 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult member volunteers to do unskilled manual work.  (c) to (e):  Mahatma Gandhi NREGA is a demand driven wage employment Scheme which provides livelihood security, i.e., fall back option for livelihood for the rural households. The demand for work is influenced by various factors such as rain-fall, availability of alternative and remunerative employment opportunities outside Mahatma Gandhi NREGS etc. During the current FY 2020-21 as on 12.09.2020, a total of 7.93 lakh households is various States/UTs, as per annexed details, completed 100 days of employment under the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS as per their demand. ****Annexure referred in reply to parts (c) to (e) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.236 dated 15.09.2020 No. of Families Completed 100 days FY 2020-21 as on 12.09.2020 Mahatma Gandhi NREGA
