                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2462






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Revival of HECL




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has prepared any scheme for revival of Heavy Engineering Corporation Ltd. (HECL), Ranchi (Jharkhand);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to transfer the administrative control over the HECL to Ministry of Atomic Energy keeping in view its contribution towards atomic energy; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                                THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                                            (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)                                                         ********(a) & (b): Government has not finalized any scheme for revival of HEC.  However, in March, 2017 Government has approved the transfer of 675.43 acres of HEC land to the Government of Jharkhand to raise Rs. 742.98 crore for liquidation of statutory dues and other liabilities.(c) & (d):  Government has not decided the transfer of HEC to Ministry of Atomic Energy.                                                        ***********
