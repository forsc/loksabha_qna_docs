                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

727






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






National Nutrition Mission




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the amount of funds allocated/released to Gujarat under National Nutrition Mission including the funds utilised during the last three years;(b)       whether the State of Gujarat has sent any proposal to the Government related to provision of balanced food under National Nutrition Mission;(c)       if so, the details thereof; and(d)       the details of action taken on the said proposal so far? 






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)         A total amount of Rs 29976.16 lakhs has been released to the State of Gujarat under POSHAN Abhiyaan (erstwhile National Nutrition Mission) during the last three Financial years. The details of funds released and utilized during the last three years by the state of Gujarat under POSHAN Abhiyaan (National Nutrition Mission) is at Annexure I.              In addition, under Anganwadi Services Scheme, Rs. 99395.5 Lakhs for Supplementary Nutrition Programme and Rs 89389.74 Lakhs for ICDS (General) have been released to the state of Gujarat during last three State Financial Years. The details of funds released and utilised during last three years by the of Gujarat under Anganwadi Services scheme is at Annexure II. (b) to (d) No such proposal related to provision of balanced food under POSHAN Abhiyaan (National Nutrition Mission) has been received by the Ministry  from the State of Gujarat. *****Annexure IStatement referred in Reply to part (a) of Lok Sabha Un-Starred Question No 727 for 23.07.2021 Details of Funds Released and Utilised by the State of Gujarat under POSHAN Abhiyaan during last three years       Rs in lakhsState	Released in FY2017 – 18 + Balance of ISSNIP	Released in FY2018-19	Released in FY2019-20	Released in FY2020-21	Total central funds released 	Total central funds utilisedGujarat	3036.66	11228.03	14863.00	0.00*	29127.69	21769.01 *Funds were not released for Program intervention during the Financial Year 2020-2021 due to availability of Rs 14779.16 lakhs as on 01.04.2020 with the state of Gujarat.
