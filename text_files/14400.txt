                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3061






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Allocation of Funds under AMRUT




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount allocated/released so far to Gujarat under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT); (b) 	the details of work done through the said amount, location-wise; and(c) 	the time by which the said work is likely to be completed and the number of beneficiaries under the mission?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a).	The  approved State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) for the 31 cities/towns selected under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) in the State of Gujarat for the entire Mission period amounts to ?4,884.42 crore, including committed Central Assistance of ?2,069.96 crore.So far, ?1,936.47 crore have been released as Central Assistance under various components of AMRUT, of which ?1,593.36 crore have been released for implementation of AMRUT projects, ?37.27 crore for Administrative and Office Expenses, ?162.73 crore as incentives for reforms, ?5.51 crore for GIS based master plan sub-scheme under AMRUT and ?137.60 crore for eligible spill-over projects of erstwhile Jawaharlal Nehru National Urban Renewal Mission (JnNURM).(b).	So far, Government of Gujarat has taken up 453 projects costing ?5,335.37 crore under the Mission, of which 179 projects costing ?1,268.33 crore have been completed, 224 projects costing ?3,619.68 crore are at various stages of implementation and 50 projects costing ?447.36 crore are under tendering. State of Gujarat has taken up projects in excess of their approved SAAP size of ?4,884.42 crore and the entire excess amount shall be borne by the State. The city-wise details of works taken up under AMRUT by the State of Gujarat are placed as Annexure.(c).	The Mission period of AMRUT scheme is five years from 25 June 2015 to 31 March 2020.  The 31 Mission cities of Gujarat covers about 78% of the State’s urban population.                                                 ****
