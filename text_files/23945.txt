                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4962






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Production by Thermal Power Stations




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of total electricity produced by thermal power stations in the country;(b) 	the details of new thermal power stations that have been established in the country during the last three years and the current year; and(c) 	whether the Government proposes to construct new thermal power stations in the future and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) : 		As per Central Electricity Authority (CEA), during the year 2020-21 (April’ 2020 - February’ 2021), generation from  thermal power stations utilities in the country was 925.14 Billion Units (BUs). (b) : 		The detailed list of thermal power plants which are commissioned during the last  three years and the current year (April’ 2020-February’ 2021) is furnished at Annexure.(c) : 		Under section 7 of Electricity Act 2003, generation of electricity has been  delicensed and Techno-Economic Clearance from Ministry of Power/Central Electricity Authority (CEA) is not required for Thermal Power Projects. *************
