                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4744






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL)




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the business carried out by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) during the last ten years with specific details of the years in which it was in profit and the years in which it was in loss during the last ten years; and (b) the list of defence equipment including the parts that has been manufactured by HAL so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a)  Business done by HAL in last ten yearsRs. In CroreParticulars	2010-11	2011-12	2012-13	2013-14	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Revenue from Operations	13124	14211	14328	15135	15730	16758	17950	18519	20008	21438Profit before tax	2839	3328	3497	3578	3172	3207	3583	3240	3742	3928(b)  The list of defence equipment manufactured by HAL is given below:1.  Fighters:•	Su-30MKI•	Light Combat Aircraft LCA•	MiG-21/Bison•	Jaguar2.  Trainers:•	Kiran MK I/II•	Hawk3.  Transport Aircraft:•	Do-228•	HS-7484.  Helicopters:•	Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH)•	Chetak•	Cheetah•	CheetalHAL is also manufacturing Engines, Communication equipment, Navigation equipment, Display systems, Hydraulic systems, Electric equipment etc., required for aircraft/helicopters.	Currently, out of the total airborne fleet in use by Indian Defence Services, HAL has supplied about 61% and is supporting 75% of all the aircrafts/helicopters.*****
