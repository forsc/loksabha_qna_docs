                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2481






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






High Security Registration Plate on Vehicles




Brijendra Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether all the States and UTs have implemented mandatory pre-fitting of High Security Registration Plate (HSRP) on all the vehicles as per rule; (b)	if not, the States/UTs which are yet to implement the same; (c)	whether any study has been conducted to examine the efficacy and utility of having HSRP; and (d)	if so, the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)and (b)The Ministry has issued regulations regarding mandatory pre-fitting of High Security Registration Plate (HSRP) as per Central Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989. The Ministry has not received any information from any State(s) that have not implemented the provisions of High Security Registration Plate (HSRP).(c) and (d) No such study has been conducted to examine the efficacy and utility of having HSRP.*****
