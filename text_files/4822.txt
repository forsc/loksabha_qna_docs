                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4382






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Data Entry Operators under NHM




Sakshi Ji Swami Maharaj














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Data Entry Operators working in the National Health Mission for the last 10-12 years are getting less emoluments than the outsourced Data Entry Operators; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government proposes to abolish the posts of Data Entry Operators working in various departments under his Ministry and outsource it entirely and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (d) whether the welfare of employees are likely to be affected by outsourcing these posts; and (e) if so, the steps taken by the Government to protect the interest of the employees?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e):   Public health being a State subject, the primary responsibility for fixing the emoluments of manpower including Data Entry Operator (DEOs) under National Health Mission (NHM), lies with the State Governments.  Under NHM, 5% annual increment is given to all staff every year based on performance appraisal, which is applicable to DEOs as well.At present there is no such proposal under NHM for consideration to abolish the posts of Data Entry Operators (DEO).  In 2016, States were advised to do the data entry to the extent possible, on tasks rate basis or through outsourcing and with the option to continue with the existing posts.………………
