                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

68






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Foodgrains to Jharkhand




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the district-wise details of the food grains provided to Jharkhand so far under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY); (b) 	the number of families benefitted under this scheme from entire Jharkhand, district-wise including Ranchi Parliamentary Constituency; and (c) 	whether there is any proportionate share of Central Government and State Government under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b):  Additional foodgrains @ 5 kg per person per month for around 264 lakh beneficiaries including those in Ranchi Parliamentary Constituency under Phase-I-Phase V of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) have been allocated to State of Jharkhand for free of cost distribution. 	Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) is operated under the joint responsibility of the Central and the State/UT Governments. The responsibility of identification of beneficiaries under National Food Security Act (NFSA) (and PMGKAY also, as targeted beneficiaries are same) and further sub allocation and distribution of foodgrains to districts within the State/UT lies with respective State/UT. Information regarding district-wise details of foodgrains distributed to Jharkhand and beneficiaries benefitted under PMGKAY as received from State Govt, is at Annexure-I & II respectively.(c): 	Govt. of India is bearing the entire expenditure towards this Scheme without any contribution by States/UTs*****ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.68 FOR ANSWER ON 02.02.2022 IN THE LOK SABHA.District-wise details of Foodgrains distributed in Jharkhand under PMGKAYSl.
