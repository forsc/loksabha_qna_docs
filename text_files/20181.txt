                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1603






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Change of Address in Aadhaar Cards




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has any proposal to automate the change of address in all the documents linked to Aadhaar card, when request to change the address in Aadhaar card is made by the Aadhaar card holder; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and  (b): No, Sir. There is no such proposal. However, the Ministries  and  Departments  of the  Central  Government  and  the State  Governments  and  other Requesting  Entities,  who  have seeded  Aadhaar  in  their  lists  of beneficiaries  (e.g.  Ration Card, MNREGA Job Card etc.) may carryout Aadhaar eKYC authentication, if so   allowed, with   specific consent  of  the  resident  to  fetch address    data    available in Aadhaar  in  conformity  with  the provisions of the Aadhaar Act, 2016 and the regulations framed thereunder.******
