                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3608






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






NABARD Assistance to Rural Banks




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any scheme has been started for providing financial assistance to the banks functioning in rural areas of the country or is proposed to be started by the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD); (b) if so, the details thereof and the purpose of granting this financial assistance; and (c) the details of the assistance provided by NABARD during the last three years and current year, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) to (c): As apprised by National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD), they have established a fund called ‘Cooperative Development Fund (CDF)’ to provide financial assistance to the Rural Credit Cooperative Institutions including Rural Cooperative Banks (RCBs). The financial assistance under CDF is provided to RCBs for the following purposes: i.	Assistance to Cooperative Training Institutes (CTIs) under “Scheme of Financial Assistance for Training of Cooperative Banks’ Personnel (SOFTCOB)” for programmes approved by NABARDii.	Infrastructure facilities to Primary Agricultural Credit Societies (PACS) in all Statesiii.	HRcapacity building measures, training for staff and management, conference and seminars iv.	Studies, Organisation Development Initiative (ODI), Workshops and Training through NABARD/Bankers Institute of Rural Development (BIRD)v.	Setting up of PACS Development Cells (PDC) in District Central Co-operative Banks(DCCBs) &State Co-operative Bank (StCB)vi.	To support exposure visits to well- functioning banks/societies across the statesvii.	Scheme for Comprehensive Support plan for NER including Sikkim, Jammu & Kashmir, A& N Island. Extension of grant support of maximum of ?100.00 lakh per stateviii.	Computerisation of PACS ix.	Setting up ‘Business Diversification and Product Innovation Cell’ in RCBs. Extension of grant support of ?66.00 lakh per statex.	Grant support to the extent of ?1.00 lakh per PACS to those Cooperative Credit Societies availing the facility of special refinance from NABARD under “PACS as Multi Service Centres (MSC)” refinance schemexi.	Assistance to BIRD towardsCentre for Professional Excellence in Cooperatives(CPEC) and on-location programmesThe details of the assistance provided by NABARD during the last three years and current yearare given at Annexure.****** AnnexureDetails of the assistance provided by NABARD during the last three years and current year (Rupees in Lakh)Sr No	Name 	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22 (upto 30.11.2021)1	Andaman  & Nicobar 	4.89	2.46	8.88	2.582	Andhra Pradesh	102.80	126.98	128.73	15.603	Arunachal Pradesh 	5.31	0.00	6.62	0.004	Assam 	12.00	14.78	34.31	0.365	Bihar 	10.87	11.25	18.14	1.756	Chhattisgarh 	12.64	8.46	16.05	0.027	Goa 	2.36	3.39	5.69	0.138	Gujarat	122.73	111.15	52.51	9.589	Haryana	61.07	56.61	11.18	2.8510	Himachal Pradesh 	33.77	54.56	50.05	5.4411	Jammu & Kashmir 	3.57	0.06	0.05	0.0012	Jharkhand 	2.58	7.04	7.94	0.0013	Karnataka 	82.98	103.12	80.18	5.6314	Kerala 	133.10	163.19	71.25	11.8915	Madhya Pradesh 	31.80	60.19	45.12	8.0816	Maharashtra 	85.37	91.10	53.62	13.5817	Manipur 	2.80	3.49	19.96	5.0018	Meghalay	4.97	0.00	4.80	1.3719	Mizoram 	13.03	15.26	19.11	3.5420	Nagaland 	3.18	2.16	3.60	0.0021	Odhisa	102.92	138.22	50.17	3.4922	Punjab 	89.12	69.98	70.79	13.423	Puducherry	0.00	0.00	0.00	0.0024	Rajasthan 	16.08	24.00	55.46	15.0125	Sikkim 	3.95	2.00	15.49	8.4626	Tamil Nadu 	142.02	131.30	91.29	33.5127	Telangana 	70.95	132.36	591.46	17.4028	Tripura	0.00	12.16	4.29	0.0029	Uttarakhand 	24.04	86.36	39.62	500.0030	Uttar Pradesh 	138.86	45.23	101.24	500.0931	West Bengal 	69.78	62.33	13.26	14.8232	Head Office	276.13	250.44	200.17	38.40 	Total 	1665.67	1789.63	1871.03	1231.98
