                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3248






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Staff in Kendriya Vidyalaya Libraries




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has made provision fordeployment of sub-staff in its school libraries;(b) if so, the details of KVs of Delhi Region where sub-staff is deployed;(c) whether in-spite of instructions issued by the KVS, it is not being followed byPrincipals of some Kendriya Vidyalayas and if so, the details thereof includingDelhi region along with the reasons therefor and action taken thereon;(d) whether the Librarians have been assigned class teachership without deploymentof sub-staff and if so, the details thereof in Delhi region; and(e) the steps being taken by the Government to ensure deployment of sub-staff forefficient functioning of school libraries?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Instructions have been issued by Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) to all itsRegional Offices to advise the Principals under their administrative control to deploy onesub-staff available in the school to assist the Librarian.(b) The details of the KVs in Delhi Region where sub-staff have been deployed areas under:-<pre>Name of KVAGCR Colony, Andrews Ganj, Chhawla Camp, No. 1 Delhi Cantt., No. 2 Delhi Cantt.,No. 3 Delhi Cantt., Sec-5 Dwarka, Sector-12 Dwarka, Gole Market, Keshavpuram,Masjid Moth, Paschim Vihar, Pitampura, Pushp Vihar, Sec-2 RK Puram, Sec-4 RKPuram, Sector-8 RK Puram, AFS Rajokri, Sec-3 Rohini, Sec-8 Rohini, Sector-22Rohini, Sec-25 Rohini, Sec. 28 Rohini, Sainik Vihar, Shalimar Bagh, Tagore Garden,AFS Tughlakabad, NFC Vigyan Vihar, Dwarka SPG, Dr. Rajendra Prasad KV(President Estate), CRPF Jharoda Kalan, JNU New Mehrauli Road, Narela, Khichripur,NTPC Badarpur & AAI Rangpuri.</pre>(c) Consequent upon a few cases of non-compliance of the above instructions byPrincipals of some KVs, these instructions have been reiterated by KVS.(d) No, Sir.(e) The instructions issued in this regard have again been reiterated on 25.11.2019for ensuring strict compliance.*****
