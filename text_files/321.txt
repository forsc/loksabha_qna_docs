                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

335






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Regulatory Mechanison in Higher Education




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government/University Grants Commission propose to review thepresent regulatory mechanism in the field of higher education in privateuniversities/colleges in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps taken by the Government/University Grants Commission to deal with theemerging challenges in the field of higher education?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): No Sir. Presently, Private Universities are regulated by University GrantsCommission (UGC) as per the provisions of the UGC (Establishment of and Maintenanceof Standards in Private Universities) Regulations, 2003.(b): In view of the (a) above, does not arise.(c): In order to deal with the emerging challenges, the Government / UGC has takenseveral measures which include promotion of autonomy to Higher EducationalInstitutions, encouraging institutions to improve the quality of research through variousschemes viz. Impacting Research Innovation and Technology (IMPRINT), ImpactfulPolicy Research in Social Sciences (IMPRESS), Global Initiative for Academics Network(GIAN), Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds (SWAYAM), StaffTraining and Research Institute of Distance Education (STRIDE), etc. The focus is onLearning Outcome based Curriculum (LOCF) to make learning oriented expectationsand outcome at the end of Under Graduate / Post Graduate level. The UGC has alsobrought out Regulations on Promotion of Academic Integrity and prevention ofPlagiarism in Higher Educational Institutions and Regulations on Open & DistanceLearning in 2018.*****
