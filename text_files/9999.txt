                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

306






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






National  Food  Security  Mission




V. Kalanidhi













                                                    Andimuthu Raja
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the features of the National Food Security Mission (NFSM); (b) 	the details of the number of components being covered under this Mission and the details of the number of States/districts in which this Mission is being implemented; (c) 	the details of the targets set and achievements made so far under this Mission; (d) 	whether it has increased the production of the components being covered under this Mission and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	whether the Government is planning to include Tamil Nadu under NFSM to promote pulses and oil seeds cultivation and if so, the details thereof, district-wise and the target likely to be set in this regard along with the funds likely to be sanctioned for this purpose, district-wise in Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (e):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 306  DUE FOR REPLY ON 10TH  DECEMBER, 2019.(a): 	In order  to  enhance  the  production  of rice, wheat,  pulses,  coarse cereals, nutri-cereals, commercial crops and oilseeds in the country, the National Food Security Mission (NFSM) is being implemented through area expansion and productivity enhancement in a sustainable manner; restoring soil fertility and productivity at the individual farm level; enhancing farm level economy i.e. (farm profits) to restore confidence amongst the farmers; and enhancing post-harvest value addition at farm gate for better price realization to farmers through efficient market linkages. For food grains and oilseeds, assistance is being provided on interventions such as cluster demonstrations on improved package of practices, cropping system, seed distribution & production of high yielding varieties, farm machineries/resource conservation machineries/tools, efficient water application tools, plant protection measures, nutrient management/ soil ameliorants, cropping system based trainings to the  farmers. (b):	There are seven components being implemented under NFSM i.e., NFSM-Rice, NFSM-Wheat, NFSM-Pulses, NFSM-Coarse Cereals, NFSM-Nutri-Cereals, NFSM-Commercial Crops and NFSM-Oilseeds.
