                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4341






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Physiotherapy Clinics




DNV Senthilkumar. S.













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of Government and private physiotherapy clinics functioning at present in the country;(b) whether there is acute shortage of physiotherapy clinics in the country;(c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the corrective steps in this regard; (d) whether the Government has fixed any criteria and qualification to run a physiotherapy centre;(e) if so, the details regarding requirement of certification to run a physiotherapy clinic to ensure public safety;(f) the number of sanctioned post of various grades of physiotherapists in general and SC/ST/OBC in various Central Government hospitals in the country;(g) whether the Government has failed to fill up the posts of physiotherapists resulting in heavy rush in hospital, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor and the steps taken by the Government to fill up the posts in a time bound manner; and(h) the other steps taken by the Government to promote physiotherapy in Government hospitals in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Health being a State subject, the information is not maintained Centrally.  Government of India has notified Clinical Establishments Act, 2010 and Clinical Establishments (central Government) Rules, 2012 thereunder for registration and regulation of Clinical Establishments in the States/UTs wherever the Act is applicable.   As on date Clinical Establishments Act is applicable in 11 States and 6 Union Territories namely Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Himachal Pradesh, Jharkhand, Mizoram, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Haryana, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Chandigarh, Dadra & Nagar Haveli, Daman & Diu, Lakshadweep, Jammu and Kashmir, Ladakh and Puducherry However, the implementation of the Act is within the remit of respective State/UT Government.  As per information available in the State-wise online data of clinical establishments registered under the Clinical Establishments Act 2010, the number of Government and Private clinical establishments providing Physiotherapy services is given in the table as under: State Name	Total	Govt.	PrivateAnaman & Nicobar Islands (UT)	10	4	6Arunachal Pradesh	2	1	1Assam	186	30	156Chandigarh (UT)	35	7	28Dadra & Nagar Haveli (UT)	10	2	8Daman & Diu (UT)	14	6	8Haryana	84	20	64Himachal pradesh	97	13	84Jharkhand	182	12	170Puducherry (UT)	15	1	14Rajasthan	37	10	27Uttarakhand	31	7	24Total	703	113	590(b) & (c):	 No such data is maintained Centrally.(d) &(e):  Under Clinical establishments Act, 2010 National Council for Clinical establishments framed the Minimum standards for physiotherapy centers which is available at web-link: http://www.clinicalestablishments.gov.in/WriteReadData/597.pdf	The registration of a Clinical Establishment including physiotherapy centre is mandatory in the States/UTs wherever the aforesaid Act is applicable. For registration and continuation, the physiotherapy clinic/centres are required to comply with the minimum standards.
