                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Hindi Vital for Nation’s Global Identity




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government considers only Hindi can work to unite the country and that onecommon language that is Hindi is vital for nation’s global identity;(b) whether the Government is taking steps to realise the above facts and an all out efforts are being made topopularise and/or promote Hindi language; and(c) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Article 351 of the Constitution provides that it shall be the duty of the Union to promote the spread of theHindi language to develop it so that is may serve as a medium of expression for the elements of thecomposite culture of India.(b) & (c) Ministry of Human Resource Development has established 2 institutes for promotion of Hindilanguage namely, Kendriya Hindi Sansthan (KHS), Agra and Central Hindi Directorate (CHD), New Delhi.KHS, Agra conducts various Teachers-Training courses for the candidates of Non-Hindi states and CHDworks for preparation of dictionaries, conducts correspondence courses, standardisation of Devnagri Scriptand Hindi Vartani and provides financial assistance to Voluntary Hindi Organizations for promotion ofHindi.Commission for Scientific and Technical Terminology, New Delhi evolves and defines scientific andtechnical terms in Indian languages including Hindi and publishes technical dictionaries/glossaries,definitional dictionaries and encyclopedias.To implement the provisions of the Official language policy of the Govt. of India, the All India Councilfor Technical Education (AICTE), has introduced “TAKNIKI PATHYAPUSTAK PURASKAR YOJANA”since 1998 with a view to provide state of the art knowledge of Engineering and Technology in Hindilanguage.****
