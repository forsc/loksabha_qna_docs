                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1028






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Indigreen Compressed Bio-gas




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of setting up of production plants to produce Compressed Bio-Gas (CBG) underthe brand name of Indigreen;(b) the list of the plants under process for production of Indigreen CBG;(c) whether the Government proposes to open up again to call for new entrepreneurs to set upIndigreen CBG Plants; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) and (b) Compressed Bio Gas (CBG) is not produced under the brand name Indigreen.Currently Indian Oil Corporation Limited markets CBG procured from 12 CBG plants under thebrand name Indigreen.(c) and (d) Government does not call for entrepreneurs to set up Indigreen CBG plants. Oil andGas Marketing Companies continues to invite Expression of Interest for production and supplyof CBG under Sustainable Alternative Towards Affordable Transportation (SATAT) initiative.*****
