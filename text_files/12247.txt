                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

111






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Devolutionary Grants




Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil













                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether Maharashtra has got an increase in devolutionary grant under the recommendation of Fourteenth Finance  Commission;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the funds received by the State during the last three years under this head, Category- wise;(c)	the total Central loan given to Karnataka Government as on date, indicating the principal amount and interest separately; and(d)	the details of loan paid back to the Union Government during the last three years and the steps taken to recover the balance amount of the loan?






ANSWER
