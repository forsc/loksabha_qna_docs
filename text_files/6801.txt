                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

467






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Wi-Fi in Villages




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to provide Wi-Fi in all the villages across the country through GramNet, specially in Maharashtra State; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of villages where the Government has already provided the aforementioned services, State/UT-wise; and (d) 	whether any private agencies are involved as intermediaries or implementing agencies in the process and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to  (c)	Under BharatNet Project (earlier known as National Optical Fibre Network), all the 2,50,000 (approx.) Gram Panchayats of the country are planned to be provided with broadband connectivity. As on 07.11.2019, by laying 3,80,988 km Optical Fibre Cable (OFC), a total of 1,40,668 Gram Panchayats (GPs) have been connected, out of which 1,27,393 GPs have been made Service Ready. In addition, 983 GPs have been made Service Ready on satellite media. As part of BharatNet project, the Last Mile connectivity, through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband technology to access broadband /internet services is to be provided at all the 2.5 lakh GPs in the country. As of now, Wi-Fi hotspots have been installed in 45769 GPs (including GPs connected through Satellite media) and out of them, services are being provided in 16,151. In Maharashtra Wi-Fi APs have been installed in 776 GPs, out of which services have been provided in 330 GPs.The Details of Gram Panchayat where Wi-Fi Services have been provided, State/UT-wise is attached as Annexure-I.(d)	Wi- Fi hotspots are being provided by CSC e-Governance Services India Limited, a SPV under MeitY, Govt. of India and RISL (a PSU of State Government of Rajasthan) under phase I of BharatNet projects.
