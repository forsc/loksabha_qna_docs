                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3738






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Proposal for Construction of Hostels




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3738TO BE ANSWERED ON 17.03.2020PROPOSAL FOR CONSTRUCTION OF HOSTELS †3738. SHRI VISHNU DAYAL RAM: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has received any proposal from various States of the country for the construction of hostels for students belonging to Scheduled Castes and Other Backward Classes; (b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise particularly in Jharkhand and Bihar; (c) the time by which the said proposal is likely to be approved/implemented; and (d) the details of the funds allocated, sanctioned and released for the said purpose, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	to (d):  Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has received a total of 20 complete proposals from States under the Schemes of ‘Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojna (BJRCY)’ and ‘Construction of Hostels for OBC Boys and Girls’ during 2019-20, for construction/expansion of hostels for students belonging to Scheduled Castes and Other Backward Classes in the country. No such proposals have been received from Jharkhand and Bihar during 2019-20. The State-wise details of complete proposals received during the year are annexed.State-wise funds are not allocated under the Schemes of Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojna (BJRCY) and Construction of Hostels for OBC Boys and Girls.  However, the State-wise details of funds sanctioned and released under these Schemes during the year 2019-20 are annexed.   Proposals for construction of hostels for SCs and OBCs under these Schemes are approved by the Ministry on receipt of Utilization Certificates against earlier releases and availability of fund.  As such, no time frame can be prescribed for approval of the proposals.   ***** AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a) & (d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3738 for 17.03.2020 asked by Shri Vishnu Dayal Ram regarding “Proposal for Construction of Hostels”.State-wise details of complete proposals received in 2019-20 for construction of hostels and fund released during the period under the Schemes of ‘Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojna’ and ‘Construction of Hostels for OBC Boys and Girls’I.
