                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2973






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Z-Morh Tunnel Project




Farooq Abdullah














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the pace of work on Z-Morh Tunnel project in Jammu & Kashmir is very slow and needs to be accelerated;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the present status of work on the said tunnel; and(c)	the time-frame set for completion of work thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)     Yes Sir, The Z-Morh tunnel project was awarded on BOT (Annuity) basis with Concession Period of 20 years including 5 years of construction period.  But, due to financial crisis of IL&FS group of companies, the Concessionaire stopped all work w.e.f. 20.07.2018.  NHIDCL is in the process of foreclosure of Concession Agreement.  The project has been re-tendered on 24.06.2019.  The bid due date is 01.08.2019.(c)	The Construction period for the project would be 42 months from the Appointed Date.*****
