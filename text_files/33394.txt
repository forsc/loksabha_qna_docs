                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

855






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Pradhan Mantri Laghu Vyapari Maan-Dhan Yojana




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the date on which Pradhan Mantri Laghu Vyapari Maan-Dhan Yojana was started and the process of selection under it;(b)whether any criteria has been set for the beneficiaries of the said scheme and if so, the details thereof; (c)the total number and details of eligible persons under the said scheme since its inception till December, 2021, State and year-wise; and(d)the district-wise details of the beneficiaries under the said scheme in Rajasthan including the details of numerical data of approval and the amount of benefit during the year 2019-20 to 2021-22, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	The Pradhan Mantri Laghu Vyapari Maan-Dhan Yojana  renamed as National Pension Scheme for Traders, Shopkeepers & Self Employed Persons (NPS-Traders) was started on 12.09.2019.(b):  The traders in the age group of 18-40 years with an annual turnover, not exceeding Rs.1.5 crores and who are not members of EPFO/ESIC/NPS (Govt. funded)/PM-SYM and not an income tax payer, are eligible to join the scheme. (c): 	The State and year-wise details of number of person enrolled under the scheme since its inception till December, 2021 are at Annexure-A.(d):	 The district-wise details of the beneficiaries enrolled under the NPS Traders scheme in Rajasthan are at Annexure-B. The beneficiaries are entitled to get the pensionary benefits after attaining the age of 60 years.**                                                      ******
