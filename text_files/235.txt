                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

362






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Special Assistance under the National Mission on Libraries




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds provided for archives, Government libraries and museums in the State of Gujarat during the last three years and the current year, year-wise and scheme-wise;(b)	whether any special assistance is provided under the National Mission on Libraries for setting up of libraries in Gujarat and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the time by which the said special assistance are likely to be provided to Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Culture and Tourism(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Sir, the details of funds sanctioned for archives, Government libraries and museums in the State of Gujarat during the last three years and the current year, year-wise and scheme-wise are given at Annexure.(b)	& (c) Rs.210 Lakh has been sanctioned for State Central Library, Gandhinagar during 2017-18 under National Mission on Libraries (NML) Model Library Scheme.
