                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3014






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Houses for Poor under PMAY-G




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the total allocated budget under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) for the poor people living in rural areas during each of the last three years, year/State-wise;(b) 	the details of the target fixed for the housing of rural areas of Maharashtra in the current financial year; and(c) 	whether any survey was conducted in this regard and if so, the details of the target fixed, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)        The Year-wise and State-wise details of the total funds released under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) for the poor people living in rural areas during each of the last three years i.e. financial year (FY) 2018-19 to 2020-21 is given at Annexure. (b)        Under PMAY-G, a target of construction of 3,91,921 houses has been allocated to the State of Maharashtra during current FY i.e. 2021-22. (c)        The beneficiaries under the scheme are identified from Socio Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 and Awaas+ survey. Against the overall target for construction of 2.95 crore houses under PMAY-G, a target of 2.62 crore (approx.) houses has already been allocated to the States/UTs from financial year 2016-17 till 2021-22. The Year-wise breakup of the targets allocated to the States/UTs is given in the table below:                                                                                      (Units in nos.)             Year	                    Targets allocated to the States/UTs2016-17	                        42,32,3682017-18	                        31,53,8722018-19	                        25,13,6622019-20	                        58,62,1992020-21	                        43,69,6902021-22	                        61,02,372Total	                      2,62,34,163  *****
