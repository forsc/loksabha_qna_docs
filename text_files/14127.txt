                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Bharatpur Station as Model Railway Station




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Bharatpur railway station has been declared as a model station; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of the works completed or works still left over under model station project at the above said railway station?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): Bharatpur railway station was declared as Model station in the year 2006.  The station has already been developed under the Model railway station scheme and several works like retiring rooms, common waiting rooms with bathing facilities, separate waiting rooms for ladies and gents, separate waiting rooms for upper and second class, circulating area with parking facility, train indicator board, water coolers, signages, cloak room, enquiry & computer based announcement, public address system, refreshment rooms, booksalls and other stalls for essential goods have been completed under this scheme. *****
