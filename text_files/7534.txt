                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1348






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Institutional  Support for  Marketing of Tribal Produce




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the details of amount of funds allocated under Institutional Support for Development and Marketing of Tribal Produce Scheme during the last five years, State-wise;     (b)    the funds utilised under Institutional Support for Development and Marketing of Tribal Produce Scheme during the last five Years, State-wise;(c)      the total market size of Tribal Produce in crores, State-wise; and  (d)      whether the market size of Tribal Produce increased or decreased, year-wise in    last five years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)   	The scheme being demand driven. State-wise allocations of funds are not made. However, funds sanctioned to various States under the scheme is at Annexure.(b) 	Details of amount of funds Utilised under ‘Institutional Support for Development and Marketing of Tribal Produce Scheme’ during the last five years, State-wise are at Annexure. (c)     No survey has been undertaken to identify the market size of tribal produce.
