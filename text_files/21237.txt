                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2466






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Inclusion in the List of OBCs




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2466TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.03.2021INCLUSION IN THE LIST OF OBCs2466. DR. MOHAMMAD JAWED: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to include Surjapuri caste of Bihar in the Other Backward Classes (OBCs) list; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the State Government of Bihar had sent its recommendation in this regard to the Union Government; (d) if so, the details of the action taken by the Government so far in this regard; and (e) the time by which the Government is likely to include the Surjapuri caste of Bihar in the OBCs list?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) to (e):   No proposal for inclusion of the Surjapuri caste of Bihar in the Central List of Other Backward Classes (OBCs) has been received from the Government of Bihar in the Department. Accordingly, the issue is not under consideration of the Government at present.*****
