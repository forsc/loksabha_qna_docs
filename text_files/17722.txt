                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1392






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






CSR Funds




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has allocated funds to Madhya Pradesh under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) through the Ministry of Heavy Industries during the last three years;(b) if so, the names of aspirational districts of Madhya Pradesh along with the details thereof; and(c) the efforts made by the Government to monitor the expenditure of the said funds by the Ministry of Heavy Industries?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c): Department of Heavy Industry does not directly allocate or monitor funds under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR). This is being done by Central Public Sector Enterprises. Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL), a CPSE under the administrative control of this Department has spent Rs.7.36 Lakhs in 2018-19 and 2019-20 in the five aspirational districts of Madhya Pradesh, namely Damoh, Guna, Khandwa, Rajgarh and Vidisha.                                              ---------
