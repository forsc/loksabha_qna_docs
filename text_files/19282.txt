                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Allocation of Funds for Minority Programmes




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi













                                                    Subhas Sarkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated, released and spent/utilized under various welfare schemes/programmes for the minorities during the last five years, States/scheme-wise;(b) the amount spent on the scholarship of the students of minority communities;(c) the number of students benefited from such scholarship during the said period;(d) the classification of scholarship beneficiaries among six religious minorities during 2015 to 2020 viz. Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains and Parsis; and(e) the details of beneficiaries under above schemes/programmes in the country including West Bengal during the above period?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):        Scheme-wise details of the  funds allocated and released/utilised under various schemes to meet the scheme objectives during the last five years i.e. 2015-16 to 2019-20 is  at Annexure. No state-wise allocation is done in the Budget. (b) & (c): Since 2014-15 to till date, more than 4 crore scholarships have been sanctioned to the students belonging to economically weaker sections of minority communities, and Rs.11726.45 crores have been released to the beneficiaries. An amount of Rs. Rs.9223.68 crores has been spent for providing scholarship to about 3.06 crore beneficiaries under various scholarship schemes of the Ministry during the period from 2015-16 to 2019-20, out of which about 54% scholarships have been awarded to minority girl students.(d):  Details of community-wise number of beneficiaries under scholarship schemes during the last five years are given below:	              Name of Minority Community	Muslims	Christians	Sikhs	Parsis	Jains	BuddhistsTotal No. of Scholarshipsfrom 2015-16 to 2019-20	2,33,15,751	36,04,499	25,41,879	4,562	4,08,415	7,44,440(e). During the last five years total 3.19 crore beneficiaries have been benefitted under various schemes of the Ministry out of which 50.96 lakh beneficiaries belong to West Bengal.Under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karykaram (PMJVK), the Ministry accorded approval for plans/projects of the State of West Bengal having total project cost of Rs. 2514.6136 crore and central share of Rs. 1629.62 crore. An amount of Rs. 1680.1977 crore has been released by the Ministry as installments of Central Share.The projects approved by the Ministry include School Buildings (63), Additional Class Rooms (8627), ACR Blocks (107), Cyber Gram (170005), Hostels (161), ITI Buildings (15), IAY (6517),Skill Centres (4), Health Projects (342), Working Women Hostel (1), Drinking Water Supply (5779), Anganwadi Centres (2765), Sadhbhav Mandaps (151), Teaching Aid (169), Market Sheds (138), Toilets Blocks (2842) etc.*****
