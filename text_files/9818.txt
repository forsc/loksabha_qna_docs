                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3402






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Development of Udaipur as World Class Tourist Destination




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government of India is considering to make Udaipur district of Rajasthan which is also known as the city of lakes, a world class tourist destination; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b):	Ministry of Tourism under Swadesh Darshan Scheme is developing thematic circuits in the country in planned and prioritised manner with following objectives:•	Enhancing the tourist attractiveness in a sustainable manner by developing world class infrastructure in the circuit /destinations;•	Follow community based development and pro-poor tourism approach;•	Position tourism as a major engine of economic growth and job creation;•	Make full use of the potential and advantages in terms of available infrastructure, national culture and characteristic strong points of each and every region throughout the country by development of theme based circuits.	The projects under the scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Submission of project proposal by the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations and its sanctioning is a continuous process. 	Ministry under the Heritage, Krishna and Spiritual themes of Swadesh Darshan Scheme has sanctioned projects for development of tourist facilities in and around the Udaipur.
