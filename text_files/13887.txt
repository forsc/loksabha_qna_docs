                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Audio Description




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has issued a directive to film and TV series producers, including online streaming platforms to include audio description within the production;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether his Ministry proposes to make audio description mandatory for producers and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the steps taken by the Government to ensure accessibility in media for people with visual impairment?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR):(a) to (d): An advisory was issued by the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting on 01.10.2019 to major Film Producer Associations to motivate and persuade their associated members to make Audio Description a part of the production and distribution process of a film itself in order to make cinema viewing more inclusive and promote non-discrimination of persons with disabilities in public places like theatres.
