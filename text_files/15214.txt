                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3824






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Increasing Allocation for MGNREGS in Drought Hit Areas




Narayana Swamy Abbaiah














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any proposal to increase the allocation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) for the drought hit areas including central Karnataka;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether additional employment under MGNREGS for the drought affected districts in Karnataka is under consideration and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether any additional categories of works are considered in the drought affected districts under MGNREGS in Karnataka and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)to(c):          The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) provides for at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult member volunteers to do unskilled manual work. However, an additional 50 days of wage employment is provided over and above 100 days in the notified drought affected areas or natural calamity areas in the country on recommendation of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare.  During current FY 2019-20 (as on 13.03.2020), on the recommendation of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, the additional wage employment of 50 days over and above 100 days has been provided to drought notified areas of 34 Blocks from  11 Districts of Karnataka State under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS.  (d):      No Sir, There are no additional categories of work for drought affected areas. However the permissible works under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS also includes drought proofing work.*****
