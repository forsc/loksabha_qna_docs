                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Funds Allocation to Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the financial allocation made by the Ministry over the last five financial years across the country;(b)	the details of the financial allocation for the same period to Odisha; and(c)	the details of fund released and spent, district wise?






ANSWER

The details of scheme-wise Budget allocation during the last five years are at annexure-I.(c)	Under Centrally Sponsored Scheme, funds are released to States.
