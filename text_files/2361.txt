                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2064






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Work Progress of NH-58




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the work of proposed National Highway No. 58 from Udaipur to Idar, Gujarat via Jhadol has been completed;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	the total funds allocated to be paid as compensation to persons whose land have been aquired for the construction of NH-58E up to Idar (Gujarat) via Udaipur - Jhadol and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the total amount paid so far as compensation along with the details thereof; and(e)	the amount remained to be paid as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	& (b) Works on Udaipur-Rajasthan/Gujarat Border via Jhadol section of NH-58E commenced in June 2018 with scheduled completion in December, 2019.  Work of Rajasthan/Gujarat Border to Idar section is at DPR stage.  (c)	to (e) An amount of Rs. 121.56 crore has been allocated as compensation for Udaipur to Rajasthan/Gujarat Border section, out of which Rs. 54.13 crore has been disbursed so far.****
