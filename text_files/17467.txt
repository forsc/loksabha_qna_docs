                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Oil Industry Safety Directorate




Bhola Singh













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has set up the Oil Industry Safety Directorate (OISD) whichhas been entrusted with the responsibility of formulating standards, overseeing itsimplementation through safety audits in petroleum industry to enhance safety levelsand reduce risk inherent with this industry and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether OISD also carries out pre-commissioning safety audits of Greenfieldprojects across the Oil & Gas Industry, if so, the details thereof;(c) whether OISD carries out periodic safety audits of all types of Oil & Gasinstallations to monitor their compliance with the OISD standards in the Country andif so, the details thereof;(d) the details of Standards of Oil Industry developed by OISD during the last threeyears; and(e) the other major achievements of OISD during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROELUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) OISD, a technical body under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas has beenestablished in January, 1986. It has expertise in the design of facilities, operations,maintenance, fire and safety aspects etc. OISD, over the years has developed technicalexpertise in overseeing safety in entire Oil & Gas Sector including onland& offshoreexploration. OISD conducts safety audits of oil and gas installations at regularintervals and audit reports along with recommendations are sent to respectivecompanies for implementations. OISD has statutory powers only for offshoreoperations as per the Petroleum and Natural Gas (Safety in Offshore Operations)Rules, 2008.(b) To ensure safe & productive capitalization, OISD carries out pre-commissioningsafety audits across the Oil & Gas Industry. These audits are conducted on the requestof industry for green-field developments. Details of the pre-commissioning safetyaudits carried out by OISD in last three years is at Annexure-I.(c) OISD carries out periodic safety audits to ensure safety measures are beingfollowed by the industry as per the laid down standards and to identify areas of noncomplianceand concern for taking corrective actions. These audits include ExternalSafety Audits & Surprise Safety Audits at periodic intervals to cover all the existinginstallations. Details of the External safety audits done by OISD in last three years isatAnnexure-II.(d) OISD develops Standards for the Oil & Gas Industry through a participativeprocess involving all the stakeholders by leveraging the experience of the committeemembers and also referring to international standards. These standards cover inbuiltdesign safety, asset integrity and best operating practices in the various facets of Oil &Gas Industry. OISD standards are reviewed periodically to ascertain needs ofdeveloping new standards, updating/amending existing standards to incorporate thelatest technological developments as well as current experiences on the ground.(e) Some of the other major achievements/significant activities of OISD during lastthree years are enhanced focus on compliance of long pending recommendations foroil and gas installations, timely external safety audits, knowledge disseminationinitiatives – Organizing Technical Seminar/ Conference/Workshops/Trainings tospread awareness about safety and acquisition of ISO 9001:2015 certification forOISD.
