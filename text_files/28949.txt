                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

406






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Sustainable Agriculture Policy




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that India has not signed for the Sustainable  Agriculture Policy Action agenda at COP26, if so, the details thereof;  (b) Whether the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) under the National Action Plan for Climate Change is in line with the  CoP26 Agenda;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the actions taken by the Government during 2020 and 2021 under the NMSA and the funds allocated therein? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):    India has not signed the  sustainable Agriculture Policy Action Agenda at COP26.(b) & (c):  National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) is one of the eight missions outlined under National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC). The mission aims to evolve and implement strategies to make Indian agriculture sustainable and more resilient to the changing climate. The NMSA & schemes of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare are generally in-line with CoP26 Agenda.(d):    Action taken by the Government during 2020 and 2021 under National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA):NMSA was approved for three major components i.e. Rainfed Area Development (RAD); On Farm Water Management (OFWM); and Soil Health Management (SHM). Subsequently, four new programmes were introduced under the ambit of NMSA namely Soil Health Card (SHC), Parampragat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), Mission Organic Value Chain Development in North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER) and Sub Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF). During 2015-16, Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) was operationalised wherein the OFWM component of NMSA was subsumed under Per Drop More Crop (PDMC) component of PMKSY. Additionally Restructured National Bamboo Mission (NBM) was also launched in April 2018 under ambit of NMSA. All these components were implemented during 2020 and 2021.The fund allocated under NMSA sub-missions are at Annexure-I
