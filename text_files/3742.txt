                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Loans to Logistic Companies




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that the logistic companies are facing difficulties in getting loans from banks even after a New Logistic Policy has been declared; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the corrective steps taken/ proposed to be taken by the Government to provide easy and quick loans to logistic companies?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(a) to (c): As per inputs received from the Department of Commerce, a National Logistics Policy is in the draft stage. The Department has further apprised that the Indian National Shipowners Association had represented seeking  setting up of a Maritime Development Fund to provide competitive and long-term financial assistance, supplement the present sources of finance available from banks, and design financing schemes appropriate for individual sub-sectors, and that the same have been discussed with  stakeholders.Banks lend to businesses, including logistics companies, as per their Board-approved loan policy. With regard to difficulties in getting loans from banks, Public Sector Banks have informed that no such instances have been observed. *****
