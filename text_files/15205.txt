                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3769






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Rural Household




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the definition of ‘rural household’ by the Ministry;(b) 	whether it is true that those localities not visited by census collectors are classified as rural households and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the number of rural households in the country, State-wise and the percentage of such households in terms of the State-wise and overall population of the country; and(d) 	whether the Government is considering a change in the definition of rural household, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): The Ministry of Rural Development followed the ''Census-2011'' definition of rural household for conducting Socio Economic and Caste Census(SECC), 2011 in rural areas.  A ''Household’ is defined under Census-2011” as a group of persons, who normally live together and take their meals from a common kitchen/common cooking unless the exigencies of work prevent any of them from doing so". SECC, 2011 for rural areas was conducted using the same Enumeration Blocks (EBs) as defined under Census-2011.  The State-wise number and percentage of rural households in the country, according to SECC, 2011 is given at Annexure-I. (d): As on date no change in the definition of rural household is under consideration in the Ministry.  ANNEXURE IANNEXURE REFERRED IN STATEMENT IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3769 ANSWERED ON 17.03.2020 REGARDING ‘RURAL HOUSEHOLD’
