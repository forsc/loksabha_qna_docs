                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2611






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Report of NCRB




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) has submitted its report for 2017;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of the action taken on the report of 2016?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) National Bureau of Crime Records (NCRB) publishes crime statistics in its report “Crime in India”. ‘Crime in India’ for the year 2017 has not been finalised by NCRB.(b) Does not arise in view of reply at (a) above.(c) With a view to ensure that published data suits the requirements of various stakeholders, NCRB has comprehensively revised the proforma for collecting crime statistics. NCRB has trained States/ Union Territories personnel on the new formats. The data in the revised proforma from all States/ Union Territories has not been received.(d)	The statistics in the ‘Crime in India’ reports, including the report for the year 2016, are used by Central and State Governments for framing various policies and initiatives and also by various other stakeholders for research purposes etc.*****
