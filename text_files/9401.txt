                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2834






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Financial Assistance for Safe Drinking Water




Balak Nath













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the criteria fixed by the Government for providing financial assistance for safe drinking water;(b) the details of the proposals pending in this regard till date, State-wise; (c) the details of projects implemented across the country for providing safe drinking water to all, State-wise; (d) whether sewage water treatment plants have been established in the country; (e) if so, the details thereof; and (f) the funds released and spent on safe drinking water projects in the country including all the districts of Rajasthan especially in Sikar during the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) The approved criteria for providing financial assistance for potable water under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) are as under:Criteria	Weightage(in percentage)Rural Population (as per last Census)	30%Rural SC and ST population (as per last Census)	10%States under DDP, DPAP, HADP and special category Hill States in terms of rural areas	30%Population (as per DDWS, IMIS) residing in habitations affected by chemical contaminants including heavy metals (as on 31st March of preceding financial year)	10%Weightage for balance individual household connections to be provided	20%(b) For the financial year 2019-20, Government of India has released the funds to the all eligible States/ UTs against the proposals received in this department so far.(c) Drinking Water being a State subject, it is States who plan, design, approve and implement drinking water supply schemes. As reported, the States/ UT-wise details of number of completed schemes from 2009-10 to 2019-20(as on 03.12.2019) under erstwhile National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) is at Annex-I.(d)&(e) Thedetails of sewage treatment plants in rural areas are not maintained by this Department.(f) As reported, the State-wise details of fund released and expenditure incurred during the last three years under erstwhile NRDWP is at Annex-II. Further, district-wise details of fund released and expenditure incurred in the last three years including Sikar, Rajasthan are at Annex-III.		***
