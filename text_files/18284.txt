                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1878






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Solar Charkha Clusters Project




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of the Solar Chakra Clusters Project implemented across the country; (b) 	the details of financial assistance provided by the Government to complete the project; (c) 	the details and the number of companies that are involved in the project; and (d) 	the roadmap and timeline for completion of the project?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OFMICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a):   	Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) has launched the Mission Solar Charkha(MSC) for implementation of 50 Solar Charkha Projects across the country. As on date, 10 clusters have been sanctioned by the Scheme Steering Committee of the Scheme.(b):	Rs.64.32 crore has been sanctioned to 10 Promoter Agencies(PAs) and an amount of Rs. 48.55 crores has been released to KVIC to implement 10 Solar Charkha Projects. (c):	The details and the number of companies involved in the project are as under:<pre>Sr. no.	Name of the cluster	State	District1.	Shyamsons Trading India Pvt. Ltd.	Uttar Pradesh	Lucknow2.	Nandi Khadi Solar Charkha Cluster	Karnataka	Chikkabalapur3.	TRIWE, Jagdalpur	Chhattisgarh	Bastar4.	Khadi GramodyogVikash Mandal, Budhanpur	Uttar Pradesh	Bijnor5.	Mukund Education Society	Maharashtra	Washim6.	Marwar Muslim Educational and Welfare Society	Rajasthan	Jodhpur7.	Vishwakarma Silk Khadi Weavers Samity	Odisha	Boudh8.	Gujarat State Co-op Cotton Federation Ltd.	Gujarat	Ahmedabad9.	WardhaKukkutpalanVaKrishiUdyogSanshodhan Co-operative Society Ltd.	Maharashtra	Wardha10.	Nadagouda Solar Charkha Cluster	Karnataka	Vijapur</pre>(d):	As per the Scheme Guidelines, the time frame for implementation of these projects is 2 years. The target is to cover 50 Solar Projects across the country.
