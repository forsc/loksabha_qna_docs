                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2704






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Entry and Exit Gate




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh













                                                    Dinesh Chandra Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) 	whether only one entry and one exit gate has been kept operational in Delhi metro rail/stations due to Covid and if so, the reasons therefor;(b) 	whether the physically challenged persons and old age people have to face a 	lot of difficulties in the absence of gates on both the sides of the roads whereas 	the road opens to only one entry point from both side gates despite that gates are not opened on both the sides; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                                                 THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                     MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):	 No, Sir. After removing of restrictions by Delhi Disaster Management Authority (DDMA) in March 2022, all gates of metro stations have been opened.(b) & (c): Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has informed that it has received feedbacks from some senior citizens and persons with disabilities during the last six months to open all gates. With the lifting of Covid related restrictions by DDMA, all the gates have now been opened.                                    *****
