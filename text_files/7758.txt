                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

139






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






One Country-One Voter List




Vinod Kumar Sonkar














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken/proposes to take any steps for preparing ‘one country-one voter list’ in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of the progress made so far in this regard; and(d)	the time by which such list is likely to be completed? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	to (d) : A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN PARTS  (a) TO (d)  OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION  No. 139  FOR REPLY ON 27TH NOVEMBER, 2019 (a)	to (d): The  Election Commission of India has informed that  electoral rolls are maintained Assembly Constituency-wise which are used for both State Assembly and Lok  Sabha Elections. For using the same electoral rolls for local body elections in the States, the State Governments have to amend their election laws in terms of clause (4) of article 243K and clause (2) of article 243ZA  of the Constitution.
