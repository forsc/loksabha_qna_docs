                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






CCTV in Stations at Bundelkhand




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether CCTVs have been installed at the crowded railway station areas and outside the premises in Manikpur, Banda, Chitrakoot and Mahoba station of Bundelkhand in Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, whether the Ministry proposes to install the same and the time by which these are likely to be installed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS &INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b) Closed Circuit Television Cameras (CCTVs) have been installed at Manikpur station (27 cameras), Banda station (40 cameras) and Mahoba station (27 cameras) of Bundelkhand region in Uttar Pradesh. CCTV cameras are provided in station premises and at Entry/Exit gate of stations.(c) CCTVs at Chitrakoot station are targeted for installation by October, 2022.*****
