                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1410






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Vacant Posts of Chairpersons in RCI, National Trust & CCPD




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that there are many key posts lying vacant in the Department of Person with Disabilities like Chairperson for Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI), Chairperson of National Trust and Chief Commissioner Person with Disabilities (CCPD);(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government is taking immediate steps to fill up such vacancies for speedy work and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) & (b) Yes, the status  of key posts lying vacant in the Department of Empowerment of Person with Disabilities (Divyangjan) is as under:-Name of Organization	Designation	No. of vacanciesRehabilitation Council of India	Chairperson	1National Trust	Chairperson	1Office of Chief Commissioner of Persons with Disabilities	Chief Commissioner 	1National Institutes	Director	6Total	9(c) The additional charge arrangement of the above posts has been made. The Government has taken necessary steps to fill up the above vacancies.******
