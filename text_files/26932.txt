                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2673






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






On demand tickets to passengers




Jayanta Kumar Roy













                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government had proposed to provide on demand rail tickets to passengers and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether getting a confirmed rail ticket on many routes is still difficult as reflected in number of waitlisted tickets;(c)	if so, the details of waitlisted tickets alongwith the amount during the last three years;(d)	whether a large number of tickets are cancelled due to various reasons including long advance booking period of 120 days and if so, the details of tickets cancelled along with the cancellation charge collected during the last three years;(e)	whether there is an urgent need for reforms in ticketing system of Railways like reducing the advance booking from 120 to 90 days, gradual release(monthly) of tickets, faster refund, capacity augmentation, etc.; and(f)	if so, the other steps being taken by the Government to provide on demand rail tickets to passengers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (f) : A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
