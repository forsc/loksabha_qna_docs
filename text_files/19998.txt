                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Panchayat Buildings




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Panchayat Buildings have been constructed in all the Gram Panchayats of the country; (b) 	if not, the number of Gram Panchayats where buildings have been constructed and the numbers of Gram Panchayats deprived from it, State/Union Territory-wise; (c) 	whether the Panchayat Buildings are likely to be constructed in the remaining Gram Panchayats; and (d) 	if so, the time by which it is likely to be started and completed along with the amount likely to be spent thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a)	No Sir.(b)	As per the information available, out of 264963 Gram Panchayats (GPs)/ Rural Local Bodies (RLBs) / Village (VCs), 66326 no. of GPs/RLBs / VCs are without Panchayat Bhawan. State/UT-wise details of no. of GPs/RLBs / VCs without Panchayat Bhawan is placed as Annexure.(c)&(d) Panchayat, being local government is part of the State list of 7th Schedule of the Constitution of India. Being a State subject it is the responsibility of the State. The States/ UTs are expected to mobilise funds for GP Bhawans from various sources. Ministry of Panchayati Raj (MoPR) supplements the efforts of the State for construction of Panchayat Bhawans through its schemes and programmes. Under the scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA), approx. 4740 GP Bhawan have been approved for construction. Under the Garib Kalyan Rojgar Abhyan (GKRA) construction of 1347 new GP Bhawans have been completed and 24808 Bhawans are under various stages of construction. In view of gaps in Panchayat Buildings, States/ UTs have been advised to take up the construction of GP building on priority in a phased manner by converging funds/resources under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee scheme (MGNREGS), FFC with other schemes of concerned States/UTs so as to saturate the gap by end of 2022.   *****
