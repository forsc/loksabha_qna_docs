                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2456






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Urea Import




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the total quantity of urea imported from other countries during the last three years, countrywise;(b)	 the action taken for rehabilitation of sick fertilizer units, State-wise; and(c) 	whether there is any proposal for expansion of existing urea manufacturing units and if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI  D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a):	A statement indicating total quantity of urea imported from other countries during the last three years is placed at Annexure “A”.(b):	“At present, Fertilizers and Chemicals Travancore Limited (FACT), Kerala and Madras Fertilizers Limited (MFL), Tamil Nadu are sick fertilizers Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs). In a high level meeting held recently, it has been decided that FACT and MFL should inter alia try to monetize their vacant lands, enhance operating efficiencies and rationalize their manpower to achieve the desired restructuring.  As a result, they have undertaken enhancement of their operating efficiencies through asset monetization and manpower rationalization.”(c):	No, no decision has been taken to increase the capacity of any existing urea unit.********
