                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Facilities to Licensed Vendors




Lallu Singh













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the facilities made available by the rail administration to the licenced vendors providing catering services on railway stations during the Covid pandemic;(b) whether the Government has received any demand letter from the ‘Akhil Bhartiya Railway Khanpan Licensees Welfare Association’ in this regard; and (c) if so, the details of the demands and the action being taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): The facilities made available to licensee vendors during COVID pandemic are as under:(i) The non-operational period of all catering and vending contracts to be treated as ‘dies non’ under Force Majeure clause and contract period to be extended accordingly.(ii) Relief in  license fee of all catering and vending stalls in view of  reduction in traffic volume has been considered and implemented.(iii) Reduction in the Performance Security from the existing 10% to 3% for all catering /vending contracts.  (b) and (c): Yes, Sir. The Akhil Bhartiya Railway Khanpan Licensees Welfare Association also demanded for relief to deal with Covid related hardship, which has been examined and implemented as outlined in item (a) above.*****
