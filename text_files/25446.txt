                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1341






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Implementation of Kuttanad Package




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Kerala Government has requested the Union Government to increase the financial assistance component under the Kuttanad package and if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; (b)	whether the Government is intending to implement a second Kuttanad package as a continuation of first Kuttanad package that was implemented in Kuttanad region for ensuring development of livelihood and agriculture and allied activities and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is aware of a proposal by the Kerala Government for second Kuttanad package that has been conceptualized for mitigating the crisis faced by the region;(d)	if so, the details thereof and (e)	whether the Kerala Government has submitted Detailed Project Report (DPR) for outer bunds of paddy polders under Kuttanad package and if so, the details thereof  including the funds sanctioned and released thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????D? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) &(b) :   The Government had granted ‘in-principle’ approval of Kuttanad Packagein July, 2008 for providing financial support amounting to Rs.1840.75 crore for implementing various programmes/interventionsby the concerned Ministries/Departments viz. Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation, etc. within their existing schemes.  The package was suggested by M. S. Swaminathan Research Foundation for development of Kuttanad Wetland Eco-System.  The Government of Kerala was required to submit proposals after formulation of detailed Project Reports (DPRs) to the concerned Ministries/ Departments as per their existing schemes. The programmes/interventions involving financial outlay upto Rs.50 lakhs were to be implemented by the State Government of Kerala from their own funds under their schemes.Implementation period of Kuttanad package has ended in July, 2012. The Union Government has not extended the period but had advised the State Government to implement the approved activities of Kuttanad package under various on-going schemes/programmes like RashtriyaKrishiVikasYojana (RKVY), National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) etc. (c) to (e):     Union Government has no information about the second Kuttanad Package. However, State Government of Kerala has submitted a list of 10 numbers of new projects in Kuttanad region for inclusion under Flood management and Border Areas Programme (FMBAP) of Ministry of Jal Shakti during the period 2021-26 in the Kuttanad region. The details of the projects is at Annexure I. State Government has informed that the Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) of these projects are still under preparation stage. As per provisions of the ongoing "Flood Management and Border Areas Programme (FMBAP) of Ministry of Jal Shakti, it is not be possible to include any new projects of flood management for funding under this Programme at this stage. The process of formulation and approval of the centrally sponsored scheme, Flood Management and Border Areas Programme (FMBAP) of Ministry of Jal Shakti for the period 2021-2026 is presently underway. Inclusion of new projects for funding will depend upon the contours of new scheme as and when approved by the Government of lndia.
