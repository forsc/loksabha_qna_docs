                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3668






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Jan Aushadhi Sugam App




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of users who have been connected with Janaushadhi Sugam App in the country till date and the details of benefits provided to them, State-wise; and (b) whether the Government is planning to update app with technological advancements to compete with other apps and if so, the details therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)  As on 04.08.2021, approximately 11.74 lakh users are connected with the Jan Aushadhi Sugam App.  The users of the App are able to find details about name and price of medicines, comparison of prices between generic drug and their equivalent branded drug, location of nearby Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendra (PMBJK), quality assurance and transaction details, etc.   The State/UT- wise data of users of the App is attached as Annexure. (b) Jan Aushadhi Sugam App is regularly updated with new technological advancements to make it more user-friendly.
