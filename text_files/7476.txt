                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

102






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Shell Companies




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of shell companies against which the Central Bureau of Investigation has registered cases during 2015-2018 along with the details of the number of cases registered; (b) the details of irregularities detected during the investigation; (c) the outcome of the action taken against the directors/operators of the said shell companies; and (d) the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS			(Ms. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)????? ??? ????????? ????? ?????? 			(??????? ??????? ???????) (a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.****** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE STARRED QUESTION NO. 102 FOR ANSWER IN LOK SABHAON 25.11 .2019.(a)	to (d):Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) has registered 30 cases against 104 shell companies during the last 4 years i.e. 2015, 2016, 2017 and 2018. Further, during investigation of 108 cases, 894 Shell Companies have been detected wherein irregularities such as falsification of financial statement, bank fraud, diversion of funds, impersonation, cheating, abuse of official position, criminal conspiracy, criminal misconduct etc. were found. The summary of cases are enclosed at Annexure “A” & “B” respectively.
