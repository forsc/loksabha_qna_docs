                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4058






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Reduction in Net Borrowing Ceiling




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Department of Expenditure under the Ministry has taken into consideration the under-borrowing of Rs. 26,830.10 crore of Andhra Pradesh State in the earlier years (2005-14) before reducing the Net Borrowing Ceiling (NBC) for the State for the current fiscal;b)	if so, the reasons for reduction in the NBC for Andhra Pradesh; andc)	if not, the reasons for not giving any weightage to the under-borrowings of the State, which far exceed the alleged overborrowing?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY) (a) to (c) No Sir, the flexibility of rolling over of under-utilized borrowing limit was introduced by the 14th Finance commission for its award period (2015-16 to 2019-20). However, such flexibility was not available during the period prior to the 14th Finance Commission.                                            ********
