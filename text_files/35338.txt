                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2405






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Hydrogen Fuel Cell Based Trains




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is formulating any scheme to run hydrogen fuel cell based train on its network across the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c)	the estimated cost likely to be sanctioned thereunder;(d)	the details including the number of States where such trains are likely to be introduced; and(e)	the targets fixed in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	to (c) As a Pilot project, Tender for Hydrogen Fuel cell based retrofitment for Conversion of 02 nos 1600 Horse Power Driving Power Cars (DPCs) into Hydrogen Fueled Power Car at an Estimated cost of ?84.2 Cr, was floated on 15.12.2021 and has been opened on 31.01.2022.(d) Presently the Pilot Project is for Sonipat-Jind section of Northern Railways catering to the State of Haryana.(e) As of now, only the Pilot Project is sanctioned.*****
