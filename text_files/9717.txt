                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3089






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Increase in MBBS Seats




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has received any proposal/request from the State Government of Chhattisgarh for increasing MBBS seats while upgrading its medical colleges and also for setting up of super speciality hospital by upgrading the hospitals associated with medical colleges at Jagdalpur-Bastar;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the decision taken by the Government on both the proposals?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Health and Family Welfare  is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme(CSS) for up-gradation of existing State Government/Central Government medical colleges to increase MBBS seats in the Country. The State/UT Governments have been requested to send their proposals for consideration in the Ministry. However, so far, no proposal has been received from the State of Chhattisgarh under this Scheme. In respect of super-speciality hospital by upgrading the hospitals associated with medical colleges at Jagdalpur-Bastar, it is informed that  Government Medical College, Jagdalpur (Chattisgarh) has been taken up for upgradation under Phase-lV of PMSSY at a total cost of Rs 200 Cr (Centre Share: Rs 120 Cr; State Share: Rs 80 Cr).
