                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4489






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Assistance to Jharkhand for Afforestation




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has launched any scheme of providing financial and technical assistance to the people of Jharkhand for afforestation and provides any other kind of assistance in this regard;(b)	if so,  the details thereof, scheme-wise;(c)	the value of financial assistance provided in Jharkhand during the last three years; and(d)	the number of trees planted through the said assistance during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) & (b)The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change is implementing National Afforestation Programme and National Mission for a Green India with people’s participation. As per information received from Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, a Sub-Mission on Agroforestry under National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture is being implemented to promote tree planting on farmlands. A restructured National Bamboo Mission (NBM) has also been launched by the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare. The objective of the National Bamboo Mission includes increasing the area under bamboo plantation on non-forest government land and private lands to supplement farm income as well as availability of quality raw material for industries.(c) & (d) As per the information received from Government of Jharkhand, the value of financial assistance provided to Jharkhand and the number of trees planted in Jharkhand during last three years is given in Annexure.*****
