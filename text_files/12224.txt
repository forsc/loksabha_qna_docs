                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1039






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Increase in Plantation




Ravneet Singh













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the Forest Advisory Committee (FAC) has recommended for private players to be permitted to raise plantations to be later used for Compensatory Afforestation (CA); (b)	if so, the details thereof and whether the Government has accepted or likely to accept this recommendation of FAC; and (c)	the details of the ownership over the land and source of land in case the FAC recommendation towards involvement of private players in raising plantations is accepted by the Government?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c) The Forest Advisory Committee (FAC), a statutory body under the provisions of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 in its meeting held on 19.12.2019 has recommended to Government for considering plantations already raised in non-forest lands in lieu of stipulated compensatory afforestation (CA) subject to certain conditions. It has also been recommended that, such land as provided by the user agency shall be notified as protected forest/reserved forest for bringing it under the scientific forest management.	***
