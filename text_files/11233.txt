                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






NISHTHA Programme




L.S. Tejasvi Surya













                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has introduced National Initiative for School Heads and TeachersHolistic Advancements (NISHTHA) for providing training to 42 lakh teachers across thecountry and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has also included the teachers working in schools under StateBoards in NISHTHA and if so, the details thereof and the actual financial allocation proposedby the Government for this purpose;(c) whether NISHTHA covers the entire teaching workforce at the elementary level and if not,the basis on which 42 lakh teachers were chosen for the programme;(d) the criteria for identification of the key resource persons;(e) whether translations of the modules available online into vernacular languages is beingdone and if so, the timeline for completion of the same along with the methods proposed forpromotion of experimental and joyful learning; and(f) whether there is a timeline for SRG training in each State and Union Territory and if so,the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) to (f): Yes, Sir. The Department of School Education and Literacy has launched aNational Mission to improve learning outcomes at the Elementary level through an IntegratedTeacher Training Programme called NISHTHA – National Initiative for School Heads’ andTeachers’ Holistic Advancement under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Samagra Shikshain August 2019. This integrated programme aims to build the capacities of around 42 lakhparticipants covering all teachers and Heads of Schools at the elementary level in allGovernment schools, faculty members of State Councils of Educational Research andTraining (SCERTs), District Institutes of Education and Training (DIETs) as well as officialsand Resource Persons from Block Resource Centres (BRCs) and Cluster Resource Centres(CRCs) in all States and UTs. The total estimated cost of NISHTHA training is around Rs.1118.70 Crores. The Key Resource Persons are identified by the respective State and UnionTerritory Government. The English and Hindi version of all the modules are available on thewebsite of NCERT (nishtha.ncert.gov.in). The modules of NISHTHA are activity basedincluding educational games and quizzes, socio-emotional learning, motivational interaction,team building, thus focus on experiential and joyful learning with the aim to empowerteachers to work with creativity. All the States and UTs have been requested for translation ofall the modules in regional languages as also to implement the NISHTHA trainingprogramme in a time bound manner.****
