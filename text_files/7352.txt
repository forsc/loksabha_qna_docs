                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1122






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Ayurvedic Medical Colleges




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) whether there exists any special reservation scheme for admission in Ayurvedic Medical Colleges in the country, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether admission in Ayurvedic Medical Colleges is provided on basis of the NEET score in the ratio of 70 per cent and 30 per cent, itself in Maharashtra including Vidarbha and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the number of Ayurvedic Medical Colleges in Vidarbha and in rest of Maharashtra is higher than in Marathwada region creating difficulties for the students of that area; and (d) if so, the steps likely to be taken by the Government including addressing the issue of reservation ratio of 70 per cent and 30 per cent to help the students of Marathwada region to get admission in Ayurvedic Medical Colleges?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):    Yes, there exists two types of quota seats for admission in Ayurveda Medical Colleges, including all India Quota and State/ UT Quota.Under All India Quota, the reservations are applicable for SC/ST/OBC/PwD and EWS in Central Universities and National Institutes.  In respect of State Govt. and State Govt. aided colleges reservation is there for SC/ST/PwD.Under State Quota seats, the reservations are as per the norms of the concerned State Governments.-2-(b):     NEET Percentile required for eligibility in admission to Ayurveda UG course is 50th Percentile for Unreserved category candidates and 40th percentile for reserved candidates including SC/ST/OBC. In respect of candidate with benchmark disabilities specified under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the minimum marks required is 45th Percentile for General Category and 40th Percentile for SC/ST/OBC.(c) & (d):    Ministry processes the applications that are received from the respective applicants from all over the country and the Ministry has no role in number of applications being received from any particular Region or State.
