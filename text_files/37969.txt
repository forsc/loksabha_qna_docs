                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Smart Classes in North East Schools




Agatha K. Sangma














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has conducted any audits on the coverage, status and functionality of Smart Classes in rural India; (b)if so, the details when such an audit was last conducted and whether the report of the same can be accessed and if so, the details thereof; and (c)whether the Government has made any special provision in budgetary allocations, approvals, and utilizations for Smart Classes under various schemes, for North Eastern Region and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SHRIMATI ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (c):Education is in the concurrent list of the Constitution and majority of the schools are under the domain of respective State and UT Governments.Department of School Education and Literacy, Ministry of Education prepared “The India Report on Digital Education” which shows the digital and remote learning initiatives through the use of digital technology across the country. These digital initiatives of the DoSE&L and the State and UT Governments have helped approximately 25 Crore students in learning. Link for accessing the report is given below:The India Report on Digital Education 2020: https://www.education.gov.in/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/India_Report_Digital_Education_0.pdfThe India Report on Digital Education 2021: https://www.education.gov.in/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/irde_21.pdfThe ICT Labs/Smart Class Rooms are approved in schools under the centrally sponsored scheme of Samagra Shiksha to all the States/UTs including North Eastern States. State/UT wise, details of financial allocation/ approval & utilisation, year-wise for years 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 (till 31.01.2022) under the scheme are at Annexure.*****
