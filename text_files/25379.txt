                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1224






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Allocation for Farm Plan




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that the Union Government has spent only 0.5 percent of the earmarked Rs.2,000 crore for the year 2018-19 for farm plan; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the reasons for not spending the earmarked amount; and(d) the total amount earmarked, allotted and spent by the Union Government for the farm plan during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) No sir. No scheme by the name of ‘farm plan’ is operated by the Department in the last 3 years.(b) to (d) Not applicable. *****
