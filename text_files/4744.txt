                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Export of Textiles




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat













                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the export of textiles and apparel has been falling every month for the last three years despite the Government’s efforts to give the segment a boost, if so, the extent to which it has declined alongwith the reasons therefor;(b)	whether data compiled by the Confederation of Indian Textile Industry (CITI) showed sharp decline in the export of textile and apparel since 2017 as compared to 2013-14,2014-15 and 2015-16, year-wise, if so, the response of the Union Government in this regard; and(c)	whether the Government is aware that the industry needs immediate relief in the form of a minimum two per cent on the Merchandise Export from India Scheme on cotton yarn and a RoSL package for fabric and cotton yarn to retain competitiveness in the global market and if so, the steps taken by the Union Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c) :	A statement is laid on the Table of House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No.*399 for 19.07.2019 REGARDING EXPORT OF TEXTILES ASKED BY SHRI GIRISH BHALCHANDRA BAPAT, DR. SHRIKANT EKNATH SHINDE(a):	As per DGCIS data, export of textile and apparel has been increasing by around 2% CAGR in last three years i.e. from 2016-17 to 2018-19.(b):	As per the data of Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence and Statistics, export of textile and apparel has increased by 2.3% year on year from US$ 35.7 bn in 2017-18 to US$ 36.6 bn in 2018-19. Export of textile and apparel year-wise in 2013-14, 2014-15 and 2015-16 is as follows: Year	Textile and Apparel Exports (US$ mn)	Year on Year Growth2013-14	36,717	2014-15	36,805	0.2%2015-16	35,692	-3%(c):	The export of cotton yarn and fabric has been growing year on year in last three years i.e. 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19. Export of cotton yarn and fabric during last three years is as follows:Year	Cotton yarn	Year on Year Growth	Cotton fabric	Year on Year Growth2016-17	3,353		1,820	2017-18	3,443	3%	1,920	5%2018-19	3,910	14%	2,146	12%Therefore, support for cotton yarn and fabric under MEIS and RoSCTL is not required at present.*****
