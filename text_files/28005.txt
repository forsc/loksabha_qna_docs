                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






PMGKAY in Maharastra




Navneet Ravi Rana














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware about negligence, arbitrariness and corruption in the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) launched in providing free ration to each and every family during the corona pandemic in Maharashtra including Parliamentary Constituency of Amravati where needy poor families are not getting ration and are running from pillar to post; (b) 	if so, whether the Government has taken any steps in this regard; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):   		State Government of Maharashtra have informed that no such cases were reported in Maharashtra including in Parliamentary Constituency of Amravati.(b) : 		Question does not arise.(c) :  		Question does not arise.****
