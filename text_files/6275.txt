                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

82






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Development of Tourism in Kartarpur Sahib Corridor




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has constructed adequate infrastructure to facilitate tourism in Kartarpur Sahib Corridor; (b) 	the details of the number of people the Government expects will visit the corridor; (c) 	whether the Government has taken adequate security measures keeping in view high-profile visits to the corridor; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Ministry of Tourism under Swadesh Darshan Scheme provides financial assistance to State Governments/UT Administrations/ Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country. The projects under the scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.This Ministry has sanctioned a project for Rs.99.95 crore during 2018-19 for Development of Anandpur Sahib – Fatehgarh Sahib – Chamkaur Sahib – Ferozpur – Amritsar – Khatkar Kalan – Kalanour - Patiala in Punjab under Heritage Circuit Theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme. The site Kartarpur Sahib Corridor is not included in this project.(b) to (d):	Does not arise.******
