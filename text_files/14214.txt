                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2767






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Authority for Cyber Security




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology (a)	whether the Government has any plan to set up any authority for cyber security in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken / being taken by the Government in this regard; and  (c)       the composition of the said authority along with the criteria laid down by the Government for the selection in the said authority.






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):  Under the provisions of section 70A and section 70B of the Information Technology (IT) Act, 2000, Government has established National Critical Information Infrastructure Protection Centre (NCIIPC) for protection of critical information infrastructure in the country, and the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In) for incident response. These two agencies  perform as per the rules notified under section 70A and 70B namely “Information Technology (National critical Information Infrastructure Protection centre and manner of performing function and duties) Rules, 2013” and “Information Technology (The Indian Computer emergency response team and manner of performing function and duties ) Rules,2013” respectively. *******
