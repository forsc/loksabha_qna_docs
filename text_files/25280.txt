                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Tourism Projects for Maharashtra




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the projects for development of tourism approved, sanctioned and executed in Maharashtra during the last two years including the current year; (b)	the details of the Tourism Projects completed so far and the time by which the remaining projects are likely to be completed; (c) 	the reasons for the delay in completion of the pending projects; (d) 	the details of funds allocated, released and utilised for the development of tourism sector in the State during the said period, year-wise; and (e) 	whether the State Government of Maharashtra has submitted any proposal to the Government and if so, the details thereof along with the present status of ongoing projects in the State of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	Development and promotion of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government / Union Territory Administration. However, the Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/ Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. The projects are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc.  The projects sanctioned in Maharashtra are at various stages of implementation/ completion. The  Ministry of Tourism has  informed  all the  State Governments  including Maharashtra  that  the  proposals  submitted  by  them  for  consideration under  Swadesh Darshan  scheme  are  deemed  to  be  returned since the  scheme is  being reviewed. The details of the projects sanctioned, amount released and amount utilized till date under ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘PRASHAD’ schemes to Maharashtra are annexed.******
