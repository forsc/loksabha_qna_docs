                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1849






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Hospitals in Areas of Contaminated Groundwater




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to establish hospitals in the areas where people are affected by arsenic and fluoride contamination in groundwater particularly in Assam;(b) If so, details thereof; and(c) If not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	To Manage the problem of Fluorosis due to contamination of drinking water with Fluoride, a National Programme for Prevention and Control of Fluorosis(NPPCF) is presently being implemented in 157 districts of 19 States/UTs including Assam. The Diagnosis and treatment of diseases caused due to high levels of Arsenic and Fluoride in ground water is done in general health services and in medical colleges. There is no proposal to establish hospitals only for this purpose in these areas.
