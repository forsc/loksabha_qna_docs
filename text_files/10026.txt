                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

310






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Villages Notified as Urban Towns




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of villages which qualified and were notified as urban towns during the financial year 2018-19;(b)	whether there has been an increase or decrease in the total number as compared to the previous financial year; and(c)	if so, the details thereof ?       






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the table of the House. Statement referred to in the reply to part (a) to (c) of the Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 310 for answer on 10.12.2019(a) to (c):	As per the information provided by the Ministry of Panchayati Raj, based on the data uploaded by the State Panchayat Raj Departments in the website specifically maintained by National Informatics Centre for Local Government Directory, the number of villages which qualified and were notified as urban towns during the financial year 2017-18 and 2018-19 are 1304 and 1524 respectively which represents an increase of 220 villages notified as urban towns during 2018-19 over 2017-18.
