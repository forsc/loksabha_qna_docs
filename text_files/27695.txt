                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Petrol Pumps by New Entities




Margani Bharat













                                                    Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil
                                                








                                                    Brijendra Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has issued Gazette Notification dated 08.11.2019,S.No.355, for setting up of petrol pumps by new private entities in the field ofmarketing of transportation fuels and if so, the details of private parties to whichthe authorisation has been granted in respect of the above notification along withtheir regions of sale;(b) the details of source of supply for newly authorised private entities and themanner in which Ministry has planned to supply and the price at which the sameis to be done;(c) whether PSU oil companies have given consent to new private entities forsupply of gasoline and diesel at their cost price and if not, the reasons therefor;and(d) the quantum of change in country-wide auto fuel supply as a result of newentrants?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURALGAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)to(c) : Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MoPNG) vide Resolution dated08.11.2019 has revised the guidelines for authorization to markettransportation fuels which have been published in the Gazette ofIndia. The revised guidelines would promote ease of doing businessand boost private players to invest in retail sector.MoPNG has granted marketing authorisation to the following entitiesunder the said resolution :-(i) M/s RIL(ii) M/s. IMC Limited(iii) Onsite Energy Pvt. Ltd.(iv) Assam Gas Company Ltd.(v) M/s M K Agrotech Private Limited(vi) M/s RBML Solutions India Ltd. (RSIL)(vii) M/s Manas Agro Industries and Infrastructure Limited.Further, the entities while applying for marketing authorization haveto, inter alia, provide details about their marketing plan includingdetails of source of supply. Such entities are free to source theirsupplies from different sources including PSU OMCs in their bestcommercial wisdom.(d) At present, it may not be possible to gauge the quantum of change incountry-wide auto fuel supply as the new entities have been granted marketingauthorization fairly recently.****
