UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3142






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Amrut Mahotsava




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps being taken for Amrut Mahotsav of 2022 on the 75th year of our Independence so far and to resonate with the vision of Atma Nirbharta ;(b)	the funds sanctioned/spent so far along with future action plan worked out in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	& (b) A National Implementation Committee under the chairmanship of Hon’ble Home Minister has been constituted (Annexure-I) for planning activities under the commemoration of 75th Anniversary of India’s Independence (Amrit Mahotsava). So far, two meetings of NIC have been held, on 25th November 2020 and 26th February 2021. A Committee of Secretaries has also been set up for this commemoration (Annexure-II), which has met five times so far-on 20.08.2020, 30.10.2020, 07.11.2020, 16.12.2020 and 28.01.2021.Now Government has constituted a National Committee headed by Hon’ble PM consisting of 259 members vide Gazette Notification issued on 5th March, 2021 (Annexure-III). First meeting of NC was held on 8th March,2021 through virtual mode where Hon’ble PM interacted with members of NC to discuss modalities for celebration of 75th Anniversary of India’s Independence under the commemoration. A number of activities have been identified for celebrating the event in a 	befitting manner at national and international level. It has also been decided that activities will begin 75 weeks prior to 15th August 2022.   Accordingly, 	l2th March 2021 has been chosen as the date of launch of these celebrations. On the day, a freedom march from Sabarmati Ashram will be flagged off by Hon’ble Prime Minister which will culminate at Dandi in Navsari district in Gujarat. State Govts/UT Govts have also been requested to form State/District level committees for guiding the Amrit Mahotsav commemoration on themes such as Atma Nirbharta.No specific allocation of funds has been made in the Union budget. All the 	Ministries of Government of India will work out their action plan within their regular budget allocation.
