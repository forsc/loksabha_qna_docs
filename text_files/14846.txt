                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

302






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Religious Sites in Uttarakhand




Ajay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the religious sites in Uttarakhand have been linked to any spiritual circuit and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	if not, the time by which various religious sites in Uttarakhand are likely to be linked to the religious circuits; and (c) 	the other steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Union Government to promote religious tourism in Uttarakhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c): 	A statement is laid on the table of the House.********* STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.†*302 ANSWERED ON 16.03.2020 REGARDING RELIGIOUS SITES IN UTTARAKHAND.(a) and (b):		Ministry of Tourism under its schemes viz. “Integrated development of Theme Based Tourist Circuits- Swadesh Darshan” and “National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive” (PRASHAD) provides financial Assistance to the State Governments/UT Administrations for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country including Uttarakhand. Submission of project proposals by the State Governments/UT Administrations under the above schemes is a continuous process. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Based on the above criteria, Ministry has sanctioned following projects in Uttarakhand covering the religious sites:<pre>S.No.	Scheme	Circuit/Year	Name of Project	Amount sanctioned(Rs. in crore)01	Swadesh Darshan	Heritage/2016-17	Development of Kumaon Region – Katarmal –Jogeshwar -Baijnath-Devidhura	79.1302	PRASHAD	2015-16	Integrated Development of Kedarnath	34.7803	PRASHAD	2018-19	Development of Badrinath Ji Dham	39.24</pre>(c):	Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination under the Incredible India brand-line. As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry annually releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country, including religious sites of Uttarakhand.  In addition, the Indiatourism Offices in India and overseas disseminate information and undertake various promotional activities with the objective of showcasing the varied tourist destinations and products of the country.*********
