                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3181






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Foreign Investment in Renewable Energy Sector




P. Ravindhranath














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any scheme to invite foreign investment for development of renewable energy sector including solar power generation industry; (b)  if so, the details thereof; and(c) the total investment received by this sector during the last five years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) and (b) Government have allowed 100% Foreign Direct Investment in Renewable energy sector including solar power industry through automatic route. (c) The Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade has reported FDI equity inflow in renewable energy sector in the last five years as under:Year	FDI (In US$ Million)2014-15	615.952015-16	776.512016-17	783.572017-18	1204.462018-19	1446.16Total	4826.65*****
