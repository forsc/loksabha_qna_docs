                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Review of Performance of Defence PSUs




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the details of the Defence Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) engaged in shipbuilding in the country;(b) whether the Government has recently reviewed the performance of such PSUs;(c) if so, the details and the outcome thereof;(d) whether these PSUs are also exporting their products to various countries;(e) if so, the details thereof along with the revenue generated through exports during the last three years, country-wise; and(f) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to improve the performance of such PSUs?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (f):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*******
