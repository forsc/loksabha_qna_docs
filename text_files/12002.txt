                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

89






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Establishment of Medical Colleges




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the details of the proposals received from the States/UTs under Phase-III of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for the ‘Establishment of New Medical Colleges attached with existing District/Referral hospitals’ in aspirational districts and underserved districts, State/ UT-wise; (b) the details of the medical colleges established under Phase-II and Phase-I including funds allocated and utilized, State/UT-wise; (c) whether any new Government Medical College/ Institution (GMCI) or upgradation of existing GMCI is planned in Wayanad district/constituency; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (d):  A statement is laid on the Table of the HouseSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 89 FOR 7TH FEBRUARY, 2020(a)  The Cabinet has approved the setting up of 75 new Government Medical Colleges under the Phase-III of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for “Establishment of new medical colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals”. The Central Government has requested all State/UT Governments to send their proposals along with Detailed Project Report (DPR). As on 4.2.2020, 89 DPRs have been received including 26 in Aspirational Districts. 57 DPRs have been approved as on date. The details are at Annexure-I. (b) Under Phase-I of the Scheme, 58 districts in 20 States/UT have been identified and approved for establishment of medical colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals. Total cost of establishment of one Medical College is Rs.189 crores to be shared by Central Government and States in the ratio of 60:40 (90:10 for NE/Special category States). Funds to the tune of Rs.7507.70 crore have been released to the State/UT Governments for the approved medical colleges under the Scheme. As on date, 42 medical colleges have become functional. Details are at Annexure-II.Under Phase-II of the Scheme, 24 new medical colleges in 8 States have been identified. The total cost of establishment of one Medical College under this Phase is Rs.250 crore to be shared by Central Government and States in the ratio of 60:40 (90:10 for NE/Special category States). The State Governments have identified the locations on Challenge mode. Out of 24 medical colleges, 22 have been approved till date and funds to the tune of Rs.2254.59 crore have been released to the State Governments. Details are at Annexure-III.(c & d) Under Phase-III of the Scheme, so far no proposal has been received for establishment of new medical college at Wayanad district from the State Government of Kerala.
