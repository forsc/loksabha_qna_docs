                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1652






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Vocational Training Institutions in Jharkhand




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of vocational courses provided as electives to students in Governmentinstitutions in Jharkhand;(b) the steps taken by the Government to promote vocational education in Jharkhand; and(c) the details of year-wise number of students enrolled in vocational courses in Governmentvocational training institutions in Jharkhand since 2014, district-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): As per the information received from All India Council for Technical Education(AICTE), AICTE does not provide any special Vocational Course as elective for the studentsstudying in Government institutions of Jharkhand.However, AICTE in the year 2018, introducedthe vocational Degree/ Diploma programmes leading to Degree or Diploma in VocationalEducation under National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) for AICTE approvedinstitutions.In Jharkhand, 7 institution were approved to run Vocational Courses in the year2018-19 in Automotive Manufacturing, Graphics & Media, Software Developer, Refrigeration &Air Conditioning, Industrial Tools and Electronics Manufactures, the details of which are atAnnexure-I. In order to create an industry ready workforce and increase employability ofstudents, UGC has facilitated institutions to provide skill based programmes under NationalSkills Qualification Framework (NSQF). UGC has so far implemented three skill based schemes,namely Community Colleges, B.Voc Degree Programme and DDU KAUSHAL Kendra.Currently there are 188 Community Colleges, 289 institutions offering B.Voc DegreeProgrammes and 68 DDU KAUSHAL Kendras. As on date, 4 institutions from the state ofJharkhand are offering skill based programmes with the approval from UGC, the details of whichare at Annexure –II. As per the information received from Ministry of Skill Development andEntrepreneurship, under PradhanMantriKaushalVikasYojna (PMKVY 2016-20), training isbeing imparted in 371 National Skills Qualification Framework aligned job roles of level 3 and 4including technical courses across 37 sectors throughout the country.The list of district wisenumber of candidates trained/oriented under PMKVY (2016-20) in State of Jharkhand is atannexure-III.*****
