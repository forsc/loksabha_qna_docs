                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1681






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Status of Development Works




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether several development works and the schemes being implemented by this Ministry have been affected are lying pending during the COVID-19 pandemic period and if so, the details thereof?(b)	whether the Government has made any assessment in this regard as the pandemic period is now coming to end and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the roadmap prepared by the Government to speed up the said works and schemes; 	and(d) 	the details of the development works and the schemes being implemented particularly 	in Chhattisgarh which have been affected during the said period along with the roadmap prepared by the Government to complete the said works and schemes at the 	earliest?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE ) IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	to (d) Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs(MoHUA) facilitates and assists States/Union Territories (UTs) through its flagship Missions/Schemes - Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), Smart Cities Mission (SCM), Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U), Affordable Rental Housing Complexes (ARHCs) Scheme, Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), Prime Minister Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PMSVANidhi) Scheme and through Urban Transport programmes in development of urban areas. The Schemes are implemented by the States/UTs in accordance with the Mission guidelines.  Delhi Development Authority, Central Public Works Department and National Building Construction Corporation (NBCC) also take up developmental works. The Covid-19 pandemic is a global challenge and has affected the implementation of developmental works under the Schemes in the initial stages of pandemic. However, the pace of works picked up with necessary safety precautions and following the Covid-19 protocol. In spite of the pandemic the works have progressed well and efforts are being made to ensure their timely completion. The progress of the Missions/works including those in Chhattisgarh are given in Annexure.                                                *****
