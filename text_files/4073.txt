                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3754






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Delay in Installation of CCTNS




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been a delay in the execution of the Crime and Criminal Tracking Network and System (CCTNS) Project;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) the number of police stations where CCTNS have been installed;(d) whether CCTNS Project is being integrated with Inter-operable Criminal Justice System and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the likely benefits of the said integration of systems?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	Against a target of covering 14306 police stations, a total of 14874 police stations (excluding police stations in Bihar) have been covered under the Crime and Criminal Tracking Network and Systems (CCTNS), which includes new police stations. A total of 20.10 crore records of crime/criminals are available at national level.(d) and (e):	Yes, Sir. The objective of integration of CCTNS with Inter-operable Criminal Justice System is to facilitate use of information on crime and criminals in CCTNS with other databases on prisons, prosecution, forensics and courts in a holistic manner for administration of criminal justice.*****
