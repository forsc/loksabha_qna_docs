                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1964






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Common Application Software




Karti P. Chidambaram













                                                    Dushyant Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the implementation status of Common Application Software across the country;(b) 	the parameters being captured in the data collection process currently;(c) 	whether there is any integration of data from the AWD level to the school level, if so, the States that have a system where the two databases are linked with each other at the backend; and(d) 	if not, whether there is any plan for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT( SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI )(a)	As on 28.11.2019, the ICDS Common Application Software (ICDS-CAS)  has been rolled out across 5,51,270 Anganwadi Centres (AWCs)  across 324 districts in 27 States/UTs. The details are at Annexure-I.(b	ICDS-Common Application Software has been developed under POSHAN Abhiyaan which inter-alia captures data on weight and height of children, opening of AnganwadiCentres, distribution of Take Home Ration and  attendance of children for pre-school education at Anganwadi. (c) & (d) The Applicationcaptures the Pre-School Education attendance provided to the Children (3-6 years) at therespective AnganwadiCentres. There is no plan for integration of data from the AWCs level to the school level, presently.******Annexure-IAnnexure referred to  in reply to Part (a) of Lok Sabha Un-Starred Question No. 1964 for answer on 29.11.2019Sl.
