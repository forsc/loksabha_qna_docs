                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

779






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Status of Projects under Smart City Mission




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the detailed status of projects under Smart City Mission (SCM) in Rajkot, project-wise;(b)  the funds allocated and utilised till date, project-wise;(c) whether the Government has met its agenda for the projects or faced any bottlenecks in process of implementation and if so, the details thereof;(d)  the timeline of implementation of the projects, State-wise; and (e) whether more cities are being considered under SCM in Gujarat and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015. 100 Smart cities have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018.  The city of Rajkot was selected for development as a Smart City in Round-3 in June, 2017. The status of Smart City projects of Rajkot is given below: -(Amount in ? crore)DPR Stage	Tender Stage	Work Order Stage	Work Completed	Total Projects	Total AmountNo. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount		19	366.39	7	1,378.60	50	1,734.97	18	271.74	94	3,751.70(Source: Geo-Spatial Management Information System)(b): As on 12 November, 2021, Government of India has released ? 294 crore for Rajkot Smart City, out of which the city has utilised ? 244 crore (83%).(c): As on 12 November 2021, the 100 Smart Cities have tendered out 6,452 projects worth ? 1,84,998 crore; out of which work orders have been issued in 5,809 projects worth ? 1,56,571 crore; out of these, 3,131 projects worth ? 53,175 crore have been completed. In Rajkot Smart City, out of 94 projects worth ? 3751.70 crore, 68 projects (72%) worth ? 2,006.71 crore (53%) have either been completed or are at various stages of implementation. (d): The period of implementation of SCM has been extended upto June 2023 and all Smart Cities, including Rajkot, are expected to complete their projects within the stipulated time. (e): Presently, there is no such proposal under consideration of the Government.                              *****
