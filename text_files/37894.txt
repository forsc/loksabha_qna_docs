                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4715






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Unemployment Due to Covid Pandemic




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of people who became jobless due to the Covid Pandemic in the country so far, State-wise and district-wise details thereof especially in Bijnor, Meerut and Muzaffarnagar districts of Uttar Pradesh;(b) 	whether the Government has formulated any concrete and effective policy to provide relief from unemployment in the MSME sector; and(c)	 if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA)(a):	As MSMEs are present in both formal and informal sector; data regarding temporary or permanent loss of jobs in MSME sector are not maintained by the Government of India in Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME). However, the number of persons employed in MSMEs registered on Udyam portal in All India during 2020-21 (01.07.2020 to 31.03.2021) and 2021-22 (01.04.2021 to 28.03.2022) are 2.78 crore and 3.52 crore respectively. The State/UT wise details are attached in annexure I and District wise details for the state of Uttar Pradesh are attached in annexure II for the same period.(b) & (c):	 The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) implements different schemes and programmes aimed at promotion and development of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) including employment generation. These schemes / programmes, inter alia, include financial support. Some of these schemes programmes include Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE), Prime Minister''s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) and Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), etc.	 	The Government of India announced a series of measures under Aatma Nirbhar Bharat to support the economy, especially the MSME sector. This inter-alia includes (i) Rs. 20,000 crore Subordinate Debt for stressed MSMEs; (ii) Rs. 50,000 crore equity infusion through MSME Fund of Funds (SRI Fund); (ii) 3 lakh crore Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) for Businesses, including MSMEs (which has subsequently been increased to 5 lakh crore); etc.
