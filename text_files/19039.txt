                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

360






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Aarogya Setu App




P.R. Natarajan













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY   be pleased to State:(a) whether the Aarogya Setu App was created under the PPP mode;(b) if so, the details thereof including the terms and conditions of private developers role in the development of the app; and(c) the details of the action plan proposed by the Government to leverage the second app in view of less number of users of this contract tracing app?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):  Yes, Sir. The Aarogya Setu App has been developed by National Informatics Centre, in partnership with individual volunteers from industry and academia.(b):    The Individual volunteers from industry and academia made their contributions to the App development on a pro-bono basis in an individual capacity.(c):    Aarogya Setu is the single contact tracing App of Government of India with more than 16 Crore registered Users. This makes Aarogya Setu the largest App based contact tracing platform in the world.********
