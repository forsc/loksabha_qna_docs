                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4071






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Child Sexual Exploitation




Ajay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  	whether  there  has  been  a  sharp  rise  in  the number of incidents of exploitation/violence and sexual exploitation of children across the country;(b)  	if so, the  details thereof during the last three years,  State/UT-wise;  and(c) 	the corrective steps taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c) As per information provided by National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), the number of cases registered under crimes against children including exploitation/violence and sexual exploitation, from 2017-2019, State/UT-wise is at Annexure-I.The Government reviewed the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012  in the light of the rise of incidences of child sexual abuse in the country, inter-alia. Accordingly, the POCSO Act, 2012 was amended in 2019 in order to make it more effective in dealing with cases of child sex abuse. It addresses the need for stringent measures to deter child sex abuse in the Country on one hand and the menace of relatively new kind of crimes on the other hand. In furtherance of the POCSO (Amendment) Act, 2019, the Government notified the  POCSO Rules, 2020  for effective implementation of the Act.   *****Annexure-IStatement Referred To In Part (a) & (b) of Reply To Lok Sabha Un-Starred Question No.4071 For Answer On 19.03.2021 raised by Shri Ajay Bhatt regarding “Child Sexual Exploitation” indicating State/UT-wise Cases Registered (CR) under under crimes against children including exploitation/violence and sexual exploitation during 2017-2019SL	State/UT	2017	2018	2019		CR	CR	CR1	Andhra Pradesh	2397	2672	25242	Arunachal Pradesh	138	166	1533	Assam	4951	5447	66084	Bihar	5386	7340	93205	Chhattisgarh	6518	6924	56656	Goa	196	182	1677	Gujarat	3955	4929	46858	Haryana	4169	4869	51199	Himachal Pradesh	528	772	74810	Jammu & Kashmir	359	473	47011	Jharkhand	1247	1479	167412	Karnataka	5890	6131	630513	Kerala	3562	4253	475414	Madhya Pradesh	19038	18992	1902815	Maharashtra	16918	18892	1959216	Manipur	109	128	14817	Meghalaya	379	385	37918	Mizoram	220	198	12519	Nagaland	93	70	5920	Odisha	3185	5217	701221	Punjab	2133	2308	262522	Rajasthan	5180	5150	738523	Sikkim	190	221	16324	Tamil Nadu	3529	4155	413925	Telangana	3580	3747	421226	Tripura	276	271	31127	Uttar Pradesh	19145	19936	1894328	Uttarakhand	829	1306	121429	West Bengal	6551	6286	6286 	TOTAL STATE(S)	120651	132899	13981330	A&N Islands	119	162	15331	Chandigarh	275	288	26432	D&N Haveli	31	38	5333	Daman & Diu	24	34	4334	Delhi	7852	8246	778335	Lakshadweep	4	8	2636	Puducherry	76	89	50 	TOTAL UT(S)	8381	8865	8372 	TOTAL (ALL INDIA)	129032	141764	148185Source: Crime in India
