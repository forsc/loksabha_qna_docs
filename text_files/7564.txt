                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






National Testing Agency




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the CBSE is losing huge revenue after the setting up of National TestingAgency; and(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to compensate revenue loss of the CBSEinstead of imposing it on students and if so, the steps taken/ proposed to be takenby the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) and (b): There has been decrease in the revenue of the Central Board ofSecondary Education (CBSE) after setting up of the National Testing Agency. CBSE isa self financed institution under Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) anddoes not receive any Grant from Government of India. CBSE has not sought anyfinancial assistance from the Government of India so far.*****
