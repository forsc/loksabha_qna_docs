                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1960






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Yoga Wellness Centres




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) the details of budget allocated and actual utilization of funds for the setting up of yoga wellness centres during each of the last two years and the current year, State/UT-wise particularly in Uttar Pradesh; and (b) the details of yoga centres and the number of workshops conducted by such centres in the country during the said period, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) and (b):    Central Government is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in the country including Uttar Pradesh under which financial assistance is provided to States/UTs for setting up of Yoga Wellness Centre. The details of number of units approved, funds allocated/released and actual utilization of funds for Yoga Wellness Centre during last two years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh is furnished at Annexure.                         Further, implementation of scheme comes under purview of respective State/UT Governments and accordingly no data for workshop conducted under Yoga Wellness Centre is maintained by Central Government.
