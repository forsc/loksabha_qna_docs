                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4021






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Gender Inclusive Fund for 2020




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government allocated separate gender inclusion fund 2020 in National  Educational Policy 2020; and(b)  if so, the details of funds allocated, released and utilised, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) & (b):  	National Education Policy (NEP), 2020 provides for setting up Gender Inclusion Fund (GIF) to build nation’s capacity to provide equitable quality education for all girls as well as transgender students. The above objectives of NEP for equitable and quality education for girls and transgender children are being met through specific provisions under Samagra Shiksha 2.0 by allocating dedicated resources for Socio-Economically Disadvantaged  Groups (SEDGs).  The details of such provisions and resources allocated under Samagra Shiksha 2.0 for the year 2021-22 are as under:<pre>(Rs. in lakh)S. No.	Particulars	Amount 1	Provision of free text-books up to Class VIII to all children.	286055.882	Uniforms to all girls,  SC,  ST  children  and Below  Poverty  Line  (BPL)  children up to class VIII  	491952.503	Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (KGBVs)	243998.064	Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose  (NSCB) Awasiya Vidyalayas and Hostels	36025.275	Rani Laxmi Bai Atmaraksha  Prashikshan (Self defense training to Girls)	11657.766	Incinerator & Sanitary Pad Vending Machines	5606.077	Stipend for CWSN Girls	12257.66(Source: Prabandh)</pre> State/UT-wise details of funds allocated for the said provisions are at Annexure. *****
