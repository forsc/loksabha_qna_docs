                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2294






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Projects Related to Spiritual Circuits




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of projects related to spiritual circuits on which developmental works are being done along with the details of the developmental works thereof and the details of the developmental works being done in Ramayana circuit; (b) 	whether the said works have been completed within the stipulated time along with the work-wise details thereof; (c) 	the names of schemes on which works are currently being done in Uttar Pradesh including Prayagraj for the development of tourism along with the work-wise details thereof and the names of institutions by which the said works are being done; (d) 	whether any complaints have been received regarding the quality of the said works; and (e) 	if so, the details of the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			              (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) and (b):		For integrated development of Theme-Based Tourist Circuits in the country, Ministry of Tourism has launched Swadesh Darshan Scheme in 2014-15. Under the Scheme, 15 thematic circuits including Spiritual Circuit and Ramayana Circuit have been identified for development. The projects under the scheme are at different stages of implementation. A list of projects sanctioned by Ministry of Tourism under Spiritual Circuit and Ramayana Circuit is given at Annexure-A. (c):	The details of the projects sanctioned in Uttar Pradesh by Ministry of Tourism under ‘Swadesh Darshan’, ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ and ‘Assistance to Central  Agencies  for Tourism Infrastructure Development’ schemes  along with details of implementing agency is given at Annexure-B.(d):	No, Sir. (e):	Does not arise.*******
