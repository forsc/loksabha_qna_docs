                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Mental Health Counselling at Shelter Homes




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether shelter homes for women and children currently offer mental health counselling in India;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the number of such homes, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether there exists any monitoring protocol to ensure mental health  counselling is provided in all shelter homes; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	& (b)  The Ministry of Women and Child Development implements various Schemes which have the provision of shelter homes for providing medical care, including mental health counselling, to the women and children who are in difficult circumstances or are without any social and economic support. Details of such Schemes are given below:SwadharGreh: The Scheme caters to women victims of difficult circumstances in need of institutional support for rehabilitation so that they can lead their life with dignity. The details of total number of homes under SwadharGreh Scheme, State-wise is at Annexure I.Ujjawala: It is a comprehensive scheme comprising five components viz. prevention, rescue, rehabilitation, reintegration and repatriation of victims of trafficking of women and children for commercial sexual exploitation. The details of total number of homes under Ujjawala Scheme, State-wise is at Annexure II.Child Protection Services: The Ministry of Women & Child Development is implementing a centrally sponsored scheme, named Child Protection Services (CPS) scheme (erstwhile ICPS), which supports the measures for institutional and non-institutional care aimed at well being of children in distress situations. Under this Scheme the Central Government provides grants to each District Child Protection Units for hiring one counselor for providing counselling services to children in conflict with law and children in need of care and protection. Details of the number of Child Care Institutions being supported under CPS during the current financial year is at Annexure III.(c)& (d) Under SwadharGreh scheme monitoring is done at District level, State level and Central level to ensure smooth functioning, identifying gaps and suggesting steps that would leave to their better functioning.Under Ujjawala scheme, evaluation of projects is undertaken by Panchayati Raj Institutions, Block Level Institutions and District Level Institutions. Monitoring of the scheme is also done at State level and Central level.Under Child Protection Services, State Governments are required to appoint Inspection Committee for the State and District for mandatory inspection of all facilities housing children. *********** Annexure IAnnexure referred to in reply to Part b. of LokSabhaUnstarred Question No.4101 for reply on19.03.2021 regarding Mental Health Assistance in Shelter Homes by Shri Mohammed Faizal P.P.
