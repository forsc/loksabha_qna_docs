                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

169






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Contractual Staff under SSA




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the academic and non-academic staff working in schools on contract underSarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA), State-wise;(b) whether several States including Maharashtra have appointed teachers including technicalteachers on contract basis and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government is aware that some staff is working for more than ten years oncontract basis in some of the States including Maharashtra and if so, the reasons therefor; and(d) whether the Government has any proposal to make them permanent in future and if so, thedetails thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) to (d): Education is in the concurrent list of the Constitution and majority of the schoolscome under the jurisdiction of the respective States and Union Territory Governments. Therecruitment, service conditions and deployment of teachers including resource persons andnon-academic staff come under the purview of the concerned State/UT Government. Ministryof Human Resource Development has been requesting all the States and UT Governments forfilling-up the vacant posts of teachers and their rational deployment, for which the Ministryhas been issuing advisories to all the States and UTs from time to time. Further, the CentralGovernment, through the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Samagra Shiksha, providesassistance to the States and UTs for deployment of additional teachers to maintainappropriate Pupil Teacher Ratio as per the prescribed norms for various levels of schooling.****
