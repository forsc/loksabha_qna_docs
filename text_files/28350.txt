                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

165






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Funding under North-East Venture Fund




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of start-ups and other unique business opportunities that have received funding under the North East Venture Fund since 2017, State-wise and year-wise;(b)	the sector-wise details of such start-ups and unique business opportunities, especially food processing, healthcare, tourism, segregation of services, IT, etc;(c)	the number of jobs generated through such start-ups and unique business opportunities, including details of skilled and unskilled labour and female participation, State-wise and year-wise; and(d)	the total amount invested under the North East Venture Fund from 2017 to till date?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region  [Shri G.Kishan Reddy](a)	 The distribution of start-ups who have received funding under North East Venture Fund (NEVF)  state-wise and year wise is given in Annexure A. (b)	 Sector wise details of the start-ups who have received funding from NEVF is given in Annexure B. (c)	As on date details of job generated under  start-ups funded by NEVF is given in Annexure C.(d)	The fund is in operation since April, 2017 and has made in-principle commitment for investment of Rs. 73.76 Crore in 35 start-ups.
