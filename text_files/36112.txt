                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3381






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Financial Assistance to States




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the financial assistance provided by the Government to States for distribution of food items under the National Food Security Scheme, Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana and other public welfare schemes during the last eight years, State-wise; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to cover more number of beneficiaries under the National Food Security Scheme; and (c) 	if so, the work plan in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	Details of funds towards food subsidy released, during the last eight  years, by D/o Food and Public Distribution (DFPD) to the State Governments/ their Agencies, who have adopted Decentralized Procurement (DCP) Scheme, are enclosed at Annexure-I.Details of funds released to Food Corporation of India (FCI) during the period are enclosed at Annexure-II.Further, a statement indicating the funds released to States/Union Territories under the scheme “ Assistance to State Agency towards intra-State movement & handling of foodgrains and fair price shop dealers’ margin under the NFSA” for distribution of food grains under the National Food Security Act, 2013, Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojna and Atma Nirbhar Bharat Package is enclosed at Annexure III.(b) & (c): 	The National Food Security Act, 2013 provides for coverage of upto 75% of the rural and upto 50% of the urban population for receiving highly subsidized foodgrains under the Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS), which as per Census 2011 comes to about 81.35 crore.
