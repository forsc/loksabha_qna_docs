                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

984






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Petrol Pumps/LPG Agency




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether proposals for the opening of LPG agency and petrol pumps in Bharatpurand Alwar districts of Rajasthan have been received during the last two years;(b) if so, the locations for which the said proposals have been received;(c) the action taken by the Government so far on the said proposals; and(d) the locations where petrol pumps and LPG agencies have been opened as a resultof the said action?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d) Oil Marketing Companies(OMCs) have reported that 5 proposals for openingof LPG distributorship in Bharatpur and Alwar Districts of Rajasthan have been receivedduring the last 2 years and none of them met the feasibility norms for setting up of newLPG distributorships.Further, OMCs have issued advertisement at 685 locations in Bharatpur and AlwarDistricts of Rajasthan for setting up of new Retail Outlets in November/December 2018.Out of them, Letter of Intent in respect of 199 locations have been issued and 15locations have been commissioned till 30.06.2021.-----------
