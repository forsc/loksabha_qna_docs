                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4988






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Scientific Activities




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is conducting any programme to encourage fishermen and farmers to engage themselves with scientific activities/ undertake research work; (b)	if so, the main characteristics of research programme on farming and fishing together with the details of funds allotted for this purpose during each of the last three years and the current year and if not, the future plan of the Government in this regard; and (c)	whether the Government is offering any special plan or scholarship under this programme, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	No, Sir.  However, Fisheries Research Institute under ICAR involve farmers for carrying out location specific validation of technologies. *******
