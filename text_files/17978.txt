                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Stoppage of Trains Under Bhusawal Division




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken or proposes to take any step for providing stoppage/halt to Amravati-Pune train, Puri-Shirdi Express, Hatia Lokmanya Tilak Express, Puri-Ahmedabad Express, Puri-Ajmer Express, Nagpur-Pune Garib Rath Express, Ahmedabad-Nagpur Prerna Express, Huzoor Saheb Nanded-Bikaner Express in view of the religious and tourism importance of the Shegaon railway station under the Bhusawal division of Central Railways;(b)	if so, the details thereof(c)whether the Government has received requests from the public      representatives for approval of stoppage/ halt of major express trains at the Shegaon railway station;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) to (e) Yes, Sir.  Representations, including from the Hon’ble Member of Parliament for provision of stoppage of Puri-Ahmedabad Express (12843/12844) and certain other trains at Shegaon have been received. The same have been examined and in reference to the request, stoppage of Nagpur-Pune Garib Rath Express (12113/12114), Ahmedabad- Nagpur Prerna Express (22137/22138), Jodhpur- Chennai Egmore Express (22663/22664) and Hazoor Sahib Nanded-Bikaner Express (17623/17624) have been provided at Shegaon on an experimental basis w.e.f. 24.02.2019, 03.02.2019, 03.02.2019 and 15.09.2019 respectively. However, stoppage of additional trains at Shegaon has not been found feasible, at present.*****
