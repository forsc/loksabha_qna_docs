                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






India Post Payments Bank




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the salient features of India Post Payments Bank (IPPB); (b) 	the number of districts and the population covered under this scheme during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise including Rajasthan and district-wise; (c) 	the details of the facilities currently being provided by the Government to the account holders of IPPB; and (d) 	the details of the steps taken/being taken by the Government to make the services of IPPB more convenient in future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) is a scheduled payment bank offering a range of products such as savings and current accounts, remittances and money transfer, direct benefit transfer, bill and utility payments, enterprise & merchant payments and Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AePS). These products and related services are being offered across multiple channels e.g. Counter Services, Micro – ATM, Mobile Banking App, SMS and Interactive Voice Response (IVR) and at the doorstep.(b)      District-wise details of IPPB branches and customer base during each of the last three years, state/UT-wise including Rajasthan is placed at Annexure. (c)	IPPB offers a comprehensive suite of banking services to its account holders:•	Completely paperless and instant Account Opening•	Cash Deposits/ Withdrawal•	Money transfers•	Utility Bill Payments•	Government to Citizen (G2C) Payments including Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)(d)	IPPB is working towards enabling a complete suite of financial services including distribution of Loans, Insurance and Investments at the last mile through 3rd Party tie-ups.
