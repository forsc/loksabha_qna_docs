                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3073






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Cases of Road Accidents




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Indian roads account for the highest road accident fatalities in the world and if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto; (b) 	whether the number of deaths on road accidents rose nearly 5 per cent to 1.46 lakh in 2015 and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether 400 deaths per day or one death every 3.6 minutes occurred in India during 2015 and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the total number of cases of road accidents pending as on date, State-wise; and (e) 	whether disposal of road accidents cases takes five to ten years and if so, the reasons therefor along with the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS   (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 As per World Road Statistics, 2018 report of Indian Road Federation, India ranks highest in number of person killed in road accidents across the 199 countries. (b)	to  (c)   As per available information, total number of road accidents and the persons killed  in the country on all roads including Highways during the calendar year 2015 to 2018 are as under:-          Year	           Total Accidents	 Number of persons killed	2015	5,01,423	1,46,133	2016	4,80,652	1,50,785	2017	4,64,910	147,913	2018	4,67,044	151,417	(d)	to   (e)     This comes under the jurisdiction of State.*****
