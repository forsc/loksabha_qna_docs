                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2911






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Water Reactors in Co-operation with WEC




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether projects proposals in AP for setting up of water reactors (LWRs) in co-operation with Westinghouse Electric Company (WEC) have been finalised and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has assessed the impact of WEC being acquired by Brookfield Business Partners on the said project including time and cost overrun, if any; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR.JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)	No, Sir.  Presently techno-commercial discussions to arrive at a project proposal for setting up nuclear power reactors in cooperation with M/s. Westinghouse Electric Company (WEC) are still in progress.(b)&(c)	The various issues emerging out of the acquisition of WEC have been factored in the discussions.*****
