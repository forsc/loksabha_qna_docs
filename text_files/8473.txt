                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2001






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Mission Indradhanush




Vinod Kumar Sonkar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of Mission Indradhanush, an ambitious scheme of the Government to reduce infant mortality rate in the country;(b) the names of the districts/centres where this mission has been started, State/ UT/District-wise, including Uttar Pradesh; and(c) the time by which Mission Indradhanush is likely to be fully implemented across the country covering rest of the districts/centres, State/UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Mission Indradhanush (MI) was launched by the Government of India in December 2014 with the aim to rapidly increase full immunization coverage of children to 90% and thereafter sustain the coverage through routine immunization.A total of eight phases of Mission Indradhanush (including Gram Swaraj Abhiyan which was carried out in 16,850 villages across 541 districts and Extended Gram Swaraj Abhiyan which was carried out in 48,929 villages across 117 aspirational districts) have been carried out covering 680 districts across all 36 States/UTs. During these eight phases of Mission Indradhanush a total of 3.39 crore children and 87.18 lakh pregnant women have been vaccinated, as on 28th March’19.(b):	 Details of States/UT/District-wise including Uttar Pradesh is placed at Annexure.(c):	 Mission Indradhanush (MI) has already been implemented across all 36 state/UTs covering 680 out of 754 districts. All the 75 districts of Uttar Pradesh have already been covered during various phases of Mission Indradhanush.
