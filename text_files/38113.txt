                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5129






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Students enrolled in EMRS




M.K. Vishnu Prasad













                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Maddila  Gurumoorthy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of boys and girls students enrolled in Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) in the last five years, year-wise;(b) whether the Government has allocated/ released funds for establishment of EMRSs during the said period; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a):  As per the data shared by National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) which is an autonomous body under this Ministry, the number of boys and girls students enrolled in Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) in the last five years are as under: Year	Boys	Girls	Total2017-18	          30,784	          29,134	             59,9182018-19	          31,481	          29,805	             61,2862019-20	          37,260	          37,861	             75,1212020-21	          44,756	          45,764	             90,5202021-22	          51,267	          54,196	          1,05,463  (b) and (c): Fund released for establishment and running of EMRSs during the last five years (recurring and capital) is as under:Year	Total release (Rs. in lakhs)2016-17	 48837.56 2017-18	68203.72 2018-19	71096.76 2019-20	 145424.38 2020-21	120000.00  Note:  Funds for EMRSs was being released to states under the scheme of Article 275(1) of the Constitution till the year 2019-20 and from 2020-21 onwards funds for EMRS is being released to NESTS for further release to State Societies, construction agencies and others .*****
