                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3715






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Services of Peritoneal Dialysis in Andhra Pradesh




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has initiated a program to promote Peritoneal Dialysis under Prime Minister National Dialysis Program; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the patients suffering from kidney problems in Andhra Pradesh (AP) have availed the service of Peritoneal Dialysis; and(d) if so, the details of the patients availing the services of Peritoneal Dialysis in the last three years and the current year district-wise in AP?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR) (a) & (b): Peritoneal dialysis (PD) has been introduced under PMNDP and guidelines for peritoneal dialysis were launched on 10th October 2019. Under Pradhan Mantri National Dialysis Programme (PMNDP), State Governments are supported for providing dialysis services free of cost to all Below Poverty Line(BPL) patients under the umbrella of the National Health Mission (NHM). Such support is based on the proposals received from States in their annual program Implementation Plans (PIPs). In PIP 2021-22, 14 State/UTs were approved funds for peritoneal dialysis under Pradhan Mantri National Dialysis program for providing PD services free of cost to BPL beneficiaries. The financial support provided under this program includes provision for patient supported monitoring equipment (BP apparatus, IV stand, weighing scale), Quarterly replacement of transfer set, PD bags (including training support) etc. (c) & (d): The peritoneal dialysis under Pradhan Mantri National Dialysis Programme (PMNDP) has not been initiated in Andhra Pradesh due to Covid-19 Pandemic, as informed by the State.*****
