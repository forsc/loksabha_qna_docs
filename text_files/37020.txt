                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

379






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Employment of Persons with Disabilities




S. Jothimani













                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether out of 1.3 crore of employable Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) only 34 lakh are currently employed; (b)  if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) the number of PwDs employed under Government schemes since 2014, year-wise and during the current year;(d)  the number of PwDs that the Government has trained and the number of such trainees placed under various schemes since 2014 till date, year-wise; and(e)  whether the Government has failed to increase the budget for the National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation despite the assistance required by PwDs in employment and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR)     (a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in reply to part (a) to (e) of the Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 379 raised by MS. S. JOTHIMANI: SHRI T.N. PRATHAPAN: regarding “Employment of Persons with Disabilities” for answer on 29.03.2022.(a) & (b)  According to Census 2011, there are 2.68 crore persons with disabilities in the country which inter-alia include persons with visual, hearing, locomotor, and mental disabilities. As per this Census, 36% persons with disabilities were employed as workers.(c)  National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFD), under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) of the Ministry channelizes concessional loans to persons with disabilities (PwDs) throughout the country through its various schemes for self-employment of PwDs. Beneficiaries (PwDs) covered from 2014-15 till current year (year-wise) is as under:S No.	Year	No. of Beneficiaries(i)	2014-15	14703(ii)	2015-16	20552(iii)	2016-17	16101(iv)	2017-18	11767(v)	2018-19	11221(vi)	2019-20	17935(vii)	2020-21	18326(viii)	2021-22	11335	Total	121940Further, as per information received from Ministry of Rural Development, number of persons with disabilities who availed employment under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MNREGS) since 2014-15 is as under:Financial Year	Number of PwDs availed employment under MNREGS  (in lakh)2014-15	4.152015-16	4.592016-17	4.712017-18	4.722018-19	4.622019-20	4.632020-21	6.062021-22 (as on 23.03.2022)	5.60 (d)   The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojna (PMKVY)” for Skill based training of the youth across the country including PwDs. Further, with the objective to provide skill training to persons with disabilities (PwDs) for enabling them to become self-reliant and productive members of the society, the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) launched "National Action Plan (NAP) for Skill Development of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs)" in March, 2015. Prior to these schemes, broadly the National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC) used to provide skill training under ‘Scheme for Implementation of Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (SIPDA)’. The Government has released Rs.399.99 Cr. to National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation since its inception in 1997 as equity support. The Corporation has catered to loan assistance required by Divyangjan for self-employment out of the said funds. The Corporation has been addressing the loan assistance needs of Divyangjan by ploughing back the funds available with it and did not seek support of Government since Financial Year 2020-21. *****
