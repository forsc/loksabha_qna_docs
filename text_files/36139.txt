                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3622






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Safety Audit of Airlines




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) conducts a safety audit of airlines on regular basis and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether certain airlines have failed to clear safety audit on various parameters; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by DGCA against those airlines?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) Yes Sir, DGCA carries out Surveillance and Audits in accordance with Annual Surveillance Plan, for all commercial aircraft operators in the country. The annual surveillance plan is published on DGCA website. The details of the safety audits for airlines carried out in the past 3 years is at Annexure-A.(b) & (c) The observations made during the audits are followed up with the concerned operator for the appropriate corrective action and closure. In case of violations detected during audit/ surveillance, enforcement action is taken. Some of the common observations made during the audit are detailed at Annexure-B.The enforcement action taken for violations detected during audit in last 3 years is as under:(i) DGCA post holder approvals were withdrawn for M/s Spice Jet for the violations observed during safety audit.(ii) Financial penalty was imposed on M/s Star Air for the violations observed during safety audit.******
