                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Piped Water to every Household




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that the Union Government is intensifying the implementation of piped water to all rural households under the Jal Jeevan Mission; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Union Government has received any request from the State Government of Tamil Nadu to provide adequate funds to implement the piped water to all rural households under the Jal Jeevan Mission; and (d) 	if so, the action taken by the Union Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b)	Government of India is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in partnership with States to provide tap water connection to every rural household in the country by 2024, with an estimated outlay is Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore. At the time of announcement of Jal Jeevan Mission in August, 2019, out of 18.93 Crore rural households, 3.23 Crore households (17%) were reported to have tap water connections. Since then, 3.83 Crore rural households have been provided tap water connections. Thus, so far, 7.06 Crore (36.86%), rural households are getting potable water through tap water connection. (c) & (d)	In 2020-21, under Jal Jeevan Mission, Tamil Nadu has been allocated Rs. 921.99 Crore, out of which, till date Rs. 688.08 (74.62%) Crore have been drawn by the State. Based on physical progress and utilization of more than 80% available fund by the State Government, next tranche of fund is released.****
