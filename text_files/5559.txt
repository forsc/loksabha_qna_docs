                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5120






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






ROBs in Karnataka




B. Y. Raghavendra














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of construction of Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) in lieu of Level Crossings in the State of Karnataka;(b)	the details of the last three years, year and railway line-wise;(c)	whether the Government is aware that the work is going on at a very slow pace and causing excess of expenditure on the projects;(d)	if so, the details of the ROBs completed, that are under construction and those where work has not yet started;(e)	whether the Government has allocated adequate funds for the said projects; and(f)	if so, the details for the last three years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
