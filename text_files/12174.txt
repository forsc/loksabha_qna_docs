                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1080






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether many private medical practitioners have provided free check up and free treatment to the pregnant women in their clinics/nursing homes once in a month under Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA);(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT and districtwise;(c) the number of women benefited so far from the said scheme particularly in Madhya Pradesh; and(d) the steps taken by the Government to honour and encourage private medical practitioners to extend such essential ante-natal care?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	Under Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA) programme, obstetricians / Physicians working in the private sector are encouraged to volunteer for the campaign and provide services at designated public health facilities on the 9th of every month.State/UT wise including Madhya Pradesh (District wise) details of private medical practitioners providing services and number of pregnant women so far seen by private medical practitioner is at "Annexure-A". (d):	Following steps have been taken to encourage private medical practitioners to extend essential antenatal care;•	Appeal to Private Medical Practitioners; Appeal to private sector doctors to volunteer & join hands for PMSMA by Hon''ble Prime Minister on the occasion of "MANN KI BAAT" episode on 31st July''2019.•	Centralise PMSMA Portal: Centralise PMSMA Portal has been created for registering private practitioners.To Register as a volunteer Doctors can:o	Register by calling  "18001801104" - Call centre, agent will respond and register the volunteer.o	Register by SMS "PMSMA space their name to 5616115"  Volunteer will receive a call back from the call centre.o	Register by logging on to www.pmsma.nhp.gov.ino	Register through the PMSMA mobile application.•	PMSMA ‘IPledgeFor9’ Achievers Awards:   PMSMA ‘IPledgeFor9’ Achievers Awards have been devised to celebrate individual and team achievements and acknowledge voluntary contributions for PMSMA?	Commitment from FOGSI & IMA: MoHFW & IMA/ FOGSI have joined hands to pro- mote PMSMA through platforms such Lok Sabha TV, video conferences with member doctors, articles in annual reports etc.FOGSI felicitates PMSMA volunteers at its National level Meet.
