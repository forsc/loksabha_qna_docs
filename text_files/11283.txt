                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Setting-up of National Police University




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of the proposal to set up National Police University in the country;(b) whether it is not appropriate to set up National Police University in Telangana, particularly in or around Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel National Police Academy;(c) if so, whether any proposal has been mooted for this; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	On the request of Government of Gujarat, Central Government has planned to establish a National Police University by making the existing Raksha Shakti University, Gujarat a Central University and conferring upon it the status of Institute of National Importance.(b) to (d):	At present, there is no proposal to set up National Police University in Telangana.*****
