                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1392






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Outsourcing RRB




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has any plan to outsource its competitive exams to private agencies;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the number of recruitments conducted through RRB in the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): No, Sir. However, certain individual activities like online registration of applications, Computer Based Tests in different cities/centres and shortlisting of candidates are done in collaboration with external agencies under the strict guidance of Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs).(c): Following recruitments have been notified/conducted through RRBs during the last five years –Year	   No. of Centralized Employment           Notifications (CENs) issued	             Notified/Modified Vacancies2015-2016	03	                                  185122016-2017	Nil	                                      Nil2017-2018	02	                                    1275732018-2019	  05*	                                    1561012019-2020	Nil	                                       Nil*03 CENs i.e. 01/2019, 03/2019 & RRC-01/2019 are under process.*****
