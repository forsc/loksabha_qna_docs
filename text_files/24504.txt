                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

405






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Survey on Differently Abled Persons




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is having any data on the number of Differently Abled Persons who are doing selling activities in running trains, buses and other public places;(b)  if so, the details thereof, State-wise particularly in Tamil Nadu(c)  whether these kinds of differently abled persons are identified as platform traders;(d)  if so, the details thereof; and(e)  if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) to (e)    The Ministry has not conducted any survey on the number of persons with disabilities (PwDs) involved in selling activities in running trains, buses and other public places. Relief to the persons with disabilities is a State subject by virtue of Entry 9 of the Constitution of India. Management of public places and passenger bus transport also fall under the domain of State Governments      As regards railways, Ministry of Railways has informed that there are 81 catering/vending units on railway platforms which have been contracted to PwDs. State-wise breakup of these units is at Annexure.  *****
