                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5094






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Workers in Steel and Cement Factories




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government is aware the number of workers working in steel and cement factories located in the Tadipatri of Anantapur in Andhra Pradesh; and(b)if so, the details of health facilities and welfare measures provided to them by ESI hospitals?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	There are 5811 Insured Persons (IPs) in Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) in cement and steel factories located in Tadipatri in Ananthpur districts of Andhra Pradesh.Under ESI Act, 1948, primary medical services in the area are   provided to IPs through ESI Scheme (ESIS) state run dispensary situated at Tadipatri.   Secondary and Tertiary facilities are being provided through empanelled hospitals located in Ananthpur district.  Other benefits under ESI Act, 1948 include  sickness, maternity, disablement benefits. Further, under Atal Bimit Vyakti Kalyan Yojana (ABVKY) cash compensation up to 90 days, once in lifetime in the contingency of Insured Persons’ (IPs’) unemployment are provided. Benefits under Covid-19 Relief scheme are also being extended to the eligible beneficiaries.******
