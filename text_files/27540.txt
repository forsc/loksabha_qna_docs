Crime against Children




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has taken note of increased number of crimes against children and if so, the number of such cases reported across the country during the last three years State/UT-wise including Assam;(b) 	Whether regular review exercise of childcare homes/orphanages have been conducted across the country, if so, the details and the outcome thereof, State/UT-wise including Assam and Haryana; (c) 	Whether the Government has received complaints of atrocities committed against the inmates of such childcare homes; and (d) 	If so, the details thereof along with the action taken thereon?






ANSWER

The National Commission for Protection of Child Rights had conducted Social Audit of Child Care Institutions (CCIs) in the country.
