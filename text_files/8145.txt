                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Global Assistance for Smart Cities Mission




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the G20 Global Smart Cities Alliance has agreed to provide financial assistance to the Smart Cities Mission of India for development of smart cities as per global norms; and (b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




(a)	: No Sir. (b)	: At present, there is no such proposal under consideration of the    Government.                                       *****
