                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1962






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Legal Profession




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether land mafia and musclemen are reportedly entering in the field of legal profession in various parts of the country; (b) if so, whether the Government proposes to take any concrete steps to check such practices; (c) whether any guidelines are likely to be issued for taking strict action against the tainted advocates as per the instructions issued by the Supreme Court and various High Courts; and(d) if so, the details thereof and time by which the guidelines will be issued?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE,  COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (d) The information is being collected  and will be laid on the Table of the House.
