                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

900






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Immunity Booster




K. Muraleedharan













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                








                                                    Faizal P.P. Mohammed
                                                








                                                    Gaurav Gogoi
                                                








                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has upgraded the license for Patanjali Ayurved’s Coronil from an “immunity booster” to a “supporting measure” in treating COVID-19;(b) if so, the details thereof alongwith the data/ studies based on which such an upgrade was approved and the limitations of such data/studies, if any;(c) the details of the decision-making process in granting such approval;(d) the laws/rules/policies/guidelines under which such approval was granted; and(e) the definitions of “immunity booster” and “supporting measure” under the applicable laws/rules/ policies/guidelines, and the procedure and conditions subject to which such license is granted?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(DR. MUNJPARA MAHENDRABHAI)(a) to (c):	Application titled, “updating the AYUSH license for Divya Coronil tablet from “Immunity booster” to “Medicine for COVID-19”” submitted by Patanjali Research Foundation Trust, Haridwar, Uttarakhand to this Ministry, was placed before the Interdisciplinary Technical Review Committee (ITRC) setup by Ministry of AYUSH on 28.10.2020 for examination of the applications/claims on patent & propriety (P&P) ASU&H medicines with new indications or repurposing of licensed P&P, ASU&H medicines for COVID-19. ITRC reviewed the study and it was suggested that it may be used as supporting measure in COVID. The State Licensing Authority was also informed that Coronil tablet may be used as supporting measure in the management of COVID-19 without claiming cure.(d) & (e):	The regulatory provisions for granting license and approval of Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy drugs are prescribed under Drugs and Cosmetics Act,1940 and Rules 1945. Section 3(a) and Section 3(h) defines all Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani medicines intended for internal or external use for or in the diagnosis, treatment, mitigation or prevention of diseases or disorder in human beings or animals, and manufactured exclusively in accordance with the formulae described in, the authoritative books of Ayurvedic, Siddha and Unani Tibb systems of medicine, specified in the First Schedule.The State Licensing Authority Uttarakhand has granted license of drug “Coronil” after examination of the application submitted by the applicant. The State Licensing Authority Ayurveda government of Uttarakhand was informed that “Coronil” Tablet may be used as supporting measure in the management of COVID-19 without claming cure..
