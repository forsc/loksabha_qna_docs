                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Allocation of Funds for Chhattisgarh




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of various works as well as schemes along with the amount of funds allocated by the Union Government during the last two year 2018-2020 to Chhattisgarh;and(b)	whether the Union Government has received any proposal sent by the Government of Chhattisgarh during the said period  and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs(MoHUA) facilitates and assists States/Union Territories (UTs) through its flagship Missions/Schemes  - Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), Smart Cities Mission (SCM), Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban)(PMAY-U), Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban(SBM-U), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) in urban development. The Mission/Schemes are implemented by the States/UTs in accordance with the Mission guidelines. MoHUA extends financial assistance for the schemes/projects as per guidelines. Details of Mission/schemes, works and amount released to Chhattisgarh are given in the Annex I and Annex II respectively.(b)	Proposal for sanction of 1, 35,192 houses under PMAY-U was received during the period from the Government of Chhattisgarh which was approved.                                             *****
