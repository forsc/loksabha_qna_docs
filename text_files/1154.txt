                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1011






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Value Engineering Program




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the road construction projects that are following the “Value Engineering Program” model; and(b) the details about the cost effectiveness of this model?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b) The development/upgradation of any National Highway(NH) is taken up based on the outcome of Detailed Project Report (DPR). Various aspects such as technical, social & environmental are studied and accordingly the proposal for development/upgradation of NH is finalized. The DPR also incorporate the aspects of value engineering, quality audit, safety audit etc. in design and implementation.The preparation of DPR and implementation of projects is dealt by the various implementing agencies such as NHAI, NHIDCL, PWD etc.*****
