                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3667






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Road Map of Human Resource Developmentin the Country




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the efforts made to prepare roadmap of human resource development in thecountry to develop the field specific talent to make India a hub of export of service tothe world;b) whether India is engaged in the process of preparing a roadmap of the worldto ascertain the actual requirement of human resources to the world and to developsuch skill in India; andc) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVEVOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) has been developingthe field specific talent through various programme and schemes in addition oftransforming the education ecosystem of India by enabling: Access, Inclusion,Equality, Quality, Excellence, Professional faculty, Employability, Research &Innovation and Internationalization, through a holistic approach. Further, MHRD isbringing out a New Education Policy, which is under formulation will provide adetailed roadmap in this regard.The initiatives taken by MHRD to empower and equip the young generation of Indiawith 21’st Century skills and knowledge through major reforms in academic,institutional and governance areas may be accessed from the website of MHRD,www.mhrd.gov.in.****
