                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1503






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Setting-Up of FPIs in Punjab




Bhagwant Mann














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to set up Food Processing Industries(FPIs) in every district of Punjab;(b)	if so, the districts in Punjab where food processing zone have already been set up;(c)	the steps taken by the Government to set up food processing zone in the remaining districts of Punjab; and(d)	the financial assistance provided to the private entrepreneurs to set up such units?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) to (d): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a Central Sector umbrella scheme- Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector including setting up of Food Processing Industries across the country including all the districts of Punjab. Under its component schemes,  Ministry provides credit linked financial assistance (capital subsidy) in the form of grants-in-aid to entrepreneurs for setting up of food processing / preservation industries. A total of 3 Mega Food Parks, 3 Agro Processing Clusters, 23 Integrated Cold Chain Projects, 8 Food Processing Units, 4 Creation of Backward & Forward Linkages Projects and 9 Food Testing Laboratories has been approved for assistance under corresponding component schemes of PMKSY with total grants-in-aid of Rs 376.98 Crore. The details of Mega Food Parks and Agro Processing Clusters approved in Punjab is placed at Annexure.PMKSY is not any region or state specific but demand driven and financial assistance is provided to the eligible applicants selected on the basis of Expression of Interest (EoI) issued from time to time. Ministry undertakes advocacy campaigns to impress upon the entrepreneurs to avail the benefits available under its schemes. *****
