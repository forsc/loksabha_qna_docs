                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

576






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Toilets Constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin)




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of toilets constructed so far under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) in the country;(b) whether the Government has fixed any target for the current year; and(c) if so, the details thereof, State/District-wise?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) As per the data reported by the States/UTs on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) of Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) maintained by Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation, 10.78 crore individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) from 2.10.2014 to 19.7.2021. (b) & (c) For implementation of the Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen), each year State Governments submit their Annual Implementation Plans (AIPs), consolidated for all the districts, to the Government of India.  As per the AIPs for the current financial year, States have projected construction of 51.05 lakh IHHLs (subject to actual demand) under the programme.  State/UT-wise details are given at Annexure.
