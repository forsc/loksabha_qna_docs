                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






GHTC-India




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has initiated a Global Housing Technology Challenge-India (GHTC-India) programme and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether any technology for affordable and low cost housing has been identified under this programme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Yes, Sir. The Government of India through the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs has launched Global Housing Technology Challenge - India (GHTC-India) to enable a paradigm shift in the construction sector for affordable housing in the country through global challenge.  The objective of the challenge is to identify and mainstream a basket of innovative construction technologies from across the globe for housing construction sector that are sustainable, eco-friendly, cost effective, speedier and disaster-resilient.(b):	Yes, Sir. 54 innovative and alternate technologies in 6 broad categories for affordable and low cost housing have been identified through GHTC-India. The details of technologies grouped in 6 broad categories are at Annexure.                                               *****
