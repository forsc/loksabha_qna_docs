                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Eklavya Model Residential Schools




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Chunni Lal Sahu
                                                








                                                    Parvatagouda Chandanagouda Gaddigoudar
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has sanctioned the establishment of Eklavya Model Residential schools inthe country, State/UT-wise specially in Maharashtra, Tripura and Chhattisgarh;(b)	if so, the details of the EMRS sanctioned in the country in last two years, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether it is a fact that Government has given sanction for the establishment, if EMRS in the State of Maharashtra and Tripura and Chhattisgarh, if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether it is a fact that the work for the establishment of EMRS in Maharashtra, Tripura and Chhattisgarh is still not started; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the schedule date of starting of the work?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (c) Ministry of Tribal Affairs has sanctioned a total number of 78EklavyaModel Residential Schools (EMRSs) and Eklavya Model Day Boarding Schools (EMDBSs) during the last two years, i.e. 2017-18 and 2018-19, across the country including the States of Maharashtra, Tripura and Chhattisgarh. The State-wise details of these schools, including that of Maharashtra, Tripura and Chhattisgarh, is attached at Annexure.(d)	& (e) The construction work regarding establishment of EMRS in Maharashtra, Tripura and Chhattisgarh has been assigned to CPWD. Preliminary work including land survey and soil testing have been started by the agency.
