                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Setting up of AIIMS like Institutuions




Nishikant  Dubey













                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Kanimozhi Karunanidhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to set up new AIIMS Institutes in the country, if so the details thereof State/UT wise;(b) whether some AIIMS Institutes set up in the country are not fully operational, if so the details thereof and the time by which said Institutes are likely to be fully operational including in Deoghar (Jharkhand) and Madurai (Tamil Nadu);(c) whether the Government has proposed to update some medical colleges into AIIMS Institutes in the country, if so the details thereof State/UT-wise and college-wise; and(d) whether a number of States/UTs have requested to set up new AIIMS like institutions, if so, the State/UT wise details thereof along with its current status particularly in ESIC Kalaburagi?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b) 22 AIIMS have been announced/sanctioned to be set up under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY). Out of which, six (06) AIIMS i.e. Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Raipur and Rishikesh are functional. Other 16 AIIMS are in various stages of their construction. Timelines for their completion are as per Annexure-I.(c) Under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana, existing Government Medical Colleges/Institutions (GMCIs) are upgraded through establishment of Super-speciality blocks/Trauma Centre. List of GMCIs being upgraded under PMSSY is as per Annexure-II.(d) Requests from Government of Kerala, Karnataka, Goa, Arunachal Pradesh, Sikkim, Tripura, Mizoram and Manipur have been received for setting up of AIIMS in the State.	AIIMS for the above States including at ESIC Kalaburagi have not been sanctioned.
