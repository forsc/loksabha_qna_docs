                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2558






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Mobile Towers in Tribal And Hilly Areas




Pramila Bisoyi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the steps taken by the Government to expedite the setting up of mobile towers in tribal and hilly areas of the country including Odisha to address the problem of poor connectivity; (b) 	the steps taken by the Government to improve the reach and quality of broadband services of BSNL in rural areas in view of huge increase in internet consumption on account of online education during the pandemic years; (c) 	whether the Government is planning to provide BSNL broadband connections in rural areas and if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to provide such connections in rural areas of Odisha; and (d) 	the time by when all the rural areas will be connected with broadband services of BSNL? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	The Government through Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) provides Mobile coverage in rural areas including tribal and hilly areas in the country in a phased manner. Government has approved various schemes/projects through Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) support for mobile and broadband services in rural and remote areas across the country. The details of the schemes are attached as Annexure-I.(b) to (d) 	The Government has approved the revival plan for BSNL on 23.10.2019, which, inter-alia, includes administrative allotment of spectrum for providing 4G services with funding through budgetary allocation. With the launch of 4G services on Pan-India basis, reach and quality of BSNL wireless broadband services will expand in the country including in rural areas.
