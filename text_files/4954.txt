                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Vigyan Kendra/Taramandal




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up Vigyan Kendra and Planetariums/ Taramandals at the district level to encourage children for scientific activities and to make them understand the scientific methods; (b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c)	the alternate plan/arrangements made by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????; ??????? ?? ???????????? ??????; ?? ?????? ??????? ????????. ???? ?????(a) 	Yes, Sir. National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), an autonomous organization under the Ministry of Culture (MoC) is engaged in establishment of Science Centres throughout the country. Science Centres are set up throughout the country based on proposals received from different State Govts./UTs and with the approval of the Government as per the norms of the Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science (SPoCS) scheme of Ministry of Culture (MoC) through National Council of Science Museums (NCSM).  Portable Taramandal (inflatable dome Planetarium) are also provided to each such science centres. Department of Science & Technology (DST), Ministry of Science & Technology, Government of India, has no such initiative. Kendriya Vidyalayas Sangathan organizes Astronomical Telescope Making Workshops for students in collaboration with Vigyan Prasar,  an aided institution (AI) of Department of Science & Technology.  It is also aimed to set up Vigyan Evam Praudyogiki  Kendras (VPK) and Jila Tara Mandal(JTM) to encourage children and other levels of society for propagation of scientific activities.(b)	Ministry of Culture (MoC) through National Council of Science Museums (NCSM) has set up 23 Science Cities / Museums /Centres and 20 Regional / Sub-Regional Science Centres. Addition of 16 new Science Museums / Centres by NCSM, Ministry of Culture under the Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science (SPoCS) is an ongoing activity  of that Ministry. (c )  	Does not arise.                                                    ********
