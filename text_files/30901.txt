                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2303






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Activities of Celebration of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav in Indian Embassies




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched a week long set of activities around the theme of India’s 75th Independence Day, or Azadi Ka Amrit Mahothsav, in India and at its embassies abroad including film shows, quizzes, dramas and skits, and the launch of an e-book, Making of the Constitution; and(b)	if so, the details thereof and response received and future action plan worked out so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM, AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	and (b)   Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav (AKAM) is an initiative to celebrate and  commemorate 75 years of India’s independence.  It follows whole of Government approach with special emphasis on involvement of citizens in the form of ‘janbhagidari’.  Indian Missions/Posts abroad commenced AKAM celebrations from 12 March 2021.  The celebrations have started 75 weeks before 15 August, 2022 and shall continue up to 15 August, 2023.  Missions/Posts have accorded high importance to the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav. Over 5,000 events have been organized by Missions/Posts since 12 March, 2021.  Iconic and impactful events are curated on a regular basis to commemorate AKAM, including Constitution Day and Mahaparinirvan Diwas.  There has been enthusiastic participation of Indian Diaspora, Friends of India, Local Government and prominent individuals at AKAM events organised by our Missions/Posts.The Missions/Posts has organized over 100 Iconic Weeks till date.  Action Plan is prepared bases on local conditions.
