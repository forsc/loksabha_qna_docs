                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

864






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Construction of Roads and Bridges




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether 28 km of roads are being constructed everyday in the country including Maharashtra and Rajasthan and if so, the total length of roads constructed and repaired in Madha Lok Sabha constituency (Maharashtra) and Jalore Sirohi and Sikar district in Rajasthan during the last three years;(b)	 the present status of the road construction work in Madha Lok Sabha Constituency including Jalor-Ahore highway (NH-325) and Sikar district; (c) 	the reasons for delay in the construction work of the overbridges on the said highways and an overbridge in place of level crossing no 48 on NH-325; (d) 	the time limit fixed for construction of these bridges and roads; and (e) 	the total cost of these roads and over bridges along with the funds allocated and utilised thereon so far? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)   The average construction of Highways was 28.04 Km per day during the FY 2019-2020 in the country. The present status of construction of National Highways (NHs) in Madha Lok Sabha constituency in Maharashtra is at Annexure – 1. The present status of NHs constructed and repaired in Jalore, Sirohi and Sikar district in Rajasthan during the last 3 years are at Annexure – II. The present status of construction works of NH-325 (Pachpadra – Siwna- Jalore- Ahore-Sanderao) is at Annexure – III. (c)   There are 4 No. of ROB on NH-325 on LC No. C-24, C-32, C-44 and C-48. Out of 4 No. of ROB, 3 No. of ROB (C-24,C-32 & C-44) are included in DPR of Bypasses (Package-4). DPR & Cost Estimate for LC-48 is prepared separately and is included in Annual Plan of Rajasthan for 2020-21 with an estimated cost of Rs. 61.00  Crore.(d)   Twenty four months shall be required to complete this Road and over bridges from the award of work. (e)    As these works are at DPR stage the total cost of these roads and over bridges can only be ascertained after finalization of DPR.
