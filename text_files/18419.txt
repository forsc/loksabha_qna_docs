                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2088






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






National Rural Health Mission Survey




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is contemplating to conduct annual health survey in States under National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) by empowered working group for getting information regarding the effectiveness of the schemes;(b) if so, the names of the States where such survey is likely to be conducted;(c) the time by which the outcome of such survey is likely to be received;(d) whether the Government proposes to allocate funds to State/UT Governments for this purpose; and(e) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	No such survey is proposed by Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. However, under the National Health Mission (NHM), Common Review Missions (CRM) are conducted annually to  assess and monitor the progress and implementation status of various  schemes under the  NHM. The key strategies and priority areas of CRM are to analyze challenges with respect to strengthening health systems, identify trends in progress of key indicators,  evaluate the readiness of  the state to undertake implementation of new initiatives, and review the progress and coordination mechanisms with various partners.(b) to (e) : Do not arise.
