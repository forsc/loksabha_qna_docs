                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3980






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Projects Implemented under PMJVK




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has implemented projects under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) in Kerala; and(b)	if so, the details for the last two years and the current year, district-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. Based on the proposals received from the State Government of Kerala, the following projects amounting to Rs. 7934.86 lakh have been sanctioned, under PMJVK in the State of Kerala,during the lasttwo years and the current year:-S. No.	Financial Year	Projects Sanctioned(Rs. in Lakh)	Type of Projects(in numbers)1	2017-18	1822.10	School Buildings – 18, Additional Class Rooms – 9.2	2018-19	2985.42	School Buildings - 11, Working Women Hostels – 3, Market Sheds - 2, Toilets –13.3	2019-20(till date)	3127.34	Additional Block in Existing ITI - 1, Common Service Centres - 9, Hunar Hubs- 2, Skill Centres – 2.Details of the projects are available in the minutes of Empowered Committee meetings uploaded on the website of the Ministry at http://www.minorityaffairs.gov.in/empowered-committee-minutes***
