                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






AYUSH Scheme in Maharashtra




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) the details of funds allocated/ granted/released under various AYUSH schemes in the country, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra during the last four years, stream-wise; and (b) the time by which the Government is likely to release the remaining funds to the State of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):     The details of the funds allocated/granted/ released under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in the country, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra during the last four years as per the proposal received from them is furnished at Annexure. However, no stream-wise funds are released by Central Government.        Further, the funds allocated for promotion of AYUSH systems of medicine under the Central Sector Scheme for Promotion of Information Education and Communication (IEC) in AYUSH during the last four years is as follows:Year	Actual Expenditure/Allocation (Rs. in Crore)2015-16	27.702016-17	24.662017-18	31.072018-19	63.69Under IEC Scheme, the funds are not allocated to States/UTs separately.(b):      Under NAM, State Government of Maharashtra has submitted State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) for the current financial year 2019-20 and eligible amount of Rs. 43.09 Crore has been released by the Ministry of AYUSH as per NAM guidelines.
