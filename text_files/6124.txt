                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5544






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






National Institute of Ayurveda




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has plans to setup branches of National Institute of Ayurveda (NIA) in the country, State/ UT-wise; and (b) if so, the details thereof along with the locations identified for the purpose, and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):      It has been decided to establish an extension of National Institute of Ayurveda, Jaipur at Shri Mata Mansa Devi Complex, Panchkula, Haryana offering UG, PG, PhD degrees with 250 bedded Hospital, Boys & Girls Hostel, Guest House and Residential Buildings.………………………
