                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the work under the North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS) is under progress;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the details of the progress and intention of the Government under NESIDS;(d) 	the steps being taken by the Government for water supply, electricity and connectivity under physical infrastructure project;(e) 	the provision made by the Government to promote tourism; and(f) 	the details of the progress made in education and health sector?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh] (a) to (f) The broad objective of North East Special Infrastructure Scheme (NESIDS) is to ensure focused development of North East Region by providing financial assistance for projects of physical infrastructure relating to water supply, power and connectivity to enhance tourism and social sectors for creation of infrastructure in the areas of primary and secondary sectors of education and health. Under NESIDS, 59 projects costing Rs.1835.35 crore have been sanctioned, against which Rs.439.19 crore have been released so far. All the sanctioned projects under the scheme are at various stages of implementation.
