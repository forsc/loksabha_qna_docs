                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1826






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






BharatNet Project in M.P




Sandhya Ray














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the district-wise details of the implementation of BharatNet project to achieve the target fixed in Madhya Pradesh;(b)	the number of broadband connections in Gram Panchayats (GPs) in Madhya Pradesh particularly in Bhind Parliamentary Constituency; and(c) 	the time by which the said work is likely to be completed by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	BharatNet project is implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country including Madhya Pradesh. The district-wise details of the implementation of BharatNet project in Madhya Pradesh are enclosed as Annexure-I.(b)	As of now, 16935 GPs are Service Ready in Madhya Pradesh including 335 GPs of Bhind Parliamentary Constituency (consisting of Bhind and Datia Districts) under BharatNet project. The infrastructure created under the project is a national asset, accessible on a non-discriminatory basis to the Service Providers, and the same can be utilized for provisioning of broadband/internet services.  (c)	The scope of BharatNet on 30.06.2021 was extended upto all inhabited villages beyond GPs. The target date for completion of the project is August 2023.
