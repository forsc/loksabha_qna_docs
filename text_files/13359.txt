                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Adarsh Station Scheme




Sumalatha Ambareesh













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of railway stations identified for development under Adarsh Station Scheme in the State of Karnataka during the last three years and the current year; (b) whether all the selected railway stations under Adarsh Station Scheme are upgraded/modernized completely, if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor along with the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) :  Adarsh Station Scheme has been started since 2009-10. In the state of Karnataka, 44 stations were identified for development under this scheme, out of which 42 stations so far have been developed and the remaining stations are targeted to be developed by 2020-21. However, no station has been identified for development under Adarsh Station scheme in the State of Karnataka during the last three years and the current year.        Upgradation/modernization is a continuous and on-going process depending upon need, volume of passenger traffic and inter-se priority, subject to availability of funds.******
