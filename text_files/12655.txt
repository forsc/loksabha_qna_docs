                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1429






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Schemes for Pisciculture




Rama Devi













                                                    Dulal Chandra Goswami
                                                








                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	  whether the farmers engaged in pisciculture have been covered under Kisan Samman Yojana in the country, if so, the details of the beneficiaries State-wise; (b) 	whether any separate scheme has been formulated by the Union Government for upliftment of farmers engaged in pisciculture in Bihar;(c)	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (d)	  the amount of funds allocated to Bihar for pisciculture during the last three years along with the amount utilized in this regard; (e)	 the details of the facilities provided to the fishermen under the scheme relating to pisciculture; (f)	 the details of development made in Bihar regarding employment generation and fish production during the last three years; and (g) 	the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) : 	 All landholding farmers'' families having cultivable land in their names are eligible to get benefit under the Kisan Samman Yojana. Fisheries including pisciculture activities are not covered under the Kisan Samman Yojana. (b) to (d): The Department of Fisheries (DoF), Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has a Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on ‘Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries’ for development of fisheries in the country including pisciculture and it provides financial assistance for the purpose. The Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has approved the proposals of Government of Bihar for development of fisheries including pisciculture and post harvest infrastructure at a total cost of Rs.318.47 crore under the CSS during past three years (2016-17 to 2018-19). Out of the total central liability of Rs. 122.34 crore, a sum of Rs.60.2925 crore has already been released to the Government of Bihar during the past three years. Beside this, National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB) also provided a sum of Rs. 4.44 crore to various institutions and organizations for development of wetland, ornamental fisheries, demonstration of improved fish varieties and post-harvest infrastructure in the State. Out of this, the Government of Bihar has utilized an amount of Rs.22.46 crore for the purpose during the said period. (e) to (g): The central financial assistance has been provided to the Government of Bihar for establishment of fish seed hatcheries/prawn hatcheries, construction of new ponds, fish seed rearing unit, development of wetlands, cage/pen culture, whole sale fish markets, retail fish markets and housing for fishers. Besides, fishermen are supplied with nets, boats, fish transport vehicles, exposure visits and training and ornamental fisheries.   The Government of Bihar, during the last three years, has created 1811.27 hectares of water area for fish culture under different schemes and around 13,484 persons employed.
