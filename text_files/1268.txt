                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1113






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Data Science




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government in order to unleash the untapped potential of data science has been suitably assisting the start-ups which are working on the Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence; and(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the procedure which is to be adopted by the start-ups for getting financial assistance and projects?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????; ??????? ?? ???????????? ??????; ?? ?????? ??????? ??????(a) & (b):  Yes, Sir. The Department of Science and Technology, Government of India has been assisting the start-ups including those working on the Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence through its 140 Technology Business Incubators (TBIs).  The start-ups have to register themselves in any of the TBIs to compete for grants and seed support for their projects.                                           ********
