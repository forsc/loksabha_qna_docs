                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2376






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Doubling of Thanjavur-Villupuram Rail Line




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether  the Railways is planning  to undertake doubling work of Thanjavur-Villupuram single line section as train traffic of the section has reached saturation limit;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Railways is likely to take necessary steps to allot necessary funds to undertake and complete the said doubling work in an expeditious manner; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): Preliminary survey for doubling of Villupuram - Mayiladuthurai – Thanjavur (193 Km) has been sanctioned in 2019. Field survey has been completed and preparation of survey report has been taken up. Further consideration of the project is possible once survey report is received and results thereon including traffic requirement and financial viability are firmed up.*****
