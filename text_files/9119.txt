                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2621






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Engineering Clusters under MIIUS




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any engineering cluster has been set up under the Modified IndustrialInfrastructure Upgradation Scheme (MIIUS) in Gujarat;(b) 	if so, the district, location and date-wise clusters set up and the funds  sanctioned/allocated for this purpose; and(c) 	the names of the clusters that have started functioning and their objectives thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	No, Sir, Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under Ministry of Commerce & Industry has not set up any engineering cluster under Modified Industrial Infrastructure Upgradation Scheme (MIIUS) in Gujarat. (b) & (c):   Do not arise.*****
