                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Indo-French Knowledge Summit




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Indo-French Knowledge Summit was held in the country recently;and(b) if so, the details thereof along with the details of the joint declaration or MoUsduring the summit?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)a) No, Sir. The second edition of Indo French Knowledge Summit was not held in India but atthe University of Lyon, France on October 17 & 18, 2019.b) Based on the information provided by the Ministry of External Affairs, the following MoUswere exchanged between Indian and French institutions on co-operation in research,education and training during the above summit:i. An Indo French Alliance of higher education institutions was created. The FrenchMembers of this alliance are: École Nationale Supérieure des Mines d’Albi-Carmaux (IMT Mines Albi), National Polytechnic Institute of Toulouse (INP)Toulouse, Polytech Nantes, and Polytech Orleans. The French InstitutionalMembers are N+I, Alliance Francaise Chandigarh / Delhi. The Indian UniversityMembers are University of Petroleum & Energy Studies (UPES) Uttarakhand,Mody University Rajasthan, Jaipuria Institute of Management Uttar Pradesh, andVellore Institute of Technology (VIT) Tamil Nadu.ii. MoU between Dassault Skill Academy - College of Engineering Pune;iii. MoU between School of Planning and Architecture (SPA) Delhi - Ecole NationaleSupérieure D''architecture (ENSA), Paris, Belleville;iv. MoU between University of Calcutta - Institute of Planetology and Astrophysicsfrom Grenoble (IPAG);v. MoU between International School of Engineering (INSOFE) - Rennes School ofBusiness;vi. MoU between Réseau Figure- Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS),Pilani;vii. MoU between Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI), Mumbai and InstitutMines-Télécom (IMT), Atlantique;viii. MoU between École Nationale Supérieure De Mécanique Et D''aérotechnique(ISAE-ENSMA) – College of Engineering Pune;ix. MoU between Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Indore- National PolytechnicInstitute of Toulouse (NPI); andx. MoU between CEPT University (formerly the Centre for Environmental Planningand Technology), Ahmedabad - École Nationale Supérieure D''architecture(ENSA), Toulouse.*****
