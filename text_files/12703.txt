                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1389






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Incentives to Open Pharma Factories




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has offered/is planning to offer incentives to foreign companies to set up stents/ other pharma factories in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objective thereto; (c) the time by which the said decision is likely to be implemented/come into force; (d) whether the said policy of the Government will also provide good quality product at low cost in Indian markets; and (e) the steps taken/likely to be taken by the Government to protect the domestic companies which have been already set up in the country?






ANSWER




(a): No such decision has been taken by the Department.(b) to (e): In view of reply to (a) above, the question does not arise.xxxxx
