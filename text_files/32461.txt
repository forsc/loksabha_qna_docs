                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3894






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Funds for MGNREGS and Construction Work in Bihar




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the funds sanctioned for Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in the country for the current year have been released;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the funds sanctioned for MGNREGS and construction work in Bihar particularly in Karakat Parliamentary Constituency have been released;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b) Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme and funds are released to the States/UTs on the basis of "agreed to" Labour Budget (LB) and performance of the States/UTs during the financial year. Fund release to States/UTs is a continuous process and Central Government is committed in making funds available keeping in view the demand for work on the ground. State/UT-wise details of funds sanctioned/released under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in current financial years 2021-22 (as on 16.12.2021) is given at Annexure.  (c) to (e)   The funds under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is released as per the provisions of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act and guidelines. Wage payment is directly released into the accounts of beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) and funds for material component are released to the State Treasury under the Scheme. Under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS no fund is directly released to District/ Constituency. *****
