                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4932






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Setting up of Common Facility Centre in Rice Mill Cluster




Manjulata Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the timeline for implementation of Common Facility Centre (CFC) in Rice Mill Cluster at Balasore would be revised in the corrected administrative approval and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the grant for above purpose would be released by the Government during the current financial year and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	The approved guidelines of the Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP) permit the revision of the timelines for setting up of Common Facility Centres (CFCs) if the need arises.(b):	There is a prescribed procedure for release of grants under the MSE-CDP as per which grant can be released only on submission of request from the State Government / Implementing Agency along with the requisite documents.
