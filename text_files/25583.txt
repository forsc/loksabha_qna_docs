                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1434






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Beautification of Railway Stations in Uttar Pradesh




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans for beautification of the railway stations in Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the time by when the work in this regard is likely to commence; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.****** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1434 BY SHRI BHOLANATH (B.P. SAROJ) TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 28.07.2021 REGARDING BEAUTIFICATION OF RAILWAY STATIONS IN UTTAR PRADESH.(a) to (c) Presently, Railway stations are beautified/upgraded under ‘Adarsh’ Station Scheme. Various passenger amenities which inter-alia include improvement to façade of the station building, retiring room, waiting room (with bathing facilities), separate waiting room for ladies, landscaping of circulating area, earmarked parking, signages, Pay & Use toilets, Foot Over Bridge, ramps at entry to station for physically handicapped and senior citizens etc., separate parking for Persons with Disabilities (Divyangjan) are proposed to be provided at railway stations which are identified for development under this scheme as per the respective category of station. 152 Railway stations in the State of Uttar Pradesh have been identified for development under ‘Adarsh’ Station Scheme. Out of these, 131 Railway stations have been developed as per norms. Remaining 21 Railway stations have been planned for development in Financial Year 2021-22.  *****
