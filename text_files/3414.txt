                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3143






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Schemes Benefiting Non-minorities




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of schemes of the Ministry which benefit non-minorities also 	along with the rationale for such schemes; and (b)	 the expenditure incurred under such schemes during the last four years 	and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):- Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakaram (PMJVK) a Centrally Sponsored Scheme is for creating socio-economic and educational infrastructure as well as basic amenities in 1300 identified Minority Concentration Areas (MCAs) including various district head-quarters of the country for filling of the gap in development deficit areas and uplifting the quality of life of every section of the society.   PMJVK being an area development programme, the projects are located in areas having high concentration of needy minority population within the MCA so that the minorities benefit from the assets located close to their habitat. 	Under Nai Roshani Scheme for leadership development, women from non-minority community are also part of the projects for overall benefit of the society. 	Also non minority BPL beneficiaries may be included in USTTAD, Nai Manzil and Seekho aur Kamao schemes.(b): The details of Total expenditure incurred during the last four years and current year in such schemes are as under:-(Rupees in Crore)Scheme	Expenditure incurredPMJVK	4552.37Nai Manzil	304.49Seekho aur Kamao	771.94USTTAD	89.72*****
