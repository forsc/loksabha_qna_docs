                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1712






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Working Days under MGNREGS




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to increase the number of working days under MGNREGS in Andhra Pradesh and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the State Government of Andhra Pradesh has sent any proposal to the Union Government in this regard and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has taken any decision on such proposal; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	No, Sir. However, an additional 50 days of wage employment are provided over and above 100 days in the notified drought affected areas or natural calamity areas in the country on recommendation of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare. (b):	No, Sir.(c) to (d):	Do not arise.*****
