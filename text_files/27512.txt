                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Upgradation/Modernisation of Health Institutes under NHM




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to upgrade the National Institutes of Medical Science and Research under the National Health Mission and if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of medical colleges and institutes identified for Upgradation and modernization, State/UT-wise; and(c) the details of the infrastructure proposed to be developed and additional facilities proposed to be provided in such Institutes?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a):No such proposal is under consideration under the National Health Mission (NHM). Further, Public Health & Hospitals, being a State subject, primary responsibility to strengthen public health infrastructure lies with the respective State Government. The Central Government, however, provides technical and financial support under National Health Mission (NHM) to the States for improving their primary and secondary level healthcare infrastructurei.e. up to District Hospital, based on proposals received from respective States through Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs). (b) & (c): Upgradation of Government Medical Colleges (GMCs) is one of the components under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY). So far 75 projects of upgradation of GMCs have been approved under the Scheme. Upgradation of GMCs under PMSSY broadly involves construction of Super Speciality Block (SSB) and/or Trauma Centre / or other facilities and/or procurement of medical equipment.The details of medical colleges and institutes approved for upgradation along with approved infrastructure in such Institutes are at Annexure.
