                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2735






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






GSP Benefits




Y. S. Avinash Reddy













                                                    Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the US Government is ending the GSP benefits granted to India in 1976 and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether any discussions have taken place in this regard during the recently held Shanghai Cooperation Organisation summit; (c)	if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether it is true that India is contemplating on joining China-Russia call for new Trading System; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	  Yes, sirUS has withdrawn the GSP benefits to India with effect from 5th June, 2019 vide its Presidential proclamation dated 31st May, 2019(b) 		No.(c)		Not Applicable(d)		No.(e)		Not Applicable***
