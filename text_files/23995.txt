                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4899






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Extraction of Metals under Vehicle Scrappage Policy




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the proposed vehicle scrappage policy is likely to help in extracting metals such as lithium, copper, plastic, rubber which will help to meet the demands of lithium needed for producing electric vehicles and various other purposes;(b) 	if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c)	whether any guidelines have been changed recently in this regard; and(d)     if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) The Government has issued GSR notification 190(E) dated 15.03.2021 that provides for Motor Vehicles (Registration and Functions of Vehicle Scrapping Facility) Rules, 2021 for establishment of Registered Vehicles Scrapping Facility (RVSF). RVSF shall process scrap material as well as waste materials. In case the RVSF does not have adequate capability/provisions for responsible recycling of hazardous waste (like e-waste, lead acid batteries, lithium ion components), or for recovery of rare earth metals, etc., or for recycling of scrap material which is outside its scope, then such materials are to be sold to duly authorized recyclers/agencies, who have adequate capability and licence.*****
