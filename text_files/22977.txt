                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4009






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Jute Mills in Tamil Nadu




T.R.V.S. Ramesh














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether investments have been made in the handloom, textiles, and handicrafts sector in Tamil Nadu since 2019; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the number of textile/jute mills in Tamil Nadu, area-wise; (d) whether the Government has any proposal for establishing new textile/jute mills in Tamil Nadu and revive the sick and closed mills as part of Public Sector Undertakings restructuring or through PPP Model; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b):	Investment in the textile sector is by and large in the private sector. Financial assistance in the form of grants-in-aid is provided by Government of India to the eligible handloom/textiles/handicrafts agencies/weavers/artisans for raw materials, purchase of looms and accessories, design innovation, product diversification, infrastructure development, skill upgradation, marketing in domestic as well as overseas markets. No state-wise investments data is maintained by Government of India.   (c):	There are 979 cotton and man-made textiles mills(Non SSI) at various locations in Tamil Nadu. Government of India is operating 7 National Textile Corporation (NTC) Limited mills in the state of Tamil Nadu at various locations viz. Kamudakudi, Kalayarkoil, Coimbatore(5 nos.). (d) & (e):	The role of the government is to create a conducive environment and provide enabling conditions for textile manufacturing in the country through its various textile schemes aimed at promotion/upgradation of textile industries/units. There is no proposal at present for revival of sick and closed mills. ***
