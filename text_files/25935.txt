                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Allocation of Funds to Chhattisgarh




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The funds allocated/released by the Union Government to Chhattisgarh to make drinking water available during the last two years;(b)	Whether Chhattisgarh has provided any information/progress report to the Union Government regarding works launched by the State to make drinking water available;(c)	If so, the details thereof; and(d)	The relevant rules to send a progress report to the Union Government by the State Government of Chhattisgarh regarding drinking water supply? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d) The year-wise allocation of Central fund under Jal Jeevan Mission to Chhattisgarh to make provision of potable tap water supply to rural households, schools, anganwadi centres, ashramshalas, etc. in the last two years and current year is as under:(Amount in Rs. Crore)Financial Year	Opening Balance	Allocation	Funds drawn by the State Government2019-20	31.58	208.04	65.822020-21	58.17	445.52	334.142021-22	168.52	1,908.96	*453.71*as on 25.07.2021All States/ UTs including Chhattisgarh, report the progress on Jal Jeevan Mission– Integrated Management Information System (JJM - IMIS). The daily progress of tap water connections given to households is available in public domain on JJM dashboard at https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx. Further, this ministry regularly reviews the implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission with States/ UTs including Chhattisgarh. *****
