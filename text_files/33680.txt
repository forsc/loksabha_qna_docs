                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1102






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Implementation of PMFBY




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of success achieved in the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana(PMFBY) in the last three years;(b) the details of the total number of farmers covered under (PMFBY) from the year 2018 till date, year and State-wise;(c) the number of premium amount collected by the insurance companies from various States including Maharashtra from the year 2018 till date and the insurance claims paid by the insurance companies;(d) the average insurance payments made to beneficiaries in each State in the financial year 2020-21 under the said scheme;(e) the state-wise number of pending claims of the farmers in the country including Maharashtra from the year 2018 till date along with the reasons for delay in making payment to the farmers; and(f) the number of farmers not covered under this scheme particularly in Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (f) : The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) introduced from Kharif 2016 season in the country is voluntary for States/UTs as well as farmers.  The scheme is available for all States/UTs and all farmers for crops and areas notified by the concerned State Government.  Since inception of the scheme in 2016-17 till 2020-21, against premium of Rs. 21531 crore paid by farmers, claims of Rs. 104196  crore have been paid to 901 lakh farmer applications.   Year-wise and State-wise details of number of farmer applications enrolled, farmers share in premium,  total premium collected, Claims reported, claims pending, reasons for pendency  and claims paid per farmer application during last three years i.e. from 2018-19 to 2020-21 are annexed.
