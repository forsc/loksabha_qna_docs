                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Tribal Sub-Scheme in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of amount spent by the Government in Jharkhand during the last three years under Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-scheme for development of tribals in the Integrated Tribal Development Projects area, district-wise;(b)  	whether  any  proposal  to  include  any  area  in Jharkhand especially in Giridih,  Bokaro and Dhanbad districts  under  the  above  scheme  is  pending  with  the Government; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No.*266 for answer on 15.03.2021(a):	Funds are provided to the States by Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) under the Scheme ‘Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS)’, inter alia, for development of Scheduled Tribes in the Integrated Tribal Development Projects (ITDP) area. The details of the funds released and Utilization Certificate submitted by the Government of Jharkhand during the last three years under the scheme is as under:(Rs. in lakh)Years	Amount Spent	Utilization Certificate Submitted by State Government2017-18	11372.49	10174.602018-19	8564.52	8393.962019-20	6201.49	5980.67As informed by the State Government of Jharkhand, details of amount spent under the scheme is at Annexure.(b) & (c):As per extant guidelines, inter-district allocation of funds under the Scheme ‘SCA to TSS’ shall be the responsibility of State Government based on gaps in various sectors of development while focusing on priority districts identified by MoTAand aspirational districts identified by NITI Aayog. Giridih and Bokaro districts are already among the list of priority districts recognized by MoTA.
