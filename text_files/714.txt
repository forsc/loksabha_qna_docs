                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

603






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Distribution under PDS




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of the foodgrains and other commodities which are being distributed from the Government ration shops under Public Distribution System (PDS) including the quantity thereof; (b) 	the number of ration cardholders under Public Distribution System in the country, State-wise; (c) 	whether the cases of foodgrains and other commodities not being given to the consumers from Government ration shops under PDS in full quantity have come to the notice of the Government; (d) 	if so, the measures being taken by the Government to solve this problem; and (e) 	whether the Government is formulating any plan to provide cereals and other commodities to the ration cardholders in additional quantity and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI RAMVILAS PASWAN)(a): 	Under National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA), the eligible households are entitled to receive foodgrains, namely- Wheat, Rice & Coarse-grains through Public Distribution System (PDS). As per entitlements under the Act, the Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) households are eligible to receive 35 kg of foodgrains per family per month, whereas the Priority Households (PHH) beneficiaries are eligible to receive 5 kg of foodgrains per person per month........2/--2-		Distribution of subsidized sugar of 1 Kg per AAY family per month is also continued under PDS.		Further, clause 9(9) of Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) (Control) Order, 2015, empowers the State/UT Governments to allow sale of commodities other than distribution of foodgrains through Fair Price Shops to improve the viability of the fair price shop operations.  (b):	As per the information received from States/UTs upto 22.05.2019 a Statement indicating State-wise total No. of ration cardholders is at Annex-I.  (c) & (d): 	Few complaints about foodgrains and other commodities not being given to the consumers in full quantity from Government ration shops under PDS have come to the notice of the Government in some State/UTs.  Therefore, as and when such complaints are received by the Government, these are referred to the concerned State/UT Governments for inquiry and appropriate action.
