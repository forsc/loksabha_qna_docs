                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3207






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Model Town and Country Planning Act




 Hema Malini














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether most of the States are dependent on the Model Town and Country Planning Act, 1960 of the Government for urban planning;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether there is any proposal to carry out amendment in the said Act; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                                                     ANSWER                                THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                             MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) & (b):   Model Town and Country Planning Act, 1960 issued by Government of India was amended in 1985 titled as Model Regional and Town Planning and Development Law, 1985. This model law was issued for the guidance of the States and Union territories for preparing their Town and Country Planning Acts. (c): No Sir.(d):  Question does not arise.                                                ****
