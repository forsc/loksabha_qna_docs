                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1024






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Dialysis Centres in AP




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of dialysis patients in the country, State/UT and district-wise;(b) whether Government has noticed that patients requiring dialysis in Andhra Pradesh are going up and if so, the details thereof;(c) when the Government of Andhra Pradesh submitted proposals for establishment of Haemo dialysis centres at Jaggayapeta and Pulivendula and peritoneal dialysis services in three other centres in Andhra Pradesh under National Health Mission (NHM); and(d) the steps taken by the Ministry in approving the above proposal this year?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a): Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri National Dialysis Programme (PMNDP) under the National Health Mission (NHM) for providing free of cost dialysis services to poor patients. State-wise list of patients registered for dialysis services under PMNDP as on 31st July, 2020 is at Annexure.(b to d): State Government of Andhra Pradesh has informed that number of patients who availed dialysis services in Government facilities has increased from 4,038 in 2017 to 7,812 in 2018 and to 12,168 in 2019. State Government has further informed that dialysis centres have been set up in Pulivendula and Jaggayapeta. Peritoneal dialysis services have been sanctioned by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare under NHM.
