                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






NHAI Projects in Rajasthan and Maharashtra




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                     Bhagirath Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the length in kilometers of new highway project approved by 	National Highway Authority of India (NHAI) since the year 	2014 including the number of projects completed and under 	construction as on date in the country; (b)	whether new highways have been constructed in Rajasthan 	and Maharashtra as part of the said projects of NHAI and if 	so, the details thereof, location and kilometer-wise along with 	the funds allocated and utilised thereon so far; (c)	the time frame set for completion of the above mentioned 	approved and under construction projects; (d)	whether any proposal from Rajasthan and Maharashtra (MH) 	is pending with the Union Government regarding the road 	projects including Madha Parliamentary Constituency (MH); 	and (e)	if so, the time by which these proposals are likely to be 	approved? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) Total 26717 km length of National Highways has been awarded by National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) since 2014. Out of this, works in a length of 16394 km have already been completed and balance works in 10323 km are under implementation.(b) & (c) During this period, total 68 projects with aggregate length of about 2615 km having sanctioned cost of Rs. 42073 crore have been envisaged in the State of Maharashtra covering 26 National Highways. Out of 68 projects, 08 projects with aggregate length of 491 km having total cost of Rs. 6890 crore have been completed and work for remaining 60 projects is under implementation. All these projects targeted for completion by March, 2023. The overall expenditure incurred on these projects is about Rs. 22908 crore.Total 65 projects with aggregate length of about 4417 km having sanctioned cost of Rs. 41112 crore have been envisaged in the State of Rajasthan covering 22 National Highways. Out of 65 projects, 18 projects with aggregate length of 2029 km having total cost of Rs. 7229 crore have been completed and work for remaining 47 projects is under implementation. All these projects targeted for completion by January, 2023. The overall expenditure incurred on these projects is about Rs. 19685 crore. (d) & (e) National Highway projects have been taken up for construction depending upon project preparation as well as readiness of the proposal in terms of pre-construction activities. As such no proposal is pending with NHAI.
