                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2855






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Poor Condition of Highways




Mohanbhai Sanjibhai Delkar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any assessment is being made by the Government regarding the poor condition of Highways in the country, and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(b) whether the Government has identified such highways where number of road accidents  are increasing and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has issued any guidelines regarding the use of state-of-the-art network survey vehicles for this purpose and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the highways have been identified for using such vehicles and if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the highway towards Dadra and Nagar Haveli (UT) is covered under the identified highways and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e) The assessment of condition on stretches of National Highways is carried out before taking up the development and maintenance works. 789 black spots have been identified based on accidents/ fatalities from the year 2011 to 2014. Recently Ministry has issued guidelines for the use of Network Survey Vehicle (NSV) for assessing the road conditions for National Highway projects involving widening/ strengthening etc. in the country including Dadra and Nagar Haveli(UT).*****
