                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Modernisation of Post Offices




Debasree Chaudhuri














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has undertaken any programme for computerisation and modernisation of Post Offices in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including West Bengal; (c) 	the number of Post Offices computerised as on date in each State including West Bengal; and (d) 	the number of Post Offices yet to be computerized?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS & ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF THE LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 2 FOR 2ND FEBRUARY, 2022 REGARDING ‘MODERNISATION OF POST OFFICES’.(a) 	Yes, Sir. (b) & (c) 	Under the Information Technology Modernisation Project, the Department of Posts has computerised and modernised Post offices to provide better delivery of services. Details are as follows: All the 25,109 Departmental Post Offices in the country including 1,770 Departmental Post Offices in West Bengal Postal Circle, have been computerised. The State wise list including UTs is at Annexure-I. The Department has also modernised 1,29,238 Branch Post Offices including 7,249 Branch Post Offices in West Bengal by providing Subscriber Identification Module (SIM) based Hand Held Point of Sale Devices, for carrying out online postal and financial services. The State wise details including UTs is at Annexure-II. Core Banking Services (CBS) have been provided in 25006 Post Offices in the country including 1,737 Post Offices in West Bengal Circle. The State wise list including UTs is at Annexure-III. This facility enables customers to transact their Post Office Savings Bank (POSB) accounts from any Post Office across the country. Besides there are interoperable Post Office ATMs available in 1000 locations including 65 locations of West Bengal Postal Circle. State wise list including UTs is at    Annexure-IV. Postal Life Insurance (PLI) services are being provided through Core Insurance Solution (CIS). Through this solution, the customers can make premium payment online as well as at any Post Office. It has also enabled Central Processing Centres (CPCs) to provide faster claim settlement and after-sales service. (d) 	All 25109 Departmental Post Offices have been computerised.
