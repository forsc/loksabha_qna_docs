                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

6






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS)




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to further publicise the NMMS Scheme across the country including Bhopal Parliamentary Constituency;(b) if so, the details of the plan being prepared in this regard and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) the number of seats available under NMMS Scheme in Madhya Pradesh including Bhopal Parliamentary Constituency along with the number of such seats proposed to be increased, please also give, State-wise, details?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN PARTS (a) to (c) OF THE REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *6 ASKED BY SADHVI PRAGYA SINGH THAKUR REGARDING NATIONAL MEANS-CUM-MERIT SCHOLARSHIP (NMMS) SCHEME FOR ANSWER ON 29.11.2021(a) &(b): This Ministry issues directions to all State Governments & UT administrations to give wide publicity to the National Means-Cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS) through print/electronic media and through schools and conduct the selection test for which re-imbursement is provided to the State/UT. It is continuous endeavour of the Ministry to give further impetus to the Scheme through wide publicity for maximum coverage.(c): The total number of fresh scholarships under NMMSS is 1,00,000 at entry level in class IX. Allocation of seats under NMMSS is done State/UT-wise and not Parliamentary Constituency wise. Number of seats available at entry level in Class IX under NMMS Scheme in Madhya Pradesh State is 6446. Each State and UT has fixed allotted quota of scholarship. State/UT-wise allotted quota is given at Annexure-A.*****
