                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Criteria for Establishment of PHCs/ Hospitals




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the details of the criteria for establishing new Primary Health Centre (PHCs)/hospital in the country; (b) the hospital to population ratio in the country;(c) whether there is sufficient number of hospitals as per population in Assam, if so, the details thereof and if not, the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (d) the other efforts made by the Government to open new PHC/hospital/ super hospital HPC area of Assam, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):    One PHC for every 30,000 rural population in the plains and one PHC for every 20,000 population in hilly, tribal and desert areas, is required to be established. (b):      As per National Health Profile, 2018, one Govt. hospital serves 55591 persons on an average.(c ) & (d):    As per Rural Health Statistics(RHS), 2018, in the State of Assam, there is requirement of 5850 Sub-health Centres (SCs), 954 Primary Health Centres (PHCs),  238 Community Health Centres(CHCs) and 14 Sub-District Hospitals (SDHs) as per population norms, whereas, 4644 SCs, 946 PHCs, 172 CHCs, are available.   14 Sub-District Hospitals are functioning, which are as per requirement.  Hence there is shortfall of 21% SCs, 1% PHCs and 28% CHCs in the State of Assam.Public Health and Hospitals being a State subject, the primary responsibility for providing quality healthcare services to the population lies with the State government.  Under NHM,  support is also provided to the States/UTs for strengthening their healthcare delivery system including setting up/upgradation of SCs, PHCS, CHCs, SDH and DH etc. based on the requirement posed by the States/UTs in their Programme Implementation Plans.Details of New constructions works approved under NHM (as on Dec.2018) in the State of Assam, is given as per Annexure-I. The details of renovation/upgradation works approved under NHM (as on Dec.2018) in the State of Assam is given as per Annexure-II. In addition, in Record of Proceedings(RoP) for  Financial Year 2019-20, the  following works for new construction/upgradation/strengthening of health facilities,  have been  approved:1.	Construction of 1 new PHC- Dawdhara at Baksa district, Assam. 2.	New upgradations: (i) Upgradation of Dalanibasti Sub centre to PHC including residential quarter in Udalguri District at the Project Cost: Rs.137.50 Lakh(ii) Strengthening of Dotma CHC under Kokrajhar West (ST) LAC in Kokrajhar District at the Project Cost: Rs. 117.99 Lakh(iii) Strengthening of 542 new SC-HWC & 300 new PHC- HWCs.
