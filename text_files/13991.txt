                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2723






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Financial Grant to NGOs




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam













                                                    Ranjeeta Koli
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has granted financial aid to any NGO to eradicate malnutrition during the last three years;(b) 	if so, the details of such financial aid granted, NGO-wise;(c) 	the details of area of work for which the said NGOs have been granted such financial aid;(d) 	whether any cases of misappropriation of granted funds by these NGOs has come to the notice of the Government;(e) 	if so, the details thereof; and(f) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	Ministry of Women and Child Development does not directly give any financial aid to any NGO to eradicate malnutrition.(b) to (f) Does not arise.*****
