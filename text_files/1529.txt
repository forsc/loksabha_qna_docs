                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1410






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Institute of Eminence




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the status of the Institutes of Eminence in the field of education in thecountry;(b) whether private greenfield institutes will continue to be provided the tag of IoE ashas been the case previously;(c) whether any goals have been set to achieve in the Programme for InternationalStudent Assessment (PISA) of 2021;(d) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to achieve the said goals, at theCentral Schools level; and(e) the details of training being imparted to teachers for the PISA 2021?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The Central Government, after considering recommendation of UniversityGrants Commission (UGC) on the report of Empowered Expert Committee, issuedNotifications to 3 Public Institutions namely Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore;Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi & Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay. Apartfrom this, the Government has issued Letters of Intent to 3 Private Institutions namelyManipal Academy of Higher Education, Karnataka; Birla Institute of Technology &Science, Pilani under brown field category and Jio Institute under green field categoryfor setting up/up-gradation of Institution of Eminence Deemed to be University within aperiod of 3 years. ‘Institutions of Eminence’ tag can be used by these PrivateInstitutions only after issuance of Notification by this Ministry declaring them asInstitution of Eminence Deemed to be University.(c) & (d): The Government of India has signed an agreement with the Organization forEconomic Cooperation and Development (OECD) to participate in the 2021administration of its Programme for International Student Assessment (PISA). PISA is atriennial international survey that aims to evaluate education systems worldwide bytesting the skills and knowledge of 15 year old students in Mathematics, Science andReading. It is a competency based assessment which unlike content based assessment,measures the extent to which students have acquired key competencies that areessential for full participation in modern societies. It has been decided that the KendriyaVidyalaya (KVs) and Navodaya Vidyalaya (NVs) & Schools of Chandigarh Administrationwould participate in PISA 2021. The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) andNational Council of Education Research and Training (NCERT) will be part of theprocess and activities leading to the actual test.(e): Ministry of Human Resource Development has approved capacity building activitiesfor teachers for enhancing their technical skills and capacity to orient students for PISA2021.*****
