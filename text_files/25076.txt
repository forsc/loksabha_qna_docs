                                    FINANCE

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1013






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Incentives for GST Payment




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FINANCE be pleased to state:(a) whether it is true that the Government is awarding/ giving incentives to those companies paying the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regularly and properly to the Government; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the manner and method to be adopted to give incentives to those companies paying GST properly; and (d) the total number of such companies which were paid incentives since the implementation of GST, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE (a) to (d) Yes, the Government has awarded Appreciation Certificates, on the eve of completion of 4 years of GST, to 54,439 (Fifty-four thousand four hundred and thirty-nine) taxpayers who have made substantial contribution in payment of GST and filed their returns in a timely manner.
