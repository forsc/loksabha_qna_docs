UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2997






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Cases of Domestic Violence




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  	whether the Government has issued any directions for the disposal of cases notified under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005 within 6 months;(b)  	if so, the details thereof; and(c)  	the number of cases that are pending for more than 6 months, State/UT-wise including Bihar? 






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b): Section 12(5) of the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005 (PWDVA) provides that the Magistrate shall endeavour to dispose of every application seeking one or more reliefs under this Act within a period of sixty days from the date of its first hearing.  (c):  National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes information on crimes in its publication “Crime in India” which is available on the website of NCRB https://ncrb.gov.in. The published reports are available till the year 2019.
