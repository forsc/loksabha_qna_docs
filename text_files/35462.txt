                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2885






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Status of Buddhist Circuit Project in Andhra Pradesh




Maddila  Gurumoorthy













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of the Buddhist Circuit project in Andhra Pradesh; (b)	whether full funds under the aforementioned project have been released and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c)	the details of the financial assistance provided to Andhra Pradesh under Swadesh Darshan and Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual Augmentation Drive schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	The Ministry of Tourism under its ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme sanctioned a project in the year 2017-18 under  Buddhist  Circuit theme and the sanctioned cost of this project was  revised to Rs. 24.14 Cr. Full funds in  respect of this  project  has  been  released. (c):	The details of projects sanctioned in the State of Andhra Pradesh under the Ministry of Tourism’s schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive’ (PRASHAD) are annexed.********
