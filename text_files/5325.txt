                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4874






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






PMGSY in Kerala




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of roads currently being constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Kerala, particularly in the district of Kannur;(b)	whether the Government has introduced or proposes to introduce any new road projects under PMGSY in the district of Kannur;(c)	the number of houses being constructed under Pradhan Mantri AwaasYojana (Gramin) [PMAY (G)] in the district of Kannur; and(d)	the total number of beneficiaries belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes under PMAY(G) in Kannur?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), 131 roads including 2 bridges are being constructed in Kerala, out of which 10 roads and 2 Bridges are being constructed in Kannur district of Kerala.(b):	The entire allocation under PMGSY-I and PMGSY-II have been sanctioned to Kerala State. Further, no proposal is pending with Ministry in respect of Kerala State including Kannur district.(c):	The No. of houses constructed under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) in the district of Kannur as reflected in AwaasSoft(MIS) as on 19.07.2019 are 669.(d):	The total no. of beneficiaries finalized in Permanent Wait List (PWL) under PMAY-G belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes in Kannur District, as reflected in AwaasSoft (MIS), as on 19.07.2019 is 240 (SCs: 106 + STs: 134).  *****
