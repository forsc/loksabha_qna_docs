                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Air Defence Project




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has planned to start an air defence and weaponry project along with construction of helipad in and around Gulmarg Wildlife Sanctuary, in UT of Jammu & Kashmir;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the National Board for Wildlife (NBWL) has given its full fledged approval for the above project and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government proposes to setup a radar system in the above project to keep an eye on Pakistan-occupied Kashmir and the Line of Control;(e) if so, the details thereof; and(f) the other steps being taken by the Government for strengthening of defence system in Jammu and Kashmir during the last one year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  to (f):  The information is being collected and will be placed on the Table of the House.*****
