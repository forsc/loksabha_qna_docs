                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

89






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Funds for establishing Libraries in Haryana




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of libraries opened by the Government in the country including Haryana;(b)	the location-wise details of the libraries opened in Haryana;(c)	whether the Government proposes to open libraries in all districts of Haryana;(d)	if so, the time by which these libraries are likely to be opened; and(e)	the details of the yearly funds earmarked for one library?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Sir, as per Seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India Library is a state subject and opening of new library comes under the purview of the respective State/Union Territory authorities. However, Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata, an autonomous organization under Ministry of Culture provides financial assistance under its matching and non-matching schemes for development of public libraries for various purposes eg. books, furniture & equipments, modernization, construction of library buildings, mobile library services, establishment of children corner, facilities for specially-abled readers, seminars/workshops etc. on the basis of applications recommended by the respective State Library Authorities. (b) & (e) 	In view of the (a) above, does not arise. ***
