                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Central Equipment Identity Register




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the list of departments which will have access to the Central Equipment Identity Register (CEIR); (b) 	whether private firms will have access to the CEIR and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	whether the Government has informed and obtained consent of the mobile phone owners to share their data with other departments, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c) 	Central Equipment Identity Register (CEIR) is a multi-stakeholder system where respective stakeholders including Department of Telecommunications, Telecom Service Providers, Police, Mobile phone manufacturers and mobile phone subscribers have a limited access as per defined roles and responsibilities. In this project, Mobile phone owners’ data is not shared with other departments.************
