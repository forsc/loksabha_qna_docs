                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






NEET




Dean Kuriakose













                                                    Thomas Chazhikadan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has a list of total number of students who appeared for the NEET-PGexam from the State of Kerala and the number of exam centres that were allotted for the examin the State, the details thereof;(b) whether due to shortage of exam centres in the State of Kerala, many students were allottedcentres in neighbouring States;(c) whether the Ministry has plans/ proposals to increase the number of test centres for the NEETPGexamination in the State in order to reduce the hardships caused to students; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Yes, Sir. The information has been collected from Ministry of Health and Family Welfare andas per information, 10637 candidates were scheduled in Kerala for NEET – PG 2019. The test washeld in 12 cities across the 57 test centres.(b): No, Sir. All candidate opting for Kerala or residing in the State of Kerala were allotted centerin the State of Kerala in NEET PG 2019.(c) & (d): For NEET PG 2020, test shall be conducted across 12 cities in the State of Kerala. Atotal of 11851 test seats have been allocated for the State of Kerala which is 1214 more incomparison to NEET – PG 2019. Considering the increase in the number of candidates from theState of Kerala as compared to last year, additional test centres shall be allocated in NEET PG 2020so as to accommodate most of the applicants from the State of Kerala within the State itself.***
