                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

338






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Increase in Farmers’ Suicides




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that there has been an increase in the number of cases of suicides by farmers in the country; (b) 	if so, the reasons therefor; and (c) 	if not, the details thereof during the last five years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 338  DUE FOR REPLY ON 16TH JULY, 2019.(a) to (c): 	The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) under the Ministry of Home Affairs compiles and disseminates information on suicides in its publication titled ‘Accidental Deaths and Suicides in India’ (ADSI). These Reports on suicides are available upto 2015 on its website. The Reports for the year 2016 onwards have not been published. The total number of suicides by farmers for the years 2014 and 2015 in the country State-wise as per the ADSI reports is annexed.
