                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

308






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Funds for Minority Education




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether people belonging to minority religions constitute about nineteen per cent of the total population of the country and if so, the details thereof along with the percentage of allocation made to the Ministry of Minority Affairs compared to the total budget of the Union Government; (b) whether it is a fact that only a negligible percentage of the total budget is allocated to the Ministry of Minority Affairs and if so, the details thereof; (c) the measures, if any, taken by the Government to address this issue and enhance allocation of funds; (d) whether the Government is aware that the budget allocation for education of minorities was drastically reduced in the current budget and if so, the steps taken by the Government to increase the allocation; (e) the funds allocated in the current budget for post-matric scholarship of the students belonging to minority communities; and (f) the details of the minority students receiving post-matric scholarship between 2014 and 2021, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)	(a) to (f):	A statement is laid on the table of the House.                                   *****
