                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

47






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






National Highway Projects




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the pace of National Highways (NHs) construction in the country has reached at 37 kms per day during 2020-21, if so, the details thereof; (b) the length of each NH in kilometres where construction has been undertaken from 2014 till date along with the funds sanctioned and the constraints posed due to Covid-19 pandemic as well as loss estimated as a result thereof so far; (c) whether the cumulative cost of ongoing projects has increased, if so, the details thereof as on date, State and project-wise and the total budgetary outlay increased for each project including revised budgetary estimates for the said period, year-wise; (d) the details of annual average award of NHs in length during the said period and average annual construction of NH length; and (e) the details of stalled projects entailing an investment as on date along with the reasons therefor and likely date of completion of each such stalled project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
