                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana aims to provide health cover to the population below the poverty line;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of targets set and achievements made under the Yojana during the last five years in the country particularly in Karnataka?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Yes. Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY), was being implemented to provide health cover up to Rs. 30,000/- per family (a unit of maximum five members) per annum to families belonging to BPL and 11 other defined categories namely Building & Other Construction Workers, licensed Railway porters, Street Vendors, MGNREGA workers, Beedi workers, Domestic workers, Sanitation workers, Mine workers, Rickshaw pullers, Rag pickers, and Auto/Taxi drivers.Senior Citizen’s Health Insurance Scheme (SCHIS) for Senior Citizens of age 60 years and above belonging to RSBY beneficiary families has also been implemented w.e.f. 01.04.2016 as a top-up scheme of RSBY. Health cover up to Rs. 30,000/- per annum per senior citizen over and above RSBY entitlement was being provided under SCHIS. With the launch of Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY), RSBY and SCHIS have been subsumed in it.(c):	Financial year wise details of targeted and enrolled number of beneficiaries under RSBY are at Annexure.
