                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1875






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Protection of Textile Industry




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that a Group of Ministers (GoM) constituted to boost India‘s exports has raised concerns about some foreign funded NGOs/groups lobbying against Indian products in the Indian textile and clothing sectors; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is formulating any measures to check this malicious campaign by foreignfunded organizations in order to protect the domestic textile industry;(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e) the other steps taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (e):	Certain Indian textile products are listed in the US Trafficking Victims Protection       Re-authorization Act (TVPRA) child  labour/ forced-labour List 2020. Steps have been taken through Embassy of India, USA for delisting of such products from the List.***
