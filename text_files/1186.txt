                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

964






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Setting up of MSMEs




Rampreet Mandal













                                                     Dharambir Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) have been set up in the country particularly under the MUDRA Yojana in the country during the last three years;(b)	if so, the total number of such units set up in the country during the year 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19, State/UT wise; (c)	the total value of the business transacted by above units during each of the said year; and(d)   	the total amount of subsidy released by the Union Government to the State Government of Haryana for setting up of MSMEs? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a)to(c):	Under MUDRA Yojana, loans  up to Rs.10 lakh are provided to micro units for non farm, income generating activities.  Details of loan accounts and amount sanctioned under the Scheme in last three financial years as per information on MUDRA portal is given in the table below:<pre>Category	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	No. of Accounts	Amount sanctioned	No. of Accounts	Amount sanctioned	No. of Accounts	Amount sanctioned	(lakhs)	(Rs. in crores)	(lakhs)	(Rs. in crores)	(lakhs)	(Rs. in crores)SHISHU	364.98	85,100.74	426.69	1,06,001.60	515.07	1,42,345.25KISHORE	26.64	53,545.14	46.53	86,732.16	66.06	1,04,386.68TARUN	5.39	41,882.66	8.06	60,943.34	17.58	74,990.86TOTAL	397.01	1,80,528.54	481.28	2,53,677.1	598.71	3,21,722.79</pre>(d): 	The margin money subsidy given to Haryana under Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme for setting up of MSME units was Rs.4664.1 lakh in 2018-19.
