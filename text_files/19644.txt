                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






University Grants Commission




Harsimrat Kaur Badal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government have reduced the grant given to University Grants Commission(UGC) and Government institutions engaged in technical academic or professional education; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the reasons and rationale behind the reduction of grants to educational institutes; and (d) the achievements made so far by the Government Government by reducing the grants?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The Ministry allocates funds in individual budget lines based upon the total funds allocated by the Ministry of Finance. The Revised Estimate (RE) for the University Grants Commission (UGC) and other Institutions under the Department of Higher Education is Rs.27646.02 crores and Rs.27913.14 crores for the Financial Years 2019-20 and 2020-21 respectively, thus marking an increase of Rs.267.12 crores.(b) to (d) There has been no reduction in the RE figures.*****
