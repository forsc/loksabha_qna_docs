                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

982






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






National Mission on Cultural Mapping




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                    Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government had undertaken a cultural mapping and roadmap under the National 	Mission on Cultural Mapping;(b)	 if so, the details thereof along with the objective of the Project ''Cultural Mapping of India'';(c) 	the total number of artists registered till date along with the list of benefits received by these artists 	under this project;(d) 	whether the process was undertaken in various States of the country including Andhra Pradesh;  (e)	 if so, the details of the cultures or villages that were mapped in the State of Andhra Pradesh and Tamil          Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)       Yes(b) 	Under this Mission, at broad-level, there are three important objectives as follows:i.	National Cultural Awareness Abhiyan : Hamari Sanskriti Hamari Pahchan AbhiyanOr Our Culture Our Identity ii.	Nationwide Artist Talent Hunt/Scouting Programme: Sanskritik Pratibha Khoj Abhiyan iii. 	National Cultural Workplace: Centralised Transactional Web Portal with database and demography of cultural assets and resources including all art forms and artists.(c)	Around 14.53 lakhs artists and artisans data from secondary sources. This data base is only a 	registry of artists, without guaranteed benefits.(d)	NMCM block level programs were conducted in Uttar Pradesh, Haryana, Jharkhand and Karnataka.(e)	Does not arise
