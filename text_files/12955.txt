                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1668






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






GST on Pulses




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether 5 per cent Goods and Services Tax (GST) is levied on branded pulses by the Government and non-branded pulses are exempted from the GST and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of Government’s annual earning levying 5 per cent GST on branded pulses;(c) whether the Government proposes or thinks to replace 5 per cent GST on branded pulses by 1 per cent GST on all types of pulses to earn more revenues; and(d) if so, the time by which it will be implemented and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE                  (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a): GST at the rate of 5% is leviable on packaged branded pulses where brand owner retains enforceable rights, on such brands. Other pulses are exempt from GST.(b): Gross GST payable on such goods in 2018-19 was approximately Rs 320 Crores.(c) & (d): The GST rates are prescribed on the recommendation of the GST Council. At present there is no such recommendation from the Council.*****
