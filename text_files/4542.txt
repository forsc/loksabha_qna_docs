                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4286






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Funds for Maintenance of NHs




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds allocated by the Union Government to various States including Bihar for maintenance of National Highways (NHs) during the last two years and the current year, State/UT-wise; (b)	the details of the work undertaken by the Government of Bihar  in this direction along with the funds utilised by it during the said period; and(c)	the steps taken by the Union Government for adequate utilisation of the said funds?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The funds allocated by the Union Government to various States including Bihar for maintenance of National Highways (NHs) during the last two years and the current year, State/UT-wise is annexed.(b)	The works under periodical  renewal, flood damage repairs and ordinary repairs have been undertaken by Government of Bihar and funds of Rs. 97.29 Crore, Rs. 20.92 Crore and Rs. 0.02 Crore have been utilized during 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019 -20 (till May, 2019) respectively. (c)	Quarterly critical review meetings are taken up by the Union Government at various levels for adequate utilization of the above said funds. Besides this, Regional Officers of this Ministry placed in various states for regular inspection and monitoring the proper utilization of funds.
