                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1341






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Establishment of Indian Heritage and Conservation Institutions




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the establishment of Indian Heritage and Conservation institutions Scheme throughout the country and the reasons for the same;(b)	the details of the estimated funds for the same;(c)	the details of the targets set under this scheme; and(d)	the time by which it is likely to be established?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) MINISTRY OF CULTURE & TOURISM (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Government has decided to establish Indian Institute of Heritage and Conservation (IIHC) as part of the Budget Announcement of 2020-21. The Memorandum of Association, Bye Laws and Regulations for IIHC have been prepared. Necessary approvals are being obtained.(b)	 to (d) IIHC would conduct various Certificate, Diploma, Graduate, Post-Graduate, Doctoral, Post-Doctoral degree and Skill and Enterpreneurship Development Courses/Programs in the fields related to Heritage & Culture like Archaelogy, Conservation, Archives, Museology, Curation, Epigraphy and Manuscriptology. Estimates for the establishment of IIHC, targets and timelines are being worked out.
