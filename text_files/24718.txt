                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

628






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Grade Separators in Puducherry




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the proposal for seting up of two grade seperators at IG square signal and RG square signal in Puducherry has been approved by the Government;(b)	if so, the present status thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to construct elevated corridor between these two points;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether the Government propses to approve the long pending issue of one grade separator at Marapalam junction in Puducherry in view of heavy traffic congestion and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) IG Square (Km 0+000) and RG Square (Km 1+050) locations fall in Puducherry – Tindivanam section of NH-66, which was widened to 4-lane in 2011 and at present is in O&M stage. The proposal for engaging Consultancy Services for taking up Grade Separators at these locations has been incorporated in the current year Annual Plan 2021-22.(e)	There is no proposal to construct grade separator at Marapalam junction at Km 39+420 of NH-45A since a bypass for Puducherry has been proposed.*****
