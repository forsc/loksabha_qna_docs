                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3155






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Declaration of Manmad-Male-DhuleIndore Stretch as NH




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	Whether road on Manmad-Male-Dhule-Indore stretch has been declared as National Highway (NH);(b) 	if so, the date on which it was declared as NH;(c) 	the work done on the said NH so far; and(d) 	the present status of progress made on this NH?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) Yes Sir, The stretch from Manmad-Male-Dhule-Indore via Chandwad is a declared National Highway. (b) The stretch from Manmad to Chandwad was  declared National Highway No. -752 G vide  gazette notification dated 03.01.2017. Further, the stretch from Chandwad-Male-Dhule-Indore was  included as National Highway No. -3 under schedule of NH Act 1956{ act 48 of 1956, dated 11.09.1956} enforceable w.e.f 15.04.1957.(c) & (d)	The details of work done and present status is at annexure.
