                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2089






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Special Operation Division




Abu Hasem Khan Choudhury













                                                    Parvesh Sahib Singh
                                                








                                                    Aparajita Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the details of the level of operationalisation of the proposed Armed Forces Special Operations Division, the Cyber Agency and the Space Agency under the Integrated Defence Staff;(b) the expected financial outlay involved in setting up of the three agencies;(c) the mandate of each of the three agencies;(d) whether the Special Operations Division would have its own helicopters, planes and specialised weaponry under its command to carry out its operations; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)  (a)  to (e):  The action for operationalisation of the three agencies viz. the Armed Forces Special Operations Division, the Cyber Agency and the Space Agency has already been initiated and progressed as per schedule with appropriate financial support.  Furnishing further details in this regard may not be in the interest of national security.*****
