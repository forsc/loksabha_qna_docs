                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Textile Export Clusters




Chirag Paswan













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Santosh Kumar
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has any proposal to establish dedicated textile export clusters in different parts of the country in near future, especially in Delhi Jamui, Kushinagar, North East, Purnea and nearby areas; (b)	if so, the details thereof ;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of textiles units approved by the Government so far?   






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	There is no scheme for establishing textile export clusters at present.(b)	Does not arise.(c) & (d) Government is implementing the Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP) under which support is provided for creation of infrastructure facilities on cluster basis under PPP mode.  59 textile parks have been approved and are under various stages of implementation as per statement attached.
