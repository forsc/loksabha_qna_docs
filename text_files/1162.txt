                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1031






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Stealing of Street Lamps installed on the Expressways




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the protective railings and the street lamps installed on both sides of some of the expressways are being stolen thereby enhancing the risk for the commutors;(b)    if so, the precaution the Government proposes to ensure that such theft does not recur and the road users are safe;(c)    whether the Government would consider installing surveillance cameras to catch such culprits who cause hindrance to the development and progress of the States; and(d)     if so, the time by which a final decision in this regard is likely to be taken? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  There have been instances of stealing of some part of the fencing and street lamps installed on both side of the Eastern Peripheral Expressway (EPE).(b) to (d) The work of Intelligent Transport System (ITS) for entire Eastern Peripheral Expressway has been awarded. Installation of surveillance cameras are a part of ITS contract at Eastern Peripheral Expressway.*****
