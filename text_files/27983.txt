                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3509






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Raw and Pucca Development Work under  MGNREGS




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of raw and pucca development works undertaken annually during the last three years under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), State-wise, year-wise and districtwise including Gujarat along with the different costs of the said works and names of the places where such works were carried out;(b) 	whether the Government is amending the said scheme to provide relief to the farmers and carrying out construction of fencing and potholes in the fields;(c) 	if so, the details of the works in the State of Maharashtra including the number of beneficiaries, district-wise; and(d) 	whether the Government is likely to amend the said scheme and also use machines for the said works, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	There are 262 permissible works under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), out of this, 182 works are related to Natural Resources Management out of which 85 are water related works and out of total works, 164 works are related to agriculture and allied activities. As per Schedule I Section 4(3), Para 4(1), of the Act the works have been classified under major categories as under;I. Category: A:    Public works relating to Natural Resources ManagementII. Category: B: Community assets or Individual assets for vulnerable sections (only for households in Paragraph 5) III. Category: C: Common Infrastructure (Including for NRLM Compliant Self Help Groups)IV. Category: D:  Rural InfrastructureIn the Management information System (MIS) of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS), provision has been made to capture the details of works in 17 categories. In this regard, the detail is at Annexure-I.States/UTs-wise (including State of Gujarat) and district-wise details of ongoing works, completed works and expenditure on works under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during last three financial years are at Annexure-II.  (b)&(c): As per Schedule I of the Mahatma Gandhi NREGA, contour trenches and contour bunds are permissible activity under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA. The work is permissible for community as well as individual beneficiaries. The beneficiaries covered under Contour bund/trench work in the State of Maharashtra are 1876 numbers since inception of the Scheme. The details of ongoing and completed (since inception) individual works related to contour bunding and contour trenches in the States of Maharashtra under Scheme are at Annexure-III. As such, fencing the field is not a permissible activity under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS, however, as an item of work only green fencing is permissible.(d): 	Para 22 of Schedule-1, Mahatma Gandhi NREGA, lays down that “As far as practicable, works executed by the programme implementing agencies shall be performed by using manual labour and no labour displacing machine shall be used.” However there may be activities in executing works which cannot be carried out by manual labour, where use of machine may become essential for maintaining the quality and durability of work to be completed under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. A suggested list of machines which can be used under Scheme, are at Annexure-IV. ****** Annexure-I referred in reply to part (a) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3509 to be answered on 10.08.2021 1	Rural Infrastructure2	Bharat Nirman Sewa Kendra3	Coastal Areas4	Drought Proofing5	Fisheries6	Flood Control and Protection7	Food Grain8	Land Development9	Micro Irrigation Works10	Other Works11	Play Ground12	Renovation of traditional water bodies13	Rural Connectivity14	Rural Drinking Water15	Rural Sanitation16	Water Conservation and Water Harvesting17	Works on Individuals Land (Category IV)
