                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3268






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






ESIC Scam




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government is serious to contain the scams such as ESIC scam that was unearthed in the State of Telangana recently, if so, the measures being taken in this direction; (b)the actual extent of the ESIC Telangana Scam and details of persons/agencies involved in the scam; and (c)the punitive action being taken against the culprits?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	A news article on discrepancy in purchase of medicines for State Government run ESIS hospitals in Telangana and consequential arrest of an official, published in newspaper, has been reported to Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC), New Delhi. The matter pertains to the State Government and is under investigation of the State Government.  From time to time, ESIC issues guidelines to various State Governments for procurement of medicines through ESIC Rate Contract/State Rate Contract including compliance of General Financial Rules.*****
