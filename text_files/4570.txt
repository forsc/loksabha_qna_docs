                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Private Sector Participation in Water Resources Management




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to encourage private sector participation in planning, development and management of water resources projects in the country; (b) if so, whether any feasibility study has been conducted by the Government regarding the costs of such efforts; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c)  Water being a State subject, planning, development and management of water resources projects are done by the State Governments. State Governments do involve private companies for preparation of project reports, execution of the projects, etc., depending on the need.  Further, the National Water Policy 2012 inter alia stipulates that water resources projects and services should be managed with community participation.  For improved delivery on sustainable basis, the State Governments/Urban Local Bodies may associate private sector in public private partnership mode*****
