                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

74






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Pending Income Tax Appeals




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the no. of appeals pending as on date before the Supreme Court (SC), High Courts (HCs) and Income Tax Appellate Tribunals (ITATs) respectively in which CBDT/ITD is a petitioner;(b) 	the number of appeals pending before the SC and ITATs respectively which are some or the other way related to ascertaining the genuineness of exemptions claimed by an individual/entity;(c)	the number of appeals pending before the Commissioner of Income Tax (Appeals);(d)	the exact number of disputes which have been resolved after CBDT revised the monetary limits for filing of appeals by the Department;(e)	the exact number of disputes which have been resolved after the launch of Vivad se Vishwas scheme; and(f)	the total number of direct tax related pending cases before the SC, HCs and ITATs respectively?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	Details of appeals filed by department at various judicial fora is as below:Forum	Department Appeals pending as on 1st April,2020ITAT	16,058HC	24,886SC	4,831Contd….2/--2-Source: Quarterly report for QE March 2020 by R&S Wing,  PrDGIT (Admin &TPS) ,CBDT(b)	This information is not maintained by CBDT.(c)	4,70,658 appeals are pending at CIT(A) as on 1st July,2020.(d)	Monetary limits were last revised by CBDT Circular No 17 of 2019 dated 8.8.2019. Consequently following appeals have been withdrawn by the department:Appellate Forum	 Withdrawals as per Circular 17/2019  ITAT	6,127High Court	6,156Supreme Court	1,104Total	13,387(e)	Based on number of Form 1 submissions made to the department under Vivad se Vishwas scheme till 8th September,2020, dispute resolution process is underway in 35,074 cases.(f)	Pendency of direct tax appeals at various fora is as below: Forum	PendencyITAT	88396*HC	31822**SC	5477***Source: ITAT figures reported by Central Registry, ITAT Mumbai as on 1st March 2020.** Source: Quarterly report for QE March 2020 by R&S Wing, Pr. DGIT (Admin &TPS), CBDT as on 31st March,2020.*****
