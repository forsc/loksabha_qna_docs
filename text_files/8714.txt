                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Payment of Wages Below the Minimum Wages




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether it is true that a majority of workers in the country earn less than Rs. 10,000 a month; (b)the number of people earning less than Rs. 10,000 a month in the last 5 years, year-wise, State-wise, gender-wise, caste-wise;(c)the number of complaints received by the Government regarding payment of wages below the minimum wage; (d)whether any action has been taken on the basis of these complaints and if so, the details thereof and the status of these cases; and(e)whether any steps have been taken to ensure that workers are paid minimum wages and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b): 	No such information is available.(c) to (e): 	Complaints of payment of less than minimum wages are received from time to time and are redressed by conducting inspections and filing of claims before the concerned authority as specified under the Minimum Wages Act, 1948. Officers of the Central Industrial Relations Machinery (CIRM) are regularly conducting inspections of the establishments in the central sphere under various labour laws including Minimum Wages Act, 1948.   Whenever, the cases of non-payment of Minimum wages are detected, the claim cases are filed before the Authority under the Minimum Wages Act, 1948.  The details of action taken are at Annexure ‘A’.*                                              *******
