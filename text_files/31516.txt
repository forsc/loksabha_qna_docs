                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2680






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Funds for Sports




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated and released by the Government under various sports schemes, during the last three years State/UT-wise including the State of Haryana; (b) the details of funds spent and which remained unspent during the last three years, State/UT-wise; (c) whether the funds released are sufficient for completion of various projects under the scheme; and (d) if not, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a) and (b):	 Funds allocated by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports  are Scheme-wise, not State-wise. Details of funds allocated, utilized and savings under various sports schemes of this Ministry are at Annexure. (c) and (d): Funds are released for various projects under the scheme on the basis of requirement for completion of projects, and availability of budget for the scheme. *****
