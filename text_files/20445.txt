                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1791






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Air India Personnel Affected by COVID-19




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the  number  of  Air  India  personal  tested COVID-19  Positive  as  a  consequence  of  the  Vande Bharat flights till 1st February 2020;(b) the number of persons hospitalized as a result of  serious  complications  due  to  the  COVID-19 pandemic;(c) whether there have been any fatalities among the Crew and the staff because of COVID-19 and if so, the details thereof;(d)  whether  there  would  be  a  test  on  the return  of  the  Crew  earlier  and  if  so,  the  details thereof including the reasons of discontinuation of post flight  COVID-19  positive  tests  by  the  Air  India management;(e)  whether  this  discontinuance  of  post  flightCOVID-19 not pose a serious risk to the Crew of Air India  flights  and  their  families  and  if  so,  the  details thereof;  and(f) whether arrangements have been made for the medical care of the crew if they test COVID-19 at an international destination during a layover or otherwise and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): A total of 1995 personnel of Air India, including the crew deployed on Vande Bharat Mission tested positive for COVID-19 till 01.02.2021, out of which, 583 personnel were hospitalized.  There have been no fatalities among the crew, however, 19 ground staff succumbed due to COVID -19 and other complications. ;(d) and (e): The COVID-19 test for the crew is undertaken as per Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) issued by the Medical Department of Air India, which is based on the advisory/guidelines issued by the Government from time to time. (f): Air India covers its crew for any medical emergencies (including COVID-19) on duty/layover.
