                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EARTH SCIENCES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1162






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Supercomputers for Weather Forecasting




Santokh Singh Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to buy supercomputers to improve weather forecasting in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the problems faced by scientists in understanding and forecasting weather in India; and(d)	the steps taken and financial allocation made by the Government to improve weather and monsoon forecasting?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER   OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	Yes Sir.(b)	A high level committee is formed to look at the needs of the Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES) institutes.   Capacities of the systems to be installed by MoES will be decided by the committee.(c)	Weather forecasting in India has improved significantly in recent decade due to availability of adequate High Performance Computing (HPC) resources to the scientists and also due to their efforts in improving the models used for forecasting. (d)	Ministry of Earth Sciences has initiated several activities for improving weather and monsoon forecasting. A few of the initiatives taken up during the last 2-3 years are : Infrastructure(i)	Commissioning of a 8.0 Peta Flops Supercomputer (ii)	Commissioning of Weather Radars (iii)	Commissioning of Automatic Weather StationsResearch& Development(iv)	Development of 12kms Global Ensemble Forecasting System (GEFS)(v)	Monsoon Mission- II  for development of applications (hydrology, agriculture)Application(vi)	Initiating Block Level including the aspirational districts Forecast for farmers(vii)	Hurricane Weather Research and Forecasting Model (HWRF) for  Tropical Cyclone forecasting(viii)	Early Warning System for air pollutionFinancial allocation during the last three financial yearsare :2017-18 :   423 crores2018-19 :   348 crores2019-20  :390 crores
