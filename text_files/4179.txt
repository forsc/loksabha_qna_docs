                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3777






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Households under MGNREGA




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of households registered under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act(MGNREGA) who were able to get work for 100 days during the last three years, State-wise;(b)	whether it is a fact that the average MGNREGA wages have decreased, if so, the details thereof along with the details of wages given during the last five years; and (c)	the number of instances of MGNREGA workers being denied their wages for not having Aadhaar Card or mismatch of biometrics during the said period, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	State/UT-wise details of households completing 100 days of employment during the last three years under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) is given at Annexure.(b):	No, Sir. As per Management Information System (MIS), the details of average MGNREGA wages in the last five financial years are given below:Financial Year	            Average Wage Rate per day per person (Rs.)2014-2015	                       143.922015-2016	                       154.082016-2017	                       161.652017-2018	                       169.442018-2019	                       179.12(c):	All wages to workers under MGNREGS are not paid through Aadhaar Based Payment System (ABPS). Those workers who do not have Aadhaar number or are in process of ABPS conversion are paid electronically through account based transfers into their Bank/Post office accounts.*****
