                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4091






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Road Widening Projects on NH-730




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the time by which the widening of Basti to Pilibhit road via Bahraich-Lakhimpur under National Highway (NH)- 730 is proposed to be completed;(b) whether the clearance from forest department is still pending for some sections of the said project;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said clearance is likely to be obtained;(d) the present status of work on the said highway, section-wise; and(e) the time limit set for completion of the project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The widening/improvement of Basti to Pilibhit section of NH-730 and NH-233 is taken up in section/stretches, as detailed in Annexure. The works are likely to be completed by September, 2021.(b) No Sir.(c) Does not arise.(d) & (e) The section-wise status of works on Basti-Pilibhit stretch of NH-730 and NH-233 along with likely date of completion are Annexed.
