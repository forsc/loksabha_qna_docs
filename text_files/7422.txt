                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1115






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






AIIMS like Hospital in Arunachal Pradesh




Tapir Gao














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether AIIMS like hospital cum medical college has been proposed to be established at Pasighat, Arunachal Pradesh;(b) if so, the latest position of the same; and(c) the reasons for not starting the AIIMS like institution at Pasighat which shall take care of the medical needs of the entire central Arunachal Pradesh starting from Daporijo, Aalo, Pasighat, Roing, Tezu and Namsai belt and the Upper Northern bank of Brahmaputra in Assam from Dhemaji and Jonai area?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	No.(b):	Does not arise.(c):	The new AIIMS are being established in different regions of the country under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana, with the aim of correcting regional imbalances in availability of tertiary healthcare and medical education facilities. In the North Eastern region of the country, an AIIMS in Guwahati, Assam is being established. Further, North Eastern Indira Gandhi Regional Institute of Medical Sciences, Shillong and Regional Institute of Medical Sciences, Imphal have also been established in region as tertiary Healthcare and Medical education centres in the region.
