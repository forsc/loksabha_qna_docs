                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3423






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






ESI Hospitals/Dispensaries in Telangana




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government is planning to set up ESI hospitals in all districts of the country;(b)the locations of currently operating ESI hospitals in the country;(c)the number of new ESI hospitals set up by the Government in 2019, 2020 and 2021 and the districts in which they have been set up;(d)whether there are any pending requests from the State Government of Telangana to set up new ESI hospitals in the state and if so, the details thereof; and(e)whether the Ministry is considering any proposals to establish a new ESI hospital in the cement manufacturing belt in Suryapet district in Telangana and if so, the details thereof, including the estimated cost of the project and the time by which it will be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		Setting up of a new Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) hospitals is a continuous and ongoing process.  Once an area qualifies for setting up of new hospital based on number of Insured Persons (IPs), process for setting up of a new hospital is undertaken.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(b):		There are 160 hospitals under the Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) Scheme, of which 50 hospitals are run by the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) and 110 by the ESI Directorate of respective State Governments.   The State/UT wise details are placed at Annexure. (c):		The new ESI hospitals set up by the Government in 2019, 2020 and 2021 are: -Year 2019:1.	100 bedded ESI hospital, Angul (Odisha). 2.	100 bedded ESI hospital, Rudrapur, Udham Singh Nagar (Uttarakhand).Year 2020:3.	100 bedded ESI Hospital, Korba (Chhattisgarh).4.	100 bedded ESI Hospital,  Udaipur (Rajasthan)5.	100 bedded ESI Hospital, Raipur, (Chhattisgarh).Year-2021-        No new ESI hospital has been set-up. (d) & (e):	No request from State Government of Telangana for setting up of new ESI hospital including at Suryapet is pending with the ESI Corporation Hqrs.*                                              ********
