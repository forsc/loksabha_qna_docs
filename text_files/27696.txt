                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3379






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Review of Pipeline Projects




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any steps have been taken to make India self-reliant in pipeline project and if so, thedetails thereof;(b) whether any State-wise review has been done for this purpose; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c) Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) is the authority to grantauthorization to entities for laying of natural gas pipelines as per PNGRB Act, 2006. As on31.03.2021, Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) has authorizedapproximately 33,764 Km Natural Gas Pipeline Network across the country with the aim tocreate a national gas grid and increase the availability of natural gas across the country. 19,998km of Natural Gas pipeline (including sub-transmission pipeline & tie in connectivity pipeline)are operational and 15,369 km are under various stages of construction. Government monitorsthe progress of pipeline projects regularly.****
