                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2448






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Slump in Automobile Industry




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that there has been a slump in the sales of cars and bikes during the last four months;(b) if so, whether it will not pull down many upstream and downstream industries and services including parts and components, steel, tyre and retailing among others;(c) whether the Government has examined in-depth the downturn of automobiles industry in the country or constituted any committee to examine the whole issue;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the measures taken/being taken by the Government to revitalize and boost the automobile industry?






ANSWER




                                                                         THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                                   (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)                                              ********(a) & (b): There is cyclical slowdown across sectors including automotive sector since last few months.  But the festival demand has shown YoY growth @ 0.3% in the Passenger Vehicle Segment.  (c) to (e): Though no specific committee has been constituted, the  Government, as a policy maker, always attempts to keep and improve momentum of the economy through a package of measure for comprehensive and continued development of the auto sector as and when required.                                                 ***********
