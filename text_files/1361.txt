                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1237






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Upgradation of Akashvani Kendra




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has chalked out any scheme for the upgradation of the Akashvani Kendras operational at local level; (b) 	if so, the ‘Akashvani Kendras’ identified in this regard; and (c) 	if not, the other steps likely to be taken by the Government for their upgradation?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c):	Prasar Bharati has informed that upgradation/modernisation of Akashvani kendras is a continuous process. All India Radio (AIR) has an approved scheme for upgradation of its transmitters/Kendras. Under the scheme Low Power MW Transmitters are upgraded to FM Transmitters. The power of existing transmitters is enhanced/upgraded from time to time based on inter-se priority and subject to availability of funds. ******
