                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3535






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Construction of Hostels




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3535TO BE ANSWERED ON 10.08.2021CONSTRUCTION OF HOSTELS 3535. SHRI JAYADEV GALLA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the number of hostels built under Construction of Hostels for OBC Boys and Girls Scheme in the country during the last three years, year-wise and Statewise; (b) the impact of COVID on the scheme; (c) the hostels under construction during the last three years, year-wise and State-wise, along with approved details of construction of these hostels approve; (d) the details of funds released for hostel under construction, State-wise; (e) the division of hostels built under the scheme in Andhra Pradesh, the details thereof, district-wise; and (f) the disbursal of funds for Andhra Pradesh under the scheme along with the amount of money disbursed so far?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a)  to (d): The details of the hostels for OBC students taken up in the last three years: number sanctioned,  status of construction, details of funds released are given in Annexure. Till now, no implementing agency has reported adverse impact on the scheme due to COVID.(e) & (f): No hostel was sanctioned to the State of Andhra Pradesh during last three years. However, in the financial year 2016-17, three hostels  were sanctioned in Andhra Pradesh, details of which are as follows:-Name of Hostel/District	No. of Seats	Year of Sanction	Fund Released (in Lakh)MJPAPBCW Hostel for Girls, Onipeta/ YSR Distt.	100	2016-17	135.00MJPAPBCW Hostel for Girls, Nandlur Rajampet/ YSR Dist.	100		135.00MJPAPBCW Hostel for Girls, Tekulodu/ Ananthapuram.	100		135.00Total	 	 	405.00 *****Annexure Annexure referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3535 for answer on 10.8.2021 regarding construction of HostelsState/UTs	2018-19	No. of New Hostels Sanctioned	No of Seats	Amount released 	Status of Hostel(Completed/Under Construction)Manipur	4	400	1197.00	Under ConstructionCentral University	5	500	1438.75	Under ConstructionTotal:	9	900	2635.75	 State/UTs	2019-20	No. of New Hostels Sanctioned	No of Seats	Amount released 	Status of Hostel(Completed/Under Construction)Tamil Nadu	12	1000	1057.75	Under ConstructionTelangana	3	300	391.50	Under ConstructionSikkim	2	200	315.00	Under ConstructionCentral University	1	200	280.58	Under ConstructionState/UTs	2020-21	No. of New Hostels Sanctioned	No of Seats	Amount released 	Status of Hostel(Completed/Under Construction)Goa	2	300	360.00	Under ConstructionKarnataka	5	500	675.00	Under ConstructionCentral University	3	1700	1499.59	Under Construction
