                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4155






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






States Lacking Safe Drinking Water




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether there are many such States in the country which lack safe drinking water; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) Water is a State subject. Under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP), Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation, Ministry of Jal Shakti provides technical& financial assistance to the States to improve the coverage of safe drinking water in rural areas. Coverage of safe drinking water is maintained in terms of Fully Covered (FC) Habitations (i.e. getting more than 40 liters per capita per day (lpcd), Partially Covered (PC) (i.e. getting less than 40 lpcd) and Quality Affected (QA) habitation (i.e. containing chemical contamination).As per information furnished by the States on Integrated Management Information System (IMIS), the State/ UT wise details of FC, PC and QA habitations as on 14.07.2019 are at Annexure.
