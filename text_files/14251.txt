                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3069






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






India Sports Summit-Fitness : $ 10 BillionOpportunity




Kuruva Gorantla Madhav














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the India Sports Summit- Fitness : $10 Billion Opportunity was a successful initiative;(b) if so, the details of the targets achieved through it; and(c) the quantum/amount of funds that have been allocated and utilized under this initiative?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (c): India Sports Summit is an annual event organized by the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) in partnership with other organizations. For the year 2019, the theme of the Summit was ‘Fitness – A $10 Billion Opportunity’ which was organised on 10th October, 2019. This Summit was not organised by the Government and no funds were allocated by the Government for organising this summit.****
