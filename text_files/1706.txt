                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1452






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Bank Scams




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the incidents of bank scams and forgery in respect of cheques are rising every year in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the actions being taken by the Government to check this trend; and(c)	the outcome of the enquiry into the said incidents and role of bank officers and employees in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        FINANCE MINISTER (a) and (b): No, sir. Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has informed that as per the reports by Scheduled Commercial Banks to RBI on frauds against “cheques/demand drafts, etc.” of Rs. 1 lakh and above, based on date of occurrence, the number of frauds for the last three financial years is as under:Financial year	Number of  cheques/demand draft related frauds2016-17	2172017-18	1992018-19	104(c): As per inputs received from Public Sector Banks, during the last three financial years, in 661 incidents of frauds related to cheques etc. where lapses were found on the part of bank officials, action has been taken after completion of inquiry against the concerned officials.***
