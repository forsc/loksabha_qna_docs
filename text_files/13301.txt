                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2121






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Startup Enterprises in India




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recognized any startup enterprises under its flagship programme in the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) 	whether any startups in Tamil Nadu including Madurai districts have been recognised so far; and(d) 	if so, the details of such startups in Tamil Nadu, district-wise including Madurai and if not, the reasons therefor? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):  	Yes Sir, the Government has recognised startup enterprises under its flagship programme. 29,017 startups have been recognized by DPIIT as on 26th February, 2020.(c) & (d):  	Yes Sir, DPIIT has recognised 1,492 startups in Tamil Nadu including 24 startups in Madurai district as on 26th February, 2020. District-wise details of startups recognised in Tamil Nadu are annexed.******
