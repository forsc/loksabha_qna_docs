                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4054






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Construction of Toilets




Sudarshan Bhagat














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has achieved unprecedented success in respect of constructing toilets in rural areas in the country under ‘Swachh Bharat Mission’;(b) if so, the total number of families benefited under the said mission, State-wise;(c) whether several States are still apathetic towards implementation of the said mission; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) and (b)	 The Government had launched Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) [SBM(G)] with effect from 2nd October, 2014 with the aim to attain Open Defecation Free India by 2nd October, 2019 by providing access to toilet facilities to all the rural households in the country.  As per the data reported by the States/UTs on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) maintained by the Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation, more than 10 crore individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed under SBM(G) and, as a result, all the 5,99,963 villages have declared themselves ODF as on 2nd October, 2019.  State/UT-wise number of IHHLs constructed under the programme are given in Annexure.  The benefits of a household toilet are accrued to all members of the household and benefits of an ODF village are accrued to all the people residing in the village.  (c) 	No, Sir.(d) 	Does not arise.
