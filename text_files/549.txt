                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

520






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Wholesale Regulated Markets in Andhra Pradesh




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	 the details of wholesale regulated markets in Andhra Pradesh, district-wise;(b)	 whether only 22 mandis have been integrated under e-NAM platform in the last few years;(c)	 if so, the reasons therefor;(d)	 whether the Ministry is planning to help the State Government of Andhra Pradesh to bring in more and more mandis under e-NAM which will help farmers of the  State  to get  better and  remunerative price for their produce; and(e)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	  The district-wise details of wholesale regulated markets in Andhra Pradesh is at Annexure-I. (b) & (c):	 Yes Sir, based on the proposals received from the States, mandis are approved for integration with National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) platform. So far, 22 mandis of Andhra Pradesh have been integrated with e-NAM platform. (d) & (e):	   Yes Sir, based on the recent proposal received from Government of Andhra Pradesh, 12 additional mandis of Andhra Pradesh have been approved by Government of India for integration with e-NAM platform and Rs. 3.60 crore have been already released to Government of Andhra Pradesh.
