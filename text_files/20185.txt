                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1385






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Coal Based Industries




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of coal based industries that have been shut down due to lack of coal in Raigarh district of Chattisgarh;(b) the revenue loss being incurred due to shut down of industries;(c) whether there is any plan to re-start these industrial units; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) to (d): Coal supply is regularly made by Mahanadi Coalfields Limited (MCL) to 9 and by South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL)  to 41 Coal based industries at Raigarh district through Fuel Supply Agreement (FSA). MCL and SECL do not have any report of any of these industries which have FSA with these coal companies closing down due to shortage of coal.****
