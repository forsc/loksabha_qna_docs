                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

621






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Financial Support for Fisheries in Lakshadweep




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Government has provided financial support for the fisheries related activities in Lakshadweep; (b)   if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has proposals for upscaling the Rural Backyard Poultry  Development Programme; and (d)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b) Yes, Sir. The Government has provided financial support of Rs.82.05 lakhs for the fisheries related activities in Lakshadweep under Blue Revolution scheme during 2015-16 to 2018-19.(c) & (d) As reported by Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying (DAHD) of this Ministry, under National Livestock Mission (NLM) Scheme of the DAHD, there are different components for poultry development, and the States and UTs have open choice to choose activities/component as per their need. The DAHD has not received any proposal for Rural Backyard Poultry Development Programme from Lakshadweep Administration.
