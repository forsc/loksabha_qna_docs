                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2843






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Social Security Schemes in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu




Kalaben Mohanbhai Delkar














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of unorganized labourers covered under the Social Security Schemes of the Government in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu;(b)the norms and guidelines for the inclusion of workers of unorganized sector under the Social Security Schemes;(c)whether the Government has identified and registered the labourers of unorganized sector for providing the benefits to them under Social Security Schemes; and (d)if so, the details of the labourers identified and registered so far particularly in the Union Territories of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	Number of unorganized workers covered under the various Social Security Schemes of the Government in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu is as under:i)	Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan (PM-SYM) pension scheme for unorganized workers - a total 1560 workers have been registered as  on 18.02.2022ii)	Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY) for    organised and unorganised - 78,113 beneficiaries registered as on 27.10.2021Contd..2/-:: 2 ::iii)	Pradhan Mantri Surksha Bima Yojana(PMSBY) for organised and unorganised - 1,40,561 beneficiaries registered as on 27.10.2021iv)	Ayushman Bharat- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) - Till November, 2021, a total of 61,950 families have been covered.(b):	As per the mandate provided by the Unorganised Workers’ Social Security Act, 2008, following welfare schemes for workers of unorganised sector have been formulated:i)	Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan (PM-SYM) pension scheme which provides a monthly minimum assured pension of Rs. 3000/- after attaining the age of 60 years.  ii)	Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY) provides life and disability cover  for the people in the age group of 18-50 years. The benefit under PMJJBY is Rs. 2 lakh on death due to any reason at a premium of Rs. 330 per annum.iii)	Pradhan Mantri Surksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) is applicable for workers in the age group of 18-70 years. The benefits under PMSBY are Rs 2 lakh on death due to accident and permanent total disability and Rs. 1 lakh on permanent partial disability at a premium of Rs. 12 per annum.iv)	Ayushman Bharat- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is a universal health scheme through which health and maternity benefits are provided. Under the scheme, Rs. 5 lakh health coverage per family is provided for secondary and tertiary hospitalization free of cost.(c):	Ministry of Labour & Employment has developed e-SHRAM portal with an objective to create National Database of Unorganised Workers (NDUW) to facilitate delivery of Social Security Schemes/ Welfare Schemes of the Central /State Governments to the workers of informal/unorganised sector.(d):	As on 14.03.2022, a total of 71,473 workers of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu have been registered under the e-SHRAM portal. ******
