                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3709






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Implementation of ATMA Scheme




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Agricultural Technology Management Agency (ATMA) Scheme provides Grants-in-aid to the State Governments with objective to support their efforts to make available the latest agricultural technologies;(b)	If so, the amount of Agriculture Infrastructure fund utilized to build food storage and warehousing in rural area of district Gopalganj in Bihar; and(c)	The details of the outcome of the Scheme in Bihar? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Yes. Centrally Sponsored Scheme ‘Support to State Extension Programs for Extension Reforms” popularly known as ATMA Scheme is under implementation since 2005 for promoting decentralized and farmer-friendly extension system in the country. Presently, 691 districts of 28 states & 5 UTs are covered under the scheme (Including all the 38 districts of Bihar). Under the scheme Grants-in-Aid is released to the State Governments with an objective to support State Government’s efforts to make available the latest agricultural technologies and good agricultural practices in different thematic areas of agriculture and allied areas to farmers through different extension activities viz; Farmers Training, Demonstrations, Exposure Visits, Kisan Mela, Mobilization of Farmers Groups and organizing Farm Schools etc.(b):	Central Sector Scheme of financing facility under ‘Agriculture Infrastructure Fund’ was launched by Hon’ble Prime Minister of India on 9th August 2020. The scheme provides interest subvention and credit guarantee support for creation of post-harvest management infrastructure and community farming assets. Food Storage and Warehousing facility is one of the eligible activities under the scheme. This scheme is a demand-driven, till now nobody has applied.(c):	ATMA Scheme: During the current financial year an amount of Rs.7200.00 lakh has been allocated to the State of Bihar against which an amount of Rs.1800.00 lakh has already been released to carry out extension activities. 93875 farmers have been benefited through different extension activities (Up to 31st October, 2021) under ATMA Scheme.**********************
