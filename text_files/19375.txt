                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Work on Bridges and Flyovers




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the present status of work of flyovers on National Highways (NHs) in Gopalganj district and the time frame set for their completion;(b)	whether it is a fact that 0.8 km Dumaria Ghat Bridge and 1.866 km Flyover in Gopalganj town which were scheduled for completion within two years after award of work are yet to be started even after a lapse of two years and if so, the reasons therefor; and(c)	whether the maintenance works on all service roads of NHs in Gopalganj district are yet to be started and if so, the details thereof along with the time frame set for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Earlier, this stretch was awarded, and had to be terminated due to failure of Contractor to complete the work. Presently, Tender documents for the work of Dumaria Ghat Bridge on Gandak River as well as flyover in Gopalganj town are at final stage. Fresh tender shall be invited by June, 2021 with scheduled completion period of two years from date of award.(c)  Maintenance of service road in Gopalganj district has been completed in the month of December, 2020 and traffic is moving smoothly.*****
