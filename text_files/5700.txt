                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Officers of CSS




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the reasons for delay in finalizing select list of Deputy Secretaries of Central Secretariat Service (CSS); (b) 	the reasons for reported discriminatory treatment towards CSS compared to Central Secretariat Stenographers Service (CSSS) in finalizing select lists and giving promotions; (c) 	the number of vacancies that existed in the grades of Under Secretary, Deputy Secretary, Director and equivalent posts in CSSS as on 1.7.2012 onwards and the vacancies filled up since 1.7.2012, year and grade-wise along with the dates of filling up of such vacancies; and (d) 	the time by which the select list of Deputy Secretaries for the select list year 2013 onwards upto 2018 would be finalized and promotions to the post of Directors against existing vacancies would be made?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a):    Regular vacancies in the grade of Deputy Secretary shall be filled by regular promotion of regular officers of Under Secretary who have rendered not less than five years’ approved service in that Grade and are included in the Select List for the Selection Grade (Deputy Secretary). The last Select List for Deputy Secretaries was finalized for the year 2012.  For finalizing the Select List of Selection Grade (Deputy Secretary) of the CSS for the year 2013 onwards, the zone of consideration includes Under Secretaries belonging to Under Secretary Select List 2003 onwards,  which is presently sub-judice in Hon’ble Supreme Court of India.  (b):    Both CSS and CSSS are governed by different sets of Rules and Regulations and therefore, the question does not arise.(c):   The vacancy position and filling up of posts in the grade of Under Secretary in CSS cadre is given at Annexure-I.The combined strength of Joint Secretary (in-situ), Directors and Deputy Secretaries of CSS is 600 with inter-se flexibility,  subject to a ceiling of 44 for Joint Secretary (in-situ) and 220 posts at the Director level. The details of vacancies and number of officers promoted in the grades of Directors and DS are given in Annexure-II.The vacancy position and filling up of posts in equivalent posts in CSSS cadre is given at Annexure-III.(d):  In the grade of Deputy Secretary no regular promotion from the Select List Year 2013 onwards is possible due to an ongoing litigation pending in the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India.*****
