                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

935






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Third Party Evaluation




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government is planning to conduct a third party evaluation on the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana Scheme in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the total amount sanctioned under this scheme so far to the State of Andhra Pradesh during the last two financial years and the number of beneficiaries who availed the scheme, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b) As decided by the Government, NITI Aayog is getting the third party evaluation conducted of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY).(c)  Under PMMVY, funds amounting to ?7022.36 Lakh and ?14,101.75 Lakh were sanctioned/ released to the Government of Andhra Pradesh during the financial years 2017-18 and 2018-19 respectively. The total number of beneficiaries availed during the year 2017-18 and 2018-19 in the State of Andhra Pradesh are 1,53,099 and 5,00,361 respectively.*****
