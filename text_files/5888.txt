                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5271






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Funds for All Round Development of Minorities




Asit Kumar Mal














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government has been helping all the State Governments for all round development of minorities; and (b)	 if so, the details regarding the amount of money given for this to different States in financial years 2015-2016, 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 by the Union Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)  The Ministry is implementing the following schemes/ Programmes in the 	country involving the state / UT governments for the welfare and all round development of six centrally notified minorities, namely, Jain, Buddhist, Sikh, Parsi,Muslim and Christian:-(1) Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakaram (PMJVK) - For creation of basicinfrastructural assets and filling of gaps in deficit areas, the Ministry implements Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) through State Governments / UT Administrations with the objective of developing socio-economic assets and basic amenities in 308 Districts of the country having 1300 identified Minority Concentration Areas. (2)  National Minorities Development and Finance Corporation (NMDFC) implements schemes through its State Channelising Agencies (SCAs).The details are on the website http://www.nmdfc.org/.(3) The Central Waqf Council (CWC) gives financial assistance to State Waqf Boards (SWBs).The details are on the website http://centralwaqfcouncil.gov.in/.(b) The total expenditure incurred on schemes being run for the welfare of minorities during the last five years is available on the website of Ministry of Minority Affairs i.e. www.minorityaffairs.gov.in. The state-wise expenditure under the PMJVK scheme is also available on the website of Ministry of Minority Affairs.**********
