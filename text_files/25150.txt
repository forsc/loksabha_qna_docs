                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

980






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Expenditure Incurred on Development of Infrastructure Around Monuments




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any policy shift of ASI with regard to preference on development activities like construction of boundary walls, laying of modern pathways, illumination, ultramodern washrooms, etc. overconservation/ restoration/archaeological investigations? and(b)    the details of works along with expenditure incurred other than conservation/restoration/archaeological investigations carried out in the last three years at ASI protected monuments including the names of executing agencies in each case, circle-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)  	No Sir.  There is no policy shift of the  Archaeological Survey of India (ASI). (b)   	The expenditure incurred on development activities like construction of boundary walls, laying of modern pathways, illumination, ultramodern washrooms, Toilet Block, Publication rooms, Clock rooms, ticket counter, cafeteria, drinking water and parking etc. for the last 3 years and the names of executing agencies is as given below:S. No.	Name of  the Agency	Expenditure for the  Financial year(Amount Rs. in Crores)		2018-19	2019-20	2020-211.	WAPCOS	47.65	86.71	61.702.	NBCC	44.32	80.00	18.903.	TCIL	29.55	  6.09	  6.554. 	NPCC	    70.95
