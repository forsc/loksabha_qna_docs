                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Setting up of Solar  Power Plants




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received proposals for setting up of solar power plants from various States including Uttar Pradesh; and(b) if so, the details thereof along with the status of the said proposals, State/UT-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER AND MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP (SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) and (b) The Project proposals are received from various states for setting up of solar power plants under various schemes e.g. Development of Solar Parks and Ultra Mega Solar Power Projects, Central Public Sector Undertaking (CPSU) Scheme, Rooftop Scheme etc. The details of various Grid Connected Solar Projects State/UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh  under different  schemes are  given in Annexure.*****
