                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Electrification of Railway Tracks in Tripura




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to electrify all Railway tracks in Tripura in the near future;(b)	if so, the details thereof specially for east Tripura;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of implementation procedure for any such proposal in the near future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d)	Yes, Sir.  Ministry of Railways has planned to electrify balance Broad Gauge (BG) Routes of Indian Railways including the State of Tripura.  There is one electrification project of 590 km length with costing of ` 494 crore falling partly in the state of Tripura including East Tripura has been sanctioned.******
