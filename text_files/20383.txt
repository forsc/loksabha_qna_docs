                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1737






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Review of DRIP




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has reviewed the implementation of Dam Rehabilitation and Improvement Project (DRIP); (b) if so, the details and the targets achieved thereof; and (c) the details of funds released and utilized so far under the project, State- wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) Third party Independent Review of the Dam Rehabilitation and Improvement Project (DRIP) scheme has been carried out by Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Roorkee in the year 2017. It recommended  supporting  and continuation of the  scheme. In addition to this, Third Party Review by IIT Delhi is also currently under progress.In addition to above, the progress of DRIP is being reviewed and monitored bi-annually by National Level Steering Committee (NLSC) under the chairmanship of Secretary (DoWR, RD&GR). A Technical Committee (TC) under the Chairmanship of Member (Design & Research), Central Water Commission is in place with appropriate representation from all partner agencies, India Meteorological Department, Ministry of Power etc. to review the progress of this Scheme on quarterly basis for smooth implementation. So far, 10 meetings of NLSC and 25 meetings of TC have been conducted. The World Bank is conducting regular Implementation Support and Review Mission on bi-annual basis to review the overall progress of project implementation, key challenges, lessons learnt and provides guidance on corrective measures for speeding up implementation and disbursement. So far, 15 nos. of World Bank Missions have been conducted.The details and the targets achieved are available at Annexure-I. (c)  The ongoing DRIP is a State Sector Scheme with 100% back to back loan arrangement. There is no provision of Central Grant in the Scheme. The loan is reimbursed on quarterly basis based on the expenditure incurred by various partner agencies. The Original Cost of Scheme was Rs.2100 Cr, which was revised to Rs.3466 Cr in the year 2018. Due to COVID-19 pandemic and slow progress by the Implementing Agencies, loan amounting to US$ 101 Million has been surrendered, and revised budget outlay of the Scheme is Rs.2642 Cr.
