                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1413






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






MiG Aircrafts




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Indian Air Force plans to phase out the MiG series aircraft due to frequent accidents in recent years;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the time line for acquiring 36 Rafale air craft has been advanced due to the threat that our country is facing in eastern and north western border recently; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b):  Phasing out of aircrafts and their replacement with new generation aircrafts depend upon national security, strategic objectives and operational requirements of the defence forces and is reviewed by the Government from time to time.  This is a continuous  process.  The MiG series aircrafts follow the process of phasing out in a staggered manner on completion of their Total Calendar Life/Total Technical Life.(c) & (d):  The delivery of 36 Rafale aircraft is proceeding as per schedule.  As on date, a total of 26 aircraft have been accepted and ferried to India.*****
