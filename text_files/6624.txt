                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

420






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Pre-Matric, Post-Matric and MeritCum-Means Scholarships




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of students who received pre-matric, post-matric and Meritcum-Means scholarships during the last three years, year and category wise;(b) the total amount spent for giving scholarship to the students; (c) whether the Government has revised the amount of scholarship given to each student; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): The details of beneficiaries and amount spent under Pre Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Caste  Students, Post Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Caste  Students, Top Class Education for Scheduled Caste  Students, Pre Matric Scholarship for Other Backward Class Students, Post Matric Scholarship for Other Backward Class Students, National Fellowship for Other Backward Classes Students, Dr. Ambedkar Post Matric Scholarship for Economically Backward Classes (EBC) , Dr. Ambedkar Pre and Post Matric Scholarship for Denotified and Semi-Notified Tribes (DNT), Pre matric and Post Matric and Merit cum-Means Scholarship Schemes of Ministry of Minority Affairs, during the last three years, is at Annexure.(c) & (d): Under the Scheme of Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC students, the rates of Maintenance allowance were recently enhanced in 2017-18 from Rs. 150 to Rs. 225 per month for Day Scholars and from Rs. 350 to Rs 525 per month for Hostellers.(ii)    Under the scheme of Pre-Matric Scholarship for OBC w.e.f. 2017-18 and National Fellowship for OBC w.e.f.  01.01.2019, the amount of scholarship has been revised as per below:S. No.	Name of Scheme	Old Rate of Scholarship	Revised Rate of Scholarship1	Pre-Matric Scholarship for OBC students	Day ScholarClass I to V : Rs. 25 p.m, Class VI to VIII : Rs. 40 p.m, Class IX to X : Rs. 50 p.m (for 10 months)HostellerClass III to VIII : Rs. 200 p.m,Class IX to X : Rs. 250 p.m  (for 10 months)	w.e.f 2017-18Day ScholarClass I to X : Rs. 100 p.m (for 10 months)HostellerClass III to X : Rs. 500 p.m (for 10 months)2	National Fellowship for OBC Students	Rs.25,000/- p.m. for initial two years (JRF)Rs.28,000/- p.m. for remaining tenure (SRF)	w.e.f.  01.01.2019Rs.31,000/- p.m. for initial two years (JRF)Rs.35,000/- p.m. for remaining tenure (SRF)******
