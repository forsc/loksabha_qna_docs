                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4339






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Vacant Posts in the Category of SC, ST and OBC in the Ministry




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that a large number of posts are vacant in the category of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes in the Ministry;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of sanctioned vacant posts in SC/ST/OBC and General categories;(c) the details of the steps taken during the last three years for filling them; and(d) whether some communities are being deprived of the benefits of reservation by not making appointments on sanctioned posts?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’) (a) to (d): The detail of category-wise sanctioned and vacant posts belonging to various cadres such as Indian Statistical Services (ISS), Sub-ordinate Statistical Services (SSS), Indian Economic Services (IES), Central Secretariat Services (CSS), Central Secretariat Stenographers Services (CSSS), Central Secretariat Clerical Services (CSCS), Central Secretariat Official Language Services (CSOLS) etc. and posts under Central Staffing Scheme are maintained and vacancies are filled up by respective Cadre Controlling Authorities. As far as the posts, whose Cadre Controlling Authority is this Ministry, are concerned, a detail of category-wise sanctioned and vacant posts are given in the table at Annexure. In respect of vacancies mentioned in the Annexure, Senior Secretariat Assistant (SSA) is a centralized post in CSCS cadre but reservation roster is maintained by this Department. Filling up of vacancies is an ongoing and continuous process. The vacancies are filled through promotion/ direct recruitment/ deputation/ departmental examination as prescribed in their recruitment rules and are reported to the Department of Personnel & Training (DoP&T) or Staff Selection Commission (SSC) etc. from time to time.Zone of Consideration for promotion to the post of SSA is issued by DoP&T. Instructions issued by DoP&T from time to time for promotion are adhered to. All candidates covered under Zone of Consideration issued by DoP&T have been promoted.Vacant posts of Multi-Tasking Staff and Canteen Attendant have been reported to SSC. *****
