                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






T-18 Train




Uday Pratap Singh













                                                    Chirag Paswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has formulated/proposes any scheme to export the modern and high speed train 18 to foreign countries and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has decided to export train 18 to other countries after meeting the domestic requirement, if so, the present status of the manufacturing schedule of train 18;(c) whether the Government also has any proposal to replace all the Rajdhani and Shatabdi trains by T-18 type modern trains in the near future especially in Bihar region, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of the time-frame for the completion of the project?






ANSWER




                                                               MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY                                       ( SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL )  (a) & (b) At present, there are no enquiries from foreign countries for buying Train-18 train-sets. Two train-sets comprising of total 32 coaches have already been manufactured and turned out. It is planned to manufacture total 640 train-set coaches in the three years viz.  2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22.(c) No, Sir. New train-set services will be introduced within operational constraints, as and when new train-sets become available.(d) Does not arise.*****
