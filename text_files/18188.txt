                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2030






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Roads Pending for Construction




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the length of roads constructed during the last three years in the country, State-wise;(b) the number of tenders floated and contracts awarded thereafter for road construction during the said period in the country;(c)  the length of roads for which bidding process is still pending along with the total cost of these bids/ projects; and(d)  the length of roads pending for construction whose bidding process has been completed alongwith the total cost thereof, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THEMINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V. K. SINGH (RETD.)] (a) The State-wise construction of National Highways (NHs) during the last three years is given in Annexure-I.(b) NH projects of about 31,496 km length were tendered and awarded during the Financial Year (FY) 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20. (c)  The bidding process is still pending in about 6,532 Km length of NHs costing Rs. 1, 36,703 Cr. in the current Financial Year upto August, 2020. (d) The State-wise details of ongoing projects for construction of NHs ending August, 2020 is given in Annexure-II.
