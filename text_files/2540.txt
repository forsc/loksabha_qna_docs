                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Financial Assistance for Religious Places/Institutions




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  Whether lhe Government is providing financial assistance to Religious places/institutions to promote culture and secularism in the country; and(b)  if so, lhe details thereof during the last three years, Slate- wise 






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State (I/C) For Culture And Tourism (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. This Ministry operates a ‘Scheme of Financial Assistance for Allied Cultural Activities’.  The Scheme is launched on 15.10.2018.  Under this scheme we provide financial assistance for creation of assets for enhancing the audio-visual spectacle for allied cultural activities to give firsthand experience of live performances on regular basis and during festivals in open/closed areas/spaces where large numbers of tourists/visitors come regularly and during major events/festivals the number of visitors swells to lakhs. 2.
