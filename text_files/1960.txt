                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1797






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Higher Education Loan to OBC Students




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is providing higher education loan to Other Backward Classes (OBCs) students at zero interest rate; (b) if so, the details of the number of students belonging to OBCs who applied for loan with the National Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) to pursue education during each of the last three years, State and sector-wise; (c) the details of the students who applied and got loan to study abroad during the said period, State and sector-wise; (d) whether the Government is aware that the OBC students are facing lots of difficulties in getting such education loan; and (e) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to redress the said grievances?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a): No loan is being provided to OBC students at zero interest rate for higher education. The National Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) a corporation under the aegis of Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has been entrusted with the job of providing the Education Loan to the students belonging to Backward Classes having annual family income less than Rs.3.00 lakh for pursuing general/professional/technical/vocational courses or trainings at graduate and higher levels in the Educational Institutions duly approved by appropriate authorities such as All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Medical Council of India (MCI), University Grants Commission (UGC) etc. are covered. The rate of interest is 4% p.a. and for girl students rate of interest is 3.5% p.a. for loans for higher education studies.(b) & (c): Details of number of students State-wise whom loan have been disbursed to study in India and abroad during the last three years are at Annexure-I.(d)&(e) No such specific information has been received so far.****
