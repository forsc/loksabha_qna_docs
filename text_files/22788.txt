                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4010






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Healthcare Services




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA,YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the features of the ‘Centres of Excellence Scheme’ of the Government;(b) the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this mission in the country, State/UTwise including Tamil Nadu during the last three years;(c) the number of healthcare services being provided under this scheme, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu;(d) the details of the target set and achievements made so far in this regard in Tamil Nadu;(e) whether the Government has planned for upgradation of more AYUSH institutions under this scheme in the country, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu; and(f) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a): The features of the ‘Centre of Excellence Scheme’ of Ministry of AYUSH are as follows:i.	Under “Central Sector Scheme for assistance to AYUSH organisations for upgradation to Centres of Excellence(CoE)” Ministry of AYUSH supports AYUSH institutions in Government/ Non-Government sector to upgrade their functions and facilities to levels of excellence.ii.	It is a Central Sector Scheme, institutions/organizations engaged in one or more of the activities such as Clinical research, AYUSH Hospitals, Research based on the Fundamentals of AYUSH, Inter-disciplinary research in pharmacology, pharmacy or product development, bridging AYUSH and modern science, tertiary care Allopathic Hospitals having track record of 5 years in AYUSH research and any other specialized area of AYUSH are eligible for assistance.iii.	A maximum Rs.10 Crore is approved for the period of three years. Not more than 30% of the Government grant is permitted on civil works. iv.	 The existing facilities of the organization is upgraded to achieve higher standards as may be recognized in the discipline like NABH, GLP, GMP etc., as are acceptable to the Ministryv.	The Scheme provides for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) of the population at concessional rates/ free of cost from the facilities created under the Scheme.(b) & (c): 	During last three years, Ministry of AYUSH has utilized Rs.20.85 Crore for supporting 13 projects across the country including Tamil Nadu.
