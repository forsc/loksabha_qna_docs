                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

827






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Accident Cases during Construction of NHs




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that many accidents took place during the construction of National Highways(NHs) due to various reasons such as collapsing of wall and elevated slabs in many parts of the country during the year 2020;(b)   if so, the details thereof;  (c) whether the Government instituted any inquiry into these accidents; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken against the contractors and the employees of the National Highways Authority of India found responsible? 






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) Accidents had taken place in the year 2020 during construction of National Highways.  Details of such accidents including enquiry and action taken are placed at Annexure.
