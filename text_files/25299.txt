                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






MoU with Online Travel Companies




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recently signed an MoU with online travel companies like Clear Trip and Ease My Trip etc. (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons thereon for signing such an MoU; (c) 	the basis on which these companies have been selected and the details of the selection process of these companies; (d) 	whether any revenue sharing mechanism agreement has been entered into with these companies and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	whether any guidelines have been issued in this regard to State Tourism Departments on the modalities of such a partnership and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Tourism has recently signed MoUs with three Online Travel Companies namely EaseMyTrip.com, Cleartrip Pvt Ltd and Yatra Online Pvt Ltd to get a clear picture on the geographical spread of the Hospitality Sector, its size, structure and existing capacity so as to offer relevant services to the Hospitality Industry [like assistance in COVID-19 preparedness through SAATHI (System for Assessment, Awareness & Training for Hospitality Industry), Star Classification etc.], under National Integrated Database of the Hospitality Industry (NIDHI). As a step towards realising this vision, the Ministry has solicited assistance from the Online Travel Aggregation Companies by way of entering into an MoU with the Ministry of Tourism.(c):	A circular has been uploaded on the Ministry of Tourism website inviting all Online Travel Aggregation Companies to express their intent to participate in this exercise. A copy of the same is given at Annexure. It is an ongoing process to engage with Industry stakeholders.(d):	No, Sir. No revenue sharing mechanism in the agreement has been entered into with these companies.(e):	The guidelines regarding NIDHI & SAATHI have been issued to the State Tourism Departments & the Ministry is continuously engaging with State Tourism Departments for promoting them. The modalities related to the objectives of the MoU are in development stage.*******
