                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1547






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Sainik Schools in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey













                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the construction of Sainik School at Thajkurangti in Godda district of Jharkhand is yet to start;(b) if so, names of the States including Jharkhand where the Government proposes to open Sainik Schools;(c) whether the Government is aware of the number of Sainik Schools sanctioned and running in Jharkhand State and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Central Government has released funds to open sainik schools; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (c):  Ministry of Defence had accorded “in-principle” approval for establishing a Sainik School at Thakurgangti  Circle, Mauza Mapahari in Godda District of Jharkhand, post site survey conducted on 14-15 May, 2018.  Sequel to the site survey, few observations were conveyed to the State Government by this Ministry in 2019.  There has been no response from the State Government of Jharkhand, till date.  At present, one Sainik School at Tilaiya is functioning in the State of Jharkhand.  Details of new Sainik Schools proposed to be set up in the country, including Jharkhand, is placed at Annexure.(d) to (e):  Funds for construction of Sainik Schools, land for Sainik Schools are provided by the State Govt.                                                *****
