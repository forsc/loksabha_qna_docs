                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3616






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Strengthening of Price Monitoring Cell in Maharashtra




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of centres working under the Scheme for strengthening of Price Monitoring Cell in Maharashtra;(b)	whether any recommendation has been made to release financial assistance to Maharashtra Government under the scheme for strengthening of Price Monitoring Cell (PMC); if so, the details thereof, (c)	whether the Government has received any proposal regarding financial assistance for a new price repository centre of the State Government of Maharashtra; (d)	if so, the details along with the present status thereof; and (e)	the time by which, it is likely to be approved?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e) : There are 5 price reporting centres in Maharashtra under the scheme, Strengthening of Price Monitoring Cell wherein, funds are released to States/UTs on the basis of proposals sent by them. An amount of Rs.6,37,097/- was released during 2020-21 to the Government of Maharashtra as financial assistance for the price reporting centres. The Department of Consumer Affairs received a proposal from the State Government for 4 new price reporting centres which were approved in principle for reporting daily retail and wholesale prices on trial basis. Thereafter, upon satisfactory fulfilment of the requisite conditions, one of proposed centres namely, Mangaon in Raigad District has been added to the list of price reporting centres in Maharashtra.*****
