                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3494






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Cases Against Officers




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of cases registered for serious offences against Central Government officers, State-wise; (b) 	whether the administrative activities have been affected due to the posts lying vacant on account of absconding officers against whom the cases have been registered; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (c) The information is being collected and will be laid on the Table of the House. *****
