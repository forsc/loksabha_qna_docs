                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4779






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Employment Outlook




 Chinta Anuradha














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the National Statistical Office has released the Employment Outlook of the country covering the period from September, 2017 to December, 2020 based on the administrative records available with selected Government agencies to assess the progress; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the major findings of this report?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a) to (c): Yes Sir. The Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation has been bringing out the formal sector employment related statistics through monthly press release covering period from September 2017 onwards using information on the number of subscribers who have subscribed in three major schemes, namely the Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF) Scheme, the Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) Scheme and the National Pension Scheme (NPS) since April, 2018.   The number of new subscribers who have joined the Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF), Employees’ State Insurance (ESI), National Pension Scheme (NPS) from September, 2017 to December, 2020 were 394.11 lakh, 463.21 lakh and 23.61 lakh respectively.                                                        *******
