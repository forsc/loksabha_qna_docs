                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1358






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






People Engaged in Food Processing Sector




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the estimated number of people engaged in the food processing projects in the country, state wise;(b)	whether the Government has sanctioned a total of 134 projects in the year 2020 in various states in the country;(c)	if so, the details thereof, state wise particularly for the state of Tamil Nadu and the present status thereof and the details of employment generation through the new projects directly and indirectly; and(d)	the time by which the new projects are likely to begin their operations?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a).	Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a Central Sector scheme- PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector including off farm employment generation across the country. Major component schemes under PMKSY with employment potential include Mega Food Park, Agro-processing Clusters, Integrated Cold Chain, Operation Green, Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) and Backward & Forward Linkages. Based on state-wise projects approved under component schemes of PMKSY, the estimated number of people engaged in these projects, state wise is as under:Sl.
