                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1706






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Units of KVIC




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of functional Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) sponsored units particularly in the backward and rural areas of the country as on date, State/location-wise; (b) 	the number of units out of these located in the backward areas of the country; (c) 	the details of the works executed by these units during each of the last three years; and (d) 	the number of those cooperative and private units which have been provided assistance by the State Khadi and Gramodyog Board during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)to(c):       As on date, 2788 number of Khadi Institutions (KIs) are registered with and working under Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC), including the KIs located in the rural and backward areas of the country. State-wise list of working Khadi Institutions are placed at Annexure-I. These Institutions have created cumulative employment to 4.97 lakh persons. 	KVIC is also implementing Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) scheme, for setting up of new micro-enterprises units and to generate employment opportunities in rural as well as urban areas, including backward areas, of the country through KVIC, State Khadi and Village Industries Boards (KVIBs) and District Industries Centre (DICs), under which financial assistance is provided by the Banks, with subsidy from Government of India.  During the period from 2014-15 to 2020-21 (as on 31.01.2021), 374931 micro units (80% in rural areas) have been assisted with margin money of Rs.10036.47 crore.  Details of state-wise number of micro units established, margin money subsidy utilized and employment opportunities created under PMEGP through KVIC, including KVIBs and DICs, during the last three years (2017-18 to 2019-20) are placed at Annexure-II. 	The registered Khadi Institutions are involved in the promotion of Khadi activities.  The units setup under PMEGP belong to Mineral Based Industries (MBI), Forest Based Industries (FBI), Agro Based & Food Processing Industries (ABFPI), Polymer & Chemical Based Industry (PCBI), Handmade Paper & Fibre Industries, Rural Engineering & Bio-Technology Industry (REBTI), Solar Vastra, and Service/Textile/polyvastra Industries.  (d):	State-wise number of units assisted through State Khadi and Village Industries Boards (KVIBs) in the country including in the backward and rural areas under PMEGP, margin money distributed and employment generated during the last three years are given at Annexure-III.
