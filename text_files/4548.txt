                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4303






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Amaravati-Anantapur Expressway




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has started work on Amaravati-Anantapur Expressway; (b) 	if so, the details and the present status thereof; (c) 	whether any time-frame has been fixed for completion of the said expressway; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the measures being taken by the Government to expedite the execution of the project for its early completion?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (e) 	The alignment of an Access Controlled Green Field Expressway connecting the new capital city of Amaravati to Anantapuramu in the State of Andhra Pradesh has been finalized with a length of approximately 384 kms. Government of Andhra Pradesh has agreed to transfer the government land free of cost and bear 50% of the cost of the land to be acquired. Further, the Government of Andhra Pradesh has also agreed to obtain requisite statutory clearances (i.e. environmental, forest, wildlife etc.) so that the approval / award of civil work / execution of work for the aforesaid expressway is taken up expeditiously. Land acquisition proceedings for the expressway have been initiated. The civil work on road stretch is taken up on completion of DPR, land acquisition, obtaining of statutory clearances, firming up of construction cost, tying up of funding requirements and requisite appraisal/approval.*****
