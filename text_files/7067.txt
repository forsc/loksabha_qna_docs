                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

765






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Jetty for Navi Mumbai




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















 (a)	Whether it is a fact that construction work related to Jetty in Narul situated in Navi Mumbai under Thane region should have been completed by December, 2019 and if so, the reasons for delay and the time by which it is likely to be completed and to start water transport; and (b)	Whether any order has been passed to Maritime Board regarding it and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR SHIPPING(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	Maharashtra Maritime Board (MMB) had initially planned to complete the construction work of a Jetty at Narul situated in Navi Mumbai by December, 2019. However the work was delayed due to non-availability of environment clearance from Forest Department, Hon’ble High Court permission for cutting of mangroves and technical difficulties in the alignment. The alignment of Jetty in creek portion was kept on hold by MMB and Mumbai Port Trust (MBPT) due to requirement of navigational channel. Revised alignment is under finalisation. Construction work is targeted for completion by May, 2020.(b)	No, Sir.*******
