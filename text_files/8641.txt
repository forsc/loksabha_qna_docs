                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2281






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Funds to Madhya Pradesh under PMKVY




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the year-wise details of number of beneficiaries, allocated/utilised fund under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana in the country especially in Madhya Pradesh during the last three years;(b)	the number of persons trained under this scheme in Madhya Pradesh especially in Hoshangabad - Narsinghpur - Raisen district since its inception and during the current year; and(c)	the number of beneficiaries trained and employed under the above scheme and to provide employment to the persons trained under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20, as on 11.11.2019, 69.03 lakh (appx.) (38.01 lakh Short Term Training (STT) + 31.02 lakh Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)) candidates have been trained throughout the country. Number of candidates trained during 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 are 2.24 lakh, 21.55 lakh, 21 lakh and 24.24 lakh, respectively. Out of total trained candidates, 4.77 lakh candidates are in State of Madhya Pradesh.PMKVY has two components known as Centrally Sponsored Centrally Managed (CSCM) being implemented by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and Centrally Sponsored State Managed (CSSM) being implemented by State Skill Development Missions of the States/ UTs popularly known as State- Engagement Component of PMKVY (2016-20).Under CSCM component of PMKVY 2016-20, there is no provision of State-wise or district-wise funds allocation. However, under CSSM component of PMKVY 2016-20, 25% of the funds and corresponding physical targets of PMKVY 2016-20 have been allocated to the States for the implementation of scheme through State Skill Development Missions across the State covering all districts. Under this component, as on 11.11.2019, after evaluation of proposals received from States/UTs, Ministry has disbursed funds amounting to Rs. 808.96 Cr. to 36 States/UTs including Rs. 21.46 Cr. disbursed to State of Madhya Pradesh.	(b)	Under PMKVY (2016-20), 4,76,804 candidates have been trained in the State of Madhya Pradesh including 13,600; 8,065 and 12,570 candidates in district of Hoshangabad, Narsinghpur and Raisen, respectively.(c)	Under STT component of PMKVY (2016-20), as per data reported on SDMS (Skill Development Management System), 30.21 lakh candidates have been certified. Out of these certified candidates, 15.4 lakh candidates have been reported to be placed in various sectors across the country including 1.44 lakh candidates from the State of Madhya Pradesh.
