                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2770






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






GI tag Designated Uppada Jamdani Sarees




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of measures being taken by the Government to promote the GI tag designated Uppada Jamdani Sarees of Andhra Pradesh;(b) the details of progress made in this regard; (c) the effect of such measures/certification to promote the product; and (d) the details of other measures proposed to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (d):	The handloom product Uppada Jamdani Sarees of Andhra Pradesh has been registered under Geographical Indications (G.I.) of Goods (Registration & Production) Act, 1999. G.I. Help Desk has been set up in Weaver Service Centre Vijaywada to promote the product and address issues related under the Act including legal advice.  The handloom producers of Uppada Jamdani Sarees are facilitated to participate in various marketing expos/events including e-commerce platforms to sell their products. A Block Level Cluster has been sanctioned in Uppada village.  In addition, one Producer Company has been formed to extend the benefits of handloom schemes to the weavers and producers of Uppada Jamdani. Registration under India Handloom Brand (IHB) has also been granted to the product.***
