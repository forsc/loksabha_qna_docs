                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

117






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Mid Day Meal Scheme (MDMS)




Tapir Gao














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of manpower engaged for Mid Day Meal Scheme (MDMS) in Schools;(b) the total strength of Mid Day Meal Scheme staff working all over the country, State-wise;(c) the number of days in a week and number of months they are working in a year;(d) the details of honorarium paid to each staff engaged in the MDMS; and(e) whether there is any uniformity in the honorarium to MDMS staff and if so, the detailsthereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (e): Under the Mid-Day Meal Scheme (MDMS) one cook-cum-helper (CCH) can beengaged for upto 25 students, two cook-cum-helpers (CCHs) for schools with 26 to 100students and one additional cook-cum-helper for every addition of up to 100 students. Asagainst approval of 26.09 lakh cook-cum-helpers under the scheme during 2018-19, 24.95lakh cook-cum-helpers have been engaged by the States and Union Territories. The Stateand UT-wise details of CCHs engaged under the scheme are given at Annexure -I.CCHs engaged under the Scheme for preparing and serving mid-day meals at schools, arehonorary workers who have come forward for rendering social services. In recognition oftheir services, the CCHs are paid Rs.1000 per month for 10 months in a year. Honorariumis also provided to CCHs for preparing and serving mid day meals in elementary schoolsduring summer vacation in drought affected areas notified by the concerned StateGovernments. The details of the honorarium paid to the CCHs working under MDMS inall States and Union Territories during 2018-19 are given at Annexure-II. The honorariumexpenditure is shared between the Central Government and States and UTs as per theapproved sharing pattern. Some State Governments and UT Administrations alsosupplement the honorarium by providing additional funds from their own resources.. *******
