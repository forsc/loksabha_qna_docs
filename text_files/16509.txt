                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

272






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






National Research Centre on Camels




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps taken/proposed by the Government to set up a branch of National Research Centre on Camels (NRCC) in Gujarat, so far; (b)	the steps taken/proposed to protect Kharai camels; and (c)	the steps taken/proposed by National Research Centre on Camels to protect double humped camel in Ladakh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	 For setting up of a branch of National Research Centre on Camel (NRCC), Bikaner, an appropriate size of land and other resources on nominal cost/lease basis is desired from the Government of Gujarat.(b)	1. Kharai camel has been characterized, registered and gazette notified as breed by DARE/ICAR.2. NRCC has signed an MoU with Kamdhenu University, Gujarat for further breed conservation and development by (i) Training of farmers for scientific camel husbandry; (ii) Organizing Scientist-farmers interaction meetings; (iii) Holding animal health camps; (iv) Providing technical support for development of the entrepreneurship towards the camel milk.(c)	Following is being carried out by NRCC, Bikaner to protect double humped camel in Ladakh:-1.	Camel health management by organizing health camps in Nubra valley.2.	Nutritional management by utilizing local feed resources.*******
