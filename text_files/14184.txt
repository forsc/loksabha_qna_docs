                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2965






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Railway Projects/Schemes in North Eastern Railways




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Railway projects/schemes sanctioned during the last three years for North Eastern Railway zone, Varanasi division; and    (b) 	the details of the current status of such projects/schemes? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) AND (b) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2965 BY SHRI SHYAM SINGH YADAV TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.03.2020 REGARDING RAILWAY PROJECTS/SCHEMES IN NORTH EASTERN RAILWAYS(a) & (b): Railway projects are not sanctioned State-wise/Division-wise, but Zone-wise, as Indian Railway network straddles across various State/Division boundaries. 4 new line, 4 gauge conversion and 7 doubling projects under North-Eastern Railway Zone were sanctioned during last three years and current year (2016-17 to 2018-19 and 2019-20). Out of these, following 7 projects i.e. 1 new line, 1 gauge conversion and 5 doubling projects covering a length of 533.77 km falls fully/partly in Varanasi Division: •	   Padrauna-Kushingar via Gorakhpur (64.47 km) new line.  •	Indara-Dohrighat (34.37 km) gauge conversion•	Phephna-Indara, Mau-Shahganj excluding Indara-Mau (150.28 km) doubling •	Bhatni-Aunrihar excluding Indara-Mau (116.95 km) with electrification (117 km) doubling •	Aunrihar-Jaunpur (59.55 km) doubling •	Domingarh-Gorakhpur-Gorakhpur Cantt.-Kushmi 3rd line (21.15 km)•	Gorakhpur-Balmikinagar (87 km) doubling- Proposed in the Budget 2020-21. The project-wise detail including cost, expenditure and outlay are available in public domain on Indian Railways’ website i.e. www.indianrailways.gov.in>Ministry of Railways>Railway Board> About Indian Railways>Railway Board Directorates>Finance (Budget)> Railway-wise Works Machinery & Rolling Stock Programme>Regular Budget (year). *****
