                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1616






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Promotion and Development of Sports




Subhas Sarkar














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of financial assistance granted to various institutions engaged in promotion and development of sports in the State of West Bengal during the last three years;(b) the details of funds utilization, target fixed and achievements made in this regard; and(c) whether the State of West Bengal has requested for funds for promoting sports in rural areas during the last three years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) and (b) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the primary responsibility for promotion and development of sports is that of State/Union Territory Governments. Central Government supplements the efforts of the State/ UT Governments, under its various schemes.	During the last three years, financial assistance has been given for bridging the gaps in infrastructure in West Bengal through one of the verticals of Khelo India Scheme of this Ministry, namely, ‘Utilization and creation/upgradation of sports infrastructure’. In addition, this Ministry through the Sports Authority of India (SAI) has also been running a SAI Regional Centre at Kolkata to identify and nurture talented sportspersons in West Bengal. The details of the above during the last three years are at Annexure.	The Schemes of this Ministry are demand driven. Therefore, no targets are fixed.(c)	No, Sir.****
