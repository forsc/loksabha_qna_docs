                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Promotion of Performing Arts




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the number of public halls and auditoriums set up by the Government to promote performing arts, Statewise; b)	the details of grants released by the Sangeet Natak Akademi, Lalit Kala Akademi and Sahitya Akademi, separately, to aid research, documentation and publishing in the performing arts during the last three years, Statewise;c)	the steps, if any, being taken to promote local and indigenous fine arts and performing arts, State-wise; andd)	the funds allocated, sanctioned and disbursed to authors and artists or their survivors in indigent circumstances during the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER

Similarly, Lalit Kala Akademi encourages and promotes study and research in the fields of creative arts such as painting, sculpture and graphics, and both these akademies do not deal with performing arts. However, details of grants released by these academies are enclosed in Annexure-B & Annexure-C respectively. c)	The promotion of local and indigenous fine arts is done through several schemes and grants of the Ministry of Culture. Several activities are done through regional festivals, Guru Shishya Parampara, Theatre Rejuvenation, National Cultural Exchange programs , Documentation & Research etc, for the promotion and preservation of folk and tribal arts.
