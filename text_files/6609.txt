                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Rinderpest Eradication Programme




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of fund allocated to Uttar Pradesh Government under Rinderpest  Eradication Programme;(b) the amount of fund spent for surveillance / monitoring from the allocated fund, item-wise; and(c) the details of positive results in relation to the amount spent?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a), (b) and (c)	The amount of fund allocated to Uttar Pradesh Government under National Programme on Rinderpest Surveillance and Monitoring (NPRSM), a component of the Livestock Health and Disease Control Scheme (LH&DC), and the amount of fund spent for surveillance / monitoring from the allocated fund including surveillance results for Rinderpest is as follows:Year	Fund Allocated / Released(Rs. in lakh)	Fund spent(Rs. in lakh)	Physical achievement(in nos.)	Rinderpest positive results			Village search 	Stock route search	2017-18	2.598	2.260	17137	7246	Nil2018-19	0.338	0.332	12818	7020	Nil2019-20	0.000	0.000	4438(upto Aug.19)	1923(upto Aug.19)	Nil(upto Aug.19)******
