                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4077






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Shortage of Doctors /Staff




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether there is acute shortage of AYUSH doctors and para medical staff in Government hospitals; if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of the vacancies of AYUSH doctors in the country, State/UT-wise including Chhatisgarh;(c) whether the Government has any assessment of patients offered treatment through AYUSH including AIIMS during the last three years;(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons thereof?(e) the other steps taken by the Government to overcome the shortage of AYUSH doctors?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b):	As Public Health is a State subject the information regarding acute shortage of AYUSH doctors & paramedical staff and vacancies of AYUSH doctors in the country,State/UT-wise including State of Chhattisgarh comes under the purview of the respective State Government. Therefore, no such data is maintained by the Central Government.(c) & (d):	No such assessment has been made with regard to patient treated through AYUSH including AIIMS.(e):	The availability of AYUSH doctors in the country is ensured by imparting education at graduate & postgraduate level under different streams of AYUSH.The number of AYUSH colleges which are imparting education along with student intake capacity is furnished at the Annexure.
