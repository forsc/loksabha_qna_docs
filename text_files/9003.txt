                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2590






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






New Atomic Reactors




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government  has  planned to  construct  21 new atomic reactors across  the  country  and  complete  the  same by the year 2030;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether  the  Government  has  entered  into  agreement  with  other countries to  construct atomic power plants by bilateral collaboration; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the expected financial implication estimated by the Government for this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)	Yes, Sir. (b)	The details are as follows:        Project	                  Location & State	  Capacity (MW)        Projects under Construction        KAPP 3&4	         Kakrapar, Gujarat	     2x700        RAPP 7&8	         Rawatbhata, Rajasthan	     2X700        KKNPP 3&4	         Kudankulam, Tamil Nadu	     2X1000        GHAVP 1&2	         Gorakhpur, Haryana	     2x700        PFBR*	                 Kalpakkam, Tamil Nadu       1X500        Projects accorded Administrative approval & financial sanction        KKNPP 5&6	        Kudankulam, Tamil Nadu       2X1000        PHWRs in Fleet mode        Chutka-1&2 	        Chutka, Madhya Pradesh	    2 X 700        Kaiga-5&6	        Kaiga, Karnataka	    2 X 700        Mahi Banswara- 1&2	Mahi Banswara, Rajasthan    2 X 700        GHAVP– 3&4	        Gorakhpur, Haryana	    2 X 700        Mahi Banswara- 3&4	Mahi Banswara, Rajasthan    2 X 700        * Being implemented by BHAVINI(c)&(d)The Government has entered into agreements with the Russian Federation for setting up of KKNPP 3&4 (2X1000 MW) and KKNPP 5&6 (2X1000 MW) at Kudankulam in Tamil Nadu. The sanctioned cost of KKNPP 3&4 and KKNPP 5&6 is Rs. 39849 crore and Rs. 49621 crore respectively.  The plants are constructed with 70:30 debt-equity.  Inter Governmental Agreement (IGA) for co-operation in peaceful uses of nuclear energy have been signed with the following seventeen (17) countries: Argentina, Australia, Bangladesh, Canada, Czech Republic, European Union, France, Japan, Kazakhstan, Mongolia, Namibia, Republic of Korea, Russia, Sri Lanka, United Kingdom, United States of America and Vietnam.*****
