                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






NH Projects in Telangana




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether 20 works under various National Highway projects are under execution in Telangana;(b) 	if so, the details of each work including the time of sanction and commencement of work , funds sanctioned, released, utilised and progress of work and the time by which each project is likely to be completed; and(c) 	the details of budget provided for the Ministry in 2019-20 and whether it is sufficient to meet its requirements and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)  At present, 33 works on various National Highways (NH) are under execution in the State of Telangana. Details of these works are enclosed at Annexure.(c) 	The allocation of funds during 2019-20 for Telangana at the stage of Vote on Account  is Rs. 720 Cr. Consequent upon the approval of Demand for Grants, balance allocation commensurate to the requirement of funds and inter-se-priority will be made for the financial year 2019-20.
