                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4760






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Excavation of Crude Oil




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of ongoing excavation projects to find out new crude oil block in the country, State/UT-wise;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any private companies had been given licenses for excavation; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) Currently all new oil and gas exploration blocks are awarded under Hydrocarbon Exploration and Licensing Policy (HELP). In the first three bidding rounds of HELP, 87 blocks/contract areas covering about 1,20,000 sq.km. have been awarded. After the culmination of first round of HELP, one private company has been granted Petroleum Exploration License (PEL) in 8 offshore blocks/contract areas by Central Government and in 8 onland blocks/contract areas by State Government of Rajasthan.*****
