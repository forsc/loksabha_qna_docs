                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

543






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Backlog of Vacancies




Faizal P.P. Mohammed













                                                    Rahul Gandhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether there is a backlog of vacancies, especially for 	 reserved posts in the Indian Railways;(b)	 if so, the details thereof, including class-wise 	sanctioned strength;(c)	 whether the Railways proposes to conduct recruitment  	 drives to fill the backlog;(d)	 if so, the details thereof;(e)	 the details of number of vacancies including technical, non-technical, safety related posts in the Railways as on date, post-wise, category-wise, group-wise and zone-wise separately; and(f)	the time by which these posts are lying vacant along 	with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (f) A  Statement is laid on the Table of the House.****
