                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Complaints Regarding Construction of NHs




Kotagiri Sridhar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received several complaints relating to malpractices in the construction of National Highways (NHs) in various States in the country particularly Andhra Pradesh; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the contractors are not following the proper terms and conditions of the guidelines issued by the Government;(d) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (e) the remedial measures being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (e) Ministry of  Road Transport & Highways of Union Government receives some complaints related to construction of National Highways which are dealt as per the contract conditions and departmental procedures.  Ministry issues guidelines from time to time regarding terms & conditions to be followed in the construction of NHs in the country which are binding on  contractors and concessionaires. National Highway construction works are supervised and inspected through Authority Engineers, Independent Engineers and officers of Executing agencies apart from the Regional Officers and officers of Ministry’s Head Quarters at various levels to ensure compliance to the terms and conditions of the guidelines.  ******
