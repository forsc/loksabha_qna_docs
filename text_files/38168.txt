                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Plantation of Sapling




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government ensures that new saplings are planted to replace the trees cut in the rural areas; (b) 	if so, the number of saplings planted during the last three years and the current year State-wise; and (c) 	the details of the amount spent during the current year, State-wise and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) Compensatory afforestation is one of the conditions against the forest land diverted and trees affected thereof, for which prior approval of the Central Government is accorded under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. In addition, State Forest Department also carry our afforestation in Gram samaj land on the request of concerned Gram Sabha.(b) The number of saplings planted under the Compensatory Afforestation during the last three years and the current year, State-wise is given in Annexure-I.(c) Amount spent during the current year, State-wise and year-wise is given in Annexure-II.*****
