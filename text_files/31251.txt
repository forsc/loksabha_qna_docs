                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3058






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Excise Duty on Petroleum Products




K. Subbarayan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of excise duty collected on petrol, diesel, cooking gas and other petroleum products during the last five years, year-wise; and(b) the details of total crude oil import bills by the Government during the said period for its oil marketing companies, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a):	The year-wise details of excise duty collected on all petroleum products during the last five years are given below:	Rs./CroresYear	Excise Duty2016-17	2,42,6912017-18	2,29,7162018-19	2,14,3692019-20	2,23,0572020-21	3,72,970April-September-2021-22 (P)	1,70,894      Based on data from major oil & gas companies (b):	The year wise details of total crude oil import during the last five years are given below:Crude Oil import	Value 	Rs. Crore2016-17	4,70,1592017-18	5,66,4502018-19	7,83,1832019-20	7,17,0012020-21	4,59,7792021-22 (April-October) (P)	4,53,129Source: Oil companies, (P): Provisionalx-x-x-x-x
