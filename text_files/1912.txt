                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1732






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Death due to Violence




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the number of people who died in incidents of political violence in West Bengal during the last five years along with the number of people who died in such incidents in the country during the same period;(b) whether the Government has issued any advisory to the West Bengal Government on law and order; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c)	:	Information has been received about a number of incidents of violence before, during and after the General Election-2019 resulting in deaths and injuries to several people including political workers in West Bengal. Concern on the issue was shared by Government with the State Government and an advisory was issued on 09.06.2019 asking the State Government to maintain law and order, peace and public tranquility in the State.  *****
