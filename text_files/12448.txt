                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1261






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






List of Must See Buildings and Monuments




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Archaeological Survey of India is preparing a list  of “Must See Buildings and Monuments” in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the list of buildings/ monuments in the country which came under world heritage sites and National Heritage Sites category, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether some State Governments have submitted their proposals for recognition of some Monuments as World Heritage Site for the nomination and if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard;  (d)	whether the Government has refurbished heritage buildings and monuments in the country; and(e)	if so, the details thereof along with the expenditure incurred for this purpose during each of the last three years and the current year, heritage building and monument-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Archaeological Survey of India has a Web Portal for “Must See Indian Heritage” providing information on these monuments under Archaeological Survey of India.(b)	List of monuments/ sites of national importance inscribed on the World Heritage List of UNESCO is at Annexure.(c)	Government of Telangana has submitted the proposal of recognition of Ramappa Temple at Alampur for inscription in the World Heritage List and a proposal on serial nomination of ‘Fort and Monuments of the Deccan Sultanate’ has been received from the state Govts. of Karnataka and Telangana. The dossier with respect to these monuments has been submitted to World Heritage Centre.(d) & (e)  	Archaeological Survey of India undertake regular conservation, preservation and annual maintenance of the monuments/sites declared as of national importance.
