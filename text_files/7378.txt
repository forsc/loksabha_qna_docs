                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1049






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Super Speciality Block under PMSSY




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether super speciality blocks in medical colleges/hospitals have been sanctioned under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the names of medical colleges where super speciality wings have been started in Gujarat;(d) whether there is shortage of specialist doctors in such super speciality wings; and(e) if so, the reaction of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Yes. The Government has sanctioned setting up of Super Speciality Blocks in 63 existing Government Medical Colleges/Institutions (GMCIs) in various parts of the country under different phases of PMSSY. Details are given in the enclosed Annexure.  (c):		    Construction of Super Speciality Blocks have been approved in following three existing GMCIs in Gujarat under PMSSY up-gradation programme :(i) PDU Government Medical College, Rajkot;(ii) Government Medical College, Surat; and(iii) Government Medical College, Bhavnagar. (d) & (e): 	Deployment of manpower to run and operate the facilities created under PMSSY in existing GMCIs, is the responsibility of the State Government concerned. State Governments have already been requested to make necessary provisions thereof.
