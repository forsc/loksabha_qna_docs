                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Construction of New Airports




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is working on the construction and development of new airports;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of new international airports opened in the country including Madhya Pradesh, State/UT-wise;(d) whether the Government has received any proposals from Madhya Pradesh to upgrade airports to international level;(e) if so, the details thereof;(f) the number of airports which have been expanded/upgraded to international level in Madhya Pradesh; and(g) the funds allocated/spent for the development of airports during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Government of India, Ministry of Civil Aviation has formulated a Greenfield Airport Policy for development of new airports across the country. In line with the Policy, Government of India has granted ''in principle'' approval for setting up of 21 Greenfield Airports across the country, out of which 4 airports have been operationalized.;;(c): Two new international airports have been operationalized recently in the country viz. Shirdi in Maharashtra and Kannur in Kerala.;;(d) to (f): The State Government of Madhya Pradesh vide letter dated 05.02.2019 had requested to notify Indore and Bhopal Airports as Customs Airports for operation of full-fledged international flights. Indore airport has been notified as Customs Airport to handle International flight operations. Airports Authority of India (AAI) has upgraded Bhopal, Indore and Khajuraho Airports in Madhya Pradesh for simultaneous operation of Domestic and International passengers by constructing new Integrated terminal buildings and other requisite facilities for international operations.;;(g): During the last three years, Rs. 1,171.01 Crore have been spent on the development of various AAI''s airports across the country.
