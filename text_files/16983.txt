                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

711






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Youth Development in India




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether there is any plan for youth development in India by the Government in view of the fact that India is a country of youths who are adding to the prestige of India on the basis of their own talent and merit across the world and if so, the details thereof;  (b)  the name of the institutions and organisations which are working in the country for youth development, State/UT-wise;  (c)  the status of RGNYD Shriperambudur, N.Y.K.S., N.S.S., N.C.C. in the country and the number of youths able to accommodate under it; and  (d)  if so, whether the condition of above is satisfactory? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)  (a) Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports implements different youth centric activities for youth development through its three major schemes, namely (i) Rashtriya Yuva Sashktikarana Karyakram (ii) National Service Scheme (NSS) (iii) Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD). The details of which are at Annexure-I.  (b) & (c) There are three organisations working for the development of Youth under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports are (i) Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS); (ii) National Service Scheme (NSS) and (iii) Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD). The Status of these organisations is at Annexure-II.   (d)  Yes, Sir. Ministry has been meticulously working for outreach of its programmes/schemes for youth development.  ******
