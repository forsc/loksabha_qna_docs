                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1367






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Recession in Automobile Sector




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Balak Nath
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the automobile sector has to face heavy recession post lockdown in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the sale of two-wheelers has increased during the last two or three months and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details regarding the generation of employment in automobile industries during the lastthree years and the current year, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) & (b): Yes Sir, the Automobile industry has faced a downturn due to COVID 19 pandemic.  However, sales in some segments, including two wheelers has improved over last few months.  The details are as under:                            Sales of vehicles (figure in thousands) 	 Oct. 19 – Dec. 19	Oct. 20 - Dec. 20	% changePassenger vehicle	785	898	 14.44Commercial vehicle	195	193	-1.123-wheelers	177	72	-59.252-wheelers	4,218	4,782	13.37Source: SIAM(c):  About 37 million persons are employed in direct and indirect jobs in the Indian Automobile sector			              *******
