                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2559






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Vacancies in Safety Category




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether deaths at unmanned level crossings are hovering between 12,000 and 13,000 every year between 2015 and 2020;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the cause of these deaths have been attributed to the lesser staff posted in safety category and large number of vacancies; and(d)	if so, the details of vacancies in safety category of Railways at present zone-wise and the efforts being made to appoint personnel on regular basis?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):  No, Sir.(b):  Does not arise.(c):  No, Sir. (d):  Does not arise.  ****
