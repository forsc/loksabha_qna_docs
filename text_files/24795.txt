                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

710






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Nursing Colleges




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of nursing colleges in the public and the private sectors at present in the country, State/UT wise, location-wise;(b) whether the Government has received any complaints against these colleges with regard to quality of education being provided by them; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a):	The State-wise number of nursing colleges under Government as well as in private sector in the country is enclosed at Annexure – I.(b) & (c):	Indian Nursing Council (INC) conducts regular inspection to monitor quality of education in Nursing Colleges. It also conducts surprise inspections whenever a complaint is received against any Institution. Various steps have been taken by the INC to improve the quality of nursing education. These include improvement in the norms for Institutions (Institutions are required to have their own 100 bedded hospital and to have their own building within two years of the establishment) and various programmes for training of nursing teachers.
