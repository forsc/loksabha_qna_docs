                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5187






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Video Conferencing Facility




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the  MINISTER OF ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY  be pleased to state: -(a)	the details of the places in various States including Gujarat where video conferencing facility is available;(b)	whether the people from backward and remote areas of the country are  likely  to get benefitted there from;(c)     if so, the details thereof ; and(d)     the States where the said facility is not available?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	Video conferencing facility is available at district headquarters & State Capitals in all States/UTs across the country including Gujarat for use by Government Department/officials.(b) and (c): Yes, Sir. The facility enables realtime and close monitoring of activities at district and State levels. This leads to improvement in service delivery and efficiency in administration & programme implementation across the districts in the country. The benefit from this is derived by the people in all parts of the country including those in remote and backward areas, by way of improved administration and better delivery of services. (d):	The video conferencing facility is available in all States and UTs. ******
