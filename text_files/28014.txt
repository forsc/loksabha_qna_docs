                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Village Gram Panchayats adopted under Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of villages/gram panchayats adopted under Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) and the number of villages which have been developed so far since 2018 along with the details thereof, particularly in Jharkhand;(b) 	the details of the amount spent so far in this regard;(c) 	whether the Government is considering to allocate additional funds for all round development of more than one village under SAGY, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) 	whether the Government will consider providing special financial assistance for each of the gram panchayats selected under SAGY for implementing the activities, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) : The Hon’ble Members of Parliament (MPs) have adopted 976 Gram Panchayats for development under Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY)  till 3rd August 2021, since 2018. The Gram Panchayats identified under SAGY prepare a Village Development Plan(VDP) which is a roadmap for the holistic development of the GPs.The list of Gram Panchayats which have completed all the projects that are proposed under Village Development Plan since 2018 including Jharkhand as per the information uploaded by States/UTs on SAGY portal (http://saanjhi.gov.in) is given in Annexure-I. (b) to (d) : SAGY is a scheme based on convergence of already existing schemes of Government of India, State schemes, partnership with Private, Voluntary and Cooperative (PVC) sectors, Gram Panchayats’ own revenue, resources mobilised in kind and labour and Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds without provision of any funding specifically under the scheme. Therefore, there is no provision and also no proposal under consideration to allocate any funds specially for this programme.******* AnnexureAnnexure-I referred in point (a) of reply to Lok Sabha unstarred question No.3554 scheduled for answer on 10th August 2021The list of Gram Panchayats which have completed all the projects that are proposed under Village Development Plan since 2018 including Jharkhand as per the information uploaded by States/UTs on SAGY portal (http://saanjhi.gov.in).
