                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

141






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Rail Over Bridge/Flyover in Wardha




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the construction work of Acharya Vinoba Bhave rail over bridge/flyover in Wardha City of Vidarbha region has been stopped;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the reasons therefor and the time by which the sanctioned work is likely to be started; and (d) 	the steps being taken to complete the work on the said project in the stipulated time frame?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
