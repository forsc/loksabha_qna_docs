                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4209






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Committee on MSME Sector




Ritesh Pandey













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is taking any action in furtherance of the recommendation of Sri U.K. Sinha Committee on Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) sector;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government has any data related to Khadi export after the allocation of a separate unique HS code to Khadi;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e)	whether there is a real boost in Khadi export after the allocation of a separate unique HS code to Khadi and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b): 	The U.K.Sinha Committee on Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises has made recommendations with regard to amendments in MSME Development Act, strengthening of financial delivery mechanism, improving marketing support, encouraging technology adoption and strengthening of cluster development support for MSMEs etc. Concerned Ministries/Departments have taken necessary steps for implementation of these recommendations.(c) to (e):  	Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) does not directly export Khadi and Village Industries (KVI) products. The products produced by KVI Institutions/units get exported by them directly or through merchant exporters/agencies.  The export of KVI products during last three years and current year is as follows:<pre>Year	KVI Export  (Rs. in Crore)2016-17	269.392017-18	270.352018-19	266.592019-20(as on 31.1.2020)	110.00 (Provisional)</pre>
