                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1996






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Import of GM Seeds




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any mechanism to ban and monitor the import of prohibited Genetically Modified (GM) seeds in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether tonnes of prohibited GM seeds are being imported for agriculture activities; and (d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) & (b) Imports of Genetically Modified (GM) Organisms including GM Seeds are permitted by Directorate General of Foreign Trade only after due approvals. Import of GM Seeds for Research &Development (R&D) purpose is facilitated by Indian Council of Agricultural Research -National Bureau of Plant Genetic Resources after technical clearance from Review Committee on Genetic Manipulation. Import of GM Seeds for non-R&D purpose is allowed after approval of Genetic Engineering Appraisal Committee.(c) No Sir.(d) &   (e) Does not arise in view of reply at (c) above..***
