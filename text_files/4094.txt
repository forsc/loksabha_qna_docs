                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3645






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Construction of Roads Under PMGSY in HP




Suresh Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of roads approved for Shimla Parliamentary Constituency in Himachal Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years;(b)	the details regarding the said roads, name-wise;(c)	the amount earmarked for the construction of the said roads; and(d)	the progress made so far in the construction work?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	The information in respect of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) roads are maintained Block-wise/District-wise and not Parliamentary Constituency-wise. However, as reported by the Government of Himachal Pradesh, 289 Nos of road works have been approved for Shimla Parliamentary Constituency in Himachal Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) at an estimated cost of Rs. 1106.25 crore during the last three years. The District-wise/Block-wise list of roads sanctioned under PMGSY are available at www.omms.nic.in-> Progress Monitoring-> Physical Progress of works.    Out of 289 road works, 41 road works have been completed, 164 are in progress, 62 road works have been recently awarded and 22 are in tendering stage.*****
