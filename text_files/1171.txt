                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1042






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Target Construction of NH’s




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to construct 60,000 Km. of National Highways anddouble the length of these roads by 2022;(b) if so, whether the Government has constituted any policy regarding land acquisitionwhich is the biggest hurdle for delay in a number of National Highway projects and ifso, the details thereof and the salient features of the said policy;(c) whether the Government is focusing to expedite some of its flagship road projectssuch as Bharatmala and Sagarmala and if so, the details thereof and the steps taken inthis regard; and(d) the targets for NH construction achieved by the Government till date?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Government of India has approved Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I for development of Highway stretches with an aggregate length of about 34,800 km, which is targeted for completion by 2021-22.  In addition to this, road stretches with total length 48,877 km (approximately) have also been envisaged for development.  (b)	The Ministry of Road Transport & Highways has evolved a manual of guidelines on land acquisition for National Highways which include comprehensive policy guidelines regarding applicability of Right to Fair Compensation in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (RFCTLARR Act, 2013) to the National Highways Act, 1956, determination of market value of land, multiplication factor, purchase of land through consent of land owners, acquisition of land for missing plot, administrative charges for land acquisition for National Highway projects etc.(c)	The Sagarmala programme is within the purview of Ministry of Shipping.  However, Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I is dealt with by Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, which includes development of port connectivity routes.  The progress of projects under this programme is monitored electronically for ensuring expeditious completion of these projects.  (d)	The details of total length of highway stretches constructed for the past five years is as under:Year	Length of highway stretches constructed during last 5 years (km)2014-15	4,4102015-16	6,0612016-17	8,2312017-18	9,8292018-19	10,855Total	39,386*****
