                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1842






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Small and Medium Irrigation Projects




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any proposal regarding small and medium irrigation projects of various States including Chhattisgarh is pending with the Government; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (c) the reasons for pendency of such proposals; (d) the details of the projects sanctioned during the current year, State-wise; and(e) the details of the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to accord approval to the pending projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI  & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) & (e)    The status of medium irrigation projects including that of Chhattisgarh State, received in Central Water Commission (CWC) for appraisal where states have responded as per guidelines of appraisal is at Annexure-I. The completion of appraisal process by CWC is dependent upon compliance of the said guidelines by the concerned State Governments, in this regard.Further, appraisal of Minor Irrigation Schemes comes under the purview of the State Technical Advisory Committee (TAC).(d)      Revised-Cost Estimate (RCE) of two (2) medium projects namely Purna Barrage-2 (Ner Dhamana) and Shelgaon Barrage and one (1) new project namely Seven Pneumatically Operated Gated Weirs in series on Girna river in Maharashtra have been accepted by the Advisory Committee of DoWR,RD&GR during the year 2019-20. Further, no medium project has reached the stage of consideration by the Advisory Committee of DoWR,RD&GR during current year. Annexure-I Annexure referred to in the reply to part (a) to (c) & (e) of Unstarred Question No 1842 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 22.09.2020 regarding “Small and Medium Irrigation Projects”.
