                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Natural Calamities




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of persons affected by natural calamities during the last four years, year-wise and State-wise;(b) the details of the damages and losses suffered as a result of natural calamities during each of the last four years and the current year, State-wise; and(c) whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Governments seeking financial assistance for the losses suffered and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) and (b):	The primary responsibility for disaster management rests with the State Governments. This Ministry does not maintain the centralized data regarding persons affected by natural calamities. However, the details of damages and losses due to natural calamities, as reported by the State Governments/Union Territories Administrations, during last four years and current year are at Annexure. (c): During the year 2019-20, Ministry of Home Affairs has received memorandum from 16 State Governments namely, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Nagaland, Odisha, Punjab, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Tripura and West Bengal seeking additional financial assistance from National Disaster Response Fund.*****
