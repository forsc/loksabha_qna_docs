                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

509






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Airports in Uttar Pradesh




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) the number of airports developed in Uttar Pradesh along with the present status thereof:(b) whether the Government has selected the airports for development under Udan scheme in Uttar Pradesh; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c): Ministry of Civil Aviation launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) on 21-10-2016 to stimulate regional air connectivity and making air travel affordable to the masses. UDAN is a market driven scheme. The interested airlines based on their assessment of demand on particular routes submit their proposals at the time of bidding under UDAN from time to time. An airport which is included in the awarded routes of UDAN is developed under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme for commencement of UDAN flights. Under UDAN, 17 (Seventeen) RCS airports, namely, Agra, Aligarh, Prayagraj, Bareilly, Kanpur, Chitrakoot, Ayodhaya, Gazipur, Jhansi, Moradabad, Kushinagar, Meerut, Muirpur, Azamgarh, Shravasti, Saharanpur & Hindon  in Uttar Pradesh have been identified for operationalization so far. Out of these 17 identified airports, 5 airports, namely, Agra, Prayagraj, Bareilly, Kanpur, Hindon have been operationalized and Selected Airline Operators(SAOs) have commenced UDAN flights.
