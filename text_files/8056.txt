                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1676






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Repair of Flood Affected Roads




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has noticed that most of the roads in southern States including Kerala have been damaged due to the unprecedented heavy rains and landslides recently and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government has assessed the quantum of such damage and if so, the details and the outcome thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to give any special package for the repair/construction of such roads in the flood affected areas and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether work on any new National Highway has been started/is likely to start in Kerala and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) The aggregate length of National Highway stretches entrusted to State PWD which are reported as damaged in Southern States including Kerala is given below:  Sr. No.	State	Affected length of NH (in Km)1	Tamil Nadu	682	Andhra Pradesh	5693	Kerala	4004	Karnataka	8425	Telangana	243The details of funds allocated for incurring expenditure on maintenance and repairs of National Highways entrusted with respective State PWDs under Flood Damage Repair (FDR- New) and Periodical Renewal (PR) for the year 2019-20 are given below:Sl. No.	State	Allocation under FDR (Rs in Cr)	Sanction ceiling under PR(Rs in Cr)	Total (Rs in Cr)1	Tamil Nadu	2.00	50.00	52.002	Andhra Pradesh	4.50	88.50	93.003	Kerala	5.00	178.00	183.004	Karnataka	5.00	175.50	180.55	Telangana	0.00	41.00	41.00In respect of National Highways entrusted with NHAI, the damaged stretches have been repaired/restored from the existing Contractors/Concessionaires.  (c)	This Ministry does not give any special package for repair/construction of roads in the flood affected areas. (d)	Development and maintenance of National Highways is a continuous process and works on National Highways including new National Highways are taken up based on traffic, inter-se priority and availability of funds.	*****
