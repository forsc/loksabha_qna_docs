                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1498






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Visa Free Access




Jagdambika Pal 













                                                    Rodmal Nagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the total number of countries providing visa free access to Indian citizens;(b) 	the total number of countries offering visa on arrival access to Indian citizens;(c) 	the total number of countries to which Indian Government offers Visa free access for its citizens;(d) 	Whether the Government has made any provision to offer rebate for any country in this regard; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	As on date, 16 countries provide visa-free access to Indian citizens (list of countries at Annexure-I).(b)	As on date, 38 countries provide visa-on-arrival access to Indian citizens (list of countries at Annexure-II).  (c)	Citizens of 3 countries (Nepal, Bhutan and Maldives) are granted visa-free access and citizens of 3 countries (Japan, South Korea and UAE) are granted visa-on-arrival by the Government of India.(d) & (e)  Government of India has been progressively liberalizing its visa policy with the objective of promoting tourism and investments into India from other countries.  Keeping in mind the same objective, India has extended e-Visa scheme to 169 foreign countries/ territories till date.  These initiatives are expected to encourage other countries to reciprocate and facilitate travel of Indian citizens to their countries.* * *
