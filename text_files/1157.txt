                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

886






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Gift on Marriage of Muslim Girls




Lallu Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	whether the Government has approved the scheme of providing 51,000 		rupees as gift on the marriage of the Muslim girls who have completed 		graduation and if so, the details thereof; 	(b) 	whether the Government has issued any guidelines to disburse the said 		amount in Uttar Pradesh; 	(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and	(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	& (d)	Maulana Azad Education Foundation (MAEF) is an autonomous   body under the Ministry of Minority Affairs, Govt. of India. The schemes of MAEF are implemented after approval of its Governing Body/General Body. The General Body and Governing Body of MAEF had plannedin 2017-18 to provide one time assistance of Rs.51,000/- as ShadiShagun to beneficiaries of Begum Hazrat Mahal National Scholarship, who would be eligible for the benefit of this scheme after graduation. The aim of the scheme is to reduce the school dropouts amongst the six centrally notified minority girlsand also encourage them to complete theireducation.(b)	No, Sir.(c)	Does not arise.*****
