                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3097






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Vacant reserved posts for SCs, STs and OBC in Central Universities




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of sanctioned reserved posts for Scheduled Castes (SCs), Scheduled Tribes (STs), and Other Backward Classes (OBCs) in all Central Universities and Research Institutions and details thereof; (b) the total number of vacant reserved posts for SCs, STs, and OBCs and in IITs/IIMs, Indian Institute of Science and Central Universities and the same have not been filled so far and whether it is also a fact that certain reserved posts have been filled under General category or kept vacant for many years; and (c) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)	to (c):  The details of total number of sanctioned and vacant reserved posts for Scheduled Castes (SCs), Scheduled Tribes (STs), and Other Backward Classes (OBCs) in Central Universities (CUs), Indian Institute of Technology (IITs), Indian Institute of Management (IIMs), Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISERs) and Indian Institute of Science (IISc) under purview of Ministry of Education are as under:Name of Institution	Total Number of Sanctioned Reserved Posts	Total Number of Vacant Reserved Posts	SC	ST	OBC	SC	ST	OBC42 Central Universities            (as on 01.01.2021)	5714	3135	7589	2255	1320	39493 Sanskrit Central Universities                         (as  on 09.03.2021)	120	69	182	14	9	24Indira Gandhi National Open Universities (as on 31.012.2020)	380	180	346	157	88	231IITs (Non-faculty) (as on 31.12.2020)	901	410	1291	325	193	539IIMs 	55	24	96	34	19	60IISERs	71	19	153	28	11	67IISc	168	84	303	34	46	272Total	7409	3921	9960	2847	1686	5142Taking into account all the circumstances and in order to ensure protection of the rights of SCs, STs and OBCs in appointments, Central Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers’ Cadre) Act 2019 notified on 12th July, 2019 to provide reservation of posts in direct recruitment in teachers’ cadre in Central Educational Institutions by considering the University as a Unit.Now, after implementation of “The   Central   Educational   Institutions (Reservation in Teachers’ Cadre) Act, 2019”, the OBC reservation has been implemented at all levels.  Further, UGC vide its D. O. letter No. F. 1-14/2019 (CPP-II) dated 4th June, 2019 UGC has prepared the Guidelines for Recruitment of Faculty in Universities, Colleges and Institutions Deemed to be Universities outlining the selection procedure and the time frame for recruitment which has been circulated to all Universities to adhere the guidelines. The Universities have also been requested to take steps to ensure that the vacancies in the Universities as well as in the Colleges affiliated to University are filled up at the earliest.Subsequently, UGC vide its letter No. F. 1-14/2019 (CPP-II) dated 31st July, 2019, 7th August, 2019, 5th September, 2019 and 22nd October, 2019 again requested to ensure that vacant positions in University as well as colleges affiliated to University are filled at the earliest and the status of the recruitment process is uploaded on the University Activity Monitoring Portal of UGC. *****
