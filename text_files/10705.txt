                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3962






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Target for Construction of NHs




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had set target to construct 16,420 km. National Highways (NHs) and grant approval for another 20,000 km. NHs during the financial year 2018-19 and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government had set a target to construct 45 km. per day during the FY 2018-19; and(c) if so, the details of the targets achieved out of the above two?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) Ministry had set the target of 15,000 km for award of works and 10,000 km for construction of  National Highways against which 5,493 km were awarded & 10,855 km constructed during  the financial year 2018-19.  Construction rate was 29.74 km per day during the financial year 2018-19.*****
