                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4088






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government is currently implementing Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana;  (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the achievements made during the last three years particularly in the state of Odisha; (c) 	whether the financial assistance allocated by the Union Government under the scheme has been reaching its registered/intended beneficiaries; and   (d) 	the funds utilized under the scheme during 2019-20, State/UT- wise including Odisha?






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER FOR WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) to (c) Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Women and Child Development implements Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) w.e.f. 01.01.2017. However, in Odisha, PMMVY is not being implemented by the State Government. As on 15.03.2021, maternity benefits of Rs.8310.60 crores (including Central and State share) have been disbursed amongst 1.91 crores eligible beneficiaries under PMMVY since inception of the scheme.
