                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2151






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Elevated Flyover between Agra and Fatehpur Sikri




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any plan was drawn in the past to connect Agra city with NH-11 between 	Agra and Fatehpur Sikri by way of elevated flyover; (b) 	if so, the details and the present status thereof; and (c)	if not, the other steps taken/being taken by the Government to address the issue of 	long traffic jams at Shah Ganj area of Agra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)	The stretch from Agra to Mahuar of NH-11 (Agra Jaipur corridor) about 18 km was identified as Inter-Corridor route under Bharatmala Pariyojana. Detailed Project Report for this stretch has been initiated. Development of road stretches is taken up depending upon the outcome of DPR, project viability and availability of funds.*****
