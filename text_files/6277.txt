                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

96






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Status of Tourism Project - Ganga Ghat




Jugal Kishore Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of tourism project ‘Ganga-ghat’ being built in Sundarbani, district, Rajori of Jammu-Kashmir; (b) 	the time by which the said project is likely to be completed; (c) 	whether the total funds for this project will be released by this year; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Ministry of Tourism, has not sanctioned any project for development of ‘Ganga-ghat’ in Sundarbani, district, Rajori of Jammu-Kashmir. However, the State Government has sanctioned a project for development of ‘Ganga Ghat’ in Sunderbani District Rajouri for Rs.35.0 lakh during 2019-20.(b):	The timeline of project is two years, i.e. till 2020-21.(c) and (d):		Rs.10.0 lakh has been released during the year 2019-20.*******
