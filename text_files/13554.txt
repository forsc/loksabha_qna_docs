                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2389






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Four Laning of National Highway




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of work on the proposed four lane National Highway from Panipat via 	Shamli and Muzaffarnagar to Nagina and Bijnor to Kotdwar in Uttarakhand; and (b)	the time by which the said project is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b)  The stretch from Panipat- Shamli- Muzaffarnagar- Bijnor-Nagina/Kotwali of National Highway-709 AD is taken up for four laning by National Highway Authority of India under four packages; out of which, for two packages namely, Panipat to Shamli and Shamli to Muzaffarnagar, Letter of Award have been issued. The other two packages, ie.  Muzaffarnagar to Miranpur and Bijnor to Kotawali are at bidding stage. Further, the work of four laning in Bijnor to Kotdwar of National Highway-119 is also at bidding stage. The scheduled time for completion is 24 months from Appointed Date.*****
