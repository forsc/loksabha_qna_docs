                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2907






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Redevelopment of Airport under PPP Model




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently decided to redevelop some of the airports in the country via Public Private Partnership (PPP) model;(b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which the redevelopment process is likely to be completed?(c) whether the Government had assigned the task of redevelopment of airports to some private players during the last few years; and(d) if so, the status of redevelopment of such airports as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a)  to (d):  Airports Authority of India (AAI) has leased out Delhi and Mumbai Airports in 2006 to M/s Delhi International Airport Limited (DIAL) and M/s Mumbai International Airport Limited (MIAL) respectively for Operations, Management and Development under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode. Since then, DIAL and MIAL have undertaken and completed various development works at these airports including construction of new terminal buildings, airside expansion etc. This has led to significant improvement in the infrastructure at these airports.;;Airports Authority of India (AAI) has recently awarded six airports namely, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Lucknow, Guwahati, Thiruvananthapuram and Mangaluru for Operations, Management and Development under PPP for a period of 50 years. Development/redevelopment of airports is a continuous process and is undertaken by the concerned airport operator from time to time depending upon commercial viability, traffic demand, availability of land etc.
