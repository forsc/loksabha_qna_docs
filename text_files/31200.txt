                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2877






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Trains to Kurukshetra




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to connect newly identified religious heritage sites by train for the convenience of pilgrims; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government is planning to connect Kurukshetra Parliamentary Constituency with newly announced pilgrimage site Soran by any train for the convenience of devotees and to promote transport connectivity; (d) if so, the details thereof; (e) if not, whether the Government has any plans to connect the said sites by new special trains to improve to such heritage sites and to develop tourism; and (f) if so, the details in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (f)  Indian Railways, through Indian Railway Catering & Tourism Corporation (IRCTC), operates various tourist trains such as Bharat Darshan/Aastha Circuit Tourist, Pilgrim Tourist Trains, State Special Tourist Trains, Deluxe Tourist Trains, Buddhist Circuit Trains, and Luxury Tourist Trains which are connecting major historical cities/tourist places of the country. The itineraries/destinations covered are planned by IRCTC based on market demand and is a dynamic process.   This is an ongoing process. However, at present, there is no proposal for additional services from Kurukshetra for pilgrim site(s). Besides, introduction of new train services on Indian Railway is an ongoing process subject to operational feasibility and traffic justification.*****
