                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Industrial Training Institutes




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number of Industrial Training Institutes opened in Chhattisgarh under the Skill Development Programme;(b)	 whether all of the said institutes are functional, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c)	 the number of youth nominated for the said training programmes?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) A Statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *298 RAISED BY HON’BLE M.P. SHRI SANTOSH PANDEY REGARDING ‘INDUSTRIAL TRAINING INSTITUTES’ FOR REPLY ON 09.12.2019(a) to (c) Yes Sir. There are 233 (123 Government and 110 Private) ITIs in the State of Chhattisgarh, which are affiliated under National Council of Vocational Training and imparting training under Craftsmen Training Scheme.Total seating capacity in above 233 ITIs for August, 2018 is 24431 (11562 in 123 Government ITIs and 12869 in 110 Private ITIs) and trainees on roll is 24333 (12108 in 123 Government ITIs and 12225 in 110 Private ITIs).
