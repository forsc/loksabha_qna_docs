                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Food Allocation under PMGKAY-IV




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the allocation under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY-IV); (b) 	whether the Government has allocated a total of 198.78 Lakh MT of food grains under it in July-November 2021; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the total quota allocated for Andhra Pradesh in the fourth phase; and (e) 	the details of the quantity of stocks lifted by various States in the 3rd Phase of PMGKAY-III in May-June, 2021, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):  The Government has allocated a total of 198.78 Lakh MT of food grains under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana Phase-IV (PMGKAY-IV)  i.e. July-November 2021.(b):  	Yes Sir.(c):  	State-wise details of allocation of foodgrains under PMGKAY-IV (July-November 2021) is placed at Annexure-I.(d):  State of Andhra Pradesh has been allocated a total quantity of approx 670553 MT rice under PMGKAY-IV (July-November 2021).(e):  State-wise details of allocation and lifting of foodgrains under PMGKAY-III (May-June 2021) is enclosed as Annexure-II.***Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (c) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION No.2323 FOR ANSWER ON 03.08.2021 IN THE LOK SABHAAllocation of foodgrains under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) Phase-IV (July-November 2021)SN	States/UTs 	Total Allocation under PMGKAY-IV(July-Nov 2021)[in MTs]	Provisional likely subsidy in Rs.
