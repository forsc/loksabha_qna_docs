                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

880






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Funds For Madhya Pradesh under Jal Jeevan Mission




Sandhya Ray














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of the funds allocated, sanctioned, utilized in Madhya Pradesh under the Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in the financial year 2021-2022;(b) the details of the present status of tap water supply in rural areas of Madhya Pradesh including Bhind district, district-wise;(c)  the total number of households provided with tap water connection since the launch of the mission till date in Madhya Pradesh including Bhind district; district-wise; and(d)  the number of schools and anganwadi centres having access to piped water supply in Madhya Pradesh including Bhind district, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) The details regarding funds under Jal Jeevan Mission for Madhya Pradesh during financial year 2021-22 as of 29.11.2021 are as follows:							(Amount in ? Crore)Central Share	Expenditure under State ShareOpening balance	Allocation	Fund drawn	Total fund available	Reported expenditure	191.61	5,116.79	2,558.39	2,750.00	1,166.14	1,181.04	Source: JJM-IMIS(b) to (d) In Madhya Pradesh, since launch of Jal Jeevan Mission, more than 30.53 lakh rural households (25%) have been provided tap water connections. Thus, as on 29.11.2021, provisions of tap water supply have been made available to 44.06 lakh (36%) rural households in the State. Further, as on 29.11.2021, in 66,938 (72%) schools and 39,370 (59%) anganwadi centres, provision of tap water supply has been made in the State. The details are annexed.*****
