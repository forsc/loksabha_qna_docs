                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3803






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Beneficiaries under NRLM




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of beneficiaries of Rashtriya Gramin Arthik Parivartan Pariyojana and National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM) being run under Deen Dayal Antyodaya Yojana, State-wise;(b)	the type of benefits given to beneficiaries under the said scheme along with the details of possible changes;(c)	the amount released to the State of Maharashtra under the scheme during the last three years and the current year; and(d)	the details of the amount released or likely to be released to Sangli district of Maharashtra under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Till 31st May, 2019, 5.96 crore rural poor women have been  obilized in to 54.07 lakh women Self Help Groups (SHGs) under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM). The state-wise details are at Annexure –I. The Rashtriya Gramin Arthik Parivartan Pariyojana (National Rural Economic Transformation Project)  has approved by the Government on 24th April 2019 with loan assistance from the World Bank under DAY-NRLM..(b):	DAY-NRLM provides Revolving Funds (RF) at the rate of Rs. 10,000-15,000 per SHG and Community Investment Support Fund (CISF) to the extent of Rs. 2,50,000 per SHG to support their income generating and livelihoods activities. Till May, 2019 capitalisation support to the tune of Rs. 7,041.40 crores have been provided to SHGs and their Federations. Apart from this DAY-NRLM also facilitates credit mobilisation for SHGs in the form of bank loans. Since 2013-14 the SHGs have accessed bank credit of Rs. 2.25 lakh crore. The mission also provides for interest subvention on such loans upto Rs. 3.00 lakhs per SHG. Cumulatively, Rs. 3,300 crores has been provided as interest subvention.In order to understand the changes at the household level DAY-NRLM commissioned an independent assessment by Institute of Rural Management Anand (IRMA) in January – March, 2017. The report has brought out the following findings:(i)	The programme has helped to enhance the productive assets of the target groups.(ii)	It has also developed saving habit amongst the beneficiaries and increased their capacity to avail higher loans.(iii)	The household income of the target group has shown a 22% increase due to income from enterprises.In addition, the programme has significantly contributed to development of social capital and empowerment of women from economically weaker sections in the rural areas.(c):	The amount released to the State of Maharashtra under the Scheme is given blow:-(Amount in Lakhs)State	        2016-17	            2017-18	           2018-19	           2019-20	      Central    Central    Central    Central     Central   Central      Central     Central                           Allocation  Release   Allocation Release     Allocation Release    Allocation  Release* Maharashtra	3034.46	 3097.82   15902.89	15093.06   22106.73  29977.34	 30513.46   15256.73* Central Release up to June, 2019(d):	The details of the amount released to Sangli district of Maharashtra under the scheme during last three years and current financial year 2019-20 is given below:-                                                (Amount in Lakhs)Sr. No	  Year	     Allocation 	Release1	2016-17	        276	         2762	2017-18	        299	         2623	2018-19	        318	         3014	2019-20*       1076	          50            (Note – In 2019-20 release amount is up to June -2019)                    *****
