                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2014






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Construction of ROBs




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether several ROBs are being constructed on various roads;(b)	if so, the number of ROBs sanctioned during the last three years;(c)	the number of ROBs sanctioned for Maharashtra and Rajasthan during the last three years along with the number of those ROBs out of the same the construction of which has been completed; and(d)	the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2014 BY SHRI RAMDAS C. TADAS TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 03.07.2019 REGARDING CONSTRUCTION OF ROBs(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. As per Pink Book, total of 1571 Road over Bridges (ROBs) are sanctioned. During the last three years (2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19) 575 ROBs have been sanctioned in Pink Book.(c) & (d): In last three years (2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19), 65 ROBs has been sanctioned in the State of Maharashtra and 12 ROBs has been sanctioned in the State of Rajasthan. Out of these, 3 ROBs in the State of Maharashtra have been completed. Remaining works for construction of ROBs in both the States are at various stages of planning, estimation and execution.*****
