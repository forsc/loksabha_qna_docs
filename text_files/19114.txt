                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






AIBP in Uttar Pradesh




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any proposal sent by the Government of Uttar Pradesh under Accelerated Irrigation Benefit programme (AIBP) is pending for approval; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (c) the time by which these proposals are likely to be sanctioned?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c)  During 2016-17, ninety-nine (99) on-going Major/Medium irrigation projects (and their 7 phases) under PMKSY-AIBP having ultimate irrigation potential of 76.03 lakh hectare and balance estimated cost of Rs.77,595 crore [Central Assistance (CA) component of Rs.31,342 crore] have been prioritized in consultation with States for completion in phases. Funding mechanism through NABARD has also been approved by the Government for both Central and State share of all prioritized projects. CA is released from time to time as and when eligible CA proposals have been received from the State Government(s).Out of the above prioritized projects taken up under PMKSY-AIBP, four (4) projects, namely Bansagar Canal, Arjun Sahayak, SaryuNaharPariyojana (National Project) and Madhya Ganga Canal Ph-II are from Uttar Pradesh. Total CA and State Share of Rs 1,006.07 crore and Rs.4,111.61 crore respectively, has been released to these projects during 2016-17 to 2019-20. During 2020-21, so far CA  and State Share of Rs.391.84 crore and Rs.1,270.40 crore respectively, has been released for these projects of UP under PMKSY-AIBP. Presently, no proposal for the release CA and State Share for these projects is pending with this Ministry.*****
