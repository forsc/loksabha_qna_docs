                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Production of Fruits and Vegetables




Ramcharan Bohra













                                                    Bharti Pravin Pawar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has set any targets for the production of fruits and vegetables in various States of the country during the last five year plan and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the details of the production of fruits and vegetables in various States of the country during the last three years and the current year, State-wise; (c) 	the details of assistance provided by the Government to various States for promotion of horticulture crops during the last five year plan, Statewise; (d) 	the various steps taken by the Government to achieve the said targets during the said period; (e) 	whether the Government proposes to help vegetable and fruit cultivators in marketing of their produce so they may get remunerative prices, if so, the assistance provided by the Government in this regard; and (f) 	the steps taken by the Government to encourage research in the field of vegetable and fruit production in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	   A total production target of 310.00  Million Metric Ton  for the horticulture produce including fruits and vegetables in various States of the country was set during  the last five year plan (XII Plan - 2012-2017).     (b):	     The State-wise production of Fruits and Vegetables during 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 (3rd Advance Estimates), are at Annexure I. The details (1st Estimate) of Fruits and Vegetables production in various States of the Country for the current financial year, 2020-21 is yet to be finalized.Contd....2/--2-(c):	   The State-wise details of the funds released (GoI Share) to various States under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for promotion of horticulture during last five year plan (XII Plan - 2012-2017) are at Annexure II.(d):	  During the last five year plan, for promotion of horticulture throughout the country, financial & technical assistance was provided to States/UTs  under MIDH for following major interventions/activities: •	Setting up of nurseries, tissue culture units for production of quality seed and planting material. •	Area expansion i.e. Establishment of new orchards and gardens for fruits, vegetables, and flowers. •	Rejuvenation of unproductive, old, and senile orchards. •	Protected cultivation, i.e. poly-house, green-house, etc, to improve the productivity & grow off season high value vegetables and flowers. •	Organic farming and certification. •	Creation of water resources structures and watershed management. •	Bee-keeping for pollination. •	Horticulture Mechanization. •	Creation of Post Harvest Management and Marketing infrastructure.(e):        Under MIDH, financial assistance is also provided for setting up of terminal markets, wholesale markets, Rural Market /apni mandies, retail markets/outlets, static/mobile vending cart/ platform with cool chamber and functional infrastructures under the component of Market Infrastructures for which credit linked back ended subsidy is provided at the rate of 25% to 55% of admissible project cost. Apart from the above, Government is also implementing Market Intervention Scheme (MIS) for procurement of agricultural and horticultural commodities which are perishable in nature and are not covered under the Price Support Scheme (PSS).(f):       Under MIDH, assistance is also provided for promotion of research activities through Central Government Institutes under ICAR, CSIR, SAUs, National Level Govt. Agencies and others location specific Institutes to take up need based applied research and development works in areas of seed and planting material including import of planting material, technology standardization and technology acquisition and imparting training and FLD, on project mode with 100% assistance.            The research steps are also being taken by 23 Institutes/ Directorates/ NRCs and 12 AICRPs under Horticulture Science Division of ICAR to help increase in Fruits and Vegetables production in the country.
