                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2379






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Contract between BHEL and Essar Company




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any Contract or Sub-contract has been signed between Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) and Essar company and if so, the details thereof;(b) the status of such agreement; and(c) the details of projects commissioned, completed, abandoned and breached under the agreement?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARJUN RAM MEGHWAL)(a) to (c): Yes Sir, Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) and M/s Essar have awarded various purchase orders to each other.  Details and status of orders for the years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 (upto January 2021) are annexed at Annexure I & II .*****  ANNEXURE-I A.
