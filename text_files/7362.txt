                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1029






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Funds to AB-PMJAY




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has spent Rs. 500 cr. under Ayushman Bharat- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojna (ABPMJAY) on procedure such as cataract, deliveries and dialysis that is available free of cost under national programme, if so,the details thereof; and(b) the proposition of funds that may have gone to the private sector, State-wise, details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Hospitalization cover under Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is available through empanelled hospitals. Both Public and Private hospitals have been empanelled under the scheme. As on 20.11.2019, 20,905 hospitals are empanelled. Out of these, 9,905 are private hospitals.1392 treatment packages across 24 specialities and 1 unspecified package are available under AB-PMJAY. Inclusion of some of these procedures, which are also available under the National Health Programmes, in packages of AB-PMJAY facilitates the access of beneficiary to both public and private hospitals and also ensures quality of treatment.    The details of claim submitted by empanelled hospitals for treatment of AB-PMJAY beneficiaries State-wise as on 20.11.2019 is attached as Annexure.
