                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1922






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Biofloc Fish Farming




Nisith Pramanik














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is promoting biofloc fish farming; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the details of promotion of biofloc fish farming in West Bengal and especially in Cooch Behar Parliamentary Constituency; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING:(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) & (b) :  Biofloc fish farming is one of the advanced aquaculture technologies and Government is promoting this technology for enhancing fish production wherever feasible. This is a demand driven innovative component under the Blue Revolution scheme and hence, subsidy assistance for undertaking biofloc fish farming are considered on receipt of proposals from the respective State Governments. As per records, proposal for biofloc fish farming has not been received from Cooch Behar Parliamentary Constituency.(c) :  Does not arise.*****
