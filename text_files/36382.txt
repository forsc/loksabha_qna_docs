                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3431






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Vande Bharat Trains




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to introduce the Vande Bharat Trains through out the country in the coming years;(b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government proposes to replace Rajdhani, Shatabdi and Duranto trains with Vande Bharat Trains; (d) if so, whether the operation of these trains is likely to be discontinued completely; and (e) if so, the action plan chalked out in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e) Presently, 02 pairs of Vande Bharat Express namely, 22435/22436 New Delhi-Varanasi Vande Bharat Express and 22439/22440 New Delhi-Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra Vande Bharat Express are in operation. Operation of Vande Bharat trains and replacement of services, over Indian Railways network, is an ongoing process subject to operational feasibility, commercial justification, availability of rolling stock etc.*****
