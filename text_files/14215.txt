                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2802






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Stree Swabhiman Yojana




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a) the number of Common Service Centres set up for adolescent girls and women under ''Stree Swabhiman Yojana'' of the Government across the country’ State/UT-wise including Gujarat, Bihar and West Bengal;(b) the number of women entrepreneurs provided with employment so far under the said scheme, State/UT-wise; and(c) whether sanitary pads have been made available to women at affordable rates by the Government under the said scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): 764 Common Services Centres (CSCs) has set up for adolescent girls and women under ''Stree Swabhiman Yojana'' across the country and 3359 womenentrepreneurs have been provided with employment. The details are at Annexure.(c):  The sanitary pads under the Stree Swabhiman Project are being made available to women at an affordable rate by the Common Services Centres (CSC) Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE). The rates are decided by Common Services Centre’s (CSC’s) Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs) based on local market.
