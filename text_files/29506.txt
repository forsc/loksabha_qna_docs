                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Polytechnic Programme




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government provides financial assistance to states including Kerala for the Community Development through Polytechnic programme;   (b)	if so, the details of funds earmarked and released for the State of Kerala from FY 2018-19 to FY 2021-22 for this programme; (c)	whether the Government has received request from the Government of Kerala for expediting the release of pending funds for the above said programme in Financial Year 2019-20 & 2020-21 and if so, the status thereof.  (d)	the time by which Government will release the pending funds for the upgradation of 48 polytechnics in Kerala as requested by the State Government of Kerala? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes Sir, the Government provides financial assistance to States in cluding Kerela for the sub-scheme ‘Community Development through Polytechnics (CDTP)’.  (b)	The sub-scheme CDTP was transferred from Minisrty of Education (formely MHRD) to Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) and further to Directorate of General of Training (DGT) in September, 2018. The concurrence from Mininstry Finance to incur expenditure under the sub-scheme was upto November, 2018 only. Therefore, Grant-in-aid was not released under the sub-scheme upto March, 2020. For FY 2020-21, grant-in-aid of Rs. 80.00 Lakhs was earmarked for Government of Kerela out fo which Rs. 77.00 Lakhs released as per the availability of funds; to the Government of Kerela for CDTP sub-scheme. Fro current FY 2021-22, grant-in-aid of Rs 50.00 Lakhs has been earmarked and grant-in-aid to be released will be sublect to avialalibity of funds.(c)	The Government has received request from the Government of Kerela for expedititing the released of pending amounts for the above said programme in financial year 2019-20 & 2020-21. During FY 2020-21, grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 77.00 Lakhs has already been released for the said sub-scheme.(d)	For upgradation of 48 Polytechnics in Kerela, the Government of Kerela has been       requested to  utilize unspent funds of Rs. 13.11 crores and furnish all documents; in line with the directions from Ministry of Finance in order to process for release of pending funds;
