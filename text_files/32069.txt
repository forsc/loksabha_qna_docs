                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Buddhist Corridor in Uttar Pradesh




Bhola Singh













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	the list of places being developed as tourist destinations in the   country under the Buddhist corridor in Uttar Pradesh, district-wise;  (b)	the details of measures being taken to provide connectivity to the Buddhist corridor in Uttar Pradesh and the funds granted in this regard so far;(c) 	whether the Government intends to take measures in this regard in the near future too;(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor; and(e) 	the new initiatives/steps that were taken by the Government in this regard during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM				(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e): 	The Ministry of Tourism with an objective to create  tourism infrastructure in the country, provides financial  assistance  to the  State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations/ Central Agencies  under  its  schemes of  ‘Swadesh Darshan’  and   ‘Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive’ (PRASHAD). The Ministry  under  ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme has  sanctioned   5 projects  under Buddhist circuit theme  including 1 project  in  Uttar Pradesh,  the  details of which  are at  annexure-I.  In  addition,  another  project  ‘Development of Wayside Amenities in Uttar Pradesh and Bihar’  for  an  amount of  Rs. 17.93 Crore at Varanasi-Gaya; Lucknow-Ayodhya- Lucknow; Gorakhpur Kushinagar;Kushinagar - Gaya Kushinagar routes has  also  been  sanctioned.  Under PRASHAD scheme of the Ministry of Tourism, components of Sound and Light Show at Dhamek Stupa, Sarnath at a cost of Rs. 7.34 Crore and Buddha Theme Park, Sarnath, at a cost of Rs. 2.20 Crore have also been sanctioned in the state of  Uttar Pradesh.Hon’ble Prime Minister inaugurated the Kushinagar International Airport on 20th October 2021 which will enhance connectivity to the region. Details of the   connectivity works being undertaken in the   Buddhist sites in Uttar Pradesh by the Ministry of Road Transport  and Highways is  at annexure-II. In addition, an Action Plan has been formulated with a view to evolve a coordinated strategy for "Revival of India as a Global Centre of Buddhist Culture and Tourism with Special Focus on Bodhgaya”. The Action Plan includes interventions under 4 verticals: i) Connectivity; ii) Infrastructure and Logistics; iii) Cultural Heritage,  Research & Education; and iv) Public Awareness, Communication & Outreach and the Ministry of Tourism has been made the nodal Ministry for monitoring the implementation of the Action Plan.The Ministry of Tourism as part  of its  ongoing  promotional  activities  undertakes  holistic promotion of   various tourist  destinations including  Buddhist sites  of the country, in the  domestic  and international  markets.*******
