                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1824






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Development of NHs in Odisha




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of development work of National Highways (NHs) taken up in the State of Odisha during the last three years;(b)	whether the targets fixed for the said work have been achieved and if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the funds allocated and released for the purpose during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)   32 National Highway road/bridge projects have been awarded in the State of Odisha during the last three years.  The details of the same along with targets and achievements are annexed.(c)	The funds for National Highway Development works are not allocated work wise and are allocated State wise.  The funds allocated/released for National Highway Development works in the State of Odisha during the last three years are as below :-Year	Funds Allocated/Released(Rs. In Crore)2017-2018	2165.102018-2019	2822.292019-2020	4559.66
