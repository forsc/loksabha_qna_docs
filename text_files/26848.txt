                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2552






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Industrial Corridors




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of functioning of industrial corridors in various States in the country including Chennai - Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor, State-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has any fresh proposal to set up new industrial corridors in various States in the country;(c) 	if so, the details thereof State-wise along with the amount allocated for the purpose and the extent to which it is likely to promote trade and employment opportunities in the region; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (d):  	Government has approved development of 11 Industrial Corridors with 32 Projects to be developed in 04 phases in the country as part of National Industrial Corridor Programme (NICP) which are under various stages of conceptualization/development /implementation. The status of Projects is at Annexure.Govt. of Tamil Nadu in January, 2021 submitted a proposal for funding assistance to Chennai Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor (CKIC) under NICP. They have been requested to submit a detailed proposal (Comprehensive Development Plan/Feasibility Report) for inclusion of CKIC under Industrial Corridor framework along with detailed information of availability of land. Therefore, no amount is allocated for this corridor as yet. The Objective of development of industrial corridors is to expand the industrial output, increasing employment opportunities, provide better living and social facilities for the new and growing workforce by way of providing plug n play infrastructure at the plot level for industries.*******
