                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






CISF Personnel for Calicut airport




 Kunhalikutty P.K.














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any request for additional Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) personnel has been received from Calicut International Airport as new International Arrival Terminal has been opened but due to staff shortage, the Door Frame Metal Detectors are not being used thereby creating huge lines; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	A strength of 327 personnel has been sanctioned for deployment of Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) at Calicut airport.	In the customs area, X-Ray Baggage Inspection System and Door Frame Metal Detectors are used by the authorities concerned to clear passengers as per existing procedures.*****
