                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2088






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Financial Assistance for Development and Maintenance of Tourist Places




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government provides financial assistance to the State Governments for development and maintenance of tourist places; (b) 	if so, the financial assistance provided to States for maintenance of tourist places from 2015-16 to 2019-20; (c) 	the State-wise and tourist place-wise details of amount provided; and (d) 	the details of the district-wise amount provided by the Government to the tourist places located in Rajasthan during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Development and Maintenance of Tourism places is the responsibility of respective State Government/U.T. Administration. Ministry of Tourism, under the schemes viz., Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) and “Integrated Development of Theme Based Tourist Circuit in the Country, Swadesh Darshan (SD)" provides Central Financial Assistance for infrastructure development and beautification of Tourist Places/ Pilgrimage Centres on receipt of suitable DPRs submitted by State Governments/Union Territories, subject to availability of funds, liquidation of pending utilisation certificate against the fund released earlier and adherence to the relevant scheme guidelines.Details of the projects sanctioned under the scheme and funds released to State Governments/UTs including Rajasthan is annexed. ********
