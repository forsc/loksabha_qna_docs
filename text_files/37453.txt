                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4569






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Paddy Storage in Central Pool




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount of paddy the Government needs to store in its Central pool to ensure food security; (b) 	the actual amount of paddy stored by the Government in its Central pool; (c) 	the contribution of paddy to the Central pool since 1970, State-wise; and (d) 	the distribution of paddy under various public distribution systems since 1970, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b): 	The details of stock of rice vis-à-vis foodgrains stocking norms of rice for the 2021-22 is as under:                                                             In lakh tonsAS ON	Actual stock	Foodgrains Stocking norms01.04.2021	291.18	135.8001.07.2021	296.89	135.4001.10.2021	253.26	102.5001.01.2022	221.54	76.10……..2/- -2-(c):  	As per available records, procurement data is available since KMS 2007-08. State-wise procurement of paddy for Central Pool since KMS 2007-08 onwards is at Annex-I.  (d): 	Food Corporation of India distributes rice and not paddy under Public Distribution System.		However, as per available records State-wise allocation and offtake of rice from Central pool since 2006-07 onwards is at Annex-II.  *****
