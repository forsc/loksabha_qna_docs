                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1758






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Distribution under PMGKAY




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Rodmal Nagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of food grains distributed across the country under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY); (b) 	the number of beneficiaries under this scheme; (c) 	whether ration was also distributed to those needy people who do not have ration cards, under this scheme; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b): 	Under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PM-GKAY), so far, a total of about 549.19 LMT foodgrains have been distributed during the implementation phases I, II, III and IV, to around 80 Crore beneficiaries.(c) & (d): 	The free PM-GKAY foodgrains are distributed to all NFSA beneficiaries having either Antyodaya Ann Yojana (AAY) or Priority Household (PHH) ration cards at the scale of 5 Kg per person per month over and above their regular monthly NFSA entitlements of AAY/PHH ration card.****
