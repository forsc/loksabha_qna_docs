                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4692






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Financial Assistance to Tourism Projects in Kerala




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any plan to implement new Tourism Circuits in Kerala; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons, therefor; (c) 	the steps taken by the Government to promote tourism in Kerala during the last three years; and (d) 	the details of ongoing tourism projects in the said State along with the financial assistance given to each project during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	The Ministry of Tourism under its scheme of Swadesh Darshan is   developing thematic tourist circuits in the country. The details of project sanctioned   in Kerala under the scheme are as follows:(Rs. in crore)<pre>S. No.	Year of Sanction	Name of the project	Amount Sanctioned	Amount Released   1.   	  2015-16   	 Development     of Pathanamthitta- Gavi- Vagamon- Thekkady under Eco Tourism Circuit in     Idduki and Pathanamthitta Districts	76.55	61.24   2.   	  2016-17   	 Development     of Sabarimala - Erumeli-Pampa-Sannidhanam under Spiritual Circuit in      District Pathanamthitta 	   99.99   	   20.00    3.   	 2016-17   	 Development     of Sree Padmanabha Arnamula- Sabrimala under Spiritual Circuit 	92.22	73.77   4.   	 2018-19   	 Development     of Malanad Malabar Cruise Tourism Project under Rural Circuit	80.37	0.00</pre>The   above projects are at various stages of implementation /completion. Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic   destination under the Incredible India brand-line. As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and   domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the   country, including those in Kerala.  Promotions are also   undertaken through the website and Social Media accounts of the Ministry.******
