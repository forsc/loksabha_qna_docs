                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Allocation of Funds for Sports Schemes




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds allocated and released under various sports schemes, State-wise including the State of Maharashtra during the last three years;(b)	the details of funds spent and which remained unspent during the last three years, State-wise;(c)	whether the funds released are sufficient for completion of various projects under the scheme; and(d)	if not, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) & (b):  	Funds are allocated scheme-wise not State-wise. Funds allocated and utilized under the schemes of the Department of Sports during the last three years is at Annexure-I.(c):  Sufficient funds are released for completion of various sports projects under different schemes of the Department.(d):  In view of reply part (c) above, question does not arise.  *****
