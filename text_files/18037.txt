                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1781






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Increase in the Price of Coal




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has increased the coal prices;(b)	if so, the reasons for increasing the prices of coal during the COVID-19 pandemic and the lockdown; (c)	whether the prices of electricity, cement and other items have increased due to increase in the coal prices;(d)	if so, whether the Government proposes to retract the increase in coal prices in view of the possibility of adverse impact of the same on the public; and(e)	if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a):  Pursuant to Colliery Control Order, 2000 dated 01.01.2000, coal prices have already been decontrolled. Consequently, coal companies are empowered to take decisions regarding pricing of coal.  The Government does not play any role in it. Further, Coal India Limited has not increased its coal prices during the COVID-19 pandemic and the lockdown.  (b) to (e) : Not applicable in view of part (a) above. * * * *
