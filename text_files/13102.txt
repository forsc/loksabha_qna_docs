                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2003






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Veterinary Science and Animal Husbandry Institution in Ladakh




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government plans to open a college/institution of veterinary sciences and animal husbandry in the Union Territory of Ladakh in view of the fact that most of the people in the region rely on agriculture and cattle rearing; and (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) and (b):  No Sir, there is no decision to start a college of Veterinary Sciences and Animal Husbandry in  Ladakh  by the Central Government. (c)  Establishment of veterinary colleges is as per requirement of trained veterinary manpower in the State/UT concerned. However, as per the recommendations of National Commission on Agriculture, 1976, there shall be one veterinarian for every 5000 cattle units. As per 20th livestock census data, 184192 cattle units are present in Union Territory of Ladakh thereby having a calculated requirement of only 37 Veterinarians against a total of 61 registered Veterinarians working in the Department of Animal & Sheep Husbandry in the Union Territory of Ladakh.*****
