                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3910






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Modification of Ramanathapuram




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to modify Ramanathapuram and Paramakudi railway stations with common facilities like R.O. Plant, Granite Flooring, VIP Rest Rooms, additional shelter, etc; and (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons for delaying modification of the said railway stations?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Yes, Sir.(b): Ramanathapuram and Paramakkudi Railway stations are Non-Suburban Grade (NSG)-4 and NSG-5 category stations. Additional platform shelters have   already been provided both at Ramanathapuram and Paramakkudi Railway stations. At Ramanathapuram station, granite flooring has already been provided and the work regarding granite flooring at Paramakkudi Railway station has been sanctioned.  However, upgradation/modification of passenger amenities at railway stations like platform shelter, VIP  restrooms, granite flooring, R.O. Plant etc. is a  continuous process and works in this regard are undertaken depending upon need, volume of  passenger traffic and inter-se priority, subject to availability of funds. 	******
