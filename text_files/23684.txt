                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4681






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Average Passport Application




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the number of passport offices in the country at present, State-wise;(b) the average number of applications on which action is taken by these offices in a month; and(c) the details of time limit prescribed to issue passports?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) At present, there are 36 Passport Offices in the country. The State-wise list of Passport Offices is placed at Annexure-I. (b) On an average 6.40 lakh passport applications are received by the 36 Passport Offices, while around 6.20 lakh passports are issued by them in a month. (c) The Ministry has prescribed the indicative timeline for issuance of passport in its Citizen Charter 2020 as follows:(i) Issue of Ordinary Passport: (a) For fresh issuance, upto 30 working days [Police Verification (PV) period excluded]. (b) For Re-issue, upto 7 working days in cases where pre-police verification is not required and upto 30 working days in cases where PV is required (PV period excluded). (c) For Tatkal, upto 3 working days subject to the condition that the applicant is eligible for issue of passport under Tatkal scheme.(ii) Issue of Diplomatic Passport and Official Passport: Individual applications are processed and passport issued within 7 working days. ***
