                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2360






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Animal Husbandry and Dairy Industry in Madhya Pradesh




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the details of schemes functioning for promotion of animal husbandry and dairy industry in the State of Madhya Pradesh;(b) the details of the dairy industry in the country, State-wise including the State of Madhya Pradesh;(c) the details of the rural places in the State of Madhya Pradesh where dairy industry is functioning on large scale;(d) the details of benefits being provided for the functioning of dairy industry at rural level; and(e) the details of the eligibility for functioning dairy industry at rural level? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA) (a) The details of the various schemes being implemented by the Government for promotion and development of animal husbandry and dairy sector in the country including Madhya Pradesh are as under: I.	Rashtriya Gokul MissionII.	National Programme for Dairy Development III.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development FundIV.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities V.	National Livestock MissionVI.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development FundVII.	Livestock Health & Disease ControlVIII.	National Animal Disease Control Programme (b) As on March 2020, under cooperative dairy sector, there are 16 dairy plants with a total processing capacity of 1518 Thousand Litres Per Day (TLPD) in the State of Madhya Pradesh. State-wise details of milk processing capacity in cooperative dairy sector in the country is at Annexure-I.  Private Sector data is not compiled by the Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying. (c)  The district-wise details of coverage by dairy cooperatives in the state of   Madhya Pradesh for the year 2020-21 is given at Annexure-II.(d) The department has been implementing following dairy development schemes through State cooperatives supplementing the efforts of the State Governments for the purpose of promotion of dairy industry across the country including rural areas of Madhya Pradesh. I.	National Programme for Dairy Development II.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development FundIII.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities The above schemes envisage to create necessary dairy infrastructure for chilling, procurement, processing, marketing and testing of milk & milk products at the level from primary dairy cooperative societies at villages to the processing plants at district and State level.   (e) At the commencement of operations of dairy cooperative societies, registration of the proposed societies is taken from the office of Registrar, Cooperative Societies of the concerned State. The details of steps involved in the process of organising a dairy cooperative society at rural level are as under:i.	Identification of Potential areaii.	Organising the Gram Sabhaiii.	Society Organisation Meeting iv.	Registration of Society****
