                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3456






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Allocation for Chhattisgarh under PMGSY




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of roads to be completed in Chhattisgarh State in 2019-20 under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b)	whether any proposal has been received from the Chhattisgarh Government to increase allocated budget as per the target and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the total amount provided so far out of the allocated amount in the budget; and(d)	the time by which the balance amount is likely to be provided to the State?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As reported by the Government of Chhattisgarh, 700 road works are targeted for completion under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) including Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Areas (RCPLWEA) in the State of Chhattisgarh during the year 2019-20.(b):	The initial allocation for the State of Chhattisgarh under PMGSY (regular) was Rs. 800 crore, which has been enhanced to Rs. 1,750 crore based on the proposal received from the Government of Chhattisgarh. (c) to (d):	A sum of Rs. 786.46 crore has already been released to the State. The balance amount is released to the states on their fulfilling the eligibility criteria laid down in the programme guidelines for release of subsequent installments and receipt of proposal from the State.***
