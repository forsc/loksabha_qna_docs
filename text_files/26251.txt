                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Vaccines Preventable Diseases (VPDs)




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has any data regarding the total number of Vaccine Preventable Diseases (VPDs) covered at present under the Universal Immunization programme;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the total number of districts where 90% immunization coverage has been achieved in the country, State/UT-wise; and  (c)	the time by which the country is likely to achieve its goal of Universal Immunization of children?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	& (b) Currently under the Universal Immunization Programme (UIP), 12 vaccine preventable diseases (VPDs) are being covered, of which 10 are covered Nationally - Diphtheria, Pertussis, Tetanus, Polio, Measles, Rubella, severe form of Childhood Tuberculosis, Hepatitis B, Meningitis & Pneumonia caused by Hemophilus Influenza type B, Rotavirus diarrhoea and 2 diseases are being covered Sub-Nationally - Pneumococcal Pneumonia and Japanese Encephalitis. As per Data reported by States/UTs in Health Management Information System Portal (HMIS-2019-20), a total of 367 districts have achieved 90 percent full immunization coverage. State/UT wise details are placed at Annexure-I(c) Universal Immunization Programme (UIP) is an ongoing program across the country and all States/UTs are covered under it. With time, a new birth cohort keeps getting added and they are included to be covered under the Universal Immunization Programme.
