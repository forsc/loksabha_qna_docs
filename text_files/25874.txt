                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1758






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Progress of Swachh Bharat Mission




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status and progress of Swachh Bharat Mission in West Bengal and more particularly in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts; (b) the details of funds allocated under Swachh Bharat Mission; (c) whether the Government has achieved Open Defecation Free (ODF), ODF+ and ODF++ status as per the set deadline of 31.03.2020 and if so, the details thereof; and (d) the steps being taken by the Government to ensure better sanitation in the country?






ANSWER




                                                                 THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) and (b) : Progress of Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) in the Urban Local Bodies(ULBs) of West Bengal, including Darjeeling Kalimpong is at Annexure. The Mission-period allocation for implementation of the Mission in West Bengal is Rs.911.34 crores; out of which a total of Rs.410.42 crores have been released.(c) :  No Sir. Out of 128 ULBs, 125 ULBs have declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF) and 85 ULBs have been certified ODF,  02 ULBs (Barackpore Cantt. and Lebong Cantt.) in West Bengal have been certified as ODF+ and none of the ULBs are certified ODF++.(d) :  Besides releasing Central Share (CS) of funds to  States and Union Territories (UTs), Government has introduced ODF+,  ODF++ and Water+ protocols to impart  further impetus to sustain the ODF outcomes. Under these protocols the entire value chain of sanitation is addressed, including access to toilets, cleanliness of Community Toilets/Public Toilets (CT/PTs), safe management of feacal sludge and waste water to prevent discharge of untreated liquid waste in open land or water bodies.                                            *****
