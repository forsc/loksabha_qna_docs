                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3605






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Development Around St. Cajetan Church




Mahua Moitra














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of all original surveys and documents of the site around St Cajetan Church Goa;(b)	the details of all permissions granted for development around St Cajetan Church;(c)	whether a survey has been carried out or action taken  to confirm, if any illegal activity/encroachment has been carried out around the Church and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Site plan of the monument was published in the protection Notification, SO No. 1889 dated 1.6.1968.(b)	No permission was granted by  Archaeological Survey of India for development around St Cajetan Church at Goa (c)	Inspection has been carried out and   action against illegal activities was initiated under the provisions of Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958 and the matter is in court of law.  *****
