                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

16






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has transferred any funds under the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan during the year 2020-21;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether there is any pending amount due to be paid to Andhra Pradesh under this scheme; and (d) if so, the details thereof and a State-wise breakup of the funds transferred under this Scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (d) A statement is laid on the table of the House STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN PARTS (a) to (d) IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 16 FOR ANSWER ON 02.02.2021 REGARDING RASHTRIYA GRAM SWARAJ ABHIYAN___________________________________________________________________________(a) Yes, Sir. (b)	& (d) During the year 2020-21 (till date), funds to the tune of Rs. 429.43 crore has been released to States/UTs under the scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA). Rs. 22.339 crore has been released to Andhra Pradesh as part of 1st installment. State/UT-wise funds released under the Scheme during the year 2020-21 is attached as Annexure. (c) Subsequent release of funds to State of Andhra Pradesh is subject to submission of requisite documents as per Scheme guidelines and Department of Expenditure’s instructions dated 4th May, 2020, subject to availability of funds under the RGSA scheme during the current financial year. ****
