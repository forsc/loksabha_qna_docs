                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

225






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Hefty Fees Charged By Private Schools




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the private school are charging full fees from the guardians in the name of online education during the corona pandemic times; (b)	if so, whether the Government has issued any guidelines in this regard so that the guardians could get some relief; (c)	whether the Government has held anybody accountable in this regard; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d):	Education is a subject in the Concurrent List of the Constitution and majority of schools are under the jurisdiction of the State Governments. Thus, the matter relating to fees and its components in schools are regulated in terms of Rules and Instructions of the State Government concerned. It is for the respective State Government concerned to take necessary action against such schools which violate the Rules and Instructions of the State Government in the matter.****
