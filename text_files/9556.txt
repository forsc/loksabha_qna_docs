                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3189






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Upliftment of Weavers




Subhas Sarkar














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning for upliftment of weavers to powerloom sector under Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme in Bankura, West Bengal;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Textiles is implementing Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS) during January 2016 to March 2022 towards technology upgradation of textile industry across the country including West Bengal. Under ATUFS, 10% Capital Investment Subsidy (CIS) is provided for handloom sector. Also, 10% CIS is provided to weaving for brand new Shuttle-less Looms (including weaving preparatory and knitting).  	In addition, for the development of Powerloom sector, a comprehensive scheme viz, Powertex India is implemented across the country including West Bengal during 01.04.2017 to 31.03.2020. Details of interventions/components under Powertex India is given in Annexure-I.
