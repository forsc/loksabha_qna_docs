                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Achievements in Aviation Sector




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India has emerged as the fastest growing aviation market and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether his Ministry has requested airlines including the private players to promote the achievement of the Government in the aviation sector and if so, the details of these achievements;(c) whether certain private players have refused to promote Government’s achievement in aviation sector and if so, the reasons therefor; and(d) the response of the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): As per information made available by the Scheduled Indian Carriers, details of the number of domestic air passengers for last three years, Revenue Passenger Kilometers (RPKM) performed and Available Seat Kilometers (ASKM) performed are attached as Annexure.;;(b) to (d): Ministry of Civil Aviation has not requested airlines to promote the achievement of the Government in  the aviation sector. However, the Ministry had forwarded the message of Hon''ble Minister vide letter dated 20.02.2019 and requested the scheduled domestic passenger airlines to consider to share some of the key initiatives of the Ministry of Civil Aviation like releasing of National Civil Aviation Policy-2016,  launching of passenger centric initiative like AirSewa 2.0 and Digi Yatra , launching of Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS)- UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) and NABH  (Next Generation Airports in Bharat) NIRMAN, release of National Air Cargo Policy outline 2019, National Green Aviation Policy etc. ;;
