                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1106






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Schemes to Provide Assistance for Development of Tourism




Hemant Patil













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of schemes/programmes being implemented by the Government to provide assistance to State Governments for development of tourism in the country; (b) 	whether several tourist places in the country are being developed with foreign financial assistance; (c) 	if so, the details of the financial assistance received and utilized during each of the last three years and the current year, project and State/UT-wise; (d) 	whether the Government plans to promote tribal tourism across the country and if so, the details of the schemes and sites that have been identified for the same particularly in Maharashtra; (e) 	whether the Government proposes to develop these sites and details of assistance provided to the State Governments for the said purpose; and (f) 	whether any Scheme exists for the conservation and promotion of UNESCO World Heritage Sites in India and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):  Development of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/ UT Administration.  However, the Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/ Union Territory (UT) Administrations/ Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country.  The projects are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc.  Swadesh Darshan and  PRASHAD  schemes of the Ministry of Tourism are  central  sector  schemes  and  funds  are  provided  by the Ministry of  Tourism  to  the  State/ UT/ Central  Implementing Agencies directly. The Ministry of Tourism is not operating any scheme for providing foreign financial assistance  to the States / UTs  for  development of tourism  infrastructure.  (d) & (e): Development and Promotion of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government / Union Territory (UT) Administrations.  However, the Ministry of Tourism has identified fifteen thematic circuits under Swadesh Darshan scheme for development namely; North-East, Buddhist, Himalayan, Coastal, Krishna, Desert, Tribal, Eco, Wildlife, Rural, Spiritual, Ramayana, Heritage, Sufi and Tirthankar.  As of now, no  circuit  on   tribal theme has  been sanctioned  in the State of Maharashtra  however the  Ministry  of  Tourism has  sanctioned  2  projects  for Coastal and  Spiritual themes for total  amount of Rs. 73.07 Crore  under Swadesh Darshan scheme in Maharashtra.  The list of the  projects  sanctioned  under  Tribal Circuit theme of Swadesh  Darshan  is annexed. (f):  Archaeological  survey of India  has  informed that  there  is no specific  scheme  for the conservation  and promotion  of  UNESCO World Heritage Sites,  however  the Ministry of  Tourism holistically  promotes  various  tourism  destinations  of  India including multiple  World  Heritage Sites in  international  and  domestic  markets.   ********
