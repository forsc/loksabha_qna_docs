                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Promotion of Eco-Tourism in Assam




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to enhance eco-tourism in the country, especially in the State of Assam; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of training programs conducted in the field of eco-tourism during the last three years; (d) 	the details of the targets with respect to eco-tourism and the expected revenue generation in the next 10 years; and (e) 	the details of financial assistance extended to the State of Assam for eco-tourism during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	Development and Promotion of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/Union Territory Administration. However, the Ministry of Tourism has identified Eco Tourism as one of the Niche Tourism Products for promotion and development in the country including Assam, with an objective to promote India as a year round destination and to attract tourists with specific interest for ensuring repeat visit for the unique products in which India has a comparative advantage vis-à-vis competing destinations. Ministry of Tourism has been laying stress on maintenance of environmental integrity, considering the importance of developing tourism in an ecologically sustainable manner.  The Ministry of Tourism has evolved Sustainable Tourism Criteria for India (STCI) for major segments of the tourism industry, namely accommodation, tour operators, beaches, backwaters, lakes and river sectors, etc. applicable for the entire country. The Criteria has been evolved after consultations with the various stakeholders.  Ministry of Tourism has made it mandatory for the approved tourism service providers such as tour operators, adventure tour operators, travel agents etc. to follow the Code of Conduct for safe and sustainable Tourism.In addition, the Ministry of Tourism, under its Swadesh Darshan scheme is developing thematic circuits in the country in planned and prioritized manner in order to develop tourism infrastructure at multiple destinations. Eco-tourism is one of the identified theme under which a total of 6 projects have been sanctioned for a total amount of Rs.483.74 Crore. The details of the projects sanctioned under Eco-Tourism theme in Swadesh Darshan scheme is annexed. No projects have  been  sanctioned  in the State of Assam  under Swadesh Darshan scheme covering Eco-Tourism theme however the Ministry of  Tourism has  sanctioned  the following  projects in the state of  Assam:-  (i)	Development of Tezpur – Majauli – Sibsagar sanctioned in the year 2016-17 for Rs. 90.98 Crore under Heritage theme.(ii)	Development of  Manas – Pobitora – Nameri – Khaziranga – Dibru – Saikhowa in the  year 2015-16  for  Rs. 94.68 Crore under Wildlife theme. Tourism Awareness/Sensitization programmes for existing service providers are conducted under this Ministry''s Scheme of "Capacity Building for Service Providers". The primary objective of the programme is to eventually achieve an improved tourist specific service ambience and to further the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. Each course is of two to six day duration and includes training of Naturalists and eco-guides. This programme is available to all areas across the country including far flung, hilly and backward areas. ******
