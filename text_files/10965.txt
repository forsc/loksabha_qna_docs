                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Grasim Industries




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantum of production permitted daily in Grasim Industries located in Industrial city of Nagda, Madhya Pradesh including the present production made on daily basis during the last two years; and (b)	the number of workers along with the details of the permanent and temporary employees employed in the said industries?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):  The Ministry of Textiles does not compile any data in respect of private sector industries with regard to daily production and number of peoples employed thereon.  *****
