                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Monetary Help to Students




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to provide monetary assistance to eligible students as special welfare measure through the Direct Transfer of Benefits (DBT) across the country, if so, the details thereof along with the amount to be provided under it;(b) the details of the amount provided State-wise and district-wise;(c) the time by which it is likely to be implemented;(d) whether the Government has decided to cover girl students belonging to the Scheduled Tribes also under the scheme; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a)to (c): Ministry of Education implements various schemes to provide monetary assistance to eligible students in the form of scholarship/fellowship as special welfare measure through the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) across the country. Ministry of Education also implements‘Pradhan MantriPoshan Shakti Nirman(PM POSHAN) (earlier known as National Programme of Mid-Day Meal in Schools), one of the foremost rights based Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA). Under the PM-POSHAN, the Government has approved provision of hot cooked meal to children of pre-schools or Balvatika (before class I) in primary schools also, in addition to the 11.80 crore children of classes I to VIII studying in 11.20 lakh schools. During the current pandemic, since schools were closed, all enrolled children are given Food Security Allowance comprising of food grains and cooking cost as Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) or in the form of dry ration by States and UTs during 2020-21 and 2021-22. Further, for summer vacation period as special welfare measure States and UTs were also advised to provide monetary assistance through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) of cooking cost component of the Scheme to all eligible children. The details of such schemes is at Annexure-1. The details of amount provided State-wise is at Annexure-2.(d) & (e) All the above schemes universally cover all eligible students including girl students belonging to the Scheduled Tribes.***
