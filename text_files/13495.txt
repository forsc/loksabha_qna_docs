                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Target set for Construction of NHs




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	  the target (in kilometres) set by the Government for construction of National Highways (NHs) during the last three years, State/UT-wise ;(b)	  the average daily pace of construction of NHs and whether this pace is in line with the required average daily pace to achieve the target and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	the length of NHs passing through Maharashtra;(d)	the funds allocated to Maharashtra for maintenance and repair of these NHs during each of the last three years; and (e)	the details of all the NH projects that are proposed to be launched during the next one year?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
