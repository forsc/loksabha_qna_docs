                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Orphanages




Rama Devi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    ) the total number of orphanages present in the country, State/UT-wise including Bihar; (b) the number of orphans living in the said orphanages, State/UT-wise;(c) the amount of funds allocated and utilised for the said orphanages during the last three years, State/UT-wise; (d) whether any suggestions or proposals have been received from the Government of Bihar regarding the orphanages and the orphans living therein; and (e) if so, the details thereof along with the action taken in this regard?                                                        






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): As per the information provided by Central Adoption Resource Authority (CARA) the total number of Specialized Adoption Agencies (SAAs) present in the country State/UT-wise and number of orphans living in the said SAAs is at Annexure-I.(c): The Ministry releases funds to States/UTs under ‘Child Protection Services’. The details of funds released under CPS for institutional care (including SAAs), non-institutional care and service delivery set up is at Annexure-II.(d) & (e): No such suggestion has been received by the Ministry from the Govt. of Bihar. *** Annexure-IAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a & b) of Lok Sabha UnStarred Question No.109 for 21.06.2019 raised by Smt.
