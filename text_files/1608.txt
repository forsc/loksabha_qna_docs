                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1358






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Special Assistance to Cyclone affected States




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether cycolonic storm ‘Fani’ and ‘Vayu’ have struck several parts of the country recently; (b) 	whether tourist infrastructure and facilities have been badly affected in the cyclone-hit States; (c) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to provide special assistance to the States affected by the above cyclones for rebuilding of damaged tourist infrastructure; (e) 	if so, the details of the assistance likely to be provided in this regard; and (f) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to boost the tourism in these States?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Yes Sir.(b) & (c):	Tourist infrastructure and amenities were affected in Puri, Ganjam, Khorda, Balesore, Cuttack, Jagatsinghpur, Kendrpara and  Bhadrak districts of  Odisha  as intimated by the State Govt. of Odisha. Government of Gujarat has informed that no tourist infrastructure and facilities was affected in the State of Gujarat.(d) & (e):	Ministry of Tourism provides financial assistance for development of tourist infrastructure including restoration of historical structures/monuments etc. under its existing infrastructure development schemes.      However, there is no separate provision for special assistance to the State Government for rebuilding the damaged tourist infrastructure by the above cyclones.(f):	Ministry of Tourism, under the schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides central financial assistance to State Governments/UTs for development of tourism infrastructure in the country upon submission of project proposals by the State Governments / UTs subject to their adherence to relevant scheme guidelines, availability of funds and utilization of funds released earlier.The list of the projects sanctioned by the Ministry of Tourism to the State of Odisha and Gujarat is given at the Annexure.*******
