                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Government Officials Engaged in ControllingSpread of COVID-19




Chandra Sekhar Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the estimated number of Government officials engaged in controlling the spread of COVID-19 in the country, State-wise;(b) whether the Union Government will declare teachers and other Government officials engaged in the COVID-19 containment duty as the COVID-19 warriors;(c) if so, the details of family members of teachers who died during discharging their COVID-19 duties;(d) whether compensation has been given at par with the police and doctors in the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise and district-wise in regard to Odisha; and(e) the number of families of such officials, whose family members have died of COVID, provided exgratia announced by the Government in the country so far, State-wise along with the amount provided?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	Volunteers or government officials drafted by the Central/State/UT Government for care and who may have come in direct  contact  of  the  COVID-19  patient,  can   be   declared   by   the -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.1554 FOR 20.09.2020concerned designated authority as COVID warrior. As informed by Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, health is a State subject and data asked in part (a) and (c) is not centrally maintained.(d):	Volunteers or Government officials drafted on Covid-19 containment duty are covered under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package (PMGKP) Insurance Scheme for health workers fighting COVID-19. The State-wise list of disbursement of ex-gratia payment made under PMGKP Insurance Scheme, is at Annexure.*******
