                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2492






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Vacant Posts in Ministry




Girish Chandra














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a large number of posts related to Scheduled Tribe (ST) and OtherBackward Classes are lying vacant in the Ministry;(b) if so, the current status of posts sanctioned for these categories and for generalcategory and the details of these posts and the posts lying vacant out of them;(c) the steps taken by the Government in the last three years to fill these positions;(d) whether some communities are being deprived of getting benefits of reservation bynot filling these sanctioned posts; and(e) if so, the details therof ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(Dr. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (e): The detail of category-wise sanctioned and vacant posts belonging to various cadressuch as Indian Statistical Service (ISS), Sub-ordinate Statistical Service (SSS), Indian EconomicService (IES), Central Secretariat Services (CSS), Central Secretariat Stenographers Services(CSSS), Central Secretariat Clerical Services (CSCS), Central Secretariat Official LanguageServices (CSOLS) etc. and posts under Central Staffing Scheme are maintained and vacancies arefilled up by respective Cadre Controlling Authorities.As far as the posts, where the Cadre Controlling Authority is this Ministry, a category-wise detail ofsanctioned and vacant posts are given in the table at Annexure. Filling up of vacancies is anongoing and continuous process. The vacancies are filled up through promotion/ direct recruitment/deputation/ departmental examination as prescribed in their recruitment rules and are reported tothe Department of Personnel & Training (DoP&T) or Staff Selection Commission (SSC) etc. fromtime to time.In respect of vacancies relating to Senior Secretariat Assistant (SSA) Zone of Consideration forpromotion to the post is issued by DoP&T. All candidates covered under Zone of Considerationissued by DoP&T have been promoted.Vacant posts of Multi-Tasking Staff and Canteen Attendant have been reported to SSC.*****
