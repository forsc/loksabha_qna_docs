                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2236






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Recruitment of Merchant Navy Professionals




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the demand of merchant navy professionals has increased due to increasing commercial activities over the last few years;(b) if so, the details thereof during the last three years;(c) whether the Government has prepared any scheme to conduct a special recruitment drive to overcome the shortage of merchant navy officers;(d) if so, the time which the said drive is likely to be conducted; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)		Yes Sir. (b)    	The statistics of engagement of Indian Seafarers for the last 03 years is given below :  <pre>Year               	Total No. of Seafarers engaged               	Growth (%)2017	154349	7.232018	208799	35.272019	234886	12.492020	224478	 - 4.43*                                         </pre>* Employment in the year 2020 has decreased mainly due to complete stoppage of cruise shipping during the Covid 19 pandemic.(c,d&e)     India has over 4 lakh seafarers. There is no shortage of Indian Seafarers.                                          ****
