                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Vacancies in Kendriya Vidyalayas




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the actual strength of teachers and total number of vacancies in KendriyaVidyalayas (KVs) across the country, State/UT-wise;(b) the number of vacancies of SC/ST for the post of teachers yet to be filled upalong with the number of teachers recruited during each of the last three years;(c) whether there is dearth of teachers including Principals, PGT/TGT teachers/ labassistants in Kendriya Vidyalayas and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(d) the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to fill up the saidvacancies;(e) whether the shortage of teaching staff is affecting the teaching standards inschools and if so, the details of remedial steps taken/proposed to be taken by theGovernment in this regard; and(f) the other steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to fill up suchvacancies and to improve the student/ teacher ratio in schools?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that as on 15.11.2019, thereare 5949 teaching posts lying vacant against 48236 sanctioned posts in differentKendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in the country. State/UT-wise details are annexed.(b) The details of the total recruitment made (including SC/ST candidates) during thelast three years are as under:-<pre>Tentatively, 5206vacancies of teaching staff are to be filled up during the year2020through direct recruitment out of which 779 and 387 vacancies are earmarked forSCs and STs respectively.</pre>(c) The category-wise break-up of the vacancies of teaching posts includingPrincipals, PGTs, TGTs and Lab Assistants are annexed.(d) to (f) Filling up of vacancies is a continuous process and action is taken from time totime as per the provisions of the relevant recruitment rules. As the process of filling upof vacancies involves various methods of recruitment such as promotion, LimitedDepartmental Examination, Direct Recruitment etc. and takes time, no specific timeframe for filling up of vacant posts can be given at this stage.Teachers are also engaged on contractual basis for temporary duration by KVSto ensure that the teaching-learning process is not hampered. Directions have also beenissued to autonomous organizations including KVS from time to time, for reviewing theirvacancies and for taking prompt action for filling up of vacant posts in the Vidyalayas.*****YearTotal number ofrecruitment made(including Principals, Viceprincipals&Librarians)Category wise recruitmentSCs STs2017 6255 918 4592018 760 113 702019 8420 1238 608
