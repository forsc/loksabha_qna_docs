                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3825






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Research Papers and Patents on Atomic Energy




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of research papers published by the Department of Atomic Energy in journals and the number of patents obtained during the last five years; and(b)	the rank of India as compared to America, Britain and China with regard to research and patent in the field?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (Dr. JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)	Department of Atomic Energy has published 35,518 research papers in journals and obtained 47 patents during the last five years.(b)	India stands at the 3rd rank among aforesaid four countries after China and America with regard to research publications in the field.  India stands at the 3rd rank among aforesaid four countries after China and America with respect to the number of patents granted during 2018.******
