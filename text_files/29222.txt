                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

558






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Vistadome Coaches




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to add 5 additional Vistadome Coaches on the Vishakhapatnam Araku Route which is a prime tourist destination;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any additional vistadome coaches have been provided on any other tourist circuit route in the country; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNCATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b). Three Vistadome Coaches have been allotted to East Coast Railway for Visakhapatnam-Araku-Kirandul route. At present, two Vistadome Coaches are operational on Visakhapatnam-Araku -Kirandul route.Allotment of Vistadome Coaches is based on factors such as availability of Vistadome Coaches, its operational feasibility, load limitations, day-time journey of the train and demand of the passengers on the existing routes of Indian Railways.(c) and (d). The details of Vistadome Coaches available on tourist circuit routes over Indian Railways is appended.*****
