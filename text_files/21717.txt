                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2830






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






India Climate Change Knowledge Portal




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched the “India Climate Change Knowledge Portal” to address the climate change issues; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the objectives and benefits of such portal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) and (b): Yes, Sir. The web portal link is https://www.cckpindia.nic.in/. The portal contains information regarding, inter alia, India’s climate profile, National policy framework, Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs), mitigation and adaptation actions, etc. The benefit of the portal is that it is a single point information resource, as well as a knowledge repository regarding a broad range of topics on climate action in India and International climate negotiations.  The main objective of the Portal is todisseminate information among citizens about the Government’s stepsto combat climate change.****
