                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1980






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Inter-State Connectivity Projects




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of inter-State connectivity projects amongst Karnataka, Maharashtra and Telangana lying incomplete at present; (b) the time by which the said projects are likely to be completed; (c) whether the Government has provided financial assistance to the State Governments for the construction of inter- State bridges and roads which are important from economic perspectives during each of the last three years and the current year and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (d) the names of the States proposed to be provided financial assistance in this regard during the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V. K SINGH (RETD.)](a)  & (b) There are no incomplete projects taken up under Inter-State Connectivity (ISC) Scheme amongst the States of Karnataka, Maharashtra and Telangana at present.  (c) & (d)  State-wise  details of allocation and expenditure under Economic Importance (EI) and ISC during each of the last three years and the current year are Annexed.
