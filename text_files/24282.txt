                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Training Under PMKVY




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether unemployed youths are being trained for skill development under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY);(b) 	if so, the number of such skill development centers being operated in each district in Uttar Pradesh;(c) 	the type of technical skill development facilities available to the youths; and(d)	whether any job opportunities are provided to these trainees after completing their training and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Yes Sir. Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojna (PMKVY) training is being imparted through empanelled training centres to unemployed youth of the country including State of Uttar Pradesh. The district-wise number of empanelled training centres in the State of Uttar Pradesh is given at Annexure. (c)        Under PMKVY, training is being imparted in various National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) aligned job roles of level 3 to 5 including technical courses across 37 sectors. (d)       Yes Sir, placement opportunities are being provided to candidates certified under placement-linked (short term training (STT)) component of PMKVY.  Under this component, as on 10.07.2021, 39.15 lakh candidates have been certified; out of which, 20.69 lakh candidates have been placed in diverse sectors.
