                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Animal Data Base Scheme




Dulal Chandra Goswami













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of animals registered in the country under animal UID/Pashu Aadhaar Yojana and Animal Data Base Scheme in the country;(b) the number of animals registered in Wardha, Amravati in the State of Maharashtra, Katihar in the State of Bihar and Pratapgarh in the State of Uttar Pradesh; and(c) whether the Government has any scheme for expansion of this scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) As on 4th February 2021, 13.74 crore cattle and buffaloes have been identified using 12 digit unique identification number ear tags and their data uploaded on Information Network for Animal Productivity and Health (INAPH) data base under the scheme National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP).(b)  Details on number of animals registered in Wardha, Amravati in the State of Maharashtra, Katihar in the State of Bihar and Pratapgarh in the State of Uttar Pradesh as on 4th February 2021 is as under: SN	District	State	Number of animals registered (Cattle and Buffaloes)1	Wardha	Maharashtra	2,10,1632	Amravati	Maharashtra	3,40,8483	Katihar	Bihar	2,05,8144	Pratapgarh	Uttar Pradesh	1,64,094(c) Under National Animal Disease Control Programme identification of animals has been extended to all bovine animals as well as sheep, goat and pigs numbering total 53.5 crore. ******
