                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2110






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Urban Compost




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara













                                                    Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a)	whether it is fact that under the policy of promoting urban compost, fertilizer companies have adopted hundreds of villages to  encourage the use of urban compost and if so, the State-wise details of all such villages;(b)	the details of villages, especially in Karnataka, where these fertilizer companies are selling urban compost directly to farmers;(c)	whether the Government is running the scheme to reduce the volume of waste by converting it into useful organic fertilizers and if so, the  details thereof;(d)	the status and progress under the urban compost scheme, State/UT wise including Gujarat;(e)	whether the Government proposes to increase the number of villages adopted by the fertilizer companies to promote the use of urban compost in the country including Gujarat; and(f)	if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(DR. MANSUKH  MANDAVIYA)______________________________________________________________________________(a):   To promote the use of city compost, the Fertilizers marketing Companies have adopted 498 villages across the country. The State-wise number of villages adopted by marketing companies is at Annexure-I. (b)      No such data is maintained.(c)  & (d):  (i) For converting Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) into organic fertilizer, Swachh Bharat Mission – Urban under Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs (MOHUA) provides central share fund for establishment of Solid Waste Management (SWM) plants by States/Union Territories (UTs) in the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs).(ii)	 The specifications of 10 organic fertilizers namely city compost, vermi compost, Phosphate rich organic manure, Bio-enriched organic manure, organic manure, Fermented Organic Manure (solid), Fermented Organic Manure (liquid), Bone meal raw and Bone meal steam and Potash derived from rhodophytes have been notified in the Fertilizer Control Order, (FCO), 1985. The raw material used in some of these fertilizers are various types of organic waste.(iii)	  The Government had approved the policy on promotion of city compost which was notified by the Department of Fertilizers on 10.02.2016 wherein a Market Development Assistance of Rs. 1500/- M.T. was provided for scaling up production and consumption of city compost made from city waste. However, the scheme of Market Development Assistance (MDA) for scaling up production and consumption of city compost has been discontinued after 30th September, 2021 based on the review of the MDA Policy and recommendations of Expenditure Finance Committee (EFC) in its meeting dated 2nd August, 2021.(iv)    As  per iFMS portal,  the production of city compost for the year 2019-20, 2020-21 and  2021-22 up to  October, 2021 is as under: Sl. No. 	Year	Production of city Compost in MT1.	      2019-20	            327790.582.	      2020-21	            269110.463.	       2021-22	            194385.14(up to Oct., 2021) (v) 	The PoS sale State-wise/UT-wise by fertilizer marketing companies and bulk sale of city compost by compost manufacturers for the year 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22  up to  October, 2021 including Gujarat is at Annexure-II.(e)  :     There is no such proposal.(f)    :   In view of (e) above, the question does not arise.********                                                                         Annexure-IState/UT-wise number of Villages adopted by Fertilizer companies for promotion of usage of urban compostS.
