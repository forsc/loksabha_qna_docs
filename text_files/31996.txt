                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3381






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Compensatory Time in NEET




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes for allocating compensatory time in NEET (UG & PG) Examination;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of disabled medical aspirants appeared and qualified for NEET (UG &PG) during the last three years; State/UT-wise;(d) whether the Government is aware regarding the last minute changes in examination pattern for NEET (PG) by NAT; and(e) if so, measures taken by the Government to prevent the repetition thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (e): In pursuance of the Guidelines of Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, the National Testing Agency (NTA) for NEET (UG) and the National Board of Examinations (NBE) for NEET (PG) provides compensatory/ additional time as per provisions of the Right of Persons with Disability Act, 1916 to eligible PwD candidates. The State/ UT-wise details of disabled medical aspirants appeared and qualified for NEET (UG) and NEET (PG) during last three years have been placed at Annexure-I & II. Further, as informed by NBE, change of pattern in NEET PG was notified as part of information bulletin in January 2021 and the examination was conducted on 11.09.2021.
