                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4042






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Rail Corridor along Delhi-Mumbai e-Way




Kuldeep Rai Sharma













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government proposes to build a new rail corridor along the upcoming greenfield Delhi-Mumbai expressway, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has offered to provide a share of NHAI land without any cost to Railways to lay the new tracks, if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Ministry of Railways has nominated two names for a panel which will look into the proposal, if so, the details thereof and the time by which the panel will come up with the plan; (d) whether the Government has worked out the cost of building the new rail corridor, if so, the details thereof and the amount earmarked for this purpose; and(e) the time by which the work on new rail corridor will be started and completed ?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e):  The proposal of Railways to build a new rail corridor along the upcoming Greenfield Delhi-Mumbai expressway and discussion between Ministry of Railways and Ministry of Road Transport and Highways is at a conceptual stage.*****
