                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3671






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Repairing of NHs in West Bengal




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     whether the Government has any plan to repair the National Highways in the State of West Bengal during the financial year 2022-2023 and if so, the details thereof; and(b)     the budget allocation made for the purpose in the State, project-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) and (b) Maintenance and repair of National Highways (NHs) in the country including NHs in the State of West Bengal is a continuous process. Maintenance and repair works in Public Private Partnership (PPP) and Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) Projects are undertaken by the concessionaire/contractor as per provisions of respective concession/contract agreement. Maintenance and repair works in other National Highway stretches are taken up based on requirement of preventive maintenance, routine maintenance, extent of damages due to rains, floods, landslides etc., availability of funds and inter-se priority of works.*****
