                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3020






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






PMJD Yojana




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the present number of beneficiaries registered under the Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan (PMJD) Yojana, State-wise; (b)	whether it is true that the onset of the COVID19 pandemic has accelerated the need for providing income support; and (c) 	if so, whether the Government is considering using information from MGNREGA, PM Ujjwala, PDS and local institutions such as the Panchayat to expand the reach of cash transfers to mitigate the impact of the COVID-19 and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) As informed by banks, as on 24.02.2021, 41.93 crore accounts have been opened under Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY). State-wise number of accounts under PMJDY as on 24.02.2021 is at Annexure.(b) & (c) The Government had announced a Rs. 1.70 Lakh Crore ‘Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana’ relief package on 26.03.2020 to combat the economic impact of COVID-19 on the poor and needy and to help them in the fight against Corona Virus. The salient features of the package were:(i) Comprehensive personal accident insurance cover of Rs. 50 lakh for health workers fighting COVID-19. (ii) Additional allocation of food grain @ 5 kg per person and pulses @ 1 kg per family per month free of cost to all beneficiaries covered under the targeted public distribution system. (iii) Front loading and payment in April, 2020 itself of the first instalment of Rs 2,000 due in 2020-21 to about 8.7 crore farmers under the PM KISAN Yojana. (iv) An ex-gratia of Rs 500 per month for three months to 20.40 crore (approx) women PMJDY account-holders and release of Rs.1000/- to around 3 crore aged widows and persons in Divyang category. (v) Free of cost LPG cylinders to 8 crore beneficiaries of Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana. (vi) Providing 24% of the monthly wages into PF accounts of wage-earners earning below Rs. 15,000 per month, in businesses with less than 100 workers, for three months to prevent disruption in employment. (vii) Increasing (i) MNREGA wages by Rs. 20 w.e.f01.04.2020 and (ii) Limit of collateral free lending from Rs. 10 to Rs. 20 lakh for women organized through 63 lakh Self Help Groups who support 6.85 crore households. (viii) Amendment to Employees’ Provident Fund Regulations to include Pandemic as the reason to allow non-refundable advance of 75% of the amount or three months wages, whichever is lower, from their accounts. (ix) Directions issued to State Governments to utilize (a) Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Fund for assistance and support to around 3.5 Crore registered workers & (b) District Mineral Fund for supplementing and augmenting facilities of medical testing, screening and other requirements to prevent the spread of COVID-19 pandemic as well as treat the patients affected with this pandemic.***** Annexure as referred to in part (a) of reply to the Lok Sabha question No. 3020 for 15.03.2021 regarding "PMJD Yojana''''State-wisePMJDY accounts as on 24.02.2021S.No.	State	Total PMJDY accounts1	Andaman & Nicobar Islands	477142	Andhra Pradesh	112815593	Arunachal Pradesh	3537914	Assam	184409465	Bihar	481749846	Chandigarh	2675957	Chhattisgarh	152987228	Dadra & Nagar Haveli	1455089	Daman & Diu	5972110	Delhi	478994011	Goa	16912912	Gujarat	1596168113	Haryana	786880314	Himachal Pradesh	149506215	Jammu & Kashmir	245427816	Jharkhand	1538986617	Karnataka	1513239118	Kerala	471822519	Ladakh	2082220	Lakshadweep	599021	Madhya Pradesh	3523668322	Maharashtra	2981067323	Manipur	102197324	Meghalaya	58812325	Mizoram	32025126	Nagaland	32654227	Odisha	1719015428	Puducherry	16364929	Punjab	730646530	Rajasthan	2916113631	Sikkim	8703632	Tamil Nadu	1107836933	Telangana	1011838634	Tripura	90776835	Uttar Pradesh	7100248036	Uttarakhand	271953537	West Bengal	40151497Total	419267447Source: Banks
