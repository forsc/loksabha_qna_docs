                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1382






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Industrial Corridor




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether land has been acquired and all necessary sanctions have been issued for the setting up of a new Coimbatore-Kochi Industrial corridor via Palakkad;(b) 	if so, the progress made in approval and completion of the projects under the said Corridor along with its current status;(c) 	the details of the target set for completion of the project; and(d) 	the time by which this corridor will be made operational?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): 	National Industrial Corridor Development and Implementation Trust (NICDIT) in its meeting held on 30th August, 2019 has accorded approval for extension of Chennai - Bengaluru Industrial Corridor (CBIC) to Kochi and Coimbatore and inclusion of Integrated Manufacturing Clusters (IMCs) identified in the State of Kerala and Tamil Nadu.  As per approved financial and institutional structure for industrial Corridors by Government of India, the responsibility of making land available for the project is with the concerned State Government.  The activities relating to selection of consultant for detailed master planning and preliminary engineering of these IMCs are in progress.  Completion timelines varies from 4-5 years from the date of initiations of project development activities. Since, the project is presently under development stage, completion date of the project cannot be fixed.  *****
