                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






ESI Dispensaries in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana




M V V Satyanarayana














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether it is a fact that many districts in Andhra Pradesh (AP) and Telangana do not have Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) dispensaries; (b)the names of districts of both AP and Telangana States which have ESI dispensaries; (c)whether the Government has any plans to allocate new ESI dispensaries especially in those districts which do not have such dispensaries; and (d)if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	 Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) dispensaries are located in 13 districts of Andhra Pradesh and 15 districts of Telangana. The list of dispensaries in the States of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana is Annexed.(c) & (d):	Allocation of new ESI dispensary in any area/district is approved after receipt of the proposal from respective State Government, in accordance with ESIC norms. 		At present, there is no such proposal from State Government of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh*                                                    *****
