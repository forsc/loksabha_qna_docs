                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3049






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Akshay Urja Shops




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up ‘Akshay Urja Shops’ in all districts of the country particularly in tribal areas of Uttar Pradesh; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the total number of ‘Akshay Urja Shops’ across the country till date, State/UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to expand network of ‘Akshay Urja Shops’ by encouraging Private Entrepreneurs and Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) to set up and operate such shops in all districts of the country?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) to (c) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) had been promoting establishment of Aditya Solar Shops in major cities of the country since 1995 with a view to make solar energy products easily available and to provide easy after sales repair services. In 2005-06, it was decided to rename the programme as “Akshay Urja Shops” with a view to cover sales and services of all renewable energy devices and systems in all districts of the country. Total 412 Akshay Urja Shops were sanctioned by MNRE, of which 72 Shops were sanctioned in the State of Uttar Pradesh. State/UT wise numbers of Akshay Urja Shops sanctioned by the Ministry are given in the Annexure. The implementation of scheme was reviewed by the Ministry and it was decided to discontinue the scheme beyond 12th Plan period. *****
