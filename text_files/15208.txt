17.03.2020






Funds for Rural Development




Su Thirunavukkarasar













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total allocation for rural development during the last three years;(b) 	whether the Government has allocated any funds for the various rural development projects and schemes in Tamil Nadu and if so, the details of the funds allocated for the purpose during the said period scheme/project-wise;(c) 	whether the Government has fixed any specific norms and rules for getting both Government and external funding for the implementation of various rural development projects and schemes in Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether the Government has taken up any scheme for Digital India and Records Modernization Programme and if so, the progress in the implementation of the said programme in the country, State and district-wise?
