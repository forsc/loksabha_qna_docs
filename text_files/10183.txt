                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Protection of Paddy Fields




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the special fund provided by the Union Government for preservation of Thrissur-Ponnani kole fields which are among the largest paddy fields in Kerala State; (b)	 whether the Government will allocate more funds for further phases of the project; (c)	 the details of the Union Government’s initiatives to protect paddy fields in the country; and (d)	 the details of fund allocations to Kerala for preserving paddy fields of the State during 2014-2019 period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	In order to preserve Thrissur-Ponnani kole fields, the Government of India has allocated funds for integrated Kole Development Project in Thrissur-Ponnani Kole wet lands through Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) & Rural Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF) through National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD).          Along with assistance for infrastructure development, RKVY is also promoting fallow land cultivation and pokkali cultivation in Kerala.            Similarly, National Food Security Mission (NFSM)-Rice is being implemented in the identified districts of the country including Kerala with the objective of increasing the production and productivity of rice. Under the programme, assistance is being provided for various interventions like demonstrations on latest crop production technologies, seed distribution of latest HYVs/Hybrids, Integrated Nutrient & Pest Management (IPM), water conservation devices, farm implements/ tools and capacity building of farmers through the State Governments. Contd…..2/-- 2 - Further, the institutes/research centres of Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) and Kerala Agricultural University (KAU), Thrissur viz., Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Thrissur, Regional Agricultural Research Station, Pattambi in Palakkad district, Rice Research Station Moncompu in Kuttanad belt of Alapuzha district and Rice Research Station, Vyttila (voluntary centre of rice) undertake research and extension activities on all aspects of rice cultivation viz. crop improvement, crop protection and crop management. These research & development activities help to preserve paddy fields in Kerala.              The details of funds for the Kole Projects sanctioned under RKVY, RIDF & NFSM from the year 2014-15 to 2019-20 are Annexed.
