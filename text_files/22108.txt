                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3383






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Horticultural Production in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the production of horticulture crops production including vegetables, fruits, and flowers, in Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur districts of West Bengal;(b)	whether the  production of oranges and cardamom in Darjeeling and Kalimpong region has gone down in the past decades;  (c)	if so, the details of the steps taken by the Ministry to investigate the reasons  for the same and the steps taken to assist the cultivators; and  (d)	whether the Ministry would consider establishing a Central university  for horticulture or agriculture  in North Bengal, particularly in Darjeeling or Kalimpong District, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????       (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):   The details of the production of horticulture crops production including vegetables, fruits, and flowers, in Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur districts of West Bengal, as reported by Government of West Bengal is at Annexure-I(b) & (c):     As reported by Indian Council of Agricultural Research-Indian Agricultural Research Institute(ICAR-IARI), the area and production of Darjeeling mandarin has gone down in past decade because of shifting of area to vegetables cultivation, spices(large Cardamom), tourism and other crops.  ICAR-IARI has reported the following remedial steps for addressing the issue of decline in mandarin cultivation:- 1.	Started production of healthy planting materials (grafted and nucellar seedlings).2.	To address the issue of rootstock availability, the institute has produced more than 1 lakh rootstocks.3.	Four village cluster in the region of Sitong, Mungpoo, Balason and Iche Gaon was adopted and farmers were mobilized and trained for scientific Darjeeling mandarin cultivation.4.	 Under Indian Agricultural Research Institute(IARI) extension and technology transfer programme with the assistance of Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana(RKVY), Government of West Bengal and National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development(NABARD), so far 20,000 new healthy planting materials were distributed among the farming communities.5.	Approximately, 100 ha area has been covered under new plantation by IARI (Research Station), Kalimpong.6.	 Four (4) nurseries were established for production of healthy planting materials of Darjeeling mandarin by the farmers at village level in Mungpoo, Sitong, Balason and Iche gaon of Kalimpong and Darjeeling hills.The production of cardamom has not gone down in Kalimpong and Darjeeling region during the last decade. In fact, the production of cardamom in this region has increased marginally during the past decade from 716 tonnes in 2010-11 to 833 tonnes in 2019-20.(d):	Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) has reported that there is no such proposal under consideration in the ICAR for establishing a Central University for Horticulture or Agriculture in North Bengal.
