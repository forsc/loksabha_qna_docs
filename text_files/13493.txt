                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

213






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






NH Projects in Kerala




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of National Highways (NHs) in the country especially in Kerala that have been 	expanded into four and six lanes during the last three years;(b) 	whether there are objections against the development of NHs into four and six lanes and if 	so, the steps taken to resolve these objections;(c) 	the total allocation/utilization of funds for the development of NHs in Kerala during the  	last three years;(d) 	the length of NHs constructed during the last three years in the country and the length of 	NHs built in Kerala during the same period indicating the number of projects that have 	been funded fully by the Centre and through Public-Private Partnership (PPP) mode during 	the said period; and(e) 	the details of major NH stretches currently under construction in Kerala including PPP and 	Toll Operate Transfer (ToT) projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e)	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
