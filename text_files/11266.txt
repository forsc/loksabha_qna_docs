                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

278






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Demand for Quality Seeds




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao













                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	whether it is a fact that demand for quality seeds has been increasing over the last three years, if so, the details of the demand for quality seeds in the country for the last three years and the current year; (b)	 the manner in which the requirement of seeds during natural calamities like flood, drought, etc., would be met and the details of seeds reserved in the National Seed Reserve; (c)	 whether it is also a fact that the Union Government provides financial assistance to States and seed-producing agencies for production and distribution of seeds, if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (d)	 the crop development programmes/schemes meant for production and distribution of seeds?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The demand of quality seeds in the country for the last three years and current year 2019-20 as reported by the States is as under :S. No.	Year	Demand (Quantity in lakh Quintals)1.	2016-17	353.492.	2017-18	371.383.	2018-19	353.544.	2019-20	387.31(b):	In order to meet the requirement of seeds during the natural calamities like flood, drought etc., the seeds of small duration, medium duration, stress tolerant varieties of different crops kept in National Seed Reserve (NSR) maintain by states is distributed to the farmers.  The detail of seed quantity kept in the National Seed Reserve (NSR) in last three years is as follows:Contd…2/--2-S. No.	Year	Physical Achievement (Quantity in Quintals)1.	2016-17	2,77,3022.	2017-18	3,35,9223.	2018-19	3,14,184(c) & (d):	The Government of India provides the financial assistance to the States and Implementing Agencies for production and distribution of seeds under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) and Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Materials (SMSP).  	The details of State-wise funds released/provided for seed production, distribution and other activities under Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Materials (SMSP) is at Annexure-I.
