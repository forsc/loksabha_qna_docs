                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

418






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Traditional Tribal Art and Culture Programme under PMKVY




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing any scheme in Maharashtra which includes traditional tribal art in the training programme under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY); and(b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.	   STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) AND (B) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 418 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2020 ASKED BY SHRI RAJENDRA DHEDYA GAVIT REGARDING TRADITIONAL TRIBAL ART AND CULTURE PROGRAMME UNDER PMKVY(a) and (b)	 Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (2016-20) for imparting short duration skill development training under the component of Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) in the country including state of Maharashtra. Under PMKVY (2016-20), training is being imparted in more than 1800 job roles of 38 different sectors including various job roles of traditional arts and crafts. Under the scheme, provisions have been made to promote traditional arts and crafts in the country through imparting training in this sector. Such provision includes that State/UTs have been given flexibility to allocate upto 25% of their total allocated target for imparting skill training in State specific job roles and the sector of local traditional arts and crafts. Under PMKVY (2016-20), till now, 73,667 candidates have been trained/oriented (4,667 trained under STT and 69,000 oriented under RPL) in various traditional job roles in the country. These job roles include like carpet weaving, carving artisans, beading artisans, bamboo mat weaver, tarakashi jeweller, decorative painter, sketching & painting artisans, fashion jewellery, crochet Lace trailers, hand embroiderer etc. Out of these, 1093 candidates have been trained in State of Maharashtra under job role ‘Crochet Lace Trailers’ of Handicrafts sector. Additionally, Under Jan Shikhan Sansthan (JSS) Scheme, training in warli painting is being imparted in the State of Maharashtra.
