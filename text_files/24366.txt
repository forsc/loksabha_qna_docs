                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

172






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Research on Tribal Medicinal Plants




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether tribals have the traditional knowledge of curing disease with locally available medical plants, if so, whether any research institute has been given any project for research on tribal medicinal practices and medicinal plants, so as to preserve this knowledge; and (b) if so, State-wise/Union-Territory-wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA) (a)&(b): Yes, Sir.  Ministry of Tribal Affairs has sanctioned number of projects to State Tribal Research institute / renowned research  Institutes for under taking  research  work on Tribal medicinal practices and medicinal plants under the schemes “Support to Tribal Research Institutes ”  and ‘Tribal Festival, Research Information and Mass Education’. Moreover, Ministry has made Tribal Research Institute, Uttarakhand as the nodal & coordinating TRI for all projects related to traditional medicine and healing practices. The details of such sanctioned projects, State-wise/Union Territory-wise is at Annexure.
