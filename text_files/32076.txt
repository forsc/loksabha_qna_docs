                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Promotion of Eco-tourism




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is preparing any plan to promote eco-tourism in Jharkhand;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the funds released to the State Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM				  (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c): Promotion and Development of tourism, including eco-tourism, is the primary responsibility of the State Governments/UT Administrations. However, Ministry of Tourism provides financial assistance under Swadesh Darshan Scheme for development of Eco Circuits in the country, including Jharkhand.The projects under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Submission of project proposals by the State Government and its sanctioning is a continuous process.The details of the projects sanctioned under the Eco Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme in the country, including Jharkhand, are given at Annexure. *****
