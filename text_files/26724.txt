                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2349






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Deployment of Women Police Officers




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal for deployment of women police officers in every village for the security of women in the country; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the present status in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	As, “Police” is a State subject falling in List-II (State List) of the Seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India the deployment of police personnel including women police personnel is primarily the responsibility of the State Governments/ UTs as per requirement. The Central Government advises the States/UTs from time to time. The Ministry of Home Affairs has issued advisories dated 22.04.2013, 21.05.2014, 12.05.2015, 21.06.2019 and 22.06.2021 to all the State Governments to increase the representation of women police to 33% of the total strength. All the State Governments have been requested to create additional posts of women Constables/Sub-Inspectors by converting the vacant posts of male constables. The aim is that each police station should have at least 3 women Sub-Inspectors and 10 women police Constables, so that a women help desk is manned round the clock.******
