                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2719






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Healthcare Facilities in Himachal Pradesh and Ladakh




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is aware of the poor healthcare facilities in the remote Sub-Divisions of Union Territory of Ladakh like Nubra, Nyoma, Zanskar, Shargole and Drass and Lahaul Spiti Districts of Himachal Pradesh;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to launch/sponsor any Central scheme to improve the healthcare facilities;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which it will be implemented; and(d) if not, the alternative steps proposed to be taken by the Government for providing proper healthcare facilities to the people residing in such areas?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	Public health and hospitals being a State subject, the primary responsibility to provide healthcare facilities to their people vests with the respective State/UT Governments. However, under National Health Mission (NHM), financial and technical support is provided to States/UTs to strengthen their healthcare systems including support for providing accessible, affordable and quality healthcare services, based on the requirements posed by them in their Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs) within their overall resource envelope. As per information provided by  UT of Ladakh  and State Government of Himachal Pradesh, the details of healthcare facilities  in sub-division of Nubra, Nyoma, Zanskar, Shargole and Drass  and Lahaul Spiti distrct of Himachal Pradesh, are  given at Annexure-I.Further, the Prime Minister’s Development Package (PMDP) was launched in 2015 to develop the infrastructure including that of health in erstwhile State of Jammu and Kashmir. Under the PMDP, the following works are being undertaken in UT of Ladakh :?	Up-gradation of SNM Hospital Leh.?	PHC G. M. Pora, Kargil.?	Construction of 100 bedded DH at Khurbatan Kargil (Phase - I)?	Construction of advanced Mortuary in DH Kargil.The details of support provided under National Health Mission to Himachal Pradesh for financial year 2019-20   are given at Annexure-II. (also  available in the form of Record of Proceedings at: https://nhm.gov.in/new_updates_2018/pips /2019_20/rop/hp_rop_2019-20.zip
