                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1603






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






De-Reserved Coaches




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the initiatives taken by the Government for providing journey facilities to the daily short distance commuters using season tickets in sleeper coaches of long distance trains from the starting point and to the terminal stations;(b) the action taken by the Government to implement the assurance given by the Hon’ble Minister to provide de-reserved compartments in 13 trains in Kerala;(c) the action taken by various regional Railways on the recommendation given by Southern Railway for providing de-reserved compartments in 13 trains;(d) whether it comes to the notice of the Government that Divisional Railway Thiruvananthapuram is not providing the de-reserved facility in train no. 16346 by reducing the number of coaches; and(e) if so, the action taken for providing sufficient number of coaches so as to use the sanctioned de-reserved facility?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY                                        (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
