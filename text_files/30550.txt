                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Allocation of Urea to Tamil Nadu




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a)	the quantum of urea, DAP and Potash fertilizers demanded and supplied to Tamil Nadu during the last three years; (b)	whether the Union Government has received any request from the Government of Tamil Nadu to allocate more Urea, DAP and Potash fertilizers to meet the increased demand by farmers for their cultivation; (c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the action taken or proposed to be taken in this regard?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS & HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR.  MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	:    The quantum of Urea, DAP and Potash fertilizers demanded and supplied to Tamil Nadu during the last three years is as follows:All Fertilizers Cumulative Demand & Supply/Availability during FY year 2018-19 to 2020-21                                                                         fig. in LMTS.No  Year	UREA	                     DAP	        MOP	                     NPKS		Demand 	Supply/ 	Demand  Supply/ty	Demand 	Supply/                        Availability            Availability             Availabilaity  Demand  Supply	Demand 	Supply/Availability1	2018-19	8.50	10.92	3.00	3.28	3.50	3.50	5.50	7.572	2019-20	10.00	10.37	3.30	3.37	2.85	3.21	5.25	8.353	2020-21	10.00	11.58	3.25	3.07	3.10	3.88	6.30	9.08 Grand Total	28.50	32.87	9.55	9.72	9.45	10.59	17.05	25.00(b)to (d):  Sir, before the commencement of each cropping season, Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare (DA&FW), in consultation with all the State Governments, assesses the requirement of fertilizers. After assessment of requirement, DA&FW projects month-wise requirement of fertilizers. On the basis of month-wise & State-wise projection given by DA&FW, Department of Fertilizers allocates sufficient/ adequate quantities of fertilizers to States by issuing monthly supply plan and continuously monitors the availability. Further, State Government of Tamil Nadu had requested for additional allocation of 25000 MT of DAP and 10,000 MT of MOP fertilizers. The same has been forwarded to DA&FW for examination.
