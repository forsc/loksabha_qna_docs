                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3580






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Fair Price Shops




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the estimated number of Fair Price Shops (FPS) located in the country and number of beneficiaries thereof, State-wise; (b) 	whether it is a fact that MSP procurement in the country during the year is not robust, if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (c) 	the steps taken by the Government to improve the Central procurement and to ensure remunerative prices to the farmers in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):    Around 5.38 lakh Fair Price Shops (FPSs) under the Public Distribution System (PDS) are operational in the country and number of benefiecieries as on 30.07.2021 under NFSA are approx. 79.51 crores. A State/UT wise statement showing total number FPSs and total number of beneficiaries is annexure I.(b): 	The procurement of wheat and Paddy has been robust during the current marketing seasons KMS 2020-21 and RMS 2021-22. The procurement has reached all time high.  State-wise procurement figures of wheat and rice is enclosed as annexure II & III.(c):  	The procurement policy of Government of India (GOI) is open ended, under which paddy and wheat offered by farmers within the stipulated period (conforming to specifications prescribed in advance by GOI) are purchased for Central Pool at MSP by Food Corporation of India/ State Governments/ State Government Agencies to help farmers get remunerative price and prevent distress sale. MSP operations are given wide publicity through various means and farmers are made aware of quality specifications and purchase system etc.  However, if any producer/farmer gets better price in comparison to MSP, he/she is free to sell his/her produce in open market.  ******Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3580 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 10.08.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.Statement indicating State-wise total No. of Fair Price Shops and Beneficiaries under NFSA Sl.
