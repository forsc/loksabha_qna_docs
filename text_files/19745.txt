                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1212






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Gram Haats in Andhra Pradesh




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Gram Haats in the State of Andhra Pradesh, district-wise;(b)	whether the Government has announced any plans to develop/upgrade Gram Haats into agriculture markets;(c)	if so, the steps taken to convert Gram Haats into Gramin Agriculture Markets in Andhra Pradesh, district wise and the time by which this task will be completed; and(d)	the amount allocated, sanctioned, released and spent so far in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):   There are 275 Gram Haats / village Haats in the State of Andhra Pradesh. District-wise details of village Haats in the State of Andhra Pradesh is at Annexure. (b) to (d):	Yes, Sir. In the Union Budget 2018-19, the Government of India has announced to develop and upgrade existing 22,000 rural haats into Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs). In these GRAMs, physical infrastructure will be strengthened using Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) and other Government Schemes”. Ministry of Rural Development in consultation with the respective States/UTs is developing/upgrading village haats under MGNREGA.   In Andhra Pradesh, 14 village haats have been developed so far, while the development of 105 village haats more are undertaken. So far, an amount of Rs.1.48 crore has been spent for the development of GrAMs in Andhra Pradesh.
