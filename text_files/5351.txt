                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4958






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Kisan Vikas Patra Yojana




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of people benefited by Kisan Vikas Patra Yojana of the Government throughout the country;(b)	 the number of beneficiaries of the Yojana belonging to the State of Rajasthan and in Udaipur district along with the details thereof;(c) 	 whether  the  Government  is considering to take any new steps to cover more and more population under the current scheme;(d)	 if so, the details thereof; and(e)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	A total of 4,03,43,979 Kisan Vikas Patras have been issued in the country since the launch of the scheme.(b):	The number of Kisan Vikas Patras issued in the State of Rajasthan and Udaipur district since the launch of the scheme is 6,92,570 and 16,851 respectively.(c) to (e):	No new steps are being considered.  The scheme is already widely available through Post Offices and Banks throughout the country. ********
