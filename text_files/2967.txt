                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2704






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






National Policy on Marine Fishing




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the National Policy on Marine Fishing helps in enhancing skills and capabilities of traditional fishermen in deep sea fishing in the country, particularly from coastal areas, if so, the details thereof; (b)	the assistance being provided by the Ministry to traditional fishermen to procure vessels, capacity building, etc.; (c)	the details of financial and other assistance provided to fishermen in Andhra Pradesh during the last five years and the current year, year-wise and district-wise along with the number of fishermen benefitted therefrom; and (d)	any special assistance the Ministry is thinking of providing to Andhra Pradesh since it is the leading exporter of fish and aqua products? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a)	Yes, Sir. The National Policy on Marine Fisheries, 2017 helps in enhancing skills and capabilities of traditional fishermen in deep sea fishing in the country, particularly from coastal areas. As per the recommendation of the National Policy on Marine Fisheries,in order to enhance the skills and capabilities of the traditional fishermen to undertake and popularize deep sea fishing, a training programmeon tuna long lining and hygienic  handling and processing of fish onboard the fishing vessels has been started for traditional fishers by the sub ordinate offices of the Department of Fisheries viz; Central Institute of Fisheries Nautical and Engineering Training (CIFNET) and Fishery Survey of India (FSI) with 100% central financial assistance. Already 217 fishermen of Tamil Nadu have been trained in 15 batches onboard the fishing vessels of CIFNET and FSI under this training programme.(b)	This Ministry has introduced a sub-component namely “Assistance for Deep Sea Fishing” under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries to provide financial assistance to traditional fishermen for procurement of resource specific deep-sea fishing vesselslike tuna long liners as per the prevailing Guidelines. An amount of Rs. 302.09 crore has been provided as central assistance by this Ministry to the coastal States/Uts namely Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh, Odisha, Andaman & Nicobar and Daman & Diu towards procurement of about 918 number of resource specific deep sea fishing vessels for traditional fishermen. (c)	The details of financial assistance provided by Government of India under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries to fishermen in Andhra Pradesh during the last five years and the current year, year-wise is furnishedbelow. Release of assistance to fishermen of Districts of Andhra Pradesh is decided by the Government of Andhra Pradesh(inRs. Lakhs) Financial Years	Financial Assistance provided 2014-15	1043.602015-16	777.152016-17	1897.002017-18	2162.882018-19	1302.312019-20 ( till5th July, 2019)	200.00Total	7382.94(d)	Assistance is being provided to all the States and Union Territories including the Government of Andhra Pradesh as per the approved guidelines of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries being under implementation by this Ministry.
