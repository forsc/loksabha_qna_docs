                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Loan Waiver for the Farmers




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any steps for loan waiver for the farmers amid of major losses due to the Covid-19 pandemic out break;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the details of relief measures given to agricultural farmers to overcome from the major losses due to lockdown? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	Government has not announced any proposal for loan waiver for the farmers during the COVID-19 pandemic.  (c):	The details of relief measures given to agricultural farmers are at Annexure. All the relaxation were given for agri-sector, because of which harvesting was not impacted. Further, procurement centres were increased leading to record procurement. Summer crop sowing and Kharif sowing have also increased this year.
