                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Status of pending AIIMS projects




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) under construction in the country, State-wise along with the date on which each projectwas started;(b) the present status of the total amount of loan raised and disbursed to each such project;(c) the total amount of loan raised from foreign institutions and the actual amount disbursed forconstruction of these projects;(d) the rate of interest being charged on loan amount by the foreign institutions financing these projects;(e) the total area of land required to be acquired for each project and the areas of land that has actually beenacquired by the Government so far; and(f) the present status of each of these projects and the time by which these projects are proposed to becompleted?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (f) Setting up of 22 new AIIMS has been approved under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY), 6 of which at Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Raipur and Rishikesh are already functional.  Status of remaining 16 new AIIMS is at Annexure.The details of loan raised from Higher Education Financing Agency (HEFA) and disbursed for each AIIMS is as follows:New AIIMS	Total Loan amount (Rs. crore)AIIMS Mangalagiri	466.32AIIMS Nagpur	523.82AIIMS Kalyani	506.28AIIMS Gorakhpur	522.81AIIMS Bathinda	422.61AIIMS Guwahati	257.50AIIMS Bilaspur	524.12AIIMS Deoghar	170.25AIIMS Jammu	50.00AIIMS Rae Bareli	283.54TOTAL	3727.25AIIMS at Madurai is partially funded through loan from Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) (82% , Rs 1627.7 Crore ), at the rate of 0.95% per annum.Land for setting up of new AIIMS are provided by respective State Government, free of cost. Out of the 16 remaining AIIMS, the land identified for purpose has been transferred by the concerned State Government, except in the cases, as per details given below:   AIIMS	Status of LandRaebareli	Out of approximately 148 acre land identified for the project, 97 acre land handed over by State.Awantipora	Out of 221 acre of land, 187 acre of land has been handed over for establishment of AIIMS.   Manethi	Approximately 200 acre land was identified for the project but the site did not get FCA clearance. State Govt. has not offered alternate site.Darbhanga	Approximately 200.02 acre land identified for the project has not been  handed over by the State Govt.
