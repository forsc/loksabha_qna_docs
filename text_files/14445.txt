                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3059






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Multi-Sectoral Development Programme




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the details of schemes/programmes being implemented under Multi-Sectoral Development Programme in the State of Telangana, with a particular reference to Rangareddy district; (b)     the details of physical targets set and achieved for each of the above programme in Telangana, with a particular reference to Rangareddy district; (c)     whether there are some slippages in implementation of some of the programmes and if so, the reasons therefor and the remedial measures proposed in this regard; and (d)     the funds allocated for this scheme during 2020-21 and the manner in which  the Government proposes to utilize the same?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):       The projects approved under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK)/erstwhile Multi Sectoral Development Programme (MsDP) in the State of Telangana are Additional Classrooms (103), Anganwadi Centres (50), Common Service Centres (11), Computers in Schools (340), Health Centres (9), Hostels (14), ITI (2), Residential Schools (46), School Buildings (7), Teaching Aids (27), Toilets (5) and Bicycles (1250).   Out of these, the projects sanctioned in Rangareddy District are Hostels (4), Residential Schools (4) and Bicycles (500). (b) & (c):  The proposals under PMJVK (erstwhile MsDP) are formulated by the States/UTs on the basis of the felt need of the identified Minority Concentration Area.  As such, the scheme being demand driven, no targets are set by the Government. The projects sanctioned by the Empowered Committee of PMJVK are implemented by the States/UTs.    The projects sanctioned are required to be initiated by the State within 12 months from the date of approval, and completed within the timelines indicated in the project report.   (d)       The funds allocated for the financial year 2020-21 under Budget Estimate (BE) is Rs. 1600 Crore and the funds will be utilised as per the guidelines of PMJVK. Further details are available on the website of the Ministry at www.minorityaffairs.gov.in ***
