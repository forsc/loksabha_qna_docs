                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3688






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Funds for Damaged Houses




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government contemplates to provide funds under 	the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana or any other scheme for 	reconstructing the damaged houses or providing new houses 	to the affected families; and(b) 		if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban [PMAY-U]-Housing for All by 2022 Mission, Government of India has been assisting State/UT Governments in providing pucca houses to all eligible families in the urban areas of the country.Housing is a State subject and as such, selection of beneficiaries under the scheme of PMAY-U is the responsibility of the concerned State/UT Governments. Therefore, State/UT Governments are free to include such beneficiaries whose houses have been damaged/affected, specially due to natural calamities, provided they are otherwise eligible as per the criteria prescribed in the scheme guidelines.  (b)   Under PMAY-U, a total of 1,10,70,970 houses have been sanctioned involving central assistance of ?1,78,076 crore so far. Of the sanctioned houses, 74,83,896 houses have been grounded for construction; of which 43,32,651 houses have been completed/delivered so far.                                              *****
