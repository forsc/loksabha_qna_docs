                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5433






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Special Grants to Tamil Nadu




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is proposing to sanction special grants or special package in view of acute water crisis in Tamil Nadu; and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (b)   No such proposal is under consideration in the Ministry of Jal Shakti. However, Ministry of Jal Shakti, Government  of India is implementing the scheme of “Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies” under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY). Urban water bodies having water spread area from 2 hectares to 10 hectares and rural water bodies having minimum water spread area of 5 hectares are eligible to be included under the scheme.	        Since XII Plan, total number of 153 water bodies  from Tamil Nadu with an estimated cost of    Rs.77.75 crores have been included under the scheme of RRR of water bodies for central assistance. Total central fund released to Tamil Nadu under this scheme as on June 2019 is Rs. 16.25 crores.*****
