                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

979






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Digitisation of Museums in Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan




Kaushal Kishore













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Arjunlal Meena
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has started or supported digitization and development works in the museums of  Uttar Pradesh State and Rajasthan;(b)	 whether the Government has any outstanding payment towards the State of Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh in the project of digitization and development of works at museums;(c)	 if so, the detailed information on the scope of work and specific expenditure incurred along with the assistance provided; and(d)	the time by which the outstanding payment is likely to be paid to complete the project initiated?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM (PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Ministry of Culture  operates a Museum Grant Scheme under which financial assistance is provided for setting up of new Museums by State Governments and Societies, Autonomous Bodies, Local Bodies and Trusts registered under the Societies Act, to strengthen and modernize the existing museums at the regional, state and district level, digitisation of art objects in the museums across the country for making their images/catalogues available over the website and for capacity building of Museum professionals. Museum Grant Scheme is applicable to all States and UTs including Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan.(b)	to (d) Details of outstanding payment towards the State of Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh for Setting up/Modernization of Museums, Digitisation of Museum collections under the Museum Grant scheme along with detailed information on the scope of works and specific expenditure incurred with the assistance provided may be seen at ‘ANNEXURE’.  Funds are released to the implementing agency, as per standard scheme guidelines, upon receipt of requisite documents.
