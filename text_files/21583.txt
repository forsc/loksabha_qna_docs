                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






OBC Reservation in Medical Admission




Sumalatha Ambareesh













                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that the Government has constituted a Committee to decide the terms of implementation of OBC reservation in the all India quota scheme in medical admissions, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has stipulated any time frame for the Committee to submit its report; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):	In compliance of Order dated 27th July 2020 of the Hon’ble Madras High Court in connection with WP No 8324/2020, the Ministry constituted a Committee on 7th September 2020to finalize the manner in which the facilities of OBC reservation are to be provided for All India Quota Seats in UG/ PG medical courses in the State of Tamil Nadu with effect from next academic year 2021-22. The Committee submitted its report on 21st October, 2020 to the Ministry.
