                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

860






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Atal Jyoti Yojana




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the targets set under the Atal Jyoti Yojana for installation of street lights in the country including Maharashtra; and (b) the status of its implementation and achievements made thereunder especially in Maharashtra as on date, State/UT-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY,  POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) Under Phase-I of Atal Jyoti Yojana (AJAY), the target was to install around 2000 solar street lights (SSLs) in each of the 169 parliamentary constituencies in the states of Assam, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha and Uttar Pradesh by 31 March 2018.Under Phase-II of Atal Jyoti Yojana the target is to install 3.04 lakh SSLs in the following states/regions: i.	States of Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha and Assam, which were covered in Phase-I of the Scheme. ii.	Hilly states of Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand.iii.	North Eastern states including Sikkim.iv.	Island UTs Andaman & Nicobar and Lakshadweep. v.	Parliamentary Constituencies covering 48 Aspirational Districts of states other than those covered in (i) to (iii) above. In four Aspirational Districts namely Nandurbar, Gadchiroli, Washim and Osmanabad of Maharashtra MNRE plans to install 5,500 SSLs under Phase-II of Atal Jyoti Yojana. (b) Under Phase-I of Atal Jyoti Yojana 1,34,342 of solar street lights have been installed in the states of Assam, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha and Uttar Pradesh as on 31.10.2019. Under Phase-II of Atal Jyoti Yojana 13,583 solar street lights have been installed as on 31.10.2019. State-wise break-up of installations under Phase-I and Phase-II Schemes is placed at Annexure. For aspirational districts of Maharashtra covered under AJAY Phase-II, sanction is yet to be received for 25% of the cost of installation of Solar street lights in the concerned Parliamentary Constituencies from respective MPLADS Fund. *****
