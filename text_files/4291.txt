                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3970






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Development of Railway Station as World Class Station




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Varkala Sivagiri Railway Station in Kerala has been selected by the Indian Railway Station Development Corporation (IRSDC) to develop it  as a world class station and if so, the present status of the plan;(b) whether IRSDC prepared a master plan for the same and if so, the details thereof along with the estimated cost and funding in this regard;(c) whether the State Government of Kerala has signed an MoU with IRSDC and set up any high power committee for the same; and (d) if so, the details and outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): Yes, Sir. Varkala Sivagiri Railway Station has been entrusted to Indian Railway Stations Development Corporation Limited(IRSDC) for undertaking Techno-economic Feasibility Study as a part of the station redevelopment programme of Railways.  IRSDC has initiated a draft Memorandum of Understanding(MOU) between State Government of Kerala and IRSDC for redevelopment of Varkala station. ******
