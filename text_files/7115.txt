                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

915






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Payment Pendency to Vendors of NBCC




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 Whether the National Building Construction Corporation (NBCC)  owes large sums of money to its vendors;(b)	 If so, the quantum of sum/payment pending, vendor-wise; and(c)	 the details and reasons of such pendency?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	NBCC has informed that as on 10.11.2019, approximatelyRs. 1,668 crore (excluding Arbitration cases) is pending for payment towards vendors. Vendor-wise details are available at web address https://www.nbccindia.com/pdfData/Vendor/Appendix_I_and%20__II.pdf.This pendency is mainly attributable to delay in receipt of funds from clients because of various reasons such as pending approval for deviations, extra items, substitute items, escalation and extension of time from clients, non-submission of documents as per terms and conditions of contract by the contractors etc.                               *****
