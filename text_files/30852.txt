                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2392






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the work being carried out/still under progress under the North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS);(b) 	the objective behind the NESIDS;(c) 	the steps being taken by the Government for water supply, electricity and connectivity under physical infrastructure project;(d) 	the provision made by the Government to promote tourism; and(e) 	the details of the progress made in education and health sector?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e) Under the North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS), financial assistance is provided to enhance tourism through physical infrastructure projects relating to water supply, power and connectivity; and to upgrade social infrastructure for primary and secondary education and health. So far, 110 projects worth Rs.2563.14 crore have been sanctioned in the North Eastern States under NESIDS and are at various stages of implementation. The sector-wise details of projects approved for funding in North Eastern States under NESIDS are as under:-S. No.	Sector	Number of projects	Cost (Rs. in crore)1	Connectivity	351138.572	Power	4231.983	Water Supply	18585.864	Education	2696.265	Health	27510.47	Total	1102563.14
