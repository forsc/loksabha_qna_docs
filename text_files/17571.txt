                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1156






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Accidents in Mines




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has drawn any plan to make mines safe for the miners;(b)the details of the number of accidents in mines over the last five years and current year;(c)the details of number of deaths and injured persons in those accidents, State-wise; and(d)the steps being taken by the Government to prevent such accidents?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):		The Mines Act, 1952 and Rules / Regulations made thereunder  provides for safety measures for the persons employed in the Mines.  These provisions are enforced by Directorate General of Mines Safety to make mines safe for the miners. (b):		The details of the number of accidents in mines during calendar year 2015 to 2020 (till August, 2020) are as under:- CoalYear	Fatal Accidents	Serious Accidents2015	54	3022016	67	2682017	56	2592018	49	2652019	54	1922020*	24	47* Till 31.08.2020. Data for the year 2019 & 2020 are provisional.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::Non-CoalYear	Fatal Accidents	Serious Accidents2015	45	352016	39	372017	46	212018	47	222019	47	592020*	18	13*Till 31.08.2020. Data for the year 2019 & 2020 are provisional.(c):	The details of the number of accidents, deaths and injured persons in accidents during calendar year 2015 to 2020 (till August, 2020), State-wise, is annexed.(d):	Directorate General of Mines Safety takes following steps to ensure safety of mine workers and prevent accidents therein:-(i)	Regular inspections are done and based on the observations during the inspections, actions such as pointing out contraventions, withdrawal of permission, issue of improvement notice, prohibition of employment and prosecution in the court of law are taken to ensure compliance.(ii)	Accidents are inquired into and based on the findings of accidents, actions such as warning to delinquent, suspension of certificate, modification in the method of working, action by management like stoppage of increment, dismissal from service, recorded warning, withholding promotion and prosecution in the court of law, are taken. (iii)	Promotional measures are also taken viz. holding of National Conference on safety in mines, National Safety Awards (Mines), observance of Safety Week, safety campaigns, organization of workshops, safety committee meetings etc.*                                                                 ******
