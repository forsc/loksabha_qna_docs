                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3353






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Bonded Labourers




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Aparajita Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has any figure about the total number of bonded labourers in each of the States of the country at present and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (b)whether there is any national data on the details of conviction awarded to accused persons charged under Bonded Labour offences through Summary trial by the DM/SDM under Section 21 of Bonded Labour System (Abolition) Act, 1976 and if so, the details of the last three years; (c)the number of cases wherein FIR has been registered under the bonded labour system (Abolition) Act, 1976 in the last three years, State/UT-wise, particularly of Bhubaneswar;(d)the rate of conviction of the cases that are registered under Bonded Labour System (Abolition) Act, 1976, in the last ten years, both under Summary Trial by District Magistrate and under regular trial by the Judicial Magistrate; and(e)the measures/steps taken/being taken by the Government to adress the lack of conviction in bonded labour cases?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	State-wise number of Bonded Labourers released and rehabilitated is enclosed at Annexure.(b):	Central Government does not maintain this data. Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(c):	Central Government does not maintain this data.  (d):	No record in this regard is available with the Central Government.(e):	Government of India has reviewed the Bonded Labour Rehabilitation Scheme from time to time and the revised Central Sector Scheme for Rehabilitation of Bonded labourer-2016 has been implemented with effect from 18.05.2016.*                                                       *****
