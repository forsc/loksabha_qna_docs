                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4207






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Funds for Youth and Sports Activities




Balak Nath














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has provided sufficient funds for youth and sports activities in Rajasthan; (b) if so, the details thereof and total funds granted and utilised during each of the last three years and the current year, scheme-wise and district-wise in Rajasthan; and(c) the details and the number of players benefitted under the said schemes/programmes during the said period, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) & (b) Yes Sir, Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports provides fundsfordevelopmentofYouthandSportsActivitiesinthecountry as a whole including the state of Rajasthan. Thefunds are allocated by the Ministry Scheme-wise not State-wise. The scheme-wise details of total funds granted andutilized across the country during each of the last threeyearsandthecurrentyear areprovided atannexure.(c)As stated in (a) & (b) above, the Schemes of the Ministry ofYouth Affairs and Sports are not implemented State-wise,hence there are no such district-wise details of the numberof players benefitted under the schemes available with theMinistry.****
