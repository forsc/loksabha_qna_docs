                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3440






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Strengthening of Special Forces




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Special Infrastructure Scheme for upgradation and strengthening of Special Forces i.e. Special Operation Group and District Volunteer Force has been started by the Union Government;(b)	 if so, the details and the salient features of the said scheme;(c)	the details of the objectives of the said scheme achieved so far in various States;(d)	the details of assistance by the Union Government to various States under the said scheme, State-wise;(e)	whether the Union Government has received any request from the States for extension of the Scheme for another three years; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken so far by the Union Government on such requests?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (f):	The Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS) including construction of 250 Fortified Police Stations in Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas initially approved for a period of 03 years i.e. from 2017-18 to 2019-20 has been further extended up to 31.03.2021. The Scheme envisages upgradation and strengthening of Special Forces (SFs)/Special Intelligence Branches (SIBs) of States along with construction of 250 Fortified Police Stations. Proposals of 10 States with respect to Special Forces (SFs)/Special Intelligence Branches (SIBs) for an amount of Rs. 371 crore and  projects of 7 States worth Rs.620 crore for construction of 250 Fortified Police Stations (FPS)were sanctioned. State-wise sanctioned amount is given in Annexure. Though the scheme is implemented on re-imbursement basis, an amount of Rs.152.67 crore has also been released as advance to the States.******
