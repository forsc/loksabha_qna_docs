                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

234






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Detention Camp for Foreigners




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any detention camp for foreigners in any State other thanAssam;(b) if not, the reasons for setting up of detention camps in Assam only;(c) the number of detention camps in Assam; and(d) the number of foreigners residing in the detention camps along with theirnationalities, Camp-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):  Detention Centres are set up by State Governments/UT Administrations as per their requirement to detain illegal migrants/foreigners who have completed their sentence pending their deportation to their native country. At persent, detention centres have been setup only in the state of Assam.(c) & (d):  As on 13.11.2019, a total of 1043 Nos. of foreigners are residing in 06 (six) Detention Centres in Assam.
