                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4005






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Funds Allocated for Promotion of Rural Tourism




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated any scheme to bring improvement in various sectors to promote rural tourism in the country;(b)	If so, the details thereof; and(c)	the funds allocated during the last three years for promotion of rural tourism, State-wise and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	Yes, Sir. Recognising the potential of rural tourism in the country, the Ministry of Tourism had identified Rural Circuit as one of the thematic circuits for release of funds to States/UTs for development of tourism related infrastructure under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme. The details of the projects sanctioned by the Ministry of Tourism under the Rural Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme in the country are given below:(in Rs. Crore)<pre>State/Year of sanction	Details	Amount Sanctioned	Amount ReleasedBihar2017-18	Development of Gandhi Circuit: Bhitiharwa- Chandrahia- Turkaulia	44.65	35.72Kerala2018-19	Development of Malanad Malabar Cruise Tourism Project	80.37	23.77</pre>Apart from the above, Ministry of Tourism has also formulated a National Strategy and Roadmap for Development of Rural Tourism in India - An initiative towards Atmanirbhar Bharat.  The strategy document focuses on the following key pillars:i	Model policies and best practices for rural tourismii	Digital technologies and platforms for rural tourismiii	Developing clusters for rural tourismiv	Marketing support for rural tourismv	Capacity building of stakeholdersvi	Governance and institutional framework*****
