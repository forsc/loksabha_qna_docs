                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3005






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Six-laning of National Highways




K. Shanmuga Sundaram













                                                    P. Velusamy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has undertaken the projects of six-laning of existing Salem 	to Coimbatore National Highway (NH-544) including a ring road for the city of 	Coimbatore under the Bharatmala Pariyojana; (b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof along with the funds allocated and 	utilized for the purpose; (c)	the time by which the said work is likely to be completed; and (d) 	the other steps taken by the Government to complete the ongoing National Highway 	projects in Tamil Nadu in a time bound manner?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)	Yes Sir. Salem-Cochin stretch via Coimbatore is a part of North South Corridor classified as National Corridor under Bharatmala Pariyojana. The stretch from Coimbatore to Chengapalli is already six-lane and from Chengapalli to Salem is four-lane at present. The Detailed Project Report (DPR) for capacity augmentation for Chengapalli to Salem (103 Km) and for Coimbatore half ring road (81.80 Km) has been initiated. Development of road stretches is taken up depending upon the outcome of DPR, project viability and availability of funds. In order to expedite the completion of ongoing National Highways, coordination meetings are held with senior level officials of Government of Tamil Nadu.*****
