                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2645






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Corporate Social Responsibility




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to appeal to corporate sector to spend a certain percentage share of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) for the upgradation of Anganwadi Centres; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the response of the corporate sector thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b)  There is no such proposal to appeal to corporate sector to spend a certain percentage share of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) for the upgradation of Anganwadi Centres.****
