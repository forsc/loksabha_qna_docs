                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3264






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






ESI Hospitals/Dispensaries in Small Cities




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of ESIC hospitals providing their services across the country at present and the details of the criteria to set up new ESIC hospitals; (b)whether the Government is working on any scheme to set up ESIC hospitals in the small cities and if so, the details thereof; (c)whether the Government plans for ESIC Dispensary-cum-Branch office in every district of the country and if so, the details thereof along with the places/districts wherein the proposed ESIC Dispensary-cum-Branch offices are planned to be set up, State-wise including Odisha ; and (d)the time by which these are likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		At present, there are 160 Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) hospitals  providing medical services across the country. Out of these, 50 hospitals are being directly run by ESI Corporation while remaining 110 hospitals are being run by ESI Directorate of respective State Governments.  Details of the criteria to set up new ESI hospitals based on minimum number of Insured Persons  is placed at Annexure-I.(b):		Setting up of ESI hospital in an area is approved based on the number of Insured Persons (IPs) in that  area  regardless  of size of Contd..2/-:: 2 ::the city.  Accordingly, the ESI Corporation has given approval for setting up of 50 new ESI hospitals. The State/UT wise details of these hospitals are placed at Annexure-II.(c):		Following criteria has been framed by the ESI Corporation for setting up of a Dispensary-cum-Branch Office (DCBO):-“DCBO shall be established/operationalized in only such districts which have no ESI Dispensary and have Insured Person (IP) population of 3000 or more within a radius of 10 km.  In case a Branch Office exists in such district, then the Branch Office shall be converted into DCBO”. List of 68 functional DCBOs in the country including in the state of Odisha is placed at Annexure-III. List of 10 newly sanctioned DCBOs in the States is placed at Annexure-IV.  (d):		No timeline has been assigned.  Setting up of a DCBO is a continuous and ongoing process.****                                                             *****
