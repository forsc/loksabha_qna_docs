                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

637






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Construction of Roads




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any scheme to construct roads expeditiously in the country under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the rural roads constructed along with the expenditure incurred during the last five years in Bundelkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) is a one-time special intervention of Government of India to provide rural connectivity, by way of a single all-weather road, to the eligible unconnected habitations in the core network of designated population size. In order to expedite construction of roads in the Country under the Scheme, the Ministry has taken various steps, which are as under:-(i)	Expeditious sanctions of State Government’s proposal;(ii)	Continuous monitoring of award of works;(iii)	Use of locally available materials/industrial wastes & cold mix technology to improve pace of works;(iv)	Constant review of progress of work through Performance Review Committee meetings, Regional Review Meetings and Field visits. (c):	The details of the rural roads constructed along with the expenditure incurred during the last five years in Bundelkhand are given in Annexure.*****
