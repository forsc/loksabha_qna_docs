                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Uniform Civil Code




Dushyant Singh














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has plans to introduce bill on Uniform Civil Code this year; (b)	if so the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government plans to abolish minority status accorded to certain religions under the Uniform Civil Code; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATIONTECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)& (b)	Article 44 of the Constitution of India states that the state shall endeavor to 	secure for the citizens a Uniform Civil Code throughout the territory of India. 	The Government is committed to honour this Constitutional mandate. 	However, this requires wide scale consultations.(c) & (d) No, Sir.
