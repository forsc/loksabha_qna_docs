                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3392






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Setting up of Eco Parks




Santosh Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of State-wise number of Eco parks set up in various parts of the country by various coal sectors; (b)	 whether the Government has allocated any funds for the proper maintenance of such parks; and(c)	 if so, the details thereof during the last two years?
