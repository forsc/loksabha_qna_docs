                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1573






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Testing of PPE by BIS




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Bureau of Indian Standards(BIS) on April 14, 2020, had sought testing of Personal Protective Equipment(PPE) under ISO 11092 to ensure breathability of material, if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether this was withdrawn subsequently; (c) 	if so, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	whether there are any current standards in place to ensure breathability of material used to manufacture PPE?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a)	 :	In April 2020, the Technical Textiles for Medtech Applications Sectional Committee, TXD 36 of BIS had finalized a draft standard on Bio-Protective Coveralls which is the major component of the Personal Protective Equipment (PPE). However, the requirement of ‘breathability’ was not incorporated in that draft standard. The draft standard was subsequently not notified.(b)  & (c) :	Do not arise.(d)	      :	No, Sir.*****
