                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2709






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Welfare Schemes for Differently Abled Persons




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  Whether any scheme is being implemented by the Government for the welfare of persons with disabilities and mentally retarded;(b)  If so, the details thereof;(c)  the funds allocated by the Government for the development of the Persons with disabilities during the last three years and the current year, State-wise; and(d)  the steps being taken by the Government for making the persons with disabilities self-reliant?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR) (a) to (d): The Department has taken various steps  for making the persons with disabilities self-reliant and running several schemes / projects through its National Institutes, National Trust, National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC) and Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation of India (ALIMCO) as well as through State Governments / Autonomous Bodies/ Organizations / Institutes and Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) for welfare and rehabilitation of persons with disabilities including mentally retarded persons throughout the country. Details of the major schemes implemented by the Government for Divyangjans are at Annexure I. The schemes of the Department are Central Sector schemes implemented through various implementing agencies (GOs and NGOs). Fund is not allocated to the states. However, funds released by the Department during the last three years and the current year, state-wise under major schemes i.e. ADIP, SIPDA, DDRS, Scholarship Schemes, National Action Plan and National Trust are at Annexure-II to Annexure-VII.*****
