                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Air India’s Market Share




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Air India is not among the top three airlines in terms of market share;(b) if so, the reasons for constant decrease in the number of passengers; and(c) the different steps taken by the Government to improve the quality index of Air India?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) Air India has the largest market share in the International Market and has remained in the top three in terms of domestic market share in the current year, 2019 (from January, 2019 to September, 2019).;;;(b) Number of passengers in last three years (both domestic and international) shows increasing trend:;;Year                   No. of passengers (in million);;2016-17                     23.50;2017-18                     26.902018-19                     28.61;(c) The Government prepared a plan to bring operational and financial efficiency in Air India. This plan focuses on the financial and operational efficiencies so that substantial increase in revenue or cost saving could be achieved. Also, operational and financial performance parameters and milestones fixed are being regularly reviewed by the Government.
