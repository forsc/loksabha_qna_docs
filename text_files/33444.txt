                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

753






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






GST on Mobile Phones




T.R.V.S. Ramesh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Goods and Services Tax (GST) on mobile phones is very high; (b)  if so, the details thereof; (c)  whether the Government proposes to reduce applicable GST rate in adherence of the vision of Digital India; and (d)  if so, the time by which it is likely to be done?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (d): GST rates are prescribed on the basis of the recommendations of the GST Council. As recommended by the Council the GST rate on Mobile Phones is 18%. Council has not made any further recommendation relating to the GST rate on Mobile Phones.******
