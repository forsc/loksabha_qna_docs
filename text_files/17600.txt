                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1210






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Unorganised Workers Social Security Act




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of workers registered under Unorganised Workers Social Security Act, 2008 and the number of identity cards issued thereof during the past year;(b)the state-wise details of number of workers registered and the number of workers who availed the social security benefit under the scheme during the past year; and(c)the steps/measures taken by Government to ensure more registration of workers to avail the benefit of the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        a) to (c):	 The Government envisages  creation of a National Database for Unorganised Workers (NDUW). This database shall be seeded with Aadhaar and used for facilitating delivery of social security schemes implemented by the Government. At present unorganised workers are registered under different social security schemes to avail the benefit under concerned schemes.*****
