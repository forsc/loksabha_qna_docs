                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1472






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	the status of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana under the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture; (b):	 the number of farmers who took up organic farming as part of the scheme; (c):	the number of Participatory Guarantee Certification provided to such farmers till date; and (d):	 the number of clusters and land area brought under the net of organic farming?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a ) to (d);	 Chemical free, low input cost, sustainable Organic farming is promoted under the scheme Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) since 2015-16. Cluster formation of farmers, their capacity building, support for preparation/ procurement of inputs, certification, marketing are major components of the scheme. The scheme provides support of            Rs 50,000/hectare/ 3 years of which Rs 31,000 is given as input support directly to farmers.  As on date, 6.19 lakh hectares has been brought under organic farming and 15.47 lakh farmers in 30934 clusters have adopted the sustainable agriculture practice.  An amount of Rs1278 crores has been released to States/ UTs/ ICAR institutions/ agencies since inception of the scheme. So far, 13.54 lakh Participatory Guarantee System (PGS) certificates have been issued on PGS portal.****
