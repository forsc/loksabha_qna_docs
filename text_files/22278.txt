                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3287






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Marathi Speaking People at Karnataka-Maharashtra Border




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the native language of the major portion of population residing at the Karnataka-Maharashtra Border areas is Marathi;(b)	the names of the main cities/villages having such a population;(c)	whether there is a long-standing demand to make the Marathi dominated region at the Maharashtra- Karnataka Border a Union territory;(d)	if so, whether any representation has been received by the Union Government in this regard;(e)	if so, the details thereof;(f)	whether the Government proposes to declare the said Marathi dominated region as Union Territory;(g)	if not, the reasons therefor; and (h)	the immediate steps being taken by the Union Government to safeguard the interests of the Marathi speaking population?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (b) :	As per Census 2011, Marathi speaking population in border sub-districts of Maharashtra-Karnataka is 55,98,325.   In the towns / cities of Dudhani, Ichalkaranji, Kagal, Kamalnagar, Maindargi, Nipani, Sadalgi and Sankeshwar,  Marathi  speaking  population  were found during Census 2011.-2-LS.USQ.NO.3287 FOR 16.03.2021(c) to (h) :	Demands and representations have been received from time to time from various individuals and organisations to make the Marathi dominated area in Karnataka bordering Maharashtra as a Union Territory.  However, no such proposal is under consideration.  *****
