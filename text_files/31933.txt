                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3271






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Trauma Care Units




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether proposals have been received for setting up Trauma Care unit in various cities of Madhya Pradesh including Jabalpur;b)	if so, the details there;c)	whether the Government proposes to sanction the said Trauma Care Unit; andd)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (d): During 11th Five Year Plan (2007-2012) under scheme “Assistance for Capacity Building for Developing Trauma Care Facilities in Government Hospitals on National Highways” proposals were received from State Government of Madhya Pradesh  and five hospitals/medical colleges were sanctioned for setting up of trauma care facilities one each at Civil Hospital, Shivpuri; Gajra Raja Medical College (GRMC) Hospital , Gwalior; District Hospital,Narsimhapur; District Hospital,Sagar and District Hospital,Seoni. No proposals were received for Jabalpur from the State Government.
