                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2387






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Ring Road around Amaravati




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved 182 kms. ring road around Amaravati, the new 	capital of Andhra Pradesh and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the time by which the said project is likely to be completed;(c)	whether there is any change in the decision of the Ministry on the project in view of the 	proposed three new capitals in the State; and  (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) The feasibility report for the Ring Road around Amaravati has been prepared.  The approximate length of the above Green Field project is 189 km and involves an extent of 3325 Ha of the land acquisition. The land acquisition is to be done by the State Government of Andhra Pradesh.  It is too early to indicate the time schedule for completion at this stage.(c)	No Sir.(d)	Does not arise.*****
