                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

711






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Development and Modernisation of Facilities at Tourist Places




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated any action plan for the development and modernization of facilities provided at various tourist places of the country including Telangana;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of tourist spots that have been covered under the scheme and the details thereof, State-wise; and (d)	the amount of funds allocated for the said purpose during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM			      (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism under the schemes of “Swadesh Darshan”, “National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)” and ‘Assistance to Central Agencies for Tourism Infrastructure Development’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory Administrations/Central Agencies for development and modernization of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country, including Telangana. 	The details of State-wise projects sanctioned under the Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies Schemes for development of tourism related infrastructure and facilities at various tourism destinations in the country including Telangana is given at Annexure.*****
