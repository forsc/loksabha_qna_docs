                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1059






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Independent Commission for Higher Education




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposed to establish an independent commission for higher education with the independent academies including foreign academies for all Eighth Schedule Languages;(b) if so, the details thereof with the details of its formation;(c) whether the Government has fixed any time-frame for the said purpose; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): The Ministry of Education has announced the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 on 29.07.2020 after obtaining approval of Cabinet. The NEP-2020 envisions setting up of a Higher Education Commission of India as an umbrella body with four independent verticals to perform distinct functions of regulation, accreditation, funding, and academic standard setting. Accordingly, the Ministry is in the process of drafting a Bill for establishment of the Higher Education Commission of India.*****
