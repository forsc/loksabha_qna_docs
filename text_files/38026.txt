                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






National Water Policy




Jual Oram













                                                    Janardan Singh Sigriwal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has finalised the new National Water Policy;(b) if so, the details thereof along with its salient features;(c) the time by which the new policy is likely to be implemented;(d) whether the Government proposes to set up a National Bureau of Water Use Efficiency; and(e) if so, the details thereof along with its proposed functions and motives? 






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI  (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) to (c) At present the National Water Policy - 2012 is in effect. However, to address the present challenges in water sector, revision of National Water Policy has been envisaged and a drafting committee was constituted to revise the National Water Policy. (d) & (e) Yes, Sir. The objectives of National Bureau of Water Use Efficiency (NBWUE) include improving water use efficiency in various sectors such as irrigation, domestic water supply, municipal and/or industrial uses in the country, to plan and execute nation-wide program for promotion of efficient use of water and to take steps to encourage preferential treatment for use of water efficient equipment or appliances in consultation with the concerned States.*****
