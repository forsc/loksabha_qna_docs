                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3306






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






UJJWALA Scheme




Nisith Pramanik














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of children and women being benefited under the Ujjwala scheme; and(b)		the number of women and children rescued and rehabilitated in Cooch Behar constituency of the West Bengal as it is located close to border areas?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The children and women benefited under the Ujjawala Scheme during the year 2019-20 is 5133.(b)	No rescue and rehabilitation component of Ujjawala Scheme is functioning in Cooch Behar constituency of West Bengal. However, the number of rescued and rehabilitated women and children is 94 and 154 respectively in West Bengal during the year 2018 as per the data provided by NCRB. *****
