                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1720






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






MPLAD Fund




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amounts made available by the Union Government through the MPLAD fund in the country during the last three years along with the State/Union Territory-wise details thereof;  (b) whether the amounts released from the MPLAD fund by the Union Government has been utilised totally in all the districts of the country;  (c) if so, the State/Union Territory-wise details thereof; and  (d) the mechanism put in place by the Union Government for monitoring the use of this fund?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a)  The details of the funds released by the Union Government, State / Union Territory-wise under MPLADS during the last three years i.e. 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 are at columns 3, 5 and 7  respectively, of the Annexure to the reply.(b)  No Sir. The funds released under MPLADS are non-lapsable and the unspent balances of a particular year can be  utilized in the subsequent year(s). In view of the nature and dynamics of the scheme, the unutilized balances are bound to exist at any given point of time.(c) The details of the funds utilised  State / Union Territory-wise  under MPLADS during the last three years  i.e. 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 are at columns 4, 6 and 8  respectively, of the Annexure to the reply.(d)  This Ministry is monitoring the use of this fund as per the monitoring mechanisms, which have been duly prescribed in the Guidelines on MPLADS. The Guidelines are available in the public domain, including on the official website (www.mplads.gov.in).                                                    ****
