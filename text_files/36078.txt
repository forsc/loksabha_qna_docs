                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3320






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Funds to Odisha under PMGKAY




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of beneficiaries under the PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) till date, State-wise including district-wise details in Odisha; (b) 	the amount of food grains in terms of metric tonnes allocated to Odisha; and (c) 	the financial burden borne by the Ministry as well as the State of Odisha under the said scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	Under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PM-GKAY), the Department has allocated foodgrains to all States/UTs for additional free-of-cost distribution to around 80 Crore National Food Security Act (NFSA) beneficiaries in the country. The State/UT wise number of NFSA beneficiaries also covered under the PMGKAY scheme and the district wise details of Odisha are at Annexure-I & II respectively.(b): So far, under the PM-GKAY implementation phases I to V, the Department has allocated a total of about 30.77 Lakh Metric Tonnes (LMT) foodgrains to the State of Odisha. (c): 	Hundred percent financial burden of implementing PMGKAY scheme in all States/UTs, including Odisha is borne by the Central Government. Therefore, there is no financial burden on the State Government of Odisha for implementation of PMGKAY in the State.*****ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3320 FOR ANSWER ON 23.03.2022 IN THE LOK SABHA.State/UT wise number of NFSA beneficiaries also covered under the PMGKAY scheme (In lakh)Sl.
