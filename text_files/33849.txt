                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1282






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Digital Payments Infrastructure




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)	the details of the extent of digital payments infrastructure usage in North Eastern Region (NER), State-wise;(b)	the details of ATM penetration in NER, State-wise; and(c)	the details of the steps taken by the Government to increase connectivity and reduce server downtime/ failed transactions in the NER? 






ANSWER




                                                        ): Government of India is making all efforts for promotion of digital payments and enabling digital payment acceptance infrastructure across the country including North Eastern Region (NER). As a result of coordinated efforts of the Government with all stakeholders, a significant increase in number of digital payment transactions have been achieved from 2,071 crore in FY 2017-18 to 5,554 crore in FY 2020-21. A total of 6,380 crore digital payment transactions have been achieved till 02.02.2022 in FY 2021-22 (as per DigiDhan Dashboard: URL - https://digipay.gov.in/ ).
