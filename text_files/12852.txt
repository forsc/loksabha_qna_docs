                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1742






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Digitally Enabled Classrooms




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to improve the quality of education through digitally enabledclassrooms and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to recruit the best teachers from across the world for remoteteaching through Swayam platform and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the percentage of Kendriya Vidyalayas with all digitally enabled classrooms, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) : Under the existing Information & Communication Technology (ICT) component of SamagraShiksha Scheme, classroom centric digitally enabled provision will be made to further improve thequality of education. High quality e-content is already being provided through Digital Infrastructure forKnowledge Sharing (DIKSHA), National Repository of Open Education Resources (NROER), e-Pathshala and Study Webs of Active learning for Young Aspiring Minds (SWAYAM), DTH channels(SWAYAM PRABHA). Steps are being taken to further enhance the reach of these platforms and alsoimprove the quality of the content.(b) : Teachers are not specifically recruited for teaching through SWAYAM. As on date, courses areoffered by teachers from the premier Indian education institutions.(c) : There is no provision for having all digitally enabled classrooms in Kendriya Vidyalayas.****
