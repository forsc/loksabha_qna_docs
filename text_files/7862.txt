                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






CCTV Cameras




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated to Southern Railway for the fitting, installation and commissioning of CCTV cameras in various railway stations in the Tamil Nadu during the last five years, year-wise; (b) whether all railway stations in Tamil Nadu are under CCTV camera surveillance; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?             






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): The funds allocated to Southern Railway for providing Close Circuit Television (CCTV) cameras in various railway stations in Tamil Nadu during last five years are:-Year	 Fund allocation2015-16	 `8.26 Crore2016-17	 `22.14 Crore2017-18	 `1.59 Crore2018-19	 `1.59 Crore2019-20	 `19.54 Crore(b) and (c): At present 19 railway stations in Tamil Nadu have been provided with CCTV surveillance system. Provision of CCTV at remaining railway stations is targeted to be completed by March, 2021.  ****
