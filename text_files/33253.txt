                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

859






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Smart Class in Rural Areas




Faizal P.P. Mohammed













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has conducted any audits on the coverage, status and functionality of smart classes in rural areas of the country; (b) 	if so, the details of when such an audit was last conducted and from where the report of the same can be accessed; and (c) 	the details of budgetary allocations, approvals, and utilisations for smart classes under various schemes, year- wise from 2019 till date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION (DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (c): Education is in the concurrent list of the Constitution and majority of the schools, are under the domain of respective State and UT Governments. Central government maintains Unified District Information System for Education (UDISE) data on certain parameters for all state/UTs, such as number of ICT labs, Internet connectivity, etc.The ICT Labs/Smart Class Rooms are approved in schools under the centrally sponsored scheme of Samagra Shiksha. State/UT wise, details of financial allocation/approval & utilisation, year-wise for years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 under the scheme are at Annexure. *****
