                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5622






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Textile Training Centre




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has received any proposal from the State of Gujarat for setting up of more textile training centres; and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	There is no proposal from the Government of Gujarat for setting up any new textile training centre in the State. However, this Ministry is implementing various Textiles sector schemes/programmes and conducts textiles training activities in partnership with training institutes, industry partners, State Governments by providing them necessary support for operational technical requirements. Opening of training centres under various schemes is need based and demand driven. The various Centres in the state of Gujarat where training is provided to textile workers are furnished below:-S. No.	Name of the Centres	Location 1	Weavers Service Centre	Ahmedabad2.	Powerloom Service Centre, 	Surat3.	Powerloom Service Centre, 	Umbergaon4.	 MANTRA– Powerloom Service Centre, 	Pandesara5.	 MANTRA– Powerloom Service Centre, 	Sachin6.	 ATIRA – Powerloom Service Centre, 	Ahmedabad7.	 ATIRA – Powerloom Service Centre, 	Dholka*****
