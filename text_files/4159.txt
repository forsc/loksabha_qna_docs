                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3714






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Achievements of National Livestock Mission




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the details of major achievements of the National Livestock Mission in the country including Maharashtra and Rajasthan; (b) the funds released since 2015-16 in the country including Maharashtra and Rajasthan and the details of total funds spent under NABARD, State-wise and work-wise; and (c) the number of animals insured under Livestock Insurance since 2015-16 and the total amount given to cattle rearers as claim, State-wise and district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN )(a)	The details of major achievements of the National Livestock Mission in the country including Maharashtra and Rajasthan is annexed at Annexure I. (b)	Statewise release of funds under National Livestock Mission including NABARD since 2015-16 is annexed at Annexure II. The funds are released under the scheme to NABARD for implementing the scheme EDEG (Entrepreneurship Development Employment Generation) mainly for the four components viz., Poultry Venture Capital Fund (PVCF), Integrated Development for Small Ruminants and Rabbits (IDSRR), Pig Development and Salvaging & Rearing of Male Buffalo Calves. (c)	The number of animals insured under Livestock Insurance since 2015-16 and the total amount given to cattle rearers as claim, State-wise is annexed at Annexure III & IV. No district wise information is being maintained in this Ministry.
