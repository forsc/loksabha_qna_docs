                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Urea allocation to Telangana




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the request made by the Government of Telangana to the Union Government for allocation of urea to the State for this Kharif season;(b) the steps taken by the Government in this regard;(c) whether any balance of Rabi season is left out for allocation to the State of Telangana; and;(a) if so, the steps taken to release the balance quantity?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b):	Before the commencement of Kharif 2021, the Government of Telangana had requested for allotment of 14 LMT urea to Department of Agriculture, & Farmers Welfare. The same was conveyed to Department of Fertilizers along with month-wise break up. On the basis of month-wise & State-wise projection given by DAC&FW, Department of Fertilizers is allocating sufficient/ adequate quantities of urea to Telangana by issuing monthly supply plan. Further, the availability is being continuously monitored by the Department of Fertilizers through dashboard.(c) & (d): The details of fertilizer availability in the state of Telangana during Rabi 2020-21 is as under:-<Figures in LMT>S.NO	             PRODUCT GROUP	REQUIREMENT	AVAILABILITY1	             UREA	            9.50	11.732	              DAP	            1.20	1.463	              MOP	            1.10	1.804	              NPKS	            5.50	9.11Source: IFMS DASHBOARDIt is clear from the above table that against the actual requirement, more fertilizers were made available to the state of Telangana.                                        **********
