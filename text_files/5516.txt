                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

457






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Construction of ROB




Navneet Ravi Rana














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any proposal under the consideration of the Government to construct Road Over Bridge (ROB) between Amravati-Badnera Chaman Nagar, Panch Bangla; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the time by which the said work is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO.457 BY SHRIMATI NAVNEET RAVI RANA TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 24.07.2019 REGARDING CONSTRUCTION OF ROB(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. The work for construction of 12.0 m wide Road over Bridge (ROB) at Kms 662/20-22 between Badnera-Amravati section, near to existing ROB across Badnera Yard is proposed by State Government on deposit basis. The initial proposal of State Government has been examined and the comments of Railway have been advised to State Government on 17.10.2018. The work can be sanctioned only after the consent of State Government is received. The work of complete ROB is proposed to be done by State Government.*****
