                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

36






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






NCRB Report on Suicides




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware about recent NCRB Report, 11,716 businesspersons died by suicide in 2020, registering a 29% jump from 2019;(b) whether it is true that the majority of businesspersons who committed suicide belonged to the micro small and medium enterprises (MSMEs); and(c) if so, the steps taken by the Government to check this trend?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI AJAY KUMAR MISHRA)(a) to (c):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN THE REPLY TO THE STARRED QUESTION NO. *36 FOR REPLY ON 30 NOVEMBER 2021(a):	The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes statistical data on accidents and suicides in its publication “Accidents and Suicides in India”. As per the published reports, a total of 9052 business persons committed suicide in the year 2019 and a total of 11,716 business persons committed suicides in the year 2020.(b): 	Such an inference may not be possible since the NCRB report does not categorize the suicide data separately for business persons from micro small and medium enterprises (MSMEs). (c)  	Does not arise in view of reply to part (b).*****
