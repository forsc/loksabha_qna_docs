                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

178






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Damage to Corona Vaccine in Cold Storage




Pratima Bhoumik













                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether it has come to the notice of the Government that many doses of Corona vaccine have been found frozen and damaged in cold storage; (b) if so, the details of the vaccine doses damaged, State/UT-wise along with the reasons therefor; (c) the number of Corona warriors/Health workers who have received the Corona Virus Vaccine in the country till date, State/UT-wise; and (d) the details of the funds utilised/spent so far in the first phase of Corona Virus Vaccination Drive, State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA    STARRED QUESTION NO.178* FOR 12TH FEBRUARY, 2021(a) to (d) Yes, the Government is aware that 1000 doses of COVID-19 vaccine, were found frozen at Silchar Medical College Hospital, Assam on 15th January 2021.No other specific incidence of damage to vaccine doses in the cold storages have been brought to the notice of Ministry of Health & Family Welfare so far.Till 11th February 2021, a total of 70.17 lakh Health Care Workers (HCWs) and Front Line Workers (FLWs) have been administered COVID-19 vaccine. The State/UT-wise details of HCWs & FLWs vaccinated is at Annexure-1.Till 8th February, a total of INR 960.75 crore have been utilized for procurement of COVID-19 vaccines. Further, INR 480 crore has been earmarked as operational cost for COVID-19 vaccination drive of which INR 123.49 crore have been disbursed to States/UTs. State/UT-wise details of funds disbursed for COVID-19 vaccination is at Annexure-2.
