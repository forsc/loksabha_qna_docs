                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

202






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Modernisation of Railway Stations




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato













                                                    Dileshwar Kamait
                                                








                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                








                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of railway stations likely to be modernised by the year 2025 in the country;(b) the number of railway stations in North-Eastern States including Assam, West Bengal, Jharkhand, Bihar, Jammu-Kashmir and Madhya Pradesh where modernisation process is going on at present including the details thereof;(c) the number of railway stations of Adra and Ranchi divisions under South Eastern railway likely to be upgraded;(d) the estimated expenditure of modernisation of railway stations under the Railway including North-Eastern Frontier railway, South Eastern railway and East Central railway; (e) the number of railway stations under the railways including North-Eastern Frontier railway, South Eastern railway and East Central railway in which wi-fi facilities have been provided by the year 2021; and (f) the number of railway stations out of the total railway stations equipped with electronic display system?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	to (f):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.******
