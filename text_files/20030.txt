                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1277






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Procurement Centres of Agriculture Crops




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the name of the locations including the number of procurement centres operated to purchase agricultural crops at minimum support price from the farmers in Bihar State, district-wise; (b) 	the total value of crops procured by procurement centres at minimum support price during the last three years district-wise; and (c) 	whether the Government has received any information about partiality and corrupt practices in the procurement centres being operated in the State of Bihar during the last three years and if so and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) and (b): 	Department of Food & Public Distribution (DFPD) extends  price support for procurement of paddy and wheat for central pool which are procured by the Food Corporation of India (FCI) and State Governments/State Government agencies. The paddy and wheat conforming to uniform specifications as prescribed by Government of India are procured at Minimum Support Price (MSP). Bihar is a Decentralized Procurement System (DCP) state, wherein procurement of paddy and wheat are carried out by State Government through its agencies.As informed by the State Government of Bihar, the number of functioning Vyapar Mandal/Primary Agriculture Credit Societies (PACS) for procurement and the value of procured paddy and wheat in Bihar during the last three years are at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively. (c): 	The Government of India, as such, do not have any specific complaint regarding partiality and corruption pertaining to procurement centres functioning in Bihar during the last three years.
