                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4453






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Research Institute in Ayurveda




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to establish national level research institutes in Ayurveda and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to develop the traditional ayurvedic practices of Kerala and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether it has come to the notice of the Government that the Pancha Karma in Ayurveda is good for healthcare and if so, the details of action taken for establish a national level pancha karma research institutes; (d) whether the Government proposes to consider the request for establishing a national level Ayurveda institute in Kollam considering the availability of rarest medicinal plants and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the initiative of the Government to promote the cultivation of medicinal plants?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):     Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) conducts research activities related to Ayurveda through its 30 peripheral institutes/centres. Currently Ministry has no plans to establish National level Research Institute in Ayurveda.(b):      Currently no such proposal is under consideration of the Ministry. (c):      Ministry is not aware of such findings yet.Contd.......-2-(d):      Currently no such proposal is under consideration of this Ministry.(e):    To Promote cultivation of medicinal plants, presently the Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India is implementing a “Medicinal Plants” component of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) throughout the country. The “Medicinal Plants” component is aimed at supporting cultivation of medicinal plants in a mission mode through growers, farmers, cultivators, Growers Associations, Federations, Self Help Groups, Corporates and Growers Co-operatives. Under this scheme financial support is provided for nursery, cultivation, post-harvest management, processing, value addition etc.…………………
