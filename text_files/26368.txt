                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Budget for Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan




Pramila Bisoyi














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the information pertaining to total budget approved under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (SMSA) for the financial years 2020-21 and 2021-22, State-wise;(b) the information on total budget released under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (SMSA) as Central share for the financial years 2020-21 and 2021-22, State-wise;(c) the information on total expenditure reported of the Central Share under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (SMSA) as Central share for the financial year 2020-21, State-wise; and(d) the desegregated data on total unspent balance reported under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (SMSA) for the financial years 2020-21 and 2021-22, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): The statement showing State/UT-wise details of Budget approved, Central Share released, expenditure incurred by States/UTs (including their share) and unspent balance under Samagra Shiksha for the years 2020-21 and 2021-22 is given at Annexure.*****
