                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3309






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Hostels for Eklavya Model Residential School




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor













                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently reviewed the implementation performance of the Eklavya Model Residential School(EMRS) scheme in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the outcome of the said review;(c) the details of opening/ construction of hostels for EMRSs in the country including Gujarat, Rajasthan and Bihar;(d) whether the Government proposes to open EMRS in every educationally backward tribal dominated Tehsil in Gujarat including Dahod district in Rajasthan including Ahore tehsil in Jalore district in Bihar including Araria district; and(e) if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)(a) to (c)  The performance of Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) including quality of education, infrastructure, hygienic environment and other facilities for students in the schools is being regularly monitored by this Ministry.  These schools are envisaged to be brought at par with Navodaya Vidyalayas. National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) as an autonomous body under the Ministry has been set up to provide overall support and policy directions to the schools in order to bring uniformity in the administration of the schools. The State/UT EMRS Societies at the State/UT level have been set up for day-to-day management of the schools.  A total number of 740 schools across the country are scheduled to be sanctioned by the year 2022 out of which 632 EMRSs have been sanctioned till date. There are total 367 functional EMRSs across the country with around 85,232 students currently enrolled.   So far, 195 EMRSs are affiliated with CBSE board and 79 EMRSs have applied for the CBSE affiliation so that uniformity and quality of education in all the EMRSs can be maintained. Collaborations have been established with national apex bodies such as NCERT, NIEPA, NITI Aayog etc so as to organize and conduct professionalcapacity building programs for EMRS teachers and principals.Educational and programmes related activities like collaboration with Atal Innovation Mission for establishment of Atal Tinkering labs, Art of Living Foundation (AOL) for Training on Mental and Physical Well Being of Teachers, Spoken Tutorial, IIT- Bombay for IT Software Training Programme, Indian Association of Physics Teachers (IAPT) for Physics Teacher Training Programme, National Institute for Education, Planning and Administration (NIEPA) for School Leadership Training Programme etc. has also been established. The details of all EMRSs sanctioned and functional in the country including Gujarat, Rajasthan and Bihar as on date is at Annexure-I(d) and (e) Government has decided to sanction Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) in every block with more than 50% ST population and at least 20,000 tribal persons based on Census 2011 data.
