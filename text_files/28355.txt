                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

67






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Preservation and Protection of Cultural Places of Ernakulam




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government intends to preserve and protect the following cultural places Anchalappis oldest post office, Kerala, Synod of Damper (or Udayamperoor Synod ) and Melpazhur Mana birthplace of Adi Shankara, “Melpazhur Mana”, Veliyanadu, District, Ernakulam, Kerala;(b)	if so the, details regarding the same;(c)	whether the Government has any records regarding the protection of these sites and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether the Government is aware of any protection program undertaken at these sites under patronage of any private trusts or State Archaeology department and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	At present there is no proposal for protecting these cultural places by the    Archaeological Survey of India. (b) to (d)  Question does not arise.*****
