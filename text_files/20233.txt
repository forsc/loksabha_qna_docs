                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1676






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Development of NH-744




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps to include Kollam-Thirumangalam National Highway (NH)-744 under Sagarmala project connecting inter-state ports Kollam and Thuthukudy in Kerala and Tamilnadu; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor; (c) whether the Government has planned the development of NH 744 under NHAI;(d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard; (e) whether the Government has taken any steps to construct flyover at Ezhukone to prevent increasing number of accidents; and (f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (f) 	The Stretch of National Highway No. 744 from Kollam to Kerala/Tamil Nadu Border in the State of Kerala and from Kerala/Tamil Nadu Border to Thirumangalam (near Madurai) in the State of Tamil Nadu has been designated as Inter Corridor Route under Bharatmala Pariyoyana. Detailed Project Report for the same has been initiated as well as alignment has also been finalized.  Location of Ezhukone falls in the State of Kerala. As DPR has not been concluded, therefore, details of flyover have not yet been finalised.	*****
