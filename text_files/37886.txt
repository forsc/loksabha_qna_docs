                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4610






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






SFURTI Scheme




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of works done under the Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) in Rajasthan including Banswara-Dungarpur;(b)	the details of the number of clusters formed/developed under the said scheme; and(c)	the number of villagers covered under the said scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA)(a) to (c):	Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), is a scheme to organize artisans of traditional sectors into collectives for producing value added traditional products, thereby providing the artisans with increased and sustainable income. Under the scheme, financial support of up to Rs. 2.5 cr. is given to Regular Clusters with up to 500 artisans, and up to Rs. 5 cr. for Major Clusters with more than 500 artisans. Assistance is provided to the traditional artisans for procurement of new machineries and raw material, construction of Common Facility Centers (CFC), training, exposure visits, design and marketing interventions etc.Since 2014-15 till date, 498 clusters have been approved under SFURTI in the country with GoI assistance of Rs. 129493 lakh benefitting 2,87,159 artisans. Of these, 34 clusters, including 2 clusters in Banswara district, have been approved in the State of Rajasthan with GoI assistance of Rs.9301.40 lakh benefitting 18,249 artisans. Detailed list of 498 approved clusters is enclosed as Annexure.
