                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1805






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Night Flight from KBR Airport, Leh




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to operate civil aircrafts during night time in Kushok Bakula Rimpochee(KBR) Airport, Leh; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): KBR Airport, Leh is a Defence airport, where Airports Authority of India (AAI) maintains a Civil Enclave. It is up to the airline operator to provide air services to a specific place depending upon the traffic demand and commercial viability. AAI has not received any proposal to operate civil aircraft during night time at KBR Airport, Leh by any schedule airline operator.
