                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Passenger Security in Auto/Taxi




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the registration number is to be displayed inside meter box in all auto rickshaws/taxis in National Capital Region, Delhi so that women or other passengers could note down the same easily in case of emergency and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government is contemplating to implement this rule stringently in view of security for passengers in public transport vehicles in other States of the country including Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof ;(c)	whether GPS system has been installed in the wake of security of passengers in public vehicles in Maharashtra ; and(d)	if so, the percentage of auto and taxi drivers who have activated GPS system in their vehicles in Delhi and Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c) 	 Transportation by Road is a State Subject under the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India, therefore, the State Governments are primarily responsible through their law enforcement agencies.(d)	Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has issued notification G.S.R 1095(E) dated 28.11.2016 mandating the provision of  a vehicle location tracking device and one or more emergency button w.e.f, 1st April, 2018 in public service vehicles except two-wheelers , E-rickshaw, three-wheelers and any transport vehicles for which permit is not required under the Motor Vehicle Act, 1988.  The Ministry has notified S.O. 5454(E) dated 25th October, 2018 wherein the provision of a Vehicle Location Tracking Device  and one or more emergency buttons shall be mandated in new public service vehicles except two-wheelers , E-rickshaw, three-wheelers and any transport vehicles for which permit is not required under the Motor Vehicle Act, 1988, registered after 31st December, 2018.  As per the Ministry S.O. 5454(E) , dated 25th October, 2018 for vehicles registered upto 31st December, 2018, the concerned State or Union Territory Government may notify the date for compliance of requirements, in their respective State or Union Territory. ***
