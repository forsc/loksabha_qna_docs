                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3560






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Daily Wages under MGNREGS in Punjab and Rajasthan




Bhagwant Mann














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that according to the “Gazette of India” in the year 2020 and 2021, the daily wages given under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme have reached every person;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor;(c) 	the details of daily wages allotted and spent in Sangrur, Barnala districts of Punjab and Sri Ganganagar and Hanumangarh districts of Rajasthan along with the details of work done so far in the years 2020 and 2021 and its cost, location-wise; and(d) 	whether there is any special provision under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme for farmers having less than 5 bighas of land, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI) (a) & (b):	As per the provision of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), the State Government shall link the wages, without any gender bias, with the quantity of work done. State/UT-wise details of expenditure on wage for unskilled component under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the financial year 2020-21 and 2021-22 (as on 06.08.2021) is given at Annexure.(c):	Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand  driven wage employment programme under which Central funds are released to the States/UTs on the basis of “agreed to” Labour Budget. Wage payment is released into the accounts of beneficiaries directly. Material payment is released to the State Government. No fund is released to District/Gram Panchayat.Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is a demand driven scheme with bottom up planning. The works are identified, approved & prioritized, out of shelf of works by the Gram Panchayat, recommended by Gram Sabha in each States/UTs (including States of Rajasthan and Punjab) as per the provision of the Mahatma Gandhi NREG Act. Accordingly, the works are approved and taken up by Gram Panchayat as per the demand. The details of works undertaken by Gram Panchayat are uploaded / updated in NREGAsoft on regular basis.The details of expenditure on wage for unskilled component in the Sangrur and Barnala Districts
