                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Centralized Data for Artisanal Activities




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has centralized data of all the people who are involved in Artisanal activities; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (c):`	The Government has centralized data of all the registered handicrafts artisans.  In order to link the artisans Identity card number with central data base with Aadhar and Bank account, PAHCHAN has been visualized as new universal identity for artisans to get the benefit of all the schemes implemented by Central /State Government.  The enrolling of handicrafts artisans through the field offices by organizing the camps in the cluster area is a regular process. As on 15th July, 2021, 26.79 lakhs handicrafts artisans have been registered and provided the photo identity card to avail the benefits of the schemes. The state wise data of handicrafts artisans enrolled is annexed as Annexure I.
