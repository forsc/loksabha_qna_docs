                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

339






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






National Young Leaders Programme




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Covid-19 pandemic caused disruption in the activities under the National Young Leaders Programme;(b)  if so, the details thereof; and(c) the measures taken by the Government to keep the development programmes for the youth active during the COVID-19 Pandemic?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a) to (c) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.****
