                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






NGO Active in Consumer Welfare Sector




Dileshwar Kamait













                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any financial assistance is provided to the authorities and Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) active in consumer welfare sector for carrying out various activities relating to consumer welfare; (b)	if so, the district-wise details of financial assistance provided to districts in Bihar, Jharkhand and Jammu and Kashmir, particularly Singhbhum in Jharkhand, Supaul in Bihar and Jammu district in Jammu and Kashmir; (c) 	whether any provisions have been made to monitor the functioning of NGOs receiving the financial assistance; and (d) 	if so, the number of NGOs against which action has been taken in regard to financial irregularities?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a)  	  :	Yes, Sir.  Financial assistance is provided to various organizations including States and Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) active in consumer welfare sector as per the eligibility criteria for the assistance.  (b) 	  :	No assistance is provided to districts directly.  However, details of the financial assistance provided to Bihar, Jharkhand and Jammu & Kashmir are at Annexure.(c) & (d) :	Monitoring of functioning of NGOs receiving financial assistance is done from time to time by the Central Government.ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (b) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2524 FOR 09.03.2021 REGARDING NGO ACTIVE IN CONSUMER WELFARE SECTOR.-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------Financial Assistance provided to NGOs & State Governments in Bihar, Jharkhand and J&K (2012-13 to 2018-19)(Rs. in lakhs)Sl No.	Name of NGOs/ State Government	State	Amount Sanctioned	Activity1	Government of Bihar, Patna	Bihar	750.00	Setting up Consumer Welfare (Corpus) Fund 2	Government of Bihar, Patna	Bihar	66.11	Grants released under the State Consumer Helpline3	Government of Bihar, Patna	Bihar	822.78	Grants released under the Strengthening Consumer Fora4	Govt. of Jharkhand, Ranchi	Jharkhand	750.00	Setting up Consumer Welfare (Corpus) Fund 5	Govt. of Jharkhand, Ranchi	Jharkhand	26.80	Grants released under the State Consumer Helpline6	Govt. of Jharkhand, Ranchi	Jharkhand	195	Grants released under the Strengthening Consumer Fora7	Govt. of Jammu & Kashmir	Jammu & Kashmir	29.88	Grants released under the State Consumer Helpline8	Govt. of Jammu & Kashmir	Jammu & Kashmir	105	Grants released under the Strengthening Consumer Fora9	SAVERA, Patna 	Bihar	98.01	Rs. 31.14 lakh for establishing and managing the Grahak Suvidha Kendra (GSK);
