                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4187






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Suicides by CAPFs Personnel




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Keshari Devi Patel
                                                








                                                    Kanakmal Katara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of suicides by personnel of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF) have been reported during the last ten years, year-wise;(b) whether the Government has set up a taskforce to prevent suicides by CAPF troops in December 2021 and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any reasons have been identified by the Government for the increasing number of suicides and the measures taken by the Government for better prevention and if so, the details thereof;(d) if not, the reasons therefor;(e) whether the taskforce also will consult external mental health professionals on the matter; and(f) if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The total number of suicides by Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs), Assam Rifles (AR) & National Security Guard (NSG) personnel reported during the last 10 years is at Annexure-I.(b):	A Task force was set up in October, 2021 to identify relevant risk factors as well as relevant risk groups and suggest remedial measures for prevention of suicides in CAPFs & Assam Rifles (AR). The composition of ‘Task Force’ was modified in December, 2021.(c)& (d):	Domestic problems, illness and financial problems are some of the contributory factors among others behind the incidents of suicide. Improvement in working conditions of CAPFs, Assam Rifles (AR) & National Security Guard (NSG) is a constant endeavour. The measures undertaken to promote mental health and well-being of Jawans in CAPFs, Assam Rifles (AR) & National Security Guard (NSG) are given in Annexure-II. (e)&(f):	As per its Terms of Reference (ToR), the Task Force may associate experts from organizations such as National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences (NIMHANS).*****
