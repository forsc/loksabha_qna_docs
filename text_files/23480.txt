                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4500






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






PMGSY in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of colonies connected so far under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojna(PMGSY) in the country, alongwith the district-wise details in respect of Odisha; and(b) 	the number of roads identified under PMGSY during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	Pradhan Mantri Gram SadakYojana (PMGSY) is a one-time special intervention of Government of India to provide rural connectivity, by way of a single all-weather road, to the eligible unconnected habitations in the core network. The unit for PMGSY is a habitations and not a revenue village or a Panchayat.The number of habitations connected so far under PMGSY (250+ & 500+ population habitations) in the country as on 17th March, 2021 is as under: -Total Eligible     Total Connected     Total Habitations (Nos)    Total Connected Habitations (Nos)Unconnected         habitations        sanctioned for              under PMGSYHabitations (Nos)   through State      connectivity under	                     Schemes	       PMGSY 	1,78,184	     16,086	        1,57,398	             1,54,670The district-wise details in respect of Odisha are given at Annexure.(b)	The Status of projects under various verticals of PMGSY for all India and for the State of Odisha as on 17th March, 2021 are as under: -All India
