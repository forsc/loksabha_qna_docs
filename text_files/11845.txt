                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

863






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Investments under PMAY




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total amount of investments made under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) in the country especially in Maharashtra till date and the number of houses constructed along with the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has allowed additional investment for the construction of affordable houses for the poor people in urban areas under the PMAY during the first four months of the current financial year and if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of the poor in the urban areas who are likely to get benefited from it and the names of such States which are likely to receive funds;(d) whether the Government has granted permission for the construction of these houses under three components of the Urban Housing Mission and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of the other steps taken to provide affordable houses to all the urban poor in a time bound manner throughout the country ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) The details are at Annexure – I. (b)&(c) Yes Sir. The State/UT-wise details are at Annexure – II.(d) Yes Sir. Sanctions for construction of houses have been given under four components of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban). Details are at Annexure – II.(e) To provide affordable housing to all eligible urban poor in a time bound manner, Government, under its Technology sub-Mission, has launched  Global Housing Technology Challenge India (GHTC – India) to short list alternate and innovative technologies to fast track construction of houses. Use of Space technologies for Geo-tagging and Geo-fencing is being encouraged to monitor various stages of construction of houses. Central Sanction and Monitoring Committee (CSMC) also reviews the progress of the Mission regularly in its meetings. In addition, Ministry also monitors progress of the mission through periodic review meetings, video conferences and field visits. States/ UTs are required to transfer funds directly to beneficiaries in DBT mode to enable time bound completion of houses under Beneficiary Led Construction (BLC) component.                                               *****
