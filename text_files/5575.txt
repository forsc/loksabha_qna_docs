                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5045






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Communication System in Bihar




Pashupati Kumar Paras














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has made any assessment regarding the communication system/network in few districts of Bihar and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the BSNL is providing satisfactory services in all the rural areas of the State and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the tele-communication services have been provided in all the rural areas of Darbhanga and Hajipur districts; (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	Quality of Service is monitored by Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) & the performance of Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) is assessed against the benchmark for various Quality of Service parameters laid down by TRAI by way of Quality of Service regulations issued from time to time, through Quarterly Performance Monitoring Reports (PMRs). The performance is assessed for the service area as a whole which includes rural, hilly/remote and tribal areas as well. As per the PMR released by TRAI, the telecom services (including mobile and internet services) are by and large satisfactory.As per latest report of TRAI for the quarter ending  March 2019, all TSPs are  complying with the TRAI’s benchmarks in Bihar LSA despite rapid increase in traffic volume and more stringent benchmarks as detailed at Annexure-I.(b )	BSNL is providing satisfactory telecom services in 29064 villages by mobile services. Quality of Service is monitored by Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) & the performance of Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) is assessed against the benchmark for various Quality of Service parameters laid down by TRAI by way of Quality of Service regulations issued from time to time, through Quarterly Performance Monitoring Reports (PMRs). The performance is assessed for the License Service Area (LSA) as a whole. As per latest PMR released by TRAI for the Quarter ending march 2019, telecom services provided by BSNL are by and large satisfactory. (c) to (e)  	Yes, as per the report of the TSPs, tele-communication services have been provided in all the villages of Darbhanga and Hajipur districts.BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country, including in all districts of Bihar. Under this Project, 6881 GPs out of around 8446 GPs in Bihar state have been connected. 6216 GPs have been made Service Ready as on 04.07.2019.The status of BharatNet project in Darbhanga and Hajipur districts of Bihar is as follows:<pre>S. No.	Name of District	Total GPs	OFC laid GPs	Service Ready GPs1	Darbhanga	336	323	2212	Hajipur	305	285	282</pre>Moreover, the Government has taken several policy initiatives to facilitate infrastructure growth for delivery of quality telecom services. These include permitting trading/sharing/ liberalization of spectrum, permitting passive & active infrastructure sharing, notification of Right of Way Rules 2016, making available government land/buildings for installations of towers etc.
