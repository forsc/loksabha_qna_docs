                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4701






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Maintenance of various Forts in Allahabad




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether steps have been taken/proposed to be taken by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) for development and maintenance of Akbar Fort, Khusro Bagh, Garhwa Fort situated in Shankargarh and Shringverpur in Prayagraj (Allahabad) district of Uttar Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of the progress made in this regard till date;(d)	whether the Government has any action plan to attract international and domestic tourists at the above mentioned places; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c)  	Yes Sir. Out of these monuments, Khusro Bagh and Garhwa Fort in Prayagraj district are protected monuments of Archaeological Survey of India (ASI).  The conservation work on them is attended regularly as per the requirements and availability of resources and they are in a good state of preservation.(d) & (e) 	These monuments are maintained in presentable condition.  In addition their historic and architectural importance  acts as attraction for international and domestic tourists.
