                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

271






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Incentives under FAME India Scheme




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had invited Expression of Interest (EoI) for submission of proposal for availing incentives under Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of Hybrid and Electric Vehicles in India (FAME India) Scheme, Phase-II for development of Electric Vehicle charging infrastructure within cities;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the proposals considered and approved by the Government; and(d) the norms that were followed by the Government in the rejection of the proposals?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                     (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (d): Yes, Sir.  Department of Heavy Industry issued an Expression of Interest (EoI) inviting Proposals from Urban Local Bodies (ULBs)/municipal corporations, PSUs (State/Central)/ Govt. DISCOMS and similar other public/private entities for development of Electric Vehicles (EVs) charging infrastructure in different states/cities across the country for availing incentives under Fame India Scheme Phase-II for deployment of EV charging infrastructure.       In response thereof, about 106 proposals from public/private entities for the deployment of about 7,000 EV charging stations were received. After evaluation of these proposals as per EoI, on the advice of Project Implementation and Sanctioning Committee (PISC) the Government sanctioned 2,636 charging stations to 62 cities submitted by 19 public entities for 24 states in the first instance which are aligned with EoI guidelines and guidelines issued by Ministry of Power.                                           *****
