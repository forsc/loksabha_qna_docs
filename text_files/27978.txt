                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3473






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






PMGKAY in Andhra Pradesh




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any amount due from the Government of India to Andhra Pradesh regarding rice subsidy, if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether there is any amount pending from the Government of India to Andhra Pradesh regarding Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY); (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the Government timeline to transfer these dues?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d):  Admissible amount of Provisional/Advance food subsidy is released to Decentralized Procurement (DCP) States such as Andhra Pradesh, based on their quarter-wise claims.An admissible amount of Rs. 8424.72 Crore and Rs. 3303.39 Crore has already been released in FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 (till July’21) respectively to Andhra Pradesh as provisional and advance food subsidy including their claims of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY), details of which are annexed.**** AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3473 FOR ANSWER ON 10.8.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.Subsidy released during Financial Year 2020-21 (including PMGKAY)Sl.
