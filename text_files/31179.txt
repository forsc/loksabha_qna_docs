                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2827






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Maintenance of Stations by Private Contractors




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Konkan Railways has decided to award the work relating to the maintenance of sixty nine (69) railways stations to private contractors;(b)	 if so, the details of the entire process in this regard; and(c)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): There are 72 stations on Konkan Railway including 6 Halt Stations. The work related to housekeeping / upkeep of stations is being carried out through station housekeeping staff as well as private contractor.(b): The work is awarded through open tender on Indian Railway Engineering Procurement System (IREPS)/ Government e-Marketplace (GeM) tendering portal.(c): Does not arise.****
