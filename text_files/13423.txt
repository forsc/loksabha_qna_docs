                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2272






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Communication Facility in Remote Areas




Ajay (Teni) Misra













                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has fixed any target to provide communication facilities to remote areas of the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard along with the time by which the above target is likely to be achieved; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) to (c) 	The National Digital Communications Policy – 2018 (NDCP-2018) which was notified on 22.10.2018 inter-alia envisages for ensuring connectivity for all uncovered areas by 2022. The connectivity to these uncovered areas is being provided by the Government and the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in a phased manner.************
