                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4476






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Rail Coach Restaurant




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a rail coach restaurant has been set up in the circulating area of main station under West Central Railway zone in Jabalpur;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the general public has also been extended the facility along with the passengers;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the food items are made available at economical rates and if so, the details thereof;(f) whether starting of such restaurants at other stations of Jabalpur is also proposed; and(g) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. It is located in the circulating area (on the side of platform no. 06).(c) & (d) Yes, Sir. Since it is in the circulating area, general public may also avail its services.(e) Rates are being decided by the licensee, as Railway-approved rates are not applicable for the rail coach restaurant. However, MRP is clearly mentioned in the catalogue displaying prices.(f) & (g) Yes, Sir. At Madan Mahal railway station of Jabalpur, a contract has been awarded to start a rail coach restaurant.*****
