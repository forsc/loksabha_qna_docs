                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5212






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Electronic Park in Telangana




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has sanctioned electronics hardware park/manufacturing centre or units in Warangal, Karimnagar and Hyderabad in Telangana; and (b)	if so, the details thereof and progress made by the Government in this regard ?					






ANSWER




                                                        (a)   and (b):  Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India has accorded approval to M/s Telangana State Industrial Infrastructure Corporation Ltd. (TSIIC) for setting up of  two (02) Greenfield Electronics Manufacturing Clusters  (EMCs)  at e-city Hyderabad and Maheshwaram in the state of Telengana. These Electronics Manufacturing Clusters are being developed over an area of 912.24 acres   with a project cost of Rs 1104.85 crore including a Grant-in-aid of Rs 391.03 crore from Government of India.  Till now Grant-in-aid of Rs 78.20 crore has been released to these projects.
