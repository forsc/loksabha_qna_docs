                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

561






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Setting up of Container Yard




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether the Government has received any representation for setting up of empty containers yard and servicing centre (Not ICD/CFS) at Morbi by private players; (b)  	if so, the details of the criteria laid down for setting up of such empty containers yard and servicing centre (Not ICD/CFS) at Morbi; (c)   	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	whether any action has been taken by the Government in this regards and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) 	Yes Sir. Ministry of Port, Shipping & Waterways has received a representation on setting up of empty container yard and servicing centre at Morbi, Gujarat. (b), (c) & (d) The matter has been considered in consultation with Logistic Division, Ministry of Commerce, who has communicated that they have not been mandated for laying down criteria for setting up of empty container yard and service center. However, they have opined that such facilities will lead to optimal use of stock and overall reduction in logistics cost.                                             ******
