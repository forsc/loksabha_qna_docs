UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3480






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






National Mission on Libraries




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of public libraries selected and those completely upgraded under the National Mission on Libraries, State-wise;(b)	the reason for low spending of the amount allocated for Development of Libraries and Archives during 2019-20;(c)	the reason for low payment release of the sanctioned amount to the two libraries in Rajasthan under the National Mission Libraries-Model Library Scheme; and(d)	the status of upgradation of the public libraries selected under the above-mentioned scheme in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Ministry of Culture & Tourism(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	As per approved guidelines of National Mission on Libraries (NML) Model Library Scheme, financial assistance is provided to one State Central Library and one District Library in each State / UT for infrastructural development, modernization etc., as recommended by the State / UT Library Authorities.State wise number of proposals approved under the NML Model Library Scheme and status are provided at Annexure – A.(b)	Sanction of proposal under the NML Model Library Scheme depends on submission of proposal(s) from the respective State/UT Library Authorities and release of payment depends on submission of certified claims, other requisite documents and state contribution from the respective State/UT Library Authorities.Implementation and execution of the approved projects are directly controlled by the respective Library Authorities.Thus, National Mission on Libraries (NML) is dependent on State/UT Library Authority for sanctioning of proposal and release of payment under the NML Model Library Scheme.(c)	Implementation and execution of the approved projects are directly controlled by the Library Authority of Rajasthan. Release of payment depends on submission of certified claims and other requisite documents from the Library Authority of Rajasthan.State Central Library Authority, Jaipur and Kota District Library have been disbursed financial assistance based on fund utilization and further claims received. Fund sanctioned and released status is provided at Annexure-A.(d)	Please refer Sl.
