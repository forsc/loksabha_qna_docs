                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Beneficiaries of PMUY




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Vishnu Dayal Ram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of State-wise amount spent by the Government especially in Maharashtra under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) since the launch of this scheme;(b) the district-wise number of LPG connections given under PMUY including Latur district in Maharashtra and Jalore and Sirohi districts in Rajasthan;(c) whether beneficiaries under the said scheme are unable to refill the cylinders as they belong to BPL category;(d) if so, whether the Government proposes to refill the 5 kg LPG cylinder either free or on instalments under the said scheme; and(e) the details of the steps taken by the Government to educate the LPG consumers for complying with safety norms?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) So far, the Government has reimbursed ? 10,675 crore to Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) in lieu of LPG connections released by them across the country including in the State of Maharashtra under PMUY. The total subsidy on domestic LPG including PMUY beneficiary provided by the Government in 2018-19 is ? 37,220 crore including the LPG customers of Maharashtra.(b) OMCs have released 8 crore LPG connections including 44.36 lakh in Maharashtra and 63.91 lakh in Rajasthan under PMUY. The district-wise details of LPG connections released under PMUY in Latur district of Maharashtra and in Jalore and Sirohi districts of Rajasthan are as under :Latur district, MaharashtraJalore district, RajasthanSirohi district, Rajasthan1.29 lakh1. 89 lakh1.17 lakh(c) & (d) As on date, about 87 % of PMUY beneficiaries have returned for at least second refill. In order to improve the refill consumption, PMUY beneficiaries have been offered swap facility of 5 kg cylinder against 14.2 kg cylinder as per their requirement.(e) OMCs undertake various awareness programmes to promote awareness on safe usage of LPG, viz. engaging mass communication channels such as audio visual and print media, banners/hoardings, leaflets, pamphlets, conducting LPG Panchayat/Safety Clinics, regular customer contact by OMCs/distributors, etc.---------
