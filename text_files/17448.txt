                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






PMKVY in Jharkhand




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the details of the numbers of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras in the State of Jharkhand, district-wise;(b)       the details of such Kendras functioning at present in the districts of Kodarma and Giridih; and(c)        the details of the number of trained students who have been provided employment?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)        Twenty Four Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras (PMKK) have been established In the State of Jharkhand, with one PMKK in each district. (b)       PMKKs in Kodarma and Giridih districts of State of Jharkhand have been established and operationalised by JITM Skills Private Limited and Surya Wires Private Limited, respectively. The  details of candidate trained and job roles offered   in these PMKKs so far are as follows: PMKK	Candidates Trained	Job RolesKodarma	1320	Sewing Machine Operator – knits, Emergency Medical Technician-Basic, Home Health Aide and Front Line Health WorkerGiridih	201	Mobile Phone Hardware Repair Technician, Baking Technician/Operative, Retail Sales Associate, Solar PV Installer (Suryamitra) and Customer Care Executive - (Telecom Call Centre) (c)        Under PMKVY (2016-20), as on 17.03.2020, 63,119 candidates have been trained and 48,557 candidates certified through empanelled training centres, including PMKKs, in the State of Jharkhand. Out of these certified candidates, 22,642 candidates have been provided with placement. Also, 80,463 candidates have been oriented under recognition of prior learning component of PMKVY (2016-20).
