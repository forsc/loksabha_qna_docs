                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

88






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Development of Tourist Places of Bundelkhand and Uttrakhand




Pushpendra Singh Chandel













                                                    Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the number of persons employed in tourism sector in Bundelkhand and Uttarakhand; (b) 	the details of efforts made by the Government for the development of tourism in Bundelkhand and Uttarakhand to provide the world class facilities to the tourists; (c) 	whether the Government has made special plan to publicize tourist places of Bundelkhand and Uttarakhand; and (d) 	it so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Ministry of Tourism, Government of India does not maintain region wise data on number of persons employed in tourism sector. However, Ministry of tourism prepares Tourism Satellite Account (TSA) of India and Regional TSA (R-TSA) for all States/UTs in conceptual compliance with the recommended methodology of the United Nations World Tourism Organization (UNWTO) with an approximate gap of 5 years. As per the Regional TSA for reference year 2015-16,  for  Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh and Uttarakhand, total jobs in  tourism characteristic industries are 34.90 lakhs, 10.05 lakh and 3.84 lakh respectively. (b):	Development of tourism and employment generation through it, is primarily the responsibility of the concerned State Government/Union Territory Administration. However, Ministry of Tourism, Government of India under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies, provides central financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territories/Central Agencies for development of tourism related infrastructure and facilities. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with State Governments/UT Administration and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.Details of projects under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD in Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand are at annexure .(c)&(d):  Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a  holistic destination  and as part of its on-going activities to promote various tourism destinations and products in the different States and Union Territories of the country including those in Uttrakhand and bundelkhand. In addition to that, Promotions are also undertaken through the website and Social Media accounts of the Ministry. As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country. Promotions are also undertaken through the website and social media accounts of the Ministry. In addition, the India tourism offices in India and overseas disseminate information and undertake various promotional activities with the objective of showcasing the varied tourist destinations and products of the country                                       ********
