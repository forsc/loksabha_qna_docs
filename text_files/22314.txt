                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Funds for FPI Scheme




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Balak Nath
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has reappropriated the funds for the FPI schemes/programmes during the years 2019-2020 and 2020-2021; (b)	if so, the scheme-wise/programme-wise details thereof alongwith the reasons therefor; (c)	whether the FPI Ministry was in non-obligatory category in regard to earmaking funds for Scheduled Caste Sub Plan (SCSP) and Tribal Sub Plan (TSP) till the years 2017-18; (d)	the year-wise details of total amount of budget allocated for the said components/schemes during the years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21; and (e)	the total amount of funds spent under the said plans in Pindwada of Sirohi, Satara, Solapur districts, Chatra and Latehar districts of Jharkhand, Alwar and Sikar districts and north eastern States including Tripura? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a)  & (b): Yes Sir. The details of re-appropriation of funds done for Schemes under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 is at Annexure-I. (c) to (e): The MoFPI was in non-obligatory category till 2017-18 for earmarking funds for Scheduled Caste Sub Plan (SCSP) and Tribal Sub Plan (TSP). The Ministry earmarked funds @ 4.3 % of BE each for SCSP & TSP Components in the Budget of 2018-19. For the year, 2019-20 and 2020-21, the Ministry has earmarked @ 8.3% for SCSP and @ 4.3% for TSP of the allocated funds under the schemes of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) being implemented as a Central Sector Scheme. A Statement indicating year-wise allocation and utilization thereof is at Annexure-II. The Schemes of PMKSY are demand driven. No State-wise or district wise fund allocation is made for any of the schemes under the Umbrella Scheme of PMKSY. However, a new scheme namely; Prime Minister Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme has been launched in June, 2020 for implementation as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under which for the year 2020-21 the following funds have been allocated under SCSP & TSP including provision for release to State/UTs:  (Rs.in Crore)Component of PMFME	BE 2020-21	RE 2020-21SCSP	0.00	33.20TSP	0.00	17.20 *****
