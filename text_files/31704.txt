                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3191






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Ease of Doing Business Programme




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether  the Government has taken any steps for creating/providing favorable conditions for the development and promotion of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) under the Ease of Doing Business Programme;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES    (SHRI  NARAYAN RANE )(a) & (b): 	The Government has taken several steps to create a conducive environment for the promotion and development of MSMEs and under Ease of Doing Business. Some of these are given below:(i) 	Udyam Registration (UR) Portal to provide fully online, paperless and transparent MSME registration process. No documents or proof are required to be uploaded for registering an MSME. Only Aadhaar Number and PAN is enough for registration. PAN and GST linked details on investment and turnover of enterprises are taken automatically from Government databases. (ii) 	Digital Payments to pass on the benefits of the schemes of Ministry of MSME through digital payment gateway.(iii) MSME SAMBANDH Portal- helps in monitoring the implementation of Public Procurement Policy for micro and small enterprises. (iv) MSME SAMADHAAN Portal- helps in empowering micro and small entrepreneurs across the country to register their cases relating to delayed payments.(v) 	MSME SAMPARK Portal- A digital platform, wherein jobseekers (pass out trainees / students of MSME Technology Centres) and recruiters get connected.(vi) Champions Portal for speedy redressal of grievances. (vii)Under Public Procurement Policy for MSEs order, 2012, the number of regulatory compliances in regard to Government to Business and Government to Citizen, has been reduced from 7 to 4. (viii) Government e-Marketplace (GeM)- facilitates online procurement of common use Goods & Services required by various Government Departments/ Organizations/ CPSEs.(c):		Does not arise.
