                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3588






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






MoU between RLDA and DRP




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed by Railway Land Development Authority (RLDA) and Dharavi Redevelopment Project (DRP/SRA) to transfer 45 acres of Railway Land for Dharavi Redevelopment Project;(b)	if so, the  details thereof and whether DRP/SRA has provided any amount to RLDA according to MoU and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the latest position of the said proposal and the time by which it is likely to be approved;	(d)	whether the operational land can be transferred within 30 days of signing of MoU and the reasons for 45 acres of land not being transferred to DRP/SRA despite above payment and the subsequent action thereon; and(e)	the time by which the open land and operational land will be given to DRP/SRA?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b):  Yes, Sir. A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed on 03.03.2019 between Rail Land Development Authority (RLDA) and Dharavi Redevelopment Project/Slum Rehabilitation Authority (DRP/SRA) (setup by Govt. of Maharashtra) for joint redevelopment of land parcels at Matunga.  Under this MoU, DRP/SRA is required to redevelop Railway Quarters and Service Buildings etc. at Matunga integrated with redevelopment of Dharavi area on a pre- defined revenue sharing basis between Government of Maharashtra (DRP/SRA) and Indian Railways (RLDA). Further, DRP/SRA has provided `800 Cr. as 1st installment of upfront payment.(c) to (e):   In terms of MoU, handing over of Railway’s  land parcel at Matunga is to be facilitated on transfer of `800 Cr by DRP/SRA and signing of Definitive Agreement between DRP/SRA and RLDA. The Definitive Agreement as prepared by RLDA has been shared with DRP-SRA for finalization.  Railway land at Matunga will be handed over after signing of Definitive Agreement between RLDA and DRP/SRA.	*****
