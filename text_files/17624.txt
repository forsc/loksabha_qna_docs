                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Workers for Construction Industries




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether some foreign countries have shown interest in calling workers from India for their construction industries;(b)if so, the details of such countries and steps proposed to be taken to send skilled/unskilled labourers from our country; (c)the details of the existing policy of the Government in this regard and current system of sending workers to the international market;(d)the state-wise number of registered agencies which send workers abroad;(e)the state-wise number of people sent abroad annually by such agencies during the last three years along with the details of trades/skills in demand in international market; and(f)the details of cases, where these agencies have exploited such workers?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):  The foreign employers having the demand of workers recruit the workers and the recruitment for the demand  is a matter between foreign employer and the recruitment agent. Indian nationals holding Emigration Check Required (ECR) category passports and immigrating for employment purpose to any notified ECR country take Emigration clearance through Emigrate system of Ministry of External Affairs. The e-Migrate system provides the Government a comprehensive and online database of Emigrant workers, Missions, Recruiting Agents, Foreign Employers, and Insurance Agencies. E-Migrate is aimed at making the emigration process faster, transparent and allows online authentication/verification of credentials of all stakeholders. In addition, the data in e-Migrate also includes those persons going for employment under Emigration Check Not Required (ECNR) category if they voluntarily register in the system. (d):        State-wise number of registered agencies is enclosed herewith at Annexure-“A”.(e):     The state-wise number of people sent abroad and details of trades/skills during the last three years are enclosed herewith at Annexure-“B” and Annexure-“C” respectively. 	(f):          The year-wise complaints against these agencies during the last three years are enclosed herewith at Annexure “D”.****                                                      ******
