                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

240






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Scrap Fee Subsidy in Educational Institutions




Kuldeep Rai Sharma













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to scrap fee subsidy in top educationalinstitutions like Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and if so, the details thereof and thereasons therefor;(b) whether the Government has worked out any mechanism to help weaker sectionstudents who would be affected as a result thereof, to avail subsidy for study in topeducational institutions;(c) whether the Government is also planning to set up a national crowdfunding platformand if so, the details thereof and the objective behind the move;(d) whether the Government has also launched nationwide movement called Each One,Teach One urging families to support the educational needs of at least one student;(e) if so, the number of students who got benefited out of this national movement; and(f) the other steps taken by the Government to make this movement successful one andachieve the objective for which it was set up?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’)(a): No Sir.(b): Does not arise.(c) : No Sir, the Government is not planning to set up a national crowd funding platform.(d); No Sir, the Government has not launched nationwide movement called Each One,Teach One. However, Government has launched a number of schemes for education for alllike SWAYAM PRABHA-group of 32 DTH channel devoted to telecasting of high educationalprograms 24x7, SWAYAM-to provide best teaching learning resources to all including themost disadvantaged, Samagra Shiksha- for improving school effectiveness measured interms of equal opportunities for schooling and equitable learning resources.(e) & (f): Does not arise.*******
