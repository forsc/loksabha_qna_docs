                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1015






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Development of Temples in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the pilgrimages like temples and ancient monuments being developed in Kalahandi and Noapada districts in Odisha;(b)	whether steps have been taken for the beautification of such temples and monuments;(c)	if so, whether the Odisha unit of Archaeological survey of India (ASI) has made any recommendations in this regard;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the funds allocated for beautification and development of such temples and monuments?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Conservation, preservation and environmental development work of centrally protected monuments and temples in the country including one monument in Kalahandi and Noapada districts in Odisha is a continuous process and same is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) as per the availability of resources.(b)	Yes Sir. In addition to conservation of monuments the development works are attended keeping regard to the archaeological norms and principles and Conservation Policy of ASI, 2014, with a thrust on retaining originality, authenticity & integrity of protected monuments. (c) & (d)	ASI Odisha Unit submits Annual Conservation Programme following conservation guidelines, archaeological norms & principles and Conservation Policy of ASI which is implemented after due scrutiny.(e)	The funds allocated for conservation, preservation & environmental development work of protected monuments of ASI in Odisha, for current financial year 2020-21, is Rs. 363 lakh.
