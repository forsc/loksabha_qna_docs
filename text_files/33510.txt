                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

932






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Increase in Milk Production




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Rodmal Nagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantum of increase in milk production every year alongwith the details of milk production during the current year and the last six year, State-wise; (b)	 the position of India in milk production in the world; and(c)	the steps being taken by the Government to increase milk production and details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a): The details of milk production along with quantum of increase for the country during last six years are given as under: Year	Milk Production(In Million Tonnes)	Percentage increase (%)2015-16	155.5	6.27 %2016-17	165.4	6.38 %2017-18	176.3	6.62 %2018-19	187.7	6.47 %2019-20	198.4	5.69 %2020-21*	210.0	5.81 %Note:  “*”- ProvisionalState-wise details of milk production and quantum of increase for last six years are placed at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.(b):  India is at the First position in the world in milk production. (Source: Economic Survey report 2021-22 and Food and Agriculture Organization-STAT)(c): In order to enhance milk production in the country the Government of India has been implementing following major Central Sector and Centrally Sponsored schemes: i.	Rashtriya Gokul mission: This scheme has been implementing since 2014 with the aim of development and conservation of indigenous breeds, genetic upgradation of bovine population and enhancement of milk production and productivity of bovines thereby making milk production more remunerative to farmers engaged in dairying.ii.	National Programme for Dairy Development: It has aim to enhance quality of milk and milk products and increase share of organized milk procurement.iii.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund.iv.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations: This scheme is engaged in dairy activities and this scheme was approved to provide working capital loan to State Cooperatives and Federations.
