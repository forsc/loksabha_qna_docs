                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Health Centres in Assam




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of healthcare centres functional in Assam specially in Dhubri and Goalpara districts;(b) the number of them sponsored by Union Government and the State Government, seperately;(c) the details of staff, doctors and nurses posted at all the healthcare centres, district-wise in Assam;(d) whether the Government has any plan to set up more healthcare centres in Assam particularly in Dhubri and Goalpara districts; (e) if so, the detail thereof; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):    As  reported by the State Government,  total number of functional healthcare centres   in Assam is 5935, out of which the number of functional healthcare centres in Dhubri District are 221  and  in Goalpara District are 202.(b):    Public Health being a State subject, the primary responsibility for setting up the healthcare centres lies with the State Governments. However, under  National Health Mission (NHM), Central Government provides financial  & technical support to States/UTs to strengthen their  healthcare facilities based on the requirements posed by the States/UTs in their  Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs). (c):    As informed by  the State Government, details  of staff, doctors and nurses posted at all the healthcare centres, are  enclosed  at Annexure –I.(d) & (f):     Public Health being a State subject, the primary responsibility for setting up the healthcare centres lies with the State Governments. However, under National Health Mission (NHM), Central Government provides financial  & technical support to States/UTs to strengthen their  healthcare facilities based on the requirements posed by the States/UTs in their  Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs).
