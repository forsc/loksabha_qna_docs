                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2574






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Corporate Social Responsibility




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number and names of the subsidiary companies under the Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) of Railways; (b)	the action plan under the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) adhered to by these PSUs;  (c)	the present status of the implementation of the works under CSR in the country, State and location-wise; and (d)	whether any project has been undertaken with the collaboration of any Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) or trust under the CSR and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION    (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	There are 14 Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) under the Ministry of Railways, and their names along with their subsidiaries are appended as Appendix – I. (b) & (c): CPSEs have their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) & Sustainability Policy under the frame work Section 135 of the Companies Act, 2013 and the guidelines of Department of Public Enterprises. The policy covers various items of the Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013, including items like ‘Rural Development’, ‘Education’, Skill Development’, ‘Health & Sanitation’, ‘Environment Sustainability’, ‘Heritage Protection’ etc. Details of CSR works approved for implementation by CPSEs of Ministry of Railways is appended as Appendix –II.(d): Works undertaken with the collaboration of Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) or Trust under CSR are appended as Appendix-III.*****
