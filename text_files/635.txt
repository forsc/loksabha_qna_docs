                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

66






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Missing of AN-32 Aircraft




Asaduddin Owaisi













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that AN-32 aircraft went missing recently in Arunachal sector and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the number of persons including crew members on board in the aircraft;(b) 	whether the said aircraft has been traced and if so, the details thereof along with the details of special squads deployed/assigned for rescue and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) 	whether it is a fact that AN-32 series of aircrafts are meeting with accidents frequently and if so, the details thereof along with the accidents occurred during the last four years;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to upgrade or phase out these aircrafts in a time-bound manner;(e) 	if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard; and(f) the number of aircrafts/choppers of Indian Air Force collapsed during the last four years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF DEFENCE                                    (SHRI RAJNATH SINGH) (a) to (f):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*******
