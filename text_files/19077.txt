                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

481






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Affordable Housing




Pushpendra Singh Chandel













                                                    Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the details of the scheme for providing affordable houses to persons belonging to economically weaker sections of the society; and (b) 	the details of the beneficiaries, State-wise including Uttarakhand and Bundelkhand regions? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                            (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):  Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) has been implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) since 25.06.2015 for providing assistance to States/ Union Territories (UTs) for addressing the housing requirement of the people belonging to Economically Weaker Section (EWS), Low Income Group (LIG) and Middle Income Group (MIG) categories in urban areas through following four verticals: S. No.	Vertical	          Carpet Area of the house	Central Assistance per house1.	In-situ Slum              Upto 30 square meter (sqm)	Rs. 1.00 lakh         Redevelopment (ISSR)	2.	Credit Linked Subsidy     Upto 30 sqm for EWS(annual household income up         Scheme (CLSS)	Upto 30 sqm for EWS(annual household income up to ?3,00,000), 60 sqm for LIG(annual household income from?3,00,001 to ?6,00,000),160 sqm for MIG-I(annual household income from ?6,00,001 to ?12,00,000), and 200 sqm for MIG-II (annual household income from ?12,00,001 to ?18,00,000).	Interest subsidy of 6.5 %, 4% and 3% on loan amounts upto Rs. 6 lakh, Rs. 9 lakh and Rs. 12 lakh for beneficiaries belonging to EWS/LIG, MIG-I and MIG-II categories respectively.3.	Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP)	Upto 30 sqm	Rs. 1.50 lakh4.	Beneficiary-led individual house construction/ enhancement (BLC)	Upto 30 sqm	Rs. 1.50 lakhThe scheme covers the entire urban area consisting of Statutory Towns, Notified Planning Areas, Development Authorities, Special Area Development Authorities, Industrial Development Authorities or any such authority under State legislation which is entrusted with the functions of urban planning & regulations. All houses constructed under PMAY-U have basic amenities like water supply, electricity, kitchen and toilet. The Mission promotes women empowerment by providing the ownership of houses in name of female member or in joint name. Preference is also given to differently abled persons, senior citizens, Scheduled Castes (SCs), Scheduled Tribes (STs), Other Backward Classes (OBCs), Minority, single women, trans gender and other weaker & vulnerable sections of the society.Based on the project proposals received so far from the States/ UTs under the Scheme, a total 110 lakh houses have been sanctioned; out of this, 71.4 lakh are at various stages of construction and 41.8 lakh have been completed/ delivered. (b):  Out of total sanctioned 109,82,808 houses; 98,85,386 houses have been sanctioned for EWS, 6,04,826for LIG and 4,92,596 for MIG category beneficiaries across the country. These include 40,821 houses in the State of Uttarakhand and 2,27,227 in the Bundelkhand region (Banda, Chitrakoot, Hamirpur, Jalaun, Jhansi, Lalitpur, and Mahoba districts of Uttar Pradesh and Chhatarpur, Damoh, Datia, Panna, Sagar and Tikamgarh districts of Madhya Pradesh).
