                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Objectives of DAY-NULM




Sumalatha Ambareesh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Deendayal Antodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) is introduced to narrow the gap of inequality between rich and poor;(b)   if so, the details thereof including the activities taken up under the DAY-NULM to achieve the target;(c)  the number of people benefitted under the said scheme during the last three years, year / State-wise; and(d)  whether the Government is contemplating to make NULM more effective to reach the benefits of development to the poorest of the poor and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) & (b)	The Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is administering DAY-NULM w.e.f. September, 2013, to reduce poverty and vulnerability of urban poor households by enabling them to access gainful self-employment and skilled wage employment opportunities, for improvement in their livelihoods on a sustainable basis. The mission also aims at building strong grass root level institutions of the urban poor, providing shelters equipped with essential services to the urban homeless persons and addressing livelihood concerns of the urban street vendors by facilitating access to suitable spaces, institutional credit, social security, etc.(c)         A statement showing the State-wise physical progress under the DAY-NULM Scheme during the last three years i.e. 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 is attached. (d)      	The Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs regularly reviews the Mission implementation in States/ UTs through meetings, video conferences, visits and correspondences for effective implementation.                                                *****
