                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3657






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Identification and Beautification of Tourist Places in Kishanganj




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any scheme has been proposed for identification and beautification of tourist places in Kishanganj Parliamentary Constituency, Bihar; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the amount of funds proposed to be spent in this regard; and (d) 	the details of the facilities proposed to be provided for tourism development?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	 No, Sir.(b) and (c):		Does not arise .(d):	Ministry under its scheme of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides financial Assistance to the State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Ministry has not received any proposal from the Government of Bihar for development of Kishanganj.********
