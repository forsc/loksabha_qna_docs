                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3137






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Financial Assistance to MSME Traders




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether the Government has decided to provide financial assistance to MSME traders  under various schemes of the Government in the country particularly in Bihar;(b)  	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the schemes that are being implemented in the State of Bihar; and(d) 	the details of quantum of funds being released under each scheme? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b):	The Ministry of MSME implements Credit Guarantee Scheme (CGS) for Micro & Small Enterprises for providing guarantee to Banks and Financial Institutions for credit extended to Micro and Small Enterprises. Retail Traders have also been included under the ambit of this scheme since 01.04.2018. In addition to this, Prime Ministers Employment Generation Programme also focuses on certain selective trading activities. The Government has also launched Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme to provide collateral free automatic business loans. This scheme also covers MSMEs, including traders. Since 02.07.2021 the Ministry of MSME has allowed enterprises engaged in the wholesale and retail trading activity to get themselves registered on ‘Udyam  Registration  Portal’ to enable them to  apply for  loans under Priority Sector Lending.  (c):	 The above mentioned schemes are implemented across the country, including in the state of Bihar. (d):	The status of implementation of the schemes in the country during the current financial year is given below:Amount of Credit Guarantee provided under CGS & ECLGS  and Margin Money provided under PMEGP in the Financial Year 2020-21 and 2021-22<pre>Name of Schemes	Amount of Credit Guarantee / Margin Money provided (in Rs. Crore)	FY 2020-21	FY 2021-22Credit Guarantee Scheme (CGS)	36,899.39	22,958.58(as on 30.11.2021)Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS)	2,33,980.22	31,412.07(as on 29.11.2021)Prime Ministers Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)	2,188.80	1,324.85(as on 01.12.2021)</pre>
