                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2303






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Data of Detained Persons in J&K




Sisir Kumar Adhikari













                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any record on the number of political, civil society activists, public figures and journalists of Jammu & Kashmir who are detained or are under home arrest under the Public Safety Act since 01.08.2019; and(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of persons who have been released since then thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY) (a) & (b):	The Government of Jammu and Kashmir has reported that in view of the constitutional changes effected by the Parliament with regard to the erstwhile State of Jammu and Kashmir in August 2019, various measures were taken in the interest of securtiy and public order which included preventive detention of certain persons.Since August 1.8.2019, 627 persons including separatists, over ground workers,  stone  pelters  etc.  were  detained  at  various points of time.  Out of --2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 2303 FOR 09.03.2021these, based on regular review and ground situation, 454 persons have been released till date.The Government of Jammu and Kashmir has further reported that no person is under home arrest under Jammu & Kashmir Public Safety Act.******
