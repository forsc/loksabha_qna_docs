                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

281






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Scheme for Pension and Medical Aid to Artistes




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  Whether the Government bring in few changes in the Scheme for Pension and Medical Aid to Artists from the Ministry.(b)   Whether it is a fact that the deserving artists are left out without pension and the rural folk arts need to be considered with priority and clear cut definitions.(c)  Whether the Government had ever come across the matter regarding certification of the artists at the district level for proper identification of the artist during the past.(d)   if so, the details thereof ; and(e) if not the steps taken/proposed to be taken in this regard to rectifying the   loopholes in the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)   No Sir, there is no such proposal  under consideration.(b)  The Scheme is open for all the eligible artists who are falling under the prescribed guidelines of the Scheme.(c) and (d)  To identify the genuinity of the applicant it is mandatory for the applicant artist to get pension of atleast Rs.500 per month from the State Government/U.T. Administration.(e)   Does not arise in view of the above reply of (c) and (d)
