                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2534






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






COVID-19 guidelines for Passengers




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has issued any health related directions/instructions/warnings to the railway passengers in the country;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is aware that a large number of railway  employees were affected with corona while performing duties; and(d)  if so, the details of casualties reported, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)     (a) & (b) Yes, Sir. Detailed Covid-19 related instructions/warnings governing entry and movement of Passengers at the stations and in trains has been issued. The instructions cover precautions to be taken while booking/cancellation of tickets/catering and linen on trains and protocol regarding entry and movement of passengers in stations/trains in line with advisory issued by Ministry of Health & Family Welfare and Ministry of Home Affairs.     (c) & (d) Yes, Sir. 32,641 Railway Employees were affected with Corona, out of which 592 Railway employees died.****
