                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






SBM-U in Rajasthan




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the salient features of Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban ( SBM-U); (b) 	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated to and utilized by 	Rajasthan during the last three years under the mission; (c) 	the details of the targets fixed under the mission and the 	achievements made under it so far; (d) 	the details of the cities of Rajasthan that have become open 	defecation free; (e) 	whether it is around the target so fixed; and (f) 	if not, the reasons therefor including the steps being taken to 	achieve the target as per the timeline? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                           (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) :   Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) was launched with the  aim of making urban India Open Defecation Free (ODF) and achieving 100% scientific management of Municipal Solid Waste (MSW).  (b) : 	Allocation of funds to the States/union Territories is made for the Mission period and not on yearly basis. Details of the funds allotted, released and utilized for the Mission period (including last three years period) in respect of Rajasthan is as under :(Amount in Rs. Crore)Mission period  allocation	Funds released(from 02.10.2014 to till date)	Funds Utilized (from 02.10.2014 to till date)705.46	611.35	602.83(c) : 	The detailed status of state’s mission target and achievements are as per Annexure.(d) &(e) : 	All 192 Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) of Rajasthan have been certified as Open Defecation Free (ODF). (f) : Does not arise                                                         *****
