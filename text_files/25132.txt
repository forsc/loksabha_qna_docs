                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

96






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Uniformity in Prices of Petrol and Diesel




Uday Pratap Singh













                                                    Rodmal Nagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is formulating any scheme to maintain uniform prices of petrol anddiesel across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government proposes to formulate any policy to bring uniformity in tax systemin States so as to control the prices of petroleum products?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 96asked by Shri Uday Pratap Singh and Shri Rodmal Nagar to be answered on 26th July,2021 regarding “Uniformity in Prices of Petrol and Diesel”.(a) & (b): No such scheme is under consideration before the Government. The prices ofpetrol and diesel vary from market to market due to various factors like freight rates, VAT, locallevies etc.(c): Article 279 A (5) of the Constitution prescribes that the Goods and Service Tax Councilshall recommend the date on which the goods and services tax be levied on petroleum crude,high speed diesel, motor spirit (commonly known as petrol), natural gas and aviation turbine fuel(ATF), also as per the Section 9(2) of the CGST Act, inclusion of these products in GST willrequire recommendation of the GST Council. So far, the GST Council has not recommendedinclusion of oil and gas under GST.x-x-x-x-x
