                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3405






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Promotion of Islamic Culture




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to promote the Islamic Culture in the country;(b)	the amount of money allocated by the Government to promote Islamic culture by Islamic artists in the country;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the list of beneficiaries practicing Islamic Art, year-wise, under the scheme of providing 400 scholarships to young artists during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)    Government promotes all forms of Indian Art & Culture irrespective of Culture of any specific community.(b)	No allocations of budget are made by the Ministry on the basis of culture of any specific community.(c)      Does not arise.(d)	Under the scheme component of ‘Scholarships to Young Artistes in Different Cultural Fields (SYA)’, the following fields are covered for grant of Scholarship: -(i)  Indian Classical Music(ii)  Indian Classical Dance/ Dance Music(iii)  Theatre(iv)  Visual Arts(v)   Light Classical Music(vi)  Folk, Traditional and Indigenous Arts*****
