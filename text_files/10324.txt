                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3703






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






AC-III Tier Coaches




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of AC-III tier coaches available with Railways;(b) 	the details of average AC-III tier coaches attached to each train;(c) 	whether it is a fact that there is immense demand for AC-III tier coaches as passengers are mostly preferring to travel by AC-III tier;(d) 	the details of the average manufacture of AC-III tier coaches in the last five years, year-wise; and(e) 	the details of efforts being made to double production of AC-III tier coaches to meet the demand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY( SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)  to (e):A Statement is laid on the Table on the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3703 BY SHRI MARGANI BHARAT TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.12.2019 REGARDING AC-III TIER COACHES(a): 	The number of AC III tier coaches in service as on 31st March, 2019 on Indian Railways is 7329.	(b):	The number of coaches including AC-III tier coaches, in the composition of trains depends,  inter-alia, on factors like traffic pattern, schedule of train etc. further, the composition of a large number of trains such as unreserved trains, Antyodaya Express Train, Day time Intercity Trains, Shatabdi Express Trains does not include AC-III Tier Class.(c) and (d) Yes, Sir. The number of AC-III tier coaches manufactured by the Production Units of Indian Railways are as follows:-Year	No of AC III tier Coaches2014-15	4882015-16	5972016-17	5342017-18	5892018-19	885(e)	 The production of AC-III tier coaches, has been scaled up in the last five years, as is evident from the above given table.***
