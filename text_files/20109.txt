                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






AAmount allocated for development of Classical Lanugages




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the amount allocated for the development of classical languages and Sanskrit language after 2014;(b)	the details of amount disbursed, language-wise;(c)	whether the amount is equally allocated  to each language; and(d)	if  not, the reasons for unequal  distribution?  






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)  to (d) :  The policy of the Government is to promote all Indian Languages including Classical Languages.  Central Institute of Indian Languages (CIIL) works for the promotion of all Indian languages including four classical languages namely Kannada, Telugu, Malayalam and Odia.  Development and promotion of Classical Tamil is done by  Central Institute of Classical Tamil (CICT). Sanskrit is promoted by  Central Sanskrit University , New Delhi.  -2- The details of funds  provided  for various classical languages is given below: (in crore)No	Classical language	2014-15 	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-201.	Kannada	    1.09	  1.00  	  1.00	   1.00	   0.99	1.072.	Telugu	    1.09	  1.00  	  1.00	   1.00	   0.99	1.073.	Tamil	    8.00	 11.99	  5.00	  10.59	   4.65	9.80Funds are provided to Central Sanskrit University for their normal functioning  which  includes for  promotion of  Sanskrit language.  Classical Malayalam and Classical Odia centres are recently established.  Grants are released to language institutions as per their requirement and on need basis.  ***
