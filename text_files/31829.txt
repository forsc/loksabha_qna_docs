                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3372






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Janaushadhi Kendra near Hospitals




Balak Nath













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Ranjeeta Koli
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Jan Aushadhi Kendras set up across the country since the launch of Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP), State and district-wise; (b) whether Jan Aushadhi Kendras are set up near every Government district hospital in the country and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether a number of districts are deprived of such Kendras so far and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (d) whether the Government proposes to set up the said centres in such districts also and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the time by which the said centres are likely to be set up in the said districts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(Dr. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)                               (a)   With an objective of making quality generic medicines available at affordable prices to all citizens, especially the poor and the deprived ones, Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) was launched by the Department of Pharmaceuticals. Under the scheme, dedicated outlets known as Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) are opened all over the country to provide quality generic medicines at affordable prices to the masses. State/Union Territory-wise list of PMBJKs is enclosed as Annexure-I and the District-wise list of such Kendras is enclosed as Annexure-II.(b) Department of Pharmaceuticals as well as the Pharmaceutical and Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI), the implementing agency of the scheme have been regularly requesting the State Governments/Union Territories Administrations to facilitate opening of PMBJKs in the premises of Primary Healthcare Centers (PHCs), Community Health Centers (CHCs), Hospitals, including Super Specialty Hospitals by providing rent free space within their premises. (c) (d) & (e) As on 12.12.2021, about 8,578 PMBJKs are functional in all the districts of the country.
