                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1334






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Ayurvedic Medicines




Ramesh Chander Kaushik














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether his Ministry and the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) are going to launch a major programme to develop new Ayurvedic medicines through dozens of laboratories; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the States/UTs which are going to be included in this programme; and (c) whether any time limit has been fixed in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):   The Ministry of AYUSH and CSIR had entered into Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) in April 2019 for cooperation in research and education in areas of traditional systems of medicine and its integration with modern science by utilizing the infrastructure available under CSIR as well as Ministry of AYUSH to pursue R&D covering fundamental research; AYUSH specific diagnostic tools; linking microbiome, gene expression and prakriti; multi-ingredient herbal formulations, including their standardization; exploring modern scientific methods for integration with traditional Indian Systems of Medicine (ISM); linking disease signatures, etc.(c):      No specific timeline has been decided in this regard.……………………
