                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2492






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Fertilizer and Urea Production




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of production and demand of fertilizer and urea per year in the country along with the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (b)	whether the Government has ensured availability of sufficient quantity of fertilizer and urea in the country so that timely and sufficient supply of fertilizer and urea may be made to the farmers, if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government is considering to set up new plants for urea and fertilizer in other parts of the country including Rajasthan; and (d)       if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	& (b):	 The production, demand and availability data of fertilizer including urea for the year 2020-21 is at Annexure- A and B.(c)  & (d):  There is no proposal of Government of India to set up new urea plants in other parts of the country including Rajasthan. However, Government of India has decided to revive Ramagundam, Gorakhpur, Sindri and Talcher Units of Fertilizer Corporation of India Ltd. (FCIL) and Barauni Unit of Hindustan Fertilizer Corporation Ltd. (HFCL) through nomination route by forming Joint Venture Companies of nominated PSUs to set up new Ammonia-Urea plants as per details given below: -Sl.  No.	Name of Fertilizer plant / Joint Venture Companies	Location & State where plant is located	Capacity (Lakh Metric Tonne per annum)1	Talcher Fertilizers Ltd.	Talcher, Odisha	12.72	Ramagundam Fertilizers & Chemicals Ltd.	Ramagundam, Telangana	12.73	Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Ltd.	Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh	12.74	Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Ltd.	Sindri, Jharkhand	12.75	Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Ltd.	Barauni, Bihar	12.7 In addition to above, Cabinet in its meeting held on 21.05.2015  approved to install a new urea plant of 8.646 LMT per annum in the existing premises of Brahmaputra Valley Fertilizers Corporation Ltd (BVFCL) which would replace the existing old urea units Namrup-II and Namrup-III.********
