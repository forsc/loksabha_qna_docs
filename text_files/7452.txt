                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

942






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Anganwadi Centres




Jayant Sinha













                                                    Pramila Bisoyi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of Anganwadi centres and the number of Anganwadis with	separate kitchens, State-wise as on date;(b)	the details of the attendance rates of students at Anganwadi centres during the year 2017-18, State-wise;(c) 		whether the Government is planning to mandate early childhood education centres as part of Government-run elementary schools, and if so, the details thereof;(d)	 the number of Anganwadi Centres running on rented premises, State-wise;(e)	the number of Anganwadi Centres in the country without adequate water facilities in toilet; and(f)	the steps taken to construct Anganwadi centres through convergence with MGNREGA and budget provision made in 2019-20, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	As on date, out of 13,99,697 sanctioned Anganwadi Centres (AWCs), 13,77,595 AWCs are operational across the country. The details of AWCs having separate kitchen facility is not maintained at central level. State-wise details of sanctioned and operational Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) is at Annexure-I.(b)	State-wise details of number of children visiting the Anganwadi Centres and availing the benefits of the Scheme in year 2017-18 is at Annexure-II.(c)	Early Childhood Care & Education (ECCE) is a non-formal education provided at the AWCs to children (3-6 years) as a part of Anganwadi Services.(d)	State-wise number of AWCs running from rented premises is at Annexure-I.(e)	Out of the total operational AWCs, 10,14,655 AWCs are having toilet facilities. The data regarding water availability in toilets is not maintained centrally at the level of Ministry. (f)	Under Convergence with MGNREGS, funds for construction of 29,941 AWC buildings during 2015-16 and for construction of 81,447 AWC buildings during 2016-17 were released to States/UTs. Further, a budget provision of Rs.735.75 crore has been made for construction of 58,670 AWC buildings in APIP 2019-20.
