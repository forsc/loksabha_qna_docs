                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Animal Welfare Board of India




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the date on which Animal Welfare Board of India was set up including the details of its jurisdiction; (b) the number of cowsheds recognised along with the amount of funds provided for their functioning and tin sheds and ambulances provided by the said Board during the last five years, year, State and gaushala wise; (c) whether the applications for giving recognition and assistance received from the institutions/gaushalas of the State of Rajasthan are lying pending with the Board; and (d) if so, the date from which these applications are lying pending including the reasons for their pendency along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING                                                                              (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	 The Animal Welfare Board of India was set up on 19.03.1962 and its jurisdiction is all over India.(b)	The Animal Welfare Board of India recognizes the Animal Welfare Organization working for Animal Welfare activities including the gaushalas. As on date total 3644 number of AWOs have been recognized.The details of last five years State wise recognised AWOs is available in the Animal Welfare Board of India website at http://www.awbi.in/awbi-pdf/Recognition%20list%2016.09.2020.pdf The amount of funds provided for their functioning and ambulances provided by the said Board during the last five years, year, State and gaushala wise is also available in the website of the Board http://www.awbi.in/grants_released.html(c)	& (d) Yes Sir. The details are at Annexure I
