                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4874






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






New Hydel Power Projects




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to start new hydel power projects during the current year;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c)	the total MW of additional electricity likely to be generated thereby?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c) :  	One Hydro Electric Project (HEP), namely, Luhri Stage–I HEP (210 MW) in Himachal Pradesh has been taken up for construction during the current financial year. The project is scheduled for commissioning in the year 2025-26. Luhri Stage-I HEP (210 MW) has an annual design energy of 758.2 Million Units. ************
