                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3579






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Online Portal to Showcase India’s Culture




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry has unveiled an online portal to showcase the country’s culture across the globe and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the portal will be available in all other Indian languages and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether it will help to create awareness among the citizens and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Ministry of Culture & Tourism(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	An online portal, namely Indian Culture Portal was launched on 10th December, 2019 by the Hon’ble Union Minister of State for Culture & Tourism (I/C) Shri Prahlad Singh Patel to showcase information about the rich cultural heritage of India across the globe.  (b)	The Indian Culture Portal is available in two languages i.e. Hindi and English.(c)	& (d) The portal will create awareness about both the tangible and intangible cultural heritage of India, through the content available on the portal, which comprises mainly of rare books, e-books, manuscripts, artefacts from museums, virtual galleries, archives, photo archives, gazetteers, Indian National Bibliography, videos, detailed accounts of Indian UNESCO World Heritage site, Musical Instruments of India, write-ups and beautiful pictures on cuisines, festivals, paintings, folk art and classical art from different States of India etc.*****
