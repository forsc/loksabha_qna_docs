                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1813






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Cauvery Water Regulation Committee




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to appoint a full time chairperson of the Cauvery Water Regulation Committee; and (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)  As per the order of constitution of the Cauvery Water Management Authority (CWMA), the Member (Water Resources) of the CWMA is also the ex-officio full time Chairman of the Cauvery Water Regulation Committee (CWRC). The Member (Water) CWMA and ex-officio Chairperson of CWRC has already been appointed by the Government on 05.03.2019.*****
