                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4690






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Data on Construction of toilets under SBM




Anto  Antony













                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                








                                                    Adhir Ranjan Chowdhury
                                                








                                                    K. Muraleedharan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of toilets constructed under the Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM), year-wise from 2014 till date;(b) whether the Government has taken any action to address reports that thirty per cent of the data reported for both Swachh Bharat Mission - Urban and Gramin on the official websites is manufactured;(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government has investigated claims that twelve lakh toilets reported as constructed between 2014-2020 are actually fictitious;(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(f) the steps being taken to ensure that manufacturing of data does not continue?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) 	Under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM(G)], 11.03 crore individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed since 2014-15 as per the data reported by the States/UTs on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS).  Year-wise number of IHHLs constructed are given below:Year	No. of IHHLs constructed 2014-15	56,64,9112015-16	12,2,39,1612016-17	2,12,57,6432017-18	2,87,85,9072018-19	2,36,47,5182019-20	1,19,47,0952020-21	47,66,1172021-22	20,71,793Total	11,03,80,145Under Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) [SBM(U)], 62,64,914 individual household toilets have been constructed since 2014-15, as per the information received from Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MOH&UA).  Year-wise details of toilets constructed under SBM (U) are not maintained by MoH&UA. (b) to (f)   No such report regarding manufactured data has been received. Geo-tagging of toilets has been made mandatory to ensure that manufactured data do not creep in. *****
