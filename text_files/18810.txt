                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

147






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Setting up of Cold Storage




Sarmistha Sethi













                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any proposal to set up more cold storage facilities in the country;(b)	If so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c)	the details regarding total number of functional and dysfunctional cold stores in the country and also the stores that can be revived States/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) & (b): The Government does not setup cold storage of its own. However, the Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DoAC&FW) is implementing Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for development of Horticulture in the country. Under MIDH, assistance is provided for development of Post Harvest Management (PHM) including establishment of cold storages.              The components of PHM are demand/entrepreneur-driven from among entrepreneurs, private companies, cooperatives, farmers group etc. through commercial ventures for which assistance @35% of admissible project cost in general areas and 50% in hilly and scheduled area is available as credit linked back ended subsidy through respective State Horticulture Missions. (c):	As per available information, the State-wise details of cold storage sanctioned since 2014 under MIDH is given in Annexure. However, no separate data regarding functional and dysfunctional cold stores in the country is maintained in the Department.    *****
