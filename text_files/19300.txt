                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Preservation of Parsi Shrines




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has any proposal to preserve Parsi shrines in Pakistan and bring them under the Pant-Mirza Agreement of 1955 and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a): At present there is no proposal under consideration for preservation of Parsi shrines in Pakistan. However, visits by Pilgrims from India and Pakistan to mutually agreed list of religious shrines in each other’s country are facilitated under the “Bilateral Protocol on Visits to Religious Shrines” signed in September 1974.*****
