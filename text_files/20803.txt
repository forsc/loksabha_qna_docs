                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1947






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






National Level Yoga Webinar Event




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that large number of non- Hindi speaking South Indian participants, especially doctors from Tamil Nadu had demanded the use of English language in National level Yoga webinar conducted in Hindi in August 2020, if so, the detailsthereof;(b) whether the Government had refused to conduct separate English medium yoga programme for South Indian delegates/participants, if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it is a fact that the non-Hindi speaking Tamil/South Indian participants were asked to leave the National level Webinar event as a result, if so, the details thereof; and(d) the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (d):	A National level virtual training on Yoga was organized by the Ministry of AYUSH from 20th to 22nd August 2020 for the Master Trainers from all the States/UTs. During the training program, both Hindi and English languages were used for convenience and understanding of the participants belonging to different States/UTs and all the power point presentations and training materials was in English. In spite of that, few participants were continuously demanding for the use of English language, and they were asked not to disturb the smooth running of the program. The event continued in bilingual format and was concluded successfully as per schedule.
