                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1389TO BE ANSWERED ON 07.12.2021PRE-MATRIC SCHOLARSHIPS 1389. DR. G. RANJITH REDDY: SHRI VENKATESH NETHA BORLAKUNTA: SHRI PASUNOORI DAYAKAR: SHRIMATI KAVITHA MALOTHU: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether out of 7.46 lakh applications received in the Ministry between 2018-19 and 2020-21 under PreMatric Scholarship Scheme in Telangana only 4.6 lakh applicants have got scholarship; (b) if so, the reasons for sanctioning only half of applicants; and (c) the details of scholarships sanctioned during the abovesaid period to various States, State-wise and district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR)(a) & (b): The Ministry deals with the following Pre Matric Scholarship Schemes in all concerned States/UTs including Telangana: Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC students studying in class IX and XThe States/UTs are the implementing agency for selection and award of scholarship to all the eligible candidates under  the scheme. No direct applications are invited by the Department. Pre Matric Scholarship Scheme for OBCs and othersPre-Matric Scholarship for OBC and Others is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme and funds were released to the State Governments for further disbursement of scholarship. No funds were released to  Telangana State under Pre Matric Scholarship Scheme for OBC between 2018-19 & 2020-21,Pre-matric Scholarship for Students with Disabilities:Under the scheme, Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (Divyangjan) has received 72 applications from the state of Telangana for the period 2018-19 to 2020-21 on the National Scholarship Portal and scholarship has been disbursed to all the 72 beneficiaries.(c): The Scheme wise and State-wise details of scholarships sanctioned during the year 2018-19 to 2020-21, as reported by the State Governments, is attached as Annexure-I.State wise release of funds released under Pre Matric Scholarship Scheme for OBCs and Others is attached as Annexure-II.*****Annexure-IStatement referred to part (c) of LOK SABHA
