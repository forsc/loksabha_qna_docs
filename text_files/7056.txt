                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

821






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Mega Solar Power Plants in Naxal Areas




Chirag Paswan














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to establish mega solar power plants in naxal hit areas in near future;(b) if so, the details thereof especially in Jamui district of Bihar;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of plan outlay and implementation procedure for any such proposal in near future?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY,  POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) to (c) The State Government of Andhra Pradesh has reported development of Ultra Mega solar parks in left wing extremist Mandals of Nambulapula Kunta (1000 MW) and Ramagiri (160 MW) in Ananthapuramu District.This Ministry has not received any proposal from State Government of Bihar for setting up any solar park in Jamui district of Bihar.(d) The Government provides Central Financial Assistance @ Rs. 20 Lakh per MW for development of Ultra Mega Solar Power Projects and Mega Solar Parks across the country including in Naxal hit areas.  State Governments are required to submit project proposals indicating land availability etc.  as per scheme for consideration of the Central Government for approval.*****
