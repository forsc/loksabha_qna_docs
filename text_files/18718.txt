                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

48






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Sale of Sanitary Napkins




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) the number of sanitary napkins sold under Jan Aushadhi Suvidha Sanitary Napkin Scheme at Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojna (PMBJP) Kendras from the launch of the scheme to till date, State/UT-wise; (b) the steps taken by the Government for increasing the distribution of sanitary napkins during the period of lockdown in the country; (c) whether the Government is planning to make the sanitary napkins available at e-commerce sites at the reduced price of Re 1; and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)                               (a) As on 22.01.2021, about 9.65 Crore pads of Sanitary napkins have been sold under Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana through Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) since launch of the scheme. The State/UT-wise list is enclosed as Annexure. (b) During COVID pandemic, the Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) has rendered essential services to the nation. The stores remained functional during the lockdown and maintained operations as part of their commitment to ensure uninterrupted availability of essential medicines including Suvidha Sanitary Napkins.  (c) & (d) There is no plan to make Suvidha Sanitary Pads available at e-commerce sites. However, various steps have been taken to upscale the outreach of Suvidha Pads through opening more stores, creating more awareness through advertisement & publicity, strengthening of procurement and logistic systems, etc.
