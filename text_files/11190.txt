                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






National Child Labour Project




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether any notification has been issued in all districts of the country regarding National Child Labour Project (NCLP) societies;(b)if so, the details thereof;(c)the details of the new NCLP societies set up in order to complete new Central Sector for rehabilitation of bonded labourers; (d)whether it is true that NCLP Scheme has been discontinued in Karnataka; and(e)if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	The Government  had started the National Child Labour Project (NCLP) Scheme in year 1988 to rehabilitate working children rescued from any occupations or processes  as mentioned in part ‘B’  of the Schedule to the Child and Adolescent Labour (Prohibition and Regulation) Act, 1986. The scheme is reviewed and evaluated from time to time for its effective implementation.            The entire project is implemented through District Project Society (DPS) under the Chairmanship of administrative head of the district, namely; District Magistrate/Collector/Deputy Commissioner of the District.  The new DPS is set up based on the survey conducted in the districts of the country. Presently the scheme is sanctioned in 324 districts of India. The detail of such districts is annexed.     (d):		No, Sir.(e):		Question does not arise.*                                                      *****
