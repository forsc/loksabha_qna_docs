                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

884






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Projects Undertaken in Santhal Pargana




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the Union Government has undertaken any projects for enhancing basic amenities in Santhal Pargana of Jharkhand and if so, the details thereof;(b) the funds earmarked for this purpose since last three years;    and(c) the time line by which the projects would be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)to(c) The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs(MoHUA) facilitates and assists States/Union Territories (UTs) including the towns in Santhanal Parganas of  Jharkhand, through its flagship Missions - Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban(SBM-U), Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), Smart Cities Mission (SCM), Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban)(PMAY-U), to improve urban infrastructure, enhance basic amenities and quality of life in cities and towns covered under these Missions. Central Assistance is allocated and released to the States/UTs as a whole, and not for any particular area therein. The States in turn release funds to cities/towns. Infrastructure projects take about 3 years or more for completion after award of contracts. Details of development projects taken up in towns/cities in Santhal Pargana area are given in the Annexure.                                           ******
