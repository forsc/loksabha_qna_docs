                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

967






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Tourism Development Projects for Karnataka




Devendrappa Y













                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the tourism development projects sanctioned for Karnataka and Northern Karnataka during the last three years and till date and the funds sanctioned and released for each of these projects, district-wise; (b) 	the number and details of the proposals received from the State Governments for tourism development indicating the status of each project and reasons for delay, if any, in sanctioning these proposals; and (c) 	the time by which the pending proposals of the State are likely to be given clearance?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan and National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country.  The projects are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc.Sanctioning of projects under these schemes is a continuous process however sanctioning of new projects under Swadesh Darshan scheme is currently under review.  No project has been sanctioned to the state of Karnataka. However, ‘Chamundeshwari Devi’ (Mysuru District, Karnataka) is one of the identified site for development under the PRASHAD scheme of the Ministry.*******
