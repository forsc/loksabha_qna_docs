                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5029






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Increase in FDI




Asit Kumar Mal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    whether the FDI is gradually increasing in amount in the country and the different States and if so, the amount of FDI received by Government of India and the different States during 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL) State-wise data of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) inflow in the country is not centrally maintained. The year-wise FDI inflows in the country during 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 can be seen in the following table: <pre>S. No.	Financial Year	Total FDI Inflows (in US$  billion)1.	2015-16 	55.562.	2016-17 	60.223.	2017-18  (P)	60.974.	2018-19  (P)	64.38</pre>        (P) - Figures are provisional subject to reconciliation with RBI.********
