                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1798






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons




Ganesh Singh













                                                    Ajay Nishad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of ‘Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund’ for Sportspersons launched by the Government;(b) the funds released/utilized/disbursed/sanctioned under the said scheme, State-wise including district Satna;(c) the details and the total number of sportspersons benefited from this fund during the each of the last three years and current year, State/sports-wise;(d) the total amount provided to sportspersons alongwith the criteria/ procedure adopted to fix the amount of assistance to be given to sportspersons from this fund;(e) whether any awareness programme has been launched in Madhya Pradesh for implementation of the above scheme for medical treatment of sportspersons and if so, the details thereof since inception of the said scheme, year-wise and if not, the reasons therefor; and(f) whether the Government has achieved the objectives under this fund and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[KIREN RIJIJU](a) : The salient features of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS) are as follows :(i) To provide suitable assistance to outstanding sportspersons now living in indigent circumstances;(ii) To provide suitable assistance to outstanding sportspersons injured during the period of their training for competitions and also during the competitions, depending on the nature of the injury;(iii) To provide suitable assistance to outstanding sportspersons who bring glory to the country in international sports field and who are disabled as an after effect of their strenuous training or otherwise and to provide them assistance for medical treatment;(iv) To provide suitable assistance to promote welfare of outstanding sportspersons generally in order to alleviate distress among them and their dependents in indigent circumstances.(b) and (c) : Funds are not sanctioned/released under the Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons scheme area-wise. Information of funds sanctioned to athletes under the scheme during each of the last three and current year (as on 25.11.2019) is at Annexure-1.(d) : The financial help is provided to athletes depending upon their financial and medical conditions. The information of funds provided to athletes during the period under question is at Annexure-1.(e and f) : Sports being a State subject the responsibility for medical treatment of athletes of Madhya Pradesh rests with the Government of Madhya Pradesh. Under the Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons, this Ministry supplements efforts of the State Government. Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons scheme has been circulated to the Sports Authority of India, Indian Olympic Association, all recognized National Sports Federations, all State Governments/UT Administrations, including Government of Madhya Pradesh. The scheme is also available on the website of Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. The purpose of the scheme is being served as is evident from Annexure-1.*****
