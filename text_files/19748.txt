                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1221






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






In-situ Management of Crop Residue in Madhya Pradesh




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a need to implement in-situ management of crop residue scheme in Madhya Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether a proposal for the said scheme sent by the Government of Madhya Pradesh is pending/under consideration;(e)	if so, the reasons therefor; and(f)	the time by which the said proposal is likely to be approved? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)   (a) to (f):	Inclusion of Madhya Pradesh under the Central Sector Scheme on ‘Promotion of Agricultural Mechanization for In-Situ Management of Crop Residue in the States of Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh and NCT of Delhi’ as per proposal of the State submitted in 2019 was found not feasible as the scope of the scheme as per Budget 2018 announcements was approved for implementation for 2 years during 2018-19 & 2019-20 (extended till 31st March 2021) to support the efforts of the Governments of Haryana, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh and NCT of Delhi to address air pollution and to subsidize machinery required for in-situ management of crop residue.  *****
