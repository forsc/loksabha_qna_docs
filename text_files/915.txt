                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

857






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Rail Connectivity to Ports and Industries




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Railways has any proposal for gauge conversion, 	new railway lines and doubling of railway lines for development 	of ports and industries in the State of Gujarat; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the time by which it is likely to be sanctioned?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) to (c):  As on 01.04.2019, four new lines, twenty five gauge conversion and eleven doubling projects covering a length of 474.45 km., 2220.12 km and 905.47 km and costing ` 5260.87 crore, ` 13013. 52 crore and ` 6511.23 crore respectively falling fully/partly in the State of Gujarat are in various stages of execution/sanction. These will help in development of ports and industries. On these projects, an expenditure of ` 7384 crore has been incurred upto March, 2019, with an outlay of ` 2702.50 crore for the year 2019-20.*****
