                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Children Hospital in Bundelkhand, Uttar Pradesh




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any 200 bedded children hospital has been constructed in Banda and Chitrakoot districts of Bundelkhand region in Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which these hospitals are likely to be made functional?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): As per the information received from the Government of Uttar Pradesh, Banda and Chitrakoot districts of Bundelkhand region have not constructed any 200 bedded Government facility for children. The services for children are provided at Special Newborn Care Units (SNCUs) and Nutrition Rehabilitation Centres (NRCs) at District Hospitals in both the districts. In addition, Newborn Stabilization Units (NBSUs) have been established at sub-district level in Banda and Chitrakoot districts of Bundelkhand region.
