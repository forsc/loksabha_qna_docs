                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1054






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






PMKVY in AP and Telangana




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      whether new norms are being drawn up for Rs.948 crore project in the third phase under PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana(PMKVY) for the current year including Andhra Pradesh and Telangana; (b)      if so, the details thereof, State-wise and progress made? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Yes Sir. Under Skill India Mission, Ministry has launched the third phase of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY 3.0) on 15.01.2021, to train 8.00 lakh youths across the country with an outlay of Rs. 948.90 crore.  PMKVY 3.0 is a demand driven scheme, with bottom-up planning for identification and mapping of job roles. Under the scheme, District Skill Committee (DSC) shall be the focal point for its implementation. DSC shall have major role in preparation of district level plan, mobilization & counseling of candidates, formation of training batches, monitoring of quality assurance & post training support. The scheme is aligned to Common Cost Norms and National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF).  	As on 29.01.2021, 35,644 candidates have been enrolled across the country including State of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. States/UTs wise details of training under PMKVY 3.0, as on 29.01.2021 (since launching of scheme on 15.01.2021), is given at Annexure.
