                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3584






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Visakhapatnam-Chennai Industrial Corridor




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the status of the Visakhapatnam - Chennai Industrial Corridor at  present; (b) 	whether due to non-payment of bills, the contractors are not coming forward to continue their works;(c) 	if so, the details and the reasons therefor; and(d) 	the amount of funds earmarked for this project in the current year‘s budget?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (d):	Visakhapatnam Chennai Industrial Corridor (VCIC) is being implemented as Phase 1 of East Coast Economic Corridor (ECEC). Three nodes namely Chittoor (Srikalahasti), Visakhapatnam and Kadappa have been identified under VCIC. Consultant for master planning and preliminary engineering for Chittoor (Srikalahasti) & Kadappa Nodes has been appointed by National Industrial Corridor Development Corporation Ltd. (NICDCL). For Visakhapatnam node, the master planning and preliminary engineering is initiated by the State Government.  The projection of Fund requirement is ascertained  on completion of master planning and preliminary engineering. As the project is in initial stage of development, no funds have been allocated/released. After master planning and preliminary engineering, projects are poised for consideration and approval of National Industrial Corridor Development and Implementation Trust (NICDIT) and Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA).   ********
