                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the schemes to provide infrastructural assistance in order to preserve culture at present;(b)	the amount spent for infrastructural assistance during the last three years;(c)	the State-wise details of important projects particularly in Uttar Pradesh;(d)	whether the Government proposes to provide infrastructural assistance in order to preserve culture in small towns and districts particularly in Uttar Pradesh;(e)	if so, the details thereof; and(f)	if not, the reasons therefor along with the intention of the Government?
