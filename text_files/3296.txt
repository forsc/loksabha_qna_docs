                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3026






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Settlement-cum-Closeout Agreement




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether NHAI has entered into settlement-cum-closeout agreement with Gammon 	Infrastructure Projects Ltd. in connection with project of six laning of Vijayawada-	Gundugolanu section of NH-5 and six-laning of Hanuman Junction bypass and four-	laning of Vijayawada bypass in Andhra Pradesh under National Highways 	Development 	Project (NHDP) phase-V; (b) 	if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c) 	whether NHAI has entered into an agreement with some other company to execute the 	said projects and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	if not, the steps taken/being taken by 	NHAI for timely completion of the works on 	these projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)	The settlement agreement between National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) and the concerned Concessionaire has been signed. The stretch has now been divided into 04 nos. packages, out of which 01 no. package (i.e. Kalaparru to Chinna Avutupalli) has been awarded and agreement has been signed in January, 2019. Bids for civil works of another package i.e. from Gundugolanu to Kalaparru has been finalized.  For remaining 02 nos. packages, the work for Detailed Project Report (DPR)/feasibility study has been initiated.  *****
