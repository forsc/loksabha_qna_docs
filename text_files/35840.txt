                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Production of Organic Mannures




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is encouraging the farmers for the production of organic manures for its use in agriculture;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the schemes being implemented and the quantum of funds being released under the  said programme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	Government is implementing dedicated organic farming schemes of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) and Mission Organic Value Chain Development in North East Region (MOVCDNER) since 2015-16. Under these schemes, farmers are primarily encouraged to adopt organic cultivation using various organic inputs including organic manure and provided end to end from production to marketing of organic produce. Hands-on training to farmers about on-farm production of organic fertilisers and its use are integral part of these schemes. Farmers are provided subsidy of Rs.31000/ ha / 3 years under PKVY and Rs.32500/ ha/ 3 years under MOVCDNER for various organic inputs including organic fertilisers. An amount of Rs 650 crores is approved for promotion of organic farming for the year 2021-22.                                                                 *************
