                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4706






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Scholarship Programmes




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the funds sanctioned and disbursed under pre-matric, post-matric, merit cum means and Begum Hazrat Mahal scholarship programmes; (b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to increase the amount disbursed per student in these scholarship programmes;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government has any proposal to increase allocation according to the higher Gross Enrollment Rates achieved by States; and(f) if so, the details thereof?  
                                                





ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) & (b): The State-wise details of funds sanctioned under Pre-Matric, Post Matric and Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Schemes and Begum Hazrat Mahal National Scholarship Scheme (implemented by Maulana Azad Education Foundation),from 2014-15 to 2021-22 is annexed.(c) & (d): All aspects including raising the number and amount of scholarships are looked into by the Ministry during the process of scheme revision which is a continuous process.(e) & (f):The budget allocation for educational empowerment of minority communities which was Rs. 1888.50 Crore in 2013-14 has been increased to Rs. 2515.00 Crore in BE 2022-23. The distribution of Scholarship among the States/UTs is made on the basis of population of minorities in the State/UT as per the Census. It was based on Census 2001 up to 2017-18 and thereafter, it is based on Census 2011.
