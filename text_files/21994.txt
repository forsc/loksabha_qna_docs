                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3092






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






MoU with Insurance Supervisors




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has signed Multilateral memorandum of Understanding (MMOU) with the International Association of Insurance Supervisors Forum for mutual cooperation and information exchange between Insurance Supervisors;(b)	If so, the details thereof; and(c)	If not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) to (c):India, represented by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI), was admitted as a signatory to the Multilateral Memorandum of Understanding (MMoU), in May 2013, with the objective to establish a formal basis, inter alia, for cooperation and information exchange among the signatory authorities regarding the supervision of insurance companies where cross border aspects arise.**
