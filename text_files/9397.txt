                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2920






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Haj Pilgrims




Rampreet Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of applications received from all States/ UTs of the country including Bihar for Haj pilgrimage during the last three years along with the number of pilgrims who visited the holy pilgrimage of Haj; (b)	the quota fixed for Bihar in terms of Haj pilgrimage and the increase made in the same, year-wise; and(c)	the number of female pilgrims out of such total pilgrims during last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	The number of applications received from all States/ UTs of the country including Bihar for Haj pilgrimage during the last three years along with the number of pilgrims who actually visited Saudi Arabia for Haj is given in Annexure.(b)	The quota allocated to Haj Committee of India (HCoI) is distributed amongst the States/UTs on the basis of their respective Muslim population, based on the 2011 census figures.The Haj quota of the State of Bihar, number of applications received and the final quota, during the last three years is as under: Year	Original Haj quota allocated	No. of applications received	Final quota	Actual pilgrims proceeded for Haj2017	12125	6963	6963	64592018	12630	5147	5147	47302019	12630	4950	4950	4588(c)	The total number of female pilgrims went on Haj pilgrimage through Haj Committee of India during last three years is as under:Year	Number of female pilgrims2017	578102018	604952019	67144*****
