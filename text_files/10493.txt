                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

352






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Privatisation of Airports




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to privatise the Vijayawada and Tirupati airports and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether consultants have also been appointed to attract investment in airports and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement in reply to parts (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.352 dated 12.12.2019 regarding "Privatisation of Airports".; ; (a): No, Sir.  There is no proposal to privatise Vijayawada and Tirupati Airports at present.; (b): Does not arise in view of (a) above.
