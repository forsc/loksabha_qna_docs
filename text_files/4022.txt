                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3794






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Irrigated/Unirrigated Land




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the total agricultural  land in the country in terms of irrigated/unirrigated and barren land during the last three years;(b)	 whether there is any proposal with the  Government  to  develop  a  system  to evaluate  irrigated  agriculture  land  area programme; and(c)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):   As per publication on Land Use Statistics for 2014-15 (latest available), the net irrigated land, net unirrigated   land  and  barren & unculturable land in the country from 2012-13 to 2014-15 are detailed below:                                                                                                                                                                        (million hectares)Year	Net Irrigated Area	Net Unirrigated Area	Barren and unculturable land2012-13	66.29	73.65	17.072013-14	68.12	73.31	16.952014-15	68.38	71.75	16.99(b) & (c):     Agriculture being a State subject, the State Governments are primarily responsible for the growth and development of agriculture sector, developing perspective plans for their respective States and ensuring effective implementation of the component/schemes of programmes like  Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana .  The schemes are also monitored, evaluated and revamped on the basis of the feedback from time to time. Government of India supplements the efforts of States, through appropriate policy measures and budgetary support.********
