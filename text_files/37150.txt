                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4526






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Cases with CBI




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the PRIME MINISTER be pleased to state the details including the number of registered cases which are under investigation with CBI during the last five years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)  Details of CBI cases are as following: -Year	No of cases registered(RCs) 	No of cases (RCs) under investigation with CBI including those registered in previous years 2017	939	20202018	765	20212019	608	20162020	589	17422021	680	17292022 (till 28.02.2022)	122	1054 *****
