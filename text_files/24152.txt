                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

198






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Production Capacity of Steel Plants




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steel production capacity of the country at present including the details of the units engaged in steel production and mining;(b)	the places where Government, Semi-Government and Non-Government units are producing steel, State-wise;(c)	whether the steel production of these plants/units is adequate and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to improve the steel production of these plants/units?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)&(b): The total steel production capacity of the country at present is 143.91 million tonnes. The details of units engaged in steel production, both in Public and Private Sector are enclosed as Annexure. As per Indian Mineral Yearbook, 2019 published by Indian Bureau of Mines, there were 254 reporting mines in 2018-19, out of which 35 mines were in the Public Sector and 219 in Private Sector.(c)&(d): The details of production, consumption, export and import of finished steel for the last two years and current year are as following:-Year	Total Finished Steel (million tonnes)	Production	Consumption	Export	Import2019-20	102.62	100.17	8.36	6.772020-21	96.20	94.89	10.78	4.75April-June, 2021*	26.35	24.85	3.56	1.16Source: Joint Plant Committee (JPC); *provisional
