                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Upgradation of IIT




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of upgradation of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT); and(b) the remedial steps taken/being taken by the Government to address theissue of implementation of uniform syllabus in all engineering colleges/institutes of technology during the last few years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): Out of the 23 Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) functioning in theCountry as on date, 17 are functioning from their permanent campuses withnecessary academic and administrative infrastructure whereas the remainingsix IITs at Palakkad, Tirupati, Jammu, Dharwad, Bhilai and Goa, which startedtheir academic session from 2015-16 and 2016-17 are functioning from thetemporary campuses. Government provides them budgetary support to developacademic and administrative infrastructure such as classrooms, hostels,medical and sports facilities, laboratories, library etc. With a view to improvingthe overall academic and research eco-system in these institutions, schemessuch as Impacting Research, Innovation and Technology (IMPRINT), UchhatarAvishkar Yojana (UAY), Prime Minister Research Fellows (PMRF), establishmentof Research Parks, Global Initiative for Academic Network (GIAN) etc. have beenlaunched by the Government. So far as uniform syllabus is concerned, All IndiaCouncil for Technical Education (AICTE) has released a Model Curriculum forundergraduate degree courses in engineering and technology for the technicalinstitutions/affiliated universities to adopt.*****
