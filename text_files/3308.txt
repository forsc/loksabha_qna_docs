                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3144






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Allotment of Slots for Airlines




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a number of slots along with schedule per airport of recently defunct airline, across the country are lying vacant and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether any request for allotment of slot has been made to the competent authority at New Delhi to operate everyday an additional Delhi-Rajkot-Delhi flight and if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon; and(c) whether any mechanism is being worked out for allotment of slots of the defunct airline and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Yes, Sir. The details are at Annexure-I.;;(b): Yes, Sir. Airports Authority of India (AAI) had received the request from Air India for operation of flight at Delhi-Rajkot-Delhi route. However, the same was denied by AAI as the requested times (arrival and departure) at Rajkot were beyond watch hours.;;(c): The Government has constituted a Slot Allocation Committee having representatives from DGCA, AAI and JV/Private Airport operators for allocation of domestic slots vacated by Jet Airways to other scheduled airlines on adhoc basis in most transparent and equitable manner, as per the Standard Operating Procedure devised for the purpose.
