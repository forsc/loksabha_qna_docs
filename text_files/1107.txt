                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

906






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Bharatmala Pariyojana in Kolar




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan to implement “Bharatmala Pariyojana’’ in 	Kolar district of Karnataka and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether funds has been allocated and released for the same; and (c)	 if so, the details of the roads identified for the same and work likely to be initiated on the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) 	The following projects stretches have been envisaged for development in Kolar district of Karnataka under Bharatmala Pariyojana:Sr. No.	Name of project stretches	Length in Kolar District (km)(i)	Bangalore – Chennai Greenfield Expressway passing through Kolar District starting Gangapura of Malur Taluk and ending at NG HulkurBangarpet Taluk	59.318(ii)	Greenfield project from Tamil Nadu/Karnataka Border – Hoskote section of NH-648 passing through Kolar District starting from Sampangere village and ending at Dommaluru village	8.6The projects are awarded/funds are allocated based on the outcome of detailed project report / feasibility study. The work of detailed project report / feasibility study for aforesaid project stretches has been initiated. *****
