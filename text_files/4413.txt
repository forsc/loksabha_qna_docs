                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Submarines under Make in India




A.K.P. Chinraj













                                                    Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has invited proposal from Indian companies to partner with foreign firms for manufacturing six new submarines under ‘Make in India’ initiative  and issued the Expression of Interest (EoI) to shortlist Indian companies as strategic partners in it;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c)  the total number of EoI been received from Indian companies on this project;(d)  whether the Government has shortlisted companies based on their capability for integrations of system of systems, expertise in shipbuilding domain and the financial strength; and(e)  if so, the details thereof and the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  Yes, Sir.  An Expression of Interest (EoI) for shortlisting of Indian Strategic Partners (SP) has been issued in June, 2019.  The last date for response by SP is 21.08.2019.(c) to (e):  Does not arise in view of (a) & (b) above.*****
