                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Post Circles and Post Divisions




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of places where new post circles and new post divisions have been created during the last three years as on date; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to open new post circles and new post divisions in the country; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) 	The total number of Postal Circles created in last three years is one. The total number of Postal Divisions created in last three years is 5. The details of these Postal Circles and Postal Divisions are given below:Details of Creation of Postal Circles in last three years<pre>Sl No.	Name of Postal Circle	Headquarters1.	Telangana Postal Circle	Hyderabad</pre>	Details of Creation of Postal Division in last three years<pre>Sl No.	Name of Postal Division	Headquarters1.	Katihar Postal Division in Bihar Postal Circle	Katihar2.	Mandla Postal Division in M.P. Postal Circle	Mandla3.	Rayagada Postal Division in Odisha Postal Circle	Rayagada4.	Rourkela Postal Division in Odisha Postal Circle	Rourkela5.	Gumla Postal Division in Jharkhand Postal Circle	Gumla</pre>(b) 	There is no proposal for creation of any new postal circle.However, two proposals for creation of new Divisions are under examination. (c)	The following proposals for creation of new Divisions are under examination.<pre>Sl.No.	Name of the Division	Name of the Postal Circle/State1.		Karad Division	Maharashtra Postal Circle2.		Baramati Division	Maharashtra Postal Circle</pre>
