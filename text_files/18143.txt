                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1877






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Revival of Water Bodies




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had sanctioned the repair, renovation and revival of water bodies during twelfth Five Year Plan; (b) if so, the details thereof, location-wise; (c) the names and location of water bodies where repair, revival and renovation has been performed and completed; and (d) the names and location of water bodies where repair, renovation and revival has not been completed along with the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI  & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (d)  Under the Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies scheme which is now part of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY), 2219 water bodies have been taken up with an estimated cost of Rs. 1910.69 crore since XIIth plan onwards. Central Assistance (CA) of Rs.433.85 crore has been released for these water bodies to various states upto March, 2020 and an expenditure of Rs.925.14 crore has been reported to be incurred. Out of above, 1465 water bodies have been reported to be completed upto March, 2020. The completion of balance water bodies is dependent upon annual budgetary allocations by the concerned States, project site conditions, work plan, scheduled of completion etc. State wise details of water bodies which have been taken up since XIIth Plan onwards under the scheme are as under: Sl.
