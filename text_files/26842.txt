                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

237






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Silapathar-Bame(Along) Line




Pradan Baruah














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has initiated a new Broad Gauge Line from Silapathar-Bame (Along); and(b)	if so, the details thereof including Gauge, length and States covered?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b): 	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) AND (b) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 237 BY SHRI PRADAN BARUAH TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 04.08.2021 REGARDING SILAPATHAR – BAME (ALONG) LINE(a) and (b):     Ministry of Defence has identified Broad Gauge line of Silapathar - Bame - Along falling in North Eastern Region for detailed study. Accordingly, Final Location Survey (FLS) and preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) have been taken up.  As per field survey, total length of new Broad Gauge line Silapathar – Bame – Along is 96.25 km.The alignment passes through Assam and Arunachal Pradesh. The project is not sanctioned.*****
