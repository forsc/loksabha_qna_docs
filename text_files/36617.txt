                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3696






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






One Rank One Pension Scheme




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji













                                                    Sougata Ray
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has successfully implemented One Rank One Pension Scheme (OROP) for the soldiers;(b) if so, the details and the present status of the implementation of the scheme;(c) whether there has been undue delay in the implementation of the scheme and if so, the reasons therefor along with the expected time scheduled to complete the said process;(d) whether there is any loop-holes in its implementation that is making few of the soldiers to runfrom pillar to post in order to get their grievances rectified; and(e) whether there is a separate cell in the Ministry with dedicated staff to look into the matters of OROP and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a):  Yes, Sir.(b):  The Government has implemented One Rank One Pension (OROP) vide MoD letter no. 12(1)/2014/D(Pen/Pol)-Part-II dated 07.11.2015 and tables for fixation of pension were issued vide MoD letter no. 12(1)/2014/D(Pen/Policy)-Part-II dated 03.02.2016.(c):  No, Sir.(d):  No, Sir.(e):  No, Sir. *****
