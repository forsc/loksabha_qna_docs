                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4362






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






New Schemes for Madhya Pradesh




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	Whether any new scheme has been approved for Madhya Pradesh and if so, the details thereof;b)	the details and the number of institutions registered by Ministry of Cooperation in Madhya Pradesh; c)	whether any separate scheme is proposed for the benefit of the tribal areas;d)	Whether any new scheme is proposed in Khargone-Barwani Parliamentary Constituency and if so, the details thereof; ande)	the details of various projects implemented in the aspirational districts?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF COOPERATION ???????? ??????  	(SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a)	Presently, under Central Sector Integrated Scheme on Agricultural Cooperation (CSISAC) Ministry of Cooperation provides financial assistance in the form of subsidy to Cooperative institutions/societies through National Cooperative Development Corporation. Cooperatives in the state of Madhya Pradesh are also beneficiaries of the financial assistance under CSISAC Scheme. Details of financial assistance in the form of subsidy provided to Madhya Pradesh is mentioned below:  Year	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22 Grant of Subsidy under CSISAC scheme (Rs. in Lakhs)	876.384	521.379	629.855	404.92(b)	Number of institutions (sector wise) registered by Ministry of Cooperation under Multi-State Co-operative Societies Act, 2002 in Madhya Pradesh is attached at Annexure 1 (c)	Presently, under CSISAC Scheme financial assistance to cooperative units with specific preference to tribal areas is provided. During 2020-21, NCDC disbursed Rs. 68.64 crore for 143 tribal cooperative units.(d)	Under Integrated Cooperative Development Project (ICDP) of CSISAC Scheme Khargone-Barwani Parliamentary Constituency had been given a financial assistance of Rs. 807.345 lakhs in the year 1995-96.(e)	Ministry of Cooperation under Central Sector Integrated Scheme on Agricultural Cooperation (CSISAC) has provided financial support to four Cooperative Education Field Projects (under aspirational districts) in Raghogarh, Ratlam, Ujjain and Bhopal districts of Madhya Pradesh through NCUI. NCDC has also covered all the aspirational districts in Madhya Pradesh under  CSISAC and other Schemes.
