                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2168






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






IIEST




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken steps to upgrade certain Universities as IndianInstitutes of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST);(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c) whether the Government has taken up the issue of upgradation of these Universitieswith the respective State Governments; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The Anandakrishnan Committee, which was constituted in 2005 to assess thepotential of some selected Institutions for their transformation to high-level technological‘Institutes of National Importance (INI)’, had recommended 05 (five) Institutes to be convertedinto ‘Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST)’ through an Act ofParliament. The five recommended Institutes were (1) Bengal Engineering College – Shibpur(West Bengal) [later became Bengal Engineering and Science University (BESU) – Shibpur];(2) Institute of Technology, Banaras Hindu University (BHU) – Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh); (3)Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) – Kochi (Kerala); (4) Andhra UniversityCollege of Engineering – Vishakhapatnam (Andhra Pradesh); and (5) University College ofEngineering (UCE), Osmania University – Hyderabad (Andhra Pradesh).(c) & (d): In consultation with the respective State Governments, the Government of Indiahas upgraded IT (BHU) as Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi w.e.f. 29.06.2012.Similarly, the BESU has also been upgraded as IIEST, Shibpur w.e.f. 04.03.2014.*-*-*-*-*
