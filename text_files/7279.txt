                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

949






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Shortage of AYUSH Practitioners




Pratima Bhoumik














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that there is a huge shortage of Registered Practitioners of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, Yoga, Naturopathy and Sowa-Rigpa, if so, the State and UT-wise details thereof along with the action taken by the Government thereon; (b) whether it is a fact that in the State of Tripura there is no Registered Practitioners of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, Yoga, Naturopathy and Sowa-Rigpa, if so, the details thereof and action taken by the Government to fulfil such posts; and (c) whether it is also a fact that there is no Licensed Pharmacies of AYUSH medicines in the State of Tripura, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):    No huge shortage of Registered Practitioners of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, Naturopathy has come  to the notice of this Ministry. The State and UT wise details of the AYUSH practitioners is annexed (Annexure).  (b):  Yes, there are no Registered Practitioners of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha and Naturopathy in the State of Tripura, Registered Practitioners of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha Yoga, Naturopathy and Sowa – Rigpa registered in neighboring state of West Bengal are allowed to practice in the State of Tripura and the posts in Tripura are being fulfilled by such Registered Practitioners.(c ):    Yes, there are no Licensed Pharmacies of AYUSH medicines in the State of Tripura.
