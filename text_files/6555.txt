                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

396






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Suvidha Programme




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS¸ be pleased to state:(a) the salient features of Suvidhaprogramme started under the PradhanMantriBhartiyaJanaushadhiPariyojana (PMBJP); (b) the number of stores where Suvidha sanitary napkins are available, State-wise including Chandigarh; and (c) the steps taken by the Government to publicize the programme through Television, Radio, Print and other forms of media?






ANSWER




MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)                               (a)  	Sale of  ‘JanaushadhiSuvidha’ Oxo-Biodegradable Sanitary pad was launched under PMBJP on 4th June, 2018. Initially these sanitary pads were made available for sale at Rs. 2.50/- per pad.  In order to make these sanitary pads more affordable, Government of India decided to make these pads available at the rate of only Rs 1.00/- per pad. From 27.08.2019, these pads are available @ Rs 1.00/- per pad at all JanaushadhiKendras. (b) ‘JanaushadhiSuvidha’ Sanitary pads are available in all PMBJP Stores. State-wise number of stores, including Chandigarh, where Suvidha sanitary pads are available, is enclosed as Annexure-I.(c)	The awareness about this programme is being spread through advertisements in Print Media, Radio advertisements, TV advertisements, Cinema advertisements, Outdoor publicity is done through Hoardings, Bus Queue Shelters, Bus branding, Auto wrapping. In addition to this, social media platforms like facebook, twitter, are being utilized. Awareness workshops are also conducted.
