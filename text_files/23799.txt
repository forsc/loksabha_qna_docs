                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4723






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






BTS in Maharashtra




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to increase Base Transreceiver Stations (BTS) in Maharashtra to increase mobile connectivity;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, location-wise; and (c) 	the details of BTS towers currently active in Maharashtra, district-wise and telecom company-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b)	As on 16.03.2021 there are 198691 number of active Base Transceiver Stations (BTSs) of Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) providing mobile network coverage in Maharashtra. Further, TSPs install mobile towers and BTSs to improve coverage and/or capacity of their networks based on their techno-commercial assessments. Mobile coverage in uncovered villages in the country is being provided by the Government and TSPs in a phased manner. Under Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) Left Wing Extremism (LWE) Phase-II scheme of Government of India, 125 mobile towers are provisioned to increase mobile connectivity in Maharashtra. The list of 125 mobile tower locations is given at Annexure-I.(c)	The details of active Base Transceiver Stations (BTSs) as on 16.03.2021 in Maharashtra, district-wise and telecom company-wise are given at Annexure-II.
