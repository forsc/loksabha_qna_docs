                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3885






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Assistance for National Agriculture Development Programme




Girish Chandra













                                                    Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of the States which receive financial assistance from the Union Government under the National Agriculture Development Programme and the funds allocated under the said scheme, State-wise; (b) 	whether there is any mechanism to monitor the utilization of the funds released under the scheme; and (c) 	the objectives achieved out of the objectives mentioned in the scheme, so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):  	National   Agricultural  Development  Programme (NADP)/Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) was launched  in  2007-08 and the scheme has been  revised  with effect from 1.11.2017  as Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana - Remunerative Approaches for Agriculture and Allied sector Rejuvenation (RKVY-RAFTAAR) with more focus on development of pre and post harvest management. Year-wise and State-wise allocation and release of funds under this scheme during the last two years and the current year is given at annexure-I. (b) & (c): 	To monitor the utilization of funds under the scheme, States are required to regularly enter and update financial and physical progress of the project on a web based RKVY portal. Besides, monitoring of the scheme is done through geo-tagging of RKVY assets, Desk Review Meetings , Video- conferencing, third party evaluation etc.  The funds under this scheme are released to the States in two installments of 50% each. States are required to submit Utilization Certificates for 60 % of the funds released to them as first installment in the current year and 100% of funds released in previous years to avail 2nd installment of funds under the scheme. States have approved a number of projects in various sectors in agriculture and allied sectors, including projects for pre and post harvest management, for implementation under this scheme. State-wise cost of projects approved under the scheme in various sectors during the last two years and the current year as per the data entered by the States in web based RKVY portal (RDMIS) is given at Annexure-II.
