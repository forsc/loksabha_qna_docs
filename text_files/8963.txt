                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2415






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






MGNREGA in Andhra Pradesh




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the implementation of MGNREGA in all the districts of the country during the last three years and the current year, year-wise and State-wise;(b)	the amount pending for release to States during the last three years and the current year, year-wise and State-wise;(c)	whether it is true that the Government of Andhra Pradesh has submitted audit report for the expenditure made between April to September, 2018-19 to the Ministry requesting to allocate funds from October, 2018 to March, 2019;(d)	if so, the reasons for delay in allocating money for the above period; and(e)	the time by which the Ministry would release funds to the State of Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As per the information uploaded by the States/UTs in Management Information System (MIS), details of persondays generated under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) during the last three years and current financial year 2019-20 (as on 04.11.2019) is at Annexure.(b) to (e):	The Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is a demand driven wage employment programme.  The Ministry releases funds to the States/UTs based on the agreed to Labour Budget, opening balance, pending liabilities of the previous year for the financial year, if any, and overall performance. The State Government of Andhra Pradesh has submitted the Audited Report for the expenditure for the financial year 2018-19. Fund release to the States/UTs is a continuous process and Central Government is committed to making funds available keeping in view the demand for work. The Ministry has released Rs. 6011.93 crore for implementation Mahatma Gandhi NREGS to Andhra Pradesh during the current financial year 2019-20 (as on 27.11.2019).   Annexure referred in reply to part (a) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.2415 dated 03.12.2019
