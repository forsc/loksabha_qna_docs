                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3476






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Innovative Learning Programme




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is in the process of setting up of a ‘Centre for talented children’at the ‘National Bal Bhawan’ under Prime Minister’s innovative Learning programme;(b) if so, the details thereof and the motive for setting up this centre; and(c) the manner in which the proposed Centre is likely to operate?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) Government intend to implement the Pradhanmantri innovative LearningProgramme for talented and gifted children. The modalities for the same are being workedout.****
