                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Capital Goods Industry




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the performance of the capital goods industry in terms of annual percentage increments in the production output of the sector during the last five years; and(b) the share of exports of the sector and the manner in which it has changed during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                  (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)                              ********(a):  The annual growth rate of Capital Goods Industry as measured in the Index of Industrial Production for the last five years is indicated as under: Year	Growth in Production output (Capital Goods) (%)2014-15	-1.12015-16	3.02016-17	3.22017-18	4.02018-19	2.7      Source: Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation(b):  The data is being collated.                             ***********
