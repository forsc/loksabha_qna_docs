                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1527






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Procurement of Agricultural Produce




Preneet Kaur














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether agricultural produce not purchased by private companies can be sold to the Government at Minimum Support Price (MSP);(b) 	whether a farmer desirous of selling his agricultural produce solely to the Government can do so and if so, the Government will purchase the same at MSP; and(c) 	the details of the mechanism adopted by the Government to procure agricultural produce from the farmers? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (c):	There exists a transparent & uniform policy for procurement of agricultural produce by Government agencies across the country. The Government extends price support to paddy and wheat through the Food Corporation of India (FCI) and State Agencies. The procurement policy is open ended. Under this policy, whatever food grains are offered by farmers, within the stipulated period & conforming to the specifications prescribed by Government are purchased at Minimum Support Price (MSP) by the State Government agencies including FCI for Central Pool. Further, oilseeds, pulses and copra of Fair Average Quality (FAQ) are procured from farmers under  Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay SanraksHan Abhiyan (PM-AASHA), as per its prescribed guidelines at MSP in consultation with the concerned State Government as and when market price of these produce fall below the MSP. However, if farmer gets better price in comparison to MSP, they are free to sell their produce in open market.  *******
