                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3158






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Custom Hiring Centres




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                        (a) whether there is any proposal for setting up 3000 new custom hiring centres in Madhya Pradesh; (b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which the said centres are likely to be set up; (c) whether there is any proposed target for setting up skill development centres for new custom hiring centres along with Farmer Producers Organization (FPOs) and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether a target of setting up of mechanized farming system in 600 villages has been set in collaboration with major manufacturers of yantradoot machines in order to establish the agricultural machine skill development institutes; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):	 As per the information submitted by the Government of Madhya Pradesh, it has targeted to set up 3000 new Custom Hiring Centres under Atmanirbhar Madhya Pradesh programme. At present Skill Development Centers are operational in Bhopal, Jabalpur, Satna, Sagar and Gwalior. The Government of Madhya Pradesh has targeted to setup four new Skill Development Centers in Ujaiin, Narmadapuram, Chambal &Shahdol Divisions. It has also been targeted to setup mechanized farming system in 600 villages under Yantradoot village scheme of Government of Madhya Pradesh under which large level cluster demonstrations are organized along with training and visit programmes in a planned manner.*****
