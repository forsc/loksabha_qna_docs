                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1787






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Farmers Suicide




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of farmers suicides reported during the last three years and the current year, State-wise; (b) 	whether the Government has received proposals from various States including Kerala to address the issue of farmers distress and waived-off crop loans from various banks including co-operative banks, if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	whether the Government is considering a special package for severely affected districts in the country including Idukki district of Kerala, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) under the Ministry of Home Affairs compiles and disseminates information on suicides in its publication titled ‘Accidental Deaths and Suicides in India’ (ADSI). These Reports on suicides are available upto 2015 on its website. The Reports for the year 2016 onwards have not been published.(b) & (c): 	The Government has received request from the various quarters to address the issue of farmer distress on various accounts. To address the issue of agrarian distress, Government of India have taken several steps for the welfare of the farmers. A list of various interventions taken by the Government is annexed.          There is no proposal on waiver off crop loans under consideration of the Government of India.            The Government of India has implemented a package for mitigating agrarian distress in Idukki district of Kerala with financial assistance of Rs.764.45 crore for implementing various programmes/interventions suggested by the M.S. Swaminathan Research Foundation. The implementation period of the package has ended in November, 2013.  State Government of Kerala had requested for extension of period of package upto November, 2016, to which. the State Government  was advised to implement the approved activities of Idukki package under various on-going schemes/ programmes of Government like Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), National Food Security Mission (NFSM),  Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) etc.
