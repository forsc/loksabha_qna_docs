                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2050






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Construction of Nullah Project in Maharashtra




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of work completed of Nullah construction under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) in Mira Bhayander Municipal Corporation in Maharashtra;(b) whether this work has been discontinued or it is continued uninterrupted and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) the present status of the work; and(d) the steps taken by Municipal Corporation to remove the difficulties of work in rainy season in Uttar complex?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) and (c): Government of Maharashtra has taken up construction of 22 Nullahs in Mira Bhayander Municipal Corporation under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT). Work order has been given for 12 Nullahs (Length 4.27k.m.), of which 2.7k.m. of Nullahs has been completed. Remaining 10 Nullahs are in Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ). Municipal Corporation had sought CRZ’s permission for construction of Nullah through proposal dated 05 August 2017, but the permission is yet to be received by them.(b):   Mira Bhayander Municipal Corporation has informed that work of construction of Nullah has not been discontinued. (d):    Mira Bhayander Municipal Corporation has informed that construction work of Nullah are stopped during rainy season.                                *****
