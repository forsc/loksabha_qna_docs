                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Proposal to Grow Sandalwood




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government has any proposal to encourage farmers to grow sandalwood trees in their fields;(b)if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government has any estimation of the area of land in acres used at present to grow sandalwood in the country including in Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE????????????????????????	 (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (b): Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is presently implementing Sub-Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF) Scheme in 23 States/UTs including State Govt. of Tamil Nadu with an aim to encourage tree plantation on farm land along with crops/ cropping system to help the farmers get additional income and make their farming systems more climate resilient and adaptive.Multipurpose tree species including Sandalwood are promoted for plantation on farm land along with agricultural crops.Under the scheme, assistance to farmers is given through State Governments  for nursery development, boundary plantation and block plantation of prominent tree species. (c)Government has not done any estimation of the area under sandalwood cultivation. However, As per information received from the State of Tamil Nadu,sandalwood saplings are planted inan area of 302.5 acres under Tamil Nadu Mission on Sustainable Green Cover on Farmland (TNMSGCF)and SMAF.
