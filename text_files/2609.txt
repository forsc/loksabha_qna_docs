                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2404






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Containers Detained by SIIB and CIU




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Special Investigation and Intelligence Branch (SIIB) of Customs Department and Custom Intelligence Unit (CIU) has detained containers at ports due to mis-declaration of products in the financial years 2014-15, 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 and if so, the details thereof; (b) the total amount of additional custom duty/penalty charged by the Government under mis-declaration of products by Customs during the said financial years; (c) the amount of foreign currency paid by Indian importers to shipping companies as detention charges on containers detained by SIIB and CIU during the said financial years; (d) whether more than expected time has been taken by the Customs authorities to clear files related to the said matters; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to expedite the file process?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE [REVENUE](SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) (a to e)   The information is being collected and will be laid on the table of the House.
