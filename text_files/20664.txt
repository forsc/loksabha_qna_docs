                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Setting up of AIIMS like Institutions




Adoor Prakash













                                                    Abdul Majeed Ariff
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the details of the AIIMS like institutions sanctioned since 2014, status of their completion, funds allocated and utilised for each project, State/UT-wise; (b) whether there has been delay in completion of any of these AIIMS like institutions, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor, project-wise and State/ UT-wise; (c) the details of the proposals received from States including Kerala and Maharashtra for establishing AIIMS like institutions and the action taken by the Government on each such proposal, State/UT-wise; and (d) the time by which each of the sanctioned AIIMS like institution will be functional/ operational, State/UTwise and project-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA    STARRED QUESTION NO.164* FOR 12TH FEBRUARY, 2021(a) to (d) 15 new AIIMS have been sanctioned since 2014 under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY). The details of 15 new AIIMS including total approved cost, funds released, status of their completion and reasons for delay are at Annexure.Apart from 22 new AIIMS which have been sanctioned under PMSSY since its inception, proposals for setting up AIIMS like institutions have been received from Arunachal Pradesh, Goa, Karnataka, Kerala, Tripura, Sikkim and Mizoram. AIIMS are being set up in various parts of the country in a phased manner as per budget announcements keeping in view gaps in tertiary healthcare infrastructures in different regions of the country.
