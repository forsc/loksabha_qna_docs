                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PLANNING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2801






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Achievement of Sustainable Development Goals




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





PLANNING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has undertaken any initiative in the direction of achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs);(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	India’s stand at present regarding achievement of goals and targets of SDGs?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION(RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) and (b) The States and Union Territories (UTs) are the actual implementers of the country’s ambitious development agenda. NITI Aayog has constructed the SDG India Index and Dashboard 2019-20 which measures the progress achieved by all States and Union Territories in their journey towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals and associated targets. The SDG India Index 2019 is available via an online dashboard (http://sdgindiaindex.niti.gov.in). The report is in the public domain and can be found in https://niti.gov.in/sites/default/files/2019-12/SDG-India-Index-2.0_27-Dec.pdf The SDG India Index provides critical insights on the country status of SDGs. The respective Ministries/Departments and States and UTs were consulted on the usage of the indicators and data availability. The SDG India Index Report has a Section on profiles of all 37 States & UTs to analyse their performance on SDGs. (c) The SDG India Index 2019 evaluates India’s performance on each of the SDGs. The all India SDG Index Score stands at 60 against the target of 100 to be achieved by 2030. The India’s performance across goals is given below: SDG	Target for 2030	SDG Index ScoreSDG 1 No Poverty	100	50SDG 2 Zero Hunger	100	35SDG 3 Good Health and Well-Being	100	61SDG 4 Quality Education	100	58SDG 5 Gender Equality	100	42SDG 6 Clean Water and Sanitation	100	88SDG 7 Affordable and Clean Energy	100	70SDG 8 Decent Work and Economic Growth	100	64SDG 9 Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure	100	65SDG10 Reduced Inequalities	100	64SDG11 Sustainable Cities and Communities	100	53SDG12 Sustainable Consumption and Production	100	55SDG13 Climate Change	100	60SDG15 Life on Land	100	66SDG16 Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions	100	72Composite SDG	100	60 *****
