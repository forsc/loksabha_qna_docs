                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4356






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






New International Flights




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any details about the new international flights which started from new airports in India; and(b) if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Five new greenfield airports have been constructed and operationalized across the country. These airports are (i) Kazi Nazrul Islam Airport, Durgapur in West Bengal, (ii) Shirdi Airport in Maharashtra, (iii) Pakyong Airport in Sikkim, (iv) Kannur Airport in Kerala and (v) Kalaburagi (Gulbarga) Airport in Karnataka. International flights have started from Kannur Airport. During the Winter Schedule 2019, international flights have operated from Kannur airport to Abu Dhabi, Muscat, Bahrain, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Doha, Sharjah and Dubai.
