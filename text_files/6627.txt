                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

426






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Pre-Matric Scholarship for OBCs




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Pre-Matric scholarship is being provided to Other Backward Classes at present; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the names of the States where the said scheme is being implemented, State-wise; (c) whether the benefit under the said scheme is also being provided to the students enrolled in private schools and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government proposes to introduce any other scheme to improve education particularly for the said section; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. The Ministry is implementing Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for providing financial assistance to OBC students. The said scheme is being implemented in following States and UTs:-  Andhra Pradesh, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Punjab, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, West Bengal, Assam, Manipur, Sikkim, Tripura, A&N Island, Chandigarh, D&N Haveli, Daman and Diu, Delhi and Puducherry.(c): No, Sir.(d) & (e): There is no proposal to introduce any additional scheme for education of OBC students.******
