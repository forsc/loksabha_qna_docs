                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Development of Water Reservoirs and Ponds in  Bundelkhand




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to develop various big water reservoirs and ponds in Bundelkhand as tourist spots; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) and (b):		Ministry of Tourism under Swadesh Darshan  Scheme provides financial assistance to State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. Under the scheme ministry has sanctioned projects for development of sites near big water reservoirs and ponds under its various thematic circuits including in Bundelkhand. The list of projects sanctioned under the above scheme which have components pertaining to water reservoirs, ponds and other water bodies are as follows: (Rs. in crore)<pre>Sr. No.	Circuit/ year of sanction	Name of project	AmountSanctioned1.	Ramayana Circuit (2016-17)	Development of Chitrakoot and Shringverpur in Uttar Pradesh 	69.452.	Spiritual Circuit (2016-17)	Development of Bijnor- Meerut- Kanpur- Kanpur Dehat- Banda- Ghazipur- Salempur- Ghosi- Balia- Ambedkar Nagar- Aligarh- Fatehpur- Deoria- Mahoba- Sonbhadra- Chandauli- Mishrikh- Bhadohi in Uttar Pradesh 	63.773.	Heritage Circuit (2016-17)	Development of Heritage Circuit Gwalior – Orchha – Khajuraho – Chanderi – Bhimbetka – Mandu in Madhya Pradesh	92.97</pre>********
