                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1154






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Dumping  of  Steel




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the number of companies identified as dumping steel which is harmful for the domestic steel manufacturers;(b)	the details of the damage suffered by the steel industry as a result including year-on-year slowdown, if any; and(c)	the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to counter these anti-competitive practices in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)&(b): The details of the number of companies identified as dumping steel which is harmful for the domestic steel manufacturers are placed at Annexure-I.(c)	Steel industry is a deregulated sector and steel prices in the country are market driven and commercially determined. However, the Government has taken various initiatives to protect the domestic steel sector through various trade measures such as Anti-dumping Duties, Countervailing Duty and Minimum Import Price. In addition to trade measures, Government has also rolled out Domestically Manufactured Iron & Steel Products Policy for procurement of domestically manufactured Iron & Steel products by Government agencies as well as Quality Control Orders to prevent import of substandard steel into the country.********
