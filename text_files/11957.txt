                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

906






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Engagement of Private Companies by NHAI




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether private companies are engaged by the Government for construction of National Highways (NHs) and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the work of construction of roads has been entrusted to some agencies by the National Highway Authority of India (NHAI) in Bundelkhand region during the last five years and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the assigned roads were constructed by these companies within the stipulated time and as per the prescribed criteria/quality norms and if not, the reasons therefor along with the action taken by the Government in this regard;(d) the details of the companies blacklisted by NHAI during the last five years; and(e) the details of the penalties recovered therefrom and legal action taken against them?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS  (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) Yes Sir. Private companies are engaged by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) for construction of National Highways (NHs). As on date, most of the works are being executed by the private parties.(b) Details of construction of roads entrusted to some agencies by the NHAI in Bundelkhand region during the last five years are at Annexure.(c) Wherever there is a delay in completion of NH project, action against the contractors/concessionaires is taken in accordance with the agreement provisions, if delay is attributed to them.  The action taken depends upon delays and varies from financial penalty to termination of the contract.	The National Highways are constructed taking into consideration the provisions laid in MoRT&H Specifications for Road and Bridge work & IRC/BIS guidelines. The contractor has to submit quality assurance plan prior to the commencement of works for approval of the concerned Engineer associated with the project. The Engineer has to ensure the quality of works as per specifications and quality assurance plan.(d) to (e) The NHAI has declared the following 8 firms as Non-performers and restrained them from participating in the on-going and future bids of NHAI for a period of 3 years:(i)	M/s SREI Infrastructure Finance Ltd.(ii)	M/s Pratibha Construction Engineers & Contractors (I) Pvt. Ltd.(iii)	M/s Gujarat Construction Company(iv)	M/s Johnsons Enterprise Ltd.(v)	M/s Transstroy India Pvt. Ltd.(vi)	M/s Open Joint Stock Company Corp Transstroy (OJSC)(vii)	M/s Madhucon Projects Ltd.(viii)	M/s Madhucon Granites Ltd.
