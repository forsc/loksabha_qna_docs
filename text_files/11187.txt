                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Maternity Benefit Act




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has come out with a report on working of the Maternity Benefit Act in 2014; (b)if so, the number of establishments covered by the Act and the number of women in such establishments who claimed maternity entitlements; (c)the number of women who were provided maternity entitlements including the amount of entitlement paid out and the number of complaints received for not implementing the Act and action taken thereof;(d)if not, whether the Ministry is monitoring implementation of the Act; and(e)whether such data has been compiled for years 2015-19, if so, the details thereof and if not, reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):		  Yes, Sir. Contd..2/-::  2  ::(b) to (e):	 The number of establishments covered by the Act and the number of women in such establishments who claimed maternity entitlements is at Annex-I.The number of complaints received and prosecutions launched (by States) during the year 2014 to 2016 is at Annex-II.  The Maternity Benefit Act, 1961 is implemented by the Central Government as well as the State Governments in their specific spheres. The complaints received for violation of provisions of the Act by respective Governments are dealt with as per the provisions of the Act.**                                                   ******
