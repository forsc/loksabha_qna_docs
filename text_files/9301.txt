                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2864






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Ports and Harbours under Indo-Bangladesh Protocol Route




Durga Prasad Rao Balli














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of ports and harbours under Indo-Bangladesh Protocol Route; and(b)	whether the Port of Chittagong and Mongla are included in the said route and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) 	Under the Protocol on Inland Water Transit and Trade (PIWT&T) between India and Bangladesh, the following are existing Ports of Call in the two countries on Indo-Bangladesh Protocol (IBP) route:<pre>India	Kolkata	Haldia	Pandu	Karimganj	Silghat	DhubriBangladesh	Narayanganj	Khulna	Mongla	Sirajganj	Ashuganj	Pangaon                                                 </pre>(b)	The Port of Mongla is declared as Port of Call under PIWT&T and Chattogram port is not part of PIWT&T.  Mongla and Chattogram are declared as Ports of Call under the “Coastal Shipping Agreement” between India and Bangladesh. A MoU, Agreement and Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) on the use of Chattogram Port & Mongla Ports for movement of goods to & from India have been signed by India and Bangladesh..                                           *****
