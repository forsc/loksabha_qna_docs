                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Dedicatecd Freight Corridors Project




Keshari Devi Patel













                                                    Kanakmal Katara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC) projects under Indian Railways;(b) 	the steps taken to ensure timely completion of the Dedicated Freight Corridor projects along with the details thereof;(c)	the names of the institutions which have been assigned work under East-West DFC as well as in Prayagraj under Eastern DFC;(d) 	whether the quality of the work is being examined and if so, the details thereof;(e) 	whether any complaint has been registered against any of the above mentioned institutions and the action taken on the said complaints along with the details thereof and the estimated cost of the projects;(f) 	whether cost has increased due to the slow pace of work related to these projects and if so, the details thereof; and(g)  the details of the land acquisition for approach roads in Uttar Pradesh along with the details of approach roads?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY  (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b): Two freight corridors namely Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor (EDFC) (from Ludhiana to Dankuni – 1875 km) and Western Dedicated Freight Corridor (WDFC)  (from Dadri to Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust - 1506 km) are under implementation. Several proactive measures have been taken  to  fast  track  the  project  such  as  hike  in  fund  allocation,  close monitoring, resolution of contractual issues, resolving land acquisition issues etc.(c): The following major contractors have been awarded work in EDFC & WDFC:(I)   In EDFC: i)    TATA ALDESA JV, GMR India Ltd -  TATA Projects Ltd. JVii)   GMR India Ltd – SYSTRA JV iii)   L&T iv)   BSC – C&C JV v)    ATEPL MGCPL JV, TEXMACO vi)   ALSTOM India Ltd – ALSTOM Transport SA JVvii)  L&T – KYOSAN Consortium viii) L&T – INABENSA JV ix)   SIEMENS & KEC Consortiumx)    SAI - TYPSA JVxi)   TYPSA – ICT – BARSYL JVxii)  SYSTRA – MOTT. MacDonald JV xiii) DB E&C GmbH Germanyxiv) SMEC , CDM Smith JVxv)  SMEC RODIC JVIn Prayagraj – i)   GMR India Ltd – SYSTRA JV, ii)   L&T – INABENSA JV,  iii)  SIEMENS& KEC Consortiumiv)  SYSTRA – MOTT. MacDonald JV.(II)  In WDFC: i)	     Express Freight Consortiumii)    Express Freight Railway Consortium iii)   Sojit-L&T  Consortiumiv)    IIS-L&T Consortiumvii)   JFE-Gammon Consortiumviii)  M/s Sojitz-L&T-Gayatri Consortiumix)    SAFE Consortiumx)     I-N signal Consortium(d): Quality of work is being checked and monitored as per Quality Assurance Plan (QAP) approved by the Project Engineer. Moreover, the materials being used and finished works on the project has to be conforming  to  the  relevant  codes  &   specifications,   which  is   ensuredCont...3/--:3:-through regular periodical testing at site and at in-house/reputed National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) accredited labs, as prescribed in various codes and contractual provisions.(e): Currently, no complaint is existing/pending against above mentioned institutions/ contractors. The estimated cost of these projects is as under:-(i)   EDFC is ` 30,358 crores including  cost of land; and (ii)  WDFC is ` 51,101 crores including cost of land.(f): Most of the construction activities have got adversely affected due to impact of Covid-19 pandemic and imposition of lockdowns. Price escalation on account of time extension necessitated due to Covid/lockdown is payable under Price Variation clauses in contracts.  (g): Total 56.8772 Ha. land has been acquired for approach road mainly for ROBs over DFC alignment in the State of Uttar Pradesh.****
