                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

208






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Thiruvananthapuram Airport




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has awarded or propose to award the services of Thiruvananthapuram Airport to a private company/organisation and if so, the details including present stage thereof;(b) the details of the terms and conditions on which award has been/proposed to be given including the ownership of the land; and(c) whether some issues in regard to the award are yet to be resolved and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement in reply to part (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.208 for 05.03.2020 regarding "Thiruvananthapuram Airport". ; (a) to (c):  Yes Sir. Union Cabinet in its meeting held on 08.11.2018 had accorded "In-principle" approval for leasing out six airports of Airports Authority of India (AAI) namely Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Lucknow, Guwahati, Mangaluru and Thiruvananthapuram under Public Private Partnership  (PPP).  Airports Authority of India floated a Global Tender for inviting bids in respect of these six airports and also proposed to lease out city side land available at these airports for a lease period of 50 years.  The bids of the qualified bidders in respect of Thiruvananthapuram airport were opened on 25.02.2019 and  M/s Adani Enterprises Limited has quoted the highest Per Passenger Fee.   Award of the Thiruvananthapauram Airport to the winning bidder is held up due to pending litigation.
