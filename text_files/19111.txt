                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

682






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Encroachment upon Public Land




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there have been proliferation of encroachments of public land and unauthorised colonies in urban areas in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has promulgated the NCT of Delhi (Special Provisions) Amendment Ordinance 2020 and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the other remedial steps being taken by the Government in this regard; and(e)	whether the Government has taken any initiative to regularize the unauthorized colonies in urban areas of the country and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	to (e) Regulation of land use and town planning are State subjects. The matters related to illegal encroachment on public land and unauthorized colonies and their regularization in urban areas comes within the purview of State/ UT Governments concerned.In order to protect certain forms of unauthorised developments in Delhi from punitive action, the NCT of Delhi Laws (Special Provisions) Second (Amendment) Ordinance 2020 was promulgated on 29.12.2020 under Article 123 (1) of the Constitution, for extending the National Capital of Delhi Laws (Special Provisions) Second Act, 201l for a further period of three years w.e.f 01.01.2021 to 31.12.2023.Government has also notified Pradhan Mantri Unauthorised Colonies in Delhi Awas Adhikar Yojana (PM-UDAY) [National Capital Territory of Delhi (Recognition of Property Rights of Residents in Unauthorised Colonies) Regulations 2019 for conferring / recognising ownership or mortgage / transfer rights to the residents of 1731 unauthorised colonies of Delhi.                                                   ******
