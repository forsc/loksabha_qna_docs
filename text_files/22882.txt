                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3947






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Violations of Environmental Regulations by Steel Manufacturing Factories




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any complaints regarding violations of environment regulations by various steel manufacturing factories in Jharkhand; (b)	if so, the details thereof especially in Bokaro, Dhanbad, Giridih and Ramgarh districts of Jharkhand; and (c)	the action taken by the Government against such Steel manufacturing factories?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c) Seven significant complaints have been received regarding violation of environment regulation by steel manufacturing factories in the Jharkhand. The details of industries against whom the violation of environment regulations have been reported along with action taken against the violators are as follows: S. No. 	Details of Complaints 	Action Taken 1	M/s Electrosteel Steels Limited, at Siyaljori PO-Jogidih, District Bokaro for violation of EIA Notification 2006. 	Legal proceedings have been filed against the responsible persons and M/s Electrosteel Steels Limited under the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, which is under judicial process.2	M/s Saluja Steel and Power Pvt. Ltd., at Manjhiladih, Gadisrirampur, District Giridih	A Committee under the Chairmanship of Director, Department of Industry, Government of Jharkhand has been constituted to undertake the inspection against the raised complainsts. The inspections has been conducted.3	M/s Santupuriya Alloys Pvt. Ltd., at Mohanpur, Mhtodih, District Giridih	4	M/s Venkateshwar Sponge and Iron Pvt. Ltd., at Mahuatand, District Giridih	5	M/s Atibit Industries Company Ltd., at Manjhiladih, Tundi Road, District Giridih	6	M/s Balmukund Sponge and Iron Pvt. Ltd., at Shatro, Manjhiladih, Tundi, DistGiridih	7	Complaints of Shri Ranjeet Pandey & Others of PradushanMuktAbhiyanSamiti, Ramgarh, Jharkhand, dated 15.05.2017 and 27.07.2017  about Jharkhand Ispat (P) Ltd., Dist. Ramgarh. 	Complaint was forwarded to Jharkhand SPCB  and  CPCB-Regional Directorate - Kolkata  for carrying out necessary action. Closure Direction was issued by CPCB vide letter dated 13.02.2018 and after compliance  closure direction it was revoked on 14.11.2018 ***
