                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Modernisation of ESIC Dispensaries




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has taken a decision to modernise Employee’s State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) dispensaries in the country;(b)if so, the details thereof, State-wise including West Bengal;(c)whether some of the ESIC hospitals are still being operated in rental buildings;(d)if so, the details thereof; and (e)the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (b):	The ESIC Dispensaries are adequately equipped with required equipment/medicines. However, ESI Corporation has taken a decision to set up one Dispensary cum Branch Office (DCBO) in every district of the country in a phased manner. List of such DCBOs functional in the country including West Bengal is placed at Annexure.(c):		No ESIC hospital in the country is being operated in rental building.(d) & (e):   Does not arise.*                                                  *******
