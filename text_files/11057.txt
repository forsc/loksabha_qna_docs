                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Off-Budget Expenses




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy













                                                    Gaurav Gogoi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that expenditure of close to Rs. four lakh crores has been recorded as off-budget expenses;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the details of off-budget spending and the sectors the money was targeted towards including the way the Government generated the money for the said off-budget expenses; (c)	the details and value of off-budget liabilities incurred by the Government since 2014; (d)	whether the fiscal deficit for the financial year 2017-18 is at least 0.9 per cent more than that was reported;(e)	if so, the details thereof along with the details of fiscal deficit during the last five years, including off-budget liabilities; and (f)	the details of reasons due to which these liabilities have been included in the budget?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a), (b) and (c) Statement 27 of the Expenditure Profile 2019-20 contains the details of the Extra Budgetary Resources (Government Fully Serviced bonds) of the Government of India since 2016-17. A copy of the said Statement is at Annexure.(d) and (e) No. As per Section 2(a) of the Fiscal responsibility and Budget Management Act, 2003, “fiscal deficit means the excess of total disbursements, from the Consolidated Fund of India, excluding repayment of debt, over total receipts into the Fund, (excluding the debt receipts), during a financial year”.
