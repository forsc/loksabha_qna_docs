                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Promotion of Handicrafts




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps for the promotion of handicrafts in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, particularly in Rajasthan; (c) whether a large number of handicrafts industries have been closed;(d) if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Rajasthan;(e) whether the Government has taken any steps for revival of the closed handicrafts industries; and(f) if so, the time by which the said industries are likely to be revived?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	Yes Sir, The Government of India implements various schemes for promotion and development of handicraft sector and artisans through “National Handicraft Development Programme (NHDP)” and Comprehensive Handicrafts Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) to emphasize integrated approach for development of handicraft in a holistic manner. In the state of Rajasthan the following activities have been undertaken for overall development of artisans:(i)	  02 clusters have been adopted  in the state of Rajasthan under Cluster Development         Mentorship Programme benefitting  1000 	artisans directly at Bikaner and Chaksu .(ii)	 Mega cluster has been undertaken in the state of Rajasthan benefiting approx. 20,000   artisans directly at Jodhpur and Barmer area.(Details enclosed at Annexure-I)(iii)	Approximately 1.18 lakh artisans have been enrolled under PAHACHAN initiative in the state of Rajasthan.  The NHDP has following components:(i)	 Base Line Survey & Mobilization of Artisans under Ambedkar Hastshilp Vikas Yojana(ii)	 Design & Technology Up gradation,(iii)	 Human Resource Development ,(iv)	 Direct Benefit to Artisans,(v)	 Infrastructure and Technology Support,(vi)	 Research and Development,(vii)	 Marketing Support & Services.  The CHCDS has following components(i)	  Mega Cluster(ii)	  Special projects under Integrated Development and Promotion of Handicraft (IDPH).(c) to (f ):	  Do not arise.
