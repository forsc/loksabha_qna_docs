                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1942






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Suicide by Sportspersons




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the number of sportspersons who commit suicide annually across the country; (b)   the details in this regard during the last three years; (c)   the efforts being made by the ministry to prevent it; and (d) the manner in which assistance is being provided by the Government to victim sportspersons? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI KIREN RIJIJU]  (a) & (b): During the last three years, none of the sportspersons supported under sports promotional schemes of the Department of Sports, have been committed suicide.  (c) & (d):  A Call Centre has been established in Sports Authority of India wherein the athletes can call and lodge their grievances for speedy resolution. Further, workshops are organized with trainees to sensitize the sportspersons about various recourses available to them in case they face any kind of harassment.  *****
