                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Development of Kutiya of Mahatma Vidur as Tourist Spot




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Vidur Kuti which is a historical place belonging to the legendary Mahabharata era situated in Bijnor, Uttar Pradesh has been included in the list of 30 places identified to be developed as tourist destination in June, 2018; (b) 	if so, the efforts taken in this direction till date; (c) 	if not, whether there is any plan to develop the Vidur Kuti as a tourist destination; and (d) 	if so, the time by which it is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	The Ministry of Tourism under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme is developing thematic tourist circuits in the country under a planned and prioritised manner. Under the Spiritual Circuit theme of the scheme the Ministry has sanctioned the project Development of Bijnor- Meerut- Kanpur- Kanpur Dehat- Banda- Ghazipur- Salempur- Ghosi- Balia- Ambedkar Nagar- Aligarh- Fatehpur- Deoria- Mahoba- Sonbhadra- Chandauli- Mishrikh- Bhadohi in Uttar Pradesh for Rs. 63.77 crore. in the year 2016-17. Vidhur Kuti in Bijnor is one of the site covered under the above project. The development work at the site includes Landscaping, Solar Lighting, Multipurpose Hall, Changing Rooms, Public toilets, Cafeteria, Benches, Solid waste management and Parking for Rs. 2.53 Crore.********
