                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1951






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Optical Fibres




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total length of optical fibre laid down under Bharat-Net project, especially in rural areas, State/UT-wise; and (b) 	whether the Bharat Broadband Network has any significant Non-Performing Assets and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	BharatNet is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity at all Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country. As on 20.06.2019, a total of 3,40,085 km Optical Fibre Cable (OFC) has been laid connecting 1,29,760 Gram Panchayats (GPs) over OFC, of which 1,19,741 GPs have been made Service Ready.  Further, 828 GPs have been made Service Ready over satellite media. The State/UT-wise details of optical fibre, laid under BharatNet project, are given in Annexure-I.(b)	BharatNet is under implementation and the infrastructure, being created under the project, is a national asset, which is accessible on a non-discriminatory basis to all the Service Providers.
