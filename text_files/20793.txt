                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1865






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Naturopathy Hospitals in Tribal Areas




Sunil Dattatray Tatkare













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the number and details of Naturopathy hospitals operational in the backward tribal areas of the country, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government has set up any Naturopathy hospital in the tribal areas, if so, the details thereof and the number of tribal people who are likely to be benefitted by these Naturopathy hospitals in the country, State/UT-wise;(c) whether these hospitals will act as a training centre for the Tribal Youth to incorporate the skills of Naturopathy and Yoga into their health practices and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government is considering to study tribal Health practices and develop a repository of tribal health practices and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the steps being taken by the Government to train the young tribal men and women to deliver simple Naturopathy treatments which will benefit the entire community in future?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b): As public health is a State subject, setting up Naturopathy Hospitals in the backward tribal areas in the country comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. As such the Ministry of AYUSH does not maintain State/UT wise data of Naturopathy hospitals operational in the country.However, National Institute of Naturopathy (NIN), Pune an autonomous Institute under Ministry of AYUSH has started   a Naturopathy Clinic in Tribal Area of Pune Dist. at Gohe (Bk), Ambegaon for the welfare of the tribal people with particular emphasis on Yoga and Naturopathy with a 20 bed capacity.(c):  Yes, the above tribal unit will act as training centre for the tribal youth to incorporate the skills of Naturopathy and Yoga into their health practices.  NIN in association with Shabari Tribal Finance, Govt. of Maharashtra is offering free training to Tribal youths on Treatment Assistant Training Course (TATC). (d): The Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS), New Delhi an autonomous body of Ministry of AYUSH has been executing a “Tribal Health Care Research programme (THCRP)” in 15 States through 15 CCRAS institutes under Tribal Sub Plan (TSP) the list of the same is given at annexure. Under this programme, the Council is generating awareness about health wellness, and prevention of diseases among the tribal population besides extending medical aid at their door steps. The council also collect information on folk medicines/ Local Health Traditional (LHT) practices prevalent in the tribal area.(e):  NIN in association with Shabari Tribal Finance, Govt. of Maharashtra is offering free training to Tribal youths to deliver Naturopathy treatments.
