                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

205






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Plastic Parks




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether there is any scheme to establish Plastic Parks across the country;(b) If so, the States from which requests have been received in this regard during the lastone year and the current year;(c) the details of the action taken thereon;(d) whether the environment is likely to be impacted adversely by establishing PlasticParks and if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether any survey has been conducted by the Government in this regard and if so,the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINSITER OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) to (e) : A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in parts (a) to (e) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.205 foranswer on 09.03.2021 regarding ‘Plastic Parks’(a) Yes, Sir. The Department of Chemicals and Petrochemicals has been implementingthe Scheme for setting up of Plastic Parks in the country.(b) & (c) The Department had received requests from seven State Governments that isGujarat, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Himachal Pradesh andGoa against the proposal asked by the Department to establish two new PlasticParks. However, preliminary proposals outlining the location, estimated project costand feasibility before due date, was received only from the State Government ofKarnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar and Himachal Pradesh. “In-principle” approval hasbeen accorded for the establishment of Plastic Parks in Karnataka at Ganjimutt,Dakshina Kannada, near Mangaluru and Uttar Pradesh at Gorakhpur based on therecommendations of the Expert Committee which had carried out the technicalevaluation of the preliminary proposals.(d) No, Sir. All units being set up in Plastic Parks have to obtain necessary clearancesand comply with statutory environmental norms.(e) No, Sir.*****
