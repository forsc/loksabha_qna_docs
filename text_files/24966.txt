                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

695






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Anganwadi Centres




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the annual budgetary allocation to Anganwadi Centres in the last five financial years, State/UT-wise, year-wise; (b) 	whether the Government conducts any audit of all the centres annually; and (c) 	if so, the progress made therein so far since its inception, State/UT-wise including Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a): The details of the annual budgetary allocation to Anganwadi Centres in the last five financial years, State/UT-wise, year-wise is at Annexure. (b) to (c): The scheme is implemented by the State Governments.
