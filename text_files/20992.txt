                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Revenue Deficit Compensation




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government intends to provide for  the release of Rs.18,830 crore to Andhra Pradesh as revenue deficit compensation;(b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	If not, the reasons therefor; and (d)	The details of other steps being taken by the Government to financially support the State Governments?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE & CORPORATE AFFAIRS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c):  As per the recommendations of the 15th Finance Commission, Government of India has released the Revenue Deficit Grant of Rs.5,405.59 crore to the State Government of Andhra Pradesh in the current financial year 2020-21(up to February 2021).  (d)	The Union Government provides funds/ assistance to the State Governments under Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS), Central Finance Commission grants and Special Assistance as per the guidelines of the respective Schemes within the availability of Gross Budgetary Support with the Central Government.  Besides, funds are also released directly to implementing agencies under the Central Sector Schemes (CS) for developmental activities in the States. *********
