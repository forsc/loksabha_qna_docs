                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2926






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Drinking Water Connections in Uttar Pradesh




Sangeeta Azad














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of houses in which drinking water connections have been provided under Jal Jeevan Mission in Uttar Pradesh including the number of persons benefited thereby, district-wise; (b) 	the number of drinking water connections in Azamgarh district under the said mission; (c) 	the number and details of persons who are still deprived in Azamgarh district under the said mission; and (d) 	the details of funds allocated in Uttar Pradesh under the said mission, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c) 	Since August, 2019, Government of India in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM), to make provision of tap water supply to every rural household, including  those in rural areas of Uttar Pradesh inter alia which includes Azamgarh, by 2024. The district-wise details of the number of households, including those in Azamgarh district, that are provided with tap water connections and households that are yet to be provided tap water supply in the State of Uttar Pradesh, is annexed.(d) 	Under Jal Jeevan Mission, Rs. 1,206.28 Crore in 2019-20, Rs. 2,570.94 Crore in 2020-21 and Rs. 10,870.50 Crore in 2021-22, have been allocated to Uttar Pradesh. District-wise allocations are not made and maintained by the Department.*****
