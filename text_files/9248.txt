                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Construction of National Highways




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta













                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the total length of National Highways namely two-lane, four-lane, six-lane    and eight-lane, constructed in the country including the scheduled/backward/rural areas during the last five years, State/UT-wise; and(b) the details of the proposals received/executed in this regard during the said period,      State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
