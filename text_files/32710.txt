                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

26






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Nai Roshni Scheme




Talari Rangaiah













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)	the details of funds sanctioned under the Nai Roshni Scheme during the last three years, State-wise; (b) 	the details of funds utilized, out of the allocated funds, during the said period; (c) 	the details of physical and financial progress made so far under the said scheme; and (d) 	whether the Government has taken steps to ensure that beneficiaries have attained socio economic empowerment post training under the scheme and if so, the details thereof?  






ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	to (d) :  A statement is laid on the table of the House.
