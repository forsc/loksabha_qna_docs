                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

976






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Anganwadi Kendras




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of Anganwadi Kendras sanctioned across the country, State-wise; (b) 	the number of most backward districts in the country where Anganwadi Kendras have not been sanctioned as yet; and (c) 	the details of the proposals, if any, of the Government to sanction Anganwadi Kendras in the said backward districts?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	As on 30th June, 2020, State/UT-wise details of Sanctioned Anganwadi Centres across the country are at Annexure-I.(b)	and (c) Anganwadi Services Scheme under Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is  a universal, self-selecting scheme, open to all eligible beneficiaries. All the 14 lakh AWCs/Mini-AWCs/Anganwadi-on Demand (AoDs) approved by Government of India under 3rd phase of expansion of ICDS Scheme have already been allocated to the States/UTs.While issuing the sanction of new Anganwadi Centres, all States/UTS have been advised to locate Anganwadi Centres in the areas as mentioned below:(i)	Villages pre-dominantly inhabited by population belonging to SC/ST and minority community should be given priority(ii)	Within a village, location of AWC, as far as feasible, should be in the areas inhabited by population from SC/ST and minority community.******** Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 976 FOR ANSWER ON 18.09.2020 REGARDING “ANGANWADI KENDRAS” ASKED BY DR.
