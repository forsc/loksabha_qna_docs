                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1494






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






COVID-19 Cases in CAPFs




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of cases of COVID-19 recorded among the members of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF) along with the details of death rate and recovery rate, force-wise; (b) the steps taken by the Government to support the recovery of members of CAPF who have tested positive for COVID-19; and (c) the steps taken by the Government to support the family members and dependents of the CAPF personnel who died of COVID-19 while on duty?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)  The force-wise details of COVID cases, death rate and recovery rate in CAPFs as on 14.09.2020 is given below:- Name of the Force	Number of Covid cases	Deathrate in %	Recovery rate in %BSF 	8934	0.26	80.41CRPF	9158	0.39	84.04CISF	5544	0.43	75.25ITBP	3380	0.21	69.79SSB	3251	0.22	70.77NSG	225	Nil	76.44ARs	1746	0.40	61.63--2--L.S.US. Q. No. 1494 FOR 20.09.2020(b) The Government has established COVID-19 Hospitals, Covid Care Centres & Dedicated COVID Health Centres (DCHC)for treatment and to support the recovery of CAPF personnel, who are found positive for COVID-19.(c) In addition to normal benefits available to a CAPF personnel on death, it has been decided to provide    Rs. 15 Lakh to Next of Kins through “Bharat Ke Veer” funds, in cases CAPF personnel dies due to COVID-19 infection contracted while deployed on Covid-19 related duties. Immediate grant for funeral expenses and support for expeditious processing of family pension and other dues is provided.******
