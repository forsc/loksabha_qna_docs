                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3887






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Agricultural Land under Special Category




Santosh Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                                                        LOK SABHA          UNSTARRED QUESTION No. 3887          TO BE ANSWERED ON 21.12.2021Agricultural Land under Special Category3887.    SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR:Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has set up any institutes for management, development and conservation of land resources in the country including Bihar; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) whether the Government has issued any guidelines to the State Governments to notify agricultural land under the “Special Category” to check its use for any other purpose?                           ****






ANSWER




                                                                                                           ANSWER                  MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT                             (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE) (a) to (c) As per the Seventh Schedule of Constitution of India, ‘land’ falls under the purview of State Government and, therefore, authority to make policies on management, development and conservation of land lies with State Governments only. However, Government of India supplements the efforts of State Governments through various programmes.Government of India has not set up any institution specifically for the management, development and conservation of land resources in the country including Bihar.However, Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR), an autonomous body of Government of India has its regional institutes in the States / UTs (as given in Annexure). These regional institutes conduct basic and strategic researches to develop location - specific, cost effective, eco-friendly conservation and management technologies for sustainable management of natural resources. As per information available, no such direction has been issued to State Governments to notify agricultural land under the “Special Category” to check its use for any other purpose.                                       ****
