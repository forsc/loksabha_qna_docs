                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Conversion of SHs into NHs




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of requests made by Government of Telangana for conversion of State Highways (SHs)/roads into National Highways (NHs) during the last five years and the current year, year-wise;(b)	the steps taken by the Government on each of the said requests;(c) 	the details of proposals accepted; and(d) 	the details of proposals rejected along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)	 The Ministry of Road Transport & Highways keeps on receiving proposals from various State Governments/Union Territories (UTs) etc., including the State Government of Telangana for declaration of State roads, including State Highways (SHs), as new National Highways (NHs).  This Ministry considers declaration of some State roads, including SHs, as new NHs from time to time based on requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority and availability of funds. This Ministry has declared 1366 Km of State roads in Telangana as National Highways since 2014 as per details given in Annexure I.
