                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4302






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Assistance to Karate Federation of India




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is providing any assistance to Karate Federation of India, United Karate Do Association Assam and Barpeta District Karate Do Association for promotion of martial arts and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government is aware that one sportsperson from the district of Barpeta of Assam State got Gold at championship held in Durban, South Africa in 2018; and(c) if so, the details thereof and assistance provided by the Union Government to the said sportsperson and to such other sportspersons?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) This Ministry provides assistance to recognized National Sports Federations (NSFs) under the Scheme of Assistance to National Sports Federations (NSFs). For the sports of Karate, this Ministry has recognized Karate Association of India as National Sports Federation (NSF). Karate Federation of India, United Karate Do Association Assam and Barpeta District Karate Do Association are not recognized by this Ministry as National Sports Federations and hence no assistance is provided to them.(b) & (c) Yes Sir. Karate Association of India has informed that Nishad Ali, who is from Barpeta District has won Gold Medal in 9th Commonwealth Karate Championship, 2018 held at Durban, South Africa. As per the norms of the Scheme for Assistance to National Sports Federations (NSFs), financial assistance is only provided to recognized National Sports Federations (NSFs) and not to individual sportsperson. Accordingly, no financial assistance was provided by Government of India for participation of said sportsperson or to such other sportspersons.*****
