                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3383






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Restarting Cooked Meals in Trains




Dayakar Pasunoori













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways has decided to restart cooked meal in trains, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Railway Board has asked the zonal railways to verify catering and other charges;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Railways also proposes to provide bed rolls in trains; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): Ministry of Railways have issued instructions on 19.11.2021 to resume the services of cooked food in trains. The service of Ready To Eat meals will also continue.(b) and (c): Yes, Sir. Verification of catering charges was to be done as a onetime measure for feeding catering charges in Passenger Reservation System for the trains having prepaid catering service. This is a standard protocol being followed and these instructions are being complied by Zonal Railways.(d) and (e): As a part of passenger amenities linen/ blankets/ curtains are provided in AC coaches of Indian Railways. However, with the outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic, as precautionary measures, linen/ blankets/ curtains were discontinued inside the trains. In terms of letter no. TC-II/2910/2020/SOP-Rail-Covid 19 dated 10.03.2022, instructions for discontinuation of linen/ curtains have been withdrawn. Action has been initiated by Zonal Railways to start linen/ curtain at the earliest.*****
