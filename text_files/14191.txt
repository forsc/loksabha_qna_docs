                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2963






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Post Offices




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of post offices in the country in villages and cities separately, State/UT-wise; (b) 	the number of new post offices opened at village, tehsil and at district level during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise; (c) 	the budget allocated for this purpose during the said period; (d) 	the population required for opening a post office facility as per the International norms along with the ranking of the country in this regard; (e) 	the details of various types of public facilities available in the post offices; and (f) 	the number of post offices digitized in the country, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) 	Sir, the number of post offices in the country in villages and cities separately State/UT-wise is at Annexure-I.(b) & (c) 	The number of new post offices opened at villages, tehsil and at district level during each of the last 3 years State-UT-wise is at Annexure-II.During the said period, the year wise budget allocated is as under:<pre>2016-17	2017-18	2018-19? 1.00 Cr.	?3.16 Cr.	?3.8415 Cr.</pre>(d) 	The Government is not aware of any established population based international norms for providing facility of post office.(e) 	Post offices provided for postal, banking, insurance & other facilities. An indicative list of various types of public facilities (products/ services) available in the post offices is at Annexure-III.(f)  	The number of post offices digitized in the country State/UT-wise is at Annexure-IV.
