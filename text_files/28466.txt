                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Mid Day Meal Scheme




Navneet Ravi Rana













                                                    Dileshwar Kamait
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the mid day meal along with components (raw material, cookhonorarium etc) for each student, year-wise and State-wise particularly ofParliamentary Constituency, Amravati;(b) whether the Government has conducted any survey or complied to any surveyfor minimum necessary amount to provide nutrition food to each child and if so,the details thereof;(c) whether during the complete lockdown due to global corona pandemic onlineclasses were arranged in all the Government schools across the countryparticularly in Maharashtra including Amravati;(d) if so, the details of the assistance provided by the Union Government to theStates for online education; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) : Central Government has approved the Centrally Sponsored Scheme “PradhanMantri Poshan Shakti Nirman (PM POSHAN)” earlier known as ‘NationalProgramme for Mid-Day Meal in Schools’ for providing one hot cooked meal inGovernment and Government – aided Schools from 2021-22 to 2025-26. Thescheme is implemented uniformly with same components across the countryincluding the Parliamentary Constituency Amravati. The details of componentsof the scheme are given at Annexure.(b): As per the Schedule II of National Food Security Act, 2013, the followingnutritional content based on research, is prescribed in the Scheme:<pre>Components	Primary	Upper PrimaryCalories	450 Cal	700 CalProtein	12 gms.	20 gms.</pre>The above nutritional norms are met by providing the following food items to thechildren of primary and upper primary classes under the scheme all over thecountry:<pre>S. No.	Items	Primary Stage	Upper Primary Stage		Food norms (in gms)	Food norms (in gms)1.	Foodgrains (Rice / Wheat)	100	1502.	Pulses	20	303.	Vegetables (Leafy & Others)	50	754.	Oil & Fat	5	7.55.	Salt & Condiments	As per need	As per need</pre>(c) to (e): A comprehensive initiative “PM eVIDYA” has been initiated as part ofAtma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan on 17th May, 2020, unifies all efforts related todigital/online/on-air education to enable multi-mode access to education. Thisinitiative includes the following:(i) DIKSHA (one nation, one digital platform) is the nation’s digitalinfrastructure for providing quality e-content for school education instates/UTs and QR coded Energized Textbooks for all grades are availableon it.(ii) One earmarked Swayam Prabha TV channel per class from 1 to 12 (oneclass, one channel).(iii) Extensive use of Radio, Community radio and CBSE Podcast- ShikshaVani.(iv) Special e-content for visually and hearing impaired developed on DigitallyAccessible Information System (DAISY) and in sign language on NIOSwebsite/ YouTube.All the above schemes/programmes are available to all the students across the nation.For connecting all the rural schools in the state of Maharashtra, online education,audio lessons through Radio, Video lessons on TV Channels, DD Sayadri etc arewidely used by the students to continue learning during the pandemic period.***
