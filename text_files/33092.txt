                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

539






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






NCC Battalions




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to raise more NCC battalions in Kerala so as to allot NCC units in each school and college, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether there is any proposal received from Government of Kerala during the year 2014 and thereafter to raise a new NCC Battalion in Kasaragod Constituency;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the sanction of his Ministry has been issued in this regard; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons for the delay in according sanction particularly when the New Education Policy encourage opening of NCC wings in schools?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a)  Currently there is no proposal for raising new NCC battalions in Kerala.  However, two NCC Army Battalions and one NCC Air Force unit were raised in Kerala State since 2011 under 2 lakh cadet expansion plan.  Also, under the scheme of expansion of NCC in Border/Coastal talukas, four units of NCC were upgraded by allotting additional cadet strength.(b) to (e): As per available records, no proposal was received from the Government of Kerala for raising new NCC units in Kasaragod district, during the year 2014 or afterwards. *****
