                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

99






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Financial Assistance to Rajasthan




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details  of category wise number of people benefitted by financial assistance and loans in Rajasthan from the financial package released by the Union Government during corona pandemic till date, district-wise; (b) 	whether the   Government had fixed any target for sanctioning loan by banks to various categories of people including cart-puller and shopkeepers  under Financial Package for corona; and (c) 	if so, the details of circulars/ orders issue in respect of Rajasthan in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c): The Union Government has announced ‘Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan’ amounting to Rs 20 lakh crore- equivalent to 10% of India’s GDP. As per data reported by Public Sector Banks, an amount of Rs.4,266 crore has been sanctioned to 1,57,549 borrowers and an amount of Rs.3,460 crore disbursed to 1,01,713 borrowers under Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS), as on 05.09.2020, in the State of Rajasthan.  Category-wise and District-wise data in this regard is not maintained. The scheme would be applicable till 31.10.2020, or till an amount of Rs.3,00,000 crore is sanctioned, whichever is earlier.  As part of the Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan around 12.47 lakh Kisan Credit Cards (KCC) have been issued to the farmers including PM KISAN Beneficiaries, as on 04.09.2020. This will facilitate credit to the farmers of Rajasthan to the extent limit sanctioned under KCC. Under the Prime Minister Street Vendor’s AatmaNirbhar Nidhi (PMSVANidhi) launched on 01.06.2020 to provide micro credit facility  to the street vendors whose businesses have been affected due to COVID-19 lock down, as on 09.09.2020 a total of 26,667 street vendors have applied for loan, 4,940 loans have been sanctioned  and 386 loans disbursed in the State of Rajasthan.  The scheme targets to benefit over 50 lakh street vendors, across the country, who have been vending on or before March 24, 2020 in urban areas including those from surrounding development / peri-urban/rural areas,. No state/UT-wise, Bank-wise or Category-wise targets of street vendors have been fixed under the scheme.*****
