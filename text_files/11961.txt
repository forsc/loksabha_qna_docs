                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Ship Breaking Industry




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps to promote ship breaking industry in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has taken any steps to attract the foreign ships in the country and promote the industry in a eco-friendly way; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)  Yes Sir. India has acceded to the Hong Kong International Convention for Safe and Environmentally Sound Recycling of Ships, 2009 on 28.11.2019. The Recycling of Ships Act, 2019, which contains legal framework for implementation of provisions of the Hong Kong Convention has been enacted. The Gujarat Maritime Board is also implementing a project for upgradation of the environment management plan at Alang-Sosiya ship recycling yard, which too will facilitate environmentally sound and safe recycling of ships and thereby help to attract  foreign ships for recycling in India.                                             ******
