                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

519






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Promotion of Fisheries in Karnataka




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry of Commerce and Industry has been taking any initiative to promote and protect the growth of the fisheries business in the State of Karnataka; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the funds released till now for the development and growth of the business pertinent to fisheries, along with the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) Yes, Sir. As per the details received from the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, the Marine Products Export Development Authority (MPEDA) is implementing following schemes in the country including the State of Karnataka for increasing aquaculture production, upgrading infrastructure for value addition of seafood, quality assurance and market promotion for export of marine products:(i)	Market Promotion(ii)	Support for high end/innovative value addition for exports.(iii)	Certification for export traceability of wild caught and farmed products. (iv)	Export oriental aquaculture technology incubation.(v)	Quality Assurance   (c) The details of funds released by MPEDA under the above schemes for the development and growth of the business pertinent to fisheries in the State of Karnataka during the last five years are given below:
