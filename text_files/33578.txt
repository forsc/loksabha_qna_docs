                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1000






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Operation Flood’s Phase II




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that the Government is considering to launch phase-II of Operation Flood;(b) whether it is true that the phase-II of Operation Flood was limited to only one district in the entire nation as against the Operation Flood was carried throughout the length and breadth of the country which really helped the country to achieve self sufficiency in milk; (c) if so the details thereof; and (d) the details of target and funds earmarked for the phase-II of operation Flood?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) to (d) No, Sir. There is no such proposal namely phase II of operation Flood is under consideration of Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying.  However, this Department is implementing following schemes for Dairy Development across the country:1)	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)2)	Dairy processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF)3)	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities (SDCFPO)****
