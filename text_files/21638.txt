                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2945






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Expansion of CGHS facilities




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to expand CGHS facilities across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is contemplating upon opening CGHS hospitals in metropolitan cities; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d): CGHS Wellness Centers  at new locations are opened as per  extant  norms and subject to availability of resources. At present, there is no proposal to open CGHS hospitals in metropolitan cities.
