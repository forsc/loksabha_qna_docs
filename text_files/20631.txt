                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1838






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Housing Shortage




Pratima Bhoumik













                                                    Virendra  Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has assessed the shortage of housing 	units in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof including the estimated shortage of 	housing units at the end of year 2018; (c) 	whether the Government has set any target for the construction 	of housing units by 2019-2020; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof including the number of units to be 	constructed under the said target and the total amount of funds 	likely to be spent on it?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)&(b) Yes, Sir. Based on the demand survey assessment carried out by States/Union Territories (UTs),  under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U), the housing shortage in urban areas had been assessed at around 112 lakh.(c)&(d) PMAY-U Mission has strategized to complete the construction of houses sanctioned under the Mission by the year 2022.So far, 110 lakh houses have been sanctioned under the PMAY-U, out of which 71.4 lakh are under construction and about 41.7 lakh had been completed/ delivered. The central assistance during the duration of the scheme will be about Rs.1.77 lakh crore.                                                  *****
