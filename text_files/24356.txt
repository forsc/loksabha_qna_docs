                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

91






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Eklavya Residential Schools




Tokheho Yepthomi













                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has sanctioned the establishment of Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) in the country, State-wise specially in Karnataka and Nagaland;(b) if so, the details of the EMRS sanctioned, operational, under construction and yet to be completed in the country in last two years, State-wise;(c) whether it is a fact that Government has given sanction for the establishment of EMRS in the State of Karnataka, if so, the details thereof;(d) is there any plan to start new EMRS-Eklavya schools in Koppal District and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of the Tribal Hostels approved for the staff of Nagaland in 2020-2021?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA) : (a): Yes Sir.  (b): A statement indicating state-wise numbers of EMRS sanctioned, operational, under construction and yet to be completed in the country in last two years is at Annexure-A. (c) and (d): As per budget announcement 2018-19, every block with 50% or more tribals and at least 20,000 tribal persons is to be sanctioned an EMRS by the year 2022.  Based on these criteria 452 tribal blocks of the country have been identified for establishment of EMRSs and no block of Koppal District qualifies the above criteria.  However, a total number of 12 EMRSs have already been sanctioned for the state of Karnataka as per list as per Annexure-B.(e):  The layout plan for construction of an EMRS across the country including Nagaland, interalia, includes construction of academic building, hostels for students, kitchen and dining hall, staff quarter etc. . Therefore, no separate approval is required in respect of tribal hostel for the staff of EMRSs. Annexure A as mentioned in the reply to part (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Qs.
