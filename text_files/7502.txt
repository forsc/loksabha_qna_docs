                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1195






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Integrated Tourism Circuits for Kerala




Hibi Eden













                                                    Abdul Majeed Ariff
                                                








                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has sanctioned six integrated circuits to Kerala under Spiritual, Rural and Eco-Tourism themes under Swadesh Darshan and if so, the details thereof along with their status; (b) 	whether the Government has received any proposal to include Kerala’s Coastal Circuit under Swadesh Darshan Scheme; (c) 	if so, the details thereof along with the decision taken thereon and if not, the reasons therefor; (d) 	the details of the tourist destinations in Ernakulam District of Kerala State which are covered under the above circuits; and (e) 	the funds allocated for development of spiritual circuit under Swadesh Darshan Scheme in Kerala including Alappuzha?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	The details of projects sanctioned by the Ministry of Tourism in Kerala under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme –Integrated Development of theme based tourist circuits in the country along with the status is at annexure.No Proposal from the State Government of Kerala under the Coastal Circuit Theme of the scheme is under consideration of the Ministry.In Ernakulam district, destinations taken up for development includes: Thirumuppam Mahadev Temple, Muslim Jama-ath Mosque, St. Mary’s Basillica Church, Mar Behanan’s Dayara Chapel, Moozhikulam Sree Lakshmana Perumal Temple, Payammal Sree Shathruknha Swaamee Temple and St. Thomas Church.*******
