                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

334






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






COVID-19 Vaccinations Centres




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of a study which indicates that 89 percent people desired to have the operational hours of vaccination centres extended beyond 9 am to 5 pm and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government proposes to provide vaccination round the clock to accelerate the administration of second dose and help informal sector workers along with those in full day employment to get vaccinated; and (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	to (c) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 334* FOR 25TH MARCH, 2022(a) to (c): The specific study referred to in the question has not come to the notice of Government of India. The timings of COVID-19 Vaccination Centers (CVCs) are subject to demand and requirement at a particular place where the CVC is situated and can be decided by the hospital authorities and/or district administration. Government of India has issued advisories to all States/UTs informing no rigid timing has been set for operationalization of CVCs and it can be flexible based on the availability of human resource, infrastructure, and number of beneficiaries due for vaccination.*****
