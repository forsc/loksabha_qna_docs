                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Modernisation Programme for Anganwadi Centres




Kirron  Kher













                                                     Sunil Kumar Pintu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has completed the modernisation programme of all the Anganwadi Centres across the country with a view to mobilise rural women and children to increase their participation in community development and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Punjab and Bihar;(b) 	whether the modernised Anganwadi Centres also follow the ICDS programme to combat nutritional deficiency and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	whether the Government is partnering with the private sector to successfully modernise Anganwadi Centres across the country and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor, State/UT-wise including Punjab and Chandigarh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)  	& (b): Providing modern and essential facilities to cater the needs of women and children at the Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) is a continuing process.  A number of steps in this direction have been undertaken in all States including Punjab and Bihar, some of which are as under:(i)	Revised joint guidelines have been issued by Ministries of Women & Child Development, Rural Development and Panchayati Raj on 17.02.2016 for construction of 4 Lakhs AWC buildings across the country under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in convergence with Anganwadi Services (ICDS Scheme). The State-wise statement is at Annexure-I.(ii)	Drinking water facilities have been provided in 87.4% Anganwadi Centres.(iii)	Sanitation facilities have been provided in 71.98% Anganwadi Centres.(iv)	Grants are sanctioned for purchase of water filter for serving safe drinking water, furniture, equipments, etc. (v)	Swachhta Action Plan – Rs.12,000/-per AWC are given for providing drinking water facilities and Rs.10,000/- per AWC for providing toilet facilities.(vi)	As on 30.06.2019, 3.54 lakh Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) have been provided with Smart Phones for IT-enabled monitoring under ICDS-Common Application Software (CAS).  State/UT-wise statement is at Annexure-II.(vii)	Under POSHAN Abhiyaan 11,51,517 weighing machines and 5,37,187 Stadiometer have been provided for growth monitoring. (viii)	Funds for ECCE have been enhanced to Rs 5,000 per AWC.  Activity books have been introduced in the Scheme.  (ix)	For nutritional deficiency, norms prescribed in Schedule-II of NFSA, 2013, are followed: Sl. No.	Categories	Revised norms (per beneficiary per day)		Calories (K Cal)	Protein (g)1.	Children (6-72 months)	500	12-152.	Severely malnourished children (6-72 months)	800	20-253.	Pregnant women and Nursing mothers 	600	18-20 (c):  	A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed between Ministry of Women & Child Development and M/s Vedanta on 21st September, 2015 for construction of 4,000 Anganwadi Centres by M/s Vedanta, through its own resources, as part of their Corporate Social Responsibilities (CSR) primarily in the States of Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Rajasthan, Telangana and Uttar Pradesh.         			M/s. Vedanta has already started construction of Anganwadi buildings in Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh.
