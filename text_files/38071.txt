                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4680






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Upgradation of State Highways as National Highways




Pritam Gopinath Munde














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received proposals from various State Governments for declaration of State Highways (SHs) as National Highways (NHs) during the last three years and the current year and if so, the details and the present status thereof, State-wise;(b) the details and the present status of the proposals which are found eligible as per the laid down criteria for declaring as NHs, State-wise including Maharashtra; and(c) the time by which these proposals are likely to be approved?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) The Ministry keeps on receiving proposals from various State Governments / Union Territories (UTs) etc., including from the State Government of Maharashtra, for declaration of State roads, including State Highways (SHs), as new National Highways (NHs). The Ministry considers declaration of some State roads, including SHs, as NHs from time to time based on the requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority and availability of funds.State/UT-wise details of State Roads, including SHs, converted to NHs, including for the State of Maharashtra, during the last three years and the current year are annexed.
