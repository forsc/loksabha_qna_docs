                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2810






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Nasal Vaccine for COVID-19




Nihal Chand Chauhan













                                                    Jaskaur Meena
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to manufacture a nasal vaccine to protect against COVID-19;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the tests done and the outcome in this regard;(c) whether the Government has adequate mechanism to carry out the vaccination across the country in a timely manner, if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government is working on a scheme for prevention from this deadly disease in near future and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation (CDSCO), under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare in consultation with Subject Expert Committee (SEC), has granted permission on 12.02.2021 to M/s Bharat Biotech International Limited for conduct of Phase-I clinical trial of Chimpanzee Adenovirus Vectored COVID-19 Vaccine (BBV154) for Intranasal route of administration.(c):	Yes. The COVID-19 vaccination has been ongoing across the country since 16.01.2021 using the infrastructure available under Universal Immunization Programme which has further been augmented through inclusion of Private COVID-19 Vaccination Centres (CVCs) from 01.03.2021.(d):	The Integrated Disease Surveillance Programme (IDSP) under National Center for Disease Control, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare is mandated with disease surveillance activities through establishment of surveillance units at Centre, State and District level along with a laboratory based IT enabled disease surveillance system for epidemic prone diseases to monitor disease trends and to detect and respond to outbreaks in early rising phase, so that timely and effective public health actions can be initiated in response to public health threats like COVID-19. Also under the Integrated Health Information Platform (IHIP), a web-enabled near-real-time electronic information system, has been launched to provide state-of-the-art single operating picture with geospatial information for managing disease outbreaks and related resources.       Further in context of COVID-19 prevention, a number of advisories and SOPs have been issued by Ministry of Health & Family Welfare wherein adherence to COVID appropriate behavior i.e. physical distancing, use of mask, hand hygiene has been recommended. Government of India has also been initiated COVID-19 vaccination initiative since 16.01.2021.
