                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Setting up of Industry for Educated Youth




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the Central schemes to provide employment to the growing number of well-educated youth in the country;(b) the action taken by the Government on the request of local MP who had demanded to build/create an industry like Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. on the available 373 hectares of land under Osmanabad Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) to provide employment to this section of unemployed youth during zero hour on 21 June 2019; and(c) the steps being taken by the Government to stop the migration of well educated unemployed youth from Osmanabad district to other districts due to unseasonal rains, drought hit farming, under developed MIDC and absence of employment opportunities?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES               (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)                     ********(a):   All PSUs and units of Heavy Industry recruit youths as per requirement.(b):   Setting up of industries is a state subject.(c):   Does not pertain to the Department of Heavy Industry.                    ***********
