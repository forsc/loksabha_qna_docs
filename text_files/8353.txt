                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1863






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






In-Vitro Fertilization




Supriya Sadanand Sule













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the details of total number of registered and unregistered In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF) clinics functioning in the country, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government has formulated any guidelines for the functioning of these clinics, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether these clinics indulge in unfair practices and duped married couples, if so, the number of complaints received against these IVF centres and the action taken by the Government against such centres; (d) whether only a small number of IVF centres are opened/operational in the Government hospitals across the country; (e) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (f) whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Governments to establish IVF centres under National Health Mission and if so, the action taken by the Government thereon and the present status of the said proposal?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY) (a) Details are annexed as Annexure.  	(b):     Yes. National Guidelines for Accreditation, Supervision and Regulation of Artificial Reproductive Technique (ART) Clinics in India, have been formulated by Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR).These guidelines have been circulated to all States/UTs for follow-up. (c):    The National Registry of ICMR, received complaints against 15 enrolled ART Clinics. Status of these complaints is as under:-i.  Complaints found incorrect       – 9ii  Complaints under investigation   -4iii  Complaints found factual          - 2and enrollment of clinics cancelled(d) & (e):     Yes. As per database of National Registry of ART Clinics and Banks in India, of ICMR, only two ART Clinics are enrolled under National Registry. Eight clinics are under process of enrolment.(f):       Yes, a proposal for establishment of IVF centre at EK Nayanar Memorial Women & Child Hospital at Mangattuparamba,  Kannur District in the National Health Mission –PIP 2016-17 (Innovations) of Kerala State, was received and has been processed.
