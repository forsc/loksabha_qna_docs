                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2006






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Contribution of Small and Medium Industries in Manufacturing Sector




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Small and Medium Industries play an important role in the manufacturing sector of the country;(b) 	if so, the percent-wise contribution of small and medium industries in the total production of manufacturing sector during the year 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21;(c) 	whether certain units of Small and Medium Industries have been closed also during the aforesaid period; and (d) 	if so, the number of units closed during respective years of the aforesaid period and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b): 	Yes, Sir. The contribution of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises’ (MSME) manufacturing Gross Value Added (GVA) in all India manufacturing GVA (at current prices 2011-12) during 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 was 40.38 %, 40.38 % and 40.40 % respectively.(c) & (d): 	As MSMEs are present both in formal and informal sector, data regarding units closed during respective years of the aforesaid period are not maintained in this office.
