                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






One Rupee Clinic




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh













                                                    Kaushalendra Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether it is true that a ‘one rupee clinic’ is being operated at the Thane Railway Station by a private organization and this experiment is proving very successful;(b)  if so, whether any provision is being made to operate this kind of clinics at the other big railway stations of the country;(c) whether any arrangement has been made in the said clinics for the treatment of all types of patients; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) Yes, Sir. The ‘One Rupee Clinic’ at Thane Railway Station has been established by M/s Magicdil on open tender basis. So far, the performance of Emergency Medical Rooms has been found satisfactory.(b) to (d): Yes, Sir. As per orders of Hon’ble Bombay High Court, clinics have been established at 24 suburban stations of Mumbai run by non-railway medical professional groups. Such clinics have also been established at 29 other railway stations in the country. These clinics can attend any patient besides rendering emergency medical care to bona-fide railway passengers.*****
