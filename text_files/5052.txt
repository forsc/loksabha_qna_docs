                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Financial Assistance to Students




Ganesan Selvam













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government provides financial assistance to meritorious students of weaker sectionto pursue higher studies under the Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship scheme for College andUniversity Students; and(b) if so, the details thereof along with the quantum of financial assistance provided during each ofthe last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) to (b): The Ministry of Human Resource Development has been implementing the ‘CentralSector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students’ since the year 2008. Theobjective of the scheme is to provide financial assistance to meritorious students from weakersections having family income of less than Rs. 8 lakhs per annum. Students who are above 80thpercentile of successful candidates in class XII can apply for scholarship under this scheme.. Therate of scholarship is Rs. 10,000/- per annum for the first three years and Rs. 20,000/- per annumfor the fourth and fifth year. The details of the financial assistance provided during each of the lastthree years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu is at Annexure.****
