                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

170






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Summit




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed













                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether India participated in the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Summit held recently in Bishkek (Kyrgyzstan); (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the details of the countries which participated in the said summit; (c)	the details of leaders of different countries with whom India held meetings outside the SCO Summit at Bishkek along with the issues discussed and consensus arrived at the meetings;(d)	whether leaders of different countries have asked India and Pakistan to resolve issues bilaterally and start discussion; and (e)	if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS(DR. SUBRAHMANYAM JAISHANKAR)(a) to (e)	A statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (E) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*170 REGARDING SHANGHAI COOPERATION ORGANISATION (SCO) SUMMIT TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.07.2019	The SCO Summit was held in Bishkek on June 13-14, 2019.  All the SCO member States- India, Kazakhstan, China, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, Russia, Tajikistan and Uzbekistan- participated in the Summit.  The Indian delegation was led by Hon’ble Prime Minister.  SCO Observer States- Iran, Afghanistan, Belarus and Mongolia- joined the Members in the expanded session of the SCO Summit in Bishkek.   	On the sidelines of the SCO Summit, Prime Minister held meetings with President Xi Jinping of China, President Vladimir Putin of Russia and President Ashraf Ghani of Afghanistan. After the SCO Summit, Prime Minister and President Sooronbay Jeenbekov of the Kyrgyz Republic had a bilateral Summit meeting. During this meeting there was in-depth exchange of views between India and Kyrgyzstan on bilateral relations, international and regional issues of mutual interest. 	There was no mention of bilateral relations between India and Pakistan during the SCO Summit.  						***
