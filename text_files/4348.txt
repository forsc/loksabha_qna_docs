                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4077






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Industrial Clusters




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether China is interested in developing clusters in Telangana including incubation centre with Joint Ventures; and (b)	 if so, the details thereof and role of NASSCOM in this regard in Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ???????????? ( ???? ??? ??????) THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE &INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):	Yes, Sir. The Government of Telangana has received proposals from few Chinese Companies who have shown interest in developing Electronics Manufacturing Clusters in Telangana. The proposals are under examination and no final decision has been taken.(b):	None of the above proposals have been received through NASSCOM.*****
