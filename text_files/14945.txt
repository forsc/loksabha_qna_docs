                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3571






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Funds for Maintenance of Hindu Religious Places




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government provides funds for the maintenance and management of Hindu religious places and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the funds received as donations at these religious places are spent on Hindu Religious places along with spending them for other social development works; and (c)	 if so, whether there is any system/rule/Act in place for utilizing the said funds for the development of persons belonging to some other beliefs and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Funds are provided through budgetary allocation for conservation of protected monuments under the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) including for Hindu religious places. The details of expenditure incurred for conservation of protected monuments in the country including Hindu religious places,  during the last three years are given below:  Sl. No.	Year	Expenditure incurred (Rs. in Lakhs)1	2016-17	30129.372	2017-18	41076.793	2018-19	40502.01(b)   &  (c)	There is no control of ASI over the donations received by the any religious place under the protection of ASI. ********
