                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1463






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Agricultural Centres in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the reasons for the delay in functioning of e-NAM facility sanctioned in 2015 for Siliguri Regulated Market, despite having all the infrastructure set-up ready; (b) 	the reasons why the Pineapple Development Centre at Bidhan Nagar (Darjeeling district), inaugurated in 2011, has not been functional till date; and (c) 	the details of Central Government funding/assistance for development of agriculture in Darjeeling, Kalimpong, and North Dinajpur districts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Proposal/ Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) for integration of total 17 mandis which also included Siliguri Regulated Market, as received from Government of West Bengal for integration of aforesaid mandis with National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) platform, had been approved by Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) of Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW), Government of India, on 28.03.2017 for sanction of one time grant. Based on the preparedness of the State with sufficient computers & other hardwares and requisite manpower in the mandis, the aforesaid mandis of West Bengal had been integrated with e-NAM platform in March, 2018.(b):    As informed by Government of West Bengal, the infrastructure at Pineapple Development Centre facility is completed and one M/s Himalayan Foods Pvt. Ltd. has been selected by Siliguri & Jalpaiguri Development Authority (SJDA) as the O&M partner. There was a demand from the local pineapple merchants for construction of an approach road to the facility. SJDA has already initiated process for construction of approach road and the centre shall come into operation after an inception meeting with all stakeholders of the centre. This has been held up due to present pandemic situation. (c):	   The details of Central Government funding/assistance for development of agriculture in Darjeeling, Kalimpong, and North Dinajpur districts is as under: Contd…2/- -2-National Food Security Mission (NFSM)1)	Darjeeling: Rs. 10.08 lakh (coarse cereal, nutria, cereal)2)	Kalimpong: Rs. 8.5 lakh (coarse cereal, nutria, cereal)3)	Uttar Dinajpur: Rs. 376.49 lakh ((coarse cereal, rice, pulse)Rastriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)1)	Darjeeling & Kalimpong: Rs. 20.57 lakh 2)	Uttar Dinajpur: Rs. 504.7 lakh Soil Conservation 1)	Darjeeling: Rs. 80.40 lakh 2)	Kalimpong: Rs. 71.13 lakh Bringing Green Revolution to Eastern India (BGREI)1)	Siliguri Sub-Division: Rs. 110.30702 lakhUttar Dinajpur: Rs. 25. 16884 lakh******
