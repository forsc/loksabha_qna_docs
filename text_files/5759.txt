                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

471






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Khelo India Scheme




Margani Bharat













                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Khelo India scheme is being implemented across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it has been launched in Rajahmundry in Andhra Pradesh also and if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor and the time by which the scheme is likely to be implemented there?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }*****(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *471 TO BE ANSWERED ON 25.07.2019 REGARDING “KHELO INDIA SCHEME’ ASKED BY SHRI MARGANI BHARAT AND SHRI ADALA PRABHAKARA REDDY, HON’BLE MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT, LOK SABHA.(a) Yes Sir, the Khelo India Scheme is being implemented for the entire country.(b) State-wise details of infrastructure projects sanctioned, funds released, talents identified, academies accredited and Sports Authority of India (SAI) Khelo India Centres under Khelo India scheme are given in Annexure – I, Annexure- II , Annexure – III, Annexure - IV and Annexure - V respectively.(c) and (d) “Sports” being a State subject the responsibility of promotion of sports, including in Rajahmundry in Andhra Pradesh, rests with the Government of Andhra Pradesh. The Central Government is supplementing the effors of the State Government. While the Khelo India Scheme is not district specific, athletes from across the country, including from Rajahmundry in Andhra Pradesh, are eligible to participate in Khelo India Games and benefit through training facilities and sports infrastructure set up by State/Central Government.*****
