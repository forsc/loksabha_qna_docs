                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

311






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Diversion of Government Allotted Rice




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that rice allotted for the State of Meghalaya was diverted to a private ware house, if so, the action taken thereon; (b) 	the quantity and amount of rice/wheat allotted for the Government of Meghalaya yearly for the last five years; (c) 	the process and formula of calculations base for the allotment of rice and wheat to the State Government; and (d) 	whether any enquiries are pending against any States regarding diversion of Rice and wheat for the last five years, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI) (a): 	Of-late a report had appeared in the media (The Shillong Times) titled “Mysterious and non-mysterious silence” alleging seizure of one lakh bags of rice weighing 50 kg each by the Assam Police from a private godown in Chhaygoan, Boko meant for Meghalaya Social Welfare Department under the Wheat Based Nutrition Programme (WBNP), and as per the State Govt. of Meghalaya this matter does not relate to the Public Distribution System (PDS). (b): 	The Department allocates foodgrains (Rice/Wheat) to all States/UTs, including Meghalaya, for distribution under various schemes i.e. National Food Security Act (NFSA), NFSA-Tide Over, PM-Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PM-GKAY), Atma Nirbhar Bharat Scheme (ANBS) and Other Welfare Schemes (OWS). A statement showing scheme wise allotment during the last five years is enclosed at Annexure-I. (c):  	Under NFSA, the foodgrains are allocated to States/UTs according to number of Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) households and number of Priority Households (PHH) beneficiaries in the State/UT, basis the respective scale of issuance i.e. 35 Kg per AAY Household per month and 5 Kg per PHH beneficiary per month. Besides, some additional foodgrains as tide-over quota to some States for covering some additional population over and above NFSA coverage. Further, for distribution of additional 5 Kg per person per month to all NFSA beneficiaries (over and above normal NFSA foodgrains) additional quota of foodgrains (Rice/Wheat) are allocated under separate PMGKAY scheme. And other scheme allocations as per the requirements of the State Govt.
