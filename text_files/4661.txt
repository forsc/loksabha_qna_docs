                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Encouragement to New Entrepreneurs




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government encourages new entrepreneurs with structural and administrative setup under Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs); (b) 	if so, the details of new entrepreneurs registered since 2014 to till-date, State/UT and year-wise; (c) 	the details of initiatives of the Government for the jute and handloom weavers under MSMEs projects; and (d) 	the details of incentives, structural, technological support to MSME industries on manufacture and procurement benefits for farmers who grow jute and weavers of handloom of the country for these purposes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a) to(d):	The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises implements various schemes and programmes for promotion and development of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) across the country. These include Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry and Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE), Credit Guarantee Scheme, Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS), Technology Centre Systems Programme (TCSP), Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), etc. The details of entrepreneurs registered since 2014 to till-date, State/UT-wise and year-wise, are given in Annexure-I & II.  The Scheme and programmes of the Ministry of MSME are for promotion and development of all sectors of MSMEs including jute and handloom weavers.
