                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

548






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Stoppage at Bagdogra




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any new steps are being taken to create train stoppages at Bagdogra, Naxalbari, Phansidewa, Chopra and Thakurganj.(b)	if so, whether the Ministry is aware that this route caters to the most important Agricultural, Horticultural and Animal Husbandry belt in Darjeeling Terai Region; and (c)	if so, the potential steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Ministry to address the transportation of Agricultural and Dairy products from this belt to the rest of the nation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) Provision of stoppages of trains on Indian Railways, is an on-going process, subject to operational feasibility, traffic justification, etc. Accordingly, stoppage of 15721/15722 Digha- New Jalpaiguri Paharia Express has been provided at Thakurganj station w.e.f. 10.03.2019. Further, both Bagdogra & Naksalbari are well served by 03 pairs of Mail/Express trains. However, no stations named “Phansidewa” and “Chopra” are located on Kishanganj-Thakurganj-Siliguri sector of the Northeast Frontier Railway. (b) and (c) There is a potential for parcel traffic including agricultural products through Brake Van of train no. 15464/15720/15722. However, at present no demand has been received from any “Stakeholder/Party.”  *****
