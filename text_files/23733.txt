                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Coal Supply to Thermal Power Stations




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the year-wise details of quantum of coal supplied by the Government to thermal power stations of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan;(b) whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Governments regarding shortage of coal supply and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the States have surplus quantity of coal for thermal power and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of quantum of coal supplied to industries and private sector in Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a): 	The total coal supplied by Coal India Limited (CIL) and Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL) to thermal power stations of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra for the year 2019-20 & 2020-21 (up to Feb, 2021) is as below: Company	States	Coal Supplied in Million Tonne (MT)		2019-20	2020-21(upto Feb, 2021)CIL	Maharashtra	57.31	46.27	Uttar Pradesh	81.65	72.34	Rajasthan	14.34	9.03SCCL	Maharashtra	6.157	4.237	Uttar Pradesh	0.004	Nil	Rajasthan	Nil	NilTotal	159.451	131.877 (b) & (c) :  The coal stock at the power plant end and CIL end for all power plants and power plants of state of Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan are as given below:                                                                                                    (in MT)Date	CIL Stock	Stock at Power House end	Power Plants of  Maharashtra	Power Plants of Uttar Pradesh	Power Plants of Rajasthan01.04.2019	54.15	30.92	4.12	5.71	0.6101.04.2020	74.89	44.95	6.37	7.13	0.9715.03.2021	85.01	30.29	3.91	4.76	0.45It can be seen from above that there has been no shortage of coal at the power plants. (d): 	The coal supplied by CIL and SCCL to industries and private sector in Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan the year 2019-20 & 2020-21 (up to Feb.21) is as below:Company	States	Coal Supplied (in MT)		2019-20	2020-21CIL	Maharashtra	8.71	12.15	Madhya Pradesh	10.20	12.91	Rajasthan	0.24	3.39SCCL	Maharashtra	0.004	0.012	Madhya Pradesh	Nil	Nil	Rajasthan	Nil	NilTotal	19.154	28.462 ****
