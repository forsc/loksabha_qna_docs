                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1130






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






National Youth Policy




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government intend  to bring a new National Youth Policy; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) 	the steps taken by the Government to develop leadership qualities and national consciousness among youth?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTERYOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS{ SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) to (c) The National Youth Policy (NYP), 2014 provides a holistic vision for the youth of India which is “to empower the youth of thecountry to achieve their full potential, and throughthem enable India to find its rightful place in thecommunity of nations”. The Department of Youth Affairs runs several programmes under the umbrella scheme  “Rashtriya Yuva Sashktikaran Karyakram” and National Service Scheme with a view to  develop leadership qualities  in the youth to enable them to realize their full potential and to instil  social values and promote community service to strengthen nationalism in the country. The NYP 2014 is currently under review.****
