                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Skill Development Centre in Maharsshtra




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Skill Development centres established in Marathwada and Vidarbha regions of Maharashtra so as to promote Skill Development;(b)	the details of the achievement made as a result of Skill training being imparted in private institutions under Skill Development schemes; and(c)	the details of the amount spent so far on the Skill Development of the youth out of the total funds allocated to other States including Maharashtra so far since the inception of the scheme along with details of the funds allocated for the year 2020-21?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill development centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras (PMKK) in every district of the country. As on 30.06.2021, 44 PMKKs have been established in 36 districts of the State of Maharashtra covering Marathwada and Vidarbha regions.Further, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) does not mandate the establishment of training centres (TCs), however, training is being imparted through empanelled training centres. As on 10.07.2021, 1,664 training centres have been empanelled under PMKVY in the State of Maharashtra covering Marathwada and Vidarbha regions.The district-wise number of TCs including PMKKs in the State of Maharashtra is given at Annexure.(b)       In the State of Maharashtra, as on 10.07.2021, 11.63 lakh candidates have been trained / oriented under PMKVY.(c)        Under PMKVY, funds amounting to Rs. 8,805.81 Cr. (approx.) has been released, till June'' 2021 for the implementation of the scheme across the country including Maharashtra. For implementation of PMKVY 3.0, Rs. 948.9 Cr. have been allocated during FY 2020-21.
