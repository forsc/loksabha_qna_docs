                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4545






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Ayurvedic Products in Global Market




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the details of Ayurvedic products originating/ arising from India for the global market;(b) whether the Government has constituted any council on the lines of Allopathy for improving the quality of Ayurvedic pharmacy in the country; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) the details of Unani hospitals and dispensaries functioning in the country, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):	So far, more than 50 products (Unani and Ayurveda) have been registered in 08 countries namely Kenya, USA, Russia, Latvia, Canada, Oman, Tajikistan and Sri Lanka under the scheme of the Ministry. Total export of Ayurvedic products for the financial year 2018-19 is USD 143.10.  	Quality certification system under WHO-GMP guidelines is implemented under the administrative purview of Drugs Controller General for promoting export of Ayurvedic, Siddha and Unani herbal medicines. National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB) under the Ministry of AYUSH is supporting large scale cultivation of medicinal plants to make available quality raw materials to the AYUSH drug industry.The following HS codes are given for export of Ayurvedic products:•	30039011      Medicants of Ayurvedic system•	30049011      Medicaments of Ayurvedic systemFollowing is the details of export under these HS codes for the last three years are:  HS Code	 PRODUCT DESCRIPTION	2016-17USD mn	2017-18USD mn	2018-19USD mn30039011	Medicants of Ayurvedic system	15.68	14.24	14.2630049011	Medicaments of Ayurvedic system	105.60	126.52	128.84  (b) & (c):  	For improving the quality of Ayurvedic medicines, Pharmacopoeia Commission for Indian Medicine & Homoeopathy (PCIM&H) has been established.  Ayurvedic Pharmacopoeia Committee is engaged in developing the quality standard of ayurvedic drugs for the use of drug manufactures. The “Ayurvedic Pharmacopeia of India” has been published which contains 645 monographs of single drugs and 202 monographs of compound formulations. 	The Ministry of AYUSH has set-up Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS), which serves as an apex body for formulation, coordination, development and promotion of research activities in Ayurvedic system of Medicine(d):   	22 hospitals/ dispensaries under Central Council for Research in Unani Medicine (CCRUM), 01 hospital of National Institute of Unani Medicine (NIUM), and 1657 dispensaries all over the country are functioning. (State wise details of Unani dispensaries and CRIs, RRIs and CRUs under CCRUM are attached at Annexure I and II respectively). ******
