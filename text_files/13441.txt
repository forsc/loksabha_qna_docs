                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Suicide Cases in Defence Forces




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed













                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether cases of suicide in the defence forces are on the rise year after year;(b) if so, the details thereof during the last three years and the reasons therefor;(c) whether a report by Defence Institute of Psychological Research (DIPR) has pointed out perceived harassment and humiliation, occupational and familial causes as the main reasons for suicide and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has assessed the reasons for suicide in armed forces; and(e) if so, the details and the outcome thereof along with the steps taken or being taken by Government to make the working conditions for armed forces work friendly as well as better working condition with social intactness?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  The number of suicide cases in Indian Air Force, Indian Navy and Indian Army during the last three years are as follows:Year	Number of suicide cases	Air Force	Navy	Army2017	21	05	772018	16	08	832019	20	02	73(c) to (e):  DIPR after conducting a number of studies since 2006, has, inter-alia, listed Domestic and Personal problems, Marital Discord, Stress and Financial problems as the major causes of suicides by armed forces personnel.	The Government has taken several steps towards stress amelioration amongst troops, which include, deployment of trained psychological counsellors, improvement in the quality of food and clothing, training in stress management, provision of recreational facilities, buddy system, leave concessions, approachability of seniors, facilities for movement of troops from border areas and establishing a grievance redressal mechanism at various levels, etc.*****
