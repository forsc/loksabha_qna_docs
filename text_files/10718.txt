                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4001






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Road Projects under Bharatmala Pariyojana in Jharkhand




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of districts in Jharkhand connected and proposed to be connected with National Highways under Bharatmala Pariyojana;(b)	the present status of work undertaken so far in this regard;(c)	the funds allocated/released for Jharkhand for this purpose along with the amount utilized thereunder; and (d)	the details of the national highways constructed with the use of said funds?		






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Total seventeen districts namely, Ranchi, Lohardaga, Latehar, Palamau, Garhwa, Simdega, Gumla, Khunti, Ramgarh, Bokaro, Saraikela–Kharsawan, East Singhbhum, Kodarma, Hazaribagh, Godda, Dumka and Sahibganj are connected  with National Highways under Bharatmala Pariyojana in the State of Jharkhand.(b) to (d) Total 8 nos. of works with an aggregate length of 343 km having cost of about Rs. 6531.00 crore have been sanctioned and awarded till date under Bharatmala Pariyojana in the State of Jharkhand. *****
