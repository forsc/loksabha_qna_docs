                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4070






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Fund Allocation to Nehru Yuva Kendras




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Kirit Premjibhai Solanki
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated to and utilized by Nehru Yuva Kendras during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government has introduced any scheme named Punarjagaran under auspices of Nehru Yuva Kendra Sanghathan (NYKS) and if so, the details along with salient features thereof;(c) the details of the schemes/programmes being implemented by to nurture hidden talent of the youths in the country;(d) whether the Government has addressed the issue of a large number of vacant posts in NYKS, as identified by the independent evaluation agency in 2014-15 and if so, the details thereof;(e) the details of the current age-wise distribution of National Youth Volunteers and whether a majority of volunteers are still above the age of 40 years, if so, the details thereof;(f) whether the Government proposes to restructure NYKS and if so, the details thereof; and(g) whether the NYKS have been successful in achieving their objectives and if not, the details of the remedial steps proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a): Ministry releases Grant-in-Aid to Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) which in turn allocates funds to their State Offices. Funds allocated to and utilized by Nehru Yuva Kendras during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise is at Annexure-I.(b): No Sir, no scheme named Punarjagaran has been launched. However, on 2nd October, 2014, the birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi a year long Punarjagaran Programme across India was launched from the four corner tips of India viz. from Leh (J&K), Okha (Gujarat), Kanyakumari (Tamil Nadu) and Roing (Arunachal Pradesh). The Punarjagaran Yatra culminated at Mathura (UP) on 25thSeptember, 2015 on the occasion of 100th birth anniversary of Pandit Deen Dayal Upadhyaya.(c): Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan(NYKS) through its 623 District level Nehru Yuva Kendras(NYKs) undertakes programmes and activities all over the country. Details of programmes and activities being organized/conducted under the Nehru Yuva Kendras (NYKs) are at Annexure–II.(d): 824 posts out of sanctioned strength of 2273 posts were lying vacant for many years prior to 2014 and were deemed abolished. With the approval of Ministry of Finance all these 824 posts in different categories have been revived. NYKS is in the process of filling up these posts. As of now, 453 posts have been filled through an open competitive examination which includes 201 posts ofDistrict Youth Coordinator (DYC), 148 posts of Accounts Clerk cum Typist (ACT) and 104 posts of Multi Tasking Staff (MTS). Written exam for filling up of other 337 approved posts( which includes 160 posts of DYCs, 58 posts of ACTs, 23 posts of MTS and 96 posts in other categories) has already been conducted. Departmental promotion is also in progress to fill up the promotional posts.(e): As per the National Youth Corps(NYC) scheme guidelines, the youth in the age group of 18-29 years only are deployed as National Youth Volunteers initially for a period of one year and extendable only for one more year subject to their performance. Age wise distribution of National Youth Volunteers is at Annexure-III.(f): No Sir, there is no proposal of restructuring NYKS at present.(g): NYKS have been successful in achieving its objectives.*****
