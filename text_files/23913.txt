                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Green Tax on Older Vehicles




Jayanta Kumar Roy













                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to impose green tax on older vehicles to curb air pollution in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of estimated number of older vehicles plying on roads in the country, State-wise;(c)   whether the said green tax will vary from State to State in the country and if so, the details of rates, State-wise;(d)   whether the Government has information regarding rate of green tax levied by major developed and developing countries and if so, the details thereof; and(e)  whether the Government has made any impact assessment of imposing green tax and if so, the details thereof along with the other measures being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
