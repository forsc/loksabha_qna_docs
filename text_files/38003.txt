                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5111






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Domestic Workers




Brijbhushan Sharan Singh













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Srinivas Kesineni
                                                








                                                    Mahendra Singh Solanky
                                                








                                                    Gautam Gambhir
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the key features of the All India Survey of Domestic workers launched by the Government and the timeline for the survey to be completed;(b)the State-wise details of the number of domestic workers registered under the Domestic Workers (Registration, Social Security and Welfare) Act, 2008, and district-wise in particular for Uttar Pradesh;(c)the details of the number of complaints of sexual harassment received, resolved, withdrawn and convicted under the aforesaid Act or the Prevention of Sexual Harassment at Workplace Act, 2013;(d)the State-wise data of the average minimum wages received by domestic workers at present; and (e)whether the Government has conducted any survey or study to check the implementation of the 2008 Act, if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	Labour Bureau under the Ministry of Labour and Employment has launched the fieldwork for the All India Survey on Domestic Workers on 22 November, 2021. The Survey Covers sample size from All India (37) States /UTs and 742 Districts. Contd..2/-:: 2 ::The objective of the Survey on Domestic Workers (DW) are as follows:(i)	To estimate the number/proportion of DWs at National and State level. (ii)	 To estimate percentage distribution of DWs Estimates: Live-in/ Live-out; Formal/ Informal Employment; Migrant/ Non-Migrant; their wages and other socio-economic characteristics.(iii)	 Household Estimates of Live-in/ Live-out DWs. (iv)	 Average number of DWs engaged by different types of households.(b):	As per Census 2011 data, the number of domestic workers in the country were 47,81,355. State-wise details are given at the Annexure.(c):	The national Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) is collecting data regarding sexual harassment in work/ Office premises (Section 354A of IPC). The published reports are available till the year 2020, which are available at its website https://ncrb.gov.in. (d):	Such data is maintained by the States.(e):	No such survey has been done.*                                                          *******
