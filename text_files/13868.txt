                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2718






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Institutional Deliveries




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of institutional deliveries that took place in the country during each of the last three years and the number of women who got financial assistance;(b) the details of the funds allocated and utilised for the promotion of institutional deliveries, State-wise during the last three years;(c) whether there has been a decline or increase in the rate of institutional deliveries in the country during the last three years; and(d) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	The details of the institutional deliveries that took place in the country in last three years and the number of women who got financial assistance are attached in “Annexure-I & II”.(b):	Details of funds allocated and utilised for the promotion of institutional deliveries, state-wise during the last three years is attached in “Annexure-III”.(c):	There has been increase in the rate of institutional Deliveries in the country during the last three years. The details are follows;Year	% Institutional Deliveries to total reported delivery (As per HMIS Data)2017-18	92.2%2018-19	93.4%2019-20 upto Dec	94.3%(d):	The State/UT wise details is in “Annexure-IV”
