                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4022






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Setting up of Sports School




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up a sports school in each district of the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and the number of sports school set up till date, State-wise;(c) whether the Government has received any proposal to set up any such schools in various States including Rajasthan; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) and (b) No Sir. At present Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports is not considering any proposal to set up sports school in each district of the country. However, 04 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) have been adopted as State Level Khelo India Centres (SLKICs) under Khelo India Scheme and 10 schools are covered under National Sports Talent Contest (NSTC) Scheme of Sports Authority of India. Details of the same are at Annexure I.(c) No Sir. No specific proposal has been received to set up sports school in various States/Union Territories including Rajasthan. However, proposals from 06 schools have been received from various States/ Union Territories, including Rajasthan, requesting assistance under Khelo India. Details of the same are at Annexure II.(d) Does not arise.
