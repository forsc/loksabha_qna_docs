                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1601






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Establishment and Strengthening of Veterinary Hospitals and Dispensaries




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of veterinary hospitals/dispensaries functioning in the rural areas of the country as on date, State-wise;(b)	the details of funds provided to these veterinary hospitals/dispensaries during the last three years and the current financial year, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government has proposed to open new veterinary hospitals/dispensaries in the country particularly in the rural areas deprived of such facilities; (d)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (e)	the time by which the above veterinary hospitals/dispensaries are likely to be opened in all the villages of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	There are 12129 Veterinary Hospitals, 25597 Veterinary Dispensaries and 28168 Veterinary Aid Centres /Stockman Centres /Mobile Dispensaries in the country.  The state-wise details of these Veterinary Institutions is at Annexure-1.(b)	The Department has released Rs.304.48 lakh, Rs.719.80 lakh and Rs.247.05 lakh during 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, respectively, under “Establishment &Strengthening of Veterinary Hospitals & Dispensaries (ESVHD)” a component under the centrally sponsored Livestock Health & Disease Control (LH&DC) scheme.The details of funds released to the states is annexed as Annexure-II.(c)	To (e) Veterinary hospitals and dispensaries are set up by States/Union Territories. However, in order to supplement the efforts of the States/ Union Territory Governments, Central Government is implementing the“Establishment & Strengthening of Veterinary Hospitals & Dispensaries (ESVHD)”, a component under the centrally sponsored Livestock Health & Disease Control (LH&DC) scheme, to provide financial assistance for establishment & strengthening of veterinary hospitals & dispensaries as well as for running of mobile veterinary clinics. *****
