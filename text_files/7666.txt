                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






GST on Tamarind




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken a decision to waive off the collection of Goods and Services Tax (GST) on tamarind;  (b) if so, the details thereof; and   (c) the time by which this will be coming into force? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE  (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)   (a), (b) and (c): The GST Council in its 37th Meeting held on 20th September 2019 had recommended to exempt from GST to Tamarind with effect from 1st October 2019. The notification to this effect has been issued.   *****
