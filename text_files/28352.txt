                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

17






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Aazadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the programmes organised under the Amrit Mahotsav of Independence, that have been organized by the Ministry and by the Central Public Sector Undertakings under the Ministry of Culture and other ministries of Government of India; and(b)	the details of the proposed  programmes in the coming days under the Amrit Mahotsav?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)  As on 24/11/2021 a total of 6714 programmes have been organised by Ministries of Government of India (including Ministry of Culture) under the Amrit Mahotsav of Independence.(b)  As of now, a total of 2622 programmes have been proposed to be organised by Ministries of Government of India (including Ministry of Culture) in the coming days.
