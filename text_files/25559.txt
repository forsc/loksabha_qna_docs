                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Welfare Schemes for PwDs




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated any scheme for the welfare of Person with Disabilities (PwDs) in the State of Maharashtra;(b)	if so, the details thereof, and the amount provided by the Government for the welfare of person with disabilities in Maharashtra;(c)	whether the Government has provided any fund/equipment to uplift PwDs in the State of Maharashtra; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) to (d) Relief to the persons with disabilities is a State subject by virtue of entry ‘9’ of the State list of the Constitution of India. However, the Central Government supplements the efforts of the State Governments for the welfare of persons with disabilities.The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) implements various schemes/projects including through its National Institutes (NIs)/ Composite Regional Centres (CRCs), National Trust for the Welfare of Persons with Autism, Cerebral Palsy, Mental Retardation & Mental Disabilities, National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC) and Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation of India (ALIMCO) as well as through State Governments/ autonomous bodies/ organizations/ institutes/ Implementing Agencies/Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) for the welfare and rehabilitation of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) throughout the country, including in the State of Maharashtra.Details of the major schemes of this Department along with the funds released and number of beneficiaries benefited in Maharashtra State are at Annexure.
