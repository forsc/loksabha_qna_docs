                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1316






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Kendriya Vidyalaya in Chhattisgarh




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to open Kendriya Vidyalayas in variousStates/UTs of the country;(b) if so, the number of Kendriya Vidyalayas opened in various States/UTs of thecountry during the last three years and the current year;(c) whether any proposal has been received from Chhattisgarh State Governmentregarding opening of Kendriya Vidyalaya in Bendranawagarh under Devbhogblock;(d) if so, the details of action taken/proposed to be taken in this regard; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Opening of Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) is a continuous process. Proposals foropening of new KVs are considered only if sponsored by Ministries or Departments ofthe Government of India/State Governments / Union Territories Administrations therebycommitting resources for setting up a new KV as well as the availability of necessarysanction of the Government. The proposals received from various sponsoringauthorities for opening of new KVs also have to compete among other proposals underthe “Challenge Method”.(b) During last three years i.e. 2016-17, 2017- 2018, 2018-19 and current year 104Kendriya Vidyalayas have been opened in the country.(c) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has informed that no proposal has been receivedfrom the State Government of Chhattisgarh for opening of a new KV at Bendranawagarhunder Devbhog block.(d) & (e) Do not arise.*****
