                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3575






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Import and Export of Onion




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has any statistics regarding production and consumption of onion in the country, if so, the details of production and consumption of onion during the last ten years, year-wise and State-wise; (b)	whether the import and export of onion is taking place in the country; (c)	if so, the details of import and export of onion during the last ten years, year-wise, country-wise and price-wise; (d)	whether there is any income generated through import and export of onion; and (e) 	if so, the details of such income generation during the last ten years, year-wise and country-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a)         :  Production of horticulture crops including that of onions are brought out by Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and farmers Welfare and is available at link http://agricoop.nic.in/statistics/state-level The all India Household Consumer Expenditure Survey of the National Sample Survey Office (NSSO) provides the per-capita consumption of onions. The monthly per-capita consumption of various items including that of onions as per the 68th NSS Round is available at link http://mospi.nic.in/sites/default/files/publication_reports/Report_no558_rou68_30june14.pdf(b) to (e) :  Details of import and export of onions in quantity and value terms as published by Department of Commerce, Government of India is at link https://commerce-app.gov.in/eidb/.*****
