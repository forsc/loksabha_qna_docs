                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4455






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Research on Medicinal Plants




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether less research has taken place in the field of medicinal plants in the country despite the huge potential of utility and development of medicinal plants; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has a plan to start internship for research on medicinal plants to promote this sector; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):    No. Research has taken place in the field of medicinal plants in the country owing the huge potential of utility and development of medicinal plants. For Research and Development in the field of Medicinal Plants National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB), Ministry of AYUSH under “Center Sector Scheme on Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants” has given project based assistance for collection, compilation, documentation, preparations of comprehensive monographs, conservation biology, ethno-medicinal documentation and exploration, Bio-activity Guided Fractionation, Bio-prospecting, development of DNA barcoding, post-harvest treatment, quality planting material, Marketing, germination and seed treatment protocols, identification of substitutes/adulterants.(c) & (d):   The NMPB, Ministry of AYUSH has initiated a 1-3 months duration internship programme with the objective to develop manpower to address the quality, standardization, marketing along with policy formation aspects and promote medicinal plants sector in year 2019-20. Besides it, Central Council for Research in Siddha (CCRS), Ministry of AYUSH also provides internship for Siddha Medicinal Plants Garden (SMPG). ………………..
