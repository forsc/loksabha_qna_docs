                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3556






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Implementation of PMGKY in Bihar




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has announced Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY) to get basic facilities to the weaker sections of society in Bihar and Parliamentary constituency Katihar;b)	if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives of the said scheme; c)	the details of financial assistance released to Bihar and Parliamentary constituency Katihar under the said package; d)	the number of people who are benefited from this package; ande)	the details of the criteria laid down by the Government for availing the benefits of this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (e): The Government had announced a comprehensive relief package of Rs.1.70 lakh crore for the welfare of weaker sections of the society in the fight against COVID-19.  The package covered the entire country, including the parliamentary constituency of Katihar in Bihar.   Under the package, additional benefits were provided to all existing beneficiaries of various welfare schemes of Government of India.  A Statement indicating details of the package & criteria for availing the benefit is at Annexure-IFinancial Assistance was provided directly under the following schemes: (a) A comprehensive insurance scheme providing Rs. 50 lakh to health workers fighting COVID-19 effective from 30.3.2020 to 18.04.2022 under which 84 eligible claims from Bihar, including 3 from Katihar Parliamentary Constituency have been settled, (b) An ex-gratia of Rs. 500 per month for three months to women Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) account holders, under which an amount of Rs. 365523.65 Lakhs has been credited to banks accounts of 24362070 women in the State of Bihar, including Rs. 10185.47 Lakhs to 688146 women in Katihar district and (c) Ex-gratia of Rs.1000 to the existing Old Age, Widow and Divyangjan beneficiaries under Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS), Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (IGNWPS) and Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme (IGNDPS) respectively of National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) under which the scheme-wise details are as under:  Name of  Scheme	No. of beneficiaries covered	Fund released (in lakh)IGNOAPS	2996472	29964.72IGNWPS	547777	5477.77IGNDPS	120562	1205.62TOTAL	3664811	36648.11The funds released and details of beneficiaries at district-wise and Parliamentary Constituency-wise are not maintained centrally.The details of benefits provided in respect of the State of Bihar under PMGKP, including financial assistance and number of beneficiaries is at Annexure-II.
