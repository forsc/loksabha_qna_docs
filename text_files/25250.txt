                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1121






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Company Owned Company Operated Pumps




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State/UT-wise number of Company Owned Company operated (COCO) pumpsoperational in the country at present including Rajasthan;(b) whether such pumps are also handed over to private institutions for operation and ifso, the necessary guidelines in this regard;(c) whether the Government has received any complaints or disputes related to suchpumps in Rajasthan or other parts of the country and if so, the details thereof along withaction taken by the Government in this regard; and(d) whether the Union Government proposes to hand over more COCO pumps toprivate institutions for operation in near future and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) State/UT-wise number of Company Owned Company operated (COCO) pumpsoperational in the country including Rajasthan is given in Annexure.(b)&(d) As per extant policy, there is no provision for appointment of Private Institutionsas Service Provider for COCO retail outlets.(c) The details of selection related complaints received by Public Sector OMCs on allIndia basis and for the State of Rajasthan as on 30.6.2021 with regard to appointmentof service providers for operation of COCO Retail Outlets along with action taken isgiven below:Particulars All India (including Rajasthan) RajasthanTotal No. of Complaints Received 36 1No. of Complaints Established 5 0No. of Complaints not established 19 1Investigation Pending 12 0Details Action taken onestablished ComplaintsBPCL (4 cases)?
