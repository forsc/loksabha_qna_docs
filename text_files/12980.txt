                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1789






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Direct Benefit Transfer Schemes




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has achieved the desired results of developing the IT applications to improve efficiency, transparency and accountability in Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) Schemes in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof, scheme-wise and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	the number of beneficiaries of the said schemes, as on date, State/UT-wise and scheme-wise;(d)	whether the Government has received complaints in implementation of the said schemes since inception;(e)	if so, the details thereof, scheme-wise and the reasons therefor along with the present status of such complaints; and(f)	the other steps taken by the Government for effective implementation and to increase coverage of the said schemes in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c) : Yes. 427 schemes of Government of India have been on-boarded on DBT Bharat Portal (as on 26.02.2020). The details regarding the on-boarded schemes are available on the DBT Bharat Portal, dbtbharat.gov.in.(d) to (f) : The schemes are implemented by respective Ministries/Departments. DBT Mission coordinates with various stakeholders for effective implementation of DBT in Government schemes.                                       ****
