                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3505






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Kendriya Vidyalayas




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Kendriya Vidyalayas functioning in the country alongwith thenumber of students enrolled in each of the said vidyalayas, State-wise/UnionTerritory-wise; and(b) the details of amount of funds released for construction of kendriya vidyalayabuildings, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) As on date there are 1234 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) including 3 abroad.State /UT wise number of students enrolled in each of the KVs in the country are atAnnexure-I.(b) Grant-in-aid is provided to Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) under threedistinct heads viz. Salaries, General and Capital Assets. This allocation is made on aconsolidated basis and not State / UT-wise. The State / UT-wise details of fundsreleased by KVS for constructions of KV buildings during the year 2019-2020 are atAnnexure-II.*****
