                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Atrocities Against Scheduled Castes




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of cases related to atrocities reported to the National Scheduled Castes Commission during the last three years;(b) the number of cases against which action has been taken and the number of cases still pending;(c) the number of cases in which the accused have been sentenced after being found guilty, State-wise; and(d) the compensation provided to the victims?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	The Ministry of Home Affairs does not maintain data on the  number of cases related to atrocities reported to the National Commission for Scheduled Castes (NCSC). However, as informed by the NCSC, it has received 17901 representations during the last three years and it has taken action on all the representations. The NCSC has also informed that it does not award any sentence. Further, data on compensation provided to victims is not maintained centrally.*****
