                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4160






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Auction of Oil Fields




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao













                                                    Ramya  Haridas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the State-owned ONGC is looking for an auction of oil fields to increase stagnant oil production in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether it is also a fact that the agencies finally selected would be allotted new fields for extraction of oil or these would require to operate already operational oil fields; and (d) if so, the details of additional oil expected to be produced from the planned auction of oil fields?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) ONGC carried out bidding of 64 fields to enhance production of oil & gas from matured marginal fields. Out of these, forty nine fields have been awarded on revenue sharing basis. No bids were received for fourteen fields and single bid for one field was rejected.(c) All the Sixty four fields offered for bid out are existing operational fields of ONGC.(d) The fields were awarded for enhancing the production of oil & gas from base line production profile under Business-As-Usual (BAU) scenario. The revenue of the bidders depends upon the revenue generated from additional oil & gas produced above the base line production.******
