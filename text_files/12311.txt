                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1207






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Employment to Trained People




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of the organisations engaged by the Government providing employment to those people who successfully complete their training and if not, the reasons therefor; and(b)	the efforts made by the Government for increasing employment opportunities for the above people?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (2016-20), the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, various organisations/service providers namely; Sector Skill Councils (SSCs), industrial bodies, District Employment Exchanges/ Model Career Centres  National Career Service and empanelled training providers/training centres are facilitating trained candidates in accessing employment opportunities.(b)	Various initiatives are taken under PMKVY to enhance placement opportunities to the certified candidates viz. conduct of Rozgar Melas; placement-link payout and incentivisation @ Rs. 3000 per trainee to TCs post 12 months of trainee retention on employment, post placement support of Rs. 1500 per month per trainee belonging to special areas/special groups; implementation of employer led training model; reallocation of target to TCs based on past placement performance, etc.
