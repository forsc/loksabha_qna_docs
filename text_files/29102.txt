                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Subsidy for Production and Distribution of Fertilizers




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the Government has any data regarding the total subsidy and production support extended to States for producing and distributing fertilizers in the country for the last three years; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise particularly in West Bengal; (c)	 whether the Government has data regarding the import of fertilizers for domestic consumption for the last three years; (d)	 if so, the details of its distribution thereof, State/ UT-wise and particularly in West Bengal;(e) 	whether the Government has any proposal to increase the production and consumption of Bio-fertilizers in the country; and (f) 	if so, the initiatives taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS (SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b):  Yes Sir, the subsidy figures for indigenous Urea and P&K fertilizers is enclosed as ‘Annexure - A’. (c) & (d): Yes Sir, the data regarding the import of fertilizers (Urea, DAP, MOP & NPK) for the last three years is enclosed as ‘Annexure - B’. The details regarding the quantity of fertilizers (imported & manufactured) to all the states (including West Bengal) during 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 are enclosed as ‘Annexure – C’. (e) & (f):  Yes Sir, for increasing production of bio-fertilizers in the country, under Capital Investment Subsidy Scheme (CISS), credit linked back ended subsidy @ 25% of total cost with maximum limit of Rs. 40 lakhs is provided to private entrepreneurs/FPOs through National Bank for Agricultural and Rural Development (NABARD) for establishment of Bio-fertilizer production unit [200 Ton Per Annum (TPA) capacity].Under Soil Health Management (SHM), financial assistance upto Rs. 160 lakh/unit (200 MT capacity) is provided to State Governments for establishment of bio-fertilizer production unit on 60:40 sharing between GoI and State Government.Government is also promoting the use of bio-fertilizers under organic farming in the country through dedicated schemes namely Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)and Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER) since 2015-16. Bio-fertilizers are also promoted through Integrated Nutrient Management (INM) and are made integral part of package of practices developed by Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) and State Agriculture Universities. 	-2- ANNEXURE – APayment of subsidy to the indigenous Urea manufacturing companiesYear	Subsidy paid2018-19	Rs. 32189.50 crores2019-20	Rs. 43050.00 crores2020-21	Rs. 68807.41 croresPayment of subsidy in r.o P&K fertilizersYear	Subsidy paid2018-19	Rs. 24090.35 crores2019-20	Rs. 26368.85 crores2020-21	Rs. 37372.47 crores**There is no state-wise data for subsidy given to indigenous Urea manufacturing   companies. ** The state-wise data for subsidy given in r.o.
