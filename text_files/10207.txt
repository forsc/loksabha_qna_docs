                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3491






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Research on Dr. Ambedkar and SCs Group




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of various activities undertaken regarding research/symposiums on Dr. Ambedkar and the Scheduled Caste group; (b) the details of various publications brought out by this institute since its inception; and (c) the details of the number of books purchased for the library established in this centre during the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): Dr. Ambedkar International Centre (DAIC) has undertaken the following activities on                Dr. Ambedkar, since its inauguration on 07.12.2017: •	20 Quiz competitions for schools•	5 Inter-college quiz competitions•	2 Street play•	1 National Seminar•	5 Symposium•	6 Internship on Social Services•	Haryana Police Officer’s Training •	Dr. Ambedkar Memorial Lecture by CIC of India•	Mock Parliament in Socio-economic Policies of Dr. Ambedkar•	Panel Discussion on  thoughts and Philosophy of Dr. Ambedkar(b):   Dr. Ambedkar National Museum (DANM) Book, Brochures of DAIC and DANM and One research report on Health Services in Aspirational District, Sohna, Haryana have been published by the Institute, since its inception.(c):  6,084 books have been purchased for establishment of library in DAIC till date.*****
