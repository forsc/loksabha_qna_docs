                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3367






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Generic Medicines Centres in Bihar




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up sales centres of generic medicines on a large scale throughout the country to ensure their availability to people at affordable rate; (b) the number of such sales centres, established across the country so far; (c) whether such sales centres have been established in Bihar; and (d) if so, the details thereof, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(Dr. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)                               (a) & (b): With an objective of making quality generic medicines available at affordable prices to all the citizens, especially the poor and the deprived ones, Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) was launched by the Department of Pharmaceuticals. Under the scheme, dedicated outlets known as Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) are opened all across the country to provide quality generic medicines at affordable prices to the masses. As against target of 8,300 PMBJKs for the current financial year, already 8,578 PMBJKs have been approved covering all the districts of the country. (c) & (d):   As on 12.12.2021, out of 8,578 PMBJKs, about 272 Kendras have been established in Bihar. District-wise list of functional PMBJKs in Bihar is enclosed as Annexure.
