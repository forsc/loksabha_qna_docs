                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3668






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Set up of New Hotel Management Institutes




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has decided to set up new hotel management institutes besides introduction of hospitality education through Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs); (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the total number of hotel management institutes functioning at present in the country, location-wise; (c)	whether the Government has identified the sites therefor; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM					    (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d): Ministry of Tourism has set up 21 Central Institutes of Hotel Management and provided support to State Governments/ UT Administrations to set up  State Institutes of Hotel Management for imparting education, training and capacity building in hospitality sector. The State-wise details of such Hotel Management Institutes set up by Governments are given at Annexure.******
