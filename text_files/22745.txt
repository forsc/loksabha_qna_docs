                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Swachh Iconic Place




Kirti Vardhan Singh














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Ministry has selected several tourist sites for SwachhTourist destinations under Phase Four of Swachh Iconic place  forpromoting tourism in the country and if so, the details thereof along withthe number of such destinations selected;(b) Whether the Ministry of Jal Shakti would be taking the help of otherministries like Tourism and Urban Development for the development of thesesites and if so, the details thereof; and(c) Whether this development of tourist destinations would help ingenerating employment for the local people and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI RATTAN  LAL  KATARIA)(a)	Under Swachh Bharat (Clean India) Mission, in phase IV of Swachh Iconic Places, Department of Drinking Water & Sanitation, Ministry of Jal Shakti has selected the following 12 sites :SL. No	Sites	State/UT1	Ajanta Caves	Maharashtra 2	Sanchi Stupa 	Madhya Pradesh3	Kumbhalgarh Fort	Rajasthan4	Jaisalmer Fort	Rajasthan5	Ramdevra, Jaisalmer 	Rajasthan6	Golconda Fort, Hyderabad	Telangana 7	Sun Temple, Konark	Odisha8	Rock Garden	Chandigarh9	Dal Lake, Srinagar	Jammu & Kashmir10	Banke Bihari Temple, Mathura	Uttar Pradesh11	Agra Fort, Agra	Uttar Pradesh12	Kalighat Temple	West Bengal(b) The objective of Swachh Iconic Places is to achieve a distinctly higher level of Sanitation/Cleanliness at these places, especially on the peripheries and in approach areas. This is being coordinated by Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation, Ministry of Jal Shakti in collaboration with Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Ministry of Tourism, Ministry of Culture, State/UT Govt., Municipal and local agencies. Also, Ministry of Power, Ministry of Steel, Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas, Ministry of Coal, Ministry of Mines and Ministry of Corporate Affairs is being asked for additional support from their concerned Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) and Private companies through CSR funds.State Govt. being the key stakeholder will be appointing a Nodal person for the above 12 no. sites for coordination and execution. This officer of sufficient seniority would coordinate with various Departments/Ministries and Stakeholders, Trust/Shrine/Management Board, supporting PSUs etc. Ministries would be asked to support the selected sites from their ongoing programme to maintain highest standard of cleanliness.(c) As the objective of Swachh Iconic places is to achieve a distinctly higher level of Sanitation/Cleanliness at these places, especially on the peripheries and in approach areas, this will be helpful in generating employment for the local people. *****
