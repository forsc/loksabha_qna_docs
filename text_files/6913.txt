                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Expansion of Bhandara Road Railway Station




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans for the development and expansion of Bhandara Road Railway Station of Maharashtra;(b)  if so, the details thereof and the time by which the said work is likely to be completed; (c) whether the Government proposes to provide stoppage to Bilaspur Rajdhani and other trains at Bhandara Road Railway Station; (d)  if so, the details thereof; and(e)  if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)&(b) : Minimum Essential Amenities as well as desirable amenities as per norms have already been provided at this station.  Bhandara Road station has also been developed as Adarsh station.(c)to(e): Rajdhani Express services are premium class services with very limited stoppages enroute.  Stoppage of 12441/12442 Bilaspur – New Delhi Rajdhani Express at Bhandara Road station is neither operationally feasible nor commercially justified.  Besides, 3 pairs of express services provide connectivity for Delhi area to the passengers of Bhandara Road.	                                                               *****
