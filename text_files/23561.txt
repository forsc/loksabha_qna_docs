                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4580






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Boosting Food Processing Industries




Raksha Nikhil Khadse













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the share of agriculture based food processing sectors in country’s GDP has risen to the extent of 17.8% for the fiscal year 2019-20 and the same has worsened to 3.4% due to Covid pandemic; and (b)	if so, the details of schemes and projects which the Government has proposed to boost the agricultural based food processing industries, including funds allocated, State-wise particularly Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a): The average annual growth rate (AAGR) for the food processing sector for the five years ending 2018-19 has been 10.0% as compared to 8.25% in manufacturing and 3.11 % in agriculture. The share of food processing sector GVA (gross value addition) out of the manufacturing sector GVA and agriculture sector GVA were 8.98% and 11.11% respectively in 2018-19. The GVA data for sub-sector of manufacturing for 2019-20 has not yet been released by Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation. (b): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a central sector  scheme Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for overall development of food processing sector. The various component schemes of  PMKSY are demanddriven and no State-wise allocation of funds is made under the schemes.  The projects approved in the country and in Maharashtra under component schemes of PMKSY during FY 2020-21 is at Annexure-I.              As part of the Aatmanirbhar Bharat Initiative, MoFPI is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme-PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises  for providing financial, technical and business support for upgradation / setting up of 2 lakh micro food processing enterprises during the five years from 2020-21 to 2024-25 with an outlay of Rs.10,000 cr. The state-wise outlay is at Annexure-II.                 MoFPI has also extended financial assistance for transportation and storage under price stabilization component of Operation Greens scheme from Tomato, Onion, and Potato (TOP) to all notified fruits & vegetables (Total) during 2020-21.  *****
