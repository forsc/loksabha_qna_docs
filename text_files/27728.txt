                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3261






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Action Taken to Improve Tourism Potential in North-Eastern States




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the measures taken by the Government to improve the tourism potential in North-Eastern India and the revenue generated from International tourism; (b) 	the details of the State which has the highest revenue from tourism in last three years along with the details of the State which has the highest potential for tourism and unable to harness it; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Tourism provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) for the projects as submitted by the State Governments including the North Eastern States under its various scheme guidelines, subject to submission of Detailed Project Report (DPR), their adherence to scheme guidelines, submission of Utilization Certificates and availability of funds. The Detail of projects sanctioned in the North Eastern States under Swadesh Darshan & PRASHAD Schemes in the last three years is given at Annexure-I. For promotion of tourism in North Eastern Region, Ministry carries out special promotion in domestic and international markets highlighting region’s diversity, tourism products and  its rich culture. The Ministry organizes International Travel Mart (ITM) every year in one of the North Eastern States, with the objective of providing a platform to them to showcase their tourism potential to prospective international and domestic buyers and opportunity to interact with them. The last edition of ITM was held in Manipur.Ministry of Tourism also provides Central Financial Assistance under the relevant Scheme guidelines for proposals submitted by State Governments/Union Territory administrations for organizing fairs/festivals/events. The State-wise details of  funds sanctioned for fairs & festivals and project sanctioned under the Assistance to Central Agencies Scheme for the year 2018-19 to 2020-21 is given at Annexure-II.The Ministry does not maintain data on revenue generated from Tourism.
