                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5256






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Jal Shakti Abhiyan




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has authorised or proposes to authorise village panchayat administration and Gram Sabha to implement Jal Shakti Abhiyan and Jal Kranti Abhiyan successfully; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	& (b) Ministry of Jal Shakti has launched Jal Shakti Abhiyan (JSA), which is a time-bound, mission-mode water conservation campaign. Under Jal Shakti Abhiyan (JSA), Government has deputed a team led by Additional Secretary/ Joint Secretary Level Officer posted in Government of India as Central Nodal Officer (CNOs) and consisting of DS/Director level officer and Technical Officer to each of the identified  256 water stressed districts, who will coordinate the implementation, review progress and provide feedback on five interventions namely (i) water conservation and rainwater harvesting, (ii) renovation of traditional and other water bodies/tanks, (iii) reuse and bore well recharge structures, (iv) watershed development, and (v) intensive afforestation.
