                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4235






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Day Boarding Centres




Sanganna Amarappa Karadi













                                                     Gopal Jee Thakur
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the process of establishing Day Boarding Centres by the Sports Authority of India at State/ district level in the country including Karnataka-Koppal;  (b) the details of the facilities being provided/ proposed to be provided to sportspersons and institutes in such centres;  (c) the number of Day Boarding Centres established at Mithila in Bihar during the last three years, sport-wise and district-wise; and  (d) the details of the kits provided from time to time to the Day Boarding Centres and sportspersons in Bihar during the last three years along with the amount provided in this regard? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)  (a) to (d): The Sports Authority of India (SAI) does not set up Day Boarding Centres.  SAI runs Regional Training Centres, SAI Training Centres and National Institute of Sports (NIS), Patiala.  These SAI training facilities are available to residential as well as non-residential athletes selected under various sports promotion schemes of the Department of Sports and SAI.  In the State of Bihar, SAI is running its training centres at Patna, Kishanganj, Gidhaur and Dhanpur.  In these 04 SAI Training Centres, a total of 154 athletes are benefitting which includes 12 non- residential athletes.    *****
