                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






FOBs in Telangana




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that 15 stations have been identified in Telangana for construction of Foot-Over-Bridges (FOBs); (b) if so, the details thereof, district-wise; (c) whether it is not a fact that these were supposed to be completed by March, 2021; and (d) if so, the reasons for delay and the time by when these are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	and (b) Yes, Sir. The district wise details are appended.(c) and (d) Yes, Sir. These 15 Foot Over Bridges were completed till November, 2021 due to COVID-19 pandemic situation during 2020-21 and 2021-22.
