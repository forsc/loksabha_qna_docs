                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3744






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Rural Sanitation Survey




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any Rural Sanitation Survey Campaign has been started in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c) the details of the success of Jal Shakti Abhiyan among the States along with their names?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation had conducted three rounds of National Annual Rural Sanitation Survey (NARSS) by Independent Verification Agency (IVA) during 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 under World Bank support to Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen).  Under NARSS, approximately 92,000 households in about 6000 villages have been covered in each round.  Survey was undertaken to gauge the following three disbursement linked indicators (DLIs):(i) 	Reduction in the prevalence of Open Defecation(ii) 	Sustaining Open Defecation Free (ODF) status in villages(iii) 	Increase in population with access to Solid & Liquid Waste ManagementState/UT-wise, sanitation coverage and usage as per the results of NARSS 2019-20 are given at Annexure.(c) 	Government of India launched Jal Shakti Abhiyan (JSA) to spread awareness among all stakeholders in 256 water stressed districts about water conservation in two phases – Phase-I from 1st July, 2019 to 30th September, 2019 and Phase-II from 1st October, 2019 to 30th November, 2019 in the Southern States which receive retreating monsoon.  Huge public awareness vis-à-vis water conservation/harvesting has been generated due to JSA and further, it has been observed that various stakeholders, viz., Government departments/agencies, NGOs, Panchayats, individual etc. have started taking steps towards water conservation. *****
