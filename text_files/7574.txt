                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1209






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






University Grants Commission




Shantanu Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Paramarsh– A University Grants Commission (UGC) scheme is able topromote quality assurance in higher education;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the scheme will lead to enhancement of overall quality of the MenteeInstitution and enhance its profile as a result of improved quality of research,research teaching and learning methodologies; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b): The objective of the University Grants Commission (UGC) scheme‘Paramarsh’ is to promote quality assurance in higher education. ‘Paramarsh’scheme will promote well performing accredited institutions to mentor aspiringinstitutions to upgrade their academic performance and get NAAC accredited.(c) & (d): The scheme intends to bring about enhancement in the overall quality ofthe mentee institution and enhance its profile as a result of improved quality ofresearch, teaching and learning methodologies. Guidelines of the ‘Paramarsh’scheme are available at https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/8400459_ParamarshEnglish.pdf .******
