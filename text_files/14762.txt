                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3466






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Peeth




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik













                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to establish a Peeth in the Universities across thecountry in the name of renowned women;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives thereto, university-wise;(c) the criteria fixed by the Government for this purpose;(d) the amount of fund sanctioned and released for the said purpose;(e) the names of distinguished women in whose names the Peeth is likely to beestablished in the different Universities; and(f) whether the Government proposes to establish more such Peeths in the Universitiesand other institutes in the country to empower the women and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. The University Grants Commission (UGC) has informed that with theassistance of UGC, Ministry of Women and Child Development is setting up 10 Chairs indifferent fields with an aim to carry out research activities to encourage women. Theinitiative is called ‘Establishment of Chairs in the University in the Name of EminentWomen Administrators/ Artists/ Scientists/ Social reformers’. The main objective ofestablishing the Chairs in the name of eminent women from India, is to inspire women topursue higher education and to achieve excellence in their various areas of work. Thisinitiative of highlighting and celebrating women achievers of the country will motivate theyoung girls and women towards pursuing higher studies.(c): The criteria fixed by the Government for selection of Chair Professor is as under:<pre>Qualification An Academic/Scholar of outstanding track in theirrespective subjects of specializationAge 55-70 yearsScale of Pay Professors Scale of Pay : Pay level 14,Rs. 1,44,200-Rs. 2,18,200-Period ofAppointment5 years (can be extended for another five yearssubject to maximum of 10 years, not exceeding 70years of age)Duration ofthe ChairMaximum of 10 years or until the incumbent attainsthe age of 70 years whichever is earlierMode ofNominationThrough Nomination and/or invitation on therecommendation of a 3 member Committeeconsisting of two eminent persons to be constitutedby the Vice-Chancellor of the University and onenominated by UGC(d): The financial implication of establishing the Chairs is Rs. 50.00 lakhs per Chair perannum.(e) & (f): Details of the names of distinguished women in whose names the Peeth is likelyto be established are as under:S.No. Proposed name of chair Subject1. Devi Ahilyabai Holkar Administration2. Mahadevi Varma Literature3. Rani Gaidinliu Freedom Fighter (North East)4. Anandibai Gopalrao Joshi Medicine & Health5. M.S. Subbulakshmi Performing Art6. Amrita Devi (Beniwal) Forest/Wildlife Conservation7. Lilavati Mathematics8. Kamala Sohonie Science9. Lalleshwari (Lal Ded) Poetry & Mysticism10. Hansa Mehta Educational Reforms</pre>******
