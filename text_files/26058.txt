                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1664






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Review of Maintainance of Stadiums




 Hema Malini














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the number of stadiums for all types of sports constructed by the Government in the country, State-wise;(b) the details and the number of stadiums out of these which are functioning and are in regular use, State-wise;(c) whether maintenance of the said stadiums and facilities being provided by these are reviewed regularly; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the responsibility of development and maintenance of sports infrastructure rests with the State/Union Territory Governments. Central Government supplements their effort by bridging the critical gaps. Under the “Utilization and Creation/ Upgradation of Sports Infrastructure” vertical of the Khelo India Scheme, since its inception in 2016, this Ministry has sanctioned 272 sports infrastructure projects of different types across the country, such as synthetic athletic tracks, synthetic hockey fields, synthetic turf football grounds, multipurpose halls, swimming pools, etc. The State/UT-wise details of the same are at Annexure.(b) to (d) This Ministry does not maintain any State-wise data about the number of stadiums constructed/functioning in the country and the details regarding their maintenance and facilities provided.
