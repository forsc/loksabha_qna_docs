                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4329






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Storage Facilities for Farmers




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the storage facilities provided by the Government to the farmers in the country are sufficient;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government is contemplating provision of additional storage facility specially in Rajasthan;(d) 	if so, the details thereof, location-wise; and(e) 	if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):	 The Government of India is providing storage facilities through creation of Agricultural Marketing infrastructure including Scientific Storage for farmers by implementing “Agricultural Marketing Infrastructure (AMI)”, a sub-scheme of Integrated Scheme for Agricultural Marketing (ISAM) across the country including rural areas. It is a credit linked scheme wherein back ended capital subsidy @ 25% and 33.33% is available based on the eligible category of beneficiary.  Assistance is available to Farmers, Group of farmers/growers, Individuals, Agri-preneurs, Registered Farmer Produce Organizations (FPOs), Cooperatives, state agencies etc.The scheme is demand driven, therefore, no State/UT-wise allocation has been made. However, since inception, a total of 41,001 storage infrastructure projects (Godowns) with capacity of 709.00 Lakh MT have been assisted in the country benefiting number of Agri-preneurs including farmers, out of which, a total of 1585 projects, with storage capacity of 30.98 Lakh MT in Rajasthan have been assisted under AMI sub-scheme of ISAM.               ******
