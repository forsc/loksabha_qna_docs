                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3140






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Highway Projects in Karnataka




Narayana Swamy Abbaiah














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    be pleased to state the present status of various National Highways (NHs) development and widening projects in Karnataka, project-wise particularly four-laning project on Hospet-Chitradurga section of NH-13 and six-laning project on Chitradurga-Davangere section of NH-4?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)At present,  2/4/6-laning, strengthening, improvement etc. works in the length of about 3,200 km have been started during the last 4 years and the current year in the State of Karnataka, out of which, length of about 2,000 km have been completed.  Length of 117 km out of 120 km and 46.50 km out of 72.70 km has been completed in development/ upgradation to four-lane of Hospet –Chitradurga section of NH-50 (Old NH-13) and six-lane of Chitradurga – Davanagere Section of NH-48 (Old NH-4) projects respectively.*****
