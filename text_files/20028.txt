                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1207






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Purchase of Crops on MSP




Nayab Singh













                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has purchased crops under the implementation of Minimum Support Price (MSP) during Kharif season 2020 and 2021; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the names of the crops alongwith their quantity purchased under the MSP during kharif season 2020 and 2021 in Uttar Pradesh and Haryana?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) and (b): 	Department of Food & Public Distribution (DFPD) extends  price support for procurement of paddy for central pool which are procured  at Minimum Support Price (MSP) by the Food Corporation of India (FCI) and State Government agencies.   Further, Coarsegrains are procured at MSP by State Governments  as per the procurement plan prepared in consultation with FCI and approved by the Central Government, subject to  its distribution by the State Government  under National Food Security Act (NFSA)/ Other Welfare Schemes (OWS).The quantity of paddy (as on 02.02.2021) and coarsegrains (as on 18.01.2021) procured for central pool during Kharif Marketing Season 2020-21 are as under: S. No.	Items	Quantity1	Paddy	606.34 LMT2	Jawar	44334.00 MT3	Bajara	276301.00 MT4	Maize	157188.00 MT(c): 	The quantity of paddy (as on 02.02.2021) and coarsegrain (as on 18.01.2021) purchased at MSP during Kharif Marketing Season 2020 – 2021 for central pool in Uttar Pradesh and Haryana is as under: S. No.	Items	Uttar Pradesh	Haryana1	Paddy	63.71 LMT	56.12 LMT2	Bajara	-	75000.00 MT3	Maize	106413.00 MT	-****
