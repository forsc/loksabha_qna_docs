                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Placement of Women Under PMKVY




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      the details of the sectors identified by the Odisha Skill Development Authority; (b)      the number of job roles identified under the Centrally Sponsored State Managed (CSSM) component of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) for each of the sector; (c)        the placements offered in each sector every year in the last 4 years; (d)       The number of women offered placements in each sector every year in the last 4 years; and  (e)       the number of candidates who were not offered placements after successful training in the last 4 years?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)      Odisha Skill Development Authority (OSDA) had identified sectors namely; Agriculture, Apparel, Automotive, Beauty & Wellness, BFSI (Banking, Financial services & Insurance), Capital Goods, Construction, Electronics & Hardware, Furniture & Fittings, Green Jobs, Healthcare, IT-ITeS, Logistics, Media & Entertainment, Plumbing, Retail, Telecom and Tourism & Hospitality  for imparting short term training under Centrally Sponsored State Managed component of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2.0. (b)       The number of job roles in each sector in which OSDA has been imparting training under the Centrally Sponsored State Managed (CSSM) component of PMKVY 2.0 is given at Table 1 of Annexure. (c) to (e)          Under CSSM-PMKVY 2.0, as on 19.01.2021, 8,002 and 5,376 candidates have been trained and certified, respectively, in State of Odisha. Out of certified candidates, 507 candidates have been placed. Sector-wise and financial year-wise number of total and women candidates placed under CSSM-PMKVY 2.0 in State of Odisha in last four years, as on 19.01.2021, is given at Table 2 of the Annexure.
