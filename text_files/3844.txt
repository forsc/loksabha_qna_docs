                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3632






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Khader Commission Report




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the Government of Kerala has implemented the KhaderCommission Report in Secondary and Higher Secondary level in the State; and(b) if so, the details thereof along with the reason therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) and (b) The Government of Kerala has informed that the State Government had constituted anExpert Committee headed by Dr. M.A. Khader for recommending an exhaustive and full scaleimplementation of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education (RTE) Act, 2009 after acareful assessment and review of all levels of School Education (-2 to +2) system. Upon receipt of thereport, Kerala Government have approved the report ''in principle'' since the report entails a futuristicand holistic approach to transform the school education system of Kerala to ensure quality education.The State Government has also informed that they undertook consultations with representatives ofService Organizations, Managers of Corporate Educational Agencies, Representatives of Chairpersonsof Local Self Government Institutions and Students Organizations; and after considering thesuggestions that emerged in the meeting with the above mentioned stake holders, Kerala Governmenttook a policy decision to implement some of the recommendations in the first phase.***
