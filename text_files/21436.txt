                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2610






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Uranium Production




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Government has plan to fulfil the requirement of uranium to fuel nuclear power plants; (b)	if so, the time by which Department of Atomic Energy (DAE) is likely to achieve uranium production; and(c)	the details of the number of uranium plants available in country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH):(a)	Yes, Sir.(b)	Uranium Corporation of India Limited (UCIL), a Public Sector Undertaking under the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE) has prepared a vision document in line with DAE’s requirement of uranium to fuel nuclear power plants.  As per this document, next 15 years timeline has been drawn for achievement of substantial increase in Uranium Production.(c)	Presently, there are two uranium ore processing plants in the East Singhbhum district of Jharkhand and one in the YSR district of Andhra Pradesh.                                              ******
