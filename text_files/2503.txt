                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Allocation for Tribal Welfare




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether there has been a decrease in allocation of Central funds for tribal welfare over the past few years;and(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE STARRED QUESTION NO. * 220 FOR ANSWER ON 08.07.2019(a): 	No, Sir. There has not been any decrease in allocation of Central funds for Tribal Welfare over the past few years. Annual budget in respect of the Ministry of Tribal Affairs as well as those of the Scheduled Tribe Component of other Central Ministries has seen an upward trend during last three yearsas detailed below:(i) Budget Allocation of Ministry of Tribal Affairs.(Rupees in Crore)Year 	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Budget Allocation	4826.50	5329.32	6000(ii) Allocation of funds under Scheduled Tribe Component by Central Ministries/Deptts.(Rupees in Crore)Year 	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Budget Allocation	23206.14	30962.47	37802.94(b):	Does not arise in view of (a) above.*****
