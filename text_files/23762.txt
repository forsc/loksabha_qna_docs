                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Employment to Youth




Arvind Dharmapuri














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the programmes being run by the Government to connect youths of all the North-Eastern States to the various flagship programmes of the Government;(b) 	the details of the funds allocated for these programmes, State-wise; and(c) 	the number of youths who have acquired employment by participating in these programmes, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a) to (c)	Department of Youth Affairs, Government of India is implementing various schemes/programmes viz. National Youth Corps Scheme (NYCS), National Young Leaders Programme (NYLP), National Programme for Youth and Adolescent Development (NPYAD) for promoting national integration, personality and life skills development, volunteerism, leadership development and awareness on local contemporary issues confronting youth. The Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) through its programmes, schemes and projects create awareness among youth on various flagship programmes of the Government and also motivates and encourages them to avail benefit under the schemes of Government by approaching concerned departments and agencies. 	In addition, various Central Ministries/ Departments also implement different schemes/ programmes which connect the youth of the country including from the North-East Region to the Flagship Programmes of the Government.State-wise details of the funds allocated during the year 2020-2021 for programmes under Department of Youth Affairs are as follows:-<pre> S. No.	Name of State 	NYKS Scheme (Block Grant)(Rs. in Lakh)	National Youth Corp Scheme(Rs. in Lakh)	National Young Leader Programme(Rs. in Lakh)1	Arunanchal Pradesh	602	135	8.152	Assam	1,160	260	16.683	Manipur 	430	96	7.114	Meghalaya	301	67	4.265	Mizoram	344	77	4.976	Nagaland	473	106	6.027	Sikkim	172	39	2.128	Tripura 	172	39	2.12</pre>A compendium of all Flagships Programmes of the Government of India was prepared and sent to all NE States and Aspirational Districts of the North Eastern Region for awareness by North Eastern Council, Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region.
