                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

841






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Barshi Textile Mill




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of workers employed at the time when Barshi textile mill was set up along with the present status thereof; (b) whether it is a fact that Barshi textile mill was shut down due to spread of Coronavirus; (c) if so, the time by which it is likely to be reopened; (d) whether the Government has received any proposal or letter for the reopening of this mill; and (e) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a):	At the time of nationalisation of Barshi Textile Mill under The Sick Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) Act, 1974, there were 914 workers employed in this mill and at present 215 workers are employed.(b) to (e):	Due to coronavirus, the operation of the mill was put on hold since 25.3.2020.  The mill has resumed its operation with the raw material available, from the month of July 2021.  ***
