                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1935






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






PHC/CHC/District Hospital




Sanganna Amarappa Karadi













                                                    Ram Kripal Yadav
                                                








                                                    Sunita Duggal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) the details of AYUSH services being provided through upgradation of AYUSH hospitals and dispensaries, co-located Primary Health Centers (PHCs), AYUSH facilities in Community Health Centers (CHCs) and District hospitals; State/UT-wise;(b) the details regarding strengthening of institutional capacity at the State level through upgradation of AYUSH educational institutions, State Government ASU and H pharmacies, drug testing laboratories and ASU and H enforcement machinery established in the country, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government proposes to set up compulsory component AYUSH services, ASU and H quality control of medicines, medicinal plants, AYUSH educational institute in the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise, particularly in Koppal in Karnataka and Karnal and Sirsa in Haryana?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYURVEDA, YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL) (a): The Ministry of AYUSH provides financial assistance to State/UT Governments to upgrade the infrastructure and supply of essential AYUSH medicines to the AYUSH health facilities under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM).  Out- Patient services are being provided through the AYUSH hospitals and dispensaries, co-located Primary Health Centers (PHCs), AYUSH facilities in Community Health Centers (CHCs) and District hospitals. In-Patient services are provided through AYUSH hospitals & District hospitals. The State/UT-wise status of supported AYUSH hospitals & dispensaries and co-located AYUSH units are furnished at Annexure-I.(b): Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision of financial assistance for infrastructural development of AYUSH Under-Graduate & Post-Graduate Institutions for undertaking different activities like Construction of OPD/IPD/ Teaching Departments/Library/Laboratories/Girls’ Hostel/Boys’ Hostel, procurement of equipment, furniture and library books. Under NAM Grant-in-aid was provided for strengthening of State/Govt. Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy (ASU&H) Pharmacies, State Drug Testing Laboratories of ASU&H Drugs and ASU&H Drug Control Framework. The State/UT-wise status of AYUSH Educational Institutions, State Government ASU and H pharmacies, Drug testing laboratories and ASU and H enforcement mechanism supported is furnished at Annexure-II. (c) & (d): The AYUSH services and AYUSH Educational Institute components have been approved by the Central Government for continuation under NAM from 01.04.2021 to 31.03.2026. However, the Quality Control of ASU and H drugs and Medicinal Plants components have been delinked from NAM. The State/UT wise resource pool has already been communicated for the year 2021-22 and States/UTs were requested to submit the State Annual Action Plans as per the NAM guidelines and felt need & priority of the respective State/UT Government. Further, under NAM, there is no District-wise allocation made to the States/UTs to the States/UTs including in the States Karnataka and Haryana.
