                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Data under National Digital Health Mission




Abdul Khaleque













                                                    K. Muraleedharan
                                                








                                                    M. Selvaraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has collected or proposes to collect user data under the National Digital Health Mission; if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of Government and Non-Government entities that will manage the data and have access to data collected through the Mission; and(c) the manner in which personal data of persons under the National Digital Health Mission will be protected?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	National Digital Health Mission (NDHM) is being implemented in 6 Union Territories (Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Chandigarh, Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu, Lakshadweep, Ladakh and Puducherry) in Phase: I, on pilot basis.Under Phase: I, NDHM seeks to provide services like creation of Health ID for patients, Facility registry and Practitioner registry. The NDHM system can be used to create Personal Health Record for individual from data from various health information providers, facilities and laboratories.(b):	The NDHM is being directly managed by Government of India.The patient will have the option to share his personal data through a proper consent management system.(c):	The NDHM is being setup on one of the principles of ‘Privacy by Design’. The National Digital Health Blueprint (NDHB) has proposed for anonymization of data and all processes to be framed duly following the guidelines and relevant Acts like IT security Act, Personal Data Protection Act etc.
