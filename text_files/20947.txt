                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Inflation for Industrial Workers




Parvatagouda Chandanagouda Gaddigoudar














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has any proposal to implement a new series of retail inflation for industrial workers;(b)if so, the details thereof along with its features and the extent to which it will improve their earnings and livelihood; and(c)the time by which it is likely to be put into operation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	Yes, Sir.(b):	The New Series of Retail Inflation for Industrial Workers based  on Consumer Price Index for Industrial Workers has been updated with new base of 2016=100 replacing the old base of 2001=100 from September, 2020. The new series index is based on latest consumption pattern reflected by working class family income and expenditure survey (WCFI&ES) conducted in 88 industrially important centres during the year 2016. The scope and coverage of the new series has been extended in terms of number of centres, markets, outlets and items. As the dearness allowance payable to industrial workers are linked to CPI-IW, the earnings and livelihood of Industrial workers will interalia, depend on the actual movement in CPI-IW on month to month basis.(c):	It has been put into operation from September, 2020.*****
