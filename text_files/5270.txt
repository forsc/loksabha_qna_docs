                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Farmers Suicide




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has introduced 20 point programme to curb the incidents of suicides by the farmers; (b) 	if so, the outline of the above mentioned programme; (c) 	whether any directive has been issued or likely to be issued by the Government to various States of the country, if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to withhold farmers loan waive-off and write-off agriculture loan of farmers under the programme which is the main reason of suicide; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):       There is no such proposal. However, Government is providing financial assistance to States/ UTs through the various programmes for increasing agriculture production, productivity and returns to farmers.(d) & (e):        There is no such proposal of Government of India. ********
