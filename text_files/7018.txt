                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

810






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Credit Guarantee Fund




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Rahul Gandhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up a Credit Guarantee Fund for encouraging startup companies in the country with a view to providing funding facilities and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the number of companies that have availed the scheme so far and the details of the incentives being provided to the startup companies to overcome their initial hurdles;(c)	whether the Government has conducted any survey to assess the adverse affects of angel tax on the Indian startup eco-system;(d)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to address the concerns of entrepreneurs and investors regarding angel tax;(e)	whether the Ministry has taken up initiatives to encourage Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in aspirational districts, particularly Wayanad and if so, the details thereof; and(f)	whether collateral-free loan has been made available to MSMEs under the Credit Guarantee Trust Fund in aspirational districts, especially Wayanad and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI) (a):	National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company Ltd. has already been set up by the Department of Financial Services, Ministry of Finance, Government of India to function as a common trustee company to manage and operate various credit guarantee trust funds. To meet the funding needs of Startups, Government of India has established Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) under Startup India Programme. (b):	Under Startup India initiative, all applications can be submitted to the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade. The incentives provided to Startups under Startup India initiative are as follows: i.	Self-Certification under 6 Labour Laws & 3 Environmental Laws.ii.	Relaxations in Public Procurement Norms.iii.	Faster exit under the Bankruptcy Code.iv.	Rebates on Patent & Trademark filing fees, support from facilitators and expedited examination of Patent application.v.	Income Tax exemption under section 80 IAC. vi.	Exemption from Income Tax on investments received above fair market value under clause (ii) of the proviso to clause (viib) of sub-section (2) of section 56 of the Income Tax Act. vii.	Funding support under the Fund of Funds for Startups. viii.	Guidance and facilitation support from Startup India Hub.(c):	No Sir. (d):	 Does not arise in view of (c) above. (e):	 Yes Sir. Ministry of MSME has taken several initiatives to encourage Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises. This covers the Aspirational Districts across the Country including Wayanad in Kerala. These initiatives  include the schemes/programmes such as Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry and Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE), Credit Guarantee Scheme (CGTMSE), Credit linked Capital Subsidy-Technology Upgradation Scheme (CLCS-TUS), Micro & Small Enterprises- Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), National Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe Hub (NSSH), Procurement and Marketing Schemes (PMS) etc. National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) under the Ministry of MSME also boost and supports Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) Sector by providing integrated support services encompassing marketing, finance, technology and other services.  Till date, 1254 number of units are registered under Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum (UAM) in Wayanad District. (f):	Yes Sir. Collateral-free loans have been made available to MSMEs under the Credit Guarantee Scheme in Aspirational Districts including Wayanad District. Credit Guarantee Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) has approved 4299 guarantees amounting to Rs. 103.17 Crore (upto October 31, 2019) in Waynad District of Kerala since the inception of the Scheme.
