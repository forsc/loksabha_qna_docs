                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Decline in Production of Heavy Industries




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether productivity of heavy industries has been affected drastically in the country due to Corona Pandemic;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government have formulated any action plan to increase the production; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) to (d): The productivity of industrial sectors under the Ministry of Heavy Industries has been affected due to Corona Pandemic because of disruption in domestic/ global supply chains, frequent disruptions in operations of factories and localised containment measures.Steps taken to deal with the situation and increase production inter-alia include active dissemination of guidelines related to containing the spread of Covid-19 and adhering to social distancing and other Covid-19 protocols, vaccination of employees, addressing liquidity issues of companies, thrust on faster adoption and manufacturing of electric vehicles, introduction of Production linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for manufacturing Advanced Chemistry Cells (ACC) etc.-----------
