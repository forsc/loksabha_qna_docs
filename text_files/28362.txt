                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

216






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






MoU Between Science Museums and CSIR




Talari Rangaiah













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the National Council of Science Museums have signed an MoU with CSIR for the setting up of Science Museums at selected CSIR laboratories to promote scientific temper among the people of India;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether there is any proposal to establish a science museum in the State of Andhra Pradesh; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM & DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)&(b):	Yes, Sir. An MOU has been signed between National Council of Science Museums (NCSM) & CSIR on 29.09.2021 for setting up of Science Museums/Centres within selected CSIR premises for popularization of science in the country. (c)&(d):	NCSM is setting up a Science Centre (Category-II) at Rajahmundry in Andhra Pradesh at the cost of Rs.15.20 crores, shared between the Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India (Rs.6.55 crore) and Govt. of Andhra Pradesh (Rs.8.65 crore).*****
