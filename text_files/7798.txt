                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1418






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Coal Supply to Power Plants




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                








                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of coal supplied by the Government to thermal power stations of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan and the year-wise details thereof;(b) whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Governments regarding shortage of coal supply and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the States have surplus quantity of coal for thermal power and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of quantum of coal supplied to industries and private sector in Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan and the details thereof? 






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a): The coal supplied by Coal India Limited (CIL) & Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL) to Thermal Power Stations of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan for the year 2018-19 and current year up to 31.10.2019 is as under:(quantity in Million Tonnes)Year	2018-19	2019-20 (Up to 31.10.2019)* 	CIL	SCCL	CIL	SCCLUttar Pradesh	75.72	-	38.70	0.004Maharashtra	59.95	5.547	31.27	3.075Rajasthan	13.33	-	6.90	-* Figures are provisional(b) & (c): The coal stocks at different power houses decline in certain phases due to logistics issue. However, whenever such decline is observed, coal companies take immediate necessary action to replenish the stock. In addition to the monitoring mechanism available at coal companies, coal supplies to Power Sector is monitored regularly by an inter-ministerial sub-group comprising representatives of Ministry of Power, Ministry of Coal and Ministry of Railways, CEA, CIL etc. constituted by Infrastructure review Committee of Cabinet Secretariat. This Sub-Group takes various operational decisions for meeting any contingent situations relating to Power Sector including critical coal stock position for power plants. As a result of these steps taken for augmentation of supplies, the coal stock at Power House end improved significantly. Presently as on 21.11.2019, the coal stock at Power House end stands at 23.09 Million Tonne (MT), equivalent to 14 days consumption, as against the stock of 11.45 MT on 21.11.2018 which was equivalent to 7 days consumption. (d): The total coal supplied by CIL and SCCL to Non-Power Sector of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan in the year 2018-19 and the current year (up to 31.10.2019) is as under: (quantity in Million tonnes)Year	2018-19	2019-20 (Upto 31.10.2019)* 	CIL	SCCL	CIL	 Uttar Pradesh	15.71	-	9.08	-Maharashtra	8.32	0.560	4.63	0.202Rajasthan	0.77	-	0.62	- * Figures are provisional. *****
