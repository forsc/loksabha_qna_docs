                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3087






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Linking of Birth Places of Folk Deities of Rajasthan under Religious Tourism




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names and places of the circuits in Rajasthan which have been identified under Swadesh Darshan Scheme including the funds allocated in this regard along with the item-wise details of the utilized funds; (b) 	whether considering the glorious history of folk deities in Rajasthan, the Government proposes to seek proposal to include their birthplaces under this scheme; (c) 	if so, the time by which the Government proposes to seek the said proposal along with the details thereof; (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor along with the details thereof; (e) 	whether the Government proposes to formulate any scheme to link the birthplaces and temples of folk deities in all the States of the country including Rajasthan under religious tourism; and (f) 	if so, the time by which the Government proposes to formulate the said scheme along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (f):	Development and Promotion of Tourist places is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/Union Territory (UT) Administration. However, Ministry under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides financial Assistance to the State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. No new project from Rajasthan is under consideration under the Scheme by Ministry of Tourism. List containing names of places, funds allocated and utilized for Projects sanctioned in Rajasthan under Swadesh Darshan Scheme is annexed. *******
