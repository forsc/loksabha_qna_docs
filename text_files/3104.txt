                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2920






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Scholarship from NDF




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Nitesh Ganga Deb
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government grants scholarship to the children of working, retired and deceased defence and para-military force personnel through ‘National Defence Fund’ (NDF);(b) if so, the details thereof and the eligibility criteria for the same; (c) whether the amount of National Defence Fund has been increased or propose to be increased and if so, the details thereof;(d)  the total amount paid as scholarship so far through the ‘National Defence Fund’ across the country, State-wise; and  (e)  whether Government proposes to extend the facility to the children of State Police officers who die in terror and maoist attack and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  Yes, Sir. The Government grants scholarship to the children of working (except defence and State Police), retired and deceased defence and para-military force personnel through National Defence Fund. (b)  The Prime Minister’s Scholarship Scheme (PMSS) under the aegis of the National Defence Fund was instituted with effect from 2006-07. Scholarships are available for higher education at  technical  institutions  (medical,  dental,  veterinary,  engineering, MBA and MCA etc.) and are available for a period of four years for each student and 5 years for medical students. The academic requirement for enrolment in the scheme is 60% marks at 10+2 level and 50% marks for renewal each year. The existing rates of scholarship (Academic Year 2019-20) is Rs. 2500/- per month for boys and Rs. 3000/- per month for girls. The details of the scholarship and budget provision are given below:-S. no.	Nodal Ministry	Number and amount of scholarship @ Rs. 2000/Rs.2250 for boys/girls respectively for AY(2018-19)	Number and amount of scholarship at enhanced rate Rs.2500/Rs.3000 pm (for boys/girls respectively for AY (2019-20)	Additional Budget		Number of scholarship	Total amount per annum	Number of scholarship	Total amount per annum	i	Defence	5500	14,02,50,000	5500	18,15,00,000	4,12,50,000ii	Home	2000	5,10,00,000	2000	6,60,00,000	1,50,00,000iii	Railway	150	38,25,000	150	49,50,000	11,25,000 iv	State Police(MHA)	Does not exist		500	1,65,00,000	1,65,00,000	Total	7650	19,50,75,000	8150	26,89,50,000	7,38,75,000(c)  Yes, Sir. The amount of National Defence Fund has been increased.  The details of the amount increase are shown in above table of part (b).(d)  The total amount paid as scholarship so far through National Defence Fund is Rs.347.50 crore. The State wise breakup is not maintained as the allotment of the scholarship is based on merit list. The breakup of the total amount paid as scholarship so far Ministry wise is as under:-Ministries	Amount (Rs. in crore)Ministry of Defence	Rs. 299.73Ministry of Home Affairs	Rs.   41.72Ministry of Railways	Rs.     6.05Total	Rs. 347.50(e)  Yes, Sir. The scheme has been extended to the wards of State Police Personnel who are killed during terror/Naxal attacks from the Academic Year 2019-20 onwards.  A budgetary amount of Rs. 1.65 crore has been made for this category for 500 scholarship @ Rs.2500/Rs.3000 for boys/girls respectively. The nodal Ministry for such scholarship will be Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA).*******
