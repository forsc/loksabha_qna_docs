                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

690






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Conversion of Coaches into Covid Care Units




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian













                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken effective steps to open Passenger railway transport as its operations were halted amid the outbreak of the COVID-19;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the approximate time by which the passenger train services are to be restored;(c)	the appropriate action plan decided or to be taken by the Government to mitigate the problems faced by people once the train services restart all over the country after COVID-19 lockdown;(d)	whether the Government has provided large number of rail coaches for temporary COVID-19 treatment and care units during the lockdown crisis;(e) 	if so, the details thereof and the utility status of such transformed rail coaches in the country, railway zone-wise; and(f) 	the approximate time by which these transformed coaches are likely to be put into passenger transport again?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f)  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
