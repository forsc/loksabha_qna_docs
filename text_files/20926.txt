                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






PMKVY in Tamil Nadu




S. Jothimani














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of skill development initiatives taken under PMKVY 2.0 and PMKVY 3.0 in Tamil Nadu and Karur constituency, in the last two years; (b)  the number of women enrolled and trained in the NSTI Trichy during the last three years; (c)   the details of start ups and Entrepreneurs supported via the Pilot project on Entrepreneurship by the State; and (d)  the details regarding implementation of STRIVE in Tamil Nadu and especially Karur constituency;






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       Under PMKVY 2.0, as on 19.01.2021, 5.2 lakh candidates have been trained in the State of Tamil Nadu including 7,317 candidates in Karur constituency. (b)       Financial year-wise women trained in NSTI (W) Trichy for the last three years areas follows: S. No.	FY	Women Trained1	2017-18	3472	2018-19	2953	2019-20	127 (c)        Ministry is implementing a pilot project on Pradhan Mantri Yuva Udyamita Vikas Abhiyan (PM-YUVA Yojana) to create an enabling ecosystem for entrepreneurship development through entrepreneurship awareness, entrepreneurship education, training, advocacy, and easy access to entrepreneurship network, with focus on student/ trainees and alumnae from the skilling ecosystem. Under Entrepreneurship Awareness Programs (Student Orientation), 34,760 candidates have been engaged including 2,995 candidates in State of Tamil Nadu. Further, Mentoring camps are being organised to provide support to start-up and scale-up the enterprises. Consequently, 13 and 16 enterprises have been start-up and scaled-up, respectively, in the State of Tamil Nadu. (d)       Skills Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE) is a national project of the Ministry of Skills Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) in collaboration with World Bank. In the state of Tamil Nadu 16 ITIs and 03 Industry Clusters has been selected under STRIVE scheme. Under the project, Rs. 17.24 Cr. have been released to State of Tamil Nadu so far.
