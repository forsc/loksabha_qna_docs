                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5530






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Comprehensive Drug Monitoring System




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether it has come to the notice of his Ministry about the introduction of Comprehensive Drug Monitoring System in Andhra Pradesh to link Aadhaar with doctors and pharmacies to weed out quacks and generate e-prescriptions and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has studied the same to replicate in other States; and (c) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken so far in this direction?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):     As per the information received from the Drug Control Administration of the State of Andhra Pradesh, Comprehensive Drug Monitoring System (CDMS) is not introduced in the State of Andhra Pradesh. ……………………..
