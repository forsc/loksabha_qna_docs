                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4402






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Social Welfare Schemes




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4402 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021SOCIAL WELFARE SCHEMES 4402. DR.VISHNU PRASAD M.K.: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of the social welfare schemes being implemented by the Government under public private partnership; (b) whether the Government proposes to organize any national level programme to popularize the said schemes; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) the action taken so far in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a):	No Sir, there is no social welfare scheme being implemented by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment under public private partnership.(b) to (d):   Does not arise.******
