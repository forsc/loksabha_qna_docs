                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

139






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






NEEM




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the State-wise distribution of registrants under the National Employability EnhancementMission(NEEM) Scheme;(b) the State-wise distribution of NEEM facilitators/NEEM Agents under the NEEM Scheme;and(c) number of registrants/trainee sunder the NEEM scheme whose employment have beenregularized/recruited into organizations after the completion of their training under theNEEM Scheme?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The state-wise distribution of registrants and facilitators under the NationalEmployability Enhancement Mission (NEEM) Scheme is at annexure I and II respectively.(c): NEEM Scheme is not an employment guarantee scheme. As per the data available withAICTE, 21 NEEM facilitators and 2574 no. of trainees have been regularized/ placed in theirtrainee company after the completion of their training under the NEEM Scheme.*****
