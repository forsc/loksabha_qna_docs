                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1207






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Bonded Labour Rehabilitation Scheme, 2016




Dean Kuriakose













                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has maintained data on the proposals received from States for reimbursement of funds towards bonded labour rehabilitation under the Bonded Labour Rehabilitation Scheme, 2016;(b)if so, the details thereof and whether such data is publicly accessible and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c)the details of the amounts requested by states for reimbursement of bonded labour rehabilitation under the aforesaid Scheme, with the corresponding details of reimbursement by the Union Government, State and year-wise from 2016 till date? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c):	Yes, Sir. Such data on the reimbursement proposals from States/UTs are available with the Government. The amounts requested by States for reimbursement of bonded labour rehabilitation have    been reimbursed by the Union Government. The details are enclosed at the Annexure.*                                            ********
