                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3222






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Setting up of New Projects




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that Andhra Pradesh, as per Project Today Report, is the second most favoured destination as far as setting up of new projects are concerned;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of sectors in which investments have been received in Andhra Pradesh;(d)	whether the Government would help Andhra Pradesh in becoming a major destination for investments in the country; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARJUN RAM MEGHWAL)(a) to (e): The information as sought is not collated by the Department of Heavy Industry. The Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) policy is an enabling policy that is uniformly applicable across the country. The Government reviews this policy from time to time to ensure that India remains an attractive and investor friendly destination.*****
