                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1870






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Open Air Prisons




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to open new open air prisons in the country during the year 2020-21;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to convert some existing prisons into open air prisons in future; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d) : No such proposal is under consideration of the Central Government. ‘Prisons’ and ‘persons detained therein’ are ‘State’ subjects as per Entry 4 of List II of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India. The State Governments are competent to establish new open air prisons and to convert any existing prison into open air prison in their respective jurisdictions.******
