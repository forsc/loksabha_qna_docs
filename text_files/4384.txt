                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3984






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Mobile Connectivity Scheme in LWE Affected Areas




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the State Government of Odisha has sent a list of 483 locations to the Government for installation of mobile towers in the Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas of the State under phase-II of the Mobile Connectivity Scheme in “LWE affected areas”; (b) 	if so, the details and the status thereof and the response of the Government thereon; and (c) 	the status of installation of mobile towers in 368 of such agreeable locations along with the steps taken by the Government to expedite the said installation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	Yes Sir. The Union Cabinet has approved the ‘Universal Service Obligation fund (USOF) supported scheme to provide mobile services at 4072 tower locations, out of these, 483 towers were sanctioned for Odisha. However, Odisha Government had provided locations of 252 towers only.(b)	Tower locations details:<pre>S. No	District 	No. of Towers	Status 1	Boudh	8	LWE Phase –II tender is under preparation.2	Bargarh	3	3	Nuapada	36	4	Nabrangpur	35	5	Malkangiri	76	6	Koraput	94		Total 	252	</pre>(c)	No Such agreeable 368 mobile tower locations are there. However, Floating of RFP for 3465 number of towers (as received by MHA, including 252 towers of  Odisha) is under preparation.
