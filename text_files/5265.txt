                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4825






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Supply of Coconut Seedlings




Dhanush M Kumar













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that coconut seedlings are being produced through some private coconut nurseries; (b) 	whether the Government is supporting coconut nurseries in private sector by extending financial assistance under Coconut Development Board’s scheme and if so, the quantum of funds and assistance provided during each of the last three years and the current year; (c) 	the number of private coconut nurseries which are operating in the country especially in the State of Tamil Nadu; (d) 	whether the Government has any regulation to control them and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the number of regional coconut nurseries functioning in the country along with the achievement made by each regional coconut nursery?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Yes, Sir.(b);	Yes, Sir. The assistance provided during each of the last three years and the current year is as under: Contd…2/--2-Year 	Number of units	Financial assistance (Rs. in lakhs)2016-17	22	9.592017-18	24	20.372018-19	7	8.05During the current year, an amount of Rs.203.25 lakh is allocated for assisting small Coconut Nurseries in 9 States. (c):	A total of 120 no. of coconut nurseries by individuals or on community basis by Self Help Groups/Farmer Producers’ Organizations have been assisted by Coconut Development Board (CDB) including 5 in the State of Tamil Nadu during the last five years i.e. from 2014-15 to 2018-19. (d):	The nurseries which are established as per the guidelines of the Board, are financially assisted by the CDB.  Nurseries which adhere to the guidelines and which operate satisfactorily for a period of 5 years are considered for the accreditation programme of the CDB.(e):	The scheme ‘Regional Coconut Nurseries’ was operational in 36 farms of 8 States during last year for production of 7.30 lakh coconut seedlings.  The State-wise details are annexed.
