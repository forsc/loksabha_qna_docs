                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Khelo India Sports Centre




S.R. Parthiban













                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to start one Khelo India Sports Centre/Khel India Centre in every district of the country including the State of Tamil Nadu;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any plan has been prepared for the same and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details and the number of proposals received from Tamil Nadu in this regard till date and the specific games to which these are related?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) to (c) Yes, Sir. The Union Government has decided to start one to two Khelo India Centre(s) in each district across the country, including in the State of Tamil Nadu, on the basis of the proposals received from various States/UTs.(d) One proposal has been received from the Sports Development Authority of Tamil Nadu, Government of Tamil Nadu, for setting up of a Khelo India Centre in Kanyakumari for Athletics.*****
