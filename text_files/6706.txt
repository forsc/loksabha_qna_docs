                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

355






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Laying of Railway Lines




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry of Home Affairs has received any proposal from the State Government of Maharashtra to give 50 per cent share of the amount likely to be expended for the laying of railway lines in naxal affected area of the country, especially in Vadhasa-Gadchiroli and Nagpur-Nagbhir;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the action taken by the Government so far in this regard;(d) the reasons for delay in giving 50 per cent amount by the Ministry for the laying of railway lines in naxal affected areas; and(e) the proposed time-line to complete the said works?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	No Sir, Ministry of Home Affairs has not received any such proposal.*****
