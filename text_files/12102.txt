                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

948






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Facilities at Tata Memorial Center




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has taken note of the fact that the Tata Memorial Center, Mumbai is flooded with thousands of patients, if so, whether the Government proposes to put in place a proper monitoring system for its smooth functioning controlled by the Health Ministry keeping in view the increasing inflow of patients; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) the other steps taken by the Government to prioritize the treatments based on the severity of the disease?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):   As informed by Department of Atomic Energy (DAE), considering the increase in the number of cancer patients visiting Tata Memorial Centre (TMC), Mumbai for treatment, Government of India has sanctioned 6 new cancer hospitals in various parts of the country. Out of this, a hospital in Sangrur, Punjab and two hospitals in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh are already commissioned and started functioning. A 100 bedded hospital in Visakhapatnam , Andhra Pradesh and another 250 bedded cancer hospital in Mullanpur, Punjab are in advanced stages of being commissioned. In addition TMC/DAE has taken over 260 bedded Dr. Bhubaneswar Borooah Cancer Institute (BBCI), Guwahati, Assam. Over 50,000 cancer patients have received treatment at these centres. To reduce inflow of patients in Tata Memorial Hospital (TMH), Mumbai, TMC has begun web based second opinion services, where more than 30000 opinions have been given.DAE has further informed that, as far as TMC is concerned the clinicians triage patients who require immediate attention as compared to those where treatment is not on an emergency basis. TMC also have a full fledged casualty department to deal with emergency and complications. ...................
