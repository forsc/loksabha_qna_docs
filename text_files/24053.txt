                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Aquatic Sports




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps taken to promote aquatic sports in the country especially in Kerala;(b)	whether the Union Government intends to establish an advance academy of aquatic sports;(c)	if so, whether any proposal has been submitted by the government of Kerala to the Union Government in this regard; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the funds allocated for the purpose? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a)	‘Sports’ being a State subject, responsibility for promotion and development of sports rests with the concerned State / UT Government. However Government of India supplements the efforts to promote aquatic sports through Swimming Federation of India (SFI) and Sports Authority of India(SAI), which assists the government to prepare sportspersons for national and international competitions, and also by developing sports infrastructure from time to time as per the requirement. (b) to (d) At present no proposal relating to establishing an advance academy of aquatic sports is under consideration of the Central Government. However, Central Government, through SAI, has set up training centers for Swimming across the country including in state of Kerala. The information of such training centers is given in Annexure.*****
