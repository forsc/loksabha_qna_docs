                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1114






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






MoU with UK in Crop Science




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has achieved the various aims and objectives laid down during formalizing the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Government of UK in Crop Science;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor along with the time by which such aims and objectives are likely to be achieved by the Government;(c)	whether the Government proposes to sign such MoUs with other countries;(d)	if so, the details thereof, country-wise; and(e)	the other steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY, EARTH SCIENCE AND HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a)& (b) 	Yes sir. To achieve the aims and objectives of the MoU under consideration, thirteen institutions (7 institutions from UK and 6 institutions from India) have come together and formally recorded their intent to establish joint India-UK partnership in crop sciences. The agreement was signed on 21/01/2020. Ten Indian Postdoctoral Fellows are being supported per year for three successive calendar years starting from 2020. The partnership is on enhanced research and knowledge exchange with a focus on developing Resilience in food security,Plant-pathogen/pest interactions, Wheat pre-breeding and Computational Biology.(c) to (e)   No such proposal is under active consideration of the Department.*****
