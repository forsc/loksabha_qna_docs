                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1901






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Maternal Mortality Rate (MMR)




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether Maternal Mortality Rate (MMR) has registered any decline during the last three years in the country;(b) if so, the details of MMR during the said period, State/UT-wise;(c) the expenditure incurred by the Government to bring down the Maternal Mortality Rate during the last three years, State/UT-wise particularly in Delhi?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b): As per the Sample Registration System (SRS) report by Registrar General of India (RGI) for the last three years, Maternal Mortality Ratio (MMR) of India has reduced from 130 per 100,000 live births in SRS  2014-16 to 122 in SRS 2015-17 and to 113 per 100,000 live births in SRS 2016-18.During the said period, the State/UT wise details of MMR given at Annexure-I.(c): State/UT wise expenditure incurred during the last 3-year period, from FY 2017-18 to FY 2019-20 under NRHM-RCH flexible Pool including Delhi is given at Annexure-II.
