                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

62






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether implementation of Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana is being implemented in the State of Maharashtra;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the funds released for the projects under the said scheme in the State of Maharashtra so far; and(d) the up to date status of these projects?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Yes(b):	Following projects have been undertaken by the Government under various phases of Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) in the State of Maharashtra: i.	Setting up of new AIIMS in Nagpur (Phase-IV)ii.	Up-gradation of Government Medical Colleges:1.	Grant Medical College, Mumbai (Phase-I).2.	Government Medical College, Nagpur (Phase-II).3.	Government Medical College, Aurangabad (Phase-III).4.	Government Medical College, Latur (Phase-III).5.	Government Medical College, Akola (Phase-III).6.	Shri Vasantrao Naik Government Medical College, Yavatmal (Phase-III).(c):	The details of funds released for the projects under PMSSY in the State of Maharashtra are given in Annexure.(d):	The up to date status of the projects is given in Annexure.
