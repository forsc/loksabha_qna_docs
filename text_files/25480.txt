                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1192






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






National Fisheries Development Board




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether the  Government  has  set  up  National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB) for development of fisheries sector;(b) 	if so, the details of schemes implemented by the Board  along  with  the  achievements  made  and  funds spent  during  the  last  three  years,  scheme-wise  and State-wise;(c)  	whether the  NFDB  performs  any  activities  in training and extension facilities to Fishermen;(d) 	if so, the details thereof including beneficiaries therefrom, State-wise;(e) 	the details of projects undertaken along with the achievements  made  and  funds  spent  during  the  last three years, scheme-wise and State-wise;(f)  	whether  the  Government  has  reviewed  the functioning of the NFDB;(g) 	if so, the details thereof; and(h)  	the  details  of  other  steps  being  taken  by  the Government to strengthen the NFDB?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING		(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) to (e):	Yes, Sir. The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying had in July, 2006 set up National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB) with its head office at Hyderabad for development of fisheries in the country. The National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB) since its inception has taken up various fisheries developmental activities like cage culture, domestic marketing, ornamental fisheries, technology upgradation, seaweed cultivation, wetland development, quality seed production, species diversification, aquatic quarantine facilities, Aquatic Animal Health Laboratories, use of Solar power in fisheries and Innovative activities. Besides, the Board has funded various Training and Extension programmes for skill development of fishers, fish farmers and fisheries officials. Activity-wise and year-wise details of the funds released by NFDB during the past three years for development of fisheries including human resource development are furnished at Annexure-I.(f) and (g):	Yes, Sir. The activities and functioning of the National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB) has been periodically reviewed through the Executive Committee (EC) chaired by Secretary, Department of Fisheries and Governing Body(GB) chaired by Union Minister for Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying and provided guidance from time-to-time. Since inception of NFDB, 39 meetings of the Executive Committee (EC) and 8 meetings of Governing Body (GB) have been held so far to review the activities of NFDB and to oversee its functions.(h):	    Under the  “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) - A scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” implemented by the Department of Fisheries(DoF), Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying,  NFDB has been  entrusted with the work of appraisal of proposals received from the States/UTs. Besides, under PMMSY, NFDB has been designated as a nodal agency for implementing Capacity building programmes, Entrepreneur Development Models, Insurance for fishers (GAIS) and subvention of insurance premium for fishing vessels, Fish Farmers Producer Organizations(FFPOs), traceability & certification, river ranching and outreach activities. NFDB is also the Nodal Implementing Agency (NIA) of ‘Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund’ (FIDF) implemented by the DoF. The NIA under FIDF, scrutinizes, evaluates, apprises the proposal submitted by Eligible Entities (EEs).
