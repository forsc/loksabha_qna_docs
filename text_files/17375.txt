                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

877






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Proposal for Development of Airstrips




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta













                                                    Chandra Prakash Choudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering new proposals for the development of air strips throughout the country and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the States of Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh have communicated to the Government in this regard and if so, the details thereof along with the names of designated places for this purpose; and(c) whether the Government has any plan to utilize the pilgrimage tourism potential of the States of Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Government of India, Ministry of Civil Aviation has launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Naagrik) in October, 2016 with the objective to facilitate / stimulate regional air connectivity to  underserved and unserved airports. The revival of airstrips/airports is ''''demand driven'''', depending upon firm commitment from airline operators as well as from the State Government for providing various concessions. Development of Bikaner, Jaisalmer, Kishangarh, Kota and Uterlai airports in Rajasthan and Agra, Aligarh, Prayagraj, Bareilly, Kanpur, Chitrakoot, Aayodhya, Gazipur, Jhansi, Moradabad, Kushinagar, Muirpur, Azamgarh, Shravasti, Saharanpur and Hindon in Uttar Pradesh has been undertaken by Airports Authority of India (AAI) under RCS-UDAN.(c): Development of Kishangarh Airport in Rajasthan and Chitrakoot, Ayodhya, Kushinagar and Shravasti airports in Uttar Pradesh has been undertaken by AAI to utilize the pilgrimage tourism potential of the area.
