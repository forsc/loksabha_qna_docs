                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1273






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Naturopathy Wellness Centre




Balak Nath














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to set up any naturopathy wellness centres in various parts of the country, especially in Rajasthan; (b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which such centres are likely to be set up; (c) if not, the reasons therefor; (d) whether specific guidelines have been set in this regard, if so, the details thereof; and (e) whether a new naturopathy policy is likely to be formulated in the near future and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (d):      Public Health is a State subject. Setting up of Naturopathy Wellness Centres comes under purview of State/UT Governments. However, Central Government is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in the country including Rajasthan under which there is a provision for financial assistance to States/UTs for setting up of AYUSH Wellness Centres including Yoga & Naturopathy. However, no proposal received from the State of Rajasthan for this purpose under National AYUSH Mission.(e):      There is no such proposal.………………
