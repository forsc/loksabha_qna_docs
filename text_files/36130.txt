                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3445






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Funds to Assam for Rice Distribution




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the funds allocated by the Government to Assam for distribution of rice under National Food Security Act(NFSA) and other welfare schemes in 2021; and (b) 	the number of beneficiaries under NFSA in Assam, District-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):	The details of funds released to State Government of Assam under the scheme of “Assistance to State Agency towards intra-State movement & handling of foodgrains and fair price shop dealers’ margin under the NFSA” for meeting the expenditure towards intra-State movement & handling of foodgrains and fair price shop dealers’ margin under the National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) and Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) during the financial year 2021-22, are as follows:(In crore)NFSA	PMGKAY	Total220.79	324.96	545.75(b): 	The number of beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Act, 2013 in the State of Assam is as follows:(In lakh)Antyodaya Anna Yojana	Number of persons under priority households	Total number of persons under NFSANumber of households	Number of Persons		6.92	28.08	223.08	251.17District wise data is not maintained by the Central Government.*****
