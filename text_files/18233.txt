                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1890






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Houses Constructed under PMAY-U




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of houses constructed under each component of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) and allotted to the beneficiaries under its each component, State-wise;(b)	the details of houses constructed under the yojana that are vacant, State-wise;(c)	the details of the social-audit conducted as per the guidelines of PMAY-U, State-wise; and(d)	whether sufficient social audits have been conducted and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b): The State/ Union Territory-wise details of houses constructed and allotted to the beneficiaries along with houses in process of allotment under each component of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) are at Annexure. (c)	& (d): States/ Union Territories (UTs) are undertaking social audit of the projects being implemented as per the guidelines of PMAY-U Mission. So far, seven States namely Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Jharkhand, Kerala, Uttarakhand, Manipur and Mizoram have conducted social audit and remaining States/UTs are in the process of conducting Social Audit as per Scheme Guidelines.                                    *****
