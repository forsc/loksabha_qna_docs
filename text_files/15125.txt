                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3714






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Expansion of Visa on Arrival Facilityto Promote Tourism




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Chirag Paswan
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has extended the ‘Visa on Arrival’ facility to promote tourism sector;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the number of foreign tourists has increased after expansion of ‘Visa on Arrival’ facility; and(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government has any proposal to enlist more countries under the said scheme to increase foreign tourism in the near future; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d):	Presently nationals of Japan, South Korea & UAE have been granted the facility of visa-on-arrival (VOA). This facility can be availed by such nationals for business, tourism, conference and medical purposes.--2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 3714 FOR 17.03.2020Number of VOA passengers from these countries during the period 2017 to 2019 is given below:	2017	                    2018	                    2019Japan	12025	                  13053	                   13810South Korea	-	                    7735	                   28098United Arab Emirates	-	-	                       434(e) & (f):	Expansion of VOA facility is an ongoing process which is taken up after appropriate consultation with all stakeholders.******
