                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2246






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Rail Connectivity to Dataganj




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any proposal has been received by the Railways to provide rail connectivity to Dataganj falling  under the Parliamentary constituency, Aonla;(b)	if so, the action taken by the Government in this regard so far; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	Requests/demands for new lines, gauge conversion and doubling, both formal as well as informal, are received at various levels, Railway Board, Zonal Railways and Divisional Headquarters, etc.  As receipt of such requests/suggestions/demands is a continuous and dynamic process, centralized compendium of such requests is not maintained. Presently there is no proposal to provide rail connectivity to Dataganj under Parliamentary Constituency Aonla.   (b)	 and (c)  Do not arise. *****
