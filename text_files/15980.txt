                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4379






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Geriatric Healthcare




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether geriatric healthcare is part of the primary healthcare services in Government hospitals in the country, especially in Rajasthan; (b) if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether there are any Government sponsored insurance schemes which cover geriatric care for the elderly without hospitalisation; and(d) the number of Government hospitals with geriatric care units in the country, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):      Government of India is implementing National Programme for Health Care of the Elderly (NPHCE) with a view to provide geriatric health care facilities to the elderly population at various levels of state health care delivery system at primary, secondary and tertiary health care including outreach services.       Objective of the programme is to provide accessible, affordable, comprehensive and dedicated health care services to elderly population. The programme has been sanctioned for implementation in 713 districts of the country including 33 districts of State of Rajasthan.     Following health care services at district and sub district level are being provided under the programme:•	Geriatric OPD and 10 bed Geriatric Ward at   District Hospitals.•	Bi-weekly Geriatric Clinic at Community Health Centres   (CHCs) •	Weekly Geriatric Clinic at Primary Health Centre   (PHCs). (c):     Elderly health care services are also covered in the expanded range of services under Ayushman Bharat – Health and Wellness Centres.  Under the Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, which provides for health cover of Rs.5.00 lakh per family, to the poor and vulnerable families as per socio-economic caste census data, there is no restriction on age or family size and all members of the entitled family, including elderly, are covered.(d):      The State/UT wise details of district hospitals sanctioned for 10 bedded geriatric care units under NPHCE, is at Annexure-A.………………
