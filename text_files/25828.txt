                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1621






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Construction of Houses in Uttar Pradesh




Vijay Kumar Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of houses constructed by the Government under various schemes during the last three years in Uttar Pradesh Region, district-wise; (b)	the amount of expenditure incurred thereon, year-wise;(c)	the number of houses constructed for people belonging to Uttar Pradesh; and (d) 	the other the steps taken/being taken by the Government to provide housing facilities to all the tribal population of the country?






ANSWER




                                                                         THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE  MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)& (b): The district-wise details of total number of houses constructed by the Government under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) scheme during the last three years in the Uttar Pradesh Region and year-wise amount of expenditure incurred thereon, are at Annexure. (c): So far, 17,29,421 houses have been sanctioned for people belonging to Uttar Pradesh under PMAY-U, out of which 8,65,295 houses have been completed and delivered to the eligible beneficiaries.(d): Under PMAY-U scheme, demand survey for providing housing facilities is conducted by States and Union Territories (UTs) based on the eligibility criteria laid in the scheme guidelines. All eligible beneficiaries irrespective of caste, creed and religion including Tribal population of the country are covered in PMAY-U scheme with the vision of providing “Housing for All”.                                 *****
