                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1993






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Construction of Bypass




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of heavy traffic jam in Bhiwani town and Charkhi Dadri due to absence of bypass in the said locations; (b) if so, the steps taken by the Government to address the problem of the said traffic jams; (c) whether the Government proposes to construct bypasses at the said locations; (d) if so, the details and the present status thereof; and (e) the time by which the said work is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V. K. SINGH (RETD.)](a) to (c) Yes, Sir. Bypasses have been proposed to address the problem of traffic jams in the towns of Bhiwani & Charkhi Dadri.(d) & (e) The details of bypasses along with their present status and timeline are at Annexure.
