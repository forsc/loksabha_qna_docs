                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

308






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Allocation  for  Modernization of Police  Forces




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of funds allocated by the Government and utilised by various States including Assam and other North- Eastern States and Haryana for modernization of police forces during the last three years, State-wise;(b) whether the State Governments have spent the said funds within the fixed time limit;(c) if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(d) whether the Government proposes to increase the said funds keeping in view the sensitivity of the States of Assam and Haryana; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):  	   A statement is laid on the Table of the House. --2--STATEMENT IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *308 FOR ANSWER ON 10.12.2019 REGARDING “ALLOCATION FOR MODERNIZATION OF POLICE FORCES”(a) to (c) : ‘Police’ and ‘Public Order’ are State subjects as per the VIIth  Schedule to the Constitution of India. However, to supplement the efforts of the States, assistance is provided to the State Governments for equipping and modernizing of their police forces under the scheme of ‘Assistance to States for Modernisation of Police’ [erstwhile scheme of Modernisation of Police Forces]. The amount of funds allocated and released by the Government and utilised by various States including Assam and other North Eastern States and Haryana for modernization of police forces under the scheme during the last three years, State-wise are at Annexure.  As may be seen from the Annexure, all the State Governments do not spend the funds released to them within the time limit fixed.(d) and (e) : The Government of India has been increasing allocation of the scheme every year since 2016-17, i.e., Rs. 595.00 crore  for 2016-17, Rs. 769.00  each for 2017-18 & 2018-19 and Rs. 811.30 crore for 2019-20. ******
