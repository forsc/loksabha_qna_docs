                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Upgradation of Physiotherapy Diploma Courses




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the rationale/objective behind upgrading physiotherapy from Diploma course toDegree course;(b) whether the Government has conducted any study to know the impact;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the specialisation courses like Ortho, Cardio, Sports and Neurology etc.in physiotherapy have been started during the last four years;(e) if so, the details of objectives achieved in this regard; and(f) the details of employment opportunities in Government sector for SpecialistPhysiotherapists along with the pay scales and group of services they are likely to beinducted and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) to (c): The University Grants Commission (UGC) has not issued guidelines forupgrading physiotherapy from Diploma course to Degree course. However, UGChas specified Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) and Master of Physiotherapy(MPT) degrees in its list of ‘specified nomenclature of degrees’ published in theGazette of India on 5th July, 2014. The Universities are free to offer thesecourses, if they so desire, as per the specification of degrees for BPT and MPT.(d) & (e): Ministry of Health & Family Welfare has informed that no specializedcourses like Ortho, Cardio, Sports and Neurology etc. in Physiotherapy have beenstarted during the last four years.(f): Ministry of Health & Family Welfare has informed that Physiotherapy positionsare advertised by Government/Autonomous institutions/hospitals, based on theirrequirement and as per their extant rules and regulations. 7th Central PayCommission has recommended replacement pay levels for physiotherapists.******
