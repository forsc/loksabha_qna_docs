                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3805






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Zika Virus




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of Zika Virus cases along with deaths reported during 2021 in Maharashtra, district-wise; and b)	the measures taken to prevent the spread of Zika virus in the above State?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (b): As informed by the Government of Maharashtra one case of Zika virus disease is reported in Belsar village in Pune district during 2021. The case has since recovered.State Government has taken series of activities for management and prevention of spread of Zika virus. These include streamlining the water supply in the village. Fever survey was taken in the villages within the radius of 5 Kms from Belsar village. Health teams had taken house to house visits in these identified villages. 215 samples including samples from pregnant women, collected during survey were found negative for Zika virus infection. Entomological survey and Integrated Vector Management was also taken up. To control the breeding of mosquitoes in affected area house to house survey, fogging, entomological source reduction, and use of anti-larvicidals, were taken up. Mosquito nets distribution for febrile patients as well as pregnant women in the village was also taken up.
