                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1667






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Construction of New ROBs




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has conducted feasibility study regarding new Road Over Bridge (ROB) projects in the country;(b)	if so, the details of ROB projects started during last three years in the country, State/UT-wise;\(c)	the measures taken by the Government for mobilizing funds to complete these projects lying pending/ incomplete for want of funds, State/UT-wise; and(d)	total expenditure incurred on execution of ROB projects on National Highways in the country during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)  For replacing all level crossings on National Highway corridors by Road Over Bridges/ Road Under Bridges (ROBs/RUBs), Ministry has to construct complete ROBs/ RUBs (including railway bridge portion) on National Highways in the country based on feasibility study either as stand-alone project or as a part of major Highway development project. The State/UT-wise details of ROBs/RUBs started during last three years in the country is placed at Annexure-I.  Fund is not a constraint for completing any approved ROBs/RUBs project. Expenditure on any ROB project is incurred as a part of all highway development project. Total expenditure incurred on execution of all projects on the National Highways in the country for last three years is as under:-Financial Year	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20, till 30.09.2019Expenditure (Rs. Crores)	73,741	1,00,178	1,27,993	62,179
