                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the blocks with more than 50% ST population and at least 20,000 tribal persons identified for construction of the Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS)(b) the details of the sub-districts identified for construction of the Eklavya Model Day Boarding School (EMDBS);(c) whether any EMRS are non-functional in Rajasthan, Delhi/NCR, Jharkhand and Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof and if not the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)  (a) and (b)   The details of the blocks with more than 50% ST population and at least 20,000 tribal population (as per 2011 census), identified for construction of the Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) and the list of sub-districts identified for construction of the Eklavya Model Day Boarding School (EMDBS) is at Annexure.(c):   No EMRS is sanctioned in Delhi/NCR.  As on date, total number of non-functional EMRSs in the state of Rajasthan, Jharkhand and Maharashtra are 9, 70 and 7 respectively.  The States have been asked to make a sanctioned schools functional by hiring a alternate suitable building till the construction of the school building is not complete.  For making a school functional from an alternate building, the per-requisite is that the state should have started construction of school in the said block and has requisite teachers for making the school functional. Some states find it difficult to arrange  suitable alternate building or teachers for making the school functional, as most of the schools are in remote locations of the country.  Regular follow-ups with concerned State/UT and other agencies is being made to expedite and finish the construction work and make schools functional from alternate suitable building.
