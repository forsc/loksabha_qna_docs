                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3828






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Bulk Drug Parks




Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi













                                                    Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the current status of process of setting up bulk drug parks in the country; (b) whether the Government has received any proposals for setting up bulk drug parks; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):   Department of Pharmaceuticals is implementing a scheme for ‘Promotion of Bulk Drug Parks’ under which grants-in-aid is to be provided to three States in the country for creation of common infrastructure facilities in Bulk Drug Parks. The details of the Scheme along with its guidelines can be accessed at department’s website at https://pharmaceuticals.gov.in/sites/default/files/Guidelines%20of%20the%20Scheme%20Promotion%20of%20Bulk%20Drug%20Parks_1.pdfProposals from 13 States, viz., Uttar Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh and Odisha have been received under the scheme.  *****
