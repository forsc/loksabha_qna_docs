                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2043






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Proposals from Andhra Pradesh




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of proposals received from Andhra Pradesh for road construction projects   during the last three years and the current year, year-wise; (b) the details of proposals approved by the Union Government; (c) the details of proposals which are under consideration and pending; and (d) the time by which the pending proposals are likely to be approved?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) The number of proposals received from Andhra Pradesh for road construction projects during the last three years and the current year, year-wise are as under; Year	No of proposals received 	Length (In km)	Amount (In Crores)2018-19	             7	99.52	358.352019-20	            18	419.25	1474.312020-21	            23	513.67	4365.922021-22	            12	330.20	2602.70 (b) to (d) The proposals received in 2018-19 to 2020-21  are all approved. 3 proposals out of 12 proposals received in 2021-22 have been approved and the rest are in various stages of approval.******
