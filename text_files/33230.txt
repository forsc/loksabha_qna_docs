                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

788






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Student-Faculty Ratio at IIT




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the number of faculty members employed by Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT) with a breakup of reserved  categories and the number of vacancies at each IIT;(b) the student to faculty ratio at each IIT;(c) whether the Government has taken any steps to address the number of 	vacancies and reach a more effective student to faculty ratio and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government is considering relaxation of reservation 	requirements in order to fill the vacancies and if so, the details thereof 	and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e) whether the Government has taken measures to increase the pool of candidates who can fulfil the qualification requirements from reserved categories by providing incentives of any sort and if so, the details thereof 	and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) & (b): The details are at ANNEXURE.(c) to (e): IITs are autonomous institutes governed by the Institutes of Technology Act, 1961 and the Statutes framed there under from time to time. Filling up of vacancies is a continuous process and IITs issue rolling Advertisement, which is open throughout the year for all candidates, who fulfill the required qualifications and experience for faculty positions in IITs. Ministry of Education has requested all IITs to fill up the vacancies in faculty cadre through Special Recruitment Drive in Mission Mode. The Ministry has asked IITs to follow the reservation as per Central Education Institutes (Reservation in Teacher’s Cadre) Act, 2019. In Ph.D programme, candidates from reserved categories are given relaxation in qualifying marks, application fees during admission process and granted fellowship so as to increase the pool of candidates available for the faculty posts in IITs. *****
