                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4427






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Lift Irrigation Projects in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number of lift irrigation projects started in Odisha with NABARD assistance;(b)	 whether most of these projects have not been completed;(c)	 if so, the reasons therefor; and(d)	  the steps  taken to expedite the completion of these  projects?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	As reported by National Bank for Agriculture & Rural Development (NABARD), 18,986 Lift Irrigation Projects have been sanctioned in Odisha since inception (Tranche I to Tranche XXVI) under Rural Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF).  Out of the total Lift Irrigation Projects sanctioned under RIDF, 15,334 have already been completed.(d):  Monitoring of projects sanctioned under RIDF is done to facilitate timely completion of projects, avoid cost/time overrun and add quality to the projects   by NABARD and State Government. ******
