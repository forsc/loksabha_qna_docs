                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3789






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Modernization of Police




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds sanctioned and released by the Government for modernisation of Police in the country during each of the last three years, State-wise particularly in Rajasthan;(b) whether the Government is aware that the police is facing shortage of arms in Rajasthan; and(c) if so, the details of demands/proposals of the State Government of Rajasthan regarding modernization of Police?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) Although ‘Police’ and ‘Public Order’ are State subjects as per the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India, to supplement the efforts of the States, assistance is provided to the State Governments for equipping and modernizing of their police forces under the centrally sponsored scheme of ‘Assistance to States for Modernisation of Police’ [erstwhile scheme of Modernisation of State Police Forces]. The details of central funds allocated and released to the State Governments under the scheme during the last three years as well as the current year are at Annexure.(b) & (c) The information regarding availability of weapons, equipment etc. is not maintained centrally. However, under the scheme of ‘Assistance to States for Modernisation of Police’, the Government of Rajasthan has proposed to procure weapons at a cost of Rs. 402.88 lakh in the State Action Plan (SAP) for 2020-21.*****
