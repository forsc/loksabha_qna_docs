                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3960






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Study on Traditional Water Harvesting Structures




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has studied and identified traditional water harvesting structures of the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government proposes to revive all such traditional water harvesting structures in the country including the State of Andhra Pradesh; and (d) if so, the steps taken for improving Centre-State cooperation in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (d)  Government of India launched Jal Shakti Abhiyan which is a time bound campaign with a mission mode approach intended to improve water availability including ground water conditions in the water stressed blocks of 256 districts in India. In this regard, teams of officers from Central Government along-with technical officers from Ministry of Jal Shakti were deputed to visit water stressed districts and to work in close collaboration with district level officials to undertake suitable interventions that included renovation of traditional and other water bodies/tanks amongst others.Central Government supports construction of water harvesting and conservation works primarily through Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS).As per Management Information System (MIS), the detailed works of renovation of traditional water bodies completed during last five years under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS including in Andhra Pradesh is given at Annexure.Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) – Har Khet ko Pani- Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies Scheme has multiple objectives like comprehensive improvement and restoration of water bodies. So far, since XII Plan, 2219 number of water bodies have been included in various States including Andhra Pradesh for Central assistance, out of which, works on 1254 bodies have been completed. Water being a State subject, initiatives on water management including conservation and artificial recharge to ground water, revival of traditional water harvesting structures etc. in the Country is primarily States’ responsibility.  However, the important measures taken by the Central Government for conservation, management of ground water and effective implementation of rain water harvesting in the country are at the following URL: http://mowr.gov.in/sites/default/files/Steps_to_control_water_depletion_Jun2019.pdf.*****ANNEXURE Annexure referred to in reply to parts (a) to (d) of Unstarred Question No. 3960 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 12.12.2019 regarding “Study on traditional water harvesting structures”.Details of works of renovation of traditional water bodies completed during last five years under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS:Renovation of traditional water bodies SI.
