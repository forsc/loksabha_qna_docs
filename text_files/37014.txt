                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Implementation of PMAY-G




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the main features and objectives of the Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin     (PMAY-G);(b) 	whether the targets of Housing for all by 2022 have been achieved;(c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor, State-wise;(d) 	the details of the targets fixed for construction of houses in the country under the said yojana during the last three years and the current year; and(e) 	the details of the steps being taken by the Government to complete the pending projects under the said yojana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a) to (e):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *373 TO BE ANSWERED ON 29.03.2022 REGARDING “IMPLEMENTATION OF PMAY-G” (a)        In order to achieve the target of “Housing for All” in rural areas, the Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) with effect from 1st April 2016 to provide assistance to construct 2.95 crore pucca houses by March, 2024, which has the following main features:  i.	Identification of beneficiaries based on the housing deprivation parameters under Socio Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 & Awaas+ survey 2018 ii.	Financial assistance of Rs.1.20 lakh in plain areas and Rs.1.30 lakhs in  IAP districts & other areas iii.	Additional assistance of Rs.12,000/- for construction of toilets through convergence with Swacch Bharat Mission, etc.iv.	Additional support of unskilled wages of 90/95 person days in convergence with MGNREGS.v.	Transferring the funds to the beneficiaries through DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) mode.vi.	Evidence based monitoring of construction of house through uploading of geo-tagged photographs with time and date at each stage of house construction.vii.	Micro-monitoring of the Scheme through dashboard.  (b) & (c) As on 23rd March 2022, out of the overall target of 2.95 crore houses, the target of 2.60 crore houses have already been allocated to States/Union Territories (UTs) against which 2.28 crore houses have been sanctioned to beneficiaries and 1.76 crore houses have been completed. The State/UT wise details are given in Annexure-I. The timeline for completion of PMAY-G has been extended to March 2024. The construction of houses has got delayed due to COVID-19 induced restrictions, delay in release of Central & State Share from State Treasury to State Nodal Account of PMAY-G, delay in allotment of land to landless beneficiaries by the States/UTs and at times General/ Assembly/ Panchayat elections and unavailability of building materials. (d) 	The targets allocated, sanctioned and houses constructed (as on 23.03.2022) under PMAY-G during the last three years and the current year are given as under: Financial Year	     Targets allocated by        Houses sanctioned               Houses                     the Ministry	          by States	                 completed2018-19	               25,13,662	           23,77,516	                44,72,4032019-20	               58,62,199	           46,97,491	                21,28,8542020-21	               43,69,690	           49,88,280	                33,99,5112021-22	               59,13,357	           37,89,915	                37,87,201Total	             1,86,58,908	         1,58,53,202	              1,37,87,969(e) 	The Ministry is taking the following initiatives to ensure timely completion of the houses under PMAY-G: i.
