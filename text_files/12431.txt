                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






NCERT Department




Abu Hasem Khan Choudhury














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is a rule of rotation of headship at NCERT;(b) if so, the details of the rule and the date from which this rule came into effect;(c) whether there have been cases wherein particular set of individuals weremade heads even after completion of three years as heads by transferring them todifferent departments, divisions, cells, groups;(d) the year-wise list of heads or in-charge heads of departments, divisions, cells,groups from January, 2010 to January, 2020;(e) whether any individual has been serving as head for more than three years ina department, division, cell, group at NCERT headquarter, including the ones whohave been transferred to different departments, division, cell, group after completionof their headship in one department from January, 2010 to January, 2020; and(f) if so, the names of all those individuals along with the duration for which theyserved as head from January, 2010 to January, 2020?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) Yes, Sir.(b) The Establishment Committee (EC) of National Council of EducationalResearch and Training (NCERT) in its 42nd meeting, held on 22.02.2012, decidedthat the tenure of Head of the Departments in NCERT will be 3 years. TheCommittee also decided that in cases where no Professor is available in aDepartment, the senior-most faculty member will be eligible for Headship. Thisdecision was implemented in NCERT vide their order dated 26.04.2012.Subsequently, the Establishment Committee in its 43rd meeting, held on 06.07.2012,decided that in order to become eligible for Headship of a Department, a professorusually should have worked for three years in the same Department.(c) Yes, Sir.(d) The requisite information, as provided by NCERT, is placed at Annexure-I.(e) Yes, Sir.(f) The requisite information, as provided by NCERT, is placed at Annexure-II.****
