                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EARTH SCIENCES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3118






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Sea Erosion




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the metros and towns in the country which are under threat of sea erosion and rise of sea level; (b) 	whether the Government has issued any warning on new construction in areas under threat of sea erosion and rise of sea level; and(c) 	the details of the preparations/action plan proposed by the Government to save the lives and property in such areas?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND MINISTRY OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a)	National Centre for Coastal Research (NCCR) an attached office of Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES) monitors Shoreline Changes along Indian Coast.List of erosion hot-spot locations along Indian coast is provided at Annexure-I. (b)	No Sir. MoES, Government of India has not issued any warning on    new construction in areas under threat of sea erosion and rise of sea level.(c)	The Ministry of Environment Forest & Climate Change has issued the Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) 2019, Notification with a view to conserve and protect the unique environment of the coastal stretches and marine areas, besides livelihood security to the fisher communities and other local communities in the coastal areas. The new constructions in coastal areas are being governed by this notification. Further, the National Disaster Management Plan (NDMP) implemented and monitored by the Ministry of Home Affairs provides general framework for facilitating preparation of Disaster Management Plan by States, Union Territories and Central Ministries for all phases of disaster management.
