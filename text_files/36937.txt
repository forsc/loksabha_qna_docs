                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4025






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






National Mission on Libraries




Sukanta Majumdar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a proposal to fund various digital library portals in the Country under “National Mission on Libraries” and Raja Rammohan Roy Library Foundation;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has received any proposal from State Governments for establishment of public library in their respective State;(d)	if so, the details thereof and the funds allocated and expenditure incurred on establishment / restoration / modernization of libraries during the last three years, State-wise including West Bengal; and(e)	whether the Government has signed any agreement with ''International Federation of Library Associations (IFLA)'' or any other international organization for development of public libraries in the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b)	No Sir. However, under National Virtual Library of India (NVLI) component of National Mission on Libraries (NML), Indian Culture Portal (ICP) has been developed and launched which is available on public domain at https://indianculture.gov.in/.(c) & (d)	Library is a State subject as per the Seventh schedule of Constitution of India and establishment of Public Library comes under the purview of respective State/U.T Authorities. However, Ministry of Culture through its National Mission on Libraries (NML), under Setting up of NML Model Library component, provides financial assistance to 1 State Central Library and 1 District Library in each State /UT, as per the recommendation of the State Authority concerned. Also, Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation (RRRLF), an Autonomous Body under Ministry of Culture, through its matching and non-matching schemes provides financial assistance to public Libraries for various purpose e.g. construction/renovation of library buildings, up-gradation of infrastructure with furniture and equipment, modernization, procurement of books and reading resources, organization of seminar/training and capacity building of library professionals / workshop / outreach programmes, establishment of children corner, opening of various sections, creating facilities for specially-abled, brail corner etc.The State-wise details of financial assistance extended by NML from its inception are annexed at Annexure-A.The State wise details of assistance sanctioned and released during last three years under the Matching Scheme of Financial Assistance towards Increasing Accommodation and Modernization of Library services are annexed at Annexure-B.(e)	  No Sir.
