                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2787






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Houses to People from Weaker Sections




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of persons belonging to weaker sections who have been allotted houses under various schemes in Maharashtra during the last three years;(b)	the estimated number of persons in that State who are homeless; and(c) 	the outcome of the ‘Housing for All’ scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs has sanctioned 9,03,689 houses for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) during the last three years under various components of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] in Maharashtra.(b) As per Census 2011, homeless population in urban area of Maharashtra is 1,11,373.(c) Under PMAY(U), 93.18 lakh houses have been sanctioned as on 25.11.2019 against validated demand of 112 lakh houses in various States/Union Territories. Out of the sanctioned houses, 55.86 lakh houses have been grounded for construction of which 28.36 lakh houses have been completed.                                             *****
