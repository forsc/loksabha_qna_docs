                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

510






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






New Missile Defence System




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has any proposal to purchase new missile defence system from Russia, in near future;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the time-line for any such proposal in near future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (d):  Based on threat assessment and security imperatives, Government constantly reviews the national security scenarios and takes steps to induct appropriate defence equipment to meet the security challenges and operational requirements.  This is a continuous process undertaken through procurement from various indigenous as well as foreign sources.  Highest priority is accorded to optimally equip the Armed Forces and keep them in a state of readiness.*****
