                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3035






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Promotion of Village Industries




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of zones created to promote village industries; (b) 	the names of the States included in these zones; (c) 	whether it is proposed to include some other States as well in these zones; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)&(b):	The Government of India, through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) functions with 6 Zonal offices located all over the country for promotion of Village Industries. The names of the States/UTs included in the Zones are as follows:<pre>Sl. No.	Name of the Zone	Head-quarters	States/UTs of the Zone1.	North Zone	New Delhi	1.	1.Delhi 2.Haryana 3.Himachal Pradesh 4.Punjab 5.Rajasthan 6.Jammu & Kashmir-UT 7.Ladakh-UT 8.Chandigarh-UT2	Central Zone	Bhopal	1.Chhattisgarh 2.Madhya Pradesh, 3.Uttarakhand 4.Uttar Pradesh3	North East Zone	Guwahati	1.Assam 2.Arunachal Pradesh 3.Manipur 4.Meghalaya 5.Mizoram 6.Nagaland 7. Sikkim  8.Tripura4	East Zone	Kolkata	1.Bihar   2.Jharkhand 3.Odisha 4.West Bengal  5.A&N Islands-UT5	West Zone	Mumbai	1.Goa 2.Gujarat (including Daman & Diu-UT) 3.Maharashtra (including Dadra & Nagar Haveli-UT)6	South Zone	Bengaluru	1.Andhra Pradesh 2.Telangana 3.Karnataka 4.Kerala 5.Tamil Nadu 6.Puducherry-UT  7.Lakshadweep-UT</pre>(c):	At present, all the States are included in these zones.(d):	Does not arise.
