                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Khadi Village Outlets Operated by KVIC




Vijay Kumar Dubey













                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Khadi Village outlets in the State of Uttar Pradesh and Tripura which are being operated by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC); (b) 	the number of Khadi clusters functioning in the State of Uttar Pradesh and Tripura; and (c) 	whether the Government has taken any steps for the development of Khadi Industries in Uttar Pradesh and North Eastern Region especially in Tripura and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	One branch of Khadi Gramodyog Bhavan, New Delhi (Departmental outlet of Khadi and Village Industries Commission) is functioning in Varanasi. 1835 Sales outlets managed by Khadi Institutions are functioning in Uttar Pradesh. One sales outlet of Tripura State Khadi and Village Industries Board is functioning in Tripura. (b):	No Khadi cluster is functioning in the states of Uttar Pradesh and Tripura.(c):	KVIC has been implementing Khadi Vikas Yojana (KVY) for the development of Khadi Sector all over the country, including Uttar Pradesh and North Eastern Region, during the year 2019-20, under which financial assistance in the form of grant and subsidy is provided under i) Modified Market Development Assistance (MMDA), ii) Interest Subsidy Eligibility Certificate (ISEC) Scheme, iii) Workshed Scheme for Khadi Artisans, and iv) ‘Assistance for Marketing Infrastructure’ scheme, details of which are annexed.   In addition, 91 and 7 Khadi Institutions were assisted in Uttar Pradesh and North Eastern Region, respectively under Khadi Reform Development Programme (KRDP), one of the initiatives taken by KVIC for development of Khadi in the country.
