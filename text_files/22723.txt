                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3774






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Nal Jal Yojana




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The number of villages where Nal Jal Yojana under Jal Jeevan Mission has been implemented in Bihar alongwith the progress report thereof;(b)	the total funds allocated as well as spent on the Nal Jal Yojana in Bihar as on date; and(c)	whether the Mission is likely to be completed by 2021 or additional time is likely to be given for completion of this mission?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	To enable every rural household in the country to have assured potable water through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024, Government of India, in partnership with State Governments is implementing  Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal after subsuming erstwhile National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) since August, 2019. As on 15.03.2021, as reported by the State, out of 196.87 lakh households in 39,518 villages of the State of Bihar, 134.57 lakh (68.35%) are having tap water connection in their households. (b)	The total allocation of the fund under JJM and their expenditure on the Nal Jal Yojana in Bihar are as follows:									(Amount Rs. in Crore)Financial Year	Central fund	State share expenditure	Opening Balance	Allocation	Release	Expenditure	2019-20	313.16	787.31	417.35	473.33	150.342020-21	257.18	1,839.16	353.60	488.76	206.64Source: JJM-IMIS(c)	As reported by the State, all remaining rural households in Bihar are planned to be provided with tap water connection in 2021.****
