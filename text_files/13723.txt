                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Smart City Projects in Tamil Nadu




Dayanidhi Maran














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the status of projects planned for the Smart Cities in Tamil Nadu  since the inception of the programme in 2015 and details thereof, city-wise; (b) 	the reasons for delay in implementation of integrated water and sewage management, if any and the details thereof, city-wise; (c)   the status of implementation for piped gas projects; and (d) 	the projects to be implemented in 2020 in the cities chosen from Tamil Nadu and expected dates of completion, city-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                    (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	The  City-wise status of projects in 11 Smart Cities of Tamil Nadu  under Smart Cities Mission (SCM) is given in Annexure-I.(b)	There is no delay in implementation of Integrated Water and Sewerage Management projects under SCM.(c)	None of the Smart Cities in Tamil Nadu have taken up piped gas projects under SCM.(d)	 The projects being implemented by Smart Cities in Tamil Nadu  are expected to be completed within five years from the date of their selection which are as follows: •	Chennai & Coimbatore (Round-1) by March 2021.•	Madurai, Salem, Vellore and Thanjavur (Round-2) by February 2022.•	Tiruppur, Tirunelveli, Tiruchirappalli and Thoothukudi (Round-3) by June 2022; and •	Erode (Round-4) by January, 2023.                                                   *****
