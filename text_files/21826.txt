UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3017






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Popularisation of Ancient Monuments and Cities




Pushpendra Singh Chandel













                                                    Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has made any specific efforts to restore the popularity of the cities and monuments built by the popular kings of ancient and medieval period which have been almost forgotten; and(b)	if so, the State-wise details of the works carried out and funds allocated to restore forgotten heritages and the contribution of the kings in the country including Bundelkhand and Uttarakhand during the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE &TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)   The process of protection of monuments/sites is a continuous process which is done as per the provisions of Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958. All Efforts are taken for maintenance of   all centrally protected monuments in a good condition. Regular   conservation and preservation is taken up depending upon need and requirement of the monument /site considering availability of funds and resources. Provision of visitors amenities like drinking water and toilets and facilities   like pathways, ramps, signage’s in various languages, sound and   light shows, illumination, etc. is also taken up by  Archaeological Survey of India. The    expenditure incurred on all centrally protected monuments/ sites, State-wise, including   monuments/sites within the State of  Uttarakhand and Bundelkhand  region is given at Annexure.
