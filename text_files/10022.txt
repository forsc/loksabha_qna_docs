                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3522






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Gaushalas in the Country




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of Gaushalas in the country including Uttar Pradesh, State/UT-wise;(b)	whether the Government provides financial assistance for setting up of Gaushalas in the country and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has received any proposal to set up any new Gaushalas in Pilana village of Baghpat district, Uttar Pradesh; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) As per information received from State Governments and Basic Animal Husbandry Statistics, 2019, list consisting of total number of gaushalas is annexed. In Uttar Pradesh there are 532 registered gaushalas.(b) Many State Governments provide the Grants to the Gaushalas. However, the Animal Welfare Board of India provides grants-in-aid under the Scheme for Shelter House for looking after the animals. The objective of this scheme is to establish and maintain shelter houses for distressed animals in the country. Primary, Non-Governmental Organizations and Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCAs) are given grants for construction of boundary walls, shelter, water tank, drains, in-house Dispensary, medical equipment, contingencies etc for a maximum grant of Rs. 22.50 lakhs excluding 10% of contribution from Animal Welfare Organizations. (c)  The Central Government has no such information.(d) Does not arise.
