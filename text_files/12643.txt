                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1568






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Processing of Indian Gooseberry




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any proposal to set up a mega Indian Gooseberry (Amla) food processing industry in Pratapgarh or whether any such proposal is pending with or under consideration of the Government in view of large scale farming of Amla in the said Parliamentary Constituency;(b)	if so, whether any industry for processing Indian Gooseberry produce has been set up in Pratapgarh Parliamentary Constituency; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has not received any eligible proposal from Pratapgarh District in the State of Uttar Pradesh for setting up of mega Indian Gooseberry (Amla) food processing industry under the Scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY).(b) & (c): Does not arise.*****
