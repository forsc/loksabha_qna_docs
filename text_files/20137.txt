                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1369






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Land Record Management




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                                             LOK SABHA                    UNSTARRED QUESTION No. 1369                    TO BE ANSWERED ON 9.2.2021              Land Record Management1369. SHRI NALIN KUMAR KATEEL:Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:(a)  Whether the Government has introduced the block chain technology for land record management throughout the country and if so, the details thereof;(b) Whether the Government has maintained data of the implementation of the block chain technology for land record management;(c) If so, whether the Government has set any target for achievement under the programme; and(d) If so, the details there of along with the status of the programme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (d) The Department of Land Resources is implementing Digital India Land Records Modernization Programme as per the guidelines. The land records are maintained, using different technologies adopted by the States / UTs. As on date, there is no proposal in the Department to adopt block chain technology for management of land records. Further, there is no information from States/UTs about use of block chain technology for maintaining land records data.                                             *****
