                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






B.Ed. Colleges




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of teacher training schools/ colleges functioning in the country, State-wise particularly in Bihar;(b) whether the National Council for Teacher Education grants recognition to B.Ed. colleges in the States;(c) if so, the details in this regard; and(d) whether focus is laid on the quality of training imparted by private training institutes and that training is being given on regular basis?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) : State-wise list of teacher training colleges functioning in the country as Annexure-A. Total number of teacher training colleges functioning in Bihar is 432. (b) & (c) : Yes Sir. State-wise list of recognized B.Ed. Colleges as Annexure-B.(d) : As per National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) Regulations, NCTE Act, 1993 and Norms & Standards the Regional offices of the NCTE periodically adheres to ascertain the quality and periodicity of training imparted by the private teacher training Institutions. *****
