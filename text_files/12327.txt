                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Import of Steel




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether India has become a net importer of steel over the last few years;(b)	if so, the quantum of steel imported during each of the last three years and the current year, year-wise;(c)	whether India is likely to import 50 MT of steel in the next few years due to growth in demand for steel; and(d)	if so, the steps were taken/to be taken by the Government to meet the demand particularly against the objectives set in New Steel Policy?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	India was a net exporter in FY 2016-17 and FY 2017-18 but become net importer of total steel in FY 2018-19.  However, in the current year FY 2019-20 (upto Dec.’19), it has regained the status of net exporter.   (b)	The quantum of total steel imports in the last three years and the current year, year-wise is as under:-Year	Import (Quantity in million ton)2016-17	7.972017-18	8.402018-19	8.792019-20 (upto Dec., 19)(Prov.)	5.82       	Source: JPC(c)&(d): The Indian Steel Sector is a de-regulated sector and Importers take their own commercial decisions based on market dynamics and requirement.  Government is focusing on Make in India and domestic manufacturing of steel and the current crude steel capacity in the country is sufficient to meet the domestic consumption.********
