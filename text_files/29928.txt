                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1584






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Infrastructure for Sports at Panchyat Level




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to launch any scheme for development/improvement of basic infrastructure for promotion of sports at the village/panchayat level in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; State/UT-wise, particularity in the State of Uttar Pradesh; and(c) if not, the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) to (c) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the primary responsibility to develop/improve basic infrastructure for promotion of sports at the village/panchayat level in the country rests with the State/Union Territory Governments. The Union Government supplements their efforts. This Ministry has sanctioned 267 sports infrastructure projects of various categories under the Khelo India Scheme across the country, including the rural areas and out of these, thirty projects are located in the State of Uttar Pradesh.****
