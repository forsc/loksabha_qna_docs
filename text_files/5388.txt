                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4879






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Fertilizer Allocation to Gujarat




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of fertilizers allocated to Gujarat during the last two years and the current year, fertilizer and year-wise; and (b) 	the details of the subsidies given by the Government on the fertilizers issued?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI D.V.
