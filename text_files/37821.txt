                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4953






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Medical Facilities under National AYUSH Mission




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Aparupa  Poddar
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether any new initiatives have been taken by the Government to provide more health cards and basic medical examination facility under the National AYUSH Mission (NAM) programme and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of various components of centrally sponsored scheme under NAM for categorization and infrastructural strengthening of AYUSH in the country; (c) whether the Government has made sufficient fund allocation for promotion of AYUSH in the country, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and West Bengal during the last three years and the current year and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has sponsored institutions and also established/ likely to establish new academic institutes under NAM; and (e) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) Under various components of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there are provisions for financial assistance to make available necessary equipments in the AYUSH Hospitals & Dispensaries which may include essential diagnostics for providing basic medical examination facilities to the public.  State / UT Governments may avail the financial assistance by projecting their requirement as per NAM guidelines. However, there is no provision for health cards under the existing guidelines of NAM. (b) & (c) Government of India is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) for development and promotion of AYUSH in the country. The Mission inter-alia makes the following provisions which include upgradation and strengthening of the infrastructure of AYUSH:(i) AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres under Ayushman Bharat(ii) Co-location of AYUSH facilities at Primary Health Centres (PHCs), Community Health Centres (CHCs) and District Hospitals(DHs) (iii) Upgradation of existing standalone Government AYUSH Hospitals(iv) Upgradation of existing/setting up of new standalone Government/ Panchayat /Government aided AYUSH Dispensaries(v) Setting up of up to 50 /30 /10 beddedintegrated AYUSH Hospitals (vi) Infrastructural development of State Government Under-Graduate and post-Graduate Educational Institutions.(vii) Establishment of new AYUSH Colleges in the States where availability of AYUSH teaching institutions is inadequate in the Government Sector.Funds allocated/released to the States/UTs by the Central Government for the promotion of AYUSH in the country during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and West Bengal are furnished at Annexure-I.(d) & (e) Public Health being a State subject, establishment of new academic institutescomes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, under NAM, there is a provision of establishment of new AYUSH Colleges in the States where the availability of AYUSH teaching institutions is inadequate in the Government Sector.TheMinistry of Ayush has approved for setting up of new AYUSH educational institutions under NAM as per the proposals received from State Governments through State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). The details State-wise are furnished at Annexure-II.
