                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1699






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Greenfield Airport




P. Velusamy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details regarding greenfield airport projects approved for execution since notification of the greenfield airport policy in the country;(b) number of greenfield airport projects completed and progress on other such projects approved in the country; and(c) the progress on development of greenfield airport in Tamil Nadu and action being taken to speed up the project?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): After notification of the Greenfield Airport Policy in the country, Government of India, Ministry of Civil Aviation has granted ''In principle'' approval for setting up of Greenfield Airports namely Sindhudurg and Shirdi in Maharashtra, Bijapur, Gulbarga, Hassan and Shimoga in Karnataka, Durgapur in West Bengal, Datia (Gwalior) in Madhya Pradesh, Pakyong in Sikkim, Kushinagar and Noida in Uttar Pradesh, Dholera and Hirasar in Gujarat,  Karaikal in Puducherry, Dagadarthi, Bhogapuram and Oravakal (Kurnool) in Andhra Pradesh and Hollongi, Itanagar in Arunachal Pradesh.;(b): Durgapur, Shirdi, Kannur, Pakyong and Gulbarga airport projects have already been operationalised and trial landing has been done at Sindhudurg Airport. The progress on other airports is at Annexure A.;(c): The State Government of Tamil Nadu has informed that they intend to establish a new Greenfield Airport near Chennai. However, no concrete progress has been made in the matter.
