                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Telecom Services in Tribal and LWE Affected Areas




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of schemes formulated by the Government for expansion of telecom services in tribal and Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas of the country; (b) 	the targets fixed and achieved by the Government with regard to the expansion of telecom services across the country, State/UT-wise; and (c) 	the funds allocated/released/utilized by the Government in this regard during the last three years and the current year so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) to (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *242 FOR 11TH MARCH, 2020  REGARDING “TELECOM SERVICES IN TRIBAL AND LWE AFFECTED AREAS”.(a) & (b) 	The schemes, formulated by the Government for expansion of telecom services in Tribal and Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas of the country along with target fixed and achieved by the Government are as below:(1)	PROVISION OF MOBILE SERVICES IN LEFT WING EXTREMISM (LWE) AFFECTED AREAS:(i)	LWE PHASE-I:Mobile connectivity at 2343 tower locations, identified by Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) in the Left Wing Extremism affected areas (LWE Phase-I), has been implemented through Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited, and the project has been completed. The State-wise details are given in Annexure-I.(ii)	LWE PHASE-II:Provision of mobile connectivity by using 2G+4G technology at 2217 tower locations, identified by Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) in the Left Wing Extremism affected areas (LWE Phase-II), has been approved. The implementation period of the project is 18 months from the date of signing of contract with the implementing agency. The tender is under process. The State-wise details are given in Annexure-II.(2)	354 UNCOVERED VILLAGES SCHEME:A Scheme has been approved for providing mobile connectivity in 354 uncovered villages of J&K, Ladakh and other priority areas. The implementation period of the project is 9 months from the date of signing of the contract with the implementing agency. The Letter of Intent (LoI) has been issued to the successful bidder. The State-wise details are given in Annexure-III.(3)	MOBILE CONNECTIVITY TO COVER UNCOVERED VILLAGES AND ALONG NATIONAL HIGHWAY (NH 223) IN ANDAMAN & NICOBAR ISLANDS:A scheme has been provisioned to provide mobile connectivity to 85 uncovered villages and along NH-223 in Andaman & Nicobar Island. The implementation period of the project is 12 months from the date of signing of contract with the implementing agency. The tender is under process.(4)	COMPREHENSIVE TELECOM DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR LAKSHADWEEP ISLANDS:The project for Augmentation of 2G Mobile Coverage with EDGE Technology in Lakshadweep Islands is completed in May 2018. (5)	COMPREHENSIVE TELECOM DEVELOPMENT PLAN (CTDP) FOR THE NORTH-EASTERN REGION (NER):(i)	As part of the CTDP for the NER, Mobile Services are being provided in the uncovered villages in NER and along National Highway for seamless coverage in NER [in Assam except 2 Districts, Sikkim, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura and Arunachal Pradesh (NH only)] by setting up 2004 towers. As of February 2020, 1269 towers have been installed, out of which 1153 sites are providing services. The State-wise details are given in Annexure-IV.(ii)	A Scheme has been approved for providing mobile connectivity in 1164 uncovered villages and along National Highways of Meghalaya. The Tender is under process.(6)	BHARATNET:BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country. As part of this project, the last mile connectivity, through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband technology, is to be provided at all the GPs in the country including telecom services in tribal and LWE affected areas.As on 28.02.2020, a total of 1,36,693 Gram Panchayats have been made Service Ready. The State-wise details are given in Annexure-V.
