                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1895






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Construction of Shelter for Urban Homeless




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds released to construct shelters for urban homeless in Haryana during the last three years along with the details of schemes under which the said funds have been released;(b)	whether the Government has set any target under the said scheme;(c)	if so, the details thereof during the last three years, year-wise and the success achieved thereunder; and(d)	whether the shelters to be given to urban homeless are being upgraded and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS               (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is administering a scheme “Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH)”, as a component of Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), through respective States/Union Territories. In this Mission, funds are allocated to States/ UTs in a consolidated manner. Out of this, appropriate amount is apportioned by States/UTs for different components of Mission including SUH, as per their requirements. During 2018-19, no funds were released to Haryana. During 2019-20 and 2020-21, ? 29.63 Crore and ? 10.54 Crore respectively were released to the State for DAY-NULM.  (b)&(c)	Cumulatively during the last three financial years, Haryana had planned to establish 44 shelters; out of these 26 shelters have been already made functional. (d):	States / UTs have been directed to provide basic amenities in shelters which include ventilated rooms, lighting, kitchen, separate toilets for male and female, drinking water and storage space etc.                      *******
