                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

761






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Industrial Incentives




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to extend the scheme of package of industrial incentives for promoting industrialisation in the State of Karnataka;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) 	the funds allocated/utilised during the last three years; and(d) 	the other steps taken by the Government to enhance industrialization in Karnataka?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): 	Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade administers ‘Industrial Infrastructure Up-gradation Scheme (IIUS)/ Modified IIUS (MIIUS)’ to upgrade common industrial infrastructure in industrial clusters/areas/estates across the country. Under this scheme three projects have been sanctioned in the state of Karnataka. The details of the projects are at Annexure I. Rs. 13.272 crore has been released under MIIUS during the last three years*********
