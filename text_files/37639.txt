                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4921






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Ordnance Factories




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of ordnance factories that have been set up in the country so far along with such factories proposed to be set up by the Government under the Aatmanirbhar Bharat scheme;(b) the total expenditure incurred by the Government in setting up these ordnance factories; and(c) the time by which the armaments manufactured in such factories are likely to be made available to the defence forces in the country and also for export purpose?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) & (b):  Total 41 numbers of Ordnance Factories have been set up by the Government in the country so far.  However, presently, there is no such proposal of setting up of new Ordnance Factories under Aatmanirbhar Bharat scheme by the Government.  Further, seven new Defence Public Sector Undertakings have been set up with effect from 1st October, 2021 by converting these 41 Ordnance Factories, for manufacturing of modern weapons and armament indigenously by transferring assets worth over Rs. 90,000 crores developed over the years to the new companies. (c):  The armaments manufactured by these Ordnance Factories is already in service with the Indian Armed Forces and is being exported to various countries as well.*****
