                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

551






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Integrated Rural Energy Programme




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the manner in which the Integrated Rural Energy Programme is being implemented across the country along with the salient features of the said programme; (b) whether any survey has been conducted regarding the progress of implementation of the said programme in the said states; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the said programme is achieved the expected outcomes in the states; (e) if so, the details thereof; and (f) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (f) Ministry of New and Renewable Energy is no longer implementing Integrated Rural Energy Programme in the country. The programme was implemented as a Centrally Sponsored scheme up to 31-03-2005.*****
