                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Ayushman Bharat Card




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of Ayushman Bharat cards issued in the country and the number of people benefited by it, State/UT-wise including Jharkhand;(b) the number of people who have been benefitted by ABY in Jharkhand; and(c) the number of people who have not been issued the said cards till date and the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): State/UT-wise details of Ayushman cards issued in the country are at Annexure-I.Ayushman Bharat -Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is an entitlement based scheme. No enrolment or registration is required under the scheme. Beneficiary can directly visit any empanelled public or private hospital to avail cashless health benefits.As of 5th December, 2021, more than 10 lakh hospital admissions worth over Rs. 1057.50 crore are authorized under AB-PMJAY for the eligible beneficiaries from Jharkhand.State/UT-wise details of families without any member having Ayushman card are at Annexure-II.
