                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2812






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Computerization of Post Offices




Ravindra Kushawaha














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to make post-offices competitive vis-a-vis their private counterparts; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the total number of post-offices in rural areas of the country, State/UT-wise along with the number of post-offices which have been computerised and those which are under the process of computerisation as on date, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Yes Sir. (b)	Under the Information Technology Modernization Project, the Department of Posts has harnessed technology to provide delivery of services in a competitive environment. These include :  i)	All 25487 Departmental Post Offices are computerized. Moreover, 129076 Branch Post Offices are provided with SIM based Handheld Devices to provide networked services to the rural population on par with urban customers under Digital Advancement of Rural Post Office for A New India (DARPAN) Project. ii)	Network connectivity has been provided to Departmental Post Offices through a single Wide Area Network (WAN). As on date 27362 locations including administrative and mail offices are operating in the WAN system. iii)	Financial Services Integrator (FSI) has been implemented for solutions for Core Banking and Core Insurance in Post Offices. This provides multiple service delivery channels through internet banking, ATM, Interactive Voice Response System (IVRS) and SMS among others. 997 ATMs have been installed which are inter-operable.iv)	Core System Integrator (CSI) project has been implemented for providing solutions for Mail Operations, Retail Products, Logistics and Human Resources. v)	Mobile App based delivery has been introduced to provide real time delivery information. Postmen have been provided with smartphones.  vi)	Department has introduced electronic clearance of letter boxes through a mobile app based ‘Nanyatha’ App to provide for a mechanism to check the clearance of letter boxes. vii)	Online Track and Trace system for Speed Post is provided for real time tracking. viii)	Short Messaging Service (SMS) is utilized for giving the delivery status of an article to the customer.ix)	Dynamic Queue Management System (DQMS) has been implemented in the Post Offices having six or more than six counters. (c)	All the Departmental Post Offices in the Country are computerized. Details of State/UT-wise number of computerized Departmental Post Offices is annexed as Annexure-I.
