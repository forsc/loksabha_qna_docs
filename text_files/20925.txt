                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2183






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Set up Skill Development Institute




Sanjay Bhatia













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                








                                                    Nayab Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up Skill Development Institutes on the lines of Industrial Training Institutes (ITI);(b)	if so, the number of districts of Uttar Pradesh, National Capital Territory of Delhi in which Skill Development Institutes are proposed to be set up and whether the said institutes are also proposed to be set up in Karnal, Panipat and Kurukshetra parliamentary constituencies;(c)	the names of the places where institutes are likely to come up including the number of these institutes;(d)	the time by which these institutes are likely to be set up; and (e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e) 	Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Directorate General of Training is implementing a scheme titled “Skill Development in 47 Districts Affected by Left Wing Extremism (LWE)”, under which Government of India is funding the establishment of 47 new Industrial Training Institutes in 47 districts of 10 States @ 1 ITI per district and 68 Skill Development Centres in 34 districts of 9 States @ 2 SDCs per district in the LWE affected districts in the country. Under the scheme, Industrial Training Institute at Village-Vishundhari, Post-Ghorawal, Sonebhadra District of Uttar Pradesh has been established.  The details of districts covered under the scheme is given at Annexure. There is no proposal to set up such institutes in Karnal, Panipat and Kurukshetra parliamentary constituencies.Further, under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, aspirational model training centres for short term training called Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) are being set up in every district of India, while ensuring coverage of all the Parliamentary Constituencies (PCs). As on 19th January 2021, 738 PMKKs have been already established. Of these 10 are in Delhi, 87 in Uttar Pradesh and 34 in Haryana, including in Karnal, Panipat and Kurukshetra.AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARA (a) to (e) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION No.2183 DUE FOR REPLY ON 08.03.2021Skill Development in 47 Districts Affected by LWE: - The details of districts covered under the scheme is below: - Sl.
