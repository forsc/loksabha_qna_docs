                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






National Highway Projects




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government is aware that National Highway (NH)-55 has been shut since 2009 due to landslides at Tindharay (Darjeeling, West Bengal) and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; (b)	 the time by which the said NH is likely to be opened and the steps being taken by the Government in this regard; (c) 	whether there is any proposal for widening of NH-31 along the Chicken’s Neck corridor and if so, the details thereof; (d)	 whether the Government proposes to construct flyovers, walkways and underpasses to ease multiple traffic chokepoints in Siliguri and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the present status of the Trans-Asian Highway projects connecting India, Myanmar, Thailand and Singapore?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	NH-55 has been opened to traffic under controlled conditions at Tindharia since September 2016. Restoration of damaged road bench at Tindharia is in progress & targeted to be completed by March 2020.(c)        The “Chicken Neck Corridor” portion of NH-31 from Islampur (km 498) to Ghoshpukur (km 551) is already developed to 4-lane except the Islampur bypass from km 498 to km 507, which is under progress. The stretch from Ghoshpukur (km 551) to Bihar More (Km 563) section is being 4 laned.(d)	Ministry has already constructed flyovers and underpasses under AH-2 project in the stretch from Bihar More to Shivmandir of NH 31. DPR is taken up for the stretch from Shivmandir to Sevok Army Cantonment.e)	Improvement of Imphal- Moreh section of Trans Asian Highway connecting India, Myanmar, Thailand and Singapore is taken up. *****
