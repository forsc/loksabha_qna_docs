                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

608






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Gram Panchayats under Atal Bhujal Yojana




 Chinta Anuradha













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government has launched Atal Bhujal Yojana only in some gram panchayats belonging to a few States; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the reasons for not launching this scheme pan-India, despite the depleting ground water being an issue of deep concern; (d) whether the Government has any plan to implement this scheme in the entire country; (e) if so, the details thereof; and (f) if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	 & (b)  Atal Bhujal Yojana (Atal Jal) is being implemented in identified water stressed areas of seven States, viz. Gujarat, Haryana, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh. State wise details of number of districts, blocks/taluks and gram panchayats are given in the table below:S. No.	State	Districts	Blocks / Taluks	Gram Panchayats1	Gujarat	7	34	2,2012	Haryana	13	36	1,8953	Karnataka	14	41	1,1994	Madhya Pradesh	6	9	6725	Maharashtra	13	38	1,3396	Rajasthan	17	38	1,1447	Uttar Pradesh	10	26	550	TOTAL	80	222	9000(c)	  Atal Jal has been launched in certain States as a pilot scheme based on the consultation, criticality of groundwater situation, willingness and degree of preparedness.(d) & (e) No Sir. However, proposals of inclusion of more water stressed areas within the scheme may be considered in future depending upon the outcomes of the scheme and its implementation status.(f)	 In view of (d) & (e) above, question does not arise.*****
