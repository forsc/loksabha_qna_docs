                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1758






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Import of Edible Oils




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of quantity of edible oils imported and exported during the last three years; (b)	whether the Government is formulating any scheme to promote the production of edible oils to make India self-reliant; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a):	The details of quantity and value of edible oils imported and exported during the last three years and current year are given below:Years	Import	Export	Quantity (Thousand Tonnes)	Value (US$ Million)	Quantity (Thousand Tonnes)	Value (US$ Million)2017-18	15361.03	11637.48	37.11	87.832018-19	15019.31	9890.32	50.02	106.792019-20	14722.12	9672.88	85.29	170.092019-20 (Apr-Jul)	5017.51	3068.00	41.23	80.212020-21 (Apr-Jul)*	4433.65	3027.58	20.36	45.08Source: DGCI&S, Kolkata (*: Provisional)(b) & (c):	The Government is implementing the National Food Security Mission (NFSM) – Oil seeds & Oil Palm to increase the production and productivity of oil seed crops and area expansion of oil palm in the country with the objective to increase the edible oil production in the country and for the benefit of the farming community.Under the Mission, various interventions are undertaken, such as improved seed replacement rate, increased seed production, large-scale demonstrations, supply of critical inputs, arrangement for life saving irrigation, mechanization inputs, postharvest management, promotion of oil seeds in rice fallow, non-traditional states, crop diversification and inter cropping. The mission is implemented through the state governments.The area under oilseeds has increased from 26.09 million hectares in 2015-16 to 27.04 million hectares in 2019-20.  The production of oilseeds has increased from 25.25 million tonnes in 2015-16 to 33.42 million tonnes in 2019-20. 	Edible oil production was 8.63 million tonnes in 2015-16 and this has gone upto 10.53 million tonnes in 2019-20. The area under oil palm has increased from 3 lakh hectare in 2015-16 to 3.57 lakh hectare in 2019-20.***
