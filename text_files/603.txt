                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

578






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Support for Hatcheries




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is providing any innovative technology to support farmers for Fish Hatcheries, if so, the amount allocated and the details of the schemes;(b)	the quantum of Fish produced in the country during the last five years, year and State-wise; and(c)	whether the Government has detected any foul play by transporters using formaldehyde to preserve fish in any part of the country, if so, the name of the Companies or Associations involved therein and the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme ‘Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries’ under which financial assistance is being provided to various States/UTs  for establishment of Fish seed hatcheries for Indian Major Carps and other specific cultivable fin fish and establishment of Freshwater/ Brackishwater Prawn Hatcheries with the unit costs of Rs.25.00 lakh and Rs. 50.00 lakh respectively.  Further, Central Institute of Freshwater Aquaculture (CIFA-ICAR) has also developed a technology of Portable Fiberglass Reinforced   Plastic (FRP) Carp Hatcheries and it has been popularized among fish farmers for decentralized fish production.  Under the Blue Revolution Scheme, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has sanctioned 523 Nos fish/prawn hatcheries by releasing its central share of Rs. 29.34 Crore to various States/UTs of the country.(b): The State-wise details of Fish production of the country during the last five years is at Annexure-I.(c): Based on the media reports about formaldehyde being used for preserving the fish, the Department of Fisheries, has issued advisories to all the State Governments/UTs suggesting following measures to be taken urgently:i.	To ensure quality certification jointly by State Department of Fisheries and Food Safety Agencies, etc.ii.	To set up a task force including concerned State Department/agencies for joint inspection and testing formalin content in fish loads using CIFT test kits/ other suitable test kits in the respective States.iii.	To establish task force for inter State coordination mechanism, with the concerned States exporting and importing fish for cross testing at exit and entry points for building confidence among States.iv.	To consult and coordinate with Food Safety and Standard Authority of India (FSSAI) for evolving suitable mechanism based on the State’s specific requirements.Further, no report has been received in this department, regarding the foul play by transporters using formaldehyde to preserve fish in any part of the country.
