                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1363






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






PMGKAY in Maharastra




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of eligible citizens, including interState migrants who utilise PDS in 2019-2020 in the State of Maharashtra; and (b) 	the total number of beneficiaries of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojna (PMGKAY) in 2019- 2020 in the State of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):  		National Food Security Act (NFSA), 2013 provides a maximum coverage for 700.17 Lakh persons in the State of Maharashtra to receive the benefit of monthly subsidised foodgrains through Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS). During the year 2019-20, the State Government had done an off-take of 87.49% foodgrains from FCI/Central Stock for distribution to NFSA beneficiaries including inter-State migrants.(b): 		The Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PM-GKAY) was not existing during the year 2019-20. ***
