                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SPACE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Space Technology




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





SPACE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to leverage India’s outstanding space technology capabilities for expanding its diplomatic initiatives of assisting other countries in the world; commercially non-commercially; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the efforts made during the last three years, country-wise, year-wise and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PG & PENSIONS AND IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH):(a)	Yes, Sir. (b)	During the last three-year period India has shared space expertise through signing of cooperative documents, training on nano satellite building and training on space technology applications.
