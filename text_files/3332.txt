                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2997






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Proposals from State of Maharashtra




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received any proposals from the 	Government of Maharashtra relating to Housing and Urban Affairs sector;(b)	if so, the details thereof indicating the total number of proposals received 	and approved including the total number of proposals lying pending; and(c)	the time by which the Union Government is likely to give approval to the 	pending proposals?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c)	The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs facilitates and assists States/Union Territories(UTs) including Maharashtra, through its Missions/Schemesin development of cities and towns covered under these Missions/Schemes. Details of   the proposals received and approved under the Missions/Schemes is given in the Annexure.                                *****
