                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

241






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Sick Heavy Industries




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government have identified the heavy industries which have fallen sick;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for their sickness; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to remove the bottleneck so that these units can recover from their sickness and if so the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) to (c):	Government has identified and approved the closure of eight loss making / sick Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs), under administrative control of Ministry of Heavy Industries viz. HMT (Watches) Ltd., HMT (Chinar Watches) Ltd., HMT (Bearings) Ltd., Bharat Pumps & Compressors Ltd., Scooter India Ltd., Instrumentation Ltd. (Kota Unit), Hindustan Cables Ltd., and Tungabhadra Steel Products Limited after compliance of prevalent guidelines.*******
