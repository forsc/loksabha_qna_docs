                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1887






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Scheme for Town Development




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated any scheme for town 	development; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of proposals received from the Government of 	Madhya Pradesh for town development, district-wise; (d) 	whether the civic bodies of Seoni Balaghat district of Balaghat 	Parliamentary Constituency are covered in the town 	development 	schemes and if so, the details thereof; (e) 	the proposals wherein civic bodies in Balaghat have been 	covered and the details of development work likely to be 	undertaken there?






ANSWER




                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS             (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) & (b):   Yes, Sir. Realizing the significance of planned development in brownfield and green field area, a sub –scheme was launched by Government of India on Formulation of Local Area Plan (LAP) & Town Planning Scheme (TPS) under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT). 25 cities across various states have been identified for implementation under the sub-scheme. The sub scheme provides financial assistance of ? 2.0 Crore for each city for formulation of Local Area Plan and Town Planning Scheme. The details of 25 cities are given in Annexure.(c) :    Indore is the only city in Madhya Pradesh which has been covered in the sub scheme. State Government of Madhya Pradesh had prepared following 02 Town Development Schemes for Indore city:(i) Town Development Scheme.(ii) Local Area Plan (LAP).(d) & (e) : These civic bodies have not been covered in the said scheme.
