                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Allocation of Funds and Food grains to Karnataka




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any efforts to expedite the allocation of adequate foodgrains, pulses, sugar and edible oils to Karnataka; (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the total quantum supplied to Karnataka during the last three years; (c) 	whether the Government has provided adequate funds for the development of godowns and cold storages in Karnataka; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the total amount of funds disbursed by the Union Government for the purpose during the last four years, till date?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a)& (b): Govt. of India makes allocation of foodgrains(wheat & rice) to the States/UTs under Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) in terms of National Food Security Act(NFSA), 2013.  The Act is being implemented in all the States/UTs including Karnataka.In addition to NFSA, Govt. of India also makes additional allocation of foodgrains for festivals & natural calamities based on the request of State/UT Govt..............2/--2-Govt. of India also allocates foodgrains under five welfare schemes namely Wheat Based Nutrition Programme(WBNP), Mid Day Meal Scheme(MDM),  Scheme for Adolescent Girls(SAG), Allocation of foodgrains under Welfare Institutions & Hostel Scheme and Annapurna Scheme. Foodgrains are allocated under MDM and WBNP Schemes at NFSA rates and under SAG, Annapurna and Welfare Institutions and Hostels Scheme at BPL rates. Responsibility for distribution of Foodgrains within the State/UT rests with the concerned State/UT Govt.The details of foodgrains allocated by Government of India to the State of Karnataka for distribution under the above mentioned schemes during the last three years and the current year is as under:                                                              ( Figures In Lakh Metric Tonnes)Year	Allocation of foodgrains	Rice	Wheat	Total2016-17	23.54	6.15	29.692017-18	28.65	1.15	29.802018-19	28.32	0.82	29.142019-20*	17.60	0.78	18.38*Upto October, 2019Further, a scheme is also implemented by Govt. of India for release of pulses to States/UTs at discounted rate to be utilized viz. MDM, ICDS, PDS from the stock of pulses procured under PSS with a Central Subsidy of Rs.15 per kg over the issue price as a “One time measure” on “First Come-First Serve” and “First in-first Out(FIFO) basis for a period of 12 months or till present PSS pulses stock of 34.88 lakh MT lasts, whichever is earlier. The duration of the scheme was elapsed on 30.09.2019. Based on the indents received from Govt. of Karnataka under the above scheme, allocation was made for the paid stock of 99,486 MT of Toor. Till, 25.11.2019, State Govt. has lifted 98,717 MT. The Central Government is reimbursing a fixed subsidy of Rs. 18.50 per kg @ 1 Kg per month per AAY family to participating States/UTs. Accordingly,  monthly quota of 1199.70 MT of sugar(@ 1 kg per family)for 11.997 lakh AAY families, has been fixed for PDS under Sugar Subsidy Scheme in respect of Karnataka.There is no scheme for allocation of edible oil by the Central Government to any State......3/--3-(c) & (d): 	No funds have been released under Central Sector Scheme for construction of godowns by the Department of Food & Public Distribution in Karnataka. Hence, no new development (construction) of godown has taken place in Karnataka for last four years. However, adequate funds have been provided on regular basis for repair/maintenance & upgradation of godowns provided/allocated in last four years to Karnataka. All States and UTs are covered under MIDH.
