                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

170






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Promotion of High Quality Cotton




Mahendrabhai Kalubhai Munjpara














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has envisaged a textile policy for high quality ‘Shankar’ Cotton produced at Surendranagar district in Gujarat owing to its reputation as a hub of cotton activities in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has any proposal/plan for setting up of a textile park in Surendranagar district; and (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c):-  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *170 TO BE ANSWERED ON 12.02.2021.(a):	A new Textile Policy is being formulated for overall promotion and development of the Textile Sector. Cotton is the backbone of Indian textile industry. The proposed Textile Policy envisages strong collaboration with Textile Industry, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare and various Agriculture Research Institutes to improve the acreage, productivity and quality of the cotton.(b) & (c):	There is no park under the Scheme of Integrated Textile Park (SITP) of Ministry of Textiles at Surendranagar district and there is no proposal for setting up textile park under SITP in Surendranagar district.  ***
