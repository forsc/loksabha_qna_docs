                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

818






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Infrastructure and Academic Facilities in North Eastern Universities




Agatha K. Sangma














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to improve infrastructure and academic facilities in North-Eastern Universities for giving them more academic autonomy ;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the funds sanctioned and allocated for the purpose. (c) whether the Government has received any proposals from Universities in North-Eastern region for the improvement of colleges and campus facilities during the last three years;(d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) the action taken/likely to be taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SHRI SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (e): Education being in the Concurrent List, creation of new institutions and enhancing the quality of education are the responsibilities of both the Central and State Governments. However, recognizing the need for central assistance, the Central Government has implemented various schemes for supporting State Governments such as the centrally sponsored scheme of Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan(RUSA).	 States are supported under various components of RUSA. As regards to Universities, in the North-Eastern Region, 2 Universities in Assam have been approved to support under the component of Infrastructure Grants to University (IGU) in the last three years (2018-19 to 2020-21) with an approved fund of Rs. 40 Crores. As regards to colleges, State-wise details of number of colleges approved under the component of Infrastructure Grants to Colleges (IGC) in North-Eastern States in the last three years (2018-19 to 2020-21) are as below:<pre>Name of State	Number of Units supported under IGC	Total fund approved (Rs. in Crores)Arunachal Pradesh	4	8.00Assam	117	234.00Manipur	10	20.00Meghalaya	12	24.00Mizoram	15	30.00Nagaland	5	10.00Sikkim	6	12.00Tripura	7	14.00Total	176	352.00</pre>****
