                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Ayurveda Research Institutes




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the total number of Government and private Ayurveda Research Institutes across the country, State/ UT-wise;(b) the total number of new Ayurveda Research Institutes established in the country during each of the last five years, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Govt. proposes to set up more new Ayurveda Research Institutes across the country in the near future including Ratnagiri & Sindhudurg Districts of Maharashtra in view of the increasing demand for such Institutes; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	The Government of India has established  Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic  Sciences (CCRAS), as an autonomous  organization with the mandate to undertake research in Ayurvedic  healthcare services and research on life-style and non-communicable diseases. As on date, there are 31 peripheral Institutes/Centres of CCRAS in different States.  The details of such Institutes/Centres are given at ANNEXURE.(b):	Three  peripheral Research Institutes/Centres of CCRAS have been established during  the last three years, as under : - 1.	Regional Ayurveda Research Centre, (RARC), Dimapur, Nagaland2.	Regional Ayurveda Research Centre (RARC), Agartala, Tripura3.	Regional Ayurveda Research Institute for Mineral and Marine Medicinal Resources (RARIM&MMR), Goa.(c):	There is no proposal to set up more Ayurveda Research Institutes in the country.  (d):	The existing set up of peripheral Institutes/Centres under CCRAS is considered adequate for undertaking research in Ayurveda.
