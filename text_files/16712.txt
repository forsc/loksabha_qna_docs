Beneficiaries under PMGKAY

                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether a large number of people in the country have got benefited from ration distributed during the lockdown period under PM Gareeb Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY); (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the number of persons benefitted therein, State-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has extended the facility after the post-lockdown period also; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	whether the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana offers free foodgrains in the States including additional wheat or rice and lentils to all households with ration cards but it is utterly inadequate to address the massive and growing problem of malnutrition in the country as the majority cannot afford a balanced diet as at present the nutrient-adequate diet costs $2.12 a day; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof State-wise and the steps being taken to increase the quantity of each item in future under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO).........2/--2-(a) to (f): 		Under the PM Garib Kalyan Package announcement, it was decided by the Govt. to provide additional allocation of food-grain @ 5 kg per person per month free of cost for all the beneficiaries covered under Targetted Public Distribution System (Antyodaya Anna Yojna & Priority Households) including those covered under Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) for a period of 3 months i.e April-June, 2020. In this package, vide this Department’s letter dated 30.03.2020 (copy enclosed at Annexure-I) about 121 Lakh Metric Tons ( LMT) of food-grains were allotted to approximately 80.96 Crore beneficiaries. The said package was extended for a further period of 5 months i.e. up to November 2020.  Accordingly, allocation of food-grain of approx. 201 LMT for these five months (i.e. July 2020 to November 2020) was made vide this Department’s letter dated 08.07.2020 (copy enclosed at Annexure-II) for distribution to approx. 80.43 Crore beneficiaries.This free allocation under PMGKAY was in addition to the normal allocation being done through the National Food Security Act, 2013.**** ANNEXURE -IANNEXURE REFFERED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.394 FOR ANSWER ON 15.09.2020 IN THE LOK SABHA.F.No.7-1/2019 (ii)-BP.IIIGovernment of IndiaMinistry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public DistributionDeptt.
