                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

217






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Gross Enrolment Ratio




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has listed out/published data on its progress reportas per the various parameters in the past seven years in schools and highereducations institutions and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is true that Pupil Teacher ratio has come down from 34 to 26 in primaryschools and from 23 to 18 in upper primary schools, from 30 to 18 in secondary schooland 39 to 26 higher secondary schools during the last seven years and if so, the detailsthereof;(c) the details of work done by the Government to lower the Pupil Teacher Ratio;(d) whether the Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER) for girls has increased during this periodand if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether several new Universities and Higher Educational Institutes have beenopened up with health education being a major focus area and if so, the details thereof,institution and university-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): The Department of School Education and Literacy (DoSEL), Ministry of Educationhas developed the Unified District Information System for Education Plus (UDISE+)system to record data on indicators of school education provided by all the States andUnion Territories. The UDISE+ results have been published up to academic year 2019-20 and the same is available at the link https://www.education.gov.in/en/statisticsnew?shs_term_node_tid_depth=394&Apply=Apply. The Department of Higher Education,Ministry of Education conducts the All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE). TheAISHE result are available upto the year 2019-20 and the same is available at the linkhttps://www.education.gov.in/en/statistics-new?shs_term_node_tid_depth=383&Apply=Apply .(b): Yes, Pupil Teacher Ratio (PTR) has decreased in all the levels of school educationduring the last seven years. The PTR for Primary, Upper Primary, Secondary andHigher Secondary for 2013-14 to 2019-20 is given at Annexure –I.(c): Improving PTR is a continuous process that includes learners, teachers,teachinglearning process, learning environment, curriculum, pedagogy, learningoutcomes, assessment, etc. Samagra Shiksha focuses on improvement in quality ofeducation by providing support for different interventions to all States and UTs, like inservicetraining of teachers and school heads, conduct of achievement surveys at stateand national level, composite school grant to every school for providing a conducivelearning environment, grants for library, sports and physical activities, Support forRashtriya Avishkar Abhiyan, ICT and digital initiatives, School Leadership developmentprogramme, remedial teaching for academically weaker students, support for PadheBharat Badhe Bharat, etc.(d): The details of Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER) of girls for Primary, Upper Primary,Secondary, Higher Secondary Higher Education for 2013-14 to 2019-20 is at Annexure-I.(e): As per All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE), total no. of Universities andHigher Education Institutes, including newly opened ones, having specialisation insubjects related to medical, nursing, para medical, pharmacy, physiotherapy andveterinary category during 2018-19 and 2019-20 are as under:-<pre>Institution 2018-19 2019-20University 73 80College 3158 3757Stand Alone Institution 3109 3652</pre>******
