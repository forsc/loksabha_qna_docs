                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

573






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






RUB at Shahjahanpur




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is proposal to completely shutdown the unmanned crossing halt Gate No. 41-C and Km No. 44/89 by the railway department near village Chakasfaura in block Nigohi of Shahjahanpur Parliamentary constituency of Uttar Pradesh State;  (b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has taken any steps to construct underpass/railway crossing on railway gate at Talgon-Rohania link route and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government has received requests in this regard from public representatives in July,2021; and(e)	if so, the details thereof along with updated status?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e):  Level Crossing (LC) No. 41/C at Km. 44/8-9 has already been closed in 2019 with consent of District Administration by providing connecting road to adjacent Subway constructed in lieu of  LC No. 42/C. A letter has also been received from the Hon’ble Member of Parliament  in this regard. However, there is no proposal to construct subway at location of LC No. 41/C by Railways. *****
