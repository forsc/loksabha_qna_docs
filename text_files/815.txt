                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

876






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Holistic Industrial Policy




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is actively devising holistic industrial policy based on the inputs of all the stakeholders for rejuvenating and equipping the industrial sector to cater to the needs of future;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the initiatives taken by the Government to reduce the burden of the existing regulations on industrial sector?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): 	Yes, Sir. The Government is in the process of formulating a new industrial policy which will be a roadmap for all businesses in the country.  (c):         	The Ease of Doing Business initiative works to create a conducive environment to entrepreneurs by reducing the existing regulatory burden on industry which, can lead to reduction in time, cost and efforts required to do business. The Government is working for improvement in World Bank’s Doing Business rankings released annually based on regulatory burden in cities on Delhi and Mumbai on 10 identified indicators and is also working with States and Union Territories to improve business environment at sub-national level. Department shares an annual action plan with States/UTs and evaluates them on the basis of implementation of the action plan.  ******
