                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

750






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Development of Airport in Berhampur




Chandra Sekhar Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any representations/demands for development of airport in the main city of South Odisha viz Berhampur; (b) if so, the details in this regard; (c) whether the Government have examined the demand for setting up airport at Berhampur, Odisha; (d) if so, the details and the present status thereof; and (e) the time by which demand of airport in South Odisha is likely to be acceded by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (e): Ministry of Civil Aviation has been receiving requests for development of airport in Berhampur from time to time. However, no proposal has been received as per the Greenfield Airport Policy.;;Government has launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS)- Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik (UDAN) to promote regional connectivity. An airport which is included in the awarded routes of RCS-UDAN and requires upgradation/development for commencement of RCS operations is developed under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme. RCS - UDAN is a market driven scheme. The interested airlines based on their assessment of demand on particular routes submit their proposals at the time of bidding under RCS - UDAN from time to time. Berhampur (Rangeilunda) airport figures in the tentative list of unserved airports of RCS document. No valid bid connecting Berhampur was received after four rounds of bidding under RCS-UDAN. However, if any valid bid is received in future rounds of RCS-UDAN, same shall be considered as per the scheme document.
