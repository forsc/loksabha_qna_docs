                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1571






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Assistance for Dairy Production




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is providing financial assistance to various States including the State of Tamil Nadu for setting up of dairies in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof including the salient features of the schemes for the purpose along with the amount of assistance provided by the Government to various States during the last three years and the current year, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government has any scheme for training of milk producers in the use of new techniques for development of dairy production sector with upgraded technology; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	Yes Sir, the Government is providing financial assistance for setting up of dairies in the country through dairy development schemes (b)	This Department is implementing Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Fund scheme with an objective of Creation/ strengthening of Milk Processing, Value addition & chilling facilities with total outlay of Rs. 11,184 crore, including loan component of Rs.8004 Crore. Under the scheme, National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) raise funds from the market and disburse loan to National Dairy Development Board (NDDB)/ National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) which in turn disbursed by them to End Borrowers (Dairy Co-operative, Multi State Dairy Cooperative, Milk Producer Companies (MPC), NDDBs subsidiaries, Self Help Groups (SHGs) and Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) registered under the State Cooperatives and the Companies Acts). GoI provide 2.5% interest subvention to National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD). So far, 41 Projects from 13 States with a total outlay of Rs.5419.47 Crore with a loan component of Rs. 3716.36 Crore have been sanctioned by NDDB and NCDC. Out of the loan component of Rs. 3716.36 Crore, an amount of Rs. 1225.40 Crore has been disbursed. State-wise year-wise financial details of projects under DIDF is at Annexure-IAs on 31.10.2021, 9 projects have been sanctioned with a total outlay of Rs. 686.74 Crore having a loan component of Rs. 549.39 Crore. for establishment of  milk and milk product plants in the  State of  Tamil Nadu  This Department is also implementing a scheme namely National Programme  for  Dairy Development (NPPD)  under which there is provision  for setting up of milk processing & manufacturing  plant  under a component “Dairying through Cooperative” (DTC) primarily   in   the  States of Uttar Pradesh and Bihar.(c)	& (d) Under the component “Dairying through Cooperative” (DTC) of NPDD scheme there is provision of Training and Capacity development among others components.  Under the component financial assistance in the form of loan of Rs.924.56 crore from Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) is provided.  An amount of Rs.. 475.54 crore is  Grant by Govt. of India and Rs.168.8 crore is  Participating Institutes’ (PI) share of for the period of 5 years from 2021-22 to 2025-26. Loan to participating agency will be available @ 1.5%.****
