                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EARTH SCIENCES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1063






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Installation of Lightning Arresters




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken or proposes to take steps to install lightning arresters considering the cases of lightning strikes in Maharashtra, particularly in Buldhana Parliamentary Constituency; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND MINISTRY OF EARTH SCIENCES (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) & (b) 	Yes Sir. National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) has recently constituted a technical committee including India Meteorological Department (IMD) to explore the technological options of low cost Lightning Arrester Solution in certain States including Maharashtra. This initiative is taken under the World Bank assisted National Cyclone Risk Mitigation Project (NCRMP) Phase-II and the States of Goa, Gujarat, Kerala, Karnataka and West Bengal are also supposed to get benefitted from this initiative.
