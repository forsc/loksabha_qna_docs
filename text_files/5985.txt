                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5507






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Health Protection for the Poor




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE   be pleased to state: (a) the details of initiatives taken by the Government to protect the poor families across the country from financial crisis to maintain their healthcare; (b) the details of budget allotment and allocation made for the said purpose during each of the last three years for protecting the poor families so far; and (c) the details of funds allotted with reference to Gross Domestic Product (GDP) growth in the current budget session?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):  The Government has launched National Health Protection scheme known as Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) on 23.09.2018, to provide health assurance/insurance of Rs. 5.00 lakh per family to around 10.74 crore poor and vulnerable families as per the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC). As on date, 33 states/UTs have signed the Memorandum of Understanding for implementation of AB-PMJAY. (b):    Year-wise Budget allotment made for the AB-PMJAY is as under - Year	Budget allocation (In crore)2018-19	2400.002019-20	6400.00(c):    As per the economic survey 2018-19, expenditure by Centre and States on Health sector as a per cent of GDP is 1.5.      In the current financial year, Rs. 6400.00 crore have been earmarked for AB-PMJAY.……………
