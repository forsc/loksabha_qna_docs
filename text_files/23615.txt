                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






FOBs/Subways in Sirohi




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the location-wise number of Foot Over Bridges (FOBs) and sub-ways proposed to be constructed by the Railways in North Western Railway Zone especially in Sirohi district;(b) whether any target has been fixed to complete the work of all such proposed (FOBs) and sub-ways and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any over bridge is under construction to connect platforms by Rail Vikas Nigam Limited on passenger tracks at Swaroopganj railway station, if so, whether in view of the interest of the city, it is proposed to extend this foot over bridge on the DFC line connecting the eastern part of the city to the western premises; and(d) if so, the total budget allocated for this and the time limit fixed for its construction?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
