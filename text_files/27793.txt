                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Implementation of Atmanirbhar Bharat Initiatives




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is pushing for expeditious implementation of Atmanirbhar Bharat initiatives (ABN) announced in the financial year 2020- 21; (b) if so, the number of such initiatives which have got approvals of the Government as schemes/ programmes and date of their respective notifications for implementation; (c) whether the Government has announced or proposed to announce any additional schemes during the financial year 2021-22 under ABN; and (d) if so, the details of subject-wise ABN schemes/ programmes and their physical and financial achievements so far?






ANSWER




                                                             THE MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)    (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) & (b):  The Government regularly reviews the progress of implementation of the Atmanirbhar Bharat initiatives (ABN) announced in the financial year 2020- 21.  A Statement indicating details of schemes/programmes approved by the Government, including date of notification/orders, wherever applicable is at Annexure. (c) & (d):  The Government announces various schemes/programmes from time to time after taking a holistic view of the requirements of the economy.  A COVID Relief Package of Rs. 6,28,993 crores has been announced on 28th June 2021 to support Indian economy in fight against COVID-19 pandemic.  The schemes announced under the package are separate schemes and not a part of the Atmanirbhar Bharat Packages announced in the year 2020-21. The package has been announced recently on 28.06.2021 and physical and financial progress would be visible in due course.******
