                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3295






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Disabilities Included in Census Survey




S. Jothimani














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the different types of disabilities included in census survey process along with the types of training given to census workers to include different types of disabilities;(b) whether the Government is aware of underestimation of persons with disability population in the census reports;(c) if so, the steps the Government is taking to correctly enumerate the number of persons with disability in the upcoming census;(d) whether the Government has conducted or is planning to conduct special training for census enumerators for correctly recording information about people with disabilities; (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(f) whether there is a database/record of persons with disability for Karur Parliamentary Constituency; and(g) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The Questionnaires for the forthcoming Census are finalised in consultation with various stakeholders.   Seeing, hearing, speech, mobility, -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.3295 FOR 16.03.2021intellectual and other disabilities including multiple disability are to be collected during forthcoming Census. (b) & (c):	The information recorded in Census is respondent based.  As per Census 2011, the total disabled persons in India are 2,68,14,994, constituting 2.2% of the total population.  As per Report on National Sample Survey conducted by National Statistical Office, Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation during July-December 2018, the persons with disability in India are 2.2%.  (d) & (e):	The forthcoming Census is to be the first digital Census and there is a provision for self-enumeration. Adequate training is given to Census Enumerators to ensure complete coverage of people with disabilities in Census.   The awareness campaigns are also part of Census Operations. (f) & (g):	As per Census 2011, total number of disabled persons in  Karur District of Tamil Nadu are 14,042.  Parliamentary constituency-wise data are not maintained.*****
