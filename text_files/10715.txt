                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3995






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Conversion of SHs to NH in Odisha




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of State roads/State Highways (SHs) in Odisha that have been  approved for declaration as National Highway (NH) during the last five years;(b)    the present status thereof, NH-wise;(c)    the time by which the work on these upgraded NH is likely to be completed;(d) whether the Government proposes to approve Bhawanipatna-Muniguda-Bissamcuttack-            Rayagada-Parvatipuram (Andhra/Odisha Border) as NH and if so, the details thereof; and (e)  the time by which the four laning work thereon is likely to start?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c)  Twelve roads having an aggregate length of 1,114 km have been notified as National Highways (NHs) during the last five years in Odisha. Development and maintenance of National Highways (NHs) is a continuous process and works are taken up depending on the inter-se-priority of works, traffic and availability of funds, etc. (d) to (e)    This Ministry considers declaration of such State Roads/Highways as new NHs from time to time keeping in view the requirement of connectivity, inter-se-priority and availability of funds, etc.  *****
