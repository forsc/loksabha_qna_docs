                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1834






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






BPL Data




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has the state-wise data on the number of families living Below Poverty Line (BPL);(b) if so, the district-wise details thereof in Madhya Pradesh; and(c) whether the number of families living below poverty line has reduced during the last two years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) to (c): Based on the Household Consumer Expenditure Survey data collected by National Statistical Office (NSO) in its 68th round  of National Sample Survey (NSS) conducted in 2011-12, the erstwhile Planning Commission estimated poverty lines and poverty ratios in 2011-12 following the Tendulkar methodology. The poverty lines and poverty ratios, thus estimated, were released through a Press Note issued on 22nd July 2013.The numbers of persons living below poverty in 2011-12, State/UT-wise, as per the Press Note, are given in the Statement at Annexure.                                                    *****
