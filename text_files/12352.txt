                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1181






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Status of ST List in Uttar Pradesh




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of castes along with their number which have been accorded the status of scheduled tribes in Uttar Pradesh by the Government;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has taken any steps for the upliftment of such tribes; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)	Information is placed at Annexure I.(c) & (d)	Details of steps taken for the upliftment of Scheduled Tribes including Uttar Pradesh is placed at Annexure II. Annexure IList of Scheduled Tribes in the State of Uttar Pradesh 1. Bhotia2. Buksa3. Jaunsari 4. Raji5. Tharu6. Gond, Dhuria, Nayak, Ojha, Pathari, Raj Gond (in the districts of Mehrajganj, Sidharth Nagar, Basti, Gorakhpur, Deoria, Mau, Azamgarh, Jonpur, Balia, Gazipur, Varanasi, Mirzapur and Sonbadra)7. Kharwar, Khairwar (in the districts of Deoria, Balia, Ghazipur, Varanasi and Sonbhadra) 8. Saharya (in the district of Lalitpur)9. Parahiya (in the district of Sonbhadra)10. Baiga (in the district of Sonbhadra)11. Pankha, Panika (in the districts of Sonbhadra and Mirzapur)12. Agariya (in the district of Sonbhadra)13. Patari (in the district of Sonbhadra)14. Chero (in the districts of Sonbhadra and Varanasi)15. Bhuiya, Bhuinya (in the district of Sonbhadra)  Annexure IIDetails of upliftment programmes for Scheduled Tribes including Uttar Pradesh: -1.	Pre-Matric Scholarship for ST students (Class IX and X).2.	Post-Matric Scholarship for ST students (Class XI onwards).3.	National Fellowship & Scholarship for Higher Education for ST students.4.	National Overseas Scholarship for ST students for studying abroad.5.	Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS).6.	Development of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs)7.	Grant-in-aid to Voluntary organizations working for the welfare of Scheduled Tribes.8.	Scheme of Strengthening Education among ST Girls in Low Literacy Districts.9.	Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution.10. Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) for the welfare and development of Scheduled Tribes.*****
