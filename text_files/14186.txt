                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2945






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Single Sub-Post Office




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of single sub-post offices currently operational in the State of Rajasthan, district-wise; (b) 	whether the Government intends to convert/ demolish all the single sub-post offices working in the Ajmer Lok Sabha constituency into binary post offices; (c) 	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which it is likely to be done; (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the existing vacancy for the post of Multi Tasking Staff, Postmen, Postal Assistant, Higher Selection Grade (HSG)-I and HSG-II in the Ajmer along with the sanctioned strength for the aforesaid posts in the said division and the time since when these posts are lying vacant? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)  	Sir, a total of 728 Single  handed Sub Post Offices are currently   operational in the State of Rajasthan. The district -wise details are at Annexure- A(b) to (d) 	No, Sir. No proposal to convert/ demolish all the single handed sub- post offices working in the Ajmer Lok Sabha constituency into binary post offices is under consideration, since these Single handed Post Offices provides for easy access to public of all   their post office requirements. (e) 	A total 138 posts comprising of Multi-Tasking Staff, Postmen, Postal Assistant, Higher Selection Grade(HSG-I)and HSG-II are lying  vacant in Ajmer Division from the date of retirement/promotion/transfer/death/resignation  of last incumbent. The   details of category wise  existing vacancy   are as under: -<pre>Category	Sanctioned Strength	Existing vacancyHSG-I	9	8HSG-II	25	25PA	174	27Postman	138	63MTS	59	15Total	405138</pre>
