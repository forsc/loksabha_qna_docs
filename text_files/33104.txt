                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

668






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Use of Drones




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is using drones for the purpose of security of the country;(b) if so, whether there is applicability of rules and regulations on use of drones by common people;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the State-wise details of the sectors in which services are being provided using drones along with the number of such drones being used?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a)  Yes, Sir. The Government is utilizing Drones for the purpose of security of the country. (b) & (c):  The Ministry of Civil Aviation has issued Drone Rules 2021 vide Gazette Notification Number 477 dated 25th August, 2021 for regulation of operations of Drones in India constituting aspects like classification, certification, registration, operations, Remote Pilot licensing of drones in the country and prescribing offences and punitive actions. (d)  With the technological advancements, a large number of drones are used in diversified sectors including infrastructure development, Agriculture surveys, Industrial applications, Ground Mapping, Aerial Photography, Payload delivery, Urban Air Mobility solutions, Forestry services, Fish and Wild Life observation, Emergency Medical Response, Maintenance of Law and Order and Disaster Relief operations etc. depending upon requirement of the states. *****
