                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

778






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Price Rise of LPG and NPG




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been continuous increase in LPG and NPG prices for last several months; (b) if so, the reason for the extent of rise in price of LPG and NPG along with details of amount increased during the last three years including current year, year-wise; (c) whether any steps/measures have been taken by the Government to control the price rise of LPG and NPG;(d) if so, the detail thereof; and(e) the details of the present cost of a LPG and NPG in the country, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) to (e): The prices of petroleum products in the country are linked to the price of respective products in the international market. However, for domestic LPG the Government continues to modulate the effective price to consumer to insulate the common man from rise in international prices. Government of India has allocated domestic gas to various City Gas Distribution (CGD) entities for CNG (Transport) and PNG (Domestic) purpose. However, retails price of CNG/PNG is determined by CGD entities after considering state taxes, tariff and other components.The details of average retail selling price of domestic LPG during the last three years and current year is given below:Average RSP of Domestic LPGYear	Rs./14.2 Kg Cylinder2018-19	768.122019-20	694.732020-21	695.152021-22 (till 1st November)	857.40Note: Simple average of applicable prices at Delhi during the periodThe details of retail selling price of domestic LPG in state/UT capitals effective 1st November 2021 is given as Annexure.x-x-x-x-x
