                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

528






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Supply of Coal




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government supplies domestic coal to PSUs/IPPs of the Centre/States;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the quantity of coal supplied by the coal companies in the year 2020-21 is more as compared to 2019-20; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) & (b): Coal India Limited (CIL) and Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL) supply coal to their linked Power Consumers comprising of Centre & State Generating Stations as well as Independent Power Producers (IPPs). The coal supplied to Power Sector by CIL and SCCL during the current year (till October) is as under:Figures in Million Tonnes (MT)Type of Generating Station /Auction window 	CIL	SCCLCentral Generating Station	120.35	8.25State Generating Station	78.10	22.33Independent Power Producers	71.37	-Special Forward e-Auction	21.90	-Total	291.72	30.58(c) & (d): The quantity of coal supplied by CIL & SCCL to the Power Sector in the year 2020-21 is less than the year 2019-20. For the year 2020-21, CIL has supplied a quantity of 444.97 MT to the Power Sector in comparison to the quantity of 465.72 MT in the year 2019-20. SCCL has supplied a quantity of 40.90 MT to the Power Sector in comparison to the quantity of 52.77 MT in the year 2019-20.*****
