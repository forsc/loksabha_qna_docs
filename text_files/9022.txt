                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2573






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Pod Hotels on Railway Stations




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Railways has plans to prepare Pod Hotel or Capsule Hotel for providing stay facility for passengers on Railway stations; (b) 	if so, the State and station –wise details thereof; (c) 	whether Jaipur Junction is also included in this scheme; (d) 	if so, the time by which this facility is likely to be made available; and (e) 	if not, the time by which the Government proposes to provide this facility at  Jaipur Junction?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. An initiative has been taken up by Indian Railway Catering & Tourism Corporation Ltd. (IRCTC) to start a project of setting up of Pod/Capsule Concept Retiring Room at Mumbai Central Railway Station on Public Private Partnership Model. Initially, only one station has been selected for this project.(c) to (e): No, Sir. Currently this facility is not available in Jaipur Junction. However, the facilities in the currently run retiring rooms at Jaipur Junction has been upgraded under the scheme of upgradation and renovation of retiring rooms w.e.f 22.03.2019.*****
