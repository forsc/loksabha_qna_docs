                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Setting up of New Broadcasting Kendra




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is contemplating to set up new broadcasting kendras for the purpose of providing broadcasting facilities in far-flung and remote areas of the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and location-wise; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c): Projects have been approved for setting up of new AIR FM Broadcasting Kendras in far-flung and remote areas of the country as mentioned in Annexure and also for setting up of 5 new television high power transmitters at Green Ridge, Patnitop, Rajouri (2 transmitters, one each for DD National & DD News) in Jammu & Kashmir and at Himbotingla in Ladakh. *****
