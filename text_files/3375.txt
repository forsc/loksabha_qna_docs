                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2954






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Adequate Supply of Drinking Water




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether adequate drinking wateris not being supplied to the people particularly in district Dahod of Gujarat;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) the funds allocated under Waterand Sanitation Management Organisation(WASMO) scheme during the last threeyears to enable the supply of drinkingwater in adequate quantity to the peopleof Dahod district in Gujarat; and(d) the details of the schemes beingrun to make drinking water available in thesaid district?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) Water is a State subject. As reported by State Government of Gujarat, all villages of Dahod district are covered with service level of 40 litre per capita per day (lpcd) as per norms. The funds allocated under Waterand Sanitation Management Organisation(WASMO) scheme during the last threeyears are as under:-Financial Year	Amount (in Rs. Crore)2016-17	6.402017-18	15.122018-19	15.98(d) The details of the schemes beingrun to make drinking water available in Dahod district as per information received from the State Govt.are as under:- •	Regional Water Supply Schemes - 7 Nos. •	Individual Water Supply Schemes - 562 Nos. •	Mini Pipe Water Supply Schemes – 1718 Nos. •	Hand pumps – 43134 Nos.
