                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1979






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Funds for Environment Impact Assessment




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether no funds have been allocated for Environment Impact Assessment (EIA) this financial year;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c)	the total number of EIA’s that have been carried out since 2014 till date, year-wise and State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)& (b) Funds for undertaking Environment Impact Assessment (EIA) are not required in the Ministry. The EIA reportfor grant of Environmental Clearance to a project or activity is required to be prepared by Project Proponent.(c)	Since 2014 till date, total of 4957 EIAs have been carried out by Project Proponents for various proposals. The State/UT-wise and year-wise details are enclosed as Annexure-1. ***
