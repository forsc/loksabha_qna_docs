                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2816






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Quality of medical/diagnostic Equipments in Government Hospitals




Ganesan Selvam













                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Arvind Ganpat Sawant
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has assessed the quality of medical/diagnostic equipment used in Government hospitals and if so, the details and outcome of the assessment;(b) whether most of the medical/diagnostic equipment used in All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) and other leading Government hospitals are not of international standards, if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto;(c) the details of amount spent on the purchase of new medical/diagnostic equipment during the last three years by AIIMS, institution-wise;(d) whether there is a shortage of such equipment in the Government hospitals, medical colleges, affiliated hospitals including AIIMS; and(e) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and the remedial steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b):   The medical/diagnostic equipment procured for All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) as well as other leading Central Government Hospitals are as per requirement of different disciplines and Departments. Further, specification for the medical/diagnostic equipment procured are recommended by Technical Specification Committee consisting of technical experts  of the field, drawn from Institutes of National Importance (INIs) /AIIMS, and other renowned hospitals, keeping in view the relevant international quality standards.(c):  Details of medical/diagnostic equipment procured from 2017-18 to 2019-20 for/by all AIIMS, as per information received from the Institute/Agency, are given at Annexure.(d) & (e):   Procurement of medical equipment is a continuous process and the same is procured as per requirement of the Institutes as per the extant rules/guidelines.
