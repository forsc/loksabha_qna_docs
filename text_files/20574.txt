                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1805






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Support to MSME Sector




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to empower Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises(MSMEs) sector to utilise the limited human and economic resources in an optimum manner;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the system in place to educate MSMEs about global developments and help them to acquire skills necessary to keep pace with these developments?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTRY OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)& (b):	The Ministry of  Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) through its Policies and various Schemes viz., Credit Linked Capital Subsidy and Technology Upgradation (CLCS-TU), Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS), Interest Subvention Schemes for MSMEs 2018 and Credit Guarantee Trust Fund Scheme (CGTMSE), Micro and Small Enterprises- Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP) etc. empower Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) sector to utilize the limited human and economic resources in an optimum manner.(c):	 The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises through its various field Institutions have been creating awareness/ Training programmes to educate the MSMEs to acquire the skills in line with the global development.
