                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Skill Development Centres in Tamil Nadu




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the number of Skill Development Centres operational in the country; (b)       the number of such Skill Development Centres operational in Tamil Nadu; (c)        the number of people who have been provided training by these Centres; and (d)       the number of beneficiaries who have been provided assistance by Government for their startups?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       Under Skill India, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing various programmes / scheme for the skill development. The number of operational skill development centres across the country under the programmes of MSDE is given below: Programme/Scheme	No. of operational centres across countryTraining Centres (TCs) under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (including operational Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK))	1,484Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) (including newly sanctioned)	307Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs)	14,716 Total  operational skill development centres 	16,507 (b)       The number of operational skill development centres in the State of Tamil Nadu under the programmes of MSDE is given below: Programme/Scheme	No. of operational centres - IndiaTCs under PMKVY (including operational PMKK)	93JSSs	8ITIs	494Total 	595 (c)        The number of trained in various programmes by MSDE across India and in the State of Tamil Nadu by skill development training centres is given below: Programme/Scheme	No. of Candidates trained - India	No. of candidates trained – Tamil NaduPMKVY (including PMKK) (since 2015;  till 21.11.2021 )	132.54 lakh	7.36 lakhJan Shikshan Sansthan (2018-22)	10.95 lakh	0.35 lakhIndustrial Training Institutes (2014-2020)	86.87 lakh	2.68 lakhTotal	230.36 lakh	10.39 lakh (d)       In order to inculcate self employment skills in the candidates for entrepreneurship / micro entrepreneurship, apart from domain / core curriculum, training in English, Employability & Entrepreneurship (EEE) module (155 hours) is being imparted under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY). Also, Training Providers provide support to the candidates for entrepreneurship development through facilitating bank linkages along with providing guidance and mentoring support. Further, under demand driven PMKVY 3.0, the aspirations of the candidates have been given primacy which aims to support the self-employment as well. The scheme has benefitted youth across the country including the State of Tamil Nadu. Under PMKVY, as on 21.11.2021, around 2.94 lakh candidates have been self-employed across the country. Out of which, 24,445 candidates are from the State of Tamil Nadu. Also, Ministry is implementing Pradhan Mantri YUVA (PM YUVA) Yojana, a pilot scheme, for creating an enabling ecosystem for entrepreneurship development through entrepreneurship education, training, advocacy and easy access to the entrepreneurship network in skill training institutes. So far, around 60 thousand candidates benefited from this scheme across the country including the State of Tamil Nadu. Indian Institute of Entrepreneurship (IIE), Guwahati, an autonomous organization under this Ministry, is also providing training, research and consultancy activities in Small and Micro Enterprises (SME), with special focus on entrepreneurship development. So far, around 2.5 lakh persons have been trained through more than 6,700 programmes conducted by IIE.
