                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

653






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






NH Projects in Odisha




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the technical/financial sanction has been granted for construction of National Highway on Motu to Mathili section in Malkangiri district of Odisha in Vijayawada-Ranchi corridor and Raipur to Vishakhapatnam economic corridor in Odisha;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) the present status of other related works including acquisition of land in this regard; and (d) the steps being taken by the Government to expedite the process to complete the projects in a stipulated time frame?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d) In the Motu to Mathili section of NH-326 of 141.444 km in Malkangiri district of Odisha under Vijaywada-Ranchi corridor,  the widening of the section from km 327/700 to km 413/700 (42.70 km) has been recently completed in December, 2019,  work has been sanctioned for  Strengthening work from km 413/700 to km 429/700 (16 km) and periodical renewal work from km 429/700 to km 475/900 (46.200 km) of existing  2 lane   has been taken up.  Strengthening of existing 2 lane from km 475/900 to km 512/444 (36.544 km) is included in Annual Plan 2021-22.  Raipur to Visakhapatnam economic corridor on NH-130CD is of 464 km of which 240 km is in Odisha. Out of 240 km a length of 124 km at a cost of Rs 2869 Crore has been approved till date. Land acquisition and Forest clearance are in advanced stage. Environmental Clearance has been obtained. All the stages of the development of the corridor are being closely monitored by NHAI to complete it in stipulated time.*****
