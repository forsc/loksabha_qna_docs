                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

140






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Renting of Monuments




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is renting out our old heritage structures &       monuments for the photography & film shooting purposes;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the details of the rules framed up for  this purpose;(c)	the list of the sites State/UT-wise which the Government is considering for renting out including Uttar Pradesh; and(d)	whether the use of drones especially at night in these archaeological sites is permitted for films photography  and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY) (a) &   (b)	Permission for photography and filming are granted in protected monuments as per the provision of  the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Rules, 1959.However, no monuments is rented out in this regard. (c)	Question dose not arise. (d)	Photography or filming is permitted only during the opening hours of the monument.
