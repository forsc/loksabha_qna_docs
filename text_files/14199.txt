                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2796






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Harassment of Soldiers




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government is aware of the reports of soldiers being subjected to harassment and menial jobs by senior officers;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the cases of the deaths of such soldiers brought to the notice of the Government;(c) whether the Government has ordered an inquiry into the cases reported by the relatives of the victims alleging harassment as the prime cause of the soldiers’ suicide;(d) if so, the details thereof and whether the Government is considering to grant compassionate employment to the spouse or the next of kin on merit; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  Yes, Sir.  A few allegations of harassment have been received by the Indian Army.  These allegations predominantly were anonymous or third party complaints and generic in nature.  However, no such case has been reported in the Indian Navy and Indian Air Force.(b) to (e):  Details of alleged cases of harassment in the last three years in Indian Army are as under:Year	No. of cases received2017	1132018	1542019	115All such complaints were investigated and found to be unsubstantiated.  No cases of death due to the alleged harassment have been reported in the last three years by the Indian Army.*****
