                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

545






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Development of River Ports in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the active river ports in the State of Assam at present;(b)	whether the Government proposes to bring any plan for the development of river ports in Assam and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether Government has any plan to revive inactive/ dormant river ports and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether Government has also any plan to revive Kholabonda river port in Barpeta district of the State and if so, the details thereof;(e)	whether the tender floated for Development of Inland Water Transport (IWT) Terminal at MMLP Jogighopa, Assam has been finalised, if not, the reasons therefor; and(f)	the time by which the terminal is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)   	There are two river port/ permanent terminals constructed by Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) on National Waterway-2 (river Brahmaputra) at Pandu (Guwahati) and Dhubri in Assam.(b)	Comprehensive development of National Waterway-2 (NW-2) (river Brahmaputra) has been initiated by IWAI, which inter-alia, also include construction of Inland Water Transport (IWT) Terminal at Jogighopa.   Apart from this, IWAI has entrusted work of establishment of 4 tourist jetties at Biswanath, Jogighopa, Neamati & Pandu to National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL).(c) & (d) p is no plan to revive inactive/ dormant river ports and  Kholabonda river port.(e) & (f) Work of construction of IWT Terminal at Jogighopa, Assam on NW-2 has been entrusted to National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL), which has been awarded by NHIDCL to M/s. DVP Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. on 06.10.2021 at a cost of Rs. 63.90 crore with target completion in December, 2023.                                              *****
