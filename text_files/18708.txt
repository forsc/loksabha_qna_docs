                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

15






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Centrally Funded Rural Development Schemes




Raju Bista













                                                    John Barla
                                                








                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                








                                                    Nayab Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the allocation of funds under the heads of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS), Rural Infrastructure Development Fund(RIDF) and other development schemes from 2014 to till  date in the districts of Kalimpong, Darjeeling and North Dinajpur of Assam, District Kurukshetra of Haryana, Alipurdwar in West Bengal and district Etawa of Uttar Pradesh along with the details of utilisation of the funds;(b) 	whether the Government is aware of allegationsof massive corruption in projects undertaken underPMGSY, PMAY, RIDF, and other development schemesin the districts mentioned above and if so, the follow upaction taken by the Government thereon;(c) 	whether any audit has been conducted by theGovernment and C&AG for centrally funded projectsunder PMGSY, PMAY, RIDF and other developmentschemes and if so, the details of the audit report in thisregard; and(d) 	if not, the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (d):  The information is being collected.*******
