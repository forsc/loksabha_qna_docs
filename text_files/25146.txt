                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

998






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Sponge Iron Plants




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of sponge iron plants currently functional in public and private sectors in the country, State/UT-wise including Chhattisgarh;(b)	the details of the employment prospects created in these plants;(c)	whether any provision has been made to provide job opportunities to the local people in these plants; and(d)	if so, the details thereof particularly with regard to Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)	The number of sponge iron plants functional in the country including Chhattisgarh during FY 2020-21  is  as following:-              Region Wise Details of Indian Sponge Iron Industry, 2020-21State	No of UnitsJHARKHAND	24ODISHA	76WEST BENGAL	36EASTERN Region Total	136CHHATTISGARH	69GOA	3GUJARAT	10MAHARASHTRA	7WESTERN Region Total	89UTTAR PRADESH	4NORTHERN Region Total	4ANDHRA PRADESH	6KARNATAKA	35TAMIL NADU	6TELANGANA	9SOUTHERN Region Total	56Total	285Source: JPC(b)	As per Survey conducted by the Joint Plant Committee (JPC), total employment in the domestic sponge iron market stood at 84,632 during 2019-20. (c)&(d): The decision on recruitment of employees in private steel sector is taken by individual steel companies/investors on the basis of their requirement.  In the case of CPSEs, recruitment is based on Government Guidelines.********
