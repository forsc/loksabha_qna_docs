                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Colleges/Universities for Women




Sunil Dattatray Tatkare













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Srinivas Kesineni
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any recommendations for setting up ofexclusive Universities for women in the country;(b) if so, the total number of Women’s Colleges/ Universities that are functioning inthe country, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the existing women colleges have adequate infrastructure and facultystrength and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government is planning to open more Colleges/ Universities forwomen including hostels in view of the shortage of these Colleges/ Universities inthe country and if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Government has any proposal to construct buildings for womenunder the Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (e): In order to encourage girl education, the Sub-Committee of CentralAdvisory Board of Education (CABE) was constituted to look into the issues relatedto “Girls Education”. The Committee has recommended establishing of an exclusiveuniversity for women in each State.As informed by University Grants Commission (UGC), there are 16universities exclusively for women and 3950 colleges exclusively for girls in thecountry. The State/UT-wise details of women colleges/universities are Annexure I.The details regarding infrastructure in existing women colleges is at Annexure II.Further, under the Centrally Sponsored scheme of Rashtriya UchchatarShiksha Abhiyan (RUSA), central support is provided to increase access, equity andquality of Higher Education. Till date, under the scheme, central assistance has beenapproved for 342 women colleges and 7 women universities including for thedevelopment of hostels in these institutions across various States and UnionTerritories. The funds for these institutions have been approved under variouscomponents such as creation of Model Degree Colleges (MDCs), new colleges(professional & technical), upgradation of colleges to MDCs, upgradation of existingautonomous colleges to Universities, equity initiatives, infrastructure grants tocolleges, infrastructure grants to universities etc.***
