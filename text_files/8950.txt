                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Self Employment




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether various schemes are being undertaken by the Government for holistic development of rural areas and generating self-employment including in district Gopalganj of Bihar, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b)	whether the goal set under various schemes for rural areas in Bihar have been achieved by the Government, if not, the reasons therefor and the measures taken by the Government to achieve the set goals;(c)	the total number of employment generated in the rural parts of the country along with the total number of employment likely to be created for the coming five years; and(d)	whether the Ministry has evolved multi tool system for monitoring and evaluation of implementation of rural development programmes and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	The Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM), Deen Dayal Upadhyaya – Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Rurban Mission and National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) and Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) for holistic development of rural areas  of the country including Gopalganj of Bihar, through employment generation, strengthening of livelihood opportunities, promoting self-employment, skilling of rural youths, provision of social assistance, infrastructure development etc. The targets and achievements under various rural development schemes in respect of the State of Bihar including  Gopalganj  are given at Annexure-I(a) and I(b). In order to ensure the programme benefits reach the rural areas in the country, including the State of Bihar, the Ministry has evolved a comprehensive monitoring and evaluation mechanism for implementation of rural development programmes. The comprehensive system of monitoring and evaluation not only bring out success but also areas which need improvement.MGNREGS is a demand driven scheme. The total number of persondays generated under MGNREGS in rural areas of the country during 2019-20 (as on 29.11.2019) is 167.38 crore. The persondays targeted for generation in the coming five years is as under:-Timeline/Milestones	             2019-20	 2020-21	2021-22	    2022-23	2023-24Person days generated (in crore)	258	   270	          270	      280	 280Monitoring and evaluation of the rural development programmes is done at multi-level with a multi-tool framework approach through Performance Review Committee Meetings, District Development Co-ordination and Monitoring Committee named as “DISHA”, National Level Monitors, Area Officers Schemes, Common Review Mission, Concurrent Evaluation and Impact Assessment Studies.
