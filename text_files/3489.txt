                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Eradication of TB




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether some international organizations are co-operating to eliminate Tuberculosis (TB) in the country and if so, the details thereof indicating the names of such organizations; (b) the number of TB cases reported in the country during the current year; (c) whether any target has been set for complete eradication of TB in the country; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (d):  A statement is laid on the Table of the HouseSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 293* FOR 12TH JULY, 2019(a)  Yes. Several International Organizations are collaborating with the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare to eliminate Tuberculosis (TB) in the country.                    The details of such organizations is Annexed.(b) 11,84,778 TB cases have been reported in the country during the current year from 01.01.2019 to 07.07.2019(c)&(d) Government of India has set the target for achieving Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) related to TB by 2025 as against the global target of 2030. Accordingly, the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has prepared the National Strategic Plan to achieve the SDG target of reduction in mortality due to TB by 90%; and reduction in incidence of TB by 80%; by 2025, as compared to 2015 baseline.
