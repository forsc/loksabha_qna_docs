                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Acreage under Cultivation




Ajay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether during the recent years the acreage under cultivation is declining constantly; and (b)	 if so, the extent of decline in the above acreage during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	As per the latest available data on Land Use Statistics for the year 2014-15, cultivated land/ area under cultivation has remained stagnant at around 155 million hectares during 2012-13 to 2014-15. The details of acreage under cultivation during 2012-13 to 2014-15 are as under:(thousand hectares)Year	Cultivated Land/ Area under cultivation2012-13	1552232013-14	1555812014-15	155221******
