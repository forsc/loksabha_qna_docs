                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

743






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Inclusion of Idukki and Malankara Dam Under Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from the State of Kerala requesting the inclusion of Idukki and Malankara dam under the existing Swadesh Darshan scheme;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has withdrawn the project of spiritual circuits under the Swadesh Darshan scheme allocated for the State of Kerala; (d)	if so, the details thereof;(e) 	whether the Government has received any request from the State of Kerala to reconsider the above said withdrawal; and(f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (f):	 The Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned the project for ‘Development of Pathanamthitta- Gavi- Vagamon- Thekkady’ under Eco Circuit theme of Swdesh Darshan for an amount of             Rs.76.55crore which includes development of tourism related components in Idukki.  The project is physically complete. No project under the scheme is sanctioned in Malankara. The Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned 5 projects in the State of Kerala under Swadesh Darshan Scheme and include 3 projects under Spiritual theme.  One project ‘Development of Spiritual Circuit: Kasaragod, Wayanad, Kannur, Kozhikode, Palakkad, Mallapuram, Thrichur, Ernakulam, Idukki, Kottayam, Allaphuza, Pathananamithitta, Kollam, Trivandrum in Kerala’ was dropped for administrative reasons. No proposal of the State Government for revival of this project is under consideration before the Ministry of Tourism.********
