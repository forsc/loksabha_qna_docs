                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2130






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Development of Coastal Circuit in Kakinada




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that Development of Coastal Circuit in Kakinada Hope Island Konaseema in Andhra Pradesh was approved in 2014-15 under PRASAD Scheme and an amount of Rs. 70 crores was also sanctioned for this purpose by the Ministry and if so, the details of the circuit; (b) 	the date on which the State Government has submitted project proposal along with the action taken by the Government on the above proposal; (c) 	whether Ministry sought any further clarification on the final DPR and if so, the details of queries and response of the State Government thereon; (d) 	the time by which the said project is likely to be completed; and (e) 	the details of amount allocated for 2021-22 for this circuit and other circuits in Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	The project Development of Kakinada - Hope Island - Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary - Passarlapudi - Aduru - S Yanam – Kotipally  was sanctioned for a total amount of Rs. 67.84 crore on 31.03.2015 under the Coastal Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan scheme of Ministry of Tourism.The revised proposal was submitted by the State Government on 27.03.2015 and the proposal was approved by the Mission Directorate on 30.03.2021 and by Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC) on 31.03.2021. The project is complete. Sanctioning of projects under the schemes of Swadesh Darshan and National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) of Ministry of Tourism is a continuous process. The scheme of Swadesh Darshan is currently under review and no new projects are sanctioned under the scheme. However, the details of projects sanctioned by Ministry of Tourism in Andhra Pradesh under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD are annexed.******
