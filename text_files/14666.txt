                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Jute Development Fund




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had set up a Special Jute Development (SJD) Fund for the revival and integrated development of the Jute Industry; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the amount released from the SJD fund to the Jute Corporation of India for disbursement of subsidy to State level cooperative procurement agencies was lying unutilized;(d) if so, the quantum thereof during the last two years; and(e) the steps taken by the Government to utilize the unutilized fund?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	Yes Sir. Government of India had set up a Special Jute Development (SJD) Fund for all round development of jute sector. (c) to (e):	Jute Corporation of India (JCI) Limited had received Rs. 2.00 crores under SJD Fund out of which  Rs. 83.44 lakhs were disbursed and the remaining fund was refunded to Government of India as State Apex Cooperative Societies did not approach JCI for disbursement of subsidy. ****
