                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1827






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






MoU by IREDA with NHPC




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Indian Renewable Energy Development Agency Limited (IREDA) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with NHPC recently for providing its technical expertise in developing renewable energy projects;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether IREDA is technically supporting State Governments for developing renewable energy projects; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. Indian Renewable Energy Development Agency Limited (IREDA) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with NHPC Limited on 08.01.2021.As per this MoU, major activities for which IREDA has agreed to provide consultancy services to NHPC Limited, are as follows: i.	Undertake Techno-Financial due diligence (excluding Accounting, Tax and Legal due diligence) of renewable energy & energy efficiency / conservation projects.ii.	Assist in developing an action plan to create and acquire renewable energy (RE) projects (as the case may be) for the next five years.iii.	Ascertain valuation of brownfield assets and also provide assistance for acquisition of such assets including financial modelling & returns thereon.The MoU is for period of five years, extendable further upon mutual consent.(c) No, Sir.(d) Question does not arise.*****
