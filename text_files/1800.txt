                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1785






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) for the benefit of farmers in various States of the country and if so, the details and the objectives thereof; (b) 	the details of the facilities being provided by the Government under the said Yojana to Bihar; (c) 	whether the Government has reviewed the overall implementation of the Yojana; (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the results achieved so far under the Yojana, State/UT-wise including Bihar; and(e) 	the other steps being taken by the Government to ensure that water reaches every farm in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) was launched during the year 2015-16. The scheme is being implemented in various States of the country including Bihar. The components of PMKSY are as under:i.	Accelerated Irrigaion Benefit Programme (AIBP):  To focuses on faster completion of ongoing Major and Medium Irrigation projects including National Projects. Implemented by Ministry of Jal Shakti.ii.	PMKSY-Har Khet ko Pani (HKKP):  Source augmentation, distribution, ground water development, lift irrigation, diversion of water from water plenty to water scarce areas, repair, restoration, renovation of traditional water bodies. Implemented by Ministry of Jal Shakti.Contd…2/--2-	During 2016-17, ninety-nine (99) on-going Major/Medium irrigation projects under PMKSY – (AIBP), having ultimate irrigation potential of 76.03 lakh ha. have been prioritized in consultation with States, for completion in phases up to December, 2019 along with their Command Area Development & Water management (CADWM) works.	iii.	PMKSY- Watershed Development (WDC):  Focuses on development of rainfed portions of the net cultivated area and culturable wastelands. Activities include ridge area treatment, drainage line treatment, soil and moisture conservation, rain water harvesting, nursery raising, afforestation, horticulture, pasture development, livelihood for assetless persons, etc. Implemented by Department of Land Resources (DoLR).iv.	PMKSY-Per Drop More Crop (PDMC):  Focuses on enhancing water use efficiency at farm level through Micro Irrigation technologies viz. Drip and Sprinkler Irrigation. Implemented by Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DAC7FW).The details of Central Assistance released to Bihar under PMKSY from 2015-16 to 2018-19 is as under:Ministry/Department	Component 	Amount (Rs. In Crore)Ministry of jal Shakti	AIBP	125.65	CAD	  43.57	SIM	  38.64	RRR	    6.26DoLR	WDC	  99.24DAC&FW	PDMC	  90.61	CAD:  Command Area Development; SMI: Surface Minor Irrigation;	RRR: Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies.(c) & (d):	Implementation of the programme is being reviewed regularly by respective Ministries/Departments through review meetings, video conferences, web based Management Information System (MIS) etc.	State wise achievements made under the components of PMKSY including, Bihar is given at Annexure-I to III.(e):	In addition, to ensure that water reaches every farm in the country, following steps have been taken by the Government:•	A special Package for completion of Irrigation Projects to address agrarian distress in Vidarbha, Marathwada and other chronically drought prone areas of rest of Maharashtra has been approved during 2018-19.Contd…2/- -2-•	To help in addressing the problem of water-logging in Muktysar, Faridkot and Ferozpur districts in South-West Punjab, relining of Rajasthan Feeder and Sirhind Feeder Projects is implementing.•	Shahpurkandi Dam (National Project) on River Ravi in Punjab State has been approved on 06.12.2018.•	Polavaram National Project (PIP):  A multi-purpose irrigation project on the river Godavari in Andhra Pradesh State which has been declared as a National Project.
