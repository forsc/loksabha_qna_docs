                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1017






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






PMFME Scheme to Entrepreneurs




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of entrepreneurs that have been provided support under PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme, in Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh from 2020-2021 and 2021-2022; and  (b)	the annual targets of the Ministry under PMFME Scheme for Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh for the next two years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a):	The details of number of entrepreneurs that have been provided support under PM Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme, in Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh from 2020-2021 and 2021-2022 are as under:  (i). Credit Linked Subsidy to individual micro enterprises: Financial Year	No. of applications submitted	No. of loan sanctioned by the Bank	No. of beneficiaries provided credit linked subsidy2020-21	23	1	12021-22	98	17	8(ii). Seed Capital assistance to Self Help Group (SHG) Members:Financial Year	No. of SHG Members	Amount released (In crore)2020-21	--	--2021-22	918	3.56 (b):	Target of 11083 individual units in respect of Andhra Pradesh has been fixed for 5 years, from 2020-21 to 2024-25.*****
