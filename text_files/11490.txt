                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Hormonal Injection for Increasing Production of Milk and Eggs




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether it is true that to increase production of milk and eggs, the hormonal injections are given to animals(b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	Whether such injections will affect the human body after consumption of those products;(d)	Whether any research or study has been undertaken to make the process more organic; and(e)	If so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	to (b) No Sir. There is no documentary evidence available with the Department regarding use of hormonal injections to increase production of milk and eggs. However, use of oxytocin has been reported for letdown of milk for which Department has periodically issued advisories to create awareness among dairy owners regarding ill effects of non-judicious use of oxytocin.        (c) No such information is available with the Department.        (d) to (e) No such information is available with the Department.
