                                    DEFENCE

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Private Participation in Defence Manufacturing




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the steps being taken to promote Indian private defence industry and to ensure that there is level playing field between private industry and Defence Public Sector  Undertakings (DPSUs) in large Defence Procurements;(b) the quantum of investment by the private sector in defence manufacture during the last five years;(c) whether the Indian private defence industry matured to be at par with DPSUs and if so, the criteria/procedure and security protocol adopted while awarding tenders/projects to private firms; and(d) whether the tenders are still being awarded on nomination to DPSUs, if so, the reasons therefor and the total value of such tenders awarded over the last five years?
