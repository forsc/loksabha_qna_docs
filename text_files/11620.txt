                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

529






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Halt at Satnali Station




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that there is no train from Loharu, Satnali, Mahendragarh, Kanena Khas and Gurugaon railway station of Haryana to capital Chandigarh while train number 22481/82 remains standing idle whole day in Delhi;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to run train number 22481/82 via Satnali to Chandigarh and if so, the time by when it is likely to be done and the details thereof; and(c) whether there is any proposal to halt train 22471/72 at Satnali station and if so, the time by when it is likely to be done and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) & (b) At present, there is no direct train service connecting Loharu, Satnali, Mahendragarh and Kanina Khas to Chandigarh. However, connectivity between Gurgaon and Chandigarh is available by 22451/52 Bandra (T)-Chandigarh Express (Bi-weekly). 22481/82 Jodhpur-Delhi Sarai Rohilla Express has a lie-over period of approx. 18 hours at Delhi Sarai Rohilla. Owing to operational constraints on Delhi-Ambala-Chandigarh section, there is no proposal for extension of this train upto Chandigarh, at present.(c) No, Sir. At present, no proposal for provision of stoppage of 22471/72 Bikaner-Delhi Sarai Rohilla Express at Satnali is under consideration. However, provision of stoppages is an on-going process on Indian Railways, subject to operational feasibility, commercial justification, etc.     *****
