                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4346






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Cluster Development Programmes under MSE-CDP




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the clusters developed under the Micro and Small Enterprises- Cluster Development Program (MSE-CDP), State/UT-wise;(b)	the details of the potential clusters identified and the clusters developed under the MSE-CDP in Kerala during the last three years;(c)	the details of the Common Facility Centres set up under the MSE-CDP in Kerala; and(d)	the details of jobs created in operational clusters under the scheme in the country including Kerala during the last three years, State/UT-wise?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a):	76 Common Facility Centres (CFCs) and 169 Infrastructure Development (ID) Projects have been developed under Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP). State/UT-wise details are enclosed at Annexure.(b):	MSE-CDP is a demand driven scheme and proposals under it are received through respective State Governments. 2 clusters have been developed in Kerala under MSE-CDP during the last three years. (c):	11 Common Facility Centres have been set up in Kerala under MSE-CDP.(d):	Under MSE-CDP, Common Facility Centre is set up for enhancing the productivity and competitiveness as well as capacity building of Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs). Job creation in the clusters depends on various factors such as aggregate domestic & global demand, seasonality etc. in and around the clusters.
