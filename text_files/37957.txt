                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Allocation of Funds for Reconstruction of Shakti Peeth Temples




Queen Oja














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether any scheme and fund has been determined by the Union Government 	for the reconstruction of the Shakti Peeth (temples), ancient temples, already 	located in every State of the country including Assam and if so, the details 	thereof;(b)	Whether any plan and fund has been determined and Shakti Peeth (temple), 	ancient temples located in the all the North-Eastern States including Assam have 	been identified for the reconstruction and if so, the details thereof;(c)	The amount of fund fixed by the Government for the reconstruction of Shakti 	Peeth 	(temple), ancient temples located in all the North-Eastern States including 	Assam; and(d)	The details of funds provided by the Government as on date for the 	reconstruction of Shakti Peeth (temple), ancient temples located in all the North-	Eastern States including Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Archaeological Survey of India (ASI), an attached office of Ministry of Culture is 	mandated with the responsibilities of conservation and development of Centrally 	Protected Monuments and Sites. However, ASI has no specific scheme for 	reconstruction of the Shakti Peeth (temples) which are not declared as 	monuments of national importance.(b) to (d) Does not arise, in view of above.*****
