                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

22






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Curb on Import of Oil from Turkey and Malaysia




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to cut oil imports from Turkey and also extend curbs imposed on imports from Malaysia to oil, gas and other products;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the overall impact on the oil availability due to this decision;(d) whether the Government is taking any steps to offset any shortfall due to these curbs imposed on imports from Turkey and Malaysia; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (e): The Government has no plans to cut oil imports from Turkey and Malaysia at this stage. *****
