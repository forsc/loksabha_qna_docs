                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Soil Testing Labs




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the total number of Soil Testing Labs, State/UT-wise;(b	 whether all the Soil Testing Labs are adequately equipped, if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	the percentage of samples collected and tested under the programme;(d)	the funds allocated, under the programme and the details of the funds utilized, State/UT-wise; and(e)	whether the Government has experienced any obstacles in the implementation of the programme and if so, the details thereof?   






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR )(a): 	State/UT-wise number of Soil Testing Labs is given at Annexure- I.(b): 	All Soil Testing Labs are adequately equipped.  (c):	Under the Soil Health Card Scheme, the percentage of samples collected and tested are 100% percent during cycle- II (2017-19). (d):	State/UT- wise funds allocated and utilized by State/UTs is given at Annexure – II. (e): 	Punjab and Assam States have reported obstacle in implementation of the programme during 2020-21 due to COVID- 19.
