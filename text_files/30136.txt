                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1678






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






PMGKY in MP




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of beneficiaries benefited under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY) in Madhya Pradesh (MP), district-wise; (b) 	whether the Government has received any complaint regarding irregularities committed during implementation of this scheme; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):  	Additional foodgrains @ 5 kg per person per month for around 546.42 lakh beneficiaries under Phase-I and Phase-II and for around 482.58 lakh beneficiaries under Phase-III, Phase-IV and Phase-V of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) have been allocated to State of Madhya Pradesh (MP) for free of cost distribution. Information regarding district-wise beneficiaries under PMGKAY from May,2021 to November, 2021, as received from State Govt, is at Annexure-I.(b) and (c): 		Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) is operated under the joint responsibility of the Central and the State/UT Governments. The responsibility of identification of beneficiaries under TPDS (and PMGKAY also, as targeted beneficiaries are same) lies with respective State/UT.  Complaints regarding NFSA and /or PMGKAY or any State/UTs which are received in this Department, regarding any irregularities in the functioning of the TPDS are forwarded to the concerned State/UT Government for inquiry and appropriate action at their end. An offence committed in violation of the provisions of PDS (Control) Order, 2015 is liable for penal action under the Essential Commodities Act, 1955. Thus, the Order empowers State/UT Governments to take punitive action in case of contravention of relevant provisions of these Orders. The State Govt of MP has received few complaints regarding irregularities committed during implementation of this scheme.
