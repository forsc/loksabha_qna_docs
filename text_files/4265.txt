                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3919






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Manufacturing of Complete Train Set




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government proposes to manufacture complete train sets in the rail coach factories;(b)  if so, whether the Government has taken any steps in this regard; and(c)  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY( SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL )(a) to (c)  Indian Railways are already manufacturing train sets in Integral Coach Factory (ICF), Chennai. Two train sets comprising total 32 coaches have been turned out from ICF so far. Orders exist on ICF to manufacture 29 more rakes consisting of 624 coaches by March 2022.******
