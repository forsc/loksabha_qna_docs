                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1449






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Violation of Ceasefire




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there are reports of frequent violations of ceasefire along the Line of Control; and(b) if so, the steps taken by the Government to reduce the said violations?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Yes Sir.(b):	All the forward posts along the Line of Control (LoC) are adequately strengthened to withstand enemy fire. There is well established Standing Operating Procedure (SOP) to safeguard against enemy action along the LoC and to minimize casualties. While safeguarding all the forward posts, immediate and effective retaliation is undertaken by the security forces in cases of ceasefire violations. All such incidents of ceasefire violations are also taken up with Pakistan at the appropriate level through established mechanism and flag meetings.*****
