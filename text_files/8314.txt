                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Regional Newspaper and Magazines




Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the year-wise and language-wise details of regional newspaper/magazines in the States and Union Territories during the last three years;(b)	whether the cases of newspapers/magazines being run illegally have come to the notice of the Government; and(c)	if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof along with the steps taken by the Government to stop it?ANSWER






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES {SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR}(a)	: The language-wise, region-wise details of newspapers and periodicals, registered with Registrar of Newspapers for India (RNI), in various States/UTs are published annually in the form of a publication titled ‘Press in India’. This publication is available on the website of RNI (www.rni.nic.in).(b)	and (c) : Violations of the provisions of the Press and Registration of Books Act, 1867, as and when noticed, are handled as per the provisions of the Act.*******
