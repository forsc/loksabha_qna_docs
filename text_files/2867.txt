                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2600






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Rural Roads




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number and details of rural roads connected to the main roads for emphasising on the development of rural areas during the last three years, State-wise; and(b)	the funds allocated for the development of villages in the rural areas during the said period and the details of funds spent out of the allocated funds, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The number and length of rural roads completed under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years, State-wise is given at Annexure. I.(b):	Details of Central Share released for development of villages in rural areas under PMGSY, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin) and Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Guarantee Employment Scheme (MGNRGES) and their expenditure during the last three years are given at Annexure-II, Annexure-III and Annexure-IV respectively. *****
