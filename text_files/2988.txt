                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2791






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Black Box in Trains




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Railway proposes to place black box in 500 trains on the lines of the aircrafts within next ten months which would play an important role in emergency situation of accidents;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)and(b) Indian Railway has started installing Loco Cab Audio Video Recording system (LCVAR)/ Crew Voice/Video Recording System (LCVR/CVVRS) in Diesel and Electric locomotives.  This system, which is similar to Black Box,  provides invaluable data to investigators which helps them in understanding the sequence of events leading up to an accident and for identifying operational issues and human factors. 	32 locomotives (25 Diesel and 7 Electric) have already been provided with LCVRs/CVVRSs. Further, Purchase Order for 480 Nos. CVVRSs has already been issued.  (c) 	Does not arise.*****
