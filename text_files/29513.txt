                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1224






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Implementation of Sivagiri Tourism Circuit Project




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of the implementation of Sivagiri Tourism Circuit project in Kerala;(b) 	whether the Government has released any funds for the same; and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	if not whether the Government is considering to release funds for the implementation of project without delay; and(d)	whether the project will be completed within the targeted period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (b):	  The  Ministry of  Tourism under its  Swadesh Darshan scheme  has  sanctioned  the project  ‘Development of Spiritual Circuit : Sivagiri Sree Narayana Guru Ashram – Arruvipuram – Kunnumpara Sree Subrahmania – Chembazhanthi Sree Narayana Gurukulam in Kerala’ for total cost of Rs. 6947.00 lakh. The Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned release of Rs. 388.40 lakh to India Tourism Development Corporation Ltd., the implementing agency of the project, as per the scheme guidelines.  (c):	  Does not arise. (d):	  The date of completion of this project is 31.12.2022. ********
