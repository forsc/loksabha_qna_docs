                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1211






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Decline in Food Grain Productivity




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the production of food grains has declined during the last three years due to drought conditions;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the details of the farmers who have committed suicide during the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	No sir, the production of food grains has not declined in the country during last three years. The details of production of foodgrains in the country during last three years are as under:Year	Production (Million Tonnes)2017-18	285.012018-19	285.212019-20 #	296.65		# - Fourth Advance Estimates.(c):	The State-wise details of the farmers / cultivators suicide during 2017, 2018 and 2019 is at Annexure.
