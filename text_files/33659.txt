                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Production of Sugarcane




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the total production of sugarcane in the country in the last three years and thecurrent year;(b) the details of sugarcane production and exports, State-wise;(c) whether the Government has incentivized sugarcane farmers in the light of increasedsugarcane exports; and(d) if so, details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):   The details of State-wise and all-India production and export of sugarcane   during last three years and the current year i.e. 2018-19 to 2021-22 are given at Annexure I & II, respectively.(c) & (d): Major share of sugarcane produced in the country is utilized by sugar mills for production of sugar and remaining share are utilized for production of Gur, Khansari, ethanol etc. In order to encourage farmers to produce sugarcane, the Central Government fixes Fair and Remunerative Price (FRP) of sugarcane on yearly basis before commencement of every sugar season.The FRP of sugarcane for the current sugar season 2021-22 is Rs. 290/quintal at 10% recovery.
