                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5070






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






National Council for p;romotion of Urdu Language




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of amount allocated/released to National Council for Promotion of Urdu Language (NCPUL) during the last five years; (b) the detailed expenditure of NCPUL during the said period, year-wise; and (c) the schemes being implemented by NCPUL at present? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) & (b) : The quantum of amount allocated/released and expenditure during last five years to National Council for Promotion of Urdu Language (NCPUL), New Delhi is as under:-<pre>                                                                                   (Rs. In lakh)Year	Grants allocated	Grants released 	Expenditure incurred2017-18	7000.00	7000.00	7848.00*2018-19	7040.00	7040.00	7452.00*2019-20	8400.00	8400.00	8652.00*2020-21	8600.00	8600.00	9078.00*2021-22	8950.00	8950.00	9600.00*</pre>* Expenditure includes the expenditure from internal resources generated by NCPUL.(c) : The NCPUL, New Delhi has been implementing the following schemes for promotion of Urdu language in all over India.(i)	One Year Diploma CABA-MDTP(ii)	Two Year Diploma in Calligraphy(iii)	One Year Diploma in Urdu(iv)	Two Year Diploma in Arabic- 2 - (v)	One Year Certificate in Arabic(vi)	One Year Certificate in Persian(vii)	Certificate Course in Paper Machie(viii)	Publication of Books/periodicals(ix)	Exhibition/Book Fair under Books Promotion(x)	Academic Project(xi)	National/International Seminar(xii)	Workshop on Capacity Building of Urdu Working Journalists(xiv)	Production telecast Urdu Duniya on TV channel through ‘News-18’(xv)	Financial Assistant for Seminar/Lecture Series(xvi)	Financial Assistance to Author for Bulk Purchase of Books/Periodicals(xvii)	Financial Assistance to Author for Publication of manuscripts(xviii)	Financial Assistance to Scholars for Research Projects(xix)	Advertisement and subsidy to News Papers under Urdu Press Promotion *****
