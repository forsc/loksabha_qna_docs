                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

272






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Modernisation of State Police




Dileshwar Kamait














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that the financial assistance is provided by the Union Government to modernize State Police and if so, the details thereof; and(b) if so, the details of budget allocated to modernize Bihar police?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) and (b):   ‘Police’ and ‘Public Order’ are State subjects under the seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India.  However, the Government of India supplements the efforts of the State Governments towards equipping and modernizing of their police forces, by providing financial assistance under the scheme of ‘Assistance to States for Modernisation of Police’ [erstwhile scheme of Modernisation of Police Forces (MPF)]. Under this scheme, the States have been provided central assistance for acquisition of advanced weaponry such as INSAS Rifles & AK series rifles; all types of intelligence equipment including Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs),  Night  Vision  Devices  (NVDs), CCTV  Surveillance  system & body -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.272 FOR 20.07.2021worn camera systems; modern communication equipment and state-of-the-art equipment for  security / training / forensics / cybercrime / traffic policing. Further, ‘construction’ and ‘purchase of operational vehicles’ are permitted in the insurgency affected north-eastern States and Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected districts. The State Governments are free to include proposals in accordance with their strategic priorities and requirements. The scheme, in its present form, was approved by the Government for implementation from the year 2017-18. The details of budget allocated and released amount, under the scheme to modernize Bihar Police since the year 2017-18 are as below:                                                                                                                                (Rs. in crore) Year 	Allocated amount 	Released amount*2017-18	33.73	5.732018-19	29.90	13.182019-20	27.62	9.422020-21	27.62	19.12          *Release is less than allocation on account of non-submission of Utilization Certificates(UCs).   *******
