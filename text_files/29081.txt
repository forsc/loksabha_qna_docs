                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

952






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Medical Devices Parks




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Khan Saumitra
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) the objective of the scheme ‘Promotion of Medical Devices Parks‘ which is being implemented in the country along with the budget outlay thereof; (b) whether the Government has received any proposals to esablish Medical Devices Parks in various States including Gujarat, Karnataka, West Bengal and Uttar Pradesh; (c) if so, the details and the current status thereof, State/UT-wise project-wise; and (d) the other measures taken/being taken by the Government to promote establishment of medical devices parks/medical technology parks?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS                                     (DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a): The Scheme “Promotion of Medical Devices Parks” has the following objectives: (i)	Creation of world class infrastructure facilities in order to make Indian medical device industry a global leader.(ii)	Easy access to standard testing and infrastructure facilities through creation of world class Common Infrastructure Facilities for increased competitiveness will result into significant reduction of the cost of production of medical devices leading to better availability and affordability of medical devices in the domestic market.(iii) Exploit the benefits arising due to optimization of resources and economies of scale.The total outlay of the scheme is Rs.400 crore. (b) & (c): The Government received proposals from 16 State Governments / Union Territory Administrations including the proposals from Gujarat, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh. No proposal was received from West Bengal. Out of these 16 proposals, Government has given in-principle approval for financial assistance under the scheme “Promotion of Medical Devices Parks” to the proposals of Himachal Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.(d): The scheme “Promotion of Medical Devices Parks” was earlier being implemented as the scheme “Assistance to Medical Device Industry for Common Facility Centre”. Under the scheme “Assistance to Medical Device Industry for Common Facility Centre”, final approval of assistance up to Rs. 25 crore was given to the project of Andhra Pradesh Med Tech Zone Ltd. (AMTZ) for establishment of Common Facility Centre for superconducting magnetic coils testing & research facility. Two installments amounting Rs. 14.99 crore have been given to AMTZ.*****
