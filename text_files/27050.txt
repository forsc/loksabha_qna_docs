                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2742






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Donating Doses




Kuruva Gorantla Madhav













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has received any further requests from neighbouring countries for exporting and donating doses since February 2021; and(b) if so, the details of requests received and the status of fulfilling these requests?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	A number of countries, including some neighbouring countries, had signed contracts with Indian vaccine manufacturers for the procurement of COVID-19 vaccines. At present, priority is being accorded to the implementation of our national vaccination programme. ***
