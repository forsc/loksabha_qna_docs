                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2650






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Road Projects in Jharkhand




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to construct an elevated road/highway in Ratu, Ranchi, if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be constructed;(b)	the areas of Ranchi which are proposed to be included under Bharatmala Pariyojana along with the funds likely to be spent thereon; and(c)	the number of toll plazas operating in the State of Jharkhand, district-wise including Ranchi and the amount of revenue collected from toll plazas under Ranchi?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	The construction of the Elevated Corridor in Ranchi City from km. 0.000 to km. 3.570 of NH-75 including Down Ramp on NH-23 from km. 0.000 to km. 0.610 has been planned on EPC Mode. The project comprises construction of 4 lane elevated section from Zakir Hussain Park to Piska More on NH-75 and a 2 lane Down Ramp towards NH-23 at Piska More. To decongest Ratu Chowk and Kishore Yadav Chowk, construction of a single lane Up Ramp and Down Ramp have also been envisaged. The estimated cost is about Rs. 534.73 crore with construction period of 2.5 years from date of start and same has been targeted for award in March, 2022.(b)	Projects comprising length of 898 km with cost of Rs. 27,137 crore have been identified for development in the State of Jharkhand under Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I including areas near Ranchi.
