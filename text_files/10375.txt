                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Bullet Train Projects




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of number of bullet train projects that are going on in the country; and (b)	if so, the status thereof and the amount of budget that has been allocated for these projects so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):  At present, only Mumbai-Ahmedabad High Speed Rail (MAHSR) Project has been sanctioned by the Government of India, which is under implementation. A Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) namely National High Speed Rail Corporation Limited (NHSRCL) has been constituted to execute this project.         The budget allocation made by the Government for this project in last 3 years is as under:-(i)	F.Y. 2017-18 –  ? 150 crore(ii)	F.Y. 2018-19 –  ? 2400 crore(iii)	F.Y. 2019-20 –  ? 7100 crore.*****
