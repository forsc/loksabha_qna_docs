                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1375






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Borrowings by DHFL




Asaduddin Owaisi













                                                     Imtiaz Jaleel Syed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Registrar of Companies has submitted a report to the Government about Rs. 83,873 crore borrowings by DHFL;(b)	if so, the details thereof including the sources of borrowings by DHFL i.e. from banks, external commercial borrowings and National Housing Bank etc.;(c)	whether the Government has ordered a probe by Serious Fraud Investigation Office (SFIO) after the report of the Registrar of Companies, if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether involvement of some banks and officials have been established; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the time by which the report of SFIO is likely to be received?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS			(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)???????????????????????????????????????????? 			(???????????????????)(a)& (b) Yes Sir.  The Regional Director (Western Region) has conducted an Inspection u/s 206 (5) of DHFL and submitted the inspection report to the Ministry on 24.10.2019.  As per inspection report, the company has taken the following loans as on 31.03.2019:- 2 -Particulars	INR in CroreRetail Loan	Housing	44,851Non-Housing	13,590SME	4,924Wholesale Loan	Housing-Residential projects	15,655SRA Projects	7,021Non-Housing	9,340Commercial	233Total Loan Portfolio	95,615(c) Yes Sir.  The Ministry has ordered Investigation of DHFL and 05 other companies namely Immediate Real Estate Pvt. Limited, Tenancity Real Estate Pvt. Limited, RKW Developers Pvt. Limited, Darshan Developers Pvt. Limited and RajenSkycrapersPvt. Limited to be conducted by SFIO vide order dated 06.11.2019.(d) The inspection report submitted by the Regional Director (Western Region) has not gone into the involvement of banks and officials. (e) The investigation of DHFL and 05 others as in para (c) above, have been assigned to SFIO on 06.11.2019 and the time for completion cannot be indicated as of now. ******
