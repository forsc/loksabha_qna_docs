                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1689






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Road Projects in Nagaland




Tokheho Yepthomi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the approved road projects from 2018 to March 	2021 in the State of Nagaland which are of economic importance 	and those having inter-state connectivity also including those 	which are covered under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund 	(CRIF), location-wise;(b)    the estimated cost of these projects; and(c)    the funds allocated and utilized for these projects during the said 	period? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)  (a) to (b): During the period April, 2018 to March, 2021, one work of Construction of Intermediate lane road from Yongnyah EAC Hq. to 186MW Dikhu Hydro Project, having length 22 Km at a cost of Rs. 74.06 crore was sanctioned under Economic Importance scheme on 30.01.2019. In addition, 30 works for a length of 605 Km amounting to Rs 6018 crore have been sanctioned under NH(O), SARDP-NE and Bharatmala Scheme during the above said period in the State of Nagaland. (c)  The funds are not allocated project wise, the same are allocated State-wise. The details of funds allocated for all ongoing EI works and expenditure incurred for the above said sanctioned work in the State of Nagaland are as under: Sl. No	Year	Allocation for entire state (Rs in Cr)	Utilization/Expenditureon the said EI project (Rs in Cr)(i)	2018-19	55.47	-(ii)	2019-20	147.8	4.27(iii)	2020-21	72	9.46  *****
