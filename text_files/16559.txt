                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Soil Health Card




Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Soil Health Testing Laboratories established in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana and the number of farmers who got soil test done along with the details of their districts during the last four years; and(b) 	the total number of farmers who have been provided with Soil Health Cards under Soil Health Management Scheme for increase in the agricultural production across the country during the last years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	Sixty soil testing labs (STLs) and 1328 Mini Labs @ 2 per Mandal have been established in Andhra Pradesh under Soil Health Management Scheme. In Telangana State, 3 Mobile Soil Testing Vans and 2050 Mini Labs were procured/ established under Soil Health Management Scheme. 	Year wise, district-wise details of the number of farmers who got soil test done during the last four years in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana is given at Annexure. (b):	The total number of farmers that have been provided with Soil Health Cards (SHCs) across the country during last four years is given below:Year	Number of farmers provided with SHCs2016-17	8,17,28,4222017-18	3,02,14,4682018-19	8,73,50,6862019-20	16,76,289
