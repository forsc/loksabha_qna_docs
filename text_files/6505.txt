                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

37






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Insurance Amount for Fishermen




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plans to increase the insurance amount in case of death or injury, under the centrally sponsored National Scheme of Welfare of Fishermen, to fishermen licensed/identified or registered with the State/UT Governments; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a)	and  (b)  :        A Statement is placed on the Table of the House.***** Statement referred to in reply to the Lok Sabha Starred Question No.*37 put in by Shrimati Kanimozhi Karunanidhi, Member of Parliament for answer on 19th November, 2019 regarding Insurance Amount for Fishermen(a) and (b) : 	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under National Scheme of Welfare of Fishermen, a component of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme(CSS) on Blue Revolution : Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries has been implementing Group Accident Insurance for Active Fishermen to provide insurance coverage to Active fishermen licensed/registered with the State Governments and Union Territories.  The Group Accident Insurance for Active Fishermen is being implemented under the CSS in align with the Pradhan Mantri Surakasha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) with effect from June, 2017. The insurance coverage to fishermen under the under the Group Accident Insurance for Active Fishermen was enhanced with effect from May, 2014 (i) from  Rs. 1.00 lakh to 2.00 lakh against death or permanent total disability and (ii) from Rs. 50,000 to 1.00 lakh for partial permanent disability. Presently, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has no plans to increase the insurance amount to the fishermen. *****
