                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Bypass at Attingal in Kerala




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of construction of new bypass in Attingal on 	Kazhakkuttam -Kadambattukonam stretch of NH-66 in Kerala;(b)	whether it is a fact that 3-A notification had been issued for this 	project and the same was lapsed due to delay in issuing 3-D 	notification;(c)	if so, the details thereof, date wise; and(d)	the steps taken by the Government to complete the project 	without further delay?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Bidding process for awarding the work of 6 laning of Kadambattukonam - Kazhakkuttam section of NH-66 including Attingal Bypass on EPC mode has been initiated.(b) & (c) The 3A notification for Kadambattukonam to Kazhakottam project issued vide S.O. no. 2368(E) dated 08.06.2018 had lapsed as 3D notification was not published within the stipulated period. Subsequently, 3A notification was again notified vide S.O. no. 395 (E) dated 24.01.2020 and 3D notification has also been notified vide S.O. no 2841(E) dated 20.08.2020.(d)	Bidding and land acquisition process has been initiated for awarding the work of 6 laning of Kadambattukonam - Kazhakkuttam section of NH-66 on EPC mode in the current financial year.*****
