                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Health Warning on Tobacco Products




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya













                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether new sets of health warning to be depicted on the pack of tobacco products have been notified; and (b) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b): Section 7 of the Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products (Prohibition of Advertisement and Regulation of Trade and Commerce, Production, Supply and Distribution) Act, 2003 (COTPA 2003) mandates for display of specified health warning on all tobacco product packs. As per Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products (Packaging and Labelling) Third Amendment Rules, 2020 notified vide G.S.R. 458 (E) dated 21st July, 2020, new set of images (Image 1 and Image 2) have been notified to be displayed on all tobacco product packs. These Rules have come into force on the day of 1st December, 2020. Image-1, shall be valid for a period of twelve months up to 30th November, 2021. Image-2, shall come into effect from 1st December, 2021 and shall remain in force up to 30th November, 2022. ********
