                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

33






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Development of Hatya Haran as Religious Site




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government has taken or proposes to take steps for development and maintenance of Hatya Haran religious site at Balamau in Uttar Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the details of the progress made in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	 A statement is laid on the table of the House.******* STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.†*33 ANSWERED ON 24.06.2019 REGARDING DEVELOPMENT OF HATYA HARAN AS RELIGIOUS SITE.(a) to (c):	 Development and promotion of tourism is primarily the responsibility of the concerned State Governments/Union Territory Administrations. The Ministry of Tourism, however, under its Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies scheme guidelines, provides central financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. The projects for development under the above schemes are identified in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations/Central Agencies and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, their adherence to relevant scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. No project has been sanctioned by the Ministry for development of Hatya Haran religious site at Balamau, Uttar Pradesh. **********
