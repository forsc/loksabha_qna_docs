                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

26






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Protection of Handloom Industry




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether eighty per cent of the Handloom  Cooperative Societies in the State of Andhra Pradesh have become powerless and are on the verge of extinction, if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has taken any steps to safeguard the handloom societies in the State;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d)      the incentives and financial assistance being given to these handloom societies  and handloom workers? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	Handloom Cooperative Societies come under the ambit of the State Government.  No such report has been received from State Government of Andhra Pradesh regarding the Handloom Cooperative Societies.(b) to (d):	Government of India is implementing various welfare and developmental schemes/programme, viz. National Handloom Development Programme, Handloom Weavers’ Comprehensive Welfare Scheme, Yarn Supply Scheme and Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme for the holistic and integrated development of Handloom sector and welfare of Handloom Weavers.  Apart from the above, State Government of Andhra Pradesh is also implementing Yarn Subsidy Scheme, Pavala Vaddi Scheme, Training and Infrastructure Development Scheme, Replacement and Modernization of looms, 30% Special Rebate Scheme, Providing marketing facility through conduct of Exhibitions, Cash Credit Limits, Thrift Fund Scheme and Old Age Pensions for Socio-economic development and sustainability of handloom weavers. Financial assistance upto Rs.2.00 crore is provided to Handloom Cooperative Societies of Andhra Pradesh for setting up of a Block Level Cluster under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP). The financial assistance provided by Government of India to Handloom Cooperative Societies of Andhra Pradesh during last three years is as under:-Year	Amount released (Rs. in crore)2016-17	1.582017-18	1.412018-19	1.27The funds are released to the Handloom Cooperative Societies as per the proposals initiated by them and utilisation of the previously released funds. ********
