                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Upgradation of Employment Exchanges




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of existing Employment Exchanges in the country;(b)	whether all Employment Exchanges have been upgraded as envisaged in the 12th Plan;(c)	if so, the State-wise details thereof;(d)	the manner in which Model Career Centre is different from Employment Exchange; and(e)	the year-wise and State-wise details of persons obtaining employment through Exchanges in the country during the last five years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT (SHRI RAMESHVAR TELI)(a)	to (e): As per the information received from the State/UT Governments, there are 997 Employment Exchanges functioning including 76 University Employment Information and Guidance Bureau (UEI&GB).The Ministry is implementing National Career Service (NCS) Project since 2015 to provide a variety of employment related services. Under the NCS project, Government is providing grant-in-aid for establishment of Model Career Centres (MCCs) including through upgradation of existing employment exchanges. Model Career Centres have state-of-the-art infrastructure and are envisaged to act as a hub to provide all career related services like career counselling, vocational guidance, organising job fairs/job drives, conducting outreach activities in this regard etc. These Centres can be replicated by the States from their own resources. As of now, Government has approved financial assistance for 200 Model Career Centres, which include 175 Employment Exchanges. The State/UT-wise details are at Annexure-I.Beside above, Government is also providing grant-in-aid to the State Governments under the Interlinking component of NCS project for organizing job fairs, up-gradation of Information Technology and refurbishment of the existing employment exchanges. The State/UT-wise detail of number of placements made through employment exchanges is placed at Annexure-II.
