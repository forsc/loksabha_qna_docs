                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2137






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Duration of Graduation Programmes




Sumalatha Ambareesh













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to extend duration of various graduation programmes/ courses under the New National Education Policy (NEP) 2020;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has set up NEP Implementation Committee to formulate the guidelines in this regard;(d) if so, whether the Government has taken the views of various sections of the education sector to arrive at a conclusion in this regard; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b): As per National Education Policy 2020, the undergraduate degree will be of either 3 or 4-year duration, with multiple exit options within this period, with appropriate certifications- a certificate after completing 1 year in a discipline or field including vocational and professional areas, or a diploma after 2 years of study, or a Bachelor’s degree after a 3-year programme. The 4-year multidisciplinary Bachelor''s programme shall be the preferred option since it allows the opportunity to experience the full range of holistic and multidisciplinary education in addition to a focus on the chosen major and minors as per the choices of the student. The students will have option to choose either 3 year or 4 year degree courses without any compulsion.(c): Department of Higher Education has constituted an Implementation Committee, consisting the representatives of concerned Ministries/Departments, to ensure timely and proper implementation of each aspect of National Education Policy 2020 in respect of Higher Education at the Central level to achieve the goals of the policy in a clear and phased manner.(d) and (e): The National Education Policy 2020 has been finalised after a highly participative, inclusive and multi-pronged consultation process, through online, grassroots and thematic expert consultations, involving State/UT Governments, Government of India Ministries, Members of Parliament, public etc.*******
