                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

593






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Har Ghar Jal Yojana




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania













                                                    Balak Nath
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the “HAR GHAR JAL” Yojana;(b)	the details of various schemes being implemented by the Government to provide clean and pure water to the people, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government is making any provision to provide clean water to the people of Assam who are presently helpless to consume saline water;(d)	the details of steps taken by the Government to alleviate the problems faced by the people living in lower regions of our neighbouring country Bhutan; and(e)	the details of the number of villages provided water in the Assam’s BTR as well as the time by which all the villages are likely to be provided water under this scheme along with the details of all villages thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) &(b) Since August, 2019 to enable every rural household in the country to have assured potable water supply through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024, Government of India, in partnership with States is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) –Har Ghar Jal.(c) As reported by the State Government, Assam is not affected by salinity.(d) This Department does not maintain such information about people living in neighbouring countries. (e) As reported by the State Government, every household in only 1 (one) village and 92 habitations of Assam’s BTR started getting potable water supply through tap water connection. As reported by the State Government, all householdsinAssam BTR are likely to be provided with 100% tap water connections by March, 2024. ***
