                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1332






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Handloom Clusters




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number and details of the existing handloom clusters developed by the Government over the last four years in the country especially in Bihar along with the incentives/benefits extended to the people employed therein;(b)	whether there is any new proposal to set up handloom clusters in Bihar; (c)	if so, the details thereof including the amount allocated for the purpose and the places identified therein; and(d)	if not, whether the Government is considering to set up additional handloom clusters in Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	The Government of India, Ministry of Textiles has sanctioned 412 Block Level handloom clusters across the country including Bihar under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP)/ Comprehensive Handloom Development Scheme (CHCDS) covering 2,96,809 beneficiaries and released Rs.215.63 crore during the last four years. Under the Scheme benefits such as Support for Product diversification, Design innovation, Worksheds, Common Facility Centre(CFCs),Technology Upgradation, Skill upgradation and lighting units are provided.(b) to (d):	On receipt of viable proposal from State Government as per the extant guidelines, the same are considered by Government of India.*****
