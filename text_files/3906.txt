                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3431






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Dispensary for Labourers




Mukesh Rajput














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government proposes to open CTI in unserviced areas;(b)if so, the details thereof; (c)whether the Government proposes to open any dispensary for labourers in the extremely backward area of Farrukhabad in Uttar Pradesh; and(d)if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	As informed by Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship,  there is no proposal to open Central Training Institute (CTI) in unserviced areas. However, there are 30 central institutes i.e. National Skill Training Institutes(NSTIs) that offer training to instructors of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) under Crafts Instructor Training Scheme (CITS). (c) & (d):	At present, one ESI Dispensary at Farrukhabad, UP is functioning and there is no fresh proposal from State Govt. of UP for setting up of any other ESI dispensary at Farrukhabad. Further, as per ESIC norms, proposal for setting up of ESI dispensary in an area is considered based on the number of Insured Persons in that area, instead of backwardness of the area.                                             ******
