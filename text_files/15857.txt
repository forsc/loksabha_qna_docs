                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

406






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Travel Grant to Indian Artists




Nishikant  Dubey













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched any scheme for providing travel grant to Indian artists for holding events abroad to promote Indian culture;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of funds allocated for the purpose during each of the last two years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (I/C)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred question No. 406 for 23.03.2020 regarding Travel Grant to Indian ArtistsMinistry of Culture had launched the scheme for ‘Promoting International Cultural Relations’ in 2014 during the 12thFive Year Plan period with the objective of disseminating and creating awareness about various forms of Indian culture abroad.  However, no grant was released under the scheme as none of the applications received for the purpose during the period wasfulfilling the criteria prescribed in the scheme. The scheme was proposed to continue beyond 12th plan period (i.e. beyond 31st March, 2017) with modification and renamed as ‘Travel Grant for promotion of Indian Culture’.However, final decision on continuation of the scheme has not been taken.The details of token budgetaryprovisions made during last two years and the current year are as under: Financial Year				Amount (Rs. in lakh)	2017-18					      50.00	2018-19					      100.00 	2019-20					       50.00	****
