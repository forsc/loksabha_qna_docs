                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

970






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Implementation of Food Processing Plants and Food Parks




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of foods processing plants and food parks that have been established in the Kishanganjand Purnia districts of Bihar so far; and(b)	the schemes that have been launched under the Ministry of Food Processing Units for Bihar and Kishanganj Lok Sabha constituency in particular?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):       Each of the component schemes of the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojna (PMKSY) is a demand driven scheme amongst individuals, entrepreneurs, firms, FPOs, PSUs, etc. Expression of Interest (EoI) are floated from time to time for inviting applications for project proposals as per scheme guidelines through the on-line portal. The selection is made based on scrutiny of proposals on eligibility and assessment criteria prescribed in scheme guidelines. Ministry provides credit linked financial assistance in the form of grants-in-aid to successful applicants for setting up of food processing/preservation infrastructure in accordance with the guidelines.  A total of 11 projects have been approved for Bihar, out of which one is located in the district of Kishanganj.  The details of these 11 projects are as under:Mega Food Parks	Cold Chain	Agro Processing Cluster (APC)	Creation & Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacity (CEFPPC)Two (02)-Pristine Mega Food Park Pvt. Ltd., Khagaria-Bihar Industrial Area Development Authority, Muzaffarpur	Seven (07)-Ganga Dairy Ltd., Begusarai-Mahua Cooperative Cold Storage Limited, Vaishali-RK Agri Biz LLP, Muzaffarpur-Shalimar Cold Storage, Patna-ABZ Agro Pvt Ltd, Kishanganj-Saran Dairy Producer Company Limited, Gopalganj-Maniyarpur Dairy, Patna	One (01)-Shreekangan Food Park, Begusarai	One (01)-Natraja Nutrifeed Pvt Ltd, Khagaria (b):    The schemes of Ministry are implemented across the country and are not region or state specific. The benefits of the schemes of the Ministry including PMKSY and Pradhan Mantri Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) may be availed by the applicants from each district of Bihar including Kishanganj. *****
