                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3391






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Creches for Working Women




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava













                                                    Chhatar Singh Darbar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether adequate number of creches have been set up across the country for the children of working mother especially in rural areas;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UTs wise;(c)	whether the Government proposes to create more such creches across the country; and(d)	if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)***** (a)	to (d):  Ministry of Women and Child Development is implementing the National Crèche Scheme as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme through States/UTs with effect from 01.01.2017 to provide day care facilities to children (age group of 6 months to 6 years) of working mothers.  As per National Crèche Scheme guidelines, the concerned States/UTs undertake an analysis of the requirement of crèche services at the district level based on a survey and proper mapping of the existing crèches so as to assess the demand of crèches in the State/UT. A baseline survey is conducted by State Government in the month of March every year to ascertain the requirement/willingness of children to attend the crèches. Also, the Implementing Agencies conduct field surveys for assessment of needs of community, identifying location of the crèches and also eligible children.As on 11.03.2020, 6453 creches are functional across the country. The States/UT wise number of creches setup and functional across the country is at Annexure I. Setting up of creches is an ongoing process. The Ministry approves the proposal for setting up of creches as and when application for opening a crèche is recommended by the concerned State/UT as per guidelines of the National Creche Scheme.************** Annexure-IAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3391 for 13.03.2020 raised by Shri Mansukhbahi DhanjiBhai Vasava, Shri Chhatar Singh Darbar regarding “National Creche Scheme”  S.No.	State/UTs	No. of Functional Creches1.
