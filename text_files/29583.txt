                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1165






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Central University in Jharkhand




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of foreign subjects which are taught in Central University in Jharkhand;(b) the details of the subjects related to skill development which are taught in the Central University in Jharkhand;(c) the details of the number of subjects on which research facility is available in the Central University in Jharkhand and the total number of students enrolled therein;(d) whether the Government has any plan to expand the Central University in Jharkhand in future; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) As informed by Central University of Jharkhand, the foreign subjects which are taught in the University are Korean, Chinese and Tibetan language courses.(b) Central University of Jharkhand is offering M.Tech and Ph.D programme incorporating skill development courses in 05 (five) Engineering disciplines namely 1. Nanoscience and Technology 2. Energy Engineering 3. Water Engineering and Management 4. Transport Science and Technology and 5. Computer Science and Technology.(c) There are 23 subjects on which research facility is available and 118 Ph.D Students are enrolled in Academic year 2020-21.(d) & (e): No such proposal is under consideration at present.*****
