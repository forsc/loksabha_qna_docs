                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

489






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Naxal Attacks




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Suresh Kodikunnil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the country has witnessed a spurt in naxal attacks during recent times;(b) if so, the details of the instances of naxal and left wing extremist attacks and loss of lives in various parts of the country between 2015 and 2019, State-wise and year-wise;(c) the details of steps taken by the Government to curb naxal presence in the country;(d) whether the Government has in place a framework to regulate propagation of left wing and naxal ideology and propaganda in the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	No, Sir. The LWE scenario in the country has been consistently improving over the years both in terms of violence and geographical spread. The last five years has seen a significant improvement in the LWE scenario across the country. The number of violent incidents and resultant deaths have reduced by 43.4% and 60.4% respectively during last 05 years (2014-18) compared to the preceding 05 years(2009-13). It supplements the efforts of the State Governments with a wide range of measures, including security, development and ensuring rights & entitlements of local communities. Steadfast implementation of the Action Plan-2015 has resulted in consistent decline in both LWE violence and geographical spread of LWE influence.(d) & (e)	Legal provisions of Indian Penal Code, 1861 and Unlawful Activities and Prevention Act (UAPA), 1967 are available for dealing with the left wing extremism. The Communist Party of India (Maoist), its affiliates and front organizations are banned under UAPA, 1967.*****
