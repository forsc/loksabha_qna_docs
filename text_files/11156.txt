                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

58






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Auction of Oil Fields in AP




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether 16 oil fields in and around Rajahmundry in Andhra Pradesh (AP) are going to be auctioned by ONGC;(b) if so, the details of fields that are going to be auctioned and the anticipated output from these fields;(c) the time by which the entire bid process is going to be completed and fields become operational; and(d) the details of benefits likely to be accrued particularly to the city of Rajahmundry in particular and the district in general?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): ONGC has invited offers to bid out sixty four operational fields under nomination regime and interested agencies have been requested to quote incremental oil/gas, which could be produced from these fields based on their assessment, above the base line production profile under Business-As-Usual (BAU) scenario. Out of these, sixteen fields are from Rajahmundry Asset in Andhra Pradesh. The names of sixteen fields are annexed.(c): The technical bids were opened on 17th January, 2020. The successful bidders have mobilization period of 210 days from date of award of contract to start their operation.(d): The increased production leads to increased revenues to the State and benefits to the local economy.****
