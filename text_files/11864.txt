                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

828






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






SFURTI Yojana




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of new provisions of the Government for Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) Yojana; (b) 	the manner in which it is beneficial for Khadi, honey and bamboo industries;(c) 	whether the Government intends to include more commodities under it by expanding its base and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the number of rural people estimated to be benefited by it including the details of funds provisions made in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a): 	The details of new provisions of the Government for Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) Yojana is placed at Annexure-I.(b):	A statement showing the benefits to khadi, honey and bamboo industries is placed at Annexure-II.(c):	At present, no such proposal is under consideration of the Government.(d):	On an average 500 artisans/workers are expected to be benefited in one cluster set up under SFURTI Scheme.  A provision of Rs.125 crore was made under SFURTI for the year 2019-20 (BE) and which has been increased to Rs.185 crore at RE stage.
