                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2894






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






National Textiles Corporation




T.N. Prathapan













                                                    Aparupa  Poddar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of textile mills operational under National Textile Corporation Limited (NTC) across the country, State-wise along with the number of employees employed in these mills; (b) whether the Government had closed/shut down such textile mills under NTC during COVID-19 lockdown, if so, the details thereof including the number of closed mills that have been revived/made operational; (c) the number of mills that have remained closed, State-wise especially in Kerala; and (d) the steps taken by the Government to reopen these mills?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a):	There are 23 operating mills under National Textile Corporation (NTC) Limited.  State-wise details along with number of employees in such mills are as under:-S. No	State	Name of the Mill	No of Employees1	 Andhra Pradesh	Tirupathi Cotton Mills, Renigunta	1882	 Gujarat	Rajnagar Mills, Ahmedabad	4543	 Karnataka	New  Minerva Mils, Hassan	3884	 Kerala	Algappa Textile Mills, Alagappanagar	3005	 Kerala	Cannanore Spg.& Wvg.Mills, Cannanore	5106	 Kerala	Kerala Laxmi Mills, Trichur	2987	 Kerala	Vijayamohini Mills, Trivandrum	2988	 Madhya Pradesh	Burhanpur Tapti Mills, Burhanpur	8719	 Madhya Pradesh	New Bhopal Textile Mills, Bhopal	83510	 Maharashtra	Podar Mills, Mumbai	23811	 Maharashtra	Tata Mills, Mumbai	61512	 Maharashtra	India United M Ill No.5, Mumbai	49513	 Maharashtra	Barshi Textile Mills, Barshi	24714	 Maharashtra	Finlay Mills, Achalpur	83515	 Mahe	Cannanore Spg.& Wvg.Mills, Mahe	39316	 Tamil Nadu	Pioneer Spinners Mills, Kamudakudi	44517	 Tamil Nadu	Kaleeswarar Mills ''B'' Unit, Kalayarkoil	44818	 Tamil Nadu	Cambodia Mills, Coimbatore	31719	 Tamil Nadu	Coimbatore Murugan Mills, Coimbatore	22720	 Tamil Nadu	Pankaja Mills, Coimbatore	27221	 Tamil Nadu	Sri Rangavilas S.& W. Mills, Coimbatore	42222	 Tamil Nadu	Coimbatore Spg & Wvg Mills, Coimbatore	14423	 West Bengal	Arati Cotton Mills, Dass Nagar	448Total	9688 (b):	Manufacturing activities in the 23 working mills of NTC were put under suspension pursuant to the imposition of nation-wide lockdown as a measure to avoid spread of COVID-19 pandemic.  State-wise details of mills made operational so far are :-S. No	State	Name of the Mill1	Tamil Nadu	Sri Rangavilas Mill, Coimbatore2	Tamil Nadu	Kaleshwarer B Mill, Kalayarkoil3	Tamil Nadu	Pioneer Mill (Unit-2), Paramakudi4	Kerala	Cannanore Spg & Wvg Mills, Cannanore5	Madhya Pardesh	Burhanpur Tapti Mill (Synthetic Unit), Burhanpur6	Maharashtra	New Finlay Mill (Spinning Unit), Achalpur, Amaravati7	Maharashtra	Tata Mills, Mumbai8	West Bengal	Arati Cotton Mills, Dass Nagar (c) & (d):        Manufacturing operations in 15 mills including 3 out of 4 mills in Kerala continue to be under suspension on account of poor market demand and financial constraints of the Corporation. NTC mills selected for re-opening are those which are expected to generate substantial positive cash flow because of their inherent capabilities.***
