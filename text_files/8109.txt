                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Releasing of Funds under AMRUT Scheme




Pashupati Nath Singh













                                                    Annpurna Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the funds released under AMRUT scheme to Jharkhand during the last three years;(b) 	the funds utilised out of the funds released;(c) 	the funds allocated and utilised in Jharkhand, sector-wise and city-wise; and(d) 	the number of people benefitted from the above scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	 & (b)	Central Assistance released for implementation of projects under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) scheme to Jharkhand during last three years and funds utilized out of funds released is given in the table below:                                                     (Amount in rupees in crore)Description	    F.Y.2016-17	      F.Y.2017-18	        F.Y.2018-19Central Assistancereleased	   33.16	      52.48	                84.00Utilisation Certificatesreceived	   33.16	      52.48             	26.71(c)	Under AMRUT, Central Assistance is not allocated /released sector-wise or city-wise.  Central Assistance is released against the approved State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs).  For the State of Jharkhand, the Ministry has approved SAAPs amounting to Rs.1,245.74 crore for the entire Mission period, which includes committed Central Assistance of Rs.566.17 crore.  As on date, Central Assistance amounting to Rs.332.45 crore has been released to Government of Jharkhand for AMRUT projects, out of which State Government has submitted Utilization Certificates for Rs.139.94 crore.(d)	Seven cities of Jharkhand have been selected under AMRUT covering a population of 30 lakh as per census of India, 2011.  However, details of beneficiaries under AMRUT is not maintained in the Ministry.                                *****
