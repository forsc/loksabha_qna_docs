                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1363






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Schools and IITs in Rajasthan




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to startnew Kendriya Vidyalayas andNavodaya Vidyalayas in the State of Rajasthan and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to set up new IITs in the State of Rajasthankeeping in view the fact that many students from different parts of the countrycome to Kota, Rajasthan for preparing for the IIT entrance examination; and(c) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are opened primarily to cater to the educationalneeds of the wards of transferable Central Government Employees includingDefence personnel by providing a common programme of education. Proposals foropening of new KVs are considered only if sponsored by Ministries or Departmentsof the Government of India/State Governments / Union Territories Administrationsthereby committing resources for setting up a new KV as well as the availability ofnecessary sanction of the Government. The proposals received from varioussponsoring authorities for opening of new KVs also have to compete among otherproposals under the “Challenge Method”.Navodaya Vidyalaya Scheme envisages opening of one Jawahar NavodayaVidyalayas (JNVs) in each district of the country. All the districts of the country (ason 31.5.2014) including the districts of Rajasthan have been covered under theScheme. Two additional JNVs have also been sanctioned at Banswara andSriganganagar Districts of Rajasthan.A new Kendriya Vidyalaya was sanctioned in March, 2019 at PratapgarhDistrict in Rajasthan. Administrative orders for opening of this Vidyalaya can beissued by Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) only after the State Government ofRajasthan transfer the requisite extant of land in favour of KVS and hands over thepossession of temporary building for commencement of the Vidyalaya.(b) & (c) One IIT is already functioning at Jodhpur in the State of Rajasthan. Atpresent, there is no proposal for establishment of a new IIT in the State.*****
