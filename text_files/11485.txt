                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

309






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Beneficiaries of Dairy Internship Scheme




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	the total number of people belonging to extremely backward category benefited under dairy internship Scheme; b.	whether any type of subsidy is being provided to the beneficiaries under this Scheme by the Government; c.	if so, the details thereof; and d.	the details of the beneficiaries receiving subsidy in Uttar Pradesh, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) to (c) Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is not implementing any Scheme titled dairy internship Scheme. However, this Department is implementing Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS) through National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) in which back ended subsidy (25% of the project cost for General category and 33.33% for ScheduledCaste (SC)/Scheduled Tribe (ST) beneficiaries) is provided benefitting all dairy farmers including SC and ST category beneficiaries. Altogether 65782 SC/ST beneficiaries have been assisted since 2010 (d)	The details of the district-wise beneficiaries receiving subsidy in Uttar Pradesh is at Annexure.*****
