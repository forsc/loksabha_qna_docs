                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1696






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Air Connectivity to Nilgiris




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to establish direct air connectivity service to Nilgiris under UDAN Scheme;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of the efforts made by the Government to connect direct air services to tourist places across the country, including Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Upto Phase II of 4th round of bidding, the implementing agency, Airports Authority of India (AAI) has awarded 39 UDAN routes in Tamil Nadu. Out of these, 12 routes have been operationalized by the selected Airline Operators. Details of the routes awarded under UDAN and status thereof are attached as Annexure-I. The implementing agency, AAI, subsequently cancelled 19 RCS routes due to non-compliance with the  Agreement (SAOA) by the Selected Airline Operator(s). The UDAN scheme launched by the Ministry of Civil Aviation in 2016 is a market driven scheme. The interested airlines based on their assessment of demand on particular routes submit their proposals at the time of bidding under UDAN from time to time. Details of the Tourism RCS routes awarded by the Airport Authority of India, the Implementing agency and details of the operationalization of Tourism RCS routes are attached as Annexure-II and Annexure-III respectively.
