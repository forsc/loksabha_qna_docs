                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Exploitation of Labourers




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government is aware that under Misrikh Parliamentary Constituency of Uttar Pradesh, labourers are mentally exploited and harassed by the operators of many companies situated in Sandeela Industrial Area in gross violation of Labour Laws; (b)if so, the details thereof; (c)whether the Government has received complaints from the Public -Representatives in this regard; (d)if so, the details thereof; and(e)the details of the action taken or proposed to be taken in this regard till date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	Yes, Sir.(b):	The Government of Uttar Pradesh, being the appropriate government of the subject matter, informed that 31 online complaints regarding mental exploitation and harassment of labours in Misrikh Parliamentary Constituency in the Financial Year 2020-2021 were received by the state Government. Detailed report received from the Government of Uttar Pradesh is at Annexure “A”(c):	Yes, Sir. (d):	This Ministry has received your letter dated 13.8.2020 informing about exploitation and harassment being faced by workers of Saf Yeast Company Pvt. Ltd. and questioning this Ministry about actions taken on the issues mentioned.(e):	The matter was examined and referred to concerned authorities for necessary action and reports were also called for. In turn, the Government of Uttar Pradesh, which is the appropriate Government, sent action taken report as mentioned above.*                                                                 ******
