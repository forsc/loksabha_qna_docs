                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2966






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Cooperative Sugar Mills




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the State-wise details of cooperative sugar mills in India along with the number of farmers benefited by these mills; (b) 	the sugar statistics for the last two sessions of sugarcane, particularly for the State of Uttar Pradesh; (c) 	whether the ethanol production from the sugar factories has increased; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	the details of brown sugar produced by both the cooperative and private sugar mills; and (f) 	the units of electricity produced by the sugar mills annually along with the revenue generated through electricity production?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):   The state-wise number of cooperative sugar mills operated during 2019-20 and 2020-21 sugar season is given at Annexure. About 40 lakh sugarcane farmers associated with these co-operative sugar mills were benefitted.......2/--2-(b):    The data on cane produced, cane crushed by the sugar mills and sugar produced during last two sugar seasons 2019-20 and 2020-21 in the country and particularly in the state of Uttar Pradesh is as under:Particulars	All India	Uttar PradeshSugar Season	2019-20	2020-21	2019-20	2020-21Area under cane crop(In Lakh Hect)	51.18	54.76	26.79	27.54Cane Produced (In Lakh MT)	3968	4376	2156	2203Cane Crushed (In Lakh MT)	2523	2989	1132	1027.5Net Sugar Produced (In Lakh MT)	273.85	310.00	126.37	110.59Further, in Uttar Pradesh, 24 co-operative sugar mills having daily cane crushing capacity of 66,625 Tonnes operated during sugar season 2020-21 and 4,68,751 sugarcane farmers were benefitted.(c) & (d): 	Yes, Sir. Production of ethanol from sugarcane based feed-stocks and its supply to Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) under Ethanol Blended with Petrol (EBP) Programme has increased. During Ethanol Supply Year (ESY) 2019-20, the total ethanol produced and supplied under EBP was 173 crore litres whereas during ESY 2020-21, 302 crore litres of Ethanol has been produced and supplied to OMCs. More than 86% of ethanol has been produced from sugarcane based feed-stocks by distilleries attached with sugar mills as well as molasses based standalone distilleries.(e): 	Many sugar mills produced brown sugar but data of quantity of brown sugar produced is not maintained.(f): 	Sugar mills are generating electricity mainly for their captive use and also exporting surplus power to grid. Data on electricity generated by sugar mills and the corresponding revenue generated through electricity production is not maintained by the Department.  **** ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2966 FOR ANSWER ON 15.12.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.List of operated cooperative mills during 2019-20 and 2020-21 Sugar SeasonState	2019-20	2020-21Punjab	9	9Haryana	11	11Uttarakhand	2	2Uttar Pradesh	24	24Madhya Pradesh	2	2Chattisgarh	4	4Gujarat	13	13Maharashtra	79	95Odisha	1	1Andhra Pradesh	4	3Karnataka	13	14Tamilnadu	11	12All India	173	190*****
