                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

694






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Chennai Metro Rail Project




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan to expedite the Chennai metro rail project phase-II, corridor 3 and corridor 4; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the amount allocated, disbursed and utilized so far;(c)	whether the Government has allowed Chennai Metro Rail Limited (CMRL) to mobilize funds from private players or foreign financial institutions; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the expected time of completion of the two corridors?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Chennai Metro Rail Project Phase-II is at present being implemented as a State Sector Project. (b)	Does not arise.(c)	The proposal for Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) loan for ? 20,196 crore (equivalent to 357,476 million Japanese Yen) for the project as a State Sector Project was supported by the Ministry. Loan agreement for Tranche I for the project was signed on 21.12.2018 with JICA for 75.519 billion yen. Also, different components of the project, have been posed to Asian Development Bank (ADB), Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB), New Development Bank (NDB) and World Bank for external assistance as a State Sector Project.(d)	As per Detailed Project Report, the two corridors are expected to be completed in seven years from the commencement of work, by 2025-26.                                              *******
