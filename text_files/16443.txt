                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






National Career Service




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of aspirants registered under National Career Service (NCS) in the country at present with particular reference to Maharashtra and Tripura;(b)	the total number of job-seekers that have been employed through the NCS portal during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(c)	the total number of persons who have secured jobs through the portal in the public sector and private sector;(d)	the total number of persons who have secured jobs through the Job Fairs organized by the Ministry, State/UT-wise;(e)	whether the Ministry has signed MoU with TCS iON to offer free online career skills training through NCS; and(f)	if so, the details thereof along with number of active beneficiaries, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR & EMPLOYMENT (SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c) At present, the total number of active jobseekers registered on National Career Service Portal is 1.03 crore, of which 13.34 lakh job-seekers are from Maharashtra and 1.99 lakh  job-seekers are from Tripura. Notifying final hiring figures is not a mandatory requirement on NCS portal. However, a total of 77.56 lakh vacancies have been mobilized on NCS portal till 9th September, 2020 including the vacancies of public and private sector.(d) State/UT-wise number of persons who have secured jobs through job fairs organized by the Ministry are annexed (Annexure-I).(e) and (f) The Ministry has signed an Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with TCS iON with the objective to provide career skills training to jobseekers free of cost. State-wise list of beneficiaries are annexed (Annexure-II). 	******
