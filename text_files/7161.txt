                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

700






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Approval for New NH




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether in-principle approval has been given for the construction of a National Highway from Bharatpur’s Pahadi to Vayanagar, Kathumar, Mahua, Hindon to Mandrayal; and (b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	and (b) Yes Sir. The Ministry has accorded ‘in-principle’ approval to this State Road to be declared as new National Highway depending upon the outcome of Detailed Project Report (DPR). However, the Government is in the process of framing revised guidelines for declaration of State Roads as National Highways, which will be used for declaring such State Roads based on the inter-se priority, traffic density and availability of funds. *****
