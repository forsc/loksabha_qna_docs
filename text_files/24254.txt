                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

204






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Increase in GST Collection




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the collection of Goods and Services Tax (GST) has increased in the country for the months of March, April and May, 2021 as compared to the year 2020; and(b) if so, the collection of GST for the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) & (b): Yes Sir. The gross Goods and Services Tax (GST) collection has increased in the country for the months of March, April and May, 2021 as compared to the year 2020. The details of gross Goods and Services Tax (GST) collection for the months of March, April and May in the year 2020 and 2021 are as under:Gross GST Collection (in Rs. Crore)March 2021	April 2021	May 20211,23,902	1,39,708	1,02,709 March 2020	April 2020	May 202097,590	32,172	62,151*****
