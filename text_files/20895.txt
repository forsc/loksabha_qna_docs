                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2290






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Setting up of Central University and Strengthening of Schools




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up a Central University in Leh for access to 	higher education in Ladakh;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	 whether the Government has proposed to qualitatively strengthen around 15,000 	schools in the country to include all components of the National Education Policy;(d)	 if so, the details and the features thereof; and(e) 	the other steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’)(a) to (e) Hon’ble Prime Minister in his address to the nation on 15th August 2020 announced several major initiatives including setting up a Central university in the Union Territory of Ladakh. This proposal was also included in the Union Budget 2021-22. This budget also mentions strengthening of 15000 schools to include all components of the National Education Policy with an objective to provide targeted and inclusive intervention in the schools to help them emerge as exemplar schools in their regions to achieve the ideals of the Policy.*****
