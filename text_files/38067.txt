                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4670






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Investment in Road Infrastructure Projects




 Chinta Anuradha














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to allow common citizens to invest in road infrastructure projects in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government is likely to follow Real Estate Investment Trust and Infrastructure Investment Trust models to encourage common public investment into the road infrastructure projects; and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)  Government has authorized National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) to set up Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT) and monetize completed and operational National Highway projects.        The monetization of projects under InvIT Bundle-1 was successfully achieved and the concession fee of Rs. 7,350.40 Crore (including debt of Rs. 2,000 Crore availed by concessionaire) was received by NHAI. Accordingly, the Appointed Date was declared with effect from 16.12.2021.*****
