                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Urban Development and Poverty AlleviationProgrammes




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of names of various programmes evolved for urban development and urban poverty alleviation till date;(b)	the details of funds provision made, allocated/ utilised during each year of the past three years including current year particularly for poverty alleviation; and(c) 	the achievements made so far and the present status of these programmes?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a):	For urban development and urban poverty alleviation, programmes under implementation by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, include Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) (PMAY-U), Smart Cities Mission (SCM), Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) and Swachh Bharat Mission- Urban (SBM-U).(b)	Under DAY-NULM, the funds to the tune of Rs. 556.7 crore were released in 2017-18, Rs. 439.84 crore in 2018-19 and Rs. 647.78 crore in 2019-20 (so far). Further, the amounts utilised include Rs. 555.45 crore in 2017-18, Rs. 798.33 crore in 2018-19 and Rs. 617.32 crore in 2019-20 (so far). Utilization figures include the unspent balance of the previous years. (c)	Under DAY-NULM, 4.5 lakh Self Help Groups have been formed; 10.6 lakh persons skill trained; 5 lakh beneficiaries assisted for setting up Individual/ Group micro enterprises; 15.5 lakh street vendors identified; and 1,367 shelters for urban homeless established.  Under PMAY(U), 1.04 crore houses have been sanctioned, 62.3 lakh houses grounded for construction and 32.3 lakh houses complete/ delivered, so far.  Under SCM, so far, an amount of Rs. 19,104 crore has been released to States/UTs out of which Rs. 11,181.61 crore has been utilised by the Smart Cities Mission.  Under AMRUT, so far, contracts have been awarded for 5,384 projects worth ?73,641 crore including 2,529 completed projects worth ?9,025 crore.  SBM has achieved the target of creating urban India Open Defecation Free. A total of 61.4 lakhs individual Household Toilets and 5.82 lakhs seats of community/ public toilets have been constructed. Till date, 1304 cities have been certified ODF+, and 481 have been certified ODF++.                                                  *****
