                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2526






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Construction of National Highways




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received proposals from various State Governments for construction of National Highways during the last three years and the current year; (b) if so, the details thereof indicating approved and pending proposals, State-wise; (c) the details of targets fixed by the Government for construction of National Highways during the said period, State-wise; (d) the total length of roads constructed/proposed to be constructed during 2019-20; and (e) the funds earmarked/released/utilised for the purpose during the said period, State-wise?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Details of proposals approved for execution by State PWDs, State-wise during the last three years and current year up to Dec. 2019 are at Annexure-I.(c) & (d) Target of construction of NHs during the last three years and current year is tabulated below. Further, length of 6940 km of NHs has been constructed in the current Financial Year up to December 2019.Year	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Construction target (km)	15000	15000	10000	11000(e)	The State /UT-wise details of funds allocated/utilized for development of NHs during each of the last three years and current year are at Annexure-II.
