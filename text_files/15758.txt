                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4151






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Damanganga River under NRCP




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any proposal from Daman & Diu Administration for conservation of Damanganga river under National River Conservation Plan (NRCP) to ambit pollution from this river in coordination with State Government of Gujarat and if so, the details thereof; and (b) the time by which the Union Government in coordination with State Government of Gujarat intends to complete this project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b)  Project proposals are received from the State Governments from time to time for taking up pollution abatement works in towns along various rivers, and are considered for financial assistance under the National River Conservation Plan (NRCP) subject to their conformity with the scheme guidelines, pollution status, prioritization, appraisal by independent institutions and availability of Plan funds. With regard to pollution abatement of river Damanganga, proposal has not been received either from Governments of Daman & Diu or Gujarat.*****
