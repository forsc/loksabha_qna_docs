                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2594






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Decline in Gold Demand




Mimi Chakraborty














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether gold demand in the second half of 2019-20 Financial Year (FY) has crashed over 38 percent in the country;(b)	whether economic slowdown is the main reason behind the sales drop of over 35 percent over the second half of FY 2018-19; and(c)	if so, the details of the steps taken/ to be taken by the Government to revive the gold demand and sales?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	to  (c) 	There is no mechanism to measure the demand and sale of gold in domestic market.****
