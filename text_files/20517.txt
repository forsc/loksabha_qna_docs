                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1937






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






PG Medical Education




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that the Indian students who pursued PG Medical Education in various Universities in Nepal in the year 2014 are yet to be awarded their degrees;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor, University-wise and their department-wise;(c) whether any representations have been received to facilitate issuance of their degrees, if so, the details thereof;(d) the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard; and(e) the time by which the issue of issuance of PG degrees is likely to be resolved?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e): The National Medical Commission (NMC) informed that the concerned foreign University awards degree to the candidates enrolled by it and such matters do not come under the purview of the Commission. The NMC also informed that no such representation has been received by them in this regard.
