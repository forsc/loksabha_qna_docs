                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3166






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Upliftment and Development of SCs in Jharkhand




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †3166TO BE ANSWERED ON 22.03.2022UPLIFTMENT AND DEVELOPMENT OF SCS IN JHARKHAND †3166. SHRIMATI GEETA KORA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the total funds provided by the Government for development of Scheduled Castes(SCs) of Jharkhand during the last two years, head-wise; (b) the details of the schemes implemented by the Government for upliftment and development of SCs of Jharkhand; (c) whether the Government has constituted any working group for smooth implementation of the said schemes; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a):  The funds under the Development Action Plan for Scheduled Caste(DAPSC) is not provided to the State head-wise. However, the funds that have been provided to the State of Jharkhand during the last two completed years under various schemes being implemented by different Ministries as available in e-utthan portal of this Ministry is as follows:Year	Funds provided to Jharkhand2019-20	1314.42 Cr.2020-21	906.44 Cr.(b):  The details of the schemes implemented by the Government for upliftment and development of SCs of Jharkhand can be seen in detail in the portal https://e-utthaan.gov.in maintained in this Ministry. However, details of the five best performing schemes being implemented by different Ministries for upliftment and development of SCs of Jharkhand in the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 are as follows:Year: 2019-20 Sl. No.	Ministries/Deptts.	Scheme	Expenditure(Rs. in Cr.)1	 Rural Development	PM Awas Yojana	443.532	School Education & Literacy	Samagra Shiksha	128.503	Health & Family Welfare	National Rural Health Mission	120.044.	Women & Child Development	Anganwadi Services	106.965	Rural Development	National Rural Livelihood Mission	73.73Year: 2020-21 Sl. No.	Ministries/Deptts.	Scheme	Expenditure(Rs. in Cr.)1.	Agriculture Cooperation & Farmers’ Welfare	PM Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)	172.712.	 Rural Development	PM Awas Yojana	130.263.	School Education & Literacy	Samagra Shiksha	120.144.	Rural Development	National Rural Livelihood Mission	114.675.	Coal	Exploitation of Coal & Lignite	40.26(c):  No, Sir.(d):  Does not arise.*****
