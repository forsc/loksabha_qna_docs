                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Command Battle School




Hasnain Masoodi














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the functioning of Command Battle School (CBS) 15th Corps at Khrew, Jammu and Kashmir;(b) the initiatives taken by the Government for employment of local residents at CBS;(c) the post retirement benefits of local employees of CBS; and(d) the steps being taken to give reservation in jobs to the children of employees of CBS?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  Command Battle School (CBS) at Khrew, imparts pre-induction training to the troops posted to the Corps Zone in counter insurgency related operations.(b) to (d):  In CBS employment of permanent nature for civilians does not exist.  However, locals are hired on daily wage basis whenever there is requirement.*****
