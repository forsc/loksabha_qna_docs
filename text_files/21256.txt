                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Dairy Development Scheme




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the State Government of Gujarat has submitted any dairy development scheme related to districts of the State of Gujarat during the last three years; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of action taken by the Government, scheme-wise on the said schemes and the result thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        (ANSWER)THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN) (a) to (c)  The Department has been implementing following dairy development schemes in the State of Gujarat during the last three years. (i) National Programme for Dairy DevelopmentState Government of Gujarat had submitted four projects under National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD) scheme during the last three years (2017-18 to 2019-20). All the four projects have been approved by the Department with the total outlay of Rs.10910.94 lakh (Central Share Rs.7007.20 lakh).
