                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3953






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Singareni Coal Mines




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Ministry is aware that Singareni Collieries estimated that it requires 10 crore tonnes of coal by 2025;(b) if so, whether Singareni Collieries has been requesting for allocating its coal mines in Chhattisgarh and Jharkhand;(c) if so, the time when such request has been received in the Ministry and the action taken in this regard;(d) the steps being taken to expand/give blocks for Singareni Coal Mines to improve the production of coal in future;(e) whether the State Government has requested the Union Government in this regard;(f) if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken in this regard in last five years and the current year as on date; and(g) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF COAL, MINES AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) to (g):  Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL) coal production projection for the year 2024-25 is 85 Million Tonne (MT) with the existing reserves in Telangana States and the new coal blocks which have been allocated to SCCL. Requests have been received from time to time from Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL) for allocation of coal mines. The following mines have been allotted to SCCL under the provisions of the Coal Mines (Special Provisions) Act, 2015: S.No.	State	Name of Coal Block	End use/purpose1	Odisha	Naini	Power2	Odisha	New Patrapara	Sale of Coal3	Telangana	Penagaddppa	Sale of Coal  *****
