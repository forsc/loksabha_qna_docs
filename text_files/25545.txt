                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1172






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






PMKSY in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of various Schemes/Projects being implemented under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) and the status of investments committed under the said scheme in respect of the State of Odisha; and(b)	the details of funds allocated and utilised under the said Scheme during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) & (b): The Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has been implementing Central Sector Umbrella Scheme - Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector of the country. The component schemes of PMKSY are (i) Mega Food Park, (ii) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, (iii) Creation /Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities, (iv) Infrastructure for Agro-Processing Clusters, (v) Creation of Backward & Forward linkages, (vi) Food Safety and Quality Assurance Infrastructure, (vii) Human Resource and Institutions, (viii) Operation Greens. Under component schemes of PMKSY, MoFPI provides mostly credit linked financial assistance (capital subsidy) in the form of grants-inaid to entrepreneurs for setting up of food processing / preservation infrastructure as well as food processing units. PMKSY is not region or state specific but demand driven and is implemented across the country including in the state of Odisha. The  status including investment for projects approved under PMKSY in the state of Odisha is placed at Annexure I. The budgetary allocation and expenditure incurred under relevant component schemes of PMKSY during last three financial years is placed at Annexure II.****
