                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2830






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Bengaluru-Mysuru Highway Project




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of the Bengaluru-Mysuru highway construction project;(b)	the time frame set for completion of the project; and(c)	the details of the highway construction projects undertaken in the State of Karnataka during the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) 6 laning of Bengaluru-Mysuru Stretch of NH 275 has been taken up in 2 construction packages namely (i)  Bengaluru-Nidagatta & (ii) Nidagatta-Mysuru  packages. The length of packages, completed length and physical progress are as under: Package	Length of package (in Km)	Length completed (in Km)	Physical Progress	Expected date of completionBengaluru-Nidagatta	56.20	47	80.49%	May, 2022Nidagatta-Mysuru	61.104	29.78	73.70%	June, 2022(c)	The details of highway length constructed in the State of Karnataka during last three years are as under: Year	Length completed (in Km)2018-19	7792019-20	5002020-21	425*****
