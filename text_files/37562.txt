                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4985






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Pradhan Mantri Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission




Jyotsna Charandas Mahant














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of progress of work undertaken and funds spent under Pradhan Mantri Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission (PM-ABHIM) in the country, State/UT-wise including Chhattisgarh; and(b)    the criteria and mechanism for setting up of public health facilities/centres in remote, rural, inaccessible and Tribal areas under PM-ABHIM, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	The PM – Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission   launched recently and its objective is to fill critical gaps in health infrastructure, surveillance and health research – spanning both the urban and rural areas so that the communities are Atmanirbhar in managing pandemic/health crises. State wise financial allocation and physical deliverables during the scheme period i.e FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26 has been conveyed to all the States/UTs including the state of Chhattisgarh. The States/UTs have been oriented for the operational as well as technical aspects of the scheme and for preparation & submission of the annual plans.  The scheme was launched in the 3rd quarter of the FY 2021-22 and funds released to states/UTs including Chhattisgarh as on 28.03.2022 is at annexure. (b)	The criteria and mechanism for setting up of public health facilities/ centres under the scheme including remote, rural, inaccessible and Tribal areas has been detailed in the Operational Guidelines and two Technical Guidelines (Critical Care Hospital Block and District Integrated Public Health laboratory) which are available at https://nhsrcindia.org/pradhan-mantri-aatmanirbhar-swasthya-bharat-pm-asby .
