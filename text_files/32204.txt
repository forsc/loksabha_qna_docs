                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3658






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Advance Technical Centre in Belagavi




Suresh Chanabasappa Angadi














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       whether the proposed or sanctioned Advance Technical Centre (Tec Hub) in Belagavi, Karnataka has not been functional even after the lapse of two years of sanction; and (b)       if so, the present status and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       Under Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, there is no   proposal to established Advance Technical Centre (Tec Hub) in Belagavi, Karnataka. However, two model aspirational skill centre known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) are functional in Belagavi (Belgaum), Karnataka. Further, Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) is implementing a Scheme "Establishment of new Technology Centres/ Extension Centres", to establish 20 new Technology Centres (TCs) and 100 Extension Centres (ECs) across the country, including in Karnataka State, to enhance competitiveness of MSME Sector across the country. (b)       Above reply (a) may be referred to.
