                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

661






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Gramin Dak Sevak Posts




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that nearly 40% of Gramin Dak Sevak posts are lying vacant in Telangana out of the total 8,200 employed; (b) 	if so, the details of vacancies, district-wise; (c) 	the time since when these posts are lying vacant and the steps taken by the Government to fill in all the vacancies?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)		No Sir. Sanctioned strength of all approved categories of Gramin Dak Sevaks (GDS) of Telangana is 9,991. Only 1651GDS posts are lying vacant.The percentage of vacancies is 16.5%.(b)	The details of vacancies, district-wise, is furnished in the Annexure.(c)	The details of the time since when these posts are lying vacant is furnished in Annexure. Engagement of GDS is a continuous process. Out of 1651 vacant posts of GDS, 733 posts have been notified on 27.01.2021.
