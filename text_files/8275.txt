                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Powerloom and Handloom Sector




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the classificatory distinction between powerloom and handloom sector;(b)	whether any changes to this classification have been made over the last ten years, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether there is any possible overlap between these two sectors and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):    The classificatory distinction between Handloom and Powerloom is indicated under Para No. 2(b) and 2(d) of the Handloom (Reservation of Articles for Production) Act, 1985. As per the definition provided under 2(d) of Handlooms Act, 1985, the powerloom is distinctly classified as a loom which is worked by power. Handlooms means any loom other than powerloom as defined under section 2(b) of the Act, 1985. (b):    There has been no change in the above classification over the last ten years.(c):     There is no possible overlap between these two sectors. ***
