                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2122






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Promotion of Maithili language




Dileshwar Kamait














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Maithili language has a history of more than 1500 years and it fulfills the criteria of having the status of classical language; (b) whether the Government releases the funds for promotion of classical languages; and (c)  if so, the institute-wise details of the funds released for promotion of Maithili language during the last five years? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) : Recognising the status of any language as Classical Language falls under the domain of Ministry of Culture.  Ministry of Culture has informed that Sahitya Akademi is mandated  to review the criteria to classify a language as “Classical Language” and also to indicate how many other languages are likely to become eligible to be classified as “Classical Language” under the reviewed guidelines.    (b) :   Yes Sir.  Funds are released to Classical Languages (c) :   Funds are released for promotion of Maithili language through Central Institute of Indian Languages  (CIIL), Mysore under Grant in Aid programme  and an amount of Rs.21,84,706/-  has been released during last five years. ***
