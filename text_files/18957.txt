                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

453






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Extension of routes in Madhya Pradesh




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the surveys conducted for construction of new railway routes and extension of existing routes in Madhya Pradesh, district and route-wise; (b) the names of routes out of the above on which the construction is prioritised or proposed to be prioritised;(c) whether the survey for a new railway route from Katangi to Seoni under Seoni-Balaghat district of Madhya Pradesh has been sanctioned; (d) if so, whether the survey has been completed in this regard; and (e) whether it is proposed to sanction the construction of the said route and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                             MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) Railway projects are not surveyed district wise but zone-wise as Indian Railways’ network spans across various Districts/States boundaries. During the last three years and current financial year, surveys for 19 new railway lines have been completed falling fully/partly in the State of Madhya Pradesh.   Details are appended.(b) Out of above, two new line projects namely Budni-Indore (205 km) and Badi Sadri-Neemuch   (49  km) have been sanctioned.(c) Yes, Sir.(d) No, Sir.  (e) Decision to sanction the project is taken after examination of survey report on merits including its financial viability.*****
