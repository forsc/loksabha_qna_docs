                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4435






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Modern Storage Facilities at Block Levels




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has drawn any action plan to create modern storage facilities at grass root and block levels for essential commodities as these have the potential to increase farmers income; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the Government is laying emphasis on covergence and aggregation of all storage infrastructure in the country; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons tehrefor?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (d): 	Creation of block level storage is Government priority and State Governments are reviewing block level storage infrastructure at their level. Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution has not drawn up any action plan at present. However, Ministry has requested the Directorate of Marketing and Inspection under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare to hold meetings with the state and other stakeholders (like National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD), Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI), National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) etc.) to inform the States of details of various schemes offered by various Ministries/organizations.*****
