                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4747






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Motor Accident Claims




Su Thirunavukkarasar













                                                    Ravneet Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that there has been a huge increase in the number of fake/false motor accident claims in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof along the reasons therefor, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government proposes to set up a new portal and generate unique number to curb filing of fake or multiple motor accident claims in the country;(d) if so, the details thereof and the extent to which the new portal is likely to check such fake/false claims;(e) the time by which it is likely to be introduced;(f) the other measures being taken by the Government to curb such fake/false claims in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	 to (b) No such data is maintained. (c) to (f) The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has issued notification GSR 164 (E) dated 25th February, 2022 to mandate the procedure for detailed investigation of road accidents, the Detailed Accident Report (DAR) and its reporting, along with timelines for different stakeholders, including electronic submission of the details on eDAR portal for quick settlements of claims by the Motor Accident Claim Tribunal (MACT) as well as to check fake/false motor accident claims.******
