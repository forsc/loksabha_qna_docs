                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

686






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Registered MSMEs




Krupal Balaji Tumane













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) registered in the country including Maharashtra, State/UT-wise; (b) 	whether any project of Maharashtra is pending for Government’s approval;(c) 	if so, the details thereof including the reasons therefor;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to introduce any scheme for development of MSME in the States like Maharashtra; and (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	As per Udyog Aadhaar Portal, number of MSMEs registered (since September 2015 to 30.06.2020) in All India was 102,32,468. The State/UT wise details are attached as annexure-I.	On 1st July, 2020, after adoption of new definition of MSMEs, a new registration portal ‘Udyam Registration’ has been launched by M/o MSME and so far 36,70,538 classified MSMEs are registered on the portal in All India (from 01.07.2020 to 15.07.2021). The State/UT wise details are attached as annexure-II.(b) & (c): 	No Sir. There is no project (under MSE – CDP) of Maharashtra which is pending for Government’s approval in this Ministry.(d) & (e):	All Scheme of Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) are available for development of MSMEs of Maharashtra.
