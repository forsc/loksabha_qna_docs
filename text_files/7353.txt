                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1025






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Central Government Hospitals in Assam




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the number of Centrally sponsored hospitals in Assam at present;(b) the number of doctors, nurses and other staff at each hospital;(c) whether any hospital has been established/upgraded during the last five years;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Lokopriyo Gopinath Bordoloi Regional Institute of Mental Health, Tezpur, Assam is a fully central government sponsored hospital located in the State of Assam. The total sanctioned strength of the institute is 394. The details are given as under:	Sanctioned Strength	In position	Vacant positionDoctor	95	59	36Nurse	77	77	0Other staff	222	211	11Total	394	347	47(c) to (e):	Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) is a Central Sector Scheme, being implemented by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare directly through Executing Agencies and Procurement Support Agency. The scheme has two components:(i)	Setting up of new AIIMS; and (ii)	Up-gradation of existing Government Medical Colleges/ Institutions (GMCIs)		Under PMSSY, two Govt. Medical Colleges in Assam i.e. (i) Assam Medical College, Dibrugarh and (ii) Guwahati Medical College, Guwahati have been taken up for upgradation under Phase-III of PMSSY at an approved cost of Rs. 150 crore for each project (Central Share – Rs. 120 crore and State Share – Rs. 30 crore). The upgradation involves construction of Super Speciality Block and procurement of Medical Equipment. Apart from this, an AIIMS has also been approved to be set up in Guwahati at a cost of Rs. 1123 crore.The Lokopriyo Gopinath Bordoloi Regional Institute of Mental Health, Tezpur, Assam has been upgraded during last five years with allocated fund of Rs. 275 crore.
