                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

38






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Hospital Management Information System




Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi













                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways has launched Hospital Management Information System as a trial project; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether this facility is available only for the employees of the Indian Railways and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) : A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO.38 BY DR. BEESETTI VENKATA SATYAVATHI AND SHRI ADALA PRABHAKARA REDDY TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 03.02.2021 REGARDING HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM(a)  & (b): Yes, Sir. A Pilot Project of Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) has been launched on 28.12.2020 inter-linking Northern Railway Hospitals located in Delhi. The Pilot Project is helping in better healthcare management of railway medical beneficiaries. (c) Yes, Sir. The facility is for railway employees and their eligible dependents only. ******
