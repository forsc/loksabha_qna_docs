                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1095






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Private Companies




Kalyan Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether in the name of Covid-19 more than lakhs of private companies have been shut down during the last 6 months;(b) if so, the details of closed units and new companies registered during April 2020 to June 2021, State-wise;(c) the number of unemployment registered and losses incurred reported thereto; and(d) whether the Government proposes to assess the facts and reality in this regard and if so, the details thereof along with the action taken/proposed to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION; MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING; AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                                                     [RAO INDERJIT SINGH](a) and  (b): The  Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) administers the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 (the Act), the Limited Liability Partnership Act, 2008 and the Insolvency  and Bankruptcy Code, 2016.  The term of “shut down” or “closed unit” is not defined under the Act. However, during April 2020 to June 2021 name of total 16527 number of Companies were struck off from the register of Companies pursuant to the provisions of section 248 of the Act. …2/--2-Details of new companies registered during April 2020 to June 2021, State-wise is as under:State/ UT	NUMBER OF COMPANIESAndaman & Nicobar	51Andhra Pradesh	4229Arunachal Pradesh	85Assam	1513Bihar	6797Chandigarh	762Chhattisgarh	1664Daman and Diu	32Delhi	19626Dadra & Nagar Haveli	64Goa	510Gujarat	10192Himachal Pradesh	793Haryana	9484Jharkhand	2436Jammu & Kashmir	1061Karnataka	15178Kerala	7658Lakshadweep	7Ladakh	22Maharashtra	35088Meghalaya	62Manipur	270Madhya Pradesh	5224Mizoram	27Nagaland	75Orissa	3925Punjab	2643Pondicherry	172Rajasthan	6878Telangana	12586Tamil Nadu	12041Tripura	161Uttar Pradesh	19616Uttarakhand	1655West Bengal	8982Total	191569(c) and (d): Does not arise. *****
