                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

496






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






High Costs of RT-PCR Tests




M. P. Abdussamad Samadani














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government has taken note that the passengers are compelled to pay high amount for the RT-PCR test that is conducted at the airports;(b) If so, the reasons for the higher rate, compared to the test done outside the airport;(c) Whether the Government proposes to take necessary steps to make the rate equal to the normal rate which is prevalent outside the airports; and(d) If so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d): The testing at airports is coordinated by the Ministry of Civil Aviation wherein the rates for RT-PCR / Rapid RT-PCR are primarily either as per the rates notified by the respective State/UTs wherein the airports are located or through a tender by the respective airports.The rates of testing at airports have also decreased with time with the wider availability of Rapid RT-PCR/RT-PCR machines and reagents across the country.***
