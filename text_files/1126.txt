                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1025






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Indira Gandhi Feeder




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether capacity of Head Works of the Indira Gandhi Feeder situated at Harike is 15000 cusecs whereas its design capacity is 18500 cusecs and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Union Government has taken cognizance of the fact that capacity of Indira Gandhi Feeder can be used more efficiently by increasing the capacity of Harike Head Works; and (c) if so, whether the Union Government proposes to increase capacity of Head Regulator of Indira Gandhi Feeder from 15000 cusecs to 18500 cusecs or has received any proposal from the State Government of Punjab in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  Yes, Sir. the capacity of Head Works capacity of Indira Gandhi Feeder situated at Harike is 15000 cusecs whereas design capacity of the feeder canal is 18500 cusecs.(b) & (c)  No proposal has been received from Government of  Punjab in this regard.*****
