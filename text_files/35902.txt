                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3176






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Targets under PMAY-G




Bhola Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the measures taken by the Government to achieve the target of constructing 2.95 crore houses under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) by the year 2022; and(b) 	the details of houses sanctioned for Uttar Pradesh thereunder the period 2017-2021, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)        The Union Cabinet has approved the continuation of Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G)  till March, 2024 for completion of remaining houses within cumulative target of 2.95 crore houses. The Ministry has taken up many measures to ensure timely completion of the houses under PMAY-G, main among which are the following initiatives :i.	Regular review of progress at different levels of the Ministry. ii.	Launch of PMAY-G Dashboard for monitoring and supervision of the scheme by the functionaries at various levels.iii.	 Timely allocation of targets to the States/UTs and release of adequate funds at the level of the Ministry. iv.	Regular follow up with the State to ensure release of central and state share of funds and provision of land to landless beneficiaries in rural areas.
