                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4923






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Conversion of Tractors into CNG Fuel Category




Sanjeev Kumar Singari













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved the conversion of tractors into CNG fuel category;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether any study for retrofitting of tractors has been undertaken to understand the benefits of such conversion to the farmers; and(d)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. The Government vide GSR 1151(E) dated 29th November, 2018 had already notified norms for dual fuel use of CNG/Bio-CNG/LNG in agricultural tractors /CEV / power tillers/ combine harvesters. Further, the Government has notified GSR 56(E) dated 29th January, 2021 to amend the Central Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989 to include retro fitment option for agricultural tractors/ CEV/ power tillers / combine harvesters for dedicated CNG/Bio-CNG/LNG operation either by conversion of diesel engines or by replacing the engines.(c) and (d) No such study has been undertaken by this Ministry. *****
