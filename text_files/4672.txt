                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4124






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Status of Sports Authority of India




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the status of Sports Authority of India (SAI) in different States is at par with the international level;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor along with the measures being taken by the Government to improve the status of SAI in various States?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c): The Sports Authority of India (SAI)under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports (MYAS) has 5 world class stadia, 10 Regional Centres and 2 Educational Institutions. These facilities promote excellence in sports, provide international level sports infrastructure, sports science experts and coaches to sports persons in various sports disciplines and are at par with international levels. The locations are at various places across the country and cater to the needs of the States and Union Territories. The details are at Annexure-I.In addition SAI is implementing the following sports promotional schemes in the country:1. National Sports Talent Contest Schemea. Regular Schoolsb. Indigenous Games & Martial Arts (IGMA)c. Akharas2. Army Boys Sports Company (ABSC)3. SAI Training Centre (STC)4. Special Area Games (SAG)5. Extension Centre of STC/ SAG6. Centre of Excellence (COE)7. SAI National Sports Academies (NSAs)The details of the locations of various SAI centres across the country, through which the above schemes are implemented, is at Annexure- II. The Central Government provides yearly budgetary support and funds are released to SAI for the ongoing development/ upgradation of these SAI centres.****
