                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

12






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






FM Channels in the Country




Prataprao  Jadhav













                                                    H. Vasanthakumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of FM radio stations operational in the country, State-wise;(b)	whether the Government has taken any steps/proposes to take any steps to set up FM radio facility in the district headquarters including Buldhana under Buldhana Parliamentary Constituency in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is earning revenue from FM radio stations and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(d)	the average estimated cost of setting up a FM radio station;(e)	whether the Government has sanctioned new FM radio channels for farmers and fisherman and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(f)	the population norms fixed for setting up of new FM channels?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION & BROADCASTING(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a)	to (f) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.                                     *******  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *12 FOR ANSWER ON 21.06.2019(a)	to (f)    Prasar Bharati has informed that as on date AIR has 458 FM broadcasting stations.  State-wise details is given at Annexure-I. A 100 W FM transmitter has also been commissioned by AIR at Buldhana on 15.02.2013. The average cost for setting up of a 10kW FM transmitter with 100-meter tower and multi-purpose studio is about Rs 15 crore. AIR has not set up FM channels exclusively for farmers and fishermen.  However, special programmes have been made for broadcast to farmers and fishermen on FM Channels. AIR has the mandate to provide coverage at every nook & corner of the country. As far as private FM radio is concerned, at present 381 Private FM Radio channels are operational in 107 cities spread across 27 States and 3 UTs in the country.   State-wise details is given at Annexure-II. The revenue earned from Private FM Radio stations, State-wise, is given at Annexure-III. Private FM Radio Phase-III Policy being city centric provides for setting up of FM radio stations in all cities having population above one lakh as per census 2001, unless it is getting covered from an adjacent city.  Further, 11 cities in Border Areas of Jammu & Kashmir and North East States having population less than 1 lakh have also been approved for setting up FM radio stations.
