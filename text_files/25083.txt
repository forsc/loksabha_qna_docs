                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1024






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Relief Package




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has announced any relief package under Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan for citizens and entrepreneurs of the country; and(b)	if so, the district-wise number of citizens and entrepreneurs who have been given assistance under this package in Nanded, Hingoli and Yavatmal districts of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)    (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) and (b):  The Government has announced various relief measures for several sectors under the Atmanirbhar Bharat Packages 1.0, 2.0, and 3.0 announced on 13th  May, 2020 to 17th  May, 2020, 12th October, 2020 &  12th  November, 2020 respectively involving a total amount of Rs.29,87,641 crore (including amount provided for the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package, Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana from July to November, and measures announced by RBI).  The Government had, on 26.03.2020, announced a relief package under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package which included schemes for the welfare of the poor in their struggle against COVID-19.   Besides,  a Rs. 6.28 lakh crore  Covid stimulus package  has also been announced on 28.06.2021. The details of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package, Atmanirbhar Bharat Packages and the announcements made on 28.6.2021 are in Annexure.    An Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) was also launched to provide 100% guarantee coverage to banks and NBFCs to enable them to extend emergency credit facilities to Business Enterprises/MSMEs in view of COVID-19 to meet their additional term loan/additional working capital requirements. The number and amount of guarantee issued to Hingoli, Nanded and Yavatmal districts of Maharashtra are as under:District-wise Guarantee issued under ECLGS as on 14.7.2021S. No.	District Name	Number of Guarantee Issued	Amount Of Guarantee Issued(Cr)1.		Hingoli	7140	28.252.		Nanded	36660	196.93.		Yavatmal	21217	144.83*****
