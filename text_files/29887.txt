                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1543






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Expansion of FPIs




Nihal Chand Chauhan













                                                    Rahul Kaswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the progress made with respect to setting-up/ expansion of Food Processing Industries       (FPI) in various States/UTs of the country including Rajasthan during the last three years, State-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has carried out any study for setting-up and expansion of such industries in the country;(c)	if so, the details thereof including the steps taken by the Government for expansion and dissemination of food processing industries in the country;(d) 	the number of Mega Food Parks set-up so far in the country;(e) 	whether the Government is working on any roadmap for setting-up of these parks in future; and(f)	if so, the details thereof.






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a): 	Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a Central Sector umbrella scheme- Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector including setting up/ expansion of Food Processing Industries across the country including Rajasthan. No of projects sanctioned and completed during last three years under component schemes of PMKSY across the country and in Rajasthan is placed at Annexure.(b) & (c): MoFPI has not conducted any study specifically for setting up/ expansion of Food Processing Industries, though evaluation study of the relevant component schemes of PMKSY has been conducted. Under the component schemes,  Ministry provides credit linked financial assistance (capital subsidy) in the form of grants-in-aid to entrepreneurs for setting up /expansion of food processing / preservation industries.(d) to (f): Out of 41Mega Food Parks (MFP) approved across the country under PMKSY, a total of 22 MFPs are operational. Regular monitoring & coordination with  Implementing Agencies, State Authorities etc is done to expedite implementation of the MFPs. A Special Food Processing Fund of Rs. 2000 crore has been set up with NABARD to provide affordable credit for investments in setting up Mega Food Parks as well as processing units in the MFPs. Ministry also incentivizes setting up/ expansion of Food Processing Units in these parks through relevant component scheme of PMKSY****
