                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3536






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Achievement of NCRMP Phase-II




Francisco Cosme Sardinha














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of progress achieved in the State of Goa under each of the four principal components under the World Bank Funded centrally sponsored scheme of National Cyclone Risk Mitigation Project Phase-II from 2015-16 till March 2019, year-wise;(b) whether the stakeholders, coastal communities and local authorities have been consulted and involved for the above;(c) if not, the steps proposed to be taken to ensure local community participation in the above scheme; and(d) the financial contribution of the State of Goa for the above scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):  The details are at Annexure-I. (b):	The stakeholder coastal communities and local authorities have been consulted and involved by the State Project Implementation Unit, Goa in the project. (C):  Does not arise(d): Out of the four components of the Project, the States are to contribute 25% of expenditure incurred under only one component i. e. Component B (Cyclone Risk Mitigation Infrastructure). An expenditure of Rs. 2.12 crore has been incurred under Component B till September, 2019, out of which the financial contribution of State of Goa is Rs. 53 lakh.*****
