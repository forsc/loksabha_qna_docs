                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

14






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Bonded Labour




S. Jothimani














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of the scheme for Rehabilitation of Bonded Labour implemented by the Government; (b)the details of funds allocated in the last five years, district and State-wise; (c)the total number of bonded labourers released and rehabilitated across the country as on date; (d)whether the Government will consider implementing the recommendations of the International Labour Organization report on bonded labour, 2014, if so, the details thereof; and (e)the details of measures/steps taken by the Government to spread awareness for eradication of bonded labour?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	The Government of India has implemented a Central Sector Scheme for Rehabilitation of Bonded Labourers-2016 w.e.f. 17.05.2016 under which financial assistance to the tune of Rs. 1.00 (one) lakh, Rs.2.00 (two) lakh & Rs. 3.00 (three) lakh are provided to released bonded labourers based on their category and level of exploitation along with other non-cash assistance for their livelihood. Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(b):	 Fund allocation during last five years under the Scheme for Rehabilitation of Bonded Labourers-2016  is as under :-Financial Year	Amount(Rs. in crore)2015-16	5.002016-17	5.002017-18	10.002018-19	10.002019-20	8.00No District, State-wise fund allocation is made.(c):	 The total number of bonded labour identified and released is 3,13,687 and total number of bonded labour rehabilitated is 2,93,725 (19962 bonded labourers are not available for rehabilitation. Either they have died or left the place without leaving their addresses).  (d):	 No such proposal is under consideration of the Government.(e):	  Implementation of the Bonded Labour System (Abolition) Act, 1976 is the responsibility of the State governments. Under the Scheme for Rehabilitation of Bonded Labourer – 2016, Central government provides assistance of Rs. ten lakh per annum per state for undertaking awareness generation activities. ******
