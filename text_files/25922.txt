                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1684






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Funds under Atal Bhujal Yojana




Sunita Duggal













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of allocation of funds provided to water stressed Gram Panchayats of Haryana and Andhra Pradesh under Atal Bhujal Yojana during 2019-20 and 2020-21; the district-wise; and (b) whether any improvement has been noticed under Atal Bhujal Yojana in ground water level in notified Gram Panchayats of Haryana and Andhra Pradesh after the introduction, if so, the details thereof along with its achievements thereunder?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) Atal Bhujal Yojana started from the year 2020-21 and the program implementation is still under progress. During 2020-21, Rs.20.80 crore was disbursed to Haryana under this Scheme.              Scheme is not being implemented in Andhra Pradesh. *****
