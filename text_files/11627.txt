                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

532






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






ROBs in Karnataka




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any representations seeking construction of Road Over Bridges (ROBs) in lieu of Level Crossings in the State of Karnataka and if so, the details thereof along with the number of requests considered in the State; (b) the number of ROBs constructed in lieu of Level Crossings during the last three years and till date;(c) the details along with the works of ROBs completed, under construction and those proposals pending;(d) whether the Government has set any timeframe to complete the construction of such ROBs; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. *****
