                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3345






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Quarterly Rate of GDP




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) during the years 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021,22 in the various States and Union Territories of the country including Chhattisgarh along with the State/UT/year-wise details thereof;  (b) the details of quarterly GDP growth rate in respect of the above; and  (c) the details of the components of GDP in agriculture, service and manufacturing sectors, State/Financial Year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION, MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a): As per the latest information received from State Governments, State/ Union Territory-wise Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) during the years 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021,22 in the various States and Union Territories of the country including Chhattisgarh at Current and Constant (2011-12) Prices is enclosed in Annexure I. (b): As per information received from State Government, quarterly estimates of GSDP are prepared by Government of Utter Pradesh only. The quarterly GSDP growth rate at Constant (2011-12) Prices of Utter Pradesh is enclosed in Annexure II. (c): Financial Year-wise details of the components of Gross State Value Added (GSVA), as per the information received from State Governments, at Current and Constant (2011-12) Prices is given at Annexure III.                                             ******
