                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3706






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Formation of New SFURTI Clusters




Goddeti Madhavi













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has recently inaugurated 50 new Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) clusters in 18 States across the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the number of clusters that are of regular or major types out of aforesaid clusters, State –wise and the funds allocated for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (c):	Yes, Sir. The Government has recently inaugurated 50 new clusters under the Scheme of Funds for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) in 18 States across the country. The details of these clusters including their types, State – wise distribution and the funds allocated for the purpose is placed at Annexure.
