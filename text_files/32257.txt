                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3689






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






EWS Reservation




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3689TO BE ANSWERED ON 21.12.2021EWS RESERVATION 3689. SHRI RITESH PANDEY: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the number of persons who have availed the benefit of EWS reservation for an appointment in Government services till date; (b) the total number of seats increased/additional seats created over a period of two years to provide a ten per cent EWS reservation in the central universities; and (c) the number of funds/additional funds provided to the Ministries for the implementation of the EWS reservation?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR)(a) :   As per information given by the Department of Personnel and Training a total of 84 persons have availed the benefit of EWS reservation for an appointment in Government services till date.(b):  the total number of seats increased/additional seats created during the last two years to provide ten per cent EWS reservation in the central universities is as under:2019-20	2020-2120240	26983 (c): An additional amount of Rs. 4315.15 Cr. was provided for the implementation of the EWS reservation during the last two years.*****
