                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4941






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Guidelines for Promoting Floating Jetties




M V V Satyanarayana













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently issued guidelines for promoting floating jetties/platforms for various usages all along the Indian Coastline;(b)     if so, the details thereof;(c)    whether the Government has set a benchmark and evolved standard specifications in these guidelines at par with international standards; and(d)      if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)&(b) Yes, Sir. Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways circulated the ‘guidelines for promoting floating jetties/platforms for various usages all along the Indian Coastline’ to all the Major Ports, States Maritime Boards and coastal States/ UTs. This guideline sets out various technical aspects for implementation of Floating Jetties / Platforms for Marinas, Minor Harbors, Fishing Harbours, Fish landing centers, Waterdromes and such other similar facilities in coastal areas, estuaries, waterways, rivers and reservoirs.(c) & (d) There are no relevant Indian or International standards, which can be directly relied upon while formulating the guidelines for the floating jetties/ platforms. However, the standard specifications, in these guidelines, are drawn on par with other International engineering practices in various countries.                                              *****
