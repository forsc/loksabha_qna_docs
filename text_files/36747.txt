                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






SAATHI Initiative




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of States in which the System for Assessment, Awareness & Training for Hospitality Industry (SAATHI) Initiative in collaboration with Union Government has been implemented at present;(b)	the number of hotels that have self-certified themselves under the Initiative and the details thereof, State-wise;(c)	the number of interactive webinars that have been undertaken and the details thereof, State-wise; (d)	the number of hotels that have been site assessed till now and the details thereof, State-wise; and (e)	the plan to extend this initiative beyond the COVID-19 pandemic to continue operations safely and remove risks?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM				   (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	 to (e):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. ******* STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *354 ANSWERED ON 28.03.2022 REGARDING SAATHI INITIATIVE(a) & (b):	The System for Assessment, Awareness & Training for Hospitality Industry (SAATHI) has been implemented in all States and Union Territories. The State-wise number of units that have self-certified themselves under the above initiative is given at Annexure I.(c):	20 interactive webinars have been conducted with 1185 participants. The webinars were open to participants from all States and Union Territories.  This is a continuous process. The State-wise details of number of participants are given at Annexure II.(d):	The number of hotels that have been site-assessed till now and the State-wise details are given at Annexure III. (e):	The initiative will continue to assist the hospitality industry in their preparedness to continue operations safely and mitigate risks both present and future.*******
