                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2961






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Service Centre of SAI




Rajdeep Roy














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to start a service centre of the Sports Authority of India (SAI) at Silchar; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) & (b)	Sports Authority of India (SAI) centres are set up after taking into account the need for such centres in a particular geographical region. At present, there is no proposal under consideration in this Ministry to set up any SAI centre at Silchar. However, the North Eastern Sub Centre (NESC) of SAI, located in Guwahati, Assam, inter-alia, covers the State of Assam, thus catering to Silchar as well. Details of States covered by SAI, NESC, and important sports disciplines catered to are given here under :States covered by SAI, NESC	Important sports disciplines catered toAssam, Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh and Sikkim	Archery, Athletics, Badminton, Boxing, Football, Fencing, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Swimming, Weightlifting, Taekwondo, etc.*****
