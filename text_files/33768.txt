                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Collection of Funds under DMF




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the funds that were annually collected and utilized by Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh under District Mineral Foundation (DMF) for 2019-2020, 2020-2021 and 2021-2022;(b)	the details of projects funded by DMF funds for the year 2020-2021 and 2021-2022 for Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh; along with the name and objective of the project, funds allocated and implementation status; (c)	whether it is true that some districts have constituted ‘Project Management Units’ by hiring professional agencies to improve the planning and management for DMF fund utilization; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?
