                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

349






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Horticultural Training




Keshari Devi Patel













                                                    Kanakmal Katara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the programmes implemented to provide training for plantation of horticultural crops during the last five years, State-wise and district-wise including Uttar Pradesh (Prayagraj district) and Rajasthan; (b) the details of new orchards planted under Agriculture Development Project; (c) the number of farmers given training in Prayagraj in Uttar Pradesh for development of basic infrastructure by giving financial assistance; (d) the percentage of farmers working on the basis of training imparted under the said scheme; (e) whether the same has been reviewed; and (f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) and (b): 	Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is implementing a Scheme namely Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), a centrally sponsored scheme, for the holistic development of horticulture in the country. All States/UTs including Uttar Pradesh (Prayagraj district) and Rajasthan are covered under MIDH. Under MIDH, Human Resource Development (HRD) is one of the component under which assistance is provided for awareness programmes, farmers training, exposure visit, etc. Under this component training is imparted by the States on the latest technologies on production and post-harvest management to women and youth. 		State-wise details of the number of farmers trained under MIDH throughout the country including Uttar Pradesh (Prayagraj district) and Rajasthan during the last five years is at Annexure. 		There is no project called by the name of “Agriculture Development Project” under Ministry of Agriculture. However, under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) an area of 9.76 lakhs hectare has been brought under different horticultural crops including fruits orchards, vegetables, flowers, spices and aromatic plants since its inception in 2014-15.  (c) A total number of 200 farmers of Prayagraj in Uttar Pradesh have been given training under MIDH during last five years. (d) to (f):  MIDH being a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented by the States/UTs, and  therefore, the farmers level data is not centrally maintained.
