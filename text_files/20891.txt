                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Vehicular Compatibility of E20 Fuel




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry is contemplating on use of E20 Fuel - a blend of 20 percent of ethanol and gasoline as an automobile fuel in order to reduce vehicularemissions as well as reduction of country‘s import bill and if so, the detailsthereof;(b) whether the vehicular compatibility of the fuel has been examined and if so, thedetails of the report; and(c) the details of the prospects of introducing E20 fuel for general use?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): Government of India vide its notification dated 5th February, 2019 directedOil Marketing Companies (OMCs) to sell 10% ethanol blended petrol or anypercentage specified by the Government, as per Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS)specifications. The notified National Policy on Biofuels-2018 provides anindicative target of 20% blending of ethanol in petrol by 2030. Accordingly,Government has initiated several steps both on the supply and demand side underthe Ethanol Blended Petrol (EBP) Programme to achieve the objectives ofreducing import dependence, promote clean fuel and provide boost to agriculture.(b): A joint study by Indian Institute of Petroleum (IIP) Dehradun, AutomotiveResearch Association of India (ARAI) Pune and Indian Oil Corporation Limited(IOCL) R&D on E20 as fuel with existing vehicles was undertaken under the aegisof Department of heavy Industry (DHI) and BIS has issued specification for E20 in2018. As per the study, it was recommended to adopt E20 fuel in the compatiblevehicles labelled for the same.(c): Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas (MoP&NG) has setup an interministerialcommittee to suggest a roadmap for introduction of E20 fuel in thecountry.******
