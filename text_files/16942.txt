                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

673






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Mobile Towers in Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Government proposes to install more BSNL mobile tower for better connectivity in Ratnagiri and Sindhudur districts of Maharashtra; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) 	the time by which such mobile towers are likely to be installed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) to (c) 	Yes Sir. BSNL has informed that 81 towers in Ratnagiri and 56 towers in Sindhudur have been commissioned between 2017-2019 with a target of installing towers at another 19 sites in Ratnagiri and 8 sites in Sindhudur  by 31.03.2021. ************
