                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






5G Services




Kotagiri Sridhar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of 5G services in the country; (b) 	whether the Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) is preparing to launch 5G services inspite of missing out on 4G and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) 	whether the Government proposes to lay down any plan to privatise BSNL and MTNL and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	The 5G services have not been rolled out in the country as yet.  (b)	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has informed that it has not yet planned for introducing 5G services in its network.(c)	Government has no plan to privatise BSNL and MTNL.************
