                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3411






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Setting up of Major Industry in Nanded




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up a major industry in Nanded, Maharashtra. the land of internationally acclaimed ‘Guru-Ta-Gaddi‘ of Guru Gobind Singh Ji; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has sought any proposal from the Government of Maharashtra for setting up of a large industrial unit in Nanded; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a ) to (d):   	The primary responsibility of industrial development rests with the State Governments. The Union Government supplements their efforts through various schemes launched  by it for different sectors with a view to promote industrialization.Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI), Government of India has sanctioned an Agro Processing Cluster (APC), namely, M/s India Mega Agro Anaj Limited under "Creation of Agro Processing Clusters Scheme" on 15.3.2018, at  MIDC, Krushnoor, Nanded District with project cost of Rs.30.99 Crores and grant-in-aid of Rs.10 Crores.  The Ministry has also sanctioned 5 food processing units under the "Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation of Infrastructure (CEFPPC) Scheme" with a total project cost of Rs.37.52 crores and grant-in-aid of Rs.9.14 crores in the above said Agro Processing Cluster. ******
