                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1034






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Anganwadi Kendras




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the State-wise details of new projects and AnganwadiKendras started under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) scheme across the country including Jharkhand; and (b) 	the details of the benefits of the said projects and AnganwadiKendras set up thereunder across the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILDDEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The State-wise number of projects and Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) sanctioned and operational across the country including Jharkhand is Annexed.(b)	Under the Anganwadi Services Scheme, six services i.e. supplementary nutritional, pre-school non-formal education, immunization, nutrition & health education, health check-up and referral services are provided to the children (6 months - 6 years) and pregnant women & lactating mothers with the aim to improve nutritional status and enhance early development and learning outcomes of children under 6 years of age and improve care and nutrition of pregnant women and lactating mothers.
