                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2154






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Sports Promotional Schemes




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Durga Prasad Rao Balli
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Sports Authority of India (SAI) is implementing sports promotional schemes to identify talented sportspersons in the age group of 8-25 years and nurture them to excel at national andinternational competitions and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether SAI has established regional centres and educational institutions to implement the above sports promotional schemes in the country;(c) if so, the number of regional centres and educational institutions functioning for this purpose;(d) the number of sportspersons who availed the benefits across the country during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(e) the funds provided/utilized for the above schemes during the said period, State/ UT-wise;(f) whether the Government proposes to open such regional centres in various States and if so, the details thereof along with the locations where such centres are proposed to be opened; and(g) the other steps taken by the Government for effective implementation of the sports promotional schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) Yes Sir, Sports Authority of India (SAI) under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports (MYAS) is implementing the following schemes for identifying sporting talent of all age groups and its nurturing to excel at National and International level:-• Khelo India Scheme. National Sports Talent Contest (NSTC) (61 Centres)• Army Boys Sports Company (ABSC) (26 Centres)• SAI Training Centre (STC) (59 Centres)• Special Area Games (SAG) (21 Centres)• Extension Centres of STC/SAG (90 Centres)• Centre of Excellence (COE) (16 Centres)• National Sports Academies(b) & (c) Yes Sir. The list of SAI Regional Centres and Educational Institutions under MYAS is at Annexure -I(d) The information is at Annexure- II. (e) MYAS provides funds to SAI for implementation of schemes for promotion of sports through training centres established by SAI, assisting National Sports Federations (NSFs) and sportspersons of high performance potential. State and Union Territory wise funds are not provided to SAI. Information of funds released by MYAS to SAI during the last 3 years is at Annexure –III.(f) No Sir. Does not arise.(g) The Government monitors sports promotion schemes on regular basis, approves Annual Calendar for Training and Competitions (ACTCs) of various National Sports Federations and oversee training facilities established by SAI besides regularly interacting with sports persons.*****
