                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2522






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Education Projects




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                








                                                    Dileshwar Kamait
                                                








                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                








                                                    Raksha Nikhil Khadse
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the education projects in the country, State-wise particularly in West Bengal, Maharashtra, Jharkhand, Jammu and Bihar district-wise;(b)	the details of the funds allocated/released on the said projects in the said States during each of the last three years and the current year district-wise;(c)	the funds spent on the higher education projects during the said period along with the details thereof district-wise; and(d)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government for effective implementation of the education projects in the above- mentioned States?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a):  Details of education projects/schemes of Ministry of Education are indicated at Annexure-I.(b): The States/ UTs-wise details of funds allocated/released on the said projects/schemes for the last three year and the funds approved for current year, including the States of West-Bengal, Maharashtra, Jharkhand, Jammu and Bihar are given at Annexure –II.(c):  Total amount spent on Higher Education Projects during the last three years and the current year is at Annexure-II.(d):  The steps taken/being taken by the government for effective implementation of projects/schemes are indicated at Annexure-III.****
