                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2121






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Import of Crude Oil Substitute




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the monthly oil import prices during the year 2018-19; and(b) whether the Government is deliberating on an import substitute product which is pollution-free and cost effective and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
