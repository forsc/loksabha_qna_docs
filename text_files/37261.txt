                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Constituting Standing Committees




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to constitute a standing committee at the village and block level in all the panchayats of the country and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government proposes to constitute a Standing Committee at the Block/Village level in Haryana in coordination with the Haryana Government and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL)(a) & (b) No, Sir._____
