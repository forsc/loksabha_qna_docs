                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Fertility Rate among Working Women




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether it is a fact that working women have fewer children; (b)	whether the fertility rate of working women was 3.3 per cent before ten years and has currently declined to 2.9 per cent; (c)	whether the Government has constituted any task force in this regard to tackle the issue of low fertility rates among working women; (d)	whether the Government proposes to launch any campaign to create awareness among working women; and (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (b):   As per the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, no such reference data is available.(c):	As per the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, the Government has not constituted any task force in this regard.(d) to (e):  As per the National Population Policy 2000 and National Health Policy, 2017, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare through various intervention, aims to reduce the Total Fertility Rate by improving awareness generation activities through media campaigns.*****
