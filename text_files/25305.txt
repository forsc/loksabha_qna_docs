                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

953






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Training under PMKVY in Maharashtra




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      the number of unemployed youth trained for skill development under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) in the last two years; (b)      the number of PMKVY Kendras functioning in Maharashtra, district-wise;(c)	the type of technical skill development facilities available to the youth; and(d)	whether job opportunities are being provided to these trainees after completing their training and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under PMKVY, 65.26 lakh candidates trained in last two years i.e. FY 2019-21.(b)      District wise PMKKs allocated and established in State of Maharashtra are given at Annexure.(c)       Under PMKVY, training is being imparted in various National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) aligned job roles of level 3 to 5 including technical courses across 37 sectors.(d)       Yes Sir, under placement-linked (short term training (STT) component of PMKVY, training providers facilitate placement of certified candidates. Under this component, as on 10.07.2021, 52.46 lakh candidates have been certified; out of which, 23.2 lakh candidates have been placed in diverse sectors.
