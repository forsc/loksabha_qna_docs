                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Night Landing Facility in Airports




Umesh G. Jadhav













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether there is no night landing facility in many airports in the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(b) whether the Government is considering to upgrade technology to provide night landing to airports in Karnataka State Tier 2 cities, if so, the time by which it is to be upgraded and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the mechanism employed by AAI to measure visibility in airports without night landing facilities; and (d) the procedure followed by ground-staff in permitting an aircraft on flight to land at airports without night landing facilities?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a): The airports of Airports Authority of India (AAI) which are not equipped with night landing facility are Cudappah in Andhra Pradesh, Rupsi in Assam, Tezu in Arunachal Pradesh, Diu in Daman & Diu, Kandla in Gujarat, Dharamshala, Kullu and Shimla in Himachal Pradesh, Kalaburagi in Karnataka, Agatti in Lakshadweep, Juhu and Kolhapur in Maharashtra, Ludhiana in Punjab, Puducherry in Puducherry, Pakyong in Sikkim, Salem in Tamil Nadu, Pantnagar in Uttarakhand, Bilaspur in Chhattisgarh, Kushinagar in Uttar Pradesh and Cooch Behar in West Bengal.(b): The upgradation of airports, including provision of night landing facility is a continuous process, which depends upon operational requirements, traffic demand, commercial feasibility etc. and varies from airport to airport in the country. In Karnataka, AAI has planned to install navigational aid to enable night landing facilities at Kalaburagi airport and to install Instrument Landing System (ILS)  to upgrade existing night landing facility at Belagavi airport.(c) and (d): Landing of flights are permitted only during day time where night landing facilities is not available. ;
