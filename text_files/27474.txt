                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3151






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Mega Investment Textiles Parks




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the main objectives and features of proposed Mega Investment Textiles Parks (MITRA); (b) whether any target have been set or reserve funds created for the scheme; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) the details of the sectors likely to be given priority under the scheme along with the items being given for export?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (d):	In the Union Budget 2021-22, it has been announced that a scheme of Mega Investment Textile Park will be launched. Under the scheme, seven Mega Textile Park will be set up in the country over 3 years. Ministry of Textiles is in the process of obtaining approval of the Cabinet for the proposed Scheme.  ***
