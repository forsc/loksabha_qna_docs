                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2525






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






PMGSY in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of progress made in the construction of roads in the State of Odisha under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) as on 31st March 2020;(b) 	whether a large number of roads are yet to be completed under the PMGSY in the State;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	the target set for the years 2020-21 and 2021-22 to provide rural connectivity under PMGSY?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) 	The details of progress made in the construction of roads in the State of Odisha under various verticals of  Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) as on 31st March, 2020  are as under:-Vertical	Sanctioned Roads	         Completed Roads	            Nos.     Length (Km)	   Nos.	      Length (Km)PMGSY I	          15,834	61,163	         14,710	      57,413PMGSY II	     635	 3,658	         35	       2,756RCPLWEA	              48	   486	          8	         178Total:	          16,517	65,307	         14,753	      60,347(b) & (c)    The details of road works which are yet to be completed under various verticals of PMGSY as on 3rd March, 2021 are as follows:-Vertical	No. of Roads	Length (km)PMGSY I	          553	          722PMGSY II	  259	          179RCPLWEA	           31	          262PMGSY-III*	  494	        3,328Total:	        1,337	        4,491* PMGSY-III works have been sanctioned during the year 2020-21.-2- (d)	During 2020-21, against the target of construction of 3,685 km road length, a total of 1,457 km road length has been constructed in the State of Odisha. As against the target of connecting 317 habitations, 253 have been connected by the State as on 3rd March, 2021. The targets for 2021-22 has not yet been fixed.   *******
