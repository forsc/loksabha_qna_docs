                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3105






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Funds Allocated to Panchayat of Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has allocated funds to Panchayat of the State Government of Odisha during the last three years;(b)	If so, the purpose or the scheme for which funds have been allocated; and(c)	the details of funds utilized by the State Government of Odisha including Kalahandi and Nuapada districts of Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL) (a) to (c) An amount of Rs.6931.07 crore was allocated as grants under the Fourteenth and Fifteenth Finance Commissions for the Panchayati Raj Institutions in the State of Odisha during the last three years.  Of the allocated grants, Rs.6415.98 crore was released for the socio-economic development of Panchayats that includes water supply, sanitation, waste management, maintenance of community assets, maintenance of roads, foot paths, providing street light and the like. Under the    schemes of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) and Incentivisation of Panchayats (IoP), Rs.10.10 crore was released (no prior allocation is made under the schemes) to the State Government of Odisha during the last three years for strengthening of the Panchayati Raj Institutions in the State and incentivising the Panchayats doing good developmental works for which they have been awarded under the National Panchayat Awards. Funds under the Schemes of Ministry of Panchayati Raj is not released to any Districts including the Districts of Kalahandi and Nuapada.  ***
