                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4319






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Manpower Audit in AIR and Doordarshan




C. Lalrosanga














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Prasar Bharati has conducted manpower audit in AIR and Doordarshan, if so, the details thereof; and (b) the ratio of staff/officials in programme cadre, engineering cadre and administrative cadre along with the number of staffs/officials working in each of the three cadres at present?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES [SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR] (a)  Prasar Bharati has entered into a contract with M/s. Ernst &Young LLP (E&Y LLP) to carry out the work of Manpower Audit of Prasar Bharati.  The Report on Manpower Audit has been submitted by M/s. E&Y LLP to Prasar Bharati.(b)  The ratio (in percent) of staff/officials in programme cadre, engineering cadre and administrative cadre along with the number of staff/officials working in each of the three cadres working in Prasar Bharati as on 02-12-2019 is as under:-Sl. No.	Cadre	Total Strength	Ratio (in Percentage)i	Administrative 	7753	31.7%ii	Engineering	12820	52.4%iii	Programme	3891	15.9%	Total	24464	*****
