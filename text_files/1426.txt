                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1236






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






AIIMS, Jodhpur




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of works being carried out in All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) located in Jodhpur, Rajasthan on contract basis through private firms/companies including the names of such private firms/companies engaged therein; and(b) the year-wise details of amount paid by the said institute from 1st April, 2014 to 31st March, 2019 along with the work for which said payment was made as well as the names of the companies/firms to which payments were made?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	As per the inputs provided by AIIMS Jodhpur, the details of works carried out by various private firms/ companies in AIIMS, Jodhpur is enclosed Annexure-I.The payment made to the firms listed in Annexure – I is annexed at Annexure – II.
