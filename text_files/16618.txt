                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

285






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Decline in Sale of Automobile Sector




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the retail sale of two-wheelers and four wheelers have reduced considerably in the last few  months;(b) if so, the details thereof during the last three quarters, quarter-wise;(c) whether the Government has taken any steps to improve the sale of automobile sector in the coming years and to provide sops to the sector particularly for the 2-wheeler sector;(d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) & (b): Quarter wise details of sales from OEMs(Original Equipment Manufacturers) to dealerships, during the last three quarters, as per the report of Society of Indian Automobile  Manufacturers (SIAM) is as under:Category	July 19-Sept19	Oct. 19-Dec.19	Jan 20-Mar 20Passenger Vehicle	7,84,734	6,55,590	1,53,734Commercial  Vehicles	1,95,222	1,46,986	31,636Two-wheelers	42,18,241	35,02,642	12,93,113(c) & (d): The Government, as a policymaker, always attempts to sustain and improve momentum of the economy through a package of measures for comprehensive and continued development of the auto sector as and when required. (e): Does not arise. ----------
