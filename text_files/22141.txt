                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Pomegranate Research Institute




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the National Research Centre on Pomegranate in Solapur has demanded additional funds from the Ministry for the construction of buildings, purchase of vehicles, construction of boundary wall and essential materials for the scientists; (b)	if so, the funds earmarked by the Ministry during this financial year for this purpose; (c)	if not, whether the Government proposes to provide the fund demanded in this financial year; and (d)	if not, the reasons therefor along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	The Institute ICAR-NRCP, Solapur has not raised any such additional demand of funds from the Ministry (ICAR) for the construction of buildings, purchase of vehicles, construction of boundary wall and essential materials for the scientists.	(b)	No fund has been earmarked by the Ministry during this financial year for the purpose.(c)	As at (b) above.(d)	Same at (b) and (c) above.*******
