                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

534






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Illegal Encroachment of Port Trust Land




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the coastal operational areas of Mumbai port trust and the M.V.P.T. roads have been encroached illegally; and(b)	if so, the initiatives being taken by the Government to make the land free of illegal encroachment?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Some portions/patches of roads in Mumbai Port Trust (MbPT) estate have been encroached and few hutments/ encroachments are existing since decades.(b)	MbPT undertakes periodical programmes for removal of such encroachments. As a result of these initiatives, approximately 1,09,874 sq mtrs of MbPT land area have been made encroachment free.                                              *****
