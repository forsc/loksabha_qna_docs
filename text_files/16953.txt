                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

593






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Construction of New Railway Line Between Ghughuli-Anand Nagar




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the construction of new rail line  from Ghughuli to Anand Nagar via Maharajganj under the north-eastern Railway is under consideration or pending;   (b) if so, the time by which the said project is likely to be sanctioned; and(c) the amount spent on the said project so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):  The work of new Broad Gauge line Anandnagar-Ghughli via Maharajganj was included in the Budget 2013-14 subject to the condition that the work would be taken up once the Government clearances are obtained. Government clearances have not been given, since project was found financially unviable and therefore, could not be taken forward.  (c) Does not arise.*****
