                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

633






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Four Laning of Prayagraj-Lucknow NH




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the present status of four-laning work on Prayagraj-Lucknow National Highway (NH);(b)     the funds allocated and released for the purpose; and(c)     the time frame set for completion of the project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c) The Prayagraj to Lucknow stretch consists of two sections (i) Lucknow to Raebareli & (ii) Raebareli to Prayagraj. At present Lucknow to Raebareli section of NH-24B has been awarded on BOT (Annuity) with total project cost of Rs. 635.90 crore and is 4-laned. The physical progress in Raebareli to Prayagraj section has been awarded on BOT (Toll) with total project cost of Rs. 499.93 crore. The progress work is 98%. 4-lane work on Lucknow to Raebareli section was completed on 14.04.2015. The expected date of completion of 2-lane + PS work of Raebareli to Prayagraj Section is 31.03.2021.*****
