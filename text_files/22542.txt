                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3547






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Projects by NEC




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of projects taken up by NEC over the last three financial years in the districts of Barpeta, Nalbari, Bongaigaon, Goalpara, Baska and Chirang in Assam;(b)	the projects under consideration for being taken up in these six districts; and(c)	the number of project proposals received by NEC so far?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a)	Six (6) projects worth Rs. 27.98 crore have been sanctioned by NEC in the districts of Barpeta, Goalpara, and Baksa in Assam. The details are as under:                                                               (Rs. In crore)<pre>Sl. No.	Name of Project	Financial Year (FY)	Approved Cost	District1	Aspirational District Barpeta, Assam	2019-2020	1.00	Barpeta2	Aspirational District Baksa, Assam	2019-2020	1.00	Baksa3	 Aspirational District Goalpara, Assam	2019-2020	1.00	Goalpara4	Tele-education Project-Establishment of Smart Virtual Classrooms in Aspirational Districts of NER	2020-2021	10.12	Barpeta & Goalpara5	Development of Darranggiri Banana Market at Daranggiri, Goalpara, Assam	2020-2021	9.90	Goalpara6	A/E Measures to protect B/B of River Darranga at different reaches, Baksa District, Assam	2020-2021	4.96	BaksaTotal	27.98	</pre> (b) 	No other project is under consideration for being taken up in these six districts. (c)	Project Proposals specific to these districts which have been received in the NEC, have already been sanctioned.
