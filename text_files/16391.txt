                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Development Work under Krishna Circuit




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has accomplished the works under Krishna Circuit Phase-I in Kurukshetra; (b) 	if so, the work-wise details thereof; (c) 	if not, the time by which it is likely to be completed; (d) 	whether the Government has started development work under Krishna Circuit Phase-2; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):		The Ministry of Tourism under Swadesh Darshan Scheme has sanctioned the project “Development of Tourism Infrastructure at places related to Mahabharat in Kurukshetra” Haryana under Krishna Circuit for an amount of Rs. 97.35 Crore in the year  2016-17. Various Components of the project cover development of  Braham Sarover, development of  Jyotisar, Narkatari, Sanhit  Sarover, development of city infrastructure, CCTV, Wi Fi, Security System etc. In view of the  covid-19 crisis,  the  completion  timeline   has  been  extended  till  31st  March, 2021.The  Ministry of Tourism  has  sanctioned  another project “Integrated Development of Govind Dev ji temple (Jaipur), Khatu Shyam Ji (Sikar) and Nathdwara (Rajsamand)” in the year 2016-17 under Krishna Circuit, in Rajasthan for  a  sanctioned cost of   Rs. 75.80 Crore.*******
