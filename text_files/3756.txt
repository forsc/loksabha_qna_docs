                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Digital Payments in University Campus




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched any drive to turn University Campuses cashless inthe country and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether all Centrally funded higher educational institutions would also go cashless inthe year 2017; and(c) if so, whether the service providers and vendors are working on campuses to put digitalpayment mechanism in place and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) :- Yes Sir. Ministry of Human Resource Development had launched “VittiyaSaksharta Abhiyan” in December 2016under which an appeal was made to all the highereducational institutions to develop a cashless campus. Further, Government has takenseveral initiativesto turn University Campusescashless by promoting digital transactions. AllUniversities/affiliatedInstitutions, Technical Institutions and Centrally FundedHigherEducational Institutions including IITs/IIMs/IIITs etc. were instructed to ensure making allreceipts and payments related to the functioning of the Institution viz. student fees, examfees, vendor payments, salary/wage payments etc. only through on line or digital modes.They were also asked to encourage all thecanteens and business establishments on thecampus to use only digital modes for their receipts and payments. All the CentralUniversities under this Ministry , all Indian Institutes of Technologies( IITs) , all IndianInstitutes of Management (IIMs), all National Institutes of Technology ( NITs), and allIndian Institutes of Information Technologies (IITMs) are fully digitalized in their receiptsand payments. Point of Sale (POS)machines have been kept/ installed in their canteens andcampus stores.***
