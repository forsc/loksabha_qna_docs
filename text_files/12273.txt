                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1204






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan Dhan Yojana




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil













                                                    Jagdambika Pal 
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the provision made or proposed to be made by the Union Government under Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan Dhan Yojana to provide benefits to maximum workers of unorganised sectors such as farm labourers, hawkers, tailors, artisans, rickshaw-pullers etc.; (b)whether any instruction has been given or proposed to be given by the Union Government to State Governments or district administration in this regard;(c)the district-wise number and details of beneficiaries included/likely to be included in the State of Maharashtra under the said Yojana;(d)the total number of people enrolled under Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan Dhan Yojana and the total amount of contributions received, State-wise; and(e)whether the Government intends to take further measures for the welfare of workers in the unorganized sector and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (e):	Ministry of Labour & Employment has launched Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan (PM-SYM) Yojana on 05th March, 2019 under section 3(1) of Unorganised Workers and Social Security Act, 2008 to provide old age protection to them in the form of monthly pension, which is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme for providing monthly minimum assured pension of Rs. 3000/- on attaining the age of 60 years. The unorganised workers such as farm laboures, howkers, toilers, artisans, rickshaw-pullers Contd..2/-:: 2 ::etc.in the age group of 18-40 years whose monthly income is    Rs.15000/- or less not an income tax payer and not a member of EPFO/ESIC/NPS can join the scheme. Ministry of Labour and Employment is implementing the scheme in close coordination with the State/UT Governments. In each State/UT, State Level Monitoring Committee (SLMC) under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary has been constituted for overall monitoring of the scheme. Further, in each district, District Level Implementation Committees (DLIC) has been constituted in every district under the Chairmanship of District Collector. DLIC is in close interaction with President/ Secretary of District Level Unions/Associations and Trade Unions to create awareness amongst the traders, Shopkeepers and Self-Employed Persons and mobilise them to join the pension scheme.The total no. of enrolment under this scheme till 04.02.2019 is 4075814, and District wise details of beneficiaries in Maharashtra is at Annexure.  Government of  India  has  enacted the  Unorganised Social Sector Act, 2008 to  provide  the  Social  Security benefits related  to  life and  disability cover,  Health  and  maternity benefits, Old age  protection  and  or  other  benefits as  decided  by  the  Central Government under the Act, State Governments are  also mandated  to  formulate schemes  relating  to provident  fund, housing, educational  scheme for  children,  employment, injury benefits, skill upgradation  etc.*                                                   *******
