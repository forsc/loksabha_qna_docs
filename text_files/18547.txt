                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2260






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Funds for Rashtriya Arogya Mission




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to allocate more funds in order to further strengthen Rashtriya Arogya Mission;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	to (c): Funds allocated for the National Health Mission as per Budget Estimates       (2020-21) is Rs. 33,400 crore. Government is also implementing an Umbrella scheme of Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi for providing financial assistance for treatment of poor patients suffering from life threatening diseases for which the fund allocated in Budget Estimates 2020-21 is Rs. 177.32 crore.
