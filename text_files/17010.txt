                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

806






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Khelo India Scheme




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received proposal for construction of a 400 metre synthetic track at Medical College, Pariyaram in Kannur under Khelo India Scheme and if so, the details thereof;  (b) whether the Government has sanctioned or proposes to sanction the same;  (c) if so, the details along with the status thereof; and  (d) if not, the reasons therefor? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS  { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }  (a) to (c) Yes, Sir. One project proposal for construction of a 400 metre synthetic track at Medical College, Pariyaram, Kannur, Kerala at an estimated cost of Rs. 7.00 crore has been received under the Khelo India Scheme. The project proposal has been recommended by the Project Appraisal Committee of Khelo India Scheme in its meeting held on 05.08.2020. A number of similar project proposals have been received from various other States/Union Territories and other eligible entities also. At the next stage, based on the project proposals’ technical feasibility, adherence to the related parameters prescribed as well as availability of funds under the Khelo India Scheme the project proposals recommended by the Project Appraisal Committee goes to the Departmental Project Approval Committee of Khelo India Scheme for approval.  (d) Does not arise. ****
