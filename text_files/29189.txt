                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Ground Water Recharge in Haryana




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds provided to the State of Haryana by National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) to recharge ground water through dug wells during the last three years; (b) the amount of subsidy given to the farmers through this scheme; and (c) the funds to be provided to the State of Haryana for this scheme through NABARD?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) As per information received from National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) no refinance has been made to the state of Haryana under the previous scheme to recharge ground water through dug wells during the last three years. Further, the scheme for recharge of ground water through dug wells has been closed. However, NABARD has disbursed long term refinance under Long Term Rural Credit Fund (LTRCF), in which some part of dugwell activity may be permissible, as per the details given below:   									      (Amount in Rs crore)State	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Haryana*	252.85	29.59	185.09* No separate information is available for dug well refinance. (b)  As per information no subsidy is available.   (c)  An amount of Rs. 96.10 crore has been allocated to Haryana during current financial year under LTRCF.*****
