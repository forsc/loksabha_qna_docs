                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Funds for PMS Scheme




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3811TO BE ANSWERED ON 17.03.2020FUNDS FOR PMS SCHEME 3811. SHRI RANJEET SINGH HINDURAO NAIK NIMBALKAR: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the amount of funds released by the Government under the Post Matric Scholarship (PMS) Scheme for Scheduled Castes (SCs) during the last three years and the current year, year and State-wise, including Maharashtra; (b) whether any pending amount is to be released under the scheme to Maharashtra, specially in Madha Lok Sabha Constituency area; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) the number of students who have availed the scholarship during the said period, year and State-wise?
