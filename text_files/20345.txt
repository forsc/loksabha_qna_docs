                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Drinking Water Facilities




Raksha Nikhil Khadse













                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to improve, strengthen and ensure availability of drinking water in rural area of the country in the next five years; and (b) the details of the rural areas identified to implement the scheme, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI  RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b)	Since August, 2019 to enable every rural household in the country to have assured potable water supply by 2024, Government of India, in partnership with States is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal with an estimated outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore. Based on the requirement, under JJM, States/ UTs are vested with powers to plan, approve and implement water supply schemes in rural areas.  The present status of implementation of JJM across the country is available on JJM dashboard at https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx.****
