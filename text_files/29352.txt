                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1039






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Malaria outbreaks in Meghalaya




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	Whether Government is aware that Meghalaya is a Category 3 zone for Malaria outbreaks in the country, if so, the details thereof;(b)  	Whether the Government has undertaken any awareness initiative in Meghalaya to prevent spread of Malaria; and(c)  	The number of deaths recorded under different age-groups due to Malaria in North Eastern Region, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) 		Meghalaya state is under Category-3 as per the National Framework launched in February, 2016 and targeted for malaria elimination by 2027. In 2014, Annual Parasite Index of Meghalaya state was 12.52, since 2019 state has achieved API less than 1. (b)  	The awareness initiative taken by the Government in Meghalaya state to prevent Malaria are as follows:•	Observance of World Malaria Day every year on 25th April and anti-malaria month every year in   June for generating community awareness regarding the disease, its prevention and control measures.•	39 Mega-Phone have been procured for the Malaria Technical Supervisors (MTS) for dissemination of key malaria messages to the community.•	10,137 Information Education and Communication (IEC) board carrying key malaria messages have been installed in schools.•	Posters and Pamphlets were distributed carrying key malaria messages.•	Faith based organization are utilized to disseminate malaria messages.•	Behaviour Change Communication and community mobilization   is conducted at state, district and local level.
