                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5123






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






CCTV in Trains




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided for setting up of CCTVs in all coaches in long distance trains;  (b)	if so, the present status of this proposal and the total number of trains equipped with CCTV cameras and the financial allocation made by the Government for this purpose till now;  and  (c)	the steps taken by the  Government to ensure installation of CCTV cameras in all coaches including passengers trains?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Ministry of Railways have taken steps to install CCTV cameras in coaches of Premium, Mail/Express and Suburban trains.(b): CCTV cameras have been fitted in 1300 coaches of 100 Mainline passenger trains so far. An allocation of ?280 crores has been made during the financial year 2019-20 for the work of provision of CCTV cameras in coaches and at stations.(c): Works costing ?2050 crores have been sanctioned for installation of CCTV cameras in 58276 coaches of Premium, Mail/Express and Suburban trains.   In Phase-I, CCTV cameras in 7020 coaches of such trains have been targeted for completion by March 2021. The work has been entrusted to RailTel, a Public Sector Undertaking under Ministry of Railways.****
