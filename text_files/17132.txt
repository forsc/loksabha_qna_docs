                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1071






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Ecologically Sensitive Areas




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has issued final notification regarding demarcation of Ecologically Sensitive Areas (ESAs) in Western Ghats as per Kasturirangan report;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the time by which final notification is likely to be issued;(c) 	the action taken by the Government on the recommendations submitted by the State Government of Kerala in this regard;(d) 	whether the Government has taken note of the delay in issuance of final notification which has affected developmental projects like construction of houses, schools, hospitals, roads etc.; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO) (a) to (e) The draft notification re-published by the Ministry of Environment, Forest & Climate Change on 3rd October, 2018 based on Kasturirangan Report, is valid up to 26th September, 2020. The suggestions and feedback from the States and other stakeholders are under consideration for finalisation of the notification. The Government has been consistently making all efforts to bring all the States on board and for this purpose consultations have been held on 15th February, 2019, 21st May, 2020 and 9-10 July, 2020.  Efforts are being made to finalise the notification taking into consideration the environmental concerns of the region while simultaneously accommodating the recommendations of the State Governments including Government of Kerala. Till the finalisation of the notification, the directions issued by the Ministry on 13th November, 2013 in regard to administration of activities in the area proposed, in the draft notification are valid.*****
