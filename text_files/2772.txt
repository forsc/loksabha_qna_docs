                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Food Processing Plant




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering the proposal for setting up any new ‘food processing plant’ in the Kurukshetra district of Haryana;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SMT. HARSIMRAT KAUR BADAL) (a) to (c):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *231 FOR ANSWERED ON 09TH JULY, 2019 REGARDING FOOD PROCESSING PLANT(a) to (c): No, Sir the Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has not received any proposal to set up food processing plants in Kurukshetra. The MoFPI does not set up any food processing industries/ units/projects/plants on its own. It provides financial assistance as capital subsidy in the form of grant-in-aid to the individuals, farmers, Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), Entrepreneurs, Cooperatives, Societies, Self Help Groups (SHGs), Private Companies and Central/ State PSUs etc. under its umbrella scheme of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for setting up of food processing industries/ units/ projects/plants. *****
