                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4117






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Proposals under Swachh Bharat Mission




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of proposals cleared and funds sanctioned/released along with the details of works conducted or likely to be conducted under Swachh Bharat Mission so far, State/UT-wise;(b) the details of proposals lying pending along with the time by which pending proposals are likely to be cleared, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government has received any complaints regarding misappropriation of funds under the Mission; and(d) if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise along with the action taken/being taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):  Funds are released under various components of the Swachh Bharat Mission- Urban (SBM-U) to the States/ Union Territories (UTs) against the proposals, duly approved by the respective State High Power Committee (SHPC), submitted to the Government. State/ UT- wise details of the funds released are at Annexure-I. State/ UT- wise details of the work done are at Annexure-II, Annexure-III and Annexure-IV.(b): No Sir.(c) and (d): As the funds under SBM-U are released to the States/UTs and the execution of the work is done by the States/ UTs,  complaints received, if any, by the Government, are referred to the concerned States/ UTs for taking necessary action at their end.                           ******
