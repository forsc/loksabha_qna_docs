                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3034






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Skill Development Centres under PMKVY




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                     Bhagirath Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      whether the Skill Development Centres under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana have been set up in all the districts of the country;(b)      if not, the State-wise names and details of the districts where such centres have not been set up;(c)	whether the Government has launched Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikash Yojana 3.0(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the extent of target proposed to be fixed by the Government under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana for the next financial year including the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)      Under PMKVY, short term training (STT) is being imparted through empanelled training centres across the country. PMKVY does not mandate the establishment of training centres but empanel through the process of accreditation and affiliation as defined in Guidelines. The State-wise number of empanelled training providers / training centre under STT component of PMKVY 2.0, as on 19.01.2021, is given at column (a) of Annexure.  (c) to (e)          Yes Sir. PMKVY 3.0 was launched on 15.01.2021 with aims to impart demand driven short duration skill training to 8 lakh youths across the country. As on 26.02.2021, 75,950 candidates have been enrolled. The State-wise number of empanelled training providers / training centre under STT component of PMKVY 3.0, as on 26.02.2021, is given at column (b) of Annexure.
