                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3080






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Tribal Freedom Fighters Museums




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the proposed scheme to build tribal freedom fighters’ museums, including the selected locations;(b) the status of completion of the aforementioned scheme; and (c) whether the Government would consider expanding the scheme to include Odisha, and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): Ministry of Tribal Affiairs has sanctioned Tribal Freedom Fighters Museum in 10 States under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme ''Support to Tribal Research Institutes''.  The details of sanctioned Tribal Freedom Fighters Museum, State-wise, are annexed. (c): Yes, Sir. If Ministry recieves such proposal from the  State of Odisha, the same will be considered under the relevant scheme.  AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a) & (b) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.3080 for answer on 15.03.2021 S. N.	State	Location	Status1	Gujarat	Rajpipla	Civil work expected to be completed by July-2021 & curation work expected to be completed by July, 2022.2	Jharkhand	Ranchi	The work for construction of museum is complete,3	Andhra Pradesh	Lambasingi	For undertaking Civil construction works, tenders have been floated on 25.01.2021. As per the milestones for construction of the Museum, it is 20 months and the likely date of completion of the work is by the end of March, 2023.4	Chhattisgarh	Raipur	Land identified and final acquisition is under process.5	Kerala	Kozhikode	A primary DPR has been prepared for which includes design, creation and implementation of Tribal Freedom Fighters Museum of Kerala and renovation and upgradation of existing ethnological museum.6	Madhya Pradesh	Chhindwara	The museum building is under construction and shall be completed by end of Financial Year 2021-22.7	Telangana	Hyderabad	Historical Research Work, Curation Studies, Preparation of storyline has been completed.8	Manipur	Senapati	The State Government is yet to finalise site/location for setting up of the Tribal Freedom Fighters Museum in Manipur.9.	Mizoram	Mualngo, Kelsih	Earthworks have been completed and work execution started. Expected to be completed by January, 202310.	Goa	Ponda	TRI Goa has been requested to submit DPR of Project and land availability.*****
