                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1369






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Thirukkual




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any proposal from Tamil NaduState Government or any organisation/NGO/individuals to declare and notify‘Thirukkual’ as National book which is classical Tamil language text consisting of1,330 couplets widely admired by scholars and influential leaders over its historyalso known for its universality and secular nature written by the saint poetThiruvallur;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the action taken by the Union Government on the request and to declare itas national book/library at the earliest?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) : A request to give status of National Book of India to ‘Thirukkual’ hasbeen received from Shri R.B. Sreekumar, IPS (retd) former DGP Gujarat.Presently, there is no concept of giving National Book status to any book.
