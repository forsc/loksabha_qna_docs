                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Features of Khelo India Programme




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features and objectives of Khelo India Programme;(b) whether the Government has sanctioned adequate funds for youth and sports activities in the country;(c) if so, the details thereof including Chhattisgarh, State/UT-wise;(d) whether the Government proposes to plan any strategy for improvement/development of youth and sports activities in the country;(e) if so, the details of youth hostels and stadiums constructed during each of the last three years and the current year along with the funds allocated for the said purpose, State/UT and programme-wise; and(f) the steps taken by the Government to increase allocation of funds for the State Governments?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) The salient features and objectives of Khelo India Scheme are given in Annexure - 1.(b) to (f) “Sports” being a State subject, the responsibility of development of Sports, including sanctioning adequate funds and planning strategies for improvement/development of youth and sports activities in the country and construction of youth hostels and stadiums, rests with the State/Union Territory (UT) Governments. This Ministry supplements the efforts of State /UT Governments in this regard, by providing financial assistance for promotion of sports activities through National Sports Federations, training in Sports Authority of India (SAI) Centres ,support under Khelo India Scheme and for youth activities in the country through its organizations namely Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) & National Service Scheme (NSS)State-wise allocation of funds is not done in respect of the Schemes of this Ministry.
