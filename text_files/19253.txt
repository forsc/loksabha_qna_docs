                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

732






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






PMSSY Projects in Kerala




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the present status and plan of inauguration of PMSSY project to be implemented in Government Medical College, Calicut;(b) whether the Government has stipulated any time frame for completion of the project at the Government Medical College, Kozhikode;(c) the details of funds allocated and disbursed by the Union Government for NHM projects in Kozhikode during last four years; and(d) whether Member of Parliament has been apprised of or invited for any of the NHM projects in last four years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	The present progress of civil construction work at the PMSSY Project at Government Medical College, Calicut is 92 %.   The installation of medical equipment and setting up of Modular Operation Theatre (MOT) are also in progress.     The project therefore is not ready for inauguration.(b) :	The stipulated date of completion of the project is April, 2021.   (c):		Under the National Health Mission (NHM), Grants-in-Aid are released to the States/UTs on a lumpsum basis under the flexible pools and not for any activity and district-wise, in order to provide greater flexibility to the States/UTs to utilise these funds.  The details of Central Grants released under NHM during financial years 2016-17 to 2019-20 to the State of Kerala are as under:-Financial year	Amount  (Rs. Crore)2016-17	452.362017-18	586.522018-19	887.902019-20	836.14(d):  As informed by the Government of Kerala, Members of Parliament are invited for the inaugural functions of NHM   projects in the District.   They attend the programmes according to their availability depending upon their presence in the concerned District.
