                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1743






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Tribal Dominated Areas in Tamil Nadu




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Tribal dominated areas in Tamil Nadu have been identified/demarcated and the steps taken by the Government for improving their living conditions;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): Tribal Sub-Plan Area and Tribal Dominated District with tribal population more than 25% have been identified.  Central Government as well as State Government have been taken various initiatives for welfare and development of Scheduled Tribes in the country including Tamil Nadu.
