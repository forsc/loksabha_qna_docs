                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Transporting Foodgrains to Godowns




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the district-wise number of wheat godowns in Maharashtra especially in Parbhani Parliamentary Constituency; (b) 	whether wheat is regularly unloaded at various railway stations of Maharashtra and is transported to godowns; (c) 	if so, the facilities provided by the Government in this regard along with the area and name-wise details of the contractors to whom the contract has been given; (d) 	whether it has come to the notice of the Government that wheat under Food Corporation of India (FCI) had been kept carelessly at Parbhani railway station and animals including pigs have eaten such wheat; (e) 	if so, the names of the quilty persons mainly responsible for the said negligence; (f) 	whether any inquiry has been conducted in this regard, if so, the action taken against the culprits; and (g) 	whether any inquiry committee has been constituted in this regard, if so, the details of the report of the said committee and the action taken by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	List of District-wise number of godowns in Maharashtra Region including Parbhani Constituency with Food Corporation of India (FCI) (Owned + Hired) is at Annexure-I..……2/--2-(b) & (c): 	Yes, Sir. Facilities such as water, electricity & security arrangement etc. are provided by Railway Authorities at the Railway Goods Shed.  List of contractors is at Annexure-II.(d) to (g): 	Information in this regard was received by FCI, after which a committee was constituted by Zonal Office, FCI headed by a General Manager level officer. Committee noticed certain lapses. The existing Handling & Transport (H&T) contractor and supervisory staff of FCI were found responsible for negligence. Accordingly, following actions were taken:-1) 	Notice issued to defaulting H&T contractor for taking action as per Terms & conditions of contract. 2) 	Four employees (one Manager & three Assistants) of FCI have been put under suspension. 3) 	Matter has been taken up with Railway authorities for improving security & supervision at Railhead.***** Annexure-IANNEXUE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4458 FOR ANSWER ON 30.03.2022 IN THE LOK SABHA.List of District-wise number of godowns with FCI (owned & hired) in Maharashtra Region including Parbhani Constituency (As on 15.03.2022)SR.
