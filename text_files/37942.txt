                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Funds for North Eastern Council




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                      Will the Minister of DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION be pleased to state:(a)	the details of the funds allocated to the North Eastern Council (NEC) during the last five years;(b)	the details of the areas in which the said funds were spent during the said period;(c)	the details of the institutions and funds allocated to them since 2010 by NEC for North East Festivals;(d)	the details of funds allocated to various Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) by NEC under various schemes since 2010, NGO-wise;(e)	whether it is a fact that NEC has spent crores of rupees on schemes for the betterment of the tea garden workers of Assam; and (f)	if so, the details thereof including the total amount of funds spent by NEC in different tea gardens of Assam since 2010 till date?






ANSWER




(a)	The summary of fund allocated for NEC during the last five years is given below:(Rs. in crore)Sl. No.	Financial Year	Revised Estimates1	2017-18	1261.372	2018-19	1225.673	2019-20	1243.284	2020-21	852.525	2021-22	1133.55Total	5716.39(b)	Three schemes of MDoNER viz. “Schemes of NEC”, North East Road Sector Development schemes (NERSDS), Non-Lapsable Pool of Resources- Central (NLCPR-C) are being implemented through NEC. Broadly, the focus sectors as presently under these schemes are Bamboo, Piggery, Regional Tourism, Higher Education, Livelihood Projects, Science and Technology interventions in the NER (STINER),  Surveys & Investigations, Promotion of the North Eastern Region, road connectivity, irrigation and water management, power, art & culture etc.(c)	A total of 75 sanctions for organising North East Festival, amounting to Rs. 31.21 crore, have been issued to various institutions since 2010. The details are at Annexure I.(d)	A total of 78 projects/ other interventions amounting to Rs. 67.86 crore have been sanctioned by NEC to NGOs since 2010. The details are at Annexure II.(e) & (f) Funds approved by NEC for the betterment of the tea sector of Assam is given below:Sl. No.	Name of Project	Approved Cost(Rs. in Crore)1	Pilot Project for Development of Tea, Kiwi, Floriculture, Mushroom and Pineapple in the NERCORMP project districts including Assam 	3.802	Tea Museum at Dibrugarh, Assam 	3.14Total	6.94
