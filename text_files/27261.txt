                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2845






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






NHAI Projects in Karnataka




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of various projects proposed to be executed by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) in the State of Karnataka during the next three years; (b)	the length of new National Highways constructed in the State during the last three years; (c)	whether any of these projects connects Karnataka with another State and if so, the details thereof; and (d)	whether there is any financial crunch for development of such projects in the State and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)       The details are as under:  Year	No of Projects	Length (in Km.)2021-22	13	561.172022-23	15	702.85Beyond 2022-23	19	880.87(b)	The details of highway length constructed in the State of Karnataka during last three years are as under: Year	Length completed (in Km)2018-19	7792019-20	5002020-21	425 (c)	Two Projects (i) 4-laning of Bellary - Byrapura section of NH-150A (Package-I) (ii) 4/6 laning of Hospet – Bellary – Karnataka / AP Border of NH-63.  (d)	There is no financial crunch.  However, one project i.e. 4-laning of Hospet –Bellary Section is delayed due to financial issues of the Contractor. Relief under Atmanirbhar Scheme has been extended to the Contractor and the present physical progress is 52.74%.
