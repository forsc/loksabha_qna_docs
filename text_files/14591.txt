                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

295






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Madhav National Park




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to develop Madhav National Park in Shivpuri district of Madhya Pradesh and resume tiger safari project in the park; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the time by which the said project is likely to be initiated?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a)to (c) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. ***STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a), (b)AND  (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *295 REGARDING “MADHAV NATIONAL PARK” BY DR. KRISHNA PAL SINGH YADAV DUE FOR REPLY ON 13.03.2020(a)to (c) The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change provides funds to Protected Areas in the country under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme- ‘Development of Wildlife Habitats’. Accordingly, an amount of Rs. 100.44 lakhs has been sanctioned for  Madhav National Park in Madhya Pradesh under this scheme during the year 2019-2020.Further, the Ministry has not received any proposal from the State Government of Madhya Pradesh for resuming Tiger Safari project in Madhav National Park.  	***
