                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

815






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Progress under Saubhagya




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of progress of Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana- (Saubhagya), State-wise;(b)	the total number of beneficiaries under Saubhagya Scheme in Chhattisgarh, district-wise including Bilaspur, Mungeli and Gaurela-Pendra- Marwahi;(c)	the number of families yet to be given the benefits as on date; and(d)	the details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred during the last three years and the current year under Saubhagya, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a)	The Government of India had launched Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana – Saubhagya in October, 2017 with the objective of achieving universal household electrification. The scheme entailed providing electricity connections to all un-electrified households in rural areas and all poor households in urban areas in the country. Under the Saubhagya scheme, as on 31.03.2021, all the States have reported 100% electrification of all the willing un-electrified households, identified before 31.03.2019. As reported by the States, 2.817 crore households have been electrified since the launch of Saubhagya, up to 31.03.2021. State-wise details are as per Annexure – I.(b)	District-wise details of the total number of Beneficiaries under the Saubhagya Scheme in Chhattisgarh, including Bilaspur, Mungeli and Gaurela–Pendra-Marwahi are as per Annexure – II.(c)	The State of Chhattisgarh has reported that there are no households that are yet to be given the benefits under Saubhagya Scheme as on date.(d)	Year-wise details of funds allocated and funds released to the States during the last three years and the current year under Saubhagya are as per Annexure – III.
