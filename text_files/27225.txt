                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2910






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Jawaharlal Nehru Port




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that Jawaharlal Nehru Port is facing revenue loss and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons for such revenue loss;(b)	whether the Government has any plan to review the functioning of Jawaharlal Nehru Port in near future; and (c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	No, Sir.(b)&(c) Functioning of Major Ports in the country, which inter alia includes review of expansion, modernization and upgradation, is an ongoing process to keep the ports abreast with new technologies and also to promote trade and cargo.                                                  *****
