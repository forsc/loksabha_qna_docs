                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Electric Buses




Midhun Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has approved/sanctioned 5595 electric buses to 64 Cities/State Govt. Entities/STUs recently for intra-city and inter-city operation under phase-II of the FAME India Scheme; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the number of buses allotted to the State of Andhra Pradesh in the current financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                 (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)                          ********(a) & (b): Yes, Sir, the Government has sanctioned 5095 electric buses to 64 Cities / State Transport Corporations for intra-city operation; 400 electric buses for intercity operation and 100 electric buses for last mile connectivity to Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) under Phase-II of FAME India Scheme. The number of e-buses allotted to different cities of the State of Andhra Pradesh are as given below: -S.No.	Name of City	E-Bus allocated1.	Visakhapatnam	1002.	Vijayawada	503.	Amravati	504.	Tirupati	505.	Kakinada	506.	Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation- Inter-City Operations	50Total	350 	The expenditure of Rs. 175 crore (approx.) for purchase of these buses is being born by the Department of Heavy Industry.                                *****
