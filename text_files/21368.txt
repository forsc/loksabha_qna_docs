                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2617






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Internet Facility in Bihar




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has provided internet and Wi-Fi facilities in villages of the country particularly in Bihar for the benefit of farmers with a view to enhance their knowledge in agro-sector and if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	the action being taken by the Government to enhance internet facility in the rural and remote areas of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	& (b) 	Internet is being provided in rural areas by the Government and the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) through 3G/4G wireless mobile and fixed wireline broadband. In addition to this, Government of India is implementing BharatNet project to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) (approx. 2,50,000) in the country. As a part of BharatNet project, the Last Mile connectivity is being provided through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband technology.	The status of Internet facility in rural & remote areas of the country including Bihar, as on 26.02.2021, is as follows:<pre>S.N 	Parameter 	In the country	In Bihar1.	No of inhabited villages (as per Census 2011) having 3G or 4G mobile wireless coverage	5,58,537out of5,97,618 villages	38,733out of39,073 villages2.	Service Ready GPs (including BHQs) with broadband infrastructure	1,54,096	8,8213.	Number of GPs where Wi-Fi hotspots have been installed	1,03,400	5,345</pre>	Further, Government has created mKisan Portal to provide free advisory services to the farmers and all others associated with the farming and allied activities. More than 5 crore farmers including farmers from Bihar are registered on the portal and getting benefitted from this service.************
