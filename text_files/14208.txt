                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2897






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Export of Tejas Fighter Jet




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Chirag Paswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has any proposal to sell Tejas Combat Aircraft to other countries in near future;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) the details of timeline of any such proposal in near future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  to (d):  Yes, Sir.  HAL is currently producing Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) – Tejas, to meet Indian Air Force requirements.  In order to export LCA to other friendly countries, the following activities have been carried out:(i)	The platform has been showcased by way of flying displays at various foreign exhibitions and also briefing about the platform has been given to various foreign delegations who have visited HAL facilities.(ii)	The company has responded to Request for Information (RFI) from overseas.  Formal Request for Quotation (RFQ) has not been received in order to process the proposal.*****
