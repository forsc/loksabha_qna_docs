                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

79






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Survey of Tribal Communities




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any study/survey has been conducted/proposed to be conducted to find out the population of various tribal communities living in forest areas of the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the base/criteria adopted by the Government for introducing welfare measures for the tribal people?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (b):	No such study/survey has been conducted during last five years to find out the population of various tribal people living in forest areas of the country. There is no proposal under consideration of the Ministry for conducting new study for the same.In reply to Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.2487 answered on 31.07.2019, the information regarding total number of tribals and other traditional dwellers living in forest villages in the country, State/UT-wise has been given as at Annexure. http://164.100.24.220/loksabhaquestions/annex/12/AU2487.pdf(c)	The basic criteria for adopting welfare measures for tribal people is to bring the tribal population at par with other social groups in the country by reducing existing gaps in various sector of development as well as raising the level of administration in the tribal dominated areas, as enshrined in the Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India.*****
