                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1643






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Curbing Child Labour




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the new initiatives undertaken by the Government have been successful in curbing the Child Labour;(b)if so, the comparative data during the last three years and current year; and (c)the time by which child labour is likely to be eliminated completely?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	As per 2011 Census, the number of main workers  in the age group  of 5-14 years  in the country  is 43.53 lakh  which shows a decline  from 57.79 lakh as per  2001 Census. 			The details of number of children rescued/withdrawn from work, rehabilitated and mainstreamed under National Child Labour Project (NCLP) Scheme during the last three years and current year are as under:  Year	 Number of children rescued/withdrawn from work, rehabilitated and mainstreamed under National Child Labour Project (NCLP) Scheme 2016-17	 30979 2017-18	 47635 2018-19	 50284 2019-20 (till date) 	 46198 Since child labour is a result of socio-economic conditions, a definite timeline for elimination of child labour cannot be ascertained.*****
