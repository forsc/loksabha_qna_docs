                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

598






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Development of Qualitative Plantation Materials




Rodmal Nagar













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the new steps taken by the Government for developing qualitative plantation materials of medicinal plants and herbs of different Agri-climatic regions in the country, State/UT-wise including Gujarat;b)	whether any action plan has been prepared by the National Medicinal Plants Board for promoting the farming and production of medicinal plants and herbs in the country; andc)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a): Yes Sir & Madam, The National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB), Ministry of Ayush as per guideline’s provision of “Central Sector Scheme for Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants” has established 7 Regional Cum Facilitation Centres in different geographical regions of the country.  Through these Regional Cum Facilitation Centres, the financial and technical assistance is provided to farmers / other stakeholders for development of Quality Planting Material (QPM) of medicinal plants and herbs of Region/State specific needs including the state of Gujarat.And, under this scheme project based support is also provided for creation of Nursery/Quality Planting Material (QPM) throughout the country. Total 24 projects have been supported under Nursery/QPM component of this scheme to the various organizations/Institutions. Details of projects supported for Nurseries/Quality Planting Material during five years is placed at Annexure I.(b) &(c): Yes, Ministry of AYUSH has prepared a draft scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri VRIKSH AYUSH Yojana” for the promotion of Medicinal Plant Cultivation. The scheme is submitted for the approval of Cabinet.
