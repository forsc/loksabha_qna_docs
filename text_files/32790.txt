                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

83






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Textile Parks




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has approved the construction of any textile parks in Uttar Pradesh; (b) if so, the details thereof along with timeline set for this purpose; (c) whether Uttar Pradesh has the potential of emerging as a major textile hub in India and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government is considering any further projects for development of textiles in Uttar Pradesh; (e) if so, the details thereof; and (f) the steps taken by the Government recently for the development of the textile sector?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	No proposal for construction of Textile Park under Scheme for Integrated Textile Parks (SITP) from the state Government of Uttar Pradesh is pending in the Ministry.(c) to (e):	The programmes/ schemes run by the Ministry of Textiles are not state specific and run on pan-India basis.(f):	Steps taken by the Government for development of textile sector including the state of Uttar Pradesh are:-(i)  Production Linked Incentive Scheme for textiles for Man Made Fibre, Apparel, Fabrics and Technical Textiles. It is expected to attract investment of Rs. 19000 crore for manufacturing of notified product and will be able to provide employment opportunity for 7.5 lakh persons.(ii)  PM- MITRA Park Scheme to set up 7 Mega Textiles Manufacturing Parks in the country. This will reduce logistics cost and will improve Competitiveness of Indian textile manufacturing. Once completed one such park is expected to provide employment to 1 lakh persons directly and 2 lakh persons indirectly.(iii) Apart from this, the Government is also running Schemes viz. Amended Technology Up-gradation Scheme (ATUFS), Integrated Processing Development Scheme (IPDS), Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP), National Technical Textile Mission (NTTM), etc. for holistic development of textile sector.***
