                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

627






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Promotions in CGPDTM




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that in the office of the Controller General of Patents, Designs and Trade Marks (CGPDTM) Data entry Operators have been working at the same post since 2011 and neither they got any promotion nor is there any channel for their promotion;(b) 	whether it is true that the department had decided three years back to merge the post of Data Entry Operator with the post of UDC;(c) 	whether it is true that rules for recruitment to various posts in the office has not been updated since many years as per DOPT rules; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and the since by which pending cases of the employees working on the post the said Data Entry Operator will be decided?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):	Sir, the Data Entry Operators (DEO) selected by the Staff Selection Commission, in the office of Patents, Designs and Trade Marks have been working as per existing Recruitment Rules (RRs) for the post of DEO. (b):  	Sir. No decision to merge the post of DEO with Upper Division Clerk has been taken.  (c)  	Sir, the RRs for various posts in the Office of Patents, Designs and Trade Marks have been revised as per the requirement from time to time.  (d): 	Sir, details of the RRs for various posts in the Office of Patents, Designs and Trade Marks as revised from time to time are at Annexure-I and no such case of the DEOs is pending with the Department. ******
