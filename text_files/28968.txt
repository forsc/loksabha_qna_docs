                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

436






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the attention of the Government is drawn to the Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme aimed at promotion of Entrepreneurship Development and Employment Generation Programme for development of animal husbandry and dairy industry in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the number of entrepreneurs who availed the opportunities in the last three years particularly in Andhra Pradesh; (d) the quantum of financial assistance being given under this scheme and the details thereof; and (e) the status of the programme as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) & (b) Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS) was implemented by Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Government of India through National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) from September 2010 to March 2020. (c) & (d) State-wise number of beneficiaries and back-ended subsidy disbursed by NABARD under DEDS including in the State of Andhra Pradesh during the last three years are given at Annexure. (e) The scheme has been discontinued from April 2020.****
