                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2565






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Reproductive and Child Health Flexi Pool




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the details of the break-up of fund allocations and expenditure for family planning activities under Reproductive and Child Health (RCH) flexi pool during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise; and (b) the details of break-up of allocations and expenditure for each activity under family planning such as permanent methods, spacing methods, training, IEC/ BCC, procurement of equipment, drugs and consumables, ASHA incentives, and other activities under the RCHflexi pool of NHM during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) & (b):   Under the flagship programme of National Health Mission (NHM), the allocations are made under the various flexible pools to provide greater flexibility to States to utilize the funds as per their felt need and priorities. Separate State/UT wise fund allocations in respect of Family Planning activities are not made. The activity-wise break up of expenditure reported regarding permanent methods, spacing methods, trainings, IEC/BCC, procurement of equipment, drugs and consumables, ASHA incentives and others by the States/UTs under RCH flexible pool of NHM during the last three years i.e. 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 are given at Annexure- I to Annexure-III respectively.……………………
