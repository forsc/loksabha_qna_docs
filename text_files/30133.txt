                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1658






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Violation of Consumer Rights




Nalin Kumar Kateel













                                                    Sumalatha Ambareesh
                                                








                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the number of cases of violation of consumer rights is increasing in the country; (b)	if so, the details for the last three years, year wise and state-wise; (c)	the measures taken to create awareness among consumers to protect their interests; and (d)	the steps taken against violators of the consumers rights? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b) :  The details of the cases filed in the Consumer Commission in the country is at Annexure.(c)	   : Department of Consumer Affairs generates consumer awareness through video spots, banners, hoardings, posters etc. on issues of like salinet features of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, Packaged Commodities Rules, Hallmarking, consumer grievance redressal mechanism etc. Awareness and advocacy is done inter-alia through the departmental website, Panchayat Bhavans, Common Service Centers etc. Other Central Government Departments like Ministry of Railways & Department of Posts as also State Governments/UT Administrations are also involved in dissemination of consumer awareness messages. (d)	  : The Consumer Protection Act, 2019 provides for establishment of a three tier quasi-judicial mechanism – District Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission and National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, where consumers can file consumer complaints for redressal.  The concerned Consumer Commissions adjudicate on these cases and pass orders as per due procedure.
