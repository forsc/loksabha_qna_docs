                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Export of Steel




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi













                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantity of steel being exported and the revenue earned therefrom during the last two years, country-wise;(b)	whether the export-oriented policy of steel is the primary cause for escalation of steel prices in the domestic market;(c)	if so, the details thereof and the corrective measures taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to bring down the prices;(d)	whether the Government is aware that the Steel industry needs to be upgraded with the latest modern and world class technology to improve its production capacity; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	Data on overall export of total steel - both volume and value - in last two years is placed at Annexure-I.(b)&(c): No, Sir. Steel is a deregulated sector and Government has no role in steel price regulation. Steel is a completely deregulated sector and market driven.(d) and (e): Indian steel industry uses latest technology in steel making and Indian steel companies feature among the top steel companies of the world. The issue of technology upgradation are techno-commercial decisions of steel companies and Government has no role in such decisions. Ministry of Steel also funds certain Research & Development (R&D) projects for the steel sector.********
