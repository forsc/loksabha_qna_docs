                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5130






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






RRBs in Rajasthan




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	the details of the number of Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) in Rajasthan;(b) 	whether the Government is conducting the recruitment examination of these banks in the local / regional language and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government is considering to conduct the recruitment examination of public sector banks in the regional language with a view to provide equal opportunities to the youth of the local / rural areas;(d)  	if so, the details thereof; and (e)  	the time by which it is likely to be implemented?	






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of Finance(a) to (e)  Baroda Rajasthan Kshetriya Gramin Bank (BRKGB) and Rajasthan Marudhara Gramin Bank (RMGB) are the two RRBs functioning in the state of Rajasthan.  BRKGB with its Head Office at Ajmer, is operating with 12 Regional Offices & network of 861 branches in 21 districts of Rajasthan.  Similarly, RMGB with its Head Office at Jodhpur, is operating with 14 Regional Offices and network of 701 branches in 15 districts of Rajasthan.  The examinations for direct recruitment of Officers (Scale-I) and Office Assistant (Multipurpose) in RRBs, includingthese banks are being conducted through Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) in 13 regional languages (Assamese, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Konkani, Malayalam, Manipuri, Marathi, Oriya, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu and Urdu), in addition to English and Hindi.	In respect of Public Sector Banks (PSBs), Government vide letter dated 22nd October, 2021 has issued instructions to all PSBs to conduct examination for recruitment for clerical cadre in 13 regional languages, as mentioned above, in addition to English and Hindi.*****
