                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1274






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Jan Shikshan Sansthans




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	the number of Jan Shikshan Sansthans (JSS) in the country, State-wise; (b) 	whether the new guidelines published aligning JSS with National Skills Qualification Framework has been implemented across all JSS;	(c) 	the number of beneficiaries who have availed benefits through JSS; (d) 	the variety of jobs, the beneficiaries have secured post vocational training through JSS; and(e)    	whether the Government has put in place a system of incentives to reward better performing JSS and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)  	There are 248 Jan Shikshan Sansthans (JSS) in the country. State-wise list of JSSs attached at Annexure-A.(b) 	Yes, Sir. JSSs are conducting National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF) aligned   courses.(c)  	The number of beneficiaries trained by JSSs during 2018-19 & 2019-20 (up-to Jan-2020) is: 2018-19 :  1,67,2632019-20 :   2,40,315(d)  	Keeping in view the objective of supplementing the income level of the target group, the JSSs imparts training to the beneficiaries for their livelihood linkages, primarily in the skill-sets required in the informal sector of employment/wage-employment viz. cutting & tailoring, beauty culture & heath care, electrical technician, plumbing, refrigeration and air conditioning, etc. (e)  	Annual Recurring Grants-in-Aid to JSSs are released after taking into consideration their overall performance. In addition, Government is also actively considering incentivizing good performing JSSs.
