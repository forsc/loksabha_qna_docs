                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2060






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






PM Kisan Yojana in Rajasthan




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of number of identified beneficiaries, amount and installments provided under the Pradhan  Mantri  Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana to the Rajasthan, the details thereof, year and district-wise; (b) 	whether the payment of all instalments have been made under the said schemes in Rajasthan and if not, the time by which such instalments are likely to be paid to the eligible beneficiaries; and (c) 	whether the Government intends to take stringent steps to include more eligible farmers by conducting a phased campaign under the said scheme in Rajasthan and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):    Under PM-KISAN Scheme, benefits of Rs. 6000/- per annum in three equal installments of Rs. 2000/- every four months is provided to eligible farmers after receipt of corrected data from States/UTs and their validation through Aadhaar/PFMS. The district-wise and period-wise details of the beneficiaries registered on the PM-KISAN portal and benefits provided in various installments as on 27-02-2020in the State of Rajasthan are at Annexure-I.(b):	PM-KISAN is a continuous and ongoing Scheme under which the funds are transferred to the eligible beneficiaries as identified by the respective States/UTs. Once the data of the beneficiaries has been uploaded by the States/UTs and has passed all the levels of validations and verifications, the payments are then made to these beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode. The benefits provided so far in the State of Rajasthan is indicated at Annexure-I.(c):	   Government of India has requested the States/UTs, including Rajasthan to organize camps for registration of new farmers under the Scheme and for correction of the data of farmers already registered under the Scheme.The progress of implementation of the Scheme in the country including Rajasthan is monitored every week through Video Conference.
