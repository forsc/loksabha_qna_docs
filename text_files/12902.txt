                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1795






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Preservation of Paintings and Caves




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that due to the lack of maintenance the paintings, caves and the religious places of historical importance are in dilapidated condition;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the names of the States in the country including Bihar from which the proposals regarding preservation of religious places, painting and caves of historical importance have been received; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the amount allocated annually  for their maintenance along with year-wise details of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)	No Sir, conservation work of protected monuments comprising caves, religious and other places, paintings etc. is attended by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) regularly as per the availability of the resources and they are in a good state of preservation.(c) & (d)	No such proposals have been received.
