                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Four Year Integrated Teacher Education Programme




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently notified a Four Year Integrated Teacher Education Programme;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the said programme is proposed to be implemented on pilot mode inselected institutions across the country;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of the institutes from the State of Andhra Pradesh selected for the implementation of this pilot programme?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (e): A statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF LOKSABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 104 FOR REPLY ON 6TH DECEMBER,2021 ASKED BY HON’BLE MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT SHRI POCHABRAHMANANDA REDDY AND SHRIMATI GODDETI MADHAVIREGRADING “FOUR YEAR INTEGRATED TEACHER EDUCATIONPROGRAMME”:(a) & (b) : Yes, Sir. National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) has notified theNCTE (recognitions, Norms and Procedure) Amendment Regulations, 2021 videGazetted notification dated 26th October, 2021 which is available athttps://ncte.gov.in/WebAdminFiles/RecentAnnouncement/0_27_10_2021_637709061829643386.pdf(c) & (d) : Yes, Sir. It is being implemented in a pilot mode in 50 selectedUniversities/Institutions.(e) : NCTE has not invited any applications for ITEP programme till date.*****
