                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Meeting on PM Kisan Yojana




K. Subbarayan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether  it  is  a  fact  that  the Government had called a high-level meeting of the State Agriculture Ministers to discuss the PM-Kisan scheme, pension scheme to farmers etc.; and(b)	 if so, the details of the discussion took place and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	 A meeting of Agriculture Ministers of all States/UTs had been held on the 13th June, 2019 under the Chairmanship of the Minister of Agriculture & Farmers’ Welfare, through Video Conferencing, to discuss (i) the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana (PM-Kisan), (ii) Pension Scheme for Small and Marginal Farmers and (iii) Kisan Credit Cards (KCC). The States/UTs were urged to expedite the process of enrollment of all eligible farmers’ families under the PM-Kisan Scheme in a time-bound manner. They were further informed about the rolling out of the pension scheme for farmers and requested to create awareness therefor. They were also requested to organise village-wise campaign for increased coverage of KCC Scheme.  ********
