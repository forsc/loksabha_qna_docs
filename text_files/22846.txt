                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3741






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






SAROD - Ports




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Union Government has launched Society for Affordable Redressal of Disputes - Ports (SAROD-Ports); (b)   if so, the details thereof; (c)   whether the SAROD-Ports will ensure the right kind of environment and inspire confidence in private players to promote ease of doing business in the Maritime sector; and (d)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d):  Yes, Sir. In order to seek speedy, affordable, just and reasonable Redressal of Dispute/Differences between Major Port Trusts and Concessionaires/ Contractors arising out of and during the course of execution of various contracts, a Society for Affordable Resolution of Disputes - Ports (SAROD-Ports) has been formed under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 with registration No. S-E/1715/ Distt. South East/2020 dated 30th January, 2020.  The formation of SAROD - Ports aims at enhancing confidence of concessionaires and promoting ease of doing business in the Maritime Sector.                                                 ******
