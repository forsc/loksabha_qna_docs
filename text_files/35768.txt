                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3082






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Complaints Received through CPGRAMS




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number and details of complaints received by the Government regarding misuse of funds, corruption from the State of Tamil Nadu, district-wise;(b)	whether the respective State Governments send a copy of action taken on complaint received which were through Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System (CPGRAMS) and if so, the details thereof and if not reasons thereof;(c)	whether the Government has received two sixty-eight complaints through CPGRAMS; and(d)	If so, the details thereof, complaint-wise and the officers suspended;






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL) (a)	A statement showing district-wise complaints with allegations of corruptions and misuse funds received from the State of Tamil Nadu since 2018 till date is given in the Annexure.(b)	The complaints received through Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System (CPGRAMS) are forwarded to the concerned States including the State of Tamil Nadu for appropriate action as Panchayat is a State subject.(c)	& (d) During the last three years 268 number of complaints with allegations of corruptions and misuse funds were received from various States including the State of Tamil Nadu and those were forwarded to the respective States for appropriate action including suspension of officers on establishment of corruption charges as the Panchayat is a State subject. No such cases of suspension of officers has been reported to the Ministry of Panchayati Raj.  ***
