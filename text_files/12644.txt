                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Warehouse Infrastructure Fund




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the total allocation made by the Government for Warehouse Infrastructure Fund for the last three years; (b) 	whether the Government has sanctioned financial assistance from Warehouse Infrastructure Fund for the States to construct new warehouses mainly to prevent post harvest losses; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof and the total number of scientific warehousing storage houses developed under this fund in the last three years, State-wise including the State of Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Warehouse Infrastructure Fund (WIF) was constituted in National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) with a corpus of Rs. 5,000 crore for the year 2013-14. No allocation was made towards WIF during the last three years. The last allocation was made during the Financial Year 2014-15 to the tune of Rs. 5000 crore. (b) & (c):	Under WIF, NABARD has sanctioned financial assistance to 20 State Governments for constructions of warehouses. The details of projects sanctioned and fund sanctioned under WIF to State Governments is at Annexure-I. The State-wise number of scientific warehousing storage houses developed under this fund in the last three years including State of Tamil Nadu is enclosed at Annexure-II.
