                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1023






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Status of Bhogapuram Airport




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a): the progress of setting up of Bhogapuram airport in Andhra Pradesh;(b): whether Airports Authority of India (AAI) would continue its operations from Visakhapatnam airport inspite of Bhogapuram coming into being; and(c): if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Government of India (GoI), Ministry of Civil Aviation (MoCA) has granted approval to Bhogapuram International Airport Corporation Limited (BIACL) for setting up of new Greenfield Airport for public use in Vizianagaram District, near Visakhapatnam on 07.10.2016.  ;(b) & (c): As per the approval granted by GoI, MoCA, the scheduled operations at the existing Vizag Naval Airfield will be stopped for a period of 30 years with effect from the date of commencement of operations at the proposed Greenfield airport at Bhogapuram.
