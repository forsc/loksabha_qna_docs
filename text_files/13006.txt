Proposal for Welfare Schemes in Maharashtra




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †1966TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.03.2020PROPOSAL FOR WELFARE SCHEMES IN MAHARASHTRA †1966. SHRI PRATAPRAO PATIL CHIKHLIKAR:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the number of proposals received by the Union Government from the State Government of Maharashtra during the last three years and the current year along with the number of sanctioned and pending proposals; (b) the details of the pending proposals and the time by which these are likely to be cleared; and (c) the total amount spent under various schemes in Maharashtra during the said period, scheme and year-wise?
