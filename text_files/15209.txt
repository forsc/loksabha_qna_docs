                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3799






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Growth in Rural Wages




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the percentage of growth in rural wages after adjusting for inflation during the last three years and the current year, month-wise;(b) 	whether the Government is of the opinion that the current rural wage rates are adequate to maintain a rural household;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	if not, the measures being taken by the Government to increase rural wages?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As per the information shared by Ministry of Labour & Employment, Labour Bureau compiles and maintains Consumer Price Index Numbers for Agricultural and Rural Labourers (Base 1986-87=100) (CPI-AL & RL) and average daily wage rate data in respect of selected agricultural and non-agricultural occupations based on the data collected by the Field Operation Division (FOD) of National Sample Survey Office (NSSO) form a fixed set of 600 sample villages spread over 20 states. Month-wise wage rate & percentage of growth rate in wage rate w.r.t previous year in respect of Non Agriculture Occupation along with CPL-RL with effect from January 2016 onwards is at Annexure-I.(b) to (d): As per section 6(1) of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), 2005, the Ministry of Rural Development notifies Mahatma Gandhi NREGA wage rates every year for States/UTs based on Consumer Price Index for Agricultural Labour (CPI-AL) published by Labour Bureau, Shimla to compensate MGNREGA workers against inflation. The wage rates are made applicable from 1st April of each Financial Year.  State/UT-wise wage rate under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA during the financial year 2016-17 to 2019-20 is given at Annexure-II.
