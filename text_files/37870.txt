                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Training Centres for PVTGs




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the number of operational Vocational Training Centres with placement facilities for Tribals including Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs) in Andhra Pradesh;b)	the number of beneficiaries from PVTGs benefitted from the Vocational Training Centres in the State;c)	 whether there is a proposal to set up new training Centres in the State; and d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to(d):        A statement is laid on the Table of the House. ***** Statement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *458 for answer on 04.04.2022 (a) to (d):  As informed by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, 23 Youth Training Centres are functioning in the state for providing training to all STs including PVTGs, and 5,132 students have been trained in the training centers from  2015-16 to 2021-22.  Further, Government of Andhra Pradesh has informed that Tribal Welfare Department (TRICOR) has taken up many initiatives like Direct Placements, Pre-Examination Coaching, Training & Placements, Self-Employment (Entrepreneurship Development Trainings, Contractor Development Program etc.), Bhavitha programs in collaboration with Andhra Pradesh State Skill Development Corporation (APSSDC). An MoU was entered with APSSDC on 22nd August, 2015 as strategic partner to impart training in various sectors to ST youth at all (23) YTCs. Consequent on entering MoU with the APSSDC has conducted self-employment Training to the unemployed ST youth in all (23) YTCs.
