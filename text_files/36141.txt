                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Evacuation of Citizens from Ukraine




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to introduce a scheme for evacuation of Indian citizens from foreign countries during emergency situation like war disaster etc;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government arranged flights for the evacuation of Indian citizens from Ukraine;(d) if so, the details of the action taken thereon;(e) whether the Government evacuated all Indian citizens in Ukraine free of cost and if so, the details thereof;(f) the details regarding the number of citizens evacuated from Ukraine without levying flight charge;(g) the details of flight services operated from neighboring countries of Ukraine for the evacuation of Indian citizens; and(h) the total expenditure of the Union Government for arranging flight service for the Indian citizens in connection with the evacuation process?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) and (b) In case of an outbreak of conflict or any other exigencies, the evacuation of Indian citizens is planned on a case-to-case basis, based on a number of factors, like number and location of Indian nationals within the country, security situation on ground and availability of evacuation/exit routes within the country and those with neighbouring countries. Appropriate advisories are issued by the Indian missions in such situations so that the Indian citizens can move out of the conflict areas in time.(c) to (h) Around 22,500 Indian nationals have returned from Ukraine to India from 1 February - 11 March, 2022. 90 evacuation flights were operated under Operation Ganga, including 14 Indian Air Force flights. The details of the flights operated under Operation Ganga are given at Annexure.The air fare for all the flights operated under Operation Ganga has been borne entirely by the Government of India.******
