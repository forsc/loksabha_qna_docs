                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2650






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Coal Mines




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of coal mines in Uttar Pradesh;(b) whether any new coal reserves has been explored in Uttar Pradesh and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any coal mining scheme is also pending for approval; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)  (a): There are 4 number coal mines of Coal India Limited in Uttar Pradesh. (b): No Sir. (c): No Sir. (d): Does not arise in view of reply to part (c) above. ***
