                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

17






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






New Kendriya Vidyalayas in Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to set up new Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in Sindhudurg and Ratnagiri Districts of Maharashtra;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the criteria laid down for opening of such new KVs?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.******STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 17 RAISED BY SHRI VINAYAK RAUT, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT TO BE ANSWERED ON 19TH JULY 2021 REGARDING NEW KENDRIYA VIDYALAYAS IN MAHARASHTRA.(a) to (d) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has not received any proposal in the prescribed proformae for opening of a new KV in Sindhudurg and Ratnagiri Districts from the State Government of Maharashtra.Opening of Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) is a continuous process. KVs are opened primarily to cater to the educational needs of the wards of transferable Central Government Employees including Defence & Para-Military personnel, Central Autonomous Bodies, Central Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) and Central Institutes of Higher Learning (IHL) by providing a common programme of education. Proposals for opening of new KVs are considered only if sponsored by Ministries or Departments of the Government of India / State Governments / Union Territories (UTs) Administrations and committing resources for setting up a new KV. The proposals received from various sponsoring authorities for opening of new KVs have to compete with other proposals under the “Challenge Method”.******
