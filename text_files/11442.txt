                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

249






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Shortage of Urea




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is huge shortage of urea in the country and the farmers are not able to carry out their farming activities;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c)  whether it is true that heavy rains boost the demand for fertilizers in rice growing areas of Kerala and other parts of the country; and(d) if so, the steps taken by the Government to cater to the demand of urea in those States?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a):    No, Sir.(b):    In view of (a) above, question does not arise.(c) & (d):  Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) assesses the requirement of Fertilizers on the season to season basis. Before each season i. e Rabi and Kharif, DAC&FW organizes Zonal Conferences with States to assess the requirement of fertilizers. The requirement for the ongoing Rabi 2019-20 season was assessed in September 2019 during the Zonal conference.Due to good rains and soil moisture, there is an increase in the requirement of fertilizer for the Rabi,2019-20 in comparison to 2018-19  in most of the states. The details are summarized as under:-<figures in’LMT>Name of the Product	Requirement assessed for Rabi 2018-19	Requirement assessed for Rabi 2019-20Urea	                162.74	                                182.04DAP	                50.46	                                52.09MOP	                17.28	                                17.74Complexes	        52.19	                                51.87 The State wise requirement of fertilizers assessed for Rabi 2019-20 and Rabi 2018-19 are placed at Annexure –I. Further, the availability of fertilizer is regularly monitored during the weekly video conference. In case of any shortfall, efforts are made to ensure adequate availability.******
