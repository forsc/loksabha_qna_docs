                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

778






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Financial Assistance to Jhansi For Creation of Infrastructure




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has provided any assistance to Uttar Pradesh specially in Jhansi for creation of infrastructure to increase tourist inflow in the State;(b) 	if so, the details thereof during each of the last three years and the current year, project-wise;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to provide assistance to the State in the current year; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 				(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d): 	Development of tourism infrastructure is primarily the responsibility of the concerned State Government/ Union Territory Administration. However, Ministry of Tourism provides Financial Assistance to State Governments/Union Territory Administrations/Central Agencies for tourism development under its Schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’, ‘Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive’ (PRASHAD) and ‘Assistance to Central Agencies’. The assistance is provided in consultation with the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations/ Central Agencies and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc. Providing financial assistance to the states under the schemes for infrastructure related development is an ongoing process. Development of Jhansi is not included in the projects sanctioned under the above schemes in Uttar Pradesh. The details of the projects sanctioned in the State of Uttar Pradesh by the Ministry of Tourism under the schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’, ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive’ (PRASHAD) and ''Assistance to Central Agencies'' including for last three years is as mentioned below.
