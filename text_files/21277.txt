                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2377






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Foreign Support to Terrorists




Prataprao  Jadhav













                                                    Gopal Chinayya Shetty
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the terrorist organizations active in the country and the number of organizations which have received foreign support out of the said organizations;(b) the number of terrorists killed during the last three years and the current year till date;(c) the number of people victimized by the terrorists during the said period till date, State-wise;(d) the steps taken by the Government to stop terrorism till date; and(e) the extent to which success has been achieved so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) : The Government has declared 42 organisations as terrorist organisations and listed their names in the First Schedule of the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act, 1967. Terrorism in India has largely been sponsored from across the border.(b) & (c) : The numbers of the terrorists and persons killed during terrorist attacks in the hinterland of the country and Jammu and Kashmir during the last three years and current year is at Annexure-I.	-2-L.S.US.Q NO. 2377 FOR 09.03.2021(d) & (e): The Government has taken various steps in this regard including strengthening of legal framework, streamlining of intelligence mechanism, setting up of National Investigation Agency (NIA) for investigation and prosecution of terror related cases, having various hubs of National Security Guards (NSG), stepping up border and coastal security, modernization of police forces and capacity building of state police forces, etc. Due to concerted and coordinated efforts of all the stakeholders, terrorism related violence has been contained to a large extent in the country.                                                 *****
