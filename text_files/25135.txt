                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1137






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Privatisation of PSBs




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that the Government is considering to privatise nationalised banks and the bidding in this regard will commence with privatisation of two such banks during the current year;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether it is also true that the Government has set a target of Rs.1.75 lakh crore for 2021-22 from disinvestment, which includes the sale of two banks;(d)	whether it is also true that the target of the Government is to privatise around a dozen public sector banks; and(e)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(a) to (e): The Budget Estimate for disinvestment in the financial year (FY) 2021-22 has been set at Rs. 1,75,000 crore. The Finance Minister, in her speech on the Union Budget for the FY 2021-22, announced Government’s intention to take up privatisation of two PSBs, after legislative amendments and also announced approval of a policy of strategic disinvestment of Public Sector Enterprises (PSEs). As per the New PSE policy for Atmanirbhar Bharat issued by the Department of Investment and Public Asset Management, NITI Aayoghas been mandated to make recommendations with regard to Central PSEs under Strategic Sectors, which includes Banking, Insurance and Financial Services Sectorsas well ason Central PSEs to be retained under Government control or considered for, inter alia, privatisation or merger or subsidiarisation with another PSE,for subsequent approval by an Alternative Mechanism that has been approved by the Government.***
