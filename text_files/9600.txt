                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3141






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Akashvani in DTH




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Akashvani has not been included in the Direct-to-home (DTH) free channel platform; (b) 	whether radio channels have very limited access; (c) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (d) 	whether any time limit has been fixed to include Akashvani in the free channel platform of DTH? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (d):	Prasar Bharati has informed that 37 Radio channels of Akashvani are available on Prasar Bharati’s “DD Free Dish” free to air DTH platform, which can be received throughout the country except in Andaman & Nicobar Islands. *****
