                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Digitisation of Banswada State Museum




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the action taken by the Government to initiate the process of digitisation in museums;(b)	the details of the works proposed to be undertaken for digitization in Banswada State Museum;(c)	 the time by which these works are likely to be completed along with the estimated cost thereof;(d)	 whether any deadline has been fixed to complete this task; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM (PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL )(a): 	The Ministry of Culture has taken up the task of digitisation of collections of ten museums under its domain through Jatan programme since April, 2014. This aside, the Ministry also operates Museum Grant Scheme under which financial assistance is provided to State Governments, Societies, Autonomous Bodies, Local Bodies and Trusts registered under the Societies Act for digitisation of art objects in the museums across the country for making their images/catalogue available over the website. (b) to (e):   There is no proposal under consideration at present in the Ministry to  undertake digitisation work in Banswada State Museum.
