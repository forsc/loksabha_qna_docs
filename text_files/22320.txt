                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3374






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Growth of Processing Sector




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that primary processing sectors will grow at 5 to 7 per cent Quarter on Quarter (QOQ) while secondary processing sectors will grow at 10-15 per cent QOQ and thereafter with a stable compound annual growth rate (CAGR) levels of 7-10 per cent and 15-20 per cent respectively for next at least five years; (b) 	the measure taken/to be taken by the Government to minimize the risk of social unrest as the sector employs intensive manpower; and (c)	as the consumer demand has to be fostered to pull up all supply chains, the details of the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regards? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a):	 Estimates of Quarterly growth of Gross Value Added (GVA) of various sectors of the economy is published by Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation (MoSPI). The Quarterly estimates of growth of GVA (at 2011-12 prices) for the manufacturing sector and overall GVA released for 2019-20 & 2020-21 are as under: Sector	2019-20	2020-21	Q1	Q2	Q3	Q1	Q2	Q3Manufacturing	0.6	-3.0	-2.9	-35.9	-1.5	1.6Overall GVA	5.0	4.6	3.4	-22.4	-7.3	1.0 MoSPI has informed that Quarterly estimates are not prepared for specific manufacturing sectors. However, the GVA for food processing sector has grown at a Compounded Annual Growth Rate of 9.85% for the 5 year period ending 2018-19 as compared to 8.21% for the manufacturing sector.(b) & (c): In order to mitigate the impact on workers particularly the migrant workers and protect them against economic disruptions during the Covid pandemic period, the Government has taken several measures to provide them financial assistance, ration and other benefits.        The Government’s package included relief measures under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana, Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Rozgar Abhiyaan, various relief measures for MSMEs, increased allocation under MGNREGA, Atmanirbhar Bharat Rozgar Yojana, additional outlay for PM Awas Yojana (Urban). Under Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan, the PM-Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PM-FME) scheme has been launched to formalise and support 2 lakh micro enterprises in the country. In addition, the Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme for MSMEs, businesses, MUDRA borrowers etc. has been extended till 31.3.2021.           All these measures are expected to help the workers, support the micro & small enterprises including in the food processing sector and boost the economy.*****
