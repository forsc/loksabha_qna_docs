                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4601






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Power Project by Neyveli Lignite Corporation




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any power project is proposed by Neyveli Lignite Corporation (NLC) at Jayakondam in Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether any project is in the offing under Neyveli Uttar Pradesh Power Limited (NUPPL) and if so, the details of the status of power purchase agreement thereof along with issues pertaining to surrender of power purchase agreements;(c) whether any new thermal power station is commissioned in the recent past at NLC and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the scheduled Commercial Operation Date (COD) has been achieved for these projects and if so, the details thereof;(e) whether any accidental death was reported in NLC during the last three years along with the negligence on the part of the authority thereto; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a): No, Sir. (b): Ghatampur Thermal Power Project (Coal fired) of Neyveli Uttar Pradesh Power Ltd.  (NUPPL- 3 units of 660 MW each) which is a subsidiary of NLCIL being implemented in UP at an estimated cost of Rs 17237.80 Cr.  The status of power purchase agreement details along with issues pertaining to surrender of power purchase agreements in respect of NUPPL are as under:  i. Power Purchase Agreement for 75% already signed with Uttar Pradesh Power Company Limited (UPPCL) for period of 25 years.ii. Ministry of Power has allocated 1843.68 MW to UP state on 02.01.2020 (136.32 MW shall remain unallocated).iii. On 11-Feb-2020, NUPPL has requested UPPCL to sign the amended PPA agreement and the case is under hearing with Uttar Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (UPERC). (c) and (d): Yes, Sir. The Neyveli New Thermal Power Project (NNTPP) - 2x500 MW, (Lignite fired) Unit-1 and Unit-2 has been commissioned in December, 2019 and February, 2021 respectively after successful completion of 72 hours full load trial operation completion and certification by SRLDC. Both the above units achieved Commercial Operation Declaration (COD) from 28.12.2019 and 10.02.2021 respectively.
