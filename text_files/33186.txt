                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

64






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Dilapidated Historical Buildings in Gujarat




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of dilapidated historical buildings in Gujarat and the number of historical buildings repaired during the last three years, year-wise;(b)	the quantum of funds allocated and spent for the maintenance of said buildings during the last three years; and(c)	the number of tourists who visited these historical buildings and monuments along with the revenue collected from these buildings and monuments during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to 	(c)     A statement is laid on the table of the House.   STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *64  FOR 07.02.2022.(a)     There are no dilapidated monuments under the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) in Gujarat. There are 203 Centrally Protected Monuments (CPM) in the state which are regularly conserved & maintained and they all are in good state of preservation. (b) 	The details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred on the conservation & maintenance of CPM’s in Gujarat  for the last three years are given as under:-                (Amount in Rs.)S. No.	Year	Allocation 	          Expenditure1.	2018-19	11,32,54,487.00	          11,32,53,487.002.	2019-20	  9,10,98,514.00	  9,10,98,514.003.	2020-21	  8,27,77,625.00	  8,27,77,625.00(c) 	The details of number of tourists and revenue collected for the last three years from the 7 ticketed monuments in Gujarat under ASI are as under:-S. No.	Year	Number of Tourists	Revenue (Amount in Rs.)1.	2018-19	  6,81,490	        2,39,10,657.002.	2019-20	 10,41,945	        3,86,97,385.003.	2020-21	  3,20,956	        2,25,13,300.00*****
