                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

838






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Increasing of Retirement Age of Doctors




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has implemented the decision regarding raising the age of retirement of all the doctors including Allopathy and AYUSH from 60 to 65 years, if so, the details thereof including the orders issued in this regard;(b) whether the entire control and funding of National Institute of Ayurveda, Jaipur and other autonomous institutions under Government of India are being looked after by his Ministry;(c) if so, whether the Government intends to raise the age of retirement of AYUSH doctors (teachers and doctors) working with the National Institutes of Ayurveda, Jaipur and other autonomous institutes under Administrative Control of AYUSH Ministry from 60 to 65years; and(d) if so, the time by which it is likely to be decided and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(DR. MUNJPARA MAHENDRABHAI)(a):	Yes, the superannuation age of General Duty Medical Officers of Central Health Service, Non-Teaching and Public Health sub cadres has been enhanced by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare w.e.f. 31.05.2016. Subsequently, the Ministry of AYUSH enhanced the age of AYUSH physicians w.e.f. 24.11.2017 in pursuance of the decision of Union Cabinet meeting dated 27.09.2017. The links of the orders dated 31.05.2016 and 24.11.2017 are https://main.mohfw.gov.in/sites/default/files/32025322481464694567.pdf  and https://cdn.ayush.gov.in/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/24-11-2017.pdf  respectively. (b):	Yes.(c) to (d):	A consolidated proposal for increasing the superannuation age of teaching and non-teaching faculties of National Institutes under Ministry of AYUSH up to 65 years has been sent to Ministry of  Health and Family Welfare for further action.
