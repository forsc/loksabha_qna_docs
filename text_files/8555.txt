                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2075






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Old Pension Scheme




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to implement old pension scheme for the new Central Government employees; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which it is likely to be implemented; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of Finance (a) to (c): No, Sir. There is no such proposal under consideration of the Government.The Government made a conscious decision to shift from the Old Pension Scheme to a Defined Contribution Pension Scheme, named as the National Pension System (NPS) due to rising and unsustainable pension bill.*********
