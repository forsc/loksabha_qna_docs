                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4281






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Promotion of Agro and Food Processing Business in Assam




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the initiatives taken by the Government for the promotion of Agro and Food Processing Business in Assam;(b)	whether the Government has taken several initiatives for backward and forward linkages for perishable agriculture products assuring quality and food safety and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the total number of food processing industries in Assam; and(d)	the number of Mega Food Parks, Integrated Cold Chain and other Value Addition Infrastructure in Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES  (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) & (b): Ministry of Food Processing Industries has been implementing a Central Sector Scheme- Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) since 2016-17 to achieve overall development of food processing sector in the country including Assam. The scheme aims to incentivize investment in creation of capacity in preservation and processing infrastructure thereby creating effective linkages of farmers with processors and markets, creating effective supply chain for perishable products, augmenting value chain of different perishables, promoting R&D and quality assurance.  Ministry is also implementing a Central Sponsored Scheme - ‘Pradhan Mantri Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises’ (PMFME), in partnership with the State/ UT Governments, under Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan with the aim to enhance the competitiveness of existing individual micro-enterprises in the unorganized segment of the food processing industry and promote formalization of the sector.  Further, a Central Sector Scheme, namely “Production Linked Incentive Scheme for Food Processing Industry (PLISFPI)” has been launched by the Ministry in the country to support creation of global food manufacturing champions commensurate with India’s natural resource endowment and support Indian brands of food products in the international markets.   (c):	As per Annual Survey of Industries (ASI) data for 2018-19, the total number of food processing industries in Assam were 1569 in registered sector, and as per National Sample Survey (NSS) 2015-16 report, there were 65,997 number of enterprises in unregistered sector in the state of Assam. (d):	One (1) Mega Food Park, six (6) Agro Processing Clusters, two (2) Integrated Cold Chain projects, eighteen (18) food processing units, one (1) Backward and Forward Linkages project and six (6) Food Testing Laboratories have been sanctioned by the Ministry in the state of Assam. *****
