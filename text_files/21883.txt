                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3083






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the manner in which Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat programme is likely to be implemented in view of the current situation of Covid-19 and the details thereof; and(b) the names of the States where the said programme is being implemented ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) In view of Covid-19, activities under Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB) programme are being carried out in online mode. The activities inter alia include learning the language of paired State, culinary exchanges, celebration of EBSB Days, webinars/ lectures on festivals/ food/ culture/ other information etc. of paired State/ UT, quiz/ essay/ painting competitions, etc. A Digital Repository has been developed on EBSB Portal for all online/ digital material developed by participating Ministries. ‘Aaj Ka Vakya’, a language learning initiative in which a sentence is shared everyday in 21 Indian languages, is being run through EBSB Portal and social media of Ministry of Education. A ‘Recipe Corner’ has also been launched where people can share recipes of their regions. Ministry of Tourism is conducting Dekho Apna Desh webinars. Department of Sports is streaming documentaries of Indigenous games. More details about the programme and activities conducted are available at https://ekbharat.gov.in/.(b) Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat programme is being implemented in every State/ UT on the concept of State/ UT pairing.
