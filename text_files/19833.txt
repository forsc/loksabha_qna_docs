                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Funds to West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of disbursement of funds to West Bengal under various DBT schemes of the Government during Covid-19 lockdown period from March 2020 to till date;(b)	the details of disbursement of funds to Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts of West Bengal under various DBT schemes of the Government during Covid-19 lockdown period from March, 2020 to till date; and (c)	the details of financial assistance provided to West Bengal for economic revival and post Covid-19 lockdown?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) : Rs. 14,971.87 crore in cash and Rs.8,117.63 crore in kind has been disbursed to   West Bengal under various DBT schemes in FY(2021-21) till 01.02.2021. (b) :The details of disbursement of funds to Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts of West Bengal under various DBT schemes are as follow:	           Cash	          In-Kind	 TotalDarjeeling	Rs.68.04 cr	Rs.147.39 cr	Rs.215.43 crKalimpong	Rs.6.53 cr	Rs.46.02 cr	Rs.52.55 cr(c) : Additional borrowings upto 2% of GSDP has been allowed to States over and above normal borrowing limit of 3% of GSDP for the year 2020-21. This has enabled additional resources amounting to Rs.27,149 crore to the State of West Bengal. Borrowings of 1% of GSDP amounting to Rs.13,574 crore has already been issued to West Bengal. Under the scheme of Special Assistance to States for capital expenditure, West Bengal has been allocated Rs.630 crore out of which Rs.315 crore has been released to the State.
