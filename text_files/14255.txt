                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Construction of IHHLs and PTs




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds allocated, released and utilised for construction of Individual Household Latrines (IHHLs) and Public Toilets (PTs), waste management and capacity building in urban areas since 2014, State-wise;(b) 	the details of funds allocated, released and utilised for Information, Education and Communication (IEC) and public awareness under Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM), State-wise;(c) 	whether the Government has conducted any survey to assess the demand for public toilets and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (d) 	the details of IHHL and CT/PT constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission, State-wise; and(e) 	whether the details of projects/activities and funds released from Swachh Bharat Kosh are not maintained by the Ministry and if so, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) to (e): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 267 FOR 12.03.2020 REGARDING “CONSTRUCTION OF IHHLs AND PTs”.(a) and (b) :  State/ UT-wise details of funds allocated, released, and utilized for Individual Household Latrines (IHHLs) and Community/ Public Toilets (CT/PTs), Waste Management(Solid Waste Management), Capacity Building and Information Education Communication (IEC) & Pubic Awareness (PA) under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) is at Annexure-I.(c) :  The demand for public toilets is assessed by the concerned State Government/Union Territory. (d) :  State/ UT-wise details of Individual Household Latrines (IHHLs) and Community/ Public Toilets (CT/PTs) constructed in urban areas of the country is at Annexure-II and III.(e) :  Swachh Bharat Kosh is administered by Ministry of Finance and not by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.                                            *****
