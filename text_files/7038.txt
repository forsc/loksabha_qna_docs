                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

813






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Schemes for Academic Improvement of Minorities




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                           (a)   the details regarding different schemes of the Ministry which aim at improving  the academic aspects of the minorities;        (b)   the details of amounts allocated for such schemes during the last two years;        (c)    the funds that have been spent and remain unspent during the said period;        (d)   whether the Ministry is taking assistance from the NGOs in implementing such schemes in the country; and        (e)    if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	Ministry is implementing following schemes aimed at improving the academic aspects of the six (6) centrally notified minority communities namely, Buddhists, Christians, Jains, Muslims, Sikhs and Parsis.  These schemes / Programmes in brief are as under:-                           	Educational Empowerment•	Scholarship Schemes – Pre-Matric, Post-Metric, Merit –cum- Means •	Maulana Azad National Fellowship (MANF)•	 Padho Pardesh - Scheme of Interest Subsidy on Educational Loans for Overseas       Studies for the Students Belonging to the Minority   Communities•	Naya Savera - Free Coaching and Allied Scheme•	Nai Udaan - Support for Students for preparation of Main Examination 	who clear Prelims conducted by UPSC/SSC, State Public Service Commission (PSC) etc.•	 Nai Manzil - Under the Nai Manzil scheme, there is an education component for Minority Youth who were left out from Formal Education.•	Besides the above, Begum Hazrat Mahal Scholarship Scheme for girls is 	also implemented by Maulana Azad Education Foundation (MAEF) an 	autonomous 	body of the Ministry.(b)	& (c) A total of Rs.4821 crore was allocated to these schemes in last 2 years of which Rs.4344.17 crore was spent.  During 2017-18 a sum of Rs.16.14 crore only was unspent.  The unspent balance for 2018-19 was Rs. 498.94 crore.  Against this, the disbursement figures for three scholarship schemes for 2019-20, against undisbursed scholarships of 2018-19, is Rs.213.50 crore till date.(d ) & (e)   Under schemes meant for educational empowerment of minority communities, only  the  scheme Free Coaching & Allied Scheme is being implemented in the country through 130 empanelled coaching institutions/organizations.   The detail of these institutions / organizations is available on the website of this Ministry (www.minorityaffairs.gov.in).*****
