                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2679






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Increase in Price of Platform Tickets




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the price of platform tickets has been increased immensely by Railways during Corona Pandemic;(b) if so, the details of hike in the price of tickets along with the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government proposes to rollback this price hike in future; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                           MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY                               (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): Normal rate of a  Platform ticket is `10.  Moreover, Powers have been delegated to Divisional Railways Managers to increase the rate of platform tickets beyond `10 to regulate rush at platforms during specific requirements like Mela, Rally etc., Accordingly, rates of platform tickets over some of the stations have been increased to regulate rush at Platforms in view of Covid 19 protocol.  Review of Rates of Platform tickets is an ongoing and continuous process.*****
