                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Indian Institute of Management, Visakhapatnam




Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi













                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has completed the construction of campus as envisaged in Government approved Detailed Project Report (DPR) for establishment of Indian Institute of Management, Visakhapatnam; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) if not, the reasons thereof indicating the details and percentage of unfiished work; (d) whether the Government has set any deadline to finish the said construction under Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act, 2014 in this regard; and (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBASH SARKAR)(a) to (e): The construction of campus for Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Visakhapatnam, as envisaged in Government approved Detailed Project Report (DPR) for phase-1, has been completed to the extent of about 40% so far. Majority of the remaining work comprises equipment installation and commissioning, Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) services, interiors, furniture and fixture, etc. The project got delayed by about four months because of disruption on account of second wave of COVID-19. The completion of this phase stands to be as September 2022 considering the testing, commissioning and handing over.*****
