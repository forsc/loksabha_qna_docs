                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4692






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Distribution of Solar Pumps to Farmers




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has distributed solar water pumps to small and marginal farmers from Central Financial Assistance under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM);(b) if so, the details of solar water pumps along with their capacity distributed, State-wise;(c) the number of beneficiaries from Rajkot, Morbi and Sabarkantha districts of Gujarat who have availed the benefits, district-wise;(d) whether any targets have been set for distribution across the Gujarat;(e) if so, the details thereof, district-wise; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (f) Under the Component-B of the PM-KUSUM Scheme, Government has sanctioned more than 3.59 lakh standalone solar water pumps for farmers including small and marginal farmers in various States/UTs across the country. This includes 2000 pumps allocated to state of Gujarat in FY 2020-21. The state implementing agencies have reported installation of more than 82,400 solar water pumps of capacity 1 HP to 10 HP as on 28.02.2022. State/UT-wise sanctions and installations are given at Annexure. The district-wise number of beneficiaries from Rajkot, Morbi and Sabarkantha districts of Gujarat under Component-B of PM-KUSUM are as given below:Name of District	Number of beneficiaries Rajkot	-Morbi	2Sabarkantha	3No district-wise targets have not been fixed since the PM-KUSUM scheme is demand driven.*****
