                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

233






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Welfare Schemes for Senior Citizens




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam













                                                    S. Venkatesan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †233TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.02.2020WELFARE SCHEMES FOR SENIOR CITIZENS†233. SHRIMATI POONAMBEN MAADAM: SHRI S. VENKATESAN:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of the schemes being implemented by the Government for the welfare of senior citizens and elderly people along with the details of beneficiaries during the last five years, State/ UT-wise, particularly in Gujarat and Tamil Nadu; (b) the amount of money allocated and utilized for each of these schemes during the said period, with special reference to Gujarat and Tamil Nadu; (c) whether Gujarat has requested to allocate more funds for the said schemes for welfare of senior citizens and if so, the details of action taken in this regard; (d) whether the Government is planning to introduce or implement more welfare schemes for senior citizens and elderly people across the country and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the number of old age homes functioning in Gujarat at present, district and location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  & (b): This Department is implementing a Central   Sector Scheme of Integrated Programme for Senior   Citizens   (IPSrC) since 1992, under which grant in aid is given to   Implementing Agencies (IAs) such as NGOs/VOs/States/UTs through registered   Societies for running and maintenance of Senior Citizens Homes/ Continuous   Care Homes/ Mobile Medicare Units etc. The main objective of the Scheme is to   improve the quality of life of Older Persons by providing basic amenities   like shelter, food, medical care and entertainment opportunities etc. Details   of funds released to IAs including in Gujarat and Tamil Nadu are  at Annexure-I.The Department has also launched a Central Sector    Scheme   namely “Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana” (RVY) on 1st April, 2017 with   the objective to restore near normalcy in the bodily functions of Senior   Citizens, suffering from age related infirmities/disabilities. Physical aids   and assisted living devices such as walking sticks, elbow crutches,   walkers/crutches, tripods/quadpods, hearing aids, wheelchairs, artificial   dentures and spectacles are provided free of cost to Senior Citizens belonging to BPL category. The Scheme is   implemented through the ‘Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation (ALIMCO) (a Public Sector Undertaking under the Ministry of Social Justice and   Empowerment). Funds under the Scheme of RVY are allocated to  ALIMCO   being the implementing agency for conducting assessment and distribution   camps in selected districts in the country. (c) In the above two schemes, funds are   released only to above mentioned implementing agencies and not to the State Governments.   Therefore question of request from the Government of Gujarat for more funds   does not arise.(d):  The Department has recently launched the  National Action Plan for the   welfare of Senior Citizens (NAPSrC) to enable them to lead a life of dignity.   It envisages programmes   for financial security, healthcare and nutrition,   shelter and welfare, protection of life and property, active and productive   ageing etc. NAPSrC is to be implemented   by the States and other implementing agencies  (e):  Details of old age homes functioning in Gujarat at present, district-wise and location-wise are given in Annexure-II****
