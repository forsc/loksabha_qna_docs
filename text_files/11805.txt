                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Development of Airport Infrastructure




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount spent for the development of airport infrastructure across the country during the last three years;(b) the number of airports for which the development proposals are pending with the Government; and(c) the details of the works carried out for the airports of Rajasthan and Maharashtra during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Airports Authority of India (AAI) has spent Rs. 8855.82 crore during last three years on the development of airport infrastructure across the country.;(b) & (c): Development of airports is a continuous process and is undertaken by airport operators from time to time depending on the availability of land, commercial viability, socio-economic considerations, traffic demand / willingness of airlines to operate to/from such airports. AAI has requested concerned State Governments to provide land for development of Dibrugarh, Guwahati, Gaya, Purnea, Goa, Ahmedabad, Porbandar, Surat, Madurai, Kangra, Kullu, Mangalore, Varanasi, Gorakhpur, Agra etc. airports. During last three years, AAI has undertaken expansion works at Kolhapur and Pune airports in Maharashtra and Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Udaipur airports in Rajasthan. The details of the works undertaken at these airports are as follow:;;Kolhapur : Extension of Runway with Blast Pad, construction of New Domestic Terminal Building and other associated works;Pune       : Construction of New Integrated Terminal Building, Link Taxi track etc.;Jaipur      : Expansion of existing terminal building, construction of additional parking bays, construction of Cargo Complex etc.;Jodhpur   : Construction of new apron and link taxi track;Udaipur   : Construction of apron, link taxi & expansion of isolation bay
