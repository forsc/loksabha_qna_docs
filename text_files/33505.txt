                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

927






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Training Programme for Rural Women




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has started a countrywide training, capacity building and personality development programme for rural women in dairy farming in order to empower and to make them economically independent, if so, the details thereof;(b)	the details of targets fixed under the programme along with the funds required for the purpose;(c)	the number of States likely to be covered under the programme;(d)	whether the Government proposes to cover the State of Rajasthan under the programme; and(e)	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which it is likely to be covered?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing following schemes under which assistance is provided for orientation/ training programme for Dairying activities:i.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM): Funds are released to all States/ Union Territories (UTs) for skill development namely Basic training of Multipurpose Artificial Insemination Technicians for Rural India (MAITRIs), training of existing AI workers and training of professionals etc.ii.	Dairy processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF):  Interest subvention on   loan assistance is provided to End Borrowers for establishment/ construction of training center (complete with civil and other necessary infrastructure) to Dairy Co-operative, Multi State Dairy Cooperative, Milk Producer Companies (MPC), NDDBs subsidiaries, Self Help Groups (SHGs) and Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) registered under State Cooperative and Companies Act.iii.	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD): Grant assistance is provided for training and awareness programme to farmers on good hygienic practice & good manufacturing practice, for milk testers, DCS staff, dairy personnel on plant operations etc.(b)  The DIDF and NPDD schemes are demand driven and no such target has been set. Only under RGM a target to induce 90958 AI Technicians in next 5 years has been set. (c ) to (e) These programmes are implemented across the country including Rajasthan. ****
