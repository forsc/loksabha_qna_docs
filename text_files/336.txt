                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

386






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Hindi Teaching Institutions




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that a large number of foreigners are visiting India to learn Hindi;(b) if so, the details thereof during the last two years; and(c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to improve the infrastructure of Hindi teachinginstitutions in view of the rising number of foreign students visiting in the country to learn Hindi?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Yes Sir.(b) Kendriya Hindi Sansthan (KHS), an autonomous body under this Ministry conducts variousprogrammes to cater to the need of foreigners visiting India to learn Hindi. The details of foreignstudents studied at KHS under the International Hindi Teaching Programme, during the last twoyears is as mentioned below -(c) KHS organizes following courses for the foreign students under the scheme for Propagation ofHindi Abroad - Hindi Language Proficiency Certificate, Hindi Language Proficiency Diploma, HindiLanguage Advanced Diploma, Post Graduate Hindi Diploma. The foreign students of KHSInternational Hindi Teaching Programme are taught Hindi through traditional classroom teachingmethod along with the modern ICT equipped teaching aids. KHS also provides facility of wi-fiInternet in the campus, Digital Language Lab, Computer Lab and Multimedia Class Room forensuring the quality of Hindi Language Learning. KHS also has a library which caters all theacademic needs of these students. Moreover University Grants Commission also gives grants toUniversities for the establishment and upgradation of Hindi Departments.Sr. No.
