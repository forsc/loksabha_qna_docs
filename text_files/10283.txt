COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Khammam district has more than 500 granite slab industries and 200 granite tile industries and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the steps being taken for exports of such granite by setting up of Railway Siding near Pandillapalli railway station in Khammam district to generate more revenue; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?

The Council has informed that the Khammam Granite Association has reported 469 Granite Slab factories located in the District and the Granite Tile Association, Khammam has 114 Granite Tile Industries.
