                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

36






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Welfare of Divyang Children




Ranjeeta Koli













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether welfare work of ‘Divyang’ children is being badly affected due to utilization of only 60 percent of the funds allocated for the work related to empowerment of persons with disabilities;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto;(c)	the details of the funds allocated/released/utilized by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities during the last three years, year-wise; and(d)   the details of the reform measures undertaken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(DR. THAARWARCHAND GEHLOT)(a)	to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. Statement as referred in reply to part (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Question No. 36 for answer on 19.11.2019 regarding “Welfare of Divyang Children” raised by Shrimati Ranjeeta Koli and Shri Pashupati Nath Singh.(a)	: No, Sir. The fund utilised by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (Divyangjan) during the last three years is more than 95% of the fund allocated.(b)	: Does not arise. (c): The funds allocated/utilized for Persons with Disabilities by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (Divyangjan) for last three years are as below:-Rs. in croresYear	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Budget Estimates	783.56	855.00	1070.00Revised Estimates	783.56	955.00	1070.00Funds released/ Actual Expenditure	772.66	928.32	1017.56(d): Does not arise. *****
