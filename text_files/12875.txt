                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Establishment of Central Universities




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Central Universities in the country along with the places where they aresituated and the date on which they were established and came into operation;(b) whether UGC rules are being followed in the appointment, transfer and time-boundpromotion of the gazetted and non-gazetted officials in the above Central Universities;(c) if so, the University-wise details of the appointments, transfers and promotion during the lastthree years;(d) whether UGC rules/guidelines are followed in the CAS promotion of professors in the aboveCentral Universities;(e) if so, the names of lecturers who were given promotion in the above universities along withthe dates of promotion and the rules under which they were promoted; and(f) the University-wise/Institute-wise/year-wise details of the employees promoted?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): At present, there are 43 Central Universities under the purview of Ministry of HumanResource Development (MHRD). A list of the Central Universities indicating their location andyear of establishment is at Annexure.(b) to (f): The Central Universities are autonomous bodies established by the respective Acts ofthe Parliament and are governed by their respective Acts and the Statutes and the Ordinancesframed there under. All the Central Universities follow the Rules and Regulations ofGovernment of India and UGC on appointment, promotions etc. Ministry of Human ResourceDevelopment generally does not interfere in the internal academic and administrative matter ofthe Central Universities as they are autonomous bodies and details of the appointments,transfers and promotions in Central Universities are not maintained centrally in MHRD.*****
