                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2722






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Profit of Petroleum Companies




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has the data of yearly turn over, gross and net profit of Petroleum and Natural Gas producing companies, marketing companies in the public sector and in the private sector during the last five years; and (b) if so, the details thereof, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) & (b) The year-wise details of Turnover, Gross Profit and Net Profit (profit earned after tax) by CPSEs under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas during last 5 years as under: -Year	Turnover(in Rs. Crore)	Gross Profit(in Rs. Crore)	Net Profit(in Rs. Crore)2016-17	11,65,922	1,04,620	64,3332017-18	13,14,934	1,16,057	69,6362018-19	16,01,252	1,23,361	69,7632019-20	15,04,234	64,845	26,9272020-21	13,46,955	1,08,121	74,720Source: ONGC, OVL, OIL, GAIL, IOCL, BPCL, HPCL, CPCL, NRL, MRPL, EIL, BLLMinistry of Petroleum and Natural Gas does not maintain data of turnover and profit earned by private sector petroleum companies. **********
