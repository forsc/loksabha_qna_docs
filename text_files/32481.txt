                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Special Economic Zones




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the, State-wise, names of areas selected under Special Economic Zones (SEZs) in the country along with the present status of the said areas;(b)	whether the Government proposes to develop some areas after identifying the same under SEZ in Bihar in view of the backwardness of the State;(c)	if so, the names of the said areas;(d)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(e)	the, year-wise, amount of revenue being earned by the Government from the areas developed under the SEZ rules during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)  (a)	to (e):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT  REFERRED  TO  IN  REPLY  TO  PARTS  (a) to (e)  OF  LOK  SABHA  STARRED  QUESTION  NO. 4(H)   FOR  ANSWER  ON  02nd FEBRUARY, 2022  REGARDING   “ SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONES”.(a):	 States/Union Territories-wise details of Special Economic Zones (SEZs) are at Annexure. Details of locations of these SEZs are available at www.sezindia.nic.in. (b) to (d):	Post enactment of SEZ Act, 2005 the Central Government has not set up any SEZ in the country. SEZs are primarily private investment driven initiatives. Department of Commerce has not received any proposal for setting up a SEZ in Bihar as on date. (e):	The revenue received by the Government from SEZs during the last five years, year-wise is as below:Year-wise revenue from  duty for the Domestic Tariff Area Sale TransactionsYear	Duty Amount Paid (Rs.
