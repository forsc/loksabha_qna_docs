                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

840






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Raw Material Assistance Scheme




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of MSME units which received financial assistance under Raw Material Assistance (RMA) Scheme;(b)	the number of MSME units from Maharashtra State which applied for assistance under RMA;(c)	the details of amount received and name of such units from Maharashtra which were benefitted under the Scheme; and(d)	the details of initiatives taken by National Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe Hub scheme?                                                                                                   






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):       The details of MSME units which have been provided financial assistance under Raw Material Assistance (RMA) Scheme by NSIC in last 2 years and current year are as under: <pre>           Financial Year	No. of Units	Amount(Rs. In lacs)2019-20	2842	524495.622020-21	2699	439843.752021-22 (upto 26.11.2021)	2446	308580.63</pre>(b) & (c):     The details of MSME units from Maharashtra which have been provided financial assistance under Raw Material Assistance (RMA) Scheme by NSIC in last 2 years and current year are as under:Pre>Financial Year	No. of Units	Amount(Rs. In lacs)2019-20	322	45780.692020-21	320	43988.892021-22 (upto 26.11.2021)	310	30863.99</pre>(d):          National Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe Hub (NSSH) was launched in October, 2016 with the objective to provide professional support to SC-ST entrepreneurs for fulfilling the mandate of 4% procurement from CPSEs as per the Public Procurement Policy, adopt applicable industry best practices and leverage Stand-up India initiative. The above scheme is being implemented by National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC), a CPSE under the Ministry of MSME. The following interventions / sub-schemes are being implemented under NSSH for capacity building and market linkage of the SC-ST entrepreneurs. The progress on these interventions on Pan India basis since inception of the Scheme is as follows: •	Special Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (SCLCSS) which has a provision of 25 % capital subsidy for procurement of plant & machinery (maximum subsidy - Rs.25 Lacs), for new and existing SC/ST MSEs. Till date, subsidy of Rs.88.24 Cr has been provided for 849 SC-ST entrepreneurs. •	Special Marketing Assistance Scheme (SMAS) to enhance marketing capabilities & competitiveness of the SC-ST MSMEs. Under this sub-scheme, 7521 SC-ST entrepreneurs have been facilitated to participate in domestic / international exhibitions. •	26563 SC/ST candidates have been provided skill and entrepreneurship development training under the scheme and 4,483 sector-specific toolkits distributed to the trained candidates. •	378 Special Vendor Development Programs (SVDPs) have been organized through NSSH Offices for product matchmaking, sensitization and augment SC-ST participation in public procurement processes.  •	Besides, 23026 SC-ST entrepreneurs have been provided subsidy for enrollment on the B2B Portal “MSMEmart.com” of NSIC and 3892 MSEs were registered under Single Point Registration Scheme (SPRS) of NSIC. •	In addition, 47 State Conclaves have been organized with participation from 24,342 SC/ST entrepreneurs, CPSEs, Banks and State Governments etc. to create awareness amongst SC-ST entrepreneurs.
