                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Talcher Fertilizer Plant




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has any revival plan for the Talcher Fertilizer Plant; (b) if so, the details about the employment opportunities for the local people; (c) the benefits which will be given to the local population?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a):	 Yes Sir, Government of India has decided to revive Talcher unit of Fertilizer Corporation of India Ltd (FCIL) by setting up a Ammonia Urea plant of 12.7 Lakh Metric Tonne per annum capacity by forming a Joint venture Company (JV) of nominated PSUs i.e. Gas Authority of India Ltd (GAIL), Rashtriya Chemicals & Fertilizers Ltd (RCF), Coal India ltd (CIL) and FCIL. Accordingly, a JV company named “Talcher Fertilizers Ltd (TFL)” has been incorporated on 03.11.2015.(b):  The direct employment opportunities would be about 510 and indirect employment opportunities would be 4500 (during construction phase) for theTalcher Fertilizer Plant.(c):  Besides employment opportunities to the local people, Talcher Fertilizer Plant would boost economic development in the eastern region and would ensure availability of urea to the farmers in the state of Odisha.*********
