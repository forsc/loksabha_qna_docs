National Saving Schemes




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FINANCE be pleased to state:(a)	the details of the national saving schemes including special schemes being run for the senior citizens in the country during the last three years and the current year, scheme-wise; (b)	whether the Government proposes to bring a new policy for senior citizens and weaker classes; and (c)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?
