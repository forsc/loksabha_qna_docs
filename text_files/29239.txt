                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

590






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Train Routes Under PPP




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that one of the routes identified out of 150 trains proposed to be run under PPP is Hyderabad-Tirupati Via Vikarabad, Raichur, Guntakal, etc.;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the proposed train runs daily; and(d)	 if so, whether the fare would be affordable and the details of facilities that are likely to be provided in this trains?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b)    Indian Railways had invited bids for private participation to induct 151 trains (rakes) over 12 clusters through Public Private Partnership (PPP). The list containing details of these train services in the notified projects (clusters) spread across the entire Indian Railway network, including those serving the route from Hyderabad to Tirupati is available on http://www.indianrailways.gov.in/IndicativeRoutesfor12clusters.pdf.  The Bids for the RFP stage were opened on 23rd July, 2021. In all 5 bids were received for three clusters namely Cluster-2(Mumbai-2), Cluster-3(Delhi-1) and Cluster-4(Delhi-2); no bids were received for the remaining 9 clusters. The Tender Committee, after due deliberation, recommended for discharge of the Tender. Competent Authority has accepted the recommendation of the Tender Committee.  (c) & (d) Do not arise.*****
