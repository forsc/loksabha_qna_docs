                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1348






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Land Records




Thalikkottai Rajuthevar Baalu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                                          LOK SABHA                  UNSTARRED QUESTION No. 1348                  TO BE ANSWERED ON 9.2.2021                  Land Records1348. SHRI T.R. BAALU:Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:(a) the efforts made by the Government for providing error-free,  tamper-proof  and  effortless  accessible  land records  which  have  been  the  prolonged  governance challenge in the country; and(b)  the  steps  taken  by  the  Government  to  find  asolution for computerization of land records which has been dragging on for decades due to systemic lack of capacity,  will  and  resources  and  land  being  a  State subject?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) 	Digital India Land Records Modernization Programme (DILRMP), the erstwhile National Land Records Modernization Programme was launched in 2008-09 by the Government of India to digitize and modernize land records and develop a transparent and Integrated Land Information Management System (ILIMS) which will inter alia provide error-free, transparent and tamper-proof land records and their hassle-free online access to the land owners as well as delivery of the copies of records of rights at their door step through kiosks, in a time bound manner, without interface with the revenue officials. Citizen Charters have been prepared by many States to ensure time bound delivery of services.		Many States are issuing Record of Rights (RoRs) through Kiosk / Common Service Centre at Town/Village level or have made possible taking of printouts of the RoRs from the State’s website anytime as records have been placed on the State’s website.(b)	The subject of ‘Land’ and its management falls within the legislative and administrative jurisdiction of the respective States as provided under Entry Numbers 18 and 45 of List II (State List) of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution. The Central Government, through DILRMP, a Central Sector Scheme, is providing financial assistance to the states, inter alia, for (i) Computerization of Land Records(ii) Computerization of Registration and its integration with land records (iii) Training & Capacity Building - Creation of DILRMP Cells and training of Revenue officials etc.(iv) Modern  Record  Room  for  secure storage  of information and delivery of services (v) State Level Data Centre - to maintain data repository and backup (vi) Project Management Unit - to provide human resources and other infrastructure etc. to provide support for the effective implementation of various components of DILRMP. Regular review of the progress is monitored through Regional Review Meetings, Video Conferences, Central Management Information System (MIS) and visit of senior officers etc. *****
