                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Funds under IHSDP




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the funds released to various States/UTs under the Integrated Housing and Slum Development Programme (IHSDP) during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(b) the details of the funds utilized by the various States/UTs under the programme during the said period, State/UT-wise; and(c) whether there has been regular inspections to check the utilization of the funds under the programme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b): The period of Integrated Housing and Slum Development Programme (IHSDP), a component of Jawaharlal Nehru National Urban Renewal Mission (JnNURM) was upto March, 2012. However, to complete the on-going projects sanctioned upto 31.03.2012, the period was first extended upto March 2015 and then upto March 2017.   Details of the funds released and utilized by the various States/UTs under the IHSDP during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise are annexed.(c):  IHSDP projects are implemented by State/UT Governments through its implementing agencies/Urban Local Bodies concerned. The Ministry reviews status of progress and utilization of funds released under the programme on a regular basis with States/UTs through Monthly and Quarterly Progress Reports, in the meetings of Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee, through Video Conferences, field visits etc.                                         *****
