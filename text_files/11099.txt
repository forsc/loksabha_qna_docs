                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

94






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Financial Assistance for Celebrating State Festivals




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has released any financial aid for celebrating State festivals during the last three years; (b)	if so, the details thereof, festival-wise and year-wise; and (c) 	the steps taken by the Government to promote tourism in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Tourism provides funds for celebrating festivals to the States and UTs for promotion of tourism under the Scheme “Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality” (DPPH). The detail is given at Annexure-I.(c):  Ministry of Tourism has granted Central Financial Assistance to Government of Rajasthan under the National Missions of Swadesh Darshan and Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) to enrich the tourist experience, to increase the opportunity for employment, attract the domestic and foreign tourist to visit these destinations, to improve the connectivity and improve the thematic tourism circuits in the country. The list of projects sanctioned under Swadesh and PRASHAD Schemes are given at Annexure-II. Short promotional films on various destinations and products have been produced by the Ministry to showcase Rajasthan as preferred tourist Destination.  Additionally a dedicated photoshoot of Rajasthan as a tourist destination has been undertaken.Monuments of Rajasthan have been promoted through annual promotional Calendar of the Ministry.Publicity material has been produced by the Ministry, the same has been disseminated through Domestic and Overseas India Tourism Offices.*******
