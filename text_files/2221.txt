                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

198






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Affordable Housing




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of agencies which have been given the responsibility by the Government to provide houses at low prices in cities;(b) the number of the houses costing below one lakh rupees constructed by the said agencies in cities during each of the last three years, city-wise;(c) whether these agencies have succeeded in providing houses in cities atlow prices and if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                [SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI](a) to (d): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the Sabha.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 198*DUE FOR 04.07.2019 REGARDING “AFFORDABLE HOUSING”(a) to (d):	No Agency has been given the responsibility for providing houses at low prices in cities by this Ministry.  However, State Level Nodal Agencies (SLNAs) have been designated in each State/Union Territory (UT) for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] through Urban Local Bodies (ULBs), Housing Boards, Development Authorities etc.The cost of construction of houses in urban areas under the PMAY(U) is more than one lakh.
