                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3038






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Poshan Abhiyan in Andhra Pradesh




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has taken note that Andhra Pradesh (AP) has got first position in Poshan Abhiyan; (b) 	whether Andhra Pradesh has also bagged second position in enhancing capacity of workers, implementation of various schemes/programmes of the Government of India, better coordination in implementing schemes, etc.; and(c) 	if so, the special incentive/ encouragement that the ministry will give to Anganwadis in AP so that they can work with more vigour to implement schemes of the Ministry?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT( SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI )(a)	Under POSHAN Abhiyaan Award 2018-19, Government of Andhra Pradesh has won 1st position in its group in ICDS-CAS implementation for which a cash award of  ? 1.00 crore was sanctioned.(b)	Government of Andhra Pradesh has also won second position in its group in overall excellence in implementation of POSHAN Abhiyaan for which a cash award of  ? 0.75 crore was sanctioned.(c)	Total 10 field functionaries comprising of Anganwadi Workers, Anganwadi Helpers, Lady Supervisors, Accredited Social Health Activist (ASHA) and Auxiliary Nurse and Midwives (ANM)  of Andhra Pradesh were also given a cash prize of Rs. 50,000 each, a certificate and a medal during the award ceremony. Further, Anganwadi workers are also paid @ ?  250  per Community Based Events (CBEs) for 2 CBEs per month and Anganwadi helper is paid incentive of Rs 250 per month for opening of Anganwadi Centre.  ******
