                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

620






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Projects in NER




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of total number of projects started and completed in the North Eastern region in the last three years;(b)   the details of educational and health related projects running in the region; and(c)  the details of plans to increase the connectivity in the region ?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)        The details of total number of projects sanctioned and completed under various schemes of MDoNER during 2016-17 to 2018-19 is  given below:-                                                             (Rs. in crore)<pre>S. No	Scheme	Project sanctioned	Project completed		No.	Cost	No.	Costi	NLCPR-State	76	1893.97	103	1332.04ii	NESIDS	37	1119.89	0	0iii	Special packages (BTC, KAATC & DHATC) of Assam	25	173.52	4	67.34iv	Schemes of NEC 	265	2475.96	355	3664.63v 	NERSDS	03	213.97	02	62.96Total	406	5877.31	464	5126.97</pre>(b)       Under various schemes of the Ministry,122 projects costing Rs.879.02 crore under education sector and 35 projects costing Rs.612.12 crore under health sector have been sanctioned and are ongoing. (c)	 Several initiatives have been planned to improve the Air, Rail Inland Waterways, Road & Digital connectivity in the Region. These inter-alia include a new airport at Holongi, Arunachal Pradesh, upgradation of Airports at Guwahati, Imphal, Dibrugarh etc.; Agartala-Akhaura new rail link project, capital rail connectivity with Gangtok(Rangpo), Imphal, Kohima(Zubza) and Aizawl(Sairang); development of Barak waterways; new bridges across Brahmaputra at Jogighopa, Dhubri-phulbari; wi-fi connectivity for village panchayats in NER and mobile connectivity in all States.</pre>
