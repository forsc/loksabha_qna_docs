                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2876






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Mines Closed Down in Karnataka




Narayana Swamy Abbaiah














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The number of mines closed down in Karnataka due to Judicial Intervention of Supreme Court (SC) of India during the last five years; and (b)	the reasons cited by the SC to order closure of such mines; and(c)	the details of measures proposed by the Centre to avoid such closure of mines, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)	 As per data furnished by Indian Bureau of Mines (A Subordinate Office of Ministry of Mines), during last 5 years no mine has been closed in the State of Karnataka due to judicial Intervention of Hon’ble Supreme Court of India.(b)	& (c) Does not arise in view of reply furnished at item (a) above.****
