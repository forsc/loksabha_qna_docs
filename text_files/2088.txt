                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Frequency of 22221/22222




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways plan to run train 22221/22222 Mumbai (CSMT) to Hazrat Nizamuddin (NZM) throughout the week and if so, the details thereof; and(b) the problems being faced due to absence of pantry car till date in train No.12171/12172 running from Lokmanya Tilak Terminus to Haridwar Junction?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTSRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) No, Sir. At present, there is no proposal to increase the frequency of 22221/22222 Hazrat Nizamuddin-Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (T) Rajdhani Express from bi-weekly to daily.(b) It is the continuous endeavour of Indian Railways to ensure availability of catering services to passengers on trains and at stations. For train No. 12171/12172 Lokmanya Tilak (T)-Haridwar Express, passengers can avail of Static Catering Units at en-route stations and e-catering services. Further, platform vending has also been permitted to provide catering services to the passengers.*****
