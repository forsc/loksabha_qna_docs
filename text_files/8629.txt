                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2141






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Facilities in Schools




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of funds released for development of basic infrastructure in KendriyaVidyalayas during each of the last three years and the current year, State andyear-wise;(b) whether the Government has any mechanism to check whether the fundsreleased for basic infrastructure are properly utilized and if so, whether theGovernment has taken action against principals who have failed to utilize theamount or failed to carry out the work after utilizing the amount, State/ UT-wise;(c) whether in most of Kendriya Vidyalayas the toilets for girl children are not in agood condition putting them in lot of problem; and(d) if so, the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) The Government of India does not allocate funds to Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan(KVS) state wise. No funds have been allocated to KVS separately for the purpose ofdevelopment of basic infrastructure. The details of funds released for basic infrastructure inKendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) during last three years and current year are annexed.(b) Construction works are executed as per Deposit Work System through various Govt.construction agencies like CPWD, MES, Railways, State PWDs, other Central/State PSUsetc. Funds are released after receipt of physical and financial report from constructionagency through Principal alongwith photo graphs. Progress of work is also reviewed atVidyalaya level by the Vidyalaya Management Committee (VMC) every month.(c) & (d) Separate toilets for boys and girls are available in good condition in all the KendriyaVidyalayas.*****
