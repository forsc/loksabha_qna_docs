                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3929






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






India Post Payments Bank




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has set up India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) as a Public Limited Company under the Ministry of Communications; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the response received by the Government from the public in this regard; (c) 	whether employment has been generated in the country by the setting up of IPPB and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the details of the branches opened by IPPB in the country so far, State/UT-wise; (e) 	whether various institutions including global banking institution like World Bank have shown interest to get associated with IPPB; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?   






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE) (a) & (b) 	Yes sir, the government has set up the India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) as a Public Limited Company with 100% government equity. IPPB has also been designated a Scheduled Payments Bank by the Reserve Bank of India. IPPB has 650 branches and 1,36,078 banking access points. It also provides doorstep banking services through postal delivery agents. In a little over 1 year of functioning, as on 10.03.2020, IPPB has acquired 2.26 crores customers and has conducted financial transactions worth Rs. 14,191 crores. Further, 22.04 Lacs transactions of Aadhar Enabled Payment System (AePS) have been conducted involving a sum of Rs. 686 crores.  (c) & (d)	A total of 2096 personnel are employed by IPPB currently. These include those taken through direct recruitment and also on deputation. A list of IPPB branches across the country, State wise (including UTs) is at Annexure-I.(e) & (f)    Given its rural reach, various global and domestic institutes have shown interest in getting associated with IPPB but no formal agreements have been entered into as on date. IPPB is in discussion with various global & domestic institutions to enhance its product suite, as per the Payment Bank guidelines governing third party products.
