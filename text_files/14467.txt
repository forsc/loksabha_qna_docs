                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3185






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Status of Small Scale Industries




Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of Small Scale Industries (SSIs) in creating employment opportunities and revenue earnings; (b)	 the status of policy formulated for the development of Small Scale Industries; and (c) 	the details of funds released and utilised under various schemes for MSME Sector in the country including Uttar Pradesh during the last two years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	 As per 73rd Round of NSS (National Sample Survey) Report on Unincorporated Non-Agricultural enterprises, (June 2015-June 2016) conducted by Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation, estimated number of workers in Micro, Small & Medium sector was 11.10 crore. As reported by National Statistical Office (NSO), the share of MSMEs in total Gross Domestic Product (GDP) during 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 was 29.5%, 29.3%, 29.7% and 30.3% respectively.(b):	Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development (MSMED) Act has necessary provisions for promotion & development of MSMEs.(c):	 The details of funds allocated & utilized under various Schemes/Programmes of the Ministry, during the last two years are annexed.
