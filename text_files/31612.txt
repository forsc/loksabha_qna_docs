                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3051






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Designing of Offices/Complexes Buildings




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to design offices/ complexes and other buildings to be eco-friendly in future and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and(c) 	the details of involvement of architects in this regard?






ANSWER




                     THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                  MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (c): ‘Land’ and ‘Colonization’ are state subjects. As such, the matter falls within the domain of the respective State and Union Territory Governments. For the guidance of the States/UTs, the Ministry has issued Model Building Bye Laws, 2016 and Urban & Regional Development Plans Formulation and Implementation Guidelines (URDPFI), 2014 which, inter alia, include eco-friendly provisions for buildings and urban development. Architects are involved in architectural design in accordance with applicable norms.                                     *****
