                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4753






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Green Economy




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India is one of the fastest growing green economy of the world;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps undertaken by the Government to reduce carbon emission and move towards sustainable development goals?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c) As per IMF’s World Economic Outlook (WEO), January 2022, India is projected to grow at 9.0 per cent in the year 2022 which will be the highest rate of growth among major economies. Further, for reducing emissions the Government has taken a number of measures. Some of these measures are listed at Annexure.*****
