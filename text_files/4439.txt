                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3931






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Agreement with Sri Lanka




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of important agreements signed by India and Sri Lanka during each of the last three years; and(b) the significant outcome of bilateral ties between India and Sri Lanka during the above period?? ? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b) The following Agreements were signed with Sri Lanka during the last three years: <pre>Sl.No Name of the Agreement/MoU Summary of Agreement/MoU2016-Nil20171. MoU between Kelaniya University and ICCR for Long Term Hindi ChairThe MoU was signed on 17 January 2017 in Colombo to establish long term Hindi Chair in Kelaniya University, Sri Lanka. 2. MoU for Cooperation in Economic ProjectsThe MoU was signed on 26 April 2017 in New Delhi. It outlines the various joint projects as well as the modalities and timelines for implementation. 20183. MoU for Cooperation in IT & ElectronicsThe MoU was signed on 15 January 2018 in Colombo. The purpose of the MoU is for cooperation in IT and Electronics between the countries to enhance Digital Literacy and e-governance.4. MoU for the Establishment of a Collaborative Relationship to Combat Transnational Illegal Activities at Sea and Develop Regional Cooperation Between the Indian Coast Guard and the Sri Lankan Coast Guard.The MoU was signed on 09 May 2018 in Colombo between the Coast Guards of India and Sri Lanka for establishment of collaborative relationship to combat transnational illegal activities at sea and develop regional cooperation.20195. Arrangement on Gainful Occupation for Family Members of Diplomatic Mission or Consular PostThe Arrangement was signed on 05 March 2019 in Colombo. It facilitates the gainful occupation for family members of Diplomatic Mission or Consular Post of Sri Lanka and India.</pre>	   During the last three years, the Government has taken several steps to further strengthen the close and friendly ties with Sri Lanka. The enhanced relationship has been marked by close contacts at all levels. Various high level visits have taken place during the last three years. Trade and investment have also grown during this period. There is increased cooperation in the fields of development, education, culture and defence. Both countries share a broad understanding on major issues of international interest. Significant progress in implementation of developmental assistance projects in Sri Lanka has helped further cement the bonds of friendship between the two countries.***
