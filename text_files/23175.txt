                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

376






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






MSP for Minor Forest Produce




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether his Ministry has revised the Minimum Support Price (MSP) for Minor Forest Produce;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the additional items that have been classified under MSP Scheme since 1st January, 2020; and(d) whether the Government is providing any financial assistance to Van Dhan Vikas Kendras and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (d)        A Statement is laid down on the Table of the House.  Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *376 for answer on 22.03.2021 tabled by SHRIMATI SAJDA AHMED regarding ‘MSP for Minor Forest Produce’. (a) to (c): 	Yes, Sir. Ministry of Tribal Affairs revised Minimum Support Price (MSP) for Minor Forest Produce (MFP) guidelines on May 1, 2020 thereby increased the MSP of 47 MFPs. Further, vide notifications dated 26.05.2020 and 11.11.2020, 37 new items were included in the list of MFPs thereby making the total number of MFPs under the scheme to 87 as at Annexure 1. (d):	Yes, Sir. State-wise details of Van Dhan Vikas Kendars (VDVKs) sanctioned through Tribal Co-operative Marketing Federation of India (TRIFED) including the funds sanctioned for VDVKs is at Annexure 2.
