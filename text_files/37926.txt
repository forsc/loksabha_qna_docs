                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Self Defence Training for Girls




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  Self  Defence  Training  is  imparted  to Scheduled  Castes/Scheduled  Tribes/Economically Weaker  Section  girl  students  belonging  to  Government Schools  under  Samagra  Shiksha Abhiyan;(b) if  so,  the  details  thereof  along  with  the  age group  of  girls  shortlisted  for  this  purpose;(c) the  number  of  schools  engaged  in  conducting self defence trainings and for safety and security of girls during each of the last three years and the current year;(d) the funds allocated/spent during the said period on such training and the number of girls trained during the said  period;  and(e) whether the Government has been able to ensure safety  and security of girls by imparting self Defence Training and if  so,  the  details  thereof  and  the  achievement  made  by the  Government  in  this  regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (c): 	Department of School Education and Literacy (DoSEL), Ministry of Education is implementing the centrally sponsored scheme of Samagra Shiksha, under which self defence training is imparted to girls of classes VI to XII,  studying in Government Schools, irrespective of their social categories. Self defence training is also given in Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (KGBVs) which are residential schools for girls of Class VI to XII belonging to disadvantaged groups such as SC, ST, OBC, Minority and Below Poverty Line.  Details of number of schools approved for  Self Defence Training for Girls under Samagra Shiksha from the year 2018-19 to 2021-22 are as under:<pre>Sl. No.	Year	No. of Schools		Elementary	Secondary1	2018-19	134835	765812	2019-20	180160	818003	2020-21	147784	814384	2021-22*	163045	56869                        (Source: Prabandh)</pre>(d) & (e):	Samagra Shiksha scheme has now been extended till 2025-26 and the Self Defence training has been renamed as ‘Rani Laxmi Bai Aatma Raksha Prashikshan’. The fund for this component has been increased from Rs. 3000/- to Rs. 5000/- per month per school for three months. Self Defence training aims to ensure safety and security of girls, empower girls to tackle risk of assault and boost their self confidence.
