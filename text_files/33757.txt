                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1263






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Utilization of Coal Mines




Mimi Chakraborty














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government is aware of the underutilization of existing coal mines in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)  whether the Government has taken any action to enhance such utilisation;(c)  if so, the details thereof;(d)  if not, the reasons therefor;(e)  the details of Public Sector Enterprises (PSE) which have been allocated coal blocks, year-wise from May 2014 to December 2021; and(f)  the details of cancellation of allotted mines to PSES during this period, if any?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) to (d): Yes Sir. Government is aware of the under capacity utilization of existing coal mines in India. Main reasons for less capacity utilization are difficulties in possession /acquisition of land, EC restrictions, extended rainy season and excess rain, unexpected geological disturbances, poor performance of some of the Coal /OB removal contractors, COVID-19 pandemic etc. Steps taken to improve the efficiency of mines are as under: (i)    These projects are regularly monitored by the Government as well as Coal Companies and necessary actions are taken for early execution of projects. The issues related to EC, FC, Land acquisition and R&R are rigorously followed up at District / State level /MoEF&CC for early resolution.(ii)   Regular liaison is being done for availability of permissions, clearances, land acquisition/Possession.(iii)   High capacity HEMMs are deployed wherever feasible to enhance production and productivity.(iv)   OB removal and coal transportation contracts are awarded in advance.   (e) & (f): Details of Public Sector Enterprises (PSEs) which were allocated coal blocks, from May, 2014 to December, 2021, and the details of cancellation of allotted mines to PSEs during this period is annexed. ***
