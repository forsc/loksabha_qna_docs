                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Conversion of Nehru Museum




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is proposed to convert the Nehru Museum Memorial and Library, Delhi as a monument for all former Prime Ministries of India;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (c)	the time by which the proposed monument is likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c) : No, Sir. However, the Government of India has approved a proposal to set up a new Museum on the Prime Ministers of India in Teen Murti complex, New Delhi with the prime objective to broaden a better understanding of India’s democratic ethos, create curiosity about the office, life and personality of Prime Ministers among the visitors and the public at large. The  Museum is likely to be commissioned by October, 2020.*****
