                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3405






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






MGNREGS in Rajasthan




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of works being executed under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in Rajasthan, particularly in Dausa district;(b) 	the number of unskilled labour included under this scheme in the said district during the last three years; and(c) 	the details of steps being taken by the Government to cover unskilled labourers coming from remote areas of the district under this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment scheme with bottom up planning. The works are identified, approved & prioritized by the Gram Sabha at the Gram Panchayat level in each States/UTs including Rajasthan as per the provision of the Act. Accordingly, the action plan (shelf of work) of the permissible works is prepared & approved by the Gram Sabha and can be taken up as per the demand. The planning of works is done as per the estimated person days to be generated for the work in a financial year at the time of assessment of labour budget. The details of category wise works being executed under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in Rajasthan as well as in Dausa district of Rajasthan in financial year 2020-21 is given at Annexure.(b)	The details of individual unskilled workers worked under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in Dausa District of Rajasthan during the last three years and current financial year 2020-21 (as on 12th March, 2021) is as under:Progress	                          2020-21	2019-20	   2018-19	2017-18Person-days Generated so far [In Lakhs]	    45.1	35.35	    28.19	20.04Total Individuals Worked [In Lakhs]	     1.39	0.97	     0.87	 0.66 (c)	Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme which provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. It provides livelihood security i.e., fall back option for livelihood for the rural households when no better employment opportunity is available. The households can register themselves under the Act and seek employment at multiple fora including the GPs. The field authorise have been instructed to organise Rojgar Diwas at least once every month for covering willing unskilled labours.*****
