                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

843






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Gap Between Craftsmen and Digital Markets




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of efforts made/to be made by the Government to bridge the gap between craftsmen and digital markets; (b) whether the Government has provided/planning to provide specific training to artisans on searching online marketplaces, Instagram and Pinterest for comparable products and incorporate the latest designs, colours and materials; (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) the details of the initiative taken by the Government to provide adequate inventory and warehousing for listed products to artisan groups?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (c):	Office of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts), Ministry of Textiles has started registering artisans under Government e-marketplace (Gem), a digital platform to sell the products throughout the country with the support and assistance by the field offices.  In addition, the O/o DC(Handicrafts) provides trainings and handholding support to the handicraft artisans on continuous basis for developing new designs with the assistance of empanelled designers. (d):	The O/o Development Commissioner (Handicrafts) has kept new updated designs as sample at every Handicraft Service Centre to extend the support and benefit to artisans on continuous and regular basis as per the market trends. In addition, supports are given to all State Handicrafts Corporation for construction/renovation of emporia, Urban Haat, Raw Material banks etc.***
