                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

329






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Apprenticeship Training in Industrial Units




Rodmal Nagar













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to increase allowance per trainee for apprenticeship training in Industrial Units;(b)	if so, the outline of the proposal prepared in this regard; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor along with the views and the action plan of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (c)	Government do not have any proposal under consideration to increase allowance per trainee for apprenticeship training in industrial units.  However, establishments are free to pay stipend to the apprentices engaged by them, at a rate higher than that prescribed under the Apprentices Act, 1961.
