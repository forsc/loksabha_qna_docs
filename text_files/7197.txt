                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

774






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Bypass on BhavnagarAhmedabad Sector




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that bypass on Bhavnagar-Ahmedabad national 	highway is Kuccha/temporary due to which the route gets blocked even on slight rain 	and causes heavy traffic jam;(b) 	if so, whether the Government proposes to construct that route and make the said 	bypass 	pucca/permanent;(c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d)	 the time by which it is likely to be completed and the reasons for delay in completion 	of the 	said project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)  Presently four laning work on Bhavnagar-Ahmedabad National Highway is in progress between Adhelai to Nari Junction only. There is no proposed bypass on this small section of National Highway. The work is targeted for completion by August, 2021. *****
