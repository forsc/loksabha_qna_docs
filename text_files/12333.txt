                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






MoUs under ‘Adopt a Heritage’ Scheme




M V V Satyanarayana













                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                








                                                    Jagdambika Pal 
                                                








                                                    Uday Pratap Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government had recently introduced the ‘Adopt a Heritage: Apni Dharohar, Apni Pehchaan’ project for developing tourist amenities at heritage/tourist sites and making them tourist friendly in a planned and phased manner; (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the State-wise number of heritage structures in the country including Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu which were adopted by firms, corporates, etc. under this scheme for conservation and restoration; (c) 	whether there is a huge increase in the footfall of tourists after adoption of these monuments and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government has signed 27 Memorandum of Understanding (MoUs) till date under this scheme and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the details and the measures taken by the Government to improve the existing facilities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	The Ministry of Tourism, Government of India has launched the “Adopt a Heritage: Apni Dharohar, Apni Pehchaan” project which is a collaborative effort by the Ministry of Tourism, Ministry of Culture, Archaeological Survey of India and States/UTs Government for developing tourism amenities at heritage/ natural/ tourist sites spread across India for making them tourist friendly, in a planned and phased manner and for increasing the tourist footfall at the adopted sites. The project aims to encourage companies from public sector, private sector, corporate citizens, NGOs, individuals and other stakeholders to become ‘Monument Mitras’.The Monument Mitras are required to take up the responsibility of developing new tourist facilities/ amenities and upgrading the existing facilities at their opted sites. The indicative list of tourist facilities under the project includes toilet facility, drinking water kiosk, signages, benches, audio guides, ease of access, dustbins, cleanliness of the site, etc. They would also look after the operation & maintenance (O&M) of the facilities. These tourist facilities are undertaken as per their interest and viability in terms of a sustainable investment model under CSR.27 Memorandum of Understandings (MoUs) have been awarded to 12 Monument Mitras for twenty-five (25) sites and two (2) Technological interventions across India. The list is enclosed at Annexure.********
