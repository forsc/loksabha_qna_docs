                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2503






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Literacy Rate of Tribal Women




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the steps taken by the Government to increase the literacy rate of tribal women;(b) the details of the State-wise data of ST literacy in 2020;(c) the Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER) of STs in both school education and higher education;(d) whether the Government is taking any special efforts to increase the GER; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): Ministry of Education was implementing the scheme of ‘Saakshar Bharat’ in order to improve literacy rate with special focus on women and other disadvantaged groups, since October 2009. The scheme was extended upto 31.03.2019. Thereafter, a new Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Adult Education "PadhnaLikhnaAbhiyaan" is being implemented in rural and urban areas of 33 States/ UTs from the financial year 2020-21, which will include tribal women. Ministry of Tribal Affairs is assisting NGOs/VOs to run projects for strengthening education among ST girls in low literacy districts in the country for enhancing the literacy rate of tribal girls.  (b): As per Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) report (July 2019 - June 2020), published by Ministry of Statistics &Programme Implementation (MoSPI), literacy rate for Scheduled Tribes in India is 70.6%. However, State-wise details of literacy rates are not available in PLFS report.
