                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3035






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






National Common Mobility Card




Vishnu Dutt Sharma













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	Whether the Government is moving forward with the project of national common mobility card as suggested by Nandan Nilekani committee;(b)	If so, the details thereof and the status of implementation of project; and(c)	The timelines to start, scale up and complete the project?






ANSWER




                      THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                  MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)a.	Indigenously developed National Common Mobility Card (NCMC) along with complete ecosystem consisting of Automatic Fare Collection (AFC) System with Validation Terminal, Metro Gate and interface with Banking System, QR Ticketing were launched in March, 2019 on the basis of the recommendation of committee constituted by this Ministry in 2014.b.     i)    Final Interface Specifications of NCMC ecosystem and Specifications of QR Ticketing System Specifications have been released for adoption by State Governments, UT Administrations, Public Transport Operators, Metro Corporations and BRTS Operators across the country.ii)   NCMC has been made operational in Airport Line, Delhi Metro   Rail Corporation since December 2020.iii)	NCMC may be currently used for online transactions across different use cases. National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) has informed that 0.71 million NCMC Cardonline transactions have been processed in the month of November, 2021.iv)	As per data from National Payments Corporation of India(NPCI):•	25 banks along with 26 credit card/PPIs issuing agencies are live, with issuance volume of 49.26 million cards in Debit Card segment as on October, 2021.  Credit Card and PPI issuance volume has also scaled up to 1.65 million collectively.•	22 banks have been certified as NCMC acquirer.•	9 banks have rolled out terminals for offline transactions with volume of 47,273 transactions as on October, 2021. c.	Scaling up & Implementation of NCMC in Public Transport Operators (PTOs) across the country is a continuous process and both card issuance/terminal deployment are increasing every month, with NCMC issuance exceeding 5 million per month.                                ****
