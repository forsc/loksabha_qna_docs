                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

960






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Employment Provided under Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the percentage of employment provided under Swadesh Darshan Scheme from 2014 till date; (b) 	the number of circuits identified; (c)	whether the historical city Hastinapur in Meerut District has been included in any circuit; and (d) 	if not, the time by which the said city is likely to be included in a circuit?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			                     (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	The percentage of employment scheme-wise is not  maintained by Ministry of Tourism,  however  a  third  party impact  assessment of  ‘Swadesh  Darshan  scheme’ was  undertaken  by  National  Productivity Council (NPC) in  July, 2019;  which states that ‘Swadesh Darshan   scheme  has  been  able  to give a  fillip to livelihood  opportunities  and  create  employment for the local  communities  in  construction  phase.  (b):	Fifteen thematic circuits have been identified under Swadesh  Darshan  scheme.(c) and  (d):	Development and Promotion of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government.  The Ministry of Tourism, under its Swadesh Darshan scheme is developing thematic circuits in the country in a planned and prioritized manner in order to develop tourism infrastructure at multiple destinations.   Projects under Swadesh Darshan scheme are identified in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.Hastinapur in Meerut  is  not  included in  any  project  sanctioned  under  Swadesh Darshan  scheme,  however the Ministry of  Tourism has   sanctioned  8  projects  under  Swadesh Darshan  scheme  in the State of  Uttar Pradesh which  includes  interventions at  2 locations in Meerut  District  under  ‘Heritage Circuit’ and  ‘Spiritual Circuit’.*******
