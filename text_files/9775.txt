                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3368






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Cultural Talent Discovery Scheme




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Central Government had recently introduced cultural talent discovery scheme under which a cultural data bank is being prepared by registering artists;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the objectives of this scheme is to appreciate new talent and protect old arts and artists; and (d)	if so, the details thereof.






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)  “Ministry of Culture is working on the National Mission on Cultural Mapping (NMCM) for building a comprehensive data base of artists, art forms and other resources gathered from cultural organizations in the Ministry of Culture, other ministries and also states (c ) & (d)  Besides, Ministry of Culture has been administering a few schemes to identify, nurture, hand hold and train the upcoming and budding artists on a sustained basis.  This is carried out by way of grant of scholarships, fellowships, pensions and other forms of grants/assistance throughout the country.  Details are available on Ministry’s Website.***
