                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2054






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






City Gas Distribution Network




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of cases pending in various courts across the country against operators for declaring City Gas Distribution (CGD) Networks as Common Carrier of Geographical Areas (GAs) where the marketing exclusivity has expired, operator and State-wise; (b) the particulars of GAs where the marketing exclusivity has expired, date-wise;(c) whether the Government is aware of the monopoly pricing of gas being supplied throughthe CGD network which is hampering the end-users; and (d) whether any mechanism has been worked out for the speedy disposal of pending cases and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)	There is no case pending in any court across the country against the operators for declaring City Gas Distribution (CGD) Networks as Common Carrier.(b)	The list of Geographical Areas (GAs) where period of exclusivity, from purview of common carrier/contract carrier, as mentioned in the authorization given by PNGRB, has been completed is at Annexure.(c)	Government of India has allocated domestic gas to various City Gas Distribution (CGD) entities for CNG (Transport) and PNG (Domestic) purpose. However, retail price of CNG/PNG is determined by CGD entities after considering state taxes, tariff and other components.(d)	Does not arise in view of reply of part (a) above.****
