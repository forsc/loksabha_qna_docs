                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

723






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Skill Training in Aviation Sector




Hemant Patil













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Airport Authority of India (AAI) has entered into partnership with the Construction Industry Development Council (CIDC) to impart skill training to youths in the aviation sector;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of youths who got trained and the quantum of funds released to CIDC for the purpose;(c) whether job opportunities for those who complete the training have been given priority and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether AAI also entered into a tripartite agreement with National Skill Development Fund and National Skill Development Corporation for imparting skill training to the youths in the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof along with the financial support extended during the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. Airport Authority of India (AAI) has entered into partnership with the Construction Industry Development Council (CIDC) to impart skill training to youths in the aviation sector. Over the past four years, 4047 candidates have been trained in the said skill training programmes and 911 are being trained in the said undergoing skill training programme. A sum of INR 29.07 crore has been released by AAI to the CIDC.(c) Yes, Sir. The youth trained under the skill training programmes have been provided job opportunities. Out of the total trained candidates under the skill training programmes, 60 percent candidates have been employed in various job roles.(d) & (e) Yes, Sir. AAI has signed two tripartite Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with National Skill Development Fund and National Skill Development Corporation for setting up multi skill development centres. Over 1600 candidates have been trained in the  training centres. For setting up multi skill development centre, INR 3.82 crore has been paid out of the total budget of INR 7.25 crore as on date.
