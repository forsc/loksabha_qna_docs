                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4150






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Exclusion of Madrassa from School Category




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the Government of Maharasthra has excluded Muslim Minority Madrassas from school category;  (b)	if so, the reasons therefor and whether a State Government can take such decisions; (c)	whether the Government proposes to chalk out a clear policy to ensure that all the subjects are taught in all the Madrassa across the country; and (d)	if so, the time by when it is likely to be implemented and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b):	As informed by the State Government of Maharashtra, there are 150 Madarsas in the State covered under UDISE and the State’s SARAL database. Hence, they are not excluded as schools.	(c) & (d): 	The Ministry of Education has been implementing a Scheme for Providing Quality Education in Madrassa (SPQEM) since 2014-15. The scheme provides financial assistance to encourage traditional institutions like Madarsas and Maktabs to introduce modern education through subjects such as Science, Mathematics, Social Studies, Hindi and English in their curriculum so that academic proficiency for classes I-XII is attainable for children studying in these institutions.*****
