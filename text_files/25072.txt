                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

740






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Ayurvedic Doctors under AYUSH




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) the number of posts sanctioned for Ayurvedic doctors under AYUSH Ministry in Madhya Pradesh including the posts lying vacant at present district-wise along with the reason for these vacancies;(b) the details of the authority, State or Centre responsible to fill these posts;(c) whether there is any provision for filling the posts under National Health Mission (NHM) in case the State does not fill the posts;(d) if so, the reasons for not filling the posts thereunder;(e) if not, whether the Government is likely to make provision to fill the posts under National Health Mission (NHM); and(f) the number of Ayurvedic colleges currently functional for Ayurveda medical studies in the country including the number of students being awarded degrees each year, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(DR. MUNJPARA MAHENDRABHAI)(a) & (b):	Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National AYUSH Mission (NAM), 337 posts of Community Health Officers (CHOs)- Ayurveda  have been approved on a contractual basis in the State of Madhya Pradesh for operationalization of AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres. District wise number of CHOs-Ayurveda approved in Madhya Pradesh is furnished at Annexure-I. However, sanctioning regular posts and filling vacant posts comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments, as Public health is a State subject. (c) to (e):	Under the National Health Mission, support for engaging AYUSH doctors on a contractual basis at co-located health facilities are provided based on their requirement posed by the States/UTs in their Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs). 172 Ayurvedic doctors are working under National Health Mission in Madhya Pradesh on a contractual basis.(f):	The number of Ayurvedic colleges currently functional for Ayurveda medical studies in the country including the number of students being awarded degrees each year, State/UT-wise is furnished at Annexure-II.
