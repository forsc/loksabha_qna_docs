                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2110






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Scholarships for Reserved Category Students




Chhedi Paswan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the facilities including scholarship being provided to the students belonging toScheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe and Other Backward Class category in the country State-wise;(b) the funds sanctioned and released State-wise including Bihar for the said purpose during eachof the last three years and the current year;(c) the details of the scholarship amount to be provided to the students belonging to ScheduledCaste, Scheduled Tribe and Other Backward Class Categories in Bihar;(d) whether the prisoners in jail are being given more facilities in terms of food as compared tothe Dalit students and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of the expenditure made during each of the last three years for providing food tothe students belonging to Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe and Other Backward Class categoryin Government residential schools of Bihar district-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c): The Department of Higher Education, Department of School Education and Literacy,All India Council for Technical Education, University Grants Commission, Department ofScience and Technology, Ministry of Tribal Affairs and the Ministry of Social Justice andEmpowerment are providing financial assistance in the form of scholarship /interest subsidyschemes for the students belonging to Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe and Other BackwardClass category students. The details of the scholarship/ interest subsidy schemes and thescholarship amount provided to the students belonging to Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe andOther Backward Class Categories including the State of Bihar are provided at Annexure-1(available at https://www.education.gov.in/en/parl_ques ). The State-wise and year-wise detailsof funds disbursal including the State of Bihar are at Annexure-2 to Annexure-21 (available athttps://www.education.gov.in/en/parl_ques ).(d) to (e): As per the information received from the concerned Ministry, ‘prisons’ and ‘personsdetained therein’ are ''State'' subjects as per Entry 4 of List II of Seventh Schedule to theConstitution of India. Administration and management of prisons is primarily the responsibilityof State Governments, who are competent to take appropriate decisions in their respectivejurisdictions.Mid-Day Meal Scheme (MDMS) is being run by the Department of School Educationand Literacy, which is a Centrally-Sponsored Scheme covering all school children studying inClasses I-VIII of Government, Government-Aided Schools, Special Training Centres includingMadarsas and Maqtabs supported under Samagra Shiksha. The Scheme is implemented all overthe country irrespective of the caste, creed and religion for its target group. Residential schoolsare not covered under the scheme.
