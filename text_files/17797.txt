                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






CRPF Raising Day




C.N. Annadurai













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has observed the 82nd CRPF Raising Day of Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) in the country;(b) if so, the details of the programmes organized by the Government on this occasion;(c) the contribution and achievements made by CRPF in respect of internal security since its inception;(d) whether the Government proposes to enhance the strength of CRPF in view of huge workload and if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government has taken steps to overhaul and strengthen Central Reserve Police Force and turn it into a world class counter-insurgency force in order to keep pace with the dynamic nature of insurgency and terror situations, if so, the details thereof and the success achieved in this regard; and(f) the other steps taken/being taken by the  Government for the welfare of CRPF personnel as well as their families?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):  82nd Raising Day of CRPF was observed on 27th July, 2020 in e-format following due COVID protocol.  The following Programmes were organized on this occasion. -2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.1421 FOR 20.09.2020(i)    Three short  video films on “Nature Warriors”, “Covid Warriors” and “Internal Security Warriors”  as well as “Sambhav” App were released;(ii)   Neemuch, the birth place of CRPF was connected through Webinar and  wreath laying ceremony was  held at Shourya Sthal;(iii)  1624 officers/men and 3248 officers/ men were awarded “Ati Utkristh Seva Padak” and “Utkristh Seva Padak” respectively.(c):  CRPF, the largest Central Armed Police Force, has immensely contributed to internal security and is deployed in various major theatres of internal disturbances viz. LWE Areas, J&K and North East.  CRPF is also deployed for maintenance of law and order in various parts of the country on the request of State Governments.  In addition, CRPF has also been guarding some important installations/institutions and providing security to VIPs.(d): To cater to internal security requirements, 04 new battalions were approved for raising in the year 2018.(e):  Strengthening of Central Armed Police Forces including CRPF, is an ongoing continuous process. To modernize and improve efficiency of CAPFs, the Government has approved modernization plan for equipping them with latest technology, modern weaponry and vehicles. Further, 18 pre-induction training nodes  have  been established in CRPF to  impart theatre  specific training  to the-3/...-3-LS.US.Q.NO.1421 FOR 20.09.2020newly inducted personnel in order to physically and mentally prepare them to meet challenges posed by adversaries in the respective theatres and to augment their operational performance/ efficiency.   (f): Some of the steps taken by the Government for welfare of CAPFs personnel and their families, including CRPF, are given at Annexure.*****
