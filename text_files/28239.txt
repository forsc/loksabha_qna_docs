                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

13






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Funds Allocated for Welfare of Tea Workers




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether it is a fact that the Government in Budget 2021 has declared to provide Rs.1000.00 crore for the welfare of tea workers of Assam and West Bengal;(b)	If so, the details of the funds allocated to the respective States; and (c)	if no funds have been allocated so far, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        FINANCE MINISTER				(SMT. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN) (a)  to (c): A statement is placed on the Table of the House.*****Statement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No.*13 for answer on November 29, 2021 raised by Sh. Pallab Lochan Das, Member of Parliament regarding “Funds Allocated for Welfare of Tea Workers”:(a) to (c) A provision of Rs.200.00 crore is available in Budget of 2021-22. Allocation/release from the Budget for a scheme is determined by the guidelines therefor.
