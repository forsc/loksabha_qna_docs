                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Online Information for Availability of Beds




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to start providing online information about availability of beds and ventilators in Government hospitals so as to facilitate the general public with this information at home;(b) if so, the details thereof, State wise; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c)	‘Public Health and Hospitals’ being state subjects, such information is not maintained centrally.However, as far as three Central Government Hospitals in Delhi i.e. Safdarjung, Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia and Lady Hardinge Medical College & Associated Hospitals are concerned, the online information regarding availability of Intensive Care Unit (ICU) beds and ventilators is being provided on respective hospital’s website. Further, centralised information regarding availability of Intensive Care Unit (ICU) beds and ventilators for above mentioned Central Government Hospitals is now available on website of Safdarjung Hospital. The URL of the website is as under:http://www.vmmc-sjh.nic.in/forms/contentpage.aspx?lid=3255
