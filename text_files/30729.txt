                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

217






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Committee to Promote Indian Languages




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has constituted a high-powered committee toexplore and recommend pathways for promotion and growth of Indian languagesas envisaged in the National Education Policy (NEP), 2020;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of members nominated in the said committee along withtheir qualifications; and(d) the time by which the said committee is likely to submit its report to theGovernment?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) : A statement is laid on the table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOKSABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.217 TO BE ANSWERED ON 13.12.2021ASKED BY DR.T.R.PAARIVENDHAR, HON’BLE M.P. REGARDING“Committee to Promote Indian Languages”(a) to (d): Yes Sir. Government of India has constituted a High PoweredCommittee under the Chairmanship of Padmashri Chamu Krishna Shastry toexplore and recommend pathways for the holistic and multidisciplinary growth ofIndian Languages as envisaged in the National Education Policy, 2020. TheCommittee’s mandate is, inter-alia, to advise the Ministry on all matterspertaining to revitalization of existing language teaching and research and itsextension in various institutions in the country. Director, Central Institute ofIndian Languages (CIIL), Mysuru, Vice Chancellor, Shri Lal Bahadur ShastriNational Sanskrit University, New Delhi and Joint Secretary in the Ministry ofEducation are made as ex-officio members of the High Powered Committee. TheCommittee has been asked to submit a road map and periodic reports from timeto time.****
