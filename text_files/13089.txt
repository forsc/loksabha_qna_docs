                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2068






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Loans for Sugar Mills




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has sanctioned affordable loans to the sugar mills to clear their dues payable to sugarcane growers for the sugarcane crushing seasons of 2018-19 and 2019-20; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):		With a view to facilitate payment of cane dues of the farmers for the sugar season 2018-19, the Central Government notified a Scheme for extending soft loan to sugar mills vide notification dated 02.03.2019.(b):		Under the scheme, loans of about Rs.7402 crore have been disbursed by the banks to 321 Sugar mills for which interest subvention amounting to Rs. 518 crore @ 7 % per annum for one year would be borne by Central Government. A statement giving details of State/UT- wise soft loan amount is annexed. ****ANNEXURESTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (b) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2068 FOR ANSWERED ON 03.03.2020 IN THE LOK SABHA Name of the State	Number of sugar mills	Amount of Soft Loan Disbursed by Banks(in crore Rs.)Karnataka	39	884.00Tamil Nadu	28	168.35Andhra Pradesh	9	112.83Uttar Pradesh	82	2817.50Maharashtra	131	2753.00Bihar	8	181.57Punjab	12	155.50Gujarat	12	329.54Total	321	7402.29******
