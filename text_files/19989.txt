                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Transfer of 5G Technology




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the list of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed by the Ministry with other countries during each of the last five years for the transfer of 5G technologies;(b) 	the objectives of the said MoUs;(c) 	whether BSNL has planned to introduce 5G services in the country; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof along with the expected year of introduction of the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b) Sir, Department of Telecommunications, Ministry of Communications has not signed Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for transfer of the 5G technologies. However, following Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)/ Memorandum of Cooperation (MoC) have been signed with other countries during each of last five years which envisage cooperation in various areas in the field of ICTs/telecommunications including 5G technologies:    <pre>Year	Country with which MoU/ MoC is signed	Signing Date2016	NIL	------2017	NIL	------2018	South Korea	10.07.2018	Vietnam	20.11.20182019	Cambodia	18.03.20192020	Myanmar	27.02.20202021	Japan (Memorandum of Cooperation)	15.01.2021 </pre> (c)	BSNL has not yet planned for launch of 5G services in its network. (d) 	Not applicable in view of (c) above. 	*****
