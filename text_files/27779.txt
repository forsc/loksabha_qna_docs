                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3403






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Funds to Museum for Tribal Freedom Fighters




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether any steps have been taken by the Government to setup museums for tribal freedom fighters of India, details thereof; (b) the details of the progress of gathering information and documentation of the contributions and sacrifices made by tribal people in the freedom struggle; (c) whether the Government has invited comments and contributions by the public to gather information and documentation on contributions and sacrifices made by tribal people in the freedom struggle and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therfor; (d) whether any proposals for any museums have been approved and the details of funds released by the Government to setup such museums; and (e) whether the Government has any plans or received any proposal to setup a similar museum or a division in any national museum in New Delhi and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA) (a) to(d) Yes Sir, Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) provides financial assistance  under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme ''Support to Tribal Research Institutes'' to states/UTs for setting up Tribal Freedom Fighters Museum to acknowledge the heroic and patriotic deeds of tribal people who struggled against the Britishers and refused to be bowed down so that the coming generations may know how tribals were involved in making sacrifices for the nation.  Under the scheme, Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) has constituted a National Level Committee (NLC) for monitoring of such projects.  Ministry has sanctioned 10  projects  to construct museums  for tribal freedom fighters in different States based on their proposals.
