                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3672






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Assistance Under VKGUY to promote export of Skimmed Milk Powder




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government provides assistance under the Vishesh Krishi and Gram Udyog Yojana (VKGUY)to promote the export of skimmed milk powder and if so, the details thereof;(b)	 the percentage of increase in exports of skimmed milk powder as a result of such assistance programs;(c)	the quantum of skimmed milk powder exported from the country during each of the last three years, year/country-wise; and(d)	whether the Government is running any scheme to promote production of skimmed milk in the country and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the details of other steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)		As per the information received from Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry, the benefits under Vishesh Krishi Gram Udyog Yojana (VKGUY) were available only for exports made upto 31.03.2015. For exports from 01.04.2015 to 31.12.2020, the Merchandise Exports from India Scheme (MEIS) was under implementation under the Foreign Trade Policy 2015-20. MEIS rewards for Skimmed Milk Powder, ITC HS Code – 04021010 were only for a period of about six months in the FY 2018-19, at 10% of Free on Board (FOB) value of exports, for exports made between 13.07.2018 to 26.09.2018 and 20% for exports made between 27.09.2018 to 12.01.2019. From 13.01.2019, no rewards under MEIS on export of Skimmed Milk powder were available. MEIS Scheme has been discontinued from 01.01.2021.(b)		As per the information available on the website of Ministry of Commerce and Industry, the export during 2018-19 for skimmed milk powder (ITCHS code 04021010) was 154.29% higher than that during 2017-18.(c)		As per the information received from Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence and Statistics, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, export data of skimmed milk powder for three years is attached at Annexure-I.(d)		This Department is implementing following schemes to develop processing infrastructure of milk and milk products including skimmed milk powder in the country:i.	Dairy Processing Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF)ii.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF)(e)		As per the information received from Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry, a scheme named “Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (RoDTEP)” has been introduced in March 2020 to create a mechanism for re-imbursement of taxes/ duties/ levies, which are currently not being refunded under any other mechanism, at the central, state and local level, but which are incurred in the process of manufacture and distribution of exported products. Major component of such taxes is electricity duty and VAT on fuels used in transportation/ distribution. The scheme has been operationalized for exports with effect from 01.01.2021.*****
