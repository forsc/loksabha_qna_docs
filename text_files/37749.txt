                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4967






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Fratricide in Armed Forces




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the cases of fratricide in Army and other armed forces have increased in the recent years;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to prevent such incidents in the future?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) to (c):  No cases of fratricide have been reported in the Navy and Air Force in recent years.  The number of cases of fratricide in the Indian Army since 2019 till date are as under:Year	2019	2020	2021	2022Fratricide case(s)	02	02	02	01The Government has taken several steps towards stress amelioration amongst troops, which, inter-alia, include deployment of trained psychological counsellors, improvement in the quality of food and clothing, training in stress management, provision of recreational facilities, buddy system, leave concessions, approachability of seniors, facilities for movement of troops from border areas and establishing a grievance redressal mechanism at various levels, etc.******
