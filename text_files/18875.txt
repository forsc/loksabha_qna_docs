                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Doubling of Railway Tracks




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of railway tracks where doubling work has been under taken during last three years in Bharatpur and Alwar of Rajasthan;(b) whether Agra-Bandikui has been included in the above doubling work; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the amount proposed to be spent on this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): Railway projects are not sanctioned District-wise/State-wise but zone-wise, as Indian Railways’ network spans across various District/State boundaries. Doubling of Alwar-Bandikui (60.37 Km) falling partly in Alwar District has been completed. Doubling of Agra Fort-Bandikui (150 Km) section falling partly in Bharatpur district has been included in the Budget 2018-19 subject to requisite Government approvals. Detailed Project Report (DPR) for doubling of Agra Fort - Bandikui has been prepared. Project is not yet sanctioned.*****
