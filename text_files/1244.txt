                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1339






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Separate University for AYUSH




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has any proposal to set up separate university for AYUSH in order to promote various disciplines under AYUSH and if so, the details thereof; (b) the number of colleges set up under this scheme so far, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra and Rajasthan; (c) whether the State Governments of Maharashtra and Rajasthan have sent any proposal to the Government for setting up of AYUSH universities;(d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government thereon along with the time by which the said proposals are likely to be approved by the Government; and (e) whether the Government is likely to help in discontinuing old practices and beliefs which are still present in the system and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a)	 & (b):    The Government  has not received  any proposal to set up separate university for AYUSH in order to promote various disciplines under AYUSH.(c) & (d):   The State Governments of Maharashtra and Rajasthan has not sent any proposal to the Government for setting up of AYUSH universities.(e):      All AYUSH education institutes are imparting education according to the Indian Medicine Central Council (Minimum standards of education in Indian Medicine) amendment regulations and Homeopathic (Degree course) Regulations.………………..
