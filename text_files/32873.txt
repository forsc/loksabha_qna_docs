                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Involvement of Locals/NGOs in Urban Schemes




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the involvement of locals/NGOs was missing in some interior areas in States where insurgency was affecting the lives of the people and also affecting various schemes and programmes such as Housing for All, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana  etc; and;(b) if so, the details thereof and the corrective steps being taken by the Government during the last five years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                                      THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Urban Development is a State subject. Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs assists the States / Union Territories (UTs) in urban development through its Missions- Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) 2.0, Smart Cities Mission (SCM), Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) 2.0, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) and also addresses livelihood concerns under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) and PM Street Vendor’s Atma Nirbhar Nidhi(PM SVANidhi).  During the implementation of the Schemes the States/UTs have not highlighted any issue which is affecting the progress of the Scheme due to non-involvement of locals/NGOs.(b)	Does not arise.                                      *****
