                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

38






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Availability of Fertilizers




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has ensured the sufficient availability of essential fertilizers to the farmers in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the farmers strike has, in any way, affected the transportation of such fertilizers; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a):	Yes Sir;(b):	For the ongoing Rabi-2020-21 season (as on 26.01.2021), the data has been shown below:-                                                                                       <Fig in LMT>S.NO.	PRODUCT               PRO RATA         AVAILABILITY AS        DBT SALES        GROUP	              REQUIREMENT AS   ON 26.01.2021          AS ON                               ON 26.01.2021		              26.01.20211	UREA	                145.98	         180.43	              121.792	DAP	                47.07	         73.96	              53.473	MOP	                11.17	         25.43	              11.084	NPKS	                39.97	         72.27	              39.28The above figure shows that there is comfortable availability of fertilizers in the country.(c) & (d):	 Yes Sir, due to farmers strike and subsequently the Rail blockage, the transportation of fertilizer was affected in J&K, Punjab and Himachal Pradesh. However this Department ensured sufficient availability of fertilizer at state level by transportation of fertilizer by Road.                                              ********
