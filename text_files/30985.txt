                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Minor Millet Rice




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that minor millet rice would be procured by cooperative societies and distributed through the Public Distribution System (PDS) in cities/ towns in each State; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY, CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION AND TEXTILES(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE STARRED QUESTION NO.247 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 15.12.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.(a) & (b): 	As per guidelines for allocation, distribution and disposal of coarse grains, States are allowed to procure coarse grain (Jowar, Bajra, Maize & Ragi etc.)  from farmers at Minimum Support Price (MSP) under central pool with the prior approval of Government of India. The procured quantity has to be distributed under Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS)/Other Welfare Schemes (OWS) in the States/UTs and the combined allocation of wheat and rice to the equivalent quantity of coarse grains shall stand automatically reduced. Government of India permits procurement of those coarse grains/millets for which MSP is declared on the basis of the recommendations of the Commission for Agricultural Costs and Prices (CACP) to ensure remunerative price of produce to farmers. Presently, millets like Jowar, Bajra and Ragi are covered under MSP. Government of India has not received any procurement proposal for procurement of Minor Millet Rice from any State Government.*****
