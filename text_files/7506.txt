                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1219






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Financial Assistance for Rajkiya Mahotsav in Itkhori




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any financial assistance has been given for the Rajkiya Mahotsav in Itkhori, Jharkhand during the last five years; (b) 	if so, the amount given and the date on which it was released; (c) 	the reason for not releasing funds for Rajkiya Itkhori Mahotsav in 2019; and (d) 	the time by which the Government proposes to release this fund?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir. In the year 2017-18 Central Financial Assistance of Rs.25 lakh was provided to Government of Jharkhand on 23.03.2018 for Celebration of Itkhori Festival.(c) & (d):	Ministry of Tourism has already sanctioned Rs.50 lakhs for Celebration of Bhairavnath Mahotsav and Sharad Mahotsav, Nehtrahat amounting to Rs.25 lakhs each. As per the relevant guidelines, Ministry of Tourism provides maximum of Rs.50 lakh Central Financial Assistance to various States for Celebration of Fairs & Festivals in one financial year. ********
