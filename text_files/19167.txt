                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Procurement of Agriculture Produces by FCI




Anto  Antony













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                








                                                    Anil Firojiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of procurement of wheat, rice and other agricultural produces by Food Corporation of India (FCI) during the last five years, year-wise and itemwise; (b) 	the details of storage capacity of FCI godowns/ warehouses in the country during the said period; (c) 	whether the FCI is facing shortage of storage capacities; (d) 	whether the Government has given permission to establish warehouses in the private sector; (e) 	if so, the details of such warehouses established in private sector during the last five years, year-wise and State-wise; (f) 	whether FCI has taken loan from the National Small Saving Fund or any other agencies; (g) 	if so, the details of such loans taken by the FCI during the said period, year-wise; and (h) 	whether the Government has any plan to privatise the Public Distribution System and if so, the details thereof?

(d) and (e): The Government has collaborated with private sector to meet the requirement of storage space for foodgrain under following schemes:1- Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG): Government of India has formulated a scheme called Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) Scheme for construction of covered storage capacity.  Under this Scheme, storage capacity is created by private parties, CWC, SWCs and other State Agencies for guaranteed hiring by FCI. The State wise details are at Annexure-II. State/year-wise achievement of PEG scheme during the last five years and current year are attached at Annexure-III.    2.
