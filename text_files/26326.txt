                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

189






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Standardisation of Online Education




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the University Grants Commission (UGC) has released any recommendations on the standardisation of online education taking into account that classes in many educational establishments have moved online;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether measures have been taken to change assessment and evaluation practices in a uniform manner across the country due to a change in class nature and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government plans to formulate long term digital education strategy for Secondary Education also?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)Part (a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the table of the House.*****2STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *189 TO BE ANSWERED ON 02.08.2021 ASKED BY SHRI Y.S. AVINASH REDDY, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING “STANDARDISATION OF ONLINE EDUCATION”.(a) to (b): Yes Sir, the UGC has notified University Grants Commission (Open and Distance Learning Programmes and Online Programmes) Regulations, 2020 on 04th September, 2020, which can be accessed on https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/221580.pdf. Subsequently, an amendment was notified on 1st July, 2021 in the Gazette which is available on UGC website at link https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/6852408_UGC(ODL-Programmes-and-Online-Programmes)(Amendment)Regulations,-2021.pdf(c): Yes Sir, the UGC has issued guidelines on Examinations and Academic Calendar July-2021, in view of emergence and prevalence of online education, in order to tackle Covid-19 Pandemic. This can be accessed on-https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/3342282_UGC-Guidelines-on-Examinations-and-Academic-Calendar---July-2021.pdf(d): The long-term Digital Education Strategy for Secondary Education is included in the National Education Policy 2020.*********
