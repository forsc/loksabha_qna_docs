                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4728






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Vostok Oil Project




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India and Russia have begun preliminary talks on the participation of Indian companies in the Vostok Oil project in Siberia, if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether any agreements/MoUs have been made to this effect, if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) A consortium of Indian public sector oil companies comprising ONGC Videsh Ltd. (OVL), Oil India Limited (OIL), Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) and Bharat Petro Resources Limited (BPRL) continue to explore opportunities for participating in Vostok project of Rosneft Oil Company.(b) An Agreement was signed on 17th September, 2019 between Consortium of Indian Companies and Rosneft Oil Company for cooperation in participation in the Vostok project. *****
