                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3635






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Use of Drone in Transportation and Logistics




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to use drone as a technology for various transportation and logistics related work; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b)  Drones offer tremendous benefits to almost all sectors of the economy and in public welfare. Drones can be significant creators of employment and economic growth due to their reach, versatility, and ease of use, especially in India''s remote and inaccessible areas. The Government proposes to use drone as a technology and as a service in all sectors of the economy, including transportation and logistics related work. Various Union Ministries have been requested to encourage entities under their purview to further promote use of drones. The drones are already used for various purposes in transportation, surveillance, incident response, inspection and maintenance, project monitoring, disaster management, etc.;******
