                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1863






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Assistance for Sports Infrastructure




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao













                                                    Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the assistance being provided to States by the Union Government and Sports Authority of India for construction of sports infrastructure has been stopped, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;  (b) whether the Union Government provides funds to State Governments for setting up and running sports academies for different games;  (c) whether several States, particularly Karnataka, have prepared proposals regarding sports infrastructure and setting up of sports academies and sent it for approval to the Union Government; and  (d) if so, the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Union Government for approval of such proposals? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS  { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }  (a) No, Madam/Sir.  (b) Yes, Madam/Sir. Under the Khelo India Scheme, there is a provision to provide funds to eligible agencies of State Governments for setting up and running Sports Academies.  (c) and (d) The receipt of proposals from State/Union Territory Governments including those from the State of Karnataka, and other eligible entities for development of sports, including creation/upgradation of sports infrastructure and setting up of sports academies, is a continuous process. Proposals, when received, are considered for financial assistance subject to adherence to the norms of the Scheme, their technical feasibility and availability of funds. So far, a total of 08 sports infrastructure projects have been sanctioned in the State of Karnataka under the Khelo India Scheme.  ****
