                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4507






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Urban Haats




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















a)	whether the Government proposes to establish ‘Urban Haats’ in various districts of Bihar, particularly Kishanganj; and b)	If so, the details thereof, if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): Yes sir, one proposal has been received to establish Urban Haat for Handicrafts and Craftsman at Madhubani, Bihar from District Rural Development Authority (DRDA) Madhubani, Bihar and was approved by Project Approval Monitoring Committee (PAMC) held on 31st July 2018. There is no proposal received for “Urban Haat” at Kishanganj.********
