                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2656






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Galwan Encounter




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether China has admitted loss of lives of their commandos in the Galwan encounter;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the present status of disengagement at China border?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (c)	 On 19 February 2021, the Central Military Commission of China announced awarding posthumously honorary titles and merit citations to Chinese soldiers. As per the announcement, these titles were awarded for the role of these soldiers in the faceoff at Galwan Valley in June 2020.Hon’ble Raksha Mantri had made a statement in the Rajya Sabha on 11 February 2021, wherein he conveyed that India and China had reached an agreement on disengagement in the North and South Bank of the Pangong Lake. The disengagement in Pangong Lake area has been completed.Government will continue discussions with the Chinese side to resolve the remaining issues along the Line of Actual Control in Eastern Ladakh and achieve the objective of disengagement from all friction points and restoration of peace and tranquillity in the India-China Border Areas at an early date.***
