                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

69






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Funds under NCLP




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under the National Child Labour Project (NCLP), State/ UT wise particularly in Maharashtra during the last three years and the current year;(b)the details of beneficiaries of PENCIL (Platform for Effective Enforcement for No Child Labour) which had been created under the NCLP scheme; (c)the data of web visits on PENCIL (Platform for Effective Enforcement for No Child Labour) which had been created under the NCLP scheme;(d)the data of adolescent children rescued from hazardous work situations; and(e)whether the Government is able to raise awareness amongst the target communities and the other stakeholders regarding child labour about the functionality of NCLP?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	The Grant-in-Aid released during 2017-18 to 2020-21 under the National Child Labour Project (NCLP) Scheme, State-wise including Maharashtra is at Annexure-I.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(b):	The details of beneficiaries of PENCIL (Platform for Effective Enforcement for No Child Labour) under the NCLP scheme is at Annexure-II. (c):	The total number of web visits as on 24.11.2021 on PENCiL (Platform for Effective Enforcement for No Child Labour) by PENCiL registered users is 85,23,763 .(d):	As per data available on PENCiL portal as on 24.11.2021, 1151 number of adolescents in the age bracket of 14 to 18 years have been withdrawn from work.  (e):	The Ministry carries out public awareness about child labour through its website and various social media handles. In addition, District Project Society also carries out the awareness programme for which funds are provided by Ministry of Labour & Employment.**                                                 *******
