                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3753






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Crisis in Food Processing Industries




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any crisis in the food processing industries;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of the proposals from FPI units still pending for tax exemption; and(d)	the details of the remedial steps proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): No, Sir.(c) & (d): The proposal pertaining to direct taxes including those seeking concessions or exemptions are received every year and are examined during the budgetary exercise for the Union Budget. The outcome of the examination of the proposals is reflected in the Finance Bill which is tabled before the Parliament along with the Budget. The Finance Bill, 2020 was presented in the Lok Sabha on 1st February, 2020.*****
