                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Janjatiya Vikas Mission and if so, the details thereof and the aims and objective behind the move;(b) the number of most backward tribal dominated districts that will be targeted under this mission;(c) the estimated number of tribals in remote and maoist hit areas who will be provided employment opportunities; and(d) the other steps taken by the Government for the welfare of tribals living in most backward areas and maoist hit areas?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)   (a) to (c) Ministry of Tribal Affairs has not so far launched Pradhan Mantri Janjatiya Vikas Mission. The Ministry has envisaged a comprehensive livelihood Mission Mode development programme catering to STs for a period 2021-22 to 2025-26.(d) The Ministry of Tribal Affairs implements various schemes for the welfare and development of Scheduled Tribes (STs) including education, health & nutrition, livelihood, agriculture, horticulture, skill & entrepreneurship development, drinking water & sanitation, road connectivity etc. and infrastructure incidental thereto.  Details of funds provided by the Ministry to the States and other implementing agencies under various schemes for the last three years is at Annexure.
