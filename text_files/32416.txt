                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3848






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Vacant posts of IPS




Bhagwant Mann














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is contemplating to fill the vacant posts of IPS in the country especially in the State of Punjab, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(b) the number and details of vacant posts of IPS have been filled during the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)	The vacant posts of IPS in the country including in the State of Punjab are filled up on regular basis. The Government of India has increased the recruitment to IPS from 150 to 200 from 2020 Civil Services Examination.(b)	From 01.01.2019, 300 IPS officers have been appointed through the Civil Service Examinations and 239 IPS officers inducted from the State Police Service. *****
