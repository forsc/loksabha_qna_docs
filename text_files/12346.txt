                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Coastal Tourism Clusters




Francisco Cosme Sardinha














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up coastal tourism clusters and tourist parks to encourage tourism on a large scale; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the details of such clusters and parks proposed to be set up in Goa and Karnataka?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Tourism under the  Swadesh Darshan Scheme is developing tourism infrastructure in the country, in a planned and prioritized manner. Coastal Circuit has been identified as one of the fifteen thematic circuit for development with an objective to develop basic infrastructure in the Coastal areas of the country including Tourist Parks and Clusters.The projects under the scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Based on the criteria, Ministry has sanctioned two projects in Goa under the Coastal Circuit:-•	Development of Sinquerim-Baga, Anjuna-Vagator, Morjim-Keri, Aguada Fort and Aguada Jail for Rs.99.99 Cr in 2016-17.•	Development of Rua De Orum Creek - Don Paula -Colva - Benaulim for Rs.99.35 Cr in 2017-18.No project has been sanctioned in Karnataka under the scheme.********
