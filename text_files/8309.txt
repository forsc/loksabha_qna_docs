                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1942






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Departmental Promotion Committee




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the number of posts lying vacant in Directorate of Doordarshan and Directorate of Aakashwani as on date, State-wise;(b) the steps taken by the Government to fill up these vacant posts in the country including Uttarakhand;(c)	 the progress made in regard to conduct Department Promotion Committee(DPC) for regular promotions by the Ministry; and(d)   the time frame fixed for the same by the Ministry?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) State-wise number of posts lying vacant in Doordarshan and All India Radio as informed by Prasar Bharati is enclosed at Annexure-I&II respectively.(b) to (d) Limited recruitment has been undertaken as a special dispensation through Staff Selection Commission for critical Group “B” and “C” vacancies, approved by the Government, on an All India basis, including vacancies in Uttarakhand.              The Ministry and Prasar Bharati have conducted DPCs in accordance with the extant instructions of Department of Personnel & Training (DoP&T).*******
