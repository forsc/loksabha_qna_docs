                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Code of Conduct for Pharma Companies




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government proposes to direct all pharma companies to put an end to the practice of offering money/gifts to doctors for promotion and sale of their drugs; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government proposes for a code of conduct to be followed by the pharma companies as well which may be binding in nature and prevents the pharma companies from gifting doctors; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




(a) to (d): The Uniform Code of Pharmaceutical Marketing Practices (UCPMP) announced by the Department of Pharmaceuticals in December, 2014 and which is in operation since 01.01.2015 provides guidance to the industry for promotion and marketing of drugs and medical devices. At present the code is voluntarily adopted and complied with by the pharma industry. UCPMP is available on the website of the Department of Pharmaceuticals www.pharmaceuticals.gov.in.xxxxx
