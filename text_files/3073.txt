                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2929






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






MoU with Poland




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Government has signed an MoU with the Ministry of Energy, Republic of Poland recently;(b)    if so, the details thereof along with its objectives;(c)    the details of the fund estimated for this;(d)    the details of the States likely to be covered initially under this programmme within the country along with the details of the targets set; and(e)    whether the Government has included Gujarat in this programme, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES ( SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI ) (a) to (e)    A non-binding Memorandum of Understanding (MoU), on cooperation in the area of coal mining with an objective to emphasize on coal exploration and exploitation, research and development, clean coal technologies and capacity building, was signed between the Ministry of Coal of the Republic of India and the Ministry of Energy of the Republic of Poland on 04.02.2019. The participants are to bear the respective expenditure related to Working Group meetings and any other activities under this MoU. The Ministry of Coal, Republic of India is the executing agency of the MoU. *****
