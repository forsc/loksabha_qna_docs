                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3394






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Achievements of Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the achievements of the Swadesh Darshan Scheme as on date; (b) 	whether the Government has achieved the objective of the said scheme and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	whether the Government plans to expand the said scheme to include more theme-based Circuits in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	For integrated development of Theme-Based Tourist Circuits in the country, Ministry of Tourism launched Swadesh Darshan Scheme in 2014-15. Since its launch, the Ministry has sanctioned 76 projects for    Rs.5588.44 crores pertaining to tourism infrastructure development, out of which 11 projects are physically complete and 34 projects are nearing physical completion. The remaining projects are under various stages of implementation. Sanctioning of new projects  in  the  scheme is  under review.********
