                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3363






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






PMMVY




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether it is true that around 1.28 crore beneficiaries have received Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) directly in their bank accounts amounting to Rs.5280 crore and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the number of beneficiaries who have received full financial assistance of Rs.5000 under the scheme; (c) 	the total number of children born during the last three years; and(d)	the number of births registered under the PMMVY during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is a maternity benefit scheme and as on  9th March 2020  1.36 Crore of eligible beneficiaries have received maternity benefit of one or more than one installment through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) directly in their bank accounts amounting to Rs.5657.10 Crore. Number of beneficiaries paid State-wise and amount disbursed is annexed.(b)	As on 09.03.2020, 85,56,731 beneficiaries have received full financial assistance of Rs.5,000 under the Scheme.(c)	The number of children born during last three years is as follows:Financial Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 (Till January 2020)Number of children born	2,07,84,374	2,08,89,154	1,75,45,264(d)	The number of births for which third instalment under PMMVY has been claimed by the eligible beneficiaries is as under:  Financial Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20(As on     6.03.2020)Number of births  for which third
