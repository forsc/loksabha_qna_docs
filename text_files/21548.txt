                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2641






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Funds allocated for South Western Railway




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has allocated Rs 3245 crore for South    Western Railway to implement railway projects for 2021-22;(b)	if so, the details thereof including new lines sanctioned, doubling projects, electrification works, etc. that have been approved;(c)	the present status of Kadur to Chikmagalur, Mysore to Mangalore, Birur to Talaguppa, Chikkbanavar to Hassan and Mysore to Chamarajanagar electrification projects as on 31 January 2021;(d)	the present status of doubling projects viz. Birur to Shimoga, Kankanadi to Panambur, Arasikere to Tumkur, Hubli to Chikjajur and Hospte to Vasco as on 31 January, 2021;(e)	whether there are still more than 3000 km of railway lines to be electrified which is falling fully/partly in the State of Karnataka till date and if so, the details thereof; and(f)	the quantum of funds allocated for electrification during the current year and whether it is sufficient to meet the target?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	to (f) : A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
