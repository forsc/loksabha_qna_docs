                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Women Beneficiaries under PMJDY




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the number of beneficiary women account holders of Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package in Delhi;(b) the details of the amount of money distributed by the Government in Delhi; and(c) the details of the number of beneficiaries who withdraw money from their bank accounts within 15 days of receiving the money?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) to (c) The scheme of one-time ex-gratia payment of Rs 500/- per month for 3 months (April, May and June 2020) to women Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) account holders, announced by the Union Minister for Finance on 26th March 2020, as part of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY), was administered by the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD).As per the reports submitted to MoRD by various banks, a total of 20,63,036 women Jan Dhan Account holders have benefited through the ex-gratia payment of Rs.500 per month for three months (April-June 2020) under PMGKY in the state of Delhi. An amount of Rs.30,626.515 lakhs has been credited to the bank accounts of eligible beneficiaries in the state of Delhi. The data regarding the number of beneficiaries who withdrew money from their bank accounts within 15 days of receiving the money is not centrally maintained.*****
