                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

41






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






E-Shram Portal




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                








                                                    Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has launched e-Shram Portal and if so, the details thereof and aims and objective behind the move;(b)the number of workers from unorganized sector who have been registered on e-Shram Portal;(c)the details of benefits of various welfare schemes that will be available to workers after joining the portal;(d)whether the Government has been able to create a National database of unorganized workers with the launch of the portal and if so, the details thereof; and (e)the States that have largest number of registration under the portal? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (e):	Ministry of Labour & Employment has developed e-SHRAM portal which is a National Database of the Unorganised Workers seeded with Aadhaar. It has been launched on 26.08.2021 and made available to the States/ UTs for the registration of unorganised workers on e-SHRAM portal.  As on 23rd November, 2021, 8.97 crore unorganised workers have been registered on e-SHRAM portal. All eligible registered unorganised workers will get benefit of an accidental insurance cover of Rs 2.0 Lakh for a year free of cost through Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY). One of the key objectives of the portal is to deliver the benefits of Social Security Schemes of Central and State Governments through e-SHRAM portal. The state wise registration of unorganised workers as on 23.11.2021 is annexed. *                                                    *******
