                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3378






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Jaisalmer-Kandla Railway Line




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways proposes to sanction Jaisalmer-Kandla via Sanchor railway line;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total cost and distance covered by the above rail line; (d) whether this project is likely to be implemented in partnership of Union and State Government and if so, the share of Gujarat, Rajasthan and Union Government in the above said project;(e) whether the Government of Rajasthan has given in principle approval for giving land for Jaisalmer-Kandla railway line; and(f)  if so, the details thereof and the follow up action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b): Jaisalmer is already connected by Broad Gauge rail line to Kandla Port via Jodhpur. However, for direct connectivity, surveys for Jaisalmer to Barmer and Barmer to Bhabhar via Sanchore were completed in 2012-13. As these projects were financially unviable and since adequate connectivity already exists, these projects have not been taken forward.(c) to (f): Do not arise.*****
