                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3762






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Funds to West Bengal under Poshan Abhiyan




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)   	the districts covered under Poshan Abhiyan in the State of West Bengal and details regarding Anganwadi Centres in each district;(b)   	the details of funds released by the Union Government under Poshan Abhiyan to the State of West Bengal during the last three years, district-wise;(c)   	the details of funds released and number of beneficiaries under Scheme for Adolescent Girls during the said period in the State of West Bengal, district-wise;(d)   	the details of funds released and number of beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana during the said period in the State of West Bengal, district-wise; and(e)   	the details of funds borne by the State Government for these schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) 	The details of districts covered under POSHAN Abhiyaan with corresponding Anganwadi Centres in each district in the state of West Bengal  is at Annexure I.  (b) The district-wise information relating to funds released under POSHAN Abhiyaan is not maintained centrally. However, funds released by the Government under POSHAN Abhiyaan to the state of West Bengal as on 1st January 2022 is as under:Financial Year	Funds Allocated/Released(Amount in Rs. lakh)2017-18 to 2021-22	26,751.08(c) The district-wise information relating to number of beneficiaries and funds released under Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) is not maintained centrally. However, funds released and number of beneficiaries under SAG in the State of West Bengal, during the last three years, is as under:Year	Funds Released (Rupees in Lakhs)	Number of beneficiaries2018-19	101.85	551472019-20	126.72	17052020-21	31.71	Not reported(d) The details of funds released and number of beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana during the said period in the State of West Bengal, district-wise; and The funds are released to the States/UTs on the basis of indicative targets and utilisation of released funds. Further, the funds under PMMVY are released State/UT-wise. Thus, the Government does not maintain district-wise amount of funds released and utilization under PMMVY. Funds were not released to West Bengal in 2020-21 as the State Government had changed the name of the scheme.
