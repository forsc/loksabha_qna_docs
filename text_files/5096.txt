                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4659






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Utilisation of GST Collection




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Goods and Services Tax (GST) collection by the State/Centre during the last three years;(b) whether the Ministry has settled all the GST amount to the States/UTs during the said period and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and(c) the details of utilization of GST collection for various schemes at the Central/State level during the period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a): The month-wise gross Goods and Services Tax (GST) collection for FY 2017-18, FY 2018-19 and FY 2019-20 are as under:Month	GST Collection (in Rs. Crore)	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20April	--	1,03,459	1,13,865May	--	94,016	1,00,289June	--	95,610	99,939July	--	96,483	--August	95,633	93,960	--September	94,064	94,442	--October	93,333	1,00,710	--November	83,780	97,637	--December	84,314	94,726	--January	89,825	1,02,503	--February	85,962	97,247	--March	92,167	1,06,577	--Average	89,885	98,114	1,04,698(b): The details of GST paid to States/UTs may be seen at Annexure.(c): Revenue from GST collected by Centre and States is credited to Consolidated Fund of India and Consolidated Funds of States respectively. The expenditure of the Centre/State governments is met out of the Consolidated Funds and it may not be possible to track utilisation of GST collection to meet the expenditure for various schemes of the Centre/State governments.*****
