                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

179






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Atal Beemit Vyakti Kalyan Yojana




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the total number of beneficiaries under the Atal Beemit Vyakti Kalyan Yojana; (b)whether the scheme had expired on 30th June 2021; and (c)whether the Government has extended the duration of the scheme; and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		 As on 31.10.2021, total number of beneficiaries under Atal Beemit Vyakti Kalyan Yojana (ABVKY) is 59507.(b) & (c):	 No, Sir.   The scheme has not expired.  The scheme Atal Beemit Vyakti Kalyan Yojana was started w.e.f. 01.07.2018 for two year on pilot basis. It has been extended twice from 01.07.2020 to 30.06.2021 and again from 01.07.2021 to 30.06.2022. *******
