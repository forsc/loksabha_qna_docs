                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2809






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Excessive Fluoride in Water of Wildlife Sancturies




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the excessive levels of TDS (fluoride) present in water in certain wildlife sanctuaries in the country has resulted in harmful effects on the wildlife; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether any study has been conducted by the Government in this regard; (d)	if so, the details thereof along with the harmful effects on animals and birds noticed during the last three years; and (e)	the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) and (b) There are no reports in the Ministry indicating excessive levels of TDS(fluoride) in water in Wildlife Sanctuaries.(c) The Ministry has not conducted any study in this regard.(d) and (e) Does not arise in view of (c) above.*****
