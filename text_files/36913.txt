                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Promotion of Tourism in Thiruvarur




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any proposal to bring Thiruvarur of Tamil Nadu on to the World tourism map is under consideration of the Government; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the special steps taken by the Government to bring the religious, spiritual, cultural heritage of the country before the world community; (d) 	whether the Government takes the help of private, self-help organizations to promote the tourism; and (e) 	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			              (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	to (e):	The Ministry of Tourism undertakes holistic promotion of various tourism destinations and products of the country. The Ministry of Tourism is promoting Thiruvarur globally through its promotional website:  www.incredibleindia.org. The Ministry also provides financial assistance to the State Governments/UT Administrations under its ‘Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) scheme for development of tourism infrastructure with the objective of integrated development of identified pilgrimage and heritage destinations. In addition, the Ministry under its   ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme has also sanctioned   tourism infrastructure projects in the country covering various themes including Spiritual, Buddhist, Krishna, Ramayana, Teerthankar & Heritage. The details of the projects sanctioned under PRASHAD scheme and Spiritual, Buddhist, Krishna, Ramayana, Tirthankara and Heritage themes of   Swadesh Darshan scheme is annexed. The Ministry of Tourism regularly interacts with various stakeholders including federations, hotelier, tour operators, travel agents etc. to seek inputs for development and promotion of tourism in the country. *******
