                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3241






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






BSNL and MTNL Subscribers




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of telephone subscribers of BSNL and MTNL in various States of the country, District-wise; (b) 	the number of Telecom Advisory Committee (TAC) headquarters located in various States and the heads of TAC; (c) 	the manner in which jurisdiction of TAC is determined; (d) 	whether TAC is applicable in the entire Parliamentary constituency or in the legislative assembly constituency; and (e) 	whether TAC will be for one district or in all districts if there are many districts in a Parliamentary constituency and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN) (a) 	The Licensed Service Area (LSA) wise details of telephone subscribers of BSNL and MTNL are at Annexure.  (b) to (e) 	There are 358 Telecom Advisory Committees (TACs) in BSNL/MTNL. The Member of Parliament who is a member of a TAC is given the status of Chairperson in the TAC. In case two or more MPs are nominated in a TAC, the meetings of that TAC are chaired by the MPs on rotational basis. The jurisdiction of a TAC is determined by Telecom District of BSNL/MTNL. TACs are constituted as per Telecom District of BSNL/MTNL and not on the basis of Parliamentary Constituency or Legislative Assembly Constituency.
