                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1570






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Funding under BADP to West Bengal




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated to West Bengal under Border Area Development Programme (BADP) during the last five years;(b) whether the Government has received utilisation certificate from the State Government; and(c) the projects initiated in various border districts of West Bengal under the said programme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (b):	The details of the amount released to the Government of West Bengal under BADP during the last five years  and the pending utilisation certificates (UCs) are as given below:                                                                                 Rs.in croreYear	Amount	Pending utilisation certificates2014-15	97.39	0.002015-16	161.79	0.002016-17	108.32	0.132017-18	122.00	42.212018-19	85.40	0.00Total	574.9042.34(c):	The Government of West Bengal has utilized the funds under BADP towards construction of (i) Roads/link roads, (ii) bridges/ culverts, (iii) education facilities, (iv) health facilities, (v) strengthening of power infrastructure, (vi) drinking water supply facilities, (vii) community centres etc.**********
