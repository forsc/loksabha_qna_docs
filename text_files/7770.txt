                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1447






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Cases of Illegal Mining




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes any steps against such companies which 	have been accused of illegal mining;(b) 	if so, the number of companies which have been found guilty of illegal mining of 	minerals in the last three years including the current year; (c)	whether any status report has been sought by the Union Government from 	States about illegal mining; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra along with the 	action taken by the Union Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS  (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (d): Under Section 23C of the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957 (MMDR Act, 1957), State Governments have been empowered to frame rules for preventing illegal mining, transportation and storage of minerals for the purposes connected therewith.
