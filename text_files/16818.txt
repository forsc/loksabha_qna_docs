                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1073






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Availability of Material for Masks/PPE Kits




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether adequate material is available in the country for manufacture of masks, PPE kits keeping in view the gravity of COVID-19 across the country; and(b) the countries from which health related material/ equipment has been imported/exported by the textile industry?






ANSWER




                                                        ?????? ?????? (??????? ?????? ?????? ?????)MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	Yes, Sir.  From zero manufacturers in March 2020, 1100 indigenous manufacturers of PPE kits have been developed by the Government till date, most of them being from MSME sector. The capacity and production of PPE Coveralls for COVID-19 touched a peak of 5 lakh per day in mid May 2020.    As on 13.9.2020, a total of 1.42 crore PPE kits have been supplied to M/s HLL Lifecare Ltd. - the procurement arm of Ministry of Health & Family Welfare (MoHFW) for use of health professionals in government hospitals.(b):	During March to June 2020, exports of PPE Coveralls was under prohibited list. From 28.7.2020 to 24.8.2020, the exports of PPE Coveralls were allowed under quantities restriction limited to 50 lakh per month. During this period, the export authorizations were given for United States of America, United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates, Senegal and Slovenia, the details of which are shown in the table below:-S. No.	Country	Authorization for Exports of PPE Coveralls in July-August, 2020 (Quantity in numbers)1.	United States of America (USA)	6,00,0002.	United Kingdom (UK)	4,00,0003.	United Arab Emirates (UAE)	3,00,0004.	Senegal	4,89,5005.	Slovenia	5,00,000Total	22,89,500The exports of PPE Coveralls were allowed without any restrictions with effect from 25.8.2020.  With regard to N-95 masks, the exports were under prohibition with effect from 31.1.2020. Thereafter, with effect from 25.8.2020, the exports have been permitted with quantitative restriction of 50 lakh per month. ****
