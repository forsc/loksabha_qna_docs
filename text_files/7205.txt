                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

840






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






NHs in Sri Anandpur Sahib Region in Punjab




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number, length and details of National Highways (NHs) running through Sri Anandpur Sahib Parliamentary constituency in Punjab indicating their starting and end points;(b) the funds spent on the maintenance of the said NHs during each of the last three years and the current year;(c) whether the Government proposes upgrading/constructing/repairing of any specific NH out of the said NHs during the current financial year 2019-20; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) There are five National Highways passing through Shri Anandpur Sahib Parliamentary Constituency in Punjab. The details of these National Highways, funds spent on their maintenance and the development proposed during the current financial year 2019-20 are Annexed.
