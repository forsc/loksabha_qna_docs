                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4311






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Skill Development of Minority Youth




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the number of individuals/youth who have received the skill development training under the scheme for Skill Development of Minorities; (b) the number of women/girls from minority groups who have been trained under the scheme for Modular Employable Skills (MES) since its inception; and (c) the total number of Institutions/ Societies/Organisations/NGOs working with the Ministry for the implementation of the said schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)  The Ministry of Minority Affairs is implementing the following four Skill Development schemes for six centrally notified minorities, viz, Jain, Buddhist, Sikh, Parsi, Muslim and Christian: (i) “Seekho aur Kamao (Learn & Earn)”- Employment-oriented Skill development Initiative for minorities:. (ii) Upgrading the Skills and Training in Traditional Arts / Crafts for Development (USTTAD): (iii) Nai Manzil (iv) Garib Nawaz Employment Training Scheme (Maulana Azad Education Foundation). Since its inception, total 332961 individuals/youth have received skill development training under the scheme for Skill Development of Minorities.(b) A total of 1,52,267 women/girls from minority groups have been trained in various skills including Modular Employable Skills (MES).(c) ) A total number of 396 Institutions/ Societies/Organisations/NGOs are working with the Ministry for the implementation of the said schemes.******
