                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2336






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Procurement of Rice and Wheat




P. Velusamy














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received any report from Food Corporation of India (FCI) regarding increase in the procurement cost of wheat, rice and other items; (b) 	if so, the estimated cost of wheat and rice and the total stock of wheat and rice as on date; (c) 	whether the Government will ensure the status quo of the price for wheat and rice will be supplied as per the existing price; (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor; (e) 	whether the Government has any proposal to distribute the excess storage of items at PDS price to the public?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):	No, Sir.(b): 	The Average acquisition cost for 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 are as under: Rice :                                                         Rate Rs/Qtl2019-20	2020-21	2021-223023.42	3163.10	3274.85Wheat:                                                        Rate Rs/Qtl2019-20	2020-21	2021-222071.30	2165.85	2240.62The total Central Pool stock of wheat & rice as on 16.07.2021 is as under:-                                                       (Fig. in LMT)Commodity	Stock PositionRice	293.71Wheat	589.06Total	882.77(c) and (d): 	Under the National Food Security Act, 2013 the Central Issue Prices of foodgrains i.e. Rs.3/2/1 per kilogram for rice/ wheat/ coarse grains has not been revised since July, 2000.(e): 	No, Sir.*****
