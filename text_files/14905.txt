                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3510






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Registered Companies




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara













                                                    Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of companies registered in the country with the Registrar of Companies as on date, State-wise;(b)    the number of companies, out of these, which have been closed during each of the last three years, State-wise; (c)    the reasons for closure of these companies; and(d)  whether the Union Government have taken steps to revive these companies, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS	                                 (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)????? ??? ????????? ????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? 			                 (?????????? ???? ?????)(a) and (b): The details of the number of companies registered in the country with the Registrar of Companies as on date, State-wise are given in Annexure-A and the details of number of companies, out of these, which have been closed during each of the last three years, State-wise are given in Annexure-B. (c) and (d):  There are no provisions in the Companies Act to monitor the reasons for closure of the companies and revive under the said Act. However, there are provisions for removal of the name of the companies Under Section 248 of the Companies Act, 2013 to remove name of the company from the Register of Companies if it is not carrying on any business or operation for a period of 02 immediately preceding financial years and has not made any application within such period for obtaining the status of a dormant company. Besides, some companies are dissolved by the orders of the High Court or NCLT.					**************Annexure AThe number of companies registered in the country with the Registrar of Companies as on date, State-wise:Name of Regional Directors	No. of companies registered as on dateNR	571643NER	16412NWR	220061SR	211280SER	272398WR	406634ER	281159Total	19,79,587Annexure BThe number of companies, out of these, which have been closed during each of the last three years, State-wise:Name of Regional Directors	2017	2018	2019NR	64469	41911	22733NER	286	369	803NWR	18560	14655	8450SR	29123	11754	9229SER	35313	21403	15032WR	2871	50053	46021ER	12609	15133	11338Total	163231	155278	113606******
