                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

795






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Expansion of Cargo Facilities




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to expand the cargo facilities in the country and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether any airports in Telangana have been identified for this purpose and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a)   Airports Authority of India Cargo Logistics and Allied Services Company Limited (AAICLAS) which is a central public sector enterprise has been assigned the responsibility to develop air cargo facilities at Airports Authority of India (AAI) managed airports across the country. Accordingly AAICLAS  has planned the expansion of cargo facilities at the following airports:;(i)    International cargo facilities at Ahmedabad, Aurangabad and Gaya,;(ii)   International courier facilities at Trichy and Thiruvananthapuram, and;(iii)  Domestic cargo facilities, including integrated cargo terminals at Agartala, Amritsar,  Belgavi, Bhopal, Coimbatore, Dehradun, Dibrugarh, Gaya, Hubballi, Imphal, Jaipur, Kanpur, Lucknow, Rajahmundri, Srinagar, and Surat.;;At airports leased out under concession or other agreements to private entities by AAI, modern facilities for promotion of growth and development of air cargo have been developed for all three categories, namely, domestic, international, and transit and transshipment international air cargo.;(b) No, Sir. Presently, there is no proposal for development of such facility in Tier-II and Tier-III airports in the State of Telangana. The proposals raised by stakeholders are taken up on the basis of feasibility studies, including considerations of techno-commercial viability of the project.;
