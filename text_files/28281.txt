                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

207






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Disbursement of Funds under DBT Schemes




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds disbursement to West Bengal under various DBT schemes of the Central Government during COVID lockdown period from March 2020 - till date;(b)	the details of disbursement of funds in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts of West Bengal under various DBT schemes of the Central Government during the said period; and (c)	the details of financial assistance provided to West Bengal for economic revival and post- Covid lockdown?






ANSWER




                                                                                                ANSWER                             MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE                               (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) : Rs.75,233 crore has been disbursed to West Bengal under various DBT schemes of the Central Government  from 01.04.2020 till date. More details are available on DBT Bharat Portal (www.dbtbharat.gov.in). (b) :Rs.1,094.40 crore has been disbursed to Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts of West Bengal under various DBT schemes of the Central Government. More details are available on DBT Bharat Portal (www.dbtbharat.gov.in). (c) : Additional borrowings upto 2% of GSDP has been allowed to States over and above normal borrowing limit of 3% of GSDP for the year 2020-21. Out of 2% of GSDP, the State of West Bengal had been granted total additional borrowing permission of 1 % of GSDP amounting to Rs. 13,574 crore.
