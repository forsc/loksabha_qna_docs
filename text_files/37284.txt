                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4310






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Review of DDU-GKY




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the ongoing Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) has been reviewed and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether any targets have been stipulated for imparting training and providing employment under this yojana during the current year;(c) 	whether this yojana has been successful in enhancing employment opportunities and providing employment to the rural people;(d) 	if so, whether the rural youths have shown less interest in this yojana in comparison to last years;(e) 	if so, the reasons therefor; and(f) 	whether the Government proposes to take any corrective measures in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)  	Periodic review of the implementation of Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) is being carried out with States/UTs. Further NITI Aayog had conducted evaluation of National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM) and DDU-GKY as a component of NRLM was also evaluated. No recommendation requiring any changes in the DDU-GKY was made.(b) 	Yes, it is targeted to train 2.80 lakh candidates and place 1.96 lakh candidates under DDU-GKY during 2021-22.(c) Yes, The details of progress under  DDU-GKY scheme from 2014-15 to 2021-22 (upto Feb, 2022) is given below: Year	       Total no. of candidates trained              Total no. of candidates                  under DDU-GKY	                            placed under DDU-GKY2014-15	             43,038	                                21,4462015-16	           2,36,471	                              1,09,5122016-17	           1,62,586	                              1,47,8832017-18	           1,31,527	                                75,7872018-19	           2,41,509	                              1,37,2512019-20 	   2,47,177	                              1,50,2142020-21	             38,289	                                49,5632021-22(till Feb''22)        52,060	                                24,178Total	          11,52,657	                              7,15,834  (d) to (f)  No, there is no indication of Rural Youths showing less interest in DDU-GKY in comparison to last years. The number of candidates trained under DDU-GKY during 2020-21 and 2021-22 has been impacted due to pandemic COVID-19 resulting in training centres being closed for considerable period of time. So far a total of 32.22 lakh candidates have been registered for training and currently 1.28 lakh  candidates are undergoing training in various training centres spread across the country. *****
