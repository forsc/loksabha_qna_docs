                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






National Job Portal for PwDs




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The salient features and aspects of the National Job Portal for Persons with Disabilities (PwDs);(b)	the total number of persons who have applied for an employment or educational loan using the portal since its inception; and(c)	the total number of banks/companies that have registered with the portal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a)	The National Job Portal for Persons with Disabilities (www.disabilityjobs.gov.in) was launched on 27.01.2016 with the following salient features:(i)	It is Disabled Friendly {Compliance as per the Web Content Accessibility Guideline (WCAG) 2.0 and Government of India web Guidelines}. (ii)	Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) can apply through the portal for Jobs, Skill Trainings, Education Loans and Self Employment Loans.(b)  32338 PwDs have applied for an employment/educational loan using the portal since its inception.(c)  107 banks/companies have registered with the portal since its inception.*****
