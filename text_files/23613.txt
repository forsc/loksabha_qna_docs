                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4648






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Rail Coach Factories




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of rail coach factories in India at present, state-wise; and(b) the number of metro train coaches that are manufactured in these factories?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS,  COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) 	Presently, there are three Railway coach factories under the Ministry of Railways, shown below State-wise:Production Units	                         StateIntegral Coach Factory (ICF), Chennai,	       Tamil NaduRail Coach Factory (RCF), Kapurthala,	       PunjabModern Coach Factory (MCF), Rae Bareli,        Uttar Pradesh(b)	Integral Coach Factory (ICF), Chennai is manufacturing coaches for Kolkata Metro. The number of the metro train coaches  manufactured by ICF, Chennai during the last three years is as under :Year	             2017-18	   2018-19	2019-20Metro Coaches	       24	     16	          40*****
