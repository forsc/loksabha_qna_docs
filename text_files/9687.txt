                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3175






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Hospitals in Ladakh




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has any plans to administer the hospitals of Union Territory of Ladakh through All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS);(b) if so, the details thereof and when does the Government plan to do so; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	There is no plan to administer the hospital of Union Territory of Ladakh through All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS).
