                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2653






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






PMFBY in Uttar Pradesh




Ashok Kumar Rawat













                                                    Arun Kumar Sagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether work is being carried out under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) in the country, particularly in Misrikh and Shahjahanpur Parliamentary Constituencies of Uttar Pradesh; (b) if so, the details thereof, particularly in Misrikh and Shahjahanpur Parliamentary Constituencies including updated status in Uttar Pradesh as on date; and (c) the details of the Central funds allocated including the utilization of the said funds and the number of beneficiaries during each of the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c) : 	As per provisions of the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY), State Governments notify the crops and areas under the scheme.  Uttar Pradesh Government is implementing the PMFBY and notifying all districts including Hardoi, Kanpur Nagar, Sitapur and Shahjahanpur districts of Misrikh and Shajahanpur Parliamentary Constituencies for Bengal Gram, Groundnut, Maize, paddy, Pearl Millet, Pigeon pea, sesame and sorghum during Kharif seasons and barley, lentil, mustard, pea, potato, wheat etc. during Rabi seasons.  	Details of farmer applications enrolled and premium collected in these districts and in Uttar Pradesh State during 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 under PMFBY are given in Annexure-I.	Being Central Sector Scheme, State-wise allocation/release is not made under PMFBY.  Funds are released to the Central fund routing agency i.e. Agriculture Insurance Company of India Ltd. (AIC) which in turn releases the applicable Central Government’s share in premium subsidy on receipt of respective State Government’s share, to the concerned insurance companies.  State-wise details of number of farmer applications enrolled who got the benefit of premium subsidy, farmers premium, State Government and Central Government share in premium subsidy during 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 are given in Annexure-II.
