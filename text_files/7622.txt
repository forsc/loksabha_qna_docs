                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Kozhikode




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether large number of Kendriya Vidyalayas buildings across the country arein a dilapidated condition requiring complete destruction and construction ofnew buildings including KVS-1, Kozhikode as these are highly dangerous toboth the students and the faculty and other staff of these KVSs; and(b) if so, the present status of the proposal for the new construction and the timeframe for issuance of approval and completion of the construction, for KVS-I,Kozhikode?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has informed that 21 Kendriya Vidyalaya(KV)buildings have been found to be in dilapilated condition requiring totalreplacement or major repairs. KV No. 1, Kozhikode is also one among them.(b) Replacement/special repairsof unsafe buildings depends on various factorssuch as submission of drawings / estimates by the construction agency, obtainingrequisite approvals and availability of funds etc. As such, no definite time frame canbe given in this regard.*****
