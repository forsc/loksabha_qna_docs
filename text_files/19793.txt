                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

984






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Job Loss in Kerala Tourism Sector




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of the fact that Job Loss In Kerala Tourism sector that contributes approximately 10 per cent of the State GDP is anticipating a cumulative loss of Rs 25,000 crores; (b) 	if so, whether the Government has received any representation from the Government of Kerala in this regard and also seeking fiscal support for the tourism sector; (c) 	if so, the details thereof and measures initiated by the Government in this regard; (d) 	whether the Government is further aware of the losses suffered by backwater tourism and houseboats sector as well as home stay sector; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof along with the details of aid provided to the sector for its revival by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	No formal study has been instituted by Ministry of Tourism, Government of India to assess job losses in Kerala Tourism sector.However, as per the information received from Department of Tourism, Government of Kerala  loss of Rs.30,711/- crores has been estimated in tourism sector in Kerala due to Covid -19. (b) & (c):	Ministry has not received any proposal from Government of Kerala with regard to the issue and seeking fiscal support for the tourism sector. (d):	Government is aware of the fact that the closure of the travel and tourism industry has had a cascading effect on the livelihood of people dependent on tourism including backwater tourism and houseboats sector as well as home stay sector in Kerala. (e):	Details of Initiatives taken by Kerala State Government are given at Annexure.*******
