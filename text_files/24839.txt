                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

80






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






AYUSH Hospitals




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has taken a decision to establish dedicated AYUSH hospital in each district of the county to offer effective medical treatment in Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, Homoeopathy and Naturopathy (AYUSH); (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and stream-wise; and (c) the details of hospitals proposed to be made operational during the current and the following years to cover each district in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYURVEDA, YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	to (c) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO.80* FOR 23RD JULY, 2021(a) & (b) Establishment of dedicated AYUSH hospitals in each district of the country to offer effective medical treatment in Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, Homoeopathy and Naturopathy (AYUSH) comes under the respective State/UT Government’s purview as public health is a State subject. However, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision for financial assistance for setting up of up to 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital. Grant-in-aid is being provided to State/UT Governments as per the proposals submitted through State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) and as per the guideline of NAM. The State/UT wise list of up to 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals approved for providing treatment through various Ayush systems of medicines is furnished at Annexure. (c)  The Grant-in-aid is provided to the State/UT Governments for the establishment of up to 50 bedded integrated AYUSH hospitals as per the proposals reflected in the State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) based on actual need and priority. Therefore, no district wise targets have been made by the Central Government to make such hospitals operational in each district during the current and the following years.
