                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2036






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Allocation of Budget in AYUSH




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state:(a) the details of the budgetary allocation made under different heads by the Ministry of AYUSH, particularly in Rajasthan covering the heads/subheads of Ayurveda education, drugs/medicine, AYUSH physicians, in Department of Ayurveda and publicity/awareness campaigns of AYUSH, head-wise; and (b) the details of the facilities, resources, medicines etc. which the Government provides/proposes to provide to promote AYUSH in view of its relevance and easy availability of its treatment in rural areas of Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):     Ministry of AYUSH is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) through States/UTs to develop and promote AYUSH systems of medicine through cost effective AYUSH services, strengthening of educational systems, facilitate the enforcement of quality control of Ayurveda, Siddha and Unani & Homoeopathy (ASU &H) drugs and sustainable availability of ASU & H raw-materials.  During each financial year based on the total budget availability under NAM, resource pool for each State/UT including Rajasthan is allocated as per standard parameters. Under National AYUSH Mission Rs. 506.00 Crore has been allocated as Budget Estimate for various activities such as AYUSH Services, Strengthening of AYUSH Educational Institution, Quality Control of Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani & Homoeopath (ASU&H) and Medicinal Plants. In this regard, Rs. 1987.595 lakhs has been released to State Government of Rajasthan for various activities including Rs. 27.60 lakhs for AYUSH education, Rs. 1001.82 lakhs for procurement of   AYUSH drugs/medicines and Rs. 12.00 lakhs for publicity/awareness campaigns of AYUSH. Further, Budget Estimate of Rs. 93.50 Crore has been allocated to National Institute of Ayurveda, Jaipur during 2019-20.
