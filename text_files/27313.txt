                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2874






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Construction of Houses in Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of houses constructed in Maharashtra during the last four years, city/project-wise and urban housing scheme-wise;(b) 	the names of the cities of Maharashtra in which onstruction of houses is in progress presently, urban housing scheme-wise and project-wise;(c) 	the number of beneficiaries of these construction works, city and project-wise; and(d)	whether there has been any delay in aforementioned housing construction works and if so, the reasons therefor along with the names of delayed projects






ANSWER




                                                                 THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (c) The details of city/project-wise number of houses sanctioned, constructed and under construction underBeneficiary Led individual house construction/ enhancement (BLC), Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP) and “In-situ” Slum Redevelopment (ISSR) verticals of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) is annexed as Annexure.Besides in the State of Maharashtra, apart from houses sanctioned in above three verticals, 3,79,479 houses have been sanctioned directly to the beneficiaries under Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS) vertical of PMAY-U through housing loan from Primary Lending Institutions with interest subvention.(d) 	PMAY-U Mission has strategized to complete the construction of sanctioned houses within the Mission period.                                            *****
