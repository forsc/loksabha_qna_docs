                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Campaigns of Ayurveda




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) the details of the campaigns being organized by the Union Government for taking Ayurveda to every village in the country; and (b) the efforts being made by the Government for bringing Ayurveda at par with Allopathy?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):   Central Government is implementing Central Sector Scheme for Promotion of Information, Education and Communication (IEC) in AYUSH, under which the Ministry takes up initiatives for propagation, promotion and awareness of AYUSH systems of medicine including Ayurveda by organizing Arogya Fairs, Ayurveda Parvs, Conferences, Exhibitions, Seminars, Workshops, Symposium, celebration of important days viz. Ayurveda Day and also undertaking publicity activities through electronic media, print media and social media amongst the citizens all over the country. The status of Ayurveda Parvs organized is furnished at Annexure. Further, the Central Government is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) through States/UTs to promote AYUSH systems of medicine including Ayurveda through cost effective AYUSH services, strengthening of educational systems, facilitate the enforcement of quality control of Ayurveda, Siddha and Unani & Homoeopathy (ASU &H) drugs and sustainable availability of ASU & H raw-materials.
