                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3721






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Expansion of Oil Palm Cultivation




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any request from the State of Kerala for including all the districts in the State in the expansion of oil palm cultivation;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the decision taken by the Government;(c)	the details of annual action plan submitted by the State under National Mission on Edible Oils-Oil Palm and the status of approval of the same;(d)	whether the Government is planning to drastically increase palm oil production in the country;(e)	if so, the details thereof and the measures proposed for expansion of palm oil cultivation; and(f)	the details of oil palm cultivation and palm oil production in the country presently, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDERA SINGH TOMAR)(a to c):  Annual Action Plan of Oil Palm for the State of Kerala under National Mission on Edible Oils- Oil Palm (NMEO-OP)during 2021-22 was received and same has been approved by the Government of Indiawith total outlay of Rs.111.00 lakhs. The State Govt has proposed for area expansion of oilpalm cultivation in all districts for additional area of 6500 ha from 2022-23 to 2025-26. District wise proposed area by the State Govt. for the period 2022-23 to 2025-26 is given at Annexure-I.(d & e): The National Mission on Edible Oils - Oil Palm (NMEO-OP) has been launched with the aim to augment the availability of edible oil in the country by harnessing area expansion, increasing crude palm oil production with the aim to reduce the import burden. The salient features of NMEO-Oil palm include assistance for planting material, inputs for intercropping upto gestation period of 4 years and for maintenance, establishment of seed gardens, nurseries, micro irrigation, bore well/pumpset/water harvesting structure, vermi compost units, solar pumps, harvesting tools, custom hiring centre cum harvester Groups, farmers and officers training, and for replanting of old oil palm gardens etc. The total approved cost of the NMEO (Oil Palm) Scheme is ? 11,040 crore, out of which ? 8844 crore is Central share and  ? 2196 crore state share. (f): The State-wise details of Oil Palm area covered upto March, 2021 and crude palm oil production in the Country during the year 2020-21 is given at Annexure-II.
