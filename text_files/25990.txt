                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1688






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Bidding Process




Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that bidding process for six laning of Kagal-Satara section has not been completed yet and pending since December 2017;(b)	if so, the reasons for the delays in this regard; and(c)	the present the status of the bidding process and the expected date of commencement and completion of the project? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)      4 laning of Kagal - Satara section of NH-48 was done on BOT (Toll) mode with concession period upto May, 2022. Earlier, NHAI prepared Detailed Project Report (DPR) for 6 laning of this stretch and bids for civil work were invited in 2017. Due to non-availability of land required for 6-laning and the existing concession agreement in force, it was decided to annul the bidding process in 2017. Award of 6-laning work is targeted in Financial Year 2021-22 for completion in scheduled period. *****
