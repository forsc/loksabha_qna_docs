                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Loan Schemes for Tribal Women




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)thescheme-wisedetails of the proposals received and  approved  under  various  loan  schemes  for  the development of tribal women in Rajasthan during each of the last three years and the current year;(b) the number  of  beneficiaries  under  these  loan schemes, year-wise; and(c)the  number  of  tribal  women  who  have  been provided loans under various loan schemes in Banswara-Dungarpur,  Rajasthan  to  set  up  industry  by  imparting training in this regard along with the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):        A Statement is laid down on the Table of the House.  Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. †*184 for answer on 08.03.2021 tabled by SHRI KANAKMAL KATARA, regarding ‘LOAN SCHEMES FOR TRIBAL WOMEN’.(a) & (b): National Scheduled Tribes Finance and Development Corporation (NSTFDC), under the Ministry of Tribal Affairs extends concessional loans to the eligible Scheduled Tribe persons for undertaking any income generation activities/ self-employment through its implementing agencies. These concessional loans of NSTFDC in the state of Rajasthan are extended through its State Channelising Agency named Rajasthan SC ST Finance and Development Co-operative Corporation. In addition, in the state of Rajasthan loans are also extended through Punjab National Bank.
