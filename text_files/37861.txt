                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4806






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Completion of Work under Smart Cities Mission




Sandhya Ray














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the volume of work under Smart Cities Mission completed in Bhind and Datia districts under the Bhind Lok Sabha Constituency including the amount of payment made in this regard; and(b) 	the details of works carried out online or offline by each municipality during the last three years and the current year including the amount of payment made in this regard along with factual status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                             THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                   MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) & (b): Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015. 100 Smart cities have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018.  Bhind and Datia are not part of the list of 100 selected Smart Cities.  As on 4 March 2022, Government of India has released ? 29,213.60 crore for 100 Smart Cities, out of which ? 25,177.65 crore (86%) has been utilized.  So far, 100 Smart Cities have tendered out 6,928 projects worth ? 1,91,238 crore; work orders have been issued in 6,282 projects worth ? 1,65,503 crore; 3,576 projects worth ? 59,958 crore have been completed.                                      ******
