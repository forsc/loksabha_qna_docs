                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2331






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Vacancy in Libraries




Jayanta Kumar Roy














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether several libraries functions under the Ministry of Culture;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether several posts under these libraries are lying vacant for a long time adversely affecting their performance;(d)	if so, the details of vacant posts, library-wise;(e)	whether the Government is taking any steps to fill up these vacant posts; and(f)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)&(b) Yes, Sir. The following 7 Libraries / Organizations are financed by Ministry of Culture:-i.	Rampur Raza Library, Rampur, Uttar Pradeshii.	National Library, Kolkataiii.	Khuda Bakhsh Oriental Public Library, Patna, Bihariv.	Delhi Public Library, Delhiv.	Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkatavi.	Central Secretariat Library, New Delhivii.	Central Reference Library, Kolkata(c)&(d) Details of posts under these libraries lying vacant are at Annexure-A.(e)&(f) Yes, Sir. From time to time, as and when the vacancy arises, the Government takes necessary steps such as holding DPC, advertising the vacancy and referring the proposals to UPSC/SSC, as the case may be.
