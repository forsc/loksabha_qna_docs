                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1167






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Development of Tribal Forest and Wildlife Tourism in  Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to develop the Tribal life tourism, forest and wildlife tourism in different States; (b) 	whether the Government is aware of the greater scope to promote tourism in the above sector in the State of Odisha; (c) 	if so, the policy of the Government in that direction in different States and the emphasis being laid in this regard in the State of Odisha; and (d) 	the fund provision made for the purpose in the State of Odisha and also for the tribal areas of other States?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	The Ministry of Tourism has launched the Swadesh Darshan (SD) Scheme for Integrated Development of Theme-Based Tourist Circuits in the country in 2014-15 for development of tourism infrastructure in the country including Tribal & Wildlife Circuits. Tribal Circuit is one of the 15 thematic circuits identified for development under SD Scheme. The details of funds sanctioned under the Tribal Circuit theme & Wildlife Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme are Annexures-I & II respectively. In the year 2011-2012, The Ministry of Tourism, Govt. of India had sanctioned Rs.508.77 Lakh for Infrastructure Development of Tribal Circuit - Gajapati – Rayagada in the state of Odisha.*******
