                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1628






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Setting up of Women Battalions




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has received several proposals for setting up of women Battalions/Reserved Battalions in the Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs); and(b) if so, the details thereof and the present status of such proposals along with the locations identified for setting up of such battalions, force and State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (b) No proposal has been received from Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) for setting up of Women Battalions/Reserved Battalions at present. However, Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) have 6 Mahila Battalions located across the country one each in the States of Delhi, Gujarat, Maharashtra, West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh and Karnataka.  *****
