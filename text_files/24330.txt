                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

161






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Development of Ramayana Circuit




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to develop Ramayana Circuit in the near future; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; (d) 	whether any other religions circuit is also under consideration; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	Ramayana Circuit is one of the identified thematic circuit of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme of the Ministry of Tourism under which 2 projects have been sanctioned. The Ministry has also sanctioned projects under Buddhist, Tirthankar, Krishna and Spiritual Circuits. In addition, the Ministry of Tourism  provides  financial  assistance  to  State Governments / Union Territory  Administrations for  integrated  development of  identified  pilgrimage and  heritage  destinations  under  ‘National Mission on  Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and  Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’  scheme. The detail of projects sanctioned under Ramayana, Krishna,  Spiritual, Tirthankar and Buddhist Circuits under  Swadesh Darshan  scheme  and  the  detail of  the  projects  sanctioned under  ‘PRASHAD’  scheme is  annexed.    *******
