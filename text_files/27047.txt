                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2711






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






G7 Agreement




Amar Singh













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    Gaurav Gogoi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Indian Government has signed the G7 Agreement on free speech which affirmed, "freedom of expression, both online and offline, as a freedom that safeguards democracy and helps people live free from fear and oppression" and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government believes that signing such a statement contradicts the rise in sedition cases in India against dissenters and critics of the Government, rise in use of Unlawful Activities Prevention Act against such persons and the frequent resort to internet shutdowns;(c) if so, the details thereof including the steps being taken by the Government to act on its commitment made by signing the agreement; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS [DR RAJKUMAR RANJAN SINGH](a) G7 countries at the Cornwall Summit in June 2021 adopted the ‘Open Societies Statement’.(b) Does not arise.(c) Not applicable.(d) Not applicable.***
