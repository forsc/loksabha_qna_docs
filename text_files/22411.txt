                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3495






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






IPPB in Bihar




Dileshwar Kamait














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number and the details of India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) set up in Supaul, Bihar; and(b) 	whether Gramin Dak Sewaks do not have biometric devices and smart phones as required in their job which is causing problems in service delivery and if so, the details thereof and remedial measures being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)     India Post Payments Bank has 204 Banking Access Points in Supaul, Bihar. The details of these access points is placed at Annexure.(b)	   No Sir, over 2,00,000 Postmen & Grameen Dak Sewaks have been provided biometric devices and smart phones across the country.
