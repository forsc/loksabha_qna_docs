                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3597






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Assistance under Integrated Horticulture Development Mission




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government provides financial assistance to farmers under Integrated Horticulture Development Mission to increase horticultural production and enhance income of farmers;(b) 	if so, the details thereof along with provisions made for farmers to get the financial assistance/grant-in aid under the Mission; and(c) 	the grant-in-aid provided to farmers under the said scheme during the last three years, State/UT-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	 Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme namely Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for increasing horticultural production and enhancing income of farmers. All States/UTs are covered under MIDH.	Under the scheme, financial assistance @ 25% to 55% of the total cost for different components such as Production of Planting Material, Vegetable Seed Production, Cultivation of high yielding varieties of Fruits & Vegetables, Rejuvenation of Senile Orchards, Protected Cultivation, Creation of Water Resources, Integrated Nutrient Management (INM), Horticulture Mechanization, Creation of Post Harvest Management (PHM) and Marketing Infrastructure is provided to farmers for holistic development of horticulture in the country.  	Under the scheme, the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers’ Welfare allocates funds to State Horticulture Missions and not to the farmers directly. The State Horticulture Missions in turn provide the financial assistance to farmers/beneficiaries based upon the approved Annual Action Plan.  (c):	 State-wise/UTs details of the funds released (GoI) under MIDH during the last three years is placed at Annexure.
