                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Landline Connections




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of existing landline connections in the country, State/UT-wise;(b) 	the reasons for decline, if any, in the number of landline connections during the last three years;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to use the existing landline connection infrastructure/facilities for any other purposes;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	 if not, the future of the said infrastructure/ facilities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (e)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) TO (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *315 FOR 17th MARCH, 2021 REGARDING “LANDLINE CONNECTIONS”(a) & (b) The service area wise number of landline connections in the country during the last three years and current year is given at Annexure. The decline in the number of landline connections is, inter-alia,  due to the users finding mobile services to be more convenient with regards to its utility, mobility and availability at all times.(c) to (e) The service providers are progressively making use of the existing landline infrastructure for providing Broadband services in the country.
