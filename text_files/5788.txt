                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5449






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Road Projects in NER




Tapir Gao













                                                    Agatha K. Sangma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total length of roads constructed in various States in North Eastern Region (NER) during the last five years, State-wise; (b)	      the details and the present status of East-West Corridor;(c)	the present condition of Doboka road in Assam along with the time when the road was last repaired; (d)	whether the construction work on Naharkatia (Assam)-Hukanjuri-Khonsa (Arunachal Pradesh) sections of NH-315 has started and if so, the details and the present status thereof; (e) 	whether the DPR of the above road has been completed and if so, the details thereof; and (f) 	the time frame set for completing the project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)   This Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways(NHs). The total length of NHs constructed in various States in North Eastern Region (NER) during the last five years is as under:                                                                                                            (Length in Km.)Name of States	Year	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Arunachal Pradesh	88	80	174	188	157Assam	178	87	165	302	323Manipur	46	6	4	231	318Meghalaya	88	50	6	48	13Mizoram	5	15	88	43	59Nagaland	13	25	4	0	34Sikkim	30	0	0	45	83Tripura	18	0	42	82	41(b)   Out of the total length of 637 Km. under East West Corridor in the state of Assam, 610 Km length has been completed and work is taken up/ being taken up in remaining length. (c)   At present the Doboka Road is in good condition. Routine maintenance is carried out as and when required.(d ) to (f)  DPR for improvement of Naharkatia(Assam)-Hukanjuri-Khonsa(Arunachal Pradesh)sections of NH 315 is taken up. The work will be taken up based on outcome of DPR, availability of funds and inter-se priority.*****
