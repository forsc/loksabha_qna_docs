                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3004






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Naya Savera Scheme




Nisith Pramanik














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps the Government is taking for the minority students in the Naya Savera Scheme; and(b)	the details of Minority students of the country and State of West Bengal benefited out of it, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS    (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	The Ministry of Minority Affairs implements Naya Savera Scheme under which free coaching is provided to students of six centrally notified minority communities namely Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains, and Zoroastrians (Parsis). The scheme provides coaching to students so as to enable them to qualify in entrance examinations for admission in technical/ professional courses, and various Competitive examinations for recruitment to Group ‘A’ , ‘B’ and ‘C’ services. The scheme is implemented through Project Implementing Agencies/Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs).(b)	State-wise details of 1,01,675 youths benefitted under the scheme including West Bengal, is at Annexure.
