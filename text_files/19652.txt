                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Modification of Forest Rights Act by States




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether some States have modified the Forest Rights Act to enable tribals and other traditional forest dwelling families to build houses in the neighbourhood forest areas; and(b) if so, the details and present status thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFIARS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)(a)	Yes.   As per information available, Hon’ble Governor of Maharashtra has issued notification dated 23.09.2020 to provide for housing to the Forest Dwelling Scheduled Tribe families and Other Traditional Forest Dwelling Families belonging to the concerned village by extension of village site/hamlet /habitation for area, along with proviso mentioned in the said notification.(b)     State Government of Maharastra has informed that Rules and Guidelines is being prepared for the implementation of this notification for the field authorities. *****
