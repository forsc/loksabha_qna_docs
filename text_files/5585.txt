                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5106






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Communication System in Uttar Pradesh




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has assessed the loss caused to communication system by floods in riverside villages in Lucknow and surrounding districts in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years, if so, the details thereof, year-wise and the reaction of the Government thereto; (b) 	the remedial steps taken by the Government to streamline the communication system in riverside villages to avoid the losses caused by floods; (c) 	whether the Government has formulated any roadmap to further strengthen the communication systems in the country including the State of Uttar Pradesh especially in the district of Barabanki; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (d) 	Information is being collected and will be  placed before the House.
