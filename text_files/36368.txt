                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3386






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Escalators at Shahjahanpur Station




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether approval for installation of escalators and lifts at Shahjahanpur railway station under the Northern Railways has been given in view of the difficulties faced by the public especially senior citizens in the use of stairs;(b)    if so, the number of escalators and lifts at Shahjahanpur railway station for the installation of which, approval was granted and the date on which it was granted;(c)  whether an escalator and two lifts have been installed at Shahjahanpur railway station at present;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons for delay therein?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. Two (2) numbers of escalators and two (2) numbers of lifts at Shahjahanpur Railway Station has been sanctioned in the financial year 2018-19.(c) to (e) The above sanctioned escalators and lifts have yet not been installed due to non-feasibility at the existing Foot Over Bridge (FOB).*****
