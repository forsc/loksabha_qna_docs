                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1105






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Construction of Bypasses and Flyovers in Kerala




Adoor Prakash













                                                    Suresh Kodikunnil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of the construction of new bypass by the National Highways 	Authority of 	India in Attingal in Kerala; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to take immediate measures for land acquisition 	and to 	complete the project without further delay and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government is intending to propose new bypasses or flyovers in 	Kottarakkara	town of NH 744 in Kerala in order to address the persistent traffic 	congestion; (d) 	if so, the details thereof including the allocations made for the purpose and if not, the 	reasons therefor; (e) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from Kerala for construction of 	bypass 	or flyover and widening of the present NH 744 at Kottarakkara in the State; 	and (f) 	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (f)	Various National Highways of Kerala are identified under Economic Corridors (about 720 km), Inter-corridor Route (about 128 km), National Corridor (about 118 km), etc. of Bharatmala Pariyojana in order to optimize the efficiency of freight and passenger movement.  Presently the works are at DPR stage.  This also includes construction of Bypass at Attingal & development of NH at Kottarakkara.  The development work of various stretches is taken up depending upon preparedness in terms of pre-construction requirement, inter-se priority, availability of resources, etc.*****
