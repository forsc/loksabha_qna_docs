                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1685






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






District Disability Rehabilitation Centres




Kuldeep Rai Sharma













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of District Disability Rehabilitation Centres (DDRCs) functioning in the country at present;(b) the details of rehabilitation activities carried out in each of the above centres, during each of the last three years and the current year, State and centre-wise; (c) the details of funds allocated/ utilized in each DDRC scheme during the said period;(d) whether the Govenrment has assessed the performance of DDRC since its inception and if so, the details thereof; (e) whether the Government has received proposals from the States for setting up of DDRCs in the districts where such centres have not been set up so far and if so, the details thereof during the said period along with the present status of each proposal, State-wise; and (f) the other steps taken by the Government to set up more DDRCs in the country and also to improve its working condition?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT (SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) The total number of District Disability Rehabilitation Centres (DDRCs) functioning in the country is 264. A Statement indicating the details of locations, State-wise is enclosed at Annexure-I.(b) The Department provides financial assistance to DDRCs working for the welfare of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) at District level. The DDRCs are aimed at carrying out the following activities:-•	Early Identification and Intervention.•	Awareness Generation.•	Assessment of need of assistive devices.•	Therapeutic Services e.g. Physiotherapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy, etc. •	Survey & identification of persons with disabilities through camp approach etc.(c) A statement indicating State wise fund allocated/utilized in various DDRCs during the last three years and current year is at Annexure-II.(d) Yes sir, recently the Department has conducted impact evaluation of DDRC scheme in which five DDRCs were randomly selected across the country.The evaluation report submitted by the team is as under:-•	The performance of 56% of the centres was found pretty satisfactory.•	The performance of 34% of the centres was found less satisfactory.•	The performance of 10% of the centres was found unsatisfactory.(e) All complete proposals, which fulfill norms of the scheme, received during financial year, are processed during the year itself, subject to availability of funds. However, in case of deficiency in any documents/proposals, the grant is released only after rectification of the deficiency, in consonance with the provisions of General Financial Rules. A Statement indicating State wise details number of proposals received for setting up of DDRCs during 2018-19 is at Annexure-III(f) To set up more DDRCs and to improve the working condition, the Department has taken following steps:(i) Advertisement through newspapers of the scheme all over India.(ii) The scheme is available on the website of the Department www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in(iii) A national conference on 14.09.18 was organized in New Delhi in which 500 delegates participated including representatives of the DDRCs, State Govt. Officials and District Social Welfare Officers. *****
