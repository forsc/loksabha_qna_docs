                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Development of Metro Rail Network




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the developing metro rail network and housing development have been the top priority of the Government’s urban development strategy and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of funds allocated and utilized for developing metro rail networks and housing facility during the last four years;(c) 	the proportion of funds allocation for metro rail and housing projects; and(d) 	whether the Government has any inter ministerial mechanism to avoid further delay of the scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	Urban transport including metro rail projects and urban housing development are an integral part of urban     development which is a state subject. Respective state governments/Union Territory administrations are therefore, responsible for initiating and developing urban infrastructure including metro rail projects and housing development. The Central Government provides financial assistance for metro rail projects, subject to feasibility of the project and availability of resources, as and when posed by the concerned state governments. Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is implementing the scheme of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY (U)] which aims at providing affordable houses to all eligible beneficiaries in urban areas of the country by the year 2022 through the following four verticals namely,(i)	In-Situ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR)(ii)	Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS)(iii)	Affordable Housing in Partnership with public or private sector (AHP), and (iv)	Beneficiary-led individual house construction/ enhancement (BLC)The details of funds allocated, released and utilized during the last four years (2016-17 to 2019-20) for metro rail projects and PMAY-(HFA-U) are as under:                                                                  (Rs. in Crore)      Metro Rail Projects	                                PMAY-HFA-UFunds allocated     Funds utilized/  Funds (Revised Estimate   Released        sanctioned         Funds Released	Funds Utilizedfigures)	                   (for construction                    by States/UTs/                                     of  89,17,395                       central nodal                                    houses)                             agencies during                                                                        2016-20  64,008.39	   62,520.38	    1,40,706.68	         65,955.68	46,384.44For FY 2019-20, the proportion of funds allocated for metro rail projects and PMAY-HFA (U) against the total budget of Ministry is about 40% and 14% respectively.(d)	The progress of Metro Rail projects is reviewed periodically by Government of India, State Governments and concerned metro companies from time to time. Appropriate action such as holding of meetings for resolving the issues causing delay in the project, etc. with the concerned Ministries/Departments/Agencies is taken up by the Government of India/ State Government.	The progress of projects under PMAY-HFA(U) are constantly monitored through Central Sanctioning And Monitoring Committee (CSMC) meetings, Video Conferences, field visits and also through national/regional/state level review meetings for ensuring timely completion of the projects.                                               ****
