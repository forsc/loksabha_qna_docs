                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Economic Relief




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the features of the economic relief announced by the Government;b)	the details of funds sanctioned, allocated and utilised under this economic relief within the country;c)	the details of the target set and achievements made thereunder so far;d)	the details of additional money which the Government has transferred in the hands of the people who lost their jobs and who were on massive salary cuts; ande)	the steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to emerge from low GDP, low demand, huge unemployment and huge inflation?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (e): The Government has announced the following relief/economic packages, which including measures announced by RBI, involved a total amount of Rs.29,87,647.28 crore: (i) Relief package “Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package” on 26.03.2020 (ii) AatmaNirbhar Bharat Packages (ANB) 1.0, 2.0, and 3.0 on 13th -17th May, 2020, 12th October, 2020 & 12th November 2020 respectively (iii) Covid stimulus package on 28th June, 2021. A Statement indicating features of the package along-with funds allotted, wherever applicable is at Annexure-I.   The packages comprise of various schemes/ programmes/policies concerning various Ministries/Departments, who have been tasked to meet the expenditure from the budget allocated to them and seek additional funds if necessary.  They being long-term schemes/programmes, their outcomes would be visible over a period of time.  Under Atma Nirbhar Bharat Rozgar Yojana (ABRY), Government of India contributes both the employees’ and employers share of contribution (12% of wages), in respect of eligible employees, in EPFO registered establishments, for two years, in respect of new employees employed on or after 1st October 2020 and up to 31st March 2022 or those who lost jobs during COVID 19 and did not join any EPF covered establishment up to 30.09.2020.   As on 18.11.2021, total benefits of Rs. 2561.10 Crore have been given to 39.35 lakh employees through 1,15,550 Establishments.   Further, under Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) launched to help MSMEs/Small businesses to meet their operational liabilities and resume businesses, loans for a total amount of Rs.2.82 lakh crore have been sanctioned and guarantee issued to 1.15 crore borrowers involving Rs.2.62 lakh crore.  It has impacted around 5.45 crore employees as on 12.11.2021.   Also, under PM Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi) scheme providing working capital loan up to Rs. 10,000/- to street vendors adversely affected due to COVID, loans have been sanctioned and disbursed to 29,90,763 & 26,51,652 vendors respectively.  A Statement indicating benefits, including cash benefits provided under PM Garib Kalyan Package, wherever applicable is at Annexure-II. The relief /economic packages comprise of Schemes/Programmes/Policies, that have been envisaged to increase economic growth, increase demand and create employment opportunities, with the ultimate objective of making the country self-reliant. ****
