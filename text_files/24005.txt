                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4950






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Development of NHs




Asaduddin Owaisi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that condition of the National Highways (NHs) having no toll is very poor and if so, the reasons therefor;(b) the funds allocated and utilised for maintenance of such NHs during the last three years;(c) whether it is a fact that the work on many State Highways which were declared as National Highways is yet to start and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor, NH-wise; and(d) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to start widening and maintainence work on these NHs?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) and (b) 	The development and maintenance of National Highways (NHs) is a continuous process. The works on NHs, including those on newly declared NHs, are accordingly taken up as per inter-se-priority, progress of ongoing works, availability of funds and traffic density, to keep the NHs in traffic worthy conditions.The Maintenance and Repair (M&R) of stretches of NHs, where either Development works have commenced or Operation, Maintenance and Transfer (OMT) Concessions/ Operation and Maintenance (O&M) Contracts have been awarded, are entrusted to the Concessioners/ Contractors till the Defect Liability Period (DLP)/ the Concession Period. M&R of balance stretches of NHs are carried out annually as per available budgetary outlay, inter-se priority and traffic density to keep such NHs in traffic worthy conditions.The Ministry has taken policy decision to also implement M&R of NHs entrusted with State Governments/ Union Territory (UT) through Short Term (One Year) and Medium Term (Three Years) contracts effective from Financial Year 2021-22, besides implementation of works such as Ordinary Repairs (OR), Flood Damage Repairs (FDR), etc., through State Governments/ UTs as per extant policies.	The details of funds allocated and utilised for maintenance and repair of NHs during last three years is as below:Rs. in croreYear	Funds allocated	Funds utilised2017-18	2,966.752,654.782018-19	2,331.181,697.172019-20	2,000.001,593.56(c) and (d)	The projects are at various stages of evaluation, handing over by States, financial allocations and technical approvals. *****
