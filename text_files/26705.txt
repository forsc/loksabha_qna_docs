                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Impact of Cyclone Tauktae




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       whether fishing community in the State of Maharashtra is still reeling from the destruction caused         by the Cyclone Tauktae; (b)      if so, the reasons therefor; (c)      whether the Union Government has assessed the loss suffered by the fishermen due to Cyclone Tauktae; (d)     if so, the details thereof; and (e)     the details of the assistance provided by the Union Government to fishermen who were affected by Cyclone Taukte in the State of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY DAIRYING(PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	to (e) The primary responsibility of disaster management rests with the State Governments. To supplement their efforts, financial assistance is provided to the affected States from State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) and National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) as per laid down procedure for management of relief necessitated by notified natural disasters. As per the information received from Government of Maharashtra, some fishermen have lost their fishing nets and fishing vessels have been either fully or partially damaged due to Cyclone Tauktae. The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), Government of India constituted an Inter-Ministerial Central Team (IMCT), which had visited the affected areas of Maharashtra and assessed the damages caused due to cyclone. As per the reports of the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), 1215 boats & 21,836 nets have been lost due to Cyclone Tauktae in Maharashtra. Financial assistance of Rs. 92.37 crore has been sought by Maharashtra. The Central Government released the first installment of Central share of SDRF for 2021-22 amounting to Rs. 8873.60 crore on 29th April 2021 in advance to all States, including the cyclone affected States. *****
