                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

16






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Tourism Projects Launched in Maharashtra




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sunil Baburao Mendhe
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of tourism projects launched by the Government in the recent past to promote tourism and also create employment opportunities in tourism sector; (b) 	the details of each tourism project sanctioned/being implemented in Maharashtra during the last five years for tourism development, particularly in Ahmednagar, Kolhapur, Nandurbar, Jalgaon and Thane district; (c) 	the details of employment potential created/ proposed to be created due to above projects; (d) 	whether the Government is planning to start any new schemes in the Ministry to attract more foreign tourists and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the other corrective measures taken/being taken by the Government for the better and helpful journey of the foreign tourists?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination and releases domestic and international print, electronic and online media campaigns under the ‘Incredible India’ brand-line, to promote various tourism destinations and products to attract more tourists and generate employment. In addition, Ministry of Tourism is also focussing on the development of tourism related infrastructure under its flagship schemes of Swadesh Darshan and National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) which were launched in January 2015. Details of projects sanctioned under the Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD Schemes to different States/UTs including Maharashtra is given at Annexure.	The projects sanctioned under the above schemes are under various stages of implementation and on completion would lead to more footfalls to these tourism destinations/sites which in turn would help to create more job opportunities for the local communities.(d):	Ministry of Tourism has identified 19 sites under the Development of Iconic Tourist Destination Scheme. The development of tourism infrastructure projects under the Iconic Scheme will be undertaken in collaboration with Central Ministries, State Governments, Urban Local Bodies, Local Community and Industry players/Private sector, etc.(e):	Ministry of Tourism has taken several steps for the better and helpful journey of the foreign tourists which are:i.	Launched 24x7 toll free Multi-Lingual Tourist Helpline. ii.	Providing facility of e-Visa for 5 sub-categories i.e. e-Tourist visa, e-Business visa, e-Medical visa, e-Medical Attendant visa and e-Conference visa for nationals of 171 countries.iii.	E-Visa has been further liberalized and the visa fee has been substantially reduced.iv.	Launched Incredible India Tourist Facilitator Programme, a Pan-India digital initiative to provide basic, advanced, spoken foreign language and refresher courses for creating a pool of trained and certified tourist facilitators which would help in creating employment opportunities at the local level. v.	Conducting Programmes under the ‘Capacity Building for Service Providers’ (CBSP) Scheme to train and up-grade manpower to provide better service standards. vi.	On the recommendation of the Ministry of Tourism, 46 tourism routes have been awarded to the identified airlines by Ministry of Civil Aviation under the RCS UDAN Scheme. 27 of these routes have been operationalized till date.vii.	Revamped Incredible India Website having information on tourism sites/ destinations in different States/Union Territory Administrations.*******
