                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4698






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Operation Smile




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of children rescued under Operation Smile or Operation Muskan;(b)the total number of FIRs registered in this regard; and(c)the number of missing or rescued children reunited with their families?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	As per information received from Ministry of Home Affairs, Operation Smile/Operation Muskan were organized by some State Governments in the past. No Central data is compiled in this regard.******
