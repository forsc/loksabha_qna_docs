                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Fraud by Bit Coin Companies




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is seized of the growing reports of cheating and fraudulent practices by bit coin companies;(b) if so, whether such companies are repeatedly violating rules laid out by the Corporate Affairs Ministry by not filing annual balance sheets; and  (c) if so, the details thereof and the action taken against such companies so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS	                                  (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)????? ??? ????????? ????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? 			               (?????????? ???? ?????)(a) to (c): The Bit coin companies are not defined under the Companies Act. However, the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) has conducted inspection through Registrar of Companies (RoC), Ahmedabad in the matter of ZEB IT Services Private Limited to examine the aspect with regard to dealing in Bit coins and based on the findings, Prosecutions have been filed for violations under various provisions of the Companies Act and the same also have been concluded on payment of penalty on the compounding application(s) filed by the company. The said company has filed its Balance Sheet as at 31.03.2019 and relevant Annual Return and the Company is Under Liquidation.There is one more company registered with RoC, Karnataka called Unocoin Technologies Private Limited which is in the business of developing online market platform to facilitate and conduct buy, sell, store and accept bit coins and this companies is up to date in filing statutory returns and no complaints has been received. 					*********
