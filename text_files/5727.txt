                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5273






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Road Projects under TOT Model




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to auction the road projects identified under the Toll  Operate Transfer (TOT) model to monetize public funded national highways in the country;(b)	 if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c) 	whether the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) plans to lease 75 national highway projects under TOT model and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether many foreign companies have shown interests on Government-to- Government (G2G) deal for projects under TOT model and if so, the details including the names thereof;(e) 	whether some of these companies want the projects to be awarded under a G2G deal on a negotiated basis; and(f) 	if so, the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. First Bundle of TOT with a length of 680 km has been awarded to M/s MAIF Investments India Pvt. Ltd. and Ashoka Buildcon Limited Consortium at Bid Concession fee of Rs 9681.5 cr. Concessionaire has deposited Rs.9681.5 crore to NHAI on 28.08.2018 and TOT bundle 1 project has started w.e.f. 29.08.2018. Bid for TOT Bundle-2 was invited w.e.f. 06.08.2018 and three Bids were received on bid due date of 19.12.2018. However, the Bidding process was annulled due to low quote by highest bidder M/s Cube [14% below Authority Estimated Initial Estimated Concession Value (IECV)]. Bid for TOT Bundle-3 has been invited on 13.06.2019 with bid due date of 11.09.2019. (c) Yes Sir. National Highway (NH) projects having approximately 6400 k.m. of length have been identified under TOT Model.(d) to (f) No Sir. No such proposal has been received.*****
