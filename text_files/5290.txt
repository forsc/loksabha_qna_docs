                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Soil Testing




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether soil testing work is being conducted in various district of Odisha; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the number of people who have been benefited by the said soil testing work during the last three years?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir.  Soil testing work is being conducted in 27 static soil testing labs and 17 mobile soil testing labs in Odisha. The location-wise and district-wise details are given at Annexure-I.(c):	The number of people benefited / soil health cards distributed to the farmers by the said soil testing work during last three years in Odisha is as under:Year	Number of people benefited / soil health cards  distributed to the farmers2016-17	17199272017-18	5096852018-19	854236
