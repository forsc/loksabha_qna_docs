                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1785






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Central Road and Infrastructue Fund




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of new roads identified for development under the Central Road and Infrastructure Fund in the State of Maharashtra, district-wise; (b) the details of roads selected and construction work started on the recommendations of the Governemnt of Maharashtra and Members of Parliament from the State; and (c) the funds allocated and released by the Union Government to Maharashtra for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) 	The district-wise details of new roads identified by the State Government of Maharashtra for development under the Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) in the State of Maharashtra are at Annexure – I.(b) 	The details of roads selected and construction work started on the recommendations of the Government of Maharashtra and Members of Parliament from the State are at Annexure – II.(c )	The details of accrual/ allocation of funds and the funds released/expenditure incurred under CRIF  for the State of Maharashtra during last three years and current year  are as under:Sl.
