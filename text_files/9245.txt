                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

250






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Maintenance of NHs




Rama Devi













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry of Finance is providing only 40 per cent of the funds required for maintenance and repair of the National Highways (NHs) in the country;(b) if so, whether his Ministry has held any discussion with the Ministry of Finance in this regard and if so, the details and the outcome thereof;(c) the funds received from the Ministry of Finance for maintenance and repair of NHs during the last three years and the current year; and(d) the funds spent by his Ministry for the purpose during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
