                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






New Medical Colleges in Aspirational Districts




Chandra Prakash Choudhary













                                                     Bhagirath Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of proposals received and approved under phase III of the centrally sponsored scheme for establishing  new  medical  colleges  in  aspirational  and backward districts attached with existing district/referral hospitals, State/UT wise, especially in Jharkhand; (b) the districts  whose  proposals  are  under consideration for establishing new medical colleges under the  said  scheme;(c)the  details  of  medical  colleges  established  in such  districts/areas  under  phase  I  and  II  of  the  said scheme along with the funds allocated and utilised for the  purpose,  State/UT-wise;(d)the reasons for delay if any, in establishing the medical colleges under the said scheme, State/UT-wise, particularly in Jharkhand and Rajasthan; and(e)the status of completion of work of each of such medical colleges approved under the said scheme as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b) Under Phase-III of Centrally Sponsored Scheme(CSS) namely ‘Establishment of new Medical Colleges attached with existing district/referralhospitals’, the Ministry had received 27 proposal from States/UTs for establishing new medical colleges in aspirational districts. Out of which, 21 proposals have been approved based on the criteria of Scheme. List of these approved medical colleges is at Annexure – I. Further, under Phase-III of the Scheme, all the 75 approved medical colleges have already been allocated and therefore, no further proposals are under consideration. (c) List of approved medical colleges in aspirational districts under Phase-I and Phase-II of the said Scheme is at Annexure-II.(d) & (e) Under the Scheme, a total 157 medical colleges have been sanctioned, out of which, 47 have become functional. The concerned State/UT Governments are the implementing agencies under the Scheme. As per information provided by the States/UTs, the works of the remaining medical colleges are at various stages of completion. The State-wise list of 47 medical colleges that have become functional is at Annexure-III.
