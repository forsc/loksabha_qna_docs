                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1187






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






INX Media Case




Vinod Kumar Sonkar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has completed the probe of money laundering case of INX Media;  (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the details of property seized;(c)	whether the Government has calculated the loss of exchequer in the said  case and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the roles of the accused including several senior government  officers have been established in this case; (e)	if so, the details thereof along with the action taken thereon; and(f)	the other steps being taken by the Government for preventing such type of financial scams in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (f) : Based on FIR of Central Bureau of Investigation alleging irregularities in grant of FIPB approvals to INX Media and related entities, Enforcement Directorate has registered a case under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA). Assets totalling of Rs. 53.93 crores have been provisionally attached. Disclosure of details of investigation in the case at this stage may not be in larger public interest as the same may adversely impact the ongoing investigation                    *****
