                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Nationalized Coal Mines




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the nationalized coal mines under the Coal Mines (Nationalization) Act, 1973; and  (b) the names of schemes being run for promoting coal based industries like cement plants, power plants, textiles etc. in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) At present, 4 coal blocks under Coal Mines (Nationalization) Act, 1973 are in production whose details are attached at Annexure. (b)  No schemes of the Government of India are being run in Rajasthan to promote coal based industries such as cement plants, power plants, textiles etc.  *** Annexure The details of coal blocks under Coal Mines (Nationalization) Act, 1973 presently in production:-   Sl. No	Name of Coal Block allocated	State	Name of the Company	End -Use	Date ofAllotment1	Tasra	Jharkhand	Steel Authority of India Ltd.(SAIL)	Sponge Iron	17.04.19962	Pakri-Barwadih	Jharkhand	NTPC	Power	11.10.20043	Moher	Madhya Pradesh	Sasan Power Limited (SPL)	Power	13.09.20064	Moher-Amlori Extn	Madhya Pradesh	Sasan Power Limited (SPL)	Power	13.09.2006  ***
