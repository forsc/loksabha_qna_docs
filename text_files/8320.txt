                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1843






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Implementation of PMSSY




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                








                                                    Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether implementation of Swasthya Suraksha Yojana has taken up in the State of Andhra Pradesh and Gujarat, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether it is a fact that the Government has released Rs. 150 crore for the projects under this scheme to Anantapur and Vijayawada in Andhra Pradesh, if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of targets fixed and time line proposed to complete these projects under the PMSSY; and (d) the details of the funds released so far for the projects under this scheme to Gujarat and the status of implementation of these projects as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):   Yes. Government of India has taken up following projects under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) in the State of Andhra Pradesh and Gujarat :I. Andhra Pradesh1.	Upgradation of SVIMS, Tirupati2.	Upgradation of Siddhartha Medical College, Vijayawada3.	Upgradation of Government Medical College, Anantapur4.	Setting up of new AIIMS at MangalagiriContd……-2-II. Gujarat1.	Upgradation of BJ Medical College, Ahmedabad2.	Upgradation of Government Medical College, Rajkot3.	Upgradation of Government Medical College, Bhavnagar4.	Upgradation of Government Medical College, Surat5.	Setting up of new AIIMS at Rajkot  (b) & (c):  Siddhartha Medical College, Vijayawada and Government Medical College, Anantapur have been taken up for upgradation at an approved cost of Rs. 150 crore each (Central share – Rs. 120 crore and State share – Rs. 30 crore), under Phase-III of PMSSY. Details of funds released are as follows:Project 	Central share released	State share released	StatusUpgradation of Siddhartha Medical College, Vijayawada	109.12 crore	Nil	Civil work completed.Upgradation of Government Medical College, Anantapur	120 crore	Nil	Progress of civil work – 91.85%            Timeline for completion of the ugradation work depends upon various factors like approval of authorities concerned, approval of DPR and issue of notification of award. The timeline for completion of works is mentioned in the notification of award and is generally for a period of 16 to 24 months from date of award of work depending upon the size of the project and local site specific conditions.(d):      PMSSY is a Central Sector Scheme, being implemented by the Ministry directly through Executing Agency and Procurement Support Agency. Under PMSSY, funds are released directly to Executing Agency and Procurement Support Agency.
