                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1774






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






National Sports Award to Sportspersons




Vishnu Dutt Sharma













                                                    Sandhya Ray
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of sportspersons who have been shortlisted and given National Sports Award for the year 2019 by the Selection Committee;(b) whether the Government has also awarded special honours and cash award to sportspersons who have brought glory to the country and if so, the amount of cash award announced and given to sportspersons during each of the last three years and the current year;(c) whether there is any scheme for providing assistance for livelihood to these sportspersons after their retirement or at elderly age;(d) if so, the number of sportspersons benefited so far from this scheme;(e) if not, whether the Government proposes to work out any such scheme in the future and if so, the details thereof; and(f) the other steps taken by the Government for the welfare of sportspersons in the country?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[KIREN RIJIJU](a) : Information relating to number of sportspersons shortlisted for Sports Awards by the Selection Committee and of sportspersons given awards is as below :-<pre>S. No.	Name of Sports Award 		No. of Sports Persons Shortlisted	No. of Sports Persons given Awards1 	Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award			06				022	Dronacharya Award	(Regular Category)				06				033	Dronacharya Award	(Life Time category)				07				034	Arjuna Award					36				195	Dhyan Chand Award				08				056	Rashtriya Khel Protsahan Puruskar Award		09				037	Maulana Abul Kalam Azad (MAKA) Trophy		04				01</pre>(b) : Cash award is given to the sportspersons for winning medals in international sports events under ‘The Special Awards to Medal winners in International Sports events and their Coaches Scheme’. The information relating to the amount of cash award given to sportspersons during each of the last three years and the current year (as on 25.11.2019) are as under :<pre>S. No.	Year			No. of Sportspersons/ coaches		Amount (in Rs.)1	2016-17			854					20,95,82,3212	2017-18			489					11,60,12,5003	2018-19			307					24,86,20,0004	2019-20			502					41,68,34,000</pre>(c): Yes Sir. Ministry is implementing the Scheme of ‘Pension to Meritorious Sportspersons’ for providing lifelong monthly pension to medal winners of Olympic Games, Asian Games, Commonwealth Games, etc. after they retire from active sports and the Scheme of ‘Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons’ (PDUNWFS)’ for providing financial assistance to sportspersons of yesteryears now living in indigent circumstances.(d) : At present, 627 sportspersons are getting lifelong pension ranging from Rs.12000-20000 per month under the Scheme of ‘Pension to Meritorious Sportspersons’. Details of financial assistance given to sportspersons under PDUNWFS scheme during last 3 years and the current years (as on 25.11.2019) are as under :<pre>S. No.	Year		Number of sportspersons given assistance	Amount (in Rs.)1	2016-17		03						12,85,0002	2017-18		06						25,37,5003	2018-19		12						66,00,0004	2019-20		12						49,32,415</pre>(e) : Does not arise.(f) : Other steps taken by the Government for welfare of the sportspersons include the facility of complimentary card passes given by Ministry of Railways to Arjuna Awardees, Dronacharya Awardees etc., which entitle them lifelong free rail travel for self and for companion on attaining the age of 65 years and provision of upto 5% reservation in direct recruitment in Group ‘C’ posts for meritorious sportspersons in Central Government.****
