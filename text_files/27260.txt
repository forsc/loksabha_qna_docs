                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2842






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Six-laning of Jetpur-Gondal-Rajkot Section




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved existing four lane Jetpur-Gondal-Rajkot section into six lane;(b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof along with the time frame set for completion of the said project;(c)	the total estimated cost of the said conversion project;(d)	the funds allocated, released and utilised thereon till date; and(e)	the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)   The development of this stretch has been envisaged in the category of National Corridor (East-West Corridor) in Bharatmala Pariyojana. The Land Acquisition Committee of NHAI has approved the alignment. The project is targeted for award in the current Financial Year with tentative cost of Rs. 1,572.69 Crores.(d) & (e)   Does not arise as the work has not been awarded.   	*****
