                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2682






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






New Oil Terminal/Depots




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government proposes to install new oil terminal/depots of various oil producing companies at certain places in the country;(b)    if so, the details thereof including Karnataka;(c)   the time by which the said projects are likely to start and to be completed; and(d)  the details of the fresh steps taken by the Government to improve the availability of all the petroleum products across the country? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) to (d)   As on 01.02.2022, there are 306 Oil Terminals/Depots in the country. The installation of new Oil Terminals/Depots is a continuous process depending upon the requirement of a particular region including fulfillment of statutory norms and commercial viability. Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have informed that 14 new Oil Terminals/Depots are under construction in the country. Recently, a new Oil Depot has been commissioned at Gulbarga in Karnataka during the year 2022. Presently, availability of petroleum products is sufficient to meet the full market demand of the country.*******
