                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1538






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Vacancies in Post Offices




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of vacancies of Multi Tasking Staff, Postmen and Postal Assistants in Bihar Postal Circle;(b) 	the time since when these posts are lying vacant in the circle, Post-wise;(c) 	the steps taken by the Government to fill up these vacancies and the time by when it is likely to be done?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) 	421 posts of Multi Tasking Staff, 494 posts of Postman and 308 posts of Postal Assistant are vacant in Bihar Postal Circle. (b) & (c) Vacancies arising on account of retirement, promotion, death and deputation are being filled up periodically by holding departmental examination, direct recruitment through Staff Selection Commission and also by individual Circle as per the provisions of the relevant recruitment rules. Recruitment to fill up vacant posts is a continuous process.******
