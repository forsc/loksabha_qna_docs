                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3769






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Release of Performance Grant




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received representation from the State Government of Tamil Nadu for release of the pending performance grant for 2017-18 and 2018-19 as per 14th Finance Commission recommendations;   (b)  if so, the details thereof;   (c)  the details of the grant allocated to Tamil Nadu for rural development during the last five years; and  (d) the reasons for not releasing the performance grant on time as per the recommendation of the 14th Finance Commission?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. The details are provided at Annexure I. Holding election of local bodies in the State is a pre-condition for recommendation for release of the Fourteenth Finance Commission (FFC) Grants, this is pending for Tamil Nadu since October, 2016.(c) The details are provided at Annexure II.(d) In the absence of duly constituted Gram Panchayats by elections, FFC Performance Grant could not be recommended for release to the State of Tamil Nadu, after FY 2016-17.******
