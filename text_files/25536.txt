                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Funds Sanctioned under MGNREGS in West Bengal




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount sanctioned for the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Gurarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in the country during the current year;(b) 	the amount sanctioned for MGNREGS and construction work in West Bengal State, particularly for Maldaha Uttar parliamentary constituency?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):	The Government of India has provided Rs. 73,000 crore at Budget Estimate stage for current financial year 2021-22 for Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS).(b):	Fund is released to State Government. No fund is released to districts by Central Government. During the current financial year 2021-22, a total of 3869.15 crore has been released to State of West Bengal so far. A total of 25,20,465 works have been taken up in the State of West Bengal (including Malda District) in the current financial year 2021-22 (as on 23.07.2021) under the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. The details of category-wise works taken up and expenditure incurred in the District of Malda of West Bengal in financial year 2021-22 (as on 23rd July, 2021) is given at Annexure.******
