                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2795






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Spiritual Circuit Scheme




M. P. Abdussamad Samadani














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the scheme of Spiritual circuit formulated by the Ministry of Tourism;(b) 	the details of other projects announced along with this;(c) 	the details of the schemes announced along with the Spiritual circuit scheme that are now being implement; and(d) 	the details of the schemes which have been cancelled?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 				(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism in the year 2014-15 launched two schemes – ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive’ - (PRASHAD) with the objective to develop tourism infrastructure in the country. Under Swadesh Darshan scheme, the Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned 76 projects in identified thematic circuits including Spiritual circuit.  In addition the Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned 39 projects under PRASHAD scheme. The details  of the projects  sanctioned  under  various  themes  of the  Swadesh Darshan scheme  and  PRASHAD  scheme is  annexed. (d):	The two schemes i.e. Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD were launched in 2014-15 and   are ongoing schemes of the Ministry of Tourism. *******
