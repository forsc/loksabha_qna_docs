                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2477






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Housing Targets for Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of targets set by the Government to construct housing units in Santhal Pargana of Jharkhand under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) during the last three years, year-wise;(b)	the details of housing units constructed in the above State during the said  period location-wise; and(c)	the details of funds allocated in this regard during said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                      (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Year-wise and location-wise details of target houses sanctioned during the last three years in the Santhal Pargana of Jharkhand under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) Mission are at Annexure-I. (b) & (c): Location-wise details of housing units constructed in the State of Jharkhand and funds sanctioned/allocated in this regard during the last three years under PMAY-U Mission are at Annexure-II.                                     *****
