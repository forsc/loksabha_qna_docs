                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

559






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Indians Trapped Abroad




Dayakar Pasunoori













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any details of Indians who are/were trapped/held captive in Iraq, Libya and other countries and if so, the details thereof during the last three years and the current year;(b)  	whether the Government has provided any financial assistance to such stranded persons in each country and if so, the details of the help desks opened and treatment meted out to such trapped persons abroad and the steps being taken to safely evacuate them; and(c)  	the number of Indians who have returned to the country and the number of Indians who are still trapped in Iraq, Libya and other foreign countries?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS  [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN]	(a)  	As per available information, 77 Indians have been trapped/ held captive in various countries during the last three years and the current year. Country-wise list is given at Annexure.(b)      The safety and security of Indian nationals in foreign countries is a matter of foremost concern for the Government of India. The Government keeps a close watch on the situation in strife-torn countries, issues advisories wherever required and engages with the local authorities regularly to prevent incidents of hostage/ kidnapping, while simultaneously making every effort to secure the release of those in captivity.        Ministry/ concerned Indian Mission/ Post abroad also provides timely information to their family members. Financial support to such stranded Indians is made available by Indian Missions/ Posts abroad in deserving cases on a means tested basis under the Indian Community Welfare Fund (ICWF).(c)	 As per information available in the Ministry, 73 out of 77 Indians have returned to India, 1 died during captivity and 3 Indians are still in captivity. Government of India is pro-actively pursuing the cases of the remaining 3 Indians for their early return to India.<pre>AnnexureLAFAQ 559 Indians trapped abroad (To be answered on 20.11.2019) SNoName of CountryNo. of indians trapped/held captive in foreign countries1Afghanistan 102Cuba23Iraq14Kenya105Myanmar56Nigeria437Serbia18South Africa39South Sudan2Total77</pre>***
