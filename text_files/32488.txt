                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

20






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Telecommunication Facilities




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has conducted any survey in Rajasthan to find out the areas without the facility of telecommunication till date; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, district-wise including Dausa; (c) 	whether the Government is aware that the people of the State living in villages particularly in Dhania village have to face a lot of difficulties especially during emergencies due to the lack of telecommunication facility and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to provide telecommunication facility there?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS & ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF THE LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 20 FOR 2ND FEBRUARY, 2022 REGARDING ‘TELECOMMUNICATION FACILITIES’.(a) & (b) 	Yes Sir. The Department of Telecommunications (DoT) identifies uncovered villages of telecom connectivity of the country. As per data provided by Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) and field units of Department of Telecommunications as of December2020, it is estimated that out of 43,264 inhabited villages (as per Census-2011) in the state of Rajasthan, 941 villages have no telecom connectivity. The District wise details of the uncovered villages are given in Annexure-I.(c) & (d) 	As per data provided by Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) and field unit of DoT, it is estimated that out of 1079 inhabited villages (as per Census-2011) in Dausa district of state of Rajasthan, only 2 villages namely Dhaun (Census code-78756) and Ghata (Census code-78755) have no telecom connectivity. To further enhance the mobile connectivity in the State of Rajasthan, 226 villages of Rajasthan State have been included in Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) schemes. The remaining villages of the country, including the villages of Rajasthan, are covered by the Government and TSPs in a phased manner.
