                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4084






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Revision of Toll Rates




Arvind Kumar Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government fixes toll rates to be charged by concessionaire under Build, Operate and Transfer (BOT) model;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any policy in place to regularly revise these toll rates as per changing traffic volume so as to pass on the benefits of increasing traffic volume to commuters; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) &(b) User fee is levied and collected on National Highways as per the Rule 4(2) of the National Highways Fee (Determination of Rates and Collection) Rules, 2008. These rates are revised annually as per the Rule 5 of the National Highways Fee (Determination of Rates and Collection) Rules, 2008.(c) No, Sir.(d) Does not arise. *****
