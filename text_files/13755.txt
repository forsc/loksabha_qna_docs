                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2667






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Integrated AYUSH Hospital




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has approved the setting up of a 50-bedded integrated AYUSH hospital in Anantgiri, Vikarabad, Ranga Reddy District of Telangana; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether DPR has been prepared for the said project; and (d) if so, the details thereof, the funds allocated for the same and the time by which this hospital is likely to become operational?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (d):    Under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), Government of India has approved setting up of upto 50-bedded integrated AYUSH hospital in Anantgiri, Vikarabad, Ranga Reddy District of Telangana during 2016-17.State Government has prepared the DPR for Rs. 600.00 lakhs and submitted to Government of India. Accordingly, proposal of 50-bedded integrated AYUSH hospital in Anantgiri, Vikarabad, Ranga Reddy District of Telangana has been approved and grant-in-aid of Rs 360.00 lakhs has been released as central share during the year 2016-17. Further, as per information received from State Government of Telangana, this hospital is to be made functional after recruitment of manpower. …………………
