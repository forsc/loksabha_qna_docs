                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Resumption of Train in Kerala




M. P. Abdussamad Samadani














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the sufferings of train passengers due to the withdrawal of passenger train services in the Shoranur-Nilambur route under the Palakkad Division in Kerala;(b)	if so, the reasons behind non-resuming of the passenger train service in this route;(c)	whether the Government proposes to take necessary steps to resume all the train services including passenger trains in the route and to mitigate the difficulties being faced by the people; and (d)	if so, the details in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW) (a) to (d) To prevent the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, Indian Railways had discontinued all regular passenger carrying trains w.e.f. 23rd March, 2020. Since November, 2021 Mail/Express trains are being operated with regular numbers as per rationalised time table.  To cater to service of Shoranur-Nilambur section, 16349/16350 Kochuveli-Nilambur Rajya Rani Express, 16325/16326 Nilambur-Kottayam Express, 06466/06473 Nilambur-Shoranur Jn Specials are being operated.  Besides, passenger train services are being restored in a phased manner.*****
