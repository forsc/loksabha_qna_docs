                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

909






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Proper Implementation of Educational Schemes




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that special schemes for higher and technical education meant for poor students are not being implemented properly;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(c) the concrete steps taken/being taken by the Government for proper implementation of these schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a)to (c): The Government is committed to make higher and technical education accessible to all sections of society including poor students. The Department of Higher Education as well as other Ministries implement scholarship schemes and interest subsidy schemes for the benefit of such students. The schemes are implemented as per instant guidelines of respective implementing agencies. For the scholarship/ interest subsidy schemes run by the Department of Higher Education (DHE), the amounts are disbursed to the beneficiary account through Direct Benefit Transfer with Public Finance Management System (DBT with PFMS) integration to ensure proper implementation.*****
