                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Mining Industries




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government encourages setting up of manufacturing industries where these are backed by mining industries which has the potential of development of even remote and inaccessible areas and also helps in creation of a large number of jobs;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the extent to which and the manner in which the Government will involve the private players for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)  (a) to (c):  The Mines and Mineral (Development & Regulation) Act, 1957 provides for development and regulation of mines and minerals in the country. The Ministry is committed towards development of mineral sector in the country and thrust has been given to exploration of new areas including  remote and inaccessible areas through the agencies notified under section 4(1) of the MMDR Act. These agencies are being funded through National Mineral Exploration Trust (NMET) for exploration projects in addition to their own budgetary resources. This will also encourage new industries to be set up in  remote and inaccessible areas which have mineral potential.  (d): Section 5 of the MMDR Act, 1957 provides for grant of mineral concessions to an Indian national or a company as defined in the Companies Act, 2013. The mining leases are granted  through e-auction. Therefore,  private players are actively involved in mining sector.  *****
