                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

631






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Deaths due to Heatwaves




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details regarding deaths from the heatwaves in the country during the last five years;(b) whether the number of deaths due to heatwaves has increased due to the lapses in providing facilities to the poor for their protection from heatwave; (c) if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(d) whether the number of deaths due to heatwaves are higher among the poors/ labourers residing near cities as compared to rural areas where nature has not been destroyed so brutally; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The data on deaths due to heatwave is not being maintained centrally by this Ministry. However, as per the information received from Integrated Disease Surveillance Programme in the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, the deaths reported due to heat related illness during the last five years are as under:-2-L.S.US.Q.NO.631 FOR 25.06.2019YearNo. of deaths (Provisional)2015	20402016	11112017	3842018	252019(till 16.06.2019)	94(b) to (e):	There is no segregated economic status-wise or residential place-wise information available on deaths occurred due to heatwaves. However, with the efforts of the Central Government and State Government, numbers of death due to heat wave have declined significantly during 2016-2018.******
