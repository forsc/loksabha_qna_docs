                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Steel Scrap




Prataprao  Jadhav













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the demand and production level of steel has been assessed for the next decade and if so, the details thereof along with the action plan initiated in this regard; and(b)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to reduce the dependence on steel scrap imports in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	As per the National Steel Policy, 2017, the expected crude steel demand/production in the country is likely to be 255 MTPA and total domestic finished steel demand/production would be 230 MTPA by 2030-31. The Government has notified National Steel Policy, 2017 with a view to encourage long term growth of domestic steel sector.   (b)	The Steel Scrap Recycling Policy notified by Ministry of Steel in Gazette of India on 07.11.2019 provides a framework to facilitate and promote establishment of scrapping centres for scientific processing & recycling of ferrous scrap generated from various sources in an organized and environment friendly manner to give boost to import substitution of Steel Scrap.********
