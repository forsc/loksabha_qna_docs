                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1752






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Stoppages under Eastern Railways




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the stations under East-Central Railway and Eastern Railway at which halts of new trains have been demanded by the local public representatives; and(b) the steps taken in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b)  Requests/representations from various quarters viz. public representatives, Members of Parliament, etc. for provision of stoppages of trains, are received at various levels of Railways administration.  These are examined and action as found feasible and justified is taken from time to time keeping in view the operational feasibility, commercial viability, availability of alternative services, traffic demands etc.*****
