                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4652






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Mining Work in HZL Plants




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the mining work in the mining plant of Hindustan Zinc Limited (HZL) Dariba/Sindesar Khurd is done by the State/Central Government Explosive Wing or by any other licensed company; and(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the quantity of explosives used for mining work in the mining plant of HZL etc.?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) & (b): The Petroleum and Explosive Safety Organization (an Organization under the Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade) issues explosive license for mining work under Explosives Rules, 2008. Mining work in Dariba and Sindesar Khurd Mines are being done by Hindustan Zinc Limited.  The explosive license for doing mining work in Dariba and Sindesar Khurd Mines are in the name of Hindustan Zinc Limited (HZL). As per information provided by HZL, the details of explosive license are given below:Number of LicenseE/NC/RJ/22/213/(E9829)E/NC/RJ/22/217/(E9843)E/NC/RJ/22/644/(E24549)E/HQ/RJ/22/189/(E24494)E/NC/RJ/22/584/(E24292)E/HQ/RJ/22/237/(E36258)E/HQ/RJ/22/310/(E24935)E/HQ/RJ/SM/10 (E79718) As per information provided by HZL, it  has used 917809 Kg & 3531648 kg explosive quantity at Dariba & Sindesar Khurd Mines respectively during the year 2020. *****
