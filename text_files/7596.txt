                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






UGC




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the proposals of the colleges are referredto Regional Office at Bhopal in the absence of State/Regional Office of UniversityGrants Commission (UGC) in Chhattisgarh and colleges face difficulties to contactthis office which is 700 km from the State capital;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to open State/Regional office ofUniversity Grants Commission (UGC) in Chhattisgarh;(c) whether the Government has received any proposal in this regard from theGovernment of Chhattisgarh;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the decisions taken by the Government on it ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e): Presently, the University Grants Commission (UGC) already has sevenRegional Offices at Hyderabad, Pune, Bhopal, Kolkata, Guwahati, Bangalore andDelhi for implementation of various schemes/ programmes relating to college sector.The Regional Office of UGC situated at Bhopal looks after the work related toMadhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and Chhattisgarh States.The UGC has informed that, at present, there is no active proposal to open aRegional Office in Chhattisgarh.******
