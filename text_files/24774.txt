                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether instances of corruption has come to fore in the ongoing lift irrigation projects of Telangana namely Kaleshwaram and Palamuru RangaReddy lift irrigation projects and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has any plans to order investigation in this matter; (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (d) whether there has been several instances of leakage from dams of above mentioned irrigation projects; and (e) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI AND TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) to (c)  Irrigation projects are planned, implemented and managed by the concerned State Governments, as per their own resources and priorities. Role of Government of India is limited to providing technical support, and partial financial support in some cases, as per the norms of the existing schemes being implemented by Government of India.                Both Kaleshwaram and Palamuru Rangareddy Lift Irrigation projects are being executed by the Government of Telangana from their resources. As informed by the State Government, no instances of corruption have come to its notice regarding these projects.(d) & (e) Government of Telangana has informed that no such instance of leakage from the dams of the above projects has come to its notice.*****
