                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

738






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Kuttanad Inland Faring Region




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has allocated and released any funds for the purpose of strengthening of the outer bunds of Kuttanad inland faring region in Kerala State during the last three years; (b) if so, the quantum of funds allocated and released for the purpose, year-wise; (c) whether the Union Government has received any request from the Government of Kerala in this regard; (d) if so, the details thereof along with the response of the Union Government thereon; (e) whether the Union Government is aware that the strengthening of outer bunds is necessary to protect the paddy farming in Kuttanad which is a below mean sea level farming zone; and (f) if so, the details of action taken in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a ) & (b) Four Projects in the Kuttanad region of Kerala were approved during XI Plan under Flood Management Programme (FMP) of the Ministry. The details of Central Assistance (CA) released during last three years is as under:S. No.	Scheme	Central Assistance released in Rs. Crores	Total Central Assistance released till date in Rs. Crores		2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	1.	KEL-1Mitigation of Flood in Group-I (Padasekharams) in Kuttanad region 	-	1.29		11.202.	KEL-2Regulation of flood water in Kayal area, 4 padesekharams and Mitigation of floods in group 9, 5 nos. Padasekharams in Kuttanad region	-	-	-	65.863.	KEL-3Mitigation of floods in Kuttanad region – Phase-I (group 2-5, 7, 8, 10-19), 231 padasekharams (paddy fields)	-	17.76	-	49.644.	KEL-4Mitigation of floods in Onattukara region comprising of 12 watersheds in Kerala State	-	-	-	11.25Contd…P/2//2// (c) & (d)  No proposal for release of outstanding Central Assistance in respect of these four projects, as per extant guidelines of FMP, has been received from the State Government of Kerala in this Ministry, in the current Financial year 2019-20. (e) & (f) Union Government is aware that the strengthening of outer bunds is necessary to protect the paddy farming in Kuttanad which is below mean sea level farming zone.  The flood management schemes are formulated and implemented by concerned States as per their priority. The Union Government supplements the efforts of the States by providing technical advice and promotional financial assistance for management of floods in critical areas. Considering the situation in Kuttanad area above four projects were approved under FMP of Union Government wherein, outer bunds were strengthened in an area about 16000 hectares.*****
