                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Seafood Export




Nalin Kumar Kateel













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    B. Y. Raghavendra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of India’s seafood exports during the last three years and the current year;(b)	whether it is true that there is increasing demand for India’s seafood from other countries;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether there is a growth of 13.7% in quantity and 10.1% in value, if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether it is a fact that researchers in 2019 India’s seafood exports will grow at 17 to 18% clearly showing a demand for Indian products, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (e): The quantum, value along with annual growth of the major group of seafood exported from India from 2016-17 to 2018-19, and the latest available data for the month of April of the current year is as under:  Value of India''s Export of Seafood (in US$ million)HSCode	Commodity	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 (April 2019)03	FISH AND CRUSTACEANS, MOLLUSCS AND OTHER AQUATIC INVERTABRATES.	5501.05	6,850.91	6,256.57	429.67	Annual Growth	22.6%	24.5%	-8.7%	--Quantity of India’s Export of Seafood (in thousand tons)HSCode	Commodity 	2016-2017	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 (April 2019)03	FISH AND CRUSTACEANS, MOLLUSCS AND OTHER AQUATIC INVERTABRATES.	1023.62	1283.04	1279.72	98.40	Annual Growth (%)	23.3%	25.3%	-0.3%	--Source: Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence & Statistics*****
