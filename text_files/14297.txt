                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Sagarmala Project in Karnataka




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans to implement     Sagarmala project in Karnataka; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the proposal/plan of the Government to protect the     livelihood of the people affected by this project?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)   31 Projects have been identified under Sagarmala Programme in the state of Karnataka for enhancing the infrastructure related to Port Modernisation, Port Connectivity enhancement and Coastal Community Development. These projects are being implemented by Central Ministries, IWAI, Indian Railways, NHAI, State Government and Major Port. The details of projects in Karnataka undertaken under Sagarmala Programme is annexed.(c)	As a part of the project development, the respective project proponents are required to prepare the Social and Environment Impact Assessment Report to ensure due diligence has been done by them to assess the potential impact of the proposed project on the community and environment. Simultaneously, they need to prepare and implement Social and Environmental Management Plan to mitigate the negative impacts which was investigated during the studies of baseline conditions, impact assessment and evaluation for the project.
