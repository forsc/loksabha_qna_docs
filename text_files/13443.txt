                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2210






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Make in India in Defence Sector




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the details of the number of defence products manufactured so far under Make in India Scheme within the country;(b) the details of the number of products exported during the said period;(c) whether the Government has set any target in this regard and if so, the details thereof along with the achievements made so far;(d) whether the original target of this scheme have yet to be achieved on a large scale; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor and the manner in which the Government proposes to achieve its original target within the fixed time?
