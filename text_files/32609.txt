                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

70






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of consumers complaints filed since inception in various agencies under National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission; (b) 	the number of cases disposed of since inception; and (c)	the number of cases pending with percentage of total disposal?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):  Status of Consumer Cases filed in Consumer Commissions across the  Country since inception is given below:Name of Agency	Cases filed since inception	Cases disposed since inception	Pendency	Disposal percentageNational Commission	139774	117455	22319	84.03%State Commissions	882857	766376	116481	86.81%District Commissions	4484105	4044657	439448	90.20%Total	5506736	4928488	578248	89.50%*****
