                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Diversion of Reserved Forest Land for Road Construction




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received any proposal relating to diversion of 8.54 hectare of reserved forest land for construction of Phaltan-Kurvali-Dalwadi-Uplave-Veloshi-Kulkjai road, i.e part Veloshi to Sitamai Temple Ghat road, District Satara from the State Government of Maharashtra for granting environmental clearance;(b)	if so, the details thereof as on date;(c)	the present status of the said proposal; and(d)	the time by which the said proposal is likely to be finalized along with the reasons for delay therein?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) & (b) No sir, no proposal seeking environment clearance for construction of  Phaltan-Kurvali-Dalwadi-Uplave-Veloshi-Kulkjai i.e part Veloshi to Sitamai Temple Ghat road, District Satara road has been received in this Ministry. (c) & (d)   The State Government of Maharashtra vide their letter dated 05.03.2013 submitted the proposal involving diversion of 8.54 ha of Reserved Forest land for construction of Phaltan-Kuruali-Dalwadi-Uplave-Veloshi-Kulkjai road in Satara District of Maharashtra seeking prior approval of Central Government under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. Regional Office (West Central Zone) of the Ministry at Nagpur vide their letter dated 10.07.2015 accorded final approval to the project under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.***
