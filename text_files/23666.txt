                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4828






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Shortage of TTE




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Southern Railways is in shortage of TTEs, especially the Chennai and Salem divisions and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether on the coach S12 on train 16232 which departed fromKarur on 02/09/2017, there was no TTE on board between Karurand Kumbakonam;(c) if so, the details in this regard;(d) the status of reserved passengers, if the train was not manned;(e) whether there is any rule in the Railways that allows reserved coaches to be left unmanned; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) There is a vacancy of 490 staff in ticket checking cadre in Southern Railway, out of which in Chennai and Salem Divisions there is vacancy of 111 and 121 respectively.(b) No Sir. S12 coach of train 16232 was manned by ticket checking staff of Salem  Division on 02.09.2017, along with S-9, S-10 & S-11 coaches.(c) and (d) Do not arise.(e) No Sir (f) Does not arise.****
