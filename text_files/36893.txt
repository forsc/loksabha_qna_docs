                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4051






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Centrally Sponsored Schemes




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Central assistance to the States under various Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) for labour during the last three years and the current year, year-wise, Scheme-wise including Gujarat;  (b)	the number of labourers who got benefitted from the above scheme during the said period;(c)	whether the State Government had requested for supplementing its efforts through financial help for scheme/programmes that it is implementing for the labourers; and(d)	if so, the details of help extended by the Union Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d): Ministry of Labour and Employment is implementing three Centrally Sponsored Scheme namely National Career Service (NCS), Pradhan Mantri Rojgar Protsahan Yojana (PMRPY) and Coaching and Guidance for SC, ST and Other Backward Classes. The central assistance to the States/UTs is provided under NCS Scheme. The funds are released to States/UTs under various components of NCS against their proposals for establishment of Model Career Centres (MCCs) and Interlinking of Employment Exchanges with NCS Portal. Details of the funds released to State/UTs (including Gujarat) under MCC and Interlinking components of NCS scheme during last three years and current year are as under: 							                                                       (Rs. in Lakhs)Financial Year	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22 (upto 21.03.2022)Funds released to State/UTs	761.98	2595.12	896.73	42.31Funds released to Gujarat	0	35.27	0	0 8.92 lakhs candidates were provided Career Counseling/Vocational Guidance/Confidence building /pre recruitment training during the last three years and current year. This has been done in collaboration with the State/UT Governments.*****
