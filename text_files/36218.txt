                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Achievements of National Urban Digital Mission




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of achievements, in terms of creating digital infrastructure and solutions for citizen-centric governance, made under National Urban Digital Mission (NUDM);(b)	whether programmes and projects have been launched under NUDM in Madhya Pradesh;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the reason therefor






ANSWER




                                                        	             THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                   MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (d): The details of work done under National Urban Digital Mission (NUDM) in terms of creating digital infrastructure and solutions for citizen-centric governance so far are as under:•	Establishment of a Program Unit at CDG, NIUA.•	Selection of an implementation partner for creation of a National Urban Governance Platform with 9 municipal services.•	Creation of beta instance of the Platform.•	Empanelment of 26 Implementation Partners to support State/UTs •	Engagement with 13 States to understand their requirements •	Letter of Intents signed with 8 States/UTs •	Publication of Standards for various services •	17 NUDM Fellows have been recruited and on-boarded.As regards the State of Madhya Pradesh, the Program Team has engaged with the Urban Development Department in Madhya Pradesh, and have apprised the State regarding NUDM.  Currently, there are no projects launched under NUDM in Madhya Pradesh.                                 ******
