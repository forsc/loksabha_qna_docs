                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

133






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Procurement Centres




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of procurement centres set up by various procurement agencies during the last five years in Rajasthan alongwith the quantum of paddy and wheat procured, agency-wise and district-wise; (b) 	the arrangement for safe storage of crops brought by/from the farmers, district-wise; and (c) 	the quantum of paddy, wheat and other crops got destroyed in the absence of safe storage of procured crops in Rajasthan during the last five years, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):  	Food and Civil Supply Department of Rajasthan State  has procured only wheat in last five years. Number of procurement centres, district/agency-wise is at Annexure-I and quantum of procurement of wheat district/agency- wise is at Annexure-II.    (b):  The district-wise storage capacity for safe storage of crops in Rajasthan is at Annexure-III.
