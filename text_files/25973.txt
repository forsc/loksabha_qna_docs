                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Flights Operation from Chiyanki Airport




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether the Government proposes to modernize Chiyanki airport in Jharkhand and start flight operations from the said airport; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b):  Chiyanki airport, Daltonganj, Jharkhand figures in the list of unserved airports in UDAN document. However, no airline has submitted valid bid for operation of UDAN flights from Daltonganj till completion of 4th round of bidding. On the request of the State Government of Jharkhand, "Daltonganj-Ranchi-Daltonganj" routes were offered in special round of bidding under UDAN 4.1 conducted recently.
