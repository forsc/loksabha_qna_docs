                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Misappropriation of Funds




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian













                                                    D. M. Kathir Anand
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has received any plea to probe the scam of embezzlement of Government funds to the tune of several crores during the last five years in the Sarvodaya Sangh located in Tamil Nadu.(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the action initiated against the erring officials of KVIC and of the Sarvodaya Sangh functioning at Avarampalayam, Koduvai and Salem in particular; and (c) 	whether the Government initiated stringent action against the KVIC officials for colluding with the scamsters and for the misappropriation of funds allocated to the weavers, spinners and artisans during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) to (c):	No Sir. However, complaints were received by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) relating to mismanagement / misappropriation / mishandling of funds from bank accounts of artisans. As per guidelines, financial audit of the Survodaya Sanghs are conducted every year and during the inspections carried out by the KVIC Officials, no adverse observation regarding, misutilization of funds by the Survodaya Sanghs and involvement of the KVIC officials has  been reported.
