                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4241






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Paraplegic Disease




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether there is any cure of Paraplegic disease, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) whether facility to treat the said disease is available in Government/private hospitals including CGHS empanelled hospitals; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	  Yes, there is cure of Paraplegic disease, depending on the cause and severity of the disease. The patient management is done through drug and surgical intervention, depending upon the cause of paraplegic disease.(b) & (c):	 Yes, the facility to treat the  paraplegic disease is available in all Government and private hospitals, including CGHS empanelled hospitals. This facility is also available under Ayurveda and Siddha system of medicine.
