                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3922






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Loss of GDP due to Corrosion




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India is losing 2-4% of its GDP every year due to corrosion;(b) if so, the details thereof, industry-wise;(c) whether the Government has created a mechanism with the help of which this loss could be reduced;(d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) to (e):  The impact of corrosion on GDP is not directly measured. However, MOSPI prepares the estimates of consumption of fixed capital (CFC) which also includes the effect of wear and tear, on capital goods. The industry wise CFC and CFC as percentage of GDP, for 2018-19 are given at annex.                                            *****
