                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Death of Forest Guards




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to engage local communities  to preserve and conserve marine resources and  if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Union Government has allotted any budget for conservation of marine life; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	Coastal Communities like Biodiversity Management Committees (BMCs), Eco-Development Committee (EDC), Van Sanrakshan Samitis etc., are an integral part of preservation and conservation of the marine resources in the country.(b) and (c)	The total funds released to the State Governments during the financial year 2020-21 for conservation of marine life under the various schemes are Rs. 790.43 Lakh. The details are at Annexure.***
