                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

53






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Municipal Bonds




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is encouraging Municipal Corporations in the country to raise funds by issuing municipal bonds which are listed at stock exchanges in India as well as abroad; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the details of Municipal Corporations which have issued such bonds till date; and (d)	the funds generated through such listings, Statewise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (d):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.                             ***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 53 FOR ANSWER ON FEBRUARY 04, 2021 REGARDING “MUNICIPAL BONDS”:(a) & (b):  Yes, Sir. Cities/Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) have been encouraged to mobilize resources by issuing Municipal Bonds since 2018-19. Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs has been providing incentive to ULBs for issuance of Municipal Bonds at the rate of      ?13 crore per  ?100 crore worth of bonds issued with maximum incentive of ?26 crore per ULB.(c) & (d):    So far, ?3,690 crore has been raised through issuance of Municipal Bonds for upgrading urban infrastructure. As an incentive, ?207.33 crore has been released to 9 Cities/ULBs namely, Ahmedabad, Amravati, Bhopal, Hyderabad, Indore, Lucknow, Pune, Surat and Visakhapatnam for raising funds through issuance of Municipal Bonds.
