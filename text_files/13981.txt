                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2671






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Indira Gandhi Matritva Sahyog Yojana




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Indira Gandhi Matritva Sahyog Yojana focuses on the pre-natal and post-natal nutrition related problems of women in Madhya Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of financial assistance is provided to the pregnant and lactating mothers especially in rural and hilly areas; and(d)	whether the Government has any proposal to increase the financial assistance under this scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d)	The Indira Gandhi Matritva Sahyog Yojana (IGMSY) was implemented on pilot basis in two Districts of Madhya Pradesh, namely, Chhindwara and Sagar till 31.12.2016. The Scheme has been discontinued since 31.03.2017.******
