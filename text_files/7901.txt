                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1546






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Rail Coach Factories




Nishikant  Dubey













                                                    Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State-wise details of rail coach factories in the country including Gujarat and Jharkhand;(b) whether the Government proposes to launch a pilot project to convert some of the said factories into modern coach factories;(c) if so, the State-wise details thereof including Gujarat;(d) the funds proposed to be spent on the said task;(e) whether the Government has signed an agreement with IIT in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and(f) the targets set thereunder?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY( SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL )(a) to (f) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
