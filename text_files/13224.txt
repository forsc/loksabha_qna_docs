                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1944






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Merger of SSB and ITBP




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to merge Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) with Indo Tibetan Border Police (ITBP) and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has held any consultation with SSB and ITBP in this regard;(c) if so, whether the Government has sought suggestion from both the forces regarding reconstruction of the forces afresh;(d) if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government in this regard; and(e) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to provide latest and advanced weapons to the SSB and ITBP?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d): --An idea was mooted for considering the possibilities of merger of ITBP and SSB and informal consultations were held in this regard. However, no such proposal is under consideration, at present.-2/…-2-LS.US.Q. NO.1944 FOR 03.03.2020(e):Modernization of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) is a continuous process. SSB and ITBP have been allocated adequate funds to equip them with latest and advanced weapons.      Apart from above, the Government has approved a Modernization Plan -III for all CAPFs (including SSB and ITBP) for equipping CAPFs with latest and advanced weapons.*****
