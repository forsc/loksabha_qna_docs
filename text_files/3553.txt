                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






National Nutrition Mission




Chandra Prakash Choudhary













                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of districts covered under the National Nutrition Mission (NNM) at present;(b)	the number of people trained so far to strengthen implementation and operational preparedness under the said Mission; and(c)	the details of funds spent for the publicity of the NNM during the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	Under POSHAN Abhiyaan [erstwhile National Nutrition Mission (NNM)], all districts of the 36 States/UTs have been covered in a phased manner.(b):	Approximately 10.55 lakh field functionaries which include Anganwadi Workers (AWWs), Lady Supervisors (LSs), Child Development Project Officers (CDPOs), District Project Officers (DPOs) and State Project Monitoring Unit (SPMU) Officers have been trained on various Modules of Increment Learning Approach (ILA) and ICDS-CAS to strengthen implementation and operational preparedness under this Abhiyaan.(c):	During Financial Year 2017-18 and 2018-19, a sum of ? 61.92 Crore has been spent under the Head -“Information, Education and Communication (IEC), Advocacy and Jan Andolan” under POSHAN Abhiyaan*****
