                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3887






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Funds for Solar Rooftop Programme in  Andhra Pradesh




Kuruva Gorantla Madhav














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of funds allocated and spent by the State of Andhra Pradesh  under Grid connected solar rooftop programme during the last three years and the current year; district-wise;(b) whether the Government proposes to extend the programme beyond December, 2022; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) Under Grid Connected Solar Rooftop Programme, the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy has released Rs.79.65 crore as central financial support to the State of Andhra Pradesh during the period 1.4.2017 to 31.12.2020. Year-wise breakup is given below:2017-18 – Rs. 4.82 crore  2018-19 –  Rs. 44.95 crore2019-20 –  Rs. 2.07 crore                        2020-21 (till 31.12.2020) – Rs. 27.81 croreThe district-wise details of central financial assistance released to the rooftop solar beneficiaries by the State implementing agency of Andhra Pradesh during the last three years and the current year is given at Annexure. (b)&(c) Presently, the Ministry is implementing Grid Connected Rooftop Solar Programme Phase II, which is targeted for completion by December 2022.  The progress under the programme is satisfactory and therefore for the present no proposal of extension of the programme is under consideration. *****
