                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Slums in India




T.N. Prathapan













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy
                                                








                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                








                                                    Karti P. Chidambaram
                                                








                                                    S. Jothimani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of slums in India and their population, State-wise;(b)	 the list of the top hundred slums in India in terms of population size and their location, State-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has assessed and recorded informal and ‘unrecognised’ slum settlements; (d) 	if so, the details thereof including the total number of such unrecognised slums and total inhabitants in those slums; and (e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	: As per Census of India 2011, a total of 6.54 crore population is living in slums, State-wise slum population is at Annexure. As per the estimation done by National Sample Survey Office (NSSO) during 69th Round on Urban Slums in India, 2012, 33,510 slums exist in the urban areas of India.  (b) to (e): Since ‘Land’ and ‘Colonization’ are State subjects, therefore, data on slums is being maintained by States/Union Territories (UTs).                                              *****
