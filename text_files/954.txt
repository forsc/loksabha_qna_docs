764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Lok Adalats




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of various Lok Adalats organised during each of the last five years district-wise in the State of Gujarat;(b) the number of cases disposed of district-wise in Gujarat by these adalats during the said period;(c) whether the Government proposes to organize more such adalats on regular basis in future, district-wise;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to organize such Adalats more frequently to bring about the early disposal of pending cases?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF LAW & JUSTICE,  COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	 A statement showing various Lok Adalats organized, district-wise, in Gujarat during the last five years as per information received, is at Annexure-I.(b)	 The number of cases disposed of, district-wise, in Gujarat by Lok Adalats viz. Regular Lok Adalats, National Lok Adalats and Permanent Lok Adalats are shown in the statements at Annexure-II, III & IV respectively.(c)	to (e) The Government provides support to NALSA for organization of Lok Adalats at district level. Lok Adalat is a forum where the disputes / cases pending in the court of law or at pre-litigation stage are settled amicably. The State Legal Services Authorities have been issued guidelines / directions by the NALSA, vide National Legal Services Authority (Lok Adalat) Regulations, 2009, to organize Lok Adalats so that the pendency of cases could be reduced. The legal services institutions under the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 organize continuous Lok Adalats during the year. National Lok Adalats are also organized once in three months for all types of cases in the country during the year 2019. Permanent Lok Adalats for public utility services also exist, and in the said Lok Adalats matters pertaining to public utility services are taken up at pre-litigative state.
