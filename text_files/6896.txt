                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

609






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






New Trains for Tamil Nadu




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of new trains that are proposed to be introduced by the Government and also those which have been introduced in the State of Tamil Nadu in the last four years; and(b) the number of persons benefited, likely to be benefited therefrom?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b)  Indian Railways do not run train services on State- wise basis as Railway network straddles across State boundaries.  However, during the last four years (2015-2019), 34 train services have been introduced serving the various stations located in the State of Tamil Nadu on originating/terminating basis. Also state-wise statistics of number of passengers is not maintained. However, the year-wise details of number of passengers booked on Southern Railway which primarily serves the State of Tamil Nadu is as under:-Financial Year	Approximate Number of passengers booked (in millions)2015-16	          798.622016-17	          798.712017-18	          814.322018-19	          837.71 Further, introduction of new trains on Indian Railways is subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources etc. This is  a continuous and an ongoing process. *****
