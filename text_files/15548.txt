                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4201






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Airports in Odisha under UDAN Scheme




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the airports selected under UDAN scheme for domestic travel in Odisha;(b) the airports which are ready and to be developed for the UDAN scheme in Odisha;(c) whether there has been demands for connecting international destinations from Bhubaneswar, if so, the details thereof including destinations already connected and those which are in the pipeline; and(d) the status of the survey conducted with regard to the airstrip under UDAN scheme in Talcher of Angul district?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): Airports Authority of India (AAI), the implementing agency, has cancelled route/network awarded in first round of bidding under Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) connecting Jharsuguda, Jeypore, Rourkela and Utkela due to non-compliance of Selected Airline Operator (SAO) Agreement by SAO. Subsequently, the Implementing Agency awarded the proposals for operations of RCS flights from Jharsuguda and Rourkela in third round of bidding to the Selected Airline Operators. Jharsuguda airport has already been operationalized under UDAN scheme. Rourkela airport is a licensed Airport under category 2B. Government of Odisha has requested to provide the International Air Connectivity from Bhubaneshwar to Abu Dhabi, Bangkok & Singapore. Accordingly, Airports Authority of India (AAI), Implementing Agency  on behalf of Government of Odisha has invited e-Proposals for International Air Connectivity (IAC) from the interested bidders by 25.03.2020. Angul Airport of Odisha figures in the tentative list of the unserved airports/airstrips. However, Angul Airport belongs to private operator. No bid has been received till third round of bidding . In case, any valid bid is received for routes connecting Angul Airport in future round of bidding, same shall be considered as per RCS provisions, subject to consent of Airport operator.
