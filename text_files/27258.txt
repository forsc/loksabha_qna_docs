                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2824






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Concession in Vehicle Tax




Janardan Singh Sigriwal













                                                    Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposed concessions in motor vehicle tax for those who take their old vehicle for scrapping;(b) if so, the details of guidelines in this regard along with the progress made/response received as on date, vehicle and State-wise;(c) whether it is true that the proposed concession is one of the several incentives planned by the Government for encouragement of scrapping of old vehicles and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether any cases of violation of these guidelines have come to the notice of the Government; and(e) if so, the details thereof along with the penalties imposed, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c)  Government has issued GSR Notification 220 (E) dated 26.03.2021 for concession in  motor vehicle taxes, upto twenty five percent in case of non-transport vehicles and upto fifteen percent in case of transport vehicles,  for vehicle registered against submission of  a certificate regarding scrapping of the old vehicle.  (d) and (e)  No Sir.*****
