                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4454






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Foreign Funding received by NGOs




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) which received foreign funds during the last three years in Bihar;(b)	the quantum of foreign funds received by NGOs during the said, period, year-wise and State-wise;(c)	whether such NGOs are submitting audited records as required under the existing laws; and(d)	if not, the nature of action taken by the Government to ensure submission of audited records?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) :  Total number of NGOs which received foreign contribution during the last three years in Bihar is  as under :-Year	Total number of NGOs2017-18	2432018-19	2032019-20	45* * The above data is as per available Annual Returns (ARs) filed by the associations / NGOs as on 15.03.2021. However, the last date for filing the ARs for the FY 2019-20 has been extended upto 30/06/2021.  -2-L.S.US.Q NO. 4454 FOR 23.03.2021(b) to (d): The Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act, 2010 and rules made there-under provide that all FCRA NGOs/Associations have to file audited Annual Returns upto a fixed deadline.  The non-compliant NGOs are issued notices and questionnaires.  Further action like audit & inspection of defaulting NGOs is also carried out.  This may lead to suspension and cancellation of their Registration Certificates.  Year-wise and state-wise details of foreign contribution received by NGOs  is available in the public domain on the web portal  www.fcraonline.nic.in*****
