                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






PM-KISAN Scheme Transactions




Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel













                                                    Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                








                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of Pradhan Mantri Kisan SammanNidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana beneficiaries and transactions that have taken place since its launch till November, 2021, month-wise and State-wise;(b)	the total number of PM-KISAN scheme transactions which have failed since its launch till November, 2021, month-wise and State-wise;(c)	whether the Government has identified the reasons for failed transactions;(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(e)	the steps being taken to ensure that beneficiaries receive their installments in situations where transactions have failed; and(f)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	As of 25.11.2021, more than 11.60 crore beneficiaries have been given the benefits of PM-KISAN Scheme. Under PM-KISAN Scheme the benefits are not transferrable every month. The benefits are transferred in three equal installments of Rs.2000/- each in every four-months.  The total number of transactions done under PM-KISAN Scheme has been 80,27,44,674 of which the number of failed transaction is less than 1%. The cumulative number of PM-KISAN Scheme transactions which have failed since 1st February, 2019 till 31st October, 2021 is 69,000,58 out of which 31,86,921 transactions were successfully reprocessed. The State-wise details of number of total transactions, total failed transactions and total successfully re-processed transactions under PM-KISAN Schemehave been placed at Annexure-I.(c)& (d):	Various reasons have been identified by the Union Government for the transactions failure under PM-KISAN Scheme such as Account closed/Transferred, Invalid IFSC, Account Inactive, Account Dormant, Amount exceeds limit set on Account by Bank for Credit/Debit per transaction, Account Holder Expired, Account Blocked or Frozen, Inactive Aadhar, Network failure, technical glitches etc.  (e):	To tackle the issues of transaction failures and re-process the payment to such registered farmers’ families, a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) has been developed and issued to all States/UTs for necessary action at their end.  In cases where the corrective measures are to be taken by States/UTs, such transaction failure records are opened to respective State/UT for correction under the “Correction Module” tab of PM-KISAN portal. Thereafter, all the transaction failure records are reprocessed for the payment of the respective installment under PM-KISAN Scheme. The Department through weekly video conferencing take up the matter with State/UT Governments to expedite the resolution of the issue and reprocess the failed transactions for the payments of the respective installments under PM-KISAN Scheme. (f):	Does not arise in view of above.
