                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

838






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Tender Process for Road Projects




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has reduced security deposits fees in the tender process for road projects and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government is planning to blacklist all such companies which take the tender by reducing tender amount and run away on agreement;(c) whether the Government is aware that most of the companies are doing this and recently a case came to notice in district Bhagalpur wherein the company took the tender and ran away at the time of agreement;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government proposes to blacklist all such companies for some time so that the road and development work is not hindered and the project can be completed on time; and(f) if so, the details of companies against which the Government has taken action till now?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) & (b) In compliance to General Finance Rule (GFR) 170(iii), only Bid Security Declaration is required in place of  Bid Security. The bidder has to furnish a Bid securing declaration, accepting that; if the bidder withdraw or modify its bid during the period of validity or if the bidder is awarded the contract and fail to sign the contract or to submit a performance security before the deadline defined in the bid documents, the bidder shall be suspended for further participation in the bidding process of Ministry for a period of one year from the bid due date.(c) & (d) yes Sir, the details are as below:Sr. No.	Name of the Work	Agency	NH No.&State	Name of the Bidder / Firm	Date of Debarment /Suspension of the firm from Bidding1.		Rehabilitation & Widening to 2L/ 2L+PS of Munger-Sultanganj-Bhagalpur section 	State PWD	NH-80Bihar	M/s A. G. Construction	26.07.20212.		Rehabilitation & widening to 2L/ 2L+PS of Bhagalpur- Kahalgaon- Mirzachowki section 	State PWD	NH-80 Bihar	M/s A. G. Construction	23.07.2021(e) & (f) There is provision in the bid document for debarment/suspension of such defaulter bidders/companies for a period of one year from the bid due date. Accordingly, action has been taken against defaulters. The details of such bidders/companies are placed at Annexure.
