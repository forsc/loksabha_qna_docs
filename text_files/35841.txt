                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2608






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Demand for Electricity




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the demand for electricity is increasing continuously in the country resulting in its acute shortage;(b) 	if so, the details of demand and supply of electricity during the last three years and the current year, State-wise;(c) 	the details of power generation from various sources during the said period, State-wise; and(d) 	the details of the infrastructural reforms undertaken to improve the power situation along with the achievements made thereunder?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) :	There is adequate availability of electricity in the country to meet the demand.  It is a fact that the demand for electricity is increasing continuously in the country, except during the period of Covid-19 pandemic.  The increasing demand of power in the country is being met with commensurate increase in power generation. A generation capacity of 15,978.84 MW has been added during the year 2021-22 (up to 28.02.2022) which includes 3,825 MW of Thermal, 213 MW of Hydro (above 25 MW capacity) and 11,940.84 MW from other renewable energy. As on 28th February, 2022, the installed generation capacity is around 395.6 GW which is sufficient to meet the demand of electricity in the country.  The peak demand experienced during the current year was only 203 GW. The State-wise details of Energy Requirement and Energy Supplied during the last three years and the current year i.e. 2021-22 (period April, 2021 to February, 2022), are given at Annexure-I.(c) :		The State-wise details of Energy Generation (Thermal, Hydro & Nuclear) and generation from other Renewable Energy Sources in the country during the last three years and current year 2021-22 (upto Jan./Feb.,2022) are given at Annexure-II and Annexure-III respectively. (d) :		The details of generation capacity addition during the last three years and the current year are given at Annexure-IV. The details of the measures to improve the power production and improve the overall power situation are given at Annexure-V.***********
