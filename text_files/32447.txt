                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3880






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Implementation of NFSM




T.R.V.S. Ramesh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the number of components being covered under the National Food Security Mission (NFSM) and the details of the number of States/districts in which the NFSM is being implemented;(b)	whether the Government is planning to include Tamil Nadu under NFSM to promote pulses and oilseeds cultivation and if so, the details thereof, district-wise; and(c)	the target likely to be set in this regard along with the funds likely to be sanctioned for this purpose, district-wise in Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): National Food Security Mission (NFSM) covers the component cropsviz;rice, wheat, pulses, coarse cereals (maize & barley), nutri cereals (jowar, bajra, ragi& small millets), commercial crops (cotton, jute & sugarcane) andoilseeds. NFSM-Rice is being implemented in 193 districts of 24 states & UT of Jammu & Kashmir, NFSM-Wheat in 124 districts of 10 states & UTs of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh, NFSM-Pulses in 644 districts of 28 states &UTs ofJammu & Kashmir and Ladakh and NFSM-Coarse Cereals in 269 districts of 26 states &UTs ofJammu & Kashmir and Ladakh. The NFSM-Nutri-Cereals is being implemented in 212 districts of 14 states. NFSM-Cotton is implemented in 15 states, jute in 9 states and sugarcane in 13 states of the country. The NFSM-Oilseeds is implemented in 27 states and 3 UTs of the country.(b) & (c): Tamil Nadu is already covered under NFSM-Pulses & Oilseeds. The NFSM-Pulses is implemented in 36 districts and NFSM-Oilseeds in 35 districts of Tamil Nadu. Further, in order to utilize fallow lands after rice in kharif season, Targeting Rice Fallow Area (TRFA)-Pulsesis implemented in 5 districts and TRFA-Oilseeds in 3 districts of Tamil Nadu. The details of districts covered under NFSM-Pulses and NFSM-Oilseeds along with district-wise financial targets during 2021-22 is Annexed.
