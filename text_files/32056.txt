                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3650






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






JNV in Manipur




Lorho S. Pfoze














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that students from outside the district have been admitted in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV), Churachandpur, Manipur in violation of the norms and whether any complaints have been received by the Government in this regard;(b) if so, the action taken by the Government against the persons responsible for this and if no action has been taken till date, the reasons therefor; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to prevent such instances in future?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (c) : As per the prospectus for Jawahar Navodaya Vidayalaya Selection Test (JNVST) for admission to Class-VI during 2021-22, the candidate should have studied in Class-V for the whole of the academic session 2020-21 in a Government/Government aided or other recognized schools or ‘B’ certificate competency course of National Institute of Open Schooling in the same district where he/she was seeking admission. Admission of provisionally selected students through JNVST in Class VI is done only after verification of documents, as per information submitted by students at the time of filling application, as per the norms of Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS).As per information received from NVS, as on date, 41 students out of 80 are admitted to Class-VI in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) Churachandpur after verifying documents. Complaints regarding admission of students from outside Churachandpur district in JNV, Churachandpur have been received. However, as informed by NVS, no student from outside Churachandpur district has been admitted to Class VI in JNV, Churachandpur. Further, it has also been made mandatory to get the certificates to be uploaded to JNVST portal duly authenticated by the Headmaster of the Vidyalaya where the applicant is studying in Class V for next selection test.*****
