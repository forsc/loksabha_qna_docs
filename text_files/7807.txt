                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1446






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Spot e-Auction of Coal




Kotagiri Sridhar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total quantity of coal allocated by the Government as on date in the spot e-auction of coal, special forward e-auction for power sector and also the exclusive e-auction for non-power sector; and (b) the total quantity of coal imported in the country in the current financial year as on date?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a): In 2019-20 (up to 31.10.2019), the total quantity of 30.997 Million Tonne (MT) raw coal has been allocated by Coal India Limited in spot e-auction, special forward e-auction for power sector and exclusive e-auction for non-power sector. Its break up is as under: (Figures in MT)Auction	Spote-auction	Special Forwarde-auction for power	Exclusivee-auction for non-power	Special Spote-auctionQuantity allocated 	12.595	12.904	4.838	0.660 (b): In 2019-20 (up to 31.10.2019), the total quantity of coal imported was 145.58 MT (provisional).  *****
