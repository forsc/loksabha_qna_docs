                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Delhi




Gautam Gambhir














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of proposed or under construction Kendriya Vidyalayas in the countryincluding Delhi and if so, the details thereof;(b) the total number of Kendriya Vidyalayas in Delhi, location-wise along with the studentintake capacity of the respective Kendriya Vidyalayas;(c) whether the Government is aware of the delay in completion of proposed Vidyalayas inEast Delhi Constituency and if so, the reasons for such delay ;(d) the time by which it is likely to be completed; and(e) the details about the nodal officer, budget allocation and the other modalities in thisregard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has informed that as on date 101 KVs are underconstruction and 98 KVs are under planning. Details are at Annexure – I.(b) The location-wise details and intake capacity of the 46 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs)located in Delhi are at Annexure-II.(c) & (d) KVS has informed that the construction of the school building of KV Shahdarawhich is located in the North East Delhi constituency was given to Central Public WorksDepartment (CPWD) on 13.4.2018, but due to litigation on the land allotted for this KV, thework was stopped. Constituent upon disposal of the court case, the construction work hasresumed with the stipulated date of completion as date of completion of June, 2020 subjectto availability of funds.(e) The concerned officer is Executive Engineer, CPWD, All India Institute of MedicalScience Project Division, Ayurvigyan Nagar, August Kranti Marg, New Delhi. The amount ofAdministrative Approval & Expenditure sanction is Rs. 1460.12 Lakh and the work is to beexecuted as “Deposit Work” from concept to commissioning basis.******
