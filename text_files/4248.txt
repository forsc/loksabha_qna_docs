                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3876






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Premium Trains by Private Sector




Bhola Singh













                                                    Sakshi Ji Swami Maharaj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Railway Board has formulated any scheme for introduction of two premium trains by the private sector under its 100 day action plan and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the name of the routes on which these trains are likely to be introduced along with the reaction of the private companies thereto;(c)	whether the private companies have to give one third share of their profit to the Railways and if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the details of the facilities likely to be provided to the passengers in these trains?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d) With a view to providing world class services to travelling passengers, Indian Railways are examining various options, including private participation in the operation of passenger carrying trains. However, in this regard, the details and  modalities have not been formulated, so far. *****
