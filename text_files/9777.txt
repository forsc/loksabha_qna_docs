                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3375






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Revamping of Archaeological Survey of India




S. Jagathrakshakan













                                                    Gaurav Gogoi
                                                








                                                    M.K. Vishnu Prasad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Ministry has prepared Five Year Plan to revamp Archaeological Survey of India, setting up of museums etc;b)	if so, the details thereof and the proposed budgetary expenditure, year-wise;c)	the details of financial allocation and other supports provided to continue with the excavation work and archaeological studies at Keezhadi, Tamil Nadu where rich deposits were found recently; andd)	whether any funds specifically provided for excavation in Keeladi, Tamil Nadu where rich deposits were found and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	No.(b)	Does not arise. (c)	 & (d) The   Archaeological Survey of India conducted excavations at Keeladi for four field seasons (2014-15, 2015-16, 2016-17 & 2017-18). The details of expenditure incurred by ASI for excavation at Keeladi are as under:Financial Year 	Amount (in     RS.)2014-15      7,70,010/-2015-16      48,50,798/-2016-17      35,50,000/-2017-18      22,50,000/-
