                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

363






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Reopening of Oil Depots




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of oil depots shut down by the Government during thelast three years, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government proposes to reopen these oil depots,particularly the closed oil depots in Hanumangarh District inRajasthan; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c) A statement is laid on the Table of the HouseSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 363 BY SHRI NIHAL CHAND TO BE ANSWERED ON 22ND MARCH, 2021 REGARDING ‘REOPENING OF OIL DEPOTS’.(a)	There are 10 (ten) number of Oil Depot Shut down during last three years (April, 2018- March, 2021). The details of the same is as under: OMC	State	Numbers of Depots shut down HPC	Tamil Nadu	1	Maharashtra	1	Uttar Pradesh	1	Gujarat	1IOC	West Bengal	1	Assam	1	Himachal Pradesh	2	Andhra Pradesh	1	Tamil Nadu	1BPC	Nil(b) & (c)  There is no proposal to reopen Depots which are already closed. Some of the Depots have been relocated, the detail is annexed. IOCL and HPCL Depots at Hanumangarh were non-OISD (Oil Industry Safety Directorate) compliant and closed during 2010-11. In consideration of limited volumes in the district of Hanumangarh and Sri Ganganagar, development of New Depot at Hanumangarh was not commercially viable. The markets of Hanumangarh & Sri Ganganagar are being fed from the Pipeline fed State of the Art Terminals at Jaipur & Jodhpur.*****
