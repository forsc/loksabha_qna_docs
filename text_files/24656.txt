                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

507






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Loan Schemes for MSME Sector




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government extends the various loan schemes for the MSME sector and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the number of applications for loan received from Odisha during the last five years, district-wise; and (c)	the details of number of applications granted loan along with the total amount?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) to (c):	 Sir, Ministry of MSME has been implementing a major credit-linked subsidy programme named Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) being implemented through Banks since 2008-09 with Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) as nodal agency at the national level for generating employment in the Country by setting up of micro-enterprises in the non-farm sector. Under PMEGP, general category beneficiaries can avail of margin money subsidy of 25% of the project cost in rural areas and 15% in urban areas. The district wise applications received under the scheme and the  district wise list of  units set up and margin money released under PMEGP in the state of Odisha are given at Annexure-I and Annexure- II.  Ministry of MSME also implements Credit Guarantee Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises which is mandated to facilitate credit guarantee support for collateral free / third-party guarantee-free loans to the Micro and Small enterprises (MSEs), especially in the absence of collateral. It facilitates access to finance for un-served and under-served segments of society and geographies.  Under this scheme, guarantee is extended by Credit Guarantee Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) to the proposals brought to it by the Banks/Member Lending Institutions and no eligible proposal is denied.  District wise list of beneficiaries of the Credit Guarantee Scheme in the State of Odisha is at Annexure-III.
