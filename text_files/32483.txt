                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

10






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Trade Infrastructure of Export Scheme




 Chinta Anuradha













                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status and progress of the Trade Infrastructure for Export Scheme (TIES); and(b)	the details of projects sanctioned in the State of Andhra Pradesh under this scheme and the funds allocated for these projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)  (a)	& (b):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT  REFERRED  TO  IN  REPLY  TO  PARTS  (a) & (b)  OF  LOK  SABHA  STARRED  QUESTION  NO. 10   FOR  ANSWER  ON  02nd FEBRUARY, 2022  REGARDING   “ TRADE INFRASTRUCTURE OF EXPORT SCHEME”.(a)	The Department of Commerce, Government of India is implementing a scheme namely ‘Trade Infrastructure for Export Scheme (TIES)’ w.e.f. FY 2017-18 with the objective of assisting Central and State Government agencies in the creation of appropriate infrastructure for growth of exports. Under the scheme, financial assistance in the form of grant-in-aid is provided to Central/State Government owned agencies for setting up or up-grading export infrastructure. The Scheme guidelines are available at https://commerce.gov.in/trade-promotion/trade-promotion-assistance/.The scheme has been further extended for the period 2021-22 to 2025-26 with a total outlay of Rs 360 crore. A total of 51 projects having grant component of approx. Rs 548 crore, have been sanctioned under TIES till the end of January, 2022. Out of these projects, 13 projects are already completed.(b)	The details of projects sanctioned in the State of Andhra Pradesh is as follows.  (Amount in Rs. Cr.)S.
