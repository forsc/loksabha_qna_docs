                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4041






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Second Airport at Chennai




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal to develop second airport near Chennai and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether the Ministry proposes to set up the airport at Cheyyar which is about 80 kms. from Chennai and is ideally suited for the airport and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Central Government has requested State Government of Tamil Nadu to identify suitable land for second airport for Chennai, which is likely to reach saturation within next few years. The State Government of Tamil Nadu has informed that they intend to establish a new Greenfield Airport near Chennai. However, no site for the airport has been finalized so far.;(b): No Sir, Ministry of Civil Aviation has not received any proposal as per the Greenfield Airport Policy for development of Greenfield airport at Cheyyar.
