                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4575






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Pending Funds under MGNREGS




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of pending funds under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) to the State Governments by the Union Government, State-wise, including Andhra Pradesh;(b) 	the time by which the Union Government is planning to release all the pending funds to the State Governments under the MGNREGS, State-wise;(c) 	the details of work done under the MGNREGS in Fisheries and Aquaculture and in Ponds and Water Bodies Development, State-wise, including Andhra Pradesh;(d) 	whether the demand for work has increased under the MGNREGS after March, 2020, if so, the percentage of increase in demand for work under the said scheme after March 2020, State-wise, including Andhra Pradesh; and(e) 	whether the Government has made arrangements for additional funds to meet the increase in demand due to rising unemployment on account of the COVID-19 pandemic and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)&(b):	Mahatma Gandhi NREG Scheme is a demand driven wage employment programme which provides at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.  The release of fund to States/UTs (including Andhra Pradesh) is a continuous process and the Central Government is committed to making funds available as per demand of work on the ground. The detail of dues to be released to States/UTs as on 18.03.2021 is given at Annexure-I. (c):	During the current financial year 2020-21 (as on 17.03.2021), States/UTs-wise (including Andhra Pradesh) details of works taken up under Water Conservation, Water Harvesting and Fisheries under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is given at Annexure-II. (d): During FY 2020-21, more than 369 crore person-days have been generated as on 18.03.2021 which is approximately 40% more than the employment generated during the same period in FY 2019-20. During FY 2020-21, more than 1.77 crore new job cards have been issued so far which is 154% more than the total number of new job cards issued during FY 2019-20. During the current FY 2020-21, more than 10.98 Crore persons have been provided employment so far which is 39% more than the persons provided employment during FY 2019-20 under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS.State/UT wise (including Andhra Pradesh) number of households who demanded work under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in the FY 2019 and FY 2020-21 is given at Annexure III.(e):	Financial allocation of Rs.61,500 crore at Budget Estimate has been enhanced  to Rs. 1,11,500 crore at Revised Estimate in the current financial year 2020-21 under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. The Ministry has released Rs. 99001 crore as on 18.03.2021 to States/UTs for the implementation of the Scheme. Further Central Government is committed to making funds available to States/UTs for the implementation of the Scheme. The Ministry seeks additional fund for Mahatma Gandhi NREGS as and when required for meeting the demand for work on the ground.******Annexure-I referred in reply to parts (a)&(b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.4575 dated 23.03.2021Financial Year 2020-2021 (as on 18th March, 2021)
