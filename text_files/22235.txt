                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Disinvestment of PSUs




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government is taking steps to similarly incentivise States to take up   disinvestment of their Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) and work out an incentive package of Central Funds for States using a Special Purpose Vehicle; (b)   if so, the response received from each State and the present status thereof; (c)  whether the Government is taking steps to ensure timely completion of closure of sick or loss making CPSEs and introducing a revised mechanism that will ensure timely closure of such units; and (d)  if so, the details thereof and the target set/ achieved so far? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARJUN RAM MEGHWAL)  (a) & (b):  Hon’ble Union Finance Minister in the Budget Speech 2021-22 has announced a new policy for strategic disinvestment of public sector enterprises. The policy provides a road map for disinvestment in strategic and non-strategic sectors. Budget announcement also contains proposal to incentivise States to take up disinvestment of their public sector companies.  (c) & (d): Introducing a revised mechanism ensuring timely closure of sick and loss-making units has also been announced by the Hon’ble Union Finance Minister in the Budget Speech 2021-22. Department of Public Enterprises (DPE) being the nodal department will formulate the revised closure mechanism of sick and loss-making CPSEs and notify the revised mechanism on completion of due process. ***
