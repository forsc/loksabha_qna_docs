                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1344






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Khadi Uniform for Kendriya Vidyalayas




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to introduce Khadi dress code for thestudents of all the Kendriya Vidyalayas of the country to promote Khadi andVillage Industry;(b) if so, the quantum of beneficial impacts likely to have on Khadi and VillageIndustry and the impact likely to have on students’ approach towards Khadi;and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) There is no proposal under consideration at present to introduce Khadidress code for the students of all the Kendriya Vidyalayas of the country. TheCentral Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has recently issued a circularproposing that affiliated schools across the country may observe ‘Khadi WearingDay’, on a voluntary basis, once in a week/fortnight/month.
