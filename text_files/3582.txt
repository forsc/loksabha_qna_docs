                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3329






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Afforestation Programmes




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing any special scheme to promote afforestation programmes in private sector; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the amount being provided by the Government under the said scheme, State/ UT-wise; (c)	if not, the time by which the said scheme to promote afforestation in private sector is likely to be started; (d)	whether the Government is implementing any special scheme to promote bamboo plantation; and (e)	if so, the details thereof along with the amount being given to States by the Government for the purpose during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	to (c) The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change does not implement any special scheme for promotion of plantation drive in the private sector at present. Tree plantation/afforestation being the cross-sectoral effort, is however, done by various departments, Non-Government Organizations, civil society, corporate bodies etc.(d)	&(e) As informed by the Ministry of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare, the restructured National Bamboo Mission (NBM) was launched for addressing the complete value chain and holistic development of bamboo sector and to establish an effective linkage between producers (farmers) and industry for realizing better economic returns to the growers. The scheme is being implemented in non-forest, Government and private farmer’s field and in States where it has social, commercial and economic advantage.  An amount of Rs.150.03crorehas been released to States/Agencies during the financial year 2018-19, the details of which are enclosed in annexure.***
