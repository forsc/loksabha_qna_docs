                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1096






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Misappropriation of Funds under PMJAY




Sukhbir Singh Badal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the State Government of Punjab has launched a parallel scheme to PMJAY having similar features and the benefits of PMJAY are not being extended to the beneficiaries as a result thereof, if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(b) whether any misappropriation of funds has come to the notice of the Government relating to PMJAY in Punjab to any other scheme thereof;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the corrective action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is being implemented in the State of Punjab in alliance with the State Scheme in the form of Ayushman Bharat- Sarbat Sehat Bima Yojana.  This scheme covers 45.89 lakh beneficiary families including 14.86 lakh AB-PMJAY beneficiary families identified as per Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) database. All the beneficiaries are covered for health cover up to Rs. 5 lakh per family per annum. All the 1393 Health Benefit Packages defined under Ayushman Bharat – PMJAY are available under the scheme. Also, the benefit of national portability is being provided to AB-PMJAY entitled beneficiaries.Cost in respect of AB-PMJAY beneficiaries is shared between Government of India and the State Government in the ratio of 60:40. For non-SECC beneficiaries, the cost is being borne by the State Government.(b):	No.(c) & (d):	In view of the answer to part (b) above, question doesn’t arise.
