                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3046






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Collection of GST, IGST and SGST in Bihar




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total amount collected from the GST, IGST and SGST in Bihar so far;(b) whether the said amount meets the targets fixed by the Union Government; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor along with the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) The total gross CGST, Domestic IGST and SGST collected in Bihar as on 28th Feb, 2021 is as under:(In Rs. Crore)	CGST	IGST	SGST2017-18	1184.02	691.34	2362.312018-19	2595.60	1364.87	4872.122019-20	3255.02	1479.97	5842.152020-21	2861.26	1231.37	4788.68Total	9895.90	4767.55	17865.26(b) Union Government does not fix GST targets to States. (c) Does not arise in view of (b) above.*****
