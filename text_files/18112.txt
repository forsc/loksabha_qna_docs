                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1970






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






National Youth Award




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has fixed norms for National Youth  Award; and (b) the facilities being provided by the Government to the National Youth Award winners?  






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)  (a)  Yes, the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports confers National Youth Award for identifiable excellent work for youth in different fields of development activities and social service such as health, research and innovation, culture, promotion of human rights, art and literature, tourism, traditional medicine, active citizenship, community service, sports and academic excellence & smart learning. 25 awards and 10 awards are given in individual and organization category respectively.  (b) Award to individuals comprises a medal, a certificate and an award money of Rs. 1,00,000/- whereas the Award to voluntary youth organizations comprises a medal, a certificate and an award money of Rs. 3,00,000/-.  *****
