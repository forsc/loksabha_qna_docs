                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4689






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Demand of Petroleum Products




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is facing any difficulty in striking out a coordination between the present demand of petroleum products especially petrol in the country and the prevailing situation at international level in this regard; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Required steps have been taken to ensure that the domestic demand of petroleum products especially petrol is fully met considering the present demand in the country as well as prevailing situation at international level.* * * * * *
