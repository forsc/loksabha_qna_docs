                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1889






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Khadi Vikas Yojana




 Pratap Chandra Sarangi













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Arjunlal Meena
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    Rajbahadur Singh
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Agrawal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds allocated and utilized under the Khadi Vikas Yojana, State and district-wise including Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Odisha and Madhya Pradesh; (b) 	the details of beneficiaries at present and targeted under the yojana; (c) 	whether the Government is taking the steps to ensure tracking of income generated and registrations under the yojana; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	the details of employment generated under the yojana, State-wise; (f) 	whether the Government is considering revising rate of interest due to impact of Covid; and (g) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a): 	Details of State-wise funds allocated and utilized under the Khadi Vikas Yojana (KVY), including Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Odisha and Madhya Pradesh are placed at Annexure-I. (b):	Details of beneficiaries under KVY during the year 2020-21 are placed at Annexure-II. Weavers, Spinners, Artisans, etc. are specifically targeted under the KVY scheme.  (c) & (d): 	No Sir.  However, an online application system has been made operational for disbursement of Market Development Assistance (MDA) and ISEC scheme to Khadi Institutions and Artisans.(e):	State-wise details of employment generated under the KVY during the year 2020-21 is placed at Annexure-II.(f) & (g): 	No such proposal is under consideration of the Government regarding revising rate of interest due to impact of COVID.
