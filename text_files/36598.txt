                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Road Projects in Himachal Pradesh




Pratibha Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated and utilised for construction of National Highways (NHs) in the State of Himachal Pradesh during each of the last three years and the current year;(b) the details and the present status of the road projects sanctioned during the said period in the State;(c) whether the length of roads per kilometer constructed in the State is less than the average at National level; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to bridge that gap?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 The funds allocated and utilised for construction of National Highways (NHs) in the State of Himachal Pradesh during each of the last three years and the current year are as follows:-(Amount in Rs. crore)Year	Allocation	Expenditure2018-19	2098.41	2076.062019-20	1207.74	1146.332020-21	1922.11	1921.922021-22 (Till February, 2022)	2834.42	2782.34 (b)	Total 60 works costing Rs. 17,931 crores have been sanctioned during the last three years and the current year. Out of these, 8 works have been completed and remaining are at various stages of construction/ award.(c) 	During the current Financial Year (FY),140 km of National Highways (NHs) have been constructed till 17th March, 2022 against the target of 122 km in the State of Himachal Pradesh.   (d)	Does not arise.*****
