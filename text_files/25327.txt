                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1171






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






e-NAM in Gujarat




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of farmers in Gujarat linked with e-National Agriculture Market (e-	NAM) Scheme/online Mandi so far;(b) 	whether e-National Agriculture Market/online Mandi is providing any 	assistance to the farmers in selling agricultural products in Gujarat;(c) 	if so, the details of the number of farmers assisted during the last three years 	including the type of assistance provided, year-wise;(d) 	whether any complaint has been received regarding the functioning of the 	scheme in Gujarat during the last three years;(e) 	if so, the details thereof; and(f) 	the outcome of the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	 National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) is a virtual platform integrating physical wholesale mandis/ markets of different States/ Union Territories (UTs) to facilitate online trading of agricultural commodities through transparent price discovery method to enable farmers to realize better remunerative prices for their produce. 	Various facilities/ assistance is provided to the farmers in selling agricultural produce in e-NAM mandis in the country including that in Gujarat, some of which are as follows: (i)	Farmers are free to register without any cost on e-NAM platform.(ii)	Farmer can access the prevailing commodity prices information on e-NAM mobile app. prior to even going to the mandi.(iii)	The infrastructure created for e-NAM is used to provide free of cost quality assaying facilities to farmers for trading of their agriculture produce for facilitating quality based price discovery. (iv)	e-NAM mobile app facilitates direct online payment by buyers to the bank account of the farmers in a transparent, speedy and safe manner. (v)	Under the scheme, various training & awareness programmes are conducted free of cost for farmers for successful implementation of e-NAM.  	As on 15th July, 2021, a total of 8,68,601 farmers have been registered in Gujarat State to avail various facilities and assistance under National Agriculture Market (e-NAM). (d) to (f)  As informed by Gujarat State Agricultural Marketing Board, no complaint have been registered in last three years.*****
