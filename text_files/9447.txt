                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2804






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Disinvestment of Air India




Mimi Chakraborty














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether  the Government  has received any proposal from foreign entities on disinvestment of Air India;(b) if so, the details thereof and the objections of the foreign part, if any;(c) whether TATA group has also showed interest on Air India and if so, the details along with proposals thereof; and(d) whether the Government wants to run the PSU professionally towards profit making industry and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): No sir. ;(d): The Government is committed to the disinvestment of Air India. In order to effectively prepare Air India for disinvestment, the Government prepared a plan to bring operational and financial efficiency in Air India. This Plan focuses on the financial and operational efficiencies so that substantial increase in revenue or cost saving could be achieved. Air India Specific Alternative Mechanism (AISAM) has been reconstituted to guide the process of strategic disinvestment from time to time.
