                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






PMGSY in Kerala




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of roads identified for development under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Kozhikode, in particular and other districts of Kerala, in general;(b) 	the total funds sanctioned, spent and un-spent under the scheme during the last three years particularly in Kozhikode district and in the State of Kerala, in general; and(c) 	the manner in which the Government proposes to speed up the road connectivity?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to Parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *138  due for answer on 11.02.2020(a): The State of Kerala has been sanctioned its full entitlement under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)-I and PMGSY-II. Details of district-wise roads sanctioned and completed under PMGSY-I and PMGSY-II including Kozhikode district of Kerala is at Annexure. Under PMGSY-III, 1425 km road length has been allocated to the State of Kerala and the state has initiated planning activities for road identification. (b): The total funds sanctioned, utilized and unspent under PMGSY during the last three years in the State of Kerala and Kozhikode district are as under:-                                                                       (In Rs Crore)Year	Fund sanctioned           Fund            Fund               Fund             Fund Utilized         to State            Utilized (State)   un-spent (State)     sanctioned to    by Kozhikode*       (Central Share &                                              Kozhikode*          State Share)  2016-17	    305.67	       267.43	         38.24	               18.98	          18.982017-18	    280.19	       206.09	         74.10	               13.30	          13.302018-19	    162.92	       225.16	           0	               10.28	          10.28* Funds are sanctioned to each district (PIUs) based on their actual fund request.(c): The progress of implementation of rural roads under PMGSY is regularly monitored by way of Regional Review Meetings (RRMs), Performance Review Committee (PRC) Meetings, Pre-Empowered/Empowered Committee Meetings, Video Conference with the State. At District level, the District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (Disha) headed by a Member of Parliament (LS) monitors the implementation of various schemes of Government of India including PMGSY. In addition to this, special review meetings are also held by Secretary/ Additional Secretary/ Joint Secretary, Ministry of Rural Development with Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries of the States. *****
