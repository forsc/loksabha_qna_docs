                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Policy for Strategic Disinvestment




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                








                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government under the Atmanirbhar Package  has come out with a policy of strategic disinvestment of public sector enterprises and provides a clear roadmap for disinvestment in all non-strategic and strategic sectors by keeping four areas that are strategic where bare minimum CPSEs will be maintained and rest privatized and all the remaining sectors/all CPSEs will be privatized; and(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) and (b) Yes Sir.  The Government has announced the New Public Sector Enterprise (“PSE”) Policy for Atmanirbhar Bharat.  The new PSE Policy is available on the website of DIPAM at www.dipam.gov.in ******
