                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3653






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Details of World Heritage Sites




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the State-wise details of the World Heritage Sites;b)	whether any steps have been taken for the development and proper preservation of those sites; andc)	if so, the amount spent in the development and preservation of such sites during the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) At present, India has 38 World Heritage Sites. The details of World Heritage Sites are placed at Annexure-I.(b) & (c) The world heritage sites are well conserved and in good shape. These sites are conserved as per ASI’s Conservation Policy.
