                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5455






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Sports Infrastructure in Assam




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the existing infrastructure for sports is in very bad shape in Nowgong in Assam;(b) if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken to revive and maintain these facilities;(c) whether the Government proposes to start/open up a full-fledged sports academy to promote young talents inNowgong; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }*****(a) and (b) ‘Sports’ being a State subject the responsibility for developing sports infrastructure, including in Nowgong in Assam, rests with the State Government. Central Government supplements efforts of the State Government by providing expert training facilities in Sports Authority of India (SAI) Centres, supports to National Sports Federations (NSFs) and support under Khelo India Scheme. SAI infrastructure does not exist at Nowgong. Information relating to sports training facilities provided by this Ministry in Assam is given in Annexure – I.(c) and (d) There is no proposal under consideration of this Ministry to start/open up a full-fledged sports academy in Nowgong. Information of infrastructure projects sanctioned under Khelo India Scheme for Assam is at Annexure – II.*****
