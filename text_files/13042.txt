                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2000






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Household registered under MGNREGS




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of households registered for work under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) since 2014, year and State-wise including in Tamil Nadu;(b) the number of households provided work since 2014, year and State-wise including in Tamil Nadu;(c) whether the Government has ensured timely payment of wages under this scheme and if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the details of instances of wages being paid late? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a):      As per information uploaded by the States/UTs in the Management Information System (MIS) of MGNREGS, the State/UT-wise details of number of Registered households under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) including  Tamil Nadu, since 2014-15 to 2019-20 (as on 27.02.2020) is at Annexure-I. (b):       The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme.  As per information uploaded by the States/UTs in the Management Information System (MIS), the State/UT-wise details of households provided employment under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) including  Tamil Nadu, since 2014-15 to 2019-20 (as on 27.02.2020) is at Annexure-II. (c)&(d):          The Ministry has issued Standard Operating Procedure (SoP) on timely payment process for direct payments into the account of beneficiaries. States/ UTs have been advised to generate pay orders in time. This has resulted in considerable improvement in the status of timely generation of pay order leading to improvement in actual time taken to credit wages in the workers account. During the current Financial Year 2019-20 (as on 27.02.2020), around 96.5% pay orders have been generated within 15 days from the date of closure of the Muster Roll at National level.Annexure-I referred in reply to part (a) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.2000 dated 03.03.2020
