                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Blue Revolution in Andhra Pradesh




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of benefits that pisciculture farmers in the State of Andhra Pradesh received under Blue Revolution during the last three years, year-wise and component-wise; and (b)	the details of help/assistance given to above farmers in creating post-harvest infrastructure during the last three years, year-wise, under Blue Revolution in the State of Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) and (b): The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY), during the financial year 2020-21, has accorded approval to the proposal of Government of Andhra Pradesh at a total cost of Rs. 275.60 crore  and first installment of central share of Rs. 48.89 crore released to the State Government for development of fisheries including pisciculture and creation of post harvest infrastructure facilities. Besides, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under the erstwhile Centrally Sponsored Scheme on Blue Revolution : Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries accorded approval to the proposals worth Rs.355.18 crore and central share of Rs.38.58 crore released to the State Government during the period from 2018-19 to 2019-20 for development of fisheries in the State including pisciculture and creation of post-harvest infrastructure facilities.	Year-wise and component-wise details of benefits provided to pisciculture farmers in the State of Andhra Pradesh under Blue Revolution and PMMSY as reported by the State Government is furnished at Annexure-I.  Further, year-wise and component-wise details of financial assistance provided for creating post harvest infrastructure for the benefit of pisciculture farmers  and fishers in the State of Andhra Pradesh during the past three years (2018-19 to 2020-21)  as reported by the Government of Andhra Pradesh is furnished at Annexure-II.*****
