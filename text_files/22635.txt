                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3909






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Connectivity of Jewar Airport




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government proposes to connect Jewar airport  with all the districts of Uttar Pradesh with a specialised road; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the name of the districts likely to be connected  with Jewar airport?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Government of India granted ''in principle'' approval to Government of Uttar Pradesh (GoUP) on 08.05.2018  for setting up of a new greenfield international airport at Jewar, Noida. State Government has awarded the project to M/s Zurich Airports International for development of the airport under Public Private Partnership (PPP). The proposed airport is located at a distance of 750 meters from Yamuna Expressway, to be linked through an interchange. GoUP has plans to provide links to the Jewar airport by way of:-;1. Connectivity from Northern Side of airport through 75 meters wide Road;2. Road connectivity from Eastern Side to proposed MRO site;3. Interchange connectivity between Yamuna Expressway and Eastern Peripheral Road;4. Connectivity to airport through Delhi-Varanasi High Speed Rail Link.;
