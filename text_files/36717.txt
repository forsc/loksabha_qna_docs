                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3658






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Visakhapatnam Coffee Cluster




Goddeti Madhavi













                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has prepared a detailed project report for the Visakhapatnam Coffee Cluster and if so, the details thereof; and (b)	the details of the cost approved in the said project till now for establishing common facility centre, product development and design intervention? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA)(a):	The Detailed Project Report (DPR) of Visakhapatnam Coffee Cluster was prepared by Nodal Agency i.e. Indian Micro Enterprises Development Foundation (IMEDF) for intervention under the Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) of Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), Government of India.(b):	The proposal of Visakhapatnam Coffee Cluster was presented to the 32nd Scheme Steering Committee (SSC) Meeting of SFURTI held on 15.01.2020 and committee approved the project with total cost of Rs. 1,79,49,500/- including GoI grant of Rs. 1,65,25,000/-. The funds of Rs. 1,55,84,750/- out of approved GoI grant has been released till now for various interventions under the project.
