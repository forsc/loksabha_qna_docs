                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2471






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Left Wing Extremism




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the districts in Chhattisgarh where programmes of assistance/schemes for Left Wing Extremism (LWE) is implemented;(b) whether Rajnandgaon and Kabirdham districts are included in that list;(c) if so, the amount of funds received by Rajnandgaon and Kabirdham districts from Central and State Governments, district-wise;(d) whether any committee is constituted for action plan approved and review for spending LWE funds;(e) whether the Government is contemplating any provision of the role or advise of MPs in the said Committee; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	At present 14 districts of Chhattisgarh including Rajnandgaon and Kabirdham, are covered under Security Related Expenditure (SRE) Scheme for the purpose of reimbursement of expenditure incurred by the States on counter LWE operations. (c):	The funds are not released directly to the districts but to the respective State(s).  The details of districts of Chhattisgarh and amount of funds released to -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.2471 FOR 3.12.2019Chhattisgarh Government under various scheme like Security Related Expenditure (SRE) scheme, Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS) and Special Central Assistance (SCA) Scheme to supplement the development efforts are annexed. (d):	The Government is monitoring the progress of the schemes in the LWE affected areas at various levels. Each scheme is monitored by a Committee, which approves the plans of States, fund releases to the States and monitors the progress. As regards to the development initiatives an Empowered Committee set-up by Ministry of Home Affairs regularly monitors and reviews the progress of the schemes being implemented by other line Ministries. Further regular reviews are undertaken by HM, Cabinet Secretary and Union Home Secretary with the Central Ministries/Departments and State Governments.  (e):	No, Sir.(f):	Does not arise in view of reply at (e) above.********
