                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

920






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Allocation and Utilisation of Funds under SBM-U




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) 	the quantum of funds allocated under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) scheme during the last three years, State-wise; (b) 	the funds released, State-wise including Maharashtra thereunder during the last three years;  (c) 	the funds spent/unspent, State-wise under the above scheme; and(d) 	whether the Government has achieved the desired objectives of the scheme ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)and (b):  Allocation of funds under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) to State Governments and Union Territories (UTs) is done for the entire Mission Period (2014-2019) and not for individual financial year separately.  Accordingly, the allocation of funds, State/UT-wise for the entire Mission Period and release of funds for the last three years has been given at Annexure-I.  (c) : State/UT-wise details of Utilisation Certificate (UC) due and received, including the State of Maharashtra, is at Annexure-II.(d) : SBM-U, inter alia, has two prime objectives, namely, to achieve 100 per cent Open Defecation Free (ODF) status  and 100 per cent scientific processing of the  Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) being generated in the country. At present, all 35 States and Union Territories, except for 52 Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) of West Bengal, have been declared as ODF. The Door-to-Door (DtD) collection of MSW is being practised in 96% and source segregation in 74% of 84,475 wards in the country. Further, out of 1,48,945 Tonnes Per Day (TPD) waste generated per day, 60% (i.e., 89,367 TPD) is being scientifically processed.                                        ******
