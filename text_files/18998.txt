                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

358






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






National Mineral Exploration Trust




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government established a PPP model ''National MineralExploration Trust'' (NMET) to encourage private sector participation and for giving a fillip to mineral exploration in the country;(b) the number of private exploration agencies empanelled for the NMET till date; and(c) the number of projects approved for NMET along with the details of the Mineral-wise  and  State- wise  distributions  of  mineral exploration  projects  approved under the aforesaid project till date?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)	The Government has established National Minerals Exploration Trust (NMET) for giving a fillip to mineral exploration in the country. It has not been established on a PPP model.(b)	No private exploration agencies have empanelled for the NMET till date.(c)	A total of 191 number of projects have been approved till date by NMET. Details of the mineral and state wise approved projects are given below;i.	Mineral-wise distribution of mineral exploration projects approved till date;Sl. No.	Commodity	No. of Projects1	Iron Ore	182	Limestone	773	Manganese	184	Bauxite	165	Copper	76	Gold	67	Gold and Basemetal	48	Basemetal	79	Copper and Gold	210	Fe-Ni-Cr	111	Tin	112	Coal/Lignite	413	PGE	114	Diamond	215	Andalusite	116	Fertilizer Minerals	417	Tungsten	118	Magnesite	119	Environmental Studies	120	Associated Minerals	521	Molybdenum	122	Baseline Geoscience Surveys	13 	Total	191 (ii)  State-wise distribution of mineral exploration projects approved till date;S. No.	Name of the State	No. of Projects1	Andhra Pradesh	122	Chhattisgarh	183	Jharkhand	94	Karnataka	115	Kerala	26	Madhya Pradesh	127	Maharashtra	78	Nagaland	29	Odisha	3210	Rajasthan	1911	Tamil Nadu	912	Telangana	4413	Uttar Pradesh	114	Baseline Geoscience Survey	13 	Total	191 *****
