                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1661






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Fund Allocation for Minority Communities




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FINANCE be pleased to state:(a) the quantum of fund allocated for various schemes meant for the welfare of minority communities during the last three years along with the trend of  expenditure, year-wise and scheme-wise; and(b) the percentage of increase in budget for minorities during the said period, year-wise and scheme-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) Year-wise and scheme-wise details of allocation of fundsand actual expenditure during the last three years and current year forvarious schemes meant for the welfare of minority communities are atAnnexure.
