                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Promotion of Eco-Tourism in Bihar




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to involve the local community in contributing to the overall economic development of the national parks and wildlife reserves in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has any proposal under its consideration for promotion of eco-tourism in the State of Bihar and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of all the ongoing eco-tourism projects in the country by the Union and State Governments, State-wise along with amount sanctioned, progress so far and timeline for completion; and (d) 	whether the Government is offering any incentives to private sector for promotion of eco-tourism in the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Ministry of Tourism under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme is developing thematic tourist circuits in the country in a planned and prioritized manner. One of the objective of the scheme is to provide job opportunities to local community by developing the tourism infrastructure in the identified circuits.(b):	Eco and Wildlife are among the 15 thematic circuits identified for development under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme. Submission of project proposals by the State Governments under the scheme and its sanctioning is a continuous process. The projects for development under the scheme are identified in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to submission of project proposals, their adherence to relevant scheme guidelines, submission of suitable detailed project reports, availability of funds, and utilization of funds released earlier. (c):	Ministry has not received any proposal from the Government of Bihar under Eco Circuit.  The details of projects sanctioned under the Eco Circuit theme are annexed.(d):	Ministry of Tourism (MoT) is offering incentive in the form of Market Development Assistance (MDA) for Promotion of Domestic Tourism to private sector in the country to tourism service providers, viz., hoteliers, travel agents, tour operators, tourist transport operators etc. approved by the MoT*******
