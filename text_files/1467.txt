                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Knowledge Resource Centres




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether any Knowledge Resource Centres are being run by the Government;(b) 	if so, the number of the said centres running presently in the country, State/ UT-wise; and(c)	 the State/ UT-wise details of the amount of the fund allocated in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to part (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 131 for 1.7.2019.(a)	to (c)  :- Ministry of Culture has a number of  Knowledge Heritage Resources. The details of such institutions and libraries and amount of fund allocated during the current year are given in the Annexure.
