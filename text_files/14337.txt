                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Cancellation of Flights due to Corona Virus Threat




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any details regarding total number of flight services cancelled by various carrier operators to different destinations due to corona virus threat and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has assessed the loss incurred by the Aviation Industry due to corona virus threat and if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government has issued any notification/ban on flight operations to different destinations and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) The details of cancelled flights of foreign airlines to/ from India as on 06.03.2020 is at Annexure I. The details of cancelled flights of Scheduled Indian Carriers as on 06.03.2020 is at Annexure II;(b) No such assessment has been done by this Ministry, so far.;(c) The details of suspended flights of foreign airlines to/ from India after notification issued by DGCA as on 06.03.2020 is at Annexure III.
