                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Development of Religious Circuit in Punjab




Harsimrat Kaur Badal














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that Sri Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple) at Amritsar and Sri Anandpur Sahib and other religious Takhts of Sikh religion and places are world class monuments in Punjab attracting millions of pilgrims tourists; (b) 	whether the Government has given any funds for development of tourism in Punjab; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government has built any amenities to help Punjab in this regard; and (e) 	whether the Government proposes to give funds for development of religious circuit in Punjab?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Yes, Sir. Sri Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple) at Amritsar and Sri Anandpur Sahib and other religious Takhts attract  pilgrims and tourists. (b) to (e):	The Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for  development of  tourism infrastructure.  In addition, projects pertaining to development of tourism infrastructure are also sanctioned under ‘Assistance to Central Agencies for Tourism Infrastructure Development Scheme’.  Sanctioning of projects under the schemes is a continuous process and is subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc.A list of projects sanctioned by Ministry of Tourism in Punjab is annexed. *******
