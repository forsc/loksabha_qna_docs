                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2978






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Free Electricity for BPL Families




Topon Kumar Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any provision for the BPL families to get free electric connection for those who were left in the previous scheme of ‘Electricity for Everyone’; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) : 	Electricity is a concurrent subject and providing free power or subsidy or concession to any category of consumers including industrial consumers is within the purview of the State Government as per Section 65 of Electricity Act, 2003.However, Government of India launched Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana (DDUGJY) in December, 2014 for various rural electrification works. Under the scheme free electricity connections were provided to all the BPL households in rural areas. State-wise details are at Annexure-ISubsequently, Government of India launched the Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana – Saubhagya in October, 2017 to achieve universal household electrification by providing free electricity connections to all un-electrified households in rural areas and the remaining un-electrified households of economically poor in urban areas across the country.Under Saubhagya scheme, as on 31.03.2021 all the States have reported 100% electrification of all the willing un-electrified households including BPL families, identified before 31.03.2019. Total 2.817 crore households have been electrified since the launch of Saubhagya, up to 31.03.2021. State-wise details are at Annexure-II. ***********
