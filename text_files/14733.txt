                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Track Child Portal




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the date since which training programme on Track Child Portal under Child Protection Services Scheme is being organized; (b) 	the details of the locations where the said training programmes have been organized, State/UTwise; and (c) 	the total number of the officers trained under the said programmes and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Women and Child Development (MWCD) has developed web  portal “TrackChild” since, 2012 to track the missing and found children. The TrackChild Portal is implemented involving various stakeholders including Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), Ministry of Railways, State Governments/UT Administrations, Child Welfare Committees, Juvenile Justice Boards, National Legal Services Authority, etc. The details of the officers trained under the said programmes are not maintained by this Ministry.As per information provided by the NIC, the details of training programmes held is Annexed.***‘AnnexureAnnexure referred in reply of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.3365 for answer on 13.03.2020 raised by Dr. Virendra Kumar regarding “Track Child Portal”.
