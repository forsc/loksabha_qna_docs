                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

914






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Homoeopathy System




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Union Government has received any proposals under the Homoeopathy system from the Government of Maharashtra during the last three years, if so, the details thereof;(b) the total number of proposals that have been disposed of so far;(c) the total number of proposals under the consideration of Government at present; and(d) the time by which these pending proposals are likely to be approved?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b): Under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), Ministry of AYUSH is providing grant-in-aid to State / UT Governments including Maharashtra for development and promotion of AYUSH Systems including Homoeopathy as per the proposals received in their State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). The status of grant-in-aid released to the State Government of Maharashtra during the past three years for different activities as proposed in the State Annual Action Plan is furnished below:Year	Amount Released (Rs. in Lakhs)2017-18	1784.2852018-19	Nil2019-20	4308.643 Ministry of AYUSH has further received one proposal from Government of Maharashtra to start a new Government Homoeopathic College in the name and style of “Government Homoeopathic Medical College and Hospital” at Chincholi, Jalgaon, Maharashtra with 50 seats in BHMS Course from the Acedemic year 2020-21. The proposal was forwarded to The Central Council of Homoeopathy (CCH). The Central Council of Homoeopathy has, accordingly considered the proposal.(c) & (d): The proposal from Government of Maharashtra to start new Government Homoeopathic College in the name & style of “Government Homeopathic Medical College and Hospital” at Chincholi, Jalgaon, Maharashtra with 50 seats in BHMS Course from Academic Year 2020-21 was further considered by The Central Council of Homoeopathy and Letter of Intent was issued.
