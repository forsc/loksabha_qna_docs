                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4415






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Healthcare Centres in Tea Plantations




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has any proposal to set up healthcare centres under NHM in various tea plantations of Assam, if so, the details thereof; (b) the names of the places where healthcare centres have been established under NHM in Bodo Territorial Council (BTC); and (c) whether the healthcare centres set up there have sufficient staff and facilities and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):    The Government has not received any proposal from the State Government through their Programme Implementation Plan (PIP) FY 2020-21 under National Health Mission (NHM) to set up healthcare centres in tea plantations of Assam.(b) &(c):    As per information received from the State Government,  there is total 709  established and functional Health Institutions i.e District Hospital-4, Sub Divisional Civil Hospital-1, Community Health Centre-21, Primary Health Centre-127 and Sub Centre-556 in Bodo Territorial Council (BTC). The details are at Annexure-A.These facilities have been set up by the State Government.  Support is provided under NHM to the States/UTs to address insufficiencies of human resource, equipment & infrastructure based on proposals of the States in their annual PIP.……………………
