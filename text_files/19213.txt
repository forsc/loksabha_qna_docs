                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Online Initiative of KVIC




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched the ekhadiindia.com as the first ever effort of the Ministry & Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) in online & B2C outreach; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the targeted sales and revenue generation projection for the next financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)&(b):    Yes Sir, Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC), a statutory body, working under the aegis of the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, Government of India, established a B2C official online e-commerce portal namely ‘ekhadiindia.com’ to support the online marketing facilities for its stakeholders on 1st January, 2021. This portal has been established on Design, Build, Finance, Operate and Transfer (DBFOT) model through Public-Private Partnership (PPP) mode on revenue sharing basis. The contract is initially for a period of 10 years. (c): 	There is no sales target fixed as on now for the next financial year.
