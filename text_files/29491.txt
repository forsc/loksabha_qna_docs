                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1281






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Financial Assistance to Asiatic Library




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of libraries which have been accorded status of Institution of National Importance in our country since independence;(b)	whether there are any plans to accord status of Institution of National Importance to Asiatic Library in Mumbai;(c)	if so, the details thereof.(d)	whether the employees working in Asiatic Library, Mumbai have not been paid full salary for almost one year and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the employees have served the notice of agitation for their non-payment of salary and if so, the details thereof;(e)	the quantum and details of financial assistance given by the Ministry of Culture or Government of India to this historical library; and(f)	the details of other institutes who provide financial assistance to Asiatic Library?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY) (a) 	There are 3 libraries namely (i) National Library, Kolkata; (ii) Rampur Raza Library, Rampur, Uttar Pradesh and (iii) Khuda Bakhsh Oriental Public Library, Patna which have been accorded status of Institution of National Importance since independence.(b) to (f) The ‘Asiatic Library, Mumbai’ is not funded or administered by the Ministry of Culture.Moreover, Libraries is a state subject as per the Constitution of India.
