                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2716






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Development Sportspersons in Rural Area




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds released to various States for development of Youth and Sports during the last three years and the current year, particularly in Uttar Pradesh; (b)	whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Governments in this regard; (c)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise and the action taken by the Government; (d)	whether the Government proposes to open up more Sports Authority of India (SAI) centres in the States besides the existing SAI facilities; (e)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and (f)	whether any scheme is being proposed by the Government to exclusively develop women sportspersons specially rural women and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a):	Funds allocated by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports  are Scheme-wise, not State-wise. Details of funds allocated and utilized under the schemes of the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports are at Annexure-I. The total amount of financial assistance sanctioned and released to various States (particularly in Uttar Pradesh) under NSS Scheme during the last three financial years and current financial year for regular activities and special camping programmes of the National Service Scheme (NSS) are:-(in Rs.)<pre>Financial Year	Funds released to various states	Funds released to Uttar Pradesh2018-19	126,59,30,982/-	           --2019-20	126,51,88,274/-	10,98,27,000/-2020-21	66,20,43,590/-	95,13,698/-2021-22	91,32,32,284/-	10,95,82,500/-</pre>Moreover, 267 sports infrastructure projects of various categories have been sanctioned under the Khelo India Scheme. State-wise details are at Annexure-I.(b) and (c): Yes Sir, the number of proposals received from the State Governments and the proposals approved for the Khelo India Centres and Khelo India State Centre of Excellence under the Khelo India Scheme are at Annexure-II.  Moreover, on the basis of proposals including the Utilization Certificates, received from State Governments every year, grants under NSS are released by the Union Government to State Governments for organizing regular activities, special camping activities and other NSS activities.(d) and (e): At present, there are no such proposals. (f): One of the components of the Khelo India Scheme, namely, “Sports for Women”, is specifically dedicated towards promotion of sports among women, including in rural areas. Details of the scheme are in the public domain on the website of this Ministry.*****
