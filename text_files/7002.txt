                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

709






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Layout Plan for Unauthorised Colonies of Delhi




 Jai Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to prepare layout plan for about 1800 unauthorised colonies/ habitats of Delhi through Delhi Development Authority;(b)	if so, whether Delhi Development Authority has sought satellite images of said colonies from Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO); and(c)	if so, the time by which the said layout plan is likely to be prepared?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)to(c): Delhi Development Authority has informed that it shall prepare Local Area Plan for Unauthorised Colonies in Delhi. The satellite imageries of 2015 have been procured by Delhi Development Authority. Local Area Plans/ Lay out plans for unauthorized colonies in Delhi shall be prepared after land rights are given to the residents as per recently notified Regulations in this regard.                            ******
