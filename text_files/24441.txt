                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

415






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Setting up of Private/Public Sector Industries




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of the backward and rural circles of the country in which the Government is contemplating to set up private or public sector industries as on date;(b)	the details of the requests received from the State Governments in this regard during the last three years;(c)	the action taken or being taken by the Government in this regard;(d)	whether the Government proposes to set up public or private sector industries especially in the backward and rural circles of the country;(e)	if so, the details thereof; and(f)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) to (f):	Since Industry is a State subject, no centralized data regarding establishing industries in various areas of the country is maintained by the Ministry of Heavy Industries (MHI).  There is no proposal in MHI to set up industries.---------
