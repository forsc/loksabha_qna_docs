                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

720






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Expansion of National Highways




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of the dilapidated condition of National Highway Nos. 326 and 26;(b)	 if so, the action taken by the Government for the reconstruction of these highways;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to start work on four-laning of these highways and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details of all the by-passes proposed around major towns Rayagada, Koraput, Jeypore on these highways?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	Due to extended monsoon & excessive rain till end of October 2019, certain stretches of NH-26 & NH-326 in Odisha have been damaged.  Temporary restoration works have been taken up in the damaged stretches by filling depressions & potholes by granular material and removing fallen trees to make the road in traffic worthy condition. The work for repair of the damaged stretches with bituminous material has already been taken up.((c)	Four laning of these National Highways will depend upon the traffic, availability of funds and inter-se priority.(d)	The details of the bypasses are as under:i.	Rayagada bypass- No proposal has been received in this Ministry from the State PWD of Odisha.ii.	Koraput bypass- The alignment plan has been approved by this Ministry. However the Land Aquisition is stuck up due to issues raised by the Railways.iii.	Jeypore bypass- The alignment plan has already been approved by this Ministry.  The Land Acquisiton for the project under Section 3G of NH Act is in process.                                                                          *****
