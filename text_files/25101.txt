Claims under PMJJY




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Government has any data with regard to the applications pending before the banks for claims under Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY) for the persons who died due to COVID-19 in the last year and current year and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefore, State/UT-wise, and(b) whether the Government has any record of the claims passed in the last five years under PMJJBY and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor, State/UT-wise?
