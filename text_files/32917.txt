                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Dams in Seismic Zones




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has the details of the seismological zone in which all the dams in the country are situated;  (b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) Whether the Government has any plan to install a seismograph in the Mullaperiyar dam; and (d) if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) & (b) Yes. As per Bureau of Indian Standards Code IS 1893 (Part 1) 2016: ‘Criteria For Earthquake Resistant Design of Structures’, Country has been divided into four seismic zones (Zone II, III, IV & V).National Register of Large Dams 2019, compiled by the Central Water Commission, contains the State-wise seismic zone information of the dams along with other dams'' basic features. This document is accessible through the link - http://cwc.gov.in/publication/nrld  (c) & (d)  Tamil Nadu Government has given the work order for installation of seismic instruments in the Mulla Periyar dam to the National Geophysical Research Institute, Hyderabad. ******
