                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Online Ticket for Night Viewing of Taj Mahal




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the problem of the visitors of Taj Mahal who plan to see it at night but have to buy physical tickets from the office of the ASI one day ahead of their day of visit;(b)	if so, the action plan if any, for making the ticket for night viewing available online on the same day; and(c)	the total number of year-wise visitors who visited Taj Mahal to see it at night and the amount collected from such visitors?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a)&(b)	Archaeological Survey of India has been issuing tickets from its office at Agra, 24 hours in advance as per the directions dated 25.11.2004 by the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in WP (Civil) No. 13381/1984 by M.C. Mehta vs Union of India.
