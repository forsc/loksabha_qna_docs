                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2446






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Hospitality and Tourism University




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is considering to set up a hospitality and tourism university in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to setup the said University in the State of Gujarat; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said University is likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b):	Ministry of Tourism has accorded its approval on the proposal of Indian Institute of Tourism and Travel Management (IITTM) for its Deemed to be University status. As advised by UGC the proposal is being submitted to UGC, as per revised guidelines which is being finalized by the UGC.  (c) to (d):	IITTM, Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh is proposed to be granted the status of “Deemed to be University” with its existing off campuses at Bhubaneswar (Odisha), Panaji (Goa), Nellore (Andhra Pradesh) and Noida (Uttar Pradesh).*******
