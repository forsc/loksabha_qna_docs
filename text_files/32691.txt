                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

166






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Discontinuation of Trains




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that eight pairs of trains plying between Bihar, Jharkhand and Bengal have been permanently discontinued; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Ministry has data regarding the total number of trains in Bihar suspended during the last five years; (d) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (e) the steps taken by the Government to decrease suspension of trains?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e)  Indian Railways (IR) does not operate train services on State-wise basis as railway network straddles across state boundaries and as such state-wise data regarding discontinuation of trains is not maintained.  Indian Railways undertakes rationalization of time table and keeping in view occupancy of trains, augments/de-augments the load of trains, increases/decreases the frequency of trains and only as a last resort undertakes, cancellation/ discontinuation of services. *****
