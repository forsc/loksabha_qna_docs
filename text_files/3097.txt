                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2810






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Defence Deals with Foreign Countries




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to enter into defence deals with foreign countries;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the names of countries and total quantum of money involved in the deals;(c) whether any Indian company is involved in the deals;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (e):  Procurements of military equipment and systems are undertaken based upon operational requirements of the Armed Forces as per Defence Procurement         Procedure (DPP).  Such procurements from Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) are made through Inter Government Agreements (IGA) with friendly foreign countries and/or through contracts based on global RFPs after necessary approvals.*******
