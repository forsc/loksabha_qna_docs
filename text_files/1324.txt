                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1335






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Textile Excellence Centres




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















a)	Whether the Government has any proposal to establish textile excellence centres in different parts of the country, especially in Tripura;b)	If so, the details thereof;c)	If not, the reasons therefor; and d)	The details of professional textile courses approved by Government so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c) : In silk sector, under the Integrated Sericulture Development Project of North East Region Textile Promotion Scheme, a component “Centre of Excellence for Capacity Building” is approved for Tripura State at GoI share of Rs. 45.00 lakhs. The project is in the process of implementation. Also, Bamboo & Cane Development Institute (BCDI) at Agartalla provides technical training to the artisans including processing facilities in Bamboo craft. (d) :	The details of professional textile courses as approved under Ministry of Textiles is at Annexure.
