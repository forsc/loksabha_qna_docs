                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

431






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Funds under Dairy Schemes




Giridhari Yadav













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    ) the amount of funds released by the Government under Clean Milk Production and National Dairy Development Programme in Bihar during the last three years including the amount of funds out of the said funds utilised in this regard, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether any complaint has been received regarding utilization of the said fund; and (c) if so, the details of the action taken on these complaints along with the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	The Department is implementing National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD) scheme for clean milk production among other objectives. The details of funds released by the Department and funds utilised by Bihar State Milk Co-operative Federation Ltd. under NPDD during last three years is as under:-								(Rs. in lakh)Year	Funds Released	Funds Utilised2016-17	1287.34*	1180.652017-18	3566.59	3506.052018-19	3793.47	1236.68			*Released under IDDP project(b) No Sir.(c) Does not arise.****
