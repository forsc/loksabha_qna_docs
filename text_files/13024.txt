                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1868






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Training to Rural Youths under DDU-GKY




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether training is provided to rural youths under the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya-Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) ;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of rural youths from the districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur who have been trained under the Yojana;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of post training placements, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c) The Government is implementing Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), a placement linked skill development program for rural poor youth under National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) successfully all over the country.
