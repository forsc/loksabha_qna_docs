                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3738






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Registered Scientific Societies and Charitable Religious Endowments




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of registered scientific societies/ associations and charitable religious endowments relating to Indian System of Medicine including Homeopathy available across the country; (b) the details of central and centrally sponsored schemes being implemented in the country; and (c) the steps taken by the Government to familirise urban and rural people to AYUSH medicines?          






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	The data of registered scientific societies/ associations and charitable religious endowments relating to Indian System of Medicine including Homeopathy is not maintained by the Ministry of Ayush. However, the information received from voluntary action cell, NITI Aayog, which include data of Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) uploaded on NGO-Darpan Portal of NITI Aayog on voluntarily basis or when the NGOs wants funds from Government of India, is attached at Annexure I. (b)	The details of central and centrally sponsored schemes being implemented by Ministry of Ayush are attached as Annexure II. (c) 	The Ministry of Ayush implements Information, Education and Communication (IEC) scheme for creation of awareness among the members of the community about the efficacy of the AYUSH Systems, their cost-effectiveness and the availability of Herbs used for prevention and treatment of common ailments at their door steps through various channels including the production of audio-visual educational material.     The Research Councils and National Institutes under the Ministry of Ayush conduct IEC activities and distribute the IEC material. Brochures, bulletins, Peer Reviewed Journals, books, monographs and reports are published by them. They conduct exhibitions, health melas, seminars, health camps and various other public outreach/awareness programmes to familiarise urban and rural people to AYUSH medicines.   The website of Ministry of Ayush and the Research Councils and National Institutes under the Ministry of Ayush publish material on Ayush system which include advisories for prevention and management of diseases through AYUSH systems. The AYUSH systems are also promoted through social media.
