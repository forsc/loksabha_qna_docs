                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Health Checkups for Air Passengers




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has issued any guidelines/warnings/precautions for domestic air passengers due to Coronavirus and if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken by the Government in this regard;(b) whether the Government is conducting health checkups for the air passengers coming from abroad and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any details about the passengers who have been denied entry to the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) Ministry of Civil Aviation, from time to time, reiterated the instructions issued by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, to control Coronavirus. These inter-alia, include undertaking Information, Education and Communication (IEC) for COVID-19 at all the airports including domestic airports, mentioning the sign and symptoms of the disease  apart from instructions regarding disinfection of domestic flights. (b) Yes, Sir. Government has mandated all airports with international operations to screen all passengers arriving from international flights. So far over 13 lakh passengers arriving from international flights have been screened at 30 airports.;(c) & (d) Due to outbreak of Coronavirus, the Government has suspended all existing visas (except diplomatic, official, UN/International Organizations, employment, project visas) till 15.04.2020 w.e.f.13.03.2020.
