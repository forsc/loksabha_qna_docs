                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1777






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Completion of Dedicated Freight Corridor Project




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the time by which the ‘Dedicated Freight Corridor Projects’ is expected to be completed;(b) 	whether land acquisition problems are being faced in the said projects;(c)	if so, the state-wise details thereof including Maharashtra;(d) 	whether the states are not lending their co-operation to settle the issues regarding land acquisition under this project; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Ministry of Railways is implementing two Dedicated Freight Corridors (DFCs) viz. Eastern DFC (Ludhiana to Sonnagar)  and Western DFC (Dadri to Jawaharlal Nehru Port Terminal in Mumbai).Both the corridors are targeted to be commissioned in phases by June 2022.(b) to (e): As of now 97.6% land in EDFC and 99.85% of land  in WDFC  has been acquired. Regular follow-up meetings are held with the State Governments to expedite the remaining land acquisition and requisite co-operation is being given by states.*****
