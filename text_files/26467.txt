                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Funds for Maharashtra




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated/granted under various schemes of the Government to the State of Maharashtra during the last three years;(b) the details of funds released under such schemes to the State during the last three years;(c) the details of funds yet to be released/ due to the State; and(d) the fixed time-frame by which these funds would be released to the State?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c): The details of funds released to the state of Maharashtra under various schemes implemented by Ministry of Education, during the last three years are as below:Department of Higher Education(Funds released in Rs. Crore)S.No.2018-192019-202020-21i.Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA)45.1887.700.0ii.Improvement in salary scale of university and college teachers1001320.93-iii.Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College & University students15.5315.0414.95Department of School Education & Literacy(Funds released in Rs. Crore)S.No.2018-192019-202020-21i.Samagra Shiksha950.51734.23635.59ii.Mid-Day Meal Scheme981.85994.681002.50iii.Scheme for Providing Education to Madarsas/Minorities (SPEMM)--0.44iv.Saakshar Bharat Programme17.94--v.National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS)75.7436.0470.90(d): The funds are released to the States regularly on the basis of progress of the scheme. In scholarship schemes the amount is sent to the bank accounts of the students directly by the direct benefit transfer mode.***
