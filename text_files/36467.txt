                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3699






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Mahila E-Haat Yojana




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of beneficiaries under Mahila E-Haat Yojana across the country including Odisha, districts-wise; and (b)  	the amount of funds allocated and spent under the said scheme? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b) The Ministry of Women & Child Development (MWCD) launched an online e-marketing platform namely “Mahila E-Haat” on 07th March 2016, to support and empower women entrepreneurs, Self Help Groups (SHGs) or Non-Governmental Organizational (NGOs). The State-wise list of women entrepreneurs registered at "Mahila E-Haat" is at Annexure. Government has promoted platforms such as Government e-Market Place (GeM) and e-market place managed by National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) to support the entrepreneurs. In view of this, the Ministry has closed the Mahila E-Haat since July, 2020. ********   AnnexureStatement  referred  to  in  reply  to  part  (a)  of  the  Lok  Sabha  Unstarred Question No. 3699 to be answered on 25.03.2022 asked by Shri Basanta Kumar Panda regarding "Mahila e-Haat Yojana”State-wise number women entrepreneurs registered under Mahila E-haat Name of State/UT	Number of women entrepreneursAndhra Pradesh	05Assam	02Bihar	03Chhattisgarh	15Delhi	36Goa	01Gujarat	12Haryana	09Jharkhand	07Karnataka	12Kerala	35Madhya Pradesh	37Maharashtra	54Meghalaya	07Odisha	03Punjab	07Rajasthan	09Tamil Nadu	08Telangana	09Uttar Pradesh	33West Bengal	17TOTAL	321
