                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Damage due to Locust Attack




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	whether any compensation has been provided as a result of the survey conducted by the Government regarding damage caused by locust attack in Rajasthan; (b):	if so, the details thereof; (c):	whether the Government has considered locust attack in the category of natural calamity and if so, the details thereof; (d):	if not, whether the Government proposes to issue notification for considering locust attack in the category of natural calamity; and (e):	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	State Government of Rajasthan has reported that during 2019-20, the State Government has disbursed Rs. 106.21 crore as agricultural input subsidy to 64,087 farmers from the State Disaster Response Fund and Rs. 9.19 crore was spent on infrastructure of locust control. Government of India has approved and released assistance of Rs. 68.65 crore from National Disaster Response Fund to the State of Rajasthan for locust attack during Rabi 2019-20. For 2020-21, State Government of Rajasthan has issued instructions for conducting survey to estimate crop losses. (c):	Pest attack (locust) has already been notified as a natural disaster by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India and States are eligible for assistance from, State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) and National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) in the event of locust attack. (d) & (e):	Question does not arise*****
