                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

891






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Jiyo Parsi Scheme




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the salient features of JiyoParsi scheme launched by the Government;(b)	the funds allocated and spent so far for the implementation of Jiyo Parsi scheme; and(c)	the details of achievements made under the scheme so far since its launch?






ANSWER




MINISTER OFMINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a): 	Ministry of Minority Affairs  implements Jiyo Parsi- Scheme since 2013-14. The objective of the scheme is to contain the population decline of Parsis in India. The scheme adopts a scientific protocol and structured interventions to stabilize Parsi population. The Scheme has three components given as under: (i)	Advocacy	-	includes counseling of couples with fertility, marriage, family and elderly counseling including workshops on relationship management, parenting, drug awareness etc.  (ii)	Health of Community	-	Includes creche/child care support, senior citizen  honorarium for child care, assistance to elderly etc.(iii)	Medical Assistance	-	includes Assisted Reproductive Technologies             (ART) which also includes In-vitro Fertilization and Intra Cytoplasmic Injection (ICS) and other modes including surrogacy. Benefits under this component can be availed by any eligible Parsi couple.(b):	The details of funds allocated and released since inception of scheme are given as under:(Rs. in crore)     Year	Fund allocation	Fund Released2013-14	2.00	0.412014-15	2.00	0.502015-16	2.00	1.182016-17	2.00	1.542017-18	3.00	3.002018-19	4.00	4.002019-20	4.00	3.45(c):	The total number of babies (upto 31.12.2019) born under the scheme since inception of the scheme is 233.
