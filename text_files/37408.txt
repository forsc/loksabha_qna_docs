                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Public WiFi Hotspots




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Maddila  Gurumoorthy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds allocated under the BharatNet project for setting up of public WiFi hotspots; (b) 	the number of public WiFi hotspots set up in rural areas under the Bharatnet project, State-wise, including Andhra Pradesh; (c) 	whether the Government has any data on usage of such WiFi hotspots by rural public? If so, the details thereof; and (d) 	whether the Government is planning to set up such hotspots in more public areas in the rural regions and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	As per the Cabinet approval dated 19.07.2017, Rs. 4,066 crore has been approved for providing Last Mile Connectivity, through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband technology, at all Gram Panchayats (GPs) of BharatNet Phase-I and Phase-II. (b)	The State/UT-wise details of the Wi-Fi hotspots, installed at GPs in the country including Andhra Pradesh, from BharatNet network, are given in Annexure-I.(c)	During February 2022, the total data consumption, including that of Wi-Fi hotspots, was 4029 Terabyte (TB) through BharatNet network. (d)	The infrastructure created under BharatNet project can be utilized for provisioning of broadband/internet services through Wi-Fi Hotspots, Fibre to the Home (FTTH) connections, leased lines, dark fibre, backhaul to mobile towers, etc. The work of providing Wi-Fi hotspots in about 1.20 lakh GPs of BharatNet Phase-I,  about 5500 GPs of BharatNet Phase-II ( being connected  on satellite media), and at about 1400 Digital Satellite Phone Terminals (set up at Government offices/institutions in remote areas) is implemented with funding from Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF). For BharatNet Phase-II (being implemented on OFC/Radio), the respective Implementing Agencies are responsible for the utilisation of the created network.
