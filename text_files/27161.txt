                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2938






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






MSME Technology Centre Project




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    whether the MSME Technology Centre project in Puducherry will be dedicated to the community so that these facilities should be made use by the student community and they can produce the end products utilizing the state of art machineries available in the centre?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NARAYAN RANE)Yes Sir, The construction of Technology Centre-Puducherry is completed and is ready for dedication to the community/students.
