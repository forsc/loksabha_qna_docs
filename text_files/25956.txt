                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1620






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Drones for Vaccines




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) the details of steps being taken by the Government in supplying vaccines to inaccessible and remote areas of the country including Odisha for protection from Corona;(b) whether the Government is using unmanned aircraft, drone etc. for supplying vaccines; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) During the period 12 Jan 2021 to 22 July 2021, approximately 42.2 crore doses have been transported. Out of these approximately 35.8 crore doses have been handled at domestic airports and approximately 6.4 crore doses have been dispatched to international destinations. Approximately 1.2 crore doses have been transported to Bhubaneswar airport during the period 12 Jan 2021 to 22 July 2021. ;(b) & (c) Conditional exemption from Unamnned Aircraft Rules (UAS) Rules, 2021 has been granted to Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) in collaboration with Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur, Government of Telanagana and Department of Health and Family Welfare (DoHFW) of Government of Meghalaya for conducting experimental drone flights for delivery of vaccines and healthcare items.
