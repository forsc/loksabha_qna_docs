                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Raipur-Sarangarh Railway Line




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the names of 33 stations of 310 Km. long proposed Raipur-      	   Sarangarh-Jharsuguda railway line were announced in the year 2011-12;(b) if so, the names of the stations; and(c) the time by which the construction work of these stations is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b) The work of Raipur-Sarangarh-Jharsuguda new line was included in Budget 2013-14 subject to obtaining requisite approvals. State Governments of Chhattisgarh and Orissa were approached for providing land free of cost and to share 50% cost of work. As no response has been received from both State Governments, the project could not be taken forward.However, project of Raipur-Kharsia new line has been identified by Chhattisgarh Rail Corporation Limited(CRCL) State Joint Venture for feasibility study.(c) Does not arise, as project is not sanctioned.*****
