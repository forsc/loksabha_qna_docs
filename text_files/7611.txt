                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Schemes for Conservation of Archaeological Sites




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of the schemes being implemented by the Government for conservation of archaeological sites throughout the country;(b)	the details of the works carried out by the Government for conservation of archaeological sites in Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Maharashtra;(c)	whether the archaeological sites are likely to face any kind of risk and if so, the details of the said likely risks; and(d)	the arrangements made for protection of the said sites against the likely risks or damages?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) 	Conservation work of protected monuments is executed by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) under the budgetary head/scheme as “Conservation of ancient monuments”(b) 	Conservation & maintenance work of protected monuments in the country including Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Maharashtra is a continuous process wherein essential repair work  is attended as per requirements of different sites and availability of resources. (c) & (d) 	Regular care is taken of protected monuments to guard against any threats and they are in a good state of preservation.
