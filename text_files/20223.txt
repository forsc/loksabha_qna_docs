                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1413






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Train from Amravati to Pune




Navneet Ravi Rana














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether thousands of travellers travel from Amravati to Pune on daily basis for work, study or any other purpose; (b) if so, whether any direct superfast train is proposed to be introduced from Amravati to Pune in the near future; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):  The total number of passengers who travelled from Amravati to Pune during the financial year (F.Y.) 2018-19 and F.Y. 2019-20 is 31,000 and 30,635 respectively.(b) & (c):   Presently 02117/02118 Pune-Amravati AC Special train is available between Pune and Amravati. At present, there is no immediate proposal for running of new train between Pune and Amravati, owing to operational and resource constraints. However, introduction of trains is an ongoing process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand and availability of resources etc.*****
