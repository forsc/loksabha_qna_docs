                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4846






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Food Processing Industries




Nitesh Ganga Deb













                                                    Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan/proposal to set up food parks in the country to create modern infrastructure for food processing especially during the current year and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (b)	the details of parks identified/established/approved over the last five years along with financial assistance or funds provided to such parks and the procedure to select such projects; and (c)	whether the Central Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Odisha for setting up/opening up of Food Parks in the State particularly in Barkot block under Deogarh district of the State and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	No, Sir. Ministry of Food Processing Industries is presently not implementing Food Parks scheme. However, Ministry of Food Processing Industries has been implementing Mega Food Park Scheme (MFPS) to create modern infrastructure for the food processing. Government has sanctioned a total of 42 slots of Mega Food Park Projects in the country. Out of these, Ministry has accorded final approval to 38 Mega Food Park Projects and in-principle approval to 4 Mega Food Park projects. Presently, there is no vacant slot to be filled in.  Ministry of Food Processing Industries does not establish Mega Food Parks in the country on its own. The proposals under the scheme for Mega Food Parks of Ministry are invited through Expression of Interest (EOI) from time to time. At present, Ministry has not floated any EoI for submission of proposals under the Scheme. As on date, there is no proposal under consideration to setup any new Mega Food Parks in the country. (b):	Ministry has approved setting up of 27 Mega Food Parks in the country during last five years under the Mega Food Park scheme. Out of these, 23 Mega Food Parks have been accorded final approval, 4 Mega Food Parks accorded in-principle approval by the Ministry. The details of these 27 Mega Food Parks approved by the Ministry during last five years under the Mega Food Park Scheme with their status of implementation along with amount of grant released is at Annexure-I. The proposals under scheme for Mega Food Park are invited through Expression of Interest (EOI) from time to time. Selection of suitable proposals for these projects is done as per the Scheme guidelines, based on the merit, through a stringent appraisal process as per the prefixed criteria. The proposal submitted in response to the EoI are evaluated by the Program Management Agencis (PMAs). The PMAs undertake evaluation on a scale of 100 points on the basis of proposals received in response to EoI; while the TC undertakes independent evaluation on a scale of 50 points as per scheme guidelines. Technical Committee headed by the Additional / Joint Secretary provide its recommendations to the Inter-Ministerial Approval Committee. The Inter-Ministerial Approval Committee (IMAC), headed by Hon’ble Minister of Food Processing Industries selects the projects and grant “In-Principle” and “Final Approval” to the projects as per the scheme guidelines. (c):	No, Sir. However, Ministry has earlier received proposals from State Government entity namely Odisha Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation(IDCO), Odisha and accorded final approval to the project on 06.11.2015 for setting up of Mega Food Park at Khurda, Odisha, which is under Implementation.
