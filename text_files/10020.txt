                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3516






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Blue Revolution




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has provided any financial assistance to States under Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund, if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) 	whether the productivity of inland fisheries is just three tonnes per hectare; (c) 	the extent to which the Blue Revolution is helping the Government to address this problem;(d) 	the details of production and export of fish and fish products in the country during last three years, year-wise and State-wise; (e) 	whether the Government has drawn up plans to increase marine exports to Rs. one lakh crore over the next five years, if so, the details thereof;(f) 	the financial assistance given to States during the last three years for improving fishing infrastructure and strengthening processing and post harvest management; and (g) 	the details of financial assistance sanctioned for the development of fishing harbours and the number of fish landing centres commissioned, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING    	(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) : 	The Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF) provides concessional finance to the Eligible Entities (EEs), including State Governments/Union Territories and State entities for development of identified fisheries infrastructure facilities. The concessional finance is provided through Nodal Loaning Entities (NLEs) namely (i) National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD), (ii) National Cooperatives Development Corporation (NCDC) and (iii) All Scheduled Banks. The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying provides interest subvention up to 3% per annum for providing the concessional finance by the NLEs at the interest rate not lower than 5% per annum under the FIDF. The proposals to the tune of Rs.1715.04 crore received from various State Governments and other Eligible Entities (EEs) have been recommended.(b) to (d):	 As per the fisheries statistical data compiled in 2014 by Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry the average inland fish productivity was registered at 2.9 tonnes/hectare/year. The Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries being implemented since from 2015-16 by the Department of Fisheries (DoF), Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has made vital contributions towards enhancement of fish production and export earnings.  The fish production in the country has increased from 10.26 million metric tonne(2014-15) to 13.34 million metric tonne (2018-19). Similarly, the export of fish and fisheries products from the country has also witnessed a significant growth and enhanced from Rs. 33,441.61 crore during 2014-15 to Rs.47,621 crore during 2018-19. State-wise and year-wise details of the fish production and export of fish and fisheries product in the country during the last three years are furnished at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.(e): 	 In addition to the above stated CSS and FIDF,  the union Government in its Budget, 2019 announced a focused scheme the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) for fisheries. These schemes/programmes are primarily focus on enhancement of fish production, infrastructure and enhancement of export of fish and fisheries products. (f):	Under CSS the central funds are released to the State Governments/Union Territories (UTs) based on the detailed proposals submitted by them. The central financial assistance of Rs.1186.89 crore has been released to the State/UTs during last three years (2016-19) for development of fisheries including the fisheries infrastructure and post harvest management. State-wise details of the central funds released during the last three years under the CSS are furnished at Annexure-III. (g) :	The State-wise details of Central financial assistance provided to the States/ UTs for development of fishing harbours and fish landing centres during the last three years (2019-20) are furnished at Annexure- IV.  The State- wise details of fishing harbours/fish landing centres constructed and commissioned with Central financial assistance are furnished at Annexure- V.*****
