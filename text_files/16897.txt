                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

602






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Speed Post Centres




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the State/UT-wise total number of operational Speed Post Centres in the country; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to set up more Speed Post Centres in the country during the current financial year; (c) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (d) 	whether all operational Speed Post Centres are both receiving and delivery centres; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) 	The Department of Posts, under a new operational arrangement has changed the nomenclature of Speed Post Centres to National Sorting Hubs (NSHs) and Intra Circle Hubs (ICHs). As on date there are 90 NSHs and 151 ICHs. The State-wise details are at Annexure-I.(b) & (c) 	As of now no proposal for creation of National Speed Post Hub is under consideration.(d) & (e) 	NSHs process the incoming and outgoing articles. Articles are delivered through delivery post offices.
