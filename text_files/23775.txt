                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4778






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Setting up of Industry




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any Industry is being set up at Banda and Chitrakoot districts including Bundelkhand of Uttar Pradesh;(b) 	if so, the details and locations thereof;(c) 	the time by which the said industry is likely to be established; and(d) 	if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):	As per information received from Department of Industries, Government of Uttar Pradesh, a total number of 3,146 MSMEs have been established in Banda district and 4,372 MSMEs have been established in Chitrakoot district till 30.06.2020. In Bundelkhand region, total 27,886 MSME units have been established. Further, total 26 MOUs have been signed for Bundelkhand region. The progress of     13 MOUs is as given: 05 industrial units in Jhansi district (4 MSME and    1 Heavy) have started commercial operations; 05 (4 MSMEs and         1 Heavy) are under various stages of implementation in Jhansi         (2), Lalitpur (2) and Jalaun districts and likely to be completed by December 2023; and 03 units in Chitrakoot (1) and Hamirpur (2) categorized as heavy are likely to be completed by 2023. The remaining 13 MoUs have shown intent to invest but no progress has been informed.   (b):		The requisite details and location are as stated under: <pre>S. No.	District	Udyog Aadhar Regd.	Micro	Small	Medium1.	Banda	3146	2834	306	62.	Chitrakoot	4372	4216	154	23.	Hamirpur	2625	2480	140	54.	Jalaun	2959	2780	160	195.	Jhansi	10033	9236	774	236.	Lalitpur	3648	3465	180	37.	Mahoba	1103	831	262	10 	Total	27,886	25,842	1976	68</pre>(c) & (d): 	The industrial units under various stages of implementation are likely to be established by 2023. 13 MoUs for which intent to investment has been shown, specific time lines are not available with the Department. *****
