                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

245






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Production of Fruits and Vegetables




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has set any target for production of fruits and vegetables in different States of the country for the next five years and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of production of fruits and vegetables in different States during the last three years and the current year; and(c) 	the details of likely assistance to be provided by the Government to promote fruit production during the next five years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The Government of India does not fix specific targets for production of Fruits and Vegetables for the country. It is for the respective State Governments to consider. The funds are allocated to State Governments under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for holistic growth of the horticulture sector, as per state Action Plans with respect to area expansion of Fruits and Vegetables in their respective states.(b):	The State-wise production of  Fruits and Vegetables during 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20(1stAdvance Estimates), are at Annexures I and II,  respectively.(c):	MIDH, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme is being implemented with effect from 2014-15, for holistic growth of the horticulture sector covering Fruits, Vegetables, Root and Tuber crops, Mushrooms, Spices, Flowers, Aromatic Plants, Coconut, Cashew & Cocoa and has been extended till the expiry of 14th Finance Commission i.e. by 2020-21. An amount of Rs. 2160.25 crore has been allocated for MIDH scheme for 2020-21.
