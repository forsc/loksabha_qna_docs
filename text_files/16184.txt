                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya in Manipur




Lorho S. Pfoze














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of blocks identified as Educationally Backward Blocks (EBBs) in Manipur by the Government of India;  (b) the number of Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (KGBVs) established in Manipur along with their details such as location and the fund spent on them during the last three years; and  (c) whether there is any proposal to open more KGBVs in Manipur and if so, the details thereof? ANSWER 
                                                





ANSWER




Minister of Human Resource Development (Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’)              (a) Department of School Education & Literacy (SE&L) has identified the following as Educationally Backward Blocks (EBBs) in the state of Manipur: <pre>S. No. District NAME 1 Tamenglong Tousem T.D. Block 2 Chandel Chakpikarong T.D.Block 3 Chandel Machi T.D.Block 4 Chandel Tengnoupal T.D.Block 5 Churachandpur Henglep T.D.Block </pre> (b) 16 KGBVs have been sanctioned in the State of Manipur upto 2019-20 as per list given below: <pre> Sl. No District Name Block Name KGBV Name 1 Chandel Toupokpi, Chakpikarong Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya, Toupokpi 2 Churachandpur Tuilaphai, Henglep Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya, Tuilaphai 3 Machi Langol, Machi Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya, Langol Khullen 4 Tengnoupal Aimol Khullen, Tengnoupal Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya  Aimol Khullen 5 Tamenglong New Mandeu, Tousem Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya 6 Bishnupur Thamnapokpi, Moirang Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya, Moirang 7 Kangpokpi Ekou Mullam, Saikul Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya 8 Imphal East Loupaochum, Andro Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya 9 Imphal West KonchakYangbi, Wangoi Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya 10 Thoubal Ukhongshang, Thoubal Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya 11 Ukhrul Poi, Chingai Poi KGBV School, Ukhrul 12 Churachandpur Henglep Henglep H/S 13 Chandel Machi KGBV Centre Langol Khullen 14 Chandel Tengnoupal KGBV Centre Aimol Khullen 15 Tamenglong Tousem KGBV New Mandu 16 Chandel Chakpikarong KGBV Centre Toupokpi </pre> The Status of funds approved and spent on KGBVs in Manipur State during the last three years is given below: <pre>(Rs. in lakh) 2016-17 2017-18 2018-19 Outlay Approved Expenditure Outlay Approved Expenditure Outlay Approved Expenditure 735.60 874.49 618.29 520.89 1068.98 718.86   </pre> (c) All the EBBs in the State have already been covered with KGBVs.  *****
