                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

18






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






National Council Vocational Training Courses under PMKVY




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has conducted any National Council Vocational Training (NCVT) Courses/Short term skill courses under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) across the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof since inception, State/UT-wise along with the number of youth benefited from the said courses so far across the country, State/UT-wise;(c)	the number of Engineering Colleges/ Polytechnics identified and affiliated to run the said courses so far across the country, State/UT-wise;(d)	the cases of irregularities in implementing the said scheme which have come to the notice of the Government during the said period along with action taken thereon; and(e)	the other steps taken by the Government for training and skill development of youth and to ensure their employment in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.	   STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (E) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 18 TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.02.2020 ASKED BY SHRI BHARTRUHARI MAHTAB REGARDING NATIONAL COUNCIL VOCATIONAL TRAINING COURSES UNDER PMKVY(a) and (b)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) for imparting Vocational Courses (Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)) to the prospective youths of the Country. Under the scheme, as on 17.01.2020, 73.47 lakh (appx.) (40.27 lakh STT + 33.20 lakh RPL) candidates have been trained/oriented in around 371 courses throughout the country. The State/UT – wise details of Short Term Training and RPL of PMKVY (2016-20) are given at Annexure I and II, respectively.(c)	Under PMKVY, ‘Best in Class Academic Institutes’ are empanelled to run Vocational Courses in various job roles. The detail of such empanelled institutes is given at Annexure III.(d)	Under the monitoring framework of PMKVY (2016-20), as on 13.01.2020, number of 304 Training Centre (TCs) have been suspended due to non compliance of PMKVY operational guidelines/irregularities.  (e)	Government has promoted industry to design competency based curriculum and standard for skill training courses. For this, Government has constituted industry – led body known as Sector Skill Councils (SSCs). SSCs in consultation with sector expert and relevant academia assess skill needs of various job roles and develop job specific standards called Qualification Packs – National Occupational Standards (QP-NOS). These QP-NOS are aligned to competency based framework known as National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF). MSDE through Directorate General of Training (DGT) has introduced Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) for ensuring a steady flow of skilled workers in different trades. CTS is being implemented through Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) for imparting vocational /skill training courses under the domain of long term training (LTT). MSDE is also implementing the Scheme of Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) for imparting skill development training to non-literate, neo-literate and person of Socio-economically and educationally backward group. Skills Career Counselling Scheme known as Skill Saathi is being implementing to counsel the youths to make the right choice of training and subsequent employment/entrepreneurship.Under PMKVY (2016-20), various provisions/initiatives are being taken to enhance placement of the trained candidates. These includes organization of Rozgar Mela with the support of Sector Skill Councils, 20% of total training payout (last tranche) to TCs based on placement, post placement support of Rs. 1500 per month per trainee is applicable for special areas/special groups, implementation of employer led training model, reallocation of target to TCs based on placement performance etc. Annexure IState/UT – wise candidates trained, assessed, certified and placed under Short term Training of PMKVY (2016-20), as on 17.01.2020, are given below:S.
