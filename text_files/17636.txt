                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1329






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Labour Courts/Tribunals




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the total number of labour courts/tribunals set up across the country especially in Gujarat and West Bengal; and(b)the total number of cases of industrial disputes filed, disposed and lying pending in the labour courts/ tribunals in the country in general and especially in Gujarat during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): 	22 Central Government Industrial Tribunals-cum-Labour Courts (CGIT-cum-LCs) have been set up under Section 7A of the Industrial Dispute Act, 1947 across the country including  one CGIT-cum-LC at Ahmedabad (Gujarat) and two 2 CGIT-cum-LCs  at Asansol and Kolkata (West Bengal).(b): 	Details of industrial disputes filed, disposed of and lying pending in each CGIT-cum-LC during the last three years are at Annexure-I.*                                                  ******
