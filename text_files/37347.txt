                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Incentives to Cement an Steel Plants




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has given any incentives to cement and steel plants in the country, particularly in Andhra Pradesh; and(b) 	if so, the details of the funds allocated and released State-wise, particularly in Anantapur district of Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b): 	Government of India provides an enabling ecosystem of overall industrial development in the country through appropriate policy interventions.As development of industry is primarily the domain of State Government, Government of Andhra Pradesh has stated that they have provided incentives to Cement and Steel Plants in Andhra Pradesh as per the policies of Target 2000, Industrial Investment Promotion Policy (IIPP) 2005-10, IIPP 2010-15 and Industrial Development Policy (IDP)        2015-20 and same is also continue in APIDP 2020-23.********
