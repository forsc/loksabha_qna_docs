                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Circuits Based on State’s Cultural Heritage




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle













                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    B. Y. Raghavendra
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the ministry is planning to start any circuits based on the cultural heritage of the States; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM				(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	The Ministry of Tourism, under the SwadeshDarshan scheme is developing thematic circuits in the country in planned and prioritized manner. Under this scheme,  heritage  is one  of the 15 identified  themes.  The detail of projects sanctioned under Heritage Circuit of SwadeshDarshan scheme are annexed.  ******* ANNEXURE  STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4 ANSWERED ON 29.11.2021 REGARDING CIRCUITS BASED ON STATE’S CULTURAL HERITAGE THE DETAILS OF PROJECTS SANCTIONED UNDER HERITAGE CIRCUIT OF SWADESH DARSHAN SCHEME.(Amt. in Crore)S.No.	Name of State	Name of theme & Year	Project Name	Amt.
