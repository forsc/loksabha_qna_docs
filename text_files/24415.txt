                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

456






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Food Grain Godown




Malook Nagar













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of godowns FCI currently operating in the country including those constructed after 2014 along with the number of farmers benefitting from them, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) 	whether the Government has checked that these godowns are sufficient for the procurement and storage of food grains for the farmers of Bihar, if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the reaction of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	As on 30.06.2021, Food Corporation of India (FCI) is operating 2323 warehouses (owned & hired) all over the country.  Details of godowns including those constructed after 2014 is at Annexure-I.State-wise details of number of farmers benefitting from Paddy and Wheat procurement operations since 2015 is at Annexure-II.(b): 	Being a Decentralized Procuring (DCP) State, procurement of paddy & wheat is done by the State Govt.Approximately 4.26 LMT capacity is available with the State Government of Bihar for storage of Wheat & Rice. In addition to the above capacity, 12.18 LMT (Covered – 11.18 LMT and CAP – 1.00 LMT) storage capacity is available with FCI (as on 30.06.2021) for storage of foodgrain meant for National Food Security Act (NFSA) in Bihar.  (c): 	FCI continuously assesses and monitors the storage capacity and based on the requirement and storage gap assessment, storage capacities are created / hired under following schemes :-1.	Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) scheme.2.	Construction of Silos  3.	Hiring of godowns from Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC)/ State Warehousing Corporations (SWCs)4.
