                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

89






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






CSR Fund




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any data regarding Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds spent by Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs)/ Public Sector Enterprises (PSEs) and corporates during the last three years;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the number of women organizations or societies or women benefitted from the CSR? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a)	& (b): As per information furnished by Ministry of Corporate Affairs on the basis of filings made by the companies in MCA 21 registry, the CSR amount spent by all companies, including PSUs during the last three years (upto 30.09.2020) is given below:PSU/Non PSU	Total Company FY 2017-18	Amount Spent during FY 2017-18 (Rs in crore)	Total Company FY 2018-19	Amount Spent during FY 2018-19 (Rs in crore)	Total Company FY 2019-20	Amount Spent during FY 2019-20 (Rs in crore)PSU	535	2704.32	609	3835.50	14	438.29Non PSU	20915	11185.53	24323	14819.32	1061	7384.21Grand Total	21450	13889.86	24932	18654.82	1075	7822.50(c): The benefits of CSR activities undertaken by Central Public Sector Enterprises include women however, no separate details on number of women organizations/ societies / women is maintained centrally by the Department. *******
