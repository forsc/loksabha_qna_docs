                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

784






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Four-Laning Projects in Karnataka




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken up the works of four-laning projects in the State of Karnataka and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the said projects are running behind schedule;(c)	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government has undertaken any measures to review the work schedule of the said projects; and(e)	if so, the details thereof along with the time frame set for paying the supplementary award to landowners with due interest?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d) 14 projects for 4-laning in the State of Karnataka comprising length of about 1,209 km with total cost of about Rs. 17,263 crores are in different stages of implementation. The list of projects along with progress is provided at Annexure. Out of 14 projects, 06 projects are running behind schedule due to delay in land acquisition, forest clearance, COVID-19 pandemic etc. and stoppage of work by local residents for want of higher compensation etc. Several meetings have been conducted at various levels for expeditious completion of the highway projects.  (e) The payment of supplementary awards to the land owners has been planned to be completed by December, 2020.
