                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1923






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Healthcare under Ayushman Bharat Scheme




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that Ayushman Bharat Yojana seeks to provide a benefit cover of Rs. 5,00,000/- per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization;(b) if so, the details of the number of claims made all over India under the Yojana since 2018 to till September 2019, Statewise;(c) whether it is also a fact that around 1,50,000 Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) are to be established under Ayushman Bharat Yojana and the number of such HWCs established in the State of Odisha; and(d) the number of HWCs established since 2018 to date, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) provides health cover of Rs. 5,00,000/- per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization to the entitled beneficiaries. (b):	The State-wise number of claims under the AB PMJAY are at Annexure-I.(c):	Under Ayushman Bharat , 1.5 lakh Sub Health Centres and Primary Health Centres are to be transformed into Health and Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs) for provision of comprehensive primary care. On 25.11.2019, as per the AB-HWC portal, 1006 AB-HWCs are functional in Odisha.(d):	State-wise details of functional HWC as on 25.11.2019 is at Annexure II.
