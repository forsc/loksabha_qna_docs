                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Cities/Towns Covered under AMRUT




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of cities/towns covered under Atal Mission for 	Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) in Bihar to 	promote planned integrated development of cities; and(b) 	the details of the financial outlay for AMRUT projects during 	the year 2021-22?






ANSWER




                 THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):	A total of 27 cities /town of the State of Bihar have been covered under Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT).(b):	Under AMRUT, financial outlay for projects have been earmarked for the entire Mission period. It has not been earmarked year-wise.  The total approved State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) for all the States/ Union Territories (UTs) for the entire Mission period amounts to ?77,640 crore, which include ?35,990 crore of committed Central Assistance.  So far, Central Assistance amounting to ?30,295 crore have been released to States/ UTs for AMRUT projects.The approved SAAP of State of Bihar amounts to ?2,469.77 crore, which include committed Central Assistance of ?1,164.80 crore.  So far, Central Assistance amounting to ?1,055.87 crore have been released to the State of Bihar for AMRUT projects.                                                ******
