                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






PMGSY in Madhya Pradesh




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of projects under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) which have been successfully implemented by the State Government of Madhya Pradesh during the last three years and the current year;(b) 	the total number of cases related to rural development that are still pending in Madhya Pradesh;(c) 	the reasons for pendency of these cases; and(d) 	the time by which the same are likely to be settled?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) 	Details of projects completed during the last three years and the current year in the State of Madhya Pradesh is as under:-Year	           Sanctioned	                      Completed	     No. of Roads	No. of LSBs	No. of Roads*	No. of LSBs2017-18	        913	           233	              1692	    522018-19           1	           236	               859	   1402019-20	        112	            31	               694	   1712020-21	        377	           167	               261	   185TOTAL	       1403	           667	              3506	   548LSB= Long Span Bridge* Completed Roads includes those sanctioned earlier. (b) 	Vertical-wise details of pending works in the State is as under:- Vertical	Sanctioned	           Completed	              Balance as on 18.03.2021	      No. of Roads No. of LSBs	  No. of Roads No. of LSBs     No. of Roads No. of LSBsPMGSY-I	       18951	       659	     18846	    492	           105	       167PMGSY-II	 374	       245	       343	    192	            31	        53PMGSY-III	 485	       194	         3	      4	           482	       190RCPLWEA	           6	        14	         0	      8	             6	         6TOTAL	       19816	      1112	     19192	    696	           624	       416 (c) & (d)    Works under PMGSY-I, II and RCPLWEA are pending mainly due to the issues related to forest clearance, availability of land, lack of response to tenders, etc. As reported by State Government, all the pending works under PMGSY-I & II are proposed for completion by March, 2022. Works under RCPLWEA are proposed for completion by March, 2023. Works of PMGSY-III are recently sanctioned and will be completed as per programme guidelines.*****
