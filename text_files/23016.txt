                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3912






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Aanganwadi Buildings




Ashok Kumar Rawat













                                                    Narendra Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) functioning in the country along with the funds allocated by the Government to these AWCs during the last three years, State/UT-wise including Rajasthan;  (b) 	whether the Government has taken note that most of these Anganwadi Kendras are functioning in dilapidated/damaged buildings and if so, the total number of such buildings along with the time by which funds are likely to be released for their repair, State/UT-wise;  (c) 	the number AWCs operating from rented building in the country, State/UT-wise; and  (d) 	the number of new Anganwadi Kendras proposed to be set up at present along with the time by which these Kendras are likely to be set up, State/UT-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) As on 30th September, 2020, there are 13,86,990 operational Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) across the country. State/UT-wise number of operational AWCs and funds allocated for these AWCs during last three years is at Annex-I. (b)  Government releases funds for repair/maintenance of AWC buildings (Govt. owned).(c)  As on 30th September, 2020, there are 3,58,446 AWCs run from rented premises across the country. State/UT-wise number of AWC running from rented premises is at Annex-II.(d)  Presently, there is no proposal to set up new Anganwadi Centres. ***** Annex-I ANNEXURE REFRRED IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3912 FOR 19.03.2021 REGARDING “AANGANWADI BUILDINGS” ASKED BY SHRI ASHOK KUMAR RAWAT AND SHRI NARENDRA KUMAR.
