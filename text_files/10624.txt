                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Inclusion of Bhopal under Smart City Mission




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Bhopal city of Madhya Pradesh has been included under the Smart City Mission;(b)	 if so, the details of the Detailed Project Report (DPR) in this regard and the amount of budget released during the last four years;(c) 	whether the utilisation certificate supported by audited statement of report of the said budget has been received and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	 whether the proposal to modify the sanctioned DPR of Bhopal smart city has been received and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	 to (c) Yes, Sir. The City of Bhopal was selected to be developed as  Smart City in Round-I in January, 2016. For the Smart City of Bhopal, the Government of India have released Rs. 490 crore to the State Government of Madhya Pradesh out of which the State has transferred Rs. 360.28 crore to Bhopal Smart City. As against the requirement of contributing matching share, the State Government of Madhya Pradesh has released Rs. 200 crore to the Bhopal Smart City and as on 29th November, 2019, the Bhopal Smart City has reported utilization of Rs. 392 crore.(d) The Smart Cities Mission has not received any proposal for modifying the SCP of Bhopal Smart City.                                        *****
