                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Shelter Homes for Transgender Children




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3231TO BE ANSWERED ON 16.03.2021SHELTER HOMES FOR TRANSGENDER CHILDREN 3231. SHRI P.C. MOHAN:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to establish shelter homes for transgender children in need of care and protection across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the places identified for the purpose; and (c) the time by which such shelter homes are likely to be made operational?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) : As of now, there is no proposal for setting-up of shelter homes for Transgender children.(b): Does not arise.(c): Does not arise.*****
