                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






National Digital Policy




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the National Digital Policy has been implemented; (b) 	if so, the details of State-wise implementation of the National Digital Policy including Odisha; and (c) 	the total amount of fund sanctioned and actually utilized in implementing this policy during the last financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b) 	The National Digital Communications Policy-2018 (NDCP-2018) is implemented since 2018 with the aim to support India’s transition to a digitally empowered economy and society. The details of State-wise status (including Odisha) of various telecom parameters as on 30 June 2021, published by TRAI are given in Annexure.(c) 	NDCP-2018 is not a Scheme, but a policy document which does not has a separately sanctioned head under budget.
