                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2786






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Construction of Toilets




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether toilets have been constructed in the State of Odisha after commencement of the scheme of ‘Clean India Toilet’;(b) if so, the details thereof including the number of toilets constructed;(c) whether the funds allocated by the Government have been utilized completely;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) 	There is no such scheme with the name of ‘Clean India Toilet’. However, 68,36,650 individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed in Odisha under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) [SBM(G)] as on 02-12-2019 as per the data reported by the State on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS).(c) to (e) 	Year-wise details of funds released by the Government of India to Odisha under SBM(G) and their utilization by the State as per the Utilization Certificates (UCs)/Provisional UCs furnished by the State are given below:(Rs. in crore)Financial Year	Funds released	Funds utilized2014-15	65.84	83.772015-16	571.50	525.852016-17	863.65	839.842017-18	457.02	574.152018-19	1367.62	1066.792019-20	1021.16	616.11Total	4346.79	3706.51Funds released during 2019-20 includes the amount of Rs.636.50 crore released in third week of November, 2019.
