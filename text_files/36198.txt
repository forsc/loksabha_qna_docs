                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3601






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Ultra Mega Solar Power Projects




Chandra Sekhar Sahu













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had proposed to set up at least 25 Solar Parks and Ultra Mega Solar Power Projects targeting over 20,000 MW of solar power installed capacity within a span of five years starting from 2014-15 in the country;(b) if so, the number of Solar Parks and Ultra Mega Solar Power Projects so far set up during the said period along with their installed as well as actual capacity, State-wise including Odisha and Maharashtra;(c) whether the Government has enhanced the production capacity of the scheme in 2017;(d) if so, the extent to which it has so far been enhanced and target achieved therefrom, State-wise;(e) whether the Government is implementing any scheme which envisages supporting the States/UTs in setting up solar parks at various locations; and(f) if so, the funds sanctioned and allocated to various States including Odisha and Maharashtra under the scheme so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (f) The Government had launched the scheme in December, 2014 to set up 25 solar parks with a cumulative capacity of 20000 MW by 31.03.2019 (Phase-I). The capacity target was enhanced to 40,000 MW in March, 2017 to be achieved by 31.03.2024 (Phase-II). As on 28.02.2022, the Government have sanctioned 56 solar parks of cumulative 38,266 MW capacity in 15 States in the country under the scheme. State-wise and solar park wise details of capacity sanctioned, capacity installed and the funds released including for Odisha and Maharashtra under the scheme are given at Annexure.*****
