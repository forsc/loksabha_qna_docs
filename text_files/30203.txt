                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

171






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






National Highways in Karnataka




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total length of the National Highways (NHs) constructed in the State of Karnataka during last five years;(b)	the impediments faced by the Government in implementing the NH projects in the State; and(c)	the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to remove these impediments and complete the projects on time?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)  (a) to (c)               A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
