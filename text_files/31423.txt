                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2587






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Registered Societies




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Sunil Kumar Soni
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of societies registered in various States across the country and the number of societies functioning out of the said societies;(b)	the amount-wise grant released for the development of the said societies and the names of cooperative societies to which it has been given under it during the last five years details thereof, year-wise; and(c)	whether the Government proposes to promote cooperative sector in the future if so, the sectors thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION??????????????    (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a): As per Statistical Profile - 2018 published by National Cooperative Union of India (NCUI), there are 854355 Co-operative Societies registered with various States/Union Territories. Details are given at Annexure – I. (b): The Government administers the Central Sector Integrated Scheme on Agricultural Cooperation (CSISAC) for the development & promotion, education & training of cooperatives in the country. The grants under the scheme are provided to the implementing agencies namely; National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC), National Council for Cooperative Training (NCCT) and National Cooperative Union of India (NCUI).Besides this, National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has released grants out of its Cooperative Development Fund. Details for the last five years are given at Annexure – II. (c):Yes Sir.
