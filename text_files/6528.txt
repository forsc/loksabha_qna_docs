                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

34






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






National Institutes for Differently Abled Children




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the National Institutes imparting special education to the visually impaired, hearing impaired, speech impaired and mentally challenged children in the country;(b)	the details of the facilities being provided by the said institutes along with the number of such children receiving education at present;(c)	the amount of funds provided to these institutes during each of the last three years, institute-wise along with the total amount utilized;(d)	whether special education is being provided to all such children, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e)	the details of the plan/scheme for providing employment to all these children after imparting education to them?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI THAAWAR CHAND GEHLOT)(a)	to (e): A statement is laid on the table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to part (a) to (e) of the Lok Sabha Starred Question No.34 raised by Shri Sumedhanand Sarswati and Shri Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangare regarding “National Institutes for Differently Abled Children”.(a) & (b) There are nine National Institutes (NIs) working under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) (Divyangjan) specializing in different kinds of disabilities.  The main objectives of these Institutes are to train rehabilitation personnel in the field of disabilities, to provide rehabilitation services to Persons with Disabilities and to undertake Research and Development. Out of these nine NIs, three NIs, namely National Institute for the Empowerment of Persons with Visual Disabilities (NIEPVD), Dehradun, National Institute for the Empowerment of Persons with Intellectual Disabilities  (NIEPID), Secunderabad and National Institute for Empowerment of Persons with Multiple Disabilities (NIEPMD), Chennai are also running the following schools/centres for children with disabilities:- Name of the National Institute	Name of School/Centre	Number of students National Institute for the  Empowerment of Persons with Visual Disabilities (NIEPVD), Dehradun	(i) Model School for the Visually Impaired, Dehradun	241	(ii) Hill Model of Inclusive Education Centre at Uttarkashi (Uttarakhand) Shillong (Meghalaya) and Dharmatul (Assam)	196	(iii) The Institute’s Regional Chapter at Kolkata runs a school programme for children in the age group of 3 to 5 years	22	(iv) Home based functional education in the slum areas of  Hooghly district of West Bengal	48National Institute for the Empowerment of Persons with Intellectual Disabilities  (NIEPID), Secunderabad	(i) A Special Education Centre at its Headquarters, Secunderabad	189	(ii) A Model Special Education Centre at New Delhi	80	(iii) A Model Special Education Centre at NOIDA for mentally retarded children.	85National Institute for Empowerment of Persons with Multiple Disabilities (NIEPMD), Chennai	(i) NIEPMD Special Model School.	107	(ii) Inclusive Play (Preparatory School)	22These Institutes are providing facilities like; transportation, accommodation, food, uniform, books, stationery, medical and therapeutic services.(c) The details of funds released and utilized by National Institutes during the last three years are as under:-Rs. in croreS.No	Name of NIs	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19		Released	Utilized	Released	Utilized	Released	Utilized1.
