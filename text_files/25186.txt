                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

974






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






National Child Labour Project




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the objectives of the National Child Labour Project (NCLP) and the details of the fund provided by the Union Government to the concerned states under the said scheme, work-wise;(b)whether the services of private organisations, NGOs are also likely to be taken on contract in the form of project organisation, executive agencies in the said project on contract basis;(c)if so, the names of the private organizations, NGOs in Rajasthan roped in for executing work under the scheme from 1st January, 2018 till date and the works for which the allocation was made from the budget; and(d)the names of the districts in which works were carried out organisation-wise, NGOs-wise, along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		The Ministry of Labour and Employment is  implementing the National Child Labour Project (NCLP) Scheme with the objective of  rehabilitation of child labourers. Under the NCLP Scheme, the  working children in the age group of 9-14 years are rescued / withdrawn from work and enrolled in the NCLP Special Training Centres, where they are provided with bridge education, vocational training, mid-day meal, Contd..2/-::  2  ::stipend, health care, etc. before being mainstreamed into formal education system.   During 2020-21, a total amount of Rs. 41.19 Crore  was released as Grant-in-Aid to the District Project Societies   for running Special Training Centers  and stipend to enrolled children under NCLP Scheme.  (b) to (d):	Under NCLP Scheme, funds are released by Ministry of Labour and Employment directly to the District Project Societies, set up  under the chairmanship of concerned District Magistrate/Dy. Commissioner / District Collector of the district under administrative control of the State government.   Ministry of Labour and Employment has released grant-in-aid to   5 number of districts  viz. Alwar, Banswara, Baran, Bhilwara  and Dausa  in Rajasthan  to implement NCLP  Scheme since 01.01.2018 till date. The Ministry does not release  funds under NCLP Scheme directly to  Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs). The District Project Society identifies the implementing agencies (NGOs/Civil Societies etc.) and releases fund to them to operate Special Training Centres.    *****
