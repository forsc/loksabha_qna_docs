                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Setting up of Fishing Harbours




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of fishing harbours set up in the country as of now, State-wise; (b) whether the Government has any proposal to set up more fishing harbours; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) whether there is any proposal to set up committees of fishermen to operate such harbours and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                         ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) :      A statement indicating state-wise details of the number of fishing harbours set up in the country with finance assistance from  Government of India (GoI) is  furnished at Annexure-I.(b) and  (c): Yes Sir. The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has launched a new flagship scheme namely Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) with an estimated investment of Rs.20,050 crore for implementation for a period of  five years from FY-2020-21 to 2024-25 for the overall development of Fisheries Sector  in the country. PMMSY inter alia envisages development of fishing harbours and fish landing centres with substantial investments to the tune of Rs.3490 crores. Further, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is also implementing the Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF) under which concessional finance is provided to the State/Union Territory governments to develop fishing harbours and landing centres. Till date, 14 fishing harbour projects worth of Rs 3451.32 crores have been approved under FIDF.  (d) :  State/UTs Governments are responsible for post construction management, maintenance and operation of the fishing harbours under their control and have arrangements for management of their fishing harbours. The fishing harbours under the control of central government are under the aegis of Port Trusts, which in turn manage them.  In view of the existing arrangements, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has no proposal for setting up Committees of fishermen to operate fishing harbours.*****
