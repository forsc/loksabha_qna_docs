                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

547






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana




Harish Dwivedi













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to implement the Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana (RVY) in the entire country and if so, the details thereof; (b) the names of the districts in Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan covered/likely to be benefited under the said scheme; (c) whether the Government proposes to expand the facilities available under Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government proposes to amend the guidelines/provisions of the RVY; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has been implementing a Central Sector Scheme namely “Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana” (RVY) for providing Physical Aids and Assisted Living Devices to Senior Citizens belonging to BPL category since 1st April, 2017 with the objective to restore near normalcy in their bodily functions. The Scheme is implemented through the ‘Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation (ALIMCO)’, (a Public Sector Undertaking under the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment) as the sole implementing Agency. List of selected districts in Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan under RVY is at Annexure-I.(c): At present, total 325 districts from all State/UTs Administration have been selected for the implementation of RVY and it is proposed to quickly expand to all other districts too.(d) & (e): As on date, there is no proposal to amend the guidelines/provisions of the RVY.****
