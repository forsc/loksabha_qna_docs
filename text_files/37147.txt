                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4493






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Lateral Recruitment Scheme




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has conducted an evaluation of the Lateral Recruitment Scheme(LSR) viz-a-viz Recruitment through Civil Services and other examinations; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the recruitment through LSR is in addition to the promotions given to the traditional recruits through Civil Services and Other Examinations; (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the proportion in which the vacancies at the Joint Secretary and Director Level are divided amongst traditional recruits and lateral entrants?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (e):  Lateral recruitment provides for appointment of persons for specific assignments in the Government, keeping in view their specialized knowledge and expertise in the domain area. The selection was undertaken by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), on the basis of transparent process, inviting applications through open advertisement.  Under lateral recruitment, candidates are appointed on deputation/contract basis, for a period of 3 years. At present, 10 Joint Secretaries and 18 Directors are in position in various Ministries/Departments through lateral recruitment.*****
