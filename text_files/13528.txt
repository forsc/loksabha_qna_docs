                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2355






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Inter-linking of Cauveri-Vaigai-Gundar Rivers




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has any proposal for linking up of Cauveri, Vaigai and Gundar rivers in Tamil Nadu; (b) if so, the funds allocated for the task; and (c) whether the Government of Tamil Nadu has made any request for approval of the said linking of rivers and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b)	 The Feasibility Report of the Cauvery – Vaigai – Gundar link project was prepared by National Water Development Agency (NWDA) in the year 2004 and circulated to the concerned States. Cauvery-Vaigai-Gundar link project is the last link project under Mahanadi-Godavari-Krishna-Pennar-Cauvery-Vaigai-Gundar link system under Peninsular Component of National Perspective Plan. The feasibility of Cauvery – Vaigai – Gundar link project depends on the waters to be brought by preceding upper links from Mahanadi & Godavari rivers.No funds have been allocated / utilized for execution of this project.(c)	   Hon’ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu has requested the Central Government to accord in-principle approval for the Cauvery-Gundar  link project.*****
