                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2623






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Rate of Broadband Services




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to reduce the rate of broadband services; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (c) 	if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) to (c)	As per the existing tariff regulatory framework, tariff for telecommunication services is under forbearance except for leased circuits, national roaming, rural fixed line services and mobile number portability. The service providers have the flexibility to design and offer tariff by taking into account several factors including, inter-alia, input costs, level of competition, their understanding of prevailing market situation and their commercial interest. ********
