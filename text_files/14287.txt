                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Selling of SCI




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided to disinvest Shipping Corporation of India (SCI) and if so, the details thereof along with the percentage of stake to be sold;(b)	whether the Government had appointed Assets Valuers for valuation of all assets of SCI and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether Assets Valuers has submitted its report to the Government and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government is likely to consult all stakeholders along with Employees Unions before making disinvestment in SCI and if so, the response of the stakeholders in this regard; and(e)	the time by which it is likely to be disinvested?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes,   the Government of India has decided to disinvest Shipping Corporation of India (SCI). On 20th November, 2019, the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) had given its ‘in principle’ approval for strategic disinvestment of Government of India shareholding of 63.75% in Shipping Corporation of India Ltd. along with transfer of management control to a strategic buyer. (b)	 to (e)  Department of Investment and Public Asset Management (DIPAM) has appointed M/s RBSA Capital Advisor LLP as Transaction Advisor (TA), M/s Luthra & Luthra Partners as Legal Advisor (LA) and M/s Protocol Insurance Surveyors & Loss Assessors Pvt. Ltd. as Asset Valuer. As per the current procedure the Asset Valuer and Transaction Advisor submit their Asset Valuation Reports and Business Valuation Reports close to the fixation of Reserve Price by the Government at the time of submission of financial bids by the prospective bidders.                                             *****
