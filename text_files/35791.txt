                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2620






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Subsidy on Domestic LPG




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the subsidy on domestic cooking gas has been abolished in the country and if so, the details thereof along with the criteria fixed by the Government to avail LPG subsidy by consumers and;(b) the corrective steps taken by the Government for release of the subsidy to LPG consumers in a time-bound manner?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	The prices of petroleum products in the country are linked to the price of respective products in the international market. However, the Government continues to modulate the effective price to consumer for Subsidized Domestic LPG.  The selling price and subsidy on domestic cooking gas increases/ decreases as the product price increases/decreases in international market and decision of Government on subsidy. The subsidy, as admissible, is credited to the bank account of eligible beneficiaries. x-x-x-x-x
