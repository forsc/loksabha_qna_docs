                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1026






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






PMGSY in Chhattisgarh




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of fund allocated, sanctioned and utilized under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years and current year in the State of Chhattisgarh, district-wise;(b) 	the current status of sanctioned projects during the above period;(c) 	whether the Government has sanctioned the implementation of third phase of PMGSY;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	the number of proposals for Rajnandgaon and Kabirdham districts in Chhattisgarh State?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE)(a)        The funds for the implementation of the scheme are released by the Ministry to the State as a whole.  The funds to the States are released on the basis of works in hand, their execution capacity & performance and unspent balances of the previous releases available with them.
