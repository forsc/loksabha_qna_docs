                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1958






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Terrorists Killed in J&K




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is an increase in number of terrorists killed in Jammu & Kashmir during the current year as compared to previous years; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the number of terrorists killed during the last five years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  Yes, Sir.  The Security Forces are combating the terrorists activities both along the LC by eliminating the infiltration bids and in hinterland by carrying out Counter Terrorist Operations and responding to Terrorist initiated incidents.(b)  Sustained synergised operations conducted by Security Forces have resulted in elimination of large number of terrorists.  Number of terrorists killed by the Security Forces in Counter Infiltration Operations, Counter Terrorist Operations and while responding to Terrorist initiated incidents in the State of Jammu and Kashmir in last five years are as under:-Sl. No.	Year	Details of Terrorist killed(a)	2014	104(b)	2015	97(c)	2016	140(d)	2017	210(e)	2018	249*******
