                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Upgraded Anganwadi Centres




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of Anganwadi centres along with the number of Anganwadi centres upgraded so far across the country, State/UT-wise including Jharkhand, district-wise;(b) 	the details of upgradation plans proposed, the funds allocated per Anganwadi centre and the proposed timeline for completion of upgradation across the country, State/UT-wise including Jharkhand; and(c)	the details of the number of Anganwadi workers and helpers currently working in Jharkhand, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILDDEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	State/UT-wise number of Anganwadi Centres along with the number of Anganwadi Centres for which grants for up-gradation has been released is at Annexure-I. District-wise number of AWCs constructed/up-graded in the State of Jharkhand is at Annexure-II,(b)	For the current financial year, an amount of Rs.271.40 crore has been allocated for up-gradation of 13570 Anganwadi Centre (AWC) buildings including 500 AWC buildings in Jharkhand @ Rs.2.00 lakh per AWC building. State-wise details of units approved and amount allocated for up-gradation of AWC building is at Annexure-III. (c)	As on date, there are 37,856 Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) and 35,303 Anganwadi Helpers (AWHs) working in the State of Jharkhand.
