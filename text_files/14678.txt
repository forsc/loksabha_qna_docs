                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3417






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Number of Weavers




Nishikant  Dubey













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the number of weavers in the country, State-wise including Jharkhand and Uttar Pradesh as on date;(b) whether the Government has sanctioned/released money for execution of various schemes for weavers;(c) if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(d) the steps being taken by the Government to ensure optimum use of sanctioned/released fund to benefit weavers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d):    According to 4th All India Handloom Census there are 35,22,512 handloom workers in all over India including Jharkhand and Uttar Pradesh State. Detail of State-wise no. of handloom workers is enclosed in Annexure- “A”.Budgetary allocation of funds is not made State-wise. The funds are released to eligible agencies based on the viable proposals received and utilization of previous release funds. The Office of the Development Commissioner for Handlooms sanctions and releases funds to the eligible handloom agencies for implementation of various schemes.  Details of funds released in the last three years and the current year for various schemes are as under: -Scheme-wise funds released in the last three years and current yearSl. In crore)(as on 04-03-2020)1	National Handloom Development               Programme (NHDP)	153.56	135.05	119.72	132.842	Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS)	40.11	31.82	16.38	14.653	Handloom Weavers Comprehensive Welfare Scheme (HWCWS)	26.56	24.98	2.06	8.014	Yarn Supply Scheme (YSS)	261.35	199.84	126.84	136.90 	Total	481.58	391.69	265.00	292.40The Office of the Development Commissioner for Handlooms makes all efforts in close coordination with the State Governments and Weavers’ Service Centres through regular correspondence, interaction and by holding meetings for optimum utilisation of funds allocated for various schemes.
