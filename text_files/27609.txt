                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Review of Educational Facilities in Bihar




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has reviewed the existing facilities at the Secondary and Higher Secondary level of education in Bihar;(b) if so, the details of the demographic characteristics, quality of school education, details of facilities, teacher-students ratio thereof;(c) the number of Government and non-Government educational institutions in each district of Bihar;(d) the number of projects approved for the development of education in the 13th Five Year Plan for Maharashtra?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): Education is in the concurrent list of Constitution and most of the schools are under jurisdiction of concerned States and UTs. As informed by the Government of Bihar, there are 9361 functional Secondary/Higher Secondary schools in the State and all 8386 Gram Panchayats have now been covered with Secondary/Higher Secondary schooling facility. The State has initiated various qualitative initiatives, i.e., e-Lots (e-Library of Teachers & Students) for online/digital learning of the students, at least one smart classroom in Secondary/Higher Secondary Government schools, e-content for classroom transaction through smart class and capacity building of secondary teachers under NISHTHA 2.0. As per UDISE 2019-20, out of 6128 Secondary/Higher Secondary schools (Government), 6119 schools have building, 5173 schools have ramp, 6075 schools have boys’ toilet, 6092 schools have girls’ toilet, 6122 schools have drinking water facility, 4571 schools have boundary wall, 4447 schools have playground and 5859 schools have electricity. 3304 schools have been opened/upgraded with class IX in academic year 2020-2021.(c): The number of Government and Non-Government Secondary/ Higher Secondary schools are 9361 and 2218 respectively. The district wise status is at Annexure.(d): This Department has launched an Integrated Scheme for School Education namely Samagra Shiksha with effect from the year 2018-19 by subsuming the three erstwhile Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA), Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA) and Teacher Education (TE). An approval of Rs. 357.72 crore has been given for projects under non-recurring component of the scheme to the State of Maharashtra from 2017-18 to 2020-21.****
