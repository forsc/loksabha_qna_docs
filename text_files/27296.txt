                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Proposals for NHs




Jyotsna Charandas Mahant














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the proposals for the sanction and construction of National Highways (NHs) from various States are received;(b)    if so, the details of such proposals which are still pending with the Government for approval, NH/ State-wise; (c)  whether the work on the NH passing through the State of Chhattisgarh especially Korba district has been completed; and (d)    if so, the details and present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Based on the proposals received from the State Governments / Union Territories (UTs), Ministry has approved an amount of about Rs. 1,03,700 crore in FY 2021-22 for sanction of projects for development of National Highways entrusted with State Governments / UTs, as part of National Infrastructure Pipeline, to be implemented in the next 2-3 years. State / UT-wise details is at Annexure-I.  (c) & (d) As of July 2021, 36  projects in a length of 1450 km costing Rs. 14103 cr. are under implementation in the state of Chhattisgarh. Projects are given a time period of 2 to 3 years for completion. Details of projects completed in last 5 years in Korba district of Chhattisgarh are tabulated below.Sl. No.	Name of project	Name of District	length (km)	Total Project Cost (Rs. In Cr.)
