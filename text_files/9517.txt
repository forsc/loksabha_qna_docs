                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3080






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






NNM in Rajasthan




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	 whether the National Nutrition Mission is being implemented in Rajasthan; (b)	 if so, the States where this mission have been implemented so far; (c)	 the funds allocated/released to Rajasthan for this purpose by the Union Government; (d) 	whether the Government is aware of the various shortcomings in the implementation of the above scheme; (e) 	if so, the details thereof; and (f) 	the manner in which the Government is overcoming these shortcomings?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT( SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI )(a)& (b) POSHAN Abhiyaan (earlier known as National Nutrition Mission) is being implemented in all the States/UTs including Rajasthan. (c)   	? 117.27 crore have been released to Rajasthan so far for this purpose by the Union Government. (d) to (f)  The shortcomings as and when reported in the implementation of the scheme are resolved through review meetings, video conferences, visit of officers to the State etc. This is a continuous process. This is a continuous process. *****
