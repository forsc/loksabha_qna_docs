                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1839






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Tap Water Connections in Odisha




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to increase the budget grants to Odisha during the current financial year 2021-22 with an aim to provide clean tap water to every household under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM);(b) if so, the details thereof along with budget allocated for the same during the current financial year;(c) whether the Government proposes to provide full financial assistance to help Odisha to achieve its target to provide tap water supply to every household by March 2024;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) the number of households in Odisha that have been provided the tap water connections under the JJM since its launch, year/district-wise details thereof; and(f) the estimated number of households likely to be provided with tap water connections during the current financial year under JJM?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d) Since start of the Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM), Rs. 364.74 Crore in 2019-20, Rs. 812.15 Crore in 2020-21 and Rs. 3,323.42 Crore in 2021-22 have been allocated to Odisha as Central grant under JJM. As compared to the 2020-21, during the current financial year, the Government of India has quadrupled the Central allocation to Odisha. Under JJM, the Government of India is committed to provides financial assistance to all States as per approved fund allocation criteria including to Odisha, to make provision of tap water supply to every household by 2024.(e) As on 15th August, 2019, at the time of launch of Jal Jeevan Mission, in Odisha, out of 85.67 lakh rural households, only 3.11 lakh households (3.63%) had tap water connections. Since then, 23.69 lakh households have been provided tap water connections, thus increasing the tap water supply to 26.80 lakh households (31.29%). The year and district-wise details of households, which have been provided with tap water connections, is annexed.(f)  In 2021-22, Odisha  has planned to provide tap water connections to 22.11 lakh households.*****
