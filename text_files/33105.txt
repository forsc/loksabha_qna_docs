                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

677






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Closure of Tents in Kolkata Maidan by Army




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Government is aware that all the tents in Kolkata maidan are forced to close down for fifteen days at a particular time of the year by the army;(b) if so, the details and the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government proposes to do away with the system of closing down of tents in Kolkata maidan; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) & (b): The Kolkata Maidan is closed from 1st October to 15th October every year as per the Notification No. G-1158/MD dated 15th May, 1997 published in the Calcutta Gazette.  The closure is carried out in order to “bar” the right of establishment over the Maidan land.  It also helps in maintenance and rejuvenation of the greens after extensive use for sporting activities.(c)  No, Sir. (d)  Not applicable. *****
