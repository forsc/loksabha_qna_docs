                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Bird Sanctuary




Durga Prasad Rao Balli














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether a group of birds come from Siberia every year to Nelapattu, Sriharikota in Doravari satram mandalam in Shri Potti Sriramulu (SPSR) Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government has any plan to sanction Bird Sanctuary in the said district; and(d)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) and (b) As informed by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, birds  like Grey Pelicans, Painted Storks, Eurasian coot, Purple Swamphen, Black Winged Stilt, Spot Bill Duck, Northern Shovler, Pintail Snipe,  Spoon Bills, Lesser Whistling Ducks, etc., come from Siberia to different areas in Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh including Nelapattu Sanctuary.  (c) and(d) The State Government of Andhra Pradesh has notified Nelappattu Wildlife Sanctuary in the year 1996 with an area of 4 sq.kms. in the Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh.	***
