                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EARTH SCIENCES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Holding of Exhibitions/Fairs




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of exhibition/fairs conducted by this Ministry along with the expenditure incurred thereon during the last three years and the current year, exhibition wise:(b)	whether corruption and financial irregularities have been noticed by the government in these exhibitions/fairs.(c)	if, so, details thereof and the action taken by the government in this regard:(d)	whether have been allegations that the officials responsible for ongoing fairs and exhibitions have amassed disproportionate assets: and (e)	if so, the details thereof and the action proposed/taken against such corrupt officers.






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER   OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	A total number of 79 (seventy nine) exhibitions/fairs including  two exhibition/fairs of current financial year have been participated by the Ministry. The total expenditure of  Rs.12,28,59,756.00 incurred on participation of these exhibitions. Exhibition wise details is placed at Annexure-I.(b)	No financial irregularities and corruption noticed.(c)	– (e)  Does not arise.
