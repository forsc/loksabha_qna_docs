                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5004






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Renewal of Passports




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any information about the renewal of the passport of the Indian citizen for shorter periods;(b) 	if so, the details of the passport issued/renewed for less than six months by Indian embassies abroad; (c) 	the details of the passport renewal applications to Ahmadabad RPO by the Overseas Citizenship of India Card holders in last three years; and (d) 	the details of the pending/cleared passport applications out of the above application during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) 	Normally, ten years validity passports are issued in fresh cases as well as reissue. However, in certain cases, Short Validity Passports (SVPs) are issued under Section 7 of the Passports Act, 1967 by Indian Embassies abroad to Indian nationals whose stay in the foreign country is illegal or whose full particulars/photo are not available in the database etc. Furthermore, SVPs are also issued in cases where there is criminal case pending in Indian courts. (b) 	The number of passports issued/renewed for less than 6 months by Indian Embassies abroad during last 4 years is as under –<pre>S. No.YearNumber of Passports 12022 (as of 20.03.2022)3322021293202015420193</pre>(c) & (d)	OCI cards are issued to the persons of Indian origin after their renunciation of Indian citizenship and having surrendered the Indian Passport. An OCI card is issued against a foreign passport. Hence, such OCI card holders cannot apply for renewal of their Indian passports anywhere.***
