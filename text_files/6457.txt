                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Quality of School Education




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether NITI Aayog has released the report/index on quality of School educationrecently;(b) if so, the details of the report along the parameters on which the NITI Aayog has prepared the said report;(c) whether it is a fact that populous States like Uttar Pradesh and Bihar rank lower in Quality Index and if so, the measures taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard; and(d) whether the data furnished by the NITI Aayog is likely to be used for incentivizingStates in budgetary allocations provided to them and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. The NITI AYOG has published the report titled “SchoolEducation Quality Index (SEQI)” in June 2019. The list of parameters onwhich the report has been prepared by NITI Aayog is given at Annexure-I.(c) The government aims to drive tangible policy improvements towardsachieving quality education in a coherent and collaborative manner based onthe performance of all the States including Bihar and Uttar Pradesh under theQuality Index by focusing on improving educational outcomes with respect tolearning Outcomes, access, quality and governance in India.(d) As mentioned in NITI AYOG’s report titled “School Education Quality Index(SEQI)” released in June 2019, the measurement of quality-related educationoutcomes is imperative for incentivising States and Union Territories toimprove the performance of their schools systems.*******
