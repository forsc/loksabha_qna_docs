                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

765






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Audit of Hospital Beds




T.N. Prathapan













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                








                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    Faizal P.P. Mohammed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has conducted audits of the total number of hospital beds in Government hospitals in the country, including hospital beds in central government-run hospitals;(b) if so, the details thereof since 2014 and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government has conducted, or intends to conduct, an audit of the total number of hospital beds in private hospitals also; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d):	‘Health’ being a State subject, it is primarily responsibility of the State Government/UTs to make efforts for increasing the bed strength in their hospital in accordance with requirements and fund availability. No such data in this respect is centrally maintained.As far as Central Government Hospitals located in Delhi namely Safdarjung, Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia and Lady Hardinge Medical College & Associated Hospitals and the North East Institutes are concerned, the current bed strength is as under:- S.No.	Name of Hospital/Institutes	Total Number of Beds1.	Safdarjung Hospital	28732.	Dr. RML Hospital	15323.	LHMC and Associated Hospital	12724	North Eastern Indira Gandhi Regional Institute of Health and Medical Sciences (NEIGRIMS)	5945	Regional Institute of Mental Science	1200
