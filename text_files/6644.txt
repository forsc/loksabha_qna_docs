                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Cold Stores in Rural Areas




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number/ break up of existing cold storages in rural and urban areas of the country;(b)	the area where the majority of cold storages are located, rural area or urban area- wise;(c)	whether the Government deems it appropriate to increase the number of cold storages; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): A total number of 5,427 cold storages with capacity of 226.69 Lakh MT have been established in the country as a part of the components of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) of Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) and through the schemes of Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) namely (i) Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for development of Horticulture in the country and (ii) Capital Investment Subsidy for Construction/ Expansion / Modernization of Cold Storages and Storages for Horticulture Products of National Horticulture Board. The state-wise distribution of financially assisted cold storages under the aforesaid schemes along with their respective capacities is placed at Annexure. No separate data on the breakup of the cold storages in urban /rural areas is maintained.(c) & (d): The Ministry of Food Processing Industries through its schemes under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) is providing financial assistance for setting up of cold storages as part of the scheme components. Further, Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare through its Schemes namely (i) Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for development of Horticulture in the country and (ii) Capital Investment Subsidy for Construction/ Expansion / Modernization of Cold Storages and Storages for Horticulture Products of National Horticulture Board supports for setting up of cold storages in the country.*****
