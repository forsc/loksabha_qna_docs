                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1529






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Rural Housing Schemes




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the criteria fixed by the Government for allotment of low-cost houses to beneficiaries under various rural housing schemes; and(b) 	the number of houses allotted to beneficiaries under low cost rural housing schemes during each of the last five years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) and (b): To achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by year 2022 in rural areas, the erstwhile rural housing scheme, Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY) has been completely restructured into Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) w.e.f. 1st April, 2016. Under PMAY-G, beneficiaries are identified from Socio Economic Caste Census (SECC), 2011 data, based on housing deprivation parameters, exclusion criteria prescribed and after due verification by Gram Sabha and following Appellate process. Accordingly, Gram Panchayat-wise Permanent Wait List (PWL) of beneficiaries has been prepared. As on 05.02.2020, the total number of houses sanctioned during 2014-15 to 2019-20 under the rural housing schemes of the Ministry is as follows:Year	      2014-15	  2015-16      2016-17	   2017-18    2018-19	   2019-20	TotalHouses sanctioned    23,75,626	  21,96,548   41,47,353	   31,41,242  25,09,656	   41,11,123	1,84,81,548********
