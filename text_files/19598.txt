                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

818






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Indian Grey Wolf Sancturary




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up Indian Grey Wolf Sanctuary at Koppal District in Karnataka; and(b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	and (b)	As informed by the Government of Karnataka, the State Board for Wild Life in its 15th meeting held on 19.01.2021 has approved setting up of “Wolf Sanctuary” in  Bankapura Block-I Reserve Forest area of Koppal Division in order to conserve the habitat of Indian Grey Wolf.***
