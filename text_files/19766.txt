                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Excavation Work in Bundelkhand




Pushpendra Singh Chandel













                                                    Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the excavation works done in new areas of the States including Bundelkhand and Uttarakhand during the last three years;(b)	the details of newer areas discovered through excavation in the country during the last three years;(c)	whether the Government has any special plan to undertake excavation work in Bundelkhand and Uttarakhand keeping in view their ancient and historical value; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM (PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)	The details of excavation works conducted by Archaeological Survey of India during last three field season 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 including excavation at Erich, District Jhansi (Bundelkhand) during the field season 2017-18 are at Annexure-I. (c) & (d)	Yes, Sir. During the current field season 2020-21, ASI is carrying out excavations at two sites of Tewar in district Jabalpur and Eran in district Sagar in Bundelkhand region whereas, at present, there is no such proposal for the Uttarakhand.
