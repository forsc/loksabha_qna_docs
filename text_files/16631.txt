                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

451






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Sahakar Mitra Scheme




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government have launched Sahakar Mitra Scheme recently; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the aims and objectives behind the move; (c) 	whether the Government has fixed any criteria to shortlist candidates for the internship; (d) 	if so, the details thereof alongwith the number of applications received for internship and the amount of financial support provided to each interns; (e) 	whether the Government has step up any online application portal for this purpose and if so, the details thereof and the steps taken to popularize the portal; and (f) 	the other steps taken by the Government to make the scheme a success by involving young professionals to provide latest technologies to help boost agriculture and its allied sector?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (f):   No Sir.  Sahakar Mitra Scheme for Internship Programme (SIP) is neither a Government of India Scheme nor funded by the Government. It is a scheme run by National Cooperative Development Corporation, a statutory corporation under the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare. (b) to (e):   In view of above, do not arise. 	******
