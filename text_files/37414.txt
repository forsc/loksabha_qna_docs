                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4483






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Unclaim money in Department of Post




Rodmal Nagar













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is no claimant of crores of rupees in the department of Post and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has identified such customers and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to spend this amount for social work, enhancing postal services and other developmental works; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)  	Unclaimed Savings Bank Accounts and Savings Certificates identified under Senior Citizens Welfare Fund Rules (SCWF) 2016 are claimed either by the Account Holders or by the legal heirs / nominees and settled as per the procedure prescribed in said Rule.(b) 	Holders of unclaimed Savings Bank Accounts and Saving Certificates are identified as prescribed in SCWF Rules 2016. As on 30.09.2021, 3.01 Crore accounts and certificates are identified as unclaimed.(c) & (d) 	The unclaimed amount, lying in Savings Bank Accounts and Savings Certificates is transferred to Senior Citizen’s Welfare Fund which is administered by Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment.
