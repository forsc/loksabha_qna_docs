                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Original Equipment Manufacturers




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has considered taking action against the foreign automobile Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) that have exited India without giving prior notice to customers and dealers, if so, the details thereof;(b) if not, whether the Government intends to take any action towards minimizing foreign automobile OEM exits or enabling smooth exits with minimal financial exposure for automobile dealers, employees and customers;(c) whether the Government has devised a plan to protect automobile dealers from unfair treatment by foreign automobile OEMs in execution of the dealership agreements; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) to (d):	Investments in the automobile sector is through the automatic route, except in respect of few instances. Further, the investments are guided by FDI policy. *******
