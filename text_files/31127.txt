                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2924






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






SBG Yojana in Andhra Pradesh




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of implementation of Sampoorna Bima Gram (SBG) Yojana in Andhra Pradesh including the number of villages covered under it, district-wise; and(b) 	the number of villages in Andhra Pradesh which are still deprived of Sampoorna Bima Gram (SBG) Yojana, and the reasons of the same? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	1930 villages in 13 districts of Andhra Pradesh have been covered under Bima Gram Yojana till date. District-wise number of villages covered under Bima Gram Yojana in Andhra Pradesh till date is enclosed as Annexure-I.(b)	 Bima Gram Yojana is an initiative of Department of Posts to provide life insurance cover to the people living in rural areas. The premium paying capacity of a person is one of the critical elements in implementation of this initiative. Continual efforts are made to cover as many villages as possible under the Bima Gram Yojana of the Department of Posts in a phased manner.
