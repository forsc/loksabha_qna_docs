                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

76






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Inclusion of Six Communities in ST List




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Union Government is aware that Assam State Government had constituted a committee of ministers few years back to give Schedule Tribe status to six communities including Koch-Rajbongshi, Adivasi and Ahom; (b) if so, the details of the composition of the committee; (c) the number of meetings of the committee that were held; (d) the details of the minutes of meeting in the floor of the house; (e) whether Union Government has taken any decision on the basis of the recommendation of the Assam Government? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU) (a): As per information received from the Government of Assam, a committee of Ministers was constituted by the then State Government of Assam on 14.01.2019. (b): Composition of the committee is at Annexure –I. (c): 11 meetings were held. (d): Minutes of the meeting are at Annexure –II.  (e): No proposal based on the recommendations of the Committee have been received. *****
