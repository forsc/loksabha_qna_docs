                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Ghatal Master Plan




Deepak (Dev) Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the current status of Ghatal Master Plan project;(b) the funds released by the Government for this project till date; and(c) the time limit set by the Government to complete the said project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) to (c) Ghatal Master Plan for West Bengal with revised cost of Rs. 1,238.95 crore at  price level 2017 was considered by Advisory Committee of Ministry of Water Resources, River Development & Ganga Rejuvenation for its techno-economic viability in 136th meeting held on 6th June, 2018. The project was accepted by the Advisory Committee subject to submission of State Finance concurrence by Project Authority. No funds have been provided by the Central Government as the project has not been included for funding under any scheme of Ministry of Jal Shakti. As informed by State Government of West Bengal, works under some components of the project have been taken up by State Government with its own fund and the duration of the project is seven years. ******
