                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4435






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Rashtriya Jan Sahyog AvamBal Vikas Sansthan Vibhag




Midhun Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  	whether the National Institute for Public Cooperation and Child Development (Rashtriya Jan Sahyog Avam Bal Vikas Sansthan Vibhag) has identified crime-prone districts across the country to create awareness with regard to the crimes and violence against women and children and to facilitate legal aid;(b)  	if so, the details thereof; and(c)  	the details of funds allocated for this purpose across the country, district-wise including in Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)     National Institute of Public Cooperation and Child Development (NIPCCD) has not identified crime-prone districts across the country to create awareness with regard to crimes and violence against women and child and to facilitate legal aid.(b) & (c) Does not arise.****
