                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2410






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Funds for PMAGY




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2410TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.03.2021FUNDS FOR PMAGY 2410. MS. DIYA KUMARI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the details of funds sanctioned, disbursed, and utilized during the last three years for the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) in Rajasthan, district-wise; and (b) the details of the indicators used to monitor the categorization of villages under PMAGY?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a):  The district-wise details of funds sanctioned by the Ministry under the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) in the last three years and disbursed to the districts of Rajasthan by the State Government,  are at Annexure-I. The districts have not utilised these funds so far.(b): There is no system of categorization of villages under PMAGY. However, the needs and gaps in the selected villages  are assessed, and progress of implementation of the scheme  is monitored, against 50 identified Monitorable Indicators in 10 Domains which are as under:i) Drinking water and Sanitationii) Educationiii) Health and Nutritioniv) Social Securityv) Rural Roads and Housingvi) Electricity and Clean Fuelvii) Agricultural Practices etc.viii) Financial Inclusionix) Digitizationx) Livelihood and Skill Development. The list of Monitorable Indicators is attached as Annexure-II.**** Annexure-I referred to in reply to Part-(a) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.2410 dated 09.03.2021 asked by Ms. Diya Kumari, MP regarding “Funds for PMAGY”.District-wise details of funds sanctioned and disbursed during last three years(Rs. in Lakh)Sl.
