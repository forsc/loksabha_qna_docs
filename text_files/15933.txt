                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4703






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Credit Guarantee Fund




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of allocation and release of the Rs. 3,000 crore per year Credit Guarantee Fund for Entrepreneurship Development in the country;(b) 	the break-up of number of borrowers and amount borrowed under different sectoral heads of entrepreneurship, year-wise and State-wise;(c) 	whether the rate of repayment has been low in some States as compared to others; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d): There is no Credit Guarantee Fund for Entrepreneurship Development managed by the Government through the trusteeship of National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company Limited (NCGTC). However, Government has created Credit Guarantee Funds for Skill Development Loans.
