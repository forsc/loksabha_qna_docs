                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Nurseries under National Agriculture Development Programme




Kanakmal Katara













                                                    Keshari Devi Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of nurseries/model nurseries and hitech nurseries set up under the National Agriculture Development Programme in Banswara, Dungarpur in Rajasthan and in Uttar Pradesh including Prayagraj district along with the details of the financial assistance provided under the programme and the year-wise number of beneficiaries under the programme during the years 2015 to 2021; (b) whether the said aided nurseries have been inspected by competent authorities from time to time and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the said nursery owners have apprised the competent authorities of their problems and if so, the details thereof; and (d) the steps taken to address the said problems?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) : 	No nurseries/model nurseries and hitech nurseries has been set up under the National Agriculture Development Programme in Banswara, Dungarpur in Rajasthan during 2015 to 2021. As regards Uttar Pradesh,  government owned nurseries/model nurseries and hi-tech nurseries have been set up under National Agriculture Development Programme. Till date, seven hi-tech nurseries seedling production unit (i.e. Mini COE for vegetable) have been established in Behraich, Hapur, Mau, Aligarh, Ambedker Nagar, Fatehpur & Rampur districts of Uttar Pradesh during the year 2018-19 to 2020-21. District wise financial details in this regard are given below:-(Rs lakh)S.N.	District 	Year	Total  	 	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	 1	Behraich 	104	-	-	1042	Hapur	52	51.33	-	103.333	Mau	-	45.5	35.1	80.64	Aligarh	-	97	7	1045	Ambedker Nagar	-	97	7	1046	Fatehpur	-	43.274	45.025	88.2997	Rampur	-	96.99	7.01	104(b) : 	These government owned hi-tech nursery seedling production unit (i.e Mini C.O.E for vegetable) have been inspected by concerned District Horticulture Officer & Deputy Director Horticulture from time to time in Uttar Pradesh.(c) : 	These nurseries are owned by State Government in Uttar Pradesh. (d) : 	Does not arise in view of  (c) above*****
