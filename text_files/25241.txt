                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1031






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Arts and Handicarft Projects




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Public Sector oil companies have taken any initiative to providesupport to various projects related to arts and handicrafts;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the efforts being made by the Ministry to ensure that people in rural and tribalareas in Gujarat get maximum benefits from it?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c): Yes Sir. Public Sector Oil Companies have intimated that underCorporate Social Responsibility (CSR) activities they have taken initiatives toprovide support to various projects related to Arts and Handicrafts in differentStates. The details of such projects are annexed. The projects are selected andundertaken by the companies themselves in line with their CSR policies.****
