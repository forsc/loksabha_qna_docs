                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

622






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Construction of Airstrips in Lalitpur and Jhansi




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the present status of construction work of airstrips in Lalitpur and Jhansi districts of Uttar Pradesh; and(b) the time by which the said airstrips are likely to be made functional?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): On request of the State Government of Uttar Pradesh (SGoUP), pre-feasibility study was conducted by a multidisciplinary team of Airports Authority of India (AAI) for development of brown field airports in Lalitpur and Jhansi. The pre-feasibility report  has been submitted to the SGoUP. SGoUP has already initiated action for acquisition of land as per the pre-feasibility study report submitted by AAI.
