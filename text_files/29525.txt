                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

858






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Four-laning Road Projects in Assam




Topon Kumar Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government is aware of the slow construction work on the four-laning project in Assam and if so, the details thereof;(b)   the measures being taken to speed up the said construction work; and(c)  whether the Government is aware that the said condition of the existing road is very pathetic and if so, the measures being taken to repair the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  Yes Sir. The details of on-going 4 laning projects in Assam is at Annexure-A.(b)     The measures taken by the Government to speed up the construction work are:(i)       Expediting the progress of pre-construction activities such as Land Acquisition, Utility Shifting, Forest Clearance/Wildlife Clearances, etc. by continuous liasoning with the State/District Administration is being done by Higher Authorities of NHIDCL at HQ level and also at field level by Regional Office/PMU/Site Offices.(ii) Review meetings/Video Conferencing are being held regularly with Contractors/Field Officers by Higher Authorities of NHIDCL to expedite the progress of projects. District Administration is also monitoring the progress of projects from time to time.(iii)  Notice to the contractors has been issued from time to time as per provisions of Contract Agreement to expedite the progress of projects.(iv)  Actions are being taken against Non-performing contractors by duly terminating the projects for poor progress and simultaneously fresh contracts are being awarded.          (c)   To maintain traffic worthiness of the affected stretches, the roads are being 	maintained through the contractor or at their risk and cost as per the contract provision.
