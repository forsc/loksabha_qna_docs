                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

808






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Public Procurement Policy for MSEs




Ganesh Singh













                                                    Rahul Gandhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether as per Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) Order, 2018, the Ministry and its subsidiaries/joint ventures have set an annual target for 25% procurement from MSME Sector and a sub-target of 4% out of 25% target of annual procurement has been earmarked for procurement from MSMEs owned by SC/ST entrepreneurs and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the CPSUs under the Ministry are implementing this policy and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the said policy is being followed in the case of OBC entrepreneurs at par with SCs/STs, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) 	whether Central Ministries/ Departments and PSUs have met the mandatory target of 25% procurement from Micro and Small Enterprises since April 1, 2015 and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e) 	the measures being implemented to ensure compliance with 25% target of procurement from MSEs?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):   Under Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs), the CPSEs have to procure 25% from MSEs including, 4% from MSEs owned by SC/ST and 3% from MSEs owned by Women. The policy is applicable to all the Central Ministries / Departments / Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) and mandates at least 25% of annual procurement by the Central Ministries/ Departments and CPSEs from Micro and Small Enterprises. (c):	No, Sir.(d) & (e):	Ministry has taken several measures for effective implementation of the policy. These include constitution of Review Committee, Launch of SC/ST hub to provide support SC/ST entrepreneurs and launch of MSME Sambandh portal to monitor the progress of procurement made by Central Ministries / Departments and Central Public Undertakings (CPSUs).As per information uploaded on Sambandh portal, the details of annual procurement of goods & services from MSEs including SC/ST and Women by the Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) during the last three years are as under: <pre>Year	No. of CPSEs (Reported)	Total Procurement (Rs. in Crore)	Procurement From MSEs (Rs. in Crore)	Procurement from MSEs owned by SC/ST Entrepreneur(Rs. in Crore)	Procurement from MSEs owned by Women Entrepreneur(Rs. in Crore)2017-18	170	114042.05	26357.46	544.72	-2018-19	164	153309.17	40343.08	823.86	231.502019-20*	106	60278.10	17427.41	305.10	249.83</pre>  * as on 15/11/2019
