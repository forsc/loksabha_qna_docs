                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2094






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a large number of Kendriya Vidyalayas in Bihar are functioning intemporary buildings including Gopalganj District and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the KVS has taken any steps for construction of permanent building forKendriya Vidyalayas in the said District by pursuing sponsoring authority inidentification of land, completion of lease formalities and all necessaryrequirements;(c) if so, the total funds released for construction of building in Gopalganj; and(d) whether the Government has taken up the matter with the State Government foracquisition of land for construction of own building and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Out of 48 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in the State of Bihar, 17 KVs namely Bela,Harnaut, Jhajha, Lakhisarai, Buxar, Motihari, Siwan, Banka, Chhapra, AFS (Purnia),CRPF (Jhaphan), Gopalganj, Hajipur, Darbhanga, Baruni, Aurangabad & Maharajganjare running in temporary buildings.(b) to (d) For construction of permanent buildings for KVs, transfer of land in favour ofKendriya Vidyalayas Sangathan (KVS) by the sponsoring authorities is a pre-requisite.Despite persistent follow up trough communications sent on various dates i.e.26.4.2013, 26.8.2014, 17.6.2015, 29.6.2017, 22.12.2017, 6.2.2018, 7.3.2018, 19.6.2019& 9.10.2019 the State Government of Bihar has not transferred the requisite extent ofthe land in favour of KVS and as such, no fund has been released for construction ofpermanent building for KV, Gopalganj.*****
