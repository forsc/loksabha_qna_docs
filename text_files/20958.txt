                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2236






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Rozgar Mela




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Union Government  has formulated any scheme in coordination with the State Governments to organize Rozgar Melas at the district level in the entire country; and(b)	 If so; the details of Rozgar Melas organized in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years along with the results achieved therein?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (b): Government is implementing the National Career Service (NCS) Project to provide a variety of employment related services like job matching, career counselling, vocational guidance, information on skill development courses, etc. Under the NCS project assistance is also provided to States/UTs for organising job fairs. The details of the job fairs organized through NCS in collaboration with State Govt. of Uttar Pradesh is as under:-Sl. No.	Year	No. of Job Fairs	No. of candidates given final offer letter1.	2017-18	161	17,7162.	2018-19	761	1,09,9103.	2019-20	385	40,808Total	13071,68,434Besides above, State Governments also organize job fairs from their resources as per their requirement. *****
