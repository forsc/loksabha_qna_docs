                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2795






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Urban Transport Matters




Subrata Pathak













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRSLOK SABHA(a)	whether coordination, appraisal and approval of Urban 	Transport matters including metro rail projects in the 	country have been carried out as per the provisions of 	National Unban Transport Policy (NUT), 2006 and if so, the 	details thereof;(b)	the details of urban transport matters so far received and 	sanctioned under such Policy, State/UT-wise;(c) 	the details of financial assistance being provided to 	various States/UTs under the said Policy; (d) 	whether the Government has made any review to know the extent to which 15 year old NUT Policy is still fruitful for the cities in various States/UTs; and(e) 	if so, the details and the outcome thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                                             THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): National Urban Transport Policy (NUTP), 2006 is a policy guideline which provides broad framework for development of urban transport project.  Urban Transport is an integral part of Urban Development, which is a State subject, as such, planning, execution and development of urban transport matters are done by the concerned State Governments/Union Territory (UTs). Financial assistance for urban transport matters like Buses and associated infrastructure, Bus Rapid Transit System (BRTS), Metro Rail Systems etc. including support for preparation of Feasibility Study Reports, Detailed Project Reports (DPR) and Comprehensive Mobility Plans (CMP) etc. are provided after evaluating various parameters such as resources by State Governments/private participation and the institutional mechanism set up for such urban transport system under different Schemes/Policies. (b):  The State/UT-wise details of urban transport projects received and sanctioned after NUTP 2006 are given in Annexure. After issuance of Metro Rail Policy 2017, metro projects are sanctioned under metro policy. (c): Under Urban Transport Planning & Capacity Building Scheme which is part of NUTP 2006, financial assistance is provided to State/ UTs/ ULBs/ Institutions/ Organizations for the following components:-	80% of cost of the study as grant for all types of traffic and transportation studies, Clean Development Mechanism (CDM) studies/ measures, studies for application of Intelligent Transport System (ITS) setting up of Traffic Information System Centres, Intermodal integration, parking complexes etc. -	upto 50% of total project cost for preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) for Mass Rapid Transit viz. Metro Rail, Mono Rail, Light Rail Transit, Tram Rail projects etc.- up to 80%  of cost of preparation of DPR of low cost projects such as Bus Rapid Transit System projects, City bus services, Intermediate Para Transit system, Non Motorized Transport (NMT), public bike sharing, Intelligent Transport System, Metro cables and Ropeway projects. Besides above, research projects and capacity building in urban transport are also funded under this scheme for which 100% of the project cost is provided as the financial assistance to the concerned Institutes/Organisations. (d):  No, Sir.(e):  Does not arise                                *****
