                                    ANSWERED ON:

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any financial assistance is being given under the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) since the outbreak of Corona pandemic;(b)	if so, the manner/mode through which it is being given;(c)	the time limit for availing the said assistance; and(d)	the details of the women benefited since 2014 till date along with the funds utilised/spent thereunder, State/UT-wise and district-wise ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER FOR WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) to (d)         A statement is laid on the Table of the House. ****** Statement referred to in reply to part (a) to (d) of the Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 164 for answer on 30.07.2021 raised by Shri Malook Nagar regarding Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (a)	to (d) Yes, Sir. Disbursement of maternity benefits of ?5,000/- to the eligible beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) has been continuing since inception of the scheme including the period of outbreak of Corona pandemic. The eligible beneficiary receives cash incentive amounting to ?5,000/- directly to their Bank/Post Office Account linked to her Aadhaar Number in Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode in three installments. A beneficiary can apply, at any point of time but not later than 730 days of pregnancy, even if she had not claimed any of the instalments earlier but fulfils eligibility criterion and conditionalities for receiving benefits. Last Menstrual Period (LMP) registered in the Mother Child Protection (MCP) card is the date of pregnancy to be considered in this respect. The State/UT-wise details of women benefited since inception of the scheme w.e.f. 01.01.2017 along with the amount spent for disbursement of maternity benefit to the eligible beneficiaries under PMMVY is at Annexure-I.
