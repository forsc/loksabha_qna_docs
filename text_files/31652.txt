                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Improvement of Highways in Gujarat




C. R. Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of road stretches in Gujarat which are entrusted to the 	National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) for improvement 	under various programmes;(b)	whether the Government is contemplating any alternate method 	for improvement of National Highways (NHs) in the State;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether there is any policy for upgradation of State Highways and 	NHs; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	At present, about 28 NH works comprising 870 km length having Cost of Rs. 27,284 crore in the State of Gujarat have been taken up for upgradation/improvement/widening under various programmes through National Highways Authority of India (NHAI).(b) to (e)  This Ministry is primarily responsible for the development and maintenance of National Highways in the country. Development works on various National Highways are taken up from time to time based on the requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority, traffic, availability of funds etc. *****
