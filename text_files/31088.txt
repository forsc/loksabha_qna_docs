                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2805






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Startup India Seed Fund




Vishnu Dutt Sharma













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether projects have been funded under startup India Seed Fund; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof including the number of projects that have been funded, employment generated and economic value created under startup India Seed Fund from its inception till date?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a): 	The Government is implementing the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) to provide financial assistance to Startups through approved Incubators for proof of concept, prototype development, product trials, market entry, and commercialization. Under the scheme, no project based funding is provided. (b): 	Under the SISFS, Rs. 945 crore has been approved for period of 4 years starting from 2021-22. As of 10th December 2021, 48 incubators have been approved for financial assistance of Rs.192.25 crore for providing onward seed fund to select startups. 115 startups have been selected so far by incubators under the scheme. *******
