                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

798






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Ayush System of Medicine Against Vaccination




Pashupati Nath Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether the use of AYUSH systems of medicine along with vaccination is successful in preventing COVID-19, if so, the details thereof indicating the extent to which it has been successful in controlling/treating COVID-19 along with the side effects, if any;(b) the number of AYUSH centres set up in the country, State/UT-wise;(c) the details of AYUSH medicines permitted/ approved for treating COVID-19 along with the number of patients who were treated and recovered by using these medicines, State/UT-wise;(d) whether the Government is allocating/investing for setting up Research and Development and AYUSH Research Laboratories for the treatment of COVID-19 pandemic; and(e) if so, the details thereof indicating the total fund allocated/utilized for the research and development for the treatment of COVID-19, State/UT-wise particularly in Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(DR. MUNJPARA MAHENDRABHAI)(a):	Ministry of Ayush has launched interdisciplinary studies involving AYUSH interventions for COVID-19. Under various research organizations and National Institutes under the Ministry of Ayush, 126 research studies at 152 centres in the country have been initiated on AYUSH interventions. Further, for prevention and mitigation of COVID-19 through the AYUSH system of medicines; the Government has taken various steps stated at Annexure-I. (b):	The total number of AYUSH Hospitals and dispensaries in the country state/UT-wise as per the Ministry’s Annual Statistical Publication “AYUSH IN INDIA-2019” is at Annexure-II and Annexure-III. (c):	During the second wave of this pandemic, AYUSH-64 and Kabasura Kudineer (KSK) has emerged as a ray of hope for the patients of mild and moderate COVID-19 infection. The scientists of reputed research institutions of the country have found that   AYUSH-64, a polyherbal formulation developed by the Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS), Ministry of Ayush is helpful in the treatment of asymptomatic, mild and moderate COVID-19 infection as an adjunct to standard care. (d) & (e):	Yes. The Ministry of Ayush invited research proposals under the Modified Extra Mural Research (EMR) Scheme for SARS CoV-2 infection and COVID-19 from 21st April 2020 to 10th May 2020. Under the Scheme, funds were released after initial screening by the concerned Research Council/ Expert and approval of the Project Approval Committee. The Ministry of AYUSH has also been investing in Research and Development through its research councils and national institutes for development of treatment of COVID-19. The details of funds allocated are as under:-  S.No	Name of the Institute /Research Council	Funds Allocated (in Rs.)1.		Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences	36 Crore2.
