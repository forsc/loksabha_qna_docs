                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






R & D Activities by KVIC




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the Research and Development (R&D) activities undertaken in the country especially in northern part of Uttar Pradesh during the last three years by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) for promotion of Khadi products; (b) 	whether any tie-ups have been formed by the KVIC with any National or State Level Institution to organize exhibitions for promotion of Khadi products in the country including Uttar Pradesh during the last three years and the current year and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and (c) 	the details of the schemes to revitalize Khadi sector in Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Details of Research and Development (R&D) activities undertaken by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) in the country including Uttar Pradesh during last three years for the promotion of Khadi products is placed at Annexure-I.(b):	No tie-up has been made by KVIC with any National or State Level Institution to organize exhibitions for promotion of Khadi products in the country including Uttar Pradesh during last three years and the current year.(c):	To revitalize Khadi Sector all over the country, including Uttar Pradesh, KVIC has been implementing Khadi Vikas Yojana (KVY) for the development of Khadi programmes from the year 2019-20 and the guidelines were issued on 08.11.2019, under which financial assistance in the form of grant and subsidy is provided under i) Modified Market Development Assistance (MMDA), ii) Interest Subsidy Eligibility Certificate (ISEC) Scheme, iii) Workshed Scheme for Khadi Artisans, and iv) Assistance for Marketing Infrastructure scheme, the last three years performance is placed at Annexure-II.To ensure genuineness of Khadi, “Khadi Mark” regulation has been notified by Government of India.In addition, 91 Khadi Institutions were assisted in Uttar Pradesh under Khadi Reform Development Programme (KRDP), one of the initiatives taken by KVIC for development of Khadi in the country.
