                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

362






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Financial Assistance to Rehabilitation Centres




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is providing financial assistance to rehabilitation centres for the welfare of differently-abled persons in the country;(b)	if so, the quantum of financial assistance provided to various State Governments including Uttar Pradesh during the last five years;(c)	whether various State Governments have submitted proposals seeking Central grants for the said purpose; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a)	and (b) Yes, Sir. While relief to the persons with disabilities is a State subject by virtue of entry ‘9’ of the State list of the Constitution of India, the Central Government supplements the efforts of the State Governments for the welfare of persons with disabilities (PwDs).The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities is implementing a central sector scheme called Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS) under which Grant-in-Aid is provided to Programme Implementing Agencies (PIAs) for running any of the prescribed model projects under the Scheme relating to rehabilitation/ empowerment of persons with disabilities. A statement indicating State-wise Grant-in-Aid released to PIAs including to the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last five years under DDRS is at Annexure-I.The District Disability Rehabilitation Centres (DDRCs) are set up in the approved districts of the country for providing comprehensive rehabilitation services to persons with disabilities at the grass root level. A statement indicating State-wise Grant-in-Aid released to DDRCs including in the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last five years is at Annexure-II.(c)	and (d) A statement indicating number of proposals received through State Governments/ Union Territory Administration and number of proposals sanctioned during the last five years under DDRS is at Annexure-III.*****
