                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2611






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Expansion of National Highway Network




Kripanath Mallah













                                                    Jaskaur Meena
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to expand the National Highway network in the country including Assam;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the funds estimated for the purpose;(d) the targets set for such expansion; and(e) the time by which the said targets are likely to be achieved? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e) The Ministry keeps on receiving proposals from various State Governments / Union Territories (UTs) etc., including from the State of Assam, for declaration / upgradation of State roads as new National Highways (NHs). The Ministry considers declaration of some State roads as NHs from time to time based on the requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority and availability of funds.	Length of NHs has been increased from about 91,287 km as on 1st April 2014 to about 1,41,190 km as on date.Works on notified NHs, including newly declared NHs, are taken up as per inter-se-priority, progress of ongoing works, availability of funds and traffic density. No financial allocation is separately made for such upgradation of State Roads as NHs.*****
