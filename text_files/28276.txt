                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the main features of the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana;b)	the details of funds sanctioned, allocated and utilised so far under this scheme in tribal dominated States including Jharkhand, Odisha, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh and Maharashtra;c)	whether the Government has received any information regarding misuse of funds allocated under this scheme; andd)	if so, the details thereof along with the measures taken by the Government to tackle the problem?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) & (b):  A Statement indicating the main features of the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package (PMGKP) is at Annexure-I.  Statement indicating State-wise details of benefits provided/funds released under the scheme, including those for tribal dominated States are at Annexure-II.(c) & (d): All the financial transactions under the PMGKP were done through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) into the account of the beneficiaries. No information regarding misuse has been received.****
