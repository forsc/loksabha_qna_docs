                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4465






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






National Commission of Indian Medicine




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) whether the National Commission of Indian Medicine has not included Naturopathy system in medical system, if so, the reasons therefor; (b) whether proposal to include Naturopathy is lying pending with the Government, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) the time by which it is likely to be included?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c):          Yoga and Naturopathy is not included in the National Commission for Indian System of Medicine Bill, 2019 as the Yoga and Naturopathy are drugless systems and the rights and privileges of Yoga and Naturopathy professionals are different from the Indian Systems of Medicine.             Further, to regulate the education in Yoga and Naturopathy, the Ministry has established Yoga Certification Board (YCB) to regulate the education of Yoga in the Country and has formed a Naturopathy Certification Board to regulate the education of Naturopathy in the Country.………………………
