                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Consumption of Fertilizers




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the annual consumption of fertilizers in LMT for each major type of fertilizer (Urea, DAP, NPK, Potash and SSP) and the annual expenditure on the same by farmers of Telangana for the past seven years from 2015- 16 to the current year 2021-22; (b) the factor by which the expenditure on fertilizers by farmers of Telangana has grown between 2015-16 and present year; (c) the trend of average per hectare consumption of each major type of fertilizer by farmers of the Telangana in the past seven years; and (d) the fertilizer component of the cost of cultivation of the three largest crops of Telangana for the six years from 2015-16 to 2020-21?
