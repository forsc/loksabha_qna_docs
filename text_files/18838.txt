                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Development of Industrially Backward Areas




Mahabali Singh













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Janardan Singh Sigriwal
                                                








                                                    Rekha Arun Verma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)    	whether the Government has identified the industrially backward districts/areas of Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh and Bihar; (b)      if so, the details thereof district-wise; (c)    whether the Government has formulated any policy for providing favourable conditions and investment  system/plan for industrial development in these areas and if so, the details thereof; (d)    whether industries have been set up on the basis of local resources in these backward districts/ areas; (e)  	if so,  the details of efforts made by the Government for the industrial development of backward districts/areas of Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh and Bihar; and (f)     if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a)to(f)  	Industrial Development of backward districts of the country is handled by various Ministries/ Departments as per Allocation of Business Rules. Ministries/Departments formulate action plans and policies for their respective schemes. This Department does not maintain information on the schemes proposed by the line Ministries/ Departments on centralized basis.*******
