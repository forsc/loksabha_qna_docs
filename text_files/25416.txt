                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1228






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Food Grains to States




P. Ravindhranath














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the allocation of additional food grains recommended by the Ministry followed by the COVID19 Pandemic, from April 2020 to June 2021, month-wise and State-wise; (b) 	whether the said allocation of food grains have been provided free of cost as part of relief measures by the Union Government to States; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c) : 		Under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana, Govt of India has made additional allocation of foodgrains @ 5 kg per person per month free of cost for around 80 crore beneficiaries of all the 36 States/UTs covered under NFSA (Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) & Priority Households (PHH)) including those covered under Direct Benefit Transfer(DBT) for a period of 8 months i.e April-November, 2020 and again for a period of 7 months i.e May-November, 2021.
