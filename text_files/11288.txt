                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

324






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Memorials for Freedom Fighters




T. R. Paarivendhar













                                                    P.R. Natarajan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government honours the freedom fighters by erecting memorials in their name and if so, the details of such freedom fighters in the country, particularly in Tamil Nadu;(b) whether the Government will consider erecting a memorial in Delhi in honour of and recognition of the freedom fighters across the country who have scrificedtheir life for the freedom/independence of the country;(c) the total number of freedom fighters who participated and lost their lives during the freedom struggle in the country, particularly in the State of Tamil Nadu;(d) whether memorials to the foreigners who have participated in the independence struggle were also having freedom fighters memorials in India; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b)	Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs (MoHUA) carries out construction work of memorials, if so mandated by the concerned Ministry. CPWD, under MoHUA, had constructed National Salt Satyagraha Memorial at Dandi, Gujarat for Ministry of Culture and Dr. Ambedkar National Memorial at Delhi for Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment.-2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.324 FOR 04.02.2020(c)		With regard to freedom fighters who participated and lost their           		life during the freedom struggle, the Indian Council of Historical           		Research has published 5 volumes (7 books) under the title of              		Dictionary of Martyrs: India''s Freedom Struggle (1857-1947).                  		The volumes contain brief biographical write-ups on the                        		Martyrs across the country who sacrificed their life for India''s               		Freedom Struggle between 1857 and 1947 based on extant                      		possible primary sources collected during the period of research  		work.  The research work only confined enlisting Martyrs not            		Freedom Fighters.  These volumes contain over 13,521                  		martyrs across the country including 154 from Tamil Nadu.  (d) & (e)	No such details pertaining to Memorials of foreigners who have           		participated in independent struggle are maintained.*****
