                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4064






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






UDAN Scheme




Anantkumar  Hegde














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of flights started and operationalised till date under UDAN scheme, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government is aware of low success rate of flights under UDAN scheme in north east region as compared to the rest of the country; and(c) if so, the reasons therefor and the corrective steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Under Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik), 236 RCS routes have been commenced by Selected Airline Operators (SAOs). Details of such RCS routes commenced so far are attached as Annexure. RCS - UDAN is market driven scheme. The interested airlines based on their assessment of demand on particular routes submit their proposals at the time of bidding under RCS - UDAN. Further, States of North Eastern Region have been included as Priority Area(s) in the RCS - UDAN version 2.0. Helicopter services have also been included in the scheme for better connectivity of Priority Area(s). In Priority Area(s), the number of RCS Flights to be operated on  RCS Routes in a week with Viability Gap Funding (VGF) shall be a minimum of three (3) and a maximum of fourteen (14) departures per week from the same RCS Airport so that the RCS Flights are operated on at least three(3) days of the week. In addition to above, enhanced VGF has also been offered on certain types of aircraft categories in the RCS - UDAN document.
