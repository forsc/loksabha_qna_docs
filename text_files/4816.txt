                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Mandate of CCRYN




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether Central Council for Research in Yoga and Naturopathy (CCRYN), an autonomous body was constituted with a mandate for scientific research in the field, has established centres to cater to the services in yoga and naturopathy across the country; (b) the number of such wellness centres and OPDs functional, locationwise; and (c) whether CCRYN proposes to establish Yoga Parks in different places of the country, location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):      The Central Council for Research in Yoga and Naturopathy (CCRYN), an autonomous body under Ministry of AYUSH has established a Central Research Institute  (CRI) of Yoga & Naturoapthy at, Rohini, Delhi where Yoga & Naturopathy treatments are being provided both under IPD (20 bedded) and OPD for the benefit of common people.In addition CCRYN also runs 15 Wellness Centres / OPDs in various Govt. hospitals / institutions in different parts of the country. CCRYN is also in process of establishment of following PG institutes and CRIs of Yoga & Naturopathy in the country:1.	Establishment of Post Graduate Institutes of Yoga and Naturopathy Education and Research (PGIYNER) with 200 bedded Yoga and Naturopathy hospital at Jhajjar, Haryana and Nagamangla, Karnataka.2.	Establishment of Central Research Institutes of Yoga & Naturopathy in Odisha, West Bengal, Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh along with 100 bedded indoor hospital facilities to carry out in depth research studies to establish the efficacy of Yoga and Naturopathy in various remedies.(b):      The details list of Wellness Centres / OPDs is given at Annexure.(c):      At present there is no such proposal..................
