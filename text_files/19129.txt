                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

579






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Implementation of JJM




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has reviewed the implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in various States and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has set any target for achieving the goals; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the details of the fund released to the State of Kerala and the amount utilized so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) 	Government of India, in partnership with State Governments, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM)-Har Ghar Jal to enable every rural household in the country to have assured potable water through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024.To ensure the successful implementation, Government of India has been regularly reviewing the implementation with respective State governments. Regularreview meetings including conferences and workshops through video conferences are held as well asfield visits taken upwherein States are advised to plan and expedite the implementation to achieve the goal in a time-bound manner.As reported by States/ UTs, as of date, out of 19.18 Crore rural households in the country, 6.55 Crore (34.20%)are having potable tap water supply. (d) 	Under JJM, allocation, fund drawn by the Kerala State and reported utilization of Central grant is as under:(Amount in Rs. Crore)Financial year	Opening Balance	Allocation	Amount drawn by the State	Reported utilization	Unspent amount2019-20	2.58	248.76	101.29	62.69	41.182020-21	41.18	404.24	101.06	86.65	55.59****
