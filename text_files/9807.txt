                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3237






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Upgradation of Food Craft Institute




Dushyant Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Tourism Secretary has received letters dated 06.03.2018 and 03.04.2018 regarding the request to upgrade the Food Craft Institute, Baran to State Institute of Hotel Management; (b) 	if so, whether the Government considers Rajasthan a heritage rich State with huge opportunities to generate employment in the tourism sector; (c) 	if so, whether the Government has plans to upgrade Food Craft Institute, Baran to State Institute of Hotel Management; (d) 	if so, the time by which it is likely to be upgraded; and (e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Yes, Sir. (b) to (e):	Ministry of Tourism, Government of India sanctioned project for establishing  of Food Craft Institute (FCI), Baran in November 2015 with Central Financial Assistance of Rs.6.45 crores. The construction is under progress.Subsequently, Ministry of Tourism received request from Government of Rajasthan for conversion of this under construction Food Craft Institute (FCI) into a State IHM in March, 2018.  The proposal has been examined in the Ministry of Tourism in consultation with National Council for Hotel Management and Catering Technology. The required details sought by Ministry of Tourism from the Govt. of Rajasthan has not been received.*******
