                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

77






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Krishonnati Yojana




Subrata Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Green Revolution – Krishonnati Yojana introduced in the agricultural sector for the period 2017-18 to 2019-20 has reached completion;(b)	the details of the achievements made by the Government in this regard;(c)	the number of schemes/missions covered under this by the Government;(d)	the manner in which it is likely to help in doubling the income of farmers by 2022; and(e)	whether the Government intends to extend it further and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	The Scheme was for the period 2017-18 to 2019-20 and was extended till 2020-21.(b):	Financial achievement for Krishonnati Yojana is at Annexure – I.(c):	A list of Schemes under Green Revolution  Krishonnati Yojana are at Annexure – II.(d):	All the Schemes of this Ministry are aimed at improving/doubling the farmer’s income.(e):	Yes, Sir.
