                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






JNU MCQ Entrance Exams




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the new JNU MCQ entrance exams formats by NationalTesting Agency;(b) if so, whether the Government is satisfied with such process of evaluating students in everysubjective courses therein;(c) the fee being charged for viewing the official answer sheet and marks therefor; and(d) the reasons for not uploading the evaluation sheets in net?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) to (d) Jawaharlal Nehru University has informed that in order to ensure admission toUniversity in an unbiased and objective manner, the Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) format hasbeen opted. The National Testing Agency (NTA) has been entrusted with the conduct of JawaharlalNehru University Entrance Examination 2019 and Combined Entrance Examination Biotechnology2019. The stipulated fee is only for challenging the answer keys for different question papers as theprocess involves inviting experts who review the objection of the candidate and the correctness ofthe original answer. Each candidate is able to view his or her answer key response for theirrespective question papers and compare it with the correct answer key.*****
