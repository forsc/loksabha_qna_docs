                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1657






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Financial Assistance to Sports Institutions




Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of financial assistance provided to various institutions engaged in promotion and development of sports in Gujarat during the last three years; and(b) the details of targets set and achievements made in this regard during the above period?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, primary responsibility for promotion and development of sports is that of State/Union Territory Governments. Central Government supplements efforts of the State/ UT Governments, under its various schemes.Financial assistance has been given for bridging the gaps in infrastructure in Gujarat through one of the vertical namely ‘Utilization and creation of Sports Infrastructure’ of Khelo India scheme and details of assistance given are at Annexure-I.The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports (MYAS) through Sports Authority of India (SAI) has also been implementing SAI Training Centre (STC) and Centre of Excellence (COE) Schemes to identify and nurture talented sportspersons in Gandhinagar (Gujarat). The details of the trainees trained during the last three years (2016-2019) at Regional Centre of SAI, Gandhinagar (Gujarat) are as under:<pre>SN 	Year		Residential Trainees	Non-Residential Trainees	Total Trainees1	2016-17			306			11				3172	2017-18			314			10				3243	2018-19			303			4				307</pre>The selected trainees are provided assistance in the form of expert coaches, sports equipment, boarding and lodging, sports kit, competition exposure, educational expenses, medical/ insurance and stipend as per the approved scheme norms.*****
