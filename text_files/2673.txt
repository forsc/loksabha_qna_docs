                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2443






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Privatisation of ESI Hospitals




Adoor Prakash













                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of hospitals functioning under Employee State Insurance Corporation (ESIC), State-wise;(b)the details of new hospitals sanctioned and the present status thereof; (c)whether the Government proposes to open new hospitals under ESIC and if so, the details thereof; and (d)whether the Government has any plan for privatization of ESI hospitals and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	A total of 47 ESIC Hospitals are functioning under Employee State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) across the country. State-wise list of these ESIC Hospitals is at Annexure-I.(b):	The details of new ESIC/ESIS hospitals sanctioned are at Annexure-II. (c):	The details of new hospitals sanctioned along with their present status are at Annexure-III.(d):	There are no plans to privatize ESI hospitals at present.  ***                                                    *******
