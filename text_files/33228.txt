                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

786






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Indian Council of Social Science Research




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the annual budget of Indian Council of Social Science Research during the last three years;(b) the amount of buget allocation utilised by Indian Council of Social Science Research out of the total budget allocation;(c) the details of the amount given by Indian Council of Socail Science Research to its subordinate institutions during the last three years, Institution-wise; and(d) the details of heads under which the allocated amount has been used by Madhya Pradesh Council of Social Science Research during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) & (b): The annual budget of Indian Council of Social Science Research (ICSSR), New Delhi and utilization by it during the last three years are given below:<pre>Financial Year	Annual Budget (Rs. in Lakh)	Funds Utilized (Rs. in Lakh)2018-19	16,289.41	15,288.222019-20	14,349.96	14,129.982020-21	11,958.43	10,438.02</pre>(c): ICSSR, New Delhi has no subordinate institution, but has six (06) Regional Centres.(d): ICSSR, New Delhi does not provide any grant-in-aid to Madhya Pradesh Council of Social Science Research. *****
