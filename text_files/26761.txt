                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2389






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Schemes for Horticulture




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of schemes for horticulture, orchards and tree plantation being implemented especially in various blocks of Shahjahanpur parliamentary constituency of Uttar Pradesh during the last three years; (b) the total amount of funds allocated separately for each of these schemes, State-wise during the said period; (c) whether any new technology has been developed for these areas by Indian Council for agricultural research or any other similar institute; (d)   if so, the details thereof; and (e) the steps taken to promote these schemes and educate potential beneficiaries?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): 	Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), a centrally sponsored scheme is being implemented for the holistic development of horticulture sector in selected districts in the country covering fruits, vegetables, root and tuber crops, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconut, cashew, cocoa. 		Shahjahanpur District of Uttar Pradesh is not covered under MIDH. However, districts not covered under MIDH are provided support for horticulture activities under another Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Ministry of Agriculture namely Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY).    Accordingly, support for horticulture activities is being provided in the Shahjahanpur district under RKVY.  (b) 	State-wise details of the fund allocated under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) and Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) during last 3 years is at Annexure – I & II respectively.(c) & (d): 	ICAR-CISH, Lucknow and ICAR-IIVR, Varanasi  under Horticultural Science Division of ICAR have developed following technologies for use in areas including Shahjahanpur Parliamentary Constituency of Uttar Pradesh:1.	Rejuvenation technology for old and senile mango orchards.2.	Centre opening technology for enhancing productivity and quality of mango.3.	Application of paclobutrazol for regularity in bearing in mango.4.	Mango orchard based poultry farming.5.	Integrated pest and disease management of mango.6.	Technology on guava cultivation.7.	Technologies/varieties developed on different vegetables.(e): Under Human Resource Development (HRD) component of MIDH, assistance is provided for awareness programmes, farmers training, exposure visit, etc. Training is provided on latest technologies on production and post-harvest management to women and youth.
