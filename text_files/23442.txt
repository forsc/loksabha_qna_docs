                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

396






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Voluntary Retirement by CAPF Personnel




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there have been a large number of voluntary retirements in Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF) particularly in BSF cadre;(b) if so, the details thereof during each of the last three years and the current year, Cadre-wise along with the reasons therefor;(c) the details of the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard;(d) whether it is a fact that the satisfaction level for housing among CAPF personnel is very low, particularly in SSB and ITBP cadres and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of the measures taken by the Government to improve the satisfaction level for housing among the CAPF personnel?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI) (a)  to (e):	    A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. --2--THE STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.396 FOR ANSWER ON 23.03.2021 BY SHRI  RAVNEET SINGH BITTU.(a) & (b): The details of voluntary retirement in Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) & Assam Rifles including BSF during the last three years and the current year are given at Annexure. An analysis carried out by the Forces indicates that personal and domestic reasons including children/family issues; health issues of self or family members; social/family obligations and commitments and better career opportunities are some of the major reasons for voluntary retirement.(c):	The corrective measures taken by the Government inter alia include organizing regular Sainik Sammelans, Seminars, Interactive and counseling sessions at various levels to redress the grievances, if any, of Force personnel. The remedial measures for increasing satisfaction level of Force personnel such as grant of leave, option for choice posting, posting of couples at same stations, providing family accommodation, provision of medical and education facilities, etc. are taken regularly.  Further, prior to acceptance of voluntary retirement, individuals concerned are counseled by the Head of office and Head of Department about consequences and likely financial hardship they might face after voluntary retirement. Cases of voluntary retirements are processed only when the individuals are not willing to continue in services even after counseling by senior officers. --3--L.S.S.Q.NO. 396 FOR 23.03.2021(d) & (e): 	The present housing satisfaction level (as on 01.01.2021) in CAPFs is 45.74% against authorized dwelling units. Presently 23,973 houses are under construction and are expected to be completed during 2021-22. With the completion of above houses, the housing satisfaction level will increase to 54.67%.The Force-wise details are given in the table below:-CAPFs	No. of authorized dwelling units 	No. of dwelling units available	Percentage(%) of satisfaction level	Dwelling units under construction 	Satisfaction level (in %) after completion of under construction housesAR	25480	13130	51.53	1568	57.68BSF	78164	35004	44.78	5399	51.69CISF#	14661	6422	43.80	2275	59.32CRPF	88523	46175	52.16	7774	60.94ITBP	28568	11408	39.93	4059	54.14NSG	3614	2923	80.88	64	82.65SSB	29331	7677	26.17	2834	35.84TOTAL	268341	122739	45.74	23973	54.67#In respect of Non-PSU deployment only(Accommodation for CISF personnel posted in PSUs is provided by respective PSU).In order to increase housing satisfaction level of CAPF personnel, following measures have been taken:-(i)	Enhancement of financial powers of Director General of CAPFs for execution of works.(ii)	Authorization to Technical Officers of Engineering Wings in CAPFs to exercise powers of technical sanction, tender acceptance, etc. at par with CPWD.--4--L.S.S.Q.NO. 396 FOR 23.03.2021(iii)	It has been decided to commence online allotment of Departmental Pool Residential Accommodation as well as Separated Family Accommodation (SFA) to CAPF personnel in order to enhance utilization of existing accommodation, apart from bringing transparency in allotment process.*****
