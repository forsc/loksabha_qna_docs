                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4208






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Rooftop Helipad in AIIMS




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is contemplating to construct rooftop helipad in All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives thereto; (b) whether the clearance and necessary permission to get the helipad operational from the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) have been taken and if so, the details thereof; (c) the total expenditure involved and the time by which the rooftop helipad is likely to be constructed in AIIMS; (d) whether some private hospitals in the country have such facility which is very costly and beyond the reach of common masses and if so, the details thereof; and (e) whether this facility will be accessible to poor patients in terms of affordability?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e): 	The rooftop helipad is a part of the Power Grid Vishram Sadan building which has been constructed under Corporate Social Responsibility funding provided by the Power Grid Corporation India Ltd. The total cost of the 10 storey building including the helipad is Rs.32.67 crore. Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) has granted site approval for construction of roof top Helipad at AIIMS, New Delhi.  DGCA has not granted operational authorization to any Roof top helipad for any private hospital till date. *****
