                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4152






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






SWAMIH Scheme




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	the total amount disbursed under the SWAMIH scheme; b.	the details and total number of projects currently being financed through this scheme; andc.	the number of applications received under this scheme? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a):	As of March 15, 2021, SWAMIH Fund has disbursed Rs. 1,036.4 crore.(b):	As of March 15, 2021, the Fund has provided final approval to 65 projects with a sanctioned amount of Rs. 6,297 crore helping to complete 41,255 housing units.(c):	As of March 5, 2021, the Fund has evaluated over 862 proposals. ****
