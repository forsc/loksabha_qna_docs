                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

743






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Scientific and Technical posts in CZA




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Scientific and Technical Posts created in the Central Zoo Authority (CZA) at the time of its inception along with the number of such posts subsequently added; (b) 	the details of the recruitment rules for such posts; and (c) 	the details of the Scientific and Technical posts newly created or abolished along with the total strength of officials presently working in CZA as on date? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a), (b) and (c) As per information received from the Central Zoo Authority, there is no distinction as Scientific and Technical posts in the Central Zoo Authority. However, details of total posts created in the Central Zoo Authority (CZA) at the time of its inception and further subsequent addition with present strength, along with details of recruitment rules is  placed at Annexure I.***
