                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3102






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Cotton Corporation of India




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Cotton Corporation of India (CCI) has procured organic cotton from farmers of various States specially from Maharashtra at Minimum Support Price in last five years; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, Statewise specially in Maharashtra; (c) 	whether the organic cotton growers are getting adequate price for their produce; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the steps taken by the Government to give remunerative price of organic cotton to the farmers? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): There is no separate Minimum Support Price (MSP) for organic cotton. Therefore, Cotton Corporation of India (CCI) has not procured organic cotton under MSP. (c) to (e):  There is no separate Minimum Support Price (MSP) for organic cotton.  However, Organic produce commands a higher price in the market in comparison to non-organic produce. Government is promoting organic farming in a cluster approach to bring down its costs which is expected to increase return to the farmers, through Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) using traditional resources and organic products.   ***
