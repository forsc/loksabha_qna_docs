                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

765






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Green Building Concept




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated any proposal to 	develop 	green building concept in order to reduce overuse of 	resources;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the steps taken by the Government to bring better 	technological 	expertise as well as green architecture in order 	to develop 	green buildings across the country?






ANSWER




                                                                      THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Housing and Urban  Affairs has formulated Model Building Bye Laws (MBBL)- 2016, which has the chapter on ‘Green Building and Sustainability Provisions’. Further, Ministry has amended the Model Building Bye Laws (MBBL)- 2016 and Urban and Regional Development Plan Formulation and Implementation (URDPFI)- 2014 guidelines for incorporating the provisions of India Cooling Action Plan (ICAP) which advocated the reduced dependence on conventional sources of energy for cooling purpose.       Under ICAP, Ministry has also issued pictorial advisory to the all the States/ Union Territories (UTs), Schools of Planning and Architecture (SPAs) and other stakeholders engaged in building construction activities on some of the technological solutions adopted on ground by different organizations.                           *****
