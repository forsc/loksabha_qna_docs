                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5332






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Construction of Bypass in Maharashtra




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government proposes to build bypass in major cities of the country and if so, the norms prescribed in this regard;(b)	the details of the cities of Maharashtra for which the construction of bypass is sanctioned or under consideration;(c)	 whether any proposal is under consideration for the construction of bypass in Wardha in view of the heavy traffic; (d)	 if so, the time by which it is likely to be sanctioned; and(e)	 the details of the cities of Maharashtra where the construction of bypass has been completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Yes Sir, Construction of bypass depends upon the volume of traffic, congestion and geometrics of the existing road along with technical, financial feasibility of proposed bypass alignment.(b)	The details of the cities of Maharashtra for which the construction of bypass is sanctioned or under consideration are enclosed at Annexure- I.(c) & (d) Existing 2-lane Wardha bypass is included in the 4-laning of Wardha Butibori section of NH-361. The project has been undertaken for construction.(e)	 The details of the cities of Maharashtra where the construction of bypass has been completed are enclosed at Annexure- II.
