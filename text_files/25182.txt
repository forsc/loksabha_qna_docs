                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

970






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Jobs for Locals in Mangalore




Prajwal Revanna














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government is aware of the recent protest in Mangalore for demanding jobs for locals in Mangalore Refinery and Petrochemicals Limited (MRPL) where employment opportunities are denied for sons of soil by the company; (b)if so, the details of locals recruited on regular/contractual basis at all levels in MRPL;(c)whatever there is any Government policy for jobs for locals in PSUs/Pvt limited companies or any other Government department;(d)the reasons for denying employment opportunities to locals in MRPL; and (e)the action taken by the Government to give employment opportunities to locals in the recent past and punitive action taken by the Government against the companies which violated such rules?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		Yes sir, the Government is aware about a recent protest in Mangalore for demanding jobs for locals in Mangalore Refinery and Petrochemicals Limited (MRPL). (b):		MRPL, which is a Central Public Sector Undertaking,  gives fair and equal opportunity to all candidates including local residents as per merit in the selection process following Government of India guidelines. The details of the employment provided by MRPL are as below :Contd..2/-:: 2 ::Employees	Total	From KarnatakaRegular Management	904	402Regular Non Management	1205	896Contract	2985	2635(c) to (e):	MRPL, being a central Public Sector Undertaking of Government of India follows Government of India orders issued from time to time for recruitments and gives fair opportunity to all eligible candidates, who are citizens of India to appear for its selection process.MRPL had given fair opportunity to all and the candidates were selected on Merit.******
