                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4152






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






DD National Channel




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of the programmes and the numbers of viewers of DD National Channel; (b)	whether the Government has allocated/released any funds for improving the programme content/increasing the numbers of viewers of the above channel; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) At present, Doordarshan has 35 operating satellite TV channels which includes 6 all India channels, 17 regional channels, 11 State networks and 1 International channel.  These are available on DD FreeDish and approximately 33 million households have access to DD FreeDish throughout the country.(b) & (c): Government has allocated Rs.234.51 crore to Doordarshan for content development under the scheme “Broadcasting Infrastructure and Network Development” for the period from 2017-18 to 2019-20. ***********************
