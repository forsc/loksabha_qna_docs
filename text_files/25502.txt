                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1273






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Artificial Insemination




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Government and private semen banks at present in the country which have been producing foreign semen for cow and buffales for the last five years, State-wise; (b) Whether any monitoring of the breeds produced with the assistance of these semen banks is undertaken by the Government; (c) if so, the number of cows and buffaloes produced through artificial insomination with the asistance of these semen banks and the total cost involved therein breed-wise; and (d) the details in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) As per the reports received from the States there are 59  Government and private semen banks in the country producing  semen doses of indigenous breeds of cattle and buffalo and exotic breeds of cattle. State wise details of the semen doses produced from exotic breeds of cattle during the last five years is given at Annexure-I. (b) to (d)   No, Sir. However, under the component of Nationwide Artificial Insemination Programme (NAIP) of the scheme Rashtriya Gokul Mission, monitoring has been done through online reporting at   Information Network for Animal Productivity and Health  ( INAPH ) portal http://inaph.nddb.coop/. Under the Nation Wide AI Programme a total of 1.81 crore cattle and buffaloes have been covered, 2.22 crore artificial inseminations performed with an assistance of Rs.300.45 crore. Data on number of progenies born under the programme is being uploaded so far data on 4.65 lakh calves born under the programme has been uploaded on the INAPH data base. *****
