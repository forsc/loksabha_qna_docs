                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Setting up Wagon Factory in Sitalapalli




Chandra Sekhar Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Railways has sanctioned a Wagon Factory to be set up in Sitalapalli in Ganjam District of Odisha; (b)	if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of estimated expenditure likely to be incurred in setting up of the said factory and the funds so far sanctioned, released and utilised in this regard;(d) whether the estimated cost of setting up factory has now increased due to delay in the project; and(e) if so, the details and the corrective steps taken by the Railways for timely completion and operation of the said factory?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY                                     (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e) Wagon factories are set up keeping in view the Railway’s overall requirement of the wagons. At present, there is no proposal to set up a wagon factory at Sitalapalli in Ganjam District of Odisha. *****
