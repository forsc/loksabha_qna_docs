                                    CULTURE

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

972






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Funds Released for Safety and Facilities in Monuments




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is concerned for safety and convenience of the tourists visiting the protected monuments after Corona pandemic;(b)	whether the Government has sanctioned/released any funds during the last one year for providing facilities to the tourists visiting the said monuments; and (c)	if so, the monument-wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Yes, Sir. The centrally protected monuments/sites have been opened for visitors subject to following the preventing measures and Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) issued from time to time by Ministry of Home Affairs, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Ministry of Culture and State/District administration.(b)&(c)	The expenditure on providing visitors facilities at centrally protected monuments/sites including ticketed monuments is met out from the overall sanctioned budget for conservation/preservation of monuments/sites.  The expenditure incurred on conservation, preservation and annual maintenance of all monuments during the last three years and provision for current year is at Annexure.
