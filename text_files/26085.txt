                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

162






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Licensing for AYUSH-64




Adala Prabhakara Reddy













                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state: (a) the number of companies that have come forward to obtain licence for manufacturing AYUSH-64, State/ UT-wise; (b) the details of approvals given to such companies by State Licensing Authorities along with the brand/ market names of this drug; (c) whether the transfer of technology for such companies is possible through the Central Council for Research in Ayurveda Science (CCRAS) and the National Research and Development Centre (NRDC); and(d) if so, whether the Central Council for Research in Ayurveda Science (CCRAS) will provide technical support to the Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani (ASU) drugs manufacturers in the manufacturing of AYUSH-64 and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYURVEDA, YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO.162* FOR 30TH JULY, 2021(a)	The list of manufacturers to whom AYUSH 64 technology has already been transferred through agreement from National Research Development Corporation (NRDC) and Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) as on 23.7.2021 is placed at Annexure-I which is also available at CCRAS’s websitewww.ccras.nic.in. (b)	The information obtained from the SLAs regarding details of approvals given to such companies along with the brand/market names of this drug is placed at Annexure-II.(c)	Yes, the details and the procedure are annexed as Annexure III. (d)	After technology transfer to a Manufacturer by NRDC and CCRAS the complete Know-how document of AYUSH-64 containing all relevant information, required for production is provided to the manufacturer. If required by the manufacturer, provision for training is also available. Also, to facilitate the Stake holders, CCRAS has issued a Notification dated 30.04.2021 for commercialization of AYUSH-64 developed by CCRAS.
