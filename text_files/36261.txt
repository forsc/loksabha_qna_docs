                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3572






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Unutilized Land of PSUs




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to return the additional or unutilised land out of the assets provided by the State Governments to Central Public Sector Undertakings under the Ministry at concessional rates to the respective States; (b) if so, whether any concrete plan in this regard is under consideration; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c)    No, Sir.      ****
