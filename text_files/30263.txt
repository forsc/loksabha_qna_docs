                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1648






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Stoppage at Panipat




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is a dire need to provide stoppage of the Garib Rath at Panipat in view of its being an international textile industry and a large number of labourers engaged here are from Uttar Pradesh and Bihar; and(b) if so, the steps taken by the Government to have a stoppage of Garib Rath From Amritsar to Saharsa at Panipat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b) 12203/12204 Saharsa-Amritsar Garib Rath Express is a long distance train having limited stoppages enroute, as such its stoppage at Panipat is operationally not expedient at present. However, provision of stoppages is an ongoing process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility and commercial justification.*****
