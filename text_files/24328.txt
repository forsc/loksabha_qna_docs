                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Development of Etawah Lion Safari Park




(Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up tourism centres to create employment opportunities in the tourism sector; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to develop Etawah Lion Safari Park Situated at Etawah, Uttar Pradesh as a national level tourism spot; (d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the total number of tourist spots established till date or proposed to be established in future in the State of Uttar Pradesh and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			              (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	Development of tourist places is primarily the responsibility of concerned State Government/UT Administration. However, the Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan- Integrated development of theme based tourist circuits and PRASHAD- National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive provides Central Financial Assistance to the State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. Proposal to develop Etawah Lion Safari Park Situated at Etawah, Uttar Pradesh is presently not under consideration of Ministry of Tourism. (e):	A list of projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD schemes by Ministry of Tourism to Uttar Pradesh is attached in the annexure.********
