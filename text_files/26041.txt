                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1801






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Setting up of Industries in Tribal Areas




Guman Singh Damor














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the scheme of the Government to set up the industries in the tribals dominated districts of the country;(b) 	whether the industries have been set up in Scheduled Tribe areas as per the scheme of the Government and if so, the details thereof, State-wise ;(c) 	whether tribal dominated districts of Alirajpur has been categorised as ‘no-industry area’;(d) 	if so, whether the Government is contemplating on any scheme to set up industries in this districts; and(e) 	if not, whether the Government proposes to formulate any such plan?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	Ministry is implementing Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) since 2008-09 through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) as nodal agency at the national level for generating employment in the country including tribal dominated districts by setting up of micro-enterprises in the non-farm sector. Under PMEGP, general category beneficiaries can avail of margin money subsidy of 25% of the project cost in rural areas and 15% in urban areas.  For beneficiaries belonging to special categories such as Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, OBCs, Minorities, Women, Ex-servicemen, Physically Handicapped, beneficiaries belonging to North Eastern Region, Hill and Border areas, etc., the Margin Money (MM) subsidy is 35% in rural areas and 25% in urban area. Since its inception till 30th June 2021, around 7 lakh micro enterprises have been assisted providing employment to an estimated 57 lakh persons. (b):	The details of micro units set up by Scheduled Tribe (ST) entrepreneurs during the last three years and current year under PMEGP is given at Annexure.(c) to (e): 	No Sir.
