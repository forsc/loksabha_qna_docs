                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1339






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Animal Husbandry in West Bengal




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the various schemes launched for the development of Animal Husbandry in the State of West Bengal and in various other States throughout the country during the last six years; (b) whether any new scheme is to be introduced in coming future, if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) the details of the fund sanctioned for the development of the Animal Husbandry in the budgetary allocation 2020 & how much percentage of fund is released/expend to the State Government of West Bengal till now; and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	The details of schemes implemented by the Government for development of animal husbandry in various States/UTs of the country, including the State of West Bengal during the last six years, are as under:I.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission(RGM)II.	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)III.	National Dairy Plan-I(NDP-I) (was closed on 29 Nov, 2019. No financial assistance was released during 2019-20)IV.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS)(discontinued from 2020-21)V.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund(DIDF)VI.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities (SDC&FPO)VII.	National Livestock Mission(NLM)VIII.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund(AHIDF)IX.	Livestock Health & Disease Control(LH&DC)X.	National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP) XI.	Livestock Census and Integrated Sample Survey(b)   Recently, Government has revised and realigned some of the components of the following existing schemes in order to have focussed development of animal husbandry and dairying across the country in the next 5 years starting 2021-22.I.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission(RGM)II.	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)III.	National Livestock Mission(NLM)IV.	Livestock Health & Disease Control(LH&DC)(c) and (d)  The details of funds allocated and funds released under schemes for development of Animal Husbandry in West Bengal, in the budgetary allocation 2020, are as under - (Rs. In Lakh)Name of the Scheme	Fund Allocation (tentative)	Fund ReleasedRashtriya Gokul Mission*	-	202.50National Livestock Mission**	0	0Livestock Health & Disease Control	2392.80	471.11National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP)#	-	1006.39*State-Wise fund allocation is not made under RGM as it is a demand driven scheme.**There was no State-Wise budgetary allocation during the year 2020 under NLM as no proposals was received from State Government of West Bengal and hence no fund was released to the state. However, during the last 6 years (from 2016-17), a total fund of Rs.1294.70 lakhs has been released to the State of West Bengal under NLM.#  Under NADCP,there is no state-wise allocation but funds are released to States/UTs on basis of action plan received from States/UTs.
