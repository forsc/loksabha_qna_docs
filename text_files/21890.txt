                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3098






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Misuse of Student Unions and Associations




Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel













                                                    Shardaben Anilbhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the fact that the misuse of student unions and associations in higher educational institutions is on rise in the country;(b) whether the Government proposes to take steps to review the objective behind setting up student unions in the country;(c) if so, whether a lot of students are participating in protests and are also involved in anti-national activities; and(d) if so, the action taken or proposed to be taken against those students?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a): Certain cases of violence were reported from some Higher Educational Institutions. (b): The Ministry had constituted a Committee under the Chairmanship of Shri J.M. Lyngdoh (Former Chief Election Commissioner) to frame the guidelines on Students’ Union Election in College/Universities. The University Grants Commission has directed all the Universities to take necessary steps to ensure strict compliance of the recommendations of the Lyngdoh committee.(c) & (d): Universities/ Higher Educational Institutions are statutory autonomous organizations. They are empowered to regulate & enforce discipline among the students by taking such actions as deemed necessary. *****
