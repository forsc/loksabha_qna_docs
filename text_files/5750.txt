                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5349






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Prescribed Standards for Helmet Manufacturing




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government recognises the helmets manufactured as per DOT, Snell and ECE standards (usually the imported helmets) as legitimate and safe along with the helmets bearing the ISI mark and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government recognises helmets with the DOT, Snell and ECE standards safer as compared to ISI marked helmets and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	whether the Government proposes to standardise imported helmets so as to meet the Indian laws and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 to  (c)   India has a Helmets Standard IS: 4151 which is recognised in the country and as  per Section 129 of  the Motor Vehicle Act, 1988, every person driving or riding (otherwise than in a side car, on a motor vehicle of any class or description) shall, while in a public place, wear protective headgear confirming to the standards of Bureau of Indian Standards.    *****
