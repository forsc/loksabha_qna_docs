                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3746






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Expansion of PM Kisan Yojana




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	 whether the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme has been expanded to cover all farmers’ families in the country, irrespective of the size of their land holdings and area of agricultural activity;(b) 	 if  so,  the  identified  target  of beneficiaries under the scheme in Kerala and the fields of agriculture to which these benefits  would  be  extended,  the  details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government also plans to  consider  establishing  a  regulator  to maintain prices of agricultural produces for the benefit of the farmers; and(d)	 if so the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 Yes, Sir. The ambit of the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme has been expanded to cover all farmers in the country, irrespective of the size of their land holdings, subject to exclusion criteria relating to higher income status.(b):	  As per the existing data of estimated number of operational holdings according to the Agriculture Census 2015-16, about 75,83,000 farmers are eligible for getting the benefits of PM-KISAN in Kerala.(c) & (d):	 The Government has already constituted the Commission for Agricultural Costs and Prices (CACP), which is an expert body for recommending Minimum Support Prices by taking into account cost of production, trends in domestic and international prices etc. ********
