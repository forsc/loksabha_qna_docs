                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Funds for FPI Schemes in Bihar




Dileshwar Kamait














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds released to the State of Bihar under different Food Processing Industries (PLIs) schemes the district-wise;(b)	whether funds released under above mentioned schemes have been utilized for the concerned schemes;(c)	if so, the details of the schemes of whose work has been completed or is near its completion; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) to (d): The Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a Central Sector umbrella scheme- Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector of the country including of Bihar. PMKSY is not region or state specific but demand driven, with no state-wise fund allocation. So far, a total of 10 projects have been approved under component schemes of PMKSY in Bihar. The status of projects along with grants released to these projects is placed at Annexure.MoFPI is also implementing Production Linked Incentive Scheme (PLIS) for Food Processing sector with an outlay of Rs.10,900 Cr. The scheme has been approved on 31.03.2021 and is to be implemented over a period of six years. The scheme is targeted to facilitate expansion of food processing capacity by creating champion brands in Food Processing Sector and there is no state-wise allocation of fund under this scheme.As part of the Aatmanirbhar Bharat Initiative, MoFPI is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme-PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises Scheme (PMFME) for providing financial, technical and business support for upgradation/ setting up of 2 lakh micro food processing enterprises during five years from 2020-21 to 2024-25 with an outlay of Rs.10,000 crore. Out of this, a total of 11,333 units have been allocated to Bihar with outlay of Rs 535.19 crore. So far, a total of Rs 9.56 Cr has been released to the state under the scheme.*****
