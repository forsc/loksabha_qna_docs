                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2598






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Rural Electrification Schemes




C. Lalrosanga














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of villages electrified under rural electrification schemes in the North-East, State-wise;(b) 	the total number of villages in different states of North-East which are yet to be covered by the said schemes; and(c) 	the quantum of funds sanctioned for rural electrification in the North-East during the last three years and the current year, State-wise?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) : 	The Government of India launched Deendayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana (DDUGJY) in December, 2014 for rural electrification works across the country including North East States. As reported by the North East States on Garv portal, all the inhabited un-electrified census villages stand electrified as on 28th April, 2018 under the scheme. The details are given at Annexure-I.(c) :		There is no upfront allocation of funds for any State/District under DDUGJY. Funds are released for sanctioned projects in instalments based on the reported utilization of the funds released in the previous instalments and fulfilment of stipulated conditions. The State-wise and year-wise details of funds disbursed during the last three years and the current year under DDUGJY (including RE & additional infra) are at Annexure-II.********
