                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2080






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Selection of Students for Republic Day




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the number of students selected and invited for the Republic Day programme along with the number and names of schools from which the students were selected;(b) the procedures followed for the selection of such students;(c) whether the Government will come up with a standard operating procedure to select students for programmes like Republic Day, Children’s Day, Independence Day etc. and to ensure a fair procedure; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  In so far as Republic Day 2020 is concerned, 636 students from 4 schools participated, as detailed hereunder:-Sl. No.	Name of Schools/Zonal Cultural Centre	No. of Students1.	AG DAV Public School, Model Town, Delhi	1602.	Vinay Nagar Bengali SSS, Sarojini Nagar, New Delhi	1623.	SKV B-Block, Janakpuri, New Delhi	1644.	West Zone Cultural Centre (WZCC), Udaipur	150(b) to (d):  There is a well established system for the selection of school children items in the Rupublic Day Parade.  The performances of the various schools which offer to be considered for selection for participation in the Republic Day Parade are assessed by an Expert Committee, consisting of eminent persons in the field of dance, music, choregraphy etc.  Based on such assessment, the Expert Committee shortlists the schools for participation in the Republic Day Parade.*****
