                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1505






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Food Processing Industry




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the current status of food processing industries along with the details thereof, State-wise including Haryana; and(b)	the total amount of grants released by the Government to all the States including Haryana along with the details thereof during the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	The State-wise total number of registered food processing factories/units and factories/units in operation as per the latest Annual Survey of Industries (ASI), 2016- 17 is at Annexure-I.(b):	The Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a Central Sector Umbrella Scheme- PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA (PMKSY) with a total allocation of Rs.6000 crore for the period 2016-2020 and with the scheme components viz: (i) Mega Food Parks (MFP), (ii) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure (Cold Chain), (iii) Infrastructure for Agro-processing Clusters (APC), (iv) Creation of Backward and Forward Linkages (BFL) (v) Creation / Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC), (vi) Food Safety and Quality Assurance Infrastructure, (vii) Human Resources and Institutions and (viii) Operation Greens for overall development of food processing sector in the country including the State of Haryana. The scheme component ‘Operation Greens’for integrated development of value chain exclusively of Tomato, Onion and Potato (TOP) crops is implemented on a pilot basis in selected States only. Under the above schemes, mostly credit-linked capital subsidy in the form of grants-in-aid to the individuals, farmers, Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), Entrepreneurs, Cooperatives, Societies, Self Help Groups (SHGs), Private Companies and Central/ State PSUs etc. is provided for setting up of various food processing industries/projects/units. The Schemes are demand-driven and financial assistance is provided to the eligible applicants and against expressions of interests issued from time to time by the MoFPI. The State-wise (including the State of Haryana) grants-in- aid released during the least three years under different component schemes of PMKSY is at Annexure-II.*****
