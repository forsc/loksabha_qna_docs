                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2143






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






City Gas Distribution projects in UP




Kamlesh  Paswan













                                                    Ajay Kumar Mandal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the cities of Uttar Pradesh which have been selected for the City Gas Distribution (CGD);(b) the areas in those cities where the distribution of gas has been started through pipes and theareas where the work related to infrastructural development is still in progress;(c) whether the Government is formulating any plan for CGD in other cities of Uttar Pradesh aswell and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether there is any plan to provide Pipe Natural Gas (PNG) connection to all under city gasdistribution in Bhagalpur Parliamentary Constituency including Bhagalpur District and if so, thedetails thereof; and(e) whether any agency has been deployed for the implementation of this scheme and if so, thedetails thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c) Establishment of CNG Stations is part of the development of City Gas Distribution(CGD) network and the same is carried out by the entities authorized by Petroleum and NaturalGas Regulatory Board (PNGRB). PNGRB identifies Geographical Areas (GAs) for authorizingthe development of CGD network in synchronization with the development of natural gaspipeline connectivity and natural gas availability. About 232 GAs covering 407 districts spreadover 27 States/UTs including Uttar Pradesh have been authorized for the development of CGDnetworks. List of GAs in Uttar Pradesh authorized by PNGRB is at Annexure. Planning andimplementation of expansion of CGD network is done by PNGRB based on availability ofnatural gas pipeline/source and techno-commercial viability.(d) and (e) PNGRB has authorized Bhagalpur District to Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL)as a part of Lakhisarai, Munger and Bhagalpur Districts GA for development of CGD network.****
