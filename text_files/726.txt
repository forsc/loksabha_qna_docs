                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Road Accidents




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of road accidents reported during the last three years along with the number of casualties therein;(b) whether the Government proposes to entrust the police with road accidents reporting apart from registering cases of accidents;(c) if so, the details and the objectives thereof;(d) the steps being taken to adopt modern and state-of-the-art techniques in investigation of accident cases;(e) whether the committee on accident cases investigations has since submitted its report; and(f) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes statistics inter alia on road accidents. The published reports in this regard  are  available  till  the  year  2015.   As  per the data compiled by -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.636 FOR 25.06.2019NCRB, number of road accidents, persons killed and injured in road accidents in the country during the year 2013, 2014 and 2015 is given in the table below: Years	Total number of Road Accidents	Total number of Persons Killed	Total number of Persons Injured2013	4,43,001	1,37,423	4,69,8822014	4,50,898	1,41,526	4,77,7312015	4,64,674	1,48,707	4,82,389(b) & (c):	All road accident cases, reported to Police, form part of the statistics compiled by NCRB. (d): ‘Police’ is a State subject under the seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India. Hence, the State Governments are to take measures to strengthen their investigation machinery, and adopt modern techniques in investigation of cases. However, the Ministry of Home Affairs has been providing funds to the State Governments for modernization of Police Forces as well as Forensic Laboratories.(e):	No such Committee has been constituted by the Ministry of Home Affairs. (f): Does not arise. *******
