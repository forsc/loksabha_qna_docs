                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

9






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Recovery of Income Tax




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the corporate houses/ individuals in the country against whom more than ten crore rupees are outstanding as income tax, excise duty, custom and service tax as on date;(b) the steps taken/being taken by the Government for the recovery of the said amount;(c) the number of corporate houses/ individuals from whom recovery is still to be made due to the matter lying under court’s jurisdiction;(d) whether there is any scope for the non-judicial or out of the court settlement in the aforesaid cases; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINSTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (e):   The information is being collected and will be laid on the table of house.*****
