                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

964






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Forest Land




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the area-wise total lands reserved for forest land across the country State/UT-wise including Rajasthan;(b) whether any assessment has been done by the Government for the unauthorised occupancy of the said land for several years, if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of the steps taken by the Union Government to remove the unauthorised occupation and whether the States have been directed to take any action in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	As per the India State of Forest Report (ISFR), 2019, the total Recorded Forest Area in the country is 7,67,419 square kilometre out of which 434,853 square kilometre is Reserve Forest. The State/Union Territory (UT) wise details are given in Annexure-I.(b)	The information received from the States and UTs regarding encroachment on forest lands is given in Annexure-II. (c) The responsibility of protection of forests from various threats including encroachment primarily lies with the respective State or UT. The respective State Governments and UT Administrations take action in such cases as per the extant acts and rules. The Ministry issues advisories from time to time to all States and UTs to prevent and remove encroachment on forest land. The States and UTs take various measures such as demarcation and digitization of forest boundaries, strengthening infrastructure for forest protection and involving fringe area forest communities such as Joint Forest Management Committees, Eco Development Committees etc. to prevent and check encroachments. ********
