                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1700






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






New Six Lane Road between Trichy and Chennai




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government proposes to construct a new six lane road between Trichy and Chennai to reduce traffic congestion on the existing road;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the time frame set for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c)	The stretch from Chennai to Trichy of National Highway - 45 has already been 4-laned. To reduce traffic congestion on the existing road stretch, an amount of Rs.56.27 Crore has been sanctioned for 8-laning from Km 28.00 to Km 38.00 (Irumbuliyur - Vandalur – Guduvancherry) within the available ROW.  The work in Irumbuliyur- Vandalur and Vandalur to Guduvancherry stretches is targeted for completion by December, 2019 and September, 2020, respectively. Detailed Project Report (DPR) for upgradation of Tambaram (Irumbuliyur) -Tindivanam (93 Km) section has also been initiated.*****
