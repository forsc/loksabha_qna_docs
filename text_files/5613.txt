                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Role of BSNL in Digital India Programme




Chandra Sekhar Sahu













                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the role and share of BSNL and other Telecom Service Providers in the Digital India Programme; and (b) 	the reasons for low participation/ achievement, if any, of BSNL in the said programme along with the steps taken by the Government to enhance the role/share of the BSNL in the same?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) & (b) 	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) and other Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) facilitate the Digital India Programme by providing the network for digital communication.  BSNL is providing mobile, wireline, Broadband and FTTH connections, which support this programme.BSNL has also taken following steps:(i) 	Broadband service has been provided in urban as well as rural areas in all of its Licensed Service Areas (LSAs).  In urban areas BSNL is also providing High Speed Fibre to the Home (FTTH) connections.(ii) 	BSNL has executed the work of laying of Optical Fibre Cable (OFC) to provide high speed broadband services at 102073 Gram Panchayats (GPs) under BharatNet Project.(iii) 	BSNL has launched M-wallet services for facilitating its customers with transaction through mobile.  (iv) 	About 2 lakh e-SIMs provided for Internet of Things (IoT) and Machine to Machine (M2M) services.
