                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2264






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Common Service Centres




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:-(a)	the extent to which the Government has achieved the objectives of implementing the Common Services Centre (CSC) Scheme in the Country;(b)	the salient features of the scheme alongwith the number of such centres set up across the Country so far during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(c)	the details of the funds provided under the Scheme during the same period, State/UT-wise;(d)	the cases of irregularities in the functioning of such centres reported to the Government during the same period along with the action taken by the Government thereon, State/UT-wise; and (e)	the other steps taken by the Government for effectively implementing the CSC Scheme across the Country ?






ANSWER




                                                        a):   Common Services Centre (CSC-2.0) project aims at setting up of at least one CSC in each Gram Panchayat (GP) to cover all the 2.50 lakh Gram Panchayats (GP) of the Country. As on 31stDecember, 2019, Overall 3,65,361 CSCs are functional across the country, out of which 2,70,132 CSCs are at Gram Panchayat level. With respect to objective of the project, 2,40,592unique CSCs are functional at Gram Panchayats level.(b):   The salient features of CSC 2.0 Project are as under:-i.	Non-discriminatory access to e-Services to rural citizens by making the CSCs complete service delivery centres, utilizing the backend infrastructure already created in terms of other MMPs.ii.	Expansion of self-sustaining CSC Network till the Gram Panchayat level-2.5 Lakhs CSCs i.e. at least one CSC per Gram Panchayat, more than one preferred.iii.	Empowering District e-Governance Society (DeGS) under District Administration for implementation.iv.	Creating and strengthening the institutional framework for the rollout and project management, thereby, supporting the State and District administrative machinery and handholding of the VLEs through local language Help Desk support.v.	Enablement and consolidation of online services under one technology platform, hence, making the service delivery at CSCs outlets accountable, transparent, efficient and traceable, with a technology-driven relationship between all stakeholders.vi.	Providing Centralized Technological Platform for delivery of various services in a transparent manner to the citizens.vii.	Increasing sustainability of VLEs by sharing maximum commission earned through delivery of e-services and encouraging women as VLEs.The number of Common Services Centres (CSC) set up across the Country, during last three (3) financial years and in current financial year is placed at Annexure.(c):    Under CSC 2.0 Project, there is no provision of funding to the States and Union Territories.(d):    No such irregularity has been reported  to this Ministry. However, in case any irregularity is found in the functioning of any CSC, appropriate action, including deactivation of the CSC ID, shall be taken. (e):  For effective implementation of CSC 2.0 Project, various steps have been taken, such as two District Managers are deployed for handholding support to Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs) for enabling new services, Entrepreneurship Training Programme are conducted, bouquet of eServices are added, etc. Specifically to overcome last mile reliable connectivity challenges at rural and inaccessible areas of the country, necessary support is provided VLE/CSCby integrating BharatNet end-points / or any other Service Providers at Gram Panchayats (GP) and extending it over a Wi-Fi
