                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






AYUSH Hospitals By PSUs




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposed that the Public Sector Units (PSUs) have to open hospitals exclusively providing for traditional systems of medicine including Ayurveda across the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC) has agreed to open five such hospitals including Kayamkulam in Kerala; and(d) if so, the details and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):      The Ministry of AYUSH, in its efforts to increase the outreach of AYUSH Systems have requested  Ministries/ Departments of the Government of India for  integrating AYUSH Systems of Medicine through their Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs)/Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) who have structure  of Health facilities/ Hospitals  under their respective jurisdiction(c) & (d):    National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC), under Ministry of Power, has agreed to develop five Ayurveda Hospitals including one at Kayamkulam (Kerala).…………………
