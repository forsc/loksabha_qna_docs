                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






NEET




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of number of students who appeared for the last NEET-PG exam from the State ofKerala and the number of exam centres that were allotted for them;(b) whether it is a fact that due to shortage of exam centres in the State of Kerala, many studentswere allotted centres in neighbouring States;(c) If so, the Government has any proposal to increase the number of exam centres in the ensuingyear to reduce the hardships of students; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The information has been collected from Ministry of Health and Family Welfare and as perinformation, 10637 candidates were scheduled in Kerala for NEET – PG 2019. The test was held in12 cities across the 57 test centres.(b): No, Sir. All candidates opting for Kerala or residing in the State of Kerala were allottedcenter in the State of Kerala in NEET PG 2019.(c) and (d): For NEET PG 2020, exam centres have been set up in 12 cities in the State of Kerala.A total of 11851 test seats have been allocated for the State of Kerala which is 1214 more incomparison to NEET – PG 2019.***
