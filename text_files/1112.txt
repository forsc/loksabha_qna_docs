                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

929






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Highway Projects in Delhi NCR




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any Highways in Delhi NCR are included under Toll-Operate-Transfer (TOT) to bring private investment in highway projects and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether any highways in Delhi NCR has been identified having more than 17,000 Passenger Car Unit Traffic to bring BOT (Build-Operate-Transfer) model and if so, the details thereof; and (c) the current status of implementation of the Vehicle Fleet Modernization Programme in the country including Delhi NCR?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) No, Sir. No toll plaza of Delhi NCR are included at present under approved list of TOT Bundle-1, 3, 4, 5 & 6. (b) At present no highways in Delhi NCR have been identified to be taken up under BOT (Build Operate Transfer) model. However, currently following two projects are being operated on BOT basis:(i)          Delhi-Gurgaon (NH 48) (length 28 km)(ii)        Delhi-Agra (NH 2) (length 179.5 km)(c)  No such scheme/proposal is under consideration.	*****
