                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1905






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Passenger Ropeways




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of proposals pending before the Ministry for establishment of passengers ropeways from various States, including Andhra Pradesh;(b)	whether National Green Tribunal has relaxed the Forest (Conservation) Act guidelines for taking up passenger ropeways in hilly areas;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the time by which the Ministry is likely to clear the above projects?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) There is no passenger ropeway proposal pending in the Ministry at present for approval under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.Whenever a complete proposal is received in the Ministry, the same is considered expeditiously as per the policy and norms. (b) and (c)Hon’ble National Green Tribunal has not relaxed any guidelines related to passenger ropeway in hilly areas under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. However, Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change has issued guidelines on 05th August, 2019 whereby the forest area under the ropeway passageis excluded from the total area sought for non-forestry use from the provisions of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 with certain conditions.(d) Does not arise.***
