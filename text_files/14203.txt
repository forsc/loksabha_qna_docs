                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2840






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Defence Corridors




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki













                                                    Parvesh Sahib Singh
                                                








                                                    Ajay (Teni) Misra
                                                








                                                    S.R. Parthiban
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the status of defence corridors in Uttar Pradesh and Tamil Nadu and the purpose of these defence corridors and the manner in which they help in achieving India’s goal of self-reliance in defence;(b) the number of projects completed and the details of production units set up in these corridors so far;(c) the incentives given to private players and foreign companies for establishing defence production units in these corridors;(d) whether the Ministry is planning to make more defence corridors including one in Delhi and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the benefits likely to accrue to defence sector as a result of these defence corridors and the employment opportunities expected to be generated therein?
