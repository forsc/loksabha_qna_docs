                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2082






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Use of Sabeya Airbase




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government is aware that the Sabeya Airbase at Hathuwa in Gopalganj of Bihar has 570 acre of land and runways for air flights are also available to use after minor repairs; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has received representation regarding use of said airbase for air flights and if so, the action taken thereon;  (d) whether the Government has taken note of encroachments on the said Airbase; and  (e) if so, the action taken to remove the encroachments with the help of district administration?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b): Sabeya Airfield in Gopalganj, Bihar is part of acquired defence land known as Hathuwa Abandoned Airfield. As per records, the total area of Defence land at Hathuwa Airfield is 473.295 acres, out of which, 101.505 acres is situated in Sawaya village. Hathuwa is a disused airfield and is not planned by Indian Air Force for operational utilization. (c) to (e): Yes, Sir. A reference from Dr. Alok Kumar Suman, Hon’ble MP Gopalganj, Bihar was received in the month of September, 2021.  As per the report received from DGDE, 1011 number of encroachments have been identified on an area of 338.66 acres by way of agricultural use and Katcha/pucca construction. Defence Estates Officer (DEO), Danapur has issued eviction notices under provisions of Public Premises Eviction Act, 1971.  DEO has taken up the matter with State Government authorities for police assistance for removal of encroachments. *****
