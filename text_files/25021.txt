                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

896






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Wages/Honorarium of Anganwadi Workers




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    whether the wages/ honorarium of Anganwadi Workers (AWs) and helpers is less than the daily wages of daily workers;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the honorarium of AWs and helpers, if so, the details in this regard and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) 	whether there is any proposal to implement the recommendations of the Parliamentary Committee in this regard and if so, the time limit fixed therefor and if not, the reasons therefor,(e) 	whether the Government has held any meeting in regard to the demands of AWs and increasing the honorarium of the said AWs and helpers and if so, the details thereof; and(f) 	the steps taken by the Government to address the problems and demands of the said AWs and helpers, State/ UT-wise including Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER  MINISTRY OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b) Anganwadi Workers being honorary workers are not covered under the Minimum Wages Act, 1948.(c) to (e)  The Government of India undertakes revision of the honorarium of AWWs and AWHs from time to time.  The Government of India has recently enhanced the honorarium of AWWs/AWHs apart from introducing performance linked incentive of 250/- per month to AWHs.  In addition to the honorarium and other incentives paid by the Government of India, the respective State/UTs also give monetary incentives to these workers.  (f)     M/o WCD  has taken steps for sustained engagements with State Governments  to address issues concerning Anganwadi Infrastructure, non-operationalization of Anganwadi Centres, filling up of vacancies and smooth delivery of services to the beneficiaries. *****
