                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5386






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Development of Sports




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji













                                                    Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of proposal received from Government of Gujarat and Maharashtra for development of sportsactivities during the last three years and the estimated expenditure involved therein, scheme and district-wise including Solapur and the details of proposals which are pending for approval along with the reasons therefor;(b) whether any time-limit has been stipulated for disposal of proposals and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government provides funds to the States for development of sports and if so, the details thereof;(d) the funds allocated and released to the State Government of Gujarat during each of the last three years; and(e) the details of infrastructure for sports created in the above States during the said period and results achieved in promotion of sports in the above States?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }*****(a) to (c) ‘Sports’ being a State subject the responsibility for development of sports,including sports infrastructure and promotion of sports,rests with the StateGovernments. Central Governments supplements their efforts. Information regarding sports infrastructure proposals, including in respect of Solapur district, received from the Government of Maharashtra and Gujarat during the last three years is at Annexure – I. Available funds have been committed for the projects already sanctioned and sports infrastructure being responsibility of the States, it has not been possible to sanction more projects.(d) and (e) Projects specific funds are sanctioned and provided under Khelo India Scheme for bridging gap in sports infrastructure. The project wise information and status with respect of the States of Maharashtra and Gujarat is at Annexure-I*****
