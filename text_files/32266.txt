                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3698






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Requirement of Pesticides




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government have chalked out any action plan to ensure dispatch of requisite quantity of pesticides which is fixed for various State in the future; and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE & FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????        (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) and (b) Pesticides are registered for use in the country by the Registration Committee, only after satisfying about their efficacy and safety to human health, animal and environment. The use of various pesticides depends on the crop and pest situations in the States/UTs. Government of India in coordination with states/UTs through Zonal Conferences for Kharif and Rabi seasons, compiles pesticides information on demand and actual consumption as received from States/UTs through their agenda reports for the Conferences. Government of India does not directly supply pesticides to the States/UTs.*****
