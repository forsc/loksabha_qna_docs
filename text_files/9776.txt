                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Cultural Exhibitions




Gautham Sigamani Pon














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to organise cultural exhibitions in foreign countries to promote Indian culture worldwide including South East Asia;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the steps taken/proposed to be taken in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a)	to (d):  Ministry of Culture signs cultural agreement, cultural exchange programmes, Memorandum of Understanding etc. with foreign countries to enhance cultural cooperation. Under such agreement the Ministry or its  Institutions organise cultural exhibitions in foreign countries including Southeast Asia to promote Indian culture worldwide. Ministry of Culture also organises Festival of India abroad through Indian Missions. Under these Festivals, cultural exhibitions are also organised on request basis. Currently an exhibition titled ‘From the Coromendal Coast to the Straights-Tamils in Southeast Asia & Singapore’ is on show at Singapore.****
