                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Gazette Publication




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any gazette is published in regard to various public records and decisions taken by the Government for the purpose of making amendments to them or bringing about some changes in them; and(b)	whether any scheme to publish them in the digital format is in the pipeline; and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Yes, Sir. The Gazette of India is published in regard to various public records and decisions taken by the Government for the purpose of making amendments to bring about some changes in them. (b)	The scheme to publish Gazette in the digital format has already been implemented with effect from 1st October 2015. The Government have decided to switch to exclusive e-publishing of the Gazette of India by uploading Gazette notifications with effect from 1st October 2015 on the official website www.egazette.nic.in.                             *****
