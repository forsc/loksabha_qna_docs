23.07.2019






Funds Allocation under MGNREGS to Gujarat




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds provided to the State of Gujarat under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme(MGNREGS) during the last three years;(b)	the details of allocation of the said funds in Gujarat, district-wise;(c)	the details of funds provided to Gujarat, district-wise particularly in Dahod district and year-wise wages paid to the labourers out of the said amount during the said period; and(d)	the details of the work completed in Dahod district of Gujarat under MGNREGS during the said period?
