                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3978






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Awareness of Citizen Centric Programmes




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the measures being taken by the Ministry for disseminating information and awareness generation on all the citizen centric programmes, schemes run by Union Government;(b)	whether the same is also being done in vernacular/regional languages and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any innovative ways have been used such as developing a common platform or a website where all the people centric schemes and services are available at single source; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES {SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR}(a) and (b): Ministry of Information and Broadcasting disseminates information on Government policies, programmes, initiatives and achievements through various media units like Press Information Bureau (PIB), Bureau of Outreach and Communication (BOC) and Prasar Bharati, the public broadcaster, through Doordarshan and All India Radio, in English, Hindi and other regional languages utilizing various media vehicles like print, electronic media, social media, internet websites, outdoor publicity, live arts and interpersonal media.(c) and (d): MyGov, Government of India’s Citizen Engagement Platform, in collaboration with various Ministries and Departments, engages people in policy formulation and seeks opinion of people on issues/topics of public interest and welfare, and also disseminates information on various Government schemes and programmes, with multiple engagement methodologies such as discussions, tasks, polls, surveys, blogs, talks, quizzes and on-ground activities by innovatively using various technology.*******
