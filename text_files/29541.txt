                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1337






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Development of Tourism in Sitamarhi




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the funds released/spent for the development of tourism in Bihar   particularly in Sitamarhi and the heads under which the funds have been spent; and (b) 	the progress made in the work of connecting Sitamarhi with Ramayana circuit till date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM  			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) and (b):		 The  Ministry of  Tourism  provides  financial assistance  to the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations/ Central Agencies under its  schemes  ‘Swadesh Darshan’  and   ‘Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and  Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive’ (PRASHAD). In addition, financial assistance is also provided to Central Agencies for tourism infrastructure development.  No  project  has  been  sanctioned  in  Sitamarhi  under the above  mentioned  schemes.
