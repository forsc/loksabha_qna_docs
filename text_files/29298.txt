                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

945






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Production of COVID Vaccines




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken cognizance of the announcement by the G-7 countries for providing one billion doses of vaccines till 2022; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto;(c)	whether one billion vaccine doses is sufficient for universal need to combat COVID-19 virus and if so, the details thereof; and (d)	if not, the steps taken by the Government to enhance the production to meet the shortage of vaccine domestically and globally?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d) India is not a member of G-7 countries. Indian companies have however significantly enhanced their vaccine production capacity and are in a position to meet domestic requirements of COVID-19 vaccines as also contribute their surplus production to global Covid-19 vaccination requirements.
