                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3697






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Critical Care Facilities at Block and District Levels




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is extending critical care facilities at block and district levels in the rural areas of the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof particularly in Karnal Parliamentary constituency;  (c)	whether the Government proposes to organize weekly seminars to focus on good health practices at local level particularly for women and economically weaker sections, in government medical colleges in the country including Karnal parliamentary constituency; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d): Under PM - Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission (PM-ABHIM), Government of India is supporting establishment of Critical Care Hospital Blocks  in 602 districts of the country having population of more than 5 lakhs to augment the capacity of public health facilities to provide critical care services. As the public health & hospitals is a State subject, the implementation of the scheme lies under the purview of respective State/UT Government. For the state of Haryana, 50 bedded Critical Care Hospital Blocks are provisioned for 22 districts including 5 Critical Care Blocks in Medical Colleges under the scheme for the period of 2021-22 to 2025-26. All the matters relating to strengthening the health care system including for organising  seminars to focus on good health practices at local level particularly for women and economically weaker sections, in government medical colleges in the country including Karnal parliamentary constituency lies with the respective state governments including the state of Haryana and details of the same is not maintained at central level.******
