                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3252






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Central Assistance under PMS Scheme




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3252 TO BE ANSWERED ON 16.03.2021CENTRAL ASSISTANCE UNDER PMS SCHEME 3252. SHRI LAVU SRI KRISHNA DEVARAYALU: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of the Central Assistance released under Post Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Caste Students (PMS-SC) scheme since 2015 for Andhra Pradesh; (b) whether the number of beneficiaries under PMS-SC in Andhra Pradesh has been declining since 2016-17;(c) if so, the reasons therefor;(d) the details of the Central Assistance released under Pre Matric scholarship scheme for Other Backaward Classes (OBC) students since 2015 for Andhra Pradesh;(e) whether the number of beneficiaries under Pre Matric Scholarship scheme for OBC stundents in Andhra Pradesh has been declining since 2018-19; and (f) if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA) (a): The details of Central Assistance released under Post Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Caste students (PMS-SC) scheme since 2015 is as under:Financial Year	Amount Released(Rs. in lakhs)2015-16	13341.002016-17	14398.002017-18	31742.542018-19	9000.002019-20	28767.002020-21	32528.00(b) & (c): The scheme is open ended and all eligible students are entitled to get scholarship under the scheme. The number of beneficiaries reported by the State under PMS-SC in Andhra Pradesh is as under :-Year	Beneficiaries2016-17	3,12,1622017-18	3,39,3862018-19	3,69,4592019-20	4,25,673*2020-21	Registrations are still open, since admissions got delayed due to COVID-19.*This includes the intermediate students covered under the AmmaVodi State scheme.(d):  The details of the Central assistance released under Pre-Matric scholarship scheme for OtherBackward Classes (OBC) students since 2015 for Andhra Pradesh is as under:(Rs in crore)Year	Central Assistance released2015-16	6.352016-17	6.812017-18	4.022018-19	8.902019-20	8.412020-21	9.67(e) & (f):  Number of beneficiaries intimated by the States for a particular year usually includes the number of beneficiaries assisted for the previous year as well as the beneficiaries assisted from their own fund.   Therefore, it cannot be conclusively said that beneficiaries in Andhra Pradesh are declining since 2018-19.*****
