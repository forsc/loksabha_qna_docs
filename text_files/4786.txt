                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Health Insurance Coverage




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the number of people who do not have health insurance coverage in the country, State/UT-wise, rural and urban area-wise; (b) whether there are any statistics indicating the percentage of people taking loan to meet healthcare needs; and (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor and the bottlenecks being faced by his Ministry in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):   As informed by Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) through Department of Financial Services, the exact number of people, who do not have health insurance coverage in the country is not available.         However, around 48.20 crore persons were covered under Health Insurance (excluding Personal Accident and Travel Insurance ) in the Financial Year 2017-18.………….
