                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2325






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






CCPWC Schemes




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) has become a nodal agency under the Cyber Crime Prevention against Women and Children (CCPWC) scheme and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government will provide budget, man power etc. to NCRB under CCPWC scheme and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has set up a cyber crime reporting portal; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):   National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) has been entrusted with the task of operation and maintenance of the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal under Cyber Crime Prevention against Women & Children (CCPWC) scheme. Adequate funds under this Scheme have been provided to NCRB for hardware, software, hiring of manpower, etc.--2--LS.US.Q.NO. 2325 FOR 03.12.2019(c) & (d):   A National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (www.cybercrime.gov.in) was launched on 20th September, 2018, which allowed citizens to report online content pertaining to Child Pornography/Child Sexual Abuse Material or sexually explicit content such as Rape/Gang Rape Content. In consultation with the stakeholders, a revamped portal has been launched on 30.08.2019 to enable citizens to report all types of cyber crimes with special focus on cyber crimes against women and children.*****
