                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

369






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Development of Airports




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of airports in the country at present, State/UT-wise;(b) the details of airports for which the construction and development works have been started during each of the last three years and the current year along with their  present  status  including  the expenditure incurred on the said airports, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government proposes to build and develop more number of airports/Greenfield airports in the country on PPP model and in collaboration with the States/UTs;(d) if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof  including  Uttar  Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan; and(e) the expenditure likely to be incurred thereon along with the time limit fixed in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement in reply to part (a) to (e) of Lok Sabha Starred Q.No. 369 for 18.07.2019 regarding "Development of Airports"; (a): There are 414 airports/airstrips in the Country and the details of airports/airstrips is at Annexure A.  (b): The development of Airports is a continuous process and is undertaken by the Airports Authority of India (AAI) or the concerned airport operator from time to time depending on the availability of land, commercial viability, socio-economic considerations, traffic demand / willingness of airlines to operate to/from such airports. The details of major construction and development works started during each of the last three years and the current year at various airports along with the present status thereof and expenses incurred on the said airports is at Annexure B.  (c) to (e): The details of Greenfield Airports proposed to be built including Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Rajasthan along with the expenditure likely to be incurred thereon is at Annexure C. The timeline for construction of airport projects depends upon many factors such as land acquisition, availability of mandatory clearances, financial closure, etc. by the individual operator.
