                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1221






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Karnataka




Shobha Karandlaje













                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the teacher-student ratio in Kendriya Vidyalayas, State-wise;(b) whether the Government has received any requests for opening new KendriyaVidyalayas in the country and if so, the details thereof during the last threeyears, State-wise;(c) the details of new Kendriya Vidyalayas sanctioned and functional inKarnataka during the last three years, district-wise;(d) whether it is a fact that several Kendriya Vidyalayas in Karnataka are runningwithout their own buildings and if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether there is any obstacle in construction of their buildings and if so, thedetails thereof and the time by when the Government proposes to constructpermanent buildings for these schools?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) State/UT-wise position of teacher-student ratio in the Kendriya Vidyalayas(KVs) is at annexure-I.(b) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that, during the last threeyears viz. 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 it has received 118 proposals from variousstate governments for opening of new KVs. The state/UT-wise details is at annexure-II.(c) The district-wise details of the 7 KVs sanctioned during the last three years inthe state of Karnataka are as under:-<pre>District	Location of KVChickmagluru	KadrimiriRamnagara	ChannapatnaKoppal	VirupapuraMysuru	BRBNMPL, MysuruChikkaballapur	GauribidanurBengaluru	Railway YeshwanthpurBelagavi	Sadalga</pre>The KV Sadalga, Distt. Belagavi has not yet been made functional as thesponsoring authority has to transfer the identified / demarcated land in favour ofKendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) and also handover possession of thetemporary building before administrative orders for opening of KVs can be issued.(d) & (e) Out of 51 KVs in the State of Karnataka, there are 11 KVs running withouttheir own buildings. The details of these 11 KVs are as under:-<pre>Buildings underconstructionKoppal, Haveri, Chikodi, Virupapura, Kadimidri & Chennapatna.Buildings underplanningMandya, Udupi, Krishnarajapuram, Gauribidanur & RailwayYeshwantpur.</pre>Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, whichdepends upon identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favourof KVS by the sponsoring authorities, submission of drawings / estimates byconstruction agency, availability of funds and requisite approvals etc. As such, nodefinite time frame can be given in this regard.*****
