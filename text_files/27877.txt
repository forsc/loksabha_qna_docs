                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3587






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm




DNV Senthilkumar. S.














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government is implementing the National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm to increase the production of oilseeds in the country;b)	If so, the details thereof and whether the Government has been able to achieve the target of increasing the production of oilseeds since inception of the mission and if not reasons therefor;c)	The projected requirement of oilseeds in the country at present and the total sowing area of oilseeds;d)	Whether the Government has taken any step to increase the sowing area of oilseeds and if so, details thereof; ande)	The challenges faced by the Government to meet the production target of oilseeds to curtail its import?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):    Yes, Sir.  The National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP) was launched in 2014-15 and subsumed with National Food Security Mission (NFSM) as NFSM(OS&OP) from 2018-19 and continuing during 2021-22 as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme with sharing pattern of 60:40 for General States, 90:10 for North-Eastern and Hill States and 100% for Union Territories.The production of oilseeds which was 275 lakh tons in 2014-15 increased to 365.7 lakh tons in 2020-21 as per 3rd Advance Estimates. Similarly, the area under oilseeds cultivation increased from 256 lakh hectares in 2014-15 to 288 lakh hectares in 2020-21 as per 3rd Advance Estimate.  (c):     The target of production for oilseeds for 2021-22 has been fixed for 384 lakh tonnes. During 2020-21, the area sown under oilseeds is 288 lakh hectares (3rd Advance Estimate) and during the current year (2021-22), an area of 173.50 lakh hectares has been covered under oilseeds during Kharif 2021 (as on 6.8.2021).  (d) & (e):	For NFSM (oilseeds), interventions such as purchase of breeder seeds, production of foundation and certified seeds, distribution of certified seeds, plant protection equipments and chemicals, distribution of Gypsum/pyrite/lime/single super phosphate, Nuclear Poly Hydrosis Virus, Supply of improved farm implements, sprinkler sets, pipes for carrying water from source to the field, integrated pest management, farmers, officer’s and extension worker’s training are undertaken. Newly released high yielding varieties of oilseeds are also distributed through the oilseeds minikit programmes to the farmers. Further transfer of technology through Front Line Demonstrations (FLDs) and Cluster Front Line Demonstrations (CFLDs) are done at farmer’s field through the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR)/ Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVKs).   During Rabi 2020-21, a special programme on Rapeseed & Mustard was implemented as a result of which the production has reached to 99.80 lakh tons in 2020-21 (3rd advance estimate) from 91.23 lakh tons of 2019-20. For Kharif 2021, a total of 922935 number of oilseed minikits of high yielding varieties of soybean, groundnut and sesame have been allocated for distribution in all the major oilseed growing states. The details are as under;S. No.	Oilseeds Crop	States and Districts	No of Minikits1.	Groundnut	7 States & 53  districts	740002	Soybean	9 states & 90 districts	8164353	Sesame	9 states &   50 districts	32500	Total	-	922935Due to the above initiatives, the production of oilseeds has increased from 314.59 lakh tons (2017-18) to 365.65 lakh tons (2020-21) respectively, where-as the import of edible oils has decreased from 154.00 lakh tons in 2017-18 to 133.70 lakh tons in 2020-21. *******
