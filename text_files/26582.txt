                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2418






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Dhaanya Lakshmi Scheme




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched the Dhaanya Lakshmi Scheme - a Village Storage scheme to be run by the Self Help Groups (SHGs) to provide a good holding capacity and reduce their logistics cost, if so, the details thereof including such village storages created since its inception, State-wise; (b) 	whether Government announced Viability Gap Funding for setting up efficient warehouses at the block/taluk level, if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether Government has planned Financing on Negotiable Warehousing Receipts (e-NWR) to be integrated with e-NAM, if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the other steps being taken by the Government to undertake warehouse building by the FCI and Central Warehousing Corporation?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):	As per the Budget Announcement 2019-20, it was proposed to implement a scheme called “Dhaanya Lakshmi Scheme” for constructing storage facilities for agriculture produce at village level by Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, which is under active consideration......2/--2-(b): 	Storage capacity of FCI is augmented through schemes with varied modes of funding like Central government allocation (Central Sector Scheme “Storage &Godowns” 2017-22 for North East and some other small/remote states), Public Private Partnership [Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) Scheme, Construction of Silos under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode, Private Warehousing Scheme (PWS)]FCI has been constructing silos in VGF mode. Till date, 8 silos under VGF have been awarded and grant has been requested for 3 locations only, as at other locations premium has been received. Locations wise details are Annexed.(c):	Warehouse registered by Warehousing Development and Regulatory Authority (WDRA) can issue electronic Negotiable Warehouse Receipts (eNWRs) against deposited goods of notified commodities. eNWRs can be used for obtaining pledge loans from banks and settlement of contracts in commodity exchanges and off market sale of goods.An integration of e-NWR and eNAM platform has been completed and any warehouse registered with WDRA and declared as sub mandi by the State Government, can trade on eNAM through e-NWR.(d): 	There is a plan to construct total storage capacity of 1,65,450 MT (1,05,890 MT in North East and 59,560 MT in other than North East) by FCI under Central Sector Scheme “Storage & Godown” (focus on NE), 2017-22.Further, Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC) has planned for construction of 16.01 LMT between 2021-22 to 2024-25 through its own funds.**** AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (b) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2418 FOR ANSWER ON 03.08.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.Silos under VGF modeLocation	Date of Award	Total Project Cost in Crores	Capacity in LMT	Grant (G) sought/ Premium (P) received in Rs.
