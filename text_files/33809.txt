                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Promotion of Traditional Handicraft




Ramesh Bidhuri














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps for the development and promotion of traditional handicrafts industry in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has formed any Schemes/ programmes for the benefit of traditional handloom factories and their workers; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	The Office of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts) implements “National Handicrafts Development Programme (NHDP)” and Comprehensive Handicrafts Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) for development of handicraft in a holistic manner and to provide sustainable livelihood opportunities to the artisans. The NHDP and CHCDS has following components:1.	Marketing Support & Services2.	Skill Development in Handicrafts Sector3.	Ambedkar Hastshilp Vikas Yojana 4.	Direct Benefit to Artisans5.	Infrastructure and Technology Support6.	Research and Development 7.	Mega Cluster(c) & (d):	The Office of Development Commissioner (Handlooms) is implementing following schemes for development and promotion of handloom sector and welfare of traditional handloom weavers and workers across the country: - 1.	National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP)2.	Raw Material Supply Scheme (RMSS)Under the above schemes, financial assistance is provided to the eligible handloom agencies/weavers for raw materials, purchase of looms/accessories/lighting units, construction of worksheds, MUDRA loans at concessional rates, design development, common infrastructure development, marketing of handloom products, developmental projects, registration of handloom projects under Geographical Indication Act, promotion of Handloom Mark and India Handloom Brand etc. ***
