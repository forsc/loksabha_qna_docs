                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3191






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Revival of District Cooperative Banks in Uttar Pradesh




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government, the State Government of Uttar Pradesh and NABARD has signed any Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) in the year 2015 for revival of some small and weak District Cooperative Banks of Uttar Pradesh and; (b)	if so, the name of such banks along with the amount of funds released in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ?????? 	(SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a)	& (b): Union of India, the Government of Uttar Pradesh and NABARD entered into a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for implementation of the Scheme for revival of 16 District Central Cooperative Banks (DCCBs) in the State of Uttar Pradesh on 16 February 2015, involving financial assistance of Rs.1639.71 crore to be released to these 16 DCCBs.In addition to the amount of Rs.1639.71 crore mentioned in MoU, Government of Uttar Pradesh has released an additional amount of Rs. 471.65 crore to 16 DCCBs over a period from 2014-15 to 2021-22. This has resulted into total amount of Rs. 2111.36 crore. The list of the banks along with the amount of funds released in this regard is as under:S.No.-Name of District Central Cooperative Banks-Total amount of funds released (Rs. in Crore)1.-Allahabad-152.052.-Azamgarh-124.513.-Bahraich-94.844.-Ballia-142.135.-Basti-128.336.-Deoria-251.257.-Faizabad-29.178.-Fatehpur-213.569.-Ghazipur-69.6910.-Gorakhpur-194.0511.-Hardoi-95.6512.-Jaunpur-181.6513.-Siddharth Nagar-63.6414.-Sitapur-131.8115.-Sultanpur-111.5616-Varanasi-127.46-Total-2111.36*******
