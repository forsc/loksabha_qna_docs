                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

118






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Beautification of  Tourist Places in Kalahandi




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any scheme has been proposed for beautification of tourist places in Kalahandi Parliamentary Constituency, Odisha as it is a very large area and tourists from the foreign countries visit there; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the amount of funds proposed to be spent in this regard; and (d) 	the details of the facilities proposed to be provided for tourism development along with the details of world class tourist facilities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d): 	A statement is laid on the table of the House.********* STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.†*118 ANSWERED ON 10.02.2020 REGARDING BEAUTIFICATION OF TOURIST PLACES IN KALAHANDI.(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism, under the Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD schemes is developing tourism infrastructure in the country in a planned and prioritized manner. The projects under these schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.No project has been sanctioned in Kalahandi district in Odisha. The details of projects sanctioned in Odisha under the above schemes are as follows:(in Rs. crore)<pre>Sl. No.	Scheme /year	Name of the project	Sanctioned Amount1.	Swadesh Darshan(2016-2017)	Development of Gopalpur, Barkul, Satapada and Tampara	70.822.	PRASHAD(2014-15)	Infrastructure Development at Puri, Shree Jagannath Dham- Ramachandi-Prachi River front at Deuli under Mega Circuit	50.00</pre>Ministry under the above schemes is developing facilities like Eco Cottages, Tourist Reception Centers, Water Sports facilities, Sewerage Treatment Plants, Solid Waste Management, Jetties, Watch tower, Public convenience, Food court, Drinking Water Facilities, Landscaping, Amphitheatre, Walkway etc.*******
