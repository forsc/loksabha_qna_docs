                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3863






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Status of PMGSY




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the current status of implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in the country;(b)	the details of roads built under the Yojana during the last five years, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government is planning for the next phase of PMGSY; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As per the available information, out of 1, 78, 184 habitations as per Census,2001 identified for coverage under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY),  1,71,655 habitations have been found feasible. Of 1,71,655 eligible and feasible habitations, 1,66, 209 habitations have been connected through all-weather roads  as on 30th June, 2019, including 16,086 habitations connected by various State Governments using their own resources.  Since inception till 30th June, 2019, a total of 6,01,779 Km road has been completed under the various interventions/verticals of the Scheme.(b):	The road length built under the Yojana during the last five years is given at Annexure.(c) & (d):	The Government has approved phase-III of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) for consolidation of Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia, to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals at  an estimated cost of Rs. 80,250 crore. The Government is taking steps for implementation of phase-III of PMGSY. *****
