                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Four Laning Projects




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has taken any steps for four laning of Kanpur-Bilhaur-	Kannauj-Etah-Bevar-Aligarh-Bulandshahr-Ghaziabad-Delhi road; (b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c)	the present status of construction of road which is passing through Mishrikh region; 	and(d)	the steps taken by the Government to ensure that the material used in road 	construction are as per prescribed standards/norms?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. Four laning of Kanpur-Bilhaur-Kannauj-Etah-BevarAligarh-Bulandshahr-Ghaziabad-Delhi road section of National Highway 91 had been taken up. The work on Ghaziabad Alligarh Section had already been completed. Work on Aligarh to Naviganj is in progress under three packages and work on Naviganj to Kanpur section under two packages is at bidding stage. (c)	Construction of bypass in Mishrik region in village Bilhaur Dehat is in progress. (d)	Independent Engineer (IE) has been appointed to check the quality of material as well as construction work.  IE is to ensure the quality/specification of material to be used in the project.*****
