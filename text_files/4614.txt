                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Upgradation of Airports




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of domestic airports being operated in the country at present which are proposed to be converted into international airports by the Government along with the names of these airports; and(b) the time by which the Government plans to convert these airports into international airports?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): The Airports Authority of India (AAI) owns and manages 103 domestic airports in the country including 23 domestic civil enclaves.  Development and upgradation of airports to international standards is a continuous process which is undertaken by AAI from time to time depending upon commercial viability, traffic demand, availability of land etc.
