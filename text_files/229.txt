                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Maintenance of Samadhi of Madari Pasi




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has taken steps/ proposes to take steps for the maintenance of Samadhi of Madari Pasi, belonging to dalit-community and a famous revolutionary of Ekka Movement during freedom struggle, situated in Paharpur Ataria village under Tehsil Sandila of Hardoi District of Uttar Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE & TOURISMSHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL(a) to (c) At present no such proposal is under consideration of the Government .
