                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1102






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Assistance for Road and Bridge Projects in Karnataka




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received proposal from the Government of Karnataka seeking financial assistance for various roads and bridges in the State; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, project-wise; (c) 	whether the Union Government has given approval for the said proposals with adequate financial assistance; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	the list of National Highways located in Karnataka which have been taken up for maintenance under the National Highways Development Programme (NHDP) over the past three years; and (f) 	the details of the work that has been completed and the funds allocated for this purpose, project-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) 	No such estimate for the annual plan of current financial year has been received and sanctioned.  (e) & (f)	Various National Highways were envisaged for development under the National Highways Development Programme (NHDP).  The national highways are maintained by the contractor/concessionaire as per the conditions stipulated in contract/concession agreement depending upon respective mode of implementation.  However, maintenance on the NHDP stretches before award of upgradation work or beyond defect liability period/concession period is carried out by Authority as per site requirement. The details of maintenance work undertaken on NHs undertaken in NHDP in the State of Karnataka are at Annexure.
