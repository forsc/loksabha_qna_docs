                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4517






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Centre of Excellence in AYUSH Medicine




Santosh Kumar













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the details of existing AYUSH facilities in the Country, State/UT-wise; (b) whether the Government has any proposal to establish Centre of Excellence in AYUSH medicine in each district of the country; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)   (a):    The details of existing AYUSH facilities in the Country, State/ UT-wise are annexed at    Annexure.  (b):    Yes.	  (c):    Central Council of Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH) is in the process of developing           Centre of Excellence at its following four ( 04) institutes in the respective areas : -District, state 	Name of the Institute 	Centre of Excellence for Kottayam, Kerala	National Homoeopathy Research Institute in Mental Health Sachivothamapuram,Kottayam-686532	PsychiatryNoida, Uttar Pradesh	Dr. D.P. Rastogi Central Research Institute of Homoeopathy, A-1/1, Sector 24, Noida, Gautambudh Nagar-201301, Uttar Pradesh	Drug DevelopmentKolkata,West Bengal	i.	Dr. Anjali Chatterjee Regional Research Institute of Homoeopathy  50, Rajendra Chaterjee Road, Kolkata-700035	Virological diseases Krishna, Andhra Pradesh	i.	Regional Research Institute (Homoeopathy), Dr. GGH Medial College Campus, Eluru Road, Gudivada – 521 301 	Rheumatology   (d):    Not applicable.
