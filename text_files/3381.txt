                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2965






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Construction of Toilets




Sukhbir Singh Badal














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of toilets constructed in the country during the last two years by the Government;(b) the number of families benefited therefrom;(c) whether any target has been fixed by the Government under Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) during each of the last two years and the current year;(d) if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(e) whether these targets have been met satisfactorily; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor along with the corrective measures taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) 	Under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) [SBM(G)], total 5,46,41,635 Individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed in the rural areas of the country during the last two years.  (c) to (f) 	SBM(G) is demand driven programme, hence fixed State/UT-wise targets are not set.  However, State/UT-wise number of Individual household latrines (IHHLs) constructed under SBM(G) during the last two years and the current year are given at Annexure.
