                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4401






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Procuring Surplus Parboiled Rice




Dushyant Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is procuring surplus parboiled rice from various State Governments for Kharif Marketing Season (KMS) 2021-2022 and upcoming Rabi Marketing Season in 2022; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the Government has any proposal to stop procurement of parboiled rice completely from the parboiled rice producing States and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the details of the parboiled rice and raw rice procured by the Food Corporation of India from the year 2015 to 2021, State-wise; and (e) 	whether the Government has noticed any decline in demand for parboiled rice in India and abroad, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons for not procuring parboiled rice from the States?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c): 	After meeting State’s requirement for Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) and Other Welfare Schemes (OWS),  only the excess/surplus stocks procured by the State Government /its agencies are handed over to Food Corporation of India (FCI)  in Central Pool in the form of raw rice or parboiled rice to meet the overall consumption requirement of the country in terms of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed between Government of India and Decentralized Procurement (DCP) states......2/--2-       Due to burgeoning stock level of parboiled rice in central pool, it was informed to the States that FCI will not be in position to accept parboiled rice during Kharif Marketing Season (KMS) 2021-22. However, States can procure parboiled rice for consumption within the State.  (d): 	State-wise details of the par boiled rice and raw rice procured by the FCI from the years Kharif Marketing Season (KMS) 2015-16 to 2020-21 is at Annexure-I.(e): 	In the last few years procurement of parboiled rice in the deficit parboiled consuming states like Jharkhand, Kerala & Tamil Nadu has increased resulting in lesser movement of parboiled rice from surplus to deficit states.
