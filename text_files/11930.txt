                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

738






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Coastal Highways in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to construct Coastal Highway from Digha to Gopalpur 	in Odisha; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the funds earmarked for the purpose; (c)	whether the work has started thereon and if so, the present status thereof; and(d)	the funds sanctioned by the Government to construct the said highway?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Yes Sir. (b) to (d)  The stretch Gopalpur-Chatrapur-Satapada-Konark-Astarang-Nuagaon-Paradip-Ratanpur-Dhamra and Basudevpur-Chandipur-Chandaneswar-Dgha has been identified for developing as costal highways under Bharatmala Pariyojana in the State of Odisha. Total length of this costal highway is about 440 km. Preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) has been initiated. Development work shall be taken up after completion of DPR. *****
