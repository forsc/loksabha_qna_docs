                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Quality of Eduction to Tribal Children




Chandra Prakash Choudhary













                                                    Sanjay Seth
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is committed to provide best quality education to tribal children in their own environment;(b) whether the Government has set a deadline of the year 2022 to start 740 Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) across the country;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the total number of such schools sanctioned and the total number of those functional in the country including Jharkhand, district-wise, till date?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.     STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *89 FOR 08.02.2021 REGARDING ‘QUALITY OF EDUCATION TO TRIBAL CHILDREN’ ASKED BY SHRI CHANDRA PRAKASH CHOUDHARY, SHRI SANJAY SETH:  (a) to (c) Ministry of Tribal Affairs has been setting up Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) since 1998-99 for imparting quality education to tribal children in their own environment. An EMRS has the capacity of accommodating 480 students. As per the budget announcement 2018-19, every block having more than 50% ST population and at least 20,000 ST population is to have an Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) by 2022. Ministry has identified 452 such blocks across the country. As on 04.02.2021, 588 EMRSs have been sanctioned across the country out of which 288 are old EMRS and 300 are new EMRSs as per the budget announcement. Another 152 schools are to be sanctioned during 2021-22 thereby taking the total number of EMRSs in the country to 740 by the year 2022.  (d) The list of total number of sanctioned and functional schools in the country including Jharkhand, district-wise, till date is attached at Annexure 1.
