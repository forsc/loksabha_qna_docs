                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Scheme to Launch New Education Policy




Jyotsna Charandas Mahant














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any scheme is being prepared to launch New Education Policy in all the States of the country at one go and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether there is any proposal to impart training for all the States at central level under New Education Policy and if so, the reasons thereof;(c) the reasons for changes in educational institutions at present, State-wise, district-wise; and(d) whether the number of school going children or their attendance has decreased in rural areas during the last three years and if so, the details thereof, State/yearwise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): The National Education Policy 2020 has been announced on 29.07.2020. The implementation of this Policy is led by various bodies including Union and State Governments, education-related Ministries, State Departments of Education, Boards, National Testing Agency (NTA), the regulatory bodies of school and higher education, National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERTs), schools, and Higher Education Institutions (HEIs). The policy provides different timelines as well as principles and methodology for its implementation. Accordingly, Ministry and other implementing agencies under its purview have started taking initiatives towards implementation of NEP 2020. Moreover, this Ministry has communicated to all States/UT Governments for taking steps for implementation of NEP 2020.(b) and (c): NEP 2020 aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education at all levels of school education and higher education including access to education. Since education is a concurrent subject, State/UT Governments play a vital role in the implementation of the National Education Policy 2020. NEP 2020 provides for different interventions in all States and UTs such as professional training to Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) Anganwadi workers / teachers, training of teachers with high-quality content. For training of teachers, technology platforms such as Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds (SWAYAM)/ Digital Infrastructure for Knowledge Sharing (DIKSHA) are utilized.(d): State-wise details of number of school going children in rural areas during the last three years are given in Annexure-I. Year-wise details of number of school going children in rural areas during the last three years are given in Annexure-II.***
