                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Beneficiaries under ONORC




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Supreme Court has directed all the States to implememnt One Nation One Ration Card system (ONORC) recently, if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the ONORC Scheme implementation has covered 86% of beneficiaries across 32 States and UTs, if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the names of the remaining States yet to be integrated in the scheme, State-wise; (d) 	the number of beneficiaries covered so far, Statewise; (e) 	whether the Government has reviewed the progress of implementation of this scheme, if so, the details thereof; and (f) 	the steps taken to eliminate bogus, ineligible ration cards and reduce leakage to make the PDS system better?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	Yes sir. The Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in its judgement dated 29-06-2021 in connection with Suo Motu Writ Petition (Civil) No. 6 of 2020 titled ‘Problems and Miseries of Migrant Labourers’  has also directed the States which have not implemented the One Nation One Ration Card to implement the same by not later than 31-07-2021.  .....2/--2-(b): 	Upto December 2020 the One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC) plan has been enabled in 32 States/UTs covering about 86% NFSA population (nearly 69 Crore of total 80 Crore beneficiaries) in the country to have an option to lift their foodgrains from any Fair Price Shop of choice anywhere in these 32 States/UT.(c): 	4 States/UT of Assam, Chhattisgarh, Delhi and West Bengal are yet to enable the ONORC plan. (d): 	A State/UT wise statement showing number of NFSA beneficiaries covered under ONORC plan is at Annexure-I.(e):  	The Department regularly reviews the implementation of ONORC plan, number of portability transactions, efforts made by States/UTs through central portals, VC meetings, letters from all levels to concerned States/UTs and technical partners, etc. (f): 	As per Targeted Public Distribution System (Control) Order, 2015 under National Food Security Act (NFSA), all States/UTs are required to review their list of eligible beneficiaries every year and are required to remove/delete the ineligible or bogus ration cards/beneficiaries from their ration card’s database.*** Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (d) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.419 FOR ANSWER ON 20.07.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.State/UT wise statement showing number of NFSA beneficiaries covered under ONORC plan in 32 States/UTs up to December 2020:Sl.
