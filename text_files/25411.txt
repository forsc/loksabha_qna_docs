                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Gap Between Consumption and Production of Edible Oil




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated any plan to bridge the gap between domestic consumption and domestic production of edible oils; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the corrective measures taken by the Government to make the country Atmanirbhar in Edible oils, State-wise particularly in the State of Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b):   The domestic edible oil production in the country has not been able to keep pace with the growth in consumption which is around 25 million tonnes. The Government of India has been implementing the Centrally Sponsored Scheme- National Food security Mission (Oilseeds and Oil palm)   to augment the availability of edible oils and to reduce the import of edible oils by increasing the production and productivity of oilseeds sources from nine Oilseeds (Groundnut, Soyabean, Rapeseed & Mustard, Sunflower, Safflower, Sesame, Niger, Linseed and Castor), Oil Palm and Tree Borne Oilseeds (TBOs) in the country.  ......2/--2-(c): 	To make the country Atmanirbhar in edible oils, Government of India is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme i.e. National Food Security Mission (Oilseeds & Oil palm) to increase the production of oilseeds and thereby edible oils. The States/UTs covered under NFSM-Oilseeds are Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Haryana, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Odisha, Punjab, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, West Bengal, Goa, Kerala, Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh and Puducherry.The States covered under NFSM-Oil Palm are Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Karnataka, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Odisha, Tamil Nadu and Telangana.The States covered under NFSM-TBOs are Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra, Mizoram, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Tripura and Uttar Pradesh.  ***
