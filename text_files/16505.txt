                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Barren Land in States




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the area of barren land out of the total geographical area falling under areas of Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand along with the details of its share/percentage in the total area; (b) 	the names of the schemes implemented for converting the said barren land into arable land during each of the last three years; (c) 	the details of the funds utilized for the said purpose during the above period, year-wise; and (d) 	the area of the said barren land converted into arable land through the said schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):   As per the latest Land Use Statistics for the year 2016-17, the details of area of Barren and Unculturable land and its share in total geographical area for the states of Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand is given in the following table: State	Geographical Area (thousand hectares)	Barren and Unculturable Land (thousand hectares)	Share of Barren and Unculturable land in geographical area (%)Maharashtra	30771	1822	5.92Madhya Pradesh	30825	1344	4.36Jharkhand	7972	583	7.32Source: Directorate of Economics and Statistics, MoA&FWContd…2/--2-(b):     As per the Seventh Schedule of Constitution of India, land comes under the purview of State Government and, therefore, it is for the State Governments to take suitable steps for converting barren land into arable land. However, Government of India supplements the efforts of State Governments through various programmes.             Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP) primarily keeping at Center Stage the rainfed & degraded area development. DoLR has sanctioned 8214 watershed development projects in 28 States (except Goa) including Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand  (now 27 States and UT of J & K and Ladakh) from 2009-10 to 2014-15 covering an area of about 39.07 million hectare under Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP). IWMP was amalgamated as the Watershed Development Component (WDC) of PMKSY in 2015-16. The activities being undertaken through the watershed development projects inter alia include ridge area treatment, drainage line treatment, soil and moisture conservation, rain water harvesting, nursery raising, afforestation, horticulture, pasture development, livelihoods for assetless persons, etc.Apart from this, Indian Council of Agricultural Research has developed location specific bio-engineering soil & water conservation measure, land management techniques, soil reclamation measures for saline, alkali, waterlogged and acid soils, and selection of suitable crop including agroforestry interventions to reclaim and develop barren lands.  (c):   The State wise details of projects sanctioned, area covered by the projects and funds released during last three years and current year as Central share to States (including Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand) under WDC-PMKSY are given at Annexure-I.(d):     As per information received from the States, since 2014-15 to 2020-21 (including Q1, 2020), 6.90 lakh water harvesting structures have been created / rejuvenated. An additional area of 14.67 lakh hectares has been brought under protective irrigation. The number of farmers benefited is 31.15 lakh during the said period.
