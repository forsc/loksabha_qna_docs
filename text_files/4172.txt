                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3751






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Effect of Ractopamine on Animals




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the volume of Ractopamine produced in India;(b)	whether the Government is aware of the health hazards of Ractopamine on animals as well as on humans, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government is taking any steps to stop its production, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	As per the information received from Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO), Ministry of Health and Family Welfare  Ractopamine is not domestically manufactured and marketed in India. (b)	& (c) Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying has never recommended any import of  Ractopamine. Till date Government of India has not received any reports on the  use of Ractopamine and the health hazards associated with it.
