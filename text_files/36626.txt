                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Construction near Defence Establishments




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has updated NOC guidelines for construction near or along defence establishments after the one issued on 21.10.2016 and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether there is any plan to modify the NOC guidelines in the interest of public without compromising safety of defence establishments and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any incidents have been reported regarding misinterpretation of NOC guidelines by the authorities in the country especially at Ramgarh in Jharkhand and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether there is any direction issued by the Ministry to ban construction within 50 meters and compulsory NOC for construction within 500 meters of Ramgarh cantonment in Jharkhand and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the amount invested by various stake holders of NIRDESH and the manner in which it can be made useful to tax payers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a):  No, Sir.(b):  The NOC guidelines issued on 21.10.2016 are presently under review.(c):  No, Sir.(d):  No such separate NOC guidelines have been issued by the Government for Ramgarh Cantonment.(e):  A total amount of Rs. 49.66 crore has been invested by various stakeholders of NIRDESH as on 31.03.2021.  NIRDESH will function as an R&D institution and as an Indian Design House to generate common ship designs required by Indian Navy and Indian Coast Guard.  The shipyards shall be benefitted by having to invest less in Design facilities, IT infrastructures, software licences, manpower etc.*****
