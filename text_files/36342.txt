                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3509






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Work on National Highway




Prajwal Revanna














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of work on Hassan-Sakleshpur stretch of 	Bangalore-Mangalore National Highway (NH);(b)	the details of funds sanctioned, released and utilised for completion 	of the said stretch and the time by which it is likely to be completed;(c)	whether the upgradation work of NH between Channarayapatna and 	Makutta has started;(d)	if so, the details of funds sanctioned and released for the purpose; 	and(e)	the time by which it is likely to be completed along with the status of 	tendering process?






ANSWER




                                                        ¬THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	At present, the physical progress of 4 laning work of Hassan-Sakleshpur stretch of Bangalore-Mangalore National Highway (NH) is 40% (18.3 km has been completed out of 45.148 km) and the work is targeted for completion by March, 2023.  The fund allocated for civil construction work is Rs.573.92 crore and till now Rs. 222.29 crore has been utilized/released.(c) to (e) This Ministry is primarily responsible for development & maintenance of National Highways and the stretch between Channarayapatna and Makutta is not a notified National Highway.*****
