                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2141






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Data of Unemployment




Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present system regarding collection of data of unemployment in the country;(b)	whether the above mentioned system is providing true information regarding unemployed persons;(c)	whether any system exists under which employed persons can be registered so as to provide opportunity of employment to them and no need to fill forms again and again and save them from payment of exorbitant application fee; and(d)	if so, the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b): Annual Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) on employment and unemployment is conducted by National Statistical Office (NSO), Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI). Estimates of Labour Force Participation Rate (LFPR), Worker Population Ratio (WPR) and Unemployment rate (UR) are presented through this survey. It is a comprehensive household survey providing labour force statistics in India. The reports of PLFS surveys are available at MoSPI website www.mospi.gov.in.(c) & (d): Government is implementing the National Career Service (NCS) Project which comprises a digital portal that provides a nation-wide online platform for the job seekers and employers for job-matching in a dynamic manner. NCS portal has also started an online Job Fair module where the complete cycle of job posting to selection of candidate can be completed on the portal. All services on NCS are free of cost.*****
