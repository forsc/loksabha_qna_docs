                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Bio-Gas Plants




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of number of Bio-gas plants, State/UT-wise including Gujarat;(b)	whether the Government proposes to increase the number of plants in this financial year in Gujarat;(c)	if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether there is any provision for households to get  financial  or  technical  assistance  for  cattle  dung based  plants; (e)	if so, the details thereof? and(f)	the details of beneficiaries which have used the said assistance, State/UT-wise?   






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) The State / UT wise number of family size biogas plants established under MNRE’s schemes, including in the State of Gujarat, are given in Annexure-I.(b) & (c) The scheme for family size biogas plants has been proposed for continuation beyond 31.03.2021 i.e. from Financial Year 2021-22 to 2025-26.  The target for State of Gujarat for the year 2021-22 will be allocated based on the need and demand raised by the State once the scheme is approved for continuation.(d) & (e) Ministry had been providing the Central Finance Assistance (CFA) of Rs. 7500/- to Rs. 35,000/- per plant for supporting family–size biogas plants (1 to 25 m3 biogas per day). (f) The State-wise cumulative number of family size biogas plants installed in the country as on 31.03.2021 (State /UT wise) is given in Annexure-I.  *****
