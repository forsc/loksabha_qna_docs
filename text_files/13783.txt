                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2587






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Trade of Spurious Medicines




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar













                                                    Vijay Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has taken note of spurious medicines sold openly and its failure to check the increasing trade of spurious medicines, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (b) the number of such cases reported, guilty persons arrested and action taken during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise; (c) the steps taken by the Government to check the increasing trade of spurious medicines in various parts of the country; (d) whether the Government has developed any other system in addition to the Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation for checking the quality of medicines; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) & (b):  As per information received from various State/UT Drugs Controllers, number of drugs samples tested, number of drugs samples declared sub-standard and spurious/ adulterated and action taken thereon during the last three years and current year is enclosed as Annexure.(c):   There is no such report that there is increasing trade of spurious medicines in various parts of the country.(d) & (e): The Central Drugs Regulatory Authority i.e. Central Drugs Standard Control Organsiation (CDSCO) is responsible for approval of New Drugs, Clinical Trials, laying down the standards for drugs, control over the quality of imported drugs, coordination of the activities of State Drug Control Organizations and providing expert advice with a view to bringing uniformity in the enforcement of Drugs and Cosmetics Act, etc.       In every State/UT, there is a Drug Regulatory Authority to ensure the quality of the drugs. The regulatory control over the manufacture and sale of the drugs is primarily exercised by the State Licensing Authorities (SLAs) appointed by the State Governments.……………..
