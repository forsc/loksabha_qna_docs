                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1647






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Status of AMRUT Scheme in Jharkhand




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of various projects under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) Scheme in Hazaribagh, Jharkhand; (b)	the details of sanitation measures taken under AMRUT Scheme along with construction of individual household toilets, community and public toilets in Jharkhand, district-wise; and (c)	whether new water treatment plants were constructed to promote reusing and recycling of water and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a).	The status of projects under AMRUT in Hazaribagh, Jharkhand is at ANNEXURE-I.(b).  Under AMRUT, 9 projects worth Rs.251.50 crore have been taken up by the State of Jharkhand in sewerage and septagemanangement sector. Contract for all these 9 projects have been awarded and work is under progress. The details of construction of individual household toilets, community and public toilets in Jharkhand under Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) is at ANNEXURE-II.(c).   Under AMRUT, 6 water treatment plants having total capacity of 171 MLD are under construction in Jharkhand, the details of which is at ANNEXURE-III.                           *****
