                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

906






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Data for Air Travel




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any data on people who got infected from a flight travel, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government intends to resume air and train travel in full swing while following all necessary covid  protocols;  and (c) the details of such analysis and study to take a data driven approach in Digital India?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) No Sir. Such data is not collected by this Ministry. Ministry of Civil Aviation has issued detailed general instructions and guidelines to be followed by the passengers and specific operating guidelines for major stakeholders (Airlines, Airports, Ground Handling Agencies, etc.). These guidelines specify certain safety measures, which include Protection kit to each passenger (Three layered masks, face shields and sanitizers), as the safety measures, to contain the transmission of COVID-19 during air travel. Further, High quality High Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters for recirculation of air are available in the aircrafts. Also, the duration of air travel is normally of limited hours. It is very difficult to ascertain the specific location of contracting of COVID-19, which may occur beyond the ecosystem of Civil Aviation;(b) Yes. Government of India has been reviewing recommencement of domestic operations keeping in view the prevailing COVID-19 situation and passenger demand for travel.;;(c) No such analysis and study has been carried out by Directorate General of Civil Aviation.;
