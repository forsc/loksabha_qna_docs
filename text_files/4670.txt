                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4106






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Pension Scheme for Female Meritorious Sportspersons




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to ensure and put in a smooth and hassle free procedure for female meritorious sportspersons to avail the benefits of the pension scheme after retirement which would play a vital role in hindering women from opting sports as their career;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (c) : Yes Sir. A scheme namely ‘Sports Fund for Pension to Meritorious Sportspersons’ already exists under which sportspersons including female sportspersons, who have won medals for the country in international competitions are given lifelong pension to provide additional financial security to them after they retire from active sports career.The amount of pension payable to different categories of meritorious sportspersons with effect from 01.04.2018 is as follows:<pre>S. No.	Category of meritorious sportspersons								Rate of Pension(Rupees per month)1.	Medallists at the Olympic Games/Para Olympic Games						20,0002.	Gold medallists at the World Cup/World Championship  in Olympic and Asian Games disciplines			16,0003.	Silver and Bronze medallists at the World Cup in Olympic and Asian Games disciplines		14,0004.	Gold medallists of the Asian/ Commonwealth Games/Para Asian Games				14,0005.	Silver and Bronze medallists of the Asian/Commonwealth Games/Para Asian Games			12,000</pre>Details of the scheme along with application form to apply for the pension are available on the website of the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. Further, the details of the scheme have also been sent to Indian Olympic Association, National Sports Federations, Sports Authority of India etc.*****
