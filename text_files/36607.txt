                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






NH Connectivity in Aspirational Districts




Ramesh Chandra Majhi













                                                    Chandra Prakash Choudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is focusing on the highway road connectivity in the aspirational districts in the State of Odisha, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) whether the Government has noticed any economic development of the aspirational districts because of construction of National Highways (NHs) and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of proposed and ongoing construction of highway projects in aspirational districts in the country including Odisha and Jharkhand? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS (Shri NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c) Yes, Sir. The details of the sanctioned and ongoing National Highway projects in aspirational districts in the country including Odisha and Jharkhand are at Annexure. Development of National Highways promotes faster and safer movement goods & passengers and reduces vehicle operating cost and travel time, which lead to economic growth for the people in the region.
