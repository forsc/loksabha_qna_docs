                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Improvement of Heritage Temples




Maddila  Gurumoorthy














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    	Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government proposes to improve Heritage Temples combined with Leisure under its 5 Trillion Economy Aspirations in Tirupathi, Srikalahasti, Sullurupeta, Naidupeta, Venkatagiri, Gudur and Satyavedu constituencies which are having prominent temples setting up Indian Tourism Development Corporation Office (ITDC) for conducting 3 Nights/4 Days, 4 Nights/ 5 Days, 5 Nights/ 6 Days, 6 Nights/ 7 Days, 8 Nights/ 9 Days Circuit Package Trips; and (b)	if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                                (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a): 	No, Sir. There are no packages designed by ITDC for these locations as on date. However, there are private tour and travel operators working in the area.(b): 	Doesn''t arise.*******
