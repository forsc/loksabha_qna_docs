                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4057






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Agreement with USA for Supply of Arms




Parvesh Sahib Singh













                                                    Vijay Kumar Hansdak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has recently signed an agreement to purchase $ 3 billion of American Military Equipment;(b) if so, the details thereof and the target set for the purpose;(c) the number of military helicopters proposed to be purchased from the US and the time by which the delivery of these helicopters would commence;(d) the other countries which are exporting defence equipments and the Indian products likely to be exported under such agreements; and(e) the percentage of the South Asian defence requirements being fulfilled by Indian exports?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (c):  One contract and two Letters of Offer and Acceptance (LOAs) have been concluded with the US side for procurement of Helicopters and Self Protection Suite.  The delivery of the helicopters will commence from May, 2021.(d) & (e):  Countries such as US, Russia, France, Germany, Israel, UK are leading exporters of defence equipment.	The Defence requirement of other countries including South Asian countries is not available in public domain.*****
