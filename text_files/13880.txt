                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2722






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Janani Suraksha Yojana




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh













                                                    Shyam Yadav Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of funds released to the State of Bihar and Uttar Pradesh under Janani Suraksha Yojana during the last three years;(b) the number of women provided financial assistance under Janani Suraksha Yojana in the State of Bihar and Uttar Pradesh particularly in rural areas during the last three years, district-wise; and(c) the details of the funds provided to each women under this scheme by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	Under National Health Mission (NHM), for Janani Suraksha Yojana Scheme, approvals given in last three years are as follows:STATE	Funds ApprovedBihar	1) FY 2016- 17 – Rs.343.40 Crores2) FY 2017-18  - Rs.344.15 Crores3) FY 2018-19  - Rs.343.19 CroresUttar Pradesh	1) FY 2016- 17 – Rs.511.29 Crores2) FY 2017-18  - Rs.529.20 Crores3) FY 2018-19  - Rs.526.18 Crores        The release of funds for the Scheme is not done separately since it is a part of RCH Flexible Pool.(b):	As per information received from the State Government of Bihar and Uttar Pradesh, the details regarding the number of women provided financial assistance under Janani Suraksha Yojana are enclosed at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively. (c):	The eligibility for cash assistance under JSY is as shown below:Category	Ruralarea(Rs.)	Urbanarea(Rs.)	EligibilityFinancial Assistance for Institutional DeliveryLow Performing States (LPS)*	 1400	1000	Available to all women regardless of age and number of children for delivery in government /private accredited health facilities.High Performing States (HPS)	  700	  600	Available only to BPL/SC/ST women regardless of age and number of children for delivery in government /private accredited health facilities.Financial Assistance for Home DeliveryLow Performing States (LPS) 500	500	Available only to BPL women who prefer to deliver at home regardless of age and number of children.High Performing States (HPS)	500	500	*Bihar and Uttar Pradesh fall under Low Performing States (LPS).
