                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3956






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Downsizing Manpower in Army andCivilian in Defence




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the number of both military personnel and defence civilians (category-wise) and amount of pension disbursed to them;(b) the proposals and timeline for downsizing defence civilians of all categories to improve the tooth-to-tail ratio and also for using technology in day-to-day administration for facilitating downsizing;(c) the proposals for outsourcing duties and account offices of defence civilians belonging to CGDA, PCDA and CDA to achieve salary and pension reduction;(d) the proposals and timeline for correction of erroneously calculated pensions using automation and payment of arrears with interest; and(e) the proposals and timeline for providing post-retirement pension information and automatic receipt of electronic copy of pension details credited and time-bound resolution of grievances?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e):  A statement is attached.*****
