                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3043






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Setting Up of Meat Processing Centres




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to set up new meat processing centres in the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Union Government has any proposal to acquire existing such centres set up by the State Governments; and (d)  	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) & (b): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has been implementing a Scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for setting up/expansion of industries in food processing sector which includes meat/poultry/fish processing. Under the Scheme, grant-in-aid is provided to eligible applicants @ 35% of the eligible project cost in General Areas and 50% of the eligible project cost in North Eastern States (including Sikkim) and Difficult areas including Himalayan States/UTs (Himachal Pradesh, J&K, Ladakh & Uttarakhand), notified ITDP areas and Islands subject to a maximum of Rs.5.00 crore.  Applications under the Scheme are invited online from potential investors/ entrepreneurs against Expression of Interest (EoI) issued by Ministry from time to time depending on the availability of funds and also vacant slots. The willing entrepreneurs may apply for setting up of food processing units  as and when the Ministry invites EoI in future. Till date, Ministry has approved 7 projects for meat & poultry processing, having total project cost of Rs. 84.39 Cr with approved grant-in-aid of Rs.23.94 Cr. Out of these, 4 projects have been completed. (c) & (d): As of now, this Ministry does not propose to acquire any existing meat processing centres set up by  State Governments.  *****
