                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

144






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Health and Wellness Centres




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has approved a plan to be set up 1.5 lakh Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) to be set up in various States of the country by 2022;(b) if so, the details of target set, yearwise;(c) the details of HWCs targeted to be set up in Krishna district, particularly in Machilipatnam, in the State of Andhra Pradesh and how many have so far been set up, district-wise; and(d) the details of medical facilities provided in the above HWCs?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	As per the budget announcement 2017-18, 1.5 lakh Health Sub Centres and Primary Health Centres are to be transformed into Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) by December, 2022. The roll out plan of Ayushman Bharat - HWCs is given as below:•	FY 2018-19 = 15,000•	FY 2019-20 = 25,000 (Cumulative 40,000)•	FY 2020-21 = 30,000 (Cumulative 70,000)•	FY 2021-2022 = 40,000 (Cumulative 1,10,000)•	Till 31st December 2022 = 40,000 (Cumulative 1,50,000) (c)	As per the information received from State Government of Andhra Pradesh:•	The district wise details of AB-HWCs made operational as on 14.06.2019 is as below:S.no	District 	No of HWcs 1	Srikakulam	1602	Vizianagaram	1783	Visakhapatnam	2344	East Godavari	2355	West Godavari	2276	Krishna	1947	Guntur	1718	Prakasam	1369	SPS Nellore	11510	Chittoor	14611	YSR Kadapa	11612	Kurnool	14113	Ananthapur	137 	TOTAL :	2190•	733 AB-HWCs have been targeted to be operationalised in Krishna District by 2022 out of which 194 have been operationalised as on 14.06.2019. •	5 AB-HWCs were targeted to be operationalised in Machilipatnam and all of them have been operationalised. (d)	As per the information received from State Government of Andhra Pradesh, following services are being provided in the above AB-HWCs:i.	Comprehensive Maternal Health care services.ii.	Comprehensive neonatal and infant health care services.iii.	Childhood and adolescent health care servicesiv.	Comprehensive contraceptive services.v.	Comprehensive reproductive health services.vi.	Comprehensive management of communicable diseases.vii.	Screening and Comprehensive management of non-communicable diseases.viii.	ophthalmic care servicesix.	Basic ENT care servicex.	Screening and basic management of mental health ailments.xi.	Basic dental healthcare.xii.	Basic geriatric health care services.xiii.	Free Drugs and Diagnostics.
