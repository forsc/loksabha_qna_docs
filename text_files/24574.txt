                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

501






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Staff Strength




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether the Government has provided adequate job opportunities for the persons belonging to SC/ST/OBC in various departments, autonomous Offices, subsidiary and attached offices under the Ministry;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the SC/ST/OBC staff strength is far less than the required strength in a number of instances particularly in Group A, B, C and D; and(d) if so, the reasons therefor and the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (d)  Yes, Sir.;The details of representation of SC/ST/OBC in Ministry of Civil Aviation and its attached/subordinate offices are as under:(i) Ministry of Civil Aviation (Main Sectt.) makes direct recruitment in the grade of Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) only. All other posts are filled up on the basis of nominations received from Cadre Controlling Authorities i.e. DoPT, Dept. of Expenditure, Dept. of Official Language etc. and by promotion of the eligible officials in the feeder grades. MTS Representation in MoCA ( Main Sectt.) :On Roll Total MTS                                 18Required SC + ST + OBC (49.5%)        09On Roll  SC + ST + OBC MTS              10(ii) Details of Staff representation in Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) :On Roll Total employees                       702Required SC + ST + OBC (49.5%)       347   On Roll  SC + ST + OBC employees    330(iii) Details of Staff representation in Indira Gandhi Rashtriya Uran Akademi (IGRUA):On Roll Total employees                       106Required SC + ST + OBC (49.5%)         52On Roll SC + ST + OBC employees       68(iv) Appointments in Bureau of Civil Aviation Security (BCAS), Commission of Railway Safety (CRS) and Airports Economic Regulatory Authority (AERA) are made through deputation/selection. There is no direct recruitment made by these organizations. (v) In respect of  Aircraft Accident Investigation Bureau (AAIB) and Rajiv Gandhi National Aviation University (RGNAU), no direct recruitment has been made by these organizations so far.All efforts are made to provide representation of SC/ST/OBC persons as per extant Government instructions/orders where shortfall is noted.
