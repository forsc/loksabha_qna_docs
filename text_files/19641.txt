                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Buildings for Kendriya Vidyalayas




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether it is a fact that the many schools operated by the Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan do not have their own buildings and enough classrooms according to the number of students and if so, the detail thereof State-wise/UT particularly in Madhya Pradesh; (b) 	the name of the schools for which funds has been released to construct buildings/classes state-wise; and (c) 	the time by when the funds are likely to be provided to such schools?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)	Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has informed that as on date, 291 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are functioning in the temporary infrastructure provided by various sponsoring authorities. The State / UT-wise details are at Annexure-I.(b)	KV wise details of fund released during the year 2020-21 for the construction of the building/rooms are at Annexure-II.(c)	Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, which depends upon identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favour of KVS by the sponsoring authorities, submission of drawings / estimates by construction agency, availability of funds and requisite approvals etc. As such, no definite time frame can be given in this regard.*****
