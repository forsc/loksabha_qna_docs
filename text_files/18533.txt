                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2222






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






COVID-19 test centres in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of COVID-19 test centres in Jharkhand especially in Bokaro, Giridih and Dhanbad district;(b) whether the Government have provided any guidelines for tracing the cases to contain the disease; and(c) the number of isolation wards/centres established in Jharkhand especially in Bokaro, Giridih and Dhanbad districts?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) As informed by the State Government of Jharkhand, total number of COVID-19 test centers in Jharkhand are 92, out of which Bokaro has 4 centers, Giridih has 2 and Dhanbad has 4 centers.(b) The Integrated Disease Surveillance Programme under National Center for Disease Control (NCDC) has issued guidelines for contact tracing of COVID-19 cases in community settings.(c) As reported by State Government of Jharkhand, total number of isolation beds in Jharkhand including Bokaro, Dhanbad and Giridih districts are as follows:Center	CCC*	DCHC**	DCH***	TotalJharkhand	14022	2915	1441	18378Bokaro	250	50	40	340Giridih	440	54	20	514Dhanbad	700	190	100	990* COVID Care Centers** Dedicated COVID Health Centers*** Dedicated COVID Hospitals
