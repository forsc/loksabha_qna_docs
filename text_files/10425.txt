                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Line of Credit to Russia




M. Selvaraj













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Union Government has sanctioned $ 1 billion Line of Credit for Russia’s Far East programmes for the first time in the history of India;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the purpose for which India has sanctioned such line of credit and the motive behind it including the mode and terms for the credit; and(d)	the advantages and benefits derived for India through this sanction of credit to Russia?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	Yes. (b) to (d)	During his visit to Vladivostok on 4-5 September 2019 for the annual India-Russia bilateral Summit, Prime Minister announced a Line of Credit of USD 1 billion for development projects in the Russian Far East region. The Line of Credit (LOC) will facilitate undertaking infrastructure and other development projects. The terms and conditions will be finalised through discussions with the Russian Government. This Line of Credit will further Indian investment in the region, in such sectors such as coal mining and power, petroleum, natural gas, diamond and gold, rare earth elements, agriculture and export of skilled manpower from India to the Russian Far East. ***
