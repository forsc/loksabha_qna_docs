                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Handloom Haat in Gujarat




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal to set up urban handloom/handicraft haat across the country including Central Gujarat;(b) if so, the details thereof including the amount and location-wise allocation made in this regard; (c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of the urban handloom/handicraft haats functional in Gujarat at present, location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Textiles has till date sanctioned 38 no. of Urban Haats in different states. During the year 2019-20, one Urban Haat has been sanctioned for Madhubani in Bihar with a project cost of Rs.300.00 lakhs. The location-wise details of Urban Haats in Gujarat are as below:S. No.	Place	Year of Sanction	Status1.		Bhuj	2002-03	Functional2.		Ahmedabad	1998-99	Functional****
