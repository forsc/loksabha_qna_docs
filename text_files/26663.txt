                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2362






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Households Completed 100 Days of Work under MGNREGS




Santokh Singh Chaudhary













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    Hibi Eden
                                                








                                                    K. Muraleedharan
                                                








                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of households who have completed 100 days of work under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) in the months of April-June during the year 2020 and 2021;(b) 	whether there is an increase in households completing 100 days of guaranteed work between 2020 and 2021;(c) 	if so, whether the Government intends to increase the number of guaranteed days from 100 to 150 in order to meet the higher demand for work; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	A total of 3,91,112 households have completed 100 days of work under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) in the months of     April- June, 2021. A total of 3,18,532 households have completed 100 days of work under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) in the months of     April- June, 2020. (b)     There is an increase in the number of households who have completed 100 days of work under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA between 2020 and 2021. In this regard the details are given below:	             April- June, 2020	    April- June, 2021	      % increase Households	         318532	              391112	                 22.78%Source: Management Information System(c) & (d) Mahatma Gandhi NREG Act, 2005 is to provide at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult member volunteers to do unskilled manual work. State can provide additional man-days of work over and above of 100 days from its own resources. An additional 50 days of wage employment is provided over and above 100 days in the notified drought affected areas or natural calamity areas in the country on recommendation of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare. At present, there is no plan to increase days of work under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA.*****
