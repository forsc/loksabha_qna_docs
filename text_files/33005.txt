                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

584






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Delay of Vizhinjam Seaport Project




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken note of the delay in the works of Vizhinjam seaport project in Kerala; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (c) the present status of the project and the time by which the project is likely to be commissioned; and (d) the details of measures taken by the Government to ensure the completion of the project without any further delay?  






ANSWER




                                                         (a)& (b)       Government of Kerala entered into a Concession Agreement with Adani Vizhinjam Port Private Limited (APPL) in August 2015 for development and operation of Vizhinjam International Deepwater Multipurpose Seaport Project on Design, Build, Finance, Operate, Transfer (DBFOT) basis for initial concession period of 40 years. The construction of the port was scheduled to be completed on 03.12.2019, however, due to delay in the construction of the critical component of project i.e. breakwater, the project is delayed.(c)& (d)	Non-Major Ports are under the administrative control of the State Government. The Government of Kerala and the Concessionaire are working together to expedite the completion of the project without further delays.  As per revised schedule, the project completion date is December, 2024. Government of Kerala is reviewing the project and timelines and is extending all support to remove bottlenecks for early completion.                                                *****
