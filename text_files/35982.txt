                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

285






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Import of Toys




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it has come to the notice of the Government that the volume of import of toys from countries like China is adversely affecting the traditional cottage industry and the other fields of the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has taken/proposes to take steps to rectify the situation in a phased manner; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d):     A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. 285 FOR ANSWER ON WEDNESDAY, THE 23RD MARCH, 2022.(a) to (d):    	Government has taken proactive steps to restrict import of sub-standard and unsafe toys and to promote domestic toy industry. As a result of various steps taken by the Government over the last 2 years, the volume of import of toys into the Indian market has shown a consistently decreasing trend.(i)	Details of import of toys during the last 3 years are placed at Annexure-A. (ii) 	Details of certain steps taken by Government to support domestic and traditional toy industry are placed at Annexure-B.*****
