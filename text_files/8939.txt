                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2513






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






CRPF Mahila Battalion




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Ratansinh Magansinh Rathod
                                                








                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Central Reserved Police Force (CRPF) Mahila battalion deployed in various parts of the country;(b) whether the Government is considering to create more Mahila battalions and if so, the details thereof;(c) the achievements made by CRPF Mahila battalion since its inception;(d) the number of welfare schemes being run by the Government for the welfare of CRPF Mahila battalion along with the quantum of funds allocated for this purpose;(e) whether CRPF has any proposal to conduct recruitment drive across the country for recruitment to various posts in CRPF Mahila battalions;(f) if so, the details thereof and the time by which such recruitment drive is likely to be conducted; and(g) whether the Government has taken initiative to recruit more and more womenin CRPF and if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	  There are 6 (six)  Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) Mahila battalions. The Mahila battalions   are made available / deployed on the request of the State Government(s),  to  assist  them  in  maintaining  Law  and  Order.   The  level  of -2-L.S.US.Q.NO. 2513 FOR 3.12.2019deployment of CRPF Mahila battalion in any State is dynamic and undergoes changes depending upon the security situation.(b):	 There is no proposal under consideration to create more Mahila battalions of CRPF at present.(c):	Mahila contingents of CRPF are successfully performing Internal Security duties in various parts of the country and abroad. 05 Mahila personnel of CRPF contingents have been decorated with gallantry medals for their brave actions as under :- Type of Medal	NumberAshok Chakra	01Sena Medal	01President''s Police Medal for Gallantry	01Police Medal for Gallantry	02(d):	A scheme i.e. Creche-Facilities for the exclusive benefit of women personnel is functioning in CRPF. “Creche” facilities are available in 49 static locations of CRPF and funds to the tune of Rs. 55 lakh for the current financial year have been allotted to CRPF, for the purpose. (e) to (g):	Recruitment of personnel including Mahila personnel  in  the Forces  is a continuous process. Government has taken necessary steps to recruit 1594 numbers of Constable/General Duty (Mahila) through the Staff Selection Commission.******
