                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4029






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Track Child Portal




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	Whether the Government has proposed to conduct training programme on ‘Track Child Portal’ under the child safety plan (b) 	if so, the time by which the said training programme is likely to be organized in the country along with names of such places where the above training programmes have been conducted during the last three years, State-wise including Maharashtra; and (c) 	the total number of officials trained under the said programme; and the outcome of said training programme thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c):  The Ministry of Women and Child Development (MWCD) has developed web  portal “TrackChild” to track the missing and found children. The TrackChild Portal is implemented involving various stakeholders including Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), Ministry of Railways, State Governments/UT Administrations, Child Welfare Committees, Juvenile Justice Boards, National Legal Services Authority, etc. The details of the officers trained under the said programmes are not maintained by this Ministry.
