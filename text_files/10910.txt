                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4272






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Virology Detection Research Labs




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to set up any Virology Detection Research Labs (VDRL) in Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof;(b) the total funds allocated for the VDRL projects in Tamil Nadu;(c) whether the Government has received any complaint regarding the interference and subsequent stalling of the VDRL projects in Tamil Nadu; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the stand of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):  The scheme of Department of Health Research is Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratories (VRDL) and not Virology Detection Research Labs (VDRL). There is no proposal with the Department presently to set-up VRDL in Tamil Nadu. (b):	 Funds of Rs. 19,20,11,987 have been allocated for nine VRDL projects in Tamil Nadu, so far.(c):	 No.(d):	Does not arise.
