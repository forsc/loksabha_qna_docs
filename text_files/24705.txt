                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

540






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Road funded under CRIF




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the roads in each district of Andhra Pradesh that are being covered for financial assistance under the Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) at present;(b) whether the funds have been released for the said roads and if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The district-wise details of the roads being covered for financial assistance in Andhra Pradesh under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) at present are given in Annexure.(b) Release of Rs 285.02 Crore during 2021-22 under CRIF for the State of Andhra Pradesh is sanctioned vide letter RW/NH-28014/61/2007-AP/P-7(Vol. -V) dated 19.07.2021. (c) Does not arise.
