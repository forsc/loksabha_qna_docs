                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

637






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






FDI




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Foreign Direct Investments into India declined in the 2018-19 financial year as compared to previous years;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has assessed the reasons for the same; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):      	No Sir. As compared to previous years, Foreign Direct Investment has increased in the financial year 2018-19. The year-wise increase in FDI inflow can be seen in the following table:<pre>S. No.	Financial Year	Total FDI Inflow(in US$  billion)1.	2015-16	55.562.	2016-17	60.223.	2017-18	60.974.	2018-19 (P)	62.00 Note: (P) Figures are provisional. Source: Reserve Bank of India.</pre> (b) to (d):  	Do not arise. *******
