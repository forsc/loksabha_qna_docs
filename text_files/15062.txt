                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3884






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Districts having Cold Chain Projects




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of districts in the country having functioning integrated cold chain projects and other facilities of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana;(b)	the total number of districts having no such cold chain projects, State-wise particularly in Tamil Nadu;(c)	whether any action is being taken or proposed to be taken by the Ministry to set up cold chain projects in those districts having no such cold chain projects  till now particularly in Tamil Nadu;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	Out of total 732 districts in the country, as on date, operational / functional Integrated Cold Chain projects exist in 122 districts, Mega Food Park projects in 18 districts, CEFPPC projects in 12 districts, BFL projects in 58 districts and Food Testing laboratory projects in 69 districts under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana of Ministry of Food Processing Industries. No operational/ functional projects are available under Agro-processing Cluster Scheme and Operation Greens. A State/ UT-wise list showing number of Districts, where operational / completed projects exist under various schemes of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana as on 29.02.2020 is placed at Annexure-I.(b):	Out of total 732 districts in the country, 566 districts have no cold chain projects sanctioned by this Ministry under the Scheme for Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure. In Tamil Nadu, out of total 37 districts, no cold chain project has been sanctioned in 21 districts. A State/ UT-wise list showing number of districts, where no cold chain project has been sanctioned by this Ministry under the Scheme for Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure as on 29.02.2020 is placed at Annexure-II.(c) to (e): Ministry of Food Processing Industries invite proposals / applications from time to time under the Scheme for Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure for setting up of Integrated Cold Chain projects by floating Expression of Interest (EoI). The prospective promoters from any district of any state including the State of Tamil Nadu can apply against the EoI as per the extant guidelines of the scheme for availing the benefit under the scheme. Presently, such EoI has been floated on 29.01.2020.*****
