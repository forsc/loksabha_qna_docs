                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2802






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Development of Waterways




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken steps to develop 36 waterways in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the waterways that have been identified;(c)	whether any stretches from Andhra Pradesh and Telangana have been identified; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)  Based on the outcome of techno-economic Feasibility Study (FS) and Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) prepared for 106 new National Waterways (NWs), technical interventions have been planned for safe navigation and shipping on technically viable NWs by the Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI).   As per the FS/ DPRs completed so far for the new NWs, 36 NWs have been found to be technically viable as per details at Annex.1 and development activities have been initiated on 10 viable NWs viz. NW-9, NW-16, NW-27, NW-37, NW-40, NW-58, NW-68, NW-86, NW-97 and NW-111.(c) & (d)  Seven rivers passing through the States of Andhra Pradesh & Telangana have been declared as National Waterways (NWs) and one existing National Waterway-4 has been extended vide The National  Waterways Act, 2016 for shipping and navigation as per details at Annex-2.
