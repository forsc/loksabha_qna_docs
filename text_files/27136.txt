                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Development of Roads under CRIF




Jual Oram













                                                    Chandeshwar Prasad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of roads in various States including Odisha which are in dilapidated conditions; (b) whether the Government provides funds to the States for repairing/development of roads from the Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF); (c) if so, the details of the funds provided from CRIF and utilised by the States for this purpose during each of the last three years and the current year, State/ UT-wise including Odisha and Jharkhand; (d) the details of road development projects taken up in various States under CRIF during the said period and the time by which these projects are likely to be completed, State/UT-wise including Bihar, Uttar Pradesh and Tamil Nadu; and (e) the measures taken by the Union Government to increase the road infrastructure in Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
