                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Ban on Photography in Military Stations




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has banned taking pictures of the top-secret military stations and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government is likely to consider and issue guidelines prohibiting the use of mobile devices inside military installations as the latest mobile technology has capabilities to collect and transmit sensitive data;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (d):  As per policy on use of mobile phones in Indian Army, carriage/usage of smart phones including mobile phones having camera/internet facility in office premises is strictly prohibited.  Compliance to policy/directives are reiterated at regular intervals for strict adherence, apart from surprise checks and initiation of disciplinary action against any violations.*******
