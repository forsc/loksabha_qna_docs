                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3435






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






KV in Pudducherry




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that the Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in Mahe region of UT of Puducherry is functioning in rented building for so many years since its inception; and (b) 	if so, by when the construction of the dedicated building for the purpose is likely to be completed for use?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	The Kendriya Vidyalaya (KV) in Mahe region of Union Territory (UT) of Puducherry is functioning in temporary building provided by sponsoring authority i.e. UT of Puducherry. (b)	Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, which depends upon identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favour of KVS by the sponsoring authorities, submission of drawings / estimates by construction agency, availability of funds and requisite approvals etc. ******
