                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

211






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Development Projects/Schemes in Tejpur




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of all the ongoing developmental projects/schemes of the Ministry in Tejpur Parliamentary Constituency ; and(b) 	the funds allocated, sanctioned and utilised in this regard during the last three years and the current year so far?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.******* Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) and (b) of the Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *211 for answer on 13.12.2021(a) and (b)  For  funding projects under schemes of Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region (MDoNER), State Governments send the duly prioritized project proposals to the Ministry. These prioritized projects then undergo several stages of examination inter alia by Inter-Ministerial Committee at two separate stages for selection and sanction, as per scheme guidelines. Under the schemes of this Ministry, 11 projects with total sanctioned cost of Rs.86.78 crore are ongoing in Tezpur Parliamentary Constituency. All these projects are at various stages of implementation.  Details of projects as on 30.11.2021 are given below: Sl.
