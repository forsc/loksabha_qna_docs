                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

577






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Centres of Excellence




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has undertaken any review including appreciation by foreign agency regarding four Centres of Excellence on advanced manufacturing set up in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has felt any need to set up more such centres in the country, particularly in Kerala;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                                     THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                                                  (SHRI ARVIND GANPAT SAWANT)(a): to (e):Under the Scheme “Enhancement of Competitiveness in the Indian Capital Goods Sector”, one of the components is Centres of Excellence (CoE). Project proposals are received from industry/ research institutes. These have national coverage and national benefits.          Regular reviews of the ongoing projects under the Scheme are done by the Project Review and Monitoring Committees (PRMC) set up for the respective projects.  The Screening Committee and the Apex Committee under the Scheme also review and monitor the progress of the projects.                                               *****
