                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2508






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Strength of IPS Officers




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that as per the latest position, the sanctioned strength of Indian Police Service (IPS) officers in Telangana is 140 and there are 35 vacancies;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the efforts being made by the Government to fill up the 35 vacancies in a time-bound manner; and(d) the total number of IPS officers likely to be allotted to Telangana in this year‘s UPSC exam to bring down vacancies?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (b):	As on 01.01.2020, the sanctioned strength of IPS officers in Telangana is 139 and 34 posts are lying vacant.(c):	The vacancies in the service are caused due to factors such as retirement, resignations, death, removal from service etc.  All these factors are recurring in nature and relative with the rate of recruitment.  To fill up the vacancies of IPS officers, the batch-size of IPS (direct recruit) has been increased from 88 to 103 from Civil Services Examination (CSE), 2005, 130 from CSE, 2008, 150 from CSE, 2009 and 200 from CSE 2020.(d):	05 candidates have been allocated in IPS to Telangana cadre through Civil Services Examination (CSE) 2019.  *******
