                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

838






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Areas Affected by Arsenic and Fluoride in DrinkingWater




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 		the number of habitations affected by Arsenic and Fluoride in drinking water as on 1st March, 2015 and 1st August, 2020 in the country, State-wise;(b) 		the details of habitations covered for piped drinking water supply under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) till date, State-wise; and(c) 		the details of funds allocated, released and utilised under NRDWP during the last year and the current year, till date, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	As reported by States, details of habitations having Arsenic and Fluoride-affected drinking water sources is at Annex–I.(b) 	As reported by States, details of habitations having piped water supply systems is at Annex–II.(c) 	NRDWP has been subsumed into Jal Jeevan Mission–Har Ghar Jal, which aims to provide tap water connection to every rural household by 2024. State-wise details of fund allocated, released and reported utilisation under Jal Jeevan Mission during 2019–20 and 2020–21 is at Annex–III.
