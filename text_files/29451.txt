STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Health Sector Grants




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether as per the recommendations of the Fifteenth Finance Commission, the Government has released funds as health sector grants for rural and urban local bodies of nineteen States;(b)	if so,  the details thereof along with the amount of such grants recommended to be released for rural and urban local bodies in the current financial year, State/UT-wise including Odisha;(c)	the present status of proposals from remaining nine States received in this regard along with the funds proposed to be released to the respective States/UTs;(d)	whether the government has also identified interventions that will directly lead to strengthening the primary health infrastructure and facilities in both rural and urban areas and has earmarked grants for each intervention; and(e)	whether the Government has set targets to strengthen the health systems and plug critical gaps in healthcare systems at primary healthcare level and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF FINANCE  (a) to (e): A statement is laid on the table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED IN PART (A) TO (E) OF THE REPLY TO THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *107 BY SHRI  BHARTRUHARI  MAHTAB REGARDING “HEALTH SECTOR GRANTS” DUE FOR ANSWER ON MONDAY DECEMBER 06, 2021.(a)	Yes Sir.  As per the recommendations  of the Fifteenth Finance Commission and based on the examination of proposals of States and consequent  recommendations by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, the Department of Expenditure has released funds as Health Grants for rural and urban local bodies of  all the States. (b)	State-wise and component-wise Health Grants released to all the States including the State of Odisha during the current financial year is given in Annexure-I.  State-wise details of Health Sector Grants for local bodies recommended by the 15th Finance Commission for the current year is given in Annexure-II.(c)	Health Grants to all the remaining nine States (i.e. Gujarat, Haryana, Kerala, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Telangana, Goa, Tripura and Uttar Pradesh) were also released on 30-11-2021. Annexure-I includes details of these nine States as well.(d)	Yes Sir.(e)	 Yes Sir. The Fifteenth Finance Commission (FC-XV) in its Final Report in Chapter 7 entitled “Empowering Local Governments” has recommended a total grant amounting to Rs. 70,051 crore to strengthen and plug the critical gaps in the health care system at the primary health care level.  In pursuance of the acceptance of the recommendations of FC-XV, a National Level Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of the Secretary, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare and States have been requested to constitute State Level Committees and District Level Committees with members from all Nodal Ministries/Departments for monitoring and approval of the health sector grants.  Further, the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has developed Technical and Operational Guidelines for implementation of all the components of FC-XV, as per the mandate given by the FC-XV which inter-alia includes  description of each of the specific components, objectives of the component, the unit cost applicable for the component, factors to be considered for planning etc.  The components identified for upgradation of primary health care infrastructure in Rural and Urban areas along with the summary of the amount earmarked year-wise are given in Annexure-III.*********Annexure-I 15th F.
