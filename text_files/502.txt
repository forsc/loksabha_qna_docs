                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

625






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






National Centres for Differently Abled Students




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the National Centers or Model Schools opened by the government for blind, deaf and dumb and other handicapped persons, State-wise; and (b)	the details of such centers/schools in Maharashtra and the number of students presently studying therein?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT (SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) & (b) The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, through its National Institutes, runs the following model schools for specific disabilities:-1.	National Institute for the Empowerment of Persons with Visual Disabilities (Divyangjan), Dehradun, Uttarkhand•	Model School for the Visually Handicapped (MSVH)2.	National Institute for the Empowerment of Persons with Intellectual Disabilities (Divyangjan), Secunderabad, Telangana•	Three Special Education Centres at Secunderabad, New Delhi and  NOIDA.3.	Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya National Institute for Persons with Physical Disabilities (Divyangjan), New Delhi•	Model Integrated Primary School4.	National Institute for Empowerment of Persons with Multiple Disabilities (Divyangjan), Chennai,Tamil Nadu.•	Model Special Education CentreThere is  no such centre/school of National Institutes in Maharashtra.However, the Department is implementing a scheme namely Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme(DDRS) under which grant-in-aid is provided to Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) for running on-going special schools. It does not provide funds for opening of special schools under DDRS. A statement indicating State wise details of special schools and number of beneficiaries under DDRS including State of Maharashtra is at Annexure.*****
