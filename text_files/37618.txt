                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4880






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Maritime Cooperation with Neighbouring Countries




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether NSA-level talks of the Colombo Security Conclave concluded recently in Maldives;(b)	if so, the details and outcome thereof; and(c)	whether the Government has called for close cooperation among India’s maritime neighbours to combat shared security challenges and if so, the response / assurance received in such meetings till date?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS(SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN)(a) to (c)  The 5th National Security Adviser level meeting of the Colombo Security Conclave was held in Maldives on 09-10 March 2022. This conclave was attended by the respective National Security Advisers of India, Maldives, Sri Lanka and Mauritius. Delegations from Bangladesh and Seychelles participated as observers.	In the meeting, key areas of cooperation to enhance and strengthen regional security were identified in the following five pillars:Maritime Safety and SecurityCountering Terrorism and RadicalisationCombating Trafficking and Transnational Organised CrimeCyber Security, Protection of Critical Infrastructure and TechnologyHumanitarian Assistance and Disaster Relief       Government of India has called for close cooperation among India’s maritime neighbours under the vision of SAGAR (Security and Growth for All in the Region) to combat shared security challenges and the above mentioned outcome of the National Security Adviser level meeting of the Colombo Security Conclave is in line with the same.****
