                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1925






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Swacch Bharat Mission in Rajasthan




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the works carried out in thedistricts of Rajasthan under Swacch Bharat Mission and the amount of funds allocated/released/utilized under the Mission during the last three years; and(b) 	the estimated number of people benefited from Swacch Bharat Mission in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): As per information received from the Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation, Ministry of Jal Shakti, under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM (G)], 83,52,303 individual household latrines (IHHLs) and 3286 community sanitary complexes (CSCs) have been constructed in Rajasthan and all the 42,860 villages have declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF).   Since SBM(G) is a demand driven scheme, no fixed allocation is made to the States under the programme.  However, Central share released to Rajasthan under SBM (G) and fund utilized by the State during the last three years are as under:Year	          Funds released (Rs. In crore)     	Funds  tilized (Rs. In crore)2016-17	                777.30	                             1065.632017-18	                981.51	                              848.212018-19	                865.88	                              350.19 (b): About 4.17 crore people ( @ 5 people/household) are estimated to have benefited from SBM (G) in Rajasthan.*******
