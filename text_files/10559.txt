                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3932






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Swachh Iconic Places




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has included more iconic sites including Sabarimala temple in Kerala under Phase 3 of Swachh Iconic Places (SIP) project;(b) If so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the activities proposed in these iconic sites along with the plan proposed for the same;(d) the details of the progress achieved in Phase 1 and Phase 2 of the project; and(e) whether any plan has been prepared for Sabarimala under this project and if so, the details thereof ? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI                     (SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c)  Yes, Sir. 10 sites have been included under Phase-III of Swachh Iconic Places (SIP) project under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen). Activities proposed in the plan to be undertaken in each of Phase-III sites are placed at Annexure-I     (d) The progress achieved, as reported by the concerned authorities, in Phase I and Phase II of Swachh Iconic Places Projects is placed at Annexure-II and Annexure-III respectively.(e) No plan has been received for the Sabarimala Temple under Swachh Iconic Places project.
