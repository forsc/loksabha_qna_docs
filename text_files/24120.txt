                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

100






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Deemed to be Universities in India




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Central, State, Private and Deemed to be Universities in India, State wise;(b) whether all the recognised Universities need to be affiliated to UGC or AICTE or some other organization and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the details of the affiliation of all Universities in India as also those not affiliated, State-wise;(d) the Institutions of National Importance who do not have affiliation to UGC or other statutory bodies, offer academic undergraduate and postgraduate programmes and if so, whether these programmes are recognised globally and if not, the reasons thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): The number of State-wise and type-wise Universities, according to the All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) report 2019-20, is at Annexure.(b) & (c): As per provisions of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act, 1956, the UGC does not affiliate universities.(d): Institutions are declared/established as Institutions of National Importance (INIs) through Acts of Parliament. The programmes offered by the Indian Institutes of Technology, Indian Institutes of Information Technology, Indian Institutes of Management, Indian Institutes of Science Education & Research; the INIs coming under the purview of the Ministry of Education; are accepted globally
