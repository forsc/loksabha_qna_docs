                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

276






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Living Standards of Religious Groups




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 276TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.02.2020LIVING STANDARDS OF RELIGIOUS GROUPS276. SHRIMATI SAJDA AHMED:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether any study has been conducted to know the level of the living standards of economically underprivileged population of different religious groups and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is true that the inequality level in the living standards of the Scheduled Castes (SCs) population has increased during the last three years and if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of monthly per capita consumer expenditure of the different households belonging to religious groups; and (d) the steps taken by the Government to bring equality in the living standards among the population of the different religious groups?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS  ATHAWALE)(a): As per the Allocation of Business Rules 1961 the matter does not pertain to this Ministry. Therefore,  no study has been conducted by the department to know the level of the living standards of economically underprivileged population of different religious groups;(b), (c) and (d) do not arise****
