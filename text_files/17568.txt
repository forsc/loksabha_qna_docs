                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1308






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Redraw norms for deemed to be Universities




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to redraw norms for deemed to be universities;(b) if so, the details thereof and by when the norms are likely to be put in place;(c) the total number of such universities in the country and the number of applications pending for grant of deemed to be University status;(d) whether the Government has consulted/ proposes to consult all the stakeholders for making entry norms more stringent; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): At present, there is no such proposal under consideration. (c) : At present, there are 124 Institutions Deemed to be Universities in the country. Further, 06 applications are received online through Deemed to be university portal for granting of Deemed to be University status. (d) & (e):  The eligibility norms for an institution to be declared as  deemed to be university are mentioned at Clause 4.0 of the UGC [Institutions of Deemed to be Universities] Regulations, 2019. The above UGC Regulations, 2019 was finalized after consultation with all stakeholders. The UGC [Institutions of Deemed to be Universities] Regulations, 2019 is available at UGC website: ugc.ac.in. *****
