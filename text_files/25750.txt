                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1522






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Infrastructure in Arunachal Pradesh




Tapir Gao














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Government is providing assistance in construction of necessary infrastructure to make Arunachal Pradesh a global tourist spot;(b)        if so, the details thereof;(c)      the details of the funds allocated by the Government for the purpose during the last three years, year-wise; and(d)     whether North-Eastern Council has allocated any funds for the above mentioned works?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION[SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY ](a) to (d)    Yes Sir. Projects to improve tourism & other related infrastructure connectivity and access to tourist spots have been undertaken to attract tourists including global tourists to NER States. Ministry of Tourism under its scheme of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ provides financial Assistance to the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations etc for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc. Under the scheme of Swadesh Darshan of the Ministry of Tourism, Mega Circuit at Bhalukpong- Bomdila and Tawang  has been sanctioned  at a costs of Rs. 49.77 crore in 2014-15 and another North East Circuit viz. “Development of Nafra- Seppa- Pappu, Pasa, Pakke Valleys- Sangdupota- New Sagalee- Ziro- Yomcha” has been sanctioned at a cost of Rs. 96.72 crore in 2015-16 in the State of Arunachal Pradesh.      Some of the other initiatives undertaken by the Government for improving infrastructure connectivity in the State of Arunachal Pradesh  inter-alia include Capital rail connectivity to Itanagar; Murkongselek – Pasighat Rail project at an estimated costs of Rs.414 crore; Tezu Airport at an estimated cost of Rs. 54.00 crore; new Airport at Holongi, Arunachal Pradesh at an estimated cost of Rs. 645.63 crore; Capital Road Connectivity to Itanagar (Arunachal Pradesh) Project (4 laning Nagaon bypass to Holongi) at an estimated cost of Rs. 4348 crore; Mobile connectivity in Arunachal Pradesh and 2 Districts of Assam at a revised cost of Rs. 2029 crore and  Comprehensive Scheme for Strengthening Transmission and Distribution System in Arunachal Pradesh and Sikkim with revised estimated costs of Rs. 9129.32 crore. Under the North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS), Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region, provides financial assistance for physical infrastructure projects relating to water supply, power and connectivity especially aimed at promoting tourism. The details of the projects sanctioned in the State of Arunachal Pradesh under North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS), Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region for water supply, power and connectivity during the last three years from 2018-19 to 2020-21, funds released for tourism projects by the North Eastern Council, Shillong during the financial years from 2018-19 to 2020-21 in the State of Arunachal Pradesh are at Annexure-I.
