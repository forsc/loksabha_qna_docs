                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Development of Handicrafts Sector




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Dileshwar Kamait
                                                








                                                    Jyotirmay Singh Mahato
                                                








                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                








                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Navneet Ravi Rana
                                                








                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the arts and crafts are struggling to survive in the country particularly in Madhya Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Jharkhand, Haryana, Bihar, West Bengal, Maharashtra, Assam and other North-Eastern States and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (b) the details of Handicraft Service Centre established under the office of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts) for the development of Handicraft sector at the central level in the country especially in Amravati, Assam and other North-Eastern States along with the achievements made during the last three years; and (c) the State-wise details of the assistance received by the artisans through these Handicrafts Service Centres in Assam and other North-Eastern States during the last three years along with the improvement made in the lives of Handicraft artisans through these centres?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a):	No, Sir.(b) & (c):	Presently there is 1 Regional office at Guwahati and 10 Handicrafts Service Centres (HSCs) in Guwahati, Gauripur, Jorhat, Agartala, Shillong, Kohima, Itanagar, Imphal, Gangtok and Aizawal. The Amravati (Maharashtra) region comes under the Jurisdiction of HSC, Nagpur. The State-wise number of programmes conducted and number of artisans benefitted in the North Eastern states is annexed at Annexure.Annexure referred to in reply part (b) & (c) of the Lok Sabha unstarred question 3227 for answer on 23.03.2022. State-wise programmes and artisans benefitted for the last three years:SL. NO.	STATE/UT''S	No. of Programmes	No. of Artisans benefitted1.		Arunachal Pradesh	28	2,3162.		Assam	187	10,0903.		Manipur	93	5,1584.		Meghalaya	25	1,3315.		Mizoram	11	3626.		Nagaland	58	1,8517.		Sikkim	39	1,2608.		Tripura	50	2,039TOTAL	491	24,407***
