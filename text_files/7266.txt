                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

926






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






FM Radio Station in Gujarat




Pradeep Kumar Singh













                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of radio stations set up in Gujarat State;(b) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to improve the radio signals in rural areas of Gujarat; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a):	Prasar Bharati has informed that presently, there are 16 AIR stations functioning in the State of Gujarat at Ahmedabad, Ahwa, Bhuj, Godhra, Himmatnagar, Rajkot, Surat, Vadodara, Mehsana, Bhavnagar, Amreli, Bharuch, Jamnagar, Porbandar, Dwarka and Junagarh. (b) & (c): 	Prasar Bharati is providing radio coverage to most of the areas including rural areas of Gujarat. To improve the radio signals in various parts of Gujarat, Prasar Bharati has implemented the replacement of outlived MW transmitters by state-of-the-art digital transmitters at Ahmedabad and Rajkot. Replacement and upgradation of FM transmitters at Ahmedabad and Godhra has also been implemented.In addition to above, a 10 kW FM station with studio facility at Dahod and 100 W FM transmitters at 10 locations have also been approved.*****
