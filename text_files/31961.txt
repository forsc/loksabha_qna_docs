                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3532






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Integrated Teachers Education Programme




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has notified a four-year Integrated Teachers Education Programme and if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives behind the move;(b) the academic session from which the said Programme will be offered along with the agency responsible to the conduct admission test;(c) the details of broad policy and the institutional framework provided by the Government for teacher education;(d) whether training of teachers is being regulated by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) and if so, the details thereof;(e) whether NCTE has failed to provide trained teachers for the primary and secondary level and if so, the details thereof and the steps taken/being taken by the Government to address the issue; and(f) whether the standard of education is declining in various States due to shortage of trained/skilled teachers and if so, the details thereof along with the corrective measures taken or proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) Yes Sir. NCTE has notified a four-year Integrated Teachers Education Programme vide Notification in the Gazette of India on 26.10.2021.(b) Four year ITEP is being offered in pilot mode initially in 50 selected multi-disciplinary institutions across the country from the academic session 2022-23. National Testing Agency is responsible to conduct the admission test.(c) The details of the four year ITEP are available at the link: https://ncte.gov.in/WebAdminFiles/RecentAnnouncement/0_27_10_2021_637709061829643386.pdf(d) and (f): NCTE exercises the powers in granting recognition for various teacher education programmes as vested in the NCTE Act, 1993.Education is in the concurrent list of the Constitution. The recruitment, service conditions and deployment of teachers come under the purview of the respective State and Union Territory (UT) Government. The recruitment of teachers in schools is a continuous process and the vacancies keep arising due to retirement, resignation and additional requirements on account of enhanced students’ strength/new schools.*****
