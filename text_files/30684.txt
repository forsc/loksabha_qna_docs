                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2192






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






VRDL Scheme




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian













                                                    D. M. Kathir Anand
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the detailed list of Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratories in Tamil Nadu and Puducherry under VRDL scheme;(b)   Whether the centre has appointed/notified principal investigators and company principal investigators for the VRDL scheme in Tamil Nadu and (c)    if so, the details thereof; (d)   whether the Government has formed the Biosafety Committee as per norms of VRDL scheme; and (e)    if so, the composition of Committee members and their roles and responsibilities?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PARVIN PAWAR)(a), (b) & (c)   The Government has set up 10 Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratories (VRDLs) in Tamilnadu and 02 in Puducherry under the Department of Health Research’s Scheme ‘Setting up of nation-wide network of laboratories for managing epidemics and natural calamities’. The Principal Investigator (PI) and Co- Principal Investigator (Co-PI) are appointed by the concerned Institute wherein the Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratory (VRDL) is set up. The details of Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratories (VRDLs) are attached in the Annexure. (d) & (e)   The Scheme ‘Setting up of nation-wide network of laboratories for managing epidemics and natural calamities,’ under which VRDLs are set up; do not mention the constitution of Biosafety Committee. However, Institute Biosafety Committee (IBSC) has been formed in JIPMER and approved by Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Govt. of India. The details of the same are given at https://www.jipmer.edu.in/research/research-committees/institutional-bio-safety-committeeibsc
