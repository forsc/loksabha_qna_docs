                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1647






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Funds Allocation under PMGSY




Ramesh Chander Kaushik














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b)	the amount of funds allocated during the last three years under the Yojana, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government proposes to bring a special scheme for modernisation of rural roads of all the States including Haryana; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As per available information since inception  till date (26.6.2019), a total of 6,93,737 km of road length has been cleared under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), out of which 6,00,941 km road length has been constructed. (b):	The amount of funds allocated during the last three years under the Scheme, State-wise is at Annexure.(c) & (d):	A proposal for rolling out Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana-III (PMGSY-III) for strengthening of rural roads those connecting villages to Gramin Agricultural Markets, Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals is under consideration of the Government. *****
