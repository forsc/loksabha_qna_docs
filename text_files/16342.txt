                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Funds Allocated for Protection of HistoricalMonuments




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details regarding important archaeological sites and protected historical monuments in the country ;(b)	the funds allocated for preserving and developing the archaeological sites during the last three years, year-wise and State-wise; and(c)	the details of measures taken/being taken by ASI for the development of various tourist and archaeological sites along with tourist amenities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) 	There are 3692 monuments/sites declared as of National importance under the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958 and looked after by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI).  A statement indicating State/UT-wise number of monuments/sites is given at Annexure-I.(b)	The funds allocated and expenditure incurred for conservation, preservation and maintenance of all centrally protected monuments / sites in the country (State/UT-wise) during the last three years is at Annexure-II.(c)	Providing facilities at protected monuments is a continuous phenomenon.  Majority of protected monuments are provided with basic amenities.
