                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3009






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Compensation to Families of Deceased Businessmen




Abhishek Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the families of accidental deaths and suicides of MSME businessmen in 2020 have been compensated by the Union Government and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the MSME schemes put in place by the Union Government are being utilized to prevent this disaster from happening again; and(c) 	the details of funds allocated and spent under MSME schemes for COVID-19 relief?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b):	The Ministry of MSME does not maintain data of accidental deaths and suicides of MSME businessmen. However, to address Covid-19 related issues, Government has taken a number of initiatives under ‘Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan’ to support the MSME Sector in the country which inter-alia include measures such as (i) Rs. 4.5 lakh crore Collateral free Automatic Loans under Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) for businesses, including MSMEs; (ii) Rs. 20,000 crore Credit Guarantee Scheme for Subordinate Debt (CGSSD); (iii) Rs. 50,000 crore equity infusion through Self Reliant India Fund (SRI Fund).	For the growth and development of MSME Sector in the country the Ministry of MSME implements various schemes and programmes. These schemes and programmes inter-alia include Prime Minister’s Employment Generation programme (PMEGP), Credit Guarantee Scheme (CGS), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry & Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE) and Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), etc.(c):	Under MSME schemes for COVID-19 relief, Rs. 2.82 lakh crore of loans have been sanctioned as on 19.11.2021 under Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS)  and a total number of 754 guarantees were approved amounting to Rs 81.47 crore as on 18.11.2021 under Credit Guarantee Scheme for Subordinate Debt (CGSSD).
