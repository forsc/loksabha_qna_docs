                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

29






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Rural Roads in Andhra Pradesh




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received a request from the State Government of Andhra Pradesh for increased allocation of Rural Roads from 3285 km to 6135 km.(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government is likely to accede to the said request; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d)  The Government has approved phase-III of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) with an aim to consolidate 1,25,000 km Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary School and Hospitals across the country. The State of Andhra Pradesh has been allocated target length of 3,285 km under PMGSY-III. A proposal was received from the State of Andhra Pradesh for revision of target length from 3,285 Km to 6,135 Km. The national target of 1,25,000 Km was fixed by the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA).  State-wise targets, including the target of the State of Andhra Pradesh under PMGSY-III have been decided  applying the uniform formula for all the States, no change is proposed at this stage. *****
