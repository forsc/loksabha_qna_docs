                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Women and Child Development Committees




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to constitute Women and Child Development Committees in rural and urban areas at Gram Panchayat and Nagar Panchayat levels respectively to identify various women victims affected by hunger, poverty & domestic violence and to secure social and economic security to them ;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the names and rights of the Members of the proposed committee;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d)	the measures likely to be devised/ adopted to ensure social and economic security to aforementioned women and children expeditiously?  






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c) : There is no such proposal under consideration of Ministry of Women and Child Development.  (d) :     Government has taken several initiatives through schematic and legislative interventions for economic security of women.  Employment generation coupled with improving employability including that of women is priority for the Government.  Government has taken various steps for generating employment in the country like encouraging private sector and through schemes such as Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), Deendayal Antodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM), Start-up Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP), Aajeevika Grameen Express Yojana (AGEY) sub-schemes under DAY-NRLM, Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana (MKSP), Pradhan Mantri Rojgar Protsahan Yojana (PMRPY), Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY), National Career Service (NCS) Project, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) etc. The Ministry of Women and Child Development also implements a number of Schemes for social and economic empowerment of women. These inter-alia include Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY), National Crèche Scheme,  Working Women Hostel (WWH), Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP), One Stop Centre (OSC), Universalisation of Women Helpline (WHL), Mahila Police Volunteers (MPV),  Swadhar Greh, Ujjawala, etc. *****
