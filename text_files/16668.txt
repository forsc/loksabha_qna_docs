                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

411






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Houses to Poor People




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah













                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number and details of houses provided by the Government to the poor people in the rural areas of the country, State-wise including Uttarakhand and Bundelkhand;(b)	the targets of the Government with regard to the houses likely to be provided in Uttarakhand and Bundelkhand in the next two to three years and the last two years respectively;(c) 	whether any survey regarding effects of providing houses and migration for employment has been conducted; and(d) 	if so, the details of the migration affected regions, State-wise including Uttarakhand andBundelkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):    The State-wise details of the houses allotted to the States/UTs including Uttarakhand under PMAY-G is given at Annexure-I.  (b):      The Bundelkhand region is spread over to two States viz. Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh. For the regions of  Bundelkhand, the year wise  targets allocated, sanctioned and completed houses under PMAY-G is given at Annexure II. As the Permanent Wait list of Uttar Pradesh has been saturated, thus no target was allocated to State for the Year 2020-21. The targets of 10.06 lakh have been allocated to state of Madhya Pradesh for the year 2020-21.                 Under PMAY-G, the State of Uttarakhand has identified a total of 13,053 eligible beneficiaries as per SECC data, out of these, 12,656 have already been sanctioned houses.  The State of Uttarakhand has not been allocated any targets during the last two years i.e. 2018-19 and 2019-20.  For the Bundelkhand region, district-wise targets for the last two years and for the present year are given at Annexure-III.  (c)        No, Sir. (d)       Does not arise. *******
