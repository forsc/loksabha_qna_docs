                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1637






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Use of Plastic Waste in Road Construction




Vishnu Dutt Sharma













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                








                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is using discarded plastic waste in road   construction in the country; (b)    if so, the length of roads constructed/proposed to be constructed as a result thereof; and (c) 	whether use of plastic waste in construction of roads is environment friendly and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Yes sir, so far 703km length of National Highways have been constructed using waste plastic in wearing coat of flexible pavement. MoRTH has issued guidelines for mandatory use of waste plastic in Periodic Renewal with hot mixes and in wearing coat of service road on NHs within 50km periphery of urban area having population of more than 5 lakhs.    (c) Yes sir, Use of plastic waste in construction of road protects the environment from adverse impact of waste plastic. *****
