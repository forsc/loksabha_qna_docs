                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

393






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Disinvestment of PSUs




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the details of Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) privatized/disinvested under his Ministryand the amount of revenue generated from this till now, PSU-wise;b) whether any discrepancies has been noticed in the process of privatization/disinvestmentof PSUs;c) if so, the details thereof, PSU-wise and the action taken in this regard;d) whether the Government has taken a decision to disinvest its share in National FertilizersLimited and National Chemicals and Fertilizers Limited; ande) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefore along with the details of revenue earnedfrom it?*
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS (SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) to (c): Under the Department of Fertilizers, Process of disinvestment of 100% share ofGovt. of India in Projects & Development India Limited (PDIL) through strategic sale wasapproved ‘in principle’ by Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) in its meeting heldon 27th December, 2016. Alternative Mechanism (AM) in its meeting held on 18th February,2019 considered and approved the following recommendations of Core Group of Secretarieson disinvestment (CGD):i). Rejection of conditional financial bid submitted by Engineers India Limited (EIL) asrecommended by CGD.ii). Authorize Department of Fertilizers to initiate the process for disinvestment of 100% shareholding of GoI in PDIL to strategic buyer identified through 2-stage auction process.Under the Department of Pharmaceuticals, Cabinet on 28.12.2016 and on 17.07.2019 has decided for closure of Indian Drugs & Pharmaceuticals Ltd.(IDPL) & Rajasthan Drugs & Pharmaceuticals Ltd. (RDPL) and Strategic Sale of Bengal Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd. (BCPL & Hindustan Antibiotics Ltd.(HAL). CCEA on 01.11.2017 has decided 100% disinvestment of Govt. of India equity in Karnataka Antibiotics & Pharmaceuticals Ltd.(KAPL).Till now, no revenue has been generated through disinvestment.(d) & (e): No, Sir. There is no such decision has been taken.*****
