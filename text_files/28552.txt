                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

168






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Registration under E-shram Portal




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of numbers of beneficiaries registered under E-shram Portal till date, State/district-wise; (b)details of total number of E-shram cards issued in the State of Maharashtra, district-wise along with the details of bank account seeded E-shram cards; (c)whether the Government will share the unorganized labourers data with State governments; if so, whether the Government has envisaged data sharing security measures for the same; and (d)whether the Government is planning for linkage of One-Nation-One-Ration Card, E-Shram Card (especially bank account seeded) and the Election Commission Card for furthering benefits to the unorganised labourers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Labour & Employment has developed e-SHRAM portal which is a National Database of the Unorganised Workers seeded with Aadhaar. It has been launched on 26.08.2021 and made available to the States/ UTs for the registration of unorganised workers on e-SHRAM portal. As on 23rd November, 2021, 8.97 crore unorganised workers have been registered on e-SHRAM portal. The details of State wise registered unorganised workers as on 23.11.2021 is at Annexure-I. There are 19,70,974 unorganised workers registered in the State of Maharashtra so far. The District wise details of unorganised workers registered in the State of Maharashtra on e-SHRAM portal as on 23.11.2021 is at Annexure-II. One of the key objectives of the portal is to deliver the benefits of Social Security Schemes of Central and State Governments through e-SHRAM portal.  **                                                  *****
