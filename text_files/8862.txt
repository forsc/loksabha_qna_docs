                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2337






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Security to Vital Installations by CISF




Dhanush M Kumar













                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of men and women Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) personnel deployed for providing safety cover in various vital installations in the country;(b) whether the Government has fixed any criteria before putting vital installations under the security cover of CISF and if so, the details thereof along with the number of vital installations guarded by CISF as on date;(c) the number of welfare schemes being run by the Government for the welfare of CISF personnel along with the quantum of funds allocated for this purpose;(d) whether the Government is considering to set up women CISF unit in the country, if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be set up along with the problems being faced in setting up of such units; and(e) whether CISF is planning to raise a Special Tactical Wing (STW) for enhanced combat and tactical advantage against terror attacks and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)	:  142561 number of CISF personnel (133922 men and 8639 women) are deployed at various installations in the country.--2--LS.US.Q.NO. 2337 FOR 03.12.2019(b):  CISF security is decided on the basis of the threat perception and the commitment of the organization concerned to bear the cost of deployment. Presently, CISF is providing security at 346 units.(c):  For the Welfare of CISF personnel the Government has a scheme to pay lumpsum compensation to the Next of Kin (NOK) of CISF personnel who die in harness in performance of the bonafide official duties.  In the current financial year Rs.3 crore has been released for this purpose.  The Government also has a scheme of Special Welfare Grant where funds are released, inter alia, for prevention of HIV/AIDS, stress related courses, professional/ vocational courses, etc.  Also, under the Prime Minister’s Scholarship Scheme Rs.3000/- per month is given for girls and Rs.2500/-  per month for boys who are dependent wards of eligible CISF personnel.  (d) & (e):    At present there is no such proposal under consideration.*****
