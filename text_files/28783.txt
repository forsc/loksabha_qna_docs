                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

396






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Smuggling of Drugs




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has noticed that the central agencies have seized large quantity of drug (Heroin) in the State of Gujarat including Mundra Port in Gujarat;(b) if so, the details thereof including total quantum and the value of drugs in international market;(c) whether the Government has conducted any detailed inquiry into the matter;(d) if so, the details thereof including the details of the sender and receiver of the drugs;(e) whether the privatization of ports and airports has caused increase in large scale smuggling of drugs; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d): Central Government Drug Law Enforcement Agencies have seized Heroin  in  the  State  of   Gujarat   including  the  port   of   Mundra.  Details are --2--LS.US.Q.NO. 396 FOR 30.11.2021at Annexure-I.  National Investigation Agency (NIA) has taken up investigation of a case registered vide RC-26/2021/NIA/DLI, dated 06.10.2021, related to seizure of 2988.21 Kgs of Narcotics Substance (Heroin) worth Rs. 21,000 Crore at T.G. Terminals Pvt. Ltd. Container Freight Station, Mundra Port, Gujarat, which was initially seized by the Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI), Gandhidham  Regional Unit, between 17.09.2021 and 19.09.2021. (e) & (f): There is no specific data or study to show any linkage between privatization of airports/seaports and increase in large scale smuggling of drugs. *****
