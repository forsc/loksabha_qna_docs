                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

324






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






DDU-GKY in Rajasthan




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the targets achieved under the Deen Dayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) in the State of Rajasthan for the skilling of rural youth as part of the National Rural Livelihood Mission, in terms of number of youths trained and number of youths placed in jobs, district-wise;(b) 	the initiatives introduced by non-governmental partner organizations under the DDUGKY for the skilling of rural youth and rural women;(c) 	the initiatives introduced to achieve the training and job placement targets to be met by March 2022; and(d) 	the details of resources and funding directed to the Rajasthan State Rural Livelihood Mission to achieve targets set for March 2022?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)      Rajasthan Skill & Livelihoods Development Corporation is the nodal agency for implementation of Deen Dayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) in the State of Rajasthan. The targets under Deen Dayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) is fixed at State Level.  Rajasthan has been approved a target of 72800 candidates to be trained and placed under DDU-GKY as per the Action Plan 2019-22. The district wise number of youths trained and placed in Rajasthan is given as Annexure.(b)      DDU-GKY Program is implemented, in PPP mode through Project Implementation Agencies (PIAs) registered with Ministry, as per its own Standard Operating Procedure.  Mobilisation of candidates, aptitude test, enrollment in training center, training, assessment & certification, placement & tracking of placed candidates go as per standard operating procedure. (c)      State of Rajasthan was sanctioned a total target of training 72,800 candidates for action plan 2019-2022 by Ministry. State has sanctioned complete target of 72,800 candidates to different PIAsfor implementation. Regular monitoring is being done for achieving the training and corresponding placement targets. Due to lock down following covid19 pandemic, trainings were suspended from 25 March 2020 and are expected to restart after 21 September 2020 following guidelines issued by Ministry of Health and Family welfare. States were advised to conduct skill gap analysis, deploying adequate human resource for program implementation, conducting job melas (onsite as well as virtual) for placement purpose and close monitoring of PIAs are conducted for milestone achievement. (d)      Rajasthan has been approved a target of 72,800 candidates to be trained and placed under DDU-GKY Action Plan 2019-22 to be implemented by RSLDC with a total budget outlay of Rs. 755.93 crores in the fund sharing ratio of 60:40. ******** ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO UNSTARRED QUESTION NO 324 FOR REPLY IN LOK SABHA ON 15.09.2020
