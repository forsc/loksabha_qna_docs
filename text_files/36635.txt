                                    DEFENCE

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3865






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Roads Passing through Cantonment Areas




Mangal Suresh Angadi














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has taken steps to improve/repair the roads passing through the Cantonment Limits of Belagavi city in Karnataka;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any funds have been earmarked to the Cantonment Board, Belagavi, Karnataka in this regard; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) & (b):  Yes, Sir.  The following works have been undertaken during the year 2020-21 to improve/repair the roads passing through Cantonment Areas: (i)  	Re-carpeting of Thimaya road & other main roads adjacent to Thimaya roads and West Street at an estimated amount of Rs. 39.43 lakh.(ii)  	Re-carpeting of Ramghat road by way of 40 mm SDBC at an estimated amount of Rs. 42.49 lakh. (c) & (d):  Grants-in-Aid & Service Charges are released to the Cantonment Board, which is utilized by them for incurring expenditure towards establishment, contingencies and public works.  Cantonment Board, Belgaum is also receiving funds based on the recommendations of Central Finance Commission and State Finance Commission.
