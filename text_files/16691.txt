                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

249






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Special Grant for Door Delivery Programme




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government plans to take a decision regarding special grant of Rs. 1,409 crores to implement door delivery program in Andhra Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government will adopt this as a national program in the light of COVID-19 pandemic for door to door delivery of essential food items to ration card holders in the light of the success of this pilot program of the Andhra Pradesh State Government; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):	No proposal regarding special grant of Rs.1409 crores to implement door delivery program in Andhra Pradesh is under consideration of the Government.(b)&(d):	Does not arise.****
