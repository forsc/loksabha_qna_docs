                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5167






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






New Railway Projects in Odisha and Maharashtra




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the preliminary survey on new railway projects including laying of new railway lines conducted by the Railways in the country including Odisha and Maharashtra during the last three years, line-wise;(b)	the details of the projects on which the further work has been started, line-wise;(c)	the details of the projects which have been shelved after the survey along with the reasons therefor;(d)	the total expenditure incurred on these surveys;(e)	whether the Government is aware that a large area is still deprived of rail connectivity in Odisha and Maharashtra and people and representatives are demanding for the rail connectivity in the respective areas;(f)	if so, the details thereof, project-wise; and(g)	the time by which the work on these projects are likely to be started? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (g): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
