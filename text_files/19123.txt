                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

528






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Drinking Water




Meenakashi Lekhi













                                                     Gopal Jee Thakur
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is contemplating initiation of new schemes with respect to water; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of progress of Jal Jivan Mission, State/UT wise; and (d) the details of number of households having clean drinking water facility?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) To enable every rural household in the country to have assured potable water at service level of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024, Government of India, in partnership with State Governments, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) with an estimated outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore. At the time of announcement of the JJM, only 3.23 Crore (17%) out of total 18.93 Crore rural households, had tap water connections.  (c) & (d) As reported by States/ UTs, since announcement, 3.32 Crore rural households have been provided with tap water connections across the country and thus presently 6.55 Crore rural households (34.20%) have tap water supply. State/ UT-wise details of total number of households and households with tap water connections is in public domain and available on the JJM dashboard @ https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx.****
