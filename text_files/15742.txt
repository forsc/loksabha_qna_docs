                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4363






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Works under AMRUT




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the works under AMRUT Yojana have been completed in the cities identified in the country there under and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (b)	if not, the details of the works under progress along with the time by which the said works are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)& (b).Under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) submitted by all States/Union Territories (UTs) for the entire Mission period (25 June 2015 to 31 March 2020) have been approved for ?77,640 crore including committed Central Assistance of ?35,990 crore.So far, contracts have been awarded for 5,384 projects costing ?73,641 crore under the Mission, of which 2,529 projects costing ?9,025 crore have been completed and 2,855 projects costing ?64,616 crore are under implementation. Further, 476 projects costing ?10,460 crore are at various stages of tendering. Some States/UTs have taken up projects in excess of their approved SAAP size. In such cases, the entire excess amount shall be borne by the State/UT.	The State/UT-wise details of progress of works under AMRUT is placed as Annexure.                                                 *****
