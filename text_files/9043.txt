                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Charges for Wi-fi Services




Behanan Benny













                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                








                                                    Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to introduce Wi-Fi services inside the trains and if so, the details thereof;(b) the time frame by when Wi-Fi service inside trains will be  started;(c) whether the passengers will be charged for the Wi-Fi services inside the train and if so, the details thereof;(d) the number of railway stations having free Wi-Fi services;(e) whether there is a delay in providing Wi-Fi services in the remaining railway stations; and(f) the routes and trains in which the said facility is planned to be provided?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): Ministry of Railways have been exploring suitable cost effective technology for providing Wi-Fi based internet facility in trains.As a pilot project, Wi-Fi based internet facility was provided in Howrah Rajdhani Express Trains through Satellite Communication technology. This technology was capital intensive, with recurring costs in the form of bandwidth charges and thus was not cost effective. Also, the internet bandwidth availability to passengers was inadequate. Hence the project was dropped.As and when suitable technology is available, necessary action for provision of facility shall be taken.(c): Decision on charging for Wi-Fi facility shall be taken as and when the facility is introduced in the trains.(d): Wi-Fi services have been provided at 5463 stations upto 26th November, 2019. (e): No, Sir.(f): This facility is planned to be provided in important passenger carrying trains on Indian Railways, as and when suitable technology is available.*****
