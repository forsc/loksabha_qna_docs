                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1999






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Operation of Train




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Lucknow – Hardoi TLC has been given approval;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether operation of this train has been started;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) if not, the reasons for delay in operation of the train; and(f) the time by which the operation of the said train is likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY   (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	No, Sir. No such approval has been given. However, at present 32 pairs Mail/Express and 3 pairs of passenger trains are catering to the needs of passenger of the said section.(b) to (f)	Do not arise.*****
