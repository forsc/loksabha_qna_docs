                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5095






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






ESIC Hospital




L.S. Tejasvi Surya













                                                    Ashok Mahadeorao Nete
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the total funds available with ESIC, year-wise since 2014;(b)the total contribution by Government-controlled bodies (autonomous or controlled or independent) since 2014 and the percentage share thereof along with the total number of tie-ups before 2014 made by ESIC with private and Government hospitals and the number of tie ups made, State/year-wise since 2014;(c)whether the ESIC has provided an extension in the coverage of medical facilities or surgeries to its subscribers and if so, the details thereof before 2014 and after 2014; and (d) whether ESIC has huge amount of unclaimed funds; and if so, whether the Government is taking any concrete steps to use unclaimed funds?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	Year wise details of fund available with Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) since 2014 to 2021 is at Annexure-I. (b):	Total contribution received by the ESIC during the financial years 2014-15 to 2020-21 and percentage of contribution income vis-a-vis total revenue income is at Annexure-II. State-wise number of ESIC tie-up hospitals since 2014 is at Annexure-III.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::No separate data is maintained in respect of contribution made by Government-controlled bodies.(c):	Before 2014, ESIC provided coverage of medical facilities to ESI beneficiaries through a network of 151 ESI hospitals, 1418 ESI dispensaries, 1017 Insured Medical Practitioners (IMPs). Super-speciality services were provided mainly through tie-up private and government hospitals. At present, medical facilities are extended through a network of 160 ESI hospitals including 08 Medical colleges, 02 Dental colleges, 1517 ESI dispensaries, 76 Dispensaries-cum-Branch Offices (DCBOs), 1003 Insured Medical Practitioners (IMPs) and 1451 ESIC empanelled hospitals. Further, ESIC has collaborated with Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) in 157 districts of the country for providing cashless medical services to its beneficiaries. Super-speciality services such as interventional cardiology, medical and surgical oncology, nephrology with dialysis, paediatric surgery, plastic surgery, neurology, neurosurgery, urology, spine surgeries, hip and knee replacement surgeries and transplant surgeries are also being provided in some of the ESIC hospitals. (d):	No unclaimed funds are available with the ESIC.***                                             ******
