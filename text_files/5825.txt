                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5355






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Encouragement to Sports Talent in Rural Areas




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the schemes being run in the country particularly in Bihar to encourage sports talents in the rural areas and to enable them to participate in National /International competitions; and(b) the details of the sports talents from the rural areas particularly from Bihar who participated in national/ international competitions, State/area-wise and sport wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) ‘Sports’ being a State subject promotion of sports, including encouraging, identifying and nurturing sporting talent in Bihar, is primarily the responsibility of the State Government. Central Government supplements its efforts by providing assistance to National Sports Federations (NSFs), expert training in Sports Authority of India (SAI) centres and support under Khelo India scheme. Information relating to SAI training centres in Bihar and athletes being benefitted presently in these Centres is at Annexure.(b) Information of athletes participating in national/international competitions is not maintained region / area wise.******
