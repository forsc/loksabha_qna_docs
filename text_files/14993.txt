                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3629






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Use of CSR Funds




Lorho S. Pfoze














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds spent by the petroleum PSUs under their CSR funds during the last three financial years in the country compare to North East including Manipur, district-wise and PSU-wise; and(b) the details of activities on which the petroleum PSUs spend their CSR funds in Manipur, project-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) The CSR expenditure made by following Public Sector Oil Companies in the country and in North-East region including Manipur during the last three financial years (2016-17 to 2018-19) are given below :-(? in Crore)Oil PSUsEntire CountryNorth East including ManipurONGC1643.98294.84BPCL434.9421.73HPCL424.794.53IOCL1035.6498.16GAIL303.109.58EIL35.781.86OIL342.34232.74NRL118.56118.11The project/activities wise details of the funds spent under CSR in North-East India including Manipur by aforesaid Public Sector Oil Companies during the last three years are available on their respective websites.****
