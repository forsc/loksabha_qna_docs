                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Protection of Children against Sexual Offences Act, 2012




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    3241. SHRI RITESH PANDEY:Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state that the number of District Child Protection Units constituted along with the details of funds allocated to them for the rehabilitation and alternative care of children along with other services?
