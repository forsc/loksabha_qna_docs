                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4208






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Sports Schemes




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of various schemes launched by the Ministry along with the funds released under such schemes in various districts of Assam;  (b) the number of training centres set up in Assam and North Eastern States along with the number of students imparted training under the said schemes; (c) the State-wise details of number of skill related employment fairs, skill camps, training competitions organised to make skill training popular among youths during the last three years;  (d) whether any initiative has been taken by the Government for the development of sports under Skill India campaign; and (e) the number of students trained and employed under Skill India campaign along with their place of appointment, State-wise? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI KIREN RIJIJU]  (a):   The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports has sanctioned seven projects under Khelo India Scheme for the state of Assam from FY 2017-18 and a total of Rs. 39.32 crore has been released.  (b):   Under the Khelo India Scheme, one National Centre of Excellence has been established at Guwahati and 204 athletes from North Eastern States have been selected for training under its Long Term Athlete Development programme. (c):   The state-wise details of number of skill related employment fairs, skill camps, training competitions organized by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship to make skill training popular among youths are given at Annexure-I. (d):    Under Skill India Mission, the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana with an objective to train one crore youth of the country in various sectors including sports sector. (e):    The number of students trained and employed, state-wise, under Skill India campaign of the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship are given at Annexure-II. ****
