                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1261






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Jai Prakash Narain Apex Trauma Centre in AIIMS




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether renovation work of Jai Prakash Narain Apex Trauma Centre in AIIMS which caught fire in the month of March has not been undertaken yet due to which patients who require to be operated upon second time are not being admitted, if so, the details thereof;(b) the number of operation theatres rendered non-functional after the said fire along with the present status thereof;(c) whether there is undue delay in the work of renovation of the said operation theatres; and(d) if so, the arrangements made by the Government to ensure that the patients are not inconvenienced?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	A major fire incidence occurred in Jai Prakash Narain (JPN) Apex Trauma Centre in AIIMS, New Delhi on 24.03.2019 due to which 5 Operation Theatres (OTs) had to be closed for repair works. However, restoration of operation Theatres (OTs) were started immediately and at present, all Operation Theatres (OTs) in JPN Apex Trauma Centre in AIIMS, New Delhi are fully functional.
