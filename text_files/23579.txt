                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4804






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Copper Production




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether India has become net importer of copper for the last three years;(b)	if so, the reasons for reduction in the production of copper along with the total production of copper during the last five years; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government to re-start the closed copper factories at the earliest?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a):  The import of refined copper has increased from 44245 tonnes in 2017-18 to 92290 tonnes and 151964 tonnes in 2018-19 and 2019-20 respectively, whereas export declined from 378555 tonnes in 2017-18 to 47917 tonnes and 36959 tonnes in 2018-19 and 2019-20 respectively. Consequently, there was net import of 44373 tonnes and 115005 tonnes in 2018-19 and 2019-20 respectively, whereas there was a net export of 334310 tonnes in 2017-18.(b):  Tuticorin smelter in Tamil Nadu, which has annual capacity of 4 lakh tonnes of refined copper, is closed since May 2018.   Closure of the plant has affected the domestic production of refined copper. The details of last five years (2015-16 to 2019-20) refined copper production in the Country are as under:                 (Quantity in Million Tonnes)Year	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	Qty	Qty	Qty	Qty	QtyProduction 	0.79	0.79	0.83	0.45	0.41 (c): During the above period domestic production declined largely due to closure of a smelter since May, 2018, which has annual capacity of 4 lakh tonnes per annum. The matter relating to re-start production from Smelter plant of M/s Sterlite Copper (a Unit of Vedanta Ltd) is sub judice.*****
