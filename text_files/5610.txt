                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5192






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Head Post Offices and Sub-Post Offices




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of head post-offices and sub-post offices in the country, State/ UT-wise including Jharkhand; (b) 	the number of the permanent and temporary staff working in these post offices, district-wise; (c) 	whether the importance of post offices has decreased due to availability of various means of communications and if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; and (d) 	the details of the steps being taken or proposed by the Government to enhance the capacity and to maintain the utility of these post offices?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) & (b)	Sir, there are a total number of 811 Head Post Offices and 24677 Sub Post Offices in the country.  The State/UT-wise details including the State of Jharkhand and the district-wise details of number of permanent and temporary staff working in these post offices, are at Annexure I.(c) & (d)	No, Sir.  The Government has taken various steps such as setting up of Aadhaar Enrolment and Updation facilities in 13,352 Post Offices; Passport Seva Kendras in 412 Post Offices; Core Banking facilities in 23,667 Post Offices; Automatic Teller Machines at 996 Post Offices, and 650 branches of India Post Payment Banks (IPPB) including 1.36 lakhs Access Points for IPPB, to enhance the capacity and maintain utility of these Post Offices.  In addition, 1,29,161 Branch Post Offices have been provided with Subscriber Identity Module based hand-held devices for providing postal and online financial transactions, thereby improving the capacity and utility of these post offices.
