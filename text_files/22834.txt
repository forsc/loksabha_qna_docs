                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3851






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Vehicle Scrap Industry




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to introduce fitness and emission regime by scrapping 15 year old Government vehicles;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has any plan to set up scrap industry in the country like other developed countries; and(d)     if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) and (b) Ministry of Road Transport & Highways has formulated a draft notification under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 in this regard. Certificate of Registration for such vehicles shall not be renewed after lapse of fifteen years. (c) and (d) Ministry of Road Transport & Highways has issued draft guidelines for establishment of Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facility (RVSF).*****
