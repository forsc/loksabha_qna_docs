                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Funds to PMAY-G




Pramila Bisoyi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the Budget allocation, funds released by the Government as its share to the States and utilization reported under Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojana-Grameen (PMAY-G) during 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, State-wise; and(b) 	the amount released as on 31st August, 2020, State-wise, along with the physical targets set for each State for the year 2020-21?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a):   The State-wise budget allocation, funds released by the Government as its share to the States and utilisation reported under Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojana- Grameen (PMAY-G) during 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 is given at Annexure-I. (b):    The amount released as on 31st August 2020, State-wise, along with the physical targets set for each State for the year 2020-21 is given at Annexure-II.******** 		Annexure-I Annexure referred to in reply to part (a) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1458 to be answered on 20.09.2020Details of Financial Progress under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin during the last  four years
