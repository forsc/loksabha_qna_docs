                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Charging Stations for EVs




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat













                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up charging stations for electric vehicles in the country under the Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of Hybrid and Electric Vehicles in India (FAME-India) Scheme, Phase-II;(b) whether the Government has started setting up of charging stations for electric vehicles in the country;(c) if so, the funds allocated for this purpose and the expenditure incurred so far;(d) whether electric charging stations are being set up in cities in Maharashtra and other States; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (e):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.                                  ********                                                                    STATEMENTStatement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (e) of Lok-Sabha Starred Question No. 333 for 17.03.2020 asked by Shri Girish Bhalchandra Bapat and Adv. Adoor Prakash regarding “Charging Stations for EVs”(a) to (e): Yes, Sir. The FAME-India Scheme Phase-II envisages support for setting up of adequate public charging infrastructure to instill confidence among EV users, through active participation and involvement of various stakeholders including Government agencies, industries and Public Sector Enterprises (PSEs). A budget provision of Rs. 1000 Crore for a period of 3 years [2019-20 to 2021-22] has been earmarked for establishment of charging infrastructure under this Phase of FAME Scheme. Department of Heavy Industry had invited the Expression of Interest (EoI) from million-plus cities, smart cities, State/UT capitals and cities from special category states for submission of proposal for availing incentives under FAME India Scheme Phase II for deployment of EV charging infrastructure within Cities. In response to the proposals received under this EoI, Department of Heavy Industry has sanctioned 2,636 Electric Vehicles (EVs) Charging Stations amounting to Rs 500 Crore (Approx.) in 62 cities across 24 States/UTs including Maharashtra under FAME India (Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of Hybrid & Electric Vehicles in India) scheme phase II as per details annexed at ANNEXURE-I.                                              *****                                                                         Annexure-IList of cities and numbers of Electric Vehicle Charging Stations considered for SanctionSr. No.	          Name of State	   Name of City	State Total1	Andhra Pradesh 	Vijaywada	2662		Vishakhapatnam	3		Tirupati	4		Kakinada	5	Assam	Jorhat	206		Guwahati	7	Bihar	Patna	378	Chandigarh	Chandigarh	709	Chattishgarh	Raipur	2510	Delhi	Delhi	7211	Gujarat	Ahmedabad	22812		Vadodara	13		Gandhi Nagar	14	Haryana 	Gurugram	5015		Karnal	16		Sonipat	17	Himanchal Pradesh	Dharamshala	1018	Karnataka	Bengaluru	17219		Gulbarga	20		Mangalore	21	Kerala	Kochi	13122		Thrissur	23		Kannur	24		Kozhikode	25	Maharashtra	Mumbai	31726		Nashik	27		Nagpur	28		Thane	29	Madhya Pradesh	Bhopal	15930		Indore	31		Jabalpur	32	Meghalaya	Shillong	4033	Odisha	Bhubaneshwar	1834	Puducherry	Pondicherry	1035	Rajasthan	Jaipur	20536		Jodhpur	37		Kota	38		Udaipur	39		Ajmer	40	Sikkim	Gangtok	2941	Srinagar	Srinagar	2542	Tamil Nadu	Chennai	25643		Coimbatore	44		Madurai	45		Vellore	46		Salem	47		Thanjavur	48		Erode	Contd…..2/--2-49	Telangana	Hyderabad	13850		Warangal	51		Karimnagar	52	Uttar Pradesh	Lucknow	20753		Varanasi	54		Allahabad	55		Kanpur	56		Noida	57		Aligarh	58		Saharanpur	59		Bareilly	60		Jhansi	61	Uttarakhand	Dehradun	1062	West Bengal	Kolkata 	141Total	2636                                  *******
