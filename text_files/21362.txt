                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2580






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Commemorative Stamp




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received any proposal for the release of commemorative postage stamp in the name of Mooloor S Padmanabha Panicker; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the decision taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) 	Yes, Sir.(b) 	The proposals for issue of Commemorative Postage Stamp are considered by the Philatelic Advisory Committee on the basis of extant guidelines. It has been decided that the proposal for issue of Commemorative Postage Stamp on Shri Mooloor S Padmanabha Panicker will be placed before the next meeting of Philatelic Advisory Committee for consideration.************
