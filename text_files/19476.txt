                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

712






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Silk production in North Eastern States




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the huge potential in silk production and weaving in North Eastern States including Assam; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the action plan formulated by the Government to promote, augment and preserve silk production and weaving; and(d) the fund earmarked by the Government for these schemes?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)   (a) & (b):	Yes. The North Eastern Region (NER) of India has potential to produce all four varieties of silk viz. Mulberry, Tasar, Eri & Muga. Muga silk (also known as Golden Silk) is produced exclusively in Assam & border areas of Assam and the Eri silk is also produced mainly in NER states. Muga Silk of Assam has already been registered as Geographical Indication (GI) under the Rules of Intellectual Property Rights of Govt. of India. The silk weaving sector of North Eastern States have a long tradition of Handloom weaving, Handicraft and Sericulture. However, most of the looms produce for domestic consumption.  The variety-wise raw silk production during last 5 years and current year 2020-21 (till Nov-20) in NER state is given at Annexure-I.(c) & (d):	Ministry of Textiles, Government of India through Central Silk Board has implemented various schemes/projects to promote, augment and preserve silk production & weaving in the country including North Eastern States during last five years and current year through State Sericulture Departments. From these schemes/projects, assistance is extended to sericulture stakeholders for infrastructure development through the beneficiary oriented components like, raising of Kissan Nursery, Plantation with improved Mulberry varieties, Irrigation, chawki rearing centres with incubation facility, construction of rearing houses, rearing equipments, support for improved reeling units like Automatic Reeling units, Multiend Reeling machines, Improved Twisting machines and support for post yarn facilities for quality Silk and fabric production. The details of the schemes are placed at Annexure-II.The funds earmarked by the Ministry of Textiles, Govt. of India through Central Silk Board for these schemes during last 5 years & current year 2020-21 (Till Dec-2020) is given below:-(Rs. In Crores)Scheme	Financial Assistance provided under the schemes for NER StatesIntegrated Scheme for Development of Silk Industry (ISDSI)(2015-16 & 2016-17)	1.773Silk Samagra(2017-21)	17.243North East Region Textile Promotion    Scheme (NERTPS)(2014-15 to 2020-21)	745.69
