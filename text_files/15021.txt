                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

325






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of farmers benefited under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana, State-wise including the State of Odisha and Kalahandi Lok Sabha constituency; and (b) 	the amount of funds earmarked to Odisha for the said scheme and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) & (b):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 325  DUE FOR REPLY ON 17TH  MARCH, 2020.(a): 	As on 11.3.2020, the financial benefit under the PM-KISAN Scheme has been released to 8,69,79,391 beneficiaries in the country. The State-wise number of beneficiaries is enclosed at Annexure. These include 36,28,657 beneficiaries from Odisha and 1,35,737 beneficiaries in the District of Kalahandi in the State.(b):	 Under PM-KISAN Scheme, funds are not released State-wise but transferred directly into the bank accounts of the beneficiaries identified by the State Governments.
