                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Loans and Credits from Foreign  Countries




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of loans, credits and grants from foreign countries, special agencies, non-governmental foundation agencies and voluntary bodies, and from multilateral agencies during the last three years, year-wise;(b)	the details of technical and economic assistance received by the country under UN programmes and ad-hoc offers from foreign countries and entities during the said period, year-wise; and(c)	the details of credit extended by India to other countries during the said period, country-wise and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	and (b) : India accepts external assistance from bilateral partners and multilateral agencies in the form of loans, grants and technical assistance for development of infrastructure and social sector at both Centre and States level. The details of authorisation of loan and grant from bilateral partners and multilateral agencies for the last three years is at Annexure-I. The details of Technical Assistance and grant projects taken up during the last three years under UN Programme is at Annexure-II.(c) The details of credit extended by India to other countries during the last three years, country-wise and year-wise is at Annexure-III. *****
