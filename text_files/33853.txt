                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1337






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Digital Village Scheme




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government had initiated the Digital Village Scheme in the year 2018;(b)	if so, the details and the salient features of the scheme along with the state-wise details of the villages benefited under the scheme;(c)	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized by the State of Maharashtra under the above said scheme till date, district-wise;(d)	the details of number of villages and districts covered in the State of Maharashtra especially in Ramtek Nagpur district under the aforesaid scheme till now;(e)	the details of the target set and achievement made under the above said scheme; and(f)	whether the Government is formulating any scheme to add more villages under the scheme and if so, the time by which these villages are likely to be added?






ANSWER




                                                        (a), (b) and (c): Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India (GoI) has initiated Digital Village Project on pilot scale on 31st October, 2018 under which 700 villages (one village from each district) of all State/UT across the country, are covered.  The list of the 700 villages is enclosed at weblink(refer https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ODney0gMJHrhaO57izoOIHymwXxY9_et/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=114104362247024536597&rtpof=true&sd=true). The salient features of the Digital Village Pilot project are:•	700 Gram Panchayat/villages with atleast one village from each district of the State/UT are covered under the pilot implementation of the project. •	Digital Seva Portal (DSP) of CSCs in villages across the country is being leveraged for the delivery of services to the rural citizens. The deliverables of the project are: •	Education Service: Courses offered under this project are as - Basic Computer Course (BCC), Course on Computer Concept (CCC), Tally – KaushalPramanPatra•	Health Services – Tele-Health & Tele-Veterinary consultation •	Financial Inclusion- Financial inclusion awareness programme  •	Solar panel powered street lights (8 Solar lights per village)•	Skill Development - skill development in - Automotive Technician, Handset Repairing, Field Technician- Home Appliances, and Electrical Technician.•	Government to Citizen Services (G2C) - online registration for professional entrance examinations, Pan Card Services, new Aadhaar enrolment, its updation/modification, online registration for service selection board, online registration for public service commission, etc.•	B2C Services – Mobile / DTH recharge, ticket booking (Rail/Air), etc.Under the Digital India Central Sector Scheme of MeitY, a total outlay of Rs 98.32 Crore is allocated for implementation of Digital Village Pilot project. State-wise/UT-wise specific allocation is not made under the pilot project.    (d): 34 villages (one village in each district) in Maharashtra are covered under the Digital Village Pilot Project. The List of villages in Maharashtra State, covered under the project is enclosed at Annexure-I.  Ramtek Nagar is a Municipal Council in Nagpur District. Village- Digdoh, Block- Hingna is covered under the Digital Village Pilot project in the Nagpur district of Maharashtra.(e): The details of targets set and achievement under the Digital Village Pilot project in the 700 villages, covered under the project, are enclosed at Annexure-II.(f):  The pilot phase of the Digital Village project is currently under implementation in which 700 villages/Gram Panchayat with at least one village from each district of the State/UT are covered. Further, 4596 villages are being made digital by CSC e-Governance Service India Limited (CSC-SPV) under its own initiative through funds sourced from Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) that includes 271 villages in Maharashtra.
