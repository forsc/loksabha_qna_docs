                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

171






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Agricultural Schemes




Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the list of the schemes being implemented by the Ministry in Maharashtra; (b) 	the financial and physical progress reported in the State in each of these schemes during the past three years, year-wise; (c) 	whether any proposal from the State Government of Maharashtra is pending with the Ministry; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the response thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.171  DUE FOR REPLY ON 3RD MARCH, 2020.(a):	 The list of schemes which are being implemented in the State of Maharashtra are at Annexure –I.(b):	The physical progress (as reported by the State Government of Maharashtra) and financial progress of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and beneficiary oriented Central Sector Schemes during the past three years is at Annexure-II. (c) & (d):	 A proposal to open a KVK in Thane has been received.
