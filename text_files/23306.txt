                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4199






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Opening of ESIC Dispensaries in Nagaur, Rajasthan




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has auctioned the opening of ESIC dispensary and branch office in Nagaur (Rajasthan); and(b)if so, the time by which the said dispensary and branch office are likely to be operational along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	One Dispensary cum Branch Office (DCBO) has been approved by the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) at Nagaur, Rajasthan.  No such DCBO has been auctioned.(b):	The revised policy for operationalization of approved DCBOs has been circulated by the ESIC on 26.02.2021.  All the Regions of the ESIC have been requested to review the districts/locations of already approved DCBOs and submit fresh proposals for establishing/operationalization of the DCBO in the district,  if the district satisfies the laid down criteria.*******
