                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1508






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Nuclear Power Production




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the nuclear power production has increased during the last five years; and(b)	if so, the details in this regard during the last five years, plant-wise and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR.JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)	Yes, Sir. (b)	The details of generation from nuclear power plants in the last five years are given in Annexure.*****
