                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Employment of Local Youth in Industries




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is contemplating to make it mandatory for industries to employ the local population trained at Skill Development Centres; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing a flagship scheme known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20 with an objective to provide skilling to one crore people under Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) through empanelled accredited and affiliated training providers/training centres across the country for four years i.e. 2016-2020. PMKVY does not mandate the industries to employ people trained at Skill Development Centres.  However, the scheme encourages the industries to give employment to trained candidates. *******
