                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

878






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Role of Solar Energy




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether solar energy will play a big role in Atmanirbhar Bharat in future; and  (b) if so, the details thereof including the progress made thereunder, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)  Low cost power is an essential requirement for competitive manufacturing. Since solar tariffs have been declining, use of solar energy could contribute to manufacturing competitiveness, and thus to achieving the goals of Atmanirbhar Bharat.The details of State-wise installed capacity of renewable power, including solar power, as on 31.08.2020 are furnished at Annexure-I.*****
