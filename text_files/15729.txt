                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4245






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Funds for Capacity Buildingof ULBs




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds spent on capacity building of Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) within the Smart City Proposal (SCP), city-wise; (b) 	the details of the local taxes that have been subsumed under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) from 2017; (c)     whether the Government has any plans to assess the revenue losses of the ULBs and compensate them for the same and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the number of States where the allocation to the urban local bodies is done through the State Finance Commissions?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	The implementation of the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) is being done by a city level Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) created for the purpose. These SPVs are Board-run entities and are Limited Companies registered under the Companies Act, 2013. The SPVs plan, appraise, approve, implement, manage, operate, monitor and evaluate their Smart City projects. The details of funds spent by the Smart Cities on capacity building of Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) are not maintained by SCM. However, under Integrated Capacity Building Programme of the Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs (MoHUA), the amount spent on capacity building of functionaries of SCM since 1st July, 2018, in various States/Union Territories (UTs), is given below :-State/UT	Amount (in Rs.)Puducherry	2,83,525Madhya Pradesh	3,92,590Madhya Pradesh	2,14,665Maharashtra	9,97,865Karnataka	6,78,459Karnataka	14,39,990Total	40,07,094(b)	to (d) No such information is maintained by the Ministry.                                               *****
