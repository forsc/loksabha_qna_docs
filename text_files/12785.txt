                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1745






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Training Targets




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has not utilised its entire budget/funds for the last three years and has achieved just 49 per cent of its training target;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of the budget earmarked, allocated and utilised by the Government for the last three years; and(d)	the reasons for not utilising the entire budget for the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing its flagship demand driven scheme, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20 for short duration skill development training (Short-Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)) with an objective to train one crore youth for four years (i.e. 2016-20) in the country.Under PMKVY (2016-20), during last three financial years funds amounting to Rs. 5439 Cr. have been allocated (Revised Estimate); out of which, as on 31.01.2020, Rs. 5203 Cr. have been utilized. 	Further, under PMKVY (2016-20), as on 17.01.2020, 40.27 lakh candidates have been trained under short term training (STT) and 33.20 lakh candidates have been oriented under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) across the country. (c) and (d)	The FY-wise details of funds earmarked and utilized for the implementation of PMKVY 2016-20, as on 31.01.2020, is given below:[Amounts in Cr.]FY	Budget Estimate	Revised Estimate	Actual Expenditure2017-18	1,300.00	1,723.19	1,721.192018-19	1,984.34	1,946.45	1,909.192019-20	2,116.00	1,749.22	1,573.12Total	5,400.34	5,439.00	5,203.50
