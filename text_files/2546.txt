                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2401






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Kendriya Vidyalaya in Bihar




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any proposal in the past to open a newKendriyaVidyalaya in Sitamarhi district of Bihar;(b) if so, the action taken/being taken on the said proposal till date;(c) if not, whether the Government contemplates to open a new KendriyaVidyalayain the said district in order to fulfill the long standing demand according to theneed;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Yes, Sir. A proposal for opening of a new Kendriya Vidyalaya in the district ofSitamarhi (Bihar) was received from the Commandant, SSB Paktola, Sitamarhi inMarch, 2018.(b) 8 Acres of land proposed by the Sponsoring Authority in the Campus of SSBPaktola, Sitamarhi for opening of the new KV was inspected by Kendriya VidyalayaSangathan (KVS) and found suitable. However, in the absence of a suitabletemporary accommodation being provided by the sponsoring authority, as per thenorms of KVS, the proposal could not be processed further.(c) to (e) Do not arise.*****
