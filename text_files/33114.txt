                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

544






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Storage of Fertilizers




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government proposes to use private warehouses to ensure   adequate storage availability of fertilizers in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Rajasthan;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) 	the funds earmarked and the nodal agency, if any, appointed for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS &  FERTILIZERS(DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) to (c):  No, Sir. Government has no proposal to engage private warehouses including Rajasthan for storage of fertilizers in the country. As per existing arrangements, fertilizers are distributed by State Governments through Markfed and dealers/ retailers network. These are stored at various points of supply chain according to the marketing strategy of respective companies in consultation with the State Governments.(d):	In view of above, question does not arise                             XXXXXXX
