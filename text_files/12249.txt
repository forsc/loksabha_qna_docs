                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1153






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Funds under Centrally Sponsored Schemes




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has allocated and released funds to all States under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) during the last three years and the current year;(b)	If so, the details thereof, scheme/State and year-wise;(c)	whether the State Governments have submitted proposals seeking additional grants and assistance under various schemes;(d)	if so, whether the Union Government has released any grants and assistance additionally to the State Governments; and(e)	if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                                                                        ANSWER                                    MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE                                       (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)&(b): The Central Government/ Ministry of Finance allocate funds through budgetary process to different Ministries/ Departments for each of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme. The respective Ministry / Department in turn allocates the Central Government share for Centrally Sponsored Scheme as per Scheme guideline to the States/UTs through the Consolidated Funds of the States. Concerned Ministry/ Department periodically monitors individual schemes. The release of funds/ installment by Central Government Ministry/ Department is based on the furnished Utilization Certificate of the penultimate (last to last) installment. The trend in outlays in the Expenditure Budget of GoI for CSSs for the last three years and the current year are as follows:                                                                                                                                                                          (Rs. in Crore)Scheme  Category	Actual  2017-18	Actual 2018-19	RE2019-20	BE  2020-21CSS	2,85,447.77	2,96,028.85	3,16,815.56	3,39,894.53The scheme-wise details of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes are available at Statement 4A of Expenditure Profile 2020-21 of the Expenditure Budget of Government of India.(c): No proposals have been received from the State Government seeking additional grants and assistance under various schemes.(d) & (e): Funds have been released to State Governments under Special Assistance for meeting (i) spillover committed liabilities for which Budget provision is not made after the implementation of the 14th Finance Commission recommendations, (ii) and other need based assistance. Funds released under Special Assistance in the last 3 years are provided at Annex.                                              ****
