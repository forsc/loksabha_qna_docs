                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

409






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






DDU-GKY in Rajasthan




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of women skilled under Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDUGKY) in Rajasthan, district-wise;(b) 	whether the Government proposes to undertake measures to ensure inclusive and equitable skilling across genders; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	The District-wise number of women skilled under the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) in Rajasthan is given at Annexure.(b) & (c): Yes, an inclusive approach towards skilling across genders has been adhered to. DDU-GKY guidelines provide that minimum one third of the persons covered under DDU-GKY should be women. This is being adhered to and presently out of total 10,89,445 candidates trained under DDU-GKY, 5,10,695 (46.87%) are women.******
