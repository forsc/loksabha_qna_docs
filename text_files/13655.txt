                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Potable Water Schemes in MP




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of schemes implemented by the Union Government in Madhya Pradesh for potable water and hygiene, district-wise; (b) whether the Union Government is aware about any type of irregularities in implementation of these schemes; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) Government of India, in partnership with the States, has launched Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) to enable every rural household in the country, including Madhya Pradesh, to have potable water at service level of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024, with an estimated cost of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore.The information about district-wise details of schemes for all States/ UTs  including Madhya Pradesh (as reported by the respective State/ UT) are in public domain and available on the website: https://ejalshakti.gov.in? IMIS Reports?Schemes handed over to Community.For rural sanitation across the country, including Madhya Pradesh, Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM(G)] is also being implemented.Under SBM(G),67,80,850 Individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed in Madhya Pradesh from 02.10.2014 to 02.03.2020 and all the 50,228 villages have declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF). District-wise details are annexed.(b) & (c)Water & Sanitation being State subject, State Govt. plan, design, implement, operate & maintain the rural water supply & sanitation schemes.Further, whenever any grievance is received in this regard, the same is forwarded to the respective State Government for appropriate corrective measures.****
