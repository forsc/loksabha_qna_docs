                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

577






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Submarine Cable Connectivity




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched sub-marine cable connectivity to Andaman & Nicobar Islands recently; (b) 	if so, the details and the features thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has proposed to launch such facility to Lakshadweep also in the near future; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) 	Yes, Sir. (b) 	The sub-marine cable connectivity project to Andaman & Nicobar Islands was inaugurated and dedicated to the nation by the Hon’ble Prime Minister on 10th August 2020. The project provides bandwidth of 2 x 200 Gbps between Chennai and Port Blair and 2 x 100 Gbps between Port Blair and other seven islands namely Swaraj Dweep, Long Island, Rangat, Little Andaman, Kamorta, Great Nicobar and Car Nicobar. It will facilitate delivery of e-Governance services, telemedicine, tele-education, e-commerce, e-learning and knowledge sharing, etc. (c) 	Yes, Sir.(d) 	Digital Communications Commission (DCC) on 20th December 2019 has approved the sub-marine cable connectivity project from Kochi to Lakshadweep’s eleven Islands namely Kavarati, Kalpeni, Agatti, Amini, Androth, Minicoy, Bangaram, Bitra, Chetlat, Kiltan and Kadmath. The project envisages providing bandwidth of 2 x 100 Gbps between Kochi and eleven Islands of Lakshadweep.(e) 	Not applicable in view of (d) above.************
