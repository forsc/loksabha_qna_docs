                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4685






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Setting up of Petrol Pumps




Pratima Bhoumik













                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of petrol pumps proposed to be opened/set up during the current year along with the locations identified in various States/districts and the minimum educational qualification fixed in this regard, State/location-wise;(b) whether the Government has conducted any survey to open/set up new petrol pumps and LPG distributorships in Tripura and Jharkhand and if so, the details thereof;(c) the names of places/sites where new petrol pumps and LPG dealerships have been opened/proposed to be opened after survey in Tripura and Jharkhand, location-wise; and(d) the procedure laid down for allotment of petrol pumps and LPG agencies along with any reservation made for minorities in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have informed that expansion of Retail Outlet network is a continuous process to ensure adequate availability of motoring fuels like petrol and diesel throughout the country. Public Sector OMCs had advertised 78,688 locations for award of new Retail Outlet dealerships on 25.11.2018 and 14.12.2018 based on feasibility study on pan India basis...2..-2-The minimum Educational Qualification is 10th Class Pass (Examination conducted by a Board/School). Certificate issued by Armed Forces as equivalent to 10th Class pass in accordance with Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pension notification dated 12.2.1986 is also considered. Minimum Educational Qualification is not applicable to Freedom Fighters.As regards LPG, appointment of LPG distributorships is a continuous process and locations for setting up of LPG distributorships are identified based on sale potential that makes them commercially viable.(c) State/District wise number of locations advertised and dealership/distributorship commissioned by Public Sector OMCs for retail outlets and LPG distributorships in Tripura and Jharkhand is given in Annexure-1 & Annexure-2 respectively.(d) Procedure / Norms / Criteria / Reservation Policy for allotment of Retail Outlet Dealerships/LPG distributorships in the country are available on the respective websites of the OMCs i.e. www.iocl.com, www.bharatpetroleum.com & www.hindustanpetroleum.com.The detailed guidelines namely “Unified Guidelines for Selection of LPG Distributorships” are available on the website http://www.petroleum.nic.in.****
