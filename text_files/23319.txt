                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4387






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Functioning of PM-KISAN Scheme in Haryana and Madhya Pradesh




Sunita Duggal













                                                    Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the district-wise number of farmers benefitted in Haryana and Madhya Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana (PM-KISAN) and the amount transferred to them;(b)	the total number of farmers in Haryana and Madhya Pradesh who have not received the benefits of the said scheme so far along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The district-wise number of farmers benefitted in Haryana and Madhya Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana (PM-KISAN) and the amount transferred to them is given at Annexure-I.(b):	As PM-KISAN is a continuous and ongoing Scheme therefore, as and when the verified and corrected data of eligible beneficiaries are received from States/UTs, the benefit of the Scheme is transferred directly into the bank account of beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer.
