                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2839






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Anti-poaching Camps




P. Velusamy













                                                    K. Shanmuga Sundaram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of anti-poaching camps (APC) available in various wildlife protection sanctuaries along with the number of foot soldiers employed in various camps in the country; (b)	whether such camps are having proper infrastructure and weapons for the safety of its employees; (c)	if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether the employees in anti poaching camps are permanent employees or are employed on contract basis; and(e)	if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken by the Government to absorb contractual employees in permanent cadre like other Government employees?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) As per the information received from the States/UTs, the details of total number of Anti-Poaching Camps (APC) along with the number of foot soldiers employed in the camps is at Annexure.(b) and (c) As per information received from the States/UTs, the infrastructure available in these camps varies from temporary to permanent structures. In some States/Union Territories, anti-poaching camps are also provided with weapons.(d) and (e) As per the information received from the States/UTs, both permanent and contractual employees are engaged by the State/UTs in anti-poaching camps.Rules/policies regarding appointment to permanent cadre vary from State to State.***
