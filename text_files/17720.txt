                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1382






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Automobile Sales




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of automobile sales in the country during the last three years and the current year, month-wise;(b) the value of automobile sales in the country, month-wise since 2017 to the present day;(c) the number of automobile manufacturing units that have closed down during the said period, State-wise including Assam;(d) the number of units in the automobile supply chain (including ancillary units) that have closed down since 2017 to the present day, State-wise including Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):   As per the information received from Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM), month-wise domestic sales of all categories of vehicles for last three years and current year is as below:       Quantum of Sale of Vehicles of all categories (number in lakhs) Month	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21April	  20.3	         23.8	         20.0	         14.9May	  20.4	         22.8	         20.9	June	  18.2	         22.8	         20.0	July	  20.8	         22.5	         18.3	Aug	  23.0	         23.8	         18.2	Sep	  24.9	         25.8	         20.0	Oct	  21.6	         24.9	         21.8	Nov	  19.4	         20.4	         17.9	Dec	  16.7	         16.2	         14.0	Jan	  21.2	         20.2	         17.4	Feb	  21.1	         20.3	         16.5	March	  22.2	         19.1	         10.5	From April 2020, only quarterly data for commercial vehicle segment is available. (b):  Turnover of the industry from the domestic sales of vehicles for the previous three Financial Years (FY) is as follows: Turnover Estimates through domestic Sales of all categories of vehicles(in crore)2017-18	2018-19	2019-204,64,447	4,90,849	3,96,002(c) & (d):  No closure of any automobile or ancillary manufacturing units has been reported to the Government.
