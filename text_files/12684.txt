                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1536






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Procurement at MSP




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the agricultural produce procured at the Minimum Support Price (MSP) during the years 2017-18 and 2018-19, district-wise; (b)	 the percentage of the production in terms of agricultural produce procured in Uttar Pradesh and the details thereof, district/division-wise; (c)	 the total amount spent in this regard and the amount paid to the farmers; and (d)	 the name of the head under which the amount was spent and the total amount spent by Uttar Pradesh, district/division-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 The details of the agricultural produce procured at the Minimum Support Price (MSP) during the years 2017-18 and 2018-19 are at Annexure-I to Annexure-V.  (b):	 The details of production, procurement and its percentage of agriculture produce procured at MSP in the state of Uttar Pradesh are at Annexure-VI and Annexure-VII. (c) & (d):	 Government extends price support for procurement operations of wheat, rice and coarse grains to the procuring agencies of the State Governments by reimbursing the expenses on procurement of foodgrains through allocated food subsidy budget regularly. Further, to ensure smooth procurement of oilseeds, pulses and cotton at MSP, Government Guarantee has been provided to lender Banks for extending credit facility to central procuring agencies (which is revised from time to time based on the requirement) to undertake procurement operations. These funds are revolving in nature. However, Budget is allocated for reimbursement of the losses, if any, incurred by Central procuring agencies. Under these schemes MSP is paid to farmers for their produce.
