                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2877






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






National Digital Communication Policy




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of National Digital Communication Policy, 2018; (b) 	whether the Government has achieved the target fixed under the said policy; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	if not, whether the Government has fixed accountability of any person in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (d) 	The National Digital Communications Policy 2018 (NDCP-2018) has been notified in the Gazette of India on 22nd October, 2018 with a vision to support India’s transition to a digitally empowered economy and society. NDCP-2018 aims to accomplish its objectives by year 2022. Action on strategies to accomplish the objectives of the Policy by year 2022 are at different stages of implementation.
