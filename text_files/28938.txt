                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

381






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Comprehensive Possibilities in Agriculture




Ramesh Chander Kaushik














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of the schemes being implemented by the Government to identify under comprehensive possibilities in agriculture in other States including in the State of Haryana; (b)the quantum of funds allocated for these schemes during the last eight years; (c)whether the funds was utilised during the fixed time frame; and (d) if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d) Details of schemes being implemented by the Department of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare in the country including the state of Haryana and year wise  allocation and expenditure for last eight years are given in the Annexure.
