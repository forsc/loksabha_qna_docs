                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2065






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Target Olympic Podium Scheme




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any proposal for increase in the budgetary allocation to the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports;  (b) the funds allocated, received and spent under Target Olympic Podium (TOP) Scheme till date;  (c) the list of sportspersons who have been provided assistance under the Scheme, till date; and  (d) the criteria in place for choosing sportspersons who will be provided assistance there under for Olympics? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (KIREN RIJIJU) (a): In financial year 2020-21, the Budget Estimate (BE) for Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports is Rs.2826.92 crore, which was Rs.2216.92 crore in 2019-20, which is a 27.5% increase. BE figures for FY 2018-19 and 2017-18 were Rs.2196.35 crore and Rs.1943.21 crore respectively, which shows that efforts have been made to increase budget allocation of the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports over the years.   (b): No separate funds are allocated for Target Olympic Podium (TOP) Scheme. However, funds are released from National Sports Development Fund (NSDF) Scheme to Sports Authority of India (SAI) for expenditure on TOP Scheme. The details of funds released under NSDF to SAI for TOP Scheme are as under: <pre>Year Funds Released to SAI (Rupees in crore) Funds utilized by SAI (Rs. in crore) 2016-17 20.00 19.61 2017-18 10.00 4.87 2018-19 10.00 8.84 2019-20 8.50 8.22 </pre>In addition, NSDF provides OPA to TOPS athletes selected in Core Group at Rs.50,000.00 per month and Development Group at Rs.25,000.00 per month. Details of funds released to athlete are as under: <pre>Year Funds (OPA) Released to Athletes in Core Group (Rs. in crore) Funds  (OPA) Released to Athletes in Development Group (Rs. in crore) 2016-17 - - 2017-18 6.51 - 2018-19 5.94 - 2019-20 5.63 - 2020-2021 2.33 1.68 </pre>(c): At present, 94 athletes and 2 teams are included in the TOPS whose details are in Annexure. (d): Selection criteria in place for selecting sports persons who are provided assistance under TOP Scheme are as follows: i) Current performance ii) Past performance iii) Performance in comparison to Global performance trends iv) Current age in comparison to High performance age v) Global competitors’ analysis vi) Performance progression rate vii) World Rankings & National Ranking ****
