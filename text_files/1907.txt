                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1721






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Assistance to Victims of Extremism




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether any  policy is in place to provide  assistance/grant/compensation/service by the Government to the family members of soldiers, jawans killed in naxal and terrorist attacks;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is aware of the number of soldiers and jawans who died in naxal and terrorist activities in various parts of the country during the last three years;(d) if so, the details thereof, incident and State-wise; and(e) the status of compensation/grant/assistance provided to the family members of the deceased along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e) The Government of India is committed to support the families of martyred security personnel. The family members of the martyred SF are given assistance in the following manner:i.	Next-of-Kin (NoKs) of CAPF personnel are given ex-gratia compensation @ ? 35 lakh (w.e.f. 01.01.2016). ii.	NoK also get the last drawn pay with DA as Extra Ordinary Pension (EoP)/ Liberal Pension Award (LPA).iii.	Provision of compassionate appointment to a dependent family member of martyred personnel against Group-C post, upto 5% of the vacancies.-2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.1721 FOR 02.07.2019iv.	Other normal benefits to the families of SFs like Death cum Retirement Gratuity (DCRG), Central Government Employee Group Insurance Scheme (CGEGIS) etc.v.	Financial assistance up to ?15 lakh from Bharat ke Veer corpus to take the total amount to ? 1 crore for the martyr’s family. In addition, Ex-gratia payment is also given to the families of the martyrs including State security force personnel, under Security Related Expenditure (SRE) scheme in LWE affected areas, Jammu & Kashmir and North East region. The State Governments also have their own compensation policy for the families of State Police martyred.Detail of security forces killed in LWE and terrorist related activities is at Annexure.
