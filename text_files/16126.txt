                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Sensitising of Staff to Deal with Foreign Tourists




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of tourists that visited India during the last three years and the current year; (b) 	whether tourism in India has been affected by the Coronavirus; (c) 	whether the Ministry has sensitized the staff of tourist spots especially in Agra and Rajasthan about dealing with foreign tourists; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Details of Foreign Tourist Arrivals (FTA) during 2017 to 2019 are given below:<pre>Sr. no.	Period	FTA(in Millions)1	2017	10.042	2018	10.563	2019 (Provisional)	10.89</pre>Provisional data of Foreign Tourist Arrivals (FTA) during last two months and those during like period of previous year are given below:<pre>	January 2019	February 2019	January 2020	February 2020FTA	11,03,380	10,87,694	11,18,150	10,15,632</pre>However, Ministry of tourism, Government of India has not undertaken any specific study on impact of corona virus on tourism in India.(c) & (d):	Ministry of Tourism, Government of India has taken various initiatives to sensitize about the measures required to be taken as well as to facilitate tourists in the country including in Agra and Rajasthan which is attached at Annexure.********
