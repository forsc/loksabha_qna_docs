10.02.2020






Direct and Indirect Taxes




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received an annual income to the tune of 22 lakh crores from both direct and indirect taxes and if so, the State-wise break-up thereof; (b) the details of the devolution of funds to the States and UTs; and (c) the details of the total loans and borrowings by the Government and the annual interests paid by the Government?
