                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Functioning of Old Age Homes




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1412TO BE ANSWERED ON 11.02.2020FUNCTIONING OF OLD AGE HOMES1412. SHRI GOPAL CHINNAYA SHETTYWill the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a)  the number of old age homes being run by the Government; (b)  the details of old age homes run by the Union Government and the State Governments; State-wise; (c)  the number of people working in old age homes, State-wise; and(d)  the number of elderly people living in the old age homes run by the Union Government and     the State Governments, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) :  Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment provides Grants-in-Aid for maintenance and running old age homes to implementing Agencies. Total number of such old age homes is 500 where Grants-in-Aid is given to Implementing Agencies.(b) & (c): This Ministry is implementing a Central Sector Scheme of Integrated Programme for Senior Citizens (IPSrC) under which grant is given for running and maintenance of Senior Citizens Homes (old Age Homes)/Continuous Care Homes, Mobile Medicare Units etc through Implementing Agencies such as State Governments/Union Territory Administrations through Registered Societies/Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs)/Local bodies; Non-Governmental/Voluntary Organizations; Institutions or Organizations set up by Government as autonomous/subordinate bodies; Government Recognized Educational Institutions, Charitable Hospitals/Nursing Homes, and recognized youth organizations such as Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS). The main objective of the Scheme is to improve the quality of life of the Senior Citizens by providing basic amenities like shelter, food, medical care and entertainment facilities.Details of Senior Citizen Homes (SrCHs) assisted under the Scheme of Integrated Programme for Senior Citizens (IPSrC), State-wise is given in Annexure.Data regarding Old Age Homes run by the State Governments is not maintained in this Ministry.(d): As per the Scheme guidelines of IPSrC Scheme, each home for 25 beneficiaries have been prescribed to have following staff: S.No.	Staff	No.i.	Superintendent  (full time)	01ii	Social Worker/Counsellor (Part time)	01iii.	Yoga Therapist (Part time)	01iv.	Nurse (Part time)	01v.	Cook (Full time)	01vi.	Multi Tasking Staff (MTS)  (3) Full time	03Additional one cook and one MTS is prescribed for Sr.Citizen Home for 50 beneficiaries.Details of elderly people living in the old age homes supported under IPSrC is given in Annexure. Data regarding State Government is not maintained in this Ministry*****ANNEXUREAnnexure referred to in reply to  Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1412 to be answered on 11.02.2020Details of number of Senior Citizen Homes (Sr.CHs) and number of beneficiaries therein, State-wise under the Scheme of IPSrC.Sl.No.	Name of States	Senior Citizen Homes(Sr.CHs)	No. of beneficiaries1	Andhra Pradesh	76	19752	Assam	28	7503	Bihar	02	504	Chhattisgarh	01	255	Delhi	02	506	Gujarat	12	3007	Haryana	16	4008	Himachal Pradesh	02	509	Karnataka	38	112510	Kerala	05	12511	Madhya Pradesh	15	40012	Manipur	35	92513	Maharashtra	55	170014	Nagaland	02	5015	Orissa	76	207516	Punjab	03	7517	Puducherry	02	5018	Rajasthan	07	20019	Tamil Nadu	61	157520	Telangana	18	45021	Tripura	04	10022	Uttar Pradesh	15	37523	Uttarkhand	04	12524	West Bengal	21	550	Total	500	13500
