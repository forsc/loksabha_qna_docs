UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3395






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






ITC Frauds of GST




S. Gnanathiraviam













                                                    Vijay Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has unearthed 8,000 cases of Input Tax Credit (ITC) frauds of Goods and 	Service Tax (GST) worth Rs. 35,000 crore in a recent nationwide crackdown against unscrupulous 	entities;b)	if so, the details thereof;c)	whether any steps are being taken to recover the amount from the tax evaders;d)	if so, the details thereof; ande)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHOUDHARY)(a) & (b) Sir, the details of Input Tax Credit [ITC] fraud detected by CGST formations under CBIC for the F.Y 2020-21 & 2021-22 (upto June 2021) are as follows:   Period	No. of Cases	Quantum involved(In Rs.
