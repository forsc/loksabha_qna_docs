                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3430






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Nutrition Resource Centres under National Nutrition Mission




Rampreet Mandal













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                








                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the funds allocated, sanctioned, released and utilised under National Nutrition Mission along with the number of States/UTs covered so far under the said scheme during the last three years, State/UT-wise including Bihar;(b)	the number of eligible beneficiaries benefited under the said scheme, district-wise including Bihar; (c)	the number of Nutrition Resource Centres set up under PoshanAbhiyan, State/UT-wise, district-wise;(d) 	whether the Government has received any proposal to provide balanced diet under National Nutrition Mission and if so, the details thereof along with the action taken on the said proposal till date; and (e) 	the outcome of the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	State-wise statement of funds released and utilisation under POSHAN Abhiyaan earlier known as National Nutrition Mission till December, 2019 is at Annexure-I. All the States/UTs are covered under the POSHAN Abhiyaan.(b)	There are no separate beneficiaries under POSHAN Abhiyaan. It ensures convergence of all nutrition related schemes. Under POSHAN Abhiyaan, Smart phones are provided to Anganwadi Workers and Lady Supervisors for using ICDS-CAS(Common Application Software) Application. As on 29th February, 2020, the total number of Anganwadi Centres using ICDS-CAS Application are 6,25,659 across 28 States/UTs. The State-wise details of ICDS-CAS users are at Annexure-II.(c)	There is a provision of State Nutrition Resource Centre (SNRC) at State level  for implementation of POSHAN Abhiyaan. Further, there is provision of  two staffs each at District level and Block level also under POSHAN Abhiyaan. State Nutrition Resource Centre has been established in all the States/UTs except Ladakh and West Bengal.(d) & (e) Government has  not received any proposal to provide a balanced diet under POSHAN Abhiyaan.
