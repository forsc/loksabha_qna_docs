                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3490






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Working of NGOs




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the number and list of Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) working under Ujjain division in Madhya Pradesh at present; (b) the details of the NGOs funded by the Union Government and State Governments from the financial year 2014-15 to the current financial year, scheme-wise and year-wise; and (c) the amount of funds sanctioned for NGOs alongwith the amount of funds due to the NGOs respectively, year-wise






ANSWER




                                                        (a): The Ministry is implementing several schemes under which financial assistance is provided to the Non- Governmental Organisations (NGOs).¬      The details of NGOs receiving assistance from this Department and working under Ujjain division in Madhya Pradesh are given in Annexure-I.(b) & (c): The details of the NGOs funded by the Department, from the financial year 2014-15 to the current financial year, scheme-wise and year-wise, are given in Annexure-II.****Annexure –IRefer to part (a) of the reply to the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3490 for 10.12.2019 S. No. 	Name of Scheme	Name of Non- Governmental Organisations (NGOs)1	Scheme of Grant in Aid to Voluntary and other organisations working for Scheduled Castes	(i) Madhya Pradesh Bairwa Sanskrit Parisad(ii) Madhya Pradesh Anusuchit Jati JanJati Pichada Varg Kalyan Sangh2	Integrated Programme for Senior Citizens (IPSrC)	-3	Assistance for Prevention of Alcoholism and Substance (Drug) Abuse	(i) Association for Social Health in India
