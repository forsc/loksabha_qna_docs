                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Privatisation of Public Sector Institutions




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the Public Sector Enterprises, Banks and Financial Institutions that have been enlisted for disinvestment and privatization during the next three financial years; and(b)	the details of the target amount set to be realised through the said process?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(DR. BHAGWAT KISHANRAO KARAD)(a)  The government, since 2016, has given ‘in-principle’ approval for strategic disinvestment of 36 CPSEs and/or Subsidiaries/ Units/ Joint Ventures of CPSEs and Banks, a list of which is annexed.
