                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

966






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Solar Energy Plants




Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Solar Energy Plants under construction in Uttar Pradesh at present, district-wise;(b) the details of constructed and under construction plants in Bundelkhand region; and(c) the quantum of energy targeted to be generated from the construction of these plants?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) The Government of Uttar Pradesh has reported allocation of 38 nos. of Solar Power Plants of capacity 1435 MW including 385 MW capacity under Open Access for sale of power to third party or for captive use for their commissioning from March to September, 2020. The district-wise details are given at Annexure-I.(b) The details of constructed and under construction plants in Bundelkhand region are given at Annexure-II.(c) The quantum of energy targeted to be generated from the construction of these plants has been reported as 2388.41 MU.*****
