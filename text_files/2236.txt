                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

193






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Upliftment of Minority Communities




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of MINORITY AFFAIRS be pleased to state: (a) the details of the existing schemes for improvement of minority communities;  (b) the funds allocated / sanctioned and utilized for this purpose during each of the last two years and the current year;  (c) whether the Government proposes to have any further programme for the upliftment of the minority communities; and   (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)   (a) to (d): A statement is laid on the table of the House.  ***
