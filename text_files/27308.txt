                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2847






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Construction of Smart Roads under Smart City Mission




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the work is in progress to construct smart roads under Smart City Mission in Jabalpur; (b) if so, the details of the work in progress of smart roads under its 	Phase-I; (c) whether there is delay in the construction of these roads; and (d) if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                    HE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (d) : Jabalpur Smart City Limited (JSCL) is implementing 4.19 km. Phase-1 Jabalpur Smart Road project under Smart Cities Mission (SCM) at an estimated cost of ? 80.47 crore.  JSCL has reported that since the project involves retrofitting of existing areas, and involves, inter alia, maintenance of existing services such as sewer lines, water supply, electricity lines, traffic movement etc. and also due to lockdowns in view of COVID-19 pandemic, there is some delay with respect to the original contract period.                                *****
