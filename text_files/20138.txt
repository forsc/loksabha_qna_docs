                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Implementation Indira Gandhi Awas Yojana




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh













                                                    Suresh Kodikunnil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount spent under India Awas Yojana (IAY) during the last five years, year-wise and State-wise;(b) 	whether there are old dues pending with the Government for release of funds to the States under the Scheme;(c) 	if so, the details of the amount pending for release to the State, year-wise and State-wise;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the amount for constructing houses under IAY in the country; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof along with the number of houses constructed under IAY during the last four years and the current year in Kerala, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c) The year wise and state wise release of funds for the last five years under Indira Awaas Yojana is given at Annexure-I.  The funds are not being released for completion of IAY houses to the States/UTs in advance from 1st April 2018 onwards. However, the States are reimbursed if they need to recoup expenditure under this head. No proposal for release of old dues is pending with the Ministry.(d)     No Sir. The erstwhile rural housing scheme Indira Awaas Yojana has been restructured into Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana -Gramin (PMAY-G) from 01.04.2016. Under PMAY-G, the unit assistance has been enhanced from Rs.70,000 under IAY to Rs.1,20,000 in plain areas and from Rs.75,000 under IAY to Rs.1,30,000 in hilly States, difficult areas and Integrated Action Plan (IAP) districts.(e)	The district wise number of houses constructed under IAY during the last four years and current year in respect of the State of Kerala is at Annexure-II.******Annexure-ISTATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (c) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1380 TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021 REGARDING IMPLEMENTATION INDIRA GANDHI AWAS YOJANA.Year wise and State wise release of funds during the last five years under Indira Awaas Yojana:Annexure-IISTATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (E) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1380 TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021 REGARDING IMPLEMENTATION INDIRA GANDHI AWAS YOJANA.Number of IAY houses constructed district-wise in the State of Kerala during the present financial year:                       (Units in Nos.)
