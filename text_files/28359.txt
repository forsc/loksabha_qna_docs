                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

169






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Development and Maintenance of Sites of Historical Importance in Jharkhand




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there are any special programs/ plans undertaken for the development and maintenance of Sites of Historical and Cultural importance in the State of Jharkhand;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the amount of funds allocated for the development and maintenance of Sites of Historical and Cultural importance in the State of Jharkhand; and(d)	the status of historical sites in Palamu?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b) 	The Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) is maintaining centrally protected monuments in the state of Jharkhand.  Following six monuments have been identified for special repair works in current financial year. 1.	Ancient Temple of Shiva Linga,Khekparta, Dist. West Singhbhum2.	Ancient Tank and remains of temples and structures at Benisagar, Dist. West Singhbhum.3.	Jami Mosque, Hadaf, Dist. Singhbhum4.	Baradari of Mouza Arzimukhimpur, Dist. Sahibganj.5.	Temple at Haradih, Dist. Ranchi6.	Palace and Temple complex, Navaratangarh, Sisai, Dist. Gumla.(c)	For the financial year 2021-22, Rs.90.0 Lacs has been allotted for the development and maintenance of protected monuments/ sites in the State of Jharkhand.(d)	There is no protected monuments/ sites in Palamu Dist. of Jharkhand.                                                                         *****
