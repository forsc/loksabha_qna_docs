                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

796






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Upgradation of National Highways




Jayanta Kumar Roy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to upgrade the National Highways (NHs) covering districts of Jalpaiguri and Darjeeling, West Bengal; (b)	if so, the details thereof including the progress made in this regard so far; (c) 	whether the bids have been invited for upgrading the NH 31, 31A, C, D from existing 2 lane to 4/6 lane; (d) 	if so, the details thereof including the progress made in this regard so far; and(e)	the details of important roads constructed in West Bengal including Jalpaiguri region during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Yes, Sir. (b)	The details of works are at Annex-I.(c)  	No, Sir.(d) 	Does not arise.(e)	This Ministry is primarily responsible for development & maintenance of National Highways. During last five years, 521 km length of National Highways has been developed in the State of West Bengal.
