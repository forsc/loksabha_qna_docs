                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






KVs Without Amenities




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Principals, Vice-Principals and Head-masters working in Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) take their teaching periods including on-line periods as per KVs norms/guidelines; (b) if so, the details thereof indicating the periods allotted on weekly/monthly basis and if not the reasons therefor; (c) whether the officers responsible for monitoring the teaching periods submit their reports to the higher authorities of KVs; and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) & (b) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that as per the practice, the following schedule is required to be followed by the Principals, Vice-Principals and Headmasters for taking classes:- Principal                -    5   Periods per weekVice-Principal         -    22 Periods per week Head Master          -    16 Periods per week(c) & (d) The monitoring of Principals are being done by the concerned Regional Office during visits / inspections by Assistant Commissioners & Deputy Commissioner and for Vice-Principals and Headmasters, it is being done by the Principals concerned.*****
