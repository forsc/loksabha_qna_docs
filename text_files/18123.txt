                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1907






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Conversion of SHs into NHs in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the length of National Highways (NHs) and State Highways (SHs) in Odisha;(b) whether there is any proposal for conversion of any SHs into NHs in Odisha and if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether SHs passing through Angul and Dhenkanal districts are proposed for conversion into NHs and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)]   (a) National Highways (NHs) in an aggregate length of 5753 km & State Highways (SHs) in an aggregate length of 4211 km pass through the State of Odisha.  (b) There is no proposal for conversion of SHs into NHs in Odisha.(c) There is no such proposal for conversion of SHs passing through Angul & Dhenkanal districts in Odisha into NHs.                                *****
