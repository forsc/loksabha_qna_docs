                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

530






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Pending Railway Projects




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether a number of other projects including Loharu to Bhiwani and Delhi to Alwar via Nuh-Firozpur Jhirka rail lines in Haryana have been pending for a long time due to paucity of funds and other technical reasons;(b)	if so, the project-wise details thereof for the last three years;(c)	 whether the Government has not allocated sufficient funds for these pending railway projects; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	 to (d) :  No Sir. However, these two Railway Projects have not yet been sanctioned. Present status of these Projects is as under :-Loharu to Bhiwani Rail LineThe Feasibility Study for the work of Loharu to Bhiwani was done by Railway. Thereafter on the directions of Government of Haryana, the Feasibility Study has again been conducted by Haryana Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd. (HRIDC) a Joint Venture between Ministry of Railways (49%) and Government of Haryana (51%). The Survey Report was submitted to Government of Haryana on 30.04.2019. Further action on the project will be taken after receiving the proposal from HRIDC. Delhi to Alwar via Nuh-Firozpur Jhirka Rail Line.The Feasibility Study for this was done by Railway. Thereafter on the directions of Government of Haryana, the Feasibility Study has again been conducted by Haryana Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd. (HRIDC) a Joint Venture between Ministry of Railways (49%) and Government of Haryana (51%). The Survey Report was submitted to Government of Haryana on 30.04.2019. Further action on the project will be taken after receiving the proposal from HRIDC.                                             *****
