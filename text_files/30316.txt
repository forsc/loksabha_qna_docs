                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Price of LPG




Debasree Chaudhuri














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of price of LPG in the country for both subsidized and unsubsidized segments over the last three years, month-wise;(b) the details of the total revenue generated by Union Government from the sale of LPG in the country; and(c) the details of the amount of subsidy that has been provided to the poorer sections of society under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana during the last three years, month-wise?
