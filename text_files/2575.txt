                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2287






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Consolidation of RRBs




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to start the consolidation process of Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) in the country;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is considering to bring down the number of such entities to 38 from 56 at present;(d)	whether the Government has asked the Public Sector Banks which are the sponsors/promoters of the RRBs, to amalgamate the RRBs within a State; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the banks thereto?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) to (e)  With a view to enable Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) to minimize their overhead expenses, optimize the use of technology, enhance the capital base and area of operation and increase their exposure, a roadmap for amalgamation of RRBs within a state was prepared in consultation with NABARD. The roadmap proposes to bring down the number of RRBs to 38 from 56. The Government has sought comments of respective State Governments and respective Sponsor Banks on the roadmap.Based on comments from respective Sponsor Banks and State Governments, 21 RRBs have been amalgamated. The details are provided at Annexure.*****
