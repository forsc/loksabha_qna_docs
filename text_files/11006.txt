                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

14






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Funds Allocated for Conservation of Monuments




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) takes up the conservation work of the monuments on a regular basis and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the total number of monuments taken up for repair work during the last three years including the current year, State/year- wise; and(c)	the total fund allocated and spent on the conservation work of the monuments during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)  to (c)	Yes Sir.  Conservation work of protected monuments is taken up by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) on  regular basis.  There are 3691 protected monuments under the ASI.  Conservation work of these monuments is a continuous process and it is attended regularly depending upon the need of different sites and availability of  the resources.  The details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred, State-wise, for the last three years including allocation for the current financial year are given in annexure.
