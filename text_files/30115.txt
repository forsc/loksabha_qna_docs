                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1693






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Import and Export of Telecom Equipments




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the imports for telecom equipment in the last five years;(b) 	the exports for telecom equipment in the last five years;(c) 	the details of domestic production of telecom equipment in India by foreign brands; and(d)	 the salient features and the progress of the Brand Building of India as Telecom Manufacturing and Services Destination initiative? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) to (d) 	The imports and exports of the telecom equipment in the last five years are as under :<pre>Year	Import (in Rs. Crore)	Export (in Rs. Crore)2016-17	42,980	3,6252017-18	48,096	5,1962018-19	55,696	6,2422019-20	38,448	5,4342020-21	47,751	7.074</pre>The data regarding domestic production of telecom equipment in India by foreign brands is not maintained. To attract investment in manufacturing and brand building India as a manufacturing destination, the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Telecom and Networking Products has been notified on 24.02.2021 within overall financial outlay of Rs.12,195 Crore over 5 years. 31 companies have been selected under the Scheme.The Government is supporting Indian Startups/MSMEs/companies to showcase their capabilities at important national and international forums/events for Brand building of India as telecom equipment manufacturing and services destination as follows: <pre>Year	No. of events2019-20	22020-21	42021-22	3</pre>				********
