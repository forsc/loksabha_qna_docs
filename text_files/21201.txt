                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2513






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Cancellation of Contracts of Warehouses and Transportations




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Food Corporation of India has cancelled the contracts of many warehouses and transportations of Maharashtra; (b) 	if so, the names of the places where these contracts have been cancelled along with the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the tender will be opened again for new contracts; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): 	No Sir, Food Corporation of India (FCI) has not cancelled handling and transport contracts of any warehouses of FCI.Hiring and dehiring of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation (MSWC) and Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC) godowns for Storage space is done on need basis.The details of hiring & dehiring of warehouses are at Annexure.(c) & (d): 	Tenders for re-hiring of dehired warehouses are issued based on assessment of storage gap.****ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2513 FOR ANSWER ON 09.03.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.S No.	Name of Center	Capacity (in MT)	Date of hiring	Date of dehiring	Reasons for dehiring1	MSWC Mehkar	2000	20.01.2017	18.11.2020	The godown was used for storage of pulses. It was dehired after pulses were liquidated. 2	CWC Karanja	1000	01.11.2018	19.11.2020	The godown was used for storage of pulses. It was dehired after pulses were liquidated. 3	CWC Nanded 	31160	27.01.2006	06.02.2021	The godown at Nanded was dehired because it was found unstorageworthy for storage of food grains during the inspection done by Food Ministry.4	CWC Vashi	28120	14.07.2013	Dehiring order dated 28.12.2020, stock is under liquidation	The godown at Vashi was dehired due to operational inability to issue stocks under PDS and other constraints.****
