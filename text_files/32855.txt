                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

377






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Procurement and Consumption of Green Energy




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposed new rules for the procurement and consumption            of Green Energy in August, 2021;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the latest rules would encourage the energy consumers including industries in                 Karnal Parliamentary Constituency of Haryana at large scale to avail the benefits of            renewable energy sources for regular operation; and     (d) if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)to(d) Yes, the Government has proposed draft rules "Draft Electricity (Promoting Renewable Energy through Green Energy Open Access) Rules, 2021" for procurement and consumption of Green Energy.  The draft rules were circulated for stakeholder consultation in August, 2021.The proposed rules will encourage energy consumers including industries in Karnal Parliamentary Constituency of Haryana, who are willing to consume Green power, i.e. power generated from renewable sources of energy to avail the benefits of renewable energy sources for regular operation.*****
