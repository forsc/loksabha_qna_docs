                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






GST Refund Claims




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that a big scam of Goods and Service Tax (GST) refund claims has come to light in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of companies involved therein so far;(c) the details of measures taken by the Government to identify the wrong claims of GST; and(d) the details and number of companies which have taken action in GST refunds or matters related thereto so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)	(a)	Yes, Sir. A number of cases of Refund frauds have been detected.(b)	The details of the fraudulent Refund claims detected by the CGST authorities during the period 01.07.2017 to 31.01.2020 are as follows:			Total No. of Entities Against Whom GST Refund Frauds Detected 	Amount Involved in GST Refund Frauds Detected (Rs. Crores)	Recovery(Rs. Crores)	No. of Persons Arrested637	1926.10	238.97	35(c)	Data analytics and intelligence gathering is done to identify the Refund frauds(d)	As per reply to part (b) above.*****
