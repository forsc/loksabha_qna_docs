                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

447






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Grants to Uttar Pradesh




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of grants issued to the State Government of Uttar Pradesh under different schemes of the Panchayati Raj during the last three years;(b)	the details of the amount of the awards given to selected Panchayats of the State of Uttar Pradesh for promotion of good work under National Panchyaat Award; and(c)	the details of the number of Panchayats selected for their good work in the state of Uttar Pradesh, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL)(a)	Grants released under the schemes of Ministry of Panchayati Raj i.e Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA), Incentivization of Panchayats (IoPs) and Survey of Villages and Mapping with Improvised Technology in Village Areas (SVAMITVA) to the State Government of Uttar Pradesh during the last three years are as follows:Name of Scheme	                Grants Released (Rs. in crore)RGSA	                               259.60IoP	                                14.14SVAMITVA*	                          1.45 *The scheme being implemented since 2020-21(a)	& (c): Under the National Panchayat Awards a total of Rs. 14.14 crore was released to the State Government of Uttar Pradesh as award money to a total of 113 Panchayats at various level under Incentivization of Panchayats (IoPs) scheme during the last three years.  The details of district-wise number of Panchayats selected for their good work in the State Uttar Pradesh during the last three years are given at Annexure. ***
