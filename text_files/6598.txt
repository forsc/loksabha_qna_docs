                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Wastage of Cereals




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that due to lack of proper storage, huge quantities of cereals (wheat-rice) was laid to waste over the years; (b) 	if so, the details of cereals wasted during the last three years, year-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has not allowed export despite adequate stocks of cereals; (d) 	if so, the reasons therefor; (e) 	whether the Government has adequate reserves of cereals to tackle drought situation in the country; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) and (b): 	No Sir.  No wastage of Central Pool Foodgrains (wheat & rice) stored in godowns of Food Corporation of India (FCI) can be directly attributed to lack of proper storage facilities. Procured foodgrains are stored by FCI in a scientific manner in covered godowns and Cover & Plinth (CAP) storage. Inspite  of all precautions, some quantity of foodgrains may get damaged/become non-issuable during storage due to transit damages viz enroute damage in wagon/truck, contamination, wagon roof leakage, Natural Calamities like cyclone/flood etc. ............2/--2-The quantum of foodgrains, which got damaged in godowns of FCI during last three years is as under:Year	Quantity of damaged foodgrains(in lakh tonnes)2016-17	0.092017-18	0.0272018-19	0.05(c) and (d): 	India is a Member of World Trade Organization (WTO) and the provisions of the Agreement on Agriculture under the aegis of WTO, restrict exports on commercial terms from public stockholding.  No export for wheat/rice has been made from Central Pool Foodgrains Stocks during the last year and the current year till date.(e) and (f): 		Yes Sir, To meet any contingency situation like drought, a strategic reserve of 20 Lakh MT of rice and 30 Lakh MT of wheat is maintained at national level at different FCI godowns, out of the foodgrains procured at  Minimum Support Price (MSP).****
