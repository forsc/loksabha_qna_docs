                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






LNG Terminal




Arvind Kumar Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has planning to give a big push to LNG (Liquified Natural Gas) aspreferred fuel for heavy vehicles in the country; and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) and (b) Government has been driving the initiative of setting up Liquified Natural Gas(LNG) stations by City Gas Distribution (CGD) entities across the Golden Quadrilateral NationalHighways, East-West Highway, North-South Highway and in major Mining clusters in India.This initiative involving establishment of 50 LNG stations in the 1st phase, would help inestablishment of LNG as a Transport fuel for increasing the share of natural gas in the totalenergy basket of the country besides providing environmental benefits to the society.****
