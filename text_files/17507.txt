                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Provision of Masks at Monuments




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps being taken for provision of masks, e-tickets at Centrally Protected 	monuments 	protected by Archaeological Survey of India;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c)	the implementation status/response received in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	The Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) issued by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW) on 4th June, 2020 has made it mandatory for use of face covers/masks. Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has also accordingly issued SOP for Opening of Centrally Protected Monuments on 6th July, 2020 which categorically mentions that, “The visitors shall follow social distancing. The use of face cover/mask is mandatory”. The provision of booking e-tickets online is made available and is mandatory for all the visitors visiting ASI monuments.(b)	The details are at Annexure-I(c)	The directives issued by the MoHFW and the SOP issued by ASI are being 	strictly adhered to by all concerned.
