                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Cancelled Flights




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of flights cancelled/diverted due to severe cold prevailed in northern part of India particularly in Delhi;(b) whether any passengers were given any compensation for cancellation of flights and late departures and arrivals; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) As per information provided by airport operators viz Airports Authority of India(AAI) and Delhi International Airport Ltd.(DIAL) , a total of 284 flights were diverted due to bad weather/ poor visibility in the Northern Region of India from 15 Nov 2019 to 15 Feb 2020.Region wise data for cancellation of flights is not maintained by airport operators or Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). However, as per monthly traffic data received in DGCA from scheduled domestic airlines, a total of 144 flights were cancelled due to bad weather at Delhi from 30th November, 2019 to 9th March, 2020.(b) & (c) As per the monthly submission of traffic data received from the scheduled domestic airlines, the compensation details for the month of November 2019, December 2019 and January 2020 for cancellations and delay of flights includes the following:;Month        Due to cancellation  Due to flight delays;Nov 2019      Rs. 50.73 lakhs       Rs. 199.97 lakhs;Dec 2019      Rs. 103.68 lakhs     Rs. 288.94 lakhs;Jan 2020       Rs. 46.73 lakhs       Rs. 180.01 lakhs.
