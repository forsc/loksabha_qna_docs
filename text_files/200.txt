                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

13






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Setting up of New AIIMS Institutions




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has started the construction of proposed AIIMS at Rajkot; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated so far and the total estimated cost of the project including land acquisition and pre-construction activities; (c) the details of the studies/surveys, clearance and NOC’s obtained for the purpose; and (d) the time by which the proposed premier hospital will be operationalized?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (d):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 13* FOR 21ST JUNE, 2019a.	Cabinet has approved setting up of AIIMS at Rajkot, Gujarat, at the cost of Rs.1195 crore on 10.1.2019. Executing Agency for pre-investment activities has been appointed. b.	Approved cost of the project including cost of pre-construction activities is Rs. 1195 crore. State Government has to handover free of cost land to Government of India for setting up of AIIMS. c.	Pre-investment activities including soil survey, topographical survey is in progress.d.  The Cabinet approved timeline for completion of the project is October, 2022.****
