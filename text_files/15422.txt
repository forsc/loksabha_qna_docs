                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4088






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






RoB at Begum Bazar




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the time by which the construction work of half built Begum Bazar bridge in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh will be completed;(b)	the reasons for not completing the construction despite spending 75 per cent of its cost;(c)	whether the construction work has been halted owing to lack of mutual coordination; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): Bridge work of railway portion is being done by railway and approaches by State Government of Uttar Pradesh. Work of railway portion as well as approaches is in progress. Work is held up in certain portion (both railway and approach portion) for want of No Objection Certificate (NOC) from Air Force Authority. Further action will be taken after receipt of NOC. (d): State Government is trying to resolve the issue with Air Force authority but till date the issue has not been resolved.*****
