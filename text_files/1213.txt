                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

808






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Passport Related Services at Post Offices




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to provide Passport related services at post offices and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the number of cities in Uttar Pradesh where the said services are proposed to be provided by the Government; and (c)	whether the Government has fixed any timelimit for implementation of the said system and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	Yes Sir, the Government has decided to provide Passport related services at Post Offices by opening Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSKs). The details of existing Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSKs) are attached at Annexure-‘I’. (b)	There are 46 Post Office Passport Seva Kendras existing in Uttar Pradesh as per list attached at Annexure-‘II’. 6 more POPSKs are likely to be opened in UP in consultation with Ministry of External Affairs subject to feasibility. (c)	No Sir, no time limit has been fixed.
