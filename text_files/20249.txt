                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1801






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






NH Projects in Madhya Pradesh




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the highways declared as National Highways (NHs) along with name of NHs which are under construction at present in the State of Madhya Pradesh;(b) the policy of the Government to execute the work on the approved new NH projects under NHAI and to ensure the quality of construction work; (c) whether the Government has received any complaints about the poor quality of construction work on ongoing four-laning project on NH-12 and other highways; and (d) if so, the details thereof along with the action taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) 	The details of the highways declared as National Highways (NHs) in the State of Madhya Pradesh are at Annexure – I.  The details of ongoing works on NHs in the State of Madhya Pradesh are at Annexure – II.(b)	The development of National Highways (NHs) is a continuous process and the projects are taken up based on fulfilment of technical requirements, inter-se priority and availability of funds. The quality of construction work during execution is ensured as per NH specifications. (c) & (d)  The Government has received few complaints about the poor quality of construction work on ongoing four-laning project on NH-12 as some of PQC panels were found cracked. These are being repaired/replaced by the contractor and no payment has been made to the contractors for replacement of cracked panels.
