                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

145






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Routes under RCS-UDAN




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) the number of routes identified under the RCS-UDAN for Birsi airport of Gondia in Maharashtra;(b) whether the Government plans to add new routes under the said scheme;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of new airline companies incorporated and operating the flights under the RCS-UDAN?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Jyotiraditya Scindia)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.******STATEMENT IN RESPECT OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 145 REGARDING "ROUTES UNDER RCS-UDAN" TO BE ANSWERED ON 29.07.2021.  (a): "Indore-Gondia(Birsi airport)-Indore" and "Hyderabad-Gondia-Hyderabad" routes have been awarded in the 4th round of bidding under Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS)-UDAN (Ude Desh Ka Aam Nagrik) for operations of UDAN flights. Gondia will become a served airport, once the routes get operationalized.  (b) & (c):  As per the UDAN Scheme, only the unserved (no air connectivity) and underserved (having upto 7 departures per week) airports are considered for providing connectivity. However, interested airlines based on their assessment of demand to & from Gondia may mount flights on commercial basis on routes other than UDAN routes.   (d): Since, launching of UDAN i.e. from 21st October, 2016, Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), being a regulator, has granted Air Operator Certificates to nine (09) Airline operators under Schedule Operator /Schedule Commuter Operator category. All nine (09) airline operators have participated in UDAN Scheme.
