                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2676






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Banning Cooperative Societies from Deposits and Investment




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any State Government registered its opposition on RBI’s new notification on banning cooperative societies from deposits/investments; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the steps taken to address their grievances?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ?????? (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) to (c): Ministry of Cooperation has not received any representation from any State Government on RBI’s Press release on cautioning members of public against some co-operative societies using the word ‘Bank’ as part of their names and accepting deposits from non-members/ nominal members/ associate members, which is tantamount to conducting banking business in violation of the provisions of the Banking Regulation (BR) Act, 1949. However, RBI has received a letter dated 01.12.2021 from the Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Kerala and a letter dated 02.12.2021 from the Minister of Co-operation and Registration, Government of Kerala requesting for withdrawal of the caution notice issued by RBI.*****
