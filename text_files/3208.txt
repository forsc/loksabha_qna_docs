                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2832






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Mobile Towers in Villages




Vishnu Dayal Ram













                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total number of villages of the country where telecom towers have been installed during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise and telecom company-wise;(b) 	the number of such towers targeted to be installed in the rural areas during the next financial year, State/UT-wise and telecom company-wise; and(c) 	the places where these towers are proposed to be established?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	Details of number of villages, where telecom towers have been installed, are not being maintained separately by the Department of Telecommunications. According to data collected from Telecom Service Providers (TSPs), State/UT-wise total number of villages provided with mobile services as on 31.03.2017, 31.03.2018 and 31.12.2018/30.04.2019/30.06.2019 as well as number of towers is given at Annexure-I (a to e).	(b) & (c) There is plan to install 57,559 towers for mobile services in the rural areas during the financial year 2019-20 by TSPs. State/UT –wise and TSP-wise details are given at Annexure-II.
