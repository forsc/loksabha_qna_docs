                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

608






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Kuttanand Package




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government is intending to implement a second Kuttanad package as a continuation of first Kuttanad package that was implemented in Kuttanad region for ensuring development of livelihood and agriculture and allied activities; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Union Government is aware of proposal for second Kuttanad package that has been conceptualized by the Government of Kerala towards mitigating the crisis faced by the region; and (d) if so, the details of assistance sought by the Government of Kerala?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF JAL SHAKTI  (SHRI GAJENDRA SINGH SHEKHAWAT)(a) to (d) Union Government has no information about the second Kuttanad Package. However, State Government of Kerala has submitted a list of 10 numbers of new projects in Kuttanad region for inclusion under Flood management and Border Areas Programme (FMBAP) of Ministry of Jal Shakti during the period 2021-26. The details of the projects is at Annexure-I. State Government has informed that the Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) of these projects are still under preparation stage. As per provisions of the ongoing "Flood Management and Border Areas Programme (FMBAP) of Ministry of Jal Shakti, it is not be possible to include any new projects of flood management for funding under this Programme at this stage. The process of formulation and approval of the centrally sponsored scheme, Flood Management and Border Areas Programme (FMBAP) of Ministry of Jal Shakti for the period 2021-2026 is presently underway. lnclusion of new projects for funding will depend upon the contours of new scheme as and when approved by the Government of lndia.
