                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Drones’ Role to Check Illegal Occupation of Land




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that Drones can play any role in checking illegal occupation of land and demarcation of unauthorized colonies;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to use drones to check illegal occupation of land and demarcation of unauthorized colonies;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor and reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (e): Yes, Sir. However, the use of Drone Technology is at a nascent stage and the competence and scalability of the capacities of agencies offering these services is yet to be assessed.                                      *****
