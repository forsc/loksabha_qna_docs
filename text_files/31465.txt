                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2629






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Assistance for Welfare of Physically Challenged




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether assistance is being provided for the welfare of physically challenged people to various organizations in the country;(b) if so, the amount released and spent during each of the last three years and the current year; and(c) the details of organisations to which assistance were provided for the purpose during the said period along with the projects and amounts released?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) Yes, Sir. Relief to the persons with disabilities is a State subject by virtue of entry ‘9’ of the State list of the Constitution of India. However, the Central Government supplements the efforts of the State Governments for the welfare of persons with disabilities. The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities implements various Central Sector Schemes and also non-scheme initiatives for the welfare of Persons with Disabilities including physically challenged people. The major schemes of the Department are; Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme, Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids/Appliances, Scholarship for Students with Disabilities, Scheme for Implementation of Persons with Disabilities Act and schemes of National Trust. These schemes are being implemented through various implementing agencies including institutes/organisations under the Department and Voluntary Organisations throughout the country.(b) and (c) The details of funds released during last three years and current year to various organisations under Schemes/Non-Schemes initiatives of the Department for the welfare of Persons with Disabilities are as under:-(Amount in Rs. crore)Details of Organisations 	Financial Year	2018-19Amount	2019-20Amount	2020-21Amount	2021-22Amount(as on 30.11.2021)National Institutes under the Department	286.88	288.19	254.52	158.63Composite Regional Centres under the Department	68.22	53.05	24.79	28.39National Trust, a Statutory Body under the Department	16.23	20.00	29.80	14.86Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation of India, a central Public Sector Enterprises under the Department	182.47	171.96	153.73	80.00National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation, a central Public Sector Enterprises under the Department	21.16	0.00	0.00	0.00Voluntary Organisations across the country	102.25	111.41	94.86	37.99Total	677.21	644.61	557.70	319.87*****
