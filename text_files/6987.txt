                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

746






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Aviation Parks




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of aviation parks setup in the country, State-wise;(b) whether the Government is contemplating to set up/develop aviation parks in Gujarat and if so, the details thereof;(c) the funds likely to be spent thereon;(d) whether the land has been handed over to the Government by the State and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the time by which it is likely to be developed?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): No aviation park has been set up by Airports Authority of India in any of the States.;(b) to (e): The master plan of new Greenfield Airport at Hirasar, Rajkot, Gujarat indicates a parcel of land for development of aviation logistic park to be developed by Civil Aviation Department, Government of Gujarat.
