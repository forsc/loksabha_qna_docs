                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2532






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Van Gram Vikas Yojana




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of forest villages situated in protected areas of the country, especially in Maharashtra under the Van Gram Vikas Yojana; (b)	 whether any proposal has been received by the Government for the implementation of Van Gram Vikas Yojana from the Government of Maharashtra in Bhandara and Gondia Districts;(c)	if so, the details thereof along with the details of the released/utilised amounts during the last three years and the current year; and (d)	the status of progress and development of these villages during the said period? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) There is no forest village in Maharashtra. All Forest Villages were converted into Revenue Villages by 1972.(b)  As informed by the Government of Maharashtra, there is no proposal for implementation of Van Gram Vikas Yojana in Bhandara and Gondhia Districts of Maharashtra by Forest Department.(c) and (d) Does not arise.	***
