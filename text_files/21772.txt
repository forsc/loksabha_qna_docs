                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2878






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Budgetary Allocation




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the budgetary allocation for the Ministry has been below one percent of the total budget despite Government assurances and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(b)	whether the Nirbhaya Fund has largely been underutilized and underfunded and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the total funds utilised for establishment of crisis centres for the victims of rape and domestic violences, shelter homes for women, female police volunteers and a dedicated helpline? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) 	A budgetary provision of Rs. 24435 Crore for the year 2021-22 has been made for the Ministry of Women & Child Development. Allocations are made on the basis of expenditure trends as well as available resources with the Government.(b) & (c)   No Sir, since, the inception of the scheme/ projects worth Rs. 9288.45 Crore have been appraised and an amount of Rs.3544.06 crore has been disbursed/ released by the concerned Ministries.  This Ministry implements three schemes under Nirbhaya Fund namely One Stop Centre (OSC), Women Helpline (WHL) and Mahila Police Volunteers (MPVs) and 2 schemes namely Swadhar & Ujjawala schemes for women Shelter Homes. The details of total funds released under these schemes are placed at Annexure-I.****** ANNEXURE-IDetails of fund released during 2018-19 to 2020-21(upto 28.02.2021)                       (Rs. in Crore)S.No.	Scheme	Total released1	One Stop Centre	367.512	Women Helpline	33.363	Mahila Police Volunteers 	5.624	Swadhar & Ujjawala (Shelter Homes)	83.69               Total:	490.18 ******
