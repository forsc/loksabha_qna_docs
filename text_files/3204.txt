                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2782






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Solar Powered Mobile Towers




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether private telecom company operators have equipped most of their mobile towers with solar cells whereas no such initiative has been taken by BSNL; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to equip each mobile tower of BSNL with solar cells and if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the time by which BSNL mobile towers in Gujarat are likely to be equipped with solar cells in Gujarat along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) 	No Sir, Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has equipped 2111 mobile towers with solar cells.  (b) to (d) 	BSNL has informed that provision of solar cells at each of its mobile tower is techno-commercially not viable.  BSNL has also informed that presently, there is no plan to equip all of its mobile towers in Gujarat with solar cells.
