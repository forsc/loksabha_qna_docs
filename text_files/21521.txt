                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2565






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Digital Gram Yojna in Rajasthan




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology be pleased to state: (a)	the salient features of Digital Gram Yojna;(b)	the details of funds approved, allocated and utilized in Rajasthan so far under the scheme, District-wise;(c)	the number of villages and districts covered under the scheme in Rajasthan so far;(d)	the details of the villages selected so far under the scheme in Dausa district of Rajasthan;(e)	the details of target fixed and achievements made by the Government under the scheme; and(f)	whether the Government is planning to link some more villages under the scheme and if so, the time by when these villages are likely to be linked?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):   Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) had initiated the Digital Village Pilot Project titled “Pilot Project- Implementation of Digital Village” on 31st October 2018 for duration of 3 years and not Digital Gram Yojna. 700 Gram Panchayats (GP)/Village (atleast one Gram Panchayat/village from each district of the State/UT) are being covered under the pilot implementation of the project. The Deliverables of the project are in the areas of Health, Education, Skill Development, and Financial Inclusion including G2C & B2C: i.  Education Service: Courses offered under this project are as (i) Basic Computer Course (BCC), (ii) Course on Computer Concept (CCC), (iii) Tally – Kaushal Praman Patraii. Health Services – Tele-Health &  Tele-Veterinary consultation iii. Financial Inclusion- Financial inclusion Awareness programme  iv. Solar street lights (8 Solar lights per village)v. Skill Development - skill development in (i) Automotive Technician, (ii) Handset repairing, (iii) Field Technician- Home Appliances, and (iv) Electrical Technician.(b):  The Digital Village Pilot Project is being implemented across India through CSC e-Governance Service India Limited under the Digital India Scheme which is a Central Sector Scheme of Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. The tentative funds requirement for the State of Rajasthan, under the project, to cover 33 villages is approximately Rs.329.83lakhs.
