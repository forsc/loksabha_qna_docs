                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

662






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Auction of Scrap Materials




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a plan to make a solid policy to promote timely and transparent auction of various scrap materials manufactured in the Railways; (b)	if so, the solid plan adopted for the re-use of various scrap materials manufactured in the Railways; and (c)	whether there are complaints of irregularities in the sale of various scrap materials manufactured in the Railways during the last three years and if so, the time by which the investigations will be done? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	There already exists a well established system for timely and transparent auction of various scrap materials generated in the Railways.  Scrap is sold in transparent manner through e-Auction. Entire process of scrap sale is online through Indian Railways Electronic Procurement System (IREPS).(b)	The various scrap materials generated in the Railways are re-used, to the extent possible, as raw materials for manufacturing wheels at Rail Wheel Factory/Bangalore and Rail Wheel Plant/Bela; and Scrap Rails by Railways, Public Sector Units and other Government Departments.(c)	No, Sir.*****
