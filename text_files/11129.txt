                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

15






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






ESI Hospitals in Madhya Pradesh




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of Employees State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) hospitals set up in the country, State-wise;(b)whether the Government proposes to set up new E.S.I. hospitals in Madhya Pradesh; (c)if so, whether the Government proposes to set up a new E.S.I. hospital in Panna district of Bundelkhand region in Madhya Pradesh; and(d)if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. **                                                     ******STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 15 FOR 03.02.2020  BY SHRI VISHNU DATT SHARMA REGARDING ESI HOSPITALS IN MADHYA PRADESH.(a):			State-wise details of Employees'' State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) hospitals set up in the country is placed at Annexure. (b) to (d):	ESI Corporation has given ‘In-Principle approval for setting up of ESI hospital at Pithampur, Madhya Pradesh. However, no proposal has been received from State Government of Madhya Pradesh, for setting up of a new ESI Hospital in Panna district of Bundelkhand region of Madhya Pradesh.
