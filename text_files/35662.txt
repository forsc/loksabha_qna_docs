                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Computers to Panchayats under RGSA




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the number Panchayats in the country, State-wise; (b)	whether the Government has provided computers under Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) to Panchayats;(c)	if so, the details of Panchayats that have been given computers and technical manpower i.e. computer literate persons in the State of Telangana, district-wise;(d)	the details of requests received for sanction of computers and technical manpower from the Government of Telangana during each of the last five years; and(e)	the steps taken by the Ministry in this regard to provide computers and manpower to Panchayats under RGSA?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a)	to (e) A statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN PARTS (a) to (e) IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 266 FOR ANSWER ON 22.03.2022 REGARDING COMPUTERS TO PANCHAYATS UNDER RGSA___________________________________________________________________________(a)	Details of all levels of Panchayats in the country is at Annexure-I. (b)	Yes Sir. (c)	to (e) Panchayat being State subject, providing computers and manpower for Gram Panchayats (GPs) is primarily the responsibility of States. However, Ministry, under the scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) being implemented w.e.f. 2018-19, has been supplementing the efforts of all States, including Telangana, towards supporting functioning of GPs by providing computers for GPs and technical manpower at Block/ cluster of Panchayats as proposed by States in their Annual Action Plan and approved by Central Empowered Committee on a limited scale.  Financial support for computer and technical support for Gram Panchayats were not provided under the erstwhile scheme of Capacity Building-Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan (CB-PSA) implemented during 2016-17 & 2017-18.  District-wise information is not maintained centrally. Details of computer and technical support proposed by State of Telanagana and approved there upon under the scheme is at Annexure-II.****
