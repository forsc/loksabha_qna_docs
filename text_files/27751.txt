                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3228






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Promotion of PSUs




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is reviewing any proposals to promote Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) in sectors such as the pharmaceutical industry which is of vital importance to public health in the light of COVID-19 pandemic; and (b) if so, whether any concrete proposals are being considered to ensure affordable drugs and other health care products as well as health care facilities to the public? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(DR. BHAGWAT KISHANRAO KARAD)(a)	& (b) Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) function under the administrative control of respective Ministries/Departments. All matters relating to revival/ restructuring/closure of CPSEs are decided by the concerned administrative Ministries/ Departments. Department of Pharmaceuticals (DoP) is the nodal Department for promotion of pharmaceutical sector in the country and management of CPSEs functioning under its administrative control. DoP has informed that there is no such proposal under consideration. *****
