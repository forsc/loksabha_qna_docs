                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

314






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Scholarships for Meritorious Students




N.K. Premachandran













                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to increase the amount of scholarship for meritoriousstudents;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the details regarding the scholarship given to students invarious categories, category-wise;(c) whether it is come to the notice of the Government that it is not possible to submit theapplication for scholarship in time due to the default in the online uploading system;(d) if so, the details of the action taken/to be taken by the Government to rectify the default in theonline system(e) whether it is also come to the notice of the Government that the eligible students are not gettingthe scholarship in time; and(f) if so, the details of the action taken by the Government to disburse the scholarship in time andissue the arrears?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) and (b): At present there is no such proposal to increase the amount of scholarship formeritorious students.The category-wise details of the scholarships provided to the students during the financialyear 2018-19 by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, the University GrantsCommission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) are:-<pre>Department /OrganizationName of Scholarship Scheme Number of Scholarshipsprovided (fresh+ renewal)General SC STDepartment ofSchool Education& LiteracyNational Means Cum- MeritScholarship Scheme1,94,356 43,282 10,984Department ofHigher EducationCentral Sector Scheme of Scholarshipfor College and University students.96,434 11,068 3,179University GrantsCommission(UGC)Post Graduate Scholarship forUniversity Rank Holders823 97 63Post Graduate Scholarship forGATE/GPAT qualified students1,330 47 2Post Graduate Scholarship forProfessional courses for Scheduled0 330 132Castes and Scheduled Tribes.Ishaan Uday-Special Scholarship forNorth Eastern Region.16,028 1,240 2,702All India Councilfor TechnicalEducation(AICTE)Post Graduate Scholarship forGATE/GPAT qualified candidates.29,108 4,458 826Saksham Scholarship Scheme fordifferently abled students.126 12 13Special Scholarship Scheme for Jammu& Kashmir10,542 996 1,045<\pre>(c) & (d): The scholarship schemes are implemented through online mode, no specific grievancesregarding default in the online uploading system has been received.(e) and (f): The Government has taken several steps for timely disbursal of scholarships.Technology is being used extensively, complete process of scholarship has been made online,scholarship amount is being disbursed directly into the beneficiary’s bank account through DirectBenefit Transfer mode.
