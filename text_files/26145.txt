                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1975






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Pollution from Old Vehicles




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that there are more than four crore vehicles older than twenty years in the country;(b)	if so, details thereof, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether vehicles from Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Madhya Pradesh have not been included in the list;(d)	whether any assessment has been made regarding the pollution caused by these vehicles in the country; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the extent to which the vehicle scrap policy announced in the Budget helps to address the said issue?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	to (c):No Sir, there are 2.14 Crorevehicles older than twenty years in the Country and details thereof, State/UT-wise is at Annexure. The list excludesAndhra Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Telangana, and Lakshadweep as they are not in centralized Vahan 4 portal.(d) and (e): No assessment has been made regarding the pollution caused by these vehicles in the country. ***
