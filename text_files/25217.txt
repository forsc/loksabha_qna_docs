                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1102






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Child Labour Working in Hazardous Industries




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether a large number of child labourers are still working in hazardous industries such as beedi, carpets, textiles and mining in the country;(b)if so, the state-wise details thereof; and(c)the number of child labourers rehabilitated from the said industries during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b):	As per census 2011, the  total number  of  main workers in the age group of 5-14 years was  43,53,247. State wise details are at Annexure.
