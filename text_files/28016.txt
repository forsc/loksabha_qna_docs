                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3564






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Beneficiaries of Pension Schemes




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the beneficiaries of various pension schemes being implemented by the Government in the State of Gujarat during the last five years including the persons belonging to the Scheduled Castes category;(b) 	whether the Government is contemplating to make further amendments in the pension schemes;(c) 	if so, the details thereof along with the steps being taken in this regard; and(d) 	the steps taken by the Government to make pension schemes more inclusive?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (d) The details of the beneficiaries covered under National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) pension schemes for  old aged, widows and disabled persons living below  poverty line in the State of Gujarat including the persons belonging to the Scheduled Castes category during the last five years are enclosed in Annexure. No such proposal for its amendment is under the consideration of the Government.					******                                                                                          AnnexureAnnexure referred to in parts (a) to (d) of reply of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question number 3564 to be answered on 10.08.2021 Financial Year	                 Number of beneficiaries under pension schemes of NSAP	                               IGNOAPS	         IGNWPS	            IGNDPS2016-2017	                        538098	          12548	              65882017-2018	                        474415	          63163	              78342018-2019	                        559443	          85122	              92652019-2020	                        580706	          97473	             107742020-2021	                        580706	         218395	             12370
