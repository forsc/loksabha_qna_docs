                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1911






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Project Tiger




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of the States in which Project Tiger is being implemented at present, State/UT-wise;(b)	the details of the funds provided to the State Government for the maintenance of each Project Tiger from the year 2018 to 2020, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government has the fund allocation for these projects and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons thereof;(d)	whether the reduction in funds is likely to affect these projects adversely and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a), (b) & (c)	The funding assistance is provided under the ongoing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Project Tiger to States for conservation and protection of tiger.  The details are at Annexure-I.(d) & (e) The budget allocation is prioritized in order to maximize protection, monitoring and eco-development activities while dovetailing with resources available with other schemes and State Governments.*****
