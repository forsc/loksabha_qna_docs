                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4824






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Funds for Development of Panchayats




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of funds allocated by the Government for panchayat development particularly in the State of Assam during the last three years;(b) the amount of funds utilised in this regard;(c) the details of the total population in Blocks/Panchayats in the State of Assam;(d) whether the Government has any guidelines regarding the total population level in blocks/gram panchayats; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	 & (b) Details of funds allocated to States for Gram Panchayats under the Fourteenth Finance Commission (FFC) award for the period 2015-2020 for delivery of basic services and under the schemes of Capacity Building-Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan (CB-PSA), Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) of Ministry of Panchayati Raj for strengthening Panchayati Raj Institutions across the country for the last three years are given at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.
