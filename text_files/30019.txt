                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1617






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Mission Purvodaya




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the Mission Purvodaya and the manner in which it is likely to be more effective for the development of the North-Eastern Region?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)	Mission Purvodaya encompasses the development of the Eastern Region in the states of Odisha, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, West Bengal and parts of Andhra Pradesh by enabling capacity addition of crude steel to facilitate production of value added steel as an integrated steel hub to unleash its true potential.********
