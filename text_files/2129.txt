                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2010






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Funds Spent under MPLADS




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government expenditure is incurred on the works like laying foundation and erecting a foundation stone through the funds spent by public representatives under MPLAD Scheme;  (b) if so, the details of the funds spent for such purposes during the last three years, State and year wise;  (c) the head under which the said funds have been spent;  (d) whether the Government has taken or proposes to take any steps for utilising the said amount in other developmental works; and  (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)  (a)  No Sir. As per the MPLAD Scheme Guidelines,  expenditure is not allowed to  be incurred on works like laying foundation and erecting a foundation stone. (b), (c), (d) and (e)  In view of  reply to part (a) question does not arise.                                           *****
