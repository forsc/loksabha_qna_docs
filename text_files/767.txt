                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

672






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Foot Over Bridges




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the number of Foot Over Bridges (FOBs) and under bridges proposed to be constructed by the Railways in the Northern Railway Zone and the locations thereof; and(b) whether any target has been fixed for completing the construction of all proposed foot over bridges and under bridges and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) AND (b) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.672 BY SHRIMATI KIRRON KHER TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 26.06.2019 REGARDING FOOT OVER BRIDGES(a) and (b)  Total 133 Nos. of Foot Over Bridges (FOBs) and 05 Nos. of extension of existing Foot Over Bridges are sanctioned over Northern Railway. 32 Foot Over Bridges are targeted for completion in 2019-20 and rest are planned for completion by 2020-21. The details of sanctioned Foot Over Bridges are as appended.602 Nos. of Road under Bridges (RUBs) have been sanctioned over Northern Railway. 200 Road Under Bridges are targeted to be completed during 2019-20.  Target of completion of RUBs depends on many factors like catchment area/extent of drainage improvement works in State Government portion, land acquisition, encroachment, if any, on the approaches etc.*****
