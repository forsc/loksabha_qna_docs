                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1260






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






AtmaNirbhar Bharat Abhiyan




V. Kalanidhi













                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of features of the AtmaNirbhar Bharat Abhiyan along with the sectors likely to be covered under this Abhiyan b)	the details of funds estimated for the said Abhiyan along with the details of target set there under; c)	the reasons as to why the Government has not covered the poor and the middle class since they are suffering due to unemployment and poverty and also the migrant labours who returned to their native home despite the existing challenges; d)	whether the Government has announced any relief package under AtmaNirbhar Bharat Abhiyan for citizens and entrepreneurs of the country; and e)	if so, the number of citizens and entrepreneurs who have been given assistance under this package, district-wise?  






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (e):  The Government announced Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan - a special economic package of Rs 20 lakh crore on 12.05.2020 with the aim of making the country self-reliant and to focus on local manufacturing, local market and local supply chains.  It focuses on preparing the country for tough competition in global supply chains, enhance the ease of doing business, empower MSMEs, attract investments including FDI and strengthen the policies for Make in India. It also aims at supporting agriculture and fisheries reforms and enhancing agriculture and animal husbandry infrastructure. It also contains measures to boost liquidity support for MSMEs, DISCOMS, NBFCs and other businesses.  In furtherance of the objective of the above announcement, Ministry of Finance announced various measures/policies/schemes from 13th to 17th May, 2020.  The Atma Nirbhar Package covers a wide section of sectors and members of society, including migrant labourers, middle class and entrepreneurs.  The facility of supply of free food grain and pulses was extended to migrants for 2 months.  A statement indicating details of features of the AtmaNirbhar Bharat Abhiyan is at Annexure-I.  Further, an Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme announced on 23.5.2020 provides support to eligible MSMEs and business enterprises in meeting their operational liabilities and re-starting the business after disruption caused by COVID-19. National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company Ltd (NCGTC) provides 100% credit guarantee on the loans under the scheme. A statement indicating District-wise details of guarantees issued by NCGTC under Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS)  is at Annexure-II. A statement indicating benefits given under AatmaNirbhar Bharat Package and PMGKP is at Annexure-III.*****
