                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Cooperation to Operation Ganga




Arun Sao













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of cooperation provided by the Ministry in Operation Ganga started to bring back Indians stranded in Ukraine;(b) the name of airlines which have provided assistance to bring back Indians stranded in Ukraine; and (c) whether the airfare for bringing back Indians is borne by the Union Government or the State Governments, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) and (b) The Ministry of Civil Aviation has coordinated with six private airlines, namely Air Asia, Air India, Air India Express, Go First, Indigo and Spicejet for evacuation of Indian nationals from countries adjoining Ukraine viz. Romania, Hungary, Poland and Slovakia under Operation Ganga.;;(c) The air fare for all the flights operated under Operation Ganga has been borne entirely by the Government of India.******
