                                    CULTURE

STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

30






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Conservation of Monuments




Ramcharan Bohra













                                                    Rajiv Pratap Rudy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken adequate steps for the conservation and preservation of archaeological/heritage sites and monuments in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the details of archaeological/heritage sites and monuments taken up for maintenance and restoration during each of the  last three years and the current year in the country, monument/site-wise and State/UT-wise including Bihar;(c)	the details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred in this regard during the last three years and the current year, monument/site-wise and year-wise;(d)   the details of sites which the Government intends to take up for restoration in the near future along with the time by which the conservation work of these sites is likely to be completed; and (e) whether the Government has prescribed  certain procedure to provide contracts for development of such sites and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	to (e)	A statement is laid on the table of the HouseSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *30 FOR 24.06.2019(a) & (b) Yes Sir, Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has 3691 Nos. of protected monuments of national importance in the country including the state of Bihar.  The conservation work of protected monuments is a continuous process and executed by the ASI regularly depending upon the requirements of different sites and availability of resources.  Due to regular care by the ASI they are in a fine state of preservation.    (c) The details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred for conservation of protected monuments in the country during the last three years and allocation for the current financial year are given in the annexure.(d) Being a continuous process the need based conservation work on protected monuments is attended regularly keeping them in presentable condition.(e)	The main thrust is for conservation of the protected monuments.  The development works related to public amenities and for enhancing the visitors’ experience are executed following standard guidelines and procedure. Some of the development works have been assigned to Government of India Public sector undertakings (PSU’s).
