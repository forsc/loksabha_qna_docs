                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1459






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Train from Balaghat




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the train running from Balaghat (Madhya Pradesh) via Gondia to Itwari, Nagpur has been stopped since last August;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government would consider to start a new train in public interest from Balaghat (Madhya Pradesh) to Itwari Nagpur to Balaghat in the evening to provide railway facility to the public; and(d) if so, the time by which it is likely to be done and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d) Due to operational constraints, the services of 68714/68715 Itwari-Balaghat MEMU have been discontinued w.e.f. 23.08.2019.  However, introduction of trains over Indian Railways is an ongoing process subject to operational feasibility and availability of resources. *****
