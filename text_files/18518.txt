                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2274






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Malnutrition among Children




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    	whether the prevalence of malnutrition is highest in the country globally;   (b) 	Whether the Government is aware that forty four percent children below the age of three years are suffering from malnutrition;  (c) 	If so, the details thereof, State-wise including Rajasthan; (d) 	the names and details of such States where the number of children suffering from malnutrition are the highest; and(e) 	the corrective steps taken by Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d) National Family Health Survey – 3 (2005-06) provides the data on malnourishment status of children below the age of 3 years. As per NFHS-3, the prevalence of stunting and underweight among children under 3 years of age was 44.9% and 40.4% respectively. The State/UT-wise details including Rajasthan  are annexed.As per the report of NFHS-4 conducted in 2015-16, 35.7% children under 5 years of age are underweight and 38.4% are stunted. Whereas, as per the report of Comprehensive National Nutrition Survey (CNNS) (2016-18), the prevalence of underweight and stunting among children is 33.4% and 34.7% respectively.  (e) This Ministry implements Anganwadi Services, Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana and Scheme for Adolescent Girls under the Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) as targeted interventions to address the problem of malnutrition in the country.Further, POSHAN Abhiyaan has been launched in March 2018 to achieve improvement in nutritional status of Children from 0-6 years, Adolescent Girls, Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers in a time bound manner by leveraging technology, convergence and Jan Andolan.******* AnnexureStatement referred to in reply to part (b) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.  2274 due for answer on 23.09.2020, raised by SHRI RAHUL KASWANPrevalence of Underweight and Stunting among children under 3 years of age as per NFHS-3 (2005-06)S.
