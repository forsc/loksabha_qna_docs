                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

44






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Skill Development Mission in Uttar Pradesh




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken any steps to promote Industry-Institute linkages in Skill Development Mission in State of Uttar Pradesh;(b)	the details of industries partnered under the scheme and nature of such partnership;(c)	whether the Government has forged partnership with foreign educational institutions and industries to provide quality training and professional skilling to the youth and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether such partnerships have been undertaken in the State of Uttar Pradesh, and if so, the details thereof, and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes Sir, steps have been taken to promote Industry-Institute linkages in Skill Development Mission in the State of Uttar Pradesh.(b)	Under Skill Development Mission, the partnered industries in Uttar Pradesh includes Rural Electrification Corporation Limited (RECL), National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC), Powergrid, Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL), Department of Public Enterprise, National Safai Karamcharis Finance & Development Corporation (NSKFDC), Simon India Limited (SIL), Hero MotoCorp Ltd., Balrampur Chini Mills Ltd. (BCML), Powerlinks Transmission Limited, etc. (c) and (d)	Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE) actively pursues global partnerships at G2G level with the aim of promoting global mobility, strengthening the Indian skilling ecosystem, and sharing of knowledge with other developing economies.MSDE and NSDC have entered into partnerships with countries namely UAE for benchmarking of qualifications, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) for Skills verification programme etc. Under the partnership with UAE, NSDC is working with Abu Dhabi Quality & Conformity Council (ADQCC) and 15 Indian Qualification Packs have been mapped to 13 UAE Qualifications in the Construction and Automotive sector. Further, partnership with Japan has also been established under Technical Intern Training Program (TITP) to send interns from India to Japan for 3-5 years. Till date, NSDC has empaneled 31 Sending Organizations (SOs) to drive the agenda of training the youth to advance their careers through TITP.
