                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2821






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Elevated Railway Tracks




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to build an elevated railway track in the Thanesar city of Kurukshetra and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government is also considering any scheme to lay a new rail track from Kurukshetra to Pehowa, Pehowa to Guhala Cheeka, Guhala Cheeka to Patiala (Punjab) with a view to promoting tourism and trade;(c)     if so, the details thereof; and(d)     if not, the reasons therefor ?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)   A Road Over Bridge (ROB) at level crossing No. 65 Kurukshetra on Narwana Kurukshetra Section was sanctioned in the year 2016-17 but the same was put on hold by the Government of Haryana as the proposal for Elevated Railway Track in lieu of ROB is under consideration by the State Government. The feasibility of Elevated Railway Track on Kurukshetra –Narwana section is being examined by Haryana Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation (HRIDC).-2-(b) & (c) The survey for new line from Yamuna Nagar to Patiala via Kurukshetra (141.8 km) was conducted in the year 2012-13 with rate of return (-) 9.83%, at cost of  1176 crore.  Therefore it was not found feasible to take this forward.(d)  Does not arise*****
