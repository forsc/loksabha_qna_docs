                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2483






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Smart City Mission in J&K




Hasnain Masoodi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps taken by the Government under the Smart City Mission in Jammu and Kashmir; (b) 	the steps taken by the Government to setup Metro Rail Project in Jammu and Kashmir; and (c) 	the steps taken by the Government for extension of metro rail from Srinagar to Awantipora to benefit commuters to reach Awantipora Airport and AIIMS, Awantipora?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	 Jammu and Srinagar were selected to be developed as Smart Cities under the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) in Round-3 of selection process in June, 2017. Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) have been formed in both the Smart Cities and implementation status of projects is given below.(Rs. in crore)Smart City	Tenders Issued	Work Orders Issued	Work CompletedJammu	49.8	215.9	0.0Srinagar	29.8	770.6	47.1Total	79.6	986.5	47.1(b) & (c): Urban transport is an integral part of urban development, which is a State subject.  Therefore, the proposals for urban transport including development of Metro Rail are prepared by concerned State Government/Union Territory and submitted to Government of India for Central Financial Assistance.  No proposal for development of Metro Rail in Jammu & Kashmir has been received in this Ministry.                                  …..
