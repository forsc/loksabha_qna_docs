                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4406






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Growth of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying Sector




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of growth of the allied sectors (fisheries, animal husbandry, and dairying) compared to food production growth in the on-going fiscal year compared to last fiscal year;(b)	whether the Government has taken any steps to improve the supply chain of allied sectors; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a):  The average annual growth of allied sectors (fisheries, animal husbandry and dairying) during last five years (2015-16 to 2019-20) was 8.65% as compared to 3.21% average annual growth of food production during the same period.(b) and (c): The Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) with highest ever investment of Rs.20050 crore in all the States/Union Territories for a period of five years with effect from the current financial year 2020-21 for development of fisheries and aquaculture sector including supply chain. Besides, the Ministry is also implementing Fisheries and Aquaculture Development Fund, scheme for creation and strengthening of fisheries infrastructure including supply chain and Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF) and Dairy Processing & Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF) for creation of infrastructure including supply chain in Animal Husbandry and dairy sector.*****
