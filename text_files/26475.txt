                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Petrol Pumps in Chhattisgarh




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of petrol pumps operating under Regional Office Raipur, Chhattisgarhdespite not having valid possession which do not comply with the rule of Gazette 636dated 31.12.2008;(b) the number of petrol pumps for which correspondence has been made with thecompany to shut them on being found dangerous from the security point of view by thedistrict administration in Rajnandgaon and Kabirdhan district of Rajnandgaonparliamentary constituency; and(c) whether correspondence has been made for necessary documents through officialLetter no.1182 dated 1.4.2021 and no.1648 dated 22.6.2021 by Office of Collector FoodBranch, Rajnandgaon to Regional Manager Office Raipur, which is received till date andif so, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) As on 30.6.2021, Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have1276 Retail Outlets (ROs) operational under Raipur territory, Chhattisgarh withvalid statutory approvals/licenses.(b) District Administration has not issued any order to Public Sector OMCs toshut petrol pump in Rajnandgaon and Kabirdham district.(c) Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited has informed that ownershipissue of Retail Outlets’ land is sub-judice at Chhattisgarh Rent Control Tribunal,Raipur.****
