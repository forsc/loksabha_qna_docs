                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

557






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






NH Projects in Tamil Nadu




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and present status of construction of the proposed 3500 km of National Highway (NH) in Tamil Nadu;(b) the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized for this prupose so far;(c) whether the Government has fixed any target in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps being taken by the Government for timely completion of this project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) As part of Budget 2021-22 announcement, 3500 km length of National Highway works, being implemented through NHAI, at an investment of Rs.1.03 lakh Crores, are proposed to be taken up in the State of Tamil Nadu. The timeline for accomplishment of the Budget announcement is proposed as 2023-24.  The details of NHAI projects which are under implementation in the State of Tamil Nadu and the projects proposed to be taken up in the State are Annexed.(b)&(c)  25  projects having length of 1154.51 Km with an outlay of Rs. 23,398.72 Crore are presently under implementation through NHAI. Further, the Concessionaires are also in place for additional seven projects covering a length of 257.44 Km awarded during 2019-20 and 2020-21, at an estimated cost of Rs. 9068.74 Crore.  Balance projects covering length of about 2063 Km with estimated cost of Rs.64920 Crore are proposed for award by 2023-24.(d)   To ensure timely/early completion of works, issues relating to the State Govt. /District Administration, Utility owning departments, Forest Department and other concerned State/Central Agencies are taken up with them for expediting Land Acquisition and other pre-construction activities.  Besides regular monitoring of projects with respective Authority/ Independent Engineer and Contractor/ Concessionaire is done.
