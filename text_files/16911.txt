                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1042






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Bharatiya Poshan Anthem




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has launched BharatiyaPoshan Anthem aiming to make India malnutrition free by 2022; and(b)	if so, the plan of action and time frame stipulated in this regard?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	Government has launched ‘Bharatiya Poshan Anthem’ on 3rdDecember, 2019, with the aim to inspire people to join Jan Andolan against malnutrition.(b)	Government implements POSHAN Abhiyaan (earlier known as National Nutrition Mission) since 18th December 2017 to address the issue of malnutrition in the Country. The goals of POSHAN Abhiyaan are to achieve improvement in nutritional status of Children from 0-6 years, Adolescent Girls, Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers in a time bound manner, by ensuring convergence, leveraging technology and encouraging people’s participation interalia.*****
