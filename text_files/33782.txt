                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






KIRAN Scheme




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of financial assistance provided by the Government to various women scientists under the KIRAN scheme in 2020-21; and(b)	the details of the recipients, State-wise?
