                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

26






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Houses under PMAY-G




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of houses proposed to be built and completed in the State of Tamil Nadu under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G);(b)	the total amount of funds allocated, disbursed and utilised, district-wise under PMAY-G for the State;(c)	the total number of beneficiaries, district-wise for Tamil Nadu; and(d)	the number of houses allocated and the number of beneficiaries for Tirunelveli?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.****** STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*26 TO BE ANSWERED ON 19.11.2019 REGARDING HOUSES UNDER PRADHAN MANTRI AWAAS YOJANA – GRAMIN(a) to (d):	To achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by 2022, Ministry of Rural Development implements Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) (restructured from the erstwhile Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY)), with effect from 01.04.2016. Under PMAY-G, 1,98,608 houses have been constructed against the target of 5,27,552, as on 15.11.2019. A total of 1,98,136 houses have been completed in Phase I (2016-17 to 2018-19) against the target of 3,27,552 houses. In the year 2019-20 of Phase II (2019-20-2021-22), 472 houses have been completed against the target of 2,00,000 houses. A Statement indicating district-wise details including Tirunelveli District, of number of beneficiaries, funds allocated, funds utilized, houses allocated and houses constructed in the State of Tamil Nadu under PMAY-G is given at Annexure-I.******
