                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2990






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Revival of Industries in Prayagraj




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether big industries were set up in Prayagraj district to promote industrialisation but at present their condition is not favourable and they are in dilapidated condition;(b)	if so, where there is any comprehensive scheme to revive the said industries or to merge them with other industries of public and private sector;(c)	whether there is any proposal to merge the BPCL company located in Prayagraj district with any other industry; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	In order to promote Industrialization, in Naini area of Prayagraj District earlier many Industries were established. (b) to (d):	The Union cabinet in its meeting held on 09/12/2020 approved for closure of M/s Bharat pumps and compressor Ltd, Prayagraj. It also approved that plant and machinery to be disposed of through e-auction and land to be returned to UP Government.
