                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4709






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Students Going Abroad




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State-wise number of students who went  abroad for studies during the last five years;(b) 	the country and State-wise details of the number of Indians living in foreign countries for employment; and (c) 	the number of Indians brought back from foreign countries under Vande Bharat Mission alongwith the State-wise details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	 The total number of students who went abroad in the last five years as per data provided by Bureau of Immigration is as follows:2016		- 	3,71,5062017		-	4,56,8232018		-	5,20,3422019		-	5,88,9312020		-	2,61,406State-wise figures are difficult to ascertain as a student domiciled in one State could have departed from another State. However, as per the issuance of passport, the numbers from different States are given at Annexure –I   (b)	As per data provided by Ministry of Home Affairs, 1,37,26,945 Indians have traveled overseas in the last 5 years (01.01.2016 to 18.03.2021), on employment visa.State-wise figures cannot be ascertained as an Indian domicile of one state could have departed from Immigration Check Post in other state. However, details as per issuance of passport (RPO) are enclosed at Annexure- II.(c)    As on 22 March, around 52 lakh people have been repatriated to India under the Vande Bharat Mission. A State-wise list (as on 22 March 2021) of arrivals in India is attached at Annexure-III. In addition, Ministry of Civil Aviation has also facilitated international travel of people including return to their country of work or residence abroad, taking the total number of people facilitated under the Vande Bharat Flights to 67.9 lakh.***
