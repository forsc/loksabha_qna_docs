                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

499






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Affiliation of Schools to Sainik Schools




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received three proposals from the State Government of Telangana for affiliation of schools to Sainik School society;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the action taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) 	No, Sir.(b)	01 application has been submitted online through State Government School and 04 from private school/Society as per details furnished hereunder:-Sl. No.	Organisation Name 	Organisation Type	City 1.	Sucheta Society	Society 	Kamareddy2.	Jawaharlal Nehru Educational Society 	Society 	Rajanna Sircilla3.	Springdales School	Private	Rangareddy4.	Telangana Social Walfare Residential Sainik School	State Government	Karimnagar5.	Veula Raghavareddy Lakshmamma Educational Society	Private	Medchal Malkajgiri(c)	Schools will be shortlisted through a process of evaluation, subject to meeting of qualifying requirements framed for the purpose and willingness to agree to the approval by-laws and signing of Memorandum of Agreement with the Sainik Schools Society.  An evaluation committee, constituted for the purpose will carry out evaluation of the applicant school in association with the school at the local level, based on details submitted by such schools in their application form.  The report of the evaluation committee will be considered at the Sainik Schools Society, through a committee formed for the purpose who will consider each application on merit and submit its comments for approval of the competent authority.*****
