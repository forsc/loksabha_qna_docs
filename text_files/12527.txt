                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1446






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Shortage of Delhi Police Personnel




Raj Hans Hans














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the situation of shortage of Delhi Police Personnel like Head Constable-Posts, 7063 lying vacant in Delhi-NCT as replied in Parliament by Home Affairs Ministry on 06.03.2018 has improved thereafter;(b) if not, the reasons therefor and the details of the steps taken by the Government to increase the strength; and(c) the details of shortfall of Police Personnel against the sanctioned strength in all Police Stations especially in the Parliamentary Constituency of North West Delhi?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) and (b) : 3279 posts of Head Constables and 7063 posts of Constables were lying vacant as on 15.02.2018 (earlier reply of Ministry of Home Affairs in Lok Sabha Question No. 1633 dated 06.03.2018).  As reported by Delhi Police, 2259 posts of Head Constables and 5872 posts of Constables are lying vacant as on 31.01.2020. Occurrence of vacancies inter-alia on account of retirements, creation of posts etc. is a dynamic process and accordingly filling up vacancies is a continuous process.  The assessment of manpower requirement of Delhi Police is a continuous process undertaken by the Ministry of Home Affairs in consultation with Delhi Police and other stakeholders. --2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 1446 FOR 11.02.2020(c): The shortfall, including vacancies, of Police personnel from the rank of Inspector to Constable against the sanctioned strength in all the Police Stations is 12,629; and the shortfall, including vacancies, against the sanctioned strength in Police Stations falling in Parliamentary Constituency of North West Delhi is 1,684. *******
