                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5258






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Construction of Toilets in Assam




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of toilets constructed during the last three years under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan at different locations in Assam;(b) whether materials for construction of toilets was purchased from outside or the same have been supplied by concerned Government Department and if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of PSUs through which toilets have been constructed in Assamunder Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, district-wise; and (d) whether amount earmarked for toilets has directly been given to the beneficiaries or some other agencies have constructed the toilets and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	Number of Individual household latrines (IHHLs) constructed in different districts of Assam under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) [SBM(G)] during the last three years are given at Annexure.(b) 	As per the information received from the State Government, few materials used in the construction of toilets (viz. Pan, Trap, PVC Pipe, Y Junction etc.) were purchased by some of the Government Offices/Divisions in Assam.(c) 	Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) are involved in construction of toilets in two districts of Assam namely Dibrugarh and Cachar.  The number of PSUs involved in Dibrugarh are four (viz. Oil and Natural Gas Corporation, Brahmaputra Cracker and Polymer Limited, North Eastern Electric Power Corporation Limited, and Oil India Ltd.) and the number of PSUs involved in Cachar district is one (viz. Oil and Natural Gas Corporation). (d) 	Direct beneficiary transfer under SBM(G) has been done in two districts namely Lakhimpur and Hojai. The other agencies involved in the construction of toilets are Non-Government Organisation (NGOs)/ Self Help Groups (SHGs)/ Community Based Organizations (CBOs).
