                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2931






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Revoked Passport of Nirav Modi




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry had revoked the passport of Mr. Nirav Modi to prevent him from evading the criminal justice system in India;(b)	 if so, the details thereof, including the date of revocation;(c) 	whether Mr. Modi travelled multiple times to foreign countries on an Indian passport which had been revoked; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	The Ministry revoked the passport of Mr. Nirav Modi as per the provisions of Section 10(3)(c) of the Passports Act, 1967, on 23 February, 2018. (c) & (d)	The Ministry has no means to verify the travels, if any, of Mr. Nirav Modi and the passport used for such travels by him.***
