                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

9






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Implementation of Citizenship (Amendment) Act




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government is considering to implement the Citizenship(Amendment) Act very soon;(b) whether the rules under CAA are still under preparation despite the fact that the said Bill was passed more than a year ago;(c) whether the said Act shall remain ineffective without the rules being notified; and(d) if so, the reaction of the Governmentthereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SH. NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d) The Citizenship (Amendment) Act, 2019 (CAA) has been notified on 12.12.2019 and has come into force w.e.f. 10.01.2020. The Rules under The Citizenship (Amendment) Act, 2019 (CAA) are under preparation. The Committees on Subordinate Legislation, Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha have granted extension of time upto 09.04.2021 and 09.07.2021 respectively to frame these rules under the Citizenship (Amendment) Act, 2019 (CAA).******
