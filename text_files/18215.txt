                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2051






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Construction of National Highways




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total length of National Highways (NHs) added/constructed in the country during the last three years; and(b) the amount of funds allocated to various States for the maintenance of State highways from Central Road Fund/Central Road and Infrastructure Fund alongwith the funds utilized by the State Governments during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THEMINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V. K. SINGH (RETD.)] (a)	30,921 km length of National Highway has been constructed in the country during the last three years i.e. from 01.04.2017 to 31.03.2020.(b)	The State / UT-wise details of accrual/ allocation of funds and the funfds released/ expenditure incurred under CRIF (State Roads) during last three years is at Annexure-I.
