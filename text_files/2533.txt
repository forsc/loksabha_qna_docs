                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2378






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






New Kendriya Vidyalayas




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to open new Kendriya Vidayalayas across thecountry including West Bengal;(b) if so, the number of New KVs opened during the last three years across thecountry, State-wise;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to open new KV in a joint venture withthe Ministry of Railways on railway land and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government has received any proposal to set up new KV on railwayland in Uluberia in West Bengal; and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) 50 new Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) were sanctioned in the country in March, 2019including 2 KVs in the State of West Bengal as mentioned below:-<pre>(i) NFR Katihar, New Jalpaiguri.(ii) CRPF Group Centre, Siliguri</pre>(b) During the last three years i.e. 2016-17 to 2018-19, 73 KVs have been opened inthe country. The State / UT-wise details are annexed.(c) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has informed that there is no proposal receivedfrom Ministry of Railways to open KVs in a joint venture on railway land.(d) No proposal has been received in the prescribed proformae from the Ministry ofRailways for opening of a new KV on railway land in Uluberia in West Bengal.******
