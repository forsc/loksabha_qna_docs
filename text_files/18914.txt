                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

33






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Khamgaon-Jalna Railway Line




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received requests from the public-representatives in the month of January, 2021 for laying of Khamgaon-Jalna railway line via Shegaon in place of Jalamb; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government has taken or proposes to take any action in this regard; and (d)	if so, the details thereof including the current status in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 33 BY SHRI PRATAPRAO JADHAV TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 03.02.2021 REGARDING KHAMGAON – JALNA RAILWAY LINE(a) to (d):    Yes, Sir.  Request has been received to consider extension of Jalna-Khamgaon (162 km) new line survey to Shegaon in place of Jalamb.  Both the stations Jalamb and Shegaon exist on Bhusaval-Wardha Broad Gauge line. Since Shegaon is already connected to Khamgaon via Jalamb (19 km), currently there is no proposal for direct survey from Khamgaon to Shegaon (17.5 km).*****
