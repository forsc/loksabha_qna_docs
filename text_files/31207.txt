                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Suryamitra Scheme




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of women targetted under Suryamitra scheme in the State of Uttar Pradesh, district wise; (b) the number of women trained under the said scheme in the State of Uttar Pradesh, district wise;(c) the number of registered training centres established under the said scheme in Jaunpur district; and (d) the total number of people trained under the said scheme from the district of Jaunpur?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) Under Suryamitra Programme of Human Resource Development Scheme of the Ministry, State-wise targets have not been set including for women candidates. (b) A total number of 165 women candidates were trained under Suryamitra Programme from financial year 2015-16 to 2019-2020 in the state of Uttar Pradesh.  The district wise break-up is at Annexure.(c) There are no registered training centres established under the Suryamitra Programme in Jaunpur district, Uttar Pradesh. (d) A total number of 54 people were trained from district of Jaunpur under the Suryamitra Programme from financial year 2015-2016 to 2019-2020.*****
