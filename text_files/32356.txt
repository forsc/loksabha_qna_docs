                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3788






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Demolition of Catholic Church




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware about the recent demolition of a catholic church at Lado Sarai in Delhi;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the competent authority has taken requisite legal steps before taking action against the Church;(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the actions taken against authority;(e) whether the Government has received any requests to maintain the pre-demolition status quo in case of the church; and(f) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (f):	As per the input provided by the Government of National Capital Territory of Delhi, no demolition of Catholic Church was carried out at Lado Sarai in Delhi. However, demolition of unauthorized structure was carried out on 12.07.2021 in Little Flower Church in Chattarpur Village at Khasra No. 36 and 120, which is a forest land. The  said  action  has  been  taken  in  compliance  of the directions of the Hon’ble High Court of Delhi in its order dated 25.08.2015 in WP(C) No.5234/2011 and of the National Green Tribunal in the matter of Sonya Ghosh Vs GNCT of Delhi & Others, observing formalities as per the process prescribed in law.  *****
