                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2279






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Anganwadi Centres under MGNREGA




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the number of approved Anaganwadi Centres in Chhattisgarh which are currently operational in rental premises along with the details thereof, district-wise; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to include construction work of Anganwadi centres under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee scheme; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and  (d)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) As on 30th June, 2020, against 52,474 sanctioned Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) in the State of Chhattisgarh, 51,462 AWCs are operational out of which 9,829 AWCs are running in rented premises. District-wise number of AWCs running from rented premise as uploaded at ICDS-RRS portal by Chhattisgarh, is annexed.(b) to (d) Joint guidelines for construction of 2 lakh Anganwadi Centre (AWC) buildings in most backward districts of 11 States (Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Rajasthan, Telangana and Uttar Pradesh) by 2019, under MGNREGS in convergence with ICDS Scheme have been jointly issued by Ministry of Rural Development and Ministry of Women and Child Development..   ******** AnnexANNEXURE REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2279 FOR ANSWER ON 23.09.2020 REGARDING ANGANWADI CENTRES UNDER MGNREGA ASKED BY SHRI SANTOSH PANDEYDistrict-wise number of reportingAnganwadi Centres (AWCs)and AWCS running from rented premise in the State of Chhattisgarh as on 30thJune, 2020S.No.	Name of District	No. Of Reporting AWCs	No of AWCs running in Rented Building1	BALOD	1523	972	BALODA BAZAR	1948	2183	BALRAMPUR	2198	6974	BASTAR	1874	2425	BEMETARA	1079	1066	BIJAPUR	837	2717	BILASPUR	2608	4698	DANTEWADA	1046	2329	DHAMTARI	1102	2010	DURG	1502	28811	GARIYABAND	1460	17712	JANJGIR-CHAMPA	2254	28913	JASHPUR	4165	101314	KABIRDHAM	1678	8815	KANKER	2077	20016	KONDAGAON	1796	37217	KORBA	2548	29118	KOREA	1793	23119	MAHASAMUND	1733	9320	MUNGELI	1075	6221	NARAYANPUR	555	14322	RAIGARH	3406	46523	RAIPUR	1877	46124	RAJNANDGAON	2581	12925	SUKMA	854	20926	SURAJPUR	1973	44227	SURGUJA	2290	714Total	49832	8019
