                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

346






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Impersonating Voices of Judges




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Delhi Police has received any representation or complaint from Supreme Court Registrar Office in relation to impersonating voices of judges, ifso, the details thereof;(b) whether any First Information Report (FIR) or complaint has been registered in Tilak Marg Police Station in relation3+6 to impersonating voices of judges; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a): Delhi Police has reported that no such complaint has been received from Supreme Court Registrar during the years 2017 to 2021 (upto 21.11.2021). (b) and (c): Delhi Police has reported that no complaint/FIR has been registered in Tilak Marg Police Station during the years 2017 to 2021 (upto 21.11.2021) in relation to impersonating voices of judges. *****
