                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4389






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has set a target to operationalise 4,200 AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) under Ayushman Bharat Scheme during 2019- 2020; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the total number of operational AYUSH HWCs in the State of Andhra Pradesh and the funds released to the State in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c):       A total 1738 AYUSH HWCs are proposed to be established in year                    2019-20 by upgradation of existing State Government AYUSH dispensaries and Sub Health Centres. Upgradation of 1037 AYUSH dispensaries to HWCs have been approved (in year 2019-20) under the existing provision of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) and Rs. 90.20 Crore have been released to the States/UTs till date. State-wise status of approved Health and Wellness Centres is furnished at Annexure.Further, 42 AYUSH dispensaries have been approved for upgradation as HWCs as per the proposal received from Andhra Pradesh State and total Rs. 3.88 Crore have been released to the State for this purpose. The operational AYUSH HWC’s are Nil as the funds are yet to be released by the State Government to Andhra Pradesh State AYUSH Society.………………..
