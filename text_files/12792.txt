Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme




Anupriya Patel














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State-wise and year wise expenditure incurred under the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme since its introduction in August 2016;(b)	the sectoral/industry-wise details of number of apprentices engaged from 2016 till date;(c)	the sectoral/industry-wise details of apprentices under the scheme who have gained full-time employment; and(d)	the details of the entities that have enrolled as Basic Training Providers under the scheme?
