                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

473






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Losses due to Agitations




S.R. Parthiban













                                                    Su Thirunavukkarasar
                                                








                                                    Vijayakumar (alias) Vijay Vasanth
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether a number of train services in the country have been blocked by agitating farmers and other organizations in the last few months;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the estimated loss incurred by Railways as a result thereof, Zone/Division-wise; (c)	the other steps taken by the Railways to facilitate cancellation/refund of ticket fare of the stranded passengers;(d)	whether the Railways has also proposed to start regular train services as per pre-Covid fares; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the time by when the above proposal is likely to be implemented?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY      (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
