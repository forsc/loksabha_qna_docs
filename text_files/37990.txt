                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5285






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Teachers Education Programme in Rajasthan




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is running four years Integrated Teacher Education Programme for the last few years and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of the institutions selected in Rajasthan for the implementation of the said programme; (c) whether the Government is paying full attention to the quality of education in two yearly and four yearly teacher’s education programmes; and (d) if not, the steps being taken by the Government to improve the quality of education in these colleges?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT ANNPURNA DEVI) (a) to (d) NCTE has notified a four-year Integrated Teachers Education Programme (ITEP) vide notification in the Gazette of India on 26.10.2021 to be offered in pilot mode initially in selected multi-disciplinary institutions across the country. However, NCTE has not invited applications from any of the institutes for the 4-Year ITEP. The details of the four year ITEP are available and may be seen by visiting the following link:  https://ncte.gov.in/WebAdminFiles/RecentAnnouncement/0_27_10_2021_637709061829643386.pdf. NEP recognizes that the teachers require training in high-quality content as well as pedagogy. NEP 2020 focuses on teacher education and by 2030, aims to move teacher education into multidisciplinary colleges and universities. With colleges and universities moving towards becoming multidisciplinary, the aim is to house outstanding education departments offering B.Ed., M.Ed., and Ph.D. degrees in education. 4-year integrated B.Ed. degree envisages to teach a range of knowledge content, pedagogy and provide strong practicum training.*****
