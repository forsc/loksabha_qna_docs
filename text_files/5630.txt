                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5100






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Procurement of Naval Helicopters




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Indian Navy is in the process of procuring utility naval helicopters for its fleet;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c) the names and the details of the main shortlisted companies for this procurement for Indian Navy;(d) whether it is a fact that Airbus which is already under cloud across the globe for reportedly corrupt practices is also likely to participate in process; and(e)  if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (c):  Yes, Sir.  Acceptance of Necessity (AoN) for procurement of 111 Naval Utility Helicopters (NUH) was accorded by Defence Acquisition Council (DAC) on 01 Nov, 2018 under the Strategic Partnership Model.  Request for Expression of Interest (REoI) to Original Equipment Manufacturers and Strategic Partners (SPs) were issued on 12 Feb, 2019.(d) & (e):  The capital procurement of Defence Equipment including NUH is carried out as per the provisions of Defence Procurement Procedure.*****
