                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

13






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Phase III of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number of districts in the country where the phase-III of the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana launched in January, 2021 has been started;(b) 	the targets fixed along with the amount of funds estimated to be spent on said phase-III;(c) 	the number of persons benefitted under Phase-I and Phase-II of the said scheme along with the amount of funds spent in this regard; and(d)	the State-wise details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (D) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 13 TO BE ANSWERED ON 19.07.2021 ASKED BY DR. MANOJ RAJORIA REGARDING PHASE III OF PRADHAN MANTRI KAUSHALVIKAS YOJANA(a)	Under Phase III of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana 2020-22 (PMKVY 2020-22), as on 10.07.2021, the training started in 607 districts across 32 States/Union Territories. (b)       Under Phase III of PMKVY (2020-22), the target is to train 8 lakh candidates with an outlay of Rs. 948.90 crore. (c) and (d)      Under Phase I (2015-16) and Phase II (2016-20) of PMKVY,  19.85 lakh and 1.08 crore candidates (as on 10.07.2021) have been trained/oriented with an expenditure of Rs. 1,174.48 crore and Rs. 7,438.23 crore, respectively.
