                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2695






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Yuva Sahakar Scheme




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) has launched a new scheme ‘Yuva Sahakar’, giving wings to young entrepreneurs in cooperatives;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the number of young entrepreneurs benefitted in Gujarat, district-wise;(c) 	whether the National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) has provided loan/incentive/subsidy to cooperative institutes for development of cooperative infrastructure in the country; and(d) 	if so, the details of such assistance/funds given by the Government to the said institutes during each of the last three years and the current year, institute and State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a): 	Yes Sir, National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) has recently launched ‘Yuva Sahakar’ Scheme to support young entrepreneurs through Cooperatives.(b):	The details of the scheme are given at Annexure-1. No Cooperative from Gujarat has so far applied for assistance under the scheme ‘Yuva Sahakar’. (c):	Yes Sir.(d):	The details of the assistance / funds sanctioned and released to States/UTs/ Institutions for the last three years and the current year are given at Annexure-2.
