                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Expansion of PMJAY Scheme




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is contemplating to further expand the scope of PM Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) health insurance cover to include the middle class people and who don‘t have cover of their own;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of modalities of the expanded scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	At present, there is no such proposal.Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) covers       around 10.74 core poor and vulnerable families identified as per Socio Economic Caste Census database. Some of the States/UTs have implemented State Schemes in alliance with the AB-PMJAY to cover additional population of the state at their own cost.  Accordingly, as of 21.09.2020, over 13.13 crore families are covered under the scheme.
