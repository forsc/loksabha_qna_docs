                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3791






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Decongesting Heavy Loaded Trains




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to divert any train from Chennai to Trivandrum via Madurai, Sengottai, Punalur in order to decongest the existing heavily loaded train route via Kadpadi, Salem, Coimbatore;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)to(c) No, Sir. Diversion of train running from Chennai to Thiruvananthapuram Central via Katpadi- Salem- Coimbatore instead of Madurai- Sengottai- Punalur is operationally not feasible, at present, due to single line section between Madurai and Virudunagar.  *****
