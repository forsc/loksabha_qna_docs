                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4420






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Discontinuation of NSSF Loan to FCI




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to discontinue the National Small Saving Fund (NSSF) Loan to FCI for Food Subsidy and the Budget Provisions have been made accordingly; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor and views of States of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): 	Yes Sir, in the para 143 of Budget Speech 2021-22, it has been proposed to discontinue the National Small Saving Fund (NSSF) Loan to Food Corporation of India (FCI) for food subsidy and accordingly budget provisions have been made in Revised Estimate (RE) 2020-21 and Budget Estimate(BE) 2021-22. For food subsidy to FCI, budget provision has been increased to Rs.3,44,077 crore in RE 2020-21 from BE 2020-21 of Rs.77,983 crore. In BE 2021-22, budget allocation of Rs.2,02,616 crore has been made for this purpose. NSSF loan is given by the Government of India to FCI only and not to the State Governments per se.*****
