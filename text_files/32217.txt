                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3545






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Skill Development of Street Vendors




Sarmistha Sethi













                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        whether any proposal have been launched under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) for skill development of street vendors to help them generate more revenue, if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Bundelkhand, Uttar Pradesh and Odisha; (b)        the details of the employment opportunities created for street vendors in the country including Bundelkhand in this regard; (c)        whether the Government proposes to extend the programme for the street vendors in Odisha and Uttar Pradesh; if so, the details thereof and it not the reasons therefor;






ANSWER




                                                        (a)        Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) in organised as well as unorganised sectors across the country. Under PMKVY, more than 20 thousand street food vendors have been benefited covering 18 States/UTs including State of Uttar Pradesh and Odisha. Under placement linked Short Term Training (STT), 3,336 and 1,403 candidates have been certified and placed, respectively. The State-wise number of candidates (street food vendors) trained, certified and placed under PMKVY-STT and oriented and certified under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL), as on 21.11.2021, are given at Annexure. Further, Ministry of Skill development and Entrepreneurship has launched a pilot project for ‘Upskilling for Street Food Vendors (for e-cart license)’ in East Delhi Municipal Corporation (EDMC) on 30.11.2021 to train over 2500 street food vendors over a period of 4 months. The purpose of this skill development is to provide skills which would lead to better services to consumers, more opportunities to the vendors in revenue and awareness of regulations, and adherence to stipulated rules and better services for the local bodies. Candidates would be provided with the dual certification from Tourism & Hospitality Skill Council and Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). (b)        Above reply (a) may be referred to. (c)        Above reply (a) may be referred to.
