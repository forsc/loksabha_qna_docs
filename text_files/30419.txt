                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1970






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Review of Ongoing/Pending Projects




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has decided to review ongoing/pending projects under Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country particularly in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the total number of such projects sanctioned and pending in the State as on date;(c ) 	whether the Government is aware that the various projects are pending due to non-sanction of bank loans in Maharashtra; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by Government to review such projects with Maharashtra and the concerned banks?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b): 	Ministry of MSME has a robust system of monitoring and reviewing its schemes. These are on real time and online/offline basis spread over the entire financial year. In the State of Maharashtra, the  Ministry of MSME runs several schemes, the details of, which are as below:<pre>S. No.	Scheme	No. of Units Sanctioned	No. of Units pending1	Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)	43119 nos. of Micro Enterprises have been set up since inception by disbursing margin money subsidy of Rs.927.10 Cr. (as on 31.03.2021).	6493 applications are pending for consideration by implementing agencies (as on 31.03.2021).2	Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI)	25 Clusters benefitting 13852 artisans assisted Rs. 55.92 cr. (as on 28.05.2021)	10 out of 25 Clusters are already functional.3	Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)	Issued Rs.55.86 Lakh guarantee amounting to Rs. 2.86 Lakh Cr.  as on date.	Nil at the level of CGTMSE.4	Micro & Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP)	9 nos. of project are ongoing as on date.	NIL</pre>(c) & (d): 	As per the data provided by Reserve Bank of India (RBI) as on 31.03.2021, a cumulative amount of Rs. 352894.81 Cr. is outstanding credit in 39.94 Lakh MSME units by Scheduled Commercial Banks in the State of Maharashtra.
