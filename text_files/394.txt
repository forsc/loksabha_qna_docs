                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Stipend to Apprentices




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the stipend paid to the apprentices have been linked with the minimum wages applicable to the respective State where the establishments have located;b)	if so, the details thereof;c)	the details of the stipend payable for the apprentices for their training period applicable under the Apprentices Act;d)	the details of the stipend paid for the apprentices engaged having completed their Diploma and Graduate in various engineering disciplines; ande)	the comparable statement for the stipend drawn by the apprentices in hand having completed their Industrial Training Institute (ITI) trades after X class and the Graduate or Diploma engineers engaged as apprentices?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	 Yes Sir. The stipend paid to the trade apprentices have been linked with the minimum wages of semi-skilled workers applicable in the respective State where the establishments are located. The details are provided below in the table. This stipend is payable to the apprentices during their apprenticeship training period as defined under the Apprentices Act, 1961 (amended 2014).Minimum stipend to be paid to the apprentices	1st Year	70% of minimum wage of semi-skilled workers notified by the respective State or Union territory	2nd Year	80% of minimum wage of semi-skilled workers notified by the respective State or Union territory	3rd Year	90% of minimum wage of semi-skilled workers notified by the respective State or Union territory.(d)	The stipend payable for the technician apprentices engaged after having completed their Diploma is INR 3542 per month and for Graduate apprentices in various engineering disciplines is INR 4984.(e)	The comparison between the stipend drawn by the apprentices who  have completed their Industrial Training Institute (ITI) trades after X class and the Graduate or Diploma engineers engaged in apprentices is given in the table below:Three categories of cities – A, B and C	Average minimum wagesfor the semi-skilled workers in INR	Average Stipend rate  for trade apprentices (1st year at 70% rate)in INR*	Stipend rate for graduate apprentices in INR	Stipend rate for Technician (Diploma) Apprentices in INRA	16042		11229.4	4984	3542B	  13702	9591.4	4984	3542C	11362	7953.4	4984	3542* This is indicative and will vary from State to State.
