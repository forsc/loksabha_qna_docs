                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2312






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Beneficiaries under PMAY-Grameen




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of beneficiaries covered under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Grameen (PMAY-G) in the State of Uttar Pradesh, district-wise;(b) 	the number of beneficiaries to whom money has been sanctioned in the State of Uttar Pradesh, district-wise;(c) 	the completion percentage of the houses in Uttar Pradesh out of the beneficiaries to whom money has been sanctioned, district-wise; and(d) 	the time by which the Government intends to achieve 100 per cent completion of the houses for the selected beneficiaries?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c)	The total number of houses allocated to Uttar Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G) is 26,15,951. Out of this, as on 29.07.2021, 25,20,478 houses have been sanctioned and first instalment has been released to 24,34,626 beneficiaries. The district-wise total number of houses sanctioned, number of beneficiaries to whom funds have been released (atleast first installment) and completion percentage of houses (75.65%) out of beneficiaries to whom funds have been released in the State of Uttar Pradesh under PMAY-G as on 29.07.2021 are given in Annexure. (d)	The houses sanctioned to the eligible beneficiaries of Uttar Pradesh are targeted for completion by March, 2022.***** AnnexureSTATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (a), (b) & (c) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2312 TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.08.2021 REGARDING BENEFICIARIES UNDER PMAY-GRAMEENDistrict-wise total number of houses sanctioned, number of beneficiaries to whom money has been released (atleast first installment) and completion percentage of houses out of beneficiaries to whom money has been released in the State of Uttar Pradesh:
