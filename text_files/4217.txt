                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3725






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Training Programmes in FPIs




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to explore the option of commercialization and market orientation methods in the Food Processing Industries (FPI) through Public Private Partnerships (PPP) and collaborations, if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government proposes to conduct training programmes for skill development of youth in different segments of FPIs?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a): 	The Ministry of Food Processing Industries does not set up any food processing industries/ units/projects/plants on its own or through any partnership or collaboration. Under the Central Sector Scheme – PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA - (PMKSY), it provides financial assistance as capita subsidy in the form of grants-in-aid to the individuals, farmers, Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), Entrepreneurs, Cooperatives, Societies, Self Help Groups (SHGs), Private Companies and Central/ State PSUs etc. for setting up of food processing industries/ units/ projects in the country. The schemes under PMKSY are demand driven and financial assistance is provided to the eligible applicants against the Expression of Interest issued by the MoFPI from time to time.  (b):   	The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is already funding skill development training programmes for various sectors including food processing under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY).*****
