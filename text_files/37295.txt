                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PLANNING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4488






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Review of Ayushman Bharat Scheme




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





PLANNING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether NITI Aayog has reviewed the Government’s Ayushman Bharat Scheme and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the people suffering from various serious diseases are not getting the benefits under the Ayushman Bharat Scheme and if so, the reasons therefor; and (c)	whether NITI Aayog proposes to send the corrective points in the Ayushman Bharat Scheme to the concerned Ministry so that the people can get complete health benefits under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS & PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION; MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a)	No, Sir.(b)	As per the scheme, benefits are provided to all who are eligible under the scheme irrespective of the seriousness of the disease.(c)	CEO, NITI Aayog is a part of the Governing Board of the National Health Authority and has been providing inputs as and when required.*****
