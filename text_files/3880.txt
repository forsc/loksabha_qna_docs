15.07.2019






Vigilant System to Track Fraudulent Activities




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is proposing a new vigilance system to track the fraudulent activities of corporate companies across the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of number of companies identified indulging in fraudulent activities and the nature of fraud committed by them during each year of the past 4 years including the current year, year-wise and State-wise; (d)	the action taken/to be taken against such fraudulent companies; and (e)	if not, the reasons therefor?

SFIO has been given vast powers under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 including the power to arrest. Based on a well laid down process, cases that involve prima facie findings of fraud are referred to SFIO.
