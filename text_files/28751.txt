                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

35






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Har Medh Par Pedh




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether with a view to promote forestry, the Government is working on ‘Har Medh Par Ped’ Scheme; and (b) 	if so, the year-wise and State-wise details of the implementation of the said scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) & (b):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.  35 DUE FOR REPLY ON 30TH NOVEMBER, 2021.(a): 	Yes, Sir. ‘Har Medh Par Ped’ Scheme is being implemented in those States that have liberalized felling and transit-regulations. Presently, the scheme is being implemented in 23 States/UTs with a view to encourage agro forestry i.e. tree plantation on farm land along with crops/ cropping system to help the farmer get additional income and make their farming system more climate resilient and adaptive. (b):      The detail of year-wise physical achievements under the scheme from 2016-17 to 2020-21 is as under:Parameters	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	TotalNo of Nurseries Established	85	206	297	126	94	808Area Coverage (In Ha)	2586.2	24195.07	26344	15759	24925	93809.27Total Plantation (Lakh Trees)	9.138	117.7	103.66	66	105.1	401.598The details of release of funds to the concerned State/UTs under the scheme over the years is at Annexure-I.
