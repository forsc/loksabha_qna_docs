                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Khelo India Scheme




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any proposal from the Government of Madhya Pradesh for Central assistance under Khelo India Scheme;(b) if so, the details including the current status of the proposal; and(c) the reasons for the delay in the construction of synthetic athletic track at Itarsi in Hoshangabad district, Madhya Pradesh for the training of players?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) to (c) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. †159 TO BE ANSWERED ON 28.11.2019 REGARDING “KHELO INDIA SCHEME” ASKED BY SHRI UDAY PRATAP SINGH, HON’BLE MEMBER OF LOK SABHA(a) and (b) This Ministry till date has received a total of thirteen proposals from the Government of Madhya Pradesh under Khelo India Scheme. The current status of all proposals received from the Government of Madhya Pradesh under Khelo India Scheme is at Annexure – I.(c) ‘Sports’ being a State subject the responsibility for providing sports infrastructure rests with the State/Union Territory Governments. Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports only supplements their efforts by bridging gaps in infrastructure. Madhya Pradesh has got adequate synthetic athletic tracks, shooting ranges and water sports facilities where larger number of athletes could be trained with higher quality coaches. Therefore, the proposal for synthetic athletic track at Itarsi was not found feasible for funding by this Ministry.ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) AND (b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. †159 TO BE ANSWERED ON 28.11.2019 REGARDING “KHELO INDIA SCHEME” ASKED BY SHRI UDAY PRATAP SINGH, HON’BLE MEMBER OF LOK SABHA**********
