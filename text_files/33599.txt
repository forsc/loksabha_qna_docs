                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1021






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Growth Rate of Livestock Sector




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is having any proposal for revising and realigning of various components in the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying in the near future; (b) if so, the details thereof and the details of budget provision made for these schemes; (c) whether the Government has any proposal for boosting the growth of livestock sector in the country, if so, the details thereof and the target fixed for the growth in livestock sector thereby; and (d) the details of growth rate of livestock sector in the country till date?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) to (d) Recently, the Government has revised and realigned various components of following existing schemes in order to have focussed development of animal husbandry and dairying across the country for next 5 years starting from 2021-22 with the outlay of Rs.9800 crore over a duration of 5 years for leveraging total investment of Rs. 54,618 crore for 5 years:-  I.	Rashtriya Gokul MissionII.	National Programme for Dairy Development III.	Support to Dairy Cooperatives and Farmers Production Organisation engaged in dairy activitiesIV.	National Livestock MissionV.	Livestock Census and Integrated Sample SurveyVI.	Livestock Health & Disease ControlLivestock Sector in our country has been growing at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 7.93% (at constant prices) from 2014-15 to 2020-21. Milk production, Egg Production and Meat Production in the country are growing at compound annual growth rate of 6.20%, 7.64% and 4.67% respectively from 2014-15 to 2020-21 (Provisional). As per National Account Statistics 2021, The Gross Value Added and its growth rate of livestock sector during last three years is given as under:	GVA (in Crore Rs.)	Growth Rate (%)	2018-19***	2019-20#	2020-21@	2018-19*	2019-20#	2020-21@At constant (2011-12) basic prices	5,40,970	5,81,450	6,17,117	7.97	6.96	5.78***: Third Revised Estimates; #: Second Revised Estimates; @: First Revised Estimates; source: NAD, NSO, MoSPI.****
