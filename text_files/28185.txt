                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Funds for BADP Projects




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of projects undertaken in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts under Border Area Development Programme (BADP) Fund; and(b)	the details of total amount sanctioned under various BADP projects during the year 2014 till date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NISITH PRAMANIK)(a):    As per the available information, details of projects sanctioned under BADP since 2014-2015 for the districts of Darjeeling and Kalimpong in West Bengal are as under:  Sl. No.	Sector	Project Sanctioned (in nos.)		Darjeeling	Kalimpong1	Agriculture Infrastructure	20	12	DWS Projects	97	743	Education Infrastructure	56	144	Health Infrastructure	24	25	Livelihood Projects	12	36	Roads and Bridges	159	337	Social Sector Infrastructure	57	158	Sports Infrastructure	20	-	Total	445	142-2-LS.USQ. NO.3563 FOR 10.08.2021(b):  During the period 2014-15 to 2020-21, a total of Rs.5564.17 crore has been sanctioned to 16 States and 2 Union Territories for projects approved under BADP.             Out of this amount, Rs.690.11 crore has been released to State Government of West Bengal for approved projects under BADP during the relevant period.  ******
