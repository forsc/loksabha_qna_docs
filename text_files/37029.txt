                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Cold Storage in West Bengal




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of cold storage facilities in West Bengal, district-wise; (b)	whether the Government has shut down any cold storage in the State; (c)	if so, the reasons therefor; (d)	whether the Government intends to set up new cold storage in Bishnupur Constituency of Bankura district; and(e)	if so, the time by which the Government intends to do the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTRY OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): 	As per report received from the State Government of West Bengal, total number of cold storages in the State is 580. District-wise number of cold storage facilities is at Annexure.(b) & (c):   As per report received from the State Government of West Bengal, Government has not shut down any cold storage in the State.(d):	Central Government does not setup cold storage of its own. However, Government is implementing various schemes under which financial assistance is available for setting up of cold storage in the country including West Bengal. These schemes are demand/entrepreneur driven for which financial assistance is available in the form of credit linked back ended subsidy. Government of West Bengal reported that it does not intend to set up new cold storage in Bishnupur Constituency of Bankura district.(e):	Question does not arise.
