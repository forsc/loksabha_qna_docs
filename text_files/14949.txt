                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3593






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Funds Allocated for Development of Culture in Rajasthan




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has provided funds for any scheme or made any effort for promoting/ developing the culture of Rajasthan;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the year-wise amount provided for the promotion/development of culture in Rajasthan during the last three years; and(d)	the details of the promotion/ development works undertaken in Rajasthan with the said amount?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	to (d): The Ministry  of Culture provides assistance for various cultural programmes and projects in the country  through  various central sector schemes.  These schemes are also applicable for the State of Rajasthan. Funds allocated/utilized under various central sector schemes during the last three years in the country including Rajasthan are as under :                                                                                                         (Rs. in crores)Year	Fund allocated	Fund utilised 2017-18	61.00	43.042018-19	65.76	48.332019-20	42.69	35.30
