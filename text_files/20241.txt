                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1734






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Bulk Data Sharing Policy & Procedure




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of Bulk Data Sharing Policy & Procedure of the Ministry;(b) 	the revenue collected by the Ministry by providing access to VAHAN and SARATHI database;(c) 	whether the said revenue is also shared with the States;(d) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh; (e) 	the list of Government and Private entities which have access to the VAHAN and SARATHI databases; and(f)	whether the Government proposes to demand deletion of all the data from private players and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	Bulk data sharing Policy & Procedure has been scrapped by this Ministry.(b)	The revenue collected by the Government by providing access to VAHAN and SARATHI database is Rupees 1,11,38,79,757.(c) and (d)	No, it is not shared with the States.(e)	The list of Government and Private entities which have access to the VAHAN and SARATHI databases is attached as Annexure.(f)	No such proposal is under consideration.
