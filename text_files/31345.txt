                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2546






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






FPI Cold Storage in Food Processing Sector




Geeta Kora













                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                








                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                








                                                    Shardaben Anilbhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps being taken to increase the number of cold storage as an important component to reduce the damage  to  yield  after  harvesting  in  food  processing sector, State-wise and the details thereof;(b)	whether any budget  has  been  allocated  and funds released so far in this regard; and(c)	if so, the details of the funds released, district-wise and State-wise including Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a)  to (c): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) does not support setting up of stand alone cold storages. However, it provides Grants in Aid for cold storage infrastructure as part of food processing projects under relevant component schemes of Central Sector Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY). As part of 41 Mega Food Parks, Ministry has approved  setting up of cold storage and deep freeze capacity of 2.3 LMT and as part of 350 Integrated Cold Chain projects, Ministry has approved creation of cold storage capacity of 10.02 LMT across the country under corresponding component schemes of PMKSY. Being Central Sector  and demand driven scheme, no state wise allocation is made under PMKSY.Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is implementing Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for development of Horticulture in the country including Jharkhand & Madhya Pradesh under which financial assistance is available, inter-alia, for setting up of Post Harvest Management (PHM) infrastructure including establishment of cold storages (Construction, Expansion and Modernization) etc. The component is demand/ entrepreneur driven for which assistance at the rate 35% of the eligible project cost in general areas and 50% in hilly & scheduled areas, is available as credit linked back ended subsidy through respective State Horticulture Missions. State-wise details of funds allocated and released under MIDH for various activities including creation of cold storage facilities to various States during last two years and current year is placed at Annexure. *****
