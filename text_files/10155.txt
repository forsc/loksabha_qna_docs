                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3635






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Misuse of PMAY Funds




Sukhbir Singh Badal














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any complaints regarding misuse of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Funds in Punjab;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government has initiated any action in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c) :  The complaints regarding irregularities in implementation and misuse of funds under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) in States/Union Territories (UTs) including Punjab are received in the Ministry. Since the State Governments/ UTs are implementing PMAY-G on the ground, the complaints received in the Ministry are forwarded to the concerned State Government/UT for redressal of the grievances under intimation to this Ministry. As per the report received from the State Government of Punjab, no complaint captioned as “Misuse of PMAY funds” has been received.
