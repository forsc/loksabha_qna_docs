                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1209






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






PM-KISAN Scheme Transactions




Mohammad Jawed













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Pradyut Bordoloi
                                                








                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                








                                                    K. Muraleedharan
                                                








                                                    Sisir Kumar Adhikari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme transactions that have taken place since its launch till June, 2021, month-wise;(b)	the total number of PM-KISAN scheme transactions which have failed since its launch till June, 2021, month-wise;(c)	whether the Government has identified the reasons for failed transactions;(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e)	the steps being taken to ensure that beneficiaries receive their installments in situations where transactions have failed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Under the PM-KISAN Scheme benefits are transferred in three, four-monthly periods. In every four months period, Rs. 2000/- is transferred to eligible farmers families. Therefore, benefits of Rs. 6000/- is transferred annually. The state-wise total number of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme transactions that have taken place since its launch till June, 2021 is enclosed at Annexure-I.(b):	Under PM-KISAN Scheme the benefits are not transferrable every month. The benefits are transferred in three installments. The cumulative number of PM-KISAN Scheme transactions which have failed since its launch till June, 2021 is 40,16,867. The number of total transaction made under PM-KISAN Scheme till 30th June, 2021 is 68,76,32,104 of which the total number of failed transaction is less than 1%.(c) & (d):	Yes, Sir. Various reasons have been identified by the Union Government for the transactions failure under PM-KISAN Scheme such as Account closed/Transferred, Invalid IFSC, Account Inactive, Account Dormant, Amount exceeds limit set on Account by Bank for Credit/Debit per transaction, Account Holder Expired, Account Blocked or Frozen, Inactive Aadhar, Network failure etc.  (e):	To tackle the issues of transaction failures and process the payment to such registered farmers’ families, a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) has been developed and issued to States/UTs for necessary action at their end.  In cases where the corrective measures are to be taken by States/UTs, such transaction failure records are opened to respective State/UT for correction under the “Correction Module” tab of PM-KISAN portal. Thereafter, all the transaction failure records are reprocessed for the payment of the respective installment under PM-KISAN Scheme.
