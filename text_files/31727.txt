                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2777






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Tele-density in Odisha




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the tele-density in Odisha, both in rural and urban areas; (b) 	the status of implementation of Phase II of the Provision of ‘Mobile Services in Left Wing Extremism Affected Areas’ scheme, and the details thereof; (c) 	the number of mobile towers projected to be constructed in Odisha under Phase II of the aforementioned scheme; (d) 	the number of mobile towers requested to be constructed in Odisha by the State Government of Odisha under Phase II of the aforementioned scheme; and (e) 	the number of mobile towers projected to be constructed under Phase I of the aforementioned scheme, as compared to the number of mobile towers actually constructed, state/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)          The tele-density in service area of Odisha in rural and urban areas as on 30.09.2021 is 61.87% and 144.14% respectively.(b) to (d)         Under Phase II of the scheme for Provision of ‘Mobile Services in Left Wing Extremism (LWE) Affected Areas’, work has been awarded for installation of 2542 mobile towers including 483 towers in Odisha. As per the list of tower locations provided by Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) to Department of Telecommunications (DoT), 483 number of mobile towers are projected to be installed in Odisha and contract has been awarded for installation of 483 towers.(e)           Phase-I of the LWE scheme had 2355 mobile towers of which 2343 mobile towers have been installed. The details are as below:<pre>S.No.	State	No. of tower location1	Andhra Pradesh	622	Bihar	2503	Chattisgarh	5254	Jharkhand	8165	Madhya Pradesh	226	Maharashtra	657	Odisha	2568	West Bengal	969	Uttar Pradesh	7810	Telangana	173	Grand Total	2343</pre>           12 towers (7 in Chhattisgarh and 5 in Odisha) could not be installed due to naxal activity and forest clearance permission. *********
