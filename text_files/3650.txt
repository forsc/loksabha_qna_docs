                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






All India Institute of Ayurveda at Tirupati




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is planning to set up All India Insititute of Ayurveda at Tirupati as per the terms of Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of the funds allocated for it and the time by which it is likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):     Presently there is no proposal under 	consideration in the Ministry of AYUSH forsetting up   an All India Institute of Ayurveda at Tirupati. (b) & (c): Does not arise.………………….
