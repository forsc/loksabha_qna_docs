                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3890






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Transfer of Defence Land




Bhagwanth Khuba














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the total area of defence land acquired by the Government after independence and the State and area-wise total land under the management of Defence Estate Organisation (DEO) as on date;(b) the estimated area of defence land which has been transferred to other agencies/institutions, till date, institution-wise;(c)  whether the Government has approved transfer of defence land to Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan for the construction of KVs in some parts of the country recently and if so, the details including the terms and conditions thereof; and(d)  the steps taken/being taken by the Government to streamline the data of defence land in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) The total area of land acquired by the Ministry of Defence after Independence in the country and State and area-wise total defence land under the management of Defence Estates Organisation is enclosed as Annexure-A.(b) The total area of defence land transferred to other agencies/ institutions, by way of permanent transfer, lease or license basis since 2016-17 till date (as on 30.06.2019), institution- wise, is enclosed as Annexure-B....2/-: 2 :(c) In last 03 years, Government approved transfer of defence land to Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan for construction of Kendriya Vidyalayas in 6 cases. Details are enclosed as Annexure-C. The land transfer was on lease in perpetuity at an annual token rent of Re. 1/-.(d) The Government has taken several steps as below:(i) Computerization of Defence land records- Raksha Bhoomi Software: The computerization of defence land records under the project ‘Raksha Bhoomi’ was undertaken by the Defence Estates Organization in association with NIC. Defence land records contained in General Land Registers (GLRs) for defence land inside cantonments and Military Land Registers (MLRs) for defence land outside cantonments were entered in the Raksha Bhoomi software which was made operational after verification and authentication of database in July, 2011.The data is updated periodically and shared with user services.(ii) Digitization of Files and Documents for Protection and Preservation of Land Records: The Department also undertook scanning, indexing and digital preservation of defence land related records. As on date approx. 4 lakh files have been scanned and digitized. The digitization of records has been made a continuing process.(iii)  For physical verification of Defence Lands: Demarcation, physical survey of entire defence land, outside Cantonments and detailed polygon survey of defence land inside Cantonments have already been undertaken. About 98.95% and 100% survey work has been completed for defence outside cantonments and that inside Cantonments respectively. 1154 survey reports have been finalized/issued in respect of defence lands.(iv) Integration of Land Survey Maps with BHUVAN: The maps of defence land pockets generated in the recently carried out land surveys (2012-13 to 2016-17) are being integrated with satellite maps of National Remote Sensing Centre under ISRO (NRSC) i.e. BHUVAN for assigning geo-coordinates and integration with Raksha Bhoomi database.(v) Archival Unit and Resource Centre (AU&RC): As part of a multi-pronged approach and disaster management strategy, a central facility has been created for preservation and back up and to provide access to all user organizations. An Archival Unit & Resource Center has been operational since 2012 in Raksha Sampada Bhawan New Delhi. Another AU&RC constructed in Kirkee Cantonment for Southern Command is also functioning.*****
