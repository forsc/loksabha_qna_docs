                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

761






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Waste Processing and Recycling in NCR




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount of waste processed and quantity of recycled end products produced by Construction and Demolition (C & D) waste treatment plants in National Capital Region (NCR);(b) 	whether it is mandatory for the Government agencies to procure a fraction of their materials from the stock of recycled products from C&D Plants and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the details of the agencies that are running the C & D waste  plants in NCR along with their financial performance, year-wise;(d) 	whether the Government has considered to nationalise and manage the C & D waste plants in the near future; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefore and whether the Government has assessed the advantages of doing so and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) :  The amount of waste generated in National Capital Territory (NCT) is 10,500 Metric Tons per day (MT/D), out of which 5,775 MT/D is processed. The figures of waste generated and processed for National Capital Region (NCR) are not separately available.   As per the Annual Report for the year 2018-19 of Delhi Pollution Control Committee (DPCC), the quantity of recycled end products produced by the three functional Construction and Demolition (C & D) waste treatment plants in National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi is as under: -S.No.	Name of the recycled product	      Quantity produced during 2018-191.	Non-structural concrete aggregate	1,38,613 MT2.	Paving blocks	                        14,831 m33.	Granular Sub-Base (GSB)                 52,955 MT4.	Soil	                                2,00,511 MT5.	Cement Concrete Bricks	                3,985 m3The figures for National Capital Region (NCR) are not separately available. (b) 	As per C&D Waste Management Rules, 2016 procurement of materials made from C&D waste is mandatory to a certain percentage (say 10 to 20 %) in municipal and Government contracts subject to strict quality control. (c) 	The three Construction and Demolition (C & D) waste treatment plants running in National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi are working under Public Private Partnership (PPP) framework with Infrastructure Leasing & Financial Services Limited (IL&FSL). (d) & (e) – No, Sir. As per the Constitution of India, sanitation is a State subject.                                 *****
