                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2618






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Capacity of Oil Refineries




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total refining capacity of the public andprivate Sector oil refineries, company and sector-wisealong with the consumption of petroleum in the country;(b) whether there is decline in the refining capacityin public and private sector; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)There are total 23 refineries in the country consisting of 19 set up by Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs), 1 by a Joint Venture Company and 3 by Private Companies with a total refining capacity of 249.216 million metric tons per annum (MMTPA).
