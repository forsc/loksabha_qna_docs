                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3023






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Traditional Medicine in Medical Colleges




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that the Government is considering to include traditional medicine in the courses/syllabus of all the medical colleges during the first three years of education, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether it is a fact that 80 per cent of Ayurvedic doctors ended up practicing allopathy; (c) if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; and (d) whether it is a fact that the Government is considering to ban all type of medical plants, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):       No such proposal has been initiated yet, for this purpose.(b) & (c):   No such reports have been received so far.(d):       No such proposal has been initiated.………………..
