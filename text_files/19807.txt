                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1040






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Dekho Apna Desh




(Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria













                                                    Nayab Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps taken by the Government to promote rich heritage of India under Webinar Series “Dekho Apna Desh” along with the names of tourist places; (b) 	if so, the names of tourist places identified and the State-wise details thereof including Haryana and Uttar Pradesh; (c) 	the names of tourist places where circuit tourist train is being run by the Government so far; (d) 	whether the Government contemplates to run circuit tourist train in Etawah Lion Safari; and (e) 	if so, the time by which is likely to be done and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) and (b):		The Ministry of Tourism has launched the Dekho Apna Desh initiative in January 2020 with the objective of creating awareness among the citizens about the rich heritage and culture of the country and to promote domestic tourism. Under this initiative Ministry is carrying out promotional activities like webinars, online pledge, road shows, talks and quiz programmes. Dekho Apna Desh initiative is being promoted extensively on social media accounts and website of the Ministry and by Domestic India Tourism offices.The Ministry is organising a series of Webinars under the overall theme of ‘Dekho Apna Desh’ with the objective of promoting various tourism destinations of India. The Webinars include glimpses of the culture, heritage, handicrafts and cuisine of the destinations, in addition to the tourist spots. Concepts of Responsible Tourism, Accessible Tourism, Wildlife, Trekking, Bicycling and Motor Cycle Tours, etc. are also been promoted through the Webinars. A total of 75 Webinars have been organised till date. A repository of all the Webinars is being maintained by the Ministry and YouTube links to the Webinars are made available on the website of Ministry of Tourism.(c) to (e):	Indian Railways Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is operating Bharat Darshan train on pan India tourist destinations. The list of tourist places/destinations where the train is operational is enclosed as Annexure.IRCTC plans and operates packages based on demand assessments. Sufficient demand for Etawah Lion Safari has not arisen, therefore IRCTC has not included Etawah Lion Safari as tourist destination in its itineraries.********
