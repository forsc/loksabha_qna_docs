                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1090






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






PHCs in Tamil Nadu




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether Cuddalore district of Tamil Nadu, which is one of the thickly populated districts of Tamil Nadu and has nearly 30 lakh people but have only 64 PHCs, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has any plan to establish more PHCs in Kattumannarkoil Taluk of Cuddalore district; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Cuddalore District in State of Tamil Nadu has population of 29,02,078 with 13 Blocks. Following Health Institutions are functioning in the district: •	64 Primary Health Centres (PHCs).•	7 Urban PHCs.•	319 Health Sub Centres.•	1 Government District Head Quarters Hospitals.•	8 Taluk Government Head Quarters Hospitals.•	1 Non Taluk Government Hospital.•	1 Tuberculosis Hospital.(b) & (c):	“Public Health and Hospitals” being a State subject, the primary responsibility of establishment of Public Healthcare Facilities including Primary Health Centres (PHCs) is that of the State Government. However under National Health Mission (NHM) support is provided to States for strengthening of their health care system including support for establishment/ upgradation of public health facilities.As per the information received from the State Government of Tamil Nadu, at present there is no proposal to create new PHCs in Cuddalore District.
