                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2346






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Roads Connecting Remote Villages under PMGSY




Vijay Kumar Dubey













                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total length of roads connecting remote villages constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during each of the last three years and current year in the State of Uttar Pradesh and the North Eastern Region (NER) including Tripura, District-wise;(b) 	the amount of funds allocated and utilized by the Government for the construction of the said roads; and(c) 	whether the Government has achieved the targets set for the last year under the said scheme in the States of Uttar Pradesh and the North Eastern Region (NER), if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER
