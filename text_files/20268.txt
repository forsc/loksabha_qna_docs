                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1502






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Unutilized Railway Land




Geeta Kora













                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched a scheme/initiated tender process for the commercial development of unutilized land at railway stations and if so, the details thereof;(b) the names of the railway stations in Jammu and Jharkhand where unutilized land has been taken for commercial development along with the details thereof; and(c) the details of the private sector companies with which contracts have been signed for commercial development of the unutilized land of the Railways State-wise as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
