                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5073






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Funds utilised for Schemes




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of schemes undertaken by the DONER in the State of Assam that have remained incomplete, half-complete or not yet started in the last ten years;(b) 	the total quantum of unutilized funds remaining with the Ministry of DONER in view of incomplete, half-complete and not yet started schemes; and(c) 	whether there is any new plan to utilise this unutilised fund remaining at the disposal of DONER?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development ofNorth Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a)	There are 243 projects which were sanctioned during last 10 years for the state of Assam under various schemes of the Ministry of DoNER, out of these 195 projects are ongoing and at different stages of implementation. In addition 51 projects of NEC are ongoing at different stages.(b)	and (c) The amount of unutilized funds lying with state government against these ongoing project are to the tune of Rs.638.25 crore. The state government is in the process of utilizing this unspent amount.
