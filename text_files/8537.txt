                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2136






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Auction of Gifts Received by Prime  Minister




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the auction of 2,772 gifts given to Hon’ble Prime Minister has been extended again;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether it is also true that one of the reasons for the extension of auction was that many items were yet to receive any bids; and (d)	 if so, the details thereof ? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) :  E-auction of 2,772 gifts presented to Hon’ble Prime Minister was conducted by National Gallery of Modern Art (NGMA) Delhi, a Sub-ordinate organization under Ministry of Culture from 14.09.2019 to 03.10.2019. Extension of e-auction was granted twice on 01.10.2019 and 17.10.2019. The auction has been closed on 24.10.2019.   (c) & (d) : Bids of all gift items were received before closing the e-auction on 24.10.2019. Extensions were given to increase people’s participation as the sale proceeds are meant for charitable cause- “Namami Gange” project of the Government.***
