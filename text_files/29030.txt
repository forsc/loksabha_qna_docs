                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1113






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Anti-drone System




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that India does not have any anti-drone system to counter attacks by drones; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (c) the details of countries which have the best anti-drone systems in the world; (d) whether the Government has any proposal to acquire anti-drone system to protect our defence bases, defence installations, etc.; and (e) if so, details thereof along with the efforts being made during the last two years to acquire anti-drone system?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a)	No, Sir. It is not true. India has already developed an indigenous Anti-Drone System. The system is capable of Detection, Tracking, Soft Kill and Hard Kill of enemy Drones. (b)	Not Applicable.(c) Information indicates that following countries have Anti-Drone capabilities:•	USA, Israel, France, UK and Germany. (d)	& (e) Order for Anti-Drone Systems has been placed by Indian Armed Forces (Navy, Army & Air Force) on M/s BEL based on technology developed by DRDO. DRDO has also transferred the technology for production of these to 04 more private industries. ******
