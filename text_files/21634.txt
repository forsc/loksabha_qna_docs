                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Ventilators in Government Hospitals




Dayanidhi Maran














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the total number of ventilators available at Government run hospitals, item-wise and State/UT-wise;(b) the total number of ventilators purchased and installed by Union Government since March 24, 2020 at Government run hospitals, item-wise and State/UT-wise; and(c) total cost incurred by Government towards purchase and installation of ventilators during the said period, item-wise and State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): ‘Public Health’ & ‘Hospitals’ being State subjects, such information is not maintained centrally. However, as on 05.03.2021, 38867 Ventilators have been allocated by the Government to State/ Union Territory (UT) /Central Government Institution. Out of 38867 Ventilators, 35269 Ventilators have been installed. State/UT/Central Government Institution-wise details are annexed.The total amount incurred by Government for procurement of 38867 Ventilators is Rs. 1850.76 Crore.
