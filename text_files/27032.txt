                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2750






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Octroi and Toll tax




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of rates of Octroi and toll tax being collected by Cantonment Boards especially Secunderabad Cantonment Board;(b) the details of Octroi and toll tax collected by Cantonment Boards during the last 5 years including the current year across the country, year-wise and Cantonment Board wise;(c) whether the Government, Post-GST implementation has directed Cantonments across the country to stop collecting Octroi and toll tax;(d) if so, the details thereof and the status of implementation of the said order;(e) whether despite of Government’s orders, Secunderabad Cantonment Board is still collecting Octroi and Toll Tax; and(f) if so, the details of action taken against those responsible?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b):  Directorate General Defence Estates (DGDE) has informed that no Cantonment Board is presently collecting Octroi. Details of Octroi (prior to subsuming of Octroi in GST w.e.f 01.07.2017) and Toll tax collected by Cantonment Boards (including Cantonment Board Secunderabad) during last five years including the current year are attached as Annexure. The Cantonment Boards not listed in the Annexure have not imposed Octroi or Toll tax during the last five years.(c) & (d):  Instructions were issued by DGDE vide letter dated 28.07.2017 that any tax/fee relatable to erstwhile Entry 52 of State List must not be imposed, having been subsumed in GST. It was further instructed that taxes on Vehicles and ‘Tolls’ relatable to Entries 57 and 59 of the State list have not been affected by introduction of GST. These instructions were issued in consultation with Department of Revenue, Ministry of Finance.(e)  Secunderabad Cantonment Board is not collecting Octroi after implementation of GST w.e.f July, 2017. It is collecting Toll tax in accordance with the instructions dated 28.07.2017 mentioned above.(f)  Not applicable.*****
