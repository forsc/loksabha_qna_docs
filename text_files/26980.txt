                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Post Offices in Uttar Pradesh




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of new post offices opened/started in Uttar Pradesh during the last five years along with details thereof; (b) 	the names of the tehsil level post offices in districts of the State of Uttar Pradesh, the buildings of which have been constructed; and(c) 	the time by which the damaged buildings of these post offices are likely to be constructed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	Sir, there are 27 post offices opened/ started in Uttar Pradesh during the last five years from 2016-17 to 2020-21. The details of new post offices opened are at Annexure-I.(b)	District wise names of the tehsil level post office buildings which have been constructed in the last five years in the State of Uttar Pradesh are at Annexure-II.(c)	Damaged Departmental buildings of Post Offices are maintained periodically as per requirement, subject to availability of funds, as an ongoing process.
