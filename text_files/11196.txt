                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

18






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Development of Schools by Private Companies




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it has come to the notice of the Government of the handing over of morethan 2,500 Government schools in Andhra Pradesh to private companies fordevelopment and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it has also come to the notice of the Government that one of the companieswho has been given nearly 500 schools for development is charge-sheeted by theCBI; and(c) if so, the reasons as to why such companies are being permitted to develop schools?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): No, Sir. The Government of Andhra Pradesh has informed that there is nosuch proposal.(b) to (c) : Does not arise.****
