                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3006






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Development of Chanderi, Shivpuri and Guna




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to develop various sites and buildings of historical importance in the country by restoring them for tourism; (b) 	if so, the outline of the same including the names of areas of Madhya Pradesh to be included under it; (c) 	whether it is proposed to develop Chanderi, Shivpuri and Guna which are areas of natural beauty as well as historical importance as tourist places; and (d) 	whether there is any plan to carry out restoration work of buildings, palaces, baolis etc., of historical importance in Chanderi, Shivpuri and Guna?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Development and Promotion of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government. The Ministry of Tourism, under the schemes of Swadesh Darshan and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in consultation with them. The   projects under these schemes are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc. Sanctioning of projects under these schemes is a continuous process however there  is  no  proposal  under  consideration  for sanctioning of  new  projects  under Swadesh Darshan  scheme. The Ministry of  Tourism  as part of  its  Swadesh Darshan  scheme  has  sanctioned  a  project  ‘Development of Gwalior – Orchha- Khajuraho-Chanderi – Bhimbetka - Mandu’  in the year  2016-17 under  heritage  theme;  wherein  components  like  Tourist  Facilitation Centre near Qila Kothi, tourist facilities, amenities & site development around Beju Bawara Samadhi,  Chakla Bawari,  Dilli Darwaza, Dholia Darwaza etc. in Chanderi  are  included. The details of projects sanctioned in Madhya Pradesh under ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘PRASHAD’ schemes are annexed.*******
