                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2215






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Dedicated Desk of Invest India for NER




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning on setting up a dedicated desk of Invest India for the North Eastern Region;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the facilities that will be extended to the North Eastern Region as a result of the dedicated desk; and (d)	whether the Government has a time-frame in mind for the implementation of such a dedicated desk and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh)(a)	& (b)  Yes, Sir. It is proposed to set up a special dedicated desk of Invest India for North Eastern Region. The desk will take up investment targeting, promotion, facilitation and website development for North Eastern States.(c) The facilities that are proposed be extended to the NER are :– (i)	Information provision(ii)	Targeting support (iii)	Facilitation support (iv)	State training and capacity building(v)	Creation and structuring of state investment teams(vi)	Advisory for states for their investment summits/roundtables(vii)	Investors targeting support(viii)	Website  All the above activities will be done in close coordination with the State Governments.       (d)  The following activities have already been initiated: – (i)	A workshop on ‘Accelerating Investment and Industrial Development in the North East Region’ was organized at Guwahati on 30th November, 2019 with participation of senior officers from all the North Eastern States.(ii)	A team within Invest India has been identified to help with the activities of the desk.(iii)	Development of the promotional website/portal has been initiated. A project has been undertaken to identify two-three most prominent industrial clusters in each of the eight NE States.
