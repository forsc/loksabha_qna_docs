                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

415






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Impact of Floods in North Bihar




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is not a fact that lakhs of farmers have lost their animals as well as fisheries ponds in more that 16 districts of North Bihar during recent heavy floods, if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether it is also not a fact that the low milk production due to heavy floods in North Bihar is affecting the livelihood of the farmer; and(c)	if so, the details of financial support being planned by the Government to be provided to those farmers in Bihar and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	The Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairy & Department of Fisheries is not in receipt of any report on loss of animals as well as fisheries ponds in the districts of North Bihar during the recent heavy floods.(b)	&(c) The Government is implementing various schemes to supplement the efforts of States/UTs through financial assistance for promotion of animal husbandry, dairy and fisheries sectors across the country including Bihar such as:-I.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission: For development and conservation of indigenous bovine breedsII.	National Programme for Dairy Development: Facilitate procurement, processing and marketing  of milk by Cooperative milk institutionsIII.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund: Creation /modernization /expansion of dairy processing and manufacturing infrastructureIV.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities: To assist the Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer organisations by providing soft loan for working capital and to provide stable market access to the dairy farmersV.	National Livestock Mission: Sustainable growth and development of livestock sector particularly sheep/goat/pig and poultry & increasing availability of feed and fodderVI.	Livestock Health and Disease Control: Control and containment of livestock diseasesVII.	National Animal Disease Control Programme: For control of Foot & Mouth Disease and BrucellosisVIII.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund: For promoting investment through infrastructure development in dairy processing, meat processing and Animal feed plantIX.	Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY): Development of Fisheries sector and welfare of Fishermen. ****
