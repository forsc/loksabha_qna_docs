                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3209






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Chandigarh-Kharar Project




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of the Chandigarh –Kharar highway/elevated road project being implemented by NHAI;(b) 	the length and the cost of the project;(c) 	the time-frame set for completion of the project;(d) 	the present status of the elevated road/flyover that passes through Kharar city of Punjab along with the time-frame set for its completion; and(e)  	the action taken by NHAI against the erring contractors of both the above mentioned projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)to (e)	The Chandigarh –Kharar highway/elevated road project including Kharar City has been started and physical progress of the project is 72%. The length of the project is 10.630 Km swith project cost of about Rs. 368.50 crores. The scheduled date for completion of the project is 31.08.2020.The project was delayed mainly due to delay in land acquisition and delay in shifting of utilities. Most of the issues concerning the project have been resolved.*****
