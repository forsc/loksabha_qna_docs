                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

753






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Ayush Treatment against COVID-19




Pradeep Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has strategically dealt COVID-19 crisis in the country for the last 15 months;(b) whether the Government has adopted Ayurvedic, Homeopathy, Unani and other Ayurveda line of treatment to fight against COVID-19;(c) if so, the number of Ayurvedic and Homeopathy Covid centres set up in the country to fight against the said pandemic, State-wise;(d) the number of COVID-19 patients treated at these centres along with the funds spent in this regard, State-wise; and(e) the estimated recovery rate for COVID-19 patients treated with these methods? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(DR. MUNJPARA MAHENDRABHAI)(a) & (b):	Yes. The Ministry of Ayush has taken several public health initiatives to prevent the spread of COVID-19 in the country in the last 15 months; the details of which are at Annexure-I.(c) to (e):	The details of AYUSH Centres including Ayurvedic and Homeopathic Centres treating COVID patients and estimated recovery rate are given at Annexure-II. The funds spent at the centres are part of overall expenditure incurred at the centre. However, as per the requests received from State/UT Governments, an amount of Rs. 2271.551 lakh has been approved by Ministry of Ayush under National AYUSH Mission (NAM) for purchase of medicines including AYUSH-64 Medicine in the context of COVID-19 pandemic, till date.
