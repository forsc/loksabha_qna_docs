                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3483






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Popularisation of Tourist Circuits in Odisha and West Bengal




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Tourism Circuits, Tourism Parks and Rural Tourism Clusters identified by the Government in each State/Union Territory including the States of Odisha and West Bengal;(b) 	whether the Government has received requests/ proposals from State Governments of Odisha and West Bengal for inclusion of more such Tourism Circuits/ Parks and Clusters and if so, the details thereof and the response thereto; (c)	whether the Government proposes to include Odisha and West Bengal as part of the International Tourism Circuit in the country to promote the culture of the States and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	the other steps taken by the Government to popularize Tourist Circuits in Odisha and West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			      (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):  The  Ministry of Tourism with the  objective  to  create  tourism infrastructure, provides financial  assistance  to the  State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations  under  its  Swadesh Darshan scheme. The  Ministry under  this  scheme  has  sanctioned  76 projects under  13   thematic circuits including  2 projects  under  rural  theme. The details of the projects  sanctioned  in  the  country  under  the Swadesh Darshan scheme  including  those in the  State of  Odisha  and  West Bengal  is  at Annexure I.  As of now, the Ministry of Tourism is not sanctioning any new project under Swadesh Darshan scheme.  The Ministry of Tourism as  part  of its  ongoing  promotional  activities  promotes  various tourist  destinations and  products  of the country holistically in the  domestic  and international  markets including those of the states of  Odisha  and  West  Bengal.In  addition,  Ministry of Rural Development  has informed that Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) attempts to  make the  selected  rural  areas  in  the form of  clusters. The  mission  focuses  on  holistic  development of  clusters and to ensure  an  optimum  level of  development,  21  components have been  suggested as  desirable  for  the  Cluster Development.   Among the 21 components, Tourism promotion is one of the major component under which States/UTs has proposed investment both under CGF and Convergence for different activities like beautification of tourist sites, infrastructure development at tourist places, Pilgrimage Development for Tourism, construction of Tourism parks, Heritage development, etc. A total of 103 clusters covering 32 States/UTs have proposed an investment of Rs. 490.70 crore both under CGF and Convergence under Tourism Promotion. The cluster wise investment proposed under Tourism promotion component is at Annexure II.  The State of Odisha has proposed different activities under Tourism promotion components in three Rurban cluster with an investment amounting to Rs. 3.40 crore. The State of West Bengal is in process of preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) for 7 clusters allocated under phase 1 of the Mission.*******
