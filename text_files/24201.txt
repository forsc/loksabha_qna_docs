                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Collection of Taxes




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the details of total collection of various taxes including corporate tax and GST during the last three years and the current financial year, tax category-wise;(b)   the details of total non-tax revenue collected by the Union Government during this financial year so far;(c)   whether it is true that the tax collection has reduced during the current financial year; and(d)   if so, the response of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a)   The category-wise details of total collection of various taxes including corporate tax and GST during the last three years and the current financial year are as under:Direct Tax(Rs. in crore)Head	FY 2018-19	FY 2019-20	FY 2020-21*	FY2021-22*upto 30.06.2021Corporation Tax	6,63,571.62	5,56,875.55	4,57,050.27	1,20,991.64Personal Income Tax including Wealth Tax etc. 	4,62,453.19	4,81,461.38	4,71,401.81	1,20,154.78Securities Transaction Tax	11,527.50	12,374.23	16,926.99	5,373.40 *Provisional Indirect Tax  			       							    (Rs. in crore)HeadNet Non- GST	FY 2018-19	FY 2019-20	FY 2020-21* 	FY 2021-22*    Upto30.06.2021Customs duty	 1,17,813	 1,09,283	 1,34,825	41,831Union Excise	 2,31,045	 2,39,452	 3,89,677	 1,01,564ST(arrears)	6,904	6,029	1,644	167Net GSTCGST	 4,57,534	 4,94,071	 4,56,348	 1,17,446IGST(residual)	28,945	9,125	7,209	25,754Comp.
