                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2094






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Data on Migrant Workers




K. Muraleedharan













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has collected data on the number of migrant workers and their family members who died during and after the national lockdown between March, 2020 and September, 2020; (b)if so, the details thereof including the number of workers and family members who died and the causes of death; and(c)if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	As per the information received from the States/UTs, no loss of lives of migrant workers are reported from Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Chandigarh, Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu, Delhi, Goa, Kerala, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Puducherry, Himachal Pradesh,  Punjab  and Uttarakhand, except 17 accidental deaths in Maharashtra.  Information/Data from the remaining States/UTs is being collected/compiled and will be laid on the Table of the House.******
