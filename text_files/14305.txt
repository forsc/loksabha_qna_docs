                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3011






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






One Nation, One Permit, One Tax System




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to launch‘One India one permit’/ ‘one nation, one permit, one tax’system in the country; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)and (b)	National Permit for goods vehicle has been mandated under rule 90 of the Central Motor Vehicles Rules 1989 and The Motor Vehicles (All India Permit for Tourist Transport Operators) Rules, 1993 contains provision regarding all India permit for tourist transport vehicles. The matter of Road Tax Policy for Motor Vehicles is a State Subject and Central Government deliberates on this issue with the States in meetings of Transport Development Council (TDC).	*****
