                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






National Nutrition Mission




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  	the measures taken or being taken by the Government so far to boost National Nutrition Mission(NNM);(b)	whether the Government has identified various districts across the country under NNM plan; and(c)	if so, the details thereof along with the names of those districts, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	National Nutrition Mission (renamed as POSHAN Abhiyaan) set up in December, 2017 has in-built components of Convergence with various other programmes, IT enabled Common Application Software for strengthening service delivery and interventions, Community Mobilization and Awareness advocacy leading to Jan Andolan-to educate the people on nutritional aspects, Capacity Building of frontline functionaries, etc. In order to boost the scheme 4 meetings of National Council on India’s Nutrition Challenges and 7 meetings of Executive Committee have been held till date. A number of visits have been carried out by officers of Ministry and meetings/Video Conferences held with States/UTs for orientation on the Scheme. Rashtriya Poshan Maah in September, 2018 and Poshan Pakhawada in March, 2019 were celebrated for Community Mobilization and awareness.(b)	& (c):	Under POSHAN Abhiyaan, all districts of the 36 States/UTs have been covered for implementation in a phased manner********
