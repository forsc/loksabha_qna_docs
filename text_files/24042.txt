                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Promotion of Yogasan




Raksha Nikhil Khadse













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                








                                                    Kapil Moreshwar Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to promote Yogasan as a sport for its effective creation of interest among children from the childhood at school levels; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has taken any initiative to promote Yogasan as a competitive sports to be taken up for globally organised events like world championship, Asian Games and even in Olympic Games, if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government proposes to establish a National Yoga Federation; and (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (e):	The Government, after deliberation at length and taking into account the relevant factors including developing Yogasana as a competitive sport has already recognised the National Yogasana Sports Federation (NYSF) as a National Sports Federation for promotionand development of Yogasana as a competitive sport in the country. The Government recognition makes NYSF eligible for financial assistance for conduct of national championship in all the categories, viz. Seniors, Juniors and Sub-Juniors and participation in international sporting events of Yogasana sport.****
