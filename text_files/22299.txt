                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3413 TO BE ANSWERED ON 16.03.2021DR. AMBEDKAR PRATISHTHAN †3413. SHRI AJAY BHATT:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the details of schemes and programmes being implemented by ‘Dr. Ambedkar Pratishthan presently in Uttarkhand;(b) the details and the number of beneficiaries who have been provided financial assistance by Dr. Ambedkar Pratishthan for the treatment of fatal diseases during the last three years and the current year, State and diseasewise; (c) whether the Government has set up any regulatory body to monitor its functioning; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER

None of the Schemes is  State-specific.(b): The information enclosed at Annexure.(c) & (d): Functioning of  Dr. Ambedkar Pratishthan is being monitored by its Governing Body headed by Honb''le Minister for Social Justice and Empowerment.
