                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

297






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Irregularities and Condition of Labourers in FCI




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the attention of the Government has been drawn towards the complaint made by Mazdoor Sangh of Food Corporation of India (FCI) regarding the irregularities and the poor condition of laboures/workers engaged in Shahjahanpur, Sandila, Barabanki, Raibareli, Koshikalan FCI depot in Uttar Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the office bearers of Mazdoor Sangh have not been called in the high level meetings by the FCI, Mazdoor Sangh Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh; (d) 	if so, the reasons therefor; (e) 	the details of meetings of FCI Mazdoor Sangh held regarding Allahabad-Nanny Depot, Lucknow, Kanpur, Banaras depot along with Shahajahanpur, Sandila, Barabanki, Raebareli and Koshi Kalan of Uttar Pradesh during each of the last three years; and (f) 	the measures taken for including FCI Mazdoor Sangh in the meetings?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):1.	Regarding Shahjahanpur – Food Storage Depot (FSD) Roza is non operational due to labour disputes and pending litigation filed by various labour union representative claiming their rights for regularization.2.	Sandila Depot was an Agricultural Refinance Development Corporation (ARDC) godown and dehired by FCI on 28.08.2000……2/--2-3.	FSD Barabanki and FSD Raebareilly are operated through DPS  labour  System  and there is no pending  complaint  and  grievance of the said Labour Union.4.		FSD Kosikalan is operated through NWNP labour System. The said union has filed various complaints before Industrial Tribunal and Writ petitions before Hon''ble High Court Allahabad/Lucknow bench also for induction of labourers in the said depot.(b):	Following litigations are pending before various forums:Kosikalan DepotS. Contempt	Allahabad High Court	2695/2010	Pending4.	Contempt	Allahabad High Court	385(C)/2012	Pending5.	ID Case	ALC(C), Jhansi	JHS-07(43)/2020	Pending6.	Writ Petition	Allahabad High Court (Lucknow Bench)	29715/2018	Pending7.	Writ Petition	Allahabad High Court	WP No.40313/2019	PendingShajahanpur, Roza DepotS. No.	Case type	Court/Forum	Writ/ID No.	Status1.	Writ Petition	Allahabad High Court 	WP No. 10123/1993	Pending2.	ID Case	CGIT, Delhi	157/2012	Pending3.	ID Case	CGIT, Kanpur	36/2016	Pending4.	ID Case	CGIT, Kanpur	66/2015	Pending5.	Writ Petition	Allahabad High Court 	32280/2018	Pending6. (d):	At Regional Office, Lucknow Uttar Pradesh no high level meeting has been called by the FCI. But the representatives of Mazdoor Sangh have had informal meeting with the FCI RO, Lucknow.(e):	No high level meetings has been called by the FCI with Mazdoor Sangh Union during the last three years. Hence, minutes of meeting is not available.  (f):	Mazdoor Sangh Union has not been recognized at National Level, hence, not included Mazdoor Sangh in the meetings.****
