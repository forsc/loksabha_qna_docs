                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Treatment for Alzheimers Disease in Ayurveda




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is aware of any treatment for Alzheimers in Ayurveda and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the diagnostic tests for Alzheimers have not been successful as yet;(c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(d) the further steps taken by the Government to promote Ayurveda to develop a drug for the treatment of Alzheimers disease under AYUSH?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (d): In Ayurveda the Alzheimer disease is considered under the broad umbrella of Manas Roga in the classical texts of Ayurveda. The Institutions under Ministry of AYUSH have not undertaken any clinical trial on Alzheimer’s disease.
