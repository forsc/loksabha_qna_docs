                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2579






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Allocation under CRIF in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated to Odisha under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) during each of the last three years and the current year for development of various sectors; (b) the funds sanctioned under CRIF during the said period for the development of State roads in the State along with the funds utilised, year-wise; and (c) the details of the road projects presently being developed in the State under CRIF?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS (Shri NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) The Allocation/Accrual of funds under CRIF to the State of Odisha during the last 3 years and current financial year for development of State roads is as under: Year	Allocation / Accrual of funds to the State of Odisha under CRIF (Rs. in Cr.)2018-19	332.232019-20	308.942020-21	286.972021-22	298.67 (b) The funds released to the State of Odisha under CRIF and funds utilized by the State Government of Odisha during the last 3 years and current financial year for development of State roads is as under: Year	Funds released (Rs. in Cr.)	Funds Utilized (Rs. in Cr.)2018-19	111.93	360.542019-20	286.96	282.162020-21	534.19	429.632021-22	535.07	360.90 (c) The details of the road project being developed in the State of Odisha under CRIF is enclosed as Annexure-I.
