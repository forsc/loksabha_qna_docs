                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4063






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Digitization of Villages in Gujarat




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology be pleased to state: -(a)  the details of the villages digitized by the Government in the State of Gujarat so far, district-wise;(b)	the funds allocated to Gujarat for digitization of the said villages so far; and(c)	the facilities being provided at present in the above-mentioned digital villages?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):  Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) initiated the Digital Village Pilot project on 31st October 2018 covering 700 Gram Panchyats/villages across the country under the pilot implementation which includes 33 Gram Panchayats (GP)/villages of Gujarat, one each from the 33 districts in Gujarat. District wise list of the Gram Panchayats/villages is enclosed at Annexure. (b):  The Digital Village Pilot Project is being implemented across India through CSC e-Governance Service India Limited under the Digital India Scheme which is a Central Sector Scheme of Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. The tentative funds requirement for the State of Gujarat, under the project for three years to cover 33 Gram Panchayat/villages is approximately Rs.326.70lakhs. (c):  The project is provisioning the services of - Health – Tele-Health & Tele-Veterinary consultation, Education, Financial Inclusion Awareness Programme, Skill Development and Solar panel powered street lights (8 lights per village) including Government to Citizens Services (G2C), B2C Services. ******* AnnexureSl No	District	Block	Village1	Ahmedabad	Detroj	Katosan road (Sunvala)2	Amreli	Khambha	Khaddar3	Anand	Sojitra	Kasor4	Aravalli	bayad	sathamba5	Banaskantha	Dantiwada	Bhadli Zat6	Bharuch	Amod	Sarbhan7	Bhavnagar	Sihor	Ishvariya8	Botad	Botad	turkha9	Chhota Udaipur	Kawant	Panwad10	Devbhumi dwarka	Dwarka	Bhimrana11	Dahod	Limkheda	Moti Bandibar12	Gandhinagar	Kalol	Jaspur13	Gir Somnath	Veraval	Bherala14	Jamnagar	jamnagar	Medi15	Junagadh	Mendarda	Nani Khodiyar16	Kheda	Mahemdabad	Modaj17	Kutch	Anjar	Navagam18	Mahisagar	Kadana	Sanghari19	Mehsana	Kadi	Nani Kadi20	Morbi	Wankaner	Tithva21	Navsari	Bansda	Pipalkhed22	Narmada	Garudeshwar	Kavadiya koloni23	Panchmahal	Ghoghamba	Jitpura24	Patan	saraswati	Vagdod25	Porbandar	Kutiyana	Bavlavadar26	Rajkot	Jasdan	Kalasar27	Sabarkantha	Prantj	Sonasan28	Surat	Mahuva	Karcheliya29	Surendranagar	chuda	Bhanejada30	Tapi	kukarmunda	Kukarmunda31	Dang	Waghai	Lavariya( Bhendmal)32	Vadodara	Padra	Kanzat33	Valsad	Kaparada	Mandva
