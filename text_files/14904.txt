                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Use of CSR in Aspirational Districts




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-
