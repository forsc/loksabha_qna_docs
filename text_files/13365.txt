                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2250






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Crime in Trains




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	   whether the incidents of molestation of women, stealing, loot and other criminal activities have increased in long distance trains in the country;(b)	    if so, the number of incidents that have come to the notice of the Government during the last one year, zone-wise including Northern Railway Zone; and(c)       the efforts made/being made by the Railways to ensure fear-free journey for the passengers and to check criminal activities in trains? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
