                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2034






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Food Processing Industries




Pashupati Kumar Paras














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government is not aware of the food processing industries operating in the States, if so, the details thereof;(b)	the manner in which the strategy for the development and expansion of the food processing industries is formulated without the said information and the reaction of the Government thereto; and(c)	the details of corrective measures adopted by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c): The State-wise number of registered food processing units/factories and number of units/factories in operation in the country as per Annual Survey of Industries 2016-17 estimates is at Annexure.*****
