                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Functioning of Cooperative Banks




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any role or control over the functioning of cooperative banks in the country, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has any statistics regarding number of co-operative banks functioning in the country, if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (c) whether it is mandatory that the details of the depositors are to be shared with Government of India and if so, the details thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has reported that the functioning of Cooperative Banks is guided by the Cooperative Societies Act of the respective States. At the State level, the Registrar of Cooperative Societies (RCS) of respective States exercises control over the Cooperative Banks. However, the banking functions of the Cooperative Banks are regulated by Reserve Bank of India under the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Cooperatives).  (b)  The list of Cooperative Banks functioning in the country as reported by NABARD and Reserve Bank of India (RBI) is given in Annexure I to IV.(c) RBI has informed that in terms of its Master Direction dated 25.02.2016 on Know your customer (KYC), all entities regulated by RBI including Cooperative Banks are required to share/submit details of transaction/accounts as required under the Prevention of Money Laundering (Maintenance of Records) Rules, 2005 with Financial Intelligence Unit-India (FIU-IND).******* Annexure I to Lok Sabha USQ 3487 for 15.07.2019Name of the Bank - State Cooperative Agriculture and Rural Development Banks (SCARDBs)/ Primary Cooperative Agriculture and Rural Development Banks (PCARDBs) Sr. No.	SCARDB	PCARDBs			Name of State		1	Gujarat	Haryana		2	Haryana	Himachal Pradesh		3	Himachal Pradesh	Karnataka		4	Karnataka	Kerala		5	Kerala	Punjab		6	Punjab	Rajasthan		7	Rajasthan	West Bengal		8	Tripura	Tamil Nadu		9	Uttar Pradesh	 		10	West Bengal	 		11	Jammu	 		12	Pondicherry	 		13	Tamil Nadu	 		Source: NABARD
