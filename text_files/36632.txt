                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3841






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Innovations for Defence Excellence Initiative




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of funds allocated for the creation of an ecosystem for technology development in Defence by engaging Research and Development institutes, academia, industries, startups and even individual innovators under the ‘Innovations for Defence Excellence’ (iDEX) initiative;(b) whether projects proposed under the iDEX have been taken up for mass production by the Government and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the incentives proposed by the Government to encourage more youth to participate in defence innovations and startups in the country?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) to (c): A statement is attached.*****
