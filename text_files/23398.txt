                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Schemes of ST in Rajasthan




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the schemes being implemented for the development of Scheduled Tribes in Rajasthan and the details thereof, scheme-wise;(b) the scheme-wise allocation made for tribal welfare in the State including Banswara-Dungarpur and expenditure incurred item-wise during the last five years;(c) whether all the allocated works have been completed within the time limit or are pending;(d) whether any complaint has been received against the executing agencies in terms of the quality of the said development works; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the details the action taken in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a): The names of the schemes being implemented for the development of Scheduled Tribes in Rajasthan and its details are enclosed at Annexure-I.(b): The scheme wise details of the funds released by Ministry of Tribal Affairs during the last five years on tribal welfare in Rajasthan including Banswara-Dungarpur is enclosed at Annexure-IIA, Annexure-IIBand Annexure-IIC.(c): The Ministry does not have district and block wise allocation details as funds are released to the state governments, the organizations working in the states or directly transferred to the beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). However, the present status of the EMRS schools sanctioned in Rajasthan is enclosed at Annexure III.(d)& (e): No complaint has been received in the Grievance cell of the Ministry of Tribal Affairs against the executing agencies in terms of the quality of the said development works.
