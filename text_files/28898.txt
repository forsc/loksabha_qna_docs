                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

427






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Caste-Based Census




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi













                                                    Malook Nagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has considered conducting a caste-based census in 2021-22;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government has formulated any scheme/policy for caste-based census in various States including Uttar Pradesh;(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(e) the number of caste communities identified across the country, State-wise; and(f) whether the Government proposes to include more castes/communities in the Scheduled Castes category, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (f):	The castes and tribes which are specifically notified as Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Scheduled Tribes (STs) as per the Constitution (Scheduled Castes)  Order  1950  and  the   Constitution  (Scheduled Tribes)  Order,   1950, as-2-LS.USQ.NO.427 FOR 30.11.2021amended from time to time, are enumerated in decadal Census. The Government of India has not enumerated caste wise population other than SCs and STs in Census since independence.  The Census Schedule is designed in consultation with various stakeholders including Central Ministries.  The intent of the Government for conducting Census 2021 was notified in the Gazette of India on 28th March, 2019.  However, due to outbreak of Covid-19, the Census activities have been postponed. *****
