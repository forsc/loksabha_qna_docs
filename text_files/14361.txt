                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3166






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Bypass Roads on NH-85 in Kerala




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to construct bypass on NH-85 from Muvattupuzha to Kothamangalam in the State of Kerala;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government is aware of the slow pace of work on National Highway development projects in the State and if so, the reasons therefor; and(d) 	the details of such major projects in the State along with the funds provided for each project during the years 2018-19 and 2019-20?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b)	The Detailed Project Report (DPR) for the Theni – Munnar – Kochi stretch of NH – 85 including Muvattupuzha to Kothamangalam has been initiated.(c) to (d)	Some 4/6-lane projects executed through National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) have been delayed due to various reasons such as Public protest, delay in land acquisition, non-availability of burrow earth & aggregates and delay in Forest clearance etc. The details of such major projects along with the expenditure incurred during the years 2018-19 and 2019-20 are as below:S.No.	Name of Project	Expenditure Incurred (Cr.)		2018-19	2019-201	6-laning of Vadakkanchery (Km 240) to Thrissur (Km 270) 	0	02	4 Laning of NH-66 Thiruvananthapuram  Bypass from Mukkola (Km. 26.500) to  Kerala / Tamilnadu Border 	158.41	123.323.	4 Laning of Thalassery-Mahe Bypass 	198.13	117.59*****
