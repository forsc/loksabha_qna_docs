                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

58






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Proposals for Allotment of Iron Ore Mines




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the proposals by the State Governments including Rajasthan to the Union Government for the permission of allotment of iron ore mines during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise; (b) the number of such proposals received/ sanctioned and pending during the said period;(c) the reasons for the pendency of the said proposals; and(d) the details of the special campaigns launched to explore and exploit the iron ore in the States?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (c): As per the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957 and the Rules framed thereunder, permission is not required from the Central Government for auction of mineral blocks for Mining Leases (ML). However, for auction of Composite Licence (CL), previous approval is required from the Central Government for notified minerals viz. limestone, Iron Ore, Manganese and bauxite . The list of Iron Ore blocks (G4 exploration level) for which previous approval of the Central Government has been granted so far to auction the blocks as Composite Licence is attached at Annexure. (d): The Ministry of Mines has amended MEMC Rules providing for auction of surfacial deposits of minerals blocks of limestone, Iron Ore  and bauxite for Mining Lease  at G3 level of exploration and composite licence at G4 level of exploration for all minerals. Accordingly, GSI has handed over five (5) G3 level reports of  Iron ore  and  17 G4 level reports of Iron Ore to various State Governments. ***** AnnexureList of G4 exploration level Iron Ore Blocks for which previous approval has been granted:S.No.	State	Block1	Rajasthan	Dhulkhera-Jipiya iron ore block n/v Dhulkhera, Jipiya, Bhdalikhera, Raneegpura and Arajiya, Tehsil & District Bhilwara2	Madhya Pradesh	Shri Sanjay Kumar Mittal, Panihar, Gwalior3		Gangotri Ispat Ltd., Barmani, Marwari Kamha, Udesa, Sidhi4	Karnataka	SR-KA-11-Ramanadurga North -II5	Andhra Pradesh	Addankivaripalem  Prakasam District6		Lakshmakapalle Prakasam District7	Chhattisgarh	Baragaon Block,  Narayanpur8		Devpura Block,  Rajandgoan9		Thakurtola Block,  Durg10		Dondi Block,  Balod11		Tendubhatha Block,  Rajnandgoan12		Chhitpuri Block 1,  Kabirdham13		Chhitpuri Block 2,  Kabirdham14		Godhatoal Block,  Kabirdham & Rajnandgaon
