                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1295






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Rural Wages




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the growth rate of rural wages in the country since 2014, year-wise;(b) 	whether the growth rate of rural wages has declined since 2014;(c) 	if so, whether the Government has investigated the reasons for the decline;(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d) Labour Bureau, Ministry of Labour & Employment has been compiling and maintaining average wage rate data in respect of selected agricultural and non-agricultural occupations on this basis of the  data collected by the Field Operation Division (FOD)  of National Statistical Office (NOS) from a fixed set of 600 sample villages spread over 20 states. The average wage rates for the year 2014 to 2020 in respect of non-agricultural Labourers are given below:Annual Average daily wage rate for Non-agricultural laborersYear	             Men 	    Women2014	            235.51	  172.272015	            246.82	  180.022016	            257.95	  191.432017	            271.17	  205.902018	            282.04	  215.302019	            291.89	  216.542020 (till November 2020*)	    310.02	  230.41(* Due to pandemic, the data for the month of April, 2020 not compiled; therefore calculation for year 2020 is based on the average of 10 months.) Under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), the wage rates for beneficiaries of  Mahatma Gandhi NREG Scheme are revised annually, based on change in Consumer Price Index for Agricultural Labour (CPI-AL) published by Labour Bureau, Ministry of Labour & Employment for States/UTs. The wage rates are made applicable from 1st April of each Financial Year.The wage rates for the financial year 2020-21 have been revised effective from 1st April, 2020.  A table indicating Mahatma Gandhi NREGA wage rates for States/UTs for the FY 2019-20 and 2020-21 is given at annexure-I.						***** Annexure-I referred in reply to part (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1295 dated 09.02.2021State wise wage rates as per Gazette Notification 2019 and 2020
