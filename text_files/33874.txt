                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1571






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2022






Aviation Turbine Fuel




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) is cheaper than petrol at present; (b) if so, the details of price thereof, State-wise along with the reasons therefor; and (c) the price of petrol and ATF in October, November and December, 2021?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c):	Prices of Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF), petrol and diesel have been made market-determined by the Government with effect from 01.04.2001, 26.06.2010 and 19.10.2014 respectively.
