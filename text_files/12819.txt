                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1618






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Delay in Identification of various Historical Buildings




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that there has been delay and other issues towards proper identification and listing of the various historical buildings which are more than 100 years old as heritage structures and their declaration as protected monuments;(b)	if so, the details thereof of all such heritage structures which are more than 100 years old along with the status of their being declared as protected monuments,  State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government is taking any steps to ensure proper identification, prevention of damage and encroachment on such buildings and listing of such heritage structures as protected monuments; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)&(b)	Declaring any monument/site as a monument of national importance (centrally protected) is a continuous phenomenon.  At present, 22 number of monuments have been identified for declaring as protected monument, the details of which are at Annexure.  (c)&(d)	In order to prevent any damage to monuments identified for Central protection, periodic visits are made by field offices and if required, assistance from District Administration is also sought.  For notifying such monument, process is specified in the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958.
