                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Ongoing/Pending Projects




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided to review ongoing/ pending projects under Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise(MSME) sector in the country particularly in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the total number of such projects sanctioned and pending in the sector as on date;(c)	whether the Government is aware that the various projects are pending due to non- sanction of bank loans; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government to review such projects with concerned States of the country and the concerned banks?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NARAYAN RANE) (a) to (d): 	The projects/schemes of Government are reviewed regularly. However, currently projects /schemes of Government are under review for their continuation in the 15th Finance Commission cycle as per Ministry of Finance guidelines. These inter alia include Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Micro and Small Enterprises-Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), Technology Centre Systems Programme (TCSP), Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme for Technology Upgradation (CLCS-TUS), Procurement and Marketing Support (PMS), Scheme of Funds for Regeneration of Traditional Industries(SFURTI).The details of projects sanctioned and pending including Maharashtra is attached at Annexure-I and II.
