                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4548






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Indian Institute of Fashion Technology




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether it is true that the Government is planning to establish Indian Institute of Fashion Technology in the State of Andhra Pradesh as per the AP Reorganization Act;(b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the details of the proposed outlay for establishing this project; and(d)	the incentives being provided for establishment of this Institute?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d):	A National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) Campus is operational at Hyderabad, which is joint Capital of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.As per policy for establishment of a new campus of NIFT, the State Government is required to submit a proposal along with following commitments:(i)	20-30 acres of land;(ii)	Rs.150-200 cr. for construction of campus at the price rates of CPWD for 2011-12; (iii)	Rs.30 cr. for provision of equipments, machines and furniture at the price rates of 2011-12; (iv)	Annual revenue grants to adjust deficit revenue, till the campus becomes financially viable.The State Government of Andhra Pradesh has so far not submitted a complete proposal along with requisite commitments, in accordance with the policy guidelines, for setting up of a NIFT campus in their proposed new capital.  *****
