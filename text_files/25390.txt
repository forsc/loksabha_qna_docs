                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Coconut Parks and Banks




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government is aware that the State of Gujarat has more than 1,600 kilometres coastline;(b)	 if so, whether the Government has plans to propose setting up of coconut parks and coconut banks, which could be an additional source of income for the farmers along the coastline;(c)	 if so, the details thereof;(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor;(e)	whether it is a fact that the proposal for setting up of a Coconut Development Board at Junagarh is at abeyance with the Government; and(f) 	if so, the steps being taken and the details thereof including the time limit fixed for its early approval?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): As per information available on website link https://www.mha.gov.in/MHA1/Par2017/pdfs/par2013-pdfs/Is-300413/498.pdf, the State of Gujarat has 1214.7 km of coastline.(b) to (d): There is no such plan under consideration as of now.(e) & (f):  A reference dated 09.06.2015 was received from Shri Rajesh Chudasama, Hon’ble  Member of Parliament (LS), seeking intervention of Hon’ble Minister of State for Agriculture and Farmer Welfare to set up a branch of Coconut Development Board at Veraval/Junagadh in Gujarat. In this regard, preliminary discussions were held on 12.09.2015 during the ‘Coconut Entrepreneurs’ Meet held at Junagadh. Subsequently, the State Government had identified land at Mangrol, Junadh for establishment of an office of Coconut Development Board. However, the former leasehold tenant, M/s Shree Bharat Saraswati Mandir Sansad (SBSMS), Sharada Gram, Mangrol, Junagadh Dist., Gujarat filed a Special Civil Application SCA/5382/2016 before the Hon’ble High Court of Gujarat. The Hon’ble High Court directed to maintain status-quo pending disposal of the case. The matter is sub-judice.  Recently, a reference has been received from Shri Rajesh Chudasama, through Prime Minister Office (PMO) regarding establishment of a Coconut Development Board office in Junagadh, Gujarat.***
