                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

969






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Land Acquisition for NHs in Kerala




K. Muraleedharan













                                                    M. K. Raghavan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the National Highways Authority of India has given any directions to the State Government of Kerala to stop land acquisition processes for National Highways (NHs) including for NH-66 and if so, the details thereof; (b)	 the time by which the same is likely to be resumed; (c) 	the details of the present status of land acquisition proceedings to acquire 1,111 hectare of land for widening of NH-66; (d) 	the status of four-laning of the NH-66 Bypass of Kozhikode for which tender has been finalised; and (e) 	the reasons for not starting the work and the measures taken for early commencement of the project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)&(b)  The Land Acquisition cost constitutes substantial portion of the total project cost. In view of large number of projects to be taken up under Bharatmala Pariyojana, a need was felt to prioritize works depending upon availability of resources. However, later on the decision has been reviewed and as of now there is no such restriction.(c)	Notification under Section 3 (D) of NH Act 1956 has been published for 802.10 Ha land.(d)&(e)  The Financial Closure has not been achieved for the project, which is prerequisite for commencement of work.*****
