                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1178






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Scheme




C.N. Annadurai













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of labour and workers in the unorganized sector who have benefitted from Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan scheme (PM-SYM) since its inception along with the amount allocated for the scheme till date; (b)whether the Government is also depositing equal matching share in the pension account of the workers every month under the scheme and if so, the details thereof; (c)whether there is steep decline in the average monthly enrollment of PM-SYM and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(d)whether unorganized workers are not keen on old-age pension scheme and if so, the corrective steps taken by the Government to increase monthly enrollment of PM-SYM and make the scheme popular among unorganized workers; and(e)the estimated number of salaried employees who have lost their jobs due to COVID-19 pandemic?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e):	For old age protection in the form of monthly pension, Ministry of Labour & Employment  has launched Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana which is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme for providing monthly minimum assured pension of Rs. 3,000/- after the age of 60 years. Contd..2/-:: 2 ::The unorganised workers in the age group of 18-40 years whose monthly income is Rs.15,000/- or less and not a member of EPFO/ESIC/NPS (Government funded) are eligible to join the scheme. So far, over 44.46 Lakh beneficiaries have been enrolled.  Total fund allocated since inception is given below:                                                         (Rs. in Crore) Financial Year 	Allocation2018-19	502019-20	4082020-21	500The Central Government’s equal matching share of contribution to the tune of Rs 417.37 crore, is deposited in the Pension Fund maintained by LIC. A number of measures are being taken to popularise the scheme. More than one crore migrant workers had returned to their home states during COVID-19 related lockdown but many of them have already returned to their work-place in the destination states during unlock process.******
