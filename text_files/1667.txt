                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1570






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Innovation Cell




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the innovation Cell has been started by the Government with the special mandate to promote activities involving education sector to foster the culture of appreciating, nurturing, supporting and handholding new ideas;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government has achieved satisfactory progress through the innovation cell and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) An Innovation Cell has been established in All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) to undertake/initiate various innovation related activities such as establishing Institution’s Innovation Councils (IICs) in Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs), ranking HEIs on innovation parameters (ARIIA - Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements ranking), Hackathons (Smart India Hackathons, International Hackathons, Grand/Mini Challenges) and other such initiatives.    Innovation Cell has inter-alia organised the Smart India Hackathon 2018 and 2019, Singapore India Hackathon 2018, ARIIA Ranking 2019,  helped establish Institute Innovation Councils (IICs) in more than 900 Higher Education Institutions and has conducted  India’s first leadership talk series to help students learn from life experiences of entrepreneurs.*****
