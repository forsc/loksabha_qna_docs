                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Higher Education Loan to SC and OBC Students




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1581TO BE ANSWERED ON 11.02.2020HIGHER EDUCATION LOAN TO SC AND OBC STUDENTS 1581. SHRI VIJAY KUMAR: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a)  whether the Government is providing higher education loan at zero interest rate to the students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Other Backward Classes (OBCs); (b)  if so, the details thereof during the last five years, State and year-wise; (c)  the criteria to be fulfilled by the students for getting higher education loan; and (d)  the number of banks against which action has been taken for their reluctance in providing such loans during the said period along with the number of complaints received by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) : No Sir, The National Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) and National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC) of this Department does not provide educational loan at zero interest rate to the students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Other Backward Classes (OBC) to pursue higher education.(b): to (d): Not Applicable in view of (a): above.						*****
