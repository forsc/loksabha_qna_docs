                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Health Services under ESIC




Anurag Sharma













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Jual Oram
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether it is a fact that the number of hospitals selected under the ESI Health Scheme in the country is very less, if so, the details thereof along with the plans of the Government for its solution;(b)whether the Government proposes to include the ESI Health Scheme with Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana; (c)the number of ESIC hospitals/ dispensaries set up in the country, State/ UT-wise including Maharashtra and Odisha;(d)whether there has been a constant increase in the number of labourers being covered under ESIC and if so, the details thereof and the measures being taken by the Government to deal with this increasing number of beneficiaries; and(e)the details of the steps taken by the Government to improve the medical services being provided by ESIC in the country; and (f)whether the Government has a proposal to set up any new hospitals and dispensaries of ESI and also to upgrade some of the existing hospitals and dispensaries and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		Setting up of hospital under Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) scheme is a continuous and ongoing process. The setting up of an ESI hospital is undertaken once an area qualifies based on minimum number of Insured Persons (IPs) as per the existing norms of the ESI Corporation. Contd..2/-:: 2 ::       		Due to rapid expansion of ESI Scheme during last few years, there has been an enhanced requirement of ESI hospitals across the country. Accordingly, number of locations qualifying for setting up of ESI hospital has surged.        		Details of the existing ESI hospital locations across the country is placed at Annexure-I. Further, ESI Corporation has given approval for setting up of 50 new ESI hospitals.  The States/ UT wise details of these hospitals are placed at Annexure-II.(b):		Yes, Sir.   ESIC has collaborated with Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) in 152 districts of the country for providing cashless medical services to its beneficiaries through PMJAY empanelled hospitals.(c):		The number of ESIC hospitals/ dispensaries set up in the country, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra and Odisha, is placed at Annexure-III.(d) & (e):	There has been increase in the number of workers being covered under ESIC in the year 2017-18 and 2018-19. However, there was a slight decrease in coverage in the year 2019-20.  The details are as under:-Year	No. of Employees2017-18	311186802018-19	314019202019-20	30966930To supplement the medical facilities provided by State Government, ESI Corporation decided to open Dispensary-cum-Branch Offices (DCBOs).i.	DCBOs are established/ operationalized in only such districts which have no ESI dispensary as well as having IP population of 3000 or more within a radius of 10 km. In case a Branch Office is there in such district, then the Branch Office is converted into DCBO.ii.	ESIC has established 68 DCBOs across the country.  It has also sanctioned 10 new DCBOs.Contd..3/-:: 3 :: Various other measures taken by ESIC for providing medical benefits to increasing number of beneficiaries include:(i)	Approval for setting of 50 new ESI hospitals.(ii)	Empanelment of Insurance Medical Practitioners (IMPs) and Employer Utilization Dispensaries (EUDs). (iii)	Collaboration with Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) for providing medical services through PMJAY empanelled hospitals.  (iv)	Empanelment of Government/ Private hospitals for providing secondary and tertiary care medical services. (v)	Enhancement of sanctioned staff strength of ESIC hospitals.     		 Further, ESI Corporation has taken various steps to improve the medical services provided to beneficiaries in the country, details of which are given below: i.	Formation of state level ESI Societies so that States have financial freedom to take decision for improvement of medical services. ii.	Project Implementation Plan (PIP) for state ESI Scheme.iii.	Enhancement of 50% of bed strength, if bed occupancy is more than 70% in ESIC/ESIS hospitals for last consecutive three years. iv.	ESIC provides Rs. 200/- per IP annum over and above ceiling to the State Government, where the bed occupancy in all the State ESIS hospitals is more than 70% during the concluded financial year.v.	Installation of Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) Oxygen plants and Liquid Medical Oxygen (LMO) tanks in ESI hospitals.vi.	Increasing the availability of   Oxygen concentrators, various type of oxygen cylinders, Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BIPAP) machines, Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) kits, Sanitizers, Medical grade masks etc.(f):		Setting up of new ESI hospitals/ Dispensaries and upgradation of existing hospitals/dispensaries is a continuous and ongoing process. Based on its existing norms/ criteria, ESI Corporation gives approval for setting up of new ESI hospitals/ dispensaries as well as upgradation of existing hospitals/ dispensaries. The States/ UT wise details of these hospitals are placed at Annexure-II.***                                                      *******
