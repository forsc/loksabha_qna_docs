                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2947






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Central University in Ambala




Rattan Lal Kataria














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has given in-principle approval for the proposal to open a Central University in Ambala Lok Sabha Constituency of Haryana;(b)if so, the details thereof;(c)whether a detailed project report (DPR) has been prepared by the Government to open the said University along with the estimated budget earmarked in this regard; (d)  if so, the details thereof;(e)  whether the Government has fixed any time limit and prepared any outline to approve the estimated budget to complete the opening process of the said University within the stipulated time  period; and(f)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (f) Setting up of a Central University is an ongoing process. There is already a Central University in the State of Haryana namely Central University of Haryana. However, at present there is no proposal under consideration in the Ministry for setting up of a Central University in Ambala, Haryana. ****
