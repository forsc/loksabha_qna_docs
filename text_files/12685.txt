                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1538






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Construction of Cold Storage




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of cold storages sanctioned by the Government in various States, State-wise including Bihar so far; (b)	whether the Government proposes to construct cold storages particularly in Bihar and Uttar Pradesh to reduce the storage cost of potato, onion and other vegetables of the farmers in view of the shortage of cold storages/godowns in the said States; (c)	if so, the district-wise and State-wise locations of the proposed cold storages and the details of likely benefits to the farmers and if not, the reasons therefor; (d)	the amount of financial assistance provided by the Government for construction of cold storages during the last three years including the amount of funds released for Bihar; (e)	whether there is any provision to give concessions to the farmers for utilising cold storages constructed with the assistance received from the Government; and (f)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor including the manner in which assistance is being provided to the farmers in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): 	The Government does not construct cold storages by its own. However, Government is implementing following schemes in the country including Bihar and Uttar Pradesh under which assistance is provided for establishing cold storages:(i):  Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH): 	Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) is implementing Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for holistic development of horticulture in the country including Bihar and Uttar Pradesh. Under the scheme, financial assistance is available, inter-alia, for setting up of Post Harvest Management (PHM) infrastructure including cold storages. The component is demand/ entrepreneur-driven from among entrepreneurs, private companies, cooperatives, farmers groups etc for which assistance at the rate of 35% of admissible project cost in general areas and 50% in hilly and scheduled area is available as credit linked back ended subsidy through respective State Horticulture Missions. The funds under the scheme are allocated to States on the basis of Annual Action Plan (AAP) received from State Level Executive Committee (SLEC), availability of resources and requirement of States. (ii):  Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY):Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MOFPI) is implementing a Central Sector Scheme of Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure (now subsumed under PMKSY) to provide integrated cold chain and preservation infrastructure facilities. Under this scheme, MOFPI provides financial assistance in the form of grant-in-aid at the rate of 35% for general area and 50% for North-East States, Himalayan States, ITDP areas and Islands for storage and transport infrastructure subject to a maximum grant-in-aid of Rs. 10.00 crore for setting up of integrated cold chain projects with cold storage as one of the component. State-wise number of cold storages sanctioned upto 31st December, 2019 is at Annexure – I.(d):	 The State –wise funds allocated and released to various States including Bihar under MIDH for setting up of cold storages during last three years are at Annexure – II. Funds are not allocated State-wise under the schemes of National Horticulture Board (NHB) and MOFPI.(e) & (f):	 There is no such provision under the Scheme Guidelines. It is entirely upto the owner of cold storage whether to give any concessions to the farmers for utilising cold storages or not.
