                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1704






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Regulating e- sports




Umesh G. Jadhav













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    B. Y. Raghavendra
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is regulating e-sports and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has recognised that there are new popular apps where games of luck are being promoted as games of skill; and(c) if so, the measures taken by the Government in regulating such apps? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a):	No Sir. However, E-sport, which is in the nature of competitive sport and which has also been included in Asian Games 2022, will be regulated as per the provisions of the National Sports Development Code 2011, to enable participation of sportspersons of e-sport from India in the Asian Games 2022 and other competitive international sports events.  (b) & (c): Online gaming sector is an evolving sector, and with rapid expansion of internet connectivity and use of smart phones, a number of gaming apps have come up. ****
