                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

428






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Funds under PM Kisan Scheme in Tamil Nadu




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of amount spent on PM Kisan scheme in Tamil Nadu particularly to the farmers of Arakkonam Parliamentary Constituency; (b) 	whether the fund allocated under this scheme to Tamil Nadu has been fully utilised and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	District wise details of funds released to farmers under PM-KISAN Scheme in Tamil Nadu is given at Annexure.  (b) & (c):	 No Statewise allocation under PM-KISAN Scheme is made. Funds to the tune of Rs.6000/- per annum per beneficiary are transferred directly to the bank accounts of beneficiaries through DBT in three equal installments, every four months after receipt of verification of beneficiaries from concerned State/UT and their validation through Aadhar/PFMS.
