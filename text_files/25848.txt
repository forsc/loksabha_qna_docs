                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Construction Stone Laid under PMAY-U




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of houses for which construction stone was laid per month as part of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban(PMAY-U) during the last three years, State-wise; (b) the details of the highest number of construction stones laid in a single day for houses in the scheme during the last five years along with the date of such occurrence, State-wise; (c) the details of minimum size determined for the houses constructed in 2021, State-wise; and (d) the details of all the changes made in size dimensions of houses by the States during the last five years? 






ANSWER




                                                                 THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE) (a):State/ Union territory (UT)-wise details of houses grounded for construction during the last three years and per month under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban(PMAY-U)are at Annexure-I.(b):  Based on the number of houses geo-tagged at foundation stage as reported by States/ UTs, details of highest number of houses grounded in a single day with date during last five years are at Annexure-II.(c):  As per Scheme guidelines, the minimum size of houses constructed under the Mission under each component should conform to the standards provided in National Building Code (NBC). If available area of land, however, does not permit building of such minimum size of houses as per NBC and if beneficiary consent is available for reduced size of house, a suitable decision on area may be taken by States/UTs with the approval of State Level Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (SLSMC).The Mission supports construction of houses upto 30 square meter carpet area with basic civic infrastructure. States/UTs have flexibility in terms of determining the size of house and other facilities at the State/UT level in consultation with the Ministry. Upto 60 sqm.	    Upto 160 sqm.
