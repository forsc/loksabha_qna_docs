                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2404






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Opening of Science and Cultural Museum in Bihar




Ram Kripal Yadav













                                                    Chhedi Paswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to open a science and cultural museum in Bihar;(b)	if so, the place and time by when this museum is likely to be opened and the estimated funds likely to be spent on this scheme; and (c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM & DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)to(c): Yes, Sir. National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), an autonomous organization under the Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India is developing a Science Centre at Bodhgaya in the state of Bihar at a cost of Rs.5.00 crores being shared equally between the Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India and State Govt. of Bihar. The project is likely to be ready by March, 2022. Further, State Government of Bihar is developing a Science City viz. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Science City at Patna, under Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science at the total cost of Rs.191.00 crores being shared between the Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India and State Govt. of Bihar. Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India has so far provided financial assistance to the tune of Rs.13.25 crores to State Government of Bihar.*****
