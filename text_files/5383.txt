                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4997






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Empowering of PRIs




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the policy and action plan of the Government to make the Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs) of the country economically capable?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)        The Ministry of Panchayati Raj has been implementing the restructured scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan with effect from the financial year 2018-19 with the primary objective of developing governance capabilities of PRIs to deliver on the Sustainable Development Goals. The scheme envisages enhancing capabilities of Panchayats for formulating holistic Gram Panchayat Development Plan converging resources available at Gram Panchayat level for economic development and social justice. The scheme also aims at enhancing capabilities of Panchayats to raise their own sources of revenue and provides for interventions relating to gap filling support for economic development and income enhancement in Panchayats.          Further, under the Fourteenth Finance Commission  for the award period 2015-2020, grants to the tune of Rs. 2,00,292.20 crore are being devolved to gram Panchayats, amounting to an assistance of Rs. 488 per capita per annum at an aggregate level to the Gram Panchayats constituted under Part IX of the Constitution to 26 States for delivering basic services, namely, water supply, sanitation, sewerage, solid waste management, storm water drainage, maintenance of community assets, roads, footpath, street lighting, burial and cremation grounds and any other basic services entrusted under State Legislations etc.**********
