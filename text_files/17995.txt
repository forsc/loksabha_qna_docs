                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1705






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Development Works in Lucknow Division




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the developmental works undertaken in Lucknow division for railway passengers at each station of Lucknow division under the North-Eastern railways during the last three years;(b) whether the Government proposes to undertake any developmental works under this division in the near future; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	to (c) The list of developmental works undertaken for railway passengers at stations of Lucknow Division under North Eastern Railway during the last three years is appended.Further, upgradation/modernization of stations on Indian Railways is a continuous and on-going process and works in this regard are undertaken as per requirement, subject to inter-se priority and availability of funds. However, priority for upgradation/modernization of stations is accorded to higher category of station over lower category of station while sanctioning and executing the work.*****
