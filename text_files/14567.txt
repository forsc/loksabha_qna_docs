                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3273






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Colleges and Research Centres under AYUSH




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) the details of the colleges and research centres which have been inaugurated under the AYUSH Ministry; (b) the steps taken by the Government to promote research and development in Ayurveda; (c) the amount spent on establishing such research centres; and (d) the total amount of the budget allocation utilised in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a),(c) & (d):     The details of the colleges and research Centres which have been inaugurated under the Ministry of AYUSH and the amount spent on establishing such research Centres, the amount of the budget allocation utilized in this regard is as under:S.No.	Name of the Institution	Project cost	Total expenditure till date1.	National Institute of Homoeopathy (NIH) Narela, Delhi as an extension of NIH, Kolkata	Rs. 259.12 Crore	72.40 Lakhs2.	National Institute of Unani Medicine (NIUM) Ghaziabad, UP as an extension of NIUM, Bangalore:	Rs. 339.68 Crore	Rs.14.12 lakh3.	All India Institute of Ayurveda, (AIIA), Phase - II Sarita Vihar, Delhi:	 Rs.228.21 Crore	Rs. 25.22 Lakhs4.	  NISARG GRAM, NIN Pune as an up-gradation of National Institute of Naturopathy, Pune	Rs. 170.29 Crore	Rs. 3.30 lakhs5.	National Institute of Ayurveda (NIA), Panchkula, Haryana as an extension of NIA, Jaipur	Rs. 270.54 Crore	Rs 0.67 Lakhs6.	All India Institute of Ayurveda, (AIIA) Goa and extension of  of AIIA, Sarita Vihar-Delhi	Rs.267.81 Crore	Rs. 13.27 Lakhs7.	North Eastern Institute of Ayurveda and Homeopathy(NEIAH), Phase –II, Shillong 	Rs. 56.03 Crore	Rs. 15.71 Lakhs8.	National Institute of Homoeopathy, Kolkata Phase –II	Rs. 37.08 Crore	Rs.18.23 lakhs 9.	New OPD for NIS, Chennai  	Rs. 31.66 Crore	Rs. 12.31 Lakhs(b):      The Ministry of AYUSH has established Central Council for Research in Ayurveda Sciences (CCRAS) which is an apex organization for developing and promoting research in Ayurvedic system of medicine. The National Institute of Ayurveda, Jaipur, All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA) have been established for the Ministry of AYUSH for Promotion, Research, Development and the Health Service delivery to the public. .....................
