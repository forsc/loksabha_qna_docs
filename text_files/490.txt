                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

431






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Upliftment of Farmers




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to formulate any special scheme for the upliftment of the farmers; (b)	 if so, whether the Government has taken any step in this direction yet; and (c)	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Agriculture is a State subject.  Government of India facilitates the State Governments through Centrally Sponsored Schemes and Central Sector Schemes, which are for the upliftment of farmers.  A list of schemes administered by the Department of Agriculture Cooperation, Farmers’ Welfare is annexed at Annexure-I.(c):	The financial allocation for all Centrally Sponsored and Central Sector Schemes during the last 3 years is as under:-(Rs. In Crore)Category	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Budget Estimate	35983.69	41105.00	46700.00Revised Estimate	39840.50	41855.00	67800.00Actual Release	37029.82	37538.77	46583.50*Actuals is provisional for 2018-19
