                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






MSP for Agricultural Products




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the list of the agricultural products purchased under the Minimum Support Price Scheme in the last three years; and (b) 	the number of agencies through which foodgrains have been purchased including the quantum of foodgrains purchased at Minimum Support Price and the product-wise status in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	List of the agricultural products purchased at Minimum Support Price (MSP) during the last three years and current year is at Annexure-I.(b):	Foodgrains have been purchased at MSP by three Central agencies viz., Food Corporation of India (FCI), National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Ltd.(NAFED) and Small Farmers Agri-Business Consortium (SFAC). The state-wise details of foodgrains purchased at MSP are at Annexure-II to Annexure-V.
