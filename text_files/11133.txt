                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

10






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Skill Training Centres in Delhi




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Skill training centres in Delhi along with the details thereof;(b)	the details of the amount allocated for development of skill training centres in Delhi vis-a-vis other States;(c)	the details of division/break up between male and female candidates taking skill training at training centres;(d)	whether the Government has taken any steps to promote women participation in skill training programmes and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether the Government is also looking to entering into skill-sharing agreements with other countries like Japan and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.	   STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (E) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 10 TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.02.2020 ASKED BY SHRI PARVESH SAHIB SINGH VERMA REGARDING SKILL TRAINING CENTRES IN DELHI(a) to (c)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship  is implementing Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20, for imparting short duration skill development training through empanelled training centre / training provider across the country including Delhi. PMKVY 2016-20 does not mandate the establishment of permanent skill development centres (except Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras - PMKKs). As on 17.01.2020, 371 Training Centres (TCs) are empanelled under PMKVY (2016-20) in Delhi State.Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship also promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) in every district for imparting skill training throughout the country. Capital Expenditure upto 75% of the project investment as well as operational support provided through implementing agency, National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). As on 17.01.2020, 9 PMKKs have been established in NCT of Delhi. Further, Scheme of Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) for skill development is being implemented through its institutional framework in the country with the objective to improve the occupational skills and technical knowledge of the non/neo literate. As on date, 248 JSSs are set up across the country including 3 JSSs in NCT of Delhi.In addition to above short-term training, Directorate General of Training (DGT) under MSDE is implementing long duration vocational courses through Industrial Training Institutes (ITI) which offer range of one or two years’ vocational /skill training courses covering a large number of economic sectors with an objective to provide skilled work force to the industry as well as self-employment of youth. 15,697 ITIs are providing long term vocational training courses all over India. Out of these, 60 ITIs are there in Delhi. The State-wise details of funds disbursed under CSSM component of PMKVY 2016-20, and JSSs, as on 17.01.2020, is given at Annexure I. The State-wise number of male and female candidates trained under PMKVY (2016-20), ITIs (2016-18) and JSSs (2018-20), as on 17.01.2020 are given at Annexure II.(d)	Under PMKVY (2016-20), there are certain incentives to encourage women participation in the skill training. All women trainees under PMKVY is provided with travelling allowance of Rs. 1000/1500 per trainee per month depending upon whether TC is within district of Domicile of candidate or outside. Similarly, post placement support of Rs. 1500 per month per trainee is applicable for special group including woman for 2 or 3 month post training depending on placement within or outside the district of the domicile of the candidate.(e)	Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship has signed various MoUs/agreements with many countries including Japan. Ministry has signed Memorandum of Cooperation with Japan in the sectors covering training of Future Shop Leaders and technical Intern Training Programme. Further, NSDC under MSDE has signed various MoUs with 5 different countries for cooperation in the field of skill development.
