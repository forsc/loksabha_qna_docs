                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Handing over of Coal Mines to Private Companies




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       whether Singareni Collieries Ltd. which is a joint venture of Government of India and Telangana Government has handed over any mines to private companies and if so, the details thereof;          (b)       the reasons for these mines being handed over to private companies;(c)        the details of amount of coal that has been mined by private companies from the mines handed over by Singareni Collieries Ltd., year wise since the mines have been handed over; and(d)       the total amount of coal production by Singareni Collieries Ltd. in the last ten years, year wise?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)   (a):                No mines of SCCL are handed over to private companies.(b) :               Not applicable in view of answer given to part (a) above.(c):               Not applicable in view of answer given to part (a) above.(d) :               The details of coal produced by Singareni Collieries Ltd. during last ten years, year wise are as below: Year	2010-11	2011- 12	2012-13	2013- 14	2014- 15	2015- 16	2016-17	2017-18	2018- 19	2019- 20Production (Million Tonnes)	51.33	52.21	53.19	50.47	52.54	60.38	61.34	62.01	64.4	64.04               *****
