                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4898






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Road Projects in North Eastern States




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the centrally sponsored projects being implemented in North Eastern States (NES), State-wise including Assam and Bodoland Territorial Council area;(b)	the names of National Highways in Assam along with the expenditure incurred on their maintenance during the last three years;(c)	the names of the companies which are engaged to implement the said projects in NES; and(d)	the details and the present status of these projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a),(c) and (d) The details of the centrally sponsored projects being implemented in North Eastern States (NES), State-Wise including AssamAssam and Bodoland Territorial Council area are enclosed in Annexure-I  (b) NH No. 2, 6, 8, 15, 17, 27, 29, 37, 115, 117, 117A,127, 127A, 127B, 127C, 127D, 127E, 129, 137, 208A, 215, 217, 306, 315, 315A, 329, 329A, 415, 427, 515, 627, 702, 702C, 715,715A, and 715K pass through the State Assam.  Expenditure incurred on maintenance in the State of Assam during the year 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 was Rs.148.08 Crore, Rs.161.25 Crore and Rs.254.36 Crore respectively.
