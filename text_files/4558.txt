                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






New Airport in Delhi/NCR




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to establish any new airport in Delhi/NCR and to start regional flights from the existing airports, if any;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) the details of the plan layout and the time limit in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Government of India has granted ''in-principle'' approval to Noida International Airport Limited (NIAL) for construction of new airport at Jewar, Uttar Pradesh.The project is being implemented by Government of Uttar Pradesh through NIAL.  The bid documents including concession agreement have been finalized and approved by Government of Uttar Pradesh. NIAL has issued e-tender on 30.05.2019 for selection of the concessionaire with last date of receiving the bid as 30.10.2019 with the  projected year of operationalisation of the airport as 2023-2024.  Government of India has also granted 1st stage ''''site clearance'''' approval to Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor Development Corporation Limited (DMICDC) for setting up of a Greenfield Airport at Bhiwadi, District Alwar, Rajasthan on 18th November, 2015. Further development of the project depends on submission of  Detailed Project Report (DPR) by the project proponent i.e. DMICDC for grant of ''in-principle'' approval by steering committee on greenfield airports.  Besides, under the   Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS)-Ude Desh Ka Aam Nagrik (UDAN) of Government of India, demand for flight operations  to/from Hindon Air Base  has been received. Ministry of Defence has agreed for operationlisation of civilian flights under RCS and accordingly, routes have been awarded to the selected airline operators. The details are at Annexure-A. Airports Authority of India has developed a civil enclave at Hindon Airport and has completed other related  works for commencement of flight operations. ;
