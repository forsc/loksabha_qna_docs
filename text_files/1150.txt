                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1093






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Supply of Yamuna Water to Rajasthan




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether appropriate share of Yamuna water is not being given to Rajasthan from Tajewala Head despite the allocation by Upper Yamuna River Board; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Union Government proposes to direct Haryana State to give Yamuna water from Tajewala Head for Churu and Jhunjhunu districts of Rajasthan; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	(a) & (b)  Rajasthan gets its share of Yamuna water from Tajewala and Okhla distribution points. Rajasthan is not getting its allocated share of Yamuna water from Tajewala Barrage due to the non-existence of any agreement between the States of Rajasthan and Haryana towards canal system to transport Rajasthan’s share enroute Haryana territory.(c)	(c) & (d)   In the 7th meeting of Upper Yamuna Review Committee held on 15.02.2018 with participation of all co-basin States including Haryana, the Govt. of Rajasthan was advised to prepare a Detailed Project Report (DPR) for “Transfer of Rajasthan’s share of Yamuna Water at Tajewala Headworks to Churu and Jhunjhnu District of Rajasthan by underground conveyance system”.  The same has been submitted by Govt. of Rajasthan in Central Water Commission. *****
