                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

950






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Shortage of Fertilizers in Kerala




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a) 	whether Kerala has been facing acute shortage of fertilizers and if so, the details thereof;(a)	whether the shortage is attributed to less supply of fertilizers to the State and if   so, the response of the Government thereto; and(b)	whether the farmers in the State are in distress due to shortage of fertilizers and  if so, the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS & HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)  (a) & (b):  As on date, fertilizers availability in the State of Kerala is 100% of the requirement projected by the State.
