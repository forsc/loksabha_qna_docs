                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3062






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Security Check at Airports




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to  adopt  any  mechanism  to  save  time  of passengers during security check at airports across  the  country  and  if  so,  the  details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to  install  automatic  ray  retrieval  systems at airports and if so, the details thereof; and(c)  whether  the  Government  is planning to introduce Biometric ID checking system there and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Yes, Sir. Ministry of Civil Aviation has taken up the ''Digi Yatra'' initiative which envisages seamless and hassle-free passenger processing at Indian airports, without the need for verification of ticket and ID at multiple touch points. This project also aims to simplify passenger processes at various check points in the airport; right from the terminal entry gate, check-in/bag drop, security check and boarding gates. ;(b): Automatic Tray Retrieval System (ATRS) has been installed at Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Hyderabad. In-principle approval has been accorded to Indira Gandhi International Airport, Delhi to run trials of the ATRS for six months. A survey was also conducted at Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International (NSCBI) airport, Kolkata for installation of the ATRS. (c): Yes, Sir. The architecture of Digi Yatra is based on entry into airports through biometric I.D.
