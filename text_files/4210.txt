                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3840






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Food Safety Standards Act




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Tamil Nadu Foodgrains Merchants Association Ltd. has requested the    Government to publish the Food Safety Standards Act, 2006, Rules and Regulations, 2011 in Tamil language for easy understanding by the roadside vendors and small merchants; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; and (c)	 if so, the action taken by the Union Government on this request?  






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)  (a) to (c):  Food Safety and Standards Authority of India under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has not received any such a request.  However, as per the provisions of Official Languages Act 1963, Acts, Rules and Regulations, notified by the Union Government, are required to be published in English and Hindi only.  State Governments may, however, arrange to provide translation in local language, if they wish to do so.    *****
