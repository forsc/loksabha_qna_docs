                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2592






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






BharatNet Project in Odisha




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether all Gram Panchayats in the State of Odisha were connected with broadband services under the BharatNet project by the end of August, 2019 and if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	if not, the target fixed by the Government for providing broadband services to the remaining Gram Panchayats in the State?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) 	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country, including Odisha. Odisha has a total of 6775 (approx.) Gram Panchayats (GPs). As of August, 2019, by laying 12,375 km Optical Fibre Cable (OFC), a total of 3977 GPs have been connected and of them, 3656 have been made Service Ready. In addition, 15 GPs have been made Service Ready over satellite media.(b)	In Odisha, the remaining Gram Panchayats under BharatNet project are targeted to be provided with broadband connectivity by March 2020.************
