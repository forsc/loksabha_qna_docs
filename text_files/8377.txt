                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1874






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Contractual Employees at AIIMS, Jodhpur




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the number and details of personnel engaged in All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) located at Jodhpur on contract basis at present including the names of various departments where they are working; (b) the names of security agencies which have been hired by the said AIIMS during the last two years including the terms and conditions of their appointment along with the comparative details of the tender issued for this purpose and the proposals received against such tenders; and (c) the names of security agencies and other agencies which have been given payment by AIIMS, Jodhpur during the last two years including the amount of money paid along with the names of each such work for which the payment was made?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY) (a) to (c):    Total 303 contractual employees are working in AIIMS, Jodhpur. M/s Kore Security Service, Delhi has been engaged and is providing security services w.e.f. 01.08.2016 in AIIMS Jodhpur as per terms and conditions of agreement dated 10.06.2016 against notice inviting tender no. Admn/Tender/007/2015-AIIMS.JDH issued dated 03.09.2015. The details of personnel engaged on contractual basis in AIIMS Jodhpur, details of above stated tender issued on 03.09.2015 & agreement dated 10.06.2016 and comparative details of the tender issued and the proposals received against such tenders as technical and financial bids can be accessed on website of PMSSY Division on following link: http://pmssy-mohfw.nic.in/files/AnnexuretoPQLS1874.pdfThe Details regarding names of security agencies and other agencies engaged in AIIMS Jodhpur for various purposes and payment made are mentioned in Annexure.
