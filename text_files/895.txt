                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

826






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Khamgaon-Jalna Rail Line




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether 26 crores of rupees have been allocated for construction of Khamgaon-Jalna new rail lines in Buldhana district of Maharashtra State in the budget of 2017-18 and survey report has been submitted to Railways during the year of 2012-13;(b)	whether 3 thousand crores of rupees have been allocated to construct the proposed Khamgaon-Jalna new rail line in capital investment programme of 2016-17 in the rail budget on 25th February, 2016; (c)	if so, the details thereof; (d)	the updated position of the said railway project; and (e)	the time by when construction works of this rail line is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e):	  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 826 BY SHRI PRATAPRAO JADHAV TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 26.06.2019 REGARDING KHAMGAON - JALNA RAIL LINE(a):	   An updating survey for new line from Khamgaon-Jalna (155 km) was completed in 2011-12.  As per survey report, cost of Khamgaon-Jalna new line was assessed as `1027 crore with Rate of Return of (-) 4.26%.  Due to unremunerative nature of the project, huge throwforward of ongoing projects and limited overall availability of funds, this project could not be taken forward.(b) & (c):	Further, Khamgaon-Jalna (155 km) new line project was included in Capital Investment Programme 2016-17 at an anticipated cost of `3000 crore in anticipation of State Government coming forward to take up the project through Joint Venture but Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Limited (MRIDL), a Joint Venture Company of Government of Maharashtra and Ministry of Railways has not yet identified this project for taking up under Joint Venture mode.(d):    However, a Reconnaissance Engineering-cum-Traffic Survey for this new line has been sanctioned in the month of February, 2019. Survey work has been taken by Railway.(e):     Does not arise.*****
