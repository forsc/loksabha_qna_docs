                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2113






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Guruvayur-Thirunavaya Railway Line




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to finish the work of Guruvayur-Thirunavaya railway line and if so, the details thereof; and (b)	the current status of the said project?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b):     The New Line Project between Guruvayur and Tirunavaya for a length of 35 Km was sanctioned in 1995-96 at a cost of ` 137.71 crore. Latest anticipated cost of project is ` 477 crore. Final Location Survey (FLS) could be done only for about 8 Km upto Kunnamkulam. Though an Agency was fixed for final location survey between Kunnmkulam & Tirunavaya and the trial survey to finalise the final alignment was already completed, the FLS had to be stopped due to severe public protest against the alignment.FLS could not be taken up further due to continuous public protest against the alignment. *****
