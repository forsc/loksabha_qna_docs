                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3214






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Construction Work on NH-709B




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to build/expand/extend National Highway-709B;(b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof; and(c) 	the time-frame set for the purpose? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	to (c) Yes Sir. NH-709B originates from its junction with NH-9 near Akshardham Metro Station and terminates at its junction with NH-344 near Saharanpur.The length of the NH-709B is 155 km. Development of NH-709B is included under Bharatmala Pariyojana. For the initial stretch of 31 Km from Akshardham Metro Station to Eastern Peripheral Expressway (EPE) junction, the DPR studies have been completed. Thework of development to fourlane in balance 124 km length from EPE crossing near Khekra, Baghpat in Uttar Pradesh till Saharanpur bypass has been awarded in two packages. The present progress of the two packages is 60% and 5% respectively. The targeted date of completion is 30.09.2021.*****
