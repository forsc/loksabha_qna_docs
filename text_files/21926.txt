                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3136






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






IT Professionals in Employment Survey




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government proposes to conduct surveys for all classes of labourers, domestic and migrant workers to yield critical data to address the labour and employment issue and drafting policy; (b)if so, the details thereof;(c)whether the Government is digitalizing this survey through IT professionals; and(d)if so, the details of the technological support providers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	Labour Bureau, an attached office of the Ministry of Labour & Employment has been entrusted with the task of conducting the following All- India Surveys: -(i)    All India Survey on Migrant Workers;(ii)   All India Survey on Domestic Workers;(iii)  All India Survey on Employment Generated by Professionals (iv) All India Survey on Employment Generated in the Transport Sector, and;(v) All India Quarterly Establishment based Employment Survey (AQEES). (c) & (d):	Yes, Sir. The Government intends to conduct these surveys in digitalization mode by leveraging and utilizing latest Information Technology (IT) tools alogwith engaging technological support providers.*****
