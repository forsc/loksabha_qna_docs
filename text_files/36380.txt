                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3422






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






SATIS-2 at Thane Station




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the work of Station Area Traffic Improvement System (SATIS-2) in east Kopri has not been started due to non-approval by the Indian Railway Stations Development Corporation Limited (IRSDC), Delhi;(b) if so, the reasons therefor and the time by which the said work is likely to be started;(c) whether the Jupiter Hospital, Thane had requested for providing emergency medical services at Thane Railway Station on which no decision has been taken till date; and(d) if so, the reasons therefor and the time by which a decision is likely to be taken in this regard ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3422 BY SHRI RAJAN VICHARE TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 23.03.2022 REGARDING SATIS-2 AT THANE STATION(a)&(b):  No, Sir. The work of ‘Station Area Traffic Improvement Scheme’ (SATIS-2) on Thane Station East side (East Kopri) in the railway circulating area is being executed by Thane Smart City Limited (TSCL), an SPV of Thane Municipal Corporation (TMC).  The preliminary work like protection piles has started. General Arrangement Drawings (GADs) of the work are being prepared by TSCL/TMC and Zonal Railway intends to approve the same expeditiously upon its submission.(c):   No, Sir.(d):  Does not arise.*****
