                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1656






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Defence Agreements and Defence Corridors




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah













                                                    Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar
                                                








                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has signed defence agreements with various countries including France to ensure security of border of the country during the last three years;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the defence products made by the Government under ‘Make in India’ programme;(d) whether the Government has any proposal to set up defence corridor in Uttrakahand also;(e) if so, the details thereof and the present status of the defence corridors being set up in the country; and(f) the scheme formulated to set up more defence corridors in future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b): The Government has not signed any Defence Agreement with France and other countries to ensure security at the border of India in the last three years.(c) Many significant projects including 155mm Artillery Gun, Light Combat Aircraft ‘Tejas’, ‘Akash’ Surface to Air Missile System, Submarines etc, have been produced in India as part of defence production in the country.(d)  No, Sir.(e) & (f):  In pursuance to the budget announcement (2018-19), it has been decided to set up two Defence Industrial Corridors in the country, one in Uttar Pradesh and another in Tamil Nadu.*****
