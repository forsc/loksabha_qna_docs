                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






UGC & AICTE




P. Velusamy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)the number of pending proposals/ applications with University Grants Commission (UGC) or All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for opening of new engineering colleges in the country;(b)	whether a large number of seats remained vacant in many engineering colleges due to under utilization during the last three years, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	State/UT-wise details of number of closed engineering colleges in the country?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)	NIL.(b)	A few seats have remained vacant in Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) approved engineering colleges.(c)	 State/UT-wise details of number of engineering colleges that closed in the country in the last three years is enclosed at Annexure.*****
