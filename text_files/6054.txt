                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5599






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether each and every district of country is benefited by Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK); (b) if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise particularly Bihar; (c) the funds allocated for the said scheme during the last three years, State/ UT-wise particularly Bihar; and(d) the number of youths benefited through RKSK programme in Bihar since its inception, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Government of India is supporting all the States/UTs   in implementing the Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK) in the Country as per proposals received from the States/UTs in their Program Implementation Plans. The detailed information is placed at  Annexure-1(c):		The detailed information is placed at Annexure-2 (d):	The detailed information is placed at Annexure-3
