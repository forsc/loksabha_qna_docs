                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1444






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






House Constructed under PMAY-G




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) in Gujarat State;(b) 	the details of houses constructed under PMAY-G from 2014-2019, State-wise;(c) 	whether the target of 60 lakh houses announced in the budget 2019-2020 has been achieved; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The status of implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G) in the State of Gujarat from 2016 to 18.09.2020 is given below:                                                                               (Unit in Nos.)Target	Registered	Sanctioned	1st Installment sanctioned 	Completed466678	318498	312049	305839	239714 (b):	The Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin is being implemented with effect from 2016-17 onwards. The State-wise details of houses constructed under PMAY-G during 2016-17 to 2019-20 are given at Annexure. (c) & (d): Against the target of 60 lakh houses, as on 18.09.2020, a total of 54,02,885 houses have been sanctioned for the FY 2019-20 as reported by States/UTs on AwaasSoft.          Further, as on 18.09.2020, a total of 25,40,770 PMAY-G houses have been completed against FY 2019-20 sanctions.********* (Annexure)Annexure referred to in reply to part (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1444 to be answered on 20.09.2020 Houses Constructed under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin from 2016-17 to 2019-20
