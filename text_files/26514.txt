                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2117






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Schemes to Promote Tourism in Haryana and Assam




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Queen Oja
                                                








                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is contemplating any scheme to promote tourism in Haryana and Assam and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has identified the places for the purpose and if so, the details thereof district and location-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from Haryana Government in this regard; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and effective steps taken by the Government in this direction?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	As part of its on-going promotional activities, the Ministry of Tourism releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country. The campaigns include Haryana and Assam as tourist destinations. Specific State wise promotions are taken up by the respective State Departments. The Ministry of Tourism does this through its schemes of ‘Domestic Promotion & Publicity including Hospitality (DPPH)’ and Overseas Promotion & Publicity including Market Development Assistance (OPMD).Under the Domestic Promotion Publicity and Hospitality (DPPH) Scheme, Ministry of Tourism provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) to State Governments / Union Territory Administrations for organizing fairs & festivals and tourism related events for the promotion of tourism. The details of funds sanctioned under Central Financial Assistance (CFA) for fairs & festivals and shopping festivals for Haryana and Assam are placed in the attached Annexure.   ********
