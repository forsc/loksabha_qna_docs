                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4407






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Electronic Nicotine Delivery Systems




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether taking into consideration the adverse effects of using Electronic Nicotine Delivery Systems (ENDS), the States/UTs have banned the sale and use of ENDS products, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government is considering a nation-wise ban on ENDS products, if so, the details thereof; and (c) whether the Government is likely to introduce punishment for the use of ENDS products after the ban comes into force and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):    The State Governments of Punjab, Karnataka, Mizoram, Kerala, Jammu & Kashmir, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Jharkhand, Himachal Pradesh, Puducherry, Rajasthan, Meghalaya and Odisha have prohibited the Electronic Nicotine Delivery Systems (ENDS).(b) & (c):   The matter is under consideration.……………….
