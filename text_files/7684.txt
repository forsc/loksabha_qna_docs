                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Prices of Petrol and Diesel




K. Jayakumar













                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of highest and lowest price of petrol and diesel during the last 10 years along with the present cost of crude per barrel and the sale price of petrol and diesel in the country;(b) whether there is a price hike for petrol and diesel in the country, if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c) whether it is true that the petrol and diesel price hike helps the Government to build more toilets in the country, if so, the details with explanation thereof; and(d) whether there is any relation between the international crude oil price and the price of petrol in the country, if so, the details thereof along with the reasons for not reducing the price of petrol by the Government in proportion with the reducing international crude oil prices?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d)The details of highest and lowest price of petrol and diesel at Delhi during the last 10 years (since 2009-10) are given as under: -` /LitreParticularsPetrolDieselHighest*84.00 (As on 04.10.18)75.69 (As on 16.10.18)Lowest**40.62 (As on 01.04.09)30.86 (As on 01.04.09)* Deregulated Prices ** Regulated PricesThe current prices of Indian Basket of crude oil, petrol and diesel in the international market vis-à-vis domestic market (in Delhi as per IOCL) are given below: -Prices as onIndian Basket of Crude oilPetrolDieselPetrolDiesel$/bbl`/Litre18th November 201963.0771.8573.8374.0565.79Price of petrol has been made market-determined by the Government with effect from 26.06.2010. Since then, the Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) are competent to take appropriate decision on pricing of petrol in line with international product prices, exchange rate, tax structure, inland freight and other cost elements. OMCs amongst other activities, also build toilets from the fund under Corporate Social Responsibility.x-x-x-x-x
