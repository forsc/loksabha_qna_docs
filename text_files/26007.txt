                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Baihata Chariali to Jamui Gadhi Highway




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of construction work of highway from Baihata 	Chariali to Jamui Gadhi on NH-115;(b) 	whether the Government has fixed any time limit for completion 	of construction of the said highway in view of its strategic 	importance; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken by the 	Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)- The stretch from Baihata Chariali to Jamui Gadhi does not fall on NH-115. However, the stretch of Baihata Chariali to Jamui Gadhi in the State of Assam is on NH-15 which has already been developed to 2-lane with paved shoulders except construction of a new bridge over river Dhansiri, which is targeted for  completion by Sept, 2022.*****
