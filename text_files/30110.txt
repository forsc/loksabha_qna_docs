                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Construction of New Sports Stadiums




Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of new sports stadiums constructed throughout the country during the last five years with latest infrastructure for the benefit of sportspersons and youth;(b) the details of funds allocated for the said purpose, funds spent so far and status of stadiums which are under construction, State-wise;(c) whether the Government has any plan to construct Olympic level Sports Complex in Satara district of Maharashtra as it has rich tradition of wrestling and other sports; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *138 ANSWERED ON 07.12.2021 REGARDING “CONSTRUCTION OF NEW SPORTS STADIUMS” ASKED BY SHRI SHRINIWAS PATIL, HON’BLE MEMBER OF LOK SABHA(a) and (b) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the primary responsibility to construct stadiums in the country rests with the State/Union Territory Governments. The Union Government supplements their efforts. The sports stadia in various parts of the country are under the jurisdiction of the respective bodies/departments of State/UT Governments. As such, the information about them is not maintained in this Ministry. However, this Ministry has sanctioned 267 sports infrastructure projects of various categories amounting to ? 2244.52 crore across the country under the Khelo India Scheme for the benefit of sportspersons and youth.(c) Khelo India Scheme is a demand driven scheme under which projects are sanctioned on the basis of proposals received from various State/UT Governments and other eligible entities from time to time. This Ministry has not received any proposal to construct Olympic level Sports Complex in Satara district of Maharashtra.(d) Does not arise.****
