                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

79






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Capacity of National Highways




Jugal Kishore Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is considering to extend and increase the capacity of National Highways in Jammu and Kashmir (J&K);(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the quota of J&K under the Central Road and Infrastructure Fund Act; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) :	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENTSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 79 FOR ANSWER ON 21.11.2019 ASKED BY SHRI JUGAL KISHORE SHARMA REGARDING ‘CAPACITY OF NATIONAL HIGHWAYS’.--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------(a)	& (b) Yes, Sir.  The details of National Highways in J&K for upgradation and increasing their      capacity are Annexed.      (c) & (d) The funds for various infrastructure sectors are to be earmarked as per the provisions under 7A of the Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF), Act 2000 amended by the Finance Act, 2018 and 2019. The share of the Fund to be apportioned to each of infrastructure projects are finalized by a Committee, headed by the Finance Minister, depending on the priorities of the project.  *****
