                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

312






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






PCPIRs




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is implementing four Petroleum, Chemicals and Petrochemical Investment Region (PCPIRs) at Dahej, Visakhapatnam-Kakinada, Paradeep and Cuddalore and Nagapattinam and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is true that projects at Dahej and Paradeep have been started and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Union Government has asked the Government of Andhra Pradesh for Viability Gap Funding (VGP), if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Union Government has asked the Government of Gujarat and Odisha for Viability Gap Funding for implementing PCPIRs and if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Union Government has not asked for VGP from Dahej and Paradeep, if so, the reasons therefor and the reasons for asking VGP from Government of Andhra Pradesh; and(f) the rate of return from the above two projects in Dahej and Paradeep?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) and (b) : Department of Chemicals & Petrochemicals (DCPC)has informed that it is implementing four Petroleum, Chemicals and Petrochemical Investment Regions (PCPIRs) at Dahej, Visakhapatnam-Kakinada, Paradeep and Cuddalore & Nagapattinam which are at different stages of implementation. The Anchor Projects of PCPIRs have been commissioned at Dahej (Gujarat) and Paradeep (Odisha) in month of March,2017 and February, 2016 respectively.(c) to (f) : As per PCPIR Policy, 2007, the Central Government provides necessary funding to ensure availability of external physical infrastructure linkages to the PCPIR including connectivity through Railways, Roads, Ports, Airports and Telecom. This infrastructure is created or upgraded, through Public Private Partnership projects to the extent possible. The Central Government also provides necessary funding to make such projects viable as well as budget support for creation of these linkages through respective Ministries including necessary Viability Gap Funding (VGF) through existing schemes.  However, DCPC has not asked the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh, Govt. of Gujarat (Dahej) and Govt. of Odisha (Paradeep) for VGF.**********
