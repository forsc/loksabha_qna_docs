                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1209






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Allocation of Funds to Chhattisgarh




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated under various items and schemes by the Union Government to Chhattisgarh State for the financial years 2019-2020 and 2020-2021; (b) whether the loan facility is provided to the State Government by the Union Government; and (c) if so, the details of loan taken by the Chhattisgarh Government during the financial years 2019-2020 and 2020-2021?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) (a): The Union Government provides resources to States under Finance Commission transfers (including devolution from divisible pool of central taxes and duties) and other grants-in-aid including Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS), Central Sector Schemes (CS) etc. as per allocations provided in the Union Budget. Funds to the State Governments including the State Government of Chhattisgarh are released by the concerned Ministries/Departments as per extant scheme guidelines and taking into consideration the pace of utilization of funds. The details of allocations to the State of Chhattisgarh for the Finance Commission transfers as per the Union Budget: 2020-21, Report of the 14th Finance Commission and Report of 15th Finance Commission are as follows:(Rs. in crore)S. No.	Description	Allocation for the year 2019-20	Allocation for the year 2020-211	Devolution of Central Taxes and Duties	20205.84     (2019-20 RE)	26803.30(2020-21 BE)2	Grants-in-Aid of which		(a)	State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF)/ State Disaster Risk Management Fund (SDRMF)	263.00	432.00(b)	Local Bodies Grants of which		(i)	Grants to Urban Local Bodies (ULBs)	485.89	700.00(ii)	Grants to Rural Local Bodies (RLBs)	1588.94	1454.00(b) and (c): Yes Sir. The Union Government under Article 293 of the Constitution of India provides consent to the State Governments including the State Government of Chhattisgarh for borrowings with in the territory of India and also passes on grants-in-aid /loans for Externally Aided Projects (EAPs).
