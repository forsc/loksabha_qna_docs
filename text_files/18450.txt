                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2199






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Chopper Services in Government Hospitals




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to introduce chopper services in important and premier Government hospitals across the country to ferry critical patients to save lives; and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):    There is no proposal to introduce chopper services under National Health Mission (NHM). Public health and hospitals is a State subject, as such  all matters  relating to infrastructure in public health facilities including patient transport lies with the respective State/UT Governments. Under National Health Mission (NHM), technical and financial support is provided to States/UTs to strengthen their healthcare systems including support for emergency medical transport of patients under  the Dial 108 and  Dial 102 ambulance services based on their proposals through Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs).
