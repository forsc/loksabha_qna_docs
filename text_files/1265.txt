                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

120






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Textile Park




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government proposes to set up a large scale textile park in Chikkodi in Karnataka considering the availability of manpower, logistics in the area and agricultural produce from the vicinity; and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): The statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. * 120 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 28.06.2019(a) & (b): Ministry of Textiles has not received any proposal for setting up Textile Park at Chikkodi, Karnataka.The Government is implementing the Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP) which provides support for creation of world-class infrastructure facilities for textile units. The scheme provides grant upto 40% of the project cost with ceiling limit of Rs. 40.00 crores for each park. The Scheme is a demand driven scheme, in which the prospective entrepreneurs can send their proposals to the Government, after fulfilling certain precedent requirements. ****
