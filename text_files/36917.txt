                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4012






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Crash Course for Covid Warriors under PMKVY




Bhola Singh













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has conducted customized crash course programme for covid warriors under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 3.0 to meet the upsurge in demand of skilled healthcare professionals and provide timely healthcare services in every corner of the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the number of job roles offered under the above crash course;(d)	the details of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras (PMKKs) and other affiliated Training Centres (TCs), State-wise;(e)	the number of candidates enrolled and number of candidates classroom trained under the above programme; and(f)	the other steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Yes, Sir. Ministry is implementing special programme, ''Customized Crash Course Programme for COVID Warriors (CCCP for CW)'' with the aim to train about one lakh COVID warriors. The objectives of CCCP for CW are to meet the upsurge in demand of skilled healthcare workers, reduce the burden of healthcare professionals and provide timely healthcare services in the country. (c)        Under CCCP for CW, training is being imparted in six customized job roles namely Basic Care Support, Home Care Support, Advanced Care Support, Sample Collection Support, Emergency Care Support and Medical Equipment Support. (d)       The State-wise list of training centres (TCs) including PMKKs providing training under the CCCP for CW is given at Annexure. (e)       As on 28.02.2022, 1,49,750 candidates have been classroom trained under fresh skilling and 9,908 candidates have been oriented under up-skilling. Out of classroom trained candidates under fresh skilling, 1,18,386 candidates are enrolled for On-the-job training (OJT). The State-wise details are given at Annexure. (f)        In order to mitigate the impact of COVID-19 pandemic and to restore the skilling activities, MSDE has taken various initiatives under the skill ecosystem such as Introduction of On-line skill training through eSkill India portal and Bharat Skill portal, Customized Crash Course Programme for COVID Warriors, Special programme for training of reverse migrants under PMKVY in 6 States, COVID Allowance to training centres, Operation and Maintenance of Pressure Swing Absorption (PSA) Oxygen Plants, etc.Annexure State-wise details for fresh skilling and up-skilling under the Customized Crash Course Programme for COVID Warriors (CCCP for CW), as on 28.02.2022 State/UT	Fresh Skilling	Up-skilling	TCs*	Classroom Trained	OJT Enrolled	OJT Completed	OrientedAndhra Pradesh	20	2853	2071	384	315Arunachal Pradesh	20	2227	1553	111	60Assam	54	8797	5428	676	1104Bihar	107	11400	9472	685	329Chandigarh	1	59	55	36	97Chhattisgarh	14	899	737	183	0Delhi	24	3316	2637	942	1539Goa	2	137	86	41	0Gujarat	12	679	448	248	340Haryana	16	1276	1049	236	187Himachal Pradesh	22	2176	1824	575	668Jammu & Kashmir	115	13539	11458	56	0Jharkhand	38	5625	3998	583	172Karnataka	32	11629	10678	130	120Kerala	23	3253	2602	858	789Ladakh	6	590	334	0	0Madhya Pradesh	130	13476	10718	1177	255Maharashtra	28	16897	14271	910	561Manipur	9	1146	692	83	0Meghalaya	2	413	151	0	0Mizoram	8	1645	510	154	0Nagaland	2	354	242	0	0Odisha	14	956	659	162	0Puducherry	4	175	122	35	0Punjab	30	8833	4565	992	812Rajasthan	58	7133	6708	358	240Sikkim	2	440	432	0	0Tamil Nadu	71	5085	4146	753	219Telangana	15	1148	783	286	20Tripura	5	1084	968	128	217Uttar Pradesh	91	15442	13031	1387	1455Uttarakhand	31	3629	3180	521	379West Bengal	39	3439	2778	459	30Total	1,045	149750	118386	13149	9908* as per Skill India Portal (SIP).
