                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

413






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Funds Allocated under CSS




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds transferred to Karnataka under different Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) during the last three years and the current year, head and department-wise;(b)	whether the Union Government has received any representation from the Government of Karnataka for more fund allocations to the State for various development works and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Union Government has taken steps to grant more funds to Karnataka for taking up developmental activities and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FINANCE(SMT NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)(a)	to (c) : A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *413 REGARDING “FUNDS ALLOCATED UNDER CSS” RAISED BY. DR.UMESH G.JADHAV, MP, LOK SABHA FOR ANSWER ON 22.07.2019(a)	to (c): Fund transfers from the Central Government to the State Governments are made under Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSSs), Finance Commission Transfers and Other Transfers such as National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF), Additional Central Assistance (ACA) for Externally Aided Projects (both grants and loans), etc. Apart from these, the devolution of central pool of divisible taxes to States are made as per the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission. The Central Government also implements various developmental programmes and projects through Central Sector Schemes 100% funded by it for the social and infrastructural development in  States/UTs.  The details of such allocation/transfer of funds made in the last three years are as under:(Rs. in Crore)S.No.	Category	2016-17(Actual)	2017-18(Actual)	2018-19(RE)	2019-20(BE)1. The Finance Commission Transfers and the tax devolutions are made as per the recommendations and formula given by the 14th Finance Commission. The transfer of funds under CSSs are made as per the sharing pattern approved by the Cabinet in accordance with the recommendations made by the Report of the Sub-Group of Chief Ministers on Rationalization of CSSs. The Central Sector Schemes are implemented by the Central Government with 100% funding by it. All these contribute towards various developmental activities in the States.******
