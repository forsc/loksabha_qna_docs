                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

590






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Warehousing Corporation Unit




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government plans to establish a central warehousing corporation unit within Chikkodi Parliamentary Constituency, Karnataka; (b) 	if so, whether the Government set a fixed time limit to set up the same; and (c) 	if not, whether the Government will consider setting up the same at the earliest for the scientific storage of Agriculture Commodities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI RAMVILAS PASWAN)(a) to (c): 	Central Warehousing Corporation already has a warehouse of 16648 MT capacity at Nehru Nagar in Belgaum district. As Chikkodi also falls under Belgaum district, Government of India, at this juncture, does not have any plan to establish another unit of Central Warehousing Corporation within Chikkodi Parliamentary Constituency in Karnataka.****
