                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Fast Track Courts for Undertrial Prisoners




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government proposes to form a fast track court to provide justice to the undertrial prisoners in various jails of the country and if so, the details thereof; (b)  whether the Government has received any suggestions/ proposals in this regard; (c )  if so, the details thereof; and (d)   the details of the action being taken by the Government in this regard ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LAW & JUSTICE(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a):  There is no proposal to form a fast track court to provide justice to the undertrial prisoners in various jails of the country at present. (b) to (d):In view of (a) above, question does not arise.************
