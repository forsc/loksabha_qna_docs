                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2432






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Development of Nasik as Model City




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether  Nasik city of Maharashtra has been included under Smart Cities Mission to be developed as a model city and if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	the details of works done so far in the direction of making Nasik a model city?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b) The city of Nashik in Maharashtra has been selected to be developed as a Smart City in Round-2 of the city challenge process in September, 2016. As on 24th February 2020, Nashik Smart City have issued tenders in 48 projects worth Rs. 3,170 crore, work orders in 42 projects worth Rs. 2,725 crore and completed 21 projects worth Rs. 441 crore.                                  *****
