                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2411






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Implementation of SPMRM




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has been implementing Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) for development of 300 clusters in the country, if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of achievements under the SPMRM so far, State-wise;(c) 	the details of clusters developed and funds sanctioned under the scheme, State-wise including the State of Uttar Pradesh;(d) 	whether many proposals from State Governments have been pending under the said scheme;(e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; State-wise including the State of Karnataka; and(f) 	the steps being taken by the Government for effective implementation of the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) Yes Sir, the Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) is being implemented by the Ministry of Rural Development to develop 300 Rurban clusters across the country, with a funding support of up to 30% (Central and State Share) of the estimated investment for each Rurban cluster, as Critical Gap Funding (CGF), while 70% or more of the funds are mobilized by the States through convergence with synergic State and Central programmes.  Under the Mission, out of total 300 clusters, the Integrated Cluster Action Plan (ICAP) of 291 Rurban clusters has been approved.   (b) & (c)  The details of State/UT-wise physical and financial progress made so far, under the SPMRM is annexed at Annexure-I.  Under the Mission, there is no provision for States/UT-wise allocation of funds.  However, the funds are being released based on fulfilment of certain progress achieved as defined in the guidelines of the Mission.  The details of funds released, State/UT-wise, is annexed at Annexure-II. (d) & (e) No, Sir.  No proposal from any of the States is pending with the Ministry of Rural Development under the Mission.(f)    The details of the steps being taken for effective implementation of SPMRM are annexed at Annexure-III. *****
