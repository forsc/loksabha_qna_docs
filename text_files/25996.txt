                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1705






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Labourer Working on Road Projects




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) is aware of the fact that a labourer working as an outsourced worker of the company died at Vaith Mor on NH-9 and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether NHAI takes any responsibility in such matters; (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (d) the action taken by NHAI in this regard; (e) whether NHAI has provided any compensation to the family of the victim; and (f) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  There has been no incident at Vaith Mor on NH-9. However, a labourer has met with an accident with a truck at Km. 82+300 (village Athseni) on 30.05.2021 and died while he was being taken to hospital.(b) to (f) A relief of amount of Rs. 3.50 Lacs has been paid into the bank account of the family of the deceased by the Concessionaire.                                                                                      *****
