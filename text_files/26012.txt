                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1781






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Road Projects in Karnataka




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and present status of on-going road projects being undertaken in the State of Karnataka;(c) the status of other sanctioned road projects which are lying pending in the State; and(d) the time by which these projects are likely to be executed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) Ministry of Road Transport & Highways is primarily responsible for development & maintenance of National Highways. Work-wise details of present status of ongoing National Highway development projects in the State of Karnataka are at Annexure –I. Summary details are as below:Number of ongoing NH development projects	Total length in km	Length completed in km	Likely date of completion of ongoing NH development projects84	2719	1936	04.06.2024(b) Work-wise details of status of other sanctioned NH development projects (other than ongoing) being undertaken in the State of Karnataka are at Annexure-II. Summary details are as below:Number  of sanctioned NH development projects which  are yet to be commenced	Length in km	Sanctioned cost in Rs Crore	Likely date of commencement of the works21	611	10958	31.01.2022 (c) The construction periods of the works are generally 2 to 2.5 years from the date of commencement. Work-wise details of likely completion of execution are at Annexure-II stated above.
