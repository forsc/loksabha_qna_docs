                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Reservation for Ecomonic Weaker Sections (EWS)




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2321TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.08.2021RESERVATION FOR ECOMONIC WEAKER SECTIONS (EWS) 2321. SHRI A.K.P. CHINRAJ: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the data or study on which the Government has given ten percent reservation for economically weaker section; (b) the basis on which the Government came to the conclusion for the income limit eight lakh for EWS Category; and (c) whether the Government considering to include ten percent reservation for economically weaker section in 9th Schedule, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a):  Ten (10) percent reservation to the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) has been given on the basis of the Report dated 22.07.2010 of the Commission for Economically Backward Classes constituted by the Government under the Chairmanship of Major Gen. (Retd.) S.R. Sinho.(b): The annual income limit of Rupees Eight Lakh per annum for EWS category was decided after detailed deliberations by the Government.(c):  No Sir.*****
