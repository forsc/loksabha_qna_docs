                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2179






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Import of Iron Ore




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has to import iron ore despite its production in the country;(b)	if so, the demand of iron being made by the public sector and private sector steel plants set up in the country;(c)	whether we are able to supply ore as per the demand of private sector and PSUs;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the steps taken/being taken by the Government to supply iron ore to these steel plants?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)to(e): The details of import, production and supply of iron ore during the last three years are as follows:-(in million tonnes)Items	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21*Import of Iron Ore 	12.804	1.240	0.760Iron Ore Production	206.49	246.08	204.28Iron Ore Demand	177	175	166[Source: DGFT & JPC/IBM, *provisional]********
