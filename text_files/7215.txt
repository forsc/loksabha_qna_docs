                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Construction of Bridge




Chhedi Paswan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to construct a bridge on the river Son from Panduka (Nohatta) in Bihar to Sri Nagar (Palamu) in Jharkhand;(b) if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c) whether all the formalities for construction of the said bridge have been completed and if so, the details thereof; and (d) whether the Government proposes to expedite the said construction work so as to provide employment to the people of the area and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b) No Sir. This Ministry is primarily responsible for development of National Highways. The bridge on river Son from Panduka (Nohatta) in Bihar to Sri Nagar (Palamu) in Jharkhand is not on National Highway.(c) Does not arise.(d) Does not arise.
