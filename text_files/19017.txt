                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

343






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Indigenously Developed Machine Pistol




Lallu Singh













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether DRDO and the Indian Army have recently developed their first indigenous 9mm Machine pistol;(b) if so, the details thereof, and the time by which it is likely to be made available to the Indian Army; and(c) the extent to which this pistol is effective in comparison to foreign made pistol?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) Yes, Sir.  First prototype 9mm carbine is developed by DRDO and Indian Army.(b)  Few prototypes will be available for evaluation by mid 2021.(c)  Then the evaluation and effectiveness trials and comparison will be conducted.*****
