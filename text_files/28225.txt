                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

14






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Seva Bhoj Yojana




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the objectives of the Seva Bhoj Yojana;(b)	applications received, processed and accepted under the scheme so far, State-wise;(c)	applications received and accepted from the State of Jharkhand, district-wise including Hazaribagh and Ramgarh districts; and(d)	whether the Government is planning any initiatives to create awareness on the scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTRY OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 14 FOR 29TH NOVEMBER, 2021(a)  The objectives of the Seva Bhoj Yojana, a Central Sector Scheme launched w.e.f. 1st August, 2018, is to provide reimbursement of Central Goods and Services Tax (CGST) and Central Government’s share of Integrated Goods & Services Tax (IGST) paid by charitable/religious institutions like Gurudwara, Temples, Dharmik Ashram, Mosques, Dargah, Church, Math, Monasteries etc., who have been distributing free food in the form of ‘prasad’, ‘langar’/bhandara (community kitchen) to at least 5000 persons in a calendar month for at least past 3 years, on purchase of specific raw food items viz. (i) Ghee (ii) Edible Oil (iii) Sugar/Burra/Jaggery (iv) Rice (v) Atta/Maida/Rava/Flour and (vi) Pulses.(b) A total of 1843 applications have been received so far under Seva Bhoj Yojana.  The State wise list of applications received, processed and accepted under the Scheme so far is placed at Annexure I.(c) The list of applications received and accepted from the State of Jharkhand, district-wise including Hazaribagh and Ramgarh Districts is placed at Annexure II.(d) The complete details of Seva Bhoj Yojana alongwith comprehensive Scheme guidelines are available on the official website of the Ministry of Culture for creating awareness amongst Charitable / Religious Institutions involved in distributing free food in the form of Prasad, langar, bhandara etc. This scheme is open throughout the year for the applicant organizations.
