                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Doctor of Pharmacy




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the degree holders of doctor of pharmacy (Pharma D) are eligible for undertaking clinical trials/clinical research/organized clinical trials in the numerous Government funded Medical and Pharmaceutical institutions with state-ofart facilities and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether such degree holders are also eligible for taking up the job opportunities in the field of clinical trials in private/corporate/autonomous institutions;(c) if so whether, any official notification has been issued in this regard and if so, the complete details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	 Clinical trials are carried out by a team. Delegation of duties and responsibilities are as per their qualification, training and experience. All members of the team have to have received training in Good Clinical Practice (GCP). A medically qualified person can take responsibility of medical care as per MCI guidelines. Pharmacist depending on his specialization e.g. clinical pharmacy can take responsibility as per the Pharmacy Council of India guidelines.
