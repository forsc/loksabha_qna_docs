                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Pradhan Mantri Laghu Vyapari-Maan-Dhan Yojana




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of enrolments in Pradhan Mantri Laghu Vyapari-Maan-Dhan Yojana, State-wise; (b)whether the enrolments are below the expectation of targets-estimated by the Government; (c)whether the Government has plans to modify the scheme to make it more attractive; and (d)if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (d):	National Pension Scheme for Traders, Shopkeepers and Self-Employed Persons (Earlier name was Pradhan Mantri Laghu Vyapari-Man-Dhan) has been launched on 12.09.2019 to provide old age protection to the Traders, Shopkeepers and Self Employed Person. The number of enrolments State-wise is given in Annexure.  		As the scheme has been launched on 12th September, 2019 only, the Scheme is being popularised among the target groups. Further, all State/UT Governments were requested for popularizing and bringing more awareness about the Scheme.  The progress of the Scheme is being reviewed regularly in the Ministry at senior level with State/UT Governments, for taking initiatives under Mission Mode.*                                              *****
