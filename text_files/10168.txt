                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3504






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Funds for Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds earmarked and allocated for the Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana during the last two years and the current year, State-wise including West Bengal;(b)	whether deep tube wells as other minor irrigation schemes are proposed to be merged in the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayi Yojana;(c)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including West Bengal;(d)	whether a comprehensive district agriculture scheme is being formulated for various districts by the Government under the Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):	Year-wise allocation and release of funds to the States under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) during the last two years and the current year, State-wise including West Bengal, is given at annexure. RKVY Scheme mandates States to prepare District Agriculture Plans (DAPs) by projecting the requirements for development of Agriculture and allied sectors of the district.  Many   State Governments such as Andhra Pradesh,   Assam, Gujarat,   Madhya Pradesh etc.  have formulated Comprehensive District Agriculture Plans   for various districts. There is no proposal for merging deep tube wells and other minor irrigation schemes   in the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayi Yojana (PMKSY).The Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) in various States of the country from the year 2015-16. The PMKSY has  following four components, namely, Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Programme(AIBP), PMKSY-Har Khet Ko Pani (HKKP) which includes minor irrigation schemes, PMKSY-Per Drop More Crop (PDMC) and PMKSY-Watershed Development Component (WDC).
