                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1750






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Assistance for Deep Sea Fishing




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the steps taken  to ensure safety of fishermen fishing in the deep sea and the assistance provided to them for marketing of their catch;(b) 	whether the Government has any plan to set up new fishery harbours in different States along coastal areas;(c) 		if so, the details thereof along with the new proposals in this regard, State-wise; and(d) 	the fund allocated/released/utilized for this purpose during each of the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING:(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI) (a) to  (d):	To ensure the safety of fishermen at sea, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries provides assistance to the Coastal State Governments/Union Territories for providing safety kits to fishermen. The safety kits provided under the CSS consists of GPS, communication equipment, echo-sounder, lifejackets, lifebuoys, Distress Alert Transmitter (DAT), life-saving appliances (VHF radiotelephones), fish finder, backup battery, search & rescue beacon etc. Besides, necessary training is provided to fishermen for safe navigation and seamanship of fishing vessels through the Central Institute of Fisheries Nautical Engineering and Training Institute (CIFNET) functioning under the Department of Fisheries. The CSS also provides financial assistance for construction of fish markets and allied infrastructure including cold storage facilities, mobile/retail fish outlets, refrigerated and insulated trucks for safe transport of marketing of fishes. The CSS on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries inter-alia provides financial assistance to the maritime State Governments and Union Territories (UTs) for (i) construction of new fishing harbours and fish landing centres and (ii) Up-gradation/expansion/repair/renovation of the existing fishing harbours and fish landing centres. Besides, the CSS also provides suitable convergence with the Sagarmala of Ministry of Shipping for development of fishing harbours and fish landing centres.  Details of proposals approved under the CSS during the past four years (2015-16 to 2018-19) for construction of new fishing harbours/fish landing centres including modernization of the existing ones under CSS are furnished at Annexure-I.  State-wise details of the funds released during last three years for construction of fishing harbours/fish landing centres including modernization of the existing ones are furnished at Annexure-II.
