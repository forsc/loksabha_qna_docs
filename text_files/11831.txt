                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

765






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Houses Proposed under PMAY




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the total number of houses proposed under each of the phases of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban and the target achieved;(b)  the total funds sanctioned during each of the phases and the amount of funds released;(c) the total number of beneficiaries under each of the PMAY components, phase-wise; and(d)  the housing shortage in urban India and the percentage of need met since 2015?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (d):	In pursuance of the Government’s vision of “Housing for All” by 2022, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) (PMAY(U) since 25.06.2015 for providing assistance to States/ Union Territories (UTs) for addressing the housing requirement in urban areas. States/UTs have undertaken demand survey under the scheme for assessing actual demand of housing.  The validated demand reported by States/UTs so far is around 112 Lakh.  Based on the project proposals received so far from the States/UTs under the Scheme, a total 1,03,22,560 houses have been sanctioned which is around 92% of the validated demand of houses reported by the States/UTs under the PMAY(U).Out of the sanctioned houses, 60,50,991 are at various stages of construction and 32,07,573 are completed/delivered. Central Assistance of Rs. 1,63,160.86 crore has been approved; out of this, Rs. 63,676.50 crore have been released to the States/UTs so far.The number of houses sanctioned for beneficiaries under each vertical of the PMAY(U) is as under:Vertical	                        No of houses sanctionedIn-situ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR)	  4.62 lakhAffordable Housing in Partnership (AHP)   28.03 lakhBeneficiary Led Individual House          62.15 lakh Construction or Enhancement (BLC)	Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS)	  8.42 lakh                                     *****
