                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2682






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Manufacturing of Automobile Components




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that manufacturing of automobile components is one of the largest industries of Andhra Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is contemplating to set up an automotive testing and certification centre like National Automotive Test Tracks (NATRAX) in Andhra Pradesh; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b):	As per information received from Automotive Components Manufacturers Association (ACMA), the Automobile and Auto Components Industry has a substantial presence in Andhra Pradesh with more than 100 auto-component manufacturing units. (c) & (d):	No sir, at present there is no proposal for setting up an automotive testing & certification centre in Andhra Pradesh.  There are presently four testing centres which are catering to the demand of testing, homologation and certification for Auto sector in India. ---------
