                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5374






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Construction of Underpasses and Overbridges




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of underpasses/over-bridges constructed on national highways in the country, State/UT-wise including Gujarat during each of the last three years; (b)	the funds spent on the construction of these underpasses/overbridges, State/ UT-wise including Gujarat during the said period; (c) 	whether the Union Government has received any request from the Government of Gujarat for construction of underpasses/overbridges on point 264 black spot on NH-8 and for construction of underpass on Chainage No.81.900 of Kanav Paatia on Dhulia Hazari Marg on NH-53; (d) 	if so, the details thereof along with th action taken by the Government thereon; and(e) 	the time by which these underpasses/overbridges are likely to be constructed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Total nos. of underpasses/over bridges constructed on National Highways in the country during last three years is 1640, out of which 92 have been constructed in Gujarat. Underpasses/over bridges are constructed generally as integral part of road projects except in few cases. Hence funds spent for construction of underpasses/over bridges are not maintained separately. (c) No request has been received from Government of Gujarat for construction of underpass at Ch. 264 (Karan) on NH 8 and Kanav Patia on NH 53.  (d) & (e) Do not arise.*****
