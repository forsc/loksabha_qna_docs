                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2028






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Yoga University




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has opened/proposes to open Yoga Universities to promote Yoga in the Country, State/UT-wise; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the further steps being taken by the Government to promote awareness about Yoga?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):  No.   However, the Government has established “Yoga Certification Board” to promote Yoga by way certification of Yoga Professionals and accreditation of Personnel Certification Bodies & Yoga institutions in 3 categories i.e. Leading Yoga Institution, Yoga Institution and Yoga Training Centre.(c):      The Government has been taking various steps like observing of International Day of Yoga, Prime Minister’s Award for Outstanding Contribution towards Yoga, Media Awards, International Yoga Conference, seminars, other conferences, Yoga Olympiad, poster making, slogan writing, usage of social media, Yoga Therapy, Protocols, Common Yoga Protocols for wellness booklets, DVDs etc. to promote awareness about Yoga.  …………………
