                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3447






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






GST on Sanitary Napkins




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the benefit of 0 per cent GST rate on sanitary napkins has been passed onto consumers and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is true that 0 per cent GST rate has led to an increase in cost of production of sanitary napkin manufacturers and a decrease in their competitiveness against imports; and(c) if so, the details thereof along with the measures planned and put in place by the Government to tackle this issue?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a): Based on the recommendations of the GST Council in its 28th meeting held on 21.07.2018, the rate of GST on Sanitary napkins was reduced from 12% to Nil. In certain cases where this benefit was not passed onto consumers, action had been taken by the National Anti-profiteering Authority under the provisions of Rule 133 of CGST Rules, 2017.(b): The GST rate on sanitary napkins has been reduced so as to reduce the cost of sanitary napkins for providing relief to consumers.(c): Does not arise in view of (b) above.*****
