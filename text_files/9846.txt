                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Revision in Safe Axle Weight




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the revision in safe axle weight done on August 2018 is applicable for petroleum tankers also;(b) If so, the details thereof; and(c) The reasons of petroleum and safety organization for not accepting this amendment/notification?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Petroleum and Explosives Safety Organization (PESO), Government of India (Ministry of Commerce & Industry) has informed that the revision in Safe axle weight done on August 2018 is applicable for Petroleum tankers also.(b) The existing tankers are to be provided with new tanks of higher capacity fitted on the Chassis and revised Automotive Research Association of India report showing curb weight and revised gross vehicle weight is obtained for the vehicle.(c) PESO is accepting this amendment/notification provided the details mentioned at point No. (b) are complied with.*****
