                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3878






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Contribution of Agriculture to the Economy




Thalikkottai Rajuthevar Baalu














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that more than 50 percent of the country’s workforce is employed in agriculture and it contributes about 20 percent to its GDP; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government has any proposal to bring a separate budget for agriculture in view of its importance to the economy; and (d)	       if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  & (b):  	As per the Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) conducted by National Statistical Office (NSO) during July 2019 –June 2020, the percentage of usually working persons engaged in Agriculture Sector is 45.6 percent. As per Provisional Estimates of Annual National Income, released by National Statistical Office (NSO), Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI) on 31st May 2021, the share of Agriculture and Allied Sectors to total Gross Value Added (GVA) of the economy is 20.2% during 2020-21 at current prices.(c) & (d):	No sir. There is no such proposal as on date.*******
