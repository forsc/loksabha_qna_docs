                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1725






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Food Grains under ANBA




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is distributing food grains under the Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan (ANBA) to migrant workers and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has any data of the number of migrant workers in the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise and district-wise; and (c) 	the total quantity of food grains distributed to migrant workers under ANBA?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	During the initial COVID-19 situation in the country in 2020 and pursuant to the announcement of Atma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan (ANBA) in May 2020, the Department had allocated 8 LMT foodgrains to all States/UTs under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat Scheme (ANBS) for free of cost distribution to migrants/ stranded migrant persons including those without ration cards, either under the National Food Security Act (NFSA) or under any other State PDS scheme at the scale of 5 kg per person per month for the period of two months of May and June, 2020. (b): 	A Statement showing State/UT-wise number of persons who moved in for work & employment as per Census - 2011 (Migrant Workers) as maintained by the Ministry of Labour and Employment is at Annexure.(c): 	Against the initial liberal allocations, under ANBS in 2020, the States/UTs collectively estimated a maximum population of about 2.8 Crore migrants/ stranded migrant persons and other such persons in need of free foodgrains. The States/UTs further reported a total distribution of about 2.74 LMT foodgrains, covering an average of about 2.74 Crore persons (98% of the estimated population) for the months of May and June, 2020. ****AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (b) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1725 FOR ANSWER ON 08.12.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.State wise Number of persons who moved in for work & Employment as per Census - 2011 (Migrant Workers)S.
