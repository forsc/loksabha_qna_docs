                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1161






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Apportioning of Tax Income




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the Government is apportioning 42 per cent of the Tax income to the State Government and if so, the details thereof;b) whether any decision has been taken to change the proportion to the State Governments and if so, the details thereof;c) whether any reduction in the proportion of allotment will affect the financial capacity of the State; and d) if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government and 15th Finance Commission in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        a) Based on the accepted recommendations of the Fourteenth Finance Commission (14th FC), the Government of India apportions 42 percent of net proceeds of Union Taxes and Duties to the State Governments. Details of quantum of net proceeds of Union Tax and Duties apportioned to the State Governments are below:<pre> Actuals	        Actuals	        Actuals	        Actuals	        BE2015-16	        2016-17	        2017-18	        2018-19	        2019-20506192.96	608000.31	673005.29	761454.15	809133.02</pre>b) There is no decision to change the proportion to the State Governments.c) Does not arise.d) Does not arise.
