                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Disabled and Elderly Accessible Government Buildings




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government’s 2015 guidelines to make all the Government buildings accessible to disabled and elderly have been implemented;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the number of the Government buildings that have been made accessible after the formulation of the guidelines. State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (d):	The guidelines relating to making Government buildings accessible to disabled and elderly have been adopted for implementation.  Central Public Works Department have made 811 Central Government buildings in different states/UTs under its maintenance, accessible to disabled and elderly.  State/UT wise details are attached as ‘Annexure A’.                                *****
