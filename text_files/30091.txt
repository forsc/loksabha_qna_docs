                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1661






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






BharatNet Phase II




Adala Prabhakara Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the funds disbursed under BharatNet Phase II to Andhra Pradesh;(b) 	the details of gram panchayats that are yet to be made service ready under BharatNet Project, district-wise; and(c) 	the steps undertaken by the Government to expedite implementation of BharatNet Phase II project in the State after Covid-related disruptions? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b)	BharatNet project is implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country, including Andhra Pradesh. In Andhra Pradesh, a total of 1,692 GPs were taken up for implementation under Phase-I through Powergrid Corporation of India Limited (PGCIL), out of which 1,681 GPs have been made Service Ready, as on 01.11.2021. The Phase-II in Andhra Pradesh is implemented in State-led model through the State Government under which 27 GPs have been made Service Ready out of 11,719 planned GPs. Further, 15 GPs were planned on Satellite media. In total, 1708 GPs have been made Service Ready in Andhra Pradesh. The District-wise details of the Gram Panchayats that are yet to be made Service Ready are given in Annexure-I. For BharatNet Phase-II, an amount of Rs. 227.14 crore has been released to the State Implementing Agency of Andhra Pradesh.(c)	For expediting the implementation of the project, close monitoring by Department of Telecommunications (DoT) and Bharat Broadband Network Limited (BBNL) is done, and regular review meetings are held with the concerned State Government/Implementing Agency. Further, State Level Implementation Committee chaired by Chief Secretary of the State has also been constituted to monitor the progress of the project and to resolve the issues in a timely manner.
