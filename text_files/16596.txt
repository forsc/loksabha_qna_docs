                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

416






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Farm Activities during Lockdown Period




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps taken by the Government to facilitate the farmers and farming activities at field level during the lockdown period;(b) 	whether the Government has launched Agri Transport Call Centre, if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the amount of insurance claims disbursed to farmers under Pradhan  Mantri  Fasal Bima Yojna (PMFBY) between March to August, 2020, State-wise; and (d) 	the quantum of pulses disbursed for delivery under Pradhan  Mantri  Garib  Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY) to States from March to July, 2020, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):    During the COVID period, farming and allied activities were exempted from the lockdown. Seed, pesticide, fertilizer etc. dealers / shops and other input related activities were allowed to open / free for making inputs available to the farmers. Inter and intra state movement of farm machinery specially combine harvesters was facilitated. As a result of the various steps taken by the Department, both harvesting activities of the Rabi Crop and sowing activities of Summer Crop took place in a systematic manner. (b):    An Agri Transport Call Centre(ATCC) was set up initially in April 2020 as an emergency response to Covid-19.Subsequently due to relaxations from the lockdown as per Ministry of Home Affairs orders, the need for this facility was no longer felt and it was closed down in July 2020.(c):   Statement on insurance claims disbursed to farmers under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojna (PMFBY) is at annexure - I. (d):   Statement on quantum of pulses disbursed for delivery under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY) is at annexure - II.
