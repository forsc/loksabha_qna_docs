                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2717






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Jan Aushadhi Kendras




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS¸ be pleased to state:(a) the details of medicines and diseases  for which medicines are being provided under the scheme formulated to sell medicines through PradhanMantri Jan AushadiKendras;(b) the details of the progress under the PradhanMantriBhartiya Jan AushadiPariyojana at present and the details of the plans being formulated for its expansion in future;(c) the number and details of centres set up across the country so far under the said scheme, State/UT-wise; and(d) the manner in which the number of such centres are likely to be increased in future?






ANSWER




MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)                               (a) A total of 900 drugs and 154 surgicals and consumables are currently included in the product basket of PradhanMantriBhartiyaJanaushadhiPariyojana (PMBJP). The list of therapeutic group-wise number of medicines included in the product basket of PMBJP is enclosed as Annexure-I. The detailed  list of medicines/surgicals is available at website janaushadhi.gov.in. (b) The statement of the progress achieved so far under the PMBJP is enclosed as Annexure-II. In the current financial year the plan isto  open  1200 new PMBJP Kendras in the country. (c) The list of State/UT-wise number of PMBJP Kendras set up across the country as on 03.07.2019 is attached as Annexure-III. The detailed list of PMBJP Kendras opened across the country so far is available at website janaushadhi.gov.in.(d) The number of PMBJP Kendras will be increased by opening more such Kendras on receipt of applications from interested and eligible entrepreneurs.
