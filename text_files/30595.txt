                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Integrated AYUSH Hospital in Rajasthan




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  whether the Government proposes to open Integrated AYUSH Hospital at all district centres of Rajasthan including Ajmer to fulfill health needs of citizens in view of the comprehensive treatment facility available under AYUSH system;(b) if so, the present status thereof along with the details of the proposal received from the Government of Rajasthan and the Members of Parliament in this regard;(c) the quantum of budget allocated in this head in last two years, State-wise;(d) whether the Government proposes to establish 100 bed naturopathy hospital at Ajmer district headquarter after conducting a survey in this regard; and(e)  if so, the time by which it is likely to be established?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) to (b) Opening of Integrated AYUSH Hospitals at all district centres of Rajasthan including Ajmer to fulfill health needs of citizens comes under the purview of State Government of Rajasthan, as Public Health is a state subject. However, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision for financial assistance for setting up new 10/30/50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals, upgradation of standalone Government AYUSH hospitals and dispensaries, AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres, Co-location of AYUSH facilities at Primary Health Centres (PHCs), Community Health Centres (CHCs) and District Hospitals (DHs) to provide AYUSH healthcare to the public. Grant-in-aid is being provided to the State/UT Governments as per the proposals submitted through the State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) and as per the guidelines of NAM. Further, under NAM the Ministry of Ayush has approved upto 50 bedded integrated Ayush Hospital each at Bhilwara, Ajmer, Churu, Bikaner, Jaipur and Sikar District in Rajasthan. Ministry of Ayush had also received the requests from the 03 Members of Parliament, LokSabharegarding setting up of AYUSH Hospital at Bikaner, Jaipur and Rajsamand Districts and accordingly it was informed to the Hon’ble Members of Parliament that State Government of Rajasthan is required to submit the proposals through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) as per the NAM guidelines. (c) Under NAM, the status of funds approved for setting up of upto50 bedded integrated AYUSH hospitals during the last two years, State/UT-wise is at furnished at Annexure.(d) & (e) Under NAM, the establishment of AYUSH hospitals including Naturopathy in the States/UTs including Ajmer District head quarter comes under the purview of the concerned State/UT Governments.
