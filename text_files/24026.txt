                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5019






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Road Projects under Bharatmala Pariyojana in Chhattisgarh




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of road projects passing through Chhattisgarh which are being implemented under Bharatmala Pariyojana along with the details of their locations, length and cost; (b)	whether any of the said roads is likely to pass through Rajnandgaon in the State and if so, the name of the villages likely to be affected as a result thereof; (c)	the criteria adopted for compensation to the land owners whose land was acquired for the purpose; (d)	whether the amount of compensation for land acquisition varies in various districts and if so, the reasons therefor; (e)	whether any central observer is appointed with the local revenue and agriculture departments for land acquistion and if so, the details therof; and (f)	whether any complaints have been recieved from the affected land owners for non-payment of due compensation for the land acquired and if so, the action taken/being taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	About 11 road projects passing through the State of Chhattisgarh have been identified under Bharatmala Pariyojana. The details of these projects including length and cost are as under:Sr. No.	Name of Project	Length (km)	Tentative Cost (Rs. in Crore)1	4-Laning of Bilaspur-Pathrapali section from Km 0.00 to Km. 53.300 of NH-111 (New NH 130) under HAM Mode	53.3	1282.472	4-Lane with paved shoulder configuration of Pathrapali Katghora of NH-111 from Km 53+300 to Km. 92+600 under HAM Mode	39.30	865.103	Bilaspur-Urga from km. 0.00 to km 70.20 of NH-130A (HAM) under Bharatmala Pariyojna Raipur-Dhanbad	70.2	1745.454	Urga-Pathalgaon under Raipur-Dhanbad economic corridor 	87.775	1855.785	4-laning of Champa–Korba–Katghora section from km. 0.000 to 38.200 of NH-149B under NH (O)	38.200	1281.416	Raipur-Vishakhapatnam 130CD under economic corridor in the state of Chhattisgarh	123	4068.167	4-lane of Durg–Raipur bypass from Km 0.000 to Km. 92.230 of NH – 53 Mumbai-Kolkata corridor	92.23	2773.338	Pathalgaon- Kunkuri- CG/JH Border (Economic Corridor)	100	2060.749	Urga-End of Korba Ring Road End of Katghora Bypass section of NH-149B in the state of Chhattisgarh.	42.095	159310	Sarangarh (Manikpur) to Raigarh (Feeder Route)	56	824.8811	Basna to Sarangarh (Manikpur) feeder route	33	486.09(b)	Durg-Raipur bypass is passing through Rajnandgaon district and the affected villages in Rajnandgaon district are Dewada & Tedesara.(c) to (e)	The compensation is worked out as per NH Act in accordance to RFCTLARR Act, 2013 by Competent Authority for Land Acquisition (CALAs) which is derived in accordance to the Land Acquisition Manual & Guidelines issued by Ministry of Road Transport & Highways. Land Acquisition is carried out by Competent Authority for Land Acquisition (CALAs) appointed by Central Government under Section 3 (a) of NH Act.(f)	Competent Authority for Land Acquisition (CALAs) deals with all the complaints received related to the compensation amount against acquisition of land.
