                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Land of Port Trusts




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the land belonging to Port Trusts are being      encroached in the country;(b)  if so, the details thereof; and(c)  the steps taken by the Government to evict the      encroachments and protect the land of Port Trusts? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)& (b) The details of extent of port land encroachment in Major Ports are as under: <pre>S.No.	Name of Port Trust	Area (in acres)1.	Kolkata Port Trust (KoPT):Haldia Dock Complex (HDC)Kolkata Dock System (KDS)	115.45179.042.	Paradip Port Trust (PPT)	73.503.	Visakhapatnam Port Trust (VPT)	Nil4.	Chennai Port Trust (ChPT)	3.885.	Kamarajar Port Limited (KPL)	Nil6.	V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust (VOCPT)	14.997.	Cochin Port Trust (CoPT)	0.158.	New Mangalore Port Trust (NMPT)	4.569.	Mormugao Port Trust (MPT)	2.6910.	Mumbai Port Trust (MbPT)	53.1711.	Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT)	19.7512.	Deen Dayal Port Trust (DPT)	389.78                                                    </pre>(c)	The following steps are taken to evict the encroachments and protect the land of Port Trusts:(i)	Eviction procedure under Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1971.(ii)	Allotment of land for construction of permanent houses to the slums dwellers under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Scheme in consultation State Government. (iii)	Construction of compound wall to protect the port land. (iv)	Regular Inspection of Port land and deployment of security guard.(v)	Period encroachment removal drives.                                            *****
