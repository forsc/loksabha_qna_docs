                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4309






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Haj Quota for Tamil Nadu




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is proposed to increase the Haj Quota for Tamil Nadu;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	to (c) The quota allocated to Haj Committee of India (HCoI) is distributed amongst the States/UTs on the basis of their respective Muslim population, based on the 2011 census figures. Further, as per the new Haj Policy for HCoI pilgrims for Haj 2018-22, in accordance with the new formula of allocation of surplus seats generated by under-utilized quota of States, after allocation of seats for Mehram, States receiving less than 500 applications, additional seats for Jammu & Kashmir as special measure and ladies without Mehram, the remaining seats are further distributed to States/Union Territories in proportion of number of applications received. For Haj  2019, initially a Haj quota of 3534 pilgrims was allocated for the State of Tamil Nadu. After increase in Haj quota of India by the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for Haj 2019, additional 778 pilgrims have been allocated to the State of Tamil Nadu, thus increasing the total quota to 4312 pilgrims.  *****
