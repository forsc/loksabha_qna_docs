                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Inclusion of Farmers in PM-KISAN Scheme Survey




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps taken by the Government to include entitled farmers and villagers left unintentionally in the survey conducted by the State Government under Pradhan Manatri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana;(b)	if so, the details of Chattisgarh State including Lakhagah village of Mahasamund district;(c) 	whether the Government of Chhattisgarh has listed entitled farmers of Lakhagah village and the number of farmers to whom PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi funds have been disbursed;(d) 	the details of farmers benefitted so far, district-wise/area-wise; and	(e)	the reasons for not taking appropriate action in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):  	Generally, for enrollment under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme, the farmer is required to approach the local patwari / revenue officer / Nodal Officer (PM-Kisan) nominated by the State Government. However, the following additional steps have been taken by the Government to include more and more eligible farmers under the Scheme.(i)	The States were advised to organize awareness campaigns and registration camps to bring the eligible beneficiaries into the ambit of the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme.  (ii)	A special feature, namely, “farmers corner” is available in the PM-KISAN portal, through which the farmers can also do their self-registration. (iii)	Farmers can also edit their names in PM-Kisan database as per their Aadhaar database/ card through the Farmers Corner in the portal. (iv) 	Farmers can also know the status of their payment through the Farmers Corner in the portal. Village-wise details of beneficiaries are also available on the Farmers Corner. (v)	Farmers can approach Village Level Entrepreneur (VLEs) of CSCs to get enrolled themselves under PM-KISAN Scheme. The facilities available at farmers corner can also be availed through VLEs of CSC. (vi)	Mobile app has been launched which also provides the facilities available at farmers corner on PM-KISAN portal. (b) to (d):	 43 beneficiaries of Lakhagah village of Mahasamund district have been given benefits under PM-KISAN Scheme. The district-wise details of farmers benefitted under PM-KISAN Scheme in the State of Chattisgarh is given at Annexure-I.(e):	Does not arise, in view of the above.
