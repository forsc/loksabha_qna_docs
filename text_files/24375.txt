                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

282






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Fertilizer Shortage




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni













                                                    S.R. Parthiban
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of fertilizer consumption in the country during the last three years, year-wise and State-wise;(b)	whether it is true that there is of 10-20% increase in consumption of fertilizer due to higher sowing and bumper rabi harvest;(c)	if so, whether the Government is ready to provide necessary fertilizer to farmers in the country; and(d)	if so, the details of fertilizers being imported to fill the gap? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS &  FERTILIZERS (SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a):	The details of fertilizers consumption/sales for the financial year 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 is attached at Annexure ‘A’.(b) & (c): No Sir. The fertilizer consumption in the Rabi-2020-21 among different fertilizer group has been uneven as compared to the last year. The details may be seen as per Annexure-B. The Department of Fertilizers ensure adequate availability of all fertilizers as per the assessed requirement. (d)	:The details of fertilizer imported in last three FY is as follows:Import of Fertilizers (Urea, DAP, MOP and NPK) from 2018-19 to 2020-21(Figures: In LMT)Year	    Urea	DAP	MOP	NPK2018-19	    74.81	66.02	42.14	5.462019-20	    91.23	48.7	36.7	7.462020-21	    98.28	48.82	42.27	13.9********
