                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4504






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Construction of Over Bridge




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether, in view of the reply to Unstarred Question No. 472 dated 1 December, 2021, the construction work of Over Bridge at LC Gate No. 109 situated between Malwada and Raniwada Railway Stations is going on at a very slow pace since the last two years;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether, due to frequent movement of goods and passenger trains on this route, LC Gate No. 109 has to be closed repeatedly due to which ambulances get stuck here and patients have to wait for hours; and  (d)	if so, the steps taken by the Government to complete the construction of this bridge at the earliest?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b): Initially progress was slow but now the progress is satisfactory.  In the beginning the work was delayed for want of shifting of Utilities.  Now the Girders have been launched, deck slab casted and finishing works are in progress. (c): Opening & closing of level crossing (LC) is being done as per normal gate working rules.  (d): The work has been expedited and major portion have already been completed.   *****
