                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2002






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Schemes Sanctioned for Promotion of North East States




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Schemes sanctioned to the States from the Ministry of DONERfor the last two years, State-wise; (b) 	the funds released to the different States during the last five years, year andState-wise;(c) 	the fund utilized/spent on exhibitions for promoting North East States during the last five years, place of exhibitions and year-wise; and(d) 	whether the Government is aware that the powers of the Secretary to NorthEastern Council has been curtailed and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a)	In the last two years, the North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS) of the Ministry of Development of the North Eastern Region has been sanctioned.(b)	Funds released to the North Eastern states during the last five years, year and state wise are given in Annexure I.(c)	Funds utilized/spent on exhibitions for promoting North East States during the last five years (year-wise) is given in Annexure II. These funds were utilized on exhibitions in various places including Bengaluru, Chandigarh, New Delhi, Lucknow, Imphal, Aizawl, Silchar, Gangtok, Kolkata, Tezpur etc.(d)	There has been no curtailment of powers of Secretary, NEC. He is empowered to sanction project proposals upto Rs.5 crore in consultation with Financial Advisor NEC and from Rs. 5 crore to Rs 15 crore on the recommendation of Standing Finance Committee chaired by Secretary NEC himself.
