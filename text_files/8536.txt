                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2132






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Tentative List for Inclusion as a ‘World Heritage Site’




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the sites in the tentative list for being declared as a ‘World Heritage Site’;(b)	whether any proposal/proposals received from the Government of Kerala for inclusion of a cultural or natural site;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the site-wise details of the funds allocated for maintenance and development in the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	At present, there are 42 sites in the Tentative List. The list is attached herewith as Annexure-‘A’.(b)	No Sir, No such proposal is received from the Government of Kerala in this regard.(c)	&(d) Do not arise.
