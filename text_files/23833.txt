                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4959






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Cases Filed under MSME Samadhaan Portal




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of cases filed using the MSME Samadhaan portal in the country, State-wise;(b)	the details of proposal, if any, made to increase the ease of usage of the MSME Samadhhaan portal;(c)	the number of rural enterprises that have filed complaints on the MSME Samadhaan portal in the country including the State of Rajasthan, district-wise; (d)	the details of policies considered, to increase ease of using the MSME Samdhaan portal; and (e)	the steps taken by the Government with regards to the same? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (c):	As per data available on SAMADHAAN Portal, the total number of cases filed using the MSME Samadhaan portal in the country, State-wise is at annex. This includes the data of  rural enterprises that have filed complaints on the MSME Samadhaan portal in the country.(b), (d) & (e):   i.	Micro and Small Enterprises Facilitation Councils (MSEFCs) have been set up in all States/ UTs to resolve the delayed payment cases by way of conciliation and / or arbitration. ii.	Eighty Nine Micro and Small Enterprise Facilitation Councils have been established in the country.iii.	Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME) continuously follows up with the State Governments & UTs for timely disposal of such cases. Regular Video Conferencing with the States/UTs are conducted and meetings/workshops/trainings have also been organized for officials of the State/UT MSEFCs.
