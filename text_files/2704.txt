                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2409






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Skill Development Centres in all the Schools




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to establish mandatory Skill Development Centres in all the schools throughout the country including North East and Delhi/NCR;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of the plan and the timeframe to implement above proposal, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship presently has no such proposal to establish mandatory Skill Development Centres in schools throughout the country.
