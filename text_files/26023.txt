                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1832






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Development of Expressway Projects




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Expressway projects sanctioned by the Government along with the targets set for their completion;(b)	the number of Expressway projects lying pending along with the reasons therefor and the steps being taken by the Government for their early completion;(c)	the details of foreign companies engaged in construction work of these projects; and(d)	whether the Government proposes to set up any panel to monitor the implementation of these projects and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Details of expressways projects sanctioned, length completed, in implementation stage and balance for award are as under: Expressways	Total length (km)	Length completed (km)	Length in implementation / Awarded (km)	Balance for award (km)Delhi Mumbai EXP	1291	350	764	177Delhi Amritsar Katra EXP	672	 	349	323Ahmedabad - Dholera EXP	109	 	109	 Bengaluru - Chennai EXP	262	 	71	191Kanpur - Lucknow EXP	63	 	 	63Dwarka EXP	28	12	 	16Delhi - Meerut EXP	82	82	 	  (b)           All Expressway projects are targeted for completion by the year 2024-25. (c)            As of now, 3 foreign agencies namely Dhaya Maju Infrastructure (Asia) Sdn. Berhad (DMI), JiangXi Construction Engineering (Group) Corporation Limited & OJSC Euro-Asian Construction Corporation Evrascon are involved in the construction of Delhi – Mumbai Expressway and Delhi – Amritsar – Katra Expressway through Joint Ventures with Indian infrastructure companies Crescent EPC Projects & Technical Services Limited (JV with DMI for Delhi Mumbai Expressway), and MKC Infrastructure Limited (JV with JiangXi for Delhi – Mumbai Expressway and OJSC Evrascon for Delhi Amritsar Katra Expressway). (d)           The implementation & timely completion of projects are monitored by Ministry and National Highways Authority of India at Head Quarter level as well as Regional Offices & Project Implementation Units level through periodic review meetings.  *****
