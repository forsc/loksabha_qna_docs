                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Sakhi Kendra Yojana




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the names of the States where the Union Government has implemented “Sakhi Kendra” Yojana; (b)	the details of centres presently operational, out of the sanctioned/approved projects in the country during the last three years; (c)	whether a “Sakhi Kendra” has been sanctioned in Deoli of Wardha district of Maharashtra; (d)	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which it will become operational; and (e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):  	The Government of India has approved the One Stop Centre (OSC) scheme across the country in all the States/Union Territories.  The names of the States where One Stop Centre-Sakhi is being implemented by the States/Union Territories is at Annexure-I.  (b): 	So far 506 OSCs are reported to be operational as against 728 centres approved.  Details are at Annexure-II.
