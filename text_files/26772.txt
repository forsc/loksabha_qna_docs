                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Funds for Pre- Matric Scholarship




Pramila Bisoyi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2371 TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.08.2021FUNDS FOR PRE- MATRIC SCHOLARSHIP 2371. SHRIMATI PRAMILA BISOYI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the total fund released under Pre-matric Scholarship for SCs students for the financial years 2020-21 and 2021-22, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) the utilization of funds reported by States under Pre-matric Scholarship for SCs students during the last three financial years, including Financial Year 2020-21, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) whether there is any plan to increase the amount of Pre-matric Scholarship for SCs students; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a): The State-wise details of the funds released under Pre-matric Scholarship for SC students during the financial year 2020-21 is attached as Annexure-I. Since the schools are yet to re-open, no release has yet been made for 2021-22.(b): Under the Pre-Matric Scholarship SC Scheme, the releases are made to the States/UTs based on the proposals received from them after adjusting the unspent amount of previous years available with the State/UT, if any. The State/UT wise funds released during the last three years under the Scheme are attached as Annexure-II. (c) & (d): The existing scheme provides for  a monthly maintenance allowance and ad-hoc allowance. It is proposed to merge these allowances and provide a lump sum annual scholarship to the students, with a marginal increase.***** Annexure-IStatement referred to Part (a) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question no. 2371 for 03/08/2021Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC students studying in Classes IX and X  during the FY 2020-21S.No	State/ UT	Funds released (Rs. in Lakh)1.
