                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3629






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






PMGSY in Bihar




Kaushalendra Kumar













                                                    Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the amount of funds released by the Government to the State of Bihar for development of rural roads under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last five years;(b)	whether huge amount of funds is outstanding to the State Government of Bihar under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana and the development of rural roads is lying obstructed due to non-payment of the said amount and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the time by which the State Government has targeted to complete the task of linking all the habitations in the State of Bihar with pucca roads under PMGSY;(d)	whether the Union Government proposes to provide full assistance to the State to achieve this target; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The details of funds released by the Central Government to the State of Bihar for development of rural roads under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last five years are as under:-                                                                                  (Rs in Crore)S.No	Financial Year	Central fund       Central Share of Funds        Total Funds                        released to        released to National          released to                        Rural Works        Executing Agencies            State of Bihar                        Department         implementing PMGSY                        Bihar	           in the State  1	2014-15	       1473.74100	       74.42290	                   1548.163902	2015-16	       2774.36000	        6.64000	                   2781.00003	2016-17	       2907.99223	       50.34300	                   2958.335234	2017-18	       1317.15500	       32.15667	                   1349.311675	2018-19	         92.25000	       47.75399	                    140.00399Total	               8565.49823	      211.31656	                   8776.81479 (b):	No Sir. As per available information, a sum of Rs 4,508 crore is available for use in PMGSY with the State of Bihar under PMGSY as on 5th December, 2019. As reported by the State, no works of Rural Roads under PMGSY is hampered on account of unavailability of funds.(c):	As informed by the State Government the task of providing of all weather connectivity to all eligible and feasible habitations in the State of Bihar is targeted for completion by June, 2020.(d) & (e):	The Ministry of Rural Development is committed towards collaborating with the State, to effectively implement PMGSY in the State. The Ministry has also been constantly reviewing the progress of the programme in the States. Provision of solutions to problems and handholding and assistance to States especially in technical matters is being done by the Ministry and National Rural Infrastructure Development Agency (NRIDA).*****
