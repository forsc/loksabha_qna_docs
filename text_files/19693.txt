                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1020






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Proposals Received from Chhattisgarh




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the State-wise details of the schemes being run by the Government in the country at present;(b)  the State-wise details of the targets of the schemes and the funds allocated therefor;(c)  the schemes for which proposals have been received from Chhattisgarh Government; and(d)   the present status of the proposals received along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) and (b): Ministry of Culture operates numbers of schemes which are applicable to all States, UTs, NGOs and individuals. There are no state specific schemes, hence funds are not allocated to state.(c) and (d): 1. One project was received for funding of payment of Rs.25 lakhs during 2018-19 under Building Grant Scheme to ''Shri Chakradhar Kathak Kalyan Kendra, Gajanandgaon, Chhattisgarh. An Amount of Rs.10 lakhs as 1st installment has already been released.2. A proposal for setting up of Science Centre at Ambikapur, Sarguja District, Chhattisgarh was received from Govt. of Chhattisgarh under the Science Cities Scheme in August 2017. The proposal was not considered for non fulfillment of the eligibility criteria.3. Under the Setting up of NML Model Libraries, a component of National Mission on Libraries Scheme being administered by Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata, an Autonomous body under the administrative control of Ministry of Culture has sanctioned Rs 174 lakhs to the Government of Chhattisgarh for setting up of two District libraries at Dantewada and Raigarh.
