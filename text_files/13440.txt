                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2190






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Air Defence Weapon System




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has made any efforts to improve the air defence weapon system arsenal of India;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is capable to create it’s own air defence weapon system; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the names of the countries that are helping us with it’s procurement?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????) (a)  to (d):  Captial acquisition of defence equipment including air defence weapon system is carried out as per the Defence Procurement Procedure (DPP) based on operational necessity and required capability to meet the security challenges.  Necessary upgrades and refurbishments of existing systems are also undertaken to maintain operational effectiveness of equipment.	Services are actively associated with DRDO to ensure that indigenous R&D efforts are focused on niche/high end complex technologies including air defence weapon systems.  Indigenous development and induction of Radars, missiles systems, Warning and Control systems have been undertaken.*****
