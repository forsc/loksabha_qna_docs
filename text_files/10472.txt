                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3841






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Procurement of Anti Tank Missile




C.N. Annadurai














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether any order has been placed for procurement of Anti Tank Spike Missiles from Israel;(b) if so, the total number of missiles to be procured and their cost in Indian rupee;(c) the advantage of such missiles over similar type of missiles produced by other countries; and(d) the other countries which are using such missiles and whether such missiles are acquired and developed by Pakistan and China?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)  (a)  Yes, Sir.(b) A suitable number of Missiles and allied systems have been procured to meet operational requirement of the Indian Army.(c)  Spike Long Range (LR) Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM) system is a man portable 4th Generation system which can engage targets in both Fire & Forget and Fire, Observe & Update mode, thus enhancing the operational flexibility and crew survivability.  Dual mode seeker of the system allows precise engagement both during day and night.  The system also has Top Attack capability to enhance the lethality of the missile against tank targets.(d)  Israel, Germany, Spain are among the other users of 4th Generation Spike LR ATGM system.  As per available inputs in open source, China has developed a 3rd Generation ATGM System while Pakistan does not have such a capability yet.*****
