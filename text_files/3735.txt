                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

312






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Programme to Attract Tourists




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to prepare any policy/programme to attract foreigners as well as domestic tourists to Nagzira-Koka Wildlife Sanctuary - Navegaon National Park at Bhandara/Gondiya in Maharashtra; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the time by which this policy/programme is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.****** STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*312 ANSWERED ON 15.07.2019 REGARDING PROGRAMME TO ATTRACT TOURISTS.(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination and as part of its on-going activities, releases domestic and international print, electronic and online media campaigns to promote various tourism destinations and products in the different States and Union Territories of the country including the Wildlife Sanctuaries in the country. Promotions are also undertaken through the website and Social Media accounts of the Ministry. In addition, the Indiatourism Offices in India and overseas disseminate information and undertake various promotional activities with the objective of showcasing the varied tourist destinations and products of the country. The Ministry of Tourism under its Schemes of  Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies, provides Central Financial Assistance to State Governments/ Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of  infrastructure and facilities at thematic tourist circuits in the country and for holistic development of identified pilgrimage and heritage destinations. Wildlife circuit is one of the fifteen thematic circuits identified for development under the Swadesh Darshan scheme. All States and Union Territories (UTs) having Wildlife sanctuaries are covered under the Wildlife circuit theme. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.No proposal has been received from the State Government for the Nagzira – Koka Wildlife Sanctuary - Navegaon National Park at Bhandara / Gondiya in Maharashtra. However, details of projects sanctioned in Maharashtra, under the schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies are at Annexure.*******
