                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Incentives under SBM-G




Kuldeep Rai Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any incentives/facilities under SBM-G have been granted/provided for construction of toilet blocks for the houses constructed in the forest encroachment areas in A&N Islands as per Supreme Court order;(b) if so, the details thereof and the number of toilet blocks constructed as on date and expected date for implementation and completion of the mission there; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) No, Sir.(b) Does not arise. (c) In compliance of the order of Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in T.N.Godavarnam Vs the Union of India & others, no toilets have been sanctioned in the forest encroachment; however, such households are covered through nearby Community toilets.
