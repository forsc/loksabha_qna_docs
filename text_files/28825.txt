                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

409






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






PMGSY in Bihar




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of roads constructed in kilometers under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Bihar particularly Jehanabad, Arwal and Gaya Districts during each of the last two years;(b) 	the quantum of funds sanctioned and released for the projects there under along with the status of their completion;(c) 	whether the roads being planned/ approved/under consideration for the upcoming projects in the State; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)    The details of road length constructed in the state of Bihar and Jehanabad, Arwal and Gaya districts during the last two years and the current year (till 25.11.2021) are given at Annexure-I. (b)  The funds for implementation of the scheme are released by the Ministry to the state as whole. During the last two financial years and the current financial year till 25.11.2021, Rs. 435.83 crore has been released as central share of funds to the State of Bihar. The details of Value of Projects (VoP) sanctioned and expenditure incurred including state share under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in the State of Bihar and Jehanabad, Arwal and Gaya districts during the last two years and the current year (as on 25.11.2021) are given at Annexure-I.(c) & (d)   The Government approved PMGSY-III in July, 2019 for consolidation of 1,25,000 km Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia, to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals. A target length of 6,162.50 km has been allocated to Bihar under PMGSY-III, out of which, 1,390.31 km has been sanctioned so far to the State of Bihar under PMGSY-III.  State has also been sanctioned 189.20 km of road length under RCPLWEA on 5.10.2021. Further Ministry of Home Affairs has recommended 29 additional road works of 156 km for the State on 13.10.2021 under RCPLWEA.  *****
