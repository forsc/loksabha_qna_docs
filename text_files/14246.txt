                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3018






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Construction of Stadiums




Dulal Chandra Goswami













                                                     Ramesh Chand Bind
                                                








                                                    S. Venkatesan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had decided to construct stadiums at State, district and block/panchayat level in the country to promote sports in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and progress made till date in this direction; and(c) the number of stadiums that have been built so far at block and panchayat levels of the country, Statewise during the last two years, till date and the number of districts in Tamil Nadu including Madurai district where stadiums have been built at block and panchayat levels and the number of Stadium proposed to be built there?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) to (c) Sports being State subject, the responsibility to construct stadiums at State, district and block/panchayat level in the country rests with the respective State/Union Territory Governments. The Union Government only supplements their efforts. There is no proposal under consideration in this Ministry to build stadia at State, district and block/panchayat levels. This Ministry has not sanctioned/constructed any new stadium during the last two years, including in Tamil Nadu.****
