                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Houses under PMAY-G




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of people who have been provided houses in Maharashtra under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) since the launch of this yojana; and(b) 	the other steps taken to provide houses to every people in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b): 	In order to achieve the target of “Housing for All” in rural areas, the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) with effect from 1st April 2016 to provide assistance to eligible rural households with overall target to construct 2.95 crore pucca houses with basic amenities by March, 2024. Under PMAY-G, the beneficiaries are identified from Socio Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 database and Awaas+ survey 2018 conducted through the States/UTs.              A total target of 15.05 lakh houses have been allocated to Maharashtra State since inception of PMAY-G, out of which only 10.54 lakh houses have been sanctioned to beneficiaries by the state and 7.82 lakh houses stand completed as on 17.03.2022. ******
