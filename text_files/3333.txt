                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3015






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Urban Infrastructure Development in Maharashtra




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds allocated and released by the Government to implement the scheme of urban infrastructure development in Maharashtra during each of the last three years and the current year;(b)	the funds utilized out of the allocated funds during the said  period; and(c)	the details of the works undertaken by using the said funds along with the 	time by which such works are likely to be completed, location/project-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c) The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs facilitates and assists States/Union Territories (UTs) including Maharashtra, in urban infrastructure development through its Missions/Schemes- Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), Smart Cities Mission(SCM), Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban(SBM-U)and Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban)(PMAY-U) to improve the quality of life in cities and towns covered under these Missions/Schemes. Details of funds allocated, released, utilised and project status under these Schemes are given in Annex.                            *****
