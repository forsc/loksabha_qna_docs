                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3028






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Setting up of Food Park




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of food processing parks approved and being established in all over the country during the last five years, State-wise; (b)	the status of all those food processing parks; (c)	the number of proposals received by the Government from the State of Bihar for setting up of food parks and the status of the said proposals along with the number of proposals approved by the Government; and (d)	the number of food parks in Bihar at present and the details of the employment and revenue generated therefrom? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b): Ministry has approved 10 Mega Food Parks (MFPs) and 68 Agro Processing Clusters (APCs) (similar to mini food parks) during the last five years in the country. Details of these MFPs and APCs along with their status is at Annexure-I & Annexure-II respectively.  (c) & (d): Ministry has received 4 and 5 proposals for setting up of MFPs and APCs respectively during the last five years from the State of Bihar. Out of these, one MFP project and one APC project have been approved by the Ministry. Presently one Mega Food park and one Agro Processing cluster projects are under implementation in the state of Bihar. *****
