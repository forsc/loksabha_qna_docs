                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Linking of Mandis with e-NAM




Pashupati Nath Singh













                                                    Annpurna Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has received a proposal related to linking any mandi located in Jharkhand with the National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) project of the Union Government; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of decision taken by the Government on the said proposal; and (d) 	the extent of progress made on the basis of decision taken so far? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (d):	Yes Sir. Based on the proposals received from the States, mandis were approved and integrated with National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) platform.  Proposal for integration of total 19 mandis of Jharkhand had been received from the State. The Proposal had been approved and all 19 mandis as proposed have been integrated with e-NAM platform. The details of Mandis integrated with e-NAM platform in Jharkhand is at Annexure.
