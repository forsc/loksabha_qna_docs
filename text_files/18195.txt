                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1891






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Privatisation of Airports




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to privatise Vijayawada and Tirupati airports in Andhra Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) whether crores of rupees have been allocated and spent for expansion of Vijaywada and Tirupati airports and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the reasons for awarding these airports to private players after investing nearly Rs. 1,000 crores for their expansion and modernization?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): No Sir. Presently, there is no proposal to privatise Vijayawada and Tirupati airports.(c):  Airports Authority of India has undertaken expansion and modernization of Vijayawada airport at a project cost of Rs. 611.80 Crore and Tirupati airport at a project cost of Rs. 177 Crore. (d): Does not arise in view of the (a) & (b) above.
