                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Contribution of MSME in GDP




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of MSME units in the country, State-wise; (b)	the details of contribution of MSMEs to country’s GDP during each of the last five years, State-wise; (c)	whether the contribution of MSMEs to country’s GDP is coming down and now stands at just 30 per cent as per Central Statistics Organisation (CSO); and (d)	if so, the reasons therefor and the manner in which the Government is planning to push it to 40 per cent in the coming four to five years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) to (d): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. 164  FOR ANSWER ON 09.12.2021(a):	As per 73rd Round of NSS Report on Unincorporated Non-Agricultural Enterprises'' (July 2015- June 2016) conducted by Ministry of Statistics & PI (MoSPI), the estimated number of MSMEs in All India was about 6.34 crore. The State/Union Territory–wise numbers of MSMEs are given at Annexure.(b)  & (c):	As per the information received from National Statistical Office (erstwhile Central Statistics Office), Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation, share of MSME Gross Value Added (GVA) in All India GDP at current prices for the year 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 were 29.5%, 29.3%, 29.7%, 30.5% and 30.0% respectively.  	As evident from the above figures, the share of MSME GVA in all India GDP has not come down and is hovering around 30%.(d):	The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) implement various schemes for the growth and development of MSME sector in the country and is continuously striving towards higher share of the GDP.
