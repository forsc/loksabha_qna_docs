                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

175






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Hydro-Electric Projects




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any policy for construction of hydro-electric projects in the country particularly in the hilly regions/States;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) 	the steps taken by the Government to have a comprehensive policy in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c) :	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.******* STATEMENTSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO.175 ANSWERED IN THE LOK SABHA ON 09.12.2021 REGARDING HYDRO-ELECTRIC PROJECTS*********(a) :		Yes, Sir.(b) :		Government of India has issued the Hydro Power Policy, 2008 for development of Hydro Power Sector in the country including hilly States. Subsequently, the Government of India has also notified measures to promote Hydro Power Sector on 8th March, 2019 which include, inter-alia, declaring Large Hydro Projects as renewable, tariff rationalization measures, budgetary support for flood moderation and enabling infrastructure.  The Hydro Purchase Obligation (HPO) trajectory, for the period 2021-22 to 2029-30 has also been notified by the Government on 29.01.2021. (c) :		The policy of the Government for promoting hydro-electric projects is comprehensive.   **************
