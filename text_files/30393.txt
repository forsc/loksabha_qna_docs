                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1822






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Establishment of Rail Factory




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    )  whether the Government proposes to establish rail factory on the land of closed factory of Dehri-Dalmianagar;  (b)    if so, the details thereof and the current status thereof; and (c)  the reasons for delay in the commencement of work despite fulfilling all formalities for setting up of above-mentioned factory and the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND                  ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY                                     (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) Indian Railways had planned to set up a Wagon Periodical Overhauling (POH) Workshop at Dalmia Nagar.(b) and (c):  The work for setting up of the Wagon Periodical Overhauling (POH) Workshop was entrusted to RITES for execution.The work has not commenced since the connectivity of the project site with IR track is not feasible as DFCCIL track is passing in between the two and connectivity with DFCCIL track has not been found viable.******
