                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5207






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Banking Licenses to Corporates




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of corporate institutions which have banking licenses in the country, State-wise;(b)	whether the Government has any mechanism to monitor the banking activities of such institutions; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(a): As per inputs received from Reserve Bank of India (RBI), universal banking license has not been granted to any corporate house. However, payments bank licenses were granted to Reliance Industries Limited and Bharti Airtel Limited after which Jio Payments Bank Limited and Airtel Payments Bank Limited were set up by them, respectively. The registered office of Jio Payments Bank is at Mumbai, Maharashtra and that of Airtel Payments Bank Limited is at New Delhi.(b) and (c): RBI has apprised that compliance to its guidelines issued to banks is examined during the supervisory assessment of banks and any non-compliance observed is taken up with banks for rectification apart from initiating supervisory/enforcement action against the banks, as it deems fit.***
