                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

859






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Setting up of Additional AYUSH Hospitals




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has envisaged proposals to sanction additional AYUSH hospitals under the National Ayush Mission (NAM) apart from the ones previously sanctioned at Kakinada and Vishakhapatnam in the next financial year;(b) if so, the details thereof along with budget allocations made therefor; and(c) the details of the break up of the Integrated Hospitals sanctioned under NAM during the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b):	Ministry of AYUSH is implementing Centrally Sponsored scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) through States/UTs and providing financial assistance to them as per the proposals received through State Annual Action Plans (SAAP). Under NAM, there is a provision of financial assistance to States/UTs for setting up of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals. Central Government has approved 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital each at Kakinada and Vishakhapatnam in the State of Andhra Pradesh as per proposal received from State Government through their State Annual Action Plan (SAAP). However, no other proposal has been received from State Govt. of Andhra Pradesh through SAAP.(c):	The Status of approved 50 bedded Integrated AYUSH Hospitals State/UT wise during the last two years is furnished at Annexure.
