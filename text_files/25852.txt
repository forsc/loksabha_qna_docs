                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1648






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Design Competition for Iconic Structure




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                








                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched a ‘restructured’ rounds of 	its design competition for the ‘iconic structure’ to commemorate 	75 years of Independence; and(b)	If so, the details thereof along with projects finalized, State-wise 	and the response received/ action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	 : Yes, Sir.(b)	: The Government has launched time bound competition to crowd-source implementable ideas for the architectural and structural design of the iconic structure on 27th July, 2021. The last date to submit design is 19th October, 2021.                                           *****
