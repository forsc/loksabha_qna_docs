                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4061






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Crime in Trains




Darshana Vikram Jardosh













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                








                                                    Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava
                                                








                                                    Rakesh Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether incidents of crimes like robberies, rape, drugging of passengers, etc. during train journey/railway premises have alarmingly increased in the recent years and if so, the details thereof during the last two years and Zone/State-wise;(b)	whether several railway employees, workers like vendors/coolies and security personnel have been allegedly found to be involved in such crimes, if so, the details thereof during the last two years, Zone/State-wise;(c)	whether there is lack of coordination between RPF and GRP in dealing with railway crimes, if so, the steps being taken by the Government for the safety and security of railway passengers in the country; (d)	whether the Government is likely to equip railway cyber system with modern technology and equipments to check such crime and if so, the steps taken in this direction; and(e)	whether the Railway Protection Force (RPF) is empowered to register such cases of crime and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY     (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
