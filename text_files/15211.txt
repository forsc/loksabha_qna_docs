                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3808






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






NRLM in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the allocations under the National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) (Aajeevika) for the districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong, and North Dinajpur in West Bengal; and(b) 	the number of Self Help Groups who have availed Aajeevika loans?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana - National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme that aims at eliminating rural poverty through promotion of multiple livelihoods for each rural poor household. The allocations under DAY-NRLM for the districts of Darjeeling (including Kalimpong and Siliguri Mahakuma Parishad) and North Dinajpur in West Bengal during last five years are given below:-Years	                 Year wise Allocation (in Rs.
