                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2561






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Growth Rate of Rural Wages




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the growth rate of rural wages in the country since 2020, year-wise;(b) 	whether the growth rate of rural wages has declined since 2020;(c) 	if so, whether the Government has investigated the reasons for the decline; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (d): As per information shared by Ministry of Labour and Employment, Labour Bureau has been compiling and maintaining average daily wage rate data in respect of selected agricultural and non-agricultural occupations on the basis of the data collected by the Field Operation Division (FOD) of National Statistical Office (NSO), Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation  from a fixed set of 600 sample villages spread over 20 States. All India annual average daily wage rates in rural area for the period 2020 to 2021 along with their growth rates are given below. The annual growth rate calculated for the period 2020 to 2021 indicates a positive growth. Annual average daily wage rates  in respect of non-agricultural labourers in rural area and their growth rates Year	           Wage Rate        	Growth Rate2019	             291.87	             -2020	             310.19	           6.282021*	             320.48	           3.32*Annual average is calculated for ten months only (Jan to October, 2021). As per Section 6(1) of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), the Central Government may, by notification, specify the wage rate for unskilled work for its beneficiaries. Accordingly, the Ministry of Rural Development notifies wage rate under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA every financial year for unskilled workers. To compensate the Mahatma Gandhi NREGA workers against inflation, the Ministry of Rural Development revises the wage rates every financial year based on Consumer Price Index for Agricultural Labourer (CPI-AL). The wage rate is made applicable from 1st April of each financial year.
