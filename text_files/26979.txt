                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2635






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Satellite Based Internet of Things Network Connectivity




Vishnu Dutt Sharma













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of COMMUNICATIONS be pleased to state: whether any specific efforts are being made by the Government to provide satellite based Internet of Things(IoT) network connectivity through BSNL to enhance the Narrow Band-Internet of Things(NB-IoT) network in the country and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)Sir, As per current regulatory framework, the licensed Telecom Service Providers can render various telecommunication services including satellite-based Internet of Things (IoT) service based on their techno-commercial considerations and scope of license obtained by them. Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) does offer Narrow Band-Internet of Things (NB-IoT) network connectivity in the country. As on 22nd July, 2021, BSNL has provided 385 NB-IoT connections.  ********
