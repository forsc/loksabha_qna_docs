                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4297






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






National Agroforestry Policy




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the efforts made by the Government to implement its National Agro-forestry Policy and the results achieved;(b) the amount given as subsidy to encourage agro-forestry in various States during last three years, State-wise;(c) whether any special drive for agro-forestry has been undertaken where the forest cover is less than seven per cent of the geographical area of a district and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of the efforts for promoting plantation of native species and developing their nurseries for saplings?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???????????????????????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is making efforts to implement National Agro-forestry Policy, 2014. As a part of the policy, this ministry is implementing Sub-Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF) Scheme in 23 States/UTs with an aim to encourage tree plantation on farm land along with crops/ cropping system to help the farmers to get additional income and make their farming systems more climate resilient and adaptive.The details of overall physical achievements under the scheme during last three years are as given below:Components	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	TotalNo. of Nurseries Established	297	126	94	517Area Coverage (In Ha)	26344	15759	24925	67028Total Plantation (Lakh Trees)	103.66	66	105.10	274.76(b)The details of overall State wise funds allocated and released during last three years to encourage agroforestry is given at Annexure-I. (c)&(d). No special drive for agroforestry has been undertaken by this Ministry where forest cover is less than seven percent of geographical area of a district. However, under the scheme, assistance to farmers is given through State Governments for nursery development, boundary plantation and block plantation of prominent tree species to promote, inter-alia, tree borne oilseeds, medicinal & aromatic plants, silk & lac rearing host plants including promotion of plantation and nursery development for native species, in addition to timber species so that farmers get early returns.
