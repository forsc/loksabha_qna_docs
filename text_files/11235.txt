                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

210






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Girls Hostels




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Kasturba Gandhi girls hostels and other girls hostels running in thecountry including Gujarat under Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan along with the number of girlsliving there;(b) the number of girls hostels running under Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan(RMSA) along with the number of girls living there;(c) whether shortage of hostel facilities at higher secondary level in comparison to thenumber of girls living in hostels for secondary level students is creating hurdles inthe education of girls;(d) if so, whether the Government proposes to increase the hostel facilities and if so,the details thereof;(e) whether the Government is also considering to upgrade all the KGBV in the countryand if so, the details thereof; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor and the reaction of the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a): A total of 3703 Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (KGBVs) had been sanctioned inthe country under Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA) till the year 2017-18. Out of it, 3603KGBVs were operational with the enrolment of 3.67 lakh girls. Out of 3703 KGBVs, 89KGBVs had been sanctioned in Gujarat State and all were operational with the enrolment of6385 girls.(b): A total of 2549 Girls Hostels (GHs) had been sanctioned in the country underRashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA) till the year 2017-18. Out of it, 1314 Girls’Hostels were operational with the enrolment of 1.20 lakh girls.(c) & (d): No Sir.(e): Under the Integrated Scheme of School Education-Samagra Shiksha, effective fromthe year 2018-19, provision has been made to upgrade the existing KGBVs from UpperPrimary level upto Senior Secondary level in phased manner. A total of 1232 KGBVs havebeen upgraded in the year 2018-19 (729 KGBVs upto Class X and 503 KGBVs upto ClassXII) and 643 KGBVs have been upgraded in the year 2019-20 upto 30.09.2019.(f): Does not arise.*****
