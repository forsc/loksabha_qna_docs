                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2124






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






One Stop Centre for Girls




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)   	whether the Government has proposed to establish One Stop Centres for girls;(b)   	if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives of the proposal; and (c)   	if not, the reasons therefor?  






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c) :  The Government implements One Stop Centre (OSC) Scheme to provide for a range of services such as police facilitation, legal counselling, psycho-social counselling, medical aid and temporary shelter in an integrated manner, under one roof, for violence affected women and women in distress.  Till date, 733 One Stop Centres (OSCs) in 730 districts of the country have been approved, out of which 684 are operational.   *****
