                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Common Service Centres (CSCs)




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the MINISTER OF ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a) the details  of Common Service Centres (CSCs) set by the Government , State-wise;(b) the detail of various services being provided by CSCs at the level of Gram Panchayats;(c) whether the Government is planning to increase the services under CSCs in near future; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):  Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has implemented the network of Common Services Centres (CSC) under the Digital India Programme. The CSC scheme envisages setting up of at-least one CSC in each of 2.50 lakh Gram Panchayats (GPs) across the country, for delivery of various Government-to-Citizen (G2C) and other citizen-centric services to citizens. It is a self-sustainable entrepreneurship model which is run by Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs). As on December 2021, total 4,46,789 CSCs are operational across all states & Union Territories of the country, out of which, total 3,48,391 CSCs are operational at Gram Panchayat (GP) level. The list of number of CSCs in the country (State/UT-wise) is enclosed at Annexure-I.(b):  400 + services are being offered to the public in these CSCs. Vvarious kind of services being offered to the public in these CSCs are placed at Annexure-II.(c) and(d): Yes, Sir.  CSC 2.0 Project has the mandate to establish 2.5 lakh CSCs to cover Gram Panchayas (GPs) across the country for providing various citizen centric services. CSC e-Governance Service India Limited (CSC SPV) is the implementing agency for CSC2.0 Project and has a mandate to add bouquet of services including Government to Citizen (G2C) and Business to Citizen (B2C) services to be delivered through CSCs. CSC is a self-sustainable entrepreneurship model which is run by Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs). In year 2014, the available services with CSCs were less than 100 and as of December 2021, 400+ services are available with VLEs for delivery to citizen.
