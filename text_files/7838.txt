                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1427






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Pending Railway Works in Maharashtra




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Bharti Pravin Pawar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of railway works for which proposals have been received by the Government from Maharashtra during the last three years and the details thereof, proposal-wise;(b)	the reaction of the Government on the said proposals and the action taken so far on such proposals; (c)	the number of proposals from Maharashtra pending so far with the railways at present and the details of such proposals, location and work-wise; and (d)	the reasons for pendency of the said proposals?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
