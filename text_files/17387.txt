                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Proposals Received from States for Development and Promotion of Tourism




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received proposals from various States including Andhra Pradesh for the development and promotion of tourism and if so, the details thereof along with the allocation of funds; and (b) 	the steps taken by the Government to promote tourism in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b):	The Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission  on  Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. Submission of  project  proposals  by the State Government/UT  Administrations and  its  sanctioning   is  a  continuous  process and  the  projects are  sanctioned  subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.The  details of the  projects  sanctioned under  Swadesh  Darshan and  PRASHAD scheme to  different States  including  Andhra Pradesh  is annexed. The Ministry of Tourism undertakes various activities for the holistic promotion of India in Domestic and International Markets. As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country. The  Ministry  of Tourism during the financial year  2019-20  has  extended financial  assistance  of  Rs. 9.92  Crore  to  various  States/UTs  for  organizing  fairs & festivals  and  tourism related  events.In  addition,  the  Ministry  of  Tourism has  also  undertaken following  initiatives/programmes  for promoting and  encouraging  tourism  in the country:(i)	Launching of  ‘Adopt  a  Heritage:  Apni  Dharohar,  Apni  Pehchaan’  project   for  developing tourism amenities at  heritage/natural /tourist  sites spread  across  India  for making  them  tourists  friendly in  a  planned  and phases  manner.(ii)	Identification of  19  Iconic  Sites  in the country for  development  by  seeking convergence  with  other  central  Ministries/Departments /State  Governments/ Urban & Local  bodies.(iii)	Promoting  India  as  a  holistic  tourism  destination  including  its  various  tourism  sites  and  products  through  Incredible  India  campaign 2.0.(iv)	Revamp of  Incredible  India website  having  information  on  tourism sites/destinations  in  different  States / Union Territory  Administrations.(v)	Launched  Buddhist  website -  indiathelandofbuddha.in  to  promote  and  showcase  the  rich  Buddhist  Heritage in  India.(vi)	Co-ordination and encouragement of tourism stakeholders on issues related to development and  promotion  of  tourism  in the  country.  *****
