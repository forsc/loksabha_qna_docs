                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1432






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Number of Registered Companies




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of companies registered by the Registrar of Companies as on date; (b) the details of the companies that got closed, State/UT -wise; (c) whether the Government has taken any steps to find out the State/UT-wise details of the companies that are non-operational or not doing any business but shown as operational in the Government records;  (d) whether the Government has done any studies to find out the reasons for these companies go out of business;(e) if so, the details thereof ; and(f) the details of the steps taken by the Government to cancel the registration of such fake companies?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS				        (SMT. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)???????????????????????????? 					   (??????????????????????)(a): As per the Ministry of Corporate Affairs data, there are 18, 94,146 number of companies registered by Registrar of Companies as on 24.06.2019. (b): As per the Ministry of Corporate Affairs data, there are 6,83,317 number of companies closed. The details of the companies that got closed, State/UT wise is as per Annexure “A”.(c): The Government has undertaken Special Drive for identification and Strike off of companies u/s 248(1) of the Companies Act, 2013 read with the Companies (Removal of Names of Companies from the Register of Companies) Rules, 2016 (amended Rules 2019), which had not filed their Financial Statements and/or Annual Returns for a continuous period of two immediate preceding financial years.(d) & (e): Nil.(f): The term fake company is not defined under the Companies Act.*******Annexure “A”State/UT wise details of closed companies:State	No. of Closed CompaniesAndaman & Nicobar	105Andhra Pradesh	9889Arunachal Pradesh	277Assam	3348Bihar	6859Chandigarh	7227Chattisgarh	2772Dadra & Nagar Haveli	129Daman and Diu	98Delhi	125937Goa	3607Gujarat	35485Haryana	11549Himachal Pradesh	2561Jammu & Kashmir	2423Jharkhand	3159Karnataka	39962Kerala	18136Lakshadweep	5Madhya Pradesh	15198Maharashtra	142425Manipur	180Meghalaya	399Mizoram	62Nagaland	303Orissa	9568Pondicherry	1813Punjab	14307Rajasthan	21041Sikkim	00Tamil Nadu	64013Telangana	42058Tripura	124Uttar Pradesh	28690Uttarakhand	2117West Bengal	67491Total	683317
