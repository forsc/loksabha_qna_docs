                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4852






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Lack of Basic facilities in Villages




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the fact that due to paucity of funds in village panchayats, there is no sign of any development including facilities for drinking water, road, toilet and street light in many villages across the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government is taking necessary steps to allot more funds to all village panchayats particularly in Tamil Nadu for overall developments in the villages; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (d)  Under the Fourteenth Finance Commission (FFC) award, grants to the tune of Rs. 2,00,292.20 crore for the period of 2015-2020 are allocated to Gram Panchayats (GPs) constituted under Part IX of the Constitution in 26 States including Tamil Nadu. The grant is provided to GPs for delivering basic services including water supply, maintenance of roads, street lighting, etc. The allocation of FFC grants to GPs is more than three times the corresponding allocation under the Thirteenth Finance Commission award to Rural Local Bodies. The State-wise status of allocation and release of FFC grants including Tamil Nadu is given at Annexure.******
