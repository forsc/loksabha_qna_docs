                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1504






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Electrification of Southern Railways




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways is considering to complete the electrification of 124 Route Km Palakkad-Pollachi-Palani rail corridors and 44 Route Km Kollam-Punalur along with 673 Route Km. targeted by Southern Railway during 2021-22;(b) whether the Railways is targeting to switch over to Green Railways by December, 2023 by electrifying all broad gauge routes to achieve 100% electrification of broad gauge routes;(c) whether 100% electrification by 2023 will result in annual savings of Rs. 14,500 crore for Railways;(d) whether around Rs. 21,000 crore are expected to be incurred for completion of the work; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*******
