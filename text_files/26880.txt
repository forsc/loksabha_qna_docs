                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2589






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






South Eastern Coal Fields




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of ITI diploma holder employees during the last five years and the number of such employees working presently in the South Eastern coal fields;(b) the year-wise details of the retired employees during the last five years;(c) the details of the new appointments made against the retired employees during the last five years; and(d) whether the operation of South Eastern Coal Fields is being done with the help of unskilled contractual employees instead of new appointments as a result of which a lot of irregularities are taking places and if so, the details thereof?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COAL, MINES AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)  (a):    The number of ITI diploma holder employees during the last five years and the number of such employees working presently in South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL) are as follows -Particular	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Non-Executive	3706	3697	3689	3666	3647 (b): The year-wise details of the retired employees during the last five years in SECL is as under -Particular	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Executive	116	115	137	171	143Non-Executive	3215	3207	3362	3308	3539Total	3331	3322	3499	3479	3682 (c):  In SECL, no new appointments are made against the retired employees, however, during the last five years, employments have been given under Coal India Special Female Voluntary Retirement Scheme (SFVRS), fresh recruitment, employment to Project Affected People (PAPs) against land acquisition and dependent employment as per provisions of National Coal Wage Agreement (NCWA), the details of which are given below -  Particular	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Against SFVRS	15	-	-	-	-Fresh Appointment	58	210	18	02	-Against Land Acquisition	360	296	343	374	253Dependent Employment	618	502	405	347	205 (d): Unskilled worker are normally not engaged in mining operation. Outsourcing through contract is resorted to keeping in view the administrative exigency of work, efficiency and economy of operations and unavoidable circumstances.  *****
