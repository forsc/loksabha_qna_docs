                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

118






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Atma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the package approved under Atma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan for ensuring growth in the sector of Dairying through Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF) towards breed improvement to increase Milk Production and the processing sector and also to increase export of dairy products and to cope up with the Standards of the countries like New-Zealand;(b) whether the Government proposes to consider he proposals for development of the dairy submitted to the Ministry under NPDD under AHIDF; and(c) if so, the list of proposals received under NPDD eligible to be considered under AHIDF especially from Co-operative Dairies of Maharashtra State?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) Prime Minister’s Atma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan stimulus package mentioned about setting up of Rs. 15000 crore Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF). The Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development (AHIDF) has been approved for incentivizing investments by individual entrepreneurs, private companies, MSME, Farmers Producers Organizations (FPOs) and Section 8 companies to establish (i) the dairy processing and value addition infrastructure, (ii) meat processing and value addition infrastructure and (iii) Animal Feed Plant.The Schedule bank will provide loan to the eligible beneficiaries up to 90% with the commitment of margin money of minimum 10%. The Central Government will provide interest subvention @ 3% to the beneficiaries. The Central Government will also provide credit guarantee up to 25 % of the borrowings to those projects falling under the definition of MSME.  In this regard, a Credit Guarantee Fund of Rs. 750 Crore has been created managed by National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD).(b) No. Sir( c) Does not arise.*****
