                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

252






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Central Database on Cooperatives




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya













                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government intends to develop a central database on Co-operatives, if so, the details thereof;(b)  the aims and objective behind the creation of a Central Cooperative Database;(c)  whether the Union Government has consulted or is going to consult States and Co-operative organisations in this regard; and(d)  if so, the response thereto and if not, the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMinister of COOPERATION???????? ?????? (SHRI AMIT SHAH) (a) :  Yes sir.(b)	: The information on number of Co-operative Societies, their business activities, their location, their financial health, their contribution to Indian economy etc. are important ingredients for taking any policy initiative or creating any welfare scheme in cooperative sector. National database will provide one such step for creating conducive environment for fulfilling mandated allocation of Business for newly created Ministry of Cooperation.(c)	& (d): Yes sir, consultation with states and other stakeholders is an integral part of   any policy making.*****
