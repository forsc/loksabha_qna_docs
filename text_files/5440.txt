                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4871






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Development of Fishing Harbours in Karnataka




B. Y. Raghavendra














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the State Government of Karnataka has submitted any proposal for the development of fishing harbours in the Bydoor Taluk of Udupi district in the last five years; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the proposals approved by the Union Government for the development of fishing harbours in the Bydoor Taluk of Udupi district during the said period; and (d) 	the central assistance sanctioned therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (d) :   The Central Institute of Coastal Engineering for Fishery (CICEF), Bangalore, under the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying at the request of Government of Karnataka, rendered necessary assistance to the State Government in preparation of Techno Economic Feasibility Report(TEFR) for development of fishery harbour at Uppunda, Madikel in Byndoor Taluk of Udupi District. The Government of Karnataka has not submitted proposal together with confirmation on availability of land and environmental clearance for development of fishing harbours in Byndoor Taluk of Udupi District.   *****
