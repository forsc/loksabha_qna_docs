                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1823






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Development of NHs by NHIDCL




Ramesh Chander Kaushik














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether some parts of highways in difficult areas in the country especially in Haryana have been indentified by the National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL) for development;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the steps taken by the Government to speed up the highway construction projects in the said areas in the country; and (d)	the details of funds allocated for the purpose during the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	whether some parts of highways in difficult areas in the country especially in Haryana have been indentified by the National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL) for development;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the steps taken by the Government to speed up the highway construction projects in the said areas in the country; and (d)	the details of funds allocated for the purpose during the last three years, State-wise?
