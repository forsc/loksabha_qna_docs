                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Allocation of Additional Funds




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the funds allocated to Department of Sports for Financial Year 2019-20 are not sufficient to meet the requirements of the Department of Sports; (b) if so, the funds allocated and spent during the last three years; (c) the schemes/programmes introduced under Department of Sports and amount spent under each of these schemes/programmes during the last three years, year-wise; and (d) whether the Government has taken steps to release additional funds for the better implementation of the schemes of the Department of Sports and if so, the details and outcome thereof? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI KIREN RIJIJU]  (a):   Adequate funds are available with the Department of Sports for FY 2019-20 after the allocation of additional Rs.400 crore in the Revised Estimates.  (b):    Does not arise.   (c):   The list of schemes implemented by the Department of Sports and expenditure incurred under each of them during the last three years is given at Annexure.  (d):  Additional funds are allocated by the Government to the Department of Sports from time to time through Revised Estimates, supplementary demand for grants etc. to meet the additional requirements raised by the Department of Sports for incurring unavoidable expenditure on different schemes. The outcomes are yet to be achieved. ****
