                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1435






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Grant of Indian Citizenship




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government grants citizenship to people from different country every year;(b) if so, the number of persons who have applied for Indian citizenship during the last five years along with the number of Indian citizenship granted during thesaid period, year-wise, country-wise and religion-wise; and(c) the details of the current conditions and formalities for non-Indians to get Indian citizenship?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)  Yes Sir. (b) Records of persons granted Indian citizenship under section 5 (by registration) or section 6 (by naturalisation) of the Citizenship Act, 1955 are maintained as per the provisions of the Citizenship Act, 1955 and rules made thereunder. These records are not maintained religion-wise.  The year-wise and country-wise details of citizenship  granted during last five  years are annexed.c) The citizenship of India can be acquired by birth or descent or registration or naturalisation or incorporation of territory. Acquisition of Indian Citizenship is governed by the provisions under the Citizenship Act, 1955 and rules made thereunder.*****
