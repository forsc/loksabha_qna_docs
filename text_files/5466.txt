                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5007






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Old Age Homes




Rahul Kaswan













                                                    Rajan Baburao  Vichare
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of old age homes set up/opened in Rajasthan and Maharashtra during the last four years, location-wise; (b) the details of financial assistance provided by the Government for such old age homes in Rajasthan and Maharashtra during the said period; (c) whether the Government has issued any direction to set up at least one old age home in each district of the country; and (d) if so, the present status thereof particularly in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): Under the Scheme of Integrated Programme for Senior Citizens (IPSr.C), this Ministry provides financial assistance in the form of grant in aid for old age homes to the Implementing Agencies such as State/UTs, Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs)/ Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs)/ local bodies. Details of financial assistance provided for Old Age Home in Rajasthan and Maharashtra under the Scheme during the last four years is at Annexure.(c): Section 19 of the Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens (MWPSC) Act, 2007 states that State Governments may establish and maintain old age homes, at least one in each district, to accommodate 150 indigent senior citizens.(d): Data in this regard is not maintained centrally. However, as per available information there are 50 old age homes in Rajasthan including 44 old age homes being assisted by the State Government.****
