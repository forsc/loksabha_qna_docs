                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4614






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






New Steel Projects for Kerala




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has recently given approval for implementation of new projects for steel factory in Attingal, Kerala;(b)	if so, the details of projects/programmes planned and the fund sanctioned for the same; and(c)	whether the State Government had submitted any proposal in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)to(c): Steel is a deregulated sector and the role of Government is limited to that of a facilitator.  The decision to set up a new steel plant/factory is taken by the individual companies based on commercial considerations, market dynamics and techno-economic viability of the projects.	Ministry of Steel does not provide any budgetary support for any projects/ programmes to the states to set up steel plants/factories.********
