                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Fish Catch




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the steps taken by the Government with regard to reducing wastage of Fish-Catch, value addition of Fish-Catch, Skill Upgradation and Capacity building of Fishermen during the last six years under Blue Revolution Scheme and other schemes of Fisheries Department; and(b) 	the amount of funds allotted to various States and how much fund was utilised by various States, details thereof, Scheme-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING:(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a):	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying during the past five years (2015-16 to 2019-20) implemented a Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on “Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries” in all States/ UTs.Presently, The Department of Fisheries is also implementing a flagship scheme namely “PradhanMantriMatsyaSampadaYojana (PMMSY)”- a scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India as a part of the AtmaNirbhar Bharat relief package with a highest ever estimate investment of ? 20050 crores in fisheries sector over a period of 5 years from FY 2020-21 to FY 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories.Further, special emphasis was made ondevelopment of Post-harvest infrastructure, processing, storage, value addition, packaging, transportation and marketing of fish and fisheries products including cold chain for reduction of post-harvest lossesunder Blue Revolution Scheme and also being continued under PMMSY.Moreover, broad activities under development of Post-harvest infrastructure area viz  processing plants, cold storages, ice plants, freezing and packing plants, fish and fisheries products transportation vehicles including refrigerated and insulated vehicles, ice flaking and ice crushing units, ice/fish holding boxes, wholesale/retail fish markets and outlets, mobile fish and live fish markets etc. were also supported including skill upgradation and Capacity building of Fishermen under Blue Revolution Scheme and also being supported under PMMSY scheme.(b):	The details of funds sanctioned and quantum of funds utilised by various States/UTs are during the last six years is annexed.*****
