                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Street Lighting National Programme




Pritam Gopinath Munde














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is implementing Street Lighting National Programme (SLNP) in some States;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c) 	the total street lights that have already been replaced in the country with LED bulbs and the estimated quantity of energy saved as a result thereof;(d) 	whether there is any proposal to replace street lights in all the States under the SLNP;(e) 	if so, the details thereof; and(f) 	the time by which these are likely to be replaced?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) :	Energy Efficiency Services Limited (EESL), a joint venture company of Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) under the Ministry of Power, Government of India has initiated and implementing Street Lighting National Programme (SLNP) in 23 States and 6 Union Territories. The details of the implementation of SLNP State/UT-wise are given at Annexure. (c) :  		EESL has replaced 1,15,35,189 conventional street lights  as on 16.03.2021 with LED street lights. This has led to an estimated energy savings of 7.75 billion units per year.  (d) to (f) :  SLNP is voluntary in nature and based on a sustainable business model, where the cost of efficient lighting is repaid by Urban Local Bodies (ULBs)/ Municipalities from savings in electricity bill over a period of time. EESL has given proposals to all States/UTs and as the State/UT administration approves the same, the agreements are signed with the respective ULBs and the replacement of street lights is taken up.*************
