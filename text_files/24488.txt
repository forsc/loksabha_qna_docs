                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Distribution Camps for Divyangjans




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 242†TO BE ANSWERED ON 20.07.2021DISTRIBUTION CAMPS FOR DIVYANGJANS†242.      SHRI RAMDAS C. TADAS:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes setting up of a free of cost medical equipment distribution camp for divyangjans post COVID-19 pandemic under National Vayoshree Yojana; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the current status of the free of cost medical equipment distribution camps in Wardha and Amarawati districts of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) to (c):   The Rashtriya Vayoshree Yojana is for supply of physical aids for the senior citizens. It does not give any medical equipment for the divyangjans. ***
