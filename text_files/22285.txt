                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3319






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Ration Money Allowance to paramilitary forces




Upendra Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Gazetted officers in CRPF are being given Ration Money Allowance (RMA) and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether Gazetted officers in other paramilitary forces such as Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP) are also being given RMA;(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government proposes to give RMA to all Gazetted officers of other paramilitary forces on the basis of parity with gazetted officers of CRPF; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (e):	All Gazetted officers upto the rank of Commandant in all Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs), including Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF), are entitled to Ration Money Allowance (RMA) with effect from 01.09.1998, subject to certain conditions, including the condition related to  place of posting/deployment.   Based on the Order of Hon’ble Delhi High -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.3319 FOR 16.03.2021Court dated 10/04/2019 in Writ Petition (C) No.4151/2015, RMA has been extended to Gazetted Officers of CRPF upto the rank of Commandant irrespective of their place of posting/deployment.  Consequent upon the Order of Hon’ble Delhi High Court, proposals have been received from BSF, SSB and ITBP for grant of RMA to all Gazetted Officers upto the rank of Commandant irrespective of their place of posting/deployment. ******
