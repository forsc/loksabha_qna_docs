                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1183






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Schemes under DBT




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and number of schemes which are under the Direct Benefits Transfer (DBT) system through the Central Government;(b)	the details of amount which has been released in last six years under DBT on yearly basis, Scheme – wise and State-wise; and (c)	the numbers of beneficiaries in each scheme, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                                          MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE                                    (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c):  381 Central Sector Schemes and Centrally Sponsored Schemes of various Ministries / Departments are under the DBT and on-boarded on the DBT Bharat Portal (as on 15.09.2020). There are around 130 crore DBT beneficiaries under these CS / CSS schemes.  Amount released through DBT in last six years are as follows:                                                                                                   (In Rs. crore)2013-14 & 2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-2046,294	61,942	74,689	1,90,871	3,29,796	3,81,632Scheme – wise and state-wise beneficiaries number are voluminous data which is available on public platform: www.dbtbharat.gov.in.                                              ****
