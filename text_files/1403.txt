                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Implementation of AB-PMJAY in States




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                     Imtiaz Jaleel Syed
                                                








                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether many States has shown reluctance or refused to implement central schemes like Ayushman Bharat etc., if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (b) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to formulate a policy to tackle this indifferent attitude of some State Governments; (c) the number of beneficiaries availed the benefit and likely to avail in 2019-20 under the scheme State-wise and total fund earmarked by Government for this purpose; and (d) whether sufficient funds are allocated for expansion of the scheme, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):    The information with respect to central schemes like Ayushman Bharat is being collected and would be placed on the table of the House.(c )  & (d):    As on 24.06.2019, the total number of hospital admissions under Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is 29,96,635. The total number of beneficiaries is around 10.74 crore.             Sufficient funds have been allocated for implementation of AB-PMJAY. At Interim Budget stage (2019-20), Rs. 6400/- crore were earmarked for AB-PMJAY.………
