                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2680






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Anganwadi Centres




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the number of Anganwadi Centres constructed across the country during the last three years; (b) 	whether the Government has proposed to construct more Anganwadi Centres across the country and if so, the details of such proposals and sites of construction, State/UT-wise; and(c)	the time by which these centres are likely to be constructed?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The number of Anganwadi Centres constructed in the country under Anganwadi Services in convergence with Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) during the last three years are as under :Financial Year	AWCs	Completed	Ongoing2017-18	12,959	-2018-19	17,687	-2019-20	10,837	59,486(b)	Under the revised scope of Anganwadi Services implemented from 01.12.2017, the Central assistance for construction of new Anganwadi Centre building is available in rural areas in convergence with MGNREGS on reimbursement basis. Under the said scope, State Government of Andhra Pradesh have constructed 3960 AWCs during 2018-19. In the current financial year, Government has proposed 58670 number of more Anganwadi Centres across the country. State/UT-wise details are at Annexure. (c)	The Anganwadi Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, wherein the Central Government in the Ministry of Women and Child Development is responsible for program planning while the responsibility of implementation of the scheme including construction of AWCs rests with States / UTs.****** AnnexureSTATEMENT REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (b) OF LOK SABHA USQ NO. 2680 FOR 06.03.2020 ASKED BY SHRI ASHOK KUMAR RAWAT REGARDING ANGANWADI CENTRES.Sr. NoStates/ UTs	No. of AWCs Buildings proposed in 2019-201	ANDHRAPRADESH	60002	BIHAR	10003	CHHATTISGARH	20004	GOA	1005	GUJARAT	20006	HARYANA	5007	JHARKHAND	08	KARNATAKA	10009	KERALA	100010	MADHYAPRADESH	1000011	MAHARASHTRA	25012	ODISHA	603613	PUNJAB	1500014	RAJASTHAN	015	TAMIL NADU	016	TELANGANA	200017	UTTAR PRADESH	50018	WEST BENGAL	600019	DELHI	020	PUDUCHERRY	2321	HIMACHALPRADESH	9022	JAMMU &KASHMIR *	50023	UTTARAKHAND	140124	ANDAMAN & NICOBAR	5025	CHANDIGARH	026	D. &NAGAR HAVELI	2027	DAMAN & DIU	028	LAKSHADWEEP	029	ARUNACHALPRADESH	030	ASSAM	150031	MANIPUR	100032	MEGHALAYA	60033	MIZORAM	034	NAGALAND	035	SIKKIM	036	TRIPURA	100Total	58670* The figures of J&K includes Ladakh UT.
