                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Supply of Fertilizer to Sri Lanka




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has supplied a large quantum of fertilizers to Sri Lanka in the recent past; and (b)	if so, the details thereof, fertilizer-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS                                 (DR.  MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b):   Department of Fertilizers (DoF) issued two NOCs dated 19.10.2021 & 22.10.2021 to Indian Farmers Fertilizer Cooperative Limited (IFFCO) to export 44064/- and 10,00,000/- bottles of Nano Urea (liquid) (Nano-Nitrogen) of 500 ml each respectively to Sri Lanka in accordance with letter No. 12019/1/2017-FPP dated 10th August, 2021 and Directorate General of Foreign Trade(DGFT) notification No. 67/2015-20 dated 07th January, 2019 issued by DoF and DGFT respectively.*********
