                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Construction Cost for Houses




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the amount given for construction of houses in rural areas is less than the total amount required;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) 	the details of the amount given under this scheme during the last two years, State-wise;(d) 	whether the Government has considered increasing the amount for individual house construction; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e): In order to achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by 2022, the erstwhile rural housing scheme Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY) has been restructured into Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) w.e.f. 1st April, 2016.  Under erstwhile rural housing scheme, IAY, the unit assistance was Rs. 70,000 for plain areas and Rs. 75,000 for difficult areas, IAP districts and Hilly States. Under PMAY-G the beneficiary is provided an enhanced unit assistance of Rs.1.20 lakh in plain areas and 1.30 lakh in hilly states, difficult areas and IAP districts for the construction of a pucca house. The Central Government provides assistance under PMAY-G for construction of houses to the beneficiaries. In addition, beneficiaries are provided following benefits:-i.	Up to 90/95 persondays of unskilled labour during house construction is being provided under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. ii.	Eligible PMAY-G beneficiaries are also provided Rs.12,000/- for the construction of a toilet under Swachh Bharat Mission (G), MGNREGS or any other dedicated financing source.iii.	The PMAY-G beneficiaries have a bouquet of options of house designs according to local geo-climatic conditions, using locally available construction materials and technologies for construction of  houses.iv.	Convergence with Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) for LPG connection, Electricity connection under Saubhagya and safe drinking water under  Ja Jeevan Mission, etc.,  At present, there is no proposal under consideration of  the Ministry for increasing the assistance for individual construction of houses to the beneficiaries.
