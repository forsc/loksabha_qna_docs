                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

454






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Rail Coach Factory in Jhansi




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways has accorded approval for setting up of Rail coach factory in Jhansi; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the construction work of the Jhansi Rail coach factory is being undertaken by VVNL company;(d) if so, the details thereof and the current status thereof;(e) whether the Railways has stopped the said work suddenly through an order by the Board; and(f) if so, the details thereof and views of the Government to restart the works?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION                                     (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b) A work of setting up of coach periodical overhauling and refurbishment workshop at Jhansi was sanctioned in the year 2018-19.(c) & (d) The work was entrusted to RVNL. (e) & (f) Based on developments in technology and rolling stock, the requirement is under review.*****
