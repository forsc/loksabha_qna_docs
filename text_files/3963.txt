                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3627






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






ESI Hospitals




Ganesan Selvam














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the funds allocated/likely to be allocated for the Employees State Insurance (ESI) hospitals projects, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu;(b)whether the Government proposes to upgrade and modernize ESI hospitals in Tamil Nadu; and (c)if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):		The details of funds allocated/likely to be allocated for the ESI hospitals projects, State/UT-wise are given in Annexure.(b) & (c):	Under Project Implementation Plan (PIP) for Financial Year 2019-20, Employees'' State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) has approved allocation of around Rs. 253 crore for purchase of latest modern  medical equipments, medicines and setting up of Ayush services so as to upgrade and modernize  Employees State Insurance (ESI) Hospitals in Tamil Nadu.*                                                *****
