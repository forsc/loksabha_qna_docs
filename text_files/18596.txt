                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

229






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Special Arrangement for Transgenders during Lockdown




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 229TO BE ANSWERED ON 02.02.2021SPECIAL ARRANGEMENT FOR TRANSGENDERS DURING LOCKDOWN 229. SHRI ANUMULA REVANTH REDDY.: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of special arrangements, if any, being made for the Transgender Community amid lockdown; (b) the details of Financial Assistance, Ration or other assistance provided to the community amid lockdown, State-wise; (c) if not, the reasons for neglecting the said community amid lockdown; (d) whether the Government is planning to provide the transgender Community with some financial assistance to support their livelihood; and (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b):  During COVID-19, the Ministry through National Backward Class Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) has given Rs.1,500/-each to Transgender persons, through Direct Benefit Transfer(DBT). NBCFDC has additionally provided assistance for ration supplies to Transgender persons through/on the recommendation of District Administration. An expenditure of Rs 98.50 lakhs was incurred in supporting 6,940 members of Transgender Community from 33 States/UTs, which includes Rs 85.66 lakhs to 5,711 Transgender persons as subsistence allowance and Ration kit of Rs 12.83 lakhs to 1229 Transgender persons.Details of State-wise Financial Assistance provided to the Community amid lockdown is enclosed at Annexure – I.Free Helpline assistance is setup to provide psycho-social support to Transgender Community during COVID period.(c):   Does not arise.(d) & (e):  THE TRANSGENDER PERSONS (PROTECTION OF RIGHTS)ACT, 2019 provides for formulation of the scheme for the welfare of transgender persons  for providing  rehabilitation,   education, health care, interest subvention, counselling, skill development, privilege of Self help Groups and financial assistance.*****Annexure 1 State wise Amount transferred through Direct Bank Transfer & Ration Assistance to Transgender Personsduring the year 2019-20 to 2020-21 ( Lockdown period)    Sr	  State	Details of DBT	Details of Ration Assistance	 Grand Total		Nos. of TGs	Total Amount Transferred (Rs.)
