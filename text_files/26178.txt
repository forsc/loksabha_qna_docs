                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2058






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Implementation of Aided Projects




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether his Ministry is implementing various aided projects in several States of the country; (b)	if so, the details of such projects including the name of funding agency and total cost of projects, State/UT-wise;(c)	the details of such projects under consideration and lying pending so far;(d)	whether any such proposals/projects from the State Governments are lying pending with his Ministry and if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon;(e)	whether any monitoring mechanism is in place to evaluate the outcome of these projects to avoid time and cost overrun; and(f)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)& (b)Several Externally Aided Projects are being implemented in the States and funded bydonor agencies (JICA, French Development Agency, GIZ, Kfw, World Bank etc.). The details of externally aided forest development projects being implemented by various State Forest Departments are given in Annexure.(c) & (d) There is no project pending with the Ministry.  (e) &(f)  The mechanism for comprehensive monitoring and evaluation of any project are included in the project design itself. It includes provision for concurrent, mid-term and post project evaluation for performance as well as impact of the project. The Project Implementing Agency (PIA) also submits quarterly progress report that are shared with the funding agency. After the completion of the project, there is provision for assessment by reputed independent institutions or organisations to ascertain the achievements and fulfillment of project objectives. ******
