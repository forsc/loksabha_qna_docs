National Creche Scheme




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds allocated/ released/utilized in respect of National Creche Scheme (NCS) during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(b)	the details of funding pattern between Central and State Governments under this scheme;(c)	whether Union Government has decided to change the existing funding pattern for this scheme, if so the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(d) 	whether the Government has made any assessment of the functioning of NCS in the country since its inception, if so, the details and the outcome thereof; and(e)	whether the Government is also considering to discontinue the scheme, if so, the details thereof along with the other steps taken by the Government to provide more facilities for working women?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)		The National Creche Scheme is being implemented as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme from 1.1.2017. The details of funds allocated/ released/ utilised in respect of National Crèche Scheme (NCS) during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise are at Annexure- I and Annexure- II(b)	The fund sharing pattern amongst Centre, States/UTs &Non Governmental Organisations /Voluntary Organisations for all recurring components of the scheme is in the ratio of  60:30:10 for States, 80:10:10 for North Eastern  States and Himalayan States and 90:0:10 for UTs.(c)	No, Sir. Does not arise.(d)	No, Sir. Does not arise. (e)	No, Sir.
