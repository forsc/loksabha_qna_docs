                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1498






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Boost Higher Education




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry is proposing to infuse Rs. 5,000 crores in various institutions toboost higher education in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of institutions selected for this purpose, State/UT-wise including AndhraPradesh;(d) whether it is also proposed to increase the number of Model Degree Colleges(MDCs) and provide funds to them under this scheme;(e) if so, details of MDCs that are going to set up/upgrade the existing ones, State/UTwise;(f) the time fixed for the utilisation of Rs. 5,000 crores; and(g) whether the amount also includes whatever has been given to new centralinstitutions such as IITs, IIMs, IIITs, NITs, etc., opened during the last 4-5 years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (g): Towards making available finance for basic infrastructure and top endresearch facilities in higher education institutions , Government of India has created theHigher Education Financing Agency ( HEFA). HEFA provides timely and project basedfunding to school, higher and medical education institutions and envisages financingprojects up to Rs. 1 lakh crore by year 2022 for the above categories of institutions.As on date, sanctions have been made by HEFA to the extent of Rs.19,558 crore,for projects worth Rs. 33,517 crore. The institution-wise details including institutions inAndhra Pradesh are at Annexure I.The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA),provides central assistance to state higher educational institutions under variouscomponents such as infrastructure Grants to Universities and Colleges, Model DegreeColleges, New Professional Colleges, Upgradation of existing degree colleges to ModelDegree Colleges etc. Under RUSA, a total of Rs. 13571.62 Crore has been approvedincluding both Central and State Share. Under RUSA Central assistance has beenapproved for creation of 130 New Model Degree Colleges and upgradation of 125existing colleges to Model Degree Colleges. The detail of institutions approved underRUSA state-wise including Andhra Pradesh is at Annexure II.During the year 2015-16, six Indian Institutes of Managements (IIMs) were set up atAmritsar (Punjab), Bodh Gaya (Bihar), Nagpur (Maharashtra), Sambalpur (Odisha),Sirmaur (Himachal Pradesh), Vishakhapatnam (Andhra Pradesh) and during the year2016-17, IIM Jammu (Jammu & Kashmir) was established. The Union Cabinetapproved a total cost of Rs. 3775.42 crore for the establishment and operationalisationof permanent campus of these seven new IIMs.Further, the Union Cabinet in its meeting held on 24.10.2017 approved constructionof permanent campuses of 6 New Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) at a total cost ofRs. 7002.42 crores under Phase A. Additionally, Rs. 144.5325 crores have beenreleased to the new National Institute of Technology, Andhra Pradesh since 2016-17.The Cabinet has also approved the setting up of Indian Institute of Science Educationand Research (IISER), Tirupati with a capital cost of Rs. 1137.16 crore. Also, an amountof Rs. 297 crore has been earmarked towards the establishment of Indian Institute ofInformation Technology Design & Manufacturing, Kurnool (IIITDMK).
