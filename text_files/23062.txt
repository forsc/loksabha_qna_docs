                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4093






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Upgradation Of Anganwadi Centres And Mini Anganwadis




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)      whether the Ministry has received any proposal from the State Government of Maharashtra seeking establishment of new Anganwadi Centres and upgradation of mini Anganwadi Centres in the State; (b)       if so, the details thereof; and (c)      the action taken on the request and the funds already released/being released to the State? 






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c)  All the 14 lakh AWCs approved by the Government of India under Anganwadi Services have already been sanctioned to States/UTs, including Maharashtra.  A total number of 1,10,219 AWCs are operational in State of Maharashtra as on 30.09.2020. Additional needs are met when proposals including UCs are submitted properly and as per norms. Through interactions with State of Maharashtra including in recently conducted review meetings, implementation related issues such as utilization of funds and need for timely submission of Utilization Certificates (UCs)/Statement of Expenditure (SoEs), etc. have been emphasized upon. ******
