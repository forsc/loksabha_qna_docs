                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1583






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Construction of Arvi-Warud Railway line




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the construction of Arvi to Warud Railway line in Nagpur division of the Central Railway is being delayed due to slow process of land acquisition; and (b)	if so, the time by which land will be acquired and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): 	No, Sir.  Arvi-Warud new railway line project is not sanctioned.(b):	Does not arise.*****
