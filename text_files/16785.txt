                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

626






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Vocal for Local




Jayanta Kumar Roy













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Government has launched compaign, “Vocal for Local” for promotion of local brands and domestic manufacturing;(b) 	if so, the details thereof including the allocation of funds in this regard;(c) 	whether the Government has assessed the impact of COVID-19 Pandemic on the Industry;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	whether trade including export has significantly reduced during the lockdown and if so, the details thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) & (b): 	Yes. Local brands and domestic manufacturing are being encouraged via marketing initiatives and customized incentives by the different Ministries. Phased Manufacturing Programme and Performance Linked Incentives have been introduced for certain specified products. DPIIT is also promoting the sale and manufacturing of Indian toys and other Indian products.(c) & (d): 	Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade (DPIIT) has been regularly monitoring and analyzingthe impact of Covid-19 pandemic on Indian industry. Various sectors were affected due to the nation-wide lockdown. However, after the lockdown was relaxed, we see significant improvement in several sectors of the economy. (e): 	Both exports and imports declined during the lockdown period. The trade balance too declined and turned into a trade surplus during June, 2020. The global disruption of supply chains and the overall decrease in demand led to the decline in exports. However, exports are now picking up and have almost reached the pre-covid levels.*****
