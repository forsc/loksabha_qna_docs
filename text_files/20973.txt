                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Setting up of New Science Museums




Chirag Paswan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up some more new science museums in the country; (b)	if so, the State-wise and location-wise details thereof;(c)  	the amount of funds likely to be spent on the said museums; and(d)	the time by which the said museums are likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)to(d): Yes, Sir. The Science Centres/Cities are set up by National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), an autonomous organization of Ministry of Culture. NCSM is implementing agency of Ministry of Culture (MoC) under the Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science (SPoCS) of MoC and sets up these Science Museums on the request of the State/UT Govt. upon submission of DPR with commitment of encumbrance free developed land, share of funds and other commitments as per scheme guidelines.Ministry has taken up projects for setting up of new science cities/centres under SPoCS Scheme of Govt. of India as per list attached at Annexure.***** AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (A)TO(D) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2249 FOR 08.03.2021New Science Centre projects taken up by NCSM under SPOCS Scheme of Govt. of IndiaSl.No.	Names of the new science centre projects taken up by NCSM under SPOCS Scheme of Govt. of India	Located in State/UT	Project Cost (Figures in Lakhs)	Share of project cost(Figure in Percentage)	Tentative date of completion of the project GoI	State/UT 1	Science City, Guwahati	Assam	11000.00	90%	10%	31.03.202404.04.2019 (date of LoI for construction work) 54 months from the date of issue of LoI2	Regional Science Centre, Kottayam	Kerala	1450.00	50%	50%	31.12.2021[Delayed due to CoVID-19 lockdown]3	Sub-Regional Science Centre, Palampur	Himachal Pradesh	600.00	100%	0%	31.12.2021  [Delayed due to CoVID-19 lockdown]4	Sub-Regional Science Centre, Udaipur	Tripura	600.00	90%	10%	28.2.2021 [scheduled date of inauguration]5	Sub-Regional Science Centre, Gaya	Bihar	500.00	50%	50%	31.12.2021[Delayed due to CoVID-19 lockdown]6	Sub- Regional Science Centre, Almora	Uttarakhand	600.00	100%	0%	31.03.20227	Sub-Regional Science Centre, Udaipur	Rajasthan	500.00	50%	50%	31.03.20228	Sub-Regional Science Centre, Mayabundar	A&N Islands	600.00	90%	10%	27 months from the date of issue of LoI.
