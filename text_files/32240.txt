                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

332






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Computerization of Gram Panchayats in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the funds allocated by the Government for the development of Gram Panchayats in the State of Jharkhand from the year 2019, district-wise;(b)	the details of the Panchayats in the State which have been computerized; district-wise;(c)	the total number of Panchayats, which have been computerized in the said State?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a)	to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 332 FOR ANSWER ON 21.12.2021 REGARDING COMPUTERIZATION OF GRAM PANCHAYATS IN JHARKHAND___________________________________________________________________________(a)   A total of Rs. 5166.71 crore has been released to the State of Jharkhand   under the Central Finance Commissions Award and the schemes of the Ministry of Panchayati Raj i.e. Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) and Incentivisation of Panchayats (IoP) during 2018-19 to 2021-22 (as on 16.12.2021). Based on the information received from the Government of Jharkhand, the District-wise funds released is at Annexure-I. (b) & (c) As informed by the Government of Jharkhand, of the total 4,350 Gram Panchayats in the State, 3,749 Gram Panchayats have been computerised. The district-wise details are at Annexure-II.***
