                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2572






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






National Portal for Transgender Persons




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2572TO BE ANSWERED ON 14.12.2021NATIONAL PORTAL FOR TRANSGENDER PERSONS 2572. SHRIMATI RITA BAHUGUNA JOSHI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the total number of registered Transgender people and total IDs and Certificate that has been issued from the “National Portal for Transgender Persons”, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) whether Government will make the arrangements to switch the General and Technical support email of this Portal from the domain of a Private Email Server (Gmail) to Government server (NIC) soon to further protection of personal data; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the reason therefore?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR) (a) : So far, 5,414 valid applications have been received on National Portal for Transgender Persons. Out of these applications, 3,846 Certificate of Identity and Identity Cards have been issued. State wise details of applications are attached in Annexure. These figures are dynamic in nature and keep changing on real time basis.(b) to (d) : Data of National Portal for Transgender Persons is now hosted only on NIC server. URL of the Portal is transgender.dosje.gov.in***** Annexure referred to in reply to Part (a) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 2572 to be answered on 14.12.2021 regarding “National Portal for Transgender Persons” State Name	Total valid applications	Certificate issued Andhra Pradesh	1472	1331Arunachal Pradesh	0	0Assam	8	0Bihar	10	3Chandigarh/UT	7	2Chhattisgarh	65	0Delhi/UT	29	13Goa	2	0Gujarat	520	402Haryana	13	4Himachal Pradesh	7	7Jammu & Kashmir	27	5Jharkhand	17	11Karnataka	92	0Kerala	224	102Madhya Pradesh	450	403Maharashtra	412	254Manipur	125	53Orissa	1381	1162Poducherry/UT	79	24Punjab	4	0Rajasthan	14	1Tamil Nadu	194	3Telangana	127	40Uttar Pradesh	67	17Uttarakhand	15	8West Bengal	53	1Total	5,414	3,846*****
