                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1788






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Turmeric Board




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to set up a Turmeric Board at Vellore, Tamil Nadu;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the expected date of setting up of the said Board; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)      (a) & (b): Presently, there is no proposal to set up a Turmeric Board at Vellore, Tamil Nadu. (c) :   The mandate for production, research, development, post-harvest treatment and domestic marketing of spices, including turmeric, is vested in the Union Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, which implements several programmes related to the production and development of spices, including turmeric, under the Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH).          The Agriculture / Horticulture Departments in various States also implement certain schemes for the promotion of Spices.            The Spices Board undertakes activities related to export promotion and quality control of spices exported from India. It has established a crop specific Spices Development Agency for turmeric at Erode, Tamil Nadu, under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary of the State for coordinating development, marketing, quality control and export promotion of turmeric. ******
