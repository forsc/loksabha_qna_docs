                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3388






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Import of Chemicals and Petrochemicals




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the import of chemicals and petrochemicals has increased during thelast three years;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether there is a decline in domestic production of chemicals andpetrochemicals during the said period; and(d) if so, the reasons therefor along with the details of the remedial measurestaken to meet the growing domestic demand ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS (SHRI D. V. SADANANDAGOWDA)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The import of chemical and chemical products for last three years isas under:Year Import( Rupees in Crore)2017-18 3178562018-19 3948342019-20 359660(Source: DGCIS, Ministry of Commerce and Industry)The import of Chemicals and Petrochemicals increased due to major factors such asinsufficient domestic production capacity to meet the growing domestic demand,comparatively higher cost of production in the country, and also due to comparativelysmaller scale of operations.(c) & (d) Based on Domestic production of major chemicals and petrochemicalsmonitored by the Department, production of major chemicals and petrochemicals in lastthree years has increased and details are as under:Production ( in 000'' MT)2017-2018 2018-2019 2019-20Chemicals 11069 11589 11943Petrochemicals 36813 37519 43524Total 47882 49108 55467****
