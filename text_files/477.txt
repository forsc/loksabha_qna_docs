                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

60






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Setting up of PSUs




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has set up any Public Sector Undertakings/Enterprises in Chikkodi area of Karnataka;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is considering to set up a PSU suitable to the area keeping in view the availability of skilled and unskilled manpower in the vicinity; and (d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARVIND GANPAT SAWANT)(a) to (d): A Statement  is laid on the Table of the House.     *****                                                                               STATEMENT      A Statement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 60* for Answer on 25.06.2019 regarding Setting up of PSUs raised by Sh. Annasaheb Shankar Jolle----------------- (a) & (b):  As on 31.03.2018, there were 21 CPSEs with registered office in Karnataka.  List of these CPSEs along with registered office address is at Annexure – I.  (c) & (d):  Information is being collected and will be laid on the table of the house.                                       -------------
