                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Sainik School in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that Sainik school of district Gopalganj in Bihar is running without full strength of staff/teachers;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to approve all infrastructure facilities to the school and increase the number of seats in the school; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the total number of girl and boy students enrolled in the school?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) No, Sir.  Sainik School Gopalganj is functioning with its full strength of authorized staff/teachers.  Few vacancies are under process for appointment due to routine turnover of staff.(b)  The following posts are presently vacant due to routine turnover/promotion and recruitment process is under progress:- (i) 	PGT (Physics) - due to Inter School Transfer.(ii) 	PGT (Chemistry) - due to Inter School Transfer.(iii) 	TGT (Computer Science) - due to Inter School Transfer.(iv)	TGT (Mathematics) – due to promotion of the teacher.(v)	General Employees (03) – Initial vacancies.(c) The provision of infrastructure in terms of Land and building is the responsibility of the State Government i.e. Govt of Bihar.(d)  Presently the School holds total strength of 476 cadets wherein no girl cadets have been enrolled except for 16 girls, who are staff children enrolled as Day Scholars.*****
