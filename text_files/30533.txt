                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2282






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Training of Students in Defence Institutions




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether some selected students of Delhi University are being imparted training at research centres under the Defence Research and Development Organisation; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the criteria for selection of students for the training in Defence institutions?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b) Yes, Sir. DRDO has a scheme known as “Training of students of Graduation/Post Graduation in Engineering/General Science at DRDO Labs/Establishments”. This scheme is open for all Indian Institutions including Delhi University. The students desirous of programme apply directly to labs/establishments for specific subjects.(c)	The criteria for selection of students for the training in Defence Institutions is as follows:•	Each year the Engineering/Science graduates/post graduates apply directly to the concerned labs for internship programmes.•	The labs screen the applications as per the availability of the lab expertise and scientists to guide the students in their projects.*****
