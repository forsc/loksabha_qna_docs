                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Dispute Between Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka Over Birth Place of Hanuman




Sanganna Amarappa Karadi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)	whether recently there was a clash between Andhra based TTD Board and Karnataka State about the Birthplace of Hanuman and was in discussion in all medias. TTD claims that Hanuman was born in one of the Seven Hills of Tirupati and is about to submit a report, whereas Karnataka had displayed documentary evidences to prove Hanuman was born in Anjanadri situated in Koppal District;(b)	whether this matter has come to the notice of the Union Government;(c)	if so, the measures which the Government has taken to investigate the fact;(d)	if not, steps taken to direct the ASI to inspect and submit a detailed report about  birthplace of Hanuman; and(e)	the action taken to declare the actual birthplace of Hanuman at the earliest to avoid further clashes between the neighbouring States?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION (SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) &(b)	Yes, Sir. Government is aware about the matter through reports in media.  (c)to(e)	No proposal is under consideration.  *****
