                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3235






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Pending Subsidy for Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether a sum of Rs. 6039 crore towards subsidy to Odisha State Civil Supplies Corporation is pending with the Government; and (b) 	if so, the time by when the pending amount will be cleared by the Government as its delay may hamper the proper procurement by the State agency?






ANSWER




A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (b): 	Admissible amount of Provisional/Advance Food Subsidy is released to Decentralized Procurement(DCP) States such as Odisha, based on their quarter wise claims. Release of food subsidy is a continuation process. The claims received from State Government are processed keeping in view the opening and closing balance of stock, procurement, allocation and distribution of foodgrains, Food Corporation of India (FCI) reconciliation, Utilization Certificate, Economic Cost of foodgrains, etc, among others. An amount of Rs.8985.73 Crore has already been released to Government of Odisha in F.Y. 2020-21 against their claims, the details of which are annexed.*****ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3235 FOR ANSWER ON 16.03.2021 IN THE LOK SABHADetails of the provisional/advance food subsidy released to Govt. of Odisha during F.Y. 2020-21Date	Amount (Rupees in Crore)27.04.2020	928.7430.04.2020	2663.0527.07.2020	1238.0324.08.2020	870.7408.03.2021	3285.17Total	8985.73Details of the provisional/advance food subsidy claims under scrutiny in DepartmentS.
