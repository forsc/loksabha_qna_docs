                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






PFC Programme




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the quantum of budget allocated to and utilized by Patent Facilitation Cell (PFC) for implementation of various programmes/schemes/activities during the last two years and the current year;(b)       the total number of patents that have been filed till date, Region-wise and Year-wise; and(c)       whether the PFC offers financial help to startups and other young entrepreneurs to file the patents and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????, ??????? ?? ???????????? ?????? ?? ?????? ??????? ??????(??. ???? ?????) (a)       Funds allocated to Patent Facilitation Cell (PFC) for Intellectual Property (IP) facilitation, conducting workshops & trainings on Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) and implementation of  Women Scientist Scheme (WOS-C) was Rs 9.5 crore during the last two financial years i.e. 2018-19 and 2019-20. The funds were fully utilized for the purpose for which it was allocated. During the current financial year (2020-21), fund of Rs 75 lakh have been allocated to PFC for release. (b)       Details of year-wise patent applications filed by PFC in India and other countries is given in following table: S.
