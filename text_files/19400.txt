                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Impact of Covid




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is providing jobs under special category for the promotion of youth affairs and sports particularly in view of impact of COVID-19 is pandemic;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a)	The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports is not providing any jobs under special category for the promotion of youth affairs and sports in view of impact of COVID-19.(b) & (c): Does not arise. ****
