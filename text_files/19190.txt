                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

50






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Hajj Embarkation Point Facilities




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  proposes  to  include Calicut  International  Airport  among  other  Hajj Embarkation point facilities in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;  and(c)  whether  the  decision  is  based  on  any  official investigation report carried out by any of the Government agencies and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Statement is laid on the Table of the House.LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 50 TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.02.2021 REGARDING  HAJJ EMBARKATION POINT FACILITIES TABLED BY SHRI BENNY BEHANAN, MP.;  (a) and (b): In view of Covid-19 pandemic situation, the number of Embarkation Points for haj 2021 have been reduced from existing 21 to 10. Calicut airport has not been designated as an Embarkation Point for Haj 2021. The arrangements for Haj 2021 are being made under special circumstances with special norms, rules & regulations, eligibility criteria, etc. due to Covid-19 Pandemic.  Provisional guidelines including Embarkation Points for Haj 2021, have been issued by Haj Committee of India after extensive consultation with concerned Ministries and Indian mission in Saudi Arabia, taking into consideration the expected reduced Haj quota for Indian pilgrims to be allocated by the Saudi Government.  ;  (c): No Sir. Ministry of Minority Affairs decides the embarkation points on the recommendation of Haj Committee of India (HCoI).
