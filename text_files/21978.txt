                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Setting up of New CNG Pumps




Diya Kumari













                                                    Ajay (Teni) Misra
                                                








                                                    Chunni Lal Sahu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the list of the total number of CNG pumps set up in the country, State-wise and district-wise;(b) whether the Government has any proposal to set up new CNG filling stations in a number ofStates for making India pollution free;(c) if so, the details thereof, State-wise/District-wise including Madhya Pradesh andChhattisgarh;(d) the number of stations proposed to be set up during the first phase and the time limit fixed forthe same; and(e) whether the Government proposes to set up such pumps, specifically near NationalHighways?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) State-wise list of the total number of Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) Stations in thecountry is at Annexure. The list is also available at https://www.pngrb.gov.in/databank/PNG.CNG.(b)& (d)Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) is the authority to grantauthorization to the entities to lay, build, operate or expand for the development of City GasDistribution (CGD) or local natural gas distribution network in Geographical Areas (GAs) as perPNGRB Act, 2006.The Minimum Work Programme (MWP) as per the authorization to CGDentities entail establishment of 8181 CNG pumps over a period of 8-10 years across 27 Statesand 3 Union Territories(c) At present, in the state of Madhya Pradesh, the MWP entails setting up of 417 CNGstations in the authorized districts over the period of 8-10 years. At present PNGRB has notauthorized any entity for development of CGD network in any GA in the state of Chhattisgarh.Details of CNG stations as per the MWP are at Annexure.(e) The CNG pumps are set up by the CGD entities also along the National Highways asper their work plan and techno -commercial feasibility.*****
